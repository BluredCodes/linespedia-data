---
title: "paradox in paradise"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "paradox-in-paradise"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48942-paradox-in-paradise-by-Seer"

I have wings
Yet I cannot fly
I feel so alive
That I might die

I live with a healthy sickness
And a lovely disease
My feet touch the clouds
And my head's in the seas

I am blinded by the vision
And deafened by the sound
I am as heavy as lead
But don't weigh a pound

I vacation to the norm
I live in the resort of hell
I am a prisoner
of freedoms cell

my life is a perfect disorder
I am so happy I'm sad
because i finally realize
that I've gone mad
