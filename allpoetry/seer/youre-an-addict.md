---
title: "You're an addict"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "youre-an-addict"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47689-Youre-an-addict-by-Seer"

Yet again i will be used
For your need for power
And your need to control
You need make me hurt
So you can see the pain
You cause so much damage
To my fragile state
And you break my heart
Over and over again
You kill my joy
And you ruin my life
You need this
To feel better about your self
You need to see me below you
Now it's apperent to me
But it's to late
I already love you
And you're an addict of my pain
