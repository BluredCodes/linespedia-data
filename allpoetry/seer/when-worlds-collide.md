---
title: "when worlds collide"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "when-worlds-collide"

soon the wandering stars
will burn as one
all the planets
will align with the sun

the moon eclipses
and darkens the sky
till every one
of the suns rays die

the planets fight
amongst the heavens
from the nine
now stand seven

life will end
when the bodies merge
then all things
the combination will purge

blackness sets in
and all is in peace
even the sun
has been deceased

this civil war
the galaxy does despise
so their punishment
is our demise

bonds are broken
and the system blows apart
they avoid one another
so conflicts wont start

try as they will
they cannot hide
when what's left of their worlds
begin to collidethis isn't about planets. it's about friends. unless you want to end like this, hold your friends close!
