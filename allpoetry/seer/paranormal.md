I look at my friend
As she lies on the bed
I look in her eyes 
They're glossy and dead

Demonic sounds 
Roll from her mouth
Entering the room
The exorcist shouts

Things lift off
And fly about the room
I fall backwards
Fearing my doom

Screams billow up
From a world of pain
I feel my blood
Freeze in my veins

She floats off her bed
And becomes a living ghost
That looks to kill
And my friend is her host
