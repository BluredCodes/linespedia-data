I've seen the thing
That should never be
I look at the graves
And say "it's because of me"

People tell me no
But i think it is
We had on our headlights
But he didn't have his

I wasn't wearing a seat belt
When the two cars became one
I flew through the windshield
Then all was done

The ambulance came
Took me away
But i passed out
And didn't wake till day

Saveing me
Was such a waste
When three could have been saved
With some haste

But i lived to tell
And now you see
My parents deaths
Where caused by me
