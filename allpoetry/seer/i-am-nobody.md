title: "I am nobody"
writer: "Seer"

I am a nobody
As are you
No one cares
About what we do

We are all lost
Frightened and scared
And nobody listens
Because nobody cares

We all need someone
To give us love
But no one is there
And it kills the dove

The only way out
Is leave it behind
So i do
And i lose my mind

I cut my wrists
And watch them bleed
Now truly i am
Nobody indeed
