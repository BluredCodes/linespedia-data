Eighteen years thrown away
i try

along the pathways at Grand Valley State University,
boys’ eyes linger on particular attributes

eighteen minutes until class begins
i try

air-brushed sapphire lips accent ice blue star eyes
sunlight reflects from eyes’ glittering green
heels clapping against cracked pavement detract from flowing ribbons
of mascara

cloaked in rain bow
and exposing enough tan to make them visualize
feel heat radiate
taste Dr. Pepper residue on artificially whitened teeth
frozen by lust conjured by batted eyes looking so fine
highlighted by mascara

harder, gentlemen,
passion requires an act of force
which will make you uncomfortable
and valerie
tattoo paintings on your skin
thrust stainless steel through your clitoris
encase your hips in meager cut-offs the boys drool over
thank you, gentlemen

eighteen years thrown away
i try to produce an admirable statement

indifferent – accustomed to strangers’ stares
they imagine hands on thighs
they fantasize beds occupied
these adolescent males are far from what you desire

jaded - habituated to strange stares
nylons Syran-Wrap Naired legs
platform steps bounce emancipated breasts
we are far from what we desire
