You are loved.
Yes, you are.
Yes, you are.
In every little thing you do.
I need you.
Yes, I do.
Yes, I do.

Pining away for the same old girl.
When you’re not with her,
hug the silly little things that make you smile.
The silly little smile she smiles when you go that extra mile.
Spend time with her – don’t let go of her-
don’t forget to remind her she makes you smile.

You are loved.
Yes, you are.
Yes, you are.
In every little thing you do.
I need you.
Yes, I do.
Yes, I do.
In every little smile you smile-
Every little tale you tell-
Every little premise you test-
Every little dream you dream.
You are loved.
Yes, you are.
Yes, you are.
I do love you.
