Jennifer Robin
Nestle down.
Your baby days
Will soon be gone.

Come sleep in my arms,
And we shall rock
In our golden chair
To the tick of the clock.

Though time, in his passage,
Will steal you away -
Away to the life you will
Live someday...

Yet, still, will I rock
In our golden chair,
To the tick of the clock
And remember you there.
