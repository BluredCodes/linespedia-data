title: "Universe, I Am Here"

Universe, I am here
among the pebbles 
of flesh.

Reduce a rock
and I
to our winding phonemes
of message.

Tell me what we sa y.

Free me from the darkness
of five senses
stewing their single
flavors into one
     impenetrable message.

Free me

from the bound ropes
of my defeat
which lectures to me.

Are there birds that sing?
Wing flights against
the flight yesness
fall.

Bullets of immorality,
vats of creed
betray my flesh.

The thread of sensuality
frays.

It breaks.

(This poem will self-destruct
in 40 years.)
