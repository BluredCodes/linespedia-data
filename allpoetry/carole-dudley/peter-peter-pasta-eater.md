Peter, Peter
Pasta eater
Had a wife
And couldn't
Beat her.
Put her in a
Metaphor
And there
He beat her
Evermore.

He pushed
And shoved
And crammed
His fox,
Into this tiny,
Little 
Box.

Slapped a label
On the crate
To mark her
Lesser than
Her mate, then
Sat upon his
wifely drone
Exuding his
Testosterone.
