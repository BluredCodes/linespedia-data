the little grove of
     weather-warped hairs
          leans awry
          full flowering
in its meadow of 
     itch.

Chaos
     descends, as
     chaos will,
     Without warning.

Clawing.  Raking.  Ravishing.
     
Why!

Why!  Why!  Why?! 
 What
     did we do to excite
          this
          cataclysmic
          malevolence?!
