In my antiquity
i aspire to be nothing -
merely,
a good animal -
like the denizens
of the forest,
keening by instinct
the environment
of my immersion,
inhaling every redolent
bush along the way
for spores dropped
by time and space in
its careening rush
down the dark
oblivion
from which we came.

Be human?

I think not.
