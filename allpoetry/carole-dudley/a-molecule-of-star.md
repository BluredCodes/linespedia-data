title: "A Molecule of Star"
writer: "Carole Dudley"

i am gravid with seeing,

heavy with question.

the blood dreams grow chill,

the godwork is done.

i have dealt with my symbols

meekly, one by one,

worshiped the season of love

for its whisper of answer.

dumb within process i flow

inept at diversions.

i cast my electric eyes

on the campfire of gypsies,

dozing while kings pass.

in highland meadow now

i hear the harvest calling.

secret in my fist i hold

a molecule of Star.
