title: "Bliss In Solitude"

Wind chimes and cyclamen,
     silence and sun,
Color and contrast and 
     fragrance at 1:00.
Pungence of puppy,
     shimmer of bloom,
Oh, Lord, let me spend
     my whole life in
My room.
