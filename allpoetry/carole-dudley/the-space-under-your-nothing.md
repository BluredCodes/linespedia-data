The poem

comes to you

when it is

hungry.

Chase it  around

your charm

 you'll never

catch it.



Stand still though

barely breathing

eyes fixed

on that space under

your nothing

and breathe. 

Like a wild 

yearling, it may

walk up to you

from behind

and nip

your jump.
