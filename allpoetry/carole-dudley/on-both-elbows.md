---
title: "On Both Elbows"
writer: "Carole Dudley"

I have just been
Bitten on Both Elbows
by an unknown 
Connoisseur of
Callous.
I'd like to catch
the little
sadist; I'd 
Teach Him to Bite
me on Both Elbows
Like he Knew What
He Was Doing.
