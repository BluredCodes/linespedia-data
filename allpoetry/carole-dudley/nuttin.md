---
title: "Nuttin"
writer: "Carole Dudley"

A universe
 can flow

  Aglow,

from nib of
  penny
   pen 

(a galaxy of
   bon mots
     from
  Rousseau's)
and splash! immortal

caper on bits of
     paper.

     Why
    must i
  pant captive

      In

 This calcine
  lair, where
drafting classic

  literature

Breeds despair.

    For here
 one fears, the
liquid quill of 
feeling's glutton

on rood and perch of
     parchment

       blazes

       Nuttin.
