title: "Lustrous Paws Falling"

Gone in the blink of the moon

Aztec dogs of delight

Hewn by the cleaver of time

They lead like arrows sprung



Aztec dogs of delight

Spirits of love perfected

They lead like arrows sprung

This shivering soul to its womb




Spirits of love perfected

Guide from vision's inferno

This shivering soul to its womb

Lustrous paws streaking




Guide from vision's inferno

Hewn by the cleaver of time

Lustrous paws streaking

Gone in the blink of the moon.This is a re-write, based on the Aztec myth of the little dogs who lead the soul safely across the 9 rivers of the underworld. Illustration by Sunny Dudley
