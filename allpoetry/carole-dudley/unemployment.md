This morning's fantasy of wealth
Shot down...
A flight
Of migratory geese
That burnt the sky,
Wing pulse of Wall Street.
Flapping up to seek
The durable good
While object-fetishists
Bagged all day like hustlers
Against the wide-eyed land,
Slouching against emporiums
of delight, harvesting their
Wealth.  I reaped no answer.

Night comes.  Oil black and
Smokestack grey.
The fields are fenced.
The hustler's satiation, good.
Technology strides seeding
Her rows of invention in
Four-square universe.
i, in my media-drawn days arc
Through unemployment,
Wing pulse of the earth.

i, in my ache, down ache through
the Pliocene of night
In sinking flight
From sky to bush.
This morning's fantasy of dough,
No go!
