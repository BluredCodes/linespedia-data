Poor Pure Little
Primapera Soul
Groans pitifully
In tertiary labor.

At 3:00 A.M. her
white loins parted to
Admit the potent
Thrust of evil
Thought.  At 5:00

The neonate
Dream, rubbery slick,
And squealing,
Squalls and wails
Cantankerously
In my virgin bed,
Its Mother, 

Dead.
