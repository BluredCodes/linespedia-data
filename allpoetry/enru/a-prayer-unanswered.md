---
title: "A Prayer Unanswered"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "a-prayer-unanswered"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

One small wish,
One simple prayer,
To end the pain,
To end the hell,
To end the life,
My one small wish,
Is a knife.

No one is there,
To hear my prayer.

Sitting down,
All alone,
No one around,
To hear me cry.
Why must I live this life?
I'd end it now,
If I had a knife.

No one is there,
To hear my prayer.

I sit and cry,
No one will miss me,
She'll be fine,
Her life will go on,
It'll be the end of my life,
Damn you God,
I need a knife.

No one is there,
To hear my prayer.
