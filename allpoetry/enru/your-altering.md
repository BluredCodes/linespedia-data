---
title: "Your Altering"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "your-altering"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34319-Your-Altering-by-Enru"
---

Don't try and change me to your way,
Through your altering I'm going to stay.
The choice is mine to choose,
Your attempts have failed, you're going to lose.
I don't know why you just can't see,
That I'll never be what you want me to be.
Your attempts have become untolerable,
I am myself, unalterable.
