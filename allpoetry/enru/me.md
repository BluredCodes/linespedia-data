---
title: "Me"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why am I me?
Why am I so ugly?
Why am I so weak?
Why can't I be like normal guys?
Who don't give a shit about anyone.
Who aren't all emotional.
Who don't get depressed.
Why do I have to be me?
I hate me.I felt bad about being a sensitive guy.
