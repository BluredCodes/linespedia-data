Why her?
Why do I like her?
Why do I want to be more than friends?
When we talk, I am happy,
But I still want more.
Why can't I just leave it alone?
Why do I try to complicate it?
I'll ruin it, like I ruin everything.This is an old one I wrote about my confused feelings for one of my female friends, I worked through these though.
