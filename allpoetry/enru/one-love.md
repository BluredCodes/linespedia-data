---
title: "One Love"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "one-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Love only a friend,
With love that is true.
Because in the end,
They are there for you.
Girls come and go.
Friends are forever.
So take life slow.
Fall in love never.
