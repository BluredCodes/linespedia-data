Sitting alone in the darkness,
No one to hear me cry,
Shielding myself from the harshness,
Wishing that I could just die,
Listening to the thoughts in my head.
My sanity is slowly slipping.
Thoughts whispering of me dead.
But onto life I am gripping.
Desperatly clawing at threads of life.
Trying to climb out of the sickness.
Nothing but darkness is what I see.
I now know that I am hopeless.
