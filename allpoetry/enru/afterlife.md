It matters not,
How it begins,
It only matters,
How it ends.

What I want to know,
Is when you die,
Where do you go?
Is it the beginning?
Or is it the end?
Do you keep on living?
Or decay and decompose?

Who is right?
The Christians?
The Hindu?
The Muslims?
The Jews?
The Buddists?
The Wichan?
The Pagans?
How do you choose?

How do you decide?
What to be?
How do you decide?
Who to worship?
How do you decide?
Who owns your soul?

Is there even an afterlife?
Is all my concern for nothing?
I can't make a decision.
So my answer is Atheism.
