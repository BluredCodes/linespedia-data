---
title: "Alone"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "alone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34293-Alone-by-Enru"
---

I'm so confused,
I can't make up my mind,
From girl to girl,
Promising commitment,
Only bringing pain,
I don't mean to,
I don't want to,
Why can't I just back off?
Take some time off,
I think it's because I'm scared,
Scared to be alone.
