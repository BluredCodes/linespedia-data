---
title: "A Question, An Answer"
writer: "Enru"

What is the meaning of life?
A question that is a paradox.
A question that everyone knows the answer to.
A question whose answer is never accepted.
A question that has no answer.
What is the meaning of life?
To live.This is an old one, not that good.
