---
title: "My World"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "my-world"

---

I feel so cold, 
But somehow it feels right,
Like my skin is what is erasing the light,
Like I'm supposed to be in this darkness,
Cold and alone, my soul forever tarnished,
Nothing seems to work out,
It all falls apart,
Everytime I pass the line,
I realise I'm back at start.

Why do I feel like this?
Going through my entire life,
Feeling the torture of the strife,
That exists between my thoughts,
Confusing, winding, tied into knots,
My head spins from all the pain,
How can I get my mind uncurled?
There is no answer.
This is my world.Welcome to my world.
