When is a crush,                   Going to far? 
When's it an obsession?      When she is everything. 
When the thought that you'll never have her makes you cry. 
When you love her, but she doesn't even know. 
When you think of nothing except her. 
When you can't live without her. 
That is what obsession is. 
That is how life is. 
My obssesion.
My crush.
My love.Dedicated to the person the first version was written for a while back, Lauren.
