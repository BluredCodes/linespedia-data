---
title: "The Meaning"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "the-meaning"
source: "AllPoetry"

A plague on mankind.
A question unanswered.
The meaning of life.
The ultimate joke of the gods.
An everlasting hell.I consider this one the worst one I've ever written.
