---
title: "Difference"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "difference"
source: "AllPoetry"

Show me a point to it all,
Then I'll decide it's worth it.
This life has no point,
It keeps on going with or without you.
Stay behind, it doesn't need you,
Life is independant, you are just a victim,
Of its grasp, living this pointless lie,
Thinking that you can make a difference,
Nothing you can do will ever matter,
All you can do is hold on,
Try to live it comfortably,
Well, I dont want to just hold on,
I'm going to make a difference,
Or else why would I want to live?
