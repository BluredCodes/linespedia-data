---
title: "Back and Forth"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "back-and-forth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34200-Back-and-Forth-by-Enru"

Back and forth,
A lie for this side,
A lie for that one,
Mind can't be made up,
Back and forth,
A reason pro,
A reason con,
What's needed is an answer,
Back and forth,
Yes, no, maybe,
Thoughts in conflict,
Mind out of control,
Back and forth,
Live or die?
Give up or fight?
Break down and cry,
Back and forth.Not my best, but has alot of my emotion in it.
