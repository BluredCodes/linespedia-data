---
title: "Don't Belong"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "dont-belong"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27236-Dont-Belong-by-Enru"
---

Sometimes I feel like I don't belong,
Like me living on this planet is all wrong,
Like nothing I do is what is right,
Like my thoughts are having a constant fight,
Like I am all alone and stranded here,
Like that's not my reflection looking out in the mirror,
Like nobody else thinks the thoughts that I think,
Like I am on a ship that's about to sink,
Like my world is slowly burning away,
Like nothing is right, Like it's going to stay.
