---
title: "My Life Is Mine"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "my-life-is-mine"

Life is so confusing,
What should I do?
It won't dictate me,
Hara-kiri.

The blue devils are here,
There is no escape.
I will not run all day,
Felo-de-se.

He comes for me,
The pale horse fast.
There is no where to hide,
Suicide.
