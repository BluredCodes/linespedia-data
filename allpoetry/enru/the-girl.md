Is it love?
Is it something else?
Maybe it's too early to tell?
But it doesn't matter, it's unreturned,
She loves another,
Who took her heart,
And shattered it like glass,
He who I hate,
Yet I've never met,
He who I hate,
More than anyone else,
Her poetry crys a painful song,
A song where he is the unreachable desire,
My song is the same,
Except I cry for her,
My unreachable desire,
My perfect one,
My tears of pain.There's double meaning in that last line...
