---
title: "My Love For You"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "my-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27239-My-Love-For-You-by-Enru"
---

My love for you will never end,
Even if you leave.
You will always be my best friend,
Losing you would make me grieve.
A helping hand you always lend,
You never seek to deceive.
On you I greatly depend.
A life without you I can't perceive.
My love for you will never bend,
Please say that I'm not naive,
And that a life with me you wish to spend,
Say these words and I will believe.
