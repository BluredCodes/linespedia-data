D on't let yourself love someone,
I  love you will bring only pain,
S omehow it'll end the fun.
T hat emotion will fall like rain,
A nd into madness it will be bent,
N ever will you find a way to be sane.
T o love is to wish to be distant.Check out the first letters of each line.
