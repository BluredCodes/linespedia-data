---
title: "Cold Reality"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "cold-reality"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The words you said made it,
The feelings you sent feed it,
This icy chill that courses through me,
Makes me know there's only one place I want to be,
I long for the warmth of your love,
Where I can be free,
Of life's pressuring grip,
Without you I fell my hands slip,
Leaving me helpless, spiraling, falling,
I reach for your outstretched arm,
But then I wake up and hit the alarm,
Another bad dream, only the chill is still there,
Please wake me up,
I've taken all I can bear.She broke up with me yesterday, she needs time...
As soon as I got off the phone with her, I started feeling cold, and I couldn't get warm no matter how hard I tried.
