---
title: "French Fried Heart"
writer: "Enru"

My young and naive heart,
Oh so naive,
My young potato heart,
Has been fried.

Deep fried until crunchy,
French fried in grease.

Now my heart,
My young and naive heart,
Is hard and brittle,
Quick to break.

What my heart needs,
Is some catsup,
Your catsup to make it soggy,
Soggy and soft,
My young and naive heart,
So pour on your catsup,
Your lovely catsup,
Onto my french fried heart.
