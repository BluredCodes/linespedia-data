---
title: "Too Uptight"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "too-uptight"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hate people,
They're all the same,
Do this, don't do that,
They're all so lame.
Can't take a joke,
They get all mad at it,
Oh no, I've offended you,
Well, I really don't give a shit.
Loosen up, you're too uptight,
Why is everything such a big deal?
You act like I killed your dog,
Don't like it?  That's just how I feel.
People suck.I was really ticked off when I wrote this.
