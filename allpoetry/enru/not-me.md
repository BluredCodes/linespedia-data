---
title: "Not Me"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "not-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34312-Not-Me-by-Enru"
---

Every time I look in the mirror,
I see someone else,
I don't see me.
All I see is what everyone else sees.
I stare deeply into my own face,
But what I see,
Is what you want me to be.
I can't find me.
