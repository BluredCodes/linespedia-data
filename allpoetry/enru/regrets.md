I am happy,
Someone loves me,
And I love her,
So why am I so miserable?
I made a bad choice.
I'm with the wrong one.
I regret my life,
Some many bad choices,
But why do I regret?
It brings only pain,
I shouldn't regret,
I am happy now,
Aren't I?This is an old one I wrote while in an unhappy relationship.
