---
title: "Begone"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "begone"
source: "AllPoetry"

I want to go to sleep,
And never wake up.
I want to close my eyes,
And slip into the darkness.
I want to disappear,
And never come back.
I want to leave this place,
And go to some place better.
I want to end my life,
And live on in memory.Written in my depression.
