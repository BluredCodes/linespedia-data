---
title: "Don't Love"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "dont-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34299-Dont-Love-by-Enru"
---

There is many a way that if you may,
Grow to care a lady fair,
But love is a lie, it'll wither and die.
You may think you love that angel above,
But your heart she'll burn, your love she'll spurn.
So live your life, and forget a wife.This one's not that good.
