---
title: "Rage"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "rage"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20015-Rage-by-Enru"
---

I can feel the rage building up inside,And every time I try,I can't seem to release the hate,While at the same time keeping my mental state.A valve for the pressure,An end to the stressors,I need something to end this feeling of madness,Before I snap.Muscles tensing, teeth grinding,Violent images dancing through my head,Thoughts become reality,When I feel the blood flow red,Throbbing slowly, seeping onto the floor,Realization, I bolt for the door,Running, hiding, time I'm biding.I give into fate.To late.
