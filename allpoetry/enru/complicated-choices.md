title: "Complicated Choices"
writer: "Enru"

I remember when life was easy,
When there weren't any choices.
There were no girls to pick from.
When none of them liked me.
Now I must choose,
Too many choices,
Too hard to decide.
Its all too complicated.
Why can't it be like it was?Two is one too many for me.
