---
title: "I Am"
writer: "Enru"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am everything.
I am nothing.
I am happy.
I am sad.
I am intelligent.
I am ignorant.
I am caring.
I am thoughtless.
I am alive.
I am dead.
I am myself.
I am nobody.
I am.Not my best work.
