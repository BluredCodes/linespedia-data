A Precious Supply
Dwindling

Endangered Species
Men seek it

Priceless and Rare
Much in Demand

Such a simple thing, 
Yet Elusive to Most

The Only Remaining Source
Is within us All

To give it is to
Gain it

The Only Remaining Source
