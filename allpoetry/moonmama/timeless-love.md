---
title: "Timeless Love"
writer: "moonmama"
slug: "timeless-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26473-Timeless-Love-by-moonmama"
---

Along ancient paths
Where rivers flow
With you, my love
I will go

Water, Fire, 
Earth, Air, Sea
To you I shall
Ever faithful be

Our love like nature
Clear and pure
'Tis the edict
And the cure

Along ruins of fortress
One thing stays
My love for you
Eternal days
