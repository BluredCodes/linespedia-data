Everything I know of bitterness
Tastes like you
Set me free
You hold onto me
Like a posession, once valuable
Now you don't know what to do with me
Keep me in a box 
Throw me away?
It isn't a casual choice
Set me free
I don't love you
You don't love me
Surrender to the unknown
That is the key
Set me freeJust ramblings of my mind...I know it needs work...
