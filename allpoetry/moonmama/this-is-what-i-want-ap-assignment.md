I Want

A world without pain
A world without hate
A life of Nirvana
Not controlled by fate

Happiness, serenity
Freedom and peace
Problems and violence
All to cease

Tolerance, Harmony
Friendship and Love
And Divine guidance 
From the Spirits AboveThis is the WORST poem I have EVER written...I was not a bit inspired...it was an assignment, and I suppose I don't work well that way.  :o(  Be gentle.  But be honest.
