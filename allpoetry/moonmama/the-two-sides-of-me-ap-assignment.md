We all speak of Dark and Light
Of course, I posess these sides
Yet as I ponder my Duality
I think of something more

By day, I am a mommy
Days filled with toys
Diapers, sippy cups
Naps and kisses on scraped knees

By night, I am a lover
Robes of satin and silk
Soft jazz replaces "Barney Sings"
Kisses not on knees...
Well...sometimes on knees...

The strong love I feel for my child
Gives way to the intense desire I feel for you
Mother
Lover

The Two Sides of Me
