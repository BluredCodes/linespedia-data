I am a Parody
of Myself
Pretending to be
The Me you've come to know

Happy, content with my lot
Never asking
Always willing
Pleasing everyone
Except, most often, 
Myself.

I am an Actor
Entertaining the discerning crowd,
Fooling the beast audiences
Even, sometimes, 
Myself.
