It's been ten years
So why do I care?
Why do I still think of you?
Years gone by
We have our own lives
Each of us married
With kids...

But somedays, my thoughts turn to 
Summer rain and kisses
Car rides and beer
Def Leppard on the tape deck
and wind in our hair

Days were so carefree then
But summer turned to fall
I went to college
You stayed home
And slowly, we drifted apart

But sometimes, when nights are quiet
I can remember
And I sigh....
