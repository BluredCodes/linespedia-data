What visions have I
Velvet tipped sword
Distant coloured shores
I can barely discern
While the river runs
I stand still
Through the looking glass
Darkly
Saw a ghost ship
When it came to harbour
It was a castle of clowns

What visions have I
Stars flicker and they fade
Sands of time run faster than me
I can't catch them
You are there in a forest glen
When I catch you
You are the hart which cannot be tamed.

What visions have I
Crystal coloured sea
Mermaid swimming
Singing silently breaking glasses
In the panes of a windowless church
I can't hear her
Yet I know 
She singsI wrote this one about three years ago just after waking from the most bizarre dream.  It's very disjointed.  By the way, I did mean to spell HART that way, because a hart is a male deer.
