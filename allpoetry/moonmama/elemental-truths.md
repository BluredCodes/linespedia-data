title: "Elemental Truths"

The Earth will hold me up
The Air will let me breathe
The Fire will warm me
The Water will wash me

This I can believe

When I can't trust in you

I can remember
These Truths
Listen to my heartbeat
Feel my pulse
Trust the Earth beneath my Feet
Inhale the Air I breathe
Feel the Fire on my own skin
Cleanse myself in the Fountain

Basic, eternal truths
I can trust
