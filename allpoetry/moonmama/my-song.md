---
title: "My Song"
writer: "moonmama"

The rhythm of my life
Is a song I like
I wrote the verses
And live the chorus
In the time and meter of my days
I live the melody
Of the love around me

Envelopes me 
Harmonizes with you
And all the notes we play
Together
Make one beautiful refrain
