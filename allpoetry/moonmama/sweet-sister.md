Sweet Sister

I know why you cry
Know that when you say "Its okay"
That you're dying inside

I know that your past
Delivers your pain
Know that when you give too much
Satisfaction you gain

I know that you say
You're doing alright
Know that all your life
You've had to fight

I know that Life
Grew you up way too fast
Know that you think true love
Can't really last

But do you know
That I love you so?
Do you know 
That you help me grow?
We share a common bond
Much bigger than blood
We share a bond of Womanhood
And I want you to know, 
That if I could

I'd bear all your pain.


I love you Sis.I really don't care about stars.  I just wanted to tell my Beautiful Sister how much I love her.  She's had a hard life...I know I need to revise this poem, but it just came out of my head today.
