title: "Hollywood Loves Halloween"

Hollywood Loves HalloweenOh, the freaks come out at nightEvery night in Holly weirdCome out the way they really areMen dressing as womenWomen dresses as menMaking believe it all is pretendHollywood loves HalloweenParties are planned for a whole yearThanksgiving and Xmas just ain’t as dearAs the Hallowed day they so revereGhosts and goblins rule the dayCostumes and makeup as macabre as can beThe uglier the better is what gives them gleeHollyweird loves HalloweenJust like we pretend Hollywood is the place to beAs though Hollywood is city we like visitors to seeFilled with the plastic, make believe and unrealDirty and filthy streets filled with homeless and the illHollywood pretending it’s a great place to visitUntil you get here and find out Hollywood isn’tHollyweird loves HalloweenHere the freaks come out night and dayThe unholy rule and reign as heads of studios, Writers, corporate bosses and people who write out your paycheck or rent you videosSucking your blood like the Vampires they areMaking you the walking dead while they profit and call you a star.Hollywood loves HalloweenHollywood and Halloween go togetherLike birds of a feather, the badder the betterJust one day with the world catching up to the wayHollywood lives and loves to play on this dayTo honor the devil, the undead, the untrueHollyweird needs it loves it…BOO Hollywood loves Halloween
