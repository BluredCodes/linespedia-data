AutumnComelay down beside meon my mattress of autumn leavesyellows, oranges, purples, green feel them spongy, crunchy, springyautumn leaves, lay, lay downLook see up with me  on clouds for dreaming, pillowshorses, dragons, flowers, chariotsride themdo turns, flips, swirls in, outwispy clouds, see, see upMattresses of autumn leavespillows of billowy cloudsjust little thingsgiven to man freeman has no timethese things to see fiddle- dee-dee
