---
title: "Goodbye my long lost love"
writer: "Richard Davis"
slug: "goodbye-my-long-lost-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

The time was not right...
I turned away lonely, crying
into the cold dark night.

We talked, that next year...
thinking of our love
our paths unclear.

The years now gone by...
our paths again cross
and we both cry.

We hold each other tight...
we talk, we kiss
what will become this night.

time then passes...
too fast too free 
We realize our love is reality.

We hug, we kiss each other goodbye...
the pain obvious
As we leave we cry.
