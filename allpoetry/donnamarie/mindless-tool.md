how could you do that to me?
how could you ditch me there?
you came with me,
you slept with her.
i was forgotten and ignored,
cause she's a stupid whore.
she's a vacuum cleaner,
and you're a mindless tool!

one minute you're with me,
being intimate and hard;
then you leave for five minutes
to do the same with her.
you'll grab anything with tits,
finger anything that moves.
but now i know why;
cause you're a mindless tool!

you're so easy to manipulate;
i control you by your dick.
you think with the wrong head,
your sckull is just too thick.
you try to use your heart,
but i know you're full of bull.
you wonder why you get hurt.
don't you know why?
you're a mindless tool!this is about three different guys that i made the mistake of getting somewhat involved with. the rest is self-explanatory...
