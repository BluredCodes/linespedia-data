title: "Stop"

I'm paralyzed down here.My chin resting on my knees.the cold wood floorunderneath my naked feet.As the tremors beginthe dangerous impulses engulf mewith every quivering, pathetic sobas the river of tearsthreatens to drown me.The quiet is so enticing;just a sweet dream away,and all the pain will dissipate.IT WILL STOP!!!
