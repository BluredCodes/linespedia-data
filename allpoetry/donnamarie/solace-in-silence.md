you tried and you liedtoo fastnow too slowwalls and fallsmisdeeds undone -redone plus oneheart in your throatmine in my stomachethe gap has spreadwhat was there - now deadmistakes will hauntfill the atticof my mindand the closet of your heartno end in sightwords filled with spitebitterness now swallowedtaste left behindmy confusionbecause of your delusionjust let it flowas the blood from my heartwhat might becould beif let beshould it be?do i want what i now regret?laugh in astonishmentcry or scream?blood or tears?solace in silence.the back story to this is kind of stupid.  i lost my voice for 4 days while my ex attempted to win me over again.  i lost my voice. told you it was stupid. ;->
