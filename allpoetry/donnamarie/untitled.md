wave your flags,march in the streets.at the bomb's first strikeyou'll cry for retreat.don't give any thought,just shoot to kill.it's for everyone's rightsbut not our free will.blindly we followand blindly they lead.we complimenttheir detriment;the 'Peaceful Nation'demands obliterationfor the nirvana it seeks.the Kingdom is safe,while the subjects die.no more depraveour King held high.destroy the worldthat does not agree.we'll kill them allto the nth degree.
