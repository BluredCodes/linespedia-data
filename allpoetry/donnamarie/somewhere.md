Somewhere
the heat’s gone broken.

Somewhere
a girl is screaming.

Somewhere
a noose hangs full.

Somewhere
there’s a cardboard mansion.

Somewhere
she has no shoes.

Somewhere
they’re lost and the engine’s died.

Somewhere
the belt comes off.

Somewhere
cries of “No!” go unheard.

Somewhere
the bottle is empty.

Somewhere
the ‘little match girl’ freezes.

Somewhere 
his father runs away.

Somewhere
a war is viewed through the banister.

Somewhere
she lights up and takes a drink alone.

Somewhere 
she is late.

Somewhere
they want to be someone else.
