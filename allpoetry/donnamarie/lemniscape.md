lemniscapeis where i createthe songs i singand this love i bringit distracts the othersas the teacher hoversmy secrets i hidefor they cause a divide.the reason that this is funny is because i wrote it during my calculus class today, which is where i got that cool word lemniscape.  it's a graph whose equation we had to differentiate.  anyway, i just wrote this for something to do, and i always have to hide my poems from my teachers since i always write instead of paying attention.  i realize, though, that this could be a pretty deep poem, if i think about it.
