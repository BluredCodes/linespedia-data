My beautiful atheist angel.
I don't know how 
you hide your wings
behind your eyes of hazel.

You carry on your lips,
as you speak with the tongue of a poet.
Your charming, adoring smile,
and your warm, gentle kiss.

Pictures of you inspire me to cry,
to our Godless Heaven
that our love
was not a lie.
