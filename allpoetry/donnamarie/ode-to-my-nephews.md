title: "ode to my nephews"
writer: "donnamarie"

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
RUN ALL DAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
PLAY, PLAY, PLAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
DON'T DO AS THEY SAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
PLAY, PLAY, PLAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
DO NOT PRAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
PLAY, PLAY,PLAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
SEIZE THE DAY!

MY FUNNY LITTLE MONKEYS,
PLAY, PLAY, PLAY!oh, i love those monkeys!  they are wonders unto themselves.  my sister says DJ got his brains from antie-donna.  oh, yes.  he did.  conniving, sneaky little monkey! oh, and the other one is just absolutely bonkers, crazy, and unstopable... zacky cooties!  he he he

i love those kids to death.  the love of children is so pure and unselfish.  they astound me everytime i see them.  :)
