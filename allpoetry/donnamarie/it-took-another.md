---
title: "it took another"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "it-took-another"

It took another
to make me appreciate your love.
It took another
for me to mourn your kiss.
It took another
for me to comprehend the cliche.
It took another
to find my true heart.
It took another
to stop me from thinking with my head.
It took another
for me to know you.
It took another
for my flame to burn brighter.
It took another
for me to dread the meaning of your words.
It took another
to realize the failure we've suffered.
It took another
for me to deny my denial.
It took another
to open my eyes.
It took another
to open my heart to you.
It took another
for me to miss you since you've gone.
