I’m sorry I’m a bitch.
I’m sorry I’m bitter.
I’m sorry my emotions are validated.
I’m sorry I’m scared.
I’m sorry I’m scarred.
I’m sorry I’ve lost touch.
I’m sorry I’m grieving.
I’m sorry I cry.
I’m sorry my friends are wrong.
I’m sorry I piss everyone off.
I’m sorry I only cause our family pain.
I’m sorry I have to be perfect.
I’m sorry I’m not perfect.
I’m sorry I want to live.
I’m sorry I want to sing.
I’m sorry I want to love.
I’m sorry I want to feel safe.

I’m sorry that I’m not.
