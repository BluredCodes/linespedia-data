i made your red nose
from a triangle of points.
your smile is bright 
and brings a grin to my face.
should I pierce?
it’s the best that I’ve drawn.
your eyebrow looks plucked,
guess I should fix it.
perfecting you 
keeps me awake
and your face 
keeps me company.just the ramblings of a girl who wishes to push away her thoughts and wories for 5 minutes ;)
