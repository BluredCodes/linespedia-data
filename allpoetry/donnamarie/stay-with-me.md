---
title: "Stay With Me"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "stay-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6999-Stay-With-Me-by-donnamarie"

You see the beauty in me.
What does this mean?
You see the strength in me.
What does this mean?
You see the kindness in me.
What does this mean?

You've found the weakness in me.
You know what I mean.
You've found the love in me.
Now where will this lead?
You've fallen in love with me;
I want to believe.
You're all I've got in me.
Please say you won't leave.
