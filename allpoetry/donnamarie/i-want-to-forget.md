---
title: "i want to forget"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "i-want-to-forget"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7379-i-want-to-forget-by-donnamarie"
---

strip the memories
from the shelf.
burn the pages 
of my mind.

shred the notes
you wrote me.
crumple the promises
discovered to be empty.

stomp out the flame 
i burned for you.
crush the heart 
that bled for you.
