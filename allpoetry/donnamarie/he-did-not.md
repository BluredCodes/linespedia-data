---
title: "He Did Not"

He did not see me
when he opened his eyes.
He did not call me
when he spoke.
He did not hear me
when I said.
He did not notice
when I cried.
He did not hesitate
when he lied.
So, as i reached out,
he turned away.
i write this 
in reproach,
in regret.wrote this when i was bored in philosophy class.  it's about a boy back home.
