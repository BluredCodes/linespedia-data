---
title: "who am i?"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "who-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6491-who-am-i--by-donnamarie"
---

i am what people perceive me to be.
if all you perceive me to be
is your own ideals,
and not mine;
you lock me up inside a cage.
because i am what you perceive me to be,
i will never be free,
unless you open up your minds.
but i am not what you perceive me to be.
if i were, 
my spirit would perish
and my soul would die.
i am what i am.
you nor anyone else can ever change me,
for what i am is independent and strong.
i will not be persuaded as easily as others;
i may not be persuaded at all.
for i know who i am.
and i know my convictions,
and i will not ignore them
or throw them away for anyone.
that is who i am,
not what i am.
what i am
is human.
