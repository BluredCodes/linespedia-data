The princess.The gifted.The strong.The quiet.I am weak.I am young.I am hopeful.I am hopeless.The disciplined.The responsible.The cute.The smart.I am timid.I am learned.I am naive.I am trained.i am sure that i will have more to add to this as the days go by.  this should be self-explanatory and i think a number of people should be able to relate.
