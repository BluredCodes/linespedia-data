title: "home"

I'm not revealing 
justification,
only motives 
and themes:

Theatre de Vampyres:
where we acted
and played.

every day was night.
he never let the sun rise.

he was nocturnal,
yet tortured us by daylight.

the bottles were 
under the backseat.

momma didn't know,
but i did.

the abuse was mental,
but don't doubt the blood.

the tears were worse.
i've cried blood-salty tears.

they did not stain the pillow,
but they did stain the floor:
where i'd lay my head
on the wood;
knees met chin,
searching to comfort 
one another,
stop each other
from quivering.father, where art thou?  hopefully in hell...
