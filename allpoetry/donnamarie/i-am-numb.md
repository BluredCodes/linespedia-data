---
title: "I am Numb"

She makes me numb

as her nails 

trail down my 

naked back.

She's unleashed 

my blood

and it trickles 

down my neck.

I see others

burst into flames

around me.

Her mind 

entrances me.

I don't want to 

follow and obey,

but I fear 

for my life.



Paralysis:

mind and body.



hallucinations

Reality



Such a fine line

now.



All i react to

are her claws 

on my back.



Our blood mingles.



I am numb.
