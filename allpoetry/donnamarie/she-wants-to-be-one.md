---
title: "she wants to be one"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "she-wants-to-be-one"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22551-she-wants-to-be-one-by-donnamarie"
---

assign the role
for this girl.
assigne her role
in this world.
makes her one
forces her many
loses herself
but pleases the others
cheats herself because
she plays by their rules.
committing no sins,
she'll never be happy.
they mold her and
fashion her to them.

she is many.
she wants to be one.

elusive are her dreams,
but theirs are
close at hand.
she's lived their lives,
now she wants to begin hers.
fulfillment for them;
emptiness is hers.
saved by her enemy.
conformity equals acceptance.
misplaced by their hand.
governed by their word.
she strives for herself
and yearns for a soul.

she is many.
she wants to be one.
