---
title: "broken and bloody"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "broken-and-bloody"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50638-broken-and-bloody-by-donnamarie"
---

i bled like a pig.
i cried like a baby -
   inside.

what was supposed to be
sweet and innocent,
tore and broke.

the confusion
the heat
the anxiety
the fear

the confusion and heat.

i could feel it rip.
i could feel it tear.

i couldn't try one more time.
i was tired and beaten.

sore to the touch:

i wanted to save him the guilt.
but my silence was misinterpreted.

the guilt
and my body
swelled.

the love is there.
so is the pain.

the confusion
the heat
the anxiety
the fear

the confusion and heat.

i hear the ocean
in the distance.
