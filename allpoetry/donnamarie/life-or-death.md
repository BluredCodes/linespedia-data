---
title: "life or death?"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "life-or-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

You convinced yourself
he loved you
to clear your sinful soul
of your blasphemous deeds
But now you are free.
Your conscience had left you -
for better or worse
because you used and abused her,
broke her in two.
Your split personality
was forced to choose:
good or evil?
mutiny or crusade?
fight or die?
It's death you chose.
Too weak to live.
Kill yourself
a slow and lonely death.
Your conscience has left you.
She couldn't bear to watch...
Her presence was futile.
You were determined
to self-destruct.
