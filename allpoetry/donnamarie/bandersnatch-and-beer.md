title: "Bandersnatch and Beer"

With the Assassins I hope to unite.
With them I shall fight
closed-mindedness (is that a word?)
and prejudice.
With our mad poetry skills
we shall fight the social ills,
which plague some minds;
their imagination it binds.

(the fun part)

But when we’re plagued with writer’s block,
and all we do is watch the clock,
together we’ll get drunk on beer
and when our heads have finally cleared,
“BANDERSNATCH!” is what we’ll cheer.sorry it took so long... extenuating circumstances held me back from my true destiny...
