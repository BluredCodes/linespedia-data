I saw his face
   - sunken and drawn.
I saw the machines
   - cold and silent.
I saw the bones
   - frail and hollow.
       (like his eyes)

I saw him quiver.
I saw him glare.
I saw him hate.

I stared
   - numb and alone
   - alone and afraid
   - afraid of freedom
   - afraid of reality

He shot up
and grabbed my 
weak and shivering arm
   -like the last time I saw him
      8 months ago.

A last attempt for control.

A last struggle to kill.

        
        His death 
        or resurrection?
        Which nightmare
        shall I live?he will always haunt me - dead or alive...
