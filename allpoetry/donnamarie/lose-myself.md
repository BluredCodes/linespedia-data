---
title: "lose myself"
writer: "donnamarie"

i'll make it,i know i will.but will i lose myselfand all that i knowalong the journey?will i single-handedlydestroy all that i've built?will i break down;become what they want,become who they are?do i have the strength to stay who i am?can i escape conformity?i don't want to forsake my individuality.all that i've got,all that i can trustis in me.but i feari shall lose myselfin my struggle to achieve consummation.
