---
title: "torrid beauty"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "torrid-beauty"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22555-torrid-beauty-by-donnamarie"
---

Why do i always want
what i can't have?

I always find
far to be better 
than near.

I struggle 
to reach
for it and them.

I don't think
the grass is necessarily greener,
just a little shorter
and a little softer.

I always want
the farthest from
my reality.

Is that it?
Is that the answer?

I escape reality
through those outside.
The bond is formed
and thought about later.

Are these bonds
tearing away at my heart?
Or do they only force me to love?

Thses outsiders
appear better
than those near.

It is a cruel game.

They leave,
fight wars,
cross oceans,
and leave this earth.

Some close off;
poisons steal others away.
Or they are burned by their own hand.

It is a shame that I must love them,
and all of their torrid beauty.
