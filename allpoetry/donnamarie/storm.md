The rain on my skinand the lightning in my eyesClear the troubles from my weary head.The booming of the thunder,Nature's heart beat,is so soothing to my ears.It entices my senses.The scent fills me anew.A refreshing calmengulfs my being.It feels as thougha new beginningencompasses the worldas a whole.i know 'weary head' kinda sounds like a cliche, but it just fit the moment.  this is basically me expressing my love for living in the country and camping out during a rainstorm.  it's not really that mystical; it's just my sad attempt at capturing a moment.  i love thunderstorms, but then again, i'm a dork...  ;)
