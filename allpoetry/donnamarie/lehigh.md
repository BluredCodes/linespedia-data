Going out tonite,
And I’m gonna feel alrite.
Going out tonite,
And I’m gonna get a bite.
Going out tonite,
And gettin’ drunk I mite.

Meeting pretty boys…
Hey, get into the funk!
Meeting pretty boys…
Maybe he’ll be a punk (rocker)! 
Meeting pretty boys…
I hope he’s not a hunk (prettier than I am).

I’m going on a date.
Gonna make myself pretty.
I’m going on a date.
Startin’ to feel giddy.
I’m going on a date.
This is the end of my diddy.

      Thank you 
        &	
      Good nite!Lehigh is the name of another college on the other side of town.
