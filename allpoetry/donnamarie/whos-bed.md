it reads like a story
i once wrote.
it seems like ages ago,
yet it's happening now.
it's like a scene out of 
"A Streetcar Named Desire".

who's bed will i sleep in tonight?
the plot twists and turns.
the characters are the same
only their namesakes have changed.
not for their protection,
but for mine.


the palm trees
are out of hollywood,
the soundtrack is from
seattle and jersey.
the voice over the radio
sounds loud and clear.
it is static.
he says these things,
these gorey things.
they are meant for me.
and he calls my name.

who's bed will i sleep in tonight?
the plot twists and turns.
the characters are the same,
only their namesakes have changed.
not for their protection,
but for mine.

the overcast became grey
and almost dreary.
if it weren't for 
that damned break in the clouds.
it reads like a story
i once wrote.
it seems like ages ago,
yet it's happening now.
it's like a scene out of 
"A Streetcar Named Desire".i must add more later
