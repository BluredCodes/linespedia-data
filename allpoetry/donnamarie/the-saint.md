She's got a tumor 
in her head.
Yes, 
that's what I said.
No rhyme was 
intenDED.

____________________________________

She is the only
saint I've known.
I've met angels
and deities.
Never a saint.

Her love is 
so pure.
She is 
a lily-scented
fairy,
here for 
my protection.
Although
her view of me
gets shady,
her love 
always shines.

______________________________________

They don't know 
what the tumor is.
She just prays
she sees the sun
in the east.

_______________________________________

When I am alone 
and forgotten,
she emerges
from the shadows
of our tree.
She places her 
child-holding hands
on my shoulder.
She makes sure 
I'm not forgotten
and alone.

She hides
in our tree.

She is 
my Saint.ode to my grandmother...
