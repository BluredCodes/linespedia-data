if i was not alone,why did they ignore the note?scotch tape forcing itto cling onto my door.it was still wetafter i bathed itwith my tears.i waited for three eternities,hoping they couldcare enough to knock;that they wouldbreak through the barrier and knock the pillsfrom my trembling hand.DO NOT BE ALARMED!!!  this is old news.  years old.  everyone involved is okay.  that is my disclaimer.  thank you.
