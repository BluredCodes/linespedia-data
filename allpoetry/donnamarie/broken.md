---
title: "broken"
writer: "donnamarie"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28398-broken-by-donnamarie"
---

i'm broken.
he's won.
i don't know how to love.

i'm broken.
he's won.
i'm still a crying dove.

i'm broken.
he's won.
all i know are push and shove.

i'm broken.
he's won.
i'll never begin to trust.

i'm broken.
he's won.
i turn my love to lust.

i'm broken.
he's won.
shut people out i must.

i'm broken.
he's won.
i'll never learn to cry.

i'm broken.
he's won.
all i believe are lies.

i'm broken.
he's won.
it will never be over,
    even when he dies.
