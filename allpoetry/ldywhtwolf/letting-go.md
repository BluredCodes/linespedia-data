LETTING YOU GO

Letting you go,
Is harder than I thought,
Letting you go,
Is ripping me apart.

But now you want to go,
Nothing left for me to do.
I hope you'll find happiness,
and happiness finds you.

You gave me your heart,
Now you've taken it away.
It was never mine to own,
and now I'm all alone.

I know this is best for you,
It's too painful for me to bare,
My heart is broken and tattered,
I know now you don't care.

Still I dream you will return,
And forever we will last,
But I know deep down inside,
Its time for letting go atlast.
..........LdyWhtWolf
