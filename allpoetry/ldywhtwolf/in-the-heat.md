"In The Heat Of The Night" 


In the heat of the night,
I awaken alone. 
Reaching out across my bed,
To find that noone is there.

In the heat of the night,
I long for your love. 
To take away my restlessness,
To give me peace of mind.

In the heat of the night,
I long to be in your arms.
To hold me so very close.
To make love to me , all night long.

In the heat fo the night,
We could talk for hours,
Or even say nothing at all.
I just need you here.

In the heat of the night, 
We could look at the stars,
Together from the yard swing,
Hand in hand, we'd share.

In the heat of the night,
Our passions run high.
Our desires to run freely, 
Giving ourselves one to another.
........written by LdyWhtWolf
