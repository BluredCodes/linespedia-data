For years confusion had been there.
Always deep inside, not to share.

So I left one comfortable place,
In search for another, off in a distant place.

I found one from in the past,
Only to find it didn't last.

Back to the safe zone I returned.
Only to end up again burned.

Then I take on another route,
Seeking happiness that I could shout.

One that would put an end to strife.
Just wanting a simple life.

Trying to start over again with anew.
Hoping for peace, for more than a few.

Working, living, keeping up the pace.
Not knowing if I'll ever win this race.

Or if this despair is way too real? 
Then where would I go from here?
