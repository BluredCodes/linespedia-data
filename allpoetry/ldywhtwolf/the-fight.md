THE FIGHT
 .............written by LdyWhtWolf 

It hurts when we fight.
Cuts right to the core.
Shoots straight thru the heart,
Another slamming of a door.

Causing all walls to come up, 
Taking forever to get back down.
The pain buries itself inside.
Here goes another roun'.

Words are spoken way too harsh.
Pasts are thrown at every curve.
Two people slicing hearts into.
Both getting what they deserve.

Each one in their seperate corners,
Thier minds in deep despair.
Not knowing just what to do.
Or how to even repair.

They let time pass right on by.
Never making it right, just defend.
Moving on to the another day, 
Another beginning to the end.
