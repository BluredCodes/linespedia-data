Sometimes in this life, It seems to not be fair. For some it  comes easy,For others, they don't care.Sometimes in this life, There's a lot of despair. Loneliness seems to abound.And so we sit and we stare.Sometimes in this life, We are forced to play,The cards we are dealt.Making the choice to stay. Sometimes in this life,We often want more, If for no other reason,but to even the score. Sometimes in this life,We need to feel deep inside,The heated passions we desire,Not wanting to within hide.Sometimes in this life, We seek what is not real.To fulfill our deepest desires, Settling for the cyber ordeal.Sometimes in this life, Two people can truly share,Those intimate moments missed,And show one another they care.
