"Walk With Me" 


Come walk with me,
Down this mountainous path.
As we observe the beauty of it all,
Sharing our lifes' stories with each other.

Come walk with me,
Down the long roads ahead.
As we share in each others triumphs,
In each others hurt and despairs.

Come walk with me, 
Down through the valleys low.
Letting our souls become entwined.
Giving ourselves to one another.

Come walk with me.
Down the beaches ahead.
Let our spirits become one.
Where only love can grow.

.........written by LdyWhtWolf
