How do I finally let go?
When you is all I know!
Deep down the pain is strong.
I know that we are all wrong.

To let go of my dreams,
Knowing nothing is real, its seems.
A silence of a dove's wing.
Love birds no longer sing.

Trying to hang on a bit longer.
When all is done,I'll be stronger.
Viewing the future seems so dim.
Somehow, I've got to let go of him.

What once was laughter,
Is now just pain, and there after.
Thinking about all the time we've spent,
Of building a life together, it was meant.

No words can fix what is not right.
Slowly our world moves out of site.
Doing what I know I should.
Finally letting go, if only I could.
