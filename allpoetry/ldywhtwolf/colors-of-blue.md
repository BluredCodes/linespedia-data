Colors Of Blue
........by LdyWhtWolf 


There is many shades of blue.
Blue like a summer sky.

A navy blue putting one deep in thought.
Soulsearching is what is sought.

And of course the baby blue,
Making one think of life anew.

Finaly the deepest blue inside,
Falling to our knees, we cried.

Its that deep blue,
Thats so hard to bare.

Its then I turn to you,
With my heart I share.
