I CAN'T TAKE IT ANYMORE.

YOUR KILLING ME INSIDE.

MY LAUGHTER IS GONE.

I NEED TO RUN, RUN FAST TO HIDE.

AS YOU LAUGH IN MY FACE,

TEARS ARE SHED ALL THE DAYS THRU.

ALL SMILES VANISH WITHOUT A TRACE.

YOU'VE DESTROYED ME, WITH NO GRACE.

WITH YOUR WICKED GAMES YOU PLAY.

YOUR MENTAL ABUSE HAS WORN ME DOWN.

ITS GOT TO END, END TODAY.

I CAN'T TAKE YOUR SO-CALLED LOVE ANYMORE.

YOU'VE BROKEN MY SPIRIT, DRAINED MY WILL.

THE KNOT AT THE END OF THE ROPE IS UNTIEING.

THE FLAMES ARE BURNING QUICKLY OUT!

KNOWING ALL THIS, YET YOU STILL!

GOD HELP ME HOLD ON, BEFORE I TAKE THIS PILL.

........written by LdyWhtWolf
