WHAT DOES IT TAKE?What does it take, to believe?What does it take receive?To believe in an ever-lasting love,To believe in the pureness of a dove. What does it take, to have faith?what does it take, to not feel true hate?To search for our everlasting soul mate.To search for our true destiny, our fate.What does it take, to find happiness?What does it take, to feel compassion?To reach way deep inside, without a trace.To reach that uncompromising place.What does it take?
