A  Cold Dark Colour


                   Oh poverty bringeth pain,
                   and thou arth such a drain!

                   A rock in my soul
                   an impediment to every goal

                   Yea poverty thou dresses
                   in the clothing of bain
                   and enjoyeth thy dark colours
                   of clouds and rain
