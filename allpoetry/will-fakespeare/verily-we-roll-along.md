Verily (we roll along!)



                   Oh thou most foolish of fowl
                   the plot doth thicken
                   but forsooth and alas
                   thou remaineth a chicken

                   Yea verily as cock crows
                   at break of day
                   imbeciles shall strut
                   their wanton ignorance
                   without slighest knowledge
                   of what they say

                   Forgive me my transgressions
                   if it casts thee in a flap
                   but as a trees blood in wintertime
                   thou doth appear one sluggish sap!
