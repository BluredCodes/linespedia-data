Spare Change Shakespeare




                   Oh for "Spare Change", I beg unto thee!
                   cans't thou part with one thin farthing
                   for the unfortunate likes of me?

                   and dear patron rest assured
                   that the gold that thou leavest me
                   will be spent on sustenance! and will
                   never giveth cause to grieveth thee!

                   Yea I beseech thee kind patron!
                   woudst thou offer thy humble servant
                   the meagre quantity of a buck?
                   and if thou answereth nay!
                   still I'll cheer thee on thy way!

                   (though I may wish thee
                   the most dastardly of luck!)
