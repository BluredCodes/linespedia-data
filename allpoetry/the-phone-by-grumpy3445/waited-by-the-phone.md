She waited by the phone
through endless, endless hours
she waited day and night
for just a word from him she loved
occasionally, a call would come
a quick "I love you"
then again she waits, and waits
and waits, and waits
then one day, a letter came
"I fear this last battle
will be the last for me
in this brief letter
I want to say how happy you made me
for the brief time I was in your arms
I will be able to sleep forever with a smile"
then the phone rang, and she knew
instantly he was gone
she whispered through her tears
"You made me happy too"I don't remember the reason I wrote this, must have been a soldier that died in Afghanistan in December.
