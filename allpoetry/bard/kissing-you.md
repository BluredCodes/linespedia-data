---
title: "Kissing you"
writer: "Bard"
slug: "kissing-you"

Curiosly our tongues explore
and passing lips we beg for more

Uneasy hands that dare not move
lest the other not approve

Now hesitantly start to search
and move from hips' safety perch

Hands trace the waist, the back and side
now too daring they subside

Kissing harder though at rest
stealing breath from passioned breast

Bold again hands stumble up
and smooth back down to hips they cup

Though dip below and waistline brush
to further go would be to rush

Happy still with kissing lips
ignoring feeling twixt the hips

Hands slip back up, now down to hips
focus now again the lips

Curiosly our tongues explore
and passing lips we beg for moreThis is easy!
