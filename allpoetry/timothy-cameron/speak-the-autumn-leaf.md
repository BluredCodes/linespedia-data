No wintery wind, 
my hands yet thin, 
do mold a festive glow.

Within the lamp of life, 
there is no strife, 
my Autumn must now show.

Tho' summer will come, 
from a fire so young, 
you know 'cause you're not dumb.

My breath exhausts for you, 
spills out like morning dew, 
it's spring, my love is true.
