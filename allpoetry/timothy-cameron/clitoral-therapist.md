A goal hidden is 
Forbidden only 
To those impatient patients of 
Imperfection 

True desire sees the unseen 
Feels its essence long before 
Tendering its unfolding 
Luxuriousness 

Traversing eternity, figure eights 
Stars kiss her palms gently 
Imploring more riches to 
Exploring 

Eyelash tremors gracefully 
Glance across moistened trails 
Barely glowing twin moons echo 
Mysteriousness 

Mountainous topography brings rain 
From far beneath the terrain 
Please let earth know my pressure 
I’m sacredly prayin’ 

A discovery of sudden subtlety 
Sensitive human seismograph 
Tastes the tender pain 
Briefly 

Pursue the retreat…over 
In addition, around now forward again 
Please release the rumbling 
Entourage 

I come as a seeker of truth 
Undeniable relaxation pursues 
The treasured protectorates 
Throne 

Face the throne!  Leave it 
Spotless and clean 
Rinse it in steam 
Serene
