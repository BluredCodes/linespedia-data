Patriotism and blood...

Thriving beyond the veil of tears,
Requires laws unfettered by living
Within the howls of the enemies estate
Nor caring of a brothers’ demise
Outside the walls of reasoned creed

Fly at the enemy by running from him
And exactly when he screams gleeful turpitudes
At his expected bloody plight to end your family’s light
Turn suddenly upon your flight, and 
Execute your preparation with silent dispatch
Eye to eye, law to law

This is true love, to give your all for law
Love of law and principal above all
Love the Lord your God with all your might
Your entire mind, and all your soul
And love your enemies
Lawfully and mercifully, when asked

For even in death so lived to the end
Will others know we cared for them?
That aint the point!
Even beyond love of our own life
This is true friendship
This is true life
To die thus, is to truly live!

God Bless America
