Your gun-fighter is quiet.

Vultures skirmish over his carcass.

A tear fell from his eye,

After he died.



He didn’t cry once in this life.

Not for me.

Not even for you.

At least God gave him a soul.



Too bad he’s in SheolSheol is a place away from God's presence, a place of spiritual death.
