Soulful apparitions flow
From lessons learned from the bow.
Unfortunately with no gain to remain
Of blackened, parched music sheets...
Only "suspicious" students resist resisting.

Violent chambers stubbornly echo
Against is-been founders who flounder.
Successors meet with tumult
As convert teachers steal and tear
Tears from venerable notes.

Quieting paces create anguish,
Full melodies pound pulse within, within,
Heaven's tick-tock trap-lock solitude.
Snare upon the thieves like gasped retributive air
Piped from "Rebellious" wind instruments.

Avenues ring of external imperatives,
As ten thousand chest-pounders thump away
Their inward howls until rhythmic 
Reigning Past Masters sizzzzzzzle upon pedestals.
Nonwithstanding frigidity, youthful sacrificial coals

Melt 
Away
the 
mute 
discoveries.How do you feel?
