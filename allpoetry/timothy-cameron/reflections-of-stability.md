Kneeling at ponds' edge, 
Peering into tranquil water. 
Reflections twist when kissed, 
By falling tears.

Corner of my eye

A flower is mirrored, too, and 
Its lovely appearance, 
Marred briefly, refreshed often, 
With each kiss’ fluid ripple. 

But oh

The flower of surrender, 
Is unmoved, quietly waiting
Smiling in sunlight. 
I go sit with my new friend.Thank God for friends
