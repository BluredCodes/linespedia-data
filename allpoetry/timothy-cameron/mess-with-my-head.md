Too hungry to eat 
Too angry to be mad 
To lonely to seek friendship 
To tired to sleep 
The last day of love 
That quit before it started 
Too stupid to write a smart poem 
Look for the good 
God don't make junk, you hope you heard 
Not bad at all, being ready to die 
Strain at me through your filter 
Smoke me 
DrugsJust want opinions.
