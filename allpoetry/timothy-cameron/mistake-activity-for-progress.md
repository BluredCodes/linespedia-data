There I cease, 
To cease, 
Being you 
For myself  

I  are a poet... 

A slave to freedom.  
Desperately quiet. 
Oh, death of desire 
Breadth of exhaustion. 

The heaven of your ideas 
Tattoos itself, 
Image upon image, 
Ying mates Yang
