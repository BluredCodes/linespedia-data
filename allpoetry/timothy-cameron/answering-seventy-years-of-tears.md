title: "Answering Seventy Years of Tears"

I learned,

Honesty without 
compassion 
is cruelty.  

The only power 
others can have over me 
Is that I don't feel 
good enough about 
myself.  

Walking with 
the enemy is never so sad 
As when it is 
A distorted perception of 
Choice.  

"But you are seventy, 
I am only forty-five".  

My knowledge does pale, 
to years and tears,
Beyond the veil.  

I can only love from where 
I am.  

I am no more, 
no less...than a response 
Of the mirror of love 
preceeding the one who received it
Before thee, 
So reflect onto me.

Time slips from mirror to mirror.

Pass it on.  
Write your faults 
in sand.  
But virtues of friends...
                       carve in granite.

                                        I learned.  
                         From your ache,
How much I love you.
