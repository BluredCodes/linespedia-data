Pain is plainly the gift some people cannot see through
But true pain sees us through
And through.
May all who see through the pane
Following behind us
Find us faithful to stroll and not saunter 
In the right direction
As a service
Of 
Slowving
Devotion.
Keeping Our Gifts Clean.
Washing the windows.
Yes, "I do" windows.
Reflect.Just interested in what people glean from what I write.  Am I too in my own little world?  Or do you find value in these words?
