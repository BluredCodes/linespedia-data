As Silent countless shadows
They stalk their prey,
Venturing to
Where dangers lay.

Unseen forces
Drive the pack,
Drifts of snow
Cover their tracks.

Wind in their face,
This crystal night,
The white wolves know
The time is right.

The kill is swift,
The blood is sweet,
The pack at last makes
Its filed retreat

The scent of the Den
Beckons them now,
Instincts advise them
Where and how.

Eerie calm settles
O’er the land,
Then howls cry out,
They’re home again!
