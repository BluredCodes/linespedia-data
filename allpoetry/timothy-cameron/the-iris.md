Quiescent
	
		Breathtaking
	
		Animated
	
		Scent of pine cocoons my fresh bodilessness.
	
		 
	
		When buried down, down, down
	
		Up north
	
		As I am
	
		In the midnight’s sky, alive.
	
		 
	
		Upon my back, 
	
		Peering up, up, up,
	
		Amid black ink of bliss, star-filled backdrop
	
		My leaping heart feels unrivaled.
	
		 
	
		Suddenly,
	
		Night Rainbow emerges!
	
		~The Iris of God splashes open~
	
		Delving down, down, down
	
		Infusing my soul with gratefulness.
	
		My soul body jumps up to DANCE!
	
		 
	
		God whispers light in gorgeous quotes,
	
		Bathes me clean in satin strokes.
	
		 


	Aurora Borealis
