title: "attempted definition of true love"
writer: "nyx"

the word love,
what does it mean?
where does it come from?
does it come from the
heart,
soul,
mind,
imagination?
why is it that whenever he says
'i love you,' i feel the sudden
urge to fly, supported by a wave of love.
love is the key to happiness.
true happiness. that can
only be obtained by 
times of melancholy.
but where does love come from?
why is the first time to say it so important?
and why is it so hard to say,
even if you -do- mean it?
love, true love, comes from two people..
who share an eternal devotion to eachother.

l is for the lust that they feel whenever they're near.
o is for the obsession that they hold for eachother.
v is for the vitality that they give eachother, just being together.
e is for the emotional values that they produce.

why is it that when i talk to you,
'i love you' just seems to always be on the tip
of my tongue?
because.. i truly love you.
i can't emphasize the word 'love' enough.
i can't even show you.
all i know is, it's true love.
boundless love. guaranteed for life.
