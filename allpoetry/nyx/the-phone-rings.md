title: "the phone rings"

i was so happy. it was purely indescribable, just lying there with you, our arms wrapped around each other. i was more beset with woe as our time together ticked by. we communicated our love; that made me so unimaginably happy. something seemed to sadden you, as we lied there, but you assured me it was just sleepiness. i was in pure heaven; you made me so happy. when the time for my departure arrived, i couldn't stop kissing you, never wanting to stop. on the drive home i was in the best of moods, and as i got home and blissfully got on the internet, looking forward to talking to you, my happiness exploded in my face as you acted uninterested to talk to me, almost blowing me off completely. as i got off and asked you to call me, you replied with an uncaring yes, as if talking to me was a chore. as i sit here and wait for your call, i dread to hear what you have to say, if you even plan on saying anything....the phone rings.heh. =/ it all turned out ok.
