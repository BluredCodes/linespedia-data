pressure is building, hate is flowing.when they'll meet is no one's knowing.the tension's seething, with no room to spare.i handle every word with delicate care.the trigger to set off your anger is near.i try to soothe and comfort you, avoiding my fear.i'm trapped in the middle of your silent rage,as your eyes glint with violence, and menacing craze.i'm scared, please stop. you're going insane.your anger, i'm afraid, i can't manage to tame.just relax, please calm down, i'm in despair.forget your troubles, i'm here and i'll always be there.it's me, can't you see? the one that you love.the one that you said was sent from above.to help ease your pain, and mend your heart.and with this devotion, we'll never part.it's ok to feel anger, just don't get too deep.your shouts and your yells are turning me weak.i promise you, it's not as bad as it seems.release your mind, forget your troubles, and come with me to a dream.yeah..the title sucks. please, someone give me a better suggestion.
