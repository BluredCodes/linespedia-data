---
title: "crying for love"
writer: "nyx"
slug: "crying-for-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4087-crying-for-love-by-nyx"
---

as tears invade my eyes,in hearing what you just said,i realize i'm not perfect for you,that it was all just in my head.while i was with you on the phone,i barely controlled my tears,i wanna be with you for so long,for boundless devoted years.i want our love to be infinite,but i can sense your aggravation,it seems you're getting tired of me,i hope it's just my imagination.i can't control my emotions,and i can't control my fears,i have the constant paranoia of losing you,and here come the tears..
