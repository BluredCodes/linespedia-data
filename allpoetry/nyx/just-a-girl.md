title: "just a girl"
writer: "nyx"

she's just a clueless little girl,stuck with her emotions,and no way to get them out.consumed only in her infatuations,caring too much,and always expecting good turnouts without a doubt.she's just a clueless little girl,using pen and paper to express her feelings,emptying her heart when something goes wrong.tears blur her eyes,as she fails to control herself,and dwells on her misery for so long.she's just a clueless little girl,making her way through life,and trying her best to prevail.she tries so hard, so long,falls on her dreams,and just accepts that she fails.
