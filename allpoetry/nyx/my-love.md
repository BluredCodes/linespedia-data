describing my love, is so hard..it'd be so much easierif we could read feelings,and sense the depths of them.the aura surrounding me isfull of my love,powered by the memories, hopes,and the desires.tears cloud my eyes, as i discoverthat at one point in my life,my soul will fade, and i won't see you.the tears then fill with happinessas i realize my love for youwill never fade.everyone is given a gift in their life.some are given the gift of speech,and some the gift of song,and even others, the gift of art.my gift is you.my task was to find you,and my goal was found.my love can't be expressed deeply enough withart, speech, or song.my love for you is simple, yet intricate,and it's all yours.my love, my best offer,is given to you, in loving hopesthat you'll desire it forever.hee..i love you, jarrod.
