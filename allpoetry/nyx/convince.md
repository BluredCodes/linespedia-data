title: "convince"
writer: "nyx"
slug: "convince"
source: "AllPoetry"

please just understand, i'm emotional girl.don't mind my worrying, just play along.i can't face my fears, or overcome my feelings.my weakness is hidden, for i try to appear strong.i'm really just a frail person, always on the edge of tears.all i'm asking is that you understand, and try your best to give me your love.as long as you stay with me, and you at least try to always be there,i'll try my best to be happy, and not constantly be so abrupt.you're the best thing that's ever happened to me,and i don't want to see it end.convincing and assuring your love for meis what you need to do.please, just do this, above everything else,as long as you mean it with your heart and soul,convincing me it's true.
