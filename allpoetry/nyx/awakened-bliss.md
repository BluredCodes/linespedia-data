very pleasantly surprised,at an unexpected time.it was time for me to get off the phone,and you finally said that line.that line that i've missed for quite some time,ever since your were sort of burnt out on me.you said 'no! you can't go!if i'm not talking to you i won't be happy!'you held me past my limit,as you stalled by acting sad.you said so many things that i've longed for so much;it made me extremely glad.you made me promise to call you,if i couldn't fall asleep.and when i'm this full of happiness,i can't sleep a wink.heh..it isn't that good..but..when i'm happy i write cheesy stuff. ^_^
