---
title: "Sick of You"
writer: "sweetOkashi"
slug: "sick-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

i'd like to think he'd understand
but he did not
i'd like to think someone would understand
but they OBVIOUSLY did not
they say im being childish
not everything is fair
that we should just make up
stop wasting hateful air
I'd like to think that just one person
would hear the voice I hear
Just one person 
wether i know or don't
Just anywhere
Where I am, was here


(**I've edited the original for obvious reasons. What annoys me most of all is people who put a label on things that already has a name. Why bother???? Because it's just the way things are)
