title: "Did I Leave It In My Pocket?"

I touched the gates of heaven once
only to be pulled away
I'd think it was a hand telling me that I didn't belong there
I'm not sure
I remember feeling claws
I touched the gates of heaven once 
only to realize it was my home
To be pulled away and directed someone else
seemed like i had my heart ripped out of my chest
I touched the gates of heaven once
and someone laughed in my ear
They told me of much better things I could have
things i always wanted
I touched the gates of heaven once
but only for 2 seconds
Then I took the elevator back to earth
all cold, afraid and alone
I touched the gates of heaven once
only once and never more
I looked up at the black sky
and screamed "Where's My Soul!!"
