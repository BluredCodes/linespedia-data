title: "Don't You Make Me Happy?"
writer: "sweetOkashi"

iro iro na koto ga  okotte     /a lot of different things are happening
soba ni iru dake de            /just because your with me
genki ni nareruwa              / I can be so full of life
Don't You Make Me Happy?       /don't you make me happy?

kaze no naka  kami o nabikase  /my hair fluttering in the wind
ORINJI no hana ga saiteru      /orange flowers are blooming
kumo wa nagarete'ru            /the clouds are floating by, the wind singing
kaze wa utaidasu

ashita ni natte mo             /even when tomorrow comes
kitto omoidashite ne           /im sure i'll always remember
wasurenai de ite               /i'll never forget
minna arigatou                 /i thank you, everyone

zutto zutto zutto              /always always always
wasurenai de ite               /i'll never forget
minna ga koko ni ita koto      /that you were all here with me
don't you make me happy?       /don't you make me happy?
