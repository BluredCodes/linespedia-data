you think that you know everything
but no one really knows
you stay to yourself, you are nameless
always know your friends from foes
you think that you know everything
but never say a word
you nothing but a shadow
not seen, not felt, nor heard
you think that you know everything
as what it means to be alone
you've touched the coldest cheek and shoulder, cold as ice cold stone
you think that you know everything
but then i believe you do not
can you translate this smile i pass to you?
or see how many voices i have fought?
you think that you know everything
so i ask you this one time, mind bared
if you know everything, all that there is...
then why do you look so scared?
