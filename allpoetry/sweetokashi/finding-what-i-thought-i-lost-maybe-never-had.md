title: "finding what i thought i lost, maybe never had"

you find it impossible
to understand everything i say
you always ask me what is wrong
when im having a bad day

you find it incredible
when i share some things with you
i tell you things no one else knows
things just between us two

you find it more terrible
when i cannot talk at all
your always there to catch me
when it looks like i might fall

you find it irresistable
to stay away from me
i always see you watching i
when i do ignore thee

you find it confusing
when i speak two languages at the same time
you think i do it on purpose
to give you pennies instead of a dime

you find it estatic
when ever i call you on the phone
especially when there's darkness
especially when we're both alone

you find it irritating
for me to watch, rewind rewind
not noticing your clear loved gestures
you think i might be blind

i find it oh so wonderful
to spend my time with you
you know my favorite color
you know when im feeling blue
