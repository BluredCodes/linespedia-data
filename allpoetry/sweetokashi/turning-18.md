it was like a dream
and not being able to go to sleep
to feel like you were carrying tons of nothing-ness
hundreds of miles
all i wanted was sleep
to lie down in a comfort of silk cushions and soft pillows
to doze off and never wake up
but of course
there was always that one sound, from somewhere in the darkness
you didn't know what it was or where it was coming from
but it kept you awake the whole night
all i wanted was my dream
my one big dream
and when i finally went to sleep
it'd be like waking up for the first time
