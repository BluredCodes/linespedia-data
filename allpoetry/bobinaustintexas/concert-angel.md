As Light plays in her hair
her face is positively gleaming
as her earrings dangle and stay atop her ear
the bracelets move as she raises her arms
her nose stud reflects the light from the sun
the doorag tied behind her bangs
as those jeans cling to her thighs
and her shirt hangs funny
feet covered by her jeans
necklace in the air
as she screams and jumps with the music
she stops to pull back her hair and scream
the barbell dangles from her tongue
she smiles at me, winks, flicks me off
God how i love that girl
