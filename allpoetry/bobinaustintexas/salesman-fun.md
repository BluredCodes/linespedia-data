Death to the consumer
right here at my feet
and not my tumor
more like an M16 shriek

be rude to a salesman
see how you won't get good help
do you feel grand?
i'll beat you to a welp

i'll gladly use my maglight
to beat you to death
so bring on the fight
and i'll take you to your last breath

when you're out cold
i'll pick up your head
put your jaw against the curb
and kick till your dead

don't look down on me at work
because without me your screwed
i'm nobody's jerk
and trust me, i can be rude

this isn't a threat
oh yea, it's a promise
how quickly you forget
you stupid doubting thomas

because i'm a salesman
you think you are better
i'll put your body in a trashcan
trust me you'll pay, down to the letter

Death to the consumer
right here at my feet
and not my tumor
more like an M16 shriek
