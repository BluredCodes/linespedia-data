always so happy to see me
makes me smile and feel good
full of life and glee
life feels the way it should

I feel you close to me
your warmth makes me sigh
your love carefree
enough to keep me high

side by side we sleep
and keep each other warm
I reach over to touch your feet
and feel your long sleek form

so nice to feel needed
feels good to be loved
you know how I like to be treated
as if you were sent from above

the bed moves slightly
you didnt hear me when I said
just ever so quietly
damn dog, get off the bed
