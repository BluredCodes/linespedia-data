Just shut the hell up and die
we're tired of your crap 
no, don't sit there and cry
just shut the hell up and die

how can i make you see
i don't want you here
you are no help to me
just shut the hell up and die

did you ride the short bus to school
are you just really this dumb
no, we don't want you here fool
just shut the hell up and die

you're not helping us out
you're pissing me off
all you ever do is shout
just shut the hell up and die

all night long you scream
and all the day  too
you have interrupted all my good dreams
just shut the hell up and die

i hate my neighbors car alarm....
i wish it would 
shut the hell up and diekinda silly, but i hate it
