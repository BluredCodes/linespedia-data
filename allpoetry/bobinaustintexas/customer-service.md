what the hell do you want
who the hell are you
what the hell are you doing here 
well, screw you too

and you want my help
you want me to what 
you can go to hell
so what if you told me that

get out of my way
get out of my face
hello.... today.....
your presence isn't grace

take it and go
i don't care about that
and i almost forgot
"Would you like fries with that?"
