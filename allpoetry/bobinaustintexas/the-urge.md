so i put my gun down, now what ?
you want to talk now, so what !
take a step closer see what happens, then what ?
you want me to do what you say, or what ?

i picked my gun back up, now what ?
you look at me with hate in your eyes, so what !
I'm going to empty my clip, then what ?
you actually want me to listen to you, or what ?

you're right it's almost over, now what ?
i've paid for my time and also yours, so what !
just keep backing up towards the wall, then what ?
looks like it's time for you to go now, or what ?

well.... just another day at target practice....was trying something out, i wanted to see if i could use the same phrase at the end and still make sense...
