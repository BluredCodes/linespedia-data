title: "Dorm Life Sucks"
writer: "BobInAustinTexas"

macaroni
ramen noodles
fake coke
crap beer
cheap toilet paper
no clean towels....again
more ramen noodles
paper plates
2 forks
homework again
more ramen noodles
ugly twin bed
don't own a tv
turn on the clock radio
down the hall to the shower
more ramen noodles
roomates laundry out again
fire alarm at 3 am
cold floor, no carpet
community shower
ramen noodles again
bathroom down the hall
nasty hallway
roaches in the trashcan
homework
more ramen noodles
