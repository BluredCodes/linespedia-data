I have a gun in my pocket as I walk on to school
It's to cover my ass from the crown jewels
This gun's not a toy and this gun's not for play
It's for survival through the end of today
Drive by shootings, a killing in the park
People afraid to leave the house when out comes the dark
I'm proud of my neighborhood, take care of my boys
we're in this together and in this we find joy
There's death all around us though, and thats said to say
I just wait my turn for it to take me away
A bullet doesn't stop to think, feel, or cry
So, I probably won't get the chance to just say goodbye
