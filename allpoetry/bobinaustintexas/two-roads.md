Two roads in school came to a cross
I took one with regret in my eye
it hit me with a loss
and almost made me cry

i left my education 
and found my life instead
i left all my friends
and walked with lowered head

money's all gone now 
and friend's are too
still trying to make the bills
and not fall to the screw

i wish i could have kept on
pursuing the noble fight
but sometimes you have to lay it down
when you're losing to the might

damn the man that made my life
and damn the kid that made that man
damn the man who can't survive
and who will live his life so bland

he will go on un-remembered
and never make a wave
just go on paying bills
and die, in not so many days

he will hate all his days
from the first one to the end
hope has all died now
a long boring trend

he picks up the gun now
and puts it to his head
carefully counts and waits for death
a countdown til he's deadthis is my first poem.... i am looking to express myself in an intelligible manner and want to work on everything to make it better... let me know what you think.
