speed up, slow down
go this way or that
turn left, turn right
hate death  fight

up this damn row
and down the next
don't let them get in
hate death fight

it's my turn now
my truck will win
get out of my way
hate death fight

You will die now
at my bloody hand
if you cut me off
hate death fight

damn you ford excursion
for taking two spaces
you inconsiderate soccer mom
hate death fight

AAAHHHHHHHH
you non driving dirtbags
why are you here
hate death fight

God I love christmas
in a mall parking lot
get in the christmas spirit
hate death fightand remember, as we forgive each other in church today, let's try to stay angels in the parking lot....
