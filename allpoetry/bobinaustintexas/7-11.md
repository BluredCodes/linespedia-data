Shotgun blast
22 in my pocket
how am i gonna last
outta here like a rocket

In the car
push the pedal down
drive away far
get on down town

another meal
some diapers for my son
with satan another deal
and they think this is fun

where to now
can't go home
not again i vow
before my brothers tomb

another day survived
another bullet dodged
one more sunrise
and another facade

one more try
one day i'll get caught
that day i'll fry
ill lessons got
