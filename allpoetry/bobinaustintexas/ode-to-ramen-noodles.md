Oh my ramen noodles
you have filled me once again
i love to eat you by the kaboodle
you are good for my brain

i eat you like the package says
i cook you with my great stir fry
i like you with a little pez
i even like to eat you dry

oh how i like to eat you
at every meal of the day
you cheer me up when i'm blue
you make me want to play

ramen noodles are the best
specially after a long hard day
easy to make and let you rest
even when i'm old and gray

now if you think i'm right
and you think i buy this by choice
this is about to incite
for me to completely rejoice

today is the day i have a job
and no longer do i eat this crap
life without ramen makes me sob
no longer the poor college chap

ode to ramen noodles
good for my brain
i hate you more with every kaboodle
my hatred will not wain
