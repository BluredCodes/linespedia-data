The death of a friend that brought us so close
The loss of the love that hurt us the most
The black of the suit that fit him so well
The one last thought, I'll see him in hell

Where do i go now with this guilt in my heart
I told him i'd catch up as i had my own car
His tailights disappeared without a sound
Later at the party he was no where to be found

We partied all night without single thought
As to where he was or if he got caught
When the next day came we knew he was missing
But where could he be, had we just missed him

We found out at school the death that had happened
Our friend was now gone, this death it had trapped him
So we went back out and drank one more time
But without our dear friend, truely a crime

We drank to remember, we drank to forget
We drank to all together and made one last bet
We'll all get together at least once on this trail
Even if it's in the waiting room on the way to hell

The death of a friend that brought us so close
The loss of the love that hurt us the most
The black of the suit that fit him so well
The one last thought, I'll see him in hellonly my second poem, let me know what ya think, honestly...
