This tree stands alone in the middle of the woods
The Sprite comes alone hoping not to intrude
The tree stands near dead, years frozen in place
from just a look or a glance, there's not hardly a trace
of the life that was once strong within there
Did anyone ever notice, did anyone even care
He sees this one tree, the first he had planted alone
there the tree stands beat, battered, caught in a still silent moan
He comes back to the start, the cycles complete
Where his power became awake, he now goes to sleep
He's the oldest in his clan, taught all that he knows
Now this old man comes back, to die here alone
This Sprite and tree have lived through it all
Wars, fires, humans, summers, springs, winters, and falls
He lays his frail body down near the trees roots
wonders where his life has gone and of all his recruits
As he lays down he feels the damp and cold earth
he recalls all of his days, right back to his birth
He can feel his life force draining back into the tree
He has lived a full life, has no more reason to flee
As the tree comes to life he forgets his own death
Feels the raw power with few remaining breaths
If the tree could speak aloud it would... only to thank him
but it's far to late... the powers been taken
The old mans true friend will now live again strong
with new creatures to live with, which will each come along
The old Sprite loved this tree dearly
and passed on in this spot so all could see clearly
The beauty of nature in just this one tree
let him give his life freely and maybe thats the key
inspired by one though and followed thorugh whole
is the way we should live to give peace to our souls
