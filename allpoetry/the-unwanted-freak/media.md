---
title: "Media"
writer: "the unwanted freak"
slug: "media"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41459-Media-by-the-unwanted-freak"
---

The media shapes my mind.
Molds it to its own shape.

It takes my belifs and manipulates then.
Takes my understanding and mashes them.

This is their only objective.
Take my mind and make it their property.

My mind melts without my knowledge.
Becomes a mash of useless knowledge.
