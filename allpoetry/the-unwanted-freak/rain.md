As I walked through town it started to pour.Why oh why must it rain while i walk?These days rain has become an enemy. It creates havoc wherever it goes.It's an enemy to drivers and event goers.But when I started to think. Where would we be without rain?Without it life wouldn't exist. This nourishing H2O brings life too little seeds.It sustains the large Oak trees.Without it we wouldn't exist.So maybe after all rain ain't so bad, lets enjoy it well we can.This one is hard to explain. I seem to write on weird topics that catch my attention. Thank to all who comment.
