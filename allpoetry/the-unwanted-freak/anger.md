---
title: "Anger"
writer: "the unwanted freak"
slug: "anger"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

His anger pulsates through his veins like a raging bull. 
He takes this anger and kicks the dog.
He feels better, for a few seconds.
Then the anger builds again. 
Why must he feel this way. 
All the stresses of life build and builds.
He cannot take it for that is the way he is.
He was born without a tolerance for stress of anger,
and he shows his stress through anger. 
He kicks the bog again. 
This time on the back. 
"Why must you bark you fucking dog?"
He repeats again and again. 
The dog lays in the corner as he walks away.
In fear of its owner, in fear of its life.This one was kinda based on my dad. He has very low tolerance for stress and anger and it seems he takes it out on the animals.
