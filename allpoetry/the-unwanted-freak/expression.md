title: "Expression"
writer: "the unwanted freak"

The loud music calms me.
It sooths me by extracting my anger,

The music flows through me.
Taking control of my feelings.
The anger is taken out through rage.

Why does this anger come?
How long will it last?

Why do they nager me?
Maybe it is my ignorance.
