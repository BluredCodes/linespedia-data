You call me a fag yet am I the one slapping my friends ass.You call me a fag yet am I the one dressed in enough GAP for an army.You call me a fag yet you use more hair products than the average girl.You call me a fag yet you towel flick each other when changing.You call me a fag yet you 'look' more gay than me.You call me a fag without even knowing me.Is it an insult you find funny,maybe even insultive.Maybe it's just become a generic term that applies to anyone'out of the norm'Now before i get nasty comments. When i say 'yet you 'look' more gay than me.' I am talking about compared to the stereotype.
