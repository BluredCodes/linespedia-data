What is perfect?Is it long grass flowing in a field?Is it a sunset with spectacular oranges greens and reds?Is it a midnight kiss from someone you love?Is it a newborn crying after being given birth?Is it a hot cup of coffee on a cold winter morning?Is it a freshly snow covered field,or is it just the way we are.The way everything is and will be.Is that what perfect is?I'm really new at this poetry thing and this one was written while having a coffee on break from work. Let me know how i can improve my writing. :)
