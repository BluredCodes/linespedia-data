---
title: "The girl"
writer: "the unwanted freak"
slug: "the-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"

She sits waiting
her bag at her side,
her shoes black like night,
her jacket long and flowing.
She is beautiful
her blond hair pulled back,
her hand gingerly placed on her lap,
her eyes gazing into nothingness.
She is untouchable.
She is unbreakable.
She is unobtainable.
