An exercise of the visualNot my bagThat's what I thoughtAt the endOf it all.Indoctrinisation,Institutionalizationand all that.An ode to men's bodies,and male bondingMacho stuff really.BUT I did LIKEthe seamless imagery ofharsh landscapesbarren rocks, glittering water,salt encrusted lake bedsand the plainnessof the story.
