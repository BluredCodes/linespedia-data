title: "Lost in fragments"

A parody of "What for?"
Arm wrestle with reality
It seems like a long time ago
That I really lived
Broken and forged anew
A battered survivor
The soul hobbling like a cripple
A survivor walking the street of life
"Dig those shiny new shoes"
A parody of the person
They once perceived themselves to be
Why not just be real please?
There's no pain
Just a basic meaninglessness
Empty.
The essence burns low
Unresolved issues waiting
for someone to recognise
the person within
A deep seated need
to disguise true motives
What you believe today 
you may not believe tomorrow
Peel and reveal.
Built in defence mechanisms
and painfully honest.
