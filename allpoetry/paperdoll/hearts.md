---
title: "Hearts"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "hearts"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

How many hearts do we have?
How many hearts must we wear?
How many hearts do we hear?
How many hearts do we see?

Heart of steel
Heart of stone
Heart of glass
Heart of bone

Heart of feathers
Heart of dust
Heart of tears
Heart of lust

Heart of cuts
Heart of pain
Heart of blood
Heart of gain

Heart of regrets
Heart of remorse
Heart of forgiveness
Heart of sadness

Heart of like
Heart of hate
Heart of life
Heart of fate

Heart of destiny
Heart of choice
Heart of love
Heart of it all.
