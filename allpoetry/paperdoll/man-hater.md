---
title: "Man Hater"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "man-hater"
source: "AllPoetry"

Men are disgustingUgly and vileVulnerable uselessweak and in denial.The fact that theyonly think of one thinglets them downAgain and again.I try not to letmy disgust showBut keep it away so they won't knowA woman is not a possessionnor an accessoryI should knowI speak from experienceMen become bitterwhen they cannot bendNasty and cruelto the very endStay away from meYou hounds who scent bloodI am not sportbut out of boundsI am aware of a manOn the huntOn the gameBefore its begunAll I have learnedis to be a bitchIt's patheticI wish it would end.
