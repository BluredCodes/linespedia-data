---
title: "Out of defiance"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "out-of-defiance"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Whip the wind around my body!
I will still be standing
through the storm
unknowing and unseeing.
I break through,
emerge as steel -
firm, rooted in the ground.
Nothing will bend
or break me down.
Strong willed -
against my will
Determination...
is my strong suit
There are no alternatives
I will be there past the end
Every time y'all broke me down
I got up with a frown
Stronger still against your will
Bruised and battered
but out of defiance 
never shattered.
