Quick! Drink,
Don't think
My mouth is a sink;
a drain, for liquor
straight to my brain, slicker,
make me drink quicker
Vodka, wine
friends of mine.
They are white, no smell
and soon you'll be mine.
Lips form words,
quite thick:
"gimme another, quick..."
Booze, oozing
through my veins,
Booze, spreading to my brain
Insane with drink,
warm, don't let me think again.
Seductively,
giving me  false security
from the  boring
tedious, reality
escapism.
Look at me!
I'm ten feet tall!
Whoops :} did I just fall?
I wanna dance, romance,
take a chance.
All of a sudden
it goes blank 
_____________
wake up naked on my back
Oh my god! am I nude?
Oh my god! who is that dude?
Laugh, yes, piece it together
yes I know that fella
There are my clothes
all over the floor
yes, that is my bra
I just saw...
Never fear
I'm intact
despite the fact
my memory I lack
and the fact
I'm laying on my back
in some man's bed
and cotton wool is
in my head,
No harm's done
I've been here before.
It's not so bad
when there is no recall.
Never fear,
It's become quite clear
I picked you up
because you were near.
Where I was
when I was drunk
never mind because
I was drunk,
intoxicated
with the drink,
medicated,
I didn't think.
It's no big deal
All I wanted was to feel.
Start Act I:
Pretend to know what I have done.
Start Act II:
Pretend I know what to do.
Look at me, I'm doing fine,
I am in charge of my mind.
(My hands are shakin',
please don't notice)
"Look at your hands."
"Yes I know."
"Would you like a drink?"
"Yes, please,
a cup of tea."
(My god, at least I had good taste for once.)
(I need to pee...)
Look at my hair!
I stare in the mirror
with full horror.
Go back, stop all acts.
(dialogue)
"What happened?"
A pause for all eternity...
“You picked me up”
“What did I say?”
“You said, “you are so good looking,
you should be in the movies”
“What else, please,
tell me for my own personal growth.”
“You said, “kiss me, so I can tell if you are good in bed”
“My god! Did I really say that?”
“Yes, and it worked.”
But my mind says 
You took me home
Memories say we were walking your way.
You say I am a bad girl
Are you a bad boy?
You say I have a drinking problem
I say “yes, it is in the blood.”
You play your guitar when I come back from the toilet
Singing “ice queen,
Melt the ice queen.”
You say “It seems appropriate.”
I am pleading
You are kneeding
My back.
Where is my memory?
Look – it’s not that scary,
Especially with
A warm body
And a chest quite hairy.
An affectionate man
With caring hands
Who has no idea
My mind wasn’t clear
An intelligent man
Who was also boozy
Don’t blame me!
He is also a floosy.
The inspection begins,
Curiousity aroused,
Handsome face;
Handsome hands.
Still hazy,
Feelin’ lazy,
Don’t want to get out of bed.
OUCH! There goes my head…
Time to leave
Time to fly
Want something more
But feeling weary
Starting to get teary
Bit hard
When breakin’ rules
We all learnt in school
What a fool.
Somethin’ you just can’t get over
Blown chances ends romances
Can’t move on
Three weeks on
Still waitin’
By the phone
Why did you
Have to ask
For my damn number
I knew you wouldn’t call
Just your conscience talkin’
I said “It’s no big deal…”
Anyway thank you
For showing courtesy
Despite the facts
Of  reality.
A paper hero
For a fallen lady
Who lost her manners
And acted shady.
Apples and tea
Sit well with me
Pretendin’ we aren’t hungover
Is really sweet.
Everything I asked for
A learned man
Who reads books
A good looking man 
Who is kind
To show me
There is hope
Despite the fact
I was inebriated
And didn’t appreciate it.
