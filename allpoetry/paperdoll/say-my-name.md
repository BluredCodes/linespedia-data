title: "say my name"
writer: "Paperdoll"

I lay in the mansion last night
and I thought your name in my head
a silent littany, over & over

silently I heard you
say my name just the once
just as I was falling asleep

it frightened me
a little, in the big eery mansion
in the silent night

I heard your voice
accross the ocean
on the first day of the year

thank you
I guess the ghosts helped me
thank you ghosts
