---
title: "Paranoia of the day"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "paranoia-of-the-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

Trepidation, as if emerging from a cocoonGingerly treading, testing the limbs,Arms fragile fronds springing forthBody making forays of normalcyQuietly denying the burning of beingIn the morning hours before sunrisePreparing for the harsh light of realityFacing a new day.
