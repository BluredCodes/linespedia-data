---
title: "Empty planet of my bed"
writer: "Paperdoll"

I see the sadness in your eye
I feel your suppressed sigh
I feel your loss, I see your pain
of something that we can't regain

I feel the sadness you try to hide
Eyes tear up against your will
Still I disengage and turn away
For I have no heart, only empty space

Empty closet, empty bed
empty closet of my head
Disengage and turn away
and start anew a lonely page

No regret and no remorse 
For there is no other course

Empty planet of my bed

Take pain and anger in your stride
I'll see you on the other side
Better still we'll start afresh
and be once more the once enmeshed
