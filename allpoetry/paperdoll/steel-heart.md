---
title: "Steel Heart"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "steel-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Steel heart
you make me kneel
on the dust
of your love

sink sharp claws
on the sky
rip it down
furled and shredded

skin bedded
with sharp swords
jack me in
thrash and turn

gravel rash
asphalt burn
turn of phrase
bitter rage

obscure grinning
hidden meaning
surface calm
embroiling storm

bad blood shared
interwoven
no words need
to be spoken

I lay my head 
on your lap
on my knees
I crawl back

past forgotten
future frozen
one more second
one more moment

all of a sudden
best friend gone
feet plod on
innocence departed
