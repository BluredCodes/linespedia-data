Universe, I ask you to grant me one wish.Hey Universe, Give to meeverything you thought I'd be,everything I thought you'd be.If not, just forget it,I don't want to waste just one minuteof my time.Happy days and memoriesI can take with mewhen I go To the placeWhere cells go.From mountain top to mountain top,From wave crest to wave crest,All o'er the ocean,I can fly.is this a poem?
