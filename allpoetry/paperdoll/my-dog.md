---
title: "My dog"
writer: "Paperdoll"
slug: "my-dog"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Image looks at me
with his big wet eyes
he follows me around
and then he gives up and sighs

I know what he wants
most of the time
an adventure in the park
meets his pals, have a lark

to run around sniffing
wee on lots of trees
scrunch up his back
and do a few poos

BUT All I have time to do
(today I'm running late)
Is chuck a bit of doggy dinner
on his little plate

Oh how I love him so
I've never seen anything more
gorgeous and miserable
in my life.I know this is rather simple but I'm not concerned as my dog is rather simple.
