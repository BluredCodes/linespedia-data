Amassed the people in their yearsAnd sanctified their hopes and fearsTrue captives in their life reposeBetrayed the word they left their causeA chance to chooseRefused the rightBeware the darkthe night shall swallow up your flamea wisp of small shall all remainAround the wheel revolves the skyand death shall never pass you byBut time shall equaland reflecteach maneach atomin the netcaught up and sortedfor the nextAnd pain shall mark your bodyYour mind shall suffer tooFor the mirrorof your misdoingsreflects and touches you.
