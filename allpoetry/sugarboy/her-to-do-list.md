---
title: "Her To-Do List"
writer: "sugarboy"
slug: "her-to-do-list"
source: "AllPoetry"

She's got a list
on a wall or in her head:

in the morning,
first cigarette.
wake the kids.
bathe the kids.
get them ready for school.

Quick breakfast-
black coffee, half a bagel

and off to work-

she's one of these babes you see
putting make-up on
in rush hour traffic

work's a bore
8 hours suffocating in an office

quitting time, happy hour
three cocktails or maybe four
her supervisor from the stifling job
relaxes, a bit, loosens his tie
and his tongue

his flirting is ignored-
she's heard and seen it all.

Evening, kids, dinner, TV
the latest tragedies
on the news

It's all the same to her.

Put the kids to bed
then bed for her
another day
of ennui
and procrastination

and meanwhile, a loaded pistol
waits for her
patiently.

This was not the day
but it is coming-
perhaps tomorrow?
