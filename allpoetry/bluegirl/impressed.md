Such a big manThe one with the lookHow do you pick themFrom the crowd of bodiesof decay and stinkof paint and sprayThe wannabe artistsIn the shallow end of lifequoting prose Feeling nothing butImportanceFor them you are the oneThey want to beYou,Who makessuch an impression on me
