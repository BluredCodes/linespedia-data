---
title: "I love you"
writer: "Tonerman"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30875-I-love-you-by-Tonerman"
---

I can't get you out of my mind
I must be a lunatic of some kind
Or am I just in love
A feeling set free like a dove
Not often do I let it show
But I just think you should know

I love you....
And only you.
