Random Short Poems.... (Updated on occasion...)


self-destruction of the highest sort...
to find that last resort...
to eat an ice-cream cone...
out in the dark all alone...


---------------------------------------

They question my sanity...
but do they understand?
for with this lack of vanity

I can put forth my hand
And rule the land......
