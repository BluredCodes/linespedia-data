writer: "Tonerman"

It returns,
Cast away,
By disease,
By sickness,
By illness.

A corruption,
Of my flesh.

But,
The medicine men,
They cure me.
Just to wound me.

I read,
I think,
I dispear.

I want sleep.
I need sleep.
I must have,
Sleep!

But it eludes me.
Sliping from,
My grasp.

My eyes,
Bloodshot.
Black bagged,
Haggard.

I'm so tired.
Routine keeps,
Me going.
I hate routine.
I must escape.
I need escape...

My mind,
Won't give it.
My mind,
Tries to give it.
But it fails.
Misserably.
Utterly.
Completely.

Why me?
Why should,
This be.
Oh, Why me?

I must rest,
I must escape,
I must sleep!
