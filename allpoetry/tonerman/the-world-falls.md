title: "The world falls"

And the world kept falling into the arms of internity...
But the world would not be held.. and fell through it's grip
The world is falling into the void of oblivion....
And life's sweet embrace was lost to all but the few....
But through the world of the dead all is lost...
Truth is lost to the seas of the imortal...
The depths of the void will be penetrated...if the way is made clear
Nothingness will embrace the universe upon which we tred.
And then the end was reached.
