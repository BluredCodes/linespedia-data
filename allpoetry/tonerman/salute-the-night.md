---
title: "Salute the Night"
writer: "Tonerman"

Salute the Night.
For all the Pain.
For all the Sorrow.
For all the hatred.
It seems to bring.
Salute the Night.

For the Depression,
That sets in...
For the Darkness,
That insues...
For the Pain,
That I wish for.
Salute the Night

For all these things,
And all those others.
Let's us lift our finger,
And salute the night....
