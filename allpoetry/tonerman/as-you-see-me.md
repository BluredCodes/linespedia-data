---
title: "As you see me,"
writer: "Tonerman"
slug: "as-you-see-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As you see me,
I am what you see.
Right?
Exactly what you see?
Right.
As you see me,

I am,
Stupid.
I don't get the grades you do.

I am,
Weak.
I can't lift the weight you can.

I am,
Benieith you.
I don't play the sports you do.

I am,
A coward.
I won't fight like you do.

I am,
Annoying.
I don't act like you do.

I am,
A pervert.
I'm not afraid of sexuality like you.

I am,
Immoral.
I don't have the religious beliefs you do.

I am,
Arragant.
I correct you when you are wrong.

I am,
Closed minded.
I won't hold the oppinions you do.

I am,
Unpopular.
I don't care about popularity like you do.

I am,
Not you!
I will never be you.
I will never not be me.
I will never give into you.

As you see me....
I am less then you.
I am less then you,
Yes.
I am,
Less. 
Less of what you are.
More of what I am.

You hate me,
Fear me.
Deride me.
Distrust me.
Dislike me.

That's ok,
The feeling,
Is mutual...

But one thing,
I don't fear you!
For as I see you,
You are less then,
what you are...
