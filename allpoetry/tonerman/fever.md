I can't think,
No focus,

My head,
Throbs
102.2 
But two,
Days ago,
Only 99.6

I can't think.
My mind is overheated.
My eyes inflamed!

Causes...
Unknown till,
Yesterday.

Sinus Infection,
Have had it for months.

Amusing,
I never knew.
But explains alot.

Fatigue.
Nausea.
Fever.

My mind is slipery
The sweat is to blaim.

It's cold,
It's to warm.

I have school.
But it hurts to much.
No energy.
Just dilusion.

Must sleep....
Must...
