---
title: "Your pain..."
writer: "Tonerman"
slug: "your-pain"

Your pain,
I wish I could,
Remove it.

Your pain is mine.
I wish it were,
gone.
Let us make it,
Gone.

I don't know,
How.
But we can,
Somehow.
I know,
We can.

My love,
For you.
Let it,
Heal you.

Let me,
Massage away,
The pain.

Let it,
Slip from you,
Off your body.
Off your back.
Off your arms,
Off your legs.
Let it,
Slip from your body.

Let me,
Remove,
Your pain.

For your,
Pain.
Pains,
Me too.
