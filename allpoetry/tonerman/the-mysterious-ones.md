title: "The Mysterious Ones"

Seekers of truth and finders of lies
Destroyers of injustice and cruelty
Death to foe and friend to allies 
Twisters of minds and twisters of reality

Writers of songs
Bringers of peace
Righters of wrongs
Causing war to cease

Changers of destiny
Praisers of the righteous
Writers of history
And Punishers of the traitorousI wrote this in the 6th or 7th grade. I think very little of it.
