---
title: "Lost Inside Of Us"
writer: "Triphazard"
slug: "lost-inside-of-us"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10148-Lost-Inside-Of-Us-by-Triphazard"
---

Do you think of me like I think of you?
Do you hear me call your name as I live in my dreams?
You're in each breath I take
and in every vision that I see.
Just as I envelop the night sky with my eyes,
you are in the depths of my soul
and in every corner of my heart.
For no one else have i ever felt this way.

This is not reality, for nothing else
reaches all that we are
or all that you are to me.
The future is unknown; the present survives.
We are what we are now.
You are all that I am
and all that I've ever been.
