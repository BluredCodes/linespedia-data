I've cried a million tears,
I've spent many a sleepless night,
just trying to go on with my life
has been a constant fight.

I only hope that you
feel half the pain you've dealt.
I hope you live to see
all the misery I have felt.

The one that you've chosen
will deal the cards just right,
to turn the tables round
so you can feel the bite.

You destroyed my spirit and my happiness.
but you will get your due,
'cause you will be the one alone
when she gets through with you.
