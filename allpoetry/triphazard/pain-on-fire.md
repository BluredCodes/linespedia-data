---
title: "Pain On Fire"
writer: "Triphazard"
slug: "pain-on-fire"
source: "AllPoetry"

My pain it thickens with every breath I take
my thoughts race and there's no one around
they say talk but it's not that easy
I feel like sometimes taking away my life
only to forget what I love and what I need
forgetting who loves me and who really cares
I'm like a bullet I raise and run
so fast I will just explode once I hit rock bottom
the pain is like venom, a bite or a knife
with no way out
I cry and I scream
but I'm still so stuck
or maybe ever trapped
no windows or doors there's no way out
I would just like to die
cause I just do not understand
there is a way out
but to find it would really
be harder than all hell
I guess that's the way it goes for now
