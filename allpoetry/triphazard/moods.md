title: "Moods"

I'm really in a depressing mood today
  it's very hard to understand
  what inner turmoil my mind has invented
  that my heart can not withstand

The mind has an unanswerable question
  only the heart could actually see
  by having a vivid imagination
  I dream of how things could be

So many unyielding circumstances 
  have caused me to realize
  how the true inner emotions
  cannot survive in disguise

Anything less than everything
  cannot seem to gratify
  the longing of my inner self
  with its needs to satisfy

I have now become elated
  my inner self is finally free
  to learn, to love, to understand
  this person I'll call me
