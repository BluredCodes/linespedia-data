---
title: "Forgive Me"
writer: "Jara"
slug: "forgive-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46360-Forgive-Me-by-Jara"
---

forgive me for my 
impotency
I find the world
scary
forgive me for my
sensuality
I find the moment
tempting

in your shadow I am
blanketed by mystery
unable to extract
what your thinking
uncertain of the substance
lingering between our bodies

forgive me for my 
curiosity
I am merely young
forgive me for the
audacity
I have nothing left

stripped of my many faces
my soul hangs about
like a star of uncertainty
unsure of what to say
or how to be

forgive me for my love
it will not die
forgive me for my hopes
tokens of a child

forgive me for leaving 
with the rising of the sun

slaves to freedom
hide among restless winds
