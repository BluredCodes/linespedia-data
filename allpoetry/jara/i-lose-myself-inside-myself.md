I lose myself inside myself
I lose myself inside myself

the walls are growing thin
forever drawing my name in red
I can't bretahe corruption again
I can't breathe corruption again
they plead me to pour my soul
like milk on a bowl of cereal
but I won't
Fuck you I won't

I lose myself inside myself
I lose myself inside myself

Do you take for granted 
the things we shared
did you ever really care
I say you won't 
make me petty
at least not again

I lose myself inside myself
I lose myself inside myself
they can't take what's gold
I lose myself inside myself
childish games I'm growing old

endless nights dancing 
to your guitar
reciting poems from our minds
now you run and hide
so here is my goodbye
for I have enough to harvest alone

I lose myself inside myself
I lose myself inside myself
but I won't lose myself in you
