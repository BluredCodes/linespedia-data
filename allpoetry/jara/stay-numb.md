Hey Girl
yeah you girl

slow down girl
slow down girl

It's crazy out there
you have enough wounds 
to heal

slow down girl 
it's crazy outside
you better stay in
because the weathers
enough to tair you down
and leave you dead

slow down girl
stay here girl

hey girl
you know the sun died
a long time ago
and the world's on
it's last leg in some minds

slow down 
you don't have to go
out in the cold
where your only friend
is lonliness

slow down girl
stay numb girl
stay numb
it's crazy out there
