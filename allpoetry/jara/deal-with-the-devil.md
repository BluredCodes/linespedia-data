title: "Deal With The Devil"
writer: "Jara"

hey play that one 
with the funny sound
when we used to laugh
and get high
smoking leaves of forgetfullness
ITS BEHIND ME NOW
remember the one
that made us smile
maybe just brought us together
yeah that one
I forgot what we liked so much
constant clouds?
drunken marks?
lost nights?
lost months?
or was it the safety 
of intoxication we chased?
hey play that one 
till my nose burns again
not for long 
my teeth are numb
ITS ALL BEHIND ME NOW
bug eyed hollow minds
deprived of sleep
no need to eat
we feed are heads
natural poisons
dropped Hoffman's solution
saw the world like children
hey play the crazy one 
empty bottle again
sobriety is a windy road
just ask my mom friend
its all out of tune
your smile looks jaded
your blow is overrated
there's a junkie in the mirror 
worth the blood of angels

play the forgotten one
but not for very long
my soul is tired of losing 
it's mind
play the forgotten one 
but don't take what's mine
my soul is tired of losing 
it's mind
