My sad attempt to make ammends is in the following lines.

Tonight sorrow filled I write
once again trying to rearrange
all I so naturally disgrace
they say some were born freedom's fool
if this be true I am
of they tell
attempting to tame the wind
empty net yet again
so sweet boy here is a poem 
from my heart for you.

DEAR DAVID

I came to search the moon's face for a sign of hope
and I sat near silent oceans
pondering life's simplicities
awakening a modern birth of god
unseaming me my weariness left me
I died while alive

along time ago I asked heaven to send me a dream
one with wings
one that will never be forsaken
my dream unfolded before me
like the ribbon rays of a 
moring sun
I lived among the dead


Then one day I pleaded heaven to send me a child
one that walks with the sun
a child forever young
I watched you fall from the sky
Child all I had left was a dream I gave to you
now all I can do is breathe my dream into you

and as I came I also left
but not unchanged and
perpetually marked
I left to mend a ruined home
among loved ones turned strangers
long ago
I left to free my soul

Tonight I pray child
I am but a comforting thought
with this in mind I thought we must have a song
a melody to remind me of the times we've shared

our song never played
I tried to write it
but the words never came
funny how I can dance to an unwritten tune
for all sung are unworthy of my love
for you

but when I remember lost times
my heart strums away untamed 
accompanied by words
I still don't comprehend

so if the wind blows you my way
my love will be here 
as it was before
in it's own bizarre way
dancing to our song

I leave with one simple request
Don't let me fall from you mind
Dont let me fall from your heart
