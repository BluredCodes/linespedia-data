---
title: "Novel Job"
writer: "Jara"
slug: "novel-job"

novel job hiding your scars
wanna write 
wanna get high

right minded freak
reading socrates
for the hell of it
do people actually do that?

tastes like a dream
catching reality
with it's teeth
and they cant
detect what it's 
really about
I like it like that

free lance for sanity
finger picking
for stability
dusting of pieces
in the attic
of my dreams
to feel something

wanna write 
wanna get high
novel job playing god

pick myself up
fall again
find a man
to call my own
leave him crying again

welcoming darkness
my faithful friend
alone in an iron bed
we laugh at our behavior
talking to one another
sipping eeriness to
escape the mirrors

entwined thorned
beaten masked confusion
all I need is love

novel job losing your mind
wanna write 
wanna get high
