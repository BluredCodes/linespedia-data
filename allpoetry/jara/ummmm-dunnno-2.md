title: "Ummmm dunnno 2"
writer: "Jara"
slug: "ummmm-dunnno-2"
source: "AllPoetry"

ruin glows
in the dark
people weep
children play
thoughts ignite the mind
confusion wins again
death takes no calls
beauty never grows old
youth never dies
hiding never works
running hurts
colors always show
gods not a man
gods not a woman
love isnt learned
hope opens doors
lifes too short
lifes too long
not enough smiles
not enough tears
loves always worth it
times are changing
people should unite
too many thoughts
gotta get em out
time works magic
gifted is the mind
hurting helps 
rivers dry out
lovers take chances
hermits are intriguing
children deserve attention
moms dont know everything
neither do fathers
karma is relentless
passion is mindless
truth is everywhere
truth is rare
good teachers listen
artists are special
so are mathmaticians
dessert never fits
dessert should come first
simplicity is releasing
freedom is internal
love is perpetual
stars are attainable
life is everchanging
friends aren't critical
life isnt definable
possibility is endless
insanity makes us colorful
sanity is boring
normal people aren't real
i could go on forever
love is unfathomable
love is everything
dreams shatter
love is nothing at all
