---
title: "Moon Drops"
writer: "Jara"

In the night 
a dream creeps
half asleep

I watch a shadow
dance naked 
in the city
of my safety

and the moon 
gently drips
splashing my face


I am elegant

He tells me there is no other
place he'd rather be.
