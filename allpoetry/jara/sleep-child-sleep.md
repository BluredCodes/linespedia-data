---
title: "sleep child sleep"
writer: "Jara"

sleep child sleep
the calling of dawn is upon us
and slightly disturbed is the light
this age rich with promise 
grows more flacid by the day
souls mascarading in a cosmic womb
abandoning their mother who nourishes
them so

sleep child sleep 
and cherish your dreams 
for tempests of doom
cant disturb slumbers of youth
I have misplaced the innocence
that once  embraced angels
they visit while I sleep
when vulnerability unleashes
scars hidden behind monumental walls
every empire inevitably falls

sleep child sleep
and let me watch you breathe
the world seamed with strings of change
carries the need to think
far away
my soul bleeds into the night
watching you rest among tired things
the frostbitten world
grows a little warmer
while you dream

sleep child sleep
in a bed far from pain
where hectic surroundings
gently roll away
scurrying about in a race 
other children give in to time
and age as raisins in the sun

you are safe with me 
sleep child sleep
