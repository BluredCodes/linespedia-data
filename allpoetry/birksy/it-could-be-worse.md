It could be worse, oh
it could be worse.
How little there is that separates me
from the wheelchair, or cane,
or from a life full of ridicule.
So I’ll stop myself groaning,
when it is nothing to
what could have been.
And I will be thankful,
will be so happy that
I'm as good as I am.
I shan't let it go to waste.
