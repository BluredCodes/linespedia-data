Just tea for now, thank you,
still have lots to do, round
the home, the garden, the cat’s take
up so much time, you know.
Can’t stop as long as I’d like to,
you must have chores, too, to do,
some tidying up, I’m sure, it’s
so hard to get on top of.
What’s that you say? No tea
today? That’s awfully strange,
I thought I saw you bring some
in, this morning, in your weekly
shopping?
My mistake, well, I mustn’t take
any more of your time, I only
popped in to see how you were,
maybe stop for a chat, to pick
up from where I left off of
just yesterday.
But I can see that you’re busy,
I’ll leave, but perhaps you’ll
have tea tomorrow? I so like
to try other peoples.
