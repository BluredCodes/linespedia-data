And so I spy cupid,
Hiding on top of my wardrobe,
Pretending to be insignificant,
But I know better.

Could that really be cupid
Sitting up there on my wardrobe?
Or have I put my hope
Somewhere other than the problem.

I think I'll just leave cupid up there,
Skulking on top of my wardrobe,
At least there'll be no-one else
You'll be bothering tonight.
