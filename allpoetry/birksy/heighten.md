I look up, you are there
senses suddenly heighten,
looking forward to our time
together.
I make my move, you respond,
and so we ease through our
worlds, tension falls, as
our eyes upon each other.
Such short time when all's
weighed up, keep things
sharp, but long enough to
feel the scorching air.
Then,I look up, and you
are gone, but only till
the next time, and
till then, everything's
heightened.
