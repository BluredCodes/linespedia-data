No one can ever find out who you really are,
We all have a sanctum, all have a place
that can never be touched. Take a second
to find it, where the thoughts we’d never speak
sit side by side with the loves we’ll never share,
it’s a strange old place, is it the 80, or the 20?

Why do I dwell here? Something pulls at me,
whispers to me, makes me follow until I am
immersed in the unsaid, the unfelt, the unknown.
At least I am alone here, warm and content
with my own self. I guess, if I revealed anything,
I’d reveal I liked it here.
