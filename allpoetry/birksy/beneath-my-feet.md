The ground gave way beneath my feet,
I thought I was walking, but found
I was falling,
thought I was falling, but found
I was floating,
able to direct my descent where
I needed to be,
perhaps even survive the impact,
but I’m not there yet, and I secretly
hope, with your help,
I’ll find myself rising.
