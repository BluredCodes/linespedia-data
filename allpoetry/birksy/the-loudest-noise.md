His small wave lights up
her face, she must wait
cold on the platform, till
the train pulls in, looking
hard through the windows.
Then, when she reaches
him, she’s cool again,
no feathers out of place,
platonic patter issues
forth; but it’s that
smile that makes the
loudest noise.
