Your hand stretches
out, and I take it, follow
lines of symmetry,
a movement towards, each
holds the others nape,
smile and look the length
of our bodies, my thoughts
in tune with yours, and
the back of our hands stroke
down our skin, and then,
they, too, hold together,
and we stand a circle complete,
hemispheres exact.
Then we move so close, meet,
then cradle, kiss, then mould,
the circle complete again,
we make the perfect love,
unbarred, then sleep,
and from above, we are just
the perfect form of
symmetry.For Sheri - miss you while you're away.
