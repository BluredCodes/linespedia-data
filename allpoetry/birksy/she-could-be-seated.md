title: "She Could Be Seated"

She could be seated
opposite me,
I'd never see,
perhaps, though,
I'd hear her,
that lilting voice
to charm the trees.
Or maybe I'd smell her,
that scent I'd imagined
a thousand times, but
most of all, I'd sense
her, for her heart
shines like a beacon
for my soul.
