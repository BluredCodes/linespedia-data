title: "The Last Page"

Come to the last page,
and it’s the same as the first,
what was I hoping for?
What could it have been?
We never believe it exists,
not real, till we get
to the end.
But I don’t look down,
don’t need to see the
last word, don’t want
to know how it
finishes.
I’m a coward and a
fool, so I hold my
breath and shut the cover,
accepting I’ll just
never know.
