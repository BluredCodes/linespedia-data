The angels sleep,
the devils play,
and us humans
love and hate,
it’s all so simple,
these dreadful days
elongate,
and become the
worst of times.
If I could pray,
I would, but I
may summon the
wrong sort of help,
whoever said things
can only get better,
was wrong.
