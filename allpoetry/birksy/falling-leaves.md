I was once in a forest, the leaves sheltered me,
ground supported me, I was happy.
Now I can see the sky, and I slip as I try to move.
I didn’t see the falling leaves, but now they stick on my shoes.

And though I know they should grow back,
I fear, for me at least, it will be far too late.
