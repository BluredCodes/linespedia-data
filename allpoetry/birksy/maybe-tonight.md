title: "Maybe Tonight"

Maybe tonight, when you're
all on your own, you'll hear
a strange noise, a growl or
a groan, from the blackened
out kitchen, and you reach
for the phone; don't reach for
the phone.

Maybe tonight, when you're
nearer to sleep, you'll hear
a strange noise, a snap or
a creak, from the blackened
out hall, and you try a
quick peek; don't try a quick
peek.

Maybe tonight, when you're
reading this verse, you'll hear
a strange noise, a whisper or
curse, from directly behind
you, and you'll know it's
for worse; hush, you don't
know it's for
worse.
