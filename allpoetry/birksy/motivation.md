Move among the whipping reeds,
Buzz in my ears as the bloodsuckers wait,
Soon to be invisible, to play amongst
the others.
Made it through the gap,
now I’ll try that green, green grass,
chew for a while, spit it back
at them, and move on before they
set on me.
Legs are wet with freedom, the dusty
yard has the spilt blood of the kill,
felt good again, felt right, memory’s
a wonderful tool, but there’s nothing
quite like the real thing.
I’ll just hide in here, nice looking
house, people asleep won’t know
the difference, meet my motivation,
it’s guaranteed to make you scream.
