---
title: "Expecting Too Much"
writer: "birksy"

Turning off the obvious,
let's pretend I'm not
perceptive, let's hope I'm
just a little wrong
about all this.

Understand the others
problems, don't think
about your own, being
brave in times is what
it's all about.

But I have to say,
a simple word would
make all the difference,
perhaps, as always, I
expect too much.
