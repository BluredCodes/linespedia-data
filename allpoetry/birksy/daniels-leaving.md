Daniel’s leaving, her Daniel’s leaving,
Alone in the dusty kitchen, she shudders,
Her tears do nothing to stop the grief,
She needs more hope than ever.

For many years she’s waited,
knew that one day it’d catch up,
wants to go back to the start again,
Doesn’t want to give him up.
