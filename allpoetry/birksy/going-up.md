---
title: "Going Up"
writer: "birksy"

Everything goes up, it's
oh, so irrisistable,
and I wonder where it'll
stop, can't keep going up
forever.

I feel it will end, and I
won't want to be around,
we'll have to wait and see,
if I am.

All going up, please can
you stop the world, I have
to go.
