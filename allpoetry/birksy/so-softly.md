---
title: "So Softly"

I stroke you,
softly at first, but then
more, and the more I
stroke, the more I can't
help myself, but we promised
it would be softly tonight.
My breath is deep, I control
my movements, so softly,
until with hardly any sound,
we combine, so softly,
the two, now one,
it’s hard not to
overcome
so softly.
