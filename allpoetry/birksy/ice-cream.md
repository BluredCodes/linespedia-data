Eating an ice cream, in the sun,
mind you don’t burn your neck, it’s too
hot almost to walk to the bus,
the sky’s so blue.

Then the ice-cream’s gone, but the sun
still glows, I’ve no more reason to
sit here, the wife of a man kisses him
and they quietly walk the street.

They quietly walk the street.
