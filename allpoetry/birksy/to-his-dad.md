title: "To His Dad"

Tired eyes, half shut,
he rests, head in his mother’s lap,
watching the scene, but only
remembering the interval ice-cream.
His hand rests, so light on my
arm, it means the world to me.
He’ll be grown up soon,
sitting in his own seat,
I hope he still wants to
hold on to his Dad.
