Written as if it’s a quote, don't blame 
it on me, I'm just the one who tells you,
the message isn't mine, I'm not the one
who says these things, I don't like
decisions, let me sit at my desk,
taking orders.
I doff my cap to everyone,
and pass the buck to all below,
don't try and corner me, I sit
close to the door for a reason.
