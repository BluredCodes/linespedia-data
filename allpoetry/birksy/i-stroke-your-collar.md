title: "I Stroke Your Collar"

I stroke your collar,
and if you purred, I
really wouldn't mind,
I'd keep you whatever
creature you turned out
to be.
The real question is,
what you'd make of me,
this ill formed
ape, who screws things
up.
Let's imagine the
menagerie, and when
we find two words that
match, let's hope they
might be love.
