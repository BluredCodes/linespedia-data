title: "Dirty Trash"

So we reach a new reality today,
those cards, annual words of love,
commitment, contentment, time to
take them to the bin.

And the bitterness in your voice, I
know why it's there, I have nothing
I can say that's going to help, that won't
blow the whole thing sky high.

So, I say, yes, throw them away,
along with the wedding pictures, I
enjoyed that day, didn't really think
it'd come to this.

And there's no need for us to say
another word, a simple act omits
the past few years, yesterday is
now tomorrow's dirty trash.
