---
title: "Anxiety Diet"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "anxiety-diet"
source: "AllPoetry"

You kept me awake
last night,
that and
feeling hungry,
in a way
they're both the same,
but I guess
you know that anyway.

This morning
I was lighter,
stepped on the scales
at least two times,
wonder where
the pounds went,
this anxiety
diet's for me.
