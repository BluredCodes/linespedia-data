title: "If I Should Die Suddenly"

If I should die suddenly,
and not get to say what I should have said,
then to my friends, I thank you, you have
made me laugh, and taught me emotions,
to my family, I thank you, you have
kept me safe, and taught me values,
to my children, I thank you, you have
shown me purpose, and taught me fragility,
and to my wife, I thank you, you have
shown me faith, and taught me love.

If I should die suddenly,
forget about the bad times,
think only of the good times,
don’t think that I have gone,
understand that I look out for you,
though only want the best for you,
to see you happy, content, and safe,
at peace with those around you,
see my space where I would have been,
and try to talk things through.

If I should die suddenly,
I will die without remorse,
For I’ve achieved so much more
than I could have ever hoped,
Gather to remember me,
Hold hands and picture me,
Support those who’ll miss me,
Then watch my casket go,
But don’t be too upset,
I will have already escaped.
