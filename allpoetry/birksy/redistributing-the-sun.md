title: "Redistributing The Sun"

I turn to the sun,
feel it warm my skin,
and life bleeds into me,
sets the standard for the day,
I hope that I can pass some out
to those who need it most,
to those with no sun at all.
It's a right we should all have,
but some take it away,
I collect the sun,
and try my best to make
it better.
