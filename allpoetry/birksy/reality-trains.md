Wouldn't it be nice
if you could vote them off the train?
Open the door,
not have to hear them again,
Till maybe there were two,
or just only you,
To enjoy the beautiful
tranquil solitude. ©  on Jul 03 2001 01:17 AM PST, Simon Birks   0  • 14
