---
title: "I Didn’t Help"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "i-didnt-help"

When she passed me, she looked my way
fleetingly, but didn’t stop, though I felt
she wanted to. I watched her walk away
continued to think she didn’t want to
go wherever it was she was going.
And then I was diverted, and forgot
all about her, my memory has never
been that good.
Next day I saw her again, though
this time she was dead, the same
expression on her face, she looked
at me, not fleetingly, and all I could
think was that I didn’t help.
