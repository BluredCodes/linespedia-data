Hands with the blood stains,
wash, but they don’t go,
Run through the scars
No flesh to stop the muck.
Keep your wits about you –
Preservation keeps you –
Words are filth to you.
Now dark, the silhouettes
glisten, you know in the light
you’ll be just the same, lie
low and softly breathe – 
hurt no-one but yourself.
