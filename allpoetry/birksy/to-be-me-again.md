title: "To Be Me Again"

The porch looks warm,
light on, clean shoes
in the rack, like where
I used to live.
Could give the new me up,
don’t want to hurt
anyone else, just need
some warmth.
I could be the old
me again.
I know someone’s
looking, if I don’t move,
I’ll be moved on, but
I’m just lost, half
searching for a new home
to accept my faults.
