title: "Too Old for My Side"
writer: "birksy"

Sleep soundly, sweet baby,
Breathe deep ‘pon my chest,
Let dark night grow hazy,
You're sweetly at rest.

At morning, we’ll wake to,
The bright sun outside,
You’ll smile and you’ll break through,
The tiredness inside.

Sleep soundly, sweet baby,
Let slow time tick by,
For tomorrow you maybe,
Too old for my side.
