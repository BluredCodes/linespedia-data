The fight broke out,
then disappeared,
left nothing else to say,
so I sat on a pillow,
and caught a cold in
my hands, then through it
far away.
We’ll never see the exact
same house, can’t believe
it’s come to this, never
laugh the same laugh,
never know what’s best.
Keep the thoughts within
us, there’s nowhere for
them to go, we’ve
shared our last time, dropped
a heavy curtain on the show.
