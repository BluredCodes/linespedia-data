---
title: "Houses for Cars"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "houses-for-cars"

A bent hinge, twisted, screamed
into submission, and
what it hid within, now
gone, raced into
oblivion. Nothing
is left for these
houses for cars,
they now just take
up the nervous
space, one by
one. And I cannot
even contemplate
what may become of them
They all just seem so static,
a monument to my anxieties.
