Mum, she won’t stop talking,
I know we’re in public,
But I rather her verbs
Were the slightest bit interesting.

Can you tell her to shut up?
I want to look at my book,
I’ll thumb through the pages,
Not reading the words.

Mum, can she sleep now?
I wanted you to see me,
Instead of looking at her,
I want to be noticed.

Mum?
