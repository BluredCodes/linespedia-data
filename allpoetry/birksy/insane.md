Enough of this wait,
it drives me insane,
I have to be there,
have to say something.
Come give me a chance,
I'm only just I,
I'll beg if you want,
and if that's not enough,
then throw me away,
but it's this wait that's
the worst,
I'm slowly creeping
insane.Um. Wonder who this is about...
