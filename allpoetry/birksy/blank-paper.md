Blank paper
No words to say
No feelings to show
No love to know,
Were we here?

Blank paper,
Forgotten pasts
Unthinkable futures
And so where has now gone
For it was never here.

As if the tide never changed
And the thought never came
I saw you there
But never dared
To ask your name.
