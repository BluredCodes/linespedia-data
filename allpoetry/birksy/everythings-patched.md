title: "Everything’s Patched"

Everything’s patched,
The owner’s success
only seen through statistics,
Come ride with me,
Show me the better
times; they look only
worse from here.

The signs aren’t a-changing,
So let’s not pretend,
And I wonder if next year,
green is not red or brown,
Your surfaces aren’t difficult
to scratch.
