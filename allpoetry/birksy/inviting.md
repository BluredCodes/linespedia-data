There you are,
inviting, in the
extreme.
Can't wait to get
you home, play
with you a while -
like a dream,
yet right in front
of me, I smile,
I have seen my
dinner this eve.
Don't move away,
as if I'd really
let you, just keep
coming my way, and
quite invitingly,
I'll keep letting
you in.
