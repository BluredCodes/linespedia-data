---
title: "Days Full of Words"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "days-full-of-words"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2651-Days-Full-of-Words-by-birksy"

You draw me in,
Your smooth skin hiding complexities
Twists and turns within.

The old, the new
The first and last that live upon each other
Keep me coming back for more.

If I had many lives, I could not read
you all, yet for each of you someone will, and keep a
phrase, wherever they end up.

Wouldn’t it be a shame if your cover
was not opened, your spine mercilessly broken
and your pages ruffled, maybe smudged.

So I, like the shelves beneath you,
will support you, and in time you will help me,
pass more of these days full of words.
