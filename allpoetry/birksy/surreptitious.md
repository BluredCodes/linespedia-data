There’s a girl I now know, Surreptitious
is her name, you daren’t be left alone,
she’ll rob you of your mind, replace
it with her own, you’ll think of
nothing but who she is, how she is,
what she could be.
I’ll be gone for a little while, can’t
trust myself to Surreptitious, keep
getting caught in endless loops,
need to find a secret way out,
but if she turns to you, smiles, says
her simple name, get out while you can
still, see yourself as
you alone.
