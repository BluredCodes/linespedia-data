title: "Place Your Hands On My Heart"
writer: "birksy"

Sons, place your hands on
my heart, it will forever
be yours. Hold it together,
for its thousand cracks may
one day shatter.
Try and remember me,
your hand in my hand,
that I love you, that it's not
you I leave. Be strong for
your mother, be strong for
yourselves.
I'll always be close, always 
be ready to come and support
you, perhaps you'll support me.
These tears that I cry you do
not understand, so simply
place your hands on my heart,
till they dry.
