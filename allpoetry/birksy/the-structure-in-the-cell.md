title: "The Structure In The Cell"

Look in the gap, but
please don't sneeze,
the structure in the
cell will fall if
you let them know.
Perhaps when you
look away, the ghost
will remain in your eyes,
these people with
the strangest lives,
living in parallel
harmonies.
The structure in the
cell, is only one in
the crowd, might
tumble when
least expected, so
please, these people
don't need you with
your infectious sneeze.
