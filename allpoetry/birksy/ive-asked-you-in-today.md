title: "I've Asked You In Today"

I've asked you in today,
Because I like what you say,
The way you say it is important,
And that's turned out okay.

You're a one in a million,
So a million in one, is it
Possible you might want to
Work for me, just for fun?

I know the pay is very nice,
Keeps those wolves from the door,
But, as I say, at what price,
Life's not expensive, anymore.

We can keep you in slippers,
Write a note for your milk,
Buy a present for the nippers,
Your wife pyjamas of silk.

I know that you're humble,
Keep your head above the board,
Never seen you so much as stumble,
Your persona isn't flawed.

So if it's okay with you,
I've signed the contract myself,
You're here till you're ninety-two,
We'll all share in your wealth.
