Is it wrong that a man
from the RSPB, can have
crows feet or pigeon toes?

Does the fisherman do justice
to a red herring,
or the kipper tie?

How far do we go,
to behave how we ought,
to stand backs straight?

Can a mole be
blind to love, or
see past the end of its’ nose?

Can any of us, still
be humane, or are all our
souls to trade?
