title: "Little Moth, Little Moth"
writer: "birksy"

Little moth, little moth,
don't eat a piece of me,
I'm only held together
by these little threads
you see.

Leave me be, leave me be,
eat a bit of him instead,
save me for another day,
I'll try and sew more
threads.
