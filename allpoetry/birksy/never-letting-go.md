title: "Never Letting Go"

The bird caught the tree,
it’s little feet splayed out,
it landed on the branch,
pinpoint accuracy, never letting
go.

And while the rest of us
spin, on worlds both inside
and out, the little bird
clings on the that branch, never
letting go.

I’d like to be like that bird,
yet I know it’ll be some time
before I can perch again, when
assuredly, I know I’ll be,
never letting go.
