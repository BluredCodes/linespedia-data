---
title: "Everybody’s Woes"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "everybodys-woes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Everybody’s woes, they’re all
here for us to hear,
realise they’re no different
from your own, and it’s
this that makes it better,
these social sharing ways,
knowing that your problems
are never yours alone.
