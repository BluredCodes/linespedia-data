Chinese whispers,
What did you say?
Because if it is what I heard
I don't know what to do.
If giving up is all so one sided
Can any of us be really safe?

Tucked away with certainties
So uncertain this evening.
My thoughts are shattered,
Splinters threatening to cut what's left,
As though I've had nothing to stand on
All along.

But all this in less time than it takes
For you to repeat
That you want to love me,
And not as I thought you had said,
To leave me,
Thank God.
