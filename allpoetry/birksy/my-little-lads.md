My little lads,
are all I know,
are all I can touch,
are all I can hug,
they are the everything
I live for, I love for,
what will I do without
them?
I entrust them to the
person I trust most,
she will keep them safe,
raise them well, and I will
always be outside, trying
my best, always, but only,
trying my best.
