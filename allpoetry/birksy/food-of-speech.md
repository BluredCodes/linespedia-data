title: "Food of Speech"

The nameless people,
  have names after all,
They stare out of windows,
  or close their eyes.
But if given food of speech,
  they move, react, smile,
They are then more themselves,
  and the trip is easier still.
So take these nameless people,
  and offer them your hand,
Offer them your gifts, and they
  will offer theirs.
