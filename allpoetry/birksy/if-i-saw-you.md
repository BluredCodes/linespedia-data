---
title: "If I Saw You"
writer: "birksy"

If I walk along,
look to the sky
and see a bird,
I'd say, 'there's
a bird.'
but if I saw you,
I'd say nothing
just try and make
sure you saw me,
try and make
you smile, even
if you didn't
want to.
If I saw you
walking down the
road, I'd make
us meet, might
have been the
only chance we
got.
