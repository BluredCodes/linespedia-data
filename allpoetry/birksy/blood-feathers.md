title: "Blood Feathers"

I've collected every feather
I've seen on the ground
sleep so soft, you'd never
know those feathers are
sticking into me,
keep changing the sheets,
but I just bleed more.
And with the blood
I write the words,
a dozen times I've
stopped before, wondered
if this writing's right,
then just kept going
writing more.
So, you see, it's not
feathers I need, just
a little understanding,
not a man to be proud
of, just need a
little understanding.
