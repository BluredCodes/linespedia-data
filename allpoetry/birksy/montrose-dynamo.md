title: "Montrose Dynamo"

I draw my strength from you,
know they'll be enough
to keep you running though,
my Montrose dynamo.
However bad it gets, I only
think of you, of your words,
your tenacity, and I can stand
from my stoop again.
I will be strong, I will keep
my fight from faltering, will
not accept we may never meet,
will never be happy.
And when we do, I shall repay
all the energy drained, and then
keep on pouring more in, give
you anything, then everything.
