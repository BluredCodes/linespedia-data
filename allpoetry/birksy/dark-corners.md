title: "Dark Corners"

These dark corners,
they hide some,
but not all, movement.
I sit very still, catch a
glimpse of something,
as it scuttles, keeps to the
edges, sometimes pauses
overhead.
I feel it close, invades my
personal space, then
moves away.
I dare not speak it's name,
though I fear it will get
me soon.
Breath drawing shallow,
dreams hardly dreams
anymore, I try to hide up
high, in unknown lands.
Will I take the pressure?
My heart grows evermore
perplexed.
