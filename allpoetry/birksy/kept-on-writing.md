She saw something on the floor,
took it in for a second or two,
then kept on with her writing,
I look to see what it might be,
that stopped the pen mid-sentence,
but I am not allowed to see the pause.
Then when I stood to leave the train,
I tripped on something that was not there,
and I smiled because of the girl who just kept writing.
