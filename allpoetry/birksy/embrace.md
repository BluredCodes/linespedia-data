title: "Embrace"
writer: "birksy"

The couple, locked in melancholic embrace,
try to keep their cool, they face
a hard time ahead.

The workers shuffle chairs,
Noisily they scrape across the tiles,
Little care taken.

While staggering passengers amble through
swishing electronic doors, their eyes
distracted by boards, and still more arrive.

A phone breaks through the background noise,
On the other end any of a million people
waiting patiently.

Business men walk with their
precious cases, past the couple still
in close embrace, they cannot break.

Screens, all white and yellow and blue
show messages that go unread, logos
that go unseen.

In the floor dull reflections with
the scuff marks and cigarette ends
give away some of what’s happened.

And still as the life empties
leaving only space, the emotions for us all,
flow from that couple’s simple embrace.
