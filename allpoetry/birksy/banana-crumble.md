Banana crumble,
Mustn’t grumble,
Mustn’t curse, or groan, or gag,
Mustn’t hurl it on the floor,
Mustn’t drop it in my bag.

Banana crumble,
Feel it tumble,
Feel it slide into my chest,
Feel it drag upon the other stuff,
Which moves beneath my vest.

Banana crumble,
Mustn’t mumble,
I guess it could be worse,
I guess it could be rhubarb,
It is? Oh, no,
Best send me to the nurse.
