Here’s to Dean,
He can spell his name,
I’ll give him that,
And he’s travelled to
nearly every window, wall
and bridge from home to work.
You can’t fault his thoroughness,
Perhaps if redirected, Dean
would be an asset, instead
of spray can painting all that
does not move.
I smile, for I wonder who he
might really be,
a politician, or the man sitting
opposite me,
or more likely, the guard
who asks for my ticket,
I stretch to read his name,
and smile, again.
