title: "When I Was Your Everything"

When I was your everything,
nothing mattered to me,
I had nothing to be sad about,
or cry about, you lit my
life and that was all I knew,
all I needed, we had such
great fun, when I was
your everyone.
But now, it is not so, now
we barely talk, and when
I think of the time you
sat on the floor, to
name all my untitled poems,
it makes me cry, for that
was special beyond words.
How cruel of life to dull
our feelings, to force us
to understand our differences
over the experiences we shared.
I remember when I was your
everything, and that makes me
very, very, sad.
