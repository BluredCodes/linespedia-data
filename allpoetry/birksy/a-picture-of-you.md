title: "A Picture Of You"
writer: "birksy"

A picture of you,
can I take a picture of you?
I thought I’d ask,
before I do,
Can I take a picture of you?

A loch of your hair,
can I take a loch of your hair?
I thought I’d ask,
before I dare,
Can I take a loch of your hair?

A part of your heart,
can I take a part of your heart?
I thought I’d ask,
before I start,
can I take a part of your heart?

A touch of your hand,
can I take a touch of your hand?
I’d thought I’d ask,
before I land,
can I take a touch of your hand?
