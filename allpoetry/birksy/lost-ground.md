---
title: "Lost Ground"
writer: "birksy"

Eyes not on the road,
no road where I’m going,
not built them yet,
gonna get my shoes dirty,
hands won’t be clean anymore,
kneeling seems too good,
and standing? No, understanding,
but when I find my way,
want to put a stake in
the ground,
want to find a stake
to put in the ground,
want to find the ground.
