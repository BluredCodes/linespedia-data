title: "Used To Be Me"

Mud on his shoes,
smell of earth surrounds him,
and a look of pure distance
in his pale eyes,
we do not surround him,
he walks through his own world,
I watch him leave through the
carriage door, and do not
pity or envy his life independence,
I guess that he just used
to be me.
