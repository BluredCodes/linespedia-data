I'd like to look you in the eye,
take your hand, show you my
sincerity,
this game we play is no game
to me.
I want you to hear my speech,
how I sound, the way I can't
pronounce my 'R's,
I need you to see all the
imperfections,
that are hidden beneath these
words.
Sincerity is who I am, my
foundation, and this, as
what's built upon it,
is yours.
