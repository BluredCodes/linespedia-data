How many cars pass by? I look in
their windows but they look straight
ahead. I still think I’m here, but I can’t
really tell, no mirrors, you see.
If you pass by today, look up, give a smile
no need to wave, I can’t wave back.
But a smile will do, for it’s cold where
I stand, it’ll help, in its own little way.
