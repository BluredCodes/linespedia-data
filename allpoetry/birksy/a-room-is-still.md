title: "A Room Is Still"

A room is still,
green lamp shines
by itself in the corner,
shadows stretch on the
dusty floor,
what a state to be in.
Tick, tock, through the
hours; no one left
to find the scene,
outside just as empty
as within.
Birds should sing in the
morning, no sound now,
though there's daylight,
light shines on through
the day, through the night.
