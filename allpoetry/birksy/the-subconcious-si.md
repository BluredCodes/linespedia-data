title: "The Subconcious Si"

All those crazy thoughts,
they are the prelude to sleep,
disassociations merge, and
what's left makes a sweet
smile cross my tired face.
It is my subconcious trying to
break through, and it's
a crazy beast, runs riot
but rarely malevolently,
it entices me to slumber,
with its tempting analogies,
and it is hard to resist
my friend, my being,
the subconcious Si,
it's a pleasent place to be.
