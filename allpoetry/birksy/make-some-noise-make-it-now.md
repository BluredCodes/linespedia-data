title: "Make Some Noise, Make It Now"

Make some noise, make it now,
Make conversation, make a row,
Take a viewpoint, see what’s wrong,
Take a stance, and make it strong.

Make a friend, make them see,
Alone you’re you, but take two or three,
And soon you’ll bring a change of mind,
From single threads, a rope is bind.

Make them laugh, see it spread,
Watch the smile tip back the head,
Strip the willow, spin them round,
Sweep their feet, from off the ground.

Make a point, make them know,
You’ll take so much, then let them go,
Make a shape that keeps them near,
But leave a gap, make it clear.

Make a gesture, make a day,
Make another feel your way,
Make a meal, make a night,
Make the morn’ a fresh new sight.

Make your mark, make your life,
Make a lover, make a wife,
Make your noise, make sure they knew,
You proved the point, you saw it through.
