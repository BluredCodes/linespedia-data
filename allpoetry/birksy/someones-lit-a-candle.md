title: "Someone’s Lit A Candle"

Someone’s lit a candle
in the window, a heart shaped
one, a beacon of their
love and affection, to be seen
by everyone who passes.
It’s very sweet, a warm glow
lights inside me as I watch, it
warms places hard to reach.
Then, as I stretch to see it
go, I think I see the
candle touch the curtains that
surround it, and in a moment
they will start to burn, but
now it’s far from sight, and
I can’t be sure that what I
saw was real, and
within my heart, those lit
places slowly cool.
