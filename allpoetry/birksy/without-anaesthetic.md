The cupboards quite empty,
pulling out bit by bit, a
surgical removal, without
anaesthetic.
In a few weeks time, they'll
be nothing left, and it's
a wonder how I'll
stand.
Keep in the picture, never
fail to show them the
love I feel, oh, Sheri, I
hope you're right.
Got to hope it'll be
okay, got to hope
they'll love me,
too.
