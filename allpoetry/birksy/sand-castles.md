Didn't think I'd be sitting here,
Youth gave me views not founded on
reason or experience,
just a misplaced feeling of right and wrong.
But, like a castle of sand, my
views have been weathered away,
and I'm happy to turn one hundred and eighty
degrees, I'll eat my humble pie with cream.
Don't dig yourself in too deep today,
for life turns on a tarnished coin,
and things do change.
