I’m stuck, frozen in spasm
eyes fixed towards her, so
deep in the trance. I’ve come
to a standstill, there’s no-one
around me, I’ve leaped out of
life, and it’s the strangest of feelings.
She keeps walking, on and past,
listens to her everyone, sees no-one
but them all, keeps her eyes off of me.
Then, in spasm I am back here,
with the crowds back around me,
and she’s gone, and I’m
surrounded, alone.
