Can’t see any light, I know it
is there, am hanging a hope
that it'll all be okay, but still
it’s only a light, and in my
experience, bulbs can often
blow.

Can't wait for an opening, yet
I'm gonna be nervous, myself
on my own, is a decade unknown,
and I may be a man who can't
instead of those simple types that
can.

Can't see a guarantee, those
only come when a replacement
is certain, and I'm not so certain
that what I do will be anything
I can prove, everything can still fall
through.
