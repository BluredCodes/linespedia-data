She sits so quietly,
as quietly as she stands,
would like to ask her why,
there must be something on her mind,
but her silence is complete,
she really doesn’t want to talk,
lucky if I even get a smile.
Going to be a long trip,
if she’s never gonna speak,
still I can always
look at her instead,
and imagine what she
and me might say.
