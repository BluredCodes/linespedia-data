She looked, but
AWOL he'd gone,
arsehole had left,
and while she waved
a finger, she was
as scared as hell.
This is just the biggest
world, and to face
it on her own, she
curls up tight and lets
the sleep take, wake,
her home.
Then he asked to
return, and she thought
what would happen if
she was left alone,
and back he came, even
AWOL's beat the solitude.
