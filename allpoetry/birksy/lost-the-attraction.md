So I'm no longer here,
lost the attraction between
some days of yore, didn't
expect it, should've
expected it, no-one to
blame but myself.
I sip my beer, and tap
my keys, I hear myself,
and just hold back, what
a lovely boy I must be,
my spine just ran away.
Keep the chins up, it's
what I believe, but don't
be afraid to bend that same
chin, let it out a little,
just do it in some privacy.
So I'm no longer here,
no bars hold me back, or
do I lean on, I sit as
straight as ever, I am the
man who always has to smile.
