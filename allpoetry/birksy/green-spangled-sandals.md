Her green spangled sandals,
understated with over confidence,
light up the floor that till now
has only seen shades of brown
through black.

An interesting find, I say to myself,
something that might set her apart
from the misery found elsewhere.
Some people are obviously not right for
this place, and the green spangled
sandals, to me, are far too good.
