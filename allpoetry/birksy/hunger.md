Got a hunger inside of me,
ate some food, but it's still there,
ate some more, but it's still there,
slept all night, but it's still there.

And this hunger inside of me,
this morning it spoke to me, explained
what it wanted of me, still smiling
at the prospect.

Going to be hungry a while,
what it needs is far away, but it
won't go till she gets here, till
then, it'll feed on imagination.
