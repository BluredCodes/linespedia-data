Contentment,
Oh, what a word,
I whirl within twisters,
and see contentment outside,
as solid an object as
I could hope to see.

I'd shed a tear if
there was time, but
I'm spun around
again, then
black-out.

And then, as calm as
a summer breeze on
my neck, I awake,
and there you are,
and there I am, too,
both content.
