Oh, to quench, to drink
a fill, then drink again,
nothing in my way, no
barriers, no borders, no
people, to be in your arms,
by your side, to sleep, then
wake with you there, to
keep you in my thoughts,
you are a drink that I
simply can’t wait to
taste.
