There's a cyclist going downhill,
he has no main attraction,
not breaking any law,
freewheeling on the friction.
His head's upon his shoulders,
his feet upon the pedals,
his eyes are on the road,
his heart is somewhere else.
Don't miss him, he's too happy,
don't hit him, he's too happy,
freewheeling on his bicycle,
he hurries home from work.
