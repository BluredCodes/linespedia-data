My heart is your heart,
Tell me some of your
hopes and fears, and I will
gladly listen, for they are
inseparable.
Then, when you shut your
eyes, before a sleep takes
your thoughts, breathe deeply, and
imagine I am near,
then your troubles will be
truly shared.
