She wakes, with half moon eyes,
a beauty, such beauty,
I can look, so I look, but
nothing more, nothing more.

She stands, with great elegance,
a beauty, such beauty,
I can watch, so I watch, but
nothing more, nothing more.

She walks, with exquisite ease,
a beauty, such beauty,
I can hope, so I hope,
nothing more, nothing more.
