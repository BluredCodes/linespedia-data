title: "Everything And More"

He rests on her shoulders,
in her mind, in her words,
her smile is his smile.
She thinks in his teachings,
she reasons on his sayings,
she loves in his tender ways.
He was all to the young girl,
all, and more and more,
she still remembers.
When she speaks to her own girls
he’s right there by her side,
a man who was everything, and more.
