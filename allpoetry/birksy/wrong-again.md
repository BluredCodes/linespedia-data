Wrong again,
Sad again,
Trying to come to terms again,
Belief that I am who I said I'd be,
Is hard when life gives out on me.

Strong again,
Tough again,
Holding back the tears again,
And now the image I slowly see
Becomes apparent, just a reflection of me,
Alone.
