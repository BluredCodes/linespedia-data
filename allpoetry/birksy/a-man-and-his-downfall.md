A man and his downfall, are
easily found, he sits beneath
it, all sad, full of woe,
but that is just the surface,
as we all know, those men
don't really feel, they just
look after number one, watch
the outer workings, without
a thought of what goes on
inside.
Those men, they find their
downfalls, all smiles, and
look at me, they deserve all
the punishment they get,
...
don't they?
