---
title: "Worth The Climb"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "worth-the-climb"
source: "AllPoetry"

Grab the side,
...hand on hand,
Get me there,
...need your help,
Take my arm,
...feel your strength,
Get me back,
...hold me tight,
Sides are steep,
...views are great,
Take me there,
...calm my nerves,
What a view,
...worth the climb.
