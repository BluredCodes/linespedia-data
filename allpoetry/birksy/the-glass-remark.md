The glass remark was
never meant to be heard
by you.

It was whispered, to another,
back to where I thought
you sat.

Now I’m on a bench, cold
hands, cold heart, looking out
for you.

It was all my fault, I so
want to change those stupid
few words.

The glass remark was
never meant to be heard
by you.
