Your car is waiting, Mr. Newman
Are you ready for your ride?
I’ve brushed the seats, and you’ll
never guess what I found down the side?

So, where’ll we go, Mr. Newman?
Same location as before?
I’ll drive around, and come
back to get you at ten past four?

I know your story, Mr. Newman,
Should I tell the tabloid news?
I’d tell them all, and keep
the change from a relaxing little cruise.

But, your eyes are blank, Mr. Newman,
It looks like I’m too late,
But never fear, I’m sure
I’ll make a tidy profit from your fate.
