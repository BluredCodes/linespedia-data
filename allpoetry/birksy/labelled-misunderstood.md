I thought there was a sign here,
when I came past the other day,
it pointed clearly which way I
ought to go.
Now, it’s gone, I can see a hole
in the ground, some rotting wood,
a hole in my heart, now labelled
misunderstood.
All I can do is stop and wait,
I look around, hope I can see
something, maybe someone will
come by, maybe they’ll know the way.
