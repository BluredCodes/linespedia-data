There’s a breath of air, can
you feel it curve between us?
We’ll touch but never here,
there’s a motion set between us.
The smiles have fallen down,
concentration all we know,
keep it all within, though I’ll
never know how.
We’ll keep this sullen pact,
furnace melts the rock and lead, can’t
cool the sudden fire, no sweat
has sweeter taste.
If we should go right now,
full of ritual, full of passion,
you’ll be my lasting thought,
the ever present crown.
