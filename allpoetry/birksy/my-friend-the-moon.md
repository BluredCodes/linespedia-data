title: "My Friend, The Moon"
writer: "birksy"

My friend, the moon,
I have known him all my
life, he is regular,
dependable,
beautiful,
if he could answer
me, what would
he say,
what could possibly
be the answer to now?
I am pulled and
pushed like the
tides he controls,
it's hard to keep
on my feet.
The moon, a
friend, dependable.
