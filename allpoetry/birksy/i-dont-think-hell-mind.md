I don’t think he’ll mind,
it’s been annoying him as well,
has tutted every time,
it played its tune.
Come on, before he gets back,
open your hand, release
it to its freedom, back
to mobile phone land,
and as it harshly falls,
it calls just one more
time, then silence,
great silence, I resume
my rhyme.
