title: "Sitting on the End"

See the lady at the end,
Her eyes flick from one friend to the next,
She seems quieter,
The need for companionship
higher,
When all around her are still two.

Is there any hope that
soon,
She will not be
the one on the end, but a lady
in the middle, and the table seats
six,
not five.
