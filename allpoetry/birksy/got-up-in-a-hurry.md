Got up in a hurry,
something I said, maybe
something I didn’t say?
Where’s your destination
going far away, had
enough of this view,
brand new goals to pursue?
Can’t blame you for that,
it’s a cold grey morning,
would join you if I
could, but, instead, I
stay right here, in the wake
you left.
