Feeling vulnerable, we
seek the reassurance,
ask the questions that
can only be answered
one way, we kid
ourselves of the feelings
with endangered eyes.

And when the camera’s
go, when we’re all
left alone, it’s these
silences that give
the game away, we
separate to corners,
that will never meet.
