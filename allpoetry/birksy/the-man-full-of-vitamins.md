title: "The Man Full of Vitamins"

I’d really like to say
That the man full of vitamins
Is fully wide awake
Can see it all, and 
Answer every question first,
Or maybe, just a small thing,
Could he be a little quieter,
As we make our way to work.
But I fear my rest is lost
As with each motion left or right,
Those tiny letters rattle in his case.

So when I sidle past,
I’ll make sure the cherub sleeps,
Then knock loudly on the window while
the train is pulling out,
that way he’ll know I meant it,
he’ll know I knew his stop.
