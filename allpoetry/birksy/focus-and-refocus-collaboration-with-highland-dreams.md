Focus, I may not be
here, but you’ll still
see my eyes behind the
words, my thoughts behind
the lines, my soul within
the poem.
Focus, I may not be here,
but a piece of me
will forever be before you.

~

Focus, I may not always 
be with you, but I 
leave my heart behind 
in words, my thoughts behind 
the lines, my soul within 
the poem. 
Focus, I may not be there, 
but a piece of you 
lives in my heart.
