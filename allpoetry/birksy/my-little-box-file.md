title: "My Little Box File"

In my little box file, sits
three hundred and fifteen cards,
and each one has a little word
for you.
No matter how I write them,
eyes shut, half cut, within
delicate little mittens, they
are all for you.
Within this web, you are a web,
and if I can't find the silken
strand that leads directly to you,
I shall find another way.
In my little box file, sits
three hundred and fifteen little
words for you, and I wonder, oh,
I wonder, what it means...
