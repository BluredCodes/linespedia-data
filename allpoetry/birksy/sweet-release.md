title: "Sweet Release"
writer: "birksy"

Sweet release,
come ease my pain,
these times are
hard, and though
salvation is near, it
is not yet near enough.
Sweet release,
you make me smile,
and my soul is calmed
for moments, letting
other thoughts come
through.
Sweet release,
you are a friend,
my constant, if quiet
companion, perhaps
soon I’ll get to
share you.
