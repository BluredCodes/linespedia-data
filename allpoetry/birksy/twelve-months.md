---
title: "Twelve Months"
writer: "birksy"

Don't leave it now,
there's another year
to fear, another twelve
months that can be spent
quietly, darkly, without
a chance of finding a way out.

She said words to me,
the seeds of thought that
she knew she would, then walked
away, let them grow, until, at last
I reaped what she sowed, and I can see
straight through her, and she doesn't like that.

Feeling better by the minute,
twelve months now to look forward to,
she'll not be looking back.
