I don't have a car, nor a bike,
just a bison, and it gets me
from the A to the B.
People don't beep, or shout
from their cars, for fear
I may just set it free.

The bison, my friend,
it knows what to do, waits until
green, overtakes any queue,
It's a great mode of transport,
for me and for you, wouldn't
you just love a bison, too?
