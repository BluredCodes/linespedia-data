There’s a bird who hates this train,
he flies high above, each 
weekday at eight, trying
so hard to avenge the poor fate
of his children.
And that’s all he does,
swoop low at the beast,
cannot possibly do anything
to hurt it, at least,
not without help.
And he’ll never avenge what
happened, he’ll just fly high
above the train till he dies,
knowing that he did all that
he could to win.
