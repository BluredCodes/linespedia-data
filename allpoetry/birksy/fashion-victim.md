Her bag comes from Paris,
it seems proud of the fact, and
her phone is a companion, she
can’t put him down. She eats
apples for breakfast, I suspect
she flosses her teeth, here
comes perfect girl, the perfect
victim for fashion.
Her hair rests just so, her
boots shiny clean, flares just
flared enough, poise simply
supreme, she reads the ‘in’
books, she can speak well at
parties and farts sweet perfume,
the distressed maiden
of haute couture.

I guess some either have it,
and some either not,
what a strange little life
it must be.
