Yeah, all right, sweetheart,
I’ll be straight home,
pop into the office first,
clear my desk and trays,
Not going to be there any more,
don’t need that sort of hassle,
Decided I’ll go somewhere else,
The Seychelles sounds nice.

Yeah, all right sweetheart,
egg and chips for tea,
sounds great, I suppose,
but start without me,
Not going to be there any more,
Had enough of happy lies,
Going to move somewhere else,
Anywhere else sounds nice.

What’s that, sweetheart?
You won’t be at home?
You’ve found somewhere else to go?
Now you’ve found the courage?
I don’t understand, sweetheart,
Where are you going to be?
Surely I didn’t just hear you say,
The Seychelles?
