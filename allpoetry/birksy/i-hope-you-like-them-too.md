---
title: "I Hope You Like Them, Too"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "i-hope-you-like-them-too"

In truth these simple syllables,
(the most have four per word)
are for no-one but myself,
but if others feel a need,
they comment, at times inanely,
and at others with a genuine insight, far
more than what I’d hoped.

But in the end, when electronic
ink is dry, and all the thoughts
have been sourly distilled,
these simple lines,
exist for no-one but myself,
although, I have to say, (in hushed tones)
I hope you like them, too.
