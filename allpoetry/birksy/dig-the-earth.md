---
title: "Dig the Earth"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "dig-the-earth"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dig the earth, breathe the
stench from people’s soiled breath.
Move the unnecessary, shred
the unkempt, stain the thoughts
long buried.

Burn the corpse, no longer covered,
waste no moments, let them lie, no
need to show them, the lights not clear,
Depress the skull to it’s lowest form.

Love thy neighbour, respect thy ancestor,
keep thy prying hands to thyself,
you’re a burglar, you’re a menace, you’re
a stealer of death throes, you
prolong each suffering.

Be brave, unlock the cage,
bring me in with an hour to spare,
you may not like what you find,
but then it’s not about you,
or me.
