Limitless,
Don’t stop here,
Resting is the easy part,
The toil is in the pushing,
The thinking, the creating,
We are all capable of our best,
But few take it a little further,
Too few are the masters.

Keep your spirits high,
When threatened with depression,
Dissolve the mortal fears,
Explode the here and now,
Come, take up the pen,
It needs you more today,
Wants to write your words,
Before they slip away.
