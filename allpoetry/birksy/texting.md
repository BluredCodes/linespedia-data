Texting his girlfriend,
shoulders hunched forward,
a man with no plan,
for anyone but himself.
He feels the nerves, hands
shake, as he finds her name,
presses send, funny how it’s
so similar to his wife’s.
But, too late, it’s gone,
and he knows what
will happen, technology’s
just the best invention.
And those shoulders, now
they hunch up, and he cries
a few tears, but don’t be sad,
they’re for no-one but himself.
