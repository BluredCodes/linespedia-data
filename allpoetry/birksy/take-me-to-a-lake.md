Take me to a lake,
roll our mat upon the ground,
then lay down next to me,
watch the clouds as they slowly float
one day we’ll be there,
come slip your hand into mine,
there'll be no more rain,
just two clear hearts
laying neath a sky.

Then when eve arrives we'll go
drink some wine to toast ourselves,
say whatever is on our minds
and within these souls of peace.
Then come with me on threads so soft
lay 'pon our future bed
and with window open,
we'll sit and watch the stars
