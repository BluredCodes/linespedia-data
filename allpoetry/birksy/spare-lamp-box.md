title: "Spare Lamp Box"

Let me in the spare lamp
box, want to know what
its locked door hides,
for there is no light
that those lamps could be
spare for.

There's a reason for
everything, that can
only be the truth, but
I see no point for the
spare lamp box,
I don't believe it's
real.
