title: "Next Move Is Yours"

Living in cartoon worlds,
each picture a drama,
no blue skies, no
reconciliation, just
step into the next
scene, say whatever
comes into your head,
you know the next pane
it'll all be in history.
I reach my hand in, let
you see it, won't make
you do anything you don't
want to do.
But what's important is that
you understand, I've said
I'd be there, offer more
than this hand, and that
it is me who is waiting
for your next move.
