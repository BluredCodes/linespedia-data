Sitting still in half light,
catching up with words,
he concentrates, hard,
he plays with what he’s
doing, first this way,
then that, he wants
to know more, yet it
all’s gone ahead of him,
but slowly he finds
the way, and then, it
comes to him, and he
can work again.
