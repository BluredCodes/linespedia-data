The long promise,
I hear it,
feel it on my
face, but it’s
not yet caught, I
take my time, choose
my words, so I won’t
scare you, need you
whole, not in sets
of pieces.
There is a tie, made
from silken rope,
and on it, sits that
promise, from my
heart to yours. Do
you feel it tugging?
Do you want to
know it’s there?
A silken promise,
woven just for you.To My Friend
