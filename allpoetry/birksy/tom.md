On a wet, windy, day
in May, a
stray by the name of
Tom, strode in.

A swift dip in the tub,
Quick rub, the stub of his tail
went so fast
I thought it might fly off.

Now he lays at my feet,
Quite sweet, the heat of his fur
Keeps us both warm,
On those wet, windy days, in May.
