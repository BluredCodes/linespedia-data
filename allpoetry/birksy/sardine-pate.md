Sardine pate,
won't be too long,
can't think of moving.
What has that man got
to make him look like that?
Oh, maybe that's his normal look,
I've just never been this close to him.
Don't want to be again.

Sardine pate,
sounds bad in a sandwich,
much worse in a carriage.
Can't wait to leave,
to ride on the bike,
so much space to exploit,
no more people around me,
I'll eat tuna all day.
