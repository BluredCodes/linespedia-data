Mr and Mrs Axe Murderer,
Sit and smile opposite,
Their teeth just don’t seem real,
Their hands move all the time.

I doubt that when they look my way,
They can see anything of me at all,
Just a shell, as I must see them,
The man behind who I hide.

Their baggage looks long and heavy,
And I hope that it won’t drop down,
There were times when I would not have noticed them,
But times have changed.

Then, without warning, they move
them and their luggage gone,
and as they leave they look at me,
as if I might be mad,
as if I was the one
with all the axes.
