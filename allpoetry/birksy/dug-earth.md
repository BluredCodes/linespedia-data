A fresh hole in the graveyard,
Could fit any one of us,
Yet not my turn today.

Dug earth in the graveyard,
A cat sits at its top,
Looks just past my shoulder.

New stone in the graveyard,
No words on it yet,
Waiting full of menace.

Other people in the graveyard,
Some of them friends,
I wait now in solace.
