---
title: "Imperfect Souls"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "imperfect-souls"
source: "AllPoetry"

He leaves her, taking
flowers, taking himself,
no words spoken,
not even a solitary look,
as the train stops at the station.
Window down, door swung open,
confident step onto concrete,
eyes focused down,
they hide their workings well.

Then they fall, all those
petals, and they blow
in the chilled wind, no
chance to catch them,
all is lost.
At last, they both look,
with some guilt, with some
feeling,
their perfect plans, falling
foul of imperfect souls.
