They used a green folder, passing from
one to the other, in secret meetings where
no-one would know.

They’d say very little, the words saying
it all, it stood on a shelf right under
their nose.

Together they loved without the touch
of the skin, the burning desire lay hidden
so long.

And then, when they went, left the
country they knew, they looked for
that folder, but that had gone, too.
