title: "I’ll Smile"

Caught up in my coat,
run round and round,
the modern day net,
of string ties and Velcro.
Sit still in a heap,
Maybe they’ll pity me,
give me some cash,
and, unable to reach it,
I’ll smile.Almost a true story - I got out in the end!
