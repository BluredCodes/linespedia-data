While I wait, the knots
start to form, it’s been a
while, though in truth,
not long.
She’s a strong lass, a
real saint in-between
us all, she gives me the
time I need.
And when the space is
in front, she’s sat behind
the rocks, holding fast
holding strong.
Friendships, not really
good at those, but I
like to try, let me be
a friend.
Then she’s back, my
friend from afar, she’s
a girl who’d open all
our eyes.
