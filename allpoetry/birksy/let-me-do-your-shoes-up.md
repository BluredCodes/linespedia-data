title: "Let Me Do Your Shoes Up"

Let me do your shoes up,
place the coat on your shoulders just right,
walk you slowly to the door, step
out with you, tonight.
Don't have to go somewhere nice,
round the block would be fine,
or further if you'd like to,
know this place that'd be divine.
Won't take us long to get there,
throw away the map in your hand,
no need to ask me questions,
one kiss, you'll understand.
We step back in the hallway,
take the coat and I'll place it here,
slip the shoes off, creep the stairs,
we'll feel less awkward once we're there.
