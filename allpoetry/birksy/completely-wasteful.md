title: "Completely Wasteful"

Completely wasteful, smiling buckets
from ear to ear, can’t stop me losing.
Takes work to win, don’t want to work,
want to enjoy something of where I sit.
Shut the eyes, till dawn prises them
open, and look through cracked curtains
as days come and wave, then move away.
Not here for the future, forgotten the past,
keep my smiles for the outside, I live for
my lighter side.
