I’m learning this lesson all
the time, and it just keeps
pulling me through,
for every down, it gives
an up-side, for every frown
I find a smile.

Keep going the same way
to work, though, in truth,
I go a little further each day,
experience is only reaped
when acted upon, I hope
I get my chance soon.
