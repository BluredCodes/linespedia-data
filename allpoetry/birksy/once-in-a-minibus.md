Once, in a minibus,
I fell asleep, or at least
pretended to, a ploy
to see what may happen next.
Suzanne, who sat so close to me,
slumbered too, two phoney’s
in the minibus that day.
And we touched, just so slightly,
first our knees, then shoulders,
fingertips, then hands.
And we smiled, whilst we slept,
partners in crime, for once,
just a nice memory.
