Listed on her hand,
1, 2, 3 of what to do,
dare not miss a task.

1.
Shop tonight, take some
time, make it right.
2.
Long bath tonight, take some
time, make it right.
3.
Meet him tonight, take some
time, make it right.

Listed on her hand,
1, 2, 3 of what to do,
not blank, like mine.
