Other people’s words,
Sometimes all I hear,
Never quite my own.
Want to shut them out,
Whistle while they burn,
Fingers in my ears.

I wonder what they say,
Never stop to listen,
Can’t be very much.
Let me talk today,
Promise I won’t mean,
Half of what I speak.
