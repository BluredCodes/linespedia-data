Feeling lonely as a tree
stood tall in a field of grass,
been here before, when I
was as young as a boy.
Now I’m old, and it’s back
again, hope I make it
through again, hope there’s
something more to smile
about. 
Won’t win no prizes this
time, not unless something
changes, stood still in a
field of grass, very alone.
