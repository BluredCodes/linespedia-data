This compromise, words over speech
is terribly strange, like you’re not really there,
amorphous, to a degree, but nice, as well.
Communication is king for the afternoon,
and, stilted, knowledge begat interest, begat
care.
Listen, I can’t hear you, save those plings,
that mark the ends of phrase, the end of days.

Keep compromise, keep silent moods,
We’ll end up knowing nothing that is
not written, and sometimes that is best.
