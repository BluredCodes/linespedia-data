title: "Many Who Don’t"
writer: "birksy"

It sits in a bag, neither
right nor wrong, just there
having grown inside them.
It’s part of them, though
we’re not preached that, to us
it’s a disease sent to kill.
So, when there’s no choice,
it has to stay, try to be proud,
be strong that you’ve had a life.
Think of those many who don’t.
