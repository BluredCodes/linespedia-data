Absolute dedication,
he cannot be faltered,
he might be asleep, with
both his eyes open,
if it were not for the hand,
that every so often
turns the next page, then
falls in his lap.
He’s reached the last
page, perhaps he will smile,
or whoop, or even dance a
little jig, but no, he
does not, instead with a
flick of the wrist,
starts the book over
again.
