Look at me again,
Pass your eyes my way, maybe
falter your step, or with one corner
of your mouth, remember why I’m here.

I know it’s been some time since
you first saw me, and I have
lost all my uniqueness, but aren’t there still
memories that I hold for you?

Touch me please, a small price to
pay, a large reward to give for
my individual attention. I sit now
disregarded, and ever so alone.
