Leant over a table,
she cries, tears fall on
unpaid bills, lit by
light she cannot afford.
Not enough just to
get by anymore,
have to have to best
of all worlds.
Hear the credit sing
the blues, too late an
hour for change.
What’s she going to do?
Please, but pleas are not
enough. Our currency
trumps all. Including
us.
