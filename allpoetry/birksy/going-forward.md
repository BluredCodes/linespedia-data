Going forward, can't
remember the last time
I did that, perhaps
it means something,
but I won't be so
confident, will be
made to eat my words,
will be meek and mild
for a while.
And I'm just a lad
who doesn't need
to be hurt anymore,
ruled anymore, told
what to do anymore.
I'll move forward, but
with a word thrown in,
cautiously.
