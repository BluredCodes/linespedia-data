This isn’t what you think, you
give it to me, and it must have hurt,
but there’s nothing but air inside,
a few empty thoughts shaped into
a rhyme, nothing to hide, it’s just
something I wrote.

You need no excuses, it’s me who’s
the enemy, who’ll never be thought
well of again. Don’t bring me false
ammo, don’t see into something
that is perfectly clear, there is
no-one to blame but myself.
