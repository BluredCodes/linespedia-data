---
title: "Drawn"
writer: "birksy"

Almost together,
drawn from infinite places,
moulded so perfectly that
they are one.
Movement, their movement,
breathing, their breathing,
heartbeat, their heartbeat.
slow motion, full motion,
holding on till they're
forced apart, exploding
silently in the dark,
then slowly drawn
again.
