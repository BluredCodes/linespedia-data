That's what I need,
a rescue system,
a hand out of
nowhere to pull me up,
perhaps a bridge across
a canyon, or a barge across
a loch, something that'll take
me all the way.
And in the distance, I can hear it
faint, but getting louder, know it's
coming my way.
A little rescue system, who can offer
all the hope, and without thought, I start
to move towards it, too.
