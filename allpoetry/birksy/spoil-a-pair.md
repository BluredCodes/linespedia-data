title: "Spoil A Pair"

The pigeon coed once a
second, I sat and listened
for a while, I doubt he
knew he kept time so well.
Then the train arrived, I heard
him stop, saw him fly, he
seems a bird that knows too
much, about my lonely day.
Then that night, he was on the
roof, I heard him scrabble
with his singular life, we
don't spoil a pair, I guess.

Coo, coo.
