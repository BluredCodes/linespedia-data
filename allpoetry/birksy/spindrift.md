Caught in the spindrift,
she teases the hope from me,
whets my appetite, and I
could walk to her, set
out upon water and
keep my head above it,
so long as she speaks,
keeps asking how I am,
perhaps makes me
feel a million dollars,
with such splendid
simple words.
I'm caught in her
spindrift, and I
so like the
feeling.
