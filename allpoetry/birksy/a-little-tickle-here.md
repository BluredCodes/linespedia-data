A little tickle here,
but I'm not ticklish anymore,
hold my face straight as a line,
let the crooked steal behind
let me turn my face and cry
forever you should be mine,
but they can't hear,
my despair is only
for my ears,

A little tickle here,
but it's only you
I want.
