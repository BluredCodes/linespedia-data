She nudges him with her elbow,
just friendly, without any thought but
‘Hey! Look at this’, though on
his face you can see he feels every
Moment of that brief touch, feels
her softness, her tiny hairs on the tanned arm,
feels the cool skin, and it makes him
want to scream, "I love you!" but
he cannot, because they are best of friends.
