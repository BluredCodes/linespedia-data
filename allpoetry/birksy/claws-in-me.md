---
title: "Claws In Me"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "claws-in-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37219-Claws-In-Me-by-birksy"
---

Damn! I feel your claws,
your hands they hold my
heart, my wrists, I cannot
yet break free, cannot yet
know if it is me who is the
foolish one.
Twisted, this way and that,
they’ll be no piece of me
as large as an acorn, fallen
on a bed of leaves that used
to be so full of belief, yet now
are simply wet.
Yet I do not tear myself away,
cannot bring myself to stand
and bravely walk the other
way, I want your voice, somehow
it’s now all I need, yet it is
not to be.
