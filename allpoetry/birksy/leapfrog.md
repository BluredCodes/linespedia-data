The children play leapfrog, and
their parents look on with sad hearts. Time's
running away with them, and they wonder
where they've been for so long.
Outside they whoop and run around, while
friendly neighbours smile at conjured memories.
And then, when their children tire, they come in
to relieve their parents from
grandchildren asleep in their old, wise arms.
