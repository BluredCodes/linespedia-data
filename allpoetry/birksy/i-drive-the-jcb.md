I drive the JCB
that digs the holes
that the boxes go in,
the boxes that we go in,
when we go.
There wasn’t a course
for this sort of work,
just fell into it, if
you pardon the pun.
And I don’t really mind,
rewards are but few, and
a really nice hole is
a pleasure to me.
So I drive the JCB, I don’t
ask for tips, just quietly
move the earth to, then fro,
then quietly walk home.
