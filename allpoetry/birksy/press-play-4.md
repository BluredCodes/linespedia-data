You want me to go?
You can't live with me?
Get a grip, girl,
we're living separate lives,
no interactions,
no intersections,
we share nothing, but nothing.
Of course, the truth is
my independance, my
new-found right to say no,
to tell you what I think,
it's an irritant you can't
scratch anymore.
I'm not going anywhere,
not until we all move out,
gonna be with my kids
as long as I can, what
a shame you won't
get your way this
time.
Press pause, my choice
this time.
