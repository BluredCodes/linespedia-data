title: "Do It While You Can"
writer: "birksy"

Do it while you can,
won’t get a second chance,
don't stand there on your own,
ask that pretty girl for the
last dance.
Help another’s cause,
maybe save another’s life,
savour the applause,
calm another’s strife.
Do it while you can,
be a while before another chance,
Go on, take the floor,
ask that pretty girl for one
last dance.
