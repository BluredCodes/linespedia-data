title: "Me Gams"

Laughing on top,
shivering underneath,
keep the hands steady.
If this mirror is working,
how come sometimes I can’t smile.
Keep the camera clicking glossy shots,
it’s nice to look back – nice to picture
where and when we could have been.
But that’s quite enough,
Put them away now,
All’s done, and
it’s time to
go home.
