---
title: "The Children On The Beach"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "the-children-on-the-beach"

The children on the beach,
they stand almost in a row,
but now the sun's going down,
they're tired, can do
little more than make footprints
that the wet sand slowly devours.
They know that home is nigh,
that a day of laughter and
adventure must be followed
by that of calm and sleep,
but still they test nature with
a fleeting move and run, a
peel of screams, splashing
cool water, a friendly slap
on the back.
Parents stand on the promenade,
they talk and watch, too, happy
that their children are happy,
and then in turn, with lazy
motion, they signal them
back for tea.
