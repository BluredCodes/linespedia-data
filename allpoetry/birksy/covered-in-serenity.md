I watch her walk, at once my leaden heart beats
and all those faults I remember making,
Sting my body.

She moves as she always does,
covered in serenity, swallowed in her world
where the horizon never changes.

I’d like to join her there, free from the gravity,
Gently strolling by,
past all her dangers.
