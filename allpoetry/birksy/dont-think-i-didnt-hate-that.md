title: "Don't Think I Didn't Hate That"
writer: "birksy"

Didn't want to go, but I'm
clever enough to know that
it was a test, if I had said
the wrong thing, it would
have been worse.
Course, you were on the
losing end, don't think I
didn't hate that, won't
be happy till I change that
way of life.
Can't wait to have you back
with me, you will come, won't
you? Not even going to think
you leave, not now I've
found you.
You are the one I want for,
the only one I think about,
the only one I now let in,
with me.
