Jack and Jill,
sat on the hill,
drinking pints of water,
Jack said “Jill,
here’s one more pill,
We’ll live happily ever after.”

Up sat Jill,
And stayed quite still,
“Do you think one more would matter?”
Jack said, “No,
Just have a go,”
Then rolled with silent clatter.
