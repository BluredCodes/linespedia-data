Oh, God, I made that casserole,
though Mum cooked most of
it (before going out),
and I stuck that rose under the table
with a lot of sticky tape,
how corny it must have been.
I didn’t even ask if she liked the meal,
before I showed her the flower.

Yeah, I know it’s incredibly cheesy,
but it’s a nice memory to have,
and in many ways it’s a shame,
I won’t get to do it again.
