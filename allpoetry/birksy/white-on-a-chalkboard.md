White on a chalkboard,
exclamation from an unknown
night, can’t see what
it says, can’t read
the emotions.
But the rain does its
job, almost gone
while I watch,
nature’s a wonderful
girl.

White on a chalkboard,
means nothing today,
it’s vanished tomorrow.Bit of a strange one - don't think I was in full flow. Feels a bit forced.
