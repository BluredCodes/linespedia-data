---
title: "Here's My Name"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "heres-my-name"

Here's my name, I'll let you
keep it, wrap it up, keep it
for a time when no-one's there,
then unfold it, say it, believe
that I can hear each syllable
as you speak.
Then I'll say yours, too, don't
know how far these atoms move,
maybe they'll collide, know
they'll bring a smile whatever
they have the chance to do.
Now fold me up once again, and
if you think you want me, hold
me to your heart, let my name
sit upon it, let you know how
it feels, how yours feels within
my own.
Here's my name, keep it safe,
hold it close.
