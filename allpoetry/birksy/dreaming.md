On the sofa, I see you,
watch you, try and feel you,
smile when you smile, too.
Then follow you upstairs, watch you
undress, watch you sleep the
long night through.
And though you may not know it,
least not yet, anyhow, while you dream
I'll be right there, dreaming along with you.
