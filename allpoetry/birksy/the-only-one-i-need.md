Daddy, can you go under the sea?
Can you fly high in the air?
Can you listen to me?

I hold my teddy close, talk and throw it around,
Cause as much disruption, make as much sound,
It hasn’t got a name, doesn’t need one, you see,
My dog is my friend, the only one I need.
