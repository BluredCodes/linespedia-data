---
title: "Tumble Down"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "tumble-down"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52110-Tumble-Down-by-birksy"
---

I see lines, they
are random in
angle, length and
placement, though
they draw no strength,
nor give power, just
merely exist, under these
eyes so weary.

They will not give me hope,
do not signify
will not lead to a
better life, they are
just there in my mind,
holding back memories,
like marbles within a
child's game, keep them
high above this troubled
conscious of mine.

Without them, I don't
know who I'll be, so just
be careful when you meet me,
can't tell just how you'll
find me, won't take much
jogging to make them
tumble down.
