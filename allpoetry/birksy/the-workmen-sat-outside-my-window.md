title: "The Workmen Sat Outside My Window"

The one that looks like a toad
rolls down the window, grins out
like an idiot, maybe
he’s seen a fly he can eat, maybe
he just needs to stretch his legs.

I thought they were statues, or just
playing dead fishes, but they do
really move, both arms and both legs,
in fact when the van comes, they
near enough run.

But there they are now, just sitting
and smoking, lungs full of dust,
full of tar, and red stuff, if tomorrow
they’re there, I’ll knock on the window,
just to make sure they’re alive.
