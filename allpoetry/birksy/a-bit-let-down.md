title: "A Bit Let Down"
writer: "birksy"

Feel a bit let down,
thought friends would help me out,
thought family wouldn’t think twice,
I wouldn’t have thought twice,
yet I’ve been let down,
and it quite strips away
the outside layers,
leaves me sore and bleeding,
licking wounds, and feeling
lonely as I can be.
Best I move, get up,
fight back a little, got
to keep going for myself,
prove to no-one but me
just how resourceful I can
make myself be,
make them see
I will carry on, even though
they let me down a bit.
