It’s slowly burning out,
Won’t be anything left tomorrow,
Hardly anything left tonight,
I wish I had more hope,
But it’s long gone for hope.
No more threads for us to cut,
Almost at the end now,
Can’t think of much else to say,
Keeping eyelids halfway shut,
I want to dream of good things,
Knowing that I can’t.
