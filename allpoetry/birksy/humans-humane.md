---
title: "Human’s Humane"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "humans-humane"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35355-Human-s-Humane-by-birksy"

Amazing the way they part for an
ambulance, when someone’s hurt
they’re as nice as cherry pie,
soon as it goes, though, back
to the fighting, waving of
fingers, words that mean
nothing.
How sad that someone has to be hurt,
to make us human’s humane.
