---
title: "Talking and Snoring"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "talking-and-snoring"

What's this I can hear in the dark,
Words trying to become sentences
Under extreme pressure.
And then I remember,
That it's not me they're aimed at,
But to your visionary subconscious,
Playing theatre inside that beautiful head of yours.

I dare not answer back,
In fear of waking your slumber,
Causing both the worlds to fall into confusion.
Instead I just smile,
And know its just another part of you I love,
Another part which brings me pleasure
At night.

I'll keep your sleep talking till tomorrow,
When you'll smile at me as I explain,
Your weird and wonderful mutterings.
Then you can complain about my snoring,
Letting me know that you enjoy our time as well,
Just talking and snoring,
Together.This is just a personal poem that makes me smile. No more - no less.
