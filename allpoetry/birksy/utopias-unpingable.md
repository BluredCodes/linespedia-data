Utopia's unpingable,
I'm sure it was there just yesterday,
Sure, it took time, took an age or an era,
But if you waited, it came back,
Came back to here.

Now it's disappeared,
Been left off the network,
Communication severed,
Maybe it's just changed it's
Name, I'll try yours instead.
