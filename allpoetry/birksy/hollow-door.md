So soon the hollow door,
my hand goes out, and
through I go, it’s a place
that’s gonna hurt so hard.
And there’s nothing that
I know, that’ll prepare me
for the other side, I just
close my eyes, and hide.
And when I’m there, look
back, what will I see, can
I live so free, with everything
that has gone before. I hate
myself, and hope that’ll
they’ll hate me.
