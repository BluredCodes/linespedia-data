title: "Heart In My Hands"
writer: "birksy"

Got my heart in my hands,
it fell off my sleeve,
got covered in sand,
that just made me sneeze,
got my heart in my hands,
it's all goey and stuff,
but it's all I can offer,
do you think it's enough?
