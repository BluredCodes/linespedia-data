title: "Beep, Beep"

There’s a beep from a machine,
Can hear it from far away,
Not getting any nearer,
Not getting any clearer.
I wonder what it means,
the beep, beep of the machine,
I heard it all day yesterday,
I’ve heard it all day today.
I guess it’s only good,
Beep, beep, goes the machine,
If only they could tell me,
What it really means.
Can’t remember how I got here,
Bandages over my eyes,
And without the use of arms and legs,
The beep, beep, is no surprise.
