Little Tommy wears long sleeve
shirts. At the back he sits, alone,
and works so hard, every question if not right,
answered exquisitely wrong.

Little Tommy holds his favourite teachers
hand a little too hard, but she says nothing.
Not a moment does he leave her, once
he wet himself.

Little Tommy needs love to
keep him going, when people
ask him questions, he defensively
stands and loves his parents.

Little Tommy’s got a sick note,
he may even know why,
but when will he break,
Some wars aren’t worth the winning.
