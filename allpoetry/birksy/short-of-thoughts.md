---
title: "Short Of Thoughts"
writer: "birksy"

I've nearly finished the task,
the drop won't be so bad, I'll
dangle the ties, now tied up
in a rope, from the top of
the sill, I'm sure they'll
take my weight, don't really
matter if they don't, the fall
might be the best option, least
I won't have to worry any more.

All these old ties, never thought
they'd be any use, but I
kept them safe anyway, stored up in
a closet, rarely opened or looked
at, but now that I need them, they've
come to my rescue, and they're not
much on their own, but put all
together, they offer some hope, to
a man short of thoughts.
