I offer my palms, give 
me yours, too, we will 
hold on together, we'll 
say, me and you.

.In the palm of your hands 
.Rests the fate of my heart 
.Treat it very tenderly 
.Lest it break apart

See my blue eyes, they
rest only on you, what
is behind us, is soon
out of view.

.See my brown eyes, 
.the windows to my soul 
.Seek all that’s within 
.and make me feel whole.

I hold my hand to your
chin, bring your lips to mine,
feel the strength of within,
a warmth for our time.

.Touch upon my mouth 
.With your soft lips 
.Savour my body 
.With your fingertips.

When morning arrives, I
shall stay here with you,
never alone now, where
there was one, now two.

.When the morning sun rises 
.As I’m lying next to you 
.I’ll know just what happiness is 
.where there was one, now two.
