Press play again,
it's none of their business,
what you talk about is none
of their business.
I thought we made a deal
long ago, in fact I know we
did, yet you forget it now;
I haven't.
We both know what it means,
it's a step down to the ground,
those same steps that once
we so quickly rushed up, or
was I being pushed? I can't tell.
We tread the steps, and watch the
floor ascend, it was hard to
soften the impact,
though maybe it was already
soft for some,
press pause, this is all
too depressing.
