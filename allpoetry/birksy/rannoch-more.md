Listen, for the clouds whisper,
they say Rannoch, Aberfeldy, Pitlochry,
they say Oban, Inverness and Aviemore, too.
Will you listen, they sing loud,
they keep their heads held so high,
which, in turn, keeps us all, fighting
through.
And when the salmon leap, when the
water falls in pools, when the snow
lies in cracks in the rocks,
I’ll hear you, hear it all, keep
on smiling, through and through,
listen, for the clouds whisper
true.
