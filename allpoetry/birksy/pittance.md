They exchange a pittance,
a strange existence
denial of the obvious
and they behave like children.
Don’t throw them copper
from a jar, it's torture to
keep them together for
a while more.
They've no need to cash it in,
no investment noted
within these walls, just
a range of stalls for white
elephants.
They exchange a pittance,
and care even less.
