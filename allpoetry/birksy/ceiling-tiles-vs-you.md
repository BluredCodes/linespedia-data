---
title: "Ceiling Tiles Vs. You"

They talk about ceiling
tiles, and in truth, it
all goes over my head,
I sit here just wondering
what it'd be like to
interlace my hand in
yours, feel the grip on
my fingers that I've felt
on my heart, and to kiss
each nail, smell your
perfume.
They talk about ceiling
tiles, but I only think
of being with you.
