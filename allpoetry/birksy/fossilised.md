title: "Fossilised"

I fire the arrow high into
the sky, lie down watch it fall,
close my eyes as it hits, a dull
thud above my head, and I dig
it out to fire again.
I could do this all day long,
it kinda gets familier after a
while, a good friend, this arrow,
well, up 'til now, might not
be soon.
And if I don't come home, won't
be long till they find me here,
eyes closed, arms holding names,
they'll be with me, but don't
move me, quite like to be
fossilised.
