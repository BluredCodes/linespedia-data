Bridge – Bank – Bridge – Tracks
Box – Sign – Bridge – Lights
Tree – Grass – Rails – Tracks
Trees – Bridge – Sleepers – Viaduct
Trees – Steep – Tunnel – Green Light
Turrets – Poles – Patio – Inspiration!
