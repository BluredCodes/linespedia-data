title: "Facts And Figures"

We share a space, and this space
is full of facts, prying questions
that we can ask with impunity,
no-one is watching, no-one is
there to judge.
And when the facts run out,
or are simply tired of, what then?
Will it be to figures we turn?
What is next?
My heart quickens, this is becoming
a hard lesson in learning.
