---
title: "A Very Real Dream"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "a-very-real-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41058-A-Very-Real-Dream-by-birksy"
---

Want you next to me,
see what I see,
experience this
life together, and
walk not alone, but
hand in hand, heart
within heart.
It's another world,
yet not foreign to me,
I hold you near, from
afar, a beauty of
mind, soul and body,
my very real dream.
