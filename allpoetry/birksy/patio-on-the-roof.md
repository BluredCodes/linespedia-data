Patio on the roof,
Mum, they’re up there again,
Laughing and playing, drinking
fizzy drinks, and playing.
Why do they do it?
What can be the matter?
They do it every day,
(Whisper) Can I go and join them?

I can see them from the garden,
They jump and run so freely,
Must be something in their food,
I’ve never been like that.
Now I see the ladder,
I’ll move it to the side wall,
then when you’re not looking,
I’ll join them up there, too.
