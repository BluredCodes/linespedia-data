---
title: "Not the Days of Others"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "not-the-days-of-others"
source: "AllPoetry"

Behind me, exasperated,
the words, ‘it’s not my day’ spit
around my ears.
And though a dropped tray is indeed
frustrating, it hardly
ruins your day, and is soon
lost again.
But I imagine, elsewhere
it’s not the days of others who
simply, carry on.
Who is there to hear them?
To make today unlike
yesterday, stop the cycle, and
offer them a hand off?
Selfishly, I must leave
these people, and hope that
someone will help them,
for all our time is precious.
