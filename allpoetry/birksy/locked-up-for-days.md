title: "Locked Up For Days"

Locked up for days,
they keep walking past,
no clue that we're here,
let's keep it that way,
what wonderful times,
and wonderful strokes,
under secret covers,
we're so full of love.

Don't want it to end,
we'll be fine one more time,
besides, our bodies can't move
they've forgot what it's like
to be seperate, alone,
so just move once again,
like all those pleasures before
lets make love some more.
