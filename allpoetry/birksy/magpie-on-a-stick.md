Magpie on a stick,
Your stare is wise, your poise
is thick with pride.

How’s the count today,
do you recognise the road,
the cars, the drivers?

I have a hidden urge,
to swap our places, let you
ruffle some feathers for me today.

The magpie on the stick,
knows who I am, but I
will never know he.
