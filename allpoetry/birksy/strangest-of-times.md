title: "Strangest Of Times"
writer: "birksy"

There's a wail from a woman,
makes me smile through strangest times,
familier words fall to my ears,
and I sing a little, way off key.

And this is all a part of me,
I wonder who will know it next,
I wonder what will change,
I wonder what will happen.

At least my socks have all their toes,
and my shirts, ironed by my own hands,
give me a tea bag and a little water,
I could live like this a little.

I leave the music slightly quiet,
won't do to have it too loud,
quite refined in a middle class way,
I smile through these strangest of times.
