Standing still on the
platform, rain's just as
wet as I remember, the
wind just as bracing
as the last time,
but unlike others here,
I smile, for I think
of you, warm, very
inviting, imagine
your soft skin, your
warm skin, and, just
like the last time,
I am there, again.
