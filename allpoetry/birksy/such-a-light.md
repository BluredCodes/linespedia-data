---
title: "Such A Light"
writer: "birksy"

Open my glass house,
mustn't make too much noise,
I'm just as bad as any other,
can't say I'm a saint, nor
a sinner, though more one than
the other, I'll sleep right
here with one eye open.

If you should chance my way,
please let me know, I've been
alone up till now, alone with
just our thoughts, the list
of questions is somewhat
endless, I'll try not to
ask them all at once.

And when no-one is looking,
I open up the secret hatch,
unfold my folding spade, and
dig some more to get to you,
it's dark under there, but the
dark isn't a problem, when there
is such a light as you at the end.
