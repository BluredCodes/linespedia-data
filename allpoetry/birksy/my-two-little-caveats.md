title: "My Two Little Caveats"

I'm all there is to know,
try hard not to hide anything,
let myself be me,
take a walk round my
person, understand who
I am, no surprise I'm
a simple man.
But then there's my
two little caveats,
I keep them safe
in my thoughts, they're
all I really have, my
two little caveats,
one day they may know
your name, what a day
that'd be.
