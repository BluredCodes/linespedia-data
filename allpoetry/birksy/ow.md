Bloody hell! What was that?
A bang on my head, it’s not even
a broadsheet, how base can
you get?
Is it my fault your windows
are keeping me in?
I’d like to get out, the sky looks
so blue, but no, I’m in here,
with a lunatic who just wants to
hit me, send be black and then
blue.
Bugger this I’m off, is it
any wonder you get stung
when you just want to kill me?
I know of a good place to
go, and when you’re asleep
I’ll come back and you’ll know
what a surprise I can give.
