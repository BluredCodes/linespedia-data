Our masks, they see us through,
and days are no more than treading
water. For those that can’t, they
sink, call breathlessly for help,
hope that some above look down, serve
charity upon them, bringing them
with care and training, back to the
surface.
Keeping eyes far down tests the soul,
as much as keeping them there shows
humility. We see nothing but
a worthy cause. We see every one
as a whole.
