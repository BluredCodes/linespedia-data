The news is brief,
It’s a sunny day
I’d be lost in grief,
If I hadn’t lost my way.
But whatever you do
Is lost on me,
This morning’s cappuccino,
The afternoon’s tea.

Hope the road’s clear,
I’m coming home,
Can you feel me near,
You won't be on your own.
Just yesterday I woke,
And I missed you,
The evening’s coke,
The night’s for two.
