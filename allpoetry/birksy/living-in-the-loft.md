Living in the loft,
just me on my own,
sit on the edge of my bed,
head in hands,
heart in loops,
only my clothes in
the cupboards.
Hear the laughing
down below, not
a part of it anymore,
but I've still got a
small handhold.
Soon, I won't be here,
this'll be a loft alone,
though not much more
alone, than now.
