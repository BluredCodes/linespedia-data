Give me your neck, I’ll submerse
myself within, the smell of your
hair is more than I could scream,
but I know I will.
Back of my hand, wherever you
like it, whisper those words, the
ones you didn’t learn at guides.
Then hush, keep it secret, watch
how intense we’ll both get,
muscles start to stretch, breath
has never been this good.
Whilst we meet, we keep the
strongest words hidden,
now no need to any more,
pour it on, whilst we move
it round, I think we
might die like this.
