So he saved her life,
and she thanked him,
the lights are flashing
one after another,
he receives his medal,
weighs heavy round
his neck, he stands
looking proud.
But it's all just an act,
the medal's now in a
gutter, and he sits at
home with his head
in his hands.
Through his mind go
the memories, that weigh
heavy round his heart,
all the screams of those
he simply couldn't save.
