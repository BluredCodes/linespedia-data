---
title: "It Gets Cold"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "it-gets-cold"

Quick, he’s out, come on in,
I’ve told the neighbours the
boilers wrong again, they think
you’re the most efficient repair
man they’ve seen.

Never mind the chat, no time
for that now, get upstairs and
warm those sheets,
been waiting for this service
all week long.

No, curtains stay open, that
wouldn’t do, of course it’s hot
in here, you won’t notice it
soon, let me take your clothes
off of you.

What’s the matter, don’t
look like that, we’re together,
it’s what we wanted, of course
it’s fine, don’t waste my time
he’s coming home soon.

What’s this? A final settlement,
no, that can’t be, you’ve
done all you can? But everything’s
ready, and I’ll be alone, without
you here, it gets cold.
