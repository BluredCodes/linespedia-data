title: "The Public Want To Know"
writer: "birksy"

Today they say they mourn,
but next week they’ll pour
out all the facts, the scorn,
the lurid little details they
can find, and what they’ll
say is the public want to
know, and, of course, in utter
sadness, I know what they say
is true.
