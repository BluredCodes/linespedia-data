---
title: "Tingles"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "tingles"
source: "AllPoetry"

There they go again,
feel them spread, feel
them awaken certain
feelings, certain parts,
that desire such tingles.
I bathe in the feeling,
I cannot break from it,
and, like the drug
they are, I only
want more.

Girl, better give me some more.
