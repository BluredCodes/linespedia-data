title: "Quite The Opposite"

Quite the opposite,
she wanted quite the opposite,
and I knew it, knew it for
so long.
Waited, lazily, should
have made an effort,
but didn't, that was all
my fault.
Now it's happened, she
has a chance to get
quite the opposite,
and that makes me
happy.
It's what I should've
always done.
