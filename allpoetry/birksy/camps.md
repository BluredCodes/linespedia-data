You are like me, young man,
your father liked camps, too,
could spend hours hidden
away, listening to footfalls,
and others talking, pretending
to be asleep, or just somewhere
else.
Thanks for letting me be with
you, camps are precious,
and to share yours an honour.
I so want you to remember,
These camps we had together.
