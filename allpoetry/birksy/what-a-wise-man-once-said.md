You lose them, bit by bit
like grains of sand they
slip through your fingers,
back onto the beach, where they
become rocks, that are then submerged
under the unknown sea.

So while you have enough sand,
enjoy them, make them
your friends, keep them safe
and warm. And above all else,
don’t hold on too tight, for the
best thing you can give them is your trust.
