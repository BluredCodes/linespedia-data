title: "I'll Let You Know"
writer: "birksy"

No time to write,
Sorry, I know we said we'd
keep in touch, but I've
no real break to sit
and compose letters
of longing to you.
And when I'm not here,
someone's always asking
for a drink, and then it's
too late to ring, and anyway
I'm too drunk.
So maybe in a few weeks
time, you'll forget all about
me, maybe you already
have, and this message is
on deaf ears.
Maybe we'll speak soon,
I'll let you know.
