Nearly there now, step
over step, field over meadow,
the sun, once high, now sits
mainly down, and the noises
have dampened into earth. I
catch myself thinking that
there's nothing new, but I forget
who here is the real habitual. Each step
is different, but I am not.
On this splendid path, I
am the only bore.
