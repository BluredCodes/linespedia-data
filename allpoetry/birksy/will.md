Keep me still, give me a will to
stay where I am, though I feel I do
not belong.

Cradle my heart, wipe away tears
as they fall from eyes wide with
innocent fear.

Love my soul, for it’s lost in twists of
guilt, and fear, and help me learn
to love back.

Stroke my hand, and speak softly
of lands and people who win and lose
just like me.

Show the way, so I might walk as you
with pride, and catch me as I
fall.

All these things, I ask you to teach
me, and I will do good by them
and never fail.
