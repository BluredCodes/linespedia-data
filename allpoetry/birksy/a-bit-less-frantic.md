A bit less frantic,
a month is a long time,
I have a slow mind,
will lengthen every second,
with a thought of you
within it, will you meet
me there?
And the pathway leads
around the sides,
each on a seperate
circuit, it feels we
may never meet, though
at times we're both
a little closer,
will we meet in there?
And then of course, the
simple truth is that if I slow
to walk, then crawl, there
is every chance I may trip
you up, and with increasing
smiles, I stop.
