There's nothing to it,
if I dropped it,
it'd never hit
the ground, instead
float away, beyond
my reach and maybe
yours.

Such substance and
yet so fragile, this
is what we juggle day
by day, a subtle
blend, we wait until
the moment is right to
pour.
