Old sheet at the bedroom window,
curtains a luxury, certainly
no necessity, old sheets
are just as good.

Heating on for an hour,
just when he takes his own
shower, the rest will have
to just get by.

No-one complains today,
they just watch him go to work,
then pack their cases, close the door
and leave those curtains behind.
