Press play, the passwords on,
won't like that, will you,
have to see what's going on,
I'm a little out of control, and
these passwords just make
you mad, have some *******,
maybe it's another one, smile
you'll never know the difference.
Sewing beans jumped away,
fell in a pot, got boiled into mush,
and I threw them all away, before
you had the chance to see.
Difficult not to smile, to see you
squirm, it's not so nice, but as you
know I'm so damn good at knowing
people, tick, tick, your mind is as
loud as that, I see all your thoughts,
and I play them back at double
speed, you twist but cannot get
away.
And then you go, meet whoever,
no surprises for l'il ol' Birksy,
could see it coming for miles,
I stop the tape, this could get
a little sour.
