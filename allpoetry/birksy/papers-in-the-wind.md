title: "Papers In The Wind"

Reading papers in the wind,
Give up now, the winning's
not for you.
Oh, stubborn man it's
time to stop pushing
at the elements.
Stop your silly games,
stop them all, grow
up and let's get on.
Look around the space
you're in, stop and
fold the pages,
in the wind.
