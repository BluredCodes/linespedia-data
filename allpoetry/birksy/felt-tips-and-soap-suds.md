title: "Felt Tips And Soap Suds"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "felt-tips-and-soap-suds"

Close your eyes, lie back,
the lights on bright above,
I take felt tips from my
box of tricks, look at the
canvas I have to draw on.
It's like drawing on the
mona lisa, a beauty in
itself, now marked with
my intense colours, flows,
doodling has never been
this good.
Upon your body I write
words of love, red, and
green and blue, a rainbow
of words describing you,
what you do to my life,
and I make you keep your
eyes shut, then let you do
the same to me, press as
firm as you like, I don't
mind one bit.
Then when we're through
our works of art, we know
where to go, what we have
to do, those pictures may
never come through, but
our memories and laughs
remain vivid.
Then, at last, the fun
time comes, we get to wash
it off, plenty of soap,
plenty of pressure, pleasure,
soap suds to wipe the boards
clean, then wipe them
some more, make us complete,
and relax at the end knowing
tomorrow we could do it all again.
