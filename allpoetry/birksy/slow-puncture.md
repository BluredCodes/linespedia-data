This slow puncture,
how long have I had it?
Breathing out seems so
easy, breathing in a little
worse.
There's no fingers that
can block it, it's a
dam of a trickle,
gonna bring me down
all the way.
So, if I'm moving
kind of slowly, don't
help me, it won't help
me, just smile, let
me slowly leak away.
