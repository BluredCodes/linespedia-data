Stand behind the yellow line,
any closer you may get
hurt, no guarantees
with me anymore,
my best may not be
good enough.
And some days the line
is far away, I walk alone
within my circle, yet
on others it is near,
and I find myself
waiting for anyone
to walk by, smile and
have a talk about
these bloody yellow
lines.
