Pardon me, if I don't doff
my cap, I am but a man, and
one of little patience, too.
I come and go, but have so
much to do that to dwell long
here would be a precious waste
of time, besides, I am lost
without her here, lost with
feelings, just plain lost at
the best of times.
I'd like to dwell here more,
but I like dwelling on her so
much more.
