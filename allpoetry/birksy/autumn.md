It must be,
It must be Autumn.
The air around me has grown cold.
The leaves have turned from green to gold.

Where are you?
Where are you, my love?
You promised me you’d always stay.
I long for your touch every day.

The days got short,
I never thought,
I’d be here on my own.
It’s been so long,
I can’t believe how insecure
I’ve become.

I miss you,
I miss you so much.
I wake each day and say your name.
You’ve gone away, was I to blame?

Please help me.
Please help me today.
Come take me from this place I haunt.
Relight this fire and give me warmth.

So save me.
So save me tonight.
I know you’re gone, but still I dream.
You’re lost to me, but still I need,
