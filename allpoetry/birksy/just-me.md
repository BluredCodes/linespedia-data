I read, I look, I see, I cry,
I laugh, I feel, I smile, I sigh.

I bend, I sniff, I say, I gaze,
I keep, I give, I hope, I pray.

I walk, I lie, I think, I sleep,
I know, I guess, I stand, I leap.

I try, I wait, I scratch, I cough,
I need, I get, I am, I love.
