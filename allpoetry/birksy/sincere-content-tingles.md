title: "Sincere, Content, Tingles"

I use a feather,
I use it well,
lie back, let it
fall on skin so
beautiful, lie back
content that I
want you so much.

I will take my time,
make you forget just
where you are, perhaps
even who you are,
keep you there, then
quite sincerely, do it,
over and over again.
