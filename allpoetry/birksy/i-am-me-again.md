title: "I Am Me Again"

I am me again,
thirteen lines of
rhyme and reason,
then up I get, wake the
boys, and become
their father, a mother’s
son, a brother’s brother,
a wife’s disappointment,
but right now, none
of that matters, I
am within myself,
and can say that I am pleased
to be me again.
