title: "Wrapped"

Wrapped, but cold, shallow breath
hardly making mist in the narrow
alleyway. She cried, but now she’s
hardly alive enough to make any sound,
quietly looking up to her trash can world.
Whilst no one knows she’s there, she’s
hardly there at all, won’t be long, and
her life will have gone, spread out to the
corners, into others, maybe they’ll be
better hosts for this pretty soul, maybe
she’ll be allowed to find who put her there,
exact revenge, but most probably not. I
hope I haven’t walked past her today, eyes
blinkered forward, I hope I never walk past
her, some people don’t realise how lucky
they are.
