---
title: "On My Mind"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "on-my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Can you think of me,
wherever you are now,
pause for thought, and
let me in, for I feel
forever near, a
constant thought
companion.

I have no real
want for thoughts,
just a need for you,
so, pause for thought,
and think of me,
for you are so much
on my mind.
