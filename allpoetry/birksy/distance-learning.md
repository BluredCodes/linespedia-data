We, like some odd
distance learning project,
trade ideas, words, emotions,
from our life havens,
wait to be marked, wait
for retort, bathe in
the others simple presence.
Yet the distance is hard
to understand, built
with bodies that yearn
for the five senses, we
ache for possibilities that
may, like our distance,
be very far away.
