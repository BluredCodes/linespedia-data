title: "She"
writer: "birksy"

The first poem, she will be
in it,
and then the others, scripted
one after the other,
she will be in them, too.

Cannot stop my hands from
speaking her,
cannot quash my mind from
thinking her,
she is everywhere I look.

And I know tomorrow will
be the same,
will wake with thoughts of
only her,
she appears upon my eyelids.

And when the last word
falls,
of the last poem I shall
ever write,
she will still be there.
