Reflection,
I have you,
too long have I hidden,
too long have you left me,
I have returned to be me,
Move out of my way.

Reflection,
I see you,
believe you once were me,
believe you once liked me,
let me walk in my old shoes,
I am the way.

Reflection,
I hate you,
I'll leave you where I see you,
I'll leave you in the mirror,
no time to turn and look again,
You are far away.
