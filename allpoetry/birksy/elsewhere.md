Cat got your tongue?
Or weren’t you listening,
eyes played elsewhere,
lips shaped around words you’d rather here.
I know I’m not 1st, 2nd, 3rd, anymore,
but disposable? I didn’t think so.
Maybe it’ll get better,
or considerably worse.
Whichever, we’ll decide, but
best start by listening to the things I
need to say.
