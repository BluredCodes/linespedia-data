There’s no more I can do,
if I thought an idea, I’d
fall sound asleep. As things
stand I sit near slumber,
eyes half closed, breathing
slow, if I thought I’d win,
I’d go.
But another battle’s
ahead, exhausted, I
try to find elusive strength,
down deep, down anywhere,
draw it through my spirit,
through my soul, invigorate
my body, let the call be swift
let it be made, today.
