title: "Too Far Away"

Too far away,
with each passing moment,
I'm too far away,
cold, and shivering,
on the edge of afraid,
right now, I'm too
far away.

Believe in that fate,
she'll take me just somewhere,
can't figure out where
I'd hate to imagine
it's not far from here,
but nothing is
beyond the girl fate.

Wonder what's next,
tomorrow, I guess, another
few hours, some more time
to kill, ain't getting
easier, there's a long
way to go, and as I say,
I'm just too far away.It's late, this might be crap.
