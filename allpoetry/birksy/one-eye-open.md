---
title: "One Eye Open"
writer: "birksy"

As the sun sleeps through
lazy days are in the minds of
others, who stare through
cooling eyes, and stoop to
keep the fresh day
churning.

Upon the step, she sits,
stares up to catch the first
drops of light soaking
through nights bleak
blanket, she stretches,
silently.

And now the night, once more,
has become no more than a
tinge in the sky, and the
memory of him greys, as the
others pull for her
attention.

Now she must sleep through
one more day, so she curls, into
the ball, while behind her, the
step slowly warms, she slumbers,
her feline form, asleep, with one eye
open.
