Wearing Dad's suit,
Bit long in the arm,
Bit loose round the neck,
But it's good enough.
Shirt hanging out,
Tres cool and nouvous,
One of a kind
See how I stand.
Wearing Dad's suit
Smell his scent on the neck,
It's good enough.
