Bang on my knee,
A bruise will be its evidence,
Though, unlike you,
the bruise will disappear.

You hurt all that you touch,
Pain that runs deep, perhaps
deeper than you can feel.

There’s envy for you,
It comes close, yet in
self defence it moves away.

We’ll never be what I
want, so like a fool,
I bruise my knee, again.
