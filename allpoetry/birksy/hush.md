Hush now, my love, take my arm,
Let’s steal ourselves away,
Away from any harm,
The moon has been our friend
He has gone away this eve,
Let us leave.

Come now, my love, time is short,
I fear that he knows,
He waits to see us caught,
Duck down and softly tread,
His spies are thick among us,
Let us rush.

What now, my love, what is wrong?
I see fear in your eyes,
It’s with me that you belong.
I thought that it was now,
But I see that one more time
You aren’t mine.
