title: "Alone"

I hold the stare of a
wooden cat, but he wins
the war, he knows I'm
easily melted, can tell
what sort of man I
am.
And the reflection, he
thinks I'm nothing,
doesn't catch my
eye, he's far too afraid
to.
So the dark is all
I have to watch, and
when ticks turn eyes
inward, there sits an
empty man.
