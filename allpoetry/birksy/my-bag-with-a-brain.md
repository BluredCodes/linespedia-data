title: "My Bag with a Brain"

I carry a brain in my bag,
Won’t let it go,
I look shiftily left then right,
Nothing’s coming
Yet I won’t move.
But you know I do,
For I live quite near you,
And at night I pass by
your door, with my bag
with a brain in it.
