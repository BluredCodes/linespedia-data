title: "My Thoughts, Too"

I've always been silent, the quiet one,
though my thoughts, are, like yours, so loud.

Do you not see the scream?

I've kept my thoughts close,
don't want to be too conspicuous.

I fear there's not enough of me to fall apart,
it's all given to you, laid on a paupers plate.

when alone I wonder...
I could be with you...
but when with you,
I want to be so much more of you.

I'll make sure you won't lost sight of me...
this heart takes time to love, but tis yours to try.
