Right now, I’m in a hurry,
for tonight, you see, it’s curry,
a Jalfrezi awaits me on my plate.

I can hear my stomach rumble,
And my tastes buds turn me humble,
I know it'll be worth the wait.For those who do not know what a Jalfrezi tastes like - my heart goes out to you.
