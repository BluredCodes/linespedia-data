title: "Just Want It To Miss"
writer: "birksy"

Just want it to miss,
Each time, every time,
I want it to just fly by,
What a difference it would have made,
No fear, no death, no media.
But it never can miss,
Just explodes
as if a crazy firework,
was all it ever was.
I’m sorry for what it was,
what it’s become,
A shocking five second
reality check,
Almost a stunt,
that very sadly went awry.
Fall silent, pray with me,
Pray that next time, against all odds,
It’ll miss.
