The greasepaint saint,
sits with lights above his head,
can't understand where the audience
went,
just cries in hands that have seen better days,
in suits that have seen better hearts,
he cries into air, now just full
of dust,
he draws in breath, but only
just.
He's a greasepaint saint if I
ever saw one, maybe best
there ever was, but he won't
accept an award in this type
of game,
rewards are never real.
Greasepaint saint's a lonely guy,
and that is why I sit right
next to him.
