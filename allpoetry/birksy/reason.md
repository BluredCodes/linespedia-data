And there's the reason
I want you, such simple
words, they set me
alight, bring spirit to my
eyes, rids sadness from
within, you are part of
me, whether you know it
or not, like it or not,
believe it or not, you
brush me dry, then
soak my skin, I will
be everything, and you
are the reason why.
