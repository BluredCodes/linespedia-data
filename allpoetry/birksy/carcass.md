Wondered why it hadn’t moved,
for weeks it was sat there,
but from the other seats, I couldn’t
see the whole story, thought it was
just its winter break.
Today was different, though,
the sun gave its secrets away, and
from this side of the train its
dereliction was evident, a
carcass devoid of life,
no more a machine than a
piece of scrap, could see it all
from this side of the carriage,
which isn’t really that very far
from before.
