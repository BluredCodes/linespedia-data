Call Julianna –
she can tell you
anything, that you’re okay
in the world, or that you’re
missing someone.
She’ll ask you some questions,
watch your hands, and in
less than five minutes, know
you better than your
friends do. Maybe that’s the
problem.

Call Julianna – 
She’s still waiting, hand
near the telephone, just
a number away. But as
you dial, try and wonder,
what makes her any different
than you, or your family.
What can she know
that you don’t know already?

Call Julianna – 
Call her now,
She must be expecting you.

Call Julianna – you know there’s no choice.
