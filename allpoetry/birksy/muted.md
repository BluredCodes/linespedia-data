Having a laugh,
a nice day for a change,
keep the fires at bay,
run through leaves at your feet.
Playing the fool, muted or not,
nothing wrong with a friend,
nothing wrong with the sun.
Keep it quite short, hold it quite firm,
breath’s for the wicked,
I breathe when I want.
See villages below me,
sweep my eyes past the sea,
home’s where you make it,
smell the fires in the bay.
