There's a room in my house,
I keep all of my failures there,
open the door, throw in another,
here it slide down to the floor.
I took the bulb out long ago,
and boarded the windows on the inside,
they see no daylight or otherwise.
I close the door again,
pick up a tile and stick it on,
this is my way of remembering them,
a mosaic of mistakes,
that each time I pass to bed,
I see and think of again.
