Always complex,
from now on always
complex, lucky I was
good at maths.
Take from one side,
then take from the other,
balance the equation,
keep them equal,
when all has gone,
you are left with an
answer.
Hope what's above the
line, goes below the
line, hope those brackets
are removable, I so
want to get it right
next time.
