A friend once told me
That the good guys always won,
And I laughed at the saying,
But maybe he was right.
Maybe he knew you.
I think I'll try and find him
To show him to you,
And reassure my friend
With his own words.
My loneliness has skulked away
To another,
And I am left feeling embarrassed
By my own impatience.
