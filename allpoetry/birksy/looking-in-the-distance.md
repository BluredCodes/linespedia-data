Looking in the distance 
he sees beyond the moment,
straight through my head,
and the bridge that blocks his view.
He looks like he’s on a mission,
eyes fixed in the distance,
I wonder how long he’s been walking,
like that,
moving forward and forward,
no need to look behind.
He moves past me,
So at least he sees the outlines,
if nothing else.
He’s looking in the distance
and without a second thought
I turn, and look there, too.
