Sometimes when I see myself
I have no eyes, just dark
sockets that give nothing away,
And it makes me stare, this
empty beast, where did the
real me go?

But then I look again, and
they’re back once more, sad
but there, seeing, slowly
disbelieving, keeping quiet
as best they can, though never far
from shut.
