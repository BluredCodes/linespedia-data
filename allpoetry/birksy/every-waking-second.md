title: "Every Waking Second"

Every waking second,
thoughts turn to you, 
turn to you again,
what you might be doing,
thinking, saying, playing.
I try and pretend this
cannot be, but before I
can discount you, you are
there again, smiling,
laughing, crying, sighing.
My face is not a face,
it's a mask behind that
I think of you, writing,
planning, coping, hoping.
Every waking second's
taken up by you,
and you alone.
