---
title: "Every Place You Go"
writer: "birksy"

There’s no need to rush things,
You’ve plenty of time,
Lie back, tug on heartstrings,
Feels like you’re swinging on mine.

You’re almost halfway home now,
You’re almost bound to know,
You’ll always be inside us,
Every place you go.
