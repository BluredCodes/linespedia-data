---
title: "Cut Me Down to Size"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "cut-me-down-to-size"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

There’s no time to explain,
That time has gone,
I’m leaving today,
Heading God knows where,
If I ring,
Don’t expect conversation,
Just listen,
There’s nothing to declare.

You left no other choice,
You left me here,
My train at platform seven,
Sits heavy on its wheels,
Overhead,
One more announcement,
Goes unheard,
There’s nothing to reveal.

It’s been a long time since
The hungry start,
We drifted different ways,
Got a taste for foreign things,
In my heart
We’re still together,
Just about,
My bitter head, though, stings.

And when you’re far away,
Somewhere nice,
I’ll listen to your message,
And smile for you aren’t tied,
Lucky you,
I’ll quickly whisper,
Down the phone,
You cut me down to size.
