That’s the tides for you,
You step off into shallows,
But instead your head’s submerged,
No signs to tell you,
Just how deep it was.

That’s the tides for you,
Calmness hides a menace,
Sometimes it’s better to stay on the edge,
Watch others wallow,
Maybe turn away.

That’s the tides for you,
They pull us slowly in,
Safely dry, now uncomfortably wet,
Yet as you watch,
Your foot is all but in.
