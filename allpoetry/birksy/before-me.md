Nothing to write about, call it quits
with the world lying like temptation
before me.
Even mediocrity seems a blessing,
nothing but what has already been
before me.
Killing time the pleasant way, a
hundred ramblings spilling out
before me.
Now I here the little bells, they
play a tune, a pretty tune,
before me.

Take my time, I think I hear
a different sound,
From behind.
