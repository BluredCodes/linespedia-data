title: "You’re Still Here"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "youre-still-here"

Wipe the sleep away,
my god, you’re still here,
nothing’s the problem anymore,
you’re still here,
get up, take a shower with you,
eat breakfast, make love to you,
all these things to look forward to,
‘Cause you’re still here.
