Splishity splosh!
The rain on the rooftops,
Never pauses for breath
Oh, how I long to be out there
with you, dancing and kicking up our heels
Tasting the drops with our open mouths
as we swing round,
The laugh so fresh and loud in my ears.

Keep me spinning! The pull ever
outward, balanced by your firm grip
How I’ve come to be used to it,
and value each crease. Each fold
reminds me of something about
my life with you.

If I kept all the moments I cherished, there’d
be no more room on the earth
for the rest,
That’s how lucky I am
to have you.
