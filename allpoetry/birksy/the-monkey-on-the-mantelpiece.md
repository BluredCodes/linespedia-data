Ever felt watched?
The monkey on the mantelpiece
has me in his eye,
he never moves,
doesn’t even judge,
he’s a good monkey
to have around.
I smile at him, he
holds my gaze,
simply stares without
embarrassment,
with no apparent
interest, yet I know
he cares, he’s such a
good monkey to
have around.
