title: "I Clean My Pores"
writer: "birksy"

I clean my pores,
wipe them slowly, my
face looks low, however
hard I try I look the same,
just good old Si,
he'll never change.
But inside I churn,
not been this bad in years.
I clean my pores, though
they still feel dirty, can't
remove the part of me that
hurts, wouldn't want to
anyhow, keep me sad, it's
what I deserve.
