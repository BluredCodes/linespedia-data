title: "Rain, You Are My Friend"

Rain, you are my friend,
come comfort me, in
misery, despondency,
come cover me with
remorseful tears.
My life shows its frailties,
through thoughts
I dread to think.
You surround me with
a storm, thunder not yet
started, lightning
far behind, but it's so
close, so close.
Will the wind stir
up and blow away the
clouds? I cannot know
yet, and till I do, rain
come be my friend.
