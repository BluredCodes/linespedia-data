Hello Mags, said the grey haired
husband whose wife was not called
Mags.
The couple sit and order tea or coffee
And Mags’ husband does not see
What may have happened a long time ago.

And as the couples talk
The man who said ‘Hello’
Watches as intently as one could do,
Mags, her stories play and entertain
One especially.

And the man whose married to Mags
speaks to his male friend, who
does his very best to listen
but the fire in his eyes has gone.

When they rise to leave they shake
hands, they kiss,
But underneath, who can tell, when he says
‘Goodbye Mags’, with that smile.
