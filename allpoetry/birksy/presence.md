Danger has a presence,
and I feel it now, the insane
uncontrollable, speaking against the rational.
I have dealt with this before,
sometimes even well, but it must
be approached with care.
There it goes once more, and like
a dazzled animal, I sit stock still,
pause my breath.
Time to move on.
