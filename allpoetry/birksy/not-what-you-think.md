I am not a dummy,
Not a fool, not dim, not thick,
Not stupid, weird, or nerd-like,
Not simple, not slow, not sick.

I am just a person,
One day you’ll see as much,
‘Til then, I guess, I’ll simply try,
To look, and taste, and touch.
