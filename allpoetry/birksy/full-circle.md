So here I am again,
Full circle from where I started from,
And as if I didn't see it coming
Through what must have been a sightless loving,
I find myself alone.

The tracks I made once
Can be faintly seen in front of me,
And the many more that lead off from here,
Still have the mark,
How I have always walked.

It seems I'll have to start anew,
So who knows when
I might return here again,
But with some thought I know
It won't be long.
