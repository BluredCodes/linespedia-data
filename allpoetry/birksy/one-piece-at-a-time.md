What’s there to be king for?
I wouldn’t rush back,
sin, like the litter around me,
is profligate, indiscriminate,
ankle high and stinks of
sewage, I can’t walk
for there is nowhere to go to,
no oasis in the melee,
just head down days
where ignorance is at least
half of bliss.
No, I wouldn’t rush back,
no simple religion will
help right now, litter has
to be cleared one piece
at a time.
