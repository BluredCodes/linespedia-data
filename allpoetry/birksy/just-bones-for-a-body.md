They’re just bones, nothing more
nothing less, that once structured
the flesh, organs, muscles of a body,
let it walk, and sit, and
breathe…

Oh, the breath was so light,
I remember it’s shallowness,
Can remember the weak and the
dying…

But, now they’re just bones
no rhyme, no feeling, for
what I was, what I knew
has become, at last,
a part of you.
