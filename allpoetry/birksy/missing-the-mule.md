title: "Missing The Mule"

Missing the mule, they
sit mid-air, didn't
realise what would happen
if they didn't believe
it was real, if the hope
they felt was lost,
and the crib's just full
of straw, they kept
walking by.
When they wake
it's all a dream,
and they raise a
boy, who they
love dearly, nothing
very special, except
their son, that, to them,
is so much more
than enough.
