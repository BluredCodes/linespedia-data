Where’s the arithmetic gone?
People read, I write, yet
maths cannot be seen, is it
not the in-thing on a train?
But wait, what am I saying?
We all sit calculating, and though
most times it is not numbers,
the formulae are the same.
And so I sit and relax,
my beloved use of math
still very alive, and I return
once more, to write.
