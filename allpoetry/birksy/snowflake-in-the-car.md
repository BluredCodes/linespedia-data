Snowflake in the car
Windows shut, heat on full,
Can’t keep the memory of her here,
Have to drive to concentrate.

She’ll never sit next to me,
never blow cold in my face,
that snowflake isn’t her,
but it’s all I’ve got left.

And it makes me feel jealous,
each time someone looks at it,
going to have to get used to it,
it’s the prettiest thing I know.

When I stop the car, turn off
the key and sit, that snowflake
let’s me dream, it’s what’s
left of you and me.
