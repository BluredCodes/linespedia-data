My lucky number is your shoe size,
what more proof do you need?
Unprecedented coincidence, you
surely must agree?

Then there's our initials,
perfectly aligned within the stars,
can't wait till our first eclipse,
heavenly bodies on level par.

But there are a hundred other such
coincidences that mustn't be ignored,
but none so more than both our hearts,
joined by that constant, woven, cord.
