I’ve lost a can, I’ve lost a bottle,
These are my gucky’s, but they’ve
gone away. I search the net, man
I’ll need a yank quite soon.
Laughter abrasive, intelligence
sometimes negligible, but without
the drip, drip, drip, where will we
be, how can we grow?
Slip into the right gear, wash
myself with tanning pills,
no need to smile, there’s a
clip on my face.
Shoot long from my hips, so damn
high I hit the ceiling, eyes
shut yet I’m looking at yew.
Deepest south, haven’t got
no hates, haven’t got no
views, clues, news to abuse.
Boy, it don’t get much better
than this.
