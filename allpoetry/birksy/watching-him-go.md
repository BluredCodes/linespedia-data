---
title: "Watching Him Go"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "watching-him-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

From the window, streaked with rain,
she watches the plane, alone with folded arms,
And if it were not for I, watching, she’d
be invisible.

On the window the signs say
- No Entry - Except For Emergency -
I feel this may be
such an emergency.

She moves to see a better view,
There’s a murmur, a groan, of engines
through the weather
sounding the start of an end.

Oh, how the rain, it mocks, not even allowing
her fresh air to breathe him away.

Turning with tears in her eyes she goes.
No-one sees her, but I.
