title: "Gotta Smile"
writer: "birksy"

Gotta smile, don’t you
see? Gotta smile, or you
won’t be me. Gotta smile,
it’s what they expect. Gotta
smile, it ain’t happened yet.
Gotta smile, don’t need you
to cry. Gotta smile, you’re
the rock by their side. Gotta
smile, almost made it through.
Gotta smile, real life won’t do.
