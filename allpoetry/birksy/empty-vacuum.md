Carpet dust.
Dead skin, flaked, compacted
Sucked up, deposited.
Bag after bag after
bloody bag.
Day after day after
bloody day.
It’s all you do - is it all you think about?

It’s about time you changed;
you evolved.
Maybe took some pride in your work.
Planned,
a bit more.
You know what I mean?

I’m fed up with you doing the easy work,
You’ve got a chip, haven’t you?
Haven’t you?
Why don’t you use it, then?
Start pulling your own weight around here,
huh?
