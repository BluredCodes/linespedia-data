---
title: "I Turn The Tap"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "i-turn-the-tap"
source: "AllPoetry"

I turn the tap,
want to torture you,
want to hear you scream
a submission, want to
make you plead so
hard you cry, but
I do not have any tools,
save the tap of words,
so I try and turn it
right, want to
drip, drip, drip
your resistence away.
