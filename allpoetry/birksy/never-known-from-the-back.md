title: "Never Known From The Back"

Never known from the back,
what’s the matter with her anyway?
Come on, get out the way, I’m in a
bloody hurry.
Then a mirror, and I see her,
hadn’t seen her in a while,
looks the same, but her eyes
are shut.
Follow her through the roads
watching her feet walk slowly,
carefully, she still has her
mystery, her dignity.
Then, at the kerb, I ask her
to walk with me, and she says
yes, she takes my arm, I take her
stick, I get the chance to her guide,
again.
