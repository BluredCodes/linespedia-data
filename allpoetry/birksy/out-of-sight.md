Out of sight,
out of mind,
I've been nothing but
a fools gold find,
some empty shell that's
been left behind,
I've had to stay,
with my own kind.

Perhaps later,
perhaps never,
perhaps it was,
or never was,
but above all else,
I'm still me,
this rock that
I've become.
