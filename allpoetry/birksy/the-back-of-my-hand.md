title: "The Back Of My Hand"
writer: "birksy"

On the back of my hand,
tiny crystals form when I
think of you, and I save
them up, they make my
tea so sweet, and not
a single calorie, too.
And if you looked hard,
brought a microscope
so I could see real close,
I'd show you the regular
shapes, that when the
crystals are thrown just
such, they compact, and
form the prettiest heart,
two foot high on the living room
floor, reflecting lights from
within, and all this built
just by thinking of you.
