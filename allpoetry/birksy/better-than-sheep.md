title: "Better Than Sheep"
writer: "birksy"

Traffic’s slow, watch the
cars, as good as sheep,
if that respite is
what you need. I seek
another, though,
eyes flick back through
lines of faces, tired,
frustrated, bang the
window, no one hears, or
wants to hear, today’s
a mess, their hope can
hardly grow.
Then, there it is, and
I watch, fleeting, as she
passes by, it’s all I’ll ever
see, but, I know, it works
better than those sheep.
