Oops, got up late again,
just like the old days, I
was thinking thoughts that
kept me awake, just like the old,
old days.
But, of course, it’s not the same
anymore, I’m changed, more
responsible, gone is that
indifference, that
naivety, now replaced with an
awkward clarity,
It’s so clear what I want,
and with who I want to be.
