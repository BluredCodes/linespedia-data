I met a man,
Aye, Meta-man,
He knew all about me,
Exact in summary,
I knew I was beaten,
made hasty retreat
and shut my open mouth.
I should have learned,
The info worm has turned,
Privacy, pure fantasy
My name is written a thousand
times, and not one of them
by me.
