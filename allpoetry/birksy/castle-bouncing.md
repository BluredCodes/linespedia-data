title: "Castle Bouncing"

A little more air,
can always bounce a little
higher,
me and my belle, we'll
play all day,
find it hard to stop.
Be hometime soon,
but don't let it worry you,
know the people on the door,
they'll let us stay on some more,
got a whole lot of castle bouncing
to do with you.
