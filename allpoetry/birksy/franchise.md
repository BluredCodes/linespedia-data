No more stops, can't
take them any more,
got to get my steam up,
gonna just keep going.
Come along, you can
join me, I'm only going
forward, feels like I'm
going all the way.
Yeah, its gonna get bumpy,
but just hang on, that's
the fun part, and if you
can't take it, don't get on.
Tracks being built as we go,
come on, buy a franchise,
this rolling stock's only
going up.
And when we're there, we
can look back down, see
the places we've been,
smile, then roll straight
back down.
