title: "Is Anybody There?"

Is anybody there?
Can you show me a sign?
Do I look too deeply,
Or just skim the surface,
for no-one arrives, least
none that I understand.
I keep in silence the hopes
I have. Want them to be more
than hopes.
Want to see what’s on the other side.
