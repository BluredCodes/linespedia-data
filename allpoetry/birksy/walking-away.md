---
title: "Walking Away!"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "walking-away"

There, as if in a dream, she strides
her long slim legs make
her glide, with her hand she strokes the
rail, and her lips, pursed, describe the fine
detail of her thoughts.

To me she is the height which
I aim to reach, despite the man
that walks just ahead,
their distance and silence,
says that they are together.

But I, like she, will work behind his
back, and know that for all the looks I
lack will soon win her round to a time
where she walks with me, and
he
will be all but forgotten.
