The day’s now gone, what did I do?
Care for the many, instead of the few?
Not bloody likely, I’m too selfish to,
Just stared at the sun, swimming in blue.

Your day’s now gone, what did you do?
Brought up our children, with deep respect to
Make the most of the day, understand they’re so few,
You’d rather be me, I’d rather be you.
