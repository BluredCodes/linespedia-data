title: "Doesn't Matter"

Even though it's hazy,
I guess it doesn't matter,
I walk between the mist
hardly even there,
part of me is part of it,
and I feel it's come
for me, a man
in pieces, wet
within the rain,
it's enough to make
me disappear.
