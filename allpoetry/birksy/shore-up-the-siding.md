title: "Shore Up The Siding"
writer: "birksy"

Shore up the siding,
One hand on top of another,
fingers interlaced with the next,
no gaps allowed to show.
And if we feel it might fall,
another battalion from behind
supports the backs of those
in front.
Now they stand in silent order,
the strain still hard on their faces,
they’ll fight it till the end,
though they know not what is
the end.
If you pass, give them only
encouragement, for if you don’t
they might just be holding up,
what you hold most dear.
