title: "Morning's Good"

Good morning?
Yes, another one,
they're all this good
just lately.
I awoke and saw a
different world,
inside, a very
different man.
I will ask of you
to walk with me,
I shall describe
exactly what I see.
When the morning's
gone, I will turn and
ask if you are free,
and can walk with me, 
for the rest of our
days.
