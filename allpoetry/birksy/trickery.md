Trickery, I know trickery,
I know the games people play,
I am a master magician,
I'll come and play today.

Lock me in a cupboard,
Board me up to die,
I'll come right back at you,
Want to see me try?

Stab me in my swollen heart,
Shoot me in my chest,
Hang me from an old oak tree,
That's what I'd like best.

Behead me in a guillotine,
Slash my wrists and let me bleed,
Poison me with sleeping pills,
You know it's what I need.

Smash me with a hammer,
Garrotte me with a wire,
Snap my neck in your own sweet hands,
You'll know I'll never tire.

Trickery, I know trickery,
It's love that I can't play,
I'd die so many ways for you,
I'd die for you, today.
