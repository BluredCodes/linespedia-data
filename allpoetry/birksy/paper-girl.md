She's a paper girl,
ain't seen it before,
and I don't know
where she goes,
before she's back again.

Haven't asked her a
dot, but she questions
what I know, have to
read a tabloid
for her fill.

And the papergirl stays
where she is, while I
move to the door, I
look back and she's
gone, to reappear.
