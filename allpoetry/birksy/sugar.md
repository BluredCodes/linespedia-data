I don’t know where it went,
I’m only four years old,
I put it somewhere really safe,
Yes, I know it was made of gold.

I’ve tried to think of everywhere,
I’ve thought and thought all day,
But my mind has simply drawn a blank,
No, I didn’t throw it away!

All right, I know that I’ve done wrong,
I’ll spend the day in my room,
It’s not my fault I can’t remember anything,
From this morning to the womb.

Oh, Dad, you’ve found it, in a sugar packet,
Now we can cease to worry,
In fact, now you say it, I can remember,
Won’t forget that in a hurry.
