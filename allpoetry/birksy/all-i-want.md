She is all I want, a
girl with a glow,
I am tired, but you
keep me from sleep,
I am old, but you
keep me from aging,
and if I don't hear you
tonight, then I grow a
little older, a
little more tired.

And when we next speak,
I will thank fate,
for letting me
know you,
thank fate, who'll
shrug her shoulders
then disappear,
leaving me alone,
beautifully with you.
