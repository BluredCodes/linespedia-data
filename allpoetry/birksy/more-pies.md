title: "More Pies"

Forgive me, for I have sinned,
I ate the pies, now the
indigestion brings water
to my eyes.

Lying down, it's hard to move,
greed is in us all, I had
to prove, I was quite different
than you.

Tomorrow will be the same,
I want everything, drink to
the fame, don't want
that fat old girl to sing.

So you don't recognise me,
So what? I'm the
celebrity, don't compare our
lives; I can buy more pies.
