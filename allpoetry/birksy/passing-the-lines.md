title: "Passing the Lines"
writer: "birksy"

If age be mine, then there's little point in holding on,
I see my lines, and they've grown deeper,
I'm slowly leaving, but I know who's coming through,
My skin is on my son's hands.

My eyes are fine, but I know they'll go,
Looking into nothing takes its toll,
My son who hugs me doesn't realise yet,
Doesn't know what it means.

My lungs fill with air, almost a waste now,
It burns the words of peace away,
I sit quite still - sometimes without purpose,
I see a gate swinging shut.
