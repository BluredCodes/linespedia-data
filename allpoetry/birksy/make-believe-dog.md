title: "Make-Believe Dog"

That man walked a dog,
which just wasn't there,
you could see it quite clearly,
in his unswerving stare.

Something large, an Alsatian,
or Retreiver, perhaps,
definitely not something that's
small and just yaps.

He cared for it, though,
could see that quite clear,
the man with the stare,
held his dog very near.
