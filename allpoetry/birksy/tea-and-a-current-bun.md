title: "Tea And A Current Bun"

Tea and a current bun,
enough to fill a gap,
but I know the gap
is wide. Need more
than food today, need
more than smiles, need
to sit beside, sit behind,
sit until the sitting hurts,
then move on somewhere
to lay my head.
