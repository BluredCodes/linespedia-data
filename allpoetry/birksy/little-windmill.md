Little windmill
I’d not seen you before
Perhaps the days are windy now,
Perhaps my eyes are open now.
Are you there for a boy
to play with , when
he’s lonely and scared,
when he wakes in the night,
and no-ones around.
Is it his breath that
spins your blades,
If I bent close enough
Would I hear tears or
laughter?
Little windmill, you do
your duty, sad though
it is.
