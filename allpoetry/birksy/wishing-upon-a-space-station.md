Been wishing on a space station,
all this bloody time,
some astronauts who know nothing,
been deflecting all my
solitary wishes, no wonder I'm in
such a mess.
Not my fault it's the brightest
body, always backed the sure
fire winners, no one told me
that wasn't a star,
I guess I never asked.
So I sit back and wait for
some contact, weekends
are a lonely time, perhaps
the next envelope in my in-tray
will be from the men by the moon,
even that would be a little
comfort, today.
