The pen isn’t hers, it’s a
gift that he took, from
a hotel that he stayed
in, in the centre of town,
and the papers not hers,
it came from the stationary,
he took it last Tuesday
afternoon.
The ring that he gave
her isn’t hers too, he
found it in his wife’s
jewellery box, and the
love that she holds so
tight in her heart, well,
you’ve guessed it, that
isn’t hers, too.
