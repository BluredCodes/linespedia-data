---
title: "The Only Copy I’ve Got"
writer: "birksy"
slug: "the-only-copy-ive-got"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wonder if you could help
me, it’s this photo, you see
I wonder if you could reproduce it,
on your machines, such
clever machines, can you do that?

I know it’s a bit old, but it’s
the only copy I’ve got,
and it was such a magical day,
I’ll never forget it,
I remember myself saying that even now.

You can do it? How splendid,
is it possible for me to watch?
My son’s into computers, though
I don’t see him much now,
he’s too busy.

Oh, this chair is comfy, yes,
I’m watching the screen,
I’m very excited, I expect you
can tell, what a nice job
you must have.

He’s my ex-husband, you see,
and someone told me, the
other day, that he’d died,
thought I’d go through our stuff,
and there I found it, again.

I know he’d like it, my son
that is, they were close for a
time, I think, closer
than they should’ve
rightly been.

Oh, yes, that’s not bad, at all,
good copy, for sure, what
a clever machine, now, please,
how much does this photo
cost me?
