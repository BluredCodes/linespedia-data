Walking the mud flats, at
low tide, digging for worms,
sometimes she finds, sometimes
it's not worth the effort.
She puts what she finds
in the humane plastic tub,
they all look the same,
just right for the hook, but
some are different, though
they just look the same,
some
are
different.
