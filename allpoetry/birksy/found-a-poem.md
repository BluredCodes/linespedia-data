Found a poem, thought you'd like it,
lie back, rest your head here,
let me stroke your tender skin,
as I recount the words to you.

But wait, the words have gone, and
I remember 'tis hard to use true words
of love, if we’ve never touched, never
met, never looked ourselves in the eye.

So, I'll just keep on stroking, hope
you feel it, keep writing words
of hope, but without your simple
presence, I cannot love you yet.
