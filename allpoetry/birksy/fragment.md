Excuse me, I’ve lost something,
I thought it might still be
here, remember me, the
other day I dropped a cup,
spilled the love from inside,
and you helped me clear it up.

Well there’s a tiny bit
missing, a fragment you
could say, I wondered whether
you may have seen it, since
that day, I’ve a feeling I saw you
pick it up, I’ve a feeling that
I left it here for you.
