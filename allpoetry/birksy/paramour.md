Paramour,
you are my step,
the one that takes me
the one that makes me,
let me stay.

Paramour,
look behind me,
I need you wholly,
we must act secretly,
forever more.

Paramour,
do not judge me,
just slowly, acceptingly,
whisper no sympathy,
this is wrong.

Paramour,
you are my step,
the one that holds me,
the one that falters me,
I must go.Paramour:A lover, especially one in an adulterous relationship
