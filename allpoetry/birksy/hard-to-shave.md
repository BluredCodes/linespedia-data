title: "Hard To Shave"

Hard to shave at the
moment, I look but I
don't see, it's as if I
don't inhabit the mirror's
shiny world.

The glass still moves if I
push it, though no finger
prints appear, I touch my
face, and it must be
numb.

And I resist the urge to
smash it, know one day soon
I'll need it, I can't keep
on being the man with an
empty soul.
