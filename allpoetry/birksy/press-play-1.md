Just press play, everything
we took to know, let's explain
what the hell we were, and
never were, one constant stream
to dip our toes, let the piranha's
have their bloody fill, they'll
not understand on what they feed,
but perhaps that's not the point.
There's a start, but it's buried
with guilt you could never forgive,
and that's one of those times I could
never forget.
While we moved, while we began the
slow descent I cried and tried to
understand that I'd got it all
wrong, that you were all right,
giving myself the blame, oh that's
so how it works when you're a
perfect girl.
Hello, tap the wood, don't let
everything disintegrate, your
memory was always so volatile,
and I pretended to forget, to
overlook the discrepencies,
though there was nothing but
a quiet life in it for me.
And now you cannot control me,
you don't speak to me, you
search for another to control,
and I walk and dance, and smirk
on my own terms, and it's the best.
Press pause now, time to take a
natural break.Something a little different.
