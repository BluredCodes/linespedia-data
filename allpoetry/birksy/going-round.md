The man walked his dog,
right round in a circle,
and the dog trotted along,
in wonderful ignorance.
At the walls he just turned,
didn't think what layed
beyond, he'd been trained
so very well.
Then when the man let go,
the dog simply stopped, was
certain he needed the man
to move, and when the man
simply fell, the dog lay
beside him, and that was
how the two of them
were found.
