Writing in the twilight,
'tween this world and the next,
I talk a dozen languages,
I hold a dozen courts.

Where one stops and one begins,
is the line that I must tread,
there are no more natural highs,
well, maybe one or two.

Keep the home fires turning,
warmed from several sides,
I thank you for your care,
it means a lot to me.
