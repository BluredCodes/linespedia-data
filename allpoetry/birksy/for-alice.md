This is for Alice, a girl
who smiled, who understood,
who was fighting all that
she could from her wheelchair,
hands stiff from illness, still
she smiled, had the patience to
smile, though she could never
speak.
This is no ordinary life for
her, yet she did not complain,
had no anxiety in her eyes,
Alice, who could teach us
all so much, just smiled,
and I smiled back, hope
she felt the warmth I felt.
This one's for Alice.
