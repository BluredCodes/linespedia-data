Fit me in your suitcase,
I curl and crawl so small,
they wouldn't ever see,
and when you're there I would
whisper, come play with me,
howling winds will leave us be.

But when I unfold, I will
show you then, show you just
how good you make me feel,
how good I want you to feel,
but for now, pack me very
tightly, and wait for my call.
