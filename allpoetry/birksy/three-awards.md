I’ve won just three awards,
a prize for science,
a prize for high jump,
and third prize for playing pool,
I’m a winner at not winning,
which is why I’m just a little
paranoid.
But don’t mind me, it’s
really quite all right,
my lack of expectancy,
is really what makes me,
me, but when I win,
oh, when I win,
it makes it all the
sweeter.
