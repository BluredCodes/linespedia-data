She won't be quiet, she gives
a running commentary, a running
down commentary, on the bleeding
bloody obvious, her husband and son
are beaten down, can't speak
as they'll just be spoken over, she
always knows best.
Wonder why both men read, does she?
Well, wonder no more, I'll tell, but
of course she won't listen, won't
want to hear, I doubt she can even
see me, best be quiet, I'm not
one to be interrupted.
