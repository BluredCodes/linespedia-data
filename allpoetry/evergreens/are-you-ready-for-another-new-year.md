title: "Are you ready for another new year?"

New year begins slowly
As a breeze entering the garden;
Midway it gains momentum
Towards the end
'Tis a swirling tornado!
On new year's eve,
The same gentle breeze creeps in.

Even mice never die
On fear of cat's jaws!
Life is full of contradictions-
Be it tragedy or a mystery
Expected or unexpected!
Come what may,face it.

Night is going to dawn
Even if you're sleeping-
New year wont stop coming
Tho' you may not be ready.
Crossing another mile-stone,
Welcome the new year which is born,
With outstretched willing palms!Time is flying,and,before we realise,new year is upon us.
Read,how it feels!
