Darwin said,'tis evolution; 
Religion pledges it is Creation! 
Life started on planet earth, 
Filling up whole length and breadth! 

Plants,animals and humans 
Form the unberakable chain 
Plants spilling out seeds, 
Which sprout and grow- 
Side by side and expanding 
Harmoniously with each other! 

Animal herds roaming, 
Breeding in the tranquil forests 
Multiplying without hindrance; 
With unspoken love and bonhomie! 

Plants have four senses; 
Five senses,animals possess: 
Man alone boasts of the Sixth sense 
Plants and animals never kill for gain 
Man alone kills and stands low in life chain!!Please email frank comments!  That'll help me
