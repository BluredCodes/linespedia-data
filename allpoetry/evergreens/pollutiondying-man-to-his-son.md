Open your eyes and look at the sky.
Rising sun emits rays-
The fog enveloping the whole town
Sunlight scarcely hits the floor!

Take a deep breath
To let the clean air fill the lungs!
Alas,the ozone layer is depleted,
The air is polluted!

Drink the spring water
To quench your thirst;
My child,the water is salty-
Contamination has spoiled its taste!

Plough the land deep
So that you could plant crops:
My dear,the land is barren
Radiation has devoured its fertility!A man in his death-bed remembers the good old times,when
everything was fresh and lovely,unpolluted.  Industrial pollution has taken its toll on nature.   This is an old villager lamenting,to his young sun.
