The pain is all encompassing,
Heart-breaking and intimidating;
Heavy numbness engulfs
Taking away hunger and sleep.

Neither go around the pain
Nor put up a proud face:
Admit,go through the ache-
Weep,cry out and shed tears.

Now, energy runs out,
Tear- drops stop and mouth dry
Strange emptiness descends in;
On its wake,mind clears,fog lifts!

World is too vast,out there!
You'll find love from unexpected quarters-
Unselfish and undemanding;
It'll make you bloom like an orchid!After seperation,what is in store for the divorcee?  Is it the

end of the world?  Hopefully,no!  New vista is waiting to open.
