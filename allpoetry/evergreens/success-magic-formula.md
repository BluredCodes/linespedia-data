For the perpetual loser-
Here is a poser!
Change the way you think and act,
Come outa the self built pit.

The perfectionist is a fool:
Workaholic,a self defeater;
The lazy one is a parasite
Busy-body,a non-starter!

Draw a delicate line
Between your job and life-
The two should'nt overlap
Or else,you'll lose sleep!

Remember,your job is what you do;
Who you are,is irrelevant
Perform,give out the best in you
That's the MAGIC FORMULA!The distinction between life and work is diminishing,due
to the fast life-style.  Where does this lead to?
Read the poem,the answer lies there!
