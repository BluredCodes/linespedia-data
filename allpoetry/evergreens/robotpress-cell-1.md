In the bright morn sunlight,
People scurrying down the street
Eyes unseeing and lips tight-
Cellphone glued to the ear.

Look up at the face
Say,"Good morning!" and smile;
Untethered electronic voice
Is increasingly becoming a vice!

Net chat,email,voice mail and what not?
Human alienation is rather complete-
Cyber revolution is great;
The unintended fallout is bitter!

Penned mail has its flavour,
Personal touch has no match!
Have self-imposed technology limit
Tame the cyber-storm,save future!Penned mail has an aroma of a special kind!  Electronic gadgets have taken the fun out of letter writing; Fellow poets,if you care for a pen mail,why not,write a mail to:
K.M.G.Vivekanandam,P.O.box #1,Tiruparan kundram,Madurai.625005,India.
