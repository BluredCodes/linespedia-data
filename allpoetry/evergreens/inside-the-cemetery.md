The wilderness is frightening,
Landscape intriguing;
Cemetery gates stand wide open
In the bright evening sun.

Cemetery floor embedded with black stones-
Eerie silence hangs like a blanket
Lawn and flower-bed seem well-kept,
Flowers,full of life and fragrant!

Crossing over several tombs
Where lifeless bodies lay underneath-
Tombstones standing dumb;
I came to the grey stone.

"Mom",A silent cry breaks out
The inscription says your name,
Wordless sadness fills my heart:
I kneel down and pray,"Ma......"A visit to the cemetery brings back memories of the loved
parents;  Walking down memory lane,we find peace.
