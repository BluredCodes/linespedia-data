I'm not asking for the moon!
Just a few pals to call my own!!
Webmail transcends the continents-
In just a matter of seconds.
The lightning speed email
Is of no avail--
If it is not for making new friends,
Setting up a new trend!!This poem has been selected for publication in,"The Best poems and Poets of 2001" and it has also brought recognition
as,'Poet of International order'  I thank God,for this honour!
