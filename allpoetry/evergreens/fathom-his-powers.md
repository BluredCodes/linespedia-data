Have you ever seen GOD?
During prayer and penance,
Inner self goes into trance
A quiet sense of holiness transcends!

Your physical self
And the rest of world-
Melt into one and merge;
Limitless awareness emerges!

Non-believers are the bunch,
Living in a spiritual no-man's land
Lost in mind bending hall of mirrors;
They can't fathom HIS  powers!

Close the eyes:
Draw your inner self,out!
Immerse in prayer:Focused.
Feel the gentle awakening-GOD!Faith in God gives meaning and purpose to human life. Non-believers miss out the wonderful experience.  How to realise God,this poem tries to explain.
