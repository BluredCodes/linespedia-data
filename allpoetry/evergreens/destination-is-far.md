Life is a speeding train,
Going along an one-way terrain,
Wherever the track leads it,
Never turning back!

Ambition and hard-work
Are these two tracks;
Parallels never meet-
Journey stretches out
Time is flying past.

Some reach the destination:
They climb out and disappear!
Others travel onward-
Hearts full of hopes-forward,
Waiting their turn!On a train journey,we meet several people,talk to them,
and when the time comes for departure,we get down and leave.
Symbolically,life is also a train.  We go on board,travel
and then leave for ever!
