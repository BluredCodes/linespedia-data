When I look deep into your eyes, surroundings disappear 
The feel of your kiss with your soft sensuous lips, as you place your strong 
hands upon my hips 
And you press your body up to mine, so we become near 
Your touch relieves me, removing any fear 
When you gently kiss my neck, the feel of your breath so hot and sweet, fills 
my body with extreme heat 
You slowly lower your kiss, down my neck, passed my stomach until nothing but 
pure bliss 
The feel of your tongue as it gently glides against my flesh sends shivers up 
my spine 
My moans and sighs show you that it feels so devine, 
I want to feel the love that causes the friction to burn 
You make my body hunger, you make my body yearn 
My insides already so wet as you finally push your manhood inside 
Then we start on our never-ending ride as we move together in synch as we 
rhythmically dance 
Our bodies entwined as one, our souls combine
