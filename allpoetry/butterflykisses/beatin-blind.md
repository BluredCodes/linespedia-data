---
title: "Beatin Blind"
writer: "ButterflyKisses"

He decides what clothes I wear,If it were up to him I'd have no hair.I'd be locked up alone in the house,But I can't leave him, he's my spouse.He makes me wash, cook and clean,Some things I do are just absence.He hides my make-up and accessories too,And if I wear them he'll beat me blue.I feel so alone, I have no life,I didn't realize he'd be so much strife.So how could I ever put this to an end,If he doesn't allow me to have one friend.I simply have no where to go,I try to get him to change, but he says 'No'I thought about dying and freeing my soul,As this constant abuse has taken it's tole.I am weak and just can't let go,But I really want the world to know.Please don't let your love control your mind,Because love has beaten me truly blind
