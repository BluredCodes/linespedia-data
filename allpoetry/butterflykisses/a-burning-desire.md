title: "A Burning Desire"

Our hearts our filled with a burning desireMay be its cause we need to be inspiredTo be in love for the very first timeOnly heavens can hold this power of all timeThe moment you and your lovers eyes meetWill cause a rare feeling of burning heatIn your hearts and in your soulsLike something you never felt beforeThe burning desire to hold the person closeTo contain dat feeling which no one knowsThe craving for a little kissBut does this love truly existThis feeling is rarely feltThat two lovers hearts should ever meltBut if this feeling is really trueThen you would never again have to feel the bluesSo why not give love a chanceFor you get to make sweet love and romanceTo hold your lover ever so tightAnd not letting go through out the nightThen you whisper I love you in thier earAnd all of a sudden everything looks so clearThe emotions you feel feel so rightKnowing that your lover is here with you tonight
