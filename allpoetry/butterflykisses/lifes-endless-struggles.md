In our lives there are many times we struggle, 

It's almost as if, we're stuck inside a big, empty bubble. 

In a place where we're the only ones to hear ourselves speak, 

And your the outcast, the freak. 


In a society like this 

There is no happiness. 

There is only hate and rage 

From being stuck inside this fucking cage. 


When your being put down left and right, 

You have to come out and see the light, 

These people aren't your friends. 

They aren't a part of you. 

Do you see what you let them try to do? 


Your losing control. 

You've got to fight back. 

Now is the time to attack. 


You want to be free? 

Don't let them take your dignity 

I know your a sensitive person and you just like to cuddle 

But this is one of life's endless struggles.
