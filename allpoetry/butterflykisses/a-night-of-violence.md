---
title: "A Night of Violence"
writer: "ButterflyKisses"

The look in your eyes was so unforgettable, as you grabbed me by my neck with a force that was incredible. You pushed me into the wall so hard and violently, I was in such shock, I just stood there silently. I kept thinking to myself, this has never happened before, as I fell to my knees and pushed you out the door. My biggest fear has just happened to me tonight, only it was so much worse then i ever thought it might. I can no longer trust another guy, for i think of that night and begin to cry. One night of violence has put me to sleep, every guy in my eyes is just another creep.
