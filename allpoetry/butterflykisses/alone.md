---
title: "Alone"
writer: "ButterflyKisses"

There is an empty feeling inside of me, 

as hollow as can be. 

The feeling, I fear, of being alone in this 

world. 

Where can I go for peace? 

Who can I turn to for love? 

For there is nowhere I can go. 

No one I can turn to. 

I do not want to live in this world, 

if I am to be alone, 

for the fear of myself and no one else, 

is too great for my soul to 

bear. 

I pray that I rather die than live in this 

world, 

alone.
