I'm not Cinderella at the ball
or Cinderella at all

I'm a human being here to fulfill my dreams
Not to fulfill your duties, hopes, and dreams

To me suicide is the best option
Slitting my wrist,drowning,gun,or knife
Maybe hanging myself would would be best

I wanna go fast and soon
I wanna go now
God take me I'm ready

My parents are making me suffer
If thats how they feel
Then let it be
I just wanna go

Take my hand Lord
Lead me home
were angels sing and praise you
not destroy your life like my parents did mine
