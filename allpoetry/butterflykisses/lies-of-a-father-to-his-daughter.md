She tried to run, 

But she was stopped. 

Told that she was to obey. 

No opinions. 

No rights. 

And no justice. 

* 

He beat her, 

Told her it was her fault. 

Ever since she was a child, 

She believed it, 

Apologizing for nothing. 

* 

Her battered soul, 

Vulnerable and revealing, 

Was an open wound 

On perfect flesh, 

Only too late 

Knowing the pain of death.
