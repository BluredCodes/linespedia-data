title: "A word of warning"
writer: "ButterflyKisses"

Don't ever fall in love, my friend
You'll find it doesn't pay
Although it causes broken hearts
It happens everyday.
You'll wonder where he is at night
And wonder if he's true
One moment you'll be happy
The next you'll be blue.
Each time you chance to meet him
Your heart begins to dance
Your world revolves around him
There's nothing like romance.
Then it happens, and you don't know why
You worry every night
Yes, my friend, you're losing him
Love never turns out right.
Love is fine, but hurts too much
