---
title: "Eternal Sleep"
writer: "ButterflyKisses"
slug: "eternal-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16927-Eternal-Sleep-by-ButterflyKisses"

Darkness consumes
my heart
my body slowly 
becomes
limp and lifeless
drowning in misery
life is meaningless
the blood drains 
from my face
the knife I hold
is my gift
to you
my scream
is forgotten
in the chilling air
I collapse
on the floor
another worthless soul
confined 
to the realm
of Darkness
