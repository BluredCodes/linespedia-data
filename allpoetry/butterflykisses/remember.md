Even when red anger flares
Through the blue of my eyes
When the sharpest double-edged words
Find freedom from my lips
Remember…

When crystal tears 
And nothing you do seems to dry them
When you swear to God with all your heart
That I am walking away
Remember…

When the joyfulness of life
Seems to leave my face
And I cannot find the words
To tell you what you mean to me
Remember…

Remember that I love you
More than simple words could ever say
More than the sun, the moon, the stars
Remember that I love you
Now and forever
And love me more.
