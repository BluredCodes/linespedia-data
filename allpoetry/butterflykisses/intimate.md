the sky limits love nowhere

i want to go somehow

division of destiny

where to? 

doors close

belted in for endless

a white knuckle trip of love

anger changes to sex

visions pass in windows

through glass eyes

a cloudless sky

rain falls

solitary dreams drift off in steam

2 people bonded

read my eyes

tell me where i am

explain

lose yourself in me as do i

dream

pushed back by excitement

scared

happy only in his arms
