should i appear so desperate, can you telli am trapped in my hell begging for life, striving for my intentioni am positive there are many things i intentionallyforgot to mentiondarkness pervadessadness invadesdays come in a mixture of light and darknesseverything is nothingi am nothingso...i am everything?harken to the soundvoices aroundcoming from the groundwhispering dark promisesin my most desperate hour
