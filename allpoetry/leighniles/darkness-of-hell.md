title: "darkness of hell"

Dancing with the
Devil on the
Demons grave
I embrace all
That is unholy.
Reveling in the
Degeneration of 
Society the otherworld
Beckons from the darkness.
Succumbing to
Immoral influences
I am engulfed 
By flames
Forsaking my soul
To the blazing inferno
of hell.
