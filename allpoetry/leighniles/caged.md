the caged birdrages valiantly againstthe obstruction of barrierserected to maintain control.struggling to preservecourage and dignityamidst the condemningjudgments of externalinvaders.free the caged birdand give flight to the soaring wingsof hope.yielding the contemptuousrantings to faithin human strength.
