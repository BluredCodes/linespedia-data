what demon beast lurksbeneath calmly serene facades...fashioning such crueltyfrom seeming innocence...to release a torturedsoul onto humanity...gives reign to a lifemired in darkness.the world must seekto expunge such perfidy...visiting retributionto those who woulddeem to forsake morality...unmasking the grotesque demonsof avarice, corruption and hate...baring those vicesto unsympathetic judgmentthus vanquishing the sourceof their power.
