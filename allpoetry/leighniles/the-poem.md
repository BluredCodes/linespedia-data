words flow freely
giving voice 
to inner thoughts;
their structure forming
into a natural
cadence of rhyme or prose.

phrases that reveal emotions
masked by velvety symbolism;
the words wander down paths
well traversed or 
expose what is behind
walls of denial.

images brought forth
using diverse styles
to entertain
or impart ideas
for others to 
revel in or deny.

immersed in the litany of verse
revealing hidden depths
of sentiment and pain
to unknown observers peering
through the windows of a soul.

taking that poems very heart
to the surface and giving.
it the value of understanding
to render its author complete.

do not forsake the necessity
of a peers approval or critique;
the search for affirmation
gives power to the individual
flaw of .....need.

so, to all that survey 
the varied musings of 
a poets wandering imagery....
give true assessment ...
a token of respect.
