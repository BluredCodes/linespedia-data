what demon beast lurksbeneath calmly serene facades...fashioning such crueltyfrom seeming innocence...releasing tortured soulsonto an unsuspecting humanity...giving reign to livesmired in darkness?the world must seekto expunge such perfidy...visiting retribution to thosewho would deem to forsake morality...unmasking the grotesque demonsof greed, avarice, and hate to judgement...exposing the depths of darknessand vanquishing their power.
