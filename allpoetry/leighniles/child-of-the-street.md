title: "child of the street"

night descends with bleak oppression..darkness advancing cloaked in shadows..city a blanket of murky obscurity..serpentine influences invitations to succumb..persuasive devilry beckons susceptible youth..relinquish grasp of morality to nocturnal comrades..abandon languor to angst in fervant supplication..unity of numbers transforming intrepid souls..to embrace the night as a yawning chasm..solitary sins of city now awaken to another time..surival only a token of the few..borne from a sufference of morose trespasses..
