Sleeplessly dreaming,
I await the arrival of my love.
Knowing that the night
will deliver him to me.
The cool breeze gently
caressing my nudity;
I feel his prescence 
and watch as his sillouette
is framed inside the door.
Walking silently towards me
he disrobes.
His muscular form bringing
a throbbing to my loins;
eyes closed in anticipation
of what is to come.
The whisper of his lips
glancing across mine
sparking an instant flame.
I turn to him in supplication
and surrender
myself to him.
As his lips touch the 
hidden hollows of my body
I arch myself closer
wanting more...more...
My breasts tender 
as his hands and lips caress...
bringing me to the brink..
almost there.
He touches me 
exploring me intimatley
his finger delving in deeply...
in a rhythmic motion
he brings me to that
special place where I 
gasp my pleasure.
Then suddenly...he is gone.
I look around 
searching for my lover
in the moonlit shadows
of my room.
Knowing that I will 
not find him.
I smile secretively
as I pass into slumber
knowing he will come again.
