its just another day...with this mangled malcontentmixing mayhem and memories...its just another day...of saccharine statements slowlyseeping sweet succulance...its just another day...trading testaments and tribulationswith tirades of transitory terms...its just another day...of prideful preening protractinga pandemonium of passionate pleasures...its just another day.just want honest opinions...am new to this...
