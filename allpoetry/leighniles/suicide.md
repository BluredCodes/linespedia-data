---
title: "suicide"
writer: "leighniles"
slug: "suicide"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

tightness
constricting bands
of pressure
binding my soul

nerves
stretched taut
control lost to
vacuous emotions

insanity 
a prison 
of remorse
borne of quilt

finality
of death
bringing
ultimate release

eyes closed
to pain
sleep
blessed oblivion
