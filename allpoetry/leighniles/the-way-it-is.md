title: "the way it is"

This is the way it it,the way it has been;and the way it will always be.I do unto you,as you do unto me,so, think before you do.Give me no ifs,no ands and no buts;not even a maybe.For you did unto me,what should never be done;that's the end can't you see?You should have thought,before and not after;I don't need your apology...
