there are badgers here on this site hiding and watching for all that is right!!you never know where they will bewho they all are and what they will see...we warily view their weaving plotshoping and praying not to get shot!!but secretly yearning to be one of the fewthat breaks the ranks of this marvelous crew...we would gladly join in and gleefully cryBANDER SNATCH, BANDER SNATCH....DO OR DIE!!!!!!!!!!
