A night of promise
Two people holding on
moving shadows in the darkness.

Bodies glistening with desire
Intertwined into one
Together, yet incomplete.

Hearts shuttered
To reality of night
No promises spoken.

Breathe quickening
Touching, feeling, discovering
Passions sated.

Early rays of dawn
Minds of confusion
Reality faced.

Passions of the night
Forgotten as two strangers meet
No words to fill the silence.

They were as one
Now far apart
THe timeless moment of darkness
Now faint memories of regret....
