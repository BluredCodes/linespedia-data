the in and outmotion of a sweating pigpaying for this little gig.grunting and groaningwith this hairy swinementally counting whatwill soon be mine.he thinks that he isgetting me hotwhen you can bet your ass he's not.as he rolls off in a snoring dozei step back into my night clothes.as i close the doori am suddenly sadfor once againi've had sex with my dad!
