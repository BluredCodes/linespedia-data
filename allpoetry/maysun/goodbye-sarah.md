---
title: "Goodbye Sarah"
writer: "Maysun"
slug: "goodbye-sarah"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31033-Goodbye-Sarah-by-Maysun"
---

No road is ever too long to walk down,
And no path is too short to lead my way, 
May my angels stay by my side each and every day, 
God give me strength, 
God give me will, 
God give me a heart made of steel, 
I need God's comfort, his joy, his heart, 
His angels, his peace, his love in part. 
When you lose someone you love, 
It doesn't seem quite real, 
You always wish you could see them one more time, 
So you could give your last good-bye, 
Kiss their lips just for one more minute, 
Look into their eyes, 
Hold them in your arms, 
Feeling so safe from harm. 
This is so hard on me, 
I'm trying to be strong, 
I try fighting my tears so no one can see, 
My soul is forever bleeding without you here with me, 
Not one day or night passes, 
When I don't see an image of your face, 
I sometimes wish someone could take your place, 
Not your place in my heart and soul, 
But in Heaven where you are so bold, 
I am so weak now, 
And you have all the strength, 
I'd do anything to have you back, 
Or just to see you one more time, 
To give my last good-byeRest in peace Sarah I love you sweetie and I will NEVER forget you.
