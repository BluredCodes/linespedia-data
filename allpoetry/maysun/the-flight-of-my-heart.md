---
title: "The Flight Of My Heart"
writer: "Maysun"

The song I hear as the wild geese fly,
Lonely and haunting and blue,
Is the song that echoes deep in my heart
Whenever I think of you.

It's the siren song of a distant land
Where my true love dreams of me.

I follow that haunting wild goose song
Off to the eastern terrains,
But the wild geese left- they've flown away;
Now only the song remains.
