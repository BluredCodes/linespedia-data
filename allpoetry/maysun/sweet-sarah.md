---
title: "Sweet Sarah"
writer: "Maysun"

Sweet Sarah, you saw the doors of heaven open up 
   and God's arms extend to embrace your loving soul.
   You felt the warmth of the Lords light cradle your heart 
   so you ventured to his land of peace and love.

Sweet Sarah, for that we understand, 
   but we will never forget the joys you have given us.
   Your smile, Your eyes, the way you said good-bye.
   Your love, Your touch, the way you cared so much.

Sweet Sarah, it is not enough just to say we love you and miss you 
   but there will be no other quite like you.
   For you are and were unique... A one-of-a-kind.
   Always in our hearts, Always in our minds.

Sweet Sarah...Rest in peace Sarah 11/1/2002
