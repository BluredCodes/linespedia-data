Maybe you can see, maybe your blind, But if love was blind then you and me wouldn't be here.   Sometimes it seems as if I didn't matter to you. If it's so then tell me true.  You crushed my life and my hope .  But maybe if there is a shredd left then I can crush yours to.  But trust me you'll pay.  Then i'll get my hopes and dreams out from your cluches and live my life normally .  Unlike you.
