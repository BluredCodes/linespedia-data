Blind are the eyes of you
So why shed a tear if not to see it drop
So that is why you came to me weeping .
Don't shed another tear for me 
Because I need no chiarty
 Not from the one who killed my father
So rot in hell for all I care.
You are no threat to me .
But never shed another tear for me
Because your tears are of no worth.
So go and leave me now before something worse happensHow is the poem it's one of my best.
