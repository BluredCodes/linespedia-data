I'm in love, when he draws near my heart pounds so loud even the person farthest from me can hear. my body yarns for the brush of his lips against mine, my soul has a hiddin delight,I was rejected by the one and only person I love.  My frinds are no help they bug him  but yet I told them not too. Yet I hope and can only hope that someday he will like me.       Oh Jason Wood how much I love you.I really have a crush on Jason but I want him to like me because he does not because he wants people to stop bugging him.            Beth
