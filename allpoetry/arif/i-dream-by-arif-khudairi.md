I dream Of the riverIn whichI swamIn delightWhen I was A little child.I dreamOf the skies,Blue and wide,Like the eyesOf my beloved.I dream Of the trees,Tall and proud,Like the gypsyPretty maidensDancing tenderly In the middleOf the night.I dreamOf the menOf my landWorking hardIn their fieldsAt dawn,And ofThe womenAt their side.I dreamOf the timeWhen I sitBy the riverOne more time,And writeMy poemsOf love,Of peace,And ofA better life.
