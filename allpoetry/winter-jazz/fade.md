Dreams of you ceaseMemories of you fadePanic and fear wells upAs I realized My image of you blurs.I cling on to your pictureAnd the very last scrapOf memories of you.I fear so much of losing themAfter I’ve lost you.The face I once loved soThe smile that radiated from youThe sweet, sweet sound when you giggleThe wonderful times we spent togetherWhy can’t I paint a picture of you nowWhy can’t I hear your melodiesYour voice in my mindIt hasn’t been that longSince I held your hand in that bedYour hand turned coldAnd your breath stopped.I hung my head in shameAs if I’ve done a great wrongIndeed a sin! A heinous sin!I thought my love is oh so strongI thought my grieve will never cease.What do I do now?What can be done now?The pain and the hurt still hereYet distant it is.I wanted to cryBut my eyes are dryWhat do I do now?Now I’ve lost her I more time.
