Screams of anguish
Piercing through the dark
Silent noises whispered loudly
Shattering the soul
The plea for peace
The search for God
The cry for help
The loss of hope
Will it never end?
Will there be salvation?
The lust for flesh mistaken as the need for love.
Immorality rules
Didn't you know?

God took a walk on earth the other day
He left in disappointment.
