Dancing for the certain idea
the perfect incomplete idea
of the blurry image
of a life that was never owned
by you

The dissatisfaction grows on you
Like a starfish on the reefs
growing, multiplying
mutilating...

And you love it
you swim in it
swirling around the Medusa
flowing the caress of cold tide

Who cares about the real world?
It's a place for suckers.
