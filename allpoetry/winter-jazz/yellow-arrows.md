They are everywhere
The yellow arrows
Boldly printed on trains, stations, buildings
Dictating you where to walk, 
what to follow
In the name of efficiency and smoothness
During the peak hours

And we follow
Spending most of our lives following
These bold arrows
that is 
painted by somebody we don’t even know

Where do these arrows lead us?
Who knows, but who 
cares anyway
We just need them there……
