Freak meAnd let the entire world seeThe torn and tattered bloody soulMy bloodless bodyContainsNever has the world cried so silentlyThat my soul shattered with the batterings of accusationsHurt meGo aheadThe tendrils of the dying octopusnever fails to attract the sharksLet not morals stop your thirst for bloodI'm waiting
