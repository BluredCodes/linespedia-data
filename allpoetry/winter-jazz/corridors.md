The corridors are paved with goldBut why do I not see the glitters?It ought to be thereI felt the cold, sleek surfaceAs it drains my heat awayLike the law of Physics sayit would.Maybe I'm color blindWhich explains whyMy reflection on thesurfaceis gray.Perhaps I forgot to take my shadesoffBut try as I mightI cannot remove it.Maybe I'm blind.
