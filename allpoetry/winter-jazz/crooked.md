title: "Crooked"
writer: "winter jazz"

Do not flicker among the whims of night     Somehow it just takes away the sighs     Never mind the simple tears you shed     Once they dry up, they are lost in the air     So push the bated breath into your stomach     Let the willowed pain drill through within     Allow the parasite to swell as you choke     out....     A crooked smile     And that's all the whole world will see.
