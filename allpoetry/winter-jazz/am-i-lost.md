Am I lost?
Perhaps I am
Turning away from the four white walls
Away from the flickering screen
And my rigid body
Cemented into the grey floor
But my mind….
A flaw from the entire structure
Of my soul.

Minutes ticked by
Perhaps the plane of has lost its engine
As it slowly muttered its prayer
And turn away from its essential existence.
But the plane is still moving
But not forward.

My bible is lost.
But it’s just a book
But yet it’s God’s Word
But it can be bought easily
Available at the nearest bookstore.

Am I lost?
