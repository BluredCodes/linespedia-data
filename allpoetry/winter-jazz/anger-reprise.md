Something went wrong     My life goes weary     I seethe     The tide turns     Against my will     I boil     The world is wrong     I am right     My eyes turn red     It is dark     But I'm not over     I turn from my grave     That is me     A man of worldly sins     Committed out of seeds planted by the first couple     that flourishes within me     With the fertilizers of my flesh.
