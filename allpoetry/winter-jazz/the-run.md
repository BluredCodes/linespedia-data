A run in the night 
Felt the cool wind blow
The world started spinning
Spinning out of control
I turn around
And saw that the sea was dark
People were fishing
But blood was gushing
From the sea, from the ground
And from the creature that emerged
Deep from the dark seas
Desperately trying to get a grip
To grab hold of my sanity
Instead I committed worldly sins
And lost my salvation.

I prayed, and prayed, and prayed
But my bible was burned
By the hatred and the angst
The world has left inside me
Now hope was rapidly diminishing
And life was creeping away.

I questioned life, and questioned fate
I saw the stars, but felt the darkness
I spin and spin, round and round
But the world stopped turning
And judgement day arrived

"Stop it" I exclaimed
A cry from within
"The world did not stop for me
It's me that stopped believing"
My eyes were closed
My ears were locked
The air that I breathe in
Simply rejected me.

I reached out, someone touched me
It was the bible that was never burned
I felt the heat, but not from burning
The heat is not cold, but full of warmth
And then I realised
As I looked into the sky
The night is over
And the sun...
Finally rises
Before me I stood
The heat enveloped me
Took me by mercy.

I am ready.
