---
title: "LOVE'S SORROW"
writer: "Aaron J Clarke"
slug: "loves-sorrow"
source: "AllPoetry"

Where are the stars?For love's sorrow is why they don't shine.Where is the sun on morn's eve?The sun will not light the world's gaiety for love's sorrow.Why do the cocks not sing the joys of life?Their hens are cold to touch for love's sorrowhas killed them.
