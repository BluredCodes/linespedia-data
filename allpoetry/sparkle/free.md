Be free little friend
Fly away with the wind
Like a feather, my friend
Fly away, fly away

The world around you doesn't see
What it's like to be free
They trap you in and never let go

If they could only realize
What it's like to be free
Oh, how truly happy they would be

Don't supress your own freedom
Let it shine through
Don't worry about the others
Don't change yourself just for them
What do they matter to you?

One day they'll realize
What it's like to be free
They'll step back and see all the things they've missed

They won't understand why they trapped you in so long ago
It will be exactly how they feel then
Unable to express any newfound joys

So be free little friend
Be free while you can
People don't understand
So fly away, fly away
Fly away while you can...
