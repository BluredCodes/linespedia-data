title: "Fire and Ice"

The door creaks open
I know it's cold there
Yet a sense of heat overcomes me
So hot that it sends chills down my spine
The outside is the illusion

The door slams shut
I feel the heat rush in
It's burning, it's burning...
Yet I sense cold

This time it can't be hidden 
No illusion for that
Yet no one seems to notice
No one seems to care

Ignorance?
No, this can't be ignored
It's obvious, yet can't be detected
Plainly revealed, yet hidden
Open, yet shut
Hot, yet cold...A long time ago...God only knows when...,
