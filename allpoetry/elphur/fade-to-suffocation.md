---
title: "Fade to Suffocation"
writer: "elphur"
slug: "fade-to-suffocation"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42831-Fade-to-Suffocation-by-elphur"
---

blurred pastel and softly,
he rolls to uncomfortable.
luke warm slow blue
makes his presence here;
smudged, scratched fault
when he falls from clouds.
lack of dimension;
my world molds his face.
sweet mouth and sour colour—
thoughtful waves subside.
old existence haze and moist,
his eyes so soothed.
a stale lullaby well sung
drifts away sluggish.
a timeless void and his tonight
breathes the fog, spits the air;
his features deep,
his tired throat,
fade by sparkling pressure.
yellows thick and broad;
his heart seeps and crawls.
blood coughed to the surface—
he chokes on truth’s tongue.
winged fear in hand,
odd numbers.

call to me—
and rest your body weary;
lest you suffocate, my love.
