title: "Fantasy"

Where bluebirds feed
and lemons grow
I will wait ‘til sunset’s glow.
As soft leaves curl
and blue light fades
I will wed the King of Spades.
Dressed with winds
and rippled robes
we’ll glide along
the golden globes.
And with toppled grace
and thoughtful care
may we live happily 
ever after there.
