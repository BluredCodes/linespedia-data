---
title: "Everlasting Echo"
writer: "elphur"
slug: "everlasting-echo"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42834-Everlasting-Echo-by-elphur"
---

fear not of the life ahead without me.
be steady, be strong for the others
for this i confide in you
and this is what shall carry through your self.
hold your head up, take in these words:

when i first saw your soul
i saw mine own.  
i saw a wholeness and a heart
and i instantly knew its strength,
one that could thaw the cold
one that drips with beautiful life.

recall what i say now
when all seems black and white,
and you will feel my breath colouring your eyes.
for i will not be above soon,
i choose to be beside
from where i can experience of your future 
your thick happiness,
and i will soothe your despair.

death is of the body,
the soul's cage that melts as the 
flesh lies to die.    
my body is limited, even now
as you have caused my soul 
everlasting, loving life.

i am forever alive.

keep your own soul living, use your strength.
speak to me often, i will always hear.
look to your hand and you will always find
that mine will be there too.
