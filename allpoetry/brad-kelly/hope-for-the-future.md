---
title: "Hope for the Future"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "hope-for-the-future"
source: "AllPoetry"

Somewhere,
                 underneath this floor
                 reflecting fluorescent light
                 and echoing hollow 
                 the synchronized footfalls 
                 of 6 billion victims,
                 is something different.

         Somewhere,
                  outside of these four
                  solid walls pushing in
                  on the contents of 
                  the thinking man's skull
                  squeezing him tight
                  like priceless fruit
                  for one drop of homogenous
                  production to be thrown in the mix
                  Is something honest.

         Somewhere,
                  withing the infinite
                  galaxy of some
                  as yet unborn knower
                  of all truth and love.
                  Inside their timeless,
                  pathetically human skull
                  is the answer to everyone's question
                  Somewhere there is freedom.
