title: "I sometimes walk . . ."

I sometimes walk
the city streets
lofty and detached.
Envisioning around
me what was once 
a great forest.
Breathing the 
distant memory
of fresh air.
Hallucinating the
sun glide effortless.
Shining down on 
a humble and innocent Earth.

Awake from my daydream to 
something completely different.
