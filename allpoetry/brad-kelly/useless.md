title: "Useless"

People talk in circles,not to solve problemsjust to kill minutes,hours,  lonelinessAll complete nonsense,repetitious and annoyingTelevision living vicariously thru us.Its words fall from eroding teethonto the floorslither tween chairs and soap boxes and up your pantlegRather sit w/ jaws wired shut,face redwith the tension mounting since birthI have already heard every feasible conversationI already know what you will say.
