title: "Dreaming"

Asleep at night
I dream of starry-eyed
angels rescuing me 
from a discarded world.
Carrying me willingly 
past old billboards
and up beyond the corpses 
of architectual acheivement.
Thru smoggy sky and
to distant planets.
