Floats in like paper
caught in silent wind,
only to remind how
lucky I once was.
Blue smoke begins 
to form a halo 
around my head but breaks
when I laugh in
exaggerated nepenthe.

I walked this same path once before.It may be a little ambiguous but this poem is about turning to drugs in times of sadness (i.e. blue smoke).  Nepenthe means a sense of pleasing forgetfullness.
