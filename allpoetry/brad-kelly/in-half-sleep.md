---
title: "In Half-Sleep"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "in-half-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"

A quarter-mile down
the road to Nirvana,
Waiting coma-like
for full sleep
My mind spontaneously
creates endlessly
joyous or disturbing 
stories so dreamlike and
real that they are immediately forgotten
My cognizant mind
unprepared to remember 
the greatest art I create.
