---
title: "Free @ Last"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "free-last"
source: "AllPoetry"

Free, @ last,
from dusty nonmeclanture.
Retired from my
failed attempts
at being worthwile.
Reste up,
fully prepared to die
old and peaceful
under a bodhi tree,
sipping tea
and watching birds flutter overhead
Alone if need be
