Under a full moon,
we hold each other
afraid that the 
slightest hesitation
will seperate us forever.

Without apparent reason
we analyze the skyline,
looking for
something better.

Already my soul too old,
confused by excitement,
disinterested in anything 
that doesn't solve my mortality.I tried to show how someone might feel about the same situation, in this case on a "date" outside, as they get older and older.  The first one is perhaps a teenager, the last an elderly person.
