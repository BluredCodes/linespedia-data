---
title: "Purity Only From Within"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "purity-only-from-within"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2325-Purity-Only-From-Within-by-Brad-Kelly"

Choking slow-like on                               re-breathed air,                                     Ideas.                      Curl into a ball                        to hide from the world                              my light inside.                      The more I oppose                         the more they threaten to                       crack me like a white-picket fence                              breakfast egg.                 And make my soul digestable to the masses.                       The more I oppose.                      Stumbling drunk arcoss                            backyard BBQs,                            million dollar TV shows,                              disinfectant lifes.
