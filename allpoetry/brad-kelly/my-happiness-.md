My happiness in current events,
is only exceeded
by my eerie realization of mortality.
Flesh and bones,
a compilation of 
complimentary chemicals.
Prone to illness, malfunction,
despite of the heart's desire.
An all together complicated menagerie of "things"
All with different abilities
combined to achieve 
a purpose which is still unclear.
