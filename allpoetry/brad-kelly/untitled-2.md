Made benign by
these unnatural conditions.
I feel the blood draining
from me.
Feel my essence
swirling in pools at my feet.
Watch thru distant eyes,
the realization that
none of this world
really mattered

A cold hand clenches 
my shoulder.
Drags me off to 
display reality in
all its sadness.
