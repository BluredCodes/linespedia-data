For whatever reason,they killed us,and so proper revengemust be the same.Beautiful Women abusedfirst by religionnow by bombs,Twisted corpses in their arms.Everyone really trying to be in love.A few angry men.Lines 3 and 4 are meant as satire, Im sure everyone got that tho.
