title: "A Morning Unexpected"
writer: "Brad Kelly"

One morning,                 blissful as soft, dewy grass,                 I arise to realize                 everything lost,                  no memory haunts,                 no attachment grinds                 the blur finally clears                 and the indulgent world                 appears as it truly is.                 All my mind's tricks have                 lost their hold,                 My name and face forgotten                 , my suffering drown in illuminating amnesia.
