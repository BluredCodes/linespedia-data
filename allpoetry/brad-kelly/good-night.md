---
title: "Good Night"
writer: "Brad Kelly"

Walking thru
small towns at 
midnight
With no one awake.
With only starry-eyed
children staring
at unmoving moons.

Sleep is essential
to keep 
us from realizing 
the void in all 
of our lives.

Dusty boots
walk dusty roads
to points along
the Great dusty horizon.
