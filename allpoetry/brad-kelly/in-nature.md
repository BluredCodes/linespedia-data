title: "In Nature"
writer: "Brad Kelly"

Sun sets blissfully
red in the sand.
Completely motivated
to scream from tops
of mountains and
echo in river vallies
that I HAVE been 
living all this time.
The benevolent moon
rises wary of the 
beasts it conjures.
Desert sand like beautiful
arctic snow.
Haunted Castle mountains
rise black on the horizon.
