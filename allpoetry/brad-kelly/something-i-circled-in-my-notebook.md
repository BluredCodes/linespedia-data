Anger shakes my telephone pole
                breaks the calm
                 Interrupts a consistent flow
                of information from the distance 
                to this point and beyond

                Anger blows my tire on the road,
                I can only ride flop-flop, and slow
                        in its wake
                When I pull over to repair,
                I see a shortcut.

                Congratulating the athletic soul for trying
                Or acknowledging the intelligent mind for learning
                Is like praising a wheel for turning.
