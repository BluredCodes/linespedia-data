I haven't used theright words to describeanything in a long timeSit up nights.glaring wide-eyed intothe mirror, all memories swirling around my head like a halo.I am no angelBut I digress All the damage done,committed by me.Cars idling over crushed bones,those lost in the panicked rush to thei jobs. Hope oozes from the marrow.
