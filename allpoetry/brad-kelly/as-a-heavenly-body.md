---
title: "As a Heavenly Body"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "as-a-heavenly-body"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2420-As-a-Heavenly-Body-by-Brad-Kelly"

The moon and I                       have hungry conversations                       about long-lost lovers                       And the sun erupts,                         it can't withstand its own purity                        Man was about to shoot it with                       his jealous slingshot anyway.
