---
title: "Everyman"
writer: "Brad Kelly"
slug: "everyman"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52575-Everyman-by-Brad-Kelly"
---

He gets up
bare feet
in dewy grass
tells himself
a story
about the one
who got away

Drinks black,
black coffee
to startle him 
out of the 
sobering fiction
of his dreams.
