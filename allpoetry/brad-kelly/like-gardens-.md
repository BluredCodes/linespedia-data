title: "Like Gardens . . ."

Like Gardens
have wonderful flowers
and nourishment
Her soul is beautiful
and refreshing.
Eyes drag me with them
to some underground
lair.
Something below
intended to teach
my pessimistic
soul the 
art of love.
To show my prying
eyes what to
really look for.
