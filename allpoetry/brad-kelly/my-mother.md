title: "My Mother"

My mother was 
a raincloud,
I think as I lay
in dreamy half-sleep.
Dropped me down to
fertile planet earth
to spark some new
thoughts,
even amongst the
contentment rampant
in the rise of the 
world's industrialized nations.
Someone must be 
thirsty for truth 
within these four concrete
walls,
or hard at work
within future
archaeological digs.
