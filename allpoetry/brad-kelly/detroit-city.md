The Tallest buildings inDetroit, MIcannot contain my contempt for their purpose.Archaic brick,self-sacrificing cement.The world's only labor leftcast on the shoulders of the city'sbrown skinned progeny.The city has inevitablyfailed at being anythingbut the sum of its parts.As have I.Ever been to Detroit?
