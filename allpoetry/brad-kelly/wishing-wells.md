I jump into 
wishing wells
hoping for
pocket change to
buy marijuana
or a sandwich

Instead I find
the active
wishes of 
a hundred random
pedestrians:

Forlorn wishes for love,
for unattainable world peace,
a desperate wish for 
insight, underneath my foot

Numerous, hopefully
unfulfilled wishes 
for a million dollars
or a menage trois.

I step out of the 
wishing well still hungry.
and now confused.
