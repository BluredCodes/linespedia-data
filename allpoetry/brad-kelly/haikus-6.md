A rock sits, gentlypolished by water and timeA rough thing made smoothThe saddest part isI love everyone I seeAnd am still lonely.Smells sweeter than rose.Delicate like a dew dropHer soul as lovely.Each stanza is an independent haiku, they are not related ( OK maybe a little).The last one is a little too sappy for me.
