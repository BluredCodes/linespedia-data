title: "Society Encapsulated"

Everyone gathered in the lobby                waits iridescently glancing                 at out of synch timepieces               Bending to manufactured will,               Feeding a disease their parents helped create.               Adorned in velvet, and velcro.               One man speaks to another:               'If I knew then what I know now'               and searches furtively for               Russian spies,               Alligators in the sewer.               'I'd still be too scared to                  do anything.'
