I walk in the sun
listen to little birds chirp
homesick for heaven

Fell asleep angry
the world vanished in my dream
now I am alone

Tried to climb the door
and fell on my stupid ass
Try again laterTo understand the last one, the reader has to understand that rock climbing is one of the most important things in my life.  therefore I try to climb everything I see.
