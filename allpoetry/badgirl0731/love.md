Do you really love me?
I've been hurt so many times I just can't believe
Is your love for realor is it all pretend?
If this is just a dream, I don't ever want it to end
Because it seems I'm starting to realize your love is true
Will I finally love you?
Do I dare try to love?
Are you a sign from up above?
Well I guess I do love you too...
So I give up my heart for you
After awhile I'm crushed
And my broken heart is mushed
I can't believe in what you did but yet I can deal
This is the last time I fall for your appeal
Hopefully my heart will mend
Because I know love,heartbreak will never endthis is actually a true poem...goes to show don't date the group.
