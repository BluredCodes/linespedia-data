Not a sound

Bird nor breeze

Could it be that I'm alone?

Mountain top

Balsam and pine

Are these my steps,

Granola bar crunches

Drowning out the silence?



When the sound of life is drained away

Can this be all that's left?

Water that trickles

The hymn of a bee

Peace

Where cityscapes and sentences

Echo only in my mind



I've been here a day now

Not a word is spoken

No engines, no cries

Only stillness

And the conversations of flies
