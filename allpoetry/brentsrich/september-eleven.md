I did not know you
Sitting alone
Kitchen table
A toaster that sparks

Nor you
Who shuddered
With the sound of
Hot water
Rumbling through
The pipes

And you
Whose only crime
A daily one
Extra pat of
Butter
For your roll

What are your names
Crushed now
Beneath the weight
Of hatred, fear

What are your lives
But echoes
Glimpses
Of mine
