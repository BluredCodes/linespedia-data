title: "And Still I Wonder"

Tortured you left us

And hid behind

Mystery

And forgetfulness



To him you gave sadness

Like ribbons

Blue and purple

Imprinted and implied



To her you fed anger

And truth

Its teeth and shadow

Of stone



For me only empty

Were the words

You called love and

Understanding



And still I wonder



While you breathed such pain

Did you have to

Exhale?
