title: "Today I Began To Die"

A black spot on my finger
Grew evil in my mind
Dark shadow, specter
Of things that I might find

While full of its silence
The shade spoke soft to me
A hint of melancholy
And all that’s meant to be

Do days of loss await me?
Can pain be all I’ve wrought?
Sorrow my lone companion
Despite the peace I’ve sought?

No, a warning doesn’t beckon
When painted skin intones
But a reminder of darkness
And the brevity of bones
