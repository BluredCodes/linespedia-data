title: "Cigarette"

Ashtray
Twisted mouth
Cawing crimes
And misdemeanors

You couldn’t rest
Relight
Shuffle cards
Solitaire in the dimly lit
Kitchenette

How lonely were those
Weeks, months, years
Night into day into night

Pain
Both imagined and real
Breathed for you
While
Darkness encroached
Never to press out
That final
Cigarette
