title: "Loud Are the Voices"

There's something about rhyming
That seems so contrived
Beauty to fit
Round into square
The truth be told
With echoes and smiles
And roses need noses
Or fragrance is lost

How clumsy, how crude
Seem words with anchors
And loud are the voices
Of mirrorless lines

But do we just wander
Less these walls imposed?
Structure, form
Giving weight to our words

And would we find light
More precious, more real
Were words to ignite
And rhymes to reveal?
