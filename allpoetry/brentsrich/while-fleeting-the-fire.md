title: "While Fleeting The Fire"
writer: "brentsrich"

When is life so true
As in the lines of a poem?
While volumes of prose
Seem no more than a tome.

Is there a more vibrant
Glance at within?
Like eternity dancing
On the head of a pin.

While fleeting the fire
Which kindles each word,
Still never more vivid
These feelings I've stirred.

And when I've departed,
My last word is read,
Will these phrases and pauses
Still breath what I've said?
