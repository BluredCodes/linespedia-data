Sunlight bathes
Sadness and smiles
Cool breeze wraps
The pure and depraved
Trees give shade
To both sides of the hunt

When children walk
On bruising ground
Their feet, their hearts
Soft and blue
Then with a lurch
Now a stare
Pause, reflect
The same air
The same air
