i GiGGlED wIth GoD,
TearS Of inSanity roLLed DoWn My Face,
Blessing mE with all of HeaVens Grace,
I've lost all seNse of whats Right or wrong,
i GiGGLed WiTh GoD,
We GiGGled So LonG,
JoYful InSaniTy,
Not SiNFul,
YoU Preach to me, then Judge Me,
F*cking PiTTiful,
Hear me NoW, It's Crittical,
I GiGGled with GoD,
Like When a Mother & Father First PlaY with THeRe ChilD,
A Setting so jOyOuS, HumBle and MilD,
Do noT Judge me,
I've GiGGled With GoD,
NoW I see ThingS just a BiT More,
I've GiGGled With GoD,
I'm LoSeing My Fear Of WaR...
