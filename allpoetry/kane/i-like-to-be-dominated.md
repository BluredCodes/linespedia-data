I Like to Be Dominated,
I like to be spanked and beat,
Take out your frustrations,
By Beating my meat,
Mistreat me,
Abuse me,
Let Me Know I'm all yours,
Make a list for me,
of all my sexual chores,
Make me Do what you want,
and If I refuse, Punish me,
Bend me over and spank me Over your knee,
Sit on my face,
Until I'm Begging for Air,
Put Me on a leash,
And take me anywhere,
Take me into your world and Dominate me,
But I'm Not Going To pay you,
It better be free...
