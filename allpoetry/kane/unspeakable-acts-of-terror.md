Unspeakable Acts of Terror,
Are Speakable to Me,
How Else are going to be prepared,
for an attack of the highest degree,
When Wicked MeN start Targeting our children,
and even our Infants will Fear the times,
We need to be prepared for these sort of Crimes,
Bombings at Elementry schools,
Shootings at Zoos,
Rampages at family resteraunts,
The Terror will spread so swiftly over the News,
You think No MeN are Mosnters,
Capable of easily doing these things,
These are just few things I think the Future Brings,
In Vietnam Little Children were sent into GI Bars,
With Bombs straped to their Chest,
But what happens when that sort of thing happens,
Out here in the West?,
I'll tell you what happens,
Our Great Nation will Die,
We'll Give in to the Demands of these MeN,
For who's Country they'll fry,
They'll Go Straight to hell,
And in Hell they'll Burn,
These MeN will die for there beliefs and Country,
That's what they were forced to Learn,
Thousands of pounds of drugs,
Easily make it accross our boarder,
from over seas,
and compared to that kind of smuggleing, 
little weapons and Bombs,
would be like smuggleing Head lice or Fleas,
But Really What Can we Do but Pray,
Pray to a GoD that some think doesn't even Exist,
And Ask him for a brighter Day?
How Do We Really Prepare,
The Only thing I can Think of Is that we strike them first,
So Many troubled decisions will have to make,
let's just face it,
We as MeN Are Cursed...

and your destruction comes like a whirlwind,
