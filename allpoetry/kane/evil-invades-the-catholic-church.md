A MaN,
A Preist,
Injures a Childs Soul,
Now Should We Punish this One MaN,
Or the Catholic Church as a Whole,
I Don't Know about You,
But to Me "Choemoes Stick Out Like a sore thumb"
How Could other members of the Church really be that Dumb,
The worst kind of Terror,
Commited in a House of Praise,
O lord why are things so fucked up these Days,
Now the Catholic Church is doing everything in it's power,
to protect it's credibility,
But now what about this childs mental stability,
Evil has Invaded the Catholic Church,
But Not Like It hasn't happened before,
I think the Pope is Right,
They NeeD to Be more Careful of who they Let in the Door...
