I've Been Mislead By My Voices,
I've Made Plenty of Disterbing Choices,
Choices is what they were,
and they were all Mine,
Now after Meeting Many others Who hear Voices,
I know I'm Not One Of a Kind,
The Emotions that come with the Voices,
Could very well make a man Go insane,
I've documented my experiances with them,
under the Name Of KANE,
Now I need help,
Help from GoD,
Maybe help from You,
Please read my past writtings,
and give me advice on what I should Do,
