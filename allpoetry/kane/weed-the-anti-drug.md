So Many People would rather smoke weed than drink,
Weed is their Anti-DruG, That's What I think,
They say weed is the Gateway DruG,
They say weed is what leads people into trying other things,
But Nobody knows for sure what the future brings,
Drinking liquor, Drinking beers,
Should really be childrens parents fears,
Drinking becomes addictive,
It becomes a sloppy High,
In the End You become an alcoholic,
You Get Weak and Die,
Alcohol give you false courage,
On weed your too paranoid to do any wrong,
WeeD is the Anti-Drug We've waited for for so long....
