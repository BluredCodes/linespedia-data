Let me play with you,
Let me massage you between your thighs,
Let me hear your moan,
Let me hear your lust filled cries,
Let my tounge enter you,
Let me taste you tonight,
Let me go down without putting up a fight,
Use me, call me what you want,
Use me, Let me taste your cunt,
Use me, I'm just another toy,
Use me, I'm just another boy,
Use me, It'll just be between us,
Use me, I won't put up a fuss,
Use me, Your fingers can't satisfy you like I can,
Use me, not only as a friend, but a man
