I'm Unstable,
I'm a Double Minded Man,
I don't think GoD Minds,
He Just wants Me to Do What I Can,
He Allows me to be Unstable,
I've excepted Jesus who's paid for our Sin,
I've felt him in my heart and under my Skin,
I'm Unstable,
Capable of Doing Anything At any time,
From sacrificing Myself to committing a Crime,
I'm Not Saying I will,
I'm just saying that it may happen that way,
Take away my Insanities O Lord,
This I Pray
