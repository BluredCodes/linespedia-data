RANDOM CHILD MOLESTER BEATINGS MUST OCCUR,
If Not because it's sick,
But think about these childrens future,
THESE 'CHOEMOES' Need to learn,
Let's beat them,
then let there souls burn,
Let's beat them good,
one at a time,
Our parts in there rehabilitation,
will be sublime,
RANDOM CHILD MOLESTER BEATINGS MUST OCCUR,
The Judicial system protects these men,
they feel secure,
The United states protects 
the worst terrorist of them all,
All these fuccking 'CHOEMOES'
MUST TAKE A FALL,
They never learn,
they always recommit,
AND I FOR ONE
HAVE HAD ENOUGH OF IT!
