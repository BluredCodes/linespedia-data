Let's have sex with no remorse,
Ride me girl, like a fucking horse,
I'm going to stick my long hard cock into your pussy 
and fuck you so hard,
In the bedroom, in the kitchen or the back yard,
I'll pick you up, legs wrapped around me
and I'll bang you against the wall,
be assured that in my arms It's my name you'll call,
whether it's for more or to stop you'll scream in pleasure,
You better believe I'm your sexual treasure,
We can live our lives anyway we want to,
but I'll always be here for you
