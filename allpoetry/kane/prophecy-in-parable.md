MATTHEW 24:27,28
Parable:

"For as lightning comes from the East,
and Flashes in the West,
So also will the comming of the SON of MaN be,
For Wherever the Carcass is,
There the Eagles will be gathered together."

Kane

An attack On our Western Civilization,
From the MeN of the East,
Assures the 2nd comming of Jesus Christ,
Wherever there is Death and Destruction,
There the Eagles will Stand United.www.thestarlitecafe.com KANE
