pulling in absence
I breath deeply
suck in my chest
and swallow its substance
now named and flowing in my veins
inside is a world of unexplained absence
that hover over excitement
that leap from one life to another
until its found and destroyed
by sound and movements
I'm apart of absence
a void of elements
that can not be touched
but felt..
but it is there..a black dot in your eye
that's magnified when you look at others involvements
It is there, a knot in your throat
untied only when you feel somethings coming
and it will never be completely gone
See its apart of us all
at one point in time...there is absence to be feltsequel by request
