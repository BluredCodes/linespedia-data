Swollen eyes cried shut
the torment of her love she sees
behind a shield of Egyptian darkness

In her black solitude,
she sketches images of her exploding moments
when her heart was double-crossed
and her face was scratched out with a sharp finger nail

She held herself safe until the end of the last second
until she saw the sight of blood in her hands
her blood when he tore her a new womb

It is a mad momentum now
remaining fresh upon her lips today

Her mind has become a journal of gathered thoughts
Now she weighs her losses and watches as the scale tips
she see the abstract memories of yesterdays grand finales

As the knowledge of her heartache spills of the page
she softly quote over her artistry,

"The pen is mightier than the sword"written a long time ago..after a dramatic end of a terrible relationship I had when I was a teenager. This poem is one of the poems that got accepted as scholarship entry to The Great River Art Institute.  They liked this poem.  I got a scholarship because of it.
