packing the clothes in garbage bags
leaving home for the first time
to be with my prince charming
who rescued me from the judgments
of being 5 months pregnant
and only eighteen
just finishing high school
barely able to fit my prom dress
but that didn't matter
we have already written our love stone
he was there..
always there
even now
standing there
to move me from one dream to another
to the house that jack built
not much..
but for a new life
where we could express ourselves freely
walk around naked if we wanted
laugh and be gods
make our own rules..
know curfews, know deadlines
just love-making
baby making
sex..all night long
and know interruptions
from siblings and parents
just a promise to faithful
to honor and respect
to worship and love
to death do us apart
and we did..
and we do.
we love and be grateful
for being given each otherjust written after chatting in the chatter box.
