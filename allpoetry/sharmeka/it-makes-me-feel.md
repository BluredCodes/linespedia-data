The taste of earl gray in the morning
makes me feel freed
strong like a man, but with the sentiments of a woman
or perhaps it the feeling of control
when my little fingers wrap around the porcelain handle
of the flowery tea cup
or is the warmth,
the enjoyment of the liquid going down
the cream coating my tongue.
No, its something else
something more complexed
yet understood
I think it makes me
content with who I'am
all the while I'm
continuously sipping it's joy 
down to the last drop
lost in quiet thinking
stuck in a solid state of mind
where all is collected and calminspired by a poem I read by a poet on here
