From this Angle
I can see that
that we'll be together
in tomorrows renaissance and popular predictions
sending blank messages in a bottle
rewriting the history of our dark poetry
when we could of been proud companions

From this angle
I can see that we could have been
married couples in love
wearing their hearts like a bage
still dating because its feels new
feeding ducks at seven springs
picking out pumpkin
and carving funny faces
fighting just to make up
talking things out
Insteading of haveing the entire world as our mediator

From this angle
I can tell 
that we should have drank cheap beer in vermont
slice after slice of lemon morange pie
but we made excuses
and never moved from the couch to the door
never planted our family tree

We had so many plans
so many intentions
but some how we got stuck trying
and now we just stopped being
human
wanting
needing more than a simple life

We did what the world said we would do
we gave up loving each other

From there point of view
the happy people are watching 
us try to pull through

But what they really want to see 
is you cleaning up your act
and me to stop licking the wombswritten during the hard times in my marriage
