every time someone hurt you
you flee to the shelter of your own arms
the only thing that keeps you warm
tucking your head between you legs
a backed rat that could attack if he could be brave
and I'm always here 
but sometimes I think your don't care 
and you don't see.
me, wipeing away your tears of fears
I always come to help you with the pain
because I know you would do the same
even if only now and then
and when, our friendship is in question
remember I'am the one
who say to you,
"As long as I'm your friend, no one will hurt you again"written today.
