Last nights moment was splendid, and spectacular a divine ritual of love-making ending in outcome of sweet satisfaction with just you and I rubbing the soft spots side ways on the living room sofa your strong hands clasping my slender waist your chest heaving and your heavy breaths in my ear the throbbing of your wood against my back your potion seeping out and trickling down my thigh I wanted so bad to create another life a younger sister for our daughter but having you inside was good enough if only for now enough to make me think about it all day long the way you whispered sweet words while stroking your fingers through my hair Last nights Momentshowed me that 7 years later and we still carewe're still going strong We're still French kissing closing the hugs snuggling in the words of 'I love you' Last nights moment when we laced I wanted more but what more could you giveyou were at your best and I was at minefalling safe heightsinto your armsthat man I marriedthe man who knows when I'm hurt the man who showed me that there is ten thousand ways to love a woman Lasts nights moment when we were pressing sweaty nude flesh against flesh when we let our hearts do the talkingI knew we still have our grooveI knew we weren't just respected lovershusband and wifebut best friendswho would always be trueif only to one thingto love one anotherhad to get this out after last nights moment
