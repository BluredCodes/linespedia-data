nothing could compareto the unique blend of my brown tonesfor I'm Africa's beautybaked to golden delightI'm the black queen of heartsloved and cherishedworship and adoredI walk proud amongst my fellow mena mahogany goddess in the glory of desireI'm wanted by manybut not many has had mehave drank of my gravyhave tasted the flavor of my chocolate ecstasyhave felt the thrust of my lips the thick black whipssweet and intelligentthey have only dreampt of having the riches of my goldthey have only imagined what is like to be my kingand feel me melt in their mouthwritten purely from inspiration
