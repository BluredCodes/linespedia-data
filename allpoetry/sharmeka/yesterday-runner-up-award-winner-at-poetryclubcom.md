Yesterday I was a hungry man's dinner thawing on the counter
today I'm your bruises swelling blue
fat flesh hanging heavily from a chicken bone
I've been beaten like raw meat hard on both sides
now in this dry spell of love 
overturning on the beach of our memory
I'm penny sinking in quick sand
buried in a moss pit of self-sacrifice and shameful obedience

 Reminded today
in loose conversations with my friends
that you were a slug, dissolving in my tears
after you said, "Its over, Its over, Its over!"
the hills in my mind heard it roll over and echo away

My face became a black board of mixed emotions
to be wiped away by you slap happy palms

If I could turn back the hands of time 
I would have signed you off
before you sucked me dry
but all I could hear then was the speeches of your hands
now all you can hear from me  
is my heart painfully murmuring swears

Now when I'm in  your path
you shift me like a chest piece
you keep my stomach turning
my diaries never finding closurethis poetry won an award at poetryclub.com
