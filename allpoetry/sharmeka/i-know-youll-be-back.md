title: "I Know You'll be Back"

It a constant reminder that you'll be back
your never leaving scent of your perfume
lingering in the house
to trouble me

You'll be back
I know you will
you always come back
to have the last word
for confusion
to slap me in the face
only to later take my body and love it
and afterwards
I'll say something stupid
and the door will swing open
and I'll be left thinking
"what did I do?"
and the seconds will pass
only to make me see that
all you wanted...was to be held

*please come to back where you know your heart belongs*sometimes I think I make my husband feel this way. *smiling*
