I'm just a girl
chasing rainbows
thinking the sun
is a gold shoe sitting in the sky
polished and gleaming
with my name on it

I'm just a girl
thinking someday I will become a woman
but not today

I'm just a girl
thinking he's the one
who will grant me 3 wishes
who will fight for me
who will die for me
and teach me how to be a wife
but he will be the first one to hurt me 

I'm just a girl
who is going to wrap her legs
around a man
who doesn't know what a woman want
just how to make her feel good
and I don't care 
cause someday I'm going to be woman
I'm going to grow up
I'm going to know my mistakes before I make them
I'm going to know what men want
I'm going be beautiful and I'm going to want love
