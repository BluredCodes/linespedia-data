She was there to psycho analize 
the pencil pushing top notch  therapist, 
positioned at the far end of the table
staring at us
the basket case
the class clown
and the jack in the box.

She has come to direct us
the misfits
the one's with misplaced anger
the degenerate children
who's pollutes her class with profanities.

We are to believe that in this little  room
anything goes and that we can build relationships

She
the soul searcher,
here to save us when no one else would or could
wants to know what makes us tick
what makes us do the things we do

She 
the mind healer
the pill popping junky 
who perpetrates as if she doesn't have any problems
dissecting us on a character sheet
here to discover the source of  our teenage rebellious behaviors
we have seen her kind to many times
tight suited egotistical guides
hiding behind their work

We thinks she's an octopus
reaching out with her fake open handiness
to change what has always been odd

She
my mothers snitch 
who schedules private meetings with our parents
to discuss our progress
in which to be congratulated for 

She performs giving us exercises
hoping to stop us from the pulling trigger
on what makes us self destructive
I'm just smirking at her as she convinces herself 
that she's in control of her life 
crossing her legs and looking over her glasses
ending the session with mental homework
but I know she's going home
the divorcee
to fight with her kids
wishing she had taken more notes
