No one has ever got out 
the pits of a broken heart 
the raven's claws 
that's why I'm afraid to 
to show my face in the light 
to answer to love 
just to have my dreams stolen away 
thoughts tanted 
emotions twisted 
I don't want to be in love's grasp 
because I know... it will never let me gowritten a week ago
