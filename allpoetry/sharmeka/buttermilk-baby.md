My Butter milk baby
you have arrive outside in
sweet as pollen,
fresh as babies breath, 
blowing in the wind.

You are my baby
my miniature me

My butter milk baby

Tiny weenie toes wiggling
tickled tummy, 
yummy soy milk just for you.
your love just for me
when your crying for affection,
I will give it to you

My butter milk baby.

Today I shall hold you longer,
than the longest hold you ever had.
cuddle you cute as you close your eyes, 
to stuffed puppies soft and secure,
pouncing in circle above youwritten for daughter who is 8months old.  I named her trinity skye wilson.  Her name means, father son and holy goes.
