Someday she will ask me
about them
and I won't be able to answer 
and then she'll search for the truth
and then they will haft to face her
the child
the innocent soul
caught in the middle 
of prejudice and chaos
the part of them
they forgot to love
and then they will hold her
and repent
somedayto in-laws who never come to visit..and think we don't exist
