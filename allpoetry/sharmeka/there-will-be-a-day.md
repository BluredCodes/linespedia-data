There will be a day
when I confront you with the evidence
that you made a mistake
that your diagnoses was made purely on cost
and I'm going have it all in blood
your signature 
permission to dispose of the fetus
and send it to the lab for scientific experiment
to enhance your research
and your going to deny it though
even when they slap the cuffs on
and hit you with tomatoes
I know your are
you'll going to claim
I'm suffering from hysteria
and try to pump me with pills
and numb my wits
you will say I suffer from a chemical in-balance of a women torn from her child to early
but I will not let you
proclaim such lies
I will have you by the throat and choke the truth out of you
until it foams at your mouth
and all you can do is gag and beg forgiveness
That is when I will have restitution
that day you will see the damage you have done
as I sue you and milk you for every pretty penny you have ever earned
you medical school drop out!
I will be there watching 
cheering as they strip you of your title
and stamp "baby killer"on your fore head and throw you in a dark cell with a bearded woman
who will rape you of your happiness
just like you did me
Oh yes, some day I'm going to come to you
fully prepared to avenge my empty womb
and make you have a heart, if only by force
to do what is right
and give back the child you have taken.written looking back
