I was thinking
(how come everything has gone right but everthing I have done was wrong"
and my friend dropped a bible in my hands,
(my wedding gift)
and told me to read it..and it said
"God is always in control"
who would have thought it would come from her
his answer to my question
his message 
that was well sentthis really happened it freaked us both out..we never spoke of it again..because it was so creepy..I was just thinking that..and I she didn't know..and I didn't know she had wrote that in the bible....just wanted to share that experience
