Love Mistaken

Was when I thought, 
I embraced a inner peace of you
but I was death to your noise
I imitated your smile 
and for a while
envied your poise

I discovered your true colors
under the covers
a world of space
between to undecided lovers

My friends
said I wouldn’t be able to see the truth
I thought if I placed the cards on the table
I would know what I meant to you

The slap of reality
one that got to core
was the sight of me on my knees
begging you to love me more

All of my pleas
were privately ignored

You took the phone off the ringer
silenced me with a finger

I pulled back the sheets
went deep
as you turned down the heat

My heart hardened
blistered
as you whispered

“I never promised you a rose garden”

nothing left to float
