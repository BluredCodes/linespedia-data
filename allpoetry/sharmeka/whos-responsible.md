Cancer was never my concern
I thought I was invincible
my squares were my wives
each one served a purpose.

Now I'm lying here on the orthopedic bed
addressing the surgeon generals warning

Am I responsible?

even though I was a good person
I didn't blame it on peer pressure
or blame it on the genetics
my friends who said,
"Everybody gotta die someday"
I tried to quite
unlike hearts, habits are hard the break
even when you know you should.

people tell me it happens to the best of people
people tell me nothing is serious as a heart attack
I say, "Nothing is as mean as cancer."

Am I responsible?

because 
I loved them all, 
100 CIGARETTES AND MORE

But who's counting 
are you?written over when I tried to quit smoking *laughing*
