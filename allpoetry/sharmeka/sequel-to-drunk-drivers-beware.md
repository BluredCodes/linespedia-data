Did you know they had names,
they had a family..
they had lives..
the many that have died
taken abruptly, taken suddenly
you do not haft to know them..
to know they existed
they were deprived of a life 
all because a car intercepted
all those peoples missed
as the drinking driving population grows 
so does the list
of people who have went.

You do not haft to know them
to know that they are gone..
to know that drinking and driving is wrong
some do it, not necessary to harm anyone
just behind the wheel..in dumb fun
and more die
and why?
because of blind visions that can't possibly guide
because of lack of consideration
because of intoxication
because lack of fear
foolishing thinking
only wanting to get there from here
a substance that gives you false hope
often used to cope

Did you know those people 
who have died
had friends, 
a career, 
children
vanishing
in a wink of an eye
sometime slowly, while others stand by and cry
all because someone chose to drink and drive
so I say to all, why do this?
Do this and never mind?
consider the decision
because there isn't always a next timefor enigma..let me know if you want something else added..or something else done with it.
