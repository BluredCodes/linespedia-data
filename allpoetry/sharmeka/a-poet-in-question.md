I'm a poet still in question
even after I've gotten
"Well done, Good job, Great write!"
after sharpening my pencil only ten times today
after looking at 5 pages of poetry
jibber jabber jumping of the page
babbling nonsense that means nothing to me
and anyone else
after bawling up the cheesy, stanzas
that was put there just to have something down.
I realize that there is no point to writing, 
if the purpose is dullfor ever poet that has done this..I did this..and funny it inspired me!
