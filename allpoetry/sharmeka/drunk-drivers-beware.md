title: "Drunk Drivers Beware!"

I'm going up a one way road
It's long way home, and there's rubber to burn

I'm flicking ashes out the window
fearless in a minefield of pot holes and road kill

I'm blinded by pest on the windshield
One stop sign and no left turn
I'm doing 85 up a dark road
Its a long way home and I'm stoned

again and again my car hits the curve
I'm out of control as I swerve

I'm split into bits 
at the drop of the cliff
now I'm lying stone dead
with my life..a blur.something to think about when you drink and drive..
written after my near death experience..
when I got in the car with a drunk driver
