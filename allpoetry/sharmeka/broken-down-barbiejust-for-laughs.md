You are nothing but a broken down barbie
who got no love for her body
with no respect.
even as a bimbo
flashing her breast out the passenger side window
giving in 
to nasty old men
going to bed 
with pimped-out old heads
letting it all hang out
flaunting yourself about
never taking out,
just brought home
by dogs who only want a bone

Its going to catch up to you
and your not going to have a clue
because
then and only then
will you know 
the price of when 
you show
and touch..to much skin.this was something I just wrote for fun...thinking about a girl I know.
