There are dreams of a desired past
that are sold to serve to psychotic needs
this evention of the magic man
can at last feed his greed
lost souls go where reality don't exist
wired inside a hypnotic product on a disk
they can travel through space and time
able to recapture
replant memories in there minds
some can survive the transition
while the devil in the flash supplies their addiction

Those who fail will be under a spell
their eyes will be stuck in a stared state
all they may see is scattered colors and shapes
they think they are dreaming
but they are awake

So they lay there for days
and they lay there for hours
forever in the void
forever they are wiredInspired by the movie "Strange Days" along time ago..
