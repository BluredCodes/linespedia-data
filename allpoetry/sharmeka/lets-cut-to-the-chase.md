lets cut to the chaseforget the missionary positionforget the leg over legfor nowlets do something we're ashamed ofNo, not petting the secret placesjust for me to squirm and scream'Take me!'I need it now..your snake-like fingers slithering on the acres of my skinstretched and pulled every which wayupside down and side waysand finally you get to the center of the toosie rollfinally...we have lift off!!!and then its over..all that hard work to make each othe tired, is overbut I want more..see we always want more'Do something else with your tongue'I sayall the pushing isn't necessary anymoreyou've already knocked my back outyou've already popped the cherryI have only one breath left'what else do you have in your bag of tricks?'he creeps into the jungle of loveand after ten minutes of pantingand ripping his hair outhe submergesflips on the switch and looks at my sweatynude bodylaying in the wet spotand then we fall asleepwith leg over legjust written
