Your a lost cause fading fasteven your smileand ridiculous laughtrying to force me to obey with your windintimidate me with your evil grincutting your eyeskeeping tiesjust to cross me againand I onced loved youhugged you..shared your sinwatched you, try to stop youfrom drinking your self under the tablewhen ever you were ablejust to prove a point to your friendsnow I look at youdislike you, but pray for youa mortal disappointment to goda fool who have sealed his fateto always be a lost causededicated to someone I use to care about along time ago..hope you've changed!
