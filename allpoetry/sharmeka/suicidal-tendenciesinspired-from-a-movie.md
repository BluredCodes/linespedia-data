This time it will work
the hard way.
and no one will know 
your in the motel 69
not until the smells creep out
no one will want to see
your stiff body laying
motionless on display
head hung over
eyes bulgging and shot
slick hair combed to one side
dreams gone of a grim life
and no one their to bring you back
to jump start your heart
to bandage your open flesh
and pray for your damned soul
just the razor that thought you wouldn't do it
and your friend who found you, and knew you wouldwritten after watching a movie yesterday just finished it today.
