Today I sifted through the pages of the open chapter of us 
the big bulk of love letters 
the endless e-mails that dragged out the truth 
that we could not go on like this 
sending and resending 
paging and waiting for response 
dreams of ways to stand face to face with eachother 
and proclaim we love each other 
without even taking a breath 
I skipped past the pretty apologies of why you didn't write back 
I skipped past the pasted pictures of you on vacation 
stroked your authentic signature with my eyes
your friendly goodbyes blinking and scrolling on the bottom 
It occured to me that we could cross the pacific just to be together, but we never did and why? 
I wondered if the answer was between the line 
So I stared at the loops and fancy fonts 
the attachments of your quickly written poetry 
Your word "love" crafted and highlighted 
I followed your voice down to the edge 
until there was no more..but void and space 
until the our signal became lost from our busy lives 
or was it because our hearts and minds collided 
and stay away in grudges? 
Then I tried to delete all the correspondence 
but I couldn't, I never will 
I'll never stop writing you
and giving you chicken soup for the soul
as long as the long as our chapter is openwritten for a friend I met a long time ago on the internet
