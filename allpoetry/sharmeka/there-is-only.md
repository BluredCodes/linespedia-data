the smooth melody of jazz 
the sound of rocking and swashing 
the city lights twinkling. 
stars in the black sheet of sky winking 
the start of new beginings 
and you don't worry about thing 
you know your free to decided 
you just know that life has opened a door..and let the wind in 
the serenity around you is sweet 
the air your breath is crisp and clean 
you peering over the balcony
