---
title: "Shattered Dreams Pieced Together"
writer: "sharmeka"

shattered dreams
I now paste together with tired hands
my dreams that fell apart because I gave up
and now I need them
more when I see others who have them
So this is my last attempt
to have all the thing I've always wanted
deprived because I was not worthy
love and justice
father and child
money and fame
and as I struggle with the pieces
the tiny specks of something out of reach
I still want them
even if they aren't real for some of us
I still want them
the dreams
that reality tells me I still can not have
because I want them... for the wrong reasonsinspired
