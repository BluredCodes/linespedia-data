"Uncle, why don't you sing
old smoky
anymore, when we come over?"

"Why don't you roll up your shirt
and flex the belly dancer 
tattooed on your arm?"

(You was a strong man)
(so nice looking, when we were children)

"I don't know kid,
"I'm an old man now." 
"kid, I'm an old man now."

(not as old as you should be)

"But, you can come down
and see us sometimes,
you can come down."

(your not in Korea anymore)

"But the family an't like it use to be, kid."
"The family an't like it use to be."

(back when the blood of your kin was to thick to stir)

"Uncle we miss you."

(the war is over)
(we won, we love)

"Uncle, come down and sing."for my uncle, I miss you, I pray you get better
