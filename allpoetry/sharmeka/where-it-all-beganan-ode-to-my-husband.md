This is how it all began
you
parking the beast
the ever leaking bronco
between to trees
hidden but not lost
climbing out and stepping into a dream
ruining my swede boots in the muddy trails
just to follow you to duck hollow
where I learned to skip stones
carve my name in a tree
to kiss you for the first time
where I opened and told you my whole life
slowly a relationship was crafted
one that would last more life times than a loud 
and we did not need chemistry
we don't need a sign
we only needed love
that wove itself into our hearts
and made us complete 
unite
that day 
where it all began

*I loved you then and I love you now*for my husband who I love..and even sometimes we may not agree at times..I know I love you and can't imagine ever being without you.  I can't imagine being without you.
