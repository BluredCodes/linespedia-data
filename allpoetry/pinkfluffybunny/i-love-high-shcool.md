I love high school
because I am soooooo popular
I am the captain of the cheerleading squad
and I am dating a star basketball player
he is in track too by the way
and all the other jocks want to date me
but my heart belongs to him
Damn
I am sooooo popular
and I am sooooo beautiful
I love high school
but not as much as I love myself!
