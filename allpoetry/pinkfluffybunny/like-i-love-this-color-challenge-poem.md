Like I seen these new pair of shoes, 
they were totally phat. 
But I would rather have them as pumps 
instead of being flat. 

Now this heinous geeky book worm, 
who was like drooling all over himself. 
I walked over closer to him, 
and he fell and knocked over the shelf. 

Great like for sure I know, 
this idiot would like ask me out. 
I said I would like these in a pink heels, 
and he stared like he didn't know what I was talking about. 

Oh it got so ridiculously worse, 
this was like the most horrible day. 
He couldn't bend to put the heel on, 
for his woody was in his way. 

Oh my god I said, 
I can't believe you can't tuck that away. 
Ewwww, I don't want the heels now 
I don't need new shoes anyway.Like majorly gross!
