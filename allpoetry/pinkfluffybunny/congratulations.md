Congratulations to ME!
I am so Happy!
You remembered this day,
and you have came here to say

Congratulations to Me?
Wow thanks! golly gee.
Here I go watch me,
Down in History!

But I stop to question
Why congratulate me? Beg your pardon?
OH that's right!
I graduated kidergarten!!!!

Well Congratulations to ME!!!
