We have like this love/hate relationship.
We started out in hate because basically you hated me and left rude comments on my stuff but I like you.

Pink Fluffy Bunnies are like cool and nice and stuff.
We are not mean rabid bunnies.  We try hard to be friends with everyone.
I would give you a great big bunny hug if I could to make you feel better and not so angry at me.

Please smile I would even let you borrow my pink sweater if you wanted and we could go get our nails done together and best friends forever!!!This is for Rage of the Machine who does not like me =)
*BIG HUG FOR RAGE*
