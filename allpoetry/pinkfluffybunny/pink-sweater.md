Oh I just seen this darling pink sweater I wanted.
Of course it was like $200.00 but I didn't care.

I knew that pink sweater would look so good on me that,
I had to do whatever it took to get it.

So, I ran back home as fast as I could,
and asked my dad and he said yeah and reached inside his wallet and like handed me over the cash. Yeah the Pink Sweater is mine!!I love the color Pink
