I wish I was care bear.
I would love to ride around on clouds and slide down rainbows every day.

I especially want a picture of a pink bunny on my belly so I can be Pink Bunny Bear and cheer people up.

Then when people are sad or angry all of us care bears could do the care bear stare and shoot volts of happiness and smiles at them!

Oh I would love to be a care bear just so I could drive that cloud car around the sky and stuff how cool would that be!Oh I miss watching the care bears but like I do own all the care bear movies!
