No-one in the world nows how I feel
No-one knows how to care
No-one

No-one ever bothers to ask how I am
No-one ever thinks i could want true love
No-one

No-one out there for me to hold
No-one could ever be so alone
No-one

No-one out there to hold me
No-one could ever be so alone
No-one

Someone is out there for me
Someone must be out there for me
Someone

No-one, until that Someone comes along
To hold me in their arms
I am still
No-one.
