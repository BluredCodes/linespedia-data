tacky neon signs glaring in my face
foriegn taxi drivers honking to their hearts content
dirty sidewalks
smoky air
noise
still no matter how loud and dirty it is
my love for New York City 
will last foreverWhat do you think? It's supposed to show that even if New York is dirty and loud you still have to love it for all of its good parts
