title: "In dreams"

Every night I see you, I hold you.
We are together forever. Every night my 
feelings grow stronger; Every night you love me;
Every night you are with me And I with you,
Laughing, dancing, having fun just being with you.
I dread the morning hours for I must wake up, Go back to reality.
I wish that my dreams were real, And for awhile that is what I feel.
But my dreams soon vanish, And tears replace when I look into the mirror at my face. A face you could never love, A person you probly hate.
I live for the nights, The nights you are with me in my dreams for life.
