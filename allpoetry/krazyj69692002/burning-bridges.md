There was a bridge between me and you
It seemed to be so beautifully true 
I crossed over the bridge, you won my heart
You dide'nt seem one to tear it apart 
I trusted you when I let you in 
I easily let our love begin 
Little by little you drifted away
you got farther from me every day
I looked at the bridge between you and me
The one that caused the misery 
The beauty was gone along with our love
shattered in pieces beyound and above
I left the bridge to be a reminder to me
Of a love never truly meant to be
But time has made the seasons turn 
All I see now is a bridge to burn
