When the moment comes-It will pass quicklyIn uncontrolled silenceLike a vanishing dream.When the moment comes-My eyes will be openedAll boundaries liftedThe words slow to speak.When the moment comes-You won't be my friend anymoreIt will be a memoryBittersweet in my subconscious.When the moment comes-Goodbyes will be forcedQuick, like a sprintLonely, as the color blueSullen, as the air.When the moment comes-I will be alone, facing the winds of changeKnowing this moment is my own.
