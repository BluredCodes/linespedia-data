Think of a place you want to be so badly that if you could be there right now you could and would be there. How do you truly feel about your new surroundings, do you feel secure and more confident? Whatever you feel its real and you can be there because the possibilities are endless, and you are free to do what ever you choose. This place you want to go is your not only an outlet for your mind to retract from everyday stress in life, but a dream you yourself can make happen. In the game of life you can achieve anything that you belieive in no matter what the obstacle is standing in your way. The only thing that is really and truly in your way is your ability to look it straight in the eye and invision it as being a small hill to climb on your way to the top. The top is ofcourse your dream, inspiration, and worth of living in a world that so many have taken for granted. Now begin your journey to your destination and dont let anything blind you on the way, however getting there is never as easy as you want it to be so tread lightly.
