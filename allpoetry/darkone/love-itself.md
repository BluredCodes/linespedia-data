'Love Itself'	What is the true meaning in the word love it can be misconstrued and constructed into being things we know nothing of. It can missinterpretted into our deepest and most intimate part of our body the human mind. Love can take the shape of so many of our faults and desires such as, lust, companionship, availability, obsession and even personal worth and valuables. A word so small that can mean so much has been the burden of so many lost souls for generations, not to mention the generations soon to come into contact with it. So much can be acheived in just saying the word that if said right can touch the person directed to so strong that they will become infactuated with hearing it. It can also be took for granted by those who wish to use it for conquering thier real passions in the quest of greed. However, when love is true and shared by two people the idea of it is so intense that no single person can ever imagine of describing it. Love is one topic that will always be talked about and discussed with the upmost confidence in its presevation in everyday life.I never really wrote this as a poem but rather as a thought on the subject itself.
