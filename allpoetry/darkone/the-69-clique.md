' The 69 Clique'	We are few in numbers but are growing strong everyday like the true roza we are each born and bred. Each of us have skills in the game of which has no end, but only a hunt we cannot stop and will never give up. Few can understand us and not everyone likes us, however we all remain a pack, like wolves who run through the cold of night in the pale moonlight and the dead still air.
