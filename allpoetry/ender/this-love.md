This Love

Smile at me through innocent eyes
Fueling a love that never quite dies
A sunset frozen in my heart
Blazing forever before night can start
But a love that's denied is a love nontheless
Burning with a fervor never to rest
Though I'm never as good as when I'm with you
I wish I could make you see what I doI really like this one... it says exactly what I want to say.
