Raped Paradise 

This isn't me, this can't be real
I forcefully deny the pain I feel
Another man's is the anger inside
Yet deeper I know, I can't hide

I can't believe it's come to this
But I push away the thing's I'll miss
I push away the spark of hate
That flickers within, burning irate

I've nowhere to go, but so far to run
Further away and into the sun
Drawing ever closer, it dwindles away
My last hope dying today

Accepting the fact I cannot escape
I stand and I scream, innocent to rape
My hatred still stains me deep within
But there it will stay, for never again
Will it come out in true trust's shape

No sun shines through, lighting my fire
No reason to live with this untouchable desire
I wish you a world of stars for your eye
And a memory that forgets me as I die
