Just Like You 

Trapped behind walls that are crashing all around
Crushing my heart without any sound
I fear I'll never be free

Forced to endure the life you defile
As I lie on the brink of ironic exile
Giving up on making you see

I realize my attempts are foolishly in vain
As I feel in my heart a new kind of pain
I'm just like you in my eyes

For my soul is worn, torn and grey
And bears the scars of surving the fray
Yet I become what I despise

Hopes for a life different from theirs
Are destroyed as my eyes bring forth new tears
That this time ring true

Because as into my image I gaze
The only thing clear in a world of haze
I know I'm just like you
