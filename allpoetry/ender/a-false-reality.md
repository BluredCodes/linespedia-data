A False Reality

Tired of this life I live, 
nothing left have I to give, 
feel no reason to forgive,
Those that make me feel this way

Run so far but I can’t hide, 
hope within has slowly died, 
nothing but this hell inside,
Wanting only to get away

I shut my eyes but I still see, 
a false world in front of me, 
everyone lives happily, 
There is no pain, no sorrow

No stress pressing from above, 
there is no hate, only love, 
gone are the things I’m sick of, 
Eyes ahead on tomorrow

There is a bit of fear though, 
a shadow looms of what I know, 
upon me hell will bestow
I think it always does

The colors fade from bright to gray, 
the pain apparently seems to stay, 
it must’ve never went away, 
and everything’s like it was.
