Together We Fly

come take my hand
be with me tonight
nothing matters but us
no trouble in sight

be one with the stars
take in a breath
dance to the music
mourning the death

friendless am i
but you're all i need
together we cry
and together we bleed
together we love
always we try
together forever
together we fly

flow to the feeling
lose all your fear
come with me away
anywhere but here

stand with me now
empty, hopeless field
i'll be your protection
i will be your shield

friendless am i
but you're all i need
together we cry
and together we bleed
together we love
always we try
together forever
together we fly

look into my eyes
find a way to trust
live like this until
the sun crumbles to dust

i lose myself inside
your smiling face
weaving so gently
a love's soft lace

friendless am i
but you're all i need
together we cry
and together we bleed
together we love
always we try
together forever
together we fly
