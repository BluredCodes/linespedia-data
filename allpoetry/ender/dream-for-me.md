title: "Dream For Me"
writer: "Ender"

Dream For Me Lying my head down to rest this nightSlipping away is my anxiety, my frightAs a blanket of solitude slowly comes over meA world of nothingness being all I seeThis nothingness though is soon long goneAs dreams of mine replace it 'til dawnIn my dreams my fantasies come to lifeWhere Revenge is my brother, Love is my wifeDon't fear these dreams that I dream for youFor in your slumber, you dream as I doPerhaps we don't dream of the same exact thingsBut relief from your life can be found in your dreamsSo while you dream for me, I'll dream for youDreams of a time when my dreams came trueDream for me love that we had in the pastIf only my lifetime these dreams would last
