---
title: "Empty Me"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "empty-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17435-Empty-Me-by-Ender"
---

didn't talk to her
time to sleep
dwell on my thoughts
maybe i'll dream
forced to think
about the day
forced to think
but maybe i'll dream

so much more
yeah we could be
so much more
but you can't see

life's so cold
for me right now
i can't breathe
anymore
and i try
to see a reason
something out there
worth fighting for

but i can't see 
past those walls
those walls i built
but they'll stay
cause there's no reason
to see past 'em
there's nothin out there
i think they'll stay

so much more
yeah we could be
so much more
but you can't see

i don't know why
or what i have to
do to make you see 
past what i try to
but the rain keeps fallin
and i keep dyin
you keep lovin
that rain's not stoppin

so much more
yeah we could be
so much more
but you can't see
just what it is
i'm fighting for
take that love
that you gave me
yeah, take that love
slowly empty me

maybe i'll dream
