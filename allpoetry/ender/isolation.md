Isolation

leave me here to die alone
trapped in invisible walls of stone
grown to hate this place

lost so deep within myself
for only i and no one else
can see the hurt on my face

freedom found in useless dreams
of a life where nothing's as it seems
make me wish i could fly

but the pain in my heart and the weight on my soul
both of which are dark as coal
make me wish i would die

lend me breath to breathe you in
as i may never see you again
i've nothing left to say

can't deny the passion burning
deep inside the painful yearning
just blow it all away
