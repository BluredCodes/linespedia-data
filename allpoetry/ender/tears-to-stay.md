Tears To Stay

i try to forget
what you said to me
whispered in my ear
so quietly

burning within
a desire to see
the death of all
humanity
churning, the sin
no hope for me
no ocean could quench
my hatered eternally

the silence around
pressing inward and down
forcing me to see
pushing me to scream
shoved to the edge
looking past my life
i know in my heart
i can fly

flung forward into
the open air before me
but my dream
is but a nightmare
that so horribly
ends in a red tears of sorrow

deep inside i feel
all of it was real
the happiness and hatred
and the sorrowful end
the reality perhaps was fake
but the feelings live
stinging strong and true
and i know only you
can make this go away
can burn the tears away

i am forced
for the second time
to see the truth once more
that you're not really there
but more like a fantasy
that in my dreams
i'm searching for

i can't make this go away
the tears will have to stay
