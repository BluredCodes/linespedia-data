Save Me

cold wind blowing into my face
piercing, cutting so deep within
shielding my eyes from the sunlight
that's burning through my skin

i am lost too far inside
a world of pain and lost hope
i feel so very long ago i died
but never really felt the rope

looking around, i can see it
falling to the ground
can't reach the bottom of the pit
yet you think you hear the sound

save me from the hate
save me from the sorrow
take away the burden
before i reach tomorrow

save me from the anger
save me from the pain
cure the deserts of my heart
with a lonely summer rain

lying here, with the sky above
i can think of nothing else
except my inner desire for love
to be with someone else

but the stars that were with me have fallen
and the moonlight that once led the way
faded out without a sound, and i
am left with nothing but to pray

enchanted are the whisperings
that tempt me to the end
coming to me deep in the night
woven into the wind

i'll never escape
i'll never be free
damned to be alone
for all of eternity
save me from the anger
save me from the pain
cure the deserts of my heart
with a lonely summer rain
