Angels Song 

I dream of a place that you can bring
Where I can listen to the angels sing
Dreamily staring to the clouds above
Caught in the whirlwind of your love

The angels of heaven though cry, not pray
Because of the tainted and the way they betray
Gleaming in the light of the stars in the sky
Wrenching my heart and wanting to die

Their song has ceased and there is no sound
Except for the silence pressing in all around
Quickly driving the weak to the brink
But I only stare, unable to blink

Staring into the eyes of the angels I love
Beautifully majestic, and white like a dove
Their tears though, still fall into a small pool
Catching the light of the moon like a jewel

Until their soft eyes are empty and dry
The sorrow's still there, but no longer they cry
Their tears are all spent, heads hung in shame
The silence herein is whispering her name
And the song they now sing will never be the same
