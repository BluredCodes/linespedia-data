Heaven Through A Kaleidoscope

I watch as before me
The flower blooms
In a burst of cryptic fire
Melting in the air
Fueling an inner desire
But wilting in despair

Through my eyes
You’ll see a world
Living black and white
Looking for a way
To get inside the light
And maybe, I just may

To you who had me
You’re losing me tonight
In another reality
Where I can live again
In a fluorescent sea
Where others have never been

One day I will sit
Atop eternity’s finger
Fighting to hold onto
A bliss out of sight
Wishing to forget you
To finally make it right

But in your eyes
I see heaven
Through a broken mirror
Distorted beauty, blind
The whispers growing nearer
Bending my own mind

Forcing me to remember
Losing control once more
But in my fantasy alone
My wingless dreams will soar
