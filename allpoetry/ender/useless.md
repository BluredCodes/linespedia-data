Useless

in my eyes can you see
the birth of fantasy
or is it forever grey?

twinkling once with happiness
but bitter now because of this
forced to desperately pray

summer innocence met winter cold
and slowly faded like burnished gold
i lost what i had before

i forgot how hard i tred
paying the price for foolish pride
can no longer fight anymore
