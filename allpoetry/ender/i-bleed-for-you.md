title: "I Bleed For You"

i bleed for you

i remember 
how i used to love
so innocently love
how i used to fall
for a smile

but yesterday's gone
and so is that smile
that i used to adore
so are the feelings
i'm yearning for

i bleed for you
i cry for you
for all the misery
i've put you through
nothing i do
can make it right
just know that tonight
i bleed for you

my tears clash
and flood on yours
two tears for different
reasons of pain
i've felt this once
i've done this before
i swore to myself
never again

i bleed for you
i cry for you
for all the misery
i put you through
and nothing i do
can make it right
just know that tonight
i bleed for you

and i feel the hate
the same hate within
the same kind of hate
i've felt before
but this time the knife
is directed back towards
this time the knife cuts
straight to the core

and i feel the anger
red like the hate
i know what i've done
but it's too late
it's too late

i bleed for you
i cry for you
for all the misery
i put you through
and nothing i do
can make it right
just know that tonight
i bleed for youA different kind of pain...
