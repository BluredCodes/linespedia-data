Stale Hope

resting on the moment
staring into space
lost inside my thoughts
wishing for a place

where i could be with you
it'd be right again
never such a feeling
of bitterness within
i know it can't be
like it was before
but maybe this time
we could make it more

in my dreams i fly
and in my dreams i feel
that i will never die
but it's not really real

to myself i imagine
living in the sky
together forever but never
having to ask myself why

but thrown before my eyes
my visions of happiness shatter
but the sad realization sinks in
as it doesn't really matter

in the back of my mind
the dream trembles and shakes
never to stop the cycle
a place reality forsakes

but deep inside i know
what's broken cannot mend
memories of a love
lie lost upon the wind
the dreams i'm forced to dream
will remain as only dreams
thrown into a world
where nothing's as it seems
