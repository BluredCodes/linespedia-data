Dreamer's Paradise

thoughts that form seem to slowly melt away
i remember innocence but i've long since felt this way

memories of days of purity
fall in a haze of heresy

lost in this moment now, here, forever
faded the things i used to treasure

hold onto hope like a rope in a storm
alas, it is torn and i am reborn

emotions too much to speak once again
it'd take me a lifetime to just breathe them in

you'll never quite grasp what i'll always say
you'll never even know that i feel this way

but the truth is plain before my own eyes
a brilliant bright flame that won't be denied

thoughts that are too beautiful to even exist
blend to tranquility which fades into blissThe title of this poem is unusual, I think.
