---
title: "A Battle Long Lost"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "a-battle-long-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17767-A-Battle-Long-Lost-by-Ender"
---

I stand on a deserted street, 
trying to see past their deceit
A shocked expression on my face

My world is rocked all around, 
torment and pain does surround
I hang my head in disgrace

I remember the happiness I once had, 
it’d been so long since I was sad
It slipped through my fingers like sand

Nothing no longer is what it seems, 
smashed like mirrors are my dreams
And everything I had planned

Still on this empty street I stay, 
bitterly laughing as they betray
Nowhere no longer to take me

Emptiness to match what’s inside, 
no one in which I can confide
Alone forever I am and will be

Dark clouds part in the sky, 
giving me an ounce of hope to try
To continue through the onslaught 

A happy song sounds from above, 
maybe then there is love
And no longer lost battles fought
