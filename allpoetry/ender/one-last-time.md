One Last Time

with every breath i take
i'm suffocating
everything feels empty
not what it used to be
but that was just a lie
hiding the truth from me

standing here in the rain
sinking to my knees
looking upward to the sky
asking mercy from the ages
relief from the mourning
the hatred inside that rages

a love that will not die
forcefully caged within
the key is buried too deep
to be recovered once more
and long since rusted over
pain like never before

burning within me a yearning
a wanting and a need
to escape from this nightmare
one day it will end
hold onto me as i fall, but
these wounds will never mend

one last time, for good
build up my false hopes
remembering a time i could
but this i'm unable to bear
one last time, show me the love
we never really did share
kill me softly, one last time
make it finally end
