Burnish

can you heal what i have done
purify my water under the sun
relieve the burden i feel tonight
replace the blindness with a new sight

make sense the ramblings i'm forced to hear
wipe away sorrow as if with a tear
dull the shade so i can see
and show me who i used to beWritten with Sun Risen.
