---
title: "To Walk With You Again"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "to-walk-with-you-again"
source: "AllPoetry"

to walk with you again

my eyelids slowly close
as i lie down to rest
my mind, so full
my heart so heavy
but all of it fades away

i open my eyes again
and i see you
we're in the clouds
a beautiful paradise
all i see is you

turned around and offering
for me to take your hand
there is no hesitation
as i follow you deeper
into this eden land

but below me
the cloud gives way
and i am falling down
rushing up so swiftly
i almost hit the ground
and i wake up

staring at the ceiling
i remember you
fondly, the look in your eyes
and i long for it again
i long for a place like that
a dream that never dies

my eyelids
grow heavy once more
and my heart
burns with desire
to walk the clouds again
a flickering fire
to walk with you again

then my eyes are opened
and everything's the same
your innocent gaze entreats
me to willingly follow
everything is perfect
i'm so happy here
surpassing other men
but i feel a fear
deeply rooted in my mind
when i'll wake up again
