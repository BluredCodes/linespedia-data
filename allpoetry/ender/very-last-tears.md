Very Last Tears 

I want to cry, but my tears are dry
Forever in torment until I die
Such anger, such hatred I feel for the world
Twisted alongside the sorrow unfurled

What was I thinking when I knocked down
Walls that had surrounded me all around
Opening myself to an angel's face
Blinded by beautiful sunlight I taste

I thought that I could try it again
To reinvite love, I might even win
Everything was perfect, it all seemed right
As I gave my trust away tonight

But more is bloodied than just my hands
Burning away are all of my plans
Burning away is the life of the fears
Yet forever to stay are my very last tears
