Betrayed By Love

tired men again
try to change the sin
that keeps them
in this horrid place
but you can't take away
the tears that stain today
you can't wipe away
the scars from my face

i've been betrayed
by the thing i relish
losing myself within
a pool of eternity
where i drown so happily
forever

look for me into
the blind mans eyes
tell me what you see
as ever so quietly
you gaze into the eyes
whose poor existence
has been fed with lies
 
whisperings insane
pushed to the side
tickle a memory forgotten
lost inside my brain
but as clouds overcome the sun
and the storm begins to rise
my soul's cries echo along
all the way to the stars
i know it's hopeless

for i've been betrayed
by the thing i relish
losing myself within
a pool of eternity
where i drown so happily
forever

sinking to my knees
before the gaze of judgment
but who can decide
if what i try to hide
is within or without
the walls of righteousness

stabbed in the back
betrayed by a love
that i've longed for
in lives lived in the past
wearily giving up

for i've been betrayed
by the thing i relish
losing myself within
a pool of eternity
where i drown so happily
forever
for blinded am i to the truth
merciful death
forever,
betrayed by love
