Bereft

nothing is like
what it used to be
it hurts to think about
all the memories
the nights of sadness
sinking to my knees

i remember how i
so foolish in my pride
thought i was right
that everything inside
wasn't just a lie

i remember how i
so blinded with a love
believed it to be true
that the angels up above
had graced my life with you

when everything is perfect
the way it's meant to be
it seems a voice inside
tries to make me see
that something there is missing
something is long gone
but i know that when i find it
it will set me free

the thorn pricks me
drawing a drop of life
which is lost forever
forgiven, not forgotten
as my words turn to stone
my sight turns to stone
i'm left all alone

my world around is changing
right before my eyes
the sorrow i'd once pushed down
now begins to rise
my heart inside is changing
it grows strong and cold
i turn my back on everything
and the blind man's gold

and when everything is perfect
the way it's meant to be
it seems a voice inside
tries to make me see
that something there is missing
something is long gone
but i know that when i find it
it will set me free
