---
title: "I'll Wait For You"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "ill-wait-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17440-Ill-Wait-For-You-by-Ender"
---

slowly spill
the blood colored wine
that flows from my heart
slowly fill
my uncorrupted mind
don't tear me apart

the winter is so beautiful
but murders well too
the life of the life
which is beautiful too
but never could be
beautiful like you

we'll run away
and leave this place behind
leave behind the empty
and beautiful too
and when i get there
to our utopia
i'll wait for you

the sun parts the
clouds as they fall apart
the silence used to hurt
now it warms my heart
i brush away the hair
fallen over your face

slowly slice
the crimson colored vine
that blocks out all the light
pay the price
what once was mine
for which i still fight

and when i whisper
in your ear
meaningful words
to block your fear
to take you back
away from here

we'll run away
and leave this place behind
leave behind the empty
and beautiful too
and when i get there
to our utopia
i'll wait for you
yes, i'll wait for you
