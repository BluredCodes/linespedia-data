Pure

light the candle of my heart's desire
filling my soul with such a fire
to burn my miseries away

leaving me pure before a new sun
if so long ago, i'd begun
i'd be pure today

with your own hands, take my eyes
so i won't have to see the life i despise
they'll be dead to me

alive to them, will i remain
for such selfishness, a world of pain
pure, i'm forced not to beThis is a strange poem, very abstract... but here it is.
