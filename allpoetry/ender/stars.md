---
title: "Stars"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "stars"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17437-Stars-by-Ender"
---

i know the truth and
i know i'm mistaken
but i keep on wishin
and i keep on lovin
i just can't stop
when i know i should
if it made you happy
i think i would

and i wish for you 
some stars
that you can watch
so very far

i'll watch em too
watch em alone
i'll watch em cold
while you watch em warm
and when it hurts
i think about
you being happy
this makes you happy

i wish for you
some stars
that you can watch
so very far
with someone you love
while he has what i've
been dreaming of

and i wouldn't trade
those stars for you
i wished em for you
some stars
you can watch em
so very far
with someone you love
while he's got what i've
been dreamin of

but i'll keep dreamin
and you'll keep watchin
those stars at night
in the sky
so far above
while i die
but you'll be happy
you'll have love
