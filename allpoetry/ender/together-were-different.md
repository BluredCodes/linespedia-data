Together We're Different 

This message of yours before my eyes
Blended so well among other lies
An ignorant thought to add to the rest
I laugh at the way you think you're depressed

A message kept within bottle of glass
No one can quite grasp it, alas
Though I might know what you're going through
I'm still amused at the stupid things you do

In this world, you think you're so alone
When others like you have demons of their own
You're outlook on life restricted so thin
In this battle of ours, no one will win

You say you're blackened when I am white
Yet still we fight the same pointless fight
Despite our reasons, we both rise and fall
Perhaps we're not so different after all
