Out of Reach 

Searching for something out of my reach
Whisper to me the things you preach
I hear them not, no longer

Search I must, and search I do
For something unknown perhaps even to you
The stress only makes me stronger

At least that is what I am told
The rust of my heart shall shine like gold
When what I'm searching for, I find

It seems as though it dwindles away
The closer I get, I'm driven astray
By the obstacles in my mind

Set by a hand that I cannot see
Put in my way to constantly
Throw me off of my task

Giving me gifts with traps inside,
Thinking I wont see past my own pride
Fools that hide behind the same mask

A flock of ravens is in my view
My first goal though I still pursue
Oblivious to the ravens' kiss

Looking around I'm where I started
Unbent reality still left uncharted
I suppose it always ends like this
