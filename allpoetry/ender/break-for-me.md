Break For Me 

Mirror, mirror, break for me
Shatter my image down constantly
For what I see in you is fake
Illusioned reflection of what I forsake

Give up and give in for I can't run away
But I try to escape this life every day
Almost to the point of caring no more
There must be something worth living for

I wonder if there is such a place
Where suicide never thought to embrace
Where happiness rains from skies of blue
The trust of love given is true

Where the rivers of my tears run dead dry
Together forever we live in the sky
If only a reality this dream could be
But mirror, mirror, break for me
