Leave Me Cured

burning within
consumable flame
killing the life
all is the same

wracked and worn
shaken with pain
i can't do this
drives me insane

leave me cured
or i'll be eaten
lying before you
hideously beaten
nowhere to run
never to hide
can't get away from
this feeling inside

so tired, useless
no longer to fight
closing my eyes
losing the light

letting it take me
wrapping my soul
filling with darkness
making me whole

purity's mirror
caressing the sin
always forever
forever again

clouding the mind
betrayed by lust
climbing the mountain
then crumbling to dust

leave me cured
or i'll be eaten
lying before you
hideously beaten
nowhere to run
never to hide
can't get away from
this feeling inside

feeling the joy
shouldn't feel at all
out of my reach
demon bird's call

taste of the joy
i always hail
burns in my mouth
filling me stale

deep in the night
peering into the sea
reflecting the secrets
of eternity

utopia's sunset
crashes all down
breaking to pieces
and hitting the ground

now leave me cured
or i'll be eaten
lying before you
hideously beaten
nowhere to run
never to hide
can't get away from
this feeling inside

running away from
my blackened heart
battle that slowly
tears me apart
losing myself within
vortex of my fears
the haunted music i play
falls upon deaf ears
everything is lost
nothing left to gain
demonic whisperings
driving me insane
