Blind Tranquility 

I'm out of the shroud that's hidden me
But they still can't see my face
Floating through a crystalline sea
Somewhere between time and space
Blue mists sparkle and swirl 'round
Captivating me, holding my sight
In a mystical tranquility I am drowned
Onward into a never ending night
