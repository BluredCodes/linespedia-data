Falling Into Place

as you look into my eyes
you don't understand
the weight upon my heart
that i feel every day
always you see past
what's written on my face
and in the end it's the same
falling back in place

stabbed in the back
plunging deep within
but i don't think that i
can feel it anymore
i'm too numb from
the pain you always bring
what i've felt before
always the same damn thing

i can't see why
everytime i try
it slips and falls away
i feel i want to die
i can't see why
i can't find a way
to make this pain subside
to make it go away

standing here tonight
looking at myself
looking at my hands
i see happiness around
but feel none inside

shining like the gold
even i once held
but in my heart it can't heal
the timeless, open gash
for the gold that i once held
long since, turned to ash

wishing to myself
that it will go away
wishing without you
only you can erase
but you don't see the hurt
painted on my face
and everything is blind
falling into place
