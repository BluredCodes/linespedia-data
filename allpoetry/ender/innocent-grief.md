Innocent Grief

repress the tears that burn
save them for another day
but it only grows to be worse
wishing for another way

walk through the sorrow again
i never knew the one i seek
though i can't call this pain mine
staring into his eyes so bleak

innocence lived in the heart
of he whom was left alone
beaten and forlorn, he stands
unbroken lies his will of stone

go on, for this isn't the end
anguish shall continue to rise
as in his ears he hears
his own heart wrenching cries

but strength shall be found
a certain strength untouched before
one last time, wishing for something
to make it better once more

over the horizon, it's felt
the radiant glow of the sun
breaking through the darkness
the hurt's not yet begunThe result of one man's suicide.
