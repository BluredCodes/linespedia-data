Emerald Skies

it feels like forever
since the last time
i felt happiness inside
when all of it was mine
i feel used and worn
worth nothing anymore
useless to the people
i'm being happy for

but i see you there
shining before me
taking away the pain
letting me breathe
i know it can't be
like emerald skies
but just the mere thought
brings tears to my eyes

i dream of a place
where our dreams fly
wher under the stars
forever we'll lie
untouched by sorrow
pure as the springs
that feed us life
together we'll fly

but i see you there
shining before me
taking away the pain
letting me breathe
i know it can't be
like emerald skies
but just the mere thought
brings tears to my eyes
