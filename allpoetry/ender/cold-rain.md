Cold Rain 

Cold rain falls down around me
Soaking my hair to my head
As I stare at you through the mist
And wish you loved me instead

Hidden in the shadows around
You do not see me as I gaze
In plain sight, you still would not notice
For your attention lies in past days

You stare ahead at someone else
Someone you care for more than I
While he receives your love 
Inside, I feel I want to die

Giving up hope, I look to the ground
And I close my eyes in pain
Lastly I glimpse a smile on your face
As my tears blend with the rain
