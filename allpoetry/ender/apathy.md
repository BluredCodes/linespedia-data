Apathy

wait no longer for this to die
close your eyes and it is gone
don't let yourself get to high
will life be the same at dawn?

ask me this i'll never know
my scars fade with pain
numbed finally, wishing to go
if i don't i'll go insane

so swallow your lies for me to see
it can't be hidden like before
nothing like this could ever be
or exist here anymore

higher than you will ever be
too bad the truth you fail to see
you'll burn to ash in the end

isolation has me cold
forced to live outside the mold
apathetic to the way i've sinned
