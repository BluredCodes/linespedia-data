Remembrance 

Looking around, I take it all in
And ask myself why I came back to this place
It’s nothing like what it once was
Of my happiness, there’s no trace 

My surroundings, they are dark
A breeze ruffles my hair
Nights like this bring back memories
Of the times we once shared 

I’d like to think I’m past this
You’re but a closed chapter in a book
But constantly I find myself reminded
I see you everywhere I look 

And I feel so helpless
Because I know there’s nothing I can do
But sit here,remember, and try to forget
The nights I spent with you

I’m tired of always feeling this way
Can’t stop, even though I try
I know it’s time to move on
Time to stop wishing to die

For I knew when I fell in love with you
That still I’d have to meet the consequence
Now that time is here and I feel it
As I am faced with my own decadence 

I turn to leave; I feel it’s time to go
And there’s no reason for me here to remain
Looking back I catch one last glimpse
With my departure, dies the pain
