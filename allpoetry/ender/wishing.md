Wishing

i yearn inside
for something for
which to live this time
when i'm thrown to the edge
too far down the line
falling down again

wishing on a star
that's falling to the ground
like music that entrances
without a trace of sound
bending into my mind

touching the color
swirling in despair
nothings what it seems
i want to love her
a beautiful thing to share
shattered like glass
are my dreams

when everything's gone around
and promises lie forsaken
burning in a lover's eyes
leaving hearts short shaken
whispering the feelings
that slip away from me
whispering the silence
but this is how it should be
this is how it should be
