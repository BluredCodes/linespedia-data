title: "For You"
writer: "Ender"

for you

lying here breathless
staring through the ceiling
at the shrouded moon

i think about the way
you shackle my heart
tight to the wall
you snip my wings
so when i fly
i only fall

but i do it willingly
for you

i remind myself again
to take a breath within
but it hurts

i look in your eyes
and i wonder
if what i see is
what you really feel
is this just a dream
or is this really real

but still i do it willingly
for you

hold me as i plummet
whisper in my ear
forgive me for
what I've done
thaw my freezing fear

when love is slowly wilting
you want to bring it back
back to life once again
when a cloud covers the moon
you want to push it away
almost nothing i can do
but this time it shall stay
i do it willingly for you
