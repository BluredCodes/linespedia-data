No Words

if only words could be
found for these emotions
living within me
i dream of a day
they will go away
when i will make you see

romance fornever
filling my heart
empty and dismal
falling apart
if only i could feel
the things others do
if only i could love
someone like you

but that's not the way
it's meant to be
i'm not even sure
if it should be
i won't deny
the pain took its toll
but in the loneliness i hear
the music of my soul
that will never go away
