Before The End

there are no words to be found
for this mass of hatred inside
stretched too thin, to quick
it's no surprise although i tried

can you see, or are you blind?
to the way i cannot take in
accept as my own the life you want
me to live for you again

screaming to the heavens
fighting at this shell
to escape, to be free for once
rescue me from this hell

take me to the stars
so far up in the sky
show me what is ours
quickly before i die

before hope is thrown away
and i give in once more to the pain
before i end it all before you
lying here in the rainThere were so many emotions when I wrote this... I probably should've waited until my mind was clearer.
