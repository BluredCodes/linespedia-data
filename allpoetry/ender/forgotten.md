Forgotten

standing there within the breeze
and hopelessly falling to his knees
pleading his lover do remain

left there all so very alone
in shadows where the sun's not shone
of trees whispering her name

but they rest and whisper no more
of whom he loved like never before
the memory fades away

left behind, an innocent pain
his eyes shine with an unfallen rain
and the trees yet silently swayI meant this poem to have ample beauty, but the effect is reversed when it comes out in a negative form... but whatever, I'm just speculating.
