Rise

blindly stabbed in the back again
betrayed by he who was my friend
but the truth's revealed in your eyes
in the end you know i'll rise

limply hanging from branch of tree
rope cutting through this neck of me
whispers ripe in my ears, your lies
despite them, inside you know i'll rise

buried within a cold hard grave
broken so boldly a heart so brave
never again will i witness blue skies
until the day you know i'll rise

lost in a sea of hopeless souls
memories blended with empty holes
another lonely hero dies
but in the end you know i'll rise
