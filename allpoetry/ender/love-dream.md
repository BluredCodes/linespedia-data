Love Dream

i am in a meadow
the breeze ruffles my hair
you are running beside me
i am free, 
without a care

you are pointing upwards
to something in the sky
twinkling stars shine down on us
together gazing
upward we lie

slowly you turn to me
and look me in the eyes
then the world disintegrates
sitting up
my dream dies
