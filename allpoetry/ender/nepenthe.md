Nepenthe

can you hear the whispers
infringing on existence
of everything
i don't think that you
can ever understand
the putrid feelings in my heart
that ail me to the end

i'm at the edge
but you will never see
the shards of my glass heart
lying here, before me

and i am falling down
into a beautiful sea
that will take my pain away
lifting the fog away from me

burning within is a hate
to match the flames of hell
but i have long been empty
cherishing an empty shell
but i cling to the hope
that in the end, you'll see
