Angel I'm Not 

Look to my sides, you see no wings
For from me they've been taken
Angelic suicide and the pain it brings
Realizing I've been forsaken

Hollow echoes of my dreams
That once filled the air and my being
Now are shattered like agonizing screams
To match these memories I'm seeing

Tears flow freely, burning down my face
As I try to see past what I know is true
Try with my might to quickly erase
Feelings of hatred I once had for you

For the angel you are is the angel I hate
I hold onto something that won't be forgot
I can no longer deny the blood on the slate
The angel you are is the angel I'm not
