Stealing

burning within me
something that just won't die
turning my heart to ashes
that are scattered on the wind
and as the rop tightens
as clouds cover the sky
i watch as my life crashes
and i'm pushed into the end

waking up in a sweat
soaking my hair to my head
i realize it was just a dream
and relinquish in relief
lying awake again
basking in lost tears shed
recalling a memory serene
but it crumbles like a leaf
in my hand, again

you're always taking away
what i cherish so dear
you're always throwing me back
when i just want out of here
you're never giving me
the life i'd like to live
and you're always throwing away
what i'm so forced to give

swimming in a sea
clear as crystal falls
i know it's just a fantasy
but i savor it while i can
floating forever into nothing
breaking unbreakable walls
i know this can't possibly be
forgot where it all began

slowly i'm brought back
to reality once more
but this time in regret
but it's lost all over again
billowing within me
frustration felt not before
darkness begins to beset
and i lose myself again
it doesn't matter anymore

you're always taking away
what i cherish so dear
you're always throwing me back
when i just want out of here
you're never giving me
the life i'd like to live
and you're always throwing away
what i'm so forced to give

when i see nowhere to go
no purpose for living a lie
hating the taste of deception
longing inside to die
wishing when i know
all of it's now meaningless
because you're always stealing
the grains of hopefulness
i'm so empty

you're always taking away
what i cherish so dear
you're always throwing me back
when i just want out of here
you're never giving me
the life i'd like to live
and you're always throwing away
what i'm so forced to give
