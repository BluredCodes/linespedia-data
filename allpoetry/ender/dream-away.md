Dream Away 


Awaken from sleep into a dream
I'm unsure of how things should seem
Tentatively walking away

Back to a place I once was before
A place on the horizon I know I adore
Holding my sorrows at bay

Voices that lie in the back of my mind
Tell me that I left something behind
But I push them back down

I stand countless feet in the air
Looking over a ledge into despair
An ocean where I happily drown

Something though, calls me back
To the place my heart emptily does lack
Colors flow in my head

Fog rises from the knee high grass
Blocking my vision, blinded alas
But I remember what you said

Whispering to myself to keep me alive
So close to the happiness for which I strive
But I'm always led astray

Floating to the sky, ever higher
Letting the fabric of life catch on fire
Dreaming my problems away
