Tonight

Lying back, my arms are spread to my sides
Falling deep into sleep where no sorrow hides
A bliss where perfection is lived every day
Nothing to stand in harmony's way

This night we're in a windswept field; look at the sky
A shooting star sweeping over where you and I lie
Take in its beauty tonight while you can
For tomorrow may end your life where you stand

Open your hand, for I have for you a prize
Whispering softly as I gaze into your eyes
Take my trust straight from my heart
Together forever and never apart

I point to a star's death again in the sky
Ironic how beauty comes as we watch the star die
Beauty from nature's wondrous demise
A beauty unequal to the happiness in your eyes

Imagine for me a world with no fears
Where never again I'll witness your tears
Where there's no reason to have to put up a fight
Imagine for me a world like tonight
