Winter Rain

if only it would stop
hurting inside
if only i could stop
wanting to hide
from all the things
that haunt me so
if only those things
would just go

the fire that once
raged within
long since has died
and never again
will it burn its way
through my soul

in the darkness
so far away
a light can be found
to save me today
to give me a chance
and kill the last pain
but my heart is cold
like winter rain

though the mountain is high
and the burden weighs
i'm forced to remember
all of those days
i spent on my knees
wishing for light
a broken man's pleas

but i'll never go back
and never give in
i'll die before i feel
that pain again
though i'm so empty
with nothing to gain
my heart is cold
like winter rain
forever to be
like winter rainHope's not that far away...
