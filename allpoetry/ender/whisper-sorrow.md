---
title: "Whisper Sorrow"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "whisper-sorrow"
source: "AllPoetry"

whisper sorrow

as the raindrops fall
crashing to the ground
i hear the echoes
making a sound
within the recesses
of my soul

for it is empty
it's meaningless
everything i try
comes back to this
hopes of mine die
it all comes back to this

haze of confusion
falls over my path
and blocking my sight
i live in the moment
and try to ignore
that haze that blocks
the light

the moonlight falls
across your face
revealing your eyes to me
losing myself within them
i could die happily
if i could gaze into them
forever

but within my soul
it is empty
it's meaningless
everything i try
comes back to this
hopes of mine die
it all comes back to this

your smile is captivating
but still it hurts me so
as the burden deep within
does nothing but grow
and it burns within my heart
as if only i know
tearing me apart
whisper sorrow
whisper sorrow

it's empty
it's meaningless
everything i try
comes back to this
hopes of mine die
it all comes back to this
