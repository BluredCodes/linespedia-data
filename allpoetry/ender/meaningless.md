Meaningless

lying here
the grass to my back
your words in my head
i see the moon
through the clouds
and my cigarette

i remember just what
you told me that day
i remember your face
i remember the way
i gave up

like dust on the wind
that's slipping away
but i don't care anymore
too empty to care anymore

cause i don't need you
i don't want you
and i'm so tired of
what i go through
i ask myself why
when i don't need you
i still want you
but still i try

so i'll just lie here
with my back on everything
the pain you gave
i try to forget
as i watch the moon
through my cigarette
