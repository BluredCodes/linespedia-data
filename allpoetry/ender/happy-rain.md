Happy Rain 

I don't care about anything anymore
Just living every day as I can
Trying to enjoy my life while I'm here
Trying to be a care free man

In fields of flowers that smile at me
I'm dancing under the sun
Smiling back so gleefully
The goal to be free is the only one

All I want is someone to save me
But I've nothing to be saved from
I've no troubles left that I can see
Cept an empty guitar to strum

Falling beautifully is scattered rain
From clouds that aren't of grey
But this rain isn't the rain of pain
Yet rain to wash the tears away

My life is my own to live as I will
And not yours to tell me how
For I'll live it like this way up until
Always, my way, forever I vow
