Scarlet Kiss

another meaningless night
wallowing to myself
wanting to sleep, escape
a temporary way out
i feel a longing inside
lost somewhere in fog
but sorrows strike me down
always, striking me down

wanting to get away
to be free from all of this
wishing for just a chance
just one scarlet kiss
in that castle we built
becoming one with a heart
but my vision of perfection
with everything falls apart

i miss the things
that i used to see
those things i remember
used to belong to me
when there's nothing to fear
and nothing to lose
i just wish that the stars
would stay the same for you

the walls of my castle
are slowly closing in
i'm losing control
of myself deep within
the key to the barrier
is lost far away
i grab hold of a hope
willing it to stay
but all i feel is the pain

wanting to get away
to be free from all of this
wishing for just a chance
just one scarlet kiss
in that castle we built
becoming one with the hart
but my vision of perfection
with everything falls apart
