Bloodied 

Trust and betrayal are very different things
Betrayal resides in the pain it brings
While a broken trust does not quickly mend
And each have prices to pay in the end

Unforgiven you are, and unforgiven you'll stay
For the things in my life I still feel to this day
Forgiveness won't erase the things that I'm feeling
Nor the love from me you're constantly stealing

The pages of past are carved in a stone
But I wasn't damned to always be alone
For the red rose of love can rise up again
From weeds of hatred in a garden of sin

I lived in a world of dark clouds and woe
Where the earth was a hell of ever present sorrow
But then the clouds parted for the light of your love
And the ground grew what I could only dream of

A flower that is bloodied and thorned all around
Yet no prettier a thing has ever been found
Picking it births the price I will pay
Bloodied are my fingers, yet my hope does not sway
