Memories of Dust

there are some things
i feel are worth
remembering
but others i want
more than anything else
to finally forget

but i've a hope
way deep inside
that someday things
will change
and that hope inside
hasn't ever died
and i don't think it will

it's painful to know
that nothing is forever
but it helps to know
that lovers together
have a happiness unmatched
by heaven's sunlight
their love won't die
but they themselves will
leaving nothing for us
except a little memory
but all memories
finally come to dust

that sunset
there on the horizon
reminds of days gone
sorrowful days among the rest
but days of happiness
far at best

as it pains me to know
that those days of sorrow
and the days of happiness
will one day leave us
as all memories
one day come to dust

steel that used to gleam
under rain will rust
as gravestones marking tombs
will be worn away
for alas, those memories
we preserve today
they can't be held onto
and one day will leave us
with nothing but
shadows of doubtfulness
ever so gently leaving us
for everything in life must
in the end, come to dustThis was "piggy-backed" off of one of another famous writer's works.  I bet you cannot tell me who...
