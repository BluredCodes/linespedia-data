Controlled 

Life around me passes like a dream, 
reflected in a translucent gleam
Feelings swirl through my brain

Though lost within a mist they are, 
they’re someone else’s thoughts by far
Of most of these emotions, I am drained

Invisible puppet strings form the noose, 
longing to be set free and loose
But these wants quickly subside

For the shadow falls upon my hope, 
the hope of a man at the end of his rope
I’ve failed every time I’ve tried

The mirror I so often see, 
it appears right here before me
Exposing to me my flaws

Flaws I live with day and night, 
they are now right in my sight,
And I am held in awe

I step through the mirror as it liquidates, 
try as I might not to desecrate
For now before me is my destiny

Do as they say, do as they do, 
follow the goals they want to pursue
If only they would leave me be
