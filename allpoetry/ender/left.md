Left 

Here I sit, emptily looking around
Your whisper in my head the only sound
Reassurance that you will always be there
Lies that burn through my every prayer

Here I sit, and here I will stay
Breaking and throwing my happiness away
Like shards of glass covered in blood
For the gash in my heart forever will flood

Stitch it together with needle and pin
Closing myself to the world again
Exile from the love I always search for
Closed tightly to be opened never more
