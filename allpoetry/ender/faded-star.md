Faded Star

lying in bed, lulled by the sound
of rain outside again
pushing me deeper into a sleep
with dreams of a hollow wind

dreams that always remind me
of what i failed to see
but sometimes i'm forced to wonder
the difference of dream and memory

though all of it's lost
in a background, darkish haze
although i don't want to remember
i'll never forget those days

i wish my hopes weren't empty
meaningless and desecrated
for the star i've always wished upon
long since away has faded
