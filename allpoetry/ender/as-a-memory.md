---
title: "As A Memory"
writer: "Ender"
slug: "as-a-memory"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17439-As-A-Memory-by-Ender"
---

touch me from behind
i look around
but you're not there
wherever i go 
i see your smile
i smell your hair
but it's lost in the wind

slipping through
right through my fingers
like meaningless sand
everything falls down
can't stop it i know
but still i try
and still it falls

but i'm still here
and you're long gone
left me to live
forced me to see
left me here
as a memory

the hour glass is broken
and it makes me wonder
if i'm out of time
but i can't see why
no, i can't see why

but i'm still here
and you're long gone
left me to live
forced me to see
left me here
as a memory

living like this
outside a breeze
that whispers through me
i can't see why
living like this
outside your care
i want to die

but i'm still here
and you're long gone
left me to live
forced me to see
left me here
as a memory
as a memory
