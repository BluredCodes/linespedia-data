Betray Again

i can see through the way
you bring me down again
and i can see that through the haze
it's left unchanged in the end

another light burns away
and another heart is jaded
no air beneath my wings
as another love is faded

it's too hard to breathe right now
but you hear my whisper anyway
too far to the end, my closest friend
died to me that day

numb to this sorrow that lives in me
that pulls me apart from inside
escape, where are you, come to me
i can't, trust me, i've tried

i have no more room for this
wash the pain away from my face
beauty dulled, empty eyes
you can never replace

there's so much i want to say
have you no hope to lend?
if only i could get away
if only i could make this end
but you betray again
