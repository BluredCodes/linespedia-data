To Stand With You 

To prevent against the terror
We must unite one and all
Or so they say, which we believe
Beckoned to their call

I'm insignificant to everyone
In this world but me
I am the only one who cares
About what I must be

But that is understandable
With the lack of beauty or love
We feel for each other, or should
Confusion reigning from above

And as they whisper 'together'
My mind screams so true
I would rather stand alone
Than stand as one with you

So stand alone I will and would
Will take every ounce of pride
I have in my heart and my mind
For alone I stood and died
