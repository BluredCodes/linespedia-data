The Fires Of Heaven

Desolate street on which I stand
Left all alone by my own command
Nothing in front of me that I wish to see
This nightmare of mine can't possibly be

But deep in my heart, I know it is true
Shot down in flight, when so short I flew
Nowhere to turn, nowhere to go
Nothing but pain left to show

The scars in my heart, the blood on my face
You never see past what I try to erase
You're stupidity shows so very clearly
As you miss what a blind man could see

Looking up I see far in the sky
Fires that burn the Heavens dry
Sweet fires of love among flames of hate
Falling to Earth, down to hell's gate

Burning my skin and searing my eyes
Birthing the pain that never quite dies
Filling me up from inside to out
A bitter remorse, I'm never without

Stitch me together all over again
Such is the price for a horrible sin
What is redone will never be the same
From the time I was burned by Heaven's own flame
