All For Nothing

she's standing there
so innocent before me
beautifully so fair
reflecting the light in her eyes
where sorrow always dies
she's got flowers in her hair

i can see her
through a spectral vision
where one could gaze forever
wishing on a star
to give a stellar collision
that would put us together

but there's no meaning
all for nothing
i will never win
throw my tears
into the ocean
they'll just come back again

lost in the mist
gone forever
will i see her once more?
it makes me wonder
about this love
i say i'm living for

the loss i feel
weighs me down
i wish i didn't care
but past it all
i can see her
with flowers in her hair

but there's no meaning
all for nothing
i will never win
throw my tears
into the ocean
they'll just come back again
