Forgotten Eulogy

pressured within and blinded by sin
wishing for sight to be clear again
but they preach the truth before my eyes
and i hear in my ears no longer my cries

longing to be where i'm not anymore
lost in the past, i've missed it before
i fear, so alone, why must this stay?
need someone here to take this away

open my mind to the sky above
bring down from the heavens a new kind of love
but though i scream, you'll never see
how so forgotten is the better part of me

and now buried in hatred, a tombstone so cold
resembling the potter's broken glass mold
listening to eulogy whispered on the breeze
my last hopes fallen before cemetery treesI didn't know what category this would be in.
