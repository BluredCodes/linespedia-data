Tired

i'm so sick of this despair
that fills me here this night
as my skin tingles against the air
and i'm sinking into a blight

my vision slowly turning to black
if you could only see through
my eyes, just once, you'd see
how it is i feel for you

hollow echoes of my tears
the mist surrounding me
the silence pressing against my ears
all of this, too much to be

standing here, i'm forced to face
the memories, distant as they are
i only want away from this place
viewing freedom from afarYeah
