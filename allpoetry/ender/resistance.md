Resistance

i wake up from my dream
into a nightmare again
where i'm running from you
but you catch me in the end

deep in my soul
i am lost beyond
ever being found once more
but my life continues
hopelessly without you
i give up on what i'm looking for
and i shall drown once more

i'm embracing it
giving in to the tide
but a spark flickers
very deep inside
something alive
that wants to fight it
that knows this isn't
the way it should be
the very last fragment
of what is left of me

but i'll never let you
control me

i see the world
through cracked eyes
of glass
for i'll never be one
with the rest of
the mass
i'll never be like
what you want me
to be
i've long given up
on making you see

as i hang my head in shame
and despair i can't explain
for broken is my heart
but only i'm to blame

i'll never let you
control me

the wind breaks through me
and i look to the sky
screaming my sorrow for you
i want to die
but i'll never let you
control me
