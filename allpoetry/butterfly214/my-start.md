Josh told me to write a poem, 
to write what I feel, and make it my own. 
He said writing would help with  pain, 
I hope writing will keep me for going insane. 
Putting thoughts on paper, feelings out in the open, 
Many of the things I feel sometimes go unspoken. 
I have been through a lot, I think it made me stronger. 
I am so lucky to have my sweetie in my life, I am not alone any longer. 
We will be together for eternity, I hope he always knows, 
Everyday we are together, my love for him only grows. 
He seems to take away all my pain, with just one simple kiss. 
When we are apart, His smile is what I always seem to miss. 
He is my hero, my inspiration to writing this,  my first poem 
writing my feelings on paper,and  making it my own...
