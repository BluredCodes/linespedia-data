The first time I knew you loved me I felt a feeling so great
I told you that I was falling, but still, I wanted to wait.
I was afraid of opening my heart to you, past hurts lingered in my mind. 
But then I saw how unique you were how gentle, sweet and kind.
I was falling faster and finally saw the end
I could finally say I love you and start a new life with my lover and best friend.
When I hear you say I love you my heart just skips a beat
it's like we were always meant for eachother, you were my missing piece.
I will always be their for you through the happy and the sad
and since you have been there for me, life hasn't been so bad.
I am so lucky to have such a great person to share the rest of my life
and someday when we are ready I will be so proud to be your wife.
