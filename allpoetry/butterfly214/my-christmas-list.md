Christmas time is approaching fast, everyone is making that holiday dash.
They run for the stores, searching for that perfect gift spending all their cash.
They get lost in all the craziness, they don't take time and see
so, think about this, how would a Christmas without spending any money be?
I understand exactly what they mean when they say, 'the best things in life are free.'
I was given the best present I could ever get when the love of my life fell in love with me.
He gives me presents everyday, they are priceless, they never cost a thing,
he gives me hugs and kisses and makes my heart sing.
The gifts he gives me are very personal and true, you couldn't find them in a store
yet they are everything I could ever want, I couldn't ask for more.
An I love you, a hug and a mistletoe kiss
is all I want from my love this Christmas.
