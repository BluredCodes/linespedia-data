I was walking down a hallway recalling our time together,
we promised eachother eternity,we needed something longer than forever.
I came upon a door and curious, looked inside,
inside was yesterday, a day when we were together, much in love, you were right by my side.
I shut the door with a smile and kept walking till I reached another door, and a new day.
I opened the door and saw us happy, sharing the tons of love we have, that day was today.
I knew the best was yet to come, I see another door,
through it, another day, tomorrow, and I'll love you more...
