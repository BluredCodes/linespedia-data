Love, what is it exactly? By definition, passion, affection

finding someone you want to be with, make your selection.

But its not as easy as this you see,

that person has to love you back for that love to be.

To love someone is great but, to have them love you back is the true test.

If you love someone and they love you, consider yourself blessed.

Love can become so powerful, you can be carelessly lost in there touch.

Just by looking into there eyes you will feel that wonderful feeling you have longed for so much.

So if you love someone, don't let the feelings slip away.

Let them know you love them, let them know today.
