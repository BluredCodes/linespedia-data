title: "Letting go of the past"

I built a wall around my heart with a door locked tight, no one could come in.
I built a wall around my heart,and wouldn't let love begin.
I have been hurt times before, had my heart broken,
sad but true,love can hurt, the reason for my wall and i left things unspoken.
I built a wall around my heart but someone came knocking.
Should I let him in? Give love another chance? that door , I thought about unlocking.
It wouldn't be easy , a broken heart is hard to mend.
Lets take our time, it will be worth it in the end.
I built a wall around my heart, but let someone in.
this hero of mine helped me move on, with time i let love begin.
When bad things happen, through them we can learn lessons.
I learned to trust not my head but my heart, it always leads you in the right direction.
I built a wall around my heart but, your love made it fall.
After all, my heart and love for you is much too big to be built inside a wall.
