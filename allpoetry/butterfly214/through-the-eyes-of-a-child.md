Look through the eyes of a child tell me, what do you see?
Do you see all the violence and destruction in the world? Or just a life of glee?
Innocent and pure are the childhood years,
a kiss from their mother can comfort all their fears.
If we saw things as children saw them, would our world be bright?
Would we see the good in the world, and be filled with delight?
A child, such splendor, only God could create.
With wisdom and power so great.
A child's mind is small,
yet through their eyes, we see it all.
Childhood holds a world of happy dreaming,
with peace, love and a special meaning.
They understand things and look at the good,
they look at things the way we all should.
Look through the eyes of a child tell me, how would it be?
If we took time and saw things as a child sees.
