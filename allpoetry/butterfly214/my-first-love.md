title: "My first love"

Everyone dreams of finding that one special person to love, 
they want it to be special, they ask for help from up above. 
Some may make a wish upon a star, star light, star bright, 
to find that one person to make everything all right. 
I was very lucky to find you, my one true love, 
unlike many others, I didn't need any help from up above. 
Looking into your eyes, a smile comes across my face, 
wherever we are, as long as we are together, it's the happiest place. 
You are my hero, my soul mate, and my best friend, 
our love is like a circe, it will never end.
