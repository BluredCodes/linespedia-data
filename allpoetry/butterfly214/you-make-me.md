You make me so happy, you bring out the best in me,
We promised eachother eternity, and for eternity we will be.
You make me feel alive, my heart sings,
you make me fly without having any wings.
You make it known, it's okay if we take our time
because I'll always have your heart, and you will have mine.
You make me feel a love so true, I lose control.
You touched me deeply, you have touched my soul.
You make me complete, I love you.
