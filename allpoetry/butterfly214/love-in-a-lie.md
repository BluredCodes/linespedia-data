---
title: "Love in a lie"
writer: "Butterfly214"
slug: "love-in-a-lie"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am only a child of 16, yet behind these eyes,
fear, broken promises, and pain lies.
I try to hide the pain with a smile,
but inside I am crying all the while.
I try not to think about it, but it doesn't make the pain go away.
I feel like I should talk about it, but don't know what to say.
How do you tell someone, the person you thought loved you, hurt you so bad?
I can't stop thinking about it, it always makes me sad.
Left alone, hurt physically and mentally,
I thought love was supposed to be special, 
how can this be?
