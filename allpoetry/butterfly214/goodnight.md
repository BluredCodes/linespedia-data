When we are together or talking on the phone,
I hear your voice, and I know I'm not alone. 
I'm sorry when we say goodnight it seems like I get mad.
but the thought of you leaving me in anyway, always makes me sad.
I try to remind myself your not  really leaving, just going to sleep, 
but then it's like I'm all alone, with a feeling that cuts deep.
Don't ask me why I feel this way, I simply do not know,
I just start thinking, and I don't want you to go.
I start to think of bad things, things from the past,
yet when I hear your voice, you take the pain away fast. 
One day I hope I can hear goodnight, and know exactly what it means,
that your not leaving me just simply going to sleep, and I'll see you in my dreams...
