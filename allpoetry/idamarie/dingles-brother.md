Dingle's Little BrotherPercy had a teen aged brotherBarry was his name.When Percy told him he was hangin' outon the corner of Fifth Street,Barry hung himself out on sixth.The newspaper reported the next day,Dingle, Barry gets ten days in jail.Percy paid his fine and made him wear his pants backwards for the next six months.That didn't work either .Every night people gather onsixth street to howl at the moon.
