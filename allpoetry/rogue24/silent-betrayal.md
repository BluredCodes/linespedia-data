I walk down these long halls 
Every day of my life
As the people pass by
I feel more empty and alone
But as you approach
A smile begins
Some of my few escapes
Come from crying with you
Smoking yet another cigrette 
As we gaze at the stars
But today...
Just like so many days before
You walk right by
And I keep all my anger in
I hold back all my tears
As I walk on
Through these distant corridors
And I'll continue to walk
Right into the darkness
Until you bring your tears to me
Your pain and agony
And I'll feel sorry for ever thinking
That I felt you were betraying me
You'll continue to walk by
Perhaps a glance every now and then
But I'll be here waiting
When the world hurts you
And I'll be here 
With my silent tears
Of betrayalthis is a poem of betrayal *obviously* but its about a friend who you never hear from and you agonize over it and think they are betraying you but you forget all about it when they actually do call or talk to you.
