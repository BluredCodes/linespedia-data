title: "Heros of the Soul"
writer: "rogue24"

i want to be your hero
the one who saves you from your darkest fears
i want you to need me
for the rest of your days
i need you to love me
so the world isnt so cruel
i want to hold you
and make you feel like everything in the world
is perfect
i want you to see something in me 
that i thought never existed
like i see all the love i ever need
in you
i have found my life
my soul
my everything 
IN YOU
