---
title: "Demons of the Mind"
writer: "rogue24"

I find it hard to breathe
Difficult to think
Every beat of my heart
Draws me closer to the end
The shadows embrace me
As your blood covers my body
My darkest demons 
Pick me up and guide me
To the last door 
The last path I will ever choose
I grasp the last portion of life that remains
My final hope
In remaining alive
As my essence escapes
I hold on to what you have given me
And all I have sacrificed to be 
All you have ever wanted
Spare me this torture
Forgive my mistakes
My imperfections
And my broken heart
I'm nothing more than a tarnished soul
That is bleeding and broken
On the verge of death
As you turn away
