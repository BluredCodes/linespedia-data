---
title: "My Imagination"
writer: "rogue24"

---

No matter how hard you try
You can imagine that its not what it seems
I'm slipping away from me
Away from the world
I cant hold on much longer
I struggle to find your hand in the dark
But I find nothing
As the emptiness consumes me
I wait in silence
But I feel the light betray me
How much longer can I wait?
For everything to slip away
My life seems to have been a dream
But how much longer can I hold myself up?
The sands of time slipping through my fingers
As I cry out for you to care
I hide it 
It just seems to be my imagination
