---
title: "self-destruct"
writer: "rogue24"

Inside and out
I'm torn apart
Forced to go on living
In this world
You call paradise
But I call it hell
For all I do is wait here in pain
In agony
Waiting for my life to change
And slip me away from you
From your hateful lies
And my self destructive life
