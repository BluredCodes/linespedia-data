I've never met you
But in my mind
You are everything

I've never looked in your eyes
But I can see so deep
I feel my soul reaching out for yours

I force myself to push you away
Struggling to grasp a portion of sanity
Thoughts of you conquering my mind

You've taken over my mind
Like a theif in the night
You've stolen my soul

How much longer must I fend for my heart?
There's nothing I can do
But scream words of pain

I deny all impulses
And I move on
As I leave you in the dark

Another night of hopeless confusion
I awake in the morning
As you've been in my dreams

I awake
And you're still there
Inside of my head

As the rain falls
I realize that the struggle continues
And I still fear the dawn

Another day
I still realize
I have never met you or even touched you

But one day I will look in your eyes
And my soul will melt just as it does in my dreams
Then I'll turn away and we'll forget
