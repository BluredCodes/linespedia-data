---
title: "Life And Death"
writer: "rogue24"

I lay in this dark room
Surrounded by dark demons
My body covered in tears and blood
With every gesture I make
My world falls apart
With every tear I cry
It adds to a river of sadness
That attempts to drown me
With every drop of blood 
That escapes my body
It adds to my suffering
I'm twisted in this world
Of life and death
