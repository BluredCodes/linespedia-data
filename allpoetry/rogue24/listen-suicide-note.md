Look into my eyes
They like to tell the truth
When I like to keep secrets

Look at me
Cant you see through my smile?
Broken and pathetic

Listen to my heart
Listen very closely...
Cant you hear my heart screaming?
Screaming for you to listen...

Put your hand on my heart
Dont you feel the scars?
The sharp edges of a broken heart

A slash of the heart
Soon...the end will begin
And you'll be so sorry
That you didnt listen
You didnt take time to wonder why
