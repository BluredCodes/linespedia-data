title: "The Hatred Of One"
writer: "rogue24"

These walls close in more everyday
I slip away from reality
My thoughts blur
So I cant seem to breathe
As I close my eyes
The tears of anger begin to fall
Dont you realize the hatred 
I have for you
That is boiling inside
I may be ignorant to your ways
But to me 
You're ignorance is on top of broken trust
But you're to blind to see
