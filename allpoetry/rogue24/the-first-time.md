We stood in the middle of a crowed room
In all eyes
We were fools
In my eyes 
I could see no one but you

The first time I saw
I could never stop thinking about you
I dreamt of your grasp and loving arms
Wrapped around my soul
Melting my heart in the palm of your hand

The moment I touched you for the first time
I felt a rush consuming my every thought
I couldnt think of anything I would rather do
Than brush my hand with yours once again

The eternal second that I had waited for
That seemed forever to come
The very second when our lips met
An icy shiver into my soul 
And I could not stop you from entering my heart

And the moment when we held each other close
And said those small words
That changed our lives forever
I could never forget the tears that weeped into my mind

The moment when I made love to you
I felt my soul explode
And my heart beat out of control
I knew I wanted to spend the rest of my life
Wanting you
Needing you
Desiring you
And loveing you
