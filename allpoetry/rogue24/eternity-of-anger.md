title: "Eternity of Anger"

There's a danger
in my anger
There's a threat
in my silence
There's a deadly consequence
in my lost heart
Do you dare ask if something is wrong?
The world is cruel!
Your soul is harsh!
The sight of you sickens me!
With every breath you take
i become more weak
I hate everything you have ever done
And all i know you will do
Stop revolving your world around my threatening soul
Why wont the darkness consume your heartless soul?
Why does the sun on the horizon awaken you morning after morning?
I hate the darkness
The suspense of another day by your side
I fear the light
That awakens my soul
And all I can think about is drowning
in the agony
