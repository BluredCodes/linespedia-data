---
title: "Forgotten"
writer: "rogue24"
slug: "forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You lead me into a world
Of magic and fantasy
Made me believe you were everything
You were just a trick 
That my imagination was playing
A lie
You helped me live
I felt lost
But now I have found
The truth 
That I am a child
I was your slave
I felt alone
But now I'm showing you
How it feels to be forgotten
I was living a dream
I was hoping for that fantasy
But all you were to me
Was my imagination
You blinded me with dreams
To keep me from the truth of reality
But I dont need this
And somehow I'll find a way to forget you
To erase you from my mind
And the scars you left
One tear at a time...
