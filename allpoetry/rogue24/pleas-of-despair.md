---
title: "Pleas of despair"
writer: "rogue24"

I feel defeated
And through all the screams
No one hears me
I know there are times when I'm wrong
For I am not perfect

I feel drowned in despair
And it seems no hope awaits me
But no one cares
I know that I'm blind
But aren't we all?

I feel lost
And the confusion ripes away at my mind
But everyone seems to look away
I know that you hate me
But can't you stop causing the pain?

I feel torn
And I feel sick in my heart
But you ignore a broken girl
I know that you don't care
But I ask please! 
Here my pleas!
