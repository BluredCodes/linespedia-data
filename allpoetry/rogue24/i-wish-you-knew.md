You slammed the door
And my heart ached
You closed a chapter in my life
As you disappeared
You left nothing to me
But a memory of the anger
The harsh words
And horrible lies we told
If only I knew why you treat me this way
And if you only knew
GOD!
I wish you knew....
How much I LOVE YOU!!!!this poem is very, very personal to me. i love people commenting on my work but also keep in mind that when people write its about how they feel so please dont explode when you read this...and think "oh god another angry writer" thats not true. Poets take their feelings out on words and masterpieces are made in the process of anger and sadness. Through loss and tears. This poem is about having an arguement with the one you love and acting as though you are about to give up but you know you never would because you love them and you hope they know it.
