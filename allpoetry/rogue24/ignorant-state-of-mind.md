title: "Ignorant State Of Mind"

I look in the mirror
I hate what I see
Mirror Mirror
May I confide in you?
Can I trust you to keep my secrets?
Will you promise not to tell? 
That I long for something more
I wish to run away
To escape from it all
Slip myself into a fairy tale
Where I belong
I want to slip out of this world
Slip into the world of fantasy
That I dream of
I sit in silence
And wish for the misery to escape me
But there is nothing else I can do
I wait
Forever waiting
I sit in silence
With the battles raging on in my head
The screams of sorrow
That echos from withen my soul
My heart beats on
As a river of tears
Find their way into my mind
As I stare at a blank reflection 
Of myself
In an ignorant state of mind
