---
title: "What I Gave"
writer: "rogue24"

I gave you all my thoughts
All my dreams
I made everything around me perfect for you
I showed you all the love inside
I offered my heart and soul
That melted in the palm of your hand
But it never seemed enough
There just didnt seem to be enough love
To make you love me as much as i do you
