My black heart
Twisted and dominated
Bleeding and broken

You have never felt the pain I have
I have wounds that will never heal
The black spiders of death encrypted in my pain

The black ice 
That surrounds my sleep
Twisting me in the blankets of fear

As I slip further from you
And step away from reality
I dissect my life 

I reach for your hand
But all I pull back is a shadow
That you left for me 
A shadow of a doubt

There’s nothing I can do
To make you believe
That you will someday be mine
