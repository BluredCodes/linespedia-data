title: "Close to the End"

Tears roll down my face,
making my cheeks moist.
It wasn't just dear Clementine
who's lost and gone forever.
I'm a loosely wound ball of string.
One tug and I completely fall apart.
From a distance I appear strong
and more beautiful than fine gold.
Up close I'm as thin as a spider web
and as strong as a newborn butterflies wings.
I lie dormant as moss and rust collect over my lifeless body.
Wishing to view the world from up above in heaven.
But forced to remain in the center of it all.
When will my time come?
