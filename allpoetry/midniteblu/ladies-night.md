The sun is setting behind the horizon.
The oranges and purples and pinks blend together.
The beauty is undeniable.
Take a deep breath and step outside.
It's dark now, the sky an unmistakeable shade of midniteblu.
Electricity charges your bones as you look around.
There's a sense of danger and you welcome it.
The chance to meet and greet.
Fall in lust or better yet, fall in love.
Eyes shining, hair flipping, heels clicking...
It's time!
The night is calling and you respond wholeheartedly.
Who needs tomorrow when tonight hasn't even begun!T.G.I.F- well, tomorrow anyway.
