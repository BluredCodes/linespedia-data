title: "Life's Race"

Wind whips my face as I shove along.
Struggling to climb the steps that lead to my happiness.
My eyes gaze heavenward and I see nothing but blackness.
Praying for daybreak...
Surrounded by storm clouds.
My body, heavy with lead, slowly falls.
Eternal sleep is my fate.
My struggle, my destiny, my life, my death...
The pain, the strife, the blood, the sweat...
It's all a part of me as I run my race to uncertain finish line.
