---
title: "A Sad Goodbye"
writer: "midniteblu"

Now I see that love is blind,
Every day and night you're on my mind.
I wanted us to have something strong and true.
The last thing I ever wanted to do was hurt you.
Only that's just what I did; but I'm hurting too.
It broke my heart once I lost you.
I know I have to move on and I'll try.
But everytime I think of you I want to cry.
What is this spell you've put over me?
Leaving me dreaming of what could possibly be:
You and me laying in the warm sand
Looking into each others eyes; hand-in-hand.
But that'll never happen, it's sad but true.
I gave it all up, the day I said good-bye to you.
