The day I touched the sky.
I remember.
You were there.
You picked me up and lifted me high.
I stepped on you.
I yelled at you when you almost dropped me.
You just smiled and laughed and never let go.
The day I touched the sky I never thanked you.
So thank you.
For never letting go,
For never giving in,
For always knowing that soon enough I would...
Touch the sky.To my mom: I love you.
