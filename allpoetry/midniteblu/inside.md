Always happy, carefree, and light.
Not what you'd expect on a cold dark night.
No one knew your pain; you wouldn't let us in.
But it's okay to vent; I swear it's not a sin.

Constantly giving so much of yourself.
Putting first (always) everyone else.
Your silent torture twisted with pain.
Even the brightest of days, in your head were rain.

If I could go back in time these two words I'd say to you...
No I'd better make it 5 so I could include an "I Love You" too.
Remember, I always cared about you.
I would have told you if you asked me to.

Anyway my final words to you my friend
Are "Don't Jump". Let this be where the story will end.
