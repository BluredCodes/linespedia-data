Dusk to dawn
I rise,
Yesterday's gone.
Sun shines full of promise.
Today's not so bad,
Time passes and I'm not so sad.

It was yo who set me free.
Gave me the freedom to cry.
No need to hide with you by my side.
It was you.

Darkness to light
I stopped the struggle; ended the fight.
You've won my heart; you cradle my soul.
Eternity in focus with you is my goal.

It was you who set me free.
Gave me the freedom to cry.
No need to hide with you by my side.
