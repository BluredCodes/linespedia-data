Today I cried.
I cried for the love lost and the blood shed.
I cried because I had no one to hold me.
I cried because I couldn't stop your pain.
I cried because I didn't know what else to do.

Today I cried.
I cried for the newborns and the old aged.
I cried because I'm not innocent anymore.
I cried because I don't know how much time I have left.
I cried because I don't know what else to do.

Today I cried.
I cried for people who will never see tomorrow.
I cried because sometimes life isn't worth living.
I cried because the bad outweighs the good.
I cried because my emotions are a jiggsaw puzzle missing pieces.

Today I cried.
I cried because I don't know what else to do.For anyone who has ever lost someone. Te quiero Flora. I miss you.
