title: "Silent Battle"

I've been lost and found.
My soul shaken like a tiny snowglobe.
Bewildered and alone.
And then rescued.
A ray of light stretched across my face.
There was warmth,
There was love,
There was you.
Suddenly I got shoved back into darkness.
My cucoon of safety destroyed.
My blood mixed with my sweat and tears.
I struggled to fight but couldn't see my opponent.
Finally, time stopped and everything stood still.
I had been fighting myself and now it's time to quit.
