---
title: "I Could"
writer: "midniteblu"

I could stare into your eyes for all of eternity.
I think that's what I'll do.
I could hold you tight and never let go.
I think that's what I'll do.
I could give you my hand and let you lead me.
I think that's what I'll do.

It'll just take a little time for me to give into you.
To let myself love so deeply and not look back.
So wait for me and don't lose sight of me.
Because....

I could fall in love with you.
I think, eventually, that's what I'll do.
