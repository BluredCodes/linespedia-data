title: "Never First"
writer: "midniteblu"

Nobody likes being second best.
But I hate it.
Competitive? Maybe.
I'm just tired of never being pretty enough.
I'm tired of not being smart enough.
So many demands are placed on me.
Most of them I heap on myself.
Still, the pain is real when I don't measure up.
When there's always that one person who shines brighter than I do.
I'm not a spiteful person but I hate them so much right then.
I hate them because they're better.
And I hate them because they don't feel the disgust I have for myself.
But the truth is... those people may emphasize my feelings...
But the feelings are already there!
