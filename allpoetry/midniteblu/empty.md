---
title: "Empty"
writer: "midniteblu"
slug: "empty"
source: "AllPoetry"

Gabbing at air.
Falling into the darkness.
Letting it take over everything.
Tears streaming, legs kicking.
I'm calling out... can you hear me?
Do you even care?
I'm fighting for me, I'm fighting for you.
I've been fighting for so long and I'm losing.
Losing strength, losing energy, losing me.
I've given you everything.
Can't you take over for a little while?
You've deserted me.
You hate me.
I hate you too.I feel like I'm missing something and I'm open for suggestions on a better title.
