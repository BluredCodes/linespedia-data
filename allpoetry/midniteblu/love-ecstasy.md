Slipping into silence on a soulful Saturday.
Sun beams spread out across your face 
making your eyes glow an intense shade of mahogany.
Our fingers get entangled delicately together.
Lips strecth into teasing grins.
You pull me close to you and touch me lightly.
Watching me watch you out of the corner of my eye.
An energy flows between us and
I know what you want and all you need from one look.
Passion errupts around us like daffodil's growing wildly in the summer.
As comfortable as a warm sweater on a cold day.
You belong to me and I to you.
It's as sure as the sunrising tomorrow.To: Mi papito; te amo pa siempre....
