---
title: "Love Defined"
writer: "midniteblu"

I'll fight for you and never lose.
I'll laugh with you and never run out of jokes.
I'll cry with you and have enough tissues for both of us.
I'll walk with you and carry you when you're tired.
I'll look you in the eye and never lose focus.
I'll be your strength when you can't make it.
I'll be your blanket on those cold winter nights.
I'll love you when no one else gives a damn.
That's love: not 70/30 or 40/60, but 50/50.
You're my one in a million. You're all I need.
