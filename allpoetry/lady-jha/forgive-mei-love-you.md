title: "'Forgive Me...I love You'"

The sea, like your loveCan be tranquil and still.Your looks, speaks volumeAnd you never utter a sound.Like a river of my tearsCascading down the emerald mountainYour sweet embrace comforts meI know where my safety lies, in your strong arms.But the sea can turn the course in our life.Thunderous emotions, falling from the sky.The hurricanes of shouts that are hurled at me.Strikes me deep inside, leaving little for me to say.A vortex of hate appears, dragging me down deep.Clouds of are no longer visible, my body goes limp. All at once, I’m lifted, being removed from the abyssThe abyss that almost consumes my heart and soul.Tightly you hold me. Sweet gently embrace. A long passionate kiss. Our bodies almost become one.Into my eyes you look, not with anger, not with hate.But with the calm and tranquility I’ve become accustom too.     And the sounds of your voice                 resonates the words I so long to hear;                      ………Forgive me, I love you.This is just my views on relationships. They remind me of thunderstorms. Some can be calm, others can be severe but all can hurt and leave scars.
