title: "'Is this a Dream?'"

A field of light surrounded me.
Filling my space, what can this be?
So warm, so inviting. So familiar.

Voices drifting away in the background. 
No longer, do they hold me here.
A choir of angels, decend down to greet me.

Soft clouds fill the space, as I rise higher.
Love ones long gone, embrace me with welcome.
Never have I felt such peace.

Is this a dream?**Ever wonder what happens after you're dead?**
