There'll come a time in a mans life when he'll look for a wife
when he'll leave his family and go off on his own
to find the woman with whom he'll be alone

He'll leave his house where he once resided and by rules abided
to make his own home and start his family for whom he's provided
and he'll find his woman and fall in love, heaven-guided

One day he'll find that his feelings have grown and are being shown
to prove that he loves this woman like he has no other before
and he'll continue to love her to eternity and evermore

He'll confess his love to her and she'll say it back if genuine
on one knee he'll bend down and tell her that to continue in
the life of love that they share he wants marriage and then

With love and with devotion and a heart full of comotion
they'll embrace eachother in their arms and hold so tight
she'll say "yes I will marry you" and he'll feel so right

And so begins the life that continues, that forever goes on
a life which all of us must seek, to steer away from wrong
