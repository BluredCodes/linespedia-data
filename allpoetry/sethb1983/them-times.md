All them times we converse about the stupidest shit
but it never got old
them secrets that I made up, never give you a fit
also stayed between us, and never got told

You know them naughty lil things we used to say to do
but swore we were so good
we kept talking about them though, but it was only me 'n' you
saying NO ONE else should

It seemed like we were far from eachother, but we were still close
when we occasionally caught a smile
I believe it was for the better of us both
since we made it worth our while

A love came out of something we didn't even expect
but who's to complain
now were planing things that only the future can perfect
but still... together we remain

Them times seemed to come to an end, and what was I now
without having you there
I can't make love, but sex isn't and wasn't how
them feelings came about to share

When I convinced to come back, I wasn't going to blow something that was real
but seems I did anyway
I tried to take the time out for you, to understand how you feel
but still, we seemed to drift away

Yet we gave it another chance at loving making it right, 3rd time's a charm
so it'll work, maybe with help of rhymes
I'll stay loving you the rest of my life, to that there's no harm
but we'll always have "them times"I wrote this poem back in the day, and now I find myself coming back to it more than 4 months since I've read it, and I never get tired of it, a poem for times to come and times before, now that there's a new love in my life, it's great...
