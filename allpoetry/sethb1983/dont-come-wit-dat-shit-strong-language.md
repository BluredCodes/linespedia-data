You can't fuck wit what you don't understand
so don't try an act like you's the man

cus if you come round my hood wit that slick shit
you bound to get hit wit -that steel 
get rich boys that don't use toys
but pack up the heat -that slay you
n sell ya body as meat, I'm trynna eat
by sellin crack to minors from the police station to dinners

bout to make it in the big leagues
selling the fig leaves
and smokin the green shit to myself
packin up the lean shit on tha shelf
usin tha dead niggaz bodies as raw product for my profit
dont get mad when ya find out ya man's dead -instead -get off it 

I told you talkin and walkin that slick shit
like you's someone special -is guranteeing a hit
and nigga I'm sending yo boys in front of a pastor
hittin em wit my nine just makes it a lil faster
so godbless you, cus you gon need jesus to forgive the sins you did
cus when me an my niggaz is done-all of yall is doin god's bida slick lil rap I made a few days ago
