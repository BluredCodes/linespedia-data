There you go, you pass me by with that smile on you face-
I wonder what it means, but I put aside for the moment...
Then wherever I go, I seem to see you in the same place-
I wonder again, but I think nothing of it...

I walk home and see you across the street as you wave hi-
Thinking of what to do I just keep on my lil mission...
I don't wanna talk, but I don't have the heart to say bye-
Seems like the person you are has me in submission...

The next day I have little expectation on seeing you again-
As I go for a bite to eat, I find you in the same store...
I try to go unnoticed by you, I'm seen and you speak but then-
I gotta turn and talk now, or else you'll bug me for sure...

As you speak I don't listen to a word, to me it's like a rhyme-
I'm concerned with how I'm going to leave the situation...
So I just agree on what you say and tell you, "Another time"-
Now when I see you I'm even more of an object of fixation!the lesson learned is to talk to the woman, let her know you aint interested, if not this is what happens!
