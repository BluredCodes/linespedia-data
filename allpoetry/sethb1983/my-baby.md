You's my baby, that woman I fell in love with a long time ago
that woman that'll just turn the playa from fast to slow
that girl who's ass moves with that special type flow
how it jiggle and how you wiggle them hips down low

You's the woman that I had to be wit when I first saw you walkin by
had to put the game on pause, and say more than just a little "hi"
you the girl I threw shit to and kept tellin you how you was fly
that you should drop them other boys cus they's just another guy

You was the girl I had to be wit no matter what was the cost
and cus of you it was them playin skills that I had lost
dropped all other hollas and put you as boss
even them girls shakin in they butt floss

But you proved worth it, with a love that's real
and showed me nothing compares to the way we feel
and I kept it goin back at you fallin in love and making a seal
constantly filling you in letting you know the deal

You kept poppin up in my mind 24 hours in everyday of the week
Was making me anxious for the next we got to speak
and thinking of you just made my knees real weak
but you're my baby and I love the treat!
