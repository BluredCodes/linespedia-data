I think I'm far enough away now... so I slow down and peek behind-
Out of site and in the clear, there's no woman on my tail...
I spoke too soon, there's her friends, now I'm an easy find-
I'll make up a story to explain why I ran, will I fail?...

"I got chased, I was talking to her and I saw a dog so I broke!"-
Thinking in my mind what to say next so I don't seem like a dick...
"Look I wrestled with it too, my pants are ripped, I almost got chocked!"-
They bought the story, now I'm off the hook for runnin, what a trick!...

But of course my luck always turns and she asks such a weird question-
"How come everytime I talk to you, we get cut off so quickly?"...
What do I do now? I'm stuck, I shoulda thought of an earlier prevention-
"I don't know why that happens, I guess it's my luck?!", that was tricky...

Only a minute after that the assault began again, with more questioning-
"Hey, wait a minute" one question then two, and one right after another...
I answer them, but hardly... doing my best but really I'm guessing-
Then I figure it out, "Wait a minute" you go out with my Twin Brother!

Case solved... no more stalking involved- what other can it be then "mistaken Identity?"I love having a twin bro
