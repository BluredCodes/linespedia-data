After that day I didn't know what to do really, so I went to sleep-
I donno how long I was out for but I imagined it to be a while...
When I left home I walked around, made sure I was on the 'creep'-
It was the next day all of a sudden, and I've yet to see that smile...

It's been a couple days now and I haven't been hawked down-
And I'm feeling quite nice in the good weather as usual...
What spoils it is I turn and I'm being so STALKED NOW-
For me this is out and weird, I'm thinking, "tottaly uncasual"...

I find a nice spot to hide out for a bit to let you pass me by-
I see you walk on, "I can't talk to her" I thought in my head...
All of a sudden out of nowhere you catch me in the blink of an eye-
You say, "Heyyyy Setthhhhh!" Now I wish I'd stayed in bed!...

You Blab your head off to me about a whole bunch of nonsense-
And, as my usual I pay you less then, well, any attention...
I wait a couple minutes and a thought comes, I break for a fence-
I slipped by this time, but there is no permanent prevention!Stay tuned for more!
