This is for my fam-



yall loved me when a nigga was down

the one's who was there when no one else was around

the REAL peoples, who know how I feel peoples

the understood niggaz, straight from my hood niggaz



and to all yall who doubted me- 



FUCK YOU, yall just mad cus only I remained true

I was stuck down and broke

didn't give a fuck how I spoke

lost myself in a daze where I was confused

had niggaz laughing at me like they was amused



but they in awe now, thinkin aww how-



did this lil poor nigga come to be?

wit true rhymes that describe me

And this wicked verse lettin yall know who bout to be

the most true, realest nigga on this rap scene!This is a true lil rap describing (somewhat) life...

for all yall in the group (including my brother) lemme know wasup, gimme your feedback



-Seth
