title: "To you, from me"
writer: "SethB1983"

Every nite when I close my eyes, I wish that you could see
what feelings you put in my heart
the desire that you inspire and everything inside of me

When I dream, I dream of the day I join with you forever
the day I hold you with joy
and we begin a course with just us two together

As I think, it's of only us, close to one another
holding hands, or kissing and hugging
staying true, staying near, and always loving eachother

A picture in my mind so perfect, a day with just you and me
whatever we do, doing it together
and never, drifting away, or going astray, soon that's true to be

I live for the day I'll be with you, to look into your heart
by looking into your eyes
and to love you, like no one else ever loved you, and never to partI wrote this to my baby, I changed a lil bit for her, but this is what I wrote... hope you enjoy, I love you Jennifer
