---
title: "I miss you"
writer: "SethB1983"
slug: "i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

When morning comes and the sun's arising from east
thoughts of the day start as I awaken 
that's when I realize I'm alone, I search for some peace
the dreams I have you're there, I'm mistaken

I go through the blankets that warm me all night
trying to find something to prove you're around
it's all wrong, but I'm thinking it's all right
I just close my eyes at the thought of this let down

Just last night I heard your voice in my ear
whisperring that you loved me
now it's the sound of birds in the morning I hear
no more you smiling above me

It's easy to say, I miss you, but hard to mean
I donno what to do without you here
I need you around, you need to be seen
I can't deal with reality without my dere
