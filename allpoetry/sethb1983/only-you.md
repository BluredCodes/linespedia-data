There's billions of women on this planet, but only you are myne
I won't claim to any other
You're the woman that's perfect, you're beyond being fine
and have more love than a brother

There's different women, with different attitudes and features
Only you fit my profile
I love you and respect you beyond all humans and creatures
and for you I'd walk the extra mile

There's more than just you saying I love you, there's emotion
You know my feeling is mutual
You gave your whole life to me, I'm doin the same with pure devotion
by giving my whole life with you

I gave up so much, but yet so little to love the woman that you are
Realizing life is nothing without love
I was so afraid of what I'd become, what I'd miss-nothing so far
it was only trouble I've got out of

What more could a man need then a feeling of belonging or of worth
Numerous times I've wondered if that's true
I kept asking myself and wondering, and it finaly gave birth
who answered my curiousity, only youthink of your loved one when you read this poem, tell me if it's what you find in them, tell me it's what you see, cus that's what I see when I look...
