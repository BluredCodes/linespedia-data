I Wonder Why
It seems that youth has hastened by-
I wonder why?
How often maidens mourn and cry-
I wonder why?
No longer, Mothers, babies love-
Nor wife and husband mate like doves
No marriage stands the push and shove
I wonder why?
Now people panic over whales-
I wonder why?
Preserve the wildlife, fish and snails-
I wonder why?
While beggars in the gutters lie,
And infants by the millions die-
It almost makes me want to cry-
I wonder why?
Nobility it seems has flown-
I wonder why?
And honesty is also gone-
I wonder why?
If some of us don't take a stand
We're going to lose this once great land-
And groan beneath the con'quer's hand-
I wonder why?
I see the end approaching fast-
And wonder why?
Our brightest hour now is past-
I wonder why?
Like Henry pledged his very breath-
And longed for liberty or death-
Of patriots, so few are left-
I wonder why?
