Speechless in amazement...
I watched you walk away.
When you told me it was over,
On that cold, December day-

We both had said "forever",
Along with our "I do's"...
Now, after nearly thirty years-
You tell me that we're through.

The glamour faded long ago,
The intrigue followed soon,
Somehow you finally figured out-
I did not "hang the moon".

I wonder where to go from here-
And wonder where you've gone...
I'm trying to remember the...
Last Christmas spent alone.
