To write, or remain silent?
I ponder more and more...
And wonder what new pain, or grief
My writing holds in store.

For writing makes the feelings real-
It has more life than art,
For words indeed, are able to-
Seduce a person's heart.

A coward? Aye, I'm that and more.
I readily admit...
And wish I had the fortitude,
To simply up and quit.

To write? Or simply fade away-
I only wish I knew...
The wisest scribes who ever lived-
Have written things they rue.
