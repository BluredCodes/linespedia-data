---
title: "Good-byes"
writer: "Gunslinger"
slug: "good-byes"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If you could see me crying-
There'd be no denying...
The truth about the way I care for you.
If you could hear my sighing,
You'd know I was not lying-
That every single "I love you" was true.

While words may flow like rivers-
There's one which gives me shivers...
A word that seems to always make me cry.
It's one that breaks all fetters-
Containing seven letters...
I guess you've figured out, I mean "good-bye"?

The fault is mine, I reckon-
Like sirens, love does beckon...
And promises so much more than it holds.
And leaves you lost and lonely...
With your one and only-
When the tragic side of circumstance unfolds.

I'm vowing now forever...
I'll keep my heart and never...
Allow myself to dream another lie...
And never hurt another-
The way I hurt one other...
With the tragic little lie, I told- "good bye".

What's good about one leaving-
When they leave another grieving?
With tear stained cheeks, and red and swollen eyes...
Pillows wet from crying-
I guess there's no denying...
There is no such a thing as "good" good-byes.
