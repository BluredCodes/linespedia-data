title: "Never Write Again II"
writer: "Gunslinger"
slug: "never-write-again-ii"

I've really been quite foolish,
With my words to touch a heart.
Inflicting pain and longing,
And to do so isn't smart.

A gift of words should never harm-
Yet mine have brought much pain.
And in consideration....
I must never write again.

A product of the sage and sand-
I blew in on a breeze...
And like the restless tumble weeds
I cannot rest at ease.

How many people have I hurt-
From reading my refrain?
I think it best for all concerned,
To never write again.

My heart is filled with wanderlust-
My mind does dote on dreams...
I write of never, never land,
Where nothing's as it seems.

I'm burying the dreamer-
Out upon the cyber-plains...
To stop his inspiration...
So he'll never write again.
