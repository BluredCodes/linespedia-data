---
title: "Now That You Are Gone"
writer: "Gunslinger"
slug: "now-that-you-are-gone"

Your memories play my heartstrings-
Like the angels play their harps...
A muted compilation...
Of mingled flats and sharps...

So sweet the hours that we had...
So sad, the parting day...
To change our dreams of sunshine,
In a cloudy, dreary way.

“I miss you” never will express-
The longing in my heart.
Nor the way the hours drag like years-
Since we have been apart.

“I love you” sounds so shallow-
In view of how I groan...
But that is all that I can say...
Now that you are gone.
