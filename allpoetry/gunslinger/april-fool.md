April Fool

Headlights cutting gaping swaths-
Through the night ahead.
Thinking of the next go-round...
Excitement mixed with dread.

Dreams of going all the way-
Plans to take the Gold!
Somewhere deep within my mind-
I hear, "You're getting old"!

Angrily, I shake my head-
There's no one there to see...
Or interrupt the argument,
That goes on...me with me...

Common sense just has no chance-
Fantasy will win...
I'll pay my dues, and draw my bulls,
And start it all again.

Thirty-five last April first-
I rode in Abilene...
An April Fool is what I am-
And doomed to chase my dream.

Each year is paying less and less-
Yet, still, my passions rule.
With forlorn hopes to be the Best,
I'm just an April fool.
