Our Love Goes On

They tell me man is mortal,
And too swiftly years do fly.
It seems he hardly enters life,
Ere comes his time to die.

But I cannot believe it,
And even if it's true-
When these old bones, are dead and gone...
I'll go on loving you.

My voice will be upon the wind-
My form among the trees-
You'll feel my kiss and my carress,
On every gentle breeze.

They tell me that my time is near-
But I know that they're wrong...
For though they lay this flesh to rest,
Our love goes on and on.
