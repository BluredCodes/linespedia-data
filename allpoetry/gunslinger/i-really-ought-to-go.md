title: "I Really ought To Go"
writer: "Gunslinger"

Some men aren't meant for marriage,
And I suppose that's me.
It's sad that twenty years ago, 
This truth I could not see.

Twenty-eight and counting...
And what have I to show?
But bitterness, hurt feelings-
I guess I ought to go.

But I can't help but wonder,
Where do you say, "That's it!"
When someone seems a part of you-
How can you up and quit?

My pride keeps railing at me-
"You don't deserve this fate!"
My conscience seems to whisper-
"You taught her how to hate."

I'm torn with indecision...
The path I do not know.
One part of me keeps saying, "Stay!"
But I really ought to go.
