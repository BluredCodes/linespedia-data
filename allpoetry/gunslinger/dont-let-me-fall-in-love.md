Please don't let me fall in love.
Don't let me hurt again.
I am a fool to Cupid's tools-
His arrows bring such pain.

You say my words effect your heart-
But you could never know...
Each gentle word you write to me-
Does strike my heart a blow.

A fool, you say; and so I am-
But still my soul can feel...
My melancholy words of love,
Though trite, are very real.

So write, my darling, I will read-
But I shall not respond...
To do so would give birth to tears...
And make a tighter bond.

Don't break the shell that I have formed-
Protective barricade...
Defense, so fragile, by love stormed-
Too often, low is laid.
