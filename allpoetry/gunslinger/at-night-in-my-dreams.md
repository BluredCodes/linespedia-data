"At Night In My Dreams"

I hung up my riggin', I laid it all down.
I've spent my last entries on fruitless go 'rounds.
I'm nearly fifty, and too old it seems-
But I'm still a champion at night in my dreams.

At night in my dreams, I'm still ridin' them high!
My spurs on my boots, hooking holes in the sky.
I thrill to the lights, and the dirt, and the screams...
And I'm still a champion at night in my dreams.

For twenty-two years I went "down the road"-
I rode some good horses, sometimes I got throwed...
My wife finally gave up on all of my schemes,
But I'm still the champion, at night in my dreams.

At night in my dreams, I am rolling in dough-
A champ on a roll in the Pro Rodeo...
On top in the standings with all of the cream-
Yes, I'm still a champion, at night in my dreams.
