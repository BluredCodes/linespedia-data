Driving far too fast around an oval,
Chasing time...
We call it entertainment...
But I think we've lost our mind.

A man who weighs one forty-
With his spurs strapped to his boots-
Stands above a ton of bull...
There at the bucking chutes.

A climber, like a spider-
Hangs upon a sheer rock wall...
A thousand feet below him-
Rocks are waiting for his fall.

Divorce is running rampant-
Marriage victimized by pride!
Why can't we realize that we're-
Committing suicide?

Life is brief, but precious...
Happiness a state of mind.
Ability to face that truth...
Is mighty hard to find.
