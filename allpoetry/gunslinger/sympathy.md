Sympathy

The rain is softly falling-
Just outside the window pane-
I think of how when you were here,
We liked to watch the rain.

I think of how your happy smile-
Brought light to every room,
Without your presence, there remains-
A house that’s filled with gloom.

I think of how your laughter-
Made me also want to smile,
And every day at sunset-
We’d go out and walk our mile…

I think of all the plans we made-
And often wonder , “Why?”
The sky looks on in sympathy,
And joins me as I cry.
