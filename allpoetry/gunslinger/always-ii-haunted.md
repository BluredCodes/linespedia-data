Can you feel my presence-
Standing by your side?
I am with you always-
No need to try and hide.

No place that you can flee me-
No chance I'll go away.
I'll haunt you in your dreams at night-
Your thoughts throughout the day.

I'll whisper in the darkness-
And tell you how I feel.
You'll know it is the truth, dear-
The feelings all too real...

The breeze will be my soft caress
Upon your fevered brow...
Each shadow is my silhouette-
You'll see my courtly bow...

The falling rain, my teardrops-
You sensed it when I cried...
Be sure I'll haunt you always-
I'm always at your side.
