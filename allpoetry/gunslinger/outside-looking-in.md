Today I stopped to think awhile-
Evaluate my life.
Relationships I have at home,
With children and my wife...

Love? I wonder what that is?
Why do we all pretend?
I am the loneliest of all...
I'm outside, looking in.

I used to be a drifter-
Footloose and fancy free...
The siren call of wanderlust-
Once placed her hold on me...

I am alone, with company-
Without a single friend,
My home's the place I hang my hat-
I'm outside looking in...

I miss the lonely desert-
The Arizona sky...
I miss the Rocky Mountains...
Their grandeur wild and high...

I hear the wild geese calling...
Their brother to the wind...
I long to spread my wings and fly-
I'm outside, looking in.
