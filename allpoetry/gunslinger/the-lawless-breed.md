title: "The Lawless Breed"
writer: "Gunslinger"
slug: "the-lawless-breed"

I made the bed I lie in-
There's no one else to blame.
I chose the path I walked upon,
To play the footloose game.

My parents tried to raise me right,
Some said I was bad seed.
A creature of the dark and night,
One of the lawless breed...

I grew up fast and left my home-
To live out on the road...
The place I hung my old black hat,
Was where I called my home.

A life like that brings only pain
And causes hearts to bleed-
And dooms a man to live alone-
One of the lawless breed.

A gun becomes a part of you-
And caution is your code...
Tell no one about yourself,
You meet upon the road...

But finally you settle down-
Beneath four feet of clay...
The bedroom of the lawless breed,
And where I lie today.

No "Rest In Peace", nor "He'll Be Missed"-
Are carved upon my stone-
Just a name, two dates, one dash...
I rest here all alone.
