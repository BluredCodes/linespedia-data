---
title: "I Still Love You"
writer: "Gunslinger"

I'm sitting here, just thinking-
Wishing I could turn back time.
Maybe twenty years or so,
When I was in my prime.

You were rather young then-
But that would be okay,
I think I'd try to win you-
And then take you away.

Way up in the mountains-
Belwo the timber line...
And build a cozy cabin,
'Twould be just yours and mine.

There you could learn to love me-
I really think you could.
And as you knew me better,
I'm certain that you would.

Children, if you wanted-
I'd teach them how to live...
As one with nature's wildness-
She has so much to give.

We'd spend our lives together-
Till parted by my death...
I'd whisper, "I still love you."
With my final, fading breath.
