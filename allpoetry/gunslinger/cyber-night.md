I took the time to contemplate-
And finally realized...
That showing my emotions,
Is not exactly wise.

There's no hope for the future-
Nor solace from the past...
And not a thing that might occur,
With any hope to last.

Perhaps we are too much alike...
And we would always fight.
Or maybe I would hurt you;
I know that is not right.

So when I finish writing-
I'll vanish out of sight...
I'll ride my ghostly pony-
Out into the cyber-night.
