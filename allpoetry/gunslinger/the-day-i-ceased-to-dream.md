title: "The Day I Ceased to Dream"

I used to dream of romance-
The perfect match, you know?
The Prince meets Cinderella,
Ah, but that was long ago.

I dreamed of fame and fortune-
A home, a happy life...
Some healthy, happy children,
A faithful, loving wife-

I dreamed of grand adventure,
A hero I would be-
Alas, I finally realize...
I'm stuck with being me.

I had my share of fantasies-
They disappeared like steam,
I knew my life was over,
The day I ceased to dream.
