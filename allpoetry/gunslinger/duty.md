Torn by my emotions-
I want to run away...
My heart tells me to flee the scene
But duty bids me stay.

What a master, Duty-
No matter how we feel...
The harsh whip of our concsience...
Still brings us all to heel.

The hasty, rash decisions-
Bind us all through life...
Like vows to be a husband,
Or vows to be a wife.

The world lies in confusion...
More often strife than beauty...
How much worse off, our world would be
If not for sense of duty.
