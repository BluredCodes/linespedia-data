The old man told me, "Listen, kid-
The desert is alive.
You'll have to learn to hear her voice-
If you hope to survive.

You must learn what to look for-
Not only look, but see!
Watch for Gambrel's quail, son
And always watch the bee.

They're never far from water-
And that's the staff of life...
Always carry salt, son...
And you really need a knife.

A gun is good, but heavy...
It's best to travel light,
And never face the blazing sun-
But only walk at night.

There's water in the wastelands-
If you know where to look...
You have to learn her language,
And read her like a book...
