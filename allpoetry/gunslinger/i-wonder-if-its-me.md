---
title: "I Wonder If It's Me?"
writer: "Gunslinger"

I was looking at your pictures-
The house, the yard, the trees-
The flowerbeds were blooming
Blossoms swaying in the breeze...

The children smiled so sweetly-
Their joy was plain to see...
But I sense there's something missing,
And I wonder if it's me?

I can smell the coffee brewing,
And bacon in the pan...
I can see you brush a wayward lock
Aside with your left hand...

Your neck looks so enticing-
Just begging for a kiss...
The river bank for walking...
I believe would be pure bliss.

Again I look them over...
There's an emptiness I see-
An element is missing...
And I wonder if it's me?
