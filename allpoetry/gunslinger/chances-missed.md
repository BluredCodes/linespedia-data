"Chances Missed..."

If I would let you love me,
I really think you would.
I wish that I could drop my guard-
I'd like to if I could.

But the wall which stands between us,
Took years for me to build...
It seems the tender side of me-
The process slowly killed.

So I am on the outside, now-
Forlornly looking in.
Wishing I could turn back time-
And start my life again.

The clock of life is wound but once-
And whether right, or wrong-
Decisions are immutable-
And chances missed...are gone!
