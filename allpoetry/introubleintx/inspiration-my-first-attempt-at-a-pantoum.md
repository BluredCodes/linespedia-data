You are the inspiration
behind the words I write
if only there was a way
together to spend the night.

Behind the words I write
there is a fantasy
together to spend the night
harshest of realities.

There is a fantasy
awaiting us someday
harshest of realities
for us to finally play.

Awaiting us someday
a night of longing passion
for us to finally play
in a uninhibited fashion.

A night of longing passion
is what I want from you
in an uninhibited fashion
what do you think we'd do?

Is what I want from you 
if only there was a way 
what do you think we'd do 
you are the inspiration.This is my first attempt at a pantoum. Someone tell me what you think?? Good or bad.. :)
