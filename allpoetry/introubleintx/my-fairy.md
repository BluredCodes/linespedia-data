I wanted to put into words
about my new tattoo
But I can't find the chosen
one's, to explain to you.

So I'll add this little rhyme
and it's picture too
so I can show my new ink
to those that know me true.

I found her picture on the net
And thought it would be cool
to have her perched upon my back
but maybe I'm a fool...

But she is there, forever more
and I know she will be
someone to watch out for those
that sneak up on me...Just something...LOL...I did it!!! YAY!!!!
