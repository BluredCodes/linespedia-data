title: "Eternal Romance  (adult language)"

You stupid bitch
someday you'll learn,
you play with what's mine
and I'll see you burn!

He'll never leave me
for the likes of you,
get over it bitch
he wants nothing from you.

I'm the one that haunts him
when he sleeps at night.
I'm the one he'll always love
and he'll forever hold me tight.

So go ahead and hope,
that you still have a chance.
While I stand aside and pity you
and stay his eternal romance.
