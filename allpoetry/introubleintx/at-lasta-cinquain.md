hidden,
locked away now
caged, self-inflicted on
yourself, to hide depressions hold
on you.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Sleeping 
to keep from pains
embrace as darkness calls
wanting you to let go forever
falling
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Into
the dark that waits
no pain, no suffering
only slumber, no fear, freedom
at last!Three together to paint the entire picture! :)
