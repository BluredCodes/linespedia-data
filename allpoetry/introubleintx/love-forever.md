Love forever
eternal bliss
seeking lasting
happiness.

Free from pain
away from fear
someone close
to hold you near.

Sharing secrets
words unsaid
cept between us
emotions read.

Feelings run
true and deep
to each other
we shall keep.

In the end
sad but true
love forever
remains for you.
