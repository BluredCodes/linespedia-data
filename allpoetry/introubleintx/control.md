I want to sleep
it's silent there
there are no worries,
there are no cares.

The darkness hides
the scenery
and the fact
you're not with me.

I want to sleep,
depression calls
so easy it is
to once again fall.

The darkness holds
the peace I seek
keeps me from feeling
helpless and meek.

I want to sleep,
but I'll not go,
I'll stand and fight,
my life, control...
