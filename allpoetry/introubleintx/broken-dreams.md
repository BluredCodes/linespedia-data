You walk in,
I glance your way
my heart starts pounding,
you look away.

Why do you play,
this little game?
Knowing that
there will be pain?

Someone hurt
in the end.
It started out
as casual friends.

But we both knew
we could be more
don't know about you,
I've been there before.

A harmless look
a gentle touch
can be mistaken
for oh, so much.

And then you wander
through broken dreams
knowing it was 
a painful scheme.
