have you looked back on your life
to gaze on the path you followed
only to realize what you've done
has ended up leaving you hollow?
has your life seemed the same
each day just like the last.
always waiting for something more
better than you've had in the past.
wanting to see what tomorrow will bring
hoping for something more
than what you've had all along
each day holding nothing in store.
living your life with no promise
of what the future might bring
no happily ever after
cause it wouldn't mean a thing.
When you spend your life
repeating the same day
you never will get anywhere
no matter come what may...
I know this to be true,
at least it is for me
because more than once now
I have stopped to look back and see..
That I have gotten nowhere
no matter what the day, or year
I have followed the same path
and each day has ended here....
