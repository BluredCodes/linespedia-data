Someday, when you look at me
with your eyes of blue
You'll see unconditional love
only meant for you.

Someday, when you listen
you'll hear it in my voice,
and you'll know to love you
there was never a choice.

Someday, when you speak,
I only hope you'll say
that all along you loved me back
in a very special way.

Someday, soon, little one
you'll know these words true,
that you are special too us all
no matter what you do.
