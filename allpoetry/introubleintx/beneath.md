What lies beneath
the mask you wear?
A broken heart
yet not a care.

Empty tears streaming
down a battered face
on a head that was once
held with dignity and grace.

What once was your spirit
now tattered and torn,
returns with a hardness
full of contempt and scorn.

All that was once
so pretty and fair
now lies behind scars
that once were not there.

Maybe someday in the future
after the passage of time
inner beauty will resurface
not just in your mind!
