Does it piss you off
to know I've won
that for him
I am the only one?

Does it tear you up
when you drive by
to know he was filling 
your head full of lies?

Do you feel used,
not to mention betrayed?
You thought you were more
than someone to whom he strayed.

You thought there was 
a future to be had
someone to love
for your kids a dad.

Instead all you got
was dreams that are broken
cause to him I meant more
than any words you have spoken.

And I'll always be the one
whom he loves and adores
and you're just a memory
like the other whores!Just something I popped off after seeing this woman drive by my house that thought she had a life with my hubby. :)
