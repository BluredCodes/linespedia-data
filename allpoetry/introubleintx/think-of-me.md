title: "Think of Me"

Think of me
when we are apart,
as sometimes we will be.

Think of me
when you sleep,
have vivid dreams, you see.

Think of me
when you're alone
and want some company.

Think of me
while you play
running wild and free.

Think of me
as I'll think of you,
for all eternity!Just something came to mind while at work...
