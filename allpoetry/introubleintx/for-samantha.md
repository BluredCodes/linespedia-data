On a day in September
eighteen years ago
I gave birth to a daughter
and I loved her so.
With bright blue eyes
and light blonde hair
that she was healthy
was my only care.
You were into everything
and try as I might
I couldn't stop you
without a fight.
You were good in school
and helped me at home
when without your dad
I could leave you alone.
You got into boys
soon I found out.
Then dating was in,
school a drop out.
You moved from the house
at just seventeen.
And then you met him
the man of your dreams.
Now you are pregnant
a baby on the way
there is no more time
for childish games to play.
You'll see on your own
and try as you might
to teach your child
to know wrong from right.
And you'll slowly learn
from this day and on
that life isn't so easy
once you become Mom...I am thinking of giving this to my daughter for a Christmas gift..Tell me what you think...
