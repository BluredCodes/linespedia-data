As it starts
from deep within
slow and languid
I do begin

my body trembles
a small earthquake
as my insides
begin to shake.

clenching and tight
enveloping you
as you thrust
can you feel it to?

impending explosion
the juices they flow
my body reacting
crying out to let go.

not wanting to stop
convulsions do cease
at last it is over
my body spent in peace.Something in answer to a personal challenge so to speak...Writing about an orgasm... :)
