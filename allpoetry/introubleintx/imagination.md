Imprisoned by lust
obscured by desire,
thoughts of your body
set me on fire.

Flames burning
passions igniting
tumultuous yearnings
I can't keep fighting.

The lure of conquest
the final seduction
How do I conquer
sensuous compulsion?

To deplete the longing
give in to allure
fulfilling the fantasies
is the only cure.

And yet as I stumble
upon this realization
I wonder if it's just
my wild imagination.
