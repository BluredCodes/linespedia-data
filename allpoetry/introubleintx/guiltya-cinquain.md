silent
like the thief in
the night, stalking his prey
lights out, no one home, a window
open
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
breaking 
glass everywhere
not a sound, no one cares
neighbors sleeping soundly, all night
he's in
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
you have
been invaded, now
violated, cheated, taken
advantage of, by someone, kept
hidden
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
slowly
recovering,
faith in another, and
yet, still unsure, the outcome waits
guilty!Okay...A set of four this time...Maybe it makes sense... :)
