title: "Forever With You"

I want you with me,
forever and a day.
To only be with you,
come what may.

To feel your body
next to mine,
to know you are there
for all of time.

To hear your heartbeat,
my head on your chest,
is how I want to sleep,
this night and the rest.

All I've ever wanted
was forever with you.
Any less time,
just will not do!
