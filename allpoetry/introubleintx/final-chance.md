Your words,
do they touch her,
the way they touch me?
Does your actions
speak as your words?
Or are the words,
eternal lies, told only to me?

Used, to hurt me
string me along,
while loving her,
missing her, and wanting her
using me, again
until you are with her
pretending you are free.

Is she part
of your plans
for the future?
To have her, on the side
and keep me here, hurting
letting you use, all that I am
for all the world to see?

Or am I the one,
that you'll finally
take notice of,
love more than any other,
share the future, as we have
shared the past,
and savor all of eternity?

The time
has come, at last
to let her go,
or let me go, to live 
my life, as you would 
live your's with her, Given,
a final chance to be happy!Okay! I know...Not the greatest, but does the point come across?
