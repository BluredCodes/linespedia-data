At times I feel alone
in this crowded place
searching for compassion
in a sea of hardened faces.

Looking for the reasons
posting it in rhymes
seeking something more
than a meeting of the minds.

Contemplating questions
whose answers that I seek
finding there are others
that seem as I am, weak.

And yet I am alone
sharing all I feel
waiting for the someone
that will make me real...To me, we are half of the whole until we meet that someone that totally completes us. Sometimes I think I have found him, while others, I am not so sure! :)
