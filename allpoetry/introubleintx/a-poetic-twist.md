Two hearts as one
a troubled mind
two souls reaching
one of a kind.

A place in time
that knows no bounds
people's laughter
amid ups and downs.

A solitary moment
that first kiss
a painful mistake
after a night of bliss

Another morning
dawns pale sunlight
a thunderstorm
so scary and bright.

A poets words
from paper and pen
then once more
we're at the end.
