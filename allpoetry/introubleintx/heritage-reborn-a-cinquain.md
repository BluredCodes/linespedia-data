---
title: "Heritage Reborn (a cinquain)"
writer: "introubleintx"

Driven
from places they loved
by forces unknown to others
bringing about their painted faces
of war.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
their food
diminished by
hunters, taking all they
can, leaving nothing left, land
barren.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Once proud
now helpless, left
homeless, starving, alone,
to live their lives the way of the
white man.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
And now
said Indian
living the only way
he can, to provide for his own,
dreaming.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
of a 
life where once more
the land belongs to those,
that were here, heritage reborn
again.
