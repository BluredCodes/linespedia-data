Once again, my heart has been broken 
seems as though it was used as a token. 

A token for tragedy, a token for pain 
a token for love, with no hope of gain. 

A token for anger, and also for fear 
of not having you with me, close and near. 

A token not meant to ever be loved 
but one to be used, pushed, and shoved. 

A token meant to wither and die, 
to be by itself, living a lie. 

A token meant to finish it's days 
living alone and wasting away. 

Wondering about what could have been 
if you hadn't wasted the token again. 

If you had done all that you could 
maybe the token would still be good. 

As it slips, and falls from your grip 
remember that it was my heart, not a tip.
