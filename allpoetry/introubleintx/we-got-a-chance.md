We got a chance
last Wednesday night
to be together
with nowhere in sight.
It was something I wanted
and I needed it too
I know I'd been hoping
for this chance with you.
We wondered between us
where could we meet
mind you now,
we must be discreet.
We decided to go
to a place from the past
we'd been there before
hoping this not the last.
We had to decide
my car or your truck.
Neither was roomy
just plain bad luck.
I'd told you before
my cars really small
especially for someone
who is very tall.
Bucket seats, stick shift
no room to be had.
No closeness at all
which felt really bad.
I finally decided
if your going to cheat
don't buy a new car
unless you try out the seats...
