It's 2 in the morning,
I'm thinking of you.
Wondering...
Are you awake, or in
bed dreaming of what we 
could be doing together?
Fantasies...
Thoughts of inhibitions
left behind and desires
coming true. 
Wishing...
Waiting for the how
and the when, just to 
share with each other.
Revealing...
Words and actions, trying
to provoke the other
to taking the next step.
Leading...
To the perfect outlet
between the two of us
finally being shared.
Tormenting...
The senses, with words
as the tension increases,
the frustration mounts.
Creating...
The time and place, for
the unleashing of desires
felt through actions not words.
Releasing...
All that has been kept
inside, together, in unison
till spent, and unburdened.
Knowing...
Next time won't take as long
to reach, now that the fantasy
has become reality, etched in time.
