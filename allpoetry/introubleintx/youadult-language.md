I see you there
playing with her
it pisses me off
not because I care,
but because you
are being what you've been
all along, and I
hate myself for
believing in your lies,
that you told with ease.

And for thinking
you were more than
what you were.
You were never my
friend, nor anything
to me. And to you
I was just someone to
be abused and then tossed
like trash into the nearest
dumpster. Thrown away when
you tired of me.

Just like you've done
to so many others.
Make them believe they
are special, when all
you want it a piece
of ass. Then you call
them names to others.
Like it's not what you wanted.
But you started it,
asked for it, and 
once it was offered,
it wasn't a chase.

It was a chance to use,
hurt and manipulate
to get your way.
And then say, Goodbye
when you decided the 
fun was over. You're
a cruel, heartless
asshole, who will get
caught someday, by
someone who sees through
the disguise, and plays you,
like you have so many others.
And then, you'll be alone
and I'll laugh in your face!
