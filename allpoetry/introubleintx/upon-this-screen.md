Upon this screen...

life displayed
passion's shared
dreams portrayed

Upon this screen...

friendships form
laughter shared
trust reborn.

Upon this screen...

tears are shed
over words written
from heart not head.

Upon this screen...

where you and I meet
we've found in common
the strength to defeat.

Upon this screen...

emotions freely flow
we learn it's okay
as we learn to let go.
