title: "My Enemy"

My mind consumed with hatred for you,
You're leaving here, I'm glad you're through.
No longer hearing you in the hall
No more of your annoying calls.
No more turning people against me
Now they'll have the chance to see,
that it wasn't how you made it seem,
they'll see past your pity me scheme.
I'm glad you're leaving to start anew,
I wonder if the next one will see through you?
I know I didn't, and it's a shame
cause I ended up taking the blame.
I was warned but didn't believe,
that in the end, you'd backstab me.
So I guess I got what I deserved,
I hope the next one has the nerve, 
to look through you, instead of fall
for the bull you'll give with all.
and see your for the person you are,
one that cares for no one and leaves a scar.
