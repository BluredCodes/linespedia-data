Memories and visions
dance before eyes
feelings left unsettled,
that is no surprise.

Pain and suffering
felt so very deep
caused by promises
never meant to keep.

Love and laughter
shown to all
leaving the truth
locked behind a wall.

Till the wall is shattered
by an unknown blow
learning to live
and letting it show!Another thought at work type thing... :)
