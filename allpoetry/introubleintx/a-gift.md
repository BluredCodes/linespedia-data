A Christmas present for my boss
is what I'm trying to find
he likes guns and Harley's
but yet he doesn't ride.
Trying to pick something out
that he will not expect
is more than I bargained for
on that you can bet.
I've made some calls,
sent e-mails too
and still don't know
just what will do.
I've taken this upon myself
this very special task
why would I do this
you might have to ask.
You see my boss is special
he's one you'll not forget
he takes the time to let you know
he needs you, you can bet.
He talks, jokes and listens
when you need an ear
his door is always open
you never have to fear.
He has a way of showing
on him you can depend
a simple way about him
let's you call him friend.
So as I ponder Christmas
and how to let him know
I decided to let this poem
be the words to show
that he is special
and thought about today
once you've had him as a boss
you'll forever want to stay.My boss is really an excellent guy..He can be hard when the need arises, but that's what he is paid for. One of the things I enjoy most about him is he has an open line of communication with his employees that you would not believe. :)
