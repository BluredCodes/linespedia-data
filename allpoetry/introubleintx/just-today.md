Just today, I thought about our life, 
you've said I will be no more your wife. 
You've told me that you'll throw me away 
and I no longer will have a say. 
I'll no longer be your hopes and dreams, 
no longer be a part of your schemes. 
No more will I matter to you, 
you've taken back your vow of I do. 
I'm sure that you wish to make me cry 
that's why this hateful goodbye. 
Too bad you took this way out 
you will be lonely, I have no doubt. 
And when the day comes that you remember me, 
I'll be gone, forever letting you be....
