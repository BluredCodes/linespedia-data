tick tock
the clock on the
wall, time ebbing away
seems faster as each day goes by
tick tock

tick tock
seconds, and then
minutes, then hours, into
days, into weeks, and into years!
tick tock

tick tock
a lifetime spent
watching the clock until
death arrives, leaving you no more
tick tock!Another one...Probably not as good as the last one though! :)
