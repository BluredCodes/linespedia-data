If you think we'll say goodbye,
you couldn't be more wrong
just cuz you're leaving for a time
from our hearts you'll not be gone!

We'll wait to hear word from you
that lets us know your fine,
and that soon you'll return
to share with us more rhyme.

Until that day, we will hold tight
to our faith and the words we share,
for there is a common strength here
to which no other can compare.

And in this strength, we'll send to you 
our thoughts and prayers you'll need
to make it through this trying time
on that we've all agreed! :)You know we all love you are you will be in our thoughts and prayers!
