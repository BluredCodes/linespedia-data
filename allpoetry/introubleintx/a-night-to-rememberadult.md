To go out one night, I packed a few things
a blanket and toys, you asked me to bring.
We went to the bar, drove out by the lake
tonight was our night, a chance to celebrate.
As we drove down the road, I gave you head.
Then it was my turn, to be played with instead.
We stopped by one lake, I took off my pants.
and you made me cum, in the seat I did dance.
You went back to driving, got scared by a cop
on the side of the road, we decided to stop.
I took over driving, and didn't get far
he decided to pull us over, and search our car.
What a laugh he did get, when from under the seat
he found my panties, and two toys as a treat.
He asked us about them, I thought I would die
I was embarrassed, and made up a lie.
I said what I could, in my best poker face
I know I was ready, to go home to my place.
"They fell out of the blanket", to him I did say.
"They'd been wrapped up, since I'd put them away.
When the blanket I grabbed, to take out that night
they must have been hidden wrapped up so tight."
He let us go home, and I wondered if he
