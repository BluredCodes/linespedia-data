His adoration she wears
like a crown of the 
finest jewels, displayed
for all to see.

His fascination for her
shown by the gifts
he so lavishly
bestows upon her.

His longing for her
heard in the unwavering
sound of sensuousness
that drips from his tongue.

There is so much more,
roads not yet traveled
as the two become one
feverishly bodies join in their minds.

Kindred spirits at long last mate
and prove once again
that destiny can be reached 
and eternity is obtainable.....
