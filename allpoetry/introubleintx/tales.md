amidst the lore
of knights and kings
of dragon's fire
and horse with wings

you'll find tales
of witches magic
and warlocks lives
that ended tragic

princess fables
of kingdoms fell
dreams proclaimed
a nightmarish spell

through maze and myth
and written word
we bring these tales
