I feel like a schoolgirl 
with her first crush. 
Things that you say 
make me want to blush. 

Then when I see you 
my knees get weak. 
My tongue gets tied 
so bad I can't speak. 

I get all frustrated 
my heart beats fast 
I wonder how long, 
how long can this last. 

They say it's not real 
it's just infatuation 
no way it will last 
for the duration. 

But I don't believe them, 
they just don't know 
how far or how deep 
these feelings go. 

It's not puppy love 
by that name or another 
It's love that is real 
we feel for each other.Let me clarify...I haven't felt Puppy Love in a long time..LOL..This is just what I would think it would feel like to go through it now. :)
