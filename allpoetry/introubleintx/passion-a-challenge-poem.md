As my lover and I were caught 
making love on the kitchen floor 
I was riding upon his horse 
and begging him for more. 
I spied a storm settling upon us 
while looking out my door. 
My blood-red screams keeping him 
from hearing what was in store. 

The stormy clouds were racing fast 
keeping with our intense heatbeats 
I knew it would be hitting soon 
for it was not at all discreet. 
As the full moon set in the distance 
I kept warm with his body heat 
Keeping him delirious with passion 
through the raging storm a feat. 

The bare naked trees I could see 
were bending to and fro 
As I was arching on my own 
his body did finally let go 
We watched the storm in silence 
our passion spent just so 
The picture painted outside for us 
building a passion that we know. 

A passion hid inside us all 
until we share the sight 
of mother nature raging on 
making longing burn so bright 
getting excited to thunderous sounds 
flashes of lightning in the night 
makes the passion so intense
