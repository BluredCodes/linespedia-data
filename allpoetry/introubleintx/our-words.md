We have but one
life to live
blessed with a gift
of words to give.

Feelings to share
through words on a page
not unlike actors 
who perform on a stage.

Yet an actor has a script 
from which he can speak
a poet searches the soul
for the words that they seek.

Our words once written
our soul is then bared
our feelings through words
to all we have shared.
