Sweet strawberry 
chocolate dipped 
tasted between 
lovers lips 
Moist 
and 
juicy 
take a bite 
juice dribbling 
down my chin 
your mouth nibbling. 

Torment me 
and tantalize 
blindfold me 
let me fantasize 
Moist 
and 
juicy 
as I savor 
the nectar within 
yearnings for fruit 
do begin. 

Luscious red 
upon the top 
of cheesecake 
oh my! don't stop! 
Moist 
and 
juicy 
In my mind 
strawberry dreams 
covered with 
whipping creams. 

Sweet strawberry 
my name you call 
to join my lover 
in a strawberry ball 
Moist 
and 
juicy 
Chocolate dipped 
and so much more 
is what my lover 
has in store...Challenge Description: Write me a poem about your favorite fruit. I want to see a lot of visual imagery in this & delicious descriptions (teehee) have fun!
