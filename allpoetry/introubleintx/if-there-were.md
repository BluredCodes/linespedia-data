---
title: "If There Were"

If there were 
a time or place
where I could feel
your warm embrace,
I'd be there 
in seconds flat
for I've wanted nothing
more than that.

If there were 
a way to show
just how deep
my feelings go
I'd do whatever
it would take
to prove my point
make no mistake.

If there were
a way to be
happy for all
eternity
I'd do it all
for only you
and that will be
forever true.

If there were
alas there's not
or maybe I haven't
found the spot
Know this though
these words I speak
for the happiness
I will forever seek...
