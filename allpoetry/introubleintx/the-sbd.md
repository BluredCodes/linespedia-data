The SBD, it reeks so bad
clears out a room in nothing
flat
The SBD, you try to blame
anyone, long as you know their
name.
The SBD, shared as a joke
watch out for the poor soul it
chokes.
The SBD, you couldn't stop  
got you out of a ticket with a 
cop.
The SBD, that you just let
the worst I've smelled, you can
bet!
The SBD, it will get you, 
so be careful who you sit next
to!It's all your fault..And you know who you are!!! BOTH of you!!! :)
