title: "Together.."

Here I am
there you are
Seems so close
and yet so far.

Wanting me,
as I'm wanting you
there's just nothing
that we can do.

Even if we had
a way to spend
through glass
we could only pretend.

So here I am
and you are there
wanting to be close
too far away to care.

Maybe someday soon
there will be 
a place for us
together eternally.....I don't know...Just one of those things...
