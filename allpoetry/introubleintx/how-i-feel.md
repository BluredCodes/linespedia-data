I can feel it
buried deep
like a secret
there for keeps.
It hurts me
from day to day
trying to get by
come what may.
And then I see you
there you are
another pain
another scar.
Another problem
I'll have to face,
living in this
troubled place.
Living on this
web of glass
afraid through it
I'll have to pass.
In order to get
on with life
and maybe feel
again the wife.
I don't know how,
I don't know when,
but I know who I'll be
