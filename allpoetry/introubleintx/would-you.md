---
title: "Would You???"
writer: "introubleintx"
slug: "would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

If you were here
Would you 
kiss me 
places I've never been kissed?
Touch me 
in places that have been missed?
Teach me 
to feel what needs to be felt?
Torment me 
with floods of passion dealt?
If you were here
Would you 
Tease me
in ways only you can do?
Desire me
so that I feel it to?
Demand me
to show you all that I know.
Deprive me
leaving me, as you turn to go.
If you were here
Would you
love me
with all that you are
show me
your true from afar?
Tell me
the words I need to hear,
Hold me
taking away all my fears.
If you were here
Would you?
