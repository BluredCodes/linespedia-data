It starts to rain
it's cold outside
the rain soon freezes
no place to hide.

A beautiful picture
sure to make
a sight worth seeing
out near the lake.

Every blade of grass
and tree in sight
covered in glass
in morning's light.

As the sunlight
shines it's rays upon
icicle's melting
but not long gone.

For as night closes
and the temps drop
tomorrow more beauty
to make your heart stop.

Winter in Texas
although it doesn't last
each one more breathtaking
than the one of the past.
