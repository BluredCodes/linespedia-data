Painting a picture
for the world to see,
but that doesn't mean
the picture is me.

What goes on inside,
Is not always out,
You learn to hide
Without a doubt.

Outside a smile
Insides a frown.
Hiding from all,
the fact that your down.

We all paint pictures
For the world to see
Scared and afraid
To be you and me.
