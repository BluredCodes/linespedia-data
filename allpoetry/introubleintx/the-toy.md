The sign said "Lacie's
Boutique for the Unique"
selling lingerie and gifts
that adults sometimes seek.

I walked in the first time
hoping not to be seen
and was greeted by the owner
so sweet and serene.

I browsed through the store
for something to buy
and she showed me a toy
that caught more than my eye.

It's called a "kanga-roo"
is what she told me
it runs off of batteries
three of them, size C.

She then turned it on
and at once I was sold.
I'd never in my life
done something so bold.

I took it home with me
headed straight to my bed
inserted the batteries
turned it on, enough said.

An experience I got,
on that glorious day
and now I look forward
to when roo and I play.

I'm telling you now
it's an enjoyable buy
go get yourself one
but don't tell your guy.

Instead you can show him
by a candlelit bed
let him know what you like
and then give him head.I couldn't resist the ending..LOL...
