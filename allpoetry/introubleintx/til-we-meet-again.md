title: "Til We Meet Again..."

As I see you there
do you want to please?
Begging for suggestions
while on your knees.

It makes me want 
to tease and torment
til you can't hold out
and you're looking to vent.

Then I'll control you
and make you obey
enveloping you in
the games I want to play.

Will it be rough
or languid and slow?
I want you to tell me
so that I'll know.

And then I will do
all that you'll ask
whilst you enjoy
this forbidden task.

When I am done
your pleasure and pain
is all I had hoped for
till we meet again.
