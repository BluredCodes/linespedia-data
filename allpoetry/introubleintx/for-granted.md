We take for granted
til it's gone.
We hurt each other
from dusk till dawn.

Hurting with
the words we use
knowing better
we could choose.

Throwing punches
pain as well
suffering as
if in hell.

Doleful glances
that we sneak
hidden emotions
to uncover, we seek.

Until nothing left
to share but pain,
is all we have
to ever gain.
