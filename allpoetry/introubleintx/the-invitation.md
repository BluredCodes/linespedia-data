I feel the pull,
the temptation,
to be with you,
an invitation.

A chance to 
go back and do,
all the things
we wanted to.

And yet, you see
holding back,
knowing all
you do attract.

Wondering if 
once again
you're looking at
where we've been.

Or maybe you see
something new
a chance again
to play a few.

For you have her
and I have him
there is nowhere
to begin or end.

It can only lead
to a frustration
as I fall again
for your temptation.Written about M. He knows who he is and why he is. :)
