title: "Burning Desires"

Hands that are cold
on a body that's warm,
passions unbridled,
secrecy sworn.

Inhibitions abandoned
there is no shame.
Drawn to each other
like the moth to the flame.

Bodies entangled,
emotions are torn
out of desperation
a hunger is born.

A hunger that grows
and feeds on itself,
not wanting to stop,
in spite of myself.

Falling into you,
as you fall into me
wanting all the more
to be finally set free.

Free of this driving,
yet hungry desire
entrapped in your flame
to be burned by your fire.
