Attention grabbing
potted plants
wonder what's
in those pants.

Crazy clowns
standing there
waving parts
in the air.

Looks like
fun ahead
wonder if
they'll spread?

just be careful,
it's a trick
cause those pants
are filled with pricks!LOL...My poem about your picture....
