Your words they hurt
Like the sharpest of knives
Cutting me through
The pain inside.

I know you don't see
Or maybe you do
But the words they keep 
Spilling from you.

The pain grows deeper
Each word you say
It chills my bones
By the end of the day.

After your through
The same words ring out
"I'm so sorry"
Those words I doubt.

If you only knew
The pain I've felt
Over words spoken
Like blows dealt.

You wonder why
I withdraw and fight
Wishing I was dead
All through the night.

It's the words you use
To make me weak
Wanting the darkness
I eternally seek....
