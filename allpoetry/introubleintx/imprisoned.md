Locked behind these four walls
the future all you see,
Knowing when I need you here,
in prison you will be.

At night longing to hold me close
and whisper in my ear,
No one is there to comfort you,
as you weep silent tears.

No one to touch, a visit through glass,
a phone call twice a week
You felt like you were stronger once
now you feel just meek.

As you look forward to a future bright
when all is said and done,
You hope that what you're longing for
will still be there, not gone.
