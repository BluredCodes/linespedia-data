Let me ask you a question,
Why should you care,
about the cost of my clothes,
or color of my hair?

Why should it matter
if I have a tattoo,
or have body parts
with holes poked through?

Why should it matter,
the color of my skin?
Does it mean
I'll never fit in?

Why it is wrong
to be different it seems?
We're all God's children,
we all have dreams.

We all have a lifetime
to spend with another
to Him we are all
sister's and brothers.

So don't judge by my clothes
or anything else
If you must judge me
Judge me for myself!Unknown,
