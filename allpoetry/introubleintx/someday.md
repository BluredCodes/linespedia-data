I miss you more than words can say.
I wish you were here, everyday.

Someday soon, you'll be right here
and I'll be able to feel you near.

See the love in the smile on your face,
feel the warmth of your embrace.

Look in your eyes and hold your hand
as we venture to the promised land.

Where together we'll live in eternal bliss
and never be longing for the other's kiss.

All these things I want from you,
someday soon will all be true.A short poem to my husband..I miss that he isn't with me..But soon...I hope!
