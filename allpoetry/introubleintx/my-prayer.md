---
title: "My Prayer"
writer: "introubleintx"

May everday
you not feel meek
and find the 
happiness you seek.

May all the people
you know and love
find peace someday
in the heaven's above.

May the one's
who've changed your life
no longer have
struggle or strife.

But most of all
may all of you
get everything
out of what you do.

All this I ask,
just one thing more,
may we all have another
sunrise in store...
