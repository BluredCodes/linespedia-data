bodies
now locked in a
once forbidden embrace
passions fire lighting up their faces
heated

by the
hunger within
chest to chest, skin to skin
breathing quickens, hearts pounding, now
sated

as we
continue this
tormenting and teasing 
pleasantly playing and pleasing
