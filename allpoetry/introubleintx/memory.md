title: "Memory"
writer: "introubleintx"

I crave your body
all through the night.
To have your near
gives me delight.

To feel your arms
as they wrap around me
fills me with the warmth
that my body seeks.

And as your body
presses against mine
I feel your hands
as they explore what they find.

Which sends me to the brink
and on over the ledge.
As my body realizes
the depth of your knowledge.

Making me want to have
all that you'll give
as if there weren't any
more days to live.

When all is over
and it's time to sleep,
I'll drift off to dreamland
with a memory to keep.
