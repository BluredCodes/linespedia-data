In a state of confusion
your words leave me
not knowing
daily
are you, aren't you
do you or don't you?
what will it
be?

Curious questions
always unanswered
until you decide to
speak
and then you leave
not knowing if
it was the answer you
seek.

Or was it a question
unanswered with words
yet realized through
dreams
and fantasies of times
you create, tantalizing
yet fulfilling
schemes.

On again, off again
questions, emotions
desires and hope fulfilled
sweetly
until opening up
accomplishes what you
wanted but couldn't say
discreetly.
