It starts with a glance
that comes from within
imagining bodies
locked skin to skin.

Then add a touch
that lingers with care
feathertip softness
with my body you share.

Mix with it a kiss
filled with desire
the touch of the lips
that sets me on fire.

Now comes the taste
random licks of the tongue
I'm in it for pleasure
but I'm glad that it's fun.

Bodies now pressing
wanting more with each taste
breathing is rapid
undressing in haste.

As flesh melts with flesh
and desires breeds
submission results
performing these deeds.

Our bodies as one
molding together in time
sating our hunger
it should be a crime.

Cumming together
as we become one
and finish this pleasure
