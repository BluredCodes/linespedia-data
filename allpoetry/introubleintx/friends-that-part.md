title: "Friends that Part"
writer: "introubleintx"

You've disappeared from my life once more 
you've disappeared into nothing more 
We were once friends, thats what I thought 
but alas, once again, I guess we were not 
You left my life with a big gaping hole 
one that reaches way down in my soul 
I know you never really knew 
how deep my friendship was for you 
But I guess it's gone, over and done 
someday you'll be the lonely one 
and wish you had a friend like me 
but no-one will be there, you will see 
I'll have gotten on with my life 
and forgotten the struggles and the strife 
Gotten over the hurt and through the tears 
over a long forgotten friendship that could have lasted for years...
