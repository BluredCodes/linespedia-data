Is there ever
a time that's right
to plan a love
and future bright?

Can anyone ever
honestly say
together they'll be
forever and a day?

Is there such
a knowing place
out there in an 
empty space?

A place that knows
all hopes and dreams
and who would make
the perfect teams.

To spend all time
living as one
a future to plan on
when all is done.

In hopes there is
such a place
so we'll forever
be in love's grace.

And eternally be
depended on
from whom our strength
will be drawn.
