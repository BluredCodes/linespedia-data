---
title: "With You..."
writer: "introubleintx"
slug: "with-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I wish you could feel
the way that I do.
That the pain and hurt
would get through to you.

To show you that,
the other side
is not so easy,
it's no joy ride.

I've got the children,
but I'm still alone.
I have no one
whom I can depend on.

The weight of our lives,
the future to be
has been laid on my shoulders,
for now it's just me.

You've never been here
so you don't know
and you'll never feel it
cause I'll never go.

Until you can see
the side I've been on
think about what you say
when you are gone.

There is only so much
that I can do,
to once again
spend my life with you.
