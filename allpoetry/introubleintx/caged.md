Locked up,
within a cage,
wanting out,
silent rage.

Frustrations,
patience thin,
each day
starts again.

Seeking rays
of sunlight,
knowing not
day or night.

Ticking time
tick tock
no good to
watch the clock.

Minutes, hours
days on end
locked in a cage
years again.

No more,
finally through,
coming home
to life with you.

Somethings different
while I was gone
my life stood still
your's moved on.

Change, hmmm,
all I can see
I just hope
you still love me......I don't know...Just something that came to mind... :)
