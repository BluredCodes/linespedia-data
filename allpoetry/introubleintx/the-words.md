In my shaking  hand I held my willing  penwaiting for somethingto at last  begin.I continued that wayall the  day longand there was nothingit all sounded wrong.and then I decidedto just let it flowto unlock the wisdomand let myself go.then something happenedthe words finally  spilledI was filled with excitementtill my body was chilled.And now I have writtenmy work of the dayI now have the timeto sit back and play.
