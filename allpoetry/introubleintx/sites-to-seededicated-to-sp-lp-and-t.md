As I enter the chats
what do I see
the newest addition
to allpoetry.

It's none other than
Sheer Poetry
streaking along
nekkid as can be!

Running amuck
with chickens on her head
whipped cream bras
and booty tassles of red.

Followed by 
Lone Poet too
is there nothing
these gals won't do?

Trayseelynn now
takes the lead
Stinketh faeries three
are back indeed.

As they run by
fellows cop a feel
making these gals
holler and squeal!

They're havin fun
can't you tell?
Just be careful of
that awful smell!

Although just trying
to provoke the muse
these gals won't stop
with oh's and ooh's!

They want to be 
the center of attention
while blowing their stink
in all directions!

I'm glad to know them
all the same, so
I think I'll join
their streaking game!!!!LMAO...Okay..The devil made me do it! Or was it SP's muse at work??? Hmmmmmm
