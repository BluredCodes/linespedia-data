title: "From Us..."

Your hands on my body
I'm wanting your touch
why is it at night
I miss you so much?

Looking for something
to remind me of you,
and of the things
together we'd do.

Closing my eyes
and seeing you here
but knowing inside
you're nowhere near.

Weeks into months,
that you've been gone
the images getting weak
that once were so strong.

Months into years
until you are free
the memories fade
from us to just me.....
