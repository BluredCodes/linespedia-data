Nicholas Williams, a life too soon
taken away, leaving memories of
a little boy at play, your first time
on your motorcycle, when you were just 
small, your first time driving your car
when you were on your way to manhood.
The first time you put on your football
uniform, and ran through for your
first, of many touchdowns. The first time you 
held your son, who is just now turning two. 
Seeing you looking so much a man, when
you went into the Army, and thinking proudly,
that is my son. My what a man he has turned
out to be. So many memories that have
become more precious than gold now that
you are not here with us. To make more
memories, for us to hold in our hearts forever.
