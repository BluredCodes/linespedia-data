Someday you'll wake
amidst a dream
or maybe a vision
as yet unseen.

A memory of
a future told
in your heart
for you to hold.

Something to haunt
through the days
wandering ever
through life's maze.

Seeing clearly
as past becomes
thoughts of futures
yet to come.
