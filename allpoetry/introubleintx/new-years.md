It's New Years
we are apart
I'll spend the night
with my broken heart.
You lied again.
You promised you'd stay.
But drinking again,
got in the way.
Now you're locked up
and I'm alone
the kids and I,
are on our own.
As the kids grow up
and away from you
there's no going back
what will you do?
Three years from now
when you return
the years you missed
are for what you'll yearn.
I can't bring them back
nor share a memory
of the things you missed
while away from me.
I can only hope
your lesson you learn
and to prison
you'll never return.I think it speaks for itself. :)
