He is tall,
brown hair,
brown eyes,
dimples to die for
could I want more?

He is mine....

He is tall,
blonde hair,
blue eyes,
He listens freely,
yet doesn't judge me.

He is hers...

I've had them both,
I belong to one.
yet wanted the other,
Am I wrong?

Was it wrong?

To take what you offered,
no strings attached,
to give and receive
thoughts, desires, each other...

Maybe I was,
but now it's too late
the damage done
I'm forever changed...

Again the test approaches
which road will I choose?
The road that leads to him
is the road to you I lose.....
