Hidden from within
yet clearly visible
to those looking into
the eyes of the soul
the righteous turmoil
that stirs creating a 
paradox of fantasies
becoming living memories.

Envisioning yearnings
of passions unparalleled
and as yet unimagined
except by a chosen few 
who stir the soul 
from within and paint
the picture using
emotions paintbrush.

Enslaved by lust
awakening desires
lying long dormant
until seduction
with reckless abandon
entangling emotions
snared in a web
of finely woven lies.
