Are we together
or are we apart
are we knowing
the other's matters of the heart?

You come, you go
and seldom return
leaving me
my feelings still yearn.

My feelings and I
my heart stands still
you'll never know truly
how you make me feel.

I love you
more than you'll ever know
It's sometimes hard for me
to let my real feelings show.

I've loved you forever
or so it seems
I hope our future 
is as strong as our dreams.

From this day forward
I will love you only more
For now and forever 
My feelings will soar.I wrote this for my husband back in 89 or 90 for our anniversary. Just wanted to post some of my older stuff. :)
