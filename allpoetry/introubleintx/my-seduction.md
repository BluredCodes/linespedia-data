You're sitting thereas if waiting for me,others have tried youit's time to break free.You're curves are amazinga beautiful sightsmooth and enticingready for any night.The feel of your leatherenveloping my skinas I slowly realizemy travels to begin.The music softly plays.It's my favorite tune,reminding me thatwe'll be together soon.I can tell you're worriedwill I put you to the testcan you prove to methat you are the best?Igniting a sparkand warming you up.I don't want you cold,I'm not pushing my luck.Showing me, yourbuckles and beltsit's okay darlindon't leave me with welts.Your motor is running.I'm ready to ride.Not wanting it roughtill slow moving we've tried.Your powerful thrustjust wants to be knownas you show me morethan any others been shown.You're ready to take meon a fantasy tripyour bodies excitedsweat beginning to drip.I look to your bed,and see it's king size,all the things we can doyou just don't realize.As this trip is endingour new one to beginI'll keep you forevernot trading you in.There's one last questionthat I have for you.Aren't you so gladI bought this ford too???
