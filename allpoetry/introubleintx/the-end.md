He said
she said
rumors they fly
a goodbye
a blow off
Dear John, whatever the label.
no face
no voice
not even pen to paper
no more
never again
just leave me alone
not friends
not lovers 
we never were anything
don't talk
don't write
don't pick up the phone
not sorry
don't care
troubles never cease.
All's said
or is it
but it's still the end.Someone I know and I thought was important to me blew me off tonight. This is the result..Just my rantings and ravings. Not verse per se..Just random thoughts put together. :)
