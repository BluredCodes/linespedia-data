What is Normal?
I know what's not normal.
Molesting your eight year old step-daughter
is not normal.
Feeling up your fifth grade student
is not normal.
Trying to have sex with your brothers daughter
(because he does it)
is not normal.
Learning to use sex in place of love, or to show love
is not normal
(unless that is what you were taught is normal at eight)
Finding out that your mother slept with your boyfriend
is not normal.
Your mother miscarrying what she claims is your boyfriends baby
is not normal.
Finding out even after you married him she is still trying to sleep with him
is not normal.
Marrying someone that cannot ever trust, and loves abuse and drugs
is not normal.
Having kids that hate each other, and constantly fight each other
is not normal.
So, I ask again,
What is normal?
Cause I know my life is not normal....More rantings and ravings..But all true in the end....Normal??? Hmmmmm...What a question.
