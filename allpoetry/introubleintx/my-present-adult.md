This year I went to visit 
my sister you see
when I got there
she had a present for me.

She showed me to her room
and then locked the door
and pulled out a package
from a dresser drawer.

She said it's best viewed
without the light
this is something to use
in the dark of the night.

I heard the bag crumple
as it fell to the ground
then she turned to me
without making a sound.

There in the package
I could plainly see
a glow in the dark vibrator
she had gotten for me.

It's been in the box
since that day in July,
but maybe someday
