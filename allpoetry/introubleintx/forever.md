We've been apart
for, oh so long
can our feelings
remain ever strong?
You've let me know
your feelings have strayed
do you still want me?
or am I being played?
Will you run to her
when you are set free?
Or will you return
to forever with me?
I ask myself this
as each day goes by.
I don't want to stay
in a life that's a lie.
I wish I could know
that your feelings are true
and that once this is over
I'll have forever with you.Something else written for my hubby.
