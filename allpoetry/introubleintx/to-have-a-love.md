---
title: "To Have a Love"
writer: "introubleintx"
slug: "to-have-a-love"

There are so many 
words to write
to tell of feelings 
through the night.

To talk about 
a love so grand
having been made
from God's hand.

And yet I find 
I am without
a well formed verse
to talk about.

To show that I
am truly blessed
with all this love
and some happiness

that anyone 
could ever crave
to have a love
like we were gave.
