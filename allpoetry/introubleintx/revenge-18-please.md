You threw me away
once again
like you do
when it's the end
don't you think 
I have a say
or am I just 
a game you play
someone who can
make you cum
when you need
to have some fun
someone that 
you can play
when your done
will go away
I fell for that
once before
should have known
what was in store
for you are a player
of all you choose
and not someone
who ever will lose.
you better think twice
before you use me again
I'll be keeping notes
this time my friend
and when you tire
of playing this game
I'll have plenty to tell
dates and names.
and when you lose
all you hold dear
I'll be there laughing
when you shed a tear
and when you need
someone you see
there will be no one
thanks to me.....Awww...The sweet taste of revenge..But I somehow think this is not enough in this person's case. :)
