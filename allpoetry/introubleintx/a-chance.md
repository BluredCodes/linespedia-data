As you take
a chance with me,
you'll get what you've
been wanting to see.

You'll get to experience,
you'll get to feel,
all that you've wondered
for so long now real.

When it's all over
and you've had your fun,
that's when the problems
you see, have begun.

That's when you change,
indifferent it seems.
I'll no longer invade
you're curiosity dreams.

Will it be worth it?
After the end,
can we go back
to just being friends?
