Ribbons 
bows 
presents galore 
boxes 
wrapping 
on the floor 
stocking stuffers 
Christmas wreath 
don't forget 
the holiday feast. 
Santa 
Rudolph 
children laughing 
carolers sing 
this is what 
Christmas brings.I won the challenge with this poem. It was to write a Christmas poem 30 words or less...Hope you enjoy!!! :)
