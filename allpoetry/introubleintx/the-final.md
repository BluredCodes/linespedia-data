Twas the day before finals,
and all through the class,
You could see the students
hoping they'd pass.
Homework was finished,
and lessons were through.
As the teacher announced,
"It's time to review,
So open your books,
take plenty of notes,
you can't pass this class
by popular vote."
And the teacher she started
the book she went over,
what seemed to the students
like cover to cover.
Then as the bell rang,
it was time to go home
as the students filed out,
you heard them grumble and groan.
"How can she do this,
it's our last day.
Surely she won't 
make us go out that way."
So the students went home
and all pulled all-nighters,
wanting to show her,
they were all fighters.
They came into class,
with very little rest
having studied all night
for this final test.
As they came in the room
and all took their seats,
you could have heard
even the smallest mouse squeak.
She asked them to please
clear off their desks,
thats when they saw it,
this monstrous last test.
As she handed the final
out one by one,
the students they thought
"We'll never get this done"
Then she turned towards her pupils
And said "Give it your best"
Then smiling she told them,
"It's an open book test!"This was something my mom, who is a teacher at college, asked me to write. She started it with the first two lines, and told me what she wanted it to say. Hope you enjoy!
