Lying naked
bodies touching
hands exploring
intense kissing
feeling
tasting
licking
longing
sensual heat
caressing softly
thrusting madly
wanting more
hands 
mouth
fingers 
tongue
closer yet
farther still
it's impending
bombarded senses
throbbing
exploding
cumming
sated.......
