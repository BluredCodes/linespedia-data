Times may be hard
and you may fear
we love you though
not always near.

We'll listen when
you need a friend
and help you get
back up again.

We'll write a verse
composed for you
so others can show
they love you too!

I know this may seem
stupid, crazy, or dumb
but I want you to know
you're never alone. (okay my rhyme sucked here)

All that are here 
at this wonderful place
would miss your dear
and wonderful face.

Your presence is known
to all that join here
the one and the only
