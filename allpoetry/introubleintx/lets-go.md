once again i've not heard
from your mouth a single word

seems as though this game you play
blatantly varies from day to day

you want from me, this i know
but when it's time you can't show

i never know truly where i stand
once close, then in foreign land

i know we both share the need
from our passions finally freed

but if you can't keep steady pace
i'll gladly leave this lustful race

for i need more than just a line
from you e-mail, when you find time

i want our bodies to become one
and share our needs, not just in fun

i don't want games forever played
been there once when i strayed

not asking for love, or forever you see
just find the time to set me free.

from the flame that's burning within
thinking of our bodies skin to skin

i know that you are feeling it as well
the longer we go, like being in hell

I'm ready for release, i hope you know
let's get together, not no, but let's go!Yeah well...You get the picture.. :)
