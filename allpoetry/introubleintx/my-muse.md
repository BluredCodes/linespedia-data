I know not from where
the inspiration
comes to enjoy
my dedication.

The one who provokes,
we'll call him my muse
I wish I knew
how he felt on my views.

I know he is the thought
behind my every line
he is the words 
behind my every rhyme.

He is the one
who starts my verse
is it a talent?
Or is it a curse?

I guess I'll never
really know
for he still
refuses to show.

If he agrees
with what I write
or if it hurts him 
all through the night.
