I have waited
and I thought it was
the same for you.
Guess not...
I told you when,
you said, can't.
Why would I 
try again?
Just to be told
sorry, it's not
possible...
I have watched
and heard you,
when you said
I was what you
wanted..Yet you
decline when I offer
myself..Is there 
something wrong?
Change your mind maybe?
Don't like the game
when it comes
to reality??
Maybe it is
just a game
you want to play
with my head.
A game
that you lost
when you said
I can't....Just something that came to mind.. :)
