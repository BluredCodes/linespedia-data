I saw you from across the room
seemed you were watching me
i didn't know who you were
let alone if you were free.
You were a bouncer at the club
at least that's  what I heard
i knew i'd seen you around
but we'd never spoke a word.
i thought you were attractive
wondered if you thought the same
all too soon i was to know
as i was pulled into the game.
a game i'd never played before
nor have i played it since
you pick someone from the bar
for the after work events.
I was the object you sought out
to take home that night
and i willingly went along
not putting up a fight.
we ended up at your house
well, the place you stayed
and that is where i found out
how the game was played.
they had their room to themselves 
the walls were paper thin
i was not sure what to do
or where exactly to begin.
i heard my girlfriend moaning
apparently having lots of fun
i wanted to remain silent
until all was said and done.
you totally amazed me
with your oral skills
shivers up and down, my body
a total state of thrills.
i'd never known such excitement
and you could tell i know
cause i had told you i was quiet
and you told me it was not so.
then your friend joined the room
and my girlfriend came in too
i tried to play it all down
on what had happened with you.
that is when it got real weird
cause she was there with you
and somehow i ended up
playing with your friend too.
it all took place in the same room
on the floor next to each other
i'd never in my lifetime been
through this with another.
We finished up and then we left
taking in all we had learned
we both now had a secret 
and we both could now be burned.
that night we came back to the club
like every other Tuesday night
and she totally ignored your friend
while i was in your sights.
but there was someone else with you
that was missing the night before
a girlfriend to whom you belonged
now I was privy to the score.
I was there when she was not
and took her place that eve
that is why I'll never play
nor be the one deceived.
the game is done and over
no more clubs do i attend
and the girl from beside me
i no longer call her friend.
