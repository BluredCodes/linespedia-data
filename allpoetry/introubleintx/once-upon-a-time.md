Once upon a time,
as stories often go
There was a fairy maiden,
and a knight that loved her so.
He asked her for her hand,
and she graciously declined,
for she was a young fairy
Not yet knowing of her mind.
She told the fairy knight,
that someday would be king
She would change her mind,
if a unicorn, he would bring.
So he started on a journey,
that would take him far
To distant fairy lands,
he followed a shining star.
He came upon a dragon
He'd heard about in lore
And asked about said unicorn
As others had before.
The dragon breathed a fire
And the fairy knight did dance
Until the dragon fell asleep
And the knight took his chance.
He slay the mighty dragon
And took with him it's head
To prove to the fair maiden
That said dragon was now dead.
That did not impress her
As the knight had thought it might
So after taking slumber
He left again that night.
He came upon a fairy land
That was unlike his own
And asked about a unicorn
And where they might be grown?
Alas, as luck would have it
They knew nought, you see
Having heard that unicorns
Were just a fantasy.
So the tired knight
Started out once more
Not knowing what this journey
For him, would hold in store.
He came upon a golden land
With knights such as he,
And took a little solstice
For which he was in need.
That is when the poor knight learned
Of games played by maidens fair
As he heard tales of others like he
Sent out upon a dare.
A dare to win the maidens hand
Or that is what he thought
For said maiden knew all along
No existence for what he sought.
So the knight decided to teach
All fair maidens well
Something that he had learned
On this journey he befell.
He found another maiden
And they soon fell in love
So they settled in to marriage
Made in the heavens above.
He took her back to his fairy land
Sported her about with pride
And introduced his new love
To what would have been his bride.
He told her he was sorry
That he failed at her quest
But that he had found something
That more than passed the test.
True love from another
Overshadowed by no lies
And that together they will live
Forever happy, no surprise.
The fairy maiden learned that day
A lesson not well received
If you play games in love
Your heart can be made to bleed..
