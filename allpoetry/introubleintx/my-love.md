My passion for you
is undying
My love for you
is untrying
My heart is yours
forever
My love for you
ends never.
My arms ache
to hold you
My body wants
to touch you.
My heart is longing
for your love
For you I thank
the heavens above.
I love you, my darling
Hurry home.
For I can't take this
time alone!Another one written for my husband when he was driving a truck long haul. :)
