Love me as I am,
or love me not at all.
without your love to guide me
I will surely fall.

I love you in so many ways,
I never want to lose you.
And I never tried or ever
wanted to use you.

I wanted you here to be
my lover, husband, and friend.
And I only truly want
our marriage not to end.

Love me for me,
not for something I'm not.
Love me because of who,
not because of what!

Love me....Again another old poem I wrote for my husband! :)
