Your moods change from day to day
I never know just what to say.
Afraid to let the words come out
for the fear of making you shout.
Walking on eggshells when you are near
scared of who or what you'll hear.
You make life harder than it seems
and keep me nervous to extremes.
I ask myself before I speak
will I look as if anger I seek.
Will I make you walk away,
give you hateful things to say?
Or will it be the turn around
another day happiness you've found?
These things I wonder night after night
to try and keep from starting a fight. 
And then at last the week is done,
till the next week upon us comes.
And then once again, we will start out
