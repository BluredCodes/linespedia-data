Written,
to share
laughter,
tears,
joy,
sorrow,
our fears.

Written,
to reach out
someone
out there,
kindred 
spirit,
we share.

Written,
hopes,
dreams,
trials,
tribulations,
sharing 
smiles.

Written,
thoughts
on paper,
with pen
parts
hidden
within.Another something thrown together. :)
