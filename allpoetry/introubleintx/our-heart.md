Our heart,
a canvas upon
which our
life is painted...
through the colors
of love's lost,
and found
the colors of
friends who have
come,
       and 
	        gone.
brush strokes of
dark hues
for pain and 
suffering,
tinted with
light strokes
from laughter
and friendship.
m
  i
    x
  e
d
to form
colors
that paint a scene
of what is
our life...
Now and then...
