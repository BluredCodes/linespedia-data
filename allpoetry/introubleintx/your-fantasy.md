If you could 
have a fantasy
guaranteed to come true.
What are some
of the things
you would like to do?

Would you like
to have my body,
for your pleasure toy?
Telling me your
needs and wants,
as I give them all with joy.

Would you share
with me your secrets
and fill me with desires?
Put my body
to the test,
as we fuel passions fires?

Would you give
a little less,
or just a little more,
than you have
given all the rest
that have come before?

I'd like to 
share a fantasy
created in our minds,
Something that
we both want
a treasure yet to find......Well????
