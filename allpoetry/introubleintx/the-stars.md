---
title: "The Stars"
writer: "introubleintx"

The stars how they twinkle 
in the night sky above,
Shining ever so brightly 
like the past of our love.

As you look to the sky, 
do you see it too?
Do the stars remind you 
when our love was new?

Some stars they burn, 
so strong and so bright,
While others you can hardly 
see in the night.

Those stars they reflect 
how I feel about you,
How our love burned bright, 
when our love was new.

I wish I could say 
it was how I feel now,
But our love has dimmed 
through the years, somehow.

I wish I could 
bring back those days, 
When our love was bright, 
and never let it fade.

Alas I know, 
you can't bring it back.
Just like the stars, 
love dims, thats a fact.

Unless you work 
to keep it shiny and bright,
Love can fade to black, 
like the stars in the night.
