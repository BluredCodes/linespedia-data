As she sits and listens to 
the melancholy sounds of 
Scott Weiland, with a 
flag tied around his waist, 
and no pants on underneath 
through his rasberry lips 
comes hypnotic lullabies. 

She feels the despair brought on 
by a tear as it falls from her 
face and slowly breaks into 
a thousand pieces, regretting 
the hidden adoration that has 
lead to darkened love, 
a naked revelation. 

No longer feeling the jovial 
laughter, but now to the point 
morbid anxiety, she thinks of 
the antagonizing kisses they 
once shared, now long gone 
and replaced by a rusting hate, 
knowing what could have been. 

Or what should have been 
had it not been for a midnight 
rave, and those beautiful 
androginous people with tattoos, 
cut in the shape of words, 
and the hysteria brought about 
as the ocean calmly engulfed the sun. 

She sits and thinks of so many things, 
giant lavender and azure elephants, 
a sarcastically drawn bunny in the grass, 
God wearing eyeliner, and so much more 
and wonders what he is doing now 
and if she is missed, or if he is as he was,
