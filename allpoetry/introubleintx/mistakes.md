title: "Mistakes"
writer: "introubleintx"

The feel of your body 
pressed against mine 
as our hearts race 
beating in perfect time. 

It's almost as if 
you are right there 
and yet there is nothing 
but cold thin air. 

No arms to embrace me 
in the cold night 
no body to snuggle 
and hold so tight. 

I'm all alone, in this 
great bed of ours 
longing and wanting 
getting over the scars. 

Living through the pain 
of wanting you near 
and knowing down deep 
my tears you can't hear. 

Nor can you see or 
ease this constant ache 
caused by another 
separating mistake. 

It won't last forever 
You'll come home someday 
and until then strong, 
and faithful I'll stay.
