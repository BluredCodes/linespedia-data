Come here to me
let me satisfy you
tell me the things
you'd like me to do.

Show me with actions
I'll show you with words,
things we can do
only others have heard.

Try this just once
you'll want it again,
I promise you that
you won't want it to end.

The pleasures you'll seek
and maybe you'll find
as you come to me
and I open your mind.

Give this a chance
whenever you dare
for between you and I
are many pleasures to share.....
