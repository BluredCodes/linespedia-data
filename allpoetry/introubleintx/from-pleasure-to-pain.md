Pleasure to pain
nowhere in between
going from one
to the other extreme.
Undoing of buttons
tearing off your shirt
get rid of those pants
before you get hurt.
Tracing your body,
scratching your back,
as pain becomes pleasure
of both you won't lack.
Nibbling of ears
on your ass a smack,
tying you down, for
the rest of the attack.
A whip and a feather
across your chest
tormenting your mouth
with more than my breast.
You want it so bad
are about to explode
but I'm not yet ready
to help you unload.
it's time for a blindfold
to finish this game
not able to see
whether pleasure or pain.
Now feather, now whip
now ice aptly placed
a drizzle of chocolate
as I sit on your face.
Random flicks of my tongue
and bites lead the way
to finalize your pleasure
no more pain, per se.
As I mount your cock,
to begin my ride
you lie in wait
for the pain to subside.
You can't help but moan
as you beg to be freed,
it's just the last pain
on which we agreed.
As we cum together
and you are untied,
you can't wait to journey
on the next pleasure ride.
