Where are you?
How are you?
Why?
I know you've moved.
Been sent a
           w
            a
             y
I knew it would happen.
But it's been almost a week.
No calls,
no letters..
Just the last o
               n
                e
saying you moved
and a message
from your mom,
saying you love me.
I'm worried
something's happened.
And you can't
or maybe won't,
let me know.
Something.
Anything...
Just a minute
of your time
to say
I Love You!My hubby was moved last Thursday..Last message I got was on Wednesday night telling me he was being moved...Now it's Tuesday and there have been no calls or letters.... :)
