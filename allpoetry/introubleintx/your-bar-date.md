You go outside, jump in my car
Head yourself to the nearest bar
You've been drinking again all night
And now you'll want to start a fight
Or get in trouble with the law
And then the house you'll call and call
You know I hate it when you act that way
But you don't care as long as I pay
You said you were just going to the store
I should have known, I've heard it before
Just when things are going right
You end up going out all night
And when the bar closes at two
I'm sure there will be someone new
But when I ask, you'll say oh no
I went to the house with so and so
Or what's his name is what you'll say
I go through this every other day
And when the bill money is all gone
You'll wonder who I spent it on
Yet you wonder why I'm not crying
When you say in jail you'll soon be lying
If only you'd look back and see
You might be sorry how you treated me
And when you are the lonely one
You'll apologize for the things you've done
But by then it will be too late
So you better keep tabs on your after bar date!
