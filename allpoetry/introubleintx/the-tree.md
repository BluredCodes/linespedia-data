I'd like to paint a picture
for all the world to see
for across the street from my room
is a lovely face in a tree.

When I lay down to sleep at night
and look out my open door
I see her there just watching me
like she has every night before.

I wonder if she's an angel
put in that very tree,
so when I go to sleep at night
she'll keep watch over me?

This winter she will disappear
when the leaves fall to the ground
and I wonder if in the spring
the leaves will bring her back around.

Cause I know I'll miss my angel
when I fall asleep at night
for when I wake up all alone
she's always there in the light.

I bet noone's ever noticed her
except that is for me
I'm glad to know she's over there
watching me from her tree.My bedroom is on the second floor of my house, so all I see is the top of the tree, and there she is at night. There is a light that shines through her from one of the stores on the next street. I really wish I could paint or draw, to share with all I see. :)
