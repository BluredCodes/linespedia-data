Sinking slowly
darkness calls,

razor's edge
cutting deep
pills to take
forever sleep.

Sinking faster
darkness surrounds.

starts warm
turns to chill
endless slumber
one more pill.

At the bottom
the end is near.

finished work
my suicide
no longer needing
a place to hide.

My depression leading
to suicidal tendencies....I don't know..I am fine...Just something that came to mind today...  :)
