With the help of Tommy
fanatic of all poetry
I'm adding more names of poets
that words have touched, you see.

There are two Mike's, Highway and MB
another poet named Fred
though some of them I have seen
other's I've not yet read.

I read a story by Sharon
at allpoetry's companion site
Sunny Dudley has read my work
I'll return the favor with delight.

Of Tinasheart I have been told
and tarnishedheart as well
wornways, Chris Kramer, and abyssia
I'm sure have stories to tell.

Maureen who tells from the heart, 
Death goddess who does so fine, 
Then granpappy who makes us happy 
and Jenneddin is a deep mind. 

Sheer poetry has been a long vet, 
remember Molassis, she's here too, 
with Dreamweaver, we can't forget, 
and others 'cause we're not through.

Add to the list Heinz and tamara
all touched as golden pen
and last of all Storm Dancer
that's all for now, the end.Okay, this was done with help..Sort of...I was given the names in a reply to my other poem by Tommy and I put them together here. :)
