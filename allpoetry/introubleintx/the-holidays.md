---
title: "The Holidays"
writer: "introubleintx"

Another holiday is here
and I am to blame
for the attitude you have
it's always the same.
I look like the bad guy
like I start the fight
but you are the one
that didn't do right.
I get all the blame it seems
no matter right or wrong,
and then you tell your family
all the things I've done.
And then when the day is over
as the night draws near
you'll be drinking again
and I'll be the one in fear.
I hate the holidays
things always end the same
and then we try again next time
with nothing ever gained.
You say you'll try harder,
and that I should try to,
but I can't hide these feelings
and that is all you do.
That's why I hate the holidays
I can't seem to forget the past
To me they never ever change,
each one the same as the last....
