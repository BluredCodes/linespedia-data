With arms wide open
Our bodies touch
Looking at you
I see wanting so much.

To feel you near me
is all that I crave
It's something I want
for days and days.

Our bodies together,
such a beautiful thought.
Always knowing,
it was you I sought.

To have and to hold
until death us do part,
living and loving
each day since the start.

Growing old together,
til alas someone dies
Waiting to once again,
look into your eyes.

Then together once more,
our hereafter as one,
we look back on the past,
our new future begun!I don\'t remember,
