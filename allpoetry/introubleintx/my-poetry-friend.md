Across the distance
of time and space
we meet together 
in a special place.
A place of words,
of hopes and dreams.
A place to vent,
a place to scheme.
A place to find
a friend indeed
and help someone
who is in need.
To listen closely
as they speak
and share their pain
when they are weak.
To let them know
they're not alone
where true emotions
are ever shown.
I'll meet you there
very soon again
we'll share our words
my poetry friend.You know who you are!! And you know I am here when and if you need me. :)
