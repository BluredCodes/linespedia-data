The feel of your kiss,
upon my lips.
As your hands they move
down toward my hips.

Pulling me closer,
our bodies they touch
in every place
I want this so much.

Getting together
our bodies as one,
Another longing
for passion begun.

Waiting in silence
my passion burns,
No time or place
for what we both yearn.

With eyes closed
all this I see,
yet no time or place
for what could be.
