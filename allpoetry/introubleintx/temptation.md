Your tempting me once again            
I want you as a close friend           
And yet when the temptation strikes    
I want more than just one night.       
Or more than just an hour or two       
I want the chance to be alone with you.
To make all things just disappear      
And enjoy the time while you are near.          
And yet I keep on holding back            
for fear the secret will attack        
And then again I will lose out         
because I didn't heed the doubt.       
And ignored the chance of once again    
sharing the fun we had back then.
