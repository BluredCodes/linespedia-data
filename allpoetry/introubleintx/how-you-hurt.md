title: "How You Hurt..."
writer: "introubleintx"

You took me to a place
I'd never been before
you told me you needed
in your life, something more.

I fell for all the lies
every line you sent my way,
I'd never thought someone else
could take my breath away.

And yet, there you were
when I was feeling low
you jumped in, said the words,
pulled me in the undertow.

I fell hard, and I fell deep
be careful though, must be discreet
was what you whispered
during passions heat.

When you used all you could
you tossed me to the side
and now I live with the hurt
of knowing how you lied.

Knowing I was nothing more
than a distraction for your life
and that you'll stop at nothing
to again cheat on your wife.

There were others, before and since
I wish I'd had a clue,
that you were in it for what you'd get
and would hurt all that fell for you....
