Last week
you opened your soul
telling me things
sharing secrets
of wanton desires
and arousing images
of lustful thoughts
better left
unearthed
and 
unspoken.
this week
your aloof, out
of reach, just
within the grasp
but yet......
so far
and the words spoken
remain a memory
or was it
a fantasy
of things
that you
and I
wish 
to 
be
