As we begin
our life together
in each day
let's find a treasure

a moment in time
to tuck away
to savor when
we're old and gray

A picture taken
for a memory
of times together
we'll keep, you see.

Stored in this chest
our hopes and dreams
of plans we've made,
shared, and schemed.

For as we start
so we shall end
memories treasured
as lovers and friends.

And when someday
in death we part
leaving a treasure
for the other's heart.

we trapped in time
