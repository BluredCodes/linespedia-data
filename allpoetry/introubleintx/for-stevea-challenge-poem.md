The thing in life 
that I love the most
is a person
that I hold close.

He is my husband
and my best friend
I'll love him until 
life's bitter end.

I came to love him
a long time ago
with a love that just
continues to grow.

He was my first
my one true love
sent to me from
the heavens above.

I fell for him
when I was just a kid
and love him forever
is what I have bid.

His smile to die for
he can be so kind
eyes to drown in
a treasure I did find.

Others have tried
to take him away
but his promise to me
is forever and a day.

And that I most treasure
I'll forever be his
and spend my life
for moments like this.
