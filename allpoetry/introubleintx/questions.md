Right now,
in this moment in time
are you thinking about me?

Are you,
wondering where I am or
what I am doing, right this minute?

What I,
am thinking or feeling,
if I'm alone, or if the kids are here?

If I'm,
wondering where you are or
why I haven't heard from you.

Why I,
am writing this poem
when you can't read it.

When you,
are somewhere, that I don't know,
wishing I would hear from you soon.

Wishing I,
knew where you were, how you are,
why I haven't heard, and when I will.

Right now,
I'm wondering, are you?, what I?, If I'm?,
Why I?, When you?, and wishing I,
had the answers to all these
Questions......Another one of those things that come about, no form, just there. :)
