You ask of someone 
who has inspired
taught me things
someone I've admired.

Well I'll answer true
from my heart
there is someone 
that plays that part.

Someone that listens
to every word
and whose voice
I've never heard.

Someone who has
inner strength
that would go
to any length

to help another 
that was in need
giving themselves
having no greed.

No matter feeling
pain or sorrow
they greet the day
like no tomorrow.

That someone special
I have found
shows compassion
that knows no bounds.

She's someone that
has taught me well
we can all get past
our inner hell.

And breathe new life
as each day will rise
to look at beauty
with more than eyes.

To see more than
just the outside
that inner strength
is nothing to hide.

In case your wondering
of whom I speak
find a mirror
and take a peek!Written for bigmammajen's challenge..To write about someone that has changed, influenced you in your life...I wrote this about her. I think she has been an inspiration and more to alot of us here..My appreciation...Add yours as well! :)
