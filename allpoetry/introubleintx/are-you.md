title: "Are You?"
writer: "introubleintx"

You left me alone
once again with the kids
the holidays upon us
you call on the phone
and tell me you are sorry
but what can you do?
I am the one left
to take care of it all
and try and make it work
when the kids won't help
they try to make it worse
and you tell me it will be okay.
how can you know?
are you even here? No...
you are there where 
you have noone 
to worry about but you
no bills to pay, plenty of food
a roof that costs nothing
you have plenty of everything
especially time.
time to reflect on the wrongs 
that should have been rights
and time to know that we are still here
waiting for the day that you return
when the kids will be grown
apart from you, and I as well.
What can you do? What have you done?
Nothing but hurt us all once again.
And yet we repay you with
our lives. Waiting patiently,
for it to be over once again.
I know, three years it will take.
What I don't know is, are you
ever worth the wait?
Are you?Something written for my husband tonight..Having a hard time dealing with him being away and the things that the kids do to make the time longer! :)
