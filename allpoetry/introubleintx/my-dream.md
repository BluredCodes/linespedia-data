title: "My Dream"

As I lay in bed,
I dream a dream.
Of your body and mine
doing the obscene.

Playing with each other,
the games we invent.
Wanting it all
with no more torment.

I cover you in oil
the flavor a surprise.
I cannot wait to taste you,
and look into your eyes.

Running my tongue over you,
thinking how good you taste.
I want to be slow,
there's no reason for haste.

We've got all the time,
the time we both need.
If only reality
was as good as my dream...
