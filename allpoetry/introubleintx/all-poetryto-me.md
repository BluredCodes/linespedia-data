From the corner of my eye
a Solitary Tear of an Angel fell,
for a young poet
known to me as nell.

As a laugh escapes me
must be bigmammajen,
or is it Erik's Girl
that's at it again?

Desdemona's stories
cause laughter and pain
as Andy Harding posts
his muse at it once again.

Antmonkey sings
and is a poet to boot
while Connor adds rhymes
that are more than just cute.

Then there is Tommy
Poetic Fanatic by name,
writing inspiration
is really his game.

Webdreamer made me 
a fairy with wings
as well as others
their praises he sings.

Other poets I love
just to mention a few,
Lurie, Molassis, Amethyst Angel,
Cinderella_au28, AnnD, and bdgrey too.

~Tina Brannon~, a great poet,
don't forget the Vampire Girl,
there's a beauty, a manicmuze,
and mystysaint in my poetry world.

There is no way on this earth
I could name each of you
but I know who you are
and that I can share with you!I'm not sure if I wrote cinders name right, but I tried from memory. There are so many wonderful author's here. I'm not saying that it's perfect, and I am sure that there are many names I have read and enjoyed that didn't come right to me. But there will be more! :)
