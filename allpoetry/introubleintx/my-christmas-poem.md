Twas on this night
so long ago,
our fate came to be.

Jesus was born
to die for us
from our sins set free.

With ribbons and bows
and presents galore
the trimming of the tree.

We celebrate Christmas
a day to rejoice
by spending lots of money.

When it should be spent
giving Thanks to God,
for what he sacrificed willingly.

To give to us
a chance to live
for all eternity.

So as you shop,
or wrap, or spend
or decorate that tree.

Remember that God
did it first,
when he sent his Son to thee...Maybe not a good poem...But I threw it together at work tonight. :)
