---
title: "Your Mask"
writer: "introubleintx"
slug: "your-mask"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I know you can
make me smile.
The things you do,
can drive me wild!

Yet you change
from day to day.
I never know
what game you'll play.

I never see
the same man twice
it's like rolling
perpetual dice.

And yet you go
and do these things.
That can impact
what the future brings.

It's almost as if
this mask you wear
is only on you
when I want to share.

To spend all the time
I can with you.
But I cannot
your mask get through.
