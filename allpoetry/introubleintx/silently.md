Silently he waits for me
his patience wearing thin
at night weeping quiet tears
because he's alone again.
No one to hold in his arms
locked in a room of men,
he swore never to return
and yet he's back again.

His promises fall upon deaf ears
all words she's heard before
the last time this happened,
she swore she'd stay no more.
Yet silently she waits for him
to walk back through the door
hoping this time will be the last
as he's promised her before.
