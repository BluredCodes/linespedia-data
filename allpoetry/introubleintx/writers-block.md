title: "Writer's Block"
writer: "introubleintx"

The words are there 
without a doubt. 
The words are there, 
but they won't come out. 

They're in my head, 
and in my soul, 
but to get them out, 
I've lost control. 

At times they seem 
within my grasp, 
but upon them 
I cannot clasp. 

At other times, 
the words they spill 
and I can't 
hold back their will. 

Simply put, 
it's writer's block 
the words they've chosen 
this writer to mock.
