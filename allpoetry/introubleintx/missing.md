As I run my hands 
across my breasts,
Imagining them crushed
under your chest. 

Knowing my nipples 
will stand erect
when across them 
fingers roam direct. 

Feeling the heat
thats igniting inside
as I wait for you,
pleasure to subside.

Sending fresh waves
desire coursing through
this body that's waiting
there's a hunger for you.

To take me to the brink
make me beg for more
of what you offer,
what I know is in store.

As my fingers move down
as I know your's will do
I can feel it clearly....
