On the wings
of her new life,
No longer just
someone's wife.
She stares in awe
at what she sees,
No longer controlled
Now she is free.
Free to live
each day as she will
free for all
feelings to feel.
Free to take charge
and to make ends meet
not trying forever
just to admit defeat.
Now knowing all things
are her own way
not choices made
afraid of what he'll say.
Free to fly
and do as she pleases
No longer imprisoned
by unrealistic reasons.
