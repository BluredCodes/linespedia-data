Yesterday we welcomed
our newest addition
So little and small
sweet disposition.

Her name is Autumn
she is just a day old
the story of her life
is waiting to unfold.

New places to visit
so much to see
her own little room
a great big family.

Aunts and uncles
and cousins galore
this little girl
has a lot in store.

A proud grandpa
and grandma to boot
will spoil this girl
cause she is real cute.

Not to mention
Great Grandma Fay
when she finishes with her
mom will give her away.

This little girl
with a life to unfold
waiting for the treasures
of a life yet untold.This is for my grandbaby... :)
