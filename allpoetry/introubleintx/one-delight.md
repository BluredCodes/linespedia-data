title: "One Delight"
writer: "introubleintx"

You say you've given
up your career
as my poet 
I love so dear.

That now others
are wanting you
to write words
to their loves true.

But your words 
only meant for me,
can't they tell,
can't they see?

You only write
to your true love
sent to you
from the heavens' above.

And that you cannot
write a word
for someone else
not known or heard.

I'm glad to know
that you won't write
for anyone else
except your one delight!!I wrote this for my husband because he decided to quit writing me poems because too many others were asking him to write for them. :)
