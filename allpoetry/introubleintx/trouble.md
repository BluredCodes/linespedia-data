My name is Trouble
that is so true,
I spend all my time
sharing poetry with you.

I'm just a woman
Yeah right, some would say
but I am what I am,
Just get carried away.

I have some tattoos,
rebelious I am,
not from the South
But who gives a damn?

My name is Trouble.
You can find me at will
I won't hurt you at all,
Just share how I feel.

My muse is an angel
sent to fill me with verse
sometimes she's great,
sometimes she's a curse.

My name is Trouble!
I've a rose on my breast
You'll only see it
If I'm baring my chest! 
(watch out for my bathing suit..lmao)

I've a devil tattoo
that sits on my arm 
I gave him a halo
to keep me from harm.

Taz sits on my ankle,
flippin the bird
Watch out for my attitude
Yep, that is the word!

My soft side has a heart
With his name within
So yes I am branded
With his name, a sin?

Just call me Trouble
With a capital "T"
Just know one thing
There's no one I'd rather be!!!!!LOL...This is me....To an extent! :)
