title: "Once Again"
writer: "introubleintx"

You've always been
such a distraction.
I feel the pull 
of the attraction.

I feel the urge 
of secrets born,
to stop myself
I am torn.

My brain says no,
it's not okay.
My body says yes,
do it anyway.

I do not know
which way to turn,
for fear that I'll
in the end get burned.

For once it starts,
it's hard to end.
And then I lose
you once again.
