Casual sex
between friends
can still hurt
when it ends.

for by no means
can you shut out
feelings that sharing
can bring about.

sex is not casual 
by any means
it starts with feelings
maybe unseen.

if you decide
to jump in the hay
think twice about it
or regret it you may....Hate to say it, been there, done that..And I got burnt in the end. :)
