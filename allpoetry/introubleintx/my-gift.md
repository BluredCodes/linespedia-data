title: "My Gift"

If I have but one gift to give, 
let my words forever live. 

If I've nothing left of me, 
my words are there for all to see. 

For all to know the pain I've felt, 
for all to know with what I've dealt. 

For all to know, you can get through 
whatever hand life deals for you. 

And if by chance someone out there, 
has similar words or feelings to share,

let by chance our paths meet, 
to share our triumphs and not defeat. 

To bask in the glow of our precious gift, 
and use our words to give others a lift...
