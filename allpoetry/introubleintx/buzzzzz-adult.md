I have a box
of adult toys, you see.
I keep them under 
lock and key.

One day my son
got one of them out,
and decided to take it
out and about.

He took it outside
showed it to a boy,
who then joked with me
about the use of the toy!

A friend of my daughter's
he was sixteen
he thought is was funny
my son, and this scene.

So now and again,
when Arthur comes near
he'll say Hi Deb!, and then
BUZZZZZZZZZ is what you'll hear!This is so bad..I know...My son (he was 7 or 8 at the time) got into that box somehow and showed it to my daughters friend. Turned it on and everything! So then he would always tease me by buzzing when he would see me! I told him that if men would do their jobs right, women wouldn't need them!!! LOL!!!!!
