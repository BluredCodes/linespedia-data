My mind keeps goingback to that nightreliving the feelingIt's useless to fight.It's almost as ifright there againand the feelingjust won't end.It haunts me dailyand it's in my dreamsI can feel you near meor that's how it seems.It grips my insidesI can't controlI can feel it in mealmost to my soul.You are luckyabove the restwhat you didpassed the test.Now I'll want moreinsatiable I'll beand I'll be hopingto once again see.And feel againwhat I have feltturning to youcause you made me melt.You set me on fireI'm burning withinlonging and wantingto let you try again.I don't know what this sounds like..I just wrote it here.
