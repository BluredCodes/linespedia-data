I've wanted you always
and in all ways
you're all I think about
for days and days.

To have and to hold
to love you true
to spend my life
growing old with you.

To see our children
get up and on their own
to feel that our house
is more than a home.

To see our grandchildren
when they are born
and hold them at night
when they're scared of a storm.

To have you near me
until we become one
we'll meet in the afterlife
look down on what we've done.

And see that no matter
how hard it was then
we have a new life
a new journey to begin.
