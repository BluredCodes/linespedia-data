---
title: "In The Early Hours"
writer: "Pleasure"

In the early hours

When broken hearts cry

The world is asleep

And I want to die.


In the early hours

When I remember

All that was and could have been

My heart just aches

And I just surrender to my pain.


Tears fall against my pillow

And I feel the sorrow that lies

Deep in my heart

And I know that I’m alone.


But when the sun rises life goes on

And I do all that is necessary to survive

But my heart sleeps during the daylight

And only comes awake in the early hours


It tortures my mind

With a love that I recall

I pray for peace and relief

From the memories, lonilness, and grief.


And I hope wherever he is

He remembers me with the passion and grief

That keeps me awake in the early hours.
