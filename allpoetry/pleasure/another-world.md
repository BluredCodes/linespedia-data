Another World

Together we created a world of love and romance

With emotions so strong and deep 

With pain and sorrow, light and dark, strength and loneliness

Guilt and grief.

We built a world of dreams and devotion

Of fairy tales and palaces of gold

And this is where we lived.

But as time weaved it magic wand

Everything grew dim and lost its shimmer

The fairies wings and dragon’s claws

All started to lose their glimmer.

When the romance lost its hope

And reality set in

We found ourselves in another world

A place we’d never been.

Together we cannot survive

In a world of sorrow and greed

With mundane jobs and crimes of the devils seed.

So now we are each in another place

Loving each other more because of our grief

A move we had to make

Because it was the only way to hold on to the dreams

And romance, the love and magic of our existence.

Now, our hearts can feel the pleasure

Of having lost a love so strong

Of knowing beyond the mortal truth

Where we truly belong.

But in each others arms, we lose ourselves

To love and romance, to loneliness and grief

So one day we will test our faith 

To love see if it still exists

But not in this world, because we belong to another

A place of joy and pleasure

A place of magic and fairies breathe, of dragons and castles

And love beyond measure

A place that’s true and honest

With golden dreams and endless nights of surrender and pleasure

One day I will see you there and we will know

That we have lived and loved, and been and seen all that we desire

For I will always be your queen and you my only sire…
