LONELINESS


Empty, forgotten,


Moments in time.


Isolated, solitude.


Cloistered and withdrawn,


Restricted, confined, prisoner.


Apathetic, emotionless,


Irksome, monotony.


A sanctuary of repressed tedious emotions.


Elevated to superficiality.


Unspoken, voiceless, silent.


Forever locked inside my heart.
