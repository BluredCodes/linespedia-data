---
title: "Secrets I Keep"
writer: "No Tomorrow Girl"
slug: "secrets-i-keep"
source: "AllPoetry"

I will never speak of
the secrets I keep…
those things that have shaped me
made me who I am

I will never express
the sorrows I've known
those cruelties that have laid heavy
on my heart

for, to do so
will allow those secrets I keep
to see the light of day
and they will grow,
take on strength,
become like rain clouds:

heavy,
gray,
bogging me down

instead
I will choose
to walk in the light
and allow the sun's warmth
to melt those
secrets I keep

and one day
I will be whole
healed
brand new…

and free

free
from the secrets…
