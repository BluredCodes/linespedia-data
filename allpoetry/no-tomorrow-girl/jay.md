---
title: "Jay"
writer: "No Tomorrow Girl"
slug: "jay"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The three days we spent together were great, 
It was by chance we met but it could be fate, 
All I know is I really like you, 
The endless smiles you gave to me, 
The laughter that made me feel free, 
The hugs that never seemed to end, 
I never once had to pretend, 
I could talk to you so easily, 
You put my heart and mind at ease, 
Just with one tight squeeze... 
We did so many things together, 
Shared so many thoughts and dreams, 
It made me realise nothing is as it seems, 
There is always a tomorrow, 
That's what your words made me see, 
That there is hope for me, 
And now that I have found you Jay, 
I hope everything is going to be okay, 
I want to get to know you really well, 
What feelings will come, only time will tell, 
But I know when I felt your lips, 
I also felt my heart beat faster, 
Then came your fingertips... 
Caressing my body, feeling my skin, 
It really sent my heart into a spin, 
And now that you're not here, 
My heart is aching to hold you tight, 
To do that would make everything seem alright, 
I know that it won't be long and I'll be with you again, 
So until then I'll look at your photo to keep me sane, 
Just know Jay that I miss you, 
With every beat of my heart it's true.... 
So I'll sit here hoping you miss me too.I wish tomorrow would hurry up! We're going away for the weekend :)
