---
title: "Love Is Confusion"
writer: "No Tomorrow Girl"
slug: "love-is-confusion"

Falling in love is easy 
Also hard to let go 
What do I do? 
I still love you so 
Love is beautiful 
But it comes with pain 
It's confusing 
Like the weather rain 
I don't understand 
What's happening to me 
Look what love does 
Can't you see? 
I have words to say 
But they won't come out right 
I'm going crazy 
Morning, noon, and night 
Confusion is love 
And it's in my mind 
It's frustrating 
'Cause I want you to be mine 
Why can't I say it? 
I know what to do 
But I'm scared to tell you... 
That I love you 
My heart says yes 
But my mind says no 
I'm trying so hard 
But I can't let go 
You think now we're just friends 
So I can't tell you 
'Cause if I did 
You'll think it's not true.
