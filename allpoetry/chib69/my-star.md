The intencity of your heart
Is too much
I quiver
And render helpless 
To your touch
Safe and warm 
Is what you make me feel
I pinch myself 
For it feels serreal
Let me gaze into those eyes
They comfort me
I just wanna be by your side
Because of you 
There is a new world to see
And in this sky
You are the only one shinging for me
You may not know this
But to me you've always been my star
Because I've always watched you from a far...Mwah:o* I hope you like it...XOXOXO ~Chibi
