Yet again I am back where I belong
Sad and hurt
Where did I go wrong
The ones closest to me leave
Their love is something I wish I could've recived
But thats the way it goes
In the end the truth shows
No room for me in their lives
I can't see why
My heart listened to all their cries
Oh well
Back into my shell
I'll soon be gone
Where I can be strong
I don't know where I'll go
All I know is i'm on my own
The things I wish I can say
Like please don't leave me!
Stay!
Wont change a thing
Because abandonment is the only thing life can bring
And when the darkness sheds 
All that will be left is my body cold and deadWhat happend,why do you avoid me now???
