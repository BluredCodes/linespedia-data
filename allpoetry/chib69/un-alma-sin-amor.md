Whats left?
I watch life as it stands 
Fate and destiny
Are out of my hands
The sun is no longer bright
The moon does not shine
And the mountains arn't at height
The water is still
And no grass on the hills
The birds do not go
Because the wind 
Does not blow
Yet I feel 
There is nothing left to see
No wounds to heal
Every thing is the same
The people in this world
Selfish,cruel,truly a shame
Why they say 
Life has so much to offer
I never understood
Because surprises and happiness
Left me at childhoodIt came to me one night...
