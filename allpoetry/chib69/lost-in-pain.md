He walks alone through the day
He cries alone through the night
The people around bring him spite
He's on his own in his fight

Memories plague him everyday
"It'll be ok" is all they say
Maybe he wont be
Is that hard to belive
No one understands him
He's always been decived

Never belive what you've been told
For there is no love from the cold
Walk he must
Walking without trust
He knows he has nothing to gain
Because he is lost
Lost in his own pain...
