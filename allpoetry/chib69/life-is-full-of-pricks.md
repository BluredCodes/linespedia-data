Did you know what you did was sick
You do this to others 
That makes you a dick
Just knowing people like you exist
It disgusts me 
I pray that no one has to go through this
Did you know what you did was sick
Manipulating others into beliving your lies
You suck out their emotions just like a disgusting tic
I know better now...
My spirit is up not down
Did you know you are sick...?
