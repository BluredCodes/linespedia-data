---
title: "Words for a song unheard"
writer: "Chib69"
slug: "words-for-a-song-unheard"
source: "AllPoetry"

Deep in my soul
I have yet to reach my goal
Finding the one 
Wanting to hold
Can it be you
Who can care and stay so true
Wont you just hold my hand

My heart you stole
When you saw me all alone
You truly are
Something to behold
Will it be you 
That will take away my pain
Don't ever go away...

May the wind never change
Here I will stay 
Oh where you go i'll be there too
Even if i'm not there
My heart will care
And you will see how much your loved

Be here and stay
In my arms you are safe
Please look into my eyes
And see the truth...

You are my dove
I belive you're from above
Time does not move
When i'm with you
Stare at the bay 
With the moon light in it's way
Here I choose to stay

Under the light 
I can see the birds take flight
Taking with them
All of my dreams
When it is time
They will start to melt away
I hope you never change

May the wind never change
Here I will stay 
Oh where you go i'll be there too
Even if  I am not there
My heart will care
And you will see how much your loved

I have to say
You are what i've wanted
My soul is safe with you
Mi amor es tu!
