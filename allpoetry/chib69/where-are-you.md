---
title: "Where are you?"
writer: "Chib69"
slug: "where-are-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You helped me when I didn't know
When your hurt you never let it show
Those blue eyes I always see
Even though your not with me
But I wait here untill the day
The day you come and take me away
My love will eternaly stay so true
My heart will always go out to you
I await your return when you come for me
And together forever we will always be...
