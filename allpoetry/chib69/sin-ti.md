title: "Sin ti"

With out you,my life isn't the same
You gave me the drop of love and tore away my chain
The lonely days I dread
It's as if the world is dead

I feel like such a mess
All my nights are sleepless
In my arms is where you belong
Holding you there and hearing our song

I want that now,I want that night
When my heart felt great delight
Smile and wait
I am all your's to take

The skys shall clear
The stars will be near
We'll be together soon
And I will caress you under the moon

For now I wait
Till the night grows late
Never forgeting the words you say
Don't worry my love,"One day..."
