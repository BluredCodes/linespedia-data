Sick in bed
Aching head
Reflecting on all the things that were said
The events of the week have left me confused
I don't know what to belive
I don't know what to do
My heart wants to belive in you
But you havn't given me reason to
I miss you like crazy 
I want it to be intence again
Not hazy
Love isn't just something that is said
It's a feeling that should be expressed instead
Just please give me substance to find you 
Give me a reason to know you're true
Be honest and come through
I need to know 
Because love can only come from two
I bid you good night where ever you may be
I look forward to another day
Tomorrow I shall see
