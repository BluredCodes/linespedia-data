Dear mom and dad
If only you knew that you make me sad
You can't even see 
That you're losing me

With every day that goes by
This bitter hatred grows inside
I do everything for you
But you don't care about what I do

It sickens me that you say I can't have "too much fun"
I go out one day and you say the weekend is done
I hate you with eveything inside
Because all you do is keep me in a bind
 
You don't care to listen about whats on my mind
Because you are ignorant,stupid,and yes even blind
It's your fault that I get depressed
You don't let me talk to anyone and ask me why i'm stressed

I hope one day you understand why
Why I am someone who wants to die
Perhaps it is my fault because I didn't do what I should
Maybe one day i'll realize why you crushed everything you could
