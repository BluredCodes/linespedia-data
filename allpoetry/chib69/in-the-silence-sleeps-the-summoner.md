Touch my hand for you may feel something
Look in my eyes for there lies my soul
Feel my aura 
Hear for the stories that are told
Listen to the wind for in it are my emotions
Drink from the water for it is my potion
Enjoy the heat of fire
Embrace the earth and your desire...
