We've always talked about how our lives would never change
Reminiscing,Drinking coffe,and playing the latest video game
I can remember countless nights that we were there for eachother
You became my sister and I your brother

I knew the day would come
When we would part like the moon and the rising sun
But I also know the we have a bond
One that will stay very strong

Go on my friend
For I know that you will succeed in the end
Never forget what we have
You can always call me even for a laugh

Keep the memories alive
Don't let them die
Though you'll be gone i'll try not to cry
These words can't describe the fear inside
Because I don't want to ever say good-byeI'll miss you so much kiki!But I want you to go and do this for you.I love you and I hope that you find happiness!
