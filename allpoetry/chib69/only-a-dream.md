I sit out side 
In the coolness of the night
The stars and the moon 
Aid my tired sight
I look at my ring finger 
And see whats not there
I guess I am stupid
Why should I even care
Lucky are those 
Who have love to share
Taking a chance
Do I even dare
I can't wait
When the solitude shall end
I will have a greater heart
With love that can only ascend
For so long 
Life has kept me captured
But the day shall come 
When I will rapture
I am not afraid anymore 
Of any pain life can bring
For one day 
I will be strong enough
To stay safe in my wingsI havn't written in a while...
