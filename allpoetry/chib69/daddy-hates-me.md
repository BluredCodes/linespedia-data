---
title: "Daddy hates me"
writer: "Chib69"
slug: "daddy-hates-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

A hero,someone to look up to and follow in his foot steps
My father,someone cold and prejudice
He only said he loved me because it was what he had to do
But a secret i've kept from him would only bring me doom
I thought in my heart that love would carry through
But when I came out to him the love wasn't true 
He told me he hated me,I felt like such a fool
I didn't know what to say,I had no clue
I fell to the ground as my sadness grew
Telling my self "What else is new..."
