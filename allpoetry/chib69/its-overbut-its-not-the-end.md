I cared for you so much,this I can't deny
When you left me with pain,I felt like I could die
You once told me that you would never make me cry
But here I am wondering if it was all a lie
I told you how I felt and all you did was sigh
There was nothing I could do to make you change your mind
I gave you my heart and my virginity too
But there was nothing I could give to ever please you
I felt so alone like no one could care
Then I realized that you were never there
You may have something that was speacial to me
But in the end it's my heart that you will never keep
I hold my head up high and face a new day
I've learned to look at life a whole nother way
So my heart maybe broken,but it's nothing I can't mend
I realize "it's over,but it's not the end..."
