title: "A new me"
writer: "Chib69"

This will be the last time I am torn apart
I have no more life or love in my heart
You've all taken what was once so precious to me
You tell me you care But it's only to deceive
No more lies no more tears
Caring for anyone was a waste of my years
So I held back from slicing my skin
It dosn't matter anymore the cutting shall begin
Oh how it comforts me to feel its sting
It removes the stress and the pain within
Being alone isn't really that bad
I finally realize that I will always be sad
The harshness of life was hard for me to see
No one could care about someone like me
I wanna thank you for what you've done
You've given me closure and now I hide from the sun
Into the darkness my soul resides
Go on and continue with your happy lives
Leave me be
As you can see
I am picking up the pieces of my shatterd dream...
