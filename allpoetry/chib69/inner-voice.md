writer: "Chib69"

There is so much I want to say
But I can't 
Because things get in the way
What I think now 
Becomes yesterday

Like the river
Time flows
I want you to know
That I wont let you go

I am creature full of light
Walking in the darkness of the night
Through the fear
I hold my dreams tight

 
How can I let you know
Words alone can't even show
Just hold me for I am shaking
My heart is aching
Because it is breaking

Can you hear me...?
