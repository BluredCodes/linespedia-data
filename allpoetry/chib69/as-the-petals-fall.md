title: "As the petals fall"

And so it goes
Like the death of a rose
What was once thriving with hope
Now lies at the end of its rope
Life reaches beyond the bend
We live and laugh
Then it comes to an end
Nothing happy can last forever
Because pain and sorrow
Are in every endevor
Nothing sacred can keep you together
For fate tears you apart 
At a time called when ever
And when the curtain comes to a close
All that is left in the dark
Is a sweet red rose
