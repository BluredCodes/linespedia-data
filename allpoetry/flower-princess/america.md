America the home of the brave.
The land of the free wave.

America the feel of liberty,
With its life of eternity.

America shinning bright.
With the stars glemming at night.

America the sweet with her head up high.
The land of the free that will never die.My feelings about the United States of America after what happened on September 11th, 2001
