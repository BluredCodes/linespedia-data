title: "Under The Stary Night Sky"

As you hold me tight.
Under the stary night.

I gaze up into your eyes.
Shinning like the silver in the sky.

You place your kiss
upon my lips.

As i melt into your arms so strong.
Hearing the sound of an angel's harp strum.

You whisper into my ears.
How you love me so dear.

As we sit under the sky so high.
Enjoying the stars in the night sky.
