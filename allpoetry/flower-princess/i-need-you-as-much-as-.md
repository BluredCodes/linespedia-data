title: "I Need You as Much As. . ."

I love you as much as plants love water.
I need you as much as a baby needs its mother.

I want you as much as a child wants candy.
I miss you so much without you handy.

So, stay by me for you'll be fine.
Because sooner or later you'll be mine.
