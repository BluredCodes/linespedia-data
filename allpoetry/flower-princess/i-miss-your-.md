---
title: "I Miss your. . ."
writer: "Flower Princess"
slug: "i-miss-your-"

I miss your kindness of caring for me.
I miss your soft kisses that were always for me.

I miss the way your strong arms held me and make me feel like I'm protected from all evil.
I miss your handsome face with your smile so great.

I miss your spirit that grows around you.
I miss you so much without you here with me

We'll be together soon just you wait and see.
