As we walk in the soft beach sand

I hold your strong manly hand.



Enjoying the beautiful sunset

Makes me think about when we first met.



When I'm here with you 

There's always something new.



But as the birds fly by up in the sky.

I notice how the time has flown by.



With you here with me each and everyday.

I pray that you'll always be this way.
