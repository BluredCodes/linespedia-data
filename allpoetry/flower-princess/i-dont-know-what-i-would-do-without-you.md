title: "I Don't Know What I Would Do Without You!"

I don't know what I would do without your kindness.
I don't know what I would do without your smile shinning my way.
I don't know what I would do without your suprising warm hugs.
I don't know what I would do without the words you say to cheer me up.
I don't know what I would do without the way you look into my eyes.
I don't know what I would do without the way you kiss me and make me want to melt in your arms.
I don't know what I would do without those seven little words that mean you care, I miss you and I love you.
I don't know what I would do without you if you ever left me!
