---
title: "A painting"
writer: "Meowy"
slug: "a-painting"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As 
I set up
a white paper
of emptiness
I
pick up my brush
and dip it
in red...
a simple stroke...
here 
and there..
completed is my masterpiece
and titled it is..
"blood that fell 
from my wrists
once before.."
a painting 
it was...
but now..
it lives
