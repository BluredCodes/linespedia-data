---
title: "[If you only knew]"
writer: "Meowy"
slug: "if-you-only-knew"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7335-If-you-only-knew-by-Meowy"

If you only knew
what i'm capable of
you say you know me..
but you;ve never really known me
when you speak to me
not realizing what you say just flies
by me like butterflies
you hug me and say I love you
but what you dont know
is I dont feel it
I dont love you
I cant love you
its not in me
chain of life continues
dont bother
your pure absence 
is my friend
if you only knew
how your eyes
whisper to me
"i love you but I dont love you"
or so i thought
your flaws are almost sweet
like candy-coated chocolate
i relish in your incompetence
in living life
yours and mine
hold me now or hold your
silence
and forever be my enemy
