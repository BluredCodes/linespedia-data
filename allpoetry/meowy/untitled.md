mr..
                        benevolent
                        here i am
                        battling my battles all alone
                        no one to cry to...
                        hey...
                        hide behind all
                        the abandoned facades 
                        win the races of time
                        as i travel alone...
                        when 
                        I cant be found
                        push the search to the edges
                        of the fallen
                        vaults of the heavens
                        demands of the quiet rustling leaves
                        of tomorrows
                        pushes me over the edge
                        here i am...
                        walking upon the earth
                        with a 
                        benevolent end...
                        listen to me...
                        hey...
                        mine is the pain 
                        i suffer alone...
                        mine 
                        is the fallen rain
                        that hurts us all in the dark
                        starry night
                        a thousand miles to go
                        with only just
                        a moment 
                        to live...
                        remember me 
                        as what I
                        was..
                        not what i used to be...
                        there comes a time
                        when you drown so deep
                        break through the ice
                        the false shell of
                        this broken world
                        an impossible dream
                        i forgot 
                        the need to be free
                        is instilled deep inside within me
                        whether
                        it makes sense or not...
                        it makes a world of difference
                        in the pure
                        existence
                        of me and you...

                        KM 99
