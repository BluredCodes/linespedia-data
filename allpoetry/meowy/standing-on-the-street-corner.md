title: "[standing on the street corner]"
writer: "Meowy"

standing on the street corner
alone under the illuminating light
i softly weep...
of my own personal pain
thousand of lies look upon me through
the darkness, taunting eyes,
pointed fingers
kleenex soaked with tears...
the whole world is a stage
and im just a little bug upon it...
your lies blanket me...
with its decieving warmth
only to coil up and strangle me
with a greed unknown
i deny what they say i am...
i am not what they think i am...
prying eyes look upon me
silenced laughter
open-mouthed
nothing but quietness
a haunting quietness..
leave me be...
leave me alone...
stop.
soon the torture begins
the stones now fall upon me
i fall but
i do not shatter
the streetlight on the corner
finds me
cradles me in its arms
whispering
"Je plie et ne romps pas"
I bend but do not break...
You bend but you will not break...
my little light..
you quieten my 
tears...
you love me
completely...

(Je plie et ne romps pas. - I bend but do not break. - Jean de La Fontaine )the quote is by J.F. -- i liked this quote so much and i 
wanted to incorporate that it into a poem.. so thats why :)
