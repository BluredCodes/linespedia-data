---
title: "[Beyond the rain...]"
writer: "Meowy"
slug: "beyond-the-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

Beyond the rain...
beyond the pain
buried in my grape acid...
I feel so placid
only a toliet knows its home
under the dome
one can only hope
i can cope
cuz i dont wanna mope
cuz im sick with freezerburn
unlike you
