You came to me like a dreamyou words were kind, a fewnever did I think you would be mine...as i wonder whyyou do what you dosweet embracescall my namethrough the mirrors of lifei plucked the petals offthe dying rosei call home...who knew?who knew?you came to me like a dreamand you left me cold in the morning lightrumpled sheets, fallen shadesstill alone in thisshadowy room..quietness, whispering quietnesswho would have known?clouds formingway up high in the perfectskieswho knew?who knew you'd say good byeleaving me cold in the morning lightbathing yourselfin the warm afternoon brightyou came to me like a dreamperfect but not perfectionflawed but not flawlesswho knew?the morning dewdrops..the soft blades of grass...the weeping willow..they all knew..KM
