Lying in this shapeless room;        	wretched sheets that tame the eye.        		Thinking with time on my side                A blue pen that was red just        	a moment ago.        Sun and moon in their pattern-less        	shape, kissing in love, kissing        		in blue sea.                Tears in the wall.        A door that when closed,        	will open again to see        		if your mind has changed.         	Closed in defeat                Spiders crawl, leave ink behind.                 A door stopper that would        	probably break the door.                Head is heavy,        	sleep is further than the sun.          Sand between the toes of the damned                Pen moves fast, hand moves slow.        Thoughts go racing through the black        		sky noon.        Two dolls stare as I write,        	watching over me, I wait.        Wait to see what they will do next,         	if anything at allColors fly by faster than thoughts                Boys & girls, I swear that's what        they say.        			Black sheets turn white.....        					If you try.unknown,
