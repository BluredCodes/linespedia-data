title: "Noble Eagle"

Noble Eagle, many in our sky, 
naught but a tear in its eye. 
Searching for enemies far and near, 
to erase the nation's fear. 
 
Also ships off our coasts, west and east, 
watching for acts from a coward beast. 
Thinking they could watch our country fall, 
they don't know Americans at all. 
 
So much destruction within our land, 
retribution now near at hand. 
Blind revenge not in our eyes, 
just punishment as that eagle cries.
