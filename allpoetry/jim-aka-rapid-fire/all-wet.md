A litter of golden retrievers brought in to me, at the vet clinic where I work you see.  They needed inoculations and de-wormed, something that this client hadn't learned.  The pups all squirmed, they wouldn't hold still,  knowing which were done was some ordeal. I decided to dip my finger into some water, so getting them straight would be no bother.  When I had finished with number four, my client wasn't talking any more. She had been gabbing right from the start, now was just gazing at their chart.  When they were all done, she quietly said, 'having thoughts running through my head. About something I never realized, do all pups here get baptized?
