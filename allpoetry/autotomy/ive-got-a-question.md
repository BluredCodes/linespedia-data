I've got a question?
I hope you can answer it
I've asked a few souls about this
And got a different answer

This will only take a second
So sit back and relax
Put your mind into a state of readiness
For what I am about to ask...


Ready?

How's Poland?If you could answer me this question, AS YOUR COMMENT. I'd be eternally grateful.

thanks
