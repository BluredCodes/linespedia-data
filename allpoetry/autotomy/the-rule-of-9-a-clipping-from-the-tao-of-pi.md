Avoid the lies
Succumb to your memories
Relive the pain

Forget what you were told
Learn for yourself
Never believe the hype

Go to sleep
Wake up in a haze
Do it all over againmy nickname at work is Pi (as in 3.14 never ending number Pi) 
I've been into Taoism for a while. 

One day I just decided to write under the title 'the Tao of Pi'. Or the way of Pi. As a whole it makes no sense what so ever. Because I have not found my way through life just yet.  

In part is starts to make some sense. This is is one of those parts, a tid bit if you will. 

If it makes sense to you great awesome. If not no worries its my tao not yours =)
