You the huntress
And I the prey
Continuing this delicate ballet

You take your time tracking me
Finding the openings to my cave
Leaving your mark to frighten me
Knowing that I would attempt to evade

You the huntress 
And I the prey
Continuing on this intricate ballet

I have faced your kind before
And I shall face your kind again
You will not be able to stop me
Too bad that you shall be disappointed in the end

You the huntress
And I the prey
Deciding to skip the ballet

I refuse to be your trophy
For you have not earned the right
Hope you brought lots of ammo
For you shall not bag me tonight

You the huntress 
And I the prey
Knowing the season is over
