Hello my name is Johnny
I like it when you anger me
I can take it

Hello my name is Tommy
I like it when you control me
I can take it

Hello my name is Sally
I like it when you hit me
I can take it

Hello my name is Mandy
I like it when you depress me
I can take it


Emotional times I’m just fine


Why do they take it?
Johnny could verbally abuse his abusers 
Yet he does nothing

Why do they take it?
Tommy could lead himself to greatness
Yet he does nothing

Why do they take it?
Sally could fight back her assailants
Yet she does nothing

Why do they take it?
Mandy could make herself smile 
Yet she does nothingfight back for yourself that's the moral
