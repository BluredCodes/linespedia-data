the voice that is heard often
deafens its listeners over the years

the message fades

the voice that is seldom heard
carries the loudest meaning 

the message stays

simple 
eloquent

the message is altered

loud 
soft

the meaning is changed

whispers are violent
screams are silent

the message destroys

some make music
others make noise

the message forgivesI used my voice for the first time today
