---
title: "did you actually say freedom?"
writer: "Autotomy"
slug: "did-you-actually-say-freedom"
source: "AllPoetry"

I follow lost souls
Trapped in purgatory
They follow me as well

Grace – in their darkest hour, they could not be there
Angst – in their darkest hour, they could not be there
Charity – in their darkest hour, they could not be there
Misery - in their darkest hour, they could not be there

For themselves in their time of need
I am here for them
They are here for me
We are now a happy family

Lost souls followed me to my prison
Trapped in purgatory
I follow them as well
