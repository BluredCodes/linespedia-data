title: "You"
writer: "Autotomy"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You appear to display controlling behavior to some degree

You seem to have certain self-esteem problems.

You do not consider yourself worthy. 

You secretly suffer in your little corner.

You have some dependence issues. 

You depend solely on her for all your needs. 

You appear to be suffering at both an emotional and cognitive level. 

You are generally pessimistic when it comes to trust. 

You generally feel threatened.

You get worked up over minor things.

You get hurt.me is you...
...you is me
