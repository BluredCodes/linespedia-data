I'm looking at a photo
of something in the past
I see eight friends sitting on a step
five up front, three in the back
all very unique, all very scared

Dallas - football star - never liked violence, just wanted to play guitar

Curling - wasn't even supposed to be there - moved to BC to grow weed

PT. Dave's - thought the army would make him a man - decided that mind was better than muscle

Sexy - outspoken about everything - never meant to break up two friends who loved each other

Gap - no other letter existed than A - maybe discipline and authority will help him along the way

Book - happiness through 90's - she transformed into a woman gracefully, properly, the way she did not want to

myself - wanted nothing but to be accepted - decided individuality came through reality

Adidas - keep to herself - finally got over him, it took her awhile 

when you take a snapshot
you're not sure whats going to happen to anyone in that photo 
in a week, a month, a year
photographs are only good as a reminders
to remind you how things were
and who they turned out to beBurden of Atlas, and myself were in the photo that I was looking at tonight. The other people I do see every once and a while. Just wondering what happened to the others in the photo, that's what this is about
