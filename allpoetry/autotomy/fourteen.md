You have the nicest name anyone could think of 
graceful, powerful, playful 
it deserves respect and adoration
yet all you want is to be loved 
and to love in return
too bad that is not in season

Do you lie in wait? 
waiting for the day to begin
the same routine day in and day out
not knowing what else to say
not knowing what to believe in...

Are you tired of weeping in silence?
is this the cure that will save you and bring you happiness?
from this terrible disease
known as isolation

Something known as hopes and dreams 
get you through the day
hopes for friends to come around and bring a smile to your face
dreams for a lover to mean what they say...
then maybe this silence will end?

Although when your hopes and dreams are answered
thorugh the sounds of friends and lovers
you scream out prayers 
for the noise to cease
and the silence to begin again

This is not how you wanted to live
in self apointed exile 
yet it is the life you choose
the life you know
the life you loveWhen I read this I felt like a teenager again.
