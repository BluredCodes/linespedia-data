As I walk I notice things that usually I would not notice
Litter covers the streets like freshly fallen snow
Lovers walk amongst the heart broken
I ask my self, why can’t anyone clean up this mess

I continue on my journey notice the unnoticeable
The feeble live amongst the waste of the powerful
There are places for people like that to go, that’s why I pay taxes
I ask my self, why won’t anyone clean up this mess

Feeling a heavy weight on my heart I walk towards safety
When will someone put an end to all of this? 
This misery, this pollution, this society
I ask myself, why don’t I clean up this messPeople live amongst the garbage right besides a fortune five hundred office building. In the dead of winter, people live in others filth and consider this the only way. As I thought of this I got all bummed out and wondered why don’t I do something about it? Then I realized something... people only will do something if it directly affects them. This is why I’m a fucking asshole and went back to where I sleep... in my tower
