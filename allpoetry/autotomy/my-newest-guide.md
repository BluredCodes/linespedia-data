‘Uncle John’s orchestrating the rest of your life up there right now’

Aunt Kathy whispers this to my ear
As she smiles… holding back the tears
While we hold each other for comfort
Giving us the strength to go on
Destroying the emptiness 
That keeps us from falling apart

Sadness

Taken in his prime
God isn't the only one that knows 
What he left behind...
One wife
Two sons
A daughter to be…
And a grandson to come

Smile

Not all is wrong
All shall be well
Death is only the beginning
For this is only the end of one path

Flight

John has not left us
He has reached immortality
His soul, his essence lives on
Eternally

Forevermore

Now he has a new task
Given to him by god
To look down on those he love
And guide them through their journey
Known as life

Conductor

He shall orchestrate from above 
For all of us
As long as we keep him in our hearts 
And in our thoughts
John shall always be here to help us
Along our way
