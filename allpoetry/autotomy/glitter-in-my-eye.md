title: "glitter in my eye"

I miss you 
Its funny, I never thought I would
You said you’d be here for me 
Always
Yet when school came knocking 
The opportunities were unimaginable
Escape seemed possible

I could not blame you for running
Yet a part of you longed to stay here
With the rest of us
Together
With your family
With your friends
In your home town

I now face the same dilemma you once did
I never dreamt it would be my turn so soon
I’ve got the chance to get out
Escape
You always said I’d go far
Hoping that my dreams would come true
Now everything seems possible
