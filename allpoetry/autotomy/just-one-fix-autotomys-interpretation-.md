I Cannot sit still
I must purge myself of you
In order to keep a grasp on this life

How did it come to this?
When did I lose control?
I wasn’t always this way you know

I once lived like the others...

Until... 

I felt your presence
Your power...
Your essence...

It was like you had lifted the wool over my eyes
Had me wondering what would come next?
Every time I took you

I loved when you’d be in me
I never wanted to part with you
I couldn't deal with me off of you

The cravings...
Unbearable...

I became addicted

Others...
Had to try you
Had to feel you

This is the way of the past
The way of the present
The way of the future

Who are to judge others?

Untill we judge ourselvesI had so many different thoughts when I was writing this. 
where was my head at?
