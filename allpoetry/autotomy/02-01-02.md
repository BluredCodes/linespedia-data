Did you ever really notice that I went away
When you’ve lost everything
Do you realize? 
What you did was a mistake

I’m not around anymore
To hear the promises, you never kept

As I lay here wishing you’d call me
I do wonder what to say
I see your smile someday
Will I notice the ways you’ve change

I never whispered a thought 
I wouldn't have kept

I fell for the hype
That you spun my way
Altering my perception
On what you were trying to say

Did I ever really notice you never left
It makes no difference that I'm not around

Now I’m under the dream
At least for the moment
I'm over at your place
and I'm over the ragethanks to Nickelback for doing this cover.. the only song I couldn't sing along to!!
