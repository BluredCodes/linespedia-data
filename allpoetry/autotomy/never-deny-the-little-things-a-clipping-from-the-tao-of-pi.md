do not linger

show them your hate

take away your self-doubt

fears are to be expected

innocence has its price

life does not wait long

remember what you have learned

teach the others well

always have somewhere to go

never have a set time to be there

the road I travel is bumpy

there are many mountains to overcome

I do enjoy the feeling of climbing to the top

to look down the road I must face

and to glimpse back to show what I've already accomplished
