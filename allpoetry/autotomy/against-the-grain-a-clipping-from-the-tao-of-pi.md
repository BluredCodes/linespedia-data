as 
i close my eyes
i drift to the back
i sway to the side
i flow to the front
i am raised
i am lowered
yet 
i do not move?

will
i feel like moving
i want to get going
i urge myself to carry on
i need to run away 
i do not move
i do not cower
yet
i remain in this place?Just like with any piece of unfinished wood
If you glide your hand against the grain 
You’re going to get a splinter in your hand 
Trust your gut feeling
For it is the way
