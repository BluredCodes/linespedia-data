title: "Dethroned and alone"

Sorry for not calling you 
I said that I would
I've never did though 
you're phone number is the same still isn't it?
Not like I'd forget it 
I've dialed it so many times, over the years
things have changed 
they won't go back to what they were
you knew that before we started this.
You did know that before you started this?
I knew that I would come out ahead
We're doing fine now, thanks for asking
My parents don't know, so please don't tell.
Lets just pretend a while longer?

I always thought that you'd be around forever 
After a few days it would be just like before
Like nothing had ever happened
you'd call me up and say it was all in my head
Its a shame dreams don't come true
only nightmares
