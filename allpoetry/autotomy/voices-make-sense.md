This is only what you want to hear

This is only what you want to hear
You want to hear

This is only what you want to hear
You want to hear
Want to hear 

This is only what you want to hear
You want to hear
Want to hear
To hear

This is only what you want to hear
You want to hear
Want to hear
To hear
Hear


Lies
The lies
Believe the lies
Yourself believe the lies
Listen to yourself believe the lies

The lies
Believe the lies
Yourself believe the lies
Listen to yourself believe the lies

Believe the lies
Yourself believe the lies
Listen to yourself believe the lies

Yourself believe the lies
Listen to yourself believe the lies

Listen to yourself believe the liesWAIT there's MORE!
