goats love parsnipp
they want you to feel at home with them
parsnipp is better than sex

goats control Crapford's parsnipp industry 
they take there time, basking in its vegtable based glow
parsnipp is better than religion

goats hate llama's
they'll murder every last one of those devil worshiping bastards!
parsnipp only pretends to be dead on your plate

goats throw pineapples at Mr. Cuddles, the inventor of insomnia
they'll throw one at him for you, if you let them
parsnipp is better than youMr. Cuddles is REAL

he has feelings too
