What shall you do?
When the clock strikes noon?
And you have only hours until
The end of the world
Is upon us all???

If you could plan for it
Would you be inebriated?
Too drunk to care
So high you wouldn’t mind?

If you had no idea of what is to take place
That you wouldn’t know what hit you..
Relax it shall be over soon
For the end is always fast approaching

I say we have a choice!
Let us all get on our knees and pray
Pray for a party!!!
So massive...

No one will see it coming
Not even the lord all mighty would know 
That the end of the world
Is only the intermission*its too much, I drank too much* spoken in an 73 year old drunken Hungarian Canadian from Headsmashedinbuffalojump Accent!

Yes folks there really is a town called that...
