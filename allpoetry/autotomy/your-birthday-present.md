Oh rat boy, how you are despised!!!
Listening to your Tom Petty albums
Singing no I won't back down
Making up the words as you go along!!
You’re a real backstabber 
Aren’t you?
Aren’t you? 

I ripped my sock
Damn that knife!!!
A pair became single
Lying in the tomb 
Sock-less as usual

praying to the gods of old
that someone
SOMEWHERE
Will save me a sliver of birthday cake

Who needs a drink?
A TOAST to the martyr of the day!!
For it is she that shall get paid
In unholy motor oil

At the strike of three
Then it shall all be told 
We knew her so
In a time long, long ago
When you were known as foethis goes out to that special someone for NOT inviting me to their birthday party... Better have saved me a slice of cake

happy birthday!
