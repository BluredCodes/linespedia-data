Jenny seems to drift farther away
The slower I seem to walk away
My hands are tied, is all she would say 

Tradition and pride
Keep this town alive
Desperation brings us all isolation

Jenny won’t recall those memories
Only stating that she can no longer handle this town
Blade in hand, she will forget it all

I am no longer around
Whispers fill the town
Questions are answered in the schoolyard

They all know about you Jenny
I can no longer hold my ideas of escape high
My hope has died, only to be replaced with sorrow

With your last breath, you decided to phone me
Even in death you looked out for me
Although you can’t come with me, at least now I know you’re free

Your words that day..
Will always stay
