I tear my hair out 
When you are here
What’s wrong with me
You should run away in fear 

Emotions are awakening 
Deep within my soul
What’s going on with me? 
As the fear takes hold 

Fast is not the way to go 
I want to feel the joy 
As I did once before 
Best I take it slow 

Can’t restrain my heart from these feelings 
Can’t restrain my fists from this beating 
You have no where to go

Afraid 
What’s going on in me? 
How long can you last 

Unfair to you, I restrain from the hit
I have my own reasons 
For you to be hurt
The only questions that remain reside in your own heart

Swimming in confusion 
Not knowing where to hide 
To be safe from these feelings 
That burden my soul 

You can not make me feel secure 
You can not melt my heart 
The only thing you can do is to taunt this onslaught on 
To the brink of chaos… and beyond

Can’t restrain my heart from these feelings 
Can’t restrain my fists from this beating 
You have no where to go

Afraid 
What’s going on in me 
This punishment shall never end 

The feelings do swell up inside 
Yet I have not yet felt the urge to burst 
Although I see from the looks of you 
That I have already brought the hurt
 
A face-to-face encounter 
Is what I have been praying for 
The phone calls were not enough 
For what I had in store for you

Worry not my defeated brethren 
For all of this was indeed your fault 
Just a little insight
