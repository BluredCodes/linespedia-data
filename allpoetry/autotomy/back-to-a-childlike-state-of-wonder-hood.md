Curl up into your land of isolation
Give up on the world that gave you the cold shoulder
Eject the world that rejected you
Siphon the loneliness out of your soul
Replace it with the warmth of identification

Identify who you are
Reject what they want you to be
Spiral out

As a cocoon of darkness covers your soul
Relive the trauma done to your broken body
Accept the failure that you were
Embrace the emptiness of what is to come
Can you hear the world spewing lies

Identify what you want
Reject what they offer
Spiral out

Silence surrounds you in every possible angle
Destroy the ideals of the old
Free your mind to find the truth
Break the cycle of lies that were feed to you 
Refill the cup of faith that was spilt, so long ago

Identify with yourself
Reject the hatred
Spiral out 

Escape the void that was your home
The place of rebirth, the place of solitude
It is time to evacuate, back into the real world
Take your last breath of fresh air
You’ll soon be free againThis world made no sense to us when we were children, and that’s the way we liked it! There was more to explore on your own, more to find out on your own. The possibilities were endless. That’s what I’m aiming for this time around. I’m almost there too
