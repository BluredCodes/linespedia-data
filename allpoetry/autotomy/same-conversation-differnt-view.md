rest
by the woods
its quiet here
get some sleep
then people came
shouted wake up
can't stay here
what right do you have 
to say that
no one lives here
yet everyone goes here 
from time to time
to seek peace
within themselves

too tired to move
this looks like a spot 
whats that noise
someone is here already
leave now
you've rested enough
go get a job
you question my reasons
to be here
your not the owner
you just show up
every now and then
to do whatever you do
to yourself
