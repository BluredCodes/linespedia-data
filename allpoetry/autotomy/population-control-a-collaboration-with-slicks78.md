They rounded up all the rowdy young men
From ages fourteen to ten
Sent them to the 'factory'
To be test dummies, you see

Strip of all their dignity 
To be left flapping in the breeze
Nurses handed them capsules
So the population would be increased

Take the blue pill and you will see
How happy these young men shall be
Not the red one though, how did that old saying go?
One of them makes you taller...the other one makes you smaller

Let back out into society
To see if the experiment shall succeed
When suddenly out of nowhere
They feel a sudden tease

Faces show signs of shock
Momentary body lock
Must find a girl, a quite place
To drop their pants to their knees

Boys chase after women
Young and old
Anywhere they feel necessary
That they can fill a hole

We’ll send out spies, with watchful eyes
To see how the population control worked out
What havoc took its toll, on the girl next door,
He took her there, took on the kitchen floor

Lustful desires make them both perspire
Flat on her back, sliding around on the mat
Old as she could be, She screams she yells YIPPEE!
Oh the humanity, the young man is doing a granny
 
Suddenly he stops the drug has worn off
Next time she has a headache
So that's how it goes, when insane minds take their toll
