The girl with the breasts
Wants everything to do with you
Yet nothing to do with herself

I want to live with a longhaired girl
Who could connect with my soul
Just by looking into my headlights

You crave adventure through mishaps
Then get going while accidents are plenty
Could my gift be I’m accident-prone

The boy with the look
Wants everything to do with your friend
Yet nothing to do with yourself
