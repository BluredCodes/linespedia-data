title: "Is there anything outside the window?"
writer: "Autotomy"

time to take flightsee the world through my eyestime to see what I have seen all alongthis place, where I'm from... its not the big picturemerely a pixel, a part of the puzzleit is where I am now thoughfour walls grinding gently into each other never quite letting one have more space than the othersyet each wall gives the other walls the respect they deservea roof, and a floor are next on the agendathe floor is cold all day, all night, same temperaturethat's why someone invented fuzzy pink bunny slippersthe roof is just there you see it, it sees youyet neither one fully comprehends the others place in the universeI have a windowone day I'll shoot something or someone through itif anything gets to close to the wallsthere is a door...its too far away thoughand all it does is make noise anyways!I've got a closet that has beer in itand clothes, and memories from the pastand my Britney Spears limited edition Doll!  then there is the bedI feel like Jabba laying on ittoo many pillows I guessI've only scratched the surface of this universethen again the younger generation needs something to explorecan't do everything for them!
