title: "pushit"
writer: "Autotomy"

your hands move like shadows...
wandering over my body
the sensation of fear starts...
it enters my mind a thousand times a second
I am terrified

you ready yourself to pounce... 
when opportunity rears its ugly head
primitive feelings come over me
run away! hide! this is too much for you... 
If you stay, you will die 

yet I stay with you 
reasons why are unknown to me...
why I stay with you 
terrified...
of what may come

you have alot of nothing to say
I hang off every word anticipating... awaiting... hopeing.. 
that the truth will come forth from your mind
and the mystery of you
will be solved
