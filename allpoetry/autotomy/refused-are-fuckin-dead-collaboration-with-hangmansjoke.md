You say you want to live forever
Yet you’ve got all the time to kill

On your grave I made a wish
You beneath me is such bliss

You are gone, you haunt me no more
This is the day I have waited for

I lay dead daises next to your tomb
And remind myself the pain ends soon

One last look to your final resting place
Piece of mind comes after everything’s erased

I take a deep breath and look to the sky
I’m glad you are gone, yet I still cryI'm sad to see this great band gone!!!
