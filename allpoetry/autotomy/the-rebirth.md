title: "The Rebirth"

You could take my heart and soul
You could have my hand to hold
Why’d you have to take advantage of me?

Don’t make me beg for the past
You took my fragile hand to have
You took my love, my heart, my soul

Is that not what you wanted from me?

You could have my heart and soul
You could take my hand to hold
Why’d you have to take advantage of me!

Don’t make me beg for the past
You took my fragile hand to hold
You took my heart, my love, my soul

Hold on to the lesson I have learned

Can I have my heart and soul?
Could I have my hand back to hold?
So no one will be able to take advantage of me

The past is in my memories
I only see you in my dreams
All I ask is to be free
From vices you left for me
All your advances are denied
Everything you are is a lie
