Standards cannot be altered for you
The selection process is too difficult
You’re not big enough for this job
Size matters this time 
And unfortunately you just do not meet the requirements
That are necessary to complete this course

Yet you keep studying
Working towards that golden prize
Cramming the night before as they say
Putting in the extra credit assignments without questioning any of it
All for me
Why?Its about L-O-V-E
