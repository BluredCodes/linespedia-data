title: "New remedies to a new emotion"
writer: "Autotomy"

How could she do this...
to me 
to you 
to us all

Yet some consider her great
a goddess
a savior
why?
are they blind?
she's a repeat offender 
likely to do it again
and again

I feel as though...

I cannot forgive 
I cannot trust
I cannot love

You on the other hand
you feel other emotions that I have already had a taste of

You feel cheated
you feel bitter
you feel anger

I do not pretend to know how you feel right now
I only know how I feel...

shocked
confused
vengeful
hopeful

I ask ALL that read this
one simple question

How can one do this to two?
that is what I ask you
How can one do this to two?If anyone has ever been cheated on before. Have you ever wanted to have a conversation with other person? The one that you were left for. What would you say? How would you feel?

That's what this is about
 
you know who you are... just remember they have your soul, but you have your heart. And you can always buy another soul
