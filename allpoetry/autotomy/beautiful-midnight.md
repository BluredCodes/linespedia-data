At the end of the ring wars 
when clarity enters your mind
I'll be waiting for you

Looking out for your idea of suburbia
when all hope is left behind
I wait for you

See baby I got something right
i fell in love
While waiting for you

Drag me down
to where you feel safe and sound
I want to feel the scratches beneth the surface

Second hand is my favourite 
time never does stands still though
And I could wait no more

Part of me still waits for you 
at the dawn of Beautiful Midnight
I wait
