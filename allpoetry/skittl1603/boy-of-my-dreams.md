title: "Boy of My Dreams"

As I am walking in the cold
with tears streaming down my face
All that I can think of is you
Your eyes
Your smile
Your voice
Your laugh
No one can feel the cold
that is wrapped around my heart
when thoughts of you flood my mind
My heart is cold
because you aren't there
You're only in my mind
The boy of my dreams
that can't be found
here on this dull earth
I try to remember you
But my body chills
because I'm forced to remember
that you are only
a figment of my imagination
Boy of my dreams...
