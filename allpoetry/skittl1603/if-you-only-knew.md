I sit here and put on a fake smile
For anyone who cares
Maybe the smile will disguise the tears
that are falling from my eyes
My throat is dry and I can't speak
Can't tell you how I feel
The words are in my eyes
but you can't see them through my tears
The blame should be placed on me
for I have done this to myself
Messed up yet again
and become another lost soul
Just another girl in the crowd
That puts on that fake smile
That others think is genuine
If you only knew...
