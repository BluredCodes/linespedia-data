title: "Heartbreaker On My Own"

I hear your words
Yet they don't sink in
They just run straight through
And all this confusion
It makes my head spin
You must be thinking
"She's brain dead"
I cry and think
Yet I just can't see
What I am doing so wrong
My emotions are so blind
This heartache I have
Feels so strong
This dream is unkind and cruel
When will I begin to see
That the only heart I will ever break
Is the one that belongs to me
His heart my tear
Just at the seems
But mine is the one
That will fall to the floor
And won't be mended or torn again
By the other heartbreakers
In this life
I'm a heartbreaker 
On my own
