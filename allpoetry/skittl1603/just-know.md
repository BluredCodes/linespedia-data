title: "Just Know"

You sit and stare
Act like you don't care
Your eyes are full of hate
Is my caring hand too late
Can I get to you
Try to help you through
The times in your life
The ones of pain and strife
Your heart is black
Emotions are what you lack
I reach my hand out
And your eyes search about
You push me away
And I wait for the day
When you know that I'm here
For You.
