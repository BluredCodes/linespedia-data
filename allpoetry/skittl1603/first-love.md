It was love
The first time I saw you
It was love
The first time I heard your voice
It was love
The first time I felt your embrace
It was love
The first time your hand entertwined with mine
It was love
The first time your lips brushed my cheek
It was love
The first time you pushed my hair out of my face
It was love
The first time you gently kissed my lips
It was love
The first time I stared into your eyes
It was love
The first
The last
Every time
It was loveI know it's repetitive, but it shows how I feel
