Tell me no
Don't let me keep going through this with this stupid boy.
Tell me no
Make my tears stop falling from these sorrowful eyes.
Tell me no
Make my heart stop racing when I hear his voice.
Tell me no
Don't let me fall victim of his shameless ploy.
Tell me no
Keep my heart from breaking all over again.
Tell me no
Step these tears
Stop these emotions
Stop...
Just tell me no.
