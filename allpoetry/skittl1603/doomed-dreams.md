---
title: "Doomed Dreams"
writer: "Skittl1603"

I drift to sleep
To escape my thoughts of you
But your presence is  still there
I close my eyes
My world turns black
And I jump into my realm
My realm of dreams
Thoughts of you
Crowd the space
That normally is so bare
My escape from you
Yet again has vanished
The feelings feel so fake
In my dreams
Why are you there?
My safehold from you is gone
How do I get it back?
I'd give away my feelings
If I could do just that
For my dreams were mine
And mine alone
Until you somehow
Found your way into there
But these feelings last
Day and night
And stay with me through and through
So I might as well let them stay
And see them right to their doom
