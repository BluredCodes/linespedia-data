title: "Crying Walls"

This house is crying,
breaking apart
The tears from within,
pull at my heart
I try to heal the wounds,
caused by years of pain
It's too much for me,
too much loss, no gain
The tears can't be stopped,
this house can't be saved
My emotions are tired,
and shall be sent to the grave
Just stop the fighting,
calm my fears
What will it take
To hasten my tears?
