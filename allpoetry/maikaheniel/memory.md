this music... 
floating 
about my ears
waves of delight
can't help but smile
as it takes me back
before the dawn of time
when life was...
naive.

never tasted
love so true
never imagined
a world without him...
a boy
a man
a person forgotten 
by their own heart
a taste on my lips unwelcome
such love...
a dream of all dreams
his lie.

sweet warmth
returning to my mind
sane... healthy... fresh
a life forgotten
a soul discarded
like an abandoned friend
returning to share the warm times again
unfathomed happiness
treasured luck
to stumble blindly
upon a forgotten love
and lost memories

friendship comes
friendship goes
family falls and life continues
but their touch...
their scent....
their sound...
the songs
it remains.
holding us through the darkness
of the night
cradling us
from our fears
protecting what remains
and fueling what grows
beyond the stretch
of time.hehe. I was going through some of my old CDs when I happened upon my old Savage Garden CD. I popped it in and it suddenly reminded me of an old love and an old friend... and of a lot of happiness. It's amazing how your senses can make you remember so many things! ^-^
