(inspired by Van Gogh's "Starry Night")

I awoke this midnight
and touched the sky
as it twisted the colors
escaping from the surface of the sun,
capturing a breath of Starfire
within my hands.

And as the wind,
in fuming anger leaped
about a sky of dusk,
awakened in its wake
the electricity of man,
danced between
the ruffled trees
hidden by the heaven's light.

Trembling earth shifted
beneath my patterned shoe
as I stole away my treasure
to admire on my own
before the gods begin to thunder
and strip before my eyes
a Starry Night not seen since
before the dawn of time.
