Sweet beautiful mortal thread 

take my hand and we shall dance until sun rises... then we shall fly away to where it is dark and dance once again... until the world ceases to revolve and the sun melts away...

until the end of eternity... until the cease of time...

Mortal child embrace this kiss of death and walk upon these foreign shores

chase the wind and kiss the starlight... 

sing to the angels and pray to the god of all creation....

take my hand this instant, or it may all slip away...

the rapture of my touch... feel beauty in my death... in my lifeless being and come to me

dance....

and we shall be one under these sullen skies

dance... and we will forever be

Reflections of life... in a mirror of death

Visions of beauty... caskets of immortal anguish and fire...
emotions that stir like the sands of storms...
