title: "I'm Sorry Sarah-Jean"

I want to say i'm sorry for
The way that I chose to ignore
I didn't go and talk to you
Because of this I feel so blue
And when you look at me so sad
It makes me feel even more bad
'Cause in my mind I only see
That you're like this because of me
And so because of this I say
In hopes that you'll forgive one day
I'm sorry for not showing care
I'm sorry for hitting you
That's why I feel I had to share
These words for you from deep in me
But will you accept my apology?PLEASE forgive me hunny!!!
