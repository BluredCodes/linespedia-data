title: "~ Rare Blood"

rare blood 
slithers through me 
 typed RH negative 

a flash flood 
of unknowns 
spills out in these poems 
in a blink of burgundy 

my pen is lazy, 
raw in its form, 
the words, like scabs torn off 
won't stop bleeding 

antibodies 
feed on each other 

the page, a grave 
for indecent burials 
black dirt, hurt 
the tears of a widow 

vultures circle 
overhead 
as what lies dead 
in a puddle of blood 

twitches on the paper
