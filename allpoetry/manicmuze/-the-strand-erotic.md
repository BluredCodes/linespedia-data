title: "~ The Strand (Erotic)"

she imagines 
if he rolled around in her
stirred slowly
toes in sand

she would thicken, turn to cream,
drip between his fingers
on his hands

stretch the moment
over August
the heat of summer
the ocean breeze

touch each other
silken mist,
then turn over, tender kiss,
please,
unbutton this
she whispers
