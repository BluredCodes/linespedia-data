We're different
As night, day, all shades of grey
We don't always agree
And we don't think the same
...Isn't it a miracle

Like a rainbow 
Is made up of all color
All levels of intensity
Brightness and clarity
It is in the combination
We become prismatic

We are given choice
We are given voice
We are given reason
...Like magic

Life isn't only pretty
Or acceptable
Sometimes it's obscene
Even objectable
...But we breathe the same air

And we share the same 
Loneliness when we're desperate

...Aren't we fortunate
This individuality
Is the ingredient of human kind

And only with maturity
Do we learn what is appropriate
And what is insecurity starving for attention
The power of the mind
The power of the word

This, 
Is reality

And we, 
Like writers, 
Made curious of it all
