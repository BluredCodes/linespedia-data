---
title: "~ I Read Him and Think"
writer: "Manicmuze"
slug: "-i-read-him-and-think"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22752---I-Read-Him-and-Think-by-Manicmuze"
---

I read him and think-
of when love abounds,
little girl searching-
he carried me home,
along riversides,
across maiden springs,
little girl searching-
a whispering ring,
I read him and say,
in ‘hope for morrow’,
a gentle verse sings

I read him and think-
of ‘thanks not given’,
when home was a poem-
he showed me the way-
‘two little socks’ cry,
and I am a tear,
when home is a poem-
hung idle to dry,
I read him and say
‘Broken Man’ of dreams
stand, be seen shining

I read him and think-
poetic at heart,
a woman once chained-
he carried the rain-
to wash it away,
‘human progression’,
a woman unchained-
so softly defined,
I read him and say,
‘Exquisite’ his mind,
Wisdom beyond time.

I read him and think-
his thoughts undisguised,
I was a poet-
he lifted my soul-
above waters hole,
articulate rhyme,
I am a poet-
his voice lingers true,
I read him and say
‘meaning of my life’,
never forgotten.
