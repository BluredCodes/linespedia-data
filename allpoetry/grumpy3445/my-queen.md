You are the queen of my heart
the ruler of my desires
if you but ask
I would storm the gates of Hell for you
no pain is too great
nor sacrifice too much
above all beauty, do you reign
your face, beyond compare
eyes like pools, that reflect the moonlight
and the loveliness of your soul shines through
like the sun, behind the clouds
all my words are solely to give you praise
to lavish all my attention
upon the splendor that is you
my eyes have no purpose
but to bare my soul to you
only with your tender attention
will your humble servant remain enslaved
and with your gentle love
will this realm of ours prosper and growTo Jeca
John
