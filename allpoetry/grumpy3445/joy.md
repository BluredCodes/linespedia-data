Joy; my heart sings when I think of you
How can I resist your beauty, your charm
or your grace
I sense a fierce independance,
and striking intelligence
that attracts my intellect
your sense of humor, your wit, energy and your fun
that draws my attention
but it is your tenderness
your passion and romantic side
that draws my heart
to you I am joyfully drawn
the wonder of your memory will sustain me
though the walls of darkness be all around
and my enemies at my back
they will not be able to stop 
the singing of my heart
