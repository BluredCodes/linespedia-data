title: "Free"
writer: "grumpy3445"

Laughing, she eludes his grasp
she wants to be chased, but not caught
the fire in her eyes shows her intelligence
her sweet voice and tender touch, her heart
she enjoys living, and plays hard
her laugh comes easily and often
her heart will be given freely
but has to be earned
Laughing, she is caught
