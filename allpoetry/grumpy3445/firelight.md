In the flickering light from the fireplace
we sat and shared our thoughts
from our hopes, and fears, our memories
our darkest thoughts, and wants
we looked into each others soul
and found there shared desire
In our caress and tenderness
we joined in heart, and soul, and mind
A brand new love, a new life, to share forever more
Who knows what the future brings, but this we both share
a hope that someday our grandchildren will say
"They were truly one"Jeca, I think about you always.
John
