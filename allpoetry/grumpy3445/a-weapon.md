Do you know the torture, the pain that I endured?
in the process of going from young man, to Marine
they stripped away my very soul, every essence of me
and in return they left, a fierce, and violent warrior

pain was just a method, another tool, 
an environment to be endured
that is why in every war, 
the Corps comes out the victor

not many men can long endure, 
the pressure of that job
because the sacrifice, not just physically
but the shear act of trying to block all emotion
is greater than most can withstand

I am a grown man now, and my feelings have returned
I do not hide my tears, nor feel shame in love
because of having stood the test
my sacrifice has been repaid, tenfoldveterans, I know you will identify with this.
