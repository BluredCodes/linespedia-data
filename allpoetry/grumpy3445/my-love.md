My love is freely given
I make no demands
there are no criticisms
I do not keep score
am not envious
nor jealous, or controlling
I do not give it lightly
and my commitments are for a lifetime
sometimes it burns brightly
and at times, is a smoking coal
but it is warm, and giving, never falters
if you leave, I will thank you
for the brief moment you shared with me
and give praise to God for the gifts
which have enriched my life
for the good times and the bad
growth and strength
comes from pain and suffering
my heart contains both love
and heartbreak
