In the morning
when the sunlight
sneaks through the shades
the light highlights your hair
and makes your cheeks glow
with a soft, gentle light
It's at this time
that my eyes mist with tears
for the joy you have brought to my life
I run my hand through your hair
then kiss your cheek
and quietly leave for work
my whole day is filled
with your wonderful love
and in this way
I'm in heaven
