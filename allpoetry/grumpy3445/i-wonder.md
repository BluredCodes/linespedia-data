I wonder, when you are with me
does your heart beat feel like it is driving through your chest
or do you feel it skip, every time I smile at you
would you yearn to learn every curve of my face
by touch, and caress
when you see me, does your skin come alive
like flames are dancing down your spine
is your every waking thought of me
of the last moment you saw me, and just waiting
for the next moment in my arms
do you have trouble sleeping, tossing and turning
needing to have your arms around me
do your lips quiver, needing to feel mine
when I look into your eyes
because if this is how you felt
it would only be half as strong as I felt for youEnjoy,
John
