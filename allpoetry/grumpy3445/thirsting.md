My soul is thirsting
looking for a drink of you
in the heat of my passion
I only find myself
in the heat of your passion
my spirit is held
my mind a slave
I am obsessed by you body
by the simple, gentle touch
of your hand along my spine
the fever of my desire haunting
keeping me from disturbing sleep
so I am incurably drawn to you
to be another possesion
discarded and lifeless
when you lose interestEnjoy,
John
