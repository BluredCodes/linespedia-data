Duty calls
the uniforms go on
the soldiers fall into formation
I do my job, giving orders
wearing the stripes of a sergeant
I do my duty, to God and Country
fulfilling my responsibilities
while I look the part
a hardened veteran, many years I've served
but deep inside, I have my doubts
afraid to let it show
my eyes are weary, my heart ignored
I must remain untroubled, certain
confident and proud
I must not let my concentration falter
for my young soldiers are counting on me
