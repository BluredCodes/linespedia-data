She waits for him
waits for him to gently hold her
to look into her eyes,
for the sweet, gentle kiss that only he can give
waiting for his sweet words
that lets her know he loves her
and she does not wait in vain
for gently, he holds her
sweetly kissing
softly whispering those endearing words
and her anger melts like an early spring
melting the snow
which is the sign for the flowers to bloom
and so, she holds him
gently, she kisses him
and whispers those words...Enjoy,
John
