title: "My heart is heavy"
writer: "grumpy3445"

My heart is heavy
from the weight, and the wait
to let you free, yet knowing you are so close
my soul cries out for your touch
and yet you are afraid of that touch
you fear the passion, frightened to lose control
so I leave you, hoping you might chose me
to touch me, want me in return
but in my loneliness, I lose sight
wanting to fall off the knife's edge
to cling to someone I do not love
which might, for a moment, make me forget
and in so doing, lose the very thing I love dearest
that knife is sharp, and cuts to my soul
but I must stand, balance, keep my heart in one piece
because I know, when you accept the warmth,
the love, and the joy
I must be there, my heart complete
