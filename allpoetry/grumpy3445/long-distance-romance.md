When I talk to you on the phone
do you know how I long to kiss your lips?
Just hearing your sweet voice
sends shivers down my spine
I long to look into your eyes
and to lose myself in their depths
my hands, longing to stroke your skin
to tenderly caress your face
gently bring your lips to mine
arms, begging to hold you
to feel your body, held against my chest
heart, yearning to show you my passion
on these long winter nights, hold you
by the fire, the heat from our love
matching the heat of the flamesJeca, I hope this brings a smile to your face for a moment, at least.
John
