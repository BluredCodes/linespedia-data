Do not pull me into the darkness
or tempt me with your empty madness
an insanity that corrupts
turns love into hate
hope into despair
for I have laughed at death
and in so doing, love life
I choose the light
and the sun
to live with the birds
joyfully singing
destroying the darkness
that clouds my soulI wrote this while watching Apocolypse Now (whole uncut version).  I thought the parts which were supposed to be horrible, were rather tame, by todays standards.  It would have been better if someone who had actually tasted the horror of war, directed it.  I did empathize with the characters falling into despair, but I rejected that despair.
John
