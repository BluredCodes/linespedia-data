Alone, he desired what he did not have
being too caught up with his passion
he did not think of what he had
his sole thought was of her
and the soft light
high-lighting her soft curves
his skin, on fire
thoughts, beat with his pulse
soul, lost to a lust that could not be satisfied
single-mindedly, he did not see his danger
alone he died, forsaking all those who loved him
for the sake of one he could not have
