Into the morning I go, confidently
knowing you are there
I can face the pressures of work
the hassle of traffic
arguments with the relatives
presents for the kids
repairs on the cars
bills that need to be paid
groceries for the kitchen
and all the other distractions
that prevent me from thinking
only of you all day
for without you, I would have no meaning
there would be no point for confidence
you are my friend, my advisor, my soulmate
my lover, my doctor, my partner, and in all ways
the center of my life
