In my dream, I hold you
I caress you, and kiss your sweet lips
make love to you, and cling to you
till the sun sprinkles the clouds with light
reflected from a million drops of water
covering the grass
in my dream, I smell your perfume
and the sweat of your body
mingled with the morning flowers
the gardenias, and roses
also the damp soil, and the fresh cut grass
in my dream, I hear you sigh in your sleep
and I hear the neighbors getting their children ready for school
farmers, driving their tractors in the field
a rooster, welcoming the sun
and only in my dream do I find happiness
for that is the only place I find youthis was my dream for you, jeca
John
