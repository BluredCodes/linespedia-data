There is a scream
a cry of fear and pain
smoke and flames fill the air
into this hell he goes
up the stairs
into this hell to help and save
his discipline and will
fight his fear
and when he has saved one life
back into the inferno he must go
suddenly, the walls collapse
he has a brief moment to call for help
then he is lifted up into the light
he is held in the arms of God
his name is called
he will be forever safe
because he has done his time in hell
