My thoughts turn to you
I smile, for you make me smile
with you, I can be playful, free
chase you around the yard
and laugh till it hurts, when you tackle me
then, hand in hand, quietly, tenderly
listen to your worries, look into your eyes
and hear your thoughts
I know what you are thinking, before you think it
when the world seems against me, you right it
tenderly touching my face
helping to keep the dark away
even apart, I am with you, and you with meEnjoy,
John
