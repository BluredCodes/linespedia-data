I look into the night sky
the stars I see, Orion
the Seven Sisters,
the Big Dipper
and Polaris
the fatal North Star
alas, that is where my heart is
why must I always want something beyond my reach
and if I were to put my arms around it
would it vanish into air
why can't I keep my eyes closer to the earth
so that I don't stumbleJeca, you are always just beyond my reach
John
