I am not worried
I am a man who lives in the desert
yearning for the rains to bring the flowers
or a man in a frozen wasteland
wishing for the kiss of spring
or a man in the humid tropics
longing for the dry, cool air of the mountains
or a man who lives in poverty
lusting for wealth
or a man who is starving
craving for a bite of bread
or a man who works, long, hard hours
aching for sleep
and so, I will waitMy first poem.  I wrote this spontaneously, for a beautiful lady I know.
