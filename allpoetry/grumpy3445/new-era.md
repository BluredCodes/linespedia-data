The Era of Romance is now
when the darkness seems to overwhelm us
and is held in check by the sacrifices of a few
when people cling to each other
and are more concerned about their children
then their own selfish desires
when those who give their lives
inspire others to serve
when people are willing to follow their hearts
and take risks, they, in the past, only dare
when Love is held in higher regard than wealth,
power, or position
when the families of our enemies
are not left to starve
when there are things worth risking
more important than our lives
when men and women share their love
and not for their own hedonism
so thank you Lord, for giving me trials
because the darkness ushers in the Light
