Since I starting falling in love with you
do you know how happy I've been?
from the moment I surrendered to the joy
to the feelings that overwhelm me
A rapture pure, and bright
like the light of God
blows all the shadows from my heart
a smile that stays on my face
lifting the frown that had too long been fixed there
the whole world seems new, like I have been born again
I can truly enjoy every moment, since my heart has been fixed
times when I would go out with my friends, but always felt something was missing
now turn to magical moments, because I know I have you
I haven't laughed this long or hard in many years
with a single word, or kiss, you turn my heart to water
and the emotions flood to every part of me
