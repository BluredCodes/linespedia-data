For years I found it hard
to have any strong feelings at all
now that I have met you
the feelings overwhelm me
like mountains of waves
the ache of desire
heartache when you are away
I can hardly bear to be away from you
but my heart is won by your intelligence
I can call you friend
and have a good conversation
which is why I miss you so much
every hour is like an eternity
a moment missed without you
is like a little of paradise lostJeca, this is another I wrote while away.
John
