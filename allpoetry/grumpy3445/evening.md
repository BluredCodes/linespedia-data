The sun sinks low, behind the clouds
evening slowly darkens the sky
my thoughts turn to you
and you are there
the light of the sunset
lights your cheeks
you smile, as you always do
I smile in return, happy for a moment
a rush of emotions, so strong, tears fill my eyes
my heart beat, hammering my chest
then I reach for you, and the illusion is gone
I have left you many miles behind
and in my dreams I travel back home
to the love and the people most dear to me
those I must lose, in order to protectJeca, I wrote this for you, while at Civil Affairs school.
John
