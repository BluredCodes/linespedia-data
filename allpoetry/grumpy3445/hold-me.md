---
title: "Hold Me"
writer: "grumpy3445"
slug: "hold-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44582-Hold-Me-by-grumpy3445"
---

I need you to take my hand
to keep the darkness from sweeping me away
at times, I feel so cold inside
that the touch of your hand, just melts my heart
I need you to hold me
to keep my tears from flowing
at times, I feel so lonely
that in your arms, I feel loved
I need you to stay by me
to keep me from losing my direction
at times, I am so far from home
and wherever you are, I feel I'm home
I need you to kiss me
to satisfy my passion, when you are near me
at times, you overwhelm my senses
and when you kiss me, I am never cold
