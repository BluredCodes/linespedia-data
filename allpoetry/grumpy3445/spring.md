Spring is the promise
the rebirth from winter
bringing growth and a hint
of the warmth that is to come
the flowers bloom, the birds return
grass turns green, and trees grow leaves
that is where my heart is
there is a hint, a small spark
a promise of things to come
but still, just a promise
a freeze could kill fragile flowers
and put the land back to sleep
so I pray for the summer of my heart
to feel the fullness of love
and hold off the autumn
which leads to winterEnjoy,
John
