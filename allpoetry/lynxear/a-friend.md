---
title: "A Friend"
writer: "Lynxear"
slug: "a-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Made a friend the other day,
known him for years now.
never spoke,
just waved,
rushing through our daily crisis.

Words were finally uttered,
common ground discovered.
a laugh,
a joke,
having coffee on a sunny afternoon.

Survivor of the Holocaust,
I listened to his tales.
of sorrow,
of sacrifice,
making my doubts seem quite petty.

His vacant eyes then brightened,
with the running of a child.
bouncing hair,
devil-may-care,
with all the joy of innocent life.

So now we meet on purpose,
talk about life and health.
gardening,
sunsets,
never at loss for words.

Walking down the street,
a smiling wave I greet.
a stranger,
from nowhere,
I’ll think I’ll have a chat.

(c)  Jim     Apr/98
