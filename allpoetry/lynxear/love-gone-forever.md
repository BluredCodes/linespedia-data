---
title: "Love Gone Forever"
writer: "Lynxear"
slug: "love-gone-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52646-Love-Gone-Forever-by-Lynxear"
---

I don’t love her now,
I wonder if I ever did,
we hardly speak,
but, we don’t fight either.
There is no emotion
between us anymore
neither cares for the other.

Was it always that way?
I don’t think so, but all 
I can remember is the fights,
the screaming, 
the verbal daggers,
never physical 
but cut just the same.

Hate is not the opposite
to Love, that is for certain.
To hate implies feelings
for the other...
I have no more hate
just a numbness
where feelings once lay.

Will I ever know love?
Will I ever trust again?
To lay my soul bare
before another...
So difficult to do,
So easy to hide
and pretend....
