title: "The Bubble"
writer: "Lynxear"
slug: "the-bubble"

Breathing life into soapy film,
watching it grow so round and perfect. 
The sunlight, dancing a rainbow 
of color upon its swirling surface.

Breaking free, it floats on currents.
We gaze in fascination as it rises
to great heights or falls to the earth, 
only to rise again, to our relief.

Don’t try to protect it, for it is a free spirit
with a will of its own to be enjoyed.
If it is touched, directed or corralled 
in any manner, it will burst and all is lost.

Love can be like that bubble....
