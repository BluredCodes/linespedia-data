title: "A Kiss  (Adult  18+)"

A step through the door,
the cool morning air
she was in my arms.
No hesitation, 
in one motion,
as if we were lovers,
head up, eyes closed, 
she received my kiss.

No ground beneath ,
no earthly bonds,
only a sensation,
of  being carried aloft.
Arms wrapped around me,
mine stretched down to 
the small of her back,
each drawing the other close.

A kiss well savoured,
no peck on the cheek,
no hurry, or rush. 
A soft crush of lips,
parted for exploration,
soft suction, snake-like caress,
tongues probing, searching,
tasting...tasting so sweet.

Time’s sense lost,
not wanting to end,
gripping eachother,
soft mews of contentment.
Searching again for lips, 
better than before, my hand
sliding up her side, palm
resting near her breast.

As with all, it ended,
not abruptly, not with guilt,
but a slow gentle release. 
Hands dragging down arms,
twining fingers, not letting go,
as we strolled to my patient car.
It was only a kiss, between friends,
who needed someone that day.
