---
title: "Alone"
writer: "Lynxear"
slug: "alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Would you go away, just leave me be,
I don’t have any need of your sympathy.
Just myself, no one around,
alone with my thoughts, as the sun goes down.
No offer of assistance, do I desire,
I’m fine, really... staring into the fire.
There is nothing you can say, to put me at ease.
Leave me alone, I beg of you......Please!
