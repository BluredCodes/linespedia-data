Come walk with me,
Take my hand,
Let me show,
A future land.

Where there is no hate,
Only love and peace,
Acceptance for all,
with worry to cease.

Shed this cocoon,
Of isolation, despair,
Discover the world,
With a fresh new air.

Full of life and
Opportunity untold,
Where, like a butterfly,
Your wings will unfold.

For beauty lies inside,
A mirror cannot see,
Just reach in and find,
Sweet liberty.
