---
title: "He Just Doesn't Listen..."
writer: "Lynxear"
slug: "he-just-doesnt-listen"
source: "AllPoetry"

He just doesn't listen,
        it's like talking to the wall.
He just doesn't listen,
        I'm at his beck and call.
Rules I find before me,
        to which I must agree.
Rules I find before me,
        they come down by decree!
He moderates my life,
        over which there's no control.
He moderates my life,
        leaving it far less than whole.
Taking my inspiration,
        snatched from me at whim.
Taking my inspiration,
        no permission given him.
This contemptible behavoir,
        makes my stomach churn.
He just doesn't listen,
        when will he ever learn?
