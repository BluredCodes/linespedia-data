she, like satisfaction in flesh-covered, coke bottled shaped perfectiondesire induced deliriumsweeter than any other tongue tasted moist confectionshe, with brown honey kisses that exceed the scope of all reasona justified homicide of my sanity,pleasing...she, unclothed and urgingbarebacked rider to the constellations shooting stars from belowwith her elliptical gyrationsshe leaves me,wanting...my body adorned with fingerprints and lipsticksin smeared color collagethe walls still vibrant with echoes of last night's euphorianext time...i'll be on top.
