as daybreak starts its westward journeysapphires sparkle where once was dewi paint my world in blueeach day of life without youawakened by your absence another azure-coated mornbefuddled in my drowsinesswearied by love too long forlorncan this emptiness last foreveror just a year or twoi paint my world in blueeach day of life without youhiding in envy's shadowagainst a wall of forget-me-notslies a pot of golden lonely soulsmy broken heart there left to rota thousand rainbows couldn't light my lifeilluminate a way that's true...i paint my world in blueeach day of life without youshattered dreams malign sweet slumberat night, a prayer to start anewuntil love lives in my heart againmy joys in life are fewtuesday's sunrise deftly tintedwith that melancholy hue...i paint my world in blue each day of life without you.
