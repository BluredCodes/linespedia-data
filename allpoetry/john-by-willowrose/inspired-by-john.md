Opening my eyes at the break of dawn
strawberry fields on the centralpark lawn lawn
already ideas have come and gone
inspired by someone and that someone is john
let the day behold a river of dreams
take a walk thru the woods a bath in the stream
inspired by john or so it may seem
freedom to live  the life  with mother nature
not as my wife but as my savior
so i walk the lonely path of desolation
inspired by john while looking for inspiration
sitting on a hill over looking the vineyard
wine is aged and poured with our dinner
walls and bridges can't stop my visions
a better wine with dandylion kittens
inspired by john or so it was written
time escapes me and so do my thoughts
between a rock and a walrus i am caught
a nowhere man with a nowhere goal 
inspired by john with a rubber soul
not fade away smell the roses
part the sea just like moses
can't relive the past no use tryin
inspired by john are theese tears of mine
the fool is on the hill today 
beautifull music is what we play
karma and peace and a soulful heart
