It must be strange to think one day all of this may changethose down on drugs and drinkturn to it like a plaguethings never stay the sameflip a coin guess which wayemotions are a strange thingthey're balanced like a karmaone day you're euphoric the next you hang on stringAll that this equates tois nothing stays the sameis far as i can tell people always changethings never stay the sameflip a coin guess which wayLike a caterpillar is reliablea butterfly will fly alongand like lightening lights up the skysoon goes leaving dreary clouds to cry
