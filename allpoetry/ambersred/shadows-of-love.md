All that is the rage in me, subsides to silent waves in 
breath. To all that listen w/ there hearts & not there minds. Settle
for the smallest soul in need. Tasting only lust & blindful aching 
greed. I sit & wonder how can this be? what is the difference 
between you & me. 

 Too many nights of conflicted sorrow barried at it's best. To hide
whats true deep beneath our vision; lyes the hardest test. Indeed love burns in the strangest places of our souls. Giving selflessness a meaning 
all it's own. It is not forgotten all that i have shared w/ you. A thousand
nights & days still fill my heart w/ praise. Never gave so much, never bled so much! in this life of mine. I'll always feel your blessed spirit w/ mine.This is only a very small piece of my poetry.
