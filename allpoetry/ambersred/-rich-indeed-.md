' Rich-Indeed '

  Listen to the echos of your voice, filling your thoughts. They are here
 to save you. What matter's deep inside burried beneath reason is(You).
 Always thinking what other's interpret, never fully seeing or accepting 
 yourself; why?. Is there some cosmic notion here that leaves you to dwell
 on the 'material' function's of life?. Worthless, I say!!. Those thing's
 you seem to think are so valuable are nothing. Nothing compared to 'YOU' 
 as a person, as a whole human being. This is what truly matter's. Family, so complex, it can get too stubburn at times. Never let us forget any of them. For they are the truth of our souls. The truth of our blood flowing
 right through our vain's, shared our connection's, no matter how far 
or near we may be. We can not be so callus to think those we love will 
be here forever here on EARTH. No!, so I plead & live thankfully for those
 around me. For what are 'Thing's' & toys that may be granted to us in life?. Just 'matter', collecting. WE ARE ALL the precious jewels. The faces 
you & I see everyday. Time sets it'self so selfishly. Open up those eyes & 
 gaze upon what's ment to view how rich  we are, & not so poorly.This reflects on how valuable I believe all of us are as people. And how sometimes we can easily lose sight of that.
