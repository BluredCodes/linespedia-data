When the golden sun begins to set
Its beautiful glaze captures my attention
I stare at its reflection on the glittering ocean
And I watch it dance gleefully along the water
The sky is splashed with red, orange and pink
And the colors begin to drip towards earth
Touching every tree and flower
Altering their hues
Then the sun slowly drops into the ground
And bright sparkling stars begin to emerge
They sprinkle across the sky
Spying on the inhabitants of earth
The moon centers itself in the darkness
As it glows over creation
Whispering comforting words
And guiding each and every lost soul home
Then the soft sun reappears
Peeking over a tangerine horizon
Ready to gleam across the land
