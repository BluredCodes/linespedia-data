title: "Hearts Don't Sink; They Float"

When you fall in love
Your heart seems to fly
And it soars along in a phenomenal heaven

Then when your heart is hurt
It begins to fall
And lands in a deep ocean of tears

But your heart doesn’t sink
It just holds its breath
And gently floats along

And when you fall in love once more
That’s when your heart has finally found shore
And it starts to drift upward to the heavens

But your heart will never give up on you and sink
It just holds its breath
