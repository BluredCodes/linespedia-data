I try to let him goI try to not to think of himI try to look to other boysI try not to hopeI try to go on with my lifeI try to think 'He just a player'I tried and when I thoughtHe was out of my mindThen he was thereHe came back to meLoves me, saying sorryBut the pain en the knowingthat he still was in my mindwas hardwhen I wake up
