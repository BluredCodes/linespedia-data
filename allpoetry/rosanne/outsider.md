I see your walking down the street
The wind blows through your hair
Your looking around
But you are just alone

Cause you don't see mee
Cause i'm just an outsider

You made the right decision
You walk now to the and
You don't want to die
But you see no other escape

Cause you don't see me
Cause i'm just an outsider

Hopefully you looking aroud again
Maybe is there something what can stop you
Bus we both know, that you not find
Where you searching for

Cause you don't want to see me
Cause i'm just an outsider

You don't want to hurt people
NO longer anymore
You think that don't hurt them now
And that you just can go

But you won't see our pain
Cause you make us all outsiders

You walking to the station now
The train and dead are coming soon
I see you running, jumping 
And closing your eyes forever

And so you would never see
How much people love you
And how much people want to help you
Cause you make us all outsiders

Our friendship was just perfect
We could tell each other everything
But at the time you died
You were like a stranger to me

And I saw you trying
And I saw you fighting
Yeah I saw everything
But you won’t let me help

And so I become an outsider
Yeah you, you, you made from me an outsider
Yeah I was just an outsider
I’m so sorry that I was…
