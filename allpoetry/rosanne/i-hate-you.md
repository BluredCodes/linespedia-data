'I hate youYou are the doubt of all my problems''I hate youYou destroy all your friendships''I hate youYour face laugh, but your heart cries''I hate youYou are not who you really are''I hate youYou are ugly''I hate youYou are crazy''I hate youYou are stupid''I hate youYou only can hurt people''I hate youCause you are you''I hate youas nobody else''I hate youwith all my heart''I hate youCause you are sturdy, but really, you are so vulnerable''I hate youYou lie to yourself''I hate youI want to kill you'I see the tears in your eyes...and turn me away from the mirrormaybe 2 years ago, I think, this is one of my best English poems (I´m a Dutch girl and can´t speak English very well, so writing English poems isn´t easy for me, so this one is in very easy English). I´m waiting for your comments, i like them!Loves Rosanne
