Please go, out of my mind
Please go, far away
Please go, don't let me cry
Please go, cry no longer

You're gone, you're gone
You're gone, you're gone
You're gone, you're gone
But only with your body

Sometimes you come back to me
Sometimes you say you love me
Sometimes you sorry
Sometimes you say you miss me

You're back, you're back
You're back, you're back
You're back, you're back
But only in my dreams

You take our love
You take our us
You take my trust
But you forgot something

Please take your look
Please take your laugh
Please take your voice
Please take my toughts with you

You're gone, you're gone
You're gone, you're gone
You're gone, you're gone
But only with your body
