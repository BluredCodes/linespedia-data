I think this is good
You think that's right
We're all different, everyone is else
Let we except, who you are and who I am
Let we except, there have to be nog war

Is there a God, or is there a Boedha?
Is there nothing, I don't know
Let we except, what you believe, what I believe
Let we except, there have to be no war

I don't believe there is someone
Who likes war
Who smiled by dying people
Who likes fighting
Let we make peace
Peace
Peace

I want this, you want this, we want the same
Let we deal, let it part, together
Let we except, what you want, what I want
Let we exept, there have to nbe no war
There have to be no war
There have to be no war
There have to be no war
