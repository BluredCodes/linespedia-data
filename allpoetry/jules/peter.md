DURING THE LONG DAYS I SEE YOU PLAYING
 YOUR BABBLING LAUGHTER THROUGHOUT THE PLAYROOM
TO SEE ALL THE CLOTHES IN YOUR SMALL BUREAU
 I CAN LOOK AT YOUR PRECIOUS LITTLE HANDS
AS THEY EXPLORE OUR MAGICAL RAINBOW
 WHEN YOU HAD THE SNIFFLES I COMFORT YOU
MY HANDS HAVE TOUCHED AND FELT AND HELD YOU
 CLAP YOUR HANDS SOFTLY AND PUT THEM TO SLEEP
QUIETLY NOW YOU ARE RESTING AND FOLDING
 HAVING YOU SO CLOSE TO ME AS YOU SLEPT
AS I WATCH YOU SLEEP THROUGH THE SILENT NIGHT
 AND PETER,YOU WILL GO TO SLEEP,MY LOVE
JOYFUL MOMENTS OF FEEDING YOUR BOTTLE
 ENJOYED ROCKING YOUR WARM BODY TO SLEEP
HE CRIED ,I WANTED TO MAKE HIM BETTER
 HIS LITTLE EMBRACE WAS MEANT MORE THAN WORDS
DELIGHTFUL CHILD I LOVE SO VERY DEAR
 YOUR LITTLE BROWN EYES FLOW INTO MY HEART
I LOVE HIM SO MUCH HE IS IN MY THOUGHTS
 TO RECEIVE YOUR EMBRACE IS TO LOVE THEE
I LOVE HIM MORE EACH DAY AS HE SMILES BACK
 WE SHARE SUCH MOTHER-SON RELATIONSHIP
REMEMBREING YOU ALWAYS IN MY DREAMS
 OF HOW SWEET AND LOVING YOU WERE TO ME
WITH YOUR LIVELY MOVEMENTS SO STEADY SO
 YOU HAVE THAT SPECIAL TOUCH DEEP INSIDE YOU
ALWAYS REMEMBER I CHERISH YOUR LOVE
 AND ALWAYS EMBRACE MY LOVE IN YOUR HEART
MY LITTLE BOY WITH SUCH A LOVELY HEART
