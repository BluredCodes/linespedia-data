title: "Rose child"

ROSE CHILD
LETTING ALL THE MAGIC ESCAPE
INTO EVERYBODY'S HEART AND SOUL
HER LIGHTLY TOUCH OF HER HAND
GENTLY STROKING THE FOREHEAD OF
A WHITE GRACEFUL UNICORN
AS THE UNICORN UNDERSTANDS
HER QUIET WHISPERS OF INTELLIGENCE
THE BLUE SAD EYES REFLECT INTO
THE UNICORN'S DELIGHTFUL EYES
HER TEARS SOON VANISH IN THE AIR
AS THE UNICORN LICKS HER CHEEK
SHE PARTS HER HANDS ON EACH SIDE
OF THE HORSE'S MOUTH TO A LIGHT TOUCH
THERE IS A STRONG EMBRACE AND SECURE
AS THE CLOUDY GRAY CLOUDS DISAPPEAR
WHEN THE SUN SHINES IN THROUGH
TO SHOW A RIVER OF A COLOURFUL RAINBOW
THE CHEERFUL CHILD SIGHS WITH HAPPINESS
AND SO EXCITED SHE RUNS ALONG THE PATH
AS THE UNICORN TROTTS BEHIND HER
TO THE PROTECTED WORLD OF GODDESS
THEN AS A ROSE BUDS SOFTLY FLOWS
TO THE GROUND AND SETS GENTLY UPON
