title: "[the moonlight was beautiful]"

THE MOONLIGHT WAS BEAUTIFUL
OUT OVER THE SETTLED GLITTERY SNOW
WHERE ITS CRYSTAL SPARKLES LIT UP
THE BIG FULL MOON
SET HIGH ABOVE THE NAKED TREES
AS DARKENED SHADOWS CREPT
GRASPING THE INNOCENT SNOW
A SUDDEN GUST OF WIND
BLEW SNOW AWAY FROM SHADOWS
THE SNOW BEGAN TO SPARKLE ONCE AGAIN
SUCH POWERFUL RAYS FROM THE GLITTER
BRINGS BEAUTY INTO ONE'S HEART
AS IT BROUGHT LOVE TO MY HEART
IT COULD BRIGHTEN UP A DREAM
FOR ALL THE LOVE,HAPPINESS AND FRIENDS
MAKES THIS SO MUCH ENJOYABLE
TO SHARE A GLITTERED MOMENT
SUCH AS A BEAUTIFUL MOONLIGHT
OVER ENDLESS BEAUTY OF A WINTER'S DREAM
