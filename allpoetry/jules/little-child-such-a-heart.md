title: "Little child-such a heart"
writer: "jules"

YOUR PICTURE FILLED THE ROOOM
AND SET MY HEART ON FIRE
I WISHED YOU WERE HERE
TO HOLD YOUR
LITTLE HANDS
TO TICKLE YOUR SMALL TOES
TO HUG YOUR WARM BODY
  SUCH A HEART
TENDER AND WARM
HEART AT PLAY
LITTLE CHILD
YOUR SMILE SO BRIGHT
YOUR EYES SPARKLING
YOUR MIND STEADY SO
LITTLE CHILD
YOU MEAN SO MUCH 
SUCH A HEART 
SWEET AND CUTE
SILENT AT SLEEP
LITTLE CHILD
I'VE TOLD YOU MANY OF TIMES
HOW MUCH I LOVE YOU
AND KISSED YOUR LITTLE HEAD
TIMES WHEN YOU WERE ASLEEP
TIMES WHEN YOU WERE AWAKE
YOU SAID MY NAME
I WAS SO SURPRISED
MY SOFT TOUCH EMBRACED YOU
SEALED WITH A FOREVER KISS
MY LITTLE CHILD
SUCH A HEART
