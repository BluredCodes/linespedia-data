You are the greatest reason for misogyny that I have ever found.
You took me over, 
You made me change all my opinions
 You made me love you
Then you took and broke me.
Now I want to shatter you as you did me.
Break you body,
Break your soul.
Desecrate the essence of all you
Until you feel the pain that I have felt because 
of my love for you
Come to me so I can
Break,
Shake,
and Kill your spirit away.
So that we can,
in spirit,
be together once again.
You love your sadistic ways?
You enjoy pain
Let’s see if you like it coming right back to you
Do you like it this way as much?
You never thought that I could change your opinions again.
Now you see me as I used to see you.
Well,
Now you will see me as I have seen you.
I hope the pain of not having me.
Breaks you forever.
