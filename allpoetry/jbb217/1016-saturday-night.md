10:16 on a Saturday Night
It’s so quiet, 
I can hear every tick of the wall clock
Tick, Tick, Tick, Tick, Tick…
It’ wouldn’t be so bad if it was Wednesday
When nothing happens, 
and even the week is bored with itself.
But it’s Saturday Night
With plenty of shit to do
59 seconds, 00 seconds, 1 second, 2 seconds
At least for everyone but me.
I will spend this Saturday (January 30th, 1999)
Alone and bitter in my room.
With just a wall-clock serenade to keep me company
16, 17, 18, 19, 20
My ex-girlfriend,
who has been promoted to best friend,
walks by my window 
On her way to a life I can’t have.
23, 24, 25, tick, tock, tick, 29
I am beginning to find the sound of the tick isn’t nearly as annoying as seeing the tick on the page
But it’s something to do at least.
36-24-36
My friends say I need get out more.
Need to find a woman,
5’5 125 lbs Brown Hair Green Eyes
I still can’t see why the thing that I need to get laid
Click, Click, Click…
Tick, Tock, Tick, Tock.
I have to get a digital clock
10:21, 10:22, 10:23
I am so in need for entertainment
I am watching Cannonball Run on TBS
I will always be alone.
With My wall-clock and 
Redneck Theatre 
Ted Turner:
Leader of the Burt Reynolds, and those with nothing to do at 
10:30 on a Saturday Night
