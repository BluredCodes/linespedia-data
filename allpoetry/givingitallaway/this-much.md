2000 years ago a Man walked a wasteland
And left me in the dust.
This is the Man I follow.
This is the Man I love.
The crazy Jewish radical
Who'd be upset right now.

They took a sword to Your name
And Your name to a sword.
It wasn't meant to be this way.
No. Stop. It's not too late.
To let go of this anger inside my mind.

For rich white men misuse Your name
For their rich white means
To obtain rich white ends
To the point where You became white.
And the world laughed (and they still do).

I fall before You
While others stand.
Seeking to live a life
Where hate ends 
And love begins.

And while the white shadow 
Falls upon us all
My tears see past it
And ask how much You love us all
And You stretched out Your arms to die and said:
"This much."
