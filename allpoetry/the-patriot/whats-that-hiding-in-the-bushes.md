What's that?Over there?Hiding in the buses.Who is it?Running scared,Hiding in the bushes.Find the man,Lying down,Hiding in the bushes.Shoot the man,Shoot him down,Falling in the bushes.Kill the man,Kill him now,Dying in the bushes.Hide the pain,Inside your mind,Hide it so,You can never find,Hide it safe,And hide it sound,The pain inside,That wont be found.Need I explain?
