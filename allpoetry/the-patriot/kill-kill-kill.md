Pain and suffering, Someone has to pay,Someone has to feel the heat,Someone has to taste defeat.Death and Destruction,Someone has to die,Someone has to get blown up,Someone must know why.Bin Laden,You have to pay,You have to die,You have to tasteWhat every member of the free world,Feels inside.Kill Kill Kill,His blood will Spill,Kill those in his power,Kill them, Show our anger.Kill Kill KillSomeone gots to pay.
