Silent face, in an unmarked land,
Devil dances, in grains of sand,
Fire burning, Scaring fast.
Pain exploring my sacred heart.
Darkness clasps my heart so cold,
Evil thoughts consume my soul,
The secret faces to shadows past,
A life no known 'till voices call.
From Silent Faces from unmarked lands,
The Devils whispers, from grains of sand.
