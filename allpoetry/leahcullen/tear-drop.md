"Tear Drop

Captured Tear Drop,
My soul entrapped,
Falling, falling,
Discarding my life
Like the 'treacherous tears rolling,
Falling, Falling
Never render dry,
Falling, falling
Infinities grasp,
Forever clutching,
Always holding,
Captured Tear Drop,
Always crying,
Falling, falling,
Always dying
