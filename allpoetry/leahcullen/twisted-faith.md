My soul died
In some twisted faith,
Stuck in purgatory,
Lost forever,
Twisting and turning.
A shadow amongst
Ignorant souls,
Oblivious to Death
Thinking of grasping
Onto lifes last breathe
Reaching and falling
Choking and crying
Drowning in a river
Of pain,
Flowing forever,
To the end of eternity
