When you Dream,
Does life pass you by?
Are Dreams connected to time?

Time doesn't count,
Dreams don't deal in time,
Therefore time doesn't exist?

So does Life, Dreams, and what we
know as "Reality",
Really exist?

Or is our so-called "life",
Just an extremely vulgar Dream,
One you can't awake from,
No matter how hard you try.



The third poem I ever wrote - 26-07-1999
