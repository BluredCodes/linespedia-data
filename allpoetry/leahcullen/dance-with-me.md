Dance with me,
In this ring of Fire.
The sands of time,
Of forgotten Souls.
Dance with me,
Through the Evil eyes.
The Souls of time,
A Secret Whisper,
A forgotten word.
Dance with me,
In this Forbidden world,
And find the life,
You once belonged.
Dance with me,
In this ring of Fire.
The sands of time,
Of forgotten Souls.Picture by: Roger Hale
