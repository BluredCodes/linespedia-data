"Angry Sorrow"

A night of pain,
Of Angry Sorrows,
Of life passing 'round like day.

A time to join,
The harsh dreams of ones thoughts,
That flow like thunder.

A blinding flash,
In ones mind.
An awakening of ones destination
To realise the truth of time.
A time to find the existance of reality.

A reality of Pain,
A reacurrance of Angry Sorrow.
Live the life,
By power of night,
To await another day.
