Heat rising,
While smoke dances through,
Fighting breath,
For breathing life,
Life in dark corners,
Trapped in infinite shore lines,
The four winds of time not known.
A forgotten coinage of ones mind eye
Dreams of life, In warm summers days,
Dancing in fields
Oh happy times out of mind.
Future moments
In contemplation of moments foretold
Of dances in the prism of light,
For another battle of secrade times.
