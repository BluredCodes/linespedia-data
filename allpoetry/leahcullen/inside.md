“Inside”

I can’t sit down and make a thought,
It’s all jumbled up inside.
I can’t decide what’s going on,
Inside this crazy mind.
I can’t just get it out you see,
It’s trapped inside of me.
I can’t get out what I’m trying to say,
It’s just so hard to be.
I don’t know what’s going on,
It’s eating me inside.
I can’t sit down and make a thought,
What’s happening to my mind?

13-03-2002
