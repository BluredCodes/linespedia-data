“Would They Understand”

Friends all around
Yet you feel so alone
There laughter bellows in your ears,
But still you feel alone.
If they could tell,
The pain you feel,
Would they understand?
Would they know what your going through,
Or stamp it in the ground?
You trust a few, and love then all
But would they understand?
I don’t know myself,
How could they comprehend?
