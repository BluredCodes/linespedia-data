title: "Entwined"

Cry tears for a happy soulFor the ones you leave behindShall wither and Die.Torn between two world's,Two separate sacred heartsBeyond the beyond,Through the near and the far,At the threshold of Death,Married to a worldI can not comprehend,Visions of purity and insanityEntwined on the battle ground ofthe final breatheFighting for that lifeOhh so infinite.
