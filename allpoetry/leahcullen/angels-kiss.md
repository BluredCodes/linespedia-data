In times not known,
And future planes ahead,
Where angels kiss the stars that shine,
A birth in Heaven
Shine down on earth.
A life past, on earthly grounds,
Still surrounding the winds of time
Shall always be with you,
Through the good and the bad.
Not visible to the naked eye,
But feels forever in your deepest heart,
Until the angels kiss a star for you
And light the path to friends long past.
