"Wonder Why

You see the few
With happy lives,
There love when holding hands.
You see them with dark jaundiced eyes
Which makes you want to cry.
You see them kiss,
And wonder why,
You left your love a while.
They float on past,
Without a glance,
Do you ever wonder why?
