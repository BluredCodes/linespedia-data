“All The Same”

I try to express myself
In many different ways,
Yet I can seem
To spell it out,
In any language ways,
I can’t explain what’s going on,
Inside this head of mine.
Day come and go,
But there all still the same,
No different things are happening,
Not any given day.
I don’t know, what I’m trying to say.
But yet it’s all the same.
No change today,
And not tomorrow,
Maybe some day,
I’ll forget my sorrow.
