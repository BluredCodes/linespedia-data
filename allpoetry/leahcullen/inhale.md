“Inhale”

Float away on clouds of smoke
Dancing in the wind.
Float away in secret times
Another inhaled breath.
Your eyes feel heavy
And blood shot red.
A smoke filled wall of light,
Smoke still dancing through wind.
Another dancing breath.
No knowledge of the time or space
A different plane to rest,
Different times and different thoughts
It’s just another inhaled breath.

13-03-2002
