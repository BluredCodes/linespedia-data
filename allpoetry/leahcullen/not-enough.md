Not Enough

One soul alone,
And never two,
No love to share,
And nothing new.
I’m all alone,
Not knowing love.
I hate this life.
It’s not enough.
As time goes past.
And I sit and stare,
The life I've known,
It's not disinterred.
How am I supposed to know,
What I'm supposed to do.
There's no-one there,
For me or you.

19-03-2002
