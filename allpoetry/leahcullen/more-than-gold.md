More Than Gold

Once happy days
That now are past.
Two smiling faces
Are now apart.
Two separate worlds,
One soul entwined.
A distant love that never dies.
You’ll meet again,
In a distant spell,
And live the life
You once had vowed.
Your love forever,
Shall never die.
You’ll be united
In the sacred skies.
And live forever,
In the land that’s always told.
And live forever,
Worth more than gold.

19-03-2002
Dedicated to Hugh (Hoogie)(Dad) and Jean (Beano)(Mum)It wouldn have been my Mam's and Dad's anniversary in a couple of days if my Dad hadnt of gotten ill.
