Voices of the Dead
Still haunting us from afar,
Secret words are whispered,
Not hidden in the stars.
A secularism life,
Devoud of time and space.
Ignorant to the lives of others,
Everyones hidding place.
Idle dreams,
Of children left behind,
Give reins to the imagination,
Open the door to your mind.
Have we fallen on Evil day's.
Or have we just woke up
