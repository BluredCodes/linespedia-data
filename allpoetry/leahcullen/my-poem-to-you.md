You used to be young....Free...full of life....
Then the Devil entered your veins,
Your blood, your soul....
He took everything.
And made you lose control.

I don't know but I feel,
The pain you felt as well.
My pain was love,
Your pain was hate, anger, love as well.

I saw you chnage,
From before....
I saw the Devil grow.....
He took your muscles....
He took your mind,
He took everything he could find.

I saw you fade,
I watched you die,
You left my world,
Without Good Bye.

In some strange way,
I think it's better,
That you're gone,
Away forever.

No more pain,
No more Devil,
Just the peace,
You'll find in Heaven.

But for now,
We'll live apart,
I know your still here, in my heart.
Until that day, until that final day.

Until that day,
I won't say Good Bye,
I'll say 'till we meet again.This poem is dedicated to Hugh Cullen (Dad) - Written on the 21-03-1999 - It's the first poem I ever wrote!! Huh!! :-)
