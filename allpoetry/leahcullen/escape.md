Trying to escape this life
Being lived in some one's
Monumental nightmare.
Chased by Demons.
Followed by Fools.
Choked by the
Ropes of purgatory,
Dragging me between.
Trying to escape.
Trying to hide.
Hating the false smile
Applied to my sullen skin.
Hiding pain.
Of my life of hidden anger
Hiding depresion.
Trying and loosing
Crying and slowly dying
Trying to escape.
Trying to hide.
