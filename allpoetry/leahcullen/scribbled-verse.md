Words going ' round my head
Scribbled Verse
No one said
Secret words
No one knows
Hidden places
No one goes,
My soul, a secret
To the  stars
A guided light
To a scriptive place.
Solidaritey existance of
Hidden disgrace
A secret mind,
To disinter,
Scribbled Verse,
No one said.
