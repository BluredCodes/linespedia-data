"Shadows"

I had a visionary Dream,
That lead to the path
Of Isolation,
To pursue others,
In a life as a Shadow.

A gathering of the
Darkest clouds,
Engaging the aura of the moon
To do battle.

The beamless moon,
Worn to a Shadow.
Nocturnal beings,
Devouring the visible darkness,
The inhabitants of the Realms of Pluto.

Dusk.
Before the afreshed day,
These powers of darkness
Depart this life,
Only to tread on the heels
Of another relentless night.
