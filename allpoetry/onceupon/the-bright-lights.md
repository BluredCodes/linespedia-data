The lights in big cities
Working non stop to keep me awake
Doing their job.

No one behind their windows
Just each of the cities solemn thoughts
Lit up on their own.

Faceless drivers
Milling round
In a faceless place.

Your neighbour a stranger,
Known only by the shutting of doors
And loud music.
