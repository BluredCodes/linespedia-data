title: "Vanessa"
writer: "OnceUpon"

How do I treat you ?
Is there something I haven't got ?
Or is it just you ?
I seem to be working so hard
To try to capture a piece of you,
But it seems I am not off the starting blocks
After the gun has gone.
And now I start to walk
Where before I might have run,
And soon now I will stop to walk
And turn away from you.
So tell me now
And tell me straight
For though I will love you forever more
I'll be able to love someone else.
Have I given you enough time ?
How much do you need I ask.
For the past few months
Have seemed so long
I will not last much more.
I do not ask
And will not plead
But your thoughts on me
I so much need to know.
And as I sit here writing
I try to look you in the eye
Try to pries the information,
You've hidden from me.
There are times I think the others are right
When they say I am foolish.
But they do not feel
As I must feel
Now.
I miss you when you are not about
And it seems all my poetry is yours.
Am I too young to be this in love ?
Are you too young to understand ?

Please tell me.
