---
title: "Reassurance"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "reassurance"

---

See the light at the end of the tunnel,
That light is you,
And though you are close enough to see,
The warmth is still not felt.
But that does not mean that I will give up,
Far from it,
I'll just keep going forward,
Until I finally become the part of you
Which you are of me.

And though from time to time
The terrain will become steep,
It only makes me try harder,
To want harder for what I know we could have.
So when I say I will wait
And try to understand,
Believe me that I can,
And feel me climbing
Towards you.

To know that there is something to aim for
Is a knowledge that thrives on you,
And the sweat which threatens to sting my eyes
Only cools my brow.
And with bated breath the crowds around
Do part with an enjoyment
Too strong for words,
With gleaming eyes they wish me on
And then they will wait and see.
