---
title: "Prisoners of Youth"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "prisoners-of-youth"

You broke the law,
And stole my heart,
And your sentence
Was me.

Did you wish then
As I wish now?
That our love was special
So special.

Am I speaking blind?
Was there ever anything as
So remote as love
In our term.

I still see you
And I know you see me
But only a nervous smile
Passes between us now.

I am not proud of myself
I understand no more than you do
I was young and stupid and scared,
I was also in love.
