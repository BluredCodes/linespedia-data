title: "The Will of an Innocent"

The flames laughed and the flames danced.
Bright eyes played
Vivid imagery,
And wished for a piece of the energy
Which was so close to hand.
But he was innocent enough
To know not to get taken in
By what he couldn't have.
He knew that the fire burnt the fools
He had seen it happen
So often.
And in the end the fire always burned itself out,
To leave the decimated consequences
Lying within hot ash.
So he just watched
And let himself be teased
With a smile.
