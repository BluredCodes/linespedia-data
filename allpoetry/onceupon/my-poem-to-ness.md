title: "My Poem To Ness"
writer: "OnceUpon"

I find it hard to write
What I mean to say to you,
And I don't know why.

I care for you,
More than I thought I did,
More than I have done in a long time.

But I will not bother you,
Nor harass you,
Just like you.

If you think I am foolish,
I think you are right,
But then are we not all fools in the end?

If you laugh then you do not understand,
I can accept that,
Because there was a time when I would have laughed too.

But I do not ask you to understand,
Just to accept
Me.
