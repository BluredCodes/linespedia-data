---
title: "Love Ends"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "love-ends"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36760-Love-Ends-by-OnceUpon"
---

There it goes again
And with an ease so alarming,
I worry.
Look at me
In to me,
And see yourself
Cowering in the dark.
When I say,
What I say,
Will be unfriendly
In its familiarity.
You do understand.
Read me and
Believe me you do.
