---
title: "No More Hope"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "no-more-hope"
source: "AllPoetry"

He looks around
Hoping to see
What he is waiting for,
But then he himself does not know
What it is.
He looks again
But there is nothing,
No glimmer
No hope,
But still he will stay,
Waiting for what he does not know.
