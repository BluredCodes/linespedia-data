title: "To Capture Memories for a Day"
writer: "OnceUpon"

I thought,
And then you came,
Could you hear me,
Or was it just meant to be.
It has been quite a time,
But your face is still as new
As it was before.
What happened last time was my fault,
And I have told you
Though there was no explanation.
So I ask you to look upon me with a new light
And I upon you,
With a longing so hard
That it hurts to think of you not here.
And with this second chance I now have
I ask you to know me
Better than of recent years.

To think a few days ago
You were but a vision.
Unable to be touched,
And when seen upon a wishful screen,
A helpless tear falls from
Ages past.
