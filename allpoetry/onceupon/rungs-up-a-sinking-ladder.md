title: "Rungs Up a Sinking Ladder"
writer: "OnceUpon"

Lies is the best description,
For there is no reasoning
For fabricating half truths
Into profiteering moves,
And the people who believe
Are still searching
For a way to move on,
To surpass the others.
But do they really fulfil their hungry desires?
Or do they stop halfway,
Never to reach their own extremes
Of self content.
So now I ask myself,
As I should really do,
If my own behaviour mirrors that
Of the people I denounce.
But the question is not one I can answer,
For my own bias will mislead me.
And not being true to myself
Is to be hypocritical,
And I must never be that.
