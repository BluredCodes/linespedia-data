title: "The Master and the Servant"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "the-master-and-the-servant"

Please stop what you're doing,
I need to talk about my troubles,
And I need to talk about the things
My friends do not need to know.
Give me your innocence for just a while
So that I can express how I feel
For the others
Which play a part in my life.
Lend me your presence
You do not even have to listen,
But if you do then
Show me your way.
Why should I ask you?
Because you are the one who is here,
Even if you are not.
And afterwards just walk away,
And although I may not have said it,
Think about what I could have announced.
