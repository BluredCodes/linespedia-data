---
title: "Good Morning?"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "good-morning"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32797-Good-Morning--by-OnceUpon"
---

From dreams of uncertain reality,
Consciousness regains its inevitable hold,
And I emerge into confusion.

Daylight hides behind the curtain,
Ready to ambush the cobwebs
To attack the dark.

Good morning?
No, just another one,
They're all the same just lately.

With a cowardly act,
Nights courage
Runs away.

Take another day off.
It's more of a statement
Than a decision.

With an indefinable regret
I submit myself to the day,
Take me as you will.
