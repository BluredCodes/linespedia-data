I talk to you,
I listen to you,
I understand you,
And I hope you do me.
What we have is special to me
And I don't think I would change it.
If there is a time for us
Then so be it.

Tell me if I'm wrong,
Now is the right time to,
But I think I've dug a little deeper
Than a lot of people have done.
Hurting you is hurting me,
And that is the truth.
And let the others worry about it,
It takes the pressure off of me.

I smile at you,
And you smile back
That is all I need to carry on.
And if there is a word for
How I feel for you
Let it be said now
So you have no doubt.
Love.
