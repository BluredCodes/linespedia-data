title: "Come and Listen to Future Song"
writer: "OnceUpon"

The message was clear,
The interpretation was not,
And we died.
Reactions along side obstinate power,
They thought the other was the enemy,
But there are no enemies of foreign nature,
Only themselves.
Now what is left,
Is left to be discovered.
And when they come
They will mock.
Death was short,
But in a way,
Death was infinitely long,
And when we had all died,
I looked around,
And saw nothing.
