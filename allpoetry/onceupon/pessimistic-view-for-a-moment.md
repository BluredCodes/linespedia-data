title: "Pessimistic View For a Moment"
writer: "OnceUpon"

Their love is halfway there,
And in time it will be whole.
But time is the stealer of emotions
I see the sparkle in the fresh eyes,
The grin for the moment is genuine,
But when does the sand run out?
That is all I have to say.
