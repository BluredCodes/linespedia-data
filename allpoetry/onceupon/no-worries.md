Money isn't everything,
Money isn't anything,
It's just an expensive waste.
I have seen rich people die,
I have seen poor people die,
And I have seen no difference.

There is no fear in death,
Not really,
Just a reassuring release of pressure.
I understand myself
Much more now,
More than I have ever done.

I am happy where I am,
Feeling what I feel,
Knowing what I know.
With the sea rolling in to the shores
I lay on the waters edge,
With a smile.
