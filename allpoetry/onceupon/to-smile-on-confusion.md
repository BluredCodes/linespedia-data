title: "To Smile On Confusion"

Beauty,
They say,
Is in the eye of the beholder,
In my eye.
It has a quality,
Unmatched.
It flows like endless poetry
Ready to capture unguarded hearts,
Susceptible hearts.
Why now
Should you ask
Do I witness such beauty?
Is it part of me growing up
Or just a mistaken identity.
I'll let you decide.
