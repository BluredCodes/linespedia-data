---
title: "Pleasant Surprises"
writer: "OnceUpon"

You caught me off guard,
And showed me that pasts
Are really never forgotten.
I was not out to show
Or impress anybody,
Just to talk
And relax a little,
With no thoughts worth thinking
Too long about.
Then you arrived and I tried
To be me
And tried to be you as well.
And for once a subtle frame
Shattered in the blink of hopes.
So now here I am
Living an unthinkable future
Only a few days ago.
Pleased.
