Waiting for a call,
That's all I'm doing.
Out on a limb
Perched above the rest
They wait to see if I fall,
To see if I scream
To see if I bleed.
Though its not suicide I contemplate,
But love.

Now you understand,
I can see the confused happiness
Of days having loved someone,
Glimmer behind your eyes.
And you know that
No amount of hiding
Will obscure those memories.
For even now the love runs deep,
And true.
