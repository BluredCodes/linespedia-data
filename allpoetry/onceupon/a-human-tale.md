While I was dreaming
I saw life
Entwined in a struggle
With doubt.
I saw no winner,
And there was no loser.
They fought in a room
That was round
With high ceilings.
And while life was climbing,
Doubt was falling,
But still there was no better.
And when I finally woke,
I found I was crying.
