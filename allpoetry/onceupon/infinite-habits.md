They walk to,
To where?
I don't think they really know.
Are their lives as bleak as they look?
Are they filled with empty chores
So that they themselves are
Kept sane.

Is their life work?
Or mundane.
Is their job fulfilling?
Or just prosperous.
What would they think
If they could see themselves
Outside of their own infinite habits.
