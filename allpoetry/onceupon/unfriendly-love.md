---
title: "Unfriendly Love"
writer: "OnceUpon"

I hope its not too late,
Not again,
Please not again.
Realisation of feelings,
Is the happiest mystery
Which I have encountered,
But there are the problems of sudden fondness,
That must be overcome.

My past is lost,
And time starts here,
And although you and I go back far,
We meet for the first time.
We smiled a moment too long
Eyes lingered a second more,
My heart fell an eternity down
And I think I love you.

I'm sorry if it is too soon,
There was no aforethought
In my actions.
And for that I know what I feel,
Is really what I feel,
And not a reaction to such beauty,
That I was blindly encountering,
And ignoring.

We need more time
To understand ourselves,
To care for each other,
To caress each other,
And love each other.
My smile is still smiling,
And I can not, will not,
Control it.
