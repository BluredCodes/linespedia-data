---
title: "To Starve Among Friends"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "to-starve-among-friends"
source: "AllPoetry"

They were crying,
The flood of tears
Knew no limits.
Their shattered hopes,
Lay on the ground,
Helpless to their own emotions.
Was it a miracle that they had been born?
Or was it the sentence for some unspeakable crime?
And if were the latter
Hadn't they suffered enough for it?
Hadn't they paid their penance
For an intangible prelude
Of which they knew nothing?
And if they have been punished,
Who had punished them?
Who had the right to torture animals
With no mercy
Or regret?
Was it the same God
They worshipped,
They loved
And who loved them back ?
Surely not.

Surely.
