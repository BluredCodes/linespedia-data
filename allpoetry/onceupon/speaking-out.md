---
title: "Speaking Out"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "speaking-out"

Listen to me,
Sit down and be quiet
This is important,
I have something to say,
I'm in love.

I don't know why,
It just seems to be,
I'm in love.

Never before
Have I felt this way,
Never again,
Will I feel this way,
I'm in love.

Why at this time?
Why at this place?
I'm in love.

Unknown to her,
Is the object
I desire.
And all I feel
Is love.
