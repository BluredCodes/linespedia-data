As I look at this bare room,
I think of old times.
Times of joy,
Times of happiness.
But what of today?
The telephone rings,
The door is knocked,
But still I feel
So alone.
Life is filled with a certain sterile friendliness,
Smiles are smiled,
And lies are lied,
With no difference.
Nature patiently waits to be abused,
At least she admits her faults.
Will they never stop
Till extinction?
Could anybody move the furniture
Back into this room.
