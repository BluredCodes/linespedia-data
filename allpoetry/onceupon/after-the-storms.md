After the storms
The rain came down,
And it saw no sorrow
For the hallowed ground,
Which knew it had to give
A little
To allow the others to live
A little,
Among the bending trees.

After the rain,
We all did say,
That for our futures
We would have to pay,
And now we had to give
A little
To allow our kind to live
A little
Among the many faults.

Now with knowledge here
We saw our chance
And one took the spear
The other the lance,
But did not fight against each other
Instead we fought for one another
And found a life
Just the other side of
Twilight.

After the war,
We all looked back,
And upon the earth
There was now a track
Which told us that we'd changed
A little,
And then we knew we'd won
A little,
And all had not been lost.
