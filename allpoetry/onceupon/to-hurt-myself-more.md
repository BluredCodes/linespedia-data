---
title: "To Hurt Myself More"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "to-hurt-myself-more"

No real time,
To look at you,
No real time,
To think things through
Please come back to me.

I thought of you
Every day,
I thought of you
And what you said,
I need you here with me.

Is it right to say
That you've gone away
And I've lost your kiss
Forever.

I know now
That my heart has gone
I know now
It was you who won,
And I will try no more.

All I have left
Are memories of you,
All I have left,
Are pictures of two,
Where now there is only one.

While here I now am,
Clutching your hand
Which is nothing but air.
And I say in despair,

That I thought you would always be here.
