When I saw you
I saw you first,
And then when I loved you
You loved me first.

I used to wonder,
How it must feel
But now
There is no need to wonder.

I tell you I'm jealous
And of whom you ask,
Of me
I say.

I know you well
Intimately.
And I'd never hurt you,
Intentionally.

So now you know
And now you smile,
Thank the world,
For you.
