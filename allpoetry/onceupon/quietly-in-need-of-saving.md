The wind whistled round the sympathetic branches,
And they leaned just that little bit further to hear,
The melancholy tone of places far
Far away.

Desolation of strong brothers and sisters,
All for power,
How greatly they thought of power,
And the great wooden invalids leaned a little more.

Not far away now they sighed,
Not far away
And when they heard this
The rain began to fall.

Listen to their thoughts
For they echo,
And then try to understand
Why they fall.
