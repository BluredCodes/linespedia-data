---
title: "Once Good Friends"
writer: "OnceUpon"
slug: "once-good-friends"

Mistakes are easily made,
But are hard to right.
I assumed
And you shunned.

Friends take time to make,
But seconds to lose.
I came forward
And you stepped back.

Can we start again?
I know where I stand now,
But that look in your eyes,
Makes me cry inside.

You know my number,
You know my feelings,
And if you ever love me,
I'll be here to love you back.
