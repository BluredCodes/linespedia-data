The loss of a loved one
Hurt so much more than I first expected,
But maybe it has given me the experience
I was needing.
If I had listened
Then this would have never happened
And life would have gone on,
None the wiser.
They say I will soon forget
And will not remember all the good times that I had,
I would like to believe them
I really would.
The first is always the hardest
And now all I can do is to look back
With fond memories
Which will never leave me.
So forward to the future
And with that I know that whatever the past has taught me
I will be able to use and understand more
Than I would have done.
