A year,
A day,
A moment,
Is so long when wasted.
I was happy,
Lost in some period of time,
And maybe I stepped forward,
When I should have stayed in line
And so now the walls have fallen,
I stand defenceless against
The elements.
Like rain against wood,
I have rotted,
And soon to crumble
In front of everyone.
Am I to lose a friend
As well as my temper,
Over a foolish relationship.
We'll see.
