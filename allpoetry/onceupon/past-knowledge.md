Why?
Why now?
Why me?
It had to happen,
I know,
But why now?
Why not later?
I don't know how to feel
I'm numb.
It doesn't seem possible,
Just doesn't seem possible.
I feel,
I feel sad
Confused
Worried
Scared,
But these feelings are coming too late,
They're worth nothing,
Nothing.
I just don't know what's happening.
What was there yesterday
Has gone today
And will never be back
How do you cope with only a part of the future?
When past knowledge remains.
