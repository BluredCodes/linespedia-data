---
title: "Love And Nature"
writer: "Versifier"

Rain looms beyond the morning rays of sunshine.
The graying clouds linger outside my bedroom window
Promising me that all, soon, will be alright.
           And those thoughts drift so casually and easily to you.

Nature is excited, for I hear a myriad of birds. 
Their loud chirping, a sign of need to seek shelter
From nature's repeated and beautiful cleansing.
           And I want the excitement, shelter and cleansing of you.

Darkness now fills my once bright sunny room.
The pummeling rain hits the earth spewing forth
The renewed fragrance only it can bring.
           And you'll never know of the love... I just made to you.
