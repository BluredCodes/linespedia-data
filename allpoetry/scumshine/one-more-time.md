title: "one more time"

taking everything they take 
boken in a halo 
made one more mistake
i need to heal these strawberry gashes 
make them go away
maybe someday soon someone will spread my ashes
biting my lip and tearing at my heart
one more mistake 
i cant call this a work of art
sitting on my ass with things around me to do
all i can do and tear myself apart
screaming in my head 
screaming at my insides
wishing everything was dead
take it away 
but i want more 
wishing for it to leave 
i dont want to become a junkie whore
curling up inside and falling free
no i dont want it 
this isnt me
