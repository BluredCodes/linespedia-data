---
title: "Broken"
writer: "Harmony"

The tears we cried as one,                   We are now crying alone.                   And I am falling so deep,                Into this heart that was my own.                        I am broken,                  Torn apart and destroyed.                       I am shattered,                 Laying forgotten in your voice.           All I wanted to know is are you there for me?       But I am not sure anymore, I can never be sure anymore.                      My heart is breaking,                   I can't feel myself anymore.                    You look at me in disgust,                   As I lay dying on the floor.                 So we throw back everyone's love,                      And try to walk away.                     My mind is so confused,                       You became my day.                       But now I am alone,                        Trying to forget,                        Trying to let go.The class that I am now, I have shared ALOT with.  We were so close.  NNow I am not sure about our love for each other anymore and my heart is breaking.  There is no other way to express it.
