Butterfly KissesBlissfully tranquil, in my dreaming place,   his butterfly kisses caressed my face.His familiar scent, so delicious to me,   ignited my spark, set my senses free.His touch, almost mystical, spangled my skin,  and opening my eyes, I looked deep within.Such sapphire! Such lapis! So deep and sincere,   the feeling electric as he drew me near.The strength in his arms, Oh! they offered so much.   They left me quivering for much more than his touch.I wanted his soul, his deep fantasies,   to consume me completely, to vanquish my needs.He dipped into my mind with his magical skill,   orchestrated my body - such a glorious thrill!The stars in the sky couldn't glitter so bright   as the eloquent love we exchanged in the night.Now sheltered and safe in his loving arms,   the flames now just embers, but still glowing and warm.I'll treasure these moments, they won't lose their shine.   He's every woman's fantasy, and he is all mine.to my personal, private fantasy man.
