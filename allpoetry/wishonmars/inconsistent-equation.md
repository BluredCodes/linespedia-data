Inconsistent EquationI sit staring - heart synchro-knocking with the clock tocks.Amusing it should be,but no..there are no secret smiles here.The insolvable, eternal why still eludes,so stop playing out that old equation.Tick tock thoughtstempting, testing, thwarting.Will I even know the solutionif I see it?October 2000
