SUNSETS

There are just so many sunsets,
we need to savor them one and all...
every single moment,
summer, winter, spring and fall.

Our lives fly by so quickly,
like the wings of a hummingbird.
So many careless moments,
so many careless words.

Your love is all the world to me,
a gift so precious and rare.
Never to be taken for granted,
anytime or anywhere.

Let's sigh with every breath of breeze,
taste every drop of rain.
Let's shiver in the flakes of snow,
see Mars over and over again.

Make Venus your first morning sight,
and wish with all your might,
that we will cherish our sweet love,
as long as hawks take flight.

When the dipper spills its milk,
it won't be sad at all,
because we'll have each other,
to count the stars that fall.

There are just so many sunsets.
I want to share them all with you.
Our lives are bound together
by a spiritual bond so true.

To My Stan -- my forever love. 
August 2001
