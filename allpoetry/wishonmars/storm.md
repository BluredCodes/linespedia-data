STORM

There's a storm in my life
with the power to destroy
my heart
my spirit
my soul
But...
The miracle of friendship
and the power of God
have given me something to hold.

When the storm draws near,
I'll soar with the hawks...
high on invisible wind.

Until the storm's gone,
and my heart can be calm,
I'll be like the willow
and bend.September 2000
