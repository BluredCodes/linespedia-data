Petals of Adoration

He worships his own throne...
delights in his women
who sprinkle petals of adoration
at his feet.
Each one secretly treasuring the belief 
that she alone holds the key
for the sparkle of sapphire 
in his eyes.
He nurtures this belief
gathers his bouquets...
Even as he leaves them all
in his wake...left in the froth
of delicious remembering
and incalculable pain.
