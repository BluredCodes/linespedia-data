Lately

Lately, 
my emotions feel
locked
in a misunderstanding 
vacuum.
  
How is it possible
that those I care for
least
surprise me with
unexpected kindness?

How is it possible
that this man,
my life's partner,
can suddenly be
blind
deaf
unaware of my pain?

My words are met
with silence.
My smiles are met 
with blankness.
My touch is met
with indifference.

Is it possible
during our journey together
I have disappointed
him?long ago
