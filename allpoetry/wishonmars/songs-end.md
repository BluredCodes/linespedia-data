Song's EndThe Artist in her saw the beautystruggling to surfacewithin the wounded psychesthe the Healer in her was soothing.And yet...She never really believed it was her Magicthat moved them.Lacking faith in her own Music,She brought her own song to an end.Perhaps she finally forgave herselfwhen she saw them weepingover her ashes.April 1988 for Jan
