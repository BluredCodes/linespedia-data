Dancing Partners

My heart tripped all over itself
  when first you led me to dance.
The air scented with the perfume
  of beer and cigarettes...
ambrosia to this city child
  playing at a woman's game.

As the music summoned our souls,
  our bodies blended to a perfect fit.
The resonance bound our hearts
  to the beat of the moment.
A fusion of twin flames
  in the fantasia of 4/4 time.
We felt the flow, reveled in the rhythm,
  whirled and swayed, spun and sang...
Alone on the crowded dance floor.

As the music ended so soon, so soon,
  breathlessly, we sought in each other's eyes
the recognition that in this moment...
  this time out of time,
our hearts and bodies discovered destiny.
  We would be dancing partners together
            forever.

The years have become a symphony of memories,
  and still my heart trips all over itself
when you hold out your hand
  inviting me to dance.
Our dance...still so sweet, so fluid, so practiced.
  We'll dance our destiny, and as Blum says,
"let the winds of Heaven dance" between us.When life interferes with life, there's always the dance.
