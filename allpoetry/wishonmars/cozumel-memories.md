Cozumel Memories

I've a treasure box full of memories
  of a time in a fantasy land
Peace and blissful happiness
  long walks on silvery sand

Making love so deliciously
  sleeping in till the sun was so high
coffee out on the balcony
  watching the boats rock on by

Palm trees would wave us good morning,
  their fronds sighing in the wind
Birds sang at the tops of their voices
  so happy our song will not end

Sparkling seas with whitecaps and breezes
  fish with gem colored scales
one navy blue with golden spangles
  shy and hiding in coral veils

Floating so safely beside you
  I feel the freedom to search
for underwater miracles
  God's very special work

Barracudas with teeth so fearsome
  glided by so gracefully
just a reminder that nature
  can be so wild and free

Iguanas majestic and primeval
  fearlessly strolled to and fro
ignoring the humans around them
  claiming the land as their own

Sex on the Beach at Senor Frog's
  dancing waiters with whistles, too
At Carlos and Charlie's the dancing was fun
  I should have gotten that henna tattoo!

Pina Coladas so icy and sweet
  they gave us a golden glow
Time stood still in our paradise
  time for our love to grow

Watching the sun as it set in the west
  feeling the magical land
We knew we had found the treasure at last
  We were holding it in each other's hands.This was about a dream come true vacation this Christmas 2001
