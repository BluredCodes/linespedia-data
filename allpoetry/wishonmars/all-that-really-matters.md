All That Really Matters

He woke me making love today,
my spirit rose like the sun.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

He says I'm pretty, he says I'm smart,
he says he thinks I'm sexy.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

He thinks we have a spiritual bond
that others just don't have.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

He takes me hunting in the dunes,
gives me the tops of all his beers.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

He says we will grow old as one,
watching sunsets from our porch.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

He listens when my tears won't stop,
and holds me tenderly.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

We've faced our demons, eye to eye,
and banished them forever.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.

I will get strong, my spirit will heal,
and my heart will sing again.
He says he'll love me all my life,
and that's all that really matters.
