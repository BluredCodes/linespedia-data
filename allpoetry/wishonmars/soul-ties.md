Sharing our hearts, our souls and our breath,
          lOve deepens through time ~ over space.
          hoUrs can fly by, or sail lazily by,
          days and weeks can sLip on with no trace.

          but Time intensifies all sweet memories,
          and Instead of dissolving away,
          Every smile we exchange, and each tear that we dry,
          is a Soul tie that lasts endlessly.Thanks to Andy Harding for inspiring me with his style, and my forever love for inspiring the content.
