Shattered Soul

Her tears sprang from her shattered soul...
  What happened to Honesty? Integrity?
Her life now changed 
  forevermore.

Her future uncertain,
  and the past now recalling a different history.
No one to turn to
No one to share the terrors unleashed on this sunny day.

Heartfelt words vowing undying love
  couldn't melt the ice 
in her frozen, broken heart.
How could he? How could they?

Questions fly through her tortured brain
  as though riding a carousel operated by a manic spirit driver.
Why? Why? Why?
  No answers to soothe her.

And...no solace for her shattered soul.July, 2000
