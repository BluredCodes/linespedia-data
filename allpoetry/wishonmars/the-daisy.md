The Daisy

He found a daisy in the dunes
and placed it in my hair.
Knowing just what daisies meant...
some love for me to wear.

Daisies are the flowers
I chose so long ago
to carry in my bride's bouquet,
so young and full of hope.

With my heart filled full of promises,
I walked right down the aisle...
to my man, my love, my hero
sapphire eyes and sparkling smile.

We've lived our dreams, a life of love,
he's been my destiny.
That's how he knew exactly what
that daisy meant to me.May 27, 2001
