Two Hearts~Two Souls~Two Minds

When you speak, even in a dream,
my heart listens.
When you love me, even from a distance,
my soul feels your touch.
When you savor a sublime memory,
my mind will taste it too.

Speak so my heart can hear you...
Love me so my soul can feel you...
Savor our memories...
So that our two hearts, two souls, two minds,
intertwined, indivisible
like no others
remain
as 
one.
