InvincibleWhat did I think I was, anyway?InvincibleLife put me to the testVulnerableIn pain about the pastMiserableUnsure about the futureFragileTrying to rebuild my heartWishfulLooking for a place to startConvincableTrust becoming realityHopefulLife begins againInvincible
