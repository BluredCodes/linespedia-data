---
title: "Heart-Strings"
writer: "Celeste"
slug: "heart-strings"

A song plays at my heart-strings
Plucking out a gentle melody
It flows through my veins; life to my soul
My poor deprived soul
My breath comes in gasps, I cannot stand
There is no peace for my poor weak body
Too weak to satisfy my yearning
My longing, ever wanting
To make love to my sweet music 
That beautiful, twisted piece of metal
Wrought from the Earth by man
Lovingly caressed by my translucent hands
But not put to my crack'd lips
No, for my haggard breaths come in gasps
I shall have to content myself
To hear my music in my head.
