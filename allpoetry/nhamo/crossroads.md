Crossroads hold nothing but confusionEyes burn from this crossed out illusion,Superficial broken china,Accrued dolorous loads require stamina,The world holds, plethora diabolical deeds untold,Intricacies exist on unknown fragmented plains,Like butterflies on a windowpane,The truth will free through scar marking pain.Land of milk and honey deceivesLike concentrated lemon juice, sweet on scorched tongue,Destiny chooses the right path for you to follow.Like a thousand skeletal black warriors, you charge with convictionWhat mad pursuits?, what struggle to escape? Is it the right road you chose to walk?,A foster child of Mr. failureBoldly go heritage of failure, dark alley fantasy stalkerLook to the right!, glorious sight!Meccadon paradise, celestial trumpets soundAll confusion crumble....Picasso's scribble of destiny's child,The meaning of emotions, motions, shapes and sounds tumbleNo crossroads....No dolorous loads.....09/10/983:23am
