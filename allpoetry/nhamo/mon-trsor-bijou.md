---
title: "Mon Trésor Bijou"
writer: "nhamo"
slug: "mon-trsor-bijou"
source: "AllPoetry"

My love, I have loved you from the first time I kissed you
From the first time I caught a glimpse of your smile
You have embezzled my heart
And if you were to stand trial
You would be found guilty as charged and I will be glad
For you are the bright star that has enslaved my heart
You are the cool waters that quench my thirst
I can’t help it but you have gone to my head
And you are spinning around like the wind
In the month of July
I’m giving you this gift today
As a token of my love – friendship
And a transmutation of emotional bondage
That I hope will last for infinity
Siphelele my Trésor Bijou
I will treasure you to my last days
For you are the rarest Jewel
And your enchanting beauty will forever enslave my heart
