title: "By the Moonlight"

By the moonlight we had our first kiss,By the moonlight we cried tears of joy,By the moonlight we held hands,And we made a wish upon a shooting star.Our souls became one.Oh! my Guantanamera!,Your eyes hold love to quench a thousand angels,Your smile brings warmth to my forlorn heart,By the moonlight I wiped a teardrop from your eye,By the moonlight we swore to never stay apart,                  It's incredible,Your kiss makes me want to reach for the stars,I will follow you to the Four Corners of the earth,For, by the moonlight, our hearts laid bare,We made a wish upon a shooting star.
