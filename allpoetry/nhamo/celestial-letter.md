CELESTIAL LETTERBe still my six winged angel,The halo ring you crave for comes with an instruction label,The hunger for knowledge you have will eat away your golden sandals,The journey from deforestation to manifestation is littered believe me my Angel, with stone age bones,We long to see you and hug you,The sister you never knew is grown and beautiful, I know the old world inflicts pain and destruction,Believe me when I tell you,The new world holds no pain, hunger, diseases or loneliness,The heavenly love we share is a million fold,The blessings in store for you are untold,Your mother has prepared your favourite cake,Hurry home my angel and God bless,30/03/992:15am
