Being a desert of emotions that I am
The sun shines and I do not feel the burn
Roses and flowers bloom and I do not smell the scent
It’s been like this for years and its not worth a cent
What do I feel?…. What do I see?….
Rain clouds forming in the sky
The smell of fresh earth and green grass causes my heart to stir
I guess that’s you waking my sleeping heart

I’m a Quasimodo for flowery sweet words
Expect not the sweetest rain
For I’m caged and until now I have been content

Please do this for me…

A pail full of words is not enough if it falls short of a smile
A hug from me means I want you with me and never be torn apart
A kiss hello or a kiss goodbye speaks a story of love from my heart to yours.
