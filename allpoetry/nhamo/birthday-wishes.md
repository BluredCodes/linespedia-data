Like birds of a feather,I thought you and I would one day be together,I dreamt of our lyrical relationship,Turning to physical and emotional companionship,I see now my heart failed to conquer,What was meant to be has gone under.A sweet rose retains its sweet smell,My emotional bondage still grows swell,A piece of my heart will always belong to you,Whatever you do and wherever you go,Remember that each and every year,With or without the sun, moon and stars,I will always remember your special day,       And on that day;I will wish you a happy birthday,HAPPY BIRTHDAY!!.
