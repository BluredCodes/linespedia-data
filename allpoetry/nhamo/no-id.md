NO .ID   My world is filled with trials and jubilationsI was put on a narrow path by inevitable situationsThe morning dew covers the highlandsAs I sit with my hands on my head, wonderingMy thoughts like vines twist and turn, thunderingWHO AM I?           WHO AM I?Am I a symbol of the New World?The seed sowed to grow and flourish?Grow I will but,The filth of the world extends its tentaclesI recoil,My soul in turmoilI carry the torch of the past, present and futureDO I? DO I?WHO AM I?       WHO AM I?Does fate grant me a life of soul searching or soul nourishmentDo I walk the line straight and truly?Like the ones before me,I look into the eyes of temptationRedemption I sortDo I deserve forgiveness from GodI know who I amDO I?WHO AM I?            WHO AM I?I search high and lowMy ancestors hear my callMy heart is heavy, for my journey is yet to beginGive me the power to find myselfFrom within.Am I what I see in the mirror?WHO AM I?            WHO AM I?                       WHO AM I?
