MA' DUKESMy earth goddess,She walks the earth with the wind as her guidePure at heart with all the beauty that overshadows any gemstoneShe gave me lifeShe is my first god.The touch of her hand gives me comfortThe look in her eyes - fluid, makes me strongHer radiant smile fills me with love and joyHer presence exceeds all those who came before and will come after herI love you ma'She is with me through thick and thinThe true essence of my beingWhere there is darkness, she shines the way for meWhen I'm lost she puts me on the correct routeI'm the seed from your wombFrom you I was createdYou are the tree that gives me shade when I’m hotYou are that everlasting well that quenches me when I'm thirstyYou are the dream that tucks me in bed at nightWhen you leave this earth I will miss you dearlyI trust your love to guide me on this complex worldAnd my love to guide you to the blessed eternal world.Just know that I love my Ma
