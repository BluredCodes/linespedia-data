---
title: "Existence"
writer: "nhamo"

source: "AllPoetry"

My existence, my presenceMy world connects like nexusesI live today, to die tomorrowThe essence is in my presenceI walk in servitude to my earthly desiresWhat is life but a mere existenceI cease to beThe end comes untimelyI lavish myself with material thingsYet, no existence is perennialI'm the master and the slavePut hereTo exist  with all the complexities of the worldMy soul will eventually leave meI will, yes , I will cease to beFlesh and bone will part waysMy existence turns to absence
