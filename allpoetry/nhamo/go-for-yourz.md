GO 4 YOURZGiven the chanceI will stretch my hand and reach for the starsMy aim is to go high and farThe path I choseIs unhindered and closeI walk long roads and dream of what mightMy ambitions reign supreme like a bird in full flightI want my fillings from the plates of the godsTo look back and say, that’s mine of making and I'm lordI want to be king of the hillThe big Kahuna!The man in charge, always behind the wheelI know I've got the willThe edge, drive and passion for the thrillToday my future beginsTomorrow my history twine spinsWRITTEN BY: Manifestoh (nhanmo)DATE: 12/08/1998
