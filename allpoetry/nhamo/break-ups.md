BREAK UPSUnlike heroes of each ancient race,Demons in act, but gods at least in face,Prepare the wedlock bungalow,For he will rise and he will shutter,The halo ring, as I see the bride sigh,Overjoyed by the vows, sweet surrender, yes the vows,Spoken in context to love themes and sorrow and blah, blah, blah....But there is that within which shall tire,Torture, time and fresh air when love expire,From mighty wrongs, to petty fights,Bonds are torn as I might have sworn,The white gown on dirty ground,Never to be sewn or worn again,They part without a word but each in pain,The route travelled will never be repeated again,28/06/991:45am
