SPACE AGE CHASERSpaced out planet chaser!!!, Spaced out planet chaser!!!.My Communions are undetected by the interplanetary globe watchers, (Zobizs)I speak in tongues,With older gods wearing ghoziq- cased thongs,As I prolong my flight between planets and stars,I stop over at the fast food milky way to order a McGonk burger cataract.My destiny lies far, a billion light years to be exact,My name is Jhepaz,Iam the son of Azura the globe trotter and Topaz the tapestry weaver.I hold court with my spaced out squads beneath living trash cans,As we meditate; we expel,Toxic fumes that carry fallen planets,Between zordok nets,While we digest earth bound pets.The holy herb- Weedora, grows colorlessAs mindless seven eyed cyclops roam star clusters and time tapestry chaptersWith guardians of the boundless one - Azura,Protecting the four corners of the first chamber - Zobid the life giver,Which I'm told, can twists the flight of the wind god, mopazInto 4.0 knots, ending up in the seven headed cauldron of bozptid the morbid eater.Make way for the space age chasing cruiser,Greet me when you pass me in time warp,And never forget to keep your third eye in attention mode with accurate suspension,Or you will be held in timeless detention.Salutations from Jhepaz::! the space age chaser.Abstract thoughts = new language. I'm just buggin' here.
