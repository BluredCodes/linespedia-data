MENTAL BLISS                                Stand still, hush, stand stillDo you hear it?The sweet pleasure of mental exfoliationWELCOME..Welcome to my mental blissBlissful for sure,If you like mental magnetic kissOpen your third eye and witnessThis, this telepathic incubationAs I travel on super charged highwaysGathering, gathering intellectual debris        Word to GodI'm floating, floating, floating palpable to intoxicationFor my mental bliss is cannibalisticRealistic to all you mental dead CadaversYes, I'm still gathering,Atoms, molecules, air particles, even cubic decibelsYou looking at me with starry eyesI'm knowledge hungry- mental exfoliatingStand still, do you hear it?The gathering of intellectual wealth to nourish the God bodyScientific permutations and mathematical copulationsStuck to your temple, as I feed of your brain patterns Ejaculating into my mental blissStand still, hush, stand still...
