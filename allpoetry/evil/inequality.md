How clear is inequality?
Can you deal with this philosophy?
We treat this, as if it were, a prophecy.
But my brothers are on the streets dealin'
Bustin there asses on some ill feelin's
Trustin the wrong class, castin and reeling-in
The same torture that we have faced
and our focus in this life has been misplaced
the future for our children has been replaced
I'm not saying things will change for the better in the past
But our lives will still lye in the dust and ash


When all else fails and your left behind, feeling blue
remember me and my next of kin and think that
their lives our destined for hard times too


There's a parallel universe in my hearts block
Who gives a fuck about inequality and that we talk
Remember the last of days and think of battle field star galactica
and the scientists with great minds
what happend to the man that stands up for inequality with sword in hand
what he weilds, though he stands strong his army falls on the earths battle fields, well man I feel his life is destined for hard times too
I'm not saying we are going to escape this dark ass cave
Or break out this psychological wave
Of feeling crazed crave craving material things and makin slave wages


Baggy pockets only got laundry fluff
Rich white boys on the corner always buyin some stuff
So you try to act tough and hide what's inside hold your head high gettin fucked up rollin dice. 
We're wastin all this time and all the money we burn
On the bullshit to make us happy sometimes we never learn
Fact is, were self centered, we think we're better
We look down as a people upon our elders.
So what would you say to inequality?
Better run, hide, duck or else you might get fucked
The system of the worlds governments so corrupt and abrupt.
How bout yours? You had enough i'm sure...
On the inequality of this worlds bores
But we still need the meek to navigate the wars


But if the meek must inherit the earth
Feelin our lives soon to short circuit
must mean the meek who inherit the earth already work it
There's a land by the sand grab the money
Now let go of it cus it's disolving,
it created all your fears and kept you hussling
made you sever all the ties to your family
How clear is inequality,
when everyone starts to peek around corners
Let go theyselves cus they know their bordersThe patriots are lying, the government is dying, inequality is rising, like the sun over Japan, the far east land. Enjoy.
