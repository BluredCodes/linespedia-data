Twilight zone
Twilight zone

Floating in the dark alone
Is there any love out there for me?

When will I be able to open my eyes and see
Abduct me to the mother star

Dead and living at the same time and loving it
Living and dying is my time for crying

I am who I am
But can you figure me out? 

I think not,
Prisoner of hate
Injust 
Booted from the site for too long

Had to come back and let you know
I'm back here to grow
So watch my style as it flows

There's a light that pinpoints the top of my dome
Can you see it when I point to it?

I believe that you know more
To survive this dimension is poor
