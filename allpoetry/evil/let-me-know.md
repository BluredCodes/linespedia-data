What does it mean to me?
What's it mean to be?
To be free?
I never knew, I was a prisoner
a prisoner for you

Whoa prisoner
A black hole shining
is the sky above
Tiny lights shine
where are you from?

Bacterial plain
She wants to fly 
and they think she's insane
But he knows what she knows
give back her wings and
that's all she knows

Twilight zone
is there any love out here
Let me know

Mutant races in an ancient universe
Dark shadows seem to be lerking in a reggae verse

Start if you think
I can see it when I blink
I believe that you know more
to survive the dimension is poor

Oh prisoner
look at the way she's searching
Always walking around aimlessly lerking
Oh prisoner
jumping the world that's hurting
so bad it makes her cry
but I won't let her say goodbye

Twilight zone Twilight zone
i'm floating in the dark alone
Is there any love out here?
Let me know
