What the fuck was I thinking
I disbelieve in what people what believe
We all had our hearts on our shirtsleeve

There's one thing i'd like to pull off
People talk shit all I do is just scoff
Anger, pain and bisbelief
the shit we have is just grief

What the fuck was I thinking
Anger, the shit is all around
I see it in my friends, families and towns
I see it all over the world
All over my would be loved girl

Pain it's heart rendering force of nature
Wait it's all off course with danger
Long time since we first came here
Sometimes we fall on deaf ears

Hey we can rock it easy, lay it in the fold
Hey, we can shake you simply just by being cold
And every time I turn the volume down
All that I hear is the deafening sound
Of your fucking hearts pound

Are you ready for armageddon
The beginning of the end
The test of bigheadedness
Use of time, crime waves
Bacterial plauges, diseases from outer space
Sent here to kill off our mother fucking race!

WELL TO HELL with your shit!
I can't seem to handle it
Slowly all the pressure recedes
And you start to decay
The pain comes forth quickly
Anger pain and disbelief


Feelin the pain
Feelin the burn
as my mind learns
as my mind turnsI don't know what the motivation of this was, just another weird thing of discovery and pain.
