My condolences to you if you shall passBut in my heart our friendship still lastsI will pray for you night and day,Wish upon a star that there might be another wayBut it seems your time has comewhile you can, take time out of your life and say it was still funWhen everything is over, when your heart refuses to beatI know you'll be up in heaven with a tear for meBecause in this right eye of mineThere is a tear for you that I cryAnd I hope that I'm wrong, yes I hope that I'm wrong but I know thatSomebody is taking you away from all of us hereWe all wish you luckWe know that life sucksAnd some of us still give a fuckBut I know that you ain't hereBut whether you far or nearMy eye sheds for you this single tearAnd sooner or later it's overI just don't want to miss you too muchAnd I thought I was the one with bad luckBut time will pass and my life's gonna be fuckedJust like you so don't wait upHeaven has got two openings for the two of usYo Jen, I didn't know you too much, but I knew you enough. Enough to know that I am gonna miss you when you are gone. As will everyone else on this site. Holla back (tear).
