Crossed in chemicals
open, your own arms

Crossed in chemicals
open, and you know

Hoping you will find
some addiction

Hoping you will find
questions, that need to be answered

Jumping to conclusions
in your mind

Jumping to conclusions
you hope to gain

Finding your own way
the ill pleasures and the drugs games

Plunge into insanity
question your own mind

Tell me one more thing
the pain you don't mind

I know you're lying
I can see it in your eyes

I know you're trying
to get more of your dope

You were just a fiend
for crackheads cheap hope

You will pay all mind
to the drug that you call coke


Quit your fucking lies

Quit your fucking life

Quit your fucking tears

Quit your bitchy whine

Quit your fucking fears

Answer to your prayers

Answer to the plain

Answer to thee insane

I know you're alive

I know you survived

I know everything
