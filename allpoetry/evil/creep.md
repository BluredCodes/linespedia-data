Every fucking day I walk down the sidewalk and here some bitch yell freak!

Damnit, heh, everyday I walk into fucking work and the same bitch looks at me so I smile and she still calls me, creep!

Well jeez I am so sorry my fucking appearance makes you think this way
But I giz' a fuck anyway

my favorite band may be "Today is The day"
But I am still the same man I was yesterday
And the day before
Which explains why my friends don't recognize me anymore

With the planet in it's corrupt ways
The wildfire of hatred still ablaze

I hope my soul can be purified
But till then I guess my life is still stupifiedWhat's your view on angst poetry? Is this what you wanna see? This is what happens. People don't like ot hear the truth do they?
