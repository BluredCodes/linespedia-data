This is life-it is not a miracle
Partial stoning of the heart
Endlessly waiting for something to start
Tasting something that is tart
While trying to compete in this worlds race
Coming and going while losing pace
Endelss illusions in front of the face

A time when all was not well
Patiently waiting for all hell
Kicked down when you fell
take away this lifes pains
break away the worlds stains
extending your own personal gains
while playing the same head games

The void I describe
This is what vacancy sounds like
a useless tapped phoneline
a confusing use of time
can you hear the hearts pleas
can you hear the whispers through the trees
can you feel the warmth that leaves
from a chilling winter breeze
Is it your life you wish you had seized
Can you hear your own insanity

Brain cells immiting illegal transactions
Hearing things in closed caption
Waiting for bad things that could have happened
Trust in your own words
Re-using the same used verbs
If and when and why
Did you really want to cry
Did you really want to say goodbye

Ride captain ride, upon your mystery ship
when you hit land, I hope you enjoyed this trip
Why captain why, did you miss my tip?
