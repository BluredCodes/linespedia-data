Once you can feel it you already knew
There was something inside of you
A fear that was searching
A fear that was lerking

Preying upon the weak
eating the meek
The kind at heart
And the terror was masked at the start

This cruelty makes me think
I don't ever wanna have to blink
Because if I do I might just miss
Cruleties wonders in all it's bliss

I wonder why we all must leave
The cruel creul world of the weak
The time will come for no survivors
The past is gone and future rises

In a world based upon fear
We draw close, the end is near
In a world that quakes with fear
We draw closer, to cruleties nearThe devils of life are as follows 1-Cruelty 2-Deception 3-Corruption 4-Illusion. Cruelty is remarked by the way people treat eachother, the way prejudice is spread throughout the world.
