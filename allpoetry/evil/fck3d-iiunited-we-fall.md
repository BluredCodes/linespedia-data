As the century nears it's formidable end
It is the lame ones who draw us closer
Despising the reason we are fucked
Our global experience of universal proportions,
have been blown sky high in turn for "unique" side effects
Predicted by wise advisors and stupid scientists,
to our solar system a disease called pestilance
Perjury, crime, evacuate before it's too late
Spare me the conformity and protect me from hate
And econonmic devastation will be neutralized by technological advancements
We are fucked into thinking we are useless as a people
A nation untied we stand
No my friend UNITED WE FALL
Open your eyes, open your mouths, close your hands and make a fist
Throw it into the air and say we will not fall down
The toleration will be supressed by governemnt funding
This is the beginning of the end
Armageddon has turned the tables on us
Terrorism and thinking of high and mightyness will be subjugated through you
Before you know, you're dead
Before you know, you go, away
United we stand?
No my friend, fellow, and goodman next to me


UNITED WE FALL!Fuck this system of a down...
