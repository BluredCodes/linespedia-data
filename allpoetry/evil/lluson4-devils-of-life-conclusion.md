When all else fails 
and your eyes hav deceived you
An illusion of mass confusion awaits
at the start and finish of heaven and hells gates

Will the void become one
Let it come unto you
You will know the pain of corrupted minds
The agony of cruelties lies

When everything in your wake lies unfolded
When you look back and see the whole world in ashes
Then the illusions of a great place
The deception of a great faced world

The hurting begins


When your desperation fails


When your presperation wails at the site of death

Fell your mind ache like a cavity
let your body flow just fall off the letter
Do what you feel the more absurd the better

Illusions are confusing
Deceptions and corruption
Cruelties are our agoniesAn overview of how you would feel if you were shown armageddon. Just makes you think, it happens soon. Destruction, war, not manly, godly. Holy wars fought in the name of god, corrupted views.
