An example of a false light
A periodic flash of darkness

Deceiving what is real
Your natural thoughts haunted by dark

Can you hear the void
Prohibited signs of life

Deception lurks within every soul
severs the ties to your family whole

A devil inside, a ghost in the machine
Deception the thought of, which means?

In an instantanious flash of light
You are deceived

Deception is conceivedDeception is like crulety, and corruption with illusion based inside of the mind.
