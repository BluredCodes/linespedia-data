Sunlight dances, through the leaves
Elvish children, run as they please
For many a year now war has past
The Elf kingdom is free at last

But somewhere lurking is a breeze
Through the whispers of the trees
The maples and oaks bow to their knees
In answer to the devilish pleas

A hot summer day scolds the land
The ground sweats from steamy glands
Hoping someday the rain will come
Hoping someday to have their fun

The children used to laugh but now they cry
The Elves and their people always tried
For too long now the rain was passed
By a warmth that seemed it would always last

But somewhere out there, there is a crowd
bringing the coolness of rain in a cloud
And when that cloud sheds it's tears
The Elves will stop their fears

So abrupt the rain did come
That the kingdom of Rivendell lost their one
The King had tried to calm their cries
But the time had come for agony to rise

For all the children in all the land
Sung Elvish songs all in a band
This came in accord to the one 'till she returned
The childrens cries, from their hearts still burned

Until this fateful day the land had had their peace
With the Oaks the Maples, and other trees
But now the Trees have left their posts
To give up lenience to other hosts

What will become of this Elvish town
Rivendell the place of frowns?
I hope someday you'll understand
That Rivendell was the saddest land"Rivendale" is actually a song from RUSH, but in no way did I copy there lyrics. If I posted their lyrics I would tell you. But this is a song done by ME.
