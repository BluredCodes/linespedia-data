Immortal beloved woe is me
For I have cut off the head of thee

You thought I was stealing happy hours
But instead I turned and ran from the cowards

I'm too pretty to have my head on a platter
Like John the baptist accept a lil fatter

I'm a lazy bastard and I cut off one head
I don't know how I did it, but the thought I dread

I am immortal
I have inside me blood of kings

I sing the song from Queen
I don't have a look on my face that's mean

Connor and Duncan are my friends
There can be only one in the end

It's not gonna be me
I get no excerise from watching TVA spoof on a fat immortal who can't cut off nones head, so he gonna die soon
