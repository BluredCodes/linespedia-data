Darkness
Fear
Pain
Corruption

If one is corrupt
The whole life balance is thrown off
Ignorance is a corruption

The government is corrupt
Peoples are corrupt
Corruption is the fear of this planet we live on

Terrorism strikes
Commendment for attacking public places
The work of a corrupt mind

Injustice, prejudice, and fear the same
All alike in the corrupt balance of power
Too much power is corruption

We take for granted our own lives
We are corrupted in our own minds

Fear based on this worlds CorruptionCorruption is seen day by day, especially brought out in the movies. Life is based on corrupt ideals. Ignorant corrupt views, crul people just contrbute to their corrupt illusions and wicked deception.
