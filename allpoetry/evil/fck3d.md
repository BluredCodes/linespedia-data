I am damned

No I am fucked

I am stuck

living in a world I hate

Becoming something I despise

After all this time

I just now realized

My life is tattered and torn

Bitchy and stressed out

Mother passed and mourned

Stoning of the heart

Waiting for something to start

Endlessly waiting for something I call nothing

I found love, but you try to take it right away

A run in with a child sircumed my heart

Antagonizing and stressing stupid shit

Hoping and waiting for it

I pray for this to end

I hope you reallize when your stuck

That your life too

My friend, is now fuckedI wrote this because I have a new g/f and we argue but always overcome our problems, but then they try to get us apart again and again, nagging and antagonizing. It's stressful, but you learn a lot after thinking you got someone pregnant.
