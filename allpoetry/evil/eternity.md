Waiting

For what,
I call an eternity

Unbound,
Hoping for an eternity


Unfound,
hoping for an eternity


Staring,
waiting endlessly


Glaring,
waiting endlessly


Waiting

For what,
I call an eternityAn endless battle of mind reveresed material
