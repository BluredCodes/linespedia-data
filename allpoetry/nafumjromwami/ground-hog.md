He thinks the same way he thought twenty years agoHe behaves the same way he did twenty years agoHe is stuck in the pastHei, rise up and go! read the signs of the timeAccept change. for if you don’t changethen change will change youThis poem is a rejoinder to my earlier poem: 'Like a chameleon'. The heading is from the name of the movie I really liked. The main actor experienced a fascinating life experience after he himself had a change of attitude toward life.
