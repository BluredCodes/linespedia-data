She was at the age of 16 when I saw her
She was all the beauty you can think of
I saw the whole world in her eyes
She had stolen my heart

But come her daughter
A little less than an angel but more beautiful 
than any of the humans I have ever seen

But then, her granddaughter was more an angel
I adored her beauty more than I can describe here

I wonder what I will do or say on the one 
in the granddaughter’s womb?
The signs are marvelous!I have learned that it is best to be content with what I have.Comparisons leave me unsatisfied. And sometimes I remain with non.
