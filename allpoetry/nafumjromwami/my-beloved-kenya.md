There is treasure in your beauty
In the mountains, in the hills
In the ocean beaches
In the rift valley
In the lakes 
in the plains
In the people …

The people co-exist with wild animals 
With elephants they interact
With lions the boys engage in friendly fights
With crocodiles they swim together in rivers
With monkeys and baboons they converse
What a beauty?

But you stink of corruption
You stink of injustices
You stink of poverty
When will you restore your past glories?
Is it just a nightmare?

Ooh, optimists , sing with me our national anthem:

“God of creation,
Bless this our land and nation,
Justice be our shield and defender,
May we dwell in unity,
Peace and liberty,
Plenty be found with our borders.

Let one and all arise,
With hearts both strong and true.
Service be our earnest endeavour,
Our homeland of Kenya,
Heritage and splendor,
Firm may we stand to defend her.

Let all with one accord,
In common bond united,
Build this our nation together,
And the glory of Kenya,
The fruit of our labour,
Fill every heart with thanksgiving”.We celebrate our independence day on the 12th of december. My heart is full of joy as I celebrate this day in St. Louis MO. To all Kenyan, happy independence day!
