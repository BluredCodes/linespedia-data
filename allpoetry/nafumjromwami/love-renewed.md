In love and marriage 
little things matter
I did not learn this from books
my lover taught me 

Our lives were changed 
Our minds were broadened
Our hearts were open to love

Our focus was no longer in bed
It was about our lives 
It was about ourselves

She realized that I am different from her
And so did I
She knew what I liked and disliked
And so did I
She new my weakest link
She filled it with her other self
And so did I 
Her warm, her smile was all for me
Love was renewed.This poem is a continuation of my other poem, "WE almost..".
