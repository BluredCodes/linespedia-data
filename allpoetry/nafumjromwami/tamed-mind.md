title: "Tamed Mind"

Tamed is the mind that makes life real
Tamed is the mind that gives meaning to things
For tomorrow wouldn’t be New Year
What is the difference between today and tomorrow anyway?

Happy are they who have tamed minds
For a tamed mind knows its boundaries
It knows its limits well
It knows it can’t comprehend itself fully

A tamed mind that respects its conscience
A mind that listens to the heart, for the ’heart 
knows reasons which reason knows not’
A tamed mind that seeks integration 

For on it's own, untamed mind is disastrous
Ask Alexander and he will tell you
Ask Nietsche and he will tell you
Ask any great minds, and they will tell you

Relations a tamed mind seeks
Dialogue it establishes
Ultimate order it seeks. Confusion is no good
With tamed minds, not the Sept. 11th, 2001 any more.
