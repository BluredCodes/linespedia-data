---
title: "With You I am Whole"
writer: "Nafumjromwami"

Morning by morning I look myself in the mirror
Looking for the answers to, whose image?
Who am I? And how do I improve my image?
Two mirrors I need for my front and back
for one is less than real.

Joy fills my heart when I realize that you
are my mirror, that you are my other self
I know for sure that I am not an island
That I can’t be myself without you
For without you I am nothing

You reveal to me my limitedness
You feel the gaps in my being
In you I draw closer to maturity
In you I draw closer to perfection
For you compliment my beingness
