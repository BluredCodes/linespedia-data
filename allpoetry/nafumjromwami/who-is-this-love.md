Love! Why are you so expensive?Why do you cause pain in me?I have longed for you!I have asked you to be with me and in me.Yet your response has always been:“If you want to be with me         Let go of your pride,	Let go of you selfishness,	Let go of your self-centeredness,	Let go of your prejudices,	Let go of your biases…”And you still demand more from me:my self-sacrifice, dying to the self, my openness to you, my self-giving to you…You leave me with nothing for my selfYou destroy what I stand forYou destroy my egoTrue Love is costly
