---
title: "Friendship of convenience"
writer: "Nafumjromwami"
slug: "friendship-of-convenience"

You are friends because of me
Because I am your common enemy
Because you can talk about me
Because you can gossip about me
Because you can plot evil against me

I am your story
I make you happy 
Your focus is me
Your attention is me
I keep you together

You project your frustrations on me
You project your hatred on me
You sink your problems in me
You conceal your wickedness 
You project it on me

You hate facing the truth 
All you can do is blame me
You hide your true selves in me
You glorify yourselves
You make yourselves righteous

If I am your source of happiness
If I keep your together 
If you can’t face reality without me
Think about it
For I am mortalI dislike friendships of convinience. People should face life on their own and not blame others for their shortcomings.
