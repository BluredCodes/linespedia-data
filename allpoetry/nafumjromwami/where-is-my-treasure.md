I am haunted by the biblical words:
“Where your treasure is, then there your heart is”
My heart is unsettled in search of its treasure

I thought I had found it, found it in money, 
in my lover, in drinking, in friends…

But the more of these, the more I was left restless
empty, empty than even before.It seems to me that as limited but spiritual(with a soul) human beings, nothing much in this world can offer us full satisfaction.
