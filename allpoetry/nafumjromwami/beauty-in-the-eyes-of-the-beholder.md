The media bombards us with the criteria for beauty
They make us reject ourselves for what we are
Why must we subject ourselves to them?

I have my cute eyes to choose what is best for me
Whether big or slim, 
Whether tall or short,
Whether smart or not smart!
Who cares? Don’t you remember that 
one person’s food is another’s poison?beauty is relative
