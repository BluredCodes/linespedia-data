I am good no matter what they say or do to me
Day and night they plot evil against me
They say: “what faults do we find with him?”
And they create some if they don’t find any 

They delight in my past faults, they capitalize on them
They want to humiliate me always
Wanting to bring me down to nothing
To a useless creature without honor

But let me tell you, I am good, I am a somebody
I am proud of who I am. I have my talents
I know I have my failings but that is being human
Who on earth is perfect, without fault?There are people in and out there who are awls looking for the the down fall of the others. I dedicate this poem as a compliment to Nell's poem: 'Dream Stalker"
