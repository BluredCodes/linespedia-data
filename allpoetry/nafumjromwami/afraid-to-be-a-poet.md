title: "Afraid to be a Poet"

How great it is to be a poet!
Poetry, a special gift to a privileged few
A powerful tool of communication
A sacred revelation of reality in symbols

But I am afraid to be a poet

Afraid, if imagination is all that is in poetry
Afraid, if fantasy is all that is in poetry
Afraid, if idealism is all that is there 
Afraid, if pessimism and sadism is all that is there 

Much worse, afraid, if suicide is the death most poets prefer
