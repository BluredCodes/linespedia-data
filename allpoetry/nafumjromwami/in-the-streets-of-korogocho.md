I see a poor man walking in the streets,
he doesn’t have anything to eat.
I see a poor man walking in the streets,
No  decency in his wear.
What can you give, what can you say,  
to this poor man that you see everyday?

I see a street child, crying in the snow, 
he doesn’t have anything to cover.
I see a street child, sniffing glue, 
wanting to be sane in te surrounding insanity.
What can you give,  what can you say, 
to this street child that you see everyday?

I see a teenager absorbed in fantasy,
Contemplating suicide, his way to be happy. 
I see a teenager rebelling against self,
and all that is a challenge to him.
What can you give, what can you say,  to this teenager
that you see everyday?
