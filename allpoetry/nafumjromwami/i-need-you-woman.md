You are all that I have
I have no mother, no father
no brother, no sister ...

Why leave me?
I never meant to hurt you!
In any case, I have always been there  for you
Why leave me now?

Can one wrong cost my life?
Without you I am ruined
I am a nobody
Please come back to me
