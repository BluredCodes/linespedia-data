How lovely to be in the present?
To be really me in the now!
If only I stopped blaming myself of my past
And stopped unnecessary anxiety of the future
Saint Augustine consoles me in his words: 
‘Trust the past to divine mercy,
The present to divine love 
And the future to divine providence”.

There is hope!
