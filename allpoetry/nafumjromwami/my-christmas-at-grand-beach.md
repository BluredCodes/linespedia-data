I wake up at 9.00.
I can’t recall where I am.
I hear the vibrating sounds,
I am confused, anxious, but excited.

Towards the window I move,
To my surprise is white snow all over.
And the vibrating water waves,
And the cloudy sky I see.

This is not Mombasa!
This is not Nyali beach,
Neither is it Indian Ocean.
And what is it? Beach Grand? Noo! 

Great joy overflows in my heart.
Gorgeous gifts I receive though I gift not,
Except the gift of self 
Friends surround me. My family in them I see

Tears of joy fill my eyes,
Speechless I stand.
Dumb or deaf I can’t tell,
For I am just there

Beautiful is the clear blue-sky even if for a while.
Out I stand facing the raving waters.
On my right New Buffalo I see, on my left 
Michigan City and of course the great Chicago

Amazing! Amazing! Amazing. Lo! The magnificent sky!
The still waters afar are a peace in my heart.
And the white capping, surfing water waves a joy
I experience in my heart even if for a moment.My chritmas with Moriaty's family:Danny, Barry, Mary, Kay, Peter, Rita, Erin, Bridgid...my my colleagues, George, Philip and Angelus.
