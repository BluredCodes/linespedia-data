title: "Not Without You"
writer: "Nafumjromwami"

I need you woman. I need you
You are my other self
I can’t do without you

Your intuition pierces my heart and my soul
You look in my eyes and you know me

You calm my fears and anxieties
When I get lost in the transcendental world of ideas,
You tell me, “ come down and be real”.

When I am out of touch with my feelings,
You remind me that I am not a stone
You bring joy to my heart
You make me be who I should be
