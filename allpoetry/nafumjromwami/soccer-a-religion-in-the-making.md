Watch world cup soccer
Watch Olympic games
Watch soccer, watch them play!
Watch them watch!

Rituals they perform
Faithful they are to the rubrics
Punishment and reward to all
Nothing escapes their way

With procession they start
A lighted candle is the symbol
They sing, they dance, and slogans 
they chant, shouts, shouts all over

Magic is the game
To craziness it drives them
To ecstasy they are moved
Mystics they become

To lose is a curse
Lest witchcraft come into play
Hooliganism is even better
Ask England they will tell you

To fight they will fight
To kill they will kill(Columbia)
Fundamentalistic they are
What a devotion they have?

And own saints they adore
Pele, Maradona, Roger Miller…
Their gods they are.
Tell me, isn’t Soccer a religion?This may be true with other sports. My concern is that many people are denying the relevance of religion, but religion is in everything we do.
