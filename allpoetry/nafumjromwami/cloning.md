You are because you are what you are 
I am because I am what I am 
You are because we are and since we are 
therefore I am

Christmas without Easter would be meaningless
Man without woman may never give life
Winter without summer may be boring
You and I are complimentary

And what about Cloning?Difference brings balance and balance makes life real. I have my reservations about cloning
