Like a Hyena on the forest path
hunting for his prey, hungry, desperate
He can’t wait, he can’t hold any longer
Anything that will come his way

At this intersection he smells his prey
From both directions, the smell comes
Sweet, delicious, scentiful
He salivates, he anticipates

The smell is too sweet
He can’t let go any of them
He can’t lose any of them
He can’t afford it

He stretches his legs both directions
Wider and wider
Just in case, lest he lose any of them
And guess what happens!

He wanted to possess us both
And so she did
He wanted to love us both
Her too. If only true love could be shared.
