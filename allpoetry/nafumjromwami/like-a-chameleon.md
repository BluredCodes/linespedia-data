title: "Like a Chameleon"

My friend, why like a Chameleon?Why unpredictable?Why keep changing? Why never consistent?Why never stable? Why never reliable?Why never faithful to your word?Why like a Chameleon?Today you are this, tomorrow you that.Today you say this, tomorrow something different.Today you do this, tomorrow something different.Why like a Chameleon?When will I ever rely on you?When will I ever trust you?When will I ever count on you?Why like a Chameleon?It is heard to live and work with unprincipled people.
