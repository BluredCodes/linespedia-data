Ooh Mother Earth!
What a shame we forget
That we are one with you!
That we come from you

You hold the key to the unknown
You hold the power to new life
You open up to those who till you
And behold new life

Your power is in your opening
Amazing is what comes out of it
New life, new existence, new hope….
Countless are the fruit of your yes

Cursed are those who misuse you
Cursed are those who rape you
For they bring abomination upon themselves
And evil surrounds them. Condemned eternally

Of all ages. From the beginning
