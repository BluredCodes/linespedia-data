My dreams:
good job, good friends,
good family, a millionaire,
a successful man.
I traveled far and wide.
Made great friends,
made great money.
But barely did I sleep,
barely did I eat
No time for myself,
no time for friends
No time for my family,
My one focus, rising up.
A sycophant I was
a tyrant I became,
nothing stopped my way.
The top I had reached!
But when you reach the top,
what next? Don’t tell!
Gold, diamond, and money: success?
Not in my case
For I became a loner,
a married bachelor.
Everything was fading away
Things were falling apart:
a divorce with my lover,
losing my  beloved kids
my friends now my enemies,
My health at risk, soon  a vegetable.
Oh, induced burn out or fading away, 
I wish nature took its coerse.
