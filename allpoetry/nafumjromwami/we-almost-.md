She was no longer I had before
Her beauty was gone
Appearance yes
But in my heart and mind, she was ugly
Where had her beauty gone?
Why no longer attractive?
Her smile was a curse to me
He laughter had become sour
And her talk was poison
Our house was a little prison 
Without ventilation
Our bed was like hell 
Her kisses were dry
and this was enough to make me go out
In search of greener pastures
What happened?
Only few years had gone by
Not even forgotten our honey moon
Our promises, our fantasies…
What went wrong?
What really was missing?You may read this with my other poem, "Love renewed" for it to make sense.
