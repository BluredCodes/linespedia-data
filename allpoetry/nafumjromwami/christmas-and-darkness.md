title: "Christmas and Darkness"

Did you ever think that winter darkness is at its height 
on 25th December? When the light of day disappears 
early, and the darkness of the night appears too early
And night is really dark? Dark for those who thrive in darkness

This darkness scares; it diminishes the hope of seeing light
It is a bother to lighted hearted people like me and you
It leaves me sad, restless, anxious, hopeless and fearful
In real need of redemption from this darkness

Why Christmas on this dark night? Why on 25th?
Is it not a battle between light and darkness?
And who wins? For what? For whom? and for who?
There must be a winner anyway!

I had never thought so until my friend told me that
 the battle is within us. He said, "don’t look far, darkness
is here and is powerful. It is in the countless murders,
in terrorism, in wars, in hatred, in anger, in revenge…"

But where is our Christmas light then?
Is it not in the hope we have that some day
things will work out better?
If only we prepared for the worst while hoping for the best!This is my reflection on the meaning of Christmas for me in the contemporary world today.
