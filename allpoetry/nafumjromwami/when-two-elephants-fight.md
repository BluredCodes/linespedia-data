I love this saying,
‘When two elephants fight, who suffers? The grass!’
Indeed, when dad and mum fight, who suffers? The kids!
When we fight the terrorists, who suffer?
Again, when two elephants fight, who suffers?In any conflict, always the poor, weak and defenceless people suffer most.
