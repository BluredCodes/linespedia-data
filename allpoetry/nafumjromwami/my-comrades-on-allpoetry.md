Great minds I have met
Good and tender hearts I have seen
Your comments have tapped my unexplored minds
Your nice words have  been a healing power
Relations we have established
Ideas we have shared
In one spirit we have been united 
Our differences brought understanding
what else is there other than say,
Thank you! Thank you! Thank you!
May God bless you abundantly.
I Wish you Happy and prosperous  New YEAR
My poem, 'Tamed Mind' a gift to you all.
