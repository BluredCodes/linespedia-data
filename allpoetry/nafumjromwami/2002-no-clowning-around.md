Sooo many sounds ringing into my ear,
So many things in my mind.
I am excited, anxious and scared too.
No more honey moon, no more safaris.
No more clowning around!
A mount of tasks lie ahead me
My friends waiting for me,
my family waiting for me
My co-workers waiting for me. 
College again I have to go
for my  unfinished business.
To finish. When? God knows!
For,  comprehensives, de universa, just around the corner,
long paper I will submit, in no time I will defend.
Grey air is appearing, even when at 30,
Under pressure! Under pressure! Big responsibility!
But I will not be cowed down,
I will not be shied away
I refuse to be scared,
I will take the bull by its horns.
For one thing do I know, and two do I remember,
that my life is not a circus, and no more clowning 
around (in the year 2002). My God-given talents at disposal!
For the good of all that will be reached.
