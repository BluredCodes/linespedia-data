NY is under pressure,There is turmoilThe inhabitants are in pain.They have losed their loved onesSome are crying,Some are weeping,Some are wailing,Some are dying Some have lost their live hood, Everybody is under pressure!You and I are under pressureThings won't be the same!In sympathy to those in New York following the double tragedies of WTC of September 11 and the plane crash weeks after.
