It growls at me as though it's angry

 I want to obey, but sometimes I can't

 It's as though it yells and twists in fury

 Making me kneel over in pain and pant



 Eventually, it gives up and rests

 Making me feel sick

 Then it wakes up and yells again

 From inside it gives me another kick



 Finally, I am back at home

 And have access to delicious chips

 I open the bag, expectantly

 And raise one to my hungry lips



 Suddenly, much to my dismay

 I hear my mother call

 " You'll ruin your appetite, put it down!"

 Till dinner's ready - No food at all
