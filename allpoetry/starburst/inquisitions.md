What if I'd made different choices?
 Where would I be right now?
 Would I be going down a different road?
 Or would I be different somehow?

 What if I'd been born in another time?
 Would I still be the same old me?
 With the same family and friends and life?
 How different would my life be?

 Would I still have the same interests,
 And like the same old things?
 Would I still love music,
 And all the joy it brings?
 
 These are my inquisitions
 My ponderings, wonderings, and thoughts
 If all these things had happened
 Would my life be so different or not?2001,
