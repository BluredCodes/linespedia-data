title: "Nature's Song"
writer: "Starburst"

Calm, cool morning on the lake;
The smooth still surface not disturbed by even a slight ripple.
I can hear the birds and mosquitos in my ears,
Singing the song of the morning.
I see the eagle swirling and wheeling overhead.
The sun is just barely up and except for me, the lake is devoid of humans
But teeming with early morning life
All I can feel is
Peace
That one word means so much
Complete relaxation
All worries banished for the moment
It's something not everyone achieves
As I drop my fishing line into the water 
I drop my worries and problems into the watery depth along with it, drowning them 
In this one spot On this one morning I've experienced  
Peace
