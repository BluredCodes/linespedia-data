---
title: "Sun - Flower"
writer: "Starburst"
slug: "sun-flower"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14232-Sun---Flower-by-Starburst"
---

Quietly stretching towards the sky
 To feel the suns gentle rays
 Regaining all of the strength it lost
 Due to those harsh winter days

 Opening wide its emerald leaves
 Brightly colored petals sparkling with dew
 At first it looked dead, as though it weren't alive
 Seeming now to start its life anew
