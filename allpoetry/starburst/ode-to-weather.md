The lightning strikes the weathered house
And breaks the windows' glass
Soon after this event takes place,
The violent storm has passed

The sun comes out
Away goes the rain
A rainbow appears
Like ease to a pain

Weather can bring terror and fright
Especially if the storm comes at night
But it can also bring joy and smiles
As the wind blows and the sun shines for miles

We try to predict things
Tomorrow rain and then snow
But I don't think we ever really
Will know which way the weather will go
