The world was rocked today
  Seems as though it's falling apart
  Many things were destroyed
  Things dear to the patriotic heart

  People hijacked four passenger planes
  And did so many things
  How could these people be so mean?
  Can't they see all the tears they bring?

  Two planes were crashed
  Into the Twin Towers
  The World Trade Center is no more
  Peoples' graves will be sprinkled with flowers

  Many jumped
  From these towers
  Hoping to save
  A few precious hours

  One plane hits the Pentagon
  The fire is burning still
  America is under attack
  Many people they do kill

  Once more a plane hits
  Pennsylvania this time
  Mouths open, no sound
  Like a silent mime

  Innocent passengers
  Bystanders, too
  Are now dead
  Who did this, who?!

  Families are torn apart
  They can only mourn
  A cloud of death
  Has been born

  In loving memory
  Of those who died
  Please know
  God's by your side

  We must stay strong
  So we aren't thought weak
  Or war will begin
  And havoc will wreak

  Oh what will become
  Of this crazy event
  Will America
  Be broken or bent?
