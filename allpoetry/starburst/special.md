title: "Special"
writer: "Starburst"

I watch from afar
 And although you do not see
 Many others know
 That you are special to me

 Slowly I became your friend
 But do you consider that of me?
 And if you do not think so 
 I sure would like to be

 So I hope that as the years go by
 We become good friends, you and I

 And if that does not happen
 If it wasn't meant to be
 Then I hope you figure out
 That you are special to me
