title: "Once One"
writer: "Starburst"

One in body

 One in mind

 One in soul

 And one in time



 One in heart 

 And one in sign

 She's the one

 He left behind

 

 Everyday he thought of her

 And loved her like no other

 Until the day 

 He went astray

 And there he found another
