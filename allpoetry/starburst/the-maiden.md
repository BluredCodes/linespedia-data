If you stand on the shores of the ocean, blue
 You can hear her song calling out to you
 
 And if you look really close, in you sight she will be
 Looking to the shore quite mournfully
 
 Then when she sees you, hope lights up her eyes
 When she sees it's not her love, the glow quickly dies

 When the sun sets, she begins to cry
 Tears of desperate longing, she turns with a sigh

 The mermaid was a maiden, a spell on her was cast
 And if her true love came, the spell would not last

 But for many years, she has watched and waited
 And the hope that she once held, has slowly dimmed and faded

 Her love has never come, maybe he never will
 But she will continue to wait for him, for she loves him still2000,
