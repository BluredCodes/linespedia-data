---
title: "Through The Eyes Of An Eagle"
writer: "Starburst"
slug: "through-the-eyes-of-an-eagle"

Outside me I see the shining waters and tall trees of my home.
 Inside me I feel love for this beautiful place and lucky that I am here.

 Outside me I see fish beneath the water
 Inside me I feel hunger and longing to tear at the flesh of a fat perch and ease my need.

 Outside me I see the water rushing up at me as I dive towards the lake.
 Inside me I feel the adrenaline rush that comes with every hunt.  

 Outside me I see the fish wiggling helplessly in my talons as it struggles pathetically, trying to get away.
 Inside me I know I am superior and at the moment I feel nothing can beat me.
 
 Outside me I hera loud roaring noises and swerve suddenly, startled at the large yellow monster tearing at my tree.
 Inside me I am outraged and terrified. What is this thing and why is it here?

 Outside me my talons open ; the meal , forgotten, drops to the ground with a thud.
 Inside me I want to fight this creature that is tearing down my home.

 Outside me I see trees falling and the color yellow as I attack the beast, finding it hard, unpenatrable, and cold.
 Inside me I feel confused and have no idea when it will leave, because I am helpless and can't make it go.

 Outside me I see night coming and the beast rumbles away leaving a path of destruction in its wake.
 Inside me I know I'll never take for granted my superiority over smaller creatures, for I have a superior, too.

 Outside me I see, years later, the trees growing back and my children feasting on a perch.
 Inside me I know that life goes on and that I must remember to tell my children to always keep their guard up and to never become so blinded by a triumph that they cannot see defeat.
