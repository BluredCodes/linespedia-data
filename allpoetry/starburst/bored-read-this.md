title: "Bored? Read this!!"
writer: "Starburst"

Have you ever just sat there
 Thinking of what to do?
 When you decide on something
 Your body just won't move?

 Have you stared into space
 And twiddled your thumbs?
 Not knowing what to do
 And feeling pretty dumb?

 Have you ever been so bored
 You just want to cry?
 You don't know what to do
 And you sit the day by?

 If you're reading this now
 Then, you're probably feeling this way
 So get up out of your chair
 Off your butt, and go play!
