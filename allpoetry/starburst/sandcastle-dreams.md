Nothing is really as it seems
 On the shores of the bright blue sea
 While drifting about in mind and soul
 I dremt up my sandcastle dreams

 At the perfect time, on the sand
 They take each other hand in hand
 And dance and prance and jump all around
 On the shores of the sea, their native land

 Then they build up their castles, it seems
 Guided by the moon's gentle beams
 These are the things that I see while I sleep
 While I'm dreaming my sandcastle dreams

 And inside these walls
 People, some big and some small
 All gather here in good times and fun
 With music playing, they have a grand ball

 They feast and they dance, all through the night
 They are all happy and feeling just right
 Then the sun quietly peeks out of the sky
 And the whole world begins to get light

 As the sun showers the world with its radiant rays
 The little people stop their parties and plays
 They scatter and dive into the ocean blue
 This cycle will go on for many more days

 The waves wash ashore and no one is here
 No traces left behind of the little people, so dear
 Their secret is kept by the sun and the moon
 You never will find them, no matter how long you peer

 Nothing is ever as it seems
 On the shores of the bight blue sea
 While drifting about in mind and soul
 I dream up my sandcastle dreams2000,
