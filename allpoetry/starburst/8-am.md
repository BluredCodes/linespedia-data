---
title: "8 AM"
writer: "Starburst"
slug: "8-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Peacefully I lie here
 Not quite awake, yet not asleep
 Inside me, my mind's a river
 And my thoughts are running deep

 Through my window streams the morning sun
 I smile as it carresses my face
 I think of how this day will affect my life
 It's like adding a mile to life's unfinished race

 The day could end in triumph
 Or, sadly, in defeat
 But I am not the type of person
 To let myself be beat

 And so as I lie here
 More awake than I was before
 I know today I'll get up quickly
 To answer the knock on life's door
