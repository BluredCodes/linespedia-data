---
title: "The Dolphin's Song"
writer: "Starburst"

There once was a girl
 Who lived by the sea
 Where there's only sand
 Not even one tree

 She splashed in the ocean
 All the year round
 She played with the dolphins
 Loved hearing their sound

 One day in the sunset
 Her age six and a half
 She swam with the dolphins
 To have a good laugh
 
 Their song sung the moon
 Into the sky
 The little girl swam
 Her parents nearby

 While she was playing
 Her parents drifted away
 They went on a boat ride
 In the neighboring bay

 Their daughter, they figured
 Would be just fine
 Their fate would result
 That night in the brine

 And so they sailed off
 Into the dark
 Not thinking of their child
 On their journey they embarked

 They thought they'd return
 But wrong they were
 They decided to stay away
 Not thinking of her

 Finally, the girl
 Back at the shore
 Curled up in the sand
 And began to snore

 The dolphins stayed 
 Close to land that night
 So that they'd have 
 The child in their sight
  
 Eventually
 As the years passed by
 The girl became 
 Much like their kind

 She swam during the day
 Slept at night
 Even the sharks 
 Didn't give her a fright

 The summer was warm
 But it was coming to an end
 The dolphins got restless
 And began to leave their friend
 
 Ever so slowly
 They drifted away
 Moving more out to sea
 With each coming day

 When the girl saw
 The dolphins go
 She jumped in the water
 And followed, low and behold

 She migrated with them
 To a faraway land
 She was one of the sea
 Of fin and not hand

 She lived this way
 For many a year
 In the sea she was fine
 Of the land she had fear

 One day a boat
 Threw its nets in the sea
 It scared the dolphins away
 All but one who was wee

 To follow the pod
 The odd pair tried
 But then they saw blood
 Most others had died

 The two rose to the surface
 Of the ocean like glass
 Thinking of the present
 Remembering the past

 Years went by
 The two did grow
 They formed their own pod
 Friends and not foe

 They were resting at the surface
 One dark misty night
 A fisherman peered into the water
 And had quite a fright

 He rubbed his eyes
 To be sure of what he'd seen
 When he looked again
 He saw the water, green

 He sailed back to shore
 To share his strange story
 "It was a girl with the dolphins!"
 He said with glory

 The story spread 
 Throughout the land
 Everyone wanted to see
 The girl not of the sand

 She still lives with the pod
 In an unknown place
 Swimming throught the water 
 With a dolphins grace

 If you look closely 
 On these nights of mist
 You can see her swimming
 Full of glee and gist

 She leaps with the dolphins
 Swimming along
 They say she fell in love
 With the dolphins' song2000,
