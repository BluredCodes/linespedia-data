Three months gone
 Since tragic events
 Took place and rocked our great world
 Planes crashing and killing bystanders
 You slimy terrorist scum
 Your goal was not gained
 We are strongThis is a type of poem called a septet.  The format is 3,5,7,9,7,5,3, ( syllables per line).
