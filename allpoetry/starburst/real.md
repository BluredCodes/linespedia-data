I'm not sure how I feel about you
 We're becoming closer friends every day
 You make me smile and cheer me up
 We talk and don't run out of things to say
 Everyone thinks that you're not too smart
 But you've revealed your true self to me
 And I feel honored that you've done so
 Because not too many people really knopw the real you
 You're an intelligent person who has a lot to say
 You just can't express yourself all the time
 But that's okay, because I've heard you say what you think
 I've seen the real you
