Yes I know
It is now 7 Am and I have yet to be to bed
I am very close though
May pass out right here

So what to say?
hello
that seems to work sometimes

Dreams of copper clouds floating upside down 
under a hazelnut umbrella still freak me out

What?

Flowing ice sheets of exasperation 
slowly sink into our collective soul

Wierd

Jungle Rabbis give prase to a pagan god
as singing monkeys dance the forbidden dance

Garbage

I am drunk
like a skunk
in a funk

In a daze
my own haze
morning glaze

I doubt anyone will ever reaad this crap enough
 to get to this point, but if you did,

my sympathies.

rasta rule
be cool
stay in school


a happy fool


off to bedwrittin in a true manner
