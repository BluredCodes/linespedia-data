The fog covers the land like a wet blanket
The beasts in the field snort and stammer
An uneasiness has invaded the countryside
Like an invisable army of malcontent

The cock loudly crows and then is suddenly silent
A wind blows through the delapadated barn
Howling like a lost wolf pup starving and afraid
Fear washes ashore like a giant tide

Somewhere in this eirie scene lurks the hidden truth
Of ancient understandings and unconsious degrees
It floats under the surface like a jellyfish
Everready to sting  with tentecles of knowledge
