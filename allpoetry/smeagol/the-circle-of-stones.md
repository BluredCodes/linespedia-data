---
title: "The Circle of Stones"
writer: "Smeagol"
slug: "the-circle-of-stones"

In the soft falling moonlight
On a cold misty midnight
Within the circle of stones

The young maiden knew no shame
Though in nakedness she came
Within the circle of stones

The druids began their chants
The maiden began her dance
Within the circle of stones

Gaelic prayers rose to the sky
A sheep was brought forth to die
Within the circle of stones

But alas evil stirred
From the hills a horn was heard
Within the circle of stones

The cold night grew deathly still
Then they appeared over the hill
Within the circle of stones

Soldiers come from hated Rome
A thousand leauges from their home
Within the circle of stones

One druid raised high his rod
"Take back thy Christian God"
Within the circle of stones

The maiden's eyes brimmed with tears
The Romans lowered their spears
Within the circle of stones

Not one pagan turned to run
Not one lived to see the sun
Within the circle of stones

Blood washes away with rain
But the gaelic souls remain
Within the circle of stones

In the soft falling moonlight
On a cold misty midnight
Within the circle of stones
