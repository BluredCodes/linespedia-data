---
title: "Goldfish Lament"
writer: "Smeagol"

Back and forth
Back and forth

Up and down
Up and down

All day long
Round and round

Under the bridge
Over the rocks

Up and down
Back and forth

Stareing out into your world
Wondering what wind is like

Listening to the noise of your life
Wondering what dreams are like

Hovering next to the broken ship
Watching you pass all day long

Back and forth
Back and forth
