---
title: "The Mask of God"
writer: "Smeagol"
slug: "the-mask-of-god"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24449-The-Mask-of-God-by-Smeagol"
---

Dreams of divinity
Whispers of wisdom
Who shall wear the Mask of God?

Songs of solitude
Poems of passion
Who shall wear the Mask of God?

Tales of triumph
Verses of vunrability
Who shall wear the Mask of God?

Fountains of fortitude
Labrynths of lovliness
Who shall wear the Mask of God?

Be not afraid
The Mask of God is but the Face of Man
For divinity dwells within

Be reborn unto yourself
Believe in your own higher power
Follow your bliss

Gatherings of greatness
Expressions of extasy
We shall wear the Mask of God!
