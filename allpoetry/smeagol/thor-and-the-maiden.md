Mighty God of Thunder
That the Norse did name Thor
One day came to Earth
And walked along the shore

He met a beautiful maiden
With beautiful flaxen hair
Who brought him to her home
And her bed with him did share

And after many hours
Of passion hot and hard
Mighty Thor did leave the mortal
And return to Asguard

But after many moons
Mighty Thor did return
To the flaxen haired maiden
His passion again did burn

He came to her home
And knocked upon the door
The maiden peered outside
He said "It is I - Thor"

The door was then opened
By the maid timid and weak
She gasped and said "Your Thor?
