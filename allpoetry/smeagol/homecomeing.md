The lone figure approached the dark house
His hands shook in his pockets
Nervous
Unsure
Afraid

Too long had he stayed away
Running from shadows and whispers of the past
Tormented
Haunted
Hunted

Now cloaked by darkness and time
He has returned to the scene of the crime
Questioning
Searching
Wanting

An unbearable need to know the truth
Has forced him back to this door
Unrelenting
Unstopable
Unending

The most important moment in his life has arived
But yet he falters and waits
Scared
Timid
Weak

Then the choice is made for him as the house lights come on
Flooding him with the white light of memories
Understanding
Awakening
Acceptence

We live with ourselvesI am not really sure what this is about, it just kind of came out.
