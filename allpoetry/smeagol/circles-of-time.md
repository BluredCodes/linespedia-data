Circles of time
Words that rhyme
A rusty dime
A silent mime
Lemon and lime
A pool of slime
Words that rhyme
Circles of time

My only hope
End of my rope
Hard to cope
Using new soap
Gave up dope
Yes means nope
End of my rope
My only hope

One last dance

Inside my pants 
One last dance
