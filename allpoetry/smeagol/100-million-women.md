title: "100 Million Women"

Red roses have covered the world
for 100 million women
Symphonies and sonnets are born
for 100 million women
Love is the true breath of eternal life
for 100 million women

100 Million Women
100 Million Women

My very soul I would freely give
for just one more moment that I could live
