Imagination

To see pirate ships
in billowing clouds,
to hope for peace
surrounded by war,
to hear colors
and to see a song,
imagination expresses
in multitude fashion.

A child's-eye view,
precocious and precious,
yet imprecise,
freedom of thought,
freedom to think,
emotions felt deeply
yet thin-skinned,
an adult's dream.

Fly on mental wings,
to battle in the clouds -
a swashbuckling hero,
physically perfect,
assured and strong,
or talk with beasts,
cure disease and hunger,
and play with friends.

Without dreams
we are without hope.
Without hope 
we are without a future.
Without a future
we are doomed
to a bleak life
of desperation.
