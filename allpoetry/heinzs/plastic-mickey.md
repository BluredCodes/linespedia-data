Plastic Mickey

O' artifact of love's behest
The joyfulness of your bequest -
Ensconced within this plastic shell,
A heart I thought I knew so well!

But lo, I fear my fumbling arms
Shall drop this favorite of your charms,
And if it falls it's sure to break -
I anguish at this grave mistake!

Your gift of love lies broken now -
I'm going to cry and have a cow.
I'll sit and mope around the house -
I broke your plastic Mickey Mouse!
