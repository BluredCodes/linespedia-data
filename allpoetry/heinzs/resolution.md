Resolution

Fear unbridled...
Hopelessness and stagnation
Inhibits every move or action.
The great demotivator
Clasps its icy grip
On hearts and minds alike.

With resolve alone
Must free will retake what's lost;
Mend with hope the rent fabric
Of trust and with the glue of love
Repair the sundered psyche
And raise it from deep depression.

Intellect itself
Falls prey to the abyss,
Feeding on self-doubt
And rampant insecurity.
Without faith doomed
To wallow in dark despair.

Resolution,
Self awareness and confidence builds
A new and stronger foundation
Beneath that teetering frame.
What once we thought our destined due
Each day we needs must earn anew.

12-17-2001
