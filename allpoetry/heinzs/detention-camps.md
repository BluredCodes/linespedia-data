Detention Camps

Detain the suspects.
No civil rights
or Geneva Convention.
No representation
or habeas corpus
because their skin
is olive or yellow
or they worship the East.
Innocent Arabs,
Innocent Japanese,
treated like Jews in Germany.
Rounded up
shipped out
to detention camps.
Hitler went too far
we follow close behind.
Political genocide
livelihoods ruined
families sundered
first step to fascism
leads frighteningly to
the "final solution".

11-29-2001
