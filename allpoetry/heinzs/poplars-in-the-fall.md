title: "Poplars in the Fall"
writer: "heinzs"

Steadfast rows of tall pillars
clothed in green finery
daubed with rainbow hues
reach majestically 
into the grey autumn sky.
Attired in fall splendor
to celebrate season's change
they bide their time
and slowly carpet the ground
at their feet in lush gold.
Soon enough bare branches
will wait for verdant spring,
but for now, festooned in glory,
the poplars stand guard
and wait for the next rain.

11-14-2001
