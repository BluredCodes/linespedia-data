Ignorance

Who's the idiot who coined the phrase
"Ignorance is bliss"?
How wrong can anyone be
and still walk the Earth?

What you don't know
can kill you!
(Or at least maim you
or hurt like bloody hell)

Of course the same is true
of what you DO know...
so does this mean
we have a choice?

Actually, NO!
Whatever eventuality
brings us our final end,
known or unknown,
will be one hell of a surprise
when it comes!
