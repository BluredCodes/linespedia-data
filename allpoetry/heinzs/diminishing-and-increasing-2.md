Diminishing and Increasing (2)

(increasing 2)

Once
more
time 
stalls.

It seems that
nothing else
is ever
really real.

Agonizing pain's
emotional toll
reduces the soul -
a whimpering mass.

Can it be the gospel truth
or is it our delusion?
What we think reality,
is really an illusion.

(decreasing 2)

Desperately, what we choose
in that moment of desire,
in love's grasp - irrational -
must last us through a lifetime.

Not knowing the "why",
yet doing the "what" -
blissful ignorance
faults passion's motives.

Life and love
heed the call -
both must rise
lest we fall!

No 
man
stays
free!
