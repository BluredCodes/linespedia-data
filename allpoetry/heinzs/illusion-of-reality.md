~Illusion of Reality~

What transpires is illusion.
Reality is a dream -
oftimes a nightmare.
Yet nothing exists
beyond perception.

Strangely enough, the
unperceived may still
have a profound effect.

A tree falling in the forest,
absent witnesses,
still makes a great sound.
So that greater realm
beyond our senses
may still affect our lives.

This must be a testament
to rationalize faith.
The unseen, the unknown
inspire awe and fear
and, above all, hope!

Nearer, my God, to thee
each day I shall ever be.
