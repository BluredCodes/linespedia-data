Another Sunrise

Another sunrise -
and nothing has changed.
Children will go hungry again tonight
and seek their bed in any available
sheltered nook or cranny.

Another sunrise -
and nothing has changed.
Mothers will cry in anguished grief
for sons and husbands lost
to the constant evil of war.

Another sunrise - 
and nothing has changed.
Political and religious rhetoric
will flow like sacramental wine -
a largess of disinformation
designed to lull our sense
of indignation in a
misguided patriotic fervor.

Another sunrise -
and nothing has changed.
The almighty dollar, symbol of greed,
will reaffirm its unquestioned claim
of dominance over the lives
of the 5 billion teeming souls
struggling to survive the day
so they may yet again see
another sunrise.

11-21-2001
