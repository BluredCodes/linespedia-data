XIX Olympiad (Winter 2002)

Personal best
the athletes' quest -
both strong and bold
reaching for gold.

Eleven nations compete this year
with but a single athlete
to carry forward their hopes -
teams of one - each a flagbearer.

Salt Lake setting -
all are betting
their own athlete
can best compete.

When all is said and done at the end,
for whom was this gala intended?
Modern gladiators on snow and ice -
will they wear the victors laurels?

What use is fame -
this costly game?
Hunger still thrives
and destroys lives!

The hoopla and pageantry done,
has anything really been won,
or have we not all really lost
and all bear the pyrric cost?

2/23/2002
