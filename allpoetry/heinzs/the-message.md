Nelson Mandela is free - 
a hero with a mission, like Ghandi. 
A message of peace and freedom, 
tolerance and unification. 

While zealots can rationalize 
cruel acts of terror, 
men of conscience speak 
but the world does not listen.
 
Doomed in the end to suffer
and repeat again past pain, 
eternal vigilance cannot suffice
with blood of innocents the price.

10/29/2001
