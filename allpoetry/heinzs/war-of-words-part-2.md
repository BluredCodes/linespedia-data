War of Words (part 2)

The battle rages still
though we have had our fill...
giant egos butting heads
when we should all be in our beds.
The verbal jousts a bore
the best thing to ignore...
give it another day
and it will go away.
For now I recommend
some time we each should spend
to read from the "Forgot"...
there really are a lot!
Beautiful poems hiding
on the back page... biding
their time to come to light...
I think it's only right!
