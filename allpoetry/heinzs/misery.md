Misery 

Anguish the heart forlorn, 
grieve the love forsaken, 
so it has always been 
the bittersweet plight of man. 
Seek that moment of rapture 
where pleasure's bounds unwrap, 
while joy and pain together strike 
the harmonic chords of the soul 
Love is, and has always been, 
the sweet meat of literature ~ 
tales told and retold time and again. 
Tristan and Isolde, Romeo and Juliet ~ 
Love's tragedies abound across cultures. 
Diverse societies share the tale 
of the star-crossed lovers' travail 
and ultimate untimely doom. 
We compare and identify 
our own failed romances, 
grateful that others, heroes, 
have fared worse than we. 
Why such sad happenstance 
draws the intellect and emotion's eye, 
instead of a successful happy ending ~ 
the answer is simple in the end ~ 
above all, misery loves company!
