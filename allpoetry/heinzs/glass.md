~Glass~

clear
tinted
opaque
frosted
phenomenon
silica plus heat
Nature's obsidian
Man's windowpanes
to see out of 
his concrete caves
silvered mirror
revealing all
his faults

what a wonderful material
to come from sand
dust to dust
shards to ashes
in the end
sand
again

2-13-2002
