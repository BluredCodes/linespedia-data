Thirty years of wedded bliss?
No, not quite so.
Thirty years of partnership
through life's tribulations...
fortune and misfortune,
joy and pain.
Births and deaths,
happiness and grief alike, shared
by two souls in this journey of life,
together.

Our marriage is a work in progress,
ever changing texture.
Sometimes comfortable,
sometimes harsh.
Just a slice of life
and just as real.
Surviving only through
committment and effort
and stubborn determination
to make it work!

Held together by bonds of love,
steel-strong, yet resilient,
able to stretch as needed
without breaking asunder.
Strength derived from mutual will
determined to survive together.
One moment, one hour, one day...
and then another, and so on.
Learning to accept each life experience
as another new adventure.

Reveling in the joys and successes
and rolling with the punches.
And thirty years from now
we will look back once again,
then go forward as before
to thirty years more.

4.15.2001
