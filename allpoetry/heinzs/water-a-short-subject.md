Water (a short subject)

liquid - oxygen dihydride
liquid - dihydrogen oxide
what we know as H2O
the substance of the river's flow.

solid - dihydrogen oxide
solid - oxygen dihydride
what we know as H2O
covers icecaps with white snow.

gas - oxygen dihydride
gas - dihydrogen oxide
what we know as H2O
makes the steam generators go.

2-12-2002
