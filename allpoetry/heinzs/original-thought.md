Original Thought

Each life discovers the world anew
Building a unique experience bank
From the empty vault at birth
To full richness by last bell.

Yet each must meet and overcome
Similar daily obstacles -
Life's inimitable lessons
As well as routine survival needs.

Every action, every conceptual thought
Has been tried and processed
By multiple human intellects
Over man's earthly manifestation.

"There's nothing new under the sun."
A truism if ever there was one -
So how to define that uniqueness -
That individual originality?

Though all words have been spoken
Once, twice - a dozen times or more -
It is the juxtaposition of ideas,
The combinations that become singular.

Originality lies in synthesis and restructuring -
Building new architectural features
With the materials left to be rediscovered,
Recycled, refurbished, reused and re-examined.
