Sung to the tune of "A Froggy would a-wooing go"


Mammajen would a-wooing go,
Hey Ho sez Rowley,
Whether her husband would let her or no,
With a Rowley-Powley gammon and spinach.

She doesn't know how she'll pay the rent,
Hey Ho sez Rowley,
Now that all the money's been spent,
Hey Ho sez Antmonkey Rowley.

But it will all turn out in the end,
Hey Ho sez Rowley,
She may even stay in Bonnie England,
With a Rowley-Powley gammon and spinach.

To England the two boys both can be sent,
Hey Ho sez Rowley,
And they'll live in Ant's Morning Glory tent,
Hey Ho sez Antmonkey Rowley.
