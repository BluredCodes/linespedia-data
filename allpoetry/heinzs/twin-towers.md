Twin towers of steel and glass, 
now on the ground, a tumbled mass! 
Majestic you stood, reaching high... 
man's attempt to touch the sky. 
With deep horror we watched you burn. 
Our eyes were not allowed to turn 
away, lest we not fully see 
your fate and final destiny. 

What fearful evil mind 
conceived this act... is Justice blind? 
Retribution cannot be denied 
the thousands who this day have died. 
Yet let us honor their surcease 
by forging forward towards peace. 
Their souls, we hope, will be at rest 
and only Time will stand the test.Written on 9/12 after the terrorist attack.
