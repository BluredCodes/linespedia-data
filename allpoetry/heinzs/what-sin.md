What Sin?

For what unholy sin
did Heaven create the punishment
of commuting?

What blaspheme did I commit
to warrant this cruel
retribution?

Interminably trapped
in traffic's strangle hold
twice each day.

On days that I must drive
I am a nervous wreck
for several hours.

Commuting on the bus
twice daily traps me
with a demonic driver.

Angels hear me - I repent!
This daily torture you have sent,
please relent!

11/1/2001
