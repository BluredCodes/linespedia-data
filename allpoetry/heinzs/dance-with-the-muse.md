Dance with the Muse

The muse grooves
pen to paper
thought to page
dreams expressed
as words presage.

She dances
in the moonlight -
midnight waltz -
every step
both true and false.

As we spin 
feel the magic! 
Come with me -
take my hand 
eternally! 

When 'tis shared
the spirit grows -
poets' Muse...
we, her love
cannot refuse
