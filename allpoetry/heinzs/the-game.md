The Game

We play at life like a game,
building up our strategy
then taking our turn 
to move upon the board.
Advantage shifts to and fro
with each roll of the dice.
Once begun, this game of life
neither ceases nor pauses
until its final, inevitable conclusion.
In the end one will be the victor -
unless a draw is declared.
Stalemate or split decision
meets with icy reception.
There is but one way 
to end the game -
but how does one keep score?
We play the game
to win, after all... don't we?
It will be The Judge's decision
whether we have won or lost -
so let us concentrate
on how we play the game.

11-20-2001
