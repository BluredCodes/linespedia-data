Teletubbies

They stare at me with unblinking eyes
from innocent plastic faces.
What are they?  No one seems to know.
They beguile small children
every day with their wiles...
mind control?  Brainwashing?
The gurgling sun rises and sets,
like a Barbara Geddes nightmare.
They are alien invaders
sucking out our minds...
Tinky-Winky, Dipsy, La-La, Po...
what frighteningly demonic soul
could create such inanity?
Beware all ye who henceforth follow...
they are the terrifying Teletubbies!

12/25/2001
