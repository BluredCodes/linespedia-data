My Cup Runneth Over

I shall partake of thee
as if thou wert of
finest spirit - a rare wine.
Your bouquet already fills
my palate with its
sweet liquor.
I savor you, and in that touch
your soul shall open
redemption's door.
Each ort, each morsel,
sanguined in glory
I make my own.
Your heavenly scent
infuses me with
soft desire.
A touch shall be my wish -
your warmth against
my pallid flesh.
Yearning hunger
feeds my ardor
with passion's fruits.
To taste of your sweet lips
shall carry me
to drunken ecstasy.

I long to hold you
in my arms
and savor of
your heavenly charms.

3/8-3/11 2002
