The Beginning of the Beginning

Vast nothing.
Vast silence.
Vast darkness.
The vastness of space
awaits its destiny.

A point singularity stirs -
nothing spontaneously
separates in a cataclysm
of positive-negative
divergence.

The vastness remains silent
but in that moment,
that spark of creation,
Time itself begins
its relentless journey.

Slowly, over millennia,
the brightness diminishes
as it spreads outward,
becoming countless points of light
imbedded in that dark backdrop.

And now, aeons later,
in one distant corner
of that vast universe,
one young man looks up
and marvels at its wonders.

2-6-2002
