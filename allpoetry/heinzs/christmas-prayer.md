Christmas Prayer

This Christmas...
May the wishes for peace on Earth
At last establish global worth.
May mothers' fears and desperate hope
Be quieted so they may cope
And families now torn asunder
Reunite in time of wonder.

This Christmas...
May the hungry and the destitute
Find succor in our resolute
And human will at last to end
The suffering and start to mend
Man's broken dream of paradise.
We make our own... that's no surprise.

This Christmas...
May the dreaded specter of disease
In human minds be put at ease.
May Science find divine insight
To fuel the universal fight
Against the pain and devastation
Wracking each and every nation.

This Christmas...
May the family now gathered here
Its mission form and resound clear,
The love we share support us all,
For all must stand so none will fall.
United purpose serves our goal...
The bell of destiny begins to toll.

That said and done I call upon
Whatever spirits we rely on
To guide us from this moment forth
So each fulfill potential worth.
May peace and joy abound this year.
May each of us live without fear.
Prosperity and health be our reward
And purpose be our two-edged sword.

Amen.
