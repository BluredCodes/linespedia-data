Benji  
(Christmas 2001)

Dearest son,
dearest of all the angels,
once more remembrance
moves my tearful soul.
Not that you are not most often
an active participant,
a furnishing in my mind,
but just one of those times
memory more poignantly strives
to insinuate itself to the fore.
I shall continue to miss you
until the day I shall once more
hold your hand and look upon your face
in His eternal presence.
Until then, my angel,
I will hold your memory dear
and send to you
these small timely tributes.

Dad.

(In memoriam, 
Benjamin Patrick Scheuenstuhl,
4-1-1981 to 9-7-1982)
