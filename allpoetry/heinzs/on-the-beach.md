title: "On the Beach"
writer: "heinzs"

We compete on Life's teeming beach, 
foraging among the tidal deposits... 
no different from our fellow crabs. 
Mostly indifferent to all 
but our own immediate needs, 
we pay scant attention 
to other beaches 
remote from our shore. 
But a cataclysmic event... 
whether man's own terror 
or some astronomical 
or geological phenomenon... 
has disturbed our peaceful sphere. 
The shock waves have set 
the normally complacent sea 
into a state of imminent unrest. 
On the horizon we see 
the pale reflection 
of our approaching fate. 
All we can do is watch 
and wait... 
there is no escape... 
there is no high ground. 
The tsunami of terror 
will rage across our island home 
leaving death and destruction 
in its wake. 
Those of us who may survive 
will have a desolate land 
to inhabit and rebuild. 
But rebuild we must! 
And we must continue so 
with each successive wave 
until our destiny is at last fulfilled 
and man's time on Earth 
is finally at its end.
