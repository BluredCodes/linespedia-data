I take the liberty of expressing these feelings on behalf of all your friends here.  I am sure none would object and I take full responsibility for my words.

Friends (for bigmammajen)

Jennifer,
brave heart,
taking on the world's cares,
faltering from the immense weight
placed upon it.
Surgeons may repair
physical defects of stress,
but only friendship and love
can repair that damage
to the deepest psyche -
the heart of the soul.
The medics have done their chore -
it is now our joyful task
to perform that next magic
that only we, her friends can do.
From the bottom of our hearts
know this -
we support and love you,
sight unseen,
without demand or expectation -
unconditionally.
May the future bring what it will,
we will remain your soul's partners
on this brief journey of life,
connected by technological marvels
as well as these invisible bonds
of friendship.
