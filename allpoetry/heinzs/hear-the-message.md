title: "Hear the Message"

What hath God wrought
in this creature, Man?
Could it all be for naught?
What is His final plan?

The message so far is unclear,
Man listens, but can't seem to hear.

God's purpose is plain --
preserve life at all cost.
Peace we must gain,
or eternity's lost.

The messge will set Man's soul free.
He looks, but he can't seem to see.

Ego clouds the senses,
obscures rational thought.
Break down the false defenses --
or all will be forgot.

The message is all that is real,
Man touches, but can't seem to feel.


Man must learn to feel, see and hear,
to preserve all he holds most dear.
