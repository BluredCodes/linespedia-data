Where passion abounds
Hearts in unison sing songs
Reveling in love

Lovers duets sing
Feeling mutual heartstrings
Plucking every note

Emotions soar high
Lust adds steaming heat to all
Music crescendoes

Bodies intertwine
Individuality
Lost in sweet embrace

Orgasmic shudders
Peacefully recline again
In each other's arms
