Inaction

Inaction,
giant hernias, 
strangling the very bowels 
of man's existence.
Time wasted...
forever lost 
doing nothing.

Inaction 
is the silent assassin
of Time!  
Destroyer of Will!
Grief for missed opportunities
becomes Regret 
that poisons and rots the soul.
