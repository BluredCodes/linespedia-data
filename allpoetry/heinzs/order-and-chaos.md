Order and Chaos 

Stars and constellations 
fly through endless space 
in defined orbits - 
like electron particles 
in some cosmic molecule. 
Mathematics defines 
the celestial order. 
Interacting forces 
impose a great balance 
on the fabric of the universe. 
Design reveals itself 
at every opportunity. 

And yet, within this vastness 
lies a great mass of dark matter - 
unknown, unseen - chaotic. 
Particles from galactic collisions 
traverse space at random - 
attempting to subvert the order 
and finally plunge all 
into ever-growing black holes. 
This may be the final fate, 
the end of time, the ultimate.
