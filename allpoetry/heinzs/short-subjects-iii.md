Short Subjects iii

Treadmill

walk the walk
gaze blankly ahead
mileage racks up
the end destination
exactly where you started
_____________________
Daily Routine

feathers fly
blood spatters on white
morning cockfight
pecking order established
announce loudly
troubled peace until tomorrow
________________________
Reclamation

cracks in pavement
blades of grass
nature reestablishing
supremacy
overgrows all

11-29-2001
