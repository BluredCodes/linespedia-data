Arachne's Web

The Weaver works wonders
Life's tapestry to unfold -
each thread a different hue,
woven with finite precision...
the removal of even one will cause
the weaving to separate at the flaw.

The grand design takes shape
in a rampant rainbow of colors -
joys and sorrows, wars and plagues -
the rise and fall of civilizations -
each scene embroidered and woven
into the ever-changing backdrop.

The shuttle zips at lightspeed
between the warp and weft.
At intervals undetermined
some threads are cut
and left to dangle a while
before being worked into the tapestry.

Our lives inexorably intertwine
at the Weaver's whim and fancy -
each thread a vital part
of the developing whole.
We are never to know 
when our thread shall break.
