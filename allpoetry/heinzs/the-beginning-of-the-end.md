The Beginning of the End

Darkness, pressure increasing -
the steady lub-dub
no longer in familiar rhythm
and fading away.
Can't kick, legs tangled -
arms bound by some 
external barrier.
What is this new sensation?
LIGHT!
The murmuring voices become louder
as if no longer muffled
by a liquid sea.
An irresistible urge ...
to breathe!
Lungs fill to capacity -
what freedom!
Cry for joy!

"It's a girl!"
The nurse places
the now silent newborn
upon mom's belly
and their eyes meet... 
for the first time!
