I've been away so long -
even now I'm not all the way back.
I don't know where I was.
It must have been a sad place,
a bad place,
without you there.

Am I still welcome here?
I'd like to come in from the cold.
I'd like to come home.
I seem to be lost.  
Where are you?
Where am I?

I need directions
and your love to guide me.
Set it in the window
and I will see the light,
like a moth,
drawing me near.

I want to embrace
the warmth of your flame.
Though it burn me,
I am inexorably 
attracted to you -
like a magnet.

I have started
on the journey to you.
The road is rough,
the potholes many.
I stumble often,
unsure on my feet.

Can you bear
my tortuous advance?
So much pain
on both sides felt,
can we endure
yet a bit more?

I do not question
my own sincerity.
Only my actions
can hope to demonstrate
that to you.
And yet I falter.


Irrational fear... of what?
It chains me with inaction.
In desperation I plead
for your patience,
already o'ertaxed.
