title: "Yes and No"

Music
to my ears.
Noise - you say,
seeking an argument ~
for what reason today?

Beauty
in my sight.
Plain and shabby,
you reply.
Arguing again ~
do you even know why?

Whatever I say,
you disagree.
Contradiction is
a way of life for me.

I say "I love you!"
You reply, "Me too!"
... and all is
suddenly well.
