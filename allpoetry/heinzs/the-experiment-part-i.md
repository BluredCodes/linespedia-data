As I sit and ponder,
In my mind I wonder
At Man's existence.
The image of persistence
Is one that worries me --
We are an experiment, you see.
Just as we, in culture dishes,
Grow disease bacteria vicious,
So our own manifestation 
Is a biological infestation
On this culture globe, our Earth.
We are tested for our worth
By super beings - gods, I guess,
Who like to put us under stress,
And in their scientific test
Find out how we respond the best.
Laboratory rats on a human scale --
The evidence will tell the tale.
We jump through hoops before us set,
Like Pavlov's dogs respond, and yet,
Something greater we've achieved.
For this I sigh, and am relieved,
For in our own inimitable fashion
We have developed what we call - Compassion!
