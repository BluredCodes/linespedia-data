Encounter 

"Hi! Nice to meet you.
What's your sign?"
"Cancer."
"Way cool! I'm a Capricorn.
That make us opposites!
Did you know that opposites attract?"
"Did you know that water and earth
make mud?"
"Huh?"
"Oh, skip it... there's my date.
See you around."
"Uh... OK... bye?"Just a little diversion for the fun of it.
