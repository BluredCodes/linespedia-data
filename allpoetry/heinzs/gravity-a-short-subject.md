Gravity (short subject)

Measured in joules and dynes,
forces exert their influence ~
body to body ~
mass to mass ~
locking the planets in orbits
around their parent star.

My rollerblade strikes
an unseen rock
and I experience the thrill
of gravity ~ first-hand.
The World rushes up to meet me
and I greet it ~ face-to-face.

2-12-2002
