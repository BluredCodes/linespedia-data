~Spirit Link~

High above, beyond treetop's reach,
he soars on feathered wings.
Freedom unlimited -
brave heart beating -
the entire landscape beneath him
is his unquestioned domain.

My spirit flies at his side,
my earth-bound flesh left behind.
I feel the rush of air -
I am one with the clouds -
and in this psychic revelation
I grow in inner strength and resolve.

At one with Nature's gifts,
accepting Earth's bounteous marvels,
peace fills my eager heart
as it beats to the shaman's drum.

The ritual is now at its end -
the Eagle becomes my guide and friend.
