Good Night, Already!

1:00 AM again.
I sit here staring blankly
at the empty pages.
Whatever possessed me
to resolve a daily writing
before my rest?
Insomnia?
I have penned volumes 
of senseless drivel -
destined for the dustbin.
The fool's muse taunts me,
while mine own
has long since gone to bed!
This is not writers' block -
just nature's way
of telling me...
"Turn off the damn light
and go to sleep!"
