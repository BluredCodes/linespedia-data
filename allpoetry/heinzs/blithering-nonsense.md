Blithering Nonsense

There is a fine line
between dream and reality.
Perception alone suffices not
to maintain severe separation.
Indeed it can augment
that blithe confusion
with its characteristic
misapprehensions.
My mind obfuscates
and purposely creates
grey areas of thought -
somewhere between logic
and utter madness -
as if terrified of clarity.
A symphony of words
flows forth in profusion
adding to the muddled state
my sanity is already in.
I do not recognize my thoughts,
and all is as it was
at the tower of Babel.

Confusion of reality, it seems
is the major purpose of our dreams.

3/2-3/6 2002
