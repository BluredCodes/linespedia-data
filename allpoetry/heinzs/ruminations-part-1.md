Ruminations (part 1)

"We're not ahead, we're behind.
We're not above, we're below...

Like the brush in the painter's hand,
we have no idea where we are."
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Rumi

...how true yet today the words.
Is it not painfully clear
that some design other than our own
directs the cosmic orchestra
in which we play upon our own instrument.
Individually our tunes may be sweet
or devoid of tonality altogether,
while the collective whole
plays the harmony of the universe.
In fact, we are the instruments
in the celestial musicians' hands,
and we are the brushes that daub color
upon the eternal canvas.
Where we alight next we know not,
nor can we predict which pigment
or which note we shall next play.
Let us strive to play our notes clear,
and mark well our page without fear.

12/28/2001
