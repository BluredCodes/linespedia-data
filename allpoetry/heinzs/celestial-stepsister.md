title: "Celestial Stepsister"

The moon shines,
her pockmarked face
ever showing one side
to her celestial stepsister.

Aeons of cycles,
full to new, light to dark,
and crescents inbetween.
Time ticking relentlessly by.

What tales could she tell,
our ever-present observer?
What killed off the dinosaurs,
and did she see God create Adam?

The moon remains silent,
keeping her secrets
evermore to herself,
and shines.

11-13-2001
