Ruminations  (part 4)

"... These thoughts have such power over you.
From nothing you become sad,
From nothing you become happy."
~~~~~~~~Rumi

Strange indeed how the mind can slip
from one extreme unto the other.
The line between emotions is thin...
almost non-existent.
We slip altogether too easily
from joy to grief...
from contentment to despair...
from love to hate.
What triggers such dichotomy...
such schizophrenic shifts?
It is a universal truth
that for each and every positive event
an equal and opposite negative exists.
This does not, however, hold true
that the obverse is inescapable.
Though balance seems to demand
this cosmic tit-for-tat, in fact
these concepts represent only potentials,
not pre-destined physical realities.
Man's God-given free will permits, nay demands
that he not sit idly by
while the world crumbles to ruin around him.
We have the power to deny evil...
to hold the pendulum's swing in place
on the positive side of existence.
That is our greatest task, our mission,
to control our acts prior to commission.
