There is a war waging,
a furious battle raging.
The poets are fighting,
backstabbing and biting,
a war of words on the internet.
No one will win, I'll hazard a bet.
Across the ether they fling invective,
their choice of target only semi-selective.
Personal attacks have joined the rounds,
and several have withdrawn to lick their wounds.
Their parting shots cannot stem the tide,
resolution remains for the other side.
They cackle and gloat and revel in glory,
immensely proud of their pyrric victory.
