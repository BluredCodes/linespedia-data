Fragments (1)

Black shapes slither
in the dark shadows,
flitting through the sight
at the corners of my eyes.

My mind sees them
only as movement -
undefined creatures
lurking at the edge of light.

Are they demons 
seeking some mischief,
impish in intent?
I fear their hidden motive.

They hound my trail,
following my steps
through the darkened streets -
ever seeming to close in.

I walk faster,
my goal approaches -
safety in numbers
my only chance.

The streetlamps
and the noise from the bar
seem to keep
the shadows at bay.

Gratefully I join the revels
of the ignorant crowd.
But what shall I do
at closing time?
