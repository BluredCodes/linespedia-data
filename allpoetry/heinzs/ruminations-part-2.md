Ruminations  (part 2)

"Oh heart,
Let go of all your worries about the future.
In this friendless world, come and join us."
~~~~~~~Rumi

In unity there is strength.
In community there is support.
Alone, whether by choice or happenstance,
We are destitute and doomed
To suffer grief and pain.
Hope thrives and uplifts the soul
In the company of family and friends.
Seek out those about you
Of like will and mind.
Compassion returns upon itself
And multiplies its actions
In accordance with the three-fold law.
If you seek love you must first give it.
This is the ultimate irony,
That servitude can make one free.
