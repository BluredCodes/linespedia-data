Empty Words

Empty words 
and good intentions -
man's two most
abused inventions.

Never clear,
causing much trouble,
strife and pain
they seem to double.

How can one
avoid these errors,
stop the spread
of grief and terrors.

Strive to reach
with great elation,
finally -
communication.
