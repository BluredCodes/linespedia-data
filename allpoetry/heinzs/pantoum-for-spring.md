When the Monarch butterflies returnIt heralds the season's change.From Winter's death, life renews.Verdant growth signals the rebirth.It heralds the season's changeWhen trees new leaves do grow.Verdant growth signals the rebirth -A new season - a new year.When trees new leaves do growThe heart fills anew with joy and love.A new season - a new yearTo treasure God's gifts from above.The heart fills anew with joy and love -From Winter's death, life renews -To treasure God's gifts from aboveWhen the Monarch butterflies return.11/1/2001Interesting format.
