Earth's changing canvas
once more turns to Autumn gold.
The die-off before Winter
promising luscious green's return
after the snowy gesso dries.
Life's rainbow glows and shines,
an inspiration to artists' hope.
Refresh the palette, clean the brush,
and fill the blank void with song!
Colorfully mastered,
with heart's-blood imbued,
to hang in the eternal gallery.Just a short "comment".
