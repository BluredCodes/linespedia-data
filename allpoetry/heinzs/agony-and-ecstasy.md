Agony and Ecstasy

Oh the agony of Love!
Nothing can compare
to the pain of a lover's scorn.
It strikes at the very root
of the fragile soul.
Unrequited love
saps strength - weakening -
so dark depression
can settle in the heart.
Agony unbearable...

Oh the ecstasy of Love!
The joyous lovers' heart
soars high on gossamer wings.
The world becomes a garden
filled with earthly delight.
Strength and courage elate the soul
and no task seems unsurmountable.
Euphoria fills the day
with mindless bliss.
Ecstasy unbearable...

From one extreme to the other
the lovers psyche leaps
in manic schizophrenia,
agonizedly ecstatic...
unbearable!
