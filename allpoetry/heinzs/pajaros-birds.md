Pajaros

El Aguila es el simbolo del valor.
La Paloma es el simbolo de la paz.
Mi alma llora porque el Aguila esta manchado,
y tiene que sanar su ala rota,
y lo que pense ser una paloma blanca
era solamente una gallina comun.


12-21-2001



Birds

The Eagle is the symbol for Courage.
The Dove is the symbol for Peace.
My soul cries because the Eagle is soiled
and has to heal a broken wing,
and that which I thought to be a white Dove
was only a common chicken.
