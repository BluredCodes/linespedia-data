God's Gift

Love, four letter word,
pervades the human psyche -
from where does it stem?
Indeed, the ultimate source
is a spark from God, of course!

1-25-2002This is a challenge submission.  It's supposed to be a tanka on the origin of love.  I like ending with rhyming couplets... just my style. (grin)
