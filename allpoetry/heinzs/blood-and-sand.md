Blood and Sand

The sands of Samarkhand and Kurdestan
Absorb the blood of the fallen
Hero and foe alike, comingled in death.
One sanguine river flowing
Feeding the hungry earth
The same end for all, no mercy.
Time's cycle renews
Yet hiccups every now and then
And the tragedies long past
Repeat once more on a new pallette.
Where is today's messenger
The Heaven-sent messiah for our time?
In this world of 5 billion souls
Would we recognize the One?
We hear not the crying of millions dying
How can we hope to hear one voice
No matter how clear or true it may ring?
Yet that voice speaks to each
And every one of us, silently from within.
It is we who choose not to heed its call
The end of this will be man's fall.
