The End of the End

Ragnarok -
Judgment Day -
the final battle -
has it begun?

Streaming towards oblivion
and self-destruction -
where is that pause
of sane introspection?

We cling desperately
to our one five-billionth
of the planet -
unwilling to share, greedy for more.

Silently stalking,
travelling through the interstellar void,
Nemesis lurks
on its inevitable collision course.

There is no time
but the present -
live life to the full ...
it's heaven sent!
