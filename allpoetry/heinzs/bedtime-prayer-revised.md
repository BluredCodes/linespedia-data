Now I lay me down to sleep,
I pray the Lord my sould to keep.
If I should die before I wake,
I pray someone finds the body
before I start to stink to high heaven
and the maggots eat the flesh from my bones
and I become a rotting putrid mess...In between inspirations sometimes something ridiculous falls.  Kerplunk.
