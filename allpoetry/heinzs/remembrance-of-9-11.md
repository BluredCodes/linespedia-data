Remembrance of 9-11

Oh day of infamy,
those desperate hours,
6 months now gone
since fell twin towers.

We must not fail
to recollect,
hold in our hearts
the retrospect.

So many souls
too soon released,
their passions all
in terror deceased.

Let tributes play
our heartfelt sorrow,
renew our sacred vow
to build a new tomorrow.
