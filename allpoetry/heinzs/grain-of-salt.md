---
title: "Grain of Salt"
writer: "heinzs"
slug: "grain-of-salt"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why does man revel so
in twisting words to suit him?
Anything can be proven
by interpretation of a phrase
and the choice of a line.
A lie becomes truth,
fantasy becomes reality,
a part of what was said
becomes the sum and total.
We read between the lines
and take fragments out of context,
and then with dedication
and blind faith defend to the death
the ascribed translation.

What is just as sad, when in reverse
truth becomes fiction
and society's blind eye is focused
against bold and tangible fact.
Creaturs of intellect and logic,
we must foster these attributes.
Examine closely what we are spoon-fed
and rely on our powers of observation
and analytical reason.
Look with a clear, jaundiced eye.
Listen with an open mind.
Feel with compassion
and above all, ask questions!
Flavor your life with a grain of salt!

11-13-2001
