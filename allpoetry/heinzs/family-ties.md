Family Ties

The maple's leafless branches
fan a dark silhouette
against evening's last glow -
stark lines upon a backdrop
of blue-grey sky.
The crescent of the young
Ramadan moon pierces the grey,
like a cookie-cutter window
punched out of crepe paper.
Bats hunt their dinner of moths
while birds sing their goodnight.
The steaks sizzle on the coals -
the bright glow a spreading sphere
of light in the encroaching dark.
Our meal will celebrate our family -
united for this purpose alone...
a solid affirmation of mutual support.
Though individuals may wander
far and wide in search of fortune,
the whole remains intact.
Nothing can sunder these bonds,
these family ties of steel.
And so, the repast done,
we bid each other adieu
and once more go our separate ways,
secure in the knowledge
of our mutual pact.
