---
title: "The Alternative"
writer: "heinzs"
slug: "the-alternative"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Life...
	Live it!
Don't waste it complaining
about how "hard" it is.
Would you rather deal with
	the alternative?

Truth...
	Honor it!
Don't profane it by ignoring
or bending it to convenience.
Can we really live
	the alternative?

Justice...
	Demand it!
Don't demean it by letting
it be circumvented.
How can we ever condone
	the alternative?

Ideals...
	Foster them!
Don't risk their disappearing
by not training our young.
Can we leave them in a world of
	the alternative?

In a world of pro and con
It's us we must rely upon
To create a state positive...
	NOT the alternative!

11-19-1990
