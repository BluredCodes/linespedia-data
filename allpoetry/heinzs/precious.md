Precious

What is most precious?
Is there a gauge
by which we can measure?

Life seems to be
at the top of the list,
but how do we rank the rest?

Love -
it may be but a four-letter word,
yet it encompasses all existence.

Happiness -
even our forefathers, in their wisdom,
knew this could only be pursued.

A child's innocence -
once taken returns no more -
it must be cherished and protected.

Personal freedom -
in no nation on Earth but America
is this so dearly held sacred.

The artist's senses -
each field with unique demands -
staggering against handicap.

Time -
perhaps the most precious commodity
without which the others also fail.

Of all we hold dear,
cherish and value, above all else
hold sacred your personal integrity.
