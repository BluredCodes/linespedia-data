Evidence 
~Religion and Science unified~

I have heard it once said
by a sage and wise old man I know,
"Religion without Science is blind,
but Science without Religion is lame."
I have often pondered on the simple truth
that God has presented us this marvel, Earth,
to examine and explore from pole to pole.
The marvels He has created are ours
to learn and seek guidance from.
He created the evolutionary process
it took a Darwin to discover and understand.
But some refuse to see the truths in what He reveals,
relying instead on more ancient revelations
as if they were from His lips instead of the pens
of His fallible secretaries, the human observers.
God reveals new truths and insights 
each and every day this planet circles His sun.
The universe is His canvas, 
and He is not done yet with the painting.
Every atom of every molecule of every element
of this existence is spawned from His will.
Does he interfere with our development?
Only as a loving parent would try to guide
his child, but greater learning comes
when mistakes are made 
and consequences are suffered.
So it is with Man.
We learn from our mistakes,
and we become greater and wiser through them.
In time we are destined by our Father
to learn to control the atoms of our existence
and to overcome our human frailties
of mind and body and suspicion of our fellows.
Yet we should ever 
hold Him dear and never lose sight
of the evidence left by His mighty hand.
