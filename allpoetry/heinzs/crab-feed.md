As I walk 
By the sea, 
Crabs come up, 
Dancing for me. 

Each one leaps 
From rocks exposed, 
Glistening like dew... 
Heaven's tears deposed. 

In my heart 
Joy wells up... 
Ketchup and mustard... 
Let's now sup! 

Meaty and juicy, 
Never too tough, 
Only few know 
Pleasure this rough! 

Quietly I rest, 
Relaxing a while, 
Safe at last... 
Then I smile. 

Upon the dunes, 
Violet in hue, 
Waiting for me... 
Xenophobe's past due. 

You, my dove,
