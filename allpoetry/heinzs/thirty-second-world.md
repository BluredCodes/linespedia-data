Thirty-second World

Inundated, day in and out,
buy this, buy that...
you can't live without!

Life reduced to half-minute spots -
no wonder we are all
attention-deficited robots!

Slam, bam - thank you, ma'am!
Instant gratification is the rule -
who has time to give a damn?

We need a chance to catch our breath,
take pause - see the world...
grasp some enjoyment before death!

Take back our souls - our lives -
from the worshippers of Mammon -
the evil empire, on greed it thrives!

Multinational corporations the world rule,
petroleum and steel, tobacco and drugs -
they make of each of us the fool.

Unity of purpose - to live our dreams -
must be our guide and motivation -
reality is not what it seems.

Take back control of life's reins -
stop the rampaging lunacy
while yet the spark of hope remains.
