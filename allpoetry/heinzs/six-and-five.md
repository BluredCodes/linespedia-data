Six and Five

Tired baby wailing -
such deep-felt pathos.

Happy infant cooing -
with unbridled joy.

How simple it all seems
within childhood dreams.

But that is not the end -
it's the beginning.

Our perception matures
with experience.

What once seemed so easy
now make us queasy.

Desperation rages,
weighing down the soul.

Wishing it were not so, 
we strive to survive.

We do our conscious best
in life's constant quest.
