In my Dreams--you are attracted to me,and together we exerciseour passions' deep desire.In my Dreams--I am virile and capable,and together we intertwinein our intimate embrace.In my Dreams--our prowess knows no bounds,and together we experiencethe Kama Sutra of love.--In my Dreams.
