Addiction

Irrepressible urge,
obsessive compulsion,
must get that fix - at all costs!!
Lies,
stolen moments,
degradation of truth
and besmirched affection
become a way of life.
Defiance and denial
contrast to withdrawal's
belligerance and nervous
shuffling.
Risking all -
family, friends,
future and fortune
for that next hit.
Toke your life away -
I have already grieved
your loss.

11-25-2001
