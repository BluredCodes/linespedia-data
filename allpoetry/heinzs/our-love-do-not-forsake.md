title: "Our Love do not Forsake"

Our Love is deep, like a dark lake--
Yet shallow, that We cannot slake
Our thirst, for Fear's own sake!

Resentment brings the heart to ache
With pain akin to a driven stake--
I wish to mend and heal this break!

What's in the past I cannot unmake--
All I can do is undertake
To avoid repeating the same mistake.

All I have's for you to take--
My love is genuine, not fake!
If only I could this World remake!

In the end, of love We will partake
Our fill, but just of what we make--
Therefore, Our Love do not forsake!
