In a Churchyard, Grieving

In the churchyard was a man
upon his knees, and he began
to moan and wail.  I heard him cry
"Why, oh why, oh why did you die?"

I could not pass but asked him, "Mister -
was this a loved one?  Wife, or sister?"
"Nay, " he said, "That is not so.
This was a man I did not know."

"Why, then," I said,"Could you reveal
the source of sorrow that you feel?"
"This one man's death has ruined my life -
the former husband of my wife!"
