Ruminations   (part 5)

"I was dead and now I am alive.
I was in tears and now I am laughing.
The power of love swept over my soul
and now I am that eternal power."
~~~~~~~~Rumi

Such is the power of love
that it can take hold of
and remake the grieving soul.
But this power comes not from outside -
it comes forth from within.
Each of us is the fountainhead -
the source of love's flowing stream.
Only by freely extending our hand
and proffering our own love
can it rebound to fulfill us.
Only by opening our own hearts
to accept whatever comes our way~
be it joyful or a source of sorrow~
can we ever hope to be complete.
Eagerly feel the flow of love,
it comes through us from God above.
