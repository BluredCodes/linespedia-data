Cry the Unloved

cry the unloved
anguish
pain
deep sorrow
no words can ever tell
the depth of tears
so shed

the only solace
from the turmoil
seems too final
death alone
may give respite
but not solution

throw off grief's shackles
and despair's bonds
revel in the joy
of life
espouse the harmony of love
not strife
