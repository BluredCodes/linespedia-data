title: "Efforts"

In efforts to circumvent sin,
we either can lose or can win.
With faith as our guide
in Destiny's ride
'twill help us through thick and through thin.

When all is at last said and done,
we neither have lost nor have won!
We haven't a clue,
so what can we do?
Live life to its full in the sun!Short poem in limerick meter.
