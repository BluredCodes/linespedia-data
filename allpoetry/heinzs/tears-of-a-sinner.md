Tears of a Sinner
a collaborative poem
by Lelo-Heinzs-Ravyn-JeffWilliams

Blood flows all around
My unborn daughter runs in a 
blood stained flower dress,
My dead brothers smoke
from my sisters skull.
I hear the laughter of
my decapitated father.

I hold my mother's arms in mine,
unable to put them back where they belong.
This nightmare keeps coming back
to haunt my sleep...
my flock of skinned and headless sheep.
Terror knows no bounds...
where reality and delusion separate. 

The smoke pours from the sockets
As the grinning carcasses caper
round my father's head, 
cabled, hanging from the derrick
Looking down at the stumps
where my legs once were
Maggots crawl in and out of my knees 

Heroin and alcohol - which one of us 
was first to fall from grace into the pit?
When once we lovingly kissed good night
now we only hiss and spit.
Our graves await us - dark and deep
And those who watch our funeral
will know we died within our sleep.
