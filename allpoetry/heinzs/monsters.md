Once considered spawn of evil,
vampires and lycanthropes
were creatures of legend.
Nightmare monsters, leagued with the devil
to torment God-fearing men.
Evidence now shows both conditions
simple manifestations of disease.
Ofttimes those same
self-named God-fearing men,
in their brutal intolerance,
are the true monsters.
Tormenting those unfortunates
declared "different" by standards
arcane and subjective,
physically deformed or mentally ill.
Man fears what he does not understand.
Unwilling to explore
the darkest reaches of his own mind,
he is doomed to remain
fearfully ignorant - a monster!

10/31/2001
