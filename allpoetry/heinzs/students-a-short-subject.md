Students (a short subject)

Students -
younger-looking every year -
throng in cacophonous groups -
blocking halls and stairwells -
unconscious, unheeding.
Single-minded mindlessness
from class to class.
Where's the party tonight?
Did you hear about ...?
Yet, somehow, despite distraction,
a fair percentage
actually learn something!

Feed these empty minds -
the future of the World's at stake!
The generations places change -
the same mistakes we dare not make!
