Diminishing:

Poetry - how bold!
In expression - strong!
The soul and heart speak
with the poet's tongue.

But thoughts do wane
as time passes...
Inspiration
does not last long.

What is wrought
stands for naught -
my love fades...
lighter shades.

Pleasing
no one
all is
soon gone.

One
soul
stands
whole.

Increasing:

One 
lone
soul
cries.

Sorrow
for all
bygone 
lost dreams.

Hope springs back
eternal -
ever seeks
Love's fountain.

The end is nigh
for all mankind -
environment
dwindles with time.

What we seek is clear -
Life - we hold so dear.
Let nothing deter
but save us from fear.
