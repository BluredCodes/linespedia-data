Light and time have favored me
They brought me something so radiant
I can't believe how you have become a part of me
I let you in so easily
Everything about you feels so right
And I feel so wrong
You inspire me to think about things I never wanted to consider
I was so angry so frustrated and alone
Just a glance from you all those feelings disappear 
I am becoming crazy,   so clumsy for you
I only want to be with you
Insanity follows every thought of you
No blink no breathe is without a whisper of you
You are a light
And I don't want you to fade
But I know that all time fades
