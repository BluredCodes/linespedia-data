Scarce is the time that brings me here
  Vast is the life that has been given
All of time is here
  Lurking in theconcept of now
Raking in to my lungs I breath your light
  Cough out the darkness I must
I feel free from the surger that must be performed
  Isolated I must keep my mind swiveling in circles
Begining to loose all control
  nothing can be found if I lose my focus
I know I must keep my life in sight
  I will not looe my reflection or shadow
Do my best with the vastness
  Of this scarce life
