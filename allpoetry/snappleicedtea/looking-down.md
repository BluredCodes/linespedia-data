title: "Looking down"
writer: "snappleicedtea"

Would the world see me through its time  if I burst into a million brilliant red piecesWould my pieces become important  or would I be lost and forlorn much like the clouds aboveWould the world notice if I was no longer apart of it Do the days still go, does time continues as it always hasMy soul has grown weak have I lost youThe doors are closing around me   and even though I can scarcely breathe the days seem to still go    and vanish without a traceTime continues as it always must with a value that I never realizedI wish that time would reverse itself  oh the things that could change   how my days might be differentA sign of love could have changed a sun rise a smile could have made a sunsetBut time is what it is now  the unnoticable day   the week that passed me by    the times I know I should have criedThe days I wish to have so many days are gone
