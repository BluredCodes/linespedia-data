When the door opens  we look awayWhen it shuts and locks  we try so hard to knock it downWhen the sun shines in   we ask it to cool  and when it rains   we beg for a little sunWe give advice, so sound and so wise   yet we don't heed our ownWhen opportunity is lurking around the corner and good things bouncing around like a basketball  we forget to notice    yet we remember to ask for more    and when it is there     when everything is alright    we cryFor we can't tell when the rain stops and when the sun sets in
