---
title: "the music of you"
writer: "snappleicedtea"
slug: "the-music-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sing to me your words,
Play me a song full of sorrowful notes.
Tell me how you feel when I dance along by,
Can I bring to you chords of life?
Oh how you inspire my every lyric,
A man of music who entrances my heart.
A rythmless notion of you is pictured in my mind,
Colorless smiles I strum from you,
What must you feel behind the pages of music.
Nothing but the memory of your voice burns in my mind,
Confused about how to slip into the thoughts of your life.
I long to show you the words I hide,
Listen to my song I have written so elegantly with time.
Won't you with your wisdom of light,
Play me a song from your heart?
