There must be something out there Something beyond the vast unknown sky   Beyond the lies we hideThere must be something out there  Beyond the pain, beyond our time    Something within the lightCant see the dark with out the light  Can't feel the cold with out the heatThere is no pain with out life  No love without sorrow    There must be something to findNo star no segment of time would shineThe snow cannot fall from a vacant skyThere must be something out there Something that can't be seen   Just     There must be something beyond the great vacant sky
