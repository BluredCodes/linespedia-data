There is no such wasted time
 No prize to claim
As fragile as glass
 You are to me like an ornament
So rare so fragile
Life without you has no place
There is no such life
I have no victory to win 
Nor prize to claim
I have just this little ornament
I may only take you out once a year
But I always think of you
Time moves so slowly
I get so nervous
My life gets in the way
I hope you know I appreciate 
  The times whey you shine for me 
The times when you hangs so vaiantly on the tree
