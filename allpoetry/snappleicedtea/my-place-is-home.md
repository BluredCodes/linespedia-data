title: "my place is home"
writer: "snappleicedtea"

If you must take this place from me
 take it with your heart
  for I give it to you with mine
It will shelter you 
  for it has me
   and know that it will take care of you
Know that the gardens are blessed
  blessed with the memories of children
   and that everything you plant will always grow to its fullest
No matter how you grow to hate this place
 please remember that it was my home
  and know that I too once hated it
This place is one you can't hate for long
It belongs to the spirit of many
  and now it is yours
I give you this place with my all
 it is my heart and soul
My memories you will now have
 and every whimper of this place belongs to you
remember this and take my home and make it your home
 the place where your memories shall follow
