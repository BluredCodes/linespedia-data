Can I ever be the sameI feel so alone My lover has left me coldI long to feel loving armsI just want to be wholeMy world is picking up my piecesAnd I shutter as I distance myself from the worldI must throw the forget me nots out the doorI may not have a loverBut my heart is mendingMy loneliness will seize to growDesires and fears will not showA man won't make me whole
