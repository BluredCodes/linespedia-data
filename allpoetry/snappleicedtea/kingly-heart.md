Your kingly heart was 
  to me all that ever could be won
You seemed so perfect in every lie you told
  I would always believe you
Your nice guy mask and beautiful face
  hid from me the truth
The way you felt
 the way you were was so utterly wonderful
But what I didn't know 
  what I was blind to see was that 
Even kings can have cruel hearts
