Broken hearts are so many
 when hearts are misunderstood
Loving arms are dismembered
 when no one is there to hug
Listening ears are deafened
 when noise blocks out the cries
Tears stream like rivers
 because of broken lies
When hearts are not taken
 they cast out who wants in
And when no one is there to love
 loving arms reach out no more
When you scream aloud
 and you are drowning in your tears
  know love is so blind there is nothing to see
And when you no longer care to feel
 and you forget the passions you felt
When you begin to feel the anger
 and forget yourself in misery
Let it go
 for hearts are always broken
  in a world that is so misunderstood.This is an old poem, I would like any feed back that people have to offer
