I can see you with the rainbows  Playing with the clouds   Dancing in the sky    Surrounded by the starsYou shine down much like the sun  Who glares at the moon   Bringing life to the skies above     And the earth belowYour voice smiles quietly   Like the waves that touch the sandKnowing such a glory  I am blessed when I can join you in the rainbows
