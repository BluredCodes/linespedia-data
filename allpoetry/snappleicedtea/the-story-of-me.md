The story of my life
  Of my time
   The pain just minutes of years can cause
My detailed explanation for everything
  A lie in every excuse
The story of me
  So tangled up in a wave of confusion
    Keeping myself as prey
I dive in to pain into life so acceptingly
My utter commitment to finding life's meaning
  has brought out its real cruelties
The story of us
  Lost people 
    Lost souls
     So lost
 People all being want they believe others want
  People so lost in pretending
Just like me being consumed
 Becoming lost
We all make excuses for how we feel
 We see what we want
  We hide from the hurt lurking inside ourselves
The story of my life 
  Is the story of people
