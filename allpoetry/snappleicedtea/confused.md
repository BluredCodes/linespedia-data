I don't know who I am anymore
My dreary eyes filled to the brim with confusion
Pounding and throbbing my head fills with thoughts of you
Screaming, wishing you out 
I am so confused at the very thought  of you
Who is it that I long for 
My mind yearns to examine your soul
You love to show me the door
frustrated at your tendency to shut me down
I think I hate you
 Though my heart wont be committed to such a crime
Tell me who you are
I am so confused
Do you know who you are
