In my eyes I can hear the band
Playing the song of my tears
My home is no longer secure by white sand
It is tormented by the fire
Into my heart do you sink
Lighting my every desire
No longer will I think of your touch
Or remember my lost garden
I miss it so much
For now I will keep my life in a pocket full of yellow
Like a picture perfect knife 
I will not remain so mellowPlease read and comment.
