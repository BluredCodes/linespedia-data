Bring into my life your peace
 Your Peices of light
  and life.

A shimmer of wisdom
 A thought of trust 
  is the definition of you.

Infiltrate my mind 
 With your ideas
  and thoughts.

Everything is part of my life
Our space, the difference you see in us
 It is not really there
  There are no holes.

once you asked me 
 to tell you this, to
  tell you everything.

My hands are so clean
 I don't even consider
  your light.

What you think about it all
 I may agree to.

Lost I find myself
  wondering if I am keeping my time 
   away from the question you once asked me.
