Can you see the clouds above the world
 The ones that surround our lives
Can you feel the rain touch your face
 The cool serene rain
Will you take in this moment
 and breathe it in forever.
Will your finger tips reach out to touch the 
 stars hidden beneath the clouds
Deep down you know they r there
With your breath
 will you breathe out the life you've touched
I can feel the rain
