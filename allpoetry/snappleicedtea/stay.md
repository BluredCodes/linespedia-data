Feed my obsession
Cure my dying soul
Remove the stain
and I can stay another day

Turn on the heat
Make me so comfortable
Color in the black and white spaces
And I will consider another day

White on white 
Is not like yellow on yellow
Do as I may
I cant stay

Kill my heart
Please help me let go
