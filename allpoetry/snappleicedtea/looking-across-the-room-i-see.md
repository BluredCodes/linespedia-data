title: "looking across the room I see"

looking across the room I see
clouds filled with rain
the rain just waiting to pour
to pour on the hungry green
the green that colors the earth

I see people filled with emotion
the emotions that will in time burst
burst out to other people
who in return may burst, 
starting the destiny our lives were meant to follow

I see the stares of time
time looking us all down the back
puncturing our wounds 
making them deeper, so deep
the wounds need stiching
but the threads are torn
and e forget where the pain started
but we hope time will mend this pain

looking across the room I see life
life pass me by like the rain that feeds my emotins
the emotions that take my time
and I stare
stare to the sky past my room
wishing I could see more
