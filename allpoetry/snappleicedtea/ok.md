Today I will let things be OKNot everything is in my controlNothing in life is predictableBut not everything is unexplainableWhat is the purpose of liesWhy do I hideMy many dreams so hastily rushed asideBeginning to see what's aheadNo longer caring what I left behindLonging to forget what I wished forKnowing that certain dreams aren't meant to beDestiny is dealing me a handMust I be patientFor today I will let things be OK
