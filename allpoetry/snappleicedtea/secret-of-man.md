title: "Secret of man"

Underneath my skin lies the secret of man,
 Glimmering red flows through my body,
  and colored jems lay within.
Inside my soul the desires of nature are found,
 Wild buffalo swim through my veins, 
  and I can feel the Flutterbys.
Gathered in my thoughts are the many questions of the land,
 Swarming insects crawl into my hand,
  and I look at the beautiful dragonfly.
Held in the palm of my heart,
 Destiny is seen.
