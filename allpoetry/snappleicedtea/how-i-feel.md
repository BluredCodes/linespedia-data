From the first moment I saw you
I saw what I could never have
I long to take that chance
But so content on being alone
So perfect your soul
As wonderful as your eyes
I could shine next to you
Wishful as it might be
I long to be in those arms
Life in your mind is so simple
Not even thinking of me in the way I wish to have you
How can I make you see that I could be
I could be all you want
Just watching you move makes me admire you
Your every silly manerism
Oh I wish I could tell you
But I will not
Your life devoted to that of god
