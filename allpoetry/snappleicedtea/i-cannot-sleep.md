I can not sleepMy eyes are closedMy heart aches My mind is spinningI can not sleepThe light is much to bright I can not close my eyesTheir weight unbearableBut when they shutI can see what I am feelingMy mind is spinningHate and disillusion are running ramp-idMy heart achesConfusion is setting inI long to understand
