The trees carry so much time
 With every vine, a secret is found
The wind tells every secret
 The trees can never share
With all time nothing is lost
 But in time it can't be discovered
The breeze tries to us all,
 Yet afraid to listen are we
A distance from nature we carry
 Something is lost in our deaf ears
Can't you hear me call
 Am I that far
The trees carry my voice
 Above all I long to have my secret told
