title: "She is"
writer: "snappleicedtea"

She became everything I wanted.
All she had, I should have belonged to.
She was so full of life,
 As though nothing could drain her.
She seemed to love everyone,
 Except for me.
I wished for everything she had,
 but was so glad she experienced i.
I am so full of misery,
 as though I was being drained.
I love all who want to be loved,
 but I am only loved by some.
I wouldn't hurt anyone,
 not even her.
She had everything I wished for,
 I envy her, but I forgive her.
All I wanted I saw her become.
All my dreams,
 were her reality.
But my mistake
   was hers.
