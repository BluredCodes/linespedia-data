A drop of loneliness will fadeThe pace of my heart will raceThe trace of his faceFirst glance can't eraseWanting less wishing for moreHe will approach theeAttraction is thereSparks fly and shine everywhereA gentle word can entrance heIf I wish it beAnd if he would want theeA first kiss... is only fairA love that can be, will beAnd when it ends he and I may part our waysBut the again tomorrow is another day
