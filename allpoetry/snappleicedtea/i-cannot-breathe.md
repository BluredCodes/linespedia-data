---
title: "I cannot breathe"
writer: "snappleicedtea"
slug: "i-cannot-breathe"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am all that is lost
  all that is sorrow
I will be someone
  someday
I may find happiness
  or sorrow
I cannot breathe
  nor eat
I am all that is lost
  all that is sorrowI
