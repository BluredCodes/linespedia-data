My heart beats inside me
 Beats like that of the wings of a bird
I cringe at lonliness
 The sorrow lurking within
My eyes die
 I become lost in the swamps of the world
Breathless screaming
 Come from my hollowed voice
Thinking empties my thoughts
 My mind swallowed in numbness
Wishing I knew
 That I could grsp the concept of you
