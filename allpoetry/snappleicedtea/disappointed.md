So disappointed in myself,
 I let you get to me,
   And I fell in love.
So disappointed in you
 For making me believe your lies,
   By hiding the obvious truth in your eyes.
So disappointed,
  I wasted my time.
Trying to make you love me;
  I could have loved someone else.
So disappointed in myself,
 I thought I needed you,
   I thought you loved me.
So disappointed that
  I couldn't see the truth
    That was hidden behind those beautiful eyes.
