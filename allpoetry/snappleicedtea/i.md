Don't misunderstand who I am I am no more than you  and no less than heI may not be seen nor heardBut my life is just as real as yours Just because all things are possible does not mean that I am notBelieve what you must all things have a truthLife ends for us all and there was never a doubt   that you or I livedCeasing to have a vesseldoes not demolish my existenceI am realThough I am no longer fleshI lived
