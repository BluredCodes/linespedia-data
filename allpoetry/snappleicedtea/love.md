Behold my heart
 Bloody and torn
I show to you my unending soul
 Color my life with the colors of yours
May we bring new meaning to joy
 Stay with me for now
My body is restless now
 I can not sleep with out you here
Acknowledge my love
 All crooked and clean
Without you I may be me
