Rigid and cold I stand alone
Broken and barren I shuffle through the snow
 Crawling on my knees toward the faraway light
 Leaves circle with the wind around my every limb
Frustrated and tired I know I must stay awake
Scared and a bit insane I try to find my way
  Standing now I try to run toward the sky
  The wind such a memory passing me by
Thinking and crying confuse my journey
Shouting no longer yelling I deafen my ears
  Trudging in to the day
  The purity of light no longer gaurenteed
Smiling in spite I look on
Wincing in pain I know the mountain I must climb
 Back to my knees, 
   in the wind I found so dear
    Gladly seeking my new life journey
