Can you see the clouds above the world
   The ones that surround our lives
Can you feel the rain 
   The cool serene rain
Will you take this moment
   And breathe it in forever
Will your finger tips reach out to touch the stars
   The stars that are hidden beneath the clouds
     Deep down you know they are their
With your breath 
    Will you breathe out the life you have touched
That life can see the sun 
    Feel the rain that touches its face
       And knows that there are stars hidden beyond the clouds
Can you feel the rain
