Yet again the sun rises 
Bringing with it the hope of a new day
And then it falls taking with it all the hopes you had for today
My heart yearns to beat next to yours 
To feel your lips caress my cheek
But time is conceded 
You disappear into the horizon
Scream as I may 
My tears fall to vacant eyes 
I know I'll love again 
My heart's so open to that of lies 
Today the moon rises 
And with its fall, all the many thoughts of you
