title: "beautiful eyes"

The most beautiful eyes you have
 those eyes so full of life 
  they say so much and look upon so many
And those eyes yet to see
 I wish they'd look upon me
  they mystify us all
I wish you could see your life
 for eyes you have
  those sea blue eyes follow the ocean waves
   not fighting the sway of the current
I wish you knew
 could see your life
  for eyes you have 
   and beautiful are they
