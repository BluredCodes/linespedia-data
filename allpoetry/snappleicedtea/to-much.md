The world is not for me
I don't belong to this earthy place
  The sun is much to brilliant,
  The night sky to Awesome'
I don't deserve its vast spaces
  Green meadows littered with wild flowers 
  flowing right into the blues of the ocean
  So much blue that I cry just to see it
I love this place
 But it is not mine
  The trees are too tall
  the sun too bright
   And the moon so very high in the sky
