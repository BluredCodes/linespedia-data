title: "left of right"
writer: "snappleicedtea"

What is left, 
 When the sun is gone,
  But the sky is stil shinning,
   And the stars aren't out yet?
What is left of right
  When left isn't an option?
What is left of people in life
 After life has happened?
What is left of me
 Who has never known what is left of right,
  When life has happened
   While the sky is shinning?
