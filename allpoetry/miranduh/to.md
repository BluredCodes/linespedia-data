It always amazes me 
How people have to commit a wrong 
Before they learn from it; 
How people are always haunted 
By the ghost of love's past; 
Why must a person seek 
A place to call his own 
Before he can find solace 
And peace in his heart? 
The truth to happiness lies 
In the beholder of it 
Who does not worry about 
What would befall him 
Instead, he anticipates.
