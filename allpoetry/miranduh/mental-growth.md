title: "Mental Growth"
writer: "miranduh"

I can't tell you I am not afraid, because
you can see it in my eyes.
I am scared, and sometimes
I lie awake unable to sleep and unable to rise.
It is paralyzing.
I was fearless once.
I was five, and Mommy said 
don't climb on that table
you will hurt yourself
and I saw her lips moving and 
her head shaking
but I thought she was ridiculous
and I didn't listen.
And then I fell, and
I learned that I am not invincible.
I stopped climbing tables.
I stopped a lot of things.
