---
title: "Rage"
writer: "miranduh"
slug: "rage"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm trying to escape from my inner rage, 
The rage that would swallow me within. 
I'm trying my best to run 
But my legs just won't give in. 
What sorrow caused me to grieve? 
And what grief drove me to self-destruction? 
I really wish I knew. 
And as I stand on the edge of oblivion, 
I falter, and plunge into the dark sea of strife.
