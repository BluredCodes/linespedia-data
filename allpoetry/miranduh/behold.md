---
title: "behold"
writer: "miranduh"
slug: "behold"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51905-behold-by-miranduh"
---

she walks along without a care in the world
kicking stones and singing to herself
stepping in every puddle she can find.
she looks up.
the stars don't look much different then they did last night,
but to her they do
and everything looks different
and the moonlight makes the streets look scary
but she tells herself time and time again,
she is not afraid.
she bends down to tie her shoe
and her sneakers have been through everything with her
from dancing in school hallways and
being stepped on at shows
to sneaking out last night,
but they're still her favorite pair and she will never throw them away.
she passes the houses of the people she used to know.
it makes her sad.
she tells herself that she's better off...
but she knows she doesn't believe it.
she'll never believe it.
she's almost home so she takes an extra turn
so it'll take a little bit longer.
shes still singing to herself.
same song -- its always that same song
that song tells her story
almost home -- walking slow
shes ready to lock the doors and listen to some music
ready to dance in her room
ready to scream.
she opens the door
licking off her lip gloss she smiles
and for a second she's beautiful -- and that's all that matters.
