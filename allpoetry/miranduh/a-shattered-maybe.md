---
title: "a shattered maybe"
writer: "miranduh"
slug: "a-shattered-maybe"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51526-a-shattered-maybe-by-miranduh"
---

don't. 
don't put all those false hopes in my heart. 
no. 
I won't listen to your "i'm sorry"s. 
please. 
I just want you to be honest. 
my mind's in a turmoil 
from all those 'maybe's that ran through my head.
