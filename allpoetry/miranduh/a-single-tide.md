---
title: "a single tide"
writer: "miranduh"
slug: "a-single-tide"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28969-a-single-tide-by-miranduh"
---

standing on the damp shore, 
scanning through the cloudy skies 
you're not coming back, I realize. 
wind blows the hurt away 
water washes the wounds 
but most still remain... ... ... 

one tide can't wash it all away.
