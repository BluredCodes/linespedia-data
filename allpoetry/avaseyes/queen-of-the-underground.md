i met  you Sunday at the Dalai Lamas         you posed for pictures in those silk pajamas         and u seemed like someone from another planet         with your eyes so jet,         your lips so pomegranate         and that coiffure you wore was so early rocket         you wet your hair , and find the nearest socket         and your blonde boyfriend wore a studded collar         is that so he'll come when he hears you holler??                  back in the Apple         high on the hog         you socialize on empty dialouge         they think  your clever         they think your smart         you're so convincing ,         you play the part         you're The Queen Of The Underground         when i went to concert          the show was sold out         so i bought a program         with a techocolored fold out         and your padded shoulders          oh ! how fantastic         it all seemed to say          you were so icinnoclastic                  one little problem still puzzles me         and u a daugher of borgeoisie??         i thought i saw u mending the sails         buying gurilla wear at bloomindales
