youll get your things                 ill send them on to you .                and all of your letters                 ill see that you get them too                 and ill make sure that you receive                 the messages that your friends leave                  but please now , leave now                 ill call you later on ,                                        go now , go now                  before my tears begin to spill                   oh , wont u go now ,                  before they flood my face                   for i have not much left you see                  except what pride that left to me so go now                  please go now                 leave the talking                 for  some future time at hand                  all the anylizying                  you know  its better left unsaid                  and maybe you could be the one                  to tell our freinds what we have done                                    but just go now , please go now                  oh wont u go now                   before i cry in front of you......
