title: "own your own pain"
writer: "avaseyes"

own your own pain             why not?? its yours                          you,ve hawked it ,pushed it , pimped it             your body breathing life , guts , luster,             sweetness , softness ,             pays the price              so own your own pain             why not??             you're eaten it for breakfast             sung it to sleep at night             rinsed it out in the basin             watched it rise with the bread              so- take it , turn it             let it slither             into blood beat, breastbone , cell-song , skin             give it a name             what u posess             cannot possess u
