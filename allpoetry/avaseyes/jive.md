after u hurt me-after i hurt you
                and all of our bad dreams
                one by one came true
                we said it was Goodbye
                but the Eyes never lie
                and i knew that it was only Jive
       
                so you found a new lover to wear your crown
                and i found a cheap flat
                up in Chinatown
                i saw you with stoned stars in cheap bars
                downtown Mars 
                but i knew it was only Jive

                you cut me loose , i hit the juice
                something deep inside me died
                 who punched that tune??
                who hid the moon??
                who made the dime store gypsy lie??
                
                you aint got no love jones
                not for me - not much
                then how come the sparks fly
                everytime we touch??
                the meaning becomes clear
                everytime you are near
                but maybe its only Jive
