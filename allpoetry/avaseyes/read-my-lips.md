title: "read my lips"
writer: "avaseyes"

hard as i try to fight it               anything can excite it               merely the meeting of our eyes               it just intensifies               i soon loose my senses               i got no defenses               there ain't nothing i can do               except stare at you                              you got the secret               combination to my heart               you want to take it all-                     please do                infauation is tearing me apart               i dont dig secrecy               i cant wait till the day               we both are free               it must be obvious i love you               but , you ,know               loose talk,               sink ships               though i cant tell u in public babe                     just , read my lips                                  you know whos my witness                 the first day at fitness                  when i saw your sultry shape                  and then my love , there no escape                  you set off my siren                   i kept pumping iron                   but all my eyes could do                   was only stare at you
