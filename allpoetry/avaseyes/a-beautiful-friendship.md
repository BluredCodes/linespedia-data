this is the end of a beautiful friendship              it ended  but a moment ago.                          this is the end of a beautiful freinship              i know , for your eyes have told me so               we were always  like sister and brother              until tonight , that is , when we look deep                into ,each other.               that was the end of our beautiful friendship               and  just the beginning of love
