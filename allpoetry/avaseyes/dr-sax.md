put it all on wax one day......                                by day , hes a grease monkey its true                a slave , fix your transmission like new                change oil , rotate your tires of course                he toils , underneath the flying red horse                    but at six he rolls down his sleeves                turns the collar up when the boss man                leaves                closes up his shop ,puts away his tools                give the shop keys to the gas pump fool                then hes home at last                 no more good wrench scene                then he scrubs his hands till there surgeon                clean                 takes a long hot shower , some cologne and then                change complete hes himself again                at night hes Dr saxx                hes Mr tenor virtuso                Mr rythym tracks on tape                they all dig Dr saxx                not a peer of his could blow so                gives girls heart attacks they say                someday he wil live just in his mind                and leave  leave all the misery behind                to chart , is his matter of cource                not to toil under the flying red horse
