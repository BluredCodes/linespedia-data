come and walk with me underneath the stars      traveled back in time      far as  you can see , there was once a wall      that no man could climb       fathers and sons lost on the other side       dreaming of ones that they'd never see       now all that remains      mountains of stone ground into dust      where gardens grow ,      all that was lost , now can be found            lately in the sky i can hear voices calling       theres a new day coming      deep and far into to the night , i hear      there is a new day dawning         come and walk with me       underneath the stars      and the endless sky      can u feel the wind reach down to u?      from the mountain on high       time marches on      changing the world we knew      still so much remains      as a society , for us to do         what good is a dream that must be      destined to die ,as too extreme      dreams of a world finally free
