darkness and insanity               i have bid a fond farewell to the thoughts ive had          old ways seem to lose meaning as days pass on,          to me , nothing real exists any longer.          I've fled this world of fantasy which was but a war ,          that i have invented inside my head          in the distance i see a light that i cannot reach,          but , what is the wrong that the darkness has done?          the darkness of my own insanity has been  a dear friend ,          for longer than insanity has been at my side .          it is attracted to me as if i were a magnetic pole,          of darkness and euphoria .          why is it , i can feel and not , be felt?          i am a plauque that can be found long after          my presense has left , your cold , cruel darkness,          to perish alone in your own insanity .
