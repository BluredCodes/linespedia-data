used to be mellow               till you got ahold of me              now i feel like Othello              I'm dying of jealously                             where were you late last night?              i passed your house i saw no light              i called u on the phone              100 times              you weren't at home              don't say ' just out with ffriends              i can't believe that line again               don't tell me 'just be cool '              you know i ain't that big a fool                 why must you play with me?              why domt you stay with me?              i'm in a difficult possition               i don't mean to push and shove              i just want baby , all your love              i'm sick and tired of this suspission                            you gave me that sweet look               and you cast a spell on me              i swallowed your meat-hook               now im dying of jealousy                 i cry when i should laugh               at your old lovers photograph               you say' no need to shout'               well how come  you never threw it out?               what did you do all day?               your mind's a million miles away               you say ' just in my room'               well i would like to know                with whom.......
