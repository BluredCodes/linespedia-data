just yesterday            i wandered lost            inside some lonliness            my love-worn heart            would not ,undo            but now , your rainbow            its shining through            somehow King , theirs no one like you                          you gave me hope            you turned my heartbeat            to one simple note            you wrote  The Answer            with your smile            so now my heart ,            is smiling too            somwhow theres no one            my freind , only you                        when life goes wrong            i simply listen to this            inner song ,             your love for things              has taught me how to hear             and now your star will steer  me true             and i know theyll never be ,             another quite like you
