---
title: "Tell him i said hello"
writer: "avaseyes"
slug: "tell-him-i-said-hello"
source: "AllPoetry"

if u run into the guy we once knew           and you stop and talk like old friends often do           tell him that i said hello ....           bout don't say i miss him so ,           and.......           look into his eyes           his warm and gentle eyes           see if he come back           if i appoligise           tell him that i said hello....           but don't say i need him so            don't give me away           please say im looking gay            even though , its a lie            but to tell the truth            the real and honest truth            im so blue            i could cry                       what a foolish game           a silly childish game           tell him i was wrong           and i take all the blame..           say i need and love him so           tell him...           just tell him ....            i said                  hello
