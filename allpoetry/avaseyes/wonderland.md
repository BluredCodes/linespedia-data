woke up in time to catch the evening news              sure got  bin laden to spread the blues              i fried up some eggs in the pan              and headed out the doorway              to Wonderland                             down at Wonderland i pass the               time away               play the piano but it aint no               Steinway               some fallen angel usually takes my hand               and leads me round the corner of               Wonderland                all the people here got wonderous plans               that never ever  make a stand               life in all her wisdom laid                them low                for life for them is just a show                  down at Wonderland the Jills aint jokin'                take u to heaven for a subway token                each night my womans with a diff man                leaning on a bar at Wonderland                                  down at Wonderland we all drown our sorrows                got to much yesterdays and no tomarrows                 so if  you need me ill be on the stand                fingering the ivories                   down in Wonderland
