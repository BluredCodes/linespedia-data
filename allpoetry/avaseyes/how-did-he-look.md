---
title: "how did he look"
writer: "avaseyes"
slug: "how-did-he-look"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6241-how-did-he-look-by-avaseyes"
---

so u ran into my former fiance .                 you saw him dancing last night ,                 at some cafe                 gee , i haven't seen him since ,                 a year ago                 and there are so many things ,                 that im just dying to know .....                 how did he look??                 did he seem happy??                 while , they were dancing,                 did he whisper things in her ear??                 and did he lit her cigrette ,                  was he attentive ,                 the way he used to be ,                 with me??                  what was she like??                 was she , attractive?                 was there a smile upon his face?                 or did he look bored?                not that i really care ,                i know our love affair                can never be                but how did he look ??                and did he ask about me ......
