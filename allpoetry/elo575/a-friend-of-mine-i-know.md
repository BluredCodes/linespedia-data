I know that what your going through
is really, really hard
You've kept secrets from your family 
and they still don't know who you really are
I know you want to tell them
each and every day
Have them accept you for yourself
and your own ways
I know you want to tell some girls
how you really feel
that you have kept an eye on them
and watched them date around
I know that you want Melissa to give you a fair chance
to stop changing her mind 
forward and then back
I know you go through life living upon a fantasy
a fantasy, that other people will soon enough see
I wish for you everything and all of the above
because you are a great person
and deserve a lot more than you have
I know you want freeson from the secret that you live
I know you want to scream
I am a Lesbian!
I know you let people conrtol you
and even put you down
you let them walk all over you
up and then back down
I know it's hard to be you
a perosn everyone thinks they know
a person who live a much harder life
than the rest of us all do
I know that you want a kids
a really great wife to
a family to love and support
on your walk to fame at a music studio
I know that you strugle through many, many hard days
wondering is this me or is this just a phase
I know you think that not many people are like you
but, I bet you there are people like you in places you never knew
I know that you dream of your future
and even what lifes like now
I know who you really are
on the inside and outFebruary,
