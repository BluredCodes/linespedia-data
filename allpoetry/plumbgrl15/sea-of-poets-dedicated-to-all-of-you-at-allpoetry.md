To walk on this fragile sea of ice,Where one false step could plunge us to the depths,Is an art of both beauty and mystery.We cast our lines into a frozen worldIn hopes of landing just one vacant heartWhile exposing our souls for all to see.To be able to share emotion of any kind and put it on display is a talent in itself.  I admire all of you!  thanks for sharing your worlds!  ~~Heather
