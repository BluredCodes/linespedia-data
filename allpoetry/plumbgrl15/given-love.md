Touch of your hand
Sweeping the hair off my face
Taking me into dimensions
Of another time and place,
Silently speaking heart to heart
Whispering promises of glittering dreams
Unbroken hearts and mended seams.
Breath of life into a dying soul,
Hope from above that brings
Sweet peace and warming glow.
Gingerly place my heart in yours
Wanting to divulge my deepest desires
Hopes, fears, all that my soul
Has acquired, the reason for this fire.
Rush of emotion floods my very being
I want to grab hold tightly and
Not have to ever worry about falling
Except into the arms of a man
Who combines his soul with mine, for all time.
Prayer to see us through, to keep us
Holding on, strong, 
Beautiful, unending,perfect love
From above.He will provide the love of my life.
