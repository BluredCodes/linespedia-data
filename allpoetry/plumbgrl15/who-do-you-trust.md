Who do you trust?

Is it the only one who will never let you down,
Never fail to show you love,
Always listen to your thoughts?

Who do you trust?

Do they know your heart's desires,
Secure your every step,
Carry you when you get weak?

Who do you trust?

Can they keep you safe from harm,
Design a beautiful life,
Provide unbelievable peace?

Who do you trust?My trust is in God, and I know I am saved through Christ.
