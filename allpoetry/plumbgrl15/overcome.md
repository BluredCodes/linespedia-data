Face to face
Longing replaced
By lips I can taste.
I gather you in
taking my sin
again and again.
Melt into one
My wall is undone
I run and run.
Into your arms
No need for alarm
enchanted by charms.
Finding a way
To be together one day
In my heart you shall stay.BJ you have become my inspiration for words I didn't know still flowed in my heart.
