I stood my ground the other night.
You asked me to change
And that just wasn't right.
I layed it on the line,
Just how things are with me now
And you couldn't accept reality.
It tore me apart
To see that look in your eyes,
The same one that tormented you two years ago,
But I survived.
The reasons you gave me to stay
Reflected a person I use to be,
But she's the one you let go of.
You know you had my heart back then
And I could easily give it again,
But I know who I am now,
And I stood my ground.
