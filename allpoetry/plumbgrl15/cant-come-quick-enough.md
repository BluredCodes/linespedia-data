Sleep can't come quick enough,
Just so I can dream of dancing 
In your sturdy arms.
Arms that will protect me,
Wrap me in tender embraces,
Carry me to bed,
Hold me forever.

Time is slowly crawling,
Teasing me with fantasies
Of you and me, the we to be.
Curl up in your promises,
Warming my heart, easing my mind,
Knowing in you I find,
Hope to love again.Worth the wait.
