Quiet thoughts filter through my mind,
Beauty and serenity in you I find.
My hope alone comes from God, my friend,
How I prayed for you, the Lord would send.
I seek to know you more and more,
I see you standing at my heart's door.
I offered up my hope in you,
If were meant to be, He'll see us through.You have touched my heart, and I pray He keeps us together.
