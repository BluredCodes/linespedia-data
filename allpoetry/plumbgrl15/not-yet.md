I can't touch your face,
I can't feel the warmth of your embrace,
Your body's lines I can't trace,
For we are distanced by space.

I can't look into your eyes,
I can't leave you a sweet surprise,
I can't kiss you goodbye,
As the glow of the screen dies.

I can't hold you just yet,
For our bodies have not met,
But I'm willing to make a bet,
That this bud of love we can't forget.In hope BJ
