Reach for me and I'll already be there,
Tell me what you're thinking and I'll already know,
Think of me and I'll bring a smile to your face,
Wait for me and never know loneliness.

I have so much I want to bestow upon your heart,
If only I could overcome this deafening pounding of doubt.
It was placed here by the careless ones who ravaged me before,
So I'm trying to overcome and swim for the shore.

So throw me your life preserver and dry me in your arms,
I'll surrender all I am again to your safe harbour.
Just keep me safe when the tumultous winds start to blow,
It's in these arms the seed of faith must begin to grow.
