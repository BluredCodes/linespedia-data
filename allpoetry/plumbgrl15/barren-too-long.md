title: "Barren too Long"

You came in with the darkness
And stole from me my light,
You came while I was dreaming
And I had no means to fight.
You came in like a viper
And slithered close to me,
You came into my trust,
And stole my eyes to see.
You came bearing crystal promises
And covered me with glass,
You came with lovely secrets,
And nothing came to pass.
You came in like the winter
And everything died away,
You came in for awhile,
And wanted me to stay.
You came in bringing change
And thought you'd find me there,
You came in all too late,
And found this heart was bare.I'm writing this out of a past relationship that tore me apart.  I'm revived now, but sometimes when I sit alone too long I find myself back in its midst.
