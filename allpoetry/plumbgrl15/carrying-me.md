When my feet dare to tread the path less traveled
I do it not alone.
When my heart is aching from the struggles I endure
It knows a healing touch.
When my soul is searching for what I do not know
My eyes are always opened.
When I am overflowing with joyful praise
I know where it's directed.
When I feel that peace within my being
I know He's carrying me.Turn your eyes to Jesus
