title: "Save A Kiss"
writer: "plumbgrl15"

Tantalizing thoughts
Are clinging to my mind
As I sit here entertaining
The thought of your breath
Mingling with mine
To feel the warmth of 
Your embrace
Wrapping me in
Love's addiction
Softly caressing my face
With smooth fingertips
Hand on the small of my back
Drawing me nearer to 
What I crave
Then
I'm jolted from
This daydream
But the tingles still remain
And I know I'll find that place again
To softly kiss your lips
And hear you whisper my name
But till that day
It is for you I'll saveI hope and pray.
