Bless me I pray
With strength to carry on
With hope to see the dawn
Love to mend this heart.
You know I've been torn apart
By choices I have made
By promises that fade
Wounds that ache and scream.

Bless me I pray
As I try to move ahead
Without a sense of dread
Into new loving arms.
I came to you in need and
You mended broken dreams
Ripped open at the seams
Created a new me.

Bless me I pray
As I find a love to stay
Until my dying day,
Hand in hand with a friend
Entering your glory in the end.God gives us new hope, hope in Him, to love again, to live again.
