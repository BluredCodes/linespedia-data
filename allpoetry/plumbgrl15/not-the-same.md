---
title: "Not the Same"
writer: "plumbgrl15"

Is it too much for you that I'm not who you think,
That I left behind that girl you once knew?
I saw that doubt in your eyes and your mind trying to digest
All the times I had given in to the thought of you.
I'll be the first to admit that you once filled this heart
But it wasn't yours to take and still you broke it.
That was then and I've moved on to a better place in my life.
One where I know who I am and one devoid of lies
That fell from the lips I longed for so much.
I left you behind and discovered I was better for it.
So don't come near me again thinking you deserve to be heard,
I refuse to settle for your version of love.
