I came to see you the other day, 
Just to see how you have been.
I was almost up to you when I caught the first glimpse
Of the one who had replaced me. 
I was paralyzed and it all became so surreal,
I tried to turn and go but I couldn't tear my eyes away.
You were looking at her the same way you had looked at me,
And I knew in that instant why I had left you behind.

You were full of so many promises and captured me with lies,
Yet you never saw just who I was or that you had my heart.
I tried so many times to quietly slip away,
But you wouldn't release me and promised me a change.
I gave you than you ever deserved
And you stole what should have never been yours.
So instead of caring for you today,
I'll turn my step and think of myself and let you slip away.
