title: "Swan Dive"

Can I hide in this shell,
Can anyone tell
Where I dwell
Or how I fell?

Do you think they all see,
Right through me
Into who I might be
As I crash to my knees.

Can this heart just survive,
Through this pain stay alive
For this hope it does strive
To make this swan dive.
