Don't wake me up, 
I'm not done feeling you yet.
Don't say it's day
And that it must stay within these walls.
Was last night such a hazy restless dream
Or was that your heart beating as fast as mine?
Keep holding me close
Letting our bodies intertwine,
You rub me the right way
And I think I might just stay and savor the scent of you.
You transformed somewhere between the sweat and sweetness of your skin,
I saw how vulnerable you were
I saw the cry of ecstasy rise and fall in your eyes.
I wanted to take all of you inside 
But the moment wasn't right,
So kiss me once and harder twice,
We'll save our lust for another night.
