Can what was
Be again?
Can I forget
The lies?
Can I forget
Your goodbye?

You tell me
You're sorry.
You tell me
Try again.
You tell me
You've changed.

My mind says
Run away.
My mind says
Nothings changed.
My heart says
Stay and see.
