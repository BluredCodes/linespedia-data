When you come
I will have been waiting here
Silently creating you in my mind.
I will hold no expectations
I will only hold you
Carefully in my arms.
These arms will have been 
Vacant too long
Longing to be warmed by your touch.
Words won't have to be created
To fill the sweet silence
For our hearts will fill the void.
A vacancy of too many cold nights
Too many useless fights
Fighting for feelings that just weren't right.
But none of that will matter
When you come.
