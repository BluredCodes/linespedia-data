---
title: "May Still Be"
writer: "plumbgrl15"

In my words
These feelings must remain
Because I dare not show
This bleeding heart
It must stay hidden
Amongst the shadows 
With all the past
I've had to shuffle 
Into a little box
Where no one can see
Just how broken
I may still beI'm a bit down tonight.
