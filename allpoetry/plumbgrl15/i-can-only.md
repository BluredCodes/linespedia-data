title: "I Can Only"

I can only read your words so many times
Before I wish I was holding more
More than a mere page of sweet words,
I want to be holding a sweet soul,
One that connects with mine
For all time.

I can only hear your voice over the line so long
Before I wish I could hear more
More than a cold, lonely distance,
That seperates you from me
Where I want to be.

I can only trust in God's will for my life
I can only trust in His perfect plan,
I only hope that He includes you in mine,
I only hope that my heart you'll find
Cherishing it till the end of time.I can only trust He will lead you to me.
