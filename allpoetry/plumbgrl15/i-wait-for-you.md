title: "I Wait For You"

I wait for you in quiet cotemplation
Of all the things I've ever thought you'd be.
I wait for you with all the patience I can muster
Screaming inside that you'll be revealed to me.
I wait for you as I cry myself to sleep
Wondering if you even exist at all.
I wait for you as I become the best that I can be
Knowing I'll give all I have to thee.
I wait for you
Are you waiting for me?I'll wait forever till He gives me my heart's desire.
