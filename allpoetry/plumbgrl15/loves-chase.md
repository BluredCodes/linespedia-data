title: "Love's Chase"

Why is it that when you aren't ready
Love tries to take hold.
You've just made up your mind
That being alone is fine,
And WHAM, here it comes again.
I'll never understand love this way
Of knocking you over
With its best intentions
But forgetting to mention
That you might get hurt.
I'm not sure where to go
So I think I'll try and hide
From the pursuit of my temptation
Knowing I'll never get away.
