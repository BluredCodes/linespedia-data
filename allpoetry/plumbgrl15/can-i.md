Can I wrap you in my thoughts
and keep you there forever?
Can I wrap you in my arms,
and warm your heart with mine?
Can I call your name so soft,
and know you'll answer back,
Can I touch your face so sweet,
and melt you with my touch?
Can I enter into your mind,
and see the things you think?
Can I enter into your soul,
and make myself at home?
Can I hold your hand in mine,
and feel your pulse increase?
Can I taste of tender lips,
and savor the lingering feel?
Can I show you all of me,
and know I'm safe from pain?
Can I traverse the space between,
and gather you unto me?Distance is a sad thing to stop what could be. I cling to hope I am finding in you, whoever you will be.
