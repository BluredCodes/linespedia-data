---
title: "Just a Word"
writer: "plumbgrl15"
slug: "just-a-word"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let go of trying to become who you are not.
You shouldn't set your hopes too high.
you'll find something more tangible.
We all dream.
These words they tore at her will to survive,
All she wanted was to be understood.
Why can't they just support her with love,
Instead of crushing the hope she has left.
She cries a lot at night when she is left to her thoughts,
She doesn't know why she's never been given a chance.
All she's ever is love with all she is,
And all she's recieved is a broken spirit.
She doesn't want to go but she finds no means to stay,
So she closed her eyes and shut out the pain
And the angels carried her away.
