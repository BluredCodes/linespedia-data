title: "Loudest Words"

The thought of you fills me up
Until I overflow.
Wanting you to be my own
Nearly drives me insane.
I dream about the day
When you'll look me in the eye
And say you're all I've ever 
Wanted, Needed, or Loved.
You'll speak this to my heart
In a mere whisper,
But I'll hear it the loudest
Of any words of love I've known.In the hopes of Him.
