I wonder sometimes why we struggle at all;
Why we put up a fight we know we're bound to lose.
Those moments of happiness are inevitably smashed by regret,
Broken by the ugliness of lies and selfishness.
We strain to catch those few words of hope,
While we block out the deafening roar of the pain.
We should all be so lucky as to be exiled to an island of loneliness,
Where we can only conceive to hurt ourselves.
