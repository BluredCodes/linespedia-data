---
title: "Where Love is"
writer: "plumbgrl15"
slug: "where-love-is"
source: "AllPoetry"

Follow me into a place where we can just exist,
No rules, no expectations, just to be,
Where it's you and me
Lovely and free.

Take me by the hand and pull me in close,
No games, no hesitations, just let go,
Where love can show
Passion and flow.

Create in me a part of your soul,
No secrets, no fears, just a way,
Where feelings can say
Capture and stay.
