title: "Seeking solace"

And if the chance of love
Came knocking at your heart
Would you open up
Or even know where to start
Have you ever closed your eyes
And wished a moment away
Running away from lies
That haunt you day by day
Escape into yourself
A quiet and peaceful place
Where no one can enter in
And you constantly set the pace
But be wary of disappearing
Into a vacant heart
For if it's solace that you seek
Away from love you can't depart
