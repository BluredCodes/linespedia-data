title: "Formation of You"

I see you in my mind, a dim outline of what I've always known,
I'm creating what I want and waiting to see what's shown.
You move in and out of the light and I get glimpses now and then,
I stay in constant dreaming wanting to know how and when.

I want you to be real for me but I'm too afraid to see,
That maybe you already are, are you right in front of me?
I'm tempted by the thought of having you as my own,
but I'm holding back, I'm so used to knowing alone.

So I'll keep you in my mind and form you from inside,
because I'm safe in there where my love can hide.
