Bringing a smile to this
Woman's face really doesn't take much.
Just a warm embrace, a touch on my face,
Pour in a little love.
This soul may be complex
I may like to seem mysterious
But this heart is simply
An open book
That melts with the sweetest of looks.
So gather me up,
Pull my face close enough to 
Feel your warm breath,
Look in my eyes, don't whisper lies,
Kiss me ever so gently.
I may have been sullen
A moment before
But captured in your 
Rapturous embrace
You'll paint a smile
On this woman's face.
