title: "Until Time Unwinds"
writer: "plumbgrl15"

The sound of your voice
On the phone last night
Was almost more than I could bear,
To hear your tantalizing words
And deep, breathless sighs
But to know I can't be there.

I could have gone on and on
Teasing your senses, imagery
Of you covering me, completely
Lost inside ourselves 
Melting, intertwining, mingling
Breath and skin, ecstasy.

I would have transcended the space
That distances you from me,
To wrap you in silken arms,
Arms that can't wait to 
Warmly embrace a man
To melt in his charms.

Take this space and the time
From now to then
And quickly let it
Decrease
And
Pass

Till then I won't open my eyes just yet
But I'll dance with you in misty dreams 
Tailored with golden seams 
Until I can gaze into your eyes
Hold your hand in mine
Until time unwinds.
