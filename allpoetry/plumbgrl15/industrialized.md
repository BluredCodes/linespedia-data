I listened to the CD today
But I found you vacant of yourself.
The songs were good,
They said some things
I'm just not sure it was
from your heart.
And maybe that's 
how you wanted it,
Maybe you wanted not to be seen,
But I just hope
For selfish me,
You didn't do it for them.I wrote this after listening to one of my favorite singer's new CD, and i just wonder if anyone will stay true to their form?
