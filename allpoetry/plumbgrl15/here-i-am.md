Here I am
My sweet release
Here I am
On my knees
Here I am
I'm crying out
Here I am
I'll scream and shout
Here I am
My hands in prayer
Here I am
With all my cares
Here I am
Please come to me
Here I am
And you're just what I needIn despair, He is there.
