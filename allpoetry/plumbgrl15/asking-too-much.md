title: "Asking Too Much"

Make me a copy of your silhouette
So I can stand you up and stare.
And when you go just leave it there
For I won't be done looking yet.
And leave for me an envelope
Sealed with love's embrace.
Fill it up with words you spoke
And kisses you lingered upon.
I won't need pictures to see you there
I can just close my eyes instead.
So just leave me the sweetest things
Of what made you and me,
And if it isn't asking too much
Please leave behind my heart.
