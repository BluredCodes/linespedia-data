---
title: "Linger with Me"
writer: "plumbgrl15"
slug: "linger-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23761-Linger-with-Me-by-plumbgrl15"
---

I want you to let me in where no one else can go,
let me see in your eyes where your deepest fears lie.
Don't make a sound but to my soul speak volumes,
don't move at all but fill me completely.
Fill me with longing to feel the heat of your touch,
wrap me in warmth until we melt into one.
Trace the lines of my body till you know them exclusively,
cover me over by your passion and beauty of soul.
Succumb to the moment of ecstasy and let time fade away,
It's just you and me in a world where love lingers on.For someone who I wish could do all this and more.
