I've been brought to my knees
I've soared on eagle's wings
I've stared death in the face
I've learned to breathe again,
Through it all, You were there.


I can only begin to comprehend
The work that You have done,
How I am a perfect example
Of the wondrous things You can do,
You were waiting there all along.

I can only offer so much in return
For all You've given me,
My life I live to glorify only You
The awe consumes my soul,
There You are walking with me, always.My life was saved only through Christ!
