Can you tell how much I'm wanting you
Can you sense just what I'm feeling.
I want to feel your warm embrace
Wrapped around me tight tonight.
This feeling is pulsating deep inside
This blushing I just can't hide.
I feel you sitting next to me
And it's all I can do to keep control.
I didn't even know your name
Until I spoke to you tonight,
And now I've got this longing
To feel your lips caressing mine.
To take you deep inside 
Taking over the thoughts of my mind.
Just to lie there with you
After complete ecstasy
gives chills throughout.
I won't say a word 
But I'm waiting for your move.
Won't you come and take me there.
