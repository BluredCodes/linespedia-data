You came upon this frail and broken girl
Who didn't have a whole lot going for her.
You came upon this scared and fragile heart
That had been bruised and torn apart.
You came upon the night she was slipping away
And you gave her a reason to open her eyes and stay.Short, but some meetings in life are but they mean so much.
