---
title: "Without Complication"

If I could just be without having to be
Anyone but me
If I could just love without having to prove
Anything but my heart
If I could just be held without worrying
When I will fall
If I could just exist as I am
Wanting to give a simple love
From a complex heart
Without complication
Of expectation
