I'll crawl upon my bed tonight
To escape the thoughts clinging to my soul.
I want nothing but the calm 
That has eluded me for so long.
But sleep merely delays the noise
For with the light it wakes again,
To run rampant through these weary bones
And bring me to my knees.
So on my face I cry aloud
With strength departed long ago,
You're the only peace I know.
