There's a chill in the air
As I gaze upon the stars
Drinking in the beauty
Wondering if you see them
From where you are.
I retreat into the warmth
Of things known too well.
Things that bring comfort
And allow me to escape
Into a world where
Nothing but 
Our words exist.
If I could whisper words
And send them on
A shooting star
I'd softly sing a lullaby
Of how truly close 
You are.
As the night wind 
Softly blows
Away these hidden walls
I feel the risk of exposure
Gently creeping in.
I'm not gonna run
Or jump with both feet,
But I'll keep you close
In a prayer
Hoping you might care.Prayers know no distance, so I keep you there, Lil Bear.
