Let me linger a little longer
Here in the darkness of my sorrow.
It's the place I know the best
And I know I'll always be alone.
Did I fool myself again
Into thinking happiness hadn't eluded me?
I guess so, I mean here I am again.
Maybe this time I won't try so hard
To fight my way back to stable ground,
Maybe I'll just put in a change of address,
111 hurts too much ave.
I can hear the melancholy tunes wafting in the air,
I'd hum along but my tears choke the sound.
No, I don't want to return to another chance of pain,
I'll just stay here,
Looks like a chance of rain.
