The lines of your body are foreign to these hands,
yet I know each curve and fleck of color.
The sensations you cause my body to feel
Surge through me with such dizzying force.
I want to take you and make you one with me,
I want to bend you and break you until you're open and I can see
All the thoughts and feelings that create your alluring aura.
I want to have you melt with me here in this moment of time 
Where nothing matters but now
And tomorrow won't ever come.
