---
title: "Have you"
writer: "plumbgrl15"

I can't be angry 
At myself
At you
I gave my chance up
I gave my heart up
Unto you
You loved me
I couldn't let go
Into you
I loved you then
Even more now
Holding on
I'll never let go
But have youEric, I still love you.
