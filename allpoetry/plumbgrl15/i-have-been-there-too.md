To all those broken hearts
Patched together with vanishing hopes,
To all those lonely dreamers
Wishing with all their might,

I have been there too.

In darkness drowning everything I am
Fighting with all I had just to survive
Another cold and lonely night,
One vacant of reassuring words
That could have pierced through
The shroud of despair,

I have been there too.

Hanging on to every bloody memory
Even as it tore me apart,
I took comfort in just having
Something to fill the void
Broken, wounded spirit
Barely able to breathe.

I have been there too.

I have been there and survived,
Walked away with my head held high
Even when inside I felt I had died.
I chose to breathe again,
To let my heart live in full glory
Unrestricted, unchained from the past
Free to feel love's passion once more.

That is where I am
Unafraid to try, again.Hurt happens, we get torn apart for loving someone so much, but healing happens too.  We have to allow that, to try again, otherwise we can't share the beauty inside us.
