Entering into my thoughts
Are images of how it could be
The way you see me,
The hope of the we.
I try not to get carried away
By the way you say my name,
In the way you reach my heart
I'm feeling my soul getting a jump-start.
I had set this heart aside,
Decided my time I would bide,
But then it fell right over you
And is wondering what to do.
I wasn't looking for you 
But now I can't let go,
So I keep my eyes towards the One
Who holds my dreams and desires,
And I'll trust in Him 
To set this heart on fire.with this heart I can only give it again to someone who God has for me, I have my hopes!
