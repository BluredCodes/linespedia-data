In a field
Swaying softly side to side
Is the openness of reality
Simple with nothing to hide.
Here in this place
Of golden colors
Drizzled by the haziness
Of lazy summer days
Is where I want my mind to stay.
Sweet tranquility seeps into my soul
The elusiveness of life
Stays hidden under the soft moon glow.
I can succumb to the pleasures
Of my deepest desires
For it's all in my mind
It's the peace of freedom I find.
Laying on my back
Drifting in and out of sleep's whispers
I'm open and bare
My soul for all to see
In a field
