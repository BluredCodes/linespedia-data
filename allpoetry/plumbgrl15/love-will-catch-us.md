Tonight, I'm standing on that edge
Between falling and scared of letting go,
To know a soul completely, 
Again, to enter in.
Wanting to dive into your world
Eyes open, arms outstretched,
Reaching into pure wonder,
Fulfillment, joy of two.
Nothing but thoughts of you,
The we that could be,
The rapture of you merging with me,
Enclosed, volumes of sweet silence.
Offering a soul that has its scars,
Carrying a love that longs to be linked
With a heart that can carry it,
Trusting, to let go,
Knowing, love will catch us.Here is am, my words belonging to you.
