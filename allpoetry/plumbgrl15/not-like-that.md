---
title: "Not Like That"
writer: "plumbgrl15"
slug: "not-like-that"

Wake me up next time
You decide to break my world apart.
Don't do it while I'm dreaming
About the things you promised me.
I'll never understand
Why you had to hide away.
I never came down on you
When you screwed up yet again.
I'd let you crawl up next to me
And mend what you had done.
You'd close my eyes and kiss me soft
Then out the door you'd go.
Maybe I knew all along
The evil you had done.
Maybe I didn't want to see
The knife you carried up your sleeve.
Still I'll never understand
What made you lie like that.
I gave you every chance I had
And still you swore to me your love.
I've said goodbye
And I walked away from what my heart longed for.
But you still appear before my view
And I battle all the more.
So don't do it while I'm dreaming
Don't break my heart that way.
I gave you everything I had,
I deserve to see you walk away.
