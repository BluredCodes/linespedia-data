title: "Heart Passed"

Don't ask me to change
When your foundation refuses to budge.
Don't question my motives
When questioning enrages you so.
Don't look upon my life
As something you should pity,
Because when I look at yours
I see only a struggling mess,
Void of all emotional bonds 
That once connected two souls.

You took it for granted
When I told you, I love you.
You built up a wall
When you saw that you might care.
You'll never break the hold
That your haunted past binds you with,
Chaining your heart, hurting every chance
You've had at letting someone in,
Ready to hold you close, heal the wounds
Never to let you fall, again.
