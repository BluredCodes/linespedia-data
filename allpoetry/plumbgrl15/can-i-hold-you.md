title: "Can I Hold You"

Can I hold you just a little bit longer,
Can I hold you until my heart is stronger?
Will you look away as the tears start to fall,
Will you look away or will you crawl?
I believed in you with everything in me,
I believed in you so how can it be?
Why is it that you spoke those lies.
Why is it that you're saying goodbye?

It's just that I wanted to stay,
It's just that I couldn't bring myself to walk away.

Can I hold you until it's my choice to leave,
Can I hold you until my heart refuses to grieve?
