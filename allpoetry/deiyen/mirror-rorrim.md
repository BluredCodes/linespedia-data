title: "Mirror rorriM"

When she looks in the mirror, what does she see?
A blouse and a skirt, a hot, sexy lady.
When I look at her, what do I see?
A simple Barbie clone, without personality.

When he looks in the mirror, what does he see?
A poor, small boy, looking back helplessly.
When I look at him, what do I see?
A child full of wonder, and filled with curiosity.

When I look in the mirror, what do I see?
An independent young man, staring back blindly.
When I look at me, what do I see?
A troubled little boy, calling for help silently.

When you look in the mirror, what do you see?Have you ever wondered which side of the mirror is more 'real'?
