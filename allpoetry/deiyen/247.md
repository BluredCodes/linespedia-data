Every month I sit and wonder if she notices me at all.
Every week I smile to myself when I pass her in the hall.
Every day I contemplate giving her a call.

Every hour I stop and think, dreaming of her and me.
Every minute I'm loving her, can't she simply see?
Every second I realize, that it will never be.
