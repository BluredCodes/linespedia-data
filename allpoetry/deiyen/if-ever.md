---
title: "If Ever"
writer: "Deiyen"

At night I lie alone in bed,
Thinking back on what you've said.
You told me that you loved me,
When all you love is him, I see.
If he's just the friend you say,
Why do you want to see him every day?
Day after day I say I love you,
Time after time I remind you it's true.
You never seem to love me back,
Because trust is exactly what you lack.
If you ever trust in anyone, please let it be me,
I'll show you... what it truly means... to be free.
