To my angel faerie kitty cow,
Listen to what I say to you now:

It's been at least a year since I first met you,
We were to become friends, that we always knew.
But there's one thing we didn't foresee,
And that, is the love between you and me.
From the Skittles and the Carebears,
To the penguins and the silent stares.
From your laughter to your tears,
You washed away my fears.

I never met anyone like you,
Across the oceans you flew,
Like a faerie through a magical world of mirth,
Or an angel across the Heavens on Earth.
You took me with you to that paradise,
And added your own, extra little spice,
To show me the beauty of your dreams,
That you never wake up from it seems.

You taught me to see in a different light,
Where the sun always shines, and it's never night.
You introduced me to the world of Block,
Where angels roam and faeries flock.
I want, someday, to lay down to sleep,
And wake up knowing that my heart you'll keep.
I hope you know what I'm saying,
To be with you forever is what I'm praying.

So my last words before ending,
To you I am sending:
For everything you've done, thank you,
And for all that it means, I love you.'Thiis one goes out tooo the one I loooove...'
