title: "Live to Love"

As I sit and stare at the screen,
I ask myself, what does this life really mean?
Some say, "To serve God, and all his children."
Others claim, "To love and lose, and love again."
To love is to live, to lose a love is to die.
After all, what's the difference? An O and an I.
"Having loved and lost is better than having never loved at all."
"Having lived and died is better than having never lived at all."
