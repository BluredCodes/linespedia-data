Dear _____,
I remember the day I met you.
I remember how our friendship grew.
I remember every time you made me smile,
Every time you made life worthwhile.
I learned so much while you were here.
Losing you was something I always feared,
And now that you're gone,
I realize that I won't be sad for too long.
I thought we'd never part,
But you will forever be in my heart.
Love,
_____For anyone who's ever lost anyone close in their life.
