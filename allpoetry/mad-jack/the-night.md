title: "The Night"

As night falls
And the twilight sighs
I sit beneath
The darkening skies
I look and wonder
At the sight
Why did God
Create the night
While I am writing 
Another line
A star comes out
And begins to shine
It speaks to me
It says, Ahem
Some people say
I look like a gem
I sit there and ponder
This flickering light
It comes to me
That's why God
Created the night
