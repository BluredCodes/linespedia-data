The propellers turned
Five days had passed
Of the child inside
Had he seen the last

A child of five
No thought of life's meaning
While his young heart
For his homeland was keening

Hands on the clock turned
As the years swept past
The shadow of the child 
Was rarely cast

Don't sit there and wonder
Why he plays the game
He's seeking the child
Not fortune and fame

Happy at last 
Cherishing every day
Sometimes the child inside
Comes out to play
