Latent thoughts, unspoken desires
Searching for release
Constant clamor, noise of restraint
I wish the cacophony would cease

Held within, bridled words
Cause rising instability
The tension increasing until it
Breeds a lack of civility

A cure Oh My! A cure Oh My!
My whole kingdom for surcease
To open the door, release the flood
And find some inner peace.

At last, I've found a way
To cause the turmoil to taper
My heart holds the key 
It is poetry, emotion on paper
