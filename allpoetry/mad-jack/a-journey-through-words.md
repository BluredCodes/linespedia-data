---
title: "A Journey through Words"
writer: "Mad Jack"
slug: "a-journey-through-words"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11012-A-Journey-through-Words-by-Mad-Jack"
---

I search my thoughts for rhythm and rhyme,delicately seasoned like basil and thyme.Words that are pleasing to the inner self,to be read and wept over, not put on a shelf.Lines that convey what I really feel,that speak to the soul with artful appeal,pleasing to a multi-faceted audience,not forced on the page for my own convenience.It's perfection in thoughts turned to words that I seek,though I perceive it eludes me with every critique.Still, I continue in pursuit of my goal,while the journey through words caresses my soul.
