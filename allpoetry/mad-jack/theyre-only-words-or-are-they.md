A whisper
A word
An old cliche

Some crisper
Some mush
Some roll and play

On paper
They stay
Waiting until

Eyes peruse
A glance
When suddenly

Off paper
They leap
Pictures, Feelings

Caressing
The soul
Heavenly balm

Ideas
And words
Thoughts in our mind
