---
title: "Gossip"
writer: "Mad Jack"

Tantalizing table talk,
use your ears and do not gawk.
Can you hear it? Listen close,
it maybe something really gross.

Titilating time for tea,
did you hear abut Mrs. B?
Go repeat it but be quick,
to not get caught is the trick.

Tempting tall tell all tale,
for your ears only not for sale.
It's a secret, please don't tell,
at least not until the book can sell.

Telling tales to two or three,
I never rat that's just not me.
Doesn't matter, women or men,
all ears turn it's gossip again.
