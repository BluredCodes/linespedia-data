---
title: "A Business Decision"
writer: "Mad Jack"
slug: "a-business-decision"

It wasn't poetic,
it wasn't prose,
just a business decision,
caused the plant to close.

What is this perfidity,
we're just left to guess,
just a business decision,
caused this hellacious mess?

It's the easiest thing,
to sign on the line,
just a business decision,
then go out and dine.

They'll say it with ease,
but won't look in your eye,
just a business decision,
you don't have to cry.

Don't worry about bills,
or your children in bed,
just a business decision,
and mine are well fed.

Left out in the cold,
to fend for yourself,
just a business decision,
causes an empty shelf.

I'll come back strong,
I'll do my part,
but it wasn't a business decision,
just a lack of heart.
