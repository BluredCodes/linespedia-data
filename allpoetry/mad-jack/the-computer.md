there it sits
all quiet and
still
put on a sweater
it doesn't feel
the chill
try giving it data
that it will take
it gives you
a giant headache
you think it is easy
click here
drag there
then it makes you
pull out your hair
its finally done
it works
tho it made you ill
there it sits
all quiet and 
stillSeptember 2000,
