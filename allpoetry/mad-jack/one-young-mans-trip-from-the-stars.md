Quark Schrz felt low
He'd bruised his toe
On a rock way up on Mars

He stooped to look
The pain he'd took
On this trip up to the stars

He left his home
An old gray dome
Of his trip he'd tell by pen

Think what you've had
Don't leave, said Dad
Up there are little green men

Still off he went
By youth was sent
To satisfy wanderlust

Quark didn't whine
Just fixed his sign
It simply read, "EARTH or BUST"
