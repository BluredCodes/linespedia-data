title: "Hope"

Snowflake falling down
Smiling face of a clown
Beauty rides on crystal wings
Comedy entertains winsome kings

Now the flake begins to melt
Face turns down, sadness felt
Beauty fades in searing heat
Praise of kings leaves fast and fleet

Snowflake now is made again
Clown repaints face and then
Beauty flies its life renewed
Kings face brightens brilliantly hued

Life is full of ups and downs
Hope remains to erase the frowns
