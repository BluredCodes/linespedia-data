Must you be blind, can you not see me for me?
Maybe I am not like you, maybe I don’t do the same things as you
So?
For some odd reason the way you live your life is right but the way I live mine is wrong
But why?
I am not like everyone else, I am not as “cool,” and I am different
Is that bad?
It is because of self-indulging pricks like you that I cry inside
What is the point?
Sinking to a new low each and every day and I hate myself more and more
Is that right?
I am frustrated and angry but the only ones who can help just laugh at me
What did I ever do to you?
I am a mere source of entertainment for you and your crew
Am I really that amusing?
One day I get so fed up that I decide to make it my last day, as well as yours
Who is laughing now?
