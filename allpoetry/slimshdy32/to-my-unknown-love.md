---
title: "To My Unknown Love..."
writer: "SlimShdy32"

My one and only is out there, please tell me it is so
I have not been acquainted with her yet, but someday I will know
At my utmost passion and in all my glory are the feelings I shall hold
A true love with whom I will grow old with and start to form a mold
My other half, the possessor of my happiness, my reason to be alive
The ultimate love is my daily motivation and the strength for me to thrive
Only she holds the real key to my heart, I only unlock so far for another
Is the one I feel most intimate with, also my children’s mother
Where you are and when I will find you only time will tell
And someday I will reminisce on how you were the one who in love I fell
