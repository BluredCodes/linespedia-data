title: "The Ultimate Infatuation"

I have never known a love like this
My heart’s dictionary defines you
Something beyond mere infatuation
Your presence reaches to all depths of my soul
More than a smile, more than a kiss
I love all of you
Entirely

I love how you try not to impress me, yet you still do
Long talks and late nights together
You love me for me
If not for any other reason
You allow me to be myself
You love me for that
Unconditionally

Stellar in every way
I cannot even begin to explain how I feel
Never so passionate about one
The ultimate crush
You have found the key to my love
We love each other
Eternally
