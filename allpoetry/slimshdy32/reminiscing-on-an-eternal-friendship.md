Remember when…
Spending the evening counting stars and imagining what it was like on the moon
Staying up till the wee hours of the morning writing poetry together
Your sweet lips caressing mine and nothing else in the world mattering
Writing notes back and forth in class talking about the latest gossip
Pillow fights and camping out under the covers
Sneaking out and risking being grounded for life just to be with one another
The times where we would just sit by the fire, cuddling with each other
Watching movies and falling asleep in each other’s arms
Dusting off old LPs of Marvin Gaye and reminiscing on how beautiful his music was
Trips to the beach building sandcastles and digging our toes into the sand
Taking long walks hand in hand even in the worst weather
Staring into your glistening eyes and reading them as if they had written something

My one love
My best friend
I love you unconditionally
Alwayssomewhat inspired by my friend brooke's poem on her perfect date only my poem is written in the perspective of someday looking back on the lifelong friendship shared with the love of my life
