I’m confused…
You tell me one thing, but it is really not how you feel
I wish I knew the right way to go, but I do not
I feel for her, but it is with you that I share a special bond
Why do I get into these situations I ask, but I am not answered

I’m not mad, but yet I am
Not at anyone, just in general 
All of this seems unfair, but so is life I guess…
I would go with my heart, but even it doesn’t know what to do
Why did it have to between you two?
My best friend and her best friend…
I love you both, but in two different ways
When I am with her I feel guilty about what I am doing to you
When I am with you I feel guilty about what I am doing to her

It is not your fault, nor is it anyone’s…
I want everything to turn out just fine, but I doubt that is possible
Someone please tell me…please tell me which way to go…
