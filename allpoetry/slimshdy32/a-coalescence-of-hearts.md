‘Tis more than a passion
Its vigor I know not
He kindles thou
Rejuvenates her soul
A prince hath lain his being upon her
Secure to all extents in his presence
He utters so tranquilly his words of simplicity
‘Tis no more than “I love you”
Yet she is drawn in to thou and two become one
Lest he shall relapse in his mind
She forbid him forget this moment of passion
His soft, yet cool breath upon thy neck awakens all sensations
“Shall I be no longer come morrow I wish for thou to remember thee”
Perhaps ‘tis a fallen angel sent to her
God’s allowance of mere pleasure turns into a stellar act of romance
Thy prince’s lips meet his woman’s
A slowly moving, vibrant hand softly caresses her bosom
With eyes closed the two lovers thrust their devotion into each other
Thy hearts mold together and beat as one
She whispers so soundly, with the profoundest of meaning
