Pop
That’s exactly what I’m gonna do if I hear about it anymore
The shiny clothes and 85,000 plays a week on the radio
Magazines telling you what new Hollywood star I should look like
Just because I see it on TV that’s what is cool?
So many into hype and too worried about the lives of the famous than their own
Popularity makes the world dumber
Everything always seems to be a popularity contest and who is the coolest
Who cares?
No variety
No variety
No variety
Get out of the hype
Don’t be blinded by what some other force tells you to be
You will be happier because of it
Expand your mind…it is powerful and can think on its own…amazing huh?
TV…ha!
Radio…ha!
I think I’ll go live in a cave

Maybe I have no business saying this and I should just go into to my underground world
But you know what?
Pop is not reality
Pop is not what happens
Pop kills
Pop makes anorexics
Pop makes people wear beauty products
Pop makes people work themselves to death over having the biggest biceps
Pop makes a mind useless
Pop is a dictatorship…it controls what we see, hear, and do
Pop creates depression
Pop is fake
Pop, pop-a-pop, pop, pop, pop
Sick of the word yet?

You may not like this poem because it is different 
Not what someone consumed in the world of popularity would write
Go ahead, disagree
I am an open-minded person
But think about why you are disagreeing…
Is it because you too are consumed by the all mighty ‘pop’?
Maybe so, maybe not…definitely wouldnt fit in the 'contemp/mainstream' category...
