On a long and cold summer night
Blending into the blackened sky
Nothing to be seen approaching
But ahead the out of control vision of a light
The driver is weary, feeling a little bit high
The vehicle is swerving and encroaching
Into the path of a nearby sight
A wrong move is made and now the question is why?
"It was just a bad mistake, why do I have to die?"
"I promise I will never do it again," he sings
This is the plea, that upward to heaven he flings
There is nothing left after the damage has been done
Because of getting out of hand and just having a little too much fun.this is a poem i wrote in ninth grade that i just found
