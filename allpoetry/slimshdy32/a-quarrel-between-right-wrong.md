My mind is at war again
Mike 1 vs. Mike 2
A-side vs. B-side
Peace vs. war
Serenity vs. rage
Open-mind vs. bias
Morals vs. risk behaviors
Euphoria vs. paranoia
Conservatism vs. liberalism
Intensity vs. relaxation
Bliss vs. depression
Sincerity vs. corruption
Non-conformity vs. popularity
Truth vs. bullshit
Selflessness vs. egotism
Smiles vs. tears
Real vs. fake
Unadulterated vs. hypocritical
Feel good vibes vs. hardcore music
Acceptance vs. racism
Faith vs. giving up
Art vs. TRL
Abstract vs. concrete

Which way do I go?
Which way is the right way?
Is there a right way?

People criticize
What makes them right?
Maybe they are
Maybe they are not
It is not for one to decide
One man’s belief is another mans hatred
Society member 1 vs. society member 2

Bickering
Lawsuits
Fights
Discrimination
Stubborn
Hatred
Evil

Popularity determines a man’s fate
That doesn’t mean it is right…just some other stuff to think about...
