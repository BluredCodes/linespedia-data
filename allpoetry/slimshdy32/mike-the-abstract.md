I
Move in mysterious ways making me hard to predict
I
Allow my soul to rid me of impurities
I
Sing words of enchantment for those around me to be invigorated
I
Am an intellectual being who fights with his mind
I
Am atypical, unlike anyone else you will ever know
I
Write emotionally with my feelings left unbarred
I
Possess a free spirit that allows me to decide my life
I
Am unchained from the chains of popularity
I
Believe in the mind and all of the wisdom it has
I
Ask questions about the unknown
I
Am abstracti am curious to see how this one goes over...i like to consider myself as different
