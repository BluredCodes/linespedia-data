On a cold and blustery evening in February
The wind whistles as if a train were nearby
The snow has fallen and covered the trees
Peace is begotten by this no less than stellar scene
A snow owl cries aloud, a fox scampers through the woods

I take a short walk through these woods, a bit chilly and dark
Yet I am alone and at peace
The winter eve hath liberated me of my fears
My sorrows are forgotten
A new life comes about me

I hear the sound of crunching
My boots trudge through the thick snow
I continue my walk and reminisce of winters past
Since mother nature was calm this year
Yet she managed to unleash her exquisite fury if not more than once

Ah yes I love the summer
Yet for one day Mother Nature invites me into her other side
Winter is the season now, though spring not far off
I enjoy this one day in all of its essence
For it I am thankfulwe have had a lot of snow fall in the past two days and the sight is just incredible...
