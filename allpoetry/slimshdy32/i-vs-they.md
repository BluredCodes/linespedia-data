---
title: "I vs. They"
writer: "SlimShdy32"
slug: "i-vs-they"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40874-I-vs.-They-by-SlimShdy32"
---

They see quietness
I see vibrancy
They see no depth
I see an intellect deeper than the Pacific Ocean at its utmost extent
They see self-infatuation
I see mostly a passion for others
They see a non-acceptance for difference
I see difference as the root of vast new experiences
They see a guy writing poetry and classify it as ‘gay’
I see a guy writing poetry who is not condemned by a concrete society, such as they
They see hopelessness
I see a future brighter than the Sun itself
They see themselves as right and all else as wrong
I see them neither right nor wrong, just lacking in authority to judge

Is ignorance bliss or is bliss ignorance?
