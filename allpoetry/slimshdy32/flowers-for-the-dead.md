title: "Flowers for the Dead"
writer: "SlimShdy32"

I miss you
What happened?
You were not here long enough
Life is so unexpected
Unfair
Why you, one of the better ones?
So innocent
You had so much living to do
Things to see
Experiences to share
Boys to fall in love with
Friends to grow old with

I cry
Because you are gone
Because of the great person you are
We were not really close
Yet I am still in pain
I love you
As is true for all of my friends
Close and far apart

I realize 
And have come to learn
Life is taken as easily as it is given
We must love
Hate is not worth it
Have no regrets
Regrets only make pain worse
Never be afraid to say what you feel
Ever
You never know when you won’t be able to say it
Live for today
Tomorrow is never guaranteed

I see
All that you have done
Lives you have touched
What you are and what you will always be
Physically you are gone
Your soul lives on
Your presence will be felt forever
I will never forget you

Rest in peace Amber Newcomer *October 18, 1985 - February 15, 2002*
