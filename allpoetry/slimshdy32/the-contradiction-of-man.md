They say a man leads a quiet life of desperation
I speak aloud and am optimistic for each new day
Man is his own worst enemy
The hate that lingers for myself exists only in small fashion
Man is exalted by his words
Exalt me
Man in society is determined by how much a man makes
Everyone is rich in his own way
Man is determined by the color of his skin
I must be color-blind
Man sees all others as inferior
Cheetahs run 70 miles per hour and bats can fly and hunt with poor vision
Man measures another man by his physical strength
I personally would rather be Bill Gates than Stone Cold Steve Austin
