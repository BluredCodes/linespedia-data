I feel used
Unappreciated
Pissed on
Like I am just along for the ride
I just want to S C R E A M
But at who?
They are my friends after all
Or are they?

The tag along
The one-dimensional figure
The ride there
The guy who can drive
The “coolest person on Earth” because of a plastic card with my photo on it

I say I won’t do it anymore over and over again
But yet I just get sucked in
Over and over again
The same old routine
It’s hard to tell apart real from fake
I just don’t know

Maybe it’s just my imagination
Maybe I truly am appreciated
Maybe all my worries are over nothing

But still I feel empty
Empty inside
I feel used
