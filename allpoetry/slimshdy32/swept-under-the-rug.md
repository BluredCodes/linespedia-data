What is justice?
A pillar that holds this country up
Our founding fathers claimed “all people deserved it equally”
Synonyms: fairness, honesty, integrity

Sure what happened wasn’t major scale
But why does this victim’s satisfaction mean any less?
My perfect little town
Neighborly and cute
Wouldn’t want to scare anyone, would we?
Ha! The key witness is only 16!  She must be full of shit!
Show us!  Tell the newspapers!  Put it on TV!
AWAKEN THIS SMALL TOWN!
Homicide, suicide, blah, blah, blah
It’s all the same, right?
Your life, my life
I kill you they give me life
She kills him it goes away all so nicely
He wasn’t anyone prominent anyway
Way to wipe the slate clean
I applaud your quiet tactics
Some role model you are
I feel even more comfortable knowing there are murderers around
What if I am next?
What will you say then?two friends of mine were the only witnesses to a murder which was thrown out and called a 'suicide' just so the community wouldnt be disrupted...
