Two wise men sit down at a table in a coffee shop
Conversation over a latte
Today’s discussion on how to change the world
The typical subjects…
Terrorism, affirmative action, foreign affairs
You know, politics as usual…
A wise liberal and a wise conservative are they
Debating, debating, debating
Only farther from compromise as the contest gets more involved
The two wise men continue to share their ideas
Chatting, chatting, chatting
A third man walks up
“Wanna hear my two cents worth?”
In uttermost disgust the snooty first man looks up at the third
All he can say, however, is “yes”
As if the third party could actually have a valid point against his
His words come out
Flowing, flowing, flowing
Believing in one reason for the world’s tribulations
Only one reason
Criticizing the two wise men
Explaining that their divided partisanship is the reason no one gets along
If we are divided how can we agree?
The third man stands up
Shakes his head, shakes his head, shakes his head
In a deep confused state the two men sit
They say nothing
Nothing at all
Two complex men drawn off by simplicity
Absolute silence lingers in the air
The first man stands up
The second man stands up
The offer each other a handshake
Nothing more
