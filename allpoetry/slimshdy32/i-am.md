I am an athletic girl who loves to talk
I wonder what the millennium will bring
I hear a thousand thoughts all around me
I see the world through my window
I want another chance at life
I am an athletic girl who loves to talk

I pretend to be a bird watching over the world
I feel the eyes upon me
I touch the rings of Saturn
I worry about the future
I cry for help when I'm in a storm of decisions
I am an athletic girl who loves to talk

I understand that love is a painful sensation
I say be who you want to be and not let others
Tell you who you should be
I dream about the day when I become a star
I try to be a successful person
I hope to have a job that pays a lot of money
I am an athletic girl who loves to talk
