title: "Night"
writer: "SlimShdy32"

Whisked away in its darkness and aroused by its presence
I am livened by the spirit of night, and it possesses me in a way that I cannot explain
A new life comes about me, one that is absent throughout the day
Its shadows and eerie touch excite the spirits within my soul
It is a new awakening, one that stimulates all of my features
Through night’s virtuosity I become one of its prodigious creatures

So dark, yet so calm
The kiss of night is a blessing
The fruits of earth are alive within
A summit where good meets evil
The stars are night’s treasures, the sky is their chest
A wolf’s howl at a full moon is solemn, but a lively cry nonetheless

A time of physical sleep but mental awakening
The later it is the more peaceful it seems
Midnight is an hour of new beginning, for it begins each new day
I envy its power because it is the only thing able to set the sun
Time is abrupt, for it does not allow night to last long
I wish time could adopt me and forever sing me its song
