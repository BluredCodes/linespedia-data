Death by Drowning

Quiet and calm,
Submerging under the cool blanket.
Surrounded by life,
And still a single breath is impossible to find.
Bubbles escapes to freedoms,
Cemented shoes preventing me to do the same.
Surface turns to ceiling,
Ceiling turns to unreachable
Unreachable turns to the infinite,
I am falling with remembrance.
 I was conceived in water,
Consist of water,
Could never survive without water,
Death by drowning seems the perfect end.
My lungs gasp for last breathes,
Instead fill with salty mixtures,
I shed a lonely tear.
I just realized as I float away,
To the bottom of the sea,
No one will ever really know,
What really happened to me.time doesn\'t  exist,
