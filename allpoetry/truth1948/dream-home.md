title: "Dream Home"

A dream used to come to me 
To build a home for my family 

Nothing special you see 
Four walls and a roof were enough for me 

Worked long and hard and saved 
For the home that we craved 

A tent had been our home for years 
For refugees life is full of hardship and tears 

The day we finished our house 
A rare moment of happiness for me and my spouse 

Nothing special you see 
Four walls and a roof were enough for me 

The soldiers came at night 
Filled my children with dread and fright 

We were ordered to step outside 
We had no choice, but to abide 

In the rubble we were left to cry and scream 
For the bulldozers had destroyed our dream 

A tent is our home now and for years 
As refugees life is full of hardship and tears 

A dream still comes to me 
To build a home for my family 

Nothing special you see 
Four walls and a roof are enough for me... 

Mike Odetalla
