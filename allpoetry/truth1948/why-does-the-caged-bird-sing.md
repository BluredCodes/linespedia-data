Why does the caged bird sing?
 
Is his song that one of joy
or is it of  loneliness?
 
Does he sing the praises of his jailer
or is he cursing him?
 
Is he singing to entertain his captor
Or is it a plea for freedom?
 
Does he sing for the love of his cage
Or is it because of his hatred for it?
 
Oh why does a caged bird sing?

Mike Odetalla
