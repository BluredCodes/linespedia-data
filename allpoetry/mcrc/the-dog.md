THE DOG
THE DOG WAS A DOG
THE DOG WAS A NICE DOG
THE DOG WAS A NICE BROWN DOG
THE DOG WAS A NICE BROWN DOG WHO LIKED TO LICK STUFF
THE DOG WAS A NICE BROWN DOG WHO LIKED TO LICK STUFF AND CHASE STUFF

THE DOG
