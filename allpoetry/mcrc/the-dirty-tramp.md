he only wants 50p
that he can by some tea

HAVE YOU EVER DONE A DIRT TRAMP?

have you ever heard him plea
plea plea
plea plea
i only want 50p

he finds it hard to do a shite
amd he can`t sleep at night
with all that blocked up shite
people are rather tight

with all that money
and act rarther funny
this tramp wants a bunny

its not fair

says the tramphomlessness
