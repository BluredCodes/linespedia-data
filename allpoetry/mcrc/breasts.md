Breasts, Breasts, Breasts,

Big round breasts,
A breast under a vest,
From a woman from the west,
Is a challanging test,

Breast, Breasts, Breasts,

Nipples,nipples,nipples,
I like when in a tipple,
Give it a suck,
For some outstanding luck,
Oh what a wonderful nipple.I am challenged when assesing the mood and meaning of the rhyme pattern in my work, and wonder as such what ryhmes best with nipple?
