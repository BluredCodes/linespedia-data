It takes eyes to see



It takes brains to know



It takes lungs to breath



It takes seeds to grow



It takes feet to walk



It takes hands to touch



It takes a face to smile



It takes arms to hug



It takes ears to hear



It takes a nose to smell



It takes a voice to sing



It takes lips to kiss.





All of these are the windows to my world...



and without them...all that would be left



are the dreams of a child sleeping.



--Uncle Greg
