Mesmerized, like a moth drawn to light...
willfully inclining like toward a magnet 
seemingly so incredibly strong
alluring, irresistible.
A craving, an urge so powerful from deep inside me...
none of my interior nuns or cops could succeed to 
restrain or interrupt.

I was a Buddha kneeling in the dark
beholding in awe, lording over 
sleek tanned legs and thighs 
at first so slightly spread 
then raised, straddled over daddy's shoulders
open for to invite me in...to recieve.
  
Drawn to slim, slender thighs
and your feminine curvatious eyes,
I ask, how can it be that one so sleek
afterall, is a flesh-craving beast?

"What compels me to seek you...
crave, even, to eat you?" I ask. 
"And what draws you to me?"

I wonder: Why do we love? 
What makes us love?

In you do I see 
the dance of countless generations... 
of women that were, and shall be.
Mother of mothers...vessal bearing...
tomorrow's children...
The buzz within amid is
like a bee hive.

I yearn to envelop, to enrapture 
my undeserving ape-like beastly mass 
around thy fine fertile form.
In but one swallow doth I crave
to ingest all that is you, 
thy every genetic History be
unto me and mine...
to makest thou one with me...
to comingle mine 
deep inside ye within me
for our shared progeny.

And still I wonder: Why? 
What fury draws me unto you?
What drives me--
this throbbing beast--
to abandon me from myself?

Driven, driven...to thine Fertile Crescent
wherein bikini undies slip slowly
to lower thighs without tan lines.
My eyes drawn, to return to the spot
where all lines converge...like 
delta 'Y'.

If I succeed finally 
returning to you, Mommy...

Will I be saved from dying?
Will my return to you
renew me...rejuvenate me?

And so...that night 
did me Mom and Dad conceive
a baby girl.

--G-man767
