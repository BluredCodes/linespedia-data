title: "Daddy's Bones"

I am a farmer,
an Oklahoma man.
A man who has seen
the Dust Bowl and Famine.
I held two hungry children
while their mother was sick.
I cried the tears
of a lonely man,
an empty man...
unable to give,
too proud to take.
And I held, I held
and I prayed.
For in that dust
blew my Daddy's bones
in my eyes and in my throat.
And I held, I held
two wailin' children
so helpless and hungry.
Aye, that day I saw myself...
And Daddy's bones blew no more.

--Uncle Greg
