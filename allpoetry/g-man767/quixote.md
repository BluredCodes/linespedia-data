A castrato out of Nero's Rome
went to New York City
to find Hermaphroditus.
A castrater eunuch
my frigid, sterile Jezebel,
my harlot, my perverse beloved son
tied in knots--O Electra-Oedipus.
My child has heard the coital parents
to hate the man
to kill the femme
to be so alone, alone in some
aloof self-denial, some self-hatred...
some strange imploding black hole
of self-annihilation.
No one can reach you
no one can touch you
My Dark Lovely Muse.
Inside out, you are outside in.
May you cast your pearls
upon unclenched fists
upon the innocent and wicked alike--
may you draw your distant life
from your lovers of death.
And laugh, in fecal sharing--oh, indeed,
may you laugh at me, Quixote,
that fecund lover of life!

--Uncle Greg
