Looking closely, at a single drop
a bead of water on a leaf, 
in just such a way that I see 
much more than my own reflection.
Peering deeply I see
drops within drops within drops
That contain many oceans, rivers, 
clouds and rainbows...
And still more drops within drops...

Beyond the beyond, within the within--
Between the in-between 
of Everything
Is a completely incomplete, 
perfectly imperfect
Selfless Self 
whose Endless Beginning is
Its Beginningless End--
the Me that is Everything; 
Seer never fully Self-Seen, 
World My Mirror...
Knower Unknown. 

--Uncle Greg
