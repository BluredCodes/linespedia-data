Notes of Promise as Ethos...



Judaism: Thine Fate thus cast, look forward.

         Become as Gods, by subduing the Earth 

         and all its ills. Tame Nature for there is

         No Super-Nature beyond Nature's supreme intrinsic

         intelligence. No personal afterlife, beyond the spacetime 

         of one's tiny life. Only the potential of remembrance & 

         posterity. (Thy Promise - This World Alone.)



Christianity: Through Good might ye ascend to              

         redeem/absolve thyselves of the Original 

         Sin of Man's Fall into Materiality via Divisive 

         Desire. Thy personal soul belongs to a Super-Nature. 

        (Thy Promise - This & Other/More/Next World(s).)



Islam: Neither East nor West...lacking Reformation from an 

       heretofore ancient Orthodoxy...saith, Prostrate 

       thyself before Absolute Allah, for thine own tiny  

       physical existence is sinful. Thy personal soul is 

       Extra-Natural.(Promise - Next World) 



Hinduism: Maya (the Physical World), as Manifestation of       

          endless karmic cycle(s) of reincarnation/rebirth, is  

          Eternity's Illusion. Thy soul's ultimate destiny is 

          to achieve/realize Oneness with/as Creator of 

          All/Atman. Thou art already One with All despite thine 

          karmic [temporal/historic] stage. Thy personal/extra-

          personal soul is at once Natural and Extra-Natural (Thy   

          Hope/Promise - This & Next World.)



Buddhism: Animal Desire perpetuates the eternal cycle of human         

          sufferring. Hence, Desire not. In the Great Non-Cyclic

          Nothingness one's person, and all suffering, ceases 

          long after chaos has blended into oblivious silence of peace.

          (Thy Promise - Nirvana/This & Next  World:)



--Uncle Greg
