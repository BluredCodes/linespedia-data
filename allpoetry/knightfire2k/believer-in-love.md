title: "Believer in Love"
writer: "Knightfire2k"

Tell me O'Lord of love and might why you have blessed me with such beauty at my sight.A delicate rose that has blossomed in your light.Protect her with angels each day and night.For never before has my heart been this touched.Never before have I yearned for love so much.Never has anyone I hardly know seemed so close.For the fact that she knows you lord is what I cherish most.For inside her heart I know true love is alive.Thus inside me her perfect man I will stive.Though it's too early to tell how your plans will unfold.From the bottom of my heart I pray O'Lordthat someday I may have her to hold.*
