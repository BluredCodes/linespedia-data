Well you told me she was the right one.you told me she's the one who could set me free.But she just turned away like the others,and I'm still locked up in this lonely misery.You told me she was special,and that she's the one who'd care.But my heart bleeds anouther night,In the lonely shaddows of a heartache again I am there.  Why, Oh why can't I trust you heart.I put my faith in you, but each time you just tear my world apart.And why, oh why can't I find a key,to a heart of gold that will set me free.I listen to you at night and you promise all my dreams will come true.And each day I wait for it to happen, but they never do.I thought that love could conquer all,and sometimes I still believe it to be.But each time that I do I always fall,and end up drowning in sarrows sea.
