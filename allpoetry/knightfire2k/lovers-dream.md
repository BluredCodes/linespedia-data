Silver cloud pouring with rainGolden sun drying it up againMorning sparrow flying aboveEarly blossom radiant with loveBlue ocean crashing the shoreYoung eagle yearning to soarShining stars in your eyesDelightful smile makes me highSweet dreams as I sleepTrue love taking me in deepGraceful swan caressing the lakeEnchanting heart as I awakeGentle hope for your delicate touchTender wishes never ment so muchSecret dreams of holding you foreverSensitive desires of being togetherHeartfelt poem to tell you trueWith sincere words-  I LOVE YOU*
