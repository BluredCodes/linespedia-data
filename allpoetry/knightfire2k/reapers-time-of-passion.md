Blackened heart filled with miseryLive a life of fear and infelicityShattered dreams affict my hopes of happinessMy heart cries tears of bloodMy pride dragged through the mudAnd now I have to fight the wars of bitternessSilent screams ringing in my earsBroken heart filled with tearsVexation of my spirit brings me to agonyWaring emotions mess up my thoughtsDecaying love leaves my heart to rotAnd now I wonder what the future holds for meWanting to die as I now plot my suicideKnowing I can no longer run or hideFor I am left to suffer all aloneI have a choice weather to live or dieI've chosen death some may question why.REAPERS TIME OF PASSION, Lowest point in my life. Thank God I made it through.
