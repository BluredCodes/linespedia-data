I'VE TRIED TO STOP RUNNING
FROM THIS LIFE
I'VE ALWAYS CONSIDERED 
MYSELF TO BE SMART
BUT I AM AFRAID OF CHANGE
SO IT BRINGS ME DOWN

I'VE TRIED TO STOP SETTLING FOR THIS LIFE
I'VE ALWASY BELIEVED
MYSELF TO BE HONEST
STILL I FIND MYSELF SUFFERING
SO IT BRINGS ME DOWN

I'VE DENIED MY MISFORTUNE
I'VE PUSHED IT AWAY
I'VE GOT NOTHING TO 
CARE ABOUT ANYMORE
I AM SEARCHING FOR TIME
AND IT BRINGS ME DOWN

I'VE TRIED TO BECOME
SOMETHING IN THIS LIFE
I'VE ALWAYS LONGED
FOR SOME RECOGNITION
FINALLY I GOT WHAT I WANTED
STILL IT BRINGS ME DOWN
