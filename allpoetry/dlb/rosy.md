title: "rosy"

There is a glow around this
only you cannot seem to recognize
what it means

I realize this depth, only I
while you pretend that you do not
see your reflection on my skin
and the thought in my eyes

I assumed that you would
completely disregard everything
that you had been taught
about befriending
about loving
about even noticing
a girl who came 
from the wrong side of the tracks

Maybe someday
we will meet again
when this
   when that
      when then
         when now
will no longer matter
everyone will be equal

Maybe then you will recognize 
what this means

Maybe then 
you will notice the
glow.
