Today I tried to create a mission
It failed so miserably
among its flock of 
destroyed brothers

I sigh at this helpless situation
I cry out of desperation 
because this is one life
that I cannot miscredit

Lives piled beside my bed
Lived with broken promises
and lost dreams and departed faith

I wrote them all
Yes, I wrote the book
Now all of the pages are missing
I am missing
and I cannot imagine a god
who would even care
if I live or die

Just a black sheep
I am the black sheep
and my shepherd has died
where I stabbed him in the back.
