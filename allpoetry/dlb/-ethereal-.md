I walked around the silver screen
and dreamed of bright, glassy
cherries shining red in the glow

The good vibrations spread 
throughout my body and I
offered it up to the 
sacrificial altar 
to please the
revolutionary one

Crunching footsteps 
amidst whistling calls
to barefoot believers

There will come a moment
when all of this will slip
through my fingers
drip forth from my pores
and I will have nothing

The dark clouds will break
without sunshine
and my body will fall 
limp to the floor

I will lie naked 
in my own blood
and blazing angels 
will stand ready
to grasp my hands.
