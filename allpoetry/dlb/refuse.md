Lustful nights seldom follow fruitless days
Many memories
have been formed or shaped
on a sandy beach
memories which will dull and fade
Over eternities of repetition.

Useless dreams often accompany harsh reality
Many oppurtunists
have found themselves trapped
in a cramped cubicle
Oppurtunists who have missed 
the train to Oppurtunity.

Countless years sometimes shelter brief moments
Many lives
have been wasted dwelling mournfully
on hardships of the past
Lives which are clouded by vignettes and prosperity
Contributes to the idiocy of this time.
