We dream of becoming
strong and independent
with our own free thoughts

but you refuse to allow 
any allowances
to those who are your
sisters, mothers, wives, daughters

Just continue to shove 
us on our backs
Just continue to turn
us to the womb
and we will burst forth

we will suffocate you
in this last-ditch attempt
to gain our own sanity.
