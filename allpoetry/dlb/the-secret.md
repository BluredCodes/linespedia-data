I too, am indulging 
in one clever idea after another
I think it would be nice
to rule the world
Me and my nations
Me and mine

I could fall in deep 
and nuzzle with the planets
As a power
A major power
I'd have all the power

I could be a hero
I could be special
I would be adored
My people would never get over me
My supervision would help guide the way

Because control is never simple
Life is just not cut and dry
Under all the buzz
Within the spin of things
Confined in the middle of nowhere
Lies a shy girl
an insecure girl

Underneath it all stands a little girl
with big dreams.
