---
title: "*Lucky Stars*"
writer: "dlb"

Stars on my pillows
Stars in her eyes
Emptiness in my heart
She doesn't show surprise

Candy pink and sweet
Candy in her hand
Somewhere I am screaming
In a far and distant land

Butterflies in summer
Butterflies in rain
She only stops to stare and smile 
as I slowly go insane

Shaking like a leaf
Shaking her blond curls
Pretending that I fit in
With all the other girls

Mocking only shadows
Mocking my own face
I listen to their laughter
and hang my head low in disgrace

Skipping rope at recess
Skipping every class
Hoping that this nauseous state
Will soon and blankly pass

Sitting in her bed at night
Sitting on the ground
Praying that what I am about to do
Doesn't make a sound

Stars in the heavens
Stars that now are lies
With this gun at my head
I'll now say my good-byes.1998,
