I once thought that the sky
looked beautiful at night...
it was when I was a little girl
and wore pink
I tied my hair up with ribbons
Played soccer at recess
Boys were gross...
The days were too short
The nights were dark and scary

But I've outgrown the pink
Now I wear only black
I hide behind closed doors
My head is shaved
I quit playing sports, oh so long ago
Boys are now men and my obsession
The nights are too short
The days seem to never end...

and the sky that once looked 
so beautiful at night...
tinted pink and purple....

is now merely a reminder 
of the polluted lies 
that we are fed as
little girls in
pink.
