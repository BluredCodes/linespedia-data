You know that feeling
It's 6 am
and you're not ready for the world
But it's out there waiting
and there is nothing 
that will slow it down

Coming to your senses
you struggle for a concept
of time of bearing of anything
Your life is draining
and a startled face appears
misshapen in the mirroe

But always pushing on...
Got to keep this going
and you want to crawl back into bed...
and pull the blinds....
just protect yourself from the evil
and the good that you know is out there

But there's no time...oh, so little time
to just enjoy the moment and STOP
You just keep trudging on into the sunset
of broken dreams and departed promises

Then your brain clicks
You slam the door with a smile pasted on...

And then you come back and get your keys.
