title: "Jagged"
writer: "dlb"

One day I knew of something
A thing
One thing
And on this day
I finally put this gift into practise
But this gift had rotted away
because I had set it out in the sun
It needed
not to sit
but to be used
I never took care of it
It is dead
Now I know of nothing
Not one thing
Not a thing
