I want to rule my own physical domain
Everything seems to veer out of control
I know that I am making
something out of nothing
This is the fashion
I am not ashamed 
of this extreme vanity
Perhaps this is why
I belong to no club
no clique
no circle
I have only myself
I am wrapped ten-fold
in my exaggerated self
and I have yet to rule my own physical domain.
