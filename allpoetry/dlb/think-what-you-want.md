We are the kids in this hall
Ranting and raving
about this or that
Discuss the wasteland of A & G
or the endless tirade of Hitler
Dissect the relevance of sex 
in this already deformed society
Break into song
Hold hands
Nothing is better than this
I've never felt tighter than this
You don't have to be a believer
nor a conformist
Just yourself
One of us
We are the kids of this hall.
