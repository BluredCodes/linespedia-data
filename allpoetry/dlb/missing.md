I'm sorry
but I'm not happy
Everyone looks at me
and they all think
There's a happy girl

But they don't know
If only they knew
If only I knew

Why my mind is always playing
deranged tricks upon itself
and delivering this satire
as an image on my face
for all the world to see

I live beneath a multiplying facade
that only pauses to extricate itself
from any kind of blame

And I go on...

My mind keeps right on analyzing
anything and everything
just to keep this going

Until another day
When I will crack and dissolve
into myself and beg 
for someone to try to know me...

I'm sorry...
but
I'm just not happy.
