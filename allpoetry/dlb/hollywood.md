I'm gonna be a star, man
Thanks to this famous land
The only thing in my way
Is that success is never planned.

I'm gonna be a stuntman
Maybe I'll get to play Clark Kent
If only I could find a way
To keep paying for my rent.

I'm gonna be like Oprah
The greatest talk-show host by far
Surprising how this face lift
Made me an instant porno star

I got a great bit part
Too bad the movie had to fold
Upon seeing all these wrinkles
I realize I'm too old.

I'm gonna do some directing
Something with a lot of class
I'm stunned when I get shot down
and kicked out on my a$$.

It is a place where dreams are made
It is a place where hopes are crushed
This is the only place
The IRS hasn't left untouched.

It's the place where leeches
Make money off of a name
This is the land of Hollywood
Where millions strive for fame.1997,
