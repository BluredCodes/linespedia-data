I was born today
Yeah, it was great to see the world
Everything looks brighter
when it's the first time...

I turned three today
and I am happy
just to love and be loved
Funny how wise this seems...

I started school today
so many people 
so many places - it was a real trip
I feel like I am drowning...

I failed grade eight today
Dad moved out last week
No one can help
because nobody cares...

Sweet sixteen today
but there's nothing sweet about it
Only the desperation 
to fit into the mold...

Found out I'm knocked up today
I cried and cried
Not for my baby but for me
I never had my life...

I slashed my wrists today
and stared as the blood seeped out
and I am dying
i am dying
but I'm not afraid of the dark
because my whole life has been in the dark

I am nothing
I am forever and ever 
and ever and never

i am  n o t h i n g.1998,
