This is a memory of someone else's existence
I wish...
I wish sometimes that I knew just what exactly
it is that I am wishing for

Blinded by my own stupidity
I just cannot change
I'm the closest thing to insanity
Because all I can taste is my life
And sooner or later this will be over

This is the memory of
who I want you to know I am
I wish that I wouldn't 
fight the tears that rage forth
But I can't get you to understand
That I've always been broken

I just want the memory
I
 just want a little.
