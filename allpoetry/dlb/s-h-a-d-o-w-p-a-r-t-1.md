i am this vice sitting beside you
head bent, pen poised
your eyes calculative behind your lashes
brain ticking, thoughts turning
intelligence glows from your skin
i am this vice sitting beside you
i want to touch your fingers
i want to caress your face
i want to be with you
yet there is something bigger than me
bigger than my love
bigger than anything
and you push me away
no response 
no cares
nothing
just your pen
just your thoughts
just my tearsThis poem is paired with s h a d o w - p a r t 2.
