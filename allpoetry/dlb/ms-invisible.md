title: "Ms. Invisible"

are you tired of being you
because I am tired of never knowing myself
at least you know
you know who you are...

because I am trapped in this fateful illusion
that only fireflies create 
in the midnight sky

I am trapped in a lover's arms
only these arms are strange to me

I am speaking with a voice 
that I don't even recognize

I am travelling on feet that feel
lifeless and separate from my body...

yet you complain that
you are tired of being you

let's trade places for a moment
and then - 

then you can let me know
just how happy you feel
to be
