Cracked sunshine
in a world where only
darkness fades
into the cesspool of
eternal discomfort...

and the days seem shorter
the nights stretch black
like the highway of life
that blooms again and again
year after year

and the sunshine
cracks into a million
different pieces of light

and somewhere children
are singing with joy.1998,
