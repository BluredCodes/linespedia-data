What are you looking for?
In your world where dreams occur
In your world where everything is fine
and everything is sure

What are you looking for?
That sets us so far apart
Have I not done enough for you?
Have I not done my part?

What are you looking for?
That has not been sought before
What you seek has been found
There is simply nothing more

What are you looking for?
There are no answers dear
The plain and simple truth is that
No one knows why we are here.1998,
