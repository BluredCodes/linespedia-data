---
title: "Untouchable"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "untouchable"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6650-Untouchable-by-LitOnFire"
---

Almost everything
inside
this space
reminds me
of those
times
when I
backed you
into those 
small,
confining
corners
and everything
seemed
to comfort you.
Little replacements
and pieces 
of you
fill empty
voids.
I must make
you feel
so pressured
when I'm 
holding you
down
and making 
you see
all the
reasons why
little things
bother me.
Almost everything
directs me
to all
the right 
places
for all
the right
reasons.
Look-
maybe you 
can see
past the
hurt
you keep
causing;
and my 
life 
you keep
shattering.
Almost everything
changes
from one day
to the next.
I want those
little romantic
moments
to happen
every second
with a 
simple
passionate kiss
that will 
make me wonder
how the rain
even dares
to fall.
For once,
almost everything
is untouchable...
even for me.
