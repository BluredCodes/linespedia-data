---
title: "Assuming"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "assuming"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I quote
my heart
when I speak
from it.
This is
the last time
I will look
for someone
other then you.
I am better.
I am here.
I exist. 
Can you please
just hold me,
until it is time
to go?
And even then,
I just want
to stay here
and rely
on my 
assumptions
that you
love me.
