They say
I have this
obsession...
I don't think I do.
I can't help the fact
that I have 
no life,
and driving
by your house
on those lonely 
weekends
is the only thing
better to do.
I am so lonely.
I'm so sorry,
but I hurt.
I wish I could 
wake up
one day,
and everyone 
would turn to me,
and stare me in the face
and scream
"I love You".
Why can't it be like that?
Why do they tell me
I need to grow up
instead?
Can't they see
I am begging for
sympathy.
Cant any one tell
I am sitting on the rocky
edge.
All I want is you.
You don't want me...
unfortunatly...
Maybe if I wasn't 
so lonely,
I wouldnt have 
drive by your house...
and I wouldnt have 
to cry my eyes out
on the way home.
I am so sorry...
I don't mean
for anyone to 
call it 
obsession...
