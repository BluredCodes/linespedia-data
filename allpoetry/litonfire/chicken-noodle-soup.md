I feel so sad.
My heart is sick.

Your never here.
I wish
you were.

I feel so bad.
My tears are sick.

Your always
uncertain.
I can't take this
anymore.

I feel so weak.
My body is sick.

I need you here.
I wish
you were.

My mind is gone.
My pain is sick.

Please come back...
I hate seconds
spent 
with out you.

Your always 
uncertain.

I want you..
not this
chicken noodle soup.I know the ending is childish...but I think its a great ending in my book.  I hope you see why I chose to end it this way, and enjoy it just as much as I do!
