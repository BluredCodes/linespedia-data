title: "In love and Out"

I heard that song,
it reminded me of you.
Someone talked about 
their boyfriend,
it reminded me of you.
I am lonely (like always)
it reminds me of you.
I saw that girl
you fucked before,
it reminds me of you.
I see blazers all the time,
it reminds me of you.
I heard that song...
it reminded me that 
I love you,
but I am not IN
love
with you.
