writer: "LitOnFire"

If I could
                         hide away
                        Make this
                          Pain
                        Go Away
                        If I could
                          Be Alone
                        And feel the
                           Hate
                        that you have
                          Grown
                         Then maybe
                         I could say
                           I dont care
                        that much
                           anyway.
