---
title: "Sick And Tired"
writer: "LitOnFire"

Aren't you sick
of being you?
Aren't you tired
of your nonsense?
Aren't you upset
you make me put up with it?
Don't you have
something better to do
with your time?
Instead of wasting mine?
How much would you pay, 
to see me again?
I think I am way to devoted
to a useless
friend.
You treat me
with no respect
and I'm sick of your game.
You never let me in
when it's pouring rain
I don't think you ever cared 
about anything that we've ever
shared.
Im tired of being broken in two,
but aren't you atleast sick 
of being you?I really really like this one...hopefully you will agree!
