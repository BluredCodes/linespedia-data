---
title: "Doubt"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "doubt"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't 
think
I ever
doubted you once-
not even
when you would 
look away
because 
you didn't like
what you were 
hearing.
Still,
I never doubted you...
when you
told me
how much
you hated me,
and you 
never wanted
to see
my face again.
I went away...
like you wanted.
But still
I never 
doubted
the hours
we would just
talk
and smile
back at
each other,
as I sat there,
amazed at
how the way
your mouth moved
when you told me
you were leaving.
I suppose
I did
the right thing
by letting you go.
But still,
I never doubted
that you actually
would.
