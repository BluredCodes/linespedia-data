Yep..   I'm ahhhhh...sitting here...  once again....       getting a little closer           to the         weekend.  You ahhhhh..still haven't bothered   to call me      (how surprising)...          (can you see the surprise on my face?)This is    just ridiculous...I wonder if you realize it (too)??    I'm ahhhh... pretty hurt.....just in case you were wondering... And ahhhhh...   NO- I didn't fall of the edge of the earth...just in case you were wondering that (too)???    It's ahhhhactually   getting kinda funny.Can't you see....what a joke you are?   I refuseto sleep with you next time!        I mean it this time!!!      But ahhhhh...yeah......      right.......I think this is funny....haha...the sad thing is that it is true...I really am dealing with an asshole....
