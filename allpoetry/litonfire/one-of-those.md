title: "One Of Those"
writer: "LitOnFire"

We 
were having
one of those
ya know-
one of those
conversations
where
we
were driving
in the car
and
we
would be
having
one of those
ya know-
one of those
fights
where
we would be
fighting
one minute
and crying
the next.
And then
your hand
would end up
down
my pants.
I regret
all the times
we made love
in warmth
in 
one of those
ya know-
one of those
moments
where day 
and night
stop for you
because you
are my 
breath
and 
hatred
all at once.
I cant breath
as we have 
another 
one of those
ya know-
one of those 
fights...
This time 
you surprised me
when you
slammed on the 
breaks
after tears
were staining 
my face.
After you asked me
what I wanted-
and I said
that all I ever
wanted
was you-
you grabbed me
and kissed me
with out a doubt
I knew-
we
had
one of those
ya know-
one of those 
"I love you too's"I know the repition is alot, but I think its what makes this one!
