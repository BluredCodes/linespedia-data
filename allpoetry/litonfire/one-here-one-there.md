---
title: "One here, One there"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "one-here-one-there"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your so there-
And I'm so not.
Your so here-
And I'm so not.
I want to love you-
your here,
and he's not.
I want to love him-
he's there,
and your not.
Your so close,
he's so far.
But your both
too close
to this 
single heart.
I'm so torn-
I love two-
I'm in love with him,
but then,
I'm in love with you
