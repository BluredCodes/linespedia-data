title: "Counting..."
writer: "LitOnFire"

First I miss your smile
Second I miss your hugs
Third I miss your lips
Fourth I miss your love
Fifth I miss your eyes, staring into mine.
Sixth I miss your kisses, they were so divine.
Seventh I miss your arms, completely surrounding me...
making you the only one, the only one I can see.
Eighth I miss my love
Ninth I miss his laugh
But tenth and most of all,
I miss my heart
the last.
