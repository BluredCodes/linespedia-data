title: "Color"
writer: "LitOnFire"

The sky is always blue
in my heart.
The sun just goes down
while the moon comes out.
Shades of darkness
and shades of light.
Please help me dear stars
get through this night...
It's odd in a way
the sky is always blue,
it hurts to live a day
without you.
Maybe that's the reason why
the color of night
is just a lie.
