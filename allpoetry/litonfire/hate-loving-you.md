I hateloving you.It makes meclose my eyes tight,and shakethose memories away.I hatewanting to kiss you.I used to love the wayyour lipsfelt so sweetpressed upagainst mine.InsidemyselfI used to be ableto see you.I hate loving you.I hate thatwe aren't meant to beyou want to many othersbesides me.It makes me so sadthat you won't take my hand.I feel so much pain,I'll never be the sameafter loving you.then again,I hate loving you.
