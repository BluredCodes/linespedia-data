I am lefthearing words I never thought I'd hear.My eyes are so blurred.I cannot see the floor, as I am doubled over.My body feels numbness,my heart, so weak.So many questions enter my head.Why???...(for a start)...I had faith as a single grain of mustard seed.The phone is loosly pressed against my ear;too many tears come between.I tried to avoid those words.I know now there was no escaping them.Now I can feel those words every day--I am thinking about those words every minute.I still ask myself ...why???But that night stays locked in my head,and you hold the key.Now my only question is how I am goingto setmyself free.This poem has an 'inside' meaning...referring to the mustard seed thing...Its this necklace I have that has an amulet written on the back.  This isn't a great poem, but it holds alot of meaning to me.  thanx
