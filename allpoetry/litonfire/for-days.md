Surelyyou knowhow i thinkaway...for daysI wont come outwhatever wayyou want to see them.perhapsunderstandingis hard to come by...for daysI sitand rotatecontemplatingabout thingsthat surelyyou alreadyknowyou musttake mefor granted.Times willneverwalk away.Treat themlike meand I wontcome out...for daystimes changebut surelyyou alreadyknew that.....for days...I close my eyeswith figmentsof times when you achedto be here...for days...
