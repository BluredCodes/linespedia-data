---
title: "Weak"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "weak"
source: "AllPoetry"

Stretched
over my
aching body
are your
firm hands
that always 
reminds me,
that not
everyone 
is weak.
When you
look at me,
how do I
appear?
I know
I don't have
that shining
blonde hair
or that 
beautiful
voluptuous 
body.
But I still
ache.
Do I
not deserve
those
firm hands,
that remind me
that not 
everyone
is weak?
When I speak,
you always
seem
to listen
to my 
irrelevance
as if
my voice
was the
only sound
making you
wake
in the morning.
I know
I'm no cup 
of tea,
with that side
of honey.
But your
my firm hands,
stretched
over my
body,
always
reminding me
that not 
everyone
