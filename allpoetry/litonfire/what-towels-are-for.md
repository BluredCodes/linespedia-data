I know you knowmeinsideand out.There is nodoubtin my mindthatI shouldeventuallydecideon where I wantto remain.Perhaps the rain,even thoughI knowyou knowI mightgeta littlewet.But I guessthat's whattowelsare for.Like beforeand thatother timeand thetimebefore that,when I watchedthe skyand the cloudscontract;spitting outlittle sprinklesthat get'smy hairall drizzly...but I guessthat's whattowelsare for....you alwaystold meto go get one-And I  wouldn't evenhave to thinkaboutthe facti knowyou know meinsideandout,but I guessthat'swhat towelsare for.Every time I read this I find it to be a breath of fresh air, I hope you enjoy it just the same!
