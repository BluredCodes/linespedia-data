Never forgottenare the wordsI have yetto say.Always in time,the pain goes away.Sometimesthe softness of the days go by,and there's so many 'hellos'in just one of my'goodbye's'.I'm out of roomto let youtake anythings place.Whenever I needed a tearto fall,I would onlyhave to rememberyour face.All I want is to closemy eyes,yet I only failafter so many tries.Looking backon memories you left;wonderingwhat I'm supposed to donext.It's so hardwhen you haveto let go,it's just so hard when you knowthe thingsI know.
