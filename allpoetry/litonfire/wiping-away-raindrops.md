I watchedyour handglide acrossmy window-smearing allthe raindropsas I watchedyour face-even thoughyou told meto put ona happier one.I suppose I could,if I tried.But then again,I would ratherwatch youtry and wipeawayall the raindropson my window-each one makingme feela little better.A little biggerof a smile growson my face,even thoughyou told meto put ona happier one.I suppose I could,if I tried.
