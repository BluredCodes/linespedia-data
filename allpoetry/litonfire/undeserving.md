It's all the samewhen it comes right down to it.Just this time aroundI know your going to break all your promisesbefore you even make them.Do you care- at allthat I think you are good for nothing?Your not even good for hating,because even that takes up too much time,when you didn't spend any on me...at all.I know your not even the least bit guilty.Even after you bother to say you are goingto do somethingwhen you know you never will.I suppose in your mind you think you are,and in that sense your not a complete asshole.I forgot how it feels to love you,because honestly you make it so hard for anyone to care.You shouldn't say things you don't mean.But it's okay.I've gotten to the point where I know to disregaurdeverything that you say your going to do.Your just so undeserving when it comesright down to it.
