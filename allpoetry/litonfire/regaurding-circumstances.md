Don't you know-
I'll love you anyway?
Maybe I don't ever
want to see you again...
but I will love you anway.
Maybe I don't ever
want to hear your voice again-
I'll love you anyway.
I know that I have had it
up to here...
but I also know
that I will always love you
anyway.
You make me depressed as fuck
but I'll love you anyway.
I won't bother calling you anymore-
but I'll still love you anyway.
Maybe I won't get to kiss your skin
ever again-
but still, I'll love you anyway.
Hopefully those 20 other girls calling you
on your cell phone can love you more-
but I will still love you anyway.
I must say,
I will miss the thing that always had me coming back to you,
and still, I will love you anyway.
Maybe I won't make those long drives anymore,
but I will love you anyway.
I might be moving a thousand miles away,
and maybe I won't ever see the look in your eyes again,
but of course, I will love you anyway.
You tell me I'm beautiful, obviously not beautiful enough,
even still, I will love you anyway.
Maybe I didn't make myself clear,
eventhough you fucked around with my enough.
Now I honestly just don't care, 
as I can blow the thought of you off.
Maybe your not around,
then again,
you never really were.
And there's really nothing left to say
besides "don't you know...I'll love you anyway"?
