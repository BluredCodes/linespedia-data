title: "Love Affair"
writer: "LitOnFire"

I onlythinkof youas my love affair.Your inmy heartbut never there.This burningpassionexistson my mind.I'm finally seeing straightwhen youmake me blind.I wantyour skin.I want to hold you closeI'm thinkingof what happened this morning...I love youthe most.You finallyunderstoodwhen you grabbedmy faceand kissedmy lipsI'm emptyinside,with coldfingertips.You sayyou care,but I reallydon't thinkI do.I really only thinkof youas mytruelove affair.
