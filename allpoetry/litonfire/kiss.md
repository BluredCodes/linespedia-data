TonightI promiseI will trynot to cry.No illusionwill makeme seethat happydaywhen wewalked inthe orchardhand in hand,heart to heart.The sun beat downon yourskin.I promise Iwill not frownwhen I thinkof the timeyou broughtme flowersthat smelledso sweet.I rememberI kept themby mybeside andthey soothedme to sleep.I keeprememberinghow yousmelled.Sad reminderstell methose days are long gone.Those daysare likesinging alongto a heart brokenlove song.No matterhow long I sing,or if I rememberthose days I miss.I will alwayssay goodnightto youfollowed by myunacknowledged kiss.I wrote this a couple years ago, but its one of my favorites..I once entered it in a poetry contest and it won!
