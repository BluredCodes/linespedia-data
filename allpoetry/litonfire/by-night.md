---
title: "By Night"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "by-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Let's slip into something
more like skin,
only the bare essentials are needed
to get things a little more heated.
As I run my fingers through your hair,
more then just secrets we long to share.
Soon we bring things more up to speed,
on top you go...you take the lead.
As you give me everything I could ever want,
because there's no hiding behind something your not.
My lips aren't the only things you caress.
My body tingles, I must confess.
Our bodies glisten with moistened sweat.
As morning breaks I will only regret
having to let you go away.
Tomorrow I vow to make you stay.
You run your fingers down my back, 
and whisper sweet nothings in my ear.
You penatrate a little deeper inside,
as I would never be anywhere but here.
I am yours, and you are mine.
Nothing stands, but only time.
Tomorrow brings a brand new sun-
by night we make our bodies one.
