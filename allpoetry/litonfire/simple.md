Wanting to feelwhat I want,Always shooting forthe stars.Trying to choosebetween two,like walkingdifferent paths;always uncertain,always afraidI will be lostin the end.with emptiness,that cold,painfully stare;not knowingwhat I want.why do Ihave to pick?would alone be better?can't this painsubside,by the time i decide,it just might.so hard,the days so long.i want youso bad though.but then I wanthim.so hard,the daysso long.I guess I amjust runningaway,its easier on methat way.I must go backand take my time.I need to talk to you;Really talk.I need to know.I want lovein simplist form.a simple hand.a simple heart.a simple love.a simple you.why do i have to be me?why do I have to choose?
