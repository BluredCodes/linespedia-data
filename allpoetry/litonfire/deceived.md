I would bekiddingmyselfif Ireallythoughtyour eyeswere aleaf ina puddlewhirling,swirlingaround.Not necessarilywanting tobe noticedbut twirlsevery autumnwhen the coldreminds meof when you left.I would bedeceivingmyself ifI criedwithfake tearsmade ofglassthat shatteron mycheeks.A milliondifferentpieces topick up,impossible toput backtogether.I would befooling myselfif I pretendedI am notempty inside.I can't seeyour eyes,my tears are obligated,and no leavesfall inautumn.
