---
title: "I'm me, but what about you?"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "im-me-but-what-about-you"

I likebeing UN original.I love to sing out loud...even though I'm awful.I like taking long hot showershoping my troubleswill wash away.I love being a little overweight.I guess it makes me; me.I wish my hair was longer,but it's not,so I guess I will let it grow.I love to embarrass youin the middle of largepublic places.I love my alone time,eventhough I wouldnt mindspending all my timewith you.I like to eat comfort food,and I like listening to songs that remind meof you.But most of allI would love to feel useful,eventhough I knowI'm really not.I love to paint,and hang all my pictures on my wall.I love pictures that I look great in!I like when you call!I love being me,I would hate being you.But then again,how great would it feelto likebeing you?I was bored, so I just typed in whatever came to mind.  I like it, but then again I don't...so go easy on me with this one!
