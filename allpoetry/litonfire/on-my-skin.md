---
title: "...On My Skin"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "on-my-skin"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23861-...On-My-Skin-by-LitOnFire"
---

I
MAY NEVER
HAVE YOU
AGAIN-
BUT I
WILL 
ALWAYS HAVE
THE KISSES
YOU LEFT
ON MY
SKIN-
I HATE
THAT I
HAVE
TO FEEL
THIS PAIN
AS I
REMEMBER
THE DAY
WE RAN 
IN THE
RAIN-
AND I
MAY NEVER
SEE YOU
AGAIN-
AS LONG
AS I HAD
THE CHANCE
TO LOVE YOU
IN THIS 
LIFE,
OF COURSE-
I WILL
ALWAYS
HAVE YOUR
KISSES
LEFT ON
MY SKIN
I HATE
THAT I
MAY NEVER
HOLD YOU
AGAIN-
BUT TIME
HAS FINALLY
TAKEN OVER
MY FRIEND-
PLEASE-
DON'T CRY-
NOT THIS TIME-
FOR ME???
YOU COULD
HAVE HAD
ME ALL 
ALONG-
INSTEAD,
YOU ALWAYS
LET ME BE.
AND I
MAY NEVER
HEAR YOUR
VOICE-
YOU MADE 
IT SEEM
AS IF I HAD
A CHOICE.
YOU AGREE
WE ARE
FROM
DIFFERENT
WORLD'S...
AGAIN,
ATLEAST
I WILL
ALWAYS
HAVE
YOUR KISSES
ON MY SKIN.
ALL THE
MEMORIES,
MOST OF THEM
GOOD-
MOST OF THEM
BAD-
I MAY NEVER
KISS YOU 
AGAIN-
WHY NOW,
DO YOU SEEM
SO SAD???
I HATE
THAT I ALWAYS
IGNORED
THE TRUTH
ATLEAST
I ALWAYS
HAD THOSE
KISSES
ON MY SKIN
FROM YOU-
UNFORTUNATLY,
I MAY NEVER
TOUCH YOU
AGAIN-
BUT I KNOW
YOU'LL ALWAYS
HAVE MY
KISSES
LEFT ON YOUR
SKIN.
RIGHT NOW,
MY TEARS
ARE STARTING
TO FALL-
AS I CAN'T 
REMEMBER 
YOUR SMILE-
AT ALL...
AND I
MAY NEVER
HAVE YOU 
AGAIN-
BUT I
WILL ALWAYS HAVE
THE KISSES
YOU LEFT
ON MY SKIN
