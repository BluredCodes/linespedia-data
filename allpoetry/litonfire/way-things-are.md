MADNESS   in this world...       The Way Things AreThe unequal way       things work...PEOPLE speaking   senseless soliloquies...        To Themselves,  But only A FEW     (including myself...)Listen in.     What Is The Point?             Money, Heart, Sweat,                Tears, Blood                  EFFORT?      For What?        To love...To bleed...to die???I put my head down.
