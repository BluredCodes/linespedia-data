---
title: "My Skin Is Waterproof"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "my-skin-is-waterproof"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22206-My-Skin-Is-Waterproof-by-LitOnFire"
---

My skin
is waterproof
like you
I will never
be
the same
again
those same
questions
run through 
my mind
all the time
I always
reach
the dead ends
when
I am through
with my thoughts
of you
I feel lucky
to be
alive
My skin
is waterproof
in some 
ways
like days
when I tell
myself you
will never
change.
You left me
so forgotten.
I too-
have forgotten
you.
My skin
is waterproof
can't you tell?
Often enough,
almost most
of the time,
I'd get that
recycled
desperation
inside
and the anger
would rise
to the 
point where
I would fall
and I'd 
realize
I wasn't 
holding on
to anything
at all.
And the only
thing that
would die
is the lies
that you told.
I no longer
can love you
cuz your shit
got old.
Sometimes
the lines
can't begin
to explain.
I wonder
if you think
of me
when it
begins
to rain.
It's alright,
I understand-
I'm nothing to you.
Anyway,
it's okay,
it doesn't matter-
my skin
is waterproof.
