---
title: "You Always Walk By"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "you-always-walk-by"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9410-You-Always-Walk-By-by-LitOnFire"
---

I know 
you see
me
walk past 
you.
You pretend
as if
I am just
a specter...
you walk by 
me
as if
I am 
see through.
Just maybe 
I am-
to you.
I really wish
you could
acknowledge
me.
I know 
you can
see
me
walk right past
you.
I always wonder
what your 
thinking...
I wonder if 
your 
noticing
the girl
you used to love
walk right by,
and you dont 
even stop
to say 'hi'.
Have you really
deleted me
out of your life
completely...
as if
I dont 
exist?
Was I that small
of a measurment
for you to walk by
me
and not be
hesitant?
I must have been...
for you keep
walking by me
again
and 
again.
And I'm always
wondering why
you always
just walk by...
with your straight
face,
and your one way
eyes...
not even caring
when I
walk by...Why do you always walk by me and pretend you don't even notice me...???
