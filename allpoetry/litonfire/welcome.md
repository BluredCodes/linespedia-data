Welcome to lonely
How does it feel?
There's no place to smile,
No heart can heal...

Welcome to Alone,
Isn't it what you wanted to be?
You thought you would be happier;
and better off without me...

Welcome to tears,
I bet you never thought they would fall...
You thought you would be better off,
with no one to love at all...

Welcome to the cold,
you thought you had a choice.
But now the only comfort you want
is to only hear my voice...

Welcome to lonely,
Welcome to alone.
Welcome to tears,
Welcome to the cold.
Welcome to memories...
trying to remember the good over the bad.
I bet you never thought
you would end up feeling
this sad...
