---
title: "Goodbye"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I want
to say
goodbye
today
I 
no longer
feel love
in your
touch.
I know 
it's going to be
difficult
but I want
to say
goodbye
so much.
This day
has come,
and soon
will go,
your sad face
I have come
to know.
And I'm sorry
but it
would be
a lie
if I
didn't say
I want to say
goodbye.
