When will these walls break?I already have a shattered heartwhat'sthe difference?I thought you loved me...things must have changedcompletely without me, even realizing it.So sudden, (I think)you left me standing in the coldwith only the memoryof how you always kept me warm.You have been goneSo long (now)...what's the difference?I already have a shattered heartWhen will these walls break?
