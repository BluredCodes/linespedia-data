Silently screaming
from all the noise.
Can't you hear
the frustration
in my voice?
Can't you see
the pain in my eyes?
after I figured out
all your hurtful lies?
You stole my right
to eat and sleep
I no longer want to keep
going on,
in, out,
inside
my mind
I can no longer
abide
with out your
shallow hands
at my side.
