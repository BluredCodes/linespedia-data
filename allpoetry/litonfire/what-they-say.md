I guess
they say
not to worry
when things
don't go your way.
But how is that possible?
How can I not worry;
when it comes 
down to you,
there's nothing else 
to do
then worry.
It seems like I am always
going against 
what they say.
I'm not even trying
to stray,
from old words 
of wisdom.
Never the less,
I worry...
about you...
I know I shouldnt.
Maybe if I went to sleep,
and dreamt,
there would be a chance
that you won't be in them.
Then again, I would worry
that your somewhere else.
I know they say not to worry
when things don't go 
your way.
But I guess,
when it comes
down to you...
I have to go against
what they say.This isn't exactly a great poem...but...it suits its purpose..for me anyway.
