I told you to call mewhenever you wanted...I asked you to cry on my shoulder,when you needed a shoulder to cry on...I made you listen to my voicewhen you were stressed outfrom the day that didn't go your way....I begged you to look into my eyes to make you seeeverything was going to be-alright...I pleaded to hold you, to make you warm.I touched your face, and sang you a song...I always waited for you to come around.You were my sky, and Iwas your ground...I longed to always touch your skinyou always made me want for tomorrow to begin...I never looked at you as if you failed...I was your kiss before you sailed...I was your wish, and you were my dream...I could love no other,I thought it seemed...Maybe I gave too much of my all....Maybe I thought I was worth the call...
