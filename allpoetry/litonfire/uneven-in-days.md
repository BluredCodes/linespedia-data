I'm unevenin daysI'm lostto the worldI'm forgotten amongstfeelingsI'm freezingin coldI'm cryingin painI'm unevenin daysI'm floatingin waterI'm beggingfor you to stopI'm searchingfor answersfrom a victim-lesswordI'm doubtingyou careI'm walkingfor waysI'm uneven in daysI'm hopelessto youI'm uncomfortedby youI send you a motionless kiss-if you love methen tell me this-why must one personpaywhen you make themso unevenin day?
