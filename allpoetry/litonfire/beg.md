title: "Beg"
writer: "LitOnFire"

It was so sad, as I turned around
and walked away;
tears staining my numb face.
You watched me go...I remember.
You didn't do anything to stop me.
I guess it was okay,
considering I hated everything you did,
and you hated how I actually cared.
You told me you wanted to be together....
we were always everything but that.
We were always like a little rain shower
that only lasted long enough to water the garden
and make the birds come out and sing afterwards.
I dont think you cared as you watched me walk away,
seeing my trail of tears behind me.
You didnt care that I could barely hold myself up straight.
I wanted so badly to just fall to the ground,
and beg.
