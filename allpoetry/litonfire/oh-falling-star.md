Oh falling star
Thou wont save me from eternal
darkness?
This cold night.
I rely only on your bright
shine; to warm my body.
Only through your gleam,
can I redeem
myself.
This time I take my spirits higher.
Oh falling star
help me be a survivor.
Not all is well.
My insides want to explode.
These lips are to weak to tell;
my trust is what you sold.
Oh falling star
Thou won't save me from night?
You make your way into
the sadness
only to leave without a sight.
Oh falling star-
I want you.
