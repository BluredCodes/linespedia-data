You turned out to be a sell out-yeah- who ever knew?The only thing I ever wanted was to wake up next to you.But now those days are gone,and you turned your back on me.You ripped my heart out and let me die,yeah- you left me there to bleed.And even though you told me -yeah-to leave you the fuck alone...no, I couldn't make it on my own...I wouldn't let you go.So that makes me a psycho.Yeah- you call me the chick thats a raving lunaticyeah- you call me a psycho.The only thing I didn't want,I didn't want to let you go...But now the only thing you say is I'm a fucking psycho.Yeah- you call me a chick that's a raving lunatic-yeah- you call me a psycho.I guess I'm gonna sink that low,cause your the only thing I know,so now you say I'm a psycho.Yeah- I guess I'm the chick that's a raving lunatic-so call me a fucking psycho!!!this does say psycho alot...but I really like it!
