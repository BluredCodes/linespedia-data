---
title: "Afterwards"
writer: "LitOnFire"

Tell me, after it's over
that you really
didn't mean
to hurt me that bad.
I know you didn't really
want to see me
that sad.
But I can understand
that you were just too mad
to let me get away with things
that were driving you up a wall.
You were sick of being
at someone's beckon call.
Tell me, after it's over
about the aftermath
that took your heart down
a different path.
You could no longer bear
to look into such painful eyes anymore.
You had no more love to live for.
You knew in your heart you could never be my friend.
I can understand.
You didn't expect it to all go down
like that in the end.
But somehow I know I will always be special,
and you will sometimes remotely care.
So tell me, after it's over,
so I don't open my eyes
and expect you
standing
there.
