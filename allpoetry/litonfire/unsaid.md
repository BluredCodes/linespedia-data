---
title: "Unsaid"
writer: "LitOnFire"

I wish I could 
change all those hurtful 
things I said.  I wish I
could speak the words unsaid.
I wish you
weren't thing only thing
I was losing,
yet there's no choice left
but I'm slowly choosing.
I wish I could wake you,
then fall back to sleep by your side.
I wish I never made you say goodbye.
But you were forced
because of all those hurtful things
I said.
I think some things
are better left
unsaid.
