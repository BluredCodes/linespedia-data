It's as ifyou didn't treat melike shitand the painyou causeddidn't feellike a hitto the headto the brainyour diving meinsane.Let's take a walkand I'll tell youthe pain.I'll show youexactly whatI feel.Because you thinkI'm no big deal.You think you can blow me offanytime,well I've got something elsein mind.This time aroundwe'll see who's boss,As I show you exactlywhat you've lost.I'm gonna flipyou off-yeah I've got a new man.Yeah were walking hand in hand.I don't think you really knowI don't think I barely showthe hatred I feel.I really thoughtwe had something real.I was mistakenI was a foolI was the fireyou were the fuel.I'm accustomedI'm aquainted.For days on endfor days I waited.It's as ifyou weren't my living hell.You made me half dead,but I've lived to tellthat I wish you didn't treat me like this...It's as ifyou didnt treat melike shit.
