I honestlydon't knowwhat's bestanymore.You tell meyou lovemeand I ask'what for'?I honestlydon't knowwhy I feellike crying.You make medeadwhenI'm alreadydieing.This time,maybeI will gainsome sense..in this nightmareI live,I'm nothingto youbut tense.I haveto find a wayoutof you...you tell meyou love me...I ask'what for'?I'm soboughtand payed for.I needto get outof you...I'm livinga nightmare...you tell meyou love me...and I ask'what for'?ahhhhhh...blah blah blah.....I am so sick of your shit....I really am......
