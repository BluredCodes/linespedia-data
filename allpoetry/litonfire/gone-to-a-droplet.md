---
title: "Gone to a Droplet"
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "gone-to-a-droplet"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4838-Gone-to-a-Droplet-by-LitOnFire"
---

Drip by drip
words are chipping 
away
at my peace
of mind.
I want to drink
the water,
your tears
are just
the same.
My face must seem
so plain.
You have to 
remind yourself
of my name.
No negotiating,
relating,
impossible.
Let me
take care
of you.
Let me cry
in your
restless arms.
Waiting takes
to long.
Impatience is my
moral,
as you wipe
the hate off
your hands.
Tears are 
useless,
so are smiles
that only last
in a forgotten
memory,
left untouched,
left to die.
Why can't tomorrow
be like
the day
we stood 
in the rain,
and it soaked
our skin,
but we didn't care.
It glistened
as droplets
formed
at the ends 
of you hair,
and ran down
your face.
Then like a finger 
running across 
your lips.
You were all mine,
and 
Drip 
   by 
Drip
I lost you.This is one of my favorites that I have ever written.
