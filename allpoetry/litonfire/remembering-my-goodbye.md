Do you remember
that time
I watched you
drive by,
when you told me stay
right there?
Any can you remember that day
when you looked in my face
and told me
how much you cared?
Can you remember that
instance when you told me 
to stop by,
but by the time I got there 
you were gone?
And do you recall that time 
you told me that 
you wish you never
met me at all?
Do you remember telling me
how much you thought about me,
during the course of your days?
But then again, what about the time 
you said I was'nt even worth the chase?
Better yet 
what about when we were having sex
and you told me that you just wanted
to go slow.
But when morning came your other girlfriend called
and you thought it would be best if I go.
You asked me not to give you anymore problems
as long as you could keep screwing me on the side
But I've wised up alot you see
and now all I have to say is "fuck you--goodbye"!
