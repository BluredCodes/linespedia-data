---
title: "Pieces"
writer: "LitOnFire"

Together
in pieces
left to break
with a simple
touch
with a little
shake.
To fragile
to pick up
or put down
To rough 
the pieces 
fall to the 
ground.
Held together
with a little
glue 
are the only
pieces
I have left 
of you.
