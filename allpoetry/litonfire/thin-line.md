This thin line I walk in darkness,This thin line I walk alone. My mind is torn between two sides,my heart is left so cold.This thin line dividesmy heart and mind,I am no longer able to see.I could no sooner chose a side,then chose in what to believe.I am stuck on the line I walk for you,with no other place to go.I walk along with my look of solitude;the only look I have come to know.I knew this day had to come,and maybe it's not too late.Yet I walk on and on in the darknessOn this thin line between         Love...             and                 Hate...I wrote this one like a year or two ago...
