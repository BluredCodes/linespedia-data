title: "Tomorrow's Rain- Yesterday's Pain"

I put my finger
to my lips
as I think
of that time
you told me
you never laid
naked
with someone 
before.
I knew you loved
me then,
I know you love
me still...
Thinking of times
likes those
comforts my soul
as I know
the whole world 
stopped
for us
at that moment.
When you are not here,
I think of moments like that...
and you appear,
like tomorrow's rain
and yesterday's pain...
