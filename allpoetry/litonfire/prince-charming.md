Trying To FindMy MindAgainWhere I LostIt AlongThe RoadI Don't Know.Trying To FeelMy Heart AgainBut All I FeelIs Alone.When Will ThatPrince Come,And Sweep Me OffMy Feet?What Is His NameAnd What DoesHe Look Like?When In Time Will We Meet?Trying LeadsTo Nowhere,And I'm GettingNowhere Fast.But When InTimeWill I Finally MeetMy Prince CharmingAt Last?
