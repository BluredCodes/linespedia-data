---
title: "...From One Minute...To The Next..."
writer: "LitOnFire"
slug: "from-one-minuteto-the-next"

My life
is what 
you freely
walk
in and out
of.
Your always
eternally
there
one minute,
and then
gone the
next.
My life 
is what
you awkwardly
walk in
and out 
of.
I'm always
left
used
and confused,
but then 
you say
"honestly,
I love you".
My life
is what
you sadly
walk in and out
of.
I want 
to hold you
so badly...
too bad
your always
here 
one minute,
and then gone
the next.
...You never
give me a chance.
My life
is what
you painfully
walk in and out
of.
It would be
so easy
on me,
if you would
either
stay 
for good,
or leave
for
forever.
...Don't 
just tell me
you want
me-
from one minute
to the
next.
My life
is what
screams
for you
in it-
so why do you
freely
walk in and out;
from one minute
to the next?
