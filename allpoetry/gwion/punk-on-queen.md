---
title: "Punk on Queen"
writer: "Gwion"
slug: "punk-on-queen"
source: "AllPoetry"

the brutal wind
whips icy knives
at the back of your skull
shaved bare to the elements

so fucking cold
it's not crisp, it's bitter
so that the metal freezes
cold sterility
into your face

and the man on the back of the bus
reminds you:

This is a fatal season
