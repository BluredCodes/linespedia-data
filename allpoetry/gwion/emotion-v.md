Fear
Clouds sense
Shrouds reason
Eats hope

Fear hollows us
Makes tombs of hearts
Burns fields and blocks rivers

Fear
Drives horses over cliffs
Warriors into battles

Spreads seeds of hatred
And regret

Makes martyrs
Of Lovers.
