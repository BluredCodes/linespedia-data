I awoke
To a light like a sunset in the East
And felt Time reverse itself
As the sky burned crimson
Reflected blood puddles
Turned ravens raw pink

Dripped embers from clouds

A tunnel of rusty trees
Shrinking and sweeping me backwards
Each faltering step forward I take

Roses bloomed late that year
Even when the leaves crunched

I didn't even notice
The ticking had stopped
