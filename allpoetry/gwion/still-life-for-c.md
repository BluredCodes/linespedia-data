title: "Still-Life (for C)"
writer: "Gwion"

My tongue
Film
Made art
Exposures on every inch of
Quivering skin
Touch light
Reflecting gels
You radiate blue....no, red



Hesitant flashbulb
Trembing fingers



You hold too much beauty for this lens
Eyes defining black and white borders
Skin meeting air
Goosebumps


I captured a shiver





I saw


No,


I was

A thousand glass fragments pulled out of my face
Explosions of you 
Still-life memories
Of hands





Later:

Darkness
Fluid
Your eyes floating underwater
Fading slowly into view
Trapping a smile forever
