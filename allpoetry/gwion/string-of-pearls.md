---
title: "String of Pearls"
writer: "Gwion"

i ate a string of pearls
tentative words lingering
on frail threads of veins,
each one glistening,
a drop of sweat, 
a bead of juice lapped from your thighs,
balancing on my tongue
resting before rolling
down the abyss of my throat.
i ate jewels off your body,
each gem a morsel of taste,salt, tang 
syllable of male flesh
i gathered coins that were shadows
resting in hollows of skin,
joints, fingers,
soft valleys of muscle.
i reaped rich rewards of silver 
in fields of hair,
breathed ambergris and myrrh
rising heated, moist.
i dreamed lush fabrics, silks
and licked every inch of parchment
between your crown and toes.
i filled my quill with ink from your eyes,
scribed poetry upon your naked body
and drank my own words,
lapped pearls from your flesh.
