title: "Conception (for V)"
writer: "Gwion"

In flashing glimpses of strobes
I saw an idea
Grasped it
Touched thought
And a thousand sleepless nights
Of inspiration
In throbbing beats
I danced to my imagination
Poured song onto latex
And fused fiction
Into blinding art

I knew emptiness between ideas
And feared failure
Was scared to shed light
On this new emotion
But instead grasped a hollow throb
In my heart
And filled it with hope

I saw Love
As a conceivable image
And drew forth power
To read my own words
On his face

In the silence between notes,
I fell in love with a dream
A concept I invented in a hundred poems
A thousand photographs
And billions of hours lost in fantasy
And saw him take his first breath
As reality took it's tender hold
On what I had always hoped forI know, I know, this is total mush and blather, but it's for him. He deserves it :o)
