title: "Mother"
writer: "Gwion"

Aged lines like cracked mirrors
Reflecting beauty.
You are too young to fade;
Only flowers fade
And Age outshines them all.

Mother,
Whose tears reflect
All joys,
All sorrows,
All regrets and redemptions.

You are Age:
Timeless,
Immortal and 
Perfection.
