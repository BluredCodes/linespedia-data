---
title: "M. on a Lonely Evening"
writer: "Gwion"
slug: "m-on-a-lonely-evening"

I am thinking of hard things
Of moistness
Of tongue and bite and kiss
Of soft touch and penetration
Blows and sighs
Hands pulling my hair
Forcing open my mouth
Fingers on thighs
Trickling dampness
Heavy breath
Teeth on knees and wrists
Nails in shoulders
Harsh words
Heat
Gasp
Ecstasy
An explosion between my lips
