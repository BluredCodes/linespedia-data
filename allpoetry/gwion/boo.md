Cat 
is staring at the wall
murmuring at shadows

Cat
makes friends with energy

throbs with freak static
sends slight blue sparks
to touch air

whispers in my sleep
come out and play

chases demons under my bed

runs feline marathons
and marches armies of claws against 
spider enemies
bites fingers that could be hidden dangers
swallows any slight threat

Cat speaks to corners

doesn't know that he's orange
thinks he wears fatigues
thinks lions and wolves
thinks big

*

Cat
is lonely after sunrise
