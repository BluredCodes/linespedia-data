There is light and warmth
And I know it as you, Lady
When that fire burns behind my eyes
And the muse takes my heart
I know your name
And take it as my own

Great fire of inspiration
The hearth of my heart
Where the flames burn eternal
Echoed in fragile words
Your face is written a thousand times over
In every allusion to my own mortality

You are a Bard
And an inferno, if not Inspiration herself
