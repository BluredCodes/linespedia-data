In a fleeting moment
I saw a corpse I knew as my own,
Dead eyes staring back
From a lost time.

I knew my own hands twisting
Like talons 
Around an idea, a shape,
Crushing it
Even as I tried to breathe life into it’s dying form.

I saw that moment die
Even as another  took hold.July 2001,
