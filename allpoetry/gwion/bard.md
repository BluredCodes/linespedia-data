---
title: "Bard"
writer: "Gwion"
slug: "bard"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34683-Bard-by-Gwion"
---

I've seen distance,
Heard voices carried on the wind,
Hands reaching up from the otherworld.
I've known a thousand truths,
Felt poetry to the core of my soul,
Smelled emotion.
Knew that somewhere
There was thought;
I've seen the currents of energy
Between the lobes of your brain.
I've flashed brilliance
And suffered darkness
To birth ideas.
I have been the silent wood,
The boiling sea,
The screaming winds.
I have inspired love
In all 3 realms
Yet never felt it touch my soul;
I have sent kisses to a thousand warriors
Marching off to battle.

I have seen horses fall,
Great people conquered,
Cities crumble
And all the while
I defined history.
