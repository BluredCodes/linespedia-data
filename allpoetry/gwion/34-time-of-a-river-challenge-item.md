Cool bubbling 
A distant orchestra 
Humming early warm-ups 
Glint of sunlight 
On liquid metal 
Rushing 
A bow across strings 
A soloist following the restrictions of Bach 

As droplets leap 
Over dank earth walls 
Quarter-notes slapping parchment with measure 
Eroding land 
Thick roots and dead leaves 
Collecting flotsam 
Bits of other places 
Floating into folds of darkness 

As flutes 
Struggling to keep time with the drums 
Are swept into the aria of liquid violin 
Bound by beat and banks of ink and parchment 

Lingering in an elbow of corroded soil 
Within the arms of a river 
Rich banks lush with the promise of life 
The hope of a thousand years of build-up and decay 
Giving form 
To this flowing scar 
Defining the land 
Binding water 
Voicing a symphony of simple chemistry
