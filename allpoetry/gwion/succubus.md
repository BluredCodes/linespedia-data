---
title: "Succubus"
writer: "Gwion"
slug: "succubus"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am eating your words
Devouring
Every decision you make with your hands
Feeding off every drop
Spilled from your lips

I can touch your heart
Through your eyes
Crush it with simple thought

I am satiated 
Only when I know you can stand no more
