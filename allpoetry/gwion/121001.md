I can't sleep tonight
This voice keeps me from rest
Tells me
To kill insects
To pick scabs
So they will not heal

Some noise
Drowned out by the throbbing
Silence
The humming
Of echoing acrylic 
Skull

Reverberation
An 8th-grade science project
That jammed doors
Stopped thought
Saw

And became shadows
Of reasons
Booming
On the underside of my face
