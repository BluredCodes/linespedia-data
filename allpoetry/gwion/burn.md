title: "Burn"
writer: "Gwion"

Seconds can burn
Can leave tight white marks
And tender aches
Raw flesh

Ina moment of brimstone
I realized
I had become on the outside
What I was within

This arm has and always will
Take the brunt of my pain
And exhibit horrid beauties
Of twisted skin
And hatred

Now
For good
I have been marked
By my battles
