---
title: "Moments (M)"
writer: "Gwion"
slug: "moments-m"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why am I stuck on the thought
That words can be cleared
With a brush of a hand
And the world turned on its axis by a thrust?

A rush
And I’m caught up in the backcurrent.
How quickly things can turn on you
In the exhale of a whisper.
How quickly the universe can crumble
When we lie to ourselves,
When our hearts change their rhythms
And run against the tides

Memories form 
As moments in our minds
And we have no control
On what we choose to retain.
I remember my first leather,
My first song
And that cold crushing glare of lust.

And moments remain
When all else is lost;
A dried rose I kept
And a scrawl on a napkin,
A bus ticket, a photograph,
A smile caught, 
Taken forever out of time.
