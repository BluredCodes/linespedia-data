i did not intend it to be
but you've inspired such emotion in me
i left behind serenity
you had seemed interested in me
did your interest turn and flee?
your heart will not hear my plea
i beg you to talk to me!
will you return my tranquility?

my emotions are laid bare
longing for your stare
or to play with your hair
to be the object of your care
but you will not dare.
    to tell me if you care
damnit!  why won't you play fair?

my jaw clenched tight
i'll summon my might
and ask you tonight
why you cause me such blight
when i expected delight.
such knowledge must be my right!
but i see you in the light
your eyes hold my sight
i no longer care of my plight
