my burden is to see the world thru disenchanted eyes 
this mortal body? 'tis but a tortured soul's disguise! 
my tranquil heart breeds no animosity 
but grants a strange facade of apathy 
truly such lasting depression holds my simple soul's demise... 

but a fiery rage erupts from the calm center of the torment 
as potent fury and fermented emotion flows from the fibers of my soul 
pure release shatters stagnant dejection 
and gloom gives way to sweet exhilaration 

no longer do i care if my body lays broken 
emotional outbursts obliterating all mental control! 
my flesh's suffering reflects my heart's sorrow 
my hatred, my thirst for agony, it unites my very being 
and my broken body consummates the torturous harmony 
and anguish's sweet accord provides such agonizing ecstasy...Well this one wasn't really written even intentially, it just kind of happened one night.  I purposefully left it all lower case and without punctuation at the end of every line in hopes of it giving it that feeling of raw expression.
