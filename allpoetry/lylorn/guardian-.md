he followed always behind
oft' obscured from sight, yet
she always knew he was there

she could sense his presense, yet 
alas! she could not embrace him
not now, perhaps not ever

and so instead he watched over her
and guarded her with even the very fiber of his soul
'twas an unspoken agreement that they had

so in trouble he 
would be there: 
always!

such that any trial his lady faced
and could not overcome alone

she overcame
