---
title: "Vortex"
writer: "Lylorn"
slug: "vortex"

a swirling vortex surrounds me,
as i struggle 'gainst the surging currents,
at time i find mys'elf in the calm center,
and my soul drinks in the serenity...
yet soon i return to the torment.

the vortex's end,
holds my destiny.
my journey began long, long ago...
as i took the hand of one,
who walked this path,
but alas: too late!
her time was short.
and the path we shared,
soon became mine,
evermore...


and alas!
this now lonely path is mine.
but i can not tarry long,
to wait for that hand.
my path calls me: it will not wait.
and so, nor can i...

long now my path has been
entangled within the vortex.
soon i hope,
this vortex will be behind me.
soon i fear,
this vortex will be behind me.
for i do not know what
unskewed reality awaits,
upon the other side...Any comments welcome.
