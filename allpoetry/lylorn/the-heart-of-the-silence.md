---
title: "The Heart of the Silence"
writer: "Lylorn"

unkempt wind,
beneath a starless sky.

rampaging storm,
nature's fury unleashed.

two silent figures,
calm amid abhorent frenzy.

arms outstretched,
summoning the heavens' wrath.

silvered lightning,
nature's potent defiance.

seething thunder,
divuldges an obscured battle.

vanquished storm,
warded by two silent figures.

blessed rain,
symbol of a sacred awe.

brilliant flash,
overwhelming illuminating cataclysm.

utter darkness,
true night's incarnation.

absolute silence,
all sounds void.

two calm figures,
the heart of the silence.dunno how else to describe that night...
