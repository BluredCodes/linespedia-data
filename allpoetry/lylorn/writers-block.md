i stare at a monitor
as a blank screen stares back at me
a thousand poetic versus are created in my brain
but they are only peices of poetry
who’s jagged edges do not fit together
so from the swirling vortex of my thoughts
i finally express my inability to expressjust something i wrote one day to get over a case of writer's block...
