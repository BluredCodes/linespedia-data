Colors can dance in my head
But I can't catch them - instead
I find their living counterpart
Do what I can to rip apart
Their tiny wings, my clumsy hands
I pin it up, and now what stands
But what once flew, their tattered sails
Bloodied on my artist's flailsAppropriate title, I think.
