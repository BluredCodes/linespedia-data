Sensations abound
Sensory detection, reflection
On life
Stimulation, motion
Strumming the cords of creation
Existence, point defined
One thousand points the same
Laws and boundaries
Broken beyond repair
Universes Alternate
Parallax du Persephone
River with Tributaries
Tribute to Human
Mind

Neuron paths twist
Bound of brain
Synapse Slow
Connection is lost
Universes Unfolding No Longer - 
The Fog was always there
Genius Appreciated
So Far as it can go
We'll never understand
