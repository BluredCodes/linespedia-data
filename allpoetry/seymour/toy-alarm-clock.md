Wind up monkey dance
The street pumps 
Blood on wheels
Light flickers on/off
Heels and toes
Heels and toes
One by one the gray suit ties
Slide upon the twisted walk
Bend around the metal bars
And drive away in brand new cars

Pack their things and drive away


It's all on one road anywayIt's a poem.  Left, center - who knows, it's just a poem.  Have at you.
