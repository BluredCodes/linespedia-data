Staring down the road
It's been the same for miles.
Four dark men stare back at me
Sharp and twisted smiles.
Pound the glass that's fogged
Scream until you're coarse.
Pale and drained of blood
And now, with driving force
Suddenly as petals
Wither, flake, and die
The laughing men that stared at us
Fall to their knees and cry
Fade to black and flicker
Vertical control
Unhinge the silver reel
Rewind and let er' rollOh dear.  This is awful, isn't it.  Dear, dear, dear.  I hate that word.
