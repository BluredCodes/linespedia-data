i dont know where to start angel of heaven
i cant sleep because i cant stop thinking about you
every breath i take every heartbeat i beat
every word i say has everything to do with
and how much i love you
a famous poet once said
that most things you can explain through words,
but sometimes you can only express yourself through feelings and love.
well i want to try both way's.
i show it through feelings and love,
but i think words could help.
you are the girl of my dreams.
i told myself when i was young that i would never meet this girl,
because the girl of my dreams is so perfect,
that the one way i could describe her would be an angel.
you are so perfect that the English language can not describe what you are to me.
but, i guess that these words might help
and i know that as soonas you read this your going to say no that's not me,
but baby you are that to me that and much more.
to put it into plain words you mean all the oceans in the world,
the highest mountains.
all the tropical paradise everywhere,
and all the beautiful sunset's at dolphin head.
your the prettiest thing on two legs to ever walk the earth, even the biblical eve doesnt have anything on you.
you are a gift from God,
just to make one guys dreams come true.
even when i say the stupidest stuff you still seem to like me and all we do is laugh at them.
i have waited everynight and day from childhood for somebody as wonderful as you to love me as much as i love you.
my love is like the sun and the moon.
when you wake up in the morning there i am looking down at your extremely beautiful face
and at night i still can't believe at how beautiful and sweet you are.
while you dream of money, pt crew's, babies, and other stuff,
i am here looking out for your well being
and cant help myself but to think about you every waking moment
because you are my angel and you mean that much to me......ok this poem is a friend of mine he wants to know what you think
