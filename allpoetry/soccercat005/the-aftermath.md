title: "The Aftermath"

He dropped me relentlessly like a ball of trash
slowly I felt my spirit depreciation
I tried to recoup in concise stories 
My life and love flowed in a long rivulet 
the brevity of my stories were opposite of my life 
I regret the little of my life and love I squandered
