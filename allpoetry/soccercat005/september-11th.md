It started off as a beautiful clear day
Everyone was getting ready for another day
A few minutes later it was dark in New York
People were crying and fearing for their lives
Nothing can describe the emotions felt that day
People here were crying for relatives
People worried about if they were safe or not
Everyone just needed someone to talk to and hold on to
You were there for me when I needed you the most
You comforted me and I comforted you
I know you'll always be there for me
and I will always be there for you
