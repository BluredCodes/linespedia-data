When i talk to you my heart melts
I feel like kissing you when i talk to you
I can imagine how you look and how your lips would taste
evrything about you is great 
No one stands a chance in comparison to you

you are different than me in so many ways
you are like me in some ways too
I can talk to you about anything
you help me with my problems
you always cheer me up
i will always love you
i will wait forever just for you
