I cry,cry and cry
I dont know what's happening
Someone, whom I care for, is about to die
There are people pacing and talking
Someone who loved so much
Who cared for my family and me
There are things worse than such
Being the wife or daughter of thee
My pain is not as bad as theirs 
But it still hurts to think about
I know that everyone still cares
But it makes me want to shout 
I know he knows I'll love him forever
And that I'll always be his favorite grandaughter
