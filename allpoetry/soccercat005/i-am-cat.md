---
title: "I Am Cat"
writer: "Soccercat005"

source: "AllPoetry"

Outside you see someone very happy
Inside I am very sad

Outside you see someone that is shy 
Inside I'm open as can be

Outside you see someone who is fragile
Inside I'm broken and beat

Outside you see someone who's stressed
Inside I'm worry free

I don't like either side of me
But if I was one I would be both
