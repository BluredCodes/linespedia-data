Of nature's flowers
spring thinks to blossom the earth.
The grandparents smile. 

Rebirth of myself,
cycle of life continues.
Tomorrow's child blooms.

The grandparents smile.
Image of daughter budding
in soft pink blossoms. 

Peony pink cheeks
mirror bright velvet petals
on innocent face.

Spring has come softly
filling my home with gladness.
The grandparents smile
