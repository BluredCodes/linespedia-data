Peace seems fleeting, hope seems far away.
Right before our eyes, they vanish in the winds of pain.        
Acts of hate begat fear.
Choose to practice compassion.
Together, we are the world.
Individual yet one; we are strong in unity,
Changing the world, one step at a time,
Embracing peace.

Practice peace in life, pray for love.
Engage your hearts in kindness.
Acts of hope begat new beginnings.
Candles lit to banish darkness,
Embrace the world in peaceful glow.
