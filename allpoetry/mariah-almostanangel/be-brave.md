---
title: "Be Brave"
writer: "mariah almostanangel"

Be brave, I heard her say.
Underneath, her tone said more.
Her hand clung to yours in soft desperation,
wanting you to linger yet go.
She told me you were afraid
of what lay beyond earth's sky of blue.
Your fight for life was near the end
and fear covered you in darkness.
I took the other hand, skeletal as it was.
Eyelids flickered as I spoke of things not clear to me.
Things like angels and prayers in quiet tones.
Remembrances of times past
when God touched your face 
with His tears of rain.
As I spoke, your fingers eased their grip.
I covered your wasted frailness
with antiseptic sheets of cool linen.
At their touch, you shivered.
I leaned close to your ear,
reminded you that angel wings
were now enfolding you.
Their soft, white warmth would ease the cold 
between earth's blue and God's gold.
And you rested,
content to be for just a moment longer
till family love would send you home
on peaceful angel wings.



~~dedicated to Dan, age 43, who left earth's blue on 8/01~~
