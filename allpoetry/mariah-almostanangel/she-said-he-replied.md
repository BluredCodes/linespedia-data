title: "She Said, He Replied"
writer: "mariah almostanangel"

She said to him:
Hold back, don't fall in love with me,
I cannot be trusted with your heart.
My emotions are used up, I have nothing left to give.
Heed my words of caution.
I am not the glimmer that love hides behind;
I am the darkness of emotions long dead.
My walls are too strong for you to breach.
Do not attempt the climb.
Take what I offer you which is momentary pleasure.
Complexity my middle name.
Convoluted paths are mine to travel.
I travel alone. No one can stand the pace.
Take care of your heart for it may get lost 
on the twists and turns on the path of love.
I have no directions to give.
I have been wandering the path for years.
And l remain lost, without hope of rescue.
Stars of desire can guide me so far, then I lose my way.
My footsteps are washed away in floods of tears I shed.
Eyes dry now, footsteps no longer visible.
Keep your heart guarded against one like me.
Warning signs unheeded once
will be heeded round the next corner.
I remain alone on my journey, impervious to any signals.
Be warned. I am not to be trusted.

He replied:
The journey of love is never taken alone.
Contact with others is mandatory.
And I want to make the journey with you.
My trust is limitless, my compassion endless.
Unconditional emotional love can be yours.
Take this hand held out 
to help you over the rough parts of the road ahead.
What you offer is more than you can see.
Your views are blinded by the wall of stones.
I will tear it down stone by stone if need be.
Sunlight will again shine on your heart.
Defend your position all you may, I am not listening.
Because I see what your heart cannot say;
what you will not allow your heart to say.
You have boundless love, let it loose.
The hurts and pain of yesterday are past.
Let the ties that bind you become unraveled
till they fall away and you are free.
I will make an altar of the crumbled stone walls
to sacrifice your fears .
Disappointments go up in smoke 
dissipated by the winds of change.
One step my way is all you need to take.
I will be there to catch you if you falter.
No longer will you walk alone.

She took the hand.
