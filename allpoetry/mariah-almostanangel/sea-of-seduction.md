title: "Sea of Seduction"

The swelling sea of madness
laps at my toes,
luring me to try 
the forgetfulness 
beneath the depths.
Murky waters to cradle
my brain into the emptiness
of escape.
I cannot drown
but will float
in the warmth of stillness
till all is lost.
The world will be silenced
by the rushing waves of lunacy
surrounding my head.
One step is needed to fall in.
