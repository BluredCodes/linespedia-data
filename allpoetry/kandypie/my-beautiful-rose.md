---
title: "My Beautiful Rose"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "my-beautiful-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

To the naked eyeThe rose is a beautiful thingThe softness of its petalsContrasting the boldness of its colourThe sweet fragranceThat drifts to my sensesEnthralls me, captivates meIts beauty runs so much deeperSpiritual, sensualTo touch the soft texture of its budFeel its smoothness on my fingertipsI long to place my lips against itAnd taste the passion I know it holdsYou are my own special roseBeautiful to the naked eyeThat beauty running soul deepJust as I long to touch that roseI yearn to hold youTo taste the sweetness of your kissSmell the fragrance of your hairFeel the softness of your skin on my fingertipsAnd gaze into the depths of your eyesDrowning in your soulYou are more beautiful than any roseThis world will ever seeAnd you are mine to hold and cherishFor my sweet, passionate roseI love you
