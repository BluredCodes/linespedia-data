---
title: "The Stars and Her"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "the-stars-and-her"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5003-The-Stars-and-Her-by-kandypie"
---

I lay back upon the mattress, my eyes roaming to the window
Beyond the pane of glass the world stands at my feet
Silence swirls around me in a dance of nothingness
The stars shine down upon me in a radiant show of light
Soft night sounds whisper words of forgotten tomorrows
Promises long made, broken, kept and treasured
I wonder to myself does She sees these very stars?
Do they speak to Her, do they reach for Her?
Do they commune with Her?
My love is like those stars
Burning so brightly, so intensely
Yet held at a distance
My spirit shines inside me, radiating outwardly like the stars of heaven
I wonder to myself does She see me in those stars?
Does She speak to me, does She reach for me?
Does She feel my love flow to Her
Silence whirls around me, my mind floating away on dreams
And as i tear my eyes from the window pane I smile
For soon my Love will be in my arms
To see the stars shine in my eyes
