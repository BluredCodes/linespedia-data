---
title: "Freedom in Death"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "freedom-in-death"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5005-Freedom-in-Death-by-kandypie"
---

Pain slices through meMy flesh tearing roughlyAs my screams drown out the worldDeath comes for meThe slow draining of my bloodFlowing from my bodySoaking the carpeted floorBlackness, coldness, inescapable emptinessSeeps into my now chilling fleshI tremble yet I'm not afraidI am numb, I am calm, I am no moreThe darks figure of death hovers before my eyesWith my last dying breathMy spirit shouts to the heavens'I am free'And I dance to the stars
