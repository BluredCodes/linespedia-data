---
title: "Without You"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "without-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

My heart is beatingYet i barely even hear itWith every breath of airMy lungs consume life's essenceYet i don't feel that awakeningThe colours of my world have fadedEverything is greyHurt, pain and lossConsume my spiritMy heart is cryingYet i cannot wipe away its tearsI'm drowning in my sorrowIn my painI said i love youYou said goodbyeAnd now all the colour of my worldHas dulled to a painful greyMy lungs fill with airMy heart beatsYet i barely noticeBecause all i can think of....Is YOU.
