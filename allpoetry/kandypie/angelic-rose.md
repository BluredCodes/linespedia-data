---
title: "Angelic Rose"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "angelic-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

I close eyes and fly away
The visions in my mind taking me to heaven
What I see before me takes my breath away
A vision of such beauty, surely it's divine
Surrounded by rose petals
This angel smiles to greet me
The smell floats on the breeze
Dizzying me, enticing me closer
I reach my hand to touch her
This vision, this goddess, this dream
Words do no justice to the loveliness she is
She is the very essence of life, she is all I need
The softness of her skin sends shivers down my spine
My fingers tingle at her touch
My heart pounding, roaring in my ears
The sweetness of her voice
Puts the most heavenly music to shame
With longing in my eyes I slowly lean towards her
Sparkling eyes gaze back at me, entrancing me
Our kiss is one of beauty, innocence
One for all the ages
One that quenches the thirst in my soul
I open my eyes to find her gone
I am alone yet she is still there with me
I can still feel the softness of her lips pressed to mine
Taste the essence of her soul
And smell the fragrance of my Angelic Rose
