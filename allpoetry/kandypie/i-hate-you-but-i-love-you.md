---
title: "I hate you but I love you"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "i-hate-you-but-i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5004-I-hate-you-but-I-love-you-by-kandypie"
---

Do you hear me crying, do you feel my tears?
Do you even give a damn?
You're sitting there laughing aren't you?
Mocking the pain eating at my soul
Burn in hell bitch
Be consumed by fire
Take your presence from my flesh
I hate you
I hate the pain you make me feel
I hate the longing, the yearning, the need
I hate the way you make me weak
But most of all I hate that I love you
That I need you, that I want you
I breathe for you, I live for you
I would follow you to hell and back
Burn with you, burn for you
Will I burn because of you?
I love you but I hate you
But I love you
Take me into you, stop torturing me
What are you afraid of?
Afraid I'll get too close?
That I'll see the real you
And know your darkest secrets?
Love me, hate me
Make a choice damn it!
Either wrap me in your arms
Or let me go and watch me fall
Don't leave me hanging over the flames of uncertainty
I hate you
But I love you
