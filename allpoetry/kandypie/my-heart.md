---
title: "My Heart"
writer: "kandypie"

I lay awake and often wish
You were there beside me
My heart cries out to kiss your lips
My soul to hold you close
Do you dream of me as I do you?
Or is this all my imagination
I close my eyes and you are there
A smile for only me
And a kiss of the purest love
Oh how i adore and cherish your world
And pray you will be a part of mine
I will love you and hold you and kiss away your tears
Until the end of time
We are the best of friends but oh so much more
My heart is here waiting with an open door
Will you come inside and fill it with your perfection?
