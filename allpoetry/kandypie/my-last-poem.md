I love herI gave her everythingBut in the end it was not enoughShe left me for anotherShe's made the biggest mistakeOne she will regret foreverBut that doesn't change the factThat I have died insideThis is the last poem that I writeFor I am no moreGoodbye~ Becca...I love you...I will love you forever ~
