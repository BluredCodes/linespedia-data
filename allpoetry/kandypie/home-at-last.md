---
title: "Home At Last"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "home-at-last"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5000-Home-At-Last-by-kandypie"
---

All of my life I have wandered alone
Lost, frightened, searching in vain for Her
For the woman who teased my daily thoughts
And tormented my nightly dreams with her beauty
But everywhere I looked I found nothing but pain
Becoming a pawn in the sick and twisted games of others
Broken...but not beaten, I protected my heart
Making the painful but necessary choice
To give up my search
And then like lightning, flashing in a raging storm
She sparked into my life - electrifying me with her presence
Right from those first moments she had me
I was lost but found, drowning but swimming in her
With every breath in my body she sucked me closer
And fight it though I tried - she stole my heart away
I dreamt every night of feeling the beauty of her love
And now I cry every day, blessed, knowing it to be true
When she sparked into my world, she set my life on fire
And like pieces of a puzzle, she fills me, she completes me
She is my Spirit, My diamond, my baby, my love
She holds the key to my heart and all locked within
Forever and a day
I am lost no more, for I have found my love
I am home at last.
