---
title: "Love V Pain"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "love-v-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4995-Love-V-Pain-by-kandypie"
---

I feel your pain
See it in your eyes
Hear it in your voice
Live it in your tears
Your heart has been broken
Torn by someone you loved
I cant heal it for you
Only show you it will heal itself
In time
For I am here
To help it heal
And fill it with more than you ever imagined
For you fill my heart with so many things
Love, excitement, desire
You have found your place inside my soul
You set my heart on fire
I cant take the pain you feel away
But if you will let me
I will share it with you
And we will get past this together
