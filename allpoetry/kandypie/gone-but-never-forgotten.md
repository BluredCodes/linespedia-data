---
title: "Gone but never forgotten"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "gone-but-never-forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4998-Gone-but-never-forgotten-by-kandypie"

Gone from my fleshCherished in my heartPain consuming everythingTears of sadnessShed for youThe pain of lossDrowning me in my sorrowMy precious childMy gift to heavenWatch over meUntil we are one againForgive me So that i may forgive myself
