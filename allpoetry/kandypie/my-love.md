---
title: "My Love"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4999-My-Love-by-kandypie"

See the love in my eyes
Taste the passion in my kiss
Feel the warmth in my embrace
Be consumed by the emotion in my heart
They are yours 
Forever and a day
