title: "Angel"
writer: "kandypie"

I close my eyes and what do I see?
An angel swims before my vision
Beckoning me
She smiles at me, with love in her eyes
And I melt inside
Filled with the warmth of her love
Laying in her arms at night
Smiling into the darkness
My heart is filled with sweet contentment
At peace and happy in her love
A love filling me completely
To taste the sweetness of her kiss
Feel the softness of her skin against mine
Tremble under her sweet caress
And whisper words of longing in her ear
She is my love, my life, my all
To her I give everything
Heart, body and soul
She completes me as no other does or ever will
She is my angel
And I am lost in her love
Completely, eternally
My beautiful angel, my lover, my friend
You have all of me
Eternally
