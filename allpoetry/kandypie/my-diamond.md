---
title: "My diamond"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "my-diamond"
source: "AllPoetry"

My sparkling diamond
My shining star
That beacon of hope and heaven
Shimmering in the darkness
Leading me through the world
I love you
I cherish you
I hold you
I own you
For nothing to Me is more beautiful
Than that shimmering image
That sparkling shine
Of My diamond in the rough
