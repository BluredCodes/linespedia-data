title: "Her Love"

Heart pounding against my chestLike a pack of wild brumbiesThundering through meHer love...calmLike the slow steady streamFlowing smoothly towards meLike a warm blanketIt wraps around meAnd the raging storm of my emotionsQuietens to a contented silenceWhen she smiles I see heavenAs though the stars have fallenTo shine brightly in her eyesWhen she laughs I hear musicAs though Pachebel plays his CanonSolely for meIn the warmth of her touchI find infinite kindnessA gentleness, a tendernessLike a mother to a childIn her i find everythingI find love, I find life...I find myself.
