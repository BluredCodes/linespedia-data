---
title: "Child Of Mine"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "child-of-mine"
source: "AllPoetry"

Every day I sense your growth
Feel you inside me
Binding yourself to me
Every day I cry silent tears
Knowing I'll lose you
Struggling to hold you
I should remain detached
Hold myself back
Shelter myself from the pain to come
But you are a part of me
Inside me
Growing every day
And every day I yearn for you
Long to hold you
To brush away your tears
Will there ever come a time
When I will hold you
Child of mine
