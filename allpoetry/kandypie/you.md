---
title: "You"
writer: "kandypie"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The sound of your name 
Is like a song played for me
The beauty of your smile
Consumes me so completely
I am drawn to you
I am glued to you
You touch the places inside me
I had long since felt were dead
You fill my very soul
With the passion in your eyes
I yearn for you
I long for you
I would beg for you
I would die for you
Everything i have to give
Is laid upon your feet
Everything i hope to be
Is moulded by my love
I come to you
My heart held within my hands
Take me
Need me
Want me
Love me
Fill me with your fire 
Consume me in its blaze
Take me to that place inside myself
And free me into the endlessness
Of my love for you
