I woke up today and smiled
Today is my day,
I am in control
My outcome, my happiness.
The first raindrop fell
Along with it my first tear
What makes happiness when we want joy
I go to have fun and what I find is misery.
misery
If I was alone, would I know misery
Is misery relative, do I make it
I tell myself I am ‘ok’
I wanted to have that good day
I smile, I act ok, the day is good
Am I really miserable if I ignore happiness
I laugh and smile with everyone else
I feel better, but should I address what I feel
I think too much
I smile too little
It is impossible to ignore misery
It is my choice to smile.
