title: "The Two Sides of Us"

We are in the same room
Same air, same conversation
We laugh, We smile.
It is clear to me how I feel
Each second we spend I grow closer
Each story we share I feel our bond tighten
I realize more and more how much I need you.
Everyone teases me for our quasi-dating
‘nothing is physical, nothing is serious.’ I say
‘It will be some time, he is still recovering from another relationship’
But we are so good together, it’ll work out
And there I was wrong.
You sit there and look at me so different
You think of me as a friend, no longer as a lover.
What makes you change, what stole your love.
Am I to blame, my first destiny spoiled.
You change your mind as I grow attached to you.
Damn, I thought we both new where this went
To love 
You feel nothing.
I wish now I felt nothing.
Happiness was going to be ours
But maybe not
We are in the same room
Same air, same conversation
Then you left.
