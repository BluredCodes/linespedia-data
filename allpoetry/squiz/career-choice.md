When I was a kid, I was always anxious to be older
I would visualize high school with a bag on my shoulders
The complexity of new subjects and multiple classes intrigued me
Now that I am in college, work and struggle is all I see.

I am finally learning about something I am passionate for
At the same time, it is so hard, I question if I like it any more
I wonder if it still worth pursuing it because I like it
Or if following something I am better at is a better fit.

Later in life will I be happier if I had good grades
I am sure the memory of classes will definitely fade
What I will notice is my occupation and my happiness
My job satisfaction and mental challenge will define success.

I guess then everything is worth the pursuit of my degree
I shouldn’t handicap myself with believing there is more I need
I like this field, I know I am smart, and that will take me far
A little dedication and two more years will get me my start.
