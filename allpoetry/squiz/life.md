There was a time in my life
When I felt confident in myself
When I was fearful of my ability
When I was hopeful for someone
When I mourned for a loved one
When I saw a perfect life
When I was disappointed 
When I was surrounded by love
When I was alone 
When others supported me 
When I was fearful
When I felt proud 
When I was ashamed
When I achieved a goal
When I made a mistake 
When I was thankful
When I was let down
When I knew what I wanted
When I was lost
When someone loved me
When I cried
When opportunity knocked
When I made bad choices
When I had tons of fun
When I was unhappy
When I won
When I lost 
When I was who I wanted to be
When I was led astray
When I was there for others
When I hurt my friends
When I realized my full potential
When I stopped short
