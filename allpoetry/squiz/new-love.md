---
title: "New Love"
writer: "squiz"
slug: "new-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

What do they see
	That makes them guess
What do I do
	That keeps you here
What should I feel
	When you call
What am I to do
	If I have your love.

Am I supposed to feel
	Great with you
Am I right to think
	You feel it too
Am I a perfect match
	Can anyone ever be
Am I what you want
	And who you love.

There is so much
	I can do with you
There is an emotion
	I cannot suppress
There might be more
	 If we were together
There are great things
 	When I am with you.

I am always sad
	To see you go
I can’t always understand
	What you want
I will be true
	If you care for me
I won’t stop my heart
	From following you.

The bond we have,
	They cannot know
The laughs that we share
	They cannot hear
The way that you feel
	I cannot know
The future you see
