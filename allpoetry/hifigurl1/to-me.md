To me life is the worst thing i ever had,
                         i just want to kill, people make me so mad,
                          To me the world is one big peice of shit
                           , people always messing around,
                            just dont know when to quit.
                             To me i could care less if i live or if i die,
                               my life sux so bad all i can do is cry.
                              To me its like my family just keeps dieing, 
                               the look on my face is always sighing.
                              To me it is to me and how i think it to be.I know thins is a crazy poem but thats ok.
