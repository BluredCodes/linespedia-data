I feel sick like my body is dieing, my eyes hurt from all of the crying. Theres this big bright light thats always gleaming, my throat hurts from all the screaming. My bones hurt from hitting them again and again. Theres blood all over from cutting at my skin. The lights are fading and all i see is black, the police come, i leave in a sack. I herd the music, its such a loud sound , as they lower my casket into the ground.Tell me what you think
