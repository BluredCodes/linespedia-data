The water was stuck in my throat i couldn't breath, it was all around my eyes i couldn't see. I jumped and panicked and put my hands in the air, but it was like noone saw me or noone cared. I felt real tired and wanted to put my hands down, but i knew if i did then i would drownd. My head went under there was nothing i could do, i knew in my head that it would soon be through. Blood came from my mouth as i went under the lake , a nother breath i did not take.
