---
title: "Like fine wine?"
writer: "allagory"

I've looked at a women older than me
she aroused me for some reason
she wore her age
her hair was pretty nice
her eyes were beautiful
why did I have this strong urge
to do her
the thing is is that she would 
let me
she told me she wanted too
but I had the urge
I didn't really want to
