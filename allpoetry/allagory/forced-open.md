How many times can a person get raped insideyou have no control, they are strongerthey have the advantagethey feel justified for what they doit's always my faultthey tell me it's my faultI alway's walk right into itI know they're around that cornerStripped naked everytime, they turn me into an animalmaking me want to sacrifice myself on thealter of self-image
