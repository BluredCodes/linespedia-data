she took the baseball bat and started hitting me in theback. I fell over when she hit me in the face.My mouth full of blood I cried out please stopNot for a second was their any guilt in her eyesShe then proceeded to break every bone in my bodyI knew that I was bleeding inside.She didn't stop until she watched me cough upsome blood.She felt as if she accomplished something.As if she did humanity a great deed.But no, not humanity, just herself.She didn't care. I couldn't move. Only being able to writh in painShe sat down in her recliner keeping her eyes on me.As she sat back she opened her textbook,she reached for her cup taking a drink of water.I ws getting weak my vision was blurry.My last vision in this world was watchingher start on her homework.
