title: "Open praries"

I've felt
what a prisoner
feels when 
he walks free
the sun warming
a face that's
been cold for
so long
feeling a breeze
as if He'd
never felt it
before
Looking at grass
thinking he's
never seen green
before
never knowing walls
come in several 
colors
and the first woman
in sight
is the most beautiful
he's ever seen
this is how I've felt
