She told them it was a badidea, they hadn't looked down therefirst to see if it was safethey teased her saying chicks worryabout the stupidest stuffshe just remained quietone of the guys jumped of the cliff into the lakewhen his head hit a rockwhen he didn't come up his friend jumpedin to see what was wrong he busted his head on the same rockthe other guys kept on doing thisthe girl walked downshe swam inand she pulled them outthen she saidStupid boys..
