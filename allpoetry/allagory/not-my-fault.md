It's not my problem
looking into the swimming
pool, a little kid
floating face down
a mother crying so
loud, you can hear her
three blocks away
the father is pissed
but the pool owner
said if you were better
parents...
why didn't you put
a fence up
why didn't you
the mother screams
In a cold and careless
face the man shrugs
Ishouldn't have to
If you were better 
parents...
The mans floating 
facedown in the pool
the fathers doing
10 to 20
