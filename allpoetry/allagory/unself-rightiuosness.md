Superiority and the feeling of Inferiority
are the same, but only diffrent
the two are related 
Superiority knows what it 
is, only it has amnesia
the feeling of Inferiority has alzeimers
that person isn't inferior, yet it
makes them believe they are
leaving the humble to catch
the brunt of it all, from the ones
with crystal humility
then all become belttled 
please, treat me like a child
just a little more
I don't feel bad enough just yet.
