Sailing in my ostentatious ship
the gigantic choppy waves bang against the 
stubborn sturn, with the mist breathing
itself into my lungs
I look across the endless waters that know no
bounds, where you don't know 
where heaven and earth end
then I see my destination 
my home awaiting
then I step onto land looking back
noticing it is just my pond in
my back yard.
That will always be mt sea.
