title: "The wind is a woman"

I watched the wind
it was grey
it spoke in my ear
and told me it
loved me
I never knew the
wind was a women
How could I not
have known
She can warm you
when she gets close
then you can freeze
as she stays that way
she can sway and
move almost anything
she has curves
no man has ever seen
no man can withstand her
she told me she loved
me, and she wanted to
make love
but I said no
because she would never
stay
