---
title: "Tissue"
writer: "allagory"
slug: "tissue"
source: "AllPoetry"

i have a cut
it doesn't hurt much at all
it bleeds just a little
you can barely see it
yeah those are the stitches
no, i don't know why they are there
maybe to fight infection
a dab of ointment helps
but it's just a scratch
