I looked at youtoday, prettyas usual, it's killingme,to think how I lostyou, I very oftenfeel as if I've been run through,the agonizingpain, sets me stillBut I still enjoylooking at you, joyfullyI keep on hurting,because your still nearme, your bodygives me life,along with your faceyou make me want to die,but as I start slippingawaymy memory stay'sand through it, you I seeso the picture of you, compellesmy lifeto return.
