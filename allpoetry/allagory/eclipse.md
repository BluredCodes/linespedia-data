Today I saw the Sun rise
It lifted 
itself above a high
Mountain, but that night
 Mountain taunted
 Sun, telling him he won't
rise above him ever
again.  Mountain convinced
Sun he never
will again. 
Mountain boasted 
of his splendor, 
of how man see's
him mightier 
than Sun. Mountain told
Sun stories
of how Man feared
Mountain, and that how death
came to men who tried
to conquer
him. The mountain told Sun,
don't rise above me again.
Because you can't.
Sun setteling behind 
the Earth, became filled with fear.
He no longer wanted
to be the Sun. He was afraid to rise
and decided not 
to. But Moon noticed sadness
in Sun. He knew he was somewhat dicsouraged.
Moon asked Sun what was bothering
him. Sun shrugged 
and he lowered 
his eyes in shame
saying, I can no longer rise above the mighty
Mountain. For he is so much greater
than I. Moon becoming angry
and told Sun to look 
him in the eyes. Why do you fear
Mountain. He doesn't have any courage.
Sun told Moon the stories
and facts
mountain had told him. 
Moon looked at Sun in compassion
saying, Mountain doesn't say those words
because they are true
but because they are false.
If his words were true
then he wouldn't have to tell you.
You would know. That is the nature of things.
Mountain does nothing mighty.
He just sets there.
Sun, if were not for you
I would not shine.
If it were not for you life
could not exist. 
And Mountain wouldn't be so great.
Man does fear Mountain.
But they respect
and  Love
you. Look around you, the planets
are your servants.
They know this and so does Mountain.
It's time for you to rise, now go! 
Sun rose, Mountain said nothing.
And he never 
did again. Because he knew.
