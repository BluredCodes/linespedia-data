The little lady gets kicked in the face screamingwhy why whyat the little boy holding her corndoglaughing so hard he can't breath ashis mother looks on and shakes her headwondering if his father will come home soonhe gets paid today but not in cash or even a checkbut in dirt, he brings home a load of dirt for the family to live onthey live in the suburbs with other neighbors with wildkids, the sixteen year old gurl down the block prefersto set at home watching television as her father sets and stares ather, while mom cooks dinnerit's hot outside because of all the pavementthe grass has died so the yard it dust and weedsthe playground toys have rusted out, causing joey to break his arm when theswing broke causing the dust to kick up when he started cryingcrying for his mommy or anyone who caredlooking through his tear filled eyes all he could seewas the dog setting in the yard licking himself
