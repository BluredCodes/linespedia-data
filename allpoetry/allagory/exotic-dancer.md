title: "Exotic Dancer"

When the men are watching you
they often wonder what your
personality is like
they think about your family
and worry if you have any
concerns
when they see you in the
porno mag
they ask if you
are doing well in school
how many brothers or sisters you have
they think to themselves
I bet she's a really
bright girl
she's probably really assertive
and smart
I figure she has a pretty good life
then they wash their
hands
and go to bed
