the turtle dodges the carhe wonders how he even made ituntil the next one gets himthe deer laughs because the turtle was to slow to get out of the waythen the deer gets shother laughing has stoppedbut her baby deer is cryingit runs off entering a cave getting mauled bya beara bird see's it all go downit flies away glad he can flyuntil it gets sucked into a jet enginea guy on the plane is griping abouthis lost luggage but he stops whenthe plane goes into a steep divecrashing onto a small dirt roadthe turtle watcheshappy that the car shot him off the road
