Look at how the water flowsgoing where it pleasesno one telling it what to dothats usually how the story goeswatch the sun burn my skinit will hurt much laternow it's red and achessoon it will turn brownbut the brown will just peel againsee that man eating his mealhe puts it away with no regretshis jowls shake as he swallows it downbut soon his life will have to endsince he made eating such an ordealthe weary old lady is hitting on the much younger guyshe's just asked him what's under his flybut without missing a beatit's something I promise you'll never seethe man and the women are there making loveit's something they both will alway's rememberbut they better enjoy it while it lastsbecause what they are enjoying now conceivesthe kid coming in December
