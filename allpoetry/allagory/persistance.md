Their are times I want to feel
good, But a bad funk
will seem to hold me back
I'll try to get through
it, but it won't let go.
It want's me to have a bad 
day, but i really
don't want to let
it, I can't give in 
to it, I won't give in to it.
If I give in my whole
day will be ruined.
The struggling sucks. 
it not much fun either.
But the struggle keeps me holding on.
Because when that wonderful event
in my day occurs, it makes the struggle well worth 
it, If I was to give in a whole day
is ruined, with no turning back. 
But when the wonderful event helps me win
that struggle, The struggle wasn't so bad
afterall.
