As drowsy as I may be
It adds to my thoughts
emotions and my vulnerability
It makes my world hazy, as if all
were a soft cloud, nothing seems real
not the green tree 
The border is fogged between the trunk
and the leaves
When the wind blows
the tree seems to be waving at me
I don't walk but almost a float
I long to rest but still I keep on
The ground so inviting
pulling me down
but I keep going 
with the faintest sound
the sound of a voice caling to me
I've heard it before
wondering who could it be
I glide past the building
that I could have walked through
Around the corner just to see who
was there
My first vision was her flowing brown hair
All else seeming elusive artifitial
but she being distinct
was all that was real
She reached out and touched me
comfort with her green hazel eyes
holding my hand hers felt like cotton
she pulled me close and leaning
her plush and warm cheek brushing mine
into my ear she whispered 
Wake up, wake up,I'm sorry
All of this goes beyond reality 
I awoke standing there 
the world now is defined
She was gone Yet I could 
still feel her warmth on my cheek
would it have been better if was
reality?
