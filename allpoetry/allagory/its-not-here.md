Can the tree run as fast as you canor can the corn stalk hanging on the cornerDon't ask him about his girlfriend nor about his family he won't answerHe concerned with the rain coming inThe rain doesn't like it to much hereit's not much fun,reallythere isn't anything to dothe wind is unemployed living on foodstamps provided bythe grease barrels down on main who tryto pimp anything that will get thema new cornerbut the butcher doesn't like any of themhe yells and rants about the skunk out backyelling never does any good because they can't hear anyway
