The rain came down oncei watched it as it came downpounding the earthdust flew as the water landedon dust dryit collected darkeningthe groundwater rose, mud wascreatednext day the earth wasdry, and from where the dustlaid grass grewthe earth's potential enemyand threat swept throughthe earth knows, therain will come againbut the rain doesn't
