running down the hillgoing as fast as I possibly canmy god this is funI have this hot girl beside meboobies bouncingbut I better pay attention towhat I'm doingI can see the bottom of the hillthe wind is cool andblowing in my faceMy girl is lagging behindI look back wondering were sheis goingand I then notice how much I really love her and wish she would come with me to the bottomshe yells something but i can't heari wave backwith the greatest sensation in the world rushes over meas i get to the bottom of the hill i goright into a barbed wire fenceshe slowly comes down, looks down at me and saysare you okay
