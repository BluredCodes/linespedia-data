---
title: "climb the fence"
writer: "allagory"
slug: "climb-the-fence"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7981-climb-the-fence-by-allagory"

when you look at 
my face
what do you 
see
do you ever
feel anything
what happens to you
is your soul moved
does your heart
break
do you want to
fall in love
all over again
i wish i knew
