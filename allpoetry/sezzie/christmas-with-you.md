---
title: "Christmas with you"
writer: "sezzie"

As the weather gets colder,
And the nights get darker,
I know the christmas season is upon me.

The cold nights and icy mornings,
 and the chill in the air,
Is here once again.

But this year the ice is magical,
As it twinkles in the sunlight, 
and smiles at me.


This year it is a kind frost, 
that greets me in the mornings.

The decorated rooms are filled with:
Happiness love and laughter.

The coal fire glows with pleasure.
The magic of christmas will feel once again.
As this year I am with you.

We will be able to share the kiss under the mistltoe.
The first magical sight of the single falling snowflake,
Will be ours to keep forever.

The magic of christmas will be alive once again.

And all because this year I have your love,
Which is the best christmas present I could ever want.
Because I return your love,
and always,always will love you.
