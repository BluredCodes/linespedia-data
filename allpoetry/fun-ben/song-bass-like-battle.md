title: "song = bass like battle"

Yes, I'm starting to know her
it's just like the hot water
she'll never have her fill of power
always adjusting and craving hotter
she's only happy in the shower
the dictator's only daughter

she's seeking only satiation
satisfactions far beyond her grip
unaware, I've got lacerations
strings like razors on my lips
she's gone so far past my patience
all snide smiles and nasty quips

CHORUS:
pound in my ears
your bass like battle
crash in fevers
a voice like gravel
grating and grating
on my addictions
baiting and baiting
my aggressions
but I'm not listening to you anymore
our song is over
our song is over

I'm reaching out with this shaky hand
when a crash of cymbals brings me down
with all my strength I try to stand
but every drumbeat shakes the ground
she won't lay off that baby grand
and that mountain-shaking sound

I see the gap between us widening -
where's the friendly treble clef
in this orchaestral war she's waging?
I get the feeling I'm going deaf
is that thunder or is she singing?
and how come I'm the one out. of. breath?
