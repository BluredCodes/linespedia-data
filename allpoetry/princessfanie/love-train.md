We jumped on this train and began a journey
We found each other

Some days our thoughts are inseprable

It seems the more our souls open to one another we always know what each other thinks

I always thought I knew where this train would lead us
I felt the heat from the two steel tracks that guided us

Suddenly with no warning this train began to slow down
Maybe it was going too fast 

Time seemed to slip away from us
Slowly gliding down these tracks my thoughts began to slow down too
I never thought I would not know what you were thinking

Confusion sets in and I feel like our train is slowly de-railing

Am I losing ....
Are our souls not fighting to be one any longer in this life time....

Trying to make the train feel it's tracks again

Hoping for the love we feel to start this journey we bagan not so long ago....
