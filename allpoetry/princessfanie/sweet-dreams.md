All I wanted was to walk through this sand with you

Hours seem to dissipate 

That walk we'll never see, but I have this smile and that's all I see

You see this smile keeps me alive

It keeps my mornings bright
And my nights full of sweet dreams

Sweet Dreams that let me live this walk through the sand with you

Thank you for these dreams
I know every night you send them my way

Send me so many feelings to feel

And all the warmth to wrap around this bare body that seems to shiver when your not here

For every morning thank you for giving another smile on my face......
