---
title: "This power"
writer: "princessfanie"

source: "AllPoetry"

I need not take anything you haven't offered me
You offer your soul with open arms

I am so into you
I feel this power that soaks through my veins

The air you give to help me breathe
It surrounds all of me

I only pray for the satisfaction I need to survive

This heart can't get run over through and through anymore
This heart always leads me down to you 

And you take and give me all of you

That's all I need to close these eyes of mine at night
