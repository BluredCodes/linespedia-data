Your breath is so warm
Your body touching mine 
All I want is this moment not to ever fade in my mind 

Just lay here still
Let me look you over and over again
I need this vivid picture to carry my thoughts into each night

The heat of these two souls wrapped together as one

I feel this serene light around us

The untouchable love given to us to hold on forever 

We will stay always...... this way
