All of this can't be so wrongHow do we run and hide from this power over loading our heartsThis numb feeling invading my thoughtsDo I have any meaning anymore....Any dignity left to hold What has failed usAll I want makes me want so much moreSometimes I just need darkness to sleep my pain awayHow did we loose ourselves here....Shall we try to open out eyes to hidden answers in the distanceToo far for us to always reachKnowing we need to keep reachingReaching for answers we are not ready to discover at timesSo I hold you as close as we can beSqueeze so tight to feel all we needTears come so sudden down your face & as I wipe each one I realize they are the same tears I cried moments before Looking so deep into tear filled eyes an wondering again how wrong this love could beThis love should not hurt so badYet it is so rightSo rightNot to ever let go of....
