---
title: "My True Sunset"
writer: "princessfanie"

The darkness surrounds me as I find my way thru

Your door is open
I quietly walk in
I make my way to you

My fragrance awakens you a bit
My touches close your eyes again
Your body starts to tremble as I reach places that begin to arose all these hidden desires

This darkness still surrounds all of me and you
But it's your body that has taken me into this darkness 

No words exchanged
Moans just fill the air as you glide into this paradise we create

The sighs
The grabbing and holding 
The releases of the soul

We finally lay still 
Our eyes meet and we give each other a smile
Without no words exchanged I quietly tuck you in

I walk to that open door I recently walked in
I quietly walk out into the darkness once again and admire the sunset thru my eyes for the first time
