Over and over I find myself waking to these desires

The touches
The kisses
The intimate looks we give one another 

You always pull me where I want you
You take me into this paradise

We play
We sing
We moan
We become one for hours at a time

Yearning for all this lust to take us into another night

Entwine into each other

Am I awake yet....
I feel so much of you in my presence
It scares me to think I could have you with these lights on all around me as I wake....
