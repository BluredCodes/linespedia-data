---
title: "Forever"
writer: "princessfanie"

Forever....love me always

Forever....touch me

Forever....caress all of me

Forever....hold all that we are

Forever....be what I need you to be

Forever....take my hands in yours

Forever....let us watch all the stars

Forever....freinds

Forever....lovers side by side

Forever....will always be forever with you and me
