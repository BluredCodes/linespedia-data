Are you really being you....
Are you protecting this night from the forbidden flight....

Seems we fly many places to see this love again

Deep inside this love never went away

Right here I stand to love only you

So this rain I walk thru to get to you will only bring me past the pain I grieve inside

For your heart
Your love
Your soul

Let these soul's rest together for awhile
Let them mend in each others arms

To figure out that this flight would be too lonley with just one heart in the sky

So take me with you 
Take each other into every morning and night
Become one set of wings forever....
