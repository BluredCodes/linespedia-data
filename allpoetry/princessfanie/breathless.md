How can these eyes call me within....

How can I discover the life enlightening every new door opening my way...

How can we blame this love for where we stand today....

I'm breathless
I'm numb
I'm engulfed in this time

Kiss these lips that bare your name

And hold this breath of mine deep inside
To make you feel the warmth that you send my way

Fill these lungs that capture you from within all of me...
