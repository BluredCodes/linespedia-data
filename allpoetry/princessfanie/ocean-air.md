---
title: "Ocean Air"
writer: "princessfanie"
slug: "ocean-air"
source: "AllPoetry"

I slowly slip your hand in mine

You push me in the right direction

You always seem to push...
So gently
So calmly

You and I still for moments

The doors open
The smell of ocean air
The wind blowing through my hair

You touch your body to mine from behind
Swaying your head back and forth
Searching for my bare neck to caress

The world seems to stand still for hours

Just you and I

When I look at you I see so much love in you

Watching you when you are unaware
What my eyes see that you don't

Your smile so tantalizing
Your eyes so warm

The sun rises
My eyes slowly open
The morning light hurts my eyes

Suddenly as I stand on my feet I briefly smell the ocean air and long
to close my eyes soon again
