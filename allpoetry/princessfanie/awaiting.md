How can I sit and write of this lonelinessThis loneliness that brings me down too many times in a daySearching my soul for answersNo reply ever found where I lookAre my eyes open to see right from wrongHow do I feel your presence so near to me everydayI feel your emotionsI feel your thoughtsEverything is so clear some daysIt's so scary when two souls think as oneWhere is this energyThis force that keeps continuing to pull us nearWhy am I standingright hereright nowHas life steered me here for a purposeA purpose yet not known to meI patiently await for the guidance I need to push me into what I've discovered all alone
