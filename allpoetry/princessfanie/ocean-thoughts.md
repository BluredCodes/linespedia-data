title: "Ocean Thoughts"

The wind can't stop pushing me down

It's so hard for me to see the sand

This hurt that takes all my breath away

I only wish for you to find me
Uncover all of me 
Hold this body near to feel all that can't be

I sit back and look at only the ocean behind us
It's so hard to get out of this sand I stand in

Too many hours to consume thoughts I never wanted to see

Can I reach across this ocean in between us just for a while

It seems that I only have minutes with you at times

How can this ocean take almost all you and leave little for me to love

It is so hard to think that this love can't be all we need it to be....
