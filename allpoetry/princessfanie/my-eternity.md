I lay here listening to this world going byWith myself in between you two tonightI look to my left and see dirty blonde hairDimples sunken inEyes glowingA smile that will take you as far as you want in life how I adore youThen as I glance to my right and see you with dark brown hairAn incredible smileA charming look about you that can melt any ones heart in twoLastly of course the angel of our familyWith silky long dark brown hair with baby curls on the endsOur princess, our CinderellaHow she has completed everyone of usI sleepBreatheWalkWould go to another world to give you my last breathYou are my eternityAnd with Alissa's wings to take all of us anywhere we want to goAlways think happy thoughts....My children...my life ...how they have made me what I am today.  I love you three as far as love can take us each and every breathing day that comes our way.  *Mommy*
