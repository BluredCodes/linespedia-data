---
title: "Holding Me"
writer: "princessfanie"

Breathe with me
Be still in my arms
Capture the silence
Feel this love how alive wrapped in these arms of ours

Don't let go
Stay forever in my arms 

Walk only my way
Love only me
Here is where our love will always be

You always soothe my confused thoughts
You give me so much love to write about 
You opened my heart when others strayed 
I never really knew the real me until you

Thanks for staying
Thanks for the love you give to me 
Thanks for opening your arms and holding only me....
