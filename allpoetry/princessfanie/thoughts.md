Tell me every thought in your head

I hear your voice echoing in my dreams
I hear each breath you take
I want to hear you forever in my life

Want to hand you this love of mine to keep forever close

Let's open our eyes together
Watch the sunrise and the sunset 

The power of our hearts slowly awakening to every new day 

For us to be one heart 

One soul....
