---
title: "Love Again"
writer: "princessfanie"

I feel broke
I feel lost so many times in one day
Will you come to me with answers....
Or will you pass by....

I hope I'm lucky enough to feel your presence

Will my hands open to your touch....
Will my arms open to your embrace....
Will my lips feel your sweet lips....

I want your answers soon
I want you to want me like always
I want this love all for my own

Don't break this love again....
