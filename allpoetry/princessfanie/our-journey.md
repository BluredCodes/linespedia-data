A lasting love like ours caresses our journeys near or far

Never letting hands go as we walk through bitter cold winter mornings

Watching one another's smiles as the smell of Spring sends a little warmth through and through

Always holding on to continue our journey hand and hand

Summer days and heated moments that do come, but having this love entwined helps us walk past all the heartache every once in a while

Leaves that fly past us so delicately as Fall soon comes

All of life’s precious seasons that come to be make our love stronger

    The challenges sometimes we must endure build a better us 

We have sacrificed each of our soul’s to be one soul; one love to travel hand and hand through all of life’s mysteries along this journey
