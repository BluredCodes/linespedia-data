Out my window the sun glistens in this big LakeThe warm air to hot to bare at timesI blink my eyes and snow starts to fallI feel these winter mornings aloneI walk to the Lake and it's covered with iceThe sun is outI can see my reflection even thru solid iceAnother reflection appearsCould it be the reflection I haven't see in yearsThe reflection that ran away not long agoI've stormed so many seasons without you by meFeeling like I was drowning in an Ocean larger than this Lake I loveI had to learn to swim again on my ownIt's the lake outside my window I started inSo if other reflections come.....My soul I have discovered will guide my way to find the peace I need
