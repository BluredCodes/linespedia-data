Trying to fight these feelingsFeelings I hope with time will fade a littleFade a little more than what I feel todayNot knowing what I am Who to be todayWhy am I hereCrossing roads I never drempt I would cross Leaving behind all I knewTo get your feeling backTurning around insideCan't go to what I feel nowHurt me nowLeave the despairI watch as you leaveCan't see you nowCan't feel your breath to mineCan't love all of what I needWhen someone else is standing in between
