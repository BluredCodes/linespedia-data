---
title: "Your Love"
writer: "princessfanie"

I'm lost in your love

Take these desires

Take what you need

I give all my love to your open arms again tonight

I climb these stairs inside your heart

I shut all those doors that you could not reach

You hold your hand out and gently place the one and only key to your heart in mine
