---
title: "Set You Free"
writer: "princessfanie"

I can't judge this life or these lies

I've tried way too hard to run from this desire

This desire that still calls out my name

I will always come back to the place where we fell in love

Keep your eyes open wide for me

Even in your distant memories

I'm there....
Always there....

I hear your echo's

I feel this love drowning out all your sorrows

You need less of me everyday to conquor all these fears

Less of me will set you FREE....
