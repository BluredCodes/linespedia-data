Have you ever come close to feel the sunlight in your hands....
Or feel the warmth shimmer through your veins....

This sunlight seems to disappear when I come oh so close to touch or grab

I fly in other directions to find what I need at this moment in time
Finding answers can only bring me comfort
So I open my wings through the rough windy storms ahead

I just wish I could get to this clear air
I feel I need to take a few deep breaths

It's too often that all I'm left with when I do feel is the smell of you in my hands

Should I love more or will it hurt more....

No simple thing about what I feel

I just need to reach the sunlight everyday and any day I feel the need
Because without this sunlight I'm nothing
I will have no meaning to this world....
