title: "Falling So"

Falling so far for youGiving my soul for this game This game of life and love we playEveryday a challenge sent my wayTake me into this journey with no fightI give my all to theeI breathe, I wake, I sleep for youDon't turn and look at me with eyes so coldI give warmth but you blow it awayMy hands are out but you close yours Take me in your arms for a while...don't push me awayIs this darkness only temporaryI can see all of you in the dark nowHow did your heart become so hollow insideYou never wanted my soul foreverYou only wanted to take a walkWhen I wanted to get on this carousel and never stop loving youThe sun is so bright and my worst fears I've metWill I ever play again in this charadeWill I ever give my soul againI will stand this go roundFalling is far behind me nowFields of butterfly's spread their wings and take me to search in the distance for one rare Caterpillar I will soon hold forever...
