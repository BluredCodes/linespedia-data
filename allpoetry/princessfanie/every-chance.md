title: "Every Chance"
writer: "princessfanie"

I ran a bath tonight
I gently stepped in
I lay my body softly into the warmth that surrounds me

I'm tired of fighting all of this

I've come to this closure in my mind

I will take you
I will love you
Every chance I get

This love is not wrong
It can't be so wrong to love something so right

To love all that we are
All that we ever felt a love to be
All that these two soul's can bare

So I gently lift my body out of the warmth

You giveth and I taketh when the time comes to be 

Love me
Love this me that can only be wrapped around this you 

We can only see that this love was meant to be....
