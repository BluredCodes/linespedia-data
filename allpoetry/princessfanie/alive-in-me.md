Slowly...gently I walked into viewEyes glancing up and down at meCaressing meReally touching meTouching all of meBreath after breath all eyes meetSlowly moving my breath up and around all that makes him moanStoppingthen startingToo many emotions traveling through my mindWrapping my hand around all that I needFighting the pleasures of bodies fur filling all we needReaching that peak inside meNever Turning backNever stoppingKeeping every living feeling alive in me....
