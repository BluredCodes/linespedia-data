I sometimes can't help wonder if these stars will ever disappear in our eyes

This glow will forever glisten in every dark night and shine bright every waking morning holding each other

It awakens all the mystery
It guides us
It supplies us with the warmth we need to feel on cold winter mornings
And leads us to the ocean water to soak our selves in on hot summer days

We hold everything here
Even one another

Always finding the right atmosphere surrounding us

These stars connect us

These stars I have no doubt will forever guide us
