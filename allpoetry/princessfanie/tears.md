I gracefully walk past all this confusion 
I walk to a vacant room where no one can see these tears
These tears that are bleeding from my heart

Wanting no one to ever get close to this heart of mine

I place these thorns around this heart of mine
So no one can find this heart of mine
So no one can find an open heart to prey upon

I take this body of mine for a long walk outside this vacant room

I consume my body with fresh air 
Waves hitting my feet as I walk along this ocean floor 
Waiting for some answers to come ashore....
