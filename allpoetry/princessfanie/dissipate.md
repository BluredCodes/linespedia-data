Breakdown these bars that surround this dayThis day filled with anger that comes too many times in one dayConsuming thoughts of what way to lead your heartThe steps I need to get away from the fear inside me at this momentMy heart never wants to feel this angerFeels like this volcano of hostility erupting in these eyes I stare intoWhere do we go from here....Why has life turned on us....Is this a sign to overcome this test that has been given to us....Should we let this go....Will the devil win at this test we try so hard to pass....Or will our faith make all this anger and evil dissipate....
