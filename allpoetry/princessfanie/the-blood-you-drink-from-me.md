---
title: "~The blood you drink from me~"
writer: "princessfanie"
slug: "the-blood-you-drink-from-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Drink my passionate blood

Make it become your own

Savor this taste forever

Drink only what I give to you

The warmth
The pain 
The love to tempt you

Love every drop as if there were none to savor
