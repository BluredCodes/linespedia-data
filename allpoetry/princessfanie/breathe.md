I crawl to the corner of my room
I curl this body of mine up and start to cry

What road led me here....
Why do a thousand voices speak to me at once....

I suffocate in this darkness that surrounds me

Someone turn this light on so I can breathe....
Someone walk me toward my destiny....

Show me new days of delight ....
Show me your sweetness like always....

I will let you hold all of me

Take me forever and I will give you me always

This warmth is a magnificent feeling 
And your love opens all corners of my soul

Thanks for your light which gives me the light I need to survive....
