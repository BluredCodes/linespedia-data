Give me the energy I need not to fail you

Clouds that circle over us

A little hard to see at times

I try to open my eyes
I try to feel all this pain at once
I try so hard for you

Too many walls around me
Running into dead ends
Knock down this wall

Don't fail me now

My entrance is never too far out of reach

Come closer

You will see clearly

The clouds will dissipate

I'm right here
I'm standing after all

Thanks for the energy you've given me to make it

To see sunny days with you after all....
