Listening to these melodies playing in my head

Hearing voices and words that seem to make so much sense of my life

Why has it come at this time....
How do these songs play such an instrumental part of the way I live now....

No answers for me to discuss with myself 
Just atonishing melodies that I listen to day after day

Waiting to follow the right song that plays the right tune in my head
Only waiting for that day to make me so right from these melodies
