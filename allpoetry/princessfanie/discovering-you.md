title: "Discovering You"

Can I teach you how to cry my tears....
Can I teach you how to hide your fears....
Come with me and I will show you change
Take these hands and that reach for you and hold tight 
I need no reason
I need no excuse
To be all what you need me to be
Let those negative feelings dissipate 
Walk my way for a while
Learn to live freely
Control all your thoughts
Don't let them control you
Open your eyes fully to the beauty all around you and within
Discover new stars to chase
Never forget to not let my hand go along the way
Because I want to discover and show you all new things that can be
The good things that can come from you and me....
