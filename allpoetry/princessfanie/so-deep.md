title: "So Deep"
writer: "princessfanie"

Will I catch my breath....
Will you catch my fall....
Will you catch my broken heart as it falls....

So into you
So into your world

Mesmirized so many times in one day

Cover me with roses

Take me into this night

Lead me to all your desires

Unfold you...to me

Your carreses are so consuming

You take all of me in your arms
You lay me down
Look deep into my soul
You slowly lay your lips over mine so gently

I'm hoplessly crazy in love with who you are

What you do to me inside

How I climb mountains in me to see your face

Every morning 
Every day
Every night

You are all of that and more to me

I love you for loving me
