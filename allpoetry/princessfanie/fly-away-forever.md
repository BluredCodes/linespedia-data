Take me down this dangerous roadLet me taste the danger beneathYours hands always pull me to safetySafe is where I'll be with youYou hold me so closeMy fears subside in your arms Here is where I want to stayI'm trying to convince myself that this is all I want to believe inThat I need this I need you I love youWe run into such familiar thingsWill we walk around and wonder what else we will find togetherOur soul's have collidedSo push away this pain of mineLead meGrow with meEntwine our emotions and desiresFeel what this has become
