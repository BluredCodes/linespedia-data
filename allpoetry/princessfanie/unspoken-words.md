I'm hereI'll always be hereAlthough I feel I am drowning somedays..You find meYou lift my life upYou see all of me when I'm not to sure  what I see in myselfYou love meLife pulls us aroundToo many rulesMust we hurt insideShould we let go of thisWhy do unspoken words cloud up our daysDo we run from these days in fear of letting goLetting go of the true hope...That keeps our web entangledThat keeps us breathingAnd that hope which keeps us warm in this cold world we walk in day today
