Sitting here thinking of our love lostOur love discoveredAnd our love yet to come each new day we look at the sun seeping thru the clouds outsideWe take each new day with every new breath of air that our bodies exhaleHolding on to this faith that has united our soulsNot many discover what we haveSome chosen to truly know what real love is......So hold on to these hands that mend into yours so perfectAnd don't resist all that can beThat will beBecause in our eyes we see our souls that love more of each other And will let no spaces in between block our way to the path we walk down every new seen filled morning and every night we hold each other to become the real us
