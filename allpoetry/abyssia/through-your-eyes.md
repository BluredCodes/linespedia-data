---
title: "Through Your Eyes"
writer: "Abyssia"
slug: "through-your-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4379-Through-Your-Eyes-by-Abyssia"
---

I see myself through your eyes, 
But without recognition.
For what I see before me
Is a girl 
With many dreams,
But no aspiration.
A soul 
Filled with love,
But which knows no commitment.
A mind
That is strong,
But a will that is weak.
And a heart
That has been reaped 
Of joy by things of the past.

I see myself through your eyes,
But do not recognize -

For to me,
My dreams
Have been realized through you.
My soul 
Is filled with a love 
That goes beyond mere commitment.

I find that it is my will that is strong,
While my mind weakens more
With your every doubt of my love for you.

And while you are correct,
That the joy in my heart
Has all but vanished...
It is not things of the past,
But things of the present
That have done the reaping. 

My heart has cried out to you,
But you have yet to hear its meaning. 

And I am slowly running out of tears.
