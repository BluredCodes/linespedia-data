Your Words -The gentle touch Of one fingertip,Grazing the surfaceOf the calm waters.My Heart -Stopping, for one Heartfelt moment to Experience the sensationAs the ripples flow.My Mind -Breaking with unblemished bliss.Your Smile -The pull that holds it together. Our love is the ocean.Inspired by and written for Kevin... (not the webmaster Kevin.. my boyfriend, duh. lol)
