Here -

Words shine like 
No stars could.
Touched by life
You breathe into me
And all I need is...

Gone.

You take away my essence.
Waiting becomes an occupation -
Payment is a luxury.
Expectations grow sour.
My fondness lies...

Here.Might add more later, but... eh. Here it is.
