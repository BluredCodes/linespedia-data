Ride on the wave -Let it take over.Roll down the highwayAs it leads you to...Nowhere.These walls seem much higherThan they did when I left.Take it in.Tomorrow never diesTo the eternal optimist.My past never dies...I wish it would.Chew it up.So many times you callTo drown yourself in my sorrows. Swallow it down.Reality creeps up my walkway, But only during the daylight hours.Bathe me in your liesSo I can be whole again.Let it sink in.Tell me I have problemsTo forget about your own;Mine seem distantSo I deal with them...Another day.Give it up.Thanx tommyjoe :)
