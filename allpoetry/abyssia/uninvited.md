---
title: "Uninvited"
writer: "Abyssia"
slug: "uninvited"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4421-Uninvited-by-Abyssia"
---

You say with bated breath
Your eyes shine only for me.
Would they though,
Shine so bright
If they saw what I see?

Starlight, star bright
On all the stars I see tonight
I wish I could
In God's own sight
Shed your blood to make it right.

As those dark eyes drink me in
Unknowing of my would-be sin
You tell me now, face set in stone
That your heart beats for me alone.

I wonder now as you draw near
Despite contempt and shedded tear
If through your chest
My claws should rip
And in them beating flesh I grip

Would it still beat for me?

Starlight, star bright
On all the stars I see tonight
I wish I could
In God's own sight
Shed your blood to make it right. 

Your eyes no longer in my view
I feel your weight come crashing through
Cries of outrage are denied
You said you loved me
I say you lied.

The moon above weeps for my pain
The sky is falling... 
No that's just rain.
Fuck those stars that still shine bright,
Like taunting children in the night.

(Nah nah nah nah nah nah)

One unclean hand runs through my hair
To comfort me...
As if you care. 

Starlight, star bright
On all the stars I see tonight
I wish I could
In God's own sight
Shed your blood to make it right.

In my ear your whispers drawl,
Then the gleaming blade I saw -
I shuddered at the cross you drew..
And licked
And traced
And then prayed to.
Between my breasts 
A sacreligious scar
Reminding me just how sick you are.

Your haunting words -
'No one will see..'
Or believe what you've done to me.
By the light of a blood moon 
Your black eyes dance
Mine would too, had I the chance
To do exactly as I see fit
What can I say?
