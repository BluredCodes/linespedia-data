Tonight my soul caught fire
And with the touch of your hand  
Two souls became one,
Melting away sorrow
And welding together the bodies
Of two so opposite, yet so similar.
With lust breathing hotly on my neck,
And dark passions slowly embracing me,
You taught me to love again
In such a way that I have never loved before.
With open eyes, you saw me for who I am,
Who I longed to be,
And who I pretended to be...
And yet, you loved me regardless.Okay... maybe its not so erotic, but just in case :)
