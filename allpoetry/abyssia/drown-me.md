Thick, pure, choking
As it surrounds me.
I drown a little more
With each gasp.

One can only take
So much virtuousity.

Make me holy, make me sane
Baptized by bubbles that
Threaten my life ....
Drink and be saved.

My sins are lost within
The depths of the wholesome.

Too much to handle,
I swallow what I can.
Liquid salvation --
Soured by my presence.

Christen me in diluted milk
And suffocate my soul.Cooooorny! :)
