---
title: "Symphony"
writer: "Abyssia"
slug: "symphony"

---

Speak to me with your smile -

Touch my being with your gentle chords.
Let them linger on my lips, 
A sweet melody to lift me higher
As your song brings flowers to my cheek.
Your music brings me closer to
The Higher Power that I worship. 

Your symphony caresses my skin
And enlightens my soul.

Your strains vibrate 
Off my canyon walls
And break into the clouds above -
Bringing honey to my salted tears
As they streak down my face
And kiss the ground that holds me

Those soft lyrics were the cure
For my lifelong drought.
