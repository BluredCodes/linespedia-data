My first genuine smile
Always under your influence.
Past cautions thrown out
As you approach with such
Eagerness. 

Mutual curiosity
Stimulates my being,
Regenerates my soul,
And once again
I can exhale.

Listening as you speak
That language I could 
Never comprehend;
Watching as you move
With a gaiety I never knew.

Blessed by the awe
That you chose to 
Share it with me.
