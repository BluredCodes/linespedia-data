Valium could not cureThe insomnia you inspire.Our late night conversationsReplay themselves Over...And over...And over again...A broken recordStuck on my favorite line of the song -I wish I knew who that singer was.Whispers leave me breathless.Your smile is so contagiousA virus to my ill-lit worldExterminate the  shadowsTo replace them with a content glow.Kisses leave me weak.My pulse racesAt every gentle touchA violent rush of shivers When you touch my hair. You intoxicate me.Dedicated to my dearest friend skeletor ;) Let me know what you think. (by the way... I'm not sure if it's totally finished or not. time will tell!)
