Tonight the world made a crash landing,
Leaving me bare for all to see.
Even the shadows of night could not
Protect me from my nakedness,
My impurity.

Gawking stares of judgement,
Reflected in the eyes of those
Who could never understand
Tore through my heart, my mind
To leave an unhealing wound.

Formerly a goddess, striped down
To worse than nothing --
A mutant, a whore, and unworthy
Of forgiveness, of love, or even a 
Cloth to hide behind. 

Run for your lives, save the women and children; 
The beast will suck you in, chew your bones, 
And spit you out again -- for nothing has changed 
Really, except for the illusion.
Once a siren, now spawn of Satan.

But you -
When the rest ran for cover
Your eyes saw no change.
You knew from the beginning ...
And loved me regardless.The moral of the story: hold on to those who will accept you for who you really are, and screw the rest.
