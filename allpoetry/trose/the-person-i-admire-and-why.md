---
title: "The Person I Admire and Why"
writer: "Trose"

I know someone who has accomplished a lot in life
But not without his share of trouble and strife
You see...he will not let things get in his way
Or even putting things off for another day

He knows more things will come for tomorrow
When one project is done another will follow
He seeks ways to assist people in anyway he can
By sharing his ideas so others can understand

He is someone who'll talk to anyone about anything
To get them to smile he would even begin to sing
He has showed me that not everything is bad
By taking things in good stride instead of getting mad

I've known him for years and am glad to say
It is great to see or hear him each and everyday
I hope to be there for him as much as he was for me
For he is the person I admire and someday hope to be
