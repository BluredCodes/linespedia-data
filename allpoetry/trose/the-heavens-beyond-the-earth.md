---
title: "The Heavens Beyond the Earth"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "the-heavens-beyond-the-earth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5825-The-Heavens-Beyond-the-Earth-by-Trose"
---

In this world we live in there is pain and anger
There are people who experience torment and hunger
In everyday life, we deal with depression and ridicule
While trying to remember that our Lord in Heaven rules

There are days when we feel everything has gone bad
We must realize that Christianity isn't just a fad
When we feel that Satan has taken all control
But a strong link to the Lord will keep our lives whole

Just to imagine for a moment the peace that we all need
By giving our pains to the Father, then this we could receive
That the love of the Lord would come down to comfort you
He'll stay with us to the end no matter what we do

Even though we live in the world in which we can see
Doesn't mean the world is it for you and me
It's through peace and growth after our spiritual birth
That we can see the "Heavens Beyond the Earth"
