Is this something we have that is our own?

- detour of roads
- what makes us mad
- work dress code
- what makes us sad

Being told our attitude can not be condoned.

- high tempature of the day
- cool air from the wind
  if I can't have my way
  should I just give in?This was thought of due to the actions of everyday activities. Traveling to work, dealing with the workforce, friends that help and hurts us at times. And yes even the weather that control some of our plans.
