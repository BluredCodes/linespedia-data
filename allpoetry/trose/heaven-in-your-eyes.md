---
title: "Heaven in Your Eyes"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "heaven-in-your-eyes"

On a lonely night I'm thinking of you
And how by myself I feel so blue
I look for you in my arms again
So to give you all the love I can

There are days when all seem wrong
You keep telling me it won't be long
When better things will come our way
You always know just what to say

Having you near me means so much
It gets me anticipating every touch
You show up like an angel in the sky
I'm seeing Heaven in your eyes

The sun rises to the calling of your name
The feeling you give erases all my pain
It doesn't matter if we are on the phone
Each sound of your voice says I'm not alone

Many thoughts keep rushing in my head
From the time I wake until I go to bed
Along with you comes lots of joy and laughter
And with you nothing else even matters

there's nothing closer than you in my heart
That's the way it has been from the start
Hearing the music from which you specialize
I'm looking up to see Heaven in Your EyesThis is also dedicated to my wife. She plays the music in my heart.
