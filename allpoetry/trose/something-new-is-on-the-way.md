The clock is ticking down
And the time is getting close
for everyone to gather around
And join in for a toast

There is a little one on its way
A baby girl to touch my heart
I'll look at her face as she lays
As tears of joy will begin to start

This baby I helped to create
Through the love for my wife
All of which was worth the wait
And will cherish for all of my lifeThis is about my baby girl I'm expecting in around 5 weeks.
