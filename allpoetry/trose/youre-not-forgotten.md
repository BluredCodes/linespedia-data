I know I am gone a lot from time to time
But I don't forget the sweetest mother is mine
She's been with me through thick and thin
And always tried to see the good within

There were moments I've shouted and got mad
And you did the same when I was being bad
But these are not the only moments we have had
Not remembering all the good times would be sad

You've tried to do the best thing for me
With me being a kid, that was hard to see
I've had many friends and bonds that were tight
You're my best friend who's been there all my life

Yes, years may go by and several things change
But my love for you will definitely be the same
Though with your Cokes you may be spoiled rotten
I wanted to remind you that, 'You're Not Forgotten'I know that the thing about Coke (the drink) may seem odd, but my mother could not get up in the morning without a Coke and a cigarette. I was the one who always got up and got her an Ice-cold drink. It is something so simple for someone who has done lifetime achievements when dealing and working with me.
