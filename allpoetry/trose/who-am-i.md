---
title: "Who am I"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "who-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the person that 

comes in out of nowhere

where I go and what I do 

not many people care



Sometimes I get lost 

as though I was in a maze

Other times I sit down 

and stare off in space



I go to places trying 

to fit in wherever I can

No phone calls do I receive 

and nor can I understand



Is it because I'm different,

unknown,or that I'm new

No, it has been years and 

my friends are still the few



They've grown up together 

ever since they were small

But having me feel left out, 

isn't right at all



I do all that I can short of 

standing on my head

And holding my breath until 

my face had turned red.



So you ask me,'What in the world 

is it that I can do

I reply,'Include me in activities 

I like to have fun, too!'



Then the bonds of friendship 

would grow a little tighter

And I will become someone other 

than just an outsider.
