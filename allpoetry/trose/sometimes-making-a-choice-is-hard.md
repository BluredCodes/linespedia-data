Deciding what to eat 
or what you're in the mood for
Or when a lady approaches 
to open the door

Who to make friends with 
that won't stab you in the back
Someone to be there 
when it is happiness you lack

Deciding where to work when 
there's problems everywhere
And finding a good job 
has become extremely rare

Sex at this time 
is something to worry about
With all the diseases 
that are easy to route

People worry if they want 
this computer or that one
When some can't afford one 
to join in on the fun

To me I have preferred for 
others to tell the truth
And anything other than that 
I find to be rude

Decisions getting harder between 
value over quality or quality over value
And then some people make decisions 
quickly and stick to it too

***But this I want you 
to decide in just a few***

When something hurts you so bad 
that you want to get rid of it, 
but at the same time you have to sacrifice 
you ever being able to feel love again?

***What do you do?***I cared for someone at one time in which I had a song to remember her by. As it turned out she didn't want to be with me. Every time I heard the song I remembered how good it felt to be kissed by her, but at the end I would be in tears knowing that she left me. I could get rid of the bad memory by quit listening to the song, but I'd also be giving up on love by forgetting the kiss.   ~ Tony
