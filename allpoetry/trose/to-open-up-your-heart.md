It's not the same, all of my feelings have changedEver since I met you my sensitivity's changed me tooYou turned my life around and lifted me off the groundI'd run a hundred miles just to see your lovely smileI'm headed on looking for my own destinationWell it's you, God's masterpiece of CreationPlease don't ever think we'll have to be apartAnd try to find a way to open up your heartSomedays I paint otherdays I singAbout all of the joys I hope you'll bringEverytime I'm down I always look to youI pray you feel the same as I always doI'm headed on looking for my own destinationWell it's you, God's masterpiece of CreationPlease don't ever think we'll have to be apartAnd try to find a way to open up your heartThis is actually a short song I wrote. Let me know what you think.
