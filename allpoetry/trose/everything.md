---
title: "Everything"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "everything"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6434-Everything-by-Trose"
---

To wake up in the morning to see a smiling face
Makes me glad that God put us here in the same place
You're someone I get close to when love is on my mind
Someone that is so special I know you're one of a kind

If something is on my mind, I am willing to tell you
And when saying, "I love you" you know it's the truth
You have my mind thinking of ways to be a better man
this, I like to do for you and hope you understand

I'd travel the world many times over to get your attention
Your love means more than anything cooked in the kitchen  =)
Your love and thoughts are just a few things you do bring
I hope to find a way to show that YOU are my everything


Dedicate to my wife, Renae @-)-)--
