New Job

I want a job with people who notice the work I do
I do all that I can and still do extra too !

More Money

I'd love money to do more of the things I like
From going to the movies to restaurants for a bite

New Home

To have a bigger home for my family to live in
If only that lottery would have allowed me to win


***None of these can keep me from getting what I need more.
   Having a healthly family that out weighs the entire world.***
