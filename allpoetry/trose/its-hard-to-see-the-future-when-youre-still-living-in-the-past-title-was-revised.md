Everyday I watch others taking things as they come
And forgetting the past as if there was none
I'm glad for another day to experience new things
To accept and handle the problems life has to bring

To realize the beauty & wonders of the earth and skies
Makes me feel guilty as though I have passed it by
The troubles of the past are the same as today
No solution in sight is the reason for how I behave

I have got on my knees and prayed many times
When I was done all I could do is ask, "Why?"
And many prayers I have heard other people ask
Makes what I want seem like a minute task

There have been many years that have passed me by
And with the times I have had many deep sighs
Holding on to small memories that make me feel good
And knowing I did absolutely all that I could

To be wanted by someone that I could give my love
A person that understands and will lend out a hug
But with the past in my way nothing else can I see
Including the answers possibly waiting there for me
