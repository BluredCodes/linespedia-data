title: "Love is..."
writer: "Trose"

Love is something to be pursued
Love is something not to be misused
Love is something that makes the world go round
Love is something to lift you when you are down

Love is something that needs to be shared
Love is something strong between those who care
Love is something that is felt only in the heart
Love is something that can give others another start

Love is something that can bring us together
Love is something needed from January to December
Love is something that runs deeper that any pain and strife
Most of all, love is something that can create a new life.
