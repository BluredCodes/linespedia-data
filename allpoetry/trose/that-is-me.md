---
title: "That is Me"
writer: "Trose"

Here's something 

that's good to know 

I tell things as they are 

and that's no joke 



I listen to one music genre 

and then another 

Even listening to music more 

than my own mother 



At times I'm calm 

and fine with everything 

When I'm in a great mood 

I begin to sing 



Then dark clouds start 

to form in my head 

These are the days 

I should stay in bed 



Chatting with people 

who are out in a crowd 

Even talk with a stranger 

for crying out loud 



When I talk it may be 

about fun and laughter 

I may be serious and get 

to the heart of the matter 



Some will think this is 

weird and strange 

I assure people I'm okay 

and not deranged 



There is no need of 

figuring out indeed 

This is who I am , 

I hope you can see 



This is me.
