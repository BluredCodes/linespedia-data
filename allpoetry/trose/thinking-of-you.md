---
title: "Thinking of You"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "thinking-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Though we are miles apart
I think of you with all my heart
And the times I wish to be with you
In hopes to prove how my love is true

Wishing to be with you during the day
And dream of you throughout the night
There is so much that I want to say
For when I'm with you everything is okay

There were the times it was a friend I did need
It has been you that I have come to see
I wish there was something else I could do
but just to say, "I'm Thinking of You"
