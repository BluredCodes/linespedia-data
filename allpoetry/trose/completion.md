title: "Completion"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "completion"
source: "AllPoetry"

LOVE
JOY
HAPPINESS
SATISFACTION
TO FEEL NEEDED
HOPE
ENCOURAGEMENT
HUMOR
PRAISED
COMFORTED


THIS IS WHAT I NEED TO FEEL COMPLETE...
