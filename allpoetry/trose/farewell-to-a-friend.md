title: "Farewell to a Friend"
writer: "Trose"

He has been a man of humor to lighten up my day
Also showing the light by paving the way
He opens the door and finds new opportunities
And what happens next we'll have to wait and see

I will miss seeing him and that is for sure
Hoping his future challenges are easy to endure
A man who can do anything he strives to do
May the roadblocks in his way be only a few

Whether it be jokes, sports, or one of many things
It is always a good conversation he has to bring
And in all hopes that this will not be the end
For I am now giving my best farewell to a friend



(Dedicated to Parnell Guyton a good friend)
