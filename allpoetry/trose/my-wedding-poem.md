This day is different from any other I have livedFor I am marrying the woman I love with all my heartAnd anything I have she wants I'm willing to giveThese are some of the feelings I've felt from the startIt's true the degree of love is hard to expressAnd the only way I know is through the things I doI don't do this a lot and and I hate to confessBut I guarantee my love will always remain trueThinking of this day has made me feel wonderfulTo have someone special in my life who loves meA person I'll spend my life with and care forTo my last breath living for her as she lives for meThis is just to let you know this is what I read to my wife in front of everyone at our wedding.
