---
title: "Remember me"
writer: "Trose"
slug: "remember-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When you lay down in bed
Just to rest your head
Think of the words I have said
And remember me

When you stay up late at night
Watching every movie in sight
Wishing we could hold each other tight
Please remember me

When you take a long drive
Drinking caffeine to feel alive
I dream of your heart next to mine
Try to remember me

Every time you look at the sky
And notice a shooting star go by
Know it's your love that lifts me high
And take the time to remember me

There is a truth you can truly believe
That the only thing in this world I need
Is for you to always be the one I see
Just that and for you to remember me

                  ~TLR
