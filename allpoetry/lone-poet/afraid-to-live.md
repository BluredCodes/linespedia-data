---
title: "Afraid to Live"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "afraid-to-live"
source: "AllPoetry"

This heart weighs heavy, an iron reel 
harder than marbled fieldstone 
bitter shards of cold steel, 
nerves brittle as severed bone 

inferno burns higher, 
heartache and greed 
unforsaken human desire, 
pyre's smoldering flames we feed 

pain shears the breath of life
cuts like a stiletto dagger, 
sharp, jaggedly edged knife
shoved deep down with anger

heaven and hell intermesh 
death chips at stubborn pride, 
rips at deadened clay flesh 
tearing your soul from inside

love melts icy veins, 
frozen block of liquid tears 
thaws out, turning to rain, 
washes away hidden fears 

panic looms, struggling to stay sane, 
dreamer screaming to be set free
light succumbs to its darkest hour,
embark upon an eternal journey on fate's A-train
tightly bound by the holiest power of three
