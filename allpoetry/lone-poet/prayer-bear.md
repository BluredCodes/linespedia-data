My little Prayer Bear 
You bring good will to all, 
You’re there to ease my fear
And catch me when I fall 

A sweet expression on your face 
That reminds me of the human race, 
How one day we’ll leave this place 
For a new world, somewhere out in space 

My caring angelic bear, 
Golden halo upon your head 
Thank you for always being there, 
Through the darkness I was led

Blessed amongst us feel love’s grace, 
While evil just turns old and dissolves
For me your wings offer a tender embrace, 
Hold me tightly in soft bearskin gloves 

Guard our flag within furry paws, 
Keep our land safe in God's hands 
Please shelter our worried souls, 
And help everybody to understand 

Let go of the violence, hate and greed
Roaming freely on this infertile Earth, 
Prayer bear, don’t let hell's demon seed 
Steal what’s left of our self-worth 


(c) 10/01, 3/04 SmSI gave a little white bear w/gold halo to my hubby when he got his appendix removed.  His paw says "hope" on it. When the war began, I took one of those little mini flags and stuck it between his praying paws.
