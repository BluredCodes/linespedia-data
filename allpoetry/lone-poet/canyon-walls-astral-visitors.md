title: "Canyon Walls (Astral Visitors)"
writer: "Lone Poet"

These great ramparts, sacredly twined canyon walls
break forth in echoes, decibles capture all space and time
an indelible resonance encompasses magnetic souls,
abstract ravines impart no purpose, reason, or internal rhyme

behind inviting walls beats a crude, but virtual titanium heart
a 25th century machine built to visit and study mortals
by three-dimensional travel, billions of light years apart,
alien marvels free to come and go between interstellar portals

imprinted upon Destiny's steamy cauldron,
amongst a lifeless crematorium of saurian geckos
temporal dreams of aeons long gone, annulled,
prehistoric raptor's resurrection beckons

beyond hollowed ages-old canyon walls
blue boulders, crystalline spirals
smoldering dark dawn of Mankind recalls
archaic, golden sandstorm whirls

sagebrush arid as a southwesterly wind,
desert hot embers breathe a rushing wildfire
barren cliffs and prairies, grey hawk spirits rescind,
dust bowl inferno's deathly sparks rage higher

imagine a futuristic world, someplace
where time has no start, ending or limits
picture an ultra smart, homonid race,
Gods of Sirius, advanced beings infinite

nature's celestial enigmas; encrypted holograms, opuses, oil paintings
hieroglyphs solidly entombed within pronged subterranean caverns,
beneath unearthly sentient ruins, desiccated clay soil lies untainted
war cries of the ancient mavens, arcana's songs nevermore chanted
