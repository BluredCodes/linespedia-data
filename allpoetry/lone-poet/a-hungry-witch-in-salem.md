There lived a young lass in Salem, Mass 
who dressed morbidly, always in black, 
Elvira and trusty cat familiar, Sassafras 
hungered for bratty kids as a midnight snack

She and her coveted ebony furball 
just moved into an old haunted house, 
her pointy hat had giant claw holes 
that rebel feline thought 'twas a mouse 

One day Elvira threw a big party 
a ploy for rich folks living in town, 
she hired some naive guy named Marty 
to serve as bartender, DJ and clown 

12 o’clock, b'witching hour, getting late 
her ambitious new employee was ready to rock, 
nobody arrived yet that evening to celebrate 
cheddar balls went sour, and still no knock; 

(as Elvira waited and waited and waited…) 

She wondered what she did so terribly wrong 
and was just trying to be a caring friend, 
so she created this magical bash to get along 
make up with locals, hurt feelings to mend 

The well-rehearsed raven-haired beauty pretended to cry 
when in burst a swarm of angry protestors and two cops, 
fearing the worst, she came up fast with a GREAT alibi 
to explain all the fresh blood on the food and soda pop 

Said she cut her hand at her flop housewarming party 
but an unneighborly snoop found an arm, ear, and thighbone, 
“She’s a child-eating witch!!”, screamed gossipy laborer Marty 
ran minus his paycheck, licking the top of an ice-cream cone 

Elvira’s only true crime, or to the world it seemed 
was that she enjoyed the taste of prime red human meat, 
she ate bad children, dined on hearts and kidneys steamed 
after picking out the fatty parts, heads, butts, and feet 

“Why were there bones in the trash can?”, asked that nosy snitch 
“I don’t know, but I did nothing wrong and you had no right to pry, 
said Elvira, “Everyone knows there’s no such thing as a REAL witch! 
I'm just hated for my unorthodox religion!”, was her brash reply;

(The mob stared intensely and left, Elvira's brows arched ever so sly) 

Her cockamamie story those peeping toms would never believe 
digging for all the proof needed to land her straight in jail,
what dark plan did this ruthless sexgoddess have up her gauzy sleeve? 
conjured an invisibility spell, mixed sleeping potion in the ale 

Cops interrogated her, drank a few glasses of beer and passed out 
awoke with hangovers early next morn, but Elvira appeared long gone, 
her cat hid under the bed, as policemen wandered aimlessly about 
gave up looking and began to leave, when a call rang on the phone 

Pathology report and DNA tests on the decoy bones were now back 
“We found them to be of animal origin”, came an officer's burly voice, 
meanwhile, witchy Elvira fixed her and Sassafras a yummy teatime snack 
everyone clueless as to why their entire town had no more noise; 

('cuz she devoured all its pain-in-the-ass little girls and boys!) 

CACKLE, CACKLE!!
