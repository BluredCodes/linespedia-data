Those stars I hate
Who am I to rate
What’s bad or great
Decide a poem’s fate

Inflated egos by the score
Yelling, “give me 5 more!”
Loyal followers you adore
Are being nice, nothing more

Fanny kissers and wannabes
Get high on poetic cannabis
Bonfire of rose-tinted vanities
Challenges our (in)sanities

No more stars for me
I’m happy with three
But some can’t see
A forest for the tree

From now on I will say
If I liked it or not, okay?
Comments mean little anyway
Unless you write just for play
