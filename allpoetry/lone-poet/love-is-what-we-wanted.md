If love is what you wanted 
then hate's what you will get 
if heartache is what was planned, 
you ain't seen nothing yet 

if happiness and caring 
was something on your mind 
then better look the other way, 
'cause a nightmare's all you'll find 

if promises of trust 
were true to each of us 
then sorry, everyone's mistaken, 
nothing here but sin and lust 

if love is what we all wanted 
then love is what we're not 
on your way to heaven, 
don't forget to rot
