A bell that goes ding dong 
I hear in my mind 
do I know what it means 
as it plays its sweet song? 

How strong its notes are 
its music takes flight 
angels from Heaven 
rejoice with delight 

Eternally serene 
a bell you should cherish 
almost like magic 
may it never perish 

Let us hear your chimes 
ring on and on and on!
