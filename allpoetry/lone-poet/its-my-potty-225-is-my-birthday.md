It’s my birthday potty
And I’ll cry if I want to,
Cry if I want to, cry if I want to…
You would cry too if it doodoo’d on you,
And it did, number 2!

Nobody knows where
My portable Johnny has gone,
But Judy’s cootie left the same time
Oh, why was she holding his pee can,
When he’s supposed to be mine!!

And I’ll cry if I want to,
Cry if I want to, cry if I want to…
You would cry too if it doodoo’d on you,
And I did, number 2!

Moody Judy and Mr. Clean Johnny 
Just walked naked thru the bathroom door,
Like a Queen on the porcelain throne with her King
Oh, what a “Shake Your Booty” birthday surprise,
Judy’s wearing his scummy toilet bowl ring!!
