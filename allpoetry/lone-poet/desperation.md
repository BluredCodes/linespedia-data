title: "desperation"

---

desperation is a sense of gloom

that overpowers you upon awakening

and you can't see the morning sky

because it's buried

beneath the dense fog

(of your brain)

you no longer smell the perfume

of the flowers in the garden

because they shriveled up and died

(from grief)

along with all your hopes and dreams        

soon you feel nothing but numbness 

because your body, mind and soul

(are unconscious)

anesthetized with a drug called fear

you'll do anything to get away

run away from the pain

and a sudden chill 

comes over you	 

(like ice)

its grip so tight

feels like you're frozen

(suffocating)

with the death grip of despair.
