Computers can be such a pain
and surely they're a hassle
it doesn't matter where they live
apartment, house or castle

You build them back up
yet once again they fall apart
whether they're from CompUSA
Radio Shack or Kmart

Computers play tactics with your mind
it's a logical game of Solitaire
but when they stop playing or grinding
you are not to unfairly blame

Modems and hard drives and mice, oh my
some are cream of the crop, dirt cheap
and some are junk, lemons priced way too high
floppies and printers and video cards
what one person keeps, another discards

Kids can work them
so can a supreme chimp
if I let a monkey outsmart me
I must really look like a wimp

There are books for "Dummies"
and all sorts of insider's tips
that makes idly surfing the Net much easier
than losing those widened hips

PC's can be lots of fun
if you know your stuff
but you might feel like giving up
when learning your ABC's gets too rough

You have to put them in their place
and show 'em who's the boss
'cause computers think they know it all
Windows or MS-DOS

So keep on clicking
your time has come
to go high-tech
XT to Pentium.
