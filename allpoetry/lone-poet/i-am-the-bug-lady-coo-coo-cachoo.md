It was held atop the playful lady's hand 
I saw as she lifted a grand legged spider, 
Afraid, I shrieked and ran, begging her to hide 'er 
tried meekly to stay and bravely withstand, 
gave a swift peek, me, this naive warrior fighter 

Three days after, I had a scary dream, 
upon my left hand was spied a hairy bug 
Cried out in alarm, half dazed, so it may seem, 
into the nightstand my arm mightily swung 

My unconscious mind awakened from the bed 
to find my hand swollen, red and in severe pain, 
A make-believe voice inside my head laughed and said: 
"Better never see a fake rubber spider again!" 

 
 

(c) 6/01, 3/04 SmSYes, this really happened. Went to a Ren. Faire, lady had a big old rubber spider on her hand. Days later, had a vivid dream and it was on my hand, so I flung my hang into the nighttable (trying to shake it off) and I ended up w/sprained hand! Moral: Don't put the nightstand too close to your bed and stay away from ladies w/bugs!
