I believe in fairies
not an ounce of greed
I believe in fairies
there for us in need

magical children of delight
wings flutter about the night
heavenly beings of starlight
hush babe, have no more fright

I believe in fairies
they will never die
I believe in fairies
for they believe in you and I


(c) 2.7.02 SmSI just wrote this as a song. I can't write music, just lyrics. A little tune came into my head so I'm dedicating this to SweetSadness and all the fairy lovers of the world. If anyone wants to add the music to it, feel free, would be gr8 for a harp or flute maybe.
