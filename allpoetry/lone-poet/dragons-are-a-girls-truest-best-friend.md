Dragons are a girl’s truest best friend 
'cause their patient love will never end 
against dark forces they fiercely defend 
alas, Draco’s broken heart may not mend 

he needs to be treated with kindness 
bow upon your knees and show respect 
like you would treat Her Highness 
kiss him gently on his scaly neck

one bad word behind his back of royalty
he'll maybe attack, come push or shove 
thus you must prove your loyalty 
a dragon senses your every move 

so be sure you’re of the sincere type
they're smarter than the average computer 
dragons are clever, know truth from hype
tho bears have fur, reptilians are cuter 

as long as you adore your flying dragon 
his hardy affection will always be strong 
just don’t ride upon him like a winged wagon 
dragging him to every party or club you belong 

most dragons like females much better 
for girls are sweeter than stubborn boys 
and enjoy writing mushy love letters 
they also have way cooler play toys 

boys tell lies, whine, and start fights
kill people and animals, and go off to wars 
prized older dragons are moody, but very bright 
don’t devour knights in shining armor anymore 

dragons, boldly striving to aid the world 
going out of their way to lend maidens a hand 
knights slay only cowardly ones with a silvery sword 
hence, these mild creatures finally understand 

they know sometimes we have to fight 
to keep all our freedoms and civil rights 
dragons are made of magical starlight 
giant tails glow, like fireflies at night 

they think mortal guys are dumb 
and don’t use their time too wisely 
smoke, overeat, drink coke and rum 
don’t treat the sick and poor nicely 

untamed and selfish, man doesn't care 
about famine, ill health, or any other race 
will naively do anything on a dare 
maybe bleed or die just to save face 

human girls aren’t quite as greedy, or cold 
neither are they all cinnamon sugar and spice 
some would rather buy diamonds and 14k gold 
than feed starving children fish and rice 

overall, dragons prefer innocent girls 
even if they can’t keep wishes or secrets 
that’s okay, dragons don’t wear curls 
as for their buddies, there are no regrets 

a dragon is a girl’s bestestest of prided friends 
through thick or thin, he'll win your trusting soul
and be there for you to confide in time and again 
then will give his life to catch you when you fall 

he'll quickly run to your side when you call 
wipe your tears away with a comforting dry paw 
and try to cheer you up if you feel lonely or small 
but don’t ever hurt a sly dragon, for he remembers all
