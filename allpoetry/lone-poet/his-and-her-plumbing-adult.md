A man and woman
are not the same
even though both human
and share a name

plumbing systems are different
males carry a faucet, females do not
a man's hairy chest is flatter in front
his skin is fatter, she just has a cunt

guys have a lubed weiner
gals have 2 boobs and a hole
guys are so much meaner
'cause they also got 2 nut balls

so when your drain impolitely overflows
and you can't stop that nightmarish pee
call Roto Neuter and I dare you to show 
your hooters, bet repairs will be free!
