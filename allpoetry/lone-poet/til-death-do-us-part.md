title: "'Til Death Do Us Part"

I loved you then
I still love you now
Until forever is when
I will keep my vow

To honor and cherish
“Til death do us part”
May breath never perish
In mind, soul or heart

You were my first
You’ll be my last
For better or worse
Put behind the past

Look to tomorrow
For brighter skies
Pain and sorrow
Dry your eyes

No rainy showers
Here comes the sun
Destiny is ours
To become one

Cupid’s bow stands still
The hands of time froze
I promise I always will
Treasure you as a rose
