title: "Merlin's Days (and Knights)"

Merlin, of a fairy birth, such wondrous enchantment he brought, 
lucid revelations, mystical secrets, that great victory was fought 
magnificent wizard, alchemist, prophet from a distant time and place, 
of a kingdom ruled with honour, Grail's quest reflected upon his face 
                             
So many long aeons ago he proudly tread majestic, foreign land, 
Camelot shielded by gallant, noble knights until its final stand 
empowered the gleaming sword Arthur carried and led him to the throne, 
legend has it the prince's mighty hands drew Excalibur out from stone 

Mesmerized fair damsels peered into intense impassioned eyes, 
tales of dragons, haunted forests, golden fortresses amidst paradise 
in a glittering cloak of royal hues, echoing divine incantations, 
Middle ages once home to chivalry and otherworldly reincarnations 

Beheaded apparitions swept medieval castles, dungeons wreaked of death, 
eeire visions of what lies beyond, chanted with every waking breath 
as was since the beginning, he let sorcery be guided by the heart, 
preordained a son born to be king would be given his rightful start 

Gaze deeply through your magic crystal and tell all you foresee,
what twist of fate beholds mankind that shall unlock its destiny; 
sacred wisdom, mysteries of pasts, to ourselves we must remain true, 
omniscience revealed to those deemed worthy, but a fortunate few 

Swore vengeance on spilt blood, spread word of a young heir to defend, 
paid the bitter price, romantic fool, claimed by deceipt in the end 
ladylove captured his tricks of the trade and exiled him to a cave, 
imprisoned there for an eternity, ageless, as her virtual slave 

I beckon you, emerge from your nirvana, and electrify your wand, 
keep strong and brave, drink of freedom's nectar, save the mortal bond 
Stonehenge ruins now buried in myth, destroyed by greed and war, 
what tragedy befell the ancient ones, they gathered there no more 

The future revolves in perpetual motion, paths never cross the same, 
form the circle, charge the runes, break forth from whence you came 
raise your arms to lift the curse, massive energy flows within, 
summon the higher forces that be, the timeline's wearing thin 

I feel his life force leave me spellbound, illusions tease my mind, 
master of a phantom art, memories of battles and dreams left far behind; 
an infinite, spiraling spectrum of glass encompasses space and matter, 
seek your mirror's doorway to the vast unknown, hurry, lest it shatter 

A brilliant aura illuminates the heavens, jasmine scents the air, 
inner essence of harmony and stardust, and a charma forever rare 
his dark world's a mere celestial haven for the everlasting soul, 
an image lacking earthly substance, yet spiritually he is whole 

Merlin, Merlin, turn to your Book of Shadows, 
for it's said that only the Shadow knows 
what lurks in the hearts of men, 
but then again...... 

The Shadow couldn't know what lurks inside of yours. 

Godspeed!
