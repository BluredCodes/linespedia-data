---
title: "I’m Pretty, Sort of"
writer: "Lone Poet"

I may not be a star or beauty queen
But I do have charisma, a special charm,
So I’m no model on the cover of “Seventeen”
Yet for some reason guys, like bees, swarm

I'm almost good enought to eat
'Tho my front teeth have a few gaps,
My cute smile looks honey-sweet
Wish they were whiter, one needs a cap

My locks are fine and short, reddish brown
I dyed my hair to give body and cover grays,
Hazel eyes are almond shaped, nice and round
I wear glasses, so nearsighted I'm in a haze

Maybe I'm a little chunky through the middle
Yet I still look sexy in dark blue and black,
Crazy as a monkey, I like to write poetic riddles
And sing karaoke, unless I have an asthma attack

I guess I’m sort of pretty
In my own unique way,
I’m fun and quite witty
What more can I say?
