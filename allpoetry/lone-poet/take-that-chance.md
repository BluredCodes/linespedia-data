What is music if no one dances,
what is life if one takes no chances?

given time, skill, and the desire
anyone can sing, rhyme or inspire

will is the strength behind the soul
courage fulfills destiny at an arm's length,
body and mind, in depth, become whole

take a chance,
win or lose
he who tries, Genie grants
a handsome prize, you must choose
