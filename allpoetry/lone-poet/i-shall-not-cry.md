---
title: "I Shall Not Cry"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "i-shall-not-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42641-I-Shall-Not-Cry-by-Lone-Poet"
---

I shall not permit pride to sit by idly and sigh, 
I shall put stifling pain aside, invisibly, then mourn 
demon clad terrorist planes and missiles hiding in the sky, 
for all those innocent children, life so inhumanely scorned 

I shall never emit a sad sound or weep 
I'll stay dry and emotionally reserved, 
I won't rest, lay down, halt, or sleep 
until sweet judicial desserts are served 

I shall not ever, ever release a blind sob 
as salty optical saline secretions of defeat well up, 
I will gratefully strive to do my patriotic job 
and seek to refill freedom's kindhearted cup 

I shall cease to leak a single drop and I will cope, 
for within this unstoppable drum that beats so freely 
echoes faith's triumph to keep alive peace and hope, 
ringing Lady Liberty's bell from sea to shining sea 

I shall never scream 
nor whimper or yell, 
of divine victory I'll dream 
comes high water or Hades' hell 

I shall not let out one somber wail or battle cry, 
but promise our great land I will honor and defend 
I'll bravely stand tall and fight, kill, even die, 
trying to save our nation's flag, to the bitter end 

I shall never shriek or bawl, 
I'll utter not one weak boo-hoo 
or be enslaved by the evil we saw, 
I shall speak only of what is good and true 

I will pray for strength, courage, God's infinite love and mercy, 
I will pray, too, for all those tragic souls, vets, firefighters, everyday heroes - living or dead; 
I will always remember the body bags upon that ill-fated anniversary, 
the September morn American blood, sweat and tears were senselessly shed; 

...but I shall NOT cry.
