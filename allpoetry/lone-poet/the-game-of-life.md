---
title: "The Game of Life"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "the-game-of-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38116-The-Game-of-Life-by-Lone-Poet"
---

We live and breathe
we love, we hate,
heart worn on our sleeve
we pick a soulmate
who fits like a glove
husband and wife
from a seed give birth 
celebrate new life
that grew on this earth 
from a heavenly grace above
we are the human race
we get sick, then heal
face different beliefs,
but bleed the same
we cry, we feel
place the blame
we learn to trust 
we all must die 
deal with grief
ashes turn to dust
leave to a higher berth,
to be judged by our deeds
where there's no more strife
we need to mourn 
we want to believe 
from death's flames we're reborn
as Lady's steel sword 
was drawn from stone 
but we can't conceive what is real
that which flesh and bone reveal
this is the game of life,
take it for whatever it's worth
