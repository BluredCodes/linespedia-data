Printer, printer
thrown at the wall,
I got a darn splinter
hacking with a buzz saw

Last night I tried to save
a poem I typed out of rage,
but Hewlett Packard won't behave
why can't I print the fonging page?

I just do not understand,
what happened to my deskjet's link?
it got lost in Windoze la-la land,
USB ports really stink!

I only wanted a hardcopy,
and searched every directory
this printer's ink is too sloppy,
I need a slave or secretary

Now I can't even save
all the stuff I wrote,
it lies in a cyber grave
where's my bloody notes?

Well, I gave it my all,
still it won't print
computers have no soul,
why can't I take a hint?
