Life is one cool ice hockey game 
and pucks are our human lives 
rules and penalties are the same, 
but only that lucky point arrives 

the hockey sticks are Mr. Fate 
who wisely decides which way to go 
oversees both side's 6 teammates, 
paces goalie, if he skates too fast or slow 

God is heaven's crazy official referee, 
faces off and calls illegal slapshots 
sinners sit ablaze in a penalty box for 333, 
maybe forever if their evil body rots 

Satan is the weak contender, 
poor loser lacks any real skill 
benched, a sneaky pretender, 
he's good at stealing free will 

watch out for him, he cheats 
when the referee is unaware, 
lights matches to your seats 
and starts fights on every dare 

but when that final bell rings 
and the season nears its end, 
Hell's rink freezes when Fate brings 
a winner, as God awaits, "Amen"!! 

His Angels won the game 
fair and square, they rule 
aren't you glad you came, 
and missed Sunday School? 

so score one big point for victory 
and none at all for the Devils' team 
Hate's not on the player directory, 
nor living this great American dream
