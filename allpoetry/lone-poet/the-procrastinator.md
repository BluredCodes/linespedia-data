If you ask me why I always hesitate,
I'll tell you because I like to wait
ask me when I will do my chores,
soon as I accumulate some more	
if you ask when I will write a book,
I'll say right after I learn to cook
if you ask why I keep postponing,
can you believe I don't know what I'm honing?
and if you ask why I leave things 'til later,
I'll reply it's 'cause I'm the Procrastinator!
