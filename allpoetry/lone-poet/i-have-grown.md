---
title: "I Have Grown"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "i-have-grown"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39773-I-Have-Grown-by-Lone-Poet"

The years have seen me grow
In more ways than I could know,
As a poet of tears, sweat, and blood
I’ve gone from okay to good

As a woman and wife
I'm changing in this life,
I am no longer a child
Though still very wild

Fire within burns
Creativity yearns,
Passionate for art
In my soul and heart

Now I’m all grown 
I feel so scared and alone,
Please show me the right way
"Wash my illusions away",
Give me courage to get through today
