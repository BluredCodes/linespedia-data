---
title: "To Be Dead (I Want To Believe)"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "to-be-dead-i-want-to-believe"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39117-To-Be-Dead--I-Want-To-Believe--by-Lone-Poet"
---

I wonder what it's like to be dead, 
does a disembodied person feel his head? 
can corpses sense anything at all, 
do we even possess an eternal soul? 

where do we go once we decease, 
when we get there, will we be at peace? 
can some folks really see ghosts, 
or are they just delusions at the most? 

I so want to believe,
but it hurts to grieve 
and strange as it seems, 
I get prophetic dreams

fascinating as it is to be dead, 
I'd still rather be alive, instead 
to breathe fresh air is all I need, 
human being of flesh and bone, I bleed 

we, the living, are lucky but don't know, 
how nice it is to hear the autumn wind blow 
to lick a sweet drop of snow or spring rain, 
or feel a beating heart, think with a brain... 

Like perennial flowers, life continues to grow.
