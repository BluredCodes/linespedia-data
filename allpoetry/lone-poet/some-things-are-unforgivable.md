Some things are just so unforgivable
deeds too horrible for anyone to believe
though my mind may seem to forget it all
inside, bleeding souls always grieve

kept hidden within memories
sins tucked away, out of sight
until the right reason decrees
when we get into a petty fight

you say it was only a little “mistake”
and promise you'll “never do it again”
I’ve heard that line, for God's sake
so many times it’s making me insane!

trust must be slowly earned
along with love and respect
'til this lesson's well learned
know your actions are suspect

I’ll never just let it slide
and no matter how long I live
I’ll hold bitterness deep inside
'cause some wrongs I can’t forgive
