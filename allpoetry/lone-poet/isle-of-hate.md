If you find your self 
on the Isle of Hate 
consider yourself 
a little while too late 

doorways to inner realms 
are closed to open minds 
but if your heart is pure evil 
come in sinner, you're just in time 

on the Isle of Hate 
one never waits 
smile, fate is on your side 

but if you stray 
be on your way 
we don't want your kind
