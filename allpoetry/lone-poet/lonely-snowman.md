There’s a forlorn snowman,
peering in my windowpane
maybe it’s a corny omen,
oh no, here we go again!

he appeared last year
and all the ones before
why does he come here,
knockin' at my door?

why’s he crying anyway,
is he depressed or lost?
please man, don’t go away,
Mr. Snowman made of frost

he’s just lonely, that's all,
for his cold wintry life
misses his friends of fall,
and his old, flaky wife
