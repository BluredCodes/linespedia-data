Americans are in a state of denial
Liberty's homeland has been defiled
Yet some go out to celebrate and play
Like it’s just a fun national holiday

Can't you feel the pain, hear humanity's lies?
Over 3,000 lives lost, maybe someone you love
Many of us unforsaken survivors don't realize
What we have, this sacred gift sent from above

Thrown away like it was a pile of junk
Smile pretty, then go get stoned or drunk
Tomorrow there may be none of us here
So you drown your petty sorrows in beer

It’s far easier to be weak, deny everything
Than dare to confront the frightfully cruel fact
We must ALL fight evil for peace’s uprising,
Words speak nothing; the world must react
