Pen and paper
Black carbon ink
Great crime caper
Makes me think

Writing is not just a chore
It’s freedom of expression
And a whole hell of a lot more
Relieves feelings of depression

I love to write
Brings me peace
Alone at night
Emotional release
	
I find writing's just right for me
Sets the passionate muse inside me free
To contemplate my every blues-inspiring word
Tirelessly write stories nobody else has heard

Writing is something I truly enjoy
Like a little kid getting a brand new toy
Can’t stop playing with it, day or night
Pondering over it makes me want to write 

If ever I publish my works for pay
I'll kiss goodbye to all those jerks
I'd never amount to much, they'd say
That I was a total failure and such

I like a poem that's fresh and new
One that speaks from a heart so true
In my sleep I dream of romance novels
With copyrights typed under the covers

Picked a nom de plume only I know
Hope my talent continues to grow
In a competitive world such as this
Doubt my real name anyone would miss

The writer in me loves to read
It fills a void, a human need
I read about anything that I like
Provides nourishment for the psyche

To act out other lives within a book
When mine was always being overlooked
Although I've tried many creative arts
Poetry holds the key to human hearts

If in life I would never have to lose 
I'd be a sci-fi writer, if I had to choose
Over being a Hollywood movie star 
For me, writing's the best by far

Writing isn't easy, it takes luck and skill
Still, it's a special gift that can fulfill
Greeting cards and children's stories
Don't quite pay your heating bill
('Tho you may get plenty of money worries)

If you truly aspire to write
It must be done purely out of fun
Unless your writings are so outta sight
Financial rewards are next to none

Opportunity is out there, just have to look
Hey, even my future is bright as the sun
I'm publishing my first poetry book
Too bad I still haven't begun
