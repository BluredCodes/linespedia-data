Caught that place as I blinked my eyes... 

glimpses of by-gone days somewhere in time           
flash frozen in an ambient sigh 
remembrances having all but stood still 
heart drumming to their vein-pulsing trill           

quaint horsedrawn cobblestones 
peeking moonlit eves 
gothic chimerin brownstones           
starry-eyed lavender dreams 

lighthouse beacons flickered high 
nautical tide-swept marbled shores 
white-tipped gulls soared New England skies 
orca sightings graced ocean's door 
                     
Such a shame I left so soon 
I'll miss it all the same 
if granted one single wish I would choose 
but for that place to charm my name 
so shall my spirit remain 

in that forgotten cove in paradise. 


(c) 1999 SmSThis is about Boston and its surrounding towns, espec. Marblehead. The scenery is beautiful there in the fall. I went in November, the leaves had already fallen, but it was still amazingly picturesque. Was there in '99, but hope to go back after the "Big Dig" is done. I'd love to be buried there, in the sea with my whale and dolphin friends.
