---
title: "Milk and Honey"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "milk-and-honey"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39492-Milk-and-Honey-by-Lone-Poet"

In this land of milk and honey 
the milk is now curdled and sour, 
Honey's not sweet, but oh, so bitter 
the land became a withered flower 

No friendly smiles, 
no happy grins; 
Only endless miles 
of hate and sins 

War games have begun 
but this time it's no game, 
Fixing up our tanks and guns 
and we know who's to blame 

Terrorists in flaming planes 
aboard 4 suicidal flights; 
Over three thousand slain, 
world horrified at the sights 

Sinister hijackers sank a crater hole 
aimed straight into America's head, 
To see those great twin towers fall 
Lady Liberty choked, rolled over dead 

Died of a broken heart, 
wounded pride and soul 
But she is pretty smart, 
you won't see her bawl 

Not a tear from her eyes, 
this old gal can't be killed 
Inside she secretly cries, 
but trudges onward uphill 

'Cause she knows freedom's ours, 
the golden land of milk and honey 
These will be among our finest hours 
bringing harmony, peace, and money 

It may take us a little longer 
but we'll get to the top of the hill, 
And become that much stronger 
to swallow life's caustic pill 

We must allow ourselves to grieve, 
then move on and boldly fight the war 
Wear those fierce lion hearts on our sleeve, 
each day climbing one mighty step more 

The milk may go sour 
the honey may be harsh, 
But we've got the power 
to destroy evil's farce 

A man self-proclaimed 
to be a fellow Muslim, 
Shunned and shamed 
no true believer of Islam 

Made a mockery of his own, 
those who worship and pray 
Peacefully together, so alone, 
now their future looks gray 

Women and children, innocents 
who have done nothing wrong, 
Civilians without real presidency 
now have no country to belong 

Enter the land of milk and honey, 
beauty prevails, no lowly slaves 
Where the air is clean and sunny, 
home of the free, land of the brave 

Creamy as butter, sweeter than sugar, 
save all our dismayed sisters and brothers 
Now launch the attack missiles and outdated Lugers, 
helpful aid from staunch allies and loyal others 

Bloodstained hands 
not upon free lands, 
The best of the best 
to hell with the rest! 

Our milk will never spoil 
and our honey won't dry up, 
As long as justice toils 
we'll refill freedom's cup; 
May Liberty fly high over US soil!!
