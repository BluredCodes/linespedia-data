---
title: "My Perfect World"
writer: "Lone Poet"

In my perfect world 
happiness flourishes
cosmic mysteries unfurled 
by its own sword evil perishes 

cats and dogs co-exist 
flowers perenially in bloom 
no crimes, nobody prejudiced 
purple wallpaper in every room 

in my perfect land
classic rock still rules 
quadriplegics stand
no cigarettes or drunken fools 

no stifling heat waves 
just cool summer breezes
no unknown soldiers or unmarked graves
no more incurable diseases

in my perfect place 
children could play safe 
fun, games, and smiley faces 
for kids of every age, race, faith

in my perfect life
hope would erase doubt
nature's resources rife
nobody would do without

love conquers hate
here nobody would grieve 
we'd make our own lucky fate
disabled able to achieve

in my perfect utopia
imagination sparks creativity
no starving people in Ethopia
no lack of sensitivity

light overcomes dark hurdles
we'd believe in fairies and gnomes
no more tight bras or girdles
nobody thrown out of their homes

in my perfect sea
dolphins understand man
blind finally can see
we'd invent the perfect tan

in my perfect sky 
not a soul would die 
and pigs could fly 
everyone naturally high

no guns or hunting knives 
animals and humans roam free 
equality amongst husbands and wives 
no war, only peace and liberty; 

In my perfect plan everything good survives.
