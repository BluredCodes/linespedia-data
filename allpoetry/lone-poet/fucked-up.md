I feel an aching bruise within my soul
a hug that yearns for real, not fake love

there's a pain above my side
that burns apart while I wait

sounds like a few loose screws in my brain
turned me around 9000 degrees when kindness died

found a deep hole in my breaking heart
throbs where the freezing rain seeps in

stuck a smart drug in my mind
helps keep this bitch satisfied

moved a new stitch in time
where fate stepped in

I have two sobbing, dry eyes
my cries for grief that flew by

I've got a fucked up life
deserves only lies and hate
