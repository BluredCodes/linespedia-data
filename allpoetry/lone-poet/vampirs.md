title: "VampI.R.S."

They'll bleed you dry 
sinking fangs of greed 
into your trusting neck 
depositing demonic seeds 
of dollars and no sense 
as seduction bursts 
from promises and lies 
loathsome vampires they are           
why they're by far the worst 
sacrificial deeds of the night 
whispering sweet nothings 
and you feel so bloody right 
'til dark bank lesions swell 
you're transported to federal hell 
and then, well... 
maybe you'll get your refund check
