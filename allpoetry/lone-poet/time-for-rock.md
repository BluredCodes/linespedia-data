title: "Time for Rock"

Golden orb of light, spirits soar 
quivering rainbows trickle off the dance floor, 
vibrations ignite in melting heat 
pulses racing to the club's riveting beat 

Bodies in motion, quick rapture 
suspended in time, clicking shutters capture, 
go one step further, if you dare 
the time is right, sparks fly in the air 

Highway to the stars, cosmic dances 
velvet fantasies and rich romances, 
sweat dripping, no time to yield 
food for feet, let down your shield 

Open up to supreme enlightenment 
throbbing rhythm flows with excitement, 
in a wink of an eye it will soon be gone 
so grab what you can, 'cause it's all or none 

Can you feel its hold on you? 
Can you see that which came before you?, 
Breathe in the sweet vapors 
emanating around you 

As the music's soaking rains 
send currents resonating through veins, 
above and beyond your wildest dreams 
taken to the highest extremes 

Wonder what I'm babbling about? 
rock 'n' roll, twist and shout, 
grooving and swaying all night long 
life is naught without a great love song, 
and an earth-shattering sound to hum along; 
         
Orbs revolving atop the dance floor, 
bring back that old-time rock, nothing more
