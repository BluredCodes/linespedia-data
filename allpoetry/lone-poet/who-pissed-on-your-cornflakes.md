I love how when I write on a controversial topic
Someone always has to put their 2 nasty cents in,
Unsolicited, unconstructive criticism, just to be mean
Akin to flaming, it’s unprofessional as well as juvenile

So I ask you, “Who pissed on your cornflakes?”,
Nobody was talking to you specifically
For someone who’s no expert, you’re pretty defensive,
What’s the matter, did I hit a raw guilty nerve?
Got something to hide?
And who anointed you Goddess of literature anyway?,
Chew on that “stuff”!!
