Roses are velvet red,
blood is cyanosis blue
please hit me in the head, 
so I can forget you

off with your slimy peds, 
and your thermal underwear, too 
every greek God, time for bed
want your delectable bod, yes I do!

forget all the nonsense I just said
Silence of the Lamb, final part two
Hannibal the Cannibal is dead (boohoo)
Lechter was my secret Valentine...who?

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
for thee my heart bleeds 
thirsty one, suckle thy wrist
thy soul is so full of greed
come, and we shall co-exist
forever, if you get thy gist

so very hungry
I wait for a drink
from your red nectar
neck soft and pink

blood oozing
artery bruising
hypnotic confusion
nocturnal transfusion

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
*sung to tune of "My Funny Valentine"

My bloody Valentine,
sweet syringe Valentine
you make my head so vile
when I think of you

deadly, bloody Valentine
tasty, luscious Valentine
you make me cringe
when I drink from you

Are your fangs a little bent
is your blood bank a little spent
when you flash that smile, do you bite?

So if you lust for me
just a tiny bit thirst for me 
please, bloody Valentine, pray (I might),
oh, ashes and dust, stay awhile, stay...!!

please say a righteous prayer for me
that I'll return from hell to thee
let's make every Fright Night, 
a bloody Valentine's Day
