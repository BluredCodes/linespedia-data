Sometimes I feel I’ve gone insane
My intense fears are driving me there,
Chronic sickness, stress, and the pain
Make me think it’s too much to bear

Am I really crazy or just depressed?
Do I hear voices and can I see God?,
If I answer yes to the last two, you guessed
I’m nuttier than a squirrel in Cape Cod

Mentally ill is but a simple paraphrase
For kindhearted people too unable to cope,
A good, long talk to offer supportive praise
Could be smarter medicine than mind dope

Am I clinically insane?
That’s open for debate
But then again,
Just ask my mate
