title: "Little Corner of Heaven"

In my own little corner 
Sitting upon an asteroid, 
Avoiding all the mourners 
From high atop the world 

Sun and moon 
Kiss and neck 
Life ends too soon, 
But it all dies, oh heck 

Oxygen depleted, 
No room to grow 
Hey, if you can't beat it, 
Join it; let our galaxy blow 

My own little corner, 
Some rocky asteroid 
Dead stars are reborn, 
As I get hemorrhoids; 

"Heavens to Mergatroid!"
