---
title: "Judgment Day"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "judgment-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

I've fasted and atoned for my sins,
cleansed of every past wrong and misdeed 
but who says the truth always wins?,
there's one too many hungry mouths to feed

"who will save your souls
when it comes to the end?",
who will catch our falls
where's my angel to defend?

this frightened, lonely little girl
and all her gifts she holds so dear,
light surrounds the whole wide world
but we live each day in dark morbid fear

down on my knees
praying for our nation,
begging you, please
for blessed salvation

confession of sins sounds easy,
only when your faith strongly affirms
but grieving just makes me queasy,
long as believing's on God's terms

will He bring comfort and joy
or hang mine out to dry in the pouring rain?,
will He let evil burn and destroy
all that's good, turned to disdain?

I don't understand
how this can happen,
this patriotic land
is sadly misshapen

Bible tells us to fight
that justice shall prevail,
but where is His trusty light
when man knows he may fail?

Liberty's children are now slayed dead,
innocence killed by cowardly acts of greed
wonder what bad dream lies far ahead,
will we die or be miraculously freed?

the truth may hold karmic weight,
atonement for grudges seems really nice
but who will abolish devilishly cold steel hate,
before love's warmth reveals glacier ice?;

Upon Judgment Day, who pays Satan's price?
