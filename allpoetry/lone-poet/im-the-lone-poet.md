I am the Lone Poet
but do you know it?

secretly come and go
my face, I won't show

like a man with a gun
my weapon's this pun

intoxicating as booze
I am drunken from muse

lonely as all losers in hell
why don't my poems sell?

I'm able to spell
or can't you tell?

just want someone to read
what's inside of my head

I fear not before I'm stone dead
hey, did you hear what I said?

I am the one and only Lone Poet
I'll bet you still don't know it

I'm done, now throw me some bread
hi-ho-hi-ho, it's off I go to bed...
