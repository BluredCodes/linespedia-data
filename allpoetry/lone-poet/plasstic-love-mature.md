Schoolkids hooking in the street 
dressed for playing trick-or-treat, 
the lousy pimp, he sold his whore 
leeches sucking at their dirty meat 
and druglords coming back to score, 

(as Johnny goes marching off to war) 
             
bimbo Bambi's slitting up her skirts 
'cuz "real men" like getting off on flirts, 
who think popularity buys fancy pearls 
or a night of mindless sordid thrills           
for some sleazoid's wasted sex toys, 
          
listen up all you little boys and girls 
   ~just spread 'em W I D E~, 
and down these tasty candy pills - 
now don't it feel real good inside? 

plastic smiley feel-me-ups, 
silicone stuffed into molded cups 
dancing tits and ass that jiggle, 
voyeurs pay to see them wiggle 
           
to the naive ones of Gen X           
love is but a 4 letter word           
and strip poker is a shady game of sex, 
the cards you deal are bare buns for hire 
your prize is a sugardaddy who never retires 
from the cheap trick you'll turn next 

in this sick game few rules exist - 
just that life's lonely, hard, and unfair, 
you must learn to trust no one in bed 
and flash it when they tell you to, 

better do them as they say, don't resist 
or you may end up dead in your underwear 
'cuz nobody you sleep with cares about you,
if you yell, they ain't ever gonna hear! 

well, if fools want to play with fire 
their cash in hand can only get burned 
   ~~by hell's wicked flame~~
don't you twisted jokers ever learn? 
in the fairyland of desire, 
                    love and lust are 
                              (one and the same)
