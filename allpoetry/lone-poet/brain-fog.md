Clear these cloudy
~~cobwebs~~,
thinking is blurred
nothing makes sense
blow the foghorn,
living in a brain mist
help, can't comprehend
it's just ramblings,
mind is possessed
by aliens in dead space
eating away at my flesh
'til no gray matter is left
atrophied,
gone
gone
gone...
