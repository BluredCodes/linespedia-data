Moo-moo
Here’s your milk
Moo-moo
Mulch
Chomp
Burp
Zzzz…
Fart 
Poop
Pee
Moo-moo
Zzzz…
Mulch
Chomp
Burp
Fart
Poop
Pee
Moo-moo
Moo-moo
Zzzz…

~~~~~~~~

A cow goes moo
What do you do?
A cow gives milk
From her teats
She can kick
With her feets,

…Moo-moo!!
