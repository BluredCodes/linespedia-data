Dost thou know how it feels, so good, 
To be suckled and sensually caressed? 
I've tasted thy flesh, thy crimson blood, 
Its warmth, so intoxicatingly intense 

I've touched thy deepest inner forces, 
Hidden desires, pulled back passion's reins 
I drank the sweet nectar of the Source, 
Saw thy future burn within those fiery veins 

Do thy virgin nipples long to harden with wanton lust?, 
Dost thou crave all that is wrong, but have it ye must? 
Corporeal flames of life and death breathe into now's being, 
Aether's cardinal power, all-knowing, hearing and seeing 

Dost thou remember how it felt, naïve pleasures so great, 
To awaken to a fun, new toy of thy own and be its first? 
So surreal to savor, such yearnings nevermore to sate, 
Give thyself unto me; feed thy hunger, drown thy thirst 

All thy darkest dreams and wishes shall be yours to tempt fate, 
Bathe naked beneath moonbeams; eternally blissful joys, want for none 
I'll be thy new toy, thou art my nocturnal underground playmate, 
Return thee to love's immortal kiss of death; soon, kindred one
