Barfy the golden tuna
was a jolly little fish
he liked to splish and splash 
around a moldy petrie dish

Barfy was so happy
as any fish could be
but one day he died, his friends all cried
a dumb kitty ate him, golly gee

Barfy no longer swims
in mirky bowls of h20 scum
now he's doing better, at last free
in pet heaven's new aquarium

Barfy the dead tuna
was loved by everyone
that darn cat's to blame, oh what a shame
he won't be having any more fun!
