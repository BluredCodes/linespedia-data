---
title: "Am I Good Enuf"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "am-i-good-enuf"

Am I tough enuf to stand alone 
or cool enuf to please you? 
hurt my feelings, they don't count, 
you know that isn't really true 

How can you judge me 
when you don't know me? 
yet you think that you're better 
I may be different, not like the rest, 
but I'm not blind, for I do see 

You laugh at me behind my back 
then go and tell your friends 
that it's ok, I'm not all there, 
I guess that all depends 

What is it you think 
that I can't do as well? 
why do you want to hurt me, 
and make me feel like hell? 

I guess you'll find out too late 
that friends just can't be bought 
there is a lesson you must learn, 
a truth that must be taught 

Not in school, but from within 
seek out your higher power 
you better change your attitude, 
you're nothing but a coward 

Why am I not good enuf
for you to hang around? 
why do you think I'm stupid, 
is that the way I sound? 

You think that you are smarter 
and take this life for granted 
but I work even harder, 
my feet are firmly planted 

Who the heck do you think you are 
to put me down, a superstar? 
I think not, for you are sick, 
my mind may be slow, but senses quick 

Am I good enuf for you? 
I doubt it very much 
but your opinions do not matter, 
here's a truth you cannot touch
