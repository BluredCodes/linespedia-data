title: "Meritless"

I wrote nothing all day
I have no words to say
Doesn’t matter anyway
My poems won’t pay

Try as I may
It’s of no use
I sit and pray
For my muse

She comes not
So I will just rot
It stinks I’ve got
But quill and inkpot
