#1
~~*~~
fall soothes weary soul
~~*~~
metamorphic crisp foliage 
~~*~~
reflective season
~~*~~

**

#2
~~*~~
tranquil reveries
~~*~~
heaven's autumnal dewdrops
~~*~~
drift upon brisk wind
~~*~~

**

#3
~~*~~
pumpkin pies simmer
~~*~~
atop warm ovens of love
~~*~~
hayrides and scarecrows
~~*~~
