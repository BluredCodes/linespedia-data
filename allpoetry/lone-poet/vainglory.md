title: "Vainglory"

These shimmering eyes
Gleam like a golden pond
Radiant pools of dry ice
Sparkling from beyond 

An enticing smile
Born to a virgin child
No beast can defile
Naked in the wild

My priestess soul
And angelic heart
Grant comfort to all
Gray matter is smart

These ruby-red lips 
Taste as sweet wine
Playful nubile hips 
Fruit swaying on a vine
