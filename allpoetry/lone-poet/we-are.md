---
title: "We Are"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "we-are"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You are ice 
as I am warm 
but you were refuge from my storm 

You are harsh 
as I am gentle 
yet at times you're so sentimental 

You are strong 
as I am weak 
still you gave me freedom to speak 

We are as One 
united and divided 
before time was begun 
two subconscious minds 
as 2 souls grown apart 
we're connected to One 
one universal beating heart 

You are smart 
as I can act so stupid 
but you carry all the knowledge of Cupid 
           
You are foolish 
as I am wise 
yet mercy lives in your compassionate eyes 

You are the moon 
as I am the sun 
we are twilight 
2 shining beacons 
darkness and light 
day becomes night 
           
We are as One 
It shall be done.
