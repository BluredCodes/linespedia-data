title: "Space People (Don't Exist)"

Twilight Zone, Jeti Knights, Borgs, and Lost in Space, 
but SETI's yet to reap a better, brighter new human race 
People are people who live and die, no matter where from, 
you can fly a pod far into orbit, for an extravagant sum 

Sentient embodiments of men in black, red, green or grays, 
are they the unseen gods to whom we all worship and pray? 
Just who are those tiny folks with silver antennae heads, 
yet another dead alien cover-up by cigarette smoking Feds? 

Hover this way, handsome interstellar moon man, 
what part of unreal do our minds not understand? 
Space guys don't exist in the stars, on the seas or land, 
fodder for wild fantasies, as Sky Lab drifts unmanned 

Face the facts, Earthlings, it doesn't take half a brain, 
to see they're a forged by-product of children and the insane 
Laughable Jetson cartoons, George and his loving wife Jane, 
in our hands alone the rebirth of civilization remains 

 

(c) 6/01, 3/04 SmsI know this sounds contradicting, but I am a firm believer (I want to believe) in the possibility of other life in space. This is merely an analytical look at the raw facts, that there's no real scientific proof of this, just speculation. But somewhere out there, I just know there has to be more..."Beam me up, Scotty!"
