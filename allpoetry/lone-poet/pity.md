I pity the poor souls who feel 
they must take advantage of the weak 
Ignorance is not a crime, 
only injustices to those who can't speak 
Healing the sick is no easy chore, 
why can't they just find peace? 
love and compassion takes heart 
but if there's a will,            
then somehow there's a way 
at least that's a start 
though there will always be 
a price to pay 
until we live to be free, 
and then maybe 
we'll all be ok.
