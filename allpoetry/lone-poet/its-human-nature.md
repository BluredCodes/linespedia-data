title: "It's Human Nature"

It’s human nature to grovel in fear
Of anything not easily understood
It’s humanity's nature to love, revere
All that's made of flesh and blood

It’s human nature to feel raging anger
To hate when confronted by opposing views
It’s man's nature to ignore pain and danger
To be careless, sing the melancholy blues

It’s human nature to manipulate, control
The native world that lives and breathes
It’s a mortal's natural law to have flaws
And pray, though not everyone believes

It’s human nature to render our timely dues
Repay the fated piper at his beckon call
It’s our nature to thrive on awful news
Surrender away our sublime ripened souls

It’s mankind's nature to laugh and cry
Then spit in the macabre face of death
It’s the human race’s nature not to die
But to survive long after its last breath
