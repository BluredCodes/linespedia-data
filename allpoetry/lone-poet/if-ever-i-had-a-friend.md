title: "If Ever I Had A Friend"

If ever I knew much about a good friend
Whose undying loyalty I would defend,
Whenever I gave my love, so very true
The same touch would be saved for you,
Two brave souls bonded from start to end

Came from faraway but to shyly say
Why your care and blessings abound,
Silver angel wings fluttering at play
Fairy heartstrings, tinkle bells resound

If ever a partner of mine...I had, indeed
To trust such a life into hands of faith,
To gladly heal my wounds when they bleed 
Feel not any hostility, strife, or wrath

Only sun-kissed warmth radiating inside
Never hate, pain, lies, or stubborn pride,
And if ever the day we must miss one another
You'd still remain as sister or brother

Nothing finer than to feed 
Your mad, contagious laughter,
A binding of real friends in need
Treasured friends last forever after
