title: "Make Time Disappear"
writer: "Lone Poet"

If I could make time disappear 
There would be no more tears 
We’d all be blissfully set free 
Nothing to feel, hear or see 

No worries, hate or fear in our never-never land’s stark void 
Merry as a fatalistic clamshell parked upon an obscure asteroid 
No heaven, no hell; only limbo’s dwelling place, oblivious in-between, 
Bedamned expelled to a darkened Halloween world, eternally wiped clean 

If history’s clock face forever stopped ticking and disappeared 
Everything on Earth’s mothership of horrors would cease to exist 
The link to time’s spatial nexus wickedly severed 
Until peace and mirth locked lips and kissed, 

That is, if this hopeless universe was even missed…
