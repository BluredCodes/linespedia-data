---
title: "Individuality"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "individuality"
source: "AllPoetry"

You think 'cause I haven't
experienced catastrophes,
that I don't feel sorrow

my eyes cry silent tears
like the rest of you,
my mind comprehends
all yours does

you think 'cause I never
suffered a major loss,
that I don't feel grief

my heart aches
just like yours,
my soul grieves
same as yours

you think 'cause I've yet
to endure torture,
that I don't feel pain

you think 'cause I didn't
give birth, 
that I don't know
what it means to be happy

everyone is different,
but equally unique
and so are our life's battles,
triumphs and losses

and I've lived all the joy
and all the sorrow and pain, 
the only difference is
the person feeling it - and that's unique

you may think your problems
are worse than mine,
or more serious 
or more important, 
or more deserving of empathy

ours are no better, 
nor worse
than the next guy's

my memories and experiences
are special to me;
to feel is to be human,
to feel nothing is to be stone-cold dead
