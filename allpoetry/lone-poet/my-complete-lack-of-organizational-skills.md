As much as I try to get organized
Junk always seems to pile up high,
From the corner of my eyes I was surprised
To see a crap heap fall from the sky

Files, disks, magazines thrown on the floor,
Dust mites and mold spores bigger than alley cats
What am I keeping all this outdated old stuff for,
To donate to my pet spiders, bugs, and rats?

I must hire a cleaning crew, a maid or two,
An unpaid salvage yard would be just fine
To inspire me to rearrange my jungle land zoo,
I’ll even hang the “Do Not Disturb” sign

My complete lack of organizational skills
Puts me in first place for the Pigpen Award,
No need to compete, I’ve already won the thrill
Of knowing I’m the world’s laziest slob and didn't have to work hard!!
