What’s become of my muse? 
She left me high-and-dry again, 
If I’m not inspired soon, I’ll sing the blues 
Out in the moonlit pouring rain

I saw her once, she blew an elfish kiss 
Said artistic dreams were made of stardust, 
And when I threw to the wind a selfless wish 
I’ll find out where muses park their karmas
