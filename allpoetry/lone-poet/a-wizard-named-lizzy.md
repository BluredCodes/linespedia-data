A lady wizard named Lizzy
Had a pet lizard, Izzy,
Lizzy was very busy
Getting into a tizzy
Over her hair, so frizzy,
That made her dizzy
So she took some fizzy
Seltzer for her tummy 
It tasted not yummy,
But kind of gummy
Lizzy pushed it down with rum
And soon began to hum,
Then she used her magic green thumb
To sprout a chrysanthemum
