title: "Small World, Big Heart"
writer: "Lone Poet"

We telecommunicate by wire
Electrostatic modulation noises
Yet somehow I will never tire
Of your poetical-inspired voices

A small world of kind hearts so big
Open-minded neighbors, near and far 
America to Europe to starboard frigate
We can chat no matter where we are


© 2/15/02 SmSI was just thinking how, since the invention of the Internet, we can "talk" with people from all over the world.  Without the net, we'd be so isolated.  It's cool to "meet" so many kindhearted souls w/similar interests. Thank you. You've been nicer to me than real "family" and "friends" and we don't even know each other.
