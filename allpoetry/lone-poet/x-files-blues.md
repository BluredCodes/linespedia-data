title: "X-Files Blues"
writer: "Lone Poet"

There lives a show on Sunday nights 
that is "spooky" as can be, 
but you ain't seen nothing yet 
like Fox Mulder and Dana Scully! 

They'll get you hopping 
while other shows are flopping 
pretty soon you'll believe, 
so keep those ratings from dropping 

If it's aliens from space, 
folks disappear with no trace 
you just gotta try it, 
don't dare to deny it 

You've got the X-Files blues!! 

Skinner's the guy who keeps them straight, 
but Mulder and Scully have been out awful late 
trying to find some mysterious clue 
to the creature in the sewer 
and animals at the zoo 

The FBI thinks they're screwy, 
and that may be right 
but the truth is out there, 
they'll never give up the fight! 

Cigarette Man will get his in the end, 
if the viewers agree 
and the writers will bend 

Before you change that channel 
and put on the cable tv 
just remember what you're missing, 
besides, X-Files is free! 

Gotta watch it in the dark 
you must not close your eyes, 
there's a message hidden on your screen 
that will surely hypnotize 

Mulder is witty, his day's never done, 
the way Scully dresses, how can she run? 
she'll never believe if God fell from the sky; 
Mulder likes birdseeds and Playboy, 
the psycho dead body fetish guy
likes Scully's hair processed and dry 

So don't forget now, Sunday at nine 
on Fox 29, right during primetime - 
turn on the tube and kick off your shoes, 
it's time to tune in to those X-Files blues!!
