I love you so, do you know it?
But as much as I try to show it
In my face you threw your bullshit
Oh, why’d you go and blow it, nitwit?

You crushed my itty-bitty heart
Who knew sex was such a lusty art?
I don’t know how I’ll ever trust
Extra massages and gold dust

I wrote this mushy old love note
Now shove it down your cranky throat
Drown my thankless message in a bottle
I’m ready to wank your cold throttle

Wedded bliss
Pop the cork
Make-up and kiss
Here comes the stork!

2 peas in a pod
2 nuts in a shell
2 squirrels in Cape Cod
2 oddballs in hell
(We're married, can't you tell?)  

Hush, let’s stay nice and quiet
Just for now, this moment in time
We’re so happy in love, we can’t deny it
I’ll bet my last dime on this crappy rhyme

Hand-in-hand, mismatched hump mates united for life
You’re my "grumpy" husband and I’m your "nagging" wife
Together, forever, two passionate souls ignite
Maybe one day we’ll actually get it right

We’re in this contract ‘til the end
Or ‘til we’re wearing Depends
Hope you kept my love letters
Well big dope, you better!

I love you, do you know it?
Well, what are ya gonna do about it?
Stop bitchin' and complainin'
Let’s defy fate, propose again,
Get re-hitched and happily ever after go insane!!

Sorry for the "vulgarities", it's there for shock appeal, considering how shocking marriage is anyway. LOL  But it was meant to show how even after we fight and call each other names, we are still together...I will be married 21 long yrs. Sept. 16 and I can tell you it's not easy! lol
