My toilet bowl goes flush, flush, flush
The seat feels cushy next to my tush
I push the lever down really hard
After eating sushi, waste to discard

I rest my rump on the porcelain throne
Just so I may be left all alone
To go about this potty biz
If I need to take a dump or wiz
