Why does love always hurt?
rips your heart out 'til the truth bleeds
soothes your lips and soul, then deserts
suckles you dry as it feeds,
ruthlessly, departing in a covert fashion

all-consuming passion
meant to sustain human life
one day comes crashing
upon your whole plane, irrationally, 
like a bone-crushing fillet knife

irony is there'd be no love
if we felt no pain, sorrow, or grief
sharp pierce of an arrow, shot from above
for Cupid is an immortal thief,
any happiness he sends our way is so brief
