Once in a la-la land called Nod
There lived a girl who was odd
She sucked her thumb ‘til 18
The yuckiest one ever seen

You see, that thumb was fake
Her real first digit fell off
Sucking so much made it break
Overstretched, becoming too soft

Then cracked, snapping like a twig
The girl bandaged half of it to a cig
Now her hands smell just like tobacco
And everybody thinks she’s a wacko
