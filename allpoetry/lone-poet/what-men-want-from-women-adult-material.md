Let me touch you, I want to caress,
Run my eyes all over your taught skin
Your virgin thighs, my thoughts undress,
Since when is imagination such a mortal sin?

To feel your flaming flesh next to mine,
These fingers get you horny in no time
Your love juices taste like honey wine,
Let’s play a sexual game of dirty mime

Come ride atop my Jolly Roger, breathe into it divineness,
I so long to fuck you inside out, make me feel like heaven
As my mind fondles your heaving breasts of lace and fineness,
Your tongue can suckle my sweet lollipop the rest of the evening

Guess what I’m trying to say,
As I use these hands to speak
Is I want you to come here and play
Into your secret garden I’ll peek,
As my unmanly condom leaks
