title: "Sky's Rainbow Tears"

Alaskan skyline's dropping pain from above,
crying frozen, rainy tears of unrequited love
sun's happy face is like a brave frown,
tundra mask of sorrow turned upside down

arced solar rays shine rainbow bright,
spectrum's amazingly graceful aurora lights
prism of psychedelic colors in every hue,
golden yellows, neon greens, azure blues

celestial Goddess in elastic sandals smiles,
blows angelic kisses, stands at high tide awhile
she nods and lends her open, helping hands,
13 white seashell candles glow in the sand;
rides the cold waves to guide us on our endless miles
