Inside of us all

lies a warm fuzzy place

that glows deep at our core

and keeps us so safe



It knows no fear

nor maddening thought

the calm inner peace

that just can't be bought



My warm fuzzy place

shows no boundaries

it's whatever I make it

a soft, gentle breeze



It leaves me secure

while the world passes by

it's enough to know

my warm fuzzy place is inside.
