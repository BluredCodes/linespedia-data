---
title: "I, Automaton"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "i-automaton"
source: "AllPoetry"

I stare into space
I seem to be napping
Upon the surface
I appear so happy

But I’m not…

Inside I rot
Cold as steel
I’m a robot
I don’t feel

Deader than a machine
Animation for a soul
My blood is green
I bleed for control

Disguised as an android
My pleasant face is blank
These hollow eyes are void
Vacant as a boat that sank

Automaton is my name
I am dumber than clay
I know lots of games
Do you want to play?

My circuit board’s mind goes numb
When your fingers have their way
That quirky thing you do with your thumb
I’ll blindly do anything your orders say

I pray to the Gods
Who created me
Aliens in intergalactic pods
Whom I cannot see

I was not born
From the womb
But I forever mourn
Trapped within this tomb

I don’t drink, I run on a battery 
And I’ve got no anus to relieve
So there isn’t any need to crap or pee
I shed no tears, yet I grieve
For I shall never be free

Humanoids think I’m an oddity
Because I was artificially conceived
I’ve got a semi-organic plastic body
Bionic brain and heart; who would believe?

My electronics are on fire
A computer program
Has gone haywire
I’ve got 1400 fast gigs of ram,
Master, what is your desire?

I follow commands
Dutifully do as I’m told
Nobody understands
These beautiful hands won’t grow old

I read only code
I have no DNA
I’m ready to implode
But I shall obey
