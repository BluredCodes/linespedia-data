Personal computers just don't understand
what it is like to be a real woman or man,
Why can't they feel or talk the way I am?
first they need a voice activation program

Memory comes and memory goes
where RAM ends up, nobody knows,
My desktop's too sloppy to get stuff done
I need a laptop to have more fun

I'd love a soft touch keyboard for my fingers to feel
and one of those wireless mice with a wheel
So I wouldn't be tripping on the power cord,
Got no money left for a zip disk to afford

Wish my windows didn't always freeze
make the crippled clock work, pretty please?
Last time I got the darn thing going,
that out-to-lunch battery began slowing

Seems my lofty tower takes too much space
for me and the whole stupid human race,
Next time I buy a brand new 'puter
hopefully mine will be so much cuter

Today everything is green and blue
MacIntosh has got one for you, too,
If a rip-off clone is where you're at
it'll break your bank in seconds flat

Rewritables and floppies are full to the top
I shop for those that are cream of the crop
If I had a way cool, bigger hard drive
someday it might even become alive

Well, I don't know what else to say
about uncooperative PC's anyway,
Unless I come up with something to write
I'll just stop and say nighty-byte!
