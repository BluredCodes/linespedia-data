The world we know 
looks hopeless and grim 
as landfills overflow 
at best, our chances are slim 

only a miraculous deed 
could save this planet's fate 
but mankind must stop mass breeding 
before time says it's far too late 

overpopulated to its brim 
global warming's fast approaching 
birds don't fly and fish no longer swim 
due to our selfish encroaching 

hopeless is all I feel 
we brought it on by greed 
Mother Earth needs balance and harmony to heal 
or there won't be enough food left to feed 

her very livelihood lies within our hands 
but the human race went and blew it 
for ages we destroyed her sacred land 
so back in our faces she threw it 

not much hope remains 
for the survival of our species 
it doesn't take much brains 
to see we're sinking in our own feces
