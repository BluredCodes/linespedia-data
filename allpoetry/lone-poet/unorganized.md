title: "Unorganized"
writer: "Lone Poet"

I'm kind of disorganized
got clutter everywhere
dustmites and dead flies
behind a trusty 233 spare

shredder overflowed, can barely close the door
air from my heater fan is blowing so cold 
letters and papers flew all over the floor
giant spiderwebs are growing next to the mold!

books, disks, cd's and audio cassettes,
if only I could locate those old, crumpled files;
my desk is buried deep in my basement,
folders dumped into a heaping pile 
hurry and dig, maybe I'll find a few just yet

the lights are blinking
new computer keeps locking
my sleepy mind stopped thinking
when the clock quit ticktocking

printer jammed, nowhere to stand
trashcan fell, rug's full of dirt 
threw a shoe and smashed my hand 
killing a damn bug, it swelled and hurts!!  

oh, how will I ever clean
my cramped work space of doom?
it's the scariest place I've seen,
can't see in this dark nightmare of a room!!!
