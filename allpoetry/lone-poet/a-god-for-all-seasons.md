Every culture has its resident god
From Christ and Yahweh to Apollo
A being of light or in a human’s body
Thorny crown surrounded by a halo

Yet not one of you agrees
On the deity all the rest sees
For every person must be right
Or else there will be an unholy fight

You believe what you feel
I’ll worship in my own way
None of us knows which "truth" is real
But we'll all find out someday;
So why can’t we just let dead dogs lay?
