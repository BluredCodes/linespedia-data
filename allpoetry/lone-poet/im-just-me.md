---
title: "I'm Just Me"
writer: "Lone Poet"
slug: "im-just-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39085-Im-Just-Me-by-Lone-Poet"
---

I'm just me, myself and I 
unlike anybody else, 
unique, as the next guy 
longing for freedom, 
a soul not meant to become 

I'm but a plain pink wrapper 
Ms. No Frills-Whistles-Or-Bells, 
seeking only to be happier 
than my crystal ball foretells, 
just a girl who casts magic spells
