---
title: "Beauty"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "beauty"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I look around and seeWhat meets my eyes is beautiful to meThis world that is full of hope and loveThis world where beauty soars like a doveChaos and fear covers up what lies insideBut I will not let the beauty of the world be covered up by lies.A song of happiness ends every time something diesBut soon enough the melody will keep on playingThe song will come to where we are stayingSoon enough there will be no fearsAnd the worlds beauty will not be washed away by our tears.
