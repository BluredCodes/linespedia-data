---
title: "Dancing Life"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "dancing-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

The life I see in your eyes brings me to a field of faeries and flowers. Dancing together in the misty light. The three of us together forever in the heavens of earth. Together for always in the paradise we created. Best friends forever. Hand in Hand dancing twirling never stop. Take a breath and fall. Down to the waters edge. Down to where the earth begins. Never stop laughing. Never stop loving. Never stop smiling. Never stop breathing. Forever and always in the Heavens of Earth.
