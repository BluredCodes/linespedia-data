Stop for a minute
Listen to me
Hold me
Care for me
Tell me you love me
Say you'll always be there
Do the things your suppose to do
Don't leave me waiting for you
Life is to short to wait
Grab what you want
Don't let go
Don't forget how to love
Don't forget how to hate
Its what makes you you
Its what makes life life
Just liveIts just a bunch of twisted feelings and words..Don't worry if you cant understand it or if you don't like it ;)
