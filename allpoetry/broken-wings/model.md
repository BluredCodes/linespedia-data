The pills she tookThe way she lookedThe lies she saidEvery thought in her headThe guys she took into her bedThe food she was not fedHer real beauty was deep insideBut no one saw it, Thats the reason she diedI haven't been writing for a while..But I came up with this when I was reading about models and eating disorders
