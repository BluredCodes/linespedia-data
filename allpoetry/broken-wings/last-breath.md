---
title: "Last Breath"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "last-breath"
source: "AllPoetry"

The last time I saw you,
The last time you were near,
you were my last breath.
I miss your voice,
I miss your touch,
your jokes,
your smile,
your tears,
I miss the way you made me smile,
I miss the way you made me cry,
I miss your view on life
I miss everything about you,
You were my last breath

For the one that got away.
