---
title: "Ocean's of Hope"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "oceans-of-hope"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27559-Oceans-of-Hope-by-Broken-Wings"
---

Burning of solitude in my head
The loneliness in my bed
Swift prays on wings
Take me away from all these things
Clear my mind of all the hate
Butterflies of destiny guide my fate
Bring me to peace of mind
Dolphins in the sea of hope, let me find
Soar threw the stars, melt away all lies
Meditate till the day the anger dies
