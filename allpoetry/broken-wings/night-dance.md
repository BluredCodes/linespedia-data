---
title: "Night Dance"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "night-dance"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The women of the Night reached their lovely veils across the sky
The soft black velvet covered the day and brought the night
The stars were high in the sky above and they smiled down on a field of children
Their soft laughter echoed around the field and reached up to the heavens
The crystal moon beams fell on their swift moving bodies
Hand in hand they danced in the moonlight laughing happily
Step by step hop by hop, they danced their night away
