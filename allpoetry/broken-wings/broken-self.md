---
title: "Broken Self"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "broken-self"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5749-Broken-Self-by-Broken-Wings"
---

More and more you pressure me.
You are there in my dreams
You are there when I go to sleep your there when I wake.
I just want to scream Leave me alone!!!
But you'll always be there.
The more I look at you the more I hate you.
I cant believe I have to live with you every day of my life.
Dying wont solve anything you'll still be there.
I guess if I hate you so much I should stop looking in the mirror.
