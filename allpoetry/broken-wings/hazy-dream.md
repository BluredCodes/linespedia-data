---
title: "Hazy Dream"
writer: "Broken Wings"

The day was almost finished and the night was soon to come 
The golden sun let out a yawn because his day was almost done 
An enchanting ebony veil engulfed what was left of the light 
And brought to the land a beautiful starry night 
This land I speak of is not too far away 
I'm sure you have been there but perhaps not today
Close your eyes my love, and dream of wonderful things 
Take my hand, I'll show you this land, even if you're without wings
Between awakening and sleep is where you'll find it 
Go there and you will be free from everything you fear
And of course in this place you will never shed a tear
A lover's paradise, a dreamer's heaven
A perfect place for everyone to go 
A place to escape all your friends and even your foes
Now close your eyes and come with me 
Do not worry, hope is the only key you will need
Take a jump and let yourself fall,
And enjoy this Hazy Dream that has captured us all
