---
title: "Lost Happiness"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "lost-happiness"
source: "AllPoetry"

Bring me back to that placeDeep in my heartWhere I loved you from the very startWhere the children playedAnd where it was always dayWhere you and I lived flying togetherAnd always imagined dieing together.But I knew it wouldn't lastAnd would someday become the past.A dark cloud took you awayAnd the night took over the dayThe children became hateful beastsAnd made my hopes and dreams their feastAnd my wings the only token of our loveSoon became mangled and broken.
