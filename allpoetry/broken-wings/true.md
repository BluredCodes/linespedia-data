---
title: "True"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "true"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7062-True-by-Broken-Wings"
---

Hold me tell me its okay
Tell me you'll be here all day
Know I need you
Because my love is true
Be here when I'm feeling blue
Your love is all I ever knew
Of course it didn't last
Of course it is now in the past
But when I lie down in bed
With all these thoughts in my head
I think of my love for you
And how bringing you back is something I can not do
Even if you are not here
Losing you completely is something I fear
But even if we are far apart
You will always remain in my heart
