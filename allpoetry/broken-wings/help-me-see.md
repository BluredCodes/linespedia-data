---
title: "Help me see"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "help-me-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6062-Help-me-see-by-Broken-Wings"
---

Darkness takes over the lightBroken wings take over flightHate covers all loveHope is shot down like a doveIn this world its hard to seeAll the beauty that you show meI thank you though for what you have doneTo show me light when there was no sunIn my heart is where you'll stayForever with the light of dayIt doesn't make much sense but its for someone I love very much and has opened my eyes to all the beauty in the world.
