---
title: "Lost Trust"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "lost-trust"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6701-Lost-Trust-by-Broken-Wings"
---

Trust is something I gave to you
Trusting is hard for me and you knew
But you threw it all away
It hurts me to this very day
Trust you or not I have to choose
Or soon you will be what I lose
I love you with all my heart
I have from the very start
I wish I could turn back time
And turn everything back to fine
But as the days go on it gets worse
Trust for me is like a curse
Always breaks hearts and never forgives
Trust is darkness, and in my soul it lives
Soon enough you'll be gone
Then night it will always be, never dawn
If I trust again please let it be you
I hope it is something that I can do
