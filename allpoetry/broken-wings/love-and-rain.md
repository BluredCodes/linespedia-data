---
title: "Love and Rain"
writer: "Broken Wings"
slug: "love-and-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6354-Love-and-Rain-by-Broken-Wings"
---

I stand outside your house, the rain soaking my bone.
I watch you threw a window, speaking on the phone.
In my eyes you can see my fears
The rain is washing away my tears.
Cold and wet I keep watching you
Wondering if you found someone new.
You vanish from my sight
The rain clouds take over the light
Your door opens and you step outside
You stare at me, knowing that I lied
Turning to leave I feel you by my side
Smiling to myself I turn around.
With your arms around me I am found
With the rain falling down on us we kiss
Kissing you is something I have missed
Love and Rain, I wish I could stay here forever
Leave your arms, I hope I will never
