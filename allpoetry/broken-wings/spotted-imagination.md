For those who always get the question 'Are you high?' and reply 'Yes! Im high on life' And also for anyone who loves to let their imagination run wild! Uh-oh! It's happening again! I swear its not a sin!But those purple frogs are out again. Orange spots all on their backs, Really, I swear I'm not whack. Who knows what they're after, Maybe they just like the sound of laughter. But once in a while everyone should seeThese purple frogs that are after me.lol no I don't take any drugs. I just wanted to write a wacky poem and a good friend of mine always talks about purple frogs with orange spots. Also its good to let the crazy side of you run free now and there
