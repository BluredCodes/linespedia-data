---
title: "My Friend"
writer: "Dark Starz"
slug: "my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9782-My-Friend-by-Dark-Starz"
---

I've shared my tears and fears
With you only
Only you know the depth
Of my grief, my pain, my anger
You have listened to me cry
And offered unconditional love
Sitting beside me
And sometimes putting your hand on mine
I love you so much 
I love you so much

But now is the time to say goodbye
It breaks my heart into a thousand pieces

I love you so, my cat of 15 years
I know we will meet again
But for now, I don't want you to suffer
Please leave this world knowing I love you
I love you, Rocky

(*Dedicated to Rocky; humanely eased from suffering on June 4, 2001*)I don't even remember writing this.
