My story has no beginning, middle, or end.

I have learned so much,
On this healing path that I'm on,
This road has had so many hills and valleys.

I have seen sunny meadows,
The sun glinting through the trees.
Birds singing, bunnies racing through the flowers.
Shadows flit along the ground,
When light breezes blow.

I've also been through deeply wooded valleys.
I can't see where I'm going when I'm here.
Anxiety rises when animals growl at me from the darkness.

I try to run through these woods.
But I trip on a mossy exposed root, 
And I'm still here,
Looking up at the tall trees all around me.

I get up and brush myself off,
And I find my path again.

Trying to rush through any part of this journey,
Just slows my progress.

I am grateful for this road,
I have finally found my path in this life.I'm trying to describe my life in this one.
