So many moods, so many moods
They overwhelm me
Where do they come from?

A calm pond on a sunny day.
Birds singing in the trees,
Unseen fish go about their unseen lives,
I am this pond.

A firecracker,
One among many on the display table.
Innocent enough it looks,
But at the touch of a match,
It explodes,
Throwing off sparks in every direction,
And people must back away.
I am this firecracker.

Despair,
Most awful of all.
Nothing to fight for,
Nothing to live for.
Threatening to break your spirit,
To suffocate your soul.
How to rise above it?
I am despair.I wrote this on a day that I was having severe mood swings.
