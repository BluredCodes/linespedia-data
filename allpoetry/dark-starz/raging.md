So much rage.
Boiling and churning,
Threatening to overflow,
Feeling dangerous and murderous,
What do I do,
With so much rage?

I rant, rave, yell, scream,
Trying to get it out.
How to express it!!
It hurts.
I hurt.
Why does it have to be this way?I wrote this the day before I went to court against an ex.  I was so angry that day!
