title: "Weakness"
writer: "SRanfield"

Strong as steel, light as a feather, 

Emotion is a double-edged sword, 

Happiness can bring out the best, 

Jealousy can tear it all away... 

Love purifies all, 

Obssession taints it... 

The mind can be a strength,

And it can be a weakness, 

Poetry is not a weakness, it purifies, 

Never has a tainted thought passed through 

the fingers of a poet, 

Without the worst intention, 

Words are not a weakness, 

They can purify, but can also taint, 

They are only a weakness to those who 

Bring out the taint, 

Who are willing to tear it all away, who are weak, 

Never underestimate Emotion's power, 

It can lighten, darken, purify, taint, help,

Criticize, understand, ignore, accept, shun,

The chain's weakest link...
