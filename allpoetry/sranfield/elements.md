Fire alluring,

Dancing wildly,

Flickering flames in hypnotic ritual



Wind capricious,

Power unrelenting,

Gusts burst into harmonious melody 



Earth rooted,

Terra firma,

The medium of our being



Water wading,

Forever patient,

Douses the flame,

Wears down the earth,

Slows the wind

Gets along with only itself
