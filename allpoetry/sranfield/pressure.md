Pressure builds inside
I'm ready to explode
My energy
So rapidly sapped
Robbing me of my strength
My life sucked away
Like a leech
I'm weak, almost too weak
To hold my pen
I feel my vision fading
I hear a distant flushing
Excellent...she's out of the bathroom...
