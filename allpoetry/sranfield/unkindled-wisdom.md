title: "Unkindled Wisdom"

slug: "unkindled-wisdom"

Loneliness crowds my mind
In the upstairs to gates of slumber
Starry nights flooding my vision
Peace and everlasting tranquility 
Lull my soul to rest
In spiritual lullaby
And rekindle the fire in my heart
In rhythmic melody of
Syncopated laughter
To cast down my eyes
And slip into eternal rest
Of starry nights
Consuming my soul
With the invading peace and tranquility
Gone, my life winks out of existence
