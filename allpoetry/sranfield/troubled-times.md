Troubled times,
Life sublimes,
Into the modern era,
Where sin is in.

Chaotic times,
Church bells chime,
Beckoning the world,
To follow before it's too late.

Dangerous times,
And dangerous lives,
Lived by the nonbeliever

Blinded by lies and fad,
Hurt by media gone mad.

Should you be fat?
Should you be thin?
Should you be muscular?
You don't know what's in.

It shouldn't matter
Because Love doesn't judge

Especially the love that comes from our Savior

Deceit rings in the empty hearts of the world
While those few believers shunned by all
Are cast away as they pray for you

In the last hours
Before we go

I want the world to know
That I hope you take my words

And think them through

And I hope to see you

In heaven too.
