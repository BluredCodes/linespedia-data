in the fluorescent black night sky
  i am within the dead cats eye.

wash away the sins of the earth
  try as you might with frankienscents and mirth.

commanding under authority i rule the fall
  do you not reconize me as i call?

i am the stumbling block in which i cast
  for times and time and half a time will i last.

for i am the vampires tooth and fang
  for the last thing you will hear from me is a bang.

you and your kind killed heavens holy lamb
