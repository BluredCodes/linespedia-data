i have danced when i could not walk
  i have joyfully sang when i could not talk.

i have bowed before gods and kings
  i have done this on top of angel wings.

i heard the dreaded voices and cried out with fear
  i heard the dreaded voices when i could not hear.

i seen the angels on the day that they fell
  i have been to their abode in a place called hell.

i seen the one who was mighty and great
  on that dreadful day when he sealed his fate.

now known as the prince and power of air
  with the head of a flatten bull and long shaggy hair.

through the winds he did utter my name
  though i be sinful, who is to blame?

through my weakness i invited him in me
  looking through a tinted glass i could not see.

seeking knowledge i did once fear
  only because i knew he was near.

lying in wait as a lion would do
  stalking me as prey, and this i knew.

lucifer smiling and sitting on his throne
  and me in front of him standing all alone.

at that moment i remembered the blood that was shed
  so i fell on my face as one that was dead.

i prayed to the father in heaven above
  and the spirit descended upon me in the form of a dove.

but before, the river styx i did cross
  through the boggy swamps and hanging moss.

the souls of those who did bad things
  the torture they endured was as a scorpion sting.

the stone and fire and narrow pathways
  where all the souls are held at bay.

the fear of falling in the lake below
  for whats in the water god only knows.

the beast that stands before me waiting to swallow me whole
  with giant fangs and eyes that glow.

inching towards me with great care
  feeling his breath through the musty air.

one giant leap he did take
  with me ducking as i could he fell into the lake.

over the edge of the pathway i did look
  and the beast was screaming as he was being cooked.

looking ahead foward i did proceed
  a sign with a warning i did not heed.

"no living fleshh beyond this point"the sign read
  "or you will become as one of the undead".

ignoring the sign and climbing a stone
  a voice screamed to me,"son of man, be gone".

i picked up a pebble and started to throw
  and the darkness around me started to glow.

i turned and ran as fast as i can
  when in front of me appeared another man.

i asked the other man what he was doing here
  he just smiled at me and told me not to fear.

he was there to gather his souls
  to save them from the burning coals.

"all my children come out of this place" he did say
  for unto you is salvation given this very day.

for i have prepared a place for you in my fathers house
  and there you will go no more out.

all your sins did i bear
  and it is for you that i do care.

so the gates of hell were broken assunder
  with a voice of authority as loud as thunder.

the prince of hell opened his mouth in protest
  he tried to keep the souls for himself, he tried his best

he cried to adam to come out of hell
  the first man created, the first man that fell.

he cried to the other souls to come out of that place
  he cried to the other souls that had fallen from grace.

the captain of death and the prince of hell were in a debate
   begging the lamb not to break the brass and iron gate.

for the prince of hell was defeated and the battle won
  for the evil was trodden under foot and there was no need to run.
