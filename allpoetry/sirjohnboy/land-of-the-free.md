the land of the free  in the media the pitchers that we see,an attack on America, the land of the free.  the two towers falling so mighty and great,and in turn, osama bin laden sealing his own fate.  boasting to the world his point of view,if he only knew what we were going to do.  osama bin laden wanted alive or dead,a five million dollar bounty the president said.  a bombing campaign that is happening this day,where all the terrorist are held at bay.  america will come out victorious as you will soon see,because this is our home, the land of the free.
