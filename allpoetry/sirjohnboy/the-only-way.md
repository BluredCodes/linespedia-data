the only way



the night is here and the cold winds blow,
  when the sun retired to the horizon below.

the winter winds make such an eerie sound 
  when the suns' place is no more to be found.

the strange sounds that happen only at night,
  will give the bravest soul somewhat of a fright.

paralized in fear and my eyes growing wide,
  something just brushed against my side.

i look to the left and look to the right,
  these are the strange sounds that happen only at night.

a movement in the trees im starting to hear,
  i am unable to move and paralized with fear.

so i closed my eyes and started to pray,
  "please, god father show me the way".

i strayed from the path and was considered wayward,
  and now i know the only true way is jesus our savouir.
