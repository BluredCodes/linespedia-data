on this night when the angels take flight
  you can see the twinkle of the lights.

on the houses and in the trees
  pass another present if you please.

the wrapping paper that is being torn
  on this night was a savior born.

the star in the east which the wise men seen
  this is what christmas truly means.

so tell a friend and tell a stranger
  about that night jesus was lying in a manger.

on the cross his blood he did shed
  on that day he was pronounced dead.

he rose once again to never again die
  to be with us always and never say bye.

it is our souls that he saved you and me
  from the bonds of sins were we set free.

and jesus said that he would come back once again
