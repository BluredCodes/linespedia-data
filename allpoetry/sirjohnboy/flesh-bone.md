---
title: "flesh bone"
writer: "sirjohnboy"
slug: "flesh-bone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/173-flesh-bone-by-sirjohnboy"
---

with his wings spread and an evil thought
  rushing to that great and terrible battle that is to be fought.

as his eyes cutting as a razor would do,
  deceiving the chosen people known as the jews.

with a tounge as sharp as a knife and a voice of hate
  speaking words coated in honey; using this as bait.

with your flesh being ripped and your brain being torn,
  of this torture you were warned.

with a mighty angel casting him to the ground,
  and his place in heaven was no more to be found
