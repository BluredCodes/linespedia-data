where did thou goest, o'happiness?
to where did thou escapeth to? i scream as one in great travail. your ear did you turn from me. your face hidden. return unto me o'happiness. my soul is left desolate unto me. sorrow has occupied the place where you once dwellest. misery has defeated me as an enemy trodden under foot. suffer it to be so when i scream....
