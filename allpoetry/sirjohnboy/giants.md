a faint echo of tiimes of old
  the voice that speaks of pure gold.

the giants that once walked the land
  over the seas and through the sand.

invisible and yet seen
  so clever and keen.

rulers of the shinning stars
  rulers of all near and far.

singing songs of worship and praise
  when god said," mankind will i raise."

finding mortal women lovely and fair
  having freewill and doing as they dared.

doing the very thing that god did hate
  and in turn sealing their own fate.

opening his mouth lucifer did fall
  and unto his angels he did call.

a third of them to be exact true
  falling to earth as the morning dew.

to the abyss they fell
  to a place known as the eternal hell.

to rise again on the last day
  to him will god repay...
