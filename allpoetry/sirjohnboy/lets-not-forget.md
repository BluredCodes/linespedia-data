for the life that was given, this be known
and never again is man alone.

  the life that was given and the blood that was shed,
the grave has no victory, no, not even for the dead.

  though the fear still lingers in all mans hearts,
this is not enough to set us apart.

  for it was from grace that all men fell,
an eternity destin to hell.

  this was our lot for the sin that was handed down'
through his death with salvation are we crowned.

  you know the one im talking about, the one pure and true
jesus christ died for mans sins, thats me and you.

  so without a doubt get on your knees and pray,
because jesus is coming back one of these days.

  so do beleive without a shadow of a doubt,
or you will be the one left out.

  for this is a saying both true and great,
this is an arguement without debate.

  so listen to the lord and what he has to say,
for jesus could come for you this very day..
