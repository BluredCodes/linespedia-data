the soldiers of marching hell
  marching out of their prison cells.

from the abyss where they were bound
  marching in formation without a sound.

though not all were loosed out of that place
  but all that are in the pit fell from grace.

bent on destruction and howling with rage
  with war i heaven and the battle thier leader did wage.

free will is something not to be taken lightly
  it is a gift from the god almighty.

in the infernal kingdom where there is a throne
  with demons falling to the earth in an inverted cone.

though through our eyes we cannot see
  this is the truth known so let it be.

lucifer plotting his next demise
  for he will come to you in the most clever disguise.

some were so evil that they cannot be set free
  and there eyes were taken away so they cannot see. 

though they can hear all that goes on
  through their ignorance they have reaped what they have sown.

the torture in that place they do bear
  trying to escape as much as they dare.

being sent early to their final destination.
  being left to their own annihilation.

a third of the angels he took
  as it is written in that wonderful book.

the dragon did battle with all his might
  when the arc angel threw him down to earth out of heaven sight.

being thrown from heaven and hitting the ground
  their place in heaven was no more to be found.

a third of heaven population can be found no more
  to look upon them now would be a great eye sore.

if you want to see them your soul you must sell
  then you can join them in a place called hell.

now you sold your soul and what have you to gain
  material goods fortune and fame.

temporary zen is what you will receive
  and eternal punishment is what thou hast achieved.

so thank the one who is good and true
