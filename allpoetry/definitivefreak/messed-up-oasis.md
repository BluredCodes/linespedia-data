How many special people change, how many lives are living strange
Where were you when I was starting to cry
Look into the wall of my mind's eye
You think you know but you don't know why
There is one thing they can never give me
Their hearts can never be my home
They're trying hard to put me in my place and they don't think it's a pity
I hitched a ride with my soul by the side of the road
As I face the sun I cast no shadow
Don't go away, tell me what to say, but say for me to stay
I don't need time, no I don't need time just to do things wrong
I feel a little down today and they've got much to say
Life is hit and miss, and this
And baby after all, they'll always forget my name
Need a little time to wake up, wake up
I've gotta say what I say, but everyone always gets in my way
This is all too much for me to take
Everybody knows and they're all saying something
And I'm a sound so very loud that no one can hear
I am like a child with nothing to lose but my mind
