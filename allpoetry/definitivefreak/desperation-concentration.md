Laura go mad
Laura go crazy
Everyone knows
She is insanely
Going over the edge
Ready to burst
Ready to face
The very worst
She could be
Schizophrenic
Or anything else
That could make her panic
She is obsessed
With the slightest things
She just wants
To sprout a pair of wings
To fly away
Far away from here
So no one could know
Why she lives in fear
No one understands
What goes on in the mind
Of someone who doesn't want to be
Left behind
Her head is pounding
With all this mess
She's telling the world
To give it a rest
The world won't listen
The world doesn't care
The world just wants
To strap her in a chair
The portal to her mind
Is completely open
Unable to close
With things that were spoken
Nobody listens
To what she has to say
They just tell her
To stay away
Letting the river flow
From her eyes does sting
So does being alone
Without anything
Her whole right side is aching
She's tired of doing the right thing
Why won't someone help her
Bring her out of suffering
Curtains move
In her room
But she never did
Make them zoom
The only thing
She realy does
Is listen to words
In a song that was
Not in her head before
Which are now
At least they don't think of her
As just a silly old cow
Nobody takes her seriously
For what she really is
They all think she's just
An outdated old fizz
She thinks they all
Want her to die
All she wants
Is to know why
Why they want her gone
Away from them for the rest of their lives
She wants to know what way
With a rope or with knives
What she really wants to say is
She wants realisation
On how to live through
Desperation concentration
When no one wants to help
She's no one to turn to
To help her, to know
What she's going through
Now she wants to say
No more bad sensation
Please, help, no more
