I thought love was blind, then I saw you 
I thought love had abandoned me 
But it brought you into my life 

All my dreams take me back to you 
I never knew, but it was what my heart wanted to do 
Deep down I know we're through 
But still my dreams take me back to you 

When you left me, my heart, it broke in two 
I didn't know how I would get through 
I don't know what I'm supposed to do 

All my dreams take me back to you 
I never knew, but it was what my heart wanted to do 
Deep down I know we're through 
But still my dreams take me back to you
