title: "Colour The Face"

Colour the faces of friends with smiles
Watch how the sun shines in their eyes
Your love, your laughter, your essence
Your sorrow, your tears, your every presence

The way you speak to the ones around you
Shows them that your love is true
You show them all what you're made of
Every trouble you rise above

And every corner my life turns
You are there to show your concerns
No matter how I tell you not to care
When I fail, you are there

I wanna tell you it's you I appreciate
For it's the ones who hurt me that you hate
So colour the face of me, your friend
