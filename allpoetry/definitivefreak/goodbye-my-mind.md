title: "Goodbye, My Mind"

Quote the songs that melt my heart 
Cos soon I know we'll be apart 
It's not the way I want to be 
This thing I feel inside of me 

I know not of what you say 
You know I want it the other way 
The times you felt and heard and seen 
The lines you read in between 

You spoke the words I wanted to hear 
But now I see you've disappeared 
I hate the way you won't come back 
Now everything I see is black 

Quote the songs that melt my heart 
Cos I've known right from the start 
That when I have sung and rhymed
