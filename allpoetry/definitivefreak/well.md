Emotions hide beneath the broken wings 
That once knew how to fly 
Saying what they are or were 
Is not enough to get by 

Friendship provokes thought 
To where I'm going some day 
Will they be going with me 
Or will they be forced to stay 

I'm tired of saying all things 
To make people feel nice 
If only they knew how I really thought 
Then maybe they'd think twice 

About knowing me 
Experiencing my thoughts and memories 
Would you? Could you?
