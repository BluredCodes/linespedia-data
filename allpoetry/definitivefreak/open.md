Open, exposed
You told me how you felt
You felt scared and alone
You made my heart melt

You told me everything
How you were afraid to let me know
I said to you to not be worried
And to let the feeling flow

You know how much I want you
I know how much you want me
We know everything
Your love you let me see

I want to be near you
I want to feel your pain
I want to hold you
And shelter you from the rain

You say you find it hard
To let someone in
But yet, your heart
You let me win

I want to let you know
Before I go to sleep
My body, my mind, my heart
Are all yours to keepWrote this in the very early hours of the morning near the end of July 2001. It's for a very special friend of mine. ©  on Oct 30 2001 11:28 AM PST, Laura Rose Knowles   personal  • love
