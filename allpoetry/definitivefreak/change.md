Sometimes there's the daytime
Sometimes there's night
An equal balance of power
That feels just right

I watch the moon change shape
And the sun change place
While my own heart and mind
Begin to race

The simplest things
Can make me smile
But the hardest things
Make me run a mile

So again I look above
To the moon and the sun
And I realise then, my life
