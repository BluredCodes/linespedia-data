A smile appeared on my lips 
A tear fell from my eye 
A memory came from my mind 
A question asking why 

Looking back over the past 
Those days I knew so well 
They left me behind and moved on without me 
And on my knees I fell 

I cried out to the sun in the sky above 
That it would darken the thoughts inside 
But like my past, it moved on 
And left me with nowhere to hide 

But I don't want to move on 
Without doing things the right way 
I wanna know where to go from here
