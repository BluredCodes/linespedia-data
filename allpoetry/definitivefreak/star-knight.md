I see the stars that shine for you 
Sparkling in the dark of night 
My heart flutters with the memories of 
Your eyes that shine so bright 

No word I say can truly express 
The feelings that we share 
My heart is wild with lust and love 
This passion I cannot bear 

Here I am alone without you 
My arms are empty and cold 
My heart is warm and full of love 
For you, my knight so bold 

Again I look for the stars 
The ones that shine above 
Many a knight may shine for me
