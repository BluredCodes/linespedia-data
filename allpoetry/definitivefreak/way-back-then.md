Way back then I had you
Way back then you were mine
Way back then you hurt me
Way back then you said goodbye

I know it's hard, it's hard to say goodbye
When it's the only thing I don't wanna do
Before I go, let me say one thing
The only thing I wanna do
Is just stay right here with you

Way back then we had something
Way back then we used to try
Way back then my heart was open just for you
Way back then you made me cry

I know it's hard, it's hard to say goodbye
When it's the only thing you want me to do
But before you go, let me say just one more thing
The only thing that I wanna do
Is just stay right here with you

The only thing I wanna do
Is just stay right here with you

With you
With you
With you, forever with you
