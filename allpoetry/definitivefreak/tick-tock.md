Mental chains are a part of me
Tying me to something not there
Unlock me? Is it possible?
Go on, try if you dare

Times are changing
Time to move on call
How can I do that?
Each time I try, I fall

Is it time to try?
Is it time to cry?
Is it time to win?
Is it time to begin?

Begin to trust again
Put all the fears behind
I must free myself
