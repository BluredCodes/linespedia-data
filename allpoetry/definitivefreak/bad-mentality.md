I haven't got a good mind
They watch me walk around
They laugh at me
See me fall over time and time again
They don't help me
They don't care
They don't give a damn what happens to me
They don't even see, they walk away
I thought they cared about me
I see now that they don't
They pretend they don't see
Me rocking back and forward
In a chair with a foaming mouth
Crying inside for peace of mind
Wanting the pain and torment to leave me alone
Just so I can get on with my life
I haven't got a good mind
It's filled with hate and anger
I'm thinking this will never stop
