There's a chill in the air
I'm in a place I don't want to be
I need to get away for a while
Maybe it'll do good for me
A place of calmness
And serenity
A place to reveal
My true identity
A place where no one else can be
No human anyhow
A place where I need to go
Where I want to be right now
I wonder where you think it is
The place I think of in my head
It's the place I'm going now
It's called..... BEDThe final part of a progression of six written between 2:52am and 3:59am on 26th February 2001. ©  on Oct 26 2001 12:23 AM PST, Laura Rose Knowles   angst
