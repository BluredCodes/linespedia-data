I try to look at the picture on the wall
But all of a sudden the phone starts to call
And slowly but surely the tears begin to fall
Tears of joy, because I know it's you

I look to the sky, to the clouds above
My heart begins to flutter, like the wings of a dove
Cos I know what I am feeling, is the feeling of love
Love, because I know it's you

I sit in my room alone, waiting for you to come in
To inspire me through thick and thin
I hope you know my heart you can win
