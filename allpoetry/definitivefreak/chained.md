The right time
When will be the right time?
To tell you I don't feel good
And that I ain't in the mood

I linger
Long enough to follow your finger
And watch what you do
For an eternity I'm chained to you

My senses
Want to build up defenses
Against your strong touch
Even though I want you so much

And you're chained to me
Do either of us want to be free?
There will never be a right time
