I thought I was a poet-
	so it seemed, 
	but not today
	reading the masters,
	did me in.
	
	They -with their virtuoso of 
	frothy mien.

	Further yet, 
	some pristine goddess
	slipped over my image
	rashly dispelling a 
	tude-

	Pensive,
        I’ll just read
	and shackle the rage
	thus, know I’ll improve 
	and finish the page!
M.D.B/CookieZeal
