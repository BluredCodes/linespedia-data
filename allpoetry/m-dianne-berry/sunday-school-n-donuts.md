title: "Sunday School 'n Donuts"

Waiting at the old church door,
	the lady in the booth
	greeted the new visitor,
	a young rebellious youth.

	This teen was so mistrusting-
	her heart to few she’d bear,
	her grandma, the only one
	who'd ever brought her there.

	But seeing her resistance,
	the lady with a grin
	offered her some breakfast treats
	instead of going in.

	The young girl started smiling
	and thought it rather odd
	that she was offered donuts
	inside the House of God.

	It was the chocolate eclair
	that made her hestitate,
	so chose to sit and talk to 
	the lady with the plate.

	“Why are you so nice to me?”
	cried out the bitter child,
	“You don’t know how bad I am,
	cause trouble and I'm wild."

	“Here’s a fresh cold glass of milk
	to go with these eclairs,”
	the lady skipped her comment
        to prove that someone cares.
	
	It wasn’t very long before
	the young girl shared her heart,
	and though they missed the service-
	this was the favorite part.

	She’s never yet forgotten
	the lady found a way-
	'twas Sunday School and donuts
	that brought her back to pray!
