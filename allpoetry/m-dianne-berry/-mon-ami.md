You are~ 
	the lilac in my rainbow
	the faintest sliver
	dividing darker hues
	these moods-
	
	a purpled piquant fragrance after
	fresh spring rain
	~ you!

	We are~
	violet rays through the window
	tiny light strands
	dancing rosy ray’s
	duo-

	a dew drop spectral to this heartful muse
	gathering
	~ us!
