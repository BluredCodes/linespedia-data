---
title: "Worst Mistake"
writer: "Bittersweet"
slug: "worst-mistake"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can't go on without you.
You are always on my mind,
no matter how much I cry.
It kills me to see you with someone else.
I just can't take it.
If only I would have payed more attention to you,
talked to you more, treated you right.
Maybe you would have stayed if I gave you what you deservered.
You deserved my cooperation, my feelings, my attention.
Yet I couldn't do something that simple.
Know that you're gone,
all that I think about is the worst mistake i have ever made,
the mistake of losing you.
