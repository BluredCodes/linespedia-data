---
title: "Being With You"
writer: "Bittersweet"
slug: "being-with-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52955-Being-With-You-by-Bittersweet"
---

Being with you makes my life seem so worth while,
I haven't ever felt this way about anyone before,
You are the only one for me.
I couldn't handle being with anyone else,
I would miss you way too much.

Being with you makes me feel,
so open, so free, so unique.
You make me feel important,
like I have a purpose.

I love being with you,
you make all of my worries go away.
You make my day complete,
like nothing can go wrong.
