---
title: "Why?"
writer: "Bittersweet"
slug: "why"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why can't I be the person you want me to be?
Why don't I do the things you want me to do?
Why do I mess everything up?
Why won't I change my ways?
Why must I suffer when I think of you?
For all these questions I have no answers.

Why can't I make you happy?
Why don't I make things better?
Why do I let you down?
Why won't I speak to you?
Why must I need help from you for every little thing?
For all these questions I have no answers.
