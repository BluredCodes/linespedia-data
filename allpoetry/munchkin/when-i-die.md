WHEN I DIE
WILL IT BE MY TIME TO GO?
WILL I HAVE DONE
EVERYTHING I WANTED TO DO?
WILL I BE READY TO GIVE LIFE UP
FOR SOME UNEXPECTED JOURNEY?
WILL I HAVE A CHOICE
TO LEAVE THE ONE WORLD I KNOW WELL?
WILL ALL MY QUESTIONS 
BE ANSWERED TO THERE FULLEST?
WILL I BE HAPPY 
TO LEAVE THIS PLACE I CALL HOME?
WILL DEATH BE PAINFULL
OR WILL I BE AT EASE?
WILL THERE BE SOMEONE 
WAITTING FOR ME AT THE GATE?
WILL I GO TO HEAVEN
OR WILL I GO TO HELL?
WILL I BE MISSED 
BY THE PEOPLE AROUND ME AT THE TIME?
WILL I HAVE ACCOMPLISHED 
ALL THAT I WANTED TOO?
WILL I HAVE MADE A DIFFERENCE 
IN THE WORLD THAT I’VE JUST LEFT?
WILL I EVER FIND 
THE ANSWERS TO THESE QUESTIONS?
I DON’T KNOW
