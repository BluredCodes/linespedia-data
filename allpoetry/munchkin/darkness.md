I think about what has gone on
I sometimes think it was for the best
I look out and all I see is darkness
No light, nothing
I wonder, will the light ever come back
Will I ever be the way I was? 
So happy to be alive, so happy to have that light
Will my life go back to the way it was?
Frigid and cold
The light is what warmed it up.
The light made me feel protected
The light gave me meaning
The light made me feel secure
The light was the best thing to happen to me
Although the light isn’t gone I feel like it is.Just clearing my head
