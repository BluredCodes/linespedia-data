All i can think about is what happened between us
How i feel about everything 
How i love you so much
To be in your arms
To be able to feel your touch
To feel secure again
To feel safe 
That's what gets me through the day
That's what gives me the motivation to wake up in the morning
That little bit of hope is what i go on

My feelings won't change
They never will
I wish i could see you
But my wishes don't come true.This is just what is going through my head... i'm sorry
