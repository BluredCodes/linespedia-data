---
title: "I Wish"
writer: "dottie"

I wish-
I had all those days back
when you were little and sweet.
I hold you here in my gray old head
my heart most nearly breaks.
For there you stand all done and all
a person all your own.
I wish-
I had all those days back
when you used your words so fancifully.
I would have paid more attention,
I would have spent more time,
I would have gone on the circus ride
one more time one more time one more time.
Now there's war a looming and economy not booming.
And there you stand all done and all
a person all your own.
I wish I had those days back
to hold just to myself,
to kiss you more and love you more
and do better than I did.
But there you stand all done and all
a person all your own
and here I sit.
To wish-
I wish -
I could do it all again.
