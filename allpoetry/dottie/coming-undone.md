---
title: "Coming Undone"
writer: "dottie"
slug: "coming-undone"

The world was bright and full of adventure
to take off on a dash -on a bet-name a venture!
The world was scary and dark and exciting-
riots, debates,and fads for igniting,
The world was not bland- just black and white-
the gray- all of it's shades-were mine and were right!
My father- old fashioned,
My mother -milk toast,
I knew all the answers,
"Ask me!" I would boast.
But now the world is just not so bright-
an adventure is putting my clothing on right.
My future is scary and the dark's not exciting-
stop that chanting, my lumbago's igniting.
Ralph Nader is older -gee, he got gray!
That shade can't be right -seems just yesterday.
My old blinding father,
My dying old mother-
these lumps in my throat-
Seems I just soon may smother.
I guess for today I will take it on faith-
Someone knows the answers-
Maybe, there are none.
Maybe it's just a stream of consciousness-
coming undone.
