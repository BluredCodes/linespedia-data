title: "This Incarnation"

Well, this incarnation really sucks.

I'm not rich or thin

or famous or 'in'

nobody knows me

'cept family and kin.

I live with an ex who is crazy and vain

who's greatest joy is to bring me more pain.

The scars and the bruises and lies I have told-

bringing up children- putting dreams on hold.

Now parents are dying and I'm getting old

where did they go those visions of gold?

Lost in the mist of days gray and old

lost in the memories of love lost and bold.

My chances have come and gone in a blink and 

now here I sit all alone just to think.

This incarnation really sucks.

I'd like another-

next time with some luck.
