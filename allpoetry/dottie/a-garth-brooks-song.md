I loved you with a loveI thought would bring world peacefor God's sake.I loved you so much I thoughtour love making caused the rainbows.I loved you so much I let you goback to that woman that tormentedyou so much that you came to me.I thought our love was the kindthat people could touch.  I loved you so much that I cutoff all of my hair when it wasover.But it wasn't over once itwas over twice and now I have a songthat talks about it.It's a Garth Brooks songfor God's sake.And I like jazz.
