title: "Momsy"

She died amid purple
surrounded by the silver of
gray haired brothers and sisters
who loved her so well.
And eternal sleep
has soothed the grimace 
of pain from forehead
mouth and cheek.
And peaceful, now, the fight
confessed was 
unwinnable anyway.
So long into the tender
ether send her spirit flying free.
I love you momsy.
Goodbye.
