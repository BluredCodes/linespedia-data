title: "There's No Dust On My Memories"
writer: "dottie"

There is dust on my tables,
furniture and fables.
There is dust on my TV,
(it's very thick and clingy).
There is dust on my porch and
dust on my cat
(imagine that).
But amongst all the dust:
bunnies and motes,
there is one thing that I can denote:
There is no dust on my memories.
For they are handled
da-i-ly.
