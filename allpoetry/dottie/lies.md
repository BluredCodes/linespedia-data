Lies lie lying
and innocence is trying
to keep her shiny satin slippers on.
As she softly treads 
beside 'em 
her shiny satin slippers
are the only things that keep her
from giving up her soul.
The slippers her creator
made her,
were promised not to rust,
and if she kept them on 
she would never loose her trust.
