Self ab-sor-bed drool-ing mass-es.Mad-ness, sad-ness, blood!Stars and ang-els,search-es, tang-els,pre-cious, flow-er buds!Love lost, tears toss'dMur-der,angst,hate,come,my love, please care!Love some words andchew some verbs andsee how fine they taste?They are feast andfit as fod-der,ne-ver one to waste.Let them flow and let them show how sim-ple,full of grace.Love toss'd, tears lost?ah but no ohno no no theworld's a lovely place.
