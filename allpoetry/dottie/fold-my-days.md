title: "Fold My Days"
writer: "dottie"

Fold my days like laundry-Hang my dreams like shirts-hem up all my qualities with all your roles for me.Wash my days like dishes-rinse my desires like cups-dry up all my uniqueness with all your needs for me.Weed my days like gardens-hoe my talents like dirt-chop up all my me-Ness with all your wants of me.To you I am an objectlike clothing, dishes, food-to me I am a subjectand life is my exam.
