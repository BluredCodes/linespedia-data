If your lover leaves youand breaks all your good shitjust rise above itand have a Happy Fit!If your checks are bouncingzinging off the wallsjust rise above ithave a Happy Ball!If you think you are a vampyreskulking in the nightdrink some blood and grow some wings and have a Happy Flight!If your life's a failureand Oprah makes your dayjust rise above itDr. Phil is on today!If your life's a veil of sorrowand corners hold your fearjust rise above itAnd drink a lot more beer!!!
