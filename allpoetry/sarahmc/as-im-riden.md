---
title: "As I'm Ride'n..."
writer: "Sarah.M.C."
slug: "as-im-riden"
source: "AllPoetry"

As I'm ridden in the drivers seat of my hummer I park it beside my Jagand walk into my mansion. Then I sit down on my $600,000 all genuine leather couch, turn on my $20,000 screen TV for looks and then I turn up my $10,000 stereo on max and at twelve-o-clock 98 people start flow'n through my blue tinted diamond front doors for the twelve hour party. I go into my kitchen with marble floor and pure gold ceiling, and tell my maids to cook up A big feast for 100 and I start to party, when I wake up and realize I'm still ridden in my '83' pinto.
