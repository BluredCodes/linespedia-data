---
title: "I wanna thank you...."
writer: "Beyond Zion"
slug: "i-wanna-thank-you"

I sit inside my head
And envy what the ocean says about you
I sit inside my room,
tired of feeling alone without you

But it's all I have
It's all that stayed

I lay in bed,
tied to my skin i feel you,
embracing me
And loving me,
Like you once said you did

Cause it fills me
with anticipation
it fills me with the hope
that there's still hope

All you want me for is my strength

Please come back to me
Don't leave me lying here 
wondering anymore

I hate it when you didn't call
and say you were sorry
about yesterday

I hate it when you say your sorry 
about everything

My walls are trembling,
because i am trembling
because you said:
"I didn't mean to hurt you."

You never mean to.
But you always do.

Thanks.
For never showing up,
for never being there
and most of all,
thanks for being like every one else.
