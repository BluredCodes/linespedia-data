There is an ice cream melting in the sand,
I remember that spot,
we use to walk on that sand when we were 10.
Remember?
Damn, I thought you were so beautiful.
As we grew up I pictured us in the morning hew,
dancing in the kitchen,
over the morning fresh breath of pancakes.
I saw the sun rise and set in your smile,
I felt the autumn breeze in your kiss,
and the nights,
Whoa, what nights we had.
Devious scampering we called it!
Haha, 
I miss you.
The end was so cold,
you were so so cold the last time I saw your face.
The last time I felt your breath,
and the last time I saw your eyes open with life.
Death stole our future.
Stole my happiness.
Fuck You!
When it's my time I am gonna fight,
fight for both of us,
fight for all the still framed 
pictures we missed because of it.
You'll see.
I'll always love you,
my first,
and my only.
I will see you in the clouds,
in the titanium glazed night,
and in my soul.
Sleep peacefully my prince,
I will wake you soon,
and I will love you beyond zion,
and beyond zion is where god can't see.
