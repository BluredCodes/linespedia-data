title: "The Dragonfly's thought"

As the moon chases the sun
over the sky,
I wonder of the moments 
they keep secret.
The thoughts.
The tears.
The screams of why.
I wonder if they are lovers,
forever searching for eachother.
Chasing eachothers shadow,
One warm with passion,
The other cold with regret.
And the stars,
maybe they are the moons tears.
A dragonfly passes me by,
and I think:
"What if?"
What if I become like the moon?
And the night becomes my tomb.
A glitter paved rendeveau with regret,
in this champagne dream.
