On the approach,
A harmless vision of chaos emerges.
The footsteps pause.
The echo is silenced.
And I stand amidst a cluttered,
frightened mind.
And what of my silent stalker?
He grabs my shoulder and whispers:
"I am affraid of god, help me."
I see nothing in him,
not a soul,
not a heart,
not a conscious thought.

It becomes darker some how,
the shadows surround us.
They seem to be fornicating against the pavement.

"I don't know your god, I only know mine."
My reply was strength,
it was my own.

He sighs softly into the breeze,
and the shadows withdraw.
He bestoes a kiss upon my forehead and says:
"True knowledge is to not seek the unseen, but to follow it."

My arms shivered and my eyes swelled.
Has this stranger shone light into an abyss?
He continues:

"Thus is the quest for faith, it a quest for the frightened."

I blinked away a tear and he was gone.
The sun tore threw the trees in the park,
and I was left with my strangers words,
pondering greater things...


Authors Note: I never remebered being given a choice in faith. Until I was older, I realized what faith was. It's not god, it's yourself. Believe in yourself. Much Love to you all, -Zion
