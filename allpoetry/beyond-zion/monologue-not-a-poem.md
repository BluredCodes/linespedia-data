You looked so lost,
and so affraid in the hall today.
What's wrong?
Oh I see.
I'm sorry i didn't know I made you feel like that.
I'm not going anywhere.
Here, use my sleeve to dry your eyes.
I got an idea!
Let me take you somewhere after school.
I can't tell you,
It's a surprise.
I'll meet you at seven o'clock.

(I know, I'll bring a him a vase,
filled with sunflowers.)

You look beautiful,
Are you ready to go?
I can't tell you what the surprise is!
You'll see.

(I love him so much.)

Ok, close your eyes.
It's a little further up,
don't worry I won't let you fall.
Surprise!
Stop that tickles!
I love you to baby,
Your welcome.

(I'm glad he's smiling.)

Yeah, we can go back to my house.


Authors Note: This isn't a poem, it's something I did for some one I once loved that made him smile. And I just wanted to share it with you, so maybe you can share it with some one you love.
