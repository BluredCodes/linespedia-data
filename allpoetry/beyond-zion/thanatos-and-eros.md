---
title: "Thanatos and Eros"
writer: "Beyond Zion"
slug: "thanatos-and-eros"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41003-Thanatos-and-Eros-by-Beyond-Zion"

Bring your walls,
and I will bring the wind.
Bring fire,
and I will bring water.
Bring hate,
and I will bring hope.
Bring violence,
and I will bring peace.
Bring ignorance,
and I will bring knowledge.
Show me a child,
and I will show you the future.
Lend me your weakness
and I will make you strong.
Give me your doubt,
and I will prove faith.
Release your fear,
and I will capture courage.
Pass me the mirror,
and I will show you your beauty.
