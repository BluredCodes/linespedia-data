Wave

Of the sea
Swelling and breaking
on the shore

Tide

Moving slow
As old as the sea
ebb and flow

Storm

Sudden fury
rage with lashing wind
Then it goes

Dunes

Wind driven
Forever restless 
moving sand

Gull

On white wings
Riding on the wind
skims the sea

Boats

Float like toys
over the vast sea
Free from landMinimalist poetry is like impressionist art.It evokes an image or thought.Like a slender gracefully silver blade evokes the concept of flight.
