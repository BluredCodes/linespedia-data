title: "Nocturne to ebon eyes"
writer: "Anonymous Poet"

I saw the sun one morning.
It smiled and seemed to say.
Why do you shield your eyes
as you go on your way?
Do you not gaze longingly
toward the rising moon?
Do the thoughts of falling
stars send your heart to swoon?
I humbly replied prey do not
plague me so the reason to display.
For love rides wings of fantasy
till stolen by the day.
All through the night my
mind is free to dream.
My memory ambles along a
running silvered stream.
Once I loved a gentle girl
with eyes of ebon black.
Only in the dark of night
will ever she come back.
We walked in dreams and fields
of stars.Two lovers hand in hand.
She triped along beside me.
Sweet scented was her hair.
I hear her tinkling voice
float lightly in the air.
She seems to fade as each
star goes out at dawn.
She slips back to my memory
as silent as a fawn.
