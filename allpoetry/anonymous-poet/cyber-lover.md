He's well past his teens
and still lives with his parents.
Each night he roams the chat rooms
Looking for hot cyber mates.
He hides behind a keyboard. 
Lies are all he presents.
He is a loser.He scores zeros
with the real girls he dates.
Why does he do it?Why does he
resort to cheap illusion?
Maybe he lives in a fantasy 
world of his own making.
I've thought about it and 
have came to this conclusion.
Why should he try the real thing
when it's easier just fakeing.Cyber sex is just mental masterbation.
