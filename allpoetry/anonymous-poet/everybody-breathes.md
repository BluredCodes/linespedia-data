Everybody breathes
Even you Labatt
Everybody breathes
All the time we're
breathing
Everybody breathes
Even me
Everbody breathes
like little pumps
you see
Everybody breathes
If you stop you die
Everybody breathes
say no and you lie
Everybody breathes
The air is every where
Everybody breathes
We do with out a care
Everybody breathes
It's natural you see
Everybody breathes
I just didSatire is the sincerest form of flattery.
