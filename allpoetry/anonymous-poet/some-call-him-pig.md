On his first night on duty.
A call from the emergency room.
Twelve year old girl beaten and
raped my her father.
Hands clamped over her eyes
as quite as a tomb.
People stood around her
seemed to talk in a blither.
He asked some questions for his
incident report.
The doctor handed him a sterile
hospital form.
Between sobs she murmured
to the rape center support.
He remembered something out
in his car.
On the dash was an object
from his own child.
Something to remind him.
A small teddy bear.
He took it back in.
The nurse gave a smile.
He held out the toy
fighting back a tear.
The girl in the bed shyly
held out her hand.
Took the bear and clasped
it to her chest.
Yes some call him a pig
But please try to understand.
Not all are bad cops.
Some are the best.
