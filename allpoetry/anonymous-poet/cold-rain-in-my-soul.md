title: "Cold rain in my soul"

Cold rain falls like tears
from a misty memory.
The sky is as grey as
a lost lover's heart.
Dark clouds weep like
mourners they all agree that
none is as lonely as
lover's souls cast apart.
I now walk alone.My face
turned from the rain.
My eyes are cast down
to reflections of the sky.
Forever I will wander.My
heart will always feel pain.
My tears have fallen and
mingled with the rain as I cry.A love poem from my youth.My love life was a roller coaster ride.
