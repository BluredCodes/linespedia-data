Dawn

Day awakes
spreads across the sky
nature yawns

Sun

Brazen eye
looks over us all
never winks

clouds

Soft white clouds
Form animal shapes
airborne zoo

Grey

Loneliness
Deep despairing sky
rain for tears

Dusk

Colors change
palette in the west
paint the sky

Night

Stars come out
each finding their place
takeing turnsThink of these as seeds to grow poems.
