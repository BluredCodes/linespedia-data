Tube tops and too tightlycra stretch pants.Bee hive hair do and painted toe nails.Watches her soapsand waters her plants.Six kids playing in the yardTwo over flowing diaper pails.She sometimes thinks fondlyof nuns and convents.Hey! Cletus I'm busyget your own beer.The saga goes on.
