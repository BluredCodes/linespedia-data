You think I canceled your last I.M.
It was'nt because I was bored.
I really wanted to talk till two A.M.
My server screwed up.My temper just soared.
It was all their fault.I'm sure they will fix it.
Yeah,yeah fix it.Thats the ticket.Inspired by the sites recent server problem.
Also inspired by a comedian whos gimmick was being a compulsive lair.
