We live in a double wide
here in the park
There are two cars on
blocks in my yard
Sit on the porch and shoot
bottle rockets in the dark
I work at the paper mill
but I don't work hard
I have six kids and
a wife I hold dear
Hey! Lurlene since your up
go get me a beer
