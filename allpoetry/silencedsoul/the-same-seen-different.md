title: "The Same Seen Different"
writer: "SilencedSoul"

I hate the way they look at me, 
I hate the stares I get.
I'm not a freak, I wish they'd see
there's more to me I bet.

I hate the tears I cry each day,
I hate the names they call.
They feed on me like I'm their prey,
to them I am nothing at all.

So what if I don't look the same,
or speak the tongue they do.
If you call me I'll come to name, 
if you hurt me I'll cry too.
