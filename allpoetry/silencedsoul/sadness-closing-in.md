title: "Sadness Closing In"

Seek and thee shall find sadness, 
and death at every door.
Cease looking for the happiness, 
for there isn't anymore.

The skys have become moonless,
the tears of heaven fall.
Stop looking for your wishing star, 
for there aren't any at all.

No one is there to help you,
terror leaks inside.
You are in the real world now,
for there's no where left to hide.

As your walls start closing in,
air is scarce to find.
Depression seeps right through you,
for sadness controls your mind.Just pure honesty, what do you think?
