---
title: "I've Lost You"
writer: "SilencedSoul"
slug: "ive-lost-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21449-Ive-Lost-You-by-SilencedSoul"
---

Stress is closing in on me, 
I don't know what to do.
My feelings haven't escaped me,
yet I feel I've already lost you.

The talks have ceased and ended,
there's a silence in the air. 
Your friendly feelings for me have ended,
and I feel like you just don't care. 

Ten times I was about to tell you,
and ten times you shut me out.
My worst fear was of losing you,
day by day that fear came about. 

Stress has taken over me, 
I don't know what to do.
My feelings still haven't escaped me,
yet I know I've already lost you.
