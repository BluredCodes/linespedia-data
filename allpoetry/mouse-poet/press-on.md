---
title: "Press On"
writer: "Mouse Poet"
slug: "press-on"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The day was long and ridged.
Time itself seemed to stand still,

but I still pressed on.

I felt a small gentle breeze
slipping through an open window,

but I still pressed on.

I heard an ever so silent voice
whispering in the wind,

but I still pressed on.

I listened to the gentle words
of the voice as it said to me 
"Come",

but I still pressed on.

I listened to the sound of the voice
growing louder, as it would say 
"Come with me",

but I still pressed on.

I felt the beads of sweat on my forehead
as the words turned into song, saying
"Come with me, and let us be together"

but I still pressed on.

The song turned into a melody
of beauty and peacefulness, saying,
"Come with me, and let us be together,
Let's sing the songs of happiness."

but I still pressed on.

Then, my hands started to tremble
and my chest pounded, as I heard the song,
"Come with me, and let us be together,
Let's sing the songs of happiness
as we dance into the winds of eternity."

and I couldn't press on...

As the music faded 
and the wind ceased to blow,
all that remained was an open window
and an empty space where a leaf now lay
with the message on it written,

"Come".
