---
title: "A Thought"
writer: "Mouse Poet"
slug: "a-thought"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I am thinking a thought
that I ought not think,
I think I should think a thought
that I have not thought to think.
But as I try to think a thought
that I have not thought to think,
I only think of the thoughts
that I thought I ought not think.
Suddenly, as I was thinking a thought
that I thought I thought before,
I thought I thought a thought
that I didn't think I thought before.
And so I thought I would think
about the thought I thought even more
until the thought I thought I thought
wasn't the thought I thought it was before.
