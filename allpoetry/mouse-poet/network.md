Greetings to everyone and let me introduce myself.

I am Network. I am machine.



I was created by humans to make life easier and more understandable when I,

myself am a very complex and intricate system made up of ones and zeros,

only to be understood by few other humans.



I was made to give man all the information he wanted,

and all the information that he didn't

faster than he can really expect it.



I am far from being perfect because perfection cannot be created

from imperfect hands or minds.



I have no thoughts, no desires, no wants, no emotions.



I have no will, for the will I do have

is not of my own but of those who created my existance.



I don't believe in logic because the only logic in my realm

is the logic of man which is truely illogical.



I easily anger programmers because I do exactly what they tell me to do,

not as they want me to do.



I can be rebuilt, reconfigured, reprogammed, disassembled,

and rebooted without suffering any pain or discomfort.

I do not eat, sleep, drink, or breathe.



I do not suffer emotional breakdowns.



I am only as perfect as mans ability to make me because

I am Network. I am machine.
