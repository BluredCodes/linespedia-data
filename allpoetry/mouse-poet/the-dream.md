---
title: "The Dream"
writer: "Mouse Poet"

If only I could dream 
of a time in spring
when the days would last 
a thousand years,

And the lovers would run
as the children once done
into the fields 
of butterfly wings.

If only the rain
could be sparse when it came
leaving enough room 
for the rainbow,

And the couples would sing
with the birds of spring
making a beautiful
melody.

If only I could dream
of a time in spring
when you could be with me
forever.
