---
title: "Foiled Suprise"
writer: "Mouse Poet"
slug: "foiled-suprise"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Working diligently
wanting everything
to be perfect.
Knowing how much
she likes an orderly home.

Hurrying his vacuuming
but not missing a speck.
Trying to finish
before she arrives.

Dusting, mopping,
sweeping, cleaning.
Chores are done,
now the surprise.

Cutting, folding,
shaping, pasting.
Now it is a butterfly.

Wings of foil
shine the light.
Tissue paper colors
make it bright.

Pipe cleaners formed
to make hands and feet.
And now the surprise
is fully complete.

Rattling door
announces her entry,
He presents to her
the precious surprise.

Smiling, she softly
kisses his cheek.
Happily he walks away
out of her sight.

Fading from her lips
is her smile of satisfaction
while gazing at the
delicate butterfly.

Crushing it easily
in her tired hands,
she casts it away
as mere waste.
