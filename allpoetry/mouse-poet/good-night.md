title: "Good Night"
writer: "Mouse Poet"

As the day fades 
and comes to an end, 
I close my eyes and 
think of the morning songbirds 
dancing and playing, 
singing to the rhythm 
of the winds. 

I think of the midday rains 
falling from the heavens 
and letting the droplets 
cleanse me with tenderness. 

I think of the sweet taste 
that comes from the blossoms 
of the evening clover. 

And I think of a starry night, 
seeing the lights of fireflies 
lining the shallow creek beds 
as they prepare 
for their winter sleep.
