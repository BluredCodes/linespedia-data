Oh, Mr. Poet,
I do love your poetry.
How your words speak volumes
and your rhythm sings.
Please write a poem for me.

Let your emotions flow 
and let your pen dance.
Your paper will sing its praises.
Paint me a masterpiece 
of words.

Oh, Mr. Poet,
How I do love your poetry.
