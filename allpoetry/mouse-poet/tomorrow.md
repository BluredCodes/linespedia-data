As I lay down to sleep
under the soft moonlit sky,
I start remembering
the times of long since past.
I remember of a time
when I first found you.
You were as a broken vessel
lying in fragments of emotion.
You were as a wilting flower
with dew drops falling from
your soft petals.
My heart cried out for you.
I gathered you into my arms
and I placed you under my wing.
I proceeded to build you up
and strengthen your emotions.
I raised you up
high into the air...
and you flew away.
I called out for you,
but you didn't come back.
My heart cried once again.
I remember the loneliness,
the anguish, the despair,
that swallowed every inch
of my sanity.
The tears of sadness would flow
like a river from my heart,
drowning me in utter sorrow.
Nothing else mattered
to me anymore.
Nothing.
I remember when I
finally found you again.
All the tortures of my mind
were instantly forgotten.
Just the sight of your beauty
made my mind grasp for a
hope that was only dreamed about.
Just the sound of your voice
made my very soul melt
into a pool of relaxation.
I remember all of the many
joyful times that followed
from that moment.
Then as I prepare to sleep,
I turn and lay on my side,
and I place my arm
around your waist.
And I start dreaming
of all the blessings
that tomorrow will bring.This was supposed to be Act Four. It pretty much covers from Act Two and on. This is the final piece in this series.
