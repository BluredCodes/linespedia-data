As I am casually walking by
I turn my head and see you
there sitting all alone.
As I start to approach
I can hear you,
softly crying
into your cradled hands.
I walk closer to you,
and I can see a small pool of water
slowly growing
with each new falling tear.
My heart melts and chills
run down my spine
and I place my arm around you.
You turn and lay your
tear softened cheek
upon my shoulder.
The sounds of your cries
echo in my ears
as I offer you words
of compassion and sympathy.
Then as your tears begin to slow
and your cries fade away,
I offer to take you home
and you hesitantly accept.Believe it or not, there is an Act Three. IMO, it is better than Act Two.
