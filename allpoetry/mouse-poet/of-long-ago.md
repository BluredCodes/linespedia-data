title: "Of Long Ago"

Many, many years ago,
there existed a time and a place
of such beauty and wonderment
that the writer of many words
was rendered speechless.
Even the painter of the pages of time
fumbled at the easel
when trying to recreate
this masterpiece of God.
And thus this momentous event
now fades away in the mind
and hearts of the few
only to be found again
in paradise.
