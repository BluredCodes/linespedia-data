Many of times the sun has set
claiming several an evening rose
And the dew far from heaven sent
a pouring out of several woes

But time is time and time again
of the thought that no one knows
That with the setting of the evening sun
a passing of not another rose.

Until that time that time is none
many will bud and evening rose
And of all the choices we do not have
to watch them come to an ending pose.This is dedicated to a friend of mine that passed away. I don't expect many to understand it, however, this is how I felt.
