title: "The End to a Perfect Day"
writer: "Mouse Poet"

As the night slowly approaches
The two sit side by side
And feel the blades of grass
tickle their bare feet
As they swing in the silent breeze
Watching the sun sink
Below the horizon
while talking about simple little nothings.

With the birth of the gentle starlight
A new conversation emerges
with the naming of names
And drawing of the pictures
On the magnificent canvas in the sky
the night creatures sing their praises.

Later as they retire 
To their haven of completeness
They immerse their feelings
In the warm water of relaxation
With bubbles of laughter about the thoughts 
Of things they have done 
And yearn to do. 

As the time slips deeper into the night
The man melts the stresses of her worries
And fears of her past
into the sheets of comfort and peace 
While her thoughts grasp for an eternity
Of hope and desire for her once childhood fantasies

With the closing of the moment
They embrace each other in their arms
And enter into a world of make-believe
Where the thoughts and expressions
Of these two will forever live
With peace and freedom until one day
When it will all become a reality.
