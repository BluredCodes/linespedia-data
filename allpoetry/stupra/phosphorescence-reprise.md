---
title: "phosphorescence [reprise]"
writer: "stupra"
slug: "phosphorescence-reprise"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your phosphorescent heart shivers
in time with the fading neon glow
of heavenly dismembered dreams
Dark caress of the soul enlivens
your search for absurdity
You reach out
Take the succulence from the sky
The stars tremble
with joy
cowering in the moonlight

This is not real
this dream
In and out of darkness
with a steady hand
Essence toiling away
in violent sparks
blinding and everlasting

You're talking to yourself again
Speaking words you don't understand
Words of thought and emotion
crashing against you
like so many fireflies
lost in the night

You don't exist
in the swirling world around you
Invisible tongue lapping
at the truth
If only for a taste
of something sweet and sublime
The god below rising
to take every man, woman and child
with slow, sensual indifference

Cover your eyes
You do not want the light
The sin of a new day
A new game...

To be alone is to be lost in past flavor
looking blindly down at the crease 
from your hand to your phosphorescent heart
Push the static behind your eye
Lay down the card and trip open the doors
Think pretty words rolling smoothly...i think this may be the strangest, most different thing i've written to date...
