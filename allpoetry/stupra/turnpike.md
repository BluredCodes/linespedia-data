---
title: "TurnPike"
writer: "stupra"
slug: "turnpike"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32370-TurnPike-by-stupra"
---

Consciousness fades
Reality remains
And all i can do is stare doe-eyed
into the light that used to live next door

Feel the breeze as it crawls down my spine
Cold water at my feet
sinking ever deeper
with nowhere to go but home

Find my body and sustain the craving
The gnawing in my side
that drags me screaming for release
from all who love
who hope
who weep

My weakness
in the shadow of the TurnPike keeps me running
Keeps me afloat on this bitter drop of love
Turn it off
turn it off i want to drown

I want to choke on all excess of existence
I want what is not mine
I need what is not yours
I hunger for what is ours

Ours to gain
Ours to lose
Ours that never will be

Leave me on the roadside
and tell me i'm in love

(A kiss in the night can lead to many things)
