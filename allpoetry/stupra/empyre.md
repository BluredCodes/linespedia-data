Today...
Today is a day like none that have passed before,
yet like so many that have yet to come
For, as if waking from a dream,
a dream so clear and unfathomable, 
a dream immediately forgotten in a squint,
I opened my eyes today as a god
and have not shut them since the smoke
first blasted from within

My em.pyre awaits

From my position amongst 
the gray and airless clouds
I have seen many states
States that lie between here and there
as souls travel to and fro

I moments past the child had died
And in an instant, i gained a new child
A child with a past life of his own
Wandering in every direction but his own
In the end, i pushed him away...
He was heavy, a stone around my finger
The destructive temptation too sweet
the bond too corrosive to the heart
Nothing under the microscope
and then he was gone, pushed away
A marble let loose

The other day i found my dragon
black as night 
and flecks of silver
with a ruby offering
glinting in the light of the light
An omen 

Now i stretch
Across the heavens
Over the other mother
the mother who cannot weep for me
for she has no more tears
I feel her underneath
Every wrinkle, every crevice beckons me
And she is alien
Inhuman
This other mother will soon be mine
For that is my destiny
She shall be my greatest conquest
in my newfound glory
She:  the instrument through which
my em.pyre will take root
The centerpiece of the great compass of life itself
