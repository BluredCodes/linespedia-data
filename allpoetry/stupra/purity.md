Where do we go from here? she asked
  although she didn't really want to know
The black tears rolled prettily over her delicate cheekbones...

All her life she had been searching for something
Something she didn't even know existed
Something almost discovered in the lonely days of childhood
Something she dared not talk about
  for fear it would somehow exist after all

Pushing away the child
  she focused on her own inability
  to create the void she sometimes longed for
Her inability to die, to live, to love

Long nights separated short days
  with a darkness she couldn't understand
A darkness she couldn't accept
A darkness she didn't believe in

The boys had always left her with a feeling of superiority
  coupled with a sense of naivete
A feeling she could never justify
Even to herself

She drank her purity from a dirty cup
  for fear that it would run over
  and flood her perceptions
  with unwanted sanctity
Uninvited divinity

She was just a little girl...

So where do we go from here? she asked
What am i supposed to do 
  with this that i've gained
What am i to do
  with what i have learned in vain?

She laid down on the dry, brittle grass
  and closed both her eyes
  leaving the other, secret one open
She left this world for one within
  not sure of her return
