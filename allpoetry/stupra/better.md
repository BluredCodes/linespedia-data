title: "better"

Sanity hiding in the corner somewhere
She won't point me in the right direction
What am i to do...
What am i doing

Voices
but they are mine
Smiles of pain
they are not

I am green
and crawling up the walls
Reaching for the light
Twisting, turning
Writhing against the trellis
of my tearful tendrils
Dew-drops in the night
offer no love
And no sleep
as i spread out
for the mourning son

Metaphorically numb
I can no longer speak
My words
Dry husks rasping in the breeze
Skin on the floor and
i've never felt better
Yes
Never better
Never

better
