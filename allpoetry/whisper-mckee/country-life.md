Sweat on tan bodies,
Makes me want to,
Dip brush into paint,

As muscles roll,
Under tight jeans,
I feel a little faint,

A swing of knife, 
As tobbaco falls,
As they work and toil,

There is something,
About a country boy,
Covered with sweat and soil.

Watching young bodies,
In the fields,
Working away the day,

I can see why,
Country girls, always find,
The time to play,
