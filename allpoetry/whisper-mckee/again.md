The cries mingle with pain, 
As crumpled she lay.
Broken.

Face turned to the rain.
Water on her face.
Tears.

He rubbed his knuckles,
Was that look,
Satisfaction.

A low chuckle,
As she gets up,
Again.If you know of any type of abuse. Report it. Or you're just as guilty as the one doing the crime.
