title: "Shared"

Harvest moon,
Shining bright,
Lovers share,
A walk at night.

Hands intwined,
As they walk,
Heads held close,
As they talk.

Secrets shared,
Getting to know,
Common feelings,
As tender grows.

Hearts beat,
With lover's song,
Lips meet,
With feelings, strong.

Just a little,
Together time,
Lets them know,
Heart and mind.
