Shadows woven of dreams once shared.
Mirror magic, when I thought you cared.
Now, ice has formed within my heart.
A life now drab, where you shared a part.

There's nothing so silent as a phone that doesn't ring.
Or the heart of a person who's forgotten how to sing.
One person alone, mixing tears with the rain.
Choking down sobs of denied pain.
