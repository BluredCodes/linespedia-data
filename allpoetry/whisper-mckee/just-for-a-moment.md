---
title: "Just For A Moment"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "just-for-a-moment"

Whirling there,
Like sunlight,
On mist.

Forgeting sandwishs,
Work-a-day things,
Stuff on her list.

Just for a moment,
Being that person,
She always wanted to be.

Dancing there,
By herself,
One moment to be free.

Lost in thoughts,
Caught up in a time,
That is long gone.

Just for a moment,
Reliving her girlhood,
In a remembered song.
