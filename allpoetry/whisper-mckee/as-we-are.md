---
title: "As We Are"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "as-we-are"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16166-As-We-Are-by-Whisper-Mckee"

Listen,
To the hush,
Silence,
At a rush,
Shadows,
Cast on the wind,
Gentle,
Breeze against skin,
Shared,
Moments of choice,
Communication,
Words without voice,
Holding,
Close and warm,
Heated,
Hunger of the storm,
Sweat,
Bodies intwined,
Steam,
Rain on lips like wine,
Giving,
No holding from,
Loving,
As desire runs.
