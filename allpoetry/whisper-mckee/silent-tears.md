title: "Silent Tears"

I walk in silent shadows,
On moss covered paths,
The wind at my back,
Searching for your face,
But of you I find no trace,
Why do you play games?
When my heart is real,
I believe the magic,
Silent tears I cry,
Because of your words,
You lied.
