title: "Rain"

Sounds of night,
Captured there,
Floral scented,
Evening air,
On the wind,
The promise of storm,
Like a shadow,
Rain forewarned,
Feathered darkness,
Like a sigh,
Flash of lightening,
Lights the sky,
Whirlwind moves,
Along the grown,
Ever so silent,
Not making a sound,
Begins gentle rain,
Sparkling mist,
Touching the earth,
In lovers kiss.
