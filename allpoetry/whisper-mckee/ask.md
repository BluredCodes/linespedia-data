title: "Ask"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

The wind lays silence to my scream.
It rises upward inside my soul.
In search of a spirit lost.
Like a dark raven climbing ever higher.
Into the darkness of the night.
Until it touches upon the face of God.
Asking the age old question.
Why?
