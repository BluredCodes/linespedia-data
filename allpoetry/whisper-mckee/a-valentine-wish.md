title: "A Valentine Wish"

Trembling,
On the brink of discovery.
Waiting,
In expectancy of what's to come.
Fragile,
Like mulity colored wings of butterflies.
Shining,
Like a new penny caught in sunlight.
Breathless,
From the ache way down deep inside.
Yerning,
For sometime to just be together.
Thinking,
You are always on my mind.
Loving,
With everything I have to give.
Shyly,
Valentine, will you be mine.
