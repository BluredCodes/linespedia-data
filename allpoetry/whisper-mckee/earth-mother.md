---
title: "Earth Mother"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "earth-mother"

Ravaged,
By to much time,
Poisoned,
By mankind.

Listen,
To her voice,
Children,
We have no choice.

She is dying in her sleep,
We die with her, our souls to keep.

Tears,
Of sorrow and her pain,
Darkened,
Skies of acid rain.

Shaken,
She draws her last breath,
Weep,
My children at her death.

She is dying in her sleep,
We die with her, our souls to keep.
