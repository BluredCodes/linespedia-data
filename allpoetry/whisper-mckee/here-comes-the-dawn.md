title: "Here Comes The Dawn"

Come ride with me,
Along the way.
What the stars,
Await the day.

Build a fire,
To light the dark.
Watch the rise,
Of the sparks.

Sit with me,
In silence share.
At phantom images,
As in the fire we stare.

Tell ghost stories,
To bring a chill.
hare on edge,
At the call, whippoorwill.

Watch the clouds,
Rool across the moon.
A lightening of the sky,
A promise of dawn to be soon.

The color awashes,
The darkened sky.
The birds begin to sing,
Biding the night good-bye.
