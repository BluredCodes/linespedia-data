---
title: "Silence,"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

Dew drips into a forest,
No other sound is heard.
A calm lays all about us,
The silence unbroken by even a bird.

One flake of snow on a roof top,
A roof already covered by snow.
Not a sound does it make,
So quiet it comes, so no one will know.

A darkened room silenced by sleep.
Nothing is heard in the night.
A bat sits outside the window,
Then in silence he takes flight.

A calm sea makes not a sound,
So dark and reaching forever,
When the tide is very calm,
Sky and sea in silence comes together.
