---
title: "Spirits Shared"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "spirits-shared"

The wolf stands,
Eyes aglow,
Watching me,
Eyes turn gold,
Where's the fear,
I should be feeling,
Of this wild beast,
Known for it's killing,
Yet there is something,
That tells me nay,
It won't hurt me,
If I stay,
My ancient totem,
Is of wolf kind,
My little brother, 
With ties that bind,
Once our spirit,
One body did share,
Walked the earth,
Without a care,
Your eyes say come,
Run wild with me,
Feel the wind,
What it is to be free,
No my brother,
I can't play,
Locked in theses chains,
The ones I made.
