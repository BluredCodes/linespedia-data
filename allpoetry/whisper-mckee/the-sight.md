---
title: "The Sight"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "the-sight"

I feel it on the wind, 
The dead moves like flickers of light,
As a crawling on skin,
A movement at the corner of sight.

A wish to be out,
To flee upon wings of fear,
Feeling thing behind me,
But there's nothing here.

The sky a vortex,
As if caught in a spell,
Of the darkest magic,
Alive with demons from hell.

The taste of the wind,
A smell of sulfer adorns,
The coming of rain,
Something wicked rided the storm.#11
