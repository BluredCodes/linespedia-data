---
title: "Calling"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "calling"
source: "AllPoetry"

Fair winds,To sunlit skies,Distant sound,Birds at fly.Bending wheat,Golden Grain,Lonesome sound,Distant train.Calling yon,Alone I stand,Call to wander,Distant lands.Turn my back,On calling song,Shrug my shoulders,Head for home.
