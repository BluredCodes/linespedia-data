She traces her sighs 
Upon the wind.
They echoed where stillness lays.
Nothing lives there anymore.

As if snow shutted by winter,
In valleys deep.
A mournful cry.
Heard by none.

Tears on cheeks,
Frozen there,
By winters sudden bloom.
Of heart grown still.

She rebuilds,
Her fortress strong,
Neither ice or winter snow,
Shall invade.

Then neither will trust,
Gain a foot hold,
Love will never enter.
Inside the fortess of ice.
