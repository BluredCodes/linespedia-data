title: "Memories"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

Jagged shadows,
Broken dreams and souls,
Stranded hearts,
Left out in the cold.

Desires that fled,
From hearts of stone,
Nothing left,
Once love has flown.

Only faded memories,
Of things in the past,
Jumbled and cluttered,
That was suppose to last.

A song brings a tear,
Of being alone,
When once we loved,
For a love that is gone.
