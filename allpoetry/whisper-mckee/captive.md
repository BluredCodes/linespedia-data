A slave, 
Free woman, 
Borned, 

A call, 
Of freedom, 
Which she'd sworn, 

Pirate cative, 
A whim, 
Of fate, 

A village, pillaged, 
Men murdered, 
Women raped, 

Yet, her spirit, 
Remains intact, 
Strong, 

She'll find an answer 
To get back, 
To where she belongs.
