If I put a penny,
In a jar,
For everytime you said,
"I love you."

Would the jar ever fill,
Or remain empty.
I dare not,
Take the chance of knowing.

When you look at me,
What do you see?
Lover, friend or servant.
Or who I really am?

A dreamer of fantasy,
If you would allow,
I'd take you there,
To the place I walk.
