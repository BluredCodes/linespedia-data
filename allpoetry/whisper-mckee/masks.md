---
title: "Masks"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

source: "AllPoetry"

Daddy's little darling,Mamma's baby girl,We all wear masks,As we face the world.A top notch student,The very best of friends,We all wear masks,Even at birth it begins.World's greatest lover,companion, husband and wife,We all wear masks,Through out our life.Hiding behind shadows,throwing up Shields of lies,We all wear masks,Even from our self do we hide.
