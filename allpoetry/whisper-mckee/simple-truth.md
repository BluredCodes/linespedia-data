---
title: "Simple Truth"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "simple-truth"
source: "AllPoetry"

I love you,
Simple truth,
What good,
To lie to you.

Your in my thoughts,
Asleep or awake,
I want to be with you,
What ever it takes.

Walk by your side,
Hear your voice,
Feel your touch,
I have no choice,

If you love me,
As I love you,
Then you need,
These same things too.

If it's a lie,
And been a game,
I have only,
Myself to blame.

I trusted you,
The words you spoke,
Words of love,
Were they a joke,

If you don't care,
Then tell me please,
Don't keep me hurting,
I beg on my knees.

Let me alone,
If it wasn't real,
If you don't love me,
Then let me heal.

I beg you go,
Let me forget with time,
If not my heart,
Leave my mind.
