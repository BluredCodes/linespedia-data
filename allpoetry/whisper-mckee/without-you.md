---
title: "Without You"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "without-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17650-Without-You-by-Whisper-Mckee"

In the window,Don't you see, There's a ghost.Oh, it's just me.Not very frightening,Really not new,It's just me,A me without you.All that is left,Of what I was,This empty shadow,That's without your love.
