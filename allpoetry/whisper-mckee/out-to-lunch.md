title: "Out to Lunch"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

The night watches,
With a thousand eyes,
Rich taste of blood,
A moan, almost a sigh.

Hidden in shadows,
Blood rich and warm,
My nightly feast,
Here safe from harm.

Out of the shadow,
A sound is heard.
I strike my prey,
With skill and nerve.

Attacking my enemy,
Like a wolf on sheep,
His mangled body,
Here at my feet.
