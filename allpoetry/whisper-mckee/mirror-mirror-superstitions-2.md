The pieces there,
Hands bleeding,
Shattered mirror,
Fate cast,
So the legend goes.

Mirror, mirror,
To hold such darkness,
A binding spell,
I think,
Of long ago.

Yet magic born,
Out of words,
Words become thought,
Thought becomes deed,
Or I do suppose.

We carry our own,
This thing called luck,
Took me long,
To come to terms,
Yet I have done it here.

I face my own challenges,
Walk my own paths,
Make my own mistakes,
Luck doesn't happen,
Because of a broken mirror.
