title: "Ghost Walk"

The ice and cold,
Surprisingly,
It burns.

As the wind,
Torments,
With each turn.

Blood there,
Painted,
On the snow.

Prodded forward,
Endless,
Always on the go.

Fallen ones,
Death,
Their only escape.

Forward march,
Hunger,
Barely keeping pace.bite me
