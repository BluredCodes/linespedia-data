---
title: "Daddy's girl"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "daddys-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25835-Daddys-girl-by-Whisper-Mckee"
---

Wipes the tears,
From her face,
Of chocolate ice cream,
Leave not a trace,

Mummbles in sleep,
My sleepy head,
Silent prayer,
As I put her to bed.

Prayers to the Father,
Let her grow up strong,
Keep her safe,
Let her know right from wrong,

Give her quiet strenght,
As she steps out into the world,
Though I may not alawys be there,
Let her know she's Daddy's girl.
