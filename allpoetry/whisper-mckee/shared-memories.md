With Stiffened hands,
She fixes her hair,
In his faded eyes,
She still sees the care.

He softly smiles,
Gives her hand a pat,
She smiles and rocks,
As he sits and naps.

She cuts his food,
And fills his cup,
Ties his shoes,
Buttons his jacket up.

Remebers all the years,
They've shared in this world.
Lost in the past,
When she was a young girl.
