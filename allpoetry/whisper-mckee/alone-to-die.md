---
title: "Alone To Die"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "alone-to-die"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34879-Alone-To-Die-by-Whisper-Mckee"
---

Is it so wrong,
To wish an ease,
Of feeling alone.

Wanting of feelings shared,
Someone to listen,
To know someone cares.

An ease of night,
Weighing darkness,
Absence of light.

A hunger stilled,
To be held close,
A moment forfilled.

A path ever walked,
Alone, in shadows,
Where no one talked.

To fine rest,
A tired spirit,
Claimed by death.

Let it then end,
Hold me close,
Plunge the knife in,
