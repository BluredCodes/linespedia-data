---
title: "Back On Top"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"
slug: "back-on-top"
source: "AllPoetry"

Wind blows,Bringing change,It no longer,Calls your name.I'm standing tall,Walking proud,The sun has come out,From behind the cloud.Beginning to forget,Remembering how to smile,Look out, here I come,Getting myself back in style.Eyes are bright,I give my hair a toss,Look at me, boy,See what you lost.Found my stride,Back in the race,Someone else,Has taken your place.
