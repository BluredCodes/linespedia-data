Silken shadows, 
Of waving palms, 
A hush of sound, 
A touch of dawn, 

A ship at sail, 
On waters deep, 
Divers search, 
Secrets to keep, 

Oh silken lady, 
Gentle host, 
She is easy angered, 
When ask to give up her ghosts, 

Fight breaks out, 
For sunken gold, 
Sand runs red, 
Story won't be told, 

But the lady, 
Has her own game of play, 
Brings up secrets,
