title: "Slipping Away"

Like shadows receding,I feel you slipping,Back into your world,Apart from me.The empty felt,Like velvet stirrings,Of leaves on wind.Change being brought forth.Most unwelcome,Trying to hold,Like fog,Disappearing in sunlight.Now as I look,On hands, empty,My eyes are full.Blinded by tears.
