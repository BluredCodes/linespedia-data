Are We Then So Different 

I walk along, 
At the edge of Shadows, 
Always alone, 
Crying in the dark. 

Searching for a flame, 
Of warmth, somewhere, 
Is it in your arms, 
Funny, I don't feel it there. 

I touch the panes of glass, 
Between you and I, 
How did they get there, 
I know not why. 

We talk to no end, 
Why can't you understand, 
Are we then so different, 
Creatures, called woman and man.
