title: "Tears Of Mother Moon"

When the sky rains stars,
It's a beautiful sight,
When Mother Moon cries,
Stars become tears in the night.

She is a strong lady,
Not often will she cry.
Only at the worlds pain,
Or when a star dies.

The heaven watch in stillness,
At the waterfall of lights,
Everyones eyes turned to the heavens,
Caught up in the beautiful night.

If your lucky enough to see,
Tears falling from her eyes.
You"ll never forget the wonder,
Of the night when the moon cries.
