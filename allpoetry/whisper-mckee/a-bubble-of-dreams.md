title: "A Bubble of Dreams"

The water stirs,
Lost in thought,
Dreaming,
Adrift,
In strawberry bubbles.
Thinking,
Of You,
Remebering,
Your gentle touch,
That fans the flame,
Your smile,
Twinkling eyes.
As with a yank,
I pulled you in,
Fully clothed,
Now you ,
Even haunt me,
In my bath.
