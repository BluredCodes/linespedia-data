title: "Taking Things Slow"
writer: "Whisper Mckee"

Whistling, you bring a smile.
Our few minutes shared,
Is like hours of knowing,

Your warm smile,
Gentle touch,
Leaves me glowing.

Long talks,
On the phone,
Becoming a must,

Friendly walks,
Holding hands,
Building trust.

Taking it slow,
Not easy though,
Keeping things at a hault,

When you held me,
As I cried,
The one kiss was no one's fault.

Just your lips,
On the path of the tears,
Softly trace,

Lightly feathered,
Like warm rain,
On my face.
