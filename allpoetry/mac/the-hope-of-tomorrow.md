---
title: "“The Hope of Tomorrow”"
writer: "Mac"
slug: "the-hope-of-tomorrow"

I sit here wondering about the unknown
awaiting the results of a nasal swab
wondering what they will show
and when I might return to the job

Though I try to feel confident 
that all will turn out to be fine
there’s always that doubt
what future will be mine

The waiting seems endless
yet it’s only been a few days
a drop in the bucket
for most folks I’d say

But still a long time
when waiting the unknown
wondering all the while
what tests may have shown

So here I sit waiting
not knowing about tomorrow
clinging to shining hope
bright against the sorrow
