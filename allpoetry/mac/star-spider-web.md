title: "Star Spider Web"
writer: "Mac"

The stars were framed
by woven leafless branches
small pin pricks of light
like miniature shining spiders
amidst a tangled wooden web
while the moon seems trapped
dangling like a silver earring
from a dark shapeless lobe
as the spiders inch closer
towards the hapless prey
but the moon laughs sliver
and shakes them off like fleas
rising above the broken web
as easy as you please
and the fleas
change back to spiders
the spiders back to stars
the wooden tangled web
now leafless branches
and I can only marvel
at it all
