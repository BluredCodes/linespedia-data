title: "“Time Wears Dark Glasses”"
writer: "Mac"
slug: "time-wears-dark-glasses"

I may live a thousand lifetimesPerhaps I’ll live a thousand moreBut this love we have togetherI’ve known some time beforeTime wears dark glassesThrough which I can not seeYet in my heart I still knowThis love has and will always beI’ve heard true love comes onceBut ours is over a thousand foldEach love for a single lifetimeYet together, thousands of years oldTime wears dark glassesOnly this lifetime may I seeBut the past and the futureTogether is ours, always to be
