title: "Song of Hearts (sonnet)"

A song of pure love plays within my heart
Music such as this world has never known
Each note more golden, though golden from start
Such melodious lyrics never shown
Yet within my beating heart, here reborn
For love plucks softly upon my heart strings
Like spun gold angel harps of heaven-born
Off highest mountaintop my love does ring
The lyrics and musical notes ever bound
As surely are the hearts of two forever
Together, no more beautiful a sound
None such as any have heard, no never
The song of our two hearts swelling with love
Heard even on high by angels above
