title: "“Poetic Emotions”"

The passion of a poet
Burns brighter than a flame
The flame has no heart
At least none it can name

The love of a poet
Grows higher than the sky
For the sky has a limit
And raindrop tears to cry

The heart of a poet
Fills fuller than the words
The words restricted to language
Some no longer heard

The tears of a poet
Flow deeper than a lake
The lake governed by river
Or locked in by mistake

The anger of a poet
Can darken the stars
The stars confined to night
Caged in midnight bars

The sorrow of a poet
Wails upon the wind
The wind blowing free
Never to find an end

The poems of a poet
Written from within
As are the emotions
Less would be a sin
