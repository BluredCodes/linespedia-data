---
title: "“Love as a Sunrise”"

I feel the zephyr’s breath
warm and gentle upon my back
The sun breaking beyond the clouds
Golden fingers reaching towards the ground
as I reach up to embrace the morning sunrise
Knowing that soon her kiss 
will be warm upon my lips
and only then
shall another day exist
Love being the sunrise
That I can never resist
