title: "Ever Green"

AnEverGreenTree standsCovered by snowShivering in the windWondering if ever to be warmWhile a brother basks in the heat insideDecorated by pretty baubles, tinsel, and bowsEach one jealous of the others seemly good fortunehalf alive halfdeadfrozenby winter’s coldor warmed in death
