title: "“My Path in the Woods”"

The path in the wood of my own making
Winding among old broken stone fences
Shaded by black walnut and mulberry trees
Down by small branch of Popcorn creek

Where wild turkey, deer and squirrel
Perhaps a cotton tailed rabbit or two
Frequent the meandering trails
Their company occasionally I keep

The design one of my choosing
Cut from the fabric of deep forest
Hand woven with practiced care
Tailored to fit a man’s needs

My path lined by gooseberry bush 
And laced with blackberry thorn
Where I may stroll in my leisure
Content in the woods of my home
