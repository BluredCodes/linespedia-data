---
title: "“Perhaps Another Time”"
writer: "Mac"

If only another time
or maybe another place
what a difference a few hours can make
even more so than the place
frozen in distant memory
preserved in a lacquered state
not so with damnable time
fleeting fickle moments
drowning in an endless ocean
leaving us gasping for air

Another time we think
when all could be different
at least in fevered imagination
the wrinkles of forlorn thought
brushed back through thinning hair

Another time
echoing  endless across the void
a time of vibrant youth spent in illusion
Lost forever and forever more
Perhaps another time
