---
title: "Star-less Mac"
writer: "Mac"
slug: "star-less-mac"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6731-Star-less-Mac-by-Mac"
---

I am a poet, not criticOne with no starsFor to be the otherWould cage me in barsI come to read and writeNot else can I doFor I’m just a poetAnd that will have to doStar-less Mac now to beThat is my place hereThough my fellow poetsEach one I shall hold dearBut I can’t rate your poemsI hope you understandIt goes against meAnd for all I standI am star-lessNo stars to giveNor do I need anyNot while I live
