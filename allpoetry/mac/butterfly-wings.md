---
title: "“Butterfly Wings”"
writer: "Mac"
slug: "butterfly-wings"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6607--Butterfly-Wings--by-Mac"

If I had butterfly wings
That I might spread
Upon summer's breeze
Filled by the wind
To fly where I please
What sights I might see
While the miles fly by
Over distant lands
So far away
If I but had
Butterfly wings
