I sit staring out the window 
A dim reflection stares back 
Looking out at the world 
The reflection looking back 

I focus on this reflection 
Trying to tune the world out 
But I cannot make it happen 
For I’m looking in, not out 

I see the world beyond the glass 
Through the reflection of myself 
I question who sees the clearest 
The dim reflection, or myself 

I watch the reflection intently 
Wondering what next he’ll do 
Imprisoned on that pane of glass 
While I am free to leave, and do
 
A backward glance confirms 
The reflection trapped within 
Growing ever smaller 
Always without, never within.
