Most folks have letters
A through Z
Today my letters have changed
A through C
Just 3 letters for 3 days
That's  the time you have to wait
To see if you have the big A
Now the B is for Bad
which it certainly is 
And my C is for Cipro
hoping it will do good.
Yes most folks have 
A through Z
Perhaps in three days
That will be Me
