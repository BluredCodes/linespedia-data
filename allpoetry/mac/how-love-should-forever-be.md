---
title: "How Love Should Forever Be"
writer: "Mac"

What we have was meant to be,
Destined by fate to happen you see.
We were meant from the very start,
For we are as one within our hearts. 

The hearts of two souls beating as one,
Together, under dark of night and light of sun.
Merged by the love we both now share,
Once alone, now a perfectly matched pair. 

What we have is such wondrous bliss,
One sealed daily with love’s tender kiss.
Our words, our touches, our stares,
All expressions of our love and our care. 

The love we see written upon each face,
Quickens the heart, makes our blood race.
For within the heart lies the truth of our love,
Showed upon us like summer rain from above. 

Warm drops of soothing rain upon our souls,
Apart we are incomplete, together we are whole.
I shall always love you as you always love me.
And that is how love should forever be.
