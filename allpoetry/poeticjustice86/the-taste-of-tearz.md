My tearz r warm and bitter.They fall frequently,Almost everyday.What causes them 2 fall so frequently,I have no clue.i try not 2 let anybody c them fall,Sometimes I fail at my attempt.When I cry people stop and stare,I look up at them with my blurred vision,But only 4 a second do they wonder why.I cry when a part of my ehart crumbles.When I'm alone,U can hear my sobbing,As I try 2 keep quiet.The taste of tearz is very bitter.                        I'LL CRY UNTIL I DIE
