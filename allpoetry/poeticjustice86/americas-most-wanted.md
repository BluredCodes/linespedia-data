It Seems like everybody wants my blood.Nobody wants me around.I don't have any friends.Somebody is constantly stabbing me in the back.Boyfriend's hate me,Their girlfriend's want me.I'm left 2 fend 4 myself,Because nobody can trust me.I keep 2 myself,Because nobody wants 2 get involved in my lifestyle.I have very few friends.I swear I'm America's Most Wanted.I have 2 watch my back,One of my friends might jump me.They may want me dead 2night.I'm the number one most wanted.I don't know,But I think u all want me dead.I hope u aren't mad because of ur girlfriend and I,I swear it was just a fling,It was an America's Most Wanted thing.
