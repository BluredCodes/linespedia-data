Can You "NOT" feel these flat blisters
that are blackened by lighters' ablaze??
~~>Their upon Your livid skin, so grey,
PainStakenly calling out to You.

Can You "NOT" notice extreme gashes
Of gore, as rivers stream down in fluent,
Subsequential crimes of Darkest Red??
~~>They shrill with audibility for that
Consoling UnDeniable Attention, Ever
So MerciFully.

Why can't You pay tribute to this
UnSpeakable mess upon this MotionLess
Skin SO Grimm??
~~>It's OverWhelmed in HeartFelt tears
Of BlooD for the warming solace of
Your adequately hidden LOVE.

Are there "NOT" eyes in Your head to
Dirrect those blindSighted hands of 
Neglect towards caressing this distroyed 
Body of deep, LifeLasting Scarrs??
~~>Such an intensely worn body "Under-
Siege" crys out to Your greatly
Accountable intercession, as it
UnDoubtedly Pleadsss.

Within All Of Your CareLess Erra,
Couldn't You Find Enough KindHeartedNess
To Wholely Flutter Around, Within, For
The Entirety Of "US" All??
~~>These Decaying Cuts Of Plenty Are WAY
Too Much The SlowDeath-Penalty And Swell
Our Forsaken Eyes With OverFlowing Jeers
Of BlooDieD ExhaustioN.

All throughout Your awry mind of fogged
Illusions, isn't it time enough to grasp
Ahold of those whom also Reside Within and
Adhere securely with Infinite "Mercy" in 
Order to let > US < All Survive At Best???
~~>We desperately DO compel You to....
..."Surround this Livelihood of Self-
Victomization with practicing Your endearing
SpiritedNess through Self-Preservation,
Smoothing away these BloodY Crevisses and
Soothing the fear You violently had pierced
In Thru Us."

~} ~} Soulzzz Do Flutter Within {~ {~
