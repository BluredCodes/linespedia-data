~ } ~ } Mannequin's Cryptic Embraces { ~ { ~


My haunted state of twisted, disembodied
EmptyNess hounds foreward by rambling
Drifts throughout gusts of atmosphere in
This barren WasteLand.
Fields gaping across the frigid night
Embraces the lair of the sole LifeForce
From a Tornado's wicked shadow.

Purely held in FaithLess sight shuffles
A fruit tree's skeliton baring babes of
PlasticNess thru these ever sullen eyes.

Your demented fertile DemoN lick's the
Mixture's spoon and will only bake such
A new birth within a charcoled
Mannequin's Livid Shceme.

Your damaged cherry trees give NO life,
And echo ForEver a MournFull shredded
Squall.  The FruitLess Cherries of these
Neon screams silently compel its DisFigured
BottomLess Pit of BlooDieD Foresaken 
TimeLess Regrets.

Such an eerie error flows sturdy masses of
Violated crimson drips from kept murders
ThroughOut our left-over World.
You clearly SeekOut dreams of vivid cloned
Stability to enhance My NightMare worned
Reality, that hastily exists amung throes
Of severely desecrated futilities within Me.

This Magic's forlorned statute execrates My
Livelihood's morbid phase as Your cryptic wish
ForceFully sets My Life Ablaze, accelerating
Amidst demeanors of a streaming DarkRed Potion
For such DeathWished Endeavors to Infinitely 
Swallow a Suicide Craze.

You have just consumed Ramblings of:
