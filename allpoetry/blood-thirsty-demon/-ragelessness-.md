An ancient rusty axe, stuck into 
this Tree-Trunk with only a 
moment's Strength to expel....

Battered and Worn-down with 
stains of sap and green vegitaion
upon its entire wooden handle....

Seeing the degenerate blade, with
splashed rust and countless scores
of scratches embedded into its usage
of a whole Generation's Worth, within
My mind's eye over and over again,
sends tidal waves of massive NightMares
in thru My Livelihood's Pitch-Blackened 
Dread....

Then, within a split second of not
actually knowing any better but wanted
to hastily please an angry adult, as I
FearLessly swung that old tattered axe
I ForceFully put its blade deep into
the Dead Silence of that trunk.....

My Grand-Mama sounded off with such
a BlooD-Curtling scream towards me
that jolted My AwareNess to the
dirrection of the other little ones
that were helping me play at chopping
wood just like Grand-Papa....

I threw My head around and closed my
attention in on My Baby Sis who had
her Strained Mouth wide open, as to
be screaming out in Pain, but, there
wasn't a sound, just that look of
a toddler's PainFelt agony thru eyes
of such grim shock....

As she screamed in Major silent tones,
that loudly shook our minds, and
violently shuttered within our bodies
from the feet, up the spine, to an
aweful feeling of tall goose bumps
that were standing all hair on end,
she seemed to slowly fall backwards
with a hand straight up in the air....

I quickly peered around that scene
to acess exactly what I had made 
happen that deserved such a brought
About horrifying calamity....

I was so confused to see BlooD from
My Baby Sis' two indexed fingers
Squirting in every posible direction,
with half of those fingers, once 
missing, now located laying on that 
stump besides the damned ole rusty
axe blade....

I didn't know what was happening with
My Sis then, or, the feelings that were
swarming my mind and drowning My Spirit 
then.  But, I waved good-bye to My Grand-Mama 
as I saw her holding those chopped off fingers
to the, now, stubb of My Sis' hand as 
they both were on their way down that 
LONG dirt DriveWay in the middle of a Huge
Vast WilderNess....

I, and the rest of my Young Kin, played 
around that meat cattle ranch for so Long
that day.  SomeHow we all ended up by this 
gigantic, fallen, dead tree that spread out 
its bare limbs upon the meadow within 
inches of a thick, green forest....

Becuzz of what took place, with terrible
astonishment from my cuzzins, I was
quickly put on trial, and was frozen stiff, 
for the murder of My innocent Baby Sis' 
BlooDieD Fingers....

EveryOne of My cuzzins shook their fingers
at me and loudly declared that I was clearly
guilty of Murder in the 1st.  And then came 
the sentencing of a Monsturous Brutality
that would definitely need a comstumized 
coffin just for Me....

There I was, Frightened Stiffly, and shaking
violently as I stood before them all to hear
what I was Purely deserving of.  Then, they
cruelly hit me with that Hard blow of truthful
judgment.  The oldest cuzzin walked right
infront of me and sternly looked into My eyes
saying,  ...."You are here-by sentenced to
Death, by Hanging by Your neck till You are
completely dead."...  

Dangling from this old dead tree, as its 
sole, frigidly-LifeLess Ornament, was just
the Sight that My imagination drowned me in
for "for ALL but 5 yrs. of My Entire LifeTime".... 

But, the moving picture of
such a Crude scene that portrayed the
reality of My Sis falling back, mouth gaping wide 
open, hand straight up with two fingers replaced by
BlooDieD Gore, is "THE" Horror for a 5 yr. old
...{Me}, to keep within her mind's memory
banks for an entire LifeTime.  To torture her
every Thought, Feeling, AwareNess, and each
emotion known to a Normal ManKind....
