~ ~ > Thru Hell's Realistic Throes In My World.


Oblique hollow shcemes trample My desolate
SpiritedNess into a, now, consumate 
obsession Of Self-DamnNation,
...Far deeper in thru the oblivion of
FreeFlowing substance, coloured all
Throughout a NightMare ScarletNess.
Here I Follow into that yearning of
GoredOut wedges I continuously seek-out
In simultaneous existances,
...CeaseLessly with DeathWished
WholeNess ~~~>> At Larged.

A BlooD ThirstY DemoN blindzzz the entirety
Of My once created Universe, executing
PureNess in illusions thru dousing My vision,
Of what LIFE may have been, and...
~~~>> Drowning this view with utter SelfHatred
As BlooDieD ApparationSSS Hauntingly dance
In concordance within.

Now that I was convinced that a Night's Sky
Mermersss obsenities of Me being filleted....

I beseech a darkened HighWay....

As My Own Way....

To Dig That Maggot Crawling Grave....

With Shimmering Jagged Edges Along
Its Eye-O-Prey....

I Fondle The Dirt Alone Watching Tin 
Vessels Of Coffin Grey....

All Being Driven Down Under From Those
Blinding Corpses Astray....

I Find MySelf Hunting Down That AgreeAble
ReTrieved Slaughterer For Me To Be
Silently Slayed....

And That "Slay", My Dear Friendsss Is What
I Was Too Chicken-Shit To Do ~~~> All Day!
