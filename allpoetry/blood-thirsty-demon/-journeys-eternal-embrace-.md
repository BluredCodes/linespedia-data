As You and I move
Each other's mind
There's a BoundLess
Peace colouring the
Amazing stages of Our
United Journey's Path.
Harmonious Freedoms of
UnChained adventures lead
Calming concordances to
FaithFully flutter within
The UnBreakable Reality of
Kindred-SpiritedNess in
Such a WholeHearted Love
We "DO" grasp together.

That Gleaming Friendship, upon
The true ability of a gesturing
SunRise, displays a force of Its
Own Genuinely smoothe, velvet-
Reflection of the Purity throughout
Love's Perfection.  Such a PowerFull
InSight influences this Life of Agape,
Bound by You and I, as We experience
An astounding, tranquil effect, taking
Much more awareness into account for 
One Another's Well-Being that carries...

On-N-On....

~ ~ > Infinitely.


OOOHHHH -> -> -> Such interminable 
TenderNess "DOES" Exhibit startling, 
Perpetual FlassLessNess Within
Qualities of a silky Pearl's delicacy.

The ever-lasting breath of My credence
Between Us, throughout the very 
"Understanding" of Our growing 
FullFillment, is >ForEver reaching-
out just to grasp the face of Your
Dreams, ...Hopes, ...Fears.<

There's a precisional truth that
"Unconditional Love" always has a 
Bountiful style of Its very Own
Within the individual apprehension 
Of the Blessed Soul whom bestows
It's Breath-Taking Essence.

So I implore You, My Precious
Angel In Shining Armor, don't
Fear Its influentially potent
Dominion in the Life 
You and I Dwell in
Together.  Just Freely
Stretch Your Spirited-
Ness of "Attainment"
In Love's Purity and
FaithFully take My
Heart thoroughly
Inside all boundries
Of Your's.

Then You will come to
A BreathLess Realization
That....

~~> There are such astonishingly
~~> WonderFilled acknowledgements
~~> In reality awaiting within an
~~> UnTouched Loving Passion thru
~~> The Kindred-SpiritedNess We
~~> Share of this almighty, united
~~> Union.  Through quite the
~~> "Ever Thriving" Spiritual Love,
~~> We ARE Experiencing, there is an
~~> OutStanding, immeasurable growth
~~> That will take on the form of
~~> Conceivable comprehensions that
~~> Seem to fly ever so Gently upon
~~> The SoftNess of Silver Wings
~~> Blowing Graciously about with
~~> An undeniable Freedom through 
~~> Supple Breezes on into a mild
~~> Summer's Difted Night.

Take hold of this My Love.  "Know" that
Our Future is continuously Fated within
An OnWard Flow of FaithFull Submission.

I am Your's, Genuinely, thru Our Journey's
Shared PathWays of an Eternal Embrace.
