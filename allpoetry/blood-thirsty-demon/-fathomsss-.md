Your entire presense BeStillss My awareness, WholeHeartedly with an intense Love Passionately gathered fully at My beseeching side.  My absorbant perceptions "DO" instinctly ingest Your Wantss,  ....Your Needss,  ....Your Desiress, yearning profoundly for My dirrected, supple touch to flood those rivetting hormones, ....all the while compelling Your sincerity.

OOOOHHH, what vibrations run from Your pores of brilliance.  Just to brush-up against Your stimulating skin sets My World ABLAZE.  A stead-fast connection quenchingly undeniable,  ...thoroughly dousing acknowledgements within the core of My famished hunger.

My craving finger-tips are WELL Aquainted with that submissively Moving Labido You continuously brandish at My longing recognition.  I silently ReachOut, ever SSSOOOO softly, the length of these fingers as My burning desiress coherently embark Our notable binded necessities,  ....in fervent driven aspirationss.

But, Your imposibly OverSumptuous, noble ambitions hold back every last bit of these LifeForce Giving Charactoristics that could timelessly replenish Your infinite, inconceivable DayDreaming.  Can You NOT Fathom the totality within Our shared Mystical Spirituality of aspired, continently benevolent Union???

SweetHeart, this all boils down to "Turning Your Ambiguous Head" from the improbabilities of those Out-Of-Reach goals You adhere ever so tightly to.

Can't You honestly open-up Your Soul to these crystaline Gifts of Genuine LOVE that We Do share???  You are Purely capable of trustingly extending those adaptable Passionss, SO well observed in clarity, towards the Never-Ending Horizons of a devoting LOVE we Both "DO" take Great parts in of Cherishing,  ....within the warmly attentive ties that Eternally BIND us into an Amazing, Solely Internal Whole.

I implore You, My Precious SweetNess, BE utterly Couragous and set those blinding ambitions FREE!!  

You neednt vex YourSelf over the future's inclinating fate within Life's Journey.  

For, in time You will slowly taste the evident OutCome of Our 
Kindred-SpiritedNess as it sends Us on Our Love's United PathWay.

You and I are undoubtedly made to be at each other's side, so just go with it and stay with Me Forever More, continuously being lead by those beconning cravings of Our Passionate Love-Fest.

I am Your's Now and Forver....
