OOOHHHH, What Mystical Symposiums You Create....

~ ~>> This TenderNess in poetic music mixes My optimistical environment with enchanting Melodies of Your bewildering OutLook with soothingly mesmerizing notes of One's LifeForce's "Audible Agape", ....filling the atmosphere within My Soul.

~ ~> Your Breath-Taking strumms along My tantilizing cordsss, which threads the FullNess of those vulnerable walls within a Heart sssoooo LEAD, does saturate anticipation as My Spirited Being beconsss for that delicate thrust of Never-Ending Motion from Your strong-willed fingers, that Forever More leaves My Hearing ability....
....Absolutely BreathLesss

~ ~>> I can litterally hear My chest pounding with OutBurstsss of enthusiasm as You pour, ever so Handsomely thru that ample capability of SmoooothNesss You produce, with an astounding Musical Symphony of entire Loving Gestures...  
....'a stimulus of earnest breezesss gently flowing softnesss in thru My Honored Ears.'

~ ~>> I see You as this Hypnotic Musician, for LOVE's WonderFull sake, FaithFully grasping ahold of My LIFE, and Infinitely adhereing with GreatNess to My Journey's very Worth.

~ ~>> My Heart only bled when there was NO sound to FullFill its destiny of an Amazing Reality in true Purity...  
....that shows much Gleaming stimulants within Your MasterFull Harmoniesss.  Such clarity in soundsss as this, thru Unison, does tug away at My Livelihood's existance.

~ ~>> Ever sssoooo EnLightenly EnRitched am I,  ...now that You've honestly joined BeLoved PathWays with Mine thru this Utter-Most TimeLess Piece of Your Soul's OpenWilled, Artistic Chorusss....

~ ~>> Just for the sake of Our United Spirit's Compositionsss throughout Astonishing Sonatasss...
~ ~> Never....
~ ~> Ever....
~ ~> Ending......
