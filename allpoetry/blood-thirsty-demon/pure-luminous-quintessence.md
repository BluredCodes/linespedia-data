That illuminating essence of gleaming Purity from Your aura brings forth Kindled Compassion of Invigorating Warmth in thru My pain filled World of cold darkness.

I urgently reach-out towards Your consoling Kind HeartedNess.
My eager finger tips extend aspiringly into such Journey's PathWay of Your beaming inner Being.  Each pore that diligently drives into My fervent felt Humanity continues to softly repeat such gallant embraced cries for that authentic intimacy of Your Spirit's ever so tender touch.

I am Amazed with BreathLess Whimsss as I stand alone within the flow of Your AweStriking Radiance,  ....absorbing every unique delicate ray that lulls My feelings into exhilarated Breath-Taking Existances.

Such very style of Your "Unconditional Love's" Purity brightly guides My once weakened Soul to a BoundLess Recovery of NO Return.  Then, within a burst of EnRaptured Sensations, jolting all hair to stand on end, My endeared cheeks receive that cleansing Blessing with each HeartFelt trickling tear-drop.

The whole totality of My Life transformed at a mere split-second's glimpse the instant Your ever so brightly lit-up Spirited Being touched My World's beseeching awareness with its "Agape Ambiance".

Please grasp ahold and adhere securely to the critical importance of this... 
->One Monumental Reality<- My Precious Steve....

> > > "It has Genuinely been Our EverWaking Destiny to be fatedly apart of One Another's Soul~~> FaithFully from the unquestionable birth of all Era,~~> Till undoubtedly enfolding quite the achieved end to Infinity's Morality of a perfectly promised Eternity.

My Livelihood's Universe is, Forever More, centered by a sole Utter-Most AweInspiring Purpose of much tangibilities in rejuvinations.  This Purely Majestic Significance thruthfully exists within the Amazing birth of each day's Rising Sun.  So You must know that...
~~~> "It's Your tremendously tantilizing touch, upon my skin so earnest, that is absolutely the core of My Soul's entire CeaseLess Necessity,  forever leading My Spirit thru BlissFull Freedoms."

With each word of splendor, that Naturally flows so fluent from Your supple lips, I can actually explore by "Taking-To-Heart" the authentic Intimacy that does wholely saturate Your Passionately Loving Nature.

As every seperate Brilliantly LitUp Star fills the sweet coolness of a Night's Sky....
~~> So shall Your StarDusted Love fill every boundry that Giftsss My Universe it's Glorious Freedom.

As plainly clear as the Sun's phenomenal presence throughout a vast, crystalline-blue sky, it has been engraved in great depth into a moment's breath of My inner Spiritual Being that....

~~~>> "The very thought of You by My side brings about the exact reflection of My Heart lightly thumping as if there were a mass of Butterflies fluttering fearlessly everywhere within the Most Central Reality of my Life."

WholeHeartedly Experienced Within:
