~>This is pg. 2 of the story "RageLessNess"<~
*********************************************


My accidental, gruesome handy-work upon
A Baby Sis so cherished, and was Whole-
Heartedly Loved by Me, was NOT over yet 
By The Life Span that She and I had to
Perfectly endure infront of the eyes of
Every adult that entered our Journey's
PathWay as "Wanna-Be" CareGivers....

During that week stay in the hospital
The surgeons worked on her fingers with 
what Technology they had, back then in
1970.  But, even with the countless
Numbers of strained Doctors and Surgeons,
working around the clock to save her Life
And Her severed fingers, those fingers
Had to be cut off once more to save Her
Very Life....

Those were the days when Medicine was
Almost PreHystoric, and the know-how
By all MD's were Practically UseLess,
Especially in the middle of NoWhere's
Ville, USA....

In that Butchery Shop of a Hospital
My Baby Sis remained lying in her
Bed, with lines of BlooD-ReD, and
Such swollen tissue surrounding the
Bones that made up the rest of Her
Hand....  

Why red Streaks within the flesh 
Of a toddler so New??  Why having
To fight so Hard to save Her Life
When all she did was lose a couple
Short limbs??  She did loose alot 
of blooD that fated day thru my Grim
View into Her finger's Death.  But,
It was the axe that was covered so
Well in Filth, and corrosion of
Thick rust, that made-up most of the
Factors.  Then came a damned 20-min.
Drive to the Hosiptal in an Old
Rambler.  But, apart of My Own Fate,
that had to be the Major reason she
almost died, was that I was Guilty of
Murder in the 1st, and BlooD all over
The ground where we played every day
Was a convincing sight, for all at that
Farm whom looked down on Me, to know
Exactly just WHO was to Blame.
