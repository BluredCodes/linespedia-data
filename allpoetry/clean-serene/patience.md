---
title: "Patience"
writer: "Clean Serene"
slug: "patience"
source: "AllPoetry"

I trust the world that now holds
me near.
The slight brush of my thoughts has
silenced the anger I respect from within.
I may not beam with great aplomb or structure.
But at least I have the patience to seek out
my dream.
This patience may not be more than a
single key to a piano,
But it sings more than the anger I used
to compose.
I can show the world how ready I am.
For I am cold.
Alone,
And still searching for that one soul that
will bind my hopes and dreams.
If it takes an eternity to wash my fear away,
I will not shiver nor flinch.
Only bear the wind that witnesses my strife.
The sheer frost I wear will be nothing.
For anything will be everything...
If I wait to be warmed.
