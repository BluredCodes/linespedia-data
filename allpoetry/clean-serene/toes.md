Your feet broke in two again.Walking around my face,So contagious.Strand after strand,I pulled.Away at your toes,Away with your dignity.Running again.It's been so long since I've seen your animosity my friend.I'll just step aside,let you crawl under my wishings.And I'll laugh...Because your so unbalanced withoutthose toes...Tripping again.It's been an hour since I've seen you fail my friend.I'll just sit next to you,let you hover above my fears.And I'll cry...Because you and I are no different.
