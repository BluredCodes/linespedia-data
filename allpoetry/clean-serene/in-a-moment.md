Would I be about a perilous
journey if I thought?
Thought of how fast the
hands circle by the quartz?
Centered...
I think I am.
Long lived are the small.
Living in the moment.
I've been racing beyond the line.
Silent...
without end.
Would I be about a perilous
journey if I was reposed?
No...
Please let me fall to rest.
