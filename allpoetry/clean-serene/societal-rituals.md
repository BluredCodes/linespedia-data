title: "Societal Rituals"
writer: "Clean Serene"

Life hasn't been what we thought
it to be.
For laughs and cries have
left me blank.
No subtle thoughts bearing
my witness.
Mearly a voice I follow.
How to perfect my relentless look.
How to make myself seem
rapturous,
judicious,
and right.
Defining my scars and marks of wealth.
Revealing to one I no longer am.
When the people exult in 
glorious fraud,
I'll find myself joining in.
Trusting my vision in looking 
for what it is.
I could.
The decision has changed your perception.
The decision shows your delinquency.
Leaping from a safety that I'll
always have.
The rocks are heaved in unrestrained
amounts.
They cry my dirge.
I join in while another gets
butchered in bare silence.
So cut down by society's sick ritual.
The people beckon to me
as they hurl themselves at one another,
so I can participate in their dire acts.
Wayward winds strip my face of 
all expression.
Strangly unworthy.
My connection lost in a constant
current of sublimity.
Uncovered by no one so glorious.
Find me there...
Spiritless.
