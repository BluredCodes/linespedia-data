There was a time when
we shared the mistakes of
our intentions.
I watched the rocks fall down
as they knocked you from your throne.
You weren't much more than a dead
leaf withered on the grass.
I must have been a catapillar...
I ate everything from your undying vine.
You must have been sick,
But I was so hungry...
Too hungry to wonder if you were even real.
Winter came without warning.
I pressed on to store you away,
and I never once stopped consuming
what was left on your vine.
I don't know what happened.
This is when the rocks fell
ruthlessly upon you.
I died slowly...
You never even knew who you were.This is about codependency.  Not my favorite, but I felt I needed to have this one out there. ~ROXY
