The weather brought 
a core to my sanctity.
Brazen winds skim the
branches with prayer.
I read my present memoir
in front of the ever changing
nature.
Beginning to depict what
was bound.
You've left before...
Unbearably humid then...
Like the hands you kept away
from my mouth.
White surounds the eves we
once stood below.
Now you stand under something
I'll never convey.
