Place lid upon this
open...
Open space.
Empty void,
left filled contempt.
Arisen,
these maskings.
Over...
and over the top.
Spoken...
ruins all,
rules undivine.
Voice screaming,
yet silent...
underlined.
Place lid upon these
depths...
my open mouth,
shall beseech you for my
death.I have no idea why this is written like this.
