FOOTSTEPS

SITTING IN MY ROOM SO LONELY AND STILL
GLANCING AT THIS PAGE,THE ONE I SHOULD FILL
I REMEMBER A DAY WHEN I WAS WALKING ALONG THE SHORE
WHEN THE SUN WAS TO SET,AND THE RAIN WAS TO POUR
PRINTING MY FOOTSTEPS AS I DRAGGED MY FEET ALONG
DROWNED IN THE SEA'S DEPTH,HUMMING A LONELY SONG
MY IMAGINARY MIND DRIFTED TO A WORLD OF IT'S OWN
A WORLD THAT DIDN'T EXIST,ONE THAT WAS LEFT UNKNOWN
I RECALLED THE DAY I MET YOU,THE DAY WE FELL IN LOVE
WONDERING WILL I EVER FORGET YOU,WILL YOU FLY AWAY LIKE A DOVE?
IN MY HEART YOU LEFT YOUR FOOTPRINTS,YOU LEFT THEM ALL AROUND
LIKE THE FOOTSTEPS I'VE JUST PRINTED ON THE WALKED OVER GROUND
I TURNED TO SEE MY PRINTS,TO FIND THEM FADED AWAY
IF MINE HAD VANISHED SO QUICKLY,WHY DID YOURS HAVE TO STAY?
WASHED AWAY BY RAIN,MY FOOTSTEPS WERE MADE UNCLEAR
WASHED AWAY BY TIME,WILL OUR MEMORIES DISAPPEAR?
MANY PROMISES WE MAKE WITH EACH PASSING DAY
TO KEEP OUR LOVE AS PASSIONATE ALL THROUGH THE WAY
WITH TEARS FILLING MY EYES I STARED AT THE SEA
WISHING I COULD UNLOAD THIS EMPTINESS INSIDE OF ME
CLOSING MY EYES FOR A WHILE,MY MIND STARTED TO REMEMBER
THE DAY WE HELD LOVE TIGHT,THE DAY WE REFUSED TO SURRENDER
ON ONE OF MY FOOTSTEPS I CAST AN ASTONISHED GLANCE
THE PRINT STILL CARVED DEEP,NOT WASHED AWAY,NO CHANCE
AS A SPARK OF HOPE LIT MY INNER DARKNESS SO FAST
