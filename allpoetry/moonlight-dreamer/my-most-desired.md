MY MOST DESIRED


EMBLAZONED IN MY MIND ARE YOU
YOUR IMAGE SO VIVID,SO TRUE
ALIVE INSIDE ME IS YOUR SOUL
IN SPRING,IN WINTER,OR IN FALL

MY MOST DESIRED

WHO COULD COMPARE TO YOUR KINDNESS
THAT I LOVE SO MUCH TO BLINDNESS
OR TO YOUR WARMTH,PASSION AND STRENGTH
TO CONTINUE I'LL NEED MUCH MORE LENGTH

MY MOST DESIRED

YOU'RE THE LANTERN THAT LEADS MY WAY
THE SOFT BREEZE ON A SILENT BAY
THE MUSIC THAT FEEDS MY HEART
YOUR TOUCH IS AS FINE AS ART

MY MOST DESIRED

GIVE ME WORDS FOR I HAVE NO MORE TO SAY
ALTHOUGH MY FEELINGS WOULD NEVER FADE AWAY
I'LL SET MY HEART FREE TO

MY MOST DESIRED
