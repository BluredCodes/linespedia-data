IN THE CANDLE LIGHT
                 DEDICATED TO Y.B  

ALONE IN THE CANDLE LIGHT
FLIPPING THE FINE PAGES OF OUR MEMORIES
ON SUCH A BEAUTIUL EVENING,SUCH A PEACEFUL NIGHT
RINGING IN MY HEAD ARE YOUR ENCHANTING WORDS
AND BREATH-TAKING MELODIES

AS THE TEARS OF SORROW FALL TENDERLY DOWN MY CHEEK
I FEEL MY HEART BEAT SLOWING DOWN AND MY BODY WEAK
YOU USED TO FEED MY HEART WITH THE DAZZELING LOVE OF YOURS
NOW IT IS SUFFERING YOUR LOSS,SUFFERING FROM THE SORES

I STILL REMEMBER YOUR EYES
THAT TORE THE MOST FRAGILE STRINGS OF MY SENSES
THOSE EYES THAT GAZED INTO MY INNER CORE
RESTORING MY DORMANT EMOTIONS BACK TO LIFE
AND BREAKING DOWN MY DEFENSES

YOUR SENTIMENTAL HEART WITH ITS RYTHMETIC POUNDS
WAS LIKE A DELICATE PIANO
ECHOING THE MOST HARMONEOUS OF SOUNDS
FOR ONLY MY HEART TO LISTEN AND SING "TE AMO"

OH..HOW I WISH TIME DIDN'T HAVE TO RUN SO FAST
HOW I WISH THOSE DAYS WERE TO LAST
BUT AT LEAST THEY'RE A PART OF MY PAST
THAT WOULD LIVE IN MY MEMORY,THAT WOULD EVERLAST.Did u like it??
