MY FAVOURITE MEAL


Honey, even though
I'm just a man I am
Aware of what you like,
When it’s too arduous
To get hard I’m not
About to get psyched.

I wield softness instead
To arouse you and make
You moan in delight,
And I can deftly resume it
From where I left off
The previous night.

I draw my tongue 
Over your inner thighs,
Just above the knee,
Alternating between
Either side, inching 
Upwards purposely...

As you guide my ravenous
Head along its gratifying
Ascending trail,
Your mound rises like a
Vesuvian announcement
That all resistance has failed.

I circle your moist
Outer lips; partly parted,
Slightly quivering,
In arid parts of the
Lowdown world this is
Still considered a sin...

Just to check I look up and
Glimpse my reward in
Your ethereal smile,
The forgiveness on your
Flushed face makes my calling
Very, very worthwhile.

Much gentler than a
Butterfly I flutter
From bottom to top,
You keep urging me
To continue as if 
I am about to stop...

I’m too enamoured by the
Animal scent of your
Velvet wetness as I kneel,
Its turn-on taste iterates
“Lust is food for the soul”
And you’re my favourite meal.
