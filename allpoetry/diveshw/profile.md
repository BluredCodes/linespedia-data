PROFILEHer shape's much too promising,Her hair’s as dark as midnight,Her clear eyes chide sobrietyAnd mischievously reflect starlight.She talks to those in cottonAbout those dressed in chiffon,She knows what both kinds wantAnd in their minds what’s going on.Her copious curves are anEntrancing argument against aging,The endless possibilities they offerSet my lack-luster libido raging.One direct gaze at at her bodyMake all my thoughts turn immoral,She evokes my most base instincts;Straight, sideways and oral.Without an iota of honourAre the thoughts I do intend.I’m surprised at my reaction,I’ll break only after I’m spent.In every pleasure of the fleshWith her I would blindly invest,In a one–night stand that’ll blurThe morning after’s consequence.
