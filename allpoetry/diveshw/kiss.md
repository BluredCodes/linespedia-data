KISSBe it passionate or gentle,Meditated or accidental,It’ll urgently awaken reasonsTo become sentimental.It’s sure-fire immediacy Will prompt two eager soulsTo unbridle their passionAnd let it swallow them whole.Questions about a headingWill quickly be dismissed -This tasty prelude shouldConclude at long-term bliss.I smile upon rememberingWhen our lips first did meetBut not upon its aftertasteThat left me so aggrieved.Those experienced enoughWill sagely grant me this;Love was never determinedBy the sweetness of a kiss.
