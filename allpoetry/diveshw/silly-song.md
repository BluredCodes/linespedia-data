SILLY SONGWe shared a few drinks, You let your tidy hair down And let a careless grin replace Your responsible half-frown. It helped you arrive Where I wanted you to be, To make a calculated mistake And get silly with me Now I can’t fit these feelings In any convenient category, I fear they probably won’t be Construed as very healthy. I’ve no idea how I was so sure How very sweet it was going to be Even before you stooped low enough And got silly with me In conversations, in concentration, While sipping intoxicating brew, Even in every song I happen to hear Too often I think about you. To get these thoughts out of my head I might require a lobotomy, But any pain the memory will dull Of when you got silly with me Tomorrow’s an irrelevant question That can’t be answered today When so many ingrained responses Keep getting in the way Of thoughts that find no easy route Upon the road-map of reality Fueled only by my crazy hopes Ever since you got silly with me Just like a free radical You’ve upset my captive rest, At first I assumed it was Just another casual request, But somehow it’s developing Into my daily desperate plea - 'Someday soon,once again, Come and get silly with me”Written about,and while in the midst of,having an ex-m affair.Completely unexpected and maddeningly wonderful...but wouldn't recommend it to everyone.
