UNDATED  ALCOHOLIA


I have just one
Large,looming fear of
Straddling the coffee wagon
And drying out...
My reawakening senses
Will try to rediscover
What I was all about.

I never liked,
I really loathed
The ugly person I was
In the first place...
That’s probably the real reason 
Why I ever fell
From sobriety grace.

But,for your sake
And our future escapades,
I'll quit the first day
Of January, my Dear...
On the sole condition
You don’t ever ask me,
“Of which year?”This is one habit/addiction/crutch that i never really wanted to beat...largely because of the times it's like Scotty-it "Beams me up" !
