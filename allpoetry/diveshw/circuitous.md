CIRCUITOUSWhy does success knockSo considerately on the doorMost often of those peopleWho don’t really need more?Why do some rise to the occasionWhile the others just sink?Why do most behave contraryTo what they really think?Why does the mind chide the soulWhenever it begins to sing?Why does sanity always demandLogical reasons for everything?Is there a good enough reasonWhy someone crosses your mindAnd lingers there for longer thanA justifiable amount of time?Why can’t I consistently startMy downswing from the hips?Why do destinies collide?Why do astral planes shift?Why do I spend precious timeTrying to figure out any of this?Will my gain in the process beCommensurate to what I’ll miss?
