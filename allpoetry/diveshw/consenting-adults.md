CONSENTING  ADULTSA frothy shower?A bubbly bath, perhaps?Either way I knowI'll irrepressibly lose trackWhile we lather each other,As the warm water flows,Oblivious to all direction exceptWhere desire freely goes...Fervid French kissesMore vital than the dawnMy light, lithe fingersStirring, running alongThe silken path from your faceHanging-fire till your breasts,Steering me toward a whirlpoolOf priapic unrest...Close your eyes in abandon,Throw your head back,Command me to fillEvery void you have lacked.Exhume your primal soul,So constrained by the world,Let not one suppressed wantNow remain spurned...As fresh dew upon a flowerTenderly opens it up,Let your palpable needGuide my loving touch.Your virtuous self-controlWon't be good enoughOnce you’ve tasted the pleasureYou’re capable of...
