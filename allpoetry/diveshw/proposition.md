PROPOSITION


She rings me up
Excited about our evening
(Two weeks in planning)
With bated breath.
Her chimera so alluring
Thankfully empowers
My listless workday.

Her concerned voice rises
So very high pitched
(So very resuscitating)
And at least a universe
More potent than
The driven office-speak
Over these telephone lines

Innocently, she inquires,
"What should I wear?"
(A little too hastily)
I boldly declare,
"Wear nothing…
I can be there
In half an hour..."
