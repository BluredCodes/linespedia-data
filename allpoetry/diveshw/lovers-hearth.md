LOVERS  HEARTH.


Running eyes closed and headlong
Into a situation fraught with risk,
Hermetically sealed by the surety
Of one another’s glistening kiss.
With a myriad diadem virtues
Embedded in their quixotic minds,
‘Midst the belief that each other
They were surely blessed to find.

No decree is as commanding
As their reciprocal wishes,
His embrace is so tender,
Her lips are so delicious.
The treadmill others run upon
They will surely disobey,
Into routine mediocrity
They will surely never stray.

Relatives, friends, and appointments
Are dispatched to the front seat,
The barriers of their upbringing,
Like their clothes, fall to their feet.
The sun never shone more radiantly
Nor was Life’s meaning more entire,
May Fate’s undeterminable rain
Never douse the Lovers fire.
