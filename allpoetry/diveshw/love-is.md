LOVE  IS...

...a road of lowly pitfalls
And lofty promises gone astray,
Often ascending all else, more 
Often descending, deepest decay.
Fueling the fire in the spirit
Abruptly looking the other way,
Fleeting, with ready-made reasons
Why it cannot stay.

...a huge mountain of joy,
Cliff-hanging, very steep,
Its challenging climb more thrilling
Than settling upon its peak.
In a perpetually uncertain world
People rule each other by fear,
Like a security blanket
Most preferred, held very near.

...a freefall dived into with a
Fifty-fifty chance to survive,
A binge like an addicts syringe
Making the victim feel more alive.
The tentative question asked
Once initial passion is spent
By scrutinizing minds, determining
If for forever they’re meant.

...an unclear, surreal concept
Melding triumphant with vanquished,
Anesthetizing the willing senses
With the ephemeral joy it brings.
Its chemistry impossible to trace
Some rare, unknown compound,
Exciting the unstable mind
Imploding it, muted sound.

...eternal, just like hypocrisy,
It pretends to but doesn’t behave
Its two-faced laughter brims with tears
And haunts every birth unto the grave.
Whether for another human being,
A lifestyle, a thought or a bird,
It’s an umbrella term, freely used
For lack of a better word.
