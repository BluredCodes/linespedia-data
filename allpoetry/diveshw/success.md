SUCCESS

As this awesome, speeding train
Hurtles past our stagnant platform,
We use any means possible
And try our best to get on.
Hurdle onto this bandwagon,
Don't miss this passing bus,
It's a ride worth fighting for,
Look at the mad rush. 

It’ll transports us to a place
A much better one than this,
From rough mouthed abuse
To a dramatic lingering kiss,
From fleeting remembrances
To a state of perpetual bliss,
It’ll lead onward to a destination
Where nothing goes amiss. 

Its shrill whistle awakens us
On cold, uncomfortable nights
With warm dreams of every need
Attended to and satisfied.
We'll be respectable, acceptable
And enveloped in happiness,
Any person who's failed knows
The successful one is blessed 

Its importance, its existence
Simple logic has often defied,
Like the massacre of Red Indians
Its glorified, romanticized,
And not once in our history
Has it been made to justify,
It removed our every inclination
To even attempt to try. 

Besides,it might just stop for us
If,somehow,we can stand out,
If we can perform on cue,
If we can twist and shout.
Or it might steam-roll over
And crush us in its wake,
A very risky proposition
But a chance we'll gladly take.
