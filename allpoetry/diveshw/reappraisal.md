REAPPRAISALDeath is where the grim reaper waitsAfter setting off the starters gun,Love is still the best descriptionOne uses to control another one,Friendship is still the pact we makeTo dislike the same things,Debt is still the closest to deathExcept you’re alive and it really stings.Work is still the most emphasizedMethod of measuring our worth,Religion still offers the best excusesFor the pointlessness of birth.Doubt still shakily presents itselfWhenever confidence is absent,God still gets our vote of thanksFor our most pleasing accidents.Luck is still the private clubOf an ever-changing chosen few,Fate is still the forwarding addressWhere happiness moved on to.Courage is still what I lackWhen it comes to stand or run,Dreams are still best dreamt aloneSo they’ll never be frowned upon.Money is still the means we useTo ensure that we get heard,Success still matters most of allBut we still say, “It’s absurd!”.Secrets we want left unknownWe still keep to ourselves,Honour is still perched up highWith the dust on forgotten shelves.A smile is still so hard to come byAt the end of a chaotic day,Tears are still all we’re left withWhen Hope has fled away.Change is still the circumstanceThat happens much too soon,Touch is still my only deterrentFrom feeling completely doomed.
