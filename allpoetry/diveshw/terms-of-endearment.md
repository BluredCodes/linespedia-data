TERMS  OF  ENDEARMENT


“I love you”
Isn’t good enough,
It’s an umbrella phrase,
It sweeps away too much.

My unique animalistic scent?
My thrilling electric touch?
A well-endowed guardian
Against life’s rougher stuff
Who,most likely,won’t betray
Your implicit trust?

Am I the nuclear meltdown
Of your resistive crust?
Or just a perfunctory ingredient
With a dash of lust?
A corny conversationalist who
Tickles your funny bone?
Or a long-term companion
Who owns his own home?

For your timorous frailties
Am I the perfect crutch?
Or just a transactional dealing
That will never go bust?
Am I the logical perpetuation
Of your survival instinct?
Or are my anomalies really
That attractive and distinct?

Sometimes you’re seductive,
Sometimes you’re gruff,
Sometimes you fold,
At other times you bluff...
I know of no other concept
As confusing as Love,
Am I a bit of all
Or none of the above?
