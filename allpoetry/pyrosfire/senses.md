The heart caged in mirrors
Look…
Closed its eyes
Pump in pain…
Push out anger…
Listen…
The sound…
Ears are covered

Blood moves here to there
Splash upon the unknowing
Feel…
Draped in steel
Lemon juice sweet…
Sweet as the piss thrown upon me
Taste…
Mouth is shut

This…
This is what I wanted you to see
Hopeless when I was hurt
All senses felt
Enemy with remedies
Breath then speak
Kiss…
Blown away…
I am now too weak
So again
Senses felt abuse
Beyond its common use
Everyday fantasies
To keep out
What I made of you
