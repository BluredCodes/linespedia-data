You’re so small
Fragile... all in one piece
Given to me
All your love
No H I V

You may bleed 
And people will flee
Hell no 
Not me!

Hospitals might seem barred and scary
To me as well!
I know I scared you with these things

Me?
Oh don’t believe
These medicines will relieve
When you run and jump
Dance with glee

Please!
If you don’t 
You will be gone away from me
Maybe this horrible rhyme 
Won’t mean much in time

H I V will always be
When we aren’t
Away from sight and sea
Where you love to be

So all in all 
You are the world to me
Including H I V
You are no different than the air I breatheits just here! :)
