Move too fast and I wont seeHurt me too longand I wont be hereUnbelievable? Believe meLash out to hardand find outI will pull your cardUnbelievable? Believe meYou broke in to deepgo... take a leapI will laugh as you weepBeat upon those doorsas your hands bleedUnbelievable? Believe meAll those story's I buyWill be torched by my new lies!Oh no, don't tryAs you say it will all pass byUnbelievable? Believe meDon't want to cry?Too late!Wasn't I your bait?Your string to hate?Ha! So goTake a leaveUnbelievable, believe me!
