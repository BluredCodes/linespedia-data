title: "You to me, With me"
writer: "pyrosfire"
slug: "you-to-me-with-me"

Bleed your thunder voice
Hold your blessing
IM cast away
My blue faced dread
Your touch encased
Damned hearts kept to wait

Waters green
With the sun of our fate
Our moon glow
Moves too slow
Malice
Will become our groove and motion
Jaded...
Will become our general notion

Knife to vein
Explore obvious exits
But with our brains combined
Explicit!
Is our true hearts
Divine 
Red moon
Blue sun
We count our days as one
