Oh how pretty
Steel melts
Fire’s around me

Ouch! That hurt!
Look to your left stupid
Your computers half way burned

Oh what glee!
The fire is on me!
Dancing 
Prancing
I do! 
I do like what I see!

Yellow… blue…
The colors are riveting
Mask the color of clothes
Red… and green…
Oh yes! The flame grows higher

Vicky wake up!
Turn off your lighter!
