Candy as tasty as dead me
Thought my lies kept them sweet
Branded with my words of killer bees
Sorry’s on both knees
Shit...
Can you believe my ignorance?
Bitter seeds make my oxygen greed
You sit and breathe
Scared to see what will exhale from me

Blood candy cane stripes
Tasted good when you read my dirty mind
Evilness has tendencies to betray the kind
Reminiscent of all pleas
Chocolate kiss
Under rotten pear trees

Random acts of pleasantries
Blindfold touch
On my behalf, all rarities
Fire love on spider webs
Break and tear when it enters me

Straw thin hatred you hold 
Makes it hard to heal these bleeding leaves
That are hidden deep behind scabbed up memories
Too graphic indeed...
But it’s in meopinions invited!!!
