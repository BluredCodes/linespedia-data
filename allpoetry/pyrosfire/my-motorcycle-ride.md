---
title: "My Motorcycle Ride"
writer: "pyrosfire"

As I ride
I think..
What was put into these billboards I pass by?
Was it the pounding of muscles,
the stench of sweat?
It is rotten and withered,
So I see how man's work is left to die

As I ride

I try to think but the dust from the road blinds my eyes.
I twist and turn,
burning my hands and thighs,
sliding fast into a rock
I start to hollar and cry.

As my blood slowly leaves me
I start to think..
When people ride or pass by,
they will never know 
who or what died...
here...
they will only give it one glance
of the eye.
