title: "Bryan please hear me!"

I thought you were with meI always feared the inevitableThe obviousThe predictableSo here I sit, aloneRealizing I never had youBut wishing I could have knownWhat your love would have shown She is want you want. . .What you needShe makes your heart bleedTo you I will plead. . .With no avail!Your love will never lean towards me!I see you as perfection. . .Never a burden to me
