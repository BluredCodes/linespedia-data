title: "I cant reply to me"

Afraid? Alone?Content in this dome of dreamsI allow the world to seeI shouldn’t reply to meI begin to thinkThere is something wrongBehind my facade Of blue and black inkCouldn’t rest on what I believeOpinions rank and slaughter meThis will never leave!Alcoholics consume my family treeSo let’s paint that picture...Blue and black inkSoon I will leaveAnd be drowned in my drinkIt will never reply to me
