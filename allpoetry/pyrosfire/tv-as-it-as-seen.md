Guess...
What...
What’s in?
Not me see I am not going to flee
I guess the "in" can be seen as sin
For what you believe in

Believe?
Believe in home shopping
And acne curing things
For sure people will see me
With costume
And accessories
Right!
I am weak from head to knee
Isn’t feet different?
Just like honey and vinegar
It seems

Cruelty...
Hand in hand with dishonesty
That’s the world on TV
Faces full of cream
The new American dream
Never true!
Down to the seams!
Fit into what it says to be

Oh!
Now I see!
Television
Became true vision
For weak and timid tuition
Believe it or not
That’s the world we all see!well thats real life as we see on T.V.
