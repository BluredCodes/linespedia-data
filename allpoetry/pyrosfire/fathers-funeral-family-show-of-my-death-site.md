Light me on fire
Upon our place of rest
Peace and sanity
Fake and false...
Our family tree

See you on Sunday
My fathers death nest
Cry and criticize
Go and cleanse your minds
Of hatred your heart grows inside
Haven’t seen me in what? 10 years or so
Go ahead and speak of me
While my back is turned 
In tears on the front side

Brother, father, lover,
All seen in blood
And relation
Heart is obscene
Until you show how your money gleams!

Facade...
No emotion in screams
Father, brother, lover,
Was gone on Christmas Eve
And all that is seen is vanity and greed

In me...
Winter in all states
Maybe later family will create and breathe
Emotional incubus mistakes me
For what it should see...
Higher branches on this family
Where money seeks the deep chest bleed

Cling and heave
On what you thought was real
Alcohol in me
Quit?
Swing on one tree to come right back
Father, brother, lover
End up eventually
Boxed and craving...
No life in the living alcohol feed

So...
Father, brother, lover
Go on and creep upon his casket
Soon you will see me
The same as he!say what you please   :)
