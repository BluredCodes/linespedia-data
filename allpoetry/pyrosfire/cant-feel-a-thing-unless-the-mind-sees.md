Candle box remedies
Light on fire…
See it bleed
Burn your brain and it’s tendencies
Unburden memories
Char in the hearts greed…
You’ll cry

Mind and body
The hearts drive
Tears from evil eyes
Brown hair and skin
Justice in lies…
When searched within
Doors shut closed
Demon with in
Rage and scar already dirty skin
Sorrows for the mind

Not truth unless the mind is on it’s normal reel
Image in eyes…
Things supposedly…
Feel…
Strength in numbers
Brain one…
Hands none
The mind controls what the heart cant hold
