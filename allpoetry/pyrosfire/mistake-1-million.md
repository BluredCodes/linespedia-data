---
title: "Mistake # 1 million"
writer: "pyrosfire"
slug: "mistake-1-million"

Tempt me with your switching expression
Give me an excuse...
Smile
Watch and see the blast of a guilty frown scar my face
I committed an act of false love

And sorry does it for you?
Oh how this blast burns harder
I betrayed you tens times over my body height
Mistakes # 1 - 99
I made you smile

Could pain grow any higher?
When you sat by me
And told me no hurt necessary
One tear dropped...
"I love you"
Mistakes # 7 9 9
Your forgiveness for my lies


Riddle me with soft breath and laugh
Smooth, sweet...
Smile
Hands press on my charcoal mask
No heat felt
Just love 
You believed in me
An echo of past mile drives
I slept while you kept on the ride
Watch and see
Whispered into sleepy ears
Mistake #9 9 3
I let you care for me

Concrete walls
Broken hands upon
Even then
Protection was seen
When you saw this from me
Making up in kiss and hands
You saw heaven in my mouth and eyes
Hugging, dozing
Oh how you felt alive!
Mistake # 1 0 0 5
I never kissed you right

Do this, take that!
Unreasonable questions to ask!
I saw you as a fake
Run through loops of snake
Test to see you break 
Battered and blundered you crawl back
"I love you"
Later to sleep in you... 
Sleep in heartache
Mistake # 7 0 0 8
Idiotic debate

By the time when the mistake of six 9's 
I made you cry
My wrist were gone...
1 million...
I left you alone in our world
Waiting to die
