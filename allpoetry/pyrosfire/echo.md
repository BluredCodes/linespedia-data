title: "Echo"

I have seen blackness in my mirror of words
Oh so its called an echo
You saw the best of me 
Only when I slept peacefully
No words...
The only time my mouth opened
Was when I breathed
You finally relaxed around me
Too bad I couldn’t see

Awake and angry
Clinging to last exchange of words...
You had with me
Room empty
Walls of white started to bleed with my tears
Angry with you?
I shouldn’t be
My metal heart bent in deep...
Break...
Splash fist in floors
Head on concrete
Shattered glass from my hidden doors
Echo...

Let it be seen
I didn’t deserve trust
That you release to me!
"I’m sorry"
Echo... for eternity
