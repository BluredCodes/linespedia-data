Mother!
How are you?
Did you see the show?
Oh...
No?

Your son was there
Right behind me 
Looking, feeling
Exploring me
In places he wasn’t supposed to be

Do you remember?
No?
When he took me in the park and said "hide and seek"
Guess what I found!
Hard and horrid
I ran and hid

Mother you didn’t see?
When I protected my sister and me
I know you were scared
But you should have stood up
You were there!

We didn’t need this 
You saw our tears
But know  
I saw your fears

Father as son
Scars mental...
Physical...
Itches worse than wool
You were scared
You would never dare

No blame mother love
But feel this pain

You keep him to you
He will say sorry
You don’t see his intent!
His fake worries!

Mother! 
Please see what my brother will do to me!please be honest forghet the fact that it is personal please!!!
