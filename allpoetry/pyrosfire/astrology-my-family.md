Signs and astrology
Scorpio, Gemini, pieces
are all gone
when the eyes are open
and can see

cancer you are
a cancer to sweet

Libra stands by on poison trees 
they seep acid into the trunks
were I wait to see

that cancer
cancer to sweet

Gemini reeks havoc 
upon aquarian eyes
and this Scorpio desperately try’s
to see that cancer 
through smoke and lies
Capricorn has sworn to me
that I will see 

my cancer
cancer to sweet

Taurus dies in the mist of Christmas
at the wake
cancer brings its mistress
can this hell go slightly well?

Leo...
mile high above me
shown this Scorpio
what sickness means
disease plagued that leo's tree
that cancer comes from leo's seed

cancer...
that sign means death for me
indeed...I just used all the signs in my family to express this
