---
title: "Gone... But I Am Here"
writer: "pyrosfire"

You left
Brown hair and eyes...
On a plane
Flew away
So did I

Just different
I drowned...
Drowned in what you gave me
A flood in my eyes
A fill of alcohol
Which you despise

A brain full of images...
Friends, parties, college
A real life
Things you like

Sending regards to my heart
In letters and pictures
So comes the image...
Happiness
Diamond in a coal piece
You’re there...
And that’s how I am seen?

Lie to keep me at peace
You’re in love...
1 2 3 
Then you’re here flirting...
Flirting with me

Make up your mind
Stay there...
Or be here...
Here with me
Ok!

So tell me
Why did you leave? 
To come back to me?
