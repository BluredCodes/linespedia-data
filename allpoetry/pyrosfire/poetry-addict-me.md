MASTER PIECE
BORED WITH ANXIETY
REACH FAR BEYOND 
THE MINDS COMPLEXITY
BRAIN STORM CLINGES ON SUDDEN PLEASENTRIES
IT MAKES CLARITY

WRAPPING THESE CORDS 
FROM THE RYHMING BASE
THE ONLY WAY EMOTIONS KEEP ON PACE
RHYME TO SOUND
HEART TO MOUTH
I LET THESE FEELINGS OUT

POEMS
ELASTIC ON MEMORY
CLAUSTROPHOBIC AROUND FAMILY
PAPER MIXES PEN...
MY PURE REMEDY
WRITING LYRICS IN THE DARK
LISTENING TO MELODIES

WORDS OFF DRUNKEN LIPS
MORE THAN I HAVE EXPIRIENCED
IT’S JUST WHAT IVE SEEN
THAT I PUT ON THIS FLASHING SCREEN
THE FUTURE MOCKS THIS ENTRY
OF MY WORDS
BECAUSE I CANT SEE FARTHER THAN WHATS IN FRONT OF ME
I GUESS THAT’S WHAT MY MOTHER TOLD ME
FAILURE TO BELIEVE
BELEIVE WHAT YOU WILL ACHIEVE
UNLESS YOU ARE HOOKED ON DREAMS
POEMS...
