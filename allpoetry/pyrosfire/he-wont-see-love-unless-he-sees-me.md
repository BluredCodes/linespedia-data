Restless burns
Burden his damped emotions
On point 
With his words of wisdom
Inattentive to his hearts call

Follow him under broken lights
I am asking his heart
Not his mind
Come close…
Feel comfort at night

Blessed heart estranged from him
Plant it above me
Bloom it into me
No more horrid darkness
I already answered his hearts plea

My courier’s message of love 
Temporarily sustain
Devotion alone made it be

Just listen
That alone will let us live to see 82 and 83
His kiss of ginger
Sweet above sour love will linger
