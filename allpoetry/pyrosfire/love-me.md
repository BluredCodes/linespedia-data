---
title: "Love me"
writer: "pyrosfire"
slug: "love-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1329-Love-me-by-pyrosfire"
---

I won't let you go
unless you give your all
So let's give in
forget control

Give in to what's under our mold
give up all strength
to what confusion holds

It is wrong!
Is what they say
But they will never see
how we lay . . .
together
