title: "Alive... For The Last Time"
writer: "pyrosfire"

Listen to my words as they are spoken
Throw away caution
Restrictions aside
I am giving you all I have inside
This is my last night alive

If you leave my blood dries 
My veins crumble
Right before your eyes
Your love releasing
Kept my walk in stride
For so long in this life time
No matter 
I am still going to die

Tonight
My last moon
No more sunrise
I want to hold you
Keep you in my eyesight
When the night surrenders to light
My soul will be crying by your side
Eternally

Believe that my last breath
Will be to confess
My love to you 
As I lay on your chest
And have my life turn to death
