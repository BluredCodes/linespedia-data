Hello father
Are your hands ok
They should be swollen
By all the hits you have laid

Oh how did I know?
You think I didn’t see?
You think it didn’t show?

Think I didn’t see 
When I was in bed
Don’t tell me your hands haven’t bled
I saw her tears stream red
So what I was only ten

What is that father?
Need the alcohol cape?
Oh yeah!
I know I am a product of rape
Did it happen when she ran down the hall
And you smashed her face against a wall

So I am here
Go ahead 
Grab the faithful beer 
I won’t feel that fear
She had to adhere

Yes father you are dead to me
What father? Why did I bother?
SHE WAS ONLY MY MOTHER!
