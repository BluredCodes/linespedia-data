---
title: "Be Kind When The Heart  Needs"
writer: "pyrosfire"
slug: "be-kind-when-the-heart-needs"
source: "AllPoetry"

Speak to me when I cry
Please be humble when I am dry
and on my knees
Back me up against your wall
If your lust is weak
I will fall

Plowed onto your dirty sidewalk
For my heart is sore and meek

So I have picked up on your pace
Encased where there is no space

I have been drugged into your maze
of heart break
Will it ever change?
