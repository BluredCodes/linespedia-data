title: "Earths Blood... Rainy Day"

I walk slowly upon your afternoon
It’s my day
The sky drips its blood across my face
So it all ends up, as it seems

Stormy clouds
Rainy day
Weather or wither
My body moves along its way
Nor voice or pain can clear this

It’s a rainy day

My body’s a wander
Dragging feet against the water
Black socks soaked
Who will care?
How will the draining color ever show?

Slow walk of the morning 
Your day
Umbrella in hand
What’s the need?
People will still stare 
And laugh at me

Body or face
Clothes drenched
With tears and rain
The earth shows us its pain

All will stand in awe and restrain
The urge to run in houses
And hide away

From our earth’s rage
For me and you...
What a rainy day!
