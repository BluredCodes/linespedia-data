In skin
In eyes 
In hair
Peek at his beauty
I won’t dare
Breathe and sleep
Rise and fall

Speak, listen
Hear, say
Conquered within
Blissful whisper
Let him play
 
Pulling, holding
Feeling, burning
Powerful wonder
Thrusting harder 
As I am held under

Mind, body
Piercing spirit
Yearning, screaming
Pleasure under minds all meaning

Fatigued, sweating soul
Loves young and old
Pass by in his dimming eyes
Nothing compares to what he felt inside

Breathe…
Out and in
Slow sleep pressures him
Content with the world we have within
