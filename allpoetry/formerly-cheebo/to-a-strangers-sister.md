title: "To a Stranger's Sister"

Like a stream in a mountain chattering sweet
Where each tiny ounce of water passes once
And each tiny ounce molds canyons and hills
And wears away rock the earth held for ages
So does each passing moment, when we were bound
Which comes from God's thought and passes from ours
And each tiny action smooth us into men
And binds us together who once stood apart

I'm sorry Rof Cau
