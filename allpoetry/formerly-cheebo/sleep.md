title: "sleep"
writer: "formerly cheebo"
slug: "sleep"

Like waters fall into sand, so my thoughts fall into sleep
The weary days, the sweetening airs, pull me to my rest
And return my plans, my asthetics, my hopes, to their keep
In the earth, in the chambers of silence, which I detest
Even as my body begs for slumber, for respite
Even as my mind wears on the losing fight
