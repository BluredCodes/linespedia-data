your eyes are so true
Your lips are so sealed
your hands are so cold and soft
your eyes are so true
Your skin is so pale
Your tears are so slow
Your eyes are so true
Your fingers are so slender
Your waist is so narrow
Your eyes are so true
Your skin is so pale
your eyes are so true
