Where is the rain that falls on you?
Where is the earth on which you walk?
Where is the darkness in which you sleep,
Under which stars and billowing clouds?
Where can we talk together, under what trees?
Where can we wander, on what lonely paths?
Where can we hide together, when nightfall comes,
Under which stars and billowing clouds?
