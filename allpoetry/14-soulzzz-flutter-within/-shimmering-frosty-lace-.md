* ~ * ~ Shimmering Frosty Lace.....

~ > ~ > Fall upon My hair with masses of flurries, reflecting Your BreathTaking, Cleansing SpiritedNess.

~ > ~ > Douse Me in an Amazing source of that replenishing Nourishment You forever bare.

~ > ~ > FullFill My pores up with Your EnLightening Nurture fruitfully enhanced by shocking inspired CoolNess.

~ > ~ > EndLessly ReachOut, ever so still, lolling indolently beneath My eagerly wandering feet as I enthusiastically stroll, all about, upon Your astonishingly EndLess "Fractals", that >>DO<< ReFresh My mind in such the Awe-Striking fashion.

~ > ~ > Observe My Love for Your very essence flourish within each breath of Frosty Mist I exhale out into the Wilderness LandScape that artistically surrounds Your SereneNess of a WonderFilled Livelihood, leaving My existence BreathLess.

~ > ~ > Watch mine eyes blazingly Light-Up, and Sparkle all throughout that Brilliance of extreme iced essence You graciously exhibit.

~ > ~ > Lovingly embrace, with such Noble Gestures, those worthy colours of PeaceFelt reflections onto Your shimmer as the Sun rises to greet that acknowledging Fate of watering this Earth's WonderLand, anxiously....
...all for Nature's intent of LifeGiving Sustenance throughout the Season's Majestic MasterPiece.

~ > ~ > Then as the warmth of the Sun slowly desolves glimmering masses of frozen Lace, straight into Your distinguished destiny, all Humans being Blessed by an OutStanding EyeFull of ThankFullNess will undoubtedly see, touch, taste, and smell the awesomely DeLightFull Miracle of Your Infinitely Changing Worldliness.

~ > ~ > You have truelly echoed around in My Soul with a Pure Mystical grasp of Beauty.  For, within each and every icey-thin pieced Molecule, I can feel that soothing TenderNess from Caressing Cuddles of FreshNess.

~ > ~ > You truthfuly embrace all objects within Your path's LifeForce....

~ > ~ > And I'll remember You FullFilling every Boundry thru My 
Spirited Being....

~ > ~ > With an Eternal OutBurst of Excitement....

~ > ~ > All of My LifeTime.
