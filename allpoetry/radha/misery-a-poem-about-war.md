Whatever happened to your limbs? 
Your limbs of lust
Did you lose them in the street?
Or in the pub?
Did you lose them to a madman
A cruel executioner of deceit?
Or did you lose them in the fields
While tending to your sheep?



I saw him spin a web
A web of limbs and suffering
Where screams and terror fill the night
And the ground opens up to a new world
Take my hand my love
And I shall set you free
Don't be frightened
For you are misery.
