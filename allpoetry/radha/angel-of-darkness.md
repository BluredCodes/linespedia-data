You are my angel
My angel of darkness
I can feel you next to my skin
Your silky scales
I know it's forbidden
For you to take me to your paradise
For us to eat
Eachothers souls
And encase our hearts in darkness
Vein against vein
Throat against thought
Slice
We are bonded my love.


Bonded to desire
To our graves
Where fire will turn our bones to cinder
And my ashes will blow into your face
Engulf your body and your shadow
Breathing harder and harder
Until you swallow
My pain.


And cut me free
From this womb of pain
From this sea of blood
Which drowns my soul
And pickles my heart.
