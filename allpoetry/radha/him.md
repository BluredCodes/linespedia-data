---
title: "Him"
writer: "radha"
slug: "him"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love is a fairy tale
Love is a fantasy
It is indestructable
The core of life
I understand.

It flows through my veins
Became a part of me
It was like water given to a seed
Flourishing inside me.

Yet I still feel him now
Long for his smell, his touch
Him.
Ripped away from me
As a baby is torn mercilessly from its mother's womb
A scar that cannot be healed
A space that can never be filled.

Time is the enemy
The traitor. 
It can destroy a person's soul
Crush the heart
Love.
Time can be a healer, a giver,
A savior
Saving my soul
Someday time will love me
Someday I will feel his arms around me
And melt away.
