This isn't the first time I have seen you
I caught a glimpse of you long before now
Superficial sacrifices plague our lives
Only the plasticity of your words can exist now
Running down a white corridor i see your face
Your image imprinted in my mind

Bathing in the sickness of my anguish i long to be free of you
Of this
Of everything that haunts my soul
To you i am a like a beautiful flower
Bearing the scars of your seeds

Your divine words echo through me
I mourn for your dedication
Crucified by hurt
Imprisoned by love
