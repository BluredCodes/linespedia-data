You are so rich
As rich and plump as a fruit cake
So rich that you give me indegestion
Intoxicate my body without any question
Without your richness you would flop
Your honeysuckle ears and raison eyes would drop
You would fall to the floor and become a mangled mush
And I would stamp on you 
And turn your flourey body to dust.
