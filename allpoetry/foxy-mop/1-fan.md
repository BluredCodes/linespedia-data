---
title: "#1 Fan"
writer: "Foxy Mop"

I bet us ugly girls
are your biggest fans,
with hands for feet
and feet for hands.

So you.re not surprised, it.s like this -
I.m in room number 666.
Yeah, the one at the end of the hall,
with muffled sounds escaping the walls.

I want you to love me, but it.s like this -
unless you.re ready, don.t open it.
Or you.ll run down the stairs, out to the streets,
and tell Jesus "that.s the sickest girl I.ll ever meet."

To the pretty girls
are you another fan?
Cause I always hoped
you would understand.

So I pick at scabs and create more wounds,
but I take my pills to be good for you.
And I pray to Jesus the words he should say
when you come screaming up to him someday.
