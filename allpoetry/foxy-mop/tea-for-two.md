TEA FOR TWO

Identical angels
in the snow
This is how I pretend
that I.m not alone

I set the table
tea for two
I drink them both
though one.s for you

There.s another hit
and you can have it
Dear, genorousity
is a killing habit

I say this seat is taken
that you.re always late
So late, in fact
you.ve never came
