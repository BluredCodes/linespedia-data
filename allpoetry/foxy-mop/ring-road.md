title: "Ring Road"
writer: "Foxy Mop"

I drive aimlessly around
and god don.t make a sound
   when I ask if he wants to meet me today

I would feel so relieved
if I could only believe
   he.s simply listening to hard to respond when I pray

But I wonder why
I am assumed as so wise 
   to understand without being told

So I go on assuming
everyone.s god is a human
   the ugly ones who control other.s movements

My usual pleas
   are soothed only by rain
I love it.s empathy
   it.s spreading of pain
And to me it seems fitting
that the dead are spitting
   on their gods below
