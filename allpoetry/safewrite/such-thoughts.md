I want you to die - 
But you’re already dead.
What I want to die
Is inside my head.

Right the wrongs
And never be
Don’t exist -
Set me free.

Trajectory shifted
Can’t reach ahead
Pulled from behind
Force of the dead.

Mortal enemy
Mortal friend
Pay the price
And let it end.

Wash the past
And cleanse it 
Wash in peace
And end it.
