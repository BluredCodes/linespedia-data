Whenever I try
To think of all else
You intrude in my thoughts
Again.

It could be your eyes
It could be your smile
They are equally guilty,
My friend

It might be your voice now
It could be your touch 
It’s so hard to think clearly 
You see

There’s only one cure-all
That I know the allure of
And it’s getting the better
Of me

Yet I say take it slow
Because we should all know
Getting there is one half of 
The play

And the anticipation
Of a closer relation
Can be lovely in its 
Burning way
