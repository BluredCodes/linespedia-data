I’m out of medication
And uninsured.
No money.
It's wearing off.
It's coming back.
And I’m powerless,
Dragged under.

Wish I had insurance,
I just want to live
Feeling alive, 
Connected.
I miss that.

Everything’s blurring
Help me, Jesus,
I want to feel normal again.

But normal
Costs about 80 bucks
And I’d rather be sick
Than homeless.
