title: "No Room at The Inn"
writer: "safewrite"

She drifts through the days, fighting,
Trying to keep her head above water.
Why is everything so hard
Such a struggle
Just to get through the day
And not lose ground.

She can’t quite keep up with the budget
The budget runs over her.
Always a day late or a dollar short.
It’s not for lack of trying
The effort takes each ounce of strength

And those who don’t understand put her down - 
Mind readers who are sure of her motivations.
She is lazy, a loser, a write-off, and a mess to them.
But it takes more effort for her to take a shower 
Than it does for the average person to climb a mountain.
At least God knows.

But the compassionate doors of the judgmental
Close in her face.
No room in the Inn for chronic losers.
You are a problem we cannot solve.
Why don’t you just go away?
