Sing of the words upon the page
Songs of lilting phrases
A paean to the written word
That humbles and amazes

See how it breaks your heart in two
See how it surprises
Watch how a reader gasps in awe
As you shatter his surmises

Is there as rich a medium?
Their minds become your canvas
Paint with a palate made of words
Landscapes and lads and lasses

Oh to be nimble as a word
To leap from thought to thought
To jump to a conclusion
And then realize you’re caught!
