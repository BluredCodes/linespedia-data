---
title: "Outside the Box"
writer: "safewrite"

There is no point in asking people to see
What is outside of their frame of reference.
I am outside of your frame of reference
And therefore, invisible.
How can I ask you to be there for me
When you don’t know where I am,
When I myself am lost -
Outside of the box.
