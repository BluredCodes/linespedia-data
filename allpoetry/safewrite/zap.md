I’ve decided to turn in my defenses
They’re malfunctioning,
Short circuited.

I think someone overloaded my senses -
In fact, I’ m pretty sure of it

I think I know the culprit
I can even give you a good description:
He’s six feet tall, and gorgeous
With cinnamon-colored eyes you could lose yourself in
Broad shoulders, and a strong chest to pull you up against him.
He has a voice like a wet dream
And the most gentle hands

If you happen to see this dangerous character…

Stay back.
Let me handle him.
