Yeah, all right.
Something’s wrong.
Why do you have to go and make it worse?
Why do you enjoy putting others down?
What do you get out of it?
What’s your problem?

I feel sorry for you.
You only have two options:
To remain as nasty as you already are,
Or to go through tragic circumstances - 
And maybe learn 
What it feels like
To be on the receiving end 
Of insult and injury.

If that ever happens,
And someone treats you as
You’ve treated me…

God help you
