Finding excuses
Reasons to touch
A fancy new pen in my pocket
A new ring, your bright sweater – 
All become pretexts for contact
That we are both otherwise 
Too shy to make

And I blush at each
Subtle caress
And you flush when I dare
To make some small dent
In our wall of reserve

All of this tells us
More than words:
Here is someone we find
Increasingly interesting
Someone with whom 
Such contact is 
Not taken lightly 

Someone to whom this is not casual at all.

In a world of indifferent intimacy,
We see another 
Who feels respect for even the possibility
Of that kind of tender trust.
