Two steps forward,
One step back.
Life crawls forward
Then yanks you back.

Even worse 
Are the times when
It’s two steps up, 
Then down again.

It’s the choke collar
Of a dog-walk life:
Things get good
Then cut like a knife.

Somehow we expected
A smooth upward climb
Where is it easy?
Only in your mind.

Two steps forward,
One step back.
Life crawls forward
Then yanks you back.

Don’t be surprised -
That’s how life is
The road less traveled
Acknowledges this.
