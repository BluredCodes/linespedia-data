Draw back
Don’t press
Avoid him –
Show no interest

Not coy
Just quiet
Not forward
Just efficient

Make him draw you out.

You are setting him free
If he does not return,
He was never yours.

Until you know
Which way it will go
Walk a tightrope with dignity,
And look the other way

Take your cue from him.
By looking into reflective surfaces
And watch
See if he follows you with his eyes.

Then make him wait a few times
Before you bother to look back.
