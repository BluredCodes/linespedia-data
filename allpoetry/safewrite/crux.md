Save me your platitudes -
You don’t understand.
If God does, I shudder.
Does that mean
That this is part
Of what Christ bore
On the cross?
Or does it mean
That when they say
Christ was tested and tried
In every way
That this was one of them?
Did he suffer abuse?
Was he raised in a dysfunctional home?
Was he hit?
Was he violated.
Yet without sin?

It is not a sin
To be a victim.
But it does tend to give people a chance
To nail you.
