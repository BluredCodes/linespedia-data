So this is what it is like
To see you again
In a professional setting
To ignore the ache
And smile in a business fashion
To fight back tears
Once you were out of sight

So this is what it is like
To speak of only work
To keep it short and bittersweet
And work hard
At keeping an even keel
(It wasn’t a shipwreck –
Just a canceled cruise…)

And that is what it was like
In a way it was goodbye
And a long distance one at that
From a distance of two feet
And one ocean apart
Sail on by.
