I understand that
If you’re dying of cancer
Life comes into focus.
And all the small stuff
Is revealed as truly small.
Only what will remain
For the next hundred years
Matters.

If you live with depression
Life may not quite focus
But remember the small stuff
Is still quite small.
Focus on what will matter
In a hundred years
And all of the rest 
Will quietly fall into place.

Even if you can’t get to it all
You can get to what counts
In a hundred years.

So plan ahead.
