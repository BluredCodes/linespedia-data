You don’t command a cat
You win it as a friend
A fellow passenger
On spaceship Earth
Who insists on
Being treated
With dignity and respect.

A dog may protect you
And it will offer you its heart
Without reservation.
It has depths of unconditional love
That cats will never see.

Be like the cat -
Insist on dignity and respect.
Be like the dog -
Give unconditional love.
Balance the two
And poets and angels
Will marvel at your wisdom.
