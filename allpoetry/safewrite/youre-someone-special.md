You’re someone special
And I can’t comprehend
How anyone could walk out on you.

What was she thinking?
Couldn’t she see?
You are:
Strong and gentle
Commanding and kind
Complex yet straightforward
And oh, so wonderful.

It would easily take 
Several lifetimes to know you
And never could the joy 
Of your companionship be tiresome.

(I suppose that it’s always
The nice ones that get hurt.)

But I shall never understand -
For the only way to understand 
Would be to be have a similar nature,
And I can’t even bring myself to think
Of hurting you2000,
