title: "Ducks in a Row"

Hear them quack -
They’re making noise.
Its the sound of progress.
Progress!
It's finally true.

I can see it happening
I never thought to see
The day I could look forward to
Having all my ducks in a row.

Hear them quack -
Lovely weather for ducks
There’s something in my heart.
Spring!
It's finally time.

I can feel it happening 
After all that snow
The time I have looked forward to -
Having all my ducks in a row.

Think of all the problems of the past
As April showers
After all that toil
New life is springing up
Through all those layers of soil. . .

Hear them quack,
Such a cheerful sound
It sounds right -
Finally!
It's about time.

I can almost touch it;
It's almost within reach,
I’ll line them up just so: 
Imagine!
Having all my ducks in a row.
