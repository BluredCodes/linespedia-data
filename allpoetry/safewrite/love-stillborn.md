Love stillborn
Will never be
But still requires
A headstone and a prayer

The cradle lovingly fashioned
Is unnecessary
The room in the heart
Uninhabited
The picture frame 
Is empty

Walk past love’s nursery
With open eyes
Let the pain happen, as it must

But do not walk in
And take the room apart
Until you are ready

And when you do
Make sure to put a keepsake
In your heart of hearts
So that the emptiness
Does not echo
