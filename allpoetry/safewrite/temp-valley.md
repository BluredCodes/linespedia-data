Temp work.
Short term assignments.
You can make a good impression
And then leave
Before the pattern emerges
And people realize
That you are defective merchandise.

Like a car that was freshly painted
Over unsanded rust spots, 
Stay too long,
And they buyers will realize
That you are not a good deal
So move on.This was how I supported my family after their father left me with three kids and no child support. I was clinically depressed, but not diagnosed. It was awful. See how much better and read Adjustments, with love to Jen.
