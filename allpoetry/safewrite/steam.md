title: "Steam"

It’s been so many years
Alone in bed
Alone every day, every night

And I’d gotten so used to it
Never particularly comfortable
But used to it, ‘til now

Now I try to get back
To that quiet frame of mind
But it’s not working

To say that he distracts me 
Is the understatement
Of the year

To say that I’ve 
Got a handle on this
Would be a gross lie

Things need to go forward –
All the way forward
And soon

Or they need to stop
Before this exquisite torture
Does me in

I’m a red-hot, magnetized,
Molten mass right now
Helpless ‘til he makes his move
