---
title: "For you"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I look into your eyes
every chance that i can find
And through them i can see
how great you are inside

I love to see you smile
I love to hear you laugh
I love it when you look at me
To show me that you care

I love to be around you
It feels so good to hear your voice
The assurance of your presence
i wouldnt take any other choice

Everything about you
Can bring a smile to my face
you lift me off my feet
and Take me away to that special place

Open up your heart
To let me inside
and show you everything
so theres nothing left to hide

And believe me when i tell you
your voice, smile, laugh, and eyes are just the start
Something inside you is even more beautiful
Its your special caring heart.

In the short time that ive known you
You've proven to be kind, sweet, gentle and so much more
your everything ive always wanted
your exactly what im looking for

I've never met anyone else like you
simply because people like you are hard to find
Thats why i feel so lucky to know you.....
and why i wish you were mine.
