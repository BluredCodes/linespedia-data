My pain
My fear
My anger and Rage

Im slain
Im seared
Im locked in a cage

My heart
My mind
Its all said and done

A part
I find
Im left here with none

I dont take
I give
I swallow my pride

My lifes at stake
I live 
with it bottled inside

Keep your words
Save your breath
Tell it to someone else

Its the worst
Heres the rest.....
Im begging for your help
