title: "Nothing"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"

Im so sure ive got nothing
So what is it that your taking?
Everyday you leave with something
and im left here bitter and shaking

you take away whats gone
And empty out the emptiness
you correct me when your wrong
and magnify the ugliness

This is all too heavy
Everythings a lie
Kill me when your ready
Im waiting here to die

I cant live anymore
The life i lead is Done
And i was just so sure
that you could be the one

you blame me for your faults
but theres nothing i can do
And im left here with Nothing
Nothing to live for.......but you.Dont think i can make it on my own.
