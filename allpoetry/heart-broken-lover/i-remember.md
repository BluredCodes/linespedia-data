---
title: "I Remember"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"

I remember your face
I remember when i met you
I remember your taste
And how i cant forget you

I remember your smile
I remember your hair
I remember all the while
you were never ever there

I remember a superman shirt
I remember a baggy pink sweater
I remember all the hurt
I remember nothing better

I remember a time
when smiling was my thing
I remember your lies
and the heartbreak that they bring

I remember the night
I told you that i loved you
I knew it was right
As i leaned in to hug you

I remember long walks
I remember your mom
I remember long talks
I remember your gone

I remember your voice
I remember your laugh
I didnt have a choice
i didnt have a path

I remember our bond
I remember your friends
i remember your gone
i remember the end

you were my everything
My reason for living
i remember your kisses
and i remember leaving

I remember crying
Endlessly and alone
I remember being scared
I remember leaving home

I still see you
here and there sometimes
But every second im without you
your always on my mind

You are and will be
My first true love
look inside and you'll still see
Everything above.This is for my ex-girlfriend. Love can be a tuff thing, but i found moving on is even harder.......
