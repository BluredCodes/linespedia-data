---
title: "Every Single Day"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"
slug: "every-single-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

Please dont runaway
please dont be scared
just give me more time
to show you that i care

Im not like all the others
I'll never turn my back on you
I'll always be here to help
and i wont stop caring about you

I know how you feel
Because I feel the same
And theres no one else that can help us
and theres no one left to blame

So well have to help eachother
Every step along the way
and i promise to remain by your side
every single day

You're always on my mind
and always in my heart
i miss you every second,
every second were apart

So give me your heart
and give me your hand
and give me a chance
to make you understand

Were perfect for eachother
in every single way
So let me hug you, kiss you, and love you,
Every single day
