Looking for a good time
Why'd you 2 have to be there?
Keep quiet to supress my anger
I sense the spitefulness in your stares

I cant stand either of you
Your so full of yourselves
I suggest not talking to me
You can both just go to hell

Then there was you,
Whom i wasnt really upset with
But your ignorant remarks
Added you to the shit list

Now 3 out of 7 are enemies
This is almost more than i can take
There presence is pushing and pulling on me
There gonna keep on pushing til i break

Sitting on the edge of my seat
Smallest incomferences set me off
Pray to god i dont hear any of you speak
Not even a sneeze, a whisper, or a cough

you have no idea how much i hate you
But its only because you hate me
You think your better than me in every way
And i cant let somethign like this be

You may have avoided it tonight
But i can see the time draws near
One of you will set me off
And you'll really have something to fear.I am a nice person when your nice to me. But i cant deal with people like that who think there so much better than everyone else. These people are gonna get whats coming to them. And they will get it from me.
