Take my life
I'll live off you
Take my air
I'll breathe through you

Take my wisdom
We share one mind
Take my water
your thrist is mine

Take my soul
Our spirits we share
Take my home
I'll live with you there

Take my body
you are my form
Take my clothes
you'll keep me warm

Take my heart
Put My love in its place
Take my Moods
and the expressions on my face

Take everypart of me
If it satisfies your needs
Im willing to give it all
If your willing to believe
