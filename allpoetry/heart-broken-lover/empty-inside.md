title: "Empty Inside"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"

I dont wanna hear it,
your lame excuse again.
your never ever sincere,
all you do is pretend.

Once i open my eyes,
your days of torturing me are through.
I ask for so little,
I want nothing more than to be rid of you

you steal my thoughts
you crush my pride
Until theres nothing left
and im empty inside.
