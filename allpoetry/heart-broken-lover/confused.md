---
title: "Confused"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"
slug: "confused"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30011-Confused-by-Heart-Broken-Lover"
---

An unshown emotion
renders itself useless
I cant use it
I dont know how

The words that need to be spoken
are right there in front of me
Why dont i just grab them?
i cant

Tears flow from my eyes
But my feelings remain trapped inside
They cant escape
No matter what i try

CONFUSED, is how i will remain
Each day until im cured
Try to make it through the daily pain
Into which im lured
