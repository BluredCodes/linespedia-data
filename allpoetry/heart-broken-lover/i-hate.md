---
title: "I Hate"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"

I hate my family
I hate my life
I hate myself
I want to die

I hate the things
you make me do
I hate the things
you put me through

I hate the fact
That im alone
I hate the way
this goes unknown

I hate my teachers
I hate my freinds
This pain i feel
It never ends

I hate the world
and whats inside
I hate myself
i want to die
