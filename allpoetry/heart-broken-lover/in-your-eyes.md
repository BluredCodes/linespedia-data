---
title: "In your eyes"
writer: "Heart Broken Lover"
slug: "in-your-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30014-In-your-eyes-by-Heart-Broken-Lover"

God you're beautful,
I love the color of your eyes.
Its such a great way,
to conceal your evil inside.

Im not fooled by you
