writer: "Heart Broken Lover"

Im so quick to listen to you
I forget to make my feelings known
and ill take anyones advice
Anyones but my own

Ill cry on your shoulder
and take comfort in your arms
But who am i going to count on
when all of you are gone?

Im so good with all of your probelms
it puts me in a state of disbelief
How could i make all of yours go away?
And i can never get mine to leave

My answers lie within me
but there just so hard to find
So ill take anyone and everyones advice
Anyones but mine.

I need to help myself
though i dont know how i feel
The answer is somewhere inside of me
and is yet to be revealed

Im the epitamy of confusion
Im the person lost inside
Im the person whos unaware of there surroundings
Im the person with no pride

But soon my day will come
Of this i have no doubt
And then ill be able to tell you
what this is all about

My answers lie within me
but there just so hard to find
So ill take anyone and everyones advice
Anyones but mine.
