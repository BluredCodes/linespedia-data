title: "Sorry, Marcel"
writer: "SubwayBoi"

Sometimes he's so dramatic that
It makes you want to scream.
He laughs about my secret crush,
And spills all my ice cream.
He talks about my sister like 
She's something of a god.
But if he knew the things I did
He'd see through her facade.
He never seems to work too hard,
His work ethics are lax.
When he sees that I'll work with him,
He thinks of it as tax.
He's gossipy and talks too much,
With something new to say.
It takes him fifteen minutes 
Just to put a coat away!
He thinks I have a crush on him,
Which simply isn't true.
I told him that to scare him off,
And cause a fright or two.
And though I lost my temper,
At him the other day,
I want to say I'm sorry,
And there's one more thing to say.
I hope that we can get along,
Like the cat within the hat.
'Cause I can't go on bitching, 
I don't have time for that!Meh.
