the old woman sits alone on the bench,
 feeding the birds with crumbs
 she's tired, she's sore, and sleepy,
 she's spoken to by a park bum

 "what happened to the world?" the bum asks.
 "where did things go so bad?" 
 the bum walked away to go find some food
 and the old woman became quite sad

 she thought about things she'd wanted to do
 when she was younger and healthy
 she never thought her back would give out,
 her eyesight would go, and she'd get a bad knee

 she thought about places she'd wanted to go
 and people she'd wanted to meet
 the beautiful dresses she'd some day be buying
 the golden shoes on her feet

 she thought of her love life, how little it was,
 the man she had always wanted to marry
 she thought of how she let him walk away
 for that, she had always been sorry

 she thought of the kids that she would never have
 it was too late for that now
 she wished that she could start things again
 she wished that were possible somehow

 she stopped feeding the birds as they pecked at her crumbs
 she got up and walked away
 the news of her suicide was all over town
 in the local paper the next day
