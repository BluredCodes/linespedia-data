you can't see the twinkle in my eyes,
 the magic's all but gone.
 the sun is setting in the distance,
 along with all the fun.
 the sparkle in the sky has fled,
 it's wonder left with mine.
 and here we sit, and here we wait,
 for the monotony of christmas time.short and sweet folks. christmas magic? ha!
