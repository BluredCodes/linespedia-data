don't let them see you
 when there's something to you
 keep it hidden
 empty looks good this season 
 hide your thoughts
 your feelings
 your wants and needs
 bury it all
 and put up that front that you wear so well
 cold, cynical, bitter,
 unemotional, objective, calm
 how do you do it so well? 
 and don't let anyone inside
 no matter how much you want to collapse
 cave in
 give in 
 rest
 but you can't 
 keep fighting your feelings
 keep your shields up
 and don't let me in
 it's like a damn
 once it cracks, it breaks
 and can't be rebuilt the same
 so keep it up
 and keep me out
 
 I'll break you yet.The title says it all, really.
