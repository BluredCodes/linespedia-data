---
title: "I'm not certain"
writer: "SubwayBoi"

We met as anyone else might,
 Two people at a bar.
 I looked into your dark eyes and saw something.
 It was there for a moment, and vanished.
 What was it? 
 I'm not certain.
 
 We walked to your place as anyone else might,
 Two strangers on the sidewalk.
 We went to your appartment and talked about things.
 About what?
 I'm not certain.

 We slept in the same bed as anyone else might,
 Two boys, never touching.
 I looked at your sleeping body and realized something.
 What? 
 I'm in love with you.
 Why?
 I'm not certain.
