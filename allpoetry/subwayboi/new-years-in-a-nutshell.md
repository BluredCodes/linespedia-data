The location is unknown,
 The excitement goes unshown,
 And your money will be blown,
 And you love it.
 Well the ticket's twenty-eight,
 And the people ain't so great,
 You can tell them when it's late,
 To go shove it.
 So get all your makeup done,
 And go out and have some fun,
 It'll be day number one,
 Before you know it.
 You'll have drinks so don't be blue
 You'll make resolutions, too
 It's so cold you'll get the flu,
 Your nose, you'll blow it.
 As the ball drops on Times Square,
 And your crazy 'bout your hair,
 You'll recall Y2K scares,
 And it's over.
 Have a happy time tonight,
 And be free of doubt and fright, 
 And tomorrow try to fight,
 Your hangover.
