home (hm)
n. 
A place where one lives; a residence. 
The physical structure within which one lives, such as a house or apartment. 

 Four walls and a roof,
 That shelter you in.
 Your personal crap,
 And pictures of kin. 
 Your clothes lay about, 
 Your bed is unmade.
 And all of your books,
 Sit there in the shade.
 You smile on entry,
 And don't wonder why.
 A place just like this,
 You could not deny. 
 You've found just the place,
 And don't have to roam.
 The place that you love,
 The place you call home. 
 
 But that's the ideal,
 And it's hard to find.
 I just want a space
 I can say is all mine. 
 For now here I'll wait,
 Within these four walls.
 And wait for the time
 When my name fate calls.
 A place of my own,
 That God's grace will bless,
 But until that day,
 I feel so,
 Homeless.Meh.
