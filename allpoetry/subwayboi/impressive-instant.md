title: "impressive instant"
writer: "SubwayBoi"

Universe is fixed full of stars,
Nothing out there looks the same.
You're the one that i've been waiting for,
And I don't even know your name.

Never seen your face before this instant,
Looking at me 'cross the room.
Look into your eyes, they're like a rocket,
Blasting off and going 'boom'.

Dancing like an angel gliding on ice,
Flowing about here and there.
Moving oh-so-freely as you glide around me,
Dancing about without care.

Simply watching you makes me very happy,
Seeing you look at me is so fun.
Then I turn away for a single moment,
And then just like that you were gone.Yeah, I know some of this is ripped off. So sue me.
