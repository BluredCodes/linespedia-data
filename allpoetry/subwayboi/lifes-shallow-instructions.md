Get your nails done to go to the hairdresser,
 Get your hair cut to go to the tanning salon,
 Get your tan crispy brown to get your legs waxed,
 Get your legs waxed to go get your makeup done,
 Get your makeup done before you get your nails done.

 Repeat. 

 Repeat. 

 Repeat. 

 Funeral.This is for every fuck who won't leave the house without spending an hour getting ready. Get over yourself!
