Held back from the promised land,
One bright boy is here at hand.
His preference mistook by me,
Pleading for a lover's fee. 
His fate sealed with one sweet kiss,
Nothing more and nothing less. 
Everyone can see, but he.
Lord, what fools these straight boys be.This is about a guy I like, who claims to be 'straight'. (Needless to say, Im gay). And yes, I know that I ripped off Puck from A Midsummer Night's Dream, so don't bother telling me. Adieu.
