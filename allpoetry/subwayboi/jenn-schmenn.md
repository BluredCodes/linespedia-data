Hair blowing in the wind, 
 Ice cream dripping from your chin. 
 Eyes shining bright like stars,
 Giving head to boys in cars. 
 Typing often, not too well,
 Always saying "lol".
 To a rave we both shall go,
 I'll do drugs, you'll say "nono!"
 Your boyfriend wants me to die,
 And you're busy eating pie.
 Now I'll go to have a nap,
 And you can't call me Luap.
