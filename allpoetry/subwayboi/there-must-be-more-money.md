title: "There Must Be More Money"

There must be more money, there must be more money. Look under couches, on the sidewalks, behind your ear. Maybe you can find that quarter that Uncle Bob is always able to produce. There must be more money, there must be more money.  Return those milk jugs and beer bottles, sell off your CDs and appliances. Take those wine bottles your dad never uses and sell them to your classmates. There must be more money, there must be more money.  Steal what you can, beg if you must. Do housework until your arms are going to fall off. Do whatever the hell you need to, you have to get that money. What will you do when you get it?
