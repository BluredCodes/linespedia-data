title: "Your Feet Are Touching Me!!!"

Your feet are touching me, 
 They're making me sick!
 Those icky skanky mouldy things,
 They make me want to spit!

 Your belly button's full of lint,
 It's nasty and insane.
 It makes me want to lose my lunch,
 And put bullets to my brain.
 
 Your nose is full of icky snot,
 It's always falling out.
 It's like some big revolving door,
 It makes me scream and shout.
 
 And finally there's one more thing,
 That gives me frights and fears.
 It's all that nasty yellow wax, 
 That's dripping from your ears!
