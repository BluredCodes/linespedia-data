Everyone is entitled to their own opinion.

 You have your opinion, and I have mine.
 And they're both a-okay.
 Because we're liberal,
 And tolerant,
 And open-minded,
 And such.

 The sky is orange.
 
 Well, that's MY opinion, at least. 
 And my opinion counts for something.
 Because we're liberal,
 And tolerant,
 And open-minded,
 And such.

 We should live on Venus.

 Why not? It's my opinion, 
 And it's completely vaild.
 Because we're liberal,
 And tolerant,
 And open-minded,
 And such.

 Mirrors will stop reflecting in 2034.

 Well, they will. You'll see.
 It's not like you can disprove me.
 Because we're liberal,
 And tolerant,
 And open-minded,
 And such.
 
 With these new tolerant ideals, soon everyone will know everything!I am so tired of hearing that everyone's opinion counts. It doesn't always!
