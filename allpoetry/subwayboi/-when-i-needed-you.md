everyone thought I was crazy
 including you
 and now you've proven me right
 but it's too late to matter
 
 where were you when I needed you?

 i felt like a fucking idiot
 for months
 embarassed when I saw you
 ashamed, but transfixed
 you carried on however you wanted
 with no one knowing what I knew
 and no one wanting to hear what I had to say

 and it's all true
 everyone knows that now
 and no one cares
 it's as though everyone expected me to suffer
 as though my suffering added to your strife
 
 where were you when I needed you?

 you disregard me now
 another stepping stone on the mountain 
 you never had to climb
 just toss me aside when I've fulfilled my purpose
 i'm not real to you
 
 you're one of the few people I want to hurt

 where were you when I needed you?

 now I know
 the worst feeling in the world

 knowing the truth
 and people either don't believe you
 or don't careThis is about a 'straight' guy I was in love with and had sex with, and then he ditched me. NOW I find out he's bisexual. Go fucking figure.
