the anticipation of waiting
has made everyone attractive.
standards weaken with my knees,
and I can smell everyone.
I see you in a sea of people,
you have no face,
and it's better that way.
your facelessness is irrelevant
you could be anyone
you could be everyone
but you are no one.
your smell and touch
and taste and sight
are so typical,
but so beautiful.
you are a tool for my pleasures
and I for yours
the manipulation is mutual
and so wonderful.This is how I feel when I get really aroused.
