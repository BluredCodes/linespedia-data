(please read from the bottom up)

E
C
A
F
But all she could see was her mother's
She tried to focus
Everything was blurry
A little girl
Finally the baby came out
Push! Push!
But the father urged her to continue
The mother was low on energy
Push! Push!
But they needed it out
The doctors would fix it
And upside down
It came out slowly
Push! Push!
And she did
The doctor urged her to continue
The screaming came from without and within
H
S
U
