Bianca is a girl I know,
 Who pisses me right off. 
 She dresses well and wears the clothes
 From Roots, that she has bought. 
 She winks and me when she gets time,
 She's always such a flirt.
 She points it out when I do wear
 My 'comic book shirt'.
 There isn't much to say of her
 She's quite a simple girl.
 She smart, funny, and oh-so-nice,
 She'll go far in this world.
 This poem is for you, my friend,
 A present for New Years.
 Now please forgive me, now I leave,
 For I must have a beer.Don't ask.
