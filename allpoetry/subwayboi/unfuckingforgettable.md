unforgettable
 that's what you are
 unforgettable
 you're not too far

 like a stain of blood that clings to me
 how you want to do mean things to me

 never before
 has someone hurt more

 unforgettable
 in every way
 and behind some bars
 I hope you'll stay

 that's why darling
 it's incredible 
 that someone so
 unforgettable
 things that I am
 unforgettable too
 
 no never before
 has someone hurt more

 like a stalker, that keeps haunting me
 you're in my dreams, always flaunting me
 
 that's why darling
 it's incredible
 you're behind bars
 unforgettable
 but still you think I'm
 unforgettable tooI tried to take the song "unforgettable" and make it about a rapist that still longs for his victum. Meh.
