they say that nothing's perfect
 and I suppose that's true
 for if this world were perfect
 there'd be no such thing as you
 it's hard to put in words just now
 how much I hate you so
 it's easier to sit way back
 and watch your cruel words flow
 your disregard is overwhelming 
 you'd backstab in a minute
 you'd step on those you claim to love
 for one impressive instant
 i've always had an open mind
 and welcomed everyone
 but it's so hard to just accept
 how you get kicks and fun
 i hope i never see your face
 its presence makes me ill
 what makes it worse is there's no way
 to crush your iron will
 you're heartless and you just don't care
 and others just don't see
 I'll stand right back, therefore, and hide,
 and hope you don't target me
