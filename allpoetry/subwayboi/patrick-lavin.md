What a little stoner, 
 That boi who's dressed in blue.
 He's such a fucking moron,
 And his breath is smelly, too.
 
 His name is Mr. Lavin,
 His eyes are brights and odd.
 He claims that he's a stoner,
 But that's just a fascade.

 His ears have way too many holes,
 He sleeps with little girls. 
 He's wearing kandy bracelets,
 They're made around the world.
 
 He thinks that he's a raver,
 But we all know his lies.
 He thinks he likes the ladies,
 In truth he sleeps with guys.

 And so I write this poem,
 For Pat, who ain't a Jew.
 Because he knows I love him,
 And I know he looooves me too.
