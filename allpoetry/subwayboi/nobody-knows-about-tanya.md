title: "nobody knows about tanya"
writer: "SubwayBoi"

nobody knows about tanya
 her bright eyes shining out
 nobody knows how fun it is
 to make her scream and shout
 nobody knows of her smile
 the brightness in her face
 nobody knows how frail she is
 when she's not in her place
 nobody knows about her bed
 the softness of her sheets
 nobody knows i go near her
 and listen to her sleep
 nobody knows how close we are
 no one, not even her
 nobody knows i watch her walk
 her safety i assure
 nobody knows about her change
 her nickles and her quarters
 nobody knows about my gift
 from her, a restraining order
 nobody knows about the time
 we went out for a drive
 and nobody knows just where she is
 or if she'll be found alive
