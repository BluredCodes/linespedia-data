You said you wanted more from me,
 You said you couldn't wait to be,
 Someone special in my life.
 Oh, but wait, I'm not your wife.
 I'm a guy; a fop; a fag. 
 Not just some two-dollar hag.
 Let's cuddle up, and then make out.
 Just so I can hear you spout,
 "Oh, mister, did you hear?
 "Turns out I'm not fucking queer. 
 "You were an experiment, 
 "To see if I like gals or gents.
 "And though I think we'll be great pals,
 "The answer, my good friend, is gals."
 I should have known how this would be,
 They always test things out on me.
 So here I sit, and here I'll wait,
 To find some boy who wants to DATE.This is (obviously) written for a guy that I very recently helped to denounce his homoerotic urges. The catch was, I actually liked this guy, and I figured he'd like me back. Nice, eh?
