the sadness inside her consumed her  she barely ate never went out cried often and alone she loved death she tried to seek out the reason for these feelings she felt she was close but always far off 'Your problem,' he said, 'is caused by a series of 'chemical imbalances in your brain, 'probably a birth defect. 'Here, take one of these every day, 'And come back in two months.' novo fluoxetine, 20 mg two months passed the sky cleared and the birds sang, all became rosy,  and it wasn't false, either she felt happy she went out frequently now she gained weight she loved life  she no longer looked for answers she had them all and she thought back to her darker times and wondered who was that girl? why did she think the things she did? and how did I change so fast?If your moods, your emotions, your feelings can be changed so quickly because of a tiny pill you take every day, how real were the feelings you felt in the first place?
