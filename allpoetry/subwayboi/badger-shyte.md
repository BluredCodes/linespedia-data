I don't care about the badger assassins,
 In fact I wish them death.
 I'm simply bored so here I go,
 I'll enter this contest.
 I'm writing here a poem that  
 I'm sure Antmonkey will hate.
 But that's okay, because one day,
 We'll go out on a date.
 I'm told to say here, 'Bandersnatch',
 And why? I just don't know.
 I'm gonna be a badger - woo!
 I'll frolic in the snow. 
 So here you go, you badger whores,
 Let's all go smoke some grass.
 And by the way, just so you know,
 Antmonkey likes it in the ass!Don't ask.
