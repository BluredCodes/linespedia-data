title: "ecstasy"
writer: "SubwayBoi"

multicolour lights
 and raging beats
 bombard your senses
 
 people around you
 crowded around you
 smiling and happy
 
 a single pill 
 has changed who you are
 for the better

 and you're convinced 
 the happiness isn't fake
 and could last forever

 the simple act of
 going to the bathroom
 brings you pleasure

 everyone has something to say
 even if it's
 absolutely nothing

 you dance, and sing,
 and talk, and love,
 for hours
 
 then it goes away
 and you're back in reality
 which seems horrible by comparison
