ackward silence starts the night
 loud music blasts your ears
 you check your coat and look around
 and order your first beer
 if beer you hate, there's other drinks
 whatever suits your taste
 there's snacks to eat and room to dance
 so drink on up now, post haste
 the night goes on till half-past one
 and tensions melt away
 then everyone seeks out a mate
 be they straight, or bi, or gay
 for those that god has chosen and
 has blessed with pretty faces
 they're taken home by other drunks
 to their respective places
 for those that god has tossed aside
 and left them there to rot
 they'll go home alone, but come back soon
 like some bar fly robotI don't know where I was going with this, hence why it's short. Enjoy, or die.
