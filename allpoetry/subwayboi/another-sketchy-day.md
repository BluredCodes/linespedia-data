My brain is like a skatterbox,
 With no rational thought.
 I tiptoed in at half-past six,
 And luckily, wasn't caught.

 The pills I bought and ate last night,
 Erasing space and time.
 I have to say, I'm quite impressed,
 That I know how to rhyme!

 It's hard to put to words just now,
 What's going on inside.
 While half of me still wants to dance,
 The other wants to hide.
 
 So now I'll drink muh orange juice,
 To get rid of these aches.
 And then I'll go and rest my head,
 And try not to look so baked!
