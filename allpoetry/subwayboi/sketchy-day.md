title: "sketchy day"

today is such a sketchy day
 with sketchy clouds in the sky
 and sketchy faces on sketchy people
 as they sketchily walk on by

 sometimes my sketchy eyes will blur
 without my own permission
 sometimes i think that i'm a holy man
 and sent here on a mission

 sometimes i sketch and listen to
 my friends who say so little
 i always shoot my own mouth off
 and try to squash a skittle

 this sketchy world of sketchy friends
 this sketchy circle that never ends
 this sketchy mind with which I live
 the sketchy trip we both can give

 are nothing

 compared to what I could be doing.
