there's nothing left to say 
 and nothing left to do
 and every thought you have
 someone else had it first
 you didn't reinvent sex
 you didn't invent anything
 why talk about love?
 why talk about hate?
 why talk about anything, any more?
 there's nothing left to say
 you can't even put an emotional spin on it
 because it's all been done
 and no one wants to hear it any more
 september eleventh
 'we're going to move on with our lives'
 say it a thousand times
 and it still isn't true
 traditions weigh people down
 monotony creeps in slowly
 magic dissapears
 what's left?
 do nothing?
 say nothing?
 be nothing?
 that's been done too
 to death
 there's nothing left to sayIs there anything left to talk about that you haven't heard 1000 times?
