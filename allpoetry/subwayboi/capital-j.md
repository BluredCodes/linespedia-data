When people come to you in tears,
 You toss them all aside.
 When there is something to be done, 
 You grab your coat and hide.
 When someone seems the least secure,
 You point out all their flaws.
 When others go for tea and cake,
 You're found in local bars.
 Though some prefer the active life,
 You're much more of a sitter.
 When people try to help you out,
 You'd rather be a quitter.
 You're always the first one to say,
 When you see someone's stout.
 When people say they want to die,
 You tell them not to pout.
 You know it's true, you've heard them all,
 You can't deny the facts.
 Though you've had years to go improve,
 Your social skills are lax. 
 You can't deny it any more, 
 Accept all that you're worth.
 You act like this and it's because,
 You're nothing but a jerk.

 With a capital J.

 So it sounds like this: JEEEEEEEERRKKK!!To Zeke.

(That's not to say that this is ABOUT Zeke).
