Drive up to me in a limo,
 And get my attention fast.
 Just give me twenty-five for head,
 Or fifty for my ass.
 Please don't ask any questions,
 As I sit upon your face.
 Just do your god damn business,
 And just get off my case.
 Then let me back out of your car,
 To find another John.
 'Cause I've got kids I need to feed,
 And pills to buy my mom.
 It's awfully cold just standing here,
 In high heals and a skirt.
 But here's the only way I know,
 To give my back a shirt.
 Another car just pulled right up,
 It's white and sort of blue.
 It's two am and I'll sleep soon,
 And count my money, too.
 A man asks me just what I'll do,
 If he give me a grand.
 I think about it, and then tell him,
 And then he cuffs my hand.
 I'm in jail now, at least it's warm,
 The people here are skids.
 I just can't help but think and fret,
 About my mom and kids.Yeah yeah yeah...
