title: "nineteen at last"

in only a few hours,
 i will enter another world.
 the world of adults,
 of nightclubs,
 of bars, 
 of strip clubs,
 of legal cigarette smokers,
 and legal drinkers.
 
 i remember waiting anxiously for tomorrow,
 counting the days, hours, and minutes remaining,
 yearning to go out
 where everyone else seems to go
 with such ease.

 we all grow older
 the alternative is death,
 so I'll take wrinkles and false teeth any day.

 birthdays are like beers:
 the more you have, 
 the better you look

 in only a few hours,
 i will enter another world.I turn 19 tomorrow woo hooooo!
