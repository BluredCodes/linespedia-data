Chocolate skin, 
 Sliding up against me. 
 Smoothness unparalleled in nature,
 Wrap my arms around it.
 I can't believe how smooth you are,
 Untouched by labour or time.
 I feel so fortunate to touch you,
 And I wish that I had six hands.
 If I could bent time and space and nature,
 I'd find myself on all of you.
 Above you.
 In you.
 No amount of time would change
 The beauty of this moment.
 Sitting here,
 Surrounded by,
 Your chocolate skin.Black guys are hot :-)
