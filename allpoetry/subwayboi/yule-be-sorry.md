---
title: "Yule Be Sorry"
writer: "SubwayBoi"
slug: "yule-be-sorry"

Christmas carols,
 Blasting in my ears.
 Roasting chestnuts,
 Least of all my cares.
 Santa Clauses,
 Marching down the street.
 Winter snowflakes,
 Freezing up my feet.
 Children singing,
 Will they ever stop?
 Blinking lights that
 Never touch my heart. 
 Malls are packed with
 People buying shit.
 I just can't tell
 What to make of it.
 Why bother with 
 Such a lame past time?
 Giving, giving, 
 When will I get mine?
 Gifts are given,
 Often without thought.
 People won't get
 What they really want.
 So I beg you,
 Listen to me please. 
 This past time is
 Nothing more than cheese. 
 Give up all your,
 Christmas sentiment.
 Go and find your
 Own good merriment. 
 Now we end this,
 Holiday story.
 Change your ways or,
 Yule be sorry.
