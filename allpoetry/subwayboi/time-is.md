it flows over your body and caresses in your soul
 it surrounds you
 moving consistantly and taking you with it
 by your volition, or no

 it can't be slowed or halted
 except within your mind
 it can't move back or forward,
 it is both cruel and kind

 a clock we use to measure it
 and run our lives by that
 but such crude understandings
 are all we hope to grasp

 it is
 unrelenting
 unapologetic
 unstoppable
 time
