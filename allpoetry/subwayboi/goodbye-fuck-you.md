Goodbye high school,
 Thanks for nothing.
 I can honestly say that,
 I learned nothing inside the class,
 And lots out in the halls,
 And writings on the stalls.
 
 Goodbye high school,
 Thanks for killing five years,
 Thanks for preventing me from working,
 Thanks for causing stress and tears. 
 
 Goodbye high school, 
 I can't believe people say
 That high school was,
 'The best time of my life'.
 I'd rather not die at 19.

 Goodbye high school, 
 I won't miss a thing here,
 Except for the people
 That you've managed to trap.

 Goodbye. Fuck you.Don't assssssk.
