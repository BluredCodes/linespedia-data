title: "my mother's funeral"

one cold day in october 
 she got up and left
 and I thought we were so close

 where the heck did she go to?
 i wish I could say
 but no one seems to know where

 at first my heart ached for her
 but now I don't care
 there's nothing to care about

 before she left our family
 she wasn't around
 always on the net, chatting

 met some man from there, I guess
 ran away with him next
 off to nobody-knows-where

 i'll never seen her again
 that's alright, I guess
 i'll never know when she dies 
 
 and so, that cold fall morning
 with leaves falling down
 was my mother's funeralmy mother left in october of 2000 and I haven't heard from her since.
