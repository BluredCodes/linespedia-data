I thought I could move on,even after all the good times we had,I thought i could move on, though it would make me really sad.I told you that I loved you,I wanted you to see.But maybe it is time that i face reality.That you will never feel the same way that I doand me as a friend is what I’ll always be to you.At first that thought made me cry,I felt like i could roll over and die.Cause without your love comforting me at night,I dont know how my life could be rightAnd somehow I need to forget you,could I find some body new?But after seeing you last night,I know what i have to do.Cause after being with you last night,you’ve truely made me see,that i love you more than anything,and you were meant to be with me.I have to keep on trying,I can’t give up the fight.I have to find a way to show you,I am Mr. Right.But untill that time comes,I want you to know this, hun.That I will always love you,your forever my #1.
