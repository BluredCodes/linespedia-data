I?ve never been so happy,
please stay tonight.
I?ll hold you in my arms, 
this feeling feels so right.
I?ve waited such a long time for you to finally say,
?I love you so much baby,
I never want to go away.?
And i always knew that one day,
you?d feel the way i do.
I always knew that one day you?d say ?yes i love you to.?
And now that you?ve said it,
I feel like i could cry.
Not because I?m sad,
but casue I?ve never felt so high.
With you in my arms,
what more could i want in life?
And now you say that some day,
you?d like to be my wife.
For since the moment i first met you,
I knew right from the start.
That we?d always be together,
till death do us part.
I can?t believe that you are mine,
these words are finally true.
And just to see you smile once,
theres nothing i wouldnt do.
But at last my alarm clock rings,
as real as this may seem.
Just another night alone,
just another dream.sometime in the past,
