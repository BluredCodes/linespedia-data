title: "Still Miss You"
writer: "Btd"

Life just seems so empty without you,and at night I pray I'll dream about you.Cause then I could hold you in my arms,be swept away by your sweet charms,Just like the times when we were best friends,but sad is how this story ends.Cause you saw you didn't need me anymore,but I was not prepared for what was in store.Pain, grief, and much sorrow,I'd goto bed knowing I wouldn't see you tomorrow.Everytime you needed someone, i was there for you,No matter what I'd drop whatever it was I had to do.I would do it without expecting anything back,Except maybe just be there when it was me about to crack.But despite the way you treated me,You're still the only one I see.No other girl could bring me such strife,Cause you're the only thing I want out of life.SO please just take me back and say,'I love you, everything will be ok.'Cause then I'll know you feel the same,only this time, please, don't make it a game.
