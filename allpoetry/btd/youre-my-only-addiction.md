I?m in a real bad condition,
I told my self I?d always be stronger.
But lately I?m not sure if i can go on much longer.
Cause ive been through enough,
enough of this pain.
Enough of your lies, 
I wont play your game.
You said you?d always care for me...
Yea right, so easy to say,
not easy to do, so easy to lie,
so hard to follow through.
My mind is so strong, 
I?ve gave up drinking and drugs,
and i have for so long.
But you I cant give up,
And I?m in a state of affliction.
I?ve tried so hard but came to see,
Stef, your my only addiction. 
So now im gonna go,
breakdown on the floor,
casue this pen, 
It cant write lonliness no more.
