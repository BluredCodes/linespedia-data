For all the things you've done without,
For every dream, that slipped away,
The Angels' give a glorious shout
On this , Your Mother's Day......

You see, they know, you hold the key
To every heart and soul within,
The existence of humanity,
Without You, would surely end.....

It's with God's love you've given birth
A long, forgotten pain,
The breath of life, upon this earth
Forever will remain......

This heartfelt thanks goes out to You,
Though words could never say,
Just how we cherish, what you've done,
On this, Your Mother's Day.....

Dedicated to my mother..who passed away four years ago.
