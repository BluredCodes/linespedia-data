As she stands in frontof the mirrorAnd puts her make-up onThe reflection showsthe reality,Of youthfulness, long goneThis is how she starts her dayJust like every otherShe wanders throughher empty life'Til she can hide'neath the covers...She waits for darknessto descendSo she can wash away the facadeNaked now, she feelsthe truthShe sees now, why he's gone...
