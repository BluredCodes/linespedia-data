Out of the DarknessWe are bathed in the white light of loveOur protection from the dark,You can feel it coursingthrough your veins,As it goes, it leaves it's markThe pureness of eternity,is all that we will needAs our soul travels through this life, On it's journey to be freeThe pain of life, we must endure,Tho, it will not lastThe future holds for us, the truthand, Out of the Darkness we'll be cast
