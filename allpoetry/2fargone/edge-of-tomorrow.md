---
title: "Edge of Tomorrow"
writer: "2farGone"

Misty eyes darkened
from tears having cried
A heart being broken, 
crumbles inside

The feeling of loneliness
in the quiet of night
fitfully waiting, 
the suns' morning light

Endless poised on
the edge of tomorrow
the day that will bring,
an end to the sorrow......
