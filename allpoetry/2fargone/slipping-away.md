Slipping Away 


Engulfed in the darkness
of another lonely night, 
she clings to her pillow,
and closes her eyes...

Surrounded by silence
there's no one to hear,
as she cries out in anguish,
cries out in fear...

Imprisoned in her nightmare,
she struggles to awake
only to find, as she 
trembles and shakes...

That she's falling, falling
ever so slow, and as she
reaches, she misses and 
looses control...

Now all that remains
is and empty embrace,
as she gathers the 
bedsheets, and covers
her face...

She feels herself slipping,
slipping away....
