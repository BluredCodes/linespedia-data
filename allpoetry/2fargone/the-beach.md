---
title: "The Beach..."
writer: "2farGone"

Strolling in the pale moonlight
two lovers, hand in hand
walking along the beach, making
footprints in the sand...

Talking about their hopes and dreams,
planning their course in life,
he got down on his knees, and asked
would she be his wife?...

Gladly she accepted, proud to 
wear his ring, promising all the 
happiness, that their love 
would bring...

Suddenly, in slow motion, like
a moment lost in time, he stepped
out of the darkness, weilding
his shiney knife...

He cut into the heart of 
the only love she'd ever known,
ripping away the dreams they had
and the future they would own...

Off in the night he ran, not
stopping to think twice, about
the horror that he caused when 
he took away a life...

She knelt down beside him,
in her arms she held his head,
she begged him not to leave
but, she knew that he was dead...

As the waves came crashing in on her
she tried to understand, she looked
up to the Heavens and asked the Lord
to take her hand...

And, the Lord, He did hold her
softly to His breast, and when He spoke,
He told her that her first
true love had to rest...

Now, she looks back upon that time
and she knows He had a plan, you see
He sent her someone else to love....
and he's a very special man...
