The Wish


I wished upon 
a star tonight
as it was falling
from the sky

A wish for life
to treat you well
as you now travel
by yourself

A wish that you
will someday find
that special love
and peace of mind

A wish that you 
will always see
the best in 
everyone you meet

A wish one day
you'll understand
just how this feels
and, how proud I am......

To be your Mother......
