title: "The Answer To His Dreams..."

He told her that she was the only
answer to his dreams,
She thought their love would always be
an everlasting thing...

They made a life together as a husband
and a wife,
They had a son who had become the 
apple of his eye...

Then one day it happened, and she
knew his love was gone,
She saw the way he looked at her 
and she felt so all alone...

The wanting in his eyes, he didn't even 
try to hide,
She heard his voice, when he spoke her name,
and she knew their love had died...

As she gathered up her baby boy, with 
her suitcase in her hand,
She tried to leave the pain behind
She tried to understand...

How her life had gotten
so full of agony...
How he had forgotten she was
The answer to his dreams....
