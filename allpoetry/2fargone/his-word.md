---
title: "His Word"
writer: "2farGone"

So precious is His Word to me 
that I must now, arise and speak
Of the horrors' that I see each day
as one more lamb is led astray....

When preachers' beg, for your last dime,
so they can live a life so fine,
The promises' that we made to Him,
are fading now, as His light grows dim..

Our children run away from home
His Word, to them, was never known,
So, on the streets, they wander free
with drugs the only hope they see,
And, babies tossed aside, from birth
for life to some, it has no worth...

How do we turn our face toward Him
and cast away, all of our sin?
You have to open up your heart,
He will know just when you start,

Ask,and you will surely see
the Grace of God, for eternity...
