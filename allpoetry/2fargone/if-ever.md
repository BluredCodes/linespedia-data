If ever you caused a smiling face
to turn into a frown, 
or said an unkind word to move
your pompous weight around...

If ever you've put a lie in motion
just to even up the score,
or preyed upon a meager soul
to prove that you've got more...

Then understand the meaning of 
the words that I write down,
everything that you dole out will
find it's way back around...

So, make your choices wisely, 
with compassion and concern,
So that when you stand 
before the Lord, you get what
you deserve...
