---
title: "Forever and Ever...."
writer: "2farGone"
slug: "forever-and-ever"
source: "AllPoetry"

I feel the fire
burning deep...
Am I awake or
am I asleep?..

I feel your touch
so soft at first,
Your lips find mine
to quench a thirst..

Arms that draw
me closer in,
Ignite the flame
that will begin..

As passions rise 
and leave us weak,
breathless as 
our bodies meet..

This is the pledge
we made together,
The vows we swore
would last forever, and ever....
