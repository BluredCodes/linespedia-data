---
title: "Games..."
writer: "2farGone"
slug: "games"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9281-Games...-by-2farGone"
---

I've wandered thru this life
taking chances now and then,

I know too well the feeling
sometimes you loose,
sometimes you win...

The winners seem to be, 
to few and far between,
the losers are gaining 
ground, I guess it's just
my destiny...

All I wanted was a love
a love that would be true,
but, you took my heart
before I realized, it was 
just a game to you...

Now, you say your sorry
but, I say it's too late
I gave you everything I had
you just threw it all away...

And, I know too well the feeling
sometimes..you loose,
sometimes..you win,
I know too well the feeling that
I can start all over again...
