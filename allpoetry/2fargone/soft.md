Soft....


Soft...as a whisper
your heart beckons mine,
Soft...as the new fallen
snow on the pine

Soft...as your breath
when it touches my skin,
Soft...as our souls
intertwine from within

Soft...as the music that 
plays its' sweet tune,
Soft...like the shadows
that dance 'round the moon

That's how our love feels
after all this time,
Soft...like the delicate
nectar of wine.......
