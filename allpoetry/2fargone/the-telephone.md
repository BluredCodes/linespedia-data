title: "The Telephone..."

It's the middle of the night
as she slips out of bed, she
turns on the light, as she, shakes
the sleep from her head..

The ringing of the telephone, has
awakened her again,
She hears him crying on line, ' I'm 
sorry for what I said'....

He'd told her it was over, that 
he'd found someone else, then 
he walked out and left her alone,
all by herself...

Now, she's done to him
what he did to her,
And he can't take it when 
it turns around, he can't
take the hurt...

Silently, she wipes a tear
as she tells him goodbye,
He's been gone about a year,
still she wonders why...

In the middle of the night
the telephone will ring,
She turns on the light and
turns off one more dream....
