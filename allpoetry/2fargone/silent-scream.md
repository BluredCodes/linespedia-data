I can see the eyesthat stare at me I can see them, but can they see me?I hear that soundlike glass shatteringto the floor, but waitOh God, I've heard that sound beforeA silent scream from deep insideA ttorturedsoula thousand tears criedThe reflection in the mirrow fadesA broken spiritflies away
