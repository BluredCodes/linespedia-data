In the heat of the moment
when the promises' are made,
you don't stop to think about
a heart that might break...

You whisper sweet words
yeah, you know what to say,
you've been here before
with the games that you play

In the heat of the moment
as the passion does rise,
you're filling the room up 
with all of your lies...

When the nighttime does fade
and the morning light comes,
will you even care, about the 
damage you've done?...

In the heat of the moment
better watch what you do,
'cause the next time it happens,
it could happen to you...
