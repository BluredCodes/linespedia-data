Gifts

I want to know more than the meaning of my gifts.
I want to know why I offer them.
Knowing they will be loved, appreciated or acknowledged;
Fearing they will be used, abused or neglected;
Is it me that is in need, or they?
Perhaps, and always, everyone.

Gifts

Freely given
Freely received
Ever owned
tho' shared and released
To be fruitful and multiply
To continue to give
