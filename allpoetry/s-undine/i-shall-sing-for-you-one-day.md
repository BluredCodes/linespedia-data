of strumming seas in chords of waves...


i'll write a song for thee one day
while resting in her pleasures

a slow and gentle sail away
of dreamseas shining treasures

a soothing, softly quiet lay
near close to zephyr's whisper

and it will swell and fill thy sails
a'billowing; a whistler

'breathes scented spice, exotic lands
pristine, untouched, uncharted

and there may you debark to find
thy self, thy soul, thy heartbed

a kismet's haunting lore; a 'frain
gifts mem'ry smoked in ages

replete with living loves dear bought
with staining loss, with praises

her siren's sweet, melodic prayer
a chanting burnt in keepings

rock thee away, away as tides
sketch sands unending shiftings

rolls in, rolls out, rolls in, rolls out...
a sky of licked blue crystal...;

a deep'ning oceans troubadour
sounds back from heav'ns' missal

eternal hymns in off'ring now
to daystar's kiss such blessings

in rev'rence, holy wonder, awe
his harp strung taut in echoings 

as falling free, a songbird 'lites
and wee hours fills with longing

i'll sing a song for thee one day 
and there thee'll be,
where thee'd be, 
sailing sea

rocking

* * *
dedicated to he who would be...
my counselor
my guardian
my wizard
my date
and
my dearest heartfriend


s
