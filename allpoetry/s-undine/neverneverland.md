if we could have but just one hour 
to feel love's touch, to play, to flow'r, 
there would this child find peace in now. oh, how my yearning heart would bow 

in humble gratefulness, sublime, 
what joy would steal this hurting time
from every pore now wracked with pain
in thinking, "Why must love remain
lost; seeking, lonely,
all askew..." 

this can't be right! 

God! help me 
view this time with patience, 
asking ne'er what can not be... for now; for there. 

for... 
if we are not to touch or play
before i leave this plane for clay 

then, 
keep this thought where
'nothing's old'... 


You were the song that filled my soul. 
ever
