sky-blue pink: an imaginary colour
not anymore... 

we've seen it
we know 

evil skied its simulacrum
scarred chryst' sky-blue
regions near 

stained with scarlet
atmosphere
evil, sky-clad
left us
here 

evil, skyjack 'guised
unholy 

shrieking voided space
their shroud 

saffa:
try to see
shufti:
a look or glimpse
shudder
scream
shush 

... ashes ashes we all fall 


shut 

the Sibylline tomes
blood-inked
rewritten 

evil
rend us
infidel
render me
like you
insatiate
evil 

that i may 

terrified
render
unto 

angel 

evil
echoes
godless torse 

kyrie eleison 

amen

--s 

At the round earth's imagined corners
blow your trumpets angels
and arise
arise from death
ye numberless infinities
of god 

and to your scattered bodies go... 

--Donne

amen

monhowever, whenever, and whichever form of prayer it is we may come to at whiles, please pray for those who remain -- us.
