... and there i splay, wide open 
to daystar's rays breathing heat into this mount's bare, ancient face... lost to the gods; far removed, all thought of man... my back sucking warmth from granite's mat... this lone child's heart once again finds promise... and hope...
 
finally, understanding...
 
as puny infant insects,
we
atop the might of nature's
cut
such pow'r as lay beneath the
sea
now evidenced; iced mon'lith's
rut
leaves in its wake this beauteous,
grand,
unchanging, vaulted
majesty...
and in her face; upon her
brow
is written runes for all to
see:
 
"These Be the Tales of Eons
Past
These Be a Testament for
Time
These Be, when All is at Its
Last,
The Living, Sole, Surviving
Rhyme"
