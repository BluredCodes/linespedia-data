I hate the way you fuck with meAnd I want you to go awayI'm nothing like youI don't want to be like youQuit the mind fuckingQuit trying to manipulateTurn and walk away from meI don't think like youAnd I don't think I ever willYou've gone two steps too farSo now I want you out I'm slamming the door in your faceDon't tell me how to live my lifeOr handle relationships with other peopleDon't do me any 'favors'That make things even worseDon't put words in my mouthOr set me up in a trapJust go! And keep awayif you are reading this then it probably isn't about you
