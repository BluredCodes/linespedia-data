Each time I see you with herI get this stab of painBecause you say you don't want meShe makes me feel so jealousWhenever she talks about youI want to reach and slap herAll I have are dreamsWith long forgotten memoriesYour girlfriend's such a bitchA two faced scheming liarWho only remembers my existenceBecause you are my friendIts time to reveal my feelingsI hated telling you the liesEmotions held back because of herDidn't want to ruin a relationshipI tried to be matureThought to myself: as long as he's happyBut I want a little happiness tooI think I derserve a peiceSo screw the things I said beforeThose words are in the pastI want you oh so badlyAs more than just a friendAnd I know you want me tooPlease tell me I'm not too lateIn case you did think...you're right.
