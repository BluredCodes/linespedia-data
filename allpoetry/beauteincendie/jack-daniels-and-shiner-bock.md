Driving from a party
Eager to get in bed
A young and handsome man
His whole life lay ahead
His fiancee sat beside him
And together made some plans
For the wedding and a honeymoon
Each made a few demands
Some asshole of a person
Was driving with his friends
The car was filled with bottles
And he with loads of attitudes
He was on his tenth drink
Of Jack Daniels and Shiner Bock
He didn't look where he was going
And drove straight off the rock
The young and happy couple
Were busy picking songs
They didn't hear the awful screaming
Which resembled howling dogs
The couple was at the bottom
As the drunken driver fell
He smashed into the couples' car
And not a soul was well
The cops were hurried to the site
To survey the gruesome scene
Then followed mighty EMS
To save and heal and clean
The handsome man's fiancee died
But the driver, he did not
The man sat and wondered madly
Why should she be the one to rot?
A nurse came in the room
Where the grieving sick man lay
She told him she had news
And he better start to pray
Your fiancee was five months pregnant
The nurse said with a sigh
And if we dont take action quickly
Your baby boy might die
Surprise and shock filled his face
He didnt believe the nurse
The only thing I want to hear, he says
Is the monster who did this leaves in a hearse
The nurse sat there astounded
Maybe he hadnt heard correctly
So she explained to him a second time
And this time he looked up with joy
I want to save my son, he said
Just tell me what shall I do please
So she told him about the operation
And he fell forward on his knees
She got him on the bed
Slapped the man to wake him up
This is an important decision, she yelled
His time is almost up!
He told her he'd do anything
So she gave doctors permission
And the surgeons worked for the boy
While the father lie without expression
Several hours passed by
Then the nurse came to visit
She woke him from his slumber
And exclaimed we think he'll make it
The man juped with excitement
And let emotions flow
He laughed,
cried,
then began to sob
As the world began to slow
He fell back to the bed 
And his pressure continued to drop
His last thoughts were of his son
And his fiancee lying before the cop
He died and left his son
Without a family or a name
And the drunk driver lived on
But went to jail without fortune or fame
He sat there in his filthy cell
Thinking about his wasted life
About his five and many kids
And pain that wouldn't heal in time
