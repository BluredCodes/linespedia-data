The kind of hurt I feel the most
Is when you don't realize you're doing it
I wish I could tell you the truth
But then I'd feel selfish for saying it


The kind of pain I notice the most
Is when its unintentional
I don't like to talk about it
So it'll keep on happening


The kind of rejection I fear the most
Is when you don't really say it
Actions speak louder than words
And you found new ways to prove it


I feel so alone
The worls seems so cold
And darkness completely consumes me


I can't see a light
Is there really a tunnel?
Or does the space remain empty and hateful?
