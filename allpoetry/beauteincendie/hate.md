Don't count on thoughts of suicide
Thoughts of homicide are good for me
Maybe you don't realize what you've done
You set me up to ruin a friendship
And fucked up my closest relationship
Images of axes, guns, even arsenic
Fill my mind rapidly these days
And you always at their end
I never should put trust in strangers
The only worthy lesson you taught me
Look what its done to me
I think I'll hate you 'til I die
For messing with my most treasured friend
Ruining the best thing in my life
I wish dementors really did exist
