She smelled the smokeBefore she saw the flamesEngulfing her long time loverShe heard the screams of agonyAnd then she saw his faceTwisted with the painFive thousand thoughtsPlus a question explodedWithin the depths of her mindBeautiful flames destroyedTheir beautiful worldAnd all that lay around herSeconds ticked bywhile she tried to decideTo run or save his lifeshe kicked herselfThen ran to his sideAnd lifted him awayShe turned aroundStarted to run awayAlmost getting burnedbut the beautiful flamesThey couldn't damage herBecause her heart was pure and trueBecause she tried to saveAnd risked her special lifeDanger couldn't get herThe beaute incendie helpedTo save the lovely coupleAnd keep them alive
