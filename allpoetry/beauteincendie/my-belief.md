To sing is to liveThat is my beliefMy dream is to spread the giftOf music throughout the worldWhether I'm a famous singerOr a high school choir directorI'll be singing til I dieCritics and judges don't matterI know what I'm capable ofTo sing is to liveThat is my beliefWithout music I'm good as deadI'm constantly tapping rhythmsOr making music in my headSometimes its the only wayI can make it through the dayTo have music is to liveThis is my belief
