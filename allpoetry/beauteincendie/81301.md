I've waited for quite awhile
To see how things worked out
Yet nothings changed!
I still see you with her
I know you're still in love
Should I just give it up?
They always say there's a time
Where you just gotta let go
I wonder if that time has come
Give me a sign, say something
Don't avoid the subject
Just like you always do
I wanna know... if I should just give up
You're an awesome friend
But I thought we'd be more
I'm guessing I was wrong againhelp me with the title guys...
