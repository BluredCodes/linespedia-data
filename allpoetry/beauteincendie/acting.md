The happy expression you seeThe excitement in my voiceIt is just me actingI don't have a lot to smile aboutThere's not a lot to laugh atI'm constantly wearing a maskThat conceals what I'm really feelingI wonder what you'd think about meIf you knew my life wasn't all you thoughtWould you still love me?Would you still care for me?Or would you just desert me...You're the only one who really makes me laughWho can get me to really smileBut what would happen if you knew my troubled days?I don't ever want you to leave meimprov! i actually sat and wrote that just now...yeah yeah, i know im weird
