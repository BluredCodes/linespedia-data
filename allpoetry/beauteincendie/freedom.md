You piss me off all the time
A little more each day
Its gotten so I count the days
Until I can get away
Why can't you see it
I'm not a little girl
You can't control me
Tell me why you even bother
I want my freedom
Don't tell me I don't deserve it
I want to go on a date
I hate it when you have to know the guy
Before he even takes me to school
You're so afraid of letting me loose
But I already know the real world
I was raised in hell
Don't you remember it?!
I can't even have a private conversation
Without your nasty commentary
Or your lurking by my computer screen
I want my freedom
Don't control me anymoreI'm sure we can all relate to this one...
