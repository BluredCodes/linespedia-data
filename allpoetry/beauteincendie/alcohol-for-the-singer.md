Whenever I feel crappyAnd there's no light aheadThe music brings me upIf I'm jamming out to hard rockOr dancing to the bluesThe music brings me upSo bring your worstGive me your best hitMusic always brings me upThen I can love againIts alcohol for singersA mood enchancing potionNo matter what the situationMusic can help you tooI just got to thinking about how certain songs make me happy when I don't feel like smiling. Usually all it takes is a little music and pretty soon I'm dancing in circles around my room.
