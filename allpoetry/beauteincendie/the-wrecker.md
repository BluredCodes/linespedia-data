She stole your heart
And it belonged to me
I watched as you went
As I cried because I'd lost you
That girl is like a wrecking ball
How can you be so blind?!
She'll only destroy
The guy I used to have

I never got the courage
To tell you how I feel
And because you never knew
She is the one you care about
I've lost all my passion
My feelings for living
She'll only destroy
The guy I used to haveI don't really like to follow a form, I just write what I'm thinking
