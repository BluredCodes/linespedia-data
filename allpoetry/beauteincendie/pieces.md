My life is in piecesLying all around youWill you help me?Or are you like the others?The others just walk right on themThe pieces of my life are shatteredHow will I ever be whole again?I trusted when I thought I couldn'tI believed and they let me fallSo now I need a helping handA little guiding lightAre you willing?Or will you keep me in the dark?
