Guess what, you guysI'm moving onIts taken me almost 7 monthsBut I'm really doing itI'm gonna learn to dealFind someone of my ownSo I can finally be happyAnd you can be proud of meCause I'm truly getting strongerI'll keep him by my sideYou know I value his friendshipBut he'll be just that- a friendI'll even be nice to his girlfriendIsn't that a change?I can smile and mean itI can laugh without fakingSo smile and say 'you go girl!'I'm finally moving onbout time, eh?
