I want my friends to be happy
I can't stand it when even one is distressed
That's why I'm the therapist
They come to me with problems
And I try to make it better
But sometimes I don't understand it
Sometimes I can't even cheer them up
And I don't know what I can do
Cause I want them to smile again
They come to me with problems
And I try to make it better
And I wish that I could help them all
No matter the situation
So tell me all your problems
I'm your therapist
But there are some things even I can't handle
Please try and give me timewhat category could this go under?
