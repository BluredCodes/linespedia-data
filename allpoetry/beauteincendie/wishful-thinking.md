I want to feel your faceSee the warmth of your tender smileHold you in my arms foreverAnd make myself feel betterMy journey in life has barely begunBut I know who I want beside meI want you there every step of the waySo don't say you don't give a damnTurn the smile to a frownOr extinguish the fires living in your eyesBecause I know its one gigantic lieI hate the way you've been manipulatedAnd I wish we'd go back through timeSo then you'd never meet her...And I'd open your eyes and let you seeAll that we are missingAll that we are lackingAll the love we could be gainingteehee
