I feel like an idiotA blind and stupid foolI look for love in the wrong placesYou'd never see me that wayAnd you-you have a girlfriendIts such a funny thingWhen you fall for your best friendI hate how I manage thisI never find pure happinessSomehow I'll screw it upAnd I never fail to be wrongMisjudging guys...hell, misjudging peopleSeems to be my specialtyWill I ever have a way out?i go through mixed emotions faster than anyone i know
