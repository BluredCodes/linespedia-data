For a long time I walked
And lived in a colorless existence
My purpose was to play therapist
Yet remain miserable myself
Then my worst day
Also became my best
I realized someone cared
The most amazing feeling
I still have the note you wrote
That day my life had light
No one made me feel good
While all that shit went on
Until the day during class
You threw the letter at my heart
I also got attacked that day
By someone who didn't understand
After class I ran to my lesson
To the only other person I could trust
Everything that happened came out
Inbetween the sobs
And it hit me like a rock
Someone really gives a damn
They care if I'm in tears
And care if I get hurt
Now I truly want to hold on
And live my life with all the love I've got
