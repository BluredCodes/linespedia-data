If I could hold you close
If I could touch your hair
If I could see your shining eyes each day
I know I'd be content
If I could laugh at all your jokes
And tease you in return
If we could just be simple friends
I know I'd be content
But nothing is that easy
Nothing is that sure
People try to pull us apart
And keep us from sharing our hearts
If I could capture all the people
Who keep us far apart
And lock them up forever
So I could keep you near
I'd touch your face and shiny hair
And scratch your broad back 
We'd stay that way and talk for hours
So I could be content
