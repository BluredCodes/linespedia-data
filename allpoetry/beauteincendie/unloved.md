title: "Unloved"

I can't stand to see your face
The light that's in your eyes
I can't listen to the soft and gentle voice
That's always full of passion
If I do I'll feel unloved
The words you say are soothing
But the words are not for me
your touch is warm and calming
Yet it always seems to hurt
I can't watch you walk away
Or play on your guitar
I can't listen to your singing
The lush and beautiful notes
If I do I'll feel unloved
I shouldn't like you anymore
And wish that you were mine
I can't let you see my life in peices
Spread all on the floor
If I do I'll feel unloved
