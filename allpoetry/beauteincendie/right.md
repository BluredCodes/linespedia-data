Take me in your arms
Say the words I long to hear
You miss my gentle touch
And you need it back so much
You're sorry, and you were wrong
After all that we've been through
I still want to be with you
Ever since the day we met
Nothing else has felt so right
Can you come back to me
Won't you come back to me
We'll make our lives so much better
Sitting in the firelight
We're resting in each other's arms
As the music gets slowly softer 
You say the words I need to hear
I missed the days without you
I'm glad and I'm here to stay
I love you, and I was wrong
And now the world will feel so right
You could come back to me
And yes, you came back to me
Now I just want to hold you closeIt actually sounds ok when I sing it too...
