I know its an actCan't you give it up?Tell them what's goin' onLet them know how you're feelingIts gone on too longHow can you do it?You've known them longer than meWhy do you trust me?What have I done To make you tell the truth?Or are you lying to meAnd not to the othersCan I be the one who betrayed you?Am I so far goneI can't tell anymoreWho lies or speaks the truth?Why won't you just say?Tell them what you tell meGive up the actLive your life the way you want it to be
