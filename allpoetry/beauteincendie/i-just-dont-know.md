title: "I Just Don't Know"

Can it get any worse?I didn't think it wouldThen you turned your back on meI trusted you unconditionallyAnd I thought you trusted meI wanna know what happenedHow could you say those words?You used to care so muchWhat has she done to you?Are you completely blinded?You used to ignore her wordsThen they were all you heardI wasn't just after your bodyYour friendship meant so much moreBut you never realized the truthI'm not really evil, scheming and kanivingWho gave you the idea?What made you listen to the others?My mind and heart refuse to believeDeep down I know I'm in your heartAnd you are praying that I'll understandBut I don't know if that can happen
