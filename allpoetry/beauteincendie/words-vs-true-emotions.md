I hate everyone todayI don't want to show my face'You made me feel like shitAnd I never want to seeYour pathetic face again'I acted like a bitchBut I was having a bad dayWhy couldn't you understand?! I wanted my spaceNeeded time to thinkYet you pressed onSo I said the worstThe worst I could think ofOnly wanted to be aloneI didn't mean a word of itAnd you believed me anywayI don't want to hurt youI know what I need nowYour shining eyes andThe lasting white smileStrong comforting armsWith a reassuring voiceI remember what I told youCold and harsh insultsBut I want the chanceTo change the nasty wordsI'd like to venture back in timeAnd erase those precious few momentsSo I can help you stay with meThen I'd tell you my true emotionsLet you hold and comfort meWait to do my thinkingWait for another timeKeep my life all togetherI'll be happy once againI acted like a bitchand pushed you far awayBut those were only wordsNot my true emotionsHey I know what y'all are thinking (nathan heth and jen), and you're wrong. This is about a dream I keep having.
