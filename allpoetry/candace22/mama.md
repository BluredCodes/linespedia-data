This woman means so much to me
I love her so because she loves me
she's my mother, my best friend
she's there for me when i need a helping hand
we haven't always had the best of years
a little sadness and some tears
but we have always managed to get through
with some smiles, laughs, and many hugs too
I thank the Lord for her everyday
as I lay down at night, rest my head and pray
because of her I am alive
because of her compassion, I will not strive
she's a blessing from above,
and for my mama, I will always
have much love.
