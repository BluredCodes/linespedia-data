A young kidtired and aloneempty inside, no place to call homehis girl left him for Caliwith an unborn babyhe is searching for someonea lover, a friend maybeI have felt that same exact pain through the yearsthe need to be lovedsomeone to comfort my fearsI needed someone to fill the spacesthe same situations, but different facestoo bad it took so longto find exactly where I belonghumans and things can only do so muchit takes so much more than an earthly touchGod is my shelter, my refuge, my one true friendHe's gonna be the only one to fill those voidsright to the end
