I am menot the girl on the magazine coveror the perfect beauty queenI am not flawlessnor thin or model likeI am meI'm not that popularI'm pretty different I guessbut no one should be chosento have a label that states'I am the best'because we are all individualswho should remember ourselvesnot the superficial headlines that sit onthe shelvesGod has made us who we areso why should we base our worth or standardson some movie star?we are who we are and I am meDoes anyone feel that the media stinx?
