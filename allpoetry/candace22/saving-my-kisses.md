I save this kiss for the tendernessof your lips alonethey have not touched another'sours is for under the coverswait for the touch of mineas the beat of our heartsand the passion of our lovecomes in twineno one else may lay by my sideonly you when i become your bridewill we have that night of love and fireyou are the one i am waiting forthe only man on earth i will desiredon't give your love awaywait for me until that dayyou will be my lover by nitemy friend by dayand i will not let this love fade away
