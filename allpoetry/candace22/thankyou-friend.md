You found me
Walking to the cafeteria 
I knew you were going to ask
"Where have you been?"
My guts sank and I felt like I was under a spotlight
I knew it was no coincidence that we ran into each other
I wanted to cry so badly, so badly
Even though I didn't tell you my whole life story
I knew that you knew what has been going on
I prayed for a friend that would take me to the side 
and force an answer out of me
it's true
I didn't expect you
I told you so much that has been going on
I poured my heart out, well, most of it
It was a relief to tell someone that I have been down 
and so good to know that someone actually cared
It's not easy to admit I have done wrong
or to talk to someone about all the anger and frustration inside of me
God gave you graceful ears and a loving heart 
I was truly encouraged and revived
It will be a while before I can stand on two sturdy feet
But you were sent that morning at that time to lift me up
even if it was a little bit
Thankyou friend
Continue to bless others with you genuine concern
You never know how it can change someones's life or perspective
Thankyou
