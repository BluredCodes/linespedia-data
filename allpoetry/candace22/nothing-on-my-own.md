I'm weak, broken, and drythere are so many pieces to pick upso I won't even tryI've cried, my eyes are tearlessI'm now so scaredbut everyone sees me as fearlessI just realized that I needed His hand to get me through this wretched landI can no longer be my own crutchI need Him and His loving touchtake me from myself and my desiresgive me passion like raging firesI know now I cannot go aloneand I am nothing on my own  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------I really can't get through life without Him.
