The world began with perfection
Adam and Eve were God's creation
But they listened to that nerd Satan
and look where we are at in this nation
So many tragedies have taken place
the sadness can't be whiped off a person's face
was September 11 enough for us?
I don't think so because look at the fuss
America is so spoiled rotten
we have the freedom to do anything we want
so many countries wish that they had our freedom 
to worship God and to openly seek Him
but we take our freedom so for granted
we find pleasures in this insane country 
and our desires are planted
in its soil
this nation was founded on God
now look at us, we really have it hard
we think God is really going to hold our nation in His hands
when we constantly push Him away and drive Him to vaste lands?
He loves us all so much 
we have the chances to feel His touch
why are we so naive?
why do we believe in things where we are decieved?
you want an answer and solution to for peace and joy in your life?
He is the answer, not all this strife
look to Him and you will see 
that you too can be delivered from pain and mourning
life is too short and there is very little time
stop all the drama and see what is right
