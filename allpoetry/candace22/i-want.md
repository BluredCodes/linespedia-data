I wait for the night when your eyes look into mine with hunger and passion
I want you to lay me upon a bed of satan sheets
Place your left arm under my head and embrace my bodice with your right
Slide your tongue in my mouth gently and kiss my lips tenderly
slowly run your hand towards my breasts and caress them with kindness
I once was afraid, but now I am anticipating this night of fire
I want to feel the heat and love between us and our bodies
Take hold of my vuluptuous thighs and enter in
Go with the flow of my moves and do not be shy
I want this moment to be remembered for as long as we live
Hold on tight as I wrap my soft legs and arms around you 
I want to travel your world with the touch of my hands
and the kiss of my lips
Continue to kiss my body and my insides
I want to whisper your name and feel droplets of sweat off your body
I want to hear you whisper in my ear that you love me
I want us to continue this journey until we are weak and can no longer give any more than what we have alreay given
I want you to hold on to me as we lay sleeping, dreaming
I want you
But, I will have to wait
I am saving myself for you
I want to give my all to you
One night.....
