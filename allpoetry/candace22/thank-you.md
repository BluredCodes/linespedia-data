I cannot explain this feeling
I have inside my heart
it's this overwhelming joy
this work more beautiful than art

You bring a smile to my face
even when the clouds are grey
You lift me from the oceans depths
You cast my fears away

And i lift my hands 
to the presence of Your grace
You are so holy, oh so pure
I desire to dwell in Your place
I give You praise for
giving me life and setting me free
Oh Lord, your love
was meant for me
