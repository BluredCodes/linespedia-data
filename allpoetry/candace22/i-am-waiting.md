---
title: "I Am Waiting"
writer: "candace22"

Oh how I dream of the day 
when i find you
not by my own eyes
but God's hand that has lead
us through these barren places
i am waiting for the life we 
will lead in twine
the love that pours from our souls
like flowing red wine
the touch of your hand
the tenderness of your mouth
we will become one as our bodies 
meet, side by side that very first time
wait for me as i wait for you
i am yours and your are mine too
i am waiting, please wait for me
