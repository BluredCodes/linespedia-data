---
title: "I am the Only Way"
writer: "candace22"
slug: "i-am-the-only-way"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see you are hurting
let Me take your hand
I can give you
peace, love, and hope
like no other can
I am not here to judge you
but to be your friend
don't listen to the voice
that leads you to unfruitful lands
I am the way, the truth, and the life
I take the pain away and the strife
you have been searching in all the wrong places
not seeking Me, but other faces
I loved you so much that I would lay down my life for you
upon a cross in Calvary, thats what I knew I had to do
please trust in Me and understand that I am here for you
no matter what you have done, or what you are going to do
I want to see you enter My home
a home that you cannot see now
I do not want you to be alone
Please ask me into Your heart 
and I will give you a fresh start
I am the only way
do not listen to what they have to say
they will only lead you astray
remember I am the only way
