Not one word alone can describe You my Lordnot one word alone can add up to you Lordnot one word alone (not one word alone)not one word aloneNot one word alone could describe Your lovenot one word could say how great You arenot one word alone (not one word alone)not one word aloneOh Lord, You are greater than any words I could sayOh Lord, no words add up to Your love or any of Your waysnot one word alone can say justhow I feel about Younot one word alone
