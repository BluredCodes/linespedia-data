I have tried to find peace and comfort in the things of this worldI've based my worth on what the world seesas true and beautifuli am no celebritynor do i have the perfect facei have let my heart be brokenby their standards and wishesbut now i choose to swim with a different school of fishessomeone found me, but knew me all my lifeHe made me feel beautifulexternally and insidei am good enough for Himso let it be who cares what the world thinks about me?they can judge melook me downturn their backs awayand give me a frownall these things will pass awayso i'll just look up and seize the day!
