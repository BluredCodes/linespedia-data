Caution! I am a teenager
Caution! I have ideals and opions of my own
Caution! I think for myself
Caution! I could be dangerous
And when you see me walking down the streets and I might want to talk to you about them!! Caution!
Caution! I have feelings and emotions, I am flesh and blood, I'm not perfact nor do I try to be Caution!
And when you see me sitting down on a bench in a park pondering my life, RUN AWAY! because I am different from you, and difference creats disput! OH NO! A PROBLEM! We can't have problems in our perfact society! There is no room for change, nor is it wanted.
