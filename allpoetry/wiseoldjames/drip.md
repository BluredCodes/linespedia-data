Drip drip a fosset of ideas has sprug a leak
Drip
Free thought
Drip
Culture
Drip drip
Individuality
Drip drip drip
Freedom
A rag of bigitry is cramed, shoved up into the fosset, clogging, and blocking the drops from working it's way into the sewers of the mind.
A cork made of small minds plugs the drain. Only we can remove it.Um, I had a REALLY good idea with this, but I f'ed up on the words
