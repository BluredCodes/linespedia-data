title: "Colors"

Black is the clothes I wear
Black is my expression, a blank stare
Black is my color black is for me
Black is the void I feel inside
Black is the emoition like a bike I ride
Black is my shadow
Black is my shirt
Black as the night
And black cuz I hurt
Then comes the orange and fills me inside
Fills me with hope and fills me with pride
Orange as the sun 
Orange as a flower
Orange gives me strength
Orange gives me power
