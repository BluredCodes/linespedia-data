No one needs me 

No on cares

No one sees me

Just blank stares

I have an angel

A beutiful orange glow

I have no more hope

I sold it for reality

I relized I hate being alone

I relized I´m afriad

I relized I´m afraid to open myself to anyone

I relized only my angel cares for me and I only care for her

I relized I love her more then words can say

I relized I shall be alone

I relized it shall always be this way

Fuck it all, just go away
