I am a prisoner of my own fear 
I am scared
I am lost
I am dead inside
I am a child
Yet I am a man
I am angry 
I am mad
I am happy
Yet so very sad
I´ve lost my path
I´ve lost my way
I´ve killed myself
I want it to go away
