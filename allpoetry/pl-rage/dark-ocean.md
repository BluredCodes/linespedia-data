title: "Dark Ocean"
writer: "pl rage"

Where hope dies I’m resting my soul
In a vast ocean of dark emotions
So tired I can barely stay afloat
Soon I’ll be asleep with no one by my side
All alone with no shore in sight
As far away from love as one can get

I see a storm brewing in the horizon
It moves with fury quickly against me
The sky rips open under the pressure of loneliness
Unleashing a blinding rain of bloodstained tears
Around me big waves of sadness is forming
The onslaught is too much and I’m pulled under

Steadily sinking deeper and deeper into depression
Soon I’m lost beyond reach in this deep dark ocean
When I’m at the bottom I continue, sinking deeper
The pressure around my heart is ever-increasing
Not designed for this kind of bottomless feelings
Overwhelmed my heart collapse and stops beating
