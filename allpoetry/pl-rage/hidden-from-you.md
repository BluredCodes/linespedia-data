---
title: "Hidden from you"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "hidden-from-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

A sorrow inside
Won’t dream tonight
Feeling cold
Left outside
Hungry for love
Starving alone
No longer whole
Broken in two
Cast aside
Already forgotten
Sad voices
Nothing to say
Lost and confused
In grievous pain
Showing no wounds
Keeping all inside
Grim reality
Roles to play
Strange life
A mask outside
