---
title: "Awaken"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "awaken"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1992-Awaken-by-pl-rage"
---

New passions stirring deep inside
Drop the mask created by society
Unveil the hidden fantasies
Break the shackles of false mirrors
Unleash the wild creature inside
Surrender yourself; be free
Touch the black rose of desire
Feel the thorn of piercing beauty
A sting of fresh hunger
Red drops staining the innocence
Taste the sweet burning fire
The fragrance of metallic hues
A thirst for life has begun
The ancient thunder awakened
Kiss all old sorrows goodbye
The sky will never look the same
A new star was born tonight
