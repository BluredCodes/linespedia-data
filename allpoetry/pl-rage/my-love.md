---
title: "My Love"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "my-love"

There she stands, brightening the day
The girl I have been waiting for
Who’s she to capture my heart so easily?
Our eyes meeting, two souls as one
The strangest feeling, I can’t even breathe
Excitement, wonder, joy, desperation
How can she bind me without a rope?
A shy smile and I’m hers forever
The moment is over; she’s walking away
I want to stop her but I’m still bound
She has taken my heart and left me bleeding
Happy to have seen her and sad that I lost her
Always on my mind, will I ever se her again?
This is not how it was meant to be
She’s still out there, so close, a world away
The days are passing and I’m still searching
I live on hope, her image in my mind
If ever I find her again, will she be mine?
