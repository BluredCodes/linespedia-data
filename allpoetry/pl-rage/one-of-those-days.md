Waking to a familiar feeling
This is one of those days
I know it deep inside
Today nothing will go my way
Don’t know what will happen
Sure it won’t be good
Might as well start moving
No difference if I rest
There’s no use hiding
A small delay at best
No chance to escape
The day is already here
Soon I’ll have my verification
It’s just a matter of time
Needn’t wait long 
As things start going wrong
I know it’s just the beginning
Still I can’t help thinking
What is coming next?
