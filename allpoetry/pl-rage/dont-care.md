---
title: "Don’t care"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "dont-care"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7886-Don-t-care-by-pl-rage"
---

Lost all desires
It means nothing to me
No longer do I care
Lost all lust
It withered away to dust
No longer do I care
Giving up my search for love
There is no hope for me
No longer do I care
Giving no more of myself
There is no soul left in me
No longer do I care
I’m drained of energy
Feeling tired all the time
No longer do I care
I’ve lost my will to live
Feeling like I’m dying
No longer do I care
Would anyone miss me? – Don’t care
Will anyone save me? – Don’t care
I just don’t care anymore
