title: "The King of Rage"

Fly on wings of sorrow
Cry a tear of sadness
There’s no tomorrow
Some kind of madness
No time to borrow

Love transformed into hate
There’s a war to wage
Fear the twisted fate
Dreams locked in a cage
Souls used for bate

The end of kindness
Wheels grinding marrow
Slowly turning darkness
A tunnel growing narrow
Feel the burning blindness

Listen to the sad sage
Maybe it’s too late
Turn the last page
Time for the final date
Meet the king of rage
