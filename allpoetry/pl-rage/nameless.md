Once a mighty wizard
Speaker of the true words
Protector against evil
Defender of those in need
I answered the call
Entered the battle with fierce determination
Gave more than was mine to give
I paid the price for my mistake
All magic spent
The gift now denied by misuse
My words have lost their meaning
The power gone from my voice
Relief or simply grief
Not sure how to feel
No pressure to save the day
No way to challenge fate
No longer do I matter
The days of glory have past
Soon to be forgotten
My name lost in the wind
