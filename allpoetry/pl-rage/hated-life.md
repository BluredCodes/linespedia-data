---
title: "Hated Life"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "hated-life"

My closest friend
The enemy I know
Free of devotion
Free from emotion
Bringing pain whenever you please
You was a gift to me
The curse I received
Somewhere along our way I lost control
Never noticed the change until it was too late
You took charge of my destiny
Chained me securely to fate
A long time I have suffered under your hand
Soon I’ll be your slave no longer
Found a way to break the spell
Forever free from you that I hate
The one I long to escape
But in the end I guess it’s actually me
For we are as one, the same
I am the one to blame
I am the one I really hate
