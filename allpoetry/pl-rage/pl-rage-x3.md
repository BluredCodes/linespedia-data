Please hear me
Listen to my words

Rich I’m not
Angry a lot
Going to take my life
Extracting now the knife


Pray for me
Lie to me

Reap my soul
Avenge me not
Grieve me not
Erase my name


Prepare to be boarded
Lay down your weapons

Resist us not
Assimilating all
Game over
Existence is futileThree short poems based on my alias
