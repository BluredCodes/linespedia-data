---
title: "Dead and Dying"
writer: "pl rage"

A tortured spirit
Dripping blood on your grave
Born to die
Can you tell me why?
No reason offered
Beyond explanation they said
No kiss goodbye
Too soon you left my side
Felt so right
Love growing day by day
Every moment together
Like a dream from heaven sent
You had strength
A will to change fate I thought
Should be me
Laying there dead in the earth
I am nothing
Alone I have no worth
I lost you
My guide in life
Losing my direction
Tears blinding my way
Can’t let go
Love more precious than air
I must follow
A bond stronger than blood
Side by side
Together forever this time
A tortured spirit
Dripping blood on your grave
