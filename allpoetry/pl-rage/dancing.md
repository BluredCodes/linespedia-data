---
title: "Dancing"
writer: "pl rage"

I stand searching for the reason I’m here
I look at you and see rain dancing on water
Such beauty in your saddened eyes
Where they lit by laughter you would dazzle the sun
Where they lit by love I hope you look at me
It hurts to see you in lonely pain
Let me share the weight of your burden
Let my steady shoulders carry your sorrow
Shed your last tear and come dance with me
