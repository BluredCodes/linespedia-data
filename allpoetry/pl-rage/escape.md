title: "Escape"
writer: "pl rage"

A trapped soul calling
Looking for life in blinding darkness
A shadowed tear falling
Measuring the depths of sadness

A towering wave of despair
Drowning all alone in cold emotion
A heart in need of repair
Trusting love will be the solution

A fleeting thought
Remembering the look in her eyes
A battle fought
Believing all the lies

A noose hanging from a tree
Binding the lost with a ring of rope
A dream breaking free
Flying away on wings of hope

A whisper in the shadows
Speaking of love, can’t you hear?
A life on the meadows
Living together, never fear
