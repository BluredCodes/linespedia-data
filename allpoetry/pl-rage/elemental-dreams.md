title: "Elemental dreams"
writer: "pl rage"

Dreams of water
Swims in our souls
A legacy from the birth of life
Ruler of the world
Dominating and shaping
A big part of who we are

Dreams of fire
Wakens our passion
A heat that cannot be denied
Needed by all
Feared by many
The light that guides our way

Dreams of air
Flows through our minds
A longing to be free
Flying like a bird
On winds of change
Above all pain

Dreams of earth
Waits at the end
Embraced by our mother in final rest
Dark and comforting
Solid it bends to our will
Never breaking, cannot be tamed
