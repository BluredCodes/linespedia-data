---
title: "Say you will"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "say-you-will"

Come with me
Follow your dream
No longer alone
By your side
Forever I’ll be

Come with me
Follow your heart
No longer apart
By my side
Forever you’ll stay

Come with me
Follow your destiny
No more pain
By going now
Forever as one

Come now
Follow me
