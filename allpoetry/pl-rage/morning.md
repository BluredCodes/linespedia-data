Beep! Beep! Beep!
My dream broken by intrusion
Fading in the confusion
Slowly cohesive thoughts comes to mind
Hitting snooze, the dream I’m going to find
A few more minutes of needed sleep
Like a stone in the sea I’m sinking deep
Beep! Beep! Beep!
Shocked nearly to fatality
I’m back in reality
Please say it isn’t time just yet
Why ever an alarm did I set?
In bed I want to stay
I don’t need another day
Dreamland is the place to be
A magic land just for me
Outside the sun shines too bright
Hurting eyes still adjusted to night
Like all mornings I surrender to reason
Feels like I’m committing treason
Getting up from the warm comfort of bed
Putting on clothes I can’t wait to shed
I know it should be fun
It’s morning and a new day has just begun
