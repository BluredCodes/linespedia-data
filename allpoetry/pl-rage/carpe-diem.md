A clear sunny day
Bright and carefree
Something in the air
Something in the earth and trees
The flight of honeybees
Greet the world with a smile
Listen to the song of birds
Hear the sound of laughter
Love surrounds us all
Making us whole and free
Let your heart speak
No wrong words today
No black thoughts
No pain, wars or pollution
The air fresh and clean
Breathe in deep the fragrance of life
Open your eyes and see
Your love stands before thee
Enjoy this precious moment
It’s time to seize the day
