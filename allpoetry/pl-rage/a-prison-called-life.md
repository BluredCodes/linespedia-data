I am but a shadow
A ghost in this prison
This frail mortal shell
My immortal self
Trapped in a ruin
Frail as it seems
I cannot break free

If you closely look
You may see my presence
Behind the green windows
Sometimes I will look out
Trying to get some attention
Most will just pass me by
Not seeing, nor caring

Some call it a beautiful temple
Still it’s just an ugly prison to me
Built to be my vessel in life
A cold cell holding my crippled spirit 
My thoughts wander almost freely
Drifting in and out of consciousness
Biding for the time of release
