I find myself standing before a wall
This place was not intended for me
So with a heavy heart I must say:
-Goodbye. I love you all!!!

I guess it wasn’t meant to be
There truly is no one responsible
I’m just another tear in the ocean
Too small for anyone to see

This is something that I must do
There are things I have left behind
Some memories for those who need
With this said I now take my leave of youGoodbye :´o(
