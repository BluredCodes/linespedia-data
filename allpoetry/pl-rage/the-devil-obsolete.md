Once I was known as Lucifer
No longer am I the bringer of light
Disguised as human I walk the streets
No one sees me for what I am
Too well I play my part
So easy to fit in – just stay apart

I plant small seeds of hate and sorrow
Filling the minds of those I pass
Later they will take root and bear fruit
Leaving chaos in my wake
Such a common sight
Nobody suspects all isn’t right

The rules have changed
My touch hardly necessary
The world isn’t the same
No longer full of happy thoughts
Half the minds boil of hate
The rest drowns in sorrow

Is it all my doing?
Can I take credit for what’s happening?
Are the humans to blame?
Are they doing this to themselves?
Have they taken my place?
Am I obsolete?
