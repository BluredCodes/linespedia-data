---
title: "Luciatrum"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "luciatrum"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Child of light and dark
A being torn apart
Pure of heart and blackened soul
The conflict is pain
Born to agony
Slave to misery
Living in haze
Lost between worlds
No place for harmony
A choice to make
Live by day and blind the soul
Live by night and cloud the heart
A sacrifice must be made
Always a price to pay
Life isn’t easy for the damned
No way to live in peace
Pain will follow all the roads
There is but one escape
