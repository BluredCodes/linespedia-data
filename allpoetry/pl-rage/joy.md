A tingling sensation
The beginning of something new?
Hands trembling, I can hardly eat
My body can’t be trusted
The joy is weakening me

The mind stronger than ever
Unleashed, soaring free
Is it really happening?
Never been happy before
The joy is filling me

Heart racing, cannot sit still
High on energy
High on life
Is this what they call living?
The joy is killing me
