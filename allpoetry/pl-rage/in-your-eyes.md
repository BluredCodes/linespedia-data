Gazing deep into your eyes
I see the loneliness you try to hide
Happy, but something is still missing
Being among friends doesn’t quite help
It’s the kind of loneliness only love can break

In your eyes there’s a story to read
Tired of short romances
Of being alone at night
Still waiting for that special feeling
To know that this is right

Gazing deep into your eyes
I see a soul most tender and kind
Caring, but with something still missing
The question that remains to be answered
Where is the love you long to find?
