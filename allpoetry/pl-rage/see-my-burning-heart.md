title: "See my burning heart"
writer: "pl rage"

There’s so much love in me
So much to give
No one to share it with
Locked inside, it fades away
Such a painful waste
It hurts, but no one sees

Take a closer look
Glowing softly inside
A fiery passion waiting
Crack a hole in my shell
Let yourself in
Add new fuel to my fire

Don’t fear the flames
They won’t hurt
Warm is my touch
Gentle and safe
No more darkness
I’ll brighten our way

I remain unseen
Hidden in open sight
Can you not see me?
Perhaps my words will reach you
Would you feel their touch?
Open your senses for me

See me! Hear me!
Feel me! Release me!
I’m burning my love to waste
Soon my fire will die and so will I
You’re my last hope
Find my heart and set it free
