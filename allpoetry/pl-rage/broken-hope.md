---
title: "Broken Hope"
writer: "pl rage"
slug: "broken-hope"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Words spoken, given in trust
Secrets revealed, betrayed by love
Left exposed, easy to hurt
Painful rejection, wounded in heart
Hope broken, crumbled to dust

Falling down the hole left behind
Once there was hope to fill the space
Pain eating away what remain inside
Bleeding tears from empty heart
Death only a drop away

Afraid to speak again
Hiding in desolate solitude
Trying to heal what’s broken
All alone there is no chance
Help arrives a bit too late

Words spoken, given in vain
Truth revealed, betrayed by fear
Fully exposed, easy to see
Painful revelation, wounded too deep
Hope broken, dying in pain
