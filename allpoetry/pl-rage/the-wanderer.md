Feeling dead, but still I’m walking
Leaving no tracks behind
Who am I?
Casting no shadow
What am I?
No reflection in the mirror
Am I dead or alive?
Cannot end this existence
Believe me, I’ve tried
Can you help?
Drive a wooden stake through my heart
Would it make a difference?
I’m desperate for an end to this curse
Can you blame me?
No longer a part of this world
I wander with the wind, searching for answers
