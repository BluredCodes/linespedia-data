A lonely soul drifts through the night
Watching the world around
Searching for a release from its plight

Somehow it got left behind
Forgotten at the critical time
Not sure what it’s now supposed to find

The situation seems hopeless at best
Already searched for so long
All the time wishing for the final rest

Invisible to the mortal eye
It must wander unseen
Even lacking tears to cry

The poor spirit cries out in vain
Without breath it has no voice
No means to commune its pain

Burdened by a story it cannot tell
Feeling more alone than ever in life
No longer sure that it isn’t in hell

And so it must remain a lonely ghost
Afraid it will never truly die
Sadly not believed to exist by most 

Waiting, hoping and everywhere peeking
Knowing that it can never rest
Until the day it discover what it’s seeking
