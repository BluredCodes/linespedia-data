title: "The Rage"
writer: "pl rage"

Brooding cloud searing
Unstoppable fury
Red rain falling
Cleansed memories
Pointed stars below
Unheeded agony
Boiling blackness blistering
Pain exploding
Winds of fire
Screaming silence
An opened bottle
Chains broken
Building a wall
Crumbled stones
Sailing cloaked ships
Life uncharted
Burdens easily dropped
Everything lost
No dreams fulfilled
All denied
Vengeance against fate
Hopeless struggle
Fighting hate uncontrolled
Always losing
Child of rage
Destiny unchangeable
