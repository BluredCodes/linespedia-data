---
title: "My last words"
writer: "pl rage"

Alas, my love
The world fades away
Grieve not for me
I’m going where I wanted to be
All pain gone at last
I have but one regret
I wish I could have told you sooner
Before it was too late
Had I only known
But how could I
You hid your love too well
I hadn’t a clue
Love must be blind in more ways than one
I guess we were not meant to be
Please, don’t cry
Surely you’ll find someone else
Just don’t hesitate when you do
Everything is so clear now at the end
Don’t be afraid to show how you feel
You may be hurt sometimes
But it’s also painful to be alone
Love is a precious thing
Meant to be shared
Such a waste if you’re alone
Don’t give up like I did
Some things are worth fighting for
Living in sadness haven’t made anyone happy
Open a window to the world
Let yourself be seen
Take a look yourself
Breathe deep in the garden of life
Be true to what you believe in
Don’t let anyone hold you back
Go now
I can speak no more
Remember what I’ve said
The time has come
Kiss me goodbye
I’m leaving now
