A falling star
Once shining bright
Lost the spark of life
Now cold and dark
Fumbling in the void
Unable to see the way
Spinning out of control
No means to set a course
Hoping for a beacon
A guide to set it straight

A fallen star
On a quest for light
Looking for another chance
A new system to reignite
Searching the depth of space
Such a lonely place
Among myriads of its kind
One silent ghost
Black against black
Never seen in endless night
