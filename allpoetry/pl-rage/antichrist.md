A black sun shines today
Risen from the deepest abyss
It marks the dawn of a new time
A time of change
Of peace and love
On this day Antichrist is born
Born to save the human race
To break the bonds of religion
To free our minds from these tainted beliefs
No longer shall we be ruled by a lie
A lie maintained by those who seek power
By people hiding behind a righteous mask
Their true faces full of hate
Religion is the way of war
A way founded on persecution and intolerance
Built from the fears of mankind
It’s time to redeem the world
To wash it clean of sins
Together we can begin anew
In a world with no need of nations
No religions to breed dissention
One people living in harmony
Sharing and helping
Telling no lies
