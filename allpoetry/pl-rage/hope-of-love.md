Getting closer now, I know
The goal somewhere in sight
After forever the time has come
The universe greets me in silent praise
Pieces of my shattered life are falling back in place
Under my feet the ground speaks in quiet vibration
A promise of love to come
The road going my way for a change
No barriers left to bar my dreams
Hope has left my mind and entered my heart

Nothing can stop me now
Too close
No one can stand in my way
Too near
Not ever turning back
Too strong
Hope of love is in my heart

The fury of a raging river flows in my veins
Tearing down the gates inside
No longer blind, love can find
Free of hate, love can survive
My life intact, love can remain
Time now to let it come
I stand ready, well prepared
Fate is waiting for my command
One final lock left to open
Hope of love is the key
