title: "The River of Life"
writer: "pl rage"

The river of life runs to the sea of infinity
There are many bends and curves along the way,
sometimes it runs slow and sometimes fast
No matter how it twist and turn,
the final destination can never change
No two rivers will ever look the same,
not even when poured from the same source
You cannot change the flow of life,
only fools would try
A mighty river it is, one that cannot be stopped,
it will run its course no matter how long or short
Don’t drink from the fountain of youth,
the balance of nature will be disturbed
No one can tell what would happen
should the same river run twice
Drink from the river if you’re thirsty,
all should experience the taste of life
The river won’t run dry no matter how much you drink
No one know how long they have,
so take a sip as often as you can
You won’t see the end before it’s too late
Don’t let anyone dump waste in your river,
it’ll leave a foul taste for a long time
So will your own too, so be careful of what you do
Jump into the river and enjoy the ride,
for no reason should you stand beside
and just watch as it run you by
