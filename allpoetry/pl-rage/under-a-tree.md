I sit under a tree watching the world pass before me

A bird lands in my hand singing a happy song
No words but its meaning clear
I should laugh and run with the wind
I know this but still I sit

A squirrel climbs up my knee holding out a nut
The gift is small but its meaning clear
I should break my shell and chase my dreams
I know this but still I sit

A golden leaf falls from the tree
Not out of season but its meaning clear
I should make my move before it’s too late
I know this but still I sit

A friend sees me and waves his hand
The gesture is simple but its meaning clear
I should go with him and not be alone
I know this but still I sit

A dog wiggles its tail and barks at me
Something dogs do but its meaning clear
I should do something wild and crazy for a change
I know this but still I sit

A spider comes and spins a web by my feet
An intricate design but its meaning clear
I should not stay in one place too long
I know this but still I sit

A girl walks up to me and gives me a kiss
The sweetest touch and its meaning clear
I should go now; my waiting is over
I know this and leave the tree

We wander off together as one with the world
