---
title: "The Way"
writer: "pl rage"

The day is fading, becoming the night
Darkness surrounds me, all is black
Lost soul drifting, never looking back
Who is to save me, show me the way?
Full moon is rising, spreading a silver light
Calling the creatures of night, beckoning me to follow
Danger lurking, strange sounds whispering
Who is to save me, show me the way?
Storm clouds coming, chasing the light
A sudden flash, a demon revealed
The chase is on, running for my life
Who is to save me, show me the way?
The air is cold, a burning breath
My strength waning, the end is near
A flickering ahead, an angel of light
Is she to save me, show me the way?
Come here my child, she cries
Singing a song of sorrow, she commands the night
The darkness lifts, she is the Lady Death
The way is clear; death alone from life can save
