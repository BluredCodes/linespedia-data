title: "Osoris Mea"

My word is power,
commander of hate
I’ll cast a shadow over the world,
clouding all your dreams
Kneel before me
I’ll crush all happiness, no more love
You’re bound to bend to my will,
if not I’ll break you like a twig
Cast aside old values,
embrace this new order
No mortal soul can escape me
Strong are the dark emotions,
hidden deep inside, patiently waiting
The dark flower will bloom under my touch,
no resistance, the seed is in you all
Feel the rage building under my pressure,
you can’t keep the lid on forever
Don’t hold anything back,
bring forth thy offering
Scream your hatred for me!

Fervidus osor intus
Aut liberatus aut dispereo
Osoris est dicio
Osor est tu
Vos mea
