The world has changed
It has grown darker
I can feel something watching
Always out of sight
A fleeting glimpse escaping
My dreams invaded by burning eyes
Who’s there?

Created by the higher arts
Summoned in hate
Always watching you
I’m in your dreams
A whisper here, a shadow there
Feeding on your fear
There’s no shelter, no sanctuary
Run all you want
Never will you escape me
Never will you be free

No, this can’t be!
What have I done to deserve this?
Never have I wronged
Never have I hurt
How could this happen?
Why me?
I say all my prayers
Please Lord – set me free!

Ha!
Your God can’t help you
Don’t you know he’s not for real?
A creation of make-believe
The Old Ones have doomed you
You’re a disgrace to mankind
Being kind isn’t for this world
I shall plague you ‘til the end
Forsake your god and swear fealty to me
Do so and the end will be quick
If not you’ll be slowly draining away
Either way you’ll be mine

Nooo!!!
