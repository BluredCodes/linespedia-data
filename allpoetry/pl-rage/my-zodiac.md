Born on a Wednesday
My heart is full of woe

Born in the dragon’s year
My heart is full of fire

Born in the sign of Virgo
My mind strives for harmony

Born to ascending Sagittarius
My mind hungers for risky challenge

Born on a day of conflict
My soul shattered by contrasts

Born to a life of pain
My soul struggles for peace
