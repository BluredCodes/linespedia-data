Goodbye Life
Can’t say it’s been good knowing you
You were always so cruel to me
Don’t know what I did wrong
Why do you hate me so?
I’m leaving now
Please don’t tell me I should stay
Hope I don’t have to see you again

Hello Death
Nice to finally meet you
It seemed like this day would never come
Long have I waited to see your face
I’ve said my farewells
Left the past behind
I stand here ready for your embrace
The netherworld is supposed to be lovely this time of year
