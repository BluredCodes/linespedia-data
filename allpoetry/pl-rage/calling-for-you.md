Fly little bird
Follow the call
Someone needs you
Someone shares your pain

Do not be afraid
The call is true
No need for hesitation
It is intended for you
To bring you here
A place were you belong

Stop fleeing
Can you not hear it?
Can you not feel it?
It is a call of love
Not meant to be ignored

Spread your wings
Take a leap of faith
Should you ever falter
Or get lost in a storm
Just listen
The call will guide you
Lend you strength to carry on

Fly little bird
Follow the call
Trust what it tells you
