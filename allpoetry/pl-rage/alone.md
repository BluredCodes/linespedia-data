title: "Alone"
writer: "pl rage"

I sit alone in a closed room,
no one sees the tears falling
Loneliness is a heavy burden,
a burden that cannot be shared
I long for the day I carry it no more,
when I’ve found the love I long for
Lately the tide has been changing,
things have been falling into place
Looking good when viewed from afar,
up close my pain still mars my soul
As long as love eludes me
happiness cannot be contained,
sadness will not leave

Alone, I see the world broken by tears
Alone, I carry my burden unseen
Alone, I cannot find love
Alone, I keep trying
Alone, I remain
Alone
