I strive to end my sadness,
to build a new life based on gladness
No matter how hard I try,
I cannot help but cry
When everything I do goes bad,
can you blame me for being sad?
A sorrow that’s growing roots,
from the depth of my soul it loots
Robbing me of vital force,
swiftly depleting my only source
The hand of death strikes my face,
leaving pain without a trace
Shocked back to life,
I continue my strife
