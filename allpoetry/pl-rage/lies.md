title: "Lies"

Let moon touch shadow
Caught in a web of deception
Shimmering clouds whispers
A star fall of lies
Nothing is untouched
Purity an illusion
Ruined by time
Soiled from the very start
Blackened by hate
Truth died on faith
A dream struggling for breath
Send sorrow down to help
Futile as the wheel turns
Leave silence behind
A trail of smoke
Unlit by sun’s grace
Follow life’s thread
Tangle untrustworthy fates
Overcast water breaks
Light finds no hold
Darkness covers all
Hiding in words posing for truth
Lies lives in all
