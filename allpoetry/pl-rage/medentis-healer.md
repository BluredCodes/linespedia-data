title: "Medentis (healer)"

Cosmic energies surround us all
I open a channel and receive my share
Strands of power flows through my veins
The power to heal is in my hands
A tingling sensation burns inside
Building with the force of unshed energies
Come before me and share my gift
Let me unleash the mighty flow
Feel the power bridge your wounds
Restoring the broken pathways
I’ll weave the threads of fine energy
The distinct pattern of your soul made whole
A creeping warmth tell it’s done
Sure sign of a working field
Restricted by the laws of creation
I cannot charge you full
I’m merely a conduit to higher powers
A tool for the living universe
Now the rest is up to you
