Flee mankind’s cruel ways
Escape into my domain
Free from pollution and envy
No greed to ruin the day
Soft rains and the touch of a breeze
No hard weather will darken the mood
Leave all burdens at the border
Nothing harmful will ever cross
Pain and sorrow are left behind
Step inside and be cleansed
Forget there was ever hate
Here all can rest at peace
Sharing the joys of life together
In harmony with all of nature
Sleep safely next to a wolf
Share a meal with a snake
There is no fear
Join me now
There is nothing to lose
All are welcome here
In my domain
