Sitting here amongst the stars,
Where no one knows who you are.
Humming to the rhythm of your heart,
Whispering on the winds so softly,
For a love that will never part.

Dreaming a dream, a flight of fantasy,
To take you into the relm of the unknown.
Giving you back the wisdom from Venus.
Showing you all that is yet to be shown.

Like a maiden, high above in her castle,
Looking down upon the world in a new light.
Moving softly like a breeze through a meadow,
Breathing in this new fragrance tonight.

A oneness of spirit in tune with nature,
My soul just a seedling ready to sow.
Bathed beneath star lights mystical magic,
The time is nigh; I am ready to grow.
