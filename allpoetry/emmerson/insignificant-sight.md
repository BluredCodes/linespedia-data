Red on Black, 
Blue on Gold. 
Transparent are the colours 
Of insignificant sight. 

The joker plays his tune 
Upon your heart. 
Every beat has become  
The rhythm of a joke.  

Silent wanderings, 
Through mountains of snow. 
Wings forming Icicles, 
Your frozen in a limerick.  

Stinging sarcasm 
Leaks from tear ducts,
Swollen by their fatal attraction. 

Compassions depths, 
Shallow into
Non-existence.

Your thoughts….?
