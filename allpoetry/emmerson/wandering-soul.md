title: "Wandering Soul"

Wandering soul searching,
For a path of distant light.
Into the abyss of nothingness,
Rendered helpless in this plight.

Eyes seek through corridors,
Vast in their expanse.
Glistening phrases capturing attention,
Silence echos into chants.

Wisdom once found amongst the rubble,
Now decayed and fallen to the ground.
Through empty corridors,
The voice of ancient lore sounds.

Seek for what lays hidden,
Deep beneath the sand.
Use your eyes and dig without looking,
Magic is born upon your hands.

Pathways of old and new collide,
A new world has been born.
Wandering soul that walks within my heart,
With each new step I am torn.

Look beyond the here and now,
There you will see what to do.
The magic that you seek..

You already have,
Its you.
