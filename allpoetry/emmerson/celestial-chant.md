O sacred moon in lunar skies,
High above hear this cry.
Gothic Angels dressed in black,
I stand before thee naked, barely in tact.

Evening enchantress of heaven above,
Fill my soul with the purest love.
Remove the lids from my eyes,
Guide me through this life of lies.

Celestial powers of good and evil combine,
Speak in tongues ancient wisdom, old as time.
Inject beneath my dehydrated skin,
Liquified knowledge to edify within.

Ecclesiastic ruler of astral skies,
Behold your daughter beneath her disguise.
Remove the shackles of this earthbound life,
Intoxicate my mind with thoughts of freedom.
