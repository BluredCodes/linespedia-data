---
title: "The Art Of My Love"
writer: "Emmerson"
slug: "the-art-of-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40868-The-Art-Of-My-Love-by-Emmerson"
---

I will paint my moods in shades of blue,
Add some magenta to the edges of the sun.
Sculpt a vision abstract and surreal,
Upon this canvas with words I seal.

Electric tones of passion,
That creates the kaleidoscope of my mind.
With thoughts so soft and tender,
I will draw you close and make you mine.

~ * ~

With intricate strokes from my pen,
I will sketch the beauty contained in my heart.
Smudge the edges with a crimson hue,
Outlining the detail of my desire for you.

I will trace the delicate pink of my lips,
With strokes so soft and fine.
For you to taste how I feel,
From the sensation of a kiss so sublime.

~ * ~

A silhouette of dark and light,
Shadows the beauty of my form.
In your essence I dip my brush,
Giving life to all that I have drawn.

With my words this portrait I paint,
The art of my love so true.
This masterpiece of my own creation
Is the colour of my love for you.

~ * ~
