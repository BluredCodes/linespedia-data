title: "Full Moon"

A full moon lights my way 
Through the dark,
Dreams for the future, 
Memories of the past.

Walking in a shadow,
Created by my own light.
The insignificance of my
Existence alarms me tonight.

Rainbow aura,
Encompassing around.
Feet walking,
Barely touching the ground.

Looking up into the sky,
Sensations invade.
The wonder of nature
And all it has made.

Such a tiny little particle,
I am on the earth.
Left quietly,
Re-examining my worth.
