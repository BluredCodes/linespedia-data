title: "Sprinkled With Dust"

Eyes of green, sprinkled with dust,
That form a veil of pure mistrust.
Words that are reflected into the skies,
Disguised in a truth, hidden by lies.

Can you see without looking into the stars,
What you have become and who you are.
Do you walk forward or are you in reverse,
Is this a song or just a verse?

So many questions I ask of you,
Maybe you could answer just a few.
What are you doing, did you come from above,
Is this the way you treat all whom you love?

I try to see through your swirl of light,
But it just keeps evaporating into the night.
Once I saw greatness in your eyes,
Now just darkness in there lies.

Are you wandering through a black hole,
Can you hear the voice that cries from within your soul?
I think you think, you know what you do,
But are you really sure, could this be true?No I am not on drugs...... yet :)
