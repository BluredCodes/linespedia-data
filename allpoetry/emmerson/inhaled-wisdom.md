---
title: "Inhaled Wisdom"
writer: "Emmerson"
slug: "inhaled-wisdom"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49682-Inhaled-Wisdom-by-Emmerson"
---

With demonic words of wisdom, 
Speak to me in angelic phrase. 
Twist your thoughts through my mind, 
Guide me through the haze. 

Skies open into tomorrow, 
Where yesterday lays it head. 
From the now into forever, 
Lay me down in eternity's bed.

Capture my imagination, 
With thoughts not yet seen. 
Paint me a picture; 
Etch in my mind what you mean. 

Knowledge sprouts from spoors, 
That can be seen through the glare.  
Inhaled pieces of wisdom, 
Exhaled back into the air.

Place your lips to mine, 
Speak to me with your tongue. 
Communicate from your soul, 
Only then, can two become one.
