---
title: "Silent Dreamings"
writer: "Emmerson"
slug: "silent-dreamings"
source: "AllPoetry"

I want to dream with you this evening, 
A silent dream or two.
Take a walk beside you, 
In a constellation I've made just for you.

Orchestrate the feelings, 
Composed of woods and strings.
To reach across the distance, 
And listen as you silently sing.

Dance a thousand dances, 
With each step mysterious and new.
Marvel at the wonder of the person, 
That lives inside of you.

Take me to that secret place, 
In the confines of your mind.
You know the place I speak of, 
Where you contemplate each moment in time.

Come out and play with me, 
Dance and smile amidst the stars.
Its time to leave the past behind you;
Time to discover who you really are.

Silent dreams are whispers
That come direct from your heart.
They will give you the direction, 
So you know where to start.

Hold your hand out and place it to mine,
Then we can start this journey that knows no distance or time.
