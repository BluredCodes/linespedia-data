Lost in a moment
Silent with wonder.
Thoughts of my own truths,
Quietly I ponder.

~~

I believe in spirits,
That guide direction.
I believe that lost souls make,
Unconscious connections.

I believe that paths cross,
For specific reasons.
That some will enter our lives,
For a lifetime, 
Whilst others for a season.

For what ever reason,
Our spirits did collide.
There are so many things
To me that cannot be denied.

An unconscious acceptance
Of things as they are.
The realization that paths chosen,
Inevitably make us who we are.

People are like roses,
The seasons teach them how to grow.
With every season brought forth for a reason.
As I watch you quietly
I wonder……

What is my season?
