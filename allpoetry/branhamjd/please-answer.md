Do I have to ask you to hold me? 
when all you want is to be free
Do I have to say I love you first?
why bother if it is meaningless and rehearsed 
Can you occasionally open the door?
I know you feel like it is a chore
Can you say your sorry when you are wrong?
why does it take so much work just to get along 
Will you just ask me how my day went? 
it just might be more important then a sporting event 
Will you really listen when I tell you?
it isn't that hard to do
How about you make the plans?
you won't even have to dirty your hands 
How about you giving in every once in a while?
you won't have to cramp your style 
Why do I have to take your hand?
if only you could understand 
Why do I feel like I am all alone?
if only you weren't such a drone
What does it take to get a little help?
it really won't kill you - just a little help
What makes you think that you have to yell? 
it really isn't cool to always rebel
Where does your money go?
never mind I don't want to know 
Where did I sign the eternal I owe you?
never mind your half is way past due
When does it all end?
I lost my lover and best friend 
When did you realize-I’m already gone?
I hope this gives you a clue, I've moved on!
