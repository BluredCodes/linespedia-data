As I stare out the window I see another evening closing in
The gray sky is changing to black
I can't remember the last time I saw a twinkling star
Or stopped to enjoy the serenity of the bright moonlight 
Reflecting back over the last few months
It is time to let go and put the past where it belongs
Time to enjoy the peace in silence
Wondering how to live again
Wanting to be comfortable with me
Knowing that how I feel is my choice 
And it is time to do things for myself
Needing to apprecaite my independance
Hoping the day I feel secure will come soon
Still needing the phone to ring
Just family and friends saying hello
To reassure me that I'm never really alone
Honestly scared that I am not in love
Relieved no one is needing or waiting for me 
I'm not crying about how I've been wronged
Instead I just sit without expectations or timelines 
I pray that I can find happiness inside of me
While understanding that love just happens 
Not just to fullfill a need
I want to truly know and believe . . . 

       THAT LOVING MYSELF WILL SET ME FREE
