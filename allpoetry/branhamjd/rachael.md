RACHAEL

THANK YOU FOR BEING MY FRIEND
A SHOULDER TO LEAN ON YOU ALWAYS LEND
HOLDING ME UP WHEN I AM READY TO BEND
THIS IS JUST A FEW OF THE THINGS I VALUE IN A FRIEND

WE ALL GO THROUGH TIMES THAT ARE TOUGH
AND AT TIMES WE HAVE BEEN THROUGH THE SAME STUFF
THANKS FOR KEEPING ME SANE WHEN I THOUGHT THAT I HAD ENOUGH
YOUR KINDNESS HAS MADE THESE TIMES NOT AS ROUGH

A FRIEND LIKE YOU IS VERY RARE
WITHOUT SAYING A WORD IT'S OBVIOUS YOU CARE
THANKS FOR UNDERSTANDING AND GIVING ADVICE THAT IS FAIR
I APPRECIATE YOUR FRIENDSHIP AND THE TIMES WE'VE SHARED

YOUR FRIENDSHIP I'VE CHERISHED RIGHT FROM THE START
HOLDING ME TOGETHER AS I WAS FALLING APART
YOU RADIATE BEAUTY, WARMTH AND SMARTS
SO THANKS AGAIN FROM THE BOTTOM OF MY HEART
