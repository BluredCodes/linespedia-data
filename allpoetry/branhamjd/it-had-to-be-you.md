---
title: "It had to be You!"
writer: "branhamjd"
slug: "it-had-to-be-you"

where did you go wrong
I know it wasn't me
so it had to be you

even though you tried
blame falls back on you
and deep inside you know

I did all I could
I was your biggest fan
not to mention your best friend too

I gave you all of me
I bought you nice things
took on all your baggage

you drank until you fell
pushed me away
and even fooled around

I was always there when you needed me
I helped you with your bills
never ever did I let you down

I hung out in bars to watch you sing
loved you for your heart and soul
never once straying toward another

you took me for granted
hoping that I would leave you
so your conscience could be free

well I took the final step
now you can say it was me
and I question why do you still call 

where did you go wrong
I know it wasn't me
so it had to be you
