I BREATHED AGAIN TODAY
THE HURT IS SLOWLY FADING AWAY
AND TODAY IS A NEW DAY

I CRIED AND TRIED TO HOLD ON
LONG AFTER YOU WERE ALREADY GONE
NOW I'M LOOKING FOR A NEW DAY TO DAWN

ANGER CONSUMED MY HEART 
ALTHOUGH I'M RELIEVED THAT WE ARE APART
AND NOW I CAN FINALLY MAKE A NEW START

LOST LOVE STILL LINGERS AND HANGS AROUND
A LEARNING EXPERINCE IS WHAT I FOUND
MOVING FORWARD IS WHERE I AM BOUND

AFTER ALL IS SAID AND DONE
I AM READY TO HAVE SOME FUN 
A NEW BEGINNING HAS JUST BEGUN
