title: "I Knew the Day Would Come"

REFLECTING BACK TO THE HARSH WORDS YOU SPOKE
THE EGO THAT WAS EVIL THAT ALWAYS LURKED
TO BELITTLE WAS PART OF YOUR GAME
AND THEN IT WAS ME WHO WOULD TAKE THE BLAME
THE BROKEN HEART THAT IS LEFT TO MEND
ALL ALONE LEFT TO CRY SEEMED TO BE A TREND 
TAKING ALL THAT I HAD TO GIVE AND MORE
CHOKING ME AND LEAVING ME ON THE FLOOR
FINDING MYSELF ON A ONE WAY STREET
THE ONE THAT LOVED YOU – YOU HAD TO DEFEAT
STAYING OUT ALL NIGHT HANGING IN BARS
WHEN WOULD YOU PUSH ME TOO FAR

WAS IT THE GIRLS CALLING THE HOUSE AT NIGHT
WAS IT EVERY CONVERSATION THAT TURNED INTO A FIGHT
DID YOU STOP TO THINK THAT ONE DAY I’D BE GONE
I WAS OBVIOUSLY QUIET, SAD AND WITHDRAWN
HOLDING ONTO HAPPY MEMORIES THAT WERE FADING FAST
HOW LONG COULD THIS DOWNWARD SPIRAL LAST
WHY DID YOU THINK I OWED YOU SOMETHING
YOU TORE IT APART AND NOW WE HAVE NOTHING
WATCHING MY LIFE SPIN OUT OF CONTROL
LEAVING ME ALL ALONE IN A BLACK EMPTY HOLE
TAKING ALL OF MY LOVE AND THE LIFE THAT WE BUILT
NOT EVER FEELING AN OUNCE OF GUILT

THE ROLLER COASTER RIDE FINALLY TOOK ITS LAST TURN
AS YOU LAUGHED AND PLAYED WITHOUT A BIT OF CONCERN
NOW THE HOUSE IS EMPTY NOT A PICTURE ON THE WALL
DID THE ALMIGHTY FINALLY TAKE A BIG FALL
DO YOU CLIMB INTO AN EMPTY BED AT NIGHT
DO YOU MISS ME KISSING YOU AND HOLDING YOU TIGHT
ARE YOU STARING AT THE CLOCK UNTIL MORNING
NOW YOU REMEMBER ALL THE COUNTLESS WARNINGS
WHY COULDN’T YOU STOP AND LISTEN TO MY CRIES
BEFORE YOU LET THIS RELATIONSHIP DIE
I’M MOVING ON,  SEEING WHAT WE’VE BECOME
NOW YOU CALL . . . I KNEW THE DAY WOULD COME
