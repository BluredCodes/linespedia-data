title: "Blank Page"
writer: "branhamjd"

crystal blue as far as I can see
not a dark cloud in the sky
orange and red leaves fill the trees
gentle yet cool autumn breeze

it seems as if it is a perfect day
the outside world is as it should be
but my world is falling apart at the seams
lost somewhere inside these desolate dreams

accepting the ups and downs of everyday life 
wishing I can find my own special place 
seeing what beauty is on the outside 
yearning to be truly happy inside

does it take another to make me complete
washed away are the bright colors of the sun and stars
now my heart is filled with memories that make me sad
and the page is blank when I count the things that make me glad
