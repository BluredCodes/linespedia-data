---
title: "Who is This Strange Man?"

A man from the past

who loses his temper fast

a man with an evil eye

who lives his life as a lie

a man who doesn't care

who treats everyone unfair

a man with hate running deep in his veins

who doesn't feel any pain

a man with love for no other

who doesn't even call his own brother

a man whos hands are a deadly weapon

when he glares at you, run

a man who thinks everyone is a liar

and when he is around your on a live wire

a man that you can't help but hate

only the depths of hell can be his fate

a man who is a complete fake

who would hurt anyone for his own sake

a man who you could never trust

walking on egg shells is a must

and I ask myself - why bother

the only response - because he's your father
