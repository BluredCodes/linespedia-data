title: "Almost There"

where are you now that I am here
this is the moment that I once feared
you'd be holding someone else instead of me
I know now that we just couldn't be
falling in and out of love is what people do
it doesn't mean that I'm sorry I met you
I don't regret the times we've shared
and I won't deny how much I cared
a song might bring me back to our place 
and I'll file that memory in its own little space
the good times I will always hold close
but your friendship is what I miss the most
