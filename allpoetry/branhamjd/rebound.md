You have given me the wings to fly
to leave the past behind me and give love another try
I count on you to hold my hand and be my friend  
and you encourage me to openly admit how I feel and not pretend     

so many times you make me smile when I think I will crack
while building my esteem so I won’t ever look back
staring deep into my eyes, wiping my tears and holding my hand
without having to say a word, you always seem to understand 

you ask how I feel, and want to know about my day
and you always seem to know exactly what to say
I appreciate you for making my happiness your goal
the emptiness is residing, you make me feel whole

you express your feelings for me in so many ways
and your actions show more then you could ever possibly say
so if you question how I feel because of some of the silly things I do 
just remember how much I care and I do LOVE YOU!
