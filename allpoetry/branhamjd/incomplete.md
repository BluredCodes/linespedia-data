this burning sensation
deep within this place inside
gravitating to a now
empty space 

this churning feeling 
in my stomach
pleading for relief
twisted memories

this dizziness stirring
in my head
nausea going over
the words you said

this tightness clenching
my heart
yearning for the time
you held me tight

this tingling sensation 
numbing my senses
desiring the times
we were as one

this heated moisture
running from my eyes
cleansing the history
of a lost love
