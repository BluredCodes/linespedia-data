I felt your presence surrounding me
the strangest sensations tingling me

Your face so familiar yet new
smiling and giggling is all I could do

a warming glow when I looked in your eyes
a different feeling then any other guy

more then just a schoolgirl crush
your smile gave me the biggest rush

I can't believe that I still get butterflies
you would think I would be a little more wise

yet I knew that I could not let you walk away
there was so much I needed to say

it must have been in another life
maybe once upon a time I was your wife

these are the thoughts spinning in my head
I believed that love at first sight was dead

I am not a psycho desperate for love
but could he be sent from up above

this has got to be my real soul mate
beyond chance this is fate

I can't explain this feeling inside
but I threw caution aside

I put my pride on the table
but for this one chance I was willing and able

he gave me his number and told me to call
he could be the one to make me fall

I love his accent and tender voice on the phone
you could feel the warmth in his tone

just one date is set before he is off for a week
as soon as he arrived my knees felt weak

dinner, wine and great conversation
laughing and talking complete relaxation  

is this the one I prayed for 
check back and see . . . there will be more

there is something that I do know
my soul is somehow attached to Mr. OwensboroI plan to continue this poem as this relationship progresses.
