Searching for happiness

not a world out of reach

no outrageous expectations

not material things



Searching deep inside eyes

for a glimpse of tranquillity

a moment of honesty

a lifetime of memories



Searching in conversations

for that kind simple word

that doesn't have an agenda

only wants to be heard



Searching for a moment in time

to take a long, scenic ride

to listen to the world outside

just to have someone by your side 



Searching for time alone

no pressure - time to laugh together

no where to go, 

Still searching . . .
