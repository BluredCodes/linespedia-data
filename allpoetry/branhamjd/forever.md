---
title: "Forever"

To the dearest women in the world . . 
the only one who was always there, 
the only one who showed how much you care
you always gave something
when you had almost nothing
all the memories
and all of life's mysteries
are gone from me
what a cold, terrible reality.

now I am all alone
to face life on my own
as I begin to cry, 
I realize we never got the chance to say good-bye
I can't understand why 
God let you die
and in the morning when I wake
I don't know why you were the one he had to take
I know it is wrong
but it is hard to be strong
when you feel pain
and it always remains
I try to be bold
but I need your hand to hold

you always understood
when nobody else could
and even though you aren't near
my feeling won't ever disappear
and when I am not sure 
and I feel insecure
it is your warm embrace
I can't replace
deep inside my aching heart
we will never be apart
you and I will always be together
......................FOREVER ! ! !
