WALKING AWAY AND CLOSING THE DOOR
WHAT THE HELL WAS ALL THIS FIGHTING FOR
THE YEARS SPENT BUILDING A FUTURE TOGETHER
WEREN’T YOU THE ONE WHO PROMISED FOREVER
THE ROOM IS EMPTY AND FADES TO BLACK
TEARS THAT I AM HOLDING BACK

HOW CAN THIS BE HAPPENING TO ME
IS THIS THE WAY IT WAS SUPPOSED TO BE
WHY DID WE ALWAYS HAVE TO DISAGREE
ARE YOU HAPPIER NOW THAT YOU ARE FREE

SHOTS OF ALCOHOL TO HELP ME UNWIND 
NUMBNESS CONSUMES MY BODY AND MIND
POUNDING HEART, DIZZY HEAD
ALL ALONE IN THIS EMPTY BED 
WATCHING THE CLOCK AS HOURS GO BY
WHILE YOU ARE OUT GIVING SOMEONE ELSE A TRY

HOW CAN THIS BE HAPPENING TO ME
IS THIS THE WAY IT WAS SUPPOSED TO BE
WHY DID WE ALWAYS HAVE TO DISAGREE
ARE YOU HAPPIER NOW THAT YOU ARE FREE 

THE SMOKE IS FADING, IT ALL BECOMES CLEAR
NO MORE WASTED YEARS AND WASTED TEARS
