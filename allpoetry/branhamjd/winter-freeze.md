the cold night
 erratic seas
deep chill 
 a winter freeze
frozen ground
 tingling extremities
the man in the moon 
 gives no guarantees 

complacent now 
 I set you free
tears fill my eyes
 why couldn't I see
this is how 
 you wanted it to be 
I wonder now
 did you ever love me
