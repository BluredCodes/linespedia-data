I lay in a hot tub of water

turning the handle making it hotter

washing away your presence on me

leaving the past behind is the key



wandering down the path I've taken  

questioning each step I am making

hoping I'm moving forward not back

knowing it is self-esteem I lack



trying not to make the same mistake

examining every move I make

proving to myself I'm over you

going back is what I'll never do



realizing that I'm not insane

the water has now gone down the drain

in good time the past washes away

chancing love is just a game we play
