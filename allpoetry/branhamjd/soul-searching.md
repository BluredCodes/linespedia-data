looking for my equal

my partner for life

wanting to share my emotions

give of everything inside

knowing that I can make you happy

believing in old-fashioned romance

never holding back

or playing stupid head games

I am the girl next door

the one you take home to mom

the friend of a lifetime

smart and witty enough to challenge

enjoying healthy competition

I even like sports

financially independant enough

to contribute and build

not needy or clingy 

no one needs or wants that

I am the friend of a liftime

someone you can always count on

sharing all that is mine

never turning my back

no need to lie - I'm understanding

for trust it is what binds

you can always believe that I am true blue

my heart belongs only to you

funny and silly; always a smile on my face

athletic and cute; I love a good game

sexy and flirty; passion is alive

so with so many depths

and levels of love unveiled

why can't I find

the one to keep my soul aliveAlmost sounds like a personals ad - damn I hope I don't have to resort to that!
