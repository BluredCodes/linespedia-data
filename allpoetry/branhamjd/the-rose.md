---
title: "The Rose"
writer: "branhamjd"
slug: "the-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

long, thick, healthy green stems with tiny sharp thorns
the foundation; life experiences that you bring
tiny, compressed, beautiful red buds
closed lips not ready to open up and share all of yourself
silky soft petals begin to release and open up
breaking down the wall and finding out the person inside
expanding and flowering
revealing your heart as the relationship begins to bloom      
released and open exposing the seed
giving all you have heart and soul
the petals darken and begin to fall
reality sets in and things take on a different light
the stem loses its strength and wilts
arguments start; sleepless nights    
water evaporates; flower dies
broken heart
