title: "Too Fast"

I placed you on a pedestal
standing far above the rest
I wanted to believe you were the one
better then all the rest

I felt all these sensations
running deep inside my mind
I needed to believe in love 
true love at first sight

I dreamed of the days 
laying beside you in your arms
I longed for the moment
we would be together as one 

I took these magical moments
spinning around in my head
I made the mistake of jumping
right into your bed
