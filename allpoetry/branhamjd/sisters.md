---
title: "Sisters"
writer: "branhamjd"
slug: "sisters"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

sisters are best friends
you cherish all of your life
they are always there
through the good times and the bad
showing that they care
offering a helping hand
knowing what is best
always proud of what you do
never giving up
telling you to stand up tall
turning frowns around
giving advice that is fair
standing by your side
proud to be your family
sisters are quite rare
