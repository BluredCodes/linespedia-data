here he is - I scored
hot! sexy! built!
look - my friends are jealous
ha - ha he is leaving with me
back home for a little tumble

mmmm……

what is that I wondered
maybe he needs a little touch
well - how about a wet kiss
no - that's not the answer
you have got to be kidding  

uck…………

finally it is over
alcohol breath
big mistake
this wasn't the answer
can't take it back

zzzzz…………

awakened by  
what - -  what is that
it sounds like running water
you have got to be kidding
warmth fills the bed

get out………You have to laugh - a night he or I will never forget!
