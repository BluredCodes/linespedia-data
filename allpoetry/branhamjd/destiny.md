did fate draw me here
to “the one”
I give all of my love

was that cupid
that gave me
a big shove

is he my soulmate
sent from 
the stars above

“the one”
I have always
dreamed of

smiling,
beaming,
yearning for more….

watching you walk out my door

hoping,
wishing,
wanting it to be….

my true love, my destiny
