THERE'S YOUR NUMBER ON MY PHONE
I PICK UP WITH THE SWEETEST TONE
BUT YOU CALLED ME TO VENT ABOUT HER
THE TIME WE SPENT TOGETHER HAS BECOME A BLUR
YOU'RE CRYING BECAUSE SHE HURT YOU
AND YOU'RE ASKING ME WHAT YOU SHOULD DO
IT HURTS TO GIVE YOU THE BEST ADVICE I CAN
BECAUSE WHEN I WAS HURT YOU TURNED AND RAN
REVIEWING ALL THE CRUEL THINGS YOU SAID
WASN'T IT ME THAT YOU PROMISED TO WED
AND NOW THAT I AM FEELING STRONG AND FREE
YOU NEED ME TO BE YOUR DEAR ABBY
I KNOW NOW THAT THERE WAS NEVER AN US
SO WHY DID I CARRY ON AND MAKE A BIG FUSS
YOU TELL ME HOW MUCH YOU REALLY LOVE ME
DRUNK AND CRYING SAYING WE WERE MEANT TO BE
WHEN YOU SAID YOU NEEDED ME I CAME RUNNING TO YOUR SIDE
BUT THE NEXT DAY I SAT ALONE CONFUSED AND JUST CRIED
I THINK IT WAS A TEST TO SEE IF I STILL CARE
AND ONCE AGAIN I WAS THE ONE WHO WAS THERE
BUT IT'S A NEW DAY AND YOUR FEELING OK
I CALL YOU ON THE PHONE YOU HAVE NOTHING TO SAY
I KNOW NOW WE WEREN'T MEANT TO BE TOGETHER
SO I'M SAYING IT'S GOODBYE NOW AND FOREVER
