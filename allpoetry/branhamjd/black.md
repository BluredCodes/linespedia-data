stained carpets and leaky ceilings
old windows and creaky stairs
cold gray days and cloudy skies
hiding the tears that fall like rain drops from my eyes 

leaves have fallen now the trees are bare
the ground is hardening from the deep freeze of winter
the bright warm sun is hidden from our sight
and I lay here alone wishing you would just hold me tight

this old place just doesn’t feel the same
somehow these walls seem to conceal the flaws and shame
all that used to be vibrant has turned to black
nowhere to run and hide it's too late -  I can't turn back
