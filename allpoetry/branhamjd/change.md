I STOPPED AND THOUGHT ABOUT WHAT WENT WRONGAND THE REASONS I LET IT GO ON SO LONGI LOVED HIM, AND HE KNEWHE DIDN’T CARE WHAT HE WAS PUTTING ME THROUGHI DIDN’T THINK I HAD THE STRENGTH TO LEAVEHE THOUGHT I WAS SO NAÏVEBUT THE DECEPTION TURNED MY HEART TO ICEAND I WAS THE ONE WHO HAD TO PAY THE PRICESOME DAYS GO BY WITH ONLY SMALL DIPS IN THE ROADOTHERS FEEL LIKE MY HEART IS GOING TO EXPLODEEVERYONE TELLS ME THAT TIME MENDS A BROKEN HEARTAND I FEEL LIKE I HAVE MADE A SMALL STARTSTARTING OVER FROM SQUARE ONESOMETIMES FREEDOM CAN BE SO MUCH FUNEXCEPT AT NIGHT WHEN DARKNESS CLOSES INMY STRENGTH AND NERVE TO BE ALONE WEARS THINI REMEMBER WHAT IT FEELS LIKE TO SMILEAND NOW I HAVE THE POWER TO CHANGE MY ENTIRE LIFE STYLENO ONE IS HOLDING ME BACK OR BREAKING ME DOWNAND I AM FIND MYSELF STARTING TO LAUGH INSTEAD OF CARRYING A FROWNTHE SEPARATION GROWS DEEPER DAY BY DAYSO THE NEXT TIME HE SEES ME OUT AND ABOUT,I WILL JUST LOOK AWAYAND NOT WAIT TO HEAR WHAT HE HAS TO SAYTRANSFORMATION, WITHOUT A DOUBT - I AM ON MY WAY!
