title: "What Will Come Next?"

Okay so let me get this straight....Man walks on moon, Man creates cars, Cars distroy the ozone, whats next?
As we look back on the years that have flew past all we can say about it is..Okay and???
We begin see the shadows in our lives and our responces to them but then we think about the dark sides of our lives and think what do I do next?
The next day you get a letter saying you have been excepted in the most wonderful thing you have hoped for all your life but when you take that wonderful thing what are you going to do next? 
You sit at home on the couch think and think some more until your friends call and ask where you have been all the time when they are drinking and telling all of the new subjects found out in the gossip line to get a beer but all you can think of is what do I do?
Last but not least you are stuck in a home watching the Price is Right and you think maybe I should have done that too....
