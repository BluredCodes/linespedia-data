title: "War"

The smell of dirt, the sound of bombs, this is war.
During the fire and screams you stood by encourageing us all. This is courage.
When the president talked about war we all said it wasnt possible but it happened and now we are one once again. But as we look back on it all we see what we have done to other people and what they have done to us. But just a question, Where do I fit??
