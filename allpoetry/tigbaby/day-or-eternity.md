---
title: "Day or Eternity?"
writer: "Tigbaby"
slug: "day-or-eternity"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43199-Day-or-Eternity--by-Tigbaby"

Did you every just want to be someone else? Just for a day or maybe eternity?
Did you ever want the perfect guy? Just for a day or maybe eternity?
Did you ever want a life where you were actually something? Just for a day or maybe eternity?
Did you ever want to be an animal that lays in the breeze and loves the smell of grass free from everything? Just for a day or maybe eternity?
Well eternities here and your numbers up. 
WHAT DO YOU WANT???
