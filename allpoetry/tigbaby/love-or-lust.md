title: "Love or Lust"
writer: "Tigbaby"

Love is something you dont find very often but when you do are you sure its not lust?

You walk down a hall holding hands, exchanging glances, and all you see is the twinkle in his eye and his awesome figure. Is that what he is thinking too? 

As you walk along the beach letting the surf hit your feet in a way that makes you just want to fall in his arms. But then you hear three words...I LOVE YOU. All falls silent and you start to think is this love or lust?

You stare at him in the most awkward way imaginable as he starts to pull back not seeming to be true of the words he had just spoken. He keeps backing until you are along on the beach thinking about you and him and then you say in a low voice...I...LOVE...YOU.
