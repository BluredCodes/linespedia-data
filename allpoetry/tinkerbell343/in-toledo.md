In Toledo is
Saint Martin's bridge and
A churchtemple with a 
crucifix        and a Jewish slanted ceiling

In Toledo is
the freedom to be
and the smell of rain
on the breeze

In Toledo
streets are paved with cobblestones
and belief

In Toledo is 
God and my heart
