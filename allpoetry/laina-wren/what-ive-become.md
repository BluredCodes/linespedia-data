---
title: "What I've Become"
writer: "Laina Wren"
slug: "what-ive-become"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12403-What-Ive-Become-by-Laina-Wren"

Angel of darkness,
mother of mercy;
come down upon you
and sweep you off your feet;
The devil in disguise,
grabbing your heart;
breaking it with love,
hurtful but yet forgiving;
Angel of mercy;
take me away, to a better place;
calm my fears, save my soul,
release the devil that I've become.
Save me now, before its to late.
