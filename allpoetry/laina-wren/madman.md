---
title: "Madman"
writer: "Laina Wren"
slug: "madman"
source: "AllPoetry"

Confused, despair, lost, 
out of control, spiralling down,
unable to catch a breathe,
Screaming for help,
but no one's there, this time,
your mind's playing a game 
but there's no end, no way to stop
the maddness, searching for clue's 
down, down, down,
spiraling faster, wanting a way out;
wanting to give up, but still wanting 
to fight for life,
begging, pleading,
Anything to end the maddness,
trying to find a way out,
searching for answers,
Been given chances, but not able to cope;
Lost, alone, crying, screaming,
not wanting to be a montster in the madness,
wanting to be free,
free from it all,
the madness, the monster from within,
but it's to late I've lost what means the most to me;
I've lost you, I've lost my sanity.
