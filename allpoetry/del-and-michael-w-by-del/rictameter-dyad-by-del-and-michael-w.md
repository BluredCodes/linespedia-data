Alone
I'm free to be
Just who I want to be
No one's here to agravate me
To change me to who they want me to be
But alone is such misery
No one's here to love me
Why must I be
Alone

Love me
Just a little
I am so alone too
Everyone needs love in their life
We know it's love that makes the world go round
Days can be so long without love
My poor heart is aching
Just for your love
Love me
