title: "DANGER"
writer: "Obiwan"

Beauty in the corner
Its danger you are in
You have tugged my heart strings
And they sing a song I’ll win

Whatever your desire
I offer you sweet wine
And pleasure of the finest type
Just come with me tonight

In the sunlight you will see
The error of your move
But that will be tomorrow
And I am in the groove

You body will never sing again so loud
I will play you such a tune 
together we’ll off to reach the clouds
and head up to the moon

When sunlight sings and heralds day
Your hearts not far away
I will have your body
Your tears with you will stay

The morrow will bring you misery
No man lay by your side
I will be a dream you lived
You'll see how much I lied.

Beauty in the corner
Run away to bed
