We used to be together
One plus one was one
Now we hate forever
Who can do that sum?

Do you love another?
Can you find the time?
For if my heart has lost you
Then destiny is mine

I cannot loose a life
I could never live to win
Whatever are the choices now
My destiny is sin.

How can I live in sorrow?
I cannot live in style
My heart was won forever
But time has missed by miles

I have nothing to love with now
I have no song to sing
Just memories of the joy we shared
And a fight I’ll never win.D I V O R C E ......what else can be said.
