title: "Taint me"
writer: "dyejunkie"

An opaque mindFloating on the surface of the OceanDrifting effortlessly into blissful ignoranceWithout any perception of life's true courseThe sun glimmering on the surfaceWater trickling from waterfallsAll becoming part of life's great OceanAs the sky glows a brilliant blueYou floated in my directionStill blind to what we sawCollided into my happinessStopping my breathing, pulling me underI escape from you, swimming awayOnce again, enjoying lifes blissSharing wonderous emotions on my plainSeemingly eternity, passing like secondsYou find me once more, tricking me, fooling meSharing your emotions and luring me inHurting me once more, stabbing me in the back, pulling me under againYou tainted me...
