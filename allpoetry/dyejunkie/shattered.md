A Jack-hammer to the Skull.A Thousand worlds brought down in vane.A heart pulled out without restraint.A thousand years of pain...Wandering the plains for a helping hand,Came a thousand or more other souls.None seemed to carry a flame,To help him make a stand...Until one angelic soul did wander through,And reach out for his hand,And lit the way for hope to strive,And brought hope to his barren land...Just a dream?Or was it reality?One true moment that lasted forever,Just a memory in his mind...
