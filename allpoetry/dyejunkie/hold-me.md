title: "Hold Me?"

Dark and lonelyI lie awakeDreaming illusions of lands far awayLooking for an escape from realityI hide inside this picturebook of dreamsMerge myself into the words on the pagesDiscovering it only to be hollow, fakeSearching for a fine line between worldsI read for you, look for you, search for youScanning words accross the page, faster, fasterWhere could you be? Hidden within the unending plotI find you, amongst the adjectives, the nouns, the verbsResting, content with life's substanceI confide in you, you take me inMy saviour from this glasshouse of mysteryYou promise me wonders and magical worldsAs I fall into your heavenly wingsAs I fall into a blissful sleep I whisper...'Hold Me...?'Blah....I have no self-confidence in my poems. Please be nice, I know I suck.
