title: "Creator"
writer: "dyejunkie"

I live in the shadows, in the depths of your minds. 
I inspire your dreams and fire your soul. 
I am the water that can withstand any fire, the bridge that can always be crossed. 
I am the pawn on the Chessboard of your mind. 
I live for your emotions, your impulses, your nightmares. 
I exist only in the night, the dark. 
I am all around you and inside. 
I am whatever you want me to be. 
I am your imagination.
