title: "One night in your hell..."

Streaming...
Cleansing...
Did it ever stop?
I tried to tell you
How much you hurt me that one night...
Twisted...
Shattered...
Threatening sounds piercing my membranes
You scream at me...
Those ear breaking words
The pain untold...
That skitsophraenic moment where you broke my heart in two...
Split me...
Fractured me...
You killed me...
Clean once more.I wrote this at half 5 in the morning on Tuesday, and I'd been out on a heavy drinking session, but I was really feeling these emotions, and was pretty much sobered up by then, but really tired. I'm not sure what to take of it, I think it sums up how I felt about something happening right then...Whatd'ya think?
