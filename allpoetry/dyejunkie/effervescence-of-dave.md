I try, I seek, I....mmm...boobs. 
I long for someone to hold and love, 
To see me in the dark...naked...*drools*
I hold you, our bodies entwine,
Suck me bitch, you know you want to!
I did not mean to say that, yes I did, no I didnt...
Heatbeat, skitsophraneia of the mind...
I waited for you, so long...so far...so what?
Boobs...YAY!Written to sum up the personality of one of my best mates Dave. He thought it was pretty apt. He's a strange guy :)
