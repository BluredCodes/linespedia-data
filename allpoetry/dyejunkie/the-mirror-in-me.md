There's something weird, when I look at youSomething in me, I see in you tooLike reflection, I'm mirrored, without glass on the wallYou shine for me, glowing through it allYou show me the good, block out the badCalming my storms, whenever I get madMy idol, that pedestal I look up toYou reassure me, comfort me with complimentsI look again, and like magic I'm up in the cloudsBalancing on the pedestal with youWe lark about, and you make me smileWith so much of myself reflected from youThe mirror in meMy brother...~ For Ric ~
