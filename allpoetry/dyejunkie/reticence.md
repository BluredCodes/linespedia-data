title: "Reticence"
writer: "dyejunkie"

A broken heart with no repairA love that slowly cooled and diedThe fire inside you disappearedWhile mine painfully burns insideAs I blindly wandered in denialThat this love, this fire still burned,I did not see your feelings changeAnd now my lessons' finally learnedMy feelings have slowly torn you awayAnd you've moved on with someone newMy love an axe, and our friendship at stakeSlowly cutting the connection in twoNo brick or mortar to build a wallTo block these feelings awayIf we could stand alone on the other sideOur friendship could grow strong and sustainedIf you took my hand and trust in meAnd saw how I cared deep within my eyesThen the struggle of this and the friendship we holdIs a battle worth countless of tries.No possessions, no demands, no forceful remarksOnly the will to keep friendship togetherThough the past built these feelings, in the present you're hereThere's a place for you in my heart waiting foreverA reminder, a quote, a feeling finally understoodRole reversals, with friendship at stake'Deep inside a candle burns,A lonely love with no returns'.
