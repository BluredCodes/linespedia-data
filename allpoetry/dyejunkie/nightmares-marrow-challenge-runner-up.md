It came, out of the mist
Floating, creaking...
On the floorboards my father said he'd fix
He never did get round to doing that...

It stumbled...had it given in?
Creaking again, that deafening noise
Always made me shiver
That hand...that bony hand...

Creeping round the door frame
Clicking and cracking as the knuckles clenched
around the wooden surface
An arm...a foot, a knee, a leg.

More and more suspense...
And then...it came, it neared...
That Skeleton I so often saw
That came into my room

He crept he snook upon my floor
Watching him through a barely open eye
He leers at me...
I cant do this anymore
I jump, awaken, It was only a dream

Until...what's this?
That hand...that bony hand
Upon my door again
The same old hand that comes at night
Will my nightmare ever end?

Please no, keep back
I cant take this anymore
You've cursed me one too many a night
Am I still asleep?

You run at me, so fast, so quick
In a second you're at my side again
Until, a flash, a spark, light regains
A dream within a dream...
...no more...Hmph...whatd'ya think?
