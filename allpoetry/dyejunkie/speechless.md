What to say when you can't make sounds
Where to turn when you don't know how
Floods of thought with no release
Need to move but still deceased
Fighting...
Screaming for release...
Break me from this prison cell
My own man-made natural cage
Holding me back
Break me, smash me, release me...
I've been in here so long...
