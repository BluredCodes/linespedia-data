Blinding light
Gloomful darkness
All meet to grey
When ultimately challenged

The blue-green sea
and the grey-black sky
eventually meet at the horizon
Or do they?

Supremely seperated by some ancient curse?
Human intervention was never enough,
Have we done too much? Too little?
Perception never was our greatest virtue.

Good...
Evil...
What is right or wrong?
With truths like these who needs lies?

A million colours collide beneach the stars
Flowing and merging together
Brilliant, glowing, radient colours,
still eventually all fade to black.I wrote thus while watching my girlfriend paint, when she mentioned I always seem to relate everything to habving a good and an evil side. Colours can show a lot about a person :)
