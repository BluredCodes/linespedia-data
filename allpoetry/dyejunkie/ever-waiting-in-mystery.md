Waiting
In silence for you
...no noise...
The air whistling down the lane
As the cars run silent
Like hover-mobiles from some distant sci-fi movie
Beam me up Scotty
To the precipice on the hill
Where I sit and watch the sun go down
Thinking of you...
Silence always helped the mind connect
With thoughts I never thought possible
Thoughts of you, of fantasy...
Dragons and Raccoons running wild...
Mages and Sages casting profound spells on the landscape
Leaving a profound mark on mother natures soil
A mark within my soul
Burnt deep by the black burning fire in your soul
You marked me
Marked me with the trademark of your illusions
And I wait...
