title: "End of an Era"
writer: "dyejunkie"

Shadowy lights descend upon my room
The yellowing sun deepens the dimensions of the objects
For a moment, everything seems clear, concise, meaningful

The curtains close and clarity fades
My minds illusions become reality
The light fades as I trap out the outside world
Leaving myself alone...alone

I think of all the times we had
The laughs, the loves, the strife’s
It all came to an end like the slip of a knife
Now the heartache begins, and the healing process must take place

There are things which help, assist in my aid
Distractions from life, myself, and the outside world
They all take some time, cheating the system
How long before I get caught?

Clouds fall out of the sky
Angels scream in pain
People walk round backwards...
