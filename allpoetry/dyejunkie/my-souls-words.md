Rambling
Did I stop to think?
Could I really describe
how my soul yearns for a description
Silver sparkles
White whispy streamers
A celestial masquerade in my mind
Was anyone invited?
What would it have to say?
Useless chit chat
Party humour
Paint me on a blank canvas
Colours merge
They form an illusion
Undescribable...
Majestical...
So awe inspiring...
...no words...
