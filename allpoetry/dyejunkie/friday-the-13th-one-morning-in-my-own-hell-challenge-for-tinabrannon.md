I wake...correction...I fall 
I stumble off the floor with a bump on my head 
Like a long fortold Mr. Men episode 
Go ahead and call me Mr. Bump why dont you? 

Where's that damn kitchen? 
Everythings a blur! 
The cold floor ~ t i n g l e s ~ at my toes 
I cant find those damn slippers again... 
There they are...all ripped and torn... 
I'll murder that dog... 

I drink my coffee... 
Making me feel particulary perky inside... 
The one thing that might help me through 
Caffiene...mmm... 

"To the bathroom!" I command myself 
I trip and tread with two left feet 
I finally find my way to the mirror 
A frighteningly bad hair day! 

I do what I can to fix that bush someone has placed on my head 
I wonder if the Barber shop stocks shears? 
Where did that leaf come from? 
Just WHAT did I get up to last night?? 

I have an amazing fear of stepping outside... 
Everything is just going wrong... 
I slowly open the fridge thinking, "Breafast can't go wrong" 
Can it? 

Until...We're out of cheese...hehe! True story!
