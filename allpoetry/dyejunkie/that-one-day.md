A whisper......faintly screamed...Expectations of wonderous glamourTorn apart by a single tear*drip...drop*They fall...creating a see of painYou tell me to be happy...smile you sayToday is a special day...your dayMY day?Surely there are more than me...Are there? Souls trapped in one place, one time...aloneWhere is their day? What made me so special? Am I even supposed to be here?Don't say those mind piercing words...That killer punchline, familiar phrase...Tomorrow is always a let downBut today is always good to endAnd yesterday begins to seem like not such a bad dealOn my birthday...Please don't say happy birthday. I hate that line. You may tell I don't like my birthday. I don't know why...
