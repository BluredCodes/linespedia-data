Darkened clouds surround the night sky
Mixing with purples and blues and greys...
The soft twlinking of the stars shining down
Was this a warning of something to come?
A celestial milestone of my mortality?
This was too much to handle...
A blur in front of my tear ridden eyes...
The drip, drip, drip of blood down my arm
Poorly disguised by the fall of glimmering raindrops
I knew that bandage wouldn't hold...
Dizzy from the pain...
Mental stimulus overcome with flesh torn wounds
Slipping into my own dazed dream...
Reality.......dream......combine....
Until it flashes...
The blinding light of a 12 wheel truck
Steamrunning its way in my direction
As if my torment wasn't enough
My body gives in....I collapse....

Black..........................
Silence..........................
Too quiet to be a dream....
Too real to be a memory...
Until I awake
Slowly the blurry image clears
Who could that be standing over me...?
*Haze*
My head throbbing from the hit with the floor
My self intented wound sealed and bandaged
My face sticky with long dried tears
I cant be? Am I?
The doctor agrees...
Suicide has a strange sense of irony
Don't you just hate it when that happens...?I wish this wasn't a true story, but sometimes life deals a bad card. I guess this was one of mine...:S Love to all xXx
