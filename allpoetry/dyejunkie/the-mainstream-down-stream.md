title: "The mainstream down stream"
writer: "dyejunkie"

Riding off everyone elses waves
Never creating my own
Always felt like an effort without exertion
Maybe once I could stand on my own?
Do my own thing? 
Just maybe once I could produce my own worth
Create my own waves for people to ride
My own man-made messiah
Leading myself on the path I am creating
Where to next?
I decide.
Just me.
