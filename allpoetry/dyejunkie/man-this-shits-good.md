title: "Man, this shit's good!"

Whats this I'm being passed?
      Some new, old, borrowed shit..
How much can I drag at once? 
   Chug! Chug! Chug!
Jeez man this shit is strong!

   Did the world just turn upside down?
      Am I having a nervous breakdown?
   Fuck man, I think I'm going blind
This stuff is 'da bomb!'

   So I'm broke and out of luck
     'Least I know how to live
   Toking seems to remove life's bliding edge
and gives that boring old scene a colourful re-master

   Painting by numbers on the canvas of my life
     Like in school when I never was too good
   being sent to corner for being bad
Man, I was a rebel in those days...

   ...B l i n k i n g...
     Did I really see that?
   A walking...breathing, penis?
Freud could analyse that for years!
     No way, I can't have seen that!
   Or did i?

   If you had eyes in the back of your head
     You'd notice me frowning at you from behind
For thinking I am insane
   Stereotypical monsters passing by...

   Lets invent a sport
     Ooh, Ooh, I got an idea
Animal Sports!
   Hitting a Pidgeon with a cricket ball!
      Badminton with mice!

   We should write these ideas down...
     They're so inspiring...
But after all, I realise, they're not...
   Are they?
No...they're not...
     Man this shit's good!Weird huh?
