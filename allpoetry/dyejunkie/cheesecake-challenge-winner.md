Yellow...
Like the sun
It shines for me
My delicious friend
Brightening up my day
With your radient taste and flavour
The brown of the base
Like an autumn afternoon 
Watching the rain outside on the trees
Inside with comfort
And of course, Cheesecake
I tear you, rip you, shred you
Yet you feel no pain
As I impale you
Lock you into my burning prison
You melt, dissolve, burn "alive"
I taste you
Lapping up the effervescence you diffuse into me
You fill me up
Erotic desire, pleasurable sensation
I fall back into a blissful content
On cloud nine with my ecstacy
For you...
My Cheesecake :~)Can you tell I like Cheesecake?
