title: "The Real Me"

I cant be nothing but honest
I am scared that i will die alone, un loved
I live by my emotions and gut instincts
I couldn’t live without my friends being there
I’m scared of the darkness within me
I cry for no reason when it surfaces
I long to be eternally happy
and I cry because I know I will never reach it.
