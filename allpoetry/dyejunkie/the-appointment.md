A hallway...
Lined with tope wallpaper...
They always said that soothes the mind...
Click....clock....click....clock...
The sounds of my nervous feet walking the hallways
Floods of thoughts in my mind
Almost drowning in anticipation
As I grow closer...closer still...
Time stricken doctors running around with flipcharts and frowns...
Running through alternate scenes in my mind...
I could turn, and run...no one would know...
Possibly hide from the world
Just for a while, until this was over...
Do I really have to do this?
Is it foretold in my stars?
An ancient mystery destined to occur
Until I reach the door
It opens...
It was that time again...
