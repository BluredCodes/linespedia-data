Here you are
Back again
You never seem to let me down
Always standing by
Waiting for that moment to happen 
When I hit the ground...
Shattering my dreams of happiness
on the cold concrete floor...
No comforting words needed
Only your softness
I cry into you...
Clench you...
Hold you...
Pull you in...
It's never enough
So it never ends.
Never...
So it continues,
seemingly endless...
But you are always there for me
That same old familair friend...
