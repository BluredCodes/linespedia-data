Calling all passengersThis is your Captain speakingWelcome to flight 501Your free flight for all those who save their air miles.Special offers, savings count.Add them up, and take a trip.Did I work out my math wrong?Excuse me Sir, I'm 27 short...Okay, so I'm poor.and you wish I was there.I guess I didn't save enoughTo catch that free flight over.I sit and think about the chance I missedThe chance to talk to a voice, a face, a feeling.Not just words, digits, pixels.Damn that flight 501.We've only known a short time, yet, you feel so close within me.We've shared so much and talked so little,it's hard to think you aren't mine.So we clash? Who cares? I'm sure heaven has Bumper CarsEating cheesecake in the celestial Internet CafeI'm sure we'd have a blast.So, yeah...it's...vague...What was i talking about again?Your heart; my life source,your words, my bibleI guess I better get planningthose fries don't eat themselveseach hundred an air mile12 million to go...Calling all passengers,Welcome to flight 749.Special Guest today,a long lost friend, Jay. ~ For Louise ~Hope you like it :)
