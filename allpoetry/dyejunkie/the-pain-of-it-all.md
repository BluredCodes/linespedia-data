title: "The pain of it all..."

Tears...
Falling a crimson red...
Rising to the surface with a flash of colour
Meeting with the shimmer of the blade
It's deep
Man, that hurts...
I dont regret this...I need this...
I'm so scared I cant stop this...
I'm dizzy...
Lost within my pain, my angst, my sadness...
The pain of it all...True story, go figure...
