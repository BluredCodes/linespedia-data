A tear...
Falling down my face to the floor...
Soaked up into the carpet and fogotten about

A tear...
Evaporating into the air...
Taken up to the sky for all the world to see

A tear...
Merging with others in a great big cloud...
Raining down my negetivity upon the world

A tear...
Falling upon your face...
It's raining...
