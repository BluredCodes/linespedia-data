title: "Under the moonlight..."

A flickering light shines from the moon
Through the windows and over your bed
The glimmering of fairy lights around your window
flash and sparkle as celestial buckets unleash water upon the world
You reach out for me, through the dimming light
Your eyes aglaze with the glowing energy

You take me in your arms, and kiss me softly
The soft scent of your hair surrounds me as I drift into your heavenly kiss
My skin shivvers with your exhuberance
as I hold you gently close to my soul
Feeling the mental and physical pull towards you...
So content...
So free...
So linked to you...
While we dance under the moonlight.
