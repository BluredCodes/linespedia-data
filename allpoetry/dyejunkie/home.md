title: "Home...?"
writer: "dyejunkie"

Wondering where to go
Which direction to choose
Dreaming if I'll ever win
Hoping I didn't already lose

I sit and think about my life
About the future and the past
Whether there's a count on mistakes I've made
and how long its due to last

Sometimes I can console myself
and be thankful for the gifts I have learned
Some nights I sit and cry till dawn
Have I already crashed and burned?

Maybe in time these feelings will pass
and lift me from my downhill slope
I can rise above, and achieve my goals
and my life will find new hope.
