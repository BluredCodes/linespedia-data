It's always nice to think of hope
That feeling we get inside
It buries itself within our cage
Worms its way through our entirety

It's nice to find a ray of light
In all this darkness that clouds the mind
No longer bumping against the sides,
with a light to guide your way again.

It's nice to think that people care
They can guide you, mould you, assist in your pain
Like a Nicotine patch for your sadness
Weening you off the negetivity

It's nice to think your feelings matter
To know your opinions count
That you actually make a difference somwehere
And have touched someones life...even ever so slightly.

It's nice to think of hope sometimes...

- To everyone thats ever been there for me, mostly Tina, The guys that live by me (my friends), Cheeky (Lou my friend in Australia), my brother Ric who always seems to be there for me when he can and always helps, and anyone else I havn't mentioned.
