---
title: "Fear of Heights"
writer: "dyejunkie"
slug: "fear-of-heights"
source: "AllPoetry"

On the edge
Which way to fall?
Left? right? Forward? Backwards?
Seeming like every way at once
Simultaneously falling nowhere
Trapped within a hidden invisible room
Boundaries closing in and shutting out air
Choking, suffocating, drowning in this space
Almost wishing for the fall
Dreaming of hitting the ground
Breaking, smashing the boundaries
Should I stay?
Suffocating and drowning?
Or should I let myself fall?
Lessening in height...
Images closing in on my retina
Life flashing before my mind
Everything seems so good for one slight moment
Until...the end...
