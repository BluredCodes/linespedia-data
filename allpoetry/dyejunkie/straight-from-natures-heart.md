Like a tree fixed deep
Is my love for you
If you stand by me
I’ll be here, always, for you

Every drop of the ocean
Cannot amount to my love for you
I will love you still and forever
No matter what we go through

On the cooling wind
I can hear you
I’m calling out for you
Can you hear me too?

To the end of time
I’ll be here for you
I can see your eyes
In the sky so blue

Skin smooth as the softest stone
As silk and soft to touch
Ever since the day we met
I always felt as such

You are the world in which I live in
And the air that I need to breathe
I will love you forever and always
