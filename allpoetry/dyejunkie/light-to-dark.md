title: "Light to Dark"

Light shines upon a searing blade
Blinding light, reflective as a million stars
A rip, a tear, a flow of red
Light blinded by a cloud of pain
A dam burst with intent
A smother of rouge masking any clarity of thought
Steel blades stained by un-cleansing liquid
Life giving air, unable to assist, drying the sticky mass
Light turns to darkness, day to night
Fires within a star blown out without salvation
Self-infliction, a murderous intent on the mind
No more...
