Those words I whispered
You hate to hear
They stab at your insides
and Pull at your hair

You wanted to love me
You need me so much
To look in my eyes
And need me to touch

Those tears keep on falling
Eternally slow
Death's shadow keeps stalking
Do you think I should go?

I'm not making any sense
These words I cant say...
I'm so sorry its over
Will you forgive me one day?:'(
