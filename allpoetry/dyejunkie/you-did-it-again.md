title: "You did it again"

You did it againChanging subjects to ease the painLosing my grip of reality never seemed so easyDid I fall?You did it againMoulding my opinion of you with a knifeThe blade cutting deep within me Was i fooled?You did it againStopping my heart with one fell swoopCrushing me with the weight of ignoranceDid I suffocate?You did it againHiding your truths with a dark cloakMysteries untold, the pages left blankWere you hiding? You did it again...This was just a train of thought that hit me suddenly. I wrote this in about a minute. It may be awful, it may not be, I'll let you decide...
