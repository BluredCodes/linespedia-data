Breathing seemed so easy
Until he stopped to feel the air
Suffocating in his thoughts
Forgetting to take a breath...

A dark cloud descends
It smothers out any light from his view
All hope of resurrection turned to dust

Itching wounds and stabbing pains
His energy fades and his chest tightens
No sense of control
A dizzying fall for eternity
Trapped in a bleak world

He sees light at the end
Falling effortlessly into a blinding energy
A brilliant white sea below him
Is this the beginning of the end...?

Just maybe?
