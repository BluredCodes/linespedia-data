Blah, you said
Blah, you screamed at me
Killing my ear drums
Blah, you looked at me
Blah, contact made
Burning your image into my retina

Did it all have to be this way?
Medusa, some ancient prophecy re-lived
You lured me in, again, too many times
It hurts...
