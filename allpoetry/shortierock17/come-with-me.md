I look into your eyes, 
I am affraid of what you could do.
I want to give you my heart, but you might abuse it.
I love you now,
I hate yo then, 
I am scared of you and what you would do!
How is it that i can trust you?
I have heard you have a past,
You have a history,
My love for you will always be a mistory.
I want you to hold me,
And tell me you care,
Just once, tell me wat we had will always be there!
I need you now,
Forge about them,
Come with me!
