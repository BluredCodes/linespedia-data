If you are such good luck,
Then why have i had none?
The luck i have had has been bad!
Everything i truly want i never get.
Don't bring me any luck, just bring me happiness!
Give me what no one else has,
Take what no one will,
Think what everyone else doesn't,
Listen when everyone else gives up.
Don't worry about what people think,
Believe what you think is true!
Don't be affraid, But i must say that i am too,
We will get through this together you and me "BUTTERFLY"
