I thought we were friends,
I trusted you with my life.
You stabbed me in the back ,
More than twice.
I don't need you know,
I didn't need you then,
But why did you pretend to be my best friend?
You knew how much i cared for him,
You knew how i felt,
But that didn't matter,
You only worried about yourself.
You slut, You bitch, You whore,
You mean nothing to me anymore!
