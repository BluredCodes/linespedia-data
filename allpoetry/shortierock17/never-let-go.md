---
title: "Never Let Go"
writer: "ShortieRock17"
slug: "never-let-go"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26551-Never-Let-Go-by-ShortieRock17"

I look at you and get happy inside.
I can't wait to see your everyday,
I have discovered i have feelings for you,
could you ever share them too?
I long for your hugs and your time,
but deep down i know you can't be mine.
You probably don't feel for me like that,
and i probably won't ever know,
but untill then i will keep my feelings, and never let go!
