Blue skys are black,
Sunny days are cloudy,
Warm nights seem so cold now,
Birds stopped flying,
Waterfalls stopped flowing.
We were so happy!
What happened?
Life came to a stop when you left.
You left me here in the dark all alone.
How could you have not even turned and looked back?
Your heart left in my hands,
Just dumped on me, cold and life less, left with no use!
Why did you leave me? 
Why did you go, but most of all, 
Why did you leave your "life" behind?
