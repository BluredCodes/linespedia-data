title: "Just How Much"

Where are you, Where could you be?
Where ever you are, are you still thinking about me?
Your warm hugs, your sweet smile.
I must admit,
Everything about you drives me wild.
Your gentle voice and soothing touch,
I wish you were here to comfort me.
I long for your kisses or just for you to hold me,
How did i get myself in all this?
Where did you come from, and why to me?
How come everything i want can never be?
If only i told you how much you meant, would things be different?
