title: "forever, always"

What just happened?
All this time making me think?
What am i supposed to think now?
What did we just do, something you'll regret?
Did you men what you said?
Can it really be true?
I have waited so long to hear those words from you!
All this time, who would have knew?
I wasn't alone.
I am releaved but at the same time scared!
I wasn't expecting it not in a million years...not from you!
The tears won't stop flowing,
the blood still racing,
How could something so right feel so wrong?
Where do we go from here? 
What are we supposed to do if these feeling we are having are true?
So many years have came and went but not the time we have spent!
Those times will stay in my heart foever and always,
Even if your feelings go astray. No matter what happens i will love you forever, always.
