Things may not look right,
your life looks like it's falling apart.
Nothing is going your way,
The end seems so far and to long.
You have made some mistakes,
You have done wrong.
When you look in the mirror,
You don't know who you see.
You don't know why your here, or what it is your supposed to do.
I think your here for me,
And for as long as you are here,
Don't worry, Cause whatever you do,
I'll never stop loving you!
