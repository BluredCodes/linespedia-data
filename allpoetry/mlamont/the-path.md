---
title: "The Path"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "the-path"

Come with me down this troubled path
Forget what you know 
and what you see.

For pain is all you have.

You take from me 
What I need
Knowing i'll never have.

I never knew this road
could have so many forks
Which way could I go
Which way should I choose?

I know if I am guided 
in the wrong direction
my pain will not 
make the world stop.

But in my agony
I cry out to the stars
in hope that they will 
somehow answer my questions

I lie awake at night
dreaming of the time 
that this will all go away

The world will never stop,
the stars will never answer
the path never ends
And I will always be here.
