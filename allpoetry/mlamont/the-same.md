---
title: "The Same"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "the-same"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Remember the times
when we were all so blind
The future was so uncertain

We left what we knew
had nothing to prove
walking into a bold new world

Four years have passed
Your future like glass
So fragile, but very clear.

You've got your own place
and soon you will face
all the things you put aside.

Before you could tell
In love you had fell
And nothing would be the same.

And nothing compares
to that one day you swear
When your child came into this world

And now that their gone
to start what you long
had forgotten.

You start to realize
your days are at end
and you've lost, your best friend.

And with all of your hope
you look at the light
and suddenly you fade away.

You look back at your life
and with so much strife,
realize it went all to fast.

But thats just how it goes
With friends and with foes,
you all face the same direction.

But before i am done
i'll look back to the sun
and kiss all my worries behind.

I'll know all that I did
and where I have been.
before I fade away.

I'll put grudges behind
and peace I will find.
My heart will never lay to rest.

And though I'm gone
They will sing this song
in hope that they will be 
the same.
