---
title: "I remember"
writer: "mlamont"

I remember the first night we met.
Your blue eyes shining at me.
Your curly hair almost inticing me
to run my fingers through it.

We laughed, and drank
and became more and more cozy 
throughout the night.

And when I went to sleep,
you quietly followed me.

From that moment on,
it was all a blur.

Even when you went away
I had Carolina on my mind.
I longed for you touch,
your sweet blue eyes.

But it all came to an end,
the day our trust was betrayed.

My heart was in your hands,
and you squeezed ever so tight, 
scarring it, for others.

I remember I was never quite the same
I remember your tears that fell.
I remember how I felt I told you 
I remember how I was angry out of spite.

I remember how I said I would never love you again.
But I remember how I ever so lied.
