title: "She"

She comes into the roomEyes sparklingHer smile shiningLike diamonds in a field of rocksHer heart warmHer intentions innocentOne could do nothing elsebut fall in loveHer curls wrap around my fingerlike her love wraps around my heartAnd when you look in her eyesit is like swimming a thousand oceansAnd although many people see her walkI see her glide past Like an angel who has not yet received her wingsShe is the fire that warms my soulShe coarses my veinsShe knows there is no otherShe is.
