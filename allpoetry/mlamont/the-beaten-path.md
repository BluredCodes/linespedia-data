---
title: "The Beaten Path"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "the-beaten-path"

Once again, I am down this path
No one seems right, not this one 
Nor the last.

Where will she be?
When will I find her?
I'm so tired of searching.

I thought I had found her,
In her blue eyes and 
captivating touch.

But like all the others,
she is a memory.

What is it that I've done wrong?
I know I deserve better then this.
All this weary heart needs 
is a little sqeeze, or a little hug.

My heart is on my sleeve,
but i've heard there's nothing wrong with that.
Then why am I here alone,
dreaming, and wishing.

The new one is so wonderful
with her captivating eyes 
and her cute laugh,
and even the way
her hair so urgently falls
across her face,
waiting for me to push it back.

But will she be a memory?
Will she lead me down this beaten path?

As of now, my heart tells me no.
But when I stand corrected,
my head will tell me so.
