---
title: "Yours nor Mine"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "yours-nor-mine"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23719-Yours-nor-Mine-by-mlamont"
---

For all the reasons
and the treasons
we have committed.

For everything this world 
has seen
and everything you never mean
and all the things that turns the world
down to this beating heart.

If countries where gone
and days were long
would are hearts still bleed the same?

And if all you save
really makes 
the person you are,
then make sure its on my grave.

Remember the reasons
and the seasons
that are ever changing

And remember in time, 
that this world was never yours, 
nor mine

And soon we will all find it 
slipping away....
