---
title: "This Watered One"
writer: "mlamont"

Who am I 
on this watered one
out of nine planets
we our chosen from.

But if the cities crumbled
and rivers dried up
and the sky turned black
would our hearts still rightly bleed?

In our universe
we are a blink of an eye
then why is so hard
when one of us dies?

Our pain, our suffering
can never compare 
to the joy and love
we should rightly share

I believe that love
does make this earth go round
and I also believe its tough
to be alive.

But in all our faith
and in all our pain
we should feel safe
without disdain.

Who am I 
on this watered one?
just one more life
in a blink of an eye.
