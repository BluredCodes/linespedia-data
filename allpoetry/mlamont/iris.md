As I climb the stars,
I get lost in their sparkle.
And as the heavens part, 
the brightness blinds me

The colors are so beautiful 
and full.
They pierce me with a sudden 
emotion.

Its like I am swimming 
but I am not. 
Its like I'm lost in different world 
But I am not. 

Everything rushes into me
And I am so overloaded with 
love and emotion

That I realize that I am
only looking into your eyes.
