---
title: "Two Similiar Hearts"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "two-similiar-hearts"

Two similiar hearts
eventually coming together.
Because of one soul that binds them,
all three share a soul.

The risk is great,
three sharing a soul.
Somehow, it ends up as only two.

The love of two people can be great,
But when one loves both lovers more then himself,
it can be tragic.

The two hearts drawn together
ignore his frightful pleas
who know's where the path may go,
she says to him with ease

He does not love her in such a way,
where you would think he wants her.
But he does love her in such a way,
where she is simply is friendship soulmate.

Walking in his mind, and touching his heart,
she knows everything there is to know.
And in times of sadness, and regret,
she is there without a doubt.

And he knows, with everything in his heart
she will be there forever
they will never be apart.

And he does not love him in such a way, 
where you would think anything else
But he does love him in such a way,
where he is his best friend, for life...
till death.

They have shared many things, the two male souls,
and will undoubtly share much more.
But when the two similiar hearts came drawing near,
things have changed, and things have grown.

And though it might seem that there is jeolousy in the air,
it is more of caring, and worrysome 
that hangs heavy in his heart.

For if he ever lost these two similiar hearts,
to anger or despair,
Then he would lose apart of himself,
and everything he knows.
