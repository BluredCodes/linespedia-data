title: "For Sharon"
writer: "mlamont"

High up in the courts of heaven today
A little dog angel waits;
with the other angels she will not play
but she sits alone at the gates,
"For I know my Sharon will come," says she,
"and when she comes she will for me"

And with Sharon on the earth below,
As she sits in her computer chair
She forgets sometimes, and she whistles low
For the dog that is not there;
And the little dog angel cocks her ears
And dreams that her Sharon's call's she hears.

And I know that at last when her Sharon calls,
Outside, in the dark and cold
For the hand of death to open the gates
that lead to those courts of gold
The little dog angels' eager bark
Will comfort Sharon's soul in the shivering dark.
