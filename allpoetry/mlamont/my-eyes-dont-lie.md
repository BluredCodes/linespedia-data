---
title: "My Eyes Don't Lie"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "my-eyes-dont-lie"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21726-My-Eyes-Dont-Lie-by-mlamont"
---

She thinks in silence,
As her world falls apart around her.
She is trapped in my mindless game.

I wonder if she will ever come around
My heart is in her hands
ever so bleeding

She looks at me 
with her stoic eyes,
Saying everything,
but nothing at all.

She dreams in colors,
She dreams for me
But what is it 
That I can't see?

Everything has gone to far
Everything is closing in
She tries to pull me
Deep within.

With all she knows
and all she has,
She picks her things up
and steps on the gas

I watch her leave
with my stoic eyes
and I can't believe
I'm living these lies.

My heart in her hand
Her weight on my shoulders
What was once would shine
has turned to sand

As I turn away 
from all of my troubles
She enters my mind
and I am humbled

With tearful bliss 
I remember that day
we shared one last kiss
The day my stoic eyes
went away.
