title: "Her  Emerald Sea"

The strands of her hair
Fall about her face
Her green eyes,
like an emerald sea.

When she looks at me
she looks into my soul
And her smile 
fills my heart.

When she moves
she glides
and when she talks
she sings

Like an angel
that has fallen.

Her skin like a pearl
Her touch like the stars
glows in my heart.

When we embrace
angels fall.

She is my friend
She is apart of me.

She is my angel...
