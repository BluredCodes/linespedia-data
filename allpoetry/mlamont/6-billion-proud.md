---
title: "6 Billion Proud"
writer: "mlamont"

Down to the ground
these walls come down
With 6 billion proud
Using one voice.

We are all differant 
in color, in creed,
in mind
But our hearts are 
all the same.

Sing with me 
through these troubled times
Sing with me
just one more time

Sing for you
and sing for us
Just sing on
until we are all 
turned to dust

Yesterday we were just
Today we never thought we could
But tomorrow in the hands of time
We live on in the sunrise

And for all the life
and for all the dying
With just one voice
we could all be fighting

Forget your gods
and forget your science
Just come with me
and we will make this our world.

Never lost, but never found
we are the middle
We never thought 
we could be 6 billion proud.
