Everything is here
But too many things are gone
There is one thing I fear
And it is all that I long

The words I couldn't say
The sadness in your eyes
Like a cold winters day
Everything has died

If only you were here
I would have come so far
In my heart your dear
Your my sun, your my star

A thousand heartbeats couldn't compare
To that one moment of love
The touch of your skin, the smell of your hair
You were my angel, you were my dove
