---
title: "Once Again"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "once-again"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It has been three years
since I last saw your face.
Since one of my last tears
fell, with your sweet embrace.

And here you are again
coming back into my life.
I'm not the man I used to be
For then it wasen't right.

All the memories,
all the times,
all the kisses,
and all the shine
that was in your eyes
came back to me all at once.

That distinct smell of your skin
that I have so longed for.
I left you in strife,
but here we are again
perhaps starting a new life.

I wish we were older then
we met at such a wrong time.
But three years later
we are doing just fine.

So we take it from here
with hope and with love
three years later
three years older
three years with love.
