title: "The dock"

As I sit here in this cold dark night
I long for your willful touch.
And even though you are far away
I still love you that much.

Your eyes were my sun
Your soul was my moon
and even though we are done
I know I'll see you soon

The sparkle in your eyes
like the stars in the sky
on that night on the dock
i knew you were mine

You were my everything
My first, my last
when the cold wind sings
I think of you.

I never thought it would come to this
your sweet kiss was like sweet bliss

And even though your far away
I will think of you everday.
