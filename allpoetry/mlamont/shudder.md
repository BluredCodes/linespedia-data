ShudderNow that your goneMy head’s in my handsAll I can wish for is a glanceOf your face.So many times I’ve criedSo many times we’ve triedI want out this placeBut all I can do is sit and waitFor you.Cause ICan’t believeThat weUsed to be.The times are gone and now I longFor the touch of your skin, the look on your face,The shine in your eyes that gives me a place,To stay.So many things said and doneSo many things that you have wonHow can I believe againHow can I breathe againWithout you.So here we are againStaring face to faceI wish it could have beenBut now its out of place09-21-01, any thing will be helpful
