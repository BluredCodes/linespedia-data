---
title: "Leo's Song"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "leos-song"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I came home cryin tonight
The rain wont stop 
beating against the window pane

All those years of never calling
and all those tears that fell
When Leo went away

He came home cryin last night
I've never seen the way 
he looked before.

The look in his eyes
told me that he never
had a chance to say goodbye.

My father told me son,
just look away, and
keep your tears at bay

For all those years of never calling 
and for all your tears that fall
Please remember
that we almost had it all.

She came home cryin tonight.
She swore she'd never talk 
to him again.

But when she saw his face
all her love and hate
faded, like the years 
that passed by.

As she turned away
she kept her tears at bay
and she swore, it was the coldest
day in June, she's ever known.

For all those years of never calling
and for all her tears that fell.
She realized it all meant nothing
in the end.

The day, that Leo went away.
