---
title: "Black and White"
writer: "mlamont"
slug: "black-and-white"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23757-Black-and-White-by-mlamont"
---

For what it is
I can no longer see
My mind is empty 
my hope is lost

I wake up day in and day out
the hours lost 
the hours past

When I dream
it is in color

But somehow my days
are in black and white

For what can do this to me?
A love lost proves much of this to thee.

Many have come, and many have gone.
But this one, I swear, was the one.

When she looked in my eyes
it was almost with a sigh
and for whatever reason
she is no longer mine.

She was my someone
she was my sea
I thought I could 
protect her,
from all that I could see.

I imagine where she is
on this black and white day
wishfulling thinking
that this grey would go away.

But now I realize 
that the world never stops
for a broken heart.

But time seemed to stop
when I gazed in her magical eyes.
And with every beat
my heart would sigh.

Soon I will forget 
this heartache and pain.
And my days will return to color,
This will never be in vain.

Wherever she is
and whatever she does
my dreams will still be in color
and my days in black and white.
