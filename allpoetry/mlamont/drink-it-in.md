title: "Drink it in"

Out of the cold 
and into my head.
I just can't remember
what it was you said.

If you keep the door open
you'll know i'll be around
When your eyes gaze upon me,
it is everything I've found.

Kiss me, wont you kiss me love
and I'll slip right into your heart.
Even if I should go
It would be no big suprise

I dont like to hear I'm wrong
come with me down this path
And though you dont see it 
Our love is an unfinished song.

With all your beauty to drink in, 
I bring you water 
In hope for exchange for your wine.

I would drink it in, 
and never spill a drop
I promise you.

When the day is done, 
and your heart is empty,
just think of me 
and fill your heart.
