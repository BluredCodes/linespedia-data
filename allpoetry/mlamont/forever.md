---
title: "Forever"
writer: "mlamont"

---

For all the thoughts I've been thinking
I can see your without a sense of feeling
I can feel our ship is sinking
And here I am before you kneeling

Many things we've been through
When you went away you took a piece of me
Not many people have the effect that you do
But if you come back, then love you will see

That in your heart I'll be
the only one you'll need
You can take me everywhere
my loneliness I just can't bear

Be my forever

The day you left 
my whole world came down
And when I slept
I dreamed you where found

You know you can't escape my thoughts
And just for this I bring my heart
So everytime the cold wind blows
and everytime in december it snows

You'll be my forever.

The other day I thought I saw you
But it was just a vision,
a dream, a fantasy

But then the cold wind blew.
and I swore I saw snow.
And there you were.

My forever.
