I fear I'm falling
The sky is falling

But will you be there to stop it
Will you stay till the end.

We think the sky is falling
And i hear the end is calling
What will you do, what will it prove
The rain it never stops
The rain, it never stops

The sky is falling down
I see myself, but can't be found
The tears fall like rain
Inside of me, I feel the pain

To many choices one by one they pass
Everything is endless, but in the end will it last?
How will you stop it
How will you see
Things are different now
But everything stays the same.

Five senses reeling
One left to be found
Somehow I'm feeling you wont be around
These times we see must be real
But what is it that we can't feel?

I fear I'm falling
The sky is falling
