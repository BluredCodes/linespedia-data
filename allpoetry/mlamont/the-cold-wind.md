It cuts through me like 
cold wind in december.

With all I have I embrace it
And it takes so much 
to hold on to.

Even with all my strength
It sometimes slips away

How can I shiver,
but at the sametime,
have my heart melt?

Forget about what I said
soon you will see
that in time, 
it all slips away

But remember this moment
and when you feel yourself slipping
take a walk in your mind

And remember what we did.

Remember how you made me shiver
and how you made my heart melt.

And when a cold day comes
and you have no one to turn to
hopefully I will be on your mind
and bring warmth to your face.

Because I know 
when the cold wind blows
somehow, 
my heart will melt away the pain.
