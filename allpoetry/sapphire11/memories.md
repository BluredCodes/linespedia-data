Moments in time fall like tears on flowers,
delicate blossoms bowed
with the weight of emotions,
fleeting and distant.

Like a falling star fading into velvet blackness,
losing itself in the moonlight,
pieces of my soul still smolder silently 
in the spaces in-between.
