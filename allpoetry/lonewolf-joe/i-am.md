---
title: "I am"
writer: "lonewolf joe"

I am the wind, I am the rain
I am your pleasure and your pain
I am the one you seek in time of need
with no one to turn to, your wishes I heed
I watch you do both wrong and right 
whether helping others or picking a fight
I love who you are despite what you do
don't be surprised it's not something new
I forgive what you do to me and my own
accept me not and you'll ne'er see my home
I am the ocean and the sea
I am in you and all that you see
mean as a lion, gentle as a lamb
you ask my name; I AM THAT I AM
