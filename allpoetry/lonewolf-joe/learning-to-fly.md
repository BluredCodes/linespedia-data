---
title: "Learning to Fly"
writer: "lonewolf joe"

Today is the day all my questions are answered
No more play 'til this simple chore is done
This is a task that for so long was deferred
Today is the day , after so many settings of the sun
I climb out of my home and walk out to the ledge
Looking down, it's twenty feet to the ground
If I fall it'll be into that brambled hedge
and I'll be dead if ever my body is found
But I have to jump, I have to fly
If I don't I'll surely die
So at the edge of the branch I stand
And when I jump I plummet towards land
I close my eyes as the wind goes by
And ever slowly I spread my wings 
When my eyes open, I'm in the sky
Below me are many tiny things
I faced my fears, and I have won
I'm flying now 'neath the noonday sun
