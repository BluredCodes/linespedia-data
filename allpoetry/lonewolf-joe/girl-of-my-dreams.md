---
title: "Girl of my dreams"
writer: "lonewolf joe"
slug: "girl-of-my-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48725-Girl-of-my-dreams-by-lonewolf-joe"
---

Who knows what dreams may come
In the time which lacks the sun
Dreams of pleasure, those of pain
Those of love, or just insane
The best dreams that I have at night
Are when I'm holding you so tight
Underneath the moon and stars
Upon a hill above the cars
So high up we'd see the world
Like land and sea for us unfurled
But in this world we seem alone
We need not ever use a phone
To see each other we close our eyes 
We need not ever say good byes
You lean close and whisper "I love you"
But my alarm goes off 'fore I say "me too"
Alas it was another dream
Things aren't always what they seem
One day I'll dream of you again
I don't know where and I don't know when
But those are the dreams I like the best
It's those dreams that help me rest
Some days to me it seems
I'll meet the girl who's in my dreams
