I.
A new light
Interferes with the known darkness
Painful sight takes the place 
Of a serene night
That light becoming my guide
I'm forced out of my sanctuary
Into the cold world

II.

Finally 
The climb
Is done
The arduous ascent
Behind me
Overcoming all adversity
I reach the peak
The sun above me
The world below
I stand on my shadow
Hiding it from view
Triumphant to this point
Only my journey down 
Remains

III.

Darkness closes in
Like a lamp slowly dimmed
Soon it will blink out
All the clocks are broken
And yet I don't care
As my eyes lose focus
My chest rises,
Rises like the tide
Then falls with a final breath
Darkness
