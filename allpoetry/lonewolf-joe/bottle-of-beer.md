---
title: "Bottle of Beer"
writer: "lonewolf joe"
slug: "bottle-of-beer"

I woke up this morning, sun shining in my eyes
Upon my face there were dancing flies
Another day has come and I'm still here
With nothing to my name, but a bottle of beer
Rustling out of my black and white bed
And a concrete pillow under my head
Only God loves me, there's nothing I can do
'Cause sometimes I think he's forgotten me too
Some say I've no hope, I should just die
If I told you I ignored them, that would be a lie
But I can stand strong and retain my fear
As long as I've got God and my bottle of beer
I live alone on a cold dark street
And I am shunned by the people I meet
I am but only a lonely man
Who eats his lunch from a garbage can
On the street corner is where I spend my time
Holding a cup out for a penny or a dime
But alas as the end of the day draws near
I only make enough for a bottle of beer
