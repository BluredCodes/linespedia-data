Is there someone up there watching us
Someone we can not see
Somewhere up there looking down
Down on you and me

Could it be my grandmother
Who said she loved me so
I sat there and watched her 
'Til she let her last breath go
Is she up in heaven looking down on me
Watching as I grow bigger 
Than I was when I was three

Is there someone up there watching us 
Someone we can not see
Someone up there looking down
Down on you and me

Could it be there's aliens
From another world
Watching us from up above 
Our lives for them unfurled
Are they up there mocking us
And how we live our lives
Are they scared of what they witness
with unbelieving eyes
But until one fateful day 
We will never know
That day if it ever comes
When I see a UFO

Is there something up there watching us
Something we can not see
Something up there looking down
Down on you and me

Could it be there's someone
Watching us each day
The father of humanity
To him each night we pray
Does he have a plan for us
Some destiny in mind
Is there some final destination
For all of human kind
Did he send his only son
To die so we may live
Is it to this omnipotent being
To whom our souls we give
Is he up there keeping track 
Of all of our deeds
I guess I'll never know
'Til on my Death's breath I feed

Is there someone up there watching us
Someone we can not see
I guess we will never know
So I'll just let it be
