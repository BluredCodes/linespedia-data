Turn around and look in
That glass who's back is painted black
Before judging others 
With a truth that you doth lack
For their journeys and their challenges
You have failed to face
So why be the jury 
Without even hearing the case
If you led a man to water 
He may just jump in
Because he does the least expected
Doesn't make it a sin
So before you judge others 
Take a look at you
You're different than they are
And you know that it's true
There was only one man
Who led life without flaw
I'm sure at some point 
Even you broke the law
So judge not others
Lest ye be judged yourself
Bag up all your prejudice
And put it on a shelf
