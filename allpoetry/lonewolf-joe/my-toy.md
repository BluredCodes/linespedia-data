title: "My Toy"

Have you seen my toy, I think I lost it
Or maybe it was stolen by the monster in my closet
It's not under my bed or in the sink
Or in the water the dog likes to drink
Where can it be?  I can not find
The wonderful toy I've got on my mind
Maybe it's now booty for pirates on the sea
But I live in Kansas, so how can that be?
I've looked everywhere, high and low
I can't understand where my toy could go
I guess I'll start a search across the land
Oh, 
would you look at that, 
It's right here in my hand
