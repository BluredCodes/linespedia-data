Your birthday is coming up.
And I don’t know what to get you.
I don’t know what would make you happy.
I only wish I did.

So for your birthday, I will give you what I can.
I will give you friendship.
I will give you support when you are falling.
I will give you a shoulder to cry on. 
I will give you no pressure, 
I will give you all the love I can. Without going over the line.

I will try to detach myself from you.
I will pull back. And leave you alone.
I will do what you want me to do.

I will give you anything I can
I would give you my life.
I’m not sure you would want it. But I would give it to you.
