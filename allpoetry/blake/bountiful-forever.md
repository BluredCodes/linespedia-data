Just ten minutes ago
I caught those clouds
diving into one another
like playful squirrels
or Moroccan sand dunes.

They were as pink, white, and light blue
as scoops of bubble gum ice cream.
Even the purple scoops 
squirming on the bottom
held a tangerine outline.

And now they toil uncolored
dark and shrouded
like hazy miasmas of seaweed
swirling under the blur of lakewater
forever fighting
forever playing
eyeless creatures
bound to feel forever.
