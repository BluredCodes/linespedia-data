As I sit here looking at the sceneryAs far as the eye can seeMy thoughts turn again to youAnd the love that we knew was so true.Oh how I loved you soI didn't want to let you goI keep thinking it was so unfairLike some terrible unreal nightmare.I've played it over in my mindAt least a thousand timesHow you told me that dreadful nightThat I could no longer hold you tight.But it is over and doneLike the setting of the sunBut that sun will rise again somedayMaybe again I'll be able to say'I love you, Mona May.'
