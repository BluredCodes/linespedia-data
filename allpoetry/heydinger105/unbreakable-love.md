title: "Unbreakable Love"

You are so pretty
Your face glows like a star
Even in the darkness of my life
I see you shine through from afar

You're all I want
You're all I need
You're everything
That I can see

But there is an unbreakable wall
That looms between us
I've tried so hard to destroy it
And turn it into dust

But the Wall of Age cannot be broken
It still stands between you and me
Keeping our hearts separated
For all eternity

But I still love her so
And I will ask her again
If she will love me too
And be my special friend

So here it comes
My love I do send
Will you accept my love?
Will you be my girlfriend?
