So many years have gone by
Since the day we first said "Hi!"
I didn't really know you then
So I couldn't trust you as a friend.

Since then we've spent much time together
Getting to know each other.
Having parties and lots of fun
Never knowing when they'll be done.


But the times that I hold most dear
Are the times when you were near.
Always telling me what I need to know
And telling me where I need to go.

The love you have for me
Is deeper than the deep blue sea.
You would never destroy me with your wrath
Or show me a crooked path.

I know I can always count on you
To be there and tell me what to do.
You will always be there till the end
That is why I call you a true friend.
