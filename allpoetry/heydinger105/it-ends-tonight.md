title: "It Ends Tonight"

Leave me alone!!
I'm not a nutcase!!
Don't look at me like that!!
Just tell it to my face!!

I know I need some help
But nobody gives care about me
I should just disappear
That way you'll be rid of this disease

I can't do anything right anyway
So what is the sense of me being here
I will end it all tonight
My mission is very clear

It sits in the corner of my room
I look at it every day
And wonder if anyone would care
If I just threw it all away

It has the power to make or break nations
It's used on the street
It's used in war
And here it sits at my feet

So I'll pull the gun out of the corner
I've lost all hope anyway
So good-bye to all those who tried
To make me happy every day

I can't say how much I'm sorry
But all your efforts were in vain
The reason I am doing this
Is to get rid of the pain
