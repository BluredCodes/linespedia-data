I've lost you again!
I don't understand
What makes you turn away
From the love I have to give

I feel such rejection
Life has no satisfaction
Without you in my life 
There is nothing left to do but die

You make me feel so happy
You give me so much life
When I am down and depressed
You were there to make me smile

Why do you love him so?
He only hurts you with his voice
He only brings you down
And makes you feel void

I would never use you 
I could never abuse you 
Why don't you see this part of me?
Before I lose you for all eternity

I've lost you again
I've lost myself again
My love for you is almost gone
And soon my life will end
