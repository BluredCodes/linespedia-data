---
title: "Why??!!"
writer: "heydinger105"

Why was I put here on this earth?
Why was I put in these rotten shoes?
Why can't I get a grip on life?
Why can't I find any clues?

I just want to get away from it all
And start my life all over again.
I'll do things my way this time
Maybe things will be better then.

I've screwed things up so many times
That I can't stand it anymore.
I have to let go of this wretched world
And do something that is not a bore.

But things are the way they are
And I'll continue to live off a dull high.
But I'll keep longing for that world
Where you don't have to ask why.
