---
title: "My Angel"
writer: "heydinger105"

Face so pretty
Skin so soft
Without you in my life
I think I'd be lost

Your eyes are so beautiful
As blue as the sea
When I stare into them
I see only love for me

All I want is to hold you close
And to keep you safe and warm
With my arms around you
Nothing will cause you harm

There always seems to be
A halo around your head
That's why your my Angel
And I hope our love will never end

When you are with me
I'm so careless and free
I'm here to love you
Because you have my heart's key
