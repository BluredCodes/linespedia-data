Sometimes I sit and wonderIs anybody there for me?So I look deep inside for an answerBut I always come up emptyWhat am I here for?Am I suppose to be funny?Do I have any friends at all?Or am I just lonely?All I want is love and attentionOr someone to be there for meBut I can't seem to find that one Who will set me freeWhy do I get a load of rejectionWhen all I give is love?This world seems like a cruel battleAnd I've been hit by the gloveSo I'll keep looking for that love and attentionIn that single form of a personAnd that person will make me feelLike the Emporer of a nation
