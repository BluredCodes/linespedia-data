title: "What Would They Say"
writer: "Stephanie"

If I were to die today
What would People say about me
Would they say,
I was a person to know
I stood up for my beliefs
Knew what was right
Helped those that I could
That I was intelligent person
Adventurous
Took life with a bound
Determined to succeed
Had goals and dreams
Would they say I loved God
And tried to do my very best to put him first
Loved my friends and was always there
Tried to be what everybody wanted and expected
Was a good girl
Was Daddy's little girl
Momma's baby
Could people say this about me?
With a smile on their face and peace in their heart

Stephanie Smith
April 20, 1999
