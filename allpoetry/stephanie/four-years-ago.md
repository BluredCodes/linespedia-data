Four Years Ago
A man with evil in his heart
A bomb in his hand
Took the security peace out of Oklahoma

What they must have been going through
The screams and cries of the children and people
Dying in the rubble
They must know they won't get there in time

Four Years Later
They still hear the voices, and see the faces
Constantly haunting their dreams and thoughts
Will the shouts and cries ever stop

That man didn't just kill 168
He as constanly killed and rekilled
The people that survived, helped, and watched
Four Years Ago


Stephanie Smith
April 1999
