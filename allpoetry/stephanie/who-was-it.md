Who was it who died,
on that cross made of wood.
Who was it who shed his blood?
Who was it who died for us all?
Cause he couldn't bare to see us suffer.
Who is it?
Who waits beside his father.
Who is it ?
Its a great man named "Jesus"!!


Stephanie Smith
August 26, 1996
