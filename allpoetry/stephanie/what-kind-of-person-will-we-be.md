title: "What Kind of Person Will We Be"

God Chooses what Kind of person,
we will be from the day that we were created.
He decides what personality we will have.
The color of our eyes and the color of our hair.
The size of our hands and feet,
and how tall we will be.
Will I be big or will I be small?
Will I make friends easily?
Or will I be a loner?
Will I be a happy outgoing person?
Or a depressed trapped soul?
God created me and everyone else special.
He made me, ME!!
