---
title: "I need not to go!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "i-need-not-to-go"

O where the birds do sing
A sad blue song 
I need to go!

Where the whales do sail
Away and unknown
I need to go!

And where the love born and rise
Again and again
I need not to go!

O I need not to go!
To paradise on earth!
To love
To kisses
And to tears

I need not to go!
To shake-hands with that old devil again

O it begins with a smile
But it always end with a tear

O I need not to go!
To love
To kisses
And to tears.
