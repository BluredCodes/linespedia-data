---
title: "Melancholy Vision"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "melancholy-vision"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20282-Melancholy-Vision-by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"

Glory & happiness have passed away
Gloom & sadness around my happy pillow
Beauty of deeper glances fled away
Breeze of deeper darkness around my warm pillow

Morning blesses fled away
Rays of glorious sun no longer on my pillow
The poet in me faded away
And melancholy vision is on my pillow
