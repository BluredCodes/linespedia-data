At last he spoke thus:

How much honey of hope I carried from here to my beehives!
How many roses of love I carried from here to my meadows!
How many poems of love I wrote from here to you!
How much tears of pain I shed from here till my tear-stream run!
And How a young heart became an old one!

O vainly I waited your love!
And all the honey of hope, the roses of love, the tears of pain and the poems of love have no meaning to you.
