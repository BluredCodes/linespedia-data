---
title: "It is the end!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "it-is-the-end"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8027-It-is-the-end--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"

Oh! I have loved
Four whole months, 
And life was perfect and sweet,
And I was so sure it would never end 


Oh! I have loved
Four whole months, 
And taste of honey was on my lips,
And I was sure the love would never end 

Oh! I have loved
Four whole months, 
And my eyes were blind to see,
And as always! I was sure it would never end 

Oh! I have loved
Four whole months, 
But love with me had made no stay,
And am sure now it is the end.
