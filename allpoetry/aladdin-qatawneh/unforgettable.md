title: "Unforgettable!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

Oh unforgettable!
Unforgettable that's what you are!

Unforgettable!
the smile,
the laugh that framed you around

Unforgettable!
The tears I shed that night

Unforgettable!
The night I walked alone in the meek light of the moon without you by my side

Unforgettable the four letters of your name

Oh!
Unforgettable the love I lost that night
