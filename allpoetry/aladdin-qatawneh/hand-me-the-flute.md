---
title: "Hand me the flute!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

Hand me the flute!
And sing!
Singing is secret of existence

Hand me the flute!
And sing!
Singing is existence

Sing!
Song of lighting
Song of clearing
And song of longing

Hand me the flute!
And sing!
Sing!
And wash the tears
And clear the sorrows
And heal the hearts

Hand me the flute!
And sing!
Sing!
Till echo of the flute last
And till the roaring of all seas are silenced
