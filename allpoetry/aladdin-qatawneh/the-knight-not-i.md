Under the silent moon in summers eve
Soft went the music in my ears
And thy sigh led me towards the star-eve
And there she stood and there I lost my fears

I turned towards thy flower scent
And I stopped then our eyes met
Oh! Silent delight ruled for a moment
And the passion-bell rang and I couldn't set

I stared and stared and I drunk her beauty up
Leaving no wine in her beauty cup
Thy dazzling frame is waiting for the knight not I
Then I smiled and left but wished the knight was I
