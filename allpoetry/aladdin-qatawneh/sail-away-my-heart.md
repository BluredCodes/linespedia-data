sail!
Sail away my heart!
Sail to the land of the rising sun,
To the land of beauty
and to the land of her arms

Sail, sail away my heart!
To the depth of her blue eyes,
To the golden beaches of her smile,
And to the soft skin of her warm hands

Sail, sail away my heart!
To the love I left behind,
To the one I lost behind,
And to the joy I miss to-night

Sail, sail away my heart!
And take me back in time
Take me to my love,
Take me to my romance,
And take me to the memories I left behind 

Sail, sail away my heart!
To the land of her arms
Sail!
And let me smell the skin of her hands.
