title: "Fallen leaves"

He whispered softly into her ears
And I heard it as if it into my ears

He cried her love and heart
And she sighed without a heart

He whispered you are my dream
And she smiled as if it a silly dream

He whispered without you I am only a tear
And the scene ended with a tear

Oh! He felt naked without her leaves
And I wondered why love faded and why fallen leaves
