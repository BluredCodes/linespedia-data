title: "Rain again!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

How shall I do?
And what can I do to reach you!
Where shall I go?
And what can I do to touch the heart of you!

I cry your mercy, love and eyes
I cry your heart, soul and sighs
Let once again these arms around
Let once again those lips be found

O! Sweet love...sweet rain!
Rain again!
Give love to me!
And let me feel her beats around.
