Like Silver staronce you shinnedAnd once your lightsAnd once your hazel-eyedWere my guiding lights?Like fairy-tale dream once I livedAnd once I lovedAnd once your imagesWere my eyes?Like faded soundAnd like empty cloudNo rain and love were foundBut sorrow and melancholy cloudLike wounded birdAnd like wingless birdNo joy and hope were foundBut memories and pain around
