In the black winter morning!
No light will be struck near my eyes
No heat will warm that old heart of mine
Nothing will make me feel the same again
And nothing will bring her back

Yes!
I am out of love, out of romance, out of happiness, and out of counting the stars in the sky
It's good---bye love, happiness, hope, warm, and rest of the soul
It is loneliness all about!

And I shall sit here in silence
With that look on my face
Till the end of time.
