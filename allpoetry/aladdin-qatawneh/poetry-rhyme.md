You came in to my life
With sweet tones and laughs
And you decorated my heart and life
With colors and laughs

You captured my heart
With glances from thy eyes
And with thy smile which framed you like art
You made me a poet adores thy eyes

Oh! Sweet angel
Let me write you a rhyme
Let me sing for an angel
And let me thank you, hold you and kiss you in away like poetry rhyme
