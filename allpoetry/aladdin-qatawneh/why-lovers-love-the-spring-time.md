title: "Why lovers, love the spring time?"

In the spring time, the only pretty time 
When birds do sing
When shepherds pipe all day long
When happiness is all about
When butterflies flying all around
And angels sing sweet love songs
When beauty is laying down on the green fields by the rivers of love, which always rolling down
When love is pure as a child
When love re-born again in this time
When happiness is all about
and roses decorating the beauty inside
Then I understand………
Why lovers, love the spring time?
