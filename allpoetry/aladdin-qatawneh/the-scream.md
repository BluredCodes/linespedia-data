---
title: "The scream"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "the-scream"
source: "AllPoetry"

It isn't an ordinary night!
Was it an ordinary day?
To you,
To me,
To all of us camping near the forest of fear
What touched me?
What spooked me!
Is it a soft wind shaking my leaves,
A paint brush drawing a frame around my fears
A musician touch playing a tune of melody towards my ears
Or an evil spirit,
A lonely and a hungry spirit needs its mate to dance with,
To melt in
Is it real or a dream or a vision of fear!
Oh! It was real & I was blind, but I could see
All I remember now,
Is the scream out of me,
In the forest!
We call it the forest of fear.
