---
title: "The boy and the girl!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "the-boy-and-the-girl"

The book of life is brief; my dear
And the world lost its glory
And nothing alive in me; my dear
And tears within my story

Do you remember when you love me?
Before the wind took you away!
Do you remember the smile within me?
Before the wind took it away!

Do you remember the boy and the girl?
And the almond tree and kisses and dreams!
Do you remember the boy and the girl?
And you and me and dreams!


The book of life is brief; my dear
And today I woke up with a tear
And I wondered where is love and smiling spring?
And where! You and me!
