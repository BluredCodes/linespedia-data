---
title: "Scene from paradise!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

O the sun kissing with golden face the meadows green
The colors of rainbow decorating the view like Christmas tree
The springs dancing toward the river in a beautiful scene

The birds are dancing and singing!
And music from paradise is what I hear!
The mountains are dressing spring’s colors
And I am here!
And she is there laying down like a flower between flowers
And like a butterfly between butterflies

The aromatic smell is in the air!
The aromatic smell is everywhere!
And I am standing here with free spirit, with free heart
And I can not believe it!
O I am looking at a scene from paradise
And I am free.
