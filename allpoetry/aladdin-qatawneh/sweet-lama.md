---
title: "Sweet lama!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "sweet-lama"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12739-Sweet-lama--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"
---

Sweet lama is a song of joyAn angel of delight fills of joyShe walks with her proud like a starAnd she is the light of the morning starSweet lama is the beauty and the conscious delightWith her sweet smiles she decorates the nightHer eyes a dance of love and divineAnd her lips are sweet and she is mineSweet lama is the bless that came my wayWith her sweet kisses she lights my dayAnd her silky hair dances in-loveAnd her soft skin smells good and I am in-loveSweet lama is my lady in whiteAn angel of love lights my nightSweet lama is the love of my lifeAnd I want her to be my lady, my love and my wife
