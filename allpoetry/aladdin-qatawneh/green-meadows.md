---
title: "Green meadows!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "green-meadows"
source: "AllPoetry"

As I look inside your eyes
I can see angels dance
I can feel heaven by my side
And I just lost in the green meadows of your eyes

As I touch the tender of your hands
I can feel the warm in my heart
I can feel the hunger of my hands
And I just lost in the palm of your hands

As I look while you smile
I can see rainbows dance
I can feel the magic all around
And I just lost in the sunlight of your smile

As I look at you!
I just smile :)
You are my love,
My friend,
And my life
