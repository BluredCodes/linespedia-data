---
title: "A dust in the wind!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "a-dust-in-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7445-A-dust-in-the-wind--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"
---

Oh! Dear friend, time of youth fled
And everything in me became old
And gray hairs were on my head
And the heart became cold

Oh! Dear friend, summers nights fled
And joy and happiness end
And long winters nights ahead
And singing and dancing are nearly end

Oh! Dear friend, my time fled
And the flame of live is in the wind
And soon my black angel will sign the end
And soon I will be a dust in the wind
