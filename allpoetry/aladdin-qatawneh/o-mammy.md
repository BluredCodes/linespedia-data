---
title: "O Mammy!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "o-mammy"

I’ll sing you a song!That what she used to sayAnd I love to sit there and hear her songO the softly breathing-songAnd gentle hands around my headAnd there I’ll lie and dreamAnd there I feel her loveAnd there!When night comes I’ll goAnd there!Always a place for me to goO Mammy!In your armsI am longing tonightBut…With silent melancholyThe tears drop downI am here and you are far!O Mammy!Memory comes!And I wish I could be that child againAnd there!There…in your armsI’ll lie and dream tonight
