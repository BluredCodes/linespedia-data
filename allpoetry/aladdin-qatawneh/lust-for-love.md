---
title: "Lust for love!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

O lust for love is within me!
And the click of the clock is dwelling into my ears

It is about midnight!
And soon she will be here
And soon the scent of her will be near
And holding her again!
O holding her again in my arms!
Is about to be real
