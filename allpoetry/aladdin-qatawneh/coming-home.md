---
title: "Coming home!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "coming-home"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19472-Coming-home--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"

I left my heart back in my old town
And since then tears and I were twin

I left my smile back in my old town
And since then gloom and sorrow within

I left my rose back in her hand
And since then colors of joy not found

I left my divine moments back in Amman
And since then no rest and peace within

O! It is about time!
For this knight to return home
And it is about time!
For this sailor to have rest and peace at home.
