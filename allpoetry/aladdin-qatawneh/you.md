---
title: "You!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You maybe the moon that lights above 
Maybe the sun that shines on me 
a beautiful view to watch & stare 
A perfect moment will never return 
A piece of dream I wish to live 
Now your are gone

You! the one I had but I lost 
The love I had I couldn't trust 
A beautiful face to watch & touch 
A candle of hope which lights my way 
A magical moment I left behind 
A beat of heart begins to fade 
Now you are gone 

you! Autumn wind blowing at me, calling my leaves to dance in love, 
you! the air that I breathe , the water to my tree 
Don't let me die 
You! everything to me,
The love, The life, the dream 
A piece of perfume to smell & feel 
A smile in my life begins to fade 
Now you are gone 

Now...I'm a shepherd with out a cattle 
Without a soul, without your love 
Without a smile, without your touch 
Memories of a man I used to know
A wounded heart, a wounded soul, a dying man
Please, please come back again just for once 
To ease the pain, to warm the heart 
The cold is near, the love is far 
Hold me too close, just hold me once
I'm dying now with out your touch.
