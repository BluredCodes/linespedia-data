---
title: "Vision Of Love"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "vision-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Every time I pass her door
I feel so strange and I feel so sure
I feel the love and I feel the flame
I feel the pain and I feel the cure
But, where to go!

A vision in my mind belongs to her
A picture, a frame, or maybe more
A figure floats like a feather in a beautiful scene 
A smile that captured my soul
A hair of gold and a heart of diamond shines on and on
I feel the magic and I smell the scent
But, where to go!

Every time I walk alone and every time I pass her door
I pictured her at the door
She is in--love but I'm not her knight
She is the love I hope to find
She is the one who made for me
But, maybe in another life

Still...
Every time I walk alone or every time I pass her door
A vision of love rolling on and on
But, where to go!!
