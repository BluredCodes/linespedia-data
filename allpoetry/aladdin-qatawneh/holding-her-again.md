title: "Holding her again!"

FACE IT, face it my heart my sweet old heart, just face it 
Face the unhidden truth now,
Face the the stormy wind, 
The dust in her eyes blinded her heart.
Face it, she is gone
Face it my beloved eyes, just face it
Face the unwelcome sight of view drawn in-- 
The reflections of her blue eyes
Face it, she is gone
Face it my hands, my dry bare hands, just face it
Face the cold touch, the unreal touch--
Which shook you last night
Face it, she is gone
Face the tears of goodbye drawn on your face now
FACE IT ALADDIN, just face it
Holding her again is adream to you now.
