---
title: "Song of a lover!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "song-of-a-lover"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7447-Song-of-a-lover--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"
---

Night has come; My soul
Only now all the songs of lovers awaken!

Stars sparkling and glowing up there; My soul
But...
I live in my own light
I live in the light of loneliness within me

Dark has come; My soul
All the stars of love can not guide me

Cold and ice is in my heart; My soul
All the lights of love can not warm me

Gloom is all around; My soul
Only a song of a lover can cheer me

Storm is within me; My soul
Only a word from her can settle me

Night has come; My soul
All the songs of lovers awaken
But...
Where is mine?
Where is mine!
