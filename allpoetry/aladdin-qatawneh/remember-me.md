---
title: "Remember me!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "remember-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh! Gathers my ashes, My love
And pick-up the pieces!
Spread my ashes, My love
And sing for me!

Sing!
Song of songs
Song of love
Song of death
And song of farewell 

Oh! I am dead, My love
The day you married not me!

Oh! consider me dead, My love
And try to live happily ever after
And remember me
