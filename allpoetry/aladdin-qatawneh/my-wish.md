---
title: "My wish!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "my-wish"
source: "AllPoetry"

Is it your wish, my love!
To go away and unknown from me

Is it your wish, my love!
To seek another land and flee

And is it your wish!
With lusting lips and hungry heart leaving me

Oh verily, my love
You galled my best honey and my matchless beats

Now it is my wish, my love
To go away and flee
