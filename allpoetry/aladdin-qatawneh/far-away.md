---
title: "Far & away!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "far-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh, far and away!
Far and away from the zone of her eyes
Far and away from the silky hair around her smile
Far and away from the beats of her heart.

Far and away from the shores of her eyes
Far and away from that magical thing around her smile
Far and away from the skin around her heart.

Far and away from the juicy lips, from the milky skin, from the aromatic smell, and from the golden beach of her smile.

Far and away from music of love, from whispers of love, and from that angelic thing that framed her around.

Oh, far and away!
All I need now...
kiss, beat and smile from her heart.
