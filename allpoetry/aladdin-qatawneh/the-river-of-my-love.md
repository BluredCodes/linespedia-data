---
title: "The river of my love!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "the-river-of-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22436-The-river-of-my-love--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"
---

Oh! My love overflows in rivers,
Downward, towards sunrise and sunset
Yet not succeed!

My love overflows in rivers,
From silent mountains
And rushes into the valleys like thunderstorms
Yet not succeed!

Oh! The river of my love fail to find its way to the sea!

Alas!
The will decided!
The river of my love will never ever find its way to the sea
