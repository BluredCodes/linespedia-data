---
title: "Vision from the past"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "vision-from-the-past"
source: "AllPoetry"

The blue sea and the long gold beach
And the dancing little waves that leap
And the yellow sand shines a long the beach
In morning glow when angels do sing and leap

The blue eyes and the long gold smile
And the dancing hearts that beat
And the red lips decorating the smile
In morning glow when kissing here is so sweet

Oh memories, of my love and I on the beach
And the dancing waves that leap
And young lovers on the beach
In morning glow when love and happiness still leap
