title: "Earth's angel!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"

O you earth's angel!
O you little butterfly!
What have you done to a poet like I

O you earth's angel!
O you beauty on earth!
What have you done to a lover like I

O you earth's angel!
O you the gift that god created!
I am calling you...
From a high mountain,
From a far mountain

O I am calling you!
And lust for love is within me
And lust for kiss is squeezing me

O you earth's angel!
Love me,
Hold me And squeeze me
