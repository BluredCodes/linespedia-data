---
title: "Poet passion!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "poet-passion"

Ask my lips, my love
They will tell you more
Ask my eyes, my love
They will show you more
They will show you spring,
They will show you heaven,
And they will show you scenes of love deep inside

Feel my heart, my love
Feel the beats inside
Feel the sense of someone in-love, just feel it my life 

Hear my voice, my love
Hear the tones of passion,
Hear the rhythm of romance,
And hear the songs of love...
Dancing, (soft and sweet) on the shores of my eyes

Feel the love, my love
Feel the passion inside
Feel the hunger of my hands toward the skin around your eyes

Scent the love, my love
Smell the Aromatic inside
I'm all yours my love; I'm all yours
But, if you want to know more, my love
Just ask, you just ask my love.
