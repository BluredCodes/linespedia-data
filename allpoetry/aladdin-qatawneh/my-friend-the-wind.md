---
title: "My friend the wind!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "my-friend-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

My friend the wind
Coming from the sky
My friend the wind
Coming from her land
My friend the wind
Coming and holding a secret
Of love and wishes from her heart

My friend the wind
Coming now to my land
My friend the wind
Blowing roses at my heart
My friend the wind
Telling me her secret
She loves me but she just shy.
