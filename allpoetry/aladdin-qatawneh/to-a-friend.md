---
title: "To a friend!"
writer: "Aladdin Qatawneh"
slug: "to-a-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20171-To-a-friend--by-Aladdin-Qatawneh"

You!
A dream!
A beautiful dream
A dream within a dream
A flower
A scent of happiness
A smile 
A delicate wind dances on trees
You!
Spring and colors
Rainbows and stars
An angel!
An angel of delight
A lovely butterfly
A milky butterfly dances in-love
You!
Piece of music rolling in ears
Drop of passion calling me
A magical moment I wish to live 
A beat of heart invaded me
A romantic rhyme calling me
You!
A rose!
A nightingale sings for me
A beat of joy
A beautiful view to watch and stare
A candle of hope lights my path,
My heart and soul
You!
The sea with its pleasures
With its glory and colors
You!
Paradise on earth
The beauty!
The inner beauty
The beauty of all times
The past
The present and future
You!
A friend forever
And you will always be
You!
An angel forever
And you will always be
Till death takes us a part
Say! Amen
