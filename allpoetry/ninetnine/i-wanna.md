title: "i wanna"

why are the clouds so light? i wanna fly,
the rat in the rotting corpse, i'm not so sly.
the beauty of night, i wanna see,
the Grim Reaper's mistress, i wanna be.
the land of no fathers, i wanna go,
the tears that i weep, who will sow?
why am i living? i want to be gone,
but to live a new day, and breathe a new dawn.
