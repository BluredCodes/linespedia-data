Close your eyes and submit to my will,open your body, accept your fill.you get my intensity, i feed off your lust,now lay down your shields and survive on our trust.your eyes undress me standing alone,the intimacy warms me deep in my bones.the way your lips curve the moment we touch,when you tell me you love me ever so much.you make it erotic just touching my hair,you turn me on with your intentful stare.our bodies melt in a lightening hot embrace,my hands have memorized the ways of your face.my mouth has felt the curves of your body, your being,i know you are loving me, i feel without seeing.your heat invades into my core,God, i would beg for just a bit more.After, we lay, and I hear your heart beat,and it takes me to heaven to know it beats just for me.
