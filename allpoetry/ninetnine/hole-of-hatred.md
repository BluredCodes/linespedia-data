---
title: "hole of hatred"
writer: "ninetnine"
slug: "hole-of-hatred"
source: "AllPoetry"

i live in a hole that is dark and deep,
i toss and turn and wait for sleep.
i hear them above, living their lives,
the day will come when i cease to survive.
i wait for the day when they stop to take note,
and look down on me to tease and gloat.
my wrath will leave them in a dreamy daze,
the hatred in my eyes will burn ablaze.
mercy will not live in my wrathful soul,
look in my eyes, with contempt they're full.
the day will come when i grow to your size,
in the shadows you'll cower, the day comes when i rise.
