I have wound deep down inside,
Are you the soul in which I confied?
the pain, the truth, the cold living box,
My mind, my body, my heart covered with locks.
Burn the key and fight the war,
So i can be alone, to myself i have swore.
Some search their lives to find a good lay,
I look for happiness and to see a new day.
