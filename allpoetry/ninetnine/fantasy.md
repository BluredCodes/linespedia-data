i live in a fantasy where anything is real,
where no one can hate and no one can steal.
i can break boundaries, over lines i will step,
and my secrets reamin foreverly kept.
the ocean is full of pirates and treasures,
the world is full of bodily pleasures.
the strok of a brush and the brush of a wand,
gives anyone the immotality, the kind always fawned.
where dragons and horses are one of a kind,
and everyone has a unicorn on which they can fly.
i dont ask for leniancy or for others to believe,
but to be all alone, my dreams on a leash.
leave me in the dark to have my illusions,
and try as you will not to voice your diffusions.
