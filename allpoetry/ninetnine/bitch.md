i try to conceal her but my vixen gets out,
i try to dissuade her,thats not what i'm about.
my cricket does tell me that i bat my blue eyes,
i treat men like trophies, i win a mere prize.
i dont treat them as people, but merely a chase,
i see a living goal, not a human face.
i twist and i turn them without regret or shame, 
after all i consider them just a part of my game.
