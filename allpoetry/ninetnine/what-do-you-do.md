what do you do?
when hate becomes the rule
not the exception

when you find yourself thinking
things you never thought you would
when life becomes a chore
and your soul becomes a whore
what do you do?

what do you do?
when you love
when you love someone so much it hurts
deep inside
like a knife in your heart
even when your happy
what do you do?
  
what do you do?
when you find that nothing can wake you up
you go through life 
like you're not
and when you get up to look around
you wonder how you got there

what do you do?
when you find comfort in the faults
and sleep is the alternative
to life

what do you do?
