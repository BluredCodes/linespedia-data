She sits alone on a cliffs edge
staring at herself through coloured glasses
so many steps ahead of her
not knowing where it all ends
believing in a god to hold her
and guide her to where she belongs
She closes her eyes 
and spreads her wings
takes one step 
and soars
to the bottom of time
regretting her beliefs 
as she dissapears from your front door
