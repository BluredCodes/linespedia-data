... You asked for perfection
and look what you get...
another dissapointment

evolution has gone too far
to live is no longer to survive and coexist
but to live
for us 
is to destroy
to complicate
to control
to become our own ruin
and their's
to dominate over them,
the wise ones
we are not stronger
merely selfish and weaker
with a shit load of weapons

HUMANITY

... Yet another dissapointment...
