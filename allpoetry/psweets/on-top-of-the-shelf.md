title: "On Top of the Shelf"
writer: "psweets"

I sit up here
Night and day
Wishing that my friends weren't so stiff
And made of clay

I sit up here
Everyday
Talking to myself and wondering why
I always feel like I am by myself

I sit up here
Night and day
Thinking that life must be more exciting
than this

I sit up here
Everyday
And wonder why I am frozen on this shelf
Alone again
