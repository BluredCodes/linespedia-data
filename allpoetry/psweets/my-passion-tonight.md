title: "My Passion Tonight"
writer: "psweets"

Rain
..floats weightlessly
...from the blues above
to gently caress
..every curve and crevess
...exploring every inch
....gently
.....awingly
......inspiringly
never resting
...just touching
..lurking
the endless possibilities
.. soothing misery
