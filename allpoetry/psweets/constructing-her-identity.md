title: "Constructing Her Identity"

A little bit of this
A little bit of that
A part of me
And a part of you
A little bit of a copy to be a little part of everyone
To fit in everywhere 
Without being questioned
But what do you really believe in?
And why do you need all of this attention?
Don't you know how it feels to just be yourself?
