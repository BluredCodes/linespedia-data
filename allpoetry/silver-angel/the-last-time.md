---
title: "The Last Time"
writer: "Silver Angel"

This is the last time
I'm going to cry
The last time
I'll have to look
At that black cloud
Over my head
The last time
Those rings are 
Coming off my finger

This is the last time
I'm going to try
The last time
To watch the blood
Drip from my eyes
Trailing my cheeks
Ice cold drops
Hitting my heart
The very last time
