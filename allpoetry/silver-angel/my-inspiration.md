I stood there breathless As glistening tears floated From his misery filled violet eyesMy shimmering lips Take the chill From his reddening cheek Never knowing I was Letting footprints on his heartAnxiousness dances in the midnight skyBecause he is mine asI am his, never needing to ask whyA warm breeze Softly chases aroundThe twinkling blue starsBut they were so far Beyond his trembling handIt didn’t matter that he could notCommand the stars to abide by himI am happily content to be under his skyHe smiled with me As we stood on the cliff Hand in hand, heart with heartOverlooking the distant seaUnder a fabulous blue-blushed skyMy heart bursting withLoving him so muchSilently I gaze upon his faceWondering if he knowsHe is my inspiration
