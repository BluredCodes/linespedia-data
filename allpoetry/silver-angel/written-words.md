Comfort radiates from written words 

Like a gentle hand caressing shy skin

Syllables of reassuring wisdom

Erasing the loneliness with memories

All that is left are the memories

Of another time, of another place

When I let out the real me

Released for a while from all the insecurities

To lay in the sunshine of life

Now I find solace, and 

From written words, comfort radiates
