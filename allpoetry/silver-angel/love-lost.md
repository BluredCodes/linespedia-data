---
title: "Love Lost"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "love-lost"

Love is so fagile
It makes holding on to it hard
Never knowing when it might slip from your hands
And shatter all that you have built
You try so hard to make things right
And yet you sit alone 
Night after dark night
You ask, why is my love lost
When did it fade away
Was I truly so busy 
That I didn't even see it leave
Or did I take it for granted
That it would always be there
Love can not be caged or framed
Or put in a safe place
Till you are ready to love it again
Because one day
You will turn your back
And that love will have ran away
Then you will sit here, like me
And ponder
Just when and why was my love lost
