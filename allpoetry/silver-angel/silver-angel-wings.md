On silver angel wings she flies 
In the magenta streaked sky
Sadness beyond belief in her eyes
In her heart, many reasons to cry

On silver angel wings she treads
Barely touching upon the leaves of golden red 
In her body, thoughts strive to breath
Yet her mind tells her, forget your needs

On silver angel wings she lingers
She is the hovering mist of the morning
The purple haze caressing the moon
She is less, but so much more

She is the glimpse, of a midnight rainbow
The shy blush of the mid-day sun
She is lost into herself once more
As she floats evermore, on her silver angel wings
