She's very little
Still quite young
There is no way to stop 
What had begun
It wasn't her fault
There was no place to turn
The words of her mother 
Still rang in her ears
"This is the lesson you must learn"
It was not the first time
She heard those words
She looks to the left
And then to the right
Then back to her mother
Her eyes huge with fright
There was no one to save her
Or calm her fears
No one caring about her tears
So with a heart filled with dread 
She lowered down her head
Then stiffened her back 
Awaiting the first crack
She could drift away
If she tried really hard 
Getting out of the body 
That stayed to receive it's pay
When it was over 
She crawled away beaten 
Praying to God 
"Please help me be good" 
As all little girls should 
Over the years 
She learned many harsh lessons
She's all grown up now 
But remembers the blows dealt
Never forgetting 
All the pain that she felt
The most important lesson 
That she has learned 
Is never abuse 
And never...
Never...

Teach with a belt!
