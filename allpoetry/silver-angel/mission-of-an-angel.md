The mission of an angel
Is to bring smiles
Even if she, herself has none

To lend a hand
To those who need it
Even if she gets no thanks in return

To give sincere hugs 
To all who need them
Even if they don't ask for it

To protect against evil
To those who are weak
Even if they don't see it

So please let me
Bring you a smile
Lend you my hand
Give you my hugs 
And protect your soul

For this is the mission 
Of an angel
