She's my little twinkle toes
Sprinkling smiles everywhere she goes
Beautiful curls surround her face
Bouncing about in satin and lace

You can't help but smile
When you watch her a while
If your heart is heavy and blue
She's cheer you up in a minute or two

She leaves glittering happy dust everywhere
If you see some just beware
There's a little fire ball not far away
Dedicated to brigthen your day

She dances high above on rainbows
Just because she can, don't you know
She sprinkles smiles everywhere she goes
She's my little twinkle toes**This poem is for my littlest princess who just turned 11 years old on Monday. She makes my life worth living. Everyday she teaches me things I've never knew and she never fails to make my dark skies blue.**
