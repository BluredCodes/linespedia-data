title: "As Tears Fall"
writer: "Silver Angel"

I thought I knew my friend
I thought I knew it all
and I don't know why
these tears continue to fall
going over everything in my mind
trying to see where or what I did wrong
but there is nothing I can find
and I've tried and tried to think
I've pushed myself to the brink
now as I sit here with an ache in my heart
a pounding in my head
almost wishing I was dead
but dying would not stop the hurt that I feel
so with life I must deal
all I want to know is
what I have done and can it be fixed
because I can't go on like this
