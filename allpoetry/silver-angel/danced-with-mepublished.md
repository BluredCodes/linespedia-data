I don't ask him to dance, it makes him uncomfortable
But watching the other couples dance breaks my heart
I see them holding each other close and see their love
I know he loves me, he just shows it in different ways

So imagine my surprise when he took my hand
and with out words, lead me to the dance floor
My heart was pumping, my breath almost gone
As he wrapped his arms about me, and spun me around

Time stood still, and I wanted it to last forever
Showing the world that I am his girl
It may be another year before he dances again
So I'll remember this moment the rest of my life

What he wore, how he smelled, seeing on his face
The most wonderful smile
So in the future, when I see couples dance
I will remember this moment, when he danced with me
