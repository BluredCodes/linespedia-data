---
title: "Where Would I Be"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "where-would-i-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10550-Where-Would-I-Be-by-Silver-Angel"

Where would I be without himHis smile, his touch , his loveMy heart is sure he's my one and onlyMy mind has known this all alongWhere would I be without himHis power, his support, his strengthI slipped, letting others tell me I was wrongI'm not wrong, he's the part of my life that's strongWhere would I be without himHis hugs, his kisses, his sweet carress'sThe love I see in his gaze is all I needWithout his love, where would I be
