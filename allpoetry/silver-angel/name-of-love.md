---
title: "Name Of Love"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "name-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5100-Name-Of-Love-by-Silver-Angel"

Pain so deep that tears won't fallRealization so strong that you gave it your allNow you dwell in uncertainnessAnd wonder why you even existOpinions you voice go unheardThey wonder why your mind is disturbedLove is your weakness It holds you powerlessBecause you'd do anything for itAnything to keep itEven if it meant losing yourselfAnd bending to it's willYou'd do it all in the name of loveSo as the tears run down your heartYou come to the conclusionYou're better off apartLooking back for one more glanceKnowing you used your very last chanceYou mouth the words that you've lived by so longYou did it in the name of love, just to find you were wrong
