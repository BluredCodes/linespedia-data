title: "Far Away Dreams"

The trees have earsAnd the flowers have eyesHigh above, are stars that singAnd a moon that triesAll these things can surely beIn the land of far way dreamsThe mountains can speakAnd the river will listenThe diamonds in the sky do glistenWantingly we walk barefoot upon silkAnd sleep in passions splendorIn the land of far away dreamsThe clouds, they do chatter Of silly things that don't matterThe sea is silently pleadingTo a beach that refuses to see himWarm sun gives his love to an icy winterIn the land of far away dreamsEverything is possibleNo need to screamGo in search Of a true loveIt's out there, for you, for meIn the land of far away dreams
