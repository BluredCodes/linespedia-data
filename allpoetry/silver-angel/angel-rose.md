**This was written as a follow through with my poem Yesterday**


Her love, her life's passion
In beautiful shades of red
A color that's dashing
Grew from her earthly garden bed

She remembers quite clearly
The man of her dreams
She loved him so dearly
Like yesterday, it seems

She knew her time
Was soon to come
Was it such a crime
To want to go home

Home is in heaven
Where her love awaits
He started her a garden
And he waits at the gate

His love, his wife
His angel, Rose
Will again share his life
Oh, how his love grows

She now is in heaven
Along the side of her love
Planting roses with him
In God's rose garden above
