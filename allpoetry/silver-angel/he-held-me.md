---
title: "He Held Me"
writer: "Silver Angel"

He held me in his hands
Then reached to turn out the light
Kissed my cheek so sweetly and
Guided me gently through the night

He held me closely to him
Confusion stole my heart
What was this I was feeling
My senses were falling apart

He held me inside his arms
Gave all his love to me 
I felt I'd gone to heaven
Or floated out to sea

He held me cradled to him
A place I loved to be
My head softly rests against him
He's everything to me

He held me until the morning
He tenderly touched my face
When I looked into his eyes
Love was all that I could see

He held me in his heart
Letting nothing break his hold
He loves me so completely
His heart is my new home
