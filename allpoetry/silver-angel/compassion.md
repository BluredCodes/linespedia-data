---
title: "Compassion"
writer: "Silver Angel"

To cry when you feel another's pain
To feel real joy in another's smile

To listen when one needs to be heard
To lift the spirits when one is at their lowest

To go that extra mile when no one else will
To give of yourself no matter how bad you feel

If you have done one small favor for another
For no other reason than to make someone feel better
To make another's life a little easier
Or to give openly and freely of yourself
Neither wanting, nor needing anything in return

Then you have experienced 
The unbelieveably
Inner satisfying
Heart warming
Soul nurturing
Ever gratifying
Emotion of
Compassion
