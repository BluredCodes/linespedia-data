**A Christmas Story**


Traveling from inn to inn
Praying one would let them within
To their dismay and all for not
A broken down stable was all they got

She looked to him with tears in her eyes
Knowing he could not answer her questions of whys
It killed him seeing her cry
So he held her close till all her tears ran dry

When the pains started, she barely made a sound
He stood looking in awe, at the love he had found
They cared not about the bright shining star
Birthing the babe was more important by far

Soon her low cries came to an end
Joseph, on his knee's he did bend
No babe more perfect had ever been born
He gently wrapped the "King" in cloth he had torn

With love in both his eyes and his heart
He held his Mary and vowed never to part
So in love with each other and the new babe
They never notice the visitors that came

Patiently they waited till each guest was gone
Caring nothing for the gifts they had brung
Wanting only to be left alone
To hold each other close and care for their son

Joseph took Mary's hand in his
Promised to make a good life for her and for him
Together they'll live life in the presence of love
With the gift God had sent from heaven above
