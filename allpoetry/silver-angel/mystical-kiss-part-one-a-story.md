She hovered gracefully above him
Her heart pounding, eyes glazed
Purest passion burning bright

She waited for this day all her lonely life
You see, she lived in the Kingdom in the Sky
She had never set foot on the earth

But she longed to run wild across a wide open field
And frolic in shallow springtime streams
These were things that were not ever to be
For they were merely dreams

As she was flying one misty morn
A dark shadow caught her eye
She dismissed it at first, thinking is was the wind

There it is again! 
Mystified, she flew closer being ever so careful
Not to touch the ground, for it she did
She'd loose her wings
And if she couldn't fly, how was she to get back 
To her Kingdom in the Sky

There is was yet again, but this time she kept 
Her eyes open and didn't let it leave her sight
It has a body simular to her own
As quietly as she could, she peeked around the tree
And came face to face with a unbelieveably masculine beauty

He had eyes the color of the deep blue sea
Hair so black it looked blue it was shiny, smooth 
Every inch of him was perfection
She instantly fell in love with him

She sensed she had the same effect on him
For the second he saw her 
Visably his breathing had quickened
He has seen this green eyed beauty many times
And he was also in love with her

How could she ever be his
When she was of the sky and he of the earth
Her mama always told her
"If a kiss makes your heart race, go and live 
In love and grace" she was so confused
Because her heart surely was racing
So she thought maybe a kiss would clear her mind

She hovered gracefully above him
Her heart pounding, eyes glazed
Purest passion burning brightThis story was inspired by a beautiful picture a found of a white pegasus who was hovering in the sky just inches above a black unicorn who was standing on the earth.  If anyone likes this, I'll post part two of this love story.  :)
