---
title: "Dreamweaver"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "dreamweaver"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dreamweaver grant me one wishSend me someone who loves me like thisPurely and openlyFor the person I amEternally and passionatelyTo match what I have givenEffortlessly and completelyThe way I'd love himSensually and tenderlyFrom this day to the endDreamweaver grant me one wishSend me someone who loves me like this
