From out of the tears
Emerges a beautiful butterfly
Perfect in every way
Warming her soul under the summer sun
Glistening wings ready for adventure
No one can stop her

From out of the tears
Comes a hardened heart
With a fragile soul
She is finished crying
Since crying gets her nowhere
It's time to move on

From out of the tears
Evolves a surviver
Steady in her steps
Confident in her independence
Happy in her silence
Comfortable with herself,,,, at long last
