title: "Always and Forever"
writer: "Silver Angel"

On a warm July nightA soft breeze blowsGently caressing tired bodiesThe sweet smell Of a recently mowed lawnOver takes their sensesFilling their heads withMemories of past childhoodsHis, with a wealthyHigh-status, well-known familyWith all the nicetiesOne could only imagine of havingHer's, with a largeMiddle-status familyWith hardships galoreOne could not imagine livingSomehow fate brought them togetherBringing him down to earthAnd lifting her to joys totally unknown to herAs they sit side-by-sideWith hands and hearts entwinedCherishing every momentEvery touchEvery kissEvery smileThey look to the starsEach says a silent prayerThat the love at their sideWill always and forever be there
