It was the grayest night I have ever seenOn this cold moonlit night of HalloweenWitches here and witches thereGhosts and goblins everywhereSnakes and spiders a ghouls delightThey want to scare you this hallow's nightMoans and groans out of thin airWalk alone, I would not dareCreatures crawling from every cornerWonder what's outside your flashlight's borderCan you fight old Drac's evil waysA pulsing neck is in his gazeLook you may, look you mightLook toward the mornings lightBats and cats will cross your pathCan you survive a Mummy's wrathIf you make it out alive from this sceneYou will pass the test of Halloween
