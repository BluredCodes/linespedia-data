It's not the flowers 
On the stand in the hall
That melt my heart
It's the fact 
That you put them there
Especially for me

It's not the taste 
Of the dinner we share
That glazes my eyes
It's the fact
That you are here
Smiling with me

It's not just the feel
Of your lips on mine
That flies me to the stars
It's the fact
That I love you 
And you love me

It's not the passion
Of your gentle touch
That sends me into sensual bliss
It's the fact 
That my touch
Does the same to you

It's not any
Of these things you do
That keeps my heart alive
It's the fact
That having you in my life
