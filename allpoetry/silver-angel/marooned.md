---
title: "Marooned"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "marooned"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I sit here so lonelyKnee's drawn to my chestTrying to keep my dreams adriftTears falling from every part of my soulArms reaching out needing to be made wholeEyes glistening from unshed tearsA heart crying out for love each yearIt's so cold living here on the moonWanting to be rescued soonHoping that he will someday seeHow easily he hurts meWhere is the knight I loveCan he not see me so far aboveCould someone please show him the wayPoint him in the right direction todayI'll be the angel sitting aloneDressed in a silvery gownIf you see a twinkle on the moonIt means I'm still here marooned.
