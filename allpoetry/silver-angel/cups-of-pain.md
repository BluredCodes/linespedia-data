---
title: "Cups Of Pain"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "cups-of-pain"

I have dreamt 1000 dreams 
That will never come true
To have them disappear by morning
Were they real, I haven't a clue
But I have had enough good dreams
To make me want to DREAM again

I have trusted 1000 friends 
Who have utterly failed me
To have them let me cry alone
As I sit here by the phone
But I have enough friends who are true
To let me TRUST again

I have prayed 1000 prayers 
That never got replies
Even though I waited patiently
As I look faithfully to the sky
But enough of my prayers have been answered
To let me PRAY again

I have drained 1000 cups of pain 
With 1000 disappointments
I've have gone 1000 days 
Without a song in my heart
But I have sampled enough sweetness from life
To give me the STRENGTH to go on
