---
title: "Out Of The Shadow"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "out-of-the-shadow"
source: "AllPoetry"

Standing away from the crowdA beautiful soul she isWatching from far awayA wonderful friend is sheCome, my friendYou are far more worthyCome out of the shadowInto the glimmering lightStand here beside meSo we can fight this worldHelp me slay this dragonThe giver of all sadnessCome out of the shadowInto the light of lifeYou are my equal Together we will fightYour smiles he can't handleThe brightness of your heartHe can never controlAnd so with head bowed he'll move onYou are a diamondShining so brightYour are so preciousThank you for shining your lightFriendships made in stoneWill never stand alone
