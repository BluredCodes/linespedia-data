---
title: "One Soul"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "one-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3292-One-Soul-by-Silver-Angel"
---

The wind
Blows gently over my shoulders
Carrying with it his scent
Telling me he is there before I see him

The light
Shines lovingly in his smile
Exciting my senses
Reassuring me our love is alive

The kiss
Rests softly against my lips
Taking my breathe away
Encircling my heart with his love

The touch
Sends me into deep desire
Dancing fingers tease my skin
Taking me to our special place

The love
Lives inside our hearts
Giving us a reason to live
Showing we are one soul
