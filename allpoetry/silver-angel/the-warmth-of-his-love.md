title: "The Warmth Of His Love"

My inspiration to write
Is unbelieveably strong
I gaze across the room
My eyes gleefully rest on
My love
My life
My partner
My husband
The other half of me

I smile as he snores 
In his sleep
My eyes tear
And I thank 
The powers that be
That he is here with me

Where would I be 
Without him
Without his smile
His love
Without his arms 
Around my soul

I would be lost
Adrift at sea
Never being able to dock
Never being able to walk
Upon the shore of his love again

He is my love 
In life
And so shall he be my love
In death

He'll always be 
The part of me
That is true happiness
May neither of see death
For a long, long time

I still feel electricity
Between us
It's so strong
As strong as the day we met

I still get butterflies inside
When I look in his eyes
He still has the power 
To take my breath away
With his kiss

He is still the only one
Who can calm my fears
And dry my tears
May I always be engulfed
