---
title: "Set Free"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "set-free"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3277-Set-Free-by-Silver-Angel"
---

Allowing blind fingers to exploreTouching places I've never touched beforeKissing lips as soft as mineIntoxicating my senses like a good wineGiving freely, his body and soulWanting to submit, but afraid to be boldOpening to sensations so unknownHolding onto in-securities of my ownWishing I could let everything goFinding the truth I didn't want to knowClosing fast so he won't seeHiding a soul that begs to be set free
