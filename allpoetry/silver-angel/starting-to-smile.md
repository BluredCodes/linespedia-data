title: "Starting To Smile"
writer: "Silver Angel"

You close and lock one door
To find another has opened spilling light on the floor

You feel can't take another step
Yet, for happiness, none can stop you from making the trip

You look at the past with a sigh and a frown
Then look to the future as your feet leave the ground

You have to let go of the sadness
And let go of all the madness

Better times are ahead of you
There is no sense in staying blue

There is only one thing left to do
Walk through the door that has opened for you

And find a friend who will be true
