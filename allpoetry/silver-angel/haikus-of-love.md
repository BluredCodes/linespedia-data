~Misty Moon~Glowing endlesslyUpon a midnight so clearPerfect for dancing ~This Heart~Pounding, beating loudShowing a love that is proudHe owns all my heart ~Midnight Song~You sing your love songInto my warm beating heartSing to me always ~Happiness~Comes to me in wavesThis happiness I'm feelingOh, what a nice wave ~Essence~The sweet smell of hisCologne on my soft pillowEvery single morn
