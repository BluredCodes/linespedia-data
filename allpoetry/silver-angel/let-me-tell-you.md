---
title: "Let Me Tell You"
writer: "Silver Angel"

Let me tell you
How I feel

In your arms I am safe
While I'm there
Nothing can hurt me
No one can touch me but you
Encircled within your heart
Is where I want to be

In your eyes I am alive
While looking there
Nothing is as clear
As the love that is there
Close your eyes 
So there is only you and me

In your heart I am home
While staying there
Nothing is painful
No one can reach us
I could stay here forever
Lock the door with me inside

Incase you can't see it in my eyes
Or feel it in my touch
Just let me tell you
I love you so much
