---
title: "Midnight Walk"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "midnight-walk"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43474-Midnight-Walk-by-Silver-Angel"
---

Midnight walk
new place
all is quiet
sigh

New place
scary sounds
alone at last
sigh

scary sounds
alone at last
feeling sad
sigh

alone at last 
feeling sad
closing doors
sigh

feeling sad
closing doors
moving on
sigh

closing doors
moving on
midnight walk
*sigh*
