I don't usually get this pissed
But this attitude I can't dismiss
Why are most movers and shakers
Most likely to be the trouble makers

I do my job, and I do it well
If you don't believe me, go to hell
I hate being tramped on by higher ups
Who have no friends except their coffee cups

In this world we are taught to succeed
Being fueled by power and greed
This is definitely not me
I don't need rose colored glasses to see

So to all you people who think you are better than me
More powerful than me
Have more money than me
Just know I do get pissed, but it won't consume me!

When I leave this office behind
You are gone from my mind
But since I have a few more hours before I can go
Stay out of my face and leave me alone!
