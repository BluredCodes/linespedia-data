title: "And If"
writer: "Silver Angel"

If 

His eyes were possibly any kinder 
I'd willingly stay lost in them forever

And if

His hands were any softer 
I'd surely die knowing the touch of love

And if

His arms could hold me any tighter
I'd be branded in his heart forever

And if

His smiles were any brighter
I'd be blinded by it's glare

And if

His kisses were any deeper
I'd never come up for air

And if

I didn't have his love
I'd be absolutely no where
