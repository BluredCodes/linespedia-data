---
title: "The Dance"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "the-dance"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Can you see my heart beating 
through my blazing eyes
or through my skin 
feel the echo of my sighs

As your lips trace 
the hollows of my neck 
I know that I should stop you 
but my mind says what the heck

Your hands upon my body 
leaves me breathless, yet on fire
Looking in your eyes 
I tingle with desire

My body betrays me quickly
knowing what's in store
All shyness set aside 
as my hands boldly explore

Gently touching places 
I knowingly touched before
Your hands glide sweetly over me 
and I ache to have more

My body answers willingly 
in full gasping replies 
I hold you closely to me
and slowly close my eyes

Passion builds as my heart trills 
eagerly needing to be one
Once the dance has started 
we can't stop until the song is done

Our music playing faster 
in a rhythm danced for years
Esctasy so powerful 
that it brings us both to tears

We reached a high-point where 
our souls have touched the stars
My mind has left my body 
as it dances from afar

Kissing you so deeply 
thankful for the love that we have found
The dance is silently slowing 
my feet back on the ground

Wrap your arms around me
as I drift off to sleep
Save all your dances for me
upon your dance list sheet
