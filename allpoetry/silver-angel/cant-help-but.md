---
title: "Can't Help But..."
writer: "Silver Angel"

I can't help but wonder
What is going through his head
Or what he thinks is going through mine
I miss him tremendously

I can't help but wonder
If he realizes the effect he has on me
Everything that touches him
Touches me

I can't help but wonder
If I'll ever get him back
Just spending time with him
Will be enough

I can't help but wonder
If he misses me too
If he does
Will he reach out and draw me near

I can't help but wonder
Why I can't get him out of my mind
Or if I ever will
Or if I really want to
