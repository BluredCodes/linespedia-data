As I sit here
My mind takes me to you
And I realize
I missed your smile today
So busy we were with 
Our morning drill
Getting dressed, brushing teeth
Helping kids off to school
Each having our own things to get done
Then having to quickly run
Out the door for work
Now I look back 
My heart aches inside
A piece is missing
And I wish to have you 
In my arms 
Wanting the smile you give me
When I look in your eyes
Regretting that I didn't slow down
Regeretting that I missed your smile
