---
title: "Never Part"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "never-part"

---

I tried so hard 
To look the other way
I tried to put you
Out of my heart

But, I can't deny
The way you make
My heart pound

I can't refuse
To feel your arms
Surrounding my soul

To the world
We are not perfect
But to each other
We are 

If I put half the time
I spend in convincing
Myself you don't care
Into treasuring what we have
We'd be so much happier

I'll hold my hands 
Over my ears
When others say
You are not good enough

Truth is
I'm not good to anyone
When I feel unloved
Unwanted

I feel wanted
And I feel loved
When I'm with you
Isn't love
The ultimate goal

Take my hand
Hold my heart
Kiss my soul

And tell me
We'll never part
