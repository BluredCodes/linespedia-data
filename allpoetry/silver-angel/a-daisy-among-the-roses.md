I'm a daisy among the roses
Yes, that would be me

There's nothing special about me
At least nothing your eyes can see

I am a simple woman
There is nothing complex about me

I am your truest friend
That's the way I choose to be

I'll help you up that mountain
With nothing but my will

I'll lead you to that river
So you can drink your fill

And if your heart gets broken
I'll fix it up again

It's not hard for me to do
Because you are my friend

In friendship I extend my hand
In love I open my heart

May we always have each other
When our lives are falling apart

Some say I am an angel
Because my heart is true

I was put here for a reason
To be a friend to you

If you see me in the distance
But you're not quite sure your right

I'm the daisy among the roses
Smiling in the warm sunlight
