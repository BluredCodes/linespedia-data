---
title: "With Me"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5024-With-Me-by-Silver-Angel"
---

Take my hand
Walk with me 
I'll show you 
Things never seen

Lend an ear
Listen with me
I'll tell you
Things never told

Give your heart
Love with me
I'll grant you
Things never known
