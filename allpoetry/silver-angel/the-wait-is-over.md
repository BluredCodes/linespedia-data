---
title: "The Wait Is Over"

He has been waiting a long, long time
Life on earth has been a joyous one for him

But that light at the end of the tunnel is so bright
Surely his wife and family will understand, right?

His bones are brittle and his eyes refuse to see
What little hair he last left is grey, no...silvery

He stayed as long as he possibly could
It's time to go, he knows he should

Saying one last prayer, he slumps his shoulders
Good-bye my wife, my sons and daughters

I see fields of rainbows where angels hover
It's time to go, the wait is overThis poem is dedicated to a very dear friend of my family who was suffering with black lung.  He rests in peace now.
