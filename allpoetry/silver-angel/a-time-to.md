A Nation in shock
Numbed by the horror
Stunned by pure hatred
Left to ask why?......Why?

Now we must pickup the pieces
It's a time to pull together
Lend that helping hand
Give your total support

We can come together as one
One great, strong, powerful nation
Those who kill will not go unpunished
God knows who they are

Now is a time to pray
Thanking God for opening his arms
Welcoming all those who have perished
Into those pearly gates of heaven

It's a time to reflect
A time to hold loved ones
A time to say "I love you"
A time to build up what has been shattered

We have inner strength
We can find the power with in
We are stronger than you think
We will survive!
