PART II

As she hovered there
Many pictures went through her mind
Her mama, her papa
Mia her sister and Bry her brother

Could she survive never seeing them again
She was only inches from his lips
Her head was spinning
Butterflies took over her tummy
The longer she looked into his eyes
The more the rest of the world melted away

Before she could completely makeup her mind
He lurched forward and claimed her lips
A volt of electricity went through her body
She could almost swear she felts stars whizzing past her

The kiss seemed to last forever
She wasn't willing to pull away
Never had she felt the emotions she was feeling now
Her young body was more alive 
Than it ever had been before

It was her first kiss and her body wanted more
The next thing she felt was 
Hard ground under her feet
Her eyes flew open, she stepped back, oh..

Her wings were gone
Her disappointment only lasted a few seconds
For he stepped close to her 
And put his head on her shoulder
He whispered...

I will show you things you have never seen
Take you places you have only dreamed of
Never doubt my love...
Even though you lost your ability
To fly, you gained so much more, a never ending love
Come, be mine

She slowly moved away
But only far enough 
To look into his eyes once more
I regret nothing she told him
I now feel complete

Teach me, love me
I am yours for eternity
She feared nothing for she knew 
She found the other half of her soul

Her blood still racing
She leaned forward and kissed him
And new pictures flashed in her mind
Pictures of love and happiness

Even though she was afire
She pulled away from him once more and smiled
Her eyes daringly taunted him
She had never felt so alive!

Together they ran over the wide oped fields
And they frolicked in the springtime streams
And lived happily ever after!!
