Reality
Coming full speed ahead
A runaway train of truth
There's no getting away
I'm not going to have 
Another piece of pain pie
"No thank you, not today!"
I've had enough of that
Buy one, get one free special
So I'll turn around
Look reality in the eye
Take it like the women I am
And start living again
