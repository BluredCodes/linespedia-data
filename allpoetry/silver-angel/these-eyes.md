---
title: "These Eyes"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "these-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

These eyes have seen so much
And yet you say, I don't see
This heart has felt so much
And yet you say, I don't feel
These arms have reached so far
And yet you say, I don't touch
These legs have walked so far
And yet you say, I don't move

I have seen miracles and misery
I have felt happiness and sadness
I have touched love and pain
I have walked in health and in death
And yet you say, I don't know this world
Truth is, I do know
And still I go on
Living with these eyes
