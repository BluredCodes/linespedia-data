title: "Crystal Heart"
writer: "Silver Angel"

Hold steady my crystal heart
In your grasp it won’t depart
Keep it far from the cold night air
It will never survive way out there

You need not put it on a pedestal up high
Or locked in a safe far from evil’s eye
Hold it where it will always be warm
Always keeping it away from harm

Hold my heart where I hold yours
A place that has no revolving door
Once your love was placed inside
It became crystal, just like mine
