~Rain~ Gently the rain fallsTickling me from head to toeI feel new again ~Morning~Daylight awakensBirds start singing morning songsFeeling fresh and new ~Cold~ Freezing to the touchMissing the warmth of your kissSun, please come out soon! ~Snow Storm~Slippery sidewalksGlazed over with winters lustThe storm rages on
