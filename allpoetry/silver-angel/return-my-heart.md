title: "Return My Heart"
writer: "Silver Angel"

I've done all I can possibly do
Cried endless hours over you
There has never been any other man in my life
I was so happy for 18 years being your wife
But time and circumstance has changed us both
Things are not unfolding as I hoped

We have gone through some really tough times
Even God agreed it was a crime
Seems we are doing things in reverse
Is being happy suppose to be a curse
We've climbed those mountains of pain
And walked with death in the rain

We made it through
Just me and you
But now all we do is hurt 
Let this continue, I can't 
I give you your freedom
Please grant me mine

Before I go, before we part
Could you please, return my heart
