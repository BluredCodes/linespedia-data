title: "Sometimes....."
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "sometimes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sometimes.....I get so angry
It's the little things that hurt so much

It's the stupid things that ruin lives
Sometimes.....I give up or in

Just to stand back and say,,,,, fool
You knew better and yet you let this go on

Sometimes......I have no reason to smile
No reason to live, no reason to exist

I know I shouldn't feel this way, but
Sometimes.....I don't know how I am suppose to feel

Feelings are hurt so quickly, and so easily
Sometimes.....I sit back and wonder about death

Sometimes I wonder if it is better to die
Where I no longer have to answer the "whys"

Then,,, as quickly as these thoughts entered my mind
They are gone, because

Sometimes.....It takes me seeing someone eles's pain
To know just how damned lucky I truly am
