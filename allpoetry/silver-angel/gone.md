---
title: "Gone"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "gone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3742-Gone-by-Silver-Angel"
---

Gone are two beautiful buildings
Gone are thousands of beautiful people
Gone is part of America

I saw first hand those beautiful buildings
I met first hand those beautiful people
I experienced first hand that part of America

My heart goes out to the families
My prayers go out to survivors
My soul goes out to all the new angels
