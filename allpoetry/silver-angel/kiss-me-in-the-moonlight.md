---
title: "Kiss Me In The Moonlight"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "kiss-me-in-the-moonlight"
source: "AllPoetry"

Do what your heart says is rightI know what you are thinkingCome, kiss me in the moonlightLove me in the shades of nightOur lips were made for kissingDo what your heart says is rightTouch me, bring me such delightFeel what our hearts were missingCome, kiss me in the moonlightShow me love, a wond'rous sightMy soul can't stop it's shakingDo what your heart says is rightLove me now, love me tonightTo stop this heart from breakingCome, kiss me in the moonlightWishing with our hearts great mightThe moon so softly sinkingDo what your heart says is rightCome, kiss me in the moonlight
