---
title: "~Dreaming~"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "dreaming"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sitting here by my window
Hands to my cheek
Dreaming of things to come
The day is rainy and dreary here
But not so far away 
The sun shines
Warming hearts 
Making souls happy and content
It would be so easy 
To float myself out of here
If only through my mind
Traveling to where 
The sun always shines
A place where prince's and princess's
Dance with rainbows
And glittery fairies play with the moon
I wish I could go there soon

Then, phones ring, voices rise
People needing this and that
Work needs to be completed
Snapping me back to reality

But...

Before life overwhelms me again
I take one more look out my window
Hands to my cheek
Dreaming of things to come
