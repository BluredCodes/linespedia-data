---
title: "I Just Don't Know"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "i-just-dont-know"

I just don't know 
why the world is the way it is
And should I be concerned 
when life has lost it's fizz

You put your trust 
in the hands of one
Then you look back 
and cringe at what you have done

I just don't know
all the ways of this world
I may live a lifetime
and not know all that is cruel

But first impressions 
are the most important
When you just don't know
Take heed your hearts warning 

Life is for experiencing and for learning
Experience may come, but not without a price
Don't sink to levels
that you know are not nice

Will we learn from our mistakes
We'll have to see
You and me... because
I just don't know
