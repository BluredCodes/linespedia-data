Sitting here
Under this canopy
Watching as
Drops of happiness
Splash on the ground
Around me

I reached out my hand
And felt something
It was warm
It was beautiful
But frightening
I retreated

But wished
The wind would blow
That sweet happiness
On me from where I sat
Under my canopy
Of darkness
