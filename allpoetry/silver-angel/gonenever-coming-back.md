I stood beside his grave today
As the wind helped the trees to sway
The flowers that I placed upon the ground
Made a strange contrast on the dead ones I found

I touched the cold grey gravestone
Looking for warmth
Like the warmth that I once found in his eyes
Tears slid down my face, as my mind asked why

Why was his life cut so short
I need to know for, my own comfort
Call me greedy, I just don't care
Looking at his grave is hard to bare

Memories crowd my head
I still haven't accepted he's dead
I have to get my life on track
And realize, he's gone....never coming back


***In loving memory of a very dear friend who died of cancer a year ago. At age 13 I fell head over heels for him. Now I'm on my knees, praying that God is caring for him!***
