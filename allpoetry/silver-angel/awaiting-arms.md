---
title: "~Awaiting Arms~"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "awaiting-arms"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh my God
I'm going to die
I can't get out
Forgive me 
For what I am about to do
My death is sure to come
There is no way out
No way up
Just down
I don't want to die this way
I don't want to burn
I don't want to breathe in
This black death
I don't want to die slow and painfully
Dear Lord,
Hear my prayers
Please help my family
To understand 
Why I choose to go this way
I'm on the ledge
I feel the wind
Dear Lord,
Catch me as I fall
Into your awaiting arms
