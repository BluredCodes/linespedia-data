---
title: "Life's Quilt"
writer: "Silver Angel"

Each picture tells a story
Some happy and some sad
Bringing to life the memories
Of the times I've had

I revile in the feelings
Just tingle from head to toe
Amazed at the small things
That get my tears to flow

I get lost in the emotions
You can see it in my face
Eyes that show devotion
With just a touch of grace

I love the way a picture
Puts a smile upon my face
Taking me back to a moment
Of another time and place

I touch pictures of my babies
And my heart nearly breaks
Because I know they love me
A feeling words can not relate

Sifting through the memories
Of the life I have built
Each photograph a stitch
On the tapestry of my life's quilt
