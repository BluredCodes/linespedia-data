title: "Gift of Love"
writer: "Silver Angel"

The sun was so in love with the moon
But the moon was miles and miles away
Each day the sun rose 
Knowing he could not touch his love
But still he would smile on the outside
Casting his rays to her for all to see
For in his heart he knew the moon felt the same

The moon was very much in love with the sun
But her beams had to travel miles and miles
Each night she rose in just enough time
To see her beloved lay down to rest
And even though she knew in her heart
That being together was just a dream
It didn't dull the smile on her face
For she knew in her heart the sun felt the same

One day the sun no longer wanted to live 
Knowing he would never be able to kiss his love
He knew that he could never hold her in his arms
For he was born a sun and she born a moon
They were far to different from each other
The moon saw her sun's rays were not as bright
She saw him let the rain take over their world
She knew in her heart he was planning to depart

She called out to him, Sun.. my love
Please don't leave me this way
I can not live, not one day without you
The sun looked to his love and cried
But how can we ever be one, you are so far away
The moon thought and thought, then all at once
She began to glow, brighter and brighter she became
Until tiny sparkling orbs burst from her heart

The orbs filled the sky with rainbows of light
The sun looked at her in awe
He had never before seen a love so strong
Could it be he loved her even more
Once the orbs had all found a place in the sky
She whispered to the sun, I love you
Now when you want to touch me
Just reach out and shine your light

When you can't see me, touch these stars
And they will bring your love to me
When I can't lay my eyes on your shining face
I'll send a smile to you through these glittering orbs
No matter where you travel 
Or how far apart we may become 
We will always be one, forever joined by the stars
