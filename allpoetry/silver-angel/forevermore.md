---
title: "Forevermore"
writer: "Silver Angel"
slug: "forevermore"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3967-Forevermore-by-Silver-Angel"
---

Raining smiles inside my headWith memories of satin upon my bedVisions of purest delightEmbedded in my heart tonightTake me to that lovely placeWhere leathery fingers touch silky laceTravel over mountains of love Across plains of pleasure andInto a forest of fantasyFlying me high to touch the starsThen gently envelope my heart In your powerful arms of loveYes, take me to that place once moreWhere it will always rain smilesUpon you, upon me, forevermore
