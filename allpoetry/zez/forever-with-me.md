---
title: "Forever With Me"
writer: "Zez"

The day you took your love from me,
My soul you crushed for all to see.
My heart went weak, my eyes went blind,
In me I lost my will to be kind.

In me I lost my rational thought,
Terribly sick and living for naught.

And when it's time to curl and die,
This bitter hate will fill your sky.
With every sun and every moon,
You'll always hear my angry tune.

And when you feel my cold wet breath,
You'll know my pain from after death.
I'll always care to hold you tight,
Project your fear, your final fight.

The day I come, yourself you've brought,
Forever we'll be, together in rot.finally figured out the last part
