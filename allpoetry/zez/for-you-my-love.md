For You My Love


As we walk through the woods,
Down the well known trail,
I feel the warmth-
Of your skin, of the sun, of my blood.
I smell the leaves, their sweet recent rot.

As we move deep into the woods,
Into darker, thicker air....,
I feel the cold.....air,
   The wet air,...

We stop.
Face eachother, .... and 
Don't understand the chill.

The chill that's killing the warmth,
With it's biting cold teeth,
And it's poison stench.

I reach to you, grasping, stretching
Needing
Wanting this painful and smashing and crushing...
And ,
Needed,
--This thing must leave!

As my vision dims, and my senses numb,
I lose track of you,
Lose your face and arms, and sweet you.

As I fall victim, I fall away,...
From whom I've tried so hard to become, 

For you my love,

For you, my love,

FOR YOU MY LOVE!!!!!the dark that haunts me, comes forth, and terrifies me.
