Thoughts on Death Row


A flash across my face I sense,
The moon on steel in slash so hence...
The end I fear has come now near. 

I look into the dark of night,
To try and see the face of light…
But all I see is emptiness.

I grasp at all the love I've known,
And try to lose the hate I've thrown…
But only find the pain I've dealt.

So now I know the time has come,
To bow to He who surely won…
The demon who has lived in me.

Now I stand above my pit,
No longer do I cringe from it...
I guide the knife into my life 

And dive into my death.
