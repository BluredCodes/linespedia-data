I eat your scent,
And suck your skin.
I taste your soul,
And feel your sin.

Your fingertips
And succulent nips
And so wet lips
And perfect hips

Under your arms,
The base of your neck,
Sweet wonderful you...
IM SUCH A WRECK!!!!
