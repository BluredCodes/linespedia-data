---
title: "My Vase"
writer: "Zez"
slug: "my-vase"

---

As your hand gently slides
Over the smooth cold shell,
You feel ripples of paint,
Fired so well.

With tall neck worn
And narrow base chipped,
You see all the signs,
Of glue quickly dipped.

A dust covered rim
Stained from all that’s died,
Leaving nothing much more,
Than emptiness inside.
