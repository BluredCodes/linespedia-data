title: "With hugs and kisses"
writer: "Zez"

With hugs and kisses,
And tight embrace.
And thoughts of your hair,
In front of your face.

In dreams I will see,
Sweet visions of you,
With your supple sweet skin
Glistening with dew.

The warm sun shines,
On your body come morn,
And with that sight
My love will be born.

I wake and I know,
Only hours ago,
Twas only a muse
I would never know.

So fore my soul shades,
And fore my eyes wake,
I plea one more time
You'll be by my side.
