The Rat In Me

Crouching, trembling,
I curled myself
Into the smallest bit 
Of walked over, passed by,
Unseen, untouched
Chunk of
Thrown away 
Life,
To ever live.

And I see where it's at,
And I grab all it's got,
The pile of sweet
And filth and rot.

And work my way in,
Sucking all the juice
From this place
Of lost dreams.
