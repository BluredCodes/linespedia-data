They say that it's a dog's life
but I beg to differ
and if you don't believe me
to my experiences, refer

Although I'm still all female
my form the Lord did re-align
where I used to be human
I am now totally feline

No more need to rise at dawn
to earn my daily bread
I can choose to lie around
or bug Rover when he's being fed

I have my master wrapped around
my sleek and silky tail
and when I rub along his leg
he grants my wishes, will never fail

I pick and choose where I will sleep
and no one disagrees
I run the home , the master too
and do just as I please

Although you may be quite concerned
don't fret for me my friend
with another eight lives to go
I'll be pampered till the end

Meoooooooooow!
