---
title: "My Eyes"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "my-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

My eyes open, I see him
My lover, my mate, my husband
My eyes open, I touch him
His face, his mouth, his body
My eyes open, I receive him
His touch, his warmth, his love

But, my eyes closed, I see the other
My dream lover, my friend, my soul mate
My eyes closed, I know him
His dreams, his thoughts, his hopes
My eyes closed, he takes me
To him, with him, in him
