---
title: "He knows who he is"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "he-knows-who-he-is"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30634-He-knows-who-he-is-by-wiccatarot"
---

I've thought of him, yes as a friend
but even more,to a better end

I've been with him, in only dreams
but what we did was real, it seems

the wild sex, so hard and fast
our bodies hot, the heat full blast

he put me up against a door
he wouldn't stop, I wanted more

he spoke to me, he teased me so
i want release, he said "no...no"

he is ruthless in his control
as am I to reach my goal

I want to see him sweat and shatter
feel him deep within me gather

my body's tense, I feel the power
I see his eyes, there is the hunger

we say no words, we understand
just one more thrust the thrill expands

as I recount this dream of mine
I relive the thrill, I cross the line

he lives so far, and truth be told
it is all good, or we'd be bold

for,tho you see, he does contest
this infatuation puts him to the test

he does deny the way we feel
afraid to cope, we're afraid to deal

we'll just keep on,doing what we do
caring, dreaming, muddling through
