title: "You"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

a touch, so soft
it entices my senses

a smile, so sweet
it brightens my day

a look, so deep
it warms my soul

a kiss, so gentle
it awakens my love

all this, my joy
which I have found in you
