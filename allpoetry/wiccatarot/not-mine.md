---
title: "Not Mine"
writer: "wiccatarot"

He is not mine,i have no say
he lives his life his way

He is not mine,i told him so
and encouraged him to go

He is not mine, i should not feel
my world spin like a wheel

He is not mine,my mind it wanders
to the whys and whats he ponders

He is not mine,i'll wait no longer
my loneliness grows stronger

He is not mine,my mind it knows
as in my heart denial grows
