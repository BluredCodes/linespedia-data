There is no more hesitation
Our movements frenzied and fast
Cotton and silk are in the way
But skin is touching at last

And still not a word from him
His eyes and hands implore me
There is no need, I can't turn back
I'm his, to do with as he decree

The petal's softness on my back
His lips hungry, on my body feast
I find him, feel him, take him
As mutual craving frees the beast

His breath is heavy in my ears
A sweet staccato song
It fires my urge, it makes me bold
And as we merge I'm where I belong

His face my only focus
Our rapture reaching its peak
Our bodies tangling, thrashing now
Heated, throbbing, sweat sleek

As we both go over the edge
From his lips escapes my name
We're anchored in fulfillment's realm
Wrapped in love's all consuming flame
