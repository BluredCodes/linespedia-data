Strawberries their heart-shaped tangy sweetness
Pick them off the plant, hands red, what a mess!

A pungent odor of long summer days
A family fun day, under sun's rays

Eat twice as many as you can pick
No need of water, fingers set, lick !

Look forward to strawberry jam, so nice
Or bathe some in liquor and add some spice
