I've tried and tried and still I fail
To write the words that souls will hail
Instead my words although not stale
Run to thoughts where senses prevail

I write of fantasies, all mine
Where bodies meet and limbs entwine
Where being naughty is so fine
And a lover's touch , the right sign

I do so try to keep it pure
By using words that are demur
Yet as I edit you may be sure
The words I change are all a lure

Well since they say "Go with the flow"
Erotic is where I will go
You are all welcome to the show
Just read them fast and shower slow !
