---
title: "Vanilla Oil"
writer: "wiccatarot"

you look at me and tease me
with the bottle in your hand
it's tiny , lovely, , and delicate
and my ticket to an erotic land

no need for you to ask me twice
my clothes i'll shed with pleasure
just the thought of your hands on me
brings me joy in great measure

you help me lie down on the bed
your touch is so electrifying
my body trembles, such sweet thoughts
my mind already soaring


you pour that sweet warm nectar
all down my waiting back
my senses start to reel and
of passion there is no lack

i smell the sweetness of vanilla
feel the warmth of your gliding fingers
my body shivers, my breathing uneven
your touch is firm , but never lingers

you gently roll me over and
prepare to complete your task
my mind is wandering, my body thrilled
my eyes reflect my soul, as in your eyes i bask

my silent wishes can't deter you
from your chosen sensual way
as you keep working your magic on me
i lose all track of time and day

but as my eyes are closed in exstacy
you reach your ultimate goal
you take me with you in untold passion
you free my mind, body and soul
