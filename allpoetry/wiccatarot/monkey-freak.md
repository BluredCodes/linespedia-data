My daughter has a sweet fixation
not strange,for a teen,you say?
it started slowly,two years ago
and keeps growing to this day

Her online nickname is Monkey Freak
so believe me when I say
she just goes gaga and loses it
when she sees something "MONKEY"

Her collection starts with furry pals
they soon will fill up her wall
they are small and big and tall and short
and she's named them one and all

A second part of this sweet madness 
is seen in the clothes she wears
it may be sockies or a t-shirt
monkeys seen in ones or pairs

The last part of this funny hang up
are the cute pictures she gets
of babies,mommies and apes galore
as cartoons or as real pets

It isn't just a flight of fancy
there is a reason for it
I asked her once about her primates
and her answer was "Please sit"

"Please mom don't think of me as silly
my reason is a good one
it has to do with thoughts of Grandma
especially since she's gone

You know, she had a pet name for me
and if you remember well
she used to call me her little chimp"
I could just feel my heart swell

So now you see why I endorse her
in her monkey search so pure
it stunts the pain felt by Grandma's loss
a fun and innocent cure.
