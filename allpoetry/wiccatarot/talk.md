title: "Talk"

it's funny how you get ideas
of how some people are
by words you hear and things they say
and all this from afar

but speak to them and listen too
and you will surely find
someone, unlike the rumors
a human soul and mind

you dig a little deeper
and get to know them better
you sometimes find a friend
a soulmate or a lover

whatever happens, just take a chance
and talk to someone new
and you may find the one you met
may be your other you
