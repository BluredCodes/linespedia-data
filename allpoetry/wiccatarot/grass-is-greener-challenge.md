she's so nice 
she listens to me 
I can tell her anything 
and no judgement will there be 

she's so sweet 
she makes me laugh 
so quick with her comebacks 
I can't seem to get enough 

she's so sexy 
she turns me on 
I love to be with her 
want her to play me, i'm her pawn 

she's so annoying 
she just bugs me 
can't she just understand 
I have others to meet and see? 

she's such a bitch 
she just can't get it 
it just won't work between us 
and the other will be here in a bit 

NOW THE OTHER 

she's so nice.........
