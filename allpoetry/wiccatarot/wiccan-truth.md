"An it harm none"
a simple creed
a belief in moon and sun

In nature's contrasts
and lunar cycles
potions are made and spells are cast

No forcing of love
no hurting of foe
good feelings enhanced, bad ones dismissed

Tarot speak to me
when they will it so
not meant to frighten,but to guide

Female form prevails
Matriarchal rule
The Goddess smiles on all

Beltane and Samain
Imbole, Lughnasadh
Celebrating life year roundTHE WICCAN LAW

Bide the Wiccan Law ye must,
In perfect love and perfect trust.
Eight words the Wiccan Rede fulfill:
An' ye harm none, do what ye will.
What ye send forth comes back to thee
So ever mind the law of three.
Follow this with mind and heart,
Merry ye meet, and merry ye part.

Blessed Be.
