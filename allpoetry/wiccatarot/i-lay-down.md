---
title: "I Lay Down"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "i-lay-down"
source: "AllPoetry"

I lay down here
my eyes they close
my love he does await me

I lay down here 
my mind it opens
my love he comes to me

I lay down here 
my heart it swells
my love he fills it so

I lay down here
my body ready
my love completes me so
