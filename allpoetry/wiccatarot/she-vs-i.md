---
title: "She      vs      I"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "she-vs-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29189-She------vs------I-by-wiccatarot"
---

she is mother and wife
i want to be woman and friend

she is caregiver and worker
i want to be cherished and protected

she is ever there and always ready
i want to be elsewhere and my own

she is dependable and manageable
i want to be free and wild

she is mate and daughter
i want to be lover and individual

She and I we are but one
THEN
Why won't She set I free?
