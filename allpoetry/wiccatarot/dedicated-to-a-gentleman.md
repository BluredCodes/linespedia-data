---
title: "Dedicated to a Gentleman"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "dedicated-to-a-gentleman"
source: "AllPoetry"

Thoughts of you run through my mind
in sleep or when awake
I smile, cherish them all
my thirst for you they slake

You bring me fun and pleasure
and make my heartbeat skip
your wit is quite refreshing
may I have one more sip?

You're different from the others
I'm not complaining dear
anticipation mounting
can't wait till you are here

You ask me how my day was
and did i get some sleep
your sweetness touches my heart
your concern runs so deep

I feel like such a lady
when in your company
your thoughtfulness amazes
could this be all for me?

Then with some well-chosen words
you light a spark in me
the lady is forgotten
as the wanton you free

You take me on a slow ride
quite focused on your task
and even now, I come first
"What are your needs?" you ask

your attention touches me
and I get lost in you
as you take me further still
my senses you renew

As the summit we approach
"Join me" I entice you
your reply, selfless yet
"If it please you, my dear"

This pleasure which you give me
it gratifies my soul
and in our final joining
you are what makes me whole
