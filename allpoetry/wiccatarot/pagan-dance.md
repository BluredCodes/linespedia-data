I lie beside my mate at night
His hand for me it searches
It finds my thigh,it wanders on
And to my core it reaches

It lingers there a little while
He knows just what to do
My body it awakens as
I feel the heat go through

The need it spirals slowly up
My mind it hears an anthem
His skillful fingers change the beat
But never miss the rhythm

He stirs,he shifts,he moves above me
I see his eyes so deep
he bends, he whispers in my ear
"Now babe, are you asleep?"

He needs no answer from my lips
He feels my legs enslave him
And then again we fall into
That Dance, ancient and pagan
