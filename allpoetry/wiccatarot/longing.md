i so wish my thoughts could awaken you
and bring you here to me
but since that isn't so
i'll close my eyes and see

you lying there upon your bed
your skin by moonlight glows

i touch your hair
i see you smile
i venture on for just a while

i graze your nipple lightly
i see u buck and moan
i can not slow now
i must go on a little lower

i feel the heat deep in my palm
i feel u hard and solid
i see your body quivering

as you come to your senses

i'm sorry love........lie back sweet love
this dream will be fulfilled
