---
title: "I wish I could"
writer: "wiccatarot"
slug: "i-wish-i-could"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41916-I-wish-I-could-by-wiccatarot"
---

rant and rave......
not be brave

make him understand.........
love can not be planned

believe him............
but his words are so dim

give this another try.........
but i just cry

make him see.........
never listens to my plea

know he wants ME........
but he wants what he wants me to be

feel like he means it.......
but he only wants me to submit

save my heart from breaking........
is this pain worth taking?
