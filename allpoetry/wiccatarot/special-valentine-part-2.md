As I walk out of the elevator
I know exactly where to go
My soul senses him, THERE !
My mind tells me , take it slow

As I reach the door it opens
He's standing there, eyes hooded
The air around us musky and thick
I'm guided into sanctuary, caution unheeded

The room glows with scented candles
Shadows they create dancing on the wall
Their soft light reflecting on the bed
The mood they set, a tantalizing call

Red rose petals strewn everywhere
Mist the air with enticing allure
Crushed under unsteady footsteps
Damaged, wilted, no longer pure

Soft music barely heard
Touching heart chords
Sweeping away tension
Souls communicating without words

In the midst of such sensual onslaught
We turn to each other as if on cue
Hesitant yet impatient for first touch
Filled with wanting and need, all passion's due

He slowly raises his hand to my face
And I am transfixed by the movement
Feel the warmth of his palm on my cheek
As his thumb strokes my lip, sweet torment

His eyes unmoving from my lips
I place my hand on his
Taken over by wanton feelings
I brush his palm with a kiss

The need explodes within us
Lips searching...tasting
Tongues meshing...savoring
Lust unleashed...desire mounting




To be or not to be continued...?That is the question....which i put to you all....
I will bend to your wishes........
