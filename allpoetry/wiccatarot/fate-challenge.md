Doctor called me today 
Today I learned my fate 
Fate has decreed my end 
End of my life 
Life to live for one more week 
Week to forgive and make peace 
Peace offered to enemies, accepted by some 
Some loved ones rage against death 
Death to all will come 
Come talk to me of memories past 
Past loves, remembered with fondness 
Fondness warming my heart 
Heart beating ever slow 
Slow to reach the light 
Light of life eternal
