V iens mon
A mour te
L ivrer a moi
E ternel sera
N otre plaisir
T ous deux
I mmerse dans l'autre
N os ames toujours
E nsemble

* * * * * * * * * * * * 

Come to me
My love
Give yourself to me
For eternal will be
Our pleasure
Both of us
Lost in the other
Our souls forever
Joined
