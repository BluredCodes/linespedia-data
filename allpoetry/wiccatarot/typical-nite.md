A typical night at my house
in the living room we'll be found
my hubby and i , relaxing
and our kids, no where to be found

My hubby lying on the couch
i'm sitting at the computer
he flips through the channels again
while online, i'm playing tutor

His eyes closing, soon he'll be gone
the TV is watching him now
let him sleep for a few hours
then wake him and tuck him in, meow

While he's sleeping i'm still online
chatting with friends and playing games
i'm having fun and time just flies
they're so real to me, not just names

time now to put hubby to bed
tell all my friends, be right back
gonna show him some sweet loving
don't want him to feel a lack

As i hear him gently snoring
i know i'll be getting up soon
you see i'm quite insomniac
go to bed bout six, get up at noon
