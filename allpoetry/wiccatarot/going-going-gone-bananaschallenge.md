Bananas are my most favorite fruit
And I mean what we eat, not slang for loot

Grown in warm climates on tall lanky trees
Bunches grown full, on trees standing in vees

From their peel all golden,shiny and smooth
The sweet smell makes my mouth water,forsooth

My mouth waters as I await the treat
Of their center all firm, fleshy and sweet

You can eat them as is, au natural
Or dress them up to just share with a pal

Take thick chocolate syrup and dip them in
Add nuts or whipped cream, watch party begin

Although those who read this had thoughts impure
My aim was innocent and that's for sure !     :)
