title: "Lost without you"

I feel a heaviness inside
since you went away
I think of you with a smile
Each and every day

I miss the way we used to talk
the way you made me laugh
the strangeness of our topics
LOL.....you were so daft

You sent me such a cryptic note
it left me in a bind
I didn't know what I could do
I felt so left behind

I finally sent a "Miss You" card
to which you DID reply
"miss you too, stay out of trouble!"
Was that your best try?

I'm so frustrated, how could you dare?
dismiss it all this way
not a reason, no explanation
was there nothing you wanted to say?

Well you're gone, but yet again
grrrrrrrrrrrrr my feelings are confused
some days I want to rage at you
on others, hold you till we're fused

All this rambling does have a point
I'd like to talk to you
So , Sweetie, if you do read this
I hope you'll know what to do.........
