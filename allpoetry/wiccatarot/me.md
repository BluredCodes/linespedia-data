I sit here as I look around
and I have to wonder
what is happening to me
as some decisions I ponder

I seem to have made up my mind
without my trying to
I'm not taking any crap
and especially not from you

I like me as I am today
and I deserve the best
if you can't give me respect
then you can simply K.M.A.

I've always done, for everyone
as daughter,wife,mother
finally now it's my turn
negative comments, don't bother

I'm not a child anymore
I'm woman through and through
I'm gonna live, have some fun
and guess what? ... with or without you !
