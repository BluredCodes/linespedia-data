Flowing waves of green grass
Touched by summer's light breeze

Dew-tipped prairie flowers
Kissed by sun's morning rays

Butterflies dancing mid air
Silent mystical ballet

Bees in their labor of love
Gathering busily yet happy

Tall trees in the distance
Guardians so proud and majestic

Birds patiently teaching
Young ones the freedom of flight

The colors, ah, bountiful beauty
God's palette, spread here before my eyes
