She comes online, she says,
"Hi hun,and how are you today?"

We chat about our daily lives, 
and what has come our way.

We confide and we opine, 
just like two sisters do.

We understand each other in 
our ups and downs and blues.

She makes me laugh when i am down, 
where does she get those jokes?

She is a font of untold hope 
but do not doubt her folks.

She is a woman experienced in life, 
through all its darkest colors.

She is a goddess, she has survived, 
with her sweet presence, my days to flavor.

Blessed Be my Sister-Friend
