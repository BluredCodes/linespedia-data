the need it grows inside me
so deep within my soul
my body cries out for relief
my heart it aches to soar
just one more touch,love
just one more kiss
your whispered words they stroke me
as do your hands, your eyes, your lips
your body, how it thrills me
now love,please love, just take me there
and don't you leave me go
I want to feel your shattering
run thru me, yes, just so........
