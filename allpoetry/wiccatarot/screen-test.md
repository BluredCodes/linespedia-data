it's easy to fall
into the trap
you chat, you laugh
you fill the gap

it's not for real
it's just for fun
you say hello 
i answer,"hun?"

you learn about 
each other's lives
the likes, dislikes
husbands? wives?

selective memory
hides some things
no need to hide
the wedding rings

it seems so innocent
but then one nite
it becomes more
can it be right?

the inhibitions
they fall aside
you take a risk
you take a ride

when next the sun
shines in the sky
you think of it
and wonder, why?

it isn't shame
or guilt you feel
which makes it worst
wow,what's the deal?

could it be this
this screen i face
that gives me flight
that gives me space?
