I want to see my children happy 
Like most mothers do
And be there when they need someone
Who knows them and is true

I want to make life easier
For my dad and in-laws, who
Have given so much to us all
But never asked their due

I want to spend time with my friends
And share all that we can
Although we are not blood-related
We are our own special clan

I want to keep growing with my hubby
And complete our dreams for two
May the Lord grant this for us
And for all of you too
