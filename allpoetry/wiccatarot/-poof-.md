title: "*** poof ***"

It's quite all right to look me up
and comment on my picture too
looking up profiles can be
an amazing, fun thing to do

Here's what surprises me about this
the fact some think it's an invite
to write to me and be quite bold
with a "Wanna Cyber?" how trite!

What happened to "Hi, how are you?"
and getting to know someone new
respectfully and tastefully
how to teach them to get a clue?

I already talk to nice guys
they are sweet and make me laugh so
talk about things under the sun
sports and life and foolishness, doh!

But these others who think they're hot
if an answer they await
will be in for a little shock
this fish ain't taking the bait!

It's not in me to be wicked
But it's a true pleasure I get
when I iggy and *** poof *** they're gone
I feel not a twinge of regret...   :)
