The card read " Come to me,
I await you for your pleasure,
Room 181, RSVP not needed,
Clothing at your leisure."

How could a woman ever think
To refuse such an offer?
I'll tell you this, my heartbeat rose
But my conscience made me suffer

Would I finally take the step
And go to meet my lover?
On a day dedicated to love?
Using work as my cover?

After many years of marriage
Of children and commitment
Our couple had grown apart
An online friendship was heaven sent

I thought the distance made it safe
To share my soul with him
But he was more courageous
Full of life, love and vim
 
He could not wait any longer
And so surprised me when
He sent that invitation
Written with love and a red ink pen

As I awoke on Valentine's day
My thoughts were all for him
I was still so undecided
And the tension made my sight go dim

And at that moment I just knew
My heart settled, my fate sealed
I'd take the step towards the unknown
My heart soared, my senses reeled

My preparations were so intimate
From my lilac scented bath water
To the application of my favorite perfume
To sexy lingerie, meant to be discarded later

As I drove towards my destiny
My body and mind in a state of longing
I could only feel and see him
Knowing I'd soon be smelling, touching, loving

As I walk to the elevator on shaky legs
My whole body aware and finely tuned
I slowly approach a state of panic
I'll be a fallen woman, i'll be ruined!

But as the elevator doors open
And I push the button for his floor
I feel a cloak of tranquility
Fall upon me, no worries, no more
