Lust : definition
an unbridled desire
an intense longing

Body vibrating
senses alive and feeling
searching another

Wanting the touching
needing to see and taste him
from his head to toes

Fingers itching now
aware of skin and pleasure
his pleasure and mine

Heart beating quickly
passions mounting, need so raw
craving heated rush
