Run the spin cycle , no lag
While it goes zig zag
Let's do it on the Maytag

You'll be my laundry room stag
Prove you're not a brag
Run the spin cycle, no lag

Shake me, wring me, I'm your rag
S&M, your bag?
Let's do it on the Maytag

I don't want to be a nag
Go babe, hoist your flag
Run the spin cycle, no lag

Standing still is such a drag
Make my tongue just wag
Let's do it on the Maytag

When we do the shag
No need for a gag
Run the spin cycle, no lag
Let's do it on the Maytag
