the rocking chair
how can it be
such an erotic piece

it sits there tamely in my room
where one and all can see

and yet with a few words
a few well chosen phrases

said rocking chair has now become
a reminder of lustful gazes

for was it not on such a chair
so innocent and wooden

where he placed me
and now i am beholden

the rocking chair i visualize
it has so been adorned

it sports four ties, four silken ties,
you're warned

he used them to enslave me
OH, how i did enjoy

his teasing, pleasing games
and being his girl-toy

so how can any say
the rocking chair is tame?

when all i see now is the place
of one Sweet & Lustful game
