Roses have been my most favorite blooms
Their color, their texture and sweet perfume
The velvet petals so soft to the touch
The spiky thorns, be careful or say ouch!
Red ones are seen for the meaning of love
Yellow are envy to contrast above
White ones are seen for purity of soul
Black ones are seen as a blatant death knoll
To "La vie en rose" sweet words we can sing
By any other name it's the same thing
While petals are strewn before a new bride
Don't forget the symbol of Texas pride
Offer me one or even a dozen
Just make them long-stemmed and keep them comin!
