you're there in front of the computer screen, as i come up behind you
you ask if u should get offline, i figure that's my cue

i tell you not to move, as i nibble on your ear
you tell me with a knowing smile "i really don't mind, dear"

your hands upon my face bring me closer to your lips
our lips they tease, they taste,they mesh, our toungues they take a dip

our lips unlock, i move before you and sit upon your lap
you  wrap your arms around my waist aand slowly close the gap

we are encumbered, just a bit, by all these clothes we're wearing
you get down to it, we lose control, our satisfaction nearing

i hear messages arrive, as the computer sings
but in a few, lost to the noises, we both spread our wings

you're there in front of the computer screen, as i come with you
you ask if you should get offline, i know that it's my cue
