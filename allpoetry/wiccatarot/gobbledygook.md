WARNING   :   THE WORDS ARE REAL...THEY HAVE NOT BEEN CHANGED TO PROMOTE THIS SILLINESS !  :)




never one to dillydally
my sweet Mr Toodles

once a week came up the alley
to share in lovers canoodles

waiting in my camiknickers
my mood quite cock-a-hoop

i'd usually be eating snickers
while empty thoughts loop-the-loop

nicknamed flibbertigibbet
i am discombobulated

but Mr Toodles sure does fret
when others think i'm blonde related

he says that it's all codswallop
and silly fiddle faddle

he just about blows his top
from his high horse and saddle

all i say is "fiddle dee dee"
and "hush my little dahhhling"

then i hug him close to me
and tease his ding-a-ling
