my hair is red
i feel so wild
have attitude
no flower child

got a tattoo
and one more thing
i'm 40 and healthy
just wanna swing

got some leather
to ride my chopper
speed down the highway
avoid the copper

i feel agressive
yeah, sexually
i am the hunter
comes naturally

i'll find him and
we'll do it right
keep up babe
we've got all nite

teach you tricks
you teach me yours
get our kicks
go on for hours

i'm satisfied
he's still wide-eyed
he's tired but happy
he thought i lied

going now
see you soon
we've got a date
the Next full moon !
