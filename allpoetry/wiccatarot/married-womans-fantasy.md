title: "married womans' fantasy"
writer: "wiccatarot"

he opens the door
   i'm breathless

he welcomes me in
   i'm trembling

he caresses my back
   i'm shaking

he embraces me
   i'm tingling

he feels my needs
   i'm melting

he begins his seduction
   i'm burning

he entices me
   i'm afraid

he touches me softly
   i'm lost
