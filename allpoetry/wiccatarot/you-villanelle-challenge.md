a touch, so soft 
it entices my senses 

a smile, so sweet 
it brightens my day 

a look, so deep 
it warms my soul 

a kiss, so gentle 
it awakens my love 

all this, my joy 
which I have found in you


****************************

I'm at a loss without you
the sight of you warms my soul
You are my life, my spiritual glue

Before you come into my view
the smell of your maleness refreshes my need
I'm at a loss without you

My excitement is at its debut
the touch of your hands entices my senses
You are my life, my spiritual glue

Like the freshness of morning dew
the taste of your lips awakens my love
I'm at a loss without you

Your expressions of love, so true
the sound of your laughter brightens my day
You are my life, my spiritual glue

I know that whatever we go through
My love for you I will pursue
I'm at a loss without you
You are my life, my spiritual glue
