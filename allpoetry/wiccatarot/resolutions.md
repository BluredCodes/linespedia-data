The new year is upon us, as the resolutions flow
We spout them out, we list them all, but what do we know?

The future holds its own surprises, for us lowly humans
It's just another Pandora's box, which each of us opens

The hopes and fears and all the rest just buzz around and then
We swat the bad, we grasp the good and start over again

So this year just to challenge fate, i propose to you
make no promises to break, no not even a few

Just take it step by step, take life one day at a time
Always be true to yourself and good to those without a dime

Forget the almighty dollar and open up your eyes
live with truth as your ally, hence no need to remember lies

In closing friends, I leave you this, my resolution is
Do unto others as you would have them do unto you........kiss kiss
