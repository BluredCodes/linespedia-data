title: "I lay down here  -  add-on"

I lay down here 
my eyes they close 
my love he does await me 

I lay down here 
my mind it opens 
my love he comes to me 

I lay down here 
my heart it swells 
my love he fills it so 

I lay down here 
my body ready 
my love completes me so

* * * * * * * * * * * * * 

I lay down here
my lips are smiling
my love he pleases me


I lay down here
my skin it tingles
my love caresses me

I lay down here
my senses reeling
my love he teases so

I lay down here
my soul it soars
my love he frees it so
