I see men in a different light 
and it gladdens my poor heart 
I look, admire and enjoy 
these objects of fine art 

be they young or old or middle-aged 
they simply attract my attention 
I can't help but stop and look 
it soothes my eyes, such a sweet lotion 

the physical is very nice 
but not the whole equation 
I look for character and charm 
and feel such sweet sensation 

I really can't say that I quote them 
from one to nine point nine 
I just give them little stars 
and yes,five means they're very fine!

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Now, since the day I posted
this silliness and fluff
I have received some comments
"Five stars are not enough!"

Well, I've come to the conclusion
that with the gents I quite agree
I have used my star system
but it's lacking, I've come to see

I've added five more stars
which makes it one to ten
but don't expect to find out
how I rate you gentlemen

You don't know when I'll show up
or how i'll chose to be
so be good to all females
and you'll get some stars from me !
