title: "Poem to my children"

They don't sell user guides
for new parents, and I know
we learn from our mistakes
as we handle the high and low

We think we're doing fine
as we watch them learn to walk
and we're proud to show them off
when they start to up and talk

We worry when they first start school
about how well they'll do
will they like their teacher
and will they make friends too?

When the teen years arrive 
we're all ready to give up
where's that sweet child of mine
and who's this who says "wassup?"?

We make it through the high school years
and we watch them choose their road
their happiness is all we want
and we're always there to lighten their load

A parents' work is never done
we love, we worry, we care, we nag
and no matter what the future brings
our feelings for our childeren will never lag.
