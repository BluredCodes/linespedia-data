"BRB, gonna shower"
Is what he just wrote

My fingers in their ways, set
And here is my quote

"Please , do leave the door open"
Then press enter,go

There before i realize
He's still on, OH NO!

"Wash my back?...and my front too?"
What have I done now?

Hormones are in overdrive
I'll answer and how!

"Do you any way you want"
His reply is slow

But finally he is typing
Anticipation grows

"Water's ready, come on in"
My heart skips a beat

My body vibrating now
The thrill is so sweet

The rest i'll leave up to you
to end as you will

Fellow poets, fantasize
And enjoy the thrill!
