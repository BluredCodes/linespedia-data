title: "Mind Games - (haiku challenge winner)"

My mind tells me 
what's right and wrong 

My heart tells me 
what's good and bad 

My treacherous body 
can not keep it's peace 

It tells me that it needs 
what my head and heart forbid

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

My mind does tell me
what things in my life are right
also which are wrong

My mind does tell me
what things in my life are good
also which are bad

But my own body
treacherous towards itself
can not keep it's peace

My body tells me
it needs what is forbidden
by my head and heart
