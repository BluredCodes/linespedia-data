The words he uses, they make me laugh 
and feel so young at heart.
We share them with innocence, 
fun and friendliness.

We share some truths, some silly dreams 
and even a few jokes.
We also share some honest tales of sadness, 
of tears and loneliness.

The words he uses, they make me quiver 
and stoke my dormant passions.
We share them with trepidation, 
urgency and heat.

We share some words of need, 
of soft touches and of want.
We also share a sweet release, 
the peace of satisfaction.
