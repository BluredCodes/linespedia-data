title: "Stages of Life"

a mother's tender kiss
soft and sweet caresses
a hand to lead you straight

first grown-up valentine
a friendly touch of lips
a hand to play a game

a nervous happiness
first deep and real kisses
a hand to wipe the tears

a lovers' fervent touch 
as two become just one
a hand to share the chores

lost memories recalled
tender friendship renewed
a hand to ease the way
