Gliding through the waves
my body slim and graceful
seashells in my hair

Tresses flowing back
my body undulating
my tail through the crests

Sun's rays shine above
reflecting down upon me
luminescent glow

Play time halted now
humans appear with their bait
fishing poles in tow

Tease them for a bit
watch the excited faces
shine as the line tugs

Swim away again
into myriad ocean views
rainbows in the sea.
