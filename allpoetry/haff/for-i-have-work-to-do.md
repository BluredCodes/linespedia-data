title: "For I Have Work To Do"

No time to sit and weep
No time to cry
For I have work to do.
What though the wound goes deep,
No time to sit and weep.Sorrow will have to sleep
While I have work to do.

The tears that blind my eyes
Blot out God's skies,
I'll banish gloom and rise
O' heart, Why mournest Thou?
No time to cry.
For I have work to do.
