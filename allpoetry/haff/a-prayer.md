---
title: "A Prayer"
writer: "Haff"

O silence and sweet Solitude
I seek thee here,
It's here I kneel and pray,
It's here I learn to say,
"Not mine, O Lord the way
Grant me Thy peace."

O love and sweet Charity
I seek thee here.
To God I kneel and pray
To Him I turn and say,
"Wash Thou my sins away
Grant me Thy peace."
