---
title: "Lament"
writer: "Haff"
slug: "lament"
source: "AllPoetry"

I would that I were dead
and laid with you
with covering earth
and starry daisies spread.
I would that I could lie
so still as you.
Then heavy heart
would cease this mournful cry.
I would my empty hands
were godly crossed 
upon my breast,
My soul to unknown lands
set winging free.
O' lily maid
To rest - to be with thee.
