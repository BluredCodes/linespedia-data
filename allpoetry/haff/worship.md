Bell and candle,
Monk and Book
All within some cloistered nook.
Music stealing 
Voices sweet
Sound of slowly moving feet.
Holy quiet
In the air
Pleading voices whisper prayer.

Red bricked churches
Harrassed priest,
Still present the eternal feast.
Shouting voices,
Stamping feet,
Rushing traffic in the street.
Tired bodies
Full of care
Pleading voices whisper prayer.

Bell or traffic
Monk or Preist
Still He bids us to His Feast.
