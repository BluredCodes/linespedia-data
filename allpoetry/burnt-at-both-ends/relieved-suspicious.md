title: "Relieved , suspicious"
writer: "Burnt at both ends"

thoughts.....
.....drip dripping
 against the top of my head....
........estranged emotions........
an incessant...
distant knocking....

exhausted....
surrendered.....
I drop.......

As the demons begin to eat......

I awaken.................

.....at once relieved ...
.........and suspicious........

Vague recollections of a hopeless horror.
  
memories of darkness 
memories of light 
a pendulum swings
alone in the night

Adrift and restless.
