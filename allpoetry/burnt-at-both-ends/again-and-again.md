Again and again.... 
I throw myself into a wordless void 
alone and floating with only the urge 
to convey something.
Suddenly 
something clicks 
and I can't write fast enough.
Sometimes nothing clicks for a long time 
and it is almost an agony.
When resolution finally comes.....
I feel grateful, relieved and so full 
because I am empty.
There is nothing more to be said.
