Living for the moment 
In less than a moments time  
Self, beholds the meaning
As reason rules the rhyme.

Abstract calculation 
Sets my sight in space,
Meanings unintended
Splash surprise across my face.

If meaning is the Object,
And realization is the Verb:

My thoughts fall more like random leaves,
My breath
Sounds less
Like words.A poem about writing poetry.... I committed to memory back
