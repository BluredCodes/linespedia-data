I come now to heat your heart
that heats the beat
that beats the part
it plays 
so deep
in a cozy 
cozy little lovespace
.
Flowers bloom
and sway
and grow
smile the rhythms
deep below.
my cozy litttle......
cozy little lovespace
.
Etherial whispers
beat
and play
far above and
high away
ooooooooo my love
my cozy little lovespace
.
The mornings we danced
we kissed
she left
we float alone
by chance we met
and we meet again
ooooooooo
cozy little lovespace

In sweat 
we sleep
at night 
we play
at dawn we meet a distant day
and bask in sleep
in our precious
cozy little love space
.
I remember the feel on me
you give
a flowers night
inside you live
my hand can touch
yessssssssss
cozy little lovespace
.
Your scent soaked skin
and deep within
a writhing lust
a need you must 
fullfill until
you can’t ache for more
. 
Whispy clouds brush a daybright moon
Your whisper breath
so dark this room
I stop my heart and listen
Listen to your heart beat
.
I come now 
to heat your heart
that heats the beat
that beats the part
it plays so deep
in that
cozy little lovespace
.
