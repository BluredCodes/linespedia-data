It's twentyone years today
Do you have you birthday wish?
I'm alive 
and so are you.

For what more can we ask?
Lets not analyse the past
Love is now 
and where are you?

It must seem that I'm leaving just to get away,
Maybe someone up in heaven knows the truth.
But I'm breathing here on Earth,
I have done so since my birth,
And I'm doing what I know I have to do.

I've known you for a long time MaryEllen,
I love you in a way that's all my own.
Please don't let yourself start to fear replacement.
'Cause no one can ever show me what you've shown.

And sometimes I can smile
In the sunshine or the rain,
Resting on the pillow
of the love that you became.

Somewhere in our futures
Our paths may cross again,
I want us to be happy,
At least
Until
Then.The first Song I ever wrote....
