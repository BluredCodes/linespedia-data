The crowded stinking bus stuffed
stinking sweating hump-o-philes
splash sewage from potholes:
Narrow escape again.

Scraping dogma shit on the curb
from its boots 
rEvolovEr laughs without mass 
like an oilfilm.
Wetly whimpering relief.

The bus bursts into flames
from afterburner ignition blows a
gaping hole through the back of the head
where sexual daydreams ooze from a pump.

rEvolovEr resting 
sits next to me. 
Others gape, slanted
in another unwritten dream.

I can't help but notice the soft flesh flower
uncomfortably close to
razor ribbons and
barbed kisses
scraping slamming dive
near misses

Bothers me....
... can I buy you a drink?

drink to: 
healing quickly 
bleeding slowly
missing the wrong busses.some people... just drive other poeople crazy.

Others, help you lose hope of ever ejoying sanity, too much.

thx EvolovE
