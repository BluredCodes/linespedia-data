Ugly as fuck...
and as far as any two things can get from each other... 
children of disneyworld fairytales....
tempt the beast that lingers at the edge of the campfirelight.
Wanting the heat
Wanting to eat
Wanting to fuck.

Innocence wanting to please is smashed, 
crushed, tangled and enslaved .. dissolved.

The flirting innocence wanting to be pleased is 
twisted and knotted and stained... 
hide it now, 
hide it no matter what.

Innocence to see without a reason...
awaits the rains... 
Miles down,
Frozen deep in a hidden grimmace,
One eye twitches 
then,
sounding of distant thunder.

Disperse the night?
or deepen it?

waiting and waiting.so much sexual abuse....
I have read so much here thatpoints to,
whispers
and screams its name....

tonight,
EvolovE 's masterstroke poems and a few others...

other works by bigmammajen and many many others I have read over these last weeks have been eating a hole in me.
