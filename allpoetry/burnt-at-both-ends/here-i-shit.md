Here I sit
In dire need

something to shit
but nothing to read

as I wait for that final splash
I'm bored of sitting on my ass.

The only thing better to do with my hand,
would mess up this stall 
more than I planned.

I think I'd better 
leave it at this,

I need my hand
to aim my piss.Wrote this 
on a bathroom wall
at college circa 1978
