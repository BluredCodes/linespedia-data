title: "Good Shit"
writer: "Burnt at both ends"

I did a shit 

and pointed to it

somebody came 

and did the same

he brought a few friends, 

his dog and his bird.

they stood and they stared

at my scary big turd.



Weighed twenty six pounds

with ridiculous girth

it stood a foot high

hurt like childbirth.



It came out in one section 

but broke on impact

I must have a guinness book

Digestive tract



It was red yellow black 

purple green midnight blue

the dog took a bite

and the bird did one too.



the police came and watched us

while some rednecks brought beer

when the news copter came 

nearly everyone cheered



This will make the papers

I started to think

I marveled how people

put up with the stink.



I spoke with reporters

and veterans of 'Nam

Someone then set up

a "tiny web cam".



I looked all around

and oh-shit it was funny

I laughed off my ass at 

the big easter Bunny.



They came and they came

it was like Mardis-gras

Guidos and gays

and the chicks with no bras.



I was a hero 

no doubt about it

People like a good party

and they love a good shit.
