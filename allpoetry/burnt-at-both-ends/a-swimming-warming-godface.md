~
~~
I love you
in my life
the way you are
~
now,
~
me sitting here
and
you sitting there 
~
a still life...
............ a quiet contemplation...
~
satisfying, for a silent while...
~
The immensity of the canvas 
seeps slowly in....
... it feels right 
and a beautiful work in progress it is...
I am constantly reminded
of surprise.
~
Incredible fortune befallen me...
I have glimpsed the face of god 
and lived..
~
Turning my eyes...
from it's infinite depths
not to drown there,
not to be lost
unready....
~
I have seen the face of love
also turning my eyes
from a sky so high and so deep..
when lovers swimming 
lost and brave there
take refuge together on shore.
We, beneath  embrace
a swimming warming 
godface.
~
~~yes.... 
fresh
again.
