I love to walk with you and Ms. Snort 
in the sun
to do pee-pees and snoopies

If everything goes 
just right
she'll drop some nice warm poopies

I never thought I'd get used to it
It always made me gag

Now I love to pick up those cute little poops
with my plastic shopping bag.
