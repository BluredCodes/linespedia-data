diamond heart
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 i find my mind
all over the floor

pieces lost
and hopelessly scattered
amidst the tangled ruins
of defunct dreams
from another life.

A diamond heart emerges
from the dust and ash

A diamond sprout
wakened and washed by shame tears that pour,
as I clean.

=8-]

A diamond seed was there all along.

Diamond love grows silently
in the earth moistened by the grateful tears 
that never leave my eyes,
In this heart thriving
in this light,
sparkling.
