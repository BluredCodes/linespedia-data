In darkness moving 
absurdity is the dawn 
standing at the edge .

not looking back now 
absurdity is the dawn 
laughing I step.

Into shit I trip
absurdity mowed the lawn
underestimate.

without smoke I breathe
without patience i break things
shopping habits change.

inconsequential 
necessities escape my
desperate grasp DAMN.

seekers of meaning
leave this place full of questions
empty of moneyfuck PM
