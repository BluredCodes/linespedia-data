Hey there sweetspot. 
I talk to you all the time 
seems almost silly 
to say goodbye 
and then right away 
start in on a poem to you. 
~
Like a sexy '69' thing only with headsets,
Your ear against my mouth. 
Licking love,
I linger, you listen. 
~
Oh god ... the listening 
to your lips 
wet with wit whispering 
'us, baby..us'.
~
And mad me here, 
snaked tongue through
candle flame flickering,
tasting, yes, baby yes.
~
Touching it and 
teasing it. 
The flame combusting when we get close 
to burn with it. 
~
The echoes between words, 
Ring loud,
loud and long.
Like we are in this big place 
somewhere deep. 
Wild mad callings
happy lost in our Spelunking... 
~
Stop here a minute 
listen. 
Let me 
catch my breath with you 
before we kiss again.
