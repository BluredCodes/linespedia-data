I hear you breathing like a lulliby

Deep deep deep
Within the womb...
Your sweet soft body
Sings a loving tune


a lovely lovely leela  plays
     play.... tire..... rest..... my lovely leela
     play.... tire..... and rest.


All memory and trace           
Erase …… erase                    
Distance lost
No time, no space

Dream ocean’s garden where you  
    play ... tire ...   rest...... in dreams
    play ... tire ....  and rest.....

leela...leela  lovely leela
      play ... tire ... rest ...... in dreams
      play ... tire ... and rest  .....

Resting you
trusting all 
sweetly swept 
down waterfalls

      play ... tire ... rest.....my sweetness
      play ... tire ... and rest.....

Spaces filling
collecting all
Settling smoothing 
connecting all 

     Rest my leela....... rest, yes,
     Rest my leela....... rest.


Infinity arise
assemble 
disperse

pulsing glowing
fire flowing

warming warming
warming the stillness.......

Lovely leela plays a leela
     play ... tire … and rest.....… so warmly
     play ... tire … and rest.


In and out
A birthing breath
Is breathing life
In love she met

Longing and living 
Enchanted games
Day and night swimming
In dreams no names

Lovely leela plays a leela
     play ... tire … rest..... no names  
     play ... tire … and rest.


A stillness aloneness
At-oneness at-homeness

Lovely leela plays a leela
     play ... tire … rest..... at home 
     play ... tire … and rest

My arms cradle
Your face basking
My kisses shining

Drink deeply of
This stary stary night
Be born again
On this first of all days

Leela plays again
This morning
Sunshine warms her face

play.... tire..... rest......my lovely leela

play.....tire.....and rest,

No trace.In dreamssss
always...
