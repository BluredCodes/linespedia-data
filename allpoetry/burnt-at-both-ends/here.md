Twas the night before valentines day
I was thinking of love
And thinking of angels 
That flap far above.

Thinking of roses and
Candy and puppies
Thinking of kittens and 
Jewlry and yuppies.

I might give you it all with a valentines card,
And bind it with ribbons, this wouldn't be hard.

But I stopped
And I realized
Just barely in time
before I squeezed out 
another really bad rhyme.

it all can happen
                    with out a heartbeat
but I have one
                    and this thrill to play

    ooooo to play inside you...

spend all that time shopping...

where would you put all that stuff anyway

it is so, so much richer

to look at you 
not knowing  a thing 

about anything
at all

and laughing laughing laughing my eyes closed

then feeling your hand on my lips
your heart in my hand

in rose petal rain
o my love
here
