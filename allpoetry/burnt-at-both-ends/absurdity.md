Absurdity
~
Face ~ Shun
Truth ~ Truth
~
But ~ But
Tickled ~ Searing
~
With ~ All
Hope ~ Hope
~
I bask ~ Not to
Fathoming ~ Obfiscate
This Dream ~ That Dreamsort of like a mirror
