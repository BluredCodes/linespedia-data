title: "Unward"
writer: "Burnt at both ends"

In the end 
there was only darkness, 
as there was in the beginning... 

Darkness begat darkness... 
diluted... faded further 
into a darker darkness. 
Deep darkness... 
dark dark, in its depths. 

darkness in the shadow of darkness, 
underneath there is more darkness... 
taking over from darkness... 
surrounding enshrouding and suffocating 
the newer darkness 
with the old.... 

The darkest cancer 
creeping slowly forward 
from the past.... 

The deepest doom: 
Extending an infinite tentacle from the past, 
overtaking these shallow moments, 
with all the hollow token angst 
the merely.... emptinessless. 
Dragging it all to slime and hell, 
piece by forgotten piece. 

Moving past this 
overtaking it creepingly 
into future. 
Dooming it all with its breath, 
paralyzing with its corrupt stench. 

Aborting it, 
before even 
conception... 

ugggggghhhhhhhhhhhhhh 

In the shadow of the deepest darkness 
laid a dream. 
forgotten by darkness... 
mislaid into the depths and forgotten. 
Forgotten far away... 

Far, far away in some forgotten 
unloved dark corner... 
suffocated in stillness 
caught frozen mid gasp 
rattling it's final breath... 
Incomplete... 
stuck in timelessness. 

Darkness in the shadow of darkness 
underneath the more darkness 
the advancing doom 
creepness ooze 
accelerating 
inward 
outward 
and unward. 
Deep and forbidden in the unward direction 
undoing all that may have ... 
who knows.... 

Death from the past. 

Cancer in time 

Advancing unwards towards the only fraction of a dream left, 
Dripping doom 
onto the only crumb of a hope... 
onto the hopelessness of hope. 

Spies ............. it 
Seeks ............it 
Stalks ........ it 
Tastes .......it 
Eats ...... it 
. 
.
