---
title: "Its Over"
writer: "Shadox"
slug: "its-over"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Like a bullet that echoes in my mind,
Your words hit home, piercing my soul.
I can't get them out of my head:
Its over.

A tear trails down my face, 
Clearing a trail for others to follow,
My heart shattered,
And this moment in time seems eternal.

How could you do this to me?
It hurts so much,
I can't bear to go on like this.
The hurt must end, somehow,
But i can't stop the pain.
I try to speak, but i can't,
My voice has left me,
Leaving me in silence to deal with my anguish.

Why do this?
We were so much in love,
Did it really have to end?
Do you not share the love i do?
Why does it hurt so much?
My heart dies, my mind scattered,
As i finally come to the realization.
Its over.
