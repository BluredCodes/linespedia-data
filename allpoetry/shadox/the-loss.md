I can still remember...
It seems like only yesterday.
You were with me, 
With your smile, brightening my soul,
Your touch, like an angel's grace,
Your kiss, soft and delicate,
Sending shivers down my spine, 
Stopping time, if even for a second.
I felt loved.

Now, its just a faded memory,
A ghost in time, almost forgotten.
How I wish I could experience it again,
To have you in my arms, 
To feel love once again.
But I can't.
It is forever gone,
Lost in the abyss of time,
Never to resurface.
I feel empty.
