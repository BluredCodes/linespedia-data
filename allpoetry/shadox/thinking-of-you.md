---
title: "Thinking of You"
writer: "Shadox"

When i think of you, 
And the times we used to have,
I see you, your smile, like a ray of sunshine, hitting me from heaven.
Now i'm surrounded by darkness.
How i long to see you, 
How i long to see your smile once again.
To feel heaven once again.
But all i see is a memory, 
A whisper in time, so delicate
So precious. 
Then i reach out, and its gone, 
Leaving me in darkness, 
Alone.
