The memory is vivid...
I stand before you,
Looking into your eyes as they shine in the sunlight,
So full of love.
Your hair silken, and whispy,
Your skin, so soft and pale,
As i move closer, i feel your heart against my chest,
A tender rythmn in my mind,
Your breath soft, and feminine
Your lips, slightly parted in anticipation.

I move closer, still staring into your eyes,
As they capture my heart, and make me forever yours.
I feel your breath on my face, 
My lips quiver, waiting,
My hands start to shake, as i close my eyes,
And taste pure perfection for the first time.

My heart explodes with passion,
And I freeze, unable to think, my mind as clear as the sky,
A ripple of pleasure runs through my body, 
Making my fingers tingle,
Experiencing the first kiss.

Then, everything darkens and blurs, 
Fading like a dream,
And i wake up, and feel nothing but emptiness.
I have lost you forever..
