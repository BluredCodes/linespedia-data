---
title: "Feeling Death"
writer: "Shadox"
slug: "feeling-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Surrounded by darkness,
Scarred by time,
Thoughts and feelings ripped from my soul,
Memories flooding my mind,
Then only to fade away,
Leaving me helpless.
Everything's dark, my vision fails me,
My fingers are cold, unable to move,
The salty taste of blood fills my mouth,
Making me gag on my own life,
The pain ebbs away, 
My thoughts crumble away,
I feel emotionless,
Still.
So, this is what death is like...
