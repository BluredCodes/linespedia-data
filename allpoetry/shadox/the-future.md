---
title: "The Future"
writer: "Shadox"

Ever distant is the future, 
Who knows what it holds?
Constantly yearning for it, 
Yet never achieving our goal,
Tomorrow is unattainable.

We often dwell in the past, 
Regretting our mistakes,
Treasuring our triumphs,
Yet, never satisfied with what we have,
Looking to the future for more.

The past defines our tomorrow,
And makes us who we are,
We are the future,
Travelers of time,
Masters of our destiny.
