---
title: "Always and Forever"
writer: "Shadox"
slug: "always-and-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13587-Always-and-Forever-by-Shadox"
---

I've never found the words to say,
Never expressed just how i felt,
Looking back, i see we were perfect for eachother,
You were always on my mind,
The angel of my heart,
Always and Forever.
Now, i've let you go.

My love grows stronger with each day,
A part of me feels empty, incomplete,
Without my love, my life,
Without you.
I can't pretend to go on,
Knowing i love you,
Knowing you still dominate my heart.
This feeling inside is so intense,
I'm incomplete, lost without you,
A part of you will always be with me,
Always and Forever.
Why won't you come back?
