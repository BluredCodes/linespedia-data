---
title: "Cookies!!!"
writer: "Shadox"

I know that smell!
Melted chocolate, and creamy peanut butter,
Softness that crumbles in your mouth,
So divine, so heavenly,
It's fit only for gods.
Wait, who am i kidding?
*smack*, *chew*, *snarf*
Mmmmmmmmmm....cookies!!
