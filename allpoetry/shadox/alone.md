---
title: "Alone"
writer: "Shadox"
slug: "alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Deafening silence,
Where the voices were plenty,
Now they're all gone, 
And an empty void is all that’s left.
A heart, broken by love lost,
Still yearning for what isn't there,
Still aching from the pain that was inflicted,
Suffers in utter silence.
Thoughts twisted by time,
Reminiscing about the past,
Wishing it were the present,
Focussed by reality,
I am alone.
