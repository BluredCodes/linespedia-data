THE BIRTH OF A POEM

Little thoughts, wisps of ideas
Remembrances of long ago
My mind suddenly connects
A beginning is born
The dormant seed is found
Nurtured and watered
I feel roots burst forth and take hold
Stem slowly pushing its way up
Finally to see the light
Each thought is a new branch
Which way shall it go
Buds swelling, leaves slowly opening
Each a new idea to explore
Some have thorns others naught
Petals slowly opening
A flower blooms, the birth of a poem
