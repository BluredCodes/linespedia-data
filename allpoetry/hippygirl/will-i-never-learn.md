WILL I NEVER LEARNWill I never learnWhy do I tell him anythingJust more fodder for his crueltyHis putrid brain twisting my wordsSpewing them back in my faceEyes glazed over with ragePowerless to penetrate No way to make him hearRanting, ravingHands pressed over my earsSilently screaming Praying for his departureCreeping away quietlyHiding, seeking serenityBy Hippygirl ©2001
