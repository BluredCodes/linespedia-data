TEARSYou kiss the tears from my cheek. Those goodbye tears, wordlessly saying,I don’t want to go.Remembering the wonderful day we shared,talking, laughing, loving each other.You kiss the tears from my cheek.No need for words,our eyes say it so clearly.I feel your warmth enfolding me,as you hold me tenderly, in your arms.You kiss the tears from my cheek.I relive the day as I drive,past all the familiar landmarks.Until I again feel the touch of your hand,these sweet memories will last.A tear trickles down my cheek.By Hippygirl
