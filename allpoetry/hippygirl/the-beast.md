THE BEAST

Tell it to stop, bombarding my brain
All I want to do is sleep and it won’t let me
Tossing, turning, thrusting my face into the pillow
Flashes of events bursting through unwanted
Tumbling, envisioning not yet borne out tragedies
My mind creating scenarios of unspeakable horror
Damn my thoughts, turn them off
Yearning for the peace of absolute oblivion
Silently screaming, clawing at the monster
A cold and clammy sweat covers me like a veil
Gooseflesh rising
Heart pounding in my head
Unspeakable dread enveloping and smothering
Waiting for the inevitable cataclysm
Hopeless, giving up, submitting to the beast
The endless night triumphs

By Hippygirl
