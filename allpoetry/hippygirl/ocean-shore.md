OCEAN SHORE

I see the waves crashing into shore,
and feel the salty spray on my face.
Nature's fury released in the roar.

The cry of the gulls above as they soar,
blending together in the oceans song.
I see the waves crashing into shore.

It's thunderous sound pierces the core,
becoming a part of my soul.
Nature's fury released in the roar.

I love the serenity but the violence more,
her healing and comforting arms.
I see the waves crashing into shore.

A giver of life but not forevermore,
the pungent odor of the cycle of renewal.
Nature's fury released in the roar.

Every moment that passes I love this more,
lying still on the sand staring into the surf.
I see the waves crashing into shore,
Nature's fury released in the roar.
