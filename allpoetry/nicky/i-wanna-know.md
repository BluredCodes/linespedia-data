to know that i love you
to talk to you hiding my feelings
just sitting there looking at you
for  you not to notice
but i heard you liked me
so why arent you making the moove
i wanna know?
why do i always have to say something
but YES you are shy, and i respect that
but this is not the time to be shy
i love you more than the air
i love you more than the trees
i love you more than anything else
on this planet
i love you more & more each day
BuT
do you love me?
i wanna know>?
