he loves me and say " he wont give up"
i love him but not in that way
i love him more then a freind 
but not in a lovers way
i love you webbo
but want to be freinds
best freinds
i know you mean well
and that you think you love me
but i am just a freind
please webbo
dont make it hard on me
love you always
and will always be here for you
till the end of time
and yes i may change my mind
but not now i wont
sorry
remember i love you and you
are the only on i can trust
