you were the best thing in my life
      then you had to ruin it
     you could of left it alone
              but no
   you had to make life difficult

  after that day i found out
   i have never felt the same 
              about you
  you were my best freind
         and now
    nothing but distint freinds

        i did forgive you
   but that didnt change anything
     you still betrayed me 
   and no sorry can take back
      the pain i went through

  now we are just freinds
        not bestfreinds
 i couldnt stop thinking i was to blame
    but now i know
 i was the perfect friend
          and you
   had to ruin it

   i still love you
   i always will
but i dont think you understand
  what i went through

you were the best thing in my life
  then we both ruined it
