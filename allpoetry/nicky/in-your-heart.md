writing a poem
 i dont know how 
i do it
 i just think of something
that is hapnin in my life
adn put my fingers to the keys
and they do the rest
sometime i dont even know
what i write till i read over them
 poems come from the heart
if you try heaps hard to write a poem it will
neva sound right
but if you just let all the words come out
it will sound perfect

 all poems come from the heart
 they have alot of meaning and feeling
neva mock a poem
they are all unique
in there own way
 
 writing a poem isnt difficult
 it is easy
just write whats in your heart
