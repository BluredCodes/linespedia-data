we have been best freinds
 for 12 years now!
 we have stuck by eachother through everything
 we get along  like a house on fire
    but
 sometimes we do fight
 and when we do i cant sleep
 or do anything
 i really love our friendship
 and if i lost you
 i wouldnt be able to live
 my life would end
     sarah
 i really do love you
 i wont our friendship to last foreva
 and it will cause i aint letting go of you,
 eva
