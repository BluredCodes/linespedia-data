---
title: "dont care for him"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "dont-care-for-him"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47063-dont-care-for-him-by-nicky"
---

to hate him
to fight with him all the time
not to get along
to completely ignore eachother
for me to tell him to kill himself
for him to do the same
we dont care about eachother at all
