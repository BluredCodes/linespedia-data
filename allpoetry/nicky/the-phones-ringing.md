title: "the phone's ringing"
writer: "nicky"

to hear the phone
for it to be you
ringing to let us know,
you wont be comeing home

for you to be staying away
again
not coming home
2 nite

pick up the phone
you dont talk to me
you ask for someone else
not even
how was school
or
how are you
just
is mum there

to hear the phone ring
for you to tell us you wont be home
again

to hear the phone ring
