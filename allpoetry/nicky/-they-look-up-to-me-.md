---
title: "** they look up to me **"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "-they-look-up-to-me-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42312----they-look-up-to-me----by-nicky"

being hurt
 always being sad
 comparing yourself to others
 wanting to be better
 then realizing
 you are better
 you are an incredible person
 unique
 different to others
 knowing people dont
 look down on you
     *but*
 look up to you!
