---
title: "he understands me"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "he-understands-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

innocent and sweet
he talks to me
sweet talking me
its nice!
he makes me feel special
inside and out
i love him
as a freind
and he is the only one i can
trust
the only person i cant talk to
without having to make sure i dont say anything wrong
he understands me!
