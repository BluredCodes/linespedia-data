the sun shines the trees sway
 i hear the car you drive away
 you drive away alot now
 i know your not trying to hurt me
 but you being away makes my heart brake
 my world shakes and tears sneek out and dwon my cheek.
 
i know you may not see it
 but seeing my big sister not around me,
 only seeing you five seconds a day
 it crushes me 
 not being able to walk to your room,
 and get advice,
 or to have our littles chats about nuthin 
 that mean everything.
 or just to know you are there.

 outside is dark the lights are on
 here you do come home again
 its nice knowing you are here,
 but during the day i miss you

    i miss you!!!!
