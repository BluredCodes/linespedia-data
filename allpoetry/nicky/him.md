i talked to him
 not realizing he likes me
 i brrake his heart by talkinbg about other guyz
  why didnt he tell me

 i feel really bad for what i have done
 if i had known i would of said some of that stuff
 he was there for me
  i thought just as a freind
 but he saw me differently
 as in love!!

 he is just a freind
 i trust him
 he could be my lover
 but i dont know!!
 i do love him
 but i cant see us togethor!>

 i love him as a freind
 the only person that i can trust
 he is not a friend
 or a lover
 he is my best friend

 best friend for life!!
