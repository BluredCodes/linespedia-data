he treats her like dirt
she is my freind
and i dont know how to help her
he hurst her in many ways
inside and out

i want to hurt him
but i dont know how to
i dont know if i should
  or what to do

listening to her talking about him
her telling me what happened
what he had done this time
   it hurts me
to see her like this

i dont know what do to
or if i should do anything
do i have the right to help
what do i do

all i can do is be there for her
listen to her
help her
but if he keeps hurting her like this
i will have to do something

 what should i do!!!
