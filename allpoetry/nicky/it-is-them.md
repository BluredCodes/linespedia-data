to hang around all pretty people
 to be the one that isnt part of the group
 the one that isnt as pretty
 or to have the perfect figure.

 for all your freinds to have the guyz
 for you to sit back and watch 
 for them to get the really hot guyz,. 
the ones right out of your league.

 and then you wonder  why they are out of your league
 you think to yourself " is it me"

 BUT noW i KNOW
 
  it is the guyz,
 all they want is pretty anorexic blonde bimbo's

 and when you are not that 
 they put you in the reject pile

 so i realize it isnt me it is them
