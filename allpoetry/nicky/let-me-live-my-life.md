that stupid bitch
saying bad shit about my poems
i dont give a fuck what you think
you little whore
i write these for my own good
not for sluts like you to tease
i wirte to let things out
show the world what i am going through
to show people all my problems
and to realieve myself of my pain
so go get bent, and stop fucking me around
i dont care what you say
live your life
and let me live mine
