you were told something
   it wasn't true
but did you belive me
         NO

you automatically assumed
  that it was true
i cant believe that little rumour
     broke us up

we were going so well
and then you freinds had to reck it all
   i was crushed
when you said you forgave me if i had done it
even though i didnt
  then you broke it off with me

we used to be so close
       now
     nothing
we are invisible to eachother now
   i dont want that

you believed a rumour
and now we are nothing
you said to me that you had lost the best thing
well...that will teach you to listen to gossip.
