bass, beats, meloday
     * a song *
 a song is the key to our feelings
 you listen to a song 
 you can relate to it

 about love, death , sadness
 or even lossing someone you love.
 you always feel better listening to someone
 else having that same problem.

 a song can heal
 when you are sad you listen to 
 depression music
 when you are happy
 you listen to music
 .................

 music can heal, help , cure
 music is the key to our feelings
 you can  relate to music

  music's da bomb!!!!!
