---
title: "he cares for me"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "he-cares-for-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

he is so sweet
 he makes me feel good
 i can trust him
 he trust's me
 we connect 

 he's the only one i feel close to
 the only one that listens
 the only that cares about me
