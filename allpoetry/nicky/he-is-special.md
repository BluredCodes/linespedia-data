to look at the person you love
being with him
in classes
for him to flirt with other people
for hin to not realize that i like him
for him to still like me
but think i dont like him
for his freinds to tell me he still loves me
and would do anything for me
but when it comes to talking about it
i cant
i am scared
of rejection i guess
but ofcourse i am afraid of losing him
as a freind too
i would rather be more
but if this is all it can be
i am happy for it
to be friends with someone so special
