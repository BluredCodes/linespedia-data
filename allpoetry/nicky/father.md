i love him
he is my father
he hurts me
he yells
he sad things in the
spur of the moment
which i know he doesnt mean
but hurts at the time
______________________

we can get along heaps good
but he recks it by getting to serios
he always is
he always has to have it his way
or no way at all
______________________

he always makes me feel bad
with those things he says
and that stuff he does
....
but in the end i will always know
he is my father and he always will be
but that name "FATHER" 
does not give him the right to treat me like that,
and it neva will
