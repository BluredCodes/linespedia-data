love is confusing
  very confusing
 you cant start it nore
    stop it
 
 your heart decides the love
     but wateva happens
    love is always there
 
 even if you dont think so
  sometimes we feel empty
 and that no-one loves us

 but i know some-one does 
         love me
 and somewhere out there 
     some-one loves
        you too.
