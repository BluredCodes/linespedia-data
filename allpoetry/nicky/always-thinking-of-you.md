as i sit here a tear falls out of my eye and dwon my cheek
 remembering how you visited with heaps of presents and all that yummy
 food from queensland
 not forgetting a single memory of us togethor

 i keep a pictue of you in my wallet
 showing everyone
 saying that you are my great nan
 who died 5 years ago

 but i think
 you have died
 but you will alway remand in my heart
 you will always own a special place in me

 rushing to that hospital after that phone call
 getting to the hospital
 realizing we were to late
 you had gone.

 the only way to say goodbye was to your body
 i kissed you on the forhead
 not really know that you were gone
(cause i was 9 )
 but i will always remember that day!

 as a terrifying one

 but i will always enjoy the times we had
 and always think of you!!
