i loved you more then you loved me
 thats why it didnt work out
 you were everything to me
 i was just a another girl to you

 you were my first real love..
 i loved you more then anything
 then you broke it off
 then asked me bak out

 i said yes

 i loved you too much to let you go again
 but after awhile 
 i realized
 you didnt love me
 you just couldn't handle
 not having a girlfreind

 so you just used me
 i asked you about that
 you denied it

 you played with my heart
 you played with my soul
 you hurt me deep inside
 you broke my heart
