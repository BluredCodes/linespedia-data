i still love him
 i thought he loved me too
 but he doesnt
 i asked him
 he said we are better of freinds

 i dont know  if that is his way of protection
 against me
 i dont know if he is pretending,
 hiding his feelings

 but sometimes he doesnt realize what he says
 and i take it to heart

 it is the hardest thing be bitched at 
 by the guy you like/love.
 when he yells at you
 call you names

 he doesnt realize that i am in love with him
 but even if he did, i dont think he would,
 treat me any different

 i still love him
 he doesnt know
 he JUST wants to be friends
