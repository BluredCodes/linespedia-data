cant wait to grow up, we all say
 but can you remember when you were about 5
 you had no worries, no responisiblity
 nothing.

 we grow up to have responsibility
 there are more dangerous things when you are older
 
 growing up isnt the best thing
 staying young is.

 not having to worry about strange men
 being careful of everything you do

 when you are young you take more risks,
 cause you dont know what can happen.
 it is more fun!

 growing up isnt the best thing
 staying young is.
