---
title: "what did those tears mean????"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "what-did-those-tears-mean"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46364-what-did-those-tears-mean-----by-nicky"

fighting with you
writing u letters to make you mad
then realizing i dont feel that way
i told you i was proud of you
you cried
i dont know if they were tears of joy or sadness
i havent seen or talked to you since that day
i really wanna ask that question
what did them tears mean?
