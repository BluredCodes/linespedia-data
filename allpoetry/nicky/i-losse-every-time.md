title: "i losse every time"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "i-losse-every-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

to be friends with a backstabber
to tell her everything
then her tell everyone else

for everyone to warn me
but i dont listen

but most people like her
and dont believe a thing people say

for her to suck everytone in
to her games
for her to hurt me
for me not to learn

for me to go back everytime
for her to win
me to loose
