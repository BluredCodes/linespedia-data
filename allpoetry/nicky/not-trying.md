---
title: "not trying"
writer: "nicky"

to have a best friend
 that you love to death
 for them not to know
 you feel this way
 for them to try to talk to you
 but they have hurt you b4 and
 you just pretend they dont mean a thing
 for you to pretend to hate them
 bitch about them
 but really
 you are thinking
 i am glad you are here
 i am glad i met you
 and that i want to tell you how
 i feel but
 you cant
 i try to find the words
 the words that say
 i love you and dont wanna loose you
 to a back stabbing bitch like her
 but i can't
 to have a best friend
 that doesnt know
 to have to live with the guilt
 that you didnt try
