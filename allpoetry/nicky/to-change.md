---
title: "To chAnGe"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "to-change"
source: "AllPoetry"

to see what is hapening
to yourself
to see yourself changing
from what you were
to what you've become
to see the darkness in yourself
to see everything you wanted
crashing down upon your lap
to see everything gone
to see your life wasted
to see the world isnt as
nice as you thought it was
to see you and your loved ones
seeing you differently
to become someone else
To ChAnGe!!!!!
