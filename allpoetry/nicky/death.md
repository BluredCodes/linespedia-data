it is an adventure
it is another part of our life
we live
we die

many people die young and
we think that is wrong
but
it is just time for them to have the
opportunity to view death before others

we think that death is the end
that all is gone
but it is just the beginning
our body's die
but our spirite live on

when we do die
we can watch over our loved ones
guide them through life
help them when they need help

death is not something to be scared of
it is something to look forward to
to see the world form a different light
to be there for other
helping them

death is an adventure
not a nightmare
