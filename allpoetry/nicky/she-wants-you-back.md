for her to lose him
for her brother to be gone
forever
for that freak accident to take his life
for her to have to live with it
for her having to cry herself to sleep
for me to try and give her advice, try and lift her up
try and help her through it, tell her that it will be ok
it is hard enough for me to deal with all this
trying to help her, give her advice about her dead brother
so i cant even imagine how hard it is for her
it is her brother
   And when people bring up this subject,without knowing how i feel
i fall apart, not that i knew him that well, but cause i see his sista
falling to the ground, drowning in tears and sadness
to have to live each day knowing she will never see him again
never talk to him never be able to have a big brother
   Why did he have to go and die on her!! why did he go!!
she is falling apart and so am i
i dont know how long i can keep telling her things that arent true;
like it will get better, you'll get over it
BUT she never will
   I just wish you were stil here by her side
so she wouldnt be like this, not teering herlsef up in side
not feeling guilty over what happened
why didnt she realize it coulda happened
   i just wish you were her by her side
again, so she wasnt go throguh this...
