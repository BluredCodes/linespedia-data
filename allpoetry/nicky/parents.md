to see the light for the first time
being held by a stange man
the past on to your mother
     to hold you
and for them to realize that
they have created a human

for the first 5 years they have watched
you crawl, talk, walk and seen you
adventure the human world

then you get older, teenager
and here comes the rules
you are realizing the world isn't a perfect
little place that you had imagined
    it is dangerous
and it is full with many lessons to learn

you disobey every rule you get
   and for what
to go out and party
to drink to much fall pregnant
and you cant even remember it

once you are older you do realize
that your parents arent the bad guys
they are trying to protect you
from the world and its dangerous things

so what is the point to not listening to them
they have been there before
and done all those things and used all of them tricks
they know better than us
they are trying to bring us up in the world
thinking we can be safe
and have a great future

parents are not the bad guys
they are you best friends
best friends in your whole life.
