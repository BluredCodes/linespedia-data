she is my sister
 the good person
 neva does anything wrong
 i am compared to her

 they say
 " why cant you be like your sista"
 it pisses me off
 always being compared to her
 
 always being the bad kid
 her always being good

 everyone knowing that she is smart
 talented, 

 then everyone knowing
 i'm notlike her
 we are different

 but they cant get it through
 there thick heads
 that
 i am not her
 i will never be her

 so deal with it.
 she is my sista not me
 and we will neva be the same
