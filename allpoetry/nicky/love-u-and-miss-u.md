---
title: "love u and miss u"
writer: "nicky"
slug: "love-u-and-miss-u"
source: "AllPoetry"

the tears sneak out and down my check
the ground is a puddle
i look up and see your picture
and i realize i have lost you forever
forever is a long time
and i will never see you again
ohh sweety, i love you
i miss you and i cant live without you!!
