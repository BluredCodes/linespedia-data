i sit here wondering 
what you are doing now
are you doing some thing wrong
harming yourself
pushing your freinds away
loosing everyone one you know
not being trusted with anything
you are changing
everyone realizes
no-one likes it
we tell you, you listen
but then dont care
just do it anyway
you are loosing me
and you are loosing others
you are loosing everything
