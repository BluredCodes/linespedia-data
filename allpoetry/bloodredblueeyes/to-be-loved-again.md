title: "To be Loved Again"

I've lost love before,but never again.I've evened the score,though your love for me was never a 10.for you to use me was so unfair,the only time i was happy was when you said you care.to be loved again i feel so much more.his warm touch, so unlike yours.his soft hands on my hips when we walk threw a store,I'll be tripped and heart broken by you no more.i would sit, my arms and heart hurting after not doing what you wanted,with him i have respect and am never taunted.walking with you your touch would sting,he never shows anger and physical abuse he never brings.you have now heard why it feels good to be loved again,to be wanted, and cared for by atleast a few men.
