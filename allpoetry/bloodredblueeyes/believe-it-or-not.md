title: "Believe it or not"
writer: "BloodRedBlueEyes"
slug: "believe-it-or-not"

Here is my story,
it's not very long. 

please listen now,
then I'll be gone.

I woke late at night,
my face stained with blood.

my skin was so pale, 
the opposite of mud

my family doesn't believe,
though some friends do

they have their reasons,
and I have mine too.

I am what some call 
a monster others a myth

I am a vampire,
My friend I live with.

she is a fairy, 
though it may sound strange.

We are 'family',
we stay from slayer range.

you can believe it
or sit and huff

But you must know,
these teeth are too tough.

there is my story,
look however you choose.

you want the rest?
find me, i'll tell!

Vampires are misunderstood,
but we don't all go to hell.
