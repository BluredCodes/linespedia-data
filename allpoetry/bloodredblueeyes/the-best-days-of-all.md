title: "The Best Days of All"

The best days of all
are the ones like today.
It's as dark as night, 
though it's still day.
Everyone wimpers 
and they run inside.
I'm not a coward. 
I will not hide.
I quietly watch, 
as the clouds roll by.
The sun is gone, 
though still in the sky.
The best days of all 
are cool,misty,and grey.
The best days of all, 
are days like today.
