Through the empty window, 
i see a little puppy.

Cold and wet.
Skin and bones.

I bring him in
to my nice warm home.

I give him milk,
a bed, a blanket.

I made the blanket 
from the finest silk.

Purple and blue.
With green and silver.

He stares at me
as if to say thank you.

I stare back 
and think how we're the same.

Cold and alone.
Shunned to the rain.

I'm all alone.
So is he.

I pick him up
and craddle him in my arms.

Kiss his wet nose,
and he licks mine.

He's so sweet 
and so loving.

I wonder what would have happened
if I had left him?

I make those thoughts leave my head
go to my room and get ready for bed.

I pull Puppy close
and shut my eyes.

To wake in the morning 
With Puppy and the sun rise.the very end line. "To wake in the morning 
With Puppy and the sun rise." kind of explains that he filled my life with light and that i had never really had much to live for. if u can understand that. most of my poems you have to read between the lines.
