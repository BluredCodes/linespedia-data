The night has come,
it's time to prowl.
Through the dark i creep,
as the dogs, they do howl.

Princess of the night
is what i may be.
No living thing 
is darker then me.

I have loved ones
as mystical as me.
We never have to beg,
or grovel on knee.

The ones who are begging
are ones just like you.
The ones who are groveling
say they will kiss any shoe.

I wear a black cloak,
a hood and some boots.
It may sound gothic but,
it beats wearing suits.

Call me what you will,
beast, monster,
creature, or spirit.
Call me slayer and i shall kill!!

Death is not pleasant,
for those whom insult.
My bite is as deadly 
as a lightenings strong jolt.

You have been warned.
You have been friended.
Be nice and i'll always
keep your life mended.i need help smoothing my poems out more.
