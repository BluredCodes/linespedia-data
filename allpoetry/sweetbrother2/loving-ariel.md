I first met her
young, lonely, blue
and strange

the vision of her
was a solitary comfort
in turbulent adolescence

Pale, near translucent beauty
spoke to shadowy voices within me

she offered pearls
for me to see and touch
and cherish

Her voice, a sad insistent murmur
haunts me across the years
between my youth and my today

I hear her whispers
in shells pressed against my ear,
and in hot breezes bringing storms;
when storms lash across my earth
her voice threatens and commands,
and when she shows me
an  instant flash of daylight
I imagine that is only she
who lets me see my landscape clearly...

Desire for her
flares and fades
inside me from time to time;
in bright times like today
passion for her is often forgotten
but when I wander in the dark,
my heart releases
an involuntary cry;
in my dark and desperate nights,
I cry for ArielAugust 4th, 2001; for those love Sylvia.
