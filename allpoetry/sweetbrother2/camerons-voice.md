I am Cam
I twenty-six years old
I sleep in alley behind
CVS store

You see me
on corner by Metro stop
I have cup in hand
and a bright smile
for you each day,
even when rain come down

Don't be fooled
because I can't read;
don't be fooled
because I can't write;
Don't think I stupid
because I talk like toddler;
I highly intelligent
inside raggedy head

You think I cute
when I flirt and smile at you
with gap between my teeth
and long hair like black Jesus;
you think it's cute
when I get up from corner
and dance for
car radio stuck in traffic light

Doctor says I dance
because I bipolar
and you not see me 
when I
curl up in ball
and cry all night

Doctor says
Mama made me what I am
when I nestled
helpless in her belly

Doctor not know
Mama love me
and I send her prayers each night
and where she is,
she sends them back


Today, I pray
you buy me something
good to eat
because I'm nice.

I dance for you
or, you give me paintbrush
and I make pictures for your children;
Don't be fooled;
I am talented,
and highly intelligent

Is a thousand people
at my breakfast table;
plenty of food
but everyone hungry

When I go to bed in alley
think of me,
smiling black Jesus at Metro stop;
put me in your prayers-
remember
I danced for you today.For a friend...
August 19, 2001
