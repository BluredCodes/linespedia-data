in my heart 

i hear a sound:

three young girls singing

christian songs

voices melded

in crystal harmony



an urge for tears

wells up inside me

inspired

by the distant days

when i was a christian

singing these same songs

warmed by faith in a loving god



cruel years between

froze something deep within me;

ringing voices resonating inside

have thawed and softened it



the voices of

warm & glowing faith

soothe even as they

cut cruelly



when the voices fade

into a final note

a song continues,

a mournful melody



a funeral dirge

for the believing child

i was
