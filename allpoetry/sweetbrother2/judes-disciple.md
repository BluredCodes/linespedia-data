---
title: "Jude's Disciple"
writer: "sweetbrother2"
slug: "judes-disciple"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Here is
a limp figure
bleeding from the heart

She holds him,
whispering words of peace and comfort

Here is a man
leading her by the hand
through lightless catacombs
their footsteps echo through stale air
his parched voice rasps a final message to her:
"This is my eternal home...
go back into the sun
where you belong"

His farewell
is not accepted

Desperate fingers work
to staunch his bleeding
from his thousand wounds

his words unheeded
("leave me at peace"),
he is weakened by their struggle,
Her sturdy hands gain power
from, of all things, her faith in him

Their tears mingle
into a pool
that becomes a river
that cascades over cliffs
overlooking boundless darkness

As life slips away
her hands on his wounds
begin to bleed

She winces briefly
then continues
healing
