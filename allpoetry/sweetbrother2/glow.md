She made me glow
this morning
with a few tender words
and an embrace

i walked for hours 
in the glow she made

feet tripping lightly
along city sidewalks

eight lines of love
were her first poem
(because she missed me)

and the space inside me
that had been bleak and empty
was filled blazing light;
this morning,
she made me glow
