OUR  FLAG  IS  UNFURLED

The music sounds with all sorrow,
our joys are flown down.

Ashes drift towards the sky,
uplifted with-out sound.

Horror mounts with odors keen,
souls move towards the sun.

What light is there to guide us through
darkness now begun?

Hearts bleed with no recourse
at the bitter arrows hurled.

Yet, our hour grows near and just
as our flag is unfurled.

Wants lead towards death and truth,
no lies can be upheld.

What answers guilty heart,
a massacre so compelled?

Tears fall countless in endless streams,
eyes drawn down from care.

Spirits drift with-out aim,
direction lost in dispair.

Hatred aimed wings of fire through
skies where clouds do swirl.

Cries of anguish create wind
causing flags to unfurl.

Heroes come from hearts proud,
self lost in moments need.

In an hour with-out hope,
hope now plants its seed

Hearts torn then filled with wonder 
at such selfless glory.

Books will bloom on paper gold,
in honor of their story.

Just arms will reach into dark realms,
searching Eastern Worlds.

Seeking harm to those that harm,
as our flag now unfurls.
