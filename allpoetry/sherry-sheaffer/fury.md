title: "FURY"

The thunder rages through me,

Lightening flashes in my mind.

Energy ignites me,

Passion is my find.

And in all of her glory,

Mother Nature stands alone.

Whirling winds of fury,

Raising hearth and home.

Dare we as mere humans,

Try to intervene,

There will be such destruction,

The likes you've never seen!

Waters will flow turbulent,

Darkness will descend.

Mountains will blow ash and fire,

All hope will have it's end!

Walk on her surface lightly,

Take care of all you see.

Or you will feel Her fury,

She has no need of thee!!!
