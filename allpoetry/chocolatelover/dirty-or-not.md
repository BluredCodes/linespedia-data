People whispering as they passQuestions asked and dirty stares followIs it true that this and that happenedDid she really kiss a girl last night?Is it really any concern of theirs?Why won't they leave her be?She stands tall with her loverAs they pass by either feeling thatGuilt they once felt before.They are in love can't they see that?Why do they critize anything that is different?I find them brave for going against it allFor finding a way to survive in this gernation.I know people can cruel and unfair to judge there is a point where it is taken to far. It really is upsetting when people of my gerneration brush a person aside because they are different then they are.
