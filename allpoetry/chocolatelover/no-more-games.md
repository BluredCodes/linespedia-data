Why do you do this? She askedDo what? He mumbledPlay with my head she whisperedHe looked at her and said I love youAnd he moved toward her leaning in toKiss her worries awayLuckily she wasn’t playing- Who was she?She asked I want to know what your deal is.Just a girl I know that’s all and he reached for her handI can’t deal with you any more- she pulled awayC’mon girl you know I love you- I need you What you need is to stop bull s**** all the timeHe looked at her confused and upset-He had been dismissed You can’t say things and make it all right, life don’t work that wayHe touched her hair and she sighed and let for a second and pushedHim away She said Stop playing games with me and walked awayToward the door knob and turned it slowly tears flowing down her faceWith her last strength she said Stay away from me we are finished
