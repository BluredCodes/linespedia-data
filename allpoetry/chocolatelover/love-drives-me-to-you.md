---
title: "Love drives me to you"
writer: "chocolatelover"
slug: "love-drives-me-to-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3499-Love-drives-me-to-you-by-chocolatelover"
---

I can't help but love you
You drive me crazy 
You drive me insane
Yet I am so in love with you

I tried to push you away
You drove me wild 
You drove me up the wall
Yet here I am still

Why am I still here?
You make me mad
You make me cry
Yet it's you I want

It's plain to see that
You made me fall for you
You made me love you
Yet sometimes it'd be easier..

If I didn't love you
If I could stay away
If I would let you go
If I could I would
Yet I didn't and I can't
