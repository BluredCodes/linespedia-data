---
title: "Little Sister"
writer: "chocolatelover"
slug: "little-sister"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let me guide you 
Let me help you
Let me talk to you
Let me know what is happening
Let me be your big sister

Let me know your thoughts
Let me be there for you
Let me help you little sister
Let me show you what you need to know

First you need to tell me
First explain it to me
First speak to me
First little sister open up to me
First you have to talk to me
I can’t help until you do
