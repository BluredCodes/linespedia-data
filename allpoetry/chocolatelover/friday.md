I have to know how this 'rumor' startedI asked you and you denied itMy friends say they saw you with some girlI can't believe this is all happening againWe had a great summer together and then thisAll these rumors are they true or are they lies?How can I trust you when my friends say I should notThis has ooccurredbefore and I am so very sick of it allI want to cry and curl up in my bedI don't like being in between my friends and youYou say you love me and they love me tooThey have no reason to tell me false thingsHowever you do,have you not?I need some time to think things throughAt least its a Friday, it gives me timeI really need to know where I stand
