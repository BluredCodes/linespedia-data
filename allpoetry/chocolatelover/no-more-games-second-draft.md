No More Games (second draft)Why make excuses? What are you talking about?He looked at her with a blank lookI love you and you know that.He moved toward trying toKiss all her fears awayShe didn’t want to play We’ve gone down this road time so many timesI can’t deal with you any more- she pulled awayC’mon girl you know I love you- I need you What you need is to stop bull s**** all the timeHe looked at her confused and upset-He had been dismissed You can’t say things and make it all right, life don’t work that wayHe touched her hair and she closed her eyesWanting him in him everywayShe sighed and let him touch herUntil his phone went offNeither moved As she heard the female voiceShe slapped him Stormed out the doorReturned later in the company ofHer three older brothers.Is this what you meant Rof Cau?
