I wrote this for my ex-mother in-law I was truely blessed
to have such a wonderful mother-in-law. In memory of Mom.

You know you're just a thought away,
so I just wanted to take the time to say.
That you're thought of more often than you know,
My love for you has continued to grow.
The years have gone by so fast,
but you're apart of my future and past.
Sometimes we take for granted people know how we feel,
Well I just wanted to tell you I love you and always will.
I have fond memories or me and you together,
You're a very special person,so hang in there through this
stormy weather.
Only God knows what's in store for us,
So lean on him, believe and trust.
The world labeled you a mother-in-law,
but to me you're my mother winter,spring,summer and fall.
   
                                        Debra DeLong
