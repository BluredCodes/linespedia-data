He was took from them, but he still lives on,
In someone else, they have a special bond.
Everyone knew a donor he wanted to be,
Maybe his eyes so someone else could see.
Or maybe his over generous heart,
For some one a new life could start.
No matter what this young man would give,
It would help a greatful person to go on and live.
The years past and a call came one day,
from the reciepeint of the gift after their son past away.
As they entered the room they took one look,
their sons eyes were looking back at them..they shook.
They cried, they smiled, they cried,
Part of their son is still alive.

                         Debra DeLong
