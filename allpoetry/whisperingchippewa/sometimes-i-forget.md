Sometimes I forget to hug, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to take the time, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to pay attention, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to say thank you, I;m sorry.
Sometimes I forget to smile, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to share, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to ecknowledge, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to apprecitate, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to praise, I'm sorry.
Sometimes I forget to say....I'm sorry.
