The grandbabies were here today,
They wanted some yogurt, I said you may.
One came to me with a turned up nose,
Don't like that color you have of those.
Sorry baby would you like some cheese,
No I want some strawberry kind of these.
So the next time I make it to the store,
I'll have to make sure I pick some up for my
girls I adore.
After they left I went to get some for myself,
and noticed everyone was open on the shelf.
Then I realized they couldn't read the flavor,
and they were looking for the strawberry to savor.

                               Debra DeLong
