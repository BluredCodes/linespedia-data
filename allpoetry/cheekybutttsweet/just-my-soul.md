JUST MY SOUL



Oh God!
I have to stop this
I have to let him go
he has gone forever
He has moved on

My every thought is of him,
I'm not functioning
I'm not living
I'm just breathing bad air.

I need him in my life
he is my missing piece
He completes  me.

I am not whole anymore.
Please God
send him back
I want to breathe again
I want to be whole
I'm not asking for much

'Just my soul'


To God
What lesson is  it I am learning from this??
