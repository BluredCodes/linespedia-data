---
title: "Darkness"
writer: "cheekybutttsweet"

Dreams of growing old with the only person I truly love
          Have turned into darkness,
Dreams of holding each other close in such content
          Have turned to darkness,
Dreams of dancing with each other alone on a quiet night
          Mmmm, still dreaming,
Dreams of tickle torture, slave days and being caught on the stairs
          All, just now darkness,
Dreams of watching a thunder storm together,
          Again, as dark as the sky
Dreams of reading to our children together in the evening
          Gone into darkness,
Playing with them in the snow, taking them fishing,
          Watching them play together,
           Like a real happy family
          Again, just darkness,
Hugs on the couch, sad movies I could freely cry at
          All turned into darkness,
      That beautiful music you play so well, 
The songs you sing, my song, those words you spoke,
          All gone forever,
The moments listening to you and your son
          You are so wonderful together,
Ohhhh!!, how I miss those times we shared,
          But now-all just darkness,
I thought we would always be the very best of friends
I never once dreamed that you would not speak to me in the end,
I love you with all my heart and soul- for eternity
           I want you to be happy
          And I hope you truly are,
         But in the times when your not
              Please remember
         I’m not really that far
