I sit here wondering
What did I do to deserve you?
The sweet music in my heart
Makes me want to sing

For along time it sang the blues
You heard them all
Yet still danced along beside me

You did not enjoy the song,
Still held me close
Still danced with me for so very long

Now the songs sound refreshed
My heart once again is beating strong
Something I never thought possible
But I was so very wrong

I gave you pain,
I gave you heartache
Yet you still love me
I did not want to be loved again

I am not going to let this music end,
I love you with all my heart,
The past is the past
We now have a fresh new start

What did I do to deserve you?
