---
title: "Never Forget"
writer: "cheekybutttsweet"

You have touched my soul forever,
You showed me that I can be loved
by just being me,
yet it is still so hard to set you free.

I try hard not to think of you,
to cherish the memories 
To colour my world that always seems to be blue.

But I find myself each and every day
Thinking of you still,
Wondering if you ever give me a thought
I still love you and I always will.

I miss our times together
although I know we could never be,
What we had was beautiful
I just hope you never forget me.

You have changed my life forever
Even though you are no longer here,
You will always be in my heart
Your in my every thought and every tear.

I will never forget you.
