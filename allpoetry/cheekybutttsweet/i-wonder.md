I Wonder


What is he thinking right now?
Does he even miss me?
Am I still in his heart?
and still in his soul?
Does he ever regret making it end?
Does he still think this is for the best?
Does his heart hurt as much as mine?

I touch his picture,
I run my fingertips across his heart
I whisper "carn't you feel me?"
"Am i still in there?"

Please God, let him feel me.
The tears flow from my eyes,
he's gone,
he's moved on with his life.
Does he ever give me a thought?

I WONDER
