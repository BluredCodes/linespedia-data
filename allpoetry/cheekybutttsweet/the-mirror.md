THE MIRROR


I want to be happy,
I remember it so well,
to look in the mirror
and smile at a girl.
A girl with bright eyes
and shiny hair,
with glowing cheeks
and no despair
her happiness shined through from within
and she would smile,
a cheeky little grin

NOW I look in the mirror
and all I see,
Is a stranger looking back at me.
I see very dull eyes
 and long limp hair.
Through those eyes,
I see pain and despair.
There is no smile,
not even a hint

She is dying from the inside!
and soon you will see,
when she looks in the mirror,
 and she says
"goodbye " to me.
