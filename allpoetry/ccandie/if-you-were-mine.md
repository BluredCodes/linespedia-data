title: "If You Were Mine"
writer: "ccandie"

I never really told youHow I really feelI know it's not a good timeBut this secret I need to revealI feel really bad about what happened to youYou're such a great guyAnd seeing you upset Only makes me cryThere are so many great things that people don't see in youLike you're really sweet and kindAnyone to give up youHas to be out of their mindI'd never give you upBut that's if you were mineIf I had you with meI'd make you happy all the time
