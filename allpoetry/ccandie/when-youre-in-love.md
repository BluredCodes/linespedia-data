title: "When You're In Love"

When you're in love
     What do you do?
 Go after him
Or let him go after you

When you're in love
     Do you sit and cry?
 Because the one you love
Doesn't want to be by your side

When you're in love
     Do you wish you were dead?
 Because of the rude things
Your love has said

When you're in love
     What do you do?
 When the one you love
