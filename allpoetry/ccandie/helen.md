Helen thought of suicide One dark and dreary dayShe thought of all the one's who  hurt herAnd knew she'd make 'em payShe didn't want to slit her ristOr hang from a treeShe wanted it done the perfect  waySo everyone could seeThat she had alot of angerAnd she had alot of painShe wanted her life to be overBecause everyone thought she was insaineThe very next dayShe bought a gun from her friendShe put the gun up to her headAnd made her life come to an endI feel so bad for HelenBecause she didn't know any betterShe didn't knowThat the people who teased her, really loved herBut now it's to late for HelenBecause she stuck to what she saidNow her loved ones are payingAnd she's in a graveyard    DEAD!
