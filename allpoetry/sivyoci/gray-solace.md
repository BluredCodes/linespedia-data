title: "Gray Solace"
writer: "sivyoci"

if you see through my gray
all the mysteries would be found
a trail of smoky rays, rising from my soul
flowing into space where only answers
ring loud like church bells
resonating outward, calling everyone, or only me
leading me to find an angel
in the sanctuary, I will keep her close
she can be my companion
and tell me to have faith
as if singing hymns, over and over
and in saying that in my grayness
life is a shower of sun
live and you will learn
that all you feel
and all it pertains to
is known as a glittering gift
and it will be held always
by the soul you reflect onto
and it will be rememberedfeelings of not being heard, understood, felt...where do we find solace?
