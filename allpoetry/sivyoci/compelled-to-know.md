title: "Compelled to know.."

Compelled to know everything, compelled to know you now
For all your words, thoughts and being....how can I wait
I do know you...I know everything...I am breathing aren't I
Breathing to let go and let love find its home
Please keep my key, my friendship, my heart and don't let it tarnish

Compelled to show you I understand...
Yet not to show...but to live with you in this, your world
Love is not lost, it is found and reborn in a new way
Pull out your map and the only road will point to a knowing
Knowing that I will give you everything I can, you need

Compelled for you to know everything, please know me now
A dream , a want, a hope...I will keep safe
Safety is faith...faith will lead me home
If home be a memory, a thought, the sound of your voice...
I thank God, for I have found myself
