I embrace your laced strands with my strength
fingers stretched long, with beams of encouragement
holding with welcome, your wounds
let it coil around me, I will turn, as I restore you
I hold your rope...

no tension, but with ease...I hold you from here
from my land, my soul is holding the remnants of you
I am your guardian angel, in my grasp I will remind
as holding becomes a tug toward healing
I hold your rope...

if there be a mark left on my hands...
it is a color of hope, a choice to live
as you hold on, know there is an end
holding onto to your life is a challenge
I hold your rope...but now let me pull...
