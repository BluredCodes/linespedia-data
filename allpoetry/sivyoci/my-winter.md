---
title: "My Winter"
writer: "sivyoci"
slug: "my-winter"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

close the door in its slumber and leave the daggers of winter behind
open up your soul with fires that light my shivering heart
receive me with your warm skin as I shake off my ice

in this daily dream, if only I could pick you from it
to steal your embraces, your stares...those eyes
my lonely winter melts inside the walls I created, you burn

as I melt, I drown in you
the covers of your love armor me from this winter, my prison
I am safe until you can be my Spring
