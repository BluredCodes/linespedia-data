---
title: "Love is Art"
writer: "sivyoci"

I gaze onto your city, a metropolis
my hand begins to compose
a rendering of its metallic features
the paper becomes filled, lines grow

the soft, edged charcoal shades
within my fingers, forgetting no detail
pins embrace you to my wall
this is your portrait, art will prevail

will you now set up your canvas
place it on an easel near my chair
mix water and color, wet your brush
paint my vines, branches, petals with care

rainbows glisten across whiteness
a landscape, my reflection is in sight
protect your art within a frame
the nail and wall hold me tight

we are different media, different scenes
yet no critique, we are eachothers' lover
if be a museum, our life is a gallery of art
each day is another chance to discover
