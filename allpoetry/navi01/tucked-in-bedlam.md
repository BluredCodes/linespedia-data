title: "Tucked in Bedlam"

lavender kisses
cerulean skies
velvet waves
inside your eyes
your gentle tide
is rolling in
brushing away
my layered skin

I've come too far
I cannot stop
I've climbed beyond
a harmless drop
I'm madly bursting
at the seams
you are a world
of living dreams
