Streetlights snickering, 
a soul is flickering 
beneath 
whose head is wreathed 
in mystification, 

her candy eyes 
lost in the skies 
leaking 
and reeking 
of contemplation. 

Swept away 
from the sleepy street 
and the weepy sleet, 
she scrapes the bottom 
of her 

seclusion.
