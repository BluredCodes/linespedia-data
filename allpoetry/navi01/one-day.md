I was lolling belly-down 
on the top of the world, 
peering into 
the vale of lifes mysteries. 

Curious, I slipped down 
and dipped the tip of my foot 
carefully 
into its liquid essence: 

lovely, lucent, 
sparkling and sweet, 
it transformed my feet 
into cherubs that danced away. 

I was frozen in a moment 
sitting up on the edge 
spitting up daisies 
and just 
as it began to rain feathers, 
you appeared: 

soaked, with 
velvet eyes, 
silken skin, 
a smile of silver, 
and a heart of gold. 

When you spoke, 
your words purred 
and bounced 
in echoing elegance, 
like wind chimes 
in the heart of a forest. 

Just as I 
was about to reply, 
you came over 
and pushed me in.
