I’ll slice through the frozen layers 
I’ll peel and strip them, slow 
And down my ragged, gaping skin 
My gallant blood will flow. 
The incision will be torture 
But I will bear with pride 
For it will be worth the pain 
To find out what’s inside.(This poem is over a year old)
