while digging my nails into the daily soot
and scraping away at murky burdens,
sometimes I etch a crack in the shell.
a sudden scent of clovers and cookies
floods my lungs; welcomes me back
to the flower-laced paint peels
and the dipping hardwood floors
that creaked beneath my soapy toes
before the world plucked the stars from my eyes.
