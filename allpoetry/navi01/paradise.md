strung from a star
in a summer night
rolling in your touch
bathing in your light
like a feather in a storm
I'm taking flight
gliding and sliding
like an ocean sprite

imbibe my vibes
embrace my grace
I'll draw you a world
they can't erase
we'll tumble away
to a distant place
and carve our names
in outer space
