Bite marks on my ego, 
Foam flowing from 
your lips. 

(and your fingertips 
will sting her hips 
but) She won’t fall. 

Her scars look like 
your nails, 
and your tail wags like 
your dick. 

(My wings are like 
the plastic bag 
your spastic jaw 
can’t nip.) 

So don’t forget 
to flick the ticks 
and lick your bones 
and bone your licks 

(‘cause the life of a dog 
goes by quick).I'm not sure if I'm allowed to use that "d" word. Someone let me know?
