empty words rip through my ears
like poison through my veins.
a spiral drills into my head
emptying the pain.
this path is worn down to the bone,
these bones have been sucked dry.
if you swallow passion whole,
why refuse to die?

beyond the flicker of stale light,
beneath the casual lies...
there is a place of crystal truth
where no heart ever dies.
the only time I find it here
