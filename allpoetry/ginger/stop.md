title: "STOP"
writer: "Ginger"

STOP!
don't do it anymore,
don't let it take you over.
STOP!
it gives you pains,
it takes away.
STOP!
don't you know what you are doing?
can't you see?
STOP!
put it down,
don't look at it again.
STOP! 
listen to me!
i'm trying to help, please listen!
STOP!
it makes you silent,
it makes you begin not to think.
STOP!
it's making you hate yourself.
you have to find ways to hide.
STOP!
doing it causes guilt, 
makes you do it again.
STOP!
cover them up,
looking only brings memories.
STOP!
you're not listening are you?
you've blocked me out,
you're not going to stop.
STOP!
before it's too late.i know this one doesn't rhyme like most of my others do and it's not my best work, but right now i just had to type it.
