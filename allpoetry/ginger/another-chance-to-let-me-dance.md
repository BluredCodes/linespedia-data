---
title: "Another Chance to let me Dance"
writer: "Ginger"
slug: "another-chance-to-let-me-dance"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don't know how to start this
or what to put in the middle or the end.
I always said, more to convince myself,
instead of others, that I just wanted you as a friend.

But now the hot tears of a painful past,
start to come out at last.

I'm not sure how to say this,
or what you'll begin to think.
I'm taking a chance worthwhile,
to step out on the brink.

I look at you now and realize something,
I never saw or appreciated before.
And as I realize this, 
my heart hits the floor.

Your laugh, your smile, your eyes.
It's becasue of these things,
that I now start to cry.

What I need now,
is a second chance,
A solution to which,
I can again hold you while I dance.

I want to give it another try,
another chance to feel your touch,
another chance to look into your eyes,
this is what i ask,
even though it may not seem like much.

The choice is yours, please understand.
But take heed, I will not ask again.
