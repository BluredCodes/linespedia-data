---
title: "This Game"
writer: "Ginger"
slug: "this-game"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

What do you do when you love so much,your heart will explode with a single touch?a single kiss will do the same,and we try to figure out this game.The heart itself is a mysterious place,with lots of room to make mistakes.So no one can figure out why emotions,can arise from such small commotions.And what happens when two become one?Is it like the moon and sun?One needs the other in order to function,so they meet at a junction.And tell me, is it fate,that brings two people to a certain place?And when they meet, is it love at first sight,or does it take many a morning and night?What about the broken hearts,that are quietly torn apart?Can they be mended back together?Or will they still be as fragile as a feather?Maybe there is someone for each of us,who can mend our hearts with a single touch.A single kiss may do the same,and then we can start to figure out his game.love, a mysterious game that no one can figure out, not even the brightest professor or scholar. it's something everyone possesses, some just show it better then others. and to some it may just be a game......
