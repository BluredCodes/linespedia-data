well here i am again
just a person in the world
waiting for the time
when my existance will come to an end
not now, i hope
not for a while, 
but i wait

i dont know what is going to happen from here
but really
i dont want to
theres no point
things will happen as they do
things will come
things will go

it doesnt matter
i will just live life to its fullest
until the day comes 
when my existance will come to an end
it will happen
eventually
but im not wishing
or hoping
im just waiting


revengeful sinnerwell, you asked for different, so here it is...
revengeful sinner
