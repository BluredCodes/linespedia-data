title: "lost"
writer: "revengeful sinner"

im lost
i have nowhere to go
i dont know who cares
i dont know who i can trust
no one knows
no one will ever know
secrets better left unsaid
silent
burning
ripping at my insides
screaming
pain
trying to get out 
tell someone
anyone
but no one to trust
no one is left
i must be silent
bear with the pain
alone
im lost

revengeful sinner
