---
title: "the end"
writer: "revengeful sinner"

this is the end
the darkness is over
i dont need it anymore
i dont want to feel it anymore
it hurt me so much
i just want it to go away
im wishing
im hoping
this will end
im wishing 
im hoping
that everything will 
start over
and begin again,
the way it used to be 
this is the end


revengeful sinner
