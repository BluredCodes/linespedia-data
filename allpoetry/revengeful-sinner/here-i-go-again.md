title: "Here I go Again!!!"
writer: "revengeful sinner"
slug: "here-i-go-again"
source: "AllPoetry"

here i go again
lost in the dark
well, not the dark
perhaps the light
i dont know
i cant tell

everything may be gray
or maybe not
i am lost
but i dont know where
regretful thoughts
of what has passed
come to me in my sleep
they come to me as i walk
talk
think

i dont know where i am 
i am lost
in a world of grey
or light
or dark
or where ever i am
