---
title: "forget"
writer: "revengeful sinner"
slug: "forget"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i will forget
in time
it will happen
all the thoughts will disappear
i hope
they will leave
and i will be free again
from all the pain
and hurt
and anger
that i have had to bear
so long
i have forgotten much
i have forgotten how to care
for others
i have left them in the darkness
they still care
they have said so
they say that i am not 
giving them a chance
and i just shut them off
its true
i have been
ive been too absorbed to notice
im sorry
its wrong
i will forget
and come back to you
i will forget

revengeful sinner
