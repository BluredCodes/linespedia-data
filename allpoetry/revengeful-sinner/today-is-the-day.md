today is the day
i think im going to do it
it might be
the day 
it just might be
im not sure
im still shy
not to sure of myself
little me
coming out
to do something like this
i think i will
i think i might
i hope that if i do
everything will turn out right
today is the day

revengeful sinnerthis came out of no where, but im not telling anyone what exaclty this is about.  no one will ever know...  unless today is actually the day, of course!!

luv to all
