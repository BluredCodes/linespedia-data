title: "Leaving or Staying"
writer: "revengeful sinner"

what is it now
what am i feeling this for
i am lost
i am confused
feeling the darkness
but not knowing why
i see it
i feel it 
every day
every night
its not stopping

no matter how hard i try
to push it all away
its not leaving
its not going away

im wishing
and hoping
that eventually i will be free
from this darkness

for now
i have to live
with the pain and hate
i feel

melodrama
"deep dark pit of darkness" 
call it what you will
i will find a way
to get outdont question, i dont know what this is really about, but tell me what you think!
