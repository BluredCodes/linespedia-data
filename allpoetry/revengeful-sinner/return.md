---
title: "Return"
writer: "revengeful sinner"
slug: "return"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i thought the end
had come
the darkness was over
the hate was gone
it came back
without warning
without consent
i never wanted it to come
i wanted it to stay 
away 
forever

i regret some things i have done
i hope some people understand
that i had no idea
of the outcome
im sorry
to all i have hurt
im sorry 
to all i have stabbed in the back
to start over
try again
it would all be better
if only i could
i wish

revengeful sinner
