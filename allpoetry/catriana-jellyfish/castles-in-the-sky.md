---
title: "Castles In The Sky"
writer: "Catriana Jellyfish"
slug: "castles-in-the-sky"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10514-Castles-In-The-Sky-by-Catriana-Jellyfish"
---

I'll tell you a story 
Of a faraway land
Where we all live freely
Without discipline or reprimand
Let me lead you down
To the land way up high
Where happiness reigns
In those castles in the sky.

The grass grows tall and curled
Up to a deep purple sky
Where the flying birds all swim
And the swimming fish all fly
The clouds are for walking on
Whilst the ground flies overhead
Trees are easily stepped on
And flowers tower above your head.

There's always something to laugh about
And never anything to hate
Elephants always forget here
And angels decide your fate
Your head is for walking with
Your legs tell you what to say
Where today is tomorrow's yesterday
And yesterday is tomorrow's today.

In this place of endless dreams
You can look forward to the past
You can just dwell upon the present
And forget your future fast
The majestic Moon reigns the whole long day
You can only see the sun at night
People only sleep when they're awake
And white is black whilst black is white.

Where words are felt instead of spoken
Sounds are seen instead of heard
We start our lives in death
Then end them in our birth
Thoughts are caught floating in the air
The air is a multi-coloured haze
Where the people who've met you don't forget you
And the world wanders round in a bleary-eyed daze.

The sun revolves around the Earth
Whilst the Earth orbits the Moon
Everything is larger than life
And evening's up by noon
Everything that isn't, is
There's no such thing as regret
Everything you aren't, you are
Everything you want, you get.

Where true love is the rarest emotion
So when you find it you never let go
Then you spend your lifetime wishing
You could tell him how you love him so
Everyone lives for eternity
Though sometimes eternity is consumed in a day
But time doesn't even exist here
So everything is always just a moment away.

This is my story
Of a faraway place
Where we all live freely
Taking life at our own pace
Let me lead you down
To the land way up high
Where happiness reigns 
In those castles in the sky.
