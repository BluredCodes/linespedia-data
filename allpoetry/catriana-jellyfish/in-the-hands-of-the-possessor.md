I see piercing shards of light
Which penetrate my squinted eyes
As I turn to the white moon
A hole punctured in black.

And from the cobwebbed corners of my mind
Through the shifting shadows of my thoughts
I gaze across the moon-washed sky
To where you roam beyond the horizon
Still in possession of my heart.

My mind has long drifted away from my head
My heart has long drifted out of my reach
Both lie with you as you sink into sleep
And stay with you as you start another day.

I can no longer believe in the things you say
No more can I hang on your every word
I cannot trust you with my feelings and thoughts
Nor hope you will hear me as I whisper for you
I stand alone without daring to believe.

And as I watch the moon fade in the clouds
As it surrenders to pending daylight
Leaving only fragments of splintered shine
My heart still sings a song of undying love
