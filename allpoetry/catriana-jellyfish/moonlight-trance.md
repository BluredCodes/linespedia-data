---
title: "Moonlight Trance"
writer: "Catriana Jellyfish"
slug: "moonlight-trance"

I write in the glow of the elusive moonlight,
I sway in a trance to its elegance and grace,
On this ominously clear, hot summer night,
It illuminates my room, 
As I stand in the path of its rays.
I take in the mysterious, white shimmering haze,
That emanates softly, silently
From within its distant craters,
One arm now outstretched, I hold my palm upwards,
And touch the shiny light
That sifts through the heavy summer atmosphere.
My silhouette is embossed on the wall of my room,
I am frozen in the bright white light,
Transfixed in the silver glimmer,
I stare up in awe
As it hangs, isolated in the starless, cloudless sky,
My skin tingles with a fresh cold feel,
Everything out there is so still and perfect,
As if frozen in time until the scorching sun
Arises again over the rolling hills.
