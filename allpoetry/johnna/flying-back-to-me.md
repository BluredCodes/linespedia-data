---
title: "Flying Back To Me"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "flying-back-to-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9772-Flying-Back-To-Me-by-Johnna"
---

We shared worlds long ago
Where mine was headed I did not know
Times were crazy, so was I
So I felt I had to let you fly
Away you flew like a bird
Years passed and not a word
You were always in thought
Your love never forgot
Myself I had to convince
You would be happy at any expense
Yet not a day passed me by
That I could forget who made you fly
You took flight and began to soar
I went behind the prison door
I did my time and was set free
Somehow you came back to me
But once again I lost focus
Once again I ended us
Yes once more stupid me
Thought it best to set you free
With broken heart and broken wing
You found the strength to do the right thing
As I watched you fly I began to cry
Wondering as you faded away
If you would come back another day
A year or two has come and gone
I know you must have moved on
Once again not a day passes by
Without me looking toward the sky
Hoping once more to see
You flying back to me...
