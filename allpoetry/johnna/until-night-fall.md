---
title: "Until Night Fall"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "until-night-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Our bodies glowing
by candlelight,
caressing, fondling
kissing...

Passion warming,
our bodies
melting, moaning,
relieving tension...

No other exists
only the two of us
in our own universe...

Collapsing into
each others arms,
sleeping until
the break of dawn.

Parting our way 
for the day
with a kiss...

Helps make it through
it all 
until the night fall.
