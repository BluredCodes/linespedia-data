---
title: "I Promise My Love"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "i-promise-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17858-I-Promise-My-Love-by-Johnna"
---

There can be no love like ours,The softness of the clouds,The beauty of the flowers,Can't describe our love making hours.Our love is something special,Something new and different,That we have yearned to experience.My love for you will never be fake,My heart, my soulAnd love will always be true.We can take our love to the moon,After what we have been throughWe deserve nothing but the stars.Our past will fade, our futureBright and new, filled with trueBeauty of life and love.Let's take a chance to liveAnd discover our inner soulWith the beauty of love.Time will heal those hauntingDeeply engraved woundsSo compressed within.No more of the past,Only bright, beautiful Days await us.
