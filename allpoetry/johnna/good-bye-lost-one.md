---
title: "Good-bye Lost One"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "good-bye-lost-one"

Many of the years are a blur.
There had to have been some good times.
...Never remember of us talking,
Or trying to be a family.
Only in the eyes of the world.
Where did it all go wrong?
Why didn't we discover the beauty
Of life and love?
Only lust and hatred,
Or was we addicted.
Today when I think of you,
I can only wonder do you think of me?
Every song has a memory
Or a yearn to hang on
To something that wasn't meant to be.
Today...tomorrow...I will hold you close
To my heart and soul.
For me...my-self worth and future
I must let go of yesterday.
For may years I stopped living.
No one understood my reason.
I was lost along with you.
My soul has been found.
My youth has been renewed.
Having goals and dreams today
Seemed imposible yesterday.
My love for you will not die
But my life must find a new way.
Sorry you haven't searched your soul.
Someday, I hope and pray
That you find a new direction.
But if you don't, 
Please remember, I loved you from the heart.  
Good-bye lost one.
