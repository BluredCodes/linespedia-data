---
title: "Your Call"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "your-call"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Thank you for coming into my life
When I most needed to drink
From your fountain of love.
Memories will be forever engraved
In the collection of my family
Picture tree heart of walls.
I have faith that we were meant to be
Written down in history with a love
Like ours...so out of control.
Our love could only have meant that we
Both shared a need and a desire
For something we have only yearned.
Waiting can't be my life, I have to soar...
Looking around enjoying what 
Real life has in store.
When the time is right or if it's meant to be
You will take flight and meet
Me at the waiting door.
Take the time you need to explore your most
Inner heart and soul patiently...
You have all the time you need.
This one isn't my call, for some angry reason
You walked away from the promises
We made and vowed to each other.
I know I was true to my heart 
And words were spoken from my soul, some things
Change and some things don't.
Living a lie or in a drugged state of mind...
Does not fulfill the mind, body and
Souls full strength of knowing...
I am willing to meet in the middle
To find that something that has been
Desired between our souls.
Now it is your call, don't let anything detour you
From your waiting and loving wife...
I forgive you...
I love you...
