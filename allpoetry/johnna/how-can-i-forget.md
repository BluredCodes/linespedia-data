---
title: "How Can I Forget?"
writer: "Johnna"
slug: "how-can-i-forget"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9789-How-Can-I-Forget--by-Johnna"
---

How can I let go?
You won't!
Will it ever be over?
Can we ever forget,
The many years lost?
We must let go.
We can!
It can be over. 
Let's forget.
Make-up the years we have left.
Let go and let it be.
I can.
Today is a start.
Let's forget the past. 
Have a bright tomorrow.
Life is to short to stop living.
Tomorrow may never come.
Live for today.
God will provide the rest.
We will find peace within.
Let go and forget.
I have. 
You can!

       Love,
            Mom12-15-97,
