title: "Lost"
writer: "Johnna"

The clouds have goneThe sun is out.Each day I seekThe way of the Lord.Some days I feel so lostTrying to find the way.The thought crosses my mindDrugs would be the answer.Thinking back, theyNever solved anything before.Everything becameWorse or destroyed.Losing all self-respectTakes years gaining back.Today, I have self-respect.Faith is the only drugI want today,That tomorrow will beBright with no clouds in sight!
