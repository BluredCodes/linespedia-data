title: "Last Hours Of My Life"
writer: "opkl"

I look back and see
All that I have done
And I ask for only a few hours
Back from the past
To change what I did.
I hurt you.
But I care about you.
So I am sad that I did
What I did.
I ask that you listen
I ask that you forgive
Maybe not for you,
Perhaps for me,
You see every second you hate me,
The worse and worse I feel.
I am sorry for what I did.
10 minutes can ruin it all
though 10 years may not fix
I ask that you let me try
Try to show you I am sorry.
For these last few hours
I have been
alone.
I will always remember them,
As I hope
You will remember how much I care.
