Someday you may see
Who I am inside
Someday this mask may slip
Until then it will stay on 
For what lies beneath 
Can only harm those close to me
If I take it off,
Now it can only hurt me,
But I can help you, 
and 
maybe
I will be strong enough
to look myself
in the eye.
