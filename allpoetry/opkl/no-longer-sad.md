title: "No longer sad"

Sad music
Paints memories in my mind
Laughter
Makes me wish I were deaf
Smiling faces
Make my wish I was blind

And then there is you

You are beautiful
You make me care
You make me love
I have just wanted solitude
Now you are here
You changed my heart 
Winter's stone now Summer's sun.
Should I hate you?
Or thank you?
Maybe I'll just love you.
