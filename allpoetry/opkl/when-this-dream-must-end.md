title: "When This Dream Must End"
writer: "opkl"

Friends are the days of summer
Gone with this winds of the North
They will walk down that hall
Perhaps they will stop
Remember times past
But no more
Round the corner
Out of site
Tears to blind eyes
Cries to the wind
They will not run
Nor shall they stand here with me
The tighter I hold 
The more they will push away
I must let them go
I will wait here for their return
Maybe all 
Maybe one
Maybe none
One to give me a hug
One to remember that I am still alive
For if they do not 
I am dead
But they must go
In the rain I will wait.
Until they return
I just hope it is before I drown

Useless wish to the wind.It did not come out like I wanted. I had the feeling that no matter how hard I tried I would someday be alone, again. No matter how I tried my friends would drift away from me. So why should I get close, it will only hurt more in the end.
