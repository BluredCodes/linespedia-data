---
title: "I wish I could still cry"
writer: "opkl"
slug: "i-wish-i-could-still-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.

The night is long, a tired mess,
sad in it self, dead in the end
crying to me, hoping for life

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.

Tired in my mind, awake in my soul,
happy in life, ecstatic in death,
living in night, shrouded in day

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.

life of a book, murder of a pen,
wishing for eyes, hoping for sight
purity of heart, stain of the mind

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.

Tears are dry, dry and dead
gone forever
into the dark, born of the light

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.

dead of mind, alive in heart
sad in soul, happy in tears
life of sight, death of eye

The music of silence rings in my head 
above the sadness of this world, into the black of the night, 
hiding my soul, killing my friends.Wow. No meaning, just words.
