You want to fling
My broken self 
Into the chasm of the dark

Shatter me soul
Pick up the pieces
The glass will cut deep and I shall laugh
Pick up your blood 
Go ahead and try
Glass can be melted, fixed
Made better then the sum of its parts

So I will come back
And laugh at you.
