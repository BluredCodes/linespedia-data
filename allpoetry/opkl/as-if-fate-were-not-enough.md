As if fate were not enough
There seems to be another force
Pushing me away
Away from my friends
Away from my family
I feel a pressure
Worse then the Abyss
Perhaps it is my need
To get good grades
Perhaps it is their demand
To make me finish my commitments
Perhaps it is my friends
Though their problems are not their fault
Perhaps it is my enemies
Trying to push me down
You know…
I need a vacation.
