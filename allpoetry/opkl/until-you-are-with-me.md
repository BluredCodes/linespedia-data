---
title: "Until You Are With me"
writer: "opkl"

Did you not know
It is the Abyss where I reside?
You pain shan't hurt me here
For I have known pain
Far to long
But it is not that 
That matters in life,
For I would take your pain on hundred times
To save you from it once

And I see you there
Cold and alone,
And I know too well
How you must feel
So my hand is here to help you up
And it will not leave
Until again
You are 
With me.
