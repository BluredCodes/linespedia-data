In the abyss I reside
Only death brings the end
The Lady of dark I stand beside
For my weary heart to mend

Sadness lives only in fear
Even though insanity is near
Though the sands of time my cease 
Pressure of water cannot decease

The fingers of cold
Will make the id to mold
Where and when will my spirit come to attack
In this hideous world of black?

Is there a light in this world of dark?
Or am I chasing a soaring lark?
I do think my greatest plight,
Is my quest for long lost light.
