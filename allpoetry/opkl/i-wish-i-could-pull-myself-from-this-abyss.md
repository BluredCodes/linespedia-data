I wish I could pull myself from this abyss 
though something is holding my down. 

Is it the miles of water? 
Pushing and crushing everything into noting 

Is it mud? 
Pulling and holding me in my watery grave 

Or is it my quest? 
for 
In this abyss 
there is 
no mud 
no water 
There is only me, 
me and my quest. 

Is it I who am drowning myself?
