title: "My Longest Day in May"

The name of freedom
The soul of life
The mind of meaning

Happened one day
It was the longest day of May

The hated word burned a hole in my heart
A feeling should have died
For its life is my death
Death of the Mind
Life of the Truth

The dagger has pierced my weary soul
Into the Abyss I fling my shattered self
Only the truth can save me 
Only the truth can kill me

For it has
