Many have told me
It has always been this way
That only grows my anger
More and more
For they did nothing
Not a thing at all
Yet now it is my fault
For wanting to help my friends
I shall never stand by 
And watch my friends get hurt
You all have
For far to long,
So does that make it okay?
Well I am here now
And not it will stop.
