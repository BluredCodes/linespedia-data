title: "Afraid"

I care so much about you,
So why are you afraid?

Some are afraid they will hurt me
Though I have no fear of pain

Some are afraid they will scare me
Though I have no worry toware fright

Some are afraid they will make me sad
Though I have no tears left for myself

Some are afraid they will waste my time
Though I have no malice to time, it is always there

Some are afraid
Of me
And I don't know why

Perhaps I am afraid of them,
Maybe it is may fault
