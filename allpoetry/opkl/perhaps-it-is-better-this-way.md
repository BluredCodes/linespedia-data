title: "Perhaps it is better this way"

I have this way
born likely in fear
I rarely trust

Perhaps it is better this way
I can't hurt anyone if I am alone

Do not quote at me
Do not waste you breath
For I have loved
And I have lost 

Not again

Perhaps it is better this way

better
AloneI'm sorry.
