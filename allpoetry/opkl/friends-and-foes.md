What is the difference between a friend and a foe?

A foe brings pain.
But doesn't a friend do the same?

A foe might destroy all that you have,
Though might not a friend do the same.

A foe is cold hearted,
Maybe friends are too.

My foes have tried to hurt me.
My friends really have.

Are you my friend?

Do I even have friends?
