---
title: "No Crystal Tears"
writer: "opkl"
slug: "no-crystal-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44748-No-Crystal-Tears-by-opkl"

I want no tears for myself
I wish no sadness for any of my friends
In this Abyss
Water fills my eyes
So I have no need for tears
Though you are not here 
In this pit of dark
You are alive and well
So cry no crystal tears for me
I will be ok
