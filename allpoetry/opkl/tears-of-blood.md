I am going with "tears of blood" just because it sounds the darkest. lol.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Crying for my life
Lying out of fear
Black tar like blood
Once was white and pure
it bleeds form my soul
to the dark cold floor
if only a single tear
would fall from my eyes of stone
incessant cold
incessant dark
life is not gone
it is only changed
mutated and evil
it does not wish for death
though for a better life
but they have never seen the sun
it is I who have
the more I try to reach it 
the deeper down they pull
red blood now black
clean hands no longer pure
burning light, cold and dark
light heart, now heavy as stone
does dark take away light?
or does lost light, leave dark in the Void?
