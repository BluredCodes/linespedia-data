Never will I forget you
Never will I hate you
It is true we are more distant then before
But I hold no malice toward you 
I have no hatred in my heart
You will always be my friend
At least who you once were
I am sorry all has crumbled to this
And I am sorry for what has happened
Someday I hope you will forgive me
Someday I hope you will see
That I was always your friend
I care about you,
That will never change.I am truely sorry.
