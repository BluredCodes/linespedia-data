title: "Why I hate you"

Do you think I hate you?
Well then you are wrong.
I do not hate, but I do dislike.
Do not ask why I don't like you
For you of all should know
Perhaps if you gave
The issue a minute of thought
You may realize feeling sorry for yourself
Due to my dislike
Will only make you a sad sad fool.
I don't like sad fools
You least of all.
