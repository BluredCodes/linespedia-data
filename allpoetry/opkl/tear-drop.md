Tear Drop of Dark Light
Evil face 
Kind heart
A tear of sadness
Escapes an eye
That has seen pain
But evil is you
You who thinks that
It is a weapon 
Used to bring 
Pity
Perhaps it is true
But then tell me why
Those deep blue eyes,
Care so much.
