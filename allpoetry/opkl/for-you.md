---
title: "For you"
writer: "opkl"

---

If it was not for you
I would not be 
who I am

If it was not for you
I would still be sad
like I was before

You are willing to take up the weight for me
no one else would
no one else can
no one else knows
no one else cares

but I know that you will
I know that you can
I know that you know
I know that you care

but I don't know if I could do that to you,
you see
I love you.
