title: "Black Blood"
writer: "opkl"

Your black blood
will fall to the clay and sand
from a wound that I have made
with this blade
of gray mist.
even if it is only a single drop
hits the hidious Earth
a rose of white
shall show the light 
of a blackened sun
My blade will not kill you
the cold wind will lift it into nothin
but the light shall melt you to nothing,
Then 
I shall sleep.
