Friends in my head 
Are because they always will be
Why will anyone else stay?
Why will they care after tomorrow?
Why will no one say 

Cry a tear for me
I will cry with you
Sing a song for me 
I will sing with you
Tell a joke for me
I will laugh with you

Care about me
I care about you
Trust me
I will not leave you standing here
Stand with me
I will stand with you
Do not leave
I will stay here with you
Love me
For I love you
