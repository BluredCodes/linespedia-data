It
Is 
My 
Fault

How?

I wish I knew
But I don't
It just is.

I care about everyone.
I may talk mean
I may say words not meant
Please forgive me
I am sorry.

If only I were alone
I could not hurt those
Close to me
For there we be none

And you will be safe 
from me.
