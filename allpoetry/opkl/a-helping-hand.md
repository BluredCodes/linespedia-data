I was alone, in the dark. I had fallen. There was nothing. Then, a bit of something appeared. A hand. Light in a darkened world. 

It pulled me from that abyss
yet as it did, darkness fell away
Hatred, hurt, immaturity
died.

I am who I am. 
A single hand never changed me
 it only freed me.
