title: "Mist"
writer: "opkl"

You can smell it
You can feel it
You can sense it
But you cannot see through it
 
Never that same
It mocks us
Making it seem so real
Then gone
The closer you draw
The farther it pulls

Don't try to hold it close,
For then you will never have it,
You will only hold
Destruction of your life

Don't hug the wolf, 
It will only hate you,
Respect it,
It will be your friend.
