Walls you buildNo compromiseEternal dodgingIn your eyesAmong a millionTimes I askedCan’t get throughA heart of glassHere I amThrowing youThe lifeline toMy worldHere I amToo much fromMeFor you to graspClimbing upFrom pains first fallDo I meanAnythingNothing at allComing fromA gentle soulEnter hereJust let goHere it isFor you to graspLoving lifelineAt first glanceYou don’t get itNever willTherapyI foot the billI foot the bill©2001 Crow MajikMay 14, 2001Frustrated love for a mate that doesn't see....
