Where do we belong?
In a book of shadows
Of the chosen few
A little skin
Of flesh and bone
Bodies of angels
Temporary home
May we live in vain
May we live in vain


Inside we struggle
With the sane
Take the water from the church
Sprinkled  on our souls
Yet we’re only chosen few
May we live in vain
May we live in vain


Where do we belong?
In the earliest days
Dancing in the book
Of shadows
Past belongs to us
Present is a gift
Future is unrest
May we live in van
May we live in vain
