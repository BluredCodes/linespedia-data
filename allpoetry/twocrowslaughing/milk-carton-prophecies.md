Have you seen your father?
Did he go away?
Have you gotten over that
Or has it caused dismay?

Have you seen your mother?
Was she there for you?
Or did her life revolve around
A man who liked abuse?

How did we come to 
this point in our lives 
how did we get here
Did we survive?

Children pay
for parents' crimes
it happens all the time

Are you paying for dad's transgressions
and does mom make you drink from the urn
of a womb thats too much like a prism
so many colors, no where to turn?

Have you seen your parents, kids?
Look on the carton that says,
Have you seen me?A new song....
