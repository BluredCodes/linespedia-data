Send me an angel
Of pure, white light
A messenger of God’s eternal love

Send me an angel 
To comfort me 
Till I stop shuddering,
Crying, hysterically

As faith of a mustard seed
So small and fragile
Though my troubles seem bigger than me
I will not falter, Father

Send me an angel
To relieve my parched lips
As I drink the blood of you
And quench my thirst
For answers whyAny improvements you can suggest??
