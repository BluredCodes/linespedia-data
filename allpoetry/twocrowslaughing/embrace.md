---
title: "Embrace"
writer: "TwoCrowsLaughing"

I’m tired of this: Tired of that
Shut your mouth
Try to see
Use your eyes: Instead of words
Open your mind: Let hatred purge
Ease your pain: Anyway you can
Don’t need this: From a fellow man
Shut your mouth : Let me live
Embrace me with: The love I give
Know it all’s : With words of praise
Embarrass me: In fits of rage
I’m tired of this: Tired of that
Shut your mouth
Try to see
Use your eyes: Instead of words
Open your mind: Let hatred purge
Seize the day: Relish your gifts
Be thankful for: What you wish
I’m tired of this: Tired of that
Shut your mouth: Let me live
Embrace me with: The love I give
To you……
