As conversation waged
It’s battle on my ears
I saw you out the window
Erasing all my fears

The subtle winds that blew
Your redness in the sky
I could feel you calling me
Beckoned to me ‘outside’

I longed to put away
This visit I endured
By someone who’s a stranger now
Yet used to make me feel secure

For to see your colors reigning
In that deep, magenta sky
Out the window of this fortress
Made me long to go outside

To feel the warmth of sunset
Feel the kiss of sweet, pure wind
And put away all troubles 
Once again

I succumb to 
A sunset
