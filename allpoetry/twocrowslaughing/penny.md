On the floor
I see a penny
Penny for your thoughts
Oh little cherished one
Who plays with friends 
She caught


Penny plays with angels
Can’t you see her laughing
Talking
Learning


Innocence within
Without a backward glance
Without a trace of fear
Penny does her dance


Cant you see her laughing
Learning
Talking
Yearning


Angels whisper softly
And give her what she needs
A penny for your thoughts
Upon a bandaged knee


Innocence within
Without a backward glance
Without a trace of fear
Penny does her dance
