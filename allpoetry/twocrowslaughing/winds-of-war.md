Winds of war
Befall me now
My lover’s gone away
To learn the ways 
Of warfare
That he will need today


I wait in earnest
Patiently
Until he does return
My heart it beats
In double time
Fighting on
It yearns


His eyes of brown
They haunt my nights
As I lay in bed awake
His calls and letters
Are enough for now
But I long to see his face

A warrior true
Will beat the risk
Thrust upon us by
The terrorists
And wait I will 
Holding hands of time 
Till I once again
Can call you mine

Winds of War
Blow fast and true
We will remember you.....
