Green, green fields
In time forgot
Flower in hand
Petals blow 
He loves her,
He loves her not
Golden haired girl
With skin of satin
Beauty unmatched
Strikes the man breathless 
Who sits upon his steed
Of black
Hidden from view
Behind the mighty pines
Images of her
Dance within him 
Now here she is 
Waiting……
