I'm praying for you
Praying for us
Praying for this world
That drove you insane
Life was just too hard
For you, my friend
The lights did not shine bright enough  
For you
And now you've left 
To touch the face of God


Tho your voice was heard
Upon the stage
And those of us who only knew you
As a passerby
Still ask the question
Why? 
Simple word
Yet many answers
No rhyme or reason
No perfect season
We just cry
Now that you've left
To touch the face
Of God


How can we begin to understand
That you are now a memory?
Flying with the angels singing...
Singing, that you loved
Your audience is now 
With the ones who fly above
Now that you've left
To touch
The face of God.




In Memory of  George Wayne Shaw Jr. AKA Blaine
"Sugarbuzz" 2-18-01
I'll never forget doing the Rage song with you
