Sleep
The dream swoons me
Dance
The crowd moves me
Free flowing
Dreaming of
Long dresses
Snow white gloves

Bonnet hats, of blue
Could never match the sky
Off we go behind the barn
To steal a kiss 
I say I’m shy…….

Innocence recaptured
Oh a grassy knoll
Beyond the great abyss
Of past
My memories, they flow

Oblivious to other worlds
We do not understand
In dreaming we go there
Every woman, every man

Music
It moves me
Instrumental buffet
Makes me hungry
For more of what I feel tonight
In dreams……..

Starry night time sky
Fancy, black tie affair
Laughter floating up from
A place I call somewhere
In time

Dance 
The music swoons me
Calls me back in time
Somewhere I left so long ago
I can visit 
When I close my eyes

Oblivious to other worlds
We may never understand
We can visit when we’re dreaming
Every woman, every man……..
