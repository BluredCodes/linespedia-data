Slamming door
Paced the floor
Watched you go
But didn’t have the guts to 
Make it right
I lost you for another night
And every time
You walk out
I regret the day we met
But the love won’t die
It just makes me cry
I never knew, have never known
And I still don’t know why
I live
In this
This one sided love
Me loving you
Is killing me
Just set me free

I don’t know what to do
Should I play it off
Or keep loving you?
All I know is I don’t wanna hurt no more
Cause all you want 
Is someone who
Picks you up and holds you upon your
High horse, of course
Cause you’re the man
And I’m the bitch
The one who always took your shit
No more
I’m out the door
From this one sided love
Me loving you is killing me
Set me free!!

I’ve packed my bags its time to go now
I know that I can’t let it show how
When I leave, I see you here, see you there
I see you every fuckin where!!!!!
You gotta let me go 
Set me free 
Let me try to find another lover
Who won’t cheat or try to smother me
But to love again?

My heart will never mend
From this one sided love
Me loving you is killing me
Killing me…….This is a song that was written a few years ago when divorce was looming on the horizon....a bluesy, slow song, kinda with a Stevie Ray feel....hope ya like it.
