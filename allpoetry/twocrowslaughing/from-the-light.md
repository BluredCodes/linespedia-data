Street light shines through a crack in the curtain
As I turn out the light
And you can stay up watchin' t.v.
All night
I'm not afraid of goin' to bed alone
You see, I've been through this before
Feign sleep as you tip toe across the floor

The love has faded
I see the look in your eyes
From the light 
That shines through our window
From the light that shines

Street light shines through a crack in the curtain
As ya climb into bed
Careful not to shake it
Don't wake the one you wed
I feel you watchin me sleep
You think I'm havin' sweet dreams
But the sandman he don't come anymore
Cause he's too busy pacin' the floor

The love has faded
I see the look in your eyes
From the light
That shines through our window
From the light that shines

I see the light 
Through a crack in the curtain
As I climb into bed
