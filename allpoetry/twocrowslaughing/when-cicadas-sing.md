---
title: "When Cicadas Sing"
writer: "TwoCrowsLaughing"

Cicadas sing sweet lullaby’s
Today outside my window
First time this year
I’ve heard their song
In woodsy trees called home


The summer winds send sweet perfume
Through windows' screen as I write this
Honeysuckle, sweetpeas
Blow in on summer’s kiss


Birds are singing
Drinking in the beauty of this place
Crickets chirp, cicada’s sing
As tears fall down my face


For I know someone
Who’s leaving 
Dying in a bed,  so cold  
In a room that has no comforts
Of what she once called home


Her fragile body’s tired 
Anytime now her last breath
Will take her to off to heaven
For her eternal rest


Maybe then she’ll hear
The sounds, 
The sounds of God’s most blessed
The souls of loved ones
Long since passed
When cicada’s sing
She’ll rest
When cicada’s sing
She’ll rest…..
