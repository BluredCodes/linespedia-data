I stumbled upon an old birthday card
Complete with the scent of moth balls
Pulled from a chest called hope
Which time long since forgot

Your handwriting was so easy to recognize
On the frilly pink trim
And those candles on front
How they made me remember
How you never forgot
A date
A year
Nothing
And in turn
I'll visit you when I can
In Section 10
Plot 22
I'll never forget you
I miss you....
