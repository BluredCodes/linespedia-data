Nostrils flared
Red pony rides
Cross the fields of barley
Raven haired girl
Petticoats fly
Racing to 
Her true love's side
She knows 
When she arrives
His arms 
They will embrace her
Take her
To heights she only dreamed
True lovers often do
And she'll break the chains that bind
Her
Forever 
Things denied her
Will be given with grace
By the man with the passion
To tame her
