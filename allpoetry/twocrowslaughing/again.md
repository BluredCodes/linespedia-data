---
title: "Again"
writer: "TwoCrowsLaughing"

Friendly eyes
Sweet, sweet smile
A lovely human
Warmth of child
Who claimed to be
Someone called friend
Betrayal lay
Around the bend
False face you wore
And all along
You tried in vain
To sing my song
Listened to
Me rant and rave
About my life
And all I gave

Saved me for
Your rainy day
When others had 
All walked away
The ones you loved
They now will see
How you practice
To deceive

We were close
There for awhile
I should’ve seen
The motive why
The way you looked
Into his eyes
And used me while
You bid your time

But now I know
I know the reason 
You were a friend 
Just for a season
To teach again
A lesson learned
So long ago
Now it’s returned
Another friend
Who wanted more
And my heart
Lays on the floor
Again
