Sun shines throughThe fresh, green leavesAftermathOf storm’s reprieve Lightening gaveIt’s way to slashAcross the skyAs Thunder crashedTorrential rainArrived in sheetsBlinding, drivingHome it’s beatThen it was goneThe dark, black cloudsQuickly racedTo the next townLeaving meWith scented mistI’d just been hitBy summer’s kissSummer storm 2001......blew up out of the blue....left without warning too!
