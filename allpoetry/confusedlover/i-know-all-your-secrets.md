I Know All Your Secrets
by Valerie- Confusedlover

I know what you do
you can't hide from me
all your faults covered by lies
I see the real you
I don't like what i see,and so i have no choice
but to rebel
im scared to be around you,
in fear that you might realize..
I know who you really are.
the manipulation and blackmail wont work with me
I have you in shame
Finally I won.
Now you go away,
get out of my life
stop torturing me
tormenting my mind
I see through the facade you put across to us
I know all your secrets
