The World at My feet
by Valerie- confused lover


I think about you and i just cant help but smile-
ear to ear as a thought about you brushes by my mind
Theres something about you,
whether its your cute smile,
playful touch,
sarcastic humor or cynical remarks
your confident attitude.
 You may not be the best for me-
but
I want you
I cant explain it I just need you.
A simple thought of you makes me go to cloud nine
I cant get you out of my head
I just wish you felt the same
because then-
 the world would be all mine
