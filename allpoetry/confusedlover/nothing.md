Nothing
by Valerie- confusedlover

Sometimes I wish I could get rid of it all,
the hurt, shame, anger, but most of all
the terror of my lifes becomings.
Where will I go?
what will I be?
Will I ever get what I want??
Be what I want?
Does it all really matter?
Will I end up alone?
No one knows or really understands me,
or anything about my life, and my goals...
but do I really have any?
I live my life day by day...
but is that the best way?
Can I go on living like this??
scared, uhappy
terrified of where my choices will take me..
all I know is
Nothing.
