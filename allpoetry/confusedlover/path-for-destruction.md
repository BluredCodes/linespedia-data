Path for Destruction
by Valerie- confusedlover

I can't sleep at night
I am never doing whats right
I'm on a path for destruction
I let my feelings out, and it does no good
I bottle them up inside, and it gets worse
My hearts wrapped in darkness
my head filled with inpure thoughts
I think I do the right thing, but things don't turn out that way.
confusion is keeping me from the truth
My heads in the clouds
dont know what to feel,
or what to do
I dont know what to believe, my head or my heart
If I get off this path, stop falling apart,
don't let anyone into my heart before i know what I want.
maybe, just maybe, I will finally get what I need
The truth may hurt, but not telling it, keeps me away from myself.late 2001,
