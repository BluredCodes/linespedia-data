Sweet Innocence,

by Valerie O'Kane



Im so confused

I'm listening to my heart,

but my head is saying no

I should give up on you

Your not good for me,

im not your kind

The taste of sweet innocence,

longing to be kept 

about to be broken.

The thoughts are'nt natural, 

you send them through my mind,

unstoppable, indestructable,

incurable.

We have so much in common,

yet were so different.

Experience holds me far away. 

I dont know what to do

Im scared-

My Sweet innocence,

longing to be kept

About to be lost.
