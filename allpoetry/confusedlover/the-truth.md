The Truth
by Valerie- confused lover

No where to run
No where to hide
thoughts trickling through my veins
my brain overwhelmed with confusion
I dont know what to do
im trying to find anything to take me away
away from the truth that stings,
like a bee, all over my body
it hurts-
the pain straight from my own heart
I trust no one
no one cares therefore no one knows
The anger that has built up inside of me
its tearing me apart
my hearts in threads
my head is cursed by my thoughts,
but with fake smiles and empty laughter,
I will keep anyone from knowing the truth,
the truth that has become my life.
