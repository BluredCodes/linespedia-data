Untitled,
by Valerie 

Hate hovers my head
I cant think anymore,
I might as well be dead
everything is all a bore

I never do whats right
nothing makes me glad
I cant sleep at night
everything just gets me mad

I cant just be happy and sing,
to my favorite song, 
I hate everyone, and everything
I feel bitter, and wrong

I think of the past
and all of the things, ive done
I gave up everything so fast,
and now im left with none.
