Forgive And Forget
by Valerie- confusedlover

I look at my life,
am I ever really happy?
when I cry,
are those stinging tears telling the truth?
Hugs and Kisses 
what do they mean?
are they messing with minds?
when I smile-
is that me being fake?
or my heart showing true happiness?
I Love You....
what do those words really mean?
why is it when a person says them,
the world stops?
No more problems
does anyone ever really love?
or is it all just lust?
Is it possible to hate?
you swear you will never forgive...
instead you just forget.
