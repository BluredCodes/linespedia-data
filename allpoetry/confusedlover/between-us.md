Between Us
by Valerie -confusedlover-

I think about you and i cringe
it seems as if i hate you so much that-
i can't stop thinking about you.
somethings there..
between us
its hard to explain-
we joke around,
but do we really feel that way?
Between us there are so many confusing feelings.
we're waiting for something to happen, 
yet we both dont make an effort.
it seems as if the world is between us
but will so many miles..
keep us apart?
