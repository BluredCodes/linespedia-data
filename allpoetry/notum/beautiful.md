We flirt, we laugh,
we talk, we smile,
I wonder how you feel,
do you want to be friends?
or more than that?
you know i like you,
so why do you play like this?
is this all a game to you?
i'm very sensitive,
i need you to care,
i dont need pain,
so please dont lie,
just tell me the truth,
tell me how you feel,
if you dont like me,
I will be hurt, but i will live,
If you do like me, it's beautiful.11-22-01,
