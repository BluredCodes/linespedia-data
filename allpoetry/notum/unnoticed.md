title: "Unnoticed"
writer: "Notum"

Unnoticed, that I am,
you dont see me walking by,
too caught up in your little world,
to ask me why i cry,
my world is falling apart,
and it all started with you,
what happened to our friendship,
the problems i helped you through,
but when i cried out for help,
it was a cry that no one heard,
i felt as insignificant,
as a little baby bird,
whom no one cared about,
or would mind if it died,
im drifting in the ocean,
getting carried by the tide,
no one will notice when im gone,
ill just be that one nerd,
that sat next to them in class,
but never said a word.
