Change

why do things have to change? 
friends move, hearts change,
I wish they would just stay the same,
"friends forever' you say,
then you start to drift apart with every passing day,
nothing good lasts, 
it never stays the same,
you open your heart and recieve nothing but pain,
sometimes i wish things could go back to the way they used to be,
back to the times when i was free,
free from hate, free from fear,
free from the pressure and bad things i hear,
but things always change and i cant stop it,
so ill just have to learn to accept it.11-22-01,
