title: "My prayer"

God,
I know i am not perfect,
but please help me to strive towards you,
please forgive me of my sins,
make my clean and new.

I know ill hit temptatition,
please help me to say no,
surround me with good people,
and protect me from the foe.

I thank you for my blessings,
and all you've given me,
clothes to wear and food to eat,
friends and family.

Please keep me safe through the night,
until night turns to day,
bless my friends and family too,
Lord this is all i pray.

Amen
