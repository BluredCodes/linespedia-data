I resolve to go through with it
My heart pounds as the scissors 
Come closer and closer 
To my security
As the long blades touch my glory
I begin to cry
And realize I can't do it
I can't lose my safety
I can't give away my mask
I can't let myself become exposed
You laugh at my weakness---
You still have a hold on me.Eh,...seems kinda stupid,but I really just can't let go of my hair!It's too personal!
