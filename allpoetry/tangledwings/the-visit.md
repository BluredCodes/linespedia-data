---
title: "The Visit"
writer: "TangledWings"

At first it was relatively awkward,
But we managed
I respected that you didn't assume you'd receive a hug
Though you were my uncle,and I,your neice.
The circumstances that brought us together
Were upsetting and I knew 
You would feel out of place
But we soon formed a bond
When we discovered we had more than just a love for the Beatles in common.
I knew we were kindred spirits
We shared similar views
And you understood me unlike 
The rest of our easily-influenced family
Because of my melancholy attitude
I could relate to your black-sheepish ways
And I had decided months prior to your visit
That although I heard somewhat negative
Accusations about you,
I would most likely appreciate you even more.
We had philosophical discussions
And you listened to me question God
I admired your outlandish spirit
And I thought it intriguing that
You could speak such wonderful Chinese
I enjoyed your fascinating stories of life
And I was priviledged that you could 
Share in such a memorable moment in my life.
I felt honored that you called me an artist
Considering that you were so educated
And suddenly,as soon as it all came,
The visit had to end...
I fear I won't see you again
Till I am of legal drinking age
And another five years will go by
Although you just left ten minutes ago
I can still smell your charming scent
And my eyes get misty
You were my one relative who truly
Understood me
We were two peas in a pod,you and I,
And I now realize
Why saying "hello" is easier
Than saying "goodbye".
