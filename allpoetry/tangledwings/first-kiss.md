Your eyes dance around me
And I wait in nervous anticipation
The quiet humming of our 
Combined breathing
Echoes throughout the room
Finally---alone
Noone to bother us
No reason to avoid what will happen
Thoughts,feelings,
Flying,fleeting
Until we make contact
This is it
This is the beginning
This is our First Kiss.Um,....I think that the title is pretty much self-explanatory...
