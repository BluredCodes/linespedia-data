The initial shock comes over my face
And then the silence
The tears begin streaming down my face
And my mascara begins to give 
My eyes the raccoon effect...
Reading the words,not quite believing them
My heart beat is pounding
Throughout my body and my
Head is feeling tangled
Thoughts fly through my mind
And I question myself as to why
I didn't know,didn't have a clue
Felt I didn't have to ask
I feel honored that you trust me so much
Yet somewhat betrayed and torn
My heart is a mess
And I resolve to keep on loving you
Because that's the only thing I know how to do
The road is going to be long and hard
And there will be times that you won't want to be 
True to yourself
But I am offering myself here and now
To hold your hand
I won't let go of you...
