In a daze
In a craze
Reaching in the drawer
Falling on the floor
Bleeding from my skin
This is my constant sin
Feeling all this pain
Am I even sane?
If hurting myself can feel so good,
Now I know why others always kick me down...I just felt like using someone else's perspective...
