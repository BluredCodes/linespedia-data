I see that you're insecure
If you weren't,maybe you'd still be pure
Instead of a person capable of being labeled "whore"---
I remember the night that we swore
Under the moon and stars to never fight,
I now sit here trying to bite
Down my tongue from calling you names
I hate how I hear that your mother blames
Your problems on me
What the hell is wrong with her?Why can't she see
That you're your own worst enemy
A sneak,a tramp---you hold the key
To your life
The way you hurt me feels like a knife
Stuck in my heart
Were we even friends from the start?
You're a hypocrite,you don't even care
I feel so betrayed so now I dare
To say what I think to the friend who ditches
I think you and your mom are two sniveling bitches!!!Eh,I was feeling a little pissed off about my estranged friend,...really hurt,so this is what I got when I tried rhyming,.....I'm really not a mean person either!!!
