The bruise was left behind
Merely a memory of the pain
That first collision
Hurt like hell,but the
Stinging slowly subsided
Now that I have this mark
I will never be the same
I won't be able to forget this
I won't be the same person I was
It won't go away
It won't leave me to swim in my thoughts
Because now I've been bruised and broken
And I can't get over it.This poem was actually quite symbolic of a circumstance in my life.I actually was bruised in gym class the day my grandmother had a brain aneurism,and I saw that bruise as a symbol of months of pain and rehabilitation on her part,so,...this poem is for her.I love you,Nana!
