BLANK PAPER

I stare at it

The thin slices of 

Processed wood

I CAN'T THINK

No subjects to debate

Or discuss

Too many distractions

UNTIL A FLEETING THOUGHT

Finds it's way into my 

Mind and is 

Expanded

I BEGIN TO WRITE

And as I write

The thoughts fly and swirl

In my head

I FINALLY FINISH MY ARTISTIC TIRADE

And as I stare at this paper

That five minutes before was blank

I discover

I HAVE WRITTEN A MASTERPIECEI wrote this on one of my "dry" days,meaning NO INSPIRATION!!!!
