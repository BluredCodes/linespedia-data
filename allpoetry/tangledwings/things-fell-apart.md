Things fell apart
And everything was surreal and morbid
The rain poured 
And poured and poured
I felt trapped and scarred
Not able to breathe
Cut,bleeding,swollen
And just as my world came
Crashing on top of me
You came and brought me a ray of light
To my never-ending night...
You were my bandaid.I admit,this isn't the best poem I've ever written,but I felt like writing something for my boyfriend.:)
He came along during an impossible time in my life,so,....what can I say?He's my bandaid...
