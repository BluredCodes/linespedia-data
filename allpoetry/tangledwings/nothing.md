Perhaps we were nothing to you.

I stare at you as you tell me 
The News,
That you don't need me anymore.

Perhaps I was nothing to you.

I don't want to show emotion 
Though inside I'm crying.
Weren't we merely an illusion?

Perhaps I am nothing.

After all,you have her
We need not ask ourselves "Quo vadimus?" anymore.
Now I am just an empty glass.This poem is about this guy I once liked,and our imitation relationship.
