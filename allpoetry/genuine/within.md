title: "Within"
writer: "Genuine"

Deep within eyes of pain

I see distrust and all it brings.

Tears fallen to a darkened sea

Wash to shore another misery.



Aged photos of long ago,

Memories from a past

Let go.



Reaching out demands a price

Yet could one exist without a heart?



Life without love is a lifeless dream.

A night void of star or scene. 

So to you I give an open heart

And kiss the seed unearthed to rot

With a sweetness of the morning dew 

Renewing the love inside you.
