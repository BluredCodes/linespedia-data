---
title: "Scar Story"
writer: "Genuine"
slug: "scar-story"

Remorse in shadows, I saw none 

As laughter echoed from an unfamiliar tongue.

Malefic eyes exposed an empty heart

Ripping dignity in the dark.

Perpetually changed by evil done

Such sweet innocence lost too young.

Another lesson on the cruelties of hate

Beckons this soul to lock it's gate.

Silent tears fall from enraged desire

Devouring essence through corrupted fire.

What should have been a tender touch,

Now cries a soul who's injured much.

Benumbed and bewildered, exceedingly unsure,

Emotions withdraw and remain obscure

Guarding this intrinsically shattered core 

Protecting the remains of all I saw pure.
