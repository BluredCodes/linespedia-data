It's all just a game
There is no feeling, no pain
An image created from ignorance
A wish for the nonexistant
A dream for something new
We long for that one presence
But there is nothing there
We imagine the feeling
Create the sensation
Then we forget its orgin
And believe in the game, the lie

It's all just a lie
Created to reinact the feeling, the pain
The image becomes distorted
The wish, an addiction
The dream, a meaningless reality
We create something more
Without realizing what has occured
We cease to see the true nature
Believing in all the false ideas
Then we remember its orgin
Yet continue to believe in the game, the lie
