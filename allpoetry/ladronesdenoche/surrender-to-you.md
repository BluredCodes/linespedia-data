---
title: "Surrender to You"
writer: "LadronesdeNoche"

Spiraling down, I fall
All light has but vanished
Everything I once knew has changed

Dizzying thoughts take over
I have lost all control
Is there no one to break my fall

Inevitability
Extraordinarily
I surrender to you
