---
title: "Forever Lasting"
writer: "LadronesdeNoche"

The night, forever lasting
All alone in the cold, lifeless room
Remembrance of you
You tender hands, your warm smile
An erruption of pain
For the joy has all but faded
The light is no more

I used to believe
In lucky charms
In falling stars
But as I waited for you
Silently hoping with teary eyes
Each wish fell to the floor
Breaking into a thousand more

To navigate through darkness
To find the story's end
My hope survives the storm
And on the day our worlds reunite
The joy will overcome the pain
For I am yours and you are mine
We are Forever Lasting
