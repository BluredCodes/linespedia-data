002

There was a parking lot
Days of candy-fields and cleats
Sunshine and his smiles
Where innocence was denied
Among the crude jokes
I always liked it better
Glory in picking the daisies
And I could have sworn we'd died
Even when it rained
Nothing was wrong to us (in fact was right)
It wasn't even God's place to judge
We'd choked on dust and survived.This one actually has a rhyme scheme...
