TEARFULLY I CRY

I sit here nude alone in my world in which I call my room. 

Only problem is your not here with me. 

Damn I want you 

I also want to be free of from all the pain of not having you inside of me 

2 wish for a body like yours 2 commit 2 your mind and soul in this lost simplicity of sex 

Our minds know its love 

But our bodies think other wise 

Don't you know I can only live once when im not inside of you 

But I can live a million times inside of you rebirth 

Because I become you 

I become all the things that make you the nearest thing to heaven 

That all men want to become one day 

Because I become you And you become I 

The simplicities we reach in time will define our past lives 

but until then Tearfully I cry 

2 not lick you in the way you wash yourself in the shower 

Is a sin in the book of our rebirth 

A crime in which death is something I pray for 

I cant live in this world as a man 

If a man you cant see me as 

To love To die To become no more 

If I cant love your body the way I love your mind 

I'm trying to swim in the rain baby please hear me out. 

feel my pain My bed is…. 

Can you see me? 

Not now but in your mind 

If by any chance I'm there please take me closer Closer to your birth 

I want you so 

Not having a taste of what makes you want Keeps me lost in a place 

Keeps me wondering why 

Why cant I have you the way I want to 

God what to do 

To kiss you softly like wind blowing a kite Higher and higher 

We will fall into a grace that you mother never told you about

A grace where you know Im the one for you And you for I 

But until then baby 

Tearfully I cry 

I cry for all the things to come I cry when we come

When we mix 

When we become one Every night, every day, in every way 

Until the rise of the sun 

But until the end of the rebirth 

When I become you And you become I 

Alone wanting waiting searching alone here nude 

Tearfully baby 

Tearfully I cry…………..
