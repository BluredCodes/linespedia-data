a man 4 all reasons 

show me a man........ 

who wont open doors for you
and ill show you a man who will open up a million doors in your mind 

show me a man..........

who wont hold you
and ill show you a man who wont let you go 
a man who will be so close to you he will become you 

show me a man........... 

who wont speak his mind, who cant say the words i love you 
and ill show you a man who will try any way he can to show his love 

show me a man 

who will let his woman celebrate on her own
and ill show you a man who would never let his woman dance and eat dinner alone 

i will be the one 

to hold you until the rise of the sun 

ill be the one 

who wont stop until the job is done winter............spring..........fall..........and summer
around the clock through all the seasons 

ill be your man 

a man 4 all reasons 

for a man.............. who rather be out with his boys 

ill show you a man who would want to be your best friend 

for a man............ who cant be with just one woman 

ill show you a man who would want you to be his only woman 

for a man.......... who always wants to play 

ill show you a man who cant stay away 

for a man who wont take you out in the rain 

ill show you a man who cant make love to you unless its in the middle of a storm 

i will be the one to hold you until the rise of the sun 
ill be the one who wont stop until the job is done winter............spring..........fall..........and summer around the clock through all the seasons ill be your man a man 4 all reasonsThis is a pome for the woman who has a man who isnt doing thing right, ive recorded the music to it but, i dont know when ill let the world hear the song
