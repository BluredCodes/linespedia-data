Sinful bliss can I have a taste

Juices in my face

and end my tour with you at mars
cause its all about you sinful bliss
you’ll be the one in charge

you fell on me like an angel
for only my eyes to see
for tonight ill be your rebirth baby
and you’ll be my fantasy
