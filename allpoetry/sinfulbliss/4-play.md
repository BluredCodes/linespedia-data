4-Play

4 Play I am romance in a bottle 

Rub me 3 time and you'll see 

A romantic tongue talented lover 

Devoted to your body 

Master what is your wish 

Ill do everything on your list 

Come Come take my hand 

4 your wish is my command 

Let me touch your mind body and soul 

From the time we take a shower 

Until I tie you up on the door 

Let me touch your mind body and soul Until I become you 

and then become no more 

Ill be the one To touch you in places your man won't explore 

Theirs a place deep in your mind 

A place if you look for me I won't be too hard to find

Close your eyes for a second 

Now open them 

What did you see or in other words what did you feel? 

Nothing ? 

Well that's how I would feel if your not here next to me

Holding me like there was no tomorrow 

But who am I ? 

I'm just the man behind the mic 

So open up your mind and tell me how you feel

And if we are inside one another right now 

Don't wake me up because the dream feels so real 

I am romance in a bottle Rub me 3 time and you'll see 

A romantic tongue talented lover 

Devoted to your body

Master what is your wish 

Ill do everything on your list 

Come Come take my hand 

4 your wish is my command 

Let me touch your mind body and soul From the time we take a shower 

Until I tie you up on the door 

Let me touch your mind body and soul Until I become you 

and then become no more 

Ill be the one To touch you in places your man won't explore
