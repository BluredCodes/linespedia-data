title: "Celestial Worth"
writer: "Jewel"

It creeps into its own cavity
aches and takes on a form 
startles you at midnight 
leaves you helpless til dawn  

the hands you long to feel 
the eyes you hope to see 
the scent you nearly take in 
all but wings to set you free  

breezes blow in murky memories 
from her futuristic land 
earth not yet walked upon  
but tells a story in your hand  

the whispers upon your shoulder 
that help you walk through day 
the music playing in your mind 
reveals words you long to say
