I begin with the end of an erafrom the place of creationI came running with a shattered soullost amongst the coral tread upon with bare feetHere I found you callingit was your name that echoedreverberated off the silent walls of my heartthat now beat, with passion and pain thumping in tune with your worldHere I found you, and here I see youdancing amongst the flames moving closer to-wards the heatburning hotter and higherand glows through-out the empty desertwhere we are left, you and meand like the legend we live up toit may float by in a dreamwhether your to cast me from your windowor burn me inside alight I've now succumb, the one you love too muchwill hurting me become your drugin a futures land because I'm longing to feel the painthe way it was upon that legenda starry night a desert rosean Indian fightall because I loved you...
