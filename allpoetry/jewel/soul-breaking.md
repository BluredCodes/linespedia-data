If your soul started breakingI would run to you my friend when darkness begins it's takingI feel your the means to an end and in this night my heart does bleed...When my hands are tiedand dreams become the only lightdo you hear the call as she cries ?well its all here, deep insideand through the day my souls not free...put your ear to the ground, hear me callit's the one you love beneath your feetyou gave your hand so I would not falland for those still lost -  I called defeatdarkness surrounds me I've lost my way...I followed the wind as it sangsince then could no longer breathecan we join hands now and run ?take our time and feel the breezethe pain has left, it's you and me.
