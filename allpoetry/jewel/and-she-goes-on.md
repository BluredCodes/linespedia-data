title: "and she goes on..."
writer: "Jewel"
slug: "and-she-goes-on"

Music no longer leads the way,memories cradled into yesterday.She walks away for the web now spunno dew drops glistening from the sun.Frost sets upon the ending daymending hearts left to find the way.No sad tears left to shedThey part from the path they we're led.Toast to the pain no longer insideno pages to turn, the story's now died.
