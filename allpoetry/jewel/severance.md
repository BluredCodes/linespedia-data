---
title: "Severance"
writer: "Jewel"
slug: "severance"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I absorb the broken memorabilia
the ice has cracked, I await the snow 
time hangs heavily upon these hands
I ponder my wall built from remaining debris.

A journey to the last stroke of happiness
more than a decade ago
misty parties, laughter and my lollipop dress
clinking glasses, maternal love and honeyed words
such warm commemorations melting through my mind.

Thereafter blackness forms, a gaping hole in perfection
the pangs of a sudden dissolution
the beginning of a rupture crumbling our cornerstone
we conceived the sad remains
prepared our kindred blood

Do I cast an anchor to time ?
return and grasp at what I now know
those moments were so simply precious.
Perhaps then I could gain insight on repair
not be worn to a shadow and nodding at this fall
No, I will make way for a bow - 
this night is now over.
