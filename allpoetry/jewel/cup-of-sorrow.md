a cup of sorrow
poured out in vain
a sip for the future
a sip for the pain

picked ripe from the vine
treated with such care
a sip for the love 
a sip for the fear

drowning from the chalice
not to feel this dying heart
a sip as she shatters
a sip as she parts...
