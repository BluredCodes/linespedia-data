She walks through the day as if it were nighttreasuring the pain insidefor its the pain that serves to remindremind her of the love cast before her eyeslove that graced her with crystal magicbefore it shattered into a thousand piecesand was swept awayleaving a trail of blood from a wound that can't begin to heal.
