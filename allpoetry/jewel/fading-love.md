---
title: "Fading Love"
writer: "Jewel"
slug: "fading-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10381-Fading-Love-by-Jewel"
---

He stands tall and proud, trying to find the place in-between, 
ever so proudly takes control, a firm hold and walks on 
yet inside I can see the pain in his eyes 
the perfect surface fading into a circle of emotion 

I too can turn my back and walk away 
outwardly you will never see a tear 
courage my virtue, confidence my resting place 
but we know it doesn't stop the fear 

lingering looks of growing passion 
stolen moments so carelessly plucked from time 
we cast them to one side, take a breathe and walk away 
carry on, standing strong - we whisper that we're fine 

Yet, it is in the darkest hours I feel it the most 
it is then I realise how we are so alone 
turning in my sleep, I will this to end 
and secretly dread the day you return home 

My heart aches for the pain you must feel 
my only hope in this dying facade 
is that of a reason why we fell on this path 
to live, to learn, and remember love 
even if it can't be carried on this way 

I will remember the pages of this untold story 
the ache inside of what we so carelessly forgot 
although I wonder if the last page has been turned 
right now in time I will close this chapter 
leave it to fate, and bury my hurt
