---
title: "Harlots of Shame"
writer: "Jewel"
slug: "harlots-of-shame"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Embedded in the roots
embelished in their fertiliser
all their watered knowledge
drowning us in disrepute
fooling us in drunken piety
selling fermenting liquor to broken ways.

What was once independance
now becomes an extrinsic affection
entagled deep in their soil
blinded by a dirty falsehood
today they still say their true...

The foundations became strong
though disjuction appeared
quickly it was filled with mossy growth
occasionly leaving a shade of a doubt
slighted uncovered, a glimpse of light
I remember it called in the darkest of nights.

We could shed the deep surface
we could break through
the time had come and dreams had floated
souls in disdain could now feel
we were summoned to the edges of the ocean.

Climbing the mountain we arrived at an orange sky
screamed at their entraped plantation
unveiled how we're no longer afraid of freedom.
Courage had burrowed up from underground,
declaring a true benevolence
we only had to make it through one final night.

The outside was no such mystery
white angels became a sweet manifestation
singing to us an allocution
they had had wings 
they could take us
we glady flew with their crowning ascent.
