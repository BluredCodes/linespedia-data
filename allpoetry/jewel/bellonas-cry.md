I cast my look unto you
It's those beautiful eyes I see,
they radiate no resemblance
of the heart previsioned to be

The music is now yours
reckoning no such bliss
for this is a final battle
one I cannot win

Distance traveled over time
now your destiny, you have fermented mine
I'm drunk from your words, an opium den
inside I regress, if only to forget.

Unsuppressed you keep ignoring 
the concurs of your soul
put it right back in your conscience
all the treason - you’re not feeling what was told

But I know...

stay asleep in your resting haven
I'll remain in the hay
I have clipped your wings 
yet you'll return, one last ronion way.

then tell me, when its over

It will beat you down 
eat at your flesh
you will think back
it is a fine mess you have left.I'm interested in your interpretation of this...
