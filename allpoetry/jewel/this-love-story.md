Feel the murder, feel the plesure, feel the pain
It's driving me insane,
I can't think for now I know it
I'm driving in a different lane.

Your love has spilled over me 
and now you say it's all to hard
for gods sake lover, take my drug
you know this is just a part 
a part 
a part
a soul breaking part
of our love story
shattering my heart

Take it slow, take it more, take it to the core
Its just not the same no more
I remember majic when we met
but I've seen it all before

We blossomed as a rose 
and when we were in full bloom
you take a rest, a sad excuse
and I'm afraid to say we're doomed
you know this is just a part 
a part 
a part
a soul breaking part
of a love story
shattering my heart.This is not my usual style at all, but it is actually a song I'm currently trying to put music too. Suprisingly it came out a little light-hearted when I wrote it, but to me it wasn't that way all. I guess that has been the medicine.
