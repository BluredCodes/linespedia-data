title: "Lost Soul"
writer: "Jewel"

A listless empty bodydrain my thickening bloodripe with the virtue by natureit carries me awaywading the waters of the unknownI untangle my self toward the abovestill to blind to seeall that's laid out before meso now...I feel my way in the darkeverything is molded into shapesI run my fingers through the contoursof a broken hearttorn from the soul I've always wantedsomewhere far away.
