---
title: "Prelude to Sanity"
writer: "Jewel"
slug: "prelude-to-sanity"

While the biggest pain encumbers my blood flow
she becomes an old woman of the sea
tethering with the ravenous waves
singlehandedly, floating to another world.

Disheartened in this passion-filled pain
I ran the race without your guidance
tripped upon the final hurdle home
and awoke, face down, deeper than dirt.

For we are children with no warning, 
no role to act, or footprints to follow
Carrying our shackles along a thirsty road
insults will not quicken us to the end.

'God befriended us as our cause is just'
oh Henry, who defines that cause ?
for our merciless wave rejected the shore
no-one here explains disparity.

Now, tired bodies search for a motive
just why did we kill our sprit?
the empty scotch and loaded ashtrays explain
unaware you'd feel- it crept away to its suicide
you've been inside your mind for years.
