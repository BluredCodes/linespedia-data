---
title: "What is this?"
writer: "starkissed"
slug: "what-is-this"

You may know,
You may feel the same,
I'm not about to let you go,
Just stay in my heart.

I always wonder what you're thinking,
Never knowing what to expect next,
Will we become more?
Or will we just stay friends?

I hope things can work out,
You don't know how much i care for you,
These words you cannot hear,
Everyday is another mystery.

Just because we arn't that close now,
Dosn't mean I can't like you,
Like you the way I do,
Caring about you in different ways.

Some may say this is just a crush,
Others may call it "puppy love,"
But I say that it is real,
The feelings just won't go away.
