---
title: "Hoping and Dreaming"
writer: "starkissed"
slug: "hoping-and-dreaming"
source: "AllPoetry"

I sit thereAs you walk byOnly inches awayUnsure what to sayWhat move to makeIf I should just watchOr if I should tryPlaying basketballIn the gymI watch as you playWith friends or with meI don't know what to saySo silence is the answerSo close yet so farSometimes it seemsYou feel the sameBut I can't be sureIt's just how I see youAs if you notice meBut it might just be meHoping and dreaming
