I believe that if you try with all your heart to get something you are only pushing  yourself back because you don't see anything except what you are working for.

I believe that those differences that set us apart bring us together and those that bring us together set us apart.

I believe that you shouldn't have to please a single person except yourself, for those people asking you to change dont care about you, they care about themselves.

I believe that true friends accept that you are human and make mistakes, love you for your imperfections, and are there for you even if you can't always be there for them.

I believe that trying to be the best at everything is different than trying to suceed at everything you do.
