Today I saw you
And asked about the game
Asked if you were going
I hoped you would
I wanted to see you there
It would make me so happy
You said you weren't
So I tried to talk you into it
Said we're 6-0
Against the defending state champs
Its the game of the year
For our football team
Yet I got no reply
Then there was an assembly
I watched you from across the gym
I hoped you would change your mind
On the bus I talked to a friend
She talked to your friend
Asked if you were going
He said he wasnt sure
Then she told him I liked you
I hope things turn out cool
Just to see you makes me happy
So hopefully you will go
Things will turn out alright
I'm sure... Maybe
