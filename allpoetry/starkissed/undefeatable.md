Today we became the first in historyThe history of the schoolTo go seven and ohThe team is the bestNever to be defeatedOnly the second playoff birth for usWe're so proudOf our teamThe team that is the best in stateForever this will be rememberedIn our schoolWith more freshman than ever tooThis is amazingTo be a part of thisJust to be a spectatorTo know the playersJust cant wait till stateOur football team is Undefeatable!I am writing this right after our football team went 7-0. At 6-0 we already had a playoff birth and we're ranked #1. Today We played the defending state champions and won! EXCITING!
