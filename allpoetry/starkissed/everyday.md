Everyday I walk by,
Wondering just one thing - why?
The way you look, the way you stare,
My friends are wondering when and where.
Not everyone may think this way,
you always give a head flip and a "hey?"
Hoping you think the same way I do,
Thinking about you everyday, praying about you too.
Everyone says I should try,
Walk by and not only say "Hi."
Wishing one day you might just say,
All those words other than "hey."
If only it woudl be soon,
Praying under the light of the moon.
Maybe I will just hang cool,
Or make the first move and possibly look like a fool
