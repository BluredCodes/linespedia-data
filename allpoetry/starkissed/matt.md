The other day we met
And just started talking
I felt something
That we might have this connection
I cannot explain
If only you can see
Just how happy I am
And how free I feel
When I talk to you
You would be able to see
That you've made a difference
Made a difference to me
You're someone I can talk to
About anything at all
You've been through some stuff
That I have felt too
I'm not sure how you
Take all this in
How you look at me
Or understand what I say
But I am happy you're there
Even when you don't know it
You mean a lot to me
Even thought I don't know you
All too well
I just wanted to say thanks
For giving me a chance
A chance to be your friend
