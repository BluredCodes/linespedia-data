title: "Trust"
writer: "starkissed"

It's an easy thing to give
When you first meet someone
But an easy thing to loose
Once it has been lost
It takes a while to regain
All the hurt you go through when it is lost
May still be there even after it is back
Nothing is gone forever
But also nothing is here forever
No one can tell when it will be lost
If it ever will be
But you know when it is gone
You know because of that uncomfortable feeling
That empty space in you
The time it hurts the most
Is when you feel you can't trust
One of your best friends
