All the frenzy
There isn't anything
Anything anyone can do
They can't see the hurt
Can't feel the pain
I go through everyday
They don't know what I think
Or remember what I do
They don't know how much it hurts
And does every time I see one
One of those people
Those people who hurt me so
They didn't even know me
They didn't want to try
All they did was say those words
I knew they weren't true
But they still hurt
Oh, how I remember those words
And hurt every time I remember them
I just wish it would go away
Never to see them again
Never to remember the pain and agony
To forget all the good times
If only to get rid of the bad too.
If only...
