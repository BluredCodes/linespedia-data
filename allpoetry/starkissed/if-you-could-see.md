---
title: "If You Could See"
writer: "starkissed"

Every person is special in itself
But there is this special person
They may not know it
But they have touched my life
They may not think so
But I do
Everything feels so heavy
But she seems to lift it all off
I wish I could tell her what I felt
But there is no way to express it
She doesn't know
Just how much she means to me
If you could get to know her 
You would feel the same way too
There is something inside her
That just touches me
Accepting everything about everyone
If only she could see
Just what she means to me
A Best Friend, Nicole.
