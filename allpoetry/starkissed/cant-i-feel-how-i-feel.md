I'm confused,angry,sad,mad,relieved,upset,and frustrated...Does that explain it?Not to mention that i want to bitch at my ex b/f,and kick the crap out his friend.What?Is there something wrong with that?Is there something wrong with me?Am I a freak?A loser?You make it sound like I can't fell like thatLike I can't be pissed off at someone who I thought was my friendLike I can just sit here while people lieLike I will just sit here and be harassedI'm sorryThat's not the way I workTo me I feel like I lost my prideI lost my sence of directionIs that what you wanted to hear?Is it?You asked how I amThen you asked me to explainI'm explainingI'm sorry you can't handle the fact that I have problems and you dontYou dont want to hear it?Well then why do you ask me?You don't care so why do you ask?
