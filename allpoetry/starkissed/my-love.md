---
title: "My Love"
writer: "starkissed"

Just to see you,It feels so good,But some things are hard to say,Tell you, I know I should.Holding you hand,Receiving the hugs you give,Your eyes warm me up,I love you for the way you live.There's not a single thing,That one can do or say,To change my feelings about you,To hold you day after day.You're the only one for me,No matter what they say,My kisses are for you only,Waiting to give you one day after day.I just hope you feel the same,Everyone tells me you do,Being with you makes me feel special,The only guy for me is you.
