title: "Is it True?"
writer: "starkissed"

From that first moment I saw you,
I hoped that you would like me,
Seeing what others never knew,
Wishing that it was something to be.

Someone said to me it was true,
But you were too shy to come clean,
Who would have ever knew,
Past my "mask" you would have seen.

As I lie here and think,
Words just flood into my head,
Always worrying if i blink,
The next moment i might be dead.

Without what could have been,
It would be something I would regret,
Just to have feelings isn't a sin,
I know you are one I will never forget.

Just to have one dream,
That will hopefully be heard,
Everyone has a "seam,"
Am i starting to sound absurd?

I will not forget this,
You I wish I would tell this to,
I just hope there is nothing I miss,
Because I didn't tell this to you.
