---
title: "Who cares?"
writer: "starkissed"
slug: "who-cares"
source: "AllPoetry"

No one seems to care at all,
They don't care if I fall,
Saying it'll be all right,
But it's not try as I might.

All I hear is an "oh,"
Just thinking no one could get this low,
I'm the only one down here,
No one here to lend an ear.

Everyone is too busy,
I'm spinning and getting dizzy,
There is no end in sight,
Sometimes I just want to see the light.

Scared to say anything to anyone,
Not even wanting to see the sun,
Wanting to get rid of this feeling,
Maybe I can fly through the ceiling.

Fly high above the clouds,
Not having to worry about the "crowds,"
Feeling not a single thing,
So carefree I could sing.

But then I come back to earth,
Having a feeling of no self-worth,
Things are just worse than before,
Comming in is more and more
