For me
There you were
To hold me
A true loving hug
Saying it will be ok
Protecting me
Just like in the movies
The way you care
You were there
There for me
Looking at you 
I see that love
Ever wondering
When we will see
Eye to eye again

You never really held me
You never gave me a true loving hug
You never were there to protect me
I always thought you cared
The way you were never there
Never there too look at
Or to see that love

You were all a dream
An image I could only hope for
If only you could come
And hold me
Just like in the movies
