I believe that there's a perfect someone out there for everyone and you don't choose who you fall in love with.

I believe that amazing things happen each and every day.

I believe that one must live like there is no tomorrow but learn as if they'd live forever.

I believe that you can't change the past, but yet plan and hope for the future.

I believe that no one can take 'that' away.
