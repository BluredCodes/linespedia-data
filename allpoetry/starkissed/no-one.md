No one knows how I feelThe empityness that I hold within my soulEveryone sees this happy girlA smart one tooOne with no worriesOne with no painsAlways wearing a smile.No one knows the real meNot the painOr the hurtThe regrets and fearsEverything being held inside.The mask I wear doesn't helpI can't get rid of itWhen it is goneEveryone gets mad at meI can't be free to be meAll the pain is inside.Piling upNot able to come outRegreting being meEverything I do just hurts more and moreCutting my life apartYet no one can know how I feel.They don't let me have any other emotions.
