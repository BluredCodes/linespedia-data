Today will be the day,I will find out the truth,The day to know,How you feel.I hope something good will come,something to make me smile,A hope may become reality,Just maybe it will be.If not, I may hurtAnd be unsure of myself,But I would still want to be,Friends with you.I know you don't know me well,But please give me a chance,To see the real me,Not what everyone else sees.Today will be the day,If only I may.I wrote this the other day. I still haven't talked to him. I know, I should.
