All the feelings
Have been lost
Not to know where they go
Just lost
Lost forever.

The person they are for is gone
They dont mean a thing
Only there to hurt you
Not to make you smile
Like they did before.

Hiding from everything
You think they are gone
Come out just to see them there
Standing right behind you
Waiting to pull you down again.

Holding on to what dignity you have left
Doesn't seem to help
Always managing to make you feel worse
Worse than the last time
Hurting like never before.There will be more added later. I'm just having a writers block at the moment.
