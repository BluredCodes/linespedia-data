That first day I saw youI said you are who?In reply the name was BradUnsure what to say or do.You said you were a freshmanThat confirmed what I knewYou just moved here from IdahoUnsure what to say or do.I thought that you seemed really coolAnd that I wanted to get to know youI played basketball in the gym at lunchUnsure what to say or do.One day Jeff said you messed upIn front of your girlfriend, WHO?!?!I hoped that he was talking about meUnsure what to say or do.Today I know less than I did beforeThe only person knowing is youPlease tell me if you feel for meUnsure what to say or do.*Note: Names have been changed to protect myself and the innocent* *wink* hehe
