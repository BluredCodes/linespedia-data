title: "Forever Friends"

There's not a single thing,That one can do or say,Not a single song to sing,You're my friend day after day.Some things will never change,And our friendship is one of them,Both of us having friends of a wide range,Yet neither of us really liking Eminem.Feeling each others happiness,Feeling each others pain,Caring about one another's joyfulness,Learning from one another is a gain.Friends forever,Friends for life,Losing you, never,Even when we each become a wife.Never to forget all those times,The ones we shared,Looking into one anothers minds,Never to forget how much we cared.BFF
