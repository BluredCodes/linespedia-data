I'm lostIn this thing called lifeHurting insideBut no one seems to careI can't just let it goIt's too hardNo one understandsNothing can be rightI wish things were the way they used to beSo easyNo hassleNothing to regretAnd nothing to changeYou cant tell me you feel the sameWanting to just cut apart everythingListening to the music, Pop,That once made me happyAnd now turning to harderHeaver music, AlternativeNow Linkin Park can set my soul at easeThe pain that runs through meHating life just to look at a friendAnd see how much I would miss herNothing seems to be the answerI can't do a thingI'm so lost
