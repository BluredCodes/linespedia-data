The day I was born was a tremendous day,
I found out I had a brother with whom to play,
Everything was different from what I had seen before,
I was just finding out what was in store.

Everyone was loving which is what I had wished for,
I never once thought that I would want more,
I have a good house and caring friends too,
I found out later of which they are who.

I thought nothing was missing from my "perfect" life,
Except for this envy which was stabbing like a knife,
I had always been accepted by my "click,"
But I always wanted to be in the popular pick.

I realized it wasn’t all it was cracked up to be,
I wanted to sit around and just be me,
All my friends were caring and thoughtful too,
They all knew I would come back to my crew.

I have been oh so very strong,
Even though sometimes my feelings were wrong,
Everyone has been very kind,
I'm happy I played things through my mind.

Without my friends, family, and God to guide me,
I wouldn’t have gotten past everything I see.
