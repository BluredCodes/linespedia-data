writer: "starkissed"

Tonight is the night,
Everything will be right.

Hope to forget about everything,
And move on with life.

I hope you will stay my friend,
Everyone makes mistakes.

You make everything my fault,
Even though it is not.

I hope you will actually see,
That the the only one making mistakes isn't me.

It hurts so much to try and fix,
All those "flaws" about me.

Those things make me,
They are me.

I just wish that you would realize,
That I just want to be me.

Sorry I can't take you "advise,"
And talk to the guy I like.

It's harder for me than you think,
But now it wont be.

Soon, he will know,
His friend will tell him.

So things will be easier,
And you can't tell me to stop complaining.

I hope you reaize that I dont mean to,
I just need to figure things out.

Please don't hate me for being me,
Just try and forgive me,
For all those things I do wrong.
