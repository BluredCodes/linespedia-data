I believe that bad times are just as good as the good, for those make us stronger.

I believe that those 'friends' who hurt you everyday are not friends at all, they're people who use hurting you to make themselves feel better.

I believe that everyone is created equal- yet not always looked at that way by others.

I believe that giving up sometimes results in succeeding.

I believe that if you care so much about a person that you spend all your time with them you are just setting yourself up for dissappointment.

I believe that you're never too big for teddy bears or blankets, bedtime stories or snacks, trick-or-treating or santa.

I believe that a simple smile can brighten someones day.

I believe that if you don't step outside just to go out you miss all of natures wonders.

I believe that without risks you cannot succeed.
