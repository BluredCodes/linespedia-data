title: "How Do I Know Who I Can Trust?"

You used to say you loved me
Loved me forever and always
You used to want to hold me
For all the time we lost
You used to say I was beautiful
Until that day you said
There is no chemistry between us
Why does this happen to be
The day after you tried to see
Just how far I would go
Was it all a plan?
Were you testing me?
I know I learned one thing
I don't know what I can trust
Was everything a lie?
Deceit?
I don't know what to say except

NEVER AGAIN!~!*!~!*!~!*!~!*!~!*!~!*!
