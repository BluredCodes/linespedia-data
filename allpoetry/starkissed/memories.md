Sometimes life ends sooner than we expect.
Show love to everyone you know, you respect.

Brandon, you were loved by many,
Even those who didn't know you well,
Not being hated by any,
We know for sure you aren't in hell.

The smile you wore was bright,
Shining on each and all,
Always wanting things to be right,
You always seemed to be so tall.

Helping people in need,
Excelling at your favorite sports,
You were the type to lead
Lending a hand in ways of all sorts.

Your time came far too soon,
Catching us all off guard,
Now you are in heaven with the moon,
This struck us very hard.

We all miss you but your not far away,
Your passion, courage, and love,
In our memories you will stay,
You were like a white dove,
			Full of love
