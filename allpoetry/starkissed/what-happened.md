writer: "starkissed"

Best friends
That's what we used to be
Then we decided to become more
It didn't work out
All either of us felt
Ended up being just lust
The trust has been lost
A great friendship is different
Just wishing this never happened
I try to tell myself it wasn't all my fault
But somehow I still feel like it is
My heart isn't broken
Yet my soul feels ashamed
Never again
That's what I say
Never again will I like a friend
Never again will I want to "date."
