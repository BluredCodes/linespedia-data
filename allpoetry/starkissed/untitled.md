Looking back on the daysI thought I was so happyJust because I was with youPutting up with everythingYour friends and bro's saidTo me and about meIt hurt so muchI never let you see itYou heard it allEvery time they talkedIt was about meAbout how I was a 'guy'How much it hurt meYou still do not knowThen you said you loved meI believed youThat summer you rarely calledWe didn't do muchAnd I went through so muchSo many hard timesThat you were never there forNever there to hold meAnd tell me it would be okThe worst day of allI decided enough was enoughAnd decided this was the beginningThe beginning of the endThe end of 'us' I found someone elseWho I thought loved meBut I was wrongJust friends we stoodThinking it was moreNow I feel that I am meI have been set freeNo one to tear me downUntil I saw him againThe one who hurt me mostOne of your friendsIt's hard to leaveBut try as I might I feel that I have grown strongerIt still hurtsJust not as much as beforeSlowley moving on
