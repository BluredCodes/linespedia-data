Friends are an endless gift,Don't loose that over small things,So help give their spirits a lift,And when you go to heaven you'll get your wings,Don't loose that over small things,Always be kind and forgiving,And when you go to heaven you'll get your wings,Or else your friendship might be fading,Always be kind and forgiving,Be sure to give unconditional love,Or else your friendship might be fading,They're a gift from above,Be sure to give unconditional love,So help give their spirits a lift,They're a gift from above,Friends are an endless gift.This poem is in the Pantoums format.
