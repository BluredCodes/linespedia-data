---
title: "Played?!?"
writer: "starkissed"
slug: "played"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2368-Played----by-starkissed"
---

You said you liked me
I thought I liked you
Yet I feel played
I trusted you
Now it seems like you just wanted
To see how far I would go
If that is the case
You didn't get to far
I didn't think this would happen
I thought you were better
Trusting a friend
But now I feel played
I don't even know
If I can trust
Trust all the things you said before
I don't know what to think is true
And what to think isn't
I don't know who is one of my real friends anymore.
