Flames surround me 
Eating away at my soul
Dissolving, devouring, and consuming.

Water drowns me,
Getting in my lungs like air once did.
Extinguishing, blotting out, and darkening. 

Air is pushed in my lungs,
Where the water just was.
Propelled, motivated, and launched.

The light is brightly seeping into my eyes, 
As my eyelids are lifted.
Oozing, trickling, leaking.what is thought bout this one...?
