title: "You Are My Angel"

You are my angel 
You are my hope
You are my soul

You are my self-reasoning
You are my self-confidence 
You are my self-control

You think with my mind
You see through my eyes
You hear what I hear

Who are you? Wouldn't you like to know...?

Well





You are me...
