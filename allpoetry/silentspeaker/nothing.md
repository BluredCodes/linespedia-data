It didn't mean anything,
Nothing at all.
There was no feeling in it,
Just a passing thing.
You didn't want to do it,
It just kind of happened. 
But, if I could,
I would relive it every second of everyday. 

You didn't mean for this to happen,
But it did.
I didn't mean to get this way,
But I did.

That kiss meant nothing,
Nothing at all.
And that is killing me...It didn't mean anything....
and truly
that is what is killing me.
