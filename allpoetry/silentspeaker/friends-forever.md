title: "Friends Forever..."

I would like to say we will be friends forever,
I know it's not true.
I wish I could say I would always be there for you,
I don't know that.
I wish I could tell you I will always be you friend,
I know I can't. 
I wish I could make everything all better,
I know that can't happen.
I wish I could say you are my friend now,
I don't know if I can....
Only you know...for you...
