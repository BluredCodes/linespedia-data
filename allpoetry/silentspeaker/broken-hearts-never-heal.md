title: "Broken hearts never heal"
writer: "silentspeaker"

I hear blood dripping on the floor
But I can not see where it is coming from
I see a growing spot appearing on my shirt
Remarkably the spot is in the shape of a heart
No I have not been shot
There is not bullet in my chest
There is just a broken heart
A heart not worth saving
One that just longs to be loved
But is tired of being thrown to the curb
A heart that just sits there 
Waiting to be picked up
Put back together again
And stepped on
Crushed and broken
My broken heart that will never heal

My broken heart 
Broken, Broken heartyea jess you know
