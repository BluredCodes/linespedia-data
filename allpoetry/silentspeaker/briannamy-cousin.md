Family in the beginning
Family till the end
When all I really wish to be is your friend

You're my cousin
And I do love you
You're more then just my cousin, you're my sister too


I love you dearly
I just wanted you to know.
I hope it will always show.


I love you Brianna
Merry Christmas


Love always
Child of the corn
