I will always remember:

I will always remember where i was
I will always remember what i was doing
I will always remember what i was thinking 

I will  remember all who died
I will  remember who was left behind 
I will  remember all who were injured

I will remember who did this
I will remember what we did to retaliate 
What I won't ever know...
Is why all this happened

Still I will always rememberif you didn't get it this is about September 11Th
