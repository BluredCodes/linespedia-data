Sleeplessness

Sleepless during the day
Tired-less during the night
Wishing only for my life to be free

Unable to move
Trying to keep my eyelids 
From falling to the ground 

My mind slowly slipping 
As I fall into a deep dark dream
A dream of running into your arms

Running away from a demon 
Running into your outstretched arms
The arms that once cared for me

The arms that used to caress 
My wounded heart.
The heart you broke

Now you see why I am afraid of sleep
Now I hope you understand, the hate for sleep
Now I can truly rest.

I am still sleepless
I am still tired-less
I am even more hopeless
