Nothing to call my own

Apparently i have nothing

No mind

No thought process 

Nothing to say that i have ever had an original thought ever in my life

My 'friends' set me straight on that one....

They let me know that i am just a slave of someone

Im just someone's slave

No mind,
No body,
No thoughts,
Are actually mine....
