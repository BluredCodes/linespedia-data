what does that word mean really...
does it mean criminally insane...
or does it mean that you are crazy cuz your different...?

what does it really mean...?
is it a way of getting away with something...
or is it just a saying that you can say to someone you dont like?

is it mean or not...?
would you take it personally if i called you crazy...
or would you just shrug it off as a friendly name...?

is crazy just a word 
or is it something that can really hurt someone...?

please think before you call someone a name that could hurt them
it does not make them feel good about them selves...tell me what you think and be honest
its kinda late and im bored 
so be brutial
