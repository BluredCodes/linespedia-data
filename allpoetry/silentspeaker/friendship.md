Today I noticed something.
You can tell who is truly your friend 
By whom you are most comfortable around.

I was talking to some of my friends last night,
And even though I did not know what they were talking about,
Yet still neither of them made me feel like I was stupid or anything.
To tell you the truth they even tried to tell me what it meant.
 

No matter what sex, religion, or race 
I don't care
As long as you make me feel comfortable 
As long as you are always true to yourself



~~to all those who are my friends...
I just want to say thank you for being there for me 
And helping me through the tough times~~~
