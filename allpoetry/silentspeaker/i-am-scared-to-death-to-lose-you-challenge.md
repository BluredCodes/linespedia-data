Challenge Description: write me about being afraid of losing someone or something. it can be a material possession, a person, or even a feeling. good luck! 



I am scared to death to lose you.
I don't want to lose your friendship, care, or your smiles.
I would rather be your best friend, 
Then to be anything else,
And inevitably lose your friendship. 
I don't think I could bare to not have you to talk to. 
I would not be able to live with out you. 
You are my best friend.
And I want to thank you for that.
And if I make you un comfortable,
Just tell me.
Tell me, if you don't want to go.
I swear, it will be ok.

Because, I don't want to lose you.
I would rather give up what I want.
Then give up what I have.
