That bastard
That Fucking bastard
What did we do to deserve this?
What have we done to him?
Why must he do this to us? 
Why must he make us feel so terrible?
Why isn't he there for us in his time of need?
Why can't he make us all believe?
Why can't he stop us from killing each other?
Why can't he help us be better people?

I have no doubt that there is someone up there
But I do have a doubt that he created us all
I do think that he has some say in what we say
But I also believe that he can not show us our way

We make our own destiny
We know what is best for us

But really, if we had all the answers in life
Would we really want to believe in him?god...does he really exist...?
tell me what you think ok...?
