I need you to be there for me
To say you care 
To just be there
You don't have to talk 
You don't have to think
All you have to do is just be there
I need someone to have fun with
I need someone to be with
I need someone so I don't go insane 
I want you to be there 
I want YOU 
You make me happy 
You make me smile
When I'm with you I have funi need you all
