Do you realize that this will never happen again…? 
the 20th day,
of the 02nd month,
in the year 2002
at the hour of 20
and the minute of 02.

Never happen ever again…
Never…

So cherish the moment you have… 
Spend it with someone you love…
Just like we should do, 
Every minute,
Of every hour.
Of every day.
Of every week
Of every month.
Of every year.
 

Spend it with those you love...I only wish i could spend it with all of you...
