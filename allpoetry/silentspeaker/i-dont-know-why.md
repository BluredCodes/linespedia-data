---
title: "I Don't Know Why"
writer: "silentspeaker"

I don't know why I feel this way
But I feel as if you don't care

You don't care what I feel
You don't care what I think

So why does it matter to me so much?
Maybe its because you are one of my best friends and I care about you

It seems to me that all you do is sit around and listen to me complain
Then you go and write a poem about how much you hate me for doing so 

Well I do care what you think of me
And I do think what you say has an impact on my life

But you don't seem to notice  
You don't seem to care 

It bothers the shit out of me 
And you still seem to not care
