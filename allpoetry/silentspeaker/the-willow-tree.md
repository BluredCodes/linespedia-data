The willow tree sits there 
All alone 
No other trees there to keep him company
No bushes to talk to 
No grass to lean on 

The only visitor he gets is the girl 
The girl who walks out 
Every day
To sit under his branches
And talk endlessly
Endlessly about all problems
Problems of her own
And problems of others

She stays for a while
And then leaves
But not before giving the willow a hug
And thanking him for listening
And for being the best friend she could ever havehmmm...
