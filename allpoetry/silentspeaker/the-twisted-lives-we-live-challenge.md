The twisted lives we live 
The friendships we have 
The friendships we hope we have not lost 
The love we have for each other 
The love that is lost when I find out you love her 
The pain that is gained when you tell me you could never love me 
The hurt in side when I hear those words 
The shame when I finally tell you and you say nothing in return 
The pride I try to keep while I see the love in your eyes for her 
The happiness I feel for you two...when I realize I have no chance... 
The hope I have for you to be happy 
The moment I realize I like you as a friend and nothing more 
The exact second that I realize I have no more feelings for you 
The fun we can now have together
