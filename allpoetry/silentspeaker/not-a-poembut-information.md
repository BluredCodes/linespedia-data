title: "not a poem...but information..."
writer: "silentspeaker"

I try talking to people about my problems, or thoughts, but every time I do, I feel as though I am annoying the person or that they are sitting there laughing at me. So generally, I keep most important things to myself, whenever something is truly bothering me, I put up a mask try not to let the rest of the world know. Because then they would ask me what's wrong. And I will end up telling them the whole story, and getting those same old feelings of annoyance again.my friends yell at me, and get mad at me for not telling them more. But they have to understand that I'm not the type to just go around and tell everyone everything. If I trust the person, I'll talk to them. But no matter how great the friend, I always worried what they are thinking. Witch is why sometimes I prefer confiding in people I will never meet. 

So for my friends...if you ever wonder why you don't know as much as my other 'friends' it is because I have to see you every day... and I will never have to meet this person.thanx for reading...
