"We are what we have now. And what we have now is each other"

~ Crossroads ~


We have the whole future in front of us,
So why do we waste our time doing the stupid things we do?

Why do we waste our time being stupid teenagers, and doing stupid teenage stuff?
Why do we spend our precious time complaining and whining about stuff that in 10 years wont matter?
 Because we are just stupid teenagers.
That is all we are.
We think we know all there is to know.
But we don't.
We don't know what it is like to loose love.
We don't know what it is like to love.
We don't know any thing about the 'real world'.

We are only teenagers, but what we do know, we keep close to heart.
We keep each other as close as possible. 
Yet we still don't realize that we have more to learn.
No matter what the age.
We all have something to learn.

So as long as we stay together,
We will constantly be learning.
More about each other,
And about ourselves.
