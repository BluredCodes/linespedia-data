You make me feel lower then I am,
You want me to be inferior.
Well guess what...
I won't stand for it any more!
You may do what you want,
Yell, scream, or beat your head against the wall...
You can't make me bow down to you.
I refuse to do what you say...
No I won't pick up your backpack...
No I won't go and buy your food.
You are capable of doing it yourself.
Your legs aren't broken, get off your butt and use those legs.
 I'm NOT stupid!!! 
So just fucking stop it!!!
I don't want to hear it. 
One minute your like...o yes "I love you"
The next your I hate you... your fat and ugly...
Well I don't care what you think of me...
You are of no importance to me....
What you think has no effect on me...
