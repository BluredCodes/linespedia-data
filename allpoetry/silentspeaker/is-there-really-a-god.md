Is there really a God?
If there is why does he let all this shit happen to us all?
Why does he let human kill human....?
Why does he let people strap themselves to bombs and walk into crowed areas?
Why doesn't he stop these terrible things from happening?
Why won't he stop husbands from beating wives,
Or Parents from beating children?
If there is a God, why won't he help, instead of hurt us...?
If there is a God, why doesn't he just stop everything from happening...?
If God really does exist why can't he make all people happy...?
If he really is up there...why won't he come down and prove himself to the non believers..?
If he really wants us to follow in his footsteps then why won't he show us the way...?
Why must we all search and search and never find....
If he really wants us up there with him he would help us get there....
