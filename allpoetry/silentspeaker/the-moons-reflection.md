---
title: "The Moon's Reflection"
writer: "silentspeaker"
slug: "the-moons-reflection"

As deep as the ocean his eyes look at me from behind his thick glasses
His glare jutting into my shallow soul.
Watching his every move, watching him glide down the embankment, on his motionless feet.
Watching his face as he saw himself in the reflection off the pond

As a child on a swing set in the back yard
As an adolescent talking to the girl out on the street.
As a teen-ager taking that same girl out on a date.
As a twenty year old college student studying his ass off to make the grade
As a twenty-one year old having his first legal drink
As a twenty-eight year old watching his first born son's first smile 
As a thirty-five middle-aged man playing catch on the street with his son
As a forty year old taking his son to his first school dance
As a forty-six year old watching his son walk across the stage and receive his high school diploma 

Watching him I slowly come to realize that his was the face of true triumph and happiness 
The face of an old man looking back at him in the glare of the moon's glow is all I see
