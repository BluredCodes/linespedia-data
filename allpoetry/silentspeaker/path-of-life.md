The path of life will lead us in our own directions.
We will eventually leave this place we know as home. 
We will all go and do things that we would not have done if our friends were there.
We all will have to decide witch road to take...
Some won't make a good decision
But sooner than later,
We will all meet back up in life.
Weather it be at a high school reunion,
A wedding,
A funeral             (heaven forbid),
Or a party.
We will get back together,
And then you will know if you took the right path.
Then you will know if giving up your friends for a job in California was all worth it.
Or if moving to Germany to further reach your studies, was worth leaving the love of your life.
Then you will know that it is not just the mind that makes our decisions in life.
But also our hearts.

Then we will know that in order to be happy in life, it is not how we look, act, or dress that makes us who we are.
