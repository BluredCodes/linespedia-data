I'M NOT LIKE HER
I DON'T ACT LIKE HER
I DON'T THINK LIKE HER
I HATE IT WHEN YOU ALL SAY THAT

IT IS THEN I REALIZE WHAT YOU REALLY THINK OF ME
IT'S THEN THAT I THINK THAT I HAVE NO FRIENDS
THEN THAT I THINK ABOUT THE DREADED KNIFE

THE PAIN CAUSING PAIN KILLER
THE BRAIN NUMBING DRUG 
ADDICTING PAIN

I KNOW THAT YOU WOULD NOT DO ANY OF THIS IF YOU DIDN'T CARE
BUT I ALSO KNOW THAT YOU DO IT TOO
SO WHY SHOULD I STOP?

IF YOU CAN KEEP ON CAUSING PAIN AND *JOY*
WHY CAN'T I?



~ If it weren't for my friends I would have done it again...I would have picked up that knife and slashed my arm, or leg. Thank you all of my friends...And to those of you who take the time to care~
