title: "I Hate Myself"

I hate myself for showing my emotions publicly. The only thing i want to show you is my 'happy' side.
I don't want you to know how sad and depressed I am all the time.
     I hate myself for not being able to help you more... If I were only a better person I could be a better friend. Then I would be able to help you more.
     I hate myself for not being more supportive of you and your emotions. I f I had not acted like I hated how you felt about him maybe you two would still be friends, or maybe even dating still.
     I hate myself for not including you more. If I had maybe we could be better friends.  If only I would include you more you might be better friends with other people.



I'm sorry I'm not good enough....I'm really sorry megsy
