Christmas time this year 
Shall bring up a tear
As I hang on the fear

As I think about the past 
And I see it go by fast
I stair in aghast

As I see all I have done to hurt you
And all that has been done to me too
The pain that is making me blue.

The last time we were all here
You yelled at me out of sheer
Hate you told me with a tear

So I don't want to make you mad 
But when I'm with you I get sad
Don't tell me about the love we once had

It is gone now, and that love
It was pushed out with a mighty shove
I can only hope the feelings come from above

The love once shared 
Is now being tarred 
From my loving heart that once caredmearry christmas
