Wipe your eyes clear
No mare reason to cry my dear.
He loves you still.
Always has, forever will.
You will hug him very soon.
Underneath the glowing moon

The very moon I stand below,
With it's white and errey glow.
Hug him tight hold him long,
Listen to his lovely song.
His song of love, written just for you.
You shall remember it during all you do.

Then you will leave, with one last kiss,
Remember the face, you will always miss.
Think of him all the way home,
While you remember, don't let your mind roam.
For when you get home, what shall you find....?
But two little boys waiting to drive you out of your mind!!!remember...we LOVE YOU!!!
and only want you to be happy...!!!!!!
