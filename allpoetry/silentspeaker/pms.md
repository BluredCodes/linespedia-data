All guys think they have it bad when they get kicked in the balls 
We should make them into women 
And see what they say when they get their periods
Its like being kicked in the balls all day for 5 days straight  

Wait to see what they say
Wait and see
Just wait and seeha 
i hope all the guys in the world who ever made a girl in the world feel bad when they were having 'girly problems' dies a horrible terrible painful death

love 
vicky
