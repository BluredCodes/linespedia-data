If i was only skinny
I could respect myself
I could like myself
I could even learn to love myself
But no
I'm not skinny 
I'm me
And that's all I'll ever be
Is me

If I were only pretty 
I could learn to look in a mirror and not flinch
I could see myself for what i really am
I could be a better person
But no
I'm not pretty
I'm me
And that's all I'll ever be
Is me

If only i were nicer
I would have more friends
I would see everyone as better people
I would be a better person myself
But no
I'm not nicer
I'm me 
And that's all I'll ever be
Is me 

If I were a better person 
I would be a smarter person
If I were a better looking person
I would be just all around better
I can only wish that all these things come truemy feelings
