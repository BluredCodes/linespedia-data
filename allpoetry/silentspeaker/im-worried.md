iI'm worried for you
I'm worried for me
I'm worried because you are my friend
I care for you

I want you to be happy
I want everyone to be happy
I want you to be healthy

If I weren't your friend
I would not be bugging you this much about something you don't want to talk about 
If I didn't care 
It would not matter to me what you do
But since I do
It does matter
And since I am your friend 
I do bug you

Thanks for understanding

Love always, forever, and no matter what,
Vicksyeaness
