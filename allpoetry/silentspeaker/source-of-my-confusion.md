Source of my confusion

You are the source
You are the reason
You are the purpose of most of my confusion.

You are also the purpose of my joy
You are the only one who makes me smile,
All the time

Thank you for the perpetual smiles. 
Thank you for the wonderful laughs.
Thank you for the joyous fun.
