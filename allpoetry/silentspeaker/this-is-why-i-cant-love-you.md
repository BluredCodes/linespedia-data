Tears are cascading down my face
As I am told the reason you can not love me.
I need to be less like her,
Like the one you hate.
You say I am just like her.
That kills me inside, 
To know what you really think of me.
You say how much you hate her all the time,
Only now do I start to believe you.
Only now do I think you really mean what you say.
Those terrible words eat at my soul 
"That is why I can't love you".
I now believe what my brain tells me
You will never love me,
You will never think of me the way you do others.
I will never be good enough for you...will I?

I truly do want to change...
It's not that I want you to love me
It's more that I want you to think of me as an equal.
If that is all I get out of this,
Fine...all I want is a friend.
I want you to be my friend,
Or at least think of me as someone you would like to get to know...
