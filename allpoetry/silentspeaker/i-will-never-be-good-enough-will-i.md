I will never be good enough will I...?
 


There will always be someone better than me....

I'll never be smart enough
Nor pretty enough.
Nor skinny enough.

I'll never be good enough...
There will always be someone better than me...

No matter how hard I try...
I'll never be what you want...
I'll never be good enough.

I'll never be strong enough.
I'll never be fast enough.
I'll never be brave enough.

Will I ever be what you want...?
Will you wake up one morning...and see that I've been here for you the whole time...?
Will you ever realize that you really like me...and only me...?

Or is this all I get...?
Is this all there is?
Is this all I will get?

Will I forever just be your best friend...?
Or one day will you realize that you want me...?

But will that day come to late........when will you wake up and realize that im here...?
