you are great
you help me through life
by just being there
you don't even have to say any thing 
by just being there you help

you mean every thing to me
in a friendly way
you help me when i need it
and when i need support 
you are there

you are the greatist
you help not only me but everyone
you are the perfect "normal" person out there

thank youfor all my friends 
(and no jess minogue is not a friend)
