Why doesn't anyone want me?
Why doesn't anyone care?
Why am I so different than other people?
Why am I on the outside looking in at my life?

All my life I have felt this way...
It seems the harder I try
The lonelier I get
It's hard to mean something to me if I don't mean something to anybody else.



Why do I notice that I feel this way, 
While others seem not to care?
