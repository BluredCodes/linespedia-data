---
title: "I Will Never Cry For You"
writer: "silentspeaker"

That was the last time i will ever cry for you
I will never cry over or about you ever again
No matter what you do
No matter how much you hurt me 
Never again will it happen

My tears are to precious for you
I could use them for something more useful

Never again will i ever cry in public
Especially since i have better things to cry for 
It doesnt matter what happens to me
It just matters how i take it
I know that i am too sensitive 
But that is just how i am
I take everything to literally
You know that
But yet you still do and say stuff 
And you know it will hurt me, but you still do it

My tears are  to precious for you
My tears will be used for someone else
They will not go to waste

I have other people to cry for
There are others more deserving
There are many more then you
They deserve them, they need to know that they are cared for
They need to know that someone will cry for them
They need to know that i care 
They need to know that my tears will not go to waste on and undeserving shit

My tears are to precious for you
What ever you do...please don't cry for me
