Challenge Description: Write a poem about a criticism you have about society or something you question society for. What is wrong with society today? What does society put too much emphasis on? How can society be hypocritical? (*Just some things to think about*) (25 points)



B eing who I am, is all I care about. 

E ating is a thing i only do when my friends make me. 

I eat when my friends are around. 

N utrition is a thing of the past. 

G etting skinny is all I want to do. 


S kin and bones is all I wish to be. 

K ind of like that girl I see in those magazines. 

I wish to be just like her, so I can wear what ever I want. 

N othing not going to eat. And when I do. It will come back up. 

N othing fattening. Nothing that could add one pound to my already fat body. 

Y hy you ask? Well because, I see all those skinny girlies around, who complain about how fat they are. 


Well if they are fat.... Then what the heck am I...?yesh.....
