The Man In The Moon.

The man in the moon, is my true friend
I tell him everything.
He knows all the joys, all the pains, and all the madness of my life.

He is always there.
He never gets bored.
He always listens.
He never talks back to me.

He is my true friend.
He never makes me feel like less than I am.
His smiling face is always there.
He is always glowing.

I only wish that he could be here with me.
And share his problems with me.
I wish I could help him with his problems.

I guess, he wants me to help others.
Just the way he helps me.
