title: "The World Is Innocent"

The world is innocent.
If just for today, 
Then so be it. 
If just for this hour,
Fine.
If just for this minute,
Ok.
If just for this second,
I'll take what I can get.

The world looks innocent.
At least until the snow melts,
And leaves everything all brown and dull.
The snow gives the world special quality, 
It is so untainted, so spotless, so pure.
If not in actions, then at least in illusion.
