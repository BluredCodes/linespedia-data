No matter how much fun I had
I can't help myself but to be jealous of you 

I see how much fun you two can have together 
And I start to think of how much I wish to be like you

How I wish that there was someone in the world for me
Someone who I could be happy with

If just for today, fine
If we could be happy for all of eternity it would be better

Just to be happy one day
I would do anything to be happy

I guess I shall take it one day at a time,
Looking for happiness
