title: "I love you PaPa"

To see you sitting there in a chair all alone 
Looking out the window at nothing in particular
It kills me inside 

It kills me to watch you sit there with no one to talk to 
With no one to be there with you
I just wish that we didn't have to put you in the nursing home

When I go to see you, I'm always so depressed afterwards
Then I start thinking about how you must feel.
I just wish that I would have had something to say in your moving

To know how people treat you and knowing how they just take advantage of you
To know that you will never be able to leave that horrid place,
I just wish that I could make it all better

I wish that I could get you out of there. I wish that I could just make you happy. I wish that I could go back in time and make it so that you didn't have to go in that terrible place.
I'm really really sorry.

I love you papa
