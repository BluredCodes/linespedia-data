---
title: "Bull Shit!!!"
writer: "silentspeaker"
slug: "bull-shit"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51076-Bull-----by-silentspeaker"
---

You used to be my best friend...
But I don't know what has happened
You are no longer the girl that I spent so much time with...
You are now a girl, who stays at home doing nothing,
Even when she is invited to go places with people.
You listen to depressing music,
Trying to get depressed.
Even though you have no reason to be.
Your family life is fine.
You were fine,
Until you realized that your sister was getting all of the attention.
So you started doing what she is.
And now you claim that you have been doing it for a year and a half.
Well I'm sorry for being so blunt.
But 
BULL SHIT!!!!!

What happened to my best friend...? 
Where did she go?
I don't know, 
And truly,
Right now I don't care!!!you called me his slave...for not doing anything but trying to get him to stop making you mad, well see what you get next time...SEE WHAT YOU GET!!!!!!
