---
title: "I Care"
writer: "silentspeaker"

Ok I don't really care what most people think about me
I care what my friends think and what I think
It normally doesn't matter to me if people think I'm a dork
Or if they think I'm really cool
I'm not
I'm none of those
I'm me
And that's it I'm nothing more

That is what I said earlier but I was just kidding myself
I was just lying to the world 
I was lying when I told myself that what others thought didn't matter in life
I was lying when I said that what they think about me does not hurt
And even more I was lying when I said I don't care if people like me or not

I do care...in fact I think I care too much
