New years eve

This is my worst New Year's eve ever
I will always remember this New Year's eve as the one where I had no friends
I will never forget the fun I used to have with you
When I could still call you a friend 
When I was still sure that I would be called a friend in return
When I was sure that we could have fun just sitting around doing nothing but talking 
When we could just be together and feel comfortable

I wish I could bring all of that back and feel like I was needed again
But I know for the life of me that is not possible

New years eve will be impossible to get through
