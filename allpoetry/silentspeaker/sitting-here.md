As I sit here staring into my computer screen
I start to think about all the 'wonderful' things I could be doing with my life
Like helping people, making use of my time, and just being a good friend
But no instead I just sit here looking in on a world of nothingness
Where nothing I see is real
Everything has been made up to make me think I am living in a happy world
Everything has been made up in my mind
The mind that has no imagination
The mind that is being wasted just sitting here
Staring
Waiting 
For something good to happen
Something that will change my life
For the better
But no, I have to force my mind to see 
That nothing good ever came out of sitting in front of a computer 
'Writing' stuff that you hope people will like
'Writing' stuff that you wish will be accepted
And waiting for something to happenhmmmm....
