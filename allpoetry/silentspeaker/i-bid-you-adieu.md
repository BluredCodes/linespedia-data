thanks for your help vicksI bid you adieu: I sit here looking at the tools of MY trade, a razor blade and a band-aide, to cover the wound. Long-sleeve shirts are the look for me; they cover the multiple lacerations on my arm. The ones that I did not want you to see,the ones that I have been hiding from you for years. But you had to go and look. You just wanted to piss me off, didn't you? Well guess what, you got your wish, I'm leaving this cruel world that you love so much. I will never be back to see you I will stay away forever don't worry I'm going to a wonderful place in the sky to sit on the clouds all day long and look down on you I hope your life is all that you want it to be. Again I bid you adieu.jess scotty i finally found the word it is Adieu
