im going to steal you
from this terrible place
im  going to take you to a far off land
where the trees grow tall and straight
and there are flowers all year long
where the sky is blue everyday
and the clouds always make a shape

the animals are always friendly here
the people love each other
and they are always nice to you
they make you feel welcome
and loved
where everyone has a purpose in life
and people always make a difference

im sorry to say but a place like this does not exisit
it is only a fragmint of my imagination
if it did exisit in human world i would take you
and i hope you would take methere is no way that this place will ever be real 
but i only wish it could be
