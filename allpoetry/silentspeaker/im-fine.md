Is it that obvious?
Can you really tell?

I'm told you can, 
By friends...
But I don't think YOU could tell 

I might be having some problems
But don't worry for me.
I'm fine

Although I can't help all of you with your problems 
I can help myself,
I have been helping myself forever.

I'm fine...if you know what this is about...
dont worry for me
im fine
