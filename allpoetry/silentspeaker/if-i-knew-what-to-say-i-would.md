If I knew how to say this I would
If I knew what your reaction would be I would do it
I would tell you
I would tell you anything
You are my best friend
But for some reason beyond my control
I can not tell you what is killing me

I can not tell you how I really feel
For you would not feel it back
I can not tell you what I wish to say
Because you would just shoot my emotions down
I can not tell you that I wish to spend time with you, and you alone
For then it would seem too friendly
And no we can't do that 
Because there are others to think about

I can not think about me and only me
I must think of you 
And of 'her'
For she is my best friend also 
And no matter what happens here
I don't want to harm any relationship I have...
