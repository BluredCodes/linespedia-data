"Your tears won't fall for ever"
Let me catch them before they hit the ground and shatter.
Let me be the one to make you smile.
Let me be near you when you need me most.


"Your tears won't fall for ever" 
I won't let you sit all by yourself in a corner crying
I won't let you sit at home on a Saturday night all alone
I won't make you cry (at least intentionally)
 

"The sky won't fall for ever" 
Your world won't come crashing down on you all the time
Your fragile blue sky will always be shining down on your face
Your grass will always be green and perfect 


"The sky won't fall forever"
Your day will never get to the point of you going and crying for no reason
Your night will never get to scary, to the point of nightmares
Your future is light and happy~~~Megan~~~

If I could stop your tears from flowing...I would
If I could make your day all better...I would
If I could make you happy...I would

Love always and NO MATTER WHAT,
Vicky (Vicks)
