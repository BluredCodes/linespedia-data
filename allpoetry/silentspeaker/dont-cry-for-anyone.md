---
title: "Don't Cry For Anyone..."
writer: "silentspeaker"
slug: "dont-cry-for-anyone"

Dry your eyes
Don't let them hurt you
With all their evil and lies.

Your tears are worth much more
Than anyone could ever wish for.

Don't cry for someone 
Until you know they are 'the' one.

Let them say what they will.
For no matter what they say,
We will LOVE you still.for no one in particular...
