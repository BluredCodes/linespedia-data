If you were still here 
Would I be more normal 
Would I be happier 
Would I have more fun 

The answer is yes 

If you were still here 
Would we all be more normal 
Would we all be happier 
Would we all have more fun 

The answer is yes 

If you were still here 
  
Would life be as boring as it is 
Would I hate people as much as i do 
Would I want to die as much as i do 

The answer is no 

If you were still here 

Would I need all the therapy i do now 
Would I have all the problems i do now 
Would I have no one to talk to 

The answer is no 

I love you and miss you 
I wish you were still here 
I can't wait for you to come backyour comming home on monday....yeah *celebrates*
she will never see this but i needed to write it....just for her
