You tell me I don't know what the fuck I'm talking about. 
Well what the fuck do you know...? 
You think you know all...well guess what...you don't.
In fact I don't think you know anything.
You think you know all there is to know about drugs, and alcohol.
Well you don't...you know what it is like to crave them. 
And you think you know what it is like to need them.
But you don't...because no one NEEDS them, they just WANT them.
And you have to learn the difference.
You have to learn that you don't need that shit.
You don't need anything but food clothing and shelter. 
 
So don't fucking pull that shit on me.
Don't think that you can tell me you need that shit, and expect me not to yell at you...
We had a deal.
I don't cut.
You don't do drugs...or drink alcohol. 

You fucking broke your side of the deal. 
So do you want me to also...?
Believe me, any moment now I will...
Just tell me...
Tell me one more time you NEED that fucking shit.
Then see what i do...
You just wait and see....OK... this is my rant...

IF YOU ARE ON DRUGS...DONT FUCKING TELL ME YOU NEED THEM. 
DONT TRY TO PULL THAT SHIT OVER ON ME...
I KNOW WHERE YOU ARE COMING FROM...BELIEVE ME...
SO DONT TELL ME I DONT FUCKING KNOW...
