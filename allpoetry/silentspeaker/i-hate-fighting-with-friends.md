title: "I Hate Fighting With Friends."
writer: "silentspeaker"

I hate it when I fight with my friends,
If it goes on for longer then an hour, 
It feels as though it never ends.

I talk as though I don't care,
You should know me better then that,
I look as though I am gasping for air.

I yell as though I'm horribly mad,
I look as though I'm going to kill someone.
Although all I feel inside is terribly sad.

I cry when you yell back at me,
You might not be able to see it,
But you can not see my crying plea.

My tears drip down my face,
As I lose my friend,
And my one saving grace.

We are always fighting about something, 
I act as though I am a princess.
And you act like a king.

We seem to start another fight each week, 
Not talking to each other,
Finally in the end starting to speak.

I love you tons, just to let you know.
I wish to hug you, everyday.
I hope through all of this, we can continue to grow.I'm very sorry,
to all my friends whom i fight with
