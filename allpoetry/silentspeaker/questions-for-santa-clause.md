Santa are you there?


Santa can you hear me?


Santa do you know what I want for Christmas?


Santa do you know that all I want is world peace?


Santa do you care that there are children in the world with not enough food?


Santa do you notice that there are kids everywhere crying themselves to sleep each night?


Santa do you take the time to tell everyone you care? 


Santa do you do you job for the fun of it
Or do you do it so kids everywhere will love you?


Santa do you really care that I am sitting in the corner with a trusty 'safety' pin?


Santa do you think about me when you see tiny droplets of crimson gold on the floor?


Santa do you care that I lay here on the floor passed out?


Santa do you care that the only way to make me happy is through pain?




Santa I don't think you care, feel bad for, or even notice me...



Santa I don't care.....this goes for everyone.
