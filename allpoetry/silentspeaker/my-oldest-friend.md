My oldest friend

You are my oldest friend
And no matter what happens in life
No matter how much it seems I don't care
No matter how mad I seem to be at you
I just want you to know I care

You have always been there
Just this is the first year that i seem to notice
And I'm sorry for that

I remember we used to be such happy children
Now look at us
What are we now?
But 14 year old depressed about nothing kids


We have all we could ask for, and more
We have friends, family, and all the necessities of life


Yet what do we do?
We complain about it

I'm sorry that I can not help you more
I'm sorry that I can not be a better friend to you
I'm sorry that I could not be there for you when you needed me most
