---
title: "LOVE"
writer: "silentspeaker"

love is a diamond...

a glorious diamond


a diamond that no one knows all of the sides of...


you don't know all the sides...



you are truly in love...

when you can say that you've seen all sides of that diamond...


the diamond is the best and most wonderful gift...


but don't give your diamond away too soon...


especially to someone who you know will not give theirs back to you...


keep your diamond for as long as you can...

it is all some have...

it is all some people will ever have.

so cherish it while you can. 

I'm not saying that you don't know what love is....

and I'm not saying that age matters 


but I'm saying that if you know the person wont give you their diamond 

don't give them yours...
