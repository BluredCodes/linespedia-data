title: "In My Time Of Need."

Every morning I wake up 
And get on the scale,
Praying to god that I didn't gain a pound.
Praying to a god I don't know exists. 
Praying that I can get down to 120.


You know what I mean.
You of all people know what I mean.
That is why I love you so much.
You know what I am feeling. 
You know that what I need least is to be pushed.
You know that all you need and should do is talk, and be there for me.

Thank you so much for helping me when I needed you most.
When no one else could help me. 
You were there.
You were the one who helped me.

Your beautiful smile, your wonderful mind.
Are all being wasted,
From mal-nourishment.
You are a beautiful girl.
And a wonderful friend.
You were there for me in my time of need. 
So as far as I am concerned, you are the best person out there.
