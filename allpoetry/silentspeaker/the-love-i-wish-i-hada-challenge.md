The love I wish I had for you 
It's missing 
It left me 
When you told me you 'loved' her 

Now that your love has changed 
And it's on me 
What am I to do? 
When I wanted you 
You hated me 
Now that you want me 
I don't know what to do 

I tried to give you my heart once 
You denied me 
You are trying to give your heart to me 
What am I expected to do? 

Should I take your love and be happy I have it 
Or should I turn and run, 
Run from the emotions that I don't want to show 
Run from the pain that comes from the love I once had 
Run from the pain that comes with it 

So should I run? 
Or put my heart out there for more painand dont jump to any conclusions....i used some basic stuff from real life...but most of it no....
