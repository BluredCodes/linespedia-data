You don't love me
You could care less
If it were possible you would just leave me to die
If you didn't have a conscious 
I would be dead by now
I would have been left by the side of the road 
With a broken heart
And a bleeding soul

My soul is not bleeding,
It is being cut into 
With your razor sharp words 
The words that kill me
The words that make me want to die

The feelings of hate
The ones that fill your eyes 
The feelings that I know are there
And you just wont let me see them

My body could care less
But my mind feels all the pain
My soul does care
My daydreams are affected by this
My night dreams are killed
I wish I could just die
