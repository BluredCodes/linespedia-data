Do you really know me?
Do you really know all you need to know to call yourself a friend?
Do you want to be my friend?

Or is that just what you say when you see me cry?
When you need something to say, and you can't seem to think of anything?

Do you really think you know all about me?
Do you really want to know all about me?
Do you try to be my friend?

Or do you just assume that you know all and go on with life?


Do you want me to be your friend?

Or do you want me to tell you my life history before you can make up your mind?
