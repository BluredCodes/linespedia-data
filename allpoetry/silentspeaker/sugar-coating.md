Am I sugar coating the inevitable?

Am I making this whole situation better for me?

I think about everything, but I can only get my honest opinion on things.

my friends, they tell me what they think, but how can I know what they are really thinking...?

I think about things how a teenager does,

always going to EXTREAMS 

thinking to much of myself

or 

thinking to little of myself



But it seems that I think to much of myself all the time...



so the answer is yes...



I AM sugar coating the inevitable.....
