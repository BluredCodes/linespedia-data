Confused
What a good word to describe me

What am I confused about you ask
Well everything 
I'm confused about life
I'm confused about friends
I'm confused about enemies 

I'm confused about my friendships
I'm confused about my whole life 

Some will say I have nothing to be confused about
That just means they really don't know me 


Being confused is very confusing
