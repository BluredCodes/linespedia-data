Boredom


I was told by a very wise friend, that it is my fault that I am always bored. And I know that it is my fault, if I wanted I could go and do something. But, NO, I sit down in front of my computer and read poetry online.  Now tell me, is that normal for a 14 year old girl to do?? Most likely not. 

Boredom is a reoccurring thing for me 
When all the entertainment I need is free

Right here on the Internet 
I can go all over the earth, with out breaking a sweat

I can read anything I want 
Although sometimes it's hard to read the font

I can write what I please 
Maybe next time I'll write about fleas

I can find anything out there
Without taking my butt out of a chair 

I can buy stuff with only a credit card
Then when I go into debt I'm called a retard

I can research stuff for the dreaded school 
So when I hand in my report I'm not called a fool

The Internet is a great tool
But if I stay in front of it too long, I may start to drool...hehehe....i would say i was bored
