"Friends aren't the ones who jump with you off that bridge, they are the ones at the bottom waiting to catch you "

I fall and every time you are there to catch me. 
I am constantly falling off of the tight rope called life.
Just to find myself in your outstretched arms once more.

I find myself falling more and more in life these days.
Yet you are always there to catch me.
But what would happen if you weren't there. 
If you weren't there to catch me, 
Where would I be in life.

I continue to fall in life.
And I forever will.
Yet I can only hope you will still be there for me.
I will always be here for you.
If you need me, 
Call me.
I'll be there for you.


So if you ever fall off that bridge, 
Ill be there to catch you.
