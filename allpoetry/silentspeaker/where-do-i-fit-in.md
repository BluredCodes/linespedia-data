---
title: "Where Do I Fit In?"
writer: "silentspeaker"
slug: "where-do-i-fit-in"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

All my friends are great people, Don't get me wrong.
I love them all, and I could not live with out any of them.

All my friends know what they want to be in life.
They all have talents, and can do anything they set their minds to. 

But then there is me.
I don't know what I want to be in life.
And I don't have any talents.
And no matter what I do, I can't do anything.


So no matter how hard I try, I will never fit in.This is how i feel...
I know that some say that i have no reason to feel that i don't fit in, but what can i say? 
 
(and to my friends, I love you all...but you all have your own thing...and then there is me....i dont have anything special about me...im sorry i feel this way)
