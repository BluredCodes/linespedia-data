Tonight I realized my worth

Tonight I realized my true family and friends

I realized that there are some certain people in the world that you would do anything for...maybe you are One of them maybe you're not...I don't know
But I realized that there are people out there that I would die to make them happy...but would they do the Same for me...?


Tonight I realized that there are people in this world I could not live with out...I don't know if you are one Of them, but they are few and far between.... But would they be able to live without me...?


Tonight I thought about you...I thought about all of the stupid things we have done together, and how much My life would suck if you weren't there to make me happy... would you be happy even if I weren't here...?


Tonight I thought about all of the things I wish to do in life...and I started thinking, would you support me In conquering those goals...?


Tonight I looked back on all the fun times I've had, and all the good times that are still to come, and I Thought do I really want you to be there with me.... Better yet do you want to be there with me...?



Tonight I started thinking about you and me...and I know my answers. 

Do you know yours...?This is not just for one person...this is for all of the people out there...all of my friends, all of their friends and so on and so forth....
