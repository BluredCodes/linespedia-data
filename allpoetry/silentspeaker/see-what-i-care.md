title: "see what i care..."
writer: "silentspeaker"

fine see what i care...
be that way be a jerk...
 my life doesnt revolve around you... 
or any of you...
my life revolves around my friends...
and family...
and me!!! 
so yea if you dont want a new friend
(witch everyone could use no matter how much you dont think you don't) 
fine then be that way...
see what i care... 
just cuz you dont want another friend... 
that doesnt mean that there are no other ppl out there that do want a new friend...
someone that they can talk to when they need to... 
or someone they can vent to... 
so i say once again... 
fine be that way... 
see what i care...this is for all those ppl who want to pick fights with me or you
i dont care what they think of me 
i dont know them they dont know me 
so it doesnt matter
