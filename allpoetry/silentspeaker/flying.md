Flying 
Flying high
Up in the clouds  
My breathing has quickend
My vision is bluring 
My pace is slowing 

I can't see anymore 
How ironic....
I've stopped breathing.
I can feel myself falling
Hitting the ground.....Thud Thud Thud.

I hear bones crunching
And people screaming.
Darkness...vision gone, completely
I lie there dying .... wishing I had told you I love you
And I need you....

Flying higher then ever before
Flying through the clouds...
Watching you constantly 
Watching you cry...
Sitting there crying for a person who doesn't
Deserve your tears........
