'Sometimes nothing is better then something, as long as it is a creative nothing.'
- Scott, the best and smartest friend I could ask for.


When asked what he wanted for Christmas, he said, if you don't know, then you can't truly say you are my friend.
When asked what he wanted for his birthday, he said, if you don't know that, then you truly aren't my friend.

Your friends are supposed to tell you what they want or need.  But sometimes you have to read their minds. You have to perceive what they want, and give them what they need.
And I know what you want for your birthday, you want to know how you feel.
Unfortunately, I can not provide that for you. Only you can do that.
I know what you need for your birthday, you need a hug.
And that, I can provide.
You also need to be left alone.
So whenever you don't want your hug.
Just tell me, they can be saved up for a later date.

You deserve what ever you want for your birthday,
But I can not provide that to you.
So I can only give you all I have.
And what I have is faith in you.
I believe that you can do anything,
So, for your birthday I give you,
The only thing that I can.
The love of a friend,
And the support of a friend,
And a person you can talk to about anything.Happy Pre Birthday Scott.
