Maybe you're right
I don't know

Maybe I do think I can make everyone's problems go away.
I'm not sure....

Maybe I do take on too much,
Maybe I am trying too hard.

Maybe I should stop trying to make you all better 
Maybe I will just stop making you tell me stuff you don't want to.

Maybe I should just go and make myself "all better"
And then come back and try to help all of you guys.

I don't know

Maybe I will go away...I think that is what I will do...



If you need me just yell, I will come out of my little hole in the wall and do your bidding....


See ya later
