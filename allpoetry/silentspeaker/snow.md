Snow

Little falling patterns of lace 
Falling breathlessly around my face

I'm standing there smiling, looking up in the sky
Looking, and longing to fly 

I clime up on the roof and look down,
A little apprehensive, and start to frown.

As I take my final step, I realize that I'm not the only one 
Who will be affected by this, too late it's already done

As I fall I see my life flash before my eyes, 
All those promises that turned into lies  

As I lay there in the snow listening to sirens wailing 
And think of all the things I'm failing

I'm failing my family, my friends, and all of humanity 
I might be loosing my life but I'm not loosing my sanity 

The snow is all I have left, all that lies around me
As I slip in to darkness I realize I can't see

My vision is gone, now what can I rely on???
I'm dying, laying here on the lawn
