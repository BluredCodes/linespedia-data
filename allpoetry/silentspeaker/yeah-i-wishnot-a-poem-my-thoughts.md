" The me I see is the me I'll be and love" 

Yeah I wish...the me i see..is the me i want to change...



the other day at the roller rink i caought myself thinking that if i was skinner...scotty would like me... than i got around to thinking...and realized that it is not him that would go that low...it is ME...



I am the one who thinks i need to be skinner...

my friends tell me im 'beautiful just the way i am
"You are one of the few people in whom true beauty still dwells"

~ Jessica 


But how ever much that is meant to make me feel better... makes me fell ten times worse....this is manily for my friends...
