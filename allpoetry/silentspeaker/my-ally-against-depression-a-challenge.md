Depression 
My ultimate enemy 
I have lost many a friend to it 
I have lost an uncle to it 
And it is 'in my blood' as I am told 

My allies against depression, along with poetry, 
Are my friends 
My friends can help me through anything 
All they have to do is be there 
They don't have to speak 
They just have to let me know that they will be there 

And to all my friends 
I just want to say thank you for being my saving grace.
