The stairway leads up. The never ending stairway to heaven.... Yea right
The stairs lead away from this hell.
The hell of life. The hell I wish to leave. 


One day I'll fly away.
But not on this day.
I'll still be near you,
As long as you keep me in your heart.

One day I'll leave this retched place,
So you'll never again see my horrid face.
Don't worry for me,
I'm finally getting what I deserve.

I'm finally in hell,
How I got here I won't tell.
It doesn't matter,
As long as I'm here.

Hell is my place to be 
I'm sure you won't really miss me
It doesn't matter at all
I know I will miss you but as long as I'm gone I know you will be happy
