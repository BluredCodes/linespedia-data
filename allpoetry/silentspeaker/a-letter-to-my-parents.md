I hate you
I hate you 
I hate you 
I hate you
I hate you
I hate you

You are supposed to be my parents
And stand up for me 
No matter what I do
You are supposed to love me
And care what I think
And care what I do
But no
I know now that you hate me
I know now that the only reason I'm still here is because you can't kill me off

You didn't say it with your words
But you said it with your actions 
And your eyes spoke the truth

You hate me
You hate my mind 
You hate my soul
You hate me
You hate my friends
You hate everything about me
You remember every little thing i have ever done wrong
And use it against me later

Well guess what 

I 
Don't
Give
A
Fuck

Go and die you stupid selfish bastardsthey will be finding this under their pillows tonight 
so i hope they have a fucking good day
