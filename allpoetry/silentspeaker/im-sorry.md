I'm sorry
For what ever I did
I didn't mean to
I don't want to hurt anyone
And even though I do
I just want you to know
I don't mean to...
I do love you
You are one of my best friends
And always...you are my 'girlfriend'

I don't know what I did 
But I do know I am sorry
And no matter what I do
I want you to know I don't mean to hurt you
