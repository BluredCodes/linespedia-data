So you think you're in love?
So you think you know what love feels like?

You are 15, you may think your IN love.
But I've seen what happens to people when they think they are IN love with someone. 
Eventually their hearts get crushed.
People think they are IN love, and they put their hearts on the line. not knowing what the other feels, and get their hearts crushed. Then their minds tell them that love only brings pain, so whenever they feel that warm fuzzy feeling inside, they try to ignore it. they push themselves into not believing what their hearts say. And they end up being alone, or sometimes even worse. They end up "falling IN love" with everyone. Getting married many times, each time claming that they fell IN love. 

So what ever you do. Don't say, "I love you" too soon. Be sure you know what you are doing, before you take that big leap, and say something you might regret later.this is for someone, who most likely will never read this. She now lives in California... and she just told me that she is IN love with her neighbor... he is 16... not bad...but still
