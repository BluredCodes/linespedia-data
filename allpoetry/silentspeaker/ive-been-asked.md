title: "I've Been Asked"

I've been asked 
I must be masked 
I can't be liked 
For who I am
It is just not possible

If you really like me 
Then why don't I feel it too?
Why can't I believe that someone 
Would like me?

Why is it so hard to believe,
That I am liked for me, 
And not for whom I hang around with?

Why must I doubt myself,
And think myself not worthy?

Why can't I believe in myself?
Why can't I believe that someone likes me? 

Is it because I am always telling myself that it will never happen?
Or is it because I've been told by numerous people that it would not 
Happen?
Am I being to self-centered...? Or am I being psyched out...?Well this started out as a poem about a guy who asked me out...and it ended as a poem about how much I don't respect myself and stuff like that... so I'm not sure what you will think...please be truthful...thank you!
