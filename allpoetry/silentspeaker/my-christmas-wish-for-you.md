My Christmas wish for you:


My Christmas wish is for you to be happy
I wish you happiness
Only because I love you
As a friend and as a person

My Christmas wish is for you to live a long and happy life
I want you to live very long
I want you to be there for every person that needs you
Including yourself
I want you to be someone special

My Christmas wish is for you to grow up to be a happy loving mother
You would be a perfect mother
You are loving
You are caring
You are you

And you are my best friend in the whole world....
Thank you for being there for me
I love you for it



Thank you Megan
