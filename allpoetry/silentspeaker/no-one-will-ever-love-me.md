no one will ever love me
i dont deserve it 
and there is no reason anyone should love me
i mean think about im not pretty
im not skinny 
and i am deffinatly not fun to be with

i would not beleive you if you tried to tell me other wise
i am a very stubbern person
i won't beleive you one bit.
there is no reason i should.

im am the most boring 
inconsiderate 
stupid
fat
ugly
girl in the world

so i dont blame you if you dont like me never mind 'love' me
i dont blame you if you never want to talk to me again 
i cant see what you see in me.
i really dont know why you are my friend.
if i didnt like you as much as i do
as a person and a friend
i would try to make you go and get the friends that you deserve
someone better then me
someone who will treat you better then i do
someone who will make you feel better about yourself
i try my hardest but that is not working 
im just not good enough...
im sorry to have failed you
