To all my friends, you know who you are, (I hope)
I just want you all to know that I don't want to hurt any of you...
I love you all dearly and I wish that I were only good enough for you
You are the best people in the world, your great friends, and great writers
You are the only thing that has kept me on this earth 
When I am with you I try my hardest to make you happy
When I see you in a corner crying I try my hardest to make you happy
When I know you are doing harmful things I try to make it stop
And when I know someone is hurting you I try my hardest to make them stop
But I know that I am not good enough
For whatever reason or another...
Weather it be I can never make you happy
I cannot seem to stop complaining, whining or bitching
I just wanted to tell you that you are the best friends anyone could ever ask for
And without you I would be lost in my own stupidity and humiliation.
Thank you for being there for me in my time of need, you have no idea how much it meant to me...

Sincerely yours,
VickyI say I hope because evidentially I am not a very good friend to anyone and I don't let it be known that I feel anything.

Sincerely,
Vicky
