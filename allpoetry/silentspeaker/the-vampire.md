He sits next to me and waits for me to let down my guard
when i finally get comfortable, he goes and does it again 
He bites my arm
and he bites it hard

The marks have been there for days now
the red marks on my arm, the pain of the touch
no more long sleeves for my arms to be desterbed

The skin has been desterbed by his sharp teath
The flesh has more marks,
The strange thing is I don't really mind it

I don't know why 
I just don't seem to notice it

He waits for my guard to go down, and just sits there watching me
as i 'scream' in pain, he watches me and smiles
And then i know that everything will be greatScotty......
