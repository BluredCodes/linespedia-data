Today I learned what life is really worth
Today I learned that people really do care
Today I learned that what happens does matter
Today I learned that if something happens all are affected 

Today I learned that life does not go on foreveryea 
ok explanation coming
the day that i wrote this we had a pep rally at school because our cross country team won state championship, and the one of the coaches was kind of old
and durring the middle of one of the team mates speeches he started to have a heat attack
so i just think this should never happen to any one else
you should never have to watch someone almost die
this would not be my first time that i had to watch someone die but i sure hope it is my last
