I have a pain in my side...
(No its not you)
It's a sort of pinch every time I inhale.
Everytime I exhale it's there...

~wait...no, now it's a stabbing pain...
to move my arm only causes me to grimace.

Breath in breath out...
Oh why not just stop...?
It hurts so much.

The pain the suffering...
What was the cause you ask?
I have no clue...that is the scary part.
It's just kinda showed up.
Pinching at first.... Stabbing...twisting...


Oh GOD I GIVE UP!!!
