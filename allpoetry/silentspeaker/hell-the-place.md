this is hell
the world is hell we have all died
and we are being punished for what we have done

this is not the place i imaganed 
it is "nice" 
there are no flames coming out from the floor
there are no devils with hornes coming out of there heads

the only thing that is so terribly wrong with this place is 
the hatred
of all human kind 
the only reason so many people are dead is because people kill 
we kill our own kind 

that is the depressing part of it all
that is why this is hell 
because people have no respect for life
and they dont care what happens to others
all they care about is them selves
its always me me me me me
never thinking about what harm it could cause other people 


so here is a question for you 
are you injoying hell?
