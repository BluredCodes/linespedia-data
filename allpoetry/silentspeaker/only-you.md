---
title: "only you"
writer: "silentspeaker"

only you could do this to me 
give me this warm fuzzy feeling inside
as if im floating on a cloud of happyness
and the sun is looking down on me 
smiling at me 

now every time that i am around you i feel...
butterflys flying in my stomach at 100mph
the just keep moving around
up, down, front, back

now that we are together i can feel like that everyday
everyday i see you
i hold your hand
and look deep in your eyes, and see 
pure happyness
