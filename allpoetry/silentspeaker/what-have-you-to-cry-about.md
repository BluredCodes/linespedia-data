~*~*~*~*~*~ What have you to cry about? ~*~*~*~*~*~

Silence thy angry cries,
Wipe thy face clean of tears.


Listen to my little voice,
Crying out in the darkness.
Hear my precious tears,
Shattering on the floor.

See the pain others go through.
See their anger, 
Watch their tears.

Now tell me,
What have you to cry about?
