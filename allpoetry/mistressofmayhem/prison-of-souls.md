---
title: "Prison of Souls"
writer: "MistressofMayhem"
slug: "prison-of-souls"
source: "AllPoetry"

No dreams for the future
A prison of minds
The hatred is brewing
All thoughts left behind.

All that I had
Has been taken away
My heart has gone numb
I've not much to say.

The burn is immense
the pain that I feel
Inward and outward
too hurt to heal.

They'll cast you away
a stone in the road
not even thinking
the love they bestowed.

I want to get out
I can't find the door
This Prison of Souls...
this, nothing more.
