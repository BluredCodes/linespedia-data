title: "He Understands Me"

He reads into my soul
watching me with those loving eyes
giving me hope of a new day
showing me that I am never alone
holding my hand through hard times
caressing my face when I cry
knowing how to make me happy
trying his hardest to bring a smile to my face
becoming the one person I need him to be
seeing my innermost emotions
trusting my every action
believing in everything I do
loving me for me  

And when eternity seems like such a short time to be together, and kisses are as soft as and as sweet as rose petals on a spring morning, you will know that love and undying companionship are the most beautiful life experiences
