---
title: "Longing for snow"
writer: "MistressofMayhem"
slug: "longing-for-snow"

Winter rain
showing no signs
of Christmas' sparkling joy.
The air is warm, 
the sun is shining
the feeling of happiness destroyed.
The trees are bare
they're nothing but brown
a depressing sight to see.
But if they were covered,
radiating white,
how happy I would be!
I miss the footprints,
the friends, the smiles,
the parties we had outside.
When it would snow
we'd all join together
and down the hill we'd glide.
It felt so good
to be bundled up,
in a blanket with the one you love.
But when I look up
and hope to see snow...
there's nothing falling from above!!!
The season is ruined,
the feeling is gone, 
I wish I could take a trip...
To see the snow
up in the hills,
hot chocolate I would sip.
I would look out the window
of my mountain top room
and definitely manage a smile.
I'd laugh at myself
and then I'd realize...
...summer will be coming in awhile.
Then I'll want
to enjoy the bright sun
that always seems to show.
I won't feel so "blah"
about the weather,
but I'll definitely be waiting for snow!LOL  Very third-gradish.  I was just in a funny mood.
