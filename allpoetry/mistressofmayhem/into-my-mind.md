---
title: "Into My Mind"
writer: "MistressofMayhem"
slug: "into-my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why do you want to know me?
My body, my soul - my fortress.
What do you want to know about me?
The reasons why I allow myself to live are foreign.
Why do you protrude into the very core of my mind,
hoping to find something valuable to call your own?
As I wonder about you, mere being,
I do not fear you.
You are prying me open like a book,
to read me,
to find out what I am all about.
"Why are you so curious about my state?" I ask.
"To save, protect you from harm."
I think to myself -protect from what-and
you read into my thoughts just as you have read my soul.
You want to save me from myself,
from the emotions that are building inside my temple.
So I wonder more...Why me?
I am not worthy of this attention.
You want to help me, but
you are hurting me in the process.
You make me feel useless, and so
I sink into the solemness of my mind,
wishing you would leave...and then,
reading my thoughts...

You are gone.
