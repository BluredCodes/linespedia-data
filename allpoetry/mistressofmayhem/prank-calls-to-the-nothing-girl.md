-PRIVATE CALL-
-PRIVATE CALL-
-PRIVATE CALL-  Blinking on the screen...


Your calls
They linger
They stay still in the humid air 
They all sound the same
Like a broken record that someone forgot to take off the player
Your voice like the devil's coming to torture me
Do you enjoy playing childish games? 
I never enjoy your pranks
They tear at me
Make me feel useless
Make me feel like it doesn't matter if you call
the ugly girl
the chubby girl
the stupid girl
But you do call and the vibrations of your words hurt my ears
The piercing sound of laughter crumbles my heart
Do you think you're funny?
You do not impress me and I can bet you do not impress your friends
Keep laughing...laugh...laugh...laugh...until you cannot take it anymore
Laugh at the nothing girl
Make the demons appear
Make bad things happen
Eventually...you will feel the hurt, too
Harassment charges
Take that you son of a bitchI keep getting prank calls from people who think they know me and hate me but are secretely jealous.  No wonder I'm morbid...how can you be happy when people are making fun of you?  This isn't supposed to be a good poem...just things that come to my head.
