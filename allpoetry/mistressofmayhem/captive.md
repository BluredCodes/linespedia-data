title: "Captive"

I lift my battered body
up off the floor
straining to move my head.

I see your figure 
walk out the door
remembering words you've said...

"You're mine to hold, 
to kiss, to touch,"
you told me time again.

"I'll love you forever, 
befriend you as much
until our lives shall end."

But as time lapsed
you went astray,
my heartstrings growing weak.

You controlled my actions,
all freedom went away;
I wasn't allowed to speak...

To speak of the ways
you beat me down;
I had to disguise my scars.

You made me your queen
without a crown,
you locked me behind bars.

My body was your temple,
but I could
no longer hide.

As I lived this awful life
questions stood
in my mind.

"You're mine to hold,
to kiss, to touch,"
you told me time again.

Why did you think that
this was enough
to take back your uncontrollable sin?

I've let you walk
all over blessed me
for the certain very last time.

I've taken control,
a new road I can see...
