---
title: "You plus Me = Meant To Be"
writer: "cheekychick"
slug: "you-plus-me-meant-to-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/811-You-plus-Me---Meant-To-Be-by-cheekychick"
---

I love you, need you, miss you so much,
I need to feel your special touch.
I've never felt this way about anyone before,
I wish I would have been around you more.
What if I never see you again?
Just thinking about that puts my heart through so much 
pain.
Please tell me how you feel.
Do you like me or love me for real?
I know that what I feel, words cannot explain.
When I thing of you and our last hug good-bye,
The tears flow like rain.
I would rather die than never ever see you,
You may not believe this but it is most true.
Don't you know that I want to and can be your everything?
You look at me in this special way and I just want to sing.
When we were together things felt so right, 
It's supposed to be that way.
But now you're gone and there's an emptiness here and it
will not go away.
I believe it's better to love than to have never loved at 
all,this is true.
The only person I want to be with in the whole world is 
you.
Now you know exactly what and how I feel for you,
So until we meet again, farewell, good-bye, 
And I hope you love me too.
