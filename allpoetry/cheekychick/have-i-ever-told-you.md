---
title: "Have I ever told you?"
writer: "cheekychick"
slug: "have-i-ever-told-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/813-Have-I-ever-told-you--by-cheekychick"
---

Have I ever told you when you look into my eyes,
You can see right through me even when I'm in disguise.
Have I ever told you if the sun refuses to rise,
That's not going to make my feelings for you die.
Have I ever told you, you mean everything to me?
Someday you'll understand, you will see.
Don't you know I'm in love as much as love can be?
Even though sometimes it seems it can't be with thee.
Hopefully you feel the same about me, this I wouldn't doubt.
Because I know you know exactly what I'm talking about.
Maybe it sounds as though I'm trying to immitate a poet.
Or maybe I'm trying to tell you something and you'll never know it.
For even though everything that I wrote is true,
It doesn't mean I'm ever going to tell it to you.
