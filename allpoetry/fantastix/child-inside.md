I hear you, but you don’t see me, you don’t know why it hurts so badly                       
He loved you; at least you thought he did
Touching you in ways he knew
Violating your innocence with every breath he stole, holding the secrets inside

Without a sound, I feel your pain; taste the bitterness he left behind
Feeling the strength down deep within he tried to drain
Your fight never left but your heart has grown scared to love 
Stolen love, emptiness, lost forever so you say 

Your soul angry, scattered millions of feelings captive 
Split, introverted, masking the many people you’re forced to become
Hurt, used for man’s pleasure, torn inside out
Ripping your heart from your body 

And he loved you; at least you thought he did
I hear you, whispering prayers of hate; you don’t see me
I am with you every time you breathe, whenever you’re scared to feel
When you hear footsteps down the hall

I am what you refuse to show, will never admit to being
The space between awake and dream is where I hide
The darkness, the uncontrolled tainted love, the tiniest hint of tears and fear
What hurts so badly is the lonely child silently insideI wrote this for my best friend, Girl your courage and heart is unbelieveable. I love you. :)
