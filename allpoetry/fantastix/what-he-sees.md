In the eyes of the Lord
We are all tainted
In need of redemption
Lost with a sense of contention

Living our lives
Walking the once straight line of a creator
Who cannot be met with the human eye?
Whose presence ultimately felt when we die?

Sorrowful silence for innocence lost
A child discovers life’s true reality
Entranced with no reason
Never to return to fantasy as season changes season

In the eyes of the Lord
We are all misguided soldiers
With different paths leading to joy and pain
Stricken both well and insane
