Last night
I whispered your name
Feeling so lost in your eyes
There was no escape

Last night 
I felt your heart beat
So strong and free
With no concept of space or time

Last night 
I melted into you
As the heat of our bodies
Dried all my tears

Last night
I sensed your spirit
Inside of me
As you smiled

Last night
I slept inside of you
Awakening with love on my mind
Longing for a life to live

Last night
I made a promise
That today as nightfalls
My soul will forever belong to you
