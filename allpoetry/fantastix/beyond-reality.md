I'm feelin so poetic right now
Like my feet have left the ground
And the trees fly by my side
Lifted higher, freed by the sound
Of your voice that sings so sweet to me
Of the truths that finally let me be
Of the beauty you saw, I now can see
Of life beyond realityThis poem was written for Nick, one of the most amazing people I have ever met. He truly makes me feel beautiful. If only he could see the true beauty inside his heart.
