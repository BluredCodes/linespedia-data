title: "Key To My Soul"

I want to breathe you in
Feeling you expand inside of me
Running through my every vein
From my head to my toe
To melt together like the wax from a candle
You hold the key to my soul

I need to hold you close
Hearing your heart beat in time with mine
If only for a fading moment
As the sun lapses into the moon
When heaven closes its eyes 
The stars guide you to my side
