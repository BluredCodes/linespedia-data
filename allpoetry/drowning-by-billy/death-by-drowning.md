Venice,is being condemned to a slow death
Gradually being removed from this earth
As it slowly sinks into it's own lagoon
The italian government,must act soon

St Marks square gets continually flooded
But does anyone really care
Or are they all just unaware
Of the impending floods
Set to reduce this romantic city
To a slow death by drowning
Are the alarm bells really sounding.

The future lives of venice folk
Are hanging by a thread
Something must be done very soon
Before this city sinks to a watery death.
