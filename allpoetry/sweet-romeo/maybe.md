I think I found the one
but how can i be sure
and if she is the one, how can she be won

Do I haved to show her my charm
or do i keep it to myself
please help me find hoe so i can start

Is she the type that is shy
or is she very outgoing
whatever it may be I have to try

But should i get to know her first
or do I just tell her how I feel
this confusing ignorence, is a curse

So I'm just going to follow my heart
and hope that she will have some feelin for me
and wish that she is a sweet heart
