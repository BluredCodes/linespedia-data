You are my flower
I am your rain
You give me power
But yet you believe, I am the same

Just like all the rest
a heartless guy
And i try to convince you, that I am the best
But you won't even try

so im writing this poem
Hoping you will change your mind
I don't want you to be alone
Please, won't you be mine
