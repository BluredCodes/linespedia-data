I made a mistake
I lied to you
I said I had no feelings
And I even convinced myself it was true

But now I realize
When I said 
there were good guys out there
It was me you were soposed to have

But I locked my feelings up
and threw away the key
trying to be sympothetic, with out crossing hte line
But every time you said loved someone I wanted you to say me

So why don't you give me a chance
Let me heal your broken heart
I will be your charming romeo
You just give me a chance and ill start
