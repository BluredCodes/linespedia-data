---
title: "No One Knows"
writer: "crestfallen"

When you see me smile or laugh
Dare do you be fooled,
The Ultimate Illusion
is by what I am Ruled

I let no one close to me
let know one get to know me,
The Ultimate Illusion 
is what I Appear to be

On the outside I am laughing
Crying on the inside,
The Ultimate Illusion
is by what I Abide

I dare let one single tear
Burn down my pale face,
The Ultimate Illusion
Forces a Smile to take its place

And when I Break,
When I Crack,
The Ultimate Illusion
will leave me with a slap

But how can I shou my true emotion?
Who would really Care?
The Ultimate Illusion
will always be there...
