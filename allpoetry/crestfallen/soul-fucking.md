Fucking lapse into the dead,lonely and unknown instead;take her soul with one hard look,read her eyes and write her book.you can't let her love your smile'cause you amused by your own bile.Set on a throne, forever to holdlike the whore you never sold.Don't you let her get away,does she know the game you play?She has eyes of golden rapturebear the fruits your latest capturestar laced eyelashes that I hate,take my word now your too late.you milked her joy to keep your ownand now she's scared to be alone,you fucked her soul and now she's deadI warned her to stay away from your bed.Promise her it's all okyou've learned just what you gotta say.you smack her when she starts to cry,and wonder why she thinks you lie.Peel My skin off of your ownOnly I can make you moan,Innocent with pixies passion,lover boy your out of fashion!
