title: "Silence in The City"
writer: "crestfallen"

That divinity which I seekfinds no boundaries in my depths,the complexity of that gifted mindby which only simplicity can surpass.Silence means more than noiseless-ness,it is the traffic of the mind only, thatstops in silence; were the world outside buzzes and breaks,through that night of rhythmic,dizzying lights, moving and slowing,stopping yet speeding, whirling pastunnoticed, by that silenceof the mind.
