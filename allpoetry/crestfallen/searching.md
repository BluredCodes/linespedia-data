title: "Searching"
writer: "crestfallen"

lavender leaves upon my feet, dancing in my eyes,
dancing in my feet so cold upon this winter floor,
the brown leaves fall from trees grown tall, 
and shower me with
divinity,
but can I ever find the truth?
will it ever come to me?
to embrace true spirituality, I must find that truth.
I see my sins and love them for thier lessons, 
but must I do more?
Open that road that I may travel on,
embrace my soul, spirituality, love.
