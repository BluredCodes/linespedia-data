let the light go to
vastness and infinity,
let the knowledge I invoke
be from that divinity.

Help my soul to light
and fill me,
open my mind and be
love that I see.

I want to find the cleanliness,
sterility, of perfection
I heed you now my goddess
to make that connection.

I am not the vision
of accepted society;
because I fill my life
with truth, and variety.
