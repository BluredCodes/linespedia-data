title: "Making Love to the Senses"

Have you ever felt so swept away that is seemsthe wind blows only for you? That the moonlooks down at you alone, and steals your heart with every touch of its light? Can you taste the sky, and feel the stars? Can you smell the mossy Earth, or hear each leaf as they play with one anotherhigh above? Do the clouds pull at your lips and makeyou want to smile? Can you hear the sap running through the trees, like blood of the Earth?Have you ever run with a coyote, or flown with a raven?Can you feel the life around you, Taste the fruits that growabundant in this garden? Take a moment, let this Earthmake love to you. Feel it inside you, feel it around you andknow that divinity is every where here. That it is you, and all these blades of grass, and every wave upon the Ocean;every flicker of flame, and all those passions within you.
