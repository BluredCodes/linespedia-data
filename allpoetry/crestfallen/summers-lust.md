title: "Summers Lust"
writer: "crestfallen"

So rich is it that fills me,
My mind and eyes in awe
as such a shiny penetration fog,
etches memory, what I saw.

Like liquid grace they dance
On a canvas painted haunting blue,
My eyes filled with their 
starry light, until the mornings dew.

A pale thin crescent of galactic rain
Hangs low within the sky,
It's sliver light shines down on me
and urges me to fly.

The gentle night now tremors
in a warm and throaty cry,
The thunderous clouds persue the moon
in hopes to win the sky.

In a breath of summers breeze
floats an apple blossoms petal,
I know I can't be safe
from the storms seducing metal.

I see it creeping closer,
Summers lightning is her hair,
The Goddess here betrays herself
and I can't break my stare.

The peaceful calm within my
field, lays in wait for mothers wrath,
But her love for all that lives
blesses innocence with a bath.

Scented by the blooming lilacs
and kisses by Ceridwens love,
Her blessing wets my nose
Gently from above.

That waxing galactic drop
has surrendered to Her charm,
knowing that her love for me
keeps me far from harm.

Though the sky around me breaks
and her hair has licked the Earth,
The Flames are doused with Silver
and land begins Rebirth.

Breathy breezes from my lover
press airy kisses on my lips,
I can feel his eyes upon me
and the touch of finger tips.

I'm embraced by his warmth
and feel the beating in my chest,
the thrill and fear excites me
though I long to rest.

I know it is inside me,
The Storms beguiling lust
my mouth is sweetly bitter,
stained with blood of rust.

Blissfully released
from the powerful embrace,
I feel the power in my veins
but never saw his face.

He hid within her main
only I could coax him out,
With nothing but a strawberry lip
placed in a delicate pout.

He came upon a strand
of Divinity and lust
because I love the rain
and was birth'd by dust.

I lay upon the grass,
The Storm has now moved on
I watch the stars pass over me,
Until the break of dawn.
