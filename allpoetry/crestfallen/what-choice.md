When have you given to much?Give me one clear sign,'cause he keeps taking moreand I can't draw the lineI hate to be so selfish,But it's a silent kind of rape,My lover or my future,The choice I can't escapeWhat am I gonna doWhen there's nothing left to giveWhen he turns his back and walks away,and I have no life to live...
