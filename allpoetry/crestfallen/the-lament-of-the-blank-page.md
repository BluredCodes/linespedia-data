---
title: "The Lament of the Blank Page"
writer: "crestfallen"
slug: "the-lament-of-the-blank-page"
source: "AllPoetry"

Damn this page so blank
mocking me with its white,
Daring me to try and write, 
to make my feelings light.

Damn this absent thought
That's gone away with the spoon,
or maybe with the cow,
who's gone to jump the moon.

It's an octopus's' garden
with plants, and grainy sand,
This page can think of nothing
it stills my eager hand.

Oh where, oh where could 
my little thoughts be?
Look into my soul,
and maybe I'll see!
