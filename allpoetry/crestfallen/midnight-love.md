---
title: "Midnight Love"
writer: "crestfallen"

Take my soul with your divinity,relinquish my heart with your eyes burning deep into mine,feel my soul cry out to youand dance upon the velvet night,hold my heart in your sun kiss'd palmsand strike me with your ever-changing face,manipulating,learning and ever-still, in the fathoms of your mind,I see through them into the truth,with that depth, only I can see...
