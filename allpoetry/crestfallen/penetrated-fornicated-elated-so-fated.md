Sated so I sat Elated thankful of a life so Fated,
still I felt that some part Hated all that I had since
Created. I still sat quite Elated never worried
still I waited, not regretting who I Mated, his arrival
I Awaited. Helpless as he Penetrated all the 
bliss Accentuated, not a single moment wasted,
I still remember how he tasted; Dare I tell you
what he stated after we had Fornicated,
never had he Speculated his heart could be so  wholly Sated.
Never too be Seperated, Cupids arrow Underrated.
