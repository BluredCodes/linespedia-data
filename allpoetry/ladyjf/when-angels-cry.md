---
title: "When Angels Cry"
writer: "LadyJF"
slug: "when-angels-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

I know that you have to go
But the reason why I wont know
This hurts more than any pain
Without you here I'll go insane
All I can do is want to die
This is when angels will cry
NO one can bring back your life
Although I was going to be your wife
You're my best friend, family, life, and love
What happens when you've gone above
You're all I have to live for
What happens when you're not here anymore
No one may care if you live or die
But as for me, I'll always ask why
Everyone will act like they care
But I know my "friends" will never be there
All they will do is tell me to pray
But not even that will take the pain away
You are the love of my life
I thought I would be your wife
You're the only person that will ever love me
And yet our love can never be
I wish I could take your place
But then you would have an empty space
I just want to know why
Why is it you that has to die
I just want to know what to do
But all I can do is love you
