Why keep writing how I feel
It is my words that seem to kill
Killing friendships with great ease
Spreading fast like a disease
Ripping down this wall of glass
Cutting pain that will not pass
Lying broken on the ground
Listening to my broken sound
Throwing down my broken dreams
Whispering my painful screams
The problems cutting at my skin
Helping the disease drip in
Screaming as I run away
Hiding for the light of day
Roaming through the evil night
Wanting to end this fight
Longing for aday of peace
This disease is what I release
Change just wont come to me
Because I am this broken disease
