---
title: "Turn Away"
writer: "LadyJF"
slug: "turn-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

They say they just want to end it all
Why should I care it’s their call
They say that they just don’t care
but wasn’t it them that was always there
I don’t care about what they say
They say this crap everyday
I shouldn’t care about their problems too
I just don’t know what to do
I turn away when there’s to much
I jerk away from every touch
I lock my feelings inside my head
And, yet, they still wish they were dead
I laugh at them inside my heart
They didn’t see me fall apart
They are blind and will never see
They put this pain inside of me
Are they laughing in my face
I can’t tell in this dark place
I’ll try to change into me again
but I fear I’m lost in sin
I lost myself in their pain
So it’s their fault if I’m insane
Like they said, they just don’t care
So they wont notice if I’m not there
