title: "What I want"

There are tears I have cryed
And fears I have to hide
Nobody can ever help me
Nobody can make me see
No one will lend me a hand
No one can understand
I have to be solo for all time
I'm telling the world in this rhyme
I love the world so much
But I get not one's loving touch
If I die tonight
Would it make it right
If I left today
Would my love be here to stay
I just want to close my eyes
And block out my painful cries
I want to forget the ones who lied
I want to kill this pain inside
I want to run or disappear
But my heart is always here......,
