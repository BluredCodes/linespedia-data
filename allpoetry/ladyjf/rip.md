When you look in my eyes 
Do you see these stupid lies
When you look deep in my heart
Do you see how it was ripped apart
Why is there so much hate
I love the world, but it's too late
I miss tall the love and peace
I can't say much for hate's release
I close my eyes and feel the pain
I think I'm now really insane
I'm ripped apart from inside out
And yet, I don't know what it's about
I'm sorry for the wrong I've done
But, unlike some, I wont use a gun
I have to go on with this painful game
I wish things could be the same
I never thought the letters R.I.P
Would open my eyes and make me see
