title: "Life's end"

I'm lying here almost dead
I wish I was home safe in bed
I can hardly speak 
And I'm growing weak
Mom, can you hear me
Tell me how this could be
I need you by my side
I know I've lied
I'm sorry it's like this
I know my life you'll miss
You'll miss the way I smiled
And cry over your loving child
Tell daddy I love him
And my brother and sister I'll miss them
I can't win this fight
Don't worry, I'll be alright
My heart has almost stopped
My pulse has dropped
Mom, you stared to cry
But my spirit did not die
Goodbye I wispered in the wind
Was it right for my life to end......,
