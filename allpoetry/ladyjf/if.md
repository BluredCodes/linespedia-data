If... If... what a word
It seems like that's all I've heard
If I were stronger, would I be okay
If I had stopped, her would she be here today
If I had hop, could things be alright
If I could, would I see her tonight
If she were here, would she love me
If she's an angel, is she happy
If we weren't sister, would we have met
If we hadn't, would I still be upset
If I forgot her, could I get well
If I stopped the pain, would I go to hell
If I pushed her away, would she get mad
If I stopped thinking, would I be sad
If I try, could  I forget the past
If I wanted, could I make her life last
Stop saying if! It seems so cold
But how much can this word hold...,
