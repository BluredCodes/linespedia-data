Here is something that I would say,
If you were here just one more day...
I never want you to disappear
but, I know, tomorrow you wont be here
I'm not sad because you lied
I'm always like this deep inside

Here is something I would say,
If you never dided away...
Please just stop popping pills
It's not just you that it kills
I don't want this to be the end of you
Just take a look from my point of view

Here is something I need to say...
I need you here forever to stay
I'll give more if you can live
But I now have nothing I can give
I would die with all your sin
Jut so you could live again

Here is something you sould say...
That you're with me every day
Not to cry over your death
Just to be happy for every breathe
To be brave when there is fear
And no matter what you're always here...
