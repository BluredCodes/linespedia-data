My teary eyes look to the sky
Now I have to say goodbye
The blood runs down my finger tips
As the tears roll over my shivering lips
I'm remembering my friends I adore
I'm sorry I couldn't take it anymore
I feel the devil will succeed
as I continue to bleed
God, forgive me once again
I'm just scared of my sin
I'm scared to live and scared to die
So all I can do is sit and cry
So forgive me, Lord, for having pain
And forgive me if I hide in shame
