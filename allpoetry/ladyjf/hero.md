---
title: "Hero"
writer: "LadyJF"
slug: "hero"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I behold his beauty
and shed a tear
I now feel safe
I haven't one fear
I feel his love 
and hold him tight
I'm happy now
Everything's alright
His smile is warm
His eyes are deep
He holds me close
then I fall asleep
His words dry my tears
My tears refresh every word
He tells me the truth 
and explans the lies I've heard
I love him more than life
I love him just too much
I love his tender kiss
My heart stops by his touch
He makes me laugh
and makes my problems go away
He's my hero
and the reason I wake up everyday...
