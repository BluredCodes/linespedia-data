My favorite color's red
Just like the blood I shed
My favorite thing is fear
This fear that's always here
I look at the night's sky
Wanting badly to die
I smile at deaths sight
As I roam through the night
I love the sound of other's pain
It helps me see what I have to gain
My only friend is my sorrow
I wounder what will happen tomorrow
I'm sickened by every one I see
They don't feel the pain inside of me
I don't know why I cry
I wish this evil would die
I never wanted to give into this 
I know it's me they'll never miss
I'm sorry for being this way
But I will change someday...,
