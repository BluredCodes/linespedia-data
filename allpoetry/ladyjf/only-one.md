---
title: "Only One"
writer: "LadyJF"
slug: "only-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Why don't they know what to say
To make the anger fly away
I've found the perfect grace
In this dark, empty place
In storms I've seen the sun
In sadness I've had fun
In the hate I've felt love
While I'm down I've soared above
I've smiled and laughed before
And now I talk more and more
But I'm the only one who sees me 
I'm the only one who's empty
I'm the only one who can understand
Even I need a helping hand
Why doesn't he hear what he's said
To keep running through his head
I've held and let him go
I've listened and now I know
My cup is full and now will drain
In love I've felt much pain
I've won the spot in his heart
I've been free and fell apart
The kisses were tender and sweet
I've fell, he put me back on my feet
But I'm the only one who sees me 
I'm the only one who's empty
I'm the only one who can understand
Even I need a helping hand
When will God take it away
The pain that's part of everyday
I've had friends that didn't care
The guy of my dreams who isn't there
The sun through all the rain
And yet I still feel pain
I've lived this life and still trying
I have smiled while I'm crying
I've had everything without knowing
But through forced smiles, pain is showing
And I'm the only one who sees me 
I'm the only one who's empty
I'm the only one who can understand
Even I need a helping hand
