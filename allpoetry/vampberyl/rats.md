My rats are the cutest things on earth!

Everything to their little noises, their eyes and their little paws is just so adorable.

Skat is the crazy one with his licking fetish and his pouncing degree..

Mr. Fluffers is shy and likes to bite me every chance he gets.

I love my rats even though everyone is afraid of them.
