Now that I've realized life doesn't have to suck.....It's up to me to make it worthwhile.....

Life is great when you want it
Life is great when you stop it

My pain is personal
My gain is living

Life can suck
When your kicked in the butt

But the most important thing of life is living in it

Moral of this story is sucicde does not work.
