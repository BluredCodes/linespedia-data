I can't find the words to say it...

I can't get a hold of the feeling I desire....

I want to shout out....

I myself is trying....

I can't express...

I want to try...

Everytime I get the nerves to say it I just...

I want to fade away from this place but....

I choke with my words...

The one world I share is yours

One thing I can say is...

I want to say it but...

I can't say it because it will hurt you or even make you want me...

I want to say .....
