Life is useless especially when you don't know what to do with it.

My life is pointless, and so is yours

Why all the pain and doubt when noone can fix it?

What is with emotions, feelings, or personallity?

Why can't we live in peace?

What is peace anyway.....someone will find a reason to rebel and destroy all happiness.

Sucide must be an answer....because humankind has tried everything else.

Life really sucks when you are a freek.
