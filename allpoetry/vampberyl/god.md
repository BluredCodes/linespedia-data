What is wrong with me?
You did'nt answer...
Why do I hurt?
Why do I feel?
You still did'nt answer me...
Why is there so much pain?
Why is there so much pressure?
Why don't you answer me?

Are you there?
Are you alive?
People say you watch
I don't believe in You

I am hurting myself
I am hurting others as they watch
Why don't you stop me?

Are you afraid to exist?
Are you afraid of me?
Why do you hide?

Why won't you answer?
