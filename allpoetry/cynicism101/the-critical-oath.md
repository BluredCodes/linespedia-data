I waste a lot of negative energy because you almost make me want to 
express myself badly

and I give in because it relieves something

in the place where there is no imagery, 
just a fierce concentration on the 
self, on the self-pity, on the self-

that is quite different than (and you can fill in the blank, there)

and of course, that entropy created when we do things to fill our 
narrowing vessels 

because we come so close to thinking that entropy is peace
