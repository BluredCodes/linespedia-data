Granted: perspective is swayed
Granted: we must do the best we can
Granted: we all want to live fully and be comforted
Granted: we start to think we don't believe we can

Granted: the pixels are too small
Granted: they're like holes in a sheet
Granted: the area codes are unfair
Granted: at the edges there's something to meet

Granted: we've been conditioned to fizz
Granted: perhaps we're not that big
Granted: they've been taught to fuse
Granted: there's far too much to dig.
