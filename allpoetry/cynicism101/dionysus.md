A little training in classical mythology is a dangerous thing.
Are we prepared to find a balance 
or vary between extremes?
Are we prepared to negate all our negatives
those inhibitions
and litter our poetry with images and passion?
Are we willing to risk chaos in the best, romantic, sense?
There are, somewhere, Che Guevara jack-o-lanterns 

and huge, surreal Christmas balls
that speak of tranquility in a time of confusion.
