The author apologizes to the reader for delibrately 
selling herself in the catchy title

The author apologizes for energy spent elsewhere

The author apologizes for lack of technical structure, the author 
apologizes for not faking it, the author apologizes for faking it, the 
author

apologizes for not yet 

allowing herself to be representational

allowing herself distance

allowing herself anything more than poetic licence and 
self-involvementi never got the message.
