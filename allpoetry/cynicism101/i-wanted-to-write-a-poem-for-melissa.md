I wanted to write a poem for Melissa, but realized I have the soul but not the expression of Leonard Cohen.

I wanted to write a song for Melissa, but realized I have the soul but not the expression of Paul Simon.

I wanted to write an essay for Melissa but realized I have the soul but not the expression of Salman Rushdie.

So, shedding all pretenses of metapoetry, I (and, apparently, three ageing men) love Melissa very dearly.
