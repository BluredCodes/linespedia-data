title: "Scent of life"

the aroma rides the wind 
like a sweet messenger of love

the faint scent of life
fills my senses with wonder

like a bloodhound I follow the smell
recklessly charging ahead

what oder from heaven has come down
to excite me so

a mystical vibration of god
that overwhelms my poor soul

I am closer now and the scent is strong
deliverance into paradise is near

OH!

the sorce of the sweet sweet oder is in sight

OH!

can it be?

Yes

Yes

YES!
