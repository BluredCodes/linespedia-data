title: "Superman is a Fraud"

Where are the heros I was promised in my youth?Where are the role models?Where are the knightsUpon shiny white horses?Where are the cowboys?Where are the SuperherosAnd the fighters of injustice?Where are the patriots?Were there ever such men?Did they ever really exist?Or is Superman just a corporate lie?I thought so.
