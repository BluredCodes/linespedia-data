I can see the clouds
filling like balloons 
with the dark dreary rain.

It is summer in the South
picnicks and water holes
are the topics of the day.

A cold slice of watermellon
on a hot Florida afternoon
would tempt me to sell my soul.

The rain looms overhead.

Why must all good times end?




The hell with it.

Lets play in the puddles!!!!
