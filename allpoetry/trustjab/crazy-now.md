I am crazy now since you drugged me with your scented perfume.
Let me be I don't want to go home with you.
I guess it's too late for that.

Chugalug it,
down it all the way.
Remembering the texture of a day.
When your anguish falls over you,
do you cry,
do you cry?

I'm so crazy now that you're obsessed.
From a day ago to now you've progressed,
and it won't stop.
Ms. Babylon...

Will you get used to,
the feeling of another day?
When the ties turn
will it stop?
You'll get used to the feeling 
of your pride,
of your pride...

From education,
to civilizations;
you talk about everything.
And you won't stop,
i'm going crazy now just thinking about it.
You tell me your all about it...
but I won't drop,
cus you won't stop.

Let it go,
let it flow
you won't stop.
Crush my heart with a hammer,
make a purer one, Babylon.
Let the kind consciousness 
yes come unto you,
let me go,
cus you won't stop.
There is one thing I will say on life
we are living, don't let it stop.
I'm going crazy now that you're gone
and I thought that this was a crazy life we were living when we were one.

But in all it just goes to show that you are what you say.
But that won't keep me alive today.
It's crazy now that we're together,
you're here to stay, this time forever.This is a different form of poetry the rhyme scheme is set on an offbeat...enjoy
