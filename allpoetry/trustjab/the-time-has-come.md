The time is now.
It has come upon me from above.
I have been showered with your love.
The expression is wow.


I know one thing is true;
That is the way you love me.
I know what I must do...
Hold you so we were meant to be.


There is only one thing that I find wrong;
The way your ex used to beat you.
I promised you many things and have always kept true.
My word is my word and I don't intend to go back.
This time I will prove it to be a fact.
You know I love you more than life;
So I asked if you'd be my wife.
Take my hand in marraige.
Be the one that I hold on to forever.
When you ask if i'll harm you, I promise you never.

The time has come for you to understand.
That I wanted to always be your man.
I always do anything and everything I can.
You are my life and my world.
Your are my wife and my girl.
