writer: "trustjab"

It doesn't matter what you say
bullshit!
You keep on livin everyday
don't trip!
You lie psycho trippin.
Then die psycho trippin.
Tell me what I wanna hear...
Tell me what's in mind.
Tell me that you got no fear...
I'll tell you that's a lie.
Those lies, psycho trippin.
Live lies psycho trippin.
Your steady psycho trippin.
