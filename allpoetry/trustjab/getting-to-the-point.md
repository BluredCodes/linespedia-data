There's one thing i'd like to tell yall
people just don't understand where I am coming from
with this poetry it's like I don't exist
no one stops to comment anymore
people just move along

I take the time to write
but it's not appreciated
why must you be hardheaded, josiah
why must you persist on something stupid
how long will you last in this hateful world
and why haven't you found the right girl

I tell you one thing my fellow people
I am not all that and a stick of butter
I am not fresh off the meet rack I know
I am not sure how to put it
but frankly I think you dissagree
especially with the way I think
life is too short to live alone
expect the unexpected
it's give cus it's real
lie, cheat and steal
rat, nark or squel
snitches get stiches
bitches get ditches



my point of view has been noticed but you still don't understand
getting to the point of things is not my strong point
patience ain't one of my virtues
and racism is in fact something I hate
don't get me wrong I don't like racist people
In fact I hate myself sometimes for saying stupid shit like that
my life ain't no walk through the park
and I for sure ain't no nark
probabtion parole pti...whatever it is stalling you
don't let it get through
not to the inside
it will eat away at your brain
you'll be in so much mental pain
don't you figure that I am capable of being leader of this site
just as you are?
stop and think about what you've been saying
don't you wish someone was there listening

how many questions do I have to ask before I close the story?
how many lives are at stake in a war over some stupid towers that were built by man
how many more members do I have to kill from the ku klux klan?
getting to the point, I need you to feel me
baby I need you to love me
I need you to squeeze me
I need you to hug me
I need you to be with me
I need you to bug me
I need communication
I need your facial
I need
I want
I crave
I strive
I live
I die
I cry
I wish
I lie
I play
I work
I duck
I fuck
I suck
I am stuck getting to the point
