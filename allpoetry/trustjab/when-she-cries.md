I miss everything we used to doI miss everything even youSometimes I cry for you at nightSometimes I wish you knew I had to fightI wish I could tell you I struggledI begged god for another chance with youJust once I had to make it rightWhen she cries at nightAnd she doesn't think that I can hear herShe tries to hideAll the fears she fears insideSo I pray this timeI can be the man that she deservesBecause I die, a little each timeWhen she criesAll of the tears she's cryingJust once I'd like to make itLast more than just one nightBecause she's all I ever hadAnd when she left me cryingGod damn I felt so sadJust once, I'd like her to see I'm dyingAnd I want more than her love I want it all from her tonightWhen she cries at nightAnd she doesn't think that I can hear herShe tries to hideAll the fears she fears insideSo I pray, this timeI can be the man that she deservesBecause I die, a little each timeWhen she criesWhen she cries at nightand she doesn't think that I won't hear herWhen she tries to hide,all of the fears she feels insideSo I pray that this timeI can be the man that she deservesBecause I die a little each timeWhen she cries
