Hold me tight and don't let go,
hold me throught the night.
I want to hold you too so you know
wrap your arms around me;
whisper in my ear,
tell me you love me.
Tell me things I want to hear
whisper things I want to come true
whisper things you want to do

Hold me close and rub me gently
touch me wherever you want don't be afraid
Don't be scared to get a little crazy
everyone has there dreams and there wishes
everyone has there times alone and times together
whisper things that you haven't told anyone else yet
I'll hold you close you'll have no regret
my love for you is greater than anything else
theres something you don't seem to know
that's the things that I don't do
I'm not an abuser, and I don't take you for granted
your love was sent here to me and for that I am grateful
don't let anyone else know or they'll become hateful

Jealousy will ring it's bell
the odor of hate will have it's smell
wrap your arms around me baby
were never gonna survive unless
you get a little crazy
we can't sit around and be lazy
