You and I
You and me
me and you

How many ways can I put it?
How many days?
How many ties?
How many lies?

How can you say you do
When you don't want me to
You don't want my love
You know you aren't my dove

I don't care anymore it's not true
But what else can I do
Say a little prayer for you

How many times will I decide,
To proclaim what you said a lie?
How many ways can I die
Without you by my side

How many lines can I go through
Just to think of an excuse to be with you
How many times can I say I love you
And not mean a word
Because the feeling isn't there

I hope you are aware
That I did not care
Before, but now is a different story
Some people stay together for a year
Some people hold eachother dear

But that is not the situation
With us a new exclamation
A different dedication
dedicated to the arguments and lies

Dedicated to the wars and the tears we cry
Dedicated to you Ashlea,
Because this was never meant to be
There was and never is a you and me
