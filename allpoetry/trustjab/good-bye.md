i said i loved you, you said it to
i should have known it wasn't true
you lied and played me for a fool
i didn't know love could be so cruel
you cheated me, broke my heart
and that's why we fell apart
so many nights i would cry
when i thought of you and i
i thought we'd always be together 
me and you now and forever
we said our love would never die
but we were wrong fairwell good-byeAugust 3rd, i wrote this peom when my boyfriend broke up with me
