One thing leads to another.
And another thing bleeds.
Aren't I worthy of thy blessings?
Or has false judgement clouded the mind?
Aren't I worthy of thy blessings?
Or hast thee awakened to false senses?
If this be, let the kind cosciousness yes
come unto you,
then you will know...
And it won't stop.

From ions to eons we strive.
In depth, their pools of hatred we dive.
And we live and so we die,
aren't I worthy of thy blessings?

For ions to eons we become,
one.
