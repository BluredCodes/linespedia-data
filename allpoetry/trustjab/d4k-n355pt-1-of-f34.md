I see now what you meant by this
I can't feel my legs cus the numbness
I'm slipping into darkness

I wanna do what's right
be kind to every single body

Open up to light
just let it blow through my body

Sometimes i'm unsure
I tell my baby
Sometimes i'm unsure
I tell my baby
i'm slipping into darkness
i'm falling into darkness
i'm creeping into darkness, yes
I don't want to start this, yes
Attracted to the darkness

If positive with love, just see the good in everybody
But sometimes i'm unsure
sometimes i'm unsure
something going on inside my heart
I can feel it pull apart
will the sky try to pull it in deeper into my arms

I'm slipping into darkness
I'm creeping into darkness
I don't want to start this
Attracted to the darkness
Yes...
