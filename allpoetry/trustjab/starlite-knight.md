---
title: "starlite knight"
writer: "trustjab"

riding through the galaxy on a metal horse
he sets himself on a planetary course
the stars that shine on him so white
the glistening in his eyes so bright
the starlite knight the man of your dreams
the starlite knight listens to your means
he can show you the world in it's glory
he can live your life like it was a story
I will show you the time has come
and then you'll realize that i'm the one

when you see me flying through outer space
remember we'll meet at another time and place
I will be your knight in shining armor
I will place my hand across your chest
and hold you tightly, promise to love you my best

and when he whispers in your ear under the velvet sky
he'll tell you secrets that will make you want to cry

he'll love you with every breath he takes
and i'll love you with every claim I stake

let the knight come unto you
let the light shine on through you
