title: "when we die"
writer: "trustjab"

When we die,
angels will run in the sky...
When we dance
angels will run and hide.
When we cry
we'll break down and die.

Will he love you,
like I loved you,
will he cherish you,
like I would do.
Will he kiss your mouth till you cry?
Will he be there when you die?

When we dance 
angels will run and hide.
I can love more than life
if you'll only be my wife,
when we dance...
angels run and hide
when we dance
angels will run and hide.

Will he hold you,
like I hold you?
Will he be there when you cry?
Like I was there when you cried.
Will he run to you,
like I ran to you before you tried?
Will he comfort you when your sad?
Will he share the same feeling when your glad?

If I could break down this whole world,
then you'd understand I want you to be my girl.
If I could run away and hide with you,
There's nothing else I would rather do,
than still love you.


When we die,
angels run and hide.
When we dance,
angels will reminisce.
I will love you more than life,
if you'll only be my wife.
I will love more than life, 
if you'll only be my wife.

Will he scold you?
Will he hit you on your face?
Will he then want to embrace?
Will he force himself upon you?
If he does i'll be there just for you.

When we dance,
angels rejoice in their skys.
When we die,
i'll see you in the afterlife.
