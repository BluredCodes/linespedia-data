---
title: "citys of the plain"
writer: "trustjab"
slug: "citys-of-the-plain"

I see the cauldron rising
across the citys of the plain
there's no swimming in the heavy waters
or singing in the acid rain
I watch the storm blow through
I wonder what I should do
the world weighs on my shoulders
and what am I to do
it doesn't make a difference what i'm going through
especially not to you
I see the cauldron riding
across the citys of the plain
black and white's a passing
red and blues a game
but did you forget about our special name
red alert aboard the enterprise
as I look deep in your eyes
we were running red lights
across the city's of the plain
incomplete
you can't compete
we open the red and blue doors
we need someone to talk to and someone to sweep the floors
I crash into you playing our little game
across the citys of the plain
