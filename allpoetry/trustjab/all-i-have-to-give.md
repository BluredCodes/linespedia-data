Everything I have is for you and you alone

All of my love, I share, I want you to own

I wish you were closer to the essanse of my heart

Always here, never far apart

I want you to know how much of you, I adore

You are the only one my heart beats for

I want you to know that I will always love you, for life

Whether or not you become my wife

All I have to give is my love

You are my angel

You're the only one I adore

You're the one that my heart beats for

Evermore,

All I have to give is my love



For Stephanie 
with love
