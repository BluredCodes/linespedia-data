Time elapsed for content to whatever it may be
I hoped you'd take one long look at me
am I everything you hoped it'd be
just when you thought I was caving in
I broke your back and said I win
I started this as fast as I could
sorry for the inconvienience but I would,
Still love you if time permitted
if you want more peace for your buck there's no luck
But if you make me smile, 
then it just might be worth your while
