I was born a believer
played the fool
lonely dreamer, left to choose
A page in a story,
A romance untold
sharing secrets we call, our own
One book of dreams
take this heart ache away from me
like a page in a story, romance untold
sharing these secrets we call our own
holding eachother closely, holding near
tightly bonded, take away, my fear

when shadows fall,
after the hurt is gone
through it all
we love and we lose
holding on,
even after having to choose
being strong,
it's you, the bond that I lost

When the drama is over
the war hasn't gone
when the hurting is burning
remember the day we fought
and won one over
with One book of dreams
you took this heartache 
away from meThis is something I go through daily, with you Rachel, and we took it and made it somethign it was not before.
