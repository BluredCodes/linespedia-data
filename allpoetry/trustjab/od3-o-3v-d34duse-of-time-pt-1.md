Tick, tick, tick the clock plunges your mind
Endlessly replaying times that were unkind
From a mere view there is no way you can tell
Curssed consciousness is your private hell
Trying to receive a message from some other being
Coping with the pain it's a subliminal message meaning,
there is blood on the tiles of the floor
there is a hand pushing open your door
a sprain in the wood that causes a splintering fall
down further until you reach rock bottom in awe
Watching the wood form over you, you're stuck
as the demons spread their darkness you're shit out of luck
I guess you could say you're fucked
Becoming darker and darker you scream
Trying not to remember a time so mean
Your screams will soon become faint
As your blood splatters the wall like paint

But everytime I turn the volume down
all that I hear is the deafining sound
of your hearts pound

With the words slaying me slow
the verbs that just don't flow
confusing use of time
A partial stoning of the heart
waiting for something to start
confusing use of time
Everytime I close my eyes
I see a vision of the devil start to rise
I can see a view of hell
standing next to you I fell
So it seems everytime I fall
the devil starts to call
By the time I go to turn around
all I hear is the same deafining sound
of your hearts pound

You can feel let down if you don't expect your world
Expect to lay awake there by your sleeping girl
Everytime I look at your face
I seem to remember that place
where the devil danced with you
And even though I push it down
I can't seem to push you around
This hellish image is too hard
for me to try and discard

Today, the longest day
Hearing repeating things you'd say
Confusing use of time
Can you hear the void that I describe
What could I say vacancy sounds like
But the trying and trying again
makes me think it's all gonna end
but I still I wander around
Even though the devils down
he left this angel town
I always manage somehow to find
the contract with Beelzebub I signed
But the trying again
makes me think it's all gonna end
Confusing use of time

When the ambition causes caution
will you want to take action
While still reading in closed caption
There's still bad things that could've happened
I'm caught in the devils maze
caught up in this daze
And i'm finally out of my body
The blue lights are now a blaze
in hell is where I spend the rest of my days
caught up in a confusing use of timeThis poem is about the life of living in hell, dreams and nightmares combined...living in closed caption. Caught in a confusing use of time.
