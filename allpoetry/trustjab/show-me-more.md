---
title: "'show me more'"
writer: "trustjab"

This is how it was supposed to be,
somewhere along the lines of you and me.
Until a dreadful thing happens then you'll see.
That it wasn't always the pain that led to other things.
Show me more than an attitude.

Of course you're my friend,
but you gotta meet me at the other end.
The line isn't thick enough to stand on,
you might just barely make it,
you might fall and break it.

Life isn't a game,
show me more than a name.
Give me time to bend the rules.
Time is an essence that we take for granted.
When it's up, what are you going to say,
I was just running out then, hey!

It can't go on

It won't be long

The voices are due

It won't go through

Can you hear the screeching tires

Along the track of life?

Make sure the voices bring you along safely...
guide me to my safety
bring me the light
make my life worth something more,
make her better not such a whore.
There's too much to expect,
especially if it's unexpected.
It's your mind that was rejected.

See me for who I am,
see me for what I believe
See what I tried to achieve?
Go to your corner and bleed
parents abusive...
heart conclusive
show me more than that

show me more than an act...
Show me more than a fact
When it's all over,
imagine that
