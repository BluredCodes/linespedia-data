You're my Angel
You're the only one I adore
You're the one that my heart beats for
evermore,
Angel
You're my Angel

Angel,
You're my angel
Yes the only one I adore
And the only one my heart beats for
evermore,
Angel

You're my angel

And since you came to me
from heaven above

You took my hand
filled my lonely heart
With your tender love

You love me with all heart and soul
You're the one that makes me whole,
this you know
Angel
You're my angel


You're my angel
The only one I adore
You're the one that my heart beats for
evermore, Angel

You're my,
AngelThis is dedicated to my girl Rosa Marie Morralis. She knows I love her with all my heart and all my soul. She is the one who makes me whole. She will never get to see this, but I will tell it to her when I see her this weekend. if you ever had a feeling of love for someone so strong within the first few days you started going out, it must be real love. Infatuation feels differently. Since I haven't even kissed her lips yet. Understand the power of a feeling?
