lemme start off by saying lol is the crapiest word i've ever heard
it makes no sense if you're a new comer to the chat scene
I happen not to be a newcomer i've been in the game for years now
but lol everytime I hear that it triggers a wow
I wonder to myself, hmm lol does that make any god damn sense
I don't think it does, laughing out loud...ok
and *L* for laughs, I never used that I always put *laughs*


I don't laugh online too much though
and still I wonder what the hell is *rotflmfao*?
does it make any sense?
I think not, i'm not proper i'm black
And that my friends and my niggaz is a fact
we thugs don't use *L* or *LOL* or *ROTFLMFAO*
no we use, what up? wasup? yo...


the thugish terms and those of you fake thugs out there that use them
get off it man
this isn't the 90's anymore
and these stupid god damn internet whores
what's the point
where's my fuckin joint?
