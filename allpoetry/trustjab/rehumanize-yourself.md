So where in time could we place ourselves,
and what ever happened to the place itself?
God forsaken placed it's welts!
You've got to humanize yourself

Policeman put on his uniform,
got to have a gun just to keep him warm
because violence here is a social norm.
You've got to humanize yourself

Where on earth is your factory?
The place where you produce love for me.
Girl there's a reason that I don't see
You've got to humanize yourself

I know you you know well of my love for you,
That's why I ask what else can I do?
I don't know why you said two.
You've got to humanize yourself

Sometime in the future you'll remember me,
Asking yourself where on earth can I be?
Maybe it's a place I picked for thee.
You've got to humanize yourself

Tell yourself maybe someday he'll be.
Hoping for your love to come back to me.
Must be a reason that I can see.
You've got to humanize yourself
