There's no one that could kill you like she does.
No one could leave you so alone.
I met this girl the other day at the mall.
She gave me her number so I could call.
Now this girl was the best lookin I ever seen.



I knew I had to get her but I wasn't mean.
I had to split time in half just to see her.
I had to run just so I could meet her.



This is the best day of my life.
God damn, were man and wife.
So tell her that we, shed her leave.
Your freedom of speech you just have to believe.
Your people ear this creed, somehow somewhere.
Your poking within with the lions scare.
Remove yourself, if you dare.



Here we go as my mind flows,
and eventually I conceive to try to piece
together why i'm here.
Look into my own damn eyes and ears,
so I sever that I believe in
I always re-ever.


If it's not good,
am I getting better?
If it's not real,
am I just severed?
So take a look at the mess your in.
Because it's placed next of kin.
You're trying to cut back time,
before the deadline.



Because the place your in
is no place like home.
