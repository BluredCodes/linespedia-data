When I come home, leave it to me
Maybe then you will understand

I was the one
Holding on to your man

Let me go just do not let me drop
Hold on to it

Maybe then you will understand
where do they go

Leave it to me,
then you will see

I was the one
holding onto what I can

But if you let me go
I will fall deeper than you know

It is not an expression
It is how much I care

It is not regression
It is a dare

I know you live
Because if you were not alive

I would die
Leave it to me in my own creedAn edit from a very old poem that I wrote.
