You're not the only one with feelingsI feel the same way tooStuck like gluealways came throughAnd though I will be always there right up till the endI want you to know it's not cus you are my girlfriendThis is me, and I would do it for anybodyIf you can think it, you can seeThat's why I believed one day you'd be mineAnd to this very day we still hold handsThis is me, and my heart still has plansTouch me, squeeze me and don't let me goI don't want to ever feel like you said noI am glad I got you right where you belongAnd I am glad that you still sing that songit rings in my ears, the voice is still in my headI still hear your voice calling me, things that you saidThese are the things that connect me to youIt's all because of those things that you doIt's me that always wanted tostuck like gluealways came throughThis is me this is youWhat am I supposed to thinkWhen am I supposed to blinkCus I don't wanna miss a thing
