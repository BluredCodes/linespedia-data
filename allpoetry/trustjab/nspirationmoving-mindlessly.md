At the end of the day when you can't relax
And still you think you just might collapse
And physically you acke
just like a cavity
Well then maybe you might realize
your inspiration is moving mindlessly
Your time was not well spent psychologically
And your lies have just become a reality

Yeah I am really amazed at this fall out shit
It is not imbiguous,
it is continuous
The continuous life there is no end
Moving through life movin through death

When the pain finally recedes
And still your body aches like a cavity
Yeah I am really amazed at this fall out shit
But at the end of the day, you still can't relax
You have physical and mental body attacks
Then you wonder when you will collapse
When you find out you have no inspiration from that fall out shit
You come up with the idea of a premenition
Your own slave to someones recognition
Some time I recognized that this life will decay
And I will fall out from this shit
And there won't be another way

Cus all in all
It's not imbiguous
it be continuous
It's all about us
It's for real my man
The continuous life there is no end
Moving through life, moving through death
Running out of time, running out of breath

I move through these cycles to be purified
But the gospels are not stupified
my inspiration is gone
My declaration had flown
Out the window it go
Along with my life and the hand in which it was carried, yo
