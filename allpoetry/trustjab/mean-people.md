But mean people I say choke on it,
gagging all the way.
Do you remember the texture for a day?
You'll get used to the feeling of your pride
of your pride, of your pride...
But you won't notice until it's gone.
And you'll come back for it some other time.
Chugalug it chugalug it time after time,
time after time, will do you fine
Learning your lesson, Learning your lesson,
will do you fine, will do you fine.
But mean people I say chocke on it gagging all the way
Will you remember the texture for days
and you'll get used to the feeling of your pride,
make them die, make them die.
This will remind you of another time.
When mean people were fine.
