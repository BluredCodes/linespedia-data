Only a little while now until the end of the world
Will I remember then what happened to everything
(in general)it all makes perfect sense once you piece it together
life ends and starts, you only live once so
when the world is running down, you make the best of what's still around
It only makes sense to run free and be what you want to be
Like a little boy and girl in the play ground then a drive by shooting at some niggaz playin ball.
It all makes sense(in general)
You see if I were to die today or tomorrow, it wouldn't make much difference
I mean i'm going to die anyway, correct, of course
So if I slit my throat or my wrists would that matter
How would a blod clot consist, how would I be killed
No the question is how will I be killed
Cancer, is that the answer? It took my mother
Why doesn't it take my brother?
Sometimes I wish my father would die too, just so I can be happy without him on my back all the time.
But then wishing death upon another is a sin, a crime if you must
I'm sure nobody wants anybody to die, as if you would say
There are psychos, killers, winners losers, all in the race of life
There are believers in destiny, objects of discrimination hate and other things that go along that line in general.
But as for me, let me live my life to the fullest, enjoy what I want to enjoy, then by all means you may take my life in your hands
If it pleases you(in general)
