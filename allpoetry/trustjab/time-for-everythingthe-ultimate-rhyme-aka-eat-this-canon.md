I see your crimes and boy your not original
I see your face in the times and boy your not original
Canon might as well give it up now
Because I have become the best now
You can't even compete with this lyrical test now
Then you wanna go around and claim that you're the best now
When I lyrically dis you, I blow you like a tissue c'mon the best even say wow especially to this right here, have no fear josiah is here to put a time for everything including the ultimate rhyme that will(whale) and shall stand to the end of time. When I shoot through your ass with this fast paced action placed well rhymed well faced little song writin, rhyme bitin thing I got goin on right here, then you'll understand why you cowered in fear.

You see the evil have no beats, have no beef
not with this high class
kick your white or black ass
see you in hell cus I passed
nigga on the east side doin his thing
up and at it in full swing
knockin your ass down
further and further into the ground
That's why I have every right to say
That your shit is was and will be fake
next mother fucker come up and still be late
his shit well arranged but still out of placed

And this one right here goes out to all of yous doin your thing
And for all of you poets and poetesses
or all you fake ass niggaz and bitches that just wanna live
Live good
live free
cus I be
and still stand firm as a tree
the only doin his thing here till and past two thousand and three
it's about to reach 600 degrees
pick my hair out let it flow in the breeze
Cus the time for everything makes the best which is was and will be me
For all yall thats chillin'
chill hard
work hard and don't discarde
you might someone pointin' at you sayin en garde!
