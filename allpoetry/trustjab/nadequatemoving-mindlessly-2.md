It would be such a mess
If this disease actually progressed
it might wind up in someones chest
Fuckin things up, tryin' it's best

I feel like a new man
It is inadequate if you can
Try to feel me and tell me my friend
Is it everything you hoped it would be

Is it not a tradgedy
To see such inadequacy
To look upon such fright
And see the murder of a bloody night

I do not understand
So I intiate a plan
Holding back everything I can
So tell me my friend

Do you feel like a new man
You are inadequate such a mess
Can you feel the heart pump in your chest
Does it burn, does it ahce, watch it explode

That is how you make me feel
Woman, unbound this load
That has been carried upon me and bestowed
Feel the pressure rise

Peers peering into eiry eyes
People watching you from behind
Clawing at your back until you turn around
And you think you are insane when no body was found

At the murder site I switched bodies
I made the wrong choice and switched lanes
On the freeway I had a thought that made me change
It was inadequate yet it still made sense

Steady searching in the nightly stocks
Homies shootin up they glocks
Life is full of these bonds
Inadequate noises from the grave and beyondPart 2 and you thought I was done, no, like I told you I can't be stopped.
