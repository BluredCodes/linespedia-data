I say fuck your gifts
fuck your stupid shit

I say fuck your holidays
fuck your pay

I say fuck this Christmas
fuck this shit

The continous life
there is no end
movin through life 
movin through death

It's not imbiguous
it be continuous

I say fuck this stupid holiday
fuck this place

I don't get gifts from you
fuck you too

Christmas has no meaning
so fuck the beginning

Fuck the 12 days we have off
fuck everything, just scoff

You wanna take it furhter
let's take that love further

You wanna feel fuckin special
we should say how we feel

Well i'm homeless
and my brothers gotta home

For this Christmas do something!
More than 2000 years of fuckin wit our fears
It's time I looked out amongst my peers
I gotta get off my feet and make a fuckin difference
Set aside my pain and the indifference

Christmas ain't a holiday till I start
To move my move in my heart
