Unable to cope with your own problems... 
Tossed aside
Forgetting about everything we have been through...
Put away
Losing touch with your only friend...
Left astray

As if I were an animal of some sort
But remember the times at Leaports
When we had a ball with our friends
And we used to think that it would never end
Seems like yesterday, 
time has caught me by surprise
We left eachother,
but never realized

You know how much I cared
what about the love we shared
everyday when I used to stare
How many times I was dared
But you never were aware

I guessed it would be a test
when there is nothing left to remember
Think about our last September
And still to this day it would make a difference
to do it all again,
as a friendThis is the beginning part to a series of love and friendship poems, which will or might have a tragic ending. I tryed to switch the rhyme scheme so it didn't seem to much like a song. I put it in the story category because that's basically what it talks about. My story.
