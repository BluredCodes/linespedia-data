If this were to be the last day of my life
Let me spend it with you
Cus you're the only thing I knew
especially by my heart
my prisoner we'll never be apart

Let me hold you tight
show me the light
take me away from the darkside
take away my fear
a blackhole shinin from the skies above
Bacterial plain
You think you can fly you're insane
Hold me close give me love

Dark shadows follow me everywhere I go
You're still the only one I know
So if this is my last day alive
take me away from the darkside
Twilight zone, twilight zone
i'm floating in the dark alone
Is there any love out here
I will never know
