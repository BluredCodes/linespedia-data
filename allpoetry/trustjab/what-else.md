What else can be said about the way you care for me?
I look at you and wonder, can this be a dream?
I understand everything now.
You showed me the way how.
You guided me with a single touch,
you made me laugh made me cry, made me blush
I cry everyday now that you're gone.


I hope one day you'll come back.
Because you're all I ever had.
What else in this world can compare to you,
and all of the special things you do.
There's nothing else that I need.
One time you ran from me.
I remember it clearly.


A year ago and a year today.
And I don't care what you say.
What else could compare to you?
What else did we have besides caring, compassion and love?
What else in this universe could care for you like I did.
It's not even a price to pay even for the highest bid.
If I had a choice I would never let you leave.


That's why today I ask you come back to me.
I earnestly beg and ask please.
Can't you look and see?
What else would this man be doing on his knees?
I love you and I want you back.
You of all people know this for a fact.
I need you in my life that you must understand.
What else could I be besides your man?


I love you
I want you
I need you
please...
understand where I am coming from.
What else is left to say?
You heard the stories and I don't play.
I already lost you the ultimate price to pay.
