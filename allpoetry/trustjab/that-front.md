You can't tell me one thing and do another without frontin'
I know you better than that gril, you nothin'

To me it's just another crazy ideal
But isn't it ironic, that you still have ideals
And I still have my heart

Can you make it past the lies that you told
Would you ever reconsider me to unfold

Is it just a front again?
Man, not another one of these lies
I about had it with the families ties

And yo, just so you know
I am through with you
I can't take anymore
Yeah I'm a walk through the door

And I'm a yell at the top of my lungs
Yes! Freedom!
No more of That Front!Reminds me of trying to get the truth outah a girl, but all she does is front, change the subject and never accept the facts.
