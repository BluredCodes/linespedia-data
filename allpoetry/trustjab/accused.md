I should let you know before you try
That i'm not the one who's going to cry
Is there an automatic blame for this
Or what is it that I missed
What did I do wrong for you to accuse me
I'm an innocent man can't you see
Before you stick the blame on someone else
Take a good look at yourself
I was accused of wrong but won't admit
To the crime that I did not commit
I'm not a cheater and so i've learned
That cheaters never prevail and always get burned
