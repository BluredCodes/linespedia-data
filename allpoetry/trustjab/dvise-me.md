If you wish upon the right star
then you might say the chances are
but then again if you don't wish
then what are you serving in your dish?

What rules follow the preceding
advise me before I slip up once again
it could mean my life or my next of kin
in a galaxy full of light
i struggle with this fight
there is one thing I will say on life
we are livin
let it flow
cuz it won't stop
and when the kind consciousness
come unto you
you will know
cuz it won't stop

when I wipe the sleep from my eyes
I wonder and lerk on why
you have chosen me to lead this whole army
of losers and lamers like I
advise me as to what I am to do next
I seem lost without a word

when I wipe the sleep from my eyes
will my fondest dream please come true

crush my heart with a hammer
make a purer one
Babylon
the leader of this whole army
the refugees and escapees ruin me
down to the dust I go crush me with your foot
cus I won't drop dead right away

advise me one more time as to why I am living
let me go
let it flow
I won't stop for the world
the world won't stop for me
so let me keep on going
I have to keep on flowing
let my mind sink further and further

release the feeling of your pride
of your pride
it won't stop
advise me why
