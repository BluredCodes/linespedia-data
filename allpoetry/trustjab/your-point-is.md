title: "your point is?"
writer: "trustjab"

You told me to look at your skin;
your point is?
You told me to look at your outfit;
and your point is?
He told me to look at his new shoes;
your point is?
He said that I have the same pair in the other color;
and your point is?
I looked at her skin;
and still your point is?
I looked at her outfit;
but your point is?
I looked at his new shoes;
but your point is?
I looked at the other pair he had;
still your point is?
Well my point is;
I'm colorblind smartass....
