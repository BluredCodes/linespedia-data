So where's my best friend
The girl who stayed till the end

So where's the one I love
The girl who's from above

Just holding onto me
Stay with me
With me

I give the world to you
All that I want to do

If you want to try and prove
Your love has basically moved
Try once more because the last time I didn't get
The truth, the words that I did regret
Holding onto me
Hold on girl and stay
Stay with me
with me

So where's my best friend
The girl who stayed till the end
It's holding onto you
I wanted to do
All for you
All that I have
was just you
just you

Holding onto you
just stay with me
with me
All that I have is for you
it's the basic things you do
That makes me love you
I want you
like a drug in my vein
I want you, like a bearer wants pain
You're all that I have



Dedicated to the one, Rachel
