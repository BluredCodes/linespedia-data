Everything's fucked up!
I want a lot of shit 
But that I never get

In 1993 I didn't want my mom to pass
But the devil took her from me in class

In fact this is personal shit
Grab the cash and run for it

Get the benefits don't give a shit or a care
Never have a girlfirend or any friends to share

I don't want it to become drama
Stealing Happy Hours for my momma

From slave trading to other fucked up things
Hold your head up high while throwing things

The meek will inherit the earth
So i've learned from the bible on my turf

You can't fuck with my style of writing!
My angst poetry is to hell and back for typing

I post my lyrical devotion
I post in mystical emotion

In this day of $19.98 plus shipping and handleing
We got the money problems to deal wit, it's not funny

It's fucked up what I can say and do
But guess what it's not for you!This is my life, enjoy...I would put it in beat but it's personal, if this sucks fuck you too...I really don't give a shit naymore, I write how I feel like!
