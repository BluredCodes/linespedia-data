Nigga I wouldn't work for the love of my set
For the coke put some snubs in yo' vest
For the doe put some slugs in yo' chest
You fuckin' with vets
I went from beatin niggaz down with pulleys and bats
To heating niggaz down in hoodies with gats.

There ain't no heart in the way we do
We just kill niggaz and run right through
Pull out da' glocks and keep on da' move
Sprayin niggaz while a.k.'in' niggaz
I hate The Hard Times of life
But I gotta family this kid and my wife
Requitted love would be da worst thing to do
Especially if I got killed by a grime nigga like you
Fuck them thug niggaz we keepin it gangsta
Fuck them blood niggaz we crippin it pranksta

When the gang wars start to collide
There's only room left for four or five 
Shit us niggaz gotta stay alive
Luckliy I was the last man standin
Or id'a been the last of a blood niggaz brandin
Tragic endin to a waste of time
A waste of life and a waste of lime
Gotta pull niggaz through just to get em back online

Fuck the frail shit that niggaz deal wit'
On the coke route we all share it
I get respect on any block whether it's the strip or da yard
Sold it all to the smokers, cooked it a soft
When I got my hands on the first pack of crack
I knew within the next year i'd adapt to da gat
How the a.k. sprays for my freedom
Niggaz please, the bloods, we dun need em

Josiah, Seth and Ean, my brothers pullin the choke
Killin yall niggaz, now yall Capone and Juv
And it's best on coke
It's a Requitted love for the set
Now let's see yall strong armin niggaz try to bully that
Ean cook up that snowy shit, and bring cookies back
While Seth gladly explain the game to you rookie cats
Hoes try to push me back, 
Bitch you only good for givin head in bed cus the pussys wack
Strip yo' deaden, if Yo' gunman don't rap
Shit, nigga don't be dumb enough to put up shop with no scat
Cus only foolish cats move in packs
Niggaz spreadin news to rats
While me, I show improve through rapThis is my new hot shit, this is prolly my best rap yet, besides the one that I got pissed off at, when yall never gave it five stars. Well tell me this one don't sound good and yall rate this shit how it deserves.
