Fuck the frail shit...
when my cash come in they gotta use the scale
they weigh the whales wit'
When I split your shit 
trying not to make thoughts your aim shit
trying to plot the same shit
makin sure you down with the say my name shit
tryin to bust on sayin 'Who you playin games wit?'
Fuck the losers
fuck the broad shit and the false accusers
You gonna take it,
and we gonna make it,
Pull your guns out, start shootin up niggaz cus they say shit
The life of a gansta makes it hard for me(may)
To live my fucking life day by day
Fuck the whole sale,
because it's all bullshit prices from the same retail
you better run for it
and try not to make thoughts your aim
because life's a joke a big fuckin game


That's all bullshit
because life means more to me than some old hoe shit
Fuck your drug routes
Because the trees is the weed that I feed on every dirty south plantation
Fuck this nation
I be tryin not to make dirty thoughts my aim while on probation
Kiss the game goodbye
because your shit is weak and you just smallfries,
you can't compare to the big leauge niggaz they call super size.
What you sayin?
You best be prepared to start prayin
Fuck you hataz
because I'm that type of nigga that ain't playin
Trying not to make thoughts your aim,
because the same niggaz that think too much start playin games.
They be the same crew that was bustin on your shoes
How many years ago? I'd say about two
Fuck your homicide
because you made thoughts your aim and started your own film 'Genocide
After that bullshit you the one that said too much and tried
That same fuckin day nigga you pulled out that gun and cried
You the same false nigga that claimed his life in suicide


So we gonna make it, 
we gonna make it, we not gonna take it
I bring this shit up about to the next level
So about-face mother fuckers this is the leader of the next rebels
The same nigga that was on the corner doin his do
Next nigga went by and came right through
brought the shipment from upstate New York
I'm not playin with them forks
That's what we call them niggaz
Cus they got the name from the same crew that eat at China
Sippin on the better things in life we call finer(finna)
I had not always been-
too far ahead, I heard mad niggaz had passed they next of kin
Stop thinkin too hard
cus makin thoughts your aim will draw bad cardThis was a song written by me using the Jadakiss beat, we gonna make it, hence I say we gonna make it in it.
