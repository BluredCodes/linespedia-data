Easy to Bitch
Easy to whine
Easy to Cry

Harder to work
Harder to strive
Harder to live
Harder to die
Harder to try to feel alive

Is it a bitch
Is it a whine
Is it a moan
Is it a cry
Is it a snitch
Is it a crime
Is it trying to feel alive

Harder to work 
Harder to try
Harder to live
Harder to die
Harder to try to feel alive

Those who bitch
Those who whine
Are the same
Who try to live
Who try to stay alive
