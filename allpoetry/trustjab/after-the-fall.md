If I had to choose between the twoI probably would be undecidedBut one thing I know for sureis that your love is undividedEven though you make me sick at timesyou know I still love you through thick and thinOne thing that I know for sureis that the love comes from withinThe choice is not too hardBecause I know who to discardThe love that comes from withinhas pulled me through thick and thinI know the choice is yourslet me in, it's not to lateThrough everything togetherwe had a quarrel not a debateAfter the fallwe had it come crashing downIt wasn't your faultOur love just broke downAfter The Fall
