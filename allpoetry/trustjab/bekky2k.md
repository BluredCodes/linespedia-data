All I want to do, hanging on to you
it's all I want to do, now you
Now you'll runaway,

hold on girl and stay
stay with me, with me...

So how's my best friend? please don't let it end
a smile was said to mend, mend you
What won't go your way, think out loud and stay

stay with me, with me...

It's all the silly things to be, 
holding onto this feels like a spell
and you'll know you're mine if it's true
only for you, only for you

so hold on girl and stay
stay with me, with me...I just thought i'd write a lil friendly love song for you Bekky...
