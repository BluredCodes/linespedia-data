title: "you are the one"

You are the one for me,
and I am the one for you
What else more is there to it

I give you everything a girl could want
but yet you still complain
I tell you I love you and you think i'm playing

How much more of this do I have to take
before my heart will break
how much pain are you going to put me through

Before I end up not knowing what to do
how much is at stake if I stay with you
How long is this relationship going to last?

If you keep on pushing me to the limit
I think I just might break...
I don't wish to hurt you but our loves at stake

I don't wish for anything to happen to us
but it's the pain that I can't endure
I loved you in the past but I don't know anymore

How much of this harrassment do I have to fake
you are the one that makes the mistakes
You are the one that always takes

You're not the one for me
