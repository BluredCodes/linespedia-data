Sunshine,
are you outside
I wonder if you hear my voice
it's a simple baffled choice


Sunshine,
why are you sleeping?
gotta get outside


Sunshine,
are you over?
Over the river and through the woods
Over the streets of bad neighborhoods


My sunshine,
are you outside?
Wanna get outside...I just made up some song about this girl who be imbiguous.
