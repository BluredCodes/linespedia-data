title: "choices"

there are many decisions we have to make
there are too many paths we have to take
there are mad thoughts coming from everyones head
there are mad vibes coming from everyones bed
there are choices that have to be made
there are bills that have to be paid
there are dreamers that want their dream to come true
there are cars on this highway of life coming through
there are so many choices it causes confusion
there are too many choices to have one conclusion
