The longest day i'll ever live
The longest word I have to say
The time has come and it won't come at any other moment than now
I leave the world in all it's glory thinking of a way to use my time
The introduction very slow
As I ascend to heaven through the snow
I feel like a cavity
The pain is hard to bare
Even though I know i'm there
The pain won't recede
The fear of being told to leave

I have no control over what must be
with the words slaying me slow
the verbs just don't flow
being told to go
partial stoning of the heart
But the trying again
makes me think it's all gonna end

But everytime I turn the volume down
All that I hear is the deafining sound
of your hearts pound

Do you want me to fucking cry?
Here I come make room for time
But the trying again
makes me thing it's all gonna end
confusing use of time
Do you hear the void I describe
This is what vacancy fucking sounds like!
I am all alone in my private hell
Wondering if it's all swell

When things are outah wack
and they might collapse
and at the end of the day
you still can't relax
and physically you ache
Well my friend I start to decay
I want to be free from this hell
A confusing use of time

Slowly I put the pressure into need
Cracking and breaking at the knees
It's not imbiguous
When we shed the skin we be continouous
The longest day
With the words slaying me slow
The answer is still a no
I put my mind to my wife
a confusing use of time

But everytime I turn the volume down
All that I hear is the deafining sound
of your hearts pound

Today the longest day
hearing repeating things you say
confusing use of time
can you hear the void I describe
What can I say vacancy sounds like
confusing use of time
