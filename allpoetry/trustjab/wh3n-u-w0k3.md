I remember the first time it came
The first time it rained, the first rhyme was sane
The last time she came, when I came out my mouth, it's was all about
This shit, this bitch and the last time it made, such a difference in my life that I knew I made this bitch I call boo my wife
I knew as soon as she went, under the knife, the blade sliced more than the skin, I knew she felt the pain within.
It's hard now since I been away from the scene about one or two dreams
Can you tell it when I say i love you, it's what I mean
I mean fuck it, all the rest didn't care, but I was there, through every step of the way, every step, every day, every corney every time that you play... it's all about the heart baby, and for this I wanna thank you
for being everything, and every dream that I had come true.

When you woke it was me,
when you had the dream it was me
everything mustah been meant to be
And every dream mustah been meant for me
everything that I say was meant for thee
Right up till the end will I be by your side
Fuck it ock, right there till you die.
Baby you is my lover and my boo,
And ya know, that i'd die for you
Bust a five or two, see the judge and lie for you

When you woke it was I, holding your hand and kissin your eyes
When I woke I seen, everything and allt he dreams I mustah meaned to be in a fantasy...Figure it out, ock...
