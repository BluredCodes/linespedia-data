So you runaway 
I told you all these things and yet you couldn't stay
wouldn't even stop to give the time of day
everything I said won't make these things ok
so don't stop to take the time away

because girl you mean so much to me
I wish that I could make you see
the way you wanted me to be
is just as easy as 1 2 3
across the world to far away
I knew the words to make you stay
of course it's what I didn't say 
still I knew the words to make you stay

you smile at me
and wave to me
I knew exactly where and when I want to be
and if I wait for this

so runaway
what were were all the times we could've had that day
I told you everything and you wouldn't stay
wouldn't even stop to give the time of day
and everything I said don't make this ok

so all I have to say is
girl you mean so much to me 
I wish that I could make you see
the way you wanted me to be
was just as easy as 1 2 3
across the world too far away
I knew the words to make you stay
of course it's what I didn't say
still I knew the words that would make you stay

you smile at me and wave to me
I knew exactly then and where I want to be
and if I wish for this
