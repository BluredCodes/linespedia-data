There's a wind blowing from the top of the highest high rise today;
it's not a breeze because it blows hard.
Not to have my fellowman discard.
I'm leaving the air behind me.
Could it be a dampening from my soul,
destroying my body whole?
If you watch closely you might learn.
Or else you might watch yourself burn.
Don't let this wind blow you down.
Don't discard it and stand around.
It's not a breeze cus it blows hard,
or should I leave in my wake a trail of tears?
I am so scared of my own fears.
The west wind take me away...
