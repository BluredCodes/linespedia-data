I see you
your selective amnesia

I see you
you're hurting your life

I see you
you're hurting your wife

but, I love you still...
I love you still

And although
you're beating the wall

I see you
standing proud and tall

I see you
pounding your children

I see you
your hate is a kildren

But I love you still

I see you
dedicating your songs

I see you
sharing your thoughts

I see you
hollering at the top of your lungs

And I love you still

And I love you StillThe view of someone who knows a lot about life. I see you, hurting beating raping, killing. Selectve amnesia. But I love you still.
