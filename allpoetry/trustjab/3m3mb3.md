I can remember,
a time when, it was better.

I wrote a letter,
to forget her forever

If you hear my voice you'll need to push it around
If you hear my voice you'll need to push down
If you hear my voice you'll watch it all come around

I can remember,
a time when, it was better

I wrote a letter,
to forget her, forever
