title: "Fought and won 1 Over"
writer: "trustjab"
slug: "fought-and-won-1-over"

I give the world to you.
All that I want to do.
If you believe the out crys,
we'll fall first and then you will die.
But I don't wanna play...
That game tonight.


I see all you were to me.
I fought and won for thee.
If you fall over the hedgeboard,
then maybe you'll notice henceforth.
That I don't wanna play,
that game tonight.


I give the best to you.
All that I want for you.
If you see me fall into,
A trap that leads to you...
Then promise me one thing,
you'll pull me out when the time has come.
Cus I don't need to be the rung.
I don't want to play that game tonight.

I fought and won one over,
all for you.
I fought and won one over,
because it was the thing to do.
I beat my chest in grief.
I even stood tall over the chief,
so if you wanna play...
I will have to say,
get out of here because we don't want you here.
And the time is near.
The time is here.

So please don't try to say,
you won in another way.




Dedicated to Ashlea-Josiah Beerle 1999
