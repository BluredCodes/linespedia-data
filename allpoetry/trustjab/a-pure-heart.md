---
title: "a pure heart"
writer: "trustjab"
slug: "a-pure-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

this is how a heart is to feel
love comes from every beat that's real
make it all the way through without leaving a touch unnoticed
this is how I feel when I am with you

my heart reaches out to touch yours
when I feel you embrace me for a while
I get used to the feeling for a smile
when you touch me I go crazy
because you're so hot
but it's not the looks that give me what i've got

the time has come to make a commitment
will you be there on the front line with me
when it's my time to die will you mourn
will you run and hide to be scorned
I got used to the feeling of your texture
I fluctuated for a minute within

but to assure you that I am still here
I cried
yes I cried
and I died inside
when you told me so
you don't love me although
I cried, then I died
I got used to the feeling of love
but heartbroken I left you, little dove

it's my pride
that died
I felt this way inside 
but never knew
that it would come from you
