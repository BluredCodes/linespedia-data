I will stand by youI will kiss away your tearsI will take away your fearsI will stand by youI will kiss away the painI will take away the rainI will stand by you foreverWe will stand togetherWe won't take this sitting downWe don't hardly make a soundWe won't make our lives harderBy being stupefiedWe won't hurt our ways togetherNo, we won't hurt our love this wayWe will stand together,We won't fall into our peersI can stand by you foreverI can hold you in the nightI won't give up on you babyI won't let you go without a fightI will let you know, I love youAnd that you're everything to meI will stand with you togetherWe will stand until, foreverAnd I can say this one for sureWe will stand together We will make our love so pureI will take your breath awayI will stand by you, foreverUntil you say we'll be togetherA repetitive verse of trying to get my point across. When I say something I will stick to it. Especially if it involves love.
