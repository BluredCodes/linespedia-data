I need you, tonight
There may be others but they're not around.
See your the lover that I want around.
And when I take you out on the town.
You strike that wild pose. 
You wanna be like some old hoe.
See you're all I've got tonight.
Yes you're all I've got.
You're all I've got tonight 
I need you, tonight
I want you, tonight

See there may be others but they're not around,
and when we go out, we go wild in the town.
We take it to the limit and push some more.
So I love you, I have told you before.
You're all I've got tonight, therefore...
You're all I've got tonight
you're all I've got
I need you, tonight
I want you, I need you, tonight

You seem so pushy and you don't even care.
You want me to take you everywhere.
You even tell me what to do.
But that's alright cus I need you.
Because you're all I've got tonight.
I want you I need you sunny dress ruby lips


You're all I've got tonight...
I want you tonight I need you tonight...
I need you to fight,
I want you tonight.

When you dance I go insane.
I'm the one that knows,
you say I am obsessed.
But remember I was your best.
And remember I was possessed.
It's a crucial thing so try to understand.
That I want to be your only man.
And please try to understand,
that you're all I've got tonight.
