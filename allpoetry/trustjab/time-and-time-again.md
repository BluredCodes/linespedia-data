---
title: "time and time again"
writer: "trustjab"
slug: "time-and-time-again"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Time and time again we've learned
that life is ours and it burns
Time and time again you will see
how much your love means to me
Time and time again you'll realize
even through all the lies
I was the one that gave you life


Time and time again
even through thick and thin,
I've kept my promises true
Always meant for you
Time and time again we've seen
through everything we call lean


Time and time again
you will notice that we win
All those times of trial
and even through tribulation
we have pulled off time and time again
our own declaration
Even when I was on probation
you kept me through promotion


Time and time again I've loved
Time and time again from above
it did seem you were heaven sent
Time and time again through an event
Even if it was one evening
together time and time again we meant things
Time and time again we've been through these things


Time and time again
even after the fall
we still stood tall
Time and time again 
through thick and thin
I've kept my promises true
and they were always for you


Time and time again we've learned
That the best part is when it burns
Time and time again we've saw
the things that came out even raw
Time and time again we've been through the worst
Time and time again it was each-other we cursed


Time and time again we've seen
how close we were to coming clean
Time and time again you will notice
That after each time I blow this
