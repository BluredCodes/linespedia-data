What will become of the two of us
what will happen when they're through with us
will they throw both of us away
or will they keep one of us for science
discard my body and keep yours
I don't want to die so young
I am still having way too much fun
Someone is holding me behind
waiting for the right time
then out of nowhere they'll split us
Knowing me i'll feel like I was hit by a bus
I can't stand losing you
It'd be an act of war
to take our big four
those four years we've loved and cherished eachother
I even looked to you instead of my mother
is there a future for us my dear
the future holds certain doors
to get there we gotta complete these little chores
