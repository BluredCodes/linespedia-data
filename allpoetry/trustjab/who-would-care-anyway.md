---
title: "Who would 'Care,' Anyway?"
writer: "trustjab"
slug: "who-would-care-anyway"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19939-Who-would-Care--Anyway--by-trustjab"
---

I post a few here and there,
but still no one gives a shit
I lost a few here and there,
but still no one gives a shit
I mean who would care anyway
I am a loser, a low life
There's shit about me no one understands
There's shit about me women understand
Then there's shit about me men understand
But all in all who gives a flying fuck
Whether I live or die, I have no luck
I have no life
I have no wife
no kids to take care of
no worrys? No plenty of that
And for those of you who don't care...
Well I say "choke on it," 
gagging all the way...
who would care anyway
if I lived or died
If I smoked and got high
If I drowned in a pool of water
or a pool of mercy
will you be my friend, persay?
