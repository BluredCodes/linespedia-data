You see it in the movies
You even hear it on the radio

You were the one I used to hold onto
You were the one who used to wear my ring



Temptation
Infatuation
Violence
Fenced



Remember,

Ooh, our last September
You can't handle the addiction

You won't be part of the friction
You won't listen to me
You won't give in to me


Our violence
Our prideance
Our addition
Our addiction
Our love
In jeapordy
Hold me close
Don't let me go

Remember,
Our last September
Don't go
Violence has passed
Our abuse will not last
Put it in the past
Remember, our last September
We used to talk
We used to hold hands and walk

No, no, no, no remember
Those nights with me
Those violent nightsI would never abuse a woman, so this is obviously a different type of violence. A different form of abuse...see what you can come up with if you think about it.
