Changed Forever

This years holiday season
seems subdued, somber, even quiet.
Haunted by September day of infamy
when the world changed forever.

Feeling like a lessening...
a reservation in spirit and soul.
Perhaps explained by honor, respect
for those who have gone before.

Our soldiers hunt for justice
seek twisted madmen responsible.
Our nation has unified with sorrow
tragedy setting granite resolve.

Everything we have cherished
forever changed, turned outside in.
We struggle with our new reality
defined with red, white and blue.

Methinks we all need a holiday
a party, joyous birthday celebration!
A reminder that good shall triumph
as the world changes forever...again.

 Merry Christmas to each and all!


  Tramp Poet  12/01
