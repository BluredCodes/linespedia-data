Last Watch

Warrior stands lonely, last watch
heavily muscled body in moonlight.
Large, lonely absolute sentinel
entrusted with comrades dreams.

Nary a sound tword wee morn
slight rustle within slighter wisps.
Sleeping nicker of faithful steeds
alert as moon slips off to bed.

Guard trusted with others lives
friend trusted with others life.
Those alseep, sleep sound, rest
knowing without a single doubt.

Warrior kneels in thanksgiving
quiet time with his quiet worship.
Man made bigger with his surrender
morn breaks as he wakes the others.
