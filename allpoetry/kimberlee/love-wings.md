If i had wings i would fly
through the air without a care
id forget all the pain and suffering if felt 
and let the wind calm myself 
but then i return put my feet on the ground
and all i hear is that clattering sound
of my heart as it breaks
the shattering pain 
it all feels the same 
as my eyes fall on you 
so i back away 
and rise again to the sky 
and so i remain
to this very day 
with the pain that grows stronger 
holding me back 
from the happiness i want so badanything?
