title: "My Crush, My Weakness"

To think of the nights 
i spent crying in bed 
and the endless dreams i had 
my crush was my weakness
a huge one at that 
but to tell you the truth 
you never deserved it
no one deserves the shit you gave me me 
you flirted and fucked around with my mind
did things that just tore me up inside
im slowly learning to get over you 
cos that will be the best thing i could ever do
maybe one day we can try for friends 
but at the moment its too soon
all i want to do 
is get over you.about you have to say about it?
