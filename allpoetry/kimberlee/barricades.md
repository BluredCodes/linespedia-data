A war between us 
like a raging sea 
separted by barracks
with soliders in between 
families at war 
like many in our streets
the kind men a few and far between 
as soon as they're found 
they die the next day
lost for words as we stare in dismay 
at this shocking display
the parading about 
like horses in a show 
one day they'll learn to live side by side
till that day i'll remain 
on this ocean
this blanket 
were it matters not 
whether your catholic 
and im protestant.basically what do you think of it?
