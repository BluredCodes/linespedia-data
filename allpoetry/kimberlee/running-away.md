I looked at you today You smiled but I looked awayWill i spend my whole life running awayWay from my feelingsSo I wont face my fears Discovering a bitternessFar worst and I burst into tears Im scared to be aloneAnd Im terrified to be with youChanged so muchIn the short time Ive know you I know love could probably be ifIt wasn't for meIf I talked instead of walking away
