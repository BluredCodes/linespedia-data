There is a little girl coming down the hall
She is dressed in her mother' clothes
Also has her shoes and make-up on
She walks up to her mom in the kitchen and says
"Look at me! I'm a model!" 

A little boy follows his dad to work
All he can see is the shiny red truck
And the big black hat
Looking up at his dad, with stars in his eyes
He smiles and says
"I want to be just like you!"

Children dream
They have the right
Some want to be like their parents
Some like friends
Everyone has the right 
To be what they want to be

Dreams are the spice of life
They make everything interesting
Give a child the path and right to dream
No matter what is happening

If a child has no dreams
What life will they have
They will not have a full life
So give a child the freedom to have
Dreams
