You never know what will happen
One day you may be rich
The next poor
Live Life
One day you may have food
The next none
Live Life
One day you may have many friends
The next none at all
Live life
We get many things from day to day
But we can lose it all in seconds
All we can do is carry on
Live life to the fullest
