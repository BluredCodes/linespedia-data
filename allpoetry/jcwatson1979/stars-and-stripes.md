You've guided us
Protected us, too
You've been through many battles
Fought many wars
For over two hundred years
You've never wavered
We've been through depression
Have had many natural disasters
But as always you stood strong
You've lost many of your children
But never thought of your own pain
Because you were trying to help others
There is only one thing left to say
Be Strong
Red, White, and Blue
Stand Proud
Stars and Stripes
For America is at attention
Ready to stand behind you
To defend our liberty once again
