A disease of the mind
A disease of the heart
Black and White 
Whats that
Is not based on color
But is not color blind
Attacks the bi-racial child
Attacks the mentally incompetent
Also the physically impaired
Attacks the poor
Even those without a home
Doesnot understand love and compassion
Those two words not in its dictionary
If we are not carful it will kill us allany pointers would be greatly appreciated
