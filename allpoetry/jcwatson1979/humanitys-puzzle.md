We have roses, tulips, and even trees.
They all have different colors, shapes,
and sizes. But there is always more than
one of the same thing.  They all fit in the 
picture of the puzzle.  There are also many 
other different things, besides these, that 
fit.  There is some pieces, that if they are 
missing they can never be replaced. These are 
unique pieces, that complete and make the puzzle
beautiful.  They are he pieces of each and 
everyone of our lifes. They will only fit if we
make our lifes what they ought to be.  If we live 
our lifes with love, kindness, and compassion then
then and only then will our life's piece fit into
the big picture of Humanity's Puzzle.


                                       Jeanette Watson
