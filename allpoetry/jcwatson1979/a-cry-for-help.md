Knock! Knock!
Is anyone home?
Is anyone listening?
Can't you hear me crying?
Why don't you see my pain?
Can't you see the tears?
Why doesn't anyone see whats happening?
Why is everyone blind?
Why is everyone afraid?
Why won't you help a hurting child?
All I ever hear is "I don't want to get involved."
Why?
What could happen to you?
Nothing!
I never have love come my way.
All I ever get is a fist.
I'm hurting, bruised, and bleeding.
And no one even pays attention.
No one even cares.
Why?
