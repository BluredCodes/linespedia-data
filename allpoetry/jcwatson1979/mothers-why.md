You hear of stories in the news
You read stories in magazines and newspapers
Of mothers hurting or killing their children
Mothers, Why?
Children are gifts from God
They endear themselves with just one smile
When they wrap their tiny hand 
Around anyone's finger
That person's heart can't help but melt
Children are sent for us to love and protect
Mothers, Why?
Each child is unique
Each meant to be something special
Something important
Their life is precious
No one has the right to take their life
Especially not their mother
A mother's purpose is to nurture and help grow
Not destroy
Mothers, Why?
After all the pain you go through
To bring a child into this world
I don't see how you could hurt and destroy 
Something so precious
Mothers, Why?
