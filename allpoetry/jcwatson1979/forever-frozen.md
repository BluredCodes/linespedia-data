A mother gazing for the first time in her baby's eyesA father teaching his son to ride a bikeA brother fighting bullies to defend his sisterFriends running, laughing, and playing in the woodsParents seeing their only child off to school for the first timeA scared teenager walking down the aisle in cap and gownA teary-eyed father walking his daughter down the aisle to her groomA grandmother holding her first grandchildThese are moments in timeForever FrozenIn our minds
