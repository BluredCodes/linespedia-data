---
title: "You"
writer: "duderag"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34005-You-by-duderag"
---

You have given me my greatest joy
You are beauty inspired 
You open my heart to such tenderness
You hug my heart and soul when I am in your arms
You are giving me my greatest love
You make me cry the happiest tears
You make my heart ache with such pain when I am not with you
You make my body tremble when I am close to you
You have the sweetest heart I could hope to find
You cry tears I want to kiss
You touch my skin and I, I melt in your hands
You give me shy sultry smiles
You make my body pulse with passion 
You have a tender soul angels would envy
You have breathed life into my heart
You give me honesty to hold
You have honey kisses that drip with desire
You make my dreams real
You hold my hands as if our hearts touch at our fingertips
You make we want to caress your soft, soft skin
You make me believe in a love, I thought my heart could never touch
You are all, you are everything
You do this not by what you do, but by who you are

29/10/00
Because you are all I have ever wanted
