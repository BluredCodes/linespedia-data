title: "I cried tonight"

I cried tonight
I’m not sure why
No attempt to wipe them away
No attempt to hide them
For who is to see, no one
No one but me
I have given to work, I have given to life, I have given to love
I’m sure tomorrow I can give some more
But tonight, tonight I am empty
Nothing to give, nothing to live, empty

I cried tonight
Head in hands
Left with dry and salted cheeks
I ponder the points
Why am I here?
I am not selfish but isn’t it something to do with me
Change? If only I would, if only I could
Though tomorrow I will again give
But I will remember
I cried tonight


03/11/00
The end of a week that I have worked, lived and loved too hard
