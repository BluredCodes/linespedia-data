Goodnight my darling
I am wishing you sweet dreams
You lay your head gently down
And listen to the rain
Draw your warm blanket slowly up
 And curl up in your cute and comfy way
Now close your eyes and think of me
As I kiss your tender cheek
Relax now my love, I will hold you till you sleep
My last words for the night are four words few
…goodnight,  I love you

I am hoping for the first night she stays with me
