Pieces of her
Left
Left in the passages of my heart
Picked up in gentle sway
Placed in places there
With sunflowers grown
From the presence 
Of pieces of her
I retreat from Fridays spent with the world
Not in recognition of that I call myself
Showered, I shed my skin
Shut out that that I can touch and all its falsities
For the truth is that that I cannot touch
The truth is that that I cannot see
But for closed eye
The only time truth can be
Never mind truth will not breathe
Never mind truth will not walk with me
But I will wander the passages of my heart
Spend the spent moments
With my sunflowers
And with pieces of her


11/01/01
Remembering pieces of her on a lonely Friday night
