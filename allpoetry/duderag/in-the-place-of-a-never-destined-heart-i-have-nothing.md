---
title: "In the place of a never destined heart, I have nothing"
writer: "duderag"
slug: "in-the-place-of-a-never-destined-heart-i-have-nothing"
source: "AllPoetry"

No matter how much I think
No matter how much I feel
It never brings you to me
No manner of praying
No manner of hoping
No manner of dreaming
Will ever bring you to me
Tears ever flowing
Sadness everlasting
Loneliness forever flowing
Tears, Years, Fears
Never captured
Never kept
Never, never, never
Why? Just cry
I have all that makes me a good man
But I have nothing
I have nought
This good man with everything
Is a good man with nothing
Crush my heart
Ground my soul
If only the Earth could eat my flesh
For it is lifeless without love
I am loveless without life
But still I am left here
Vacant for all to see
Though invisible to all
Or is it all do not care
This is so
I finish my words
These damned words to myself
For the words are but lucky
For it is I 
It is I that must keep
Keep carrying, bearing nothing
Nothing in my hands
In the place of a never destined heart
