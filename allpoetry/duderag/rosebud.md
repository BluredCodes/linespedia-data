title: "Rosebud"

She silently inspires my senses
A rosebud born on solemn night
Woken with the quiet of dawn 
‘tis beauty bathed in mornin’ light

Oh such rosebud
Glistening with drops of dew
Like little tears tenderly held
To her heart soft and true

Her lips are perfect petals
Curled, and of velvet pink 
Sweet as nature’s nectar
No sweeter taste to drink

Oh such rosebud
Shall open bloom in time 
Gently, gently, gently
To reveal a heart of tender rhyme
