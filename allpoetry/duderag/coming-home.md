---
title: "Coming Home"
writer: "duderag"

From four thousand miles away
With my eyes resting, silently shut
I could still imagine her hold
My heart did beat but softly
Eight times did the sunrise
But eight times the sunset without her
Now closer but only by a half
My heart beats harder
So much that I can feel my pulse
Through my soft aching lips
Before this sunset today
I will be home
But the waiting weathers my soul
Taking each second, each minute like days, like weeks
But I can wait as the world does take
As my soul shall survive 
For I will see her soon
For I am coming home


14/11/00
Waiting at Cairns Airport, dying to see her
