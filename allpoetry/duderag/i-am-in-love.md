---
title: "I am in love"
writer: "duderag"

I lie here with her
My head gently laying on her precious back
Soft, warm and so close to her beating heart
I am in love
Though I dare not say
I break this perfect hold
To whisper words
Why? To express to her in a thousand words
What can otherwise be expressed in six…
‘I am in love with you’
But alas, a thousand words not only expresses
But also hides the truth
The truth?
I am in love
I finish my words softly spoken
So gently, like an angel leading theirs home
She takes my hand and draws me to hold her
My hands, my arms, my hold, my heart
Have found their home
Their home? In her heart
I am in love

05/11/00
Because I am in love with her – I need to admit it to her – I need to admit it to myself
