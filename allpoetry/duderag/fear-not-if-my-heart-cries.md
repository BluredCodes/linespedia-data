Ten shades of blue has passed my eyes
Ten thousand times my heart has beat
I miss her
The light dims the day
The last cloud snails the sky
I take my place in rest
In darkness I lie
It is more apt in the absence of light
Tigerlillies...
To capture her presence this night
For I miss her
Fear not my heart if it cries
Let each tear fall
Let each tear tremble
Let each tear miss her
For I will not let this heart die
Without letting it live


8/12/01
Alone in my room as night falls – thinking of Eli
