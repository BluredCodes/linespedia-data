I do not think you to be anything
Years teach, tears reach
Lessons learned
You live not in my thoughts
But in your imperfect perceptions
Yes, natural, plan need love hate cry
...2, but
Plans in concrete crumble the most
Need for love succumbs us 
The footsteps beside our hearts
Slowly as if not noticed soften
Soon silence, footsteps are gone
Hate cry, others do all to stop
We, stop sometimes cannot
Feel all for if not, worth not
Be not what you think I think you are
Be only you
Because I do not think you to be anything
The world with me as part has no right 
Be brave, be you
No matter the world
If you be you
I will learn, but never all that is you
For you will still be learning you
To the last grain falls to place
So, I... I have no chance but to fall behind you
And learn you from you
Each and every moment granted
Will be treasured
And for me till my last grain falls to place
I will learn you from you
