The day begins
And the light peeks through my eyes
As if straddling me the day touches my forehead gently
And says 'wake, oh gentle man'
That moment that lives between dark and light
That moment that lives between sleep and wake
That moment is her now
She has transcended the nightly dreams
Lived the daily thoughts
And now she is captured in that moment
To wake me, in that moment.


21/11/01
The days begins with thoughts of her
