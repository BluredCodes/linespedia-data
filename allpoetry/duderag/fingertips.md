---
title: "Fingertips"
writer: "duderag"
slug: "fingertips"
source: "AllPoetry"

I reach for words as my fingertips
To touch her soft heart and to caress her gentle soul
I express all that is in my heart
With neither fear nor bravery
But simply truth of feeling, truth of thought
With hesitancy I feel her feelings touch my heart
Through her fingertips of tender hugs and kisses
Every moment I am with her, her fingertips touch me more
Slowly letting go of her fears, to feel more, to express more
I am overjoyed to begin to believe that I can be loved
In flesh, I tremble, I feel her delicate soft skin through my fingertips
Caresses drawn over her slender neck and her satin smooth cheeks
And caresses drawn across the sultry curves of her sensual body
My fingertips have never known a greater joy
With the touch of her fingertips my body yields in surrender
To surrender my heart, my body and my soul to her
