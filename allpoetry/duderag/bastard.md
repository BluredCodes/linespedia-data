Met a girl once, sorry twice
Could have loved
Should have loved
Brothers fucked her up, bastards
Husband bashed her up, bastard
Mixed in between cocktails of stuff
Stuff I’ve never even considered
Little boy born
Husband was with a girl but not his wife
Bastard
Two efforts to leave this world failed
One just a month ago plus sixty something hours
Mr perfect
Strength in her weakness
But not enough
Could have loved
Should have loved
Mr perfect
Hmmm… Bastard


21/01/02
Because I’m just as bad as them
