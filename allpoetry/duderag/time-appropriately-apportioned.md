Time, appropriately apportioned
To end four seasons lost to time itself
Captured hearts, lost hearts
The mind oaked in fertile soil
The soul soaked deep in yellow and blue
Time, appropriately apportioned
Baking all those true and truly wonderful
Into the mind
Hoping these four seasons to come
Will bring them the joy and all


19/12/01
In reflection of a client and warm wishes
