The 'patter' was not supposed to begin so quickly
The sweet remnants of moments of past
Falling like feathers
Covering the ground, peppered with white
What is one to say?
Say but nothing, let the feathers fall
With enough to cover the ground I stand upon
I will not walk away
For it seems soon I can lay down in comfort
To rest surrendered
Surrounded by soft down 


09/11/01
Second time around… that we could begin so quickly again
