Fifty two weeks worth of worthless weeks
And here I am again, nowhere
Close my eyes, you're not standing there
You're not anywhere around
Open my ears, no sound
Believe? No, no, no
Bravery holds no reward
Not so bravely fall to the sword
Preserve bleeding hearts and burn it in salt
Burn, burn, burn, it's always my fault


30/12/01
In reflection of 2001
