title: "River of Passion"

Through the rustling trees
I had heard the gushing, the gurgling of water flow
With this pure sound I sensed my surrounds would lead me here
Without contemplation I bounded forth
To explore childlike, an adventure found
In a matter of a few springing steps
Here I found myself, stunned in awe
On the banks of the greatest river I stand
Gun barrel blue, but not gun barrel straight
Winding up like a snake about to strike
And with enormity of power and enormity of speed
The water rushed by with an almighty roar
I stood so tall, no fear in my mind, no fear in my eye
Off with my shoes and off with my clothes
Boldly naked, nature and I 
I would like to have said I dived in so sure
But one foot in, two feet in, slowly I crept
Closing my eyes to feel its power
I fell backwards, arms outstretched
Crash! bubbles abound, my head went down
Stunned and lost for a moment so long
I struggled to surface, God as if I could
But struggle I did, not to survive this raging torrent
For I will drown in this river of passion
To enjoy, enjoy this slow surrender

The pure intensity of passion I feel for her
