I lie physically still 
Yet my mind, my heart
Ran, circling the room
Bouncing off walls
With such randomness
No pattern could plot
Sleep was my only saviour
Giving turn to the subconcious to roam
But sleep, I could not
Instead, sense packed it's bag
Leaving without as much as a turn to catch my eye
Struggle damn boy!
With due credit to Lloyd
Here I was
Watching angels mend


20/12/01
I could not sleep the night after seeing her after she tried leaving this world
