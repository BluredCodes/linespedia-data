Note:
[This was written entirely by my unconscious. I awoke from a dream, sat up in bed, and this phrase was stuck in my head. I didn't write it; I found it inside me, already written.]

A businessman from Motorola
Just addressed the Ayatollah
With two lines of coca cola
Cut with fancy red ebola
