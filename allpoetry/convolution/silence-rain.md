Walk with me this city street
Feel pavement cry beneath your feet
Every step brings novel fret
And stirs the vat of your regret

The streetlights sigh as we walk past
Immune to winter’s bitter blast
Pull that coat around you close
For god has made a frigid toast

Raindrop crashes to the ground
His crushing impact makes no sound
We watch him die all by ourselves
We know that story raindrop tells

But look up, now, and see this rain
Above this spot on 5th and Main
And watch the moonlight growing dim
As raindrop’s friends do follow him
