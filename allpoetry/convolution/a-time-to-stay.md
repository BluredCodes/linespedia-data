---
title: "A Time to Stay"
writer: "Convolution"
slug: "a-time-to-stay"

A time to stay has come this year;
This time, perhaps, we all must heed
The call inside to muffle fear.
This time, perhaps, we all shall breathe.

A time of crystal grey indoors;
This time, perhaps, we all shall help,
To lift another from the floor.
This time, perhaps, we’ll leave ourselves.

A time to call on lessons gone;
This time, perhaps, we all can learn.
A burden, gladly, we take on.
This time, perhaps, our cheeks shall turn.

A time to think of loves we’ve lost;
This time, perhaps, we’ll watch this play.
A time to know the paths we’ve crossed.
This time, perhaps, we’ll stay this way.
