title: "Vacant Potential"
writer: "Convolution"

How could you ignore her?
She’s just across from you
While she did jade
Your silence made
Your untold lie so true.

When was it you last kissed her?
You wouldn’t think to know
When you turned deaf
Her silence left
Her scars began to show.

But did you ever love her?
You claimed to in the past
But silence now
Reveals how
You couldn’t make it last.
