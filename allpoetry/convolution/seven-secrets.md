---
title: "Seven Secrets"
writer: "Convolution"

A thousand kisses I won’t give
A million smiles I won’t see
No gaze with silent, subtle words
No haze of joy blinding me

One hundred fights I’ll never have
One hundred skies a bit less starry
No guilty glances of regret
No hundred verses of “I’m sorry”

No quiet moments in the dark
No tracks to make in brand new snow
Seven ways to drown myself
Seven secrets I won’t know

I wish I’d never met you now
I wish we’d never laughed nor wept
I’ll wake one day to find you gone
And wonder why I ever slept
