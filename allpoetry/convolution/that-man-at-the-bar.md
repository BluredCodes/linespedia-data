Alone along the road at night
Coming here from who-knows-where
A journey with no end in sight
Is evident in that man there

He rode his horse until it died
And walked until the break of dawn
He found a stable, ducked inside
He bought a horse and carried on

And so it’s gone for many years
The same with every dust-filled breath
And so he’ll go without a fear
Until he finds a place to rest
