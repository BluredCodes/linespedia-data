---
title: "Half  a Hole"
writer: "Convolution"

If only simple shadow
Is cast by empty hands
Then I am half a person
I am an empty man

I am a hollow visage
An interrupted prayer
I’m my mistake incarnate
With skin and eyes and hair

If the god I have forgotten
Could forgive me of my sin
I pray that he should do it now
And make me real again
