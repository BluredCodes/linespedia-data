title: "Sodden"
writer: "Convolution"

Why do you stay in that place?
Do you not realize you’re nude?
I know that you can find that grace
That path to lunar latitudes

Why do you decide your fate?
Do you not think it’s been secured?
Sit back while I elucidate
The happiness to be procured

Why do you torment yourself?
Do you not see the good in you?
Put all your jewelry on the shelf
And see that you’re in xanadu
