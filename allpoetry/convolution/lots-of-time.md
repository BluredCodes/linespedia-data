title: "Lots of Time"

Sometimes years can make an awesome
Silence where it shouldn’t be
And sometimes they make cherry blossoms
Fall from long-dead cedar trees

Sometimes years can carve a chasm
Or justify a heinous crime
And sometimes people walk right past them
They won’t appreciate their time

Sometimes years can make us breathless
When we see just how many passed
And sometimes that will make us restless
Enough to make the present last

Sometimes years can leave us weathered
But they can edify us, too
So whether you shall stay here, tethered
Is just a choice, made by you
