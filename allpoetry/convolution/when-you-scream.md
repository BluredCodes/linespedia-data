title: "When You Scream"
writer: "Convolution"

When you scream out “I want to die!”,
And no one even seems to hear,
I’ll be the one that screams back “Why?”,
And makes you question all your tears.

When you remove your steel shell,
And they all try to make you bleed,
I’ll stitch you up and let you yell,
And rub in all the salt you need.

When you lean on that wall all day,
And try to look content alone,
I’ll never be that far away,
I’ll stick around, I’ll drive you home.
