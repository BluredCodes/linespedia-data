title: "Things We Bleed"

Let’s walk down Always Boulevard
We’ll watch the tables turn
And we’ll discuss, with mild disgust
The words that teleprompters burn

Let’s see why there was one door barred
We’ll laugh about the past
With that in mind, we’ll leave behind
The angry things that make hurt last
	
Let’s prick our fingers on the ends
We’ll bleed two drops of red
And think of not so long ago
When we bled other things instead
