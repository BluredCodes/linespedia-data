The stars in the sky,
 flow silently by, 
as the Eagle watches its prey.

The moon in the west,
sets on it's gorgeous nest,
as the Eagle watches its Prey.

The Blue water glistens,
as he leans in and listens,
to the wonderous calls of the night.

Watching steadily
 with all of his might,
awaiting his appetite.

He glides through the air,
like a steam without a care,
and quietly goes in for the kill.

And until this day,
knowing what we may,
You will always find the Eagle,
Watching it's prey.
