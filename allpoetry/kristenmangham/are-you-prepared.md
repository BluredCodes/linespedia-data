Are You Prepared?


We live everyday. We get up, we go, we live our daily lives. We follow daily schedules, but no one really knows when this may end. We may get up 365 days straight but the next day everything could be over. People think that nothing will ever happen, but they are delaying the inevitable. Are you prepared for that day? If you're not, get prepared. Decide where you want to go Heaven or Hell, more often than not people usually choose Heaven, I did and I hope you will too. Give your life to God, it's not too late it's never too late for such a big decision, especially one that could alter your life forever. Choose right, choose true. There is only choice for you, but if you decide that it is not for you or you are just undecided, pray. Pray about it. God will help you and if you don't know anything about him ASK!!!!! Get to know him,he can be and IS your one true friend. Are YOU prepared?

By Kristen Mangham
