Are There Angels Among Us?



Angels are all around us, or are they?
MiMiraclesappen everyday, or so we think.
Is everything a coincidence, or does God
have his hand in this?
Does he send his angels to protect us?
Are There Angels Among Us? The answer as I see 
it... is yes, there are angels among us. They look
after us and guide us where we need to go. There
are many angels, there is your Guardian Angel,
sent to protect you and other angels to watch
over you, but your friend that is always there
for you, the one you trust above all else, He
or She is also an angel sent from God to be a 
friend and a confidant, to share all troubles
and trials with, to laugh, to cry they will
always be there.
Are There Angels Among Us?
yes there are
YOU may even be one of them.
------------------------------------------------------------
Always smile and be polite,
You never know when you are 
talking to YOUR Angel!
By Kristen Mangham
