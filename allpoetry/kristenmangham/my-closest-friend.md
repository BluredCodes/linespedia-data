My Closest Friend





My closest friend is a person who listens to me. He is a person who cares about me and helps me through my every need. This person is well known to many people, he helps everyone who asks.
My closest friend is God, he's the one person who can help you through any problem at all. All you have to do is ask. My closest friend can be everyone's closest and Best friend. Believe in him and ALL your problems can be solved. 
He is my closest friend and now you know why. Who is your's? Do You even know? Do you know why that person is your friend? You should. Get to know MY friend, he can help you, just pray. He will help you. Get to know him. It will pay off. Believe me. Believe in my closest friend. Believe...in God
By Kristen Mangham
