Always There


There is a man, who is always there when you need him. He is the strongest man in the world and he can bring you to your knees, and no I'm not talking about Hercules. This man is no visible to human eyes but he can be heard. He has love for everyone, no one is exempt from it. No matter what you do or where you go, he is always right behind you, and if you think that no one can help you, think again. He is always there. all you have to do is pray, call on his name and he will meet you every need, IF it is in his will. SO when you are bummed out, like a surfer without his wave, or a corpse without it's grave, Pray!. Praying will solve everything, pour out your heart to that man for he is always there.
By Kristen Mangham
