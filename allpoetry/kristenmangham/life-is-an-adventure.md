Life is an Adventure 
that happens everyday.
Life is an Adventure
that just won't fade away.

Life is an Adventure
that makes your dreams come true.
Life is an Adventure
that is all about you.

Life is an Adventure
that everyone should love.
Life is an Adventure
because of The One above.

Life is an Adventure
that everyone should share.
Life is an Adventure
that shows how much you care.

Life is an Adventure 
that you cannot live without.
Life is an Adventure 
that makes me want to shout!

Life is an Adventure
because of the love in our hearts.
Life is an Adventure
if You and God never part.

Life is an Adventure
that should never, never end.
Life is an Adventure
that you can show your friend.

Life is an Adventure
that raises you up high.
So High So High
that you can touch the sky.

Life is an Adventure
til' the very end.
Life is an Adventure
If God is your friend.

By:  Kristen ManghamWritten on April 20 1998,
