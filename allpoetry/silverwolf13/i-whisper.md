title: "I Whisper"

As your memory fades, I whisper 'I love you'
When I no longer hear your voice, I whisper 'I love you'
When I think about you, I whisper 'I love you'
When I talk about you, I whisper 'I love you'
Before I fall asleep, I whisper 'I love you'
As I wonder why you had to die,
As I wonder where your spirit lies,
As I sit by your grave side,
My dear, I will forever whisper 'I love you'~Written as I sat next to the grave of my best friend~
