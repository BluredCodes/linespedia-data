Send me roses 
a week after my day. 
Send me a card 
a year too late. 
I'm an afterthought to you 
nothing more than a name 
on a calender 
on the day 
I was brought into this world. 

Please forget about me, 
I'd rather you did. 
I'll forget about you too, 
pretend you don't exist. 
I'll pretend your lies don't hurt me, 
I'll pretend your ignorance doesn't kill. 
I'll hold my head up high 
and push you out of my mind. 

But I cannot forget, 
because you remember 
ten minutes too late. 
And you remind me 
that I'm only an afterthought, 
now and forever. 

And you tell me you love me 
and I cry, 
because I love you too daddy.Yeah, This really speaks for itself.  Pretty blatent.
