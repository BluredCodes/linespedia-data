Gossamer wings 
glowing in the sunrise, 
multi-color rainbows 
glistening with dew. 
Spider webs full 
of hidden cryptic messages. 
A small secret cove 
shared by me and you. 

The silence in a heartbeat 
the hope within a hand. 
The smiles shown in moonlight 
the arm which helps you stand. 

Enchanted magical moments 
all held within a dream, 
memories of tomorrow 
where everything is more than it may seem.I absolutly love this poem.  I think it gets accross a feeling of contentment which not much of my work contains.
