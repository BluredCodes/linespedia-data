---
title: "Love is.........."
writer: "Miss Elegance"

Love is giving completely,
Giving of your heart, your soul and
your mind.
Giving whatever it takes to make him happy.

Love is caring,
Caring about what he thinks,
Caring about what he feels,
Caring about him in every way.

Love is sharing with him.
Sharing your thoughts, your feelings,
Sharing his pain, his successes and even his
failures.
Sharing with him every aspect of life.

Love is unconditional always.
Loving him in spite of his imperfections
Loving him when times are rough,
Loving him at his best and his worst.

Love is not always kind.
It can sometimes hurt
It can sometimes go away.
Love can be blind.

Love is sometimes letting go,
Because he may not love you in return.
He may not be ready.
It isn’t easy, but if you really love him,
You must let him go.
Love is wonderful.
Even through the heartaches
The pain, the hurtfulness.
The good times always out weigh the bad.
The happy times make it all worthwhile.
