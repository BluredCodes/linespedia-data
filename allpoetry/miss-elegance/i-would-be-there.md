---
title: "I Would Be There"
writer: "Miss Elegance"
slug: "i-would-be-there"
source: "AllPoetry"

When you hurt, I feel your pain.
I would gladly take it away
And bear it as my own.
When you are sad and feeling down,
I would take away that frown
And turn it into a smile again.

When you are feeling lonely and lost,
I would reach out my hand to you
To guide you back again.

When you feel that no one cares,
I would comfort you and hold you
And show you that you are not alone.

When you need to talk,
I would be there for you,
Listen to you and let you open up.

When you are happy and full of joy
I would be there,
Eager to let you share.2000,
