title: "I Used to Know a Woman.........."

I used to know a woman
Who was full of happiness,
She had dreams and hopes.
When she smiled, you could
See it in her eyes.


One day I woke up, and she was gone.
Now, she is full of loneliness,
Sadness and lost dreams
Fill her days.
In her eyes, only emptiness.


I search and search for the woman
That used to be.
She is lost in a world of sadness,
A world alone.
Her dreams and hopes are gone.


Will I ever find her?
I do not know.
Perhaps one day she will return to me,
Until then, I will continue my search.
I want to find that woman I used to be.2000,
