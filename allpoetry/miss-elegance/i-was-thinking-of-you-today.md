title: "I Was Thinking of You Today"

I was thinking of you today,
Like I always do.
I wonder if you are happy and how are you.
I was thinking of the wonderful times we shared,
The laughter and love we had.
I remembered all the rainbows and sunsets,
The gentle caress of your voice.
As I sit here and think of you,
I feel the tears streaming down my cheeks.
I mourn the loss of what we had,
The dreams we shared.
I miss the way my heart would race
At the sound of your voice,
And the thought of those sweet, soft butterfly kisses.
I was thinking of you today,
Like I always do.
Mourning the loss of a love so true.
Aching to be yours again.
Feeling the emptiness you left behind.
I was thinking of you today, like I always do.2000,
