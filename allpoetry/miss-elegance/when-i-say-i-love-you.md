---
title: "When I Say I Love You...."
writer: "Miss Elegance"

It isn't meant to frighten you,
It is meant merely to say I care.
I say I Love you,
For the kind, caring person you are.
I Love You as one friend to another.
I Love you for the good times we share
As well as the troubled times.
I Love you for the goodness in you,
Your willingness to share,
Your giving ways.
I Love you for being there.
Your thoughtfulness,
The smiles you send my way.
For being there when I need someone,
Someone to lean on, to share my burdens.
I Love You for being my friend,
For being there when I need a helping hand.
The love of friendship between two people,
Is a love to cherish.
It is a love that shouldn't diminish.
Friends should be there forever,
For each other, to help each other.
I want to be that friend for you.
I hope you are that friend for me,
So don't be afraid,
When I say, "I LOVE YOU."
