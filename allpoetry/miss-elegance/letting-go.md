---
title: "Letting Go"
writer: "Miss Elegance"

Over time, you have crept into my heart
You have become a part of me.
Your happiness is my only concern.

You say you really want me to let go.
How do I let go of part of me?
You say you are not happy,
How can I set you free?

You will never know of the pain in my heart
You will not see the river of tears.
You will not know of the emptiness I feel.
By letting you go, I lose a part of me.

So here's to you, My Love.
I open my arms, my mind, my heart 
and my soul.
So that you can fly free,
Free as the dove.

As you leave, take this prayer with you,
Be happy and God Bless.
May you find your love and true happiness.
