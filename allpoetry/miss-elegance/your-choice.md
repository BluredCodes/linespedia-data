Just remember this is your choice, not mine.
You will no longer have a nice warm welcome to come home to.
You will no longer have me here to make you smile and laugh.
You will no longer have nice cards waiting for you.
Now I know that you never meant a word you told me,
About how you felt for me, how you cared.
It was all just a cruel game to you.
One in which you toyed with my feelings and heart.
I never asked for any commitment from you,
I was willing to wait, give you time.
You wanted me out of your life,
So now I am gone.
I have no life now, you were my life.
I hope you find happiness.
For you see, I just lost mine.
I hope you are happy with your choice and your life.
Goodbye.
