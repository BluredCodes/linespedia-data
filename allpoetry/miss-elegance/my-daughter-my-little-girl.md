---
title: "My Daughter, My Little Girl"
writer: "Miss Elegance"

Through the years, I have watched you grow,
From a beautiful little baby,
To toddler, to teenager, to a young lady.
It wasn't always easy for either of us,
But we made it through all the trials and tribulations.
I have always tried to be there for you,
Someone you can always talk to.
A mother's love for her little girl
Is the most precious gifts in the world.
No matter where we go, no matter what life brings,
The bond we share will always be strong.
I am proud of who you are,
I am the proudest Mom by far.
I know I don't very often say it,
I hope you will always know it,
I love you my daughter, my little girl.
My how the years have flown,
Now, you are all grown.
No matter what happens in this world,
You will always be My Little Girl.
I love you my daughter,
I am so very proud to be your mother.
