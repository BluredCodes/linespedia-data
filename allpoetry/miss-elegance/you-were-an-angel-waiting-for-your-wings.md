You Were an Angel Waiting for Your Wings

You were gravely ill,
You knew your time was drawing near,
You accepted it with open arms
Yet you didn't let it get you down.
You always had a smile for me.
You gave me encouragement,
The strength to follow my dream.
You never complained
Though I knew you were frightened.
You were suffering,
Yet you kept it inside.
You said you had a couple of things to do
Before your time here was done.
When you said Goodbye,
I knew it was the last one.
You have now gone to be at His side,
You have been given your wings.
I will think of you often,
And remember your words.
I know you will be watching over me.
You were my Angel waiting for her wings.
