title: "These Two Are One"

For two who share the same friendship,
For two who share the same trust,
For two who share the same joy,
These two are one.
~*~
For two who share the same thoughts,
For two who share the same dreams,
For two who share the same feelings,
These two are one.
~*~
Fro two who share the same desires,
For two who share the same passion,
For two who share The same love,
These two are one.
~*~
For two who share the same lives
Together in such a way as you do
Have always been one.
~*~
Author: Sherri Emily
East Coast, North Carolina

~*~
For my parents
