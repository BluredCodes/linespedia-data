Under this rooftop we gather once more

Just as we did when were small

Here we are back at the table again 

Wanting to know where each has been 

And telling our stories of long ago.


The year has turned its circle once more 

And we are gathering here at this same door 

Year after year we greet each other here 

Where warmth and grace is nothing to compare 

Mother and Father, Brother and Sister, 

Taking this time to be together. 


Nice things to say 

New things to remember 

We’ll be back again in December. 


One last thing I’d like to say 

To all of my family on this special day…. 


By the grace of God 

May you all be blessed 

May you all only know happiness. 

I wish you love 

I wish you peace 

I wish you all that happiness brings. 

And as we part 

And go on our way, 

Don’t forget to come home 

Next Thanksgiving Day….


Author: Sherri Emily Avery

North Carolina
