title: "Settle Down Boy"
writer: "Sherri Emily"

Is she beautiful?
Is she the light of your life?
Is she the only one?
The one to be your wife?
Does she make you happy?
Will she stay by your side?
Is she ready to be your bride?

(Chorus)
You can only have one love,
You can't have your cake and eat it too.
Settle down boy and let love within,
So you better choose, before you lose.

You were once mine,
Now you belong to another.
You left me for her,
Now you deserve each other.

(Chorus)
You can only have one love,
You can't have your cake and eat it too.
Settle down boy and let love within,
So you better choose, before lose.

Is she all that you need?
Is she the one that will bring you to your knees?
Was she worth the sacrifice?
Is she willing to please?
Is she like me?
Or is she like no other?
Is she the love of your life,
Your stars moon and sun?
Or is she like me just a stop on your run?
Or will she do to you,
What you have done?

(Chorus)
You can only have one love,
You can't have your cake and eat it too.
Settle down boy and let love within,
So you better choose, before you lose.

When will you see
That you're spreading your love thin
And you know you can't win?

(Chorus)
You can only have one love,
You can't have your cake and eat it too.
Settle down boy and let love within,
So you better choose, before you lose.

Are you afraid of being lonely?
Are you afraid of being the only?
Why can't you just settle down boy?
You need to do it soon.
Or you'll be one lonely boy
And you'll lose.


~Sherri Emily Avery~


Atlantic Beach, North Carolina
