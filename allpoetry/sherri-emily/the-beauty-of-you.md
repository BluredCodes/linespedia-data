---
title: "The Beauty of You"
writer: "Sherri Emily"
slug: "the-beauty-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16814-The-Beauty-of-You-by-Sherri-Emily"
---

I could give you a dozen roses,
and they wouldn't compare to the
beauty of you.
~*~
I could give you a carat diamond
with all it's flash and flare
but it wouldn't compare
to the
beauty of you.
~*~
I could give you a mountain top
about the earth so high
all it's beauty surrounding us
and eagles flying bye
but it wouldn't compare to the
beauty of you.
~*~
I could give you the stars in the sky
but even that wouldn't compare
to the beauty of you.
~*~
I want to give you me
for your beauty brings out
the beauty in me....
