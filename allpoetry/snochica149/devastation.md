Devastation is pitch black.
It sounds like you're screaming in a crowded room and no one hears you, or even worse they hear you but ignore you.
It tastes like chocolate that has lost all sweetness.
It smells like a corpse locked in a room left to rot.
Devastation is drowning in your darkest hours when there is nobody there to save you.
