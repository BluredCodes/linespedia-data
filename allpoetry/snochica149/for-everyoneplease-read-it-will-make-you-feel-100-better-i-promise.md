YOU AREBEAUTIFULEVERY SINGLE DAY TO ME NO MATTER WHAT  Authors note: I saw the most amazing presentation to day. I was taking part in a martin Luther king peace walk today for diversity club and they had a speaker named Michale Fowlin talk to us about diversity. He did a Bunch of skits portraying teenagers and the problems we face today. It was so true and the problems were hitting so close to home. Not a single person left that show without being touched. I actually broke down in tears during some of the skits and cried hysterically. I was truly moved. Anyway the point is he said to us truthfully that we were beautiful and then he asked us to look at the person in front of us ( a complete stranger) and tell them that they are beautiful. It made me feel so good when i heard that so i figured i would tell all of you guys that i truly believe your beautiful inside and out.....always remember that even though you don't know me i think your beautiful always and forever
