I plunge like a raindrop into the sea of life.I make the suicidal plunge into the hypocrisy of society.I splatter, I blend into the nothingness our world has become.When I finally feel like I have found myself I vaporize,I disappear and get sucked up into the fakeness of our universe, and then I start the plunge again only this time I fall not knowing where I will land.The only thing I am sure of is who I am and i know that is enough to get me through anything.
