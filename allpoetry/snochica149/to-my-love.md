---
title: "To My Love"
writer: "snochica149"

I am ripe with words to speak, 
yet you drowned me with your presence. 
My voice is strayed 
in the radiance that is you. 

The way you posses me 
astonishes me, 
my soul has it's sovereignty 
because of you. 

Though I can write lyrically 
regarding my infatuation for you; 
to utter the love I posses for you 
is seemingly inconceivable.
