I'm nothing when I am around you, at least that's what you've saidI used to think i had to be what you wanted me to be, but now i know betterI was clay in you hands, you touched me and I'd meltI was clay in your hands I begged you to mold me and shape me into something beautifulAfter you were gone I sat around for hours listening to our songWhy, I thought, did you leave me alone and feeling uglyNow I realize you made me stronger by leaving me unmolded becuaseI was clay in your hands i begged you to mold me and shape me into something beautiful while all the while it was me who molded you I was already beautiful were you?
