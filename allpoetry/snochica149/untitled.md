Dear Society,If I am not perfect so be it..who is?If I am not like the other brain less barbies who cares.I would rather be myself and know where I stand, then to wonder aimlessly through life trying to please people.The fact that our world is so messed up makes me sick. I have so many things to say but like everything else the words just rot and fall away. You make me sick. How can you live with yourself and know that the world isn't perfect, that is what you want isn't it? Well I am here to tell you dream on, life sucks deal with it. Be yourself. Live like you don't have another chance, love like you never have, and dance when there is no music.                             Love always, in that fake smile kind of way,                                     Success
