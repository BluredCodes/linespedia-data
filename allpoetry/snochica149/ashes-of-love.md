A young madin walks slowly from a dark cave of love
There in this cave she was reborn from the ashes of old loves
She was revived by the sunlight of a beautiful day
A new day... a new life... a new love...

A beautiful young madin walks into the the sunlight 
She looks out across the land from her cliff high above
She realizes that she doesn't need the love that traps her above all

A Beautiful, young, and strong madin is revived from the ashes of love
She wakes to new life, new love, a new day....
Then she takes a last look at the cave that held her prisoner for so long

A beautiful, young, strong and now confident madin makes up her mind
She spreads the wings on her back and flies away
She leaves her pain behind...the only thing she has ever known...
She is now free as a butterfly and as radient as the stars in the sky
