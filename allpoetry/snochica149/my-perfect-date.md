I lay awake at night stareing into the darkness
Thoughts of you and me creep slowly into my mind
I think of the long nights on the phone
The nights we spent reading poetry and shakespeare to each other

I see the faint glow of stars on my celing
They remind me of the night we drove to the park
We layed under the stars making wishes
In the background Al Green plays and we sit there singing along

You are always so good to me
When I am around you I smile and laugh
Your my soulmate and best friend.
You know who you are and Where you want to go in life
I love you for that
I promise on my existence that i will never let you go
Your a part of me... you have my soulwell this is my idea of a perfect guy...this is the guy i am looking for to spend my life with lol i am picky aren't i lol
