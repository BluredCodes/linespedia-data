All i wanted was some greasy fast food is that to much to ask
Obliviously yes I mean I can't without sitting in the back of a cop car.
All i wanted was to hang with my best friend and eat some fries
Sorry but that is no longer aloud instead you must be scared to death
It is no longer safe to park your car and go inside, someone may get killed
INstead of two best friends hanging out together you get two best friends, only 16, becoming key witnesses to a homicide.

Now you tell me is this fair all I wanted was a double quarter pounder with cheese plain, large coke , large fry...this just happened to me last night i was a key witness along with my best friend to a homicide it the the scariest experience in my life and i hope that by my friend's and mine reports will bring justice to this case. If they need me to go to court i am fully prepared to testify because i am 100% sure i know what happened and who is guilty.
