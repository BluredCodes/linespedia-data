I am suffocating alone in a corner,
I can't breath... your beauty, your essence, it is too much to handle...
I want you to hold me and touch me like no man has ever done before,
I want to be in love and feel loved by you and only you.

The butterflies are so strong I feel I could burst...
The flutter around my insides and make me dizzy,
They stop the words from reaching my mouth,
I want so badly to talk to you,
My brain screams the words
I LOVE YOU!
only to be deflected back into my mind by the wing of a butterfly...
never to be heard,
I am crushed by my unspoken love....for you.
