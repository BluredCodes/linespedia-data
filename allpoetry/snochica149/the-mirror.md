A single tear rolls down her face
She is through with cryin
The water fall that once flowed from her eyes has dried up

She comes to the bathroom door and pauses
Memories flow back to her of the poeple she thought she knew
The poeple that she called friends

She walked in and reluctenly looked in the mirror
she saw a refelection she didn't recongnize 
Her face was painted with the mask of conformity 

She turned the water on and washed away the paint
Suddenly a feeling of familiarity came over her
She started to see a person she once knew

She dried her face and looked back into the mirror
Yes she could see it now
Familiar blue eyes and blonde hair

She no longer looked like everyone else 
She now had her own identity and mind
And for the first time in 4 years she felt truly beautiful

With a last look in the mirror she smiled
The next day all of the make up and Abercrombie clothes were gone
She didn't care to be like everyone else anymore

She was finally happy with her life and her way of doing things
She was more beautiful and more radient then ever
