---
title: "Pieces"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "pieces"
source: "AllPoetry"

Spread across the floor of my mind,
Thousands of fragments of broken dreams,
Among the bleeding shards of hell to find,
Piece them together,ignore my screams,
Find out if I there are any cares left.
But I warn you now,nothing is as it seems.
