---
title: "I cut so deep....."
writer: "Midian"
slug: "i-cut-so-deep"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I cut so deep,
my life is a crimson stain.
I find the pain so divine.
If only I could share this,
I know you would be mine.
I slash my face oh so hard,
is'nt beauty on the inside?
I write with my life on the wall,
what brought on this cold,hard fall?
I listen to myself give in,
to the pain of my existance,
to the pain in my head,
to the pain that is reality.
So much pain I wish to be dead.
Then the pain will stop,
Or will it?.........
