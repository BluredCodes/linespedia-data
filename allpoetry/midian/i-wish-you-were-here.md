---
title: "I wish you were here"
writer: "Midian"

Never have I laid eyes upon you.
So why do you haunt my heart?
Never have I held you close.
Then why do I dream only of you?
I wish you were here...

Never have I heard your voice.
So why do I feel lost without it?
Never have I kissed your lips.
So why have I fallen for you?
How I wish you were here...

Never have I touched your face.
So why would I sell my soul for you?
Never have I told you I love you.
So why would I die for you?
I wish you were here...


For me...
