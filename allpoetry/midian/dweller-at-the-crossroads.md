---
title: "Dweller at the crossroads"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "dweller-at-the-crossroads"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14362-Dweller-at-the-crossroads-by-Midian"
---

I wait for him,
at the crossroad.
My breath comes sharp,
I notice the full moon dim.

The eyes of the dweller,
How they pierce my soul.
"I am here to make a deal,
Oh,foul beast of Hell!"

I utter my desire,
And the dweller responds,
A bolt of pure hell-fire,
slams into my body.

The smoke clears,
I open blood-red eyes,
and laugh through fanged maw.
The dweller has taken my fears.

Into the mist I run,
to stalk the night.
None shall escape my sight,
You'll cry for mercy I no longer have.....

...before I am done with you!
