---
title: "Song of the Siren"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "song-of-the-siren"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18140-Song-of-the-Siren-by-Midian"

Whisper in the night.Singing so sweet.Take over my mind.Let me lose myself,in your alluring songs.Chain my soul,to your lovely voice.I'm yours forever.My love,My Siren
