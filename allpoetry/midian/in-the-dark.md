---
title: "In the dark"
writer: "Midian"

I find myself alone-IN THE DARK!
Why can't I find you?
I fear the worst.
I need you here with me-IN THE DARK!

I cry out in fear-IN THE DARK!
Are you near?
I cannot see.
Things are moving-IN THE DARK!

I sink to my knees-IN THE DARK!
I give up hope. 
They are closer now.
I hear them breathing-IN THE DARK!

They beat me down-IN THE DARK!
They laugh at my pain.
They scar my mind.
They break my soul-IN THE DARK!

I feel you now-IN THE DARK!
I scream in rage.
I throw them back
I reach for you-YOU ARE MY LIGHT!
