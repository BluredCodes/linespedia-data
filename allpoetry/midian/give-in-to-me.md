title: "Give in to me..........."

I want you now,
You can't resist it,
Forget the love, 
Give in to the moment,
let our bodies mingle,
Burn me with passion,
let me taste your love,
learn my secrets,
as I learn yours,
sweat cools our skin,
lust burns our loins,
Give in to the feeling,
I caress your thighs,
my tongue finds yours,
as our hands explore,
the rhythm is set,
to the song of our hearts,
Beating in sync,
pumping hard,
racing faster,
until we can go no longer,
we hold each other,
until our next encounter.
