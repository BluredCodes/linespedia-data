---
title: "Leave"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "leave"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I feel so gross inside.
The hate festers within.
Eats me raw like a tumor.
Rots me inside out.

Why should I trust you?
You say you love me?
So what do you want?
I'll not give you anything!

You ask why I act this way.
What act?
I am who I am.
Get over it.

I'll never care,never love,
I don't need or want you,
You'll just hold me down.
leave me alone.
