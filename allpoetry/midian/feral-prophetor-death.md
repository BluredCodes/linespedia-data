title: "Feral Prophet(or Death)"

Sightless eyes gazing inward.
but they see more than yours.
The cosmos are an open book.
Which only he can read.
Iron will parts the aether.
Skeletal hands turn phantom pages.
and his minds eye knows,
Just what our fate shall be.
