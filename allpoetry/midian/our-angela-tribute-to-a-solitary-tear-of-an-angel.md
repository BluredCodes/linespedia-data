Our Angel of mercy,
is just like us,
just as human,
just as troubled.

Our Angel of poetry,
needs our help,
just as we need hers,
none of us can do it alone.

Our solitary Angel,
Needs us to care,
needs us to love,
and wipe her tears.

We all love our Angel,
and cannot fathom,
our lives without her,
So for all your friends I speak...

We love you Karen,
and are here for you,
you need not struggle alone,
anymore......

Sean,and all your friends here at Allpoetry.com
