---
title: "Stain"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "stain"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I struggle in vain.
I cannot handle anymore.
These emotions won't be chained.
I can no longer hide behind my wall.
I open up my veins.
I feel my flesh grow cold,
as my blood spills down the drain.
Do not mourn for me,
My life was a stain.Prewrite
