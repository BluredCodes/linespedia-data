---
title: "I break you..."
writer: "Midian"
slug: "i-break-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hate everything!
No one can touch me.
My wall is made of iron.
My heart is pure stone.

I am the ruiner,
I hurt you on purpose.
Your pain is fodder,
Fed to my ego.

I break the rules,
And I'll break you.
Don't try to push me,
You'll just get burned.

I am the "anti-everything loser",
I killed my conscience,
And buried it deep,
Just so I can break you all.
