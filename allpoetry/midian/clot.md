BaRe YoUr RoTtInG GaRdEn tO mE.
bUt THe fRUiT iS BiTtEr AnD ImPuRe.
I am DEaf To yOuR SeRpEntS TonGuE.
WhiSPeeR UnhEedEd LiEs InTo My EaR.
BlOOdY yOuR hAnDs UpOn My WaLl.
yoU CaNnOT ReACh mE AnYmORe.
mY bLeEdInG HeArT hAs cLoTtEd.
yOu dO Not MaTtEr aNyMoRe.
