title: "Evolving  (strong language)"
writer: "Midian"

I am changing inside.
I feel that I am dying.
That there is someone new.
Someone who is crowding me out.
AM I INSANE?
What the Fuck is wrong with me?
Negative emotions once buried deep.
Are flowing out of me like venom.
I find it hard to control my tongue.
I feel that soon I will fall over the edge.
That this new entity will take over.
And the person I was will die.
Maybe I am wrong.
Maybe I am just paranoid.
Or maybe I am far too right.
And maybe I am evolving.
