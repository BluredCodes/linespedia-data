title: "Sinner"
writer: "Midian"

Trembling in the half-light.
Burning me raw.
Tearing me apart inside.
Oh how I sin for you.
Now I taste you.
So honey sweet.
Your finger nails rake my flesh.
Oh to sin is to love.
We finish in bursting pleasure.
I kiss you tenderly.
But you push me away.
Oh to sin is to hate.
