---
title: "In my eyes"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "in-my-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Corrupt my hope,
in joyous hell.
Between the lines,
lick cream that smells.
I wish you weren't so beautiful,
in my eyes.
Crawling down my spine,
to make you stay.
I wish you weren't so beautiful,
in my eyes.
Covering your eyes,
to make you pay.
Beyond the high,
lies burning eyes.
Crashed down to soil.
Fucked up and coiled.
You want to feel it?
But you can't reveal it.
Concieved the meaning.
And caress the demon.
All those callous eyes,
How they infect your world,
So you pretend to reason.
But you've lost your soul.
