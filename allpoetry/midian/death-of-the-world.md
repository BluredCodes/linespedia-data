title: "Death of the world"

Why won't it end
Why do you mock me?
Why am I unfazed?
Your tongue was dull.

How can I breath?
When I forever drown.
It feels so damn hopeless,
Life will never release me.

Why the hell won't you take me?
I am what you made me!
This hating machine.
Why do you stare?

Don't you like the reflection of my soul?
You are my creator.
Let me go 
you no longer need me

You spit me out. 
I Try to save face
Praying,waiting,screaming,
For death to embrace me.
