Willowy limbs caress the wind.
Cryptic kaleidoscopic vocals sing.
Burning candlelit eyes of sorrow.
True beauty breaks my soul.
Lonliness increased my hunger,
on the broken backs of mothers.
Tainted lusts predict my misery.
Cyclopean mogrels give chase,
to the red shrouded harlots flame.
Infernal machines move inside.
Twisted imps dance on the bones.
And I am the only one who knows,
that our world is already dead.this is simaler to white zombies blood,milk,and sky song but only in rhythem
