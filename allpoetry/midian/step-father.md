Beat me again.Make my life hell.Sharpen your tongue,On my bones.Make your point,upon my skin.Break my reflection,with my face.Rape my soul.as well as body.Break my resistance.With your torture.Kill my faith.leave me brokenSkin my mind.With your words.Do you feel strong,When you beat a child?Does it make you a man,To crush my hope?I hope you die!I hope Belial takes you!I hope you burn forever!In the hell you made for me!!!!!!This is dedicated to that scum sucking son of a bitch who destroyed who I was and made me into the monster I am now!!!thanx alot you fucking prick!!I think about you sometimes and want to kill you!!!one day soon you pig fucker....YOU WILL BEG FOR ME!!!!There will be no mercy in me as there was none in you....until then 'Daddy' i will wait............
