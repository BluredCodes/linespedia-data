---
title: "I am reborn"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "i-am-reborn"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47142-I-am-reborn-by-Midian"
---

Born again in my mind.
Joy is fire in the sky.
Burning light makes me blind.
No more pain I feel so high.

Burning hate to cold indifference.
Wilting love means so little.
Everyone I touch suffers.
I've buried the blessed in cold spittle.

Heaven burns before my eyes.
Hell has frozen at my feet.
I can't be stopped do not try.
anger me and die in defeat.

Cold air fills my empty lungs.
Now I marvel at the destruction.
Armageddon has begun.
I am the youth of damnation.
