title: "Impervious"
writer: "Midian"

YOU CAN BEAT ME ALL YOU WANT
BUT YOU CANNOT HURT ME
YOU CAN CALL ME FOUL NAMES
BUT YOU CANNOT BOTHER ME
YOU CAN BURN ME WITH YOUR FLAME
BUT IT WILL NOT TOUCH ME
YOU CAN VEX ME WITH YOUR HATE
BUT I WILL IGNORE YOU
YOU CAN CURSE ME WITH YOUR GOD
BUT HE HATES YOU
YOU CANNOT FAZE ME
I'M NOW IMPERVIOUS TO YOU
AND NOW I HOLD THE POWER
YOUR REIN IS OVER 
SCREAM FOR ME
