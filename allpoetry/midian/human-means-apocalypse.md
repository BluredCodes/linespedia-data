Some will say that to be 'human',is to be civilized.I say we are just as savage,as any wild beast.As 'humans',as civilized beings,we rape our own mother as she sleeps,we tear her open and pull out her life.we rend her face to all for our selves.As 'humans', as righteous man,we throw our piety in the face of the poor.we beat our children,scar thier minds.All this we do behind closed doorAs 'humans',as animalswe are no better than a virus.We destroy all we touchWe never give backIsay,that to be humanyou must help others,just because they need it.When you take away,give a little back.When our children cry,love them.that is what makes us human.....compassion.
