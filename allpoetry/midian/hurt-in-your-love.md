---
title: "Hurt in your love...."
writer: "Midian"
slug: "hurt-in-your-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16139-Hurt-in-your-love....-by-Midian"
---

Cut me open,
with sharp lies.
Scar me deep,
sever all ties.

I am your dog,
rub my nose in shit.
Pull me in deep,
your love is a pit.

I'm your toy,
break me apart.
Fuck with my head,
tear me apart.

Drag me in mud,
I am your slave.
Force me to love,
make me behave.

Stamp out my joy,
pack it in a crate.
Keep me at hand,
beat me with hate.

If you love me,
hear my plea.
break the chains,
let me free
