title: "Decay of the soul"
writer: "Midian"

I see you dead and bleeding.
fear chills my flesh,
fever burns my mind,
"what have I done?"

Eyes glazed and sightless,
no more beating heart,
lips dry and cracked,
"Who were you anyway?"

Flesh turned Blue,
A memory recalled,
cannot breathe,
"You betrayed me!"

And we both stare,
"I used to love you."
Now I smile.
"Now I hate you."

" you took all I had!
Those many years ago.
You thought you could escape!
You thought you could hide!"

"But not from me.
and now that your dead,
my murder avenged,
I can finally rest......."
