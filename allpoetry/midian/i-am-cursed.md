TERROR WITHIN,TERROR WITHOUT.
DEVILS DANCE UPON MY SOUL.
THE WELL IS EMPTY,EATEN BY DROUGHT.
I CAN NO LONGER FIND MY HOPE.

FEAR CHILLS ME TO THE BONE.
SWEATING,TURNING OVER AND OVER.
I WAKE UP STILL ALONE.
NO LOVE IN MY LIFE,SO EMPTY.

NO CAUSE,I HAVE NO STABILITY.
SPINNING DOWN THE VORTEX.
HOW DID I AQUIRE gODS ENIMITY?
WILL MY CURSE EVER BE LIFTED?Your just a copy of an imitation
