Eyes full of malice.Talons caress my flesh.Winged demoness' so closeHow long will the game go on?Wicked smiles drawn.Fangs sink in deep.Talons rend my flesh.Mouths drink away my life.But there is no pain.Just raw and pure pleasure.An almost horrid joy.But it does not last long.My life slips past their lips,and into ravenous gullets.My mind clouds as eyes go blank.So is my joyful death.So is the feast of the Succubi.A sacrifice to daemonic lords.Whose names i cannot fathom.Which Dark lord shall my death please?Succubi is plural for succubus,a type of demon that tempts men into sexual acts then drag their souls to the Infernal planes of the Abyssnote that male succubi are called incubus (incubi  plural)
