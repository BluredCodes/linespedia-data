---
title: "Ape of God"
writer: "Midian"

We are victims,
Of our own evolution.
Drag our knuckles,
Until they bleed.

If God made us in his image,
Is he a monkey too?
Monkey see,monkey do,
So many apes have said.

Maybe this question,
Is better unanswered.
Because maybe in the end,
We are all just apes of God.
