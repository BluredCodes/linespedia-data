---
title: "My pain"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "my-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44665-My-pain-by-Midian"
---

Torn between life and death.
Does hope really exist?
Painful memories won't go away.
How can I cope with this?
Lost family will not be found.
Why won't they forgive me?
Love is a myth that I disbelieve.
Why is hatred so easy for me?
If life is a struggle I cannot win.
Then why don't I just give up?
