Darkness is my home.
My soul feels at ease here.
I blanket myself with shadows.
The light is my hated enemy.
I fight it relentlessly.
But even I know.
That I can never win.
All shadows are eventually devoured,
by the so called purity of light.
I am no different.
But I shall fight to the end,
and ensure that I am not the last.
