---
title: "Lost Dreams"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "lost-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I cherish you,my life long love.Your eyes,the deep blue sea that drowns me.Your hair,the golden rays of sun that warm me.How I love you,my cherished one.How it pains me,the wrongs I've done.So cold was I,I never told you.It was so in considerate of me.I never told you this before.But now it's too late,my love.My cherished angle has left me.How I miss you,light of my life.I lift my cherished angel to my heart.I can never let your memory fade,and now though it pains me,I must let you rest,I stand....I walk away with tear in eye through the gate.I look back at your grave.My flowers a bright rainbow in a grey world,but we will be together again someday.Until then,my cherished one.........
