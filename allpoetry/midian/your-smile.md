---
title: "Your Smile"
writer: "Midian"

Your smile does so much,
you could never know,
Every time I make you laugh,
my heart skips a beat.

When I think I can't go on,
your smile gets me through,
there is no darkness,
it can't melt away.

Your Smile warms my soul,
melts the ice round my heart
let's me know that you love me,
what power you have over me.

When you smile,I don't feel numb,
I feel more alive than before,
I embrace you as I could no one else,
When you smile,my pain goes away.
