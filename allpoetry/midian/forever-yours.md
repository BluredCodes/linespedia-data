---
title: "Forever yours"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "forever-yours"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13883-Forever-yours-by-Midian"
---

I lie in bed.
Dreaming of you.
It seems so long ago,
that you were here.

Some called it young love.
They say it will pass,
I know the truth.
We were something special.

Tender kisses,which once were shared.
loving embraces,that meant so much.
How I miss my time with you.
It just seems colder since you moved away.

I write you letters,Declare my devotion.
But the miles between us are too many,
And the thoughts shared so few,
Just remember my love,I'm forewer yours.
