---
title: "Wicked"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "wicked"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44664-Wicked-by-Midian"
---

Melted hearts made of tin.
Rending wounds upon the skin.
Weeping children bathed in sin.
Horrors unknown writhe within.
Foul changes shall soon begin.
Take it in with wicked grin.
The road to hell is traveled again.
