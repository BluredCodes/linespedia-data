I am a lost soul.
Stuck forever inbetween.
Still too blind to see,
all that lays before me.

I wish I could believe.
I wish I could see,
but I'm always held back,
can never stay on track.

Every rose may have it's thorn,
but mine is made of razor-wire.
My flesh is always torn,
my blood the fuel that feeds the fire.

I break down the walls.
I let my emotions go.
I must pick myself up,
from lifes tragic falls.this is kind of a first draft I am not sure if I like it's current form feel free to give me ideas 
                      Darkside
