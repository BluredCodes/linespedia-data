---
title: "Just shut up"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "just-shut-up"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18157-Just-shut-up-by-Midian"

I am not deaf.I am not dumb.I just don't care.So save your prayer.It means nothing to me.If your God existed,He'd hate you so.But that is fine.Better you than me.I just don't believe.So save your prayer.I don't want to hear it.you must be confused,If you think I give a fuck.So just shut the hell up!heh heh heh
