---
title: "There are those who care"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "there-are-those-who-care"
source: "AllPoetry"

Even in this dark,cold world,
where the hurt infects us all,
There are those who bring us hope,
There are those who care.

Everyone is special.
Every one is loved.
Never give up on yourself.
There are those who care.

There is love in this world.
though pain and suffering may exist,
So do love and hope.
There are those who care...

for you......This is dedicated to all of us who need to know that the world is more than a theater of pain,everone is loved by someone even if we blind ourselves to that fact.
my blessing's to you all!
                          Sean
