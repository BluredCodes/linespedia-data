---
title: "Strings of barbed-wire"
writer: "Midian"
slug: "strings-of-barbed-wire"

Never shall I dream,I'll hurt forever.Pull my strings,watch me scream,I'll never know peace.I am a puppet,Society's plaything.Hurt me and love it'Pull my strings of barbed-wire.All I know is hate,Wind me up with razor blade key.I wish for another fate.wind me up watch me go.Use me for fun.Abuse me for pleasure.Throw me away when your done.Crush me at your leisure.Pull my strings of barbed-wire,Watch me dance.I can never tire,I'm locked in a deadly trance.
