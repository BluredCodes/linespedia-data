I've been a bad boy,
I've broken all your rules.
Tie me up,I'm your toy.
Now smack my ass,make me drool.

How I love the feel of leather,
Wrap it round my wrists and feet.
So much fun in any weather.
Pinch my nipples,oh thats sweet.

I feel the warmth of your body.
Every curve wrapped in rubber.
Oh thats nice,feels so naughty.
Tease my slowly,make me blubber.

Grind your love in my face,
I lick it up,sweet as honey.
Give me more,just one more taste.
I eat it up,oh so yummy.

We both tremble,bodies bare.
Fingernails leave scars on my chest.
Kiss your breasts,stoke your hair.
You relax,I've got the rest.Sticks and stones,
may break my bones,
but whips and chains excite me!
