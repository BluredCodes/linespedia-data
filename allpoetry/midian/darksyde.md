---
title: "DARKSyDE"
writer: "Midian"

i am the blackest piece of the human soul,
forever will i fear myself.
Pain and suffering are my shepherds,
flocking me towards darker goals,

At my birth the angels cried.
Despair world,your bell has tolled.
Nothing's safe until i've died.
God himself pities me,
i have no pride.

Slash myself just for grins.
Pain,sorrow,and blood mingle again.
One day all will end,
until that day i am Darksyde.this is what i think of me sometimes
all the"i"'s are lower case on purpose here
