Sky clad

I choose this moment to be free

Walking naked 

Feeling this caress against my skin

Waking softley

To this beauty that surrounds me

Every sight

A sensation beckoning me to realisation

Whispers on the wind

Remind me of other times and places that I loved

Memories of now

Bring me to thankfulness of friends and loves of my soul

This sharing of hearts

Gives me the strength to be myself when all else feels lost

Spirit I call you

To awaken my soul so that I may shine with the true light of love

Mind seek not

Answers to riddles that qualify a deeper intuitive response

Body my temple

Teach me of wisdom and the quest for healing

Life buries me

Yet I favour the courage to go on

Joyfully.
