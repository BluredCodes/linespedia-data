She smiled and looked at me

Listen to your soul she said

To the music residing there

Think of all the times when you were lost

Feel all the times when you have felt love

She then took my hands and led me outside

It was raining lightly

And the sun was peaking through the clouds in the distance

The trees around us seemed to be alive

And I could hear faint strains of gypsy music floating on the wind

She squeesed my hand briefly to remind me of our shared purpose

And started to move an ancient step

We danced together that day

Listening to our souls and rejiocing in memories

Celebrating our moment in time

I have never danced like I did that day

That day that I came alive

Listening to the loving sweet sounds of our souls.
