A poet sits upon solitary stone
Cloak enshouds billowing mysterious
Bardic clasp glitters 
Catching the last rays of winter's light
Harp curved and nestled, padded protective
No more words form from ancient lore
Silent lips bespeak whispers of a gusting wind
Eyes clouded whislt seeing other times
Capturing fleeting moments 
Like shards of shatered glass
Distant dreams affect this reality

Watching and waiting 
The hawk flys majestic 
Wing tips carressing the wind
Eyes seeing hidden dreams

So too does time enrapture
With-in the mind of the poet
Sending thoughts to those who love
Twining the streams of magic
Mind searching for esoteric truths
Waiting for that time
When the dance is all that there need be

An awakening from percieved insanity
Gives a coagulant revival
Of friends and family and lover's knots
And breath renews heart's old

A bardic song floats the wind
Dancing merrily in it's dream
Spreading in it's wake
Rites of strength, love and joy

Quitely now....
