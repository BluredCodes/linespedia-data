---
title: "Myself and I."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "myself-and-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

My heart has no tomorrow
My mind knows not yesterday's shadow
Future dreams have not yet been created

I am deep

So deep that I fear I will never reach salvation
Fears have encumbered me
Locked away my mortal soul
No one can get through to me
A barrier I have created 
From long ago hurts to fierce to name
The earth moves in strange ways
Offering false haven from the likes of I
Voices talk incesant
About how to help
To heal this monster of mine
Western medicines dull 
And take the powers born of me
No longer the person you knew
A shadow of another time
Eastern dreams beckon 
Teachings of ancient wisdoms
Yet that path takes time 
And streches this agony
To breaking point
Who am I to feel this way
To know my self 
And still not know the cure

Shall I take the dark path
With it's promises of peace
Dearly brought

Shall I take the path of light
Promise eternal in the end
Strength is it's price

Shall I hover
In this no person's land
Between two opposing forces

Myself and I


A dedication to all those who suffer..
You are loved for who you are......:)
