Crazy

What's crazy

Wind a piece of string around your finger

Till the tip is white

Take the other end of the string

Tie it to the ceiling securely

Now stand on chair

And leap off

To spin wildly

Around the room



Is this crazy?



As crazy as it is to say

Hey, I'm feeling this too.


Damn Crazy.no limbs were hurt in the making of this poem....
