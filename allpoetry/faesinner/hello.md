hello my friendi am thinking of uand of dreamspast present and foreveryou are the story that i am writingthe lover i sleep with and the exhilaration in my heartthe dream that is realthe breath of love that gives me lifeand the spark in my eyethe swagger in my stepthe bumping of teeth in a kissand the silly thoughts in my mindthe laughter i hear when sleepingthe sun and the moon and the stars when i wake besides youand the song that refuses to leave methe unexpected of my lifethe buzz in my earsand the joy on my tonguethe smile that comes from my eyesthe voice that saids i love uand the deepest blue that is my soulthe breaking of the threethe rain that i rejoice inand the inner most voice that i listen toyou are the soul i had forgottenand the dream that loves me
