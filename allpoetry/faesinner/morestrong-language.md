More, More, Fuckin' more....
It feels like this is the end of my stength
worked so hard
and for what;
not loyalty thats for sure,
not support or understanding
just money
you give more of yourself
and it gets taken away
and when it's finally used up

they still want more

more

more

more

I've got just enough strength left
to believe in myself
and live my life
for my self.

more

more

more


No moretis notice time....
