title: "Ahhh...pluto!"

Pluto dances its sluggish way
Through my quickening veins
Pumping this crimson death
Anger fuels these noble souls
As righteousness insanity takes control

Death walks these streets
Flashing fire these walls

People die

For the nobility of the upper hand
Waste not that thought for the common man

The ability to write gives you power 
Over those who know not?
Lop of that frontal lobe
And jump into the pit
Then we will see

Who is master of this universe?Damn those modern day war movies...
