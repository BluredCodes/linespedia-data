(Please no stars if you would)

These words I hear 
Are voiced true
From this soul 
I love 
No matter my attempt 
To save and revive
Somethings need 
To be learnt
It does not save
To hide the tears
That flow
Unheaded this day
Words stumbling
To say
How I feel
Always in the way

Never will you know
How I love you
Strange it may seem at fisrt
But it has always been true
Even though not having followed paths 
Dreamt by you

I can not explain why 
It has turned out thus
Old and new
The spirals shift and dream
Feelings will never change
Passions remain the same
Yet water flows 
Where it will

I reach out knowing
Deep inside
That my own death will
Produce a different sort of 
Release

All I can
Offer
Is my dreams

These I gift to you
In hope of
One day 
you will find
Deep and Loving

Peace

Cinder, I am sorry.
