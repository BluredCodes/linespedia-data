Magic floats upon the wind tonight
Bringing lost souls together
Love and journey's met
Time to tell of wounds related
As I listen gently to your call
I know you are in pain dear one
I feel it when I read 
But I know that your magic is from
New depths and old
It touches one
Deeply
By those who know
Slowly is this healing 

You will 
Find your place

The heart 
Of your dreams.:)for you Mysty....:)
