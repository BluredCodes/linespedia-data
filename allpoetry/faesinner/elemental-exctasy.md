title: "Elemental Exctasy."

Cruel flame take from me 
This elemental exctasy
Burn false inpurities
As the dross flows away
And leaves me the 

The white hot core

Of you

Thoughts wander
Inside an empty shell
That once housed
Golden halls 
And fleeting sounds
Of friends
And lover

No longer do they dance
But wrestle uselessly
Amongst themselves

There is no answer

Love lost 
Time stopped
Memories paused
Thoughts stilled
Heart beating it's last 

Emotional cry for what was
And what has 
Never 
Been

I dance alone
Upon this deserted shore
Do I remember you
You
Who are you

My lost love
