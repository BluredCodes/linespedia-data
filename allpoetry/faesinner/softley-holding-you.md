title: "Softley Holding You."
writer: "FaeSinner"

Angel
Asleep in a window
On a hot summers night
So far away
Always so close

Dreaming magic's dream
Protected and loved
By those who know
Shall never forget
The gift that you
Share

Youself

Stay in this heart
Away from scared times
Held so gently 
In this palm of mine
Safe to keep
This wild womyn
Eyes of wisdom 
Smiles of delight
Truth seeker
Dancer of old paths and new

Sleep deep little angel
Safe in this night's embrace
Held in arms ephemeral
Hearts beating
in tune

Softley
Holding
You........:)*
