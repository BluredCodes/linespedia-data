i am the wounded healeri walk the paths of nightwhen u think your blindedill be your guiding light!i am the wounded healeri wander the paths of dayand when your lost in their twisting's and turningsill show you the way!come, come,come with meI'll show you the waycome, come, come with mehip, hip, hooray!i am the wounded healerand you and i are the sameto you it can get scaryto me its just a game!i am the wounded healeri bleed day and nightonce i had a look at itgave me a bit of a fright!come, come, come with mei'll show u the waycome, come,come with mehip, hip,hooray!i am the wounded healerand in the dark i have seen my soulbut what i have seen therewas actually quite droll!i am the wounded healerno ordinary band aid will doit'll have to be something betterwhat about super glue!
