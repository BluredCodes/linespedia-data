title: "'Unchain my Teddy Bear'."
writer: "FaeSinner"

Slip in from
behind
wrap around
fingers fumbling
hook need 
red hot and bitter
into flesh
and muscle
bruise me
push past
time
these moments of
love
always that dream
what is
chased away
embraced
like
tears from
the sky

I dream
Fleeing
All
In unknown
Panic
Crouching
Frightful
Defence of 
My space
Invaded anyway
No time 
To breathe
No want to 
Say words that 
Break me
Like glass
That shatters
Into a million
And clover covers
The ground
So that 
I can
Flee
Bare footed
Across illusion

This is
The dream
Subconscious
Mental torment
Of now

Words
Scrawled
Wet 
Revealing
Before you
As I
Whisper
