That's it
I'm over this existance
No more rollercoaster ride
I'm sending my tickets back
No need for a refund
I really couldn't be bothered
No sleep
And no way through
Alone in my aloneness
Trapped as it were
Santa
Whom ever you are
Have a good one mate
And thanks for the beer
I'll see you on the 
Other side
When all is quiet and
Tranquil
And then
When the morning light hits the
The window and
Transfers it knowlegde to me
I'll disapear in a misting of smoke
Cause trying to understand two sides to any story
Is like reading a poem backwards
Unnesasary 
And hurtful...
You think I hold everything
Little do you know
I just hold myself
And rock forwards
And backwards
In time with my own thoughts
And wondering why
In this time of love
On this earth
I feel not
loved.
