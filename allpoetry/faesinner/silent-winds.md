title: "Silent Winds."

Silent winds smell of moisture and sex; desire.

Hands firm and masculine; wanting.

Emotions hot and burning; prelude.

Skin flushes in anticipation; surge.

Lips poised over areola; ascension.

Tongue flickers lightly over skin; exposure.

Teeth bite and nip; pulsate.

Need surrenders to elemental passion; seduction.

Breath sighs release; amour.is it hot tonight, or is it just me?
