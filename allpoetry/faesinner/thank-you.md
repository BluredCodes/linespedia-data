Suspension; this diamond hardness that freezes my mind
Refracted symbolism catching thoughts before impact
Silently working sacred sounds through to an outside world
Visual discordancy plays havoc upon this tired sight

Reach behind this moment and plunge into the midnight erotic
Open again this appeture to see if life still demands?
Such is the quiet time of the soul poised in the wind
Billowing darkness ravaging this one's warp into weft

~White light exhales misting upon a moon lit scape
~Grey time swirls alarming; eddies and singularities
~Figures in translucent drapery emit awareness
~Sounds rocket uncontrollably, multiplied by sentience

Hands warm and encompassing embrace a fragile longing
Soothing furrowed brow and reaching deeper into the dark
Etheric modulation warms this icy resolution
Faceted halogen realities dance to a ritual beat

Voices singing friendship and love reach this soul
until the night is over it knows now those...
Grey breaks to a dawn shadowed 
but a dawn none the least.
Time resembles
life again.

thank you.
