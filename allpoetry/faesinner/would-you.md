---
title: "Would You?"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "would-you"

What if I told you that I had the answers for all of your fears?

Would you believe me?

What if I said that when you fear all you have to do is breathe

And you would be fine

Would you breathe?

What If I said that I could hear the smile in your heart?

And that it reflected my own.

Would you then, smile? 

What if I said that the stars were friends of mine? 

And that they shown especially for you

Because I asked

Would you then look up at the night sky?

What if I said that dreams were there for the living?

And that living was a special type of dream?

Would you?

Then

Believe
