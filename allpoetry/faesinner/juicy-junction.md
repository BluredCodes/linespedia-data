---
title: "Juicy Junction."
writer: "FaeSinner"

Your the voice that speaks thus
With an age old wisom

That communicates golden during dark tides
Pushes electrodes into skin and watches gleeful
As I dance upon wires of scintilating facillity

Dark whispers in the wind
Excite these senses mine
Awaken old wants to live again
Amongst playful rabbits
And horse shoes

Flight unknown to some
Gives it's final rest
Pleasing beyound compare
For fair skinned pixies
And dark damsels

Vulnerable looking wake shy
Breaths a kiss good day
In love with this plastic
At juicy junction

Let's play...
