i miss youcan't tell how muchchains in place around my heartthe wreath drags around my neckand rather than drown i hold backand consider that death holds me in silencei am only peripheralbut the pain slaps meawake and cripplingi can't breath for its entiretyas fresh as yester yearit will never go awayi stood on the outsideand the burden trippledguilt assails meunrealistic needmeets reality founded in realitythe only way through the darknessis to taste it's bloodi never want to go through that againyet the possibily is that i willand i long to healwhat can not with wordsbut with the breath of lifebeyound death
