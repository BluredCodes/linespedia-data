title: "Staring at You."
writer: "FaeSinner"

So
You ask
Whats this
A scarey tale to frighten 
What witts you have left 
To the end of the earth
Of darkness personified
Fear and loathing to set
Your skin crawling
You want to throw up
Empty your guts
To spill on the floor
Crawl away to another place
And time
Look to some one else 
To offer saftey
Santuary
Something


There's the thing
To dull this madness
Sooth those aches and pains
To be normal again
Staring at your face
In the steel polished mirror of society
To drink and be normal 


Shall I
Dance the true dance like this
Carry away this madness of mine
To another place
Dig at this heart of mine
Or shall I
Look at this demon
You say I possess
And rightfully call it my own


Demon no longer
Power
Personified
To be part of the balance
The whole
I have my slot
Will you dance with me
Demon and all
Or will you kiss of this sacred soul
And turn from me in search of angels
Pure and forsaken
Not seeing their own 

Darkness

Scared yet
I'm not
I'm just beginning
To have fun

Lightning falls from the sky
I am free
And believed
And 
Truely whole

Smell the ozone
The thoughts I have
Is this just a poem
Or indeed
The last
Of
My magic

?
