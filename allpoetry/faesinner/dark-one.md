---
title: "Dark one"
writer: "FaeSinner"

Intriged I am by you,
dark one.

She who dances around me
in the star light.
Unable i am to catch you,
fleeting spirit.
Yet when 
I am with you,
my soul
knows no other
and I am 
electrified
by your mental touch.

I dream of when we can be more.
When touching
is a language
that we share.
I long to be with you,
to kiss your eyes,
taste your lips
and trace my tounge around yours;
lingeringly.
To communicate
tactile and explorative.
To hold you 
in one place,
at one time
and be every thing
that I dream we can be.

Then,
will I hear myself
loving you,
warm and blissful
and feel the joy of
your heart meeting mine
in its passionate embrace?

Do I intrige you,
dark one?
