Space junkies pirouette 
Upon soaring silvered spires
High above the normal world
Hypnotic trance 
Slow dance
Medicine seeking
Escaped valocity
Listening to bass beats 
And repedative noise
That spirals inside 
Vibration signals songs silent
Soothing sounds 
Animalistic joy
The beat dances still
Loud and strong
Smiling self remains
To dance alone
With ten million others
Each with his or her
Mesmerised
