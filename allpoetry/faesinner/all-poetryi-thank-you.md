He takes to the podium in his best three year old suit
And looks around as the crowd before him starts to notice his presence
Clears his throat si that the sound echoes around the stadium
Looks at the post it notes stuck to the inside of his jacket's arm

Ahem...
They look up expectant
He gathers his wits
And begins

"I would like to thank all those at all poetry of whom have made it possible for me to be here tonight...if it wasn't for all of you and you know who you are...I would not be here at this time...you who have supported me and followed my career as it were, whom have helped me become the poet I am today, allowed the true essence of Connor to come to the fore...
I thank you
 
 I'd like to thank the internet for being ever present to provide the platform for my creativity and for allowing me to discover new levels of addiction and sleep deprivation...once again thank you.

 I would also like to thank my flat mate whom has put up with my idiosyncries and knows not to come near me when in cyber trance least i rip his eye balls out in mindless rage....thanks mick...you'll get your turn sometime in the future when I switch the bloody computer of....Good luck mate.

 I would finally like to thank all of you whom have made "Ooga Booga" such a success and with out your help such brilliance of mind would not have been possible.it is so exciting that we have just recently signed a deal with a large sitcom comglomerate to do a series based on the ideals that "Ooga Booga" presents...

Wait a minute...
Ooga Boga...? 
A sitcom...
Yeah right....
Huh?
Oh....
It was a dream....


Thank god....


Gotta get out more......hey mick you makin' a coffee?Happy new year all....:)
