Five Brawny Highlanders
Did perchance happen apon a maiden tall and fair
About to be eaten by a Dragon
They set about to rescue her

Hang about Dragon they said
How about a wee bit o' brew 
To wash down with that sweet morsel
When you're through

Oh aye, the Dragon said
That I wouldn't mind at all
But then again where are my manners
Let me make a toast before I feast this maiden tall

So they poured that brew of the Brawny Highlanders
Into five horns and a bucket for the Dragon
With a toast to good heath "Slainte mhor"
They drank enough to drain two flagons

Ahh said the Dragon This is a grand brew
But the as the Five Brawny Highlanders looked on
The Dragon did gasp and flop about
It cried Out this brew is to strong!

With it's tail between it's legs
The Dragon flew away
It looked back with a baleful stare and said
I'll come back and eat the lass some other day

What do you reckon's wrong with it said one Highlander
It tastes all right to me noted another
Tha Dragons got a bit of weak stomach
He wasn't worth the bother

As they laughed amongst themselves
These Five brawney Highlanders
They looked over to the maiden fair
Whom had woken from fainting in fear

"Would ye be wantin' a wee bit o' brew?"
