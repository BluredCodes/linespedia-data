---
title: "That day."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "that-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Can't stop thinking of that day.
when your eyes told me
the truth of your soul.
You are so brave
to step out into the unknown.
I think I held my breath
the whole time
and I can't help but think,
some things are meant to be.
Would that I had more time,
to share with you,
that day.
