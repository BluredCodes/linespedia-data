Hold me in thought proactive

Dream a scape unimaginative

Scratch the chalk of that memory 

Against the wall of my reality

Push me back against my own madness

In a time when all is foolish

Strap those filaments around my torso

Flail in unconcerned distain

Trap a fire storm in my organ

As release rocks me sublime

Limbs melt trickling slowly

Sentience travels death's indifference

Drooling trancendant gastronome

Revealing deception's subterfuge

Embrace this prohibition
