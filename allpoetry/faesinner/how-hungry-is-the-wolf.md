How hungry is the wolf that sits beside meI thirst for it's knowledge and travel the old pathsShaman i may bebut not in this conscious mindI yearn to teach what i knowor at least what i think i knowA wolf spends half its life learningthe other teachingImparting knowledge in the snowIts eyes never leave mineand it knows me intrisically; as i know itWe are joined and inseparableOur strengths and weaknesses sharedFollowing the wolf is like chasing your shadowit goes where it willsand i must follow ittogethar in this halve light flickering along the pathUnhurried are our discoveriesas we travel old paths and newWe are surrounded by manyrecognition amongst the flowHow hungry is the wolf?as hungry as iand more so and the night has its shadowand i am the wolf
