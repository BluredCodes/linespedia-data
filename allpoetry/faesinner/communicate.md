Make love to me

Raise this dragon

From it's slumber

Reach into this

Sacred domain

Heart first

Followed by

Mind

Body

Soul

It's truth

What it means to you

Show me those eyes

That smile

I want to hear

Your thoughts

The voice

Inside your mind

Telling me 

Everything

Wants

Needs

Desires

Fears

Sorrows


Communicate

Impassioned

This

Ephemeral

Chaotic

Conceptualization

Of

Mutable

Exchange
