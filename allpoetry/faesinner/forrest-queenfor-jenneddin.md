Queen of the forest I call to you
Dance now for the world to see?
You are a sight enchanting to behold
But only those bravest will receive thy soul

Woman both wild and soothing
I ask for this night?s audience
To sing with thee amongst old friends
Laugh and sing away your time till dawn's end

Flash of light amongst the trees
Floating about merrily it seems
Golden glow such a sweet nimbus
Giving light and love to ancient timbers

Flickering about here and there
Little ones accompany like fireflies
All the colors of the rainbow it seems
Come from the hearts of children's dreams

Before I knew it she is here
Divinely awaiting my earnest plea
Goddess of this forested sight
Will you dance with me to night?

Eyes the color of golden hue
Skin so pale it reflects the moon
She smiles mischievously
Beckoning that she would join this dance with me

Wild the whirlwind that followed anon
In her arms we did fly so free
Like love I did all but swoon
Under the light of the fairies moon

All manners of creatures came out to play
To dance and sing and frolic about
Pixies, gnomes and wood folk nymphs
Ogres, trolls and willow the wisps

Pixies glowed with warm little lights
Unicorns amazed all with their shinny coats
A dragon or two was seen in the air
Elves provided the wonderful fare

Before we knew it dawn had come
It was time for the queen to depart
To take her entourage home for the day
To wait until another time to play

Sadly I bid my heartfelt goodbye
The queen smiled and whispered to I
Next time the moon shines with that certain glow
Forget get us not to us you may go

With a merry heart I set on my way
Tired and lightheaded to home I did sway
Next time the moon shone that glorious light
