Here comes the fire

Here comes the fire

Here comes the fire

Then why am I standing

Here 

With this cup of water
Expecting to save myself

Ha ! !

The wind gusts up
Whipping this cracling hell towards me
Hot and furious and so very beautiful

Memories of other times
Float to the surface of my mind
Of how I have used water
To circumvent this tradedy

It is only now
I realise that
I never had enough

Water

I drink enjoying these memories

and

Walk slowly forward



To embrace the flames
