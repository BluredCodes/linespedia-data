---
title: "Kissing me"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "kissing-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

fell asleep in your arms
kissing
sleep took me away
one moment to the next
dreaming
pregnant with memory
i woke abruptly
and your still there 
kissing me 
with 
your soul
