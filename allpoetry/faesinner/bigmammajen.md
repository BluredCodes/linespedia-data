Be da dar dar de da

Bigmammajen bar da de dar da

She 's the one
whom shares her laugh's with us
Who's sadness at the moment
Is causing quite a fuss

She's writting all sorts of things 
We normally wouldn't see
They are all so exceptionally brilliant
She's gonna take over all poetry....

From erotica to angst
Her poems are qiute deep and lonely
Hoping to deal with her feelings
She reaches out to all of us 
Who think of her so highly

One day I can only hope 
That you'll find your well-being again
Cause its always a pleasure
To look out for you
And call to you friend....

Bigmammajen bar da de dar da
Bigmammajen bar da de dar
Bigmammajen bar da de dar da

Be da dar dar de da
