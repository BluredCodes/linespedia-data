These 
Petals 
Float
Surrounding 
This 
Soul 
Caressing
Like dreams fleeting 
And wishes dreaming
Caputured my heart an eon ago
Wild woman dancing
Protecting the entrance 
To her soul
Eyes like a firey tempest
Defending heath and heart
No one can walk these paths
She said
Dancing ancient 
Tapestries
Before me
Whirling away 
Enchantments
To confuse
Mortal minds
Oh what joy to watch
This wild soul

Deeper than the shallows of mere time
Warmer than a sun's flaring ferocity
Caring beyound mere boundaries
Oh how these sensations beckoned
For this was her
Dancer of the shadows
Hawk of the night

Fear not wild one
I know these paths also
Walked alone forever
known lifes cruelest lessons
Lived the initiation
Tasted it's blood
Been marked by fools seeking power
Felt the inner burning
And revealed it's price
Slept the lowest pits of my eternity
Waken to grimmy truth's reality
Risen above all of this 
To follow my soul's true questing

Seek thee to scare me wild soul?
I was afire finding a soul as deep as i
To long the search had taken
I had given up
All hope searching
And now i have found 
A twin seeking soul
Dreaming the same dream
Waiting for a true heart
To beleive

Let not thee
Fail thy soul 
This way
Become the fire
That swims in thy heart
And clease thy life 
Fear not thine enemy true
Ror thou art strong beyound all measure
Reach for those who love thee
Start the dream for a life that illumintes
Believe and choose lovingly

Petals 
Float
Surrounding 
This 
Soul 
Caressing
Like 
Dreams 
Fleeting 
And 
Wishes 
Dreaming:))
