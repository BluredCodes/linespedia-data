You are my muse 
Such that has never been seen
Felt
Touched
Heard
Kissed
Licked
Scupltured
Painted
Written
Dreamt........

Will you pose for me
stand naked
And vulnerable
To my scrutiny
To this artistic yearning
What it sees
What it needs....

Will you let me
Mould you
Set your spirit free
Muliti-coloured 
Three dimensional
Mystical
Unique
As I can only dream

My hands shake
Trembling with extacy
Words tumble in my mind
Forms shape
Ideas emerge
I feel this quickening
And I begin to see you as you truley are
Your self
Free
Beautiful

Breath taking........

Oh my muse 
Come to me......
