Blue comes to mind
It's colour radiates before all others
Deep and delightful
I yearn to towards this most challenging of colours

I take my shirt of
Slowly so you can take in all of my visage
Muscles rippling like snakes guilded in silk
You seem mesmerised
Sitting there
I can see it in your eyes 
In the little smile that plays so mischeviously

I strain towards blue
Finding my way 
Each breath an intention of my purpose
You move not 
As you know it would spoil the moment
Your breast covered in the lightess of fabrics
Brushes my thigh
And sends bolts of pleasure arching through my body
I instinctivley pull towards it
Nearly losing my concentration
You laugh quitely as I come closer
You know my intent now
And the glint in your eye reflects my own
One more movement
And you are all mine
yes I can feel it
I know that I can do it
Your eyes widern in surprise
You know it to
I plunge towards my goal
And deep in my passion
I under estimate my reach
And miss
Falling past you 
To fall heavily on to the floor

Your peel of laughter
Echoes across the room 
Loud and startling
As you shout

YOU LOSE BOYYO!!!
GET YOUR PANTS OFF!!
