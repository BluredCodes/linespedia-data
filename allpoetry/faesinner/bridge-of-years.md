This bridge of years has taken it's toll
The passive ignormity locks down 
This unfortunate heart
Into many places at once
Letting the mask dissapate 
I'll listen to the words 
That i have witnessed
My own truth

I can not help any save my own destruction
Why did i even bother?

No more to live this ambiguos life......:|
