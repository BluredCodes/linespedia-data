I can not think
Words fail me
This noise 
Loud 
And insistant
I know not what to do
Should I 
Leap?

Ooga Oo Bo Bo
Boga Boga Bobo
Oog Oog Gaa?
Booga Boo Boga
Oga Oooga

Sweat trickles down
Hot and clammy
I am sticky 
With this resin of need
Hands tug
Come they say
Shall I follow?

Ooga OoOoOOga Oga
Booga Bo
Ooga Ooga Bo Goo
Ooga Boooo?

Yes!
no more will 
I sit
Resistant 
To this need
Shaking this skin
I leap into the arms
Of this beat
Dancing forth
Free and
Wild
At last!

Ooga Booga Bo
Booga Bo
Booga
