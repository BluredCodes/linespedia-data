Hey Hun, what do you want to do tonight?
Shall we play like we used to?
Just to ligten things up a bit?
You want to play 'dress-ups'?
Okay then, shall i go first?

.....He saids.....

A Vampire of old
So dark and mysterious
One who walks the shadows of the night
Looking for damsels to which to bite
Seeking with gothic glee
Nectar to sustain myself
That lays somewhere under corsetry

.....She saids.....

I want to be of an angelic nature 
A lady dressed in white
With ruby red lips
Speaking with purity
Lace and gems, and skin of ivory
Wanting someone dark to seduce me
A lady whom is naive
Wanting to be taken
By a man borne from shadows

....Ok baby
....Mmmmm
.....Maybe I could give this a go

I have always wanted to be
A knight armored and strong
clad in shinning steel
Cod piece attached willy-nilly
Out seeking great quests
And the rescuing of sweet damsels in distress
If you know what I mean
Flinging myself into the fray
To conquer and be victorious!

...I want to pretend...can we pretend?

Shall we make believe
That a king from a feuding kingdom
Has taken your angel hostage
This king has tied her to his bed
Took advantage of this lady in white
Her dress torn and in shreds
Welts and bruisers upon her skin
Her breast's exposed, naked flesh
Left in despair, wishing for a knight
A brave knight in shining armor
To rescue this lady in distress.....

...So im a vampie night 
.....And your an angelic damsel in distress
...And there is a terrible rival for your affections

..Yes honey i am....
...It's already happened babe
...I think the 'rival' skipped the affection bit

...Oh right

And i come in wresting my way 
Through foe feirce and ugly and really pissed off
And confront this terrible rival 
Whom has taken my angel thus
With a mighty clash of 'weapons' swung
We engage in mortal fury
Each impassioned to win
An angelic damsel in distess
Who watches on in dismay
To see whom will win her this day

Swinging and lunging
And clashing about
Each warrior cursing and shouting
Trying to deal deaths final blow

...Are you two done yet...

Each warrior looks at the other and grins...

Haha...saids I as I take the foe
Delivering a lucky deathly blow
And as the king falls to the ground
I step over his form to claim my prize this victorious dawn

I trip, getting caught up in my willy nilly cod peice 
And crash to the ground!

...Hun, get up and quit playing around
.....Come over here...
...And bring your fangs..
