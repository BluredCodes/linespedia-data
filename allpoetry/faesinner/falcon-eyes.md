title: "Falcon eyes"
writer: "FaeSinner"

I don't miss you now.Don't miss your stubborn, tender, quick witted and soulful mind.Not your tummy or chest or your falcon eyes.No longer do i crave to hear your laughterand see your beautiful smile.I no longer think of you and the way you step aroundyour barriers of shyness and shell,to flare up in brilliance;in anger, delight, spontaneityYou don't rock the socks off me any more.No thoughts of you buzz in my mind, after making love,seeing you asleep,dreaming of our life togetherforever...Your not hereand neither are weand missing u isn't what we areany more.Well, maybe i miss those falcon eyes...
