Here it sits perched upon this rock
High above the world
Imposing and esoteric
It dreams alien dreams
Far beyound for the like of us to understand
Last of it's kind
Majestic and terrible
Forbidden to human dreams
Metallic scales slide silkly
Over muscle and sinew
As from time to time it shifts it's graceful bulk
Like a cat stretching and loling about
Wings of leather soaked in linseed reach ever skyward
Translucent in morning light
Fragile things seemingly
Serpintine tail switches here and there
As if in search of times of a richer hue
When magic was the blood that pumped life through
The dream of dragontime
Eyes reflect many lights
Not all from sunrise splendour
Of times long past and futures death
They dream of journeys home
Long the neck and elegent the head 
Silvery mane covers the crown of needle like horns
Fearsome visage to some
Like that of exotic lion of the east
Loathsome to othres
Spider like fasination

Such a beast has no place here
It disturbs us to feel this fear
Kill the beast send it to hell
Send for the hero this beast to fell

Upon mighty war horse steaming
In morning air cold and crisp
Rides hero of mankind all 
Dazzling in polished steel and reignment bold
Mighty the lance held aloft
To vanquish foe this early day
He rides from town
Accompianied with cheers and garlands
Towards beast menacing and foul
Thundering charge and penant streaming
Deadly lance gleaming wickedly
No one can stop this dealy force
Good knight seeks to glory this day
Dragon bane of honour
Closer and closer he rides poised
Dragon breast marked he swings
His lance,nine hundred pounds of death
Polished armour sunlight flashes 
As impact seeks it's home
Dragon with delicate seeming grace
Moves with lightning speed
Around and up
To pounce upon 
Good knight and warhorse all
Like stone all come to life
The dragon hisses fury
With metallic squeel and mortal agony 
This poor knight has fallen this day 
The dragon turns about
To roar terrible at fleeing towns peolpe
And returns once more to meld with rock
To dream aloof and ignore such simple folk

What shall we do they cried
Against this beast that flys
Send for the sorcerous she has her ways
Perhaps this beast she will beguile this day

Divine of spirit that breathed within
And saddened by her crel intent
This lady of enchantments
Walked graceful and true
Towards dragon of fearful beauty
No cheers or garlands paved her way
People hid and prayed for victory
Mumuring songs of ancient lore
Passed from mother to daughter
From time forgotten
Air hummed with nature's magic 
As she gathered close to dragon
Reaching out to touch gently
She caressed silken hide
Dragon swung about to witness the threat
And felt the magic in the air
Knew that here was a kindred spirit
Of the old earth when it was young
Of dragon and sorcerous
Time did stand still
Held together in silent embarce
No one could guess what went one between them
With shuddering sigh
And blinking eyes
The sorcerous came awake
Gently kissed the dragon
And departed the feild this day
Tears of joy steamed down her face
As past people she walked
Never would i hurt such a one she said
And to the forrest she retuned

What is this!
The people hissed,
What are we to do
Lets get the bard out there,he'll talk it black and blue.

Matted hair and ragged of apperance 
The bard did gamble forward
To vanquish foe on this day
He carried harp and knowlege
The dragon seeing this leant forward intently
Another it seemed to wonder
And so the two did meet
Of equal mind and interest
The bard set down his harp and russled about
Laying a blanket on the ground
And a feast of apples
Pulling out a flask he drank deeply
Would you like an apple he said
To behomouth towering above
And tossed one up
For the dragon to close about
Now lets get down to it shall we
For we have along way to go
And so the bard did start to recite his lore
With grand geature and jumping about
He shouted and whispered and cried
For two days and four hours
This way he went
Dragon of dream and difference
Listened patiently thus
And when the bard finally finished
Exhausted beyound the pale
The dragon stretched and shuddered
Rose wings to span the heavens 
Launched it's self into the sky
Never had it heard such dribble
It was going to find a better place 
Where it could relax
Damn humans the way they scurry about
Not worth it's time 
To sit here and dream...

Look at that the people said
Of might and love, we have no winner
But when you send in a bardic poet
Then the dragon goes else where for his dinner
