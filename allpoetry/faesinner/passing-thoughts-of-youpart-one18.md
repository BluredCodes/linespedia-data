Sensualise this desire

Whence you came to me
Naked and kinked
Under cotton cloth
Ridges alluring
To one who knows
Public time
A fantasy
Our secret
My desire
Your pleasure

Laughing amongst friends
You look to me
Knowing in those eyes
Of times to come
Questioning
Had the games begun?
A nod from I was all you needed

With quiet words
You disengage
Come to me
To take this stage
Hips swaying sensual delight

Hands on knees
You bend down low
To whisper softly
In my ear
Skirt rising up
Black strap and buckle
Lace thigh high
Seen and wondered

Slowly you straighten
With me as I stand
Skirt falling slowly
Tantalising
You follow as I leave
Outside to falcilitate
These dreams 
Of sweet sensual kink

Hand curving around
Cupping flesh gently
Pulling you down
To sit beside me
Fingers reaching 
Finding....

Submisive
Agressive

Sexualise this desire...:)
