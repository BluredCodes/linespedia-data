Undress for me
Take it off slow as can be
Let this momentum rock
Towards a higher claim
Spare that cloth
Save that seam
Pull it of slowly
Let me feel what it seems
Tremble with exposure
As each piece is removed
Lay yourself out me
So I can see

What's under there

Lace spread thin to bare
Corsetry capturing
Thigh high enrapturing
High rides thong and string

Take it off slowly
As if my fingers dance
Take it slow
Maximise your mystery
Up the stakes

Pause

And reach down 
Remove long nylons 
Peel away second skin
Curving back
Taunt muscles
Set eyes alight with need
Body tingles as 
Delight plays divine
Knowing inside
I am yours
As you are mine......;)
