---
title: "To you my friend"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "to-you-my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hello my friend,
i have been waiting for these words, 
that you've written today.

I feel your lose
and i need for you to know
That i am here for you.

In silence
and in love,
unconditionally.

So i just wanted to say,
to you,
today.
Hello my friend.
