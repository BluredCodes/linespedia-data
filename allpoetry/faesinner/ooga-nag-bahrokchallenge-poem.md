Eyes flare gazing hard
Ooga nag bahrok
I am Ooga

Sighs to breathlessness
Ogbo barooo
And yet I am not

Waves arms higher
Onnah!
The complexities of life excite me! 

Fingers dancing across each other
Hibe hoi toh aglido i
Each quest for knowledge brings it's own wisdom 

Thumbs twirling together
Addaha
And it's price

Eyes close half way
Ohmi nah
I feel so many things

Head turns to the right slowly
Ahhhdo laymaru
I yearn for fulfilment of this soul

Head flicks back
Igg boogy oOga do whup
I have searched hard and long 

Chin coming up
Oonah boraki doh
Always seeking the lawful way

Jaw tightens
Oohah ecrrke
Living not the way of greater evil

Buttocks clench
Oks ouchu ummer
Yet I am a simple man

Pelvis juts forward
Oga Ooo Boo Oboo
With simple needs

Knees bend
Ooga ahhhdo ogbo dohi
And yet I wonder

Knees straighten
Ohnah iggbo boogy??
Am I more than myself??

Head turns to the left quickly
Bahooga bah!
Of course I am!
 
Eyes flare gazing hard
Ooga bo nag bahrok!
For am I not Ooga!

Dances around feverently
Nag Bahrok ogba bolay!
All wise and powerful shaman! 

Buttocks losen
Oks umber Ooo Oonah dohto
A simple man seeking to understand this wonderous world
