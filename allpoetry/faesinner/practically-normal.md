Ripshaw this rug from under me
My feet glued to it's lunacy
The world spins uncontrolably
As I am inserted head first into other dream

Old friends say hello
Walking perpendicular
Backwards waving slowly

I dream my weary self forward
Hand dragging me by collar and seat
This is where I am going
Somewhere not so far away
Where the east moon greets all in practiced surprise

Pass me a darkened mirror
And laugh that jolly laugh
We are here to have a sing~a~song
You can whistle if you like
Mesh you fingers together 
Join the holy light

Wake up it's showtime

No cybernectics please
