How I wish
I could Paint 
You naked in this sun
Trace filagrees and marbled swirls
Textures that curl loose and dreamlike
Take my paint and brush...

 sugar
   dreaming
      of 
        soft 
      white 
  sponge 
cakes 
  in 
    the 
      sun
     oh 
   chocolate
how
  I
   wish
   to 
 taste
thee

Big chocolate cheesy grins
Euphoric high
Coffee and electric words
Melts this brain 
Of late 
Of words
To many
To speak
To try
Emotions sit
Warm and curdling

waiting this yoghurt
of the morning
