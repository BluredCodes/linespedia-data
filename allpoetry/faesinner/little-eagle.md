---
title: "Little eagle"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "little-eagle"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hey little eagle.Fly with me.Reach up to the cloudsand catch the winds,that will set you free.Fly lil' eagle.Dance with me.Feel the rhythm of the worldand you'll know her joyfully.Dance lil' eagle.Sing with me.Tell me your wishesand what they will to be.Sing lil' eagle.Dream with me.Open your self upand let your spirit free.Dream lil' eagle.Play with me.Show me your heartand what we can be.Play lil' eagle.Come fly with me.Dance the winds.Sing your song.Dream the dream.Show us where we can belong.hey lil'eagle....
