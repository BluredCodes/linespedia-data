---
title: "touch you"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "touch-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

it causes me pain
that i cant touch you
not in the way i want u to hear
to you its a hindrance 
or a portent
leading to other impressions

i know it
the fact that u love me
it is the very one 
that keeps me alive
and i hear the words 
that you say with such familiarity

i love you to
but words alone aren't enough
need for you to hear
respond to my touch
don't reject it out of hand
refuse my tactile communication
and you deny my very soul

i grow speechless
and destitute of vision
crippled by my sense of love
here i am
perfect in my own identity
yet i can not express my true self
with the one i love so

i long for you to understand
that my touch is not meant for pain
but to convey joy and intimacy
it welcomes you to my soul
to reflect
my own self love
