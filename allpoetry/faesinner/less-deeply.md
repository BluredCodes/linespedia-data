Closed mind and closed mind and closed
Thinking slowly so not to take it away from the truth
Wanting to dance a soft and sihlouetted trance
Drinking pandora's subconscious melodies
Workshop this muscle till it understands
Hide in rainfall's bleeding patience
Reasoning flow of blood's passage
Winding down sky's spiraling  
Good night the dark
Good morn the light
Do I ride this breeze flickering in the moonlight
Or let go and fall
Less deeply?just thinking.....or am i...winks
