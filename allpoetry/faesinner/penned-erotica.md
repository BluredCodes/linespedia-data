Whispers amble across night's domain
As pen dipped in ink iridesent
Writes words of surrender
Upon flesh willing to release
From past's ambiguous melody
Cramped penmenship excites
Leaving trails of exotic delights
Tomorrow is just another day
When all can be had tonight
Upon this alter of 
Sacred feminity

Quill and parchment
Unrolled before
Players on the centre stage
Role play to begin
When sights meet sounds erotic
Dip before me
As I direct
Puppeteering
This punch and judy

Surreal turnings of this mind
Allow for opennings in time's shadows

Lay here for me
Naked and still
As flesh, muscle and bone settle
Creating the perfect medium
Mind ticking with remembered amure
I write with all the passion
Born within me
Of pillow talk yet to be.A slight diversion....;)
