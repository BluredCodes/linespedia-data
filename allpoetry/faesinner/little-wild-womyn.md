title: "Little Wild Womyn."

Dragon roars spitting flame
A truth unbearably hard
Clamps down
A spirit true no more
Covered in other's false claims of security
Shakes under another light
When quiet is the understanding
No more will I let
This anger cry alone
How can they take this soul  
And make it nothing
Surely there is another way
Doesn't love stand for something?
Community of spirit
Hands reaching from hearts hungry with love 
To help and give and show others the way 
No self gain 
Only for love they give
True with-in and with-out
Am I only an outsider?
No more part of the dance
Than I used to be
How do I take this gift
And gift it to you?
Then I am nothing but a stupid dream that feels so much
Yet cannot give true
Bound
Useless
Crazed with anger at those who take
My beloved's breath away
Ever heard of loving?
Ever thought beyound your own fear?
Wrap your arms around 
Instead of straight arming into madness
Do you not listen?
Not care enough for your own life 
That you take another and kill it with blindness?

Puff the magic dragon 
Roars into the night
Knows he cannot move
For he is chained
And his dreaming only takes him further 
Into himself

Under a blanket is a friend
Scared into a broken balance
That cannot be shaken
By those of us less than mortal
All is needed
Nothing is given
And life
As we know it
Lives on.A vent truely needed...
