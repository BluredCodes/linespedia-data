title: "Do I Wanna?."

Do you wannaDance for meDance and seeWhat we are to be?Do you wannaDo the tango Or the waltzMaybe disco's more your thing?Do you wanna Dress for meSo I can wear you outLeather, lace or P V C?Do you wanna fantasizeDream romance to come aliveOr perhaps it's the sting of the whipThat gets you high?Do you wanna Tie ropes to meSecure me hereTease and tempt me dear?Do you wanna Cuddle up to meWhisper sweet sorrowsEndlessly?Do you wannaScream at meShout your soulFor me to hear?Do you wannaListen to meCan i tell you trueThese feelings that i feel?Do you wanna be over meAs i am over meAs i feel right nowNah.. I don't wanna....Well i certainly sound cheery, don't i.....:P
