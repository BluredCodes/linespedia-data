Soft and gently goes this night
An alarming voice
Sends a message that clouds
Confusion sweeps startling
Patience and understanding
An abundance in me
For those who wrestle
With things that hide
Yet a fear of the unknown 
Sometimes can freeze
And I am frozen this
Hardened night
I will wait
Breathing mind
Some time futured
I will understand
And See this 
In a new
Light
