title: "Epitome paralysis."

Crimson,
this song
reveals 
the feelings of myself.

Fever, 
shaking as I lay expectant 
under the sacrificial knife;
mental glinting.

Words lay strips 
of white agony
as I type 
this death of me.

I shadow
my own execution.
Victim to prophecy;
propagander.

Foundation
swept aside
causing rifts
and uncertainty.

I no longer
know myself
yet I walk this edge
accepting.
