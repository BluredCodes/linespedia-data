we are separate, intelligent, feeling, livinghuman beingsco-existing in a world we have both manifestedour lives have drawn us togethereven as they will draw us apartwe both swim through the sublimanelseas of our feelingsrocked gently by our emotionsthe rip pulling us out to the greater depthspassionate in our search for for the esotericand wisdomwe sometimes glide through waters togetherguiding each other by the touchof our finger tipsunderstanding the signalsthrough our body's languageand the promptings of our intitionfeeling our way as we navigate our soulswe absorb each others energyas easily as a sponge does a pooling liquidit is pain that we sharepara-sympathectic in our love for anotherwe wear such with loveand endurancewe are the embodiment of lovewe are the moon and the tidesanswering to the call of one anotherwe meet to become onein our watery depthsit is the element that gives us our unityeven as we part we understandthe depths of our soulsand as we reside in these splendid hallsfears are cast asideand joy in our abilities is manifestleaping forth from our heartsfor we are the moon and the tides
