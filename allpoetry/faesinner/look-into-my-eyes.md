---
title: "Look Into My Eyes."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "look-into-my-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23469-Look-Into-My-Eyes.-by-FaeSinner"

come look into my eyes
look deep
look past all you have ever seen before
come now don't be scared
look deeper
deeper still
feel it yet?
ok may be a little
not deep enough then
come take my hand if you must
yes it's ok if you're scared
I am often that way
come we will plunge deeply
together
into my eyes 
to the very depth's of my soul

can you feel it
yes you can
I can see it in your eyes
it's reflected there
where
just there
yes in your heart
you can feel it
the passion I feel
the love that grows 
the need for justice and truth
laughter and tears
the saviour
and the sinner
the heart that knows all
and yes
the head that doesn't

yes that's it
yes we are here now
look around you
does it inspire
you feel it
you feel it stirring in your own soul
that is good
perhaps you will explore yourself some time
yes you can
that would be good

are you ready
it's time to leave now
no 
but next time we can visit your depths
and you can show me your inner truths
I would love to share that with you
yes I know 
but I will be with you
and know that you are loved 
by the soul 
of this 
Poet

you are ready
ok
just look into my eyes.....
