Wind swept above stormy sea
Cliff's towering immensity
Rocks ragged and imposing
Water roars coalescence journey
Crashing waves join in passionate unison
Ground vibrates deep and rich
Cloud's churn elemental fury
Lightning dance's ever skyward

Bitter cold chills
Wild wind whips
Misted spray drenches

I stand trembling in awe
Riding this high octane moment
Electric overload
Nature throws my energy around 
Like hail in a storm 
A scream builds 
Thunderous
Joyous

Now is the time
To follow through 
Destiny's 
Moment
My brother's 
Spirit
Ashes to the wind
A return to the beginning
Birthed by nature
And returned
To her.


Respect 
And love
Standing proud
Atop this wind swept peak

Voicing

   A 
  Long 
 Soulful
Heart felt
 Release

Peace my brotherA tribute to many...some....one.
