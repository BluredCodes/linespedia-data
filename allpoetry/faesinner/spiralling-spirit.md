---
title: "Spiralling Spirit."

Firery friend
woman warrior
weild's wildly
sword shimmering
magic mystical
wisdom wordly
love's longing
heart hopeful
soul selfless
wit wry
curious cynic
grecian grace
lucuios lips
flighty flirt
sensual sway
contures curvaceous
electric eyes
tender touch
fantasy fair
dreaming damsel
woman wild
spiraling spirit....;)
