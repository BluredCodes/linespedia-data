Hey what's this?I don't know..It looks funny...Let's break it open and see what's inside!get a stone or somethin'yeah a big one...yeah...smash it!and again!hey look!Its all gooey and soft inside..And it smells sweet and has beautiful music coming out of it!And flashes of light.....oh, like fire works!Wow....Ahhh.....Ohh.....oh.All of it has gone away...Where did it go...?It doesn't matter.It's broken anyway...Just a feelin'......pay no mind...:)
