I dream of times
When the closeness of you feels
Like a second skin

It is your love that embraces
That welcomes me to your world
Gives magic to my moments

I miss the deeps
Those places we visit when together
When you illuminate my essence

I am rocketed by wild kalidescopes
When the mind is evaporated 
By the physical

The hot wet slickness
Of your silen road
Lures me inward

Your eyes half lidded
Lips flushed
Hands stroking; clawing sheets

Breasts swaying
Nipples brushing against lips
And tongue eager for tasting

Rocking rythm for and against
Member sliding in and out
Clitorus aflame; needful 

Fingers rimming and probing
Hair spirited and living
Screams echoing of delight

Bodies dancing willingly
In this physical torture
Of electrified bliss

No other moment
Gives to the total emergence
Of two souls becoming one

In love
Divine and unique
