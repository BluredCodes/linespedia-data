title: "And So Are You."
writer: "FaeSinner"

The river flows
Never ending
Perfect
Beautiful
Calming
Majestic
we look on in wonder
At this symbol of life
How could we judge it other wise
It is what it is
Over aeons
It has grown
Gathered from each of mother earth's tears
At times a trickle
Sometimes a torrent that destroys
Coagulation
Nature's ongoing creation
And
Let just one person criticise
Millions will defend
This natural wonder of our earth
Which brings peace to some
A lively hood to others
Transportation
Inspiratition
Ecologies by the billions
It is what it is
Beautiful
And divine

And so are you...
