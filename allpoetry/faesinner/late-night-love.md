---
title: "Late night love...."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "late-night-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10038-Late-night-love....-by-FaeSinner"
---

I cannot help but write these words, 
the voice inside of me needs to be heard. 
For they rise up passionate and surreal, 
I need for you to discover how it is I really feel. 

The desire that comes deep from within, 
has a voice of its own and likes to sing. 
From the lyrics of the greatest love song ever written, 
I’m telling you, I think I am smitten. 

These feelings are so hard to hold down,
it is playful i feel when you are around.
Across the keyboard my fingers do fly,
guided by this, on line, euphoric high.

So generous of heart you are to me,
supportive enough to set us both free.
I long to fly with you like the angels above,
these feelings you envolk are akin to love.

So it is these words that i give to you,
expressing my feelings that are so true.
Perhaps one day you'll grant me my wish,
because for me,in side and out,you are such a dish....
