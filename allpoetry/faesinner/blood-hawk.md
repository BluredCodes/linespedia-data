---
title: "Blood Hawk"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "blood-hawk"

Inky black this darkness of mine
No escape for me this time
Spiralling deep
And darker i go
Where I'll stop 
I just don't know
What horrors await deep down inside
Something new here to be found
Only the same blackness
Always so much more than I can bear
Deeper and deeper
Where will it end
No more tomorrow
No love to send
Only lost
Crippled
Shadowed 
Forlorn
Unescapable
Loss of self
Death
Riding this 
Preconception of the masses
Comatose
Dull and still
adorned in rags 
I await this
Descent
Down in the bowls 
Of my self hatred

Poised before the abyss
Arms spread wide
Accepting

My truth bears
Me no I'll will
It but mirrors my thoughts
Tarnished
And forgotten

I own time no longer
It has me now
Taken by so many others 
Seeking comfort in thier own hearts
Leaving mine to die
Because of this sacrifice
This cycle kills me
Shadowed and foolish

Blood hawk

Begone

I accept this changein case you are wondering ...no it isn't...
