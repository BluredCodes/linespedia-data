Gentle firm intention
My eyes hold you entraped
As your's widen in surprise
My hands it seems have had other ideas
Than soft caress and exploration
With firm assurance I tie one hand to the bed head
You can escape if you wish
All u need do is pull away
Securing the second hand is easier
You know this game and play along 
We have played at this before, 
Both tried it's complexities
Of pleasure and teasing toture
The bed head is low as you face outwards
It's hard smooth surface
Cold against skin hot and expectant
Lower torso and body rest in firm bondage
Weight in balance to silk that binds
Upper body swaying gently
Next comes silk that blinds
To switch from light to dark
The change of senses brings an acceptance
Of games to begin in earnest

Cool touch comes from black expanse
Blended with warmth, hot and slick
Kneading, stroking, kissing, biting
Body reacts quicker than thought
Pulling away as you struggle to stay
Softly now, hands that smooth electrified flesh
Followed by sweet kisses that tingle and moisten
Hot and clammy skin
Tongue trails it's journey
Over mounded breast
Spiralling to hardened tip
Licking, flickering and teasing
Moans escape as breathing intensifies 
Sensation's afire 
Warmth flares deep within
Hands guide searching tongue
Ever downwards towards... 
You long to let go, to give in
All you want to do is lie down
Take this pleasure and relax into it's sensual bliss
Hands grip wooden frame
Slick with sweat
They slide
Halted by silk that binds
