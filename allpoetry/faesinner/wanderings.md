This sacred consuming
Bondage of the mind
Plays teasingly madly
With trendrils from past's
Unheaded thoughts
Actions

Fears
Generate 
Emotive synergy
That
Gather to hold back 
The echoing songs
Voiced so clearly 
In my mind
Fingers clench
Willing themselves
To play upon 
Those feelings

I move not

My soul

Triggered by sound
Taste 
Touch
And
Sight



Explosive plunging velocity
Purging soul
Captured by momentum
No returning to the
Cave like 
Sanctuary
Offers false
Security



Forever forward
No tomorrow
Definatley
No future

Just now

And what a wild expansive thing this is
Expanding
Contracting
Fearful
Fearless

Ahhhh

To be
Or to be
What to choose
These wanderings take me nowhere 
Yet I am everywhere
Too

And love learns 
Grows
Is.
