---
title: "Warrior's  Song."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "warriors-song"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26743-Warriors--Song.-by-FaeSinner"
---

Come Sun brother ride the winds 
And motivate this spirit within 

Come Moon sister seek the shadows 
So that we can pass from this world to the next

Come Wolf father pass your knowledge
So that we can learn of ancient lessons

Come Earth mother talk to us your will
So that we can revive our spiritual direction

Heya hiya ho ho
Hey hiya heya heya ho ho

Heal me Sun bother with your breath
Cleanse me of the past that gives no rest

Heal me Moon sister with your silent light
Release the shadows that seek to bind

Heal me Wolf father with your wisdom
So that I may walk fearless with you

Heal me Earth mother with your soul
Align with me so that I am true

Heya hiya ho ho
Heya hiya heya heya ho ho

Come and heal Sun brother I call to you
Come and heal Moon sister I call to you
Come and heal Wolf father I call to you
Come Earth mother I seek to heal

What damage has been done

With my lifes blood I give 
With my soul I offer

With my mind I sooth
With my hands I caress

With my wisdom I guide
With my love I share

Heya hiya ho ho
Heya hiya heya heya ho ho

Heya hiya ho ho
Heya hiya heya heya ho ho ho...
