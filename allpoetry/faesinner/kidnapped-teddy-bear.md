Teddy Bear, teddy Bear
Where for out you, Teddy Bear?
I've looked here and looked over there
I have looked everywhere!
Oh deary me,how can this be
For I can not see 
My teddy bear 

Have you seen my teddy bear?
He's this big and has curly hair
Doleful eyes that seem to stare
So deeply in your heart that's where

He smells like sweat
And has all his stuffing falling out
And if I dont find him soon
I'll scream and shout!

Oh teddy bear
Teddy bear
Come home now, teddy bear!

What's this I see
In my letter box
It's a ransom note
From goldilocks
She says
I can have him back
When I can find for her a shack
With three brown bears and porridge too
Oh dear what am i to do

I look and look and search about
Seeking this mysterious shack of hers
And before i know it she frolicks right up
And says she has found her bearsome sup
In dismay I say to her
Can I have my teddy bear? 
Well she says boo hoo
A strange highlander took him to
Uummmm
Somewhere

But here is a note saying.....

"Of Your teddy bear is being held at an undisclosed location, pending payment of ransom."

Oh bugger....
What am i gonna do
Oh teddy bear
It sounds like your through...LOL...:P

I wonder who...hmmmmm:P:P:P;)
