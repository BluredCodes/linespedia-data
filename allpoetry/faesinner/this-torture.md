Silently the tears gather
Not for the fisrt time
Nor for the last
Yet I feel the same way every time

Am I going mad
Am I right
Will I feel safe
Feel loved 
And respected again?

Who can I trust
When I can't even trust myself

I ride this silent torment alone
Wondering what path to take next
Not fearful of out comes
Just terrified of presents

Will this anger consume me
Or will I fall quivering
Full of shame
When do I get to be 
The spiritual warrior
The code I live by
No end in sight
For I am weak 
And have been for an eon

Where's an Angel when you need one?...*
