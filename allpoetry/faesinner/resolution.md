Sometimes it stumps me what people say and do.
The problem is that I do it to.
All this crap that's spun around.
It all makes for an ugly sound.

Can you not hear that you say.
Over and over every single day.
Very rarely a thought of love, joy or peace.
One would think your heart's on the freeze.

Go stick your negativity up your arse.
Don't you know its all just a farce.
How silly u look with your face all screwed in.
Don't you kown there's a reason it's a sin.

Try and look at the other's point of veiw.
Wear their shoe's and see where it takes you.
Talk about it and work it all out.
This is not the time to scream and shout.

Let your ego go out and play.
It's time for love to save the day.
Trust in peace, resolution and law.
God knows,
that's what they were invented for.
