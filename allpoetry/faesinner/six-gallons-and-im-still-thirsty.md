Imagination caught 
In reflection's insight
This game plays cyclic 
Spining raw against nerves 
Already tampered with
Short this shot of electricity
A burst to clarify
This maddening moment
Shakes it's head in wounded surprise
As out rolls the marching band
Tossing lunch rolls and suger cubes


The game must go on
Teeth cleanched and hands tiptoing
Dragon snoozing it's hundred year old habit
Daisies dancing
And children rejoicing
As the summer kingdom 
Reveals it's self

Back home everyone sings
Of hope and yesterdays breakfast

One can only hope
Some one sticks a spoke 
So we can all go homehehe....
