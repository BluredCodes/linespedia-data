spiralling............................my way to you

spiralling.........................my way to you

.........this dream inserts it's self into my mind

.........clasps to my sanity

spiralling...............................my way to you

.........and embraces every thought

.........deeper than mystery

.....dances with subline grace

.............it captures

.....................................every thought

spiralling..............................................my way to you

...................of you

spiralling......................................................my way to yo

..........dream scapes tumble to distant drums

......................lights flash incandescent

..............blinking I reach out

spiralling.............................................................my wa

....................and you are here

................dancing in my mind

...................laughing I cry tears that float through time

............................................to think I almost lost you once
