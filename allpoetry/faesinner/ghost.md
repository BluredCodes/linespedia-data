I am just a ghost in this machine
with words that fade as I wander.
my constant attention assures yours,
so it seems.

What does it take for you to see
that i am just a screen,
black,
reflective,
absorbant.

White illumination
highlighted thus
allowing journeys of communication
to be made simply.

Stunning
and
instant
this loving
carress of minds.

Turbulent crystalisation 
of needs and expectations
commit this electron
atomic.

Yet I fade 
as the screen goes blank
another pixel 
in another dimension.

It's cold here.
