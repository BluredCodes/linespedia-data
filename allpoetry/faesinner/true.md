title: "True."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "true"

You are just words that flicker across a page.
Love and emotion made real by the thoughts you express.
I can not touch.
Embrace.
Watch your reactions,
Or dance with you under the stars. 

And yet,

I love you true......
