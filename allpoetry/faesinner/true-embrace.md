Watching, waiting, wondering, wanting.
Needing to touch you,
to come up from behind,
to surprise you with my presence.
Hands reaching;
fingers streaming through silken strands.
Breath white hot and urgent.
Neck tingling;
Tougne dancing across naked shoulders.
Nostrils breathing in suculent aromas....

Mind urging.
Body urgent. 
Want meets desire
and as sparks fly
I trace my finger tips
up from under,
exploring each and every private curve
to gently cup and embrace your......
Fingers dancing, twirling, teasing.
Breath hotter, faster, harder,  
moaning, crying, laughing, louder....

Feeling  your need as minds meld... 
Within this moment, 
body's shout their songs.
No control left 
but to fly into each other
and release all sense of reality...

There is nothing that exists
other than
this 
passionate yearning for 
true embrace.......
