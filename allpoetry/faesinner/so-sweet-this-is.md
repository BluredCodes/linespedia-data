---
title: "So Sweet This Is."
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "so-sweet-this-is"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41410-So-Sweet-This-Is.-by-FaeSinner"

Such sweet sorrow
Can not even begin to express
The words of failed appointment
This day lamented

Those eyes of dark
That freeze this moment
Words that say
Deeply that you do

These feelings locked 
From to much time in an alien world
Struggle to reach
The one I love

Those words that caress my soul
Like no other in lightened attention
That craddles my fleeting thoughts
In a loves gentle embrace

Wild these moments between
Each thoughtless action
That pushes you to ask why
Where you should not have to

Reach out to you
With sunlit spun golden
To alight that dark fire
That I love so

Brush through hair
My fingers searching deep
For the stress you feel
Taking this time to enrapture

Breath in the scent of you
Seventh sense alerting imagery
Gulping down this taste
Such sweet madness

Pulling across this timeless millennium
Those memories like rain
Placing hands upon heart
Fingers trace illumination

Such sweet sorrow knows not
These paths I walk to you
Tears streaming relentless
Joy caressing sadness.
