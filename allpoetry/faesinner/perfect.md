She she stared at me
Sway hipped and deliscous
The fabric of her dress 
Clinging and see through
Red lucious lips 
Pouting for all the world to see
Eyes the deepest blue
Mascara thick and inviting
Blonde hair wavey 
That seemed to move with the warm wind 
That embraced me
As our world became smaller and smaller
The intimacy had started six meters away
When I first saw her
And her I
Now it had intensified beyound beleif
Almost to breaking
Point
I know you
her eyes told me
And I knew her
As I had seen her every day for 
Two years
The desire building
Day by day
Week by week
I knew one day I had to have her
If only there was just one little flaw
This perfect woman
Every man's fantasy
Every woman's nemisis
Was almost to perfect
As if some how she embodied all that was asectic
In our society
All that is unattainable
Yet mezmerising for that very fact
And desirable beyound compare
She had it
She knew it
I wanted it

Two am in the morning
No one around
Brick thrown 
Through three mill
Deafning the sound of shattering glass
Reaching in I grapped her
With no time to think
I carried her under arm  across town
Away from the scene of the crime
To a safer place
To a world I knew she would understand

My artist's studio
For I knew
All that she needed 
Was a pair of wings

Perfect.As a result of working ten hours a day and in sales at xmas
Anything might come out.......
