Naked Head Goddess
I sat with you 
For but a moment in time
As always it was timeless
And means so much to me

Your love ever present
Holds me gently
Loving true

You are my guide spirit sister
Dancer of mystery's memories
Walker of sacred paths

Deeper, you travel than any other I know
You give your life to all
Stretching forth with child like joy
The unknown your faith

Your emotions heal
Your words bless and soothe
Your insight, forever my friend
Your truth, an inspiration

Always I look to you
In friendship and love
Thank you Naked Head Goddess
