---
title: "i give u magic"
writer: "FaeSinner"
slug: "i-give-u-magic"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Star Kissed i am by you
by divinity i know not.
No one being will own.
My magic,
i walk
and dreams guide.
Shadows beckon to what can be.
Twilight i roam.
Moving with ancient grace.
Rhymes play upon the fingers of my mind
yet they remain broken for others to make amends.
Darkness reaches, 
whilst pain slaps me awake
to start another journey that i call life.
Star kissed upon my brow,
the memory of which recedes into the cosmos
and returns with riddles given to the unknowing.

Walker play your pipe,
sing your tunes,
and make believe that you're  real as i am.
Lead the dance,
twirling and prancing
and they will come.
Giddy along the heaven's path
like 'will o the wips'
and intangable moments of imagination.

Rise up with me 
broken god
and stride from here,
leading the blind; 
with their noses snifting the air 
for madness.

Come with me.
Dream of times to come
and manifest your wishes.
Listen to the intuitive of your mind.

Thunder ripples emotive and
expectant.
Daylight flashes
and reality bends
to the dances 
that perform in unison with my soul.

Call out to the wild hunt 
to rid me of this
madness?
I think not.

I ride.
Tears streaming.
Eyes shouting.
Tongue tasting.
Skin hopful and electrified.
Soul surging,
propelling me forward.

I accept
and star kissed i give to you,
All of my magic moments
and i dream with you
the longest wish.
What has been
is near to what shall be.
Divinity i wear.
