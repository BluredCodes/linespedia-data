I dislike this word

With an intensity 

Of past experience

Years upon years of rejection

Of not having someone there

Seeing mass marketing projection

Taking everyone for a ride

Cute darling cuddles 

All for a price

Red the color of the day

I hate this colour that tells me 

I love you during this time of year

And it's so special to be one of the lucky ones

I have felt the flames burn

And the deep hurt of being alone

Without the social acceptance

Of my aloness

~*.................*~

Smiling so much it hurts, 
I think now of this word and laugh
I've had a few life experiences that have taught me
That my love is not just the colour of passion 
It is all the colours I can I can imagine and then some
That I am defined by my choice of response
That a rose is a representation of love this day
As with any other

And so

To myself

To my dear friends

To those whom have gifted me 
With their unique expressions
Their own joyous realities 
That I share with love

I must say if it was not for valentines day

Would I not say 
Deeply 
Truely
Intensly
That....?

Of course i would...

Hem!

I love you......:)
