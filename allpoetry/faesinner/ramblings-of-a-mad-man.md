Do u seek to know me
I who am shaman 
And have walked 
The paths of twilight

Have navigated my soul  
Revealed the darkness there 
And danced with the Shining Ones
I have spoken with the old Gods
And made love to the Goddess...

I am the Pheonix

Whom pain of death swallows fear 
Like sweet nector of fateful acidic brew
To come again reborn 
And ready to dance the mystery

I fly as the Eagle

Soaring majestic bird of prey
That no one can 
Touch save it's 
Quarry

I hide like the Lizard

Frightened soul whom hides fearful 
And wanting
Wishing for strength and love and understanding
And for the night to set it's heart free

My soul wears the mantle of the Dragon

Warrior of the spirit 
I am song caster
Spear weilder
And slayer 
Protector & allay
Purple of soul
Imagination's friend
Companion
Always

I have lived in gentle love
And survived the anger of my heart
I am friend to those who know their fear
And enemy to those who slay my love's heart
I am dancer...liquid flame soaring flight of the mind
I am the soul who spuns thee 
And embraces thy faceless soul

Walker of this path
Singer of destinys choice
Do you want to dance the wid hunt with me?
Gather Hermes and the Huntress
And all the hounds
And blow the horn to annouce the coimng of the 
Hunt.....?

Do you dare to take in my gentle dark heart
Healer and lover and mad man
And whilst I smile I think that perhaps
I am he who rambles...In the grand tradition of 'to rant or not to rant'
