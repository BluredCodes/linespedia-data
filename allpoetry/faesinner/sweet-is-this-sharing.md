title: "Sweet is this Sharing."
writer: "FaeSinner"

Here I sit upon
earth so lovely as
mounded hill amongst 
the veld undulating.
Wistful and dreamy
grass swaying gently
soft sounds of whisper
as the wind plays with
it's euphonious nature.
Butterfly's tunble,
dragonfly's dart,
wild flower's float
luminous and odoriferous.
Admist such slendour
a simple release,
an inner truth of understanding
and acceptence if you will, overwhelmes
and arrests my biddable contemplation.
There nestled in the midst of
flora to numerous to name,
an old friend beckons;
one that I have known of late.
The presence of this 
sincere and gentle flower
pauses momentum of
illumination and I sit to 
share a while; in dreams of today.
Of breath slow and measured,
of time forgotten and past's memory released;
I commune in silence of love and loving.
And as the light gathers brightly, the sun its apex;
dazzlement flows coursing.
Through mind and soul,
cleansing;
healing, loving and sweetly,
you whom communicates so blissful
so that I to can dream and play
softly and return to haven of self.
Such is thy way;blessed and of awe.

I long to take; 
pluck this flower,
daisy of white light 
and keep forever it's sense of self,
dreaming and sharing.
Yet to take such and derive others of 
such sweet enchantment,
would be an affront
that I am unable of committing.
I know with deep and gentle light
that this dream is for others
yet I smeil perhaps in dulcet longing,
reminicent of past's loss
and futures to be found
once more amongst this
veld undulating....:)
