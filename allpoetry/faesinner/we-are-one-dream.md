we are one dream
one reality
one voice 
we are forever
eternally together
one equals two
two equals one loving soul

together in a world that lacks forever
so it is these things that i give to you

i give to you of my soul
through first love
where i will dream
with you
on the wings of lust

i give to you of my soul
through awakening
where roses are dense
and dreams have landed
I'll be with you

i give to you of my soul
when all is lost
and you think i've gone
though tears race through the farrows
i'll be there for you

for i am me
i am dreamy
and idealistic
spiritual and protective
supportive and proactive
forgiving and loving
and male

i am open and closed
serious and free
together and apart
all things that are beautiful
and not
i am mute
and compassionately poetic
i am true and fasle
to be belived in or not
and i am male

i am hot and steamy
creamy on the inside
sexual and sensual
intamicy is my guide
i love with my hands
and my voice
i fuck with my soul
these are my choices
and i am male

i will be your bastard
your anoying child
the one that dams you
and drives you wild
the hands that love you
your eyeless sight
your friend
and lover
that will cherish you whole

i will be every thing i can be
for with you i am whole
