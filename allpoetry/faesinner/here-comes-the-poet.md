Out of swirling mists of time
Comes he whom has lived magic wonders
And returned to our sleepy village
To tell tales of dreams and enchantments
Of billowing cloak and bold stride
He carries with him a volume 
Strapped cross ways his back least it encumbers
Walking staff that seems to glow
It's warm nimbus lights his way
Of tome one may see
Vellum pages leather bound 
In ancient bindings of elven song and dwarven metal
Feathers of the exotic 
Adorn hair blown gently 
By this morn's whispering wind
Pendants and crystal links 
Sway with each and every step

He comes from a time
Where time weaves a mystic web
And dreams are there for the living

Fairies dance and frolic and love
Dryads bespeak the trees
Nymphs inspire and beguile 
Dragons soar the highest places
Knights quest and feast
Elves sing nature's songs
Dwarves craft earth's blood
Wizard's learn of magic's price
And the witch heal with nature's blessing

He comes this day 
To regale his travels
Of wonders seen and lived
The Bard of mystic ways
Poet of this modern day
I hearald his coming
To all who hear
And offer with great cheer
