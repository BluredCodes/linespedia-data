Gold and lapis lazuli
Embraces your neck and chest
As it does your arms and ankles
Candle light plays it's part
Flickering it's dance here and there
Creating high lights 
And warmth to the mystery
Delicate chains of gold drape across breats
Naked and slightly perspiring
Nipples rouged
And hard
Eyes enboldened 
Framed with thick dark lines
Highlighted with blue 
Lips moist and full
Deep red with arousal
White silk flows over your reclinning form
Barely covering your lower abdomen
Showing muscles tense with anticipation
Dark hair almost black 
Sits lightly upon shoulders
Black panthers play in the back ground
As hawks watch on with distain
Music floats in the air
And exotic scents fill you with desire
To be all that your are

You hold your part well
And I come to you
Dressed as my part
Daggy paint splotched green t-shirt and jeans
Ready to paint you

To make you 
Forever.
