title: "Have You Lost Your Wings."

Hey there little one
are you ok 
you seem to be lost
oh you say you have lost your wings
what do they look like 
maybe I have seen them
oh you lost them a little while ago
thats ok well will find you new ones
don't cry
it's ok
we sometimes out grow these things
look here
I have a spare pair
you see I make them
A hobby if you will
yes have a look
see here they are
all spun gold of the most delicate hues
coated with  rare and exoctic jewel dust
and finally they are fired in the sweetest, most passionate
and divine of all emotions
just to give them a little spice
see how they glow
yes you are right
Forged in love
do you want to try them on 
you do
ok
but wait a minute
i have another pair
a very rare find indeed

Magical

yes they are
I'll tell you their secret
what makes them different to all others
and I'll show them to you
well they are all that I have said
spun gold, exoctic jewels and tempered in love
but these are special
because they radiate their own truth
they have grown
and have been forged in the Furnace of Life
imagine how they must quiver
with joy and with saddness
yes and with tears also
from the very depths
where all emotions are born
they don't glow
these wings shimmer
with a life of their own!!

you want to try them on 
I don't know if I should
are you ready 
ok
I'll stop teasing
here they are
just 
around 
here
behind you
see 
that's right
you had them the whole time
yes u did
no 
you aren't silly
you forgot to look
you just didn't realise
that you grow new wings from the old 
all the time

yes 
you can fly 
when ever
where ever
you want
after all 

these wings are yours.....This one's for you sweet solitary.....Don't forget to fly....
Love to you Karen...
Gwialchmai.
