Moon sighs with time 
On the wind as a sundial
Gathering on heavens clime
Phosphorant little gypsies
Baking in the sun as lovelies

Pink little faries glow
A hop dancing they go
Concrete singed tooties
Voices melodious hooting
Star gathering kisses

A choir of roses crimson
Sing upon loves altar
Fairies a glow with stardust
Feeling Jupiter shine
Beneath an amber friend

Goddess of this night
Awakens the God of this day
Midnight toys with sunlight
Showing that sleepless one the way
Slowly smiles creep soundless

Twirling in this whirlwind of lacquer
Moons filled with lustrous hues
Drink kisses swimming in messages
Bells chime deciphering the call
Of whispers beyond compare

Sleep takes this moment 
But memory carries on
As gentle fingers soften 
With sleep's golden song
Tides rush foward embracing

Stars 

Shine
