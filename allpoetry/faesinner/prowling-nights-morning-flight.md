A collaberation based loosely on a film we both like
By Jenneddin and myself....

no points if you guess the film...:) 

In dark twilight before dawns awakening
There's told a tale so sad and haunting
Of two hearts forever separated
By love's lost cruel misgiving

Within the moons glow lies half a heart
For his lover walks in amber under the sun
These two forever lost within a cycle
His heart stops for hers to beat again

Prowling night's lonely shadow
The wolf walks hiding heart's sorrow
Vague this longing of true love
Seeks the one whom couples his heart

This bird in flight sings no songs of morning
Her heart she left with the beast in the night
The love of her life that walks within the shadows
His whispers upon the air is all she may breathe

As moons light releases it's domain to sun's cleansing rays
Forever the other is doomed to watch the change
As one soul to another transforms
They mournfully witness each other's haunting gaze

The contact ends and in silence they cry
Her heart left to wither, until next dawn
As with many a legend and lore it was prophesied 
That the day would come
When two hearts of lonely life 
Would beat as oneThanks jennifer this was fun....:)
