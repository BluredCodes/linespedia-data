you wanna dance
then dance with me
all boogey and bright lights
funky styles
and 
groovey nights....

You wanna dance 
Then read on
Nows the time when
We are all ready for a song
Tell us your truth
So we can get along
Relate to me
Your special story
I'll return to you 
All this glory

Balance shows
What you give 
Is what you get
Invest in friendship
And your true soul will shine
Don't forget
That it's
Your time
You give
Dance with me

And you'll live.hmmmm....underneath this soft exterior beats the heart of a political and spiritual activist
