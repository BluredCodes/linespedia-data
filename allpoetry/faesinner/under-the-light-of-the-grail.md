title: "Under the light of the Grail."

Behold he said as he brandished the chalice for all to see
It is the Grail for which we have searched these centries past
We have grown enough within ourselves 
To finally find that which has eluded us
Let us rejoice brethren for this day will go down in history
As the day we came together to celebrate this victory

Each of you hold out your glassess and take from this cup
This nector that is ever lasting
For it represents all that we have learned
Drink and be in awe
For we can finally stand amongst ourselves
And be equal of heart, mind and soul

And so they drank
Laughing and dancing
All people joined together 
In celebration of life and love
No one person was exempted
All were respected
Age race sex religion politics
All were equal
Under the light of the Grail
Life it seemd had reached a turning point

......As I Look into your eyes
......I know this can be true
......For as I watch
......In the moments
......That pass between thoughts
......I can feel this passion in you also
......This inner reach of your soul
......That waits for release
......It is but a breath away

......As I look into your eyes
......I know this can be true.
