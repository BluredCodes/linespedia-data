title: "Quiet is this time."
writer: "FaeSinner"

As the moon sinks down 
Starry night's velvet curtain 
There is a stillness
That comes before
From waiting expectant
For what the light will bing.
In this place you'll find 
This special thing
The quiet time of the soul. 

Where nightmares have no place 

Love dances slowly

Innocence lost needs not be found

Passion's fire breathes languid

Sleep gives up it's final dream

As I sing softly to myself
Birds join in their communal greeting
Letting go of dreams fingers
To play the dawn of forward time

The wolf greets the hawk
As the owl settles for snowy sleep
The fowl wanders hopeful
And snakes wait for warmth
All manner of life returns
As the earth spins slowly again

Strange sounds call startling
To wake you to your self
Vision not the end
But this journey
You walk this morn
For your life has it's moments
From dusk's sleeping
To dusty dawn.
