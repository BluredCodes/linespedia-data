Do u seek to know me?

I am shaman 
And have walked the paths of twilight

I have navigated my soul  
Revealed the darkness there 
And danced with the shining ones
I have spoken with the old gods
And made love to the goddess...

I am scorpio

You think it mere science
Scoff at it not
Laugh at you whom faces no more truth 
Than your own back yard devil
Whose responsibility 
Must be the burden 
Of innocents
Asking why

I am the pheonix

Whom pain of death swallows fear 
Like sweet nector of fateful acidic brew
To come again reborn 
And ready to dance the mystery

I fly as the eagle

Soaring majestic bird of prey
That no one can touch 
Save it's quarry

I hide like the lizard

Frightened soul whom hides fearful 
Wishing for strength and love and understanding
And for the night to set it's heart free

My soul wears the mantle of the dragon

Warrior of the spirit 

I am song caster
Spear dancer
And slayer of the slayer
Protector & allay
Purple of soul
Imagination's friend
Companion always

I have lived in gentle love
And survived the anger of my heart
I am friend to those who know their fear
And enemy to those who slay my love's heart
I am the soul who spuns thee 
And embraces thy faceless soul

I am dancer...liquid flame soaring flight of the mind

Do you want to dance the wild hunt with me?
Gather hermes, the huntress and all the hounds
Blow the horn to annouce the coming of the hunt.....?
Sing passionate, the songs of destiny's choice...?
Ride wild, the night's majestry
As our senses awaken
To be all that we are 
And forever more so

Come, taste of this sweet chaotic joy
Trust the flame that engulfs all that enslaves
Burning away all that is false
Fueling the one thing that gives joy and light 
And the will to be fully alive

Awaken 

Your 

Awareness

To the ~*magnificence*~ of your soul.well....mmmmmm....:)
