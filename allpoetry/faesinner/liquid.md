I want to lay my hand on your chest
 To feel the energy that i know is there
 Bend my head down close to you neck
 Breathe hotly against your skin
 Extended my tongue so it's tip sends electic white light coursing...
 Follow through to bite
 And leave behind a little red welt
 That saids to any who enquires

 That you are 
 Mine

 I want to trail down you
 whilst my hands hold and squeeze
 With all of my lovers tools
 Engulf you with my fire
 Turn you liquid
 Trembling
 And dancing in delight
 Pull you into my  
 Energy
 My being
 Transform you
 As I am transformed by you

 I want to be this man
 That you love with your soul
 That affects you this way
 For you have the same affect
 On me
