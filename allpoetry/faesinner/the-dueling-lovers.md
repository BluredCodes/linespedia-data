One day it was heard
From the chirpings of a bird
There was a new lover in town
The very best it was renowned

Not liking what I was hearing
I set out for the clearing
To where all the women did flock
For is was said he had a well endowed body.

Edwardo!!

Upon my arrival I did see
The most alarming scene
Women were swooning everywhere
Was it just because of his passionate stare?

What other wonders did he have up his sleeve
I pondered this as I grieved
I have no chance against this man
He has the regard of all the land

In to his cabin they did passage
Out they came all bewitched and ravaged
Stumbling about in apparent bliss
Unable to stand that charm of his?

Husbands and boyfriends fled in shame
Whom else could they have blamed
This man had stolen their sweet hearts!
Whom no longer could tolerate their mundane bedroom arts!

Suddenly I knew what I had to do
To beat this stud was the only way through
Boldly I challenged this randy man!
I am the champion of this land!

Pedro!!

Now hold on he said deeply
I am edwardo and u will lose meekly
No woman here will have you
Now what are you going to do?

Wait on a minute it was heard
Twas coming from that chirping bird
It transformed into a woman both golden and fair
I will judge this challenge here

The mistress of the dawn!!

Edwardo's eyes were afire
He knew his passions would not tire
So without a second's waste
He leapt into her succulent embrace

What happened next I'm loathe to say
Edwardo manfully set out to win the day
Thrusting and grasping and rolling about
The mistress of the dawn did scream and shout

There was heaving and sighing and sweating
The towns people set about betting
Ten to one to edwardo they gave
His prowless is all the rave

With a bood curdling scream
They both came wildly as if in a team
Exhausted and barely able to move a limb
Edwardo shouted I have bested him!!

Smiling the mistress did say
I am the jugde of this day!!
You are indeed skillful almost beyond compare
But we'll see what pedro has to offer to be fair

Magically she spun about
All fresh  and ready for the next bout
Come on pedro show us
Give me a taste of your lust!

With my step sure and true 
I knew what I would do
Bending closly to her ear
I whispered sweet everythings for her to hear

Gently at first I started to kiss
Granting her every wish
Licking, nibbling and biting
Harder and harder her lust I was fighting

Lower and lower I  weaved my way
When reaching my goal I did stay
Body responding in bliss
I gave to her this womanly wish

Like a harp I did with her play
Strumming and carressing all of the way
Moaning and groaning her hands on my head
Pressure building an orgasm to be fed!

Body pulsating with intensity
She came with passionate glee
Trembling and sighing she slowly calmed down
Breathing softly she mumuered a sound

All around every one gathered close to hear
Let me tell you what I hold dear
Although I love a good fuck now and again she did say
Those who makes love will always win the day

Edwardo looked on dubiously
The women looked to their men happily
The menfolk shouted joyfully
I looked at the mistress tenderly
She returned the look
And then commenced 
To fuck my brains out
 
Furiously......
