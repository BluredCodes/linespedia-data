title: "Jaded With a Tear"

Jaded by lies I watch our love die.I can do nothing for you do not trust me,and I the same of you, love long over due.Mistakes have been made and prices now paid,yet all is nearing the end as neither will bend.Refusal to believe, of how we deceived,each other before, presents hearts without lore.Trust is so vital, and must lack deception in sidle,faith too is a must to those consisting of lust.A plague has become of what we have done,for we are sickened by dishonesty quickened.What can be done to repair this mortal pain?Nothing is the answer, as in my heart it will forever rain.We both have lost all of what once held so near,and my only reaction is a solitary tear.So maybe to me you were never that dear,as lies full of pain are Death's final leer. Jealousy is death of a lover's heart,and thus rips immortal souls apart.The only way to have a successful relationship is to trust the one you love. Without trust, arises jealousy and that is the worst feeling in the world. Trust always, but stay alert.
