title: "This One's For You Jen"
writer: "bdgrey"

Your presence here is vital to all those you have touched.
You love without asking and give without taking,
even though your whole life has been tough and mind breaking.
Never give up for we need your every word,
and you enlighten our minds with your poetic prowess.

The Lord up above will watch down on your soul,
as you undertake a journey into the unkown.
If for no other reason why you should remain on this earth,
just remember love that you have seen here tonight.
Remember your children for you are their mother,
and embrace their hugs with quiet devotion.

So when you go in and put your life at risk,
always know that you are loved and needed,
and you are supported in times like this.
Don't ever give up,
and I promise the world.

Just look at everyone who has written tonight,
for you are dear to everyone in our hearts, souls and minds.
We love you Jen for who you are,
and your heart is strong,
carried on by a divine, caring force.I know everyone's been writing dedications tonight for Jen, but I just had to put in a few good words as well.
