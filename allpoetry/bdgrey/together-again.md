---
title: "Together Again"
writer: "bdgrey"
slug: "together-again"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Please don't cry,
and do not fear.
Feel my love,
for I am near.
Though I lay lifeless,
you must live on.
It will be hard,
now that I have gone.
You are so strong,
in will and in strength.
Please live life,
to its longest length.
I have done much,
this may be true.
Yet what means most,
is my love for you.
Soon we will be
together again.
In that direct moment,
our new life begins.
Now I can watch you,
from so very high.
Just know one thing,
I never said bye.
Together again,
we are destined to be.
Eternity waits,
for us to see.
