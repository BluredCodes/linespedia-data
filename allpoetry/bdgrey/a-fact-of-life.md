Life moves along in a pace all too swift,
and many miss their golden opportunity.
Fear for some, consumes their mind completely,
and never will they be heard from again.
Others just wait expecting great things to come,
and when they awake it is all at an end.

Dreams are not seen or wanted without reason,
because they may be materialized and set to reality.
You are given a talent whatever it may be,
to live life in limbo, between substance and reverie.


So before I succumb to the winter of my life,
I must first experience Summer as I leave Spring behind.
For the greatest tragedy aside from love failing,
is failure to try and end without completion.
