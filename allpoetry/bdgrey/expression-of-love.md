My love for you is like a river....No, no that won't do. How 'bout this.If the stars above were in your eyes....No, not that either. Ah here it is.If ever there were a time,when all the world stood still... Damn! Not that either. What's going on?Oooh, I got it!I am so humble in your presence,that I am at your service.Whenever you're near,the world around becomes brighter.Well that one sucked. How can I do this?How do you describe love? Is it possible?You remind of a nice a hot bath....Now that was just stupid. What can be said about love?....    Ah, here it is.The only way to show my love,cannot be through such verse.The only way to know the truth,is when there's lack of voice.Love cannot be expressed,through futile written word.The only way to show true love,is when your heart's are near.It's a feeling beyond all reasoning,and captures from all sides.The way to expression is by way of non-utterance.Sometimes the best description,is written on the face.Look in my eyes or feel my touch,and then you'll our fate.Eternity.
