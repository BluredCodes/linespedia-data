To those who wish to leave this place,
reconsider, and restrain from haste.
You all have become dear friends,
and to lose you now would dawn the end.
Remain to write and together muse,
and move beyond capricious views.
Though apart by geological state,
you all are near through twist of fate.
You are my family, extended I know,
but together in the same boat we've begun to row.
Don't leave us now this I plea,
for you're all so important and dear to me.Remain! Let's have a picnic where we can all eat and get drunk. Just kidding, but why leave. Everyone here is so valuable to this place. Just give it time. All will make sense yet again.
