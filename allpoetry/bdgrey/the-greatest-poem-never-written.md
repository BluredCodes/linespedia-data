This is the greatest poem never written.I will reveal to you all my wisdom,in a poetic stance never to be rivaled.I will tell of how to live life fully,and tell of you love in its entirety.Love is unending and has no boundaries,so live life for love even when found.I will tell you of stories untold,such as your life completely unknown.I will muse of sorrow and heartbreak as well,ending with a message of survival's desire.Listen closely for when I am done,to your own destiny will you succumb.Nature's beauty unparalleled in grace,much as the beauty spread across the face.These are just a few of the issues I'll address,but most importantly of why not to digress.So as I ramble on in unending rhyme,just remember the limits of time.Now I begin, instantaneously ending,bestowed upon you, is a life unlived.'Where is the poem?' you ask.Well, you just missed it.For it is untold and will remain so.The greatest poem never written? This one is all about your perception.
