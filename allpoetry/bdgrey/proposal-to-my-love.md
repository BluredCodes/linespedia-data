My love for you will span eternity,
and all I ask is the same in return.
I knew from the day I met you, how special you are,
and you saw something in me that I'd never known.
You are so amazingly beautiful,
and you are so unique that you are my vision of perfection.
I have never felt anything that mattered so completely,
as much as you matter to me.
I love you so much and I feel your love too,
and I will Always be here with you,
even in death.
You are the best thing to happen to me,
and need you in my arms every night.
I can't bare to be without you,
and when I am, you're all I think about.
Even after two years of seeing each other everyday,
I love more with each passing moment.
I really am going somewhere with this, I promise.

I love the way we always say I see you later,
instead of saying goodbye.
I love the way your face looks, when you're trying to be mad,
but can't because you always forgive me after two seconds.
I love our hugs, I love our kisses,
but most of all, I just love you.

Will You Marry Me?....
