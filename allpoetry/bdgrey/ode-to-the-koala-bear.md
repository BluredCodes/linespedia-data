They have the instinct to hug
whenever picked up.
They're so fluffy and soft,
much as the deepest shag carpet.
When you look into their beady little eyes,
you can't help but melt and fall in love.
Charcoal black noses serve to accent their face,
with ears so round and always perked up.
They love to eat eucalyptus,
and climb trees so high.
They make the greatest of mothers,
to their pint size, cute babies.
They're oh so short,
and sleep the days away.

This is why koalas are beautiful,
and brighten me up, each and every day.
