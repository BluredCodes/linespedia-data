---
title: "Saying Nothing Says So Much"
writer: "bdgrey"
slug: "saying-nothing-says-so-much"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The only way to truly show my lasting love for you,
cannot be through written word, and act of futile strife.
The way my eyes look upon the glory of your soul,
and that glimmer you see is one of definite proof,
that lets you in and shows you so much future
The fact that I never tire of your gorgeous body,
and the feel of silk that is your skin,
only helps to put me in heaven.
The way I hold you with gentle care,
please know that I'll never let you go,
despite all events that cause us much despair.
The smile on my face shows how much I need you,
because when you're not there,
I'm incomplete and that smile fades to frown.
I'll never let one thing bad happen to your life,
so I always walk near the street,
with you at my side being held so tight.
The tone in my voice is nothing short of love,
as each word I say is carried by pure white dove,
to your ears and into your heart.

The only way to truly show my lasting love for you,
is when I say nothing at all,
and let my soul do all the talking,
with each emotion meant for none other.
That is how I love you,
and how I know our future will always come.
Frome here on into eternity,
I love you.
