Ten years had past since I'd seen you last.
So when my wife and I ran into you,
I reminisced of our love when it was new.
You were the one I would love till death,
even after my final, deciding breath.
All the emotions came to me,
including the day I bent down on one knee.
I proposed to you that night,
under the guidance of moonlight,
and you simply replied "yes".
A decision, though careless,
would lead us further in life,
as you were meant to be my wife.
I had spent countless nights praying
that you would spend your life next to me laying.
I loved you with all my heart,
pleading to God to not let us part.

Now I see you after so long,
knowing our love is not quite gone.
I look at you as time stands still,
knowing of God's divine and ubiquitous will.
Then I look at my wife and I have no doubt,
for she is the one I can't live without.

Thank you God, for ignoring me then,
for only now does my life truly begin.An unanswered prayer is not necessarily a punishment or overlook. Sometimes they can be the greatest gift of all. Don't give up, because all roads lead somewhere. Some are just better than others.
