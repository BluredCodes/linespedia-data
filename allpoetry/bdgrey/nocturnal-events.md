Nocturnal events remain mysterious,journeys unknown so constantly perilous.Bereavement foreseen through mindful, aged clerics,suppressed by their knowledge, hidden by barracks.Lethargic old corpses ceaselessly nameless,resounding occasion with death so shameless.Forever they saunter in bleak sanity,despondent with loss of humanity.Driven by odium's afflicted young state,spirits flee aimlessly devoid of debate.Incentive for flight inclines furtive creatures,grasping for erudition of vile features.This is percieved by those who regard it,with ubiased vision and fire soon lit.Nocturnal events with merciless malice,attest as the truth in quests of the palace.
