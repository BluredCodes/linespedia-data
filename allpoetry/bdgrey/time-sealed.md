---
title: "Time Sealed"
writer: "bdgrey"
slug: "time-sealed"
source: "AllPoetry"

We were taking a walk,through a park covered in frost,
as our breath hung in the air,when it happened.
I looked into your eyes as they reflected the stars,
and told you how I felt to the beat of my heart.
You looked so surprised with that gleam in your eyes,
as I bent down on one knee and presented to you the ring.
A tear fell down your cheek as your teeth chattered solemnly,
when I placed the saphirre around your shivering finger.
We embraced each other with a passion unmatched,
and there I knew our love would eternally last.

Out from the woods came a stranger sincere,
smiling as he uttered kind-hearted words.
He said it was great for two people to love,
and that we were both lucky with best wishes to us.
Then without compassion he yielded a gun,
wanting our money and all things of value.
Yet what he didn't know,
was that you were the one.
A struggled ensued over the birthstone of engagement,
and a shot rang out, as you fell to the ice anguished.

I held your body as you began to die,
the intruder fled, and fear entered your placid eyes.
Then without warning your body grew limp,
and there I died inwardly as my heart grew thin.
The rest of my life, I had wished for an answer,
of why I could not change the past,
or find the courage to just give up.

Given the chance I would return to the past,
So that I may die by your side,
and live in the light.
Yet I continued to live so as not to grow selfish,
in hopes that in Heaven we will be together again.
I fear that all I may accomplish,
is to live forever in that moment,
watching you die over and over.
But atleast we'd have the minutes,
leading up to that conclusion.
