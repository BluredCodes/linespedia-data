title: "Peaceful Slumber"

Tonight the universe sleeps in peace,
finally after centuries without.
For one night only everything may rest,
from the stars to trees and all inbetween.
When they awake and carry on their ways,
hopefully all will be content,
with they dreams that they had,
so that inspiration may follow in step.

But who's to say when they all will awake,
for if the sun is fast asleep,
surely it cannot rise to stir us from the deep.
So this period of subconcious clarity,
may last for all eternity,
until something awakes with a fright in the night.

So tonight the entire universe sleeps,
yet our soul remains behind,
and watches over us ensuring our destiny.
That way dreams will come,
and remind us who we are and want to be.
