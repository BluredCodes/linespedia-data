I stand by the side of the road.
My thumb is up, yet no one stops.
They do not care for me,
for they are completely oblivious.

This I know because I too
was once that way.
Now I regret all I have done.
No one stops, and no one cares.

What have I done
to receive this treatment?
The answer is nothing.
And that's the problem.We must all care for one another. You never know when you'll be in a similar situation.
