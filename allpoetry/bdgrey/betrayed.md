---
title: "Betrayed"
writer: "bdgrey"

Do you remember what happened back then,
long ago when we were still friends.
You said forever and this I believed,
how foolish was I for this I concede.
Betrayed and forgotten I stood there a while,
I envisage your face, but reminisce on your guile.
Misconstrued fabrications reside in my heart,
so from my new wisdom your aura departs.
I have done little to warrant your wrath,
so now I must decide where to turn on this path.
I believed I'd lost all, but the truth I have seen,
for now you are gone, and my mind is now clean.
I bid you adue and good luck in life,
filled with nothing but livid strife.
I must leave now because of short time,
so I turn left, hoping for events benign.
I look back once more from where I renounce,
so truly in love for whatever it counts.

Now I look upon you, years have gone by,
I loved you back then, but now I ask why.
I have lost nothing, and now am recovered,
but you..., well over your mind it forever hovers.
You cold-hearted bitch.
