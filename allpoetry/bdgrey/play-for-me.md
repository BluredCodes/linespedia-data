title: "Play For Me....."
writer: "bdgrey"

Play for me on your harmonious violin
the soft tune         
              of passing over.
So that I may find peace in life,
and move on into the depths of an undisturbed soul.
A soul that for so long
has been dead without true love near.
Play it for me
   so that we may be...
together again,
and falling in love again.
I want nothing more than to hear those strings,
brush against the chords in agreeable melody.

I cry every night in a lonesome bed,
where once she lay softly breathing.
The sheets unharmed with her pillow,
as a silhouette of where she rest her head.
I can still hear vividly as if spoken in life,
our final words of "I love you".
When I awoke I knew of what to expect,
and there it proved as a final regret.
         Regret for not loving enough.

As each note hits right to my heart,
and the world become dark,
a new light comes forth.
And in this light a shadow emerges,
coming ever closer,
and the feeling unexplainable,
fills my soul.
