---
title: "9 Ways of Love"

I love you 'cause you're beautiful,
I love you when you sigh.
I love you when you look at me,
I love you as simply stand by.
I love you with my entire soul,
I'll love you when I die.
I love you unconditionally,
I love you all the time.
I love the way you brush your hair,
I love you without reply.

You don't love me,
and this I don't know why.
