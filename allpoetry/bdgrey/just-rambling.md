In the hopes of explaining nothing for fear of nothng being
  
accomplished, I have succeeded admirably, for my art and my 

life revolve around the one thing I know not how to explain. 

It's not that I lack the one thing, I have it and I need it, 

but to no avail have my attempts, and might I add in vain as 

well, have they ended. All too often do we as a race attempt 

futilely that which is inconquerable, and in a forensic display 

of failure induced hysteria, do we portray and selfishly forget.
   
   Now don't get me wrong. I love succeeding, but isn't failure 

the cornerstone for improvement, and in that sense, is the 

person who fails the most the most educated, and the person to 

which success has so eloquently layed her fingers upon, the 

most flawed person. Oh who knows. All I know is that I have 

failed just as much as I have succeeded, but that the latter is 

a result of the beginning.This is just something I wrote when I was in a philosophical state of mind. I do that a lot. It's just something I thought I would share with you all. I hope you enjoy, and that it induces thought.
