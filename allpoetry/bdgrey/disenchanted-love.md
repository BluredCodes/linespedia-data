My love for you is like a river,
long ago run dry.
I once thought there was no limit,
further even than the sky.
We were in love that is a truth,
yet something went awry.
Now I look at you without reprisal,
with a clearly focused eye.
Now I have finally moved on,
with absence of love I can't deny.
Nor can say I wish to know you,
for that would be a lie.
So now I can say without regret,
three letters forming "bye".

I have moved on to a greater love,
that I know will never die.
Now I'm back where I belong,
upon that mortal high.
So to my town where I once dwelled,
I reminisce and simply sigh.
For I must leave and move further north,
to progress further in my life.This basically has to deal with moving away from your home town. Though I haven't moved yet, I will be as soon as my girlfriend graduates. It's about time for me to move out on my own, and reach for the stars within my reach.
