THE GAME OF GOSSIP

Chatter, chatter in the room
Words tossed from wall to wall
Each competing for a space
As they do in basketball

Passing bits of gossip
From one to one it flies
Pretending it's a secret
Delight is in their eyes

They say it in hushed voices
As they dribble every word
Passing on note paper
Everything they've heard

As the tidbits travel
All around the court
No one thinks of outcomes
No one keeps the score

Holding every tacky word
They revel in the force
To add yet another foul
And exclude one from the court

Finally as they make their goals
To bring down one's good name
They find someone else to hit
And on goes the game
