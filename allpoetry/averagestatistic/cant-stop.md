title: "Can't Stop"
writer: "AverageStatistic"

Can't stop this hurting.
I'e been misled.
I can't control it.
What should I do?
I feel so broken.
I can't stop this.
You control me.
But what do I control?
My heart ripping.
My eyes are empty.
I don't know how I should feel.
I'm used to this already.
Why can't I trust you?
Why can't I trust myself?Feb. 21,
