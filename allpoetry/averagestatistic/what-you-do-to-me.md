title: "What You Do To Me"

Look at these things you do to me...
You make me,
Cut, 
Cry,
and whimper in sadness.
Look at these things you make me say,
I love you,
I hate you,
I don't need you anymore...
Look at these things you make me do...
Care,
Love you,
Hate you...
Look what you do to me.Feb. 15,
