---
title: "Wrong Kiss"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "wrong-kiss"
source: "AllPoetry"

So ashamed.
Perfect time, perfect place, perfect kiss.
Wrong person.
Why wasn't it you?
I feel so dirty.
I feel so wrong.
Bad time, bad place, bad kiss.
It wasn't you.
Scrub my lips,
from these dirty lies.
I feel so right about the wrong things.
It wasn't you,
It wasn't you.
