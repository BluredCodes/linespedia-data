---
title: "Betrayal"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "betrayal"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47960-Betrayal-by-AverageStatistic"
---

I want you to feel the pain I'm feeling,
Experience the things I'm experiencing,
Have the tears drip down your face.
FOR A CHANGE.
Live the life I'm living,
Step in the shoes I'm wearing,
Have the knife on your wrist.
FOR A CHANGE.
Walk on the road I'm walking,
Run the path I'm running,
Have the rope around your neck.
FOR A CHANGE.
Write the poems I'm writing,
Hide the things I'm hiding,
Reveal the things I'm revealing.
FOR A CHANGE.
Say the things I'm saying,
Sacrifice the things I'm sacrificing,
Dream the dreams I'm dreaming.
FOR A CHANGE.
Do the things I'm doing.
Die the way I'm dieng,
Jump the bridge I'm jumping.
FOR A CHANGE.
Cry the tears I'm tearing,
Wonder the things I'm wondering,
Contemplate the things I'm contemplating.
FOR A CHANGE.
Fear the things I'm fearing,
Sing the song I'm singing,
Fight the fights I'm fighting.
FOR A CHANGE.
Loose the things I'm loosing,
Change the things I'm changing,
Pass the things I'm passing.
FOR A CHANGE.
Crave the things I'm craving,
See the things I'm seeing,
Love the one I'm loving...
FOR A CHANGE.
