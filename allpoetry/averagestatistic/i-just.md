---
title: "I Just"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "i-just"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I just want you to love me for me...
Not for who you think I am.
I want you to try and understand me...
Not just apologize and pretend things are okay.
I just want you to hold me...
Without having to think about tomorrow.
I just want you to live today...
Without thinking about harsh things the future holds.
I want you to hold my hand...
Without looking at the clock and watching time go by.
I want you to tell me how you feel...
Rather then walking away assuming I know what you're feeling.
I want you to cry if you need to...
Without being scared of what I would say.
I want you to understand that I understand you...
I don't want you to be afraid of me...
I want you to open your eyes so you can see what's in front of you.
You're looking too hard for someone who's so close.
You're looking too hard for someone you want, and not hard enough for someone you need.
