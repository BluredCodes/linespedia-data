PLease don't forget me
everytime you remember
about what used to happen
and about what used to be
starting out slow
and ending so fast
the fear has been forgotten now
the end is approaching, i can tell
it soon will come near
emptiness is forgotten
shadows will follow
tears are so hot
sour to the core
dreams left deniable
feelings broken and ignorable
i feel great depseration
trying to walks life morbid destination
loosing all my power
hour after hour
changing all mistakes
expectations are too high
yet self-esteem is low
running away from happiness
trying to forget my bombarded past
the destruction of my feelings will end... soon
Thanks to you.?,
