All you do is deny me,
all you do is ignore what you see.
All you do is walk away,
with absolutely nothing to say.
All you do is lie,
all you do is make me cry.
All you do is make me feel so bad,
all you do is make me sad.
All you do is make me happy,
all you do is find the best in me.
All you do is smile,
being with you is so worthwhile.
All you do is stare,
prove to me how much you care.
All you do is mean the world to me...
I love you more then anything.Feb. 20,
