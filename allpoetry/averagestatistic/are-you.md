Are you ignoring me?
So you know that I can't go on without you?
I know you want to see me suffer...
As I want to see you try.
Have I wasted all my time?
Watching you avoid me?
Seeing you talk to her, and I see myself unnoticed.
I want to give you what you need.
What you want.
What you desire.
But what do I want?
I can't beleive I let myself get so obsessed.
.. and then you left.
You pushed me away, and now I don't know where I am...
.. or who I am.
I could always give up...
.. like you've done so many times before.
Just walk away...
Unnoticed.Oct. 26,
