May I wish upon this shooting star?
It'll be the best wish by far.
Cluttered dreams I'll always keep.
Keep this wish, as I rest asleep.
Broken hearts, soon forgotten.
Left in dream land, my soul is rotten.
Need persistence, shuttered at mind.
My life has been wasted, a lack of time.
If I forgot your face, I'll wish again.
So it is on these stars, that I depend.
You will meet more people new.
Will I be the same to you?
Can I wish upon this moon?
Wishing I can see you soon.
Star dust dances in my eyes.
Awakening a new found suprise.
WIll you remember my face, my name?
Will the sun strike through more pain?
WIll the night vanish to day?
Will you love me the exact same way?
The universe abruptly tickles my face...
My planets, helping me wish you were back in this place.June. 22. 01,
