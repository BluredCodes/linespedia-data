Trying to get over the fact that...
I've wasted all my time.
Just, standing here, waiting for you to come back.
But I know you're gone forever.
Yet why do I wait?
Because I've told myself that you still love me.
For the sake of feeling better.
But as usual, I don't.
I used to love you.
I used to love us.
I still do...
I still want to.
I regret things I've said and done.
I don't know what I want.
All I know is that I want you back.Feb. 22,
