Behind this smile,
I'm fading away.
Behind my weaknesses,
I hope to be brave someday.
Behind my toubles,
you'll see my fear.
Behind this fake,
you'll see me disappear.
Behind this laughter,
you'll see my pain.
Behind these lying eyes,
you'll see I want to be the same.
Behind my face,
you'll see my truth.
Behind my innocent gesture,
you'll see I'm nothing but a wasted youth.I don\'t know,
