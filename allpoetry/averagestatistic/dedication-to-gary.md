title: "Dedication To Gary"
writer: "AverageStatistic"

I don't want to admit that I miss you...
and that my life just isn't the same without you in it.
But if you knew that...
Then I'd be letting myself down.
I'd feel so weak, so miserable.
I do miss you.
I miss the way things were.
You know me, I can't handle change.
You always seemed so close to me.
But now we've grown so far apart.
I hate distance...
Past experiences are coming back to haunt me.
I miss you!
Why can't we be the same?
Why can't we be like we were before?
We were once alike, but we changed eachother.
I can't handle it.
I love you, I miss you...
Come back.
