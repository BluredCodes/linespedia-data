---
title: "Is There?"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "is-there"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Is there a substance inside us?
That debates the way we feel?
Does it figure out how much anger you control?
Or when we're being real?
Does it prove who we really are?
Or do we already know?
Does it control what we hide?
When we really need to show?
Why is this taking over my mind?
I can't take this anymore.
I really don't want to wait for this.
What am I even waiting for?
I'm waiting in this line.
But what do I get?
Or unless I have went up already...
Why hasn't anything happened yet??????,
