title: "Great"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "great"
source: "AllPoetry"

Great person,
Great life.
Do you see the knife she uses to cut her wrist at night?
Great body,
Great smile.
Do you see the tears that drip from her eyes?
Great laugh,
Great sense of humor.
Do you see her laugh as blood falls from her skin?
Great generosity,
Great kindness.
Do you see her being nice to herself as she puts the rope around her neck?
Beautiful eyes,
Perfect words.
Do you see her jump off that bridge?
Sweet person,
Bombarded past.
Do you notice this girl die today?
