I JUST WANT TO BE PERFECT FOR YOU.
CAN'T YOU SEE?
ALL THAT I DO... IS FOR YOU.
I TRY SO HARD AND YOU DON'T EVEN PAY ATTENTION.
I STARE AT YOU AND YOU WALK RIGHT PAST.
WHAT AM I SUPPOSED TO SAY TO SOMEONE WHO IGNORES ME?
WHAT DO YOU GIVE SOMEONE WHO HAS EVERYTHING?
YOU'RE BETTER OFF WITH HER...
I'M SURE SHE CAN TREAT YOU BETTER THEN I CAN.
I'M SURE SHE CAN GIVE YOU THE THINGS THAT I CAN'T.
DO WHATEVER WILL PLEASE YOU.
DON'T LET ME GET IN YOUR WAY.I don't know what I meant by this...
