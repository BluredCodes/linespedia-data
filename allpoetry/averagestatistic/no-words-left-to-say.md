---
title: "No Words Left To Say"
writer: "AverageStatistic"

This silence makes me tremble,
I can't live without your reassuring voice.
It's just so bittersweet---
When all crashes down, what do I do?
I miss you...
You mean so much to me.
If only I could prove it to you...
You're just so amazing.
The only place I feel safe,
is in your arms, but you're not here.
There are no words that I could use to describe the way I feel for you.
I could use "love" but it's just so over-rated and over-used...
I just don't know what to do.
