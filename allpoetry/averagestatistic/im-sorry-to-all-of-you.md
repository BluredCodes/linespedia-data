title: "I'm sorry, to all of you"

I'm sorry I'm not like everyone else.
I'm sorry I'm not who you had in mind.
I'm sorry I can't do what you want... the right way.
I'm sorry I don't dress the way you do.
I'm sorry I don't do the things you do.
I'm sorry I'm not friends with who you're friends with.
I'm sorry that I embarrass you.
I'm sorry that I don't impress you.
I'm sorry if I don't have the funniest jokes.
I'm sorry if you don't understand me.
I'm sorry if I don't have the best looks.
I'm sorry if I'm not perfect.
I'm sorry if I don't say the right things.
I'm sorry if I make a fool out of myself.
I'm sorry if I express myself.
I'm sorry that I'm not exactly like you.
I'm sorry that I'm not good enough for you.
For lack of time and better words...
I'm sorry for being me.
