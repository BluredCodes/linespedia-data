What does forever feel like?
I say,"I'll love you forever."
But will you love me back, with all I love you with?
I won't drift...
I won't get over you...
And I could never actually stop loving you.
You mean the world to me...
I hope you know that.
When I don't see you... I don't see myself.
You make me whole.
When you smile.
I'm good for weeks.
Just knowing that you're happy, satisfies me.
I want to be with you forever.
Or atleast that's exactly what I want, right now.
The future will come, the past will fade...
But my love for you...
Will stay...
Forever.
