No matter how far you go...
I can still see you.
I can stil hear your voice.
No matter how long you're gone...
I'll still miss you,
I'll still love you.
Why aren't I the same without you?
I feel so useless, so broken.
I can't handle it anymore.
A life without you...
... is no life at all.March. 2,
