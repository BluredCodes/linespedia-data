---
title: "Moon Kills Night"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "moon-kills-night"

I see you with my own two eyes.
Strangling yourself in the far fetched light.
You search for truth, but lies find you.
You demand simple answers.
But get pointless questions in return.
What has happened to you?
You used to be strong, now you're whimpering in sadness.
You try to keep it all together,
yet your puzzle keeps falling apart.
Just when you think you have everything sorted out,
confusion hits you, and question marks travel through your mind.
How am I supposed to react?
I depend on you and you dissapear.
In your own troubled ray of light.
When does this evil sun ever go down?Jan. 11th,
