I hate waiting.
Looking at this clock.
Time hates me.
I can't get over you.
I don't think I even want to.
You made me feel so alive.
I hate goodbyes...
Especially when you come back completely changed.
I miss you so much.
The feelings just aren't there...
Or maybe they're just not mutual.
I can't handle this.
I can't take this anymore.
I can't take you with someone else.
Wait, wait, wait.
Tick tock, tick tock.
I need to smash this clock.
