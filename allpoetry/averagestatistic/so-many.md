There are so many things,
that you don't know about me.
You turn around and say, "Farewell".
Leaving me with these thoughts and feelings,
that can't be dealt with alone.
I stare at you, 
as you stare at me.
What am I supposed to think?
How am I supposed to react?
This can't be real...
or can it?Feb. 17,
