---
title: "Invisible... Gone"
writer: "AverageStatistic"

Another boring day without you,
I don't smile much without you here.
Broken pieces left behind,
pointless things I wish I would have said.
Because now they mean so much more.
Memories... coming back again.
I wish you'd see.
All the things that I do wrong.
You were right the whole time.
Missing you.
Staring back at an empty face.
Grieving for you infront of me.
Invisible... gone...
