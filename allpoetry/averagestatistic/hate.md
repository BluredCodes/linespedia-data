---
title: "Hate"
writer: "AverageStatistic"

I hate when you do that.
When you smile at me and I'm in the worst mood.
I hate when I tell you there's nothing wrong.
Even though you know there is.
I hate when there's a song on the radio,
and you ask me who it is.
When you know who's playing you just want to make me look,
like a fool.
I hate the way you look at me,
when I just want to punch something.
I hate when I'm aggrivated,
and you always seem to get in the way.
I hate how you know more then me.
I hate how you laugh when people make fun.
I hate how we're so alike.
I love you.
