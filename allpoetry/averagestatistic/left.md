title: "Left..."

I'm lost...
I need you now...
Best time isn't it?
Because you're not here.
You're here, but you're gone.
Now I can't remember,
How you used to  be, until you left.
Hearts are broken.
I want you to fix it.
But I don't know how.
I just know that I need you now.
All my hope for this, has gone away.
I miss it badly...
I don't know what to do anymore.
I used to be strong, but now I'm weak.
I need you to rescue me.
I can see your hand, waving in the air.
As I see you walking...
Running, so far away.
I'm desperate.
I want those feelings back.
I want to love you.
I want you to love me.
