---
title: "Help Me"
writer: "AverageStatistic"
slug: "help-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47961-Help-Me-by-AverageStatistic"
---

im stuck in this world with no answers
as i try to hide my secrets behind empty smiles
the smiles that i try to paint on everyday
but they just wear away
if i told you whats been happening
would you care or understand?
or atleast try?
would you let me cry?
if i told you my life story
would you mind?
treat me kind?
what would you do?
if i put a blade to my wrist
would you get scared?
run if you were dared?
if you knew how i was like inside
would you hide?
lend me some pride?
or let me weep
in my sleep
alone?
would you wipe the tears from my face?
or call me a disgrace?
what would you do?
if i reacted
and you werent distracted
would you bother?
what if i wanted to wipe the slate clean?
would you be all mean?
or help me change???,
