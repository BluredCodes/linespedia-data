i dont mean to hurt you like i do
ive been so selfish
you should have told me
i wonder what you do at night
all alone, replaying all the harsh things ive ever done to you
it makes me feel awful
knowing that you dwell on what i say
how can i treat you like this
you just wanted to be friends
right?I'm not expecting people to comment about this, because you don't really understand.. but if you want to comment, go for it.  i don't expect you to know whats going on in my head.. its about this girl named jordan....
