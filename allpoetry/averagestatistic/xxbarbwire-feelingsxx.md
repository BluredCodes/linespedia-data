If I had a secret, would you want to know?
Are you willing to hear the story, that was too deep to show?
Do you want to hear the problems I never revealed?
Or about this secret life that has been concealed?
Do you want to know the truth?  Figure out what's wrong?
So that you can feel all big and strong?
Will you take my hand, guide me along the way?
Take me from the places, I never wanted to stay.
Always trying to keep in touch...
Too bad I'll miss you far too much.
You take the words I'd like to speak.
Leaving me here, feeling weak.
Barbwire fences, away from harm.
Inprisoned in my self-alarm.
A white moon, a yellow sun.
Piercing thorns of shaking fun.
Too many lonely smiles...
From everything that's not worthwhile.July. 22. 01,
