I thought you wanted me and I thought it was right.
Now my mind refuses to block it no matter how hard I try,
I was thinking about you and me,thought it was going smoothe.
But when the time was up, I was the little fool,
thinking, what you led me to thinking.
You started the verse I joined at the chorus of the same song you were singing!
But you liked playing with my head keeping me on a leash,
we weren't even together, we were an unfinished masterpeice.
That was what I thought until you said I was just a waste,
I felt used and broken and feeling stuck in my mouth left a lasting hatred taste!
Now don't come running when you want to,
Right now I give beady eyes, BITCH, I taunt you!
And don't think we have a chance together,
So confused little girl good bye forever!!!!
