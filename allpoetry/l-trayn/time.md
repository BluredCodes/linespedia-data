Time is on everyones mind.
And when its up you cant do anything to fixit,
your time must have been up but you must of missed it,
you can take your life and might as well kiss it.
Cause it can be taken away at any moment as you read this fret,
Im not promising anything or giving out threats.
Im being truthful as any true that I could possibly be,
and Im being really sure that Im giving you honesty.
So think about your life dont let it pass you by,
a million things can be done on a blink of an eye.
But the question remains.Why?
Why we come and go at anytime of a day or year.
But just live one day at a time you only have one life here!!!
