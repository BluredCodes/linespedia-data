I dare you! Are you trying to test me
I dont care if you send the police to arrest me.
I'm comming after you and thats all to it,
Talk shit behind my back, trust me i'll do it.
Everything was cool until you had to judge,
you fucked up the cool in us you started up this grudge.
So don't act all innocent little boy its too late,
your jaws relying on my fist which will end this fate.
I can't believe that you stooped so low to make me look bad
you made youself look stupid, and right now I'm glad.
It just gives me the right little boy I Bestow you
and this moment and on till I get my peice of you I own you
Try to run away, I promise you wont make it
and I'll take your scrony body,I promise I'll brake it
This aint a joke, shit i still aint faking it
You know who you are, so come out of closet,
Come out now cause Me right here I Just Lost It.
