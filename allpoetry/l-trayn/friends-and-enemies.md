---
title: "friends and enemies"
writer: "L trayn"
slug: "friends-and-enemies"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37184-friends-and-enemies-by-L-trayn"
---

Friends are the people who are by your side,
Enemies are the ones who just want to fight.
Friends make you happy and just make you laugh,
Enemies irritate and always seem to make you mad.
Friends are family, the family you always want,
Enemies are like a headache something you hated but always got.
Friends forever you always said, hoping to follow through,
Enemies are confused they say shit that ain't true.
But friends do have days that make it feel like they're an enemy,
But enemies will always have their days and seem to always hate me.
BUT DO YOU WANT TO KNOW SOMETHING? I DON'T GIVE A SHIT!
