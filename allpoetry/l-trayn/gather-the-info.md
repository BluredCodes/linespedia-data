I gather info to keep up to date.
So when I talk to people my subjects won't be so late.
I do things to impress people which is wrong but i don't see it,
and when they want me to turn into someone else, Im so fast to be it.
Its hard to see yourself in anothers point of view, 
and when you finally do you can't believe its you.
You think to yourself naw its just me being me.
But in then end when you notice, it changes your heart, soul,faith, and beliefs.
In the state of mind that your in when you deny,
will effect your ablity to accomplish and to give things a try.
So when in doubt try to think things in a different Prospective.
And maybe your day will go along smoothly it won't be so hectic.
