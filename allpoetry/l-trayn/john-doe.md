I know you see me walking through the halls
Im the type most people would call 
a Loner!
By definition it means standing alone,or not having peers.
People not really caring about ideas, thoughts, intrests, or my fears
I'll be a John Doe a no named face
untalented player last one to finish a race
I'll still lose even if I dont challenge anyone.
my days are gray while yours is filled with sun.
you got it made so dont take advantage
dont be all big head or take it for granted
CAUSE I DONT HAVE WHAT YOU GOT.
Girls love you they love me not.
I want to be like you morph into your shoes
I'll put my thoughts in your head put them in use.
But i cant i just have to deal with me
God did it for a reason,i wouldnt want him to feel guilty!
