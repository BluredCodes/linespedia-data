I gave you everything your heart desired.
It was you that I loved and it was you I admired.
Wasn't I always there when you called my name.
Despite the situation I dropped what I did and came,
running to you with only you on my mind.
Running to you like it was the last time.
BOUGHT you this, PAID for that, RESERVED a night for dinner.
And i came out of the relationships a LOSER, a wanna be winner.
YOU HURT ME FOR WHAT REASON? I DIDN'T FOCUS  MY LIFE ON YOU!
WAS GIVING MY TRUST, LOVE, HONESTY AND TO ALWAYS BE TRUE!
WAS IT NOT ENOUGH OR WAS IT LIKE GAME
Or did you feel like I did, did you feel the same
WHATEVER!!!!!!!!!
