It was the best when I entered this love
Something that felt higher then the sky above
And leaving this relationship, started to make me think of.
that there are golddiggers and that they do exist.
I had it and its a lesson I learn a very tough experience.
But now all the priviledges you had it went out the door,
all the love that was there well theres no more.
Its all cause of you thats why I feel this way
and never take you back no matter how much you pray.
When you call me anytime and in the year or summer,
im just gonna pick the phone up and block your number.
When you follow me around stock me at night,
and you ask for my autograph ill sign get a life.
I'll take the diamonds off your finger undress your neck,
get all the credit cards shreded and bounce the checks.
Get all the gucci forget the smuchi leave you with zilch.
Get all the stuff that you rented and make you pay the bills. 
When i see in the corner ill go the other way,		
don’t try to talk to me cause i got nothing say.
But just to clear things make you aware,
just keep away from me just stay over there.
