You see let me start by slowly recking you with the differences
You on the other hand ran away from it, But I never hid from this.
Secrets is a simple word, but yet it can hide a lifetime of memories.
Mine could of been hidin from the world for many,many centries.
BUT YOU, you just had to let tell everyone just had to let it out.
Even though it was my secrets, shit it wasn't even you that it was about.
It was about me my inner thoughts, my mind I SPILLED FOR YOU TO READ.
But you just had to tell a soul, gave it out for free.
You see you thought you'd get away, I'm telling you it shouldn't be me you fucked with.
And in the end when it all comes down, just hope its not me your stuck with.
cause Im gonna make it hell for you, until you scream your sin
keep rubbing it in till you can't sleep, and its tatooed with in.
But for now let it be known that Im waiting for the right time.
And I'll yell the differences while I hang you for my shrine.
But I ain't that crazy but just know your ass is mine!
