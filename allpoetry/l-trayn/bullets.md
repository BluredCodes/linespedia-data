Bullets flying around and soon one will hit you,
and until the doctor pulls it out it'll stick with you.
Bullets can relate to a lot of things in life like drugs,
a lot of people are scared and most love it when life begins to bug.
People need it to set them free but honestly it causes more pain.
For your family, friends,everyone it gets worse or stays the same.
But from my experience, never tried it but my friends did from pot to rock
and with the bullet I've been emphasizing about ill shoot them I shit you not!!
Cause drugs kill so might as well just do it,
they do drugs, they think about their life and say screw it
but Im a true friend I make sure they're clean.
They do stop their shit, cause they see other druggies and hate what they see
So take this to heart and stay with the right path
Then look at the druggies, SIT BACK AND LAUGH
Cause its not you that took the risk
and its not you being escorded with platnum on your wrist.
