---
title: "The shit i face"
writer: "L trayn"

The shit is there especially on me
the shit dont care and dont come as it seems
nothing compares to the shit i face
i know life aint fair but make it fair in this case
give me a sign not problems and stress
i get confused sometimes which forces me to guess
and take the wrong choices which guides me through the course
what if i take the wrong road its my ass not yours
its my life my air that i breathe
now im begging you begging you please
dont step in my way and dont try to stop me
im doing what i want when i want when i need
running through my head thoughts yearn to burst
from the day i was born till the day i ride the hearse
i will throw my dreams and begin to chase
and knock down, erase all the shit, THE SHIT I FACE.
