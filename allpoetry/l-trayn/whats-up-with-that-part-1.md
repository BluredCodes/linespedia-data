Treated you the best and only you,
when you hung up on me i kept phoning you.
I dropped what I did when you gave the snap,	
and I cherished every moment and everything we have,
I put a diamond on your finger and i dress your neck with gold,
I gave gucci for a smuchi and a wallet you cant hold.
Cause the fifties and hundreds filled up and over flowed,
I had the cash rollin up it was outta controll.
I was making it for us and that was all that mattered,
and the more that i made it the more the pockets got fatter.
But i promised ill keep you wealthy and love you girl,
and give you what you wanted mostly love you girl.
When the money stop commin you left me in the cold,
I was lost and alone waiting for someone to hold.
So don’t say it was true love now that Im on track,
and when you try to talk to me im gonna turn to my back.
Cause I don’t need you any more so just get out of my life,
don’t pull that I still love you baby and then start to cry.
Callin my house leavin a message after the tone, 
paging and bothering me calling my cell phone.
Don’t act like your hurt your just hurt cause the money,
just thinking that I loved you is just kinda funny.
