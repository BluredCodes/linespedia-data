Remember those crazy things we did,
since day one, since we were little kids.
Went through a lot, 
and ya we fought,
agreed, apologized as if it never happen.
Now im in a situation which i would never imagine.
My friend, my best friend if you want the label.
I just wish this was just a myth, legend,or a fabel.
I dont care just give me back my friend now,
I dont care what you do or even how. 
Why did you take him,
were you mistaken
for someone else, please you still have time to make him live
Why did you lend him a life if you'll take back what you give.
Why create life if it'll be destroyed? What you think were made to dispose
your the one that chose we didnt have the right.
Then you take our love ones one by one every night
And expect us to follow what you believe or what you preach.
How when I stick my hand to you and you dont even try to reach.
And you say to practice what the lord had to teach
if only we knew what you kept under the sheets,
maybe we'll understand why you do what you do
and just maybe i wouldn't be so mad at you
Now instead you pray for me and see how it feels
if I take your friend away and it wouldn't be so real
how would you would react, the same, which is hard to conciel
But its too late you took him away
my friend I didnt even say goodbye that day
But now I realize now I understand
but for now till its my turn I LOST YOU MAN!
