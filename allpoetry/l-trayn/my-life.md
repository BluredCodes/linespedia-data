My life revolves around me. 
I create the future that i want to see.

When I reminise about the good and bad times.
Explaining it in actions like a lonesome mime.

No one hearing my voice but the purity of me.
The joy of thinking what kind of person I am of what they see.

I'd rather go in silence to see how people react,
just by going a couple days without voicing opinion would have an impact,
just observe life in a way you couldn't possibly think of,
and maybe just maybe you'll find things you'd never dream of,
silence is the answer when you need a point of view,
voicing is the answer when you finally found you!
