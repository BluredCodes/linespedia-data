Crazy Crazy I go So Damn Crazy
Good things become bad and bad seems to amaze me
I end up not caring and love it when people hate me
My parents yelling to do this and that it yearns me to say MAKE ME
and all the confusion isn't making things any better lately
So this is my proposal my speech I write for you to read
and soon it'll be well known like the apostles creed
Explaining the anger felt from the heart at the moment
This aint prewritten, proof read this aint even an enrollment
This is real, Which I call something to recite
Cause non of you can use a pencil like i write.
So your just crazy if you put yourself at a level like me
and if are can back it up top it BITE ME!!!
