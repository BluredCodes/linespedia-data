---
title: "secretly in love with you"
writer: "L trayn"
slug: "secretly-in-love-with-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You know me, but not the feelings i hold.
How would you react to it,if I told
I have to tell sooner or later its getting out of control
the thoughts in my head is starting to unfold
unravel, open up, and its taking its toll
And when it comes out I'll be ready for it
I've been ready i've lived for it
the moment, the rush to see if you feel the same
adrenaline pumping,heart beating a thousand times blood flowing through the veins
I can feel it flowing and smell me starting to get scared
of the answer she'll utter hoping it'll be fair
And I stop breathing untill she begins, and says..........
YES
