title: "The way it is"

Life can only be taken away if you deserve it or you get chosen.
And the closest way to save it is if your bones are preserved or get yourself frozen.
Cause Life has a more meaning to label its given.
And only you can set its rate its your life that your livin.
Don't let anyone tell you or try to run you.
And if they do its not their fault, you brung it on you.
Other people are like rain droping on you trying to make you soaked.
And your umbrella is like your heart you keep it from getting broke.
And your soul,inner thoughts your secrets or protected by your raincoat.
And all these elememts keep you from getting your daylights choked.
So when you think of life just think of rainfall,
and if you keep your umbrella and raincoat your life won't be so bad after all.
