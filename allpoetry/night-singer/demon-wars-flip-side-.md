title: "'Demon Wars' ( flip-side )"
writer: "Night singer"

Standing in a great hall of smooth black stone,
Darker then the deepest night.
Waiting very patiently, 
For when the time is right.
 
A great black knight enters,
And in a low rough voice speaks;
"The hoard is in place my lord"
Then leaves to join his place.

I pick up my blade,
As my brothers and I lead the hoard to battle.
Through black voids we pass,
To reach the land we seek

The Voids open at the foot of mountains
Our target on the other side.
Some scale the great height 
Some take to the skies.

We reach the summit and begin our descent.
The Great Silver Towers are in plain view now.
Faster and faster we move, screaming down the mountain side
Washing down like a great black wave upon the city.

Their once great walls hold for only seconds
Against our dark force.

Four angels rise up to escape, 
Only to meet my blade. 
Their blood splashing against my wings
Bringing a smile to my face.

Seven white priest's step out of the first tower,
And begin to weave their magic, 
When they are swarmed by thirty winged beasts,
Their pieces flying everywhere.  

Screams of death and the clash of steel
Are now all that fill the air.
But it is not long before the enemy falls
And we claim victory for ourselves.

With the last fighters dead, 
I Stand upon the tower,
I looked down to see, where there were once streets,
There are now rivers, of blood and rotting corpes'
   
We feast well that night 
For there is much freshly slaughtered meat.
The first attack is victorious. 
It is the start of the true great war.
