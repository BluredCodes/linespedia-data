---
title: "Slipping"
writer: "Night singer"
slug: "slipping"
source: "AllPoetry"

Falling through the cracks of sanity

Deeper into madness

A mind of untold fury

A realm of dark desires

Screaming out in agony

Yet feeling no pain

Reaching for the instrament 

With which I'll bring their death

Twitching uncontrolably

As my vision bleeds to red
