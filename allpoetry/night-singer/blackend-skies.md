The last war waged
Our race nearly gone
A mother and child slowly walking by,
Beneth the Charred and Blackend skies

Her son he looked up at her
With his innocent bright blue eyes
And asked her in the sweetest voice,
"Dear mother, who burnt the skies?"

She stared down at him with a single tear in her eye,
And she answered in a low soft voice, 
Trying not to cry,
"The government, our leaders dear,
  They've doomed us all to die"
