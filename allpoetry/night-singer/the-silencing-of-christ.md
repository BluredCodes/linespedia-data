---
title: "The silencing of Christ"
writer: "Night singer"
slug: "the-silencing-of-christ"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47604-The-silencing-of-Christ-by-Night-singer"
---

For centuries his voice has rung clear and strong
but it has become muffled and weak.
 
His children sell their beliefs.

(Hell's not buying)

The day draws near when his houses will burn
His armies will fail

The death of their god is imminent

The time has come to repay the christians 
For the way they've raped us

Rise Children Of the Night raise yourselves from your mortal binds
It is time we take back what is ours
It's time for a new god 
It's time for a new world
