title: "Untouchable"

you are your guarding individual obscure starlight

why does it seem 
tragic to be all alone but together 
all the piercing stares are useless 
what is cutting you off 
from your life? 
close black nail stabbing at your palms

sparse love securing you to a 
broken down cross atop
a suffocating bell tower 
only the lonely rings 
of a not-to-be-seen rope tugger 

you can disperse no mementos in the 
rocky inclined world 
spiraling to broken ends- 
stands alone 
in a disturbed void
