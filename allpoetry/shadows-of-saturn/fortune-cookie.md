your  disturbed touch never relized 
that my eyes cried but your universe -
never became contorted and
you see through
windows fogged over never able to see

but may
I see the
never heard dream  
of the nonliving creatures gasping for mercy for
the dead never creates 
life without price 
and you just turn away 

 don't fall because my 
  snapped fingertips won't always be there 
  when the arch brakes spilling 
  sore splinters of heaven 
  you don't think just feel those 
  little pins now useless to swollow 
  Ray of discarded hope no longer yours 
  and jumping off a bridge won't save anyone 

  red liquid of your discarded dreams
  running down your window 
  discarded -forgotton- crumpled 
  fortune cookie 
  can't save you anymorerevise revise revise....
