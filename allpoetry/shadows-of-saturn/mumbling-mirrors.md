---
title: "Mumbling Mirrors"
writer: "shadows of saturn"
slug: "mumbling-mirrors"
source: "AllPoetry"

shackles threatening freedom    unrequited liberation forfeits    sighs of lost    obbituaries fluttering-    like a dream    altogether meaningless    even if the faces are wet with    her sodden tears    phone rings bounce    photographic memory    whispering    Broken    piercing    ironic screams    of girl's last breath    hexagonal squares    wronged thoughts    inner validity weak    even if appreciation is on    front doorstep    four walls screeching-    as raven shatters mirrors
