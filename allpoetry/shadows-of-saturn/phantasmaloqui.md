stars from forsaken universe   where the unicorn   stares at the Earth   never tears break surface   instead fall and shatter   soul flung beneath core   memories are mists   stored in breakable bottles   freedom and life   are dropped without a thought   to the bottem   and no sound is to be heard   of screams never echoingPhantasmaloqui is a word I came up  with form the word parts   'phantasma' wich means phantom,  and 'loqui' to speak, so it literally  means 'phantoms speak'
