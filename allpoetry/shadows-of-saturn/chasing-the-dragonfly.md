why does it seem
to see a golden blue dragonfly just
a daylight dream,
fluttering in the distance?

hazy midsummer eyes
watching the world float by
never remembering the liars and sinners
everything is just a pitiful dream
of chasing the Dragonfly

stuck in the rainbows of icebergs
the Dragonfly dies-
didn't you realize?

whispering in your ear
Dragonflies transforming into the truth
now dying in your hand watching you -
through hazy midsummer eyes
 drowning  in your lies
-
seeing how winter midnight
really can kill the innocence
of a young Dragonfly
