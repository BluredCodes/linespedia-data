inflections perceived   slept without eyes   thought you were trustable   your wounded face   then your terrible voice-   as down in the dirt you hold your wounded side   'Let me see your dreams-'   Spirits sing, swallow   of newfound freedom   -cages broken   -chaotic ripped day   -instant darkness followed   of broken stone   undertone looked away   Flashes of life-death-vitality   Like Pandora's box   soulless music slits   already pained eardrums   shifting earth lifts, falls   for switched perception kills   forgotten rose dries   By the cliff edge,   I respond   'You saw my vision,   now hide from your own'
