Seamless light blazes   gray pink winds driven endless   relentless unwinds beauty   foreign sky laughing   lighthouse dying   ancient desire warms   last hope lives   all life exists   until the last extinction   beautiful breaths mists   past lives liven   midnight rest leaves   bitter cold lives   brutally torn  as cold knives  slit brittle throats   already screamingI got really off target
