light never changes-forever hiding in a shadow of dreamsThe Curse of being visible foretellingbroken fragments of lost lightgasping for breathspinning around dead stars and splinters of hopeWeak light snubbed by Hands of DarknessAre you here to drown in your own blood?then wait in lineGrab a bookand hide in the darknessand drink the lightWhile death laughsand white surrender -dreams of never to be lifelittle young girl-turns off the lightand is not afraid of the darkwhile torn from bloody lipsare her requiems for light and nightlight dreams
