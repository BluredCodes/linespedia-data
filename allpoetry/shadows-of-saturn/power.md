Lightning shoots a million sparks   from it's home in the heavens,   lighting the way for a lone traveler   He too yearns for the lightning's   speed, strength,   and destruction.   the lightning answers   his plea, and takes his life.    a world away,   a horse as white as the lightning's  flurry,   Rears up, slashing at the skies,   for his friend,   his on and only friend,   died for power.This was the first poem I had written sense I was 7.  I didnt write for 4 1/2 years, but then I got really bored and wrote this.
