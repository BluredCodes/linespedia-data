writer: "shadows of saturn"

Which road do I take?   The unreliable   or the believable?   Do I believe the liars   should I lie to find the truth   to unbury what they hid   even if it means my own end   Do I find what they took   my last foothold   handhold   or do I sing to the wind   I find it   but it is dead   my last bird   is gone   my soul only sings requiems   forever more
