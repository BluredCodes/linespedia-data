---
title: "Breaking Moonlight"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

I can see the see specter disapear
into a silver chained box
smashed into sweet splintered dreams
by your invisable hand

drowning in my own blood
as my fingers are cracked
one by one..

upon my coffin
no burial rite was read
you threw me into the dead sea
watching the dead blank moon
with a ball and chain still attached-
to my withered starved ankles

death may lie
resting on me for a moment-
but the sun may rise
casting the shadow on you

like a Curse
you breathe dark requiems 
but still show for Sunday's mass
never thinking of that broken night
you drowned the light

fading away with the tide
the moonlight has broken
