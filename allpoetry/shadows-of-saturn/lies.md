title: "Lies"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

Gaping found forgeries 
   silent streaked pasty inclinations 
   painted prerequisite signature 
   aphsalt cries 
   cracked fishbowl 
   remembers windows searching 
   passing remains 
   months end 
   but keep custody 
   cracked thin-lens ghosts
