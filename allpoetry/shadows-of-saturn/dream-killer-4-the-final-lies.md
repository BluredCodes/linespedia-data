revolt of your trusted friends-   not so trusting   how they betrayed you, as you betrayed them   they know of the ink spilled   to hide the lies   we don't care if you die breathing   nothings all right anymore   we were deaf, but not anymore   your tracks no longer can be covered   ring of power broken   you cannot suck up for the rewards anymore   your guards are gone  and you are the only one- the only   liar,hider  but you too are gone   and all is freed   the lives you held captive   the lies you planted   the ruthless, mericless rules   gone- like you   you were always weak  just hid behind broken eyes  you will never be a Dream KillerThe Last of the dream killers.
