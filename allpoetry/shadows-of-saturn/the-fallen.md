one winged angelssitting quietly;as starlight tears run porcelain trailsWhy do they Cry?Who is dying?but we only sighas deep blue engulfs usand carries us with the tide...Orphans lay in the street dyingCancer patients die is paper gownsAll they have in common theDreams never dreamteyes never wetforever gone, forever herecarried out to unknown gravesBut the angels still cryWhat is wrong nowon the heavenly serene?When will the last fallen angelmeet its cold doomsoon they saysoon the new will beginand another will fallCold foreverthe last forsaken will falland the angels will dieand never cry againthe fallen livenever underestimatedalways alivealways deadvague!!!!
