forgotten stars can never laugh 
for nothing change floating clouds or 
strife of dying sun that 
sees only life elsewhere 
no pain, no will, left in our time  
until the  music silences 

your bird dies -
space, time, being stop 
no realizations held 
unless you listen to the
songs sung in torn note 
and thin fingers broken 

useless little sounds 
of no one 
just a little visit 
from the other side
wishing to press through
