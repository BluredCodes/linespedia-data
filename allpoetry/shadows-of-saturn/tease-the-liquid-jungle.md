title: "Tease the Liquid Jungle"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

Tease the liquid jungle    heavy factor pressing encroachment    suffocating while vines thicken    reality apparently thickest    Although delicious trepidation    The face just buzzes    Wings pressing soft    strengthened the shade on the window    of the soul    Although air suffocates imitation of the souls...    teasing the liquid jungle is fatal to all    dreamers and real    for the jungle of our soul is all
