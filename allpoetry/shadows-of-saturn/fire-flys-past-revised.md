Our Half remembered song
of sad teardrops never aging is 
clenched in the jaws of our past
 
we were a free dolphin
basking in a dreamless haze-
but the dolphin has been caught in a suffocating net
as darkening shrouds drown our memories 
are love was two ghosts praying remembering 
my siren cries echoing 
the cloud's somber song 

 Somewhere  light swirled black 
our days turned dark- 

the loveless Moon bright but Dim 
sour rain etched in moonlight 
the fog ends, swollowed by the wind 
all that is left is our 
mind's eye burning
and the firefly burned by the acid rainThis one is also a revision.  the older version was Past, and I think I improved it:)
