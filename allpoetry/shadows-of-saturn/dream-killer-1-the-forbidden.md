idle midnight stillness   would screamed none   silence shattered   thousand languages sea   speak cobra songs tepid   sharp pain   wordless fears-   images haunting   safe refuge disturbed   by ghosts and horrors   known no end   awake or daze nor sleep   'Honestly', the dream killer leers,   'How were their dreams ?'
