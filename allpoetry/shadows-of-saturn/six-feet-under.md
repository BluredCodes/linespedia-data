---
title: "Six Feet Under"
writer: "shadows of saturn"
slug: "six-feet-under"

Why do you hide Like the moon, your so rash keeping anyone from noticing you and throwing you out with the trash I don't want to be Miss. Clingy but your getting kinda shady why are you fading? Because soon you'll be six feet under in the hole you dug I don't want to mislead you but the stakes are getting higher I'm heading for destruction cause you threw me on the fast lane... Oh I can't help it only you can Because soon you'll be six feet under in the hole you dug Stuck in doubt cuz' you'll be dead and gone so what the grass is always  greener on the other side repeat in to falling in deep blue remembering that you never flew Flirt with interference Because your halo is shattered on the floor pick it up and you bleed but you don't have any reflection Because soon you'll be six feet under in the hole you dug your faking it all and it only leads to your fall and there is no new Stuck in doubt cuz' you'll be dead and gone Yeah and you won't live forever Fairy tales always end six feet under
