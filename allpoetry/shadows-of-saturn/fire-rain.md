title: "Fire Rain"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

Yellowed butterfly wing    heavenward direction downward    leaves witness only instinct    change going down    shedding scaly skin    came unanimous windless warm    silent rain world    Conflicting dry-world-spell butterfly    How can you watch the world    float out of grasp    stretching confusion of alien beat    bending unsolid roads    into liquid soil    something vanishing daily    ungodly halo    laying    shattered    distraught    on the cold tiled floor    show you    fire rain    burning holes into you    why does    fire tame fire?    own fire    broken halo on the tiled    blood red floor
