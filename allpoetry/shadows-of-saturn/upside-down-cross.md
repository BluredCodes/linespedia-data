like a desperate vampire
you drain the life out of windless butterflies-

a fortelling shadow was Cast 
above in lonly heaven 
      
he was merely the fourth child
who was a 
messenger to all the 
wronged angel creatures and he said fearfully
that good will bring the fall 
vengelful of those below who are 
only dead living admist whose visions? 
who shun and hide their upside down crosses 

the wronged creatures just think they are Untouchable
unforgivable but that
angel bears
ironic burnt eyes 
of forgiveness 

so maybe you are not an angel
all  you do is drain butterflies' breaths
and use their torn wings
