Why does the past haunt me?    Where here I devout to change it, mask it,    where past left me behind.    Mind plays endless games    I am amused they do not drown themselves    Images altogether dreamy    like an enchanted slide show    of death and destruction    but stop they do not know    remembered sorrows never relived    pitiless    forsaken dreams    relentless fear    solicitous tests    while present dies    So here my mind will rot    as my crossbow of my gun    is laid to rest    my past is relevant    but not that carefulWhat do you think of the last line? I don't like it
