title: "The last Act"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

your life is just a play-
full of staged tears 
faked greif 

so now what can you do-
now that the last act has begun?  
how can you be pretender, 
so long 
that you don't know reality? 
you relize the actual you 
is just dust in your looking glass 
seeping away with time... 
now out of grasp floating to the ocean 
  
you are stuck in a 
unknown mask 
that cannot be broken 
and become a faceless phantom, 
always dying again in this 
already written play
watching the curtain fall
