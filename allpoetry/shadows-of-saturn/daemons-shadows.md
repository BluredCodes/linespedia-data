How can you embellish on such Evil?unbroken tethers unmagicalWhen you are born-to turn the other cheekand stare at the ceiling not the skyand watch your kindsoar out into the worldfreewhen you have been locked insidethis liquid chain box,for eternity and a daywhen the day the box shrunk to suffocationember eyes never to see againas once light blue cloudsturn a solemn blacknever to see the sun againand the blind falcon diesalive in a sense againto see the rainbowand soar without invisible tethersand to see the starlight again
