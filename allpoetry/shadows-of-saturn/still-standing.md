Sitting outside one daywishing I could go back againand not care about how the cards layI just wanted something fundon't know how we fellso now the birds are goneare they dead?so I burned acid into your eyesso you want to hate meso restrictions are keeping you downso I'm not sorryunlike like you I'm not fallingbecause I am not movingswollow it down Its not so mellowfreedom not so freenow its all yellowtounge in cheekmulticolor personaltydeep bluefiery redI don't fit inso I won't cryso the swirl is brownso you are forgetfulso it does't matterunlike like you (I'm different)I'm not falling(My dreams are still alive)because I am not movingNothing Mattersredo button is brokenrainy day isin'tsunny todayunlike like you (I'm different)I'm not falling(My dreams are still alive)because I am not movingI am not movingCan't you tell this is a song for my band?
