I never relised
I dream in a black and white horror picture show
watching ghastly hands
click the little buttons like flicking on emotions
waiting for a glimmer of human feeling
like a God you can take my life 
and like a Servant you can help me
so who are you today?

hazy dreams of a wanted life slip away
all I am left is a hollow space
left me wanting some
trying to fill that hole-
that you carved from my brusied heart
with a dull knife of vengence from your past

you say I may dream in black and white
but may I cry and mumble in my dreams
never leaving the negotating table
trying to run away and cut the film
seperating the world from my dreams?

today I dream in color
