title: "Black Doves"

sadistic midnight dreams of betrayed companion-

the one you loved and cared for

now crying out in sorrow

for you only doomed him here

and now all you can do is watch, and listen

to the black dove die



horror has not mercy for you

you lowly excuse for a soul

they love to watch you fall and stumble

beg for compasion when you have shown none

 a harpy whispers loudly,

"Liar, liar, liar. Liar!"

 you just stab another whole into the earth

and laugh



angels shimer in the distance holding out their hands,

but dreams they have none

becuase of a fabled soul

who would fall to temptation-

and bring a great fall of the Light



absorbing all hate to drive another rainbow-

to bend it into darkness

and filter one's own eyes

into mush and alter the lines 

of a million ghosts

all to watch the river dry up-

and you're boat to death crash

as you snap the wings of a black dovebah humbug
