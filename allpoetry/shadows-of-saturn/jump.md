a motherless childsits by the edgeof the forgotten bridgewaitingshe is sadher heart a dark blue poisoned by being truly aloneshe is to meet her fatherBut she never willthe night is full of whispersstrangers watch ready to burn hersoon she watches her own fatherjumpGirl you cant jumpeven if your eyes are swollen shutpick up no hold up run away you gotta runand fend for yourselflive has a sharp edgeeven if it has no thrillthere is no mellow yellowonly black and whiteshe met Sally that dayshe only had one thing to say'Only liars' legs sway'your luck ran dry oh wellGirl you cant jumpeven if your eyes are swollen shutpick up no hold up run away you gotta runand fend for yourselfYou gotta flyat least your not blind, for you can seeat least four not dead, for you still fleeGirl you cant jumpeven if your eyes are swollen shutpick up no hold up run away you gotta runand fend for yourselfYou gotta flyand free yourself
