I've been crying for years;
just to hear you breathe
shatters time and space
and puts worries to sleep

why don't all the birds sing
when a new world brings,
a broken highness of forlorness?
all they do is fall in too deep

like a field of tiger pits the world is sucked right in
dreaming of a perfect timely haze
I wish I could scream as the night turns black
swollowing whole like a lost fly trap

I've been crying for years...
as suddenly it all turns black
and again no one belives that 
dreams do not always live

and all they do is dream as the night turns to day
and they don't know what
 to do when it all falls
so I known all the time
so you didn't listen or care
but what will help you now?

Just to here you breathe
makes me want to crack
so take you're empty lies
to someone who gives a damn

I guess you will be blind again
and I think it is time
to turn on the lightas you can tell I got REAALLY mad today...
