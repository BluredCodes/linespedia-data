title: "Lolly Pops"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

she  is so lonely,locked in with your dreamsas they cry and smile in the darkshe can do nothing but stare at themas their revolution breaksshattering childhood night lightwhile you sleep and daze in dreams,she crysand phantoms dying,lift her into undying eviland she licks her lolly popsbathing in dirty dustsucking on the sweets,of red tears of nightmares
