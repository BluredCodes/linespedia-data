---
title: "Bliss"
writer: "shadows of saturn"
slug: "bliss"
source: "AllPoetry"

happy yesturday's memories fading   but today hopelessness drowning   internal magic found   sadness of life grey   blurring lines as tears don't come   sticky drops of bliss   transform drab life into   the surreal brightness   invention shared with all   love-hope-happiness   blinding-   as the sadness is still there   still some suffer as they   have not the luxury of Bliss   take off the so called bliss   and internal magic is gone   the sticky magic-   and the world is still 10 shades of gray   but the glimmer of hope dulls-   no longer able to shine through the gray
