---
title: "Darkness Falls"
writer: "shadows of saturn"
slug: "darkness-falls"

Forbidden secrets told   Liars, friends, reminense among   lime-lit shadows   sleepless rustlings   hushed whispers   enemies conquered   killed with our   happiness   Flashes of embarrassment   red colored memories   scorching metal   fire lake   Creeping, rustlings   giggling wars   Half forgotten past lives   only present   conceited spirit   names useless   so called   innocent darkness   darkness fallsThis is a thing I wrote at camp describing a little war between to cabins
