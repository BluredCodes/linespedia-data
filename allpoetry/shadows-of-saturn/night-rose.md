a night rose blooms,cold and bluea silent plea in the windbreaks when rose thornpricks innocent handsand again the forsaken wolf howls,for his friend is now lostforeverMoon rose droppedher last sigh breathedand she fallsfrozen blood drippingfrom staring into eyesof deathstained frozen soulforever waitsto thaw and rise againto pierce more innocent heartswith the thornsof the night rose'shate for the lukewarm
