we never speak-
hoping to keep our fragmented selves together
our silence has become golden

but how can
Unexpected enmity overcome 
solely morbid love? 
You wear a hot tragedy crown that   
lights the sky 
lucid allure absorb tainted love that
ringes like bells keeping
desire alive 

our seven shades of happy memories: 
ushering whispers 
holding broken love tight 
our listless hearts stiring 
and a million unbelievable sights seen 
we are a undying journey. 
how we held our love like a dead butterfly 
never wanting our story to end-
seeking desperate confront 

Although our truth is deceiving 
it will be found 
I'm not perfect-I falter too
for now we are together but alone
and this dead butterfly
has to flyThis is a completly revised edition of my earlier poem (I am in a revising streak :) ) and this is the only love poem I have ever written- and the first one of this was worse
