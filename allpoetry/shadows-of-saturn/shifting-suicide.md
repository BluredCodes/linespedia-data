creatures eyeing fallen soul  forever stealing what is not theirs  innocence is sweet  plexiglass faltering strength   pressing glassy eyed   sand eyes blue marbles   apathetic differences   taste blood of suicidal threats   salt of million tears   embedded in strength   doubt cool hand   hesitance held strong   carts away   defiance, conscience   like the downed dog   that was hit merciless  on the cold highway    who will be the one to see  the last one die?  surely none of mortal  only cold blood  will satisfy  the fallen angelsAhh! its scary.  First and last stanzas are shifted...
