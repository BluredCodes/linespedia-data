distressed fortelling Cast   above lonely heaven   merely the fourth child   messenger of all   created angel creature   will bring the fall   vengeful below   only dead admist whose visions   how can one be so Unforgivable?   to take one so young   hide the upside down cross   fathom all of no height   ironic burnt eyes   of forgiveness
