title: "Alone"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

You can never know 
   when the sky will fall 
   Everyone you've ever known 
   wrenched from you 
   then you would be truly alone. 
   Would life be real 
   or a horrible labyrinth 
   You would have the world to 
   yourself:all the riches and treasures 
   But would it be worth it 
   if there was no one to share it with? 
   You would be stranded on a solitary island, but this one there 
   is no escape 
   for nothing in the world 
   is worth the companionship 
   of a true friend
