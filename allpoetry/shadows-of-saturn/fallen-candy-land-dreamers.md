title: "Fallen Candy Land Dreamers"

feel-good bumble bee   frightens star crossed child   sacred sweet gumdrops   tragically empty-faced phantoms   hands key   reaches out forever   sweet gumdrops....   fall into unforgivable water   like the moon on this bloodshed night-  fallen from grace
