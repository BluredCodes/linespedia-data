"if I could break time"
she said, only a mortal gasping for breath
"forever would be a blink in time
and dreams would never die

But if time could break
what is life, besides
a short span of wandering
into the darkness searching for a light"

"a far cry," death repsonded,
"from the way you know life is,
just angel tears od pity
for you are only a mortal

so cry for your short life
and fail to live
with the fiery, immortal
dreams of the fallen

where all sorrows are gone
and angels shun at the sight
but who wouldn't give all possesions
to live with the undead?"

"but the angels cry of sorrow"
the angel sighs,
"for only dreams are immortal
living in the light
and nightmares running in the darkness

but death remains free
like a dreaded gift of Pandora
threatening and buying souls
so throw down you're swords
and fly away from here
gliding in the moonlight"
