---
title: "And Liberty to All......"
writer: "shadows of saturn"
slug: "and-liberty-to-all"
source: "AllPoetry"

cold blood spilled   out of spite and hate   falling horror of metal and cement   raining from the heavens   of something ungod like   grim frightening millions of stories   repeating as death settles   like the cloud of grit   Its like a dream   because its like a bad movie   when the bombs blow up in your face   and you'll never win the race   lives cut short   always forever alone   just part of a deadly game   thousands of souls cast into the oblivion   threatens liberty, freedom   as spiteful words thrown horribly like fire   pull bodies that are nothing but dolls   Its like a dream   because its like a bad movie   when the bombs blow up in your face   and you'll never win the race   Its like a dream isn't it?   because it floats by you   and all you do is sigh   and say, 'I'm glad I didn't die'   But that stab to your side is real   now, do you feel?
