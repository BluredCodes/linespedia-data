the frozen are soul's ungodly   clearer red blood   Faltering sparks breaths   shatter porcelain sky   the Clasped snow white breeze   Bitter frozen sighs   of my last bird's song   Heart beats like drums   last thoughts forever caught   repeating into the darkness with no one to hear   searching for a rest   All souls are drawn into ungodly lighthmmm...
