title: "Past"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

Half remembered song   teardrops aging never   dolphin's fate hazy   darkening shrouds memories   nothingness ghosts praying   siren's cries echoing   the c cloud's songs somber   Somewhere deepening light   days swirl dark   Moon bright Dim   rain etched in moonlight   Wind's fog ends   bare twilight   mind eye burning
