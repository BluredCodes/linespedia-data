title: "Cutting Angel throats"

I watched strangers cut holes
into dead glittering stars
like a dog eating the sun
they chortle and sneer

why can't I find it
I can't hide it any longer
why can you drown the light 
and still rip bats' wings 
into bloddy shreds?

Into dead and glittering stars-
flew the million souls
repeating their last words
and seeing them stuck in your
hearts spider webs

little black and blue roses 
die when you come near
but all I can hear
is the crying of forsaken angels-
their poor throats full of song splitting-
hearts of glass smashing in your handsAll hail Blondie!!!!
