title: "The Specter Knife"

red forgotten skies
of a never known time
are parted by a single soul
that rises above the rest
reality couldn't of been more wrong
as the drowned weep for their pittful souls

that single souls sang
a song long worth listening for
that found a way 
to break through the barrier
and stall the secret of hapiness

but little did that lone tiger know
that nothing could stop the 
breaking apart of the world
untill the specters  formed-
with dead hollow eyes shining like dead stars
in black and white lies
and fell flat as winds tore through the
barrier of reality and fantasy

dreamy eyes forever await 
to wake and see real sights
as a bloddied hand of-
a million spectors once again
slash the sky with the blade
of a dreamless nightI dunno what happened- it kinda went down the tubes
