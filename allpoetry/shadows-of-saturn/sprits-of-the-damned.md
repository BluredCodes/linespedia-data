damned souls find peace inmidnight seance of frozen handsas death circles the innocent and frees the guiltyghostly figures play in hidden starlightand the alive just stareat the black nightand wanderin frigid nightsyoung damned soulsdrown in tidal waveof evil light and are ungodly once againwho are they?they are the bringers of deaththat appear in black capes,and come knocking on your door,only to show their faces,and eat innocent souls starlight never diesin starlight, the damnedspeak to the mortaland feast again
