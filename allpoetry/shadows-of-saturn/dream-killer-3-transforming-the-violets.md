perfect isn't good enough for you   anymore   just sit and wait   as the glass splinters change colors   gold-silver-blue   hands clutching silence   little brown box full   of pride   on the outside   you are the same as us   on the inside you are the same as them   useless questions   small delicate leaves   ripped in madnes   drowned in pond of lies   modest childlikeness lost   blink and its lost   all the lives   you built on lies   why remeber   the little violets   in garden of your memories
