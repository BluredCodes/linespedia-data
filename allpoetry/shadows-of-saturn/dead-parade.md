---
title: "Dead Parade"
writer: "shadows of saturn"

I'm stuck in this dead parade
watching trasparent souls pay their
dues; signing papers
but why are they just random words
they must be the bitter last thoughts
ot the forsaken

floating elephants and ghoulish clowns
carry me away
didnt you hear they murmer
all thats good is gone

sudden my gravestone is staring up at me
no birds can sing and no sun shines
hungry vultures sit in the trees and I 
watch for a mourner or two as they bury me
stabbing their shovels into the ground
but really stabbing me
why is no one sad?
no one placing black
and white photos of loved ones in my stiff hands
or placing little frozen flowers relising that I will be far colder?

a specter put a hand on my shoulder and whispers
all thats good is dead
and nobody loves you when you're gone
