I came to you
my heart in hand,
a cloud above my head.

you looked at me
most thoughtfully
and parted lips, 
and said,

"I loved you once,
and once again,
and now the magic's dead.
Now go away
and stay away."
and so I did,
and quickly died.I'm not sure about some of the words. maybe the second "now" should be "so," and maybe "and now" should be "but now." can't decide.
