title: "guerilla sunrise"
writer: "katraylor"
slug: "guerilla-sunrise"

my dreamship sailor, won't you come away with me?
i know that soon the tide of war will rend eternity
and if you stay behind, my love, 
as sure as i can say,
i'll never find your body
when i come back in the Day.

can't you see and hear it, deep behind my eyes,
the sharp, dead twanging of aluminum skies? 
(we follow, we follow, we follow
watch out, for the footbridge is hollow)
"did you think to check, in the clamor of dawn
if the little granite sculpture had become the fawn?"
i tell you now, my moonlit dream, i must go on alone
i leave you by the woodland path
a wish of yesterday
and hope that one day, some day soon
you'll find the words to say. 

i am not afraid.
(you might ask the mirror 
if the leading edge of soldiers
didn't come through here.)
my ivory-tower existence would be fitting for a prince.
but you have gone away and i've been lonely ever since.
come back, my little sundrop!
come back, my precious pearl
we both shall soon regret it
if you choose the other world.one of my take-what-comes poems... just now wrote it... yes. well, then.
