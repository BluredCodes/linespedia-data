Mister Fish,
Don't you wish 
That you weren't lying in a dish?
Your graceful tail
And sequin scales
Are dirtying my fingernails.
I hover with the silver knife
And wonder where to make the slice;
For if I botch this lab, my friend,
I can't go back and try again.
A new dissection specimen
Would drain the school board's penny-tin.
And so we must conserve our fish,
And clams, and frogs
(Watch out- they squish).
In smelly hell we work, and wish
This awful year were over with.Spring 2001, Goofy little poem composed after doing a fish dissection in AP Bio last year. Yeah, that wasn't obvious... ^^ I've always sort of liked it, but I think the focus isn't very good- going from the fish to the school board to the school.
