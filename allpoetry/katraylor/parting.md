---
title: "Parting"
writer: "katraylor"
slug: "parting"
source: "AllPoetry"

i die in pieces, filling out the void
with laughter's tearful breath
and knowing you are watching me, i take
sharp pleasure in my death.

your hand is there, a warmth upon my chest
that life no longer gives,
and were it not too late, we'd share
such words as they who love, and live.

but now the time is thinning out,
and darkness billows in.
too soon the end is here, and you
are left with tears and empty skin.

too late to grasp how serious
the mortal world can be,
we fly apart, a flock of sighs
in bleak eternity.I never thought I was morbid. Guess it's my subconscious.
