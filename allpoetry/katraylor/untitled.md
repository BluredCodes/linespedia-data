hold me closer,
ever closer, 
till our heartstrings knot and blend
in the quickening of the moonlight
feel the touch of silver hands.
first the touching,
then the tasting 
of thy perfect lips in mine.
canst thou come a little closer?
can we share this kiss divine?Winter 2001, Actually not connected to anyone... I composed it while thinking out a love story in my head.
