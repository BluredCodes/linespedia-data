Wren, Wren, come again
out to the barley field
and i'll be waiting in the gloom
my breath suspended, heartbeat held
in check by promise of your eyes
that full-illustrious diamonds seem
in dark of twilight, shadows flee
and marvel at you from afar.Just another little "whatever" written in the gap before Principals class. I don't really like it, but as I said, whatever...
