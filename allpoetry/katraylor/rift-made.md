title: "Rift made."
writer: "katraylor"

anger it is, and cold and spare
with no room for friends
or making amends
on this plain of unbreakable ice.

could you but know the love i had
reserved in my being for you
i think you'd have waited
and talked to me first 
before building a wall
of gold 
and steel
and covering all of the spaces with snow.

don't speak of it now- 
you know it's too late, 
we've both left the business behind.
i think that, if one or two words had been changed,
the solid of time might have been rearranged
and we still could be two of a kind.

i'm sorry, you know.
the blame is with me,
and its splinter remains in my soul.
just walk away quickly, and don't say a word,
or the silence will choke me, i'll crumple, i'll fall
into grief's ever-widening hole.You guys, I'm sad... I did something that really hurt a good friend of mine, and I'm not sure things are going to get better.
