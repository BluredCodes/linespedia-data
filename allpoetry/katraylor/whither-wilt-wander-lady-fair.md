Whither wilt wander, lady fair?
The night is long, and damp the air.
How will you find the darkened trail
That leads you to the end?

   I had a life within the stream,
   A bright and miserable place
   When they removed the amber wall
   I found that I had been misplaced.
 
   The king, he came, amid the throng
   A diamond sparkling on his ring
   To kill the rash usurper-lass 
   Who'd dared to dismantle the story.
