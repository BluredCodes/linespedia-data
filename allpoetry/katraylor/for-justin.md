you make me feel beautiful
desirable
unparalleled
i'm a queen in your arms.

how did you know
how to touch me,
how to kiss me? 
i never knew that anyone
could love me
as you've shown you love me.

i want to hold you,
forever,
to stay within your safe embrace,
my head upon your shoulder,
your lips upon my hair.

come hold me
and be with me
show me how to love you,
show me how to be the queen
you tell me that i am.Gah- another one! I'd forgotten about this one. Well... as I said, the sentiment was there when I wrote it... maybe I'm just a little disgusted with romance at the moment.
