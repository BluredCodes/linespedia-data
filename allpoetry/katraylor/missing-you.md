title: "Missing you"

I lie awake
and imagine you lying by me,
your warm arms wrapped
 around my body.
Maybe, nestled in your
loving, soft embrace,
I could sleep in peace.

I miss you. 
It's been days since I've
  heard your voice,
  felt your touch,
  seen your message on the screen.
I never minded being lonely,
  before you.
If you were here,
I couldn't be lonely,
not in the bliss
of your embrace.

Come to me
across the miles.
If I call you,
Cloud Dragon,
will you fly to my side?
I want you
to enfold me in your wings.Found it! This poem I wrote for the first guy I was really serious about... funny how feelings change, isn't it? *grimace* I very rarely write love poetry, and this is why- I think there's probably a law against using the word "embrace" twice in the space of two stanzas! However, the sentiment expressed was at the time very strong, and I hope some of that speaks here.
