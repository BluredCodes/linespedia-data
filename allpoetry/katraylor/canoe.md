the flood of ripples attaches, then trails away from my
oar, firm against flowing resistance
beneath me. in the canoe's motion
i feel the world fly by,
smoothly and peacefully.

the water is green and glowing,
shimmered liquid jade.
damselfly- perfect jewelled sprite-
hovers on air and is gone.

we stop and feel the earth rotating slowly
around the axis of this little boat,
cool, muddy water sloshing
across bare feet.

when and how was the sky so blue?
i think i'll
paddle beyond the reach of time.Exercise in English class last year- "emotion remembered in tranquility," or something like that. i'm sure certain members of the community will now take it upon themselves to be offended on behalf of the canoe. Whatever.
