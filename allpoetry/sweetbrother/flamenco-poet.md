title: "Flamenco Poet"
writer: "sweetbrother"

It seems unfair
that so much talent is spent
on the restless and young
I sigh,
then relax, and enjoy
the spectacle
of watching her dance all over a page

She is in love, it seems
and in her supple grace
her motions
paint a vibrant portrait
of the one
who inspires her

I watch, from a seat high above her stage
bewildered by her pulsating colors
a skirt of gold and green and cream
floats softly above her bare feet
her head is thrown back, her eyes closed
her face is a mask of joy

I look on, longing to catch her emotion
for a moment

It seems unfair
that I can only watch
the lovely tempest of her dance
from a distance
