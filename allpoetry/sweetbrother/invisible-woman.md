---
title: "Invisible Woman"
writer: "sweetbrother"
slug: "invisible-woman"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I see her only
as words on a screen
and in that guise
she is sultry, and mesmerizing

She invites me to ride with her
on the unfettered mustangs
of her imagination
through the fields and forests of her dreams
to a secret garden she's created
to serve as our playground

She's captivated
by words on a screen
that come from me-
the only part of me she's known-
and now she seeks to find
all the hidden parts of me

In dreams shared with me
she places me on her back porch
sharing glasses of sweet tea
or among her silken sheets
sharing reckless passion

She might recoil
at the harsh reality
of where I spend my nights
but in the gauzy world we share
reality's not needed or desired

Our love is shared
through those words on a screen
and in the place where we dance together
we are content, if our faces remain unseen
and I can drift away in sleep
on those chilly nights
still feeling the heat of the kisses
bestowed on me
by an invisible woman
