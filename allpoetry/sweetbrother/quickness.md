title: "Quickness"
writer: "sweetbrother"
slug: "quickness"
source: "AllPoetry"

She is a festival
for my mind and eyes;
I drink each drop of her
satisfied, but wanting more

I struggle
to keep up with the quickness of her
and the quickness of my pulse around her;
she doesn't know, she is laid back,
relaxed in her delight;
she doesn't know, the quickness of her smile,
leaves me dumb and blind

After just nine days' acquaintance
she wakes the muse,
she gives me poems
written as I imagine
a barefoot walk
across her moist earth
my motives pure for now,
I struggle with the quickness
of what she does to me.
