title: "Walking Home (Black Shadow)"

Under a silver moon
the red dress girl
wobbled, unsteady on stiletto heels
and giggled, "I'm so drunk tonight!"

Her boyfriend glanced around,
and whispered, "Hush!"
as the inky shadow of my body
lapped at their feet.

Like them, I was only walking home.
