---
title: "Bridges"
writer: "sweetbrother"
slug: "bridges"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Carefully
i poured the fluid
recklessly
i lit the match
brutally
i fanned the flames
but my bridges
would not stay burned

voices call to me
i try to stop my ears
but loving words
still reach me

those who call to me
don't see the visions
that torment me

poets die each day
and the dry and empty shells
they leave behind
walk on forever
as zombies in a desert

and i crouch
in my shell of silence
praying for the voices to go away
still they call me
while my muse roasts
on an open flame
being prepared
for my last meal
