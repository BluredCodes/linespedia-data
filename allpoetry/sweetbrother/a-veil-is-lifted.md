a veil is lifted

and a face turns away in shame

he was a dream to her,
but hidden in shadow

with tender words
softly spoken,
and just within reach of his ears

she gave him gifts

hope

courage

trust

and the strength 
to step into the light

when she saw the face behind the dream,
she gave him only

silence.

he gathered up his gifts:

trust

courage 

hope

and, with a slow and measured gait
trudged back into the shadows.
