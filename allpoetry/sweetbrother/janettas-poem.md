She was
my butterscotch flavored dream
until she dreamt of me

Then, I was her target
for a river of abuse
and I took it
just so I could be close to her

And now I see her,
randomly on a subway train
sleekly curved
even more seductive
than the wanton child
that once drowned me in her sweetness

I have an urge to speak to her
and a desire for her touch
but no, today,
another man
feels her tenderness
and the hurt she brings

I remember
being cut with blades
that poured from her soft lips
and I had no way to stop my bleeding

But even now
sitting next to her in that subway car
I have an urge to touch
butterscotch skin
but I hold back
for fear of being burned
