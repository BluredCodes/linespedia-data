title: "Earth Mother (First Impression)"

Earth mother
in cut-off jeans
leaves me startled
at her sleekness

I am in her house,
among her friends
and children gathered
awaiting stories

She is in my life
suddenly, as a flash of blazing sun
to pierce my winter

in the shelter of her home
we sit in comfort
exchanging history

Years and miles and generations
are connected as minutes of conversation
erase the distance between us

I humbly offer, as gifts,
pieces of my life
encased in glitter

She returns sweet words of praise
and never before
have I felt so honored
by an audience

Earth mother.
my brother's daughter
changes my point of view
with deftly chosen words
and leaves me startled
at her wisdom
