---
title: "Night Trembler"
writer: "sweetbrother"

She wakes
in still, wee hours
in an ancient house that groans
in restless sleep

Just outside her window
she hears the low sound
of a breeze
wrestling with the boughs of trees
and nuzzling the surface
of her windowpanes

In nights past
these summer zephyrs
were cool comforts
were innocent movements of the air

But a change has come,
and to her,
this breeze has eyes and ears
and is seeking her

This breeze might have come to her
to avenge crimes
remotely imagined,
and unconnected with her life.
She's lived a life
of only love and compassion
but still, she's learned
that through the senseless logic
of random hate
that even she 
can be a target

Her heart beats a desperate, frantic rhythm
as her mind searches
for a path her feet might use to flee
but that night breeze
has a thousand eyes
and a million legs
to pursue her

The only course her terror has
is to mutely await her fate
and if she survives
this malignant wind
her shaken soul will be left to ponder
who will avenge
her murdered innocence
