---
title: "Broken Promises"
writer: "krissida"

another broken promise, 
how many more will it take
for you to realize youre breaking me in two.
you promise me this,
you promise me that.
but something always comes up,
something always gets in the way.
sweetheart, i love you, 
you say.
sometimes i have to wonder,
do you really mean it.
it seems like youll avoid me,
no matter what it takes.
i gave you my love,
i gave you myself.
wasnt that enough.
cant you see youre tearing me apart.
ive lost count of how many nights
ive cried myself to sleep. 
i can recall the countless nights
where i lie awake in bed,
wondering what i did wrong.
where are you when i need you.
you promise me this,
you promise me that.
how many more will it take
for you to realize youre breaking me in two.
