title: "Don't Fucking Tempt Me"
writer: "krissida"

one step closer to the edge,
i wonder when ill fall.
always pushing,
always shoving.
dont you know how close i am.
one little joke,
one little tease.
its nothing really..
to you, that is.
ive had so much shit in my life
that maybe that one little joke
could push me over.
youd never do it, they say.
i keep saying,
dont fucking tempt me.

..falling..

we never realized how upset she was.
who wouldve thought.
such a bright girl, 
such a pretty girl.
who knew the anguish she held inside.

didnt you listen.
didnt you pay attention.
how many fucking times did i have to tell you.
dont fucking tempt me.
