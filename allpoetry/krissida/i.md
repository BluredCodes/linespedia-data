---
title: "I.."
writer: "krissida"
slug: "i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3519-I..-by-krissida"
---

01. I hurt: inside.
02. I love: Tony.
03. I hate: people.
04. I cry: all the time.
05. I fear: myself.
06. I hope: I make it out alive.
07. I sadden: everyone around me.
08. I feel alone: all too often.
09. I kill: myself a little more each day.
10. I talk: too much.
11. I listen: very little.
12. I break: my world.
13. I see: darkness ahead.
14. I smell: nightime.
15. I taste: death.
16. I work: alone.
17. I remember: nothing.
18. I hold: your soul.
19. I hide: from you.
20. I pray: to some non-existent deity.
21. I walk: through your heart.
22. I drive: nowhere.
23. I read: between the lines.
24. I burn: when I see them.
25. I breathe: heavy.
26. I play: with fire.
