---
title: "Mirror"
writer: "krissida"
slug: "mirror"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i look in the mirror
i see myself stare back
my eyes so cold and hard
hardened by stress
and love and life
i wonder how it got that way
what happened to me
i used to be so happy
a glowing little girl
with so much promise
and such a bright future
and now i see myself
im falling apart
sometimes i cant take it
i cant even get out of bed
there isnt any point
why should i subject myself
to such pain
i know its inevitable
i have to face it eventually
i suppose
but i cant help but wonder
where it all went wrong
and if there is any going back
