why must the sun shine and blind my eyeswhy must the rain fall and leave me capsizedwhy do we try, try so hard but fail,only to fall down into the same trap againwe try to crawl back up, only to get pushed back down never to reach the top why does my heart achewhy does it have all this hurt insidemaybe because i trusted you,and you took that trust awaythrew it away, never to be found again,why do i search for the truth, when it is never there to be foundwhy does my heart fall like rainonly to be rediscover and come back againthis is about falling in love and it being the wrong person, but not wanting to admit/accept it.
