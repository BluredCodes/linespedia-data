title: "how i feel.....for someone special"

gratefulness is how i act
when i talk to you

happiness is what i show 
when i hear your voice

full of life is what i am
from your caring words

tenderness is what i imagine
from your gentle touch

love is what i have in my heart
from the way you have treated me

special is what i feel
because i was so lucky to of met you.I wrote this for someone who is truly special, i think he is the most amazing person..but he doesn't know i think this!
