My head is throbbingmy eyes are stingingwhy are these tears on my face clingingwhy does my family hate mewhy do they make me crywhy oh why do i want to diemy family has changed,for the worstnow i know why i burst,burst into tears for what has happened,burst into tears for what i fear worst will happen.This was written at a time when i felt like i didn't deserve what i was going through and felt like i was being punished.
