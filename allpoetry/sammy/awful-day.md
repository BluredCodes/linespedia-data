As I sit here on this awful day,

i wander why i will have to pay

pay for the price 

of watching my family...fall apart,

pay for the price my family has repeated

repeated over and over

only to choose to ignore,

and watching everyone fall to pieces

i wander why i have to observe these pictures

pictures of hate, pictures of depression,

those pictures will never be forgotten.I wrote this at a time where we were having many family arguments!
