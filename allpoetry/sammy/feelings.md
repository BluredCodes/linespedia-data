Why must the leaves fall to the ground?Just like my tears drift slowly, never to be foundWhy does the wind blow, so gustly through the air?In the same way as my long hairWhy does the ocean breeze cry all day long?When i can scream at the top of my lungsWhy does the rain fallm crashing to the ground?Just like when i am running, never wanting to be boundWhy do the trees crash and make that horrible sound>Louder and more terrifying than ever being foundWhy does the moon cry out for help?Just like i do wheni feel like i cannot copeWhy do these white treasures keep falling from the heavens?When my loneliness is  cursing me form what has happened.Why do these feelings occur,I will never know.Al i can do is try to comprehend.I wrote this last night. i was falling asleep and herd a gust of wind, then this poem started running through my head!hope u all enjoyed it:)
