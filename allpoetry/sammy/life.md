Life is like a dead end road

u keep on going down it,

only to find out you have to turn around

turn around and face it all again

turn around and try to hide all your anger and pain

the road can sometimes be long

the road can also be very wrong

it is hard to turn around and face all that again

the end of the road can be very long

but i fear i am not wrongThis was written at a time where my family;s problems were repeating and only getting worse.
