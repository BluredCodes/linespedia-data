~~~~~~~~I~~~~~~~                 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~But~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Haven’t been everywhere...........I‘ve been around.
Haven’t heard it all....................Listen to every sound.
Haven’t seen the world..............Look at every sight twice.
Haven’t touched but a few.........Each was a heaven’s slice.
Haven’t tasted all of life.............Savored all I could.
Haven’t learned everything........Often more than I should.
Have sensed all that I could.......Not enough to shock my sensibilities.Just something I am toying with....Any suggestions??? Like forget the whole thing? LOL
