title: "Just Once (By: Summer Dreams)"

I can feel this emotion, it takes my breath away.
Makes my body run with passion, then you go away..
My heart feels empty, longing for your words.
The want of your passion, living for every word.
It hungers for words not spoken, words it shall never know.
The want of a soft touch, that makes you want to glow.

The tenderness of your finger tips.
The feel of your breath against my skin.
The passion after the after glow.
The smile on your face.
The hunger in your heart.

But, of all the things I want, 
The one I want most, is for you to just feel loved.
To feel wanted and desired, the way I feel for you.This was written by a dear friend, Summer Dreams.  Written from the heart on the spur of the moment.  I thought it should be shared.  Thank you, Ken
