title: "But, I am In-Love, with You"

I love the smiles and giggles of little children;When excited by the simplest things we do.It doesn’t take much to get that wonderful grin;But, I am In-Love, with you.I love the smell of fresh cut hay in the rain;The same sweetness fills the air in the morning dew.How it permeates the air, regardless of the grain;But, I am In-Love, with you.I love the mist as it is released from a lake of glass;Early in the morning as the sunshine pushes through.Hovering over the water, wondering if it will ever pass;But, I am In-Love, with you.I love walking on a mountain covered in snow;The sites are plentiful and the sounds are few.Tracks of a buck pursuing the not so fleeing doe;But, I am In-Love, with you.I love the little things, I guess you can see;The things taken for granted like most people do.I would sacrifice all these things loved by me;To know you are In-Love with me as I with you.When one discovers what is really important......
