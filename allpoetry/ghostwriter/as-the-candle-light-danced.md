As the candlelight danced upon your skin;
I felt a burning passion building within.
Your beautiful body I slowly began to touch;
Sharing my love, wanting to give so much.
                
With each loving stroke your body did respond;
Crying out for the desire, to again go beyond.
With each response, I was less in control;
Guided not by my heart, but deep within my soul.

My lips met yours and both felt as if on fire;
The heat, more from yearning than physical desire.
Our bodies so close in love’s sweet embrace;
Two hearts beating as one, at a furious pace

Arms around each other, oh what tenderness;
My fears rapidly diminishing with each caress.
As the candlelight danced upon your skin;
I fell asleep, praying the night to never end.
