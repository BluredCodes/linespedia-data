title: "I Long To Hear You Say"
writer: "GhostWriter"

I long to hear you say:
“I love you” without my saying it first.
I long to hear you say:
“I’m In-Love with you” again.
I long to hear you say:
“Make love to me”
I long to hear you say:
“Make love with me”
I long to hear you say:
“I want to make love to you”.
I long to hear you say:
“My love for you is stronger than for any other”.
I long to hear you say:
“I know that I made the right choices”.
I long to hear you say:
“I want us to be together for the rest of our lives”.
I long to hear you say:
“They are just a good friend”.
I long to hear you say:
“I want to be your best friend”.
I long to hear you say:
“You are my best friend”.
I long for the day we can look back;
Seeing all as good; without the pain.
I long for the day, 
You love me the way I love you.
