Hope you have a wonderful day my love.  
You truly were a gift from above.
Whether it's giving you a back rub,
Or sitting with you in our hot tub.
Sharing our love with thee,
Is the only future I want to see.
I love you more than you will ever know,
Your returned love sets my heart a glow.
