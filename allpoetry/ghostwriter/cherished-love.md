---
title: "Cherished Love"
writer: "GhostWriter"

I cherish the moments I am given with you,
 Walking, talking, watching Ally or whatever we do.
 When I'm with you or when we are apart,
 My love for you fills my heart.

 I was blessed the day you became my wife,
 You filled the void and gave purpose to my life.
 You freely gave me the family I never had,
 You honored me with the title of loving dad.

 There is nothing that I could ever say or do,
 That could express the love I have for you.
 To the world this devoted love I profess,
 Embrace it's power to bring you happiness.
 Don't forget those you've loved before,
 Just remember, I'll always love you more.
