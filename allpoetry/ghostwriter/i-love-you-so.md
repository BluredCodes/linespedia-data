I love you so; 
    that you know.
You feel for me more; 
    than you did before.
It is not old fashion; 
    to want the passion.
When our love is flowing; 
    your face is glowing.
Those feelings, you can't hide; 
    they are not so deep inside.
The first battle is won; 
    but we've just begun.
Don't stop now; 
    let me show you how.
Both have so much to give; 
    both have so much to live.
The place we're going, I know not where;
    but our love will surely take us there.
There's nothing else we can do; 
    cause you're in-love with me and I with you.Simple....Very simple...Not my best, but was fun..So what he heck.........
