My love, my life, my reason to live. 
My heart is yours, it’s all I can give. 
My purpose, my journey, why I exist. 
I’m not in control, I can not resist.
  
I try to hold my love inside 
the joy you bring me, I can not hide 
for whenever I dare think of thee, 
a smile on my face is all people see.
  
I love you so, 
more than you know, 
with you I am whole, 
you are my twin soul.
  
My soul is yours for all time. 
For out of the shell I did climb. 
I love you with all my bieng, 
Standing before you, are you seeing? 
 
You complete me, we two, became as one, 
For with your charms, my heart, you have won! 
You dear, are my reason to breathe; to live, 
My love and devotion I now freely give!
  
A hand to hold, as we grow old 
until heaven comes to take thee 
your soul I will breath, never grieve 
for my angel as come to save me.
  
You are my littlest angel 
Sent to me from above 
God knew I needed 
Someone like you to love!
  
Tears of joy and laughter in heart,
 you and I shall never part 
Days come and days go by, 
for eternity it shall remain you and I.
  
I want you near me come what may 
you have promised forever and a day 
and to your word I'll hold you true 
for the afterlife to spend with you.
  
Walking with you through eternity 
Never will I leave your side in this journey. 
This my love is a my Promise 
And I seal this with my Kiss.
  
I feel a love for you sublime 
with endless hearts, my Valentine. 
I know we will remain as one 
beneath the ageless setting sun.
  
I love you more than life itself 
but you're my reason to live. 
I love you above and beyond 
all that is mine to give.My thanks to the contributors on my first add-a-line.
