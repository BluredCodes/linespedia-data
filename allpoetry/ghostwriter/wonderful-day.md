title: "Wonderful Day"
writer: "GhostWriter"

My love for you has never been as deep, 
My desire for your happiness,never so strong. 
When having you in my arms as you fall asleep; 
Having you there next to me all night long. 
Thoughts of you should show on my face, 
As thinking of you fills my heart. 
Our love for each other shall remain in the place, 
As it was at the start. 
Hope you have a wonderful day, 
You'll be in my heart every minute. 
There's not much more I can say, 
Except I can't fathom life without you in it.
