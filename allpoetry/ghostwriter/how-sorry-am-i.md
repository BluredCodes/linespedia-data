---
title: "How Sorry Am I?"
writer: "GhostWriter"

I'm sorry;     I woke you before the alarm. I'm sorry;     You didn't get enough sleep I'm sorry;     You are in a bad mood. I’m sorry;     My pain bothers you so much. I'm sorry;     For everything I've done and do. I'm sorry;     I hurt you. I'm sorry;     I didn't show you. I'm sorry;     I took your love for granted. I'm sorry;     My love for us isn't enough. I'm sorry;     Sometimes, that I am alive.I'm sorry;     For everything, ‘cept the things you have given me:         Your love.         Your family.          Your dreams.  These things I cherish and forever will be grateful.no more need to be sorry
