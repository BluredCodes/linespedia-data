---
title: "For All Eternity"
writer: "DrScissorHands"
slug: "for-all-eternity"

Sitting alone in the darkness,
A sudden change in the air.
The wind still blowing softly,
But it carries with it a devine scent.

Intrigue takes over,
Pure animal urges push me forward.
Riding the wind,
To find the source.

Against the outline of the land,
A lone tree stands,
But with it stands another.

With hair blowing in the breeze,
And a velvet dress that falls on her like magic,
She sings her song of the night.

Approaching from behind,
Mesmerized by the Siren.
Following her scent,
Her every word.

Next to her,
But she does not yet know.
Taking in a deep breath,
Swallowing her entire being.

Ever so gently caressing her shoulder,
Her neck,
Her long luscious neck.

Listening to her song,
Gently leaning towards her,
Sinking the teeth into her,
Drinking of her beauty.

She sings, and sings,
Until she can no longer.

And dropping to the ground,
I pick her up,
And carry her to my castle.

Where she shall stay,
Singing for me,
For all eternity.
