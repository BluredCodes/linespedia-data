I never met you.
Not on a high hill top,
Nor on the flattest of plains.

I know not your name,
Your face,
Your life.

But I close my eyes,
And there you are.

As if in a soft dream,
Your figure approaches me.

I reach my hand out,
And I can feel you touching me.

I feel your hair,
Flowing and soft.

I touch your face,
Smooth and warm.

I can smell you all around me,
As if bliss could be a scent.

I look into your eyes,
Oh how beautiful they are.

I touch your lips,
A shudder passes through me.

I watch your gaite,
Like that of an Angel.

Upon my lips I can taste you,
Within my body I can feel you,
Upon the air rides your sweet scent,
Within your eyes I see our future,
Throughout my head I hear you singing.

You are my Angel,
