Bumblebee-
You called me after Greek decifering-
referenced preistess lore, new moon mischeif and I,
smiled, an indolent sweet.
How many moons passed before the night cleared
from your vision, I stood-
complete and marbled, finally real.
The late night spinning is my holy honey making,
but you could only turn, and address-
Bumblebee-
Yet your no where near the sting.
