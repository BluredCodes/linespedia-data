THANK YOU to all who congratulated me on the birth of my sweet son...also, thanks especially to those who added to the add-a-line about my baby.  Made a mama cry.  Good tears, of course!!

For those who wanted to know, baby Greyson was born the morning of St. Patrick's Day, 2:12 a.m.  He was born at home under water in a gentle, loving environment.  He weighed 8 pounds, 20 inches, and is the new joy of our hearts!!

Busy now, so won't see my poetry friends as much for a few days, until we all figure out how to sleep again LOL!!

Thank you all!

Kym
