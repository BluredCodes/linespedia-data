Good ho, merry fellows
The Dragon of Feeling
Takes stage-

She reveals one's true nature
Both darkness and light
When in her presence
Sorrow takes flight

Emotions so deeply
And fully she feels
Embracing the depth
Allows her to heal

First heal from within
Then attend to the souls
Of all who surround her
Of hearts full of holes

Of course, this noble Dragon
Has a lighter side, too
She loves to make merry
When near her, so will you

Please be kind to the Dragon
She feels what you feel
But if you accept it,
