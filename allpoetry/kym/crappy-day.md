Roses are Red
Violets are Blue
I have dog crap
Beneath my shoe

You beg me to read
A poem of yours
But to you, my writing's 
Out the door

Roses are Red 
Violets are Blue
My poetry sucks
How's that strike you?

Oh yeah, you don't know
'Cause you don't read
But you do know how
To beg and plead


Roses are Red 
Violets are Blue
I've had a crappy day
Really nothing to do with you.Just bitchy tonight.
