---
title: "Namaste, My Love"
writer: "Kym"
slug: "namaste-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16676-Namaste--My-Love-by-Kym"

Looking in your eyes
Really looking
I see
Relections of myself

Everything that is good and perfect
I see
You truly believe in me

Namaste, My Love

I call you my love 
In my soul
Before I have even
Uttered it upon my 
Lips

Looking in the Mirror
Really looking
I see Love
And Reflections
Of You
