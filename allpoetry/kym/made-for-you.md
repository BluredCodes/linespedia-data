title: "Made for You"
writer: "Kym"

My mouth was made 
For kissing yours
Your pillow lips
Pursed and pressed to mine

My curves were made
For your hands
Touching urgently
Beneath my clothes

My tongue was made
For kissing you
And teasing you
And making you come
Alive

This night was made
For making love
Moonlight and snowfall
And the sound
of perfect pleasure
