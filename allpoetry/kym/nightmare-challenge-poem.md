Ominous sky 
Single dark figure 

I am alone 

He appears, sinister 
Beckons me 

I try desperately to 
run away 

Run away 

Run away 

My legs fail me 

I smell his sickening smell 
I can not get away, only closer 

The sky grows more evil 
Red clouds 
Black rain 

He begins his mantra 

The Bible backwards 

I feel my life 
Slowly being drained 

I reach one last pleading time 

And am saved, by my awakening
