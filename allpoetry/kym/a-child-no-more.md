She walks to the open window.
There she steps outside to the roof
What has just happened, she must take in, 
and to take it all in, she must be alone.

She takes a breath.
And then another.
Recounts it all in her mind.

Carefully weighs her feelings.  
What should she be feeling?
For once, she decides to feel.
Decides to choose joy.
Decides not to question it.

She makes her way back into her bedroom, 
And into his arms,
