---
title: "The Angels Know (for Slekky)"
writer: "Kym"
slug: "the-angels-know-for-slekky"
source: "AllPoetry"

For all the things you do
You are ridiculed
For all your patience
You are damned
For all your trials
You are tried some more
For all your generosity
You are used

It gives me so much sorrow
Your struggles, your pain
His victory, his gain
The justice undone
But not in the end

But the angels know
The angels know whose heart holds the love
Whose kisses shower them with care
Whose hard work pays the bills
Whose stories make them laugh
Whose toils make their lives possible

I see your sadness
The despair in your eyes
Don't let him break you
Think of the angels above
Think of the angels upstairs in bed

I love you...And if the angels don't know, someday I will tell them...
