title: "I Hate Humanity"

This miserable, stinking, filthy Earth
Laughs at we, the people

Hearts, rotten to the core
Mock the innocent and the young

Where is the Love?

Brotherhood is a word used 
To make us feel better
While the government
Tears away at our children
and other children

Aren't all the children of the world important?

Where is the Humanity?

I thought this land was your land and our land
But it's only THEIR land and they don't give a damn
About lending a helping hand
Only about the money that is in their handsOkay, one day I will add a recent poem, LOL. This one was written when I was fourteen and disgusted with the way humans treat each other, governments in general.
