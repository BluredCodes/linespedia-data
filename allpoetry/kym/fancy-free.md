---
title: "Fancy Free"
writer: "Kym"

Like a dream in the wind
She sails away
On the air of midnight
I hear her say
It's my nature
Can't you hear my song?
It's fancy free
So come along

Like a quiet knight
He comes to me
Touching my face
Kissing me
It's his nature
Can't you hear his song?
It's fancy free
So come along.

We both ride
A wing and prayer
Two souls free
Can you see us up there?
In the air, free to the sky
Why can't it be that way
Why oh why

Like a dream denied
I am awake
It was not for real
And my heart aches
But maybe someday
I will sing that song
Of fancy free
