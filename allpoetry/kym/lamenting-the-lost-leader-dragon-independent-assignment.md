O,ye Dragons
We lament
The loss of our Leader
Our President
Where could he be?
Where has he gone?
Why would he go
And leave us alone?
We're without direction
Without hope
Maybe he's somewhere
Smoking some dope
Wherever he is,
I hope he returns
Before my dragon heart
Crashes and burns
This poem, it sucketh
But I do not care
Without the Myst Dragon
Our group goes nowhere.There.  No comments necessary, cause I was just having fun!
