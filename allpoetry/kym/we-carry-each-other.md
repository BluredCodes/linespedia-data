You stayed

Once again, I turned on you
Once again, you stayed

It's always like this
I turn into a monster
Saying things I'd kill someone for, 
If THEY said them to you
Somehow it's excusable when I say them

For all my faults
You accept them
When I act like a miserable bitch,
You return the favor with love

How do I deserve you?

Last night, I sat and cried
For you
For all you've put up with
And continue to

I wanted to set you free
Then I realized this:

In a marriage, we carry each other

For better or worse, we said
And we meant it

When I am at my worst, you step up and carry me
When you are at your lowest, I rise up to carry you

I realize that REAL love means working through
The muck and the ugliness
To reach that beauty
The beauty which remains
The goodness that is constant
The truth about us
Which makes our family Love.Just written after a stupid fight last night.  I was telling myself that I caused everything, but then my dear husband stepped up to the plate once again and reminded me that we both act stupid sometimes and this fight wasn't the end of the world.  Before you think I am a terrible person, I am 4 days overdue and just want to GIVE BIRTH TO THIS BABY!  AGHHHH!  Thanks...
