How can you go 
From Hot to Cold?

How can you go
From "Call me" to "Who are you?"

What happened to the stars you promised me?

What became of "Your love fills me up?"

Your lies are pretty
And I wanted to believe in them
But you used them for your purpose
To take a piece of me

Your lies are ugly
All you say is deceit
I want so badly to shut you out
To take away my pain

How can I go
From Joy to Agony?

How can I tell you
Not to come back?
