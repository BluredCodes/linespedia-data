---
title: "the real me"
writer: "deeper"
slug: "the-real-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16396-the-real-me-by-deeper"
---

Judgemental eyes
cast their 
so critizing gaze my way
what do you see?
Am I too dark or 
too fat
not pretty enough
not smart enough
for your impossible expectations
of me
look deeper inside
past your harsh eyes
and see the real me
then tell me...what do you see?
