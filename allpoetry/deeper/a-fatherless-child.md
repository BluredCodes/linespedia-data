---
title: "A Fatherless child"
writer: "deeper"

A fatherless childyou can always tellwill always have questionsbout' the texture of his hairbout' the color of his skinhis last name that he shareswill always wonder whywill never know if he caresHe will look for a lovethat he might never finda mother's love is preciousbut a father's love is divineseeing visions of familyfather, mother and sonlooking for someone to blame is it something he's doneA fatherless childyou can always tellpray for the besthope that he will be wellwill always have questionswill always wonder whywill probably never know...he's a fatherless child...
