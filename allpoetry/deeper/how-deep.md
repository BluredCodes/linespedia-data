---
title: "How Deep???"
writer: "deeper"
slug: "how-deep"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

This pain that I feel
Runs so deep
So I bury it under
Nine feet deep
Words
Like a knife diggin deep
Into me
I cry tears of sadness
And shame
In the shadows of my room
But still it remains
So I bury it under
Nine feet deep
Maybe this time
My wound 
Will feel brand new
how deep?
