It is a connection between two beings
that should never be put asunder
conceiving life itself
giving birth to the future of the world
yielding sickness of the body
joy of the heart
confusion of the mind

It is the thing that attaches the soul
into deceiving (your thoughts)
into believing (their words of promise)
that you are the only one

It is a sample of happiness
a moment of passion
too much, too soon
too little, too late 
as we watch our destiny
at the cruel hand of fate

It is....
