Why do you hate me soIs it because you’reTwisted and uglyAnd have naught toStand forBecause everyone LovesMe you’re not the oneThey adore?It’s not my faultYou’re hatedYou’ve brought it upon Yourself so sit backEnjoy- I know that I willNow you know how itFeels and I’m glad it’sTrueIt feels my heart with Joy to see others laugh atYouI know why you hate Me you’re jealous – poorYou – but everyone Loves Me and I owe it all toyou...i wrote this about my sister whom i dislike a lot...i'd say hate but that's just wrong ^-^ i'm not alone in my feelings either... ja ne! =^-^=
