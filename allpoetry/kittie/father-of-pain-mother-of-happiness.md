title: "Father of Pain   Mother of Happiness"

A gloomy shadow above your head
A gentle brush of wind against your cheek
Just Deaths way of saying hello...

"You can’t stop it"

A haunting melody playing in your mind
A tainted smile with Deaths’ Love on its lip
Just an invitation from the Grim Reaper...

"Try as you may" 

The taste of Life’s poison in the sweetest soul
The smell of milk and honey with the smell of dead roses
Just Fates way of welcoming you...

"It comes for you"

A struggle for a gasp of air
The numbing of your tongue and the blackening of eyes
Just your life escaping your body...

Death comes in many forms
Only no one sees it till it comes...just a simple lil poem about death! (o^-^o)
