Why does it seem to get hot 
When you’re near me?

These flames of desire
...Desire of what...

Don’t think it’s Love
That died for you long ago

The Love I do have for you
Is a counterfeit 3 dollar bill

I feel I shall never carry 
That torch for you again

It’s a burden off of me
A tremendous wave of relief

The heat-that’s anger
Hatred and rage...

I can’t seem to control 
When you’re near

My body gets hot and flustered
To simply think of you

You bring out these emotions in me
That no one else can

The fire of my desire
Should have been Love

But that flame is extinguished
