Please Believe Me …When I say, I love you,Please believe me…I love you more than words can sayWhen I say I need you,Please believe me…I need you with each breath I takeWhen I say I want you Please believe me…I want you with me alwaysWhen I say I miss youPlease believe me …I miss you so much my heart does acheWhen I say you are my heartPlease believe me …Our hearts beat as oneWhen I say you are my reason for living Please believe me …Without you with me there is no livingi CAN write Love poems too...if i treally wanted...^-^
