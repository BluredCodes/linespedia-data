I looked in the  mirrorto see what there was to seeAnd the reflection I saw  Was not of meWhat was that phantom  image you say?that is something I’m notsure of todayit must have been part of me thoughshowing how I feel deep in my soulit was dark and twisted  a bit evil to meBut it also had a certain beautyNow that you see how I am insideDo you think it’s hatred I hidesearch your soul…the answer’llcome to you…
