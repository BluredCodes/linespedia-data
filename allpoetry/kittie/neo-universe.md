If this were a perfect world
I could be with you

I wouldn’t have to write you letters
With endearments of my Love

I could be right there with you
And show you my Love

You wouldn’t have to look at my picture
To remember how I look

I would be next to you
As we gaze into each others eyes

You’d never have to imagine
How it would feel to kiss me

I’d always be near you
Tasting the Love of your kiss

Maybe in a perfect world this is all true
But this world is less than that
Trying to make our Love impossible of coming true...this is about mi novio and my best friend! i can't figure it out!! *-*
