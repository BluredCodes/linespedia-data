About You

I'm not mad at you
It's not entirely your fault
I do still love you
Just not with all my heart

I know you have other friends
I'm not your only one
But when you left to talk to her
It nearly broke my heart

It's her I have the problem with
The problems not with you
Though I get jealous sometimes
My heart is always true

I'm not mad
I'm not angry
I am jealous this is true
I'm not upset
I'm not hurt
No ... I don't hate you

I'm not mad at you
It's really not your fault
But when you do these things to me
It always breaks my heart
