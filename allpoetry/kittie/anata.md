---
title: "Anata"
writer: "Kittie"

source: "AllPoetry"

Why do I give my Love to others
Only to have them throw it away

They like to use me and play with my heart
I guess because I’m young and so naïve

I saw the world with brand new eyes
Only to have the old life come back into existence

 Now I won’t Love another
My heart couldn’t take it anymore

I don’t feel mad, sad, angry or even upset
Not anymore

I’ve become too numb
To feel such things

i have no more Love to be wasted by you...this is just how i feel...right now actually...

oh and anata is you in japanese if you didn't know...
