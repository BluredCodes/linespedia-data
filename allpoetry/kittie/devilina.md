As the crimson moon melts my skin
I’m about to reveal my true self

In order to atone for all of my sins
Burn this body and reveal the true form

The pure spirits
Violate this black decayed soul

Look out the window
While closed eyes gaze across an open field

-Can you see my figure-

Whisper upon the wind
A wish from the depths of your heart

On my tainted lips
I lick away the remnants
Of your angelic soul

In this closed off world
I’ve opened your mind
With the cost of your soul

Only this feeling
That cannot be fulfilled
Is filled with souls...
