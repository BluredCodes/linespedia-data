Come with me and embrace the dark side
The angels of Hell give us all we need
My dark Prince invites us to spend all eternity
We are the children of sin
We give into our pleasures
The non-believers will experience his power first hand
We welcome the sins of the flesh
Mortal rules do not apply
We will live in eternity
