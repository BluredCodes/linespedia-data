title: "Never"
writer: "Kittie"

Never will I be there to comfort you
In times of need

Never will I be there to softly whisper
That I Love you

Never will I be there to listen to your 
Stories about the day

Never will I be there to hold you tightly
Through the night

Never will I be there to help you make
Future plans with

Never will I be there to greet you with
A kiss and a hug

Never will I be there to see you 
Anymore
