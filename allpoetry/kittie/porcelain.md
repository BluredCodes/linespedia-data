title: "PorcelaiN"
writer: "Kittie"

You lock yourself in a crystal world
With all your childhood fantasies
You think you’re secure in your unrealistic...insecure...world

How fragile is the glass
That hangs above your head
The slightest touch of reality
Will bring it down faster than your imagination could...

Once reality sets in
You’re no longer in that crystal place

You’re locked inside a steel world
With all your adulthood ‘dreams’

It’s cold...unloving – but –
Can fulfill your security needs

How strong is the steel 
That has now locked you in

Nothing can penetrate it
No Love, feelings or air...crystal is pretty...steel is cold but pretty too!
