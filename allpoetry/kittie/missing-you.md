title: "Missing You..."
writer: "Kittie"

I Love talking to you
You must know I do

Just the more it seems
I begin to miss you

I can look at your picture
No big thrill to me

Shown in comparison...
It’s not the real thing

I yearn to be near you
To feel of some touch

Like hugging an illusion
Shows no Kindness, Love, nor Warmth

Talking to you 
I Love to do

But the more I do 
The more I miss you...i wrote this after i talked to my fiance...that is 2 bloody states away in school....*sigh*  =^-^=
