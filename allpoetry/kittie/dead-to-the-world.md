---
title: "Dead to the World"
writer: "Kittie"
slug: "dead-to-the-world"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12713-Dead-to-the-World-by-Kittie"
---

I search for my soulOnly to wander through deserts of vast eternityMy soul is unloving uncaringI'm dead to the worldMy heart a blackened lump of coalno longer beatsI'm dead to the worldThe love I give Carries no warmthIt's colder than the first snowI no longer liveI'm dead to the world
