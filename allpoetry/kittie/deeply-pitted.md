Deeply pittedIn my stomachIs all the emotion I have for youIf it was LoveI’d Let you see itBut no-that’s not itMaybe if you looked at me-Not what you want to see-Looked at me! Then you’d knowHow I was feeling…But now it’s too lateThe last bit of LoveI may have had for you is goneIt’ll never returnJust be satisfied that I acknowledge your existence…
