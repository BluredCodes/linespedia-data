I lay in bed and count the sheep
I’m trying but can get no sleep

The time is now half past 4
When I hear a noise at door

Who could it be at this hour
 I get up and my mood turns sour

I open door and all I see
 is nothing – nothing I can see

I lay back down to resume the count
By now I start to have my doubt

As I see the approach
 of Dawn
Thoughts of Joy
are dead and gone
