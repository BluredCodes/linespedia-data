It happens once a month of every year
I dread that day when my beast appears
Dripping full of red
When it first comes it hurts more than it seems
No one likes to be around me on that
day of the week...it seems
I can't control it
I'd make it go away
But it comes from within me and remains
Tomorrow is a day i dread
That my people fear
because that is the day 
I become a werecat again...um..once again i am trying something different merely for the sake of it...
don't too much care for this...anyhoo....here it is...*wow*
