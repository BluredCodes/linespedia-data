A dead soul I hold within
Knowing each day that Living is my sin

My halo now turned into horns
My (angelic) wings now tattered and torn

Seems, perhaps, I’ve fallen from (His) grace?
But now I’m in my rightful place

In here there are no tears
This is a place shunned for years

Here is the sanction
Known as Hell

Where creatures of the night
Like me...dwell...um...this is...my poem!! ^-^v
