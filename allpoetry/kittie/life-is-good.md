title: "Life is Good..."

Life is painful
Anyone can tell you
If they say otherwise
They're simply lying to you

Your God is a fraud
A figment of your imagination
You were made to believe in him
by 'them'...

Some call me an outcast
Which is plain to see
But it's fascinating to know that they
Are just like me

Life is pain
I wish it would end

You have a false God
You were made to believe in

I'm stuck on the outside
With no way in

Alive on the outside
But dead within
