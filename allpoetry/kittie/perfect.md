You once told me pretty words
And of lands far away

I was led to believe I’d be there with you
...Someday...

You told me I was perfect for you
I was the perfect woman...you’d say

You Love me just the same
Though I don’t look your ‘way’

I thought I was perfect for you...

You told me we’re perfect together
Those words led me astray

I’m the perfect woman for you
Maybe that’s why we’re not together today...this poem just wouldn't come out right...words all jumbled...perhaps i'll revise it...bah...see! i'm not cynical about Love...
Kittie T. Moon =^-^=
