Why do I Love you  When all you do is torture me?Am I a punching bag made only for you?   I Love you and my heart is too purebecause I can’t feel the pain caused by you.  You strangle me as a tear rolls down my cheekOne tear isn’t crying...or is it?  Maybe now you can end my confusionSqueeze harder I beg you!  Show how much you really care.You say you Love me too  Now’s the time to prove it!My soul is already dead  Gone numb…it feels no more painI can feel death creep over me...  And now…my final breath...Death is upon me  Maybe now I can rest..i don't too much like this...but it was how i was feeling at the time! ^-^
