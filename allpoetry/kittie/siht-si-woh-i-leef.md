I'm enveloped in Darknessthe Shadows have consumed my SoulLeft me empty...hollow...Not knowing which way to turnI search for DeathAnd not finding HimOnly brings more pain and tormentTo my already dead SoulI guess I've been judgedMy Fate already sealedDoomed to wander this world...Alone with Misery...Drenched in my own blood...My 'Life' is all I know of heavenAnd all I need of Hell...
