Your God is dead
I am dead
Someday you'll die too
And when you do
You'll see your God
And he will laugh at you

He'll laugh at all your foolish beliefs
And everything you stand for
You'll see him for who he really is
Not the one you adored

You look at him and wonder
You look at him and think
'All my life of servitude
Just went down the sink.'

So you still don't know the story
of your only God's death - but!
He was cruel to you in life
And even crueler to you in deaththis is one of my favorite poems!
