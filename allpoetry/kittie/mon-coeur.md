Can I look up to you
As you looked down on me

Can I feel in to you
As you felt in to me

Whispers of words since long forgotten
We live inside the future of a shattered past

In this Haven of silence you’ve created for me
Everyone’s just a thought waiting to fade

Nothing is real anymore

Not you
Not me

Not our Love...
The bodies in my Cage of Life
Are empty
Empty bodies at rest
Causalities of their own flesh

My whole body is not numb
-I can talk-
Talk of illusions of false hope

What once was Love
Now is I C E

My frozen tomb of a heart
Carries warm sadness and gloom
I hurt the most where I can’t feel...

While alive I feel nothing...

The dull razors of your Love
Cut me no longer
