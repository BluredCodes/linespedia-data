Stuck between two roads
One leads to the past 
The other to what may be
My future

The first road is a Love
That is familiar and comfortable
(I like this)

The second is a Love 
That is different and new
(I like this too)

I can’t choose between the two
I’m stuck and know not what to do

Caught in the crossroads of life
My heart belonging to two
I Love them both
