Broken doll heads surround my feet
Shattered dreams fill my eyes
Butcher knife in hand
While trickles of blood run down my mouth
Such is the portait of Innocence...

Dead bodies reach for me 
And tell secrets in an etheral voice
Blackend tongues kiss lips
As icy fingers feel inside
grasping for my heart
And I, I give a sickening smile
while pleading for more...

The crimson drops spilt on the floor
Reanimate into swirls of dreams
Unsuspecting dreams of purtiy becoming renamed
Get quickly consumed by my thoughts...

Rotting corpeses with skin of perfection
Lull me away from the living
They feast upon my rose-colored life-force
Until they are intoxicated by its scent

The velvet darkness that once kept me safe
eats my vision...then my world

And i am haunted by that melody
