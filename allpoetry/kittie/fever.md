I’ve wandered through this land before
This land is so familiar

But the people I do not know
The inhabitants I don’t know them

They stare at me with doll like eyes
And grins stuck like plastic

They speak to me in rhyme
And languages not usually spoken

Who are these people
These weird creatures you might ask

They’re the voices in my head
Tiny people not living or dead…i don't know what this is about....*-*
