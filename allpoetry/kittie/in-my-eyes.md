In my world
Everyone is Grey
But I am Blue

Standing in the middle of the crowd
I’ve never felt more alone

I scream –aloud- 
Only to be surrounded by silence

Millions of eyeless status stare at me
Tongue-less mirrors speak to me about Life

The crows in the field ravish my heart
And still I am not happy

I shall remain in my cage
