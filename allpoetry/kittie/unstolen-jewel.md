---
title: "~Unstolen Jewel~"
writer: "Kittie"
slug: "unstolen-jewel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53872--Unstolen-Jewel--by-Kittie"

In this way, one story finally comes to an end.
After this long period of time has elapsed inside
a blink of dream...
Things that remain are just my memories...

However, one day you and me will meet again...
In another place, at another time...
It is not sure if we will still be able to
recognize each other or not, though...

Open the unknown door,
Meet one more reality,
And live in one more today.
Although the story has ended, life continues

So until that time has come...
farewell...this is dedicated to all my friends that the Navy has taken from me!
