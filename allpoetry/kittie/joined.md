Even though we’re miles apartYou’re never that farFor we are joined at the heartI have your face printed in my mindThat image is always kindI have your voice recorded in my thoughtsThat always does something for my heartI have your touch painted on my soulThat’s when Love does take its tollThis way we’re never far apartThough you’re gone We’ll meet againFor we are joined at the heart...*kuja* =^-^=
