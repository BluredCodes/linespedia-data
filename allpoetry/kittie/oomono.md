Why must we play this game

This game of the mind
This game of the heart...

Why can’t I just find someone
And know they’re mine

I have to search through a sea of cities
And across the deserts of Life

Sometimes I get a nibble of a
bite...
But those fish can stay in the sea

Perhaps every other blue moon
I’ll come to an oasis
That patch of water cannot quench my thirst

My thirst of Life
My thirst of Love...

I know one day soon
I’ll have that delectable feast
Or that drink to end all thirst

One day I will but until then
I play these games

These games I can never win...not my 'usual' style if i have one...but it came out...but none of it's true so i don't know where it came from...

oh and Oomono means game...like big game...the man is the game...^-^v nihongo to english translation sucks! >.<
