---
title: "Run Away"
writer: "Kittie"
slug: "run-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I wish I could go back home to youYou must know – I’ve always Loved youBut the pain I feel is much too deepAnd coming back home opens those memoriesTrue we had fun and laughterBut that was always quieted by my tears...It wouldn’t be so hard if I didn’t Love youThings would be different if I wouldn’ve stayed homeSometimes you hurt meAnd I hurted you tooBut that’s when I realized most that I Loved youI’d Love to go homeForget the places I’ve beenSee your smiling faceAnd hold you nearBut to visit your grave Is something I can’t endureI know I’d cry – that’s for sureIt wouldn’t hurt so much if didn’t Love youEven though you’re gone...know I’ll always Love you...
