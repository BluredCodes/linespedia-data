Deep in the forest
Beyond – in a clearing

Lies a princess with unimaginable beauty
She sleeps and slumbers a thousand dreams

A spell cast by a witch out of jealousy
She’s spiteful to not have been invited to the ball
To show off her magic and charm to them all

Our sleeping ‘beauty’ waits for that day
When a handsome prince shall become her...prey...

When he comes to kiss her awake
More than his Love...his heart...does she take

She ‘kisses’ him with her Fangs
Till she reaches his soul
And every drop of crimson out of his body flows

While she lays in perfect poise
Continuing this farce of sleep
Her alluring beauty leads
Another victim for her to eat

Beauty on the surface
With a secret beneath

No beauty is innocent
