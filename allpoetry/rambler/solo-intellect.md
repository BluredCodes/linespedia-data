Solo intellect
Absent the Siamese twin of prudence
Scintillating yet barren
Unlike Archimedes
Moves not the world 
But follows it
Discernment’s crippled leg dragging
Answering plaguing questions
Not through wisdom
But by the swing of the pendulum
Is it the one?
Or the many
Whose need is paramount? When?
Where does the king’s scepter light?
Maintaining oscillation
Between Scylla and Charybdis
A monotonous acrobat of weariness
Solo intellect, solo skill
Bequeathing ages of waste
In endless genuflection
To a dearth of options
Never deferring to wisdom
That solvent of mystery
Preferring rule over error
To understanding
