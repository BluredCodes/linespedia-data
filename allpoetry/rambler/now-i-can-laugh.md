title: "Now I Can Laugh"

Once they had me flummoxed
Once they had me duped
Back in those early years
Still coalescing
Leaves yet to unfold
My naiveté a neon sign
Lighting my path to slaughter
And like missing that last step
When reality mugs assumption
I stumbled into their world unawares
Full of good and simple thoughts
Easy going, assuming the best
To be leveled by their fierceness
And trampled by merciless feet
Their ears barricaded against appeal
Their decibels disproportionate
To their real importance

Subdued by their power
Cowed by their dominance
Wondering why my portion was among the weak
But with time’s layered unveiling
Things fall along lines unimagined
Through weakness developed strength
Unperceived through their myopic lens
Until it was too late
Bundled with true strength was vision
Revealing the true weakness
They had made appear strong
And I pitied them
Some came to multiply subjugation
And were dispatched easily
With economy of thought and act

Looking back on those years
Like peering through the wide end of the telescope
Is to peer upon a distortion
Thankfully retaining strength without warp
No dross of bitterness left in the wake
For I know who is strong
Now I can laugh
And my laugh is stronger 
Than all their deliberationsHate is Imminent/Category is abuse
