---
title: "The Cynic's Watch"
writer: "Rambler"
slug: "the-cynics-watch"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45923-The-Cynics-Watch-by-Rambler"
---

Cynical eyes survey the human scene
Accustomed and expectant
To spy misshapen forms
Of aborted hope and choked potential
Maintaining effortless dominance of panoramic sweep
By nature weak
Yet strong through slavish consent
Of upright souls bearing groveling hearts
Stumbling in gullible sloth’s dim vision
Seeing no distinctions
Homogenized horizons subsumed under incantations
Equality! Relative! Tolerance!
So by self-righteous certitude
And a multiplication of cheap legitimacies
The sick appear well
Solipsistic overflow turns cogent stream
Light for darkness, darkness for light
Idols of abstraction claim sacred center
A leveled realm absent wrong or right
Save in relation to the measuring stick
Coercion
Shedding its thin veils of feigned toleration
Emboldened through consent’s servile surrender
Unafraid to display gross inconsistencies
As the sheep bleat in obscene contentment

Cynical eyes survey weariness unutterable
Astonished at waste’s abundance
And the ubiquitous free thinkers
Who ensure copious redundancy of the parrot’s role
Astonished at the myriad captives
Proclaiming freedom with enslaved hearts
Hoping for the eviction day
Of reality’s vile usurper
