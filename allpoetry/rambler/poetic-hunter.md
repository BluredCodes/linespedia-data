Poets birth insight
Words fetch and carry their pleasure
Painting semantic portraits
Fathomed depths framed
The invisible finds tongue
Colors deepen, textures richen
Momentarily all is touch
Berlin Wall betwixt heart and mind
An unnatural imposition
Former groping translates
Obstacles recede
Stratosphere becomes playground
Seek the poetic?
Chase the shadow
Grasp the vapor
The poetic pervades
Intersections appearing
When sight tethers assumption
Rescinding imagination's curfew
The poetic seeks you
Be found
