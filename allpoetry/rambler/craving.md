Lovers burn for consummation
Souls hunger in famine
Junkies yearn for the fix
Cravings
Parched hearts thirst
Sick through a history of draughts
Drawn from substitution's beckoning well
Ready to sell all
For a cold drink of meaning
Weary of enticing mirage's embrace
Enfolding to ashes
A solitude of raped expectations
and dust from moths wings
When craving exceeds
Tyranny of self-definition
Meaning fills the void
Exit appears
In the room full of mirrors
