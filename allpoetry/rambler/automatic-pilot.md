Where'd I put the cereal box?
Where'd I put the brush?
Why is it that far too often,
my life's in such a rush?
Jumped in my car and drove to work
Oh no! I'm off today!
Went to the post office, wound up at the store
I'm on autopilot, I say
Walked to the bedroom, purpose in mind
Specific task to do
When I got there I tried to remember
But didn't have a clue
Automatic pilot is great for machines
Things that have no soul
But when you thought you had a mind
It's unnerving to find a black hole
