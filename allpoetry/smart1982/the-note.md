'As I sit and watch women on TV,
I think to myself that will never be me.
I will never be tall and have long legs like they do.
I always have to wear heels on my shoes.
And I don't look like a model with an over-weighted chest.
Although that seems to be what most guys like best.
To look like they do, they might have to put their bodies to the test. 
And if my looks won't be accepted, I'll just lay my body to rest.'

'Mom and Dad, I'm sorry to leave you behind.
But before I left, I had to leave you a piece of my mind.
Now you see what this world has done to me.
So, now I'm leaving this screwed up society.
I wish you luck in all you do.
And remember, I always accepted you for you.'

She kissed the note and said a prayer.
And left it on the table by the chair.
