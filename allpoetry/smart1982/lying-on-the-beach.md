Staring at the sky
Lying on the beach
Many dreams at hand
If I could only reach
Running my fingers through the sand
Drawing a heart and your name
Thinking about the distance between us
And that no one is to blame
The warm breeze blows through my hair
While I enjoy the view
Then I wonder if there's real happiness in life
And then I look over and I see you
You walk over to me
And give me a kiss
Don't believe there's anything
Better than this

I could stay right here forever
Lying on the beach together
