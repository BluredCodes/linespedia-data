There she stands, 5 foot 9 with a size 2 waist.
Long, long, flowing hair and a porcelain face.
She hardly ever eats a thing, just to keep her size.
And when someone confronts her about it, she tells them a bunch of lies.
She'll do no matter what it takes even if it will cost her life.
Just so she can live in the world and fit in with the stereotype.


There he stands, tall, with muscles from head to toe.
He works himself way too hard so he can have them to show.
He carries himself with a 'tough man' attitude, just like he's 'supposed' to have.
And when he really hurts inside, he hides it, otherwise everyone will laugh.
He'll do no matter what it takes even if it will cost his life.
Just so he can live in the world and fit in with the stereotype.


Why can't we be individuals the way God meant for us to be? 
There are too many stereotypes in the world, but a stereotype isn't me.
