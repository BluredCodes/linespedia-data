Just finished number three,
Now you're ready for number four.
You've wasted your life drinking,
Now you're about to hit the floor.
You always hung out with the wrong crowd.
You've never done anything right.
So, all your bad mistakes are about to end your life.
All you have to do is get on your knees and pray.
The Lord has been there all your life,
and He'll be there to listen to you today.


So, now you've started to change
and there is happiness everywhere.
You should be very thankful.
You're lucky He is there.
