---
title: "I Will Always Love You"
writer: "Smart1982"
slug: "i-will-always-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17934-I-Will-Always-Love-You-by-Smart1982"
---

It's been a while since that time 
When I stole your heart and you stole mine
Now I want to tell you that you shouldn't doubt
That when I say 'I love you'
I know what I'm talking about

I will always love you
No matter what we go through
I will always love you 
No matter what we do
I will always love you

Ever since I saw you, I knew you were the one
And now that our hearts are tied together, they'll never come undone
When we get old and are sitting in our rocking chairs
I will be happy as long as our love is there.

I will always love you
No matter what we go through
I will always love you 
No matter what we do
I will always love you


written: 1996 (age 14)
