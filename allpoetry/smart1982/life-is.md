Life is... a blessing of a baby to two eager parents.
Life is... the happiness you have for all special moments.
Life is... the good times that tend to overcome the bad.
Life is... the tears you cry when you are feeling sad.

Life is... the friendships you have and share everyday.
Life is... the hope that carries you along the way.
Life is... all the wishes that you have that no one will ever know.
Life is... only a dream to those who lost their chance long ago.
