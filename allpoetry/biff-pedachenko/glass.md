The sky's the limit
It was never more true
Living in a world which has been to the moon

I want you to see
What's well out of sight
Goals which are set out beyond our dreams

Pressure to reach them
Growing each day
With never time to celebrate our boom

Thank You's are lost
Praise's never for you
We struggle to alter our failures now past

We'll never reach them
If things don't change
Surviving in this world with it's sky of glass

Being a part of this world is what's holding us back.
