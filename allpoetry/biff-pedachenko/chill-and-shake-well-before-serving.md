FILTERED WATER;
Everything I know is refined
Filtered and distilled until all meaning is lost
My entire life extracted from a few insignificant moments
FRUIT JUICES FROM CONCENTRATE;
Reduced to manageable memories
From which I’m to take enough to fill in the blanks
To interpolate my life from their scattered and meaningless
   occasions
(ORANGE, LEMON, GRAPEFRUIT, TANGERINE AND LIME);
They send me different flavours
Make me believe this new trifle’s better than the last
They present to me select snapshots to make it appear to be 
   sweeter
SUGAR/GLUCOSE-FRUCTOSE;
But it is to no avail
For I’ve seen the truth, they cannot deny me that
No matter what they might do now, I know the truth and I have
   seen the light
CONCENTRATED WATER EXTRACT OF ORANGE;
I now know of that which they do
Know that they suck dry the very sweetness of it
And when they discover that I know, they sprinkle in choice
   ingredients
CITRIC ACID (CONTROLS TARTNESS);
Hoping I won’t sour on it
But how could I hope to find joy and happiness
If I know everything they give me is engineered to a purpose
NATURAL FLAVOUR;
And given under false pretences
Under the delusion that this is what real life is
That there is nothing more beyond what they show, beyond what 
   they choose to play
RETURN FOR REFUND WHERE APPLICABLE.
But I don’t want it anymore
I want the cold, hard truth regardless of its nature
Even if it shakes me to the core and shatters all that I 
   thought I knew

   At least then I know it’s real.take a look at the ingredients list on a bottle of five alive in an insomniatic hallucination at 5am and you begin to see strange things
