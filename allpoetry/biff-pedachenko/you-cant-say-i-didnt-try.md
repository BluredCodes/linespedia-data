You can't say I didn't try
For I did everything in my power
From the instant I first beheld you
I knew what I wanted
So I did everything I could
To find a way into your world
I took those jobs to be near you
I made friends of your family
I invited you to dinner again and again
And I was always there to listen
But still you hid behind the walls
And now I know why you did it
He was an asshole
And I apologize for his acts
But please try to understand
We're not all like that
Now I don't blame you for it
I don't mind now being shut out
And if ever you change your mind
You always now where I am
But whatever might happen
Please try to remember
You can't say I didn't try
