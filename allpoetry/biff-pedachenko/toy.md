God, that was fun the other night
I really needed that
We laughed and we joked
We kissed and we cuddled
Visited your friends
And walked the town for hours
Just talking and holding hands

Then I realized
I'm just your toy
And the emptiness I feel now
Makes me need once again
Someone to hold near
And to live out
What we only acted
