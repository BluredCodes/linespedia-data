Wondrous awe
She wonders why I sit
In this place meant for walking

The supposed bringer
Of order to our world
Joins in protest of the norms

Always rushing
Those who pass us by
Always hurrying to get nowhere

In a hurry
To get nowhere
To do nothing but fearing less

In their hurry
To reach that place
For fear that it won't be there laterenraptured, do you remember this, what great inspiration, hehe
