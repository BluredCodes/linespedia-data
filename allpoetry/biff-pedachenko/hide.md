---
title: "Hide"
writer: "biff pedachenko"

source: "AllPoetry"

Tonight I look to the stars
For inspiration in my life today
A guiding light of sorts

But instead I see your street lights
Blocking my view of what's beyond
Always holding me back

What is there to hide
That I could not expect to see
There is nothing
With which I could not cope

So why don't you turn off your blinders
If for no one but yourself
And give me the chance
To see what lies beyond
