I remember when things were simple
There was you
And there was me
Enjoying our time in the sun
Feeling the breeze in our hair
Living and loving

But now you're gone
And I've been blown off my feet.uhh, yeah.  It's pathetic, but it just came to me and I wanted to put it down somewhere
