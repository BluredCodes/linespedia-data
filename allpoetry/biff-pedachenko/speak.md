There is so much I want to say
That I don't know where to begin
There is so much I want to hear
But your words fall on deaf ears
There is so much now that I feel
That edges become unclear
They say it's the simple things that matter
And yet you're not simple
But your all that matters to me.You know, now that ive typed this, i can't say i like it, oh well, it's an oldie
