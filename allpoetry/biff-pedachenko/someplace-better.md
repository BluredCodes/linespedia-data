title: "Someplace Better"

There's no one else here
But this place is alive
It crawls with the unsettling life
The type that comes from nowhere
But envelopes you in its shadow

I sit here taking it all in
The scurrying rats below
And the stench of soaked cardboard
And I begin to think
'Surely I deserve better than this'

And I know that once I did
But that was a long time ago
Before it happened
Before someone told me what I should do
And before I listened
