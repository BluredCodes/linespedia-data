A friend of mine's got some problems,
She's feelin' a little blue,
And it seems that I can't help her,
No matter what I try to do.
But if she's out there readin',
Know I'm thinkin' of you,
And know you'll soon find a way back,
To the times when your spirits flew.Oh dear god, it rhymes and it has that strange punctuation the teachers try to push, somethings wrong.  Oh well, I'm sure the poor diction makes up for it.
