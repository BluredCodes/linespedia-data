title: "Empty"
writer: "wombat"

Like an abandoned pipeline in the backyard,
Like a subway station after evacuation,
Like the sign-up line for sacrificial means,
Like concern groups dealing with outcast teens,
Like a church, whose pastor likes children too much,
Like a school councelor's office during lunch,
Like the hands of the hungry and dead,
Like a hole in my head,
Like sympathy for the rich,
Like empathy for my cause,
Like the observation deck of a rape victim's recovery,
Like the camera operators at department stores,
Like their camera bubbles,
Like this poem,
Like these words,
Like me.
Like you.
