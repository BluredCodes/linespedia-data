so here we are
in a momentary seperation
feeling so distraught over something we know will pass 

it seems as though this has become a cycle

im so tired and sick of this guessing-game of a relationship
i will guess what youre thinking and be terriably wrong 

the outcome

momentary seperation

how was i to know?

you make it so easy for yourself
take it or leave it, is the only way to go 

but this will pass 
we both know it 

but i cant help but feel angry and used 
abused
bruised

so what if 
what if i shake things up
what if this isnt so momentary

thats it

thats what must be done to stop this relentlos cycle
my final words

ITS OVER!
