Damn! 
I've done it again 
Made a lover from a friend 
Why do I do this to myself 
I know I will only be dissapointed 
You will have your fun and then (like a child) will run 
I suppose you think I am here for you to use 
Like a quick fix for a down in dumps day 
An easy quick lay 
All I am left to say............ 
Damn!
