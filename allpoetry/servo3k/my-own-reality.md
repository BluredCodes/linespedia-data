---
title: "my own reality"
writer: "servo3k"
slug: "my-own-reality"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52941-my-own-reality-by-servo3k"

slip
fall
escape
in my own reality
the easy way out
without a doubt
not caring for others 
only me
no one else
only see what looks good to me
oh but what a lonely way to live
is it worth it?
