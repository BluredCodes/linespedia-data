im in an altered state of me
im surrounded by things so familier to me
but yet
i still feel as though im a stranger to myself
i look at the figure in the mirror
and i dont recognise her
who is she
i keep telling myself
its you
but it doesnt register in my mind
what the hell is wrong with you?!
i dont understand
why does this always happen to me?
i dont understand
at least i can escape the world for a while
