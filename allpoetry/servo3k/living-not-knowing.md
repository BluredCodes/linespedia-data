---
title: "living not knowing"
writer: "servo3k"
slug: "living-not-knowing"
source: "AllPoetry"

the torture
the fear
living not knowing
always in question
fear of rejection
never knowing the anwser
am i?
is he?
could it be?
oh God i fear so
i cant stand living and not knowing
a fear i should not have to face
how can i act as though everything is okay?
as if it will go away
the worst torture
the worst fear
living and not knowing
i wipe away a tear
