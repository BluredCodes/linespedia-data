You kissing whore
how dare you make yourself happy
how could you
my time with you was hell
well
why did you waste it
its your own fault
dont blame me for your mistakes
you think your jelous rage is going to tear me down
well youre sadly mistaken
for i am NOT shaken
shaken by words you do not mean
shaken by things you say that you have been holding back
i say
what took you so long 
why wasnt i told before
i could have moved on 
just get over it 
i didjust taking a little anger out on the ex
