draw a circle where you wish 
and in the center place a stone 
that is where you will find me 

staring out at you 
i am the unmoving fury 
of a murdered predator 
a terminal velocity 
that relativity has forgotten 
this stone that is me 
is inertia incarnate 
frozen like a photograph 
and polished like a mirror 
a convex memory 
that distorts all it reflects 
yet no matter how long my radius 
or distorted my circumference 
the formula is unchanged 

i thought that these theorems 
could not hold me forever 
i hoped the rules would change 
at the quantum level 
but i stand and look 
out to the horizon like 
my eyes water 
with the focusing of distance 
i turn, and by degrees 
see perimeter all around me 
even if the earth’s radius 
were to become my radius 
and my circumference infinite 
no matter where i go 
i would still be in the center 

but i cannot divide by zero 
even murdered predators 
strain and stretch to survive 
it is the arithmetic of biology 
as unchanging as pi 
from here in the center 
i triangulate the distance 
strain to see the pattern 
out there on the horizon line 

my last hope of
