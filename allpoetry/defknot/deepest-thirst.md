title: "deepest thirst"
writer: "defknot"

finding a hole
a place to runaway
laughter of evil circuts through my viens
I wanted to see you when you left me before
and now Im all alone
you had to shut the door
screaming to hear you
through this glass wall
everythings lost now
when did I fall
