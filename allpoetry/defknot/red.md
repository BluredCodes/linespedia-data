title: "red"
writer: "defknot"

i found you 
when i was looking for me
i fell on you 
when i was falling to see
i cut my lip
when i gave you my trust
i burnt my skin
when i leaned in for your touch
i lost my hope
when i dreamed for you
and now im lost in a circle
where nothing comes true
