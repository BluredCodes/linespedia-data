title: "cant look away"
writer: "defknot"
slug: "cant-look-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

i cant hurt you now
now im gone
gone from tomorrow
tomorrow consumes it all
all the windows shut
shut it out before
before you close your eyes
eyes release my pain
pain breathing through you
you are my only one
one more time
time is letting go
go to where ill be
be in the place for you and me
