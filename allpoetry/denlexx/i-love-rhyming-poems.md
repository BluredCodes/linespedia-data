I love rhyming poems
They surely are the best
They get your mind to thinking
And send you on a quest

They make more sense when reading
And can give you quite a smile
They sometimes float around your head
And stay there for a while

Some people just can't do them
They got no sense of rhyme
I think they are just lazy
As rhyming takes more timeremember it's just a poem ok, I don't want to upset no one :-)
