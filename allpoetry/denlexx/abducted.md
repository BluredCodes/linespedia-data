I've seen every science fiction Show
That you could ever See
Sometimes i even think 
I have some Alien in Me

I always had a Longing
To sail the stars i Love
And go way above the Earth
To look down from Above

I'd love to be Abducted
By some sexy alien Beauty
She'd say come into my Spaceship
you sexy earthling Cutie

I'd fly off around the Universe
With my intergalactic Lover
You never know i may find Out
