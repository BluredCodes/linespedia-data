If I had the mind of a poetI would write you words so sweetWords of love and Happiness To knock you off your FeetWords to make you Love meWords to make you MineWords to make your Toes curl upAnd make you feel divineIf i had the mind of a poetMine forever you'd beWe'd live a life of sheer blissIn perfect harmony.Just Day dreaming about Someone
