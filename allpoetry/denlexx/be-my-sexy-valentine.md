Be my sexy valentine
Give me love so true
Come girl take my body
And do the things you love to do

Hold me, hug me, and love me
Use them luscious lips
Fry an egg on my hot body
Hell, even make some chips

Give yourself to me all night
We'll rock the world away
I'll kiss every inch of you 
Come on girl lets play.just a little something for the big day :-)
