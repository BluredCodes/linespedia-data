Christmas time is here againAnd Joy is in the airWe hang her up there every yearCos Grandma needs her ChairAll the kids at the DoorSinging songs of cheerIf the little sods don't clear offI'll drown them all in Beer!!The Snow is falling on the GroundIts got no place else to goAnd little Robin RedbreastPopped in to say helloThe Turkey's in the ovenAnd the Fairy's on the TreeThe Cat chewed on the Fairy lights'He just flew right passed me'Have a very Happy Christmas Drink loads and loads of BeerHope you all have a happy timeAnd see you all next Year.just a funny little poem that came into my head ..hope you like it
