I Wonder does that spidercrawling on the ceiling thereDoes he have any worriesDoes he have a careDoes he worry about the universeand all the stars aboveDoes he worry about his Wife and kidsDoes he ever think of love As he spins is little web of lifeDoes he worry like we doWell if he does he'd better runCos I'm coming with my shoe.
