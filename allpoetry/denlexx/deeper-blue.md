Footprints on a sandy beach
Leading out of the sea
You're in my dreams every night
Every night you come to me

Your hair so filled with water
And skin so cold and blue
You cry why did you murder me
I loved and cherished you

I wake up all in a sweat
My bed is all wet through
I feel so cold and lonely now
"Why did i murder you"

And then i start to remember
The bitch that you'd become
You left me for another
The thing had to be done

But now my dreams are filled with you
You walk my room at night
I call and try to hold you 
But you fade right out of sight

Then in the morning when i awake
Thoughts of you still fill my head
As i step down on to the carpet
My mind is filled with dread
I see a set of sandy footprints
Leading from the bed.this is part two of a poem i wrote called deep blue
