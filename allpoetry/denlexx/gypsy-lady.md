Gypsy lady of my dreams 
Rock my world tonight
Put your arms around me
Come and light my light

Let me hear you whisper
Sweet nothings in my ear
Show me all your magic
You Gypsy's hold so dear

Let me rock your Caravan
All night until the dawn
Then my Gypsy lady
I'll awake and find you gonejust a dream
