The Soul Hunter came by Today
And stole my Soul away
He walked right by, and Ripped it out!
The Day you walked away.

He keeps them in Glass Orbs you know
Never lets them out to Play
But you can collect it any Time
If you come back to Stay.

He keeps the Soul's in a big Dark Room
Never seeing the Light of Day
Some he'll keep Forever 
And they'll just Fade away.

So Please come by and pick mine up
He'll give it you for Free
But only on the Condition
That you'll forever stay with Mei was watching babylon 5 when i thought of this one
