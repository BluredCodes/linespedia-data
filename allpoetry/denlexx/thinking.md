I sit here missing you so much
My Heart it aches and i wonder
If your missing me like i miss you
Or is it all a big blunder
You say that I'm your only love
That without me you'd be lonely
But why are you not here with me now  
If  I'm your one and only
If  I'm the one you think of most
And can not live with out
Why don't you get your self right here
So we could love and laugh and cry
And i would have no doubt.
