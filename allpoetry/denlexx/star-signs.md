Do you read your star signs
In the papers every day
They tell me I'm a Pisces
And Neptune rules my way

There's lots of new words everyday
To help me run my life
They say get a new job
And keep an eye on your wife

Well, their maybe something in it
Lots of people love the stuff
Me, I love to read it 
I can't get enough
