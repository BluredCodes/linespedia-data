I wish I could write a poem
That would stand above the rest
Bring happiness to everyone
And make everyone feel blessed

Its words would end world poverty
And make all men love his brother
We'd all make lots of love not war
And all hug oneanother

It would make all men be equal
There'd be no race creed or colour
Just one big world together
Where we got on with each other

Maybe it's just a dream
But I'll keep trying everyday
To write my magic poem
Hope I find it soon I prayI think we all wish we could do this I know I'll keep trying
