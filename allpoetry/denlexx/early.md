I love to get up early
Its a spring and summer thing 
I love to get up early 
To hear the birdies sing 
The air is oh so nice and fresh 
The sun it shines so bright 
And you can walk for hours
And no people are in sight 
The morning dew is on the grass 
It glitters in the sun 
And for just them few small hours
You can be the only one.
