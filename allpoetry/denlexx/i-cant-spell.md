I can't spell for toffee
But I don't give a s***
People, who can, get me down
They just think they are it!

They put big words in there poems
That I can't even read
Like equivocate 
It will turn your eyes to seed

So I just keep things simple
Guess that's the way I am
To get a smile is all I want
Who needs all that Spam?just a little one to get a smile :-)
