God as been on holiday
But he comes back tomorrow
When he sees the state of things
His head will bend in sorrow

In his time, he's been gone a week
And look at all we've done 
He'd only been gone an hour
And some fool invented the gun

He left his son to keep an eye on us
And we nailed him to a tree
When he gets back, he's gonna be mad
I'm glad it wasn't me 

We've ripped up all his garden 
And we've poisoned all his fish
Changed the shape of half his animals
I bet he'll think that's rich

We gota day to get it back
The way it was before 
And if we don't 
Well guess what, 
He'll be showing us the door,thought this one up after watching the news on tv this morning ...made me feel so sad to see the state the world is in ..maybe one day one of us will write a poem to sort it all out ,,so I'll keep trying anyway
