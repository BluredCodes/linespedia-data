Why are we here? The questionThat many men have askedWe been looking for an answerFor many ages pastWherever did we come from?And wherever will we goWe been looking for an answerBut will we ever knowAre we on some kind of journey?I think some of us could beBut I think some are here to teach usHow to set our spirits freeThey are the angels of the earthBut the thing is, they don't knowThey think they are like the rest of usAnd along their way they goI know of a few of themThey help me on my wayThere are some on the InternetThey talk to me each dayI meet some when I walk the dogOr go down to the shopYou never know when you'll find oneWhen you least expect it out they'll popSo when you're feeling lonelyAnd a bit down in the dumpsRemember you have an angel To help you through life's bumpsyep I got a few Angels here too :-)
