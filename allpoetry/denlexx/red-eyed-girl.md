Red Eyed Girl
Lay off the Booze
You're Driving me Insane
Our Love is going down the Drain
"I can take no more Pain"

You're in love with the Bottle
Not in Love with Me!
So take that Bottle up to Bed
Its more your love than Me

If you don't get rid of the Stuff
I'm walking out that Door!!
I'll find myself another to Love
You don't need Me any more.well i've done blue eyes,brown eyes,emerald eyes so i thought i'd do red eyes :-)
