Welcome to my cafe 
Grab yourself a seat
Pick up our friendly menu
And pick yourself a treat

Grab yourself some goodies
To help you through the day
Or just have a cup of tea
"Don't forget to take a tray"

If you can't see what you want
Than give Derrick a call
He'll find you something tasty
So come in, have a ballJust a little poem I did for my Brother Derrick He is starting up his own cafe and is going to put lots of my poems around the walls of his cafe for people to read, sure hope it gets people in not keep them out..lol
