I'm addicted to a Love Song
It just won't go Away
Float's around and around 
Inside my Head
And stays most of the Day.

It was the Song
That you and Me
Picked for our very Own
It made me smile when you sang It
You have a lovely Tone

Since you walked out
That song as been 
My one and only Friend
I'll love that song as i love you
Until the bitter Endeveryone as one i guess
