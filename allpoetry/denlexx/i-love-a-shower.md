I love a shower in the morning
It sets me for the day
All that lovely soapy water
And that red hot rainy spray

It wakes you up pretty dang quick
When you first turn it on
It makes you jump out of your skin
Good job it don't last long

Then you feel all nice and fresh
And really fighting fit
Ready to go into the world
