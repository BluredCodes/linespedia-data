I Wish  you all a Merry Christmas
Whether you be Friend or foe
I wish you all a happy time
Where ever you may go
My hope for you,is a Happy time
With loads, and loads to eat
And dear Father Christmas
Gives you all a Christmas treat.
Merry Christmas to all my friends 
and Foes here on AllPoetry.comjust my way of saying MERRY CHRISTMAS :-)
