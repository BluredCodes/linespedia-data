Thirty thousand light Years
I've been in outer space
Thirty thousand light Years
I came to gaze, upon your Face
Of all the Humans on this Earth
I picked you to Love
And now I've come to take you
Back to the Stars above.

For Thirty thousand light Years
I've waited for this Day
And now I'll take you Home with Me
To my Planet far away.

I spied you one Day long ago
With my one big bulging Eye!
You looked so sweet and tasty
I thought that i would Die.

My very large brain was Bursting
With the thought of having You
So i got into my Spaceship 
And off to Earth i Flew.

I hope you like my Family
As i know, that they'll Love You!!
Cos when its Winter on our Planet
We all Love Earthling stew.
