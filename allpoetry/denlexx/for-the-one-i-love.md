title: "For The One I Love"

If i could reach across the SeaAnd put you hand in mineThen you could be my only loveAnd that would be divineWe'd have no bonds to hold us backOur love would be set freeThen you would be the one for meand we'd forever beBut ocean's are between usthe void it is so wideI sit alone and wonder If we'll ever turn the tideThen i turn on my computerand the miles they melt awayAnd your sitting here beside meAnd we talk the time awayJust for them few small hoursWe are in each others arm'sI'm holding you so tightlyI can feel you in my arm'sI can feel your lips upon meYour body close to mineYou'll be my love foreverUntil the end of time.
