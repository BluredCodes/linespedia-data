We are the Children of the Sun
Born of her firey Glow
We carry Her within Us
Each spark of life we Know.

Long ago She threw us forth
To live upon this Earth
She put the fire within Us
To give us all Rebirth.

One day Her Children she'll Recall
Back to her firey Breath
And we shall all be no More
Long live the flame of Death.
