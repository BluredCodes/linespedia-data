As I sat under the stars
On a warm August night
She appeared as if by magic
In a big ball of light

She looked so stunningly beautiful
As she stood there in the glow
Her bright red hair shone so bright
And her skin as white as snow

She spoke to me so softly
With a voice that felt like silk
She melted right into my arms
Before I had time to think

She kissed me with a passion
I'd never felt before
And with my arms around her
We slid slowly to the floor

We rolled around together
In loves passionate inbrace
She set my soul on fire
And set my heart to race

Who was this wonder woman?
Kept flowing through my brain
Is she real, or am I dreaming
Will I wake up insane?

All at once a blinding light
Flashed as bright as day
And I was left in the dark
