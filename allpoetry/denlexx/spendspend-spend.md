Boy how I hate Shopping!!
It drives me around the Bend
The Wife she loves to do It
She'll spend,spend,spend

She'll walk my poor feet Off
As she flies from shop to Shop
If she didn't run out of Money
I think she'd never Stop

She loves to get the Bog Offs
That's buy one get one Free
Nowonder I'm putting weight On
She's always feeding Me

I guess woman love their Shopping
It gives them such a Thrill 
I just get an Headache 
It makes me take a Pillthursday is our shopping day and boy do i love to shop!!
