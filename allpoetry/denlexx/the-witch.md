Upon my Magic Broomstick
I fly all through the Night
I work my Wicked Magic,
When people sleep so tight
I slip in through there keyholes
While they are in their Slumber
And put spots upon their Faces
'how i love that little Number
I turn their Milk to Butter
And i make their Cheese turn Blue
But if you ever tell a Soul
I'm coming after you!!
I love to make there dogs bark
As I'm flying down the street
Ho yes!! and put sharp things
On the Bedroom floor
So people hurt there feet
But if you ever tell a Soul
I'm coming after you!!
I'll turn you into a big fat Toad
and you'll end up in the Zoo.
