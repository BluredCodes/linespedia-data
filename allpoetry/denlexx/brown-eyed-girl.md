Brown Eyed Girl 
With Olive Skin
You set my Heart Aflame
For just one Kiss
I'd be in Bliss
Your Love I long to Tame.

With Skin as Soft, as warm Silk
I long to Hold you Tight
To stay with You until the Dawn
Would fill Me with Delight.

So please will You be mine Alone
Love no one else but Me
And I will love you all my Life
Together we'll always be.for all the brown eyed girls xxx
