How I love my star gate
Good old SG1
They fly the wormhole
Every week 
And boy do they have fun

O'Neill now he's a funny guy
But keeps them all in trim
Teal'c now he's a strange one
Better keep away from him

Daniel Jackson he's brainy one
Knows the answers to it all
Now Carter she's some lady
With brains and lots of Gaul

They beat the bad guys every week 
And save the planet too
What the world would do without them
