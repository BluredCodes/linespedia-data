I awoke one morning with a fright
Someone had killed me in the night
As I sat bolt upright in my bed 
My body stayed there it was dead

It gave me one hell of a shock
To see me lying there 
With a big knife sticking out of my chest
And an Hachette parting my hair

Who had done this terrible thing?
While I had slept so snug 
Was it my lover's husband? 
Or maybe some other thug 

My lover was nowhere to be found
Why had she ran away
Had my darling been murdered too
This I could not say

Just then voices I did hear
Coming from below	
Was that my darling's voice i heard
Talking to my brother Joe

"Are you sure that he's dead
I'll go get the car
Help me put him in the boot
The rivers not that far"

Then the truth it sank right in
My brother did the deed
They are after father's money
They both did this for greed


But why am I still here on earth
Why am I not at rest?
Maybe I have to right this wrong
Before I am god's guestWell you never know there might be a part two ...lol
