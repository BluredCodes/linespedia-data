title: "Our Boy"

When I looked into your eyes On the first day of your lifeYour little face it looked so sweet Laying there next to my wifeWe took you home within a weekAnd loved you like no otherYou where our sweet and loving boy We didn't need no otherI tried to be your loving dad And also be your brother But looking back with insight now I wish we'd had anotherCos two like you the world could doA lot more with then oneWe thank god every day for you our ever loving son.
