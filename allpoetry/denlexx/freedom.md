The stars twinkle on my ceiling
The ground is my bedroom floor
A snowfall is my blanket
And the world is my front door

The open road is my garden path
And the wide-open plains are my lawns
The forests are my flowering plants
And my home is wherever I roam

The people I meet are my family
Animals and birds are my pets
I'm free as the wind on a summer day
My life is as good as it gets.just having a daydream
