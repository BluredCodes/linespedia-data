I walked the night 
My mind a mess
Wherever did she go?
She vanished! 
In a flash of light
And I sill love her so

Her lovemaking 
Played on my mind
She was out of this world
The way she kissed me 
Lingered still
I wanted more and more

Day after day I waited
For my darling to appear
Then in a flash she was back
Her magic drew me near

She pulled me into the light
Its brightness burnt my eyes
I had this floating feeling
Like I was flying in the sky

When at last I opened them
A smile beamed on my face
My sweet darling lay there
She was covered in white lace

She lay upon a golden bed
I walked over by her side
She said come Den 
Please make love to me 
In a flash I was by her side

She made love like a frantic beast
We had a wild exciting time
She said my love please stay forever
I said, boy is that the time!

Then suddenly it was all over 
She had vanished once again
Was it something I had said?
