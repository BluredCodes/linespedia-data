My computer told me to kill my wife
I heard it plain as day
It just came out and told me
Said do it!!,right away

It said she's seeing another guy
Even showed me who
It's our god dam milkman
How could she be untrue?

My computer had been spying
While I was out at work
Took a video with the webcam
I think I'll kill that jurk

My computer just said,kill um both!!
That's just what i will do
Computer can you tell me how
Your my friend,and I love you!!.and before you all start on me ,,lol its only a poem ..mind you my wife don't mind me using the computer no more :-)
