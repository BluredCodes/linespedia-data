Neighbors are funny thingsI got some next to me. You can hear them very often When they go to have a pee And i wonder very often If they can also hear me They will talk to you sometimes,And then they'll pass you byWith there faces turned awayAnd there noses in the sky. But if they want to borrow Then its a different thing They will end up at your door step With a big fat smiling grin.
