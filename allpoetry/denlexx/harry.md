I love the way your eyes light up 
When I say I love you 
I love the way you run around
When I say fetch my shoe 
I love the way you wag your tail
When I am feeling blue
But most of all I love the way
You seem to love me to 
Your big brown eyes
Light up my skies 
Dear harry I love you.
