I met her on a winter night
She sat crying in the snow
I asked her what the trouble was
She said her car wont go

I said well it's your lucky day
Let me see what i can do
So i lifted the bonnet 
And out a big bat flew

It sank its teeth into my neck
I fell back to the floor
Screaming get it off me
But me she did ignore

I gripped it hard and pulled away
At last it lost its grip
It flew off into the night
To me it gave the slip

I turned to see the woman
Who had been crying in the snow?
But she was no where to be found
Where ever did she go?
