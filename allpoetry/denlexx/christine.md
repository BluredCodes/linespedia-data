Christine,Christine with eyes of Green
The Prettiest girl I ever Seen
You make my toes curl when you Smile
For you I'd run the Three Minute Mile

I'll come to call on you one Day
And if you Love me i will Stay
Wrapped in your Arms for ever more
Because its you that I Adore

The love we'll have will be Complete
With no one else you can Compete
I'll love you till my dying Day
If you will let me come to Staypoem for a dear friend,who likes my love poems so i thought i'd do one just for her ..just for you chris xx
