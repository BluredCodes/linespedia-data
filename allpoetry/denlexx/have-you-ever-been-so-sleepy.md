Have you ever been so sleepy?
Yet you can't get off to sleep
And no matter how you try
You just can't get a peep

You twist and you turn
And your bed is, one big mess
You're feeling so uptight
And your mind is filled with stress

You try counting sheep
But it don't do any good
As the god dang things
Won't jump when they should

So you go down, make a cuppa
And watch some late TV
In a flash you're off to sleep
And snoring like a beeTry as I might I could not get off to sleep last night so this Little thing popped into my head ..lol
