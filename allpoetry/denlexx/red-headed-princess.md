Red headed princess of my dreams 
Please call on me tonight
Wrap your soul around my all
And play with me tonight

Tantalise my taste buds 
With your luscious lips 
Hold me in your tender thighs
Let's really get to grips

Melt your body into mine
Let's become as one
Hold me tight 
All through the night
Until your job is donefor my red haired princess
