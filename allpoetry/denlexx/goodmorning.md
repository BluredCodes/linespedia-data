Goodmorning,Goodmorning 
I just popped in to say
Not many days left
Before its Christmas day.

Have you cleaned your Chimney
So Santa can come down
As mum made some cookies
He likes them nice and Brown.

Don't forget his Whisky
He needs it to keep warm
As he takes your toys around
Through the winter stormjust a little christmas poem for the season
