I know an Indian Princess, 
She lives here on the net, 
She found me one Day long ago, 
And She, I'll never forget, 
The Princess name is Dear Heart
And we chat most every day, 
I think it would break my heart, 
If She ever went away, 
She is a AWESOME person, 
With a smile to warm your Heart, 
And now that i have found her, 
I know we'll never part, 
She may be on the other side of the world, 
But She will always live in my heart.
