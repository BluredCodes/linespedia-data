Happy mothers day dear wife 
You are the lover of my life
You make me happy 
All year through 
That's why I'm so in love with you 

You gave me a son some years ago
He loves us both, and lets it show
You are the apple of our eyes
We both love you and your big green eyes

Today is the day we can show it's true
For today was made just for mothers like you
Your Andy and ME's one and only
We'll keep you safe and you'll never be lonelyfor my dear wife as it's mothers day here in WALES tomorrow
