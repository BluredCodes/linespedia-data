In the deep dark dungeons 
Of my mind i keep him locked away
Never will I let him out
There he will forever stay

In my younger days he ruled
He lived, he laughed, he cried
He thought of no one but himself
But in his heart he died

He craved the love of others
But never gave it back
He took it all and saved it
In is mind, right at the back

Then one day I came along 
We had an almighty fight
I beat him and I locked him up
Now he's forever out of sight 

I set free all the love he had
Locked up inside his head
Now I am a better man
And him, I hope he's deadjust thinking back I guess
