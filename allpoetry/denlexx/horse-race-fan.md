My dad he is a horse race fan 
He loves to have a bet
He'd love to win some money
But he hasn't done it yet

I put his bets on every week
But his luck, it runs away
If it ran as fast as his horses
He'd win money every day

But he'll keep betting every week
He loves his bit of a flutter
Mam thinks he wastes his money
