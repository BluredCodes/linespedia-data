Rain, rain go away
That's all we are getting every day
From the sky it falls and falls
Soon be coming through the walls

Will it ever stop we cry
Makes you wish that you could die
Not even the poor birds can fly
The rain fills every inch of sky

I'm sure it's got  to stop real soon
Even at night you can't see the moon
Wait a min the sun is out
I'm off out to scream and shout

Boy this sun is hot today
We need some rain to cool the day
Phew wish that sun would go away
It's to dang hot out here today .....it's all we are getting here in wales ..lol
