Let me be your hero
Let me kiss away your pain
Let me walk beside you
Let me hide you from the rain

I will keep you safe
Here in my arms
Now and forever more
You are the one I live for
I'll be yours forever more

We'll grow old together
In our home down by the lake
I'll make you tea and coco
Rub your shoulders when they ache

I'll even find your glasses
When it's time to watch tv
And lay your head upon my pillow
If your hero I can be
 
Just say the magic words
I'll be yours tonight
Stay with you forever
And be your guiding light
