I sit here in this padded room
Please, please let me out
She's coming here to get me
Of that i have no doubt

The dreams are getting stronger now
She haunts me through the day
I have to go and be with her 
I'll never get away

In her ocean grave she lays
She calls each day to me
Soon, soon my love I'll be with you
If they would set me free

They say I have to pay the price
For killing you that day
But soon my love
I'll be with you
And in our grave we'll lie 

I feel the water coming
I feel your cold wet hands
Your cold dead lips are on me
