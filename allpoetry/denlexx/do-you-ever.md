Do you ever think of me?
Like i think of you
Do you ever wonder? 
If I'm feeling blue

Does your heartache
Just like mine
Are you being true?
Do you ever wonder?
If I'm true to you

Do you sit alone at night?
And crave for company 
Or have you found another 
To take the place of me

Will you be there for me?
Like I'll be there for you
Please please, tell me
Am I still your boy in blue?
