Beneath the cold icy blue Ocean
My lover waits for Me
I put her there long Ago
She wanted to be Free

She left me for Another
One cold winter Day
So i went off to find Her
For to beg her not to Stray

But she had found Another
To love her like i do
So i dragged her to the Ocean
Where she lays deep cold and Blue

One day we'll be Together 
Deep below the icy Waves
Laying side by side Together
In our deep blue icy Gravesi was just thinking about the sea :-)
