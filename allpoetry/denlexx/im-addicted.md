I'm addicted to AllPoetry 
I can't seem to stay Away
I pop in and out of Here
Twenty times a Day

They are all such a happy Bunch
With loads of things to Say
And you can Write a Poem
To pass the time Away

You get a lovely Welcome
So just log right on In
Just give yourself a Nick Name
And your Ready to BeginJust a little something i thought up while i was having a cup of coffee ...lol
