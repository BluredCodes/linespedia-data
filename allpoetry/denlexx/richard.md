My brother Richard got MarriedHe never told a soul He as good as told his FamilyTo crawl back in their HoleHe was his Mothers pride and joyBut now he's just a 'shit'He broke is Mothers little HeartThe two faced little gitWhy he did it,  we may never knowThe true reason whyBut when we all get our hands on himHe's gonna wish that he could DieThe thing  that hurt us most of allIs that the brides family wentBut none of us was invitedNot a single card was sentHe just went off on HolidayThen gave poor mam a ringTo say him and his girlfriendHad gone and tired the stringPoor Mam she nearly hit the floorGood job her heart his strongTo do a thing like that to Mam is very,very wrongNow Richard if you read this rhymeI hope it hurts you deepCos all your brothers and sistersThink you are a creep.
