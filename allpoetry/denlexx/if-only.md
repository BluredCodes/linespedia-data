title: "If only"

As I lay here in my bed 
My mind flies to you 
I think of all the things you've said 
And things I'd love to do 
I'd love to take you in my arms 
And kiss those lips so sweet 
And put you on a pedestal 
With roses at your feet 
I'd give you love beyond your dreams 
That no man could compare 
If I where yours and you where mine 
God, why can't life be fair!!
