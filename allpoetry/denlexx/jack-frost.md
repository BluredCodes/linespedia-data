I met Jack Frost this Morning
As i got out of Bed
He sent a tingle through my Toes
That went right to my Head.

I got real Cold all over
As old Frosty gave me a Lick!
So i shoved him out the Window
With my Granny's walking Stick.
  
Just has i did the Sun came up
And melted Him away
But He'll be back Tomorrow
This I promise, to be true!
Keep Granny's walking stick handy
Cos he's coming after You.my toes are cold when i get out of bed in the morning ...lol
