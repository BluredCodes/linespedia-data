My Wife she loves Her Bingo 
She plays it all she can
I used to think at one Time
She had another Man.

She never wins that often
So it doesn't pays no Bills
But it keeps her very Happy
And she never takes no Pills.

One Day she said
I'll come running Home
With a big fat Wad of Cash
And with you and our son Andy
Off on Holiday we'll Dash.

Well one Day she may do it
This you can never tell
She never misses Numbers
And she shouts BINGO! like Hellyep she loves her bingo and it gives me loads of time to write poems and talk to you lot ...lol
