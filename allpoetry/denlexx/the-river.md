I feel I'm on the river 
Of no return
On a boat
That's going 
Like a train

If I don't jump soon
I'll never get off
I just gota free 
Some of this pain

Just need a little time
All to myself
Need to be
Who I gota be

Who he is I don't know
But I gota find out
In the end
I just want to be me.just me doing a bit of deep thinking
