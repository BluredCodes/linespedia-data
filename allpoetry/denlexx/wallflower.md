She's a beautiful rose in a garden
Who won't come out to play?
She thinks she's a wallflower
So she hides herself away

Little dickey rosebud 
Would love her to come out to play
But she just hides in the corner 
Turning her face away

She as such lovely petals
Lovely red tinged with blue
But she just hides in the corner
Hiding her face from view

Wallflower she will forever be
If she don't at least take a chance
She seen dickey rosebud give her a smile
So come on girl, smile back just oncethis is for highland dreams who thinks she is a wallflower ...after seeing her photo if she's a wallflower I want a bunch ..lol
