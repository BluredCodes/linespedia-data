title: "Blink of an Eye"

We are happy souls
That sail  the world
Looking for a friend
Our ship is a computer
The sea the Internet

We can call in at any port
In the blink of an eye
Be back home for tea
In a flash
And not a day gone by

We can be with friends and family
No matter where they roam
And no matter where they go
They can always be at home.
