With your Emerald Eyes you look so Sweet
You send Me into Bliss
I'd give my very Life to You
If I could get one Kiss.

Your bright Red Hair
Flows down your Back
Like a River in full Flood
It sends a tingle through my Heart
And warms my very Blood.

If you would only come to Me
And set my soul Aflame
We'd have a stolen night of Love
"My heart is yours to Tame."This one is for you Lurie xx
