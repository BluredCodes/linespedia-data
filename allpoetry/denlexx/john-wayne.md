I Grew up with John Wayne
Upon the Silver Screen
He was the Greatest Hero
My World had ever Seen.

He always beat the Bad guy's
And always used Fair Play
The Bad guy's always had
Black Hat's
And never got Away.

These days Upon the Silver Screen
You never know Who's, Who!
The Bad guy's are the Good guy's
Then change places half way through.

Bring back the Good old Cowboy
Who always Won the Day!
He got the Girl,right at The End
Then slowly rode Away.
( Into the Sunset)
Remember the Alamo
And True Grit
Fill your Hands you Son of a Bitch!!what can i say, he was the best.
