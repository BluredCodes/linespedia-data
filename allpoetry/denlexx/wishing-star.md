I have a little wishing Star
One I call my very Own
I wish upon It Sometimes 
When I'm all Alone

Some times I go in dead of Night
Just to look up at my Star
Shining there so Brightly
So near,yet so Far

Once It was your Star Too
Before you went Away
I wish my Star could bring you Back
Maybe it will one Day

Until that Day I'll wish Alone
Upon my Star so Bright
To bring you back into my Arms
" Star grant my wish Tonight"anybody got one :-)
