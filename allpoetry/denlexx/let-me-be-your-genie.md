Let me be your genie in a bottle
Let me be your one and only love
Let me be the one to make your wishes come true
Let me be the one when your feeling blue
Let me be the one to make your smile shine through
Let me be the one to make you happy when your sad 
let me be the one to make you good when your bad
Let me be the one to pick you up when your down
Let me be the one to make you smile when you frown
Let me be the one to make you happy all your life
Let me be the one that's gona be your wife.
