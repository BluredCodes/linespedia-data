Here i am laying in Bed
Took some pain killers
For my Head.
Boy i sure feel half dead
Atishoo! Atishoo! oh my Head

Oh my god i got the Flu
Don't know what I'm gonna do
My bones are aching 
All way through.
Hang on I'm going to ..Atishoo!

Been a week now, feel much better
Head and eyes are back together
At last that flu as gone away
Think i might go out Today.my poor wifes got the flu so this just sort of popped up ...lol
