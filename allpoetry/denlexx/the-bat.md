I fly the skies
At dead of night
And I am feared by all
My screech!
Can make your hair stand up
Weather you be short or tall
Blood sucking vampire
I've been called 
But me, I'm just a bat
I catch insects, on the wing
And eat them just like that
So if you see me
In the night
Don't cringe and run away
Think of all the good I do
Keeping the bugs at bay
