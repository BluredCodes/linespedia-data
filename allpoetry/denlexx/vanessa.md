Thirty years its been today 
Since we said that we do 
And I can truly say with love 
That I have loved you to 
In all them years the happy times 
Have far out weighed the blue 
And I can say with all my heart 
I'm still in love with you 
You make my days so full of fun 
my nights so full of love 
We'll be together ever more 
My one and only love 
Yours for always Denny.
