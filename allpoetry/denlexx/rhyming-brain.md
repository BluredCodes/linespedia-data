Since I started writing poetry
I have a rhyming brain
Keep thinking what word goes with what
It's driving me insane

And when i go to bed at night
Sometimes I can't sleep
I start counting poems
Instead of counting sheep

This poetry as got me
Well and truly hooked
Is it possible one day?
I could even write a bookwith thanks to Lurie for her help ...xx
