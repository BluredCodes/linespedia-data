I wish that I'd been taught to Hug
When i was just a Kid
The trouble was in our House
Its not a thing we did.

We always kissed our mam goodbye
As we went off to School
But Dad just stood there looking On
It made me feel a Fool 

I wish i could have got Myself
Just to Hug him Once
Put my arm around him
And just say that feels Nice

But no i never, did It
And now that i am Old!
My son never does it to Me
"It makes me feel so Cold"just something that i think about now and again maybe i'll just grab hold of dad when i go over and see him on christmas day.
