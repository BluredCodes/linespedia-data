I Love to Sleep in the Nuddy
Its the only way to Sleep
If i put my Pyjamas on 
Up my legs they creep
Its nice and Cool in the Summer
Cos the Air can flow around your bits
And for a woman its even Better 
As its nice and Cool for Her Toes
