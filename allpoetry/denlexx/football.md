Football, football, football
It drives me around the bend
Grown men kick a ball around
And run from end to end

The crowd only go for punch-ups
Then watch it on TV
Well that's the way I see it
But then, I'm only me

Some people seem to love it
They'll sit and watch all day
You'd think they'd like to play it
But ask, they'll say NO WAY! 
 
Give me a game of rugby
Now that's the game to play
The fighting's always on the field
Not in the crowed " hooray"

We have good old singsong
Us welsh we love to sing
And when we beat old England
You can hear the church bells ringhope I don't upset any football fans with this one ...lol
