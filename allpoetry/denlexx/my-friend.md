I have a friend who follows me
Everywhere I go
Sometimes he walks beside me
Sometimes he walks below

I never see him in the dark
I've no idea where he goes
But he's always there in the morning
Stuck to the end of my toes

When the sun goes down he vanishes
When it comes up he's back
I tried to catch him one day
And put him in a sack

If I could get my hands on him
Maybe I'd stand a chance
But I can't get hold of him
He leads me such a danceI Wonder who he is ?
