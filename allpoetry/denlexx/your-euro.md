We don't want your Euro
We want pounds and Pence
Come on now Tony!!
Get off that bloody Fence

We got ripped off last Time
So we say, No More!!
Take your bloody Euro
And sling it out the Door.just adding my little bit to get rid of it :-) or stop us getting it i should say ..lol
