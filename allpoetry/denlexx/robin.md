I found a little robin
I called him little tweet
And when i took him to the park
He played down at my feet
He was such a friendly little bird
He'd hop around all day
But if i tried to pick him up 
He'd up and fly away
One day a big black tom cat
Came walking in the park
He killed my little robin
And made my life so dark
Now when i go out walking 
And hear a little tweet 
I think about my robin 
Running around my feet.
One day I'm going to find that cat
Who killed my little bird
And put a bell around his neck 
So he can then be heard
so no more little robin's 
will die like my poor bird
