Here I sit inside this cage
They'll never let me out
They say I am a killer
Of that I have no doubt

I just needed the money
And he got in the way
So i pumped him full of bullets
So on the floor he'd stay

But as I Rushed out of the bank
With bags filled up with cash
The sheriff was waiting real close by
So for my horse, I made a dash

I got a deputy in the leg 
Before they all jumped me
 Lashing out with all I had 
I wanted to be free

But no luck they had me down
And off to jail i went
Tomorrow I'm up before the judge
I sure hope he's bent

I've little chance of getting out
No doubt,I'll never be free
They'll take me out tomorrow 
And hang me from a treejust thinking about a way of getting hold of some money ..but not this way i guess ...lol
