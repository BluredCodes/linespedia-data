---
title: "I'm your Friend"
writer: "denlexx"

I'm your friend, and i will stand by you
I'm your friend, even when your feeling blue
I'm your friend, when your in the stew
I'm your friend and I'll see you through
I'm your friend, and I'll stick like glue
I'm your friend, and your my friend too.
