Over a million stars in the sky
And I picked you to Love
God sent you down from Heaven
 Into my arms to Love

He put me here to care for You
And take you for my Own
To be my Queen,and Marry Me
And sit upon my Throne

To be my little Honey Bee
And love me like no Other
I'd do all of this and more for You
If you would leave your Mother!!.just a little romantic one for the ladies in my life :-) xx
