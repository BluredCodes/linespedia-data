As i look up at the MoonThat shines so bright aboveAnd the Stars that shineIn the Night skyMy mind it turns to loveI see you standing next to meWith your big bright eyes of blueAnd your lips so red in the moonlightThat's been put there just for youYou turn to me,look into my eyesAnd whisper word's so sweetI put my lips right next to yoursAnd we kiss as they meetWe lay their in the moonlightTwo lovers with out a careThen i think dan i wish I'd brought my coat the grass is wet down here.
