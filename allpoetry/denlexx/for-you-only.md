For a million years or more I'd sayOur love will stay aroundIt will still be here on the InternetWhere bits and bytes aboundMy love for you will never dieIn my heart or cyber spaceAnd when they go to my dot com They can gaze upon your face.There they'll see the love I sawWhen I first looked at youYour big bright eyes your loving smileAnd they will love you toBut they'll never know the girl I knewThe one you kept insideThe funny,happy,sad oneThat I wanted by my side.So I put you here in cyber space For all the world to seeSo they will know the love we hadAnd how happy you made me.
