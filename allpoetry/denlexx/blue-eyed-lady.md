Blue Eyed Lady of my Dreams
Please come to Me Tonight
Put your Arms around Me
And hold Me, "Oh so Tight".

Put your Sweet Lips close to Mine
We'll kiss the Night Away
Till Morning comes, 
To Still our Love,
And I Curse the Light of Day.for a blue eyed lady who also loves my love poems
