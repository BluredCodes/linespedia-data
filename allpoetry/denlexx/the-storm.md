There's a storm on the way
So the weatherman said
I heard it on TV 
And it fills me with dread

The rivers gonna rise
And the winds gonna blow
How we gonna manage
I sure don't know

We need to get away 
Run to higher ground
If we don't go soon
We are all gonna drown

It's the same every year
And nothing gets done
Living on a floodplain
Sure is no fun.we got some real bad weather here in wales
