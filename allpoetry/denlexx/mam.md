I have a place here in my heart 
That i keep just for you 
Its a loving, cosy warm place 
I go to when I'm blue 
I think of all the happy times
I had when i was young 
And how we played together
All happy in the sun 
I remember sitting on your lap
And the stories you would tell 
And the songs you sang at bedtime 
your voice rang like a bell 
Yes Mam i will remember
All the loving ways you had 
You'll always be here in my heart 
In here right next to Dad.
