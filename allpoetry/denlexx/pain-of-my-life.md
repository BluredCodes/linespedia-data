My wife, my wife
Pain of my life
She nags the whole day through
Do this, do that
She's a right old bat
But I still love her true

She nags about the garden
She nags about the shed
She nags me in the morning
To get me out of bed

One day I'll stop her nagging
I'll buy myself a gun
And blow her freaking head off
Then her nagging will be doneBoy am i in trouble for this one ...lol but she was really getting on my wick this morning
