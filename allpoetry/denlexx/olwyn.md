I have an aunt called Olwyn,She's like my second Mam.And when I was very young She wheeled me in my pramHas I grew up I stayed with Her And never would go Home.She made me feel so happy So I called her house my own.If I was feeling poorly She'd write me notes for SchoolBut only if I was really bad,Cos her I could not fool.'Well not all the time anyway'.
