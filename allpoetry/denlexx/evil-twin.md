I have an Evil Twin
That lives inside of Me
He only comes out at parties
When I've had two or Three

He is a nasty piece of work
Or so i have been told!
He'll pick a fight with Anyone
And use his tongue to Scold

Can't say I've ever met Him
But my wife she hates his Guts
The family think he's around the Bend
But i think they are all Nutsi used to be a very bad boy in my younger days ;-(
