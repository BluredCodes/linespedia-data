When I was young 
I had a dream
To sail the ocean blue
Buy myself a great big boat
And sail off with my crew

Find a sun-drenched island
Off the Caribbean coast
Lay they're in the sunshine 
With the one I loved the most

Sun all day, love all night
It seemed the thing to do 
Yes stay on that lovely island 
With you and my all woman crewjust a nice little dream I used to have in my younger days ..lol
