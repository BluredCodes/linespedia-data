title: "Love you"
writer: "denlexx"

I'm your knight in shining ArmourI will love you all my daysThough we can never be togetherIn my heart you'll be alwaysYou will always be beside meTill the last breath, I ever takeThen I'll come and lay beside youTill you meet me when i wake.
