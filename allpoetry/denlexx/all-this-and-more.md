If I could catch some falling Stars
I'd give them all to you
For you to hold in your hand's
To make all your Dreams come true
I'd follow every Rainbow
To find the pot of Gold
And then I'd give it all to you 
To make your dreams unfold.

Four leaf clovers i will get
In buckets by the ton
And loads of lucky leprechaun's
To fill your life with fun

If you would only Love me
I'd give you this and more
All i ask is one sweet kiss
Its you that i adore.
