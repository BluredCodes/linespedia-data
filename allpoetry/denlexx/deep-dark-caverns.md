In the deep dark caverns 
Of your love
I sit and wait for you
But you just walk right through me
I'm never seen by you

We pass each other every day
But I'm just a ship in the night
You never see me standing there
Holding for you a light

I talk to you most every day
But you never talk, to me
Your picture haunts my every dream
Will I ever be set free?

One day will you return my love?
And set my sprit free
So we two,  can be as one
Please fall in love with me
