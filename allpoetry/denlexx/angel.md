I am your very own angel
And i look after you. 
I'll make you happy when your sad
And smile when you are blue 
I'll keep watch right by your bed
All night until the dawn
And then I'll walk beside you
To keep you free from harm
I'll make sure no one hurts you
Around you I will stay,
We'll walk through life together
I'll be with you all the way 
And when your time is over
And your need for me is through
I will guide you up to heaven
And you'll be an angel too.
