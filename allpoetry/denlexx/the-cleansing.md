For half a million Years
I lay beneath the Earth
Underneath a Mountain
Awaiting my Rebirth

I know not who i truly Am
Nor what i have to Do
All i know is a feeling
That i am over Due

I know not of my Purpose
But i know it must be done
They put me Here
Beneath the Earth
Before time had Began

If nothing happens, very soon
All life on earth is Through
I have to cleanse the Planet
"yes that's what i must do"

There is a virus on the Earth
this virus is called MAN!!
I have to wipe this virus Out
As quickly as i Can

I am awake,I must begin
The cleansing as Begun!!don't ask me where his came from i only write them ...i had a bad night O.k
