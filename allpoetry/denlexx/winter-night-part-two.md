I'm laying here not feeling well 
Got bitten by a bat
Last time I stop and help someone
If they fly away like that

I got this crazy feeling
I should be hanging upside down
My teeth have gone all pointed
And my skin is turning brown

I got this urge to drink some blood
But they got none in the shop
Think I'll fly to Wal-Mart 
Dang these feelings got to stop!

Boy it's getting very warm
The sun is on the rise
Think I'd better find a cave
And maybe eat some flies

The moon is very full tonight
Got this feeling I must eat
There's a woman with a car down there
