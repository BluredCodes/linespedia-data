The telephone, the telephone 
A wonderful device
If my family would only use it
It would be rather nice

If I could get a phone call
Even now and then
Just to say, "how are you"
Or "how's it going Den"

But they never ring me
They say,"you're on the net"
But they could ring my mobile
But they say they forget

Well sod them, that's what I say
If they don't want to call
I got loads of other friends
So they mean bugger all.this might upset the family but sod um they can phone me about it if they don't like it ...lol
