Just some words of Happiness 
To give your day a Smile
To help you through these troubled times 
If only for a while.

Jest remember that i think of You
Each and every Day
And ask the Lord 
To keep you Safe
And help you through each Day

Just remember that i love you
and will always be your Friend
and your troubles, are my troubles
up until the very end
God Bless you.just a little poem for my dear friend annD who as been a friend to me for seems like years well its gota be at least two i think, ann will put me right on that one ..lets just say she got me on the right road thanks ann xx
