My Grandma is an Angel,She lives up high above,She visits me three times a day,And fills my life with love,If it wasn't for that hill she said,I'd come a whole lot more,Mama said she could come live with us,But dad said he's not sure,If dad would only let her stay,Me and mama would be so happy,But dad said its all the cats she's got,And the big dog she calls pappy.
