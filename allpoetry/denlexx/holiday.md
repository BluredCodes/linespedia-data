Here we are on Holiday And its pouring down with rain Here we are on Holiday And we will never come back again Sitting in a caravan having loads of funWhile all the people back at home Are having lots of sun Still we got two more daysTo sit here in the gloom Playing cards and Ludo And looking round the room There's nothing on the telly And the dog is getting bored When poked him with a stick just now I,m sure that he snored.
