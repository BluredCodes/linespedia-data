Be my own sweet valentine
Come and hold me tight
Make mad passionate love to me
In the middle of the night

Hold me in your loving arms 
Never let me go
Put me on a pedestal 
Let my love, grow and grow

Feed me, feed me love so sweet
Fill me to the brim
Drown me in your love tonight
Teach me how to swim

Put your arms arm's around me
Squeeze me, squeeze me tight
Crush me with your magic thighs
Till I scream with delight
