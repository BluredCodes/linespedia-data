When I was a Boy
My Nan and me 
We loved each other Dearly
Over Hills and Fields
We would Roam
Me looking for birds Nests
On the way Home
When we got Home 
I'd make the Tea 
Nan would do Toast 
How happy we would Be
Then one Day she went Away 
Up too the Sky 
I heard them Say
I went out and looked all Day 
But all I Seen 
Was Birds at Play
Now I'm older and i Know 
Where my Nan went so long Ago 
And I will go there to one Day
Make her Tea 
Then we will Play.for my nan who still looks after me
