I am the Mother of you allAnd you treat me like dirtYou cut down my TreesYou pollute my SeasAnd you think that it don't hurtWell one day soon I'll turn on youAnd you will know my ThunderAnd the Earth you digAnd take from meOne day I'll lay you all under
