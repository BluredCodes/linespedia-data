Follow me and I will show you.
Darkness and horror, which you have never seen.
Take my hand and I will guide you.
Through a maze of tunnels tinted green. 
                                                                As I lead you deeper into the unknown
The walls crumbling with decay
Pictures of horror flash before you.
You fall to your knees and pray.
                                                                Follow me and I will show you.
More of the horror within
Along we move and you tremble
As pictures flash full of sin
                                                                You crumble to the floor
Begging to be lead out
This is where I start to fade
As I drift away I hear you shout
                                                                Who are you, and why do you leave me?
When I turn and look at you..
A scream fills the air as you see
I kneel down and whisper in your ear
 I am you, and you are me.
                                                                You wake with tears sliding down your face
As you finally begin to understand
You just took a walk within yourself
Lead by your own hand
                                                                Through those lost and forgotten horrors of your mind.
Full of memories long left to fester and boil
Memories twisted and darkened over time
