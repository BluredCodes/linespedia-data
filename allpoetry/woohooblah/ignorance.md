---
title: "Ignorance"
writer: "Woohooblah"
slug: "ignorance"

We run in circles
Trying to figure things out
By the time we begin to understand
It’s our own kid’s blood flowing in spouts

As we’re looking for answers
Trying to figure out dimensions
Our children slip away
From lack of attention

A ten year old
Still nothing more than a child
Blows away his sister
Its just another problem filed

At this very second
As your reading this line
One more little girl forcefully taken
Left in the corner cryin’

Open your eyes
Stop ignoring these cases
Do something about it
It’s our kid’s lives we’re wasting

It isn’t hopeless
We can’t let it be
These little people
Can set this world free

If we show them now
That the future can be bright
Maybe they wont be so willing
To give up the fight

An extra hug before bed
A few questions about their day
Reading along with them
Going to their school play

All these little things
Mean the world to kids
Shows them there’s hope
Gives them confidence

A few extra words
Few more minutes out of your day
Is that really so much to ask?
Lets try to listen to what they have to say

As you run off to the office
Did you happen to hear?
Your little girl say "I miss you"
Did you see that tear?

Did you hear your son’s cries for help?
In those difficult teen age years
All his questions unanswered
By himself having to face his fears

The last thing he thinks
"I’m not worth it, no one cares"
As he ties the rope around his neck
Last memory is of the time you once shared

Now that he’s gone 
You sit there and cry
Your only son gone forever
With a gag, he said his last good bye
