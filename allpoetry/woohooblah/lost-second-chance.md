---
title: "Lost second chance"
writer: "Woohooblah"
slug: "lost-second-chance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43659-Lost-second-chance-by-Woohooblah"
---

Hiding behind hollow laughter
Mouth smiling with empty eyes
A whirlwind of emotions
Never heard a sigh
All that’s seen on the outside
Nothing but a tangle of lies

Hiding behind gentleness
Listening but not hearing
Struggling with your weaknesses
Your emotions searing

How long can this go on?
This battle that rages inside
Just who are you?
Why do you continue to hide?

Searching for yourself
Your strength that you know is there
Buried beneath the confusion
Pretending you don’t care

Your time is running out
You hear the ticking within
As you wasted time hiding
That beautiful inside, it’s a sin

Who will remember you?
No one will
You wore fakeness
Like the plastic coating of a pill
Now that coating is melting
From the fires burning within
Feelings screaming to be let out
You commit a selfish sin

Too afraid of what is to come
As the blade slides through flesh
Your head begins to spin
You’ve reached the end, this is it
Fear grips you, you’ve messed up
Its too late now, you’ve dug your own pit

As your world turns to darkness
You fight to hold on
You’ve laughed in the face of death
Now you are his pawn

I’ll shed no tears for you
You were not real
I cry for the person inside
The one beneath the seal

All the people who mourn
Unknowingly crying for a stranger
Another life wasted
You feared but there was no danger

Now that you are gone
Your outer shell melted away
Your spirit long caged
Burns out like light of day
And for your selfishness 
You will have to pay
Your life’s last dance
There is no new beginning
You’ve lost your second chance
