if someday the world refuses to turnwhen the rain decides not to fallif the clouds were shy to movei'll understand the latent reasonsif the shadows weren't craven to the sunand if you refuse my heart and souli'll understand the hidden messagei'll sympathize the common errorsif my words are clumsily spokenmy actions awkwardly actedif the moon was late to eclipseand the stars forgot how to flyif tomorrow doesn't comei'll understand whyi'm not really comfortable with that first line. doesn't seem to flow.
