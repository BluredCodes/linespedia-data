life's a rock showlife's a partylife's a ravewaiting to be bustedand i was born a wallfloweri'm home grown on the wallbut i'm getting bored with sticking to my spotunfortunately cherry bubble gumgets stuck to my hairbut that's ok, it really isyou see a lot from this anglelike the words that are saidand the lives that dance byi sat out on some songsbut on occasion,not to shock you,i've jumped in and lost controlsometime, blarg. i don't know... this one just sort of popped up. but it reminds me of a movie night i once went to.
