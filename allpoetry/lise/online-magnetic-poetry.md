play with words
play with useless hate
nothing foolish has an end
begin by asking the end
if it's bored with waiting
i take it you're soft inside
i take it you make a sound 
squishI was playing around with some online magnetic poetry type thing and this was the result. I just sat there, read it over a few times and just laughed. makes absolutely no sense but I kind of like it.
