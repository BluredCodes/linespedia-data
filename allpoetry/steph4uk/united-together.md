For everything
A time, a place
To live, to die
For peace, for war

Now, for war
UNITED States
Join hands

Men young and old
Step up
Fight for our rights

Long and hard
UNITED together
We conquer
We win

UNITED together as one
We can win this war
We must be the
UNITED StatesI wrote this after the trying events of Sept. 11
