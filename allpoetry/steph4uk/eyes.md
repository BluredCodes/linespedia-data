I've been told that eyes
Are windows to the soul.

To a lost
Forgotten world.

A world of pain and anger
Or filled with truth and love.

Blue to see a world of
Peace and serenity.  

Green for seeing the 
Friendliness within.

Brown is for seeing the
Compassion in others.

Put all of these together
We can see into the soul.
