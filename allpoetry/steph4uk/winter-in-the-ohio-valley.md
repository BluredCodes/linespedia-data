Monday
Sunny with a hig of 53*

Tuesday
Partly cloudy,
30% chance of rain,
High in the low 60's

Wednesday
78% chance of rain
High of 67*

Thursday
High only in the upper 20's
60% chance of snow

Warm and sunny one day
Cold and snowy thr next.

What to wear, what to wear?
Sweats, sweatshirt,
Jacket, shorts,
t-shirt, jeans??

Help!!!  We never
Know what weather is coming next!
