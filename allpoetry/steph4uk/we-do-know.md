---
title: "We Do Know"

They say we don't know
What true love really is.

They say you are too
Young to have experienced it.

How do they know 
All of these things?

Are they the one who 
Is in the relationaship,
The only one who has
These feelings.

Are they the teenager,
Who lives their life?
Or are they the adult
Who is already married?

Why do they try 
To hold us back.
They think they
Are just protecting us.

They don't know that
By holding us back
They are making us
Try to prove we do know,
What true love is.
