To my future husband:

I promise my love for you 
Will truely be from my heart

I promise to stay by your side
Through any thing we go through

I will stay true to you
Forever and always

I will promise you these 
Plus many things throughout
Our Blessed marriage

I will love you
FOREVERThis is not complete, but i would like some input on what you all would promise your future spouse
