I am who
I say I am

I am a young and
Insightful woman

Who is always
Willing to help others

I am a free spirited 
friendly woman

I am a student,
daughter, relative, and friend

I am a valuable person
I have dignitiy and worth
What I do does 
Make a difference

I am who I say I am,
I am Stephanie.
