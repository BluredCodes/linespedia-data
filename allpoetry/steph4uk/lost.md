Lost within her thoughts
She reminises memories
Of her past

Boys
School
Family
Work
Acceptance
All keep her feeling down

She needs someone 
To confide in,
To love her, and
To help her along the way

But where will she find 
The person to share
Her dreams and secrets with?

She can't get along
With the ones who 
Are always there for her

She needs someone,
Someone new
Someone who won't judge her

Just someone to love
Her for Herself.
