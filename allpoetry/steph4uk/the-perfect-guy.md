Picture this
Tall, meat on his bones
Loving, caring
Compassionate,
like you for you.

He won't 
hurt you
verbally or physically, 
Or yell at you.

He will hold you close when
your upset,
stressed
feeling bad, or
Just to be holding you close.

He won't let 
anything 
bad happen to you.

He gives you 
sweet soft kisses
On your forhead,
Tip of your nose, 
Then your lips.
