Always running around, 
Feeling run down. 
I need to sleep. 
My eyes are so heavy, 
They can't stay open. 
My body feels as if it, 
Just wants to drag. 

My heart racing, 
My limbs limp, 
My mind wandering. 
Nothing gets done, 
Everything to do. 
No time to do it. 

Keep on going. 
Keep on pushing. 
Move, 
Push, 
Pull, 
Drag, 
Fall,
