Sir, could you spare
Just a little change?

I do know 
What you are thinking.

Your thinking, 
This guy is just posing
As a bum just to
Get some extra money.

Or your thinking,
If I give this guy
Some of my change,
Is he going to use it for food or liquor?

Well sir,
Since I don't drink
I will use the change I collect,
To help feed me and my family.

I live on the street,
No one has respect
For me or my other
Homeless friends.

So, sir could you spare 
Just a little change?
