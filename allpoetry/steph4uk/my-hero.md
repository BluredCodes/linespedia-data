He's supposed to be
His daughters hero.

He was and still is
My hero.

I will love him
With all my heart
Forever,
No matter what.

The image of His face
Implanted
Deep into my memory.

The last image I see
Is of him walking
His baby down the aisle
On her wedding day.

But the images have been
Torn apart by one
Little thing.

It is so small but
Can affect life a 
Great Deal.

This litte thing...
Cancer.

I LOVE YOU DADDY
YOUR MY HERO.This is how i feel about my father, he is still alive though.
