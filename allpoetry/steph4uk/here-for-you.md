title: "Here For You"

I will always be
here for you.

When you need a friend
I’ll be here for you to talk to.

If a guy breaks your heart
I’ll be the shoulder you cry on.

When you just want to talk
I will lend you my ear.

When we part and go our separate ways
I will give you a call just to see how you are.

And when you ever need anything
I will be here for you always.
