I gave to you my dignity
my precious secrets to behold
to hold the true of me
within slender hand
and felt fears rise as I did so.

Trust is gone, the bitch
she crushed it
trampled under her size 5 docs
spat in my face, trust women
no, to me, evil, every one.

Dignity shattered
heart in tatters
tears fell
I dwell in silence
at my most alone.i travelled across the world for this girl. the bitch! but i don't really hate women, or think they are evil! Still, i'm not bitter anymore. you live, you learn.
