The one I love has left me
gone far in the arms of another man
she took her stuff and left a note
no goodbye kiss
no parting sorrow
just empty bed
and dusty wardrobe.

Now my house seems empty
of laughter and tears
all the tokens of togetherness
severed, save the trivial abandoned
a half used tube of toothpaste
her crossword book, all puzzles done
a “wild thing” T-shirt left on the line.

I smell her still, her perfume
it lingers wantonly in the air
her scent on the pillow
onto which I can shed no tears
I feel unmanned, like I have lost a limb
we were so deeply woven
alone, can I again beginPlease excuse the depressing nature of this...i was at the time...depressed.
