title: "Two Rainbows"
writer: "tgb"

I smile when I see a rainbow,
I saw two yesterday.
Two beautiful arcs.
And I wonder,
What right have I to feel,
Despondent,
When I see,
Two perfect rainbows.

I sing when I hear song,
Tap my foot, dance.
Sing to the silence.
And I wonder,
What right have I to feel,
Alone,
When I have,
So many friends in song.

I warm when I see love,
A couple holding hands,
Twice the age of me.
And I wonder,
What right have I to feel,
Cynical,
When I sense,
In the hearts of man, love.

I laugh when I see children,
Kicking a ball, playing in snow.
Full of imagined possibility,
And I wonder,
What right have I to feel,
Doubt,
When all around,
I am embraced by hope.

I believe when I see you,
I remember, love, obsession,
Devotion, eternity made real.
And I wonder,
What right have I to feel,
Lost
