Nan & Granda

I mind when I was very young,
And my parents were na near,
I’d look around, caught in panic,
See my Nan and naught to fear.

I mind when I was maybe older,
A big lad chased me home,
My Granda chased him oot the house,
I thought him soldier bold.

I mind the wild days of youth,
When I’d ner a place tae stay,
They took me in and made me safe,
Never complained nor asked for pay.

I mind the days before a left,
Nervous steps tae bein a man,
They helped me shift and gave me strength,
And if it turned bad, just come back hame.

I mind the days of livin away,
But still just steps apart frae them,
I never went back without words of concern,
Conversation, a meal, or just a happy den.

And when they’re gone and wee us no more,
I will not cry though I will mourn,
For I know our love will never fade,
Nor the happy memories, I will treasure to my grave.


Dedicated to all my Grandparents
With love
James Angus MackayThis makes me feel sad
