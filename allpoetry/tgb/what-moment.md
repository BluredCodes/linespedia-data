What moment in life
if one you could only have
would you live within
for pleasure or purgatory
for love or for sin

your first kiss
an innocent passion shared
or a drunken fumble
at a party 
in the little room beneath the stairs

the birth of your son
who brought stability and joy
whose first word, dada, 
lit the room for you
though nobody heard, just you and your boy

for me it was the time
my soulmate, my forever companion
told me, I love you
in that moment, of perfect pure euphoria will I live
I only wait for when it comes.
