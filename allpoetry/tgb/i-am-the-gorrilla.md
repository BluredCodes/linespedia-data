I am the gorilla
And I’m here to have some fun
To get down with the honeys
And to shake my hairy bum
Armed with my bananas
I slide across the floor
Grab the 18th birthday chick
And dance, groove, some more

I am the gorilla
And she’s melted in my arms,
Of course she never had a chance
Seduced by ape-like charms
I spin her off, and see her chums
Waiting for their chance
Smiling, winking, each one eager
To taste some furry romance

I am the gorilla
And I’m drunk now by the bar
Friends and family of the girl
Buy me whiskey by the jar
When on my stool I slowly spin
And spy the best of all
A primate beauty, an ape-like lass
So to her I then do call

I am the gorilla
I have come down from the tree
And I’ll trade all my bananas
For a night of fun with thee
She nodded and I followed
To her dwelling we did go
Where she was queen of my jungle
And me her mighty joe

I am the gorilla
And I wake upon the morn
Glancing at my hairy mate 
I smile at her hidden yawn
For at night she put the mask on
And the gorrlia she is now
But no, I shriek, its not the mask
Just a smiling bearded sow

I wish I was the gorilla
As I spring now from the bed
For I did not sleep with the birthday girl
But her grandmother instead
Through toothless grin she calls to me
As I bolt out of the door
Don’t forget your bananas sonny
We’ve some left from the night before

James A(for ashamed) Mackaythe moral...don't drink to excess and do a gorillagram for a mate on the same night
