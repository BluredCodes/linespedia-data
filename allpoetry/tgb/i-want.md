title: "I Want"

I want little bit of heaven
To laugh, to smile
To cry, to hide
To see those who are close
And hear those far away
To know with surety
They’ll return someday

I want to fit in
To live within
The worlds embrace
To feel her tell me it’s ok
Her breath
My neck
To take my pain away

I want to love
And to be loved
To be held
To be drowned with comfort
To be rained with care
By all
By one

I want to see 
To know what I must
To seek and find
To want and to have
To give what I see
To those precious
To me

I want to be needed
To be thought of
Company desired
Thoughts craved
Ideas to actions
To be admired
To inspire

I want to be understood
For others to see
To read me like a book
To see my truth
To feel my truth
To trust my truth
And find some common ground


I want to sing
I want to shout
To lark about
To be a lad
Get drunk
Get wasted
Forget future, present, and past

I want to be good
To give
To help old ladies
To be for friends
A friend they need
To say good things
Things good and true

I want it to make sense
Mostly, and more than others
Why sometimes I can’t see an end
Or fit in
Love, see
Be needed or understood
