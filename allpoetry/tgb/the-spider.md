I am the spider
I crawl
I spy the world below
I see all
I spy the man
Raise fury’s’ hand
I spy the wife
On whom it lands
I spy the brother
Crawl into bed
I spy his sister
Who he makes bleed
I spy the grandma
Who stinks of piss
I spy the grandpa
Gripped in Dementia’s kiss
I spy the baby
Cry for its meal
I spy the mother
Shoot up, forget kids appeal
I spy all this
Am I appalled?
No, I am the spider
I crawl.This is not about a spider. :-)
