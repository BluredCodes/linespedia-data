The big soft snooze wagon

to there I retreat
after good day and bad
to bed
a big soft snooze wagon
transporting me to the land of nod.

I sink to mattress, close eyes
breathe…breathe…breathe
my minds awake
more alive than at day
with car chases
helicopters
shaken no shtirred
mish monypeny
GOAL
its a boy
and yellow.
Yellow?

I wake and try to remember dreams
but nod reclaims
adventures all
till late tonight
when I ride the big soft snooze wagon
and I’m again not mundane
but a thousand feet tall.
