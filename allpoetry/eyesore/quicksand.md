"Quicksand"

I'm wholly uninspired today
Will become another yesterday
Was once tomorrow

Comes and disappears
Into another night of endless dreaming
Of a simpler place

For my head to lay
Down and think about heaven
And hell

I don't even care
Less arrogance = More knowledge
To feed my mind

If I step into the light and find the way
Beyond anything I could ever imagine
Being alone

In the world without a helping hand
In hand with the Devil and God
Dam the raging river grand

Stage the great encompassing suicide
Step into the sinking sands
Of time

Will only tell
Me another story that inspires the spirit
Dance with me to a somber lullaby

The ocean still
Empty, but full of doubt
