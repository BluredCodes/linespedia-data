"Time To Fade"

~~~~~~

Long days gone by
A life engaged to the fullest
Under a dying moon
Sorrow permeates through the white-walled tomb
Clinging like a lost child found in a forest of darkness
Light reaches through the twisting branches
Strange winds circle the mind
A burning river flows 
Through the corroded roots of the heart
A body rising to an unrelenting God

~~~~~~

Face to the sky
A man cries into a downpour
Broken wings of yesterday to wither here, unborn
Floating thoughts on an invisible high
Walking barefoot in the rain, lost
Wondering
Wandering
Along an ocean of intoxicating toxic fuel
Food for the firestorm of legends
A man drowns in an empty pool of indulgence
Tapping the flat line mainline
Hanging from a twisted thread of sanity 
Surgical insurgence
Injecting the words of an unwritten suicide note

~~~~~~

As your spring turns to summer
So does your fall succumb to winter
A cold embrace smothering the warm beauty within
Even the strongest crumble, in time
Your skyline turns accepting to gray
Your trees shudder and shake under whirling brainstorms
Your fields of color collapse and welcome a blackening dust
Your world rejects the blooming of new life
You lie down deep with your fallen roses
The scents of regret surfing petals of blood red
A poor, weakened man
So high, so low

~~~~~~

Standing on the edge of sanity
To jump was his intention
Quickly the ocean rose to greet his tired soul
He could not swim in this unforgiving beast
As it took him to the depths of endless sleep
An ending no needle could revive, though some tried
In vain
In veins
Today he would not die a little more
As he had no more left
