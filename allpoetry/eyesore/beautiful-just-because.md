'Beautiful, Just Because'A cool morning in a field of soft petalsThe wind carries your scent into my chestI fear to exhaleNot certain you will returnTo savor every moment as if I have only one chanceBut as if fate is in my heart, you reappearMore tempting than everAn innocent curiosity, like a mamaidensirst danceYou whisper to me sweetness on wisps of a smileAs a candle I melt to your fireAnd forever vow debt To the hand that has guided you to me once moreThis immortal sense of onenessThrough dark days on nighttime trailsYou beckon my light to your circle of longingOur loneliness intertwining in solitudeReleasing burning red embers of purityCreating, once again, a world of unexplainable beautyUnder a fading sky with highways of blue and pinkWe touch and explode with unbridled passionA tender moment in a new worldAnd this is homeHand in hand through a field of soft petalsThis is home
