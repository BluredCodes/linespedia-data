title: "Despise And Lies"

You are there
And in my eyes you know it
in my heart you shows it.
That You have lied.
In your dreams you feel it,
In your nightmares you kill it.

You see me, everywhere you go
And every place you know.

In your love, in your heart
Its tearing you apart.
In your feelings, you bring it home
to me.
Its more then a feeling of pain or misery.
I am the one that will stand behind you
no matter what goes through your mind.
And with me there is no such thing as time.
Because I will be with you rather you lie,
Rather tonight  or tomorrow you die.

I despise the way you are,
How you've lived your whole life
the way you planned. How you given
me hope and dreams to find dry land.

But in your mouth you speak it,
and in the air you breathe it.
Those Little lies.
