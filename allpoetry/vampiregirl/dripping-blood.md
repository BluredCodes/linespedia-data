I look in the bathroom mirror while my eyes tear up
there is no relfection.
My visions are clear,
I see blood dripping down like rain drops.

Blood runs down my lips, and in to the empty drain.
I have done it again, I couldn't stop my self,
I couldn't control how I feel.

My craving was deep, And I needed the thrill.
Those I know come back to haunt me for what I've done.
I needed their blood to survive.
I needed their loving blood to warm my cold heart.
I needed their breath, so I wouldn't fall apart.
It took less then one hour this time,
I had no soul to remind me that it was the wrong thing to do
I had no feelings to lose.

I slowly let my teeth sink in to his neck
and drained every last drop.
he was pure, and my cure for death.
His soul slowly drifted away, I saw it with my own eyes.
His body fell to the floor, like his soul needed him no more.
Another dead body,
Another empty party.

I have never felt bad, for these thing's I've done.
I can't walk away now, And I cannot run.
The taste of him still is in my mouth,
the stains of liquid red still on my sharp teeth.

I need at least one person a week.
Sometimes that's not enough,
But I must survive
If I give in from this moment on I will die.
