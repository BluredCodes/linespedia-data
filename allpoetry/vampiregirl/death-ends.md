---
title: "Death Ends"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Ghosts fall through the eyes of us all.
Shadows unseen.
Places that are heavenly moments of peace.
Those places we wish to seek.
everything gone in the flash of light.
Some follow in the night
Some follow in the day
seeking for a new way.
Minds misplaced, all erased.
Everything bursts.
Destructions of emotions of us all.
We live, we fall, death catches us.
A never ending moment of thirst.
casting moments weep.
All ends and we sleep.
