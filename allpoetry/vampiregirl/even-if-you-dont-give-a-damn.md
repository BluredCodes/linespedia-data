So long I've been lying to you.
Forgetting what's false and what's true.
Its as if I was being someone else,
Living a life in another world.
For awhile I never got out of the house.
I was to scared to step foot in to the stores of down town.
I was scared of judgment, And scared of someone seeing me around.
I felt as if I was never good enough, and someone would always put me down.

For so long, I put my self in my room.
While the air was filled with gloom.
I'm tired of being someone IM not.
After all I'M just a plot.
Someone I made my self out to be,
A faded picture in memory.
I know you'll never actually know me.
I'M sorry but I must set my self free.
I must be who I am,
Even if you now dont give a damn.
