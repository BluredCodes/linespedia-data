title: "Never To Trace Him (Out Of Reach)"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In the eyes of others 
I seek for something I've never felt 
something to warm the cold dampened heart. 
In the path in which you've walked I long for you 
so far away from a dark sky that's turned blue. 
I've seen your foot steps but we've never 
had the chance to look in each others eyes 
and see the love that others have denied. 

I've never felt this empty and so departed or despised 
I've sat alone for so long for all my life. 
Never again to know what it feels like to be held.
 
I've melted my dreams in to the night, 
hearing others talk, of love, and happiness 
but never to feel my own. 
It feels as if I've lost everything even my home. 
And never again to hear a beautiful song 
only my poor aching heart dragging and draining me. 
Sometimes in large crowds I hear your voice 
but still we are never to meet face to face, 
could this one I need, be out of reach? out of touch 
for me to trace........
