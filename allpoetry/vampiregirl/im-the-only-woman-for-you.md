When I put your hands on me what do you feel?
Do you feel my heart beating, Do you feel my soul
reaching for your love.

So gracefully you were sent to me from god above.
You had never once taken me for granted
and I owe so much to you.
You were my throbbing heart, that captured
your light and let me through.

Your hands soft, and tender, and you
move so slowly upon my body, makes me tremble
and makes my heart atomic.
My body and heart, is aching, for this
empty night that's breaking.

And a calling voice that's shaking, while
I whisper I love you.

I never believed, I could fall in love again.
I never thought you could change or be a better man.
You are all that I dream of, and you are my heavenly
love. You are my lungs to breathe. And
your arms hold me tight every single night.
Your words are always perfect,
and your body is always warm.
Your kisses are always gentle, with the smallest amount of tongue.
Your so passionate, I would give everything to you.
You are my emotions when I forget the words I was going to say to you.
You can read my mind, And I would never doubt you.
You are my future love to be, My eyes opened to see
that you will always be my loving memory.
And I only wish I could be A better woman for you to see,
And open my light, for you to know, I'm the only woman for you.
