I see people walking around me, So beautiful in their hearts.
With sun shining down on all their thoughts.
And I ask my self what makes me any different then you, 
I never understood, why this could be true.

I noticed strange things flashing though my eyes.
Faded pictures of all those who died.
I simply looked away, trying to hide my eyes
so I would not feel the pain of all those who cried.

Something happened to me on Halloween day,
I walked out side and the wind breezing softly
whispering and chanting words I could not understand.

Children tricker treating under the pale moon.
the last eclipse happened sometime in June.

Something changed the earth that day,
For some reason I was looking at people in a different way.
Still not understanding what made me different then them.
till October 31st, I noticed all these children
were very pale, and their skin was mushy, and their eyes were
clear. I noticed everyone I looked at didn't notice me here.
And everything came together, When I looked in the mirror
that I was the last one a live, I'm the only one that survived the eclipse.
