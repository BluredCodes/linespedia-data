Behind the bars of pain
there is life.
Through the blocked window there is light.
Behind the dungeons there is moss.
Behind the regrets and cries there is loss.

In your mind you've followed through
you've made mistakes, and didn't know what to do.
Those you believe in, know you did wrong and
keep you away from melodies and loving songs.

From the forest you heard the breeze,
for once in your life you were pleased.
After so many years of being locked away
you found it in your heart to make you stay.
You ripped the moss away,
Light came in through the window of yesterday.

the childhood you've never had,
The parents fighting and driving you mad.
So many excuses was made
so much guilt and pain was done.

Not even your own family believed you were
innocent,
Everyone found you guilty at once.
Never even gave the chance to you, for justice.
You were taken away, from your wife and kids
placed in the cell with roaches, and Borden beds.

In the end
you were innocent
In the end no on cared.
They had to put someone in the electric chair.
