---
title: "Is It Love?"

This pounding
These emotions over whelming.
The wild butterflies floating around
the romance the best chance of our lives
the look in his eyes.

The glance
the dance
The sunset
the moon
The heaven on earth swept me off my feet
and completed my heartbeat.
The happy ending
The close kissing.
The beauty
The enjoyment of your lover.

Everything so peaceful
your sadness turned around
everything happy from here on out.
You live
You die
Yet you never say goodbye,
The True Love in our eyes.
