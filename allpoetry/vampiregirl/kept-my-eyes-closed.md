title: "Kept My Eyes Closed"

I kept my eyes closed, in case you ever thought of leaving me.
I know what kind of person I would really be if you left,
Ill keep my eyes closed, in case you come back to see me cry.

I know I should tell you to stay, But I don't know another way
other then on my knees.
I know that you don't really care for me, but I imagine of what our lives could be if you would just reach out and touch me.

This is your only chance to come back and be, the only man for me.
I don't want to be lonely.
