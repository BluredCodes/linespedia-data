The moment I enter the door, I see people dancing on the floor
Men and woman walking around, with a drink in their hand
The lights flashing in their eyes, Party life seems cool at the time.

I see woman in short skirts, men in jeans.
All are checking out each other, to see who they will end up
with tonight. Who will get it on, and who is or isn't tight.

They can't seem to get ass anywhere else.
They jam to the music, and forget their stress.
they are dancing wildly, closer then they should be
rubbing up against each other very wildly.

Kissing and sucking on each others lips, grinding hip to hip
men rub their hands all over the woman, woman rub their hands all over the men. A for feeling night of sin.

Some come for the music, others come for the drinks
to drown their lonely hearts.

Some come in just to dance.
Men are dancing with each other,
woman dancing by their selves.

The good looking people stay on the left,
the not so good looking stay on the right,
Everyone getting down, Everyone screams and shouts.

They all get it out, and dance wildly,
who's going home with who.
What road will you take, how many hearts will you break
before it gets to you.
