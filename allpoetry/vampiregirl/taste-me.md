title: "Taste Me"

Taste me now, adventurous moments of love
Feel the joy, touch the stranger or ponder on empty thoughts.
Or wonder till it makes you angry or sad. 
or wonder What would have happened if you had of tasted me
and how I would of reacted to your touch or your tongue
we both are left in wonder.
