title: "The Ride That Died"

On a trip we take, to a place we want to go.
children in the backseat, they can't wait to get there
and they have been there a dozen times before.
This time john my husband had a bad feeling,
but we went anyway.
We went to Disney world, and went on every ride that day,
there were only two more rides to take, and a long line
to wait. It was worth it,
my children waited for this moment all summer long.
I saw their eyes glow, when they saw the rollercoaster
go high, they say it goes up to 85 mph and you can feel gravity 
fly.
I had no idea, this was the last place we would go.
it seemed just like any ordinary day, 
it was once again another Saturday.
finally after hours of waiting, We got on
we buckled our selves in, and let the wind settle in
between us.
My children in the back, me in the front
suddenly it goes, and the wind catches up.

Hearts pumping,
eyes open wide.
The track breaks open
and there's no place to hide,
Memories of the past, and all the fun we had
came to mind, in my children's eyes
I saw death arise. screams from the back
rolls in to the front, coming in like a tide.
We all flew off the tracks, and we all died.
