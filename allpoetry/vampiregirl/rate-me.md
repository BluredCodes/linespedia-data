When you go down the list what do you see?
Some How your beautiful eyes skipped over me.
Can't you see the title screaming out: read me?

I wonder who will look next? if anyone will look at all?
Do they not find my title, or poem very appealing?
I worry that my writing isn't good enough?
If it was, then why would I be ignored?.

I comment on all your poems,
I say how I truly feel.
I give expressions and emotions that are real.

I give you stars, and rate your poems, as I will.
but after awhile of getting 33 views, and only ten comments from you.
It makes me worry, 
and it makes my vision go blurry.
And I wonder why?
why do you come to my poem, if you didn't count on rating me?
did you miss something? maybe a mystery?
Did you come to this poem just to see?
there must be a reason? there must be something more,
Rate this poem, And ill make sure,
That when I go to your poem, Ill rate you to.
Because I would NEVER skip over you.
