A beautiful flower starts from the core and grows larger
when it rains, when the sun comes out it Blooms.
When the moonlight comes out, it changes to mixed colors
she's gone wild, She's blue, she's red, Her moods change so frequently
in till she's picked, She roams around in the night, Looking for a thorn
so she will not be alone, She runs through the garden when she feels like no ones home, Or is watching her.
She's a wild flower that will run far,
her time slowly getting away from her.
She doesn't know how far she will make it.
her petals fall, And by winter she's just like everything else
that once was beautiful, She's dead, and her thorns let her know
he would out live her. The thorns made her over come her fears
whipped away her rain drop tears, and made her feel pain.
And in the winter another flower blooms, like a child born in to the sun,
the water pours, down, as she grows, she tries to run but doesn't get far.
winter passes through, and all the petals get blown away.
