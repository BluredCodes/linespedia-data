title: "Perfect Curse"
writer: "VampireGirl"

A dark secret he tells burns wild blossoms from the ground. For I teach him the ways of my liquid red love that burns his tormented heart I show him the razor I hold in my palm. I show him what I am cursed and damned for and still he tells me I am perfect and I can do no wrong.
