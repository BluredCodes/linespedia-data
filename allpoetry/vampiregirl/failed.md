She stands in the rain, waiting for lighting to hit the ground.
She can't smile not even when no ones around.
She's scared of being alone, but she can't give up what she's worked so hard for. She maybe rich, but her heart has always been poor.
Her mind is cluttered, Her wings are broken but she still tries to fly.
Her eyes are open but she's not afraid of death to look her in her eye.
Her dreams fail  but she still knows she's well on her way.
Everyday seems as if its sweeter then the other day
She has new meanings, and takes nothing for granted.
She seems to always know the right answers to say.Although people shattered
her crown. Everyone always wonders why she she stood in the rain
with her shattered crown, failed dreams and frown. with no wings to fly.
