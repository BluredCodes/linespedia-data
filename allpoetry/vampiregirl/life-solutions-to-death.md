title: "Life Solutions To Death"

Behind the tree of life
we seek forth to death.
Behind the book of love is pain and romance.

Behind the eyes of you is nothing but a haze
You have choices
and mistakes.
Lives giving moments awake.
Deaths dyeing moments of sleep.
Nightmares dreams, To weep.

Everything gathers together.
And in the end you die.
All happy moments turn cold.
All lonely moments trapped in your eyes.

Sooner or later you take a walk in the park
wondering who you are. How far away are you from the tree.
beneath the tree, a stone that reads your name.
Six feet under the body of clay, Your body..

The tree, you live for, the tree you die under.

Around you, other coffins beneath the surface.
Behind you, memories of the past.
in the middle, a life time that runs past,
the life you never lived,
the dreams you never finished.
The romance, and love you never had.
The friends you never made.
Everything begins to fade....... right under that tree
of life and death... nothing left, your solutions recovered
but never born again all vanished to light and dark moments
of sin.
