---
title: "Ill Be Waiting"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "ill-be-waiting"
source: "AllPoetry"

When the trees start to grow
And there is light to show.
Ill be waiting, For your hand tonight
Thinking of holding you, Holding you so tight.

Whisper softly in my ear
touch my lips, and kiss me here.

When the moon settles and my soul
sleeps tonight, guide my wings out the window
and hold me no more.

When my body turns pale
and there is nothing to do but watch me sleeping with out you,
Listen to my heart
Listen to my dreams weep away
under moonlight breeze far under the seas.

When you kiss so softly upon my moist lips
I will tremble to the soft passionate kiss.
When you forget me, ill be gone forever
so hold me, and never let me go.

When everyone around
slowly turns you down
and I'm no where to be found
call me to heaven,
speak out loud to me
Ill be listening and watching
for the one I love so much, to rescue me.

When the heart stops
and the emotional feeling dies
And there is no one holding you
But the tears in your eyes
Ill be waiting.
