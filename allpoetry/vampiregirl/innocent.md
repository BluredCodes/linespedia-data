Only one moment, till the night fog covers my eyes.
Nothing to live for, and no reasons to cry.
All these emotions hit me so hard, fell to my knees 
and said swiftly please. And as a moment pasted I hopped this moment would last, It was the only memory I have that was pleasant.
Where I was lying gently on the grass it was damp, and the sky was covered in stars. I could see all the stars, from any field, 

At that point, I knew nothing of love, or pain
I was still innocent, to a another name.
I was on guard, happy, and joyful nothing could bring me down.
The moon gazed upon me, and I knew no one was around.
Suddenly I felt lonely, no one recognized who I was.
But I had made a mistake somewhere, Asking my self for days
what I had done, then it accrued to me, that I was never as
perfect as I made my self to be.
