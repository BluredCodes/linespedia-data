Knock Knock, No ones home,
LEAVE me alone.
The phone rings but no one answers,
STOP calling me.
Everything seems silence
the only sound you hear is the sound of the trees
banging against the glass windows. so loudly it was.

knock knock, I lay in my bed afraid to move.
Scream and yelling I hear down the hall,
Foot steps, walking in my room.

Pictures on the wall swaying,
Music turning on and off.
JUST leave me here laying.

KNOCK KNOCK, Still I do not answer.
no one is to protect me from this monster
in my head, So I sleep in my bed, with the covers
over me, So gently I try to rest, But don't get a drop
of peace. I beg it to stop, But it just keeps KNOCKING
at me.

Are these angels Haunting me for killing my self two years ago
don't you see the blood flow from these bullet holes?
don't you see the red stains on my sheets, Do you hear a heart beat?

Do you see My eyes move?
I am dead, Leave me at peace, and stop playing with my head.
