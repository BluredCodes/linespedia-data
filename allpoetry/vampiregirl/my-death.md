title: "My Death"

Sleeping and
dreaming of happy things that has happened in my past.
Thinking of all these happy memories I thought would always last.

Wondering in my head What is going on.
I awake with a happy song from my dream, and look down and
There is my body lying there cold and still
Staring at my self, in a mirror.
Trying to get my thoughts together.

My hands crossed on my chest,
blankets cover me at rest,
My eyes closed, with a slight grin
on my face.
my lips strawberry red.

my family gathered around
Those that love me crying.
Today was my death.
