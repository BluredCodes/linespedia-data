Tumbling down the trees
Our memories.
Places we wish not to go, and places
that no one must know.

In my mind a faded picture 
Of us together.

In my heart an empty places
after.

In my dreams
a place stuck in-between a dream catcher
and an angel

My most charming moments was in his arms
not long after he left.

I did my best
He laid his head down to rest on my pillow of love
and never woke up.

Once again another tragic moment 
Anther moment of unluckiness
and a moment ill never forget
