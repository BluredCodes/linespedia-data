---
title: "Im The Perfect Girl"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Here I am with beautiful long brown hair
and big blue eyes that shine.
I am hollow and deep, and understand your state of mind.

I have long legs, and the best weight.
I have beautiful skin and lots of values
that cannot wait.

I am always cheerful, and joyful
even when I'm sad.
I am happy and graceful even when I'm mad.
I carry on the deep conversation when your
mind goes blank.
I put my self down so you won't rank.

I love romantic things we do alone
I never spend my time talking on the telephone.
Every Time we are together I give my full attention
to you.

I open my heart and see clear through you.
My lips red and lushes,
my eyelashes are long and black
every time I bat my eyes you smile back.

I never yak about things that matter nothing to you
I show my appreciation for all things you do.

I never cheat
I never do you wrong
I listen to whatever music that's on your CD
I dance to the music wildly.

I have never cussed, I do not drink.
We smile at each other and know exactly what we think.

Our moments are always happy, And I always make you
feel loved, your the only one that's my heaven
and my hansom sun.

You have created a desire in me, You are the only
one that can take my virginity.

I give my heart, my soul and body so easy to you.
And there will never be another.

I never lie, and always tell the truth.

I never ask ridicules questions that mean nothing to you.
But the only thing I must ask is why is another woman with you?
Aren't I perfect? am I the one you dream of?
Has fate knocked you on the head and made you forget
of love? 
I'm the only one that was made for you
but some how someone messed with fate
And now that witch is with you and you closed your gate.
