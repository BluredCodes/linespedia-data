What is suicide?
When you don't wear your seatbelt when you get in the car.
When your riding your bike, and smack in to a wall.
How do you know, is it your own fault for dieing?
Is it your fault your families in pain and crying?

When you put a gun in your mouth and pull the trigger,
When you feel small and everything gets bigger.
When you pop a bottle of sleeping pills and close your eyes.
When you have no cares, and no meanings in life.
When you die, will you be judged for all things you do?
false or true?

What does god consider a suicide,
If you get in a car wreck who's fault would it be?
are you supposed to even be in a car, or live walking
in the heat and sun in misery?

When you burn in a fire, from a cigarette you didn't
put out, Suicide?
How do you know? how are you supposed to even let that question go? or will that question let you go?

When you shoot a burglar, Are you a killer? will you be judged
in the next world, for protecting your well being, or your three year old baby that was crying?

These questions I seem to find no answer to,
How do you know what's false? or true?
