Alone while your gone on business.
I have no place to go and I don't know anyone.
Snow all over the ground winter settled in so peacefully.
Its as if a part of me is missing when you aren't here.

The heat in the house went out so I called for a repair.
When he came I didn't expect him 
to be alone, I thought the repair man would have
some helping him.

I was wrong.
Suddenly he asked for a glass of tea
Its strange how the heart cheats.
He was so hansom and I haven't had sex in so long
I knew what I was thinking was doing you wrong.

Its not that I cheated, But it entered my mind,
Does this rational moment take away my time of love for you?.

I had no form of intercourse 
I did not kiss his lips.
I did not touch his body, or his finger tips.

But it entered my mind
I wondered how it would feel
Am I doing you wrong? 
How can I change these thoughts in my mind?
All the sudden I blame you for our lost time.
How could you leave me in the lonely winter alone.
So wrong of you to not even call home?

I feel selfish as if I've already cheated
My heart beats rapidly, as I feel uncompleted. 
Its like I already committed the acts
but I know its not true.
I need you to come home
I must tell you
that my heart and my mind has been cheating on you.
