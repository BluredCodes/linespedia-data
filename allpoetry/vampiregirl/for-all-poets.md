title: "For ALL Poets!"

When you start writing, you never know where to begin.
Asking your self, time and time again,
How do you feel? what's fake and what's real?

If you say the perfect words, will they understand?
can your words be joined in to allpoetry.coms helping
hand?.

Will those that like the title read your riddle and rhyme?
will they find the time?.
You are a little nervous the first time you write,
you fill the trash can up real tight.
All those feelings and emotions trashed away.
I say gather them together, and be on your way.
Keep writing you will get stronger, don't be boggled
by what comes together, Sooner or later your piece will
rather be good, as long as its understood.
As long as the meaning is clear, and you aren't in fear
that someone might rate it as a 1,
Just live and write poetry, and have fun.

Do it to clean the soul, Listen to some music, and rock in roll.
Grab that pin, and those old papers, put your heart and mind together
and come up with something better.
You have to believe in your self, or your work will not be a success.
Have faith in all you do, and all the rest of us poets will be
watching you.
Encouraging and raging you on,
Asking you to follow along.
We are all together, we never stand alone.
We might not ever talk on the telephone.
But though your poetry we know you.
And all the things that come in thru,
Set your mind free, Write down a master piece of poetry.
