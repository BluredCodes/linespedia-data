title: "Two Lives"
writer: "VampireGirl"

To see and look at life differently, Like this time I was meant to be.
I will see things start to grow in front of me and grow with them.
My past is just a memory, and I will live life for today and yesterday.

I will be as helpful and as caring as I can.
I will remember the mistakes I've made before
and never make them again.

I now know how to take your hand, And join your guide to the promise land.
I will talk and act as you have shown me. I will walk with you gracefully
and live my heart fully.

To think I have this chance to change every regret I once had.
All bitterness, turned to mold it slowly started to grow
till it grew on me, But in this new life in 1983
I will follow a new pair of stairs, and do my best.
And making this life with happiness and no regrets.

I've never believe there could be a chance like this till it happened to me.
now to know I can lead, or go wherever I please. and leave my soul at piece
with out a worry in this world, no weight to carry on my shoulders and no more mold, Nothing could be better then this, and nothing will be missed 
in my past life.
