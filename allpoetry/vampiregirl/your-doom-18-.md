In his eyes he wonders why I have chosen him.
He's not so hansom, and is very dim.

He figures a girl like me needs a stranger for attention.
I am proving him wrong
I am your dark shallow day
that will guide you well on your way.

I am your path, Your last.
I am your dyeing breath.

You've been watching me all night
I bet you wonder how I would feel
or how rough I Might be to 
kill.

Don't be paranoid.
lye their naked till I say slide.
I'm a nice girl you'll see, dont hide.

I don't know you, and you don't know me
it will make the night that much more perfectly.

I've always liked the wild kind.

The ones that like to be blind fold.
The kind that has a good grip and a good hold.
Push my legs up,
push in, smear my makeup.
Break your self in.

everything caving in.
Bed shaking, pounding against the wall
your much bigger then I am,
and so in control.

Sweat..
everything so cold.

He falls over,
everything black for him
I hated him
he knew I was under age
He had no idea the price he would pay
this is worse then jail
He will burn in hell.
That mother fucker,
I'm the demon you just got done fucking.
How long did you think I would be waiting?
did you expect me to cum so easily?
I bet you are now getting judged
and feel bad completely.

While I'm laughing through the wind
screaming children's voices
saying you have sinned.
Death arrives
you will cry.
and things will learn to fly.

I've been waiting for these wings for a year
I'm an angel that is clear.
I had to do my test
I had to show my best
and I've won.

Twisted things you never see
it could happen to you, it could happen to me.
How horrible this might be
to see me smiling, under the moon
to your first doom.
