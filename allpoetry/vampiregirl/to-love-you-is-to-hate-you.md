To Love you is to hate you. 
For there is my dim breath, that breathes down desperately. 
I feel the saint await you so suddenly. 
I curse your name. And wish not to stay 
and show how I truly feel. And believe that your mistress woman's love is real. 
To love you, I would give my life.
To hate you, I could take your life. 
To love you, I would give my strife. 
To hate you, I could lose my heart. 
To love you, I would feel your pain.
To hate you, I could leave the seen. 
To Love you, I would have to stay. 
To hate you, I could leave.
