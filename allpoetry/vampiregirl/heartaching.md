My heartache
beats with every night of troubled sleep.
I ache and cannot kill this pain.
Disease of the past has crossed my path.
I wish things would had left me behind but im out of time. 

I cannot change my self, This is how I am,
So take me as I am, or nothing at all.

Sometimes I think I have control
over everything I see, But I am wrong.

Please say you love me
and you understand and feel the same.
and whisper my name.
I created this perfect romance in my mind
And we find our self in a place of no love
with out time.

Sometimes feelings are stronger then they should be,
Sometimes they stay in my head like a memory.
I don't know what's wrong or right or how to get along tonight with out you,
Or if I should hold you or kiss you good night.

Maybe love was not meant for me.
But I don't think I can face you again..
I feel my heart floating in the wind
Its caught in my dream catcher that's hanging over me.

I wish there was no love, then there would be no you and me.
I wouldn't have to be playing these childish games.
I locked away my heart with a key, but you set it free
In my past a memory, of how it used to be.
I wish I would forget this, and leave you far behind
and be faded in a crushing moment that's never been in my mind.
