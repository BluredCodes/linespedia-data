When the wind catches my hairand I'm standing upon a hill of green.When I missed my last story and didn't play my stolenseen.Everything around me so dusty and lonelylonging for the one and only.My true love to rescue me, save me from the dragonof destiny.I long for your claim, The claiming of me.When all shame drifts long side the summer breezeand the mountain highs.When the ocean scraps past the sands of the beach my written goodbye.
