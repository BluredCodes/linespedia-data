I hate you for Everything you are,
And everything you aren't.
You are a shitty person that doesn't deserve a heart!.

You are as bitter as an old lime tree,
You ask for WAY to much from me.
You are a lair, And an asshole
and I hope you know the smell Because you
seem to know your self a little to well.

You are a hopeless, Poor boy, That will never have
anything, including me.
You are something I hate worst then anything
i've ever seen.

You make me so angry I could scream at you,
I would never lower my level enough to actually
meet face to face with you.

The thought went to my head, And I thought it would be good.
But I made a fool out of my self, When I said I would.
I knew I shouldn't of agreed to meet you,
You are full of shit and ill always hate you.

you  are a user, With a girlfriend that's a whore.
I hate her just as much, But I hate you even more.

You make me sick, just thinking of you
I hope you chock on a nut, and die too.
I hope you get sick and suffer, and cry.

I hope YOU go fuck your self, 
Because that's what you are, a walking dick
that means nothing, and Knows nothing of what you are.
