title: "Leaving Tonight"

We've been fighting way to much
We don't know how to get along.

Our happy song turned down
No ones around. why can't we slow dance?
we were once in our remembrance kiss?

I think I missed you when I took a trip,
suddenly everything was gone.
No more happy memories, no more happy songs.
I miss you more then anything,
And I am hoping you will stop me from leaving,
Every time you look at me I feel my heart beating.

I have flash backs, of what we used to be like.
How happy we were, I thought that would never end.
The rain starts to pour, the thunder shaking the windows
yelling and screaming out the door.
One slam, everything ends,
music starts to play again,
songs of hate
and pain,
heart breaking moments
another tear drop floating in the wind.
I'm gone,
and will never be back again.
