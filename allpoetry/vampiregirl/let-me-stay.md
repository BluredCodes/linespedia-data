---
title: "Let me Stay"
writer: "VampireGirl"

For all my life I waited for a moment like this,
For all flowers to bloom with just a simple kiss.
For my heart to be misted with your love, from the heaven above.
For my eyes to sink in to yours, and I be yours forever.
I am your love, your heart, your destiny, your future and your past.
I am the only one that stood by you,
And even if I get you for just one day, I will act like it will be
forever, Just please don't say it wont be. or my heart will fall in three.
Taste my lips, what do you feel, do you think this love is fake or real?
Can I play games like this? can I make you believe you are in a faze
a slight state  of mind, still asleep, but still mine.

Let me whisper my name to you, in every dream you have
so I will not be forgotten.
Let me kiss you for every moment we share so I will not turn bitter.
Let my arms cover you like snow falling in the winter, Let me roll on top of you, and show you to my town. Let me Love you for just one day
and I promise I will not ask you to stay,
let me rub on you just one more time, I promise I will not call you mine.
let me lick your lips with my tongue, and see if you want me to leave.
But I'm begging on my knees, please give me one more day.
