You've been gone for a little over a year.
I felt your presents a little more everyday.
Suddenly you were in my dreams that felt real.
I could feel your hands on me,
rubbing and touching me. 

you gave me a taste of heaven which I thought could never be.
I missed you for the way you were,
and I knew we were meant to be,
but you were so suddenly taken away from me.

it was only two nights go,
I was smoking a cigarette and blew smoke out of my mouth
and your face was so close,
I saw you perfectly,
Just then I  knew you were there,
and you would never leave me.
Suddenly my reality changed,
Everything was much better then it used to be.

I knew you would every night lye with me.
I knew I would never be lonely again,
I knew that I wouldn't have to miss you,
or wonder where you've been, you have been here
all along, And the music places once again
a beautiful song.

you will always be in my thoughts,
you might be dead, but you will show me how to live.
We will dance one more time,
We will kiss, and you will always be mine.
I will feel your finger tips whip away my tears.

You will save my soul tonight,
you will make love to me, for once more.
We will laugh,
We will cry,
We will die, together.
