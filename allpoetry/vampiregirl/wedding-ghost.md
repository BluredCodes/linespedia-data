Beautiful black dress drags to my feet, My heart beats rapidly
as I await for my sweet monster to capture my hand.
He's handsome and tall, He's wearing a dark black suite
And he has a ring of a claw. He has eyes of blue, 
And he whispers gently I love you, while he's making love to me.
He is good with his fingers, and takes away my tingles when I'm scared.

He gave me his promises when he gave me the ring of two lives,
shortly after I put the ring on my finger I heard the church bells ring.

We picked a night, to be married, and it was October 31st
We picked a place were we would stand together till death do us part.
The cemetery, was beautiful, with ghosts, and magical spells.
Leaves, floating to the ground, on a cold winters night,
He squeeze my hand gently and whispers it would be alright,

The setting of our marriage was close to a dream,
It was scary but nothing as it seemed,
And when I said I do, and you did to, Just then I knew
we will always be together, till death do us part,
we will live and die, in the same place we were married
but nothing would ever pull us apart, for we were already dead
and we knew it in our hearts.
