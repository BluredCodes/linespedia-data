---
title: "Dark World We Created"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "dark-world-we-created"
source: "AllPoetry"

The windows broken,
and have shattered on the floor.
screaming and echo's run out the door.
Heaven is empty, hell has broken lose.
Rage steps in, and we all sin.

Happy endings turning bad,
all that was good, turns sad.
time fades away,
we all die, and go away.

Lost in a moment of our self,
longing for one more breath,
speechless, our lips were taken away,
wordless to this day.

Mindless moments
stepping in,
Birth of babies in a dark summer night,
all babies born, do not look right,
they are dark, with black eyes,
for the sins of the mother,
and the cheating father,

Heart warming moments washed away
in to the white ocean on a winter day.

Everything is different,
hell is on earth
we know nothing of what we are,
and are lost to a place we lack knowledge in.

Our eyes, silver,
Our hands gold,
our feet black with no path to hold.
We are bruised and battered,
we've done this to our selves,
we have created this hatefulness of rage.
