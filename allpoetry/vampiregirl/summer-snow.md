wild summer nights turn to snowthe breeze sets in as it starts to glowcotton and dust angels float in the airand clouds turn dark as winter arriveslike a gift from heaven moving insummer's sun sinks awayand moonlight guides snow's waystarlight breaks inand summer's love shines once againthe clouds run as if afraidof what's going onbecause spring has never comethe golden light in the skyshines brightshines highas sweet winter whispersgoodbye
