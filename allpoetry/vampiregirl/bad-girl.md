When the night is empty and your in bed with more then one (Bad Girl)
Everything dark and false where you lay, you've screwed each man
in and out all their days (Bad Girl)

When your smile is no longer around
when your face is always frown.
When your eyes aren't as pure as they once were
you did this to your self (Bad Girl)

When your aching, and breaking each and every night
your in a crowed room and feeling left in and up tight (Bad Girl)

When you forget your supposed to cry, And you don't even know why
And nothing matters to you, And you don't know what to do (Bad Girl)

Through your parents, And you're loved ones you grew up
hearing bad girl, And slut not even knowing what that meant.
Through each night your not alone, you hear it echoed like a rolling stone
coming in your ear.

You give pleasure, And they scream out Bad Girl
And you know that's who you,
You have a beautiful body and beautiful hair
you've grown in to every outfit you were (Bad Girl)

You hate your life And everything you do
but you were raised this way, and its what happened to you (bad girl)

Your dreams you once had all turned away and went bad
On a cold rainy day.
And your the reason why Its all your fault, And your mind is nothing but a plot
(Bad Girl)
