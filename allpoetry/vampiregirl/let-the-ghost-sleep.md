title: "Let The Ghost Sleep"

For so long she longed for this moment of peace
where her body would be set free.
The sound of screams slanted her beautiful voice
in to the trees.

She could not feel anything she touched.
She could not taste anything she ate.

she hadn't had a breath in years
the last breath she remembered was followed by tears.

Her skin beautiful and white as snow
hear lips soft and moist
her eyes sown shut to never see light again.

She remembers it like a birth
and she describes it in her mind like being born again.
She walks, and no one can see her
now and then those that believe, see her from the corner 
of their eyes
They see a beautiful woman with tears among her face
walking in a silk black dress waving good bye.

Her family kept her room exactly the same
they were scared she would never come back if everything
didn't remain.

She would look out the window of her room
and be covered in gloom.
She would stare out the window and see her old
things, her memories that will never be the same.

She is utterly alone and she still remains
stuck at home. She has no passage way
to her new life.

She sits in an old chair where her grand mother
once sat there, And cries tears that are clear
for her eyes will not water.

All blood was drained
and all feelings caught inside
not a day goes by she wants to hide.

her parent's finely packed her old remains
and put them in the storage along with pictures and 
other objects.
it was only three days before Christmas
and she knew things were soon to change.

She saw her family gathered around the Christmas tree
she asks of god to let her go
and screams from her lungs and sown shut mouth SET ME FREE
suddenly her family turn and look and notice
it was really me.

For so long I was ignored
and put in between two worlds
And all I wanted was to sleep silently.
