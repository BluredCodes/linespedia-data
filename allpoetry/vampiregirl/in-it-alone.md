Deeply alone I lock my self in my home at night.Separate my self from the world so I don't have to give inwhen I feel up tight.Im so lost and out of control I feel dizzy in the day.I speak words of whispers and hear mumbles of those whopray.I knew it was time to change when I felt my selfgive my life away. I tumbled through the woodsand found my self lying face down on the river sidethat's when I knew I felt my soul behind.In a house is where I will be this will be the only placeyou will find me. I will stay away from those who cause war around me.A candle in my hand, and lights off in a forbidden landis where I will drift off to.Darkened roads follow me home the very first day I walked along with you.Black roses surround my feet while our hands meet, and I accepted you for who you are.You are the devil that prays on young girls eyes.That hangs out your heart with no surprise.All the black cats fallow us down, while we walk down this roadthe fog is black, like smoke, the clouds are gray and bitter.Your skin is like leather and Vinyl rubbing up against me.your hands are like thorns poking through me.your lips are like needles sinking in to mine.Your breath is sweet, but evil.Your empty, and you want me to for feel you.You rub your fingers over my lips like I know you.You seem to know all I say, before I say it.you say you are god, so I must obey it.
