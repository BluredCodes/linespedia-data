Let your last breath, be for your last step to loneliness. 
let the darkness cover you. 
And over your grave I will be your statue 
The one with a smile upon my face 
the one Blessed in happiness and grace.
the one that covered you in love, but was considered a waste.
the one that will one day see your face in
heaven above, Yet the statue you never loved.
