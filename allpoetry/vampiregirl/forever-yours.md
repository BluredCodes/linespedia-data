Beyond my challenges
Beyond my fears.
I am returning to my journey the virtue
of love, and death. My compelled heart bound
by blood vessels and pain.

My social anxiety ringing your fame.
Through an empty soul crowd I feed
off your mind, I since your face even
when you are not here.
I glory you for all that you are.

I am obsessive to your golden hands
and your silver feet.
I am tempted by the cruel lessons of natures leafs.

My breath expends
My focus not clear
My vision blurry and gray
yet I'm wanting more.

Your flexible mind spells, and appealing
views trapped me in to you.
Your lips look delicious and true,
I want nothing more then a piece of you.

The flushing of my empty drain.
The ability and honor of moaning your name.

The unplugging of my joy,
The rapid portal of the core,
I'm confused, more so then I should be.
your organized mind has grasped hold of me.
I am horrified, And dramatic a anti-match fireing force,
your whore.

I can't breath when your not here.
Your prepared for the worst,
and surpassing taste,
your wounds of magic bind my hearts race.

Your torturing aims
of darts you throw at me
targeted to my heart to destroy
what little part is left to see.
I excuse my frequent calls for help
and beg you to come back.
The scars are deep,
the journey up the mountains were steep.
Everything so imaginable.
my directions have failed
I will go back through the steps
in which I followed, and walk again,
kissing your golden hands, and silver feet
to be your slave.
