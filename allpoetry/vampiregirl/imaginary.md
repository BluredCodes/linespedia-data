I am satisfied with your reasons,
The reason you must leave, the reason for the way you behaved.
You are no one that I believed you were,
But you lost me somewhere in the blur.
I found my self wanting you,
Someone to hold close and talk to.
I'm just confused, you don't know who I really am.
Don't get your mind mixed up at this moment of time.

You have things that's going on in your head,
You need a place to hide, someone to guard your pride.
And that's not me.

I will walk away if you ask me to,
You don't have to tell me to stay
Ill know what your mind chooses no matter what your mouth might say.

I know you've kept a secret from me, I expected your honesty,
and Look what you did to me, you looked me in the eyes and lied straight out to me.

I expected someone you weren't, someone that wouldn't hide his wings behind his coat.

I thought you could be true, but you can't be
I thought maybe if I told you I loved you, it would change the way you feel
But at this point of time, I realize gravity has gotten to me,
And you were just in my head, another imaginary friend I call angel.
