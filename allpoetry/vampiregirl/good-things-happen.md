---
title: "Good Things Happen"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In the moment of history things happen so suddenly
an interesting thing came to me
a happy memory, something I've never had before
something I thought I could live with out.
Something stepped in while I felt stepped out.

Joy was in my heart
I thought things would go wrong
I thought when things were perfect
things couldn't last long.

I was happy in this place I was at
I was excited to see that something
might happen that meant something to me
It was a beautiful moment with out gloom or misery.

My eyes started to light up like the moon.
My mouth had the right words to say
It was only an hour left till day.
I climbed back in to my coffin and
went to sleep
And in front of me, for the last time
in my chest I felt my heart beat.

My face began to smile
even if it was only for a little while.
I was only two years old when I died
All these years I learned to hide.
But it was different now
somehow I learned, to live
while being dead.
No more drowsy feelings in my head.
I sometimes feel warmth on my skin.
And right this moment everything is
settling in, Good things will happen from now on.
