title: "When You Can't Get Enough"

The crawling through your stomach.
The pressure of heartache.
The moment never eases but everything breaks.
All those kind pleases and reasons.
The break out.
The pain never goes away.
The cure of "another sunny day"

The wind blows through your hair
while your at the edge of a cliff,
You think to your self that this is your gift.
What has drove you this far?
Why would you want to take the easy way out?
Sometimes its harder to live, then die.
IT would be so easy to take everything away.

It wasn't your fault he left you,
It wasn't your fault he cheated.
Don't give him what he's needed,
He wants you to be the one to alone,
He wants you to be the one that hurts over him,
He's like the devil in black that holds your sins.

Close your eyes
And dream...
listen to music and let your self scream.
Let those thorns come out, expose your pains and let them spout.
let your heart renew.
Don't let that asshole come back and keep hurting you.
Don't give him the satisfaction all he wants is your reaction
