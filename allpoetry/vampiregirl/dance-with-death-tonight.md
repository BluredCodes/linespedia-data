title: "Dance With Death Tonight"

I dance so slowly with you,
you lead my arms around your neck
while my body presses gently against you.

I have waited for this moment for so long.
Each night I hear deaths song.
I was captured and strung out on my own.

In your mind I feel your patience as we dance along.
your waiting for my breath to go out,
and for my body to let go.

Nothing is rational, everything extreme,
nothing is what we mean.

Gentle touches of rhythm, and voice
strung out on our own, to the force

I no longer need your lead,
Lean back and fallow me.
I will guide you to the heaven above.
You are death, to guide me to the worst place.
I refuse to go
I keep saying no.
screaming yelling,
No one hears.
Aching, breaking.
Painful moments sleeping.

Dance,
Real slow
Live for the last ten minutes then let your whole life go.
