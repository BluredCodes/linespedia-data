The Killers mind is rushing
And he speaks of moments of trust, before he brushes his victims hair
with a light thrust.


The victims thoughts empty, as her body is unsteady
and shocked.

Killers reactions to her feelings
is nothing but a plot.

The reactions of the actions:
Plane and simple,

He thought of it for days
before he made his moves.

The victims comments:
I had a gut feeling about working late that night,
I saw a man walk up to me, with a big pocket knife.

Killers thoughts:
hours of planning it worked out swell,
I will bring this bitch down, even to hell.

Victims parents reactions:
My only doughtier, this must be ransom
but we aren't sure.
Mothers thoughts: the killer must want my doughtier because
she's pure:

(Truth)
She's a slut, and a whore
that feeds off men's fake love,
She's a sinner and a believer in, god and hell above.

fathers thoughts of victim:
All I need is a phone call from this son of a bitch,
Ill track the phone line, in only minutes.

Back to the killer:
When I make this kill I will look death in the eye.
She will scream out loud, while her soul begins to fly.

Victims thoughts:
I didn't have a chance to say good bye.
