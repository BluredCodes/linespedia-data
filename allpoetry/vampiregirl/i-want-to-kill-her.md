Isn't it funny how you turned away?
So many worlds broken when you walked away.

Our fate was meant to be,
I was in you, and you were in me.
Our thoughts joined by memory.
our hearts open like the sea.

All lost, to the floating wind, that captured you.
She was beautiful, and she took over you,
The moment she walked in the door I felt something
come over me, It made me angry it made me mad,
I wanted to kill her so bad.

I wanted to stab her in the chest,
I wanted to burn her in the burning bush,
I wanted to hang her from stars
or give her a little push.
And laugh while holding you.

I bet I could think of many ways to kill her to.
Ill just leave her alone and see what happens to you.
Ill make sure you don't leave me, if you do she'll pay.

I want to smash her face in to the rocks.
I want to punish her in so many ways
I want to shove her in a box and mail her out of my way.
I want to get a video camera and tape it as long as no one sees.
How dare her, for thinking she could take you.
How could she possibly think she could get to you?

I want to run over her with my car,
I want to knock her in the head with a bottle from a bar!.
I want to poke her eyes out one by one, so she cannot see
what's going on.

I want to hang her by her nipples,
and hope she doesn't wiggle,
I hate her,
She doesn't deserve you
your much better then her
I know your hearts true.

If she comes around again, Ill knock her ass down,
Ill punch her in the face.
She can't have you, you'll always be mine
you better tell her, that! or she will regret
messing with my man I will pop a cap in her ass
and run her ass out of town!
