Lying in bed
in the middle of the night.
My fears have drifted in the window leaving me uptight.
My eyes closed, with my covers surrounding me.
Its as if I was dreaming.

The mirror on my ceiling leaves
my eyes staring at me.
Everything so clear.
My last moment of fear.
Suddenly everything goes quiet not a sound in the air.
I feel hands reach out from under my bed,
and touch me here and there.
The blood drips softly through the crack in the mirror.
dropping soft splots of blood on my lips.

Everything so bitter
I whip away each drop that hit my face.
Suddenly the down pour
the blood covers my white sheets
in red. The smell of a bad odder
that quilted me.

I was held down, and drained on
for an eternity.
Just then I felt the worlds pain
in that moment everything settled in place
Those that die live on, and we treasure our own pains
for something worth fighting for, each drop of
blood shed is a moment of being pure, being human
having a soul, something those that die will treasure no more.
