title: "Window Of Darkness"

The hell
you walk upon.

The valley you spit on
 

The world that you seek
the place that causes you pain.

The feeling that never settles in your stomach.
The blood gushing through the vains.

The yelling in your ears
that screams LET ME OUT.

The lost chance you never had.
All windows of darkness
turn sad.

A faded image
A heart felt point
a moment to start over.
A moment to drop the past.

A moment in time, that will never last.

The tree with out leaves to fall.
The cold winter breezes catches it all.

The ending
The beginning
The start
The Finnish.

The window, behind the window.
The eye behind the pupil.

The word
behind the action.

The action behind the word.

The thought behind the touch
The touch behind the feeling.

Everything meaningless.

The naked body
frozen hanging from a tree
That is the place I will always be
that is the pain that will always follow me
And that is the place you will only see.
