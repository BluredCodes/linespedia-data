---
title: "Vanish You"
writer: "VampireGirl"

I'm not your seed in which you grow.
I'm not the calling of your aching soul.
I'm not the sun on a winter day.
I am the echo of your forbidden way.
I am your angel that covers your eyes
I am your sadness beneath your skin that lies.

I am the skipping rose of a forever leaf,
I am the devil, in which you love
I am the carving stone of blood.

You are my hate
You are my Bitterness
You are my emptiness.

In my life
love seemed long
like an eye of rushing and going.
Where did I go wrong?
Still your on my mind,
Still my subconscious heart
feels you near, I say get away
Say nothing, Leave me here.

My ears do not hear,
My voice does not speak.
My body tingles when weak.

I cannot sleep,
I hate you for all that you are, and what you've
become.
You are a lair, and a bum.
I hate the very clothing you wear,
I hate the way you do your hair.
I hate the very soul that reeks off you.
I hope you burn in hell for all things you do.

I cry Over you.
dreams of wishing that I knew couldn't
come true.
I've placed my heart in your very jar,
you placed it on the road of death,
Looking to see what's left?
seeking for something knew?
your gone from my mind I fucking Vanish You.
