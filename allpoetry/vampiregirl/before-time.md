She touched her face
Not knowing her future or past.
She followed the stair case to the end
she smiled and looked around and memories had began.

All her loved ones in the same place they once were.
john and Rachel playing with their Christmas toys,
So many joys.

Her eyes teared up while she saw her husband for the first time
in many years, her husband died at age 34 in a motorcycle accident
and she lived her life in fear.

She had four children she knew so much about,
She loved them with unconditional love that poured out like a spout.
They died at a young age and lived on
in the locket placed around her neck.

Everything back to normal
Living in a safe place.
Smiling to the young ones, watching them run and race.

For once in her life, Everything came to mind
the past and future no longer out of time.
She belonged in the place of heaven.
The place were all her loved ones lived
the place in her heart that she can now touch.
And in her heart is the ones she loved most sleeps.
