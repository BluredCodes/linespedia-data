The rage
The feeling
The bring you down
The rush
the killing
the pound


The break
the bones
the blood
the fractures


(Hospital Scene)
doctors rushing
Blood still gushing
everyone searching for the paddles
the pain..........

The bodies
the smell
the moment of heaven and hell
...........................................

Funeral Scene
The music
The tears
The fears
the loss
the cares
the family
the pains
the worries
the shames
...........................................
Forgotten
*******************************************

The beginning
Ghosts
watching you
following you
searching for you
-----------------No one sees.........
The ending
