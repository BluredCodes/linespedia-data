Earth’s path crosses before me
A man on his knees is holding his hands out for me to take.
A man on my right is starring right at me. A bridge is before my feet
Earth’s path is falling behind  and my mind wondering 
About many things not knowing what to do Where to go
Or who to choose, My eyes go dim my head full of sin
Why am I taking the wrong path all over again?.
Earth’s path falling on me the dream catcher in the window
Trapping me in. I am sleeping but still away from  where i should be, Whats wrong with me?
Thoughts,feelings,faith,love, has drown in the dust of my bones
Life is everything it seemed to be, but earths path was waiting for me.
Earth’s path reaching for my hand, earth’s path is yet to make me
Understand that I have failed and have learned life wrongly
I have taken people for vein and needed learn strongly that i can change.
Earth’s path has made it rain, Earth’s path never lets me win
And for all I worked for in my life, earth’s path makes me work for all over again.
