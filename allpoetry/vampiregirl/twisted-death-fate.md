Arms open I reached for you
Suddenly the sky turned dark and there was no blue.
The moon came up, and raised from the high sky.

Me and you looked up, and you were thinking 
I was going to die.
You reach out and whisper in my ear its okay
something's happen for a reason,
you kissed me so gently.

You put your arms around my waist and said you would
complete me.

I felt my heart run dry of blood.
My tears were speaking the way I feel
My mind saying this can't be real.
You tumbled before my feet
your last breath shot out like a
bee buzzing through the air.
Your eyed closed
and your soul no longer there.
I had nothing left to say
Besides fate twists and turns in so many ways.
