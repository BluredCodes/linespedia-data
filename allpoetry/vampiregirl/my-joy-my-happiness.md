---
title: "My Joy, My Happiness"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In a single moment everything
felt right.
A happy moment of love, and life.
Joys and happiness floats around.
Nothing brings me down.
I'm like a brick wall that will never fall.

In my life everything steady,
for my whole life i've been ready.

I have no excuses, and no regrets
I let things happen and that's how I met "dreams".

My dreams, my cares all as they seem
I have found a happy place, that love brings.

My success, has been carried on
I have reached my goals and going strong.
I love my self for all I am, and all I've done.
I have never had to run.

Depression is only a word to me,
It means nothing to our society.
We feel a certain way, And if the feeling is sad
it turns our day, completely bad.

I ask why?
How can we let our self get down so far.
Just when we hit bottom, It rains on top.
Why can't we bring our selves back up?.

Sometimes life has no meaning,
everything bitter and nothing sweet.

But then your heart beats
everything steady inside
the happiest moment of our lives.

We didn't have to have money or men
Didn't have to get born again.
didn't commit any sins.

Just happy,
Just painless.
Everything full of success (My Joy My Happiness).
