Chanting I hear in the wind
My long black dress has been torn again.
I am no longer dancing as I was before.
So many things change
Expressions not made.

Help was supposed to be here by now.
Once again I was forgotten somehow.
My creations where never born
and left in the foggy rain.

My mind never strong enough to make the correct dissuasions
that have already been made.
My life some how lost its balance and I said forever it's stained.

So many moments mistaken for dreams
so many emotions heard my screams.

Lovely men all turn to beasts
in the night room of gloom.
Hands turn to claws like a cloud breaking through.

I crashed, and smelt my own flesh burn
and screamed till my voice shamed.
Still no one to help, And no one to break
me from the seen.

All T's my nightmare, All in my beams.
Crossing and caving, bodies and hearts breaking.
And dark black sheets were taken away
and out came the face of a brand new day.
Lights shined as if heaven was on its way
And my death began to play.
