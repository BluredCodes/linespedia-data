title: "Drunk Till Death"

Everything so terrible through the eyes of me,
I've lost my job, actually about three.
Everything meaningless,
My mind blank.
Ill drown these feelings in rum till they sink.
Ill kiss my loved ones good bye, they all expect
me to die anyway.

Every drink, I take, My life begins to break.
But that's okay.
To die, would be better then living,
Because in death you have no worries of loosing or giving.
