Magical Mystical night, Guides my way to you.
How when I saw your face I knew I was meant for you.
So handsome you were, with golden armor. and a sword across
your belt. I knew you would chase away my shadows, and swing me
from a tree, under magical water, I can see our future
that starts with you and me. And you capture my lips of honey
with just one touch of your finger tips, leaves my heart in midnight bliss.

So breathless you left me, with elegance and mystery.
you were drooling over me, I knew you wanted me.

You were absolutely gazing in to these eyes of mine,
That's when I knew that everything was fate for us.
How even if god tried to break us apart, Nothing would ever
end our living hearts.
