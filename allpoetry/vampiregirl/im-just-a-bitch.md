Don't worry about how I am.It doesn't concern you.Don't get pissed off, when my rage over whelms you.Hang in there, Ill get off this stream.Sooner or later, Ill go to sleep.I'm sorry I cannot contact my emotions.I'm sorry I am such a bitch to you,You know I love you dearly, But I have an excuses for everything.I have so many reasons in my head,They stay with me when I go to bed,I can tell you an excuse every single day, So I will not haveto say, I'm angry.When I say rude things, I mean them at the time,forgive me for my rudeness, they just some how got in my mind.When I'm a bitch to you, I wish you would ignore me,So I wouldn't have to be so mean, I know you hate when I'm this way.It only happens once a month, it will pass away.So go out with your friends, and come back later,And ill think of another reason, to be rude to you,This is just a stage of PMS, I hope you understand.I'm not this bitch that you truly believe I am.
