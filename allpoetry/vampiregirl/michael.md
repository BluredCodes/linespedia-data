title: "Michael"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In my heart I know its true
that god has dropped a baby boy from the sky
which has landed in these arms of mine.

My face with a smile all happy inside.
This is the one time in my life I'll never have to hide.
So wonderful he will be,
to see him looking at me
With his eyes of brown.

The smile on his face
that I will grace in harmony.

His little fingers hanging on so tightly
to my thumb, while he understands
his mommy.

His father not there,
But his mother always there to care.

In time he will understand why.
In time he will be a young man.

Till then he will grow in peace, live in my heart
and learn to ease every pain he has.
I will give him wisdom and courage
to understand the limits, and lines of life.
And let him know that the sky dropped 
him in my arms, for a reason
and this is why.

The meaning of my life seemed empty
and suddenly there was a meaning
something I've never had before
He will be my kind, and my hansom baby boy
that opens my heart with love, and cares
I've never seen before.
