Down in the city of a dark town.
I see nothing and hear not a sound.
From a far distance I see a shadow of the earth
like the moon is siting down.

Far away, I see a child holding a crown of red drawing
a dark gray and black sky.
She turns her head and looks at me, her eyes are black
with red specks, She says I will hunt you down.

I run, And see no light. And nothing to save me from the choice
I have made.
I see no one to save my soul, And take me to a better place.
Suddenly fog fills the air, I can't find my way anywhere
things so dark, and almost like smoke, I put my hands out in front of me
And hope my feet lead me, to a safe place not knowing what's beneath me.

So abruptly I run in to a door, Its golden and beautiful
with a red carpet leaning to it.
white clouds, surrounding me, And a man with wings reaches out
and takes my hand. And guides me to the promise land.
I am home.
