When the sun came out again, after years of rain.
All became clear, Everything so beautiful and bright.
The heart of angels dwelled for light.

They all put their dancing shoes on, and ran for the sun.
the music played while everyone could see the white puffy clouds run.
SO many smiles flew thru the air,
The demons of hell just sat their and starred
with a shallow look on their face.
God winked and let the rainbow float across the sky.
All eyes trapped on such path of light.
so many colors almost fruity filled with beauty.
Green, as lime, yellow as a lemon,
Red as an apple, and maroon like a plum.

Things so lovely.
Graceful, and Harmony.
The whole world for once happy.
Everyone danced,
They all sang
and forgot lost romance...
So much love,
we were all family.
It was the closest thing to heaven we will ever see.
And its the exact place I want to be.
