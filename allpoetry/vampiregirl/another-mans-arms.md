I went to a party the other night,
My hormones were raging, And I would go home with any man in
sight.

I felt my blood pumping while I made my way around.
My heart thumping to the sound of music bursting in my ears.
I was making a choice,
Trying to find a man
One to understand my emotional ride
SO I would not have to hide, the way I really am.

I was told, I was looking for love in the wrong places.
I refused to listen and closed my ears to everything
but the music's base,
And told everyone I needed space.

I made my self through the party crowd, and there you
were standing proud.
You looked like the perfect catch to me
A man I would love to spend my night with
And hopefully wake up in your arms.
And if I were lucky, you would be there till
my alarms went off in the morning.

We danced, We kissed, I was lost in emotional hormone bliss.

We went home, had sex you were gone in a light sleep,
Next morning my body felt weak
I felt alone.
once again no ones home but me.
