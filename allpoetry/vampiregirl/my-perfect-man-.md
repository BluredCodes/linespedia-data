The way he looks at me with those Glimmery eyes
that shine.
So hansom and all mine.

So happy I will be, to honestly say he completes me..
The feeling over whelming me that drives me mad.

His lips, those tender lips!!!!.... 
The touch of his fingers.
The forever lasting tingle, that swindles
to every move.

The way he talks so deep, so complex
everything he says when he is so relaxed.

His palms touching mine, his world turning mine.
Everything so perfect to me
Just like it should be.

I know every expression upon your face,
I know every single race of your heart.
Everything you say, I know it before hand.
I know each and every single demand
And ill always hold his hand,
close to my heart, a lasting moment with a forever start.
