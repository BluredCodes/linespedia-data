Caution to your heart for my mouthspeaks lies.Close your ears, so you will not hear my cries.I am guilty for all that you believe, and more.My love felt rich and chocolatly, butwas molded and poor.My heart was given, And taken away.And I regret this each and every day.My heart can never be opened again.And my eyes will always look in tostrangers eyes, for affection and touchfor which my soul craves, and what I miss and wanted so much.
