SolitaryTearofangels shows no fear to express the way she feels.
Her heart open to the wide spaces of love and thrills.
Her poems give me chills, each time she writes
the beauty of her words leave my heart tense,
You amaze me with your beauty of expressions and words,
You are a kind hearted soul, with a gentle Finnish.
Your love grows through out my heart.
Your caring wonder never leaves me in wonder
about if you'll ever part.

You are a friend I'm glad I have.
A wonderful gifted person full of knowledge.
A intriguing, caring, understanding
soul, that only deserves the best.
God has made you special, and gifted
to show all of us, the wrest.
Your lights show, your eyes glow.
And we all know
That Karen is the sweetest kindest person we know.
