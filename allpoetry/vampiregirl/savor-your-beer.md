In each drop
I see your face roll down the drain.
Its such a shame, that you were taken away.
Its as if a part of me wanted you to stay.

The moment I saw you lying there I knew it was to late.
Your soul flew off before I could catch it.
Everything you meant was forgotten.
your like a mouth full of cotton candy.
So soft, and so sweet, white, blue, or purple passion, as my heart
beat.

I didn't mean to hit you over the head so hard.
I didn't mean for you to lose control of the car.
Blood  poured out all over me,
You gushed your squirting drops of blood in misery.
Its all my fault,
but you shouldn't of touched me.

I told the police officers it wasn't my fault
it was an accident, its not as if I killed the president.
He was just a sorry ass lover,
another dream game under the covers.

Maybe I can claim you as a suicide.
take your life insurance and run and hide
I excuse my stupidity as a misapply.
Excuse my reactions If I cry.
Its not as if I wanted you to die.
Maybe the metal in my hands went to high,
and slammed down to hard,
I'm sorry that you missed your favorite song,
it was playing while you swerved.
Sleep walk, our favorite song to dance to.
Ill savor your fresh beer.
you know after all it is kind of funny my dear.
