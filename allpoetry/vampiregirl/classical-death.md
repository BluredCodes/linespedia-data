In a motion we twist
Upside down, and around.
In a moment of life we seek.
Amazing moments they bring.

Life from each breath we take.
Death from each word that breaks.
So beautiful the eyes of us all.
So magical, before the fall.

The moment the single dollars get placed over our eyes.
All our family cries, as our loved ones die.
Yellow bricks falling through the air
lost magic, of cares.

Sprits of hell wanting you,
Angels of heaven guiding you.

So successful, so unbreakable.
So unbearable, and careable.
Perfection..

The guest you invite in to your chambers.
The tint of death,
the air that catches our breath.

The over whelming spell
that was caste from hell.
The longest kiss.
The longest bliss.
Classical.......

The heart
beats and seeks for another,
But finds an empty place.
The mind races, the body goes in shock
it feels like your body was barred under rocks.

Your whole life swept away,
all passed down on a rainy day.
Cares and wishes all demitted,
All spells trapped.
All worries SNAPPED.

breakable
careable
bearable.
Your okay,
The classical
dance
the romance
the death,
The seen
the moments of true meaning.

The classical death..
The space of air with nothing left
the silver dollars over your eyes
the beautiful black box surrounded by silk
your skin pale as milk, your lips red
your eyes under the lids blue.

classical...
Coffin,
Classical funeral
classical heart of pain
classical ran down shame.
Classical cries
Classical questions of why?

Nothing left
the classical death.
