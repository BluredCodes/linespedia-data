How can you run out of words to say, When your mind and your body does
millions of things each day.

You find your self wondering around, Looking down,
With nothing to look up to.

You see your self, Looking in the mirror, And no one to look back at you.
You find your self asking questions you have no answers to.

You race to win, And never find the end, So you keep driving
hoping to get somewhere, And you find out that your miles away
and you pray you have enough gas to get you there,
And when your car dies, no one pulls over to help you make it through.

You must realize, Its all you, you must depend only on your self.
When your looking for a hand to reach to, When you think someone truely cares for you, Forget it, your wrong, no one could possibly love you,
They don't even know what real love is, so how can they give it?.

When you think of your self, as being a better person what do you see?
When you look at your self as a changed person how will you be?

Why can't you just realize everything feels wrong.
When you think you found the right place, you aren't stronge.
While your walking up the stair way, thinking your on the right path
someone walks down, and knocks you on your ass,

Whats to live for?
whats to get up for?
Love ? Hope? Dreams? 
Do you see these things?, how do you know they are really there?
