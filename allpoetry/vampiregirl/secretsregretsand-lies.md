The burn is deep and I tell secrets that I can't keep I spread lies like an eagle flapping its wings in the air I do things that I regret but there's nothing I can do!. I know you wish me well but you turn on the fire that burnt me down low, You had the match that was deep in your hands with the breath of liquor I still took your hand I loved you like I've loved no other, I held you like you were the only, Why must I live by the book of lies I hear my sorrow weaken as I tell another lie, Wish me well IM leaving I must leave now before i can't say good bye, before I hurt you and before another lie!. Im the feeling of burns that burn right through you. Hush don't speak I know you will miss me, say good bye then kiss me.
