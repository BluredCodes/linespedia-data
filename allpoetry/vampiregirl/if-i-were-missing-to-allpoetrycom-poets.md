If I had been on allpoetry.com everynight for months
And one day didn't show up would you be worried?
If I didn't come back would I expect a welcome back
from all of you.

If there was no Shelly, and no poems were posted by me
would anything be missing to you? would that in some way
bother you?.

If I never came to allpoetry.com what would I do?
Would I just get on ICQ and chat to people I do not know?

If I said I was leaving forever from allpoetry.com
would it be hard for you to let me go?

IF I came in to the chatterbox should I expect at least a hello?

If I post a million poems, for the wrest of my life
does it have a single impact on you?
If I write something personally just for you
would that make you feel more special then you are?
I know that when one of you are missing, I miss you by far.

I read new poets poems, I have made lots of friends
so many I don't even know where to begin.

I don't think I would write as much poetry, as I used to
most of the poems I post are at least two years old
but each poem I write at least took me an hour to write,
or more. 

Does that make you reconsider the ranking or score?
I put a lot of emotions, and poor my guts out to you
I hope to fill the post board up with
things that are true, Poems of feelings and personality
that are built up in me, past event moments and past event history.

If I were a movie star would that matter to you
Would the name Shelly be built up in your mind
But because i'm not a movie star at this moment of time
I mean nothing to you?.

If I had died only hours ago, there would be no way
for you to know.
If I was gone for a week at a time
I doubt I would even get an *IM* from anyone.

There are so many of you, I look at like stars
you are built up in my mind, like the planet of mars.
you light the fires that are burning in me, you inspire
and teach me manually. 

I love all the emotions floating around this room
when I read each individual poem.
I love all the expressions, and comments that are made
I love to see the chatterbox get filled with words.
When I come here, its more then words could describe
so I will try and type the exact way I feel.

I love your poetry, and I want to thank you all for keeping
it real.
Each one of you are a soul, and a movie star
each one of you are a hero, and the moonavar,
Think of your self, every time you look in the mirror
say you are beautiful, and say, you see it clear.

You are so much better then you believe you are
each day you wake up, and have the strength to carry you far.
You write poetry, and you are not afraid to and from this day on
I solute you!

To all the poets on allpoetry.com you are all the best.
Shelly.
