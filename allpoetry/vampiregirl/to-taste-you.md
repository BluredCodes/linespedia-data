---
title: "To Taste You"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Today your lips taste like a flavor
that I once ate.
Every night we kiss and make love under the moon light
by the apple trees.
Everyday you wake me holding me tight.
Love is a flavor full of millions of emotions
and what you relate them to.

A blue berry is just a blue berry with out
the touch of you.
Raspberry is a beautiful color, But nothing compares
to the taste of your lips touching mine.

Your skin like a peach,
and your eyes soft green.
Everything I see, I can relate it to you
everything I taste is a taste of you.
Everything that makes my mouth water
everything I crave and desire.

When we get depressed we eat something sweet
to sooth this feeling of emptiness and a lacking heartbeat.
But I have success, in showing that I'm growing
and finding a new cure for me,
And I must say that its something that makes me very happy.
And its the taste of you,
The warmness, that melts in my mouth.
someone I can cherish and care about.
A perfect dream of all that comforts me and the flavor that never leaves!
