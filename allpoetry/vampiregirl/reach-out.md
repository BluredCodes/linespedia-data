Whisper to me, Don't speak loudly or they might hear the fear in your voice.
Get close to me, Or they may take you away. Close your eyes or you may see something you wish not to see. Come to the closet that's where I will be.
Before you walk in, whisper to me, But no matter what you do, do not look at me. I know you waited for this moment for so long, I know that if you keep quiet this moment will last forever. Don't breath or they may here you
Don't cry when you open your eyes, I don't look the same as I used to.

You ask where can the police find my body?
I tell you in the river, in the forest among the trees
My body will be found there, IM naked and in chains covered in leaves, 

I am a ghost that talks to you while you are sleeping.
Please don't cry, 
I was not supposed to die.

Please don't yell, no one can see me the way you do.
Just keep your eyes closed, and don't be scared when I reach out and touch you.
