title: "To Everyone............."
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "to-everyone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46654-To-Everyone.............-by-VampireGirl"

Tonight I was thinking about the missing comments
I did not place. All the feelings and expressions started to
ease and erase.
I started to think about those that cared.
People that bared the same life, and understood how I felt.

I started writing out a list, of people to thank.
after I realized how many there were,
I knew that would take forever.
I did not mind, taking time to write a special note,
I did not mind, taking out a few hours to write something
to your hearts.

Then I got to thinking,
there is so many I would miss.
So I copied the author page, and pasted this.

I noticed I did not remember all your names.
I noticed, I did not comment on some of your work
to help your breaking hearts, or love loss, or pain.

I should had taken the time,
I should of taken the chance to get to know who you really were.
Instead, I spent most of my time, writing about me.
Still you took the time, to come and see.

I read a lot of poetry,
I learn a lot with different things I read.
So many of you, mean so much to me.

I could not say, who I like, or love more.
I count you all the equal, even those that did or did
not take the time to get to know me
I'm glad your here.
And thinking of poetry.

I am sure, after having over 1500 comments on my work,
and 300 poems, to show my feelings.
You're realizing what kind of person I am.
but this is the only way to get my point across
and express my best, thank you to all of you.

I would had went down the list, and gave each of you a thank you.
I was scared, someone would feel uncared for.
or if a name was forgotten.
I could not imagine, to think, I would not be even in the end
of someone's list.
So I decided to do this,
To make sure everyone's added.
Thank you, for being there.
Helping me through my rough and tough times.
The ones I know that truly care.
Thank you all.
And I hope you're never forgotten.
because you are a poet, and an amazing creation.
Love you all dearly.
I'm glad you have never judged, or replaced me.

*Shelly*
