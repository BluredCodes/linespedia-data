---
title: "What If There Was No Kiss?"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "what-if-there-was-no-kiss"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20152-What-If-There-Was-No-Kiss--by-VampireGirl"

Looking forward to the world, of love.
awaking to a heart of gold, and a stage
of silver. 
Among most things the thought of love
enters my mind a million times a day.
Words of I love you, getting in my way.

I wish someone would be here by my side
to cover me, and love me, and hug me.
How sweet it would be, for him to kiss me
each night, and wake me with a kiss every morning.

And his feelings over whelmed with the sight of my beauty.
If there was no such thing as affection,
if there was no words of I love you, If
there was no kissing what all would we be missing?
