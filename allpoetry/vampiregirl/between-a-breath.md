My heart cannot ignore the way I feel,
tears of heavens spaces has brought me here. I am not alone and you are near.

My universe expands to the beat of your heart
and the breath between us.
My milky way clusters.
My breath faster, my heart stronger. 
I believe you care now, do you know how?
my Saturn rotates to the sound of your voice
my earth shifts to the touch of your hands.
My comet hits earth when your here.
I have nothing to fear.
Jupiter falls from the sky when I cry.
mercury fills my heart with joy.
My long trail of love has been complete, 
My mind expands, my constellations perfect.
Your mission accomplished.
My moon is yours.
we are ready for take off.
We will always be together. There are no spaces
between us, No empty spaces and no universe to big
for our love.
