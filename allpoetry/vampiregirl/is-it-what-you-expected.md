What a lovely night to be alive. 
Look at all of us holding hands. 
We all believe we are stepping foot in to the promise land. 
What if its nothing like we expect it to be? 
will our selfish hearts, and hands come out empty? 

If we expect something, greater then it is.
is it somewhere we want to be? 
or will our hearts not allow correction? 
will our hearts and souls be set free. 

Will we have choices, to make, will their be hearts to break? 
Will there be bright lights guiding our way. or mirrors and shadows 
of the years we were away.
