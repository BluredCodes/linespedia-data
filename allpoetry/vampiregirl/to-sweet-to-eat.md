The taste, So sweet it melts in my mouth.Something's are better then love, or heaven,I would give my soul to touch my tongue to something sosoft. To have it in my hands for the first time.To take a bite and feel the juices run down my throat.For life to feel complete. Like plumes, with a touch of sour, For Peachesthat are fuzzy and sweet, its like the color I have never seen.There are millions of ways this could be described, But nothing could compareto my option on this, Its more tender then a kiss, and washes downthe pain, Life can't get much better when we compare it to fruitSomething's bitter something's sweet. Something's are just to good to eat.
