Babe My words were so clear in my head
But when I had the chance to say them
my face turned red, My mind went blank and I didn't know what to think.
In my voice I heard it shake, And I knew it wouldn't come out clear
So I turned around and whipped away my falling tear.

My heart thumped and run so abrupt.

I thought of this perfect moment for years
and thought I had it down well
I guess it turns out that when heaven touched me
so did hell.

My lips felt as if they were soft
and sweet, they got stuck together
the moment my heart skipped a beat.

My lips went numb and I knew that moment on
that I would make more mistakes
I tried to walk away before I took the wrong
takes.

My feet were unsteady and my body felt heavy
and I tumbled to the ground
my lips still numb and you were still around
I pointed at you and waved with my hands
hopping you would understand my demands
But of course your a man
and know nothing of my feelings.

You said I can't read actions
so talk to me,
I couldn't talk
but it got to me
I was so pissed off
and so embarrassed of what I had done
My legs got the strength to get up
and RUN.

Everything in slow motion
my heart was a devotion.
And everything went rubbery
my legs went out again and I run right in to a tree
oops.

Stupid me.
Look what men do to me
I feel so insecure.
I'm such a fool

Everything numb
I'm so dumb
But I love you hun.
