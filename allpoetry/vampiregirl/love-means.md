---
title: "Love Means?"
writer: "VampireGirl"

The thorns in my palms
The doubts racing when calm.

Inside the empty floating dream the
closest thing to perfect that ill ever be.
The meaning not clear, Yet sits in front of me
Looking in to my own eyes, the person I'll never be.

Insanity, magical mystery
the calmness of desires of my own destiny.

The cold air, filled my breeze,
the swift beaches surrounded by palm trees
the coconuts ill never taste,
my whole life a complete failing trace.

Inside
A lovely heart
A wild point behind a waving dart.
The target lies on the cold wood floor,
The person that's meaningless anymore.

Inside a nightmare, a part of me
lies asleep with out a future or fantasy
But after the day light hour I will never again
know what love means, besides its a dart 
and thorn that slowly rips me apart cruelly.
