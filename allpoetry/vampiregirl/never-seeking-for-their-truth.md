The Month of January is here for this is a new year.
People around us are getting wilder everyday.
People are growing older each and everyday. 
People have forgotten to pray and restless 
To believe in what they want their dreams to be of. 
And blame the ones they thought they loved 
Never seeking for the truth of what they 
Truly want their lives to be.
And live in depressions over things called
Memories. Peoples hearts get broken everyday.
And by next January peoples loving hearts 
Will once again forget to pray.

The End
