title: "Faint Ghost"

Behind my empty faint smile there is a frown.
My inside world turned upside down.

Tears on my pillow remind me
Of the pain
I beg for the pain to be relieved, 
But suddenly it causes me blame.

When I reached out for his hand
he pulled it away
I was left on the bridge on that cold rainy day.
He was lonely Just like me.
He never noticed I was the only one he could see.
I was the person he wanted to be.

Inside the flames burned
wild blossoms turned sour at the midnight hour.
All the trees fell to their graves,
and rotted to the core.
I felt like I had been destroyed
Every time I thought I was getting strength I was getting weak
every time I could speak, he would never listen.
Hes the only one I dreamed of kissing

Peace never came, And love settled in.
I had lost my heart and my soul
to the power of his winds,

He never knew what he was created for
I had the power to show him that, and more.
I was his fate, his destiny
but he was a ghost full of dust rightfully.
Angels and Ghosts can never be.
Still everything so faint to me.
