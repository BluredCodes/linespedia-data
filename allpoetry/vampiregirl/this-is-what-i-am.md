---
title: "This Is What I Am"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Dark heavens path has crossed me, 
death only inches away. 
My last words I began to pray asking for you to forgive me, 
Asking you to understand for the sins i've done,
a moment of peace was hopefully won. 
I am a seeker and a believer, 
I am a lover and a lair, I am a killer and a stiller,
I am everything you wish me not to be, 
I blame this on a memory of how I used to be. 
I believe that I am full of pain. My soul is a test,
that lye's at rest while I am sleeping. 
My happiness has fallen to the floor, 
No one wants me or comes around anymore. 
I am a careless soul, that loves my self more then I should, 
I cannot help the way I am, 
I always was a dreamer while growing up, 
but new I would never make it to the promise 
land with out making my self up to be someone I'm not. 
Forgive me if I'm a cruel heart, I am a fool. 
I am everything I wish not to be, I am your pain and your misery. 
Please do not ask me to stay, 
I could never do things the right way. 
I am no one you expected me to be, Don't you find it funny?, 
that this is who I am.
