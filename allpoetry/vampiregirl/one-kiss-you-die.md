my lips are bitter, for so long I would kiss every loverand they would fall over me. Die with a simple touch of my lips to theirs.Their heart seemed to stop, Their feelings and emotions out of control,so over whelming their body lost it all.My lips are poison to the touch, emotional bliss with a simple kiss.You will be mine forever, And I will take your heart in to my jar.You will never leave me, or ill conquer you.My eyes are golden, and that's what gets to you.My long brown hair, with a tint of blondealways makes you, want to run your finger through.My beautiful firm ass you want to grab on to.My smooth soft skin, you want to touch.My nice slim figure you want to fuck.But my lips are bitter, one kiss and you'll die.Make no regrets and you'll know whyI am worth the pleasure, then I say good bye.
