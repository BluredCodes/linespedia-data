To the top of the hill I roll
thinking no one will know.
Thinking there is no other place to go.

walking, and running till out of breath,
Feeling like there's nothing to lose
and I have nothing left.
Wishing and hoping things will change
before I reach the top,
Tumbling and falling all the way back down
and once again I fell to the ground and 
picking my self back up again.
