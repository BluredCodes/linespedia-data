Suddenly it started
I was jointed in love and happiness.
But all good things pass.
Like love, it flows through the sea,
drifts through the heart and comes out from under me.

When the person you love dearly
is leaving, and you've lost that wonderful feeling.
Its like a thorn in your side, and there is no way
you can hide.

Its like your eyes pour out from pain
and there is no one left to blame
your stuck alone in the pouring rain,
And he's gone.
nothing left to do,
Just live your life and stay blue.
And hope in your mind he's coming back to you.
