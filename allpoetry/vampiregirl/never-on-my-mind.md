---
title: "Never On My Mind!!!"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "never-on-my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

In my head
A dream of you, 
Someone I've always looked up to.
A patents and love that was true.
Someone that stayed on my mind, for the matter of time.

In my love, A harmony.
A memory, a joy.

A false fake toy.

In my dreams, are nightmare scenes of you leaving me.
The pain you caused is not harmony,
is not true.

I dwelled on you,
I felt you.
I needed you.

You hurt me
You stabbed me
you killed me.

You left your disease growing on me.
for the tears to roll down the drain,
This pain...........


In my heart an obsessive beat
the moment of sweet cares gathered by heat.
The attraction
the affection, the feelings.
The grasps for air, that's not there.

The thought of cares, and love
something to smother me in heavenly bliss,
All in a simple kiss.

You left
you ended it all,
down came the fall.
the wreck in to the tree,
the FALLING OF ME.

The fog over my eyes,
all the night cries.

YOU were NEVER on my MIND!
