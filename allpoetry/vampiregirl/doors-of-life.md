Behind the doors, we have rumors and wars.
We have troubles, and past. Things that will last forever.

Behind Door two
we have love, and loss, pain, and gain.

Behind Door Three,
Treasures, passions and dreams.

Behind Door Four,
Suffering, killing, destruction,
infections, harsh words, and lies.

Behind Door Five,
worries, Tears, alcoholics, drugs, fears.

Behind Door Six,
Insane, Troubled, failures, jobless,

Behind Door Seven:
Success, Happiness,grateful,
cares, graceful, harmony, cherish,
Marriage, faithful.

behind Door Eight,
Hate, mean, cruel, slutty, beautiful,
whores, pimps,sex,abortions.

Behind Door Nine,
Life, Death,heaven,Hell.

Behind the doors
we all see, a place in our heart that is a part of me.
Aching and shame, love and loss, games and San moments of mistakes.
Promises to break, Places to go, people to see.
The worries in heart of memories, Joy, and sorrow, Restful tomorrow,
Echoing rains, hollows of pains. Doors of names.
The Door behind each place, Of each day
a moment awake, a moment away, A moment a sleep, a place
we all go, Tomorrows a dream, and no one will know.
Doors you close, doors you open things you will never understand.
Harmony, and joy, gages the lust, and drowns your self down
with emptiness.
