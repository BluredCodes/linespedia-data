The Seven Eleven is closed
I have no other place to go
Looking everywhere
Nothing is open on new years.

I find my self getting very angry
my boyfriend left me.
I've been out for an hour.

I didn't have my period this month
I can't imagine anything else hurting this much.

What is my family going to think?
how am I supposed to react?

I don't know who's it will be.
Its a quarter past 3:00.
Excuse me for not being calm

I have done my self wrong.
I have nothing left to do.

But understand we make mistakes.
next time
ill make him wear protection
So I won't have this terrible reaction.
the next time IM looking for a place open
to find A DAMN test.
Ill do my best to find a man
that understands that poking holes in the condom isnt a good idea
And this is the reason why!
He will never hold my hand
I am only a one night stand.
Nothing more
Nothing less.
Next time ill do my best
not to forget to buy a test before hand
so ill have one on my DEMANDS!
