title: "Dare Me Not"

Dare me not for I will do things you 
wish me not to do. judge me not for I am perfect.
Hurt me not for I cannot be hurt. seek not for me
for I am deep inside you. Hear me not for I do 
not speak the truth. Care not for I will not be 
around forever. Love me not for I cannot be loved.
Kiss me not for my lips are bitter. 
Lust not for my body is not pleasure.
Blame not for this is the way that I am.
Curse not for I have been cursed with
A life I cannot live, And cursed with
so much pain, and I am unable to forgive.



The End
