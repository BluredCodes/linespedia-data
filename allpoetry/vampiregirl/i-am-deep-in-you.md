title: "I Am Deep In You"

As I lye here I feel NothingI am a ghost that can't be seen,I am the Ghost that cast spells in your dreams.I am the tarot reading of your cardsI will let you live or let you dieI am the black side of your eyes that can'tbe seen and can't look back, I am the darkest nightmaresin your pleasant dreams,I am death when you wake, I am lifewhen you sleep. I am the thing you do not understandBut crave when you are at your worst.I am your saddness when you are sad.I am your anger when you are mad.I am your mouth when you do not have words to sayI rule your life In so many ways.
