title: "Power Of The Soul"

You have control over me
I would beg and plead for you.
I would kill, or cut my wrists to see you.

You have power over me, Like no one before.
You are my demon that closes my door.
You are my dragon that sucks the lifeless breath.

You are my mind, that makes me believe, things that are untrue.
You are the devil, And god looks down on you.

You are my freedom, and I cannot leave.
I'm in chains and have been deceived.
You are my reflection when I look in the mirror
you are my angel, with black wings.
You are my hunger, when I have nothing to eat.

you are my blood that flows through me
Everything I am, means nothing to you.
you gave everything to me, and you can just as easily
take it away. Its not a job to you, you don't get paid.
You are my ears, That whispers softly.
you are my fear, of dyeing.
you are my tears, when I'm crying.

You are the power, greater then me.
You are my soul, that's deeply abused me.
