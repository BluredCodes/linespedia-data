People suck what can I say?people lie, each and every day.You are normal, in every single way. So don't make your self feel different today.You are gross and you know it to, I hate every single thing about you.You have the worst personality i've ever seen,And you are dog ugly, and you know what I mean.Your a fuck up, through my eyes, I wish you would roll up, and slowly die. Your a piece of shit from hell, Does the name Mark ring a bell?You are white trash to me, And you made it quiet clear.I heard it in your voice, the moment you called me your dear.I think of you, as an asshole that always has to talk, you never shut your mouth long enough, to listen, SO FUCKING STOP.You look like a fag, and you walk like your mommy, Andyour name fits you well, year a bastered, that smells like something died two years ago.You think your so different, because you dress in black, I thinkyou need to take that shit back to wal-mart.Maybe if you were a true gothic, you would look past your nasty ass,And learn to get a bath!!!!..
