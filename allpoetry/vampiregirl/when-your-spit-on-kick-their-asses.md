While your down on your knees,
Those who come to you are not to please.
They push you down further and spit on you.
DON'T let them do that to you.

You want to pull your naked soul off of the ground.
and come back around, with a nice kick to their face.
You want to look them in the eyes and say BE GONE
you disgrace.

In your heart its telling you to fight,
your body feels weak, and your mind is up tight.
You are afraid of fighting back.
I say get those fearing moments out of your mind,
and Punch and kick them in the sack.

Fight till death,
WIN till there is nothing left.
Don't let people push you around.
Don't let them kick you when your down.
Bring a board to their head.
Bring a knife to their throat.
Kill them if you must, but do it with a fast thrust.
Your better then that,
Your better then them..

You can beat any man, or woman.
You are the one with all the courage, be brave,
stand strong. lift your arm up to the wind,
and beg for power to begin.
Break that assholes face in.

You are smaller, then everyone else.
You have had it hard.
Let your self break lose, and show them who's boss.
For once in your life don't be weak,
Let your pains leak,
grow some balls, and speak.
Tell them the way you feel,
Make it the real deal.
Because you are strong,
you are happy.
don't let some dip shit man or woman treat you snappy.
