title: "Dead & A Live"

Wishes under the night fall a tear upon my inner eye.
The pupil that stares inside the coffin,
While the other sees me standing extremely high.

two sides,
A twist of fate.
Living with love and hate.

A joy, a depression
a slow reaction to my needs.
The feeling soothing, and killing
the crumbling to my knees.

The aching, the pain.
The masturbation moments wasting away.
pleasure is a teasing thought roaming around
an empty mind.

breaking through my stomach with
a rusty knife.
Feel my pain.
Let the bleeding cover the floor
and spell out my dieing name.

I am haunted with gloom and light.
dead and alive under the rain,
the coven of my brain.

Inside the mirror of glass, it holds my future
A picture of me shooting my self in the head,
sleeping peacfully in a forever bed.
cutting my wrists and
Draining the pain from my finger tips.

The other side:

LIFE:

Through the white glass mirror
a glimpse of happiness, and a longing for fear.
The perfect picture of me,
The person I seek to be.

the other side:
 
DEATH:

The person I could never be
the person thats dead in me.
