Is my heart empty or incomplete. Why is our hearts empty when we stumble with our feet. Can I not find a pulse nore dream to sleep, or do I have no mind to think?. Why does thee look and smell so sweet and thee is so sour to eat.I cannot learn to drink from empty cups. when I feel abrupt. Can I ever learn to see, when I look for love I come out empty.
