If I told you I could make you disappear
and reappear anywhere.
What would you do?
would you think I was crazy? would you believe that its true.
If there is a million places for you to be
what memory would first strike you? would it have to do with me?

Does your life have pain and regrets?
what if I promised you, it could all be changed
you could make your self a brand new name.
You could be the richest in the world.
You could be anything you set your mind to.
What if you went back to the past
where we first met?
the moment that I know ill never forget.
A place where no time exists, a moment
trapped in a moonlight kiss.

The surroundings were perfect
we were under the trees
the grass was greener then ever
and that night we pleased each other.

I know I've been back in that moment a million
times before, hoping someday you will settle
the score, and talk to me,
Listen and let me teach you correctly.

I miss the long talks late at night
and the warm moments on a cold day.
You were my happiness, but it all was washed
away.

Tell me that if you had the chance
you would go back in time,
say that you promise
that I will remain yours for the wrest of your life.

Believe me when I say that I speak of our love this
way, because I want you even more everyday.

And no matter how long it takes
Ill keep drifting to the same place
waiting for you to capture my endless time
and come running through and never behind.
