---
title: "Will I Join You?"
writer: "VampireGirl"

When the night comes
and the sun falls from the sky.
my heart will stop, and my body will die.
And in the moment before peace
I will see your face flash before my eyes
and memories that will never fade, 
and always survive.

In my heart I know your waiting,
And for once this step is worth taking.
For so long I had no needs, and no cares
now I will stare death in the eye
and slowly cut my wrists and drain my life.

ashes will be scattered through the wind,
The funeral will be small, and easy
not many people will come to see me.
And that will make no difference to me.

My question for years, that I kept asking my self
while my heart was full of tears
Should I join you? will I join you?.

My choices are easy, A do or don't question
enters my mind. Nothing stopping me,
no one asking me to stay.
If I leave this life to be with you
ill no longer have to run away.

If I just once get to see you,
ill look in your eyes
and give my self to the devil,
or the lord, which ever will accept me.
Just one look at you, is all I need
to see this through.
One look at you, and all my questions
will be answered, And I would know what to do.
