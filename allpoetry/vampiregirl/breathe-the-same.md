title: "Breathe The Same"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In less then a breath she turns. 
She sees lights from the dark and fallows.
She finds paths she should not take,
she makes choices she should not make.
And lost in a spell of life & death.
her courage is strong, 
Her eyes dim, and boiling hot,
Her skin pale and cold.

Life is nothing as she expected it to be,
Pain and heartache with misery.

She loved, she lost,
Her heart had a toss of a coin for life,
and lost at death.
She worked her whole life for nothing,
she ended up with pain.
she gambled and lost the game,
and now she's six feet under and
will never breathe the same.
