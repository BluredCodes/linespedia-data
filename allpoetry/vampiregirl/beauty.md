A beautiful face behind a saddened smile.A beautiful face that will not show its 
pains. A beautiful face that has all bad memories 
erased. A beautiful heart that most people pass away from. 
A beautiful kiss that are not sweetened till they find the right person. Beautiful tears that is tears of happiness 
after they find the person of their dreams. 
a beautiful soul that gets taken after death. 
Beautiful eyes that Reveals all about them self and drives 
their loved ones mad,the beauty in you that takes time away, and makes me live life day by day.
