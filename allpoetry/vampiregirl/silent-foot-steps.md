title: "Silent Foot Steps"
writer: "VampireGirl"

When your eyes are tired and your body is week
your tongue is swollen and you cannot speak.

When everything you feel doesn't seem real
and all that you believe failed.
When your heart fells to be beating
rapidly with out an ending,
And you're soul rebelled
Remember you have to start at the worst to make it to your best.
When everything around you echo's through your ears
and you've lived your eternity in fear
run away.

if you're body begins to shake, and there is no way
to break this emptiness that holds you here,
stay.

When you feel that you are getting pushed away
and so many words are never said
remind you're self softly in your head "It will be okay"
When love falls along with the leaves, and swifts
through the trees to the ground
calm down, the love of your life has been on earth
for a long time, let him find his perfect song
let him live his life till the day (he sees your beautiful
face down the hall way of everywhere he walks)
And let him hear your voice is silent talks.
