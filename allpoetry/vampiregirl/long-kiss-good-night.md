You were everything to meDestiny fate.Jealousy.I lust, and wanted only you.You were my one heart that would beat for two.I have loved you from the momentI played quake with you.Suddenly things get dim,Things you do not understandLights off, And you back outYesterday is goneJust another sad face blowing in the wind,Fate rusts, and he moves inOne Long Kiss, Good Night, And Good Bye.
