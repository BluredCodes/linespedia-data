title: "Before Your Eyes Close"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Before Your Eyes close, See the glimpse of me.
Let the bitterness flee.
Let your heart race, like it did the first time
we were face to face.

Remember my kiss
And I will remember your finger tips touching mine.
We were together till the end of time.
There was no world between us,
No air to keep us, apart.

When I sense your soul
I feel you.
The morning and sorrow of my heart never again
to be whole.


The sound dripped like an echo, going down an empty
lifeless drain drop... after drop.....

The pain.... 
The thought of never seeing your eyes open again.
To never again feel your body over me.
and this will forever be the end of me.

To dream,
And never have a dream come true.

I hopped before you passed on
and left me, you had the chance to look in my eyes.
I begged hell to take me, but I was denied.
I love you
for all that you are,
and everything you've become.

I hope that you looked in my eyes
and felt my love was true.
Because with out the glimpse of me,
there was no real you.
