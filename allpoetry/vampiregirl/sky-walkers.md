Sky walker
Draping in sweat from the sun,
He molds, and hammers, and builds his life high,
he builds stories to the sky.

Sky walkers join together to make a building last,
to build something in their life.
They cross boards, and metal, and grease.
They hope to never fall to their knees,

They work all day, till the moon starts to show.
they get up at 5:00 in the morning
and work another hard day and never give up or let go.

They think of nothing but their family, and all their bills 
they must pay.
They think of their children,
and making enough money for Christmas to buy
their baby boy that train set, or their baby girl
the perfect Barbie.

They work, and hope to never fall,
the sky lifts them to stand tall.
thousands at a time, working spending their life
building America adding something more to this world
they gain alot, and work more then an average hour day.

At the end of six weeks, the building is built
and they are appraised, people clap and scream out their names,
their children smile, their wives happy.

And they have reached the sky.
