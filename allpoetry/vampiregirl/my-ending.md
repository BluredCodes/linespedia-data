In to the land in which I lye
Dark skies seem high.
Death has came from this shaking breath of mine
aching and pain has came from this beating
heart of rhyme.

Stormy rains, Hurricanes,
I am always standing still.
heaven falls and im outside and still left behind.
There is a loud bell ringing in my ear
this is a whisper that's been saying soft
words I was meant to hear.

Love and laughter all went after
the man that left one day.
so many words broken, so many promises
busted open, in to the world of words.

I went to a wise man, and asked for advise on me
He looked in to my eyes and said softly
nothing is made easy and all things come to an end,
live life for to day and learn to forgive.

All the problems you have in your life will never
be fixed, when the mountains tumble to the earth you
will run ahead, Take hand of all you hold
and all you love, or walk alone.
Remember the sky is big enough to each her own.

You have always been alone, and you always will
even when your with someone it never felt real.

Angels and gods over the stars will one day lead you home.
Die today, and cry tomorrow and you will find your way.

Then everything was over
it was the end and just another sad day
my mind just stopped and I reached the same place
I was yesterday
life is never what I make it, and I can't learn to live
for today.
 my breath stops
and ill be barred under the same ground you walk upon
and sing to the sky another sad song of sorrow
which pleases you most to hear, and live in fears
that I might have to go through the pain of you leaving once more
and die again under the moon till the light shines no more.
