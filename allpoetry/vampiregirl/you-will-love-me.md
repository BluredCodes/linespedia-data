The darkness fills the air. The rhythm of the night steals the moons glare. The dust in the wind is no longer swaying since you left home that day. The moment you left I felt the tornado touch down on me, I had no shelter to run to, No place to go no one to hold So I was made to let go.I still can't see through the dust but I will always leave my arms open for you. I hear the thunder when you sped away. I saw the lighting of my love that scared you in this way. I know it was tough to live with me. But I know one of these days you will love me.
