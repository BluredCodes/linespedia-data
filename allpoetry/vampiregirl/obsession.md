Obsession my fetish is rather cheap
I'm a creep.
I love your ways.
I know each expression placed on your face.
I am amazed.

Everything a faze.
A reaction, a noise pounding through my mind.
I need for your worship and your time.

I want to desecrate your mind.
Pound your heart in to my soul.
Run my nails down your spine.

You are my crush and my fantasy
If I don't have you, you will pay.
I will search for you, no matter the state your in.
I will gag you with my blood
and trap you in.

I will create your thoughts, and visions.
Ill control your eyes, and mouth.
Everything you see and touch will not be real.
I will show you how to feel.
Excuse your ignorance and your thrills.
I will drink your blood after the kill.

Everything around you poison
I am pure. Let me be your cure.

Let me sink my teeth in to you,
let me kill all woman you talk to.
My obsession is more then you know
I will hold you here by the single thread.
I will cover you in my bed.

Everything around you sinks away,
everything I will dream of doing will be done today.
You will be my pray.
Excuse my ways.

Do not panic when you see my face.
Do not scream, go down with dreams.
Close your eyes if you must,
It will all end with a simple trust.
Your eyes will bleed, your body will shiver
things will get cold and your soul will quiver.
My obsession is more then a thrill
I will Bury your body under my lust of kills.
