title: "Swollowed"

The clouds swallowed by the darkness in the sky. 
The trees tumble to their knees, and question 
mother earth of why? 

How could the lakes and oceans dry? 

How could you let all these beautiful creatures 
die? for years they lived, then suddenly surrender their hearts 
and destiny? 

The wind misses the grass, 
and the grass misses the breeze. 
The trees miss their leaves, 
And limbs of mother bees. 

Humans 
were another kind 
they can't find a place to live 
no water to pour out the leaking holes of their souls. 

Who was to blame for this destruction? 
No peace, and only a reaction....... 
Seems very typical to me, 
Uncaring, unkind people living their 
meaningless lives miserably. 

How could they look at the birds 
and not give a damn? 
The thought of never hearing a tweet again.. 
The thought of the sun covered forever by the moon. 

forever the world swallowed by gloom. 
the echoes no longer flow through the Mountains above 
they lie cold down on the ground of hate. 

Forever this is the life you've lead up to 
your children, and grand children now blame you. 
Hurricanes, And storms are at the rise. 
You will never again survive. 

and they say we had no power over this? 
Our minds are the ones that have created 
these creators of death, swallowing us, 
and taking our breaths. 

We took the path of gloom, rather then being judged. 
We took the land of death, rather then heaven and love. 
Is this truly what we get for turning on god above?
