Another sleepless night, starring at my ceiling. 
No one to talk to, No ones around. No one cares any how. 
I lye in bed thinking of you, and wondering if your sleepless to. 
I don't know where you are, or who you might be. 
But somehow your in my memory. 
are you there, I know you must be. 
How many sleepless nights have you had? 
how many nightmares have you dreaded? 
When you get online, do you think of me?. 
Probably not. 
Ill just keep hitting my self in the head asking what's wrong with me, 
I'm alone, another night. 
no one cares, where I might be. 
I wonder if anyone at all, would even think of me, 
All my family disowned me. 
I'm on my own, 
and always will be. 
I'm stressed, 
I'm panicking over faded memories. 
But my only good dream, I must keep in mind, is 
that you are out there somewhere, 
And you are thinking the same thing as me, 
the very same moment I'm thinking of you
and fate might bring me to you.
