---
title: "Thoughts On You"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "thoughts-on-you"

You are my Breeze
My Trees.

You are my dream.
You are everything I've always wanted.
You are perfect to me.
More then I ever needed.

I know one day we will meet, and never be departed.
I will show you how much I love you,
And show you the way I feel,
I will show that I'm not perfect
But my love was always real.

I will give you my heart,
And leave it open inside,
For you to find a safe place to hide.

Our first date will be taco bell.
Our first place we go will
be the cemetery not far.
I will show you the park I played at.
The hotel I pass by everyday.
I will show you my one room apartment
Near by the highway.

I will be your wonders,
And your dreams, I will feel them up
with all the special gifts and meanings.

But this,
is no sweeter then a kiss, that means nothing,
And just like everything else
Its all a lie so i will turn away
so you will not cry.
