---
title: "For The One You Love"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "for-the-one-you-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Would you try for the one you love?
Die and give all your above?
Let your heart fade, Let your body rush or misbehave?

When your body trembles to the sound
of her voice? does it make you weak?
to know she has that much control, even of things you speak?

When the one you love, wants to dance
slow. Will you give your life
for the one that plays your roles.

When she breaks your heart, and slowly
rips you apart, do you still love her?

When your heart hurts, and your body aches
and the only sound you hear is the moment of break
do you love her?


When she feels dead inside, 
and she hides all her troubles from behind.
Can you love her?

Will you forgive her for her mistakes?
Will you give your hand,
and fix her earth quakes?

If she were to die?
would you find another to take her place?
to kiss her soft moist lips and savor her taste?
would you uncover her secrets
to live through her eyes again?

Would you give her your last breathe
just to see her for the last time?
Even if she belonged to another man?

When she's with you
Will you treat her right?
Hold her tight?
Make love under the moonlight?
And never leave her?

Do you promise
never to deceiver her,
always believe her
and care for the rest of your life?
and one day consider making her your wife?
