Stuck between earth and a child. Pushed in between the weeds. Stabbed and barred with no time for pled. Aching and suffering, Everything pushes me down. I must search for a place that's no longer around. A place I can treasure, and a place of gold, something made of steal and that will never let me grow old. My wish is to live this moment forever, And to always feel the ocean on my feet, How that must feel sweet, to feel the breeze pressing against me, How everything so heavenly would surround me. Eyes that see everything so happy, and no longer gloomy. A field of grass that  no weeds could  touch me, no weeds to grow, No ground with snow, No rain to flow, no fog to glow. A beautiful sunny day, and a beautiful summer night, with  stars and a romantic moment with my love holding me tight, and a place I can grow.
