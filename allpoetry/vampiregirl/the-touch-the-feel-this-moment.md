title: "The Touch, The Feel, (This Moment)"

The feeling of a cold moment turning warm.
How everything stops, And the sun comes out to play.
How winter slowly drifts away.
Spring time is my time of the day.
I love the way, The sunshine's down on my face
And the music from the trees, And the soft
slow breeze, pushing against my skin that I feel.
its the touch I wouldn't give for anything.

Its worth a million lonely nights
Its worth being cuddled up alone
Its worth hanging up the telephone
or getting over you.

I would rather Just sit on my front porch
and watch the stars.
I would love to find planets with my telescope
places like mars.

I want to walk on the moist grass with my feet,
And not worry about a thing,
I want to whisper, to the wind and get an answer to your shame.
And hope that nothing could ever take away a beautiful
moment like this.
for I know there will always be another,
But this year it will be even harder to let go.
