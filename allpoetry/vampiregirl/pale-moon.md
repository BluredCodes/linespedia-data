Pale Moon shines down on you. from the darkness high aboveThis could be my last chance to find true love.I never expected to find someone such as youYou lived so unselfishly but so distant too.You were the one I so longed to touch, so longed to kiss.But you've been dead over 20 years and missed, I've cried at least a million tears.I went to the shed and found a shovel and I kept hearing my mind rumbleunbury you, I knew my heart was set on you.The day you died I kissed your lips one last time.and my weepy heart could no longer riddle or rhyme.I crave youyour like a taste of wine I had to drink for the first time.Everynight, I would taste the blood from your neckand hope you would not wakeup.EveryNight I would lye by your side ten minutes before the sun would rise.I knew I should have given you life, the first time I laid my eyes on youI knew that nothing, not even death could take me away from you.If I share my only breath would you reach for me?.If I gave you my only life, would you live for me?If I spare another ones life would you come back for me?If I unbury you and beg of you to stay with me, would you?Must I bleed for you too?
