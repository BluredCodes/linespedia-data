We both lye thereBreathlesspale.Words were not sponge of our mouths that swell.We prayedWe dancedwe lovedWe chanced.We lived together in a place of memorywe kissed each other, and made love so easy.Our dreams, and hopes all as one,we gave our souls to the lights and won.And caste our selves down, to the bed and gavelife for those in need,We grew a tree from just a seed.And we are happy were we are, living like thiswill get us far. And no one can break us apartyou are in me, and I'm in your heart.and this will be put down in all the books of lifethat better judgement should be left alonewhen we have bitterness or strife.Another happy ending foreverand I will always remain in your book as life, and you willremain in my book as love.
