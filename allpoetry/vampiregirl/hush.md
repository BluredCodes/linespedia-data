If you listen silently you will hear her cry, as her bodyslowly starts to die 'Hush' or you may miss the chance to seesomething you want to be.Endlessly shes in much painShe hears her name blowing in the wind.Trees dropping leaves on her.the ground shaking, she never feels steadySomething is following her, she feels hands reaching out to grabon to whats left of her life,she closes her eyes so she will not be in fear,She cries but no one wants to hear,Shes walking down the black road of deathno lights are left, the only place shes headingis straight down,And still she searches for her soul and will forever.
