While sleeping a dark shadow came to me,
With the smell of liquor and purity.

I could see his eyes that was boiling red, and
I heard all the words he kept repeating in my head.
His arms open, As he lend down to my bed,
He put his hands under my blanket
And rubbed his hands up my inner thighs.

In to the world, of lies.

In my dreams
I felt his seen
and everything he means.
Heaven has turned me down,
and has destroyed my ticket tonight
My last chance for hell, has been forsaken
on a lost lonely flight.

Every place I was headed is a place I will not go.
The devil gives me the ticket of life
and gives me all.

He says its something he's never done before.
He said he could not see such a face burn
in fire, When I am worth more.

I took a deep breath, and closed my eyes
He showed my future, and gifts 
of my life.
He gave me the passport
to make it back home to
sleep and never again be alone.
