Wet and pouring rain out side, thunder and lighting striking the ground,
tornados making no one feeling safe, and no way for them to go home,
no insurance to pay for their lose, No help from churches, or family.
It seems like god has cracked down on, us all.
The clouds, slowly start to lower to the ground, Hell breaking from the sky
winds 100 MPH, making things fly in the air,
some with shelter some with out. (animals with out shelter has gotten left out).

Cars, flipped over, men and woman with metal through their heads.
Children under bricks, and wood, Some children under the car hoods
thinking their safe.


news channel 9 telling everyone to stay calm
telling those in the town that nothings wrong.

People die,
And children with out a mother or father, to guide them through life.
And when the tornado's Go back in to the clouds, everything is
scattered, some forget who they are. And lose all that they have.
And come out half a live.
