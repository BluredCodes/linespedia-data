When you forget how to live
And you've given all you can give.
Everything you created
Everything you've done, Still your on the run.
When someone touches your face, you forgot how that feels.
And the moment someone does it again, it never feels real.

When the lips you've been kissing turns bitter
And your body cries out with shivers.
When everything you touch is cold.
When the mold grows strong and old,
And your alone, walking along.

No one to talk to,
No one to even say a word.
You've given your life to take care of someone you loved,
When death touches your face
and erases your pain,
when all you die for regains.

When you panic and go insane.
All moments of happiness turn to rain.
Sad memories of pain.

And death once again looks you in the eye
you go weak in the knees and think your going to die.
Give in, Let go,
drink your pain of poison and let no one know.
