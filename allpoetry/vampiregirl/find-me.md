title: "Find Me"

Place to place I roam
With no sudden place to go.

Trying to find my way home.
Not knowing where that might be.
calling on a cell phone, Looking
for a friend or family.
No one seems to want me.

I'm like a lost puppy.

i've been out on my own,
since who knows when.
I hit my head in a car wreck,
and I don't know where i've been.
The winter, Seems to catch up to me.
While its raining in the inside of me.

I wish someone would stop and pull
over, and ask me if I'm okay,
I wish someone would offer me a ride
to a place that might be warm.

I'm lifeless, And careless 
of everything,
I drink the cheapest bottle of
wine every night.
I have nothing to call mine.

Times wasting, and yet I feel out of time.

I have no one to turn to and I feel all alone.
Wishing someone would find me, 
Or fallow my steps or show me the way home.

I've been sleeping on this park
bench for about a month or so,
With no blanket, to cover me
from this pouring snow.

And when I'm out on my own,
I cannot seem to get my head on straight,
Or what might have happened to make me this way,
All I know to do is pray
That someone will find me someday!
