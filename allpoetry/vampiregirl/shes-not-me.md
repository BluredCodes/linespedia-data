title: "She's Not Me"
writer: "VampireGirl"

She maybe more then you ever dreamed of.She might cover you in her love. She probably pleasures you in a certain way. She probably has all the right words to say. I bet she makes you laugh when your sad, or makes you happy when your mad, And I bet she seems so perfect to you, But this can't be true because she's not me and there's no wayshe could be.
