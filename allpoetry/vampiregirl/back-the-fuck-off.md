title: "Back The Fuck Off"
writer: "VampireGirl"

You know what I hate most?
Is when someone wont stop when you say no
Someone that keeps on coming back for more
someone that's worth less then a penny and not a single cent more.

People I hate seem to always come around
Its like they always know when to poke me when I'm feeling depressed
or lying on the ground.
Why must they be so rude and cruel?
makes me want to smack the hell out of each of them
And make them answer *ILL STOP with a yes ma'am*
I can't stand Losers of all kinds
I'm a higher rank, And the kind of girl
that has many goals in life, And I know exactly what I want
I either get what I want or don't give a shit.

People around me
are falling behind I refuse to be even named *In the same group at the wrong time*.

I excuse my self with a (I'm out of here)
And they slam the door as if I'm going to fear what they might do
But here's some words that just might hit you.

I don't give a fuck what you say, or what you do
I want your ass away from me because I don't care for you.
If you aren't as successful as me
you bring me down, So don't ask for my key.
the key I hold his made of gold, and I refuse to open the door
I'm tired of hearing excuses and bitching about things like *IM SO POOR*
if you aren't rich, stop bitching
get your life on track or start hitch hiking around.

Your a bug that must get smashed
and your a big prick that's only trash
Stop messing with me
I can't stand you
I wish I would of let you go along time ago
Because today I wouldn't even be thinking this over.

Here I am so much higher then you
you moan and grown over things that can't help you.
Control your self get help,
Talk to your girlfriend and ask money from her
Your just another poor boy, or poor girl
That can NEVER understand
that I will never again offer out a free hand.
