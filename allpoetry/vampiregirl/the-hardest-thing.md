title: "The Hardest Thing"
writer: "VampireGirl"

The hardest thing was to tell you the truth,
So many lies I made you believe.
So many times did I deceive you.

I couldn't help but to try and make my self look better then I really am.
I couldn't help But to try and make you love me more.
But I failed and made my self look poor, the very day I made my first lie.
From the Moment I said, Hello, and good bye.

Everything you thought of me, was nothing but a lie.
And I made you think these things were true,
I built your trust up and I broke it down.

Almost a year later, I told you the truth, But you still wouldn't believe a single word I would say.

I told you I loved you, but by the time I told you the truth you
didn't feel the same way.

I lost my chance to be with you. And I have no one to blame but my self.
And I still have not even met you.
And now you are with another woman, And it pains me to see
that all this time you could of been with me.
But I made the mistake
I let your heart break,
All because of me.
