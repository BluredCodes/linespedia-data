---
title: "Save You"
writer: "VampireGirl"

The pain you have, drives me crazy.
Its as if I can't reach where you are.
Everything moving so fast, and I'm moving so slow.
I caught my breath, and let your hands go.

My wings, fell off
and I could not save you,
My eyes were sidetracked, and I couldn't see you.

The world is spinning, and driving me insane.
I cannot help you, when you are in pain.
I think relieving you from your sins, and your
hate, would for fill the emptiness in you.

I  believed if I reached the stars, you wouldn't have to.

I thought if I were there, everything would be okay.
But I cannot save you,
I cannot live this way.

Your body is soon to let go
your heart is soon to stop
your breath is soon to not come back.

The world will feel steady, 
I do not know if I am ready
But I will try.

Its as if I'm carrying you around
while you slowly die.
You are on my back, pushing me to walk
telling me to go, When I can't.
I can't save your soul,
I don't know how.

I can't drink your pain,
those who say I try, call me insane.

I thought of killing you,
putting a pillow over your face,
And saving you.
I thought about kissing your lips and
taking your breath.

I thought about doing, unthinkable things.
I am not the one to blame.
I cannot help this stress, my life is a mess.
Every day I live, Ill live for you,
Everyday I breathe ill breathe for you.

Every time I touch the sky, ill run my fingers
over your face.
I've relieved you, since you were only 10,
let your body die, and be born again.
RELIEVE ME...
I can't save you....
I haven't the strength to.
