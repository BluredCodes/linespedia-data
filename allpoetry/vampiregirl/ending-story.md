Another sad page turns.Our mouth runs out of words.Secrets told, promises broken life is to unworthy to be living.We gathered online for our late night talks.We promised each other our hands in a forever box.We shed our blood, and was to keep a promiseyou knew you would never keep.The page was written, and erased, And  I was leftwith out sleep, while you were going cheap.You left me in the rain,You never once whispered my name.Our perfect dreams brokenour future no longer one.We split our own wayswe left each other one dayand never came back.We knew it was ending when you stabbed me in the back.You knew it was ending when you never came to me.You thought I wouldn't understand,you thought you were all alone,you never thought of picking up the phone.I don't understand why you didn't try, why you didn'tcry when you knew it was over,Maybe you never loved me like I loved you,I thought your love was true, I thought your heartwas full. But I was wrong, songs replaced.no more dancing. Our page is turnedand our story has ended forever all in the month of November.
