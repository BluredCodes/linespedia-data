My Heart Aches
My Emotions Break
My Mind Blank
Your Love Fake
My Dreams Sank
My Eyes Sleep
My Arms Weak.
My Cares Missed with out a morning kiss
Words unspoken love was broken, Hearts
were closed no one knows.


All because you said good bye.
