title: "Eating Death and Life"

In my stomach I feel it grow,
in my mouth the dark light shows.
I cannot hide the sins I've committed.
I can not take back the things I've eaten.
So deep, I can not let it out,
So strong it over powers my soul.

Death on the left, Life on the right.
Inside my stomach it rages,
back and forth it goes.
I see it peek out of my eyes,
while light covers my air holes.

The good in me, wants out
But the bad steps in.
Everything Dim and dark
while light looks in.

Its feeding off me desperately
And the light has no where to go.
The dark fog, clogs my heart
and tells the light NO.

Now and then, the light shines through
like the sun in the mirror
it will one day reflect the darkness
and that is what I fear.

Just then everyone will see the way  I truly am,
But still to this day, I do not know if they will understand.
