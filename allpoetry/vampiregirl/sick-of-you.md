You look so beautiful standing there why can't I look more like you? Oh look at the way she smiles so gracefully she does, I bet god sets your ass on a cloud everyday and night, I bet you get your way the moment you snap your fingers, isn't that right? why do you keep smiling at me, do you think I'm not as pretty ?. Your hair is dark brown, and your eyes are green with a tint of blue, Its okay that I don't look like you, your a selfish bitch anyway. Why would anyone care to see what you had to say? you only talk about your self, Stop looking in the mirror when I'm talking to you, Yes that's right look at me in the eyes, Because I see deep in you. Wash that makeup off and come back to me, Lets see how ugly you really are. Take that nail polish off, it looks bad on you by far. Wait a minute, your wearing my mothers necklaces, and my mothers dress, And I cannot touch your face. You are a mirror and I'm just looking at my self.
