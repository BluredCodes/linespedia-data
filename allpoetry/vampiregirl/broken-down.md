In the lost night of me
Seven thousand memories come and go.
Winds fly beneath me and hold me to my doom.
Many before me has been here before and came here to soon.
Nothing was created no moon no stars.
There was hardly an earth, and sure as hell no mars.
We were on our way to heaven and broke down somewhere
stared death in the eye, and he felt our souls here.
In an earth were it started life form of different kinds.
many has traveled far just to see the natures we've
became.
But everything was gone, and we were all broken down
we were stuck in a no where town.
