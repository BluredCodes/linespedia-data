You standing there, makes me regret how we used to be.How every time the moon would smile, you would look at me.Oh how wonderful it used to be, Till the day I caught you cheating on me.It made me think evil thoughts of you, How I wanted to cut off your dickand break it in two.How happy that would of made me, to see your face when your dick fell to the ground. And when your old and gray, Ill remind you of that day, And ask you if you remember how it felt. And every time you look at me, you will regret the day you ever cheated on me and you will agree.
