So you stand there thinking your cool
But your not, and your a fool.

You think if you have every girl in your arms
that will make you feel more like a man then you really are?.

You think you write poetry, very well I say you just need
attention, and your awful as hell.

You think your handsome, when your not your as ugly
as shit. You look in the mirror at least 60 times a day.
I say you need to take that mirror down before it breaks.

You make no since to me,
You are just another cheating heart roaming free.

And your girlfriend, she makes it so easy for you,
She will take you back no matter what you do.
I think that's crazy.

Then I have people, I talk to, I expect them to be true,
then I realize that they aren't any better then you,
they make promises they cannot keep.
They say words out of anger and being cheap,
so I will not look at anyone, as any different then you.

The worst thing I cannot understand, is how ANYONE
could pride you, for being a man, when your dick is probably
as small as my pinky, I bet you get ass fucked,
when you cant get laid, you are a fag that plays like he's got aids.

You are a disease, And your ugly as hell,
your lips are bitter, and your odder smells.
You are shit, faced, and a mother fucker to be,
And I hope you die, in the cheapest coffin you see.
