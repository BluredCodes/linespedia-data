title: "Unknown Secrets"

Behind the doors
of your empty jars of life. You live in the richest
temples, and bury your strife
Gathered around a bottle of bitter
seas calm and wicked yet tender.

Tonight your heart feels untouched
Behind the silence of your voice that rusts.
The wind perfect so soft, and rapid.
The trees swift,
And still not even the man, You love can
find the hidden places.

He walks gently down your halls 
rubbing his gentle fingers down the walls,
looking for an open door that will not fall.

He locks his eyes on you,
Finally he has found the deepest secret hidden inside of you,
The secret that causes him pain.
The key now hangs over his head with a dark flame,
And plays games while your making love.

He pictures you, making love to another.
He hears the very moans you've made.

Now with out trust he follows you,
and still he needs and wants you.
His eyes still blue.
His heart and mind ruptured from finding hidden secrets in you.

Who was to think someone like you
could lose your self to a secret so suddenly?

The very thought of you fucking another drives him crazy.
You've lost everything you own,
all because of the secret unknown.
