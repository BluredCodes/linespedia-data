attractive lips are lushes, and sweet.
Every single beat of my heart rhythms to the sound of your voice
All beats incomplete with out yours.

My face beautiful, with a fair completion.
My eyes sky blue, with a touch of love from you.
In my mind are thoughts of a perfect romance,
of a pure picture of how we were supposed to be.
In my heart an empty place of what our lives should be.

The beauty that lye's inside of me is more then the clothes
I wear, or how I do my hair.
Or the makeup that covers every blemish.
through your eyes, I look beautiful
with out, all these man made cures of none perfection.

Through your thoughts, I read you're mind
and find my self in time, Every moment spent with you,
lying on the couch cuddling next to you.
Or kissing your lips, and touching you,
Every thought of you and me, are put in a safe
inside my head in memory, A place that no one can find under
an empty bed and this is where I keep my heart,
a place that can never be seen, and never ripped apart.
