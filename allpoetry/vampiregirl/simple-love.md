Its not the blossoms that grow under trees,
or leaves that fall and drift in to the breeze.
Its not the morning sunlight kiss
or the grace of a rainbow in happiness.

Its not the smile that grows upon my skin
not the frown or candle burning in.

It's not the love making
or trembling or shaking.

Its the knowing that forever ill be floating..
It's the caring and understanding..

The partner that will never leave..
This is the simple person I'll forever be.
The one that loves you most tenderly.
