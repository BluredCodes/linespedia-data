In my mind a completion is made
but never raved.
We were perfect together so many steamy nights
that would last forever.

In my heart a rapid beat, slowly trembles
to the sweetness of our black silk sheets.

In my voice the moment of orgasms.
Moans and classic moments of passions.

The sexiest thing I've ever seen before
the most sexual moment I am to indoor.
My body lying naked on the floor.

The touch of your hands
the feeling of your tongue
The poison and vietnam of love.

Your pupils staring so deeply at me
your bloody lips kissing me.

The fluids mix
everything comes together
we are never to be apart
the perfect love in a forever moment.

your finger tips thorns that poke through me
Your hands grab on to places holding me roughly.
the feeling can't be described
the pain raging through my mind
something I've never felt before
something I want to feel more.

The helpless hour of death
the beautiful power of love
the aching, the shacking
the breaking of my cherry covers you in blood.

All in the power of Poisonous love.
