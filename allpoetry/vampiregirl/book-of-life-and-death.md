In my book I write my last wishes
hoping they will be filled with kisses 
and Love from you.

My body mind and heart is tired of living,
My spirit and love just keeps on giving.
But I can't Finnish, anything I start.
My heart, my soul, my mind
will some day fade in to everything I hate.
And no one will see me the way I once was.

In your minds ill keep on living,
although my body will no longer be giving
compassion, or love.

In my dreams ill be strong
in life form it will all be wrong.
And ill never follow through.
Everything blue...

To think if I let my self go from this moment on,
I won't have to live alone when you die.
Everything floats away
everything gone in moments out of years
you've lived.
So many kisses and hugs
so many reasons to forgive.

Something's are hard
everything so rough
in this life, I live that is completely
out of control. Ill walk to the stars
Ill walk to the sky,
Ill leave my body on earth, while my soul
slowly fly's.
