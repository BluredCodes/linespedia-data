My heart aching
My body breaking.

The loneliness fills my air.
Your presents here.
The music sings.
The wind brings love.....


The feeling lost, and gained.

The smoke covers my eyes,
The snakes crawling up my legs
the thorns in my sides
the holes in our souls....

The pain..
The feeling of my veins breaking.
The slit of the wrists I couldn't resist
to see you.

My tarnished heart, that's falling apart.

The flash!..........
 your lips...
touching mine
no one around.....
So pale your skin...
The holes, and scabs
over every inch of your body
the burning flesh of your skin
still I could not resist you,
Still I need you..

The touch.....
The feeling
the over whelming.

The pale kiss that drugged me
the passions that stepped in
and walking around me.

The fog that covered my heart
the smell of death of another route.
The curse.....
the cause...
The pale kiss under the moon,
the grave...
the reactions...
the bliss....

the touch.......
the gift....

I've felt you 
I've seen you
still your sixfeet under
and I'm lying on top
digging my way down, with every last drop
...
flashes
Pale Lips,
The kiss....
The bliss....
so long....
so lovely.
gracefully

STUCK.... TRAPPED
the flash.......
pale kiss....
pale lips....
the moon
the darkness...

Pale kiss.
Your death.....
the flash...........
Us..... together
your dead.....
your here
I'm here.
we are together
I fear
you will leave
there will be tomorrow
if I live.
I can't see
lights blinding me.
Take me to hell
let my body swell.
Let tomb say my name.
Speak out, don't stay calm.
The madness.
The sadness.
Unbearable.
Tragic
......
The flash........
The terrible flash....
your burning.......
you're punished for my sins..
My fault.
My blame
My hate.
My name...............

I love you....
The flash.........
Memories.... tendencies.
