---
title: "I Need This!"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Love is a devotion, that will never lean my way.Time and time again, I ask my self rather this one will stay.I dream a lot, And act out these plays, like I'm on screen.I see nothing, And every man i've ever known, has alwaysbeen mean.I wish something would fall before me.Someone to show affection to, and someone that won't ignore me for who I ama man that's more understanding, and that will always hold out his hands.A place to stay, and hide away in his arms.A place that no one could ever take away from.He is my reason for every breath, And every momentI wake, He will give me his heart, in returnI will never break it.I want to be his fantasy, his dream girl to be.I want to be his masterpiece, his favorite spice,And his mystery.I want to be the one he looks forward to coming home to.And most of all, I want to be important and special to.I want to give him my happiness, and my love,I want to give him my body, and soul that was sent from god above.I want him to think of no one but me, and understand when I sayI need attention he better listen to me I need this more then hugsor kisses, or red wine or flowers from you. I need this more then you would like to believe so give it to me or i will leave.
