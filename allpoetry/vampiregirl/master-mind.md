---
title: "Master Mind"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "master-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

He is light on his feet while he seeps
in under the door to hear your heartbeat.
He is as light as a feather and floats in the wind
you never even know he's there.

He's a genius that never steps out
in the day.
He's been well on his way to understanding you.
His eyes dark brown with a touch of black
he never leaves prints of where he's been
and never looks back.

He runs his finger threw her hair
at least ten times
and sucks the breath from these dyeing
lips of mine.

He is the silent steps
with the long jacket
that drifts through the air.
He carries a dark black bag
everywhere.

Inside he hides the souls of those
he loves
he touches their soft faces
and smothers them to death and carries there
eyes to rest.

he leaves a dream catcher in the window
to savor what's left.
He covers their face with pale makeup
to discover their past.

he is the master mind of you
he is the master mind of me
he follows in our footsteps so no one will see.
His shadow is on the walls.
his reflection stands tall he's at least 6'3.
He is death that sees through us all so clearly.
