title: "My Love Will Never End"
writer: "VampireGirl"

A request of sorrow I must poor out.
Just praying that you will listen to me, Before its to late.
Hoping the sun will shine on my face for just one more day.
Before I leave you this way.

I hope that you cared, when I died that day.
I felt your lips kiss me, and you knew it would be the last time.
It was a day before I was buried, and it was a day I heard you whisper
that you were mine.

So sadly I knew that you would never find another to love.
So much pain I felt come off you, That nothing could
take my heart from you.

So shallow you were when people would ask you if you were okay.
You replied with a yes, but I knew you would never be the same.

When you cried, and laid a rose on my chest
I knew I had all the best, moments of life with you.

When you rubbed your hand over mine, I knew it would be hard for you.

And now that IM buried, And we had just gotten married two days ago
I knew how hard it was for you to let me go.

It wasn't your fault, When a man had a gun holding it to my head,
you tried to talk him out of it, But it was to late

I was already dead.

You saw the fear that ran through my eyes.
You were scared, that I was gone.

You requested for them to play a special song at my funeral
*love will never die* by Ashley mile.

When you left the graveyard, it started to rain
so much blame you held in your heart, so much pain.

I don't know how to tell you this, But it was never your fault.
You did all you could, And my love will never End.
