Love bites, Just when you get that fuzzy feeling in your stomach
everything turns ill. Just when you get the guts to walk up
and go in for the kill, Everything turns out bad, You trip and stumble to the ground. and look up and smile (hoping no ones around) like your just fine, waiting to run off and hide.

Just when you say I love you, He gets scared to death of you,
And doesn't know what to say back to you.

When you say i want us to be together forever, he smiles at you
And says I feel the same way to
When he turns around, he gives a strange look and askes him self
what the fuck is wrong with you.

When he starts to rub your tits, with his hands down your pants 
he all the sudden thinks hes in love with you,
But when you slap his hand and say get off me
He thinks to him self, Love bites hardly
