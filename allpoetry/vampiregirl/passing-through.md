title: "Passing Through"

Everything around me so calm and sweet it's as if I was dropped
in to nature from a crying leaf.
The rivers run through a steady stream in to my heavens the most pleasant dreams.

The trees drift softly from left to right.
and talk to the grass of green.
Under the moon light stars the blackest night
whispering soft words to me.

Between the dark stream of light
there are occasional things,
glitter bees, and deers of white, covered
in beautiful glaze.

Flowers blooming as old petals drift away
Gone through the winters nest and out from
a rainy day.
