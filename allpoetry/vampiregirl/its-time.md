Your eyes has been closed for millions of years,
your tears were faded clear.
Your eyes were once blue, But dimmed out to a pale white,
your skin, silky at one time, Now leathery and tight.
your body swollen,
and your heart  beats no more.
Your eyes was once open, I see them no more.
Your face once beautiful.
your heart once beat.
Your gone, And I must understand this,
I must know its not my fault.

I shouldn't be blaming my self, for the day you died,
I shouldn't be hiding my soul, behind my eyes.
I will miss you, and think of you always,
But I must let you go, I'm trapping you here
in earth when you should be living in heaven.
I will go to your grave once again,
and see if there is a craving for your love,
if there's not, I know I'm strong enough to give you up.
