In a dream I sleep peacefully.
While feathers guide my head so gently.
I awake from my sleep, and feel the frown upon my face.
At a waking moment my heart began to race.
Speeding, as if it was out of control.
Panicked along with my soul.

Angry beasts surrounds my bed
and raise my tempter to a boiling red.
Ringing screams, raging in my ear
for things I have done that is my worst fear.
The fire burns as it melts my skin
the heat is on, And rushing in.

The game was fun
I thought it was real, I had no idea
this is how I would feel.
Swarms of bees, suffocating me.
Aching winds shadowing of loss.
The window knocking, banging against the brick.
I have tripped in to the secret passion
that my body has longed for.
Lust, and love so faded in my mind.
A script written but out of time.

Beautiful, Beautiful moments I once had
all turns to dust and went to bed.
A passion that angers me
 another memory that haunts me.

I gasp for air, telling my self I'm not here
the things I see aren't real,
Suddenly a gust of cold air
swift cross my feet, I feel more then one heart beat.
I see the shadow of the eyes in the mirror
and I sence that his sprit is here.
