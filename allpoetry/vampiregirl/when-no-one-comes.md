When winter settles in
And there's nothing to
guard you from the snow, let me know
and ill come for you.
when the rain is to heavy and your all 
alone take me home with you.
When the sun gets in your eyes 
and you have no one to cover you, ask me to, 
when you feel like no ones on your side, 
don't hide let me be there.
when your world crumbles let me 
show you its not has bad as it seems.
when you need affection let me give it to you.
when no one else's comes around, ill be there to guide you.
