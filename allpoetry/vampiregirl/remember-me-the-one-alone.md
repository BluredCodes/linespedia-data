title: "Remember Me, The One Alone"
writer: "VampireGirl"

When you see me pass me by,
Think nothing when I look in your eyes.
When the sun glares upon my hair.
and my smile is bright like a million cares.
Think nothing of it,
My smiles aren't for you, my happiness
and delightful grace has nothing to do
But waste it on people I don't know.

When the tears in my eyes
start to show
Ill do it alone
Ill stand in the bathroom and look
in the mirror to remind me
of each falling back year.

when the clouds in the sky over come the blue.
And the rain pours down, upon you.
Think nothing of it,
Don't remember me,
think nothing of it.
Because later you'll see.

You've lost me,
you've lost your world.
in the sky over your city, and state
will be a memory of me closing my gate,
turning off my light.

No way for you to find me.
No way for you to come back home.
For now on this woman is living her life alone.
