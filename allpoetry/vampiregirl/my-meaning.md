I didn't know what was wrong with me either did you.
I didn't have the chance to say one last time that I loved you.
How my two sisters were the only two friends I've ever had.
How my father was the only thing that meant anything to me.
And for a brother that taught me how to look at life differently.
And a nephew I was blessed with so gracefully. 

And I am happy to say, I know I was loved by you all.
And how when I felt small, You showed me how to stand tall.
How all my fears disappeared when I knew you all were by my side.
And the way you taught me how to swallow my pride.
I learned how to be a better person because of you,
I learned how to look at the sky as something more then just blue.
I Found my self, Because I had you all.
The ones I care for more then anything, and there will never anyone else that compares to those who love me as much as you all.

You are my family, And I am glad that we all stood together on the same ground, And when we felt depressed or let down, We supported each other
in a special way, And for so long all I wanted to say
Was thank you.

And even when IM gone,
Don't let your face frown, Because I'M in heaven smiling down.
I'M in your hearts, guiding you the right way.
And speaking in your dreams, All the things I meant to say
when I was still with you.

And I will leave you all with a hug, And an I miss you.
And you will always be the meaning to my long life.
