---
title: "A Life For You"
writer: "VampireGirl"

You search the empty roads of life
seeking for love, and the loss of regrets and strife.
You seek upon your open grave, the tomb stone that's never been made.

In your heart you know
the government has control, taxes are outrageous
still you do not know what's false or true.

The world will one day stop, and grumbles to the sound
of what we do.

The rocks, and trees talk...
But do you  believe that they do?

your belief, and faith has ran dry
you can't remember how to run or cry..
you never thought this would come to this.
you lost your chance for forgiveness.

what are you to do when its to late?
what are you to do when you see the intentions of your own heart?
how are you supposed to react when you see through the ones you love.
The ones that burn in hell, or fly with the doves.


Are you going to die alone?
empty handed no memories to follow you?

Can you die for the one that always watches you?
the one that talks, the one that listens....
the one that made you..

are you willing to give your life?
burn in hell and be forever with regrets and strife?
Is this the way you want things to be?

the government has control, over you, and over me.

there is only one power..
out of earth..
will you burst..
can you open your ears to hear?
because his voice is near..

he's coming soon..
you're already forgiven
but do you believe you are?
can you forgive your self.... for the crimes
you've committed?

you know you are selfish, you know you are cruel..
you don't think you can change, and you don't know what to do.

change your heart, change your mind...
because slowly the ticking clock of Christ is running
out of time.
