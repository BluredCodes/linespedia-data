Before you were gone 

When you were here. The sound I heard was fear 
of my heart being alone. now that your gone 
I feel a heart that's complete. I can even finally 
feel my own heart beat. When you were 
here I felt nothing at all you did nothing to show you cared 
you did nothing to show the way you felt you said 
your just confused you don't know what to do 
every time I came around, you would act like I wasn't in the room, 
Now that your gone I know I did not need you 
I did not love you. 
You were just a person that paid the bills 
and I cooked and cleaned for, I was a maid 
that could never of gotten paid enough to stay with you.
