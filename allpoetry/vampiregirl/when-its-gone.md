When the music stops from playing
and all gods creations is crying, like angels
with out their wings, and children with out their swings.
Everything is banished in to the sun, Blazed in to the fires
of hell.
My wrists cut, and my chest swells.
Who am I to blame?
What is his name?

The one I loved
The one I needed
the one that had mistaken me, and cheated.
Everything so far fledged
while I'm holding my arms open from the ledge.
I gather my thoughts clearly
My heart is empty.

Everyone gone
no more happy songs.
