title: "How Destiny Should Be"

As he's sleepless he thinks of me 
he is my pure wonder as I wish I could be. 
He explores the open, and the heart of me 
he doesn't hold my wings but sets me free. 

He shares his joys, and happiness when he's feeling down 
he never lets his smile fall or turn to a frown. 
His eyes are as beautiful as the gracious blue sky 
and he lends a hand to elders, or those that cry. 

His favorite thing to do is dance slow under the moon light 
he is romantic, and he's always open to me 
He never refuses or tells lies to me. 
He has a complex mind that's as deep as can be, 
in the moment of the eclipse it sinks through me. 

He never has been unfaithful and he has never been untrue 
he wakes me with a good morning kiss, and a good night kiss to 
He's the perfect guy for me, and he always will be, and there 
is no one else that could take him from me. 
He doesn't judge 
people for any kind of raise, he doesn't judge others 
by the shape of their face. 

And I believe he knows the truth of how should be Destiny.
