title: "Swallow My Love"

For years we've been apart
debating rather or not if we meet again you will stay.
I have strengthen my self for this moment in case once again you walked
away.
For so long I waited for your hand
your wedding ring, and you're meanings I can understand.
for once in your life do something you won't regret
give me that wedding ring, and drink down your fears
come off the bridge and walk away from your tears.

For once in your life let me be the importance to you,
let me show you flowers bloom,
Heaven can be closer then you expect it to be
Swallow your love of my heart and dance with me.
