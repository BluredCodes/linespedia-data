In my eyes i see it.
for in my heart there is a new beat.
my suffering is over.
no more leaks.

my dreams are still.
i feel the earth go steady, i am ready..
the hush of silance fills the air.
i am no longer here.

my shadow formed a frame in which i lye,
while my dieing body cries.

my family gather around my pale stale body.
i feel the breaking hearts of many.
i had to wait for the hush to get this rush.
the atention i never had, al momories of sad igorance gone.
