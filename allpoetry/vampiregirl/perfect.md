Working harder, then I ever have before.Looking in to your eyes, and loving you a little moreeveryday. And finding words that come from my heart in so many ways.You are the most interesting creation i've ever seen,Do you know what you add to this world?Every time I look at you, I see a passion for life,Every time I look at you, I see not a drop ofstrife, You are the most forgiving person I've ever met,You are so understanding sometimes it gets to me.your words are expressions of thousands of memories.Your lips are gorgeous I love to just watch you talk.I love the way you move and the way you walk.You are the best thing I've seen in so long,Every time your happy, I hear a beautiful song.Life couldn't be better then this.And I know you will always be with me, night and day.You can't ever leave me, because your choice was to stay.And I am your way to heaven.Everyone that knows me believes I'm talking to thin air,Everyone that's someone points and stares.But this is me, And you are my bestfriend,and you are my lover, when I need a man.You are my inspiration for all things I do.Although your just an my imagination in my head I still love you.
