---
title: "I Am Your Death"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "i-am-your-death"

When you fall in to me
do you see your self go weak?
pass out so easily....

Death smothers you in passions and dusts
of ashes of truths.

wake up next to me
find your arms holding on tightly
find the demon in you
break these pains that's killing you.
Find your self wanting me
find your self needing me.

I am your disease.
this is your last chance for heaven
Look at me and turn away
guide your life and find a new day

I have fallen in love in some form of way.
Excuse the way I feel
Do not mind the fact that this is real.

Close your eyes and picture me
think of me standing over fire
you will fall inside
you will yearn to my thrives.

Can you get enough?
do you feel this trigger pulled?
echoes of death follows you
watch your steps.
shadows leaping
hearts beating.

this step isn't worth taking.
listen to your heart
stop falling apart
do not go weak in the knees
do not ask me please to stay
this is your only chance to walk away.

I haven't known you long
but I knew there was something special in you
the devil of demons want you, and god does to.

I am the one in chains
cast me to the fire and save your name.
I am 
your monster
your vampire
your death wish
don't let this kiss capture you
look away to a bluer sky
make a wish for me to fly.
Cover your eyes
do not look to deeply
or you might not escape to easy.

I hide behind a frown
that's turned upside down.
I am your ghost
I am your worst.
Please stay away.
don't say you know me.
Don't be surprised of what you might see.

Run away from me

find your self in eternity.
do not love me.
I am your death
waiting to happen
I am your life
I am your weakest moment
treasure me not
find your soul deep
hold on tight so I wont sleep.
cover your face away
hide and run away.
