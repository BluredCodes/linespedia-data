It has never snowed in this town
but some how it did today
I wished it would never stop, or run away.
The snow settled in, and my stomach began
to feel tight.
you were there with me on a long beautiful Christmas night.
There was no way to drive to the hospital
there was no way to call 911, the phone lines were
down and the snow had won.

We opened presents and spent the night together
it was the most perfect winter.
The Christmas we had is a Christmas ill never forget
its the only Christmas that Ill never let
leave my heart.

You held me tight, and knew it was back again
this time I didn't think I would make it out
my stomach was internally bleeding
from the lung that had collapsed two years ago.

I whispered and said
that this is the only night ill never dread.
Ill be happy that I spent my last night with you
and for every white Christmas ill dream of you.
Kiss me from this moment, because I wouldn't change
a thing
this moment was a happy ending, and the church bells rang
Christmas was over
and I was gone
you went to the cemetery
and said it was wrong
It was such a perfect moment
I wasn't supposed to die
you cried your last tear
and whispered good bye.
