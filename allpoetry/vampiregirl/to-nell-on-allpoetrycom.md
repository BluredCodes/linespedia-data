To you from me:
As I  Log on to allpoetry.com
I think of who has read my poems.
The first thing that comes to mind is "Nell"
I think she sure is swell. She reads my poems
and gives truthful comments.
How nice she is.
I go to read other poems, and notice
what I see, Nell had read so many poems more then three.
She has words for everything.

She has a gifted talent, at a young age
that will progress in many ways.

I am glad you are here
to express the way you feel
Each day each month and each year.

You are a talented poet
that explains the best way she can,

you are a deep, and complex mind that understands time.
You are the one that comes along like the ghost in the wind
to comment on one of our poems again.
I would like to say thank you,
And you are a sweet gal
I Hope we are friends,
any way any how ;)

Shelly
~VampireGirl~
P'S a thank To Nell For Her comments!
I hope this didn't sound to corny :)
But I didn't know how else to say it.
