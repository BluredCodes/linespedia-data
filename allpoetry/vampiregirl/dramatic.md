I read a book, six times and still I act it out, Like all the words come out like a spout. My eyes pout, when I read something sad. My voice is filled with anger when I get mad. My heart is open, when I feel it should be. But most of all IM as dramatic as I can be. I am brave for all the things I do. I am open and graceful to people I knew. I am peaceful and quiet when I do not know you. I am beautiful and stunning when IM dancing in the rain. I love to be playful, and wrestle with you. i'm sorry if IM dramatic but you know its true. I keep my eyes closed when I think things are scary that always drives you crazy, Because you think its so cute. I smile when I'M sad so I won't feel like I'M falling apart. I try to laugh when I'M disappointed so I won't break down and cry. I act weary when I'M sleepy, and I'M not real sure why. I have a beautiful voice to sing, and I have all the things I thought I needed, But I am dramatic when I don't have you.
