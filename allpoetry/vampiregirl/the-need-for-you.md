title: "The Need For You"

The moment I wake up 
I think of you 
And all we've been through. Still going strong 
and never disagreeing about who's wrong. 

We are still playful after all these years 
we support each other through all our tears. 

Every night before I go to bed 
I dream of you and each passionate word I hold on to. 
I have no regrets, of meeting you 
and I'm glad to say we have been married ever since two day's 
after knowing we were meant to be. 

ten years past, and this is a marriage that will last not just a memory. 
Love is in the air, and we treat every day 
as if it was the first day we met and full of cares. 
The moment we'll never forget. 
A place inside our hearts, that will never fade or fall apart. 

Love is something that's more then a taste 
and more then a feel. 
its more then a thought, of knowing its real. 
It is something you wake up to, 
and go to sleep to every night, 
Its the one thing you'll need for the rest of your life.
