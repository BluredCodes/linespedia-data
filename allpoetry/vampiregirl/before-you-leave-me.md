Before you leave me
think of this,

You will be the one that I will miss,
your lips, forever the last person I kiss.

There will never be another,
Ill never come together.
Ill never see you again,
your hands will pull away,
I'll never again know where to stay.

Every night you would hold me,
Every night we slept as one.
each day we had our fun.
We danced, we played in the snow.
romance filled the air on a misty night.
you covered me in loving blankets and always held me tight.
I wish you would stay.

I see you lying there,
Death waves over your bed tonight
you will disappear in to the moonlight.
I cannot breathe.
Forever gone, you will be.
Think of this before leaving me.
