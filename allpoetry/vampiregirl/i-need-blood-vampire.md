pondering on the thought of drinking you
thinking you'll be mine if I kiss you.
I know that I can have any man I touch,
I feel a rush and then I begin to pout
you then lean over and try and rescue me and figure me out
just then I lean in and suck on you,
drinking every last drop of blood from your neck.

I do not feel guilt, and I do not feel pain.
I do not feel sex, and I do not believe anything is wrong.
I didn't expect to fall in love,
with someone that's more human then me,
I didn't expect to find someone human
that would follow me or someone mortal and that cannot live for an eternity.

I am forbidden to step in the light.
I lye on my cold wooden floor, away from the sun.
In my empty coffin is where I must run in the day.
In the night I step out, and walk away.
down the long road of empty streets,
Looking for a victim that will come to my home,
so I cam seduce him in being mine.

I then make love, tenderly
I kiss and suck on his lips,
he believes he's in love with me,
but while he falls asleep
I watch him,
I am jealous that I cannot be the same,
I whisper my name in his ear,
then kiss his neck softly,
I then begin to suck,
till his soul drifts away.

I pull his lifeless body, out to my car,
I bring him down  to the river
where 40 bodies are,
And kiss his lips, once more
and tell him how sorry I am,
then sink is body in to the muddy pits of death.
and whip away a drop of blood left on my lip.
