title: "Another Lonely Night"

You sitting there, With out saying a word it makes me feel home alone.
You look at me with tears in your eyes, And you never hear when I ask if your okay. 

And when I see you talking on the telephone I wonder who your calling.
Maybe your trying to reach me. If my eyes were blind I wouldn't be able to see, That you feel the same as me. Maybe I'm wrong and this is just a dream.

Hell feels closer then its ever felt before
And I am wanting heaven to save me, each day a little more.
My body feels weak, And the lights shining down.
But I refuse to go, in less you know I love you.
I know you didn't expect me to stay, But I knew this was the only way
to show you I did care.
I knew the moment the car crashed in to me, that you would no longer treat me as if I was truly there. I am dead, And I'm in your bed another long lonely night
