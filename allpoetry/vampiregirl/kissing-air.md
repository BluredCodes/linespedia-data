---
title: "Kissing Air"

How handsome you were. 
With perfect skin.
We danced while our lives felt revived and born again.
Your lips so tender we danced forever, I knew
we belong together.

Your eyes blue, with a touch of gold.
I knew your arms were a forever hold.
Sweet touches of your hands
Lovely finger tips pressing mine.

I loved you so.

You were my everything, and I had no thought of letting you go.
Midnight strikes, I kiss your lips,
And you fade away, I was kissing air once again
and I knew you would not stay.
