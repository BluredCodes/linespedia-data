Alone for a million years 
I cried at least a billion tears. 

Suddenly things changed 
I had found you. 
you were unique and had a different style 
then anyone I've ever seen before. 
You had black hair and blue eyes, you were at least 6'4. 
You were handsome, And I felt fate grasp hold of me. 
I met you on the top of the space needle 
in Seattle Washington about a quarter  till 3:00. 

I knew that I would no longer be alone 
the moment you whispered in my ear, 
I felt your whisper get closer as you moved near. 
I felt your hands on my hips, while you leaned up against me 
I smiled and knew you were not just trying to look out the window. 
I knew that I could live my whole life just getting to know you 
if you would just kiss me. 

There was this moment were no gravity was between us. 
No hate, or bitterness could ever break us. 
That moment I looked in your eyes, I knew you were mine 
and nothing would ever change or take that away. 
You were perfect from that day on 
and in my heart ill always give you a smile 
every night you sleep, and every day you wake. 


And if I had known love could feel this good 
I never would of understood why I was alone for so long. 
and now days, you whip away every tear. 
you spend your whole life holding me here. 
and making love under the moonlight. 
your hands were always so strong, and your body 
was always under control. 

from the moment we met and nine months later 
I ask you what do you want to call our soon to be doughtier? 
and I whisper I love you 
And just then fate settled in.
