title: "Kiss My Ass"

Black hairBlack Eye LinerBlack pantsBlack ShirtBlack jacketBlack NailsPale soft SkinRed Lips I'm not blinding inI refuse to be the sameI must be different or I will blame.People believe I'm depressed but they don't know me.I refuse to let anyone get close to me.I happen to be very happydon't let your eyes fool youyou know nothing of me, and you know nothing of your self.you wear colorful clothesand pretty white socksyou have beautiful blue eyesand long blonde hairyou are a color of beautythat makes all men stare.I am different, call me uniquecall me gifted because I don't speak and I never sleep.I am just as beautiful as youthink of me as an abstract or a painting of somethingtrue,I am not fake,I am realI am good at expressing every way I feel.Through words, you must listen carefullyand learn the ways of my mind.Watch me dance slowlyand notice how I moveeverything so carefully everything so smoothI am Special, and I don't discard you,I don't put you down for how you lookor what you are.I just don't believe in going to barsor drinking,Or doing drugs,or fucking every man I see.I refuse to get a disease before I'm 23.I will excuse you, and not judge youfor all that you arebut I guarantee when I say I'm happy I mean it,if you say you are happy you don't,so don't give me shit because I'm on a differentpath, for all I care you can kiss my ass.
