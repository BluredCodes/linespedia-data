A sad teardrop falls down my cheek.My world so saddened that my mouth wont speak.I have been a mute for about a year. Living in fearof talking to those I love, and losing them to the heaven above.The white and blue sky, with wings, seems to bringall our lives to an end. And still I don't think I know where to beginto live.i've been so far off, on my own.My mind so damaged from all that ive done.And I wonder how long it will be, Before the skyopens and takes my soul from me.
