title: "Bleeding"
writer: "VampireGirl"

In the bush that burns
flowers and roses that yearn.
Aching thorns breaking through your skin.
Angels and demons fight for the strength of
your soul again.

your body becomes weak,
before you die.
You slowly let go, and learn to fly.
your eyes tear up, and the pain you cannot bare.
Those that fear death, stand around and stare.
Glaring in to the eyes, of the fading,
gazing in to the eyes, of the ending, and meanings.

Your blood gushes through out  the streets of life.
Such a tragic waste to see you be barred with
strife.

to die young
and leave your soul behind.
Your body crumbles, while your mind stays alive.
leaving you in an empty hole of debate, and darkness
of hate.
Wondering where is this faith, that you believed
in for so long?
where is the god you've never seen before.
Where is the regrets, of believing no more.

And in the end
your blossoms freeze
to the winter breeze.
Then summer rises, and questions come to mind.
by the time your answers are found
your well out of your mind
