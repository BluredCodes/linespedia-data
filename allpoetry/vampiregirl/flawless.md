Flawless, I am all that I believe I am, I have the beautiful bright eyes, and my mouth never tells lies, my heart is open and free, like a young princess guiding me. I am innocent, to the touch, I am glorious to the feel. I am perfect, so you say. I am the sun on a rainy day, I am the moon that covers your sky, my eyes never cry. I am happiness, when you are sad, I do good deeds for the bad. I give money to the poor, I love children even more. I am hope, and faith, when you do not believe. I am flawless like a seed.
