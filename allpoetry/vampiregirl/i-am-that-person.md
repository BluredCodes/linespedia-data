title: "I Am That Person"

I am that person that never tells you no. 
I am that person that never let you go.
I am that person that stood next to you, through thick and thin. 
I am that person that always held your hand. 
I am that person that always listens to you. 
I am that person you always bumped in to. 
I am that person that always cared for you. 
I am that person you always held on to. 
I am that person that always stood in front of you 
I am that person that you never see
I am that person thats been searching for you.
I am that person you will never love or hold on to
I am that person that you will never reach for
I am that person you will never need anymore
I am that person that you hate most
I am that person that you will never be
And I will always be the person that your to blind to see
I am YOU and that is me.
