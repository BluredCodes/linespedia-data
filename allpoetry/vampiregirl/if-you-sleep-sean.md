title: "If You Sleep Sean"
writer: "VampireGirl"

I had a dream last night,
that the man I love was standing beside the death box.
He had no oxygen, No air to breathe, No hope to feed.
He was standing there looking at me. 
His eyes locked
on mine, Its as if he was telling me he was out of time.

There was a black cat that walked past
my very feet, It left me rising from the heat,
I tried to run to him But I couldn't.
I don't think he understood.

There was thousands of others walking around this
cave, A deep dark circle of the unwanted ones.
They were not sad, They were not happy, nore mad.
They were there with some of their families,
They never had to worry about going hungry,
or the touch or feel of someone.

While Sean stood there
I didn't want to look at anything.
He had his hands reaching out, for me.
I had nothing to do, but turn and walk away.
Because I was peaking in his dream,
and he watched me walk away.
After I woke up, I had this chest pain.
Its as if he doesn't even remember my name.
Its terrible when the ones you love die,
most of all when you were in a fight
and didn't have a chance to say good bye.
Truth is, I love you dearly
So much scenically.
