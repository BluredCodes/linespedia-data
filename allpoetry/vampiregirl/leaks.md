Inside the leak
of life, my bitterness swollowed by strife.
The sad feeling of growing and departing. 
The utterly sound of lonesome
and not knowing where i'll be.

The feeling destroyed my belief in faith and harmony
The bleeding and leaking of me falling apart, And to never again
know where I will start.
My heart races to the sound of your voice,
it slowly replaces all soled noises.
my mind warn out and roaming around
you turned my whole life and world upside down.

The doors all locked,
My time far away from the inside ticking clock.

How could you leave me?
knowing I would be so unsteady.
I was never ready.

I expected this some day,
I heard about all the other woman and your sexual ways.
I'm sure I dimmed out, while you were with another
I guess this means that it's over?.
I guess life catches up, when someone truly loves you.
You never noticed a single thing, of all the things I do.
All sounds faded in to your empty veins.
I was just an echo, around the trees.
you slowly lost your soul, to please.
you were surrounded by regrets,
the heartfelt moment of no more accidents.

To me you are a curse,
One I'll forever love.
But in your eyes, I was only the whore you would never love.
Just someone to ease your pain,
give you pleasure and moan your name.
But in the end there was love.
But it leaked out with my bleeding heart above.
