---
title: "I Don't Need Love"
writer: "VampireGirl"

Love is just a word to me.Love doesn't mean a thing to me.Through my life, i've been hurt, and cheated on.What makes me think this will be different with you.Why would you think I should take this chancejust so you could hurt me to?I don't believe in love, It ended right afterMy dreams were broken. When three words were not spoken.When he said good bye, I felt the word love flyout from my chest, Now I am just an empty personwith a lonely heart and nothing left.Love has no effect on me, Feelings of comminationsthat's all that matters to me.A rose is just a flower, And I am just a target.A target for those that play their wicked games on me. Someone that will never find someone true, that will be faithful to me.And you know what IM feeling right now,And when you ask me if I love you, I do not.I do not need love.
