A poem is an expression, and IM afraid that I wouldn't know exactly how to put in words the exact way I think of you. but the only way I can say this is 'Thank you'

Kevin: for building such a grand website for us poets, and being such a fantastic poet that you are.

Poetic frantic: for your help, and judgement,

deathgoddess: for your wonderful poems, and humor!

Rodney: you being the first one to submit to my poems when I first came on this site.

SolitaryTearOfAnAngel: for your open heart and poems that are remarkable. And your thoughts and kindness. 

HiramDiaz: for coming to this poem and checking out
And giving your truthful option and letting your expressions
out.

JustplainJoe: for your help, and expressions of kind, and understanding words.

Channa: for your kind heart and beautiful poems.

Nauticalmyst7: for telling me the truth about my poems
and expressing the way you truely feel, and being the best!
and being honest, and real.

Xxnecovesperxx: For your words of advise, and success

Bdgery: For your dreams, and hopes, that will come true.

allagory: for your kind comments, and words.

Chrisnichols: For all yours cares, and thoughts,

youngster: for putting poetry in your young heart.

Dragonfire: for taking the time to submit such comments and words on poems i write. 

Ladychasity: For you being my best friend.

SweetPea: submitting wonderful poems, and sharing. 

Naena: for your kind words, and thoughts.

Sheerpoetry: For your honest, and helpful options.

Winglessbutterfly: That you will get your wings and fly

sadpoet: for your beautiful sad poems.

sheshe143: For your wonderful poetry you share

John.esterbrook: For your kind words, and unlimited possibilities.

Tiny Poet:. For your honest opinions

Papercut: for your remarkable attitude.

Dreamweaver: for your depth, and understanding.

Skeletor: for the deep poems you write, that are remarkable.

Whonrock: for your poems, and unique style.

Acuid_burn: for your rude attitude, that helps me write better.

Lonkert: for your hate towards me, that also helps me write better.


to all the rest of the fellow poets, I'M yet to know
I hope your expressions are powerful, and deep
and you are as swift with your hand and pin as you are with your feet.
