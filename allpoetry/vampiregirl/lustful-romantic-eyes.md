Arrive before me the man I see
a joint in anxiety.
My love my fashion my first reaction.
My pleasant sense, under the pleasant dreams of sheets.
So hansom and strong the one that lies on top.
The one with fingers that touch my skin, the mighty one 
that commits my sins.

My earth shakes, everything quakes.
The first reaction
his mouth....
So tasteful and delightful
he pleases me.
He runs his fingers through my hair
and shows me creativity.
The gravity before my feet
tumbles to every beat.
in slow motion my body breaks down,
The motions of unconscious underground.

His lips touching mine
our tongues touch and hes wine.
We exchange our vales.
He knows my growls.

He turns me upside down
and works my body in many informal ways
expressing his love in many days.

His sweet lips, his rough finger tips.
His everything.......
so wonderful to me
my hansom dream of fantasy
so perfect.. he shines.
My sun, my moon, my heaven.
My dark shadow,
my ghost.
My beast of love.....
my stars of destiny.

My lustful romantic eyes
that stare and rise.
