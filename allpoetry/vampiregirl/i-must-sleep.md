---
title: "I Must SLEEP"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "i-must-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"

This loud beeping 
And banging in my head
Makes me think of all you said.
A whisper, Is a sound I hear,
A loud deep voice speaking near.
A thumping And pounding
that's ringing in my ear.
Is keeping me from sleeping while no ones here.

I have always had a fear of dreaming and being alone.

My eyes get heavy
My thoughts get weak
my body uncontrollable
and to lazy to think.

My toes fall asleep, then my feet
and each body part, one at a time.

I sleep,
I dream, and woke up in fear
only 30 minutes after no one was near. my eyes closed shut
a rush through my body of blood, and a beating of my heart
that has passed over me, left me in a nightmare
that no dream should be.
I cried, I weeped
I must learn to sleep.
And I must learn to live and let go
of this heart that's breaking, and pounding
that's keeping me in fear
And let my self know that I'm the only one here!
