it Rains one night
thunders in the air
lightening striking the ground
and everyone's in fear.

Lifeless warm breath on my neck,
my hair is drenching wet.
growling is the sound I hear.
Screaming from the window. And a knocking at my door
no ones home, and no one has been here for years.

No lights to be found,
all light switches turned down.

Nights Over......

Everyone's dead.
