You ask me, How long will I stayI tell you I will never turn away, I see the worry in your eyesThat I will move on to other guys. I see the pain, that's happened in your pastWhere you could never make a relationship lastHow every woman you've ever been with always set you freeHow everyone that once mattered to you, some how got away.I don't know how to answer a question like thisHow I melt away in every kiss.How I get lost in this beautiful bliss.Or how you move me with a simple glimpse.The only thing that came to my heartWas a simple answer like this:When the moon passes between us, And everything turns grayI will find my way.When words seem less, and I don't know what to sayI will show emotion to you and show that I still love you.When we are old, And you forget who you are I will remind you.And If you ever think IM fading away, And you ask me to stayMy only reply will be, today,tomorrow,next week,next month, and for the rest of my life I will stay with you.To My Boyfriend Jonah
