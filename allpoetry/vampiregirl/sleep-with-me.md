---
title: "Sleep With Me"
writer: "VampireGirl"
slug: "sleep-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26623-Sleep-With-Me-by-VampireGirl"
---

My hansom dream with blue eyes
lye by my side, lets fly.
whip away those tears
fear not of dreams, or cares.

Put your hand on my chest
notice everything is at rest.
Sleep with me here tonight,
gather next to me, flowers of the storm.

Touch my face, rub your fingers over my
lonely lips.

Close your eyes and get lost in forgetfulness.
Place your cares aside
do not hide,
Death is near
know I'm here.

Things will be okay,
things will be better this way.
Night time fades in to a day.
Moon light shines upon the love of mine.
winds slowly drifting thru us.
Never will we part in the middle of the gust.
Let our souls join in to one.
Let our wings, open and fly for the sun.

Whisper in my ear
tell me the last three meaningful words
that would keep me here.

Let our family see us here
Let our friends cry their tears.
Lets rust
Lets bust
lets sleep forever.
