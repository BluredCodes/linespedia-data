Inside your head
a dream that you fear and dread.
A voice walking you through,
Ever night so terribly bad for you.

In his eyes he screams
You can't pull him out of this dream.

You found him following you,
You felt his aurora haunting you.

He wants your lips, he wants your hair,
Every time you walked in to your nightmare
he was there.

everything was so heavenly,
So blue and steady.
Suddenly roses guided the walk way,
Black petals began to bloom
the big bad storm came in and went boom.

He steps out from behind the moon and trees.
He pushes you down to your knees.
He holds the knife to your throat while your begging please.
Suddenly a gust of wind keeps you at float,
your now with hope.

You're awake but your not.
He wants your body
He wants to put his fingers
in to certain spots.

he stares, gazes in to your mind
you feel so far behind.
No one to save you from this misery,
He pours out his hopes and buries
you under the very limbs of his own tree.

Inside the coffin you now lay.
He burred you under the rocks, and roses
of 63 others.
They were lost in his dreams,
And behind his eyes you hear all those dead victums screams.
