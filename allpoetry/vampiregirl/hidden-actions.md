In the eyes Of life I seek you.
For death has arrived, and passed through.

Angels fall,
Hell echo's madness.

The mind of me, closed places of faces I've seen before.
Their faces fade.

Their eyes closed,
Their body no long functions as before.

Lips sown.

Pale skin, light as a feather, a pillow of strength.
A weak moment that shows our faith.

My hands of gold, we exchange our life for the prices we pay.
in our mouth, we hold the hands of our loved ones
that pass on each day.

In our dreams, we see the loved ones of the other worlds.
Nightmares of hell, pleasant dreams of heaven.
Ghosts following our footsteps.
knocking at our doors.

Things you could never guess, thing's you've never seen before.
The screaming of children, The noise that runs through your brain,

Everything insane.
Dragged down,

no air....
in a closet of darkness,
In the fire of flames.
In the sun that burns,

A forever lasting pain,
a forever lasting sensations,
from the actions you have created,

IN your mouth it sleeps.
IN your nose it breathes.
In your blood cells it lives.....

The actions you make,
the moment you sleep, the moment you wake
Darkness is there.

The thorns that prick you
the needles in your arms
the belt around your neck
the aching the breaking.......

Your actions Hidden......
