I am a piece of glass how bad do you want to ram your hammer through?
wouldn't you like for me to shatter all over you.
Millions of pieces flying in the air, millions of memories of someone that didn't not care.

Wouldn't you love to see my sweet eyes cry?
And my body shrivel up and die.
Wouldn't you like it if you hurt me?

This is your big chance, Ill open my arms, and wait for the hit *aim for my heart* and i will take it the best I can. 

When I shatter will it cut you?
Will you bleed when I do.
How long before you were going to tell me your words of I love you were untrue?.

Why did you make me a promise? that we would never be apart
that nothing could take me away. All you wished for was for me to stay
but you lied.

I'm in a million pieces, not knowing what's to come of me.
but at this point nothing matters to me.
And I will have to wait and see, what part next you will break of me.
