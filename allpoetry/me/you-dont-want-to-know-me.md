title: "You don't want to know me"

You don't want to know me
I'm like an annoying bee 

I'm worse then anything you know
I make everyone feel low

I wish that I would die
Make a noose be sure to tie 

Learn to love all you see
It might not be happily

Learn to see everything that goes on
Before it all is gone
