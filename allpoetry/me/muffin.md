muffin
you are always there for me when i need you
you listen to my constant whining about how bad my life is
you are stuck in a little body with a little mind..
i still love you
you are the best listener that i know
you might only be three but you know exactly what i need
an ear to listen, a heart to love, and a someone who doesnt talk back an make me feel even worse
i would like to say thank you my pressious cat
for being there and loving me like i hoped you would from the first time i loved you
thanx muffinthis is dedicated to my cat muffin i love you muff
