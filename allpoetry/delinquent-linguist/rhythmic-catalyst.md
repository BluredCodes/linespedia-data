dumbeks keep fallin' 
           on my head  
           
           dum! dum!

           this tribal style 
           marks my arrival
           to next nexus 

           I am like a 
           bloomed artichoke

           electric luminous 
           lightening 
           coming from my purple iris

           my song gets louder has we part 
           with the petals leaving only an 
           empty oblivion 
           core

           reminded of Rinzai 
           I skip the apologies 
           because there is no 'I'
           to commit the crime

           night begins to bleed the sky 
           twilight
           Soon
           I will be less than a
           frost's wisp of breathe

           cheers to the rhythm! 
           cheers to the Great Syncopated Terrestrial Tapestry!
           we tap the time
           of all timethis is just something I made up, but I like it.a dumbek is a type of drum
