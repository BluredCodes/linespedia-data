the dying man laid down 
amongst the crabs and the cacti
Increasing in heat, the sun
cracked his skin into a jagged jigsaw mask 
"Dust." the man spoke out loud
and his soul escaped
wavering in the heat of that last breathe
"Dust."
the dried carcass
the still husk
the coarse remnant of a lifetime
might have reminded some child of a sand castle
but the presence of a gust from the west
blew it off and away
like dust.its a poem.
