1. let's paint dragons over our wet paper bodies 
shuddering in the wind's morning roam 
touches, and toes tear hard. Firm,
like tree roots
feral eyes
boyish grin
your ripe lip's wandering
sending sinful expletives to my skin

2. I pull back a leaf to let the dense deciduous rain
soak my senses
I see coy pearl eyes stalking me
glimmering winks through dawn's vaporous veil
drenched and cooing as morning shadows 
break from our bodies 
we dance the rain downis it too jumbled?
