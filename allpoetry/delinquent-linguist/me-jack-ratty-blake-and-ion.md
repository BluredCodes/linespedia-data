Last night 
was alllllright!

four pale ales
sparked the engine
'trainwreck' and hash oil
rang the bell

shake, rattle, and stir 
in my carbon body
and 'God Damn! Let's Raise Hell!'

me my compatriots and I
(we were five)
went batty like 
spiders on spindles
threading atonal tomes

oh-SO delicate
and
oh-SO delicious

relaying and re-weaving 
our enebriated interpretations
of the om-mighty monotone
that speaks volumes

Letting it undulate
while we dilate
under the expanding perameters 
of our perception

Sitting back
Sipping black
porter and 
howling a palette
pouring

four more hits of
the marrakesh refinery best  
to refresh the mesh of our
sweet harmonic
rose-hips 

Oh! such marvelous
musical mandala mixtures
and painted
paisely daisies

with the little we know
and as far as we go
we slip off that
'ego'

like a snow white dress
in a heap and a mess
on prom night

yes, last night was
