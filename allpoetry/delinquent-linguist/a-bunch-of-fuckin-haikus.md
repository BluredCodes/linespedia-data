she ate me whole with
one deliberate opal
stare under the sheets

rivers are white knives
carving valleys for wise eyes
to go munching on.

Peaceful and Pleasant
Content free-range pheasants. Nap!
Nap! Nap! in the sun!

I am a cold cut.
Serve me up with seafood Hot
Excrement and brine
 
Once, for a girl, I
penned twenty-one haikus. Now
this man knows regret.

Ciao! au revoir! 
adieu! adieu! to Yieu! and
yieu! and yieu! and......................................
yieu
