"thoughts on the matter"
           I wanted to be your conquistador
           cooing an invisible 
           soft stone whisper in your ear.
           lacing your psyche with
           absynthian side effects.

           I brought little more than
           adolescent tinker-toys to your
           eight minute attention span 

           Filling you like a commercial break
           between hackneyed, gregarious, main stage 
           players. 

           So, you tell me Act II is to be some extra-fleshed, 
           Rogaine wearing, dot-com barbarian on the cusp
           of middle-age.
           With a fluoride smile like Mr. Clean meets the New Year's Baby.
