Oh to be Don Quixote!
rambling down
ambling spice routes

I'd challenge the whispers
in shadow

I'd fend off the spectres
of truth

yeah to be Don Quixote
delusion mule bring me
wary

where four-armed behemoths
sigh heavy

my scabard to carve them 
to gravy

trotting and plotting
my crickets sing 
sweet a soliloquy

red dreams and scythe dreams
in breezes a running
through willow trees

Oh OH to BE!
deluded Donkey-O-Tee
What winds will 
rot at my rind?

no NO to BE
Mister Donkey-O-Tee
letting spiders
creep in my mind.this is my first attempt at a serious rhyme
scheme.  you like?
