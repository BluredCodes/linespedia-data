September brings Orange Crush candy rainaaaaaah! remembersitting on the roof as surveyor, orsolitary spectatordigesting surroundingsI found mystics grumbling in the bramblesof my backyard.It's sinister what fromother worlds will seep through your senses.Would it be trivial to say, 'I found the corpse of an old devilentangled in the chrysalisof an Oak tree's roots.'?The mind manifesting terror through it's eyesChannelled like a televised seance.On these glucose showered, summer, daysI live and lived the ways of a pliable worlddoes this make sense to anyone?
