Here I come, Straight up from the Rockies,
Ready to rock these,
Ready to pop these,
Like my boy Nelly,
Throwin it up for the midwest,
Tellin all the motherfuckers,
we are the best.
Here comes number one,
Gettin rid of the rest.

The bearded Eminem,
Yet more hardcore,
Moutain man thug,
Straight to the core.
Smokin em fatter,
Listenin to those skis chatter,
Pushin sixty miles per hour,
Showin the midwest power.
Eminem cant hold a canle to me,
Give it a shot bitch, you'll see.
Talkin like you are so hard,
Actually actin like a retard.
Chicka-chicka your a bitch,
Let's flip the switch.

Here I come, Straight up from the Rockies,
Ready to rock these,
Ready to pop these,
Like my boy Nelly,
Throwin it up for the midwest,
Tellin all the motherfuckers,
we are the best.
Here comes number one,
Gettin rid of the rest.

Here come the boys,
Out on the scene,
Stick around a while,
Y'all see what I mean.
Bringin in the bikers,
Rollin with the trikers,
Here come the white boys,
Bald heads big beards,
Cruisin the fat boy,
Makin money to get my next toy.
Bustin it out in the rocky mountain high,
Doin' what I do,
Just gettin bye.

Here I come, Straight up from the Rockies,
Ready to rock these,
Ready to pop these,
Like my boy Nelly,
Throwin it up for the midwest,
Tellin all the motherfuckers,
we are the best.
Here comes number one,
Gettin rid of the rest.

Wether we're startin shit in steamboat,
Fightin in the cold in my down coat.
Drinkin in P-town, shit town U.S.A.,
Talkin like Chong, Fuckin Eh.
Partyin out in vegas,
My boy on the ice,
Kickin some ass,
Watchin the lights,
Smokin my grass.
We're here to party,
Comin after y'all,
To the midwest,
You will definately fall.

Here I come, Straight up from the Rockies,
Ready to rock these,
Ready to pop these,
Like my boy Nelly,
Throwin it up for the midwest,
Tellin all the motherfuckers,
we are the best.
Here comes number one,
Gettin rid of the rest.Just for fun y'all.  Throwin it up to my boys up in CO.
