title: "Mocking A Mocking Bird"
writer: "Cope"

Mocking a mocking bird, mocking me,
I know it hears me, but what does it see.
Does it think that I am an eagle, letting out a screech,
Or a sand piper, calling from the beach.

Mocking a mocking bird, mocking me,
Endless chatter, for a moment we are free.
Two different languages, yet, we understand,
This night bears perfection, both feathered and tanned.

Mocking a mocking bird, mocking me,
Like the Sirens of past, calling out from the sea.
Singing for only a minute, under the starlight,
I could have spent an eternity there, or at least the whole night.

We parted for the night, saying our good-byes,
Leaving my newfound friend, nothing but sighs.
Engraved in my mind, this night will always be,
I’ll never forget mocking a mocking bird, mocking me.
