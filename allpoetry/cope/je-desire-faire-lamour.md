title: "Je Desire Faire L'Amour."

Je desire faire l'amour.
To be held inside your love,
Caressed with your warmth,
Subdued by your pleasure.

Je desire faire l'amour.
I am so far away,
Your touch, taste, and smell,
Seem closer than my heart.

Je desire faire l'amour.
Far away from happiness,
Longing for the warmth of your breath,
Blowing gently on my neck.

Nous faisons l'amour,
I will be home soon.
Our hearts, burning with love,
Again, will be one.
