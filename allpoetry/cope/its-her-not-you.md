---
title: "It's Her Not You"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "its-her-not-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45668-Its-Her-Not-You-by-Cope"

I wish you would die,
My only prayer to God.
You have put me through so much,
I am done.
Finished, 
With you and your game.
With you and you ignorance,
For I am in love,
I have survived with out you,
Moved on to my dream,
A dream of a great woman.
The perfect girl,
You could have been her,
Now you have fucked up.
Now it is her,
She is mine,
I am her's.
She is amazing,
You are lost.
Everything I have ever dreamed of,
Confides in her.
Everything I have ever wanted,
She has been more.
I don't need you,
Again, nevermore.
She is my dream, 
Together forever we shall be.
Hopefully you will be happy,
Even though it won't be with me.
I have her,
She has me,
Together forever we will be,
Married with a ring on her hand,
Amazingly in love we are,
and always will be.Sheena I love you forever and always.
