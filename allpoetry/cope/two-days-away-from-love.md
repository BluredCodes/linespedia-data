---
title: "Two Days Away From Love"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "two-days-away-from-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37803-Two-Days-Away-From-Love-by-Cope"
---

Tonight I sit here alone,
Still complete happiness is near,
I am coming home,
To see you, my dear.

Two days to wait,
My departure is near,
Towards you,
My heart will steer.

I can't but think,
Of the times we had,
To dream of our future lives,
They could never be sad.

Soon you will be a mom,
I will be a dad.
When we are grandparents,
We'll think of the times we had.

I will be home soon,
To the one I love,
Animals mate for life,
You are my white dove.

I love you so much,
My love will never end,
Hundreds of miles away,
My love to you I send.

Tonight I sit alone,
But not sad at all,
In two days,
I'm at your becons call.

How I yearn to see you,
Your comfort by my side,
My deepest thoughts and feelings,
In you I confide.

Soon I will be there,
Right by your side,
Our beautiful love,
We simply cannot hide.

Goodnight my love,
Your sweet dreams will last,
Because me and you,
Our love will forever last.
