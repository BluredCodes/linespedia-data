---
title: "Best Friends"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "best-friends"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

They say life is different,
After the first faze of life.
Some move on,
Some stay stagnent.
Either way, life goes on,
Quicker for some,
For others it regresses.
You and I, however,
Will forever remain friends.
Can't forget the times we had,
The times we were sad,
Because of our heartbreaks,
Parts of life have been sad.
We may have struggled,
Surely you more than I,
No matter the obsticle encountered,
We will get by.
You've helped me so much,
Defended the beast,
Faught off ignorant beauty,
A true friend you are,
And that you have always been.
You have helped me so much,
If only you knew,
You are always here for me,
No matter the trouble I get into.
Sometimes I wonder,
What could have been,
And I think of what still is,
I have never had a friend,
That is as amazing as you.
For this reason,
Here for you I will always be,
Waiting and wanting to help,
Waiting for any oppertunity.
If you ever need anything,
Don't hesitate to ask,
Not even the biggest problem,
Is too big of task.
