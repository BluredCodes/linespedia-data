---
title: "A Dream's Goodnight"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "a-dreams-goodnight"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43188-A-Dreams-Goodnight-by-Cope"
---

For years now,
It has been about the game.
Now things are different,
They'll never be the same.

I played all the time,
Shining at a young age.
Days and months spent,
At the batting cage.

I worked so hard,
For nothing in the end.
Just another ball player,
Blown away by the wind.

I shouldn't be sad now,
It was over long ago.
I knew then, but now,
The doctors told me so.

Dreams last for a lifetime,
Sure this is true,
Until, your dream is over,
Then what do you do.

It seems amazing,
My dream within reach.
I was ready for it,
I even had a speech.

Two days before signing,
Just another game.
Once my shoulder popped,
I knew things would't be the same.

I fell to the ground,
Only pain no sound.
The helped me off the field,
I surrender my mound.

From that day on,
Things have never been the same,
I now live a life of love,
Not for love of the game.
