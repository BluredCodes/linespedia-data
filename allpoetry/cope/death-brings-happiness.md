---
title: "Death Brings Happiness"
writer: "Cope"

Death’s perception,
Violence.
It’s motive,
Pain.

Waiting for death,
Coming so slowly.
Wishing upon it’s wrath,
Finally taking hold.

Violent hearts,
Haltered only by death.
Wanting what cannot be.
Happiness only comes by death.

Life was great.
After death, 
Never the same.
Death brings happiness.

A new life is born.
True death,
Only bringing an end,
To true love’s life.

The death of  that life,
Distant past.
Never forgotten,
But for the best.03-01-02,
