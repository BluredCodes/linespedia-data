---
title: "Love's Dream"
writer: "Cope"

I light my bowl, just to get high.
I think of the feeling, when you touch my thigh.
You are pretty amazing, truly not a lie,
If I ever hurt you, I think I would die.
We really are amazing, you and I,
Love's true specimine, our love will never die.
When I am with you, I don't need to get high,
The touch of your hand, gets me bye.

I sit here in hell, in complete happiness,
I said it from the begining, you I would miss.
Sometimes it scares me, being as happy as this,
Spend months waiting, dreaming of just one kiss.
Even though we are away, there's a caring closeness,
Except the one time we faught, leaving me lifeless.
Sometimes I try to imagine, things not like this,
When we are together, it truly will be complete happiness.

We are a fairytale, living our dream,
From our smiles, happiness will gleam.
Like Colorado's sunsets, reflecting off a stream,
The sun will never set on us, our everlasting dream.
I think of us with kids, going to get ice cream,
Or making love passionately, strugling to quiet your scream.
Sometimes I try to figure, are things what they seem?
Then I remember being with you, is like living a dream.
