---
title: "Faster To The Eternal Stop"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "faster-to-the-eternal-stop"
source: "AllPoetry"

Stepping out the door,
I see them.
I run, 
Faster,
Faster,
I run.
Don't stop,
Keep running,
Faster,
Faster.
Each step,
Faster than the last,
Each thud,
Getting louder,
Getting closer,
They are close now.
I cannot give up,
I must run,
Faster,
Faster,
Legs weakening,
Heart throbbing,
Out of control.
Still I must run,
They are but a breath away,
Faster I must go,
Faster than I have ever gone,
Keep running,
Don't stop,
Don't ever give up.
I run faster,
Faster,
Faster,
Waiting,
Wanting,
Yearning,
That eternal stop.
