If I were there with you,
Then we could go the the mountains and fly.
Soar above Colorado,
A death of happiness we could die.

If we could grow old together,
Then every part of the world we could see.
Every day like the first,
Me forever loving you and you always loving me.

If I had not gone,
Then asleep on the couch we would be.
Together as one,
Simpley put, for eternity.

If I were not here,
Then I would never have to leave.
Ozzy said, "No more tears",
No more of this hopeless lonely eve.

If only I went back tomorrow,
Then we could go to the mountains and fly.
Five months would be over,
Never again would we have to say good-bye.Sheena my love, I miss you.
