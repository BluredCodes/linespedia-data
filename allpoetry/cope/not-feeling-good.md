---
title: "Not Feeling Good."
writer: "Cope"
slug: "not-feeling-good"

To take care of you,
Oh how I wish I could.
To be at you command,
Oh how I would.

To bring you food,
Oh what a beautiful thing.
To bring you tissues when the pain is just too bad,
Or “You Look Wonderful Tonight” I could sing.

How I wish I could be there,
To take care of you.
Make you feel happy,
Because I know that being sick makes you feel blue.

Instead I am stuck here,
Just four lanes away.
With only one thing that I can do,
Here is what I say.

When ever the pain is just to much to bare,
And it makes you feel bad.
Just think of me,
And all of the good times that we had.

Remember the walks on the river.
Remember our first kiss.
Remember that when you were away,
That it was you that my heart truly did miss.

Remember the county roads,
And the trouble that we just missed.
Remember how cute you are,
Especially when you are pissed.

But most of all remember this,
A message from above.
Anything you need I can get, 
And it is you that I truly love.


So get better soon.
I can’t bare seeing you in pain.
All that I can say is that seeing you like this,
It makes me go insane.
