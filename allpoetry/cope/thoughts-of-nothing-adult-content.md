Do blind people see things in thier dreams?
If so do they really see them?
A pickle is a cucumber,
But is a cucumber a pickle?

When flying,
I think great thoughts,
Thoughts of great importence,
Thoughts of nothing.

Why do you call a glass a glass,
and A cup a cup?
Why is chew called chew,
Exspecially when you don't chew it?

Another hit off the bong,
You can't go wrong.
Deeper thoughts begin to flow,
My brain, then seems to go.

Why is it that we are called the land of the free,
When we are controled by a hypocritical government?
Who decided that peanut butter get's to go first,
In my favorite sandwich.

Just thoughts full of nothing,
Questions full of nonsense,
The things we think about,
With our loss of sense.Sorry Just bored.
