---
title: "Miles Away From Life"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "miles-away-from-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49493-Miles-Away-From-Life-by-Cope"
---

Life’s distance,
Seems as far as a dream.
Life’s a million miles away,
Or so it would seem.

Everything worth living,
The pleasures of life,
Bonds between friends,
The love of my future wife.

Everything is gone,
Actually it’s just me.
Nothing here brings happiness,
For as far as I can see.
