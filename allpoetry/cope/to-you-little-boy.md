---
title: "To You, Little Boy"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "to-you-little-boy"

Today, within a day dream,
That of my childhood,
Of what seems eternity ago.
Years full of questions,
Inquiries of empty replies.
Thoughts that I was mistreated,
Or that my life was rougher than most.
As my dear mother once said,
You are young and foolish,
Soon you will learn.
I watch this young man grow,
Confusion dominating all.
Thoughts and questions,
Still left unanswered.
Some lessons are better learned,
To explain,
This could be my greatest task yet.
On this day I am growing,
And grow together we must.
Dont worry little boy,
I am by your side.
Trust me little boy, 
Things are good.
My heart is with you little boy,
By your side, I shall always be.DJ. . .you're the man!
