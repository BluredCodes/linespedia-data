---
title: "A Heartfelt Moment"

The night falls,
Caressing her beauty.
Moonlight gleaming,
As do her eyes.
She walks softly,
Walking happily on air.
I feel the warmth of her hand,
Masking the nights chill.
Suddenly we are stopped,
All thoughts captured,
By a simple gaze,
I am pulled in by her love.
Lost in her green eyes,
Lost in her beauty.
She pulls me close,
Hearts pounding,
Wishing to be one,
Gently she speaks,
My soul is over taken by words.
All cares in the world,
Gone in a breath.
Words overpowering,
Like nothing felt before,
Words of honest beauty,
Words of I love you.
