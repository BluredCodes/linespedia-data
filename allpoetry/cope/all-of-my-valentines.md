---
title: "All of My Valentines"
writer: "Cope"
slug: "all-of-my-valentines"

It is insane,
To only have one Valentine,
To ask one person,
Will you be mine?

I decided,
On this day of love,
A lot of people in my life,
Are a gift from above.

First to Ma Mere,
You have been though a lot,
Even through the roughest times,
I love you, you never forgot.

To my boys,
Let the party cotinue,
We will be friends forever,
No matter our venue.

Ah, the old man,
Best friend of mine,
Father becomes friend,
Our lives are always just fine.

To my family,
The Doc, My hippie friend,
My second parents,
Til the end.

My one true love,
Happy Valentines day!
It won't be much longer,
That I have to be away.

This year on this speacial day,
I want to say,
I love you all.
I miss you while I'm away.

You all mean so much,
It must be a sign,
Today I ask you all,
Be my Valentine.Happy Valentine's Day to everyone!   I miss you all.
