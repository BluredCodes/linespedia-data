title: "Before I Go"
writer: "Cope"

Today I sat here,
Sat and thought,
Thought of the past,
Thought of the present,
And wondered as to the future.

Life has been good.
I cannot complain.
I have loved.
I have lost.
And then loved again.

Friends have been made,
Some of which will last a life time.
Some often forgotten.
Friends are what life is all about.
What is life without some one to live it with?

We’ve had many great times,
And others pretty bad.
We’ve partied and been happy,
We’ve cried and been sad.
But I’ll never forget the times that we had.

Now things are changing.
As they say life goes on.
Confusion and madness,
In everyone’s mind.
Who will move on and who will get left behind.

Then I leave,
Everything’s gone.
I’ll miss everything,
And I’ll miss everyone.
The future is here.

It’s time to get things done.
Work for a happy life.
Maybe have a wife,
Maybe have a kid,
Who knows?

All I know is that my friends,
You, I will miss.
Please stay in touch,
Because no matter what happens,
Your friend, I will always be.
