Love?
So gentle,
So beautiful,
So wonderful,
So amazing.
To be encompassed with love,
These are your feelings.
Ones of great perfection,
And pure pleasure.
Such great feelings and emotions,
The warmth of a lover’s grasp,
Sheilding the winter’s chilly rath.
The excitement and comfort,
Of simply hearing that “one” person’s voice.
Life could not be better,
And, yet, happiness still seems to erupt
All due to this simple little thing,
Love.
