title: "Turn Your Face To Heaven"
writer: "uglybeautiful"

turn your face to heaven
but is there really light shining down
something seems to be missing
this precious gift was taken
from a child's grip was torn
one and all born free
but everything is birthed into slavery
enslaved within this time
you have shaken the chains loose
broke the bonds of this life
the freedom that you hold 
will that freedom ever be felt by me?
you guide me with your hands
light my path with your glow
but i stumble so often
i am afraid ill never know
the happiness you know
unleash my mind 
fly away
im weighted by my own uncertainty
who am i?
answer me this burning question
do you know the answer?
i will never know
you believe in me
but i can't feel what you do
i cant believe in me
in the end
all is stripped away
you remain
and i the same
together in our love for one
all i see is you
as one we hold on till the end of everything
and feel this warming light shine down
with tears of love trickling down
we turn our face to heavencant remember,
