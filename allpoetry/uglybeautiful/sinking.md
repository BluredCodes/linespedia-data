sinking 
sinking into this
kicking
i want to leave this place
screaming
who knows the hate i have for this?
you? me? God?
what is this? Fate?
suffering onto me like the weight of this life
crushing me
this painful something, this little something unknown
my heart my life
impaled through yours, to fuck up your own life
your own blood and flesh, ruining your life
regret everything, almost
the pain caused to you, the hurt
and to you, two angels of God
all thanks is given, to you
all love is from you, for you
such pure truth exists 
in the depths of your common mind
one goal, to help, to save, to love
you have, so unselfishly so perfectly
what is inside this, is everything to one
that one who from which all this pain ripples outward
hurting everyone he holds dear
the sorrow is so great within his heart
the love he holds for you so great, he cannot lose you ever
without losing himself completely
and in turn, losing everythingcant remember,
