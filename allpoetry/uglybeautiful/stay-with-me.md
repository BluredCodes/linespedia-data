---
title: "Stay With Me"
writer: "uglybeautiful"

i cant live with this inside get in the car lets take a ride
tell me friend, do you feel like dying tonight?
Its too late all choices crossed out, ill put you to rest then take care of myself
[chorus] they spit in my face destroy all my faith, my friends walked away all thats left is disgrace, you my last friend, my one love you are going to stay
dont go im afraid of alone, so sad i long to answer the phone
tell me friend, do you feel like dying tonight?
why is this the way it always has to be? tell me whats so fucking wrong with me
[chorus]
come on lets take a ride(2x)
come on lets die tonight(2x)
why wont you stay with me(2x)
youre gonna stay with me(2x)
i'll make you stay with me
