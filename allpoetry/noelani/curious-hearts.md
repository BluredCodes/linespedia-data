---
title: "Curious Hearts"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "curious-hearts"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see you when you watch me
Looking so gentle, yet so very strong
My heart beats longingly
Can a feeling so vibrant be wrong?
A glance stolen, I see us, a we.

If only I could tell you
How I feel inside
That it could be us two
Believe me when I say
My heart tells me true.

Everyday, you're on my mind
I see you look at me with your curious stare
Wondering what you might find
If you should discover you care
Don't be surprised, love don't care if you go against the grind.

Everytime my eyes close
It's you who I see
A vivid vision in my head shows
Together you and me
For this moment time slows.

Curious thoughts through my head run
When will our moment arise
Will I hear a few words so beautifully sung
A glorious moment flies
And the day is still young.
