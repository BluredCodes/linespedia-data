---
title: "Bound In His Words"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "bound-in-his-words"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33343-Bound-In-His-Words-by-Noelani"
---

He keeps me grounded
Farther below any other
Locking me in with his thoughts, bounded
Negativity everywhere, I feel it smother.

His eyes are so full of hate
It cuts like a knife, with each glance
Resentment of myself, is what he helped create
His startling glare keeps me in his trance.

When I finally feel content
He comes and pulls me back down
Tearing me up inside, is his very intent
And with every word he says, only deeper I drown.

As hard as I try
It’s always easier to believe the bad
Simpler, when his critical voice is nearby
Piercing my hopes, and heart that is so sad.

I’ll never understand
Why it’s me, you carefully chose
To grip only tighter in the palm of your hand
And your voice that in my head, still echoes.

I’m bound in his words
His voice, is of my brother’s.My brother can't tolerate me, and I'm honestly not sure why. He often IS tearing me down, and I often mistake his (fake) kindess for true kindness, because most of the time he's only nice and considerate if there's something in it for him, and sadly, this is the truth.
