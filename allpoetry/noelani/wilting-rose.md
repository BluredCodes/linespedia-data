---
title: "Wilting Rose"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "wilting-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7355-Wilting-Rose-by-Noelani"
---

Someone so close
And dear to my heart
Only God knows
When you from earth, will depart
Why would he grow a wilting rose?

Your beauty glows with your confident stand
You flourished with a smile
Until the day which you await and
Ready to walk down the purest isle
To embrace God's open hand.

Your unconditional love
Is a world of difference for those who care
We will always remember, as you rise above
A person like you, is really quite rare
The gentle soul of a dove.

Inspiration you've brought me
Because you had such will
Past the black you did see
Strenth you protrayed still
'Til the day your spirit soars free.

In my heart, forever you will stay
A friend who will always be there
Knowing each day
I will say a prayer
And you can hear, and caress the sky with what you may.

An angel looking upon
Someone to make you proud
A girl who will stay strong
Even when I want to scream so loud
'Cause I miss you so, now that you're gone.
