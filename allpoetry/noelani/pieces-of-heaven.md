---
title: "Pieces of Heaven"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "pieces-of-heaven"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4932-Pieces-of-Heaven-by-Noelani"

The days are so long
The hours seem days
And still, slowly each day goes on
In time, destiny will lead us to.

When I saw you that night
While you were out with your friends
I remember your face, glowing in the light
How I longed to be with you.

My soul fills with such affection
As I watch you, watching me
Wondering if you can feel the connection
Hoping you do.

I see your smile so sweet
As I shyly smile back
Our eyes for a moment meet
I hear so many unspoken words.

I dream of the day, I surrender myself in your embrace
A match made in Heaven
As I listen to your heart race
Knowing the two of us are meant to be.

Pieces of Heaven
Moments are jewels
Treasure them always
Forever and a day.
