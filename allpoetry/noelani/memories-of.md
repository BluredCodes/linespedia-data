title: "Memories Of....."
writer: "Noelani"

Memories so dear
Are always close to my heart
Days that look so vivid and clear
While adding new ones to this special place
And looking back, year after year.

My memories of glory
Times of happiness and love
Even days of sadness have a story
To tell and share
Whether it's sweet, or a little gory.

My sad memories are ones I try to brush
aside, make way for the positive
The ones that are: "Dahling, simply lush!"
Still, the sad ones seem to scream so loud
Wishing to depress, only stopping when I say "hush."

Joyous moments stand tall
Looking proud, for they're most prized
Sharp of memory, giving their all
Looking quite confident with a laugh and a smile
Happy is safe, for it won't let you fall.

Why does time seem to slip away so fast
When I'm having so much fun
Hoping it will last
Keeping it with me forever
Never too far in the past.
