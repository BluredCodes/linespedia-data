title: "Forever As One"

For our country so lovedThe limits are goneAs each day goes byWe will grow eternally strong.Forever we willBe united as oneTogether we stand'Til the battle is won.Through this time in our livesOnly faith in our LordReminds us, we're gonna make itGod, give us strength, to put down our swords.Let us pray for thoseWho are is such dire needThey need our loveWhen they are lost, we will take the lead.One day this will passWe're part of history todaySoon, only memories we'll haveOh, forever we'll remember it this way.
