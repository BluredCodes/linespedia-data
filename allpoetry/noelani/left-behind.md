---
title: "Left Behind"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "left-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It's all I can think about
You're on my mind everyday
Thinking about questions with unknown answers
And wondering why He took you so far away
Yet, to me it still doesn't seem real, and you don't seem so far
It feels more like we're in a play
That we're just actors who aren't living this life.

I was left behind with so many questions
Not knowing what I should do
Left alone in this lonely place
Not really knowing where you went off to
You just left me standing there
Without the slightest clue
When I might see you again.

I get this feeling inside of me
That tells me you really haven't gone
As if you never left me at all, standing there alone
I feel you watching over me, keeping me true and strong
Urging me to keep going until I've reached the top
Then I hear you sing such an amazing song
Telling me to follow my dreams
To keep going, believing that you would never tell a lie and do anything wrong.
I loved you before you left and I loved you even when I was left behind
Forever and always will you be with my heart.
