---
title: "Trapped"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "trapped"
source: "AllPoetry"

Enclosed by four walls that glare
I need to find an escape
To break from your menacing stare
But I can't when you stick to me like tape
And with each struggle to breathe open air
Freedom becomes seldom, as I'm hidden behind a drape
Trapped, where sunlight is rare.

Held back against my will
Bound to you so very tight
Your piercing eyes look at me so still
As if they are daring me to fight
A challenge, of the mentally ill.

Staying quiet keeps me sane
When I can dream of the day
I'm out of your reach, and free of pain
I can't stay locked up, I need my own way
Dependence can never gain
Still you force me to stay
Where my soul will continually drain.
