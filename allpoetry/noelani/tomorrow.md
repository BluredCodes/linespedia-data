---
title: "Tomorrow"
writer: "Noelani"

Open your heart
Let your spirit go
And make a new start.

Strive to find your deepest desires
That are hidden beneath
Ones buried below ashes of smothered fires.

Speak your mind
And push yourself harder
To work yourself out of this bind.

Believe in yourself
For there will be many who won't
So take that heart off the shelf.

Hold on to your dreams
And don't ever let go
People are not as they seem.

Love is the lesson
To never forget
Treasure the words so romantically sung.

Know that life is a blessing in disguise
Take it for all it is
Keep in mind that time flies
Live life to the fullest
For the clock lies
There is not always Tomorrow.

Breathe to taste life
Believe, Dream, Love and Speak
"Live like there's no Tomorrow."
