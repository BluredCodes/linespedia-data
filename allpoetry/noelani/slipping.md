There's too many choices
And my time is running low
I'm hearing too many voices
Who are telling me which way to go.

I sit here in a thick silence
With only the company of myself
And thoughts that don't make sense
Because my heart sits upon a shelf.

Seconds become minutes
And still my words collide
As this knowledge slowly hits
Your wish, so difficult to abide.

I wish I could say yes
Yet I wish to deline
When it comes to you, I must confess
Words tangle together so fine.

Even while my head and heart debate
The time quickly seeps from my grip.
Will this decision, become my destined fate?
