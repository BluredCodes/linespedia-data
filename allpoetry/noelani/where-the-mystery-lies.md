What is it about you
That makes people strive to be
Independent, confident and true
Challenging others to break free.

Questions no one dares to ask you
Though answers you do hold
And words so amazingly true
Incredible knowledge, modestly showed.

Mystery rests within your eye
Concealing your thoughts from view
Confident you appear, anything but shy
Yet only opening to a select few.

Your influence taken to heart
With words spoken, sharp to the mind
Puncturing like a dart
Painfully honest, yet kind.

Eerily aware of your presence
Words said, but not spoken
Leaving me always in suspense
Pondering your thoughts, strong and unbroken.

Why do you look at me so
With your eyes that allure
Drawing me in, ever so slow
As I search your gaze, that seems so sure.

Your charm attracts all
Who come in your way
Longing to hear your voice call
And impatiently awaiting that glorious day.I wrote this about my friend. He is so amazingly mysterious and deep. He has a personality that just draws people to him. I hope everyone likes it. =D
