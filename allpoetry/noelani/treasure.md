writer: "Noelani"

Our moments together
Mean everything to me
You make me smile
Even when I'm down
And you make me laugh
When no one else can
You knew as much about me
As I knew of you
Sometimes I knew what you were going to say
Before any words left your lips
Sometimes I could sense what you were thinking
I even shared your pain
You came into my life when I wasn't looking
These moments together are treasures.
