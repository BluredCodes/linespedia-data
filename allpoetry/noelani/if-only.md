If only I could tell you
How I feel inside
I could tell you 
About these sensations
I've never felt before
I could tell you
That I like you
Though it wouldn't be true
Because I feel so much more
I could say I love you
But that would be too strong
For I've never experienced 
Such a wonderful feeling.

If only I could tell you
The happiness I feel
When you say 'Hey'
And my heart starts beating faster.

If only I could find the right words
To say how I feel
If only I could find the courage
To tell you how much I care.
