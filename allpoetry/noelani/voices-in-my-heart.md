---
title: "Voices In My Heart"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "voices-in-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22743-Voices-In-My-Heart-by-Noelani"
---

His eyes are challenging mine
Daring me to hold
This gaze so divine
His stare is never cold
But filled with warmth, well defined.

But it's his sweet smile that invites
Me into his special place
As we roam the city's sights
In a wide open space
Through dark starry nights.

So we talked together today
Just you and I alone
I, hanging on everyword you say
With every change of tone
Wanting to always remain this way.

How I wish you felt the same
As we catch up on days not long ago
My heart leaps like a children's game
I breathe in deep, so my heart will slow
As I look into his dancing eyes that set me aflame.

I told you of my worry
Lifting all my stress
You sent it away in a hurry
Somehow making it feel less
Vanishing like a dove in a snow flurry.

We spoke of our future
After mentioning our present
I remember how you sounded so sure
Of your plans to me you sent
This day, I'll always treasure.

The time went much too fast
These minutes together flew
And now it's all in the past
A conversation so true
In my memory, it will always last.

Your voice in my heart.
