Sweet angel from above
Can you hear my silent plea
My only wish for true love
To find me, and set my heart free.

I search a love so true
Who's romantic, yet fun
Who can make the old seem new
And make my heart-beat run.

Sweet angel are you listening
To my heart that desires
Angel, can I hear your pretty voice sing
Of a guy who can light true love's fires.

Please send him down to me
From your protecting embrace
For he and I are meant to be
For me, he skipped lust's one race.

Sweet angel can't you tell
It's him I so much need
He's the one I want to tell
How I love and ache for him, my heart bleeds.I might add more to this later because they could be song lyrics if I work on them more, and add more...but I'll see how it goes just like this for now.
