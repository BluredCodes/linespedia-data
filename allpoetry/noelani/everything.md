---
title: "Everything"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "everything"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38082-Everything-by-Noelani"
---

Everything about you
Makes me feel good inside
Everything you do
makes my heart soar far and wide
Through everything, you're true.

I love your voice so sweet
That soothes me into the night
Your words a song, everytime we meet.

I love your gentle touch
That sends tingles down my back
It's your touch, I long for so much.

I love your soft, tender kiss
When you hold me tight and close
When you're away, it's you I miss.

Everything about you
Lets me know, love so true is rare
Everything you do
Lets me know you care
Through everything you're true.

***I love you***

(whispers)
