Amazing people did I see and meet
My states senators, JFK's brother, and Hillary Clinton
But what made this trip so sweet
Was getting to know you.

Days are suddenly more bright
Because of your optimistic ways
You've shown me the world, and all that is right
Simple things that were never thought twice.

You, who could make me smile
Keep me laughing
We could always talk for quite awhile
To you, I opened up and let myself go.

If I could pick a word to define
The alluring vibe you give out
Not one could descibe your aura divine
The one I've come to discover in a few days spent.

In the week I have known you
A romantic, comedy or an artist
People like you, so caring are few
A friend who's honest and true.This poem, I dedicate to all of my new friends who went on the Washingon D.C. Close Up the week of December 1 - December 8, 2001. It was great meeting so many new people, and growing close to a few of you.
