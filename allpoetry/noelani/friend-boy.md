The day we met
I saw the warmth in your eyes
Beneath your hair so wet
The windows to where your soul lies
In the moment I saw you, I knew.

A smile we did share
For a moment we spoke
Standing in a world without care
Exchanging a private joke
A friend, I have found in you.

You were so sweet
I admit, friends was not the first idea I had
The boy who smiles each time we meet
Who could resist such a tempting lad?
Was it friends, or maybe something more?

I ask myself, is it worth
Such a gorgeous friendship
As pretty as the flowers upon the earth
Will it survive if we happen to slip?
I am so scared something bad will happen.

Please tell me
That you feel the same
That you are seeing what I see
A friendship in such a confused frame
Please say that you are just as concerned.In this particular poem, I had a hard time putting what I was feeling into words. Right now, this is so confusing, wondering if it's too risky to put this friendship to test.
