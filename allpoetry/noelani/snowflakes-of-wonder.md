---
title: "Snowflakes of Wonder"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "snowflakes-of-wonder"

Snowflakes hold a certain power
When they're gently falling with such grace
An unknown strength, with beauty of a flower
Contradicting, are their delicate edges of lace.

They fall so silent
Putting peace in the air
With every snowflake from the sky sent
Each one, unique and fair.

A sense of calmness 
Feelings of love
Make my worries seem much less
All this, from a tiny snowflake from above.

The ground turns a pure white 
Making everything look so clean
Oh, it's such a beautiful sight
This world that appears so serene.

A smile mysteriously appears
While I watch with wonder and awe
And the skies frozen tears
They beauty of the season, tonite, I saw.
