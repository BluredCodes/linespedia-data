I feel my mind race
At an unbelievable pace
Running from lifes demeaning face
Thoughts never the right time or place
Abstract thoughts come and go without a trace.

Unexplainable moments never calm, but not sane
My mind dances without thought in the rain
Alone in my head to handle this pain
A pain that leaves a stain
Good times lived in vain.

It doesn't break for air
Can't chance it, it doesn't dare
Keeps going along without a care
A moment of thought that's sane is rare
Relax a moment please, much longer, I cannot bare.

I long for a moment when I'm sane and again can think
A moment when life doesn't disappear in a blink
When I don't feel as if I'm gonna sink
Into the oceans salty drink
Lost in nights blackest ink.

It has a plan of its own
To keep me imprisoned completely alone
Until one day, when it's strong and full-grown
It may empower, control me as it's own precious clone
Draining me until I'm weaker, living off my soul, until its only stone.

When will he leave, when can I walk free
Live without directions, for I cannot see
While I struggle to find thee
Who is hiding within me
