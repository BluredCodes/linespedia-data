---
title: "Smeared"
writer: "Noelani"
slug: "smeared"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I'll let you know, my greatest fear
Is because of you
With your vision unclear
Your eyes hazy with dew
Struggling to peer
To see who stands before you
As I wipe away, a solitary tear
My voice giving you a clue
Urging you to see, with what you hear
Forming an image from the heart so true
Remembering it was only back a year
When you could see us clear, and like new
I stand by you near
So you can make out how I grew
Squinting until you eyes are in gear
And in your mind you drew
An image of me so dear
I stand there, my heart heavy and blue
Looking into a world of a mirror
Where opposite is many, and true is few
And I see my own vision go fuzzy and smear
I feel tears streaming anew
In fear, oneday, you won't recognize me.
