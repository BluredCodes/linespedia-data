---
title: "Tender Love"
writer: "Noelani"

From the moment we met
I could hear it with each word
Someday, into my heart, you I'd let
For me, this seemed absurd.

I gaze into your eyes so caring
Feeling your tender touch
And I knew this wasn't just a fling
With your every expression, I see so much.

A protective arm
Wraps around me
Keeping me so very warm
You and I, are a 'We'.

A sweet kiss you then deliver
Your hands rested upon my hips
With a touch of you, attraction sends a shiver
I still feel a lingering of your lips.

I've never felt such love
As strong as yours and mine
I think it's a gift from up above
From the Heavens so divine.
