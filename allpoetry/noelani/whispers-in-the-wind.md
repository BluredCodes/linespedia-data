title: "Whispers In The Wind"
writer: "Noelani"

I hear your whispers in the windSurrounding me in your embraceTelling me that I shouldn't worryThat you're just giving me your grace.I love hearing your comforting soundsTelling me 'Everything's going to be OK'And 'Don't be alarmed, 'cause I'll always be around.''I'll be your guardian from day to day.'You're always to be found.I'm seeing everything in a blurMy heart is making loud, fast beatsI can hear your words talking in a slurYour loving words are in pain.I want to keep you with meTo keep me safe in your armsFor you and I are a 'we'We keep each other from harm.I'm not ready to be on my ownYou're the only one I have ever desiredI'll soon be aloneUntil I hear your tender whispers in the windFeeling comfortTake me overKnowing you won't be away forever.
