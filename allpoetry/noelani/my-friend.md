She’s my best friend
Someone I can always trust
Far beyond the days end
Where secrets are safe, locks never rust
You’ve always had an ear to lend.

We’ve talked about everything
From school to the hottest guy
There was always a shoulder upon we could lean
When all we wanted to do was cry
And keep the hurt from being seen.

Through disagreements and many a heartbreak
We’ve weathered it all
Sharing a smile, and easing the ache
There to break each other’s fall
And supporting each decision you or I make.

People like you
Are few and hard to find
With a heart gold and true
A one-of-a kind
A friend who always knew.

My sister, as well as my comrade
A partner in crime
I have to tell you I’m glad
We’re friends after so much time
With happy memories, to share and to add.


Dedicated to: My best friend, Tara…for always being there, no matter what.
