---
title: "Americas Angels"
writer: "Noelani"

I can still taste the fear
Feel my heart aching with sorrow and pain
As I watch with growing pride, as my angels near
Chaos is in the air, but here is where they remain
Holding my hand, wiping away a tear.

Thousands of screams fill the air
I can hear loud thumps hitting the ground
This is more than I can bare
My head spins, making a steady pound
Until the angels radiate their love and tender care.

That day, emblazoned in my mind
Sensations I've never known before
A fear I never thought I'd find
Until I saw this day of horror
And I wonder, how we came to be so blind.

A precious angel then led me into the light
Giving me new hopes and new dreams
Angel, don't leave me behind, I can help you fight
And suddenly, the world isn't as tough as it seems
Because with you close by, everything is right.

When you held my hand, as I cried
I knew I would never forget you
While you kept me safe, so I didn't have to hide
You said we'd be alright, this you knew
'Cause you'd always be at my side.I dedicate this to all the firefighters who gave and risked their lives to save many others in the 9*11 tragedy.

P.S. In case you didn't realize, the angels are the fire fighters.
