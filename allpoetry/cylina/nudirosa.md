NUDIROSA
                      
                   
                  In spring's cool shade of dawn
                  embed the honeysucked dew
                  bluebirds rise to rosey verse,
                  wings stretched skyward,
                  warbling a sunny serenade~
                  'Happy New Year's New Day'.

                 "A dream robed in vision endowed
                  with wisdom's song-soaked seed
                  ~~ Destiny's starburst kiss ~~
                  sustained by northern rainlight
                  tints the gifted blossom ablue;
                  Nudirosa is promised and true.

                  Embrace the face of her flower
                  crowned in the strength
                  of a helm driven star,
                  the tip of lips plump
                 'neath a saphire canopy
                  and cradled by an early moon".

                  I strum pluck her 
                  from an evergreen throne
                  glossed in bough-strung
                  budded teardrops,
                  her beauty I behold
                  bequeathed at hand.

                  My lustered love makes
                  a bare birthday's wish.
                  Midnight wind blows the flame
                  from a straggly street lamp
                  swiping palmed petals~
                  I kneel to save her.This poem is based on actual events.
