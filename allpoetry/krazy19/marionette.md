From our birth into life
We're trapped by the rules
Rules we can't escape
We try but we can't 
We find that it's fate

From our birth into life
We're stars in the show
The world is our set
There's no escaping the hands of the Marionette

The strings controll us 
No matter how much we fight
They'll never let go
Just growing ever more tight

Like a feather drifting in the air
Sailing and swaying without any care
It goes with the flow 
With the wind where ever it blows

Sometimes you'll see me with my head down
In the pouring rain
Trying to find answers to questions
Nobody can seem to explain

Sometimes you'll catch me with my head to the sky
Crying and yelling "God Why!"

From our birth into life 
We're trapped by the rules
Rules we can't escape
We try but we can't
We find that it's fate,
to live on the set
