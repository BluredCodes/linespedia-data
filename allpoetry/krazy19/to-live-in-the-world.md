title: "To Live in the World"

Do not let the world lead you astray
Keep what you have and the things you say might
Live it all out and continually pray

Though you think it's all over you say
It won't let you go 'till you put up a fight
Do not let the world lead you astray

Share with your feelings and they'll show you the way
But don't be suprised if it doesn't feel right
Live it all out and continually pray

As surely as it sets to end the long day
It soon will rise to pierce through the night
Do not let the world lead you astray

And the memories of her i can't keep away
Her radiant beauty, irresistable sight
Do not let the world lead you astray
Live it all out and continually pray.
