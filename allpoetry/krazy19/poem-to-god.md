For all the wrong that we've doneYou still gave us your sonWhen we were struggling to liveIn a continuous fightYou fell upon us, and showed us your lightWhen we were deep in despairAnd deep in sorrowYou loved us today, and you'll love us tomorrowWith your word fill us upOverflowing our cupsShow us the waysYou want us to goGive us the words you want us to knowOur Lord and our GodAnd our Savior from allGive us the wisdom to hear when you callAnd the power to rise up and stand tall.Give thanx with a grateful heart!
