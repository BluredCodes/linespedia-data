I heard a knocking at my door
Well it was more of a pounding
A pounding shaking the floor
So from my table i did arise
Opened the door, and to my suprise
DEATH, death was shaking my floor
Knock knock knocking at my door
My suprise was different much to my dismay
For years I've tried to get a hold of him
Now he's finally come my way
I've screamed for him
Yelled for him
But he never returned my call
But now he's here
And i found that I never lived at all

And so, I invited him in
Sat him at my table
And asked "How could I have never been"
His reply (with a subtle voice, not the voice you hear in movies 
and other fictioucious images of him)
"You call the life you live, a life well lived, or even a life to give?
You're made up in your mind
Lost and confused
Searching for something you could not find"
My mind was in somewhat of disaray
I mean what the hell do you do
When even Death won't give you the time of day?dont think that i have quite finished it yet..kinda stuck on it....
