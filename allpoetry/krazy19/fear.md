Do you see the fear in my eyes?
Im scared for my life
A life full of lies

Fear of rejection bye the ones I hold dear
What do they think? What do they say?
Are the things that I fear

The friends that I've lost
More often than not
My life and my money is all that it cost

My family and few friends, there for the extra mile
1 of my friends I live for her smile

Sarah's her name
Fear of rejection
Hoping she feels the same

Don't have much to give
No riches, no wealth
Just this life that I live

Do you now see the fear?
Fear of my life,
A life full of lies
Fear of rejection
The fear in my eyes?umm..yeah :\
