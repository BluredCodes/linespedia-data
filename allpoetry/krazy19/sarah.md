Her smile it shines
Like the sun in the dark
Holding a place deep in my heart

Her eyes have a glow
Like the stars in the night
Memories of her I hold on to tight

Her voice is a song
Music to my ears
Thinking of her brings me to tears

Her skin is so soft
Like the silk of a dress
Her undying beauty is what i confess
