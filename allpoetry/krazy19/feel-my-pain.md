title: "Feel My Pain"

Feel my Pain
Feel the same
Go through my life
That drove me insane

Watch me cry
And ask me "why"
Know the pain 
That leads me to die

Walk with me through this night
Looking for purpose, looking for light
Feel the pain 
To fight this fight

So, if you've never felt pain
You've lived your life sane

I ask before you judge me,
that you

Feel my pain
Feel the same
Go through my life
That drove me insane.A life without pain? Show me.
