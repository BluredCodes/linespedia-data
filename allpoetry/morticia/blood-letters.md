Cut her finger for a liquid was needed.. A red one..to show her love and passion..







As the needle got closer.. 
Her fragile hands were trembling.. 
With closed eyes... 
The weapon went through her.. 
She took it off as she opened her eyes.. 
Sighing... 
For she knew..She was hurt for a reason.. 
Purified an old pen.. 
And penetrated it ..into her flesh 
Blood letting to fulfill every single wound.. 
Coming from the heart!18th January 2002,
