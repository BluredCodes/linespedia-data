Moments of Stars


Beneath these all and any
far-flung specks of scattered stars,
  remains this very mystery
		of who we really are.

As if this night, like others,
should reveal some brightly why
  for my vaguest half-thought wondering
		like deep darkness of the sky.

That there's some hopeful reason
with a purpose to be here,
  and perhaps when stars have vanished
		all deep mysteries are made clear?
