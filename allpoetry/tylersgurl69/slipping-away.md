title: "Slipping Away"

Minute by minute
day by day, 
I slowly feel you 
slipping away.  
Slipping into a world
completely unknown, 
no more togetherness, 
just being alone.  
No more drives along sunflower fields, 
no more fighting with armoured shields.  
No more trips to distant lands, 
no more there who understand. 
no more wildflowers on river bends, 
no more of the excitement of making amends.  
No more anniversaries of admitting who's wrong, 
no more slow dancing to our favortie song.  
No more sunsets or showing we care, 
no more romance for us to share. 
Now, all I'm left with is the memor of your touch, 
and I know that nothing could ever mean that much.  
Mean as much as the love shared by two, 
and the beauty of saying the words, "I love you!"
