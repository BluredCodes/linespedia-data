---
title: "The Perfect You"
writer: "TylersGurl69"
slug: "the-perfect-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7516-The-Perfect-You-by-TylersGurl69"

It's amazing how your perfect in every single way How you can take my tears and make it all okay The way you take my hand when we walk The way we sit there for hours and talk I love the way you need me I love the way you care I love knowing that you will always be there I love the perfect way you look, the perfect way you smile But most of all I love how you make my day all worthwhile I can't stand to close my eyes when you're near I'm afraid i'll miss something, thats my biggest fear The way you look when you fall asleep The way you don't make even the slightest peep I love how you hold my hand and never put it down The way we look into eachothers eyes and don't make a sound I love the way we are together and nothing has to happenBut if theres one thing I love about you the most It's the way you smile the perfect way you do, to let me know everythngs ok And what we have is better than any words could ever say.
