---
title: "My love for you"
writer: "TylersGurl69"
slug: "my-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7331-My-love-for-you-by-TylersGurl69"

Thoughts of bad images run through my head But then I shake them off and think about what he said. My body seems fine to any un-aided eyes But my inside is abused battered and deprived. I still love him even though I'm hurt so bad Can't he look at me and realize I'm so sad? I wish this never happened and now I'll never see Cuz these tears in my eyes are forever blinding me. I guess these lyrics shall end I'm sorry to say But I don't want to be a bother or get in your way. I just want to say I'm sorry if this was my fault But I know it's my problem its my troubles this whole mess caused. Please take me back and give me another chance For I've lost my life, my world, & true romance.
