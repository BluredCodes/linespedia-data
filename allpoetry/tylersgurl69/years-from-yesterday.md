When boys meant 'yuck'and friends were new,dreams were unshattered,and worries few.When recess was too shortand classes too long,decisions came easywithout need to belong.When storks delivered babiesand love wasnt so strong,friendships were unbroken,right was right & wrong was wrong.When bad things didn't happen,when only skinned knees brought tears,and the night light in its socketquieted all our fears.When farewell meant just for summerand real friends didn't part,fun went on foreverand never left a broken heart
