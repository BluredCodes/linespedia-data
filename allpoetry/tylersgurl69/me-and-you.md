Skin of silkLong to caressWhen im in angerU calm my distressHand of the angelI long to holdOnly the fingertips of theeCan warm the coldLips of velvetI long to kissWithout youMy world is amissDo u feel the same?We may never knowThe world of silenceMay never be shownThis is a tormented lifeMy emotions to unfetterWithout your touchMy life we begin to shatterSo much we giveSo much we gainA life with us togetherWon’t ever bring painOnly in foreverMy heart can be trueIn heaven and on earthThis is promise youFor always and nowForever you will beI will always be thinking of YOU AND ME.
