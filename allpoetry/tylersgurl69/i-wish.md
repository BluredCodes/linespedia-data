title: "I wish..."

I wish I could sleep and never have to wake, 
Because nothing is better than the dreams my heart can make. 
You hold my hand, you always care, 
You look into my eyes, you play with my hair, 
You smile that sweet smile that makes me melt, 
And the feelings we share I have never felt. 
You always understand, without ever putting me down, 
You know exactly what to do to make me smile and not frown. 
You drop everything to rush right to my side, 
And, from me, there's never anything you'd want to hide. 
So when I awake and a tear's in my eye, 
I wonder why dreams aren't real, and I try not to cry. 
Because if dreams were real, you would be mine 
And I wouldn't have to keep all these feelings locked inside.
