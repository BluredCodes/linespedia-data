I see your smile from across the room And it's glowing like a star shining bright Everyone around us seems to disappear, Like the light escapes the sky at night. I see you starting to walk towards me Ignoring everything but my face, I cant stop my stomach from twisting And this smile I cant seem to erase. Your embrace now lets me know at last That all the love I've given you Will finally be returned to me now, And this feeling of being loved is all new. Im holding you now in my arms so tight And I don?t feel ashamed doing so, For youre holding me twice as tight I wish that I never had to let go. Suddenly Im being pulled away, But what could possibly pull me from you? It's real life coming back to face me, And my eyes open to a world without you.
