title: "I'm sorry"

I'm sorry for the pain 
I know I've put you through 
I'm sorry for the words I've said 
I didn't know what to do 
I know that I made mistakes 
and they took their toll on you 
I know you're angry and upset 
If you want to refuse you have every right to 
But I want to ask you a difficult thing 
Please consider carefully 
I don't know what changes it will bring 
Could you find it in your heart to please forgive me?
