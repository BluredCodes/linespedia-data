fallen in love fallen to fast I'm in too deep forever don't last i loved you yesterday love you still i think i always have i know i always will I know im not gorgeous not a wish come true I'm only human just a girl who's loving you everyone says i should give up on you but they don't see you like i do your the one who broke my heart and the reason my world fell apart your the one who made me cry and i love you and i dont know why i can dance like no ones watching i could love you like i'v never been hurt i love to sing when everyones listening but without you theres no heaven on earth the last time my heart was broken i thought i was through i swore i'd never love again and i believed it to be true but when you walked into my life again the secound your eyes met mine i knew for a fact this was real your worth loving one more time
