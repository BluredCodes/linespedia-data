You looked into my heart and said 
'our love will always be' 
but now i see your real intent 
It wasn't about me. 

You kissed me with your lips so sweet 
and told me how you cared 
But now i see the lies beneath 
it wasn't love we shared. 

I thought our love was real and strong 
because you held me tight 
But now i see that i was wrong 
You just blinded my sight. 

I knew that there was something wrong 
I saw it in your eyes 
you said you loved me but i knew 
eventually all love dies. 

You lied to me to keep me near 
you lied right from the start, 
I only got one thing from you, 
I bear a broken heart.
