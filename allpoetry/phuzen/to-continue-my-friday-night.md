I steady keep eyes focused
Charged to attack openings left bogused
My mouth gapes wide open
Word-prep device like an untrained novice type
Proof-fooled into situations
Tri-pod conversations 
that stood up on two legs
invisible stature and grace
on that one good limb hopping that good race
and short stopping forward the goal
for smiles stole in time to
see it seems it could be signed by the same
paring different names to a formerly famed play
for seaming preparations can never make [for]
the un-generations a two-part creates
