I call myself lacking. 
Of system. 
Of expense. 
Of love. 
Of emotion. 
Lacking of nourishment found in thought. 
Progression and most certainly interest.

I call myself you. 
Want you. 
Be you. 
Feel you. 
And then on occasion all about you.

I call myself a conjuction. 
Between us. 
But you. 
And of course me
But then I find myself singularly enforced in the I 
and by myself in the me 
in the sense self provides a divided we. 

But on occasion there could be both.
