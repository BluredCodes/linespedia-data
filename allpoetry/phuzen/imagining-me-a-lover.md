---
title: "...imagining me a lover..."
writer: "PHuZen"
slug: "imagining-me-a-lover"

I love how my skin embraces the thought of another
Folding, bending, caressing your surface style
Hugging you being around me and you become 
Mold for bodily functions. You become defined in my soft skin
Toying with nerve endings and tweeking mental capacity
With a twist and turn. You are what I imagine, a nothingness
Occupying a zone of me, so are you.
Anything I could become without corners, sharp edges
Without a horizon line to end it. 

Spouting lyrics through me, infusing me with that gentle love you diffuse
The resonance of interconnection and the harmonies we produce 
adopting eyes who gaze and the choreography of seduction
Singing sonnets to me through a medium of favorable conduction
I love how that skin takes me and separates me from that great outside.
How your body protects me and Leaves all tangibility aside.
Against darkness we assimilate a state of translucency
so I'm letting your love shine brightly through me.
We outweigh the brilliance of the moon
putting worlds to sleep as your soul sings its tune.
