to find the empty space

kneeled down all the place

sit down and you pray

to your make believe day



and still patient...

and still giving blood...to you

still giving to familiarity...between us two

my heart was made to hold you

 

to follow the parable

knocked down it's unbearable

sit down and next to you

to your make belive truth



and still patient...

still drawing blood...from me

still giving to familiarity...make belive

your heart was to hold me



my heart was made to...

hold you...

see you...

breathe you...

feel you...

sit down and next to you

to have and to hold you

to make your truth come true



darkness ascends

count to ten

get knocked down

and get up again

darkness ascends

teeth to the grin

get pushed down

and push up again



and still patient

and still giving blood 

still giving blood

still giving blood...to you

still giving to familiarity...between us two

my heart was made to hold you



and still patient

and still drawing blood

still drawing blood

still drawing blood...from me

still giving to familiarity...make belive

your heart was made to hold me

your heart ws made to hold me

your heart was made to hold me

my heart was made to hold you



to hold and to help you...

patient and impossible/ patient and indescribable
