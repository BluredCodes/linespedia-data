title: "malignant edema"
writer: "theflawsoffate"

you always make this so complicated

but, is what's right always best for us?

we talk and breathe next to us each other

always seems to happen alot, but only sometimes

put the blanket beneath for some sense of warmth

pour the water for a cup fufilled

no more light can shine through the hole

it's too dark, but we really don't see it

we conversate about suicide and our other memorablilia

but, i forget a few minutes later

my hands gain comfort in one unbroken circle

you forget how to breathe, so i choose to help you

i bury you with bankets of dirt

then, i leave us alone together

your nerves flinch over and over,

thus sending you spiraling into the glitch of infinity....



an eternity of unparallel complcations discollided....
