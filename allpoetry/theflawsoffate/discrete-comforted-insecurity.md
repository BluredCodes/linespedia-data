title: "discrete comforted insecurity"

left this place, left here

left the blood on the pavement

watched the doll decay

ran away, far away

conquered the complicated

tear away at my scars

away at my crown

i swear, i'm leaving here



gone...without a trace

gone



distinct and  distant

far from being perfect

how could i believe it-- you

leaning away in this hole

the hole you built for me

watched the decomposition

as you tore the skin away



gone...left this place

gone




inconsideration has a tedency to burrow into lives of unexpectancy.
