dead and floating
your grain is my hole
shallow and unforgiving
you're eating me whole

disdained...
hold my arm
walk with me through...
blood and tears saturated in me...
complicated

judging and oblivious
drive the ashes in my house
forgetting my name
should I take your grade?

disguised...
who are you?
walk with me through...
sweat and piss saturated in me...
neverending

breathing...
holded...
secluded...
