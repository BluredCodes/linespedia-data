---
title: "plethora"
writer: "theflawsoffate"

wake up,
understand persistence
on and on,
i'll never win
i don't think it'll matter anymore,
once it's gone,
it'll never come back.

carried it away, 
then i lost it in the fog.
it's not my fault,
but it's my blame

...to you i'm nothing
...to you i don't even have a name

i wake up,
dazed, confused
trying to understand what happened
i'll never win
i don't think it matters anymore
you left me
you'll never come back

carried it away,
then i lost it in the fog
i may smile,
but it's not the same

...to you i'm nothing
...to you i don't even have a name
