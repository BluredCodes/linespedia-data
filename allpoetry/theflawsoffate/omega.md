burrough a hole 
further
into the pill
it always made you feel good?
didn't it?
didn't it?

when the times
are going bad
you wanted it to be granted
just to give you the life you never had?
didn't you?
didn't you?

the nervousness
it eats and
it swallows you
it helps not to believe the truth?
doesn't it?
doesn't it?

you try to quit
but you never could 
seem to fight
what you never thought was right?
didn't quite
didn't quite

(a pill to make you happy)
(a pill to make you happy)
(a pill to make you happy)

now doesn't that make you feel better?
you're overdosed and feeling fine
a pill for you to be happy
and to keep it all in line
living a life with never feeling
and always wanting to die
this is something you don't belive
but the life you stole was mine

(but that doesn't matter anymore)
(but that doesn't matter anymore)
(but that doesn't matter anymore)
(I'm not happy and you're still sore)

...again
