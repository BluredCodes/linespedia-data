take all you can to soothe me
hold me, calm me
everything is messed up
take all you can
i'm suffocating...
take all you can to soothe me
make me feel better
i'm comfortable here
but, take all you can
relax me, help me
make me better
take all my imperfections
to help me
i stumble on stumbling
take all that can comfort me
make me feel better
i'm dead inside
but take all you can to soothe me,
hold me, calm me
i'm feeling intolerable
uncomfortable
but, wrench all you can
relax me, help me,
free me
free me from the emptiness
be the better person
take my heart
make me feel better
steal me away
away from my slumber
steal my cynnicallity
steal everything
take all you can to soothe me,
hold me, calm me, 
to float away in the air
to love me
to love me forever
feed on me...forever
it sucked me down

take all you can to soothe me,
hold me, calm me, 
relax me, help me, 
to float away in the air, 
to love me,
make me feel better,
to dissapoint me....

the hole in which i crawled from 
has no more room for me
i demand hostile takeover
into the void of uncertainty

....so take all you can...
...all you want...
...all you need...
...all it takes...
