The steak was as tough as leather,
The chicken smelled like a zoo
The potatoes were a day of bad weather,
The veggies tasted like shoe.
Dessert had the texture of highway,
The milk was a tall glass of foam
If I could have had things my way,
I'd have eaten my meals at home!LOL...just a little thing. You like?
