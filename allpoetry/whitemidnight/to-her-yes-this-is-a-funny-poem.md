I have so many "pet" names for her it isn't even funny
And when I call her "sweetie" she hops to me like a bunny
She loves to go out with me, we do so every night
When I scare her she "barks" at me, but doesn't run in fright
She loves to go to bed with me, or go out on a jog
I love her more than anything cause she's my puppy dog!
