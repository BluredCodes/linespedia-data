Our daughter is all grown now,
And we've had some funny times
Like the day she went to the basement
And drank 2 bottles of wine

My wife and I, one time
Had an arguement over something funny
She wanted to use disposable diapers, I reusable
Hell, it'd save us money

I once used cloth, my wife walked in
I had to think, and fast
I turned our daughter over, took the pin,
And shoved it up her ass.

I remember the time I went to buy
Baby powder and oil
I left the house and forgot I'd left
Rubber nipples on the boil.

Now she's grown and on her own,
Married and due next month
Yay, now I have another kid
With whom I can have fun!For AA, once again...
