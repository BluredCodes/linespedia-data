I needed a haircut - I'd try a new guy, maybe
The barber talked real funny - he sounded like a baby
"How would you like it cut," he asked, "spiked or maybe flat?"
I told him spiked sounded great and that I would take just that
The barber lisped in conversation, he sounded very weird
When he was done doing my hair, I told him to trim my beard
His arm 'slipped' while doing it, my eyes began to tear
The barber was Mike Tyson and he'd cut off my ear!Hmm?
