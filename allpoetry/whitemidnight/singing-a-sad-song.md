A good life's been wasted
A fine soul left out to dry
Get me out of this hell hole
Before I have to die

I'm sitting here alone
Biding my time
Bored to the bone
Writing a rhyme

While my heart gently weeps
I attempt to stay
Strong, like a rock
Day after day

Was it not just yesterday
I seemed to let it be?
Now the skies are gray
I hate being me

The green grass of home
Is slightly turning brown
That's life, I used to say,
But that philosophy has drowned

I feel so unforgiven,
So forgotten, so downtrod
Sometimes I want to die
And meet face to face with God

When to win is to lose,
And to lose is to live...
Lose.
And live.Notice the few song implications...bored to the bone (bad to the bone), while my heart gently weeps (while my guitar gently weeps), like a rock, yesterday, let it be, green grass of home, that's life, unforgiven,
