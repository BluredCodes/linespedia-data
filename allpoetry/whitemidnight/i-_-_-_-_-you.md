title: "I _ _ _ _ you"

1) I could not believe it when I met you - you were so
2) Beautiful and smart. Most like you are very
3) Stupid, arrogant, dull, lame - any way you look at it,
4) You weren't. I figured I'd tell you that
5) I hated you, but this was not the case - I
6) Love you, more and more each day. I do not
7) DESPISE you, just like everyone else. You are
8) Too good for me though, because I am
9) An asshole, a stuck up ugly asshole. I could never
10) Believe you would ever look at me, but one thing I'm sure of is that I
11) Want you. It makes me sick to even think that
12) You would love me. I'm so happy just because
13) I am in the same room as you.
