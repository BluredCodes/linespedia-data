I didn't gain any weight last night,
So why are my pants getting tight?
I think, although, I may be wrong,
That it's due to someone's thong
When they wear their pants real low,
My you-know-what does some to grow
Although seeing some makes me wanna hurl,
A whole lot don't (like deviousgirl's) :)
And sometimes when I cant see
I imagine what it'd look like to meHope this wasnt offending
