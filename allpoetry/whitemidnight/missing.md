I'm a big guy, 280 pounds to be exact
When my sister was missing my mind went whack
I was playing in the street, with her as the ball
She was tossed a bit too hard and we both hit the wall
That knocked the wind outta me, and seeing as I'm fat
I began to dream of having a drink - so I did just that.
I went inside with my friend and we sat down
Suddenly - my sis was gone - we began to look around
I began to panic, but then I said real fast,
"No need to worry, something's stuck up my ass..."for those of you with no sense of humor, he sat on her...
