Her again? I thought she was gone
But I guess my love just goes on
I thought she was out of my mind

I guess she'll be staying for a while
I keep thinking about her beautiful smile
I've had a close encounter of the love kind

I thought I'd gotten over her
Things would be back to the way the were
And everything would turn out to be fine

Well, I guess that I was wrong
I'll like her again before too long
I've had a close encounter of the love kindThese were actually lyrics to a song i once wrote...
