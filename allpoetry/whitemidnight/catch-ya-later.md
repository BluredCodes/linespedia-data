I looked at you adoringly,
Gazing into your beautiful eyes
I so wanted to tell you I loved you,
But the words escaped me.
You fell asleep in my arms that night,
My hands around you as if you were a plush animal.

And then you farted.

See ya later, babe.I dunno...just wanted to get something down. It doesn't rhyme and I actually got the idea from a stand up comedy routine. But whatever.
