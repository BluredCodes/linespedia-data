With apologies to the Beatles...

-------

﻿Let me tell you how it will be
There's one for you, thousands for me
'Cause I'm bin Ladin, yeah, I'm bin Ladin

Should three plane crashes be too small
Be thankful I don't hit your malls
'Cause I'm bin Ladin, yeah I'm bin Ladin

If you handle mail, anthrax you’ll meet,
If you try run, your ass I’ll beat.
I have henchmen to take the heat,
I’ll bomb the ground under your feet.
Bin Ladin

'Cause I'm bin Ladin, yeah, I'm bin Ladin

Don't ask me what I do it for
(ah,ah CNN)
If you don't want to suffer more
(ah,ah Mr. Bush)
'Cause I'm bin Ladin, yeah, I'm bin Ladin

Now my advice for those who die
Don’t be a baby, just don’t cry
'Cause I'm bin Ladin, yeah, I'm bin Ladin
And you're dying for no one but me.Lol
