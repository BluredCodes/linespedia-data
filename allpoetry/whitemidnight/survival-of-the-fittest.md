Having nothing else to eat, this lifeless form of meat
Almost looked good. But the closer it got to my mouth, the more
My stomach churned. The way I saw it, this was a
Battle - survival of the fittest - eat it or get eaten by it. Pushing it
Under the table was not a possibility - someone would surely see me.
Ready to hold my breath and eat the disgusting, formless
Gray lump, I picked the patty up and stared in disgust.
En route to my mouth, I caught a whiff of the stench. No matter how
Ready I thought I was, it had defeated me. Cow - 1, Man - 0
