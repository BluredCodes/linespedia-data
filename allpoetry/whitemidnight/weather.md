Winds, tornadoes, hurricanes
Thunderstorms, torrential rains
Yes, I know they are a pain
But they are all weather

Some may, indeed, kill many,
Others' damage may be plenty
Gentle enough, some may be,
To pluck off a chicken's feathers

So next time you go outside
And get a raindrop in your eye
Just be sure that you don't cry
Because it's all the weatherThis isn't real funny but it was an assignment.
