Tonight is a time to start anew
A new leaf for the year two-thousand and two
Expressing the joy around the world
Is different for many boys and girls
In Spanish-speaking countries, I tohught you should know
The people don't say Happy New Year, they say  Feliz Año Nuevo.
In Italy, the European country located near Greece,
They celebrate the new year by saying  Nuovo Anno Felice.
For me, I'm in the USA, and more often than not,
I find myself singing...Should auld acquaintence be forgot...Not as much research went into this as you think...;)

Happy New Year to All!
