My body feels like a hurricane,
My head aches like a bitch
But my mouth is numb - thanks novacaine
You will not see a twitch.

It started, you see, a few months prior
To my dental cleaning
I was playing "Chicken*", my friend was a liar,
I could feel him leaning.

I was almost victorious
I almost knocked the guy down
It would have been glorious
But my friend was a clown

He bent back a little bit
I fell and hit my mouth
It was quite a large hit -
All I could say was "Ouch".

They rushed me to the doctor
My teeth they had to pull
As for my "friend" - what a shocker -
I suffocated him with wool

The next three months I ached all around
I had a horrible migraine
But my mouth was on the ground
Thanks Novacaine!AA, for you again. Got a little off subject.

* - Chicken is a game on which one person stands on another person's shoulders and fights against someone else doing the same.
