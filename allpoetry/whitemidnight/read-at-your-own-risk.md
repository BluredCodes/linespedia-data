Hello, agent 000, this is Dick Cheney speaking
My torso's getting cramped up and my legs are weak'ning
If this goes on any longer, I'll be in the creamatorium
Oh wait - I forgot! - my battery is nickel-cadmium!
I can stick my plug into the wall and get recharged like so
As long as I don't let the American people know
Just don't use the electric, or else I'm fucked
Because then this vice president will self-destruct...
