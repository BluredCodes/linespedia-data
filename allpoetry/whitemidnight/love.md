Sitting
h e r e
l a t e 
nights
S  a  d
o v e r
m    y
latest
strife
I need
Y o u 
                                  Now m o r e  t h a n  e v e r
                                 I guess that you will never



Know
just how
much I need
you, otherwise
I’ll still be b l u e
You are         the one
and only       for me
I  t h i n k  by now
You should see
That I’m



S a d  a n d       lonely
O h,  h o w      t r u e
I  j u s t  s i t  a n d
W a i t  f o r  y o u
Countless hours
Endless nights
I believe in
Love at 
f i r s t
sight



Never have I felt as dismayed
As I did  o n  that fateful d a y
As I asked you  o u t,              
There was just  n o  doubt       
That you would  s a y  n o       
And you wouldn’t go               
You refused, nothing I could do
