There weren't any bathrooms on I-95
I had to piss so badly that I wanted to cry
Holding it didn't help when it began to rain
Even listening to the radio didn't ease my pain
I was going crazy, so I found the tree that was greenest
I went behind it, I unzipped and pulled out my penis
Suddenly I saw the cops, they were coming over
They locked me up away in jail for public exposure.I haven't written in a while and I felt pretty badly about it. This isnt that great and I wrote it in school while holding it in in math class. Once again, I apologize for any pain this poem has caused you do to its sucktitude.
