You can boil, you can fry, serving that cake and some pie.
Fill that cone with ice cream, diggin' the Diner Queen.

Friday night and the lights are low
People looking for dinner to go
Where they serve some good food, treat you like a king,
But these people eat anything
You could give them day-old pie
Raw burgers with cold french fries
With a bit of ketchup, everything is fine
You're in a mood, take a chance
Eat so much you unzip your pants...

Chorus
You are the Diner Queen, young and sweet, only seventeen
Diner queen, 3 egg omelette with a side of greens
You can boil, you can fry, serving that cake and some pie.
Slice that meat,cut it lean, diggin' the Diner Queen.

Go to the oven, you turn it on
Leave it burning and then you're gone
Cooking up yet another, anything will do
You're in a mood, take a chance
Eat so much you unzip your pants...

ChorusEh. Not my best.
