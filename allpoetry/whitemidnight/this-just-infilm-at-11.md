I stayed up hours last night, making a nice long list
I looked over it again, making sure that nothing was missed
My parents took me to the mall, where I saw Mr. Claus
When he saw the chain on my neck, he began to pause
"Boy," he said "I have to tell you something sad but true,
You aren't getting presents from me because you're a Jew"How stupid and corny was that...
