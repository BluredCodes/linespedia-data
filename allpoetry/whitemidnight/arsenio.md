To the tune of "Video Killed the Radio Star"...

________________________________________________

I used to see cheers on the Arsenio Hall Show
I used to think to myself "This show really blows".  
Even my letters couldn't stop your dumb old show though. 
Oh-a oh 


VH1 took you for another God-damned ride. 
When I saw the show, I wasn't at all suprised, 
and now I'm scared to see your show in TV Guide. 
Oh-a oh 


Wow, you convinced them? 

Oh-a oh

What did you tell them? 


Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 
Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 


Your show stinks, just like my farts. 
Oh-a-a-a oh 


Didn't you used to have a highly-rated show? 
I doesn't seem to me like it was long ago. 
And how that ever happened, I still do not know. 
Oh-a oh 


Your first show was a flub. 
Oh-a oh 


This one's also dumb. 


Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 
Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 


Music with Arsenio Hall? 
VH1 has gone too far.  


Oh-a-aho oh, 
Oh-a-aho oh 


Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 
Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 


Music with Arsenio Hall? 
VH1 has gone too far. 


I'd rather play with canned yams
Than watch Arsenio Jams. 


VH1 went too far.
VH1 went too far.


Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 
Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 


Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 
Arsenio killed VideoHits1. 


Arsenio killed VideoHits1.  
(VH1 went too far.)I was watching Arsenio Jams when I thought of how bad it is. In an ironic twist...Video Killed the Radio Star is the first song ever played on mTV, which is VH1's biggest competitor.
