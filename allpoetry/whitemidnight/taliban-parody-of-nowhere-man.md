With apologies to the Beatles...

------

﻿They are called the Taliban,
Causing havoc where they can,
Nobody quite understands
just why.

They’ve a screwed up point of view,
Know just what they’re gonna do,
Gonna scare the sh*t from you and me
Taliban, please listen,
You’re gonna have lives missin’,
Taliban, the world ain’t at your command.

They’re as blind as they can be,
Just see what they want to see,
Taliban, just heed the Prez’s call
Taliban, please listen,
You’re gonna have lives missin’,
Taliban, the world ain’t at your command.

They’ve a screwed up point of view,
Know just what they’re gonna do,
Gonna scare the sh*t from you and me
Taliban should worry,
Just wait, we won’t hurry,
Leave it all cuz no one else
will lend a hand.

They are called the Taliban,
Causing havoc where they can,
Nobody quite understands
just why.
