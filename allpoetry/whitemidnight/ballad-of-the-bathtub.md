Could this day get any better?
I am wet, you are wetter
Sitting together in the bath
Telling jokes, enjoying a laugh
I pull my lips close to you
I see you do it too
We're getting closer, we're almost there
Nothing can stop me, I haven't a care
Suddenly I stop short
I grab my shirt, put on my shorts
I'm running, and I oughta
