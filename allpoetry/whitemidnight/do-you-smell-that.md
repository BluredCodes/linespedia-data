What's that feeling in my tummy?
Did it come from the beans that were so yummy?
I hate to say it but I must confess
I went and took a pill - Gas-X

Supposedly it stops the gas
From getting into one great mass
The gurgles get louder - I am doomed
I have to run to the bathroom.

...

Here I sit, broken hearted
I came to shit, I only farted.LOL...I'm in a funny mood if you cant tell from last three poems.
