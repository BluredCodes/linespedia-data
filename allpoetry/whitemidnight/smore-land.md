I couldn't believe my eyes - flowing streams of milk, chunks of chocolate falling from the heavens like hail, and graham craker boats. On the other side of the stream of 2 percent milk was a giant fluffy marshamellow. I hopped on a graham cracker and floated to the other side and began to gnaw away at the marshamellow. 

Suddenly I woke up - it was only a dream.

But if it was a dream...why is my pillow gone?:)
