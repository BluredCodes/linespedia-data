I watched you descending down
The stairs of the school in our town
This was the first my eyes had seen
Of you, so beautiful and lean
As tou went down these stairs
I was free - no worries, no cares
Like a hawk, I looked upon
Your great presence - but then you were gone
If I could, I'd take you to my house,
We have stairs, and I can see down your blouse.
