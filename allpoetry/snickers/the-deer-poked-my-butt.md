---
title: "The deer poked my butt"
writer: "snickers"
slug: "the-deer-poked-my-butt"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12925-The-deer-poked-my-butt-by-snickers"

The deer poked me in the butt,
I wonder if he hates my gutts?
I hate his gutts,
He poked me in the butt.

Nicole (7 yrs. old )
