title: "The cat sat in the tree"

The cat sat in the tree,
and said meow to me.
I climbed the tree,
and killed the bee before it stung me.
I took the cat down,
to the grown.
I took the cat into the house,
to let it catch a mouse.

Nicole (7 yrs. old)
