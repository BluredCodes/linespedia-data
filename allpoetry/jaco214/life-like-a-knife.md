title: "Life Like a Knife"

Your power is so great
and you show no hate
and you even love me
when I turn my back on thee
you are what gets me through
because you want me to be with you

when I feel your presence
nothing matters at all
your presence it comforts me
body, mind, and all

I know I'm not deserving of this
but when I find you, I will come to you with a Holy kiss
use my life
like a knife
to spread butter
on a slice of bread
meaning to spread your truth
and be living proof

Of your unfailing love
your great power
and your sacrifice
