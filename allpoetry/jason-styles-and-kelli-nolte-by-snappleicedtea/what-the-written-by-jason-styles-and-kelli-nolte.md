How can someone dream, of the howling spectacular Crystalline?Eyes that stare like a bad nightmare into fate Unlike the pilgrim who is twisted by her own illusion   i am able to evade the shaft.How can some one know the monsters of their dreams, as themselves burnt crystalline?What truth is there in an illusion? what beauty in the path of the pilgrim?Can beauty really be a nightmare?  the cloak cast over the bottomless shaftNothing anymore my own illusion Fantastic narrow paths suspend me above the bottomless shaft.No more time to fly, no more time to dream.I cant believe that I am finally the pilgrim of this journey beyond the greatest nightmare,My devoted dragonfly lover, Crystalline.No one can dream of you my perfect crystalline.The engulfing shaft my shadows fear is mere illusion.There is no nightmare greater than that of the pilgram.This is a weird poem, my best friend spent a few days on it and we would like comments of any kind.
