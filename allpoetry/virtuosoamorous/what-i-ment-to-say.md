Life holds it for you, that path that we cannot see 

    From the moment that I breathed your air 

    we make a pack with life and this is what it says 

    "Forever in this day 

    There I will never stray 

    From the one that I hold 

    Never could this love go cold" 

    In the moment that you find that one 

    Knowing, no matter what you cannot look away 

    You know there is heaven in her eyes 

    Peace in there sys, and  

     a lasting love that Multiplies. 

    She tells you to take it slow, 

    you see all that is real and true and you know 

    no matter what you both will always be in love. 

    That is what I wanted to say instead of Hey!!
