Firefly GlowBy Richard Maxfield6-28-2000Setting on my porchAs brilliant orange sun setsDarkness filling paintbrush skyTwilight sun shine yetSmell of fresh cut grassNipping at my noseWarm gentle breezeWhisking through my toesPeaceful silence broken Knighting gale sing his songTwilight lousing its battleWill not be light for longIn the distant darkSingle firefly blinkTransports to a timeMind begins to thinkHappy days now pastWhen my children were smallThe hours spent in playLaughing having a ballChasing firefly’s twinkleDistant flicker glowBring them back to DaddySo proud for them to showEyes so dazzling to shameThousand starlight brightPut them in their jimmiesGive a kiss goodnightEver standing vigilantKeep them safe from harmWatching over my babiesTender heart with strong armBurning bright with in my mindLoving those childhood waysOf such sweet innocencesHow I miss those days
