But a Whisper from YouBy Richard Maxfield3-11-2000Come to me in the dayOr in the dark of nightI will pull you closeChase away your frightLet me touch your heartRemove all your painForever loving youI will be your swainI want to guard your heartUnder my strong wingRemove the stress of lifeHear you spirit singShall I led the steps In the dance of loveSouring ever higherThan the clouds aboveBut a whisper from youNo need to ever screamWe shall live a lifeOthers only dreamWe become as oneBodies’ souls entwinedLooking ever forwardNever gaze far behindShare your female graceBring love in to my soulSpend my life with youIs my only goalCopyright #RM99R4
