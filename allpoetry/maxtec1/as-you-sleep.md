As You SleepBy Richard Maxfield3-29-2000Laying next to you in the nightListening to each breath you takeFeeling the warmth of your body Eyes twitching, restless in sleepI think of what you may dreamFighting a battle only you knowTo rescue you from the dreamQuite all fears give you peaceLonging to be where you areAs dreams pass deeper in sleepI move in closer to cuddle a bitSmelling your hair next to meRhythm of hearts beat as oneMy breath my life my worldDo you feel my love so closeI want to wake you for a bitTaste a kiss, feel your touchI will be satisfied being closeBreaths taken at the same timeSliding into sleep thinking of usWhat will new dawns bringCopyright #RM99R4
