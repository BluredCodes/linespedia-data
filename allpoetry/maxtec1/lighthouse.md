LighthouseBy Richard Maxfield6/06/01Tattered sailors tornTossed to and froDistant harbor safeRest from cruel winds blowRiding heartless wavesSkirt the crashing rocksDiligent endless struggleTo port upon the docksThrough the misty fogFlickers beckons brightSaves the souls in perilFrom the darkest night
