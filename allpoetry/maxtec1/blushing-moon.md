Blushing MoonBy Richard Maxfield2-23-2001With the heat of a thousand firesEmotions burning hot with desireFevered passion clasping entangled heartsDrips of sweat roll off silken skinMoisten hair trapped in wispsBodies heaving in rhythmCries of passion’s ecstasyAs shadows dance by candlelightRippling sensations cascadingNerves standing on endScreaming in pleasure’s contentFulfilled over and over againThough born apartHearts beating as oneFlesh unitingSpirits keenLost in time and spaceLovers dance to a hypnotic beatThe moon blushesIn envyRM99R4
