title: "The Purple Canopy"

An explosion of purple rips along the top
   of the entire length of fence
A thick canopy of cascading flowers
A mass of Chinese chimes poised to ring
   in the slightest breeze.

Flowers bubble forth from that Jack Beanstalk vine
A purple froth from a strange chemistry experiment.
I wish I were outside with them.
   My nose itches and aches
To smell that grand perfume.

I cannot turn away, but behold the rapture
This tangled jungle spilling carelessly
   down the side of the fence
And then I see sitting down below, a small brown cat
Casually grooming himself;  unaware 
Of the crazy, giggling canopy overhead.written: --edited again Jan/5-/2001
