That keyboard dost beguile mine eye
Dazzling so, the finish glistens
Its length be grandeur, opened wide
All grace and clarity to listen.
Gentle touch brings sweeter tones
Or lustful, yearning melody
Love made whereon that touch be borne
Or passion's rage in full intensity.
To merge directly at the soul
The music, instrument; with me
What once was only part; now whole
We fly; so great that beauty be.
   Caress doth heavenly music make
   A song conceived in loving's sake.
