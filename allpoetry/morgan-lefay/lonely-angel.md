I am above youA constant specter watchingYearning for your lifeI have known you longThough never have we spokenI am afraid to What if you hate meYour guardien angel trueAnd you wish me goneWhat do I care forI, a being of HeavenOf God's most high lairI am lonely here Though I am one of manyI long to be youFriends you have in fullAnd many love you trulyYour world not emptyThis my hopeless wishTo be known as friend to youFor eterinityany suggestions please
