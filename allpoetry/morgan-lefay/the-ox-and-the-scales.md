Mighty and strong is the Ox
Though patient and bright he may be
The Scales are of justice and truth
And they demand all for to see

Justice and patience combined
How will I ever add up
To a path that I cannot walk
And a future that I cannot stop

So I am told to be quietly gentle
And not utter the things that I know
But I am not patient and trusting
For I have my own path to goany suggestions please
