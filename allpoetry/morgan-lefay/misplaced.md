Celtic bred, yet bound am IReading, finding all before meIn words of old, I’ve learned to skry. Still I live in this today,Trapped and kept from my own world,Ever beguiled by Magics lost in yesterday.Neither have I missed the allure of nowLoving and learning the culture to which I am bornSo, to you now I say goodbye and so make my final bow.this was for an assignment in geography any suggestions on how to make it flow better
