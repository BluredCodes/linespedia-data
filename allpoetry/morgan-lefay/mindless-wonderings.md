Why is it, I wonderWe close off our own thoughts?Instead we take those of the wholeAnd make our truths of lies.Perhaps we do not wantTo ponder what is trueTo see with our own eyesThe world we think is ours.But then, why should we careFor answers we don't understandTo questions that we never askedAnd mysteries we can't recall?So then it is no wonderThat we've fallen far below ourselves,Or given up our want of TruthAnd accepted lives of deception.any suggestions please
