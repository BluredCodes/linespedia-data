I sit here in the dark
Writing
My fears 
My wants 
My dreams
All of it
None of it 
Some of it
True
I don't really know anymore
I don't care
So I search for all those questions
To the answers everyone has given me
And I cry my eyes out every night
Because some where my soul is dead
It won't come back to me
No matter how I coax it
With the words I use
To pin my fairies
Thought fairies
Down amidst my troubled dreams
For deeper examination
Like a child calling for its mother
Or its cat
I call my soul with fleeting words
