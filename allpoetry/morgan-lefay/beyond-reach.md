title: "Beyond Reach"

What world is there
Beyond my sight
That hides in webs of spider's silk
And dances with a lilting step
Upon the autumn leaves
How slight the want of this world's kiss
When that is far from me
Why languish here amidst my doubt
Why ponder truth at all
Rather would I sail away
On waters deep as death
Or lay my head upon the earth
To conjure sweet and willing dream
And flit my mind through fairy place
That waits beyond my finger's reach
