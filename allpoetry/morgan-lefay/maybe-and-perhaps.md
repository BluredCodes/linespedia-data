---
title: "Maybe and Perhaps"
writer: "Morgan LeFay"
slug: "maybe-and-perhaps"
source: "AllPoetry"

A flower is just a flower
A tree is just a tree
And so perhaps in turn
A dream is just a dream

That's what they've always told us
That's what they want us to believe
But maybe, just maybe
That's not completely true

What if that flower is magic
What if that tree is home to elves
And just as well and truly
What if that dream can be made realany suggestions please
