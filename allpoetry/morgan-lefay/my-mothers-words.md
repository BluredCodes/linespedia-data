title: "My Mother's Words"
writer: "Morgan LeFay"

I love you
Words so simply put
But welcome

Day was hard
Work was such a bore
Now I'm home

Hugs and kiss
Give me back my strength
Touch my heartthis is about both the love a mother gives to a child and the love a child gives to a mother
