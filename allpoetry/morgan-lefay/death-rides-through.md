---
title: "Death Rides Through"
writer: "Morgan LeFay"
slug: "death-rides-through"

On a pale horse
On a dark stead
For men who are humble
For men full of greed
Through meadows and forests
Through gardens in seed
On a pale horse
On a dark stead
She'll come for the young
She'll come for the old
Heeding not wisdom
Heeding not gold
Taking the meak
Taking the bold
She'll come for the young
She'll come for the old
Some will know darkness
Some will know light
Many won't struggle
Many will fight
They will go blindly
They will have sight
Some will know darkness
Some will know light

On a pale horse
On a dark stead
