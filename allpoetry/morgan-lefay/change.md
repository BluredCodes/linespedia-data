title: "Change"
writer: "Morgan LeFay"

I shut my eyes... 
I say a prayer... 
I open them and see, 
That right before me 
Sits a thing 
That's glorious to be. 
I stretch my hands... 
I pick it up... 
And lay it in my palm. 
It winks at me, 
I turn it 'bout 
To find in its great depths 
A chance to know... 
A chance to see... 
A chance to be aught else. 
For in my hand 
that grain of sand 
Lies power, deep and true. 
It beckons me... 
To search, to find 
A new and dangerous path 
I raise it up... 
I stare deep to it... 
And crumple to the ground. 
The change is done and all is well, 
But, who will I be now?any suggestions please
