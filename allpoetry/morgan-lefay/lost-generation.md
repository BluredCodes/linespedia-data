What do we know 
That are not told
All that glitters is not Gold

Are we so lost
Within the sea
None were ever found as we

What are the answers
To our fate
We that were born far to lateThe title of this comes from the "Lost Generation" after the first world war which included such writers as Fitzgerald and Hemingway i guess the feelings must have slipped into my subconscious or something from english class
