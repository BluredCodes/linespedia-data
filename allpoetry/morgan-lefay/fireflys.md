once in a while
it's there
and then it's gone
again
it teases with words
whispered in ears
and some left better
unsaid
always it beckons
and never it comes
when you ask
now it flys back
and now it is gone
leaving its darkness
behind
to wait for its safe
returnOh how fickle is that sweet muse, Caliope.
