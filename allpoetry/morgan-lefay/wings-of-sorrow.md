Stretching and soaring, 
I find you 
There above my life 
A constant thing 
Though paying little 'tension 

Higher go I 
To ask you 
Simple things that be 
What is life? 
I ask you humbly 

But up you go 
Till stripped bare am I 
You stare at me 
And brush me off 
As though I mattered not 

What thing 
I wonder 
Did i do 
To cause you 
Bitter stress 

You turn and say 
'You came here 
You followed me on high 
You dare to ask the questions 
And yet you still deny' 

'The wars you fight 
The things you kill 
Are no less to me 
Than any one of your own 
So just leave me be.' 

I sped down then 
Back to where this is 
And cradled in my heart 
The truth, the bitter truth 
Of Eternal Death 

Our god has turned 
Away from us 
And we don't give a damn 
We carry on 
Just as though he cared 

So I shed no tears 
For my race 
But saved them for our Lord
