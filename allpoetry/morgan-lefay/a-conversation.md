We stand here on the brink 
Alone and yet together 
Trying to reach each other 

I, the deaf, make motions, 
While you, the blind, are speaking 
And so we cannot understand the other's words 

Then suddenly you smile, 
I smile in return. 
We speak volumes to each other. 

Still, I cannot hear, 
Still, you cannot see, 
But we feel each other calling. 

Our hearts are in communion, 
Our souls alive with chatter, 
And then we understand. 

Now we stand together 
Not lost in our own hurts
