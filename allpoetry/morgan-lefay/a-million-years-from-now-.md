A million years from now
this world will be forgotten
The hopes and dreams of billions
Will have scattered with the wind
A million years from now

A million years from now
This world will be of metal
Our petty little errors
Will crush this planet's hope
A million years from now

A million years from now
Our children's children's children
Will curse our killing legacy
And hate us for their deepest sins
A million years from now

A million years from now 
It's not that long to wait
We can't afford to lose it here
And kill our planet's future
