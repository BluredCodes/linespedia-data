When I was but a lass 
The bards would strike a chord 
And tell the tale of Camelot 
And Arthur her marvelous King and Lord 

                                      They'd sing of swords and stones 
                                      And knights and ladies too 
                                      But now I've grown to understand 
                                      So I'll tell the story true 

Arthur loved his country much 
But in truth he was no god 
He made mistakes aplenty 
Even acted like a sod 

                                      Oh yeah then there's Guenevere 
                                      England's Queen and Lady 
                                      But children weren't her strong point 
                                      And her dealings with Lance were  
                                          kinda shady
Of course that leads to Lancelot 
That most glorious shining knight 
He cheated with his best friend's wife 
And started up another fight 

                                      Merlin can't escape this either 
                                      Not for his dastard role 
                                      For baby snatching was his trade 
                                      And twas little Lord Arthur he stole 

But what of that witch, that Morgan 
You ask with bated breath 
Well I think they got her story wrong 
Twasn't she that caused her brother's death 

                                      So now you see what I mean 
                                      About mistaken bards 
                                      Hey, they had to sing you something 
                                      While the cheated you at cards 

Oh, I'm sorry did I ruin it 
That magical, lovely tale 
Well what did you expect 
From those idiots who saught the Grail?any suggestions please
