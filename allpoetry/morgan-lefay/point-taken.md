Everyone has always told meI really talk to much But I think it's kinda funnyThat they would ever say such'Cause you see when they keep sayin'That I should keep my big mouth shutThey get to really blabbingJust like some angry muttSo I just sit politelyAnd wait for them to finishThen I nod my head a final timeAnd pass them all the spinach
