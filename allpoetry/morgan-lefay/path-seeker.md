Silence booms in my ears...
I am far from the place that I know
Before me are turnings
Behind me a cloth
Woven of strings I can't touch
I look at the paths 
Set before me with ease
Finding only a puzzlement there
What way will I go?
What string shall I add?
Will it be knotted or fare?any suggestions please
