When you can stand in a flame When you can be silent when tortured When you can die for your faith That is Courage When you can stand against all When you can see your loved ones' death When you can stare your condemner in the face That is Courage When you can love they that killed you When you can know your death unflinching When you can tell the truth always That is Courage When you can stand up for what's right When you can live for life's sake When you can die for a friend That is Courage Many have tried and many have failed Still more have died for their cause All that survive give this advice: Never give up, and never forgetany suggestions please
