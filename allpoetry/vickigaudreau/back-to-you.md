Back To You

You helped me find myself inside myself
It was so hard to do
With your caring, loving guidance
I have made it through

I was scared, you held my hand
You helped me trust in you
I was scared, it hurt so much
But you had been there, too

You eased me in & out again
I learned that pain can heal
Behind the pain comes tears of joy
That bring me back to you

You helped me find myself inside myself
I felt so all alone
You showed me I had inner strength
You were always there

You helped me find
The child which has no fear
Who feels the pain but can thus receive the joy
You brought me back to you

Back to you, Lord, you brought me back to you
Back to you, Lord, you brought me back to you
Now I know
Love complete
‘Cause you brought me back to you

Thank you for bringing me back to me
I searched not knowing what I’d find
You helped me find myself inside myself
Which brought me back to you
So, Lord, I sing for you

Back to you, Lord, you brought me back to you
Back to you, Lord, you brought me back to you
Now I know
Love complete
‘Cause you brought me back to you
