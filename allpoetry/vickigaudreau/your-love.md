Your Love

Thank you, Lord, for finding me
A way out of all the misery
You showed me love
And taught me how to feel
Now I am free
I know your love that heals

I have my dreams, You gave them life
I need only follow your guiding Light
To let go of ego, Become as a child
To know the joy of Your love

Chorus

Lord, what more can I ask of you
You've shown me more than I ever knew
You gave me hope when I had nothing left
Now, dear Lord, I want to give to you

Oh, what have I but my heart & soul
Lord, they're Yours, please take control
You know the Way, so please guide my path
Thank you, Lord, I am free at last

Repeat Chorus & Fade
