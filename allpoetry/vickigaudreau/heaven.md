Heaven

Gasping
Can’t breathe
Hot chills
Feel the passion 
Heat

Massaging wetness
Tasting skin
Never enough
Wanting
Needing
More

Tongue strokes
Hunger
Licking
Teasing
Liquid pearl

Deeper

Head spinning
No more deprivation
Straddled
All of you
Inside of me

Glistening bodies
Pulsing
Driving
Arching 
To the heavens

Moving to you
Floating
Exploding
Here I am
Hold on
Oh, god! Oh, god!
