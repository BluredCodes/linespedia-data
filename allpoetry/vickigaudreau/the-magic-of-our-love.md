The Magic of Our Love

When I hold you, I feel how much I love you
When you touch me, I melt right in your arms
‘Cause your touch is not just fingers on my flesh
You’ve touched my soul (My soul)
	
There are no words, you needn’t speak
The magic of our love embraces silence
I think of you ~ I feel your kiss
I see your face & it fills my heart
It’s not an illusion
It’s the magic of our love
