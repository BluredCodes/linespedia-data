I Did It Anyway

Baby, I knew better 
I went & did it just the same
I wasn’t quite so clever
Cause I thought I’d get away

Just because you moved me
Didn’t mean I had to fall in love
But now…

I think about you every morning
Before I open up my eyes
At night, before I go to sleep
You’re the last thing on my mind

Moans of complete surrender
Escape my lips with your sweet kiss
My heart aches with longing
For your touch, your love, I wish...

(bridge)
I can’t hide my love
Can’t keep it inside
I have to let you know

Yeah, Baby, I knew better
I went & did it just the same
I wasn’t quite so clever
No I didn’t get away

No longer can I tell my heart
What to feel or do
You reached through & touched my soul
My heart belongs to you
(Yeah, I knew better)
(But I did it anyway)

I knew better 
I went & did it just the same
I wasn’t quite so clever
Cause I thought I’d get away
Yeah, Baby, I knew better 
I went & did it just the same
I wasn’t quite so clever
Cause I didn’t get away

(tag)
You filled me with desire
Touched my heart with love
Held my hand, made me believe
In honesty & trust

Fade on
So I did it anyway
