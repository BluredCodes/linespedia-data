I Can’t Pretend

I thought it would be easy  ~ I’d smile & be on my way
You’d never know the difference 
We’d be friends  ~ That’d never change
But I see your face, I look in your eyes
I hear the words you used to speak
I tremble when you’re close to me 
And I wonder if you ever cared

Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
What I don’t say, I still feel
Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
See my eyes, they’re filled with tears
Baby, I can’t pretend
Not to love you cause I do
Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
Cause, Baby ~ I do

I thought it would be easy  
I’d pretend that I don’t care
We’d talk & laugh, Just like old times
Before you took my heart

But Baby, it’s not easy
Cause I’m grievin’ over you
Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
Cause, Baby, I still do

Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
What I don’t say, I still feel
Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
See my eyes, they’re filled with tears
Baby, I can’t pretend
Not to love you cause I do
Baby, I can’t pretend not to love you
Oh, Baby ~ I still do

Repeat Chorus
