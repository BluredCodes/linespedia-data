Sometimes Love

I have so many things to say to you
I don’t know where to start
I love you more than you could know
With a love that fills my heart

I think of you, I wonder
What you’re doing now
I look at pictures of you
And wonder where you are
(Holidays, your birthday)
(Questions fill my mind)

Sometimes love means sacrifice
Sometimes brings so much pain
Love means doing your heart’s best
Sometimes love’s all that remains

I loved you before I met you
Though you may wonder how
I wanted to take care of you
To protect & keep you mine

Sometimes love means sacrifice
Sometimes brings so much pain
Love means doing your heart’s best
Sometimes love’s all that remains

Sometimes love means sacrifice
Sometimes love brings pain
Love means doing your heart’s best
Sometimes love…

Means giving up a child

Fade on chorus
