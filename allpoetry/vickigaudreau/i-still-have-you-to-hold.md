I Still Have You (To Hold)

You’re gone, I can’t hold you now
But I talk to you, I hope you understand
There were so many things I didn’t say
But how was I to know

That years gone by
Though I’d wish & I’d cry
I wouldn’t have you to hold
I wish I had you to hold

Sometimes, I lie & wonder
Did you ever know
How much I truly loved you
Did I ever let it show

You gave me all your love
And asked for nothing in return
Only that I love you back
And let you hold me, hold me in your arms

Sometimes at night I think of you
While lying in my bed
And know that somehow you have heard
Everything I’ve said

All the years gone by
Though I’ve wished & I’ve cried
I still can’t have you to hold
But memories are forever
And I still have you to hold
Yes, memories are forever
I’ll always have you to hold
