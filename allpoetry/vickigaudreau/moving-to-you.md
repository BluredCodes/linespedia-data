Moving To You 

I can’t help but tremble 
When I feel your touch 
Baby, ‘cause I want you 
Oh, God, I need you so much 
 
I just think about you 
And I feel so weak 
Darlin’, you touch me and 
I melt with sweet, eager heat 
 
(Chorus) 
Moving to you, making love with you 
All night long (sustained) 
(Hold on, Baby, here I am) (spoken) 
Our passion’s going strong 
Moving to you, making love with you 
Almost more than I can bear 
(Here I am, hold on) (spoken) 
You know how to move me/Honey, you still take me there 

Like a thundering herd of horses 
My heart races with desire 
As you gently trace with your caress 
My arousal soaring higher 

Soaring with emotion 
Fulfilled in your embrace 
I cherish your devotion 
As you gently stroke my face 

We look into each other’s eyes 
With warm & tender love 
Angels are among us cause 
Our love comes straight from Heaven above 
 
(Chorus) 
Moving to you, making love with you 
All night long (sustained) 
(Hold on, Baby, here I am) (spoken) 
Our passion’s going strong 
Moving to you, making love with you 
Almost more than I can bear 
(Here I am, hold on) (spoken) 
You know how to move me/Honey, you still take me there 

No matter how long we’re together 
Our love will be like new 
Even if it’s til Forever 
I’ll always be wanting you
