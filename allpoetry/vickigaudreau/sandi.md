Sandi

We were friends in high school
We shared our secrets, hopes & dreams
You wanted love & a happy family
All I wanted was to sing

We’d hang out on the corner
We’d flirt & tease the boys at school
We weren’t bad, just having fun
God, we were so young 

We grew up & I got married
You moved to California
I left him ten months later 
You had a baby girl

Sandi, what went wrong 
Why’d we grow so far apart
Was it the drugs or your need for love
You thought you’d never find

Now I’m singing & you’re gone
What happened to your dreams

I didn’t get a chance to say goodbye
Sandi, you were so young
Sandi, why’d you take you’re life
Oh, Sandi, what went wrong
