Fantasy & Fairytales

I believe in fantasy & wishes on a star
Happy endings, fairytales
Unicorns & love
I’m enchanted with the world
And the magic of a dream
When I see life through a child’s eyes
I can do most anything

Fantasy & fairytales
A child’s view of life
Fantasy & fairytales
Make my dreams come true

The illumination of the moon
The wonder of a gentle breeze
The beauty of an eagle’s soaring flight
These are things I see

Things I see
That make me smile
That put the sparkle in my eyes
Fantasy & fairytales
Bring my dreams to life

Fantasy & fairytales
A child’s view of life
Fantasy & fairytales
Make my dreams come true 

Repeat & fade
