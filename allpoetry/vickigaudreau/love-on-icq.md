love on icq

we fell in love on icq
you random messaged me
and i answered you
we’d chat for hours on end
and sometimes made love, too
that’s how we fell in love on icq

i’m still not sure i understand
how love moved so fast
how a man & woman can fall in love
talking on the internet

i thought i must be crazy
this just can’t be real
using just a keyboard
to talk & touch & feel

but you touched my heart
and I fell for you
we found love together
thanks to icq

we fell in love on icq
you random messaged me
and i answered you
we’d chat for hours on end
and sometimes made love, too
that’s how we fell in love on icq

we still have some obstacles
we’ll have to overcome
but we’ll stand together
we don’t have to be alone

we have help from up above
our angels are nearby
to give us strength to hold our love
and it will only grow
now that…

we fell in love on icq
you random messaged me
and i answered you
we’d chat for hours on end
and sometimes made love, too
that’s how we fell in love on icq

we fell in love on icq
you random messaged me
and i answered you
we’ll chat for hours on end
and sometimes make love, too
now that we fell in love on icq

Repeat Chorus
Fade on

we fell in love on icq
that’s how
we fell in love on icq
