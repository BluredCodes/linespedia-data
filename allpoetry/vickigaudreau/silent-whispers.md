Silent Whispers

Treading softly
Cautious
Do we dare

Silent whispers
Gently
Stroking passion's fire

How do I not touch you
When I tremble with desire

Silent whispers
Softly
Caress our fantasies

Softly
Gently
Silent whispers speak

Where do we begin
What holds us back
(Cautious)
(Do we dare)

Who moves first
Why do we wait
Don't you think it's time
(Yes, it's time)
To quench the flame

Whispers softly silent
Silent whispers speak

Silent whispers softly
(Softly)
(Gently)
Silent whispers softly speak
