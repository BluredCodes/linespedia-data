Lost In The Music

Sometimes the pain
Is more than I can bear
Feels like I wanna scream
Just wanna rip out my hair

Gotta get away
Can’t take the pain
Gotta get away
Don’t wanna hurt no more

What do I do – Where do I go
How do I keep from losin’ control

I gotta get

Lost in the music
Become one with the music
I can dance – I can sing
Music takes away the pain

When it seems I can’t hold on
When I feel like I could die
When I think I can’t go on
When all I can do is cry

What do I do – Where do I go
How do I keep from losin’ control

That’s when I get…

Lost in the music
Become one with the music
I can dance – I can sing
I can do anything

When I get lost in the music...

It takes away the pain
