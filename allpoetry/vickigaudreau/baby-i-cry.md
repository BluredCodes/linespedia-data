Baby, I Cry

Baby, I cry
When I think of you
Baby, I cry
When I feel how much I love you
Baby, I cry
I am moved to tears
So, Baby, I cry

Darlin' I'm not playin' games
Do you know my love is real
I'm not gonna hurt your heart
You mean too much to me

I didn't plan to fall in love
But now I'll take the chance
I know I can't stake a claim
But I love you, I'll love you anyway

Baby, I cry
When I think of you
Baby, I cry
When I feel how much I love you
Baby, I cry
I am moved to tears
So, Baby, I cry

Yeah, I'm afraid it's gonna hurt
But that's the risk of love
And loving you is worth the price
I might have pay

No, I'm not a martyr
But I'll make the bed where I lie

'Cause. . . . .

Baby, I cry
When I think of you
Baby, I cry
When I feel how much I love you
Baby, I cry
I am moved to tears
So, Baby, I cry
