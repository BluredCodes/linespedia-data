Break The Cycle

How can a child feel safe
When she needs protection
And the one who’s protecting her
She needs protection from

How can a child believe
His dad & mommy really love him
When one of them is bruising him
And the other just looks on

We’ve got to break the cycle
Stop the hurting of our children
We can’t let our children
Be hurt anymore
We’ve got to break the cycle
Save our children from the pain
Show them that they’re dear to us
And let them feel our love

There are precious children crying
They’re crying from their souls
How can we not heed them
And show them how to love

How can a child know
That his daddy thinks he’s teaching
That he beats & makes him bleed
‘Cause his daddy taught him how

We’ve got to break the cycle
Stop the hurting of our children
We can’t let our children
Be hurt anymore
We’ve got to break the cycle
Save our children from the pain
Show them that they’re dear to us
And let them feel our love
