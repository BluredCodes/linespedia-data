Here I Go Again

Baby, you got my attention
Looks like you might be the one
That wasn’t my intention
‘Cause this was just for fun

I’m afraid of falling
‘Cause I don’t want to go alone
I might have run the other way
If only I had known

Here I go again
Yeah, I’m starting to fall
Thought I’d choose who, where & when
But I’ve got no say at all
Here I go again
Yeah, I’m starting to fall
And now my heart can’t explain
Why I’ve got no say at all

I figured I knew better
Than to fall in love again
No emotional involvement
Just for fun instead

I thought I was tough enough
To keep you on the other side
Looks like my heart called my bluff
My love can’t be denied

Yeah, here I go again
I’m starting to fall
Thought I’d choose who, where & when
But I’ve got no say at all
Here I go again
Yeah, I’m starting to fall
But my heart still can’t explain
Why I’ve got no say at all
Oooh, I’ve got no say at all

Repeat & fade on

Here I go again 
Falling, I’m starting to fall
