Two Hearts

One man, one woman
Angels brought us together
Two hearts, three words
And one night together

You don’t understand how I love you
But Baby, I see your heart
Love needs no explanation
It only takes two hearts

Baby, please don’t be afraid
Put your heart in mine
What we have is honest love 
The kind that Heaven made

It takes…
One man, one woman
Friends, as well as lovers
Two hearts, three words
And lots of nights together

I know that she hurt you bad
But Baby, I’m not her
What we have is special
There’s no taking that away

Love hurts but it feels good, too
And there’s nothing in the world
Like a woman’s true love
To fill that aching need

I want to be with you, Darling
Our passion is so strong
Our two hearts come together
And we become as one

It takes…
One man, one woman
Angels brought us together
Two hearts, three words
I love you…

Forever
