Prowling

I'm on the prowl
Stalking like a cat
Patiently seeking
Ready to spring
Gonna take what I want (You're what I want)
Gonna get what I need (I'll take what I need)

I pursue
Til I take my prey
Don't try to hide
You can't get away

You won't know what hit you
When I go for the "kill"
I'll play with you
Til I get my fill

I'm on the prowl
Stalking like a cat
Patiently seeking
Ready to spring
Gonna take what I want (You're what I want)
Gonna get what I need (I'll take what I need)

Repeat
