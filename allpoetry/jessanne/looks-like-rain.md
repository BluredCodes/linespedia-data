title: "Looks Like Rain"
writer: "Jessanne"

Another day has passed us by
With tears and hurt and anger
Over and over I will try
Not to point my finger

I question us and all we do
We do not seem to be matching
Still, I only have my thoughts of you
No others that I am chasing

You tell me that it looks like rain
But I'm not sure what to say
I don't want to hold onto this pain
But neither one of us walks away
