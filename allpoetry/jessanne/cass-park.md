Remember that time when we sat in the car?
In the dark with Cass Park not all that far?

That's when we talked about how it would 'be'.
Since then our lives changed permanently.

It hit me, just now, this all became apparent.
That we're still the same, though we're so different.


-Jessanne
Mar. 22, 2002
