Grip it

The pen with the comfort grip

Do you think it's really comfy when I type all day anyway?

Cramp it

My wrist tenses up again

Hello Hand! Come in if you're listening.. keyboard.

Cram it

I take a real good look at this comfort grip

Before I toss it out my squeaky front door.

Start it

I pause in front of the screen

And the first word that comes out is comfort.


I got it, I gripped it, it cramped me, I crammed it, then started over.
