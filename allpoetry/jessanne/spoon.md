This spoon is no other
It has its own qualities
If this spoon had a brother
It wouldn't be a fork

This spoon has a posture
That is firm, in a curve
The magic that it fosters
Helps you stir shit

Del Monte had no clue
And neither did Campell's
Dear spoon, without you
I'd never be able to get 
   the stuff out of the 
   bottom of the cat box

-Jessanne
Mar. 20th, 2002
