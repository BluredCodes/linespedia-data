title: "Gone"

Higher Presence watching over me
As I stand at the edge of eternity
I feel like jumping
Top of the building
End my misery

Feel them watching as I step toward the ledge
Toes protruding past the edge
Some try to stop me
Others just watch me
Confusion fills my mind

I feel like jumping even more
As thier pleads grow louder than before
Perhaps this won't do
But now I have to
I prepare myself

A thousand decisions fly through my head
Won't be long before I'm dead
Someone is calling
Oh God I'm falling
My misery is gone

-Jessanne
