Oh Kitty..
    I will pet your head and stroke your tail,
And someday I know your heart will fail,
But not to worry,
    I have a plan,
I'll turn you into Kitty Spam.

I'll put your paws into the blender,
And save your tail inside the freezer,
And one day when I'm feeling lonely,
I'll wear your tail,
    Just like a pony.
