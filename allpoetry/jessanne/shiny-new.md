Shiny New   ~Jessanne


I think you should just back away
Now, while you still can
Turn tail and run a different way
Don't look back again

You don't know what I'm capable of
I promise it'll hurt you
So convinced you will earn my love
All polished shiny new

I was never really one to share
You haven't changed that
So don't do the 'I'm so hurt' stare
I'll just turn my back

Why the hell are you still standing here
There's nothing left to do
All your expectations crumble I fear
Cause I was never shiny new


   ~Jessanne
