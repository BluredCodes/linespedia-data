The bulls horns pierce me one more time
Taking what was left inside
And if I ever want it back
I'll have to look you in the eyes

I've laid down and taken it all til now
Some how convinced it'll all work out
I've now gained the sense I lacked before
This time you'll be the one walking away sore


I curse the day I walked into
Your tangled life-like web
I've lost the faith I had in you
And everything you said


I can't believe you thought
I had no chance without you
If I'd had an ounce of sense
I would have walked away from you
.... sooner.. sooner than that...

The piece of mind that I have now
Came without your expertise
So don't try and ring me any more
And don't leave a message please
.... I'm not home.. I've got things to do ya know...


I curse the day I walked into
Your tangled life-like web
I've lost the faith I had in you
And everything you said

I lost all the faith I had in you
I've forgotten everything you said
