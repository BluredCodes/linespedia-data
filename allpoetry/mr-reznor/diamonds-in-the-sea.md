The light is burning in my eyes
And I can't see you
Your so very far from here

I sometimes feel that no one tries
To let me hear you
And your so very far away

And your so far from here
I hope your dreams will never set me free
And lately I've been trying to see
Your diamonds in the sea
Diamonds in the sea
Bring you back to me...

In front Im high, inside Im sad
Cus I can't feel you
Like your a million miles away

Im singing songs that no-ones heard
And Im so lonely
I wanna fly so far away

And your so far from here
I hope your dreams will never set me free
And lately I've been trying to see
Your diamonds in the sea
Diamonds in the sea
Bring you back to me...Song I wrote for a friend who misses his girlfriend whos in another country. Whats wierd is it applies to me too so mabie its not relly his...
