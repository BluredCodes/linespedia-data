My neon blue buddy,My can dispensing dear,Rolling your shots of caffeine so lovingly,Your loyalty's so clear.You stand alone all day,To give all what they need,Selling your children away,A call you have to heed.We plug you in.We plug you out.We stack you tins.You ask for nowt...So I'm dreading of the day,My best of friends so keen,When they take you far away,And replace you with a coffee machine!Sitting in the cafe in uni, I fell in love with a Pepsi machine :)
