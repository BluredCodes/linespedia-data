Look into a mirror and tell me what you see
Not really a picture, not really reality.
And your lonely position is very appealing to me
But you won't fill a thimble with all of your honesty.

Locked out of my window a black cat was staring at me
And hes probably pondering on things that my problems could be.
Cause theres holes in my soul since your memory wont set me free
Inside I've been wandering so lost in this turbulent sea.

Oh and i miss you
   But i know its wrong
And im aching to see you
   Girl its been so long

Ive been falling so much now I think I might hit the ground
And nothing is beautifull lately cause your not around.
I feel like a fool, a fool who in nonsense is bound
Cause im hearing your voice sing its song in every sound.

Oh and i want you
   But i know its wrong
And i wish you were here with me
   But i know that its wrong
Oh and i miss you
   But i know its wrong
And im aching to see you
   Girl its been so long

So long.Well, its fun to sing so im working on a good sound for it.
