Tied to my soul
Tight, almost whole
Surrendering in sweet
All past pretensions

Giving in to bliss
We merge with every kiss
Like gossamer we glide
Away all apprehensions

But then you tore yourself away
Like a needle from my vien
Who'd have thought you wouldn't stay
And hold me silent from my painYeah, umm first ina long time. Bit sore....
