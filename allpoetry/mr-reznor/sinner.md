sing your song of seven thoughtsthinking Jesus could be boughti hope you never fall below no, don't ever let your monsters showthinking that you'll never loseyour gonna give yourself the bluesoh and sure you could complainbut still, you're living pain to painme, I'm dreaming tales of yesterdaynow my fear has flown awayand as my thoughts are pressed in vaini think ill never feel the same againabout healing
