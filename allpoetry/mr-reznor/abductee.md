Watching.Eons pass outsideThe sterile glass facadeStars lose their beautyTaken from my homeBy bleaching blue unknownI float into deathWondering.Will I see againMy love, my life, my soulOr will I die firstShould I try escapeMy metal cold futureDeath could be my gun...Work in progress. About being abducted by aliens, not the most realistic of subject matter..
