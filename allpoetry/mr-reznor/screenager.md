Its 2 A.M and I'mSlouched here. Not thinking Straight in my Self inducedSquintingSquirmingStarvation.The neon screen sears my brain.My ears scream numb to the Noise that should be musicShould be music.       Should be....Awake, but asleepI feel filthyThere are holes in meBig gaping holesAnd I can see now The cancer causedIn my new age addictionMy lightbulb eyesScan the world At the press of a button.How alone I amIn this sea of millionsOf other lonely souls.We speak in binary slangAnd Stutter in codes of Mistype and Retype and Retype and rules and...Static buzzing,My self, my soulIs neonMultiplexing.Harsh at my finger tipsCold inorganic Pure powerI am reborn in this worldAm am a better person than I Could ever be.I am of the screen age.It really is 2 AM and my head hurts but I'm too bloody addicted to the net :(
