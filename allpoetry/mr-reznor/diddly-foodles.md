Peter, are you there?Yes...Take out the rubbish.Peter, are you there?Yes...Clean your room.Peter, are you there?Yes...Go to the shops.Peter, are you there?No.Oh...A critical observation of a certain household dynamic that occurs frequently around adolescents and their parents. Also a study of the learning curve related to repeating scenarios for an individual. Are you taking this seriously? ;)
