The cycle of life . . .

To feel, to love,

To love, to yearn,

To yearn, to seek,

To seek, to search,

To search, to find,

To find, to wonder,

To wonder, to discover,

To discover, to learn,
 
To learn, to gain,

To gain, to understand,

To understand, to change,

To change, to grow,

To grow, to yearn,

To yearn, to seek,

To seek, to search,

And on goes the cycle of life in
all its simplicity . . .

kvwriterLife isn't about hate, it's about not understanding with a bit of greed thrown in for good balance.  And, yet balance is the key, isn't it?  In this life.  I'm still seeking, but I'm loving too.;)
