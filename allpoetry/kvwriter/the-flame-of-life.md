The Flame of Life
by
Kelly Varner Johnson

A match lights a candle, a baby is born,
A flame flickers, a soul is torn.

We are but fire in the wind, a struggling game,
A weak, unsteady, faltering flame.

Flames lit by the tongues of passion,
Flickers, dimming in the breeze,

Where illness is but a waltz of wind.
And death?  Merely a sneeze . . .

Kelly Varner Johnson
