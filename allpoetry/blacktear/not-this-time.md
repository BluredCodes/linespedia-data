Here you are
Performing for me
You want me to love you
But I cannot accept this,
Why didn’t you understand me?
When I said I wanted to be with you,
Now its your turn
To be the way that I was
Feeling that your heart is going to burn
And that you want God to listen  your thoughts
And make your wishes come true
Why did you avoid me?
When I looked for you
When I was looking forward for you to care about me
But you were always too busy for me
And I hated that because I knew you were lying
What I did was go home crying
But know my time came
I’m being selfish but I don’t want you in my life anymore
Go away, get lost!
And by the way -
Haven’t you heard that “what you give is what you get returned”?
