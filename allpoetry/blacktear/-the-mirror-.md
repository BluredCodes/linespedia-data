---
title: "~ The Mirror ~"
writer: "blackTear"

This is me
That person that you barely see
Almost disappearing
With all those broken-heart moments
And with all those  people’s comments.

That is my body
With scars all over it
That could fall off one of these days
If it keeps living this way.

Those are my eyes
Tired to see people die,
To be sad and angry most of the time
They are not guilty for all this 
They just want to be closed and go to the other side.

Those are my feet
Tired to run away
And to carry all my weight 
They want to step on a calmed place
And be forever safe.
