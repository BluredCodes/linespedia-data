---
title: "*You don't Know me*"
writer: "blackTear"

I AM A FREAK
THAT'S WHAT YOU THINK
DO YOU REALLY KNOW ME?
YOU DON'T KNOW A THING.

I DO STUFFS YOU DON'T LIKE
I SMOKE, I DRINK
I DON'T EVEN KNOW HOW TO RIDE A BIKE
AND I DO WHAT I THINK
THAT'S WHY YOU DON'T WANT TO GET INVOLVE WITH ME.

I SUGGEST YOU SHOULD SPEND SOME TIME WITH ME
AND KNOW ME BETTER
BECAUSE MAYBE YOU'LL BE THE ONE WRITING THIS LETTER
OR MAYBE THE ONE ASKING TO BE LOVED.

WITH ALL YOU HAVE DONE AND THE WAY YOU ARE
YOU GAINED MY HEART
AND KNOWING THAT IN THIS LIFE WE LOSE AND WIN
ONE DAY THIS SITUATION COULD SPIN.

PLEASE TRY NOT TO JUDGE ME
AND BE KIND 
BECAUSE I'M WEAK AND SENSITIVE,
WHY DON'T YOU MAKE UP YOUR MIND?

YOU DON'T KNOW ME...
