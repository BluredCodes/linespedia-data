title: "Vision (to my grandpa)"
writer: "blackTear"

In my bed I was
When I heard someone in the hall passed,
I got up to make sure who was there
I saw nothing so I didn’t really care
On my way back to my room
I heard a “BOOM!”
And behind me I saw a shadow
I was scared, I couldn’t even swallow,
I slowly turned around
And I saw a lovely white-dressed person
His face was familiar for me
I asked myself  “Who could this be?”
I think he heard my thought
Because the silence he cut
He said “ Don’t you remember me?”
I said, “No sir, I’m sorry”
He said, “Don’t worry”
Someone whispered, “is your grandfather”
I didn’t believe so I didn’t bother
Because  he, a long ago died,
He is up in the sky.
I saw him again and it was true!, It was him!
Still tall and thin,
I went to give him a hug
But he flied away like a little bug
He had wings in his back
I couldn’t see him anymore, it was too dark.
That time I couldn’t give him a hug
But I know he is always there
To help me bear my fears
And from any decision be clear
I know you love me, I love you too
And that’s the truth.
