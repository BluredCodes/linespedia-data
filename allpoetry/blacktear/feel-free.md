My friend, feel free to have that thoughtsfeel free to  feelthat someone you want to kill,feel free to feel you want to have in your hand a knifeand hurt your wife,feel free to feel you want to have in your hand a gunand shoot someone for fun,feel free to feel you want to steal someone's souland pretend that it wasn't your fault,feel free to rip out someone's heartand go with it really far,feel free to feel you want to have someone's mindand with it be one of a kind,feel free to cryfeel free to give someone a smilefeel free to think what you wantwhen you think you can'tdon't worryI have felt it toodon't feel sorrywe are not foolsdo ityou'll feel better.
