Stop coming down guys!
This world out here is not what it seems
And the situation right now its rough
Plus, what we are arguing about is not a good theme.

You are such a little thing
But you are meaningful,
You could be marching in little gangs
But you’ll make it look like a waterfall.

Your transparency makes me envy you,
I would like to be a tear
To be invisible for some
And to be from obstacles clear.
