Where is my head at?
Because in the mirror I cannot see my face
Maybe in outer space.
Why is my mind not behaving?
Because I’m not thinking well
Maybe is in hell.
What am I doing in this place?
Because Nobody understands me
Maybe this is not where I’m supposed to be…
