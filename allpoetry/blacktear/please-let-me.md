Pleople lacking personality
I'm one of those
Thanks to people on my back
Whispering on my ear what i should do
or what I should have.

Why not let me be me
Let me be who I want to be
Let me have want to have
Let me do what I think is right for me.

"Don't do this, don't do that"
"ohh no no, that desicion is bad"
"Don't choose a dog, choose a cat"
"don't eat meat, you should eat crab"

why?

Why not breath fresh air
why not take the steering wheel of my own life

Let me follow steps from people I want to
Let me develop te personality I've always wanted
Let me be who I really am
Please Let Me...
