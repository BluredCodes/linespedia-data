---
title: "Don't"
writer: "blackTear"

I might seem like a fool
but don't even dare
to take advantage of me
just try
and for nothing I'll care
if you do it you'll see what you can get
there won't be a  regret
but then don't complain
and at the end nothing I will explain
I'm not trying to scare you
I'm just warning you
that many things can do.
