I’m not happy at all 
I’m  confused
My life I want to enjoy
But tears come down and they are not from joy.

My life is kind of messed
I wanted my life to go other way
With no judgments, no laughs
But I live this one day by day.

Why am I feeling this?
I have things other people don’t have
I don’t get it
Why am I this sad?

I am not really sure what the hell I want!
I think this is the meaning of life
Having everything but what you want
I have to face it, I have to live it.

Life still goes on
Anything can happen
I hope I be “happy” before I’m gone.
