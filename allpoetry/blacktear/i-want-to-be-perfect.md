title: "I want to be *Perfect*"
writer: "blackTear"

I seriously don’t have good skills 
I do most of the things wrong
I try my best and still,
But I keep trying.

When I first started to play the sport I love
Nobody didn’t really care
I was doing my best
For me that wasn’t fair.

When I was at school
Dad, you wanted me to get good marks
You gave me everything you could
But I never gave something back.

When I was trying to get in University
I tried, but I couldn’t
I wanted to go study away from the city
But I failed.

Now here I am
Doing my best
And everything I can
I just need to be cheered up, I guess.
