This have been my dream for a long time
Being a Rock Star
But it’s a big mountain to climb
From my hands, that dream is so far.

I wish I could go up on a stage
Sing the things I feel
With all my rage
And people’s attention appeal.

Maybe act insane
Or do things I have never done
To get a bit of fame
Or be unique and never be alone.

If I could have in my hands an expensive guitar
Or a luxurious Mercedes car
I’d be happy
I’d be a Rock Star.Guys remember English is my second language. Sorry if theres any mistake. =)
