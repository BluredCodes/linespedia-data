THE NOBLE BEASTA most noble beast is the one called Horse.It has served Man for many years.So strong and proud, with head held high,Such beauty has oft brought a man to tears.He could be a Clydesdale, bold and tall,A sleek Arabian, or a cowpony, quick and smart,Or a little Shetland, only ten hands high.It matters not, for he will win your heart.What man can teach, the horse can learn.An intelligent creature is that quadruped.And many a cowboy has told this tale:Were it not for his horse, he would now be dead.My horse will have his place in Heaven;If not, I’ll know the reason why,And I’m sure that mine will wait for meAt that last big roundup... in the sky.
