title: "The Minds Eye"

The autumn leaves were falling
And a chill was in the air
As I wandered through the woodland
Sure that no-one else was there
My eyes beheld the wonder
My mind was in a whirl
When suddenly from nowhere
There appeared a little girl
She didn't seem to notice
That I had invaded her retreat
So I hid behind an oak tree
Trying hard to be discreet
She moved so very slowly
Each step was carefully placed
And with nimble little fingers
Each flower and leaf she traced
She stopped within a clearing
Her face turned to the sun
And I stepped out from my hiding
Praying hard she wouldn't run
But the wonder she was seeing
Was all within her mind
For when she asked
"Is someone there?"
I knew she must be blind
