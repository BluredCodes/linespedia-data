When the thought of christmas shopping
Is really the pits
And all that ho-ho-hoing
Just gives you the shits
Then think of poor Mary
Now there in lies a yarn
Forced to give birth
In a cold draughty barn
And when it was over
The babies proud pa
Decided to announce it
By displaying a star
As though it wasn't enough
She couldn't sleep for the light
People came by the dozen
She had visitors all night
There were kings who brought gifts
Frankincense,  gold and myrrh
But none thought to bring
So much as a daisy for her
There were drummers and shepherds
Angel choirs what a sight
So just give a thought 
To the first yuletide plight
For christmas is giving
Love for others not self
When all fights and differences
Should be put on the shelf
So stop all your whinging
Pick your chin off the floor
And try to remember
What this day's really for
