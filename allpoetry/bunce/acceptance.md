God took our precious baby
To aplace we cannot see
And although our hearts are heavy
We know that he is free
He now lives with the angels
In a very special place
For our son has gone to heaven
And received Gods'holy grace
Sometimes the tears still flow with ease
Oour hearts fill with despair
Then an angel wing will brush my face
And Iknow that he's still there
To wish him back would not be right
I know that in my heart
God sent him for a reason
And he had played his part
Wemay never know just what it was
That he was sent to do
But we know that he was special
One of the chosen few
