title: "Fading World"
writer: "PoeticKnight"

There’s no more bridges to be burned
No more lessons to be learned
No more lies to be told
No more dreams to be sold
No more waiting, no more stalling
No more feeling like your falling
No more life and no more death
No more ‘cause nothing’s left


So paint a vision, paint a line
Between my reason and your rhyme
‘Cause everywhere I turn
People crash and people burn
Yeah, nothing lasts for long
It just keeps fading till it’s gone


All the world's a lie
Doesn't really matter why
Forget the pleasure and the pain
There’s no one left to blame
They stood at the gates of death
Where man and his maker met
Closed their eyes, took one step in
Now they can never step back again


So paint a vision, paint a line
Between my reason and your rhyme
‘Cause everywhere I turn
People crash and people burn
Yeah, nothing lasts for long
It just keeps fading till it’s gone


I walk by night and dream by day
But just can’t seem to find my way
I’ll learn to live and learn to pray
For all the friends I have betrayed
So many things I want say
So many fears I’ve kept at bay
I was concerned, but it’s okay
They can come but they can’t stay


So paint a vision, paint a line
Between my reason and your rhyme
‘Cause everywhere I turn
People crash and people burn
Yeah, nothing lasts for long
It just keeps fading till it’s gone
