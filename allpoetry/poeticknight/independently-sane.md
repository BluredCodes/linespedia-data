Are you trapped in mystery’s cage?
A trigger of rage
A solider of fate
A minion of hate
Independently sane
Like a storm without rain
Like a world without change
Like infinite strange.

So wake your sleeping dreams,
Your withering wings
And learn how to run
From the moon to the sun
To the stars above
Tempted by love
For one moment of flight
Faster than night.
