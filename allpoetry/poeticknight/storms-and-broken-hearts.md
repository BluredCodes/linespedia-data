Does every broken mirror
Reflect your broken heart?
Does every whisper warn you,
“Go not gentile through the dark.”

Does every shadow
Claim to be your soul?
And when you watch a storm 
Do you feel out of control?

Well did you ever wonder
Why in every life there must come some thunder?
So, tell me, where is it all coming from?

And do you find the lightning
Disturbing or exciting,
Or is it a shadow on the run?
