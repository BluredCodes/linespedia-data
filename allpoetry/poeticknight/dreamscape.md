I’m lost in a maze of incoherent dreams
Can’t seem to fly with these neo sensitive wings
Running from the mechanical flocks
Round and round a paradox

So if you ever take reason for granted
And the world reminds you logic is slanted 
Don’t lose you sense of good and bad
Because everyone here is mad

And if you get suppressed by the fission
Overwhelmed by the voice of division  
Don’t let yourself become an empty station 
Another ghost in suspended animation

And if you realize something’s wrong
But can’t put your finger on
Just what it is, well lose or win
Don’t let those demons in

You found the key but lost the gate
Can’t reach velocity needed to escape
