---
title: "Shadows In The Wind"
writer: "PoeticKnight"
slug: "shadows-in-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The world is tiffany-twisted,
Haunted by sanity’s sins.
We all are just wandering
Shadows in the wind.

But if the magic missed its mark
And all the poets turned to stone,
Would you still stand beside me?
Or would you leave me here alone?

And if tomorrow’s farmers 
Planted weapons in their fields
And killed their neighbors one by one
Every acre that they tilled,

And if the whisper of what should have been,
The sound of words unsaid,
Sends silence echoing through the night
And ringing in your head,

And if your dreams fill the sky
And slowly eclipse the sun,
And if the night never ends
And if the morning never comes, 

And if reality 
Decides to fade away
And leave us to face the fate
We knew we’d face some day,

And if all the fears we hid break free,
And if all our spirits rust,
If all the stars we wished on fall,
And if all our wishes turn to dust,

And if the dogs of war
Are given back their bark,
And if they hunt us
As we stumble through the dark,

And if the world turns upside-down
And all the rivers run reverse,
Would our love be as strong?
Or would it break under the curse?

And if we live to see the day
It all comes to an end
Nothing would have really changed
We’d still be shadows in the wind.
