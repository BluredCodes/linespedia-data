title: "Feel"
writer: "KaNdY"
slug: "feel"

---

I feel this certain way 
That you could never understand 
I wanted you to want me 
I wanted you to take my hand. 
I feel this certain thing 
It burns inside my heart 
You broke it with a ton of knives 
As if I were your ruined work of art 

I wish I never felt 
Those things I felt for you 
You treated me like a hasben 
A friend you never knew. 

Now our friendships fading 
Like our picture on my wall 
Fading into darkness, to nothingness 
Nothing at all.
