Some days I wonder... 
Why I am here
Why I can't sleep all day
Why I can't always confide in a friend
Why the world does stupid things
Why I play the fool

somedays I wish...
This day would never end
That we would not have to say goodbye
That friends are always there
That my family understood who I am

Some days I listen...
To calm the drama
TO understand
To help

Some days I fear...
i will get to tired
i will be un-loved
i will lose hope
i will fade away
