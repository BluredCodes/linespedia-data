---
title: "What if?"
writer: "KaNdY"

What would have happened if I said something earlier
What would have happened if I said nothing at all

Now that I have said something

What would have happened if you said something different
What would have happened if you didn't want to be friends

Now that we are friends

What will the future hold with this friendship
What will happen if you change your mind

Now that I still have these feelings
I will try and keep them hidden away
So our friendship never lacks
The great qualities it has
