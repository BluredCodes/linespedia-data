---
title: "Real True Me"
writer: "KaNdY"
slug: "real-true-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45025-Real-True-Me-by-KaNdY"
---

I want to run away 
I need to run away 
From all these problems 
That I face day to day 
I fear the day 
Where I would say 
Goodbye. 
Leaving you behind 
I have to decide 
To a world that's blind 
That can never see 
The real true me 
To make a decision 
Between the two of you 
Was so hard for me to do 
One person can see right into my heart 
And that's the person 
Who will always know the real true me.
