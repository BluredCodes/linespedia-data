Love
Is an overwhelming
Heartbeat
That beats deep
Within your chest
The butterflies that sing and dance
Showing your nervousness 

Heaven
Is the pale
Iridescent sky 
With its sprinkles 
Of fairy dust falling
Within the rain

Passion
Is the fire
You feel 
For the very first time

Loyalty
Knowing that they
Will never leave your side

Sex
That night of passion
Between you that will
Last forever
