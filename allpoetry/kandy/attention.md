title: "Attention"
writer: "KaNdY"
slug: "attention"
source: "AllPoetry"

You get all the attention 
In this world of hate 
You don't care about what people think 
or what they want or even if they care 
all you think about is yourself 
I want some of your attention 
all the attention you get 
But I am so damn paranoid 
I can't handle 
The stares from the others.
