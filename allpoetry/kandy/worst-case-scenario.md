I'm blinded by my tears
Tears running down my face
soaking my skin

Why the remarks
The literal slap in the face

I thought you could overcome things
I thought you were strong
Do you just try to make things harder?

I don't understand you 
But I try so hard
Perhaps you don't understand me
won't you ever try

Friendship doesn't always have to be a struggle
Or a battle of the worst case scenario
Friendship should be being able to trust someone
Listen to them, tell them when something is wrong
or if something is bothering them!

You don't just sit there and say I don't know you
You can't hide things from me

I can see through you
Nothing everything is as bad as it seems
Your not the only one
Everyone goes through this
