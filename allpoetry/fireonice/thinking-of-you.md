---
title: "Thinking of you"
writer: "FireOnIce"
slug: "thinking-of-you"

I lapped the breeze for your beautiful scent,
It only whisked me by.
I looked to the sea to find your reflection,
But I could only see the sky.
I begged the birds to sing our song,
But it merely made me cry.

In this crippled eve,
I find no reprieve.
The past has passed me by.
