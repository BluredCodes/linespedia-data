A proper education surely
to sing in one tongue, speak in another
and save the other for servants
and truant friends.

An apprenticeship in music perhaps
so one can chant
soapboxes are socio-economic traps
play soccer or draw maps

or blend times and climes
to develop the theme
of twittering mountain streams

then praise a patch of flowers
some for the colour
other for their seeds.

A proper education surely
to know the flowers from the weeds.
