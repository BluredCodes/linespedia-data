title: "Don't Let Me Die By the Sea"
writer: "shomi"

I’d rather die in a train

smelling  sweat and steel
and hair in the mouth

or by tracks
where sparrows mate
 
(brown cricket balls
dancing a scuffle
round and round)

on firm ground.
