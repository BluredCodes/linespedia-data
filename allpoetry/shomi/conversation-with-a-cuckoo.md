---
title: "Conversation with a Cuckoo"
writer: "shomi"
slug: "conversation-with-a-cuckoo"
source: "AllPoetry"

You dismiss me with a nod
forgetting
how forgotten you are

on april white twigs
or ruthly 
gleaning
last summer’s seed.
