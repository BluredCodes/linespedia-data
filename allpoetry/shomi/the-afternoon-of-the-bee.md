title: "The Afternoon of the Bee"

The wind waits the hours
the sleeping scents of flowers
the midday white of the panes waits
its show of blue again

unaware or that snore
beating wing sting and more
till a kind soul on watch
squashed a tiger lily blotch
to honey the clammy web.

what poppies hide and roses feign
what vines dole out in sweet disdain
all end in an afternoon buzz.
