title: "Words for Music"

Only for tunes the stories wait 
to tell themes is tedious 
and sometimes tiresome

Floating words 
trap glimpses of harmony
symphonies drum on tongue tips
lips pregnant to cascade
drip spit.

Better untold wait virgin juvenilia
still borns with sterile twists
barren tales with plots unkissed
till raped in music’s bacchanalia.
