He’s no alien eye
riding high
when the lights go out

or a magic mind 
stringing words in rhyme
taking people out
to wine and dine
and undermine.

On the stage
off a book
he never wants to be 
really shook

and the man who built
the Eiffel Tower
didn't know his grandfather.
