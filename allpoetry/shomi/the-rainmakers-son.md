The rainmaker’s son is a sun worshipper. And he’s written a book. The bastard.Still borns are his specialtytimed miscarriages and  dead embryofish-finned foetuses and ill-timed abortions.If you have a lock hide him between your Ellis and your Ebingor the crafty queer will recitelines  that dry wombsfor his father’s a rainmakerand he doesn't carry an umbrella.
