A hijara touched me in the rickshaw this morningpalm cupping my chinred nails on my kneeacross the roadthe dogs were matingblack coveringbrown and whitethen an angry swirland a suddenly spat mother fuckerhit me and the dogs.Hijara - Eunuch beggar(hindi)
