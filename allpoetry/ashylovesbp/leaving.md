Im leaving today 
not to come home 
to leave this place 
to return all alone
I cant stand being different 
others cant stand being the same 

Ill miss being here 
but be glad that i left 
sure I will love you 
but it just wont be the same 

I hate the ones who made me different 
but love the ones who cared
the ones who thought me the same
