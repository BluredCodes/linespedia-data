OH some day I'll be with you 

But untill that day i have to say 

That you are my one and only 

My true friend that keeps me holy 



I keep saying that I wanna be true 

But I can't do it with out you 

I wish I could say we'll be together forever

But for that to happen I'll have to leave 



Someday I wont miss you so much

I'll forget our lives and we'll get out of touch 

I would sware I won't forget you but i cant do that much 

Our friendship is sinking fast and i want it to last and last 

And Last



~~~BFF KATHY~~~
