A friend is a thing we all come across 
 but you are the one millionth chance
 the chance to have one true friend 
 the one you'd call your best

 Sure we argue and fight sometime 
 But even God gets mad
 I'll sure miss you when your gone 
 cause your smile makes me glad
 
 not just your smile but your faith 
 to make everyone trust in you 
 ill never forget the day we met 
 the day that i met you

 I miss you already but your sitting right there 
 I dont want you to ever leave 
 Cause leaving would mean leaving a friendship 
 And only god knows what ours means.
 
 I love you like a sister, a soal mate and a friend 
 Only our friendship can make a place 
 Where man can speak to man 

 I haven't seen you in a while 
 but i know your there 
 there in my heart where you belong 
 always my caring always my friendAm i any good? I hope so, cause I'm not sure?
