Oh to be out on my own 
To live a life and be alone 
To love someone new everyday 
Without having to worry about you my babe 

Im free to live with out having to worry 
Im okay to party without a guilty concince
Im free from love and being true 
Im free from loving you 

I can man hunt once again 
To write my fellings without men 
Oh how i love them but just wait 
And see the new guys Im free to date 

Yes i loved you that is true 
But now Im free to love not only you 
I can love all of the guys 
Just wait and see my new suprise

Ive got some new men that caught my eye 
And I've seem to have caught theirs too 
Their baby blue and and green eyes too 
The ones who can all be mine 

Yes I'll miss you but now its true 
I free to live my life without you 
To live and love is what its all about
No never mind i wont miss you 
Cause i wont be living a lie thats true
