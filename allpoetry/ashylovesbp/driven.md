title: "Driven"

Ive ran forever driven to the end 
i cant stand to run much longer
i'd love to sit and rest a while 
But i still love you best 

Im here alone running from you 
wishing that you'd stop
True I loved you 
But now i dont 
So please just leave me alone 

Leave me be so you can see 
How much pain your putting me through
Pain that hasnt stopped hurting since 
You brought me to this place

Driven to the end wanting to go back 
but your standing there not letting me go 
I want to leave you but to do so i would 
hurt you more than my heart wants to 

Come on please let me go 
and be with other people 
Just let me know 
if i can go
