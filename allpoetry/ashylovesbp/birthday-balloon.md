As I walked down the street 

I saw a small boy 

He held in his tiny hands a balloon



Not just any balloon 

A birthday balloon 

Red and shiny and new 



This was the start of a chain of memories 

Starting with when i was very young

Hlding a birthday balloon just the same 



Sometime ago when i was very small

I Held a birthday balloon in my hand

And in the other,I held my mothers



My mother protecting me from the cars

Protecting me from all feared

she guided me, and smiled on me 

cause she new that i was her girl



I know that some where near 

My mother will protect me from all feared

and keep me close and tight

and keep me in her arms



Sometime ago when i got older 

I didnt have my birthday balloon

I didnt have my childish wish for a 

simple red balloon



I wanted Something more than a balloon 

something bigger, something better

maybe a car, or stereo, or bar of simple goodness

but maybe not 



maybe somewhere deep down in there 

i still wanted that balloon

That balloon by my side when mom wasnt around

to protect me from all feared



Who knows 

maybe we all need a bright birthday balloon

something to make us happy

or glad 

or to makes us not so sad 



but i know that as i walked down the street 

and looked at that small boy 

that something clicked inside my head

as spoke to me softly and calmly 



Remember when you were little

Remember all those days 

that you walked along the street

with a birthday balloon in your handIs this any good
