I've missed loving you 
watching you go round 
I wish that you would come back soon 
a thought that makes me down

Oh that night that you went away 
the screaming in my ears
the screaming that was silent then
but lays here wishing to stay 

You used to play your guitar for me
though you were kinda bad
but now it just sits in you room 
collecting dust and making me mad

I wish i could love you 
just for one more day 
one more day to sit by you 
i day that i prayed for today 

you wont be here today 
nor tomorrow nor the next 
you wont be back for ever my love 
but youll live here in my text
