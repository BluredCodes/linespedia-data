I walked in my new class romm 
to find a green baboon 
sitting where the teacher should 
trying to read the paper nude 

there were no students 
no desks no nothing 
i started to scrach my head but 
then i remembered some thing 

Once a long time ago 
in a far away land 
thier lived a man 
Bla Bla BLa 

This was what my kinder teacher told me
once so long ago 
i miss her dearly but truely i dont miss her at all
at all 



This was my brain emptier 
at the end of my day so please dont judge so harshly 
as you would if you could but you shouldn'tPLEASE dont rate this one a much as possible
