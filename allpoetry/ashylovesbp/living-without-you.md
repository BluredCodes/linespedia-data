Living here is a new beginning 
Starting a new life amoung the stangers
is a test beyond all others

Missing home is strange enough
But seeing it in my dreams 
Sooths my soal and everything in it
So that I may sleep once more

The nightmares that wake me everynight
The taunts that haunt me by day 
Remind me that once long ago you held me everyday 

I miss you,I need you 
I want to be near you 
And love you once again.

But these nightmares haunting me
Just wont leave me 
with you alone in my head

You were my love alone with me 
to keep me warm at night
But now that evil "friend" of mine 
Has stolen you from my head

My head, my soal, my every part 
Aches with ideas of you 
Ideas that once kept me warm
Now leave for cold and dead

I will always you love till the day i die
And someday you will too 
Love another just the way i did 
The way that i loved you
