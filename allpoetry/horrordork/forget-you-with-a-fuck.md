i am conveniently forgotten 
"best thing that ever happened" 
i never thought you'd be the one to rub me from your eyes 
i will always be ignored 
a year i've been the greatest 
and then you say that those were lies 

i'm tired, and all i seem to think of is your hand between my thighs 
i guess i never knew the answer, so why ask the question now? 
i never thought you'd be the one to rub me from your eyes 
doesn't matter who you lie about when there's no one else around 

guess you're free to walk away and i'm the one who's stuck 
but maybe you could help me just forget you with a fuck 

overripe and spoiled rotton 
"best thing that ever happened" 
i never thought you'd be the one to rub that in my face 
i never told you who you were, you never bit your tongue 
but when my turn to speak came up, 
it was not the time or place 

i'm tired, and all i seem to think of is your hand between my thighs 
i guess i never knew the answer, so why ask the question now? 
i never thought you'd be the one to rub this in my face 
doesn't matter who you lie about when there's no one else around 

guess you're free to walk away and i'm the one who's stuck 
but maybe you could help me just forget you with a fuck 

hey, you're great, and fuck you too 
let's be friends, and fuck you too 
you're the best, and fuck you too 
and look at what i've done for you 
you're amazing, fuck you too 
i'm being honest, fuck you too 
read the context, fuck you too 
how could i get along without you? 

i'm tired, and all i seem to think of is your hand between my thighs 
i guess i never knew the answer, so why ask the question now? 
i never thought you'd be the one to rub me from your eyes 
doesn't matter who you lie about when there's no one else around 

guess you're free to walk away and i'm the one who's stuck 
but maybe you could help me just forget you with a fuck 
maybe i was the one who fucked both of us up 
but come here and help me get that 
help me forget that with a fuck
