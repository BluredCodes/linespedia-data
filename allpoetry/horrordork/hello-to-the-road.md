title: "hello to the road"
writer: "horrordork"

she left again and waved goodbye to your shadow one more hello to your backside one more drink before she hits the road before she goes out for a ride stories taking on disguises and words are rearranged one more lie is always told she don't remember it that way and yellow lines have passed her eyes they might as well be red for all she knows she's still at home watching tv in her bed laughing at the blatant jokes never hearing what they said with all the good her will has done she might as well be dead she left again and waved goodbye to your shadow would it have been better if she hadn't said a thing would it have been better if she were never born goodbye to your backside hello to the dashboard just one more drink, just watch her drink it down she'll need more than that to numb the pain when she crashes to the ground
