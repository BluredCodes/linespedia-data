hung by the words / i thought you 
always felt the same 
i knew you felt the cold, the pain 
tore out my tongue / i could never do you wrong 
i will always be the one for you to blame 
down in the pit / my stomach churns 
and makes me sick 
and i cannot forget your name 
the thought i can't deny / i told the truth 
there's no reply 
and still i can't forget the way you taste 
filtered in your mouth / all the sweetness taken out 
nothing but your bitterness remains 
the redness in my eyes 
you are my sickness / let me die 
you are the salt that dries upon my face 
down in the pit / my stomach churns 
you make me sick 
and on my tongue i only taste your name 
you are the thought i can't deny / i always run 
but i can't hide 
i know someday you might just fade away
