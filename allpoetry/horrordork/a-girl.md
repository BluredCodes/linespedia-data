title: "a girl"
writer: "horrordork"

i know a girl who swallows down before she reads the label 
i know a girl so drunk that she forgot her name 
i know a girl, she's so confused she drinks the pissy water 
and when i look at her, she doesn't recognize my face 

i know a girl who likes to run the show and spread her legs 
i know a girl who carries a bottle of pills and a bottle of beer 
i know a girl who tastes it even when it's down her throat 
her ears are open, and i scream, but she just doesn't hear me 

in her head, she's praying even though she can't be saved 
and when she looks up to the light 
He won't look her in the face 
He wants to see her suffer 
let her choke on her disgrace 
You don't ever interfere 
You don't protect the human race 

i know a girl who's minding her own business 
i know a girl who's in a very wrong place at a very wrong time 
and there's a guy who pounces down and forces down upon her 
and where were You when she was screaming in the summer night 

in her head, she's praying 
even though she can't be saved 
and when she looks up to the light 
He won't look her in the face 
He wants to see her suffer 
let her choke on her disgrace 
You don't ever interfere 
You don't protect the human race
