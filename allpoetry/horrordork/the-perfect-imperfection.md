title: "the perfect imperfection"
writer: "horrordork"

a pretty burning light 
i think the bathroom's up in flames 
not that it's important 
cuz your hand's between my thighs 
i think i love you 
or maybe just your fingertips 
i think i'm burning 
i'm melting through my final sigh 

the mistake in the directions 
you're the perfect imperfection 
you're everything i never want to be 
broken open all the scars 
cheated all the things we are 
bastardized the things that you could be 

just one more excuse 
i think i'm falling out of place 
not that it's important 
cuz i've never felt so used 
i think i need you 
or maybe i'm just tired 
i think i'm bleeding 
but i know i'm prob'ly just confused 

the mistake in the directions 
you're the perfect imperfection 
you're everything i never want to be 
broken open all the scars 
cheated all the things we are 
bastardized the things that i could be 

i made one more mistake 
can't say that i'm not trying 
not that it's important 
cuz i'll always be to blame 
i think i hate you 
or maybe it's the same 
you tell me that you love me 
then forget my name 

the mistake in the directions 
you're the perfect imperfection 
you're everything i never want to be 
broken open all the scars 
cheated all the things we are 
torn up all the things i want 
filled up all the wounds with salt 
bastardized the things that we could beback in the day,
