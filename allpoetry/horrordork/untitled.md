i wanna feel it again 
i can taste the needle slipping 
slipping underneath my skin 
pressure grabbing, holding tightly 
let me take you in 
cold and hard 
buried deep inside of me, 
i want you hidden 
you always were the one 
so let me break you open 

i believe there's something in here 
beneath the sand there's something in here 
a presence that will always make me scream 
you are not the one i wanted 
lick the wounds before i start to bleed 
and i still need you 

i can feel it again 
i hear you whisper softly 
the multitude of lies 
i feel them underneath my skin 
they scratch, they burn and now i'm bleeding 
and will it ever end 
you're disconnected and unwanted 
but let me take you in 
you wanted something from me 
so c'mon, fucking break me open 

i believe there's something in here 
beneath the lies, there's someone in here 
your presence always made me want to scream 
you are not the one i wanted 
now you're dead, you're spoiled rotton 
lick the wounds before i start to bleed 
choke me now before i start to sing 
take everything i have away from me 
you always were the one to make a scene 
and i still need youback in the day,
