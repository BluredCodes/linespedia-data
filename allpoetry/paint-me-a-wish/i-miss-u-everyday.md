title: "I Miss U Everyday"
writer: "paint me a wish"

I can’t stand it when we’re far apart
I feel so sorry for my breaking heart
Coz I miss you everyday, please come out to play
I need to see you or I’ll fall apart

I want to see your face & touch your hair
I want everyone to look & stare
Coz I miss you everyday, wont you come & play
It’s so annoying you live so far away

I want to kiss the raindrops off your face
I want to be seen with you all over the place
 Coz I miss you everyday, wont you come & play
I’m in heaven coz you think I am ace

I can’t wait for us to fall in love
It’s you I’m always I’m thinking of
Coz I miss you everyday, please come out & play
I want to see you smile & make you laugh

I wonder where we’ll be in a year?
Maybe full of loving cheer
Coz I couldn’t bear to lose you, I feel the same way you do
I really want to hold you my dear
