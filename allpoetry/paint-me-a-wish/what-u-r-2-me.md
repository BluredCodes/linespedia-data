What U R 2 Me

U r my first love
And that fact will always remain
That U r my first love
And special people never change 
U r my first love
And I wish u never let me go

U r love
U r as sweet as can b
Coz that’s what love is
Well that’s what it means 2 me
Coz U r love
You’re the greatest thing in the world

I was thinking,
Of the good times we shared
Coz we can do it
Yeah we can do it again
If u want 2
Then u know that I’m always around

I remember a cold journey down
U said it was my fought
That the tracks freeze around
It was a great day
Please don’t let the memory fade away

I am so lonely
Without u around
I am so lonely
Without u I wish I wasn’t around

Coz you’re the best thing that’s ever happened 2 me
I never knew, I could b so happy
Because U r love
U r as sweet as can b
Coz that’s what love is
Well that’s what it means 2 me
Coz just like love
You’re the greatest thing in the world,
You’re the greatest thing in the world,
You’re the greatest thing in the world….!!!
