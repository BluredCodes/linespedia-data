I walk past places of days gone by
I’m man enough to admit I cried
Coz when I was there
The memories came flooding back
Of you and me
I really need all of that

This is where we kissed for the very first time
This is where you held my hand
This is where we said it was love
That’s all I’m ever thinking of.

Coz I missed the boat to perfection
How could I do this to me?
I hope you no just what u mean to me
My life’s so shit
Because were not a we
But I, will always, love u
But I, will always, love u

The first time I met you
You sat next to me
I said hello
I was as shy as can be
Who would of thought then?
Just what you would mean to me?
Not Me

The light shorn brightly
Your eyes crystal blue
Who would have thought then?
I would fall in love with you
Not me, did you?

But now Iv missed your boat of perfection
I’m so angry
I hope you no just what you mean to me
My life’s so shit
Because were not a we
But I, will always, love u
But I, will always, love u

So I’m in a street
My life’s in repeat 
I’m up a hill
Wishing we were there still
Coz I’m so angry 
Of what I’ve missed 
It’s my life 
Which I think I’ve kissed, goodbye
Coz Iv missed your boat of perfection
I’m a such a fool to me
You no I wish we were a we
My life’s so shit
Coz I can’t tell u what you mean to me
But I, will always, love u
But I, will always, love u

Its your little things
That make me feel great
The words you say
You are my soul mate

But I look at you now
I see what I have missed
I can’t bare it without you
I can’t even exist 

Coz I missed your boat of perfection
Your boat of perfection
Your boat of perfection
