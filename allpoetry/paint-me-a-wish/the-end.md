Things were hard but now there not
Me and you we had our lot
I see this now when I don’t see you
I want you to know I really loved you

You helped the world and his friends get though there time
I was so proud to call you mine
Things were hard but now there fine
You and me we had our time
I see this now when you don’t see me 
I really need to know if you loved me

I see your face my heart starts to race
It seems your feelings have gone without a trace
I don’t think you care know more
Is to talk to me a chore?
Maybe I shouldn’t even care
What you do or if your there
But I do, coz apart of me still loves you
Things were hard but now there fine
I’m glad you and me had a time.
