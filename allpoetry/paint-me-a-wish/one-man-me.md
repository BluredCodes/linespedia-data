title: "one man = ME"
writer: "paint me a wish"
slug: "one-man-me"

In my misery, depression and rage 
Page after page after page
In my misery, depression and rage
Page after page

One mans love is now another mans
I wish you never did stray
My love is now another mans
I wish I never got up that day..!

One mans life is gone down a big hole 
I wish you never did play away
I’m in defeat and you’re so sweet
You know I wish I never got up that day..!
