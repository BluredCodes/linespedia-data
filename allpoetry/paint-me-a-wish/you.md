title: "You"

I was on top of the mountain
When I met you
Then you pushed me off
Though I still love you

You r my savior
You make me feel great
So where was my savoir?
When I was sad and irate

I thought you loved me
Then you do this to me
I’m so sad now
I could do anything
I’m missing you badly
I could jump out a tree
I need to unlock the door to your heart
And love is the key

I’m shedding my tears now
Every night
I really hope soon,
You will see the light

Now I’m where I belong,
I’m Down with the clowns
I need you so much
You make me up when I’m down
