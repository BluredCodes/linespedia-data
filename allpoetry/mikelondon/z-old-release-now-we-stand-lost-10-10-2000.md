now we stand lost with our head in the clouds
going nowhere fast and going down without knowing how
thought we knew who we were it turned out we do
but there's so much more it seems also we're in need of towels
everywhere there is to see nothing has come unto me
standing alone tall inside a camel's eye with glass shard mysteries
never knew which way to go but now it seems that's already been taken care of
reach out for instant salvation but the man sinks in an ocean of tears
thought we knew who we were yet we did just not really
every time I turn to see there is nothing that I haven't seen before
yet somehow its all new and somehow its worthy of death
the only thing I know for sure is I've been here before
and yet somehow somewhere there is a place that counterbalances this
thought we were going there and why aren't we there now
how come we knew ourselves bet yet not as fully as to see us naked
always always covering ourselves hiding ourselves not wanting to drop the illusion
yet now here we are standing lost in the middle of a rainbow
nowhere to turn too nowhere to run we've only a place in the shadow of the sun
how do we really know we've got things correct and in order
confusion sets in and sinks us into a corpus sinew of spydery floss
everything we stood for and everything we fought has turned against look what it has bought
thought we knew what we were doing did we know anything at all my God look at us all
standing swaying in the winds of time looking for all cost's sake like a ramble shack dime
and I know as I go down I've lost it all we are going to be bound
so now look at me don't forget because now we stand lostWritten October 26th, 2001 ©  on Oct 26 2001 03:29 PM PST, Michael Edward London    abuse
