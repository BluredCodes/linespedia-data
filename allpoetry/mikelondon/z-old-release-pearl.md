they say you have a nice smile
but now I just remember the tears
there was a time when you were so happy
but now that is gone with the wind
I remember wanting to hold you
but even now I wouldn't know what to do
there used to be the day when I'd kill for you
now I don't know even where you are
I know it hurts but what can I do
I remember you watching her
in that damnable silent misery
I wanted to yank the illness out
there had to be something I could do
just to sat and watch and let her die each day
now with her gone I can't lose you
she loved you so much please know its true
I want you to have this it's her pearl
she wanted you to have this when you found him
she wanted to watch as I walked down the aisle
please come back and let me hold you dear
let me so those beautiful babies
I know it's hard we're all drowning too
at least if you cannot see me come for the necklace
I want to see your smile and hear your laugh
maybe your husband can come too
he's never met me that's alright
I'm sure he's so nice to you
just come home and let me see you
let me see your family please I'm asking you
if you don't want the necklace let me hook it
let me see how well it looks on my granddaughter
please come back we need to talk
don't run now I'm here don't hate me
let the anger pass it's not worth it
let the time heal your wounds and restore your family
forgive me I did not mean to do this thing
you must believe that don't doubt what I stand for
remember the necklace and don't be afraid to knock on the door
