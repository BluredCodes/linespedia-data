Puttin' history to a stop dontcha see I'm a cryin'
Come on welcome me and my Countryside Blues Band
Hear that slide guitar wail and hollow come on now
wontcha give me a dolla'
We've a been playing Dylan covers and Jordon covers
and those awful mean John Lee Hooker tunes
come on hear us play and give us a dolla'
My hero's name is Lennon and my friend's name is Paul
and we've been playing in our Countryside Blues Band
ever since the world began
We don't play no country tunes we play straight folk
Dontcha know we're the Countryside Blues Band
Add a little rock'n'roll to spice up the invasion
come on now dontcha know we're takin' over
got a few drum rolls from a harlot Moon and we've been learnin' 
all we can about the blues from Blind Willie McTell
We saw the flood a' Noah and we seen Adam and Eve
We seen the Civil War (dontcha know war ain't Civil)
and we been put in chain gangs
Ain't nobody been around longer than the Countryside Blues Band
you know it we can play it everyone altogether know clap your hands
throw your fists up in the air down with the establishment
We played that scene and we played political rallies
Everyone loves the Countryside Blues Band
We got a little secret we ain't never told no one
the true leader is John Lennon and he done organized our music
so here's to you John and your Fab, Fab ways
Wontcha all welcome, come on know clap your hands you know who they are
JOHN LENNON AND THE COUNTRYSIDE BLUES BAND!!!!!!!!!Written August 19th, 2000 ©  on Oct 24 2001 06:40 AM PST, Michael Edward London    contemporary  • dark  • hope  • nature  • personal  • sad  • society  • spiritual  • thoughts  • weird
