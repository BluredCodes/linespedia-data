To the dead fish in the sea
I salute you
To the decadent whore on the street
I pity your putty hands
To the Lord of all things
I salute the sex you gave us
And it is here, in divergence,
That everything converges

It is here, on this date of creation,
I have seen the impossible.
I have seen the birth of the mountain
Olympus Mons, the beautiful and sacred.

The play of the gods are here
It is their divine comedy, a supreme mockery
Of the words we know none,
Of the acts we know all.
It is here the most subtle of deceptions is practiced

Into the blackened and charred voice
The place of birth, life, sex, death
Sleep, Air, Breath, Nourishment
These we need, these we crave
Unto all I enslave you to this
Bondage of a body, entrapment of the mind
Utter folly and pride, that is my sin

To the world I grant you an audience
To my wife I grant her my sex,
To my life I grant to my God,
Yet it is God who grants life
Again, I fall into pathos and despair

The truth, unknown to so many
Works its way like a shard in my heart
Death and beauty are yet one,
For in death I walk on streets of transparent gold

Into the life of you I send my son Christ

Heresy and politics, a beautiful companion
One and the same, they rely on each other
It is here wickedness is spread
It is here that Christianity has failed

So you see, my son, said the earthworm father
To the earthworm son
I am long and elongated, yet I drown 
Drown when the rain comes
What does that tell you?

My eyes have fallen away from my flesh
Instead, I see skeletons and nightmare visions
In lucid madness I dream and sleep
In waking terror I live and breath

In all my days of life, I have come to know one simple truth
I am

Yet I am not.  If he is, I am not.

I am;
Therefore, I am not

So you see, the question is not to live and breathe
The question is how do I achieve utter sanctum

Man with no face, flesh, or ears
Came to me, looked my in my face
Touched me, though he bore no flesh
And I came to God

My sleep is apparent in my madness
There, my face is tranquil,
There, my mouth does no evil
There, my perception is no evil
There, I am peaceful, with no disturbance
There, I am abnormal,
No lust of the flesh, converging of the eyes, 
Jealousy and pride trouble me not
When unconscious, I am not amoral
To this world, that is madness
Yet to my lover [the one above] I bring joy

God, God, wherefore art thou, God?

Here I am, he cries

Deafness brought on by stupidity
With nothing but sin to block me
Save me, Oh Lord, for it is you who are the one

Emotions run rampant, my God is constantMarch 24 2000,
