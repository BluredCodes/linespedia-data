All you need is war 
Bombing and killing 
Hear those helicopters soar 
Can't find love anymore 
Of course we don't need that 
Just this sheer adrenaline 
Whatever you need its found in war 
War ain't hell its great 
Think of all the freedom you can enjoy 
Gonna study war all day and night 
Don't worry be a killer 
Everything you need can be found on the edge of a knife 
You don't think we don't know where you been? 
Of course you've been masquareing on a sea of emotions 
Lay it all down we got the answer right here 
Come and join the war 
Go kill and learn some hate 
There ya go and now we're cooking 
Ha ha that's how you do it 
Slip it in and rotate 
War is all you need
