(I) Lies become my constant companion
and shining beacons my only (false) hope
someday without warning the flesh shall snap
and then in anger will the hammer fall down
decapitation
that is the word of the hour
the hope of the month
and the fantasy of the year
so says louie the fourteenth

(II) Mantra becomes this body's only friend
in a sea of self-doubt I cast my anchor into you
The sea becomes dead with stagnant salt
and my watch of the moon becomes monotonous
no longer in joy but in fear my grip consumes my hand
I used to be in the lover circle but now the sea is my only friend
Losing, losing in blackened respite no longer sought after
but now only laughed at
do not turn away from me for my mind goes blank
conform to the methods I have constructed for you
Do not consider anything that would limit my world
rather be the shallow people I have made you to be

(III) Technology becomes man's best friend
dehumanizes and categorize and circumcise
never look them in the eyes
feel their organic composition no more
rather concentrate on what makes them machinery
subnatural paradise becomes their guilty home
To bring them into destiny becomes our method of construction
Lead them to the death chambers
and cure the world of the lecherous virus (human breed).

(IV) Freedom becomes a dirty word
songs fill the voice where only silence should be
inhabit the unnatural
and fulfill the crying game
to those of the curiosity card I say this:
Destruction casting itself into a free–falling
formation of liberty
becomes our only approach into their tortured psyche
