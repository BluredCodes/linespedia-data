He stands over me 
pencil drawn back 
the class room watches as the terror grows 
the molesting begins 

another whipping 
another callous 
another denial 
they want to hang him without trial 

the pillars crumble 
the courts will not meter out justice 
it happens again and again 
how steeped they have come in sin 

flicker flame the sun shines down not for you 
but all those hiding beneath the rocks 
wishing to die and cannot 
wanting to fly yet buried beneath the trembles of sound 

the woman to believe 
she turns and looks at the thief 
no one knows her name 
and the establishment cuts her throat 

they said she knew so much 
and so she did 
they said we're looking for the good in society 
and so again they lie 

another attempt remains jutted against the sky 
the quest for popularity (have to have that vote) 
lies with the one who can control this their emotions 
and past generations weep in shame 

adrogney and audio survellance 
we close in on the mischeif makers 
and those who will not move 
will die for this 

our establishment
