my sweetheart's burning up the sky
fill my vision with nothing but smoke screen
can't believe all the confusion setting in
can't even see my eyes watering so much
heat and fire takes me down
burning and allowing my lungs to scream in agony
sharp intake of breath
a deafening sound feels my ears
the sounds of fire consuming human flesh
vast lively orange white coloured flames
oh I'm burning where's the water
why do you do nothing for me
can't you see my sweetheart's like fire
pour water on me sooth me heal me
everyone's leaving can't you see I'm on fire
don't forsake me its the time I need you most
and yet there they all trod away
ignoring me as I burn
here's to you, sweetheart like fire
elaboration of smoke and heat-like lovers
going to consume you all going to take you down.Written November 30th, 2001 ©  on Nov 29 2001 05:30 PM PST, Michael Edward London    nature
