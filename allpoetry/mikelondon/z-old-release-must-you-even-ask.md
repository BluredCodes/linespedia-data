Must you even ask, why must you question
how can you ask if I love you
in what way have I failed to show you this
what must I do to prove you are the most important one
you are the only one for me
you make me question myself
how can you even wonder how 
or consider the possibility of my love faltering
show me in what ways I have failed you
there is no one else I'd rather have by my side
you are the wind beneath my wings
it is because of you I can reach the heights unparalleled
without my life would be so much more poor
whenever I play a song I play it for you
whenever I consider beauty you're the absolute I measure it by
whenever I set down to write you're the one I write too
so how can you ask if I love you
is it not written all over my actions and deeds
by their fruits you shall know them
I want you to know I love you in the Corinthian meaning
everything you are is the most important to me
I want to help you lodge inseparable from the identity designed for you
