With wandering eye and restless mind
until the darkness and consumption we find
consumed with the wonder of wanderlust
fought hard and died young and turned into dust
ever fleeting jumping from town to town contagious
creating fear and dread yet love that we soon heal must
speaking softly and slowly the mind and voice of the sages
take the magicians and the sorcerers and the mages
a gentle man with a great long beard
around him and in him and out of him they all jeered and leered
Its here we see the Oliba
taken to strange places and seeing strange things
here in the darkness we think we have found ourselves
yet here we are
lying in the dark, next to Oliba
dying flying sighing crying living breathing meeting
meeting the ageless ones, meeting the unknowable
watching as they put Christ on the Crucible
And we just sit there in watch, for it is all part of the plan, the show
how and how and when and where we will never know
Take us home Oliba
It is destroyed Oliba tells us
Where do you live, Oliba
Everywhere and no where says Oliba
Stranded without home or fleeting moments to worthwhile to count
Lived and quickly forgotten is another day
yet here we stand, next to Olibah
Until we have lived here time out of mind [love sick listening too]
New members of the disorganized society enters into our chambers
