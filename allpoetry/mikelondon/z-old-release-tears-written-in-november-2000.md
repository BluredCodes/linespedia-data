I take her down
to

Tears 

I never take her down
to

Laughing

Whenever I see her
her face is wet

Tears

of course I love her
I just break her heart every day

Tears

Don't you see I'm the one abused
if it weren't for her I wouldn't be causing her to shed

Tears

I love you dearest
please don't cry any more

Tears

You know my feelings are true
pay no mind to

Tears

Don't you dare be a bitch
Don't ever think of causing me

Tears

If you do that
I'll make sure you always cry
