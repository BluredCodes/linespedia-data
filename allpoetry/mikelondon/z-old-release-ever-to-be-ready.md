will I ever to be ready
to go pack to that mindless place
can I endure the empty shattered halls
the pain we shared, that ancient memory stare
a time ago I thought I knew you
a crushing defeat a horrible realization 
hardened heart and no yielding to the oil
every now and again I want to ramble
go through and see what I can salvage
but every time I near that misery place
a block of ice comes over me and I cannot enter
why did you leave me I loved you so
and yet you turned me away with no second thought
there were so many things to accomplish and do
dare I ever to be ready to enter and see what I can save
between me and you nothing shall ever be whole
a chasm has opened never to be bridged
a cry to utter tears to fall never shall we see the other
to be separated at so early an age
tender embrace a loss of thyroid gland
what shall become of you now
where shall you go who shall you meet when and if will you love
a gift you destroyed a love loss rendered [rendered loss]
a shout to be heard a flying flock of sea gulls to be lured
a sob and shudder in the darkness nothing to be seen or felt
to touch you and only you and now you're taken away
a gift I never got to use, a love I never felt
