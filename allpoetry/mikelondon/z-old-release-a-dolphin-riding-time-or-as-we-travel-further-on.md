In the wasteland of my mind I see a dolphin riding time
standing swimming moving effortlessly across breakers
breaking through the surface clean and shear
slicing back into the watery depths of murky lore
I ask him where he's going he says he's got a job to do
and curious I follow him to see what the dolphin will go too
he is carrying a burden where to I know not
but one things for certain I follow or be shot
soon he is gone longer and longer in the watery depths
soon I see him less and less as we travel further on
And every time he disappears the water gets a little stranger
and soon very soon I see things unbidden come hither
in the surface terrors wait to take us down
but then my boat is strong and sturdy there's a dolphin riding time
and then I see a really bright light and its all over
the dolphin keeps a'goin' but my ship's a'sinkin'
in the end I grab a'hold of what the dolphin's draggin'
The dolphin's riding time, protecting it from the lime salt slime
in the end he'll take it to that farthest shore
and a trumpet will blow and the stars will fall
