You've got time, that's what they always say
but that's not true now, I won't be here another day
Used to think I could conquer the world
fight anything and beat anything they hurled
but now the time is over and its all through
against time you can't beat it or cause a coup
there's so much I did do but so much more
sometimes you have to wonder what this life is even for
I had a good life, with children and family named Camble
but now the time has come going to have to ramble
I don't want to leave there's so much more to see
but the door is locked and fastened I can't find the key
what was once so abundant time now is so fleeting
I thought I knew the path but I don't know where it was leading
thought I was going to be a writer I did publish a lot of books
somehow those always garnered me some quite nasty looks
and now everything is evaporating into many mysts
I know I'm going to heaven I know I made the lysts
but it seems there were things to do things I need to finish but still there's more I want to see and do and think
but now it seems everyone above is pulling rank
time to go home they say I know now there's no denying
I love you always please don't think I'm lying
just remember life is precious there's isn't time abundant
go now to the church go attend the service of Lint 
now I feel my life passing away from me
