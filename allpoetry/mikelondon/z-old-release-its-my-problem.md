It's my problem stop trying to live through me
I know how to handle things stop juicing me
you think you have to handle everything
don't you know you're not me so back off
I have some brains believe it or not
every time I try to do something you say I'm wrong
every time I think you'll be proud you just shake your head
what's the matter am I not perfect like YOU
who are you to control my life anyhow possible
manipulation is all you're good at
thought I'd scream from everything you say
but now I learn you just think I'm a failure
my life is my own now what you say doesn't matter
you think my life is problem-ridden they're my problems now
don't you hate it if I'm a failure you're a failure too
everything I know about you you try to live your life through me
well I'm here to tell you its my life not yours
accept me or reject me the choice is yours 
if you were a quarter good as you say you are this wouldn't be an issue
you grow up and learn to be the person you're supposed to be
I'd never give my life up to live your live
so stop trying to take control of mine!Written November 30th, 2001 ©  on Nov 29 2001 05:25 PM PST, Michael Edward London    contemporary  • contest  • dark  • humor  • love  • pain  • personal  • society  • spiritual
