Take me as I am
not as I shall be
Take me right now
no conditions
no precepts
no expectations
Take me as I am
I'll be yours forever
Don't try to change me
Love me
Don't try to criticize me
Love me
Do everything in love
Take me as I am
Love me with unfailing strength
Be strong when I am weak
Be my support when I can't go on
By loving me you'll take me higher
But only in love.Written November 30th, 2001 ©  on Nov 29 2001 04:45 PM PST, Michael Edward London    spiritual
