Blood and pathos I see everywhere
There is no escape, fear everywhere
My mind is fatiguing
Fogged with confusion
Maggots of the dust
Cloud my mind's eye
In the end where only the dead do lie
A life, normal though it may seem
Is only a road to insanity, the ending
Of a faerie tale dream
It is here, on the hands, these hands, my hands
I have taken the life of several
They called it insanity, I called it escape
The Judge could not see, the attorneys did not care
It was here, in the prison cell, I have come to terms
