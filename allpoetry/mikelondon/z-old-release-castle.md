He wants to let you tour his castle
the question is -- 
will you?

He wants you to tour his castle
perhaps it isn't a choice --
should you?

He NEEDS you to tour his castle
and all its SHINY slopes --
How can you not?

Everyday in every way he wants
Surely you must be a bitch
if you decline!

Surely you are not cruel
tour his castle.
What a line.

Will you?

Should you?
