Name of the Villain					11-28-2000

what's the name of the villain you ask
its whoever against us
no one should rule
but us
every one else is in terrible danger
we are the only ones in the right
who ever thinks they can rule
are traitorous and must be hanged
executed at dawn or at midnight one of the two
we are the establishment
and as the establishment we are always right
don't ever turn against us
don't ever make us arrest you
it's for your own good we rule
without us you wouldn't know who the villain is
don't be subversive
they try to break your mind with vain philosophies
we name the villain you are to hate them
that is your goal in life, that
and to obey us
if you turn against us betray God and Country
only we have the right to
Name the Villain11-28-2000,
