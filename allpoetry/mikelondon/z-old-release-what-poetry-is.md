Free verse thought in motion
a view into the mind's eye
endless possibilities random thoughts college
an attack on traditionalism
a vision of what is might be like
to be free & solitaire to play solitaire
a callous over dripping over flow without callous curve
a singular multi dimension expansion underwritten tool
everything that's wrong with society can be found
in the slipping of the mind
and skimming for homosexual content in Allen Ginsberg's brain cells
a dinosaur driven freaking eating cancer sore
condom for insinuation thought capes unadornes
we shall not have bastardized plans & ideas
Freeflowing imagery Candy Apple Red
a premeditation of salmon poultry escape
ordered pickling of a disordered mind
red hair salon pink finger toe nails
a genie figure with 12 fingers and 3 eyes
ever scrolling never blinking humanitarian animal
