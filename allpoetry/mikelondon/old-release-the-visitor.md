A friend has come to see you
I think that's what they say
as if from a distance another knock into my consciousness
no, do not enter, stray far away
but the persistence of their pleading
comes across in a damned shouting voice
hear me they say
the children do not acknowledge the visitor's truth
no one wants to look up from their tasks
and view this entity in all its splendour
no one's really sure why the visitor stands there
looking at me with that quite intensity
no one knows but me this visitor's true nature
so you want them, these offspring of mine
and the visitor nods that phantom head of his
laughing and showing that guttural tooth grin
fanged with hollow glory 
standing in the room the surroundings become confused
the children look from their tasks screaming in horror
their voices become ripped with emotion
stabbed with the ferocious will to survive
in the corner my eyes blind themselves momentarily
and the children have been taken
to the visitor i give all
and the incredulity of youth, once vibrant
