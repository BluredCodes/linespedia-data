The mineral men come mining
searching for any precious jewels
the mineral men come mining
they want you without regulations or rules
The mineral men come mining
looking for your brain
The mineral men come mining
they want to discover your pain
The mineral men come mining
what are they searching for
The mineral men come mining
to see if you've ever been tempted to touch a whore
The mineral men come mining
to look upon your face and smile
The mineral men come mining
to dig to your heart to make a race-way mile
The mineral men come mining
to feel your flesh and your love and kiss
The mineral men come mining
do not answer them with a his
The mineral men come mining
they want to learn of power
The mineral men come mining
to disappoint them all you have to do is cower
The mineral men come mining
to see how well developed is your spirit
The mineral men come mining
only to discover how you fear it refuse to hear it
The mineral men come mining
how you are always consumed without your own desires
The mineral men come mining
how quickly we become entrenched in the mires
The mineral men come mining
lean toward no pathway that will take you from me
The mineral men come mining
