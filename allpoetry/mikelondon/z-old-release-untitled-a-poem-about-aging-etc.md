Presently I'm staring at the mirror
And trying to discover my cut lips
How the blood flows
How the showers ooze into powerfully sterile minstrelsy
In which hope lies cannot be guessed
Nor can anger or fear overshadow the lies
We come into this world already suffering
And when deceased, the world no longer wonders of us
Or how long we've been traveling
Or how long we'll be dead
None give thought to aged (ag-ed) memories
The dust on the wall, compositions in black
Within without I keep wanting to sing
Just a little life would greatly aid our cause
No victory have we known only throttling intensity
Stop, no language can express this sentiment to our life
Stop, stop, stop, stop,
Yet the biological will not give out
And the spiritual will only laugh
We watch from a distance lovers dancing on the green
Some forgotten lawn now overgrown, choked with bristles and biers
Memories and bleeding becomes age's constant companion
When lives complete their transition from undead to dead
When youth becomes no longer occupied with their own constructed narratives
But instead face the immutable and forever silencing weapon
There many turn to some pursuit that no one really understands
That of wealth cannot fulfill
And that of communication no longer holds any interested
Watch the memories as they bleed out on the fallow ground of your mind
No longer occupied with the present, the descent into the past has only just yet begun
Here the dancing can go on forever
Here romance hangs in the air and the bodies form perfect unions
Sundown will not be for several hours
Nor will the moon, that damnable moon, shine it's horrid light upon our faces
Just a while ago, soon I shall return
