Everyone is fooled
by the happy mask I wear
no one ever really sees
the heavy burden that I bare
People never notice
The scars that taint my skin
Nobody truly cares about
The demons that live within

Nobody sees...
The blood that drips from my hand
Why are you so confused???
Don't shun me cause you don't understand
I am just a quiet sheltered child
Trapped within a tortured mind
While my soul is slowly strangled
By the contsraints that keeps my blinded

What does the mirror show you?
That it does not show to me
All I see is despair and ugliness
At what I cannot and will never be
Does your mirror truly lie to you?
Or do you lie to yourself and them
Are you so scared and weak
You can't admit from where they stem

Nobody sees...
The blood that drips from my hand
Why are you so confused???
Don't shun me cause you don't understand
I am just a quiet sheltered child
Trapped within a tortured mind
While my soul is slowly strangled
By the contsraints that keeps my blinded

The lies come from you
You are no different from me
I am just twisted and strong enough
To acknowledge what I really see
How are you happy being so weak???
