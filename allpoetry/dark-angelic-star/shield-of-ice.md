title: "Shield of Ice"

I watch your face you don't know..feelings from nothingthat you cause to growI fall faster awayat the speed of lightwhile your suspendedforever in time forever at nightthere's no warmth in youbehind your shield of icebut still I am warmed by sorrow and your spiteI'm cut by your presenceas you turn away insidewishing me awayso you can continue to hidebut you want my passionto share in my heat.you desire to give in to meif known defeat would be sweetso I'll continue to feelwhile you still turn awayhoping in time maybe you'll stay.
