---
title: "Kisses Left in Frost"
writer: "Dark AnGelic Star"

I wish I could find my angel
at my window pane this night
he would be so beautiful
and make everything all right
but I know when I waken
there'll be in frost his kiss
and footsteps that lead away
lost in the grasses dewy mist
why must he only be in my dreams
and leave me kisses at night
raise me up in fanciful hopes
and let me fall from such heights
I would be his firefly under glass
and I would never want to be free
if he would just promise to stay
and be my baby...  :)
