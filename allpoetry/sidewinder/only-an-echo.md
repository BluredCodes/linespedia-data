title: "Only An Echo"

To cling within the heart
	fulfilling those immeasurable times
	held only...
	within an echo,
	I reach into a soul
	that embraces all of my feelings
	held within....
	only an echo,
	Forever met in dreams
	where those passions rise,
	I speak your name
	only to say hello
	in the summer,
	And in the winter
	say farewell
	as darkness falls
	and passion stilled
	within my heart,
	So let's gently meet
	between the shadow and light
	never forgetting our communion
	held only in the silence of our morning.
	April 18, 2000
	By; B.E. Whitehorn & Phillip Bell
