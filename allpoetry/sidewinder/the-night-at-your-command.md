title: "The Night At Your Command"

Another cup of coffee
and the hunger for a simple conversation,
Anyone will do,
Simple pleasures are taken for granted,
But what is one to do when sleep doesn't come?
Sometimes it seems a never ending circle,
But you have to grab on
and take life by the reins,
Never be contented by the cards
dealt to you,
Take the risk and reshuffle your hand,
Sometimes lady luck smiles
and the gambling pays off
in ways you never imagined,
Keep the faith
for dreams do come true,
Held by a hungry imagination,
Shared by those who care
and touch a tapestry of forbidden tales
of those forgotten
but remembered for different reasons,
And the sandman brings a final step
with the night at your command.

July 17, 1996
