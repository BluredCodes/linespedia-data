title: "In A Smile"

Silent melodies
                  found in forgotten doorways
where a whisper of a voice
pretends to care,
Vague in that completeness,
Deliberate in that covenant
of time,
Followed only...
by the slip of light,
Defined...
in that gentle shadow
contented just to be met
sorrow by sorrow
where in that afterthought
the spectator
                         indulges
                                  in a smile.

Dec.10, 2001
