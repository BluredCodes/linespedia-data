---
title: "Touch A Heartbeat"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "touch-a-heartbeat"

Bodies dancing
cheek to cheek,
Feel the heat,
Could it be more
than what some see?
Reach inside yourself
and touch a heartbeat,

Open your eyes
to
really see
the
special place
where two
hearts can be,
Open and secure
both are very sure
as
they embrace,
They share
the same space,
So within those visions
a soothing of the soul
touches upon a nights desire
sensual in its completeness,
And in its silence
passion burns in a rhythm
forever met in ecstasy.

June 22, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Laura Wisterias
