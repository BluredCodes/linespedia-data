title: "Twilight Serenade"

Another heart touches
forever filling a rush,
Could it be
that you see...
a twilight serenade?
An angel rises
in eyes
completing a circle of time
held forever within my mind
and within the revelation
a smile lights up
within a need
simple in a touch of magic,
Petal rise,
Petals Fall,
But you can hear the echoes
of a voice that touches all,
Quiet in its madness
like a winged sadness
betwixt the shadows that fall.

Feb. 13, 2001
