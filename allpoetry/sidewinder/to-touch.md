title: "To Touch"

To touch
the generations that have yet to come
and in my mind...
there's a realization
that time holds a key,
Finding it is the problem,
And making the best of it...
is the answer,
And though all of the changes
that we find,
If you can grasp the caught
in the quiet,
Hold those seasons in the sun,
Then you fare
much better than I have,
But that doesn't keep me
from trying,
Still life
in general...
is an adventure
just for the undertaking.

May 9, 1999
