title: "In The Midst Of The Night"

In the midst of the night
the essence of a heartbeat
drew me like a moth is drawn to the light
of a fire to his very death,
Somehow...
I knew that was what I wanted,
To be needed 
held only in emotion
and in that touching
her essence & love
created a bond
that can not be broken
at whatever the cost,
Damn the consequences!
Somehow I had found
that soul that I needed in my life,
And what will be
I know not!
But we'll see what the future brings,
And we will brave those future
chances
in an understanding that we'll both share.
