You say that love is just a game,
There are no winners or losers,
Just memories,
But that isn't true,
Because love means more than that,
People are involved,
And no matter how you piece it together
there's bound to be feelings,
Anger and hurting
of losing you,
Wretched joy,
Blues...
Living without you,
yes sweet emotions
that we shared
brings reflecting memories,
still water ponds
echoed in the shimmering
yet varied immortally
wherein your smile
touches in a threshold
magically awakening my soul.

April 28, 2000
