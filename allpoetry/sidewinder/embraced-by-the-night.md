title: "Embraced By The Night"

Embraced by the night
where few dare go,
Harkened by the sight
of a shining soul,
Frightened by the darkness
of misunderstood mind,
Blinded by the kindness
of a simple open hand,
Chilled by the touch
of a silence never heard,
Overwhelmed by the rush
of a moment counted by a word,
And in the heat of passion,
Torn by love,
Comes the world
full circle,
Just to find it
is like starting over again

June 1, 1996
