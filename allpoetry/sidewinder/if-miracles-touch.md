---
title: "If Miracles Touch"
writer: "sidewinder"

If miracles touch those inconsistencies,
Then your smile
opens doors
held only
by time,
And my presence
hopefully is missed
when I'm not with you,

Forever my mind touches
those fantasies
arisen in times past,
I have to be careful
not to get lost
where you're concerned,
You never know
what might be revealed,

Hurry up and touch the sunshine,
For those memories 
reflect more then what your eyes
can say in a thousand lifetimes,
Could it be that a revelation
is just around the corner?

Come let me touch the stranger inside,
Look past the differences
and see what I see,
Maybe a friend might be waiting?

I want you to remember my face,
Maybe the joy that I hold inside
reveals my child,
But in the memory
the adult holds the strength
to remember a time shared,

Tendencies that attract the unbroken,
But to find this,
You must search for the child inside,
Where I remain inside your arms
to touch past my tears
only celebrated within a kindness.

Nov. 13, 1999
