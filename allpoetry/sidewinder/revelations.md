Through the tired shadows
of a prevailing image
created through the charade
of reality
consisting of a crusade
of promise
forged by power
of rage
restless with a constant
urge to be free
with a heart held in check,
Never again will the chains
hold my restless soul,
Yet amongst the powers that be,
A key
releases a purity
that shines forever the stars.

Aug. 15, 1996
