title: "Where Some Forget"

Mother,
	In your eyes
	you find that...
	I'm wasting my time,
	Yet...
	the words that I find
	give me the comfort that I need,
	Why...
	can't you see
	that these dreams
	mean...
	so much to me?
	They give me pleasure
	where...
	in your mind
	you only see the emptiness,
	Look behind the blindness
	and find that joy
	where I smile,
	Maybe in time
	you'll find those
	lessons that I've learned so well,
	Maybe it's meant to be
	that I teach
	those crossroads
	and show some happiness
	where some forget
	to see the flowers along the way.
	Nov. 2, 2000
