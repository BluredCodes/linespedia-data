---
title: "With A Simple Look"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "with-a-simple-look"
source: "AllPoetry"

With the light of the stars
and the moon
time speaks
of those journeys and quests
met only in a revelation
where enlightenment touches
your imagination,
Awakens your soul,
Transcending your thoughts
to a world of indescribable
yearning and desire,
Proclaiming to your heart
the gift of love and the gift of life
undoubtedly you fall into it,
Touch those arcane secrets
devoted in a heartbeat
where the wind blows 
a caress of a tear,
And with a simple look
you tell more than what you could with words,
Somehow you touch those dreams,
And so we complete each other
like the freedom
that a bird finds in flight,
Isn't it funny what we find in love,
Some spend a lifetime searching for.

May 8, 2001
