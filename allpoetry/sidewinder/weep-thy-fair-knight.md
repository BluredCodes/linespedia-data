title: "Weep Thy Fair Knight"
writer: "sidewinder"

Weep 
thy fair knight
forsworn in those days
where chivalry rises
holding honor in the twilight,
But some have forgotten it,
This torn and ragged society 
lives in the philosophy of
what's mine is mine
& what's yours is mine.
 Why?
You would think that a muse
would teach some in wisdom.
But no...
It seems to be lost in the wind
and their
 kindness made by meek souls
is lost in the shadow.
 So I see
those tears which rage
in eternity
& my heart touches
emotion to emotion.
