title: "If I Had It To Do Over Again"

If I had it to do over again,
I'd celebrate each day
as if it was the last,
Find joy
in whatever I do,
Find laughter with those friends
who complete me,
Do away
with the pain
that makes me sad,
Be grateful
for the family...I have,
Explore those mysteries, 
Travel to those lands
I've never seen,
Honor the wisdom
heard in a whispering brook,
Invoke the magic
found in harmony,
Find the song
in the sunlit memory,
Whisper those messages
found only in silence,
transform thy heart to meet each day
with perfect bliss.

Dec. 15, 2001
