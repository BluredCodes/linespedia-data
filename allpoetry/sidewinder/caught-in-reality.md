---
title: "Caught In Reality"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "caught-in-reality"
source: "AllPoetry"

Blowing like gentle breeze
fresh forever in a moment of time,
Caressed by a memory
but cradled by a single hope
that is flickered like flame of a candle,
And if one listens,
 One hears a whisper,
A prayer,
A cry in the night,
And if one keeps an open heart,
Maybe a prayer is answered,
But only if one keeps forever
held tightly,
Precious to those who understand,
Blind to those who don't,
Lost forever in the wind,
Scattered in the four corners
of a world caught in reality.

May 9, 1996
