Shot by shot,
             Touch those balls
                          into an impossible shot,
Somehow you run into a wrong angle,
                                   Blocked by the wrong color,
Then where do you go?
                        A combination?
A multiple angle shot?
                        Top english?
        Bottom english?
                          Jump shot?
The possibilities are endless,
                                The choices are many,
I know...
           don't miss!
How in the heck did he make that?
                                 But there it goes...
right in the pocket,
                     Looks like somebody...
                                          better...
                                                   rack em' up!
And there the game goes
                       over and over again,
Anybody for eight ball?

Jan. 5, 2002
