Dancing through those charms
came a brown eyed stranger
with a smile
mystified by the night
whispering dreams 
celebrated in a heart at a time,
Laughter heard only in the shadows,
On comes the rain,
But those rhythms touch
in the most unlikely places
crumbling away my soul,
Safety be damned!
I want to take a risk,
Hold hands on the street
and let love take us where it will,
Tomorrow matters not,
Let's live for today,
Life's unexpected pleasures
must take place,
Monday afternoons met by new shiny eyes,
Wide awake to meet what it can.

Sept. 20, 2000
