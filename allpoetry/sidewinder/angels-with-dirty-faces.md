title: "Angels With Dirty Faces"
writer: "sidewinder"

Tears for those so young,
They gave so much,
Yet in this much abused world,
Misunderstood,
Only to die by their own hands,
Why?
No one knows,
Still they are remembered
each time their music is played,
We remember.

You touched us with your feelings
in each song, 
The tears you cry are felt,
Angels in the bright lights
never surrender your spirit,
For a part of you lives in each of us,
And through some dark night
a candle is lit for your memory.

Maybe in another lifetime
we'll meet
and sing those songs
that you gave your life to sing,
And forever there will be harmony,
I hold my hand up to you.

Aug. 3, 1985
