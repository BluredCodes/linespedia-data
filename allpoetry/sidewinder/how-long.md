No...
I don't hate my fellow man,
That isn't my way,
But I am what I am,
Can I change the color of my skin?
No...
But I can call you my friend,
How long will it take
for others to understand?
I don't know,
But maybe just a little bit
will be great for man,
But no matter what steps are taken,
Forward is the only direction to be taken,
And eventually...
Love will conquer all.

May 5, 1981
