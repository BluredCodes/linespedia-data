In the arrival of darkness
sheltered mysteries prevail,
Oh desolate one
embrace each with a song
imminent in thy shadow,
 Revealing the closing of this life,
 Reading the angle of life 
for the next,
Interconnecting a natural process,
A chain linking both
in a balance of time,
Both the rising sun
and the horizon meets in the undertone,
And yet the next year follow on
therefore completing the cycle.

Aug. 12, 2000
