---
title: "In The Eyes Of Arthur"
writer: "sidewinder"

source: "AllPoetry"

The eyes of time,
A rave to those who
see Arthur for who he is,
A memory gallantly revered,
Preserved in an ancient past,
A splendor in realms
where dragons & knights
exist forever,
Images shine by poets and bards
who praise his chronicles,
Sacred in places of the heart,
Kindred to alike souls,
Cosmic in its spirit.

June 23, 1997
