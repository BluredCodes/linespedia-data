title: "Solitary Times"

Reach into a thought
that touches into the seasons
where eyes celebrate the blue of the skies,
The brown and oranges list within the tree of autumn,
The greens that mire into the prisms of Spring,
And last but not least
the frost that rises into winter herself
unmistakable in a solitary time,
An anthology within a shadow
held in Gaia's tears,
Prime in a full moon.

May 11, 1999
