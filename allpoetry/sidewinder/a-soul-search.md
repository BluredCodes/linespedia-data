title: "A Soul Search"

Black in truth
laughter heard beyond the whisper
forsworn in a hidden dream
mirrored by those forgotten
cascading in winds pervaded
by a thought driven in focus
mediated behind a pureness
resolved in a consciousness 
considered,
Brought on in realities
prime in the fullness
to those never realising
where doors that have opened
inviting more than one can see,
Senses shared
forever blind in realms
closed, 
Traces in a familiar emotion,
Spiritual to those that reach
beyond
hidden in the frailness of time.

Feb. 13, 1997
