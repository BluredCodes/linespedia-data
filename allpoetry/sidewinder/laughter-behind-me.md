title: "Laughter Behind Me"

Laughter behind me
while the saxophone
touches the poetry within me,
Imminent in a sense
where a soul
is touched
 calling me home,
Pages in a lifetime
satisfied only by a muse,
Which whispers secrets
spiraled in a vision,
Entwined in those dreams 
which tell me the truth,
Some say that it's madness,
But you tell me who's crazier,
One who lives in this world of reality
forgetting how to have fun,
Or one who doesn't have a care in the world
and makes his dreams reality.

Sept. 24, 2000
