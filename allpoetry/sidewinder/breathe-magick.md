title: "Breathe Magick"

Look into your soul
find that joy everlasting,
Eyes that reveal...
only a completeness,
Drawing the spirit
which dances
like a faerie in the twilight
serenaded by the magic
found in the horizon,
Betwixt the light and the shadow
mystical in a heartbeat,
Can't you feel it?
Sense the beacon that calls you,
Transcending those miracles
quiet in the chaos
where we find magick
in the echo
solitary in its path,
Twisted only by the silence
of a dream.

Nov. 22, 2000
