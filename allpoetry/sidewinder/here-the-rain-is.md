title: "Here The Rain Is"

Here the rain is
touching someone
with windblown eyes,
Sheltered only by a smile,
Forgotten...
is the loneliness
shattered in the shadows,
Where is the question
that answers all
in this complicated world?
Held in the hope of dreams,
Some unfulfilled,
But more are revealed
in the listening of time.

April 26, 1999
