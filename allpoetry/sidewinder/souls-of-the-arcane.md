title: "Souls Of The Arcane"
writer: "sidewinder"

Just an illusion
creeping into your mind,
Serrated by a cut at a time,
Pain in an illusion...
varied in the thrux of time,
Innocence is lost...
Paradise is forgotten,
shadows mire step by step,
Voices deepen
in the abyss,
Calling...
Yet there is no help...
only...
an empty echo,
Silence touches in rage,
Flaws in humanity
howl in the wind,
Beckoning those souls of the arcane,
Restless in their welcome.

Oct. 13, 2000
