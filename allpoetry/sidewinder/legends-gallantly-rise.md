title: "Legends Gallantly Rise"

Awaken to the mysteries,
Traditions follow
a cycle enriched in legend,
Spirits rise gallantly
to touch on a miracle,
Intuition held in metaphysical insight,
The light rises in the moonlight
and mingles with glances of those
who are enriched with magic,
Those visions of the otherworld
which teaches us wisdom,
Rich in songs and tales
of those who were heroes and heroines
forgotten in the age of Avalon,
Pure of heart,
Golden with honey,
Sweet in its own fulfillment,
A sign of the faerie,
Those circles of champions
held in a rage of silence.

Nov. 31, 1997
