---
title: "Chronicles Of Dreams"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "chronicles-of-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44392-Chronicles-Of-Dreams-by-sidewinder"
---

Echoes of the past
rising from the spirits
touched in rituals
forever embraced in magic,
Those chronicles
that reach into a heart
forever blessed by The Goddess herself,
Seasons which enrich
those passages into the dawn,
 Dark heavens mired
in the blood of those who are brave in heart
fulfilling their own epic,
Journeys traveled in the way of Avalon,
Epilogues which touch in between myths & the truth,
Rays illuminating visualizations which
awaken transitions of the mind,
Pulses of the heart
which fulfill a vision
in the realm of dreams.

Jan. 26, 1998
