---
title: "Will The Story Be Forgotten?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "will-the-story-be-forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39868-Will-The-Story-Be-Forgotten--by-sidewinder"
---

Words rant and rave
like a ceaseless magpie
too absorbed in one's self
to really matter,
Forever kept
in an illusion after images
that seem to run together
like shadows in the night
that are only held by a memory,
Cherished by a hope,
Resolved to a prelude
of experiences and excursions,
Rushed by a voyage
into a land that holds
a thousand adventures,
Only to have been forgotten,
But...
has it been forgotten?
No!
If imagination remains,
The story will always
unfold.

Sept. 17, 1996
