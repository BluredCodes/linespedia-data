---
title: "Listen To The Voice"
writer: "sidewinder"

A masquerade
for the time of magic
celebrated in the seasons
touched by the heart
of a chosen few,
Discover those hidden influences that
impact your life,
Fulfill that journey guided by the
path that you have chosen,
Rise like the phoenix
who dies a glorious death
and then is reborn
fulfilling his destiny,
Touch the spirit within you,
Follow those doors that are open to you,
Heat the old wounds that bind you,
Walk through the veils that blind you,
Reawakened the joy that is reflected in your face,
Listen to the voice that has taught you wisdom,
Nurture the love that is within your heart,
For the vision that you seek
is celebrated by the magic within this world.

Oct. 28, 1997
