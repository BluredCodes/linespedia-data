---
title: "The Epic Of Flight"
writer: "sidewinder"

High in flight like a bird,
You see through their eyes,
You feel their excitement of freedom,
And it induces you to want more,
The dreams that you share
keeps you in being there
forever.

Sept. 2, 1984
