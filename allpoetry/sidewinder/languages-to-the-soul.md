title: "Languages To The Soul"

Telepathic languages to the soul
lost long ago,
Yet...
a reawakening
touches one
ancient in legend,
Spirits of long ago
tells a story
so vivid
in the imagination
of the great gap
to the generation
as they connect
in that space where some forget,
Remembering those past lives,
Completing those lessons 
that a heart needs,
Asking questions that forever meet
only in its answer,
The answer of life
and the souls that have passed on
to the unknown,
For this lesson reveals answers
if we know ourself,
We understand the past
and of the great things
that have come before us,
Teaching us the wisdom that we need,
Showing us patience
which sometimes is somehow forgotten,
But in that simplicity
we share the sun.

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Adrennia Cooper
