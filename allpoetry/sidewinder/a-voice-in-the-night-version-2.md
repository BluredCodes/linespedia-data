The voice of love calls
in the winds of
nightfall,
calling out to the people
within the valley depths
she touches in a song
varied only in time,
Can you hear her lull
within that harmony
that touches in magic?
She sings the melody
of a lover's heart,
When first two souls
fell in love.

Magical lyrics
summon the night,
Within the silence of dusk,
Upon a mystical night,
A full moon appears,
The dance of souls
begin,
Look into love's eye,
Tell me what you see,
Pure bliss,
Miracle of time,
Listen...
Tell me what you hear,
Love's melody in the night?

Love gazes into the wind,
A window into the world of magic & love,
Her lyrical voice
beckons to the writer,
Tell the story of magic
in the night,
Reveal dreams & fantasies
mirrored in promises,
Where a lady can find
the man of her dreams,
her soulmate who will
take her to the promised land,
Where chivalry still touches
and a knight finds courage
herd in tales told by a bard.

Mystery & intrigue
felt in the soul,
those whispers
revealed past midnight
of the joy that pampers the heart,
Mystery & fantasies of the mind,
Where a man stands in the wind
glimpsing the lady he loves
calling to her in the night,
She will hear his dreamy voice
carried across time & space,
Splendor will rise
sweet & innocent in the wind
that whispers only in the night.

Nature takes its course.

As two souls entwine
in a spiral dance,
They will celebrate
the miracle of ages,
As the veil of night
blankets the Earth,
Passion's song howls in the wind,
While the moon smiles in the night,
A wolf cries to the beating of a lover's heart.

If you listen with your heart,
You'll hear the voice
of love in the night,
telling lover's to dream.

If you will believe in magic
her voice can be heard eternally,
So when the night arises,
Listen for the voices
within the night.
