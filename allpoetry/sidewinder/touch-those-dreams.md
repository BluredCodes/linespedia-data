Touching those dreams
written by some
with pen and paper,
beloved by some,
reaching a heart,
Syncopated....
                   beat
                         by beat,
                                  Beat
                                        to beat,
And in that rhythm
time celebrates
a golden pause
forever remembering,
Isn't it funny...
that some experiments
blossom and grow
touching what it needs to?

May 15, 2001
