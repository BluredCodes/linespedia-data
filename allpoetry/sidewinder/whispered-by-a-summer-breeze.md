title: "Whispered By A Summer Breeze"

Sunrises meet
only in your eyes
finally the light arrives
and the innocent
completes a miracle,
Jasmine fills the air
when you arrive,
Yet my heart
jump starts in a season of time,
Promises touch
when an encounter begins,
That mystery reveals a smile
only you can complete,
A chronicle can be written,
But why?
For you meet that distance
where two hearts meet,
And in turn
I find those secrets
whispered by a summer breeze.

Sept. 24, 2000
