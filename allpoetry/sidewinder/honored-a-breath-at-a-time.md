title: "Honored A Breath At A time"
writer: "sidewinder"

Dark sails touch against the sky
	where a wind blows
	intense in a summer day
	existing between
	the horizon,
	
	A cloak, a wrapping,
	A warm spun cloth
	staining the strapping
	stripping away her black wrath
	in the blue uncharted waters
	Wind in her hair, wild,
	eyes wide and wet,
	She.... no longer a child
	must let go of her regret,
	a journey, a quest, a retreat at best,
	
	And as she thinks
	what will be ahead...
	Will I...
	meet my dreams?
	Find my fortune,
	Or perhaps..
	my fame,
	
	Could it be
	that I find
	my one and only true love,
	Or will the world
	find much more for me,
	
	Find that truth
	that when sought
	is hidden...
	right before my eyes
	revealed only in time,
	And in that promise
	revelations meet to a heart
	celebrated in that completeness
	honored a breath
	at a time.
	

	Dec. 18, 2001
	
	By: B.E. Whitehorn & Sasha Sargeant
