---
title: "What Is Love?"

As a bird flies
soaring through the wind
free and untouched,
So is our love,
For what is love? 
Deep emotions that arise
to thy heart,
And if nurtured
like a seed first planted,
It grows like a rose
beautiful and unsurpassed,
For love is more
than anyone can imagine,
It must be held tightly
and forever kept.

March 16, 1982
