---
title: "Innocence Found"

Elegant
within that longing,
To touch only
where a smile reaches,
And I hear...
a laughter
only hidden
by the rush of the wind,
That second nature
reaches you
only...
in a innocence,
Where you give
a breath of life
to a heart
that yearns...
to be found,
But somehow you never
even notice it.

Sept. 24, 2001
