title: "Lost....In That Original Communication"

I wanted to touch those dreams
and songs that are forever
trapped within the mind,
As I listen to my muse,
I speak in words
plainly understood by the common man
or woman,
But as a poet
spoken...
Somehow my words
were somehow... lost,
The abstractions 
                   sometimes takes over
and the listener
                is left
                          wondering
What the hell did he mean?
                              Could it be...
that sometimes that connection
made...
            is as elusive
                          as a butterfly
flying as the wind flows
                           and we as communicators
                                                    get trapped...
Forgetting the purpose
                         originally intended?

Aug. 10, 2001
