title: "Brilliant Wonderings"

Wondering in time,
Seeking the rhythm of consciousness,
Anticipating what can be
an enchanting realm,
Exploring foremost
an eye in the astral,
To touch with an age long forgotten,
Mirrored only
by a specialness,
A hidden world within,
Listen where some do not,
Understand what some refuse,
Seek and learn what the silent teach,
Remember and take not for granted
gifts given for free,
Bizarre as it seems,
But brilliant as the stars.

Jan. 24, 1999
