title: "Hidden Only By The Shadow"

To touch those
dreams...
held in the promise
of time,
To feel
in secret echoes
hidden only
by the shadow
where laughter
finds your smile
and only by a memory,
Will the rhythm
find its place?

Oct. 11, 1999
