---
title: "Gentle Hello's"
writer: "sidewinder"

I need the gentle touch
that reaches to a soul,
Familiar in your presence
where a welcome is felt,
the warmth of your smile
brightens a day
to one who resides in solitude,
And the distance between two worlds
runs a little closer,
Perhaps...
the dark is no longer frightening
when those hello's are privately met.

May 15, 1999
