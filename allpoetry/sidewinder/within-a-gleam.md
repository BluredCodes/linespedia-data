---
title: "Within A Gleam"

Within a gleam of gold
wisdom rises fulfilling a promise
along an unknown path
to an understanding
tranquil in its truth,
celebrated in joy,
Generous to a fault,
One of heart
and one of mind,
Welcomed to those
familiar with enchantments
which reaches to an uncertain fate,
And so begins a trial of head, hand and heart
piercing in the change of the world,
Proficient in the pathwork
that aligns one with energies
which compliment each other
in visions forging a link
between mind and soul.

Feb. 3, 1998
