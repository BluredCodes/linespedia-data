---
title: "Quiet In Its Madness"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "quiet-in-its-madness"

Holding those dreams
everlasting...
                 ever touching....
ever claiming...
only that love
celebrated in the heart,
Touch those quiet places
private
                    but finally lived,
Closer in those eyes
                      that see
                                 a lifetime
                                            quiet in its madness
slipping
            a moment at a time.


April 10, 2001
