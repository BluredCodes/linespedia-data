---
title: "My Car"
writer: "sidewinder"

Old blue friend we've traveled far you and me,
And we've seen good times and bad,
Through these times I've trusted you,
You know...
You've never let me down,
I know...
You ache to travel again,
But you wait patiently,
And one of these days we'll go,
Just as soon as I get your starter back together,
Still as a friend,
 I thought I'd let you know that 
I'm thinking of you,
When I get into you
the world passes by so smoothly,
And as I let your windows down,
The winds flies through my hair,
It's then I feel fine,
And I've got you to thank,
You'll always by my friend forever.

April 9, 1985
