title: "Fortunes Of The Mind"
writer: "sidewinder"

Changes and chances,
	Contradictions of an over imagination,
	Clarifications and excuses,
	Much to do,
	But it seems that nothing is getting done,
	Looking for a balance,
	But Murphy's law is taking place,
	When will it ever end?
	I just want something good to happen,
	But it seems tragedy always follows,
	I guess what you receive
	sometimes is what you give,
	So open mindedness is called for,
	Patience sometimes is worth more than a virtue,
	For the result can be seen day to day,
	Can it be said,
	When one is looking for the fortunes of the mind?
	June 19, 1996
