A baby is born,
You lose your glasses on your head,
Friends meet for the first time,
You help a friend in need,
They help you and make you laugh,
That last inch that wins the gold,
Love at first sight,
Problems solved,
Hate is extinguished,
A heart touched by the sunrise,
Those sheltered dreams that we forever hold,
Dolphin's songs beyond a horizon,
A child's laughter,
Learning to ride your first two wheeler,
An "A"...
             on a test,
Math,
A shared thought,
Self-esteem established,
The look in a child's eyes when they receive a puppy,
The feeling you had when first saw that puppy,
Four wheeling,
Hiking,
Biking,
A purr from your favorite cat,
A song that touches your heart,
A warm sunny day,
Family reunions,
Magic happens.

Nov. 18, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Allan Perry
