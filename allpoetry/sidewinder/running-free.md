Youth running free,
	Caring nothing about tomorrow
	only for the moment,
	Groups holding friendship,
	Touching those new lives
	where the wind blows,
	Different directions
	only to behold
	each in their own,
	Seeing everything
	differently,
	Unique in its own meaning,
	Strange to some...
	But to others
	found in common understanding,
	Why is it...
	that some reach for the stars
	without knowing
	that they've already found them?
	May 25, 2001
