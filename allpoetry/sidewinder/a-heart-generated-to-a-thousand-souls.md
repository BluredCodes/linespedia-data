Balloons rising
deep within the sky
shared among those that hold
dreams in a sun filled rainbow,
Look at the colors,
Rich and alive!
Somehow you can almost touch them,
reach with your hands
and feel the promise of your creator,
Tremble with the sensation
of a heart generated
to a thousand souls,
Perhaps...
when you close your eyes
the images become so real
that time stands still,
And in that moment
a universe is born.

Sept. 26, 1996
