---
title: "And Tell Me Hello"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "and-tell-me-hello"

Pleasured in dreams
thy touch
reach beyond thy eyes,
Held only in a breath
I feel your sigh,
And in the nightwind
your embrace connects with my soul,
I'm told that sometimes an echo can complete
one if only those hearts can touch a new language,
Nectar in time
fulfills those needs
meant by a hello,
And your warmth meets my needs,
Look in my eyes...
and tell me hello.

Aug. 27, 2000
