Waiting in the horizon
	for your eyes to find me,
	Yet for an instant...
	your smile in an understanding
	finds the gentleness that I need,
	New hopes...
	                          New dreams...
	found in the sun,
	Where we start in new beginnings,
	Can we find the warmth
	that we need of love?
	Nov. 23, 2000
