---
title: "As Two Souls Become One"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "as-two-souls-become-one"

The violets,
            The roses
are touched in part of you
that I see in my heart,
Those midnight rendezvous
shared in a petal of time,
Moment to moment 
                      I see you
and somehow my world is not the same,
Those thoughtful little notes that you leave
I treasure like
                    a poem left from
                                     Emily Dickinson
soft and romantic,
                   And in my mind
we embrace each other
                          slowly,
And we devour each other
                           in passionate love,
Hunger met,
               Until the next day
where we begin again,
                      And touch as two souls
become one.

Jan. 14, 2002
