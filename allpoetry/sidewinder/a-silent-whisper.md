title: "A Silent Whisper"

And at night they walked
through those dreams
touched in an essence
varied in time
wherein a vision celebrates
resigned only in an echo,

An echo heard through ages,
A silent whisper grasped at
by one and all,
Those who
hold on to the dream live
to find all yet nothing,

Serenaded through those illusions
reawakened by a heartbeat,
Touch moment by moment
to find a miracle,
Fulfilled in complement
found only in their eyes,

Those pools so deep and inviting,
I want to fall,
To be swallowed in their depths
yet that first step is the hardest,
Should I trust?
Should I allow myself to
be taken once again?

Yet....
Nothing ventured,
Nothing gained,
So let's complete each other
like two sides of a mirror
entwining, spiraling
forever in a dance.

Oct. 22, 2000
