---
title: "Eyes Through A Rose"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "eyes-through-a-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

Varied in the love I see,
Held only in the dance of life
which rages in all of its complexities,
Visions of poems,
Yes...
those eyes seen through a rose,
a perfume scented
among your arrival
held past midnight,
a spiral dance in the southwest
sacred in this whirling circle
among heaven
where I hold you
and feel your shiver,
I see the laughter in your eyes
magnificent and simple,
I don't need tomorrow
only today,
Can you feel the wind's passion
in a heartbeat at a time?

Dec. 4, 2000
