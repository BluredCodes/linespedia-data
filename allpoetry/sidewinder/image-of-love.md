Dream on my sweet love,
For the fantasies that you weave
clings for my heart,
But alas...
Celebrations
in harmony
transform a spirit forgotten,
Yet remembered,
And compassion teaches those
who need the night,
Where is the covenant
in each one's arms
felt in silence?
Does the magic reveal time
held only in your eyes?
Or is the afterthought
touched in revolutions?

April 12, 1999
