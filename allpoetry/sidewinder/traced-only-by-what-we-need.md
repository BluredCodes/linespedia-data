They say it's in your kiss,
Touch me from the rhythms of your soul,
And I find the caring in your eyes
while we watch the sun rising in the coming of the next day,
And our own private meetings contact heart to heart,
Measured by the give and take of life,
Revealed in a completeness
of a freedom of love,
Let's seek those emotions
and touch what we can,
Modest by the curves of our bodies
sheltered
in our journey
where a summer follows season after season,
Traced only by what we need.

Sept. 19, 2000
