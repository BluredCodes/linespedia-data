---
title: "Somehow With You"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "somehow-with-you"

Only you can touch the silence
                                          within my heart
forever completing a magic
                             raging endlessly,
 Could you agree to share a covenant 
                                    and let the compassion show?
 I must confess that my existence
                            would be less without you around,
Somehow with you by my side
                                  I'm unafraid to meet my challenges.
Shadows and storms come on ahead!

Dec. 8, 1999
