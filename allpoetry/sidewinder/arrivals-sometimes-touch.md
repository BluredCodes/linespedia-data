---
title: "Arrivals Sometimes Touch"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "arrivals-sometimes-touch"

Autumn is coming,
The cold is sure to come,
I must be ready,
Otherwise it'll catch me by surprise,
Bundle up when the need occurs,
Do the little things that need to be done,
Before I know it the snow will come
and another year passes on,
I need to find a warm heart
to get me through,
Passions met through dreams
just doesn't hack it,
I close my eyes,
Hoping that I'll meet her in the next day,
Patience sometimes wears thin,
Loneliness isn't always what
I would want anyone to go through,
Arrivals sometimes touch when
you least expect them.
