---
title: "Seek Those Truths"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "seek-those-truths"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26386-Seek-Those-Truths-by-sidewinder"

Seek those truths
varied within the hearts of all,
Celebrated in the brightness of the full moon,
Heightened by the rippled waves,
When serenity turned violet then blue,
the fiery soul, the earth and the wind,
And in that completeness
where in the distance
a smile is met,
Satisfied by those who reach
but for those too blind to see
the shadow within the veil
and the truths beneath the smile,
Remember to touch
a magic revealed
only in the eyes
where the wind whispers
a truth the soul can find,
A simple smile that touches the heart,
Comprehends the shadow
the truth behind the eyes
beneath the truth one desired.

May 18, 2000

B.E. Whitehorn & Ross H. Bruce
