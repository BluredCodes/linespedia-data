title: "Treasures Kept"

Court of dreams
in thy eyes
a pleasure, 
For what is a measure of a man?
Only one can tell from
the life he lives
by the way he lives it,
Roads of illusion
can sidetrack those who are not aware,
But truth can reveal
hidden agendas
that rise before all of us
crystal clear
in a moment of time,
That golden apple of opportunity
appears for those that are worthy,
For a fool only rushes...
past,
And is certain what can be held in hand,
yes...
forgets or takes for granted
treasures given freely
measured forever in the heart.

Feb. 20, 1997
