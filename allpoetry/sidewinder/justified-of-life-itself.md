title: "Justified Of Life Itself"

Howl to the wolf,
	Hear the cry?
	Tasting of life itself,
	Oh Mother Earth....
	                         Father Sky,
	Forgive those of inopportune time,
	Wasting of those....
	                                  who do not understand,
	Of those....
	                                who are greedy
	and do not appreciate your gifts,
	Those who...
	                               do not honor you
	at each feast,
	                               Or who...
	do not honor...
	                               the life
	                                        of those who
	gave their lives,
	justified
	                      of life itself.
	May 15, 2001
