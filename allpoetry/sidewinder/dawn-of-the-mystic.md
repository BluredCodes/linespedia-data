---
title: "Dawn Of The Mystic"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "dawn-of-the-mystic"
source: "AllPoetry"

Angel of the fallen moon
rises within the hour of sundown,
Illumination prevails in images
celebrated in a festival of light,
A truth shall be known,
But within its own time
sincere of its own making,
A prayer held to fulfill a trust,
To thine self open doors beyond the veil,
Rise above doubt
and grow in insight
forever in fountains of physical reality,
Tribulations come and go,
But as faith deepens
a key unlocks those worlds
some refuse to believe in,
Whispers of spirits champion
desires in open freedom,
Manipulating experiences
that we each perceive differently.
It's this that dawns the mystic
and elevates a marvel in time.

April 30, 1997
