Prayers...
             melted in the silence
verified
                                   in those chants
who blend in tears,
                     Crying,
Why me?
                              Doust thou find
that rage
             cursing thy morality?
Look for miracles
                      that seem to never happen,
Yet in life
               they do...
But we...
                         look past them.

June 4, 2001
