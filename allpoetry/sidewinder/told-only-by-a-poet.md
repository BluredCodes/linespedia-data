Little sister,
Where in those dreams
do you follow a hearts compassion?
Does it justify that tribulation
seen only in your eyes?
Rage is sometimes met in those who have no other
place to go,
See with eyes where others are blind,
Touch with a heart when others are afraid,
Hear those echoes when others refuse to hear,
Sensations greet a sunrise
within a heart twinkling in the twilight,
Yet in this wisdom that I reveal
should be met with an open mind and an open heart,
So if life's lifetime experiences
tell a story,
Then moment by moment
and decade by decade
reveals an epic
told only
by a poet
touched in whispers.

Oct. 15, 2000
