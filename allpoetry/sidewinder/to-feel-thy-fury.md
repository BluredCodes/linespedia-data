title: "To Feel Thy Fury"
writer: "sidewinder"

To feel thy fury
in thy pain
and touch the rage
least wherein
sure in the silence
that whispers
in thy need,
Pierced in daggers,
Cut....
Bleeding...
Exasperated....
Frustrated...
Enraged beyond measure by measure,
Awakened to the surrounding
happenings,
Yet...
somehow lost,
Those tears caught in between,
Touched by an emotion
completed by the heart,
Awakened by the soul,
And in that very moment
an idea
is serrated,
Frightened
by that very thought,
Ingenious
in its beginning.

Jan. 28, 2002
