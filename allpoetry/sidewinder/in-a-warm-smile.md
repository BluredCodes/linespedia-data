---
title: "In A Warm Smile"
writer: "sidewinder"

Reach past my past
climbing those mountains
that I've always dreamed of climbing,
Touching those memories
that somehow have haunted me,
Yet...
         in that repast
impressions
                        take those souls
that some take for granted,
                                           Collected
though tender,
                        Warm
                                    in thy embrace,
Sensuous
                     in a warm smile.

June 4, 2001
