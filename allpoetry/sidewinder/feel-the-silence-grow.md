title: "Feel The Silence Grow"

As you watch the sun slowly disappear
	and the night begins
	you can feel the silence grow,
	look above
	the stars glisten like jewels,
	You feel that you can
	hold the whole world in your hands,
	Dreams that once seemed impossible
	now simply exist,
	And the tears that you once cried
	become happy memories.
	Aug. 29, 1985
