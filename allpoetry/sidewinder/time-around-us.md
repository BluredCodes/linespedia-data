title: "Time Around Us"

Time...
It holds still when you're around me,
Yes,
You know that I care,
What more can I say?
Will I ever leave you?
No...
Not the way I feel,
Let me ask you this,
How do you feel?
Yes...
Always I feel your love,
Why?
Because I can see it...
in my mind,
So?
Let's not waste our time,
For...
love is all around,
And...
the moment is now
not later.

Aug 13, 1981
