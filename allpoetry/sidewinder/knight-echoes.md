---
title: "Knight Echoes"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "knight-echoes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Justice met
verified by those
who seek a balance,
Seasons touch only to reach
a link...
wherein time is
chronicled in chivalry,

Honor shown,
Rites performed,
True equality to all,
Time passes quickly,
A repast,
bards sing of feats...
of loyalty past,

Celebrations touch,
Listen to those stories
that tell about heroes and knights
where bravery is met,
Hear those echoes serenaded
in those dreams fulfilled,

Armor clashes,
Loyalties tested,
King's standard held high,
Families divided,
Intrigue surrounds the court,
Rumors self-serving,
Who dost thou trust?

Destiny is met,
Revealed in the smile
of an old dismantled warrior
who sees both the dawn and the twilight
of what is to come,
Yet wonders where
the magic disappeared
in those yesteryear's.

April 19, 2000
