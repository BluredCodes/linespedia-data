In the sliver of time
pleasures arrive
in somewhat of a surprise
touching those dreams of expectations
hiding in the shadows,
Reaching those who least expect it,
Where conversations and catch phrases
pen point those few seconds
in the minds of a chosen few.

March 23, 1999
