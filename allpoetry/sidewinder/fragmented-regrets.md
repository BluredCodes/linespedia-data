Inner reflections
center beyond the shadow,
A light revealed to touch upon
our growth with our lives,
A test to touch upon the truth
that fills us like water in a barrel,
Openings which reveal parts of ourselves
that some keep hidden
in shadow
forever forgotten,
Ancient in the past,
paths lost that once held promise,
Those changes that were never regained
in dreams
which rage like a summer storm,
Those circles fragmented and fragile
in regrets and deceptions
that some forget to release
and forgive.
