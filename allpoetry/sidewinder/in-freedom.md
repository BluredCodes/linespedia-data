---
title: "In Freedom"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch within our hearts
those who lost their lives,
In freedom
we who live it, 
Stand proud,
We must stand
and honor
that eagle 
even though
tears sometimes flow,
Some tatter
and blame this most understanding lady,
Who protects what most take for granted,
Why?
Must it be
that horror is felt
before we stand united?
Brave when we seek
that cherishment felt in our need,
And in that need
we must not be blind,
For if we do so
then we have lost
what we hold so precious,
Let's guide
this hostile world
by example,
Never forgetting
what we have spent years
striving for,
Let's reach out hand in hand,
Hold fast in our beliefs,
Forever keep that dream a reality,
Seeking a truth
some have forgotten.

Sept. 11, 2001
