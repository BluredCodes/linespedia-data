title: "Those Eyes Of A Child"

Touching those eyes of a child
where sight sees deeper than the soul
yet in the silence
innocence is finally met,
Love embraces your heart with no strings attached,
No exceptions...
Nothing but the wanting of your embrace,
A universal understanding
that some take for granted, 
Why?
Just once take a look,
Let the revelation take place
and wisdom will flow...
in tears of peace,
Joy will find you...
in all its gladness,
precious is this gift
warm in an angels flight,
Where freedom is found
completely,
Footloose in a world...
sometimes taken too seriously.

Nov. 20, 2000
