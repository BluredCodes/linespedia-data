title: "And Autumn Came"

There's something in the wind,
I can feel it,
The warmth of the sun is changing,
The green of the leaf is disappearing,
The golds, reds
and yellows,
A multitude of other colors
are crowding it out,
Soon the old north wind will be blowing,
 Winter is just around the corner,
Somehow I'll miss the warmth of the sun,
But that's alright!
It'll return next year.

April 16, 1985
