Raised to a field of wonder
primed in the fullness of the need
immersed in water like a brand new babe
where in those mysteries
rise in times
equal to the search,
Spread throughout the world
immersed in tranquility
where those gentle wonders
complete the need
and amount to the search,
Where magic fulfills a need
that the soul touches within
quiet horizons and yesterdays.

June 2, 1999
