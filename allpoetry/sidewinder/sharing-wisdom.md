A hawk sharing the wind
up in the bright blue sky
sacred by the dreams shared
magical in circles given from the heart,
A true and inner learning,
Journeys reach to the otherworld
and a light guides by mediations
 which focus a part of you
 sacred in its own magical space,
 Sharing wisdom in visions
shaped by incarnations
encountered through Mother Earth herself,
Belief imparts reality
and touches the spirit
centered by the heart.

June 22, 1997
