---
title: "Time Bides By No Man"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "time-bides-by-no-man"
source: "AllPoetry"

Visions,
Illusions of a justified past,
Creations of the bright morning dew,
Allegations of nightmares
haunting hallways of old buildings
grating on nerves
held by my making,
For whom does the bell toll?
Time bides by no man,
For the future resides
in illusions of an ungrateful world,
Recounted of an immersion
of memories
held in the past
touched by a shadow
reached by a hand
that has its grip on forever.

Aug. 17, 1996
