title: "Balance Matters"
writer: "sidewinder"

Pleasures, dreams and
colorful metaphors,
Thought and images,
Simple conceptions of the mind,
Contradictions of the thought gone wild,
Cluttered corners and dusty shadows of the brain,
Fantasies never achieved, 
A shuttered touch of pain,
 Creative memories,
 A heart touch in the past,
 Each has a place in one's life,
 But the balance matters the most, 
For without it chaos reigns.

June 2, 1996
