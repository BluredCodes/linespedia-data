title: "A Key To A Soul"

A key to a soul
touched by the memory
of time,
Like a window to the past
carrying the freedom of a moment
held forever in song,
Reaching to a heart
that looks to be held
by the kindness of the eyes,
Fearing the unknown,
Can it be said
forever is in the wind, 
Never knowing direction,
Never reaching its destiny.

May 11, 1996
