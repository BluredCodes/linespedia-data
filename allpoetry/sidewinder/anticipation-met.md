Anticipation
               met....
                      by a single glance,
Feeling your heartbeat,
                             The warmth of your eyes,
A smile touching....
                     the soul,
                                     And in that arrival
a new world is opened up
                            by your presence
                                              impression by impression,
I hear your laughter
                     and somehow
                                 the tension is gone,
Only met
                by learning who
                                  you are,
Could we?
                  Should we?

Jan. 28, 2002
