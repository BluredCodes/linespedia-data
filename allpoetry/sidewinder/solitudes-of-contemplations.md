In the solitude of nature
 an excitement of with and without,
 To touch an echo
 of a journey,
 Impatient for the secrets
 that are hidden,
 Yet to be revealed,
 A transformation
 in the making,
 Changes that are
 reached...
 improved with time,
 Miracles within the rhyme and resonation,
 A passage of mystery,
 A door to the unknown,
 gateway of the universe
 contemplated by a breakthrough
 in those thoughts that we all have.
 
 Nov. 4, 1997
