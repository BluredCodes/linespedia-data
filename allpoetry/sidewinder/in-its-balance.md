---
title: "In Its Balance"
writer: "sidewinder"

The poet within me arises
like a knight that proudly guards his poet
waiting for the arrival of those from Avalon,
Sweet in the anticipation of the songs and tales
told by the bards,
A celebration of the seasons,
Rich and full of adventure,
Listen to the hearts that have
a revelation to reveal,
Its truth can fill one with wisdom,
And its logic guides one past a hidden dream,
Look for the secret that touches one
forever in those secret realms,
Doors that open realities
sacred to those hearts
that one is willing to share
Open a consciousness
in the fullness of time,
Touch the freedom that one holds
forever in the universe,
And in its balance
connections are forever held.

Oct. 1, 1997
