---
title: "The Lace Of Romance"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "the-lace-of-romance"
source: "AllPoetry"

Freedom engrossed,
Those pleasures embraced by your eyes
reaches from a heart
touched in roses,
Melodies met
yet awakened,
Prolonged by a moment of Pleasure,
Our time passes through the night,
Another summer touches within those silences
unfurled in a breath of our stars,
Heartbeats of perfection,
Languages of the soul,
Lines completed where the wind whispers
the lace of romance,
Pardon me if I'm too bold,
Feel the flicker & the flame
of the warmth that we need,
Words spoken through our bridges
serenaded in our hunger.

Sept. 10, 2000
