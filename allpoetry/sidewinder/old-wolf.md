Old wolf...
            touch me with your eyes,
Sing to me...
               Let me hear the winds of freedom,
Come & go as you will
                       like a ghost in the night.
Let me feel...
                  your final vision,
Remind me...
                that time is precious,
Never take anything for granted,
Because you never know
when destiny
                       might call.

June 28, 2000
