title: "What I Most Treasure"

Those midday picnics,
                       That smile that you leave,
Those pangs of hunger
                             that we have....
                                             for each other,
Those walks in the park
                            Under a midnight moon,
Each day I treasure those,
The slow dancing that we do,
                                The light romantic music that we enjoy,
The night left alone
                        with a romantic movie that we enjoy,
The cuddling and snuggling
                             warm in each others arms,
Each day I treasure those,
The memories at the lake,
                            The family gatherings,
The hotsprings
                   where we wore nothing
                                          but what nature gave us,
Each day I treasure,
                      And what I most treasure....
                                                    is you,
And you being in my life.

Jan. 14, 2002
