You once asked me
If I knew what the raven meant,
I know now,
She is the messenger,
Also known as the night crow,
She teaches one how to change consciousness,
Through her lessons
one begins to undergo an awakening,
Her message can mean love,
To touch that which is in the soul,
Fills me with thy need,
 Bringer of magick,
Heal my spirit,
Fill an emptiness
only found in the great mystery,
I see...
What you need me to see, 
Beginning that understanding
only found in your wisdom.

Sept. 29, 2001
