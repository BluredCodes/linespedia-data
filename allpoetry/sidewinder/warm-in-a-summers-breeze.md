title: "Warm In A Summer's Breeze"

Here I am in a bar,
Not drinking,
Walls full of memorabilia,
Old memories of days gone past, 
Sweet in the nectar of time
celebrated by the music,
Blues I think,
Neon lights flashing,
Two lovers being sweet,
Making their own recollections,
Distant when they look back,
Remembering what it was like to be young,
And I smile...
only if it was me,
These days it's good
to have a loving hand
warm in a summer's breeze.

May 13, 2001
