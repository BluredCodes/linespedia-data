A feeling of seclusion
carried through a celebration
simplified
by a fact
that reveals itself
at the proper time,
Circumstances stand,
In conclusion,
Familiar reasons
give memories
holding a time long past,
Shadows sometimes
hide secrets
better off not known,
But who are we to judge?

Oct. 5, 1996
