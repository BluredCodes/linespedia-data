---
title: "In Thy Heart"

Against thy words
prevailing,
Yet amongst the world
the truth be known,
 For what is in thy sight?
Honesty?
Or be it known what lies in thy heart,
For thy meaning...
is not quite clear,
Still..
only thy can tell.

March 19, 1985
