title: "The Memories That One Reaches"

The memories
that one reaches
from moment to moment
held only...
by the shining of the sun,
Those gentle tears
that have been cried,
The laughter heard
from a joke or two,
The smile given
or received from a stranger
never known,
The innocence
met by a child,
The warm handshake
felt by old reunions,
God...
life is good!
Look what we've missed
just by not seeing those
times that passed us by
and taking time to smell the roses
along the way of life.

Dec. 23, 2001
