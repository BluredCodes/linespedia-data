---
title: "Within That Honored Veil"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch within that
honored veil,
A primrose sheltered
illusion
varied past the edge of dawn,
Time met
And time gone,
Where reality skirts
that fine edge
of itself and fantasy,
Met only in chronicles
sung by a bard
who mystifies
that crowd
found on the edge of their seat,
As they listen to
tales of magic and mystery
woven by a mythical
hero or heroine
who saves
the day.

Dec. 14, 2001
