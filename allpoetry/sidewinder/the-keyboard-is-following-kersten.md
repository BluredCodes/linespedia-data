The keyboard is following Kersten,
Talking to her...
in her imaginative games,
I wonder what's it saying?
It's your turn now,
Still the thought remains
a delightful time,
Laughter can be heard
down the hallway,
The joy in the eyes of a little girl,
Kirsten?
Are you having fun?
Forever remembered
and touches each of us
in our hearts.

March 22, 2000I wrote this In the home a very good friend.
Her little girl is about 4 yrs old
