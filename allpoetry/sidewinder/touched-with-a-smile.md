Touched with a smile....
sheltered with a glint in your eye, 
That harmony in your voice
somehow mine
does not compare,
Though we both share our soul
with the world as our witness,
And yet...
in your mind
you see the world as green
only to touch the peace
you wish the world to be,
My friend...
I hope that you find
that one companion that you search for,
May she...
touch those dreams
just beyond your grasp,
Tell those stories that you need to tell,
And teach the children...
May they learn the wisdom
that you know,
And may your dance
touch the rhythms of the universe.

March 23, 2001This was written for Allan Perry.
May your friendship hold always dear !
