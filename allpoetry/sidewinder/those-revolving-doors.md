---
title: "Those Revolving Doors"

Those revolving doors,
                      Paradoxes
touching on times
                  parallel
to emotions gone awry,
                       Constant
to a perspective
                                 sometimes
gone blind,
            Drawing energy, insight and passion,
Currents
         blending in and out,
Somehow getting past that wall
                               that we've built...
only a brick at a time,
                         Heal those hurts
that seem to last a lifetime,
                             But somehow it's a
beginning.

Dec. 18, 2001
