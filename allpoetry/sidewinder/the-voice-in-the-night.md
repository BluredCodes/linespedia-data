title: "The Voice In The Night"
writer: "sidewinder"

Voice of the Lady,
Calls in the night of the wind,
Calling the people in the valley,
She touches in song,
Varied only in time,
Within that harmony
that touches in magic.

Can you hear her?

Talking the old language
of a heart's lover,
When they first fell in love
the magic word was called the night,
With winds & clouds
wherein the silence
a full moon appears
and the dance begins.

Look into her eyes
& tell me that it hasn't.

Pure bliss in a miracle of time,
Forever met in romance,
Listen...
And tell what you hear?
A voice in the night,
The lady looked into the wind,
A world of magic & love,
Dreams of the writer,
The voice calls to the writer,
Tell the story....

Voice in the night.

Tell those dreams & fantasies
mired in promises,
Where the lady finds the man of her dreams,
An elfin prince,
A soulmate
who takes her to the promised land,
Where chivalry still touches
& a knight finds courage,
heard in tales told by a bard,
Mystery & intrigue,
Felt in the soul,
Those whispers revealed past midnight
& the joy pampered the heart only in time,
Mystery & fantasies of the mind,
The man, her lover,
Stands from two different pasts,
He calls to his lover in the night,
She hears his voice in the wind,
Splendor rises,
Immaculate in that wind,
Which whispers...
Only in the night.

The moon & the wolf
stand in agreement,
Nature takes its course,
When two souls entwine
in that spiral dance
celebrating the miracle of time,
They unveil of the night,
miracle love in the wind,
Wolf runs to the beat of a lover's heart,
The moon smiling in the night,
Old voices talking to the lover in the night,
Wind blowing the voice into time for all to hear.
If listened to with a heart,
the voices in the night tell lovers to dream,
Believe in the magic
Of the voice of the night,
And in that miracle
their voices are heard
eternally,
So when the night arises
some say that you can still hear them,
Listen...

Do you hear that voice in the night?

Feb. 12, 2002
