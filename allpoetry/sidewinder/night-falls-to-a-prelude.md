title: "Night Falls To A Prelude"

Night falls to a prelude
of a checkered menagerie
pledged to a separation
of wisdom,
Gentle to a fault,
Where is the proposal
authentic in the connection 
of a time lost?
Like a fledgling hawk
spreading his wings for the first time,
And In the moment of freedom
the desire
creates an image
that results in an urge
never to be satisfied.

Oct. 2, 1996
