title: "Cherished Impressions"

Cherish those thoughts
improper to some,
But yet the fantasies
rage in a result of
 unfulfilled promises
which mark a journey
that each of us make
regardless of whether we want to admit it
or not,
Unrequited love
in a collision imprisoned,
Touch those images
generated by a mind
determined to reel in a torrid impression
completed in a display of sexual abandon,
Sultry to those who can imagine
a revelation held in time.

Sept. 23, 1996
