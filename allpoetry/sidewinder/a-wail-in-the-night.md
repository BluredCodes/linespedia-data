title: "A Wail In The Night"
writer: "sidewinder"

In the wail of the night
rising like a whisper
gripping your soul,
Touched beyond those dreams,
Blades iron of strength,
Blood seen in a mire of time,
Feeding off of mind thoughts
that can never be in truth,
In life
craving for the soothing 
smile from your perfect lips
and in the glint in your eyes
wherein a search touches in the night,
To find your prey
to satisfy a hunger
deep in the darkness,
Then to discard the body
where life before thrived,
Now my eyes are dead,
Putting me in the shadows,
Never looking back
you wander away.

Aug. 1, 2000
