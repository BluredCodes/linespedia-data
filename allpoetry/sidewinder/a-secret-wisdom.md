---
title: "A Secret Wisdom"

A rebirth of a time
when a king brings forth a miracle
celebrated by those who would honor him, 
Trust followed by fellowship
in the shape of a key
that touches a destiny,
a living tradition
that is held by those
who would see beyond the otherworld,
Ancient is the Emerald forest
where the first elf made his home,
Sacred is the Blood red mountains
to the dwarves who forged their first axe,
And in the triumph of laughter
heard in the purple hills
burns the heart of the gnome,
Legends are heard by the songs of the bards,
Magic honors those who would
honor her,
For the spirit of the land
dwells in all
an echo celebrated by time
and a new dawn
rises through it's secret wisdom.

Oct 13, 1997
