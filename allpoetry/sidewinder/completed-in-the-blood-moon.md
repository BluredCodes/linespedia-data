title: "Completed In The Blood Moon"

Talons rage
in tooth and claw
varied in the night
wherein the magic
touches a full moon,
Secrets of the darkness
to find what holds
the spells and mysteries
of the shadows
where in uncommon
invocations speak,
Sorcery meets in thy eye
completing two souls
behind a forgotten tome,
An unknown for so long
only remembrances to see,
This life and greatness
to share
in the wyrms eye
met in an ancient ire,
The circle is completed
and the blood moon
fulfills its prophecy.

Aug. 1, 2000
