---
title: "Only To Rise Again"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "only-to-rise-again"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43186-Only-To-Rise-Again-by-sidewinder"
---

To touch the dreams
that we all have in our minds, 
A celebration of the soul,
to touch
heart to heart,
 Awake in this lifetime
beyond those sins
that some judge,
Where prophecies touch
in a rage, 
To see that morning light
where her eyes
shine from the night before
and fate touches
in the heart,
The warmth wells in a crescendo of time
cherished in a clamor of the day,
And in that intimacy
those oceans of dreams rise
in the solitude of a soul starved night,
Only to rise again
like the tide.

March 26, 2001
