---
title: "Words & Echoes"
writer: "sidewinder"

Words cut...
             contrasted in shape and form
where time has no meaning
   void in a linear memory
without value,
Convexed and conveyed
                        within those mirrors of abstraction,
As if
           within
outer
      space
               or a dimensional calling
that pulls one forward
                       just to touch
those collective minds
                         that rage in a moment of passing,
Listen
       and reach for
                    wisdom
                           that echoes
                                      in eternity.

May 12, 1999
