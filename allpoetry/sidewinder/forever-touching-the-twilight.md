title: "Forever Touching The Twilight"

In between the twilight holds thresholds
forever keeping the rites of passage
in an age of fire,
awakener of hearts,
Master of the crossroads,
Deepen the perceptions that rise
in courage,
Lessons mired in truths
forever changing those enchantments
of the faerie realms,
Opening visualizations common to modern magic,
Challenges acquired through balance,
Intuition resumes from the rhythm of life,
Dreams voices calling fate from The Vale of Avalon,
With every footstep that we journey
come wisdom,
By the edge of a softly flowing stream
follows a unicorn, 
Symbolizing the freedom and honesty
that we all have
forever touching the otherworld,
And who's to say that dreams
don't come true?

Dec. 26, 1997
