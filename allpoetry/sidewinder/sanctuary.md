Lost forever in a memory
creating a sanctuary of hope,
A single beacon
that cries a warning
for those who fear
reality,
Shadows open a gate
to a brand new world,
All that is required
from those who are brave
is a single step,
Who will it be?
Who can see...
freedom?
It's free for the asking,
reach out and
take a grasp
of what might be,
You never know.
