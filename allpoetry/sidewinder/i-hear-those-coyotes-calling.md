I hear those coyotes calling,
Together they're singing a midnight song,
I walk out
and see the stars shining,
Look at them glittering,
very beautiful,
one by one
Next you feel
Mother Nature crying
to all her children,
Somehow she seems complete,
Invoke that ageless wisdom,
Connect to that twilight
found only in a Rainbow stream,
Threshold touched through a dream,
A traveler seen and challenge out of his own heart,
A land of the mist....
                         where a song arises,
Listen to that spirit calling,
Totem to that great dance of life,
Sacred to all
                  who have the wisdom to learn.

Jan. 12, 2002
