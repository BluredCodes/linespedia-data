Cascading crusades,
Banners waving,
Protesters protesting
things they're not sure of,
People speaking their minds
when they should be listening instead,
Yet...
              Who understands the politics that we have?
Yes...
           I know...
                       I know....
Everyone has an opinion,
But does it matter?
Some say ....
              Yes...
                     it does!
Some say....
             No!
Recently we had an election,
And we're still waiting for a president,
They tell us that the electoral vote
elects him,
But what they don't understand
is this,
When a poll is taken
to find out how much population exists
that will tell our politician who counts,
Yet the popular vote does matter,
One vote can't change things,
But together if we all stick together
we can change the world,
When one doesn't vote
then you're giving the rich man
the power to do what he wants,
Why?
If you're not satisfied with things as they are
then change them,
And have the patience to wait for change,
It might not happen in the short run,
But it'll happen in the long run,
