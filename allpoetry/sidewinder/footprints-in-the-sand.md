---
title: "Footprints In The Sand"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "footprints-in-the-sand"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42179-Footprints-In-The-Sand-by-sidewinder"

Within a thorn of time
limited only by a frame of mind
comes the darkness
echoed by laughter
complicated only by silence,
Influences in reason
unknown to those who have forgotten
those dreams that some yearn to have,
Victories in encounters
found in a distance
met by the tear of an eye,
Interludes beyond a shadow
celebrated in private
to touch emotions
as footprints in the sand.

Oct. 16, 2000
