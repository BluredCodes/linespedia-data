title: "Found In Silent Melodies"

Found in silent melodies
reach and call
for within that miracle
I can sense
the essences
and in a harmony
spirit tells me
to find that special calling,
And I have found it
for I soar beyond
those winds rising to touch
a soul
completing a journey
that some spend a lifetime searching for,
And here am I,
Listen!
Can you hear my spirit?
It sings to those
who need to find a journey
that has meaning
and I reveal the magic
that soars within
and without.

June 15, 2001
