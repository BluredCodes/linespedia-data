Within a doubt
touches only in a shelter of time
raging in those mere moments
contrasted past a freedom,

A freedom to dream,
A freedom to choose,
A freedom you seen,
Never to lose,

So what direction do we reach for?
Only by a moment
where a keeper
nods in silence
and the world is at our command,

Do you know what to do,
When all eyes are centered on you,
When you can choose who lives or dies,
But remember the truth never lies,

But a past memory
forever holds a key,
It's those secrets
that we hold
only in time.

June 1, 2000
