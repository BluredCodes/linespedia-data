---
title: "And To Think"

Held beyond the difficulties
where within the times
a promise arises
that is touched only
by your smile,
Could it be that
only a word is needed?
And in that completeness
you have met the odds
and won!
For you have reached beyond
the plains of kindness
and touched my soul,
In this a celebration can be felt,
And to think....
You thought it was impossible.

Feb. 1, 2000
