title: "A Smile Holds Its Own Journey"
writer: "sidewinder"

Circumstance touches those dreams
where a heart must meet its destiny,
And though a road had two directions
I must follow what I think is right,
Some are willing to understand,
Some do not,
But that's life,
The tragedy is this,
Those with a closed mind
always search for their own happiness
instead of finding the happiness within,
And how can I explain this
in a confused world
complicated beyond understanding?
Yet...
a smile
holds its own journey
to those who are willing to receive.

Oct. 18, 1999
