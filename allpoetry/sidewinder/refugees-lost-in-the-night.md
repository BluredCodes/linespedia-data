title: "Refugees Lost In The Night"
writer: "sidewinder"

Refugees lost in the night
dreaming of their freedom,
Splendid where one touches
in a soulful cry,
Strangers embrace only in the twilight
different each on their own,
Completed in their varied song,
Listen to those echoes
which whisper in the wind,
A cry in the wilderness
only found past a shadow,
Edged on a fine line
wherein those prophecies touch
on thine eye,
Passion meets where one least expects it,
Yet the moonlight reveals
those secrets that a soul
keeps,
Can you hear the invitation
foreshadowed in the coming of time?
Farewell my friend,
May you understand the silence
enchanted in the veil.

Aug. 8, 2000
