The truth be told,
It can be said that there is an edge
between reality and fantasy,
But at what cost?
There are those worlds
that have yet to be found,
shadow dancing and meditating
to the sounds of songs
trapped in the minds
of those unshielded
in emotions throughout the universe,
memories...
long forgotten,
Tears touching a heart,
yet time heals
and karma come full circle,
Where life ends,
Life begins,
Mother Earth smiles,
For she knows that this story is never ending.

March 27, 1996
