Pleasures and sensations,
	destiny calls its weary voice
	like a wave
	from an ever flowing ocean
	pulsing with a heartbeat of time,
	Look at the kaleidoscope of  never ending colors
	promising another day,
	But what does it have in store for us?
	Heaven only knows,
	Keep in mind
	that the touch of time
	holds something different for each of us,
	At the same moment keeps us all on the same journey,
	It's only a matter of perspective
	forever through eternity.
	July 6, 1996
