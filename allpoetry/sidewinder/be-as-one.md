---
title: "Be As One"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "be-as-one"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50281-Be-As-One-by-sidewinder"
---

Secrets held in a miracle
where your eyes
touch beyond a horizon,
And if by chance
the night reveals a hope
then your embrace forever
meets beyond expectations,
And in that glimmer
reflections mire wherein
the hour remains,
Shall it be that you
touch a part of my soul
where none have touched before?
And in that completeness
you are my better half,
Only in my dreams will
I seek the freedom
needed to complete my psyche,
So let me ask you this,
Will we in the revelation of time
be as one?
