title: "Within The Inner Flame"

An inner flame consumes the soul,
Circles that rise
pulses into time,
rekindling those fires
which embrace the sacred
trances into a dream world,
Twilight warrior,
Lord of the dance of shadows,
Secrets paths that lead
a heart into spiritual wisdom,
A pendulum of awareness,
A connection of magic
which rises to know the child within,
Visualize the power that resides within you,
Touch those images
tangled in transformations,
Compassion & understanding
invoked by the Goddess,
Here's is the secret door of youth
sheltered in rainbows
which bridges the cauldron of immortality.

Dec. 7, 1997
