Promises held by a touch,
How does it feel
to hide beyond the illusion?
Encounters met
by the hello's
and in that distance
a goodbye make the roads that we travel 
just a wee bit closer,
So in the marrow
a yesterday touches on those secret wishes,
And in your laughter
a doorway opens to an afterthought that
contrasts in silence,
Immaculate in concepts
forsworn in it's completeness.
