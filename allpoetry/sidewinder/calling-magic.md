Through the minutes eye
a crystal of light
touches beyond the universal truth,
Incantations rise mystically
through a key
calling magic in the heart of the Goddess,
Spiritually touched
harmoniously,
Prime in its fullness,
Celebrated by a touch of nature,
held by a door
which opens new realms
in journeys that build gifts of vision,
A symbol that echoes,
An aspect of the human heart,
Paths that focus an awakening
beyond a hidden world
sacred in an ancient isle.

Oct. 16, 1997
