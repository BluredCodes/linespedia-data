---
title: "In The Nexus"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "in-the-nexus"

Refractions of light
in the nexus on my mind,
Bordering in the shadows
of an uncomplicated time,
Touched by the moment,
Raged by the night
where few dare go,
Often frightened by the sight,
Those dreams become nightmares,
Welcome, said the darkman,
Shall I show you more?
Can we reach the recesses
in the inkling of time,
Touch that very moment
when you became mine,
Those hidden desires
become more than you trust,
But search for the balance
and look for the sign,
Such of those who do this
will always do fine.

March 28, 1996
