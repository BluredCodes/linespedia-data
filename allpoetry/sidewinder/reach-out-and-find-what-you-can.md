title: "Reach Out And Find What You Can"

Melodies fair in the wind
rising through those touches
revealing the magic
surrendered from the heart
completed in the soul
where in time
you can step & see
those dreams,
Look & sense what you can find,
Listen for harmony that
touches like an angel breath
which whispers:
Peace be with you,
Find that joy
in miracles within the spirit
echoed in thy heart,
But in that search
you'll find it within,
Reach out
and find what you can!

June 15, 2001
