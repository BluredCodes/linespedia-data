title: "Is Say Yes"

Touch my dreams,
	Hold my soul,
	Relieve my fears,
	Make me whole,
	Complete the other half,
	And in that solitude is my breath,
	Be with me...
	                 At my side,
	Let me catch your tears,
	Let's share the horizon...
	                            in our completion,
	See the sunrise...
	                        at your command,
	All you have to do...
	                       is say yes
	and the world is ours.
	May 28, 2001
