Days of midsummer
rise beyond all illusions, 
Promises of tomorrow
keep a secret that only the mountains know,
And in the vale of Avalon,
Reveals the honor
that a knight keeps
by a code of chivalry,
Those truths that some forget in Misty realms
merged by magic,
A glimmering gate of wonder
beneath the sky vault where
great ravens soar,
The circle is open,
But unbroken,
Calling for a rebirth
of a day where heroes dwell
in times of revelations
initiated by dreams unspoken.

Dec. 21, 1997
