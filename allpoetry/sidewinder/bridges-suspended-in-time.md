title: "Bridges Suspended In Time"

Walking those dark streets,
Touching those shadows
that reach those dark corners
like an echo forgotten...
too long in a memory,
Reaching for those bridges
suspended in time,
Embraces hidden in a mist
like prose recited...
Yet now forgotten
and you wonder why?
Images soar in the clouds
calm in the wind
yet fragile in a dream
that somehow must be kept.
Eyes that avert pain 
amplified in sorrow
mortal in the difference.

Nov. 8, 2000
