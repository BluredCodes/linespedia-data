Remembering those moist full lips,
A smile...
that would light up a room,
And warm heart
found in the alone of the day,
Eyes met...
one to one
sensuous in that completion,
Hand in hand,
Where that touch is needed,
And your sweet voice
whispers
to meet that need
where one reaches
beyond the darkness.

Dec. 10, 2001
