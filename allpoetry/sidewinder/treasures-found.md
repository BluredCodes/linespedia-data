Touching those dreams past a midnight moon,
Zipping through those celebrations
and tears that life seems to forever offer,
Those November rains
                       cold but
they do have their benefits
when a warm heart looks
for someone to cling to,
Look for those smiles
that can brighten your day,
I found a coffeehouse once
where smiles filled those times
in faces brilliant in memory,
Open arms welcome touches
when needed,
Some need those completions
found in a soul,
It's like finding a rose
wild in the hills
intoxicated by the aroma,
Treasures found when least expected.

June 7, 2001
