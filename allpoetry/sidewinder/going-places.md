title: "Going Places"
writer: "sidewinder"

I've gotta get out of this place
before I lose my mind,
There's gotta be more than what I see,
Being free is what I need,
Celebrations touched beyond the streets,
These endless streets and gutters,
I keep falling down,
Can you save me?
Lost faith in it all,
You can't hear me,
Can you?
At the same time
there comes a need,
It's more than a promise
where those questions are answered,
Places where I want to be,
Someday I'll find them
you wait and see,
Something more and believe,
Hide and watch,
Wait and see!
We invented your world,
We've sailed the seas,
You laugh it away as your world falls apart,
And try to be bitchin' in your ford mustang,
Wig on straight!
Head full of hate,
Cussin' and smoking,
Waiting to get paid,
Meanwhile all of my dreams
that I've made...
come true,
Blazing more than a miracle
in a blink of an eye,
Somehow you're sitting still...
wondering why?

July 12, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Mike Vrtatko
