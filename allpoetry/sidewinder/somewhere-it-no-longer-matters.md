---
title: "Somewhere It No Longer Matters"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "somewhere-it-no-longer-matters"

To reach beyond those differences
in eyes...
that see beyond the cold,
And touching
that veil
that is seen
                  but met
                         somewhere
                                   between the darkness...
And the light,
                     Where the season
reaches
                   just lost in the horizon,
Where a wave reaches
                        a beach
melted by the sand,
And we find that difference
                             where somewhere
                                             it no longer matters.

Dec. 10, 2001
