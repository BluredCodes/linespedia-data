Searing heat,
	Serrated beyond imagination,
	Anger mired past a thought
	until the harshness
	is carried into the wind
	& only remembered
	by a song,
	Shattered by the embrace
	only by time,
	And if you could look
	into death's eyes
	you would find
	complete isolation,
	For his laughter
	remains ringing forever
	in a testament of yesterday.

	July 17, 2000
