There are questions in my mind
Too long unanswered,
And it occurred to me
to search for what is not known,
But where?
Many have gone through that journey
only to find more questions unanswered,
Confusion comes to mind,
Illusions become reality,
But in who's mind?
Does the teacher become the student?
Or does the student become the teacher?
Round and round the question flows,
But where it winds up,
Nobody knows,
And here I am
still with unanswered questions.

March 27, 1996
