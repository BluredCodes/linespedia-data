title: "Hear The Spirit"

The striking of hands
with the quickness of a cat,
Smooth and agile
am I,
The curve of the movement,
The bend of the wrists,
Sharpness of the elbow,
Watch the eyes
and you might see,
Yes...
learn something new,
What have I to prove?
Nothing,
Only the weak want to fight,
Me?
I'll leave it alone,
So...
let's go,
And if you're lucky,
You'll hear the spirit of what I am.

April 24, 1985
