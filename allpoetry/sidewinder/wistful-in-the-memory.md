title: "Wistful In The Memory"
writer: "sidewinder"

Traces of light,
	A rainbow dances in the sun
	fulfilling a promise
	that is made for all,
	A heart touched by the first act of love,
	Shadows disappear in the following sunset,
	Regrets like illusions
	go by the wayside,
	And in the melodies of time
	a solitude gives insight & compassion,
	Visionaries recreate miracles
	to those who need them
	reviled by its uniqueness
	and celebrated by natures silent embrace,
	Touched from sonnets,
	Those creative giants
	give revelations that
	hold true today,
	Prime in its invocation of a new ballad
	wistful in the memory.
	Nov. 3, 1997
