Sometimes the silence is overwhelming
even though the reason is not clear,
I'm touched by the blue of the sky,
The comfort of the mountains,
I look up
and hear the cry of the eagle,
I feel the coolness of the autumn wind,
And yet in all of the beauty
I feel alive!
Those whispers call to me,
Telling me those secrets
some never find,
That peace
held only in a season of time,
And I smile
thanking those
who have walked through
my life
and those who have yet to.

Oct. 6, 2001
