In the flames that are among themselves
there are dragons with ebon eyes,
Feel the spirit within that rages,
A courage searing a heart,
blood rich in hope
between images that bare 
fangs central to the trust
forever held
in time,
 Pristine of a world called Faerun,
Some that have come to understand
where chivalry in those knights
rises beyond uncommon valor,
Independent yet a soul
climbs to reach in a certain perspective
forever within a renaissance,
Bards reach to tell a moment
where only a legends
blade clash in magic,
Serenaded by an emerald flame,
Hear the dream become a reality
in a realm of the unicorn,
Where an elf considers
nature forever in those woods
of Gaia's touch
and the laughter of a gnome
reaches your heart
never letting go.

Feb. 8, 1997This is what I mean by writing a legend.
the challenge is to write froma bard's point of view.
