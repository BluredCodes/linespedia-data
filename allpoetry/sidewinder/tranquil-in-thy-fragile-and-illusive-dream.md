Touching through that ray of light,
A shivering
from a patch of time,
Consecrated
in that hallowed prism,
Spiritual wherein a miracle reaches,
Fevered in that completion
yet tranquil in thy fragile and illusive dream,
See through the quiet,
Meet that stillness
as if you found that reflection
where wisdom meets laughter,
A lesson...
            well worth learning,
                                    Though a path
                                     meets tomorrow
                                      clear in an understanding
                                            where a serene
                                                vision
                                                   spins,
                                                        Immaculate
                                                         in its conception.
Nov. 30, 2001
