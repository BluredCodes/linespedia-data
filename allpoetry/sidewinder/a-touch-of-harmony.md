title: "A Touch Of Harmony"

In the secrets of the shadows
there will be celebrations
of magic & lore,
The lessons of life
shall be in spirit
& touches more than what some suspect,
Invoking truth
in the inner reaches of a hidden path,
Clear to those who understand
the frailties of the heart
which holds the promise
of dreams
in realms to those who are open
to its richness
fulfilling a way that takes a lifetime
to comprehend,
But the simplicity
overrides the complexity,
A mirrored reflection of nature herself,
A rhyme in time
where harmony embraces itself.

March 13, 1997
