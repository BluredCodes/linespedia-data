title: "Where Some Walk Hand In Hand"

Soft melodies....
only the lonely
remembers,
Put yourself in that place
held only in a dream,
 Bright promises
varied in the luck of time,
Are you willing to remember
what it felt like?
To touch in young love...
Those faces
reflected by a smile...
Blue eyes...
that could melt a heart,
Exited only in a
memory
where some walk
hand in hand.

June 18, 2000
