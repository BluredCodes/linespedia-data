title: "In The Doors Of The Unknown"
writer: "sidewinder"

Out of the rings of smoke and flame
over the chasm of time,
Wherein a shadow rises
met only in a medium of peace,

Crystal peace disturbed,
like a blemish
by ebony shadows,
Can the amber light of time withstand?

And yet those eons
meet to touch
thy sight
wherein the silence
marks a truth
forever reaching
its completeness,

Alone on a crystalline bridge
an ethereal figure stands,
Waiting for the mist of time to pass,
To find a world once lost yet gained,
For life is fleeting,
And so fast the spirit waits without remorse
as the circle of time completes its coarse
Yet...
he remembers,
How in the world did he put himself here?
To walk as a planewalker
varied in the scape of time
eternal
in the doors of the unknown.

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Chyanne Smith
