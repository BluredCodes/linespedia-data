title: "Can't You See The Signs?"
writer: "sidewinder"

Thoughts from a shattered heart
I hear a little girl looking for understanding,
And all she gets is a reprimand,
my soul reaches out to her, 
Somehow wants to shelter her from the pain,
Can't you see
without harmony
life is too much to bear,
Those cutting words
ring forever
and haunt a lonely mind,
Is the world a blind callous memory
that grips a child 
taking away its innocence
leaving only a hollow and fearful mind?
Why?
Can't you see the signs
reaching out to destiny?

Aug. 25, 1996
