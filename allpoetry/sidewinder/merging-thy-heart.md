---
title: "Merging Thy Heart"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "merging-thy-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

Memories of the past and present
are echoed in the corners of my mind,
Dreams are held so sweetly,
Yet the newness of the dawn still lingers on,
Imaged in the shadows
are paths that rage in the silence,
This is the time to pay off old debts, 
To restore,
If not in harmony
at least in balance,
Inspired by a hidden world within,
                                  Whispers rise
       from Avalon,
And the Lady of the Lake
reveals a lesson of the heart,
              Listen....

The gateway of your soul lies in an ancient forest,
                   It's there,
          A true sign of magic,
 It exists in both worlds at once,
            Merging thy heart
          with the heart of a dragon,
             Fulfill your dreams
          and make them a reality.

     Nov. 10, 1997
