title: "Held Forever"

It only takes a spark
for memories run
like rain in a desert breeze,
Can you feel the warmth of a summer night?
Watch the moon call,
Hear the wolf's invitation,
Gather your heart
less the night rules forever,
emotions ride high,
Tomorrow doesn't matter,
dream of that single touch,
For yesteryear is like time
held forever.

May 4, 1996
