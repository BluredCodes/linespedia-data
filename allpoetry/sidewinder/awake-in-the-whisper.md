title: "Awake In The Whisper"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch those lotus blossoms
hampered by the wind
and your eyes beautiful in those crescent shapes,
Somehow I feel your exotic passion
reaching past the new moon,
Random in those notes of time
wherein the silence
echoes in the shadow,
See the ripples in the water?
Feel the rhythm in your soul?
Hear the drums in the distance?
Awake in the whisper of your heartbeat
shattered in the spiral dance,
Rituals made magic
in the wings of silver,
Secrets sustained in my heart,
And in the bright sun
a horizon meets to touch another morning

March 1, 2001
