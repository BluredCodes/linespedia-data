Arrogance
which lies below self doubt
in a mist of unsolved promises,
And that...
becomes a problem
for those immune...
at least in their mind...
to resolve or solutions,
Could it be ...
that the answer
is beyond their understanding?
Or does it seem...
that the complication
rises like a fool in time?
Some merit with reasoning
others don't,
And that...
becomes the dilemma.

Sept. 4, 1996
