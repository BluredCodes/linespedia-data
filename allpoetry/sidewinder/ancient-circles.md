Ancient voices rise to celebrate
the seasons of the year,
They dance in joy
to touch on memories
revealed in an ancient wisdom,
An invocation to magic
held in a promise
to those who hold an open heart,
A harmony in a time of mystery
emerged in trances
held to those blessed by the otherworld,
A renewal of dreams
mediated to touch the younger self,
Circles connects in images
that raise energies
in a fulfillment of love.

Oct. 28, 1997
