---
title: "Who Would Believe?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "who-would-believe"
source: "AllPoetry"

Touched in those belligerent forms
unmoved...
                uncaring....
                            inhospitable...
Defined in that connection
of isolation,
                   Obscure only
                                 in an enigmatic way,
Yet...
          because of its own definition,
 A regalness
               touches
                       independent
                                   though brilliant in its own manner,
 Who would believe
                   that a single 
mountain
 would move
                 one so?

April 4, 2001
