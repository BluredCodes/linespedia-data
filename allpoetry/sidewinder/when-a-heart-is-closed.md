title: "When A Heart Is Closed"

Changes are made by time,
But I am,
Who I am,
Somethings remain the same,
Expectations are never fully met,
How is it
that I can see things one way
and you...
another?
Without communication
that road we journeyed together on
parts forever,
Why?
Friendship
sometimes is a hard
thing to find,
Acceptance should never be taken for granted,
Maybe I was wrong,
But we'll never find that out
when a heart is closed,
Can we?

Aug. 19, 1996
