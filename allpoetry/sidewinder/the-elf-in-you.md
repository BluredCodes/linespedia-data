---
title: "The Elf In You"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "the-elf-in-you"

Serenaded by all of nature,
Touched in the completeness of all the senses,
Envision the elf that you are,
There is a genuine spirit
that you share for everyone,
I feel it every time I'm around you
By your simple smile,
You give completely,
With no expectation of anything in return,
I sense a joy within you,
A charm...
Exquisite in its own making,
Rich and full of life itself,
Never let anyone take this away from you,
For your heart
is a promise
of a world
that some take for granted,
But you...
embrace it
and keep it forever.

Sept. 29, 1996
I wrote this for a friend....
Dani Weish...
Here in Cortez, Colorado.
May she be my friend forever !
