title: "In Your Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"

In your eyes,
I see...
Something,
that scares me,
So,
I force my glance away,
Unfamiliar warmth?
Please,
believe me!
I don't,
want to break your heart,
But your warmth....
So,
All consuming...
Maybe more,
Than I can withstand,
I question myself...
Do I withstand?
Or...
Allow it
            to command me?
So let's just take this moment
and let it be,
For there is,
                    Something in your eyes
that scares me,
I dare not
look in.

March 16, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Valerie Jochum
