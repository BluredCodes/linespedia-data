title: "Invoked By Magic"

Twisted trees
as if when the shadows fell,
Suffered in some maligned way,
But when the dryad came home
its spirit welcomed her,
A mystical aura surrounded within and without,
You almost see it dance...
And without a word an enchantment began,
Softly the wind started to sing its song,
Mirrored images invoked by magic
recalled those phantasmal forces,
Sheltered by a beat of time,
Charmed only by a memory
and the reflection of her smile.

July 8, 2000
