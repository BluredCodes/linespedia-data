title: "Please Listen"

You may not always agree with what I say,
But at least have the decency to listen,
For no matter what I say,
I have the right to say it,
To shut me off
is to be narrow minded,
And maybe I am infringing on your rights,
But sometimes I do know what I'm talking about,
So please..
listen,
Then if you don't agree, 
Tell me,
And we can go from there,
How else are you to know?

March 21, 1982
