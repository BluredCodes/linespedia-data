---
title: "Awaken Thy Soul"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "awaken-thy-soul"

In the silence of the hallows,
Transformations of the spirit heal,
Twilight rises in virtue,
Offerings in a runic passage
enter in the sacred,
Inspirations strikes in whispers
to touch in magic,
Revelations that quiet the mind
hold memories
shimmering is silver
dawns rise beyond the veil,
Hear the flutter of midnight
prime in a hunters moon,
Those influences of otherworld kingdoms
give brilliance in an amber sunset,
And the spiral dances
are celebrated in harmony
just to awaken thy soul.

Dec. 11, 1997
