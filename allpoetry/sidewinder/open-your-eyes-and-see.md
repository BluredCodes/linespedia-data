title: "Open Your Eyes And See"

Why is it that we forget
that we're the keepers of this Earth?
Yes...
It's our responsibility
to take care of her,
But are we?
No...
I think not,
We destroy her forests
all in the name of progress,
We trash her streams & lakes,
Make cities, 
                             For what?
                                       For the comfort of us,
To some only money matters,
Yes...
We have needs,
But what are we going to do
when Mother Nature responds in anger?
And she will...
with droughts, floods & starvation,
When our land no longer produces our food,
When the fish & the wildlife are no longer there,
When global warming take place
as we destroy our forests,
What will we do when disease takes over?
Because we've all those possibilities
in cures that we could have found,
Why ?
Because of our short sightedness
we've forgotten the lessons that we've learned
as druids,
To some wiccan means witch, 
Something to be feared,
In reality...
it means wise one,
That see's all,
And hears all, 
understanding found
 in mysterious ways,
Open your eyes and see!

Nov. 20, 2000
