Guardians rise
to touch the unseen realms,
Those voices which merge
in harmony
in between worlds
that seek to return to love,
Perceptions amongst the interplay
of moving forces,
Starlight visions in the intangible,
Conscious of those sensations
which correspond to the talking self,
Drawing forth the magic
reflected in the stillness of time,
Sustained in the alternating currents
variated in the eternal search,
Weaved in the force of change,
Initiations in rituals,
Opening those subtle forces
which mediates an expression of the heart,
Visualizations of transcendental meditations
complex in its completeness.

Nov. 25, 1997
