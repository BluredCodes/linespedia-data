---
title: "Where Art Thou?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "where-art-thou"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46879-Where-Art-Thou--by-sidewinder"
---

To touch in those dreams
unfettered in thy heart,
Where else can a realization be met?
Am I ....
that scattered?
Lost like leaves...
                     unwanted,
Tossed by to & fro
                               where the wind
amuses itself....
                      I speak...
But no one listens,
                        Here I am!
Touched as a stranger...
                               forgotten,
Unforgiven,
               And this world celebrates
But the silence
                         overwhelms,
Here I am!
               Won't somebody
                              complete thy reach?
Where art thou?

Aug. 10, 2001
