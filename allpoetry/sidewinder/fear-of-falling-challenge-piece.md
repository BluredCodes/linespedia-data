Have you ever had one of those dreams...
that somehow turned into a nightmare ?
I have !
I'm being chased...
So we go through the neighborhood,
And here I am...
                    running my ass off !
I cut through yards,
                      Through peoples houses,
                                               Through businesses,
It doesn't matter,
                  I'm determined not to get caught,
I jump fences,
                    Go through alleys,
Somehow I wind up on a high building,
And here they come again,
The buildings are closer together
& so I start jumping them,
But the pursuers are relentless,
So I get a little reckless,
And I'm not paying attention how far
the buildings are getting,
And they are getting farther & farther apart,
But to those who are chasing me it doesn't matter,
It seems the faster I go,
The faster they go,
So we finally get to the last building,
And I rush,
I'm not sure of whether I can make it or not,
But with those right behind
I have no choice,
I run & jump,
But just as I do...
                       I slip & fall,
Here I am falling & falling & falling,
Fortunately I wake up before I hit the bottom,
And I wake up in a cold sweat,
Scared out of my mind,
I'm dead headed,
So it takes a little bit to arouse myself,
And then I realise it was only a dream,
How this began
was when I was 13 yrs old.
My brother, sister, cousins
& I had a swimming hole,
3 blocks from my house,
Where I grew up in Marble Falls, Texas.
On the swimming hole was an old tree
which we had a diving platform
about 20 ft. in the air,
We used to climb up to the platform & jump into the swimming hole,
One afternoon when we were swimming & diving
I climbed up,
Ready to dive,
But this time the wind started up,
It moved the limbs around & I froze,
The only way they could get me off
was for someone to climb up & push me off,
My cousin Rocky had been chosen,
Everyone tried to talk me down,
But I was frozen fast,
So Rocky climbed up & pushed me out,
I hit belly first,
And that's part of the reason I don't like
moving high places,
When my feet are solid
I have no fear,
But if the place where I'm standing moves,
I want down !
Between that & the nightmares
is why I can't stand moving high places.

March 5, 2002
