title: "To Survive"

Touching in the words of angels
celebrated in sounds and songs
which rage in heaven,
Mired in the sweetness
permeated on Earth,
And I find...
my breath...         
                     taken away,
For emotion leads to
                            Hell
and the vile stench
of sin itself,
                                   Forgetting that we're all
only human,
And we need to find
the humor
for all of us 
to survive,

May 15, 2001
