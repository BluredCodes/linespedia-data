Slowly moving...
born from wind,
Sculptures of God's mind
descends finding some ray of hope
that a higher love will be found
in shapes in a stream of sand,
Perhaps...
of those who look
will find a higher purpose
from dreams of the heart,
Completed in an echo of time,
I need my life to be
added to the stream of sand.

June 30, 2000
