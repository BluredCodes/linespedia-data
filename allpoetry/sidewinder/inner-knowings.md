Remember,
That a man's life is one big quest
to confirm those inner knowings
that he is born with,
Parallel to those arguments
                              Forgotten...
yet somehow found,
                       The trouble is....
subtlety is lost or abandoned,
                               Yet in that quest
remembrances touch
                    in forgotten souls
that touch in those expected
                                and reach
metaphorically
                  unfinished....
                                   unspoken....
synonyms and antonyms
                           common in those past lives
revealed in linguistic terms,
                               Yet if we look for simplicity
it stares at us
                   right in our faces,
But in looking for it
                      we miss the final meaning.

Nov. 10, 2000
