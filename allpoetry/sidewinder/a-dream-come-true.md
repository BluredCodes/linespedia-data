---
title: "A Dream Come True"
writer: "sidewinder"

Believe it or not,
	the feelings are there,
	We're sharing a love between you & me,
	I don't know if you know,
	But yes I care,
	You mean the world to me,
	Every time I see that look in your eyes,
	There's a smile on my face
	that won't quit,
	You'd have to blind
	not to see that there is love,
	sharing your life
	is a dream come true,
	A beautiful fantasy
	all I have to say...
	is come here my love
	and live your life with me
	forever.
	Jan. 7, 1981
