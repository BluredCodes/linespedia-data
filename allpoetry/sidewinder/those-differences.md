---
title: "Those Differences"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "those-differences"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Within those silences
a rage meets
to touch those memories
held against a crooked sky,
A past...
forever remaining,
Connecting only
where it matters most,
Varied through
those grey times,
Jaded beyond an inkling,
sheltered within the
right and wrong,
Those that wonder
never seem to understand,
But that doesn't matter,
If it did,
Then the voice within
would always be the same,
Sometimes
its better
to have differences
so that the world
can be interesting,
Through that touch
creativity holds
a heartbeat throughout time.

May 26, 2000
