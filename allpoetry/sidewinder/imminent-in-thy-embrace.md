title: "Imminent In Thy Embrace"

Imminent in thy embrace,
Honest in those emotions,
Mysterious beyond a shadow,
New to love,
Yet simple...
wherein those eyes touch,
Freedom reigns
to touch a new hope,
Into the great wide open
and listen to those passions
which rise under the stars,
Shed no tears
only a smile within the dawn,
Echoes within those whispers
which rise in a horizon,
Attentions met in those summers
long ago.

Aug. 5, 2000
