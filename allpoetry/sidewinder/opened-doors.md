Exaltations rising in the full moon
through the lessons of nature
I see with my heart,
Beyond mysteries,
held by valor,
Centered by truth,
Championed in images, 
Reached by those who listen
forever held in honor,
A journey celebrated
through the light,
Rainbows rising in prisms
following a touch of the mystic,
Unique by its principles,
Felt by a heartbeat at a time,
Visions considered 
one link spiraling upward,
Shaped by teachings
which open doors
manipulated in new realities
authentic by a touch of magic.

April 13, 1997
