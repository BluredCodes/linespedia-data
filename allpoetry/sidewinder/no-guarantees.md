There are no guarantees,
No certainties,
Life is what you make it,
Nothing less,
Nothing more,
With that you can't expect anything,
All though if you have what it takes
you can build a solid life,
And though now they are but dreams,
Each and everyone can come true,
But love is needed
along with trust,
Take it a day at a time,
And what you leave
can be awesome!

April 24, 1985
