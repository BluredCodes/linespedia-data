title: "This I Ask"

Give me strength
to see each day though
for the days into weeks,
Weeks into months,
Months into years,
Also I ask for wisdom,
insight & stamina
for with this I shall improve myself
and live a long life,
yes...
Peace is inside me
and happiness leads my life through eternity.

March 5, 1982
