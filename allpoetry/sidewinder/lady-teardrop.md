title: "Lady Teardrop"
writer: "sidewinder"

The dreams of yesterday have gone,
Passed away,
But in the memory
I recall a certain someone,
And I'll smile forever
because she touched me,
Yes in my heart...
there will be a place,
Why?
Because in this world
of fleeting tomorrows
she gave me my today.

Lady Teardrop,
let me hold you close
and wipe the tears away,
Don't worry about a thing,
Sure tomorrow will come
but this I have to say,
I love you!
And as long as you need me
I'm in your arms to stay,

I still can see her face,
I can feel it so close,
I can almost touch her,
And in someways...
it gives me a scare,
Why?
Because the wanting in me...
never goes away,

Yes...
I love her!
And there's never a day
that I don't wish for her,
Yes...
my hungry heart aches,
Lord, I wish that things were
still the same.
