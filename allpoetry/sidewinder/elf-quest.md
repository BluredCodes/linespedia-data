Elf Quest...
in that where is mine?
prepare myself in mind,
Body
and spirit,
Fulfilling a journey to my soul,
To touch the ecstasy
regardless of the consequences
a voice awaken my soul,
telling me wisdom
and to share it with those
who would believe,
A burning desire awaits,
In this...
I will grow,
Listen to the wind
and hear the cry of the wolf song,
tell the stories that Gaia gives,
Search for a bridge of destiny,
Rise within thy challenges,
Howl in the night
for those who would remember,
Complete thyself in heart,
Intone thy voice,
Celebrating eternity.

Jan. 28, 1998
