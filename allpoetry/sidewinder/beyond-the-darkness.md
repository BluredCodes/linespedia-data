title: "Beyond The Darkness"
writer: "sidewinder"

Does not love
seek those who
do not expect it?
Met only by beautiful strangers
who pass each other
beyond the darkness,

Eyes meet with a greeting,
Talking without words affecting
nevermore the same,

Desires touch
completing those circles
with a rage beyond a memory
fulfilling
wherein only...
the shadow meets the light,

Heart attune for all of time,
Each complimenting the other so fine,
Racing thoughts...
Breathless sighs...
Fire igniting both the eyes,

And there are those
who wait...
for the courage
just to say hello.

March 13, 2000
