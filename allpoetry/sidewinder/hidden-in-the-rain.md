---
title: "Hidden In The Rain"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "hidden-in-the-rain"

Anticipation rises out of the need
for those who dare
reaching a point where we belong,
Yet in some corner
the memory touches a heart,
Those smiles
lie in answers
found only
in the night,
Yesterday's mire into today
where laughter has its own reply,
Listen to those shadows
that echo
hidden in the rain.


May 15, 1999
