---
title: "Words By The Few"
writer: "sidewinder"

Words by the few
are like dreams of the many,
In their eyes you can see the hopes & aspirations
which take many years to make,
Tears will be shed,
But through those heartaches
something will be achieved,
What I can not say,
But if fought for
it can be said
well worth it! 

March 22, 1985
