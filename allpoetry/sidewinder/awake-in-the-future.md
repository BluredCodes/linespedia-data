---
title: "Awake In The Future"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "awake-in-the-future"

Clouds raging
               past those sunsets
fervent against Earth's final tears,
Reflections soothe the flame
radiant in the miracle of time,
                  Mystical in expressions
jaded where passion
                     touch a rainbow
exotic in spirit
                     stirring that compassion
a step
                      upon a step
wherein joy renew itself,
Enter deep expressions
                          blossoming
awake
                      in the future.

June 7, 2001
