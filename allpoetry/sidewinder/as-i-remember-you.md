---
title: "As I Remember You"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "as-i-remember-you"

I know your eyes,
They touch a soul,
Hidden to those who have not yet looked,
Ripe in celebration,
To touch a heart
held within the first moon,
Scarlet crystals shimmering red
as a petal of a rose,
And the splendor of time
varied in a song,
Where is the smile that touches me
in my dreams?
Invocations to a secret past,
Remember this,
As I remember you.

Oct 25, 1999
