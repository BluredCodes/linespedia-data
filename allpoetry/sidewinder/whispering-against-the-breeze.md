title: "Whispering Against The Breeze"

The water is glistening
green
and here are the waves
reflecting images beyond all imagination,
shared by a truth
shining like the sun in the sky
invoking the clouds
which float like a feather
whispering against the breeze,
And through the emerging day
a silence can be heard
celebrating the sheer beauty,
Can you feel the sense of it all?
A purity comes around,
life's joys can be overwhelming,
Time when taken for granted
moves like a thief in the night,
Before you know it,
You wonder....
                Where has it gone?

Sept. 27, 1996
