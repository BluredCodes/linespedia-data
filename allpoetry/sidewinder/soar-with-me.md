title: "Soar With Me"

Soar with me,
Let's touch the blue of the sky,
Find those familiar peaks,
 Revel in the freedom
met in the silence of the wind,
Come fly with me
in the forgotten canyon,
Where we can celebrate
those sunsets
touched beyond the horizon,
Meet those eternal needs
which cry & call you 
only to play
like a child
who smiles in innocence,
Find that rhythm
which dances
in the celebration of life.

Dec. 15, 2001
