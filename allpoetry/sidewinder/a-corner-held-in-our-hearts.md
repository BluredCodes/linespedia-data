title: "A Corner Held In Our Hearts"

Touching those things
arrived in my mind,
Jealous of those ideas
that most want to have,
But are afraid to put forward,
Why?
Is it that we want change,
But in that change
we are afraid to fail?
For every time we fall,
We learn something,
If nothing else,
We won't fall that way again,
We need to make that exchange
bristled in those paths
only in the night,
And touch what we need to touch
in a corner
held in our hearts.

Sept. 7, 2001
