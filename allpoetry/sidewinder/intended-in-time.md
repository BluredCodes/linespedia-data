April nights
held where a moon shines
intended in time,
Trusted by those unsung songs,
Mindful of a memory,
Serious in an uncontemporary way,
Aspects of those dreams
felt where a smile
opens up a heart,
Offered only in time,
Exotic & intoxicating
within a recognition,
Precious to those who wait.

Nov. 4, 1999
