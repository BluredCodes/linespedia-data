title: "Ghosts Of Today"

Sitting here
with a cup of coffee in hand,
My vision is fading,
I'm trying to reason out my last job,
But only time
shines like a distant star,
Those who predict the future
may see
what is held only for me,
And the ghosts of today
haunt & howl
for a dangerous situation,
But I can't let that blind me,
The winds of freedom
release those bonds of reality,
Trust the eyes of imagination,
Open up a story
to satisfy that need to dream,
Purify your words
and listen...
Spirits will always bring the need.

Sept. 7, 1996
