---
title: "Look In My Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"

Pleasant dreams
here in the night
a call is heard,
And you are there
welcoming me into your life,
Why?
If suspicion enters here
you have to understand
my eyes
and reveal what's inside,
I know...
you have to walk through the door
to know what's on the other side,
 Traces of emotions
complete a heart varied
in exchanges in the cobweb of life,
Yet those fires...
sometimes are those in the soul,
Look in my eyes
and see...
            what you can.

Oct. 2, 2000
