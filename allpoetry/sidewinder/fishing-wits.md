---
title: "Fishing Wits"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "fishing-wits"

In my excitement
the river flows to entice me,
Life reveals those secrets that flow below,
I cross that current
to hunt that shadow gleaming
underneath the flickering of the wave,
I cast my rod...
And in a rage you fight for your freedom,
yet..
in the mastery of the line
your fate is just a matter of time,
The smile on my face
rages
for the war has just begun,
Will you slight the hook?
Or will I land you in your girth?
And when the battle is over,
Will I release you
to start another battle of wits
on another day?

Feb. 23, 2001
