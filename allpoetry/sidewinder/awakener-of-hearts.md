title: "Awakener Of Hearts"

Awakener of hearts
touch thy spirit,
For I am not complete
without a soulmate,
 I yearn for the day
that I find her,
But that is yet to come,
I know,
I must have patience,
Still...
It's a lesson hard taught,
Gentle consoler...
Where are you?
In your presence I am a child of innocence
waiting for our time to come,
Perhaps...
some day.

Oct. 3, 1998
