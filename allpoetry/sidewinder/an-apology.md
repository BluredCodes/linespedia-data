If I became too close,
	If I did something to offend,
	I meant nothing
	but to hear your heart,
	Feel your warmth,
	I hope you understand,
	I really....
	don't want to lose our friendship,
	Close friends are hard to find,
	In my blindness...
	I feel your sadness
	and those walls of shadows
	begin to fall,
	If I need
	to find an apology,
	Listen to my words my dear,
	We need to find an understanding
	and try to make this clear,
	But if you find
	our roads must part,
	I'll always understand,
	May the wind
	be always at your feet,
	And may you find happiness.
	July 3, 2000
	This I wrote for a friend...
	Cheyanne Smith...
	We had a misunderstanding...
	But we worked it out.
