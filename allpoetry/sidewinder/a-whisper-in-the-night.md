---
title: "A Whisper In The Night"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-whisper-in-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

A voice whispers
and the night beckons you
where once the silence overwhelms,
To touch where some dare not follow,
And in the glittering stone
a ritual promises to awaken
a spell on concealment,
Glimpses of the future foretold
focus toward the ghostwalking
in the sliver of the moon,
Circle after circle where some
experiment with time,
Shadows rise to touch a soul,
A moment of lucidity
where a gate opens to the shadows,
Forgetting the consequences,
And in the mortal dilemma
eternity shows its face
where Gothic tension
sprawls in a tale of horror,
And the rage of the wind
howls with bitter breath
wherein balance celebrates
the order forever held
in the universe.

Feb. 13, 1998
