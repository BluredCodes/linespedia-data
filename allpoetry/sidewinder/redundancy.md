Galleries,
whethered in memories,
Shattered against the pavement
and in the concrete jungle
the sounds and music resonates
above the beeps, honks and noises
that shred the city,
Under different circumstances
it would be enough,
But time sometimes changes things,
And now the silence of the country
satisfies my mind,
Justification in a vision of casualties
invokes an emotion
that is shared by dread,
I know,
How does that apply?
That's the point,
It doesn't,
Redundancy in an eternity
generated throughout the universe.

Sept. 1996
