---
title: "Everybody Loves Dreamer"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "everybody-loves-dreamer"
source: "AllPoetry"

Everybody loves me,
For I'm so cute,
To the days when I am gone,
Everybody misses me,
Cause everybody loves Dreamer,
Sublime
within thy heart,
Touched in a smile
mirrored
forever
within those dreams,
the visions I see are so hard,
To find out why must I live within
my own mind,
The people they know me,
My heart and my longing,
they love my thoughts,
My life is sublime,

We see you everyday and wonder
what to say to a heart so free,
Yet so melancholy,
You make us smile,
You make us dance,
You have the spunk of a lemur,
Is it a wonder why everyone loves Dreamer?

June 30, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn, Adreanna Copper, Allan Perry & William Randol
