---
title: "Sowing Those Rewards"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "sowing-those-rewards"
source: "AllPoetry"

Reaching through those hard rigid
struggles,
Follow unwarned,
Wondering the reason
for the Gothic silence
mired only
in nearness of death,
Eclipsed in the invocation
of the blade
jagged
only in its measure,
That paranoia
felt in fear
which rages
in a river of blood,
Sired in the loins
of a homeless, wondering solitude,
That loneliness felt in a
cringed, helpless, smile,
Scarred beyond recognition
where you reach a lifetime
sowing those rewards that you reaped.

Dec. 5, 2001
