---
title: "Forever Be My Friend"
writer: "sidewinder"

Rising eyes,
Flirtations raised in a passion,
Touching those passions
like embers found in a fire
that none can put out,
Yes...
Sometimes that's how I see you,
At the same time...
the wisdom that you use
echoes in my mind,
Still in other times
you find time to be a mother
and the comfort you find
brings a page in the memory
of a past forever remembered,
You are my friend,
Though we don't always agree,
Still...
you remind me of my mortality,
And the mistakes...
                                I can make,
You make me laugh...
A smile I can see...
                                 in your eyes
like mischief found in a child's mind,
Touch me the way
only you can,
And forever keep me young,
Touch my soul
and forever be my friend.

Sept. 23, 2000This was written for friend called Tosha
