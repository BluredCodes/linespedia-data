---
title: "Let The Games Begin"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "let-the-games-begin"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28691-Let-The-Games-Begin-by-sidewinder"
---

Who me?
You think that I need discipline?
Forget that!!!
I am a wild child, 
Chaos is my brother
and we travel free in the night,
There are those fools
who think they can tame my soul,
But it's a game
only to be lost in the wind,
Echoes reach
but only to find silence,
And in the shadow
I can touch a vague memory,
Or inspire the violence that hides inside,
Fortune reveals a fate
forever embraced in secrecy,
And I will never tell,
So if those who would chain me
and bind my destiny
face more trouble
that one could conceive,
But maybe that's their fate,
Who can tell?
I...
on the other hand,
Prefer to make my own fate,
Let the games begin.

July 10, 1999
