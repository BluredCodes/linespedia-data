title: "Poets Of Yesteryear"

I remember those poets of yesteryear
that touched the hearts of the youth,
Some criticized what we stood for,
Folk music played as we
stood out in our sit-ins
with posters crying,
"Give peace a chance"
though we didn't see the other side,
We only knew what
the radio, newspapers & TV told us,
We never knew
that soldiers were fighting for their lives
and had to back each other up anyway
that they could,
Some left the country & wouldn't fight,
So they called them draftdodgers,
No one wanted the horror called Vietnam,
But some said that it was honor
to fight for their country,
Yet the politics
raged on,
But who could have known
that a mother's tears would flow.

May 13, 2001
