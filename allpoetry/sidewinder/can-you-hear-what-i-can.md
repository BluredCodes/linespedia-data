---
title: "Can You Hear What I Can?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "can-you-hear-what-i-can"

To those that keep
sparkle and laughter in their eyes,
A smile that touches
where a feeling spirit
touches a heart,
And the child inside smiles
transcending those dreams
forever hidden in our imaginations,
Intense in its addition,
Challenges rise
but in the innocence
a balance is met,
We just have to reach for it,
And keep in mind
those friends of Faerun
that keep the joy in all of our lives,
Yes...
I hear a changeling saying hello,
Or a troll beckoning
to travel with him on his next adventure,
Can you hear what I can?

July 10, 1999
