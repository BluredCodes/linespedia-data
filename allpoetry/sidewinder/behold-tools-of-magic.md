---
title: "Behold Tools Of Magic"
writer: "sidewinder"

Behold tools of magic
consecrated by dragon power,
Let us be one in magic,
Invoke their ageless wisdom,
Ancient magic for a new age,
Energies in harmony with the world around you,
Dragons do exist,
Ride the dragon's breath
and see with open eyes,
 Touch those astral realms between realms
and gaze into the future,
Traits within ourselves
they show us,
Mysteries within the universe can be shown
of an open mind is revealed,
Could it be that we
should listen?
Stay in touch,
Enchantments discover the astral realms,
Speak the charm of making
through journey's of myth & legend,
Build your cone of power &
direct the energy with assistance
from the faerie world.

Dec. 30, 2000
