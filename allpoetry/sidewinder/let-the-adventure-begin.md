title: "Let The Adventure Begin"

Traveling the streets
here they are telling a tale,
Welcome my friend!
It's great to be alive!
Feel the breeze of a moment
revolving a single sound,
But sometimes silence tells you even more,
For a soul harkens to a memory,
And in that light
reality fades to a melody
that calls forever in our hearts,
Come play and join in the fun,
Leave your those burdens
that you've built for so many years,
Let the dreams that you've dreamed
become a reality
and let the adventure begin.

June 26, 1996
