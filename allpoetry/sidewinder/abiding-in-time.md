---
title: "Abiding In Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "abiding-in-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

Legends are full of heroes and heroines
touched only by time,
Yet those miracles
rise in the promise
where chivalry and traditions
are chronicled by the song of the bard
and etched in the songs
are rhythms of sound
that are endlessly echoed throughout time,
Portraying those miracles
that forever last,
Recoiling the pride
of the visions of the past,
Therefore a celebration is met
awakened in passages
wherein the lore connects
in sacred understanding,
Gently touching the soul,
Gently arousing those feelings
only our hearts and minds can know
abiding in time
the miracles of life.


Oct. 14, 2000
