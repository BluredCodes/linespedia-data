title: "The Secret Garden"

Kept past of a dream,
Sheltered within a miracle,
A raven touches
within the branches
of my secret garden,
Scented in lilacs,
And you wander down a path
that ambers and stumbles
for a forgotten season
reborn in the quiet of the leaves,
 Listen to the past,
 Hear the whispers and laughter
of lovers that walked
before,
Sonnets met in memories
where eyes forever touch,
Only to find
a stream that touches the soul.

July 6, 2000
