title: "And In The Dawn"
writer: "sidewinder"

And in the dawn
an awakening
touched the wisdom
reclaimed only by a promise,
Wherein a joy is found
to touch in a memory,
Forever held in the twilight,
Those horizons only found
in those yesterdays,
Somehow you must look with an open mind,
Find those treasures
that somehow reveal themselves
within the patience of time itself,
Miracles do happen,
But sometimes...
they happen when we least expect them
or they happen right before our eyes,
But somehow we don't realize
until like the shadow of the day
they disappear,
But in that revelation
we live and learn
Can you hear the whispers in the wind?
Listen if you can.

Feb. 7, 2001
