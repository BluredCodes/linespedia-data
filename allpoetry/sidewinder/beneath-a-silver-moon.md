---
title: "Beneath A Silver Moon"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "beneath-a-silver-moon"
source: "AllPoetry"

Shadows into the night,
	Rages that touch within,
	Can I hold that which
	forever meets the dark,
	Yet in my soul
	complete the light
	where those guardians can protect
	essences of those that balance
	where in time magic touches,
	Sacred through those meditations
	drawing on my strength
	and relying on the Lady herself
	to the web of life itself,
	Beneath a silver moon,
	Forever understanding those lessons
	that challenges the heart.
	Oct 15, 1999
