Soar beyond Illusions!
The unwary soul who falls to grapple
with the mocking demon of illusion
will return to the Earth
as the slave of illusion,
Silence fills those hallways
that we all walk through,
But somehow we miss
the clues,
The whys matter not
for in wisdom
we meet to touch ourselves
like a door found only in reality,
Yet...
the dream completes itself
like those lyrics
that completes a song,
Miraculously journeys
are essential in thy sight.

Nov. 8, 2000
