title: "As The Sunlight Touches"

As the sunlight touches
	a brilliance overflows my eyes,
	The snow has been falling since yesterday,
	Finally the sun crept out
	and is making up for the rest of the day,
	Here I am enjoying the silence,
	From the distance you can hear the cars
	from a nearby highway,
	Sometimes I can hear my neighbors
	discovering the daytime,
	And here it is a quiet Sunday afternoon,
	Some would say that I am wasting the day,
	But I say...
	                  I'm just enjoying the peace,
	Though I can say...
	                          I'm glad winter is nearly over,
	I'm very much waiting for the warmth of springtime,
	I can hardly wait....
	                            for the warmth to touch my skin,
	So I don't have to hear the cold winter wind.
	March 16, 2002
