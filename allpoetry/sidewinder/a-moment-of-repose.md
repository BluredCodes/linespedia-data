In a moment of repose,
 A feeling came over me,
A warmth...
              tingling on the back of my neck,
Was it a past memory?
                           A haunting...
                                        laughing at my sadness?
Never you mind...
                    Somehow it was lost
a long time ago,
                     And now...
                                it doesn't matter,
Still...
          the longing remains.

Aug. 7, 2000
