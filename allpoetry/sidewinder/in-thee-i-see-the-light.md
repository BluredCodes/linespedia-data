In thee I see the light preordained in a festival of time
merged with the comings and goings
of a heart filled with love,
Shall it focus through an essence
of Odin's gift,
Sacred in its teachings?
Logic guides,
Opening new doors
in a successful journey,
Whether in the past
 a lifetime rejoined,
Or in the future
teachings yet to be learned,
Its only you that can be responsible,
And only then can a choice be made,
Wisdom then is learned
and laughter is sought,
For life is always a joyful journey.

July 30, 1997
