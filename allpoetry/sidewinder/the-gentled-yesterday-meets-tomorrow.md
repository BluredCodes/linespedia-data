title: "The Gentled Yesterday Meets Tomorrow"

Enchanted in thy memory,
Radiant in thy hope,
Where a warmth
reveals dreams divine,
And the wind sings a song
ancient in an echo,
a gentle breath touches a past of a soul,
Only to meet your eyes
where your smile
touches something deep inside
and the gentled yesterday
meets tomorrow.

July 10, 2000
