title: "Rebirth Of A Sheltered Time"

Forsworn
in pleasures
 created by a touch of magic,
Those traditions
in a single breath of the faerie,
Acquired in secrets
held by the otherworld
which correspond with the elements,
Those destined in realms of the sacred,
Gained by the wisdom of the shaman
in the wheel of existence
developing second sight,
For the power of the shaman and the seer
foresees the future of those
who journey in the time of renewal,
Those rituals held in blessings
provided in a guided discipline,
A flame in the spirit,
A vision for those
who reach toward the heart.
