title: "In The Shadows Of Rains"

In the shadows of rains
that fall,
A cleansing takes place
and old pains are put where they belong....
in the past,
New beginnings enrich us,
Held by a mystery
a simplicity is implied,
Fortuitous in their own pleasure,
Can it be said that time
reveals its own treasure at its own leisure?
Consider the moment held,
Never take it seriously,
For the moment can slip away
like a ghost in the wind, 
Invisible to those who look too hard
and take things too much for granted
formidable in their own making.

Oct. 4, 1996
