title: "Beyond The Prophet's Eye"

Beyond the prophet's eye
a trust evaded by lifetime's
too few
and too many to remember,
A sight to justify,
Jeered and jaded
by a judgement,
reached by the sight
of a blind man's eye,
Berated
by a selfless touch,
Frustrated
by the hush
of a silence
that rages like the townhall's bell,
Sensation's
felt in a moment
cascading in a generation of time.

Oct. 2, 1996
