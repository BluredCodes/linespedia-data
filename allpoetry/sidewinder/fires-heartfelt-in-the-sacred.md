title: "Fires Heartfelt In The Sacred"

Necessity overwhelms those
who rage in contemplations,
An inner fire
heartfelt in a sacred time,
There are those that touch the sphere,
And are above themselves in the night sky
remembering the ancient teachings,
An essence of the divinity,
A spirit made manifest,
Traditionally called the light of order,
Spiraling upwards,
Forever held by the promise of the moon,
Within this plain exists the waking world,
In this an understanding takes place
which provides insight into the energy of each cycle,
An intimate relationship with nature,
Tale after tale recounts the shamans journey
in a world of the faerie
which resides by the seven streams of wisdom,
Held by a vision quest,
An encounter with those
who rises in laughter
& magically shines with a
twinkle in their eyes.

Nov. 21, 1997
