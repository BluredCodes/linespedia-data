title: "Dark Corners Watching"

Reflections touch
like voices forgotten
distant in an echo of time,
Yet...
in that dream
shadows rage
lost behind the sanity,
A calm appearance
touches in sight,
But...
What remains?
Dark corners watch
for their chance,
Silent....
         Patient....
                      Waiting....
to the essence
of the madness
omnipotent in their delirium, 
                                 Hear those whispers
              in the night?
Mischievous in their laughter.

March 30, 2001
