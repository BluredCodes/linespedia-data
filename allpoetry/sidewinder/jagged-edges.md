I hear water running across,
And in my mind this occurs to me,
This room is cluttered and filled to the brim
like a cup running over,
Moments come like jagged edges,
And faces haunt me from forgotten moments,
Memories shout at me,
Taunting and fear overcomes,
Here I am...
Can't you see?
My mind is overclouded,
Moments are not clear,
My hand touches my face,
Rough...
Unshaven,
Pain fills my temples
and another day begins.

April 17, 1996
