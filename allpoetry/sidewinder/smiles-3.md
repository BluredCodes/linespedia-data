To touch in that soft sunshine
	                  forever met by a warm breath,
	     Creeping syllable by syllable
	               where eyes...
	                            eventually meet,
	A question comes to mind,
	                         When can I meet you again?
	Does thy soul reach for another
	         to complete thy reason?
	Or is it just
	            an
	               illusion
	                       met by a smile?
	Mysterious nymph
	                 touch me...
	for thy beauty touches
	                     beyond an horizon
	and my heart
	            will never
	                      be the same
	and your smile
	                takes...
	                          my breath away.
	
	Aug. 15, 2001
