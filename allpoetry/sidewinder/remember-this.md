Some say that Burcher's fire
was done by the government.
Caused by too many regulations.
Too much politics
made by our politicians in Washington.
It could have been stopped
But wasn't.
Just a little common sense
But somehow that was forgotten.
Why ?
Instead millions of dollars were spent
in resources
where those dollars could have helped to feed the poor.
Maybe a job that's needed.
Too much taken for granted.
We the people have a responsibility
to make sure that the politicians remember this.
If they don't....
                     then vote them out....
and find one's that will.
The cause is there.
We just have to yell loud enough
for them to hear
not say that it's someone else's problem.
Remember this...
                   Next time its time to vote !
