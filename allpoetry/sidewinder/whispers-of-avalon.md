title: "Whispers Of Avalon"

A melody awaits for thine,
Lore from thy heart
when seasons whisper in winds 
among dreams gliding in the meadows,
Listen for the Faerie,
They call when you least expect them,
Celebrate when nature
rises in full bloom,
Soothe thy soul
with only a touch of magic,
It appears in everything,
But to find it,
You have to have an open heart,
The source can fill an ancient promise,
A mystery revolving in time
when flute and harps
were more than a legend,
Castles rise beyond romance
and dragons honored by a bard's tale,
The Isle of Avalon awaits thee,
A gift of forever within thy eyes.

May 5, 1997
