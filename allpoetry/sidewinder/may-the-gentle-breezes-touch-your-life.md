May the gentle breezes
touch your life
today on the strength of heaven,
Hear the whispers that the wind may bring,
Look for the truth in the flame of the campfire,
Hear the echo of remembrance in the stillness of a pool,
Touch the shadow of your ancestors,
For their wisdom may hold promises
fulfilling a destiny,
Cling to their words,
For a reckoning may come to pass,
Cherish the freedom that is held in your heart,
Smile at the enchantment of the moon,
For she teaches the mysteries that life brings,
Coming full circle.

Sept. 24, 1998
