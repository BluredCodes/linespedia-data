title: "Phantoms Of Long Lost Heroes"

In the sliver a moon shadow
the unseen pendulum swings
in a sensuous rhythm,
A paradox in a mythical point of view,
Those phantoms of long lost heroes
who rescued maidens from dragons and villains
in fairie tales from a justified past,
A touch of magic in mysteries
where a hero can become a legend
in a time ancient in a wisdom of elves
who reveal those truths that
we all take for granted,
A sanctuary in realms
long forgotten,
A quest to the otherworld
sacred in a gateway held by a heart, 
Symbol of the flaming door.

Nov. 14, 1997
