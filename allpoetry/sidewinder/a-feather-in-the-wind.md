title: "A Feather In The Wind"
writer: "sidewinder"

Spirits guide
where a soul touches,
Mired within,
Hallowed in pride,
Metaphysical in thy reach,
These gentle transformations
in a sacred path of awakening,
Touched like a feather in the wind,
Feel thy peace,
Remembered in mediation,
Renewed in heart
unconditionally,
Fill your body and being
upward to the center of the universe,
A oneness felt in a breath at a time,
See a webwork
revealed within the flow,
Renewed, refreshed, relaxed,
I am that I am,
Life meant in that cosmic revelation,
A consciousness
encompassed
forever.

Sept. 8, 2001
