title: "A Whisper Calls You"

Touching those ponds
where a white duck
flutters,
Swirling
              where bluegills
rage amongst
                                the shore,
Geese honk in the sunset
insisting that you feed them,
Rippling,
                      Excited by their call,
The breath of spring
touches in the horizon,
And in that wind
a whisper
             calls you,
Meet me
                     in that clear note
of joy.

Dec. 18, 2001
