title: "Touched By The Past"

Reflection of the moment
charmed by a memory
of one who captures a soul,
Whispering secrets and keys
to a heart touched by the past
forever in emotions,
Reaching only for a moment
that is held by a single touch,
Like a flame of a candle
flickering in the wind,
Never knowing when it's time
has come or gone.

May 11, 1996
