---
title: "Where  Are They?"
writer: "sidewinder"

As you walk into Walmart
see those children's faces
missing in their families lives,
Who were they?
Some say...
            Who knows?
Still...
It matters,
                Why?
For the sheltered love
breathes in the air
and pain screams
even though smiles are pictured
in the window of time,
Speaking to those who would bear witness
syllable by syllable,
Where are they?

May 13, 2001
