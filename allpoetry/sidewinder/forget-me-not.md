---
title: "Forget Me Not"
writer: "sidewinder"

There raves a lady
	with auburn hair,
	And eyes as green as emeralds,
	Her love was as true as can be,
	For love to her was eternal,
	she glowed in her face,
	And the smile she shown
	could make a man disregard his own life,
	For she had a way about her,
	And in this she had her charms,
	Oh lady,
	Forget me not!
	For if you do,
	I shall turn to despair,
	Please keep my memory as you please,
	For I shall never forget you,
	You're in my dreams & fantasies,
	Yes...
	your love had gripped me forever.
	March 23, 1982
