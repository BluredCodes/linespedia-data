---
title: "But The Ghost of Time Remains"
writer: "sidewinder"

Route 66,
Ice cold coca cola,
Images of a restaurant
surrounded by a group of bikers
getting ready to take the sunset,
Memories of the 50's,
Rebels of the heart,
Bobby socks
tied by drive inns,
Buddy Holly,
James Dean,
Finbacks and roller skates,
Poodle skirts,
Jukebox a playing, 
Teens having fun,
Nearness of a dream coming to an end,
And my mind
rummages through old memories
felt by good vibrations,
And I smile...
as if I've seen an old friend,
But the ghost....
of time remains.

June 18, 2000
