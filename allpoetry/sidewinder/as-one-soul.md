title: "As One Soul"

Mired through all those promises
touched in ways never imagined,
Held in hope that still lingers,
Held in a warm embrace
that seems nonexistent,
Heart to heart,
Eyes to eyes,
Body to body,
That unites as one soul
within that miracle that we all hold
celebrated in time,
Can you hear the melody?
Will this melody last forever?
Or will it slowly fade out
like true love that is lost?

May 4, 1999

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Christina M. Schultz
