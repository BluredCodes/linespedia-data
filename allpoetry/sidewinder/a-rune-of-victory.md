title: "A Rune Of Victory"

A rune of victory,
Touch the knowing self,
Edge within thy heart, 
Celebrate the seasons,
Offerings to the Goddess,
Honor the rebirth of a new life,
Prose in light,
Share in spirit
illumination
transforming nature within the heart,
Sacred in wisdom,
Forever touch balance,
A destiny within thy reach,
A symbol shared by the past,
A door conceived in love,
Touch the center within thy self,
A breakthrough in faith,
A journey forever remembered,
But take heart....
Joy is within the water of life.

Oct. 27, 1997
