---
title: "Under An Azure Sky"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "under-an-azure-sky"
source: "AllPoetry"

Emotions touch
in rhythms
wherein the magic is met,
Gracefulness completes
the silence
varied only
beyond the shadow
of what we knew before,
Confident in the presence
 of the nimbus
that enclose us
and make us remember
the dance,
listen to the muse within,
She'll show a world
that some only dream of,
Endured through those ecstasies
and pools of dreams
in the passion of a heart
wherein a tapestry rich and dark
plays the music of the soul,
Crescendo and plummet the voice of the song,
The cadence of water
rippling through until all is spotless,
A passion renewed
under an azure sky.

Oct. 14, 2000
