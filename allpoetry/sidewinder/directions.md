Reflections of love,
perhaps a touch
where once a light shined,
Celebrations rage
in a heart that needs completion,
Forever yearning
in a time beyond common,
 Reaching into a soul
where eons stand still,
And I can see her smiling,
But the roads that we traveled
were in different directions,
Still that doesn't change
my heartbeat
or the
loneliness that rises within,
So now I ask the question...
Where is my soul mate
that fulfills my destiny?
