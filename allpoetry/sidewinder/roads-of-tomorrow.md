---
title: "Roads Of Tomorrow"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "roads-of-tomorrow"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can't find a reason...
to let go,
Just because those ghosts
of yesterday
touch those tears
that we've all cried,
But somehow...
I won't let the blues
take over,
In my mind,
I've been over and over it,
But it doesn't make much sense,
So I go on...
Hold on to the good memories
and let life take its course,
What will be...
will be...
And those roads of tomorrow
will take me
where they will.

June 18, 2000
