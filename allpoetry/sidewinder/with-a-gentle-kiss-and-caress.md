title: "With A Gentle Kiss And Caress"

Trust those who seek
those truths revealed within time,
Reawaken a heart
only met with love,

A heart that was closed
opened up to see
the world around it
and now it included me,

Midsummer in dreams
where eyes touch and reach
those places where some dare not,
Fantasy and desire her combine
in the eyes of love and mine,
Where will it take us,
Who can tell,

And in that revelation
the story only begins
wherein a possibility
is varied
to touch those
who look beyond a veil
showed only by the darkness.

March 30, 2000
