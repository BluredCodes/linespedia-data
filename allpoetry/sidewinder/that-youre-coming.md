title: "That  You're Coming"

They tell me that you're coming,
And it's not that I doubt it,
But in this world of understanding
there has to be so much more,
Many have touched on religion,
But faith is so personal,
Each stands on its own,
Who can judge what's in a heart?
Not me!
I don't even try,
But when it comes time
I think I can stand on my own merit,
It'll be between him and me,
In life you do the best that you can.
From there you go on and not worry.

May 8, 2001
