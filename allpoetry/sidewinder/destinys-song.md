title: "Destiny's Song"

Heralded in the midnight sun
	betwixt the dawn and the sunrise,
	Memories touch in the miracle of the eye
	and realization has set in,
	Illusion has its grandeur,
	Yet....
	it is beyond your grasp,
	Mysteries rise beyond the veil
	where dreams touch in your life
	caressed in the misty vale,
	Windfalls seek their truth
	down the river of time
	honored in their passing,
	And the magic cries in destiny's song,
	Expressed by a love,
	Mystical in its outpour,
	Verified by the silence
	that rages in its relevance.
	March 30, 2001
