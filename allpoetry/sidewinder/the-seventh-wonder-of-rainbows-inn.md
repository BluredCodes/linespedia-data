Twilight
at the shadow's gate
remaining at the sounds
and memories of ones mind,
sparkles and glimmerings of Rainbow's shining face,
Can it be said that one look
touches a part of one's soul?
For that is the question that remains...
unanswered,
Legends...
and tales that the bards only know,
Who was she?
a gift from the faerie world
that only few humans ever gazed upon,
Those that were honored by her presence,
Knew her only for a glimpse of time,
But in that memory
it was forever held,
Such as its known as the seventh wonder of Rainbow's Inn.

March 17, 1996
