title: "Against The Sky"

Blue waters
touch in the ethereal of time
wherein those miracles happen,
yet in the shadows...
mystery reveals
a promise
held only in a horizon,
Tenuous in its silence,
Deceptive as a shadow
where a hunger waits,
And in the lingering those gentle breaths
whisper a secret
against the sky.

July 18, 2000
