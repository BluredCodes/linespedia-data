title: "Look Beyond The Pages"

A stubborn old Capricorn
	But I'm not the predictable kind,
	I'm not practical,
	I'm emotional,
	You can't stick me behind a 9 to 5 desk,
	I must write,
	And no...
	I don't belong in the city,
	I'm a country boy at heart,
	Put a fishing pole in my hand and I'm happy,
	Yet...
	I like the Internet,
	Some say too impersonal,
	I say...
	there I can meet friends
	without being judged,
	Yet...
	I'm a walking contradiction,
	But that's the way it is,
	Some say I'm a nerd,
	But the lovable kind,
	some say a hippy and a radical,
	So..
	I'm opinionated,
	Is that so wrong?
	My point is
	that you can't judge a book
	by the cover,
	Look beyond the pages
	and find what you can. 

	Aug. 12, 2000
