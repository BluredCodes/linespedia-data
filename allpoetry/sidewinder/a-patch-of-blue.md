---
title: "A Patch Of Blue"

Out of the smoke
and the tears of someone's eyes
of an old overcrowded world
came a patch of sunshine,
a moment of love came true,
And for a moment forgotten tears were lost,
this came to be...
a moment held close
that is...
to me,
Too long has it happened, 
Tomorrow we may never see,
And the secrets of the past
no longer matter,
Can't you see?
You were...
a patch of blue
for me.

April 27, 1985
