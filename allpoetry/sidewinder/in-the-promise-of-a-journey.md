title: "In The Promise Of A Journey"

Pledged by the wisdom of Athena,
A gate from the past
to reveal a center
in the passion of life,
Journeys may go on,
Though some end,
But in the promise of the adventure
coincides in the depth
of a revelation
that points to all corners
universally
immersed in a prism
that shines brightly
for all to see
provisionally that the choice
given freely
resumes for everyone.

Oct. 2, 1996
