title: "If I Only Heard A Hello"

Dusty,
smoke filled rooms
and here I am
touching time and 
making ends meet,
Forever wishing
that a soul
would rise to meet my heart,
Too many nights spent alone,
And the seasons in the sun
have marched to years
but skies are now raining,
Where are the answers
to the questions never asked?
If only I heard a hello
then maybe...
the nights will not be so long.

May 1, 1999
