title: "A Silhouetted Promise"

In the chaos
of a summer filled
siloquence
complicated in mysteries
an answer arrives
in a silhouetted promise,
crystal shires touch
the moonlight,
In the tomorrow an expression
moves the current of desire, 
Compassionate yet fierce,
Immanent in the lesson of time,
Mocked in its very making
collected in the illusion which continues,
Harmony rides in rhythms
reflections of the world,
A soul in a journey
which is where magic is afoot,
A passage in new realms.

Nov. 17, 1997
