---
title: "Let Me Touch In Dreams"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "let-me-touch-in-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let me touch
in dreams,
Let me find my truth,
Let me show compassion
where
some need,
Let me find my soulmate
to complete my heart & my life,
And if by chance
my prayers are answered
then let me thank those above
for making sure I'm taken care of.

Oct. 26, 1998
