title: "Come Frolic In The Leaves"

Here in this world
touching a soul
where a whisper
rides on the wind,
Come frolic in the leaves,
Share in joy
and listen to the songs
in the distance,
Where the harmony
is celebrated
just for its own sake,
Remember in the dawn
those rainbows and treasures
and a smile held by a child.

April 30, 1999
