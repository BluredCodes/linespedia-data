One day while you're walking
in the woods or anywhere,
Stop...
close your eyes,
Breath deep,
Listen to the birds,
And after taking in all you can...
Open your eyes,
Experience the beauty around you...
and just listen,
Block out all other things
but your dreams and listen,
All the answers you need will be there,
So listen,
Just listen,
Listen to that wisdom
shared from the heart,
Complete from the echoes of our minds,
Show the love of just being,
No conceptions,
No prejudices,
Forget the programing,
Reach from the tears of your soul.

Nov. 18, 2000
