---
title: "In What I See"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "in-what-i-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45490-In-What-I-See-by-sidewinder"

Let me love
	that which is in my heart,
	Forever revealing that song
	which soars
	like an eagle in the sky
	met in a medieval prime
	defined
	as a poet,
	Immortal in that dream
	which drives me
	as a man,
	Eternal
	as the listener
	listens
	and finds meaning
	only....
	in what I see.
	Dec. 15, 2001
