title: "Old Mother Earth"

Green valleys as far as I can see,
So very beautiful
in my mind, 
Great looming plains
green with grass,
Rolling hills covered with blue bonnets,
These are the things I enjoy,
Natural beauty,
yes...
they are what I love,
Nothing made by man can compare with these,
Yes..
I am a naturalist,
At least by heart,
And there is nothing
that can compare with sweet old Mother Earth,
May it remain the same
forever.

March 1, 1982
