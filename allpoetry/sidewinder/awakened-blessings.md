---
title: "Awakened Blessings"
writer: "sidewinder"

Echoes in ancient stones,
Words held in shadows of the back of my mind,
Explore the gateway that you seek,
Change the time that rises between worlds,
Touch the dragon spiritual in its completeness,
Celebrate in happiness
a joy hidden right before your eyes,
Dream about mysteries that guard the otherworld,
A mirror for your conscience,
A perfect path remains
in the realm of the faerie,
Discover those days
held by a blessing,
Awakened mystically initiated to a heart within,
A rebirth for those
who see and believe in the Great Goddess.

Sept. 29, 1997
