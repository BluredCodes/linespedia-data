---
title: "If You Listen"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "if-you-listen"

Touch those dreams
that rise in expectations,
Step on top of step,
Staircasing into the sky,
But remember the celebrations of the heart,
Forever reach those memories that live on,
Rainbows and treasures found...
Yet lost,
Romances form a forgotten age
where a tear falls...
unexpectedly,
And a sigh is heard around the world,
Perhaps...
if you listen
laughter can touch
those who matter.

April 9, 1999
