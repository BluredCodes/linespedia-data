title: "Soul Touched"

Loyal as a knight
touched by the promise of dawn,
Anointed by The Lady,
Where in nature
celebrates your coming,
A true & trusted friend,
Never a care in this world,
Always something for a laugh,
Never mind the consequences,
If something is needed,
You're there,
Material things matter not,
If one needed the shirt off your back,
You'd give it,
Promise me this my brother,
When we meet in the next life,
Remember that our souls
will be connected
in those worlds
yet to be discovered.

June 6, 2000
I wrote this for my friend...Douglas Denning.
He is a true & trusted friend.
