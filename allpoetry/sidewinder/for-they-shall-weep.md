---
title: "For They Shall Weep"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "for-they-shall-weep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26382-For-They-Shall-Weep-by-sidewinder"

Forgive those
which touch in darkness
for they shall weep
sorrow in the purest of time,
Where only in thy dreams
a heart within a destiny
completed only in the shadow,
nevermore within thy touch,
Verily within thy mind,
Lost which rages only out of necessity,
yet,
Only in the silence
a soul is touched,
Spirals call in magic,
A harmony within thyself, 
Hear the changes
that we all need?
Return the breath of life
which awakens in the night.

Oct. 5, 1999
