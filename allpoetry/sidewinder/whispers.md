As through time
memories never change,
Constantly revolving,
a window of this universe,
shadows follow
like piercing shot of pain,
Edged deep within my brain,
Never letting me forget
the hollowness of my pride,
And at the same time
a voice whispers,
Hold on to what you dearly treasure most,
For it's in these troubled times
that a sparkle of hope
shines forever
like a diamond in the sun.

May 2, 1996
