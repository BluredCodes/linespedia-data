---
title: "In The Romance"

A dozen cups of coffee
and here I am waiting...
for inspiration
Yet in the silence of time
there are lessons...
to be learned,
for if
in one small measure
innocence can be captured,
The essence of your eyes
fulfills the promise of a miracle,
And life's blessings touches
in ways
intricate yet delicate,
Where an encounter
of our first meeting
is stuck in my mind,
Revolving like an old movie
caught in contrast
between black and white,
And in the romance of it all
the seasons shine in the sun.

May 9, 1999
