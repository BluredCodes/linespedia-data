title: "Those Night Winds"

Touching in the flame of night
her eyes shine
 like the stars above,
Those night winds
share a miracle
sheltered only by a promise, 
Can you find that song?
So you dare sing it out loud?
Can you complete that passion
forever met in an invitation of the heart, 
Whispered moment by moment,
Embraced
one thought at a time,
Those covenants
                    explored
                             from the heart
                                            intangible
                                                       in thy need.
Dec. 18, 2001
