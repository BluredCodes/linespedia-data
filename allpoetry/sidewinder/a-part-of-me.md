---
title: "A Part Of Me"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-part-of-me"

I know a kinship
in the likeness of a friend,
All the hopes & aspirations
rolled into one,
You my friend
are my life,
My breath flows from you,
As I take what I can
from your example,
the memories that I share from you
will always be a part of me,
And I hope that I am..
in some small way
an inspiration to you,
May life show happiness to you
forever.
