---
title: "I Am The Knight"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "i-am-the-knight"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27964-I-Am-The-Knight-by-sidewinder"
---

For those of you who don't understand fully
	the effect of wonder,
	Or perhaps won't look at fantasy,
	Please look at the words of others that do,
	So that when you want to change the way you look at
	an outcome in your life
	that was bad and make it into something good,
	If what you just read makes no sense to you,
	Put this work down,
	Make some toast,
	Go about your life
	and pick it up when you do...
	If you do.
	I am the knight,
	Look to me when you need to walk in someone's shoes,
	I am the knight,
	Look to the stars
	shining in my armor
	and wonder how they got there,
	I am the knight,
	And so are you,
	Look to yourself to find what is true,
	I am the knight,
	I take the road that's hard,
	I am the knight,
	And thoughts that are forsaken are my song,
	At night that lulls my soul to sleep,
	Through the bleak thoughts of other's lost in the shadows,
	Varied through those seasons
	held only
	by the promise that we keep,
	Rage...
	      Rage...
	least with thy hope,
	I will never forget
	those epics
	revealed in the magic of the sun,
	Hero some call me,
	But I do....
	           What I must do,
	Honor remains in a repast
	and we will be heard and seen
	in the twilight,
	Riding with banners
	high for those who came before
	and those yet to come,
	Will you keep the dream alive
	and forget not the wonder?
	To touch those stars
	far beyond reality
	and those dreams where
	chivalry touches
	those realms
	forever met in honor,
	And if you can see what it means
	than the promise
	reveals more than what
	some can see,
	Celebrate those dreams
	beyond Camelot,
	forever touched in passion
	when days were clean,
	Nights were safe
	and people true,
	Look to yourself,
	Find out who you are,
	where you fit,
	Because knights are who finish it,
	Bring my soul to arms
	so that my rage
	forever touches
	beyond a dragon's heart
	mired with passion
	fervent in a miracle
	held by a code
	serenaded in the song of a bard,
	The children's eyes...
	of faeries and
	love of nature,
	Dragons and thoughts of honor and pride,
	Thoughts of knights and compassion and mercy,
	Know that when you live in the world
	of monsters,
	Concrete and iron,
	Remember I am the knight,
	I am you neighbor,
	I am that one riding next to you on a bus,
	Sharing your roads,
	Living in your world,
	There Will always be someone like me,
	I am the knight,
	And before you forsake fantasy for fact,
	Remember I'm always at your back.
	June 12, 2000
