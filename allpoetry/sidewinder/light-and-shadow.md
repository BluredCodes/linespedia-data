Light & shadow
changing with time,
Emerging in rituals
Fulfilled in spirit,
A soul who inspires & guides us,
Balance in necessary,
Desire is the primal energy,
A song is carried forth of waves that become spheres
raging in seasons
mired in the trust that once was
celebrated & expressed through creativity,
Visualize that love,
It awakens your body center, 
Feel the energy
and place your palms flat on the ground,
Let the power sink through you in the earth.

Sept. 7, 1997
