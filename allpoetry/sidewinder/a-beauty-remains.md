---
title: "A Beauty Remains"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-beauty-remains"
source: "AllPoetry"

I regret nothing
where love finds more than just shelter,
Sometimes those rainbows that we find
are found where we least expect it,
And in the plainness of our eyes
a beauty remains past,
Only to be touched in the silence.

Nov. 13, 1999
