---
title: "I Never Knew Her (For Sarah )"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "i-never-knew-her-for-sarah-"

I never knew her, 
                    Heard her voice,
Saw her smile,
                                       But If I did...
I know that we'd be friends,
Sadness touches those who will miss her,
Why must pain touch those who
just want to be needed?
                             The world is saddened
by her going,
                              Silence overcomes,
Tears fall,
                      And somehow we must go on,
Still....
                     Why?
Could it be that she's found
a better place?
                             I'll sing a song for you tonight
and pray that you've found that place
where you can be happy,
                         May peace be with you

Jan. 10, 2002
