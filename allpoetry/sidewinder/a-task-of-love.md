---
title: "A Task Of Love"
writer: "sidewinder"

A task of love
honored beyond question
where in the eyes
a heart is met,
Touched only by a sacred understanding,
Fulfilled by glory,
Where those memories
are met by dreams
and touched in miracles,
Magic sought in sacred energy
forever reached in contentment,
deep needs are met,
Yet nature can hold only
the purest of intentions.

Oct. 18, 1999
