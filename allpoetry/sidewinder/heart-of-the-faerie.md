title: "Heart Of The Faerie"
writer: "sidewinder"

Touched In dreams
fulfilled by the miracles of time,
A sweet view of heaven,
Passions rise in the heart of love,
Flames celebrate in nature,
Wolves howl to connect themselves,
A revelation in the moon
for the night embraces
all who touch upon her silence,
And Mother Nature
laughs at those who takes her
too seriously,
For the rune of life
has its own sense of humor,
And in the touch of imagination
an elf smiles and winks,
He whispers you a secret
that you can only know,
And then promises
that he'll be back
when you least expect it,
Why is it that the young of heart
can only see the heart of the faerie?

Nov. 3, 1997
