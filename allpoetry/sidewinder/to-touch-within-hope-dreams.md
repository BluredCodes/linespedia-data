title: "To Touch Within Hope & Dreams"

To touch hopes & dreams,
To touch within that smile,
Held in a miracle
completing that circle
revolving time after time...
Bright in new promises
held only by the star above
verifying that which can be held
warm only in an embrace,
Those arms reaching out
wanting home
and found in a tear's eyes
only known by a child,
Timeless in that precarious journey of the heart,
An outstretched hand
looking for a friend

Dec. 27, 2001
