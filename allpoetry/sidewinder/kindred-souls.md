Kindred souls reach into the depths
that lie in realms of magic
held in the inner spirit
that rages in all of us,
Lore seduces the curious
authentic in its touch,
sacred to the energies
that rises beyond mere pleasure,
Created by songspell
which flows like living water,
Divine by a spark,
Place by the lady herself
to open new realms of perceptual
experience and insight.

June 22, 1997
