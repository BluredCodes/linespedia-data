Destiny rises of desolation,
 Mirrors forever held from the apocalypse,
And out of the ashes
time touches those who
claim a rage
within the chaos
linked in illumination,
Spiritual warriors battle in strength,
Transformations nourish those who look beyond
the obvious,
And a new generation calls on the shadows,
Pathfinders continue in solitary circles,
Necessity leading on the edge of humanity.

Oct. 21, 1999
