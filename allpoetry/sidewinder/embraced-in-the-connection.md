title: "Embraced In The Connection"

Rustic...
Familiar in its own rhythms,
A summer sky...
brown in the heart,
Embraced in the connection
of a season,
Yet the wind
sings these songs
echoed in a forgotten past,
Magnificent...
in a caliber of expression,
Hear a hawk in the sky
praises his freedom
and those lyrics of nature
fulfill a destiny.

July 7, 2000
