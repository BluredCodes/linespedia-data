title: "Soaring A Beat At  A Time"

Light bright wings
 fly warm in the air
touching freedom,
Soaring a beat at a time,
Celebrating those cries
where a soul
touches beyond time,
Nevermind those rude awakenings
which darken the moment,
Yet....
                   experience remains,
                                                  Can you see...
from my eyes
                                   thy completeness?
Or can it be
                    exhilaration
                                  fills
                                           thy need?
Those silver clouds
                           rage in joy,
Patient in the attention.

June 7, 2001
