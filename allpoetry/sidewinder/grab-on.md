Grab on
and touch that angel of life,
reveal that preciousness that rages on,
Let's touch in celebration
those hearts and heroes
remembered day by day,
We need this,
That cry
that lifts our hearts,
tears fall from our eyes,
yet in that moment
angels fly into a ray of light,
Beautiful....
only in a sacredness,
who says there are no miracles,
each who gave their lives...
are!
Can't we see
their brave souls
that touches us all?
Courage reaches
only in the brave of the night,
And I can't help...
being proud!!!
I sing to remember,
I pray to comfort,
Join with me
and remember them all.

Sept. 21, 2001
