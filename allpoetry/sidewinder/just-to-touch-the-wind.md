---
title: "Just To Touch The Wind"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "just-to-touch-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

I bid you goodbye,
For the highway beckons me
and I must go,
Touch those dreams that I've been searching for,
See new places
exotic and new,
Find new friends,
Maybe we'll meet again
in this world of madness,
But until then
I must do what I must do,
Touching freedom in the wind,
Living life to its fullest,
 Whispering those secrets,
 Creating new rhythms in new songs,
Finding a completeness
that some search for,
Face those fears
found in the lust of life,
Some follow the beat of a different drummer,
And I can say
that I am he,
So watch me spread my wings
just to touch the wind.

May 8, 2001
