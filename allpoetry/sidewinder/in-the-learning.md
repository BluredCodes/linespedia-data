title: "In The Learning"

In touching with another
a gentle truth is revealed 
where one blends in body & soul,
Sharing a miracle within,
And in the learning,
Discovery is felt,
Tomorrows run into today's
seeking only companionship
and the lonely nights fade away,
Will the vision of your face
touch those tangibles
that we all need?
Or does time awaken the spirit
gentled in communion?
