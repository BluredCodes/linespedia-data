title: "Lingering Glances"

Brought together by fate,
Torn apart by jealously,
Once united by friendship,
Torn apart by apathy,
A sense still lingers about
but remains silenced,
Souls have united
in a sense unknown.

                                              Am I the one that
                                              is meant for you
                                               or am I just another
                                              spirit that is forgotten?

Why have you forgotten those
dreams that we once shared?
Tears follow
and rain falls,
Poetic in a tragic sense,
Sheltered in withering hope,
Rustic in verse,
Echoed in time.

                                               Lingering glances
                                               have been sought
                                               and mysteries
                                               yet remain,
                                               Only time will tell
                                               of what lies ahead.


May 4, 1999
