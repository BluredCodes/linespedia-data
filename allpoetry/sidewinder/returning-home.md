Touching in the sun,
Feeling the rain,
Knowing that life remains
sublime
within that heartbeat
fulfilled in the rhythm of time,
Celebrated in the blink of an eye,
And in that horizon...
Laughter filled the air, 
Never mind the bumps and bruises
felt along the way,
Listen if you can, 
Maybe then
you can feel the embrace
that restores
 memories returning home.

April 9, 2001
