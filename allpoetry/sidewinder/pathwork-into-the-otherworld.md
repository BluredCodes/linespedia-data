title: "Pathwork Into The Otherworld"

Envision the magic
those spellworkings of energy
held by our own making
channeled in persuasions
endeavored by a quest
powered through creative rituals
forged into the otherworld,
Crystal blue persuasions
retrieved in practices
which compliment those forces
mired in boundaries of magical workings,
Overcoming your deepest anxieties and fears,
Invoking a guide to a personal transformation,
Understanding and traditions in variations
interlacing poetry and verse,
Rich in remembrance,
Foretold in a medieval ambiance,
Mysterious in its embrace,
To bequeath a gift of the spirit.

Jan. 23, 1998
