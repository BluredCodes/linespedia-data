---
title: "Betrayed"
writer: "sidewinder"

There you go
looking at me
with those big sad eyes,
Have you no shame?
It won't work,
it was me that caught you,
And there I thought
that you cared,
You don't even know
what love is,
It's trust,
And I've been betrayed,
At first I felt sick,
Now all I feel is pain,
Why did you do this?
I thought our love was strong,
But I guess it wasn't as strong as I thought,
What a fool I am!
Yes...
I've been betrayed,
Tears flow from my eyes 
and a pain aches in my side,
I've thought over & over again...
Where did I go wrong?
But I guess I'll never know,
Just do me one favor,
Go!
No!
I can't even talk to you,
I've just been betrayed,
Yes,
Betrayed!

April 13, 1982
