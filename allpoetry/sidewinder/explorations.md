An intuitive radiance
explored within,
Spirits rise,
Shadows fall,
Those needs and changes
freewheeling where a vision
creates love,
Essences revive where
a dream celebrates forever,
A place where alpha meets omega,
Prime rituals kiss and seeks fulfillment.

Jan. 23, 1999
