title: "Hidden In Uncommon Places"

To touch against whose walls
that some of us put up
revealing those weaknesses and inconsistencies
that we all have...
Look in that mirror,
Does it tell those secrets
 hidden in uncommon places?
Does that rage touch past
time recalled interconnected with the soul?
Revered only a bit at a time,
And then when we least expect it
change affirming our own nature,
Contradicting what some think,
But it matters not...
Only we can judge
what must be.

April 13, 2001
