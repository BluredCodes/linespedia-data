---
title: "To The Tragic Side"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "to-the-tragic-side"
source: "AllPoetry"

For you and me
the thing that we see
brings a reality
that is hard to bear,
Images...
that become some twisted nightmare,
Slowly...
It creeps into your mind,
And where are you to run?
Back to someone's version
of how things should be?
Never!!!
And when your luck runs dry
everybody tells you by and by
that you should conform
and live by the status quo,
Why?
I know that life is no bowl of cherries,
Just when you feel the sweetness
fate brings you another pit,
But think of this,
When you are handed a lemon,
You can always make lemonade,
So it's true
everybody has a tragic side
and their own crosses to bear,
The trick is to override them
and not let the tragedy override.

June 29, 1995
