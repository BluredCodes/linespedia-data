title: "The Way"

Peace and truth,
	For a hope exists
	touching eternity
	perpetual in motion,
	Where alpha starts omega ends
	just  begin the journey again,
	All that glitters is a songspell
	that touches your heart
	and the magic serenades a time
	that is never forgotten,
	Remember the lessons that
	were handed down from generation to generation,
	Touching a world
	kept in the vision
	of the faerie,
	Mired in the hall of the
	courageous,
	Listen for a moment...
	Can't you hear those that
	have walked before you?
	June 23, 1998
