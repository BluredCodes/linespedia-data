title: "A Forgotten Promise"

Energy,
Celestial in time,
Raging through the universe,
Prime in its effort,
Immersed in chaos
in a fulfillment of its original 
commitment,
A page from a forgotten tome,
Spells emerge in traces of light
like a prism touched in planes
traveled by those who arrive in destiny,
A dream held by a promise
driven through a soul of a dragon
sheltered in a concentrated trust,
A perception mediated
by a dimensional force
generated in emotion
through spiritual grace,
Rogue to those who are
fool enough not to see
what's plain in realms
of a heart satisfied.

July 20, 1997
