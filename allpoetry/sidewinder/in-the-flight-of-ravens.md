In the flight of ravens
a whispered message for those who would believe,
Touch upon the world of Faerun,
See that the word of magic is not forgotten,
A voice in song is revealed,
Sacred in its truth,
Listen
for the cry of the dragon,
Why have you forgotten me?
Seek my valor,
Remember the challenge
once made by the knight,
In this the journey returns
and the adventure begins.

Sept. 30, 1998
