---
title: "A Secret Shadow"
writer: "sidewinder"

A melody that sings to us
reviled by the rhythms
that has us going to and fro,
Jealous silences that create miracles
in a revelation of an echo,
Promises made in eclipses
held by the maiden of the moon
forever held in awe,
Variations of nature
that holds an invocation
to those with a vivid imagination,
Triumphed by a secret
held by a shadow of an elf,
Contemplated in those pages
in a long forgotten tome.

Nov. 4, 1997
