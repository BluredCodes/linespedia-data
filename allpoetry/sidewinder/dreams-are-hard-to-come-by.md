title: "Dreams Are Hard To Come By"

For years I've dreamed of owning a new Chevrolet,
But somehow other things got in the way,
Still that dream I'll cling to,
for in the back of my mind
I can see me driving down the street,
Showing Off, 
Hey!
Isn't this neat?
The reality of the price
hits me where it hurts,
In the pocket book,
Why can't things be the way I want them to be?
Oh well,
I still can dream,
Can't I?

April 17, 1985
