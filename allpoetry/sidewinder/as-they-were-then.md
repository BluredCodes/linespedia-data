---
title: "As They Were Then"
writer: "sidewinder"

I can remember as a child
walking through the garden,
Smelling the vegetables,
Touching life as it is,
Hand in hand with my grandfather
with large smile,
Both of us telling fish stories,
Him showing me where the best place
were for the worms,
And us planning for
tomorrows fishing trip,
Season to season
early in the summer,
He always made time for me
even when sometimes I took things for granted,
I wish things were as simple now
as they were then,
Maybe one day I can finally
return the favor with my own grandchildren.

July 21, 2001
