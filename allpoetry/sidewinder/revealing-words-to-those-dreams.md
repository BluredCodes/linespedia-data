---
title: "Revealing Words To Those Dreams"
writer: "sidewinder"

People having fun,
Voices growing loud
and sometimes obnoxious,
But...
I'm here revealing words
to those dreams 
that I need,
Reflections...
and emotions coming overwrought
where...
I need the calm,
Shrug it off,
It matters not,
And let things be,
Continuing what I need to do,
Touching the silence in the morning
where a new day begins,
Tenderly...
finding a melody.

Sept. 29, 2000
