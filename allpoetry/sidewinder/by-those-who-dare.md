---
title: "By Those Who Dare"
writer: "sidewinder"

Too many faces
are revealed in my dreams,
And even though I celebrate
their touch
crossing over & remaining
in the magic
where a wordless song
is delivered in time,
It's those yesterdays
that whisper secrets
that only they can tell,
Contrasted in sonnets
opened to love
by those who dare.

June 6, 1999
