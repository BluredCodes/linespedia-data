Listen now,
             to the whispers of the wind,
Hidden somehow in a shadow,
                               Like a nymph
laughing
                   with mischief on her mind,
Singing her song,
                       A romance
subtle in that breeze,
                                   A wink....
there she goes,
                           The trap is set,
Who will it find?
                             Another lost soul
heartbroken,
                             But in that horizon
she'll begin anew,
                     Listen...
along that soft brook
                         the wind tells a story
of that sweet
                     nymph
looking for love,
                   sweet love,
Who will it find?

Jan. 10, 2002
