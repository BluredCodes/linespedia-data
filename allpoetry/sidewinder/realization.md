The dreams of a lifetime
come only once,
And though they become far and few between,
the memory lasts forever,
If only one could see
time would no longer matter,
But still.
the reality is there,
And in the realization of what one is,
Life can hold so much,
All one has to do
is take it
and the world is your backyard.

March 25, 1985
