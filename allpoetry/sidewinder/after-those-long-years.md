---
title: "After Those Long Years"
writer: "sidewinder"

After those long years
you find that restlessness
just to walk away,
                     Why?
Wasn't I enough?
I held your heart
like priceless wine
but that mattered not!
                          So here's my soul
touched in pain,
                And I find
                          like those rainy days
that time moves on,
Silence creeps in
and I find
           those memories haunt me,
Reflections of the past
are bitter,
             Why?
Couldn't we somehow have
worked it out?
                No...
apparently not,
 You see those tears flowing,
But in the following days
somehow I'll make it,
                       Still...
somehow I miss you.

Jan. 5, 2002
