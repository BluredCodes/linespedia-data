---
title: "Drifting Dreams"
writer: "sidewinder"

Within the select few
an arrival of a dream
where a heart
soars in the wind
fulfilled in precocious ideals
mired in the story
that never ends
where happily forever afters
exist,
Those who touch
in clouds
have the realization
that somethings 
don't work out,
But at the same time
life lives on,
Releasing reflections
that were once a part of you,
But in the meantime they
drifted farther & farther away,
So much
you wonder
was that once me?
