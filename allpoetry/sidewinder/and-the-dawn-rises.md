---
title: "And The Dawn Rises"
writer: "sidewinder"

Hope held in pleasures,
Treasures found in smiles,
Celebrations held in festivals,
Rituals rise on the full moon,
And if you look close enough,
You'll see The Lady smiling,
For the maiden holds a promise
of love,
And its truth
a fulfillment for all to share,
Touched in magic
nature is revealed,
Harmony concedes in its blessedness,
And the dawn rises to meet the new day.

Nov. 2, 1997
