title: "Soaring Free"

I am the bird
rising in freedom
soaring free, 
Complete...
within thy soul, 
Reaching a pivotal point
wherein that dream that touches
celebrates those illusions
held in a heart,

With wings of gold
held aloft
by the sweet breath of
serenity...
Peace above me,
Tranquil waters below,
Thy fragile dream,
elusive threads
like gossamer webs
spin about me,

Can you hear thy call
of nature?
Feel...
that force which drives me,
Honored with the wind
that touches my soul,
Beat by beat
and the rhythm
felt in harmony.

Sept. 7, 2001
