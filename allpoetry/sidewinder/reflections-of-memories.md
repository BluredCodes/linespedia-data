You asked me what?
It's not just a matter of jealousy,
It's a matter of protectiveness,
You're my friend,
I hear the laughter
wishing I had someone,
I see the kissing,
Still...
My feelings are strong,
I hear the conversation,
And I remember my last lady friend,
But it matters not,
Or does it?
Here autumn is setting in,
And I'm needing someone warm
on those cold nights,
Damn...
reflections of memories
say more than what I need,
But I'll get by,
Only...
Where can I find her?
Maybe...
                      Just maybe.

Sept. 25, 2000
