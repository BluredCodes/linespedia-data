I heard this word called magic,
	They say its in everything,
	Some people say that it doesn't exist,
	But I keep hearing of it all the time,
	It's seen through the heart,
	Its joy is felt everyday,
	If it doesn't exist then why does man try to use it?
	Or if the ones that say it doesn't exist,
	Fear it,
	Because true magic comes from the heart,
	For those who don't believe
	it doesn't exist,
	Let them live in this world of reality,
	I prefer the other way,
	And touch magic from my soul,
	Some that touch magic
	call it a different name,
	To some a different past
	or a different reality,
	Magic ran for centuries,
	The uses for it had different names,
	The age of magic is back running rampantly,
	Some see it through an age of dreams
	where past lives come around
	and touch when least expected,
	It's found when the heart is open
	& magic will come,
	Some find it when the muses
	are speaking through poetry & song,
	It touches through the dreams
	and is celebrated throughout time,
	the heart weaved its own magic,
	Brilliant in many hues,
	For within this universe
	a dedication to magic
	weaves only
	a miracle in time.
