title: "Those That Wander"

Those that wander
touch those times
where blood treads
in the glimpse of an eye,
Dangerous in that game,
Lying to the liars,
Tricking the tricksters,
Feel thy darkness!
Centuries of hate!
Gleefully receiving those
curses of the damned,
Cunning...
What is thou secret?
Can you see beyond that lie
held only by the jester's smile?
Blood on blood,
An invitation to the masquerade,
The inquisition seems only like
the beginning,
What is thy horror that rages on?

Oct. 20, 2001
