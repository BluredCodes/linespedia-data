You say...
call me,
But will I remember?
Weekends sometimes become excuses,
Those promises made at 3:00 in the morning,
But forgotten the following day...
I remember the smile
But the name...
somehow escapes me
Sweet serenades...
in the middle of the night,
Darkened...
by the alcohol,
Gone too quickly...
is another memory,
How wild...
life and love can be.

Sept. 13, 2000
