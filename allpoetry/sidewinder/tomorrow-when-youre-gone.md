title: "Tomorrow When You're Gone"

Tomorrow when you're gone,
Will you find
that happiness
that you've been searching for?
Reaching that dream
in days gone past,
celebrating at last
the magic
found in your heart,
 Wear that smile
warm in its embrace,
And in your youth
meet that promise
found only in your eyes.

Dec. 15, 2001
