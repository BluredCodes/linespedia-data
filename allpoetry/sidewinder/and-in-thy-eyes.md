---
title: "And In Thy Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "and-in-thy-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

A measure in time
	where celebrations touch in the quiet
	forever reaching the light
	held warm,
	Gentled in the embrace,
	Thirst forever where
	the soul
	clings,
	And in thy eyes
	brief delights rage
	as ages come and go,
	Savored beneath the silence,
	Dagger sharpened with emotion,
	And the fine taste of those ventures
	rise through the undertones.
	June 11, 1999
