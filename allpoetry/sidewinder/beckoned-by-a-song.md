---
title: "Beckoned By A Song"
writer: "sidewinder"

Envisioned through those miracles of time,
Scavengers of life
and though the seemless seasons rise
through an eon of desperation,
A celebration of heart
touches where one would except,
An enduring gift
pleasured evermore,
Clasped into the full moon
where those that endeavor
restore life's full embrace,
Beckoned by a song,
And dreams hold in a moments passing
where a soul glimpses into paradise,
And a smile is blown from the tempest of the wind,
Yes...
mingles forever...
within the hour.

June 4, 1999
