Whispers dance
like the rain
in a summer day
needed only 
by a satisfaction,
Arrivals met
only in a smile,
Guided in mediations
yet enchanted
by those voices in tome,
and laughter reaches into your heart
in the mist of yesterday
private in an understanding,
Simple to those who are willing
to see,
gentle in a miracle
celebrated in the coming of the dawn.

July 26, 2000
