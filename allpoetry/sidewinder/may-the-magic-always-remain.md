---
title: "May The Magic Always Remain"
writer: "sidewinder"

Dreams of iridescent wings
in the light of a full moon
sings and invokes magic
in a world in the realm of the Faerie,
An invisible force
draws upon the echo of a secret wisdom of the forest
where the sound of Pan's flute
touches your heart
and beckons your return
to touch upon a time long forgotten,
But held by some
with great reverence
to a world where a legend remains
 proud and enduring,
In a circle of light
inspiration lives,
Sacred in the vision of Merlin
Arch Druid Of England
the song of the forest has not been forgotten,
May the magic always remain.

Nov. 7, 1997
