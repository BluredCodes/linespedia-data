A watcher within,
Hearing my own thoughts,
Reflecting on past lives,
Revolving on wisdom,
Here now lies the question...
Where do I go from here?
Possibilities are endless,
And the choice is mine,
Decisions.... 
      Decisions........
Forever the journey had begun
in the age of the apocalypse,
Sheltered only by a tear,
Within it is the rune of victory
and I hear it
whispered by a gentle breeze.

Dec. 30, 1998
