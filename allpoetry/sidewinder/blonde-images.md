Blonde images
                    varied through sight & sound,
Cool breezes ride in
                      these spring memories
collected within in smile
                            greeted only in a warmth,
Rhythms lyrical
                  where an embrace
touches a soul
                affirming existence
in humanity,
                           Where new hope
draws appreciation,
                       Bittersweet in lost
adolescence,
                   Where finality is realized
and found.

June 7, 2001
