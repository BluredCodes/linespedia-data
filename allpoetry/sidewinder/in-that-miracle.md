---
title: "In That Miracle"

Fatal to the realization
	of a precept of an opinion
	held by a majority
	where prejudism is felt,
	Why?
	Who is it to say
	that the color of a skin
	is held by a judgement,
	For those who hold by this
	are missing out,
	Consider this:
	If held in open mindedness
	think of the possibilities in everything
	where a world can open their hearts
	and hold out an open hand
	in friendship,
	Where there is no fear,
	No war,
	No superstition,
	And in that miracle
	this world would be a better place
	for all to live in.
	Dec. 29, 1997
