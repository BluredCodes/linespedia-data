title: "Only To Keep Our Enchantment Alive"

Rushed by blindness
held only...
by the oncoming dreams,
Remembered in tears,
Kissed in autumn,
 Betrayed in spring,
Flowing from my eyes
the bloody water stings,
Seasons of our life...
Wonder what the winter brings...
A gentle touch
forever remembered,
Cradled in understanding
only found in a simple smile,
Do we look beyond
what is found within our eyes?
And even if we find satisfaction
within a hunger,
Where do we go from there?
An epic rages
within our hearts
only to keep
our enchantment alive.

July 26, 2000
