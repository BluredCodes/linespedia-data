---
title: "A Paradox Felt"
writer: "sidewinder"

Good, bad or indifferent
to perchance a whisper
pledged,
Hear the cries at night?
How many times must the care
be realized?
Validation
lost by the wind,
A paradox felt...
But something's gone...
Why?
Could it be...
my imagination?
I feel the cynic
provided
by a frustration
implied.

Oct. 2, 1996
