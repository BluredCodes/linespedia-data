title: "Quest Of Southwest Circles"

Remaining in search
of southwest circles,
                             To see
                                      as the ancient ones
             revered from a day
                               gone past,
                                             But the honor remains,
Mystical....
                      Can you hear
                                    them honoring what they consider
sacred?
                           Nature touches....
                                                circle within circle
but the seasons go on,
                                Dreamcatcher....
                                                 Do you feel....
what I need?
                             Tell me again...
                                                those tales that teach....
Sing to me
                        those songs that fill my heart,
Let me hear the drum
                           and watch you dance
                                              until the sun sets
and meets the horizon.

June 12, 2000
