title: "To Touch Upon The Stars"
writer: "sidewinder"

It's been said
to touch upon the stars & beyond,
What then?
Who knows?
Ah....
But the adventure may be grand!
What dangers lurk
unbeknownst to all,
That reach of imagination stirs within us,
Keeps us...
going on,
Our curiosity may be the death of us yet,
But to us...
it matters not,
Somehow it keeps us driven,
Discoveries that may be discovered,
What then?
Sailing into the vast cosmos,
Exploring bizarre new worlds,
Such beauty never seen by mankind,
With risks just as great as the reward,
Would we dare to leave our comfort zone?
To possibly meet new life forms,
far above even our own golden sun?
With our minds...
we do wonders,
But how?
With knowledge?
With creativity,
Of our unlimited
ever exceeding
imaginations?
Or possibly if we...
ride the comet
within dreams,
We can then
touch upon
a star.

March 11, 2002
