---
title: "An Answer"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "an-answer"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

To become a true master of fate
you must first become the knower of self,
When you can repose in comfort between
the wings of your eternal dragon
which is not born nor died throughout ages eternal,
Then will shadows forever vanish,
Leaving that which within you knows,
For it is knowledge not of fleeting lifetimes of illusion
but of a real man that was,
That is...
            And that will be again,
for whom the hour shall never strike,
Touching those hearts which rage
                                in an echo
                forever met
                           and forever seen
completed inward
                 to reach
                         an understanding
                                          sometimes understood,
                sometimes not,
                               Only forgotten in a past
that arms reach to grasp
                        only in emptiness
yet we find....
                 an answer.

Oct. 24, 2000
