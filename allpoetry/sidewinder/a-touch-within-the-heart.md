title: "A Touch Within The Heart"

Smile,
Touch within the heart,
Hear the song that touches within,
Feel the harmony and rhythm
that dances in your soul,
Feel the music throughout
your body, mind and soul,
Become the composer
and create with new inspiration,
Become the music
that fills the page with harmony,
And in the celebration
time fulfills the need
of simply what's inside,
Forever reach the shadow
that lingers about the room,
Forever fill the void
with harmony and song,
Forever fill the void
that the composer struggles with,
Forever fill the void with
a symphony that composes itself.

May 24, 1999
