Swords edge
rising from the battle's call,
A king's summoning,
Hear the soldiers cry,
Victory!
An offering to the Lady of Avalon,
Symbols that reach a spiritual peak
forever completed in each circle,
a tradition within its own self,
Founded by the principles of chivalry,
Revered by those who
remember the legends
ancient in their knowledge.

June 23, 1997
