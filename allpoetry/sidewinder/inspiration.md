The dreams of all understanding
	is more than an inkling in someone's mind,
	It takes all the sweat you can give,
	For the idea that you gave life to
	take some T.L.C,
	That's tender loving care,
	Each day watch it grow
	like a beautiful flower,
	Then when it blossoms,
	Share it with the whole world,
	For there's nothing that compares
	with what you can give,
	Then maybe someone will take the seeds of your love
	and plant them again,
	And what you've shared will live on forever,
	Isn't that what inspiration is all about?
	Dec. 4, 1983
