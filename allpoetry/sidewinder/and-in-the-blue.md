---
title: "And In The Blue"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "and-in-the-blue"
source: "AllPoetry"

Those raging shades of grey
mired in a sun dropped elegy
forgotten in a moment of time
endowed only by the silence,
And the wind blows
wherein a memory follows
to reach
in those contemplations
frequented only in a marvel
consecrated in a feathered whisper
and in the blue
a collection of talents
torment and challenge
the universe.

May 23, 2000
