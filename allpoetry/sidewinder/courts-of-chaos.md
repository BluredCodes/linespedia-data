title: "Courts Of Chaos"

And there she be
glorified in all her luster,
Glimmering in the night,
Held in the spotlight,
If only for a single moment,
Was it as good for you
as it was great for me?
Time keeps slipping by
and words ramble,
Here I am grasping at the meaning,
But before the morning dawns
clarification becomes crystal clear,
Justice becomes lost
like a drop of rain
flowing down a river,
Perish the thought of it ever making sense,
The courts of chaos
howl in joy,
For the confusion
is their game,
Explanations?
Never!!!!
Not in a million years.

July 23, 1996
