An aspect of fire,
Creations...
molten lava
forming mountains,
forges of dwarven kind,
Tasting of steel,
 Warriors behold...
the artifacts of life! 
Dreams of glass
piercing through a single moment,
Yet...
a single heart is born
with the wisdom of generations to come,
I can hear it,
Those voices of Valhalla
and the gentle kindness Of Mother Earth,
 Behold warriors,
 Victory is within a heartbeat,
And the treasure of peace lasts forever,
Coming full circle of the universe.

March 27, 1996
