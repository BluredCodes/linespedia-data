Here the wind is
                  blowing the 
leaves                                      to and fro,
Playing like a child would, 
                           Celebrated in a game,
To some it wouldn't make any sense, 
                                   But does that matter?
It's all in fun!

                       Nature had its surprises,
Find that smile
                      In a wondrous dream,

Revealed in a miracle,
                             
                            Silly in its means!




Listen to those whispers

                           and the laughter
hidden in the trees,
                                            And find heaven
playing like a child
                       only now
without a need.

Jan. 10, 2002
