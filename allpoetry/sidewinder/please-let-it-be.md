title: "Please.....   Let It Be!"

Melodies....
rising from the air,
What a commotion...
coming from over there!
Nighttime reflections...
touching in the wind,
Lovers holding hands...
Sometimes loneliness grips me...
And I have nothing to say,
Wishful thing...
Whispers completed in the laughter,
Where can she be?
The one meant for me,
Sometimes prayers are answered,
Please...
               Let it be!
