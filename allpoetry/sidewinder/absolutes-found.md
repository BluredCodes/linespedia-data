title: "Absolutes Found"

Look to the stars to find
your place in life,
let others see only
the best you have inside,

For the dream holds only a miracle
touched in understanding,
Where one excels in anything
that one does,

Open your eyes to the world around you,
Wipe away the cobwebs,
Look closely to the beauty
that surrounds us all,
Life passes quickly,
Moments gather new meaning
when we look with bright eyes
wonders abound,

Yet...
Let's touch those kaleidoscope sunsets,
Which rise in the horizon,
Absolutes found
in those few seconds
in the magic of life itself
and a heart special
in the cosmos
where we find a beginning
in a new journey.

Nov. 20, 2000

B.E. Whitehorn & Kerri Stoetzel
