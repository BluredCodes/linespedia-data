Spirits touch
as our paths work in a feathered memory,
a shaman speaks in all his wisdom
over the campfire in the night
as the stars shine with the moon
touching forever in the ceremony
where a dreamweaver catches a soul,
Hear the drums that beat in rhythm, 
Dance forever in that completeness,
Remember Mother Nature & Father Sky,
They smile in your gladness,
But show you that wisdom
found in life,
Honor bound in a kindness.

Feb. 8, 2002
