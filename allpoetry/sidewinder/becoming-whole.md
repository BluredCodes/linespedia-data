---
title: "Becoming Whole"
writer: "sidewinder"

And the pain cometh heavy,
the tears came like the floods
And I knew not where I was,
all I had was sad memories,
No,
I wouldn't forget them,
I couldn't,
For they would haunt me for years to come,
The one thing I loved,
I felt I had destroyed,
But I hadn't,
I was coming into a new age,
A new learning,
A new love,
The past didn't matter,
And it never would,
The present was all that did matter,
For here I was living,
Growing,
Sharing,
Loving,
In every way that I knew,
I was even growing spiritually,
Yes,
I was becoming whole.

March 1, 1982
