I look at a tree
and see what I see,
The tree has been cut,
Yes,
trimmed from the sky,
But in a way it's beautiful
and pleasing to the eye,
Its branches reach out
as if to cry,
Why am I held from reaching my full potential?
Still I survive,
Yes...
That's what I see,
I wonder what the tree sees in me?
