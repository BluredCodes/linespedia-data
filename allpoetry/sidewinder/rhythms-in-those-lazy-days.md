---
title: "Rhythms In Those Lazy Days"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "rhythms-in-those-lazy-days"
source: "AllPoetry"

Rhythms in those lazy days
where a memory touches,
Never a thought,
Complete in a surprise,
Shall I smile for you?
Save a dream
for you that has been forgotten?
Tomorrows sometimes run into today's,
In that...
we find more than what we need,
Those absolutes
only found in an abstract,
An invitation
shared only by a coincidence
unaware by a future,
Do you sometimes find a certain song
that needs to be sung?
Look in my eyes
for a reflection
gently made in this world,
What you find
descends into your heart,
Eventually forgotten by things that don't matter,
Intense in a passion,
Remembered only in things that matter to you.

Oct. 1, 2000
