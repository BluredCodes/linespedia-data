title: "Sacred Wings"

Sacred wings
finding freedom
to touch the wind
imminent in a moment
where a spirit 
completes the horizon,
Desires meant to the eyes
serenaded in the dawn,
Insight varied in visions
drawing the elements together,
Tangible in its meaning,
Transformations made in a spiral of time,
Awakening a need
in the balance of my mind,
Only found in the glimmering of the moonlight
in a summer night.

Sept. 29, 2000
