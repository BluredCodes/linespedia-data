title: "Those Gentle In Times"
writer: "sidewinder"

Those gentle in times
favored to rush
in with a miracle
only met...
day by day,
Let's touch the sunrise
and reach those roads
that follows us all of our lives,
Seasons merge a rainbow at a time,
Find that gentleness
embraced in a memory,
Complete thy soul
forever in a sunrise, 
Deeper are those horizons
nourished in eternity,
And in those eyes that will follow,
Truth sings in the silence.

Oct. 20, 2000
