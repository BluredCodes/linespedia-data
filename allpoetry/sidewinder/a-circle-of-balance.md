---
title: "A Circle Of Balance"

Silently touching,
	Conjuring the inner self
	where in the sacred
	a focus is met,
	Calling on a miracle
	where in time
	those answers hold wisdom
	reached by experience,
	And in a circle of balance
	paradoxes reveal themselves,
	Only with understanding & patience,
	Illumination touches all things
	in light,
	And in that transition
	good fortune comes
	just to be met by courage.
	Oct. 18, 1999
