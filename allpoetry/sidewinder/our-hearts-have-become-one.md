title: "Our Hearts Have Become One"

My dreams are never the same
since you've come into my life.
You've taken this lonely soul
and made my life complete again,
Once I thought that love had forsaken me,
But you are love
and each time I'm near you,
My heart clings to yours,

As your desires mingle with mine
we discover a passion that is so
great it can not be measured,

Speak my name
and touch those dreams
where a miracle
we kiss and hold each other in the night,

We sleep so blissful
in each others arms
till the darkness turns to light,

Could life bring more
 than what now we share?
With every reach that we've made
our hearts have become one,
let's listen to the warmth of our souls
and whisper into the wind
a promise forever held.

April 12, 2000
