---
title: "A Step At A Time"
writer: "sidewinder"

Trust fired in inspiration
justified in a prayer
wherein time celebrates
a shadow of truth,
Chaos merges with law
virtually moments away,
While a heartbeat
rages in freedom,
Winged within the wind,
Forever blinded just with a touch,
And the rhythm fades
into a breath
a step at a time.

Jan. 15, 1999
