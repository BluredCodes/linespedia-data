Let it go,
Don't argue,
Sometimes it's better not to say anything,
Rather than to start a fight,
Only fools settle things with a fist,
And then when everything's done and settled,
What's accomplished?
Nothing but hard feelings,
Is that the way to do it?
No...
I think not,
Better to walk away
and be called a coward
than to hurt someone,
Some say I'm a pacifist,
Maybe I am,
Maybe not.

Sept. 23, 1984
