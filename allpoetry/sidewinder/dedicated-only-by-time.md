title: "Dedicated Only By Time"

Passions rise into those fires
dedicated only by time
felt in the rhythm of a horizon
imminent in that touch
where a longing transgresses
into those pillows revealed between the morning
and midnight,
Followed by a capture of a breath denied,
Slowly a journey is arrived,
Keeping those risks
needed in its final veil,
Gentled only by circumstance
and the balance
come in smiles.

June 6, 1999
