Treasures 
held in the eyes of a child
rich in imagination,
listen to the music,
Make sandcastles in the sand,
Hold thy youth forever in time,
For the pleasures are lost when
the vestiges of memories take hold
and the simple ways are replaced by responsibilities,
Why?
Time will never tell,
And that mystery is never solved,
One walks with a sigh,
But the future waits for no one.

Aug. 18, 1996
