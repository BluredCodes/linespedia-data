---
title: "Hidden Solitudes"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "hidden-solitudes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dreams and aspirations
fulfilled in a wild imagination
wherein the chronicle of time
is mired in the reflections of life
past shadows touch in miracles
striking forever in the clouds,
Perceptions rise where a web
of inspiration touches those who
celebrate new beginnings
arisen in the mind
of hopeful promises,
Solitudes hidden in the face of reality.

Feb 21, 1999
