---
title: "Perhaps Beyond Fate"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "perhaps-beyond-fate"

Held within thy embrace
moments touch in repose
celebrated in seconds
verified in the end of the summer,
Silence is found in random
wherein those journeys rise in 
peculiarities,
And I see hope
touching beyond the darkness,
Collecting shadows
a notebook at a time,
It's just became spring,
The snow won't touch until next year,
Let the sunshine come
like lovers felt in February,
Reaching those anthologies
perhaps beyond fate.

May 13, 2001
