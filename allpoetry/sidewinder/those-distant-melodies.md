title: "Those Distant Melodies"

In the Irish twilight
a magic revives a heart
encircled by the fullness
of the universe,
A body of energy
echoed in the radiance of the moon,
Brief connections
rise to touch the inner fire
which is within all of us,
A true voice in the web of life
windswept in unseen realms,
Shapeshifting in simplicity,
Those rainbows of colors
unfurled in contemplations
mediated in paths of discipline,
Those distant waterfalls
where moonbeams dance
and melodies are sung by a distant elf,
A hallowed place
where magic is forever held sacred.

Nov. 25, 1997
