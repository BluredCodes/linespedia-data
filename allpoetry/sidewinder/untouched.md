---
title: "Untouched"
writer: "sidewinder"

Through those eyes in realms that remain,
Spirits that unite through peace,
Timeless in their joy, 
Magic is their touch,
A world apart from those who do not understand,
Enchanted where a heart remains
untouched...
This circle is open
but never broken,
Unified in the energy
centered through time,
Focus thy heart and invoke change,
To touch the web that remains...
untouched,
Seek thy balance
and offer to those
all the blessings life can offer.

Feb. 7, 2000
