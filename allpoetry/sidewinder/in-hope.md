A love...
	so great,
	That she would risk everything!
	It mattered not that her very dignity was at risk,
	Only the one that she had loved was safe,
	In her eyes
	the very thought of harm
	brought tears to her eyes,
	Still...
	            somehow she must not give in,
	For if life meant anything
	then there is an answer
	& through that there is hope,
	In hope...
	                that love would survive.
	Dec. 4, 1984
