---
title: "The Guided Cage"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "the-guided-cage"
source: "AllPoetry"

Those who choose those simple ways
held deep in their hearts
reveal insight beyond those echoes 
which rage in a distant past,

Those who long to scream to break the guided cage
hold the words deep in their minds
exploding guilt beyond those echoes
which rage in a distant past,

And in that revelation
time touches a miracle
where a shadow clings,
Desperate in its attempt to touch
those roads held in the night,

And where the somber secrets are concealed
miracles restore a soul,
Shadows free to saunter,
desperate in its attempt to touch
those roads in the night,

Remember those smiles
held only in encounters,
Touched in contemplations,
Varied in existence,
sacred moment by moment.

May 18, 2000
