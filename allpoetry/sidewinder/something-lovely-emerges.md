---
title: "Something Lovely Emerges"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "something-lovely-emerges"

An inner reflection
that results in fate
placed in focus,
To thy inner self
renew a heart, 
Seek balance in all things
and honor fulfilled in wisdom,
See beyond the illusions
for all paths that are followed
illuminates the life force that
resides in all of us,
Connections in life reform those
challenges that we all have,
Seek among the ashes and discover
a new perspective and a new birth,
For thy gift of freedom
restores thyself in spirit,
Light pierces the clouds and
touches the waters just
as something lovely emerges
from the depths,
the soul is illuminated form within
and the spiritual warrior
celebrates the eternal gift of life.

Nov. 2, 1997
