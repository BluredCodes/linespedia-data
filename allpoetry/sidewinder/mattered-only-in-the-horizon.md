title: "Mattered Only In The Horizon"

I touched...
I dreamed...
I loved...
with all of my heart,
We danced...
and completed each other,
Those spring showers
shared beyond the darkness,
Awakened by our reaches,
Done within time
and in the memory
varied in our completeness,
Near and far
is the sunshine
mattered only
in the horizon.

May 12, 2000
