title: "Winds Touch Upon Echoes"

Winds touch
on echoes
risen on the wings of eagles
herald in the flight of freedom
piercing that high pitch cry
screaming,
I'm free!
I'm free!
Connecting at the touch of nature,
Cherished a heartbeat at a time,
The richness of sky
endless in that affirmation,
Touching that passion
circle to circle
where a voice
celebrates
breath after breath,
That current
and a dreamweaver
captures only freedom
under beneath
the sky .

I'm free!

 

Dec. 21, 2001
