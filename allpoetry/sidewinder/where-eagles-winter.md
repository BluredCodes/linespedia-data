---
title: "Where Eagles Winter"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "where-eagles-winter"
source: "AllPoetry"

Touch those mountains
forever in the wind,
I see
       thy
            completion
and wonder...
                     How can it be?
Against the sky
                  so proud and free,
Inward does my soul touch,
Imploring to thy horizon,
Keep me whole,
                   Breathless in the winter wind,
Revived in a heartbeat,
                        But tranquil as the season's run,
Let's inspire those who can not see
                what we have seen
and maybe
             they'll understand.

June 12, 2000
