title: "Beyond A Rainbow's Smile"

Where those whispers meet
	only in the warmth
	of a summer day,
	Where those smiles caress
	the evening order
	on a solitary day,
	Perhaps...
	when we look
	we can find that secret
	held beyond a rainbow's smile,
	Our eyes can unlock
	mysteries of the aged
	our smiles unleash
	the beauty of the demon,
	Our virtues unveil
	the innocence of truth,
	Authentic,
	Only met in time
	deepening, affecting
	those voices touched
	in melancholy
	but the language reveals
	what some say
	only in words.
	May 18, 2000
