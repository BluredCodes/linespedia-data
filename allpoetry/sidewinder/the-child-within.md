title: "The Child Within"

Child within this body of mine
may all your smiles and memories
provide strength in times if needed
sheltered by love,
Imagine the heart of magic,
Infused with life,
Soaring through the sky
like an eagle feeling free,
To explore the unchartered dream realms
of hidden reality,
To leap beyond imagination into that space
between the worlds where fantasy becomes real
in an art of changing consciousness at will,
Altered by those who have an open mind,
a struggle within to touch
what some can not comprehend,
Passions arise in all of us,
But a cleansing force
purifies our spirit,
And a candle shines
like a star forever remembered.

 Sept. 7, 1997
