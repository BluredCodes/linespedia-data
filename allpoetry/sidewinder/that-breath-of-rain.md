title: "That Breath Of Rain"

Sheltered by that breath of rain
prime in the fullness of time,
Where the shadow
 meets the light
scented heartbeat by heartbeat,
Clear in that sudden cool
where I listen to the wind,
And I find
           a miracle
                     that touches
                                    my soul.
Dec. 18, 2001
