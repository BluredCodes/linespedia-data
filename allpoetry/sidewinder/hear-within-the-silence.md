title: "Hear Within The Silence"

Rhythm through time
    within the shadows
                      forever held
                                  in promises
celebrated
                in circles of dreams
                                     where miracles touch
and in the twinkling of an eye
                     the sandman brings a moment of joy,
Where the wind carries
                       a hope
fulfilled wherein the
                          sounds of the universe
reach to those who
                     hear within the silence.

Feb. 25, 1999
