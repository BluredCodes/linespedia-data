---
title: "Where The Finale Reveals Itself"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "where-the-finale-reveals-itself"
source: "AllPoetry"

Whispers rise in spirits
where the silence touches
in lingering moments,
Catacombs twist and turn
where sounds fulfill
in spectacular amazement,
And in the first breath
I hear a sigh
that touches within a memory,
Within the last breath
another sigh is heard
that lasts eternally,
Suspended in time
held within the reach
of a smile,
Sought out as a touch
only held by thin air
lingering, changing,
Never remaining the same,
Pleasured in a vision
where the finale
reveals itself.

May 10, 1999
