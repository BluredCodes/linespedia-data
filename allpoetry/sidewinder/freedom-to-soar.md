title: "Freedom To Soar"

Suspended in the air,
The freedom to soar,
Feel the winds guide,
Let the excitement touch
you in ways to rage,
A heartbeat met only in the clouds,
Let time stand still
fever keeping me here,
Feel the undercurrent,
Move with the eagles
and meet their soul,
Howl like the wind,
Escape into the elements,
Weave in celebration a breath of fresh air,
Eternally met with the angels
and nourish what needs that need to be met.

Aug. 9, 2000
