title: "Considerate In The Silence"

Look to the future,
Passions have arisen
on places not forseen.
a sheltered moment
in a truth revealed,
 A journey forsaken
 in a secret place,
The aura shines
where shadows dare not reveal themselves,
Considerate in the silence,
Behold the strength in time,
Fragile only to those who do not believe,
Let the contest begin
in fortunes gathered,
Shrewd in their own vision,
A passion...
never forgotten.

Oct 2, 1996
