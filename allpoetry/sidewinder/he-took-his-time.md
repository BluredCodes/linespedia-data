title: "He Took His Time"
writer: "sidewinder"

All ready an old man,
White hair,
But the warmth in his eyes
I'll always remember,
He took his time
to take me fishing,
Teaching me all that he knew,
I also remember him
taking long walks in his garden,
Never letting what he loved
grow without attention,
retired in a little town
where a country store
was just at his back door,
Even though his days were at a winter's end
laughter filled me,
Content just to be at his side,
I cried...
when he left my world,
But I pass on 
his legacy
and teach those who will listen
those smiles found in a brook
with pole in hand,
And the laughter shared
in a warm sunny day.
