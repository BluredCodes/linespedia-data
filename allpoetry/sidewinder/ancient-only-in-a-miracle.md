title: "Ancient Only In A Miracle"

Thy meaning lives in hope
wherein a fulfillment is met,
To touch on the truth
which in disguise
reveals only a smile,
And each time a thought is kept,
A lingering thought
that is met within silence
to reveal an untold truth
that is fulfilled through glances
to reveal a present time,
sacred in the cutting edge
to which a soul it touched
within a second sight,
Ancient only in a miracle.

Sept. 29, 1999
