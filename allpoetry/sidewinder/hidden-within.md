title: "Hidden Within"
writer: "sidewinder"

Through those rites of passage
following a miracle in time
a heart touches wherein
a trust follows,
those within a dream
rises early pristine,
Second by second
varied within the horizon,
Enchanted near images
created between two moments
hidden within a chronicle.

Sept. 19, 1999
