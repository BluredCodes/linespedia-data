title: "In That Adventure"

By the whites of their eyes,
Celebrated in the passion of their heart,
Completed by the freedom of their soul,
Initiated by a walk of a sunny day,
Wherein...
          that first step
lead them to the
                        rest of their lives,
And what follows?
                      Who knows?
But this I can say...
                        in that adventure,
It must be
            grand

Dec. 18, 2001
