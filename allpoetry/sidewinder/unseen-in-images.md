Unseen In images
abstract to those who do not believe,
The insight is freely given to those
who are young in heart
and have an open mind to the world
of the Faerie realms,
A rich kaleidoscope of magic
held in ritual of fulfillment,
Sacred in teachings,
Honored in its wisdom,
Awakening a deeper level of consciousness,
A turning spiral that whirls in and out of existence,
Whose winking eye is the pulse of being,
a rebirth of love
whose laughter bubbles
and courses through all things,
A rain of bright spirits that filled the world
with love,
A starlight vision
where balance is necessary,
A reflection of the myriad
seen in the third self,
a sense of wonder
of joy and delight in the natural world.

Dec. 1, 1997
