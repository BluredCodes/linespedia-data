---
title: "Even When I'm Not There?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "even-when-im-not-there"

Dreams of that sheltered past
	clattered and clanged,
	Turn about fair play!
	Touching beyond the sun,
	Revelations met
	another heart has been won,
	Green glistening beyond a warmth,
	Whispered only...
	                within a breath,
	And your memory
	              tasted
	                    just a touch
	                                of my soul,
	Do you feel me,
	               Even
	                   when
	                        I'm not there?
	Aug. 24, 2001
