title: "Within The Stanza that Rises"

Trust thyself
for nights like these
holds more than just a silent embrace,
Ballads rise
in a celebration of the first full moon
pulsing a heartbeat at a time,
Belief is a must,
Eyes open
beyond the flame
aspiring to reach
among the twilight,
Mere moments
cling free behind the veil, 
Intentions lost
behind voices and thoughts
given in ancient awakenings
on the verge of a realization
reflected within the prism of time,
A rhyme of light remains to be seen,
Illuminate the path that you strive for,
Classic in its design.

March 25, 1997
