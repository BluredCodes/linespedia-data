---
title: "Found In The Moonlight"
writer: "sidewinder"

Only you
can make me feel this way,
All my dreams come true
when my eyes meets yours,
Touching the night
where only a heart soars,
Yes...
Those winds blow secrets
softly
where a whisper
is held in time,
And in your smile
fells the farewells
step by step,
Encounters that touch
where words are no longer needed
and we find what we need
only through the moment
found in the moonlight.

Aug. 13, 2000
