---
title: "Right Before Our Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"

Reaching for those tumultuous times,
Touching the sunrise that rises in our hearts,
Tripping along those roads that guide us,
Sometimes pausing to ponder the crossroads
that flairs like the brightness of the sun,
Telling us that sometimes we have to listen
and choose,
Why?
There are things to see
and things to try,
Otherwise...
life finds that voice
that rises like the wind
echoing to canyon to canyon
but never finds a home,
And we see...
only those shadows that open
right before our eyes.

May 15, 2001
