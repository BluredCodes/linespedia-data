Duality
shadowed in a sheltered past,
Forgotten in a bureaucracy,
a parchment of spoken words,
Adage to a multiverse,
Some say a portal to the universe,
A bleak cabal in the secret of survival,
Unlock the doorway to your imagination
in the pages of pain,
Proficient in the futile stalemate,
Feeling overwhelmed by the infinite,
Possibilities of the multiverse,
Reclaiming the essential spirit
revealed like a magician's personal book of shadows,
Rich in lore,
Spoken in truth.

Nov, 17, 1998
