The rising moon dances in full celebration, 
Rich in traditions,
Sparkling in the light,
The magic runs full circle
manipulating the vision that rises before my eyes,
Wheels of fire,
A circle with a dagger
sparkling in the hands of the maiden,
Mystical of the world
opened in sight to a door of new realms
Which bring dreams to those who believe,
Clear in the very foundations of physical reality,
Shared in harmony
to the four winds
raised in praise to the Lady
which promises nothing but love 
to those with an open heart.

June 20, 1997
