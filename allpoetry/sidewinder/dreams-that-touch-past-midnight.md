title: "Dreams That Touch Past Midnight"

In touch with those memories
rising in a simple bar,
Music screaming,
Neon lights flashing,
People coming,
People going,
Midnight is fastly approaching,
Celebrated from moment to moment,
Touching those places from heart to heart,
Finding that sunshine that we all need,
Wherein candlelight seems to be missing
those corners hidden in the shadows,
Yet...
If you look close enough
the loneliness seems to slip away,
Listen in the silence
and speak to the dream
that touch past midnight.

April 16, 2001
