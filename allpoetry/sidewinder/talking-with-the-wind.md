---
title: "Talking With The Wind"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "talking-with-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

To touch with those angels
celebrating with open freedom,
To see as a bird does,
Never a care in the world,
Complete 
         my
            freedom,
Soar & touch the clouds,
                        Sacred
                              in 
                                the
                                   eyes,
Bound only
          by
            the
               winds
                    embrace,
To those who have not tried this,
You should try.

June 12, 2000
