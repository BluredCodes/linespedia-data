---
title: "Release Thy Soul"
writer: "sidewinder"

When it is not yet twilight
those shadows rise
among tender mysteries
arisen where only an echo meets
the silence,
The distant sound is broken
only in the night,
Encounter those times where eyes
meet magic,
Renew thy focus,
Your mind is quiet
and spirit calm,
Rituals touch in a cone of power,
Discover those ways to touch a dream,
Infuse thy heart,
Bewitching in waves of evocation
as this last breath leaves your body,
You notice a tingling of energy
deep within you,
Close your eyes and begin breathing rhythmically and deeply,
Slow your thoughts,
Quiet your mind,
Center your spirit,
Focus inward,
Release thy soul,
Sacred realms touch in enchantment.
