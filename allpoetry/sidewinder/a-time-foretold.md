title: "A Time Foretold"

Crusading against those times
when in an age
that is shared with dragon's claws
and honored among knighthood,
Yes...
Those christened by a knight's own blade,
A sworn fealty
that is earned by his own deeds
forever held by history,
Feel its truth
within a legend,
A song merged by the rhythm of a lute,
Some that will swear its only a myth,
But the honesty will be revered,
As the tale reveals its own self,
Serenaded by the muse
that charges ahead
and considers more than time foretold,
Hear in the silence
a festival
received in medieval prime.

Feb. 11, 1997
