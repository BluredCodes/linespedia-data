---
title: "Who Will?"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch within the hear
a peace & serenity
kept for us all,
Not all of us can 
answer all of the worlds questions,
But each of us can try,
Why?
            Some ask,
Because if we don't...
                        Who Will?
Search within,
Find that strength,
To touch within each earthbound soul,
Peace & serenity kept for us all,
None can answer all of the worlds questions,
But each of us can try,
Why?
some ask,
             Because if we don't ...
Who will?
Search within,
Find that strength
and forge into destiny's jaws,
Find that courage within,
                             See...
with eyes that don't see,
Listen...
             Beyond the silence
of those who don't listen,
grip that need
that is resolved in all of us,
But somehow find
that uniqueness
in an honor of one.

Feb. 11, 2002
