title: "She Smiles On My Life"

Blades flashing,
To touch a truth
among an illusion,
Treasures found within the common,
Lessons learned from a master
who I've known all my life
intercede in mystical realms,
A vision quest anointed by the Lady
who smiles on my life,
For it's within her plans
to share her message
to those who may have forgotten
or who have yet to learn
that it's in the balance
or the blessings of life
where magic is revealed
and on this blessed day
miracles do come true.

Oct. 31, 1997
