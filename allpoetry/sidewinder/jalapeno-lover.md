Spicy hot,
I can not touch,
For I will burn in the center of the earth,
To feel your warm sexy body,
Oh mine to me I glow,
When I think of heat,
I think of when I partake of your body,
My anus turns blue,
I cry boo hoo,
Because I can't be with you my jalapeno lover,
The heat you give within my head
makes me long for a piece of bread,
And the moral of this story,
Forever so true,
Though it causes me heartburn
when I think of you,
A jalapeno lover,
You burn in a never ending day
and tears fill my eyes
when we touch,
Oh so many ways,
Never try to fill a heart
with a cry of a stomach,
You might find more pain than you can deal with,
Oh jalapeno lover
                   I ache
                           in my need,
                                        Please touch me !!!!

June 30, 2000
