---
title: "Streams Of Silver"
writer: "sidewinder"

In the twilight
streams of silver rises in
magical mysteries of the realms,
Those pages of pain
which teaches us those hard lessons,
Where a soul is hardened like steel,
Prime in the fullness of time,
Celebrated in tales of heroes
where hope is prized like a jewel
shining in the sun,
An epic begins
where a legend returns
forged in the chronicles of time,
The witching hour emerges
to test a soul
and find a destiny,
Silent passages arrive
in the wail of an echo
harmonizing images
in a chorus of valor,
Misty shadows etch
an inmost chamber
where the promise of a rainbow
beckons you to follow your heart
and discover the awaiting adventure.

Feb. 10, 1998
