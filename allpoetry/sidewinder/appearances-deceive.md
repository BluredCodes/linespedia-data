---
title: "Appearances Deceive"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "appearances-deceive"

Appearances deceive,
The truth can kill,
How will we ever
know what's real?
Can you touch the shadow
imminent in time?
Can you hear the echo
hidden behind a song?
Can you touch the magic
revealed within?
Fighting to try
to receive it...
To keep,
Yet...
stepping away,
Shaking in fear
to the sight of this truth,
Blind within a miracle,
To reach wherein a horizon
completes...
in this
we find what's in our heart,
But you see beyond the illusion
and find those reasons that are clear,
Let's touch within reality,
In a mere moment
things may change,
Touch within a silence,
Where images...
find a deceptive difference,
But if you look truly
you may find...
what's hidden behind those shadows
which rage only in destiny.

Oct. 7, 2000
