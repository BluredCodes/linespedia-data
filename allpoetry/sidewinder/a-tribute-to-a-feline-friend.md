Companion,
Friend,
It's times like these
I miss you,
For memories in the past
where once a purr
made emotion complete,
Bonded in whispers,
Hidden beyond the fragments of the wonderer,
Outcast to some
but in the path
the mystic always rings true
for reaches we both had made
in ways echoed forever
mediated by a journey
which is traveled beyond means unseen.

March 3, 1997
