Inward does my sight go,
Forever in trust,
Blessed in insight,
Touched with visions
which answers questions
that has vexed some,
But wisdom grows
doth in time,
Shall it be that miracles
rise in mysterious places?
For I hear a calling
where a shaman touches my soul,
And in the fire
I see his face smiling
where comfort fill me with his spirit.

March 6, 1999
