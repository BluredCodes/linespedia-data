title: "Feel The Rain"

Feel the rain,
There it is pouring on a desert flower,
Thunder rising in the sky,
Memories changing a heart at a time,
Hear the whispers which touch on forgiveness,
Secrets mirrored in a moment,
Rebirth of an ancient invocation,
Images feathered in magic,
Truth centered in time,
Miracles edged in hope,
And a smile blessed by a child
though twisted for a moment,
Courage brings a breath
of love within the sacred,
And a falling star blinks within the eye.

Jan. 15, 1999
