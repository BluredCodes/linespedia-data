---
title: "Colors"
writer: "sidewinder"

Look at the hue
                 in that multicolored rainbow,
Shiny new colors 
                       from a different point of view,
The blue of the ocean,
                            The silver of the beach,
The pink of the seashells
                            that most of us
                                               try to reach,
The red of the sun,
                        The brown of the skin,
But the prettiest colors
                                are found within,
The green of someone's eyes,
                                The gold of someone's hair,
Yes...
                    there's plenty of colors,
Just look
               over there,
Everyday I find them
                      where one least would think,
A smile that lights up the room,
                    And my heart thanks
for this day
                          those colors
                                        that one meets.

Jan. 10, 2002
