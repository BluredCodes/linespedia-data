---
title: "The Teacher Within"
writer: "sidewinder"

Thoughtful times,
Brief delights,
A spirit of transformation
transcending time itself,
Hidden in pathways,
A reunion to those that have a key
to the teacher with,
A journey to self-healing and self-change,
Heart and soul are united to the one mind,
Awakened to the natural forces
that challenges one in everyday life,
Examine what has taken place,
Your role in it,
Your needs,
The needs of others,
Open those gateways and doors
that can reveal more about yourself,
Be in the world but not of it.

Sept. 24, 1997
