---
title: "A Spirit Deep Within"
writer: "sidewinder"

Dark corners in a balance of time,
Wind rising in jealous moods,
A tide in a circle of secrets
forever reaching those shadows
guided by dreams of chance,
A tradition touched in transformations
compassionate and enduring,
A book of changes
calling harmony
reclaiming nature
forever in motion,
A visionary realm
glimmering in the moonlight,
A consciousness embedded
in solitude
governed by a spirit deep within,
Remember to let the winds of heaven dance
with your heart
in a legacy of time.

Nov. 6, 1997
