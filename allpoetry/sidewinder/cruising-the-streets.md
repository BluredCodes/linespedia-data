title: "Cruising The Streets"

Riding around in my
fifty-four Chevy,
I'm just out cruising,
Enjoying the sights,
Talking to familiar people,
Whistling at the girls,
I love hearing those
roars of my engine,
I'm having a great time,
Just pursuing this way,
People ask why?
And I would reply,
I don't know,
I just love doing it!
If there's nothing else to do,
Why go knocking it?
Relax....
Enjoy yourself!
Just ride on down the line,
Yes...
you're cruising the streets,
Sometimes...
I'm looking for action,
"Maybe someone is
looking for a race"-
Or you might find
a sweet lady to talk to,
You can never tell,
Hey..
I'm gonna keep on...
cruising the streets.

May 26, 1982
