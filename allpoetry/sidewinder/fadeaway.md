title: "Fadeaway"
writer: "sidewinder"

Fadeaway from a night of charms
celebrated to the fact of a memory
gone temporarily,
Why?
I'll never know,
But to only a fool it matters,
Creeping mystics,
Vagabonds,
And truth realized
though I'll never admit it,
Hidden images,
Secrets held beyond
a early silver winter,
Faces frozen in time,
Cryptic sunsets
rising blindly
only for your eyes,
To some,
I never make sense,
But if the truth be told
you only have to look in my eyes.

Sept. 1996
