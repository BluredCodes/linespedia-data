---
title: "A Distant Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-distant-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

Cool summer breezes,
Touch against the back of your neck,
A celebration heard around the corner,
a renewal of heart,
Seasons where a spirit
touches a smile,
New horizons meet the sunset,
Lovers found in the park,
And a new light touches
like sunshine found in spring,
Embraces sometimes complete thy soul,
Dreams rage where needed,
And a smile brightens the day,
laughter is heard from children's voices, 
Those generations that have life before them,
What will they learn?
And what can we learn from them?
a complete nourishment found in wisdom,
A distant time
found different
yet the same.

Oct. 15, 2000
