Like each square of a quilt
life itself unfolds,
And those that follow
remain an individual,
Look there remains the bear,
And beside him comes a deer
drinking the cool water,
As she look up

high in the sky flies an eagle
enjoying his freedom,
And through his flight he spies
a rabbit scurrying to get away,
And through his journey
a squirrel joins him,
But each must go his own way,
Still the day remains.

April 7, 1985
