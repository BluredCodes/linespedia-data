Touching those rhythms
varied eye to eye,
Complicated behind a shadow
sensed in the strength and endurance
where insight and compassion permeates
the afternoon,
But only the silence
in imaginations circle the sacred,
Yet we find beauty
in everything that we see,
And in that we know ourselves
where a quest revives without consequence,
And I find a smile
in those eyes found in every corner
waiting for a hope
just to find new love.

Sept. 23, 2000
