Risen in the dim blue light,
Vivid and intoxicating,
Subtle in figure and form,
And there she is...
watching me,
For what reason I know not?
But that's her prerogative,
What woman can you justify?
Still...
in the perception of time
her essence pervades in a wondering whisp,
Glimpsed only by a heartbeat.
