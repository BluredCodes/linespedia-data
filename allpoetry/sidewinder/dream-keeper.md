title: "Dream Keeper"

And the dawn rises
where those dreams
celebrate & touch a heart,
Dream keeper
fulfill my deepest desires,
And if by chance
the night brings the melody
that sings to my soul,
Whispering into the shadowy mists
where a heartbeat
spires
a step at a time,
Then listen to the laughter
that seeks only wisdom,
Separate
and sometimes controversial.

June 1, 1999
