---
title: "My Tan"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "my-tan"

As the warmth of the sun
touches my shin,
The depth of my color changes,
And I come from being a light colored anglo,
To a rich, dark skinned man,
Warmed by the days
though for only four months out of the year
will I experience this,
then as the snow returns my color will disappear
until the next year.

March 25, 1985
