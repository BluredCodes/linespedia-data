Yes....
	            It's true!
	                           Times have changed,
	No longer do we worry about the atom bomb,
	And for some reason we're still smoking dope,
	Is that the reason for the loss of short time memory?
	Though now we worry about Saddam Hussein
	and his vision of germ & chemical warfare,
	Kids still throw spit wads & put gum under their seats
	and teachers still lecture
	                              though I don't know why,
	No one's listening!
	                          And the parables of wisdom
	touch those who grasp
	                          for its meaning,
	Though the meaning of hip hop
	                        has waned
	                                  to today's latest
	jargon
	               all though
	                              somehow it's lost to me
	and reformed skeptics
	                              were once known as hippies
	changed to yuppies
	                       and now are known
	as today's respectable people
	                              with families & children of their own,
	What could it be
	                  with the future
	that we're likely to see?
	
	Feb. 28, 2001
