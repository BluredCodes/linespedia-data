title: "Perfect In Whispers"

Freedom comes to those who dare to be free,
Encountered only...
from those who see,
Touched in thy heart
and remembered...
by truth,
a passage of time
perfect in whispers
where shadows tilt under one evening star,
Messages arrive in an echo,
Insatiable to those who remember
and as the wind whispers
those secrets
yesterday opens a new day,
Gently taking those souls
forgotten in a horizon.

July 25, 2000
