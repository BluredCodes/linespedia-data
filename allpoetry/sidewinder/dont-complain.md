---
title: "Don't Complain"
writer: "sidewinder"

Its crazy!
While all the world complains
I'm here going up the wall,
Why can't they smile every once in a while
instead of complaining about what they haven't got?
Be grateful for what they do have,
For life is no bowl of cherries,
It has its pitfalls,
But at least they have a life,
they always gripe,
And that isn't what life is all about,
At least give life a chance,
You could always be worse off,
Be happy,
And think this,
If you're not happy where you are,
Then change it,
But don't make it worse on people around you,
They have a right to be happy too,
Think about it!

March 26, 1982
