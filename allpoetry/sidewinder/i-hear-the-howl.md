---
title: "I Hear The Howl"
writer: "sidewinder"

I hear the howl,
A song
touching your desire,
Filling me
with that need,
Serenaded in the reflection
of emotion,
Affirming those recollections
and enriches my mind,
A mere horizon of desire,
Touching that lingering
glance
where our eyes meet,
Could it be our first kiss?
Somehow forming our need
for love
which rages in a moment of time.

Dec. 18, 2001
