I remember the smile on your face,
The strength in your eyes,
The love in your heart,
The patience in your hands,
the understanding in your mind,
For you were always there for me,
And you taught me
that family matters,
And when times were bad
it's the family that bonds together,
For no matter what happens
is we remember this secret
life can always hold a joy
not yet stolen by the summer,
even though a shadow can slip through
it's the sunshine revealed only
by the truth kept by your memory,
This is what I hold most dearly
in a celebration of your life.

Dec. 26, 1999I wrote this in remembrance of My Grandmother Layton.
May she always hold peace.
