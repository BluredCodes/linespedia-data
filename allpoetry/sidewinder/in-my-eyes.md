As a child
I saw things
with a vivid imagination,
Every crook and cranny held adventure
and every tree, ditch and attic
became a hide away,
So it was a wonder
when I proceeded to make my way up our house
with screwdriver and hammer in hand,
And backing me up was my brother,
Oh up there seemed grand!
And just as a precaution
I'd have an escape route
right through another room,
Only there was one way in,
Still...
          That's alright!
                            I'll fix that!
Bam!, Bam!, Bam !
Now I no longer have that to worry about,
Or do I ?
For here comes my Mom and Dad,
And I can see the anger in their eyes,
What am I to do?
I'm only six,
Not old enough to understand
the how's and why's,
Still...
I survived,
Being myself
and looking at the world through a child's eyes.

March 20, 1985And yes...
this is a true story of me growing up.
and I got into alot of trouble then.
Aw well...
you grow up...
sometimes !
