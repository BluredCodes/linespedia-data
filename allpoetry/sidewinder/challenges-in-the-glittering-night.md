title: "Challenges In The Glittering Night"

Challenges in the glittering night,
Animated in its holy truth,
Venerated in life,
Draw those images with originate
in the mind of the imagination,
Glimpses into mind of God,
Authentic in the characteristics of intrinsic design,
Explore those dark waters
where some fear to tread,
Spoken moods magical in the stars of destiny,
Touch the edge,
Where you push your limits
and rise above the common,
Forgetting the fears that you have been taught,
Opening those new doors
where realities start a new beginning.

Sept. 19, 1998
