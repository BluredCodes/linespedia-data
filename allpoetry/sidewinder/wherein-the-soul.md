title: "Wherein The Soul"

Think...
Does my heart reach into places
wherein the soul completes itself?
Or does the shadow remain unchanged?
Forever remembered only in time,
Laughter whispered by those who
lift the veil of images
risen in echoes,
Surrender and touch those places
where only the imagination can complete itself,
And in the discovery
forever met beyond those who
reach within a breath
whispered
moment by moment.
