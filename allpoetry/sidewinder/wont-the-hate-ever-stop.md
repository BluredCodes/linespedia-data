Won't the hate ever stop?
For years the message slowly
got through,
Isn't it finally clear?
First it was the color of your skin,
Next it was the faith that you followed,
On & on it goes,
Didn't the Civil war & World War II
teach anyone anything?
Our children was learning to accept
people for who they are,
Regardless of their differences,
Now hate reveals itself,
Those that can't accept us
try to kill us,
Poison us,
Destroy our way of life,
And our dregs do the same
to our own Muslim people,
I was told that their name meant peace,
Now I'm not saying
turn the other check,
Sometimes justice must be served,
Like a cold iron fist,
But it's wrong to punish those who like us
want nothing but to live their lives,
Don't we have enough bigotry to last
a lifetime?
Let's get a clue,
To hurt our own people
doesn't make any sense,
Let our anger abide
& find those who need
to be found,
Seek truth
& show that as Americans,
We stand united, 
Not torn as those
who would believe
that we are.

Sept. 19, 2001
