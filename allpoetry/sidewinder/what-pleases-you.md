What pleases you?
A sudden smile?
A whisper only meant for you?
A cool night after a hot summer day?
A long, loving glance directed your way?
Does the memory that is shared
vary in the promise of an echo?
Or does it disappear
like the laughter of a faerie?
Some reveal that certain suddenness
found in a passion
breath in origin,
I myself....
look for the experience
of the first revelation
of love,
Can you look in my eyes
and see what I see?
Let us reach into our souls
and find what we both need.
