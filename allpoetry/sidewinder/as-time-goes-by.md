---
title: "As Time Goes By"
writer: "sidewinder"

Gestures made in days forgotten
with eyes held true
within a miracle made
for the soul,
Words do touch
in promises
pure with intentions,
Embraces...
forgotten glimpses
within a veil of dreams,
Desires
hidden behind the silence
coming together
yet staying alone,
Still...
I hear your whispers
separate as time goes by.

May 6, 1999
