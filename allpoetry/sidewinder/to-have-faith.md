The truth rests in me,
A fellowship gathered
within its own self
completed in a democracy,
A circle of hope
generated in the spirit
of things to come,
Can you hear the coming of the future?
The dreams shall be fulfilled
forever in a portrait of time,
Miracles can come true,
Sometimes in places least likely considered,
But you have to have faith
in your heart as well as your mind,
Hold on to the possibility,
It rests only with you.

Oct. 3, 1996
