Thrice through clouds of fog,
An image of a rider
shining mystically in an armor of silver
ancient in spirit
rages,
Let the vision quest begin,
And a star is revealed
to a place far away
in the land of dreams,
Where faith thrives,
Invoked by powers
of magical realms,
Guided in the veil of the Faerie,
Inspired by a heart
that has the wisdom to
look beyond the surface of the water
and the ripples that has the power to mesmerize,
Eyes that search for the rhythm of truth
in a circle of life.

June 27, 1997
