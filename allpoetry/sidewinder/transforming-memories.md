I walk softly in those eyes
wherein that melody arises,
Sensations met,
Spoken words touch
past a horizon,
Perspectives cling
where some forget to look,
And a smile brightens the whole day,
Could it be...
                that sometimes we see
                   only what we want to?
Transforming memories
                     a touch at a time.

Aug. 24, 2001
