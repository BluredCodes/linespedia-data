Oh ye walls that we put between us,
They feel like silent chasms
that reach into our souls
raging into the pits of hell,
Protruding like sharp spears,
Keeping ourselves from things we need to know,
In this witching hour what is it
that we need to learn?
Are there mirrors in a reflection
that we need to see?
Or is it that we're too stubborn
to realize that sometimes those lessons
in life may hold doors that we need to walk through?
But in our blindness in those bleak seasons
revelations become closed
forever keeping us walking in circles
that guides us to a journey
into the next life,
Where we begin again.

Feb. 8, 1998
