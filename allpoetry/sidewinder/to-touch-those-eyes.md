---
title: "To Touch Those Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch those eyes
that met mine hidden in forgotten shadows,
That smile...
brightening a day when I needed it the most,
take my heart
and let me relive those golden days
where my voice
touches in song
remembering your name
held only in a distant past,
celebrating a season of love
and mark the time
broken in the night,
Where a rhythm finds a harmony
and our hearts touch those secrets
never to be revealed,
Can you hear those whispers
varied in a lifetime?
