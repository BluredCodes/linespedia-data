To be touched with the spirit of inspiration,
A journey met with those inclinations
within only in a smile,
Those interludes
met in those crossroads
in eyes risen beyond a horizon,
That sunshine
which brings a warmth
varied from moment to moment,
A hello
hidden from the past,
But a pleasant surprise
to say the least....
Those new footprints
founded in friendship.

April 28, 2001
