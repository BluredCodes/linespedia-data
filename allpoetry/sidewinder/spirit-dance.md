Spirit dance
touch the child in each of us,
Share the joy
a fulfillment of life,
Whisper to those secrets
hidden behind the shadows
passionate to those who are open,
Rise in the embrace,
A key in the universe,
Invoke the song of warmth,
Comforter and consoler of hearts,
A wheel of truth,
An echo of wisdom,
A shimmering light
to those who need the guidance,
Eyes for those who are open,
Fire in understanding,
A mirror of self-prophecy
encircled in the freedom of life.

Oct. 27, 1997
