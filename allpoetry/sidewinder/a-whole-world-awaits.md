title: "A Whole World Awaits"
writer: "sidewinder"

Instrument of words
hear me,
Fill with thy wisdom,
Let not my eyes be deceived,
But be clear when illusions rise,
Humble me,
When my heart and my mind are not one,
Let trust hold my fate,
Natural laws do govern my soul
in realms where Faerie
gives joy,
Wherein a whole world awaits,
Songs serenade in memories
to celebrate visions,
May they grow each day
forever giving me peace,
Journeys reach into magic
forever in a harmony with the muses
and open doors sacred
in their visualization,
Let me listen to the elements
and in return I'll honor them.

May 5, 1997
