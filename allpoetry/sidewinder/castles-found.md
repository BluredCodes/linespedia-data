Castles found in a time
where sunsets touch
moment to moment,
Those that live
measure to measure
find that sometimes
living up to expectations
are much harder than
what some realise,
To touch within a soul
a heart that is pure
forever found in paradise,
And in the meantime
harmony reaches those
one strand at a time
completing an idea
that once rang true,
Preludes paraphrased in memories.

Nov. 7, 2000
