title: "Forever In The Keeping"
writer: "sidewinder"

In through the evening
	those embraces
	touch in moments
	where a smile
	and in the destiny of the unknown
	encircle your presence,
	To trace those
	who fulfill
	a miracle within,
	Rise beyond those transformations
	held by an angel,
	listen to an invitation
	that warms a heart
	and leads beyond a sheltered past
	forever in the keeping.
	May 14, 1999
