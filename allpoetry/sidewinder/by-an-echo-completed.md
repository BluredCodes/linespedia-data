title: "By An Echo Completed"

Absence of thought
revealed in circles
where shadow meets shadow,
Crystal blue persuasion 
sheltered only...
by a dream
varied in the night,
Yet those stars
touch upon a heartbeat
charmed
by the warmth
of your nearness,
Eyes feathered
by the wind
completed
by an echo.

June 18, 2000
