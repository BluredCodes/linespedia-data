title: "A Song Begins"

As the night begins
	here we are,
	Looking in each others eyes,
	Wondering what the other feels,
	Hoping for that one special moment
	that we'll keep for the rest of our lives,
	And as we hold each other
	a song begins,
	It's one of love,
	But it's only the beginning,
	For we have the rest of our lives
	to look forward to,
	What memories we will share!
	All we have to do is reach out
	and take them,
	Who knows what the future will bring,
	But as long as we're together
	nothing is in vain.
	Aug 22, 1985
