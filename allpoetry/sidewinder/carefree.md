Beside the quiet waters,
Alone in the stillness of the day,
Here I sit,
              Thinking,
How great it is just to be alive!
Some worry about their troubles,
                Not I,
Though trouble is no stranger to me,
                 But today
I'm somehow care free.

March 28, 1985
