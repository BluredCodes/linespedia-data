Allured
in the face of reality,
Where a compromise insures
or suggests ancient truths,
Fulfilled in diversity,
Transcribed by a uniqueness
wherein if one looks,
One can find contentment
in anything,
Seldom is a heart alone
or complete without a reflection,
For the shadow is nothing without the sunshine.

Jan. 22, 1999
