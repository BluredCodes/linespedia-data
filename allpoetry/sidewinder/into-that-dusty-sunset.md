title: "Into That Dusty Sunset"
writer: "sidewinder"

Into that dusty sunset
	mired into thy horizon,
	Feel those hollows
	that whisper beyond the silence,
	Feel those hearts
	which triumph
	in that time
	when seasons enrich thy soul,
	Hear the laughter in the distance
	where the summer marches into fall
	and you know....
	                     that it's good to be alive!
	Smell the rain
	                 that washes away
	the toil and dirt
	                       of everyday life,
	And it's good
	                   to see what tomorrow may bring!
	Look through a child's eyes
	                               and see
	                                           without judgment
	the innocence
	                     and curiosity
	brought on
	                   by the mischief
	                                   of a song
	that touches in springtime
	                             and you'll find
	                                               that dream
	that satisfies
	                       the soul.
	Jan. 10, 2002
