title: "Taking Things Too Seriously"
writer: "sidewinder"

Razor edged tongue
fraying on my mind,
In the background I hear swing music,
Relax...
                      I tell myself,
Don't let life's ups and downs get to you,
Smile once in a while,
Laugh if you have too...
                            Too much of the time we take
things too seriously,
Forgetting that which we all can enjoy,
              Those dreams that keep us young,
                                            Looking into the light
instead of the shadow,
                             Where it all makes sense,
Could it be...
                 only an illusion?
Or does freedom, love and the good things
                                        hold its hand...
just out of reach?

Oct. 9, 2000
