Pleasures within a celebration
conjuring rhythms
conceived out of a
truth,
Primed in with a seriousness.
Forever reaching a point,
Advancing in a twilight,
Mired in a moment,
Sired in its own completeness,
A scribe touching those heartbeats
forgotten only in inspiration,
Where a legend
writes and history remembers.

Sept. 16, 1999
