---
title: "To Touch The Mystery"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "to-touch-the-mystery"

To thee I write
from my heart
pleasures in times of need
forgotten in an enchanted world
where myths, legends & folklore are revealed,
Open tales give secrets shared
& understanding conjured,
Simply for the taking,
Why?
It's those mysteries
that make life worth living
and in the dreamer
an epic can be considered
and in time well remembered,
Verses taken
form the divine
celebrated in the mythic consciousness
where other worlds create &
respect magic
aflame in the heart.

Feb, 23, 1997
