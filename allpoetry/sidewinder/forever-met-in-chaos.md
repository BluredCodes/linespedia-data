---
title: "Forever Met In Chaos"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "forever-met-in-chaos"

Separate with those times
where magic is enough,
Interesting in an unfashionable way,
traces of those tragedies
which bare fangs to an unquestionable
truth,
Bare in mind,
It's a fool's game
that some try to follow,
Shall it be known
that the silence in time
reveals a horror forever met
in the domain of dread?
Nightmares meet rationality
in a constant battle,
Shattered only in dreams
forever met in chaos.

Nov. 11, 1999
