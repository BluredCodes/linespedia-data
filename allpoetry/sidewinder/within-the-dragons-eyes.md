title: "Within The Dragon's Eyes"

Forever sheltered with the dragon's eyes
fulfilling a rite
held by my own making,
A waking dream
to touch those champions
which fulfill my destiny, 
Visions which arise
through the lessons of time,
Those mysterious that open doors
Daring to manipulate realities of perspectives
authentic in principles of insight,
Invoking those teachings
of the hear,
Envision that magical quest
recalling past lives
which activates those energies,
Opening channels which merges
a spirit within thyself,
a soul soaring in the otherworld
celebrating freedom.

Jan. 28, 1998
