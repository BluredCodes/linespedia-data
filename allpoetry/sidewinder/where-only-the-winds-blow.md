title: "Where Only The Winds Blow"

Held in time of old
where winds blow
a soul gives birth
to lore's long forgotten,
Rejoice in its truth
for all the elements are revered
each in its own blessing,
Reach beyond time
where mortal understanding
has no meaning,
But a promise held
reveals its own treasures,
Wisdom forever remembered
in tales
sung by songs held in tribute
to those
who hold magic
in its truest form,
Never for the sake of fame & fortune,
Only for the silence
of its truth.

March 16, 1997
