---
title: "Touch Those Who Need The Company"
writer: "sidewinder"

Touch those lonely streets
and listen to the concertos
above dusty hallways
in forgotten doors
open in imagination
and I smile
revealing a part of myself,
Can you see beyond the silence?
Reach beyond those echoes
that some refuse to hear?
Look there's the sunshine
and the clouds high above in the sky!
Reach and touch those sunsets
that blossom like fields in the spring,
Remember this if nothing else...
Complete thy heart,
And touch those who need
the company.

June 4, 2001
