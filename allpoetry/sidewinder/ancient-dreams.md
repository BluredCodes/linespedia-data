To touch a remembrance,
A visionaries echo in the silence,
A counterpoint in time,
Images of souls who
cry for mercy,
Those innocents who strive for
 perfection
but never succeed,
A quiet need for solitude,
perhaps destiny's offerings
will unfold a secret or
a mystery,
those familiar waters
that are chartered by our own future,
listen to the inner voice that guides you
when you most need it,
For inspiration rises in places
where the twilight of the day
is sacred,
an enlightenment of ancient prose
where those dreams are a doorway
to a world of the star Goddess.

Nov. 13, 1997
