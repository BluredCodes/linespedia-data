---
title: "I'm Lost In The Shadow"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "im-lost-in-the-shadow"
source: "AllPoetry"

Touching forever
wherein those reflections in time
promises more,
Blind only
to a heart held
spiritually
in trust
mired only
with or without you,
Celebrations
given only in the freedom,
And if the forgotten
is remembered,
Hardly...
I'm lost in the shadow.

Sept. 17, 1999
