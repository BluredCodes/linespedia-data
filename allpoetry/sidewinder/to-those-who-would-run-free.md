To those who would 
run free
wild with courage
and face the world with dignity,
To honor a heart brave with the wind,
To celebrate a wonder
that only one with nature would understand,
Dance with the ancient ones
forever with the wolves,
Smile down on us when we are down,
Touch us with your spirit,
Forever we will remember you,
For in those crossroads of life,
Our paths touched only for a moment,
But somehow we will meet again,
Only this time as brothers, 
Soaring high with the wind,
Touching the clouds as only an eagle can,
For you have met your destiny
and someday I will meet mine.

July 4, 1999To my friend Wolf,
May he always dance with the ancient ones !

He was my friend...
he died in a car crash in
July of 1999
