title: "The Story That Unfolds"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "the-story-that-unfolds"

A touch in one's soul, 
Treasured by some,
Forgotten by others,
Held in a promise of a forgotten future,
Reached by those who matter,
Claimed by those who dream,
Rejected by those who live in reality,
Honored by those who keep a fantasy alive in their hearts,
Felt by the universal need of a tale that needed to be told,
Such is reason I hold,
For I am the bard that is never forgotten,
Listen and pass on the story that unfolds,
Precious to those who care,
A taste rich and alive for those with an imagination,
Forever hold it in your mind.

June 19, 1996
