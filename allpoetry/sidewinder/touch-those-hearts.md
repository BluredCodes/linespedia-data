---
title: "Touch Those Hearts"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "touch-those-hearts"

Child in spirit
touch those hearts
insatiable in their needs,
Where those footprints
touch in the sand,
Passion stir
breath by breath,
Enchanted in eyes
remembered a heartbeat at a time,
Those private petals
which bloom in springtime
and the mysteries of life
whisper those secrets
that fall like tears
beyond a shadow.

March 29, 2001
