Insight
 verifying that warm light
sensuous in its own being,
Never knowing
where it starts or where it ends,
But conscious of its own existence,
defined by those who
                              revile in it
like a memory
                                          half forgotten
contemporary in its own being,
Why...
               you say?
I don't know,
                        Maybe it just sounded good.

June 4, 2001
