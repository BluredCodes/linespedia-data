Golden whispers,
Wings of the wind,
 Echoes of the past,
Twilight waters rushing through
visualizations beyond the circles
whisper softly a wisdom from the past,
Pure essences rise to touch a point of transformation,
Swords of meditation,
tears of peace
sheltered by a trust,
Joy and sorrow meet as one,
Metaphors touch the shadow
and reveal the open door
to mysteries and unexpected gifts in our lives,
Destinations to truth
that transcend those miracles
in dreams sacred in healing.

Dec. 30, 1997
