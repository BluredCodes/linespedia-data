title: "All That Glitters"

All that glitters,
See the shadow from the light,
Beware of the illusions that follow,
Glimpse the magic that rises from your heart
inspired by the spirit,
 Seek to understand yourself,
Your own soul keeps an inner strength
& challenges are necessary for growth,
The sun symbolizes the path & illuminates the goal,
Which is the human heart,
Open & touch what has been kept secret,
Draw up on your courage, 
Face your darkest fears,
Illuminate the magic that touches your life,
A gift from the Goddess
who shelters your life.

Sept. 26, 1997
