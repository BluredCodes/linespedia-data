---
title: "What You've Touched"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "what-youve-touched"
source: "AllPoetry"

You've touched apart of me
that has the need
to be awakened,
For the solitude
has walked too long
alone...
And though I complain,
My life would be missing
a completeness
without in it,
So your smile has a place,
remember this
when you feel alone.

July 23, 1998
