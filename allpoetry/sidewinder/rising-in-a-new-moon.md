---
title: "Rising In A New Moon"
writer: "sidewinder"

Spirit guides
                calling to...
                              those spirits
in elements
                  varying only in thy truth,
Wisdom found in thy experience,
                                Sacred energy
complete what I need
                      to set my spirit free,
Understand those elements
                            in nature
revealing that completeness
                             awakening what's within,
Desire to reach
                    a harmony
spiraling in that dance
                        where I can find myself
beyond that which
                       is rising in a new moon,
Can you hear it?
                    I can!

June 15, 2001
