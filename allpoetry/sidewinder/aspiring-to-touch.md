title: "Aspiring To Touch"

Laughter and smiles
                   whisper to those
who aspire to dream in the rhythm of life,
                                          Touch those clouds
      which dance in the sky
                             revealed in the dawn
        by a promise
                                    staircased in silver,
Where an echo
               ripples in the wind
                                and in the horizon
      there are eyes that watch
                    aspiring to touch
                                      the dark
even though
            a mystery is revealed
                                    forever in a day.

May 5, 1999
