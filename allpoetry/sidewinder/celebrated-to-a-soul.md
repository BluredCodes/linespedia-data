title: "Celebrated To A Soul"

Touched with that freedom
celebrates to a soul
completely open
as if those eyes were a child,
Unbiased,
Unafraid,
Yet hold the capacity to love,
Wise beyond your years
yet realize that dreams
have their place too,
When I hear your laughter
it makes me realize
that these times...
are ones to remember,
Not those found
in a fantasy,
Some say that chivalry is dead,
But that I refuse to believe,
because when I'm around you
your presence brings out the 
upbringing that I was raised with,
For some forget there are some
gentleman left,
We have a kindness
that is waiting to be shared,
look in my eyes
and see...
what I see,
We can complete each other...
If you are willing.
