title: "A Splendor Of Heart"

Starlight vision,
A magical insight
to touch the younger self,
laughter is the gift of joy
that we all need,
A reflection expressed by
love,
A rising sun,
center of the whirling sky,
Splendor of gold & saffron,
Let the circle rage in its pulse
for the awakening rises in the heart of light,
Forever encircling those who would embrace her,
A sonnet in time,
a whisper within the madness,
A tangle inside a labyrinth of flame
which echoes in a silhouette of silence,
Those spirits who reawaken the sun child
that lives in all of us.
