title: "Signed .....Forever True"

A letter here,
	I have found
	just under the edge of my door,
	On the front,
	There was written;
	"Priority...
	Please read!",
	Then the story unfolds
	as she begins-
	I'm just a lady that
	you probably don't remember
	Still...
	Here's my story;
	I met you at the drugstore
	a very long time ago,
	At the time I was feeling down,
	But you cared enough to ask;
	"What was the matter?"
	"Things couldn't be that wrong !"
	And so you cheered me up,
	Never will I forget you,
	You gave me something that
	I'll always love you for,
	What was that?
	'twas love,
	You cared more than
	anyone I've ever known,
	Somehow...
	You gave me self-worth,
	And for that,
	I thank you!
	Signed...
	Forever True,
	As time went on
	we had shared
	a couple of night together,
	Nothing very heavy,
	But became closer and closer,
	What we knew
	was true friendship,
	Yes...
	You have a way with yourself,
	It's nothing for you to give,
	And that's a natural gift,
	Keep on giving!
	On through the day
	we shared many memories,
	Eventually we lost contact,
	Why?
	Mostly because of me,
	I wanted to see new faces,
	But that didn't matter to you,
	Happiness was your concern,
	So what did you do?
	You got together with your friends
	and through the biggest party
	that I have ever seen...
	Then you gave what you could,
	But it wasn't the money that mattered,
	You gave of yourself
	and that touched me,
	Thank you for giving,
	And if I can ever
	repay the kindness,
	Please let me know !
	What's mine is yours,
	And thank you again!
	I'll always love you!
	Bye for now!
	                  P.S.........
	Don't be such a stranger,
	I'll leave my name & number.
	                         
	                             Signed....
	                                         Forever True
	May 4, 1984
