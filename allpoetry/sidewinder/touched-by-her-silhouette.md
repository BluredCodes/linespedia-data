Touched by her silhouette
                         moment by moment,
Touched by her smile,
                         The smell of her perfume,
It lingers for awhile,
                          I can still...
hear her laugh,
                    Somehow....
it touches my soul,
                          That perfect moment of romance
meets until tomorrow,
                            Where we both
                                            find a way
to play life's game,
                        Somehow different and new,
We'll look just beyond that horizon,
                                     Meet the summer
and follow
                      the autumn's dew.

Jan. 10, 2002
