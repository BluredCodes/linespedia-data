Rage... 
               Rage....
within that sweet sorrow,
Cold wherein a heart touches tomorrow,
tears felt in that yesterday
where a horror rises within the eye,
It is said...
that a wrong can not right a wrong,
But where can blood be met
when spilt so needlessly,
And brother finds brother
only to find his hand cut off
while reaching only in peace,
Those that say a jihad is around the corner
fought day by day...
knows not what anger arises,
But in those talons
hard lessons finds a reply,
Look beyond that echo
reached now in a hollowness
and cries out
we must not be diswayed,
Though in this...
we must find out our path
and honor those that have fallen.
