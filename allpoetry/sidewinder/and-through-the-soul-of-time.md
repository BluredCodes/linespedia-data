---
title: "And Through The Soul Of Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "and-through-the-soul-of-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

It speaks to me
of sheltered times
within the plains of passage,
Forgotten doors
revealed within a soul,
And when the spirit speaks,
I listen,
For wisdom is calling beyond the mountains,
I hear the drumbeat of the ancient one,
Their call gladdens my spirit,
For in my dreams
I know the wolf,
We hunt to together,
Within the night of the moon,
He is my brother,
Also revealed to me
is brother cougar,
We fish together
and travel ancient trails,
And through the soul of time
Gaia smiles.

June 17, 1999
