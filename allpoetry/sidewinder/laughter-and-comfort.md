---
title: "Laughter And Comfort"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "laughter-and-comfort"
source: "AllPoetry"

Laughter is such a comforting song
that feels within a heart
or a touch
that warms the skin,
And within the memory
the smile lives on,
Just looking in the eyes
tells a story
that some hold in awe,
returning to a circle
that changes within time.

Aug 1, 2000
