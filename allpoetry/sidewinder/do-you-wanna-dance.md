Do you wanna dance?
Spend your life with me?
Find a meaning where two hearts touch?
The first time I saw you,
                          I knew then....
                                         that our lives
would not be the same
without each of us
being in each others lives,
                            I can see the roses in your eyes,
When you walk in the room....
                             your sweet fragrance
fill the air,
                    Your sudden touch
                                      soft...
embraces my skin,
                   Circle to circle,
                                     Heartbeat to heartbeat,
And what then?
                  Could it be...
that you want me
                    as much as I want you?
Somehow...
            Our journeys must meet?
Find those moments....
                       when the heart looks within,
And share those passions...
                            that some spend a lifetime
looking for.
