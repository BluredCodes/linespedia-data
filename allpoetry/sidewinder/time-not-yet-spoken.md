title: "Time Not Yet Spoken"

And now let's touch
forever reaching a pleasure
where forever more
brings to mind
those inclinations
held only in the summer
gently met by a promise of a rainbow,
Those words felt past a dream
touch in the night
and the moon meets in its invocation
where your eyes smile in its light,
Embracing a heart with a song
intense in its passion
and a season begins
where you accept time
not yet spoken.

July 18, 2000
