---
title: "In The Silence Of Prayer"
writer: "sidewinder"

Whispering secrets
	which touch on the voice of imagination,
	Treasures which hold mysteries
	that holds intrigue,
	Insight and compassion
	forever revealed
	in the twilight of the night
	forever embraced
	by sacred magic,
	Which could be revealed
	by the spirits of the world,
	A visionary who touches
	the heart of a child
	in each of us,
	Tears in nature,
	A silence of prayer
	to those who see
	a wisdom
	in this world of destiny
	forever celebrated
	by its truth.
	Nov. 3, 1997
