---
title: "A Soul Touches With Thine"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-soul-touches-with-thine"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34561-A-Soul-Touches-With-Thine-by-sidewinder"

A soul touches with thine
for completing
a contemplation,
Arriving within a miracle,
Celebrating those eons
that vary
in increments that
persevere,
Moment by moment,
Like sand throughout time.

Sept. 16, 1999
