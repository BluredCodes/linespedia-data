title: "Echoes Of Time"

Time through out time,
Fading the echoes of my mind,
Howling at the stars,
Laughing at those questions
that haunt me,
Jealous faces
making a mockery of what they don't understand.
Feeling lonely...
in a crowded bar,
Smiling...
yet not knowing of what might be,
Looking...
yet afraid of what they might see,
If only...
they look deep in their own hearts,
Differences....
forever remain the same.

March 28, 1996
