---
title: "Forever With The Moment"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "forever-with-the-moment"

Touched by a memory,
Shared by the softness of your voice,
Caressed with the gentleness of your smile,
held in time never ending,
With moments like these,
Reaching fantasies
forever with the moment,
Concerned about what can be,
Touching with a heart
forever in time.

May 4, 1994
