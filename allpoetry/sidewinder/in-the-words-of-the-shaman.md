title: "In The Words Of The Shaman"

In the words of the shaman
those visions and miracles
that reveal
more than a sheltered shadow
that curl around the body
like rings of incense smoke,
Tempting the senses one again
into a life removed of sickness
touching within and without,
A harmony in nature
completing a circle in time,
I hear those spirits
that counsel in wisdom,
Listen to the winds
which whisper secrets,
Watch eyes that see
the spiral dance,
Feel the harmony with your heart
and seek the serenity of life,
In this you will find joy,
May it follow in your journey of life.

Oct. 20, 2000
