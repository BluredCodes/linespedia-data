---
title: "A Reunion Of Heart"
writer: "sidewinder"

In whispers of ancient echoes,
I'm touched by the spirits of the elements,
A lore of magic
championed by those who revere
songs and visions
guided in rituals
shaped by my own voice
held in a promise
of journeys yet to be traveled,
 A revelation for its own sake
held by a promise of truth
united in a reunion of heart
expressed in the essence
of self healing
forever remembered
in terms of joy,
In reaching this I'm truly blessed.

July 18, 1997
