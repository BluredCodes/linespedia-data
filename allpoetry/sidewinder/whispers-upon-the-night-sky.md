---
title: "Whispers Upon The Night Sky"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "whispers-upon-the-night-sky"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48158-Whispers-Upon-The-Night-Sky-by-sidewinder"
---

Shuttered in pain,
My mind touched
shattered by the events of the night before,
Enclosed in though
my heart untouched
freed from the bonds I help make,
Jagged & jaded
yet...
in a shade of fear,
Unsure of what might be ahead,
Where do I go from here?
Standing alone
tall,
feeling the cold night chill,
trying to arrange the thoughts so wrong,
Daytime dreamer, 
Lying so still,
Is it the night
or somehow the nightmare,
Is it the devil I know,
Or the unknown I fear
that forces me back here,
Dreams of the night before
dancing in a hazy sleep, 
Twisting, turning, touching
fear
melts away into the passion
as lust sweeps over the mind,
Ravenous in its warmth,
A whisper gentled
softly, slowly
in the wanting,
Gathered a bit
at a time,
Blind...
to a need,
The hunger for a pain
that brings forth a smile,
Feeling in control
but at the same time
 letting go,
If just for an instant rush
that brings tears when the night is done.

March 6, 2002

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Adriana Monique Edwards
