---
title: "Serenades In The Night"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "serenades-in-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

And as the night falls
the rising moon calls,
Hear the howls in the night?
Those screams that everyone seems to fear,
Beware of those eyes that follow,
For hollowness of your soul draws near,
It's in those shadows
visions of the macabre
whispers like a distant voice,
Those serenades
that weep dreams to nightmares
crimson it its soliloquy,
And in that echo
the wind bears the smell of death itself,
Red in its embrace,
Blind in its touch,
Those duel natures
that draw secrets
intolerant of the hunt,
Walk that dangerous path
while trying to retain your mortality,
In some ways your humanity,
In this serenade
a song beckons you
to join them...
in the masquerade.

Feb. 14, 2001
