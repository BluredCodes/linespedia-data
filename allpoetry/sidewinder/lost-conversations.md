How are you doing?
How's your new love?
It seems it was only yesterday
when you smiled at my face,
Funny how time slips away,
Remember those rainy days when we'd sit around
& watch it rain?
Your company was needed, 
Worries were set aside,
Sometimes we could speak volumes
without saying a word,
I'm sorry
for conversations
that now we seem to lose,
Could it be
that yesterday
kind of slipped away
without us paying attention
and here we are wondering why?

April 28, 2001
