title: "A Seaside Image"

Like the white caps of an overflowing ocean
and the sound of the pounding surf,
Images of seagulls cawing,
Looking for a fish,
The horizon of a boat kept in the distance
just beyond the rising sun,
Reach into the blue of the ocean itself
just past the glimpse of a shark
reflected by the reef,
And feel the heartbeat of a million years
into an image of one.

Oct. 17, 1996
