title: "And In The Dreaming A Changeling Smiles"

It matters not
that a soul dwells
where paradise arrives within a legacy,
There in the breath of life
rises a heart that touches
in the mists of dreams,
And in the wisdom
strength fill the heart
within the veils of time,
Hear the whispers
of spirits without a care,
Feel the silence
solemn in the shadows that rise & fall,
And in the dreaming
a changeling smiles
like the child with reaches within.

June 26, 1999
