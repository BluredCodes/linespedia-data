She walked into my life
like a melody found in the sunrise,
We found both by surprise
songs that touched us,
But being together
was the best music,
Hearts in love
complete a circle in time
where the warmth
frees one's soul,
Listen to those needs
that brings the comfort
that we all need,
Random memories
found her shadow,
Laughter satisfies,
Yet her smile
brings to me what I need,
And that's to put
an end to my loneliness.

Sept. 29, 2000
