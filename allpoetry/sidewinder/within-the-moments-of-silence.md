Through the moments of silence
I glanced into your eyes,
What did I find?
A tenderness
that is only found with me,
What is shared by my heart
for you,

Through these moments of silence
where only unspoken words are heard,
We merge into a limitless depth of reality,
Touches
for the first time,
Viewed by two hearts
as pure
as a new birth,

And if by chance
I find a pureness
that is met only
in the warmth of your soul,
Could we?
Would we?

March 17, 2000
