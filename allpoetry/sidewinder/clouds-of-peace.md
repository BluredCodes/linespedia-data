title: "Clouds Of Peace"

Illusionary dreams
shadowed in a compulsory
image varied
westward
in the morning,
To touch that
secret echo
in a passion,
Clouds of peace
enter
and in the horizon 
a wolf howls in a secret song.

July 2, 2000
