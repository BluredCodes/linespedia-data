Sweet serenades,
An invitation to the realm of the faerie,
A rise in exaltation,
A journey of healing
in touch with their purification,
A rebirth of a gateway
dimensional of time & space,
Sacred to the heart,
A message in prophecy
to a world where
discovery is glimpsed with new eyes,
A symbol prime in its fullness,
With every footstep
the feel of her wisdom
beckons a new day,
Whisper of magic
await the dawn of a new age,
And you can see by her smile,
the lore of her truth,
And another mystery is revealed.

May 13, 1997
