Whispers rising
celebrated in a full moon,
Hear the call of the night?
Shivers touching...
Howling...
Can you feel the fear?
Shadows mire,
Rage met in thy eye,
Feel your blood raising,
Excitement touches a heart,
Terror meets those secrets
some dread,
Mystery surrounds those evocations 
calling the dead,
Strange things happen,
People disappear,
Forbidden circles,
Touch upon the ancient,
Warnings complete the arcane,
Enchantment fill the air,
Listen to thy awakening!
Crimson runes edged only in time,
And the reaper smiles awaiting,
Arms Open wide.

July 26, 2000
