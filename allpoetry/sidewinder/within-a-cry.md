---
title: "Within A Cry"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "within-a-cry"

She's lost in a mirror
	full of reflections, images and promises
	inside herself,
	She doesn't know where she's going,
	But it doesn't matter,
	She's just looking for herself,
	A face stares back at her,
	But it's not one that she knows,
	It's full of emptiness,
	Sorrow and woes,
	Sheltered only in a promise of time,
	Where is it that
	a spirit reveals
	more than what some seek?
	Those immortal time varied
	only by a promise of an echo,
	Yet...
	in those memories
	shadows rage in a crescendo
	and she hears those distant voices,
	Only to find
	a miracle made immortal
	within a cry.
	Oct. 13, 2000
