---
title: "Suffocating Jelly"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "suffocating-jelly"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36112-Suffocating-Jelly-by-sidewinder"

In a jar I find my life
preserved by the things that
give me strife,
The more I hate,
The more I stay,
The less I want to move,
I can't suppress these lies that I hold
and to the knowledge of the jelly,
In the jar of the space and stars
dying to know of this everyday,
To touch the flavor
in the tome of life
celebrated
by the bleeding
of nature's best,
Wading deeper,
Finding comfort in the warm sticky embrace,
Seduced by my sickness,
Wanting it in my belly,
No one cares if I
suffocate in jelly!
Oh what a life,
I live it all
and love it well,
But jelly rules
in heaven and hell.

June 30, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn, Adrennia Cooper, Allan Perry, Bill Swan & William Randol
