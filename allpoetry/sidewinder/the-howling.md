---
title: "The Howling"
writer: "sidewinder"

That howl
risen in harmony
to touch that freedom 
that some never see,
I too...
can sing,
Shall we be together as one
and touch our completeness!
And with the wind at our side
that moment will forever stand,
It matters not
that some get lost in the shadow,
I choose...
that winding path that brings us together,
And in our circle
my tears touch your heart
stolen in the mountains
which you reign.

Feb. 23, 2001
