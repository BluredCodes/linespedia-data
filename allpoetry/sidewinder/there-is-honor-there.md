---
title: "There Is Honor There"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "there-is-honor-there"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26756-There-Is-Honor-There-by-sidewinder"

Within that sacred fire
that touches within the soul
arriving moment by moment
wherein a miracle touches
like a whisper in the wind
and touches the heart
only to be free,
Look..
            Can you see the stars in the sky?
Kindle those dreams
where the wolf
hunts with you
and accepts you as his brother,
There is honor there,
Let's touch the dawn,
Let's reach that need
held only by the hunt,
Those sacred memories
forever kept
within the heart.

Dec. 21, 2001
