---
title: "It Seems Lost My Friend"
writer: "sidewinder"

It seems lost my friend
at least...
to those who read not poetry,
Sheltered only with reality,
Where is their imagination?
Forgotten...
on some long dusty shelf?
If only...
some could open their minds,
But mine...
is not to judge,
Verily...
it's these times where
some find solitude in their rap or alternative styles,
But me...
I prefer my writing...
To each their own,
Some need to reach and
find their own muse...
and in finding it
maybe their world will just begin.

Feb. 28, 2000
