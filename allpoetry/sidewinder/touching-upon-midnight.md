title: "Touching Upon Midnight"

Touching upon midnight
reaching a miracle in time,
A smile on that face
promises more
than a heart can reveal,
To touch upon a rose
completing that soul
only serenaded by a song,
I know....
you can touch upon love,
Because....
I can see it in your eyes,
Come....
walk with me in my garden,
Where in finding romance
is only a heartbeat away,
We'll soar
with the eagles
shining in the moonlight,
And in that horizon
we'll find a miracle for two.

Jan. 5, 2002
