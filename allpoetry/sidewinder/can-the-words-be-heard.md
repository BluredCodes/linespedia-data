---
title: "Can The Words Be Heard?"
writer: "sidewinder"

Feeling the silence
of an ever changing world,
Reaching out in hope,
Dreaming of a simple memory,
Can the words be heard
from a heart simply crying?
Or is it too much to ask?
Afraid of the reality
of an over complicated time
lost in the moment,
Trust is only blind,
Reach out and grab forever
for tomorrow is held only by a memory.

May 4, 1996
