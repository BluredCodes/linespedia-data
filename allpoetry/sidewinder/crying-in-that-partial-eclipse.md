title: "Crying In That Partial Eclipse"
writer: "sidewinder"

Serrated in that blind eye
where one see's one thing,
And another...
within a different point of view,
How is it that we are not finding an answer?
Only finding a silence
that somehow shouts,
Insisting
that we must remain,
defined in that partial eclipse,
Yet,
hidden here within
an impoverished spirit,
An echo drifts,
Releasing silence into obscurity,
Perhaps a glimpse of hope
or more tears to come,
Whatever happened to peace
in the myriad of chaos?
