title: "Came A Stranger"

Through the darkness of the night
came a stranger
looking for conversation
hands held open wide,
Eyes wanting friendship,
Never with a care in the world,
Opening up a piece of a soul,
Offerings a good shoulder to cry upon
if ever the need arises,
Certain to give as well as to take,
Traversing life in its bumps and grinds
on the road
just to see what's on the other side,
And living moment to moment
held in a breath of a lifetime.

Sept. 20, 2000
