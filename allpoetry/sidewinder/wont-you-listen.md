---
title: "Won't You Listen?"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "wont-you-listen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37921-Wont-You-Listen--by-sidewinder"
---

I'm just a country boy,
But I'd love you until time ends,
I know there are others by your side,
But I'm the one that would care,
I'd hold you
when others would hurt you,
They don't care about you,
Won't you listen?
You need someone that would look after you,
That's what I'm trying to do,
I know.... 
                      I know....
You're a big girl,
You can take care of yourself,
But you need someone to love you,
And I not only want to love you,
I do love you,
Can't you see?
Listen to me please?
I'm here,
If you need me.

April 23, 1982
