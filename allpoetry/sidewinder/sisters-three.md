Those sisters three
	who touch in mysteries,
	Some for the past,
	Some for the present,
	Some for the future,
	One for the maiden
	who touches the heart of many,
	One for the mother
	who begets mankind,
	One for the crone
	who understands wisdom
	yet motivates those who need her,
	Some call them fate,
	yet in the mystery of time
	an offering is made
	to those who are willing to understand.
	July 17, 2000
