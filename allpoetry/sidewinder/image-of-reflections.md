Revelations twisted in an immersion
of pleasure
complicated by time itself,
Calculated from moment to moment
declared in an image of reflections,
Perish the thought of free thinking,
For fantasy
independently shares an unanimous emotion
which forever holds the child in each of us,
Sometimes though reality
clouds the issue
and the dreams we hold
graciously takes a back seat,
Still...
the journey's we make
result in a conscience effort
of a promise
shared by everyone.

Sept. 20. 1996
