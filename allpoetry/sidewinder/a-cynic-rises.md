title: "A Cynic Rises"

Hail, hail
	great goddess,
	Forgive me for trespassing against
	those who have hurt
	my heart,
	Two wrongs do not make a right,
	Still...
	I reserve the right to
	reclaim my soul,
	Perhaps in the future
	a forgiveness
	can be celebrated,
	But for now
	a betrayal is felt,
	And truth has been forgotten?
	Why?
	What lesson has been founded?
	Shadows rise
	past the first moon
	and the cynic rises within me.
	Sept. 12, 1997
