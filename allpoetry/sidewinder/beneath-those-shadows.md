title: "Beneath Those Shadows"

To touch thine own heart
	in a remembrance of time,
	Beneath those echoes
	which rage endlessly,
	Unwarranted in the silence
	inadequate to those who
	do not listen,
	Sonnets touch the blindness
	for those who are unwilling,
	yet the poet
	reveres the present of the poems
	and the magician...
	awakens those changes
	meant to balance
	a beginning to life,
	A self-transformation into
	passages which nourish the child within.
	Oct. 8, 1999
	By: B.E. Whitehorn & Christina M. Schultz
