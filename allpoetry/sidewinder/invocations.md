---
title: "Invocations"
writer: "sidewinder"

Unfold your night...
wings wide
and treasure the visions of the dreamer,
Under sky and under moon,
Thoughts of nightstalking make my heart swoon,
Feel it invocation,
That certain call,
Revealing the magic
that touches us all,
When the wind
whispers its song
in the subconscious,
Mystery that up until
now we have not
discovered yet,
Should you understand
the voices in the wind,
Listen carefully lest you miss
the secrets of the past,
Find that echo celebrated only in time,
Held through those dreams
completed beyond all imagination,
And the illusions reach
past that whisper
held in a mind,
Varied second by second,
Say hello to my friend the ent,
Who will tell you stories of times untold,
Those songs of the heart
which complete the soul,
Dimensions reached in the astral,
Those planes which ascend to dark mountains and realms,
Red with fervent flamed undying,
Always constant,
Home to many forces known not by mortal men,
Magic from the bowels of Earth,
untamed and unyielding,
Untainted by one certain way of thinking that is ours,
Reflected through those centuries
varied in an eon,
Where simplicity touches in realms
where a horizon meets the next day.

July 20, 2000
