title: "Touch The Raven"

Touch the raven
who whispers a message
in the wind,
To meet in the crossroads
 forever learning those lessons
and dreams,
Those wings that allow you to fly
and see what you can find
in that journey
touched only by a breeze,
That magic found
in perfect understanding
wherein a breath taken in
creates that vision
revealed in a miracle of time,
Spirits risen in peace
where a heart
finds completeness
 circle after circle
silent in the night.

Dec. 21, 2001
