Earliest of my memories
was me going to the first grade,
Playing as a child does,
Learning of the simple things,
And even sampling the first on many loves,
Rhonda I believe was her name,
Thinking back I can remember
the naps that we took in the afternoons,
Also playing on the school grounds,
Many a time did I ride the merry-go-round,
Smiling like a child does,
Never with a care in the world,
Still I smile.

Sept. 9, 1984
