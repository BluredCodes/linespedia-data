---
title: "Where Spirals Rise"

Amber crystals shine
where spirals rise
and reach for the stars
in realms promises
revealed in the waters of time,
Preordained in mystery,
Sustained by those who touch
those secrets which fall
like rain,
Essence in wisdoms,
Images held by one's own mind,
Shadows fade away
like echoes touched
deep in immortality of Mother Earth,
Chronicles in perceptions of light,
Infinity smiles at those who claim to know her,
But whispers those truths hidden
in plain view,
Verily,
The feel of the wind
touches the serenity of the spirit.

Sept. 19, 1998
