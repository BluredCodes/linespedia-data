title: "You Have Inspired Me"

You have inspired me,
You've touched me with your grace,
And the wisdom that has touched us all,
Your words have brought tears to my eyes
and solace to my heart,
                           You speak in rhythms
                                                so eloquently,
           I try at times to follow you
                                        in your journey....
         but somehow...
                         it does not compare,
Nor should it,
                  For each that has a journey in words
has his or her own,
                            Sometimes followed by a surprise
that twists and turns its own way,
                          You once said that your poems
were letters to the world,
                          And we as the world
appreciate it!
                For how can we not
                                      appreciate the romance
that you've touched?
                        I for one...
                                    have loved...
                                                 and been loved,
Still...
             a journey remains,
                                 Keep touching me
with your words
              and those dreams
                               revealed in the heart of you.
