---
title: "Dolphin's Song"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "dolphins-song"
source: "AllPoetry"

Whispers that touch a heart,
Prayers met in echoes
passed on in the sea,
Songs and tales forever
met by sailors
in things that they've seen,
Mysteries to some
common only to dolphins
who travel their own creative
language,
Passing from generation to generation
those civilizations that have come and gone
forever throughout history,
Serenade to a mermaid
in the seven seas,
Those poems of ancient Gods
alive in a forgotten rhythm,
Touched by life itself,
Only forever reached and met
by constellations of the stars themselves.

Nov. 16, 2000
