In the distance I hear the hounds of the hunt,
Who is the legend of the day?
What hero glitters in the eyes of the Lady?
A favor that we all sought to be,
Let's journey in the realm of magic
and seek adventure that the bards
will sing in song,
A tale never forgotten,
Rich in magic that holds joy
precious to a heart
that celebrates all of faerie kind
ancient in its promises,
A symbol sacred to a gateway
experienced in rebirth to love, peace and harmony,
Forever may it reign.

June 21, 1997
