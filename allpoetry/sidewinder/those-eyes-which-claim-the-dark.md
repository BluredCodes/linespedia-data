title: "Those Eyes Which Claim The Dark"

Insignia's touch
withered through time
reaching a war fevered pitch,
Hear the war cries!
Twisted in cruelty,
Masked only...
in the pain,
Our rightful claim....
is to rule the world!
Power succeeds,
Power strengthens the strong,
Manipulations rise
where cunning is revealed,
Shadows touch the path
of deception,
Mysteries complete
those eyes which claim the dark.

March 27, 2000
