A moment of magic
is touched by the brightness of your eyes,
Celebrated in kindness,
Fulfilled in a simple generosity,
Held by a secret
that you only know,
In the twinkling of an eye
came the reflection of your smile
touched in the joy of a heart,
A warrior in shining armor
came to take your hand
sacred in chivalry,
Victory is in the grasp of romance
and forever after's
came in a moment of time,
A moon touches on a new beginning,
And the will of heaven
foresees what the future will bring.

Nov. 7, 1997This was written for my friend...
Christina Schultz.
May she always be my friend !
