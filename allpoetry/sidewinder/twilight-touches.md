---
title: "Twilight Touches"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "twilight-touches"

Twilight touches
	in those echoes
	revealed
	wherein the horizon
	serenades
	and in the reflection
	I can see a smile
	only met in laughter,
	there was once a story
	that reminds me of you
	of a faerie so fair,
	And filled with thoughts of morning dew,
	There is no telling where she flew,
	or if she'll be back
	to renew the hope that I lack,
	When I see that we'll never
	be together alone under the stars,
	Our love so close,
	I can hear your voice,
	It's almost like a whisper in the wind,
	Yet the moments that you're gone,
	I miss the touch of your heartbeat,
	A completion of two souls
	held in the horizon,
	When together in this
	faerie gaze I melt,
	But with you in my heart
	is felt more full than the warmest summer rain,
	My soul is filled with
	endless gratitude just to be with you
	again,
	So let's meet together,
	Share this precious time
	hour by hour,
	Watch the sun rise
	early in the morning
	and find a way to bright
	our heart together,
	A poets heart is filled
	with happy endings of what could be,
	But I know you'll never love me that way,
	So I write the words and tell
	stories that I can't say,
	On this paper I have your favor and live in your glory,
	But it ends up just a fantasy story,
	So somehow I'll have to to be satisfied
	with only your company
	and enjoy life moment by moment,
	Touch those rainbows
	and share the new dawn.
	Feb. 14, 2001
	By: B.E. Whitehorn & Allan Perry
