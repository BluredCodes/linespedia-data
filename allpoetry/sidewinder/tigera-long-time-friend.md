A long time ago
I had a friend named Tiger,
A crossbred cat who cared for me
like no other,
He wasn't small by any means,
Tho when he first came into my life
a little redheaded girl brought him,
So tiny then
that he'd fit in the palm of my hand,
In a year he grew
to lay clean across the back of my neck,
Where he loved to lay,
Kitty, kitty, kitty!
I'd call,
Meow !
As if to say:
What do you want now?
then we'd both keep each other company
on the back porch
watching the stars late at night,
These days I miss him,
Long gone from my life,
He tarried too far from home
wondering in his ways,
Too close to the highway
and that day it got him,
A friend that I had worked with
had knocked on my door
and told me that my friend was gone,
Tears still roll on my face
when I think of that day,
Yes...
I miss you my friend,
maybe somewhere you're hunting
in that big blue sky,
Good hunting!

Jan. 30, 2001
