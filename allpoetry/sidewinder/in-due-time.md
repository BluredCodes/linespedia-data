In due time
forever casted in that moment,
Somehow held...
for the ripples of my mind
flowed with the shadows,
As I reached out
only for a second
for a grip of insanity
engulfed me,
Forever I'll live in my fantasies,
Hoping for a glimpse of
the shimmering corner
edged deep within my mind,
A glistening angel
speaking as my guide,
Telling me there is hope.

March 29, 1996
