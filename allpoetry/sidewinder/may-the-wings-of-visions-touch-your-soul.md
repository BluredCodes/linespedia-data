title: "May The Wings Of Visions Touch Your Soul"

May the wings of visions
touch your soul
revealed by the wisdom of your heart,
Touch those dreams
and find  a freedom
that soars,
Honor is found
in your vision quest
where the secret is told
by a lynx,
And a message is revealed by a raven,
And that joy
celebrated by the wings of the hummingbird,
Maybe a wolf
could be teacher
and the shaman of your soul.

Dec. 21, 2001
