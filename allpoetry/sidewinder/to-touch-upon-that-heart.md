title: "To Touch Upon That Heart"
writer: "sidewinder"

To touch upon that heart
wherein a miracle happens
and find in eyes
that glow
or a smile revealed in passion
bedazzled of that one friend,
Know that I am he,
Too much in this world is loneliness,
Here is my hand,
Take it!
Now that when you need me,
I am here,
If you just look...
We'll find those seasons in the sun,
And the shadows
will no longer find a place
in our hearts or our dreams,
We'll find in the path
those crossroads that touch
and forever keep us whole.
