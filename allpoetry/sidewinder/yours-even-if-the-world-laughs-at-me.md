title: "Yours Even If The World Laughs At Me"

It matters not
what the world thinks,
That is...
with you by my side,
For the comfort I need
is held in your eyes,
I need not ask the whys,
For you hold every answer,
So when I'm tired, hungry & cold,
I look to you,
And in your never ending wisdom
you fill my every need.

March 20, 1985
