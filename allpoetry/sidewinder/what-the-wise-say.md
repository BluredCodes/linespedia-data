---
title: "What The Wise Say"

Wise ones say...
that the joy in life
is what you make of it,
For the comfort in heart
promises new adventures
forever completes in mysteries,
For life has its dangers & uncertainties,
But in all there is
order & balance,
New moons rise to complete the heavens
but the fire in the night
is recognized by the mother of memory,
Those voices in the sun
explain ancient experiences
that remind us of our own past
sometimes held in the silence
of a forgotten echo.

Feb 1, 1998
