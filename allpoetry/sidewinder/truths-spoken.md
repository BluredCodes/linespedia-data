Behold!
Listen to my words,
For I reveal mysteries
that are unanswered,
Touch upon truths spoken by the voice of the dragon,
Open the doorways of your imagination, 
Teach ways that are lost to those without
an open mind,
It is this vision
ancient in sight
that touches an open path
reawakened in wisdom,
Listen to you inner voice,
Touch upon the magic which lies in your very own heart,
Heal those wounds that scar your soul,
It's in the crossroads of life
that the journey begins,
What will you find?
The possibilities are endless,
But the choice is up to you.
