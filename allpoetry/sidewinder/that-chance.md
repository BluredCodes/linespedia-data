Here I am,
Feeling alone in an overcrowded room
wanting to be the center of attention,
But not knowing how...
In a sense
feeling afraid
of those who might judge...
But still...
willing to take a risk
just to meet someone new,
Where are you?
Will you meet my eyes?
Will you tell me your life story?
Given the chance
I'll make a new friend,
Well...
Here I am,
Will you take that chance?

April 23, 1996
