The insight
that he struggles to find
reveals a mirror
to his soul
only...
What will he find?
That truth
that some refuse
raging in thy eyes,
                      Look!...
                                Look!
But in the blindness
revelations came to pass
like leaves in the wind,
Moments in repose
                  reveal in shadows
                                    collections of the soul,
Difficult as it seems,
                       Yet vulnerable
to those who listen in wisdom,
Elders to those introspectives
in a final vision,
                                Yet...
                                         Who can say?

Nov. 10, 2000
