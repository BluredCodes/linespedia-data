title: "As I See Myself"

As I look through a mirror,
I see myself,
What do I see?
A man
who tries to think,
And as I think,
I try to listen,
To what?
Whatever I can,
And I try to put it use,
That is in my own life,
Though some people
don't have the same opinion about me,
I really try,
Can anyone else do more?

March 21, 1982
