Trumpets blowing,
                    The harmony has begun,
And in my mind I can see the melodies flowing,
A note here,
                                               A note there,
In the background you can hear
                                          the base flowing strong,
Rhythm by rhythm
                              you can feel the base,
All around you
                                there's music touching,
All the singers have found the sound,
It's a miracle
                                  forever taken place,
Some listen for the symphonies,
                                 Others blues will do,
Some dance to that swing music,
I myself...
                      like it all,
For in my heart
                         there's rhythm in my soul.

Jan. 15, 2002
