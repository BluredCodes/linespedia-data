title: "Surest In Thy Vision"

Surest in thy vision
                       completed in heart and mind,
Purest of thy reason
                         to see beyond the soul,
Listen past that miracle
                          crystal clear
                                        a voice
                                                which teaches wisdom
heralded in a minute of time,
                               Warmth felt
heartbeat by heartbeat,
                          Do you feel thy need?
Secrets found
                     little by little
reaching in a dream,
                                       Sunsets
in that circle of light,
                                   Horizons met
                                                 only in
freedom,
                       Somehow I find
that whisper
                      calling...
just beyond the horizon.

Jan. 10, 2002
