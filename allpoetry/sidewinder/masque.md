Behind that facade that you put on
is a real person,
Someone that one can relate to,
Dreams are fine,
But to find them,
You must look inside yourself
and find the real you,
The whole you,
The one and only....
you,
What has one to prove?
It's this,
Each has a special purpose in life,
And to find it
each reaches inside a heart,
Creating motion
that defines a uniqueness
that only you can find.

May 2, 1985
