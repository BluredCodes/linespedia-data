---
title: "What I Am"
writer: "sidewinder"

When someone looks at me,
They see a boy waiting for a dream,
But what they don't know
is that it's more than a dream,
It's something I see,
I feel it,
I hear it,
I know it,
I envision it,
I believe in it,
And someday I'll make it a reality,
For it's here
in my writing,
And it'll be here forever in print,
Because here are my dreams,
My emotions,
My knowledge,
It's me,
Read it,
Read between the lines,
Then you'll know me,
Here lies before you 
what I have in my heart,
Do with it as you will.

May 13, 1982
