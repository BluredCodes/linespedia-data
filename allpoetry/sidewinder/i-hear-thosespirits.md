I hear those
	                spirits
	touching my mind,
	                          Some say hello,
	   Some want to just visit,
	                             Some like to haunt,
	Find the fear within,
	                                              Some just want a friend
	to bind the loneliness,
	                           Some want revenge...
	                                                 for the pain
	                    that others caused,
	Some like playing practical jokes,
	                                     No harm intended,
	Some sing a song,
	                       They want you to join in,
	Some howl in glee,
	                          Aren't they a sight !
	Some like the dark,
	                           They howl in the night,
	Some look for a love
	                          they're lost in an accident,
	       Some look for a release
	                               from the madness
	                                               and the neitherworld,
	Some are just stuck,
	                        Going where they can
	                                              and they cry...
	For their torment
	                        is everlasting.
	
	Jan. 17, 2002
