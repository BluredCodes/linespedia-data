title: "Revealed Within The Soul"

From the spirit of the dawn
a numbness touches those
who are revealed within the soul,
A mask in time forever celebrated
in magic,
Metaphors risen in poetry,
Images touched in glamour,
Wherein a heart
finds some meaning
and within a consciousness,
Indomitable to those
who never say die,
Sympathetic to a sacred discovery,
Insight in revelations
wherein you find the oracle within.

Nov. 24, 1999
