---
title: "Cherish"
writer: "sidewinder"

source: "AllPoetry"

Unto thy self
a renegade of hearts
notorious in a celebration of dreams
forever creating chaos,
Mixed emotions,
Blind passion,
But building a bond,
trust following time, perhaps a romance
and a new dawn
rewriting forevermore
surrendered in innocence
cherished by a caress 
magical in its design.

July 11, 1997
