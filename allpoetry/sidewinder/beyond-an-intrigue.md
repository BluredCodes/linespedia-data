---
title: "Beyond An Intrigue"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "beyond-an-intrigue"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31348-Beyond-An-Intrigue-by-sidewinder"

Held within a rainbow
is a smile
that is revealed in your eyes,
And if my existence
touches your soul,
Then time must hold
those special places
that remain secret
where a whisper
touches only beyond an intrigue.

Nov. 8, 1999
