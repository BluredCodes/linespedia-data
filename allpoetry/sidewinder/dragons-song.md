---
title: "Dragon's Song"
writer: "sidewinder"

Shelter my heart
	with dreams that reach beyond
	imagination,
	realms are opened and touch the inner rhythms,
	A pyramid is revealed within my mind,
	Magic is echoed the voice of Earth herself,
	A pattern of a simple state of consciousness,
	Shadows hide in memories
	raged from a past
	which a dragon's legend
	rises in flames
	sacred in honor,
	Shall my heart feel it's beat
	within a victory holds more
	than what can be seen on this plane?
	For spirits do rise
	and dance in its circle of power,
	A celebration of a time never forgotten,
	A code of chivalry sings proudly,
	Remember me!!!
	June 1, 1997
