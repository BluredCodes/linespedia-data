title: "A Walk With The Wolf"

Song of the wolf
howling with nature
fulfilling the heart,
Prime in the tale
of expectations
mired in the fullness of time,
Where is the hunt
Hear the chill of your soul
Solitudes serenading a completeness,
Holy Mother
remember me,
Touch the magic within me,
Reveal the wisdom of your teachings
crystal clear in its meaning,
Elders in time
come and go
disappearing from an age
wherein there is
a walk of inspiration.

March 6, 1998
