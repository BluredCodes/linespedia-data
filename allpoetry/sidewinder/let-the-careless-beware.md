title: "Let The Careless Beware"

Colorful memories rising from a heart
grasping from time,
A touch of melancholy,
Emotions driving one blind,
Rich in fantasies from a virgin's soul,
A panther on the prowl,
Engrossed by a blood hunt,
Tracking a prey
and biding his time
for the foolhardy to walk by,
Let the careless beware,
But karma has a way of evening things out,
Never let trust lead one to a never ending road,
For it has a way of catching one
when least expecting it.

June 5, 1996
