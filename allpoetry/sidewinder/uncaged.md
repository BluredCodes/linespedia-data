title: "Uncaged"

Faces
in the edge of shadow,
Uncaged,
Free to roam where a mind
is not bound to reality,
An imagination is the key
to a self made promise
that rules a journey
to a universe held by fantasy,
An accessory to a never ending story
purchased by the blood of those who hold
myths and stories to be the meat and lifeblood
of the creative and the heart of a dream
celebrated by life itself.

July 13, 1996
