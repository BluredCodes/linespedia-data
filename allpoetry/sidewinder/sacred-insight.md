---
title: "Sacred Insight"
writer: "sidewinder"

Fire bright
in the night
and stars whispered
those secrets spirits reveal
only in the ghostdance,
Winds howl in desperation
meant for those who understand nature's call,
Languages touched in rhythms
made by our own bodies,
Fevered whirlwinds completed in a pitch of time,
Beneath the sky
for all to see,
And for any who want to participate,
Visions celebrated in a hunger
within our souls,
Sacred within our own insight.

Sept. 16, 2000
