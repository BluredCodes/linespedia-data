title: "In This A Judgment Stands"

A jester
laughing within the times
meanwhile 
a silence touches
those places
revealed only in the shadows,

Darkness and light
fulfilled only in a completeness
within a soul
and I weep
for those who have been forgotten,

Can I help it
if a mirror reflects
the truth
beheld by the world?
In this a judgment stands.
