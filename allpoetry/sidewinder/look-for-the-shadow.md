---
title: "Look For The Shadow"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "look-for-the-shadow"
source: "AllPoetry"

There are those reflections in time
where things seem to be a mystery,
Where thoughts occur and yet
mysteries remain in the mind,
Emotions occur,
And thoughts remain,
But still puzzles remain in my dreams,
In my eyes
and in my heart,
I only wish that we could see...
Eye to eye,
Heart to heart,
And emotions to emotions,
Souls touch and remain forever,
Wherein magic immerses
into a promise,
Promises arise and fall,
But the touch that is
given remains forever,
Only true magic
can become love and light,
And it's that light
which makes one complete,
Look for the shadow.

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Christina M. Schultz
