Though the moments of silence
I glanced into your eyes,
What did I find?
A tenderness that only you can show,
Seasons touch sometimes in spring,
Where the truth of emotions reveal more that what we see,
But in the romance of it all,
I still can find your warmth,
Let's touch within those embraces
composed in rhythms of out heartbeat,
What we'll find who knows.
But we'll never know until we try,
What do you say?
Could it be...
that your silence
signifies a welcome in open arms?
Smile for me in innocence
and capture my soul,
Whisper a secret to me
that only we can know.

Oct. 18, 2000
