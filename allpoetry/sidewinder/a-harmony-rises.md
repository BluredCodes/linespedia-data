---
title: "A Harmony Rises"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-harmony-rises"

Within its own evolution
a harmony rises
to touch a soul,
Yet those jealous faces
complete the story,
The chapter was written
long before the need
rose to the surface,
Single notes
edging their way in
to the music,
Smooth in those rhythms
syncopated in those beats,
 Somehow it touches your soul,
Close your eyes
and feel it,

It's the music that gives you chills
when you hear it
for the first time,
It's the tune that plays
inside your head
even when the record...
stops,
telepathic in its ever changing echo
reflective within its own serenity,
Flowing gracefully in its own time,
Connected to a spirit held blind,
Nourished carefully in a gathering of that melody
felt sublime,

Thought reaching out
rippling, swaying,
Stirring the heart, 
Touching that which
is found,
Only when it needs to,
raging in its completeness,
held like a prism
multifaceted, 
shared in those colors
one by one,
as if a note could
reveal that passion,
Prime...
only found in a miracle.

Dec. 18, 2001
