Though sometimes misunderstood and
sheltered,
Moods run like visions,
Beautiful
from a rainbow's point of view,
Sparkling from every color imaginable,
Held only from a moment,
Transparent
only a pleasure in someone else's mind,
A kaleidoscope
changing with every turn,
Yet a revelation,
Something brand new,
For each...
it's something different,
The sensation
shared in a moment's return
composed by a brief
eloquence,
Brilliant and rich
almost prophetical
in a wonder of life.

Oct. 7, 1996
