A flickering candle
within the shadows
the ritual has begun
in perfect love and perfect trust,
Arms outstretched,
Letting the power flow,
Taking in all that is good,
Courage is celebrated,
A change to touch within,
Images from a sheltered past
and the future revives its magic,
A spark within the spirit,
Those white wings
which rise in the wind
created by a circle
open to the starlight
held in perfect freedom
availing the mystery
discovered by the cauldron of life.

Dec. 1, 1997
