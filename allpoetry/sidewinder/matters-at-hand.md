They say that the meek inherit the earth
But occasionally you have to speak up
or the silence overwhelms
forever caught in the quiet
indivisible by the shadow and the sun
mired sometimes by injustice,
Principles touch in ways
some forget
but in the memory
hope does survive,
Just remember
those matters at hand.

May 6, 1999
