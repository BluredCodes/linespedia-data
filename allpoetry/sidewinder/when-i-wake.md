Soft Sunshine
creeps through my window
& the sleep leaves my face,
waking me up,
Good morning!
How are things today?
Promising?
             Well.. 
                    Good!
The smell of breakfast enters the room
which makes me in a hurry,
Can't wait until I taste those hotcakes!
Another day begins!

April 7, 1985
