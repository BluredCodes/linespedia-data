---
title: "Tangled In Those Tears"
writer: "sidewinder"

Tangled on those traces
silent but clear,
Those sweet persistent
tears
felt by the warmth
 of your smile,
A hunger met
in the twinkle of your eyes,
A flutter in the sweetness
of your kiss,
Slowly you meet my heartbeat,
And we find
those dreams
celebrated
in the fullness
of time.

Dec. 18, 2001
