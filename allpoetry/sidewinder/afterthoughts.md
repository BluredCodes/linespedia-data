As I look around me
I see the clouds before me,
appearing to grey into the blue of the mountains,
I feel the chill of the winds 
as fall come into winter,
I see the green of the pinion pines,
Next to me I feel the heartbeat of my cat
laying in my lap,
I hear the bird calling to his mate,
And the river rushing down below
I see snow capping all the mountains around me,
Winter is not yet here,
but it will come soon,
I can feel it,
 Another year passes on.

Oct. 1, 1986
