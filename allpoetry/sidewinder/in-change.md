---
title: "In Change"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "in-change"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

To touch with thy vision,
Those sights that one has a longing to see
wondrous in the times
invoked by an ageless wisdom & power,
Come,
those in search of initiation 
into the ancient mysteries
to a consciousness within thyself,
Sacred in the dreams,
 Guided in a journey
that heals one
of past wrongs,
celebrated by a joy
that one finds
in change.

Oct. 20, 1998
