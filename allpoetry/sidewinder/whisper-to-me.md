---
title: "Whisper To Me"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "whisper-to-me"

Those songs of passion
reminds me of you,
Like the brightness of a summer day
in your smile,
Touched only on an echo
wherein we meet,
And in our beginning
the necessities of life
is more rich,
For we touch in ways
some cannot imagine,
Could it be
that we see things
in each others eyes?
And in that revelation
the poetry that touches our hearts
reveals more
than what some find
in a lifetime,
Whisper to me
those secrets that you yearn to tell
and I'll find 
a miracle within a soul.

April 22, 2000
