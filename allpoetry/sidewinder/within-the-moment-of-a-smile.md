title: "Within The Moment Of A Smile"

The moment I saw you,
I knew it was true,
The love that we would share held
a fulfillment in time,
Like a rose blossomed,
Wherein a memory touches,
 Keeping those special promises
and within those rare moments
I see within my mind
your special smile,
And I knew
that life touches
in echoes
complete within itself.

Sept. 16, 1999
