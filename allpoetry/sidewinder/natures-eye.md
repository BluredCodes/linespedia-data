---
title: "Nature's Eye"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "natures-eye"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My eyes tired from the night before,
Too little sleep
and yet the day
had arrived,
I'm setting at a coffee shop
pondering my thoughts...
Where do I go from here?
In the distance I hear a voice singing, 
The words are not too clear,
But I like the melody,
Right in front of me
I see a giant photograph,
With a woman crossing the river
on an old suspended bridge,
Beautiful river rolling through the mountains,
Lush green countryside,
Opposite of the room
there's another giant photograph,
In it there's a man rock climbing
freehand style,
Right beside a waterfall,
Of again beautiful country scenes!
And right to the side of it
there's one more giant photograph,
This one is showing people climbing
sandstone formations,
I believe that was
taken out of Moab,Utah
in Monument National park,
Whoever took these pictures
has an eye for scenery,
Well taken
with Nature's eye
in her own art.

Feb. 16, 2002
