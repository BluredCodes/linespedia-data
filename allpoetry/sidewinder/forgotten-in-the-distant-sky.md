Bang!
                   Bang!
Another body falls,
 Ground red from the impending battle,
And if you listen carefully,
You might hear...
the laughter
of those spirits that remain,
What tales can be learn
from those whispers
raging against the sky?
I don't know....
and probably never will,
But a chill
                 runs down my neck
                                   of the thought 
                                                  of those cries
forgotten...
              in the distant sky.

June 28, 2000
