A child cries
within the spirit
touching that song
varied within time
only to touch
my child within,
Souls completed
With those worlds between
a reflection
met beyond the silence
risen only in the wisdom,
Listen....
                 and reach in harmony
within a melody
                           sung in ancient times
a philosophy touched
                          only in thy completeness.

June 15, 2001
