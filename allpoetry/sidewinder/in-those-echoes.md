---
title: "In Those Echoes"

With the winds prevailing
open minds rise beyond most understanding,
In those veils that open
knowledge within voices that we all hear
but take for granted,
It's in those echoes that wisdom starts,
And a heartbeat rises within the night,
Listen for the voice of the wolf
in the solitude of the full moon,
Reflections revealing images that center a hallow truth,
We must remember to see not only with our eyes,
But also with the heart,
Enchantment flows within the vision
like music mysteriously,
Wild but beautiful,
A celebration after a fashion,
It's the adventure with the fellowship that counts
not the journey.

April 15, 1997
