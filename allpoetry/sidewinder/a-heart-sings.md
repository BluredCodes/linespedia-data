title: "A Heart Sings"

All it takes is a simple drop
to cause a ripple
in which the world
acquires change,
Can you feel it?
Open your mind
and let it flow,
Can you hear it?
Listen beyond the silence
and touch the unknown,

The unknown
in which oblivion lives
and sunshine gives,
Secret redemption 
where freedom sings and gives you wings to fly
in a perfect childhood
bliss never dies,

Where in a passion
a heart sings
touching those meanings
forever meant
for those who need it,
And find solitude
in those forever afters
wherein a dream
celebrates the heart
familiar
to those who touch
past a horizon.

Dec. 18, 2001
