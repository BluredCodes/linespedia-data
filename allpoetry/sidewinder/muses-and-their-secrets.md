title: "Muses And Their Secrets"

Listen...
touch those sounds around you,
feel that rhythm
touching you in the soul,
Forever in a minute,
Complete....
within that hold,
Feel the moons invocation
serenaded in the night,
Hear the beats of the drum
embracing the welcome
only a heartbeat away,
Interconnections of celebrations
of those muses
that share those secrets
whispered to Mother nature
who smiles
in imagination
and keeps our dreams alive,
Only to touch with her sisters
which reach in destiny
forever in the stars.

Nov. 16, 2000
