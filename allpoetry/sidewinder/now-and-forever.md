title: "Now And Forever"
writer: "sidewinder"

Funny....
What life can put you through,
One moment can be nothing but a memory,
And then when you least expect it,
It will give you something that will last forever,
I think that's what I have
It's just a miracle for two,

From the first time I laid eyes on you,
And you laid yours on me,
I had already seen my future and I knew,
That you would never be a memory in my mind,
For you would always be my side,
Although we will still carry the memories of our yesteryears,

Could it be
that some dreams do come true?
To have
and hold forever,
To reach inside yourself
and give all that you can 
until the completeness
completes us into one?

A bond we share,
Where not a soul could break
a wonderment between one another,
That not a person would understand,
Why are so many unfortunate
to never find love?
To never feel love?
Am I selfish for keeping all this love for ourselves?
To not share it with the world,
Even though,
you are my world,

So let's touch beyond a memory,
hear the wind whisper what we need,
Embrace now and forever
completing our joy,
And maybe by our example
the world can feel our love.

March 21, 2000
