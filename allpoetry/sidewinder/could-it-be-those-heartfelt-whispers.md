title: "Could It Be Those Heartfelt Whispers"
writer: "sidewinder"

Do you know where you're going to?
Do you have faith in destiny?
Or is life more
an open door
through which we walk in uncertainty?
Could it be that the questions inside you
are but the mirrors to realities?
Do they surprise in ways...
which you cannot comprehend?
if this is so
just remember this:
An open heart is an open hand, 
A bridge touching many lives
in ways you may never understand,
But forever met in kindness,
and leads to hope
for all of man, 
Do you hear the laughter within time?
Can you help dry the tears of the past?
Could it be those heartfelt whispers
calling us to bridge the gap?
Do you hear the voice within us all...
that says,
 "just reach out,
Please hold my hand!"
Let us walk this path of uncertainty
as brothers
and as friends,
Let us hear together the laughter in hope,
 Let us dry the tears of the past
only to be held in the memory of today.

March 10, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Valerie Jochum
