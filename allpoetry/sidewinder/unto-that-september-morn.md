title: "Unto That September Morn"
writer: "sidewinder"

Unto that September morn,
Thou finds life's spark,
Warm into an embrace of time,
Compete...
             moment by moment,
Where a heart touches
                       celebrated by life's wonder,
Those eyes
                   Found in a miracle,
Laughter heard
                      in a sweet surprise,
Can you feel it?
                      That joy found
                                        heartbeat by heartbeat,
Those whispers
                arise in the morning dew,
Fingers green with life,
                           And in the distance
another day begins    
                        to follow
that reason
                     sheltered by a promise
heralded with the wind.

Jan. 10, 2002
