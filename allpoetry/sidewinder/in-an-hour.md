title: "In An Hour"

Collections in time,
Perhaps not to your taste,
But lost to those who
yearn for recollections
seldom heard
where a discontented mind
reveres what has been said,
And yet...
in an hour
a heart,
Where cupid rises,
Touches more
than some can find in a lifetime.

Feb 9, 1999
