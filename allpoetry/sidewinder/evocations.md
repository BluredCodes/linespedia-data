Flames among the night
rising from a future
that encircles all of us
in the trials of life
invoking magic
that rises in spirals
summoned by dreams
awakened through memories
chaotic in its essence
touched by a past never forgotten
and a destiny that is yet to be reached
immersed in passions,
Defined,
Eloquent in its own design,
Competed outside of what was expected,
But yet a surprise
through mysteries hold more
 then what one can imagine.

April 4, 1997
