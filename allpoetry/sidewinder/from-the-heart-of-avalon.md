title: "From The Heart Of Avalon"

A dance of dreams
celebrated in runes
of the seasons,
An oracle of time
mediated through initiations,
An invocation of magic,
New connections conceived in time
emerged in promises,
a circle of life
touched in a rebirth
awakening the child in each of us,
A fulfillment of balance
that carries a spiritual trust,
Glimpses of the future
which centers on our own karma,
In the heart of the night,
Changes arise to touch the promise of an echo
fashioned in a white mist
arrived from The Heart Of Avalon.

Oct. 31, 1997
