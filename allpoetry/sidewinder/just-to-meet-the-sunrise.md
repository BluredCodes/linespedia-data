---
title: "Just To Meet The Sunrise"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "just-to-meet-the-sunrise"

One night with you,
To touch those dreams
varied in the night,
Pleasures relived
brings a smile on my face,
And yet...
those memories
forever meet more
than sometimes a heart can conceive,
I wonder when...
you'll walk back into my life,
To only reflect...
in our beginning,
Those passions
touch in the misty dark,
Are you ready to touch past the echo
risen in the horizon?
Because the dawn is seen in your eyes
as we touch
just to meet the sunrise.

May 26, 2000
