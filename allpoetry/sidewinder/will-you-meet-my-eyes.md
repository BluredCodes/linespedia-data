---
title: "Will You Meet My Eyes"
writer: "sidewinder"

That risk we took
sheltered by the new moon,
Those whispers we spoke,
Hushed only in that innocence,
And yes...
the hour is late,
But somehow our needs are met,
Replenished by stolen love,
Passions arise,
Shyly
within your eyes
I hear your breath,
Softly against my lips,
Intense
forever completing our love,
Will you meet my eyes
and touch a
heartbeat at a time?

Dec. 18, 2001
