title: "Listen For The Whispers"

Crystal palaces
in an aura of fire
sheltered in the trinity,
An inner trust
met by the sacred,
Magic spirals through images
raised by the circle,
Visualize pure light
and then look for a rainbow
which holds the key
that bounds love within,
Imagine a candle of blue
celebrated in peace,
Sparkling shades of green
held in the calm of the Earth,
Shadows of fear disappear,
And In the open door
listen for the whispers
that hold joy
rich in the otherworld.

Dec. 10, 1997
