title: "For Those Who Remain"

For those who remain
at a lost,
Where were the shadows
when the horizons touched the edge
of time?
And all that remains...
is somewhere
a rainbow finally opens a portal
between reality and dreams
so the soul has a road to travel
whispering sonnets to those who will listen
echoed in its own intensity,
Gifted by a destination,
Revealed within those thoughts
that are forgotten,
Yet it's in that silence
that wisdom is found.

June 16, 1999
