---
title: "This Is What I Want"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "this-is-what-i-want"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I want to touch as heart 
that's willing to meet mine,
To find that honor
found only once in a lifetime,
To travel worldwide
as a poet & find those
who are willing to listen
with an open heart,
To find simple friendship, trust
and acceptance
with those who judge not
on your looks, beliefs or position in life,
Where your dreams become a reality,
And find that happiness
within myself,
Celebrated in a miracle of time,
And in time my soul finds wisdom
where a spirit guide teaches
those values in life,
And I find that freedom
that forever keeps me free.

Feb 22, 2002
