Force,
Forging tremendous power
in a array of blinding light,
To what ends?
That is yet to be found...
in this troubled mind,
Shadows crossing over
to a reality barely touched,
Conceptions...
Contradictions...
Merely objections of obscurity,
For thy forethought
is forever seen in truth,
But guided through dreams and aspirations
of those yet to be born,
Future generations
bring on a new hope
and a new day is born.

March 27, 1996
