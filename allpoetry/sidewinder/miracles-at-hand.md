title: "Miracles At Hand"

A presence felt
in the silence,
a shuddering glimpse in the shadows
and in the cold wind
among the four corners laughter is heard
Visions from the otherworld
which brings forth wisdom and prophecy,
Journeys hold dreams
through spirits that contemplate
the truth at hand,
One with nature and the elements,
A reflection of the one,
A duel god of light & dark
seen dearly in the light,
A heart sheltered from the rain,
Energy in fulfillment
as an enlightened child,
One gifted beyond his years
and came to be remembered
by a concept of truth,
Held in awe,
But remembered by the miracles at hand.

Sept. 14, 1997
