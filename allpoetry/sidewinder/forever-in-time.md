---
title: "Forever In Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "forever-in-time"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48239-Forever-In-Time-by-sidewinder"

Freedom & trust,
 Concepts we understand & share
through the fullness of thought,
For is we give from the heart
life brings treasures & gifts
to our lives,
And in that
our dreams are forever filled
creating a special bond
that is forever remembered,
And in that kindness
a miracle shines forever in time.

April 9, 1996
