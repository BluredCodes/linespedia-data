title: "Touching That Gentleness"

Touching that gentleness
	forever met...
	in grace,
	For in the spectrum of time
	I see those eyes
	in those seasons
	where a year
	mires into the millennium
	and the echoes
	that ring
	endlessly touch the silence
	without ever knowing
	those songs that harmonize in the night.
	May 29, 2001
