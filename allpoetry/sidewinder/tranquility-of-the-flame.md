title: "Tranquility Of The Flame"

Ambrosia in the flame of heart,
An Awakening in the promise of time,
Cherished by the voice in the wind,
To touch in imagination,
Invoked by an echo
which shimmers in inspiration,
Those secrets in understanding,
A raging passion touched
in the essence of magic,
Pentacle of transformation
to change within our lives,
A balance forever held,
Reach from the center
to touch the tranquility in your life,
Sacred in its own making,
A fulfillment in the wheel of life,
Incantations visualize in the mystery,
An ancient journey
to a magical place
which resides in the heart.

Nov. 30, 1997
