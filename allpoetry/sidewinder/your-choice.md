title: "Your Choice"

Temperatures are rising,
Emotions are running high,
The passion is there,
Can't you feel it?
We want each other,
No one else,
So let's go for it,
Let's make love tonight,
Go ahead,
I'll give a signal,
And I'll hold you the whole night through,
But it's gotta be up to you,
I won't force myself,
You're too much of a lady,
So I'll leave it up you,
I'm waiting,
Do you want me to hold you?
I think you do,
But..
it's your choice.
