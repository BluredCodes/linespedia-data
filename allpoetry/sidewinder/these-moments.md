Sun shining in my eyes,
The breeze cool on the back of my neck,
At the left of me
a trio of boys trying their best to catch a fish,
As I softly listen,
I can hear the water brushing aside the back,
These moments are apart of me,
And I will never let them go.

March 13, 1985
