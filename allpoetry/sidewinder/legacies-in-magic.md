---
title: "Legacies In Magic"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "legacies-in-magic"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37227-Legacies-In-Magic-by-sidewinder"
---

Rhythm in dreams
wherein those in time
cherish a heart
to meet those
variances
which touch
note by note,
And if listened to a horizon
will tell the heart
willing to listen
of battles that
changed history
and the start of legacies,
Maybe not manifest
physical legacy,
But certainly
battles between shadow and light,
Of negativity and magic,
When finally thoughts spring forth
and a new way realized
with but a single word
spoken by one man,
woman or child that sees things as they are,
Not the way they should be,
Touched in magic,
Beyond those illusions
varied in the dance,
Mired in a song
that has risen in the night,
Forever met in a completeness,
Hour by hour,
Yet in those whispers
revelations can be met
only in your realization.

July 22, 2000
