Desperado,
Who are you?
Living your life on the run,
You stand alone
quiet and still,
Shadows of the past surrounding you,

Mysteriously
you appear to be content,
yet something in your eyes says otherwise,

What happened in your past?
You shun all emotions,
All but the hate you keep inside you,

Desperado,
Could it be
that you see
a desperateness
held only deep within your soul,

Those shadows reach deep
but the stillness of your face
walls off emotions
of what your life had done to you,

A tear drops...
regrets of past events haunting you
...in the darkness you are naked,

Silently...
you find that completeness
that you need,
reaching into a light...
Gently...
you as she
finds that new start
whispering
in the new day.

Oct. 2, 2001
