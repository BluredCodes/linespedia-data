---
title: "Truth In The Wind"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "truth-in-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hear those winds whispering
those truths
only held by a kindness....

...Strange,
These tainted walls
would return
with the worlds
and rush their 
disdain,
A long inanimate arms brushed
with the pains,
Taking youth,
These drugs
of truth
that fall from
the flag
of kingdom
come,

Listen...
to that wisdom
forever met in dreams,
Yet celebrated...
within those times
resounding in a miracle,

listen past that silence
yet an echo resonates
in a mind forever closed,
only truth
will open
to a world
too afraid
to see beyond the blindness.

May 8, 2001
