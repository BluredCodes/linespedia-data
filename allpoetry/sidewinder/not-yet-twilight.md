When it is not yet twilight
those shadows rise
among tender mysteries
arisen where only an echo meets
the silence,

The nightfall lifts my spirit
as Luna surfaces above
the trees that now glimmer
with the gift of her
dark light,

Could it be
that there is more than what meets the eye?
For in that silence
a longing touches one
in messages
promised only by what follows
a travelers heart,

Search....
And ye shall find
time through ancient mysteries,
A knowledge reawakened
in lore.

May 18, 2000

By: B.E. Whitehorn & Sherry Sheaffer
