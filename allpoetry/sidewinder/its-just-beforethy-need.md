title: "It's Just Before.....Thy Need"

To touch that guided bird
enraged in his imprisonment,
Look freedom is just a glance away!
Never to spread wings in an endless sky,
Feel the air!
Smell the flower in the nectar of spring,
Why....
                                           Oh why can't it be?
Sympathy in the need,
Complete thee in thy heart,
Hear the song,
Tears in harmony
probing to find a reason,
But there is none,
It just is...
Touching thy perch,
Calling out in thy sight,
Year by year,
Moment by moment,
Yearning to complete a soul,
Lost in the bars of judgment,
Only in the realization
of what can never be,
Still...
It's just before
                                               thy need.

May 12, 2001
