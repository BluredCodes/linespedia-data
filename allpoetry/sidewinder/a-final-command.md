---
title: "A Final Command"

Wiser in experience
touch by touch,
I feel your warmth
and by our encounters
our eyes reach into places
forgotten by most,
Somehow our souls touch
yet the silence completes us,
the moonlight reaches the dawn
and draws a mystery
forever whispering a secret,
Can you hear it?
You welcome me just by your presence
and a single afternoon
touches in a final command.

Aug. 20, 2000
