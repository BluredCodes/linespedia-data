Reflections of the shadows
of a mirror image 
held forever in a moment of time,
Dissecting it
bit by bit,
But holding on to my humanity,
Barely...
A constant battle
of good and evil,
Revolving around the why's,
Keeping tabs on my sanity,
Foolish thoughts,
But I never take them seriously,
Only in my fantasies,
For reality tightly grasps my soul,
Never letting me go.

May 2, 1996
