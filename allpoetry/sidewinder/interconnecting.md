Perceptions within my mind
interconnecting soul to soul,
freeing the hearts
which complete
a certainty arrived
within a sunrise,
sometimes those dark distances
are compatible
even with those strangers
who are open,
understanding those shadows
which hide from those who prefer the sunrise,
Yet...
finding a common kinship
within those yesteryear's,
Some may call it fate,
But I prefer calling it...
destiny.
