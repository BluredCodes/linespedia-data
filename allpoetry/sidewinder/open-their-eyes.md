Confine yourself to observing and you always
	miss the point of your own life,
	The object can be stated this way:
	Live your life the best way you can,
	Life is a game whose rules you learn
	if you leap into it and play it to the hilt,
	Otherwise, you're caught off balance,
	Continually surprised by the shifting play,
	Nonplayers often whine and complain that luck
	always passes them by,
	They refuse to see that they can create
	some on their own luck,
	      Why?
	           Because sometimes....
	                                they forget to simply
	                               open their eyes.
	
	April 4, 2001
