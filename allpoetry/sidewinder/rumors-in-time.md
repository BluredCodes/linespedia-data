---
title: "Rumors In Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "rumors-in-time"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43571-Rumors-In-Time-by-sidewinder"

Shadows fading into oblivion
glittering images
 rising into the sunset,
Remembrances of those gifts
mirrored a step at a time,
Unique in the masquerade,
Controlled by his fate, 
Held in the role,
Blind by its creation,
Sired in a fulfillment,
Myths trapped in its own being,
Reflections in dimensions,
Journals written from rages of his own past,
Connected & explored within the personae
of his own mind,
 Fortunes made & fortunes lost, 
Sheltered by a heart tempted by illusions
touched in its existence,
Illusive in a rumor of time.

Jan. 23, 1998
