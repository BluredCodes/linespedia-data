Through the circles of light
visions rise beyond what some can endure,
Shaped in the mystical realms,
Touched by magic
held in the realm of fire,
The heart of the dragon
rises to guide those who would follow
in the hour a true master of fate,
transformed in true fashion
within the wings of comfort
comes the age of magic,
Spiritually touched
by those in the realm of the Faerie,
Blessed by the Goddess
a joy is revealed
through dreams of the heart,
In this the power of love
forever creates a bond.

Oct. 13, 1997
