title: "Those Unbroken Circles"

Secret dreams in splendor,
Golden paths invoked in memories
roaming a step at a time, 
      Tranquilities arrive from journeys
hidden in brief mysteries
forever celebrating balance,
Soul names challenge those bonds
of nature prime in its fullness,
Offerings guiding forces that watch
those outcomes that we all see,
Traditions in breakthroughs unfolding
the circle of the self,
Aspects in dimensions of the spirit in the heart,
Wisdoms in the emergence of the runes,
A magic secret that consists
of discovery of the heart,
Explorations touch those influences
open in unbroken circles.

Jan. 7, 1998
