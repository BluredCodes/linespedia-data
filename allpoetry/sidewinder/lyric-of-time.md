---
title: "Lyric Of Time"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "lyric-of-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

In this vision quest
may your mind forever sparkle like a star,
Your heart remain pure as new fallen snow,
And your spirit forever sense the wonderment
of a child,
Touch those realms in a circle
where a breath keeps magic alive,
And enchantment remembers,
Waiting for the bridge that completes
those summerlands
immortal in a heart,
Listen for the lyric of time
blessed in a legend,
Mediations meet 
in rituals,
Discover the joy held in simplicity
and the voice in laughter
revived in passion.

July 19, 2000
