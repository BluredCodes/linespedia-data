title: "Pristine In A Moment"

Immaculate in time,
Cherished by the prisms of imagination,
Generated in the fortune foretold,
One thought
pristine in a moment
forever held,
Reach into a heart that clarifies
a single wish,
Signifying those pleasures
that we all hold,
Measured by dreams
felt in a single night,
Provided we keep in mind
those memories
forever kept in a twinkling star.

Dec. 5, 1996
