Impressions...
climbing a meter at a time.
raging where a challenge remains,
Fate emerges through passages 
                separate....
in dreams channeled like
rhetoric in shadows,
A guise held
for those
blind to a truth,
Varied to where true nourishment lies,
Subtle in its completeness,
Impulses mark emotion
rising from the heart of a poet,
Feral in nature,
Unproved by the talented,
Only within a mark in time,
But the fulfillment is met
when the moon rises
beyond the first sunset.

Oct. 1, 1999
