In those silent tears
I often wonder
where your smile went,
Could it be
that you long for
warm arms that touch in the night?
Our youth passed us by so fast,
Why did we waste so much time
on the unimportant things in life?
So now the moment is on us,
and we are reaching for
those warm sheltered times,
The breath of laughter
rises like a simple rain,
What else can life bring?
A tender look from your handsome face,
A gentle squeeze from your embrace,
a hug so I sleep tight
is what I need the most tonight,
So let's keep the tenderness
that we both seek,
And hold our truths
forever in completeness
rich in the miracle
that is met in our eyes.

April 18, 2000
