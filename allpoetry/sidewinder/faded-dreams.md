---
title: "Faded Dreams"
writer: "sidewinder"

Faded dreams have taken the best part of my heart,
Things aren't what they used to be
since you left me,
Why did you have to go?
The smile on my face
has gone to tears
and the pain lives on,
I try so hard to forget all the mistakes,
I have made to escape the memories
that left shadows in my mind,
each time I see your face
etched by those rhythms
varied behind a syncopated
heartbeat I remember,
Will your touch
be forgotten only...
When I reach for you?
Will the memories be forgotten
only until I dream of you?
I can still see your face
when I close my eyes,
My heart aches,
The pain won't subside,
The visions still break me,
And I smile no more these days,

so will the silence be met

only by the shadow

of your memory?

 

April 3, 2000
