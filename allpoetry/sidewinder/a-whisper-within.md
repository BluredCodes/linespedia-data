title: "A Whisper Within"

A whisper within the madness,
 Those unconfined wings
where freedom unfurls its absolutes
tangled in the webs of chaos,
A fulfillment in time,
Pleasures seeked by hope, 
Voices that cry in the wilderness, 
A tempest in the making,
Oblivious to those who can not see,
A moonlight which touches those
who rise in its invocation,
Celebrations rise in a fulfillment
of those visions mingled in a quagmire
of shadows which strive for honor,
A rage in the flame,
a soul who wonders
melancholy into the night,
Looking for his one & only love,
Blind in its completeness.

Nov. 30, 1997
