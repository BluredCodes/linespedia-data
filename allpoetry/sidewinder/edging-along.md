Edging alone,
That's what I'm doing,
 Trying to find out who I am,
Excitement
is what I want,
But the world want me to live
as something I'm not,
So I go on
and I survive,
The world is supposed to be black and white,
But it has many greys,
Still...
life lives on,
with beauty and passion,
Life...
with its ups and downs,
the troubles and woes,
They come and go,
 This essence of within,
I find the will to live 
and play the game of living, 
So on I smile
and touch those travels
met in wisdom,
Find those passages of second sight
and touch what magic I find
remembering the practice of patience.

May 18, 2000
