---
title: "Forever Illuminated"
writer: "sidewinder"

Visions forever held
in time understood
where another place came into view,
Otherworlds rise in invocations
and songs immortalize forgotten souls,
Lessons studied in nature
where only those in wisdom
shape shadows of time
where miracles are awakened
and challenge the inner spirit,
Veils of illusion touch those
who silently pass into the night
forever illuminated in the Isle of Avalon,
Where gateways & portals allow the
human hearts to view another dimension,
And Mother Nature invites you to be
one with the world of Faerie,
Bringing peace to a world of conflict & chaos.

Feb 20, 1999
