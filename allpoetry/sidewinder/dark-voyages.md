Dark is the time
held by a mystery,
Sheltered by a touch,
Empowered with a spirit of the heart,
Seeking balance
in those beliefs
aligned with the soul,
A quest in magic
merged with what you can achieve,
Paths shared with those
dreams in kingdoms of the otherworld,
A book of shadows
that reveals a part of you
only to touch
your secret self,
A voyage in circles
touched in great wisdom
raptures celebrated in rainbows.

Oct. 25, 1997
