---
title: "Celebrate The Magic"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "celebrate-the-magic"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dreams of the spirit,
Rise and touch my heart,
Teach me those lessons
that the dragons have held in ancient times,
celebrated in harmony,
Let me see those views
that were meant to honor and embrace,
Concepts that some have a hard time with,
Wrap me in guidance,
So that I have an open heart and understanding,
Ancient strength you will provide
for those times that test my soul,
Let me feel the freedom
amongst the wind,
Releasing the child in me,
So I again can feel the wonder in everything,
Transform the need in me
and touch my curiosity,
Celebrating the magic in my heart.

Nov. 3, 1998
