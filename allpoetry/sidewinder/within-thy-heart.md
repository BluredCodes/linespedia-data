---
title: "Within Thy Heart"

Forgetting thyself
once within thy heart
a rage plagues
forever,
Reaching a darkness
mired in the shadow
a hallowness prevails,
wherein a trace
meets within the blindness
only to touch the truth,
But the truth has been forgotten,
And in thy sight
a hope disappears.

Sept. 16, 1999
