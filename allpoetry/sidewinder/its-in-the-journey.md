title: "It's In the Journey"

Traces in a merging
forever lost in a memory,
Seconds touched,
But in the hour
lessons reveal
uncertain moments,
Perhaps crystal visions are
sacred,
Why?
because it makes you think,
And In the thinking
treasures can be found
in the most inconspicuous places,
Search for the quest
that you want to find,
Sometimes...
the journey matters more
than the completion of the end.

Jan. 15, 1999
