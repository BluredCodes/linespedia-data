In the silence
                a door opens
moving to touch those words we trip over,
Revealing in echoes
                       those tributes
found by those who seek them,
                                        Why?
I don't know,
                  Do you?
Maybe together we...
                            can find out,
Whisper in my ear...
                         what most want to hear,
And maybe...
                 we'll find out what it's like...
not to be alone.

June 4, 2001
