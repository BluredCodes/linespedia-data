title: "To Touch Upon Thyself"

To touch upon thyself
seeking wisdom,
Holding courage
beyond meaning that few can understand,
traveling beyond time and
this plane,
Immersing one's self in a pathwork,
Touching understanding
that one can only find in one's own heart,
Overcoming the fear
that rules one's life,
Changing the ignorance & arrogance
that sometime stifles one
when searching for the truth,
Allowing the sacred to guide one
in ways of mystery,
Fulfilling a dream
and enjoying the journey
just for its own sake.

Sept. 22, 1998
