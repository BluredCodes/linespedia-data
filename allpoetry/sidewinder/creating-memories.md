Tracing down time,
Pleasures in celebrations,
Creating moments
long time remembered,
Created by the sun
in images
mired by a look
chronicled in mystical
pages forever held
through gold runes,
As a horizon
rises into the night
and Lady Luna
smiles
in spirit,
A legend of trust,
The love that has been created
surrounds the fullness of the night
and is shared by those around
like echoes of ancient sounds,
a healing journey
sacred in its place in time.

July 8, 1997
