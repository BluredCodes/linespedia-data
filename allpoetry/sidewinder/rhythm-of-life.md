There is a rhythm of life,
Touching where some take no notice,
A calm in the night
where the silence
reveals more
than time can tell,
Or can it?
That dependence
made clear,
Forever completing that circle
held in paradise,
Adaptation made possible
heartbeat to heartbeat,
seeing somehow within
and finds what needs to be found,
Intricate in that need,
Celebrated second by second.

Sept. 3, 2001
