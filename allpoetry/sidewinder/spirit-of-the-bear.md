title: "Spirit Of The Bear"

Power flowing from within,
	Peaceful when left alone,
	But when enraged terrible, quick death,
	Wisdom entices those who listen,
	Freedom in the wild,
	Symbol of the west,
	Teach me what you know
	& maybe I'll learn
	from your heart,
	Claw & fang touches those who dare to intrude,
	Yes.... I hear your roar,
	It wakes me in my dreams,
	Perhaps...
	         soul to soul
	I can find those truths
	that are found in the spirit dance,
	Was it you that was trying to reach me
	through the spirit realm?
	I can see the shaman
	that guides me as my spirit guide,
	Red man
	can you see me now?
	I can see your smiling eyes,
	Teach me from your heart & soul,
	I hold your wisdom
	that we all need,
	Follow me when I need guidance
	within my life
	& my dreams.
	Feb 27, 2002
