---
title: "With Thy Distant Fire"
writer: "sidewinder"

If I see your eyes
then know this
that the sun touches
beyond the sunrise
and my spirit
is inflamed
with thy distant fire,
Those echoes that touch
charm into thy soul,
forevermore will I hold
those poetic wings
that soar into the night
forever reaching a harmony,
Urgent in thy grasp
amongst those seasons
which tell a destiny.

May 19, 1999
