The wind blowing
	a blistering cold,
	Brisk against my neck
	and here I am,
	Looking against the horizon,
	Hoping for a look at an eagle,
	Enjoying this winter day,
	But right now.....
	                  No such luck,
	Aw well....
	              Maybe another day!
	Still...
	           there's sunshine to enjoy,
	It was time to get out,
	Didn't want to look at the same old room,
	It was stuffy there,
	I can hear the river rushing
	forever against the bank,
	In the distance I can see a raven
	looking for another meal,
	I wish him luck!
	I can see the clouds
	                        floating against the sky,
	And I smile,
	                   It's gonna be
	                                  another gorgeous day!
	Jan. 13, 2002
