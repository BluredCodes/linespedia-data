When the darkness overcomes,
Whispering....
Nevermore,
I keep in my mind,
There must be a light,
Maybe I don't see it,
But it's out there....
Somewhere,
I just have to keep looking,
For reality
mirrors those answers
of questions that must be asked,
And karma revolves
those lessons that must be learned,
Some painful,
Some joyful,
But never the less,
This world reminds me
of life eternally.

April 23, 1996
