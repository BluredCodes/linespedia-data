Spiritual warrior
hail in time,
The urge toward conquest,
Focus on the struggle with self,
Devotion to a cause,
Patience, courage and devotion
are goals worth achieving,
Legends shape the mystically powerful,
Journeys and vision quests play to a key,
A rune of destiny that will not be denied,
Visualize the rite of the active door,
Here is the secret wisdom of the forest,
Consult the knowing self within,
Know thyself
and the world is yours,
But life can be hard,
At the same time a challenge,
In silence there is opportunity,
In life of the spirit you are always at the beginning.

July 12, 1997
