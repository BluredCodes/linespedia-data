title: "Like Echoes Answered"

Laying against your head
I felt the comfort,
Like a  child feeling the completeness of his bed,
Forever wondering...
Will I ever dream?

Listen only for a soft memory
hidden behind the shadow
of what once was,
And in that pain
the image of you rises,

Verily I hear your voice,
Only in the distance
like echoes
answered in the night,

How is it that the courageous in love
are forgotten?
Past over like a jealous wind
which rages in pandemonium.

Dec. 13, 1999
