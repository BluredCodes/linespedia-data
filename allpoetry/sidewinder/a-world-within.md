---
title: "A World Within"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "a-world-within"
source: "AllPoetry"

Circle within a circle
varied by the time,
Free among the proud,
Serene in a blind
fury,
Contrasted in dimensions,
Where clouds consider more
than the illusions climb,
Thoughts distorted by a vision
of to that feeds of the scraps,
Created through worm holes
that are illusions withdrawn,
Where dimensions vary by time,
Perceptions touch
within the arisen
cresending above the horizon,
And if thoughts could be seen,
What a sight it would be,
For within those dimensions
universes arise
and fulfill a completeness.

Sept. 24, 1999
