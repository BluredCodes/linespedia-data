---
title: "There Be Dragons"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "there-be-dragons"

To touch upon high
the sky and endless clouds
frolicking are those who would
in their freedom
touch beyond imagination,
They dance with grace
held only by their beauty,
Invoked by magic,
Ageless in lore,
Open the doors between realms
most have not heard from,
Hear a dragon's cry,
Their song makes a heart grow strong,
Venture where some only fear to tread,
Strive to meet their courage
dedicated with a soul,
And if by chance
you are lucky,
A transformation takes place
within your soul
to find peace
which journeys into gateways
of worlds only dreamed of.

Oct. 27, 1999
