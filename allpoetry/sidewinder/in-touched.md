---
title: "In Touched"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "in-touched"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hidden among the tales,
A flickering of a choosing
focuses into realms
inhabited by those who
choose to impart their wisdom,
Simple times for simple folk
to touch the thread of life,
Taunting a heart
but in the same moment
a reflection embraces what you seek,
Guides in imagination
held by a promise
to a veil in a garden
that celebrates a gate into the otherworld,
Welcomes by those who are
revered in rituals
sacred with aspects
in touched with one's self.

Feb. 14, 1998
