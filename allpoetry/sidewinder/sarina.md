---
title: "Sarina"
writer: "sidewinder"
slug: "sarina"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30622-Sarina-by-sidewinder"
---

She speaks with her eyes,
Defies with lies,
No...
tries to despise love gone wrong,
In song,
Not long...
before death traps,
Grasps...
Falling perhaps she sees eyes disease,
As she dances, prances
life's foul romances...
Dead,
Strange head,
lead to be fed to fire...
conspire
to life's hell,
sickly spell,
A knell of pain...
Rain,
So insane like her name,
Sarina,
Ballerina on codeine,
A fallen star,
Far beyond the rim,
Give in,
Begin to define
the live reality,
Finality,
And find 
behind life's blindness, 
An evil,
Dishevel and fall,
And at last
the past,
Rest like the best,
and send her to the end,
her life,
All strife,
A knife,
....it's over,
But is it?
Touched in that soul
life crosses over
and over
and over,
Doomed until those mistakes made...
repaid,
Lessons learned,
Or are they?
Tears fall,
Touched in that heart
that's been mauled,
dreams shattered
within the clatter
varied within the spiral dance,
A circle within
like an echo
touched plain
in pain,
Where will it end?
Could it be
that she see's
only where some dream?

Dec. 1, 2001
