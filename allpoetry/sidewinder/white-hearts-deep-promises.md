White hearts & deep promises
	touched in a world
	where chivalry revives
	in a miracle of time,
	Mystical of touch
	fired within a heart
	and only thine can embrace
	forever those celebrations
	caressed within thy blossom,
	At last...
	the melody arrives
	to touch the rhythm
	forever met by shadow
	and the light,
	Can you finally hear
	what those have heard before?
	Feb. 14, 2000
