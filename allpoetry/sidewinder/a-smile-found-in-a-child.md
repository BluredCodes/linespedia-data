title: "A Smile Found In A Child"

And the stars shown
	like a glittering light
	through out time
	eternal in its flow,
	Returning me to a place
	where peace remains in my mind,
	Calling me,
	Begging me
	like an echo
	whispering in the wind,
	Each day I remember,
	Touching that need met in a heart,
	Drawing those mysteries
	forever more
	where the light meets a mystic
	who reveals in nature,
	Discovering the warmth
	and a smile
	found in a child,
	Celebrating what some
	refuse to see.
	Dec. 15, 2001
