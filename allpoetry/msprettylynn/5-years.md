Smiles
Tears
Kisses
Pain
Love
Words
Life
Fists
You said you loved 
and always would
more
Fighting,screaming, yelling, cussing
Another good-bye
Back again, knowck on the door
Tears in your eyes
Know more good-byes
A mistake was made
and you apologize
I go to bed
you by my side
Why do i still cry
Morning comes
Another day
Will you stay
