I'm afraid to be alone,
     but not sure if I want you
I'm scared to hold your hand,
     but long for your touch
I'm afraid to tell you why,
     but know you will listen
I'm scared to walk away 
     even if it is better
I'm afraid to stand under your gaze,
     but feel so secure
I'm scared to let you hold me, 
     even if it feels good
I'm afraid to let you love me  
     in fear that you will hurt me
