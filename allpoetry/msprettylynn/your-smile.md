You were a Bitch then and you are one nowYou messed with my life to many timesI can't stand your weak little smileHow many people really think your the victimOnly I know you secretsWhere you've beenWhat you've seenWho you've doneWhen you did it Why you did itYou don't fool meYour just like everyone elseYour a Bitch and a skankIf you die tommorow i wont' think twiceI hate you and your fucking smile
