title: "Grabbed Your Coat"
writer: "msprettylynn"

Your gone again
with nothing to say
you grabbed your coat 
and left today

I'm sitting here
alone once more
and nothing but fear
is what i feel

You said you loved me
was that a lie
for once I wish you could see
how hurt I am inside

But today is everyday
and there is nothing I can say
you leave when you want 
and say what you want

You don't care about me 
or you would see
how hurt I am
to see you leave

I'm crying now
and the tears are hurting
but do you care..
no
because you grabbed your coat and left today
