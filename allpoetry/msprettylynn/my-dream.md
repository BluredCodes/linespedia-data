When I was a little girl it was my dream to fall in love
a dream every little girl has
You made mine come true
You walked into my life
took my hand
and held it tight
You held me
and never let me go
You kissed my hands 
and then my lips
You told me I was beautiful
and said you would never lie

I would lay awake 
and smile
I would whisper in you ear
"I love you"

Now are paths have changed and I don't know if my childhood dream is the same
youknow longer 
have my hand
or 
hold me close
or 
kiss my lips
You tell me lies
looking into my eyes

I cry and try to whisper
"I love you"
