---
title: "Sitting Here"
writer: "msprettylynn"
slug: "sitting-here"
source: "AllPoetry"

I sit her waiting for you to callI sit on the steps waiting for your carI sit on the couch waiting for you to sit with meI sit on the floor hoping you will cuddle with meI sit on the bed waiting for you to hold meI lay on the pillow waiting for you to love meI look at you through the dark, and then i start to thinkI roll over, look out the window, and then I start to cry
