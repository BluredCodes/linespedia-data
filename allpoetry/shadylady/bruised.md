---
title: "Bruised"
writer: "shadylady"

You make me cry 
Please tell me why 
What did i do
why are you mad 
what about the love we had 
don't you even care 
you're never even there 
all this abuse, i cant bare
all this pain just isn't fair
please, no more violence 
just leave me here in silence 
there's more to me than meets the eye 
just go away and let me cry 
you have bruised my heart and soul 
you took my life then took control 
you put me under your vicious spell 
you turned heaven into hell
get the fuck away from me 
go away and let me be 
i need to let my bruises heal 
and let you know how you made me feel
at first everything was just fine 
iwas yours and you were mine 
then i recieved that first blow 
i forgave you and let it go 
then it happened once again 
i let it slide until then, 
i told you to cut it out 
then you started to scream and shout 
you told me then that i would lose 
you made sure i felt the bruise 
my bruises arent just skin deep 
you made me afraid to even sleep 
but now it hurts more than before 
so ill hurt you now to even the score 
im kicking you out, onto the street 
ill show you who you can and cant beat 
theres something wrong inside your mind 
i know the truth youll never find 
until you meet someone more demented than you 
then maybe youll see how much i went through
my scars will finally heal someday 
and for your sins, you will pay 
and youll know that im amused 
because someday youll get bruised
