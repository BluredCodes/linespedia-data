The touch of your skin
The feel of your face
Moments like these
Never replaced
My baby so dear
Held next to me 
The love of my life 
How can this be 
Life is so perfect
With him nearby 
I can't stop loving him
Nor could i try
When we're together
Passionately drawn
Next thing we know 
the night is gone
The feel of my body
Held in his arms
Nothing on earth
Could do me harm 
Whenever he's near
Sent from above 
Thats what i call
Us making lovei love you tommy.
