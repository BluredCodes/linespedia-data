your mom is nice
she knows whats fun
she keeps busy
till the night is done
once i saw a butterfly
it had umm shoes
i don't like stuff in my eye
when you dont win you lose
muy bien
alguien
muchas cosas
i'm not ten
hi
boo
my god
how are you?
i am fine
i'm not done
i won't stop
i'm number one
i'm flying high
with a kazoo
i wish i had 
a kangaroo
your mom has
a great ass
