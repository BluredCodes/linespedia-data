I have a friendHe's special to mehe has a tailand lives in a treehe likes bugsand bananas toobut most of allhe likes youMr. Monkeyjumps aroundSenor monkeywears no frownhe likes Mrs. monkeycause she's cutewhen she's around he gives her fruitthe monkeys like to get it onthey prefer the back lawn monkey lovin is the bestit doesn't compare to the restthe monkey is my best friend we'll be best friendstill the end.
