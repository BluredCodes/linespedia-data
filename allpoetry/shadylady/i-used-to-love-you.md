The sun goes downthe moon comes upthe phone rings i pick it upits you again you always callyou said you're in love you said you'd fall i used to love it it gave me a rush i used to enjoy this little crush i knew sooner or later id have this doubt sooner or later it wouldn't work outi used to love you i used to care but now there's no lovethere's nothing there i used to love it when youd call now theres nothing down that hall you may still be in love theres nothing i can do i pick up the phone and begin to tell you
