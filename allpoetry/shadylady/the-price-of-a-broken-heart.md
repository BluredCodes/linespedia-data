---
title: "The Price of a Broken Heart"
writer: "shadylady"

A broken heart a shattered dream 
a fateful night a tragic scream 
a deadly secret she will hold 
her tears will dry as the night gets cold
her heart now broken, her mascara smeared 
her nightmare now reality, as she always feared
the sun came up, she touched his hand
its all over, just as planned 
she wipes the blood off her face
she must hurry and leave this place
she paid the price from the start
she paid the price of a broken heart
