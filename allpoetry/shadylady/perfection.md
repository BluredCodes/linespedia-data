Blonde hair and blue eyes a tiny waist and slim thighs is this the girl that we see is this how perfection will always be?why is she cool , why is she sweet? is it because she will not eat?this isnt perfection, this is a mask she will never tell if you dont ask she lives in fear and it will grow that someday you will know that shes not perfect and wants to be a normal person like you and me
