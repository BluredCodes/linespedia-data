---
title: "Band-Aid"
writer: "shadylady"

I can do everything that you want me toBe the perfect little girl from a fantasy worldDo everything you expect me toIt doesn't matter anymoreI can smile, but it's fakeIt's just a random meaningless attempt to feel normalIt doesn't mean a thing. It's all fabricationJust monotonous actionI no longer feel anything I'm so numb insideI just give in and make everything OK Everyone is happy and it's all better No one cares how I really feelIt's like someone ripped my heart to piecesAs if it didn't matterPut a band-aid on the wound and it's OKYou don't understand. This wounds not just skin deepIt's not that simple anymore I'm giving up trying to protect myselfI'm sick of always crying When things don't go your wayMaybe I'm selfish for wanting to be saneor maybe I'm just stupid for wanting to be happySo I'll just give in and be your little dollAnd do whatever you commandGo ahead and put a band-aid on my lips
