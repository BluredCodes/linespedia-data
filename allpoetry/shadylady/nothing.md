---
title: "Nothing"
writer: "shadylady"
slug: "nothing"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

of all the people i have met 
i had to fall in love with you 
my angel, my love
I pine for you and I wait
for the day i let you see
how much you truly mean to me 
you are the air i breathe 
the ruler of my soul 
without you i could not live 
but sadly, you are not mine 
My love, the pain i feel 
i cant control 
just like the love i have for you 
you are the moon in my sky 
you hold the key to my heart 
do you love me? does it matter? 
i can't have you, so whats the use
I only live to see your face
without it i am nothing
