title: "A Moonlit Night"
writer: "shadylady"

All the sleepy stars above 
Shimmer light onto my love
They all whisper through the night
Of how he sparkles, shimmers bright
He treats me good, his love he shows
And through each day, our love still grows
We sit below the moon so high
We suddenly hear a celestial cry
The jealous stars must sit and see
And wish and dream that they were me
I know their wishing is in vain 
And they pine away in such pain
As the comets soar overhead 
They all retreat into their bed
Out peeks the sun into her place
Her envious tears run down her face 
Her gassy mass is no match for me
Why? 
I have all his love, you see
