I remember the day that i met you I remember the way you found me tooI remember the love that we shared I remember the time you said you cared I remember the day you broke my heart I remember when you said we should part I remember not knowing what to doI remember the way i missed you I remember when you weren't there I remember when you didn't careI remember how you made me cry I remember you said i shouldnt try I remember how you made me hide I remember how you took away my pride I remember the way i put up with you I remember the way you hit me too I remember believing every lieBut loving you? I dont remember whyI've seen this happen to way too many of my close friends, so its kinda personal
