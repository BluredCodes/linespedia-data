all snuggled up in this cozy bed inside mommys tummy is where i lay my head a few months along i'm starting to show a new mommy and daddy watching me grow a new baby, its me! i'm on the way daddy tells mom, of course he'll stay a little while later mommys alone young mommy is scared who would've known its alright, mommy we'll be ok daddy was scared and he ran away now daddy is gone and mommys a ho all because i decided to grow i cant wait until i come out i want to know what the worlds about wait mom whats happening now? i'll be good mom i promise, i vow just give me a chance i want to live i'll make you proud, i have potential to give mommy, why are you getting rid of me? i'm your baby, part of you see? now all i see is a big bright light i'll follow it to heaven this isn't right a baby unborn never given a chance all because daddy got in moms pants i still love you, in heaven i'll wait when you come to meet me, i'll open the gate the child of yours that you never knew you wasted my life and ruined yours too mommy, we'll be together someday i wish you hadnt taken my life awayThis one is personal, it reminds me of how i feel about my mother. As if she had abandoned me, although not quite as drastically
