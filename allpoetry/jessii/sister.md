---
title: "Sister"
writer: "Jessii"
slug: "sister"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

A sister is someone that will always be there for you,
Is someone you can trust,
Is someone that if you ask she will do,
To her you are 100 times greater than a peice of rust,

A sister is sweeter than candy,
She is loving and daring,
She always tells you things are great and dandy,
She is brave and daring,

A sister can lie,
She can say mean stuff,
But her shoulder is there when there is a tear in your eye,
She is there for you when times get rough.

She's the best sister!!
