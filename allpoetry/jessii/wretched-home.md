I Live In a Wretched Home

                    All Strapped Down Tight

                      Im all Shackled In A Dome

                   Wishing Things Were Alright

                    I Live My Life... Piece by Piece

                   Hoping Things Will Get Better

                    All I ever Get Is Grief

                   I Write Loving Letters

                     To Those Who Dont Exist

                   All I Do Is Come and Go

                       No one Knows I Keep A List

                    I Try To Walk, But Can Only Rome

                   I Try To Hide Out and Keep Low

                     'Cause I Live In A Wretched Home
