title: "Lost You Forever"
writer: "Destiny55316"

They said that was true, 
the saying of breaking up,
leaving you be,
and letting you think.
Work out your confusion
and giving you space…
and I believed it.
I believed that you could come back,
and love me.
I believed you couldn’t live without me,
and we were meant to be.
You didn't come back;
but I still believed…
for too long,
and I sobbed for you.
I made a mistake
and I realized I have believed the wrong thing,
believed at the wrong time,
believed with the wrong person.
I let myself get lost in those emotions
and as I tried to find myself,
I lost you.
