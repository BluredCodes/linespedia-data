Sitting on the beach
6:00 in the morning
waiting for the sun to rise,
and make the dark night
a beautiful day. 

Relaxing and not realizing the beauty to come
complaining of the time, and the weather
and taking for granted,
everything,
every single thing...
everything I am about to experience. 

Soon it shines bright,
and I realize it all and wonder;
"when have I ever deserved such gorgeousness?" 

This ray of light
shining across my face
as it gets brighter
so do I. 

And it continues to rise 
letting its majestic rays of beauty be shared with everyone
even those not fully deserving
no one knows why
but it does
it is just one of those things
that mysterious, majestic beauty.
