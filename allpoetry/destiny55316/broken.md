My love for you is so very strong
And it hurts
It runs through every vain in my body
Extremely fast,
Burning me,
Like your odd love for me back.
You love me one day?
Swear it will never go past friends the next?
I want more,
You know I want more.
All I ask is that you not play with my head.
You want someone to return that love you long for?
She’s standing right here...
that hurts…
You make me hurt,
And I dwell on this hurt.
Yes, I dwell on that hurt.
It treads through my skull
On the same direct path,
Into darkness.
And I and lie, 
broken.
