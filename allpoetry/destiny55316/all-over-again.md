We went out tonight 
and I loved every minute of it.
We talked and walked 
about our new love,
and what it meant.
Ate and drank 
only the finest
because you said it was what I deserved.
We smiled and kissed,
for we knew this was right.

You dropped me off, said goodnight,
you held me tight,
and for a while I knew I would be all right.
But when you left,
I felt alone again...
until you called that afternoon
and I fell in love all over again.I don't like this one... what can I work on?
