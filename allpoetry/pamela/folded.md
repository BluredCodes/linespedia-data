---
title: "Folded"
writer: "Pamela"
slug: "folded"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i have been so many things, in my mind

 always successful,content

knowing the full capacity of my life

 feeling it all to some extent

i awaken, full of hope, to see you there

 i tremble inside

out of  fear  that i've come to know so well

 i hang my head, 

feeling the hot sting of tears that flow so quickly

 feeling awkward and empty

i know i shall never become all that i am
