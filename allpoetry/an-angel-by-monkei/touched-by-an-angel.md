title: "Touched by an Angel"

I watch him...
Worship him...
Adore him...

He smiles at me...
Speaks to me...
Laughs with me...
Kisses her...

He loves me...
Touches me...
Cries with me...
And leaves...
With her.

But I..was touched by an angel.
