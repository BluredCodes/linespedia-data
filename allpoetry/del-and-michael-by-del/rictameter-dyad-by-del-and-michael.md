Good night
To you, my love
So lonely without you
Miss those sweet passionate kisses
The many nights you showed me what bliss is
My heart wants to beat next to yours
Two hearts beating as one
As you kiss me
Good night

In dreams
I'll think of you
Of all you've done for me
Of the glorious times we've shared
We've grown so close in such a little time
I've never felt so loved before
To sleep - such loneliness
But you're with me
In dreamsThese Rictameters are written by two.  They are easy to compose and fun.  Try some..... Del
