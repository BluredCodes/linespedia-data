Again
I've lost a love
I cherished so deeply
Why must I feel my destruction?
Why must I repeat this cycle of loss?
Have my mistakes taught me nothing?
One day I'll learn, but now
My hearts broken
Again

Tear drops
Fill my eyes
Miss you so much, my dear
My dreams are filled with memories
Of those passionate nights you and I shared
Our bare bodies clung together
Your kisses were devine
Life now - empty
Tear dropsThis Rictameter was written with a friend, Michael. Del
