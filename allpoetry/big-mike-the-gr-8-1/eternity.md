title: "Eternity"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"

How can I put into words what I feel 
I just can not deal
Cause I am afraid of what I feel
I know I want to be with you
But I don't know what to do
My mind seeing you smile
My heart floating a mile
My mind and heart have not agreed
My heart says you are what I need
My mind thinks I should flee
I fall to my knees
I am begging you please
For your love I would cross the seas
I know I want to be with you
But will you be with me 
For all eternity
