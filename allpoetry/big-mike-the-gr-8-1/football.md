FOOTBALL IS A WAY OF LIFE 
FOOTBALL IS PAIN, GLORY,..... VICTORY
JUST AS LIFE IS YOU FEEL MUCH PAIN
AND AT TIMES FEEL THOSE MOMENTS OF VICTORY
FOOTBALL IS THAT FEELING YOU GET RUNNING ONTO THE OPEN AND EMPTY FIELD
THE SAME FEELING YOU GET WHEN YOU SEE THE ONE YOU LOVE
YOU GET WEAK IN THE KNEES
THE THOUGHT NOT KNOWING WHAT WILL HAPPEN
WILL YOU EMERGE VICTORIOUS
OR BE DESTRUCTED
AS A GREAT MAN ONCE SAID 
"FOOTBALL IS A GAME OF INCHES, THE VICTORES ARE THE ONES WILLING TO TEAR THEMSELVES AND OTHERS NEXT TO THEM JUST FOR THAT INCH"
MANY KNOW OF WHAT I SPEAK
MANY HAVE NO CLUE
BUT I TELL YOU THIS YOU HAVE NOT LIVED....
IF YOU HAVE NOT PLAYED THE LIFE AND THE GREAT GAME OF FOOTBALL.
