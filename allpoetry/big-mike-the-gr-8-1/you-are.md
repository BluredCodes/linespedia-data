---
title: "You Are"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"

You are the meaning to my life
You are the point to my excistence
You have the eyes I wake up every morning to see
You have the hair I wake up every morning to feel
You have the voice that I live for everyday
You are what I live for and what I would die for
And only if I could make you see
All that you mean to me.
