title: "Beggining"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"

In the beggining God gave us life
In the beggining God gave me you
Sometimes for you I know not what to do
How come is it that I cannot have you
Someday I pray
That you will stay 
Here with me, because you have the key.....
the key to my heart, my soul
In the end i do not want to be left with a hole.....
A hole in my heart
