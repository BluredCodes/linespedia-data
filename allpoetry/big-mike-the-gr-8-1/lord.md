LORD....
I ASK YOU TO TO BE WITH ME
EVERY STEP I TAKE
SHOW ME THE PATH
LEAD ME YOUR WAY
EVERY MORNING THAT I AWAKE
MAY I PRAY AND THANK YOU FOR THIS DAY
MAY WE BE GRATEFUL FOR THE FOOD YOU GIVE US
FOR MANY PEOPLE DON'T HAVE TO EAT
THE THINGS WE DO ALL DAY
GUIDE ME YOUR WAY
HELP ME THROUGH SCHOOL
HAVE ME MAKE THE RIGHT DECISIONS
AND NOT ACT LIKE A FOOL
I ASK YOU OH LORD
" TO FORGIVE MY SINS IF I COMMITTED ANY FORGIVE THEM ALL IF I COMMITTED MANY"
I ASK YOU YOU LORD....
TO BE WITH ME, AS I CHOOSE TO FOLLOW YOU.
