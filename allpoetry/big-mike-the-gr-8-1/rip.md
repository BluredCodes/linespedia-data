THIS IS ALL JUST A BAD DREAM
HOW CAN LIFE BE SO MEAN?
HERE YOUR CAR AS IT STEERS
WHY DOES IT HAVE TO BE YOU?
I KNOW IT CANNOT BE TRUE
SEEING YOU LYING ON THE FLOOR
I CAN'T HANDLE THIS NO MORE
ALL THAT YOU HAD GOING SO RIGHT
ALL THE PAIN WE MUST FIGHT
RIGHT HERE BY YOUR SIDE WHY WAS I LEFT BEHIND?
IT MAKES ME SO MAD 
THAT I AM SO SAD
YOU HAD SO MUCH TO GAIN
I HOPE YOU LEFT WITH NO PAIN
