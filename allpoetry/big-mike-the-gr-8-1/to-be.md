---
title: "To Be"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"

They say we were meant to be 
But i just can't see
Why you must be with me
How is it that 
You can treat me like a door mat?
I cared for you
You cared for who?
You'll miss me when i'm gone
You became such a yawn
I treated you well
All you did was give me hell
