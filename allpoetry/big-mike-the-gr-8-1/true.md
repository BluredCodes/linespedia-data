title: "True"

How can I be true
To what I feel for you
If I know we cannot be true
You made time stand still
I cannot change the way I feel 
You helped my heart heal 
From a past heart ache
I still feel like an empty lake
I thought I could not go on
She hurt me like the pain of the burning sun
Now much time has past
I really hope you and I will last 
I want to love again
But I can't have my heart broken
As my feelings grow
My heart does not feel so low
With you time seems to go slow
For i want to live every minute of every day
Cause this time I want to stay
Only if I could put into words
What I feel 
So you could see
Exactly everything you mean to me
