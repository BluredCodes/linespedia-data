title: "Why?"

Death comes to us all
You live you die
Occasionally you love
Love is a beautiful thing
Something that makes you want to sing
Yet, love does not last
It is a thing of the past
It passes by so fast
Leaves your heart in a cast
Puts you through pain
There is nothing left to gain
Makes you go insane
How can you live
When it takes all you've got to give
I cannot forgive
The pain you've caused me
Why can't you see
The pain you've caused me
Why can't you see
WHY?
