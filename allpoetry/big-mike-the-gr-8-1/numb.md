title: "Numb"

MY BODY GOES NUMB AS I SEE YOU
KNOWING THAT I WANT TO BE WITH YOU
KNOWING I WANT TO HOLD YOU IN MY ARMS
KNOWING I CAN'T HAVE YOU
KNOWING YOUR HEART STANDS NOWHERE NEXT TO MINE
DREAMING ONE DAY YOU'LL BE MINE
MAYBE EVERYTHING WILL BE FINE
HOW CAN I LIVE WITHOUT YOU KNOWING WE CANNOT BE TRUE
MY HEART CAN'T GET THE CLUE
THAT I JUST CAN'T HAVE YOU
