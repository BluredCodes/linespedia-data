When I die will I be Missed?
By all the people I have kissed
What will I do
When I hear them say "What will I do, without you?"
Will I rest in peace?
Will I miss my niece?
I have felt pain
I felt Like abroken cane
Itmakes me go insane
I have lost
At a great cost 
I have found
And have fallin to the ground
Now I am lying in my bed
With these thoughts in my head
In life I do not want to fail
I can not go to hell
I just can not fail
I want to be with God
Will I be missed?
Will I?
