How will I live Without you
I hope you'll miss me to
You were always there for me
Your love stung me like a bee
In time I will move on
This is a battle I have not won
I thought you were going to stay with me 
How could you flee from me
My heart will never let you go
How could my heart fall so low
In time I will move on
My heart no longer feels like a burning sun
In time I will move on
In time
