Life comes and goes
But for those who have felt love
Life goes a bit shorter
With love you have somthing to live for
Something you wake up every morning to feel
And to help your heart heal
Love helps you when your down
Puts a smile to a frown
Love gets you weak in the knees
In loves name I would cross most seas
