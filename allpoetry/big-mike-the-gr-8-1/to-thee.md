---
title: "To Thee"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"
slug: "to-thee"
source: "AllPoetry"

There are a thousand words i can say to 
Bring you back to me
Why can't you see
That my heart belongs to thee
I love you with all my heart
I wish us never to part
I drop to my knees
I'm begging you please
Please baby please come back to me
Why can't you see
My heart belongs to thee
And only to thee
