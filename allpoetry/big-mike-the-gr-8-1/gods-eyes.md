title: "God's eyes"
writer: "Big mike the gr 8 1"

With God's eyes I saw you
Like an angel standing in my path
Beauty like snows wrath
My heart stopped with the sight of you
What shall I do
For you, Tell me what shall I do
I have nothing to speak
You are what I have seeked
God's eyes have let me seen you
I know just what to do
I have to tell you
That I Love You!
