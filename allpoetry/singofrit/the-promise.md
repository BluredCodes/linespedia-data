The Promise  

In the house of the Lord
They promise
To run hand in hand in the sun and in the rain.
They promise 
To share their laughter and their tears.
They promise
To make a home 
And decorate it with flowers and stars.
They promise
To make their dreams reality.
They promise
To carry each other over the sea.
They  promise  
To cherish their love forever.
They promise
To live and die together.
