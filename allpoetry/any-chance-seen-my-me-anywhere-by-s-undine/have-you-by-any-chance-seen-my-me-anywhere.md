excuse me,... 
but i seem to have misplaced my 'me' near somewhere
it can't have gone far
for i don't drive a car

are you quite sure you haven't had even a glance?
not even a spark?
i once shone in the dark

if it happens by chance my 'me' catches your eye?
please, don't even blink!
she'll fly free if you think

if you would, slip this 'round her and link up her chain
what is it? you ask
why, 
it's my 'me's' mother's mask

s
