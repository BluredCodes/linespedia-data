You are magic in the darkness,a dark figure in my corner.You are sly with swiftness.I love you as I fear you,Yielding completely, fondling soul,I have lost all control.I'd stop if I could, but I can't.Fold your ears for a full throated rant.Slideout.You know me too well.You press me with kisses,sweaty lover's palming touch.You,my every spare moment,my best friend.When can we do it again?I am doing bad bad things...
