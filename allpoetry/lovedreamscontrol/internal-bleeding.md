title: "internal bleeding"

How long will you pretend
that there is nothing wrong?
In your eyes I see it all,
but I won't look away.

Smile for me one last time
before the world kills you.
I would have been good.
I could have been fun,
but I wasn't meant to be.
I wasn't the one
to be you release,
your peace of mind.
wanted to help you fly
despite your beaten broken wings,
but gods laugh and devils sing.
The two are not to be. 
This twist of fate 
would complicate
the winding roads ahead,
but I see hope in you 
and love you for it.
I see depth in you and
I won't ignore it. 
I'll be there for you 
whenever you need.
I'll dry your tears
when you internaly bleed.
My heart ascends
when I hear your laughter
and flies high above.
Hold this true,
'till death and thereafter;
you will always have my love.
