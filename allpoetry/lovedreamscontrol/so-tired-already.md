I am green, a babe at her mother's breast,a new soul, barely caught it's first breath,an open mind, no need to reset,a blank tape, just put in the deck.Open eyes that see it all,blood and love, the crying call,sex and cum, a lover's fall,crying eyes, they see it all.Eyes are decomposing in mold,why feeling so very old,want to sleep out in the cold,wanting to die in the fold.Why do I feel like its over,being in love with no lover,like a war when I need to take cover,like breathing yet being smothered.
