title: "No love for pain."

Am I deceived?Oh, please, let your tounge speak lightly.Do not thrash me with that awful truth...we are alone, alone togetherfrom birth and thereafter.Is this illusion?Oh, please lie and tell methis time isn't wasted. All the same.Two people alone.We are one,in a dream I have.Have I waken to this nightmare?Or am I asleep still?Drowning, dripping, leaking.Intertwining veins, like vines, so softly twisted,lead to one heart in time.We are one.Then I wake....You're not there.I cry myself to sleep, again.This is not about losing a spouse...it is about losing anyone who may be close to you and losing them to a realization, an evolution of the mind.
