Sleep in silent dreamswith closed lips and deafened ears..Dream of morning striking my facewith a sun so terrible it re verbs in my headI wait for liberation.Freedom of the chains I’ve wrapped aroundYou and I in love, in hate, jealousy, pain…Exhale into darkness all that is badand inhale the sweet breath of life that lets me sleep in silent dreams...once again...
