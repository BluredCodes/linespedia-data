title: "I know I dream control"

So you've found me here...
my life in wind about me.
I stand on shaky ground.
I see you through the haze;
You,
the embodiment of my childhood fantasies.
I want to feel your warmth,
but it seems you've been along a journey,
relization is the atempt to 
reconstruct my broken heart
that is just learning how to heal
on it's own,
with out substitute for you.
Am I still dreaming of control?
Or have I snared love not wanted,
and gained my peace of mind 
that is truely gone inside?
For I lie everytime, I say 
I don't want you in my arms.
