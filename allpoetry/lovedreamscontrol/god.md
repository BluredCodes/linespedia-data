god if your there i forgive you
i know my mom was a good woman
she wanted life 
and deserved to live
I've asked you why

maybe it all went wrong
the instinct of thought
the consequence of action 
you know what i mean
maybe you never wanted us
its a shame

speculation
why wont you answer me
I've called you a thousand times
and there was no answer

all those people who died?
oh yes i forgive you
just another tragedy unclaimed
another head stone in the sand
of the desert i call life

so what was your vision
of the clay in your hands
and if you died on the cross
you werent like me
you knew you were god
no matter
i forgive yousorry if this offends
