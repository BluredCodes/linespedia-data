I came to this alternate universe,
Where the stars were always falling.
I looked beyond this horizon,
And found you reaching out for me.
White-sanded beaches, like we always dreamed.
In Unison...
How long your journey has been.
You never knew I stood beside you,
Waiting for your destination.
You did get get distracted along the way,
Dreaming things you now behold,
With my heart bleeding in teeth all the while.
But now do you see my dream? 
The nights of leisure in a small bed,
Under a small roof.
We sing, while we may,
In Unison...
Welcome to the place,
Just close your eyes, 
And find the days that have past,
In our future.
We strive toward each other,
In Unison...long distance relationships don't work.
