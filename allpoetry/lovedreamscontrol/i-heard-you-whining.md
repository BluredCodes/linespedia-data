Hey was that you crying,
 now what has been so terrible for you?
 Hey I heard you whining
 and I think I've had enough of it this year
 
 Can you tell me where all that light has gone?
 It's been sucked inside your black hole soul.
 
 Yeah I hear you clearly 
 but I think I would rather put it down
 Can you tell me where all the substance goes
 draining down the tube
 with all your tears.
  
 Teeming with you fears,
 steaming with your doubts,
 dont you get sick of it yourself?
 
 It looks like in your world 
 you've been stuck out in the cold
 for all your life
 Hey now, look around,
 it's just a choice to feel as useless as you do.
 
 Hey I heard you whineing 
 and I think I've had enough of it this year.
