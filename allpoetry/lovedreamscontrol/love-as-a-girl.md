---
title: "Love as a Girl"
writer: "lovedreamscontrol"
slug: "love-as-a-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"

I will be her queen
and she will be mine.
With worship,
we will survive time.
If one of two should falter,
we will run, take cover,
in a secret place for lovers.
We would roll over,
in a rose petal bathing
of the love we are saving,
dispersed along our bodies
with sweet kisses,
we will be playing.
And, oh how I will dote
on love, my angel. 
Put flowers in her hair.
When she fails to notice,
I will show her I still care.
If she leaves, 
I would call to love crying,
trying
to get her back,
dying 
when it is her I lack.
If all survives
the test of time,
when I die
she will be on my mind,
in my heart.
She and I shall never part.
