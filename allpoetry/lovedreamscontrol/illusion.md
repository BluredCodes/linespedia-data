---
title: "Illusion"
writer: "lovedreamscontrol"
slug: "illusion"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17322-Illusion-by-lovedreamscontrol"

Find me with no answers you can be.Steal me away from my hole of insanity.Help me find my way out of this mystery.Enjoy me with deepest pleasures of humanity.Love me with a power that can't be real to me.I believe in you, take my pain away.Find you I've come without invitation.Steal you and magically you become my motivation.Help you with a fix of pure admiration.Enjoy you and become the epitome of every dictation.Love you feel it in every sensation.Believe in me and I'll take your pain away
