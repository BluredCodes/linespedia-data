Could you take my heart captive?
It is alive and beating,
so no man can break this love.
Just you and I
and our heavy breathing.
If it was just us,
you know I would be okay.
If you were the only one,
I would feel surrendered saftey,
to know that you would hold me
until you could do no more breathing...
beating...breaking.
Alone together.
Not so scared anymore.
No, not so scarred anymore.
When I kiss her mouth
it is your lips touching hers.
When I touch myself
it is your hand I await.
When I think on loneliness
it is your tears pouring down.
When I dream of love
it is your thoughts running through my head.
I am yours and yours alone.
All there is to do is wait
and how long can this be?
You for you, me for you,
all the way.This isn't too spicy ,I know, it's just that I don't like to offend...:)
