judgment day is heremy hand is on the gun your feeling quite amazedi can feel this endyou sink into your realityforgetting what you came to seeputting this system/machinebetween we, us, iwont diewill learn to glide even if there's no place to flyill be in semi circle spiral suckingslearning not to hideforget you were my friend once my peace of mindthat diedalive and burningthough we could begin where this wreck should endi came for you left sad faced and emptihandedwhere did you go?cold and six feet under
