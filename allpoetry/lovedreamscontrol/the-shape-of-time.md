While studying this wrymeof mine,you'll finda piece of mind, some shape in time.You'll stumble on my mind'strips and whipsand soon discovera number of dips and dripsfrom the corner and behind. Set asidefrom the outside.If I were to find a rideto bring you to my side,would you run and hide?Relax? Recline?Be jumbled the mix? Rewind.Take a minute from this trip,don't cry.Don't dieinside.It's nice,the shape of time, your peice of mind.
