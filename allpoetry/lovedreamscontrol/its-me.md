Again...darkness
Again...silence
Again...nothing

A solitary heartbeat,
a bit too slow.
One more distraction,
too much to go.
A solem wish, a fretful regret.

A somber saddened sickness
is rotting out my head...
feeling like we'er dying
wishing I was dead.
Zoning like a zombie
it's all the shit I am fed.

I am waiting for the ride that never comes.

I am the girl that felt too much to love.
