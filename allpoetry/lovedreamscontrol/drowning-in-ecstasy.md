title: "drowning in ecstasy"

Can you feel it?
My body is calling yours.
Come to me,
come to me.
Feel the heat
escaping from deep inside.
Limbs locked, a sweaty embrace.
Come to me, no need to hide.
Finding me,
Finding me.
Into the heat
building from my feet,
bending 'round your shoulders
straining from the heat.
You and I,
become some strange animal,
In my bed
