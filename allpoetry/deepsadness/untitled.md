baby, take me to that bed of yours
and cover my face w/ sweet kisses
gradually remove my clothing 
piece by piece
then look at my sizzling body
already waiting for you 
to seduce the pleasure into it
close those lips onto my spot
so that i sink lower
into your arms
slowly come up 
and lie beside me
so i can take you in
baby, give it all to me
so i can watch you enjoy yourself
erupt in my mouth 
so i can swallow your rhapsody
go back to the place
you know so well
and let yourself press against me
bringing yourself inside
for that ever so joyful ride
baby, make this head of mine
fall back on the pillow
as you propel yourself forward
drive my hands to your back
as you touch my face
w/ your lips
slow down and taunt me
w/ those alluring eyes
so that my pleasure elevates
kiss me w/ such a fire
that i feel a wave
of tumultous delight
thrust in and out
w/ a might unknown
to others
encourage my body to sweat
like no one has or will
have tears run down my face
from such delicious lust
then lay next to me
so our bodies
can shiver from ecstasy together
