title: "just another day"

images of death
grow inside me
taunting me
w/ their evil eyes
dreams of
surrendering to their wishes
looking back at innocence
knowing its warmth 
will never kiss my face
when i wake
the magic once there
has disappointed
around the bend,
given to other
girls to hold onto
so why may i ask
was i your conquest?
