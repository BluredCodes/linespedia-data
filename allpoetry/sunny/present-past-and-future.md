title: "Present, past,  and future"

Well here i sit fat and fifty.Gee it isn't so very nifty.Just ten plus years ago i was forty,With lots of energy lordy, lordy.And twenty years before, I was a sexy thirtyPeople said i was a little to flirty.Oh my, now i go back three score years to twenty,when the years ahead of me were plenty.Forty years before now I was in my teens,bobby socks and Levi jeans.Now I started this journey some fifty years past.It has gone by, my life, so, oh so, fast.Now looking back and looking at today, There is not a single thing i would take away,or change,or add to my lifes ups and downs.this is, what is, life's smiles and frowns.My only hope is that i get the chance in time,to reflect on this when i'm ninty-nine!!!
