---
title: "I BEG OF YOU"
writer: "Sunny"
slug: "i-beg-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1124-I-BEG-OF-YOU-by-Sunny"
---

My life's the pits what should i do?
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

I am so tired, I am so blue
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

I hate my neighbor, he is a jew
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

I stole a car cuse it was new
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

Been doing drugs and drinking too
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

Can't take this world, it is a zoo
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

I'd be better off dead I tell you true
TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

You want me to pray? that's something new
YES, TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

I don't know how, words go all askew
JUST TALK TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

But I am not worthy, if you only knew
GOD LOVES ME AND GOD LOVES YOU

Can it be that i am of some small value?
OH YES, TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

IT MATTERS NOT WHAT YOU SAY OR DO
JUST TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU

IN GODS HEART THERE IS NO TABOO
IF ONLY YOU ASK HE WILL LISTEN TO YOU

HE WILL FORGIVE ANYTHING YOU DO
PLEASE TURN TO GOD I BEG OF YOU
