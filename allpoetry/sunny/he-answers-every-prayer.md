The prayer:
           Please give me strength and energy to make it through the day.
The answer:
           From dawn to dusk a constant stream of chores did come my way. 
           I knew that I must do my best, 
           For not a minute did I have to rest.
           But God gave me what I asked, 
           And I finished each and every task.

The prayer:
           Please give me tolerance and patience for what might lay ahead.
The answer:
           From the start of my day to the end as I crawled into my bed
           One thing after another frayed at my nerves.
           Screaming children , rude people, burnt dinners.
           God answered the prayer from that morning
           My day had no anger. It, was not, annoying. 

Our prayers:
            Be careful how you pray tonight
            God answers every prayer.
            You may not recognize it  
            And you may not think its fair,
            But if you think  it  through,
            And  trust how much he cares,
            You will know that he has heard you,
            And answered all your prayers.
