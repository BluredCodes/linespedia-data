---
title: "HEART STOP"
writer: "Sunny"
slug: "heart-stop"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My heart my heart
dont stop beating
not today i am not done

tomorrow maybe then
i will be ready
today i have to run

today i have to say
i love you to all
of those i care for

today i have to hold
the baby and cook supper
and go to the store

today i have to clean
the house and wash
the dog and cat

today i have to watch
my soaps and read
and buy that new hat

today i have to find 
that shoe and toy
my son has lost

today i have to pay
the bills and go to
work at any cost

and when i am through
i have to sleep so i can
wake and do it all again

oh hell go right ahead
dear heart, STOP,
 you can 
you can 
you can
