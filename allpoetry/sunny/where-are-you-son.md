Where are you son?
Please don't hide.
I am so worried hon
I've looked so far and wide.

come out come out 
My precious little dear
You must be here about
For i smell your sweetness near

Don't tease me anymore
I do not like this game
Your mothers darling boy
You must here me call your name

Remember what you promised
You said "don't worry mom,
I'll alwas be here with you
No matter what gose wrong"

Well something is wrong
I can't find you there or here
You've been gone so very long
My hearts so full of fear

For thirty some-odd years
My arms held you tight
And sometimes dried your tears
And sometimes laughed all night

You told me secrets
And made me very proud
With your talented feats
Of artistic creations, my child

I miss you baby
With my weeping heart
And one day, maybe
We wont be apart

When I find you finaly
In your hiding place
And take you oh so gently
And kiss again your sweet face

I will be filled
With peace and joy
