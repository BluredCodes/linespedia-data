title: "A FAMILIAR VOICE"

Tonight I am not blue or sad
and I must tell you why.
The most wonderful thing just happened
out of the clear blue sky.

I was sitting in my favorite chair
musing about the day.
When suddenly I heard
a familiar voice call out
my name and say.

"Hi mom, what are you doing?
Thought I might drop by
and visit with you a while.
I wanted to say how much I love you
"oh mom don't you cry".

I've come by to tell you 
that evethings ok
and I'm very happy now.
I live in the most beautiful place
you will see it someday.

And when you get there, I'll
be waiting just for you,h
I'll put my arms around you mom
and never let you go, like i do now,
'cuse that,IS, what i do.

It is hard for you to understand
that I am with you all the time
becuse until you get there 
you can't imagine what is 
yours and mine.

I wish I could stay longer
but I really must go on home.
Please mom don't be sad.
You can't see me but you can feel me
You are NEVER, EVER alone..........."

Then he was gone and strangely I felt not sad 
but peaceful and relived.
My darling son was happy in a place
I'ed one day be

Now I can not tell you if I was asleep
and saw him in my dreams.
Or if I was wide awake,
but i care not for it was real to me
and I know what it means.

(yes darling I can feel you now,and I am not afraid
to let you go ahead and wait for mothers final day.)
