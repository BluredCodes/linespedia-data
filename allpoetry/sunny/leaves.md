title: "LEAVES"

Does it make you sad to see a leaf descending from a tree?Does it hurt your heart to know that life is gone when it lets go?Do not weep when autumn nears, this is Gods way my dearsTo show us that we all must die, for better things for us do lieJust as dead leaves enrich the earth, as do our souls at rebirthWhen we see our final days we to will enrich with praiseOur Fathers house for-evermore this is his way its what's in store
