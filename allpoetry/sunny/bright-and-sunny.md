title: "BRIGHT AND SUNNY"

Bright and sunny 
What a day!
Happy joyful
lets go play.

You, gloomy Gus,
Can come along
Bet you'll be smiling
Before to long.

I tell you now,
I tell you true,
we're all to serious
Fun times are few.

Come one come all!
Now don't be shy,
Relax a little 
Before you die.
