Well I got this song about something wrongthe  Way we’re living todayEverybody’s got something to cry aboutThe whole worlds singing the blues hey. heyThe whole worlds singing the bluesYou can hear it from Maine down to Texas land Their singing The  blues Lord any way they   canThe rich man the poor manThe little kids tooThe whole worlds singing the bluesGod it breaks my heart it just tares me apartto see the violence and the crime,You read it in the news And the stories are trueThe whole worlds singing the blues my, myThe whole worlds singing the bluesNow I don’t know why and I don’t understandWhy this goes on throughout our landBut I do know this and  I tell you trueI won’t join in and neither should youWhile the whole world sings the blues.
