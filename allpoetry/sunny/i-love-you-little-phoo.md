I love you little Phoolaughing all the time when she speaks her rosy cheeksturn into valentinesHer eyes so green and big and brightsee many wondrous thingsit makes you wish that you just mightjoin her in her dreamsWhen she cries tears in her eyeswill break your heart intobut when you kiss the hurt away her eyes say I love youYes little Phoo I love you you fill my life with gleeyour in a place within my heartthats special as can beThis is dedicated to my niece. She is autistic and can not speak nor can she take care of herself, I wrote this when she was very young she is twenty nine now and when I sing it to her she still loves it.
