(TO THE CO-WORKERS WHO DID NOT ASK ME TO GO)Ok  girls go on and have your funDon’t think about leaving that poor someoneDrive right across that little old Texas lineDon’t give a thought to the gal you left behind.Feed those slots and roll them diceHoller twenty-one now isn’t that niceBut don’t worry bout me that would'nt’t be rightI’ll be fine all alone back in the heights.Now I’m not sad and I’m not blueBut I’m sure jealous of the two of you,Now that’s ok cause you know its funnyWhen you come home broke, I’ll still have my money
