---
title: "So Subtle"
writer: "redmoon"

The obsenities, they've
ruined my serenity, I've
never noticed so much-pain,
until now.
The dirty looks, you've
attached to me, just
because I'm different
it doesn't make you god like.
So keep your judgements
subtle, 'cause
I don't care enough for them to
stand out.
Keep the loud colors of your lies to 
a minimum, oh, 
because my favorite color is
black.
My favorite color is black,
not just 'cause I'm depressed but, 
'cause you piss me off, you
always screamed at me, you've
made me ashamed 
to even know your name.
And I've heard it all before,
I can't stand it anymore!
You've thrown me out, and
pushed me to the ground,
and the last was the 
last time.
You're gone.
Gone
Gone without a trace. So subtle remains your
embrace.
Apology unaccepted.
