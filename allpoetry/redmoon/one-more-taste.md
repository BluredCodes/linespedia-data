---
title: "One More Taste"
writer: "redmoon"
slug: "one-more-taste"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23642-One-More-Taste-by-redmoon"
---

Drop you knife,
stop your hate;
collect my 
tears-
sell them as ocean water. All
I want is a 
taste.
Another taste of your
saltiness, of your 
bitterness.
I crave you as I
always have, if not
more now than
usual.
I've never felt like this before
you- I
fear you realize this.
If you do- you'd know how
infactuated I've
become.
HOw sureal my world is
when I think of you.
Keep your distance-
you'll be proud of yourself
when it's over.
I can almost 
hear 
you reject me already.
I just crave 
one more taste.
One more moan of your satisfaction.does it flow? does it make any sense at all?
