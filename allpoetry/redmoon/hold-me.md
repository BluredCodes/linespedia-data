title: "Hold Me"
writer: "redmoon"

You wanna try me out
but you don't know where to start.
You wanna eat me out
of my shellful space.
The time is near,
dramatic pause composed.
And you've seen me get this far,
now it's your turn to shove.

Shove me.
Shove me.

This life:
Bitter,
Fucking tragedy.
Understandable and true,
we're bleeding.
Our time is near,
dramatic pause composed.
We're all dying.
evil, poisonous, malicious
this world proves.
Prove.
Prove the unjust free of
redemption.
Oh just fuck me, 
let me stop breathing.
Hold my soul in your hands and
grip tightly,
Grip tightly.
For you never know when you'll have to
let go.
