I feel like God's 
pulling me in both directions.
Not knowing anything
but of my past progressions.
I cannot
live this life
with so much pain and
Hollow.
When will I love, suffice
and let the rain still follow.
I learned to love the 
Rain
again,
coulda been much worse, 
coulda been my end.
I learned to love my
Self
again,
shoulda been perverse
shoulda been the end.
And I'm still drowning in
my sorrow of absolution.
I never knew that I could
mend this way.
And I'm lost in all my
effervescent lost emotion.
I never knew my soul was
possible of such decay.I, as always, didnt concentrate on the rhyme scheme, however when re-reading I noticed a scatered pattern, is this bad?
