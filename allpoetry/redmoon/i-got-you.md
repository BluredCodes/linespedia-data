---
title: "I Got You"
writer: "redmoon"

You found me in the mud.
Sweater zipped up, rain proves you
superior.
Looking down on me, so dirty.
You didn't know what to say.
All you could do was stare, not knowing why
you looked so abruptly.
Thoughts of me pain stakingly still 
lingered;
and I'm falling in love in spite of
your bitter toungue.
My ways shown in a funnel of 
soul;
mind taught with anger of an
unkept time.
Space between us obvious yet
undesired.
I got you. He
has someone else.
