Emebrs fall from 
the sky, I
open my mind to a brand new side, watch
the way the clouds swerve as an empty bird flies
away from me.
My hope diminishes, then returns with each
inhale, my
body's deep in denial of the penetration felt of 
grand scale.
My soul's never been this 
torn
before, never been so badly abused, I
feel as though I'm dying 
inside,
never been quite this confused. My heart is
racing as he creeps by,
trying to seem invisble-why?-
i never understood the message you bring, and if only
my fingers would stop typing this thing,
this thing in which i like to call my love,
god-sent words sent from high above,
i hope i can get though this place,
this earth to me is a 
hollow space.
