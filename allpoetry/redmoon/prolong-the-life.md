---
title: "Prolong The Life"
writer: "redmoon"

We'll lay under the stars,
my friends,
the clouds,
who are my friends, too.
We'll entangle eachother in one another's
limbs,
threatening to never let go.
We'll lay, witnessed by these
stars, by these
clouds, the moon will feel nothing but joy for our
union.
Just lay with me like this 
forever, 
I want to always feel your
heart
so close.
Our seperation comes oh too soon,
yet we'll lay here in hopes
to prolong the life of our love,
withstand, 
endure society's wrath.
Because we're of the same
sex
we're banned from one another,
and secluded into our own lifeless chamber.
But baby,
my heart will always contain you,
and only you,
for you are the one whom has not
failed me.
You've captivated me,
and forever I shall remain
yours.
So let's just lie here,
entangled, intertwined,
and prolong the life of our
love
for as long as time permits.is it too vague?
