You know you're so
beautiful.
Why try?
I need someone capatible, but
none catch my eye.
If noly things were escapable,
I'd leave this all behind.
In my my thoughts my heart decays,
spirit in less design.
I know I only have one chance
to prove myslef to you.
But doesn that mean I run the risk
of learning to be true?
I'll try to hide behind my face,
but I want to be new.
And thought It's unmistakable,
the one I want is you.
