---
title: "Rust (short but sweet)"
writer: "redmoon"
slug: "rust-short-but-sweet"
source: "AllPoetry"

Nobody told me this was a 
possibility,
No one said this was the trend.
No one had any answers for me, 
and now its abruptly coming to the end.
It happened so suddenly,
this phase, this lust,
this infatuation of a new-found trust.
Never had I imagined myself capable of such,
an admiration quickly gone to rust.
