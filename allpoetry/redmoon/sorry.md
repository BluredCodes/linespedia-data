title: "Sorry"

Increadibly sorry
I wasn't good enough.
Unfortunately, this time
I'm not lying to myself.
You had to stray,
you had better things to do,
better people to fuck.
i'm sorry I ever let you ruin my day.
I'm sorry you were too supreme compared to me;
I know you are better looking, 
but what ever happened to people being
non-superficial
these days?
I hurt, I ache, with the
torcher
of knowing I've lost your
embrace, lost your kiss,
lost your beautiful blue
eyes.
