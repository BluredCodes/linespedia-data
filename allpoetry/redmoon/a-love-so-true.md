Find a spot in my haert,
dig a hole,
find my whole,
seek a start.
Never knew i could be marked
as a dart
of humility,
prosperity lingers in
reality,
brutality's
inviting me-
to another place,
another space, I
can feel it,
feel it inside of me, knowing 
generosity, finding exactly
who i:m allowed to be,
not knowing my spirituality,
not considering
my christianity
Not following my
taught philospphies,
not remembering my
past identities,
not commending my
sociality.
Oh, and I can feel it,
it's creeping out inside of me,
this new found freedom 
exciting, free;
I just know it wont be the end
of me, 
I KNow it'll be the new beginning of
a new generation of hope, Love
lingers and its fingers,
they grasp, oh so tight, this
new life,
its withholding, all my
endurance flowing, showing
no emotion,
swelling every ocean, pouring
out a new potion:
A Love So True.
