---
title: "Drowning"
writer: "Ice Cubism"

Down here eyes lie blind, one with the sand	
Outside it’s quiet and numb, hushing the screams
Have patience; please come and hold my hand

Too far to fall short, please understand
Living denial of life, hungry for dreams
Down here eyes lie blind, one with the sand

Less love and more pain, strand by loose strand
Falling from above and dumb, breaking the seams
Have patience; please come hold my hand.

Wake me up and cry, help me withstand
Begging for help just a crumb, floating in streams
Down here eyes lie blind, one with the sand.

So where are you now, make me demand
Thirsty for love and light, searching more means
Have patience; please come hold my hand.

Come tell me I’m wrong, invert my plan
Drowning I sink and succumb, I’m dead it seems
Down here eyes lie blind, one with the sand.
Have patience; please come hold my hand
