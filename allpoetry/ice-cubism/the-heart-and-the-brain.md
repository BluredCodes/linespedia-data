---
title: "The heart and the brain"
writer: "Ice Cubism"

The heart, and the brain
together they strain
living as one
never are they done,
hating myself while they're playing their game

Flowing with life
but causing my strife
Never is it tame
my heart plays this game,
if only thoughts and feelings were one in the same

Spinning it's gears
without smiles or tears
the brain cannot feel
and only see's what is real,
hating myself while they're playing their game

Contempt for one another
never one or the other
like day and night
but neither ones right,
if only thoughts and feelings were one in the same

Perhaps passionate thought
is neither learned or taught
and a logical feeling
is something worth stealing,
if only thoughts and feelings were one in the same,
hating myself while they're playing their game.stupid llamas
