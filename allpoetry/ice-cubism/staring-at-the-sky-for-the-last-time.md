title: "Staring at the sky for the last time..."
writer: "Ice Cubism"

Try to restore it
but missing a piece
swallowed away
with my inner peace
searing my eyes
and burning my mind
painfully searching
for parts i can't find
lost somewhere in time
back when i still knew
how my heart would chime
when thinking of you
breaking my back
and crippling my spine
a relentless attack
but outside i'm fine
stinging itself dry
this void in my chest
breathing hard as i try
to remain at rest
i'll put it together
whole once again
but in time it will weather
my soul will weaken
fall apart slowly
on the cold dirt of the land
rotting pale and lowly
in that final yet whole sand
