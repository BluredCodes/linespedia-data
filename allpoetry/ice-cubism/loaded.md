---
title: "Loaded"
writer: "Ice Cubism"
slug: "loaded"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49469-Loaded-by-Ice-Cubism"
---

We can't be together
you must find another
longing for no other
but loathing eachother

Exploited we're toxic
the black fire from a wick
on a dark candle stick 
deadly,twisted,and sick

Are we harmless apart?
or just waiting to start
our perfected grey art
of a tempted black heart

Who's to blame when it's done?
when we take shots then run
who's to blame for the fun?
the bullets or the gun?
