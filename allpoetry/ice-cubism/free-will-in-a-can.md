title: "Free Will In A Can"

Welcome to teenage wasteland
take off your coat, stay awhile
we're all friends here understand
friends of neglect and denial
and yet...still i ponder,
where is home?

The cracked streets our homeley homes
shy of love but lacking tears
as minds of the young roam
crying through cold pain and fears
and yet..still i wonder,
what is love?

Hate's brother, friendship's daughter
the maker and the breaker
which drains the weak of water
and persuades him to take her
and yet...though we're under
why fight on?

If we don't, silence lives on
that heartless wraith of sunlight,
and when all of heart are gone,
fills the sky faster then night
and yet...through our thunder
can we live on?
