Frame:
        lines smooth like overpriced caterers
        taking the right things
        to the right places
        & always dodging just in time
        height quite right
        a ruler to measure perfection by
        if gods should ever find themselves 
        wanting of such

Paint:
        liquid smooth in application
        like a lion on the serenghetti
        heavy forepaws resting on well-deserved alms
        logical infusion of colors
        a taste of what the masters meant
        but could not convey

Picture:
        abstraction
        concussive streams of seamless being
        slipping by with scientific ease of reason
        seemingly perfectly
        youDon't look for very many other "love" poems out of me...  these days, I'm about as un-sentimental as an artist can get.  This was written for my exgirlfriend before she held the office of ex and was simply my girlfriend.  She was not flattered by the poem:  not one for pretty talk.  But I still like it, if not the subject.  Maybe I'll present it to my next girlfriend, who will undoubtedly seem (to me) just as beatiful...  any takers?
