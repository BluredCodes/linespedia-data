in the night of a thousand wishes
we crossed our fingers and wondered why nothing was happening
when we looked up at the sky
and prayed deeply 
like the souls of people who had passed on
while the music was playing

in the night of a thousand wishes
we turned our heads to the south
and wondered if there was something better
waiting for us beyond the distant horizon

in the night of a thousand wishes
we were talking about how those people who used to sing
never come to see us anymore
and wait for their loves
to return to them
in the isolation of wishing for something more

every night
the night of a thousand wishes

in the night of a thousand wishes
we wandered aimlessly through the forest
waiting for the moths to carry us off
and chrysalis into sleep so happy
waiting for the fireflies to light the way to nowhere

in the night of a thousand wishes
we talked with sweat beading on our brows
and felt our way around in the dark
(tired of waiting for the firefilies)
a little girl was crying for her mother
and a little boy was wondering about the future
and thinking that maybe it wouldn't be there

in the might of a thousand wishes
every night is the night of a thousand wishes
