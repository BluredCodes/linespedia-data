so it was nighttime-
that's where the beginnings end, 
swirl with the face of the moon
on still waters
& flee to become another day
equine the girl's colt legs upon
her shivering return
the chrysalis we built of kind words
was now running in rivulets
of colors meshed & mixed
like mingled blood in separate veins
transluscent twilight
the introspective boy who waited
twiddling his thoughts
like an army of broken thumbs
is now a part of that night
the people the crowd the voice united
like a river snake their way
past the bristling lines of blue
soon the still waters
stir with a feast for the sharks
look out below
