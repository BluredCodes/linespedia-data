title: "unmarked"
writer: "The CanarY"

listen little fairy
i'll fly you off to the moon on dragon wings
that beat the air like tattered insults
and i'll nail your feet to the ground
so you can never reach the water
never touch the sunshine
i'll wear your wings until you can't see them
and find myself a better maria
sifting through the sands of the gobi
with a pencil and a wish
i'll tie the moon to the stars
and think it far from my heart
until the silver fish leap out of the deepest pond
and call my name
call my name and hope me back
i'll go swirling into the fractured universe
like a little lost comet 
crying as it speeds away
listen little fairy
i know where they hide the helicopters
i know where they hide the helicopters
i know where they hide the helicopters
and i can't find the ground
