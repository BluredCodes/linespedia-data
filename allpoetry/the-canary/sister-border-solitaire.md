"an awful lot" lemon
                     pucker
lime pie
she paired her fingers
wistful sisters sitting out
                     the super nova
on two royal blue love seats
& managing all the while
you looked away at the traffic
flowing speech of a stttttutterhead boy
to sit there legs crossed like a bank vault
and knit you
            a sock
            a sweater
            a kindly heart
            a footrest a fig leaf
a hideaway
to brandish your contempt
like fluttering lillies
saying with blink blink eyes
