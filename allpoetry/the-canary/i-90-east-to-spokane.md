the whocars spin their
                                          prettywheels
the whykids blow their
                                          nose
the seeme dolls can't fix their 
                                          eyes
but know one always 
                                          no's

the whatgirls fix their
                                          prettyhair
the meyous shine their
                                          gold
the margins always seem to
                                          shake
until the story's 
                                          told
