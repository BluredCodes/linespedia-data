i could have paved her streets
in fine thin sheets of diamond
weeping rainbows from face to face
encased her worries & world-weary 
lack of levity in heaviest suits of gold
sunk them deep in silent waters
to land in atlantis among the ivory spires
i could have
instead i held her feathers firmly
invisible injustice held her down
pinned her down to the ground i discovered
hovered above her cowering
and starting to take rootSometimes, as badly as we want to be the best person that we can be, we're only able to be ourselves.
