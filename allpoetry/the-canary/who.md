moreover & importantly
than
father
        brother poet lover
cynic smiler
time wasting
        father
brother
sonofabitch
sassafrass roots firmly planted
walker gazer
        (stars so lovely)
bather in the distant
waterfall
        (sweet glide slide guitar)
moonlight soaker
runner hider
fugitive
moreover & importantly
one gaping huge allconsuming
iThe bit about the "sweet glide slide guitar" is actually a reference to the Jimi Hendrix tune "Waterfall."
