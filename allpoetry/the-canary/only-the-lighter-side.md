title: "only the lighter side"

small wonder the sky's so jealous
blushing neatly at its own dawn
when the pastures below
are able to march in such a neat formation
of postage stamp green
dinner plate trees
all the way back to the spaghetti grey road
small wonder the sun's hung itself
in a considerate suicide
allowing the ivy to stretch its arms
and learn all about 
the finely laid red of brick
that it always comes to call home
small wonder your smile is wide and inviting
waiting to drink up
the sun and the sky and the fields
that wave their hair in kiss me circles
saying "hello hello hello"
