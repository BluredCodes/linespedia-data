if the world were dying
a little faster maybe than it already is
and getting ready to sigh its last sigh
like a child never getting what it wants
until the feeling means nothing
i'd want your hand in mine
and a road forever in front of us
if we had to die alone
we could go for a walk 
and watch the bugs fuck
until the sky explodes with one last sunset
and i look at you
goodbye goodbye
on your lips
and it's over
