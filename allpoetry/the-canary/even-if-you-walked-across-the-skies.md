sweet violet 
summer coming in off the horizon
lifting the heads of forlorn lillies
with the softest touch of breeze
find them in their quiet garden
shivering 
when the night crawls in on tired knees
and begs for a place to stay
sweet violet
like an emperor who cannot speak
i know you
i know you
