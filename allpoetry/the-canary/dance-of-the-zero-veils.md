couldn't wait to get things started
left the broken far behind
wings flapping like the jaw of rusty scisors
one last try at flying
then abandoned on the ground
the mirage fades swiftly
between the rain and the light
as the children go running away
into the trees
altered spirits flailing
what's the use of sunshine
when your eyes are filled with clouds?

couldn't wait to get things over
brought the freshly written smiles
a book full of lies
pages flapping like the wings of a canary
broken in the corner
thirsting for the last mirage
wear a pretty face
a happy love me can't leave face
and tell me a story that lives forever
never been to see the sunshine
from out in space
and i never will
