title: "red dress logic"
writer: "The CanarY"

when i was younger,
i had so much to say
about a world i knew nothing about.
i've still a thing to say, 
but you can't hear me...
...i'm crouched down in a tunnel
underground, 
a worm that consoles itself
with the music of the dead.
i've taken to the piano, 
where i can sing without my voice as well.
in a velvet lined basket, 
time brings me the gift of imperative.
someday, it will all be too late.
did you hear the wind at your front door
asking to be let in?
when the ash of the last city
settles itself in our hair, 
we will begin to move again.
we will breathe and smile
and blink appropriately at
confusing ideas.
then, i will tell you
what it was all for.
