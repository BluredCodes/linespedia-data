When the 
           ashes settled back into the ground
& the people who were 
           hiding 
came back out
into the light,
there wasn't really anything left to 
           see.  A streetcorner five 
blocks down 
was 
          all there was left for the postal industry to 
write sad sad Letters Home 
about.  A few looked 
          around, 
and said, "So
what... isn't this the 
song we were waiting for?"  Commenced to
dance in the rubble of the 
          gone 
                   gone world.My first (and certainly last) "political" poem.  How did that song go?  "All we are saying is..."
Maybe when the monkeys in suits that tell us what to do from behind the pristine lawn of their white houses have finally been forgotten, the scars of their charade might be worth living in.
