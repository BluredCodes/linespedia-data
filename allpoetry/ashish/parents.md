---
title: "Parents"
writer: "Ashish"
slug: "parents"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50896-Parents-by-Ashish"
---

Oh! what a wounderful relationship this is
the relationship we have with our parent's
their hearts full of love & forgiveness
their only concern is the well being of their kids,
their only wish is that their kids be happy forever...
Oh! what a wounderful relationship this is
that the Almighty God left Heaven to experience this...
