title: "I have a friendship with You"

I have a friendship with you  a friendship with you that Works 
That works without any agreement  without any condition being  imposed
A friendship which does not ask any questions a  friendship which does not  require any explanation….
A friendship which nourishes with the joy of another … 
A friendship which is based on my present condition …
A friendship which does not want Me to change
A friendship which accepts Me as I am…
Want nothing in Me to change…
Oh! Lord – My best friend, Thank you! 
Thank you for letting me know this.
