lets pretend
i am not just a girl,
and i am an individual too.
lets pretend 
i am not just here to fuck,
and i am here to be respected.
lets pretend
i am not opinionated,
and i am passive and easily swayed.
lets pretend
i am not strong,
and i am here only for you.

lets pretend i actually like lying to myself.
