inject the infection 
to hide the disease
cover it in camouflage
so no one can see

looking through stained glass
to have a different view
covering the scars
to be better than you

acid dripping off my tongue
now swallow the flame
poison all the children
to have someone to blame

no good anymore
rip out my heart
all i ever wanted
is tearing me apart
