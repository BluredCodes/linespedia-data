weakness.something i don't cope with welli don't like being perceived as weaki see it as a flawa blemish in my charactera small holewaitingto be picked ati used to be weaki'd get tossed aroundthrown aroundpushed downand i'd let it allhappenover and overso i put up a wall i don't want it brokennow there's a boythe wall still standsthe only weaknessi show is my fearto let him in.
