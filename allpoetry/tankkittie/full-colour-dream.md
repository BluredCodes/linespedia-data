i see you in the city
wiping smog from your soul
glancing in the endless windows and pretentious minds
paying to go from east to west
north to south
here to there
realizing there is here
your infinate dreams screaming
your thoughts dying at noon
your art of falling to pieces
your song of liberation
tiny dreams flowing overboard
the unwritten monologue of fortification from the twelve step programs

and you look so beautiful 
in technicolour
turned black and white.
