sitting calmy
pretty, pretty
hush my words
and take my pity
steal my soul
and melt my ice
i'll take your heart
and fold it thrice

standing slowly
perfect, perfect
sell my words
just like a merchant
see through me
like a sheet of glass
kiss my lips
and die at last

hands touching
waiting, waiting
hold me tighter
but see me fading
black as heaven
bright as hell
walk away now
ignore my yell

sitting calmy
pretty, pretty
i have no words
nor self-pity
