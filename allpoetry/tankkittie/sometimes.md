title: "sometimes."
writer: "tank.kittie"

sometimes
i'd rather you say
nothing
then say what you
dont mean
cause you think 
it's what i
want to hear.
sometimes
i'd rather you feel
nothing
because you seem to take
things the wrong way
and get hurt.
sometimes
i'd rather you do
nothing
then try so hard
for the things
that dont mean
anything.

sometimes i wish you could just read my mind.
sometimes i wish the impossible.
