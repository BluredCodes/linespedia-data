---
title: "what now?"
writer: "tank.kittie"

sometimes everything is lost when freedom is gained,
yet everyone still hopes tomorrow is like yesterday.

everyone wants a piece of you for peace of mind,
but no one notices when youre purging yourself away to nothing.

all of you seem to believe you arent alone just because the radio gives off enough light to comfort.

you're frantically trying to buy into teenage angst and/or rebellion,
but theyre movements and emotions that cant be bought into.

today you wake up and make a promise to do something that will change tomorrow...
tomorrow you wake up and are too lazy to even put your promises off until the next day.

a rose no longer holds as much beauty as packaged products,
called "NSYNC" and "BRITNEY".

the new york city skyline doesnt even illuminate anymore.

falling in love with exploited by the one being fallen for.

you're raised to please at any cost.

makes you wonder what the next big thing will be and how long it will take for it to sell out.
