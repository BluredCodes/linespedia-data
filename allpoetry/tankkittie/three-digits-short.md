he slipped up.
he fell down.
no more worries,
he mutters, 
as he shoots up
and gets lost
while finding his way
back to azure skies
that hold his trust.
scattered words
slurred through
the receiver pleading
for a connection
to what he left.
who he left.
numbers given three
digits short
stand as proof
that strength is 
something
that can easily be
lost.my uncle -was- clean for five years.
