---
title: "permanent imagination."
writer: "tank.kittie"

my empty bedsheets are cold underneath my skin
and the light won't seem to turn off 

i swear i can see my breath in here 

this comforter doesn't comfort
false advertisment on their behalf

i swear i can hear you move in here 

the echoing chorus is 
that i am living in permanent imagination

i swear i saw you smile 

am i really going mad?
