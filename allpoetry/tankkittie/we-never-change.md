stop playing jesus christ to my virgin mary
i cannot process anything with all this stuck in my spine
it all adds up when you hit the bottom
it makes it hard to climb back out
makes it hard to do anything but wade around
we never change
do we
i still sin every single day
how are you living life as a felon
non convicted 
no trial
no burning at the cross

but in the mind youre up on that cross
nails and thorns youre only defense
