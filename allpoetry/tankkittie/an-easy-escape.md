and so it sat
proud and taunting.
my eye traced every curve
of the bottle;
every onze of liquid
screaming for comsumption.
temptation hung heavily
in the air.
i ached for one shot,
one taste to burn
down my throat
and into my stomach.
i wanted that almost
replusive after-taste.
i wanted that buzz
and i wanted it more
than ever.
and so i gave in.
like i always do.
vodka as they call it,
is my companion
throughout the day.
empty bottles hidden
in various corners of my room
are constant reminders
of my weakness
for an easy escape.
