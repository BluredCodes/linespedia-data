for some reason
he reminded me of
winter.
beautiful as
it enters,
but bitter as
it lingers on.
crisp at the first
snowfall,
but painful as
it's wind stings
your face.
pleasant at the
first meeting,
but tiresome as
the days pass.

and so i ask,
when will spring 
arrive?
