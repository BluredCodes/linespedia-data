What is it that you wanted again?
Peace of mind,
Piece of (my) mind,
I'm a simple (lacking depth) soul,
Heartbreak Girl,
Victim of a boy,
Chased by el Diablo,
The one adorned with a white Nirvana shirt and Buddhist literature.
You will see,
Oh will you ever see,
As I once did but shut the curtains when it started to blind me,
You'll see soon enough.
But it is too late for me,
I took the last exit before I would have been in Banality.
So now I am dwelling under the tinted window and beat up body of my massacre on wheels,
Glancing in the rearview mirror occasionally,
Just making sure,
Just making sure,
Just making sure he didn't follow my trail of dirt.
I am running,
And running,
Hearing Ani in my dreams,
But the scenery never changes.
Sometimes I stop,
For those split seconds I take note and wonder what was my tragic flaw,
I am the protagonist.
Dean says it was my existence,
Allen mentions my stature,
Bret just goes on about her undying love,
Vincent paints me his answer,
And his is the only one I can actually decipher.
Far be it for me to deny his beauty.
Jack slowly opened the door and slid in the passenger's side,
Where we going, Jack?
I don't know but we gotta go.
