title: "i cannot stop it."

every time you take the pittance of your pivotal thoughts, add zero and then divide by one, that is what it is.
it is measured and doubted and taken back whenever my heart becomes the center of all things gravitational and, like the sun, threatens to pull in everything and burn, burn, burn.

and i cannot stop it.
and you cannot hear it.
and rushing it does not help.

your mindset is the bowl of batter that sits beside my preheated oven.
but you are also that fire alarm that keeps me awake at midnight.
you are all of it and some of everything. 
you're so much and nothing all at once; 
    the screaming madman they all wanted to lock up; 
    the breath of sanity and sprinkle of irrational thoughts.

but you cannot hear it.
and rushing it does not help.
and i cannot stop it.
