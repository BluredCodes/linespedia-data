---
title: "no isn't always enough"
writer: "tank.kittie"

no.
such a simple phrase
that to some
has no meaning.
they toss it
aside
and do as they please.
forcing
and 
fumbling,
avoiding my pleading eyes.
i know 
my words can
be heard.
i know that
i am not caught
in a moment leaving
me silent.
i say it over
and over.
i struggle to
cover myself.
to push away.
to push him away.
i am covered in
disgust and 
soiled in
thoughts that maybe
'no' isn't always enough
to let someone
know they are not wanted.
