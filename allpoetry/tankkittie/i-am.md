i am a tank with no army. 
i am a star with sixteen points and no sky to shine in. 
i am a slut kissed princess with acid burnt love. 
i am a mind-fucking metaphor with stainless steel balls. 
i walk with the wisemen and speak lies of the truth. 
i can stop at any time, i simply chose not to.for school. nothing major.
