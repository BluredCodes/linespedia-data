---
title: "nothing more than regret"
writer: "tank.kittie"

i don't know enough to just walk
i don't say enough to just know
he's supposed to read my mind
and leave me alone
but he can't 
he won't
won't
i don't want him
to know me
to want me
to touch me
to feel me

but he has

drunk from apathy and lust for another
i gave into him
with him
and i've regretted it ever since
you see i am not supposed to regret
i don't know how to go about doing it

cliches are unimportant here
as are your comments

all this is
is a girl
the experience
the regret
her thoughts
