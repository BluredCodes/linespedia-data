he'd tell me i was beautiful,
and tie back my drooping hair before i went home to sleep, after a night of him messing it all up.

he'd sputter words of elegance,
and twirl me through the kitchen as if we were magnificant ballet dancers on a lighted stage in some old theatre.

he'd speak of all the love he had,
that he promised wouldn't leave, no matter what happened after he moved away from me...

and to think i actually fell for something so intangible as words. ha.
