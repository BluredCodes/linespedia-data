the overwhelming scent of coffeeinvades my nasal passagesas i walk into work.the sneaky eyes of maleswatching as i bring them menus.the need to vomitas they winkand request a side of gravy.the way they slidecrumpled paper with phone numbersscribbled in faint crayonacross the sticky tables,makes me feel cheap.the manner in whichthey think i can be purchased on the menu, along with their chocolate cake,makes me want topoint to my plastic name tagand insist i am not only a female,but a person as well.
