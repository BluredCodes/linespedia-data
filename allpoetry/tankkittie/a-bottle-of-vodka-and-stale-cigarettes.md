waking up to 
a bottle of vodka
and stale cigarettes.
trying to recall
what you did the
night before.
straining to read 
the numbers
flashing red on the clock.
examining the bruises
the cuts
and the emptiness
while in the shower.
finding new faults
and more reasons
to not care.
waking up to 
a bottle of vodka
and stale cigarettes
and not wanting 
anything more
than an explaination.
