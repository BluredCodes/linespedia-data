he talked in sweet language she described as delicious
his prose was like poetry 
and his eyes were caverns into hidden valleys
that she admired from miles away
he smiled with a genuine smirk and mischief 
was ever so prominent
hatred and malice were never his outlets toward
the fucked up things occurring in the world today
he was pure and true 
and completly unattainable
maybe this is because she dreamt 
him into exsistence.
