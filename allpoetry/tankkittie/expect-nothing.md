by definition,
i am a female.
however,
dispite popular belief,
i will not let a boy
push me around,
throw me around,
or tell me what to do.
i will drink beer from
the bottle
and not from a glass.
i will belch afterwards as well.
i will wear white shirts with
black bras
and say the word fuck
without hesitation.
i wont match my bra to
my panties
and i will never think i 
have to do anything 
in order to gain affection.
nothing should be an oligation.
it seems now a days,
with all our politically correct
terms and actions,
there is still too much
that girls feel they -need- to do.
give head, write cute notes, have perfect hair.
its bullshit and i wont take it 
any longer.
i've experienced too much 
and see too much shit.
its not worth it.
i dont care if you call me
a slut
or a tight bitch.
i dont care for your false judgements.
i just want to see people
get along.
both male and female.
we think sexism is obsolete,
but its not.
its still present.
hidden behind all we thought
was safe.
i see it everyday.
i've had enough.
i am a female.
accept that
and expect nothing.
