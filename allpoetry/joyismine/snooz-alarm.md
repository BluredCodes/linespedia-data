title: "Snooz alarm"
writer: "joyismine"

I roll from side to side, 
place the pillow over my head,
and as I toss and turn
and wonder why I went to bed.

I fine tune the temperature,
turn the radio off and on,
put ear plugs in both my ears, 
and softly grunt and moan;

I've drank "Sleepy Time"
and other herbal teas galore;
I've laid on pillows soft and thick, 
and even tried the floor.

I roll my wife from side to side,
and she comes rolling back;
I've counted sheep so many times,
but I keep losing track.

I've had it up to here,
I can take no more,
as I lie awake night after night
and hear my good wife snore.
	
			Joyce GuyI wrote this from my husbands point of view as I snore and he does not.  Although we eventually worked out an agreeable solution (ever hear of a noise maker?) I kept him awake a few times.  He is a good guy, and made the best of a bad situation.
