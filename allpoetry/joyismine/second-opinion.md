The doctor said I was too fat; 
The weight would have to go.
I said to him "Dear Doctor,
There is something you should know.

A second opinion is required 
to give me peace of mind;
So I checked the yellow pages
to see what I could find.

If my weight will be discussed
Experts should be consulted
So I gave the matter serious thought 
And this is what resulted.

The second opinion will be done
by the most qualified contestant;
One who would not betray my trust,
The chef from my best restaurant."

			Joyce Guy
