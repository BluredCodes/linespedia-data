AWFULLY THIN

It must be awful 
to be so thin
That you have to eat lots
over and over again.

They say metabolism 
is your secret weapon,
But I want to know,
How does it happen?

You can eat ice-cream
bowl after bowl,
loaded off with toppings
delicious and cold.

You can eat cake,
two pieces of pie
piled with whipped cream
full of calories high.

Your waistline stays trim,
Your cholesterol low,
no matter how idle 
or how old you grow.

So please tell me, Why,
When I eat like a cat,
my lipids go up,
and my body gets fat.
