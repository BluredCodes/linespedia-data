WEIGHT

If you keep nagging about my weight, 
Your rapier tongue may seal your fate.
All I hear is,"You don't need that, 
think of the calories, think of the fat."

I can't have cake or a slice of pie, 
without your letting a comment fly.
"You have salad," you say to me;
while you have a Serloin, or maybe a "T"

You really pig out on your steak and your fries;
while I eat my salad with tears in my eyes.
Sure you can eat and never gain weight, 
no matter how much, no matter how late;

While I eat an ounce and put on a pound;
my wrinkles smooth out and my body gets round;
But one more comment, my love, and you're done;
for my next meal...you're right...it's your tongue.

			Joyce Guy
