The sun is shining, a warm breeze blows by. Flowers are standing upright.Mother Nature is at her best.Bees, butterflies, and that troublesome Chipmunk runs across my lawn snickering catch me if you can.I can breathI can see I can smellEvery beautiful flower emitting scents of lavender, and rosethat I planted in this garden,it is beautiful here.350 miles away the sun is not shining.The world trade center is not standing upright.People cannot breath.People cannot see 3 inches in front of them.The air is filled with smoke, dirt, dust, and debris.Terror has struck our homeland.Thousands upon thousands of human beings lie dead or dying WHY?Lucifer is alive and livingThe Son Of A Bitch.
