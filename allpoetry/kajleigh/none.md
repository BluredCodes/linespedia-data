Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
Time is running out
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
The knife is getting sharper
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
My skin is getting thinner
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
My blood is flowing faster
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
My heart beat is getting slower
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
Time is running out
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
Im going im going im gone
Tick Tock....................Tick Tock
Things are not what they seem

Goodbye my friend 
Goodbye my love
Goodbye the worl which gave me you!!just let me know what you think??
