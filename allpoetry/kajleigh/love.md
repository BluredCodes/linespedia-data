title: "Love!!"
writer: "kajleigh"

source: "AllPoetry"

Why does love cause so much pain?
Just to be loved by someone must
Be special i dont know love i have 
never been loved what i see makes
me cold so i can't love i dont
want to love!
I would rather have my heart ripped
out an eaten then have my heart
broken by someone i could love!
It causes so much pain
No one understands love it's just
a word used for guilt
No one can ever love another person
It's a word made to hurt people!!
