World's apart
But yet so close
If you were here
All i would want to do is hold you near!
I feel safe when i hear your voice calling my name 
If you were here i would feel so much more sane
Your the only one who understands
How i feel, what i do & where i go
Why did you leave me here so alone all on my own?
