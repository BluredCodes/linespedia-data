title: "Pain"
writer: "kajleigh"

Hear the peacful sound of my
blood dripping off my finger tips
Hear the screams others make
feel the tears falling from your face
feel the earth open to take you away
Say your good byes tho no one hears you 
see the pain they are feeling
How could you be so selfish?
You hear them saying
But your not the sefish one 
The lies they told you 
the way they treated you
You deserved to die!
But what they dont know won't hurt them!!!
