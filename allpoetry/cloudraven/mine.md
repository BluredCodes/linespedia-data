title: "Mine"

My life is not your
To take or have
It is Mine and
I will do what I need to keep it.
I will kick and scream till the end
I will not go out with a whimper, 
but with a war cry
This is my life and you will have to fight me for it.
So back off.!
Crawl back under the rock from which you came.
This is one fight you cannot win, 
cause I cannot lose it.This one needs some help. I can't seem to find what it needs.
