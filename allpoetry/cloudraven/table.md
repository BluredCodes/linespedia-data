My head falls to the table, BAM! 
'Ouch' he screams. 
'Oh, sorry man' I reply. 
What? I just appoligized to a table. 
'Why the hell woun't you' the table screamed! 
'You hurt me' he whimpered. 
'But your a table' I stammered, 
'a table doesn't feel'. 
'Well I sure as hell felt your hard head 
crashing down on me' pouted the table. 
Oh can I have lost it, or just hit my head too hard as 
I rub my forehead. 
Tables don't talk, they don't feel I'm just insane. 
'OK 'screamed the table, 'move it'! 
I jumped up startled and confused. 
'Find another piece of furniture to abuse' 
he screams as he walked across the room.what you have never had a table talk to you?
