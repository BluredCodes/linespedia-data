---
title: "WAR"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "war"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46566-WAR-by-CloudRaven"
---

You permit yourself to sleep, but awake screaming.
A blood curling sound in the dead of night.
War has invaded your dreams.
Horrors you kept locked away,
tucked inside, never to see the light of day.
Terror again surges through your body.
The war long gone, but still vivid in your dreams
And as you awake you look around.
Suddenly realize that reality is the true nightmare."for you Dad"
Inspired by a MASH episode
