No Choice

I need help I whispered into the phone.
Help me please I whimpered.
No answer was the reply I got.
Please, I need you, Please.
No response again.
I promise I’ll never do it again.
Please help me.
NO was the answer I tried not to hear.
Please Mom can I come home?
NO she said harshly and hung u p.
Please I cried in vain, PLEASE….
I sunk down to the floor of the booth.
The tears poured out of me.
I let the phone swing on its cord.
I dried my tears and fixed my face.
I knew what I had to do.
I had no choice.
I straightened my skirt and fixed my hair.
No choice, I reminded myself.
No choice, No choice….
My first customer came quickly.
No choice I repeated as I got into his car.
No choice…..
