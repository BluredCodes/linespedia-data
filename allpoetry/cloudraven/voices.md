The voices told me to do it, 
DON'T you believe me!!! 
I tell you the voices told me to do it, 
I can't help it that they talk to me and not you. 
Don't stare at ME! 
Grow up and leave me alone. 
I am not insane so STOP staring, 
the world is just too chaotic that's all. 
STOP looking at me, I told you 
I am not insane, I am sane 
it is the voices that are insane. 
Run, Run, RUN AWAY!!! 
The voices say to kill and you know that 
I always listen to the voices.
