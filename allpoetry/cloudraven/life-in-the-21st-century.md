I went on a journey
I traveled for a long time
My thoughts drifted
My past flashed by
I questioned my life
I feared my death
All the "what if's"
All the "I should have"
My mind raced
Love, hate, lust despair
My trip ended as
I woke from my dream
The IV dripped into my veins
The monitors beeped and buzzed
I questioned this kind of life
I looked toward death
I wish I had listened
I wish I could do it all over again
I wish I had used a condom
