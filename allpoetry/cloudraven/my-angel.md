My Angel

The angel fell,
I mended her tattered wing.
I whipped her tears and
held her trembling hand.
What sins did this
angelic creature commit to deserve
her descent to the cold ground.
Her destiny untold in a book
never to be written.
I shed my own tears as the angel
lay cold in my quivering arms.
Death to innocence.
Death to beauty.
I left the angel and awoke
from my horrific dream.
With lessons learned,
a tear in the corner of my eye and
a single feather clutched in my shivering hand.1994,
