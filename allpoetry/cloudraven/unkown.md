When from above you have run.
How blue was her skin?
On this day of the full moon.
Only with clandestine whispers.
She tells filth and truth.
Yet, see what the dark desires.
Only courage can let you ask,
not of man for this desire.
Like we are or will do,
deep in tongues of woman.
For now let’s dry him and
Teach him love.No idea what goes through my mind....
