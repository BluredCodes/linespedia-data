I don’t believe in you.
I don’t believe in me.
There’s nothing to live for.
All our heroes are dead.
The stars are out of reach.

I don’t believe in G*d.
I don’t believe in your religion.
There are no survivors as bloody
bodies lay in piles in Jerusalem

How can I believe in hope?
When all around me is destruction.
You tell me to look upward for solutions,
as reality smacks me across the face.

I don’t believe in you.
I don’t believe in me.
All my heroes are dead,
And the stars are fading away.
