Nightfall Encounter
 
The moonlight illuminates the path to your bedroom.
Stardust glitters onto your bed, as I climb in.
Oh,so gently touching your radiant skin.
Gazing into your piercing eyes.
Deeply breathing your intoxicating aroma.
Caressing your body so tenderly.
Slowly feeling every curve, making sure not to miss 
an inch of your exquisite, soft body.
As ecstasy comes over me, I am utterly at peace.
Sometimes as I hold you, I never want to let go.
Because sometimes it's so magical with you that 
it goes beyond the realms of reality.
Afraid to release you and wake from this mystical dream.
Never to touch your rose-fragrant, chestnut hair,
Would be too much for me to endure.
So as I climb from your bed and follow the moonlight.
I swear to return when the night does and again permit 
the stars to guide me back to you.
