It's time, but I'm not ready. 
It's time as the monitors beeped and the IV dripped. 
It's time as the wheeled me into the OR, 
but I'm not ready I whisper as I drift into sleep 
Pain awakes me as I hold the pictures in my hand 
two Polaroid pictures of two tiny infants. 
They weren't ready I whisper as I first see them. 
Machines breath for them and wires come from every part 
of there miniature bodies. 
I love you I whisper, as love overwhelms me, I am a Mother.was a challenge poem
dedicated to my sweet Sarina and Elizabeth
