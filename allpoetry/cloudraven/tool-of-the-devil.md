TOOL OF THE DEVIL

The tool of the devil or a toy
I ask you this question as I tear my hair out
a toy for all and yet it taunts me
stares at me and taunts me
bright red casing, big black knobs
blank screen laughing at me
A toy then say, no way
I swear it is the tool of the devil
a tool to drive me over the edge
turn the knob to go up, 
the other to go sideways
damn wrong knob again
shake and start over
a toy, yea right
I swear this damn thing is the tool of the devil
parents united remove the devil from your home
Destroy all Etch A Sketches!!!!this one is for you Autotomy
