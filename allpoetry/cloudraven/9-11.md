9-11

The blood spills from their mouths
they reek of hatred,
yet feel they are right
they are more important
above it all, just in there terror

The use the word of G*d as a badge
kill in his honor
destroy in his honor
mutilate in his honor
die in his honor

They leave terror and fear behind them
around and through them
they are just and we are wrong
they are right and we should die

Hatred is taught from conception
pounded into the hearts of there men
fear pounded into the hearts of there woman

our lives are unimportant for are belief are different
they see from one side only
their side is not only right, 
but the only view 

they look through their window 
a window of pain and terror
they started and have left us to finish
they deal in hatred and we deal in patriotism

they use G*d to condemn us
we use G*d to love
they use hate to divide us
we linked arms and fought back

the reek of hatred and pain
they blew holes in our flag,
but it still stands, we still stand
