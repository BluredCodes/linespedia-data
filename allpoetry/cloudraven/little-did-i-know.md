---
title: "Little Did I Know"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "little-did-i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

Little did I know that all the fun I had. 
All the desires I gave into. 
All the rules I broke. 
All the times I said they didn't understand 
and I knew better. 
That all I did. 
All that I needed. 
All that I wanted so badly, 
had a price. 
A price way too high. 
Little did I know that the 
price would be something so dear. 
That the price would be my very soul.
