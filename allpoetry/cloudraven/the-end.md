The End


Confusion has set in
I am unsure of life or death
Just pain and torment
 
My body lies in the dirt
As my mind screams out
Yet no one notices
No one hears, no one cares
 
Life goes on for them
As I pray for an end
 
I drift through space and time
Wondering through the reality of 
life and death
 
No one looks at me
A huddled mass of blood and fear
As I lay in the dirt 
Praying for the end.
