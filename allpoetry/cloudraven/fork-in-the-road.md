---
title: "Fork In The Road"
writer: "CloudRaven"

A crooked road awaits me
A straight one behind.
 
One is easy, too easy
One is hard, maybe too hard.
 
Which path to take?
Safety or uncertainty?
 
Toss a coin to decide,
But pockets are bare.
 
My head hurts,
My heart races.
 
Decision is too hard for me.
 
Standing in the middle of
The fork in the road.
The fork in my life.
 
Ahead or behind
Crooked or straight?
 
A deep breathe, my heart races.
 
Decision is made, on I go
The path of my life waits.I think it needs something, not sure what. Any ideas?
