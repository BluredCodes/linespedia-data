---
title: "Not Perfect"
writer: "CloudRaven"

source: "AllPoetry"

Not perfect, but mine.
She is so beautiful, but not perfect.
So precious in my eyes.
I know that she is different, but she is mine.
I know she will need a little help, but don't care.
Love knows no different.
Love doesn't care if your not perfect.
She holds my hand and looks in my eyes.
I know she is not perfect, but then who is?
