---
title: "Imagine"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "imagine"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49365-Imagine-by-CloudRaven"
---

Image a beautiful world.
Image peace and love.
Image joyful children.
Imagine a world of innocence, 
not tainted with random violence.

Dream a magical dream.
Dream of happiness.
Dream of children,
fee to play in safety, 
without fear of sounds of gunfire in the dark.

Live for now.
Live with love.
Live through the eyes of a child.
Fight to keep their innocence in a world filled 
with violence and needless hate.
