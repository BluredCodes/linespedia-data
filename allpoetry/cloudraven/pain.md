The pain is like a giant wave
crashing on me, giving
no warning or relief.
My mind aches, I can't think.
The world is spinning.
The hammer beats a rhythm,
my head is going to explode
I need something,
anything to dull the pain.
I take another pill,
then another, then another.
No relief in sight.
One more pill and I will find sleep.
I drift off...
