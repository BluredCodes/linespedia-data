Out of the whirlwind a small soft voice,
speaks from the heart and soul.
It speaks of love and dreams.
It speaks of hope and faith.
Listen to the voice,
do not shut it out.
Respect the voice for it's right.
The voice is in all of us.
Listen careful, it's there.
It tells of truth.
Listen to it, for it knows.
Listen to it, for it's your spirit.1998,
