Modern Sex
 
 
We embraced. 
i pushed my tongue deeper into her mouth.
 
oh... she whispered
i crushed against her.
 
My temperature rose.
Oh baby..she cried.
 
I presses against her harder.
ooooh...she moaned.
 
I continued to push against her frantically
i rammedher over and over again.
 
She painted,
I climaxed.
 
wow.. i said as i turned the computer off,
the image disappeared.
 
i walked into the bathroom,
i definitely needed a cold shower.
