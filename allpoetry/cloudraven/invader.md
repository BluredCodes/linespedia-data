INVADER


Kidnapping my soul.
As I fall into your eyes.
The echoes on the wall
of pain from the past.
The ghosts still lives in
my mind, my heart and
my memories.
You hold my soul in your hands.
as you bring back the old days.
My soul bleeds scarlet droplets
on the hardwood floor.
Leaving a trail of my past
and still fresh future.
