The angels wept
America cried
Hero's died

All was not lost
Faith is still here
In the hearts and souls

The angels hovered around
As the towers fell
And the smoke rose

Angels held the hands of the dying
And helped carry out the living
Angels comforted the hero's

The angels' tears fell
As the clean up begin
The angels' tears feel as
We all held hands

United we stand
United we grieve
