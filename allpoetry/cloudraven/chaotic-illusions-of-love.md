Chaotic Illusions of Love 

As I leave the bar my lust for you is gone, 
The loud whispers in the bar 
have deafen my soul and spirit 

I cross the street, but first must wait for the 
spotted elephants dancing in ballerina shoes to pass. 
I wonder if I had too much to drink 

As I pass the coffee house, tears fall 
I realize I have truly lost you 
How long must I grieve for you? 

The store window shows exotic underwear, 
as the passion build up inside me 
It was a hateful love we shared, 
but the lust I have for you at this instance is overpowering 

Was it just chaotic illusions of love we had? 
Sensual tears hit the cold pavement 
as I walk lost in my pain and needs 

I hear a  moonlight serenade coming from the 
restaurant our restaurant,as I see 
short people in tall clothing dancing 

I think maybe I need another drink! 
I think I need to call you 
To drench the thirst I have now in the pit of my stomach. 

The needs I have at this minute overcome my senses 
as I call you and begin another round 
into our chaotic illusions of love.challenge poem
