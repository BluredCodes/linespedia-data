---
title: "Emotions"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "emotions"

Emotions beneath reality, 
unknown lost because time clouds the soul.

Delicate journey forever taints with 
dark pleasure and secret dreams.

This will have only dissolve as reality 
returns to the conscience.

Rose age, fade and die.

Dreams lost because unknown pain 
turns into nightmares.

Fear paralyzes as emotions bore deeper from 
the light as we mature and continue our mundane lives.
