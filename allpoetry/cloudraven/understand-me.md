---
title: "Understand Me"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "understand-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If you could understand the fears I have
The monsters that live in my closet
The sharks that swim under my bed

If you could see the pain I see
The blood that comes from the walls

If you could hear the conflicts I hear
The voice that scream and yell

If you could live in the world I live in
The pain I am trapped in

If you could understand me
Would you release me or
At least loosen the straightjacket?
