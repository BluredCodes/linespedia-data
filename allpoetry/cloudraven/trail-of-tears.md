---
title: "Trail of Tears"
writer: "CloudRaven"
slug: "trail-of-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"

I take a long drag off the pipe of peace. 
I close my eyes and allow the steam to embrace me. 
I go on my mystical journey. 
As the visions in front of me become clearer. 
I walk in the heat, 
as all around me I feel the pain. 
The women’s tears litter the path, 
as they clutch there children tightly. 
The sun pours down, as the soldiers push us along. 
I become bitter as the elders fall 
and are left to find the Great Spirit on there own. 
I walk on as I lose my respect for myself. 
My bitter tears fall down along the path 
to the loss of freedom and self dignity. 
I open my eyes as the vision blurs 
and finally disappears. 
I walked the trail of tears and realize now 
why my nation is in the ruins I see before me.
