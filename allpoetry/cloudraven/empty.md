Empty


Empty Mind
Empty paper

     Blank paper
     Blank thoughts

Tears in my eyes
Tears in my heart

     Fear of life
     Fear of death

Pain in my head
Pain in my heart

     Need to write
     Need to talk

Nothing comes
Nothing flows

     Blank paper
     Blank mind

Life is too hard
Life is almost over

     Too much to say
     Too much to feel

Blood flows
Blood falls

     Empty paper
     Empty life.Not sure the style works. Any comment welcomed.
