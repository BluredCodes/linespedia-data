title: "FIRE"

I walk through the fire
The inferno on either side of me
The Blaze grows with each heartbeat
The flames leap from the floor and fall from the sky
The firestorm I started grows

She tries to put it out
She dampens the floor
She soaks the walls
The ceiling drips with moister

But the flames continue
The heat rises as I walk through the fire
I walk in the inferno to her
As the firestorm expands all around

The steam rises as the fire mixes with the water
We embrace as the mist grows
Her dampness clings to me as my fire ignites her
Ignites the moment, the passion

The delight intensifies inside me
I become craze with the fire
The fury of the moment
As we combust into pleasure
