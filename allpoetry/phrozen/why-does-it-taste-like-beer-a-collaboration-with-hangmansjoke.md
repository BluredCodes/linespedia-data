Where have all the pancakes gone?
I have the munchies

The fork is jealous
Why does everything dance?
Fuck it

It goes down good with sub sauce
Boobs... happy

More doggy snot... why must you always sneeze on me?

I trip Mr. cuddles and giggle till I wet my pants
Why does it taste like beer?
What are you looking at?

The Scot, the Brit, the Priest, and the Hanged Man
Why does it taste like beer?
