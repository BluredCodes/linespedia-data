I dream of masked galleries 
Endless hung in magnificence
Nailed to the sky
Gallivant halogens light the night
Pulsing while fornicating
The senses blind

Times unfrequented aspiration
Seditious this provocative alignment
Femine sureality of self
Takes hold in augmentation
Deeper than seeds sown
Midnight the hour of thy bewitchment

Unrequited this ghostly life wilts
Clambering upon the deadened shelf
Wistful dreams of brilliance
Harkens inside the fall of light
Beauty's realization becomes hidden
Behind the moons resilience

Fevered this frantic masturbation
Inconsistencey provides a failed yeild
Moisten the dreams of this life time
Seeking soul light to provide emergence
Shatter those darkened truth's reliance
Sake the hand of self's dissonant tremblings
