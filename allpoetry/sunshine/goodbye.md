title: "Goodbye"
writer: "Sunshine"
slug: "goodbye"

Pained and tortured soul
I can not survive the agony
Let this be my goodbye
For all who knew me
Shall sing of my cheer
I have kept secret the demons that taunt me
Forgive me my friends
I will leave you with your tears for me
Don't forget me
Or what happened to me
Remember my performed joy
And think it to be my true character
Goodbye, my friends, goodbye
