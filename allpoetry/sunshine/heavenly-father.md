Heavenly Father
	I pray to thee
	Heavenly Father
	Wash over me

Wash my sins away
	Like you always do
	Because Lord Jesus
	I worship you

Father I’m asking
	For some advice
	To do what is your will
	To do what is right

You are my leader
	And you guide me so
	Wherever you want me
	Dear Lord I will go

Heavenly Father
	I will do what you say
	I will be loyal
	Until my living day
