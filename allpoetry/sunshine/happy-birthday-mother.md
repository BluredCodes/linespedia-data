I love you, mom
Like no words can say
I thank the Lord in Heaven
For you everyday

You have given me
Everything I’d ever need
A mother’s love, your tender touch
They mean so much to me

You live in my heart
For all my years to come
And who taught me to love?
Mom, you’re the one

So now it’s your birthday
Special as can be
But nothing is as special
As what you mean to meI'm a mamma's gal
