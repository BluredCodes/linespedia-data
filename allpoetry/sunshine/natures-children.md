The Fire rages
The Water is flowing fast
The Wind whistles and billows
The Earth trembles

These are Nature's children

Love, Respect, Fear them
They have power to harness
But can be oh, so deadly
Unpredictably harsh and destructive

These are Nature's children

The four elementsof Creation
Untainted by the passing of time
Fire  Water  Wind  Earth
The endless untaimable forces

These are Nature's children
