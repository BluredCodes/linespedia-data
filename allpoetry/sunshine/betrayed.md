You say you’ll call
---But you won’t
You said you love me
---But you don’t
You said you’d never leave
---But you did
How long until
---I blow my lid
You were always at my side
---Now you’re not
You said we’d never cool down
---We sure ain’t hot
How many times must I withstand?
-BABY NO MORE-
How many times must I give in to demand?
-BABY NO MORE-
How many times must I relent to make you stay?
This is the last time you’ll make me feel betrayed!
