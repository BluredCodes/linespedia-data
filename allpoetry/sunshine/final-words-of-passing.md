As I leave this world
I pray my life was not in vain
I've always tried to keep a smile
Even through my pain

I pray I touched at least one life
While my stay on Earth
For I devoted well-spent time
To help and encourage others

I thank you Lord for all my friends
Everyone I've known
I thak you Lord for mom and dad
And the endless love they've shown

I'm sorry for all the things I'll miss
I'm grateful for the things I did not
So keep me in your hearts my friends
Now memories are all we got

Goodbye my precious loved ones
Please try not to cry
I hope to see you all in Heaven
Sincerely yours, Sunshine
