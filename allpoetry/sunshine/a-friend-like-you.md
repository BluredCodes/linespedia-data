---
title: "A Friend Like You"
writer: "Sunshine"
slug: "a-friend-like-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

We are as close as sisters
Other people see it too
I hope every one on Earth
Will find a friend like you

I thank my Lord in Haven
Seriously I do
He has surely blessed me
With a friend like you

During all my pain and heartache
Everything that I've been through
I never would've made it
Without a friend like you

We have built a kind of kinship
One that's strong and true
I'm happy to have such a wonderful friend
Even more so because it's you
