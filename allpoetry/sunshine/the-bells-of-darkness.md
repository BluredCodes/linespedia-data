Anger, anger
The Bells of Darkness toll
Hatred is flashed across the sky
As thunder and lightning roll

Insanity creeps to any man
Who hears their evil chime
Sent to start his blood to boil
And deteriorate his mind

Once all the mind is gone
The plague is free to roam
Now it’s easy for the demons to find
Their way into the soul

So beware the songs of Darkness
Whose music is straight from hell
I know this cursed legion for
