---
title: "Fighting the Storm"
writer: "Sunshine"
slug: "fighting-the-storm"

The anger is swelling
The thunder rolls of our tongue
Rain is pouring from my eyes
Inside I feel the lightning burning in my soul
-This storm has just begun

Thank you for this strength!
For every drop that falls from me by your account
Another brick is added to my wall
To my defense against my pain
-This storm is almost gone

Your thunder crashing can not scar me
You fiery lightning can not harm me
No matter how fierce your storm you can not beat me down
-This storm is now calm
