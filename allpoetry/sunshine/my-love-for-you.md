---
title: "My Love For You"
writer: "Sunshine"
slug: "my-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35244-My-Love-For-You-by-Sunshine"
---

My heart can't lie
No matter if I try
I can never deny
What I feel for you

You have my heart lit
It's no secret
Wouldn't want to hide it
You give your love so true

We're never apart
And we'll never start
Because you have my heart
And that's where my love is stored

Wit you I'm complete
Your great love knocks me off my feet
It's you that leads
Jesus Christ the Lord
