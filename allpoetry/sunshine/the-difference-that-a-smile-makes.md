title: "The Difference That a Smile Makes"

I hide my face 
Under my hair
So you cannot see
What’s under there

So you can’t see the hurt
And you can’t see the pain
And you can’t see the fear
That flows through my veins

And you can’t see the worry 
And you can’t see my tears
I’m so glad you can’t see  
Under here

But I can see you 
Clear as plain day
And I can hear
The awful words they say

I think no one loves me
I fear that it’s true
But all I’m asking for
Is a smile from you

Some conformation
That somebody cares
So I won’t have to hide 
My face under my hairTo all the ignored people out there---:-} (a smile)
