Now all you people
Listen to this
It’s the fight between evil
And righteousness

The devil walked up 
To a man & said
“Bow to me
I’m the lord of the dead

“Creating mankind
That was my idea
If it wasn’t for me
You wouldn’t be here

“Look around you
I created all this
I created the night
I made the mist

“So come with me
Take my hand
I’m not only your king
But I’m your friend”

The man was about to
But he said, “Wait
If I go with you
What’s my fate?”

The GOD came down
To the devil he said
“You’re right you are
The lord of the dead

“But evil one
Lay off your tricks
I created the night
I made the mist

“I made everything 
Here on Earth
Except the evil
But that’s your turf

“I am everything
That’s good & pure
My love is ever lasting
And I’m forever more”

Then some heavenly light 
Shone down from HIS hand
The guy looked up
Said, “Hey, you’re the man

“In the light 
I felt peace & tranquil 
With GOD’s great love
Did my heart fill

“I’ll go with you
No matter what the devil says
Praising the LORD
For the rest of my days”

That was the blow 
That ended the fight
The devil took off 
Into the night

Remember this battle 
Takes place form with in
If you join GOD’s army
The war you’ll win
