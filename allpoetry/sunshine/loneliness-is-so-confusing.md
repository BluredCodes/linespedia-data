---
title: "Loneliness is So Confusing"
writer: "Sunshine"
slug: "loneliness-is-so-confusing"
source: "AllPoetry"

I watched a movie today
That we once watch together
It was a comedy, though
I thought I'd cry forever

So many momentos
Of you and I 
You're my screen-saver
A picture of you next to where I lie

My heart starts to wilt
When I see you with her
I try to act as if I'm over you
I tell myself it doesn't matter

Maybe it's not you I'm missing
It's just somebody to love
I want some one to hold me again
To make me free as a dove

Maybe it's not your hand I'm yearning for
It's a loving touch
But I think it's both
That I'm missing so much
