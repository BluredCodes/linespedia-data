---
title: "This is What I Feel"
writer: "Sunshine"

So happy
My life has come to a time
A time of joy unmatchable

LOVE
This is what I feel

My soul is dancing
I will never again walk upon the ground
For my heart lifts me high above the Earth's sinking floor

LOVE
This is what I feel

The whispering voices of the thoughts of my love
Surround me and sing me into my gleeful state
The state of eternal bliss

LOVE
This is what I feel
