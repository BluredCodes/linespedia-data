title: "How The Hell..."
writer: "Sunshine"

How the hell can you still look my way?
How the hell can you still smile after what you put me through?
How the hell can I ever stop hurting?
How the hell can I get over you?

How the hell are you so happy?
How the hell are you so cruel?
How the hell can can I ever stop hurting?
How the hell can I get over you?

How the hell can you cut me so deep?
How the hell can you tourture me so?
How the hell can can I ever stop hurting?
How the hell can I get over you?

Why the hell can't I stop loving you?
