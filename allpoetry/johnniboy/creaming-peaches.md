title: "Creaming Peaches"
writer: "JohnniBoy"

Patience is bitter but,
its fruit is sweet, 
and I, my friend, just had 
one luscious peach.

Between the branches, 
is where I retreat,
to find that fleshy nub 
within tongues reach.

With my mouth 
I break its skin,
and its sliky nectar
moistens my chin.

The fine whiskers
tickle my face.
It's these old memories 
I want you to erase.
