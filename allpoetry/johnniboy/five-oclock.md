Five o'clock,
the whistle screams,
our shakels drop.
Like inbred livestock
we shuffle out.

Observing mannerisms;
carefully planned.
Language; plastic,routine.
Executed with dull finesse.
Blunt rods falling on my ears.

Our ties could so 
easily be nooses. 
If only a proper 
platform found,
to sway
back and forth 
side to side.
Tongue, swollen and ajar
Not very proffesional at all.
