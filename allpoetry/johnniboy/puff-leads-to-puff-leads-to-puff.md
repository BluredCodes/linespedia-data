Managing a steering wheel in my 
				right,
and a familiar lighter in my
left, 
I slowly pull some 
life
into yet another cigarette, 
puff 
leads to
puff
leads to
puff
I discard the  ghostly ashes out the window 
and feel the air
		play
			on my fingers.
How I wish I could sacrifice it to the wind 
And not check the mirror for the shattering
		embers.
