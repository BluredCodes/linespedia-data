---
title: "MIslead"
writer: "Hayley"
slug: "mislead"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The direction of our love is unclear.
The intention is unstated.
The reasons that we are searching for are 
non-existant.
Is this just a script, of which we follow?
Reading our lines strangley, perfect.
Are we running towards truth?
Or are we circling our steps on the 
round-about of deception?
To know we must let go.
And find the answers on our own separate 
roads.
