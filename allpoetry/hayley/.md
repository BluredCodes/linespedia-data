I hate it when it rains it fills me with hatred and fear like when you turn on the radio and the song that makes you cry comes on and you turn into a ball of anger. I love him so much and want so much for him to understand yet he understands the radio and the rain, and not me.This was just a thought, I usually don't write like this! Sorry.
