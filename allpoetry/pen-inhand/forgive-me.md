Forgive me when
I doubt you.
Always in my
past, lay a stream
of broken promises,
always broken
way to fast.
In my heart
are many patches,
of the years
and pain gone by.
But in your arms
I soar the clouds,
I feel a natural high.
So when I look
into your eyes,
and ask you if you care,
I feel an emotion
I can't explain, a
love beyond compare.
For when you say
"I Love You"
my doubts just disappear.
I cherish our Love
so darling, it's a treasure
I hold so dear.Written December 2nd, 2001 ©  on Dec 02 2001 01:36 PM PST, Kelly Brega   personal  • love
