---
title: "Lost Again"
writer: "pen inhand"

A silent farewell
bides love to my heart.
Glidding effortlessly
out of my life.

Moon beam chokes
me hot with lights
of laborous tension.

Crimson thoughts
strewn carelessly
about. Passion Like
dew drops lay on
petals of lonliness.
