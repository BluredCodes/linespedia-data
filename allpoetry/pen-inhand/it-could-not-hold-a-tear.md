title: "It Could Not Hold A Tear"

It could not hold a tear,
my heart, my treasured space.
It could not hold sweet words
of love greedily consumed.
It could not hold unbridled passion,
nor the courage to seize.
It could not hold the lies to me
of spoken words of yesterday.
It could not hold a loves parade
of relationships gone askew.
How I gingerly, carefully, tiptoe
around my heart, my life place.
Trodden on by life's misfortunes.
Fleeting memories line my mind,
