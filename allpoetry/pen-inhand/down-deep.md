Way down deepbeneath my mold,lies turmoilof a lesser time.An aged dream.My atmosphereinhibits me.I shudder withde'ja vue.I wish I couldencase my thoughtsto hold before me.Exposing my complexities.Unfolding myrealities. For a better understandingof what lieswithin.
