Pin pricks of sunrise
Dance upon the
Morning mist.
Igniting the trees
Into the fiery
Hues of fall.

Acres of farmland
Long sense harvested.
Barren in their
Seasonal slumber.

In the distance the
Geese cry their
Farewell song as
Their migration
Leads them south.

My sleeping senses
awakened oh  so
Gloriously to the
Sights, the sounds,
The smells of a
