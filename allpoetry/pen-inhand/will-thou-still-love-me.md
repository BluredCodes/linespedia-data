title: "Will Thou Still Love Me"
writer: "pen inhand"

Does thou dance thru
  the gardens,
of my soul?
Does thou give me life
with each
sweet kiss? 
Will thou love me until
the sun no
longer rises?
Will thou see beauty in thee
when lines of
time appear?
Thou lies in my deepest
heart place,
safe and loved.Written November 19th, 2001 ©  on Nov 18 2001 03:08 PM PST, Kelly Brega   love
