---
title: "The Curse"
writer: "pen inhand"
slug: "the-curse"
source: "AllPoetry"

The putrid
decay
of society
lay
scattered
about
the harridans
feet.
Like a
malignant
tumor
intoxicating the
hag.
A malediction
on 
her soul.
