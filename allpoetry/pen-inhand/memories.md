The memories of himwill always be,a part of me.It was hard towatch him go.But I know that weeach must go ourseperate ways, becauseif he stayed, thingsjust wouldn't work.I thought we'd never part!But now my heart isfilled with regrets,that we met at a timewhen we thought weneeded someone to love.I think of him throughoutthe day. I'm wonderingif there is a way, thatlove could bring usclose again.
