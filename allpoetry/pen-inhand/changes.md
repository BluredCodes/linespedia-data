Phenomenal changes happening
in my life.
Every day a new indifference turning
out my guiding light.
I'm trying to pull together my hopes,
my plans, my dreams.
And try and be the person God 
intended me to be.
But the world is so confusing,
shielding wickedness from our eyes.
We fall into the Devil's snare, and our
words become his lies.
If we fight off the temptations of
evil in this life, and follow in God's 
spoken words....
