When I feel down
You have an uncanny
sense that I need you.
So many times
on the phone,
my tears would fall.
The simple release of
hearing your voice.
You make me feel
accepted.
You help me to 
except myself.
Our friendship has
enriched my life,
warmed my heart.
A lifetime of knowing
you, would never 
be enough.
I love you dear friend,
