---
title: "Fear of Dreams"
writer: "Alkexia"
slug: "fear-of-dreams"

The lights fade around me
Is I drift into the dream world
I fall through the floor 
Into deep water far above my head

My screams are not heard 
But only feed that which causes my fear

I am alone in the woods
No light from the clouded night sky
Something brushes against my shoulder
And gives a ghostly laugh

My screams are not heard 
But only feed that which causes my fear

I'm on a balcony
Many stories from the ground
I hear a rumble from the stone
I fall to the ground with it

My screams are not heard 
But only feed that which causes my fear

I'm in a secluded cabin
I look around as I start to smell smoke
Panic raises in my chest
As burning beams collaps around me

My screams are not heard
But only feed that which causes my fearEveryone tell me what ya think!
