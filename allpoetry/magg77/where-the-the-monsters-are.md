title: "where the the monsters are"

The dark still scares me
at the age of 21
i still fear the monsters
around the corner
down the hall
i hear echoes of their voices
exploding in my mind
they force me to remember
what i try so hard 
to just forget
the images of broken glass
the sound of violent screams
hiding underneath the covers
hoping,she'll think that i'm asleep
i cant tell you what it feels like
becuase i hide it
in the dark
knowing,i'd never go in there 
not to where the monsters are.
