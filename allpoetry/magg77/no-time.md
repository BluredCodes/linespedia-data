Rush Rush
Hurry Hurry
Go through life 
in such a scurry
Things to do 
Things not done
Spend my life
on the run
No time to chat
I'm on my way
So many things 
on my list today
Perhaps tomarrow
I'll have less to do
and i can take the time
to talk with you.
