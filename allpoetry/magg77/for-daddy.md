title: "for daddy"
writer: "magg77"

mother is sick
will you stay home today?
sure daddy
what ever you say
mother dropped her pills today
will you help her sort them out?
yes daddy
i'll help if you ask
mommy needs the house cleaned
and i worked all day
right away daddy
spotless as can be
mommy made so daddy
cant buy this house we live in
will you help me?
sure thing daddy
where should i sign?
mommy passed away today
will you help me pay the bill
to bury mommy all alone?
yes daddy
heres some money,right away
daddy found a new wife
will you take this house
and the responsibilty of it all?
i guess i have to daddy
i'm sorry i cant help you
buy a stone for mommys grave
but i'm living my new life now
gee thats okay daddy
at least you taught me one thing
to never walk away.
