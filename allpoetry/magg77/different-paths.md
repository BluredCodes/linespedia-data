---
title: "different paths?"

i tire so quickly
of your ranting and raving
pretending to know me 
believing i have no faith
that hell is my enternal home
i do not sit in pews
praying to god each sunday
reading "his good book"
but my faith is ever stronger
than you could ever know
when the world draped over me
a life of pain and grief
he was my only friend
the one i did confide in
when the  beauties of this earth
stand before me every day
when i see my nieces run to hug me
and his angels grace my life
i know his hand is over me
i am not "saved"
you say i need to be 
to be close to him
but how can you say
how can you judge
the buddist,the muslim,
the ones such as i
who do not fallow "your" path
he is in my heart,he is in yours
are we really traveling 
differant roads afterall?
