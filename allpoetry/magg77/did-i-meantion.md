---
title: "did i meantion?"
writer: "magg77"

let me tell you of my sister
shes a teeny little thing
with the biggest heart of gold
and the loviest smile in the world 

did i meantion that shes beautiful?

let me tell you of her laughter
how it infects you merely with its sound
like a cackle and a giggle
that tickles you softly

did i meantion thats shes beautiful?

let me tell you how she amazes me
the way she bravely raises her girls
teaching them that they are better
than any dumb ol' boy!

did i meantion that shes beautiful?

let me tell you shes the greatest
cuase i dont think she really knows
how she is pricless to me
and the princess of the world!

and as i've mentioned....she is beautiful!
