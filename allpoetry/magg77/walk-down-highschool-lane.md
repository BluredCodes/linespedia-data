my very first car,
broken down all the time!
my after school job,
i quit three times!
theater arts class,
the skits we put together last minute!
watching bob play 7th period,
walking her to every class!
hanging out in the choir room,
cornelia singing jewel!
steve and his mustang,
and his dixie horn!
sleeping in english,
how did i pass?
doing nothing in photography class,
hell,all my classes!
the fashion mob,
what is she wearing?
graduation,
my cheering section!
i cant believe,
its been five years!it came to me the other day,its been five years since i ran off the football feild,so excited to finnaly be out of high school.i REALLY never thought,i want to go back to those days!but alas some days i miss it.
