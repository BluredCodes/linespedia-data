WHY ARE YOU GOING?
i'm confused
ANSWER ME!
i'm hurting
WHERE DID YOU GO?
i'm alone
WHAT HAVE I DONE?
i'm searching
AM I SO HORRIBLE?
i'm afriad
DONT YOU CARE?
i still do
I'LL DO ANYTHING!
come back
CAN YOU HEAR ME?
you cant can you?
I STILL CARE!
does it matter now?
WAIT!
you forgot to... say goodbye
