i have heard the weary travelertell of pains he bears so heavyand the kings of far off landsspeak of worries large and smalli have listened quite intentlyto so many,wrought with troublesand it amazes me to the very endhow they believe they have it badwhen i have seen Gods very angelsshuffle through the sanddirt covered faces,they baretattered clothing on their shouldersyet always filled with loveif i could brings these awesome foolsto see the wonders i have seenwould they walk away inspired?or find more reason to complian?i am humbled by the strengthof these angels in my lifeand find them my reason to go on,my head held high.i know at times i almost started to rhyme then didn't.like i said before my writings just come out and thats how i let them lie.my heart knows no true structure!;-)
