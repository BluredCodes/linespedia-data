i have embraced 
sweet moments of truth
truth of all that i am.
i am you
i am he
i am she
i am all of this world
and it is me.
i am the trees in their splender
on cool breezy nights.
i am the sun in the dawn
warming the dew.
i am the rain as it rushes
down to the earth.
i am the moon as shines
on your face as you sleep.
i am here
i am there
i am all around you.
so you see my dear child
i am here with you always.
you made me shine
in all of my glory
you are me
i am you.
we are one.
