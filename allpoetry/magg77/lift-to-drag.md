---
title: "lift to drag"
writer: "magg77"

the smoke curls up
from my freshly lit cigarette
wrapping itself around
thoughts of you
as they drift slowly,lovingly
from my clouded mind
ashes drop as i lift to drag
as if pieces of mistakes made
in years past
my ashtray is full of them
they scatter on the desk
i know i could clean them up
but what good would it do?
when i'll only light another
and drop more
as i lift to drag.
