You're sifting throughthe crumbs of your existence.Finding onlyyour dusty past,cradledin your weathered hands.Who you wish to beis sadly peering overyour heavy burdened shoulders,with tear stained dreamsin a dirty sack.Silently begging you,to just turnand see,the open door behind you.
