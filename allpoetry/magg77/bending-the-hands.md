this cant be realcant be trueI've waited so longprayed,begged,bargainedwith godthat you and i would see each otherbe it onlyjust one more timeI'd give up this life i knowto bend the hands of fate.now my wish has come trueyour eyes i smiled intowanting nothing morein that momentthan to be there alwaysnow i fear every passing day,that one is all i getlord,here i pray,i beg,i bargainwith youbend those hands of fate,of time,to let me be therewith my friend,alwaysnever let us part again.
