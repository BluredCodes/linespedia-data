what do i say?
as i watch the tears fall
i went to say my freindly hello
the look on your tattered face
of saddness unbareable
told me somethings wrong
but how could i know?
would i know what to say
even if i did
i dont believe language
made words for such things
i cant even prentend
to understand god's reason
but rest easy in these words
there is always a reason
when god calls his angels home.

To Teri and Rob,and thier family.God blesswritten for a  friend at work whos boyfriends brother took his own life this weekend.and no one knows why.i ask for everyones prayers for them to find peace somewhere in it all.
