---
title: "The world around me"
writer: "magg77"
slug: "the-world-around-me"

i saw you today
in a memory
shadows of your smile
dance in my eyes
i heard you today
your voice echos
and vibrates through my mind
there is not a day 
when i can see
or hear
or taste
or smell
or feel the world around me
without a reminder of you.
and though it hurt for so long
that we had drifted so far
it is now my blessing
from you
to see 
to hear
to taste
to smell
to feel this world around me
and to smile and know
you are a part of that world
and for a short time 
you graced me,
by being a part of mine.
