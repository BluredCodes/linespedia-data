title: "i'm a self rightous bitch"
writer: "magg77"

everyday i want more and more
to smack you
in hopes to drive your ignorence away
i feel prententious in your presence
and i dont like to feel this way
this is not to say
you are unintelligent
but you bore me sensless
with your petty gossip
and your fourth grade verbal skills
i feel shameful in your presence
you bring me to your level
perhaps we just dont understand eachother
perhaps i just dont understand you
perhaps i dont understand me
perhaps i'm just a self rightous bitch!
