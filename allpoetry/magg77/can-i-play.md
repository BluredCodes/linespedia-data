title: "can i play?"

the toys on floor
the dishes in the sink
the bills on the desk
the things on my mind
my life gone astray

the new shoes you just bought
becuase they made you feel better
the outfit to match

the catlitter to clean
the stairs to be vacumed
the phone calls to make
my life is in tassels

the things that you bitch about
the little things that upset you
i'm walking on eggshells

the second job on weekends
the home repairs
the fatigue on my face
my mind in pieces

i'm tired of working 
can i play your game?
of prentend and hide 
from the world 

i'm tired of being 
your mommy AND sister
i'm tired of being 
the older younger sister


i'm tired now
can i be the the 
one
that prentends 
the world 
away


can i play your game?written two years ago when i was a little ticked at my older sis,but its okay now.she know i love her,the little witch!
