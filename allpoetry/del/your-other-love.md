---
title: "YOUR OTHER LOVE"
writer: "Del"
slug: "your-other-love"

The sun is slowly setting, casting shadows over the land,While you and I are sitting on the still warm sun kissed sand.To feel you oh, so near me, your soft warm hand on mineIs a memory that I'll cherish until the end of time.But time we have so little,dear, tomorrow you must go,To your other love that beckons you, and why I'll never know.You spend much more time with her than you ever do with me.Oh, darling, I'm so jealous of that bewitching restless sea.She cast a spell upon you when you were just a little boy.From now until the day you die, she'll be your fondest joy.I love you oh, so dearly, there's no one else for me.So I guess, my dear, I'll always have to share you with the sea.
