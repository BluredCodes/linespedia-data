I'd like to wish you allA memorable Christmas this year.May it be filled with love and happiness,As we spend it with those we hold dear.May peace forever be with us.Let's all keep love in our heart.If everyoe loved one another,There'd be no reason for wars to start.Let's pray to the one God up above,To help show us the way.To make this Christmas, and every one,A most joyous and memorable day.This poem is for Sammy and all my friends on Allpoetry.  Luv,  Del
