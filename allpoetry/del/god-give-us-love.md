title: "God Give Us Love"
writer: "Del"

God, I pray there will be a day
When wars will be no more.
When men can be with family
And not on a far distant shore.

A nation can declare a victory,
But no one wins a war.
Because of such horrible memories
Life will never be as before.

Brave soldiers will kiss their sweethearts,
Holding them oh, so tight.
Wondering how they'll make it
Thru each lonely day and night.

So many sacrifices will have to be made,
To help keep America free,
Both men and women will lose their lives,
To save America for you and me.

Love should make the world go round,
Not hate and terror and greed.
Please, dear God, put love in our hearts,
That's the one emotion we all need.
