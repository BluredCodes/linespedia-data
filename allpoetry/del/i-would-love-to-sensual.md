---
title: "I Would Love To (Sensual)"
writer: "Del"

I would love to feel a soft warm breeze
From my window cooling the air.
I would love to gaze into your eyes,
And see desire there.

I would love to feel your tender caress,
And your velvety finger tips,
Stroking my hair and my face
And touching my warm moist lips.

I would love to feel the heat of your body,
As you kiss all my cares away.
How I'd love to have you near me, darling!
Each and every day. Love ya so...
