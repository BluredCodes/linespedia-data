---
title: "LOVE IS LIKE A ROSE (Amorous)"
writer: "Del"
slug: "love-is-like-a-rose-amorous"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8651-LOVE-IS-LIKE-A-ROSE--Amorous--by-Del"
---

The rose in my hand, sparkles like a diamond,
From the fresh early morning dew.
It reminds me of the love we shared
And vowed we'd always be true.

I know this rose will wither and die.
No way can it live forever.
Just like my heart, it fell apart
But will forget you never.

I'll always remember the sweetness of passion,
The night I proved my love for you.
The feeling I had was so incredible,
I thought you felt it too.

During all those nights of loving,
We learned to please each other.
I was so completely satisfied
I could never love another.

But, your love was just like this rose,
So fresh, so lovely, then it died.
You'll never know, my darling
Just how much I cried.

I live for the day you might realize
Your heart still belongs to me.
Until that day, I know for sure
My heart will never be free.
