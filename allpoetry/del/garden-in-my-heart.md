title: "Garden In My Heart"

There's a beautiful garden in my heart,
And lately the flowers are growing.
They were dormant for such a long time,
Now their brilliant beauty is showing.

The flowers are all so gentle,
The rose being one exception.
No one knows what the thorns may inflict,
No one has that perception.

The times we feel the rose's thorns,
Are the times our heart is aching.
The power of the gentle flowers,
Keeps our heart from breaking.

So, if some days you're feeling sad
And don't know what to do.
Remember there is a garden in your heart,
So let the sun shine thrue.
