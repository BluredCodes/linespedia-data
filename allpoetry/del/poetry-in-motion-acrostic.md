P erhaps, my dear, we can go swinging on a   star,O ver the lakes and streams, while you   strum your guitar.E ven the angels won't mind that at all.T hey'll watch over us, to make sure we   won't fall.R oller coaster rides are also quite   thrilling.Y ou can tell me, my dear, if you are   willing.I f you are, we'll find a carnival nearby.N othing can stop us as we ride way up   high.M aybe tomorrow night we'll take a long   walkO n Lover's Lane where we can kiss and   talk.T hen for something a little more sound,I suggest a ride on a merry-go-round.O h, I guess, you and I with such a loving  devotion,N ow just expressed our love with Poetry   In Motion.
