title: "Dream, Please Come True (Sensuous)"

A cozy cabin in the woodsI've been seeing in my dreams.The moon is full, the stars so bright.Oh, how real it seems!On a bearskin rug by the firesideYou're holding me in your arms.As we slowly sip our glass of wineYou begin to show me your charms.You kiss me oh, so tenderly,I can taste the wine on your lips.Then you softly and so slowlyCaress my lips with fingertips.You whisper softly to me,"I'm so in love with you."I can feel the tear drops formingAs I tell you, I love you too.Soon we're lying on the rug,So much passion in our hearts.Just like two peas in a podAnd then, our love making starts.You undress me, I undress you.We're trembling with desire.No one has ever done this beforeSet my heart on fire.You kiss my lips, caress my body.What a grand and glorious sensation!Then the ultimate feeling of becoming one,With the act of penetration.Maybe some day an angelWill come flying out of the blue,She'll touch me with her magic wandAnd make this heavenly dream come true.
