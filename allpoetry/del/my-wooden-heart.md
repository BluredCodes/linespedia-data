---
title: "My Wooden Heart"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-wooden-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14234-My-Wooden-Heart-by-Del"
---

I wish my heart was made of wood,So no one could break it in two.I'm such a fool when it comes to love,And now I'm being a fool for you.When things go astray, I wonder.Am I being too easy going?Maybe I should hide my feelingsAnd keep my heart from showing.For me that's just too hard to do.My heart has a mind of its own.Feelings start right from the heart.I have to make my feelings known.I'm so much in love with you, my dear.You make me feel so good.I have a heart that loves you so,But it's not made of wood.
