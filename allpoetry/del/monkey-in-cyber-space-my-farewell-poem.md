Have you ever heard of a monkey
Running around in cyber space?
This monkey isn't even funnny,
Just a terrible disgrace.

Trying to ignor him
Was very hard to do.
So with a great deal of sadness,
I am leaving too.

I met so many GREAT poets
The short time I was here.
Your comments were so enlightening,
I'll always hold them dear.

So, I wish the best to all of you.
May your future be happy and bright.
Maybe we'll meet again someday
On another poetry site.I am not withdrawing any of my poetry.  I spent too much time sending them in.  Del
