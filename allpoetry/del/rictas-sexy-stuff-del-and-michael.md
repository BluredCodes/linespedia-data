Lovin'
That's what I want
My heart is just aching
It wants to be right next to yours
While I feel your hot eager lips on mine
Feel those fingers caressing me
From my head to my toes
Oh how I need
Lovin'

Slowly
Work that magic
That has spellbound my heart
Mix a potion made just for two
As our passions overflow the cauldrom
Spilling our love all o'er the room
I'll lap it up for you
Edging closer
Slowly

Kiss me
Right there, my dear
And there, and there and there
That erotic feeling is coming on
Please don't stop, darling, please don't stop
Heaven is almost here
Ohhhhhh, sweetheart, now
Kiss me

Patience
Oft a virtue
There's no place for it now
Take me now with all your power
I feel it pulsing deep within your soul
Hurry, don't let the moment pass
Love me harder, faster
No time now for
