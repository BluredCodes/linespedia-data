---
title: "NOW THAT YOU'RE HERE"
writer: "Del"
slug: "now-that-youre-here"

What a beautiful sight, this prelude to night.The heavens are all aglow.A salute to the sun for a job well done.How fast this day seemed to go.It's so wonderful, dear, having you hereWatching the day fade from sight.When you were gone, the days were so long,And oh, how I dreaded the night.So often it seems I'd see in my dreamsThe two of us sitting right here.It was oh, so divine, your lips kissing mine.Those dreams kept you ever so near.Now all that is past, you're with me at last.I love you with all of my heart.It's been a wonderful day, I hope and I prayThat we'll never again be apart.
