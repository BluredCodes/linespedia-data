---
title: "Being lonely"
writer: "Del"
slug: "being-lonely"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't mind being lonely, my dear,
For I know you are lonely too.
I don't mind if you're so far away,
I know you will always be true.

You seem to have me under your spell
And there's not much I can do.
But I love the sensual feeling I get
Whenever I think of you.

My dreams were always so empty,
Now they seem so real.
You are always right besides me
Telling me how you feel.

Then I feel your tender caresses,
And your warm moist lips on mine.
It's something I haven't felt, my dear
For such a very long time.

So I really don't mind being lonely,
If I have something to look forward to.
I can't wait to crawl into bed at night,
Because I know that's where I'll find you.
