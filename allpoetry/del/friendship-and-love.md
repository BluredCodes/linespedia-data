---
title: "Friendship And Love"
writer: "Del"

I'm walking down this rocky road,
Of that strong emotion called LOVE.
The skies were always so bright and sunny.
Now there are dark clouds up above.

I hear from you so seldom, my friend.
Thru no fault of your own.
I miss the sweet loving words you
speak.
I feel so all alone.

I cherish all the memories
Of this wonderful friendship we share.
I'm writing this for you to let you know,
How much I really care!

Friendhips that can be shared with LOVE,
Can give you so much pleasure.
We have this kind of friendship, dear.
I know it will last forever.
