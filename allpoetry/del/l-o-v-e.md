---
title: "L o v e"
writer: "Del"
slug: "l-o-v-e"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12944-L-o-v-e-by-Del"
---

L  is for the LOVE light I see in your    eyes.
   They light up like stars in the 
   heavenly skies.
O  is for the ONLY love in my heart.
   I love you more each day, dear, 
   And pray we'll never part.
V  is for the VALUE I place on our
   devotion.
   You're worth more to me
   Than all the pearls in the ocean.
E  is for EVERYTHING you give me each
    day
    Laughter, joy and happiness.
    What more can I say?
