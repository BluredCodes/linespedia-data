title: "DREAM"

Dream, dream, dream, that's all I ever do,And in each and every dream, dear, my only thought is you.You're walking right beside me all thru the long, long, day,And if pillows would start talking,Gee, what mine would say.I simply can't forget you, oh, how I wish I could.When will you start to realize that I'm not made of wood.I want you so, my darling, why can't you want me too?I'm so tired of just dreaming, please make my dreams come true.
