---
title: "LONESOME SOLDIER"
writer: "Del"
slug: "lonesome-soldier"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6363-LONESOME-SOLDIER-by-Del"
---

Every night I see the same old stars,Same old moon shining thru.Every night in dreams you're in my arms,But, darling, where are you?What good is a moon without you?What good are all the stars above?My heart is oh, so lonely,Begging for your love.Those long cold night when things look blackYour laughter fills the air,No matter where I am, my dear,You're with me everywhere.Some day soon, the sun will shineThru a bright clear sky of blue,This lonesome soldier will hurry backAnd fulfill his dreams with you
