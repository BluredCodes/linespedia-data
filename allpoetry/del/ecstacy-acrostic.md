E njoying each others love making charms.
C uddling so close in each others arms.
S avoring each cuddle and love being 
  kissed
T hinking how much of this lovin' we've
  missed.
A wakening all our smoldering fires,
S atisfying all our sexual desires.
Y es, darling you fill my life with 
  ecstasy.
