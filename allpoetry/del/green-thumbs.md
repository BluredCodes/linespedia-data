---
title: "GREEN THUMBS"
writer: "Del"
slug: "green-thumbs"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Darling, let's put our green thumbs togetherAnd create a beautiful garden of love.Where we can stroll hand and handBeneath the peaceful blue sky up above.Where children can play, day after dayWithout a single worry or care,Knowing there'll always be someoneWith whom their love they can share.Where no one will ever go hungry,Will have a nice warm place to sleep.Where everyone will help one another,Where no one will have reason to weep.We'll plant lots of colorful flowers,And as they bloom each year,We'll take them to the less fortunateAnd give them a bit of good cheer.So let's get our green thumbs together,dear,And see what we can do,To make our world a better placeAnd keep our love so shiny new.
