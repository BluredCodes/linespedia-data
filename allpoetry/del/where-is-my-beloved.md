---
title: "Where Is My Beloved?"
writer: "Del"
slug: "where-is-my-beloved"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10241-Where-Is-My-Beloved--by-Del"
---

Where can he be tonight,
This wonderful guy I adore?
Is he still at the army training camp,
Or on some far away distant shore?

All I'm doing is pretending
That he's still right here with me.
I pray each night that in my dreams
His sweet laughing face I'll see.

My heart is so filled with sadness.
But there's nothing I can do.
To me our love is so precious.
I just know we'll see this thru.

I have so many cherished memories
That will last thru out the years.
But now whenever I think of them,
They only bring me tears.

I know the whole world's praying
To the one God, up above.
Please rid this world of hatred
And fill our hearts with love.
