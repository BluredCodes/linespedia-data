Why do I feelThis elated adorationFor the one whoGives me so much inspiration?Why? Because he's so amiable,Kind and sweet,So complimentaryEverytime we meet.When I do something for him,He's so appreciative.When it's time to be poeticHe's so very innovative.A sweet dear friendIs what he is to me.And a long time friendI hope he'll always be.
