---
title: "What God Means To Me"
writer: "Del"
slug: "what-god-means-to-me"

God is my closest, dearest friend.
He's by my side all day and night.
No matter where I go or what I do
He's never out of my sight.

I see Him in everythig that grows.
In the rapture of children at play.
I see Him caressing the leaves and flowers
With the sun on a bright summer day.

I see Him in twinkling stars in the sky,
And the bright yellow moon up above.
Even in darkness He's right by my side,
Protecting me and giving me His love.

For all of His love, he's not asking for much,
Just maybe a prayer now and then.
A prayer for no more wars in this world,
Just peace and good will to all men.
