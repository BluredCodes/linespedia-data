---
title: "Oh, I Miss You So"
writer: "Del"
slug: "oh-i-miss-you-so"
source: "AllPoetry"

I miss you so much, my beloved.Much more than you could guess.Miss the anticipation of being together,Sharing our woes and happiness.To me you're like the brightest starThat brightens up the skies.How I'd love to now be holding you,While I gaze into those dreamy eyes.My days are so lonely now,Since you're away from me.We're living in a different world,A world that isn't free.Freedom is what we're praying for,Each and every day.Maybe someday very soonYou'll be back in my arms to stay.
