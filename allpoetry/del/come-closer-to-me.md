---
title: "Come Closer To Me"
writer: "Del"

Come closer to me.
I need you right next to my heart.
Hold me tight in a warm embrace.
Promise we will never part.

Come closer to me.
I need to feel secure.
I need your heart right next to mine,
A heart so loving and pure.

Come closer to me
So our warm bodies can entwine,
So I can kiss your soft sweet lips
And your lips can be kissing mine.

Come closer to me
My heart has something to say.
"The love you share together,
Will never go away."

Come closer to me
I have so much love to give.
I want to keep sharing it with you,
As long as we both shall live
