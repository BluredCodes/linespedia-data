---
title: "Finding True Love"
writer: "Del"
slug: "finding-true-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You filled my life with sunshineThe moment we met.I knew you were that someoneI'd never forget.The star I always wished onHad finally come true.Sending me from heavenWonderful you.I yearned for love thru all my lonely years.Searched for love thru laughter and thru tears.Prayed each night someone would come along.Then I found you, and life was a beautiful song.I gave you love, I gave you my heart too.You'll never know, how much I trusted you.I gave you my life, all the love that I possess.I was so very sure I'd brought you happiness.My friends all say, you took me for a fool.You play at love and break each golden rule.They were right I know, but what else could I do,But hold my head up high and try to see it thru.You handed me the key that opened up my heart,Giving me hope, another love could start.Never did I find real happiness with you,But now I found a lover who is faithful,loving and true.
