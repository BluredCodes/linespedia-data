---
title: "Your Love"
writer: "Del"
slug: "your-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your love is like the sunshineOn a warm and summery day.Your cheerful dispositionChased all the clouds away.Your love is like the raindropsFalling from above.Saturating my bodyWith the essence of your love.Your love is like a rose petal,So fragil, so fragrantly sweet.I love you more and more, my dear.Every time we meet.Your love is like the morning dew,Clinging like silvery mist,Fresh and delightfulLike the sweetness of your kiss.Your love means all the world to me.You are my brightest star.Everything that's wonderful,My dear, is what you are.
