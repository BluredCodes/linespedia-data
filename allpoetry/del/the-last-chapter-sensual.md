Fate makes us go thru many chapters.
The chapters before I ever loved you
Are faded memories, now stored away,
Replaced by memories of a new love so true.

We proved our love to each other
At the beach under the pale moonlight.
We could hear the waves kissing the shore,
While stars sparkled like diamonds so bright.

You hands so tenderly caressed my body.
Kisses scorched me, like sparks from our fire.
My trembling body kept pulling you closer.
Never had I felt such a burning desire.

No way could you keep from showing
The desire you felt for me.
Two heated bodies became only one.
Two lucky hearts won't ever be free.

Two hearts together, to begin the last chapter.
To live, laugh and love together.
Faithfully promising each other that night
That their love would last forever.This poem was written to enter a challenge where the words (the last chapter had to be used in the title or poem)  Del
