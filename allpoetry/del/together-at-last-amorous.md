You unlocked the motel door,And we slowly entered the room.I never thought we'd get this chance,At least not quite this soon.The love we felt for each otherWas bursting at the seams.All these months our precious loveWas only released in dreams.I sat on the bed, you pulled the drapes,Than sat very close to me.You caressed my face so lovingly.My whole body filled with ecstacy.It didn't take us very longTo shed our summer clothing.You placed your heated body over mine.I could feel your maleness growing.Your kisses were hot and heavy now.My body trembled with desire.We were both so very nervous.Our hearts were now on fire.The release you had you didn't expect.It was just a little too fast."Oh, my darling, I'm so sorry." you said.I really wanted it to last.You kept apologizing while holding me tight.I laughed and said, "Hey, it's really quite alright.Just let me take over now, my dear.We've got the rest of the night.
