title: "Who Needs Someone?"

Who needs someone
To tell her troubles too,
To cheer her when she's lonely
And when she's feeling blue?

Who needs someone
To love her every day,
To kiss her waiting eager lips
In an amorous adoring way?

Who needs someone
To cuddle up to at night,
To feel their bodies warming up
While holding each other tight?

Who needs someone?
I guess it's plain to see,
That this someone has to be,
No one else, but ME.
