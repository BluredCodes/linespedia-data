Two lips aching to be kissed
As I gaze into your dark brown eyes.
You reach out your arms to enfold me,
Feelings of passion give way to sighs.

I can feel the hardness of your body,
As you press me so close to you.
Kissing my eager lips so hungrily.
Two tongues are making love too.

I feel your hands caressing my back,
Then my lips, then my thighs.
So swiftly you unbutton my blouse
Giving me such a pleasant surprise.

You release my breasts from their confinement,
Take one in each of your hands.
Right now I'm ready for anything
Regardless of your demands.

Very swiftly we take off our clothes,
Once again you hold me so tight.
Then when both of our hearts entwine,
We make love all thru the night.
