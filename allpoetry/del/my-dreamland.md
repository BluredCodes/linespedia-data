---
title: "My Dreamland"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-dreamland"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

A big full moon is shiningThru my window tonight.As I lay here trying to sleep,Your face is always in sight.It seems like you're watching over me.An angel from heaven above.Trying to reasure meOf your kind and patient love.I wish you were here besides me,Real snug in my loving arms,So I could prove how much I love you.And you could show me all your charms.When I finally fall asleep, my dear,One thing I'd love to do.Is meet you in my dreamlandSo I can prove my love for you.Dreams are like our tomorrows.You'll never know what they will bring.If we're lucky to meet in my dreamland tonight,It'll sure make this lonely heart sing.
