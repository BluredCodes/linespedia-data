---
title: "Live For Today"
writer: "Del"
slug: "live-for-today"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Love lights up my lifeLike the sun lights up the sky.When I think of the one I love,I feel like a bright star up high.When I need him, he's always there.His sweet loving ways make me smile.I never had any faith in love.But I know now it's still in style.My love for him is like a fairy tale.I'm up in the clouds night and day.I feel that God up above,Just wants it to be this way.There are things in life we cannot change.So there's only one thing I can say.Do what your heart tells you to do.Forget about tomorrow, LIVE FOR TODAY.
