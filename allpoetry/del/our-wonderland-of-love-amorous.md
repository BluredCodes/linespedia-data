title: "OUR WONDERLAND OF LOVE (Amorous)"

Let's meet tonight in WonderlandOur Wonderland of Love.So we can fulfill this yearning of ours,Under that big, bright moon up above.Where you can hold me in your loving arms,And we can share a night of bliss.Our hungry lips have never touched,Not even in a friendly kiss.My lips are waiting to devour you.I have such a burning passionnate desire,To kiss you wherever you want me to,Then set your smoldering heart afire.Then I want to feel your body close to mine,Just as close as close can be.When I whisper, "Darling, I love you so,"You pledge your undying love for me.We can hear the nightingales singingSomewhere up above.Oh, what a heavenly night we'll spendIn our Wonderland of Love.
