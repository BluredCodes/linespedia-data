title: "MY SILENT HEART"

Silent is my love for youAs it's growing in my heart.Like a rare orchid, so precious,But it can never speak its part.This silent heart of mineWill only speak to me.It just told me you would like to beKissing me tenderly.Tell me more, dear heart of mine.Does he love me too?Will he treat me lovinglyAll our whole lives thru?"Yes, he loves you dearly,But his heart is silent too.It will only speak to him,Like I am speaking to you."Maybe someday, who knows when?Our silence can be broken,And the lovely expressions of our loveCan easily be spoken.
