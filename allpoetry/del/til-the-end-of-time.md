title: "TIL THE END OF TIME"

How long will I love you?I will love you til the birds stop singing,Until the bells stop ringing,Until the sky's no longer blue.That's how long I'll love you.I will love you til the rain stops falling,Until the whippoorwills stop calling,Until there's no morning dew.That's how long I'll love you.I will love you til the winds refuse to blow,Until there is no more falling snow.Until the colors lose their hue.That's how long I'll love you.So, darling, to make my love poem rhymeI will love you TIL THE END OF TIME.
