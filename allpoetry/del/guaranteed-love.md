---
title: "Guaranteed Love"
writer: "Del"

Please be gentle to my heart.It will only handle a love that's true.It needs a definite guarantee,Before I can offer it to you.Your sweet and passionate kissesAre filling me with desire.Oh, how I want to give you my love.You're setting my heart on fire.It's such a beautiful night for romance.A gentle breeze cooling the air.So tenderly you kiss my lipsAnd softly stroke my hair.You hold me so very close to youAnd whisper in my ear."You know I've loved you for oh, so long,And will forever, my dear.""You mean all the world to me.I'll never let you go.Please tell me you'll accept my love.This heart of mine loves you so."A soft voice coming from up above,Spoke these words to me.Accept this sincere and honest love.I'll give you that guarantee.
