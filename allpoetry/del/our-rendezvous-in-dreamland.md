---
title: "Our Rendezvous In Dreamland"
writer: "Del"
slug: "our-rendezvous-in-dreamland"
source: "AllPoetry"

When I am in bed at night
And just can't fall asleep,
I pretend I'll meet you in dreamland,
And a rendezvous we'll keep.

But when I finally fall asleep
And see this wonderland,
I search and search, but all I see
Are footprints in the sand.

I follow these footprints one by one
See a silouette near the shore.
I call your name, you turn around,
And I search for you no more.

We run into each others arms
You hold me oh, so tight.
Darling, don't ever let me go.
I want to love you tonight.

What a wonderful glorious feeling,
Proving our love so true,
But only in my dreams, my dear,
Could I spend a night with you.
