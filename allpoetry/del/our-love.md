---
title: "OUR LOVE"
writer: "Del"
slug: "our-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9251-OUR-LOVE-by-Del"
---

Our love is as sweet as the first breath of spring.That inspires the birds and starts them to sing.Our love is as warm as a summery day,That bring on the laughter of children at play.Our love in the autumn takes on a new hueJust like the trees with their colors so new.Our love is so beautiful, it sets me aglowLike a wintery day after the first fallen snow.The world at its best is how I compareThis wonderful heavenly love that we share.
