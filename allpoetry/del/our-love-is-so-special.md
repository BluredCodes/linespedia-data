---
title: "Our Love Is So Special"
writer: "Del"
slug: "our-love-is-so-special"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11182-Our-Love-Is-So-Special-by-Del"
---

There are tears on my pillow tonight.Wishing you were holding me tight.But you're always so far away from me.Our two loving hearts will never be free.Free to prove how much we care,And the tender kisses we'd love to share.Free to hold each other tight.Free to love all thru the night.I love all the sweet things you have to say.You always seem to make my day.So I count my blessings, thank God above,For this very, special kind of love
