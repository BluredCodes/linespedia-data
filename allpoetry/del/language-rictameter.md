Language
Words that you use
Reflect what's in your heart
Kind words spoken bring us sunshine
Four letter cuss words bring dark clouds and gloom
It's love that makes the world go round
Hate brings on wars and strife
Emit LOVE with
LanguageThis Rictameter is written for all the poets who do emit LOVE and sunshine with their poems, not hate and gloom.
