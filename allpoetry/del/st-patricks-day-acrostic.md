S ave those kisses for me, 
  darling.
T ell me you care.

P lease say you'll always love 
  me,
A nd say you'll always be there.
T his love of mine is tried and
  true.
R emember, my sweet, it comes 
  from my heart.
I promise I'll never leave you,
C ause this feeling will never
  depart.
K isses you give me are sweeter
  then honey.
S unshine follows wherever you 
  go.

D arling, you mean the world to 
  me.
A nd now I'm sure you know, I
  LOVE YOU SO.
Y ours forever and ever.

HAPPY ST. PATRICK'S DAY
