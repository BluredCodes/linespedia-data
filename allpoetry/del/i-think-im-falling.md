title: "I Think I'm Falling"

Sometimes I think I'm falling
Right out of a clear sky of blue,
Right into the arms of someone
And that someone, my dear is you.

Sometimes I think I'm falling
In love with all of your charms.
I was so thrilled the day you caught me
Right in your loving arms.

Sometimes I think I'm falling
Too deeply in love with you.
Cause I'm not too sure you'll always
Feel the way you say you do.

Sometimes I think I'm falling,
But, dear, you know very well.
I don't have to think I'm falling
Because you know I already fell.
