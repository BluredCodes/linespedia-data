---
title: "THE STARS KNOW"
writer: "Del"

My dearest one, here I am again,Gazing out of my window at the quiet star studded sky.So alone and lonely, thinking of you only,Wanting you so much that I could cry.All those tiny twinkling starsIn the wondrous heaven above,Are sending tonight their consoling lightTo this poor stupid fool in love.I should have known the moment we metOur love could never be.But for a precious short time your sweet love was mineAnd, darling, it was heavenly.Now all those tiny twinkling starsThat brighten up the sky,Seem to know I love you soAnd will until I die.All good things must end, they say,Even fools in love must part.But, darling, I'll wait and trust in fateAnd keep your love locked in my heart.
