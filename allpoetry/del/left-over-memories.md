---
title: "LEFT OVER MEMORIES"
writer: "Del"

Once you told me this heart of yoursBelonged to only me.Now it seems this heart of yoursWants only to be free.Tell me, darling, what can I doTo put that spark back in your heart?I'll do anything you want me to.I'm so miserable since we're apart.I have such wonderful memoriesLike the first night me made love.It was at the beach, under the starsWith a bright full moon up above.Lips so tenderly kissed me,As your body pressed close to mine.We both admitted the thrill we feltWas so competely, supremely devine.I'll never forget you, darling.I'll just keep on loving you,And if you stay away forever.Left over memories will have to do.
