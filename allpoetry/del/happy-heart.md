title: "HAPPY HEART"

I have a happy heartThat's warm, sincere and true.As long as you want it, dearIt will only belong to you.It's so completely satisfiedWith the thrills you give to me.You have captured it completely,It never wants to be free.You have filled my life with sunshine.You have taught me how to love,You are my special angelGod sent from up above.Things you say excite me so,Start all my emotions reeling.Everytime I talk to youI get that erotic feeling.I hope your heart is happyAnd feels the same way too.No feeling could be greaterThan sharing a love so true. i
