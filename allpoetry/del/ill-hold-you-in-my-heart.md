---
title: "I'll Hold You In My Heart"
writer: "Del"
slug: "ill-hold-you-in-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You brightened up my life.
I adore you, sweetheart.
You make all my days worthwhile.
I hope we'll never part.

My angel on earth, my wonderful one,
To you no one can compare.
Have faith in me, I implore you.
For you I'll always be there.

How can I ever forget
Those nights we shared together?
We've known each other for such a short time,
But it seems like its been forever.

Things will never change, I'm sure.
We'll always be apart.
I can't hold you in my arms, my dear,
But I'll always hold you in my heart.
