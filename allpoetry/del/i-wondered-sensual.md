I often wondered, darling,
What it would be like to be
Hugged and kissed by you.
Now it's happening to me!

I wondered what it would feel like
To have your nude body so close to mine,
And to smell the heated maleness of you.
Oh, the feeling is so devine.

I wondered if your hands would touch me
And give me that exotic feeling.
Yes, yes, right there, sweetheart.
Your touch is so appealing.

I wondered about so many things
That the two of us would share.
I'll return to you all these favors,
To prove how much I care.

This rapturous feelng I'm feeling now
I know you're feeling too.
I won't have to wonder any more
If this love of ours is true.
