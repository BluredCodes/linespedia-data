---
title: "LOVING YOU"
writer: "Del"
slug: "loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9415-LOVING-YOU-by-Del"
---

My darling, I love you so.I know in a few short moments I'll have to go.But before I do, there's so much I want to say.First, that I'll miss you, miss you more and more each day.How could I have lived in a world without you?You are my inspiration in everything I do.A million time a day I stare into space,Trying so hard to recapture your sweet loving face.I want you so and need you, more than you ever could guess.Sweetheart, need I tell you, you're my every happiness?No other love could be as sweet, as the love I share with you.Every night I thank God for your love so warm and true.One more thing I'd like to say, before we have to part.You'll ALWAYS be the one I'll love,So goodnight, sweet dreams, sweetheart.
