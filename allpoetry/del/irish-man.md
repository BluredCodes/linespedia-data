title: "Irish Man"
writer: "Del"
slug: "irish-man"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm an Irish man from Ireland.
I always were a smile.
My thinking's always positive.
I make your life worthwhile.

You know I'll aways love you.
I will never make you blue.
Forever you will own, my dear,
This heart so warm and true.
