You just stood there looking at me,Guess you didn't know what to do.Seems like both of our wildest dreamsHave finally come true.You took both my hands and drew me close.Two aching hearts found each other.As you enfolded me into your loving arms,Two aching hearts began to flutter.Then for the first time your lips brushed mine.At first so slowly, so softly, so tender,Then with the strong desire we feltTwo tongues touched in sweet surrender.With trembling hands we both undressed.I felt hot moist kisses caressing me.As we lie down on the bed,I felt such a breathtaking ecstasy.Hand so tender searching my body,Touching all the right places,Filling my senses with euphoriaWhile enjoying those exotic embraces."I love you so much." I whispered to you.You said you've loved me for oh, so long.Now these two aching hearts that werecryingAre singing a victory song.
