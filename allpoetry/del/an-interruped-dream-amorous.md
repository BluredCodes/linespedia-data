I had a crazy wonderful dream last night.I dreamt I felt someone in bed with me.It was a little scary at first,So I flicked on a light to see.You were in the nude, like me.A smile on your face and quivering with desire.You tenderly touched my face, then kissed my lips.The passion I suddenly felt was a burning fire.Your kisses so gentle, felt our tongues embrace.Experenced hands caressing me - simply devine!Couldn't wait to get closer to you,So I could make you mine.We were both so ready to fulfill our desires.I get so hot and bothered and I wonderHow this heavenly dream would have ended,Had I not been awakened by that loud crash of thunder.
