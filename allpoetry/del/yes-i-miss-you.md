---
title: "YES, I MISS YOU"
writer: "Del"
slug: "yes-i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Thank you for the E-mailYou sent to me today.I am so excited,I don't know what to say.I had told myself you didn't care,It was just another fling.When I read the words "I love you."It made my sad heart sing.You asked me if I missed youSince we've been apart.Darling, words just can't expressThis longing in my heart.My nights are oh, so lonely.It's so hard to dismissThe wonderful memories of youAnd your sweet and tender kiss.Yes, my dear, I love you too,So hurry back to meTo share our love foreverThru all eternity
