title: "NEW YEAR'S EVE 2002"

The man in the moon is sad tonight,As the world begins a new year.He sees so many, with such  sad hearts,Because their loved ones aren't here.Their hearts are filled with much sorrow,Because of that fateful day in September.A day that shocked the whole wide world.A day we will forever remember.Why can't we all just love one another,As we live our lives each day?Let's all pray to our God up aboveTo keep all this evil away.
