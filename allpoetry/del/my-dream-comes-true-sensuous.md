title: "My Dream Comes True (Sensuous)"

The logs in the fireplace are dimming.
We're dancing to our favorite tune.
Two cheeks pressed together so tightly.
I've dreamed about this for so long.

I can sense the heat in your body.
The thrill I'm feeling is so new.
My heart is so hungry for your love.
Is my nightly dream finally coming true?

Slowly your lips move to touch my cheek,
Then I feel your kiss so tender.
Darling, my passion is heating up,
With all my heart I surrender.

Now your lips are crushing mine.
Slowly we sink to the floor.
It doesn't take long to shed our clothes.
Our passion is beginning to soar.

Your hands caress my body,
While you hungrily keep kissing me.
Then your lips move to my waiting breasts,
I'm getting drugged with ecstacy.

I whisper in your ear
As I draw you close to me.
Take me now, my darling,
I'm ready, can't you see?

So the nightly dream I've been dreaming.
Is finally coming true.
Tonight's the night I'm going to prove,
How much I really love you.
