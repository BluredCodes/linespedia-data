---
title: "Pain of love"
writer: "Del"

Many times during a lifetime, we feel pain,
Sprains, aches, hundreds of afflictions.
Many cure themselves or can be 
helped,
With our family doctor's prescriptions.

The one pain that can't be helped,
With any kind of medication,
Is the deep hurting pain that affects the heart,
Which needs lots of inspirational stimulation.

My heart is now feeling the pain of love,
And oh, how the sensual thrill it misses!
Every night it feels so sad and blue
Without the nourishment of your kisses.

The deep pain I'm feeling in my heart,
Just won't ever go away.
Until you come back into my heart
And promise me, it's there you'll stay.
