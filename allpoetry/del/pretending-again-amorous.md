Your moist warm kisses were so sweetAs you kissed my eager lips.When I put my hand on your shoulderYou caressed me with finger tips.First my hair, then my neck,Then down to my waiting breasts.So gently, so softly you fondled them.No way did I protest.My body quivered as you resumedYour journey toward the utimate spot.You exchanged finger tips for your lips.Now your kisses were moist and hot.The rapture was overwhelming.You knew exactly what to do.Soon our bodies were joined as oneWith a lovin' so warm and true.As I lay here in my cozy bed,Hugging my pillow to my cheek.I realized my crazy pretending was overAnd now I must get some sleep.
