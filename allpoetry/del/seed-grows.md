title: "Seed Grows"
writer: "Del"

My love for you is sincere, my dear.Never doubt it for one minute.Not one hour of a day goes by,When thoughts of you aren't in it.Our love started with a tiny seed,Which slowly grew and grew.Will it grow forever, dear?I wish I really knew.Nothng will last forever,Of that we are aware.Many of us love and lose,No matter how much we care.While love is here, enjoy it,Keep that fire in  your heart.Give that seed your loving care,And it won't ever want to depart.
