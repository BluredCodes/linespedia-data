title: "DON'T LET ME WAIT FOREVER"
writer: "Del"

My darling, can't you really seeHow very much you mean to me?I miss you more than you could guess,Longing so for your caress.Don't let me wait forever.I can't forget your kisses so sweet,Only you could make my life complete.It's you my heart is crying for,Oh, just to be with you once more.Don't let me wait forever.Love is such a crazy thing,At times it makes you want to sing,But when your lover's gone from youAll it does is make you blue.I've been so very blue, my dear,Wishing so that you were here.Now I've confessed my love for you,So, darling, if you love me too,Don't let me wait forever.
