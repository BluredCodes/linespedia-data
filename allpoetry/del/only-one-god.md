---
title: "Only One God"
writer: "Del"

If there were only one religion in this world,
Our world would be filled with love.
Everyone woould direct their prayers
To this one God up above.

No one will ever know, just how this world began,
No one can tell us what happens when we die.
All of our beliefs were started by someone.
We're wondering the reason why.

Let's all fill our hearts with love,
Show the world we really care.
Send all our prayers to God above,
For we know that He'll be there.

The mysteries of life are miracles
We'll never quite comprehend.
We know God had a hand in it.
He'll be with us until the end.

So often these different religions
Are the cause of wars and strife.
If we all only believed in one God,
We'd live a much more peaceful life.
