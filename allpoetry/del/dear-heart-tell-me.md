---
title: "Dear Heart Tell Me"
writer: "Del"

My heart longs so much for someone.I just can't tell him how I feel.I never believed in true love.Never thought it was for real.There are many different kinds of loveInvading your heart thru the years.You love your friends and your family,In your teens love can bring you tears.Then you finally think you found it.For years and years it seems,Because of some exotic evenings,You found the man of your dreams.Your love for him keeps growingIn another different way.You would never hurt each other,And life goes on day, by day, by day.Then like a bolt of lightningOut of a dark cloud up above,Your heart is again invadedBy another kind of love.This kind of love is so different, neverplanned.If you're not together every day, you feelso blue.I implore you, dear heart of mine,If this is truly LOVE, please tell me what to do.
