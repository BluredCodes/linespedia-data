Top of the morning to you, dear lassie,
A top of the morning to you.
I greet you on this Paddy's Day,
With my love so warm and true.

Come walk along besides me, dear,
Thru the doorway of tomorrows,
To share our joy and happiness.
And together endure our sorrows.
