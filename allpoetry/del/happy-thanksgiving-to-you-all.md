We have so many things to be thankful for
On Thanksgiving Day this year.
Let's pray for all the families
Whose loved ones won't be here.

Our men and women are fighting for us,
On some far off distant shore.
So many Americans have been killed,
While our country was not at war.

One country fighting another country,
Is how we used to define a war.
But now with these scheming terrorists,
Life will never be the same as before.

So on this Thanksgiving Day,
Let's all pray to God above.
He needs everyone's prayers every day,
So he can fill all our hearts with LOVE.I want to wish all the great friends I met on this site a very HAPPY TURKEY DAY.     Luv,  Del
