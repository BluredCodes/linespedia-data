---
title: "Together, At Last (Sensual)"
writer: "Del"
slug: "together-at-last-sensual"

We're soaring upward toward heaven,
Two hearts beating as one, for the first time.
We've waited for this moment for so long,
But thought it could never be.

Seems like an angel sent from above,
Touched us both with her magic wand.
When you walked thru the door, I was shocked.
I thought, how real this dream seems to be!

We looked at each other, I held my breath.
Slowly, you closed the distance between us.
Then you took me tenderly in your arms.
And just kept holding me so close.
Not a word was spoken, so quiet, then only sighs.

Finally we gazed into each other's eyes.
Your finger tips slowly caressed my face.
Our lips just aching to be kissed.
First, just touching, slowly, tenderly.
Then your lips are crushing mine.
Two tongues savoring each other.

Oh, how I dreamed of this scene so many times.
Never thought it could ever come true.
You are everything I dreamed you would be.
So tender, so loving, so caring, so precious.

The esctasy I felt was overwhelming my heart.
I could feel the throbbing of yours, next to mine.
When we reached the untimate goal of fulfillment,
Our long awaited heavenly dream had come true.
