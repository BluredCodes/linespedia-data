---
title: "Should I?"
writer: "Del"
slug: "should-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Should I give up my heart again
To someone as sweet as you?
Should I believe in love again?
Where will it lead me to?

Should I dance with you in the moonlight,
With millions of stars up above?
Should I stay in your arms forever,
And whisper, "It's you I love?"

Maybe you think I'm foolish
To doubt romance as I do,
But so many things can happen,
That will break a heart in two.

Should I believe in your kisses?
Please don't tell me a lie.
Should I believe in your charms when I'm in your arms?
Darling, please tell me, should I?
