---
title: "True Love"
writer: "Del"
slug: "true-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10701-True-Love-by-Del"
---

Love often leaves you with much to desire.
At first, it sets your heart on fire.
You think your love is steadfast and strong.
Then you realize how things can go wrong.
When true love hits, you'll be sure to know, 
Because true love has much more to show.
True love means much caring and sharing,
Not how you look or what clothes you are wearing.
True love grows deeper day after day,
Cause the one you love wants to stay.
Wants to be with you for ever and ever,
Promises faithfully to leave you never.
