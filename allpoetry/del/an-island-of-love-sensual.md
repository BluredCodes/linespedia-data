In Paradise there's an island called Love.Where there's always a bright shiny sun up above.No dark clouds to darken the skyWhere euphoria keeps our spirits up high.Where you and your love can stroll hand in hand.Then stop to make love on the warm glistening sand.Everyone's nude in this glorious place,No signs of sadness on anyone's face.All hearts are singing with rapture and joy,No reason for anyone to be bashful or coy.My love stroked my body so eager and willing,Kissed me all over, so breathtakingly thrilling.Then slowly entered my chamber of love,While we heard the morning doves coo up above.Entrance to this heavenly island, it seems.Can only be obtained thru our amorous dreams.
