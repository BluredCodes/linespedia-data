---
title: "It Feels So Good"
writer: "Del"
slug: "it-feels-so-good"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11184-It-Feels-So-Good-by-Del"
---

Your smile is like the sunshine
Glimmering thru my window each day.
When we're close together every night,
I adore the sweet things you say.

I feel as cozy as a bug in a rug
When your strong arms hold me tight,
And like a cherub little angel,
While you're loving me at night.

You make me feel as precious as
A multi karat diamond ring.
You make my heart so happy
It always wants to sing.

I know you love me very much,
Maybe a little more than you should.
You know exactly what pleaures me,
And, baby, it feels sooo good.
