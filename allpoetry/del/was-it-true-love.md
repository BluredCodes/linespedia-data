---
title: "Was It True Love?"
writer: "Del"
slug: "was-it-true-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11628-Was-It-True-Love--by-Del"
---

Don't ever say "I love you,"Until you feel it down deep in your heart,Cause if you do and it isn't true,You'll just tear someone's world apart.Too many hearts are broken,When these words are carelessly used.A heart is like a butterfly's wing,It is so very easily bruised.The word "love" is so fickle.It is filled with imitations.True love coming straight from the heart,Is love without reservations.If you believed those three little words,And now found out they weren't true.Time will heal sorrow, of that I'm sure.Someday true love will come to you.
