title: "A Father To Be"
writer: "Del"

The time has come for my darling
To bring into this world a new life.
After nine long months of waiting,
What a thrill this will be for my wife!

A new life to love will soon be ours,
To feed, to clothe and enjoy.
We have no preference for either sex,
Be it a girl or a boy.

When this baby comes into our world,
Please let it be healthy and strong.
We pray to you every day, dear God
That nothing will go wrong.

When we come home with this bundle of joy,
We promise you faithfully,
We'll give it all the love we have
Thru all eternity.
