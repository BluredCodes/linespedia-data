---
title: "My Lover's Charms"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-lovers-charms"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13158-My-Lovers-Charms-by-Del"

I can feel the soft warm breezeFrom my window, cooling, the air.As I gaze into my lover's eyesI can see the desire there.His eager hands are caressing me.I feel his velvety finger tipsStroking my hair and my faceAnd touching my warm moist lips.I feel the heat of his body,As he kisses my cares away.How I'd love to feel this thrillEach and every day!Now I'm lying here so close to him,Nestled in his arms.So completely satisfiedWith all my lover's charms
