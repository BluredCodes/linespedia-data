title: "LOVE FINALIZED (Amorous"

My lips were clinging to yours,
Not wanting to let you go.
I've been waiting for this day for so long,
Because I love you so.

I want your arms around me
Holding me oh, so tight.
I want you to make love to me
All thru the long, long night.

You slowly unbuttom my blouse
As your sweet lips keep kissing mine.
After I help you remove my clothes,
Your kisses are now, even more devine.

Your tender hands are caressing my body,
I run my fingers thru your hair.
By now you must know, my darling
How much I really care.

The door to my heart is opening,
Waiting for you to go inside.
My heart has now reached fulfillment
And is glowing with so much pride.
