What would we do without E-mail?
This wonderful, fantastic inovation?
It certainly has exhilarated our lives 
By giving us this communicable inspiration.

There are so many friends we wouldn't have,
Without this means of communication.
It brightens so many hours each day
And gives us such augumentation.

How many times have you forgotten
To send someone congratulations.
Just go ahead and E-mail them
Without any reservations.

Once your life has revolved around it,
With so much gratification,
You know you'll never be without it.
This wonderful, fantastic inovation.
