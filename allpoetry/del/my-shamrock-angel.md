---
title: "My shamrock angel"
writer: "Del"

I see your Irish eyes smiling
Whenever I look at you.
You're sweeter than all the roses
Sprinkled with morning dew.

You're my darling Shamrock Angel,
Sent to me from God above.
You have made my life worthwhile
There is no one else I could love.

You always make me happy,
Never sad or blue.
I adore my Shamrock Angel
If you met her, you would too.
