title: "Love On Hold (Sensual)"
writer: "Del"
slug: "love-on-hold-sensual"
source: "AllPoetry"

I just can't give you the love you're craving.My darling, I wish I could.You have such a sweet gentle heart,Certainly not made of wood.Your words of love are enticing,I can't get them out of my mind.I could search the whole world over,And no one like you could I find.I'd love to hold you in my armsAnd kiss you tenderly.And keep caressing your bodyWhile you make love to me.I know I'll keep on wishingOn every falling star,That time will never change you,You'll stay as sweet as you are.Some day at the end of the rainbow,We'll find a pot of gold,Until that day, my sweetheart,We'll put our love on hold.
