---
title: "JUST FRIENDS"
writer: "Del"
slug: "just-friends"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I've tried so hard to captureYour wild and reckless heart,But I realize you don't intendTo give our love a start.I've spent so many days and nightsJust dreaming of you, dear.Oh, how I wished with all my mightThat I could hold you near.I feel so very much for you,But love's a funny thing.It doesn't always cooperateAnd let a sad heart sing.True love comes right from the heart,And all hearts don't agree.So even tho' I want your heart,I guess it's not for me.Nothing I would say or doCould ever make amends.So, darling, I will have to beContent with being, just friends.
