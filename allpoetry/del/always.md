---
title: "Always"
writer: "Del"
slug: "always"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14241-Always-by-Del"
---

There are so many memories I have of you.So many dreams that really came true.I'll remember them always.Those long walks we took every night,Sometimes when the moon was shining so bright.I'll remember them always.You tenderly took me in your arms,Showing me all your wonderful charms.I'll remember them always.The rapture I felt was completely divine,When your soft sweet lips were kissing mine.I'll remember them always.All these precious memories I will endeavorTo store in my heart forever and everI'll remember them always.
