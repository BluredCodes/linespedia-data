D ays are filled with sunshine.R omantic thoughts are yours and mine.E verytime you're near, my heart is   singing.A nd when we touch, I feel a tingling.M y dream lover, I adore you.L ately you're always on my mind.O nly you can break the ties that bind.V ery surely my love is growing.E ven my heart tells me it's showing.R omantic lover, I adore you.
