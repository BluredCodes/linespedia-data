---
title: "OUR ROSE"
writer: "Del"
slug: "our-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6146-OUR-ROSE-by-Del"
---

Deep in my heart you planted a seedThat memorable night, so long ago.And from that tiny little seedA beautiful rose did grow.Oh, what pain the thorns inflictedOn my faithful tender heart,During all the long and lonely nightsYou kept us far apart.But that lovely rose refused to die.It struggled so to stay alive.My deep and devoted love for youHas helped the rose survive.Now our rose is an everblooming oneFor you came back to me.We'll share its beauty and its thornsThru all eternity.
