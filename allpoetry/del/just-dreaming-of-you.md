---
title: "JUST DREAMING OF YOU"
writer: "Del"
slug: "just-dreaming-of-you"

The lake is serene, the waters so blue,I sit at the shore, dear, just dreaming of you.The fleecy white clouds are having such funPlaying hide and go seek with the warm summer sun.So gently the waves are fondling the sand,Like the soft gentle touch of a lover's hand.Children all around us so carefree and gayRejoicing in the splendor of this heavenly day.These wonders of nature go on endlesslyLike my love for you, that forever will be.I love you so, darling, and miss you so too.Oh, why must I go on, dear, just dreaming of you.
