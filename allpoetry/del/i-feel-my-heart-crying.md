title: "I Feel My Heart Crying"

I can feel my heart crying
Every night and day.
My heart wants you near me,
Not so far away.

It flutters like a butterfly
Whenever I think of you,
Then I feel the tear drops
Like tiny drops of dew.

Please stop crying, my dear heart.
There's nothing we can do.
We have to pretend everything's fine,
And try to see this thru.

Maybe someday an angel
From heaven up above,
Will tap us on the shoulder
And sanctify our love.
