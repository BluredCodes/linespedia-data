title: "My Special Friend"

A friend in need, is a friend indeed.
How true these words are!
You manage to acquire so many friends
All thru your lifetime.

Remember back to your childhood.
Memories of some friends
Are etched in your mind forever.

During each phase of your life,
There always was a "special someone"
Someone, who seemed to have
Borrowed a piece of your heart.
You trusted this person
So sincerely, without reservations.

As you grew older
These special friends were much harder to find.
The fast pace of living, daily pressures
All seemed to occupy so much of your time.

Ironically, this is the time
When you truly need a "special friend,"
A special friend you can count on,
A friend who has borrowed
Even a bigger piece of your heart.

Someone you trust so sincerely.
Someone you know will cheer you when you're sad.
Someone you know you can tell all your troubles to.
Someone who enjoys doing favors for you.
Someone like this is really hard to find.

So, I thank God every day, my dear friend.
To have given me someone as wonderful as  YOU.I will welcome comments on this one, since it is one of my few attempts at non-rhyming poetry.  Del
