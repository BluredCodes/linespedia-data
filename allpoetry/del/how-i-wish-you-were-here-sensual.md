I just can't help wondering, darling,
Just where you are tonight.
Wish you were here besides me
Holding me tight.

You're so far away from me.
I really don't know where.
I know you're thinking of me.
We have a special love, so rare.

I have to live with memories,
Maybe for a long, long time.
Now the clouds are dark, my dear.
Someday again the sun will shine.

Every night while I'm lying in bed,
My mind goes reminiscing.
I feel the thrill of your roaming hands,
While my erotic site you're kissing.

I get so hot and bothered,
There's only one thing I can do.
I get out my little personal friend,
And make believe it's you.
