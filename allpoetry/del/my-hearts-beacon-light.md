---
title: "My Heart's Beacon Light"
writer: "Del"

I'm going to love you tonight, my sweet,While my heart is still beaming.Our tomorrows will only come,If I can keep right on dreaming.The beacon light in my heartIs flickering like a candle.I know that all this depressionIs more than it can handle.It was shining so brightly now, for so long,As our love was growing stronger.Now it feels so desolate,And can bear the pain no longer.Please don't give up on me, dear heart.As long as dreams, I can still endeavor,This love I cannot lose.I want your beacon light to shine forever.
