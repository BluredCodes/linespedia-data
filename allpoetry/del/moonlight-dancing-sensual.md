title: "MOONLIGHT DANCING (Sensual)"

Let's go dancing, darling,Just you and I,Under this pale full moonAnd star studded sky.Hold me close to you,As we dance barefoot in the sand.This is a first for you and I.Gee, it feels so grand.Near by a band is playingAt a concert in the park.Two silhouettes, so very closeAre dancing in the dark.Don't hold me quite so close.Your body is too entrancing.It's not so easy, darlingTo make love while we're dancing.You whisper, "I really want you now."So how can I resist?I slowly sink down to the sand,Two hungry lips, ready to be kissed.I thank God every dayFor your love so warm and true.I hope way down deep in your heartYou feel the same ways too.
