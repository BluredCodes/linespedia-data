title: "SO VERY PRECIOUS (Amorous)"
writer: "Del"

True love is so very precious,Your heart is filled with desire.A desire nothing can erase.Sometimes you say to yourself"Stop it, it's crazy to feel this way."True love is such a strong emotion.Usually felt only once in a lifetime.So different from hate,Which manifests itself many times.True love makes you long to hold someoneSo close to your hungry heart.You want to smother him with kissesWhile your hands are stroking his body.You want him to return all those favors.You want him to be in total command.You want him to ignite a fire in your sensesTil you both reach the explosion point.Then you want him to hold you in his armsWhile he tenderly and lovingly kisses you goodnight.
