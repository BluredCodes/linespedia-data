---
title: "MY TRUE FRIEND"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-true-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's great to have a friendI can tell my troubles to.It's great to have a friendWho's faithful, kind and true.It great to have a friendWho cheers me when things look grim.Who appreciates so very muchLittle things I do for himHe's like the brightest shining starThat lightens up the night.He never leaves me feeling sadMakes everything seem right.I hope he never leaves meThis special friend of mine.I'd like to be his friend forever,Until the end of time.
