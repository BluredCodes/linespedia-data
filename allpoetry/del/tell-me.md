---
title: "Tell Me"
writer: "Del"
slug: "tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10698-Tell-Me-by-Del"
---

Tell me, dear, all the things I want to hear.
Tell me, you love me and always want me near.
Tell me, my kisses fill you with desire.
Tell me, my loving sets your heart afire.
Tell me, you miss me, whenever we're apart.
Tell me, you'll always own my heart.
Tell me, our love is a blessing from above.
Tell me, forever I'll be your only love.
Tell me, you know, you and I are meant to be.
Tell me, you know I'll never set you free.
