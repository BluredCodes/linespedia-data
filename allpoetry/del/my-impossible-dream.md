---
title: "My Impossible Dream"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-impossible-dream"

Lately I'm dreaming impossible dreams,That could never come true.The nights that they seem so very realAre the nights my dreams are of you.When you kiss me, darlingAnd hold me so close to you,No one in this wide, wide worldCould thrill me more than you do.An impossible dream can be a pleasureKnown by no one, but me.There is no one I have to account to,And my heart will always be free.Impossible dreams leave no expectations,Since these dreams can never come true,But in the morning it's so refreshingTo have spent a night, dear, with you.
