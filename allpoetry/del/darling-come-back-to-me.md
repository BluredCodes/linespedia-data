---
title: "Darling, Come Back To Me"
writer: "Del"
slug: "darling-come-back-to-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13173-Darling--Come-Back-To-Me-by-Del"
---

Love is an emotion ruled by your heart,Loving forever and never to part.So many times things go astray."What went wrong?" I heard you say.You thought your love was so pure,so strong,But even then things can go wrongYou're so unhappy, that I can see.Please, darling, come back to me.I've missed you so since we've been apart,So once again I'm offering you my heart.For so long we enjoyed a wonderful romance,So why not give our love another chance?
