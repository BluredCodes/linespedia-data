title: "THE LONG AND LONELY NIGHT"
writer: "Del"

So slowly the sun is descending,So soon the day will be ending,And then the long and lonely night.Alone am I with thoughts of you,Wondering how I'll see it thru,The never ending -- long and lonely night.The days are not so hard to bare,Since now I know you really care,But then -- the long and lonely night.I go to bed, but just can't sleep,My thoughts of you are oh, so deep,All thru -- the long and lonely night.I pray that someday soon you'll be,Right here, darling, holding me,For then there'll never ever beAnother lonely night for me.
