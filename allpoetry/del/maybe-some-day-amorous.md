---
title: "Maybe Some Day (Amorous)"
writer: "Del"
slug: "maybe-some-day-amorous"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12060-Maybe-Some-Day--Amorous--by-Del"

How I'd love to meet you in dreamland tonight!
I'd snuggle up and hold you oh, so tight.
Millions of stars shining way up above,
Shining down on the one I love.

I think about you before I sleep,
But seldom in dreams do we ever meet.
Maybe tonight will be lucky for me.
What joy and rapture that would be!

Your kisses will be so sweet and tender.
All I can give you, I will surrender.
If I'm lucky we'll be together all night,
But even just an hour would be alright.

I have so much love here in my heart.
Wih no one else will I let it depart.
Now, I feel your strong body next to mine,
Nothing in the world could be more sublime.

Those hot moist kisses are arousing me,
Closer and closer I want to be.
We both have tear drops in our eyes.
Never thought we'd find our paradise.

Maybe someday my dreams will come true.
Until then, my darling, I'll keep loving you.
