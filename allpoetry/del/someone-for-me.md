---
title: "SOMEONE FOR ME"
writer: "Del"
slug: "someone-for-me"

I dream of you every morning,Day dream of you every day.I'm so tired of dreaming of you,I'm gonna chase those dreams away.Instead of dreaming of you, my dear,I'll dream of someone new.Someone who'll share my feelingsSomeone who'll love me too.Someone whom I can confide in,Someone who won't cheat on me,Someone who'll love me for what I am,Not for what he wants me to be.Someoe who'll kiss my eager lipsWhile his warm body is so close to mineSomeone who'll vow to love meUntil the end of time.If this someone is out there somewhere,Please hurry and touch my soul.Right now my sad heart is brokenPlease come and make it whole.
