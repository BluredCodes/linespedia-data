title: "Make Believe, Pretend And Dream"
writer: "Del"
slug: "make-believe-pretend-and-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why am I such a pretender?Just dreaming the hours away.All day long just making believeYou are with me here today.I'm pretending you love me.Making believe you're here to stay.Hoping we'll meet in my dreamland.Don't ever take my dreams away.In dreams I pretend you're kissing me,Making believe you're holding me tight,Feeling your soft sweet lips on mine,Dreaming you'll stay with me all night.It's fun to pretend and dream.One thing you can do forever.I am going to believe with all my heart.That you will leave me NEVER.
