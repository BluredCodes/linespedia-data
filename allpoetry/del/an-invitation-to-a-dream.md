---
title: "AN INVITATION TO A DREAM"
writer: "Del"

You are cordially invited to dream a dream with me.Oh, I'd be so delighted if in my dreams I'd seeYour dear sweet face, your laughing eyes,Your smile so warm and true.I know I'd make you realize that you still love me too.All thru the day I think of you,No one else but you.Then at night you're out of sight,And this will never do.Those dreams at night are emptyYour face I never see,So here's an invitation to dream a dream with me.  R.S.V.P.
