Somewhere there's an island.
Where your romantic dreams come true,
Where the love of your life is waiting
To cuddle up close to you.

There are never any dark clouds.
The sun shines every day.
"I love you, darling, very much."
You hear your lover say.

The fragrance of flowers fill the air,
Sandy beaches along the shore.
The paradise you've always dreamed of,
No one could ask for more.

Beautiful orchids for your pickin'
Colorful roses are everywhere.
A Garden of Eden in dreamland,
And  your dream lover is there.

Someday, I'll find this island, 
Somewhere I know it exists.
Where the one I'm always dreaming of
Is just waiting to be kissed.
