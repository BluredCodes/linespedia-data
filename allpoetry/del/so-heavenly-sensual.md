On the bed we are lying in the nude.An open window emitting a cooling breeze.We're so quiet, just holding hands, speechless.The dream we've been dreaming for so longHad finally been fulfilled.We never thought this chance would come,When we could finally get together.I guess sometimes fate does lend a hand.It helps two aching hearts like ours,Banish all their sorrow and cares.For such a long time, we ony had our dreams.So far apart, like we were in different worlds.Then tonight you walked thru the door!It had to be a dream, it couldn't be you!I was so happy and surprised, I cried.You took me lovingly in your arms,And just held me so close to you.Then you looked into my eyes,And slowly, softly started kissing me.Then I kissed you, so hungrily, no hesitations.We could hardly wait to get undressed,The desire we felt was so overwhelming.When our two bare bodies entwinedAs close as we could ever get.We needed no more stimulation.Heaven opened up its door and let us in.
