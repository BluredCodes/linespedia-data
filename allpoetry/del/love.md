---
title: "Love?"
writer: "Del"
slug: "love"
source: "AllPoetry"

How do you know you're in love?
Do you feel a fluttering in your heart?
Does it feel strange, yet wonderful?
What makes this feeing start?

Does God send down an angel
From the heavens up above?
Or does cupid use his arrow
To make us fall in love?

Love can be so fulfilling
When the right one comes along.
How do you know it's the right one?
Maybe you could be wrong.

The feelings we have thru out the years
Vary so much it seems.
Is this person really the one for me?
Is this person the love of my dreams?

The mystery of love has gone on and on
For years and years and years.
It has bought so much joy and happiness,
But also barrels of tears.

Enjoy love while you have it.
It's not always here to stay.
Nothing in this world is forever.
So enjoy your lover today
