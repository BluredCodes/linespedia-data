---
title: "Dreaming, Always Dreaming"
writer: "Del"
slug: "dreaming-always-dreaming"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Dreamng, I'm always dreaming,
My life's an intricate maze.
Dreaming, I'm always dreaming,
Thru lonely nights and days.

Dreaming dreams that could never come true.
Dreaming dreams that are only of you.
Dreams keep frustration away from my heart,
Let's me endure life while we're apart.

Dreaming, I'm always dreaming,
My dreams I'm sure will never come true.
Hoping, oh, yes I'm hoping,
Someday I'll stop dreaming of you.

But I know I will never stop dreaming of you.
You're just too sweet, wonderful, so faithful and true.
After many years, I'm feeling an emotion called LOVE.
I believe it was sent to me from God up above.
