title: "My Lover And I (Amorous)"
writer: "Del"
slug: "my-lover-and-i-amorous"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am on a blanket, at a quiet beach,
With my lover, cuddled oh, so near.
Sounds of ripples lapping the shore
Are the only sounds we hear.

My lover puts one arm around me,
He gently tilts up my face.
His warm soft lips are kissing mine.
Now my heart's beginning to race.

Slowly he eases me down.
His trembling hands are fondling me.
As his moist lips explore my body,
I shiver with ecstacy.

The barriers between us are quickly removed.
The hardness of his body I can feel.
Our breathing becomes quite labored.
Tell me, darling, is this for real?

Paradise is just around the corner.
Over the lake is the setting sun.
What a lovely memory to cherish!
As my lover and I become one.
