Heat rises from the pavement
Short waves distort the air
Sweat drips from wet hair
As the great yellow orb beats mercilessly into the land
Air then smells of death
A freekish haze befouls the air

Then it falls at my feet
And the multitude looks toward the sky
Refreshing water restors life
Clensing the mist
Purging the air
Life, is restored, by summers rain.To explain the name.
