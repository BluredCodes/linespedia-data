Life is not easy
Life is not simple
Life is yet a lesson 
A lesson yet to learn

Life is like fire
Spreading fast and very hot
Life is like a block of Ice
Solid as rock anc very cold

One foot in front of the other
One step at a time
My life is mine

Life is not easy
Life is not simple 
Life is yet a lesson 
A lesson yet to learn

Now it is my trun 
Taking one step at a time 
Walking in the foot prints 
Of life.My friends and I all ways get into so really deep conversations about our lives. So one day I wrote this about it. It's not the best poem I have wrote but, it's a poem you can read. Let me know what you think about it. Thanx
