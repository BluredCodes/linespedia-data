You say, You love me so true
But do you mean it? 

You say I am so beautiful
But do you mean it?

You say I am as beautiful 
As the lights in the sky
At the break of dawn
and at the end of each day
But do you mean it?

Everything you say
Has it's meaning
That touches my heart
And brings tears to my eyes
Your tring so heard 
But you know 
You did wrong
This heart of mine 
Has been hurt 
One to many times 
To even trust nor love again

Everything you say and do
Is so very sweet and caring
