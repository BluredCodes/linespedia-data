The sun is shining every day
The clouds never get in the way
This is such, A wonderful Place

Clear Blue Waters
Beautiful blue skys
Fields of Daisy's

One small tree
Sits in the mist of it all
over looking 
Such a wonderful place
Everytime I see this place
A smile appers upond my face

Sitting under, this tree of faith
With you right beside me
makes my smile even biggerThis is just a short but sweet poem
