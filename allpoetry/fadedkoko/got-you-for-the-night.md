You caught my eye
The moment,you walked by
I rolled the dice right
And got you for the night

We held each other tight
Till morning light
We walked to the door
You said, Goodbye
Deep inside
I started to cry

You kissed my softly
On the cheek
Turned and slowly, 
Walked away
I stood at the door, 
watching you
As you slowly walked
Out of my life and out of sight

I will always remember, The night
I rolled the dice right
