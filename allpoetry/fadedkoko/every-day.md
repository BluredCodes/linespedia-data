Every day we learn something new 
Like the day the birds flew 
I fell in love with you

Every thing we do together
Turns into another memory
That I look back upond and say
We sure had a lot of fun
Theres still a lot we could of done

Every day we see new things
Like the day our eyes meet 
I never did feel a train go by 
But I was sure blown away

Every Day is just another day 
For me and you
Doing the things we love to do
As I look deep inside my heart
Finding those three little words
You love to hear me say
