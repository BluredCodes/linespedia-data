During these hard days,
We all need someone to lean on when we fall down and skin our knees.
Someone to heal that opened wound and leaned a helping hand. 
Someone who is not afraid to get their hands dirty to help a stranger, 
A new best friend in need.
Someone who may one day depend on you to pick them up when they may fall.
We all lean on one another some time in our life.
So give someone your shoulder to cry upond,
Because you may never know when you just might need 
A shoulder to cry upond.
Who? What? When? Where? or even why?
We may never know.
Just remember those who didn’t have a shoulder to cry on, 
or a shoulder to lean on.  
You may never know when you will need a lending hand.
Just remember all those who gave you a helping hand.
