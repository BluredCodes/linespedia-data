From the first time that I saw you
I know I had seen it all
Now ever time I close my eyes
I see you standing 
In the moonlight
The small,small stars
cricling around my head 
Is me!!
Dazed and Confuzed 
From being so close
To You!!This is the first poem I ever wrote. It is short and sweet, Well at least I think it is. Let me know what you think of it though.
