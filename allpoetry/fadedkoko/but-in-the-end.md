I am tired of being told 
That I am doing 
something wrong
Tired of everyone saying
That I am doing something
I am not even doing
Tired of my friends acting as if
I am not even there
Who even cares?
I try and do something right
But in the end 
Everything still turns out wrong

I have all ways been there for you
Watching your back
Helping you solve 
The problems in your life
Most of all 
I was your friend
But in the end
You are never
there for me

Tring to be a good friend
Tring to get things right
Tring to be someone
Everyone can trust
Even tried to blend in 
With everyone else
But in the end
Nothing seemed to work

Not even doing 
The best I could
But in the end
Nothing can stop me 
From trying to do 
The best I can

But In The End ......This poem was wrote when I was upset. Well it is really explains it self.
