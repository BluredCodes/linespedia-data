Loving you with all I have
Getting nothing back in return
Feels all to much,
Like a Dream
When you Stand,
In the sunlights beam
Should I expect something back?
A hug? 
A Kiss? 
Or even snuggling all night
on the coach? 
In a room Lite by candle light!

Still Standing in sunlights beam
All Still feels, Just like a dream.
Losing you, Losing your love
Would be losing it all in one day

The sunlights beam 
Is not shinning bright
Waking up 
From what 
I thought was 
A dream
