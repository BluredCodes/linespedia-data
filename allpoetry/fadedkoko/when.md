When you smile,
I feel like I am on fire.
When you laugh
I split in half.
When your driving
I feel like I am flying
When you come around
I fall to the ground
When I hold your hand 
I will all ways understand
When our lips touch
I think of how 
I miss you so much
When I feel like
I am going to fall apart
I think of you because
You are in my heart
When I look at the stars
I wish you was in my arms
When I first saw you 
I fell in love
I thought I saw and angel
That came from above.I wrote this about one of my x-boyfriends. He was so sweet and I loved his smile a lot. Well let me know what you think of it. Thanx
