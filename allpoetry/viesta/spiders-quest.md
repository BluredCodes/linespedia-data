---
title: "Spiders Quest~"
writer: "Viesta"
slug: "spiders-quest"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The spider travels across its gossamer trails,hunting by tiny vibration never fails,its destination a fallen fly,already lost, no more effort to try,the spider never rushed,the flies spirit already crushed,as it lays already entrapped,the spider makes certain with its silk its wrapped,life once again has taught a creature its rules,be on your guard, nature doesn't tolerate fools,are we not all like the fly at some point,living our lives until something falls out of joint,then we fall trying our best to escape poverty's net,live our whole lives without any regret,the spider is fate, which we can not avoid,sooner or later in its jawbone we will be enjoyed~Viesta ~
