"Interference"



I can't have any friends
I can't have a life
I've let it go but
Its gone to far this time

I stand up for you 
But you scream me down
Leaving me without
Any hope to be found

I fight and fight
But get beat up
I try and try
not to cause a fuss

You critisize me
you call me names 
I'm just a child
Stuck in all you games

I can't enjoy
My personal life
Without holding on
With all of my might

No one to share
My secret secrets
No one who cares
What im thinking about

I'm scared of you both
But most of all
 I afraid of
Losing my friends
The ones that mean the most
In times like this
