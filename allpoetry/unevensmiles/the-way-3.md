“THE WAY”



Why does it have to be this way
We love each other but we couldn’t say
The things we wanted too
The things that meant the most
We wanted to admit the things that wouldn’t 
And do the things we know we shouldn’t

Do I want it this way
The way you make me feel
The way you touch me
(The way) How you push me away
The things that you say
The things that you do
(Does it have to be this way)

Why does it have to be this way
Does it have to stop
The love that we share
The love that is eternal
We do the things we do
The things that make us whole

Do I want it this way
The way you make me feel
The way you touch me
(The way) How you push me away
The things that you say
The things that you do
( Does it have to be this way)

Why does it have to be this way
Every time I warm up to you
You shove me into the cold
The cold of uncertainty
The cold of fear
Where screaming voices I always hear

Do I want it this way
The way you make me feel
The way you touch me
(The way) How you push me away
The things that you say
The things that you do
(Does it have to be this way)

I don’t know how it’s going to end
The way it will all turn out
It’s the mystery I’m awaiting
