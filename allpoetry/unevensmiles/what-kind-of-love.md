"What kind of love"



You don't know 
But you think you do
You take everything away
But you took it all when you took my friends

You think you know
What I've done and said
But for you care
I could have wound of dead

Your so fucked up
so you just can't see
What the hell 
Your doin to me

They talk about you
You talk about them
But you don't understand
that I'm  stuck between

What kind of love
Do you have for me
It doesn't seem like love at all
Why don't you know your ripping me apart
