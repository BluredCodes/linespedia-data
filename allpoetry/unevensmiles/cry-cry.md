I miss the times of it all

I wish it wouldn't end 

I've been going fast

But now I'm down to a crawl



Everything is changing

By that old brown rope

My body is hanging

Strangleing with nothin but hope



Tied down by the unknown

My spirit will not lift up

I try all the old tricks

But nothing seems to work



Cry cry cry,

Why do I cry?

I dont know why,

Why I cry

Cry cry cry,

Why do I cry ?

I dont know why

...I cryOk i know its not the best but I havent found how to really release what I feel, any comments will be welcome.
