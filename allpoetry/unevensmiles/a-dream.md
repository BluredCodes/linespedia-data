Are my Dreams memories
Of my lives before this one
Memories of flying in the heavins
Falling from the top of the Mountins
Being chased by demons
I wonder about what will become of me

Is life a dream
 Or is it really there
A wonderful expertience
Or a hideous Nightmare 
Is life a dream
To know the truth is only fair
But the trueth is something
 I don't know if I can bear

Are my dreams just dreams of this life
Just living the dreams of others
Strang dreams of mothers
Of sons and daughters
Being me and living my life
In the middle of the night

Is life a dream
 Or is it really there
A wonderful expertience
Or a hideous Nightmare 
Is life a dream
To know the truth is only fair
But the trueth is something
 I don't know if I can bear

Do my dreams hide the meaning of life
The meaning of death
Do they hide all the secrets
That roam in my mind
Why do I dream
