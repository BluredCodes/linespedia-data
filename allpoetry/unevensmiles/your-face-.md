When I see your face
I think of her and her loving ways
Oh, when I see your face
I wonder why I'm in this place
And then I think of her

I cant stand to see your face
When I do it tears me down
(down) to a crawling pace
Putting me further behind in this race
...Because of you

When I see your face 
It puts me in so much pain
Oh, when I see your face
Inside my heart fills with hate
...Because of the way you treat her

I can't stand to see your face
When I do I tears me down
(down) to a crawling pace
Putting me further behind in this race
...Because of you

When I see your face
Her kiss I can taste
Oh, when I see your face
My mind drifts off into space
And I know I love her...
