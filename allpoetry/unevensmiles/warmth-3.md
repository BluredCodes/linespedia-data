“Warmth”



Confused and cold
I am strangled from her warmth
The warmth that makes me feel as a person
Of this world
The love and ambition
Of being held
Held by a person
Full of warmth

Warmth, Warmth
It catches me by the heart
It makes me feel alive and well

The thoughts and feelings 
That race through my mind
That keeps me warm
At unbearable times
Loved and excited
In her arms
I’m feeling the warmth
The warmth that I’ve been awaiting

Warmth, Warmth
It catches me by the heart
