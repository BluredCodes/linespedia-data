"Lost"



I've lost it all
All that I wanted
Left with nothin more
Than a lifetime oh haunted dreams

No more fun
With my two best friends
Because of you 
It's all come to an end

Lost talks about dreams
Lost talks about girls
No more health
From my new found wealth

Kiss all the jokes good-bye
You make it so hard
to undersand why
It's over...
