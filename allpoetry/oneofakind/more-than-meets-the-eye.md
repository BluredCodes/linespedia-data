title: "More Than Meets The Eye"
writer: "oneofakind"

What you see is what you get.No fine print.Won't change for anyone.If you don't like what you see,keep on walking.I'm not a Barbie doll,you can buy at the store.I am oneofakind,not sold in stores.Don't waist your time trying to change me.I am the same person I was yesterday,and I will be the same person tomorrow.You think you know me,but I am more than meets the eye.
