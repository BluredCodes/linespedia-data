---
title: "Better off not knowing"
writer: "oneofakind"
slug: "better-off-not-knowing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6267-Better-off-not-knowing-by-oneofakind"
---

To look in your eyes,
to know that those eyes
stared me down with power.
To look at your hands,
and know that those hands
touched me in such angry.
To look at you 
and remember what you
did and said to me.
To look at your feet 
and know that those feet
walked away from me and left me,
would cause me too much pain.
So I close my eyes and walk away.
