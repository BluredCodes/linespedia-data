---
title: "I dare you!"
writer: "oneofakind"
slug: "i-dare-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6058-I-dare-you--by-oneofakind"
---

I dare you
to live your life to the fullest
I dare you
to take chances
I dare you
to be successful
I dare you
to love every chance you have
I dare you
to have fun
I dare you!
I dare you!
to be yourself!
I dare you to live!
What!? Are you afraid?!
