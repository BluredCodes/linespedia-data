You say those words,
'I love you'
To make yourself feel better
and not so guilty for leaving.

Those three words are so empty.
But somehow echo through my head.
There is a pause of when
I return the phrase.

It's hard for me to say those words
maybe because of my past.
It wasn't a common phrase
and hardly felted.

You say those words 
to make yourself feel better
But now I'm older dad,
I can read you like a book.

I return those three words
out of guilt to make myself feel better
about wanting you out of my life.
