---
title: "Reality"
writer: "oneofakind"

source: "AllPoetry"

A young girl standing on the roof of a business building, just staring down at the busy world. 
What could she possibly be thinking? 
What is she feeling?
What drove her to this place in her life?
She slowly moves her eyes from the busy ground to the clouds in the sky and she closes her tired eyes. 
She slowly raises her arms ready to fly like a bird, as a single tear rolls down her cheek.
She stepped her foot into the empty air, and let herself go but as she did she realized the truth, the reality, the reality of forever. 
But it was too late.
-Think twice-
-Think forever-
