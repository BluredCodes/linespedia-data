I'm on the life sucks boat,and it's slowly sinking.My heart is hurtingand I have no pain medicine left.Losing touch with friends,and getting farther.My mind is flooding with thoughts,but I have no drain.I'm lostand there is no exit sign in site.Just looking for a sign of hope.
