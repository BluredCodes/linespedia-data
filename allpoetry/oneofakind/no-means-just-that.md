title: "No Means Just That"
writer: "oneofakind"

too young to even drive
a child holds her child in her hands
she never noticed how much her baby had her eyes
she sells her body,
to make the pain go away
she walks down the street
and gets nothing but disapproved looks staring her down
but this child in her arms
keeps her from selling herself
from ending her life
now she has something to live for
she regrets that night
and wishes he believed her when she screamed no
but she will never regret her key
her key that keeps her heart from crumbling
her key, her child
her childhood was stolen from her in seconds
and forced her to grow up
she promises herself that her child will not 
experience as much pain as she has
as a child carries her child in her arms
she knows now her life is starting over
she kisses her child's forehead
and walks with her chin held high.
