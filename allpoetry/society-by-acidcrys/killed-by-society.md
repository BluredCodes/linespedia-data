I licked to taste the poison we're all drowning inI reached out to be stabbed by thorns that are draggin us downI breathed to be intoxicated by what we're feeding onI closed my eyes to fall into the nonsense we're living byI came to untangle the webs we've spunI lost falling into the herd of cattlehumans lined up for death.i bled, releasing my life onto cold concrete pathsi cried nuturing the grounds the bastards held strongi died giving up to them my soulkilled my understandingkilled my hopes and dreamskilled my heart.but not my mindI searched, why did i try?why did this happen?why is it happening to you?I dont usually write on this type of topic.. so its a new thing for me.
