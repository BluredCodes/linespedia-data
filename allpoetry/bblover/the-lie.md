Look me in they eye,
Don't tell me a lie.
Why weren't you there,
And tell me you swear.
You said you'd meet me at seven.
I felt as if I were in heaven,
And when you didn't come by,
I just wanted to cry.
I don't want you here,
Because you didn't swear.
That's alright,
I don't care.
My life will go on,
I don't need you here.
Don't stop by,
I don't want you to see me cry.
I have two words left for you,
And that's GOODBYE!
