We used to be together
Happy and free, we said that forever.
Until one day, one thing went wrong.
We tried to hold on, I thought we were strong.
And then came the words, I'm sorry we are through.
And until that day I never realized how much I love you.
And I miss you so much,
That words can't explain,
How much I want to call and say that I love you,
But I can't so I'll just have to refrain,
From cousing both of us more pain.
