---
title: "Let Go"
writer: "BBLover"
slug: "let-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I love you so,
Why can't you see?

But I know,
Our love will never be.

I had you once,
But it didn't work out.

I knew our love was just a doubt.
Now I see how much you mean to me.

I can't let you go,
But I have to let you be.
