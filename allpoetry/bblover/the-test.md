title: "The Test"
writer: "BBLover"

I'm putting you to the test,
To see if you are the best.
I'm seeing if you care for me,
I'm seeing if we are meant to be.
I'm seeing if it is you that I can trust,
And that the feelings I have are love, not lust.
I want to see if it's me you love,
And not some girl you can push and shove.
You passed the test,
You have an A,
Now I know you love me that way.
