Happy New Year, Son!

Today is New Years Eve Day
I have something to say
My son Kenny wrote me
I am as proud as I can be.

He got married this year
I stand up and cheer.
He married a beautiful spirit filled lady
She is now going to have his baby.

He has made a career in the Army
He will retire next June, you see.
To the Philippines he does go
To retire as his family grows.

I will miss him and I know
He loves me and is with me wherever I go.
Deep within my heart,
We will never part

It is so nice that he cares
And speaks to me and shares
Those moments he tells no other
He just stops to speak to his mother.

I love you, Kenny with all my heart
You are such a joy to see your new start
You have a good plan for your life,
You are blessed with a good wife.
