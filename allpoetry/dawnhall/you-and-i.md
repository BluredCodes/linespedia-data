You and I

You and I were meant to meet;
God really knows why;
His plan is hard to beat
I am not going to try.

When I  review my life
I know I am not alone
I give God all of my strife
He will guide me home.

As I share my insides with you
To the surface comes the pain.
I put my life on review
I ask you to do the same.

You see until we share;
Ourself, we hide away;
How can we ever care,
If in life we only play.

I am glad you are my friend
I love you unconditionally
We can speak out no matter when
And God will set us FREE.



Praise the Lord!
