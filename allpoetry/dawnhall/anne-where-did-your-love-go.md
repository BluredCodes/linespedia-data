Anne, Where Did Your Love Go?

My daughter will not speak to me
I do not count in her life
When did it happen? Was it meant to be?

It started about 3 years ago 
She told me I was her best friend
My life began to glow.

I had my daughter’s love?
I did not expect it to go,
Love flew away like a dove.

When will she call?
When will she care?
What is this wall?

I love her unconditionally.
My woe is in God’s hands. 
Thank God, He loves me! 

With His strength I can stand.
I know my life will move on.
