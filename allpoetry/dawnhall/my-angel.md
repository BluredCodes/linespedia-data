If I were only three, where would I be?
I'd be in your arms or on your knee.
I'd give you sugar and hugs, 
I'd gather a whole bunch of bugs.

I would be proud, I'd shout out loud:
"Mommy, Daddy, I love you and you love me too."

If I were three, I could be four, 
when Daddy came home I'd run to the door.
I would jump in his arms with love, He'd hug me from above. 
Mommy would smile, she would hold my hand awhile.
She would love me so and I'd never have to go.

Yet, I am 5 months old and I did what I was told
God sent for me and three I'll never be.
God's little angel am I, please do not cry.
I am no longer in pain, I do not feel the rain

Mommy,  Daddy, I am happy, I am with God you see.
Watch me throw the stars for fun... 
I swing on the moon, in front of everyone.
Look Mommy, I can walk. Look Daddy, I can talk...
Please let me go, God needs me so
I'll always be near, please, don't shed a tear.
