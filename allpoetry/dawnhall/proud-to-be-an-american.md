I Am Proud To Be An American

Sept 11th, 2001, what a tragedy
We woke that morning, divided as we could be.
The buildings were filling, with souls to be lost.
America was attacked, many lives were the cost.

One Airplane hit, then came another.
The building shook, like wings all a flutter
Never given a chance, no time to pout.
Run down those stairs, got to get out.

As the people ran out, firemen ran in the Towers
Determined to help others, they were not cowards. 
The buildings tumbling down, crushing all in its way
Thousand of heroes, were buried that day.


All were running, some jumped to the ground.
Soot, smoke, and darkness was all around
Through all this tragedy, who would guess
That America would Unite, among this mess.

The American Spirit, arose that day
With all the despair, it is here to stay.
People helping people, giving each other a hand
Some staying behind, to help their fellow man

There were many, heroes that day.
Super people with heart, their love was on display.
There were men in the Air, who helped save our Land.
Love, support, and care, God guided their hand.

We are fighting back, the enemy can not hide.
Our song is sung. We have our pride.
Divided We Fell, United We Stand! 
We have God’s love, I am proud to be an American.
