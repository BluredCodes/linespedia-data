MAKING A DIFFERENCE

Making a difference in your life;
Is all I ever wanted to do;
For me to make a difference;
I have to open myself up to you!

I have to tell you who I am;
I have to speak up now and then;
I have to risk being your friend;
I have to Love Me - Again!

I can no longer hide behind my 450 lb. fat;
My 130lb. weight is beginning to appear;
I move, I do, I am energized;
I even enjoy Looking in the Mirror!

As I share my inner self with you;
You see what I am going through;
We can team together - you and I;
AS YOU MAKE A DIFFERENCE TOO!
