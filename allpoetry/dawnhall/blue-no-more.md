BLUE NO MORE! 

I was so blue 
I did not know what to do 
I read an email from a new friend. 
" Never speak to him and his team, again" 
I erased the email that I had 
Pondered on the message and felt sad. 
I opened the next one. 
My heart felt the sun. 
Helen and Jim emailed me 
And gave me this message gracefully 
They said, "To me, you mean so much 
Your poems have given my heart a touch 
My love for you. I share 
Though I am not there. 
Click this link and you will see 
How much you mean to me! 
www.rainbowusa.net/rightnow.htm 
I clicked, I watched, I smiled, I cried. 
My heart jumped for joy and I sighed. 
Thank you God, for allowing them to share. 
You shined through them, you were there.
