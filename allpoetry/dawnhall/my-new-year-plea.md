My New Year Plea 

Here I am at the brink of the New Year. 
What do I do now – what do I fear? 
My brother said he was going to visit me 
In March and I am afraid as I can be. 
You see my home is a mess 
My husband blocks me from happiness 
He will not let me clean 
He is so mean. 
I have been very sick and I am better now. 
I have to get out of my room and how. 
I have to be able to clean each day 
So I can be proud of my home this way. 
Lord I ask you to intervene 
Block him and allow me to Clean. 
Give me the strength to take a stand 
And do all I can do and you take care of my husband. 
I am tired of people wanting to visit me 
And I have to say no – please I cannot let you see. 
Lord, speak to a housekeeper who can help me 
Keep my home in shape, as it should be. 
Praise you Lord in advance.
