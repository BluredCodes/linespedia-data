Happy Valentines's Day

Be my Valentine,
My heart needs a friend
Will you be mine?

The time has come to speak out
God sends His blessings to us 
Please do not pout,

Look at your life
Examine it thoroughly
Give God your strife.

My arms are outstretched to you
Give me a hug, today
Your love for me is true.

I ask God to bless
You and I thank God 
You give me happiness.

On this Valentines Day,
I love you!
Is all I have to say.
