GOD GRANT ME A FUTURE! JANUARY 2003

God has healed my body!
I give Him all the glory.
I fellowship with others
Here is my story.

I lost 320 lb.  With God’s guidance
Our Learning Center has begun to display.
Standing tall, uniting, as we serve 100 percent
we coach others to follow God’s way,

We are teaching the handicapped
To be financially independent,
God loves our positive, loving, handicappable 
Special people, God is our Superintendent.

To be all that we can be
Is part of our group goal?
As God directs our future,
We stand up, as we are bold.

Our Learning Center becomes National
We each own a part.
Pride, security, and self confidence
Established in our heart.
