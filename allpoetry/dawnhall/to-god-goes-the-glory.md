Here in my adult years,
I live in my bedroom.
Hiding all the tears and fears.
If I can not leave – I am a mentor
I can use my computer to reveal my vision.
I started Healthy USA Training Center.
The Christian Way to Health.
With the products we have,
God can lead us to His Wealth.

Social Security is not here to stay.
Soon it will be history
I have to plan the future today.
Hand in hand, we will take a stand,
Christians reach out and unite.
With God’s guidance, I'll work His plan.

No more tears or fears today,
I came out of my room and shouted.
“I am tired of closing myself away.”
"I am out of my room to stay
I will begun my vison of love.
As we unite we will display,
Ourselves as we build His steeple,
Follow Him in all our ways
To God goes the glory, from His people
