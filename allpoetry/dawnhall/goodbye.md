Goodbye

There he was
My Ex-husband in my dream.
I feel so much love and passion,
I wonder what it could mean.

He wants no touching
He acts as if he doesn’t care. 
Yet I feel his compassion
I feel his body there.

I am happily married
Yet he is in my dream tonight,
Instead of a loving jester,
He walks away with no fight.

Now he is gone
I think I know why
He came in my dream
To say, I love you, goodbye.
