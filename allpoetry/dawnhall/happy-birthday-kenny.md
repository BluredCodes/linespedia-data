Happy Birthday, Kenny

Now is the time to say
Have a Happy Birthday
I think about that special day
I brought you home to stay.
I held you in my arms
I remember all of your charms.
Your little buns fit inside of my hand,
And now look at you - you are a man,
You have kids and a child on the way,
I am a blessed mom that is all I have to say!
