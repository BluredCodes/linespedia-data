God Heard Your Prayer

God gave you beauty
His presence shines through you
Your eyes radiate His love
I ask you; "Stop being blue!"

God never makes mistakes;
Only you can fulfill
His special Purpose.
Listen, hear Him and stay still.

Your family is crying
They need your love
Let go and let God
He will heal you from above.

Be a family, come together
Go to the bible, study as one.
Find a church that can minister
Stand up, in the name of God's Son.

As you listen to God
Your blessings are revealed
Your heart is filled with joy
Prayer answered, your family is healedThis was dedicated to a friend that had a lot of battles happening in her marriage.  I know that the good Lord has healed her family and her heart.  Praise the Lord!
