Schizophrenic is my husband’s disability 
And most of the time he is as loving as he can be. 
My littlest angel, my love, my friend, my mate 
When he not himself, is what I hate. 
He cares, He is full of life,
I am proud to be his wife. 
I Praise God even when I am weary, 
That He choose Kirk for me to marry.
We have been wed, seventeen years.
