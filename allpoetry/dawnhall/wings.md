Wings

Jesus I am so blessed with a whole bunch of happiness
Your special Angel am I. I never have to cry.
You see, I was so ill; I could not take a pill.
Mommy took care of me, I was not healthy.

Up to heaven I did go, my heart beating so
Chrismas is so near, I am full of cheer.
I am grateful for my family - you gave me.
it's not to late, to realize mom was great.

I never wanted to roam, I had a beautiful home.
Yet you needed me so, I just had to go.
I am thankful today and all I have to say
The luckiest Angel am I. I no longer cry.

Here I am again, playing with my friend
The clouds are my bed, stars under my head.
I put on my wings today, I stood tall to display.
I can fly and sing, I can do everything

Happiness and kisses to all, I heard you call.
Jesus is special to me and now I can see.
Jesus is at my best friend, my kisses I send.
Don’t worry mommy, I am close to you for eternity.
