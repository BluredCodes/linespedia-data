Grant me a U-Turn 

Here I am, sick again 
I am tired of this strife, 
I ask for a breakthrough 
My body healed for life. 

Do I trade illness for love? 
No, no that will not do. 
Is this my cry, “help me”? 
Helplessness, I show you? 

Hold me, Lord, love me 
U-turn my life around 
You are my number one 
Lift me up, as I am down. 

Thank you, Lord, for your hugs 
Thank you for my family, 
Thank you for listening 
Thank you for healing me.
