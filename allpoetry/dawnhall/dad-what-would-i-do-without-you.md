Dad, you showed me sunshine
Where I saw none
You let me know you were mine 
When I had no one.

You loved me with all your heart
No matter if I did right or wrong
Thank God you were always my loving part
As you held me in your loving arms.

You see - I know what you went through
To love me and be my superman
What you told me was so true
Your principles I live upon.

I am blessed - you love me
You make a difference in my life
Thinking of you made me happy
Dad, where would I be without you?

I love you, Dad

Dawn
