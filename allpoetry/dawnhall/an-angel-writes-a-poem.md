An Angel Writes a Poem!

Jesus, here I am throwing the stars
Driving my pretend cars
Jumping from the moon
Singing my Holy tune.

Your Birthday is almost here
I want to give a gift full of cheer
Jesus, I have nothing at all
To give you, I am so small.

Jesus, help me know today
How to please you on your birthday
Thank you, I know now
I will write a poem, that’s how.

“Jesus, I am only 5 months old
I can’t give you silver or gold
I can gather the stars
Give you my pretend cars.

I can give you my heart
I know we will not part
Mommy and my sisters are happy too
Because they know my best friend is you!'In memory of my Grandson; John Graenser. From the mouth of babes, as I feel he would say as he is in heaven and at peace.No more pain, no more aches.  He is with his best friend, Jesus!Dawn Anne Hall
