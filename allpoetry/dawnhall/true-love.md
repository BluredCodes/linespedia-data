TRUE LOVE

What would I do without you?
I don't want to know
I never wanted to go there
I never wanted to show
How much you mean to me
Deep inside my heart
You are there
And we will never be apart.
Though there may be space
There may be miles
There may be people to stop us
Inside I feel your smiles.
They can not break us up
No matter what they do
You see inside of me
Is my best friend - You...
I love you with all my heart
You are the best part of me
I miss you - I love you
In God's arms - we will always be
