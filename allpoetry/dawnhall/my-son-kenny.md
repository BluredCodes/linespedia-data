My Son, Kenny

My son sent me a gift;
He gave my heart a lift.
A beautiful scroll handwritten
As I opened it my heart was smitten.
There are roses painted on the top
My  heart did a flippy flop
I read those enchanted words he wrote
I stand tall as I quote,
"I love you my dear son,
You are my number one
You make me so proud
To be your mom, I shout our loud,
My son Loves me, I shout with pride!
He is in my heart deep inside,
When I am loney and sad
I can read my scroll and I am glad.
What a blessing God gave me today
To know my son's love is here to stay, Hurray!"
