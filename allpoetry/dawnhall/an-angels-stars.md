An Angel’s Stars 

Wow I am so blessed 
With love and happiness 
Jesus is 7 days old 
I am His friend I am told 

Look at the stars dance 
Wow, what a romance. 
Everyone is happy today 
Jesus was born on Christmas day. 

My stars are shining bright 
They twinkle red, blue, yellow and white. 
I am the happiest Angel alive 
I can jump, run, hop, and slide. 

Hey grandma can you play? 
Can I visit you today? 
I want to let you know 
I love you so! 

Now I am going to play with my stars 
As they light up the skies – see Mars? 
Thank you Jesus for being my savior, 
Let me know if I can do you a favor. 

Here a bunch of sugar and hugs 
I have a handful of pretend bugs, 
I have everything I could ever ask for.

God is blessing us!
Dawn Anne
