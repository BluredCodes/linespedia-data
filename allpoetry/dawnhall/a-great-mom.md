My step mom, Mom Mina, 
Died today.  I can’t believe it!  
The doctors said that 
She was Anemic and changed her 
blood two times. 
They decided that this 
Activated her cancer cells 
This week she had Leukemia, the kind 
That moves through the body quickly.  
It only took three days for her to leave us 
And go to heaven.

She was a mom to six kids 
And five stepkids. She was 
loved and had enough love for all. 
She was so happy, 
she always smiled and laughed. 
She was there for all.

She was married to my dad 
for twenty-five years!  
They had a mutual love and 
Respect for each other.

I was so blessed to have known her.  
She accepted me 
and loved me, 
And I loved my Mom Mina.  

She is now God’s Angel 
And I can see her loving her
Angel friends and sharing her 
Unique love.  She asked
Jesus to take care of Dad 
And all her kids.  
She will miss us and yet 
She is happy. I know she is 
speaking to my mom, Anne, 
And comparing notes. Sharing 
love and experiences with each other.

There is Grandma Petrossi, 
Grandma and Grandpa Angaran, 
Aunt Mary, and my grandson Johnny.  
Hey look, there comes 
Tony Rossi, Aunt Del and 
Uncle Regie Miller, walking 
towards our family of angels.  
They are all gathering 
around my Mom Mina welcoming 
her to heaven.  Thank God 
that Mom Mina had asked 
Jesus to be in her heart.

I love you, Mom Mina and I miss you!   
I know you are 
Beside me, holding me as you whisper 
In my ear.  You asked me to tell all 
Of your family to remember to
Love each other for you.  
Mom Mina, you were so 
Good to me, thank you for being 
Part of our family. 
Thank you for caring for my dad 
And being “A Great Mom”.
