I am treading water alone admist an inner whirlpool 
that threatens to suck me down into it's drain. 
A puddle of sorrow with no more tomorrow's

I feel that lonely place 
Where there is no one there but me
A lost soul ready to give up

Careful, careful to not step on my puddle
I do not want to look at tomorrow
I will not be sucked down the drain of my inner whirlpool.

When my life is turned upside down
I wish I could hide and bury my head in the sand.
I praise God and His strength moves inside of me.

I will stand up and stand tall
Look within and use the talents God gave me
Write, love, care, share.

I refuse to drown in my puddle
With God's strenght, I can create a U-Turn
I will dance in my sorrow and know God's handling tomorrow!
