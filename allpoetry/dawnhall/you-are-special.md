You Are Special!

When no one is around and you feel down,
Pick up your Bible and read.
Quiet, listen for God's sound,
'I love you - you are special!'

Skip, shout, because of who you are.
God is carrying your load,
Praise Him, sight unseen,
Follow His well lit road.

Set your goals toward His purpose.
Try to put God first in your mind.
Jesus died for you, you are special!
Look toward your future, not behind.

Pray, praise Him, listen!
He has a unique purpose for you.
You are loved - you are special.
Do what He tells you to do.

Love the God-like self in you,
Get a life, you are a star!
Remember, Jesus loves you,
Be proud of who you are.
