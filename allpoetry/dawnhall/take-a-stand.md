When life gets you down;                             
You feel all alone;                             
When you feel nothings left;
Not a dog - nor a bone.

You want to scream, shout, cry;
Hide, bury your head in the samd;
It is so hard to face life again;
That is when you TAKE A STAND!

THANK GOD for your problems.
PRAISE Him sight unseen;
Cause without a problem;
What miracle would He bring?

It is hard sometimes to stand still;
Let Him take control of your life;
Just know deep inside of you;
He will put an end to your strife.

Be still, quite Listen for God's sound;
He will speak to you
SHHH! Not a sound, Let His peace seep in.
