two figures on the chess board: a bird-eyed rainbow and a halcyon smile of wine let's play a mocking grimace of a square makes it black and white a whispering mask emerges – a dancer out of 1 and 1000 nights lips tight hips quavering dragging the shadows out of their looming caves at the end of the maze there's a dove sealing with its crumbling wings any redemption for fidgeted cries let's never escape ***
