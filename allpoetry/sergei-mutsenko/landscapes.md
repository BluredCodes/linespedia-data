---
title: "Landscapes"
writer: "Sergei Mutsenko"
slug: "landscapes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46598-Landscapes-by-Sergei-Mutsenko"
---

wires
     constructions
               spirals

somebody leaked
out of triangle

became 
the embodiment of
     jealousy

impersonation of
at any cost
investment
     My God

someone
      is selling corals
myths
ulcer
copulas

     motions
     of steel
     of barbed wire

in the emptiness
in the bathroom
     in the autumn

the bushes are begging
     kneeling
          insolently
