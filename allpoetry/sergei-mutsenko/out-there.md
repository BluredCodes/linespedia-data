---
title: "Out there"
writer: "Sergei Mutsenko"
slug: "out-there"

a man
with a loop 
on his neck
was standing lonely
in front of my
     window

the crowd receded
leaving the garbage

the dried up angels
of the dogs’ skeletons

the car’s curtains
drew open
revealed a scene
an orgy of lips and 
     the beast

rain interfered 
entered the eyes
with its glassy fingers 
look out the shell of 
     the brain
sucked out a pearl

sprinkled in
the black sand
with the insolence 
of the Black Empress
the dust of the Black Empress

cutting

was receding
leaving a tree
standing lonely
with a loop of 
     a man
