Whenever I sawYour soulIn the white snowLegsSpread for loveThe beggarly angelOf the fir treePicks by handfulsOur shadowsNo my GodKind of scaredThe sun is rising with the dullnessOf  Medusa’sChopped off headOn the television is being runThe last series of ParadiseThe exileWith the blood More skittishThan the last candleIn the blind yolkOf the dark*
