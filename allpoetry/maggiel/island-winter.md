Island Winter

Soldiers gather in the marketplace
lingering ghosts
maimed and broken
They watch the silky girls
shimmer in the dust

Dozing mothers tell tales
to brown children
as they dash thru
the vegetable carts

Bright splashes of lime 
and apricot 
Venders hold canary fruit
with sun-ripened hands
