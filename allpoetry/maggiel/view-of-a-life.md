View of a Life

Goodbye again.
A hug,a kiss,a wave goodbye
and I am without you.
I read my books,
wash the baby's toes
and think of you.
I live suspended and apart
from time. 
I watch the lazy turn of the fan
above our bed
and I can't wait for your return.
To view life through
your smile
and escape somewhere behind your eyes.
