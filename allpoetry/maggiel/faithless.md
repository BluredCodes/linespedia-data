Faithless

Let sleeping dogs lie,you say
I watch the flicker dim your eyes
I see your lips move
and I know you are faithless

She's out there
Ethereal and Mysterious
I'm sure

Does she cross her legs just so?
Perhaps she speaks
in whispered tones,
strokes your cheek with painted nails?

I smile and hold your hand
Knowing I will stitch up
your bleeding wound

I weave a tapestry for you
with hands that smell of
warm earth
I will soothe your broken spirit
with a voice like
a graveled drive
