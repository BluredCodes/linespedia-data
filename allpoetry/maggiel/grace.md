Grace

Somewhere past the stars
in your eyes
is my favorite place
A place of candles
and darkness
deep chasms where
your soul used to be
Somewhere past your heart
is where I lay
open naked to you
my limbs a violent ache
Always reaching
catching nothing
Somewhere past your memories
I remain
still pictures
in the moonlight
scent of cloves,touch of Grace
my voice whispering to you
Somewhere
