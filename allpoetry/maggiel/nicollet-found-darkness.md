Nicollet Found Darkness

Love dies again
Tommorrow morning 
will end the mangled evening
soon enough
"Drop Dead"
she screams
but it is she who is 
the corpse
Her bloodless heart
already galloping toward
the next fatality
Leaving him to wonder
"What the hell did I do?"
