Blue

When I remember blue
a swirling mosaic
in my mind,
filled with indigo
and navy.
The bluest of blue moons,
the color of your eyes 
after a hard rain.
The blue of my sheets
tangled around your leg.
Sleepless nights leave 
my eyes blue shadows.
My heart filled blue
when you go away.
