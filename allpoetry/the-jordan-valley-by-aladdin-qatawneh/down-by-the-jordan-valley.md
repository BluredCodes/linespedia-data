title: "Down by the Jordan valley!"
writer: "the Jordan valley  by Aladdin Qatawneh"

Down by the Jordan valley my love and I did meet
She called me baby and stood on my feet
She kissed me softly and tickled my ears
But I was unfaithful and full of fears

Down by the Jordan valley my love and I did meet
She whispered softly, I love you my sweet
She laid her head on my shoulder and felt my beats
But I was unfaithful and full of fears

Down by the Jordan valley my love and I did meet
She called me liar and ended the meet
She left me swiftly and shook my leaves
And I was unfaithful, now am full of tears
