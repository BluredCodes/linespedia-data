Whiplash and Orgasms

Jimmy lived a hermitic married life
from the day he turned eighteen
he never loved another woman
when he met his sweet Darlene

Thirty years passed blissfully
he thought this would be the course
but then one day, his darling wife
sued him for divorce.

Suddenly, thrust into a  worldly life
poor Jimmy was now alone
he decided to see things he had missed
since he was now on his own.

With money in hand, he bought a car
a cherry red Mustang convertible
he put down the top and drove off
seeking women that were adorable.

He drove down the streets of a college town
quickly gazing left then quickly right.
Jimmy saw women everywhere
that made his jockeys feel too tight.

He drove fifteen miles to the hour
breathing heavy, his lips dry
He saw halter tops overflowing 
and shorts cut way up high.

Black leather skirts and thigh high boots
a red slinky dress over there
luscious women of every kind
poor Jimmy could only stare!

He turned around at the end of the street
to see it a second time
little did he know he was about
to commit a serious crime.

His head turning this way, that way and back
left little time for his driving ability
suddenly, when he was ready to explode
he rear-ended a Sport Utility!
