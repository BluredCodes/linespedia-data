My petals quiver with anticipation
of thy assualt to end this frustration
they open to you with moist delight
take them now, Master of the Night!

Our garden bed is strewn with lust
Thrilling me with each naked thrust
Though you rake me with desire
You will not stop this flow of fire

Come end my wanton lustful need
Pluck both vine and sensual weed
As I seek you on bended knees
Take this seed of pleasure please

When we’ve supped our stem and flower
We shall rest in this lover’s bower
Lovers in this garden we till
Lovers now, ever still.
