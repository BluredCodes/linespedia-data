---
title: "As My Lover Sleeps"
writer: "Sharon"

I stand silently by the bed and watch
You breathe softly as you sleep
Fully naked in the moonlight
This is a memory I’ll keep

Furiously we loved tonight
As though it would be our last
Forgetting we have no future
Not remembering our separate past

No man has ever taken me
To that place we often go
Ecstasy was a foreign place
And I had no passport to show

Your tenderness in my taking
Gave me gasps of wanton pleasure
With each thrust of your hips
Sealing my love’s precious treasure 

Completely and with abandon
I gave into your every desire
Blushing now at my boldness
Marveling at your fire

Your eyes that burned into my heart
Are closed in satisfaction’s sleep
And so I turn and leave you here
Knowing you are not mine to keep
