---
title: "The Knight and the Maiden"
writer: "Sharon"
slug: "the-knight-and-the-maiden"
source: "AllPoetry"

Fair Knight seeking water and rest for his steed
found the lake in the woods suited his need
dismounting and walking to the azure blue shore
he discovered that someone was here before

Hidden in a holly bush lay a warrior blade
he knelt down to better see the warrior in the glade
sounds of a splash brought his head around
and then came a most delicious sound

he heard the laugh of a delicate maid
splashing as she emerged to the glade
a tuneful humming from a lady so small
he knew there wasn’t a warrior after all

She walked from the lake with a purposeful stride
fiery hair down her back, a strand clung to her side
cradled her waist as though pointing the way
to the fiery bush where a lover might play

He rose from his place as she neared the shore
saw the flash of blue eyes as his manhood did soar
she stood proudly naked, as his eyes drank their fill
and her chin raised higher, unbending to his will

She walked to the rock and picked up the blade
then brandished it quickly, such a sight she made
naked and lovely, wanton warrior was she
the Knight knew in a moment he would never be free
