Luminescent and sublime
love glows in her eyes
fragrant and forgiving
seeking lover’s sighs.

Radiant is her love for him
shining for all to see
She breathes her life just for him
without guile or duplicity.

The touch of her hand has healed
his once scarred, wretched heart
The warmth of her love gives him
a haven - a place to start.
