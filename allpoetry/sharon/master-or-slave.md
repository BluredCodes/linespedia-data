Your hardness held in hand
Is offered willingly
suck me you beg
I smile cunningly

Maybe, I say slowly
If you’re a very good boy
I will be, you promise
And sigh with great joy

You wore my red panties
And let me spank you there
You knelt in supplication
Tongue groomed my womanly hair

Your face wet with my lust
Your need throbbing for release
Should I give you want you want?
Just because you whispered please?

Smiling now I straddle and squat
To dance upon the tip you gave
Turnabout is fair I think
Who is master, who is slave?
