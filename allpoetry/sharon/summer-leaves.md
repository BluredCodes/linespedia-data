The soft sounds of summer leaves 
That dance in the forest cathedral 
Songs of birds never heard 
Resound in the forest primeval 

A place within this world untouched 
Never seen by human eyes 
A pristine place that hides my soul 
Where Angels wings are sighs 

Worldly is my everyday name 
But my heart holds a passion true 
Every day, every moment I live, 
I live to love just you.
