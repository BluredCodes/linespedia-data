title: "Moonlit Shadows on Silken Skin"
writer: "Sharon"

Soft summer kisses
tongues of desire
tender bites of pleasure
lighting passion’s fire

Moonlit shadows
on silken skin
naked bodies now
sculptured in sin

Melding into one
fitting hard into wet
glistening with lovers
luminescent sweat

Sweet release gained
sleep beckons now
pledge fulfilled in lust
consummating lover’s vow
