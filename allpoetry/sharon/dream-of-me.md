---
title: "Dream of Me"
writer: "Sharon"
slug: "dream-of-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25429-Dream-of-Me-by-Sharon"
---

Above the clouds my love drifts to you
pushed along by Angels wings
softly my love reaches you
and a  lovers song it sings

Dream of me
and I 
will be
your 
destiny

The dark of night is where I’ll be
in the shadows of your sleep
closer than a breath away
softly to you I will creep

Dream of me
and I 
will be
your 
destiny

Quietly I slip to your side
and take you in my embrace
my heart has found in you
a memory it will not erase

Dream of me
and I 
will be
your 
destiny
