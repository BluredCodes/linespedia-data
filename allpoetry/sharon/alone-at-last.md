---
title: "Alone At Last"
writer: "Sharon"
slug: "alone-at-last"
source: "AllPoetry"

Left alone at last my love,
you holding my hand so tight.
The doctor gave his final report,
that I was losing this mortal fight.

I feel my life slipping away;
and see that truth in your eyes.
You watch me with such tenderness
counting all my breathless sighs.

The last few months has made me glad, 
to leave this cancerous place.
And the last thing I want to see in this life
is your sweet and smiling face.

With a moan I take my last breath
my eyes tell you it is so
and closing 
forever 
in this 
life
I watch you 

let 

me


go.
