My dear friend you may be older
But the years I’ve spent with you
Will always bring a smile to my face
Just like the one I  ‘stuck’ on you.

These ears will hear the laughter
Though you can’t hear it with me
But somehow yours went missing
Somewhere in the old pantry

Your body may rot now and then
If not changed out in time
But I remember playing with you
And so, I pen this rhyme

Many hours of pleasure were had
Making a new man out of you
Each time I played with your sweet head
You became someone brand new

As you celebrate this fiftieth year
Accept all accolades as said
You are a man who daily changes
Happy Birthday Mr. Potato Head
