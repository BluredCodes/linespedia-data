title: "Calgon Take Me Away!"

Wedded bliss
should be like this -
candlelight and flowers.
Not laundry and dusting
and vacuuming for hours.
Passion left when we,
took that final vow.
Pots and pans and 
scouring pads, 
are what talk about now.
At the end of every day
we smile across the room,
you there with your dusting cloth
me here with my broom.
Too tired to be more than friends
exhaustion holds us at bay
The only romantic words I want
are  “Calgon, take me away!”
