Almost asleep I reach for you
touch your warm hard thigh
seeking out your manliness
you awake with a sigh.

You let me take charge of you
you sense I need it this way
I take control of your body
and begin my lover’s play.

You have a certain flavor
a taste that’s all your own
I hunger to devour you
that place where seeds are sown.

I lie between your legs
in that most intimate embrace;
glancing up, I see that I,
put a smile upon your face.

Your hands grab my hair
I’m a prisoner to your desire
as you explode into me
drowning me in your fire.
