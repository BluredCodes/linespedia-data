I felt the surge the poetic urge
To put my words in rhyme
Hasn't happened this to me
In such a very long time

Forgive me then, my trebled posts
My voluminous threads in here
I take what comes when it does
I live in constant fear

Some day this muse will leave for good
I'll be nothing but a shell
But til that day I'll write on my friend
Though not always very well!
