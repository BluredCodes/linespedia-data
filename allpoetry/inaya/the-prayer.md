The Prayer

humming
soft warm murmur
deep guttural shivering voice
louder
refuses to be a secret any more

tears 
warm
voluptuous 
heavy 
block out all vision

isolation
chill 

sin
Repentance
purging tears
salvation

voice 
steady
forehead 
Shy 
head 
tilts up
determination
exposure
hope
face catches
light 
bliss 
peace
 
words forth come
eloquent
sure 
heart 
cleansed
redeemed
accepted

eyes close
body relaxes 
silence reigns
