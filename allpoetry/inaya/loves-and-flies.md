Loves and fliesFresh air.. Good morning start..Startled!! Two bodies Mating Outside No remorse!!!!!!!!All could see!!!!!!!!Rolled eyes Kept going!!!!!!!!!Stood In a freezeMouth openHeart pounding Mind blankSatisfaction? Fake triumph?Real thing?Rolled eyes Fluttered wingsTwo directionsMating sceneEnds. Inaya Nasser©2001
