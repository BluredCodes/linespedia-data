A ButterflyBlueWhiteRedBlackThose are my colors.I'm a butterfly.Eyes gazingFingers pointingA fluttering kaleidescopeAll comes back…Wriggling greedy worm,Craving, munching,Never having enough,Maggoty larva hidingIn a silky tenebrous shellAll comes back…Fingers pointEyes gazeThreads wear outPopLet outAlmost a thrushReaches the heightsA fluttering kaleidescopeBlueWhiteRedBlackThose are its colorsIt is a butterfly.Inaya I.
