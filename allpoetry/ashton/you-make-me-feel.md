---
title: "You make me feel"
writer: "ashton"
slug: "you-make-me-feel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/195-You-make-me-feel-by-ashton"
---

You make me feel hated
because of you, I'm jaded.

You make me feel low
You're my solitude foe.

You make me feel like I don't belong
I now question, weather I'm right or wrong.

You make me feel like I have not one friend
No one who cares, no one who will help me mend.

You make me feel like I should go away
Never to come back, never will I stay.

You make me feel like crying
Because of you, I' now dying.
