I know it must be hard, to be going through what you are
Yet there's no need to cry, for I am not far

I'm as close as you need me to be
Please, understand that I will not flee.

I can be brave, I can be bold
I can hug, I can hold.

I'm not one to judge by first glance
Please, will you just give me a chance.

Do you not think I'm real..
I'm human, it takes time to heal.

I hope your not one to doubt..
Please, I need you to not shut me out.
