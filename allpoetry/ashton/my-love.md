---
title: "My Love"
writer: "ashton"
slug: "my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

My love for you is deeper than an ocean
It's much stronger than just one emotion.

When I'm with you I'm flying
When I am not, I'm dying.

I want to grow old with you
Learn about everything you do.

I will embrace you with open arms
The way you caught me, with your your charms.

Us, we'll be together for always
Even walking down heavens hallways.
