---
title: "I try"
writer: "ashton"
slug: "i-try"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I push myself to my farthest limits.
I try my best in everything I do
Yet it never seems to be enough for you.

In your eyes I am failing.
I am the best I can possibly be!
I wish one day, this you will see.

I'm now frustrated.
I can not push myslef anymore!
I'm tired, weak, I am sore.
