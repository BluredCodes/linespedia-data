Was it something I did?
Was it something I said?
What happened to us?
All alone in my bed.
You spoke with a smile
but that smile said it all.
You looked into my soul
but that look made me fall.
Did I fall too hard?
Did I fall too fast?
Have I failed your love?
I thought this time would last.
Now what more can I do
But hope, watch and wait.
Hope you feel, watch your eyes
unless now it’s too late.
