Once upon a time things were normal.
Day in
Day out
You in my life as a friend
To my disappointment.

Then like a shot in the dark
It happened.
Turning my world upside down
Inside out and I can’t even breathe
Without whispering your name.
I lost my sense of reality
Normal became yesterday
Today painful
Tomorrow uncertain.

Once upon a time things became normal.
Day in
Day out
You in my life as a friend
To my complete confusion.

Then like a gift from the sky
It resurfaced.
Melting my heart like a ray from the sun.
You by my side, in my arms
Without me even trying.
I lost myself in your eyes.
Yesterday so far behind
Today pure bliss
Tomorrow assured.

Once upon a time things were normal.
Day in
Day out
I’m just a girl with a breaking heart.
Save me before I fall again.
