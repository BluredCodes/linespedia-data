Beyond my control.


Time.
It's time now to think - 
   about the past few months
   we've spent together and apart
   on, off, on, erratic as life itself.
   A switch only you can operate
   Key to a door that only you can open.
   Days spent wondering if it's for real
   or if tomorrow the other side will be 
   unreachable again.

Time.
It's time now to think - 
   but not too much, or else
   I'll find myself questioning everything
   everyone, every aspect of my existence
   even those I'd come to rely on as true.
