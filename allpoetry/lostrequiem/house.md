title: "House"
writer: "lostrequiem"

One night you held me in your arms
Our hands linked,
I felt our souls connect, and
melt into one.
That one night was pure beauty
in my mind, complete and flawless.
Like a perfect circle,
without beginning or end.

But behind my back
Beyond my reach
Evil dwelled, rearing its head
And snatching you away.

One night you held me in your arms
once more, in perfect isolation.
An intimacy beyond the comprehension 
of other human souls.
Transcending reality.
Never have I dreaded sunlight more,
For tonight, it meant the end
of our time together.

Behind my back
Before my very eyes
Logic vanishes
Friendship hides.

One night,
Once more you'll hold me in your arms.
I'll feel the passion reignite
Our hearts will once more illuminate
with a forbidden love.
The only aim in my dimming life,
One goal, one single road to happiness.
Your strong embrace to shelter me again.

Behind my back
Into the weeks to come
Wishes unfulfilled
Life come undone.
