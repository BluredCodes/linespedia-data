I used to stand with the wind at my back,
pushing me forward towards the edge.
Nearer and nearer it grew until one day
The wind pushes me over.
I fall and fall.
But once I reach the bottom,
I can stand with the sun in my face
Walk forward and stroll in the breeze.
Who knows when I may
meet a cliff again.1999,
