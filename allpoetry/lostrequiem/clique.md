So here I sit alone
in both senses of the 
word. An empty vessel, 
a newly cleared slate, 
wiped of old wants and wishes,
ready to be made and
moulded into something
new.
While you. You sit there
among the birds, smiling
and laughing as always.
Maybe you'll speak to
me, maybe you won't, but
never changing unless the
birds change direction.
North to South.1998,
