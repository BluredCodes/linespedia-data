One.I've done itBefore, and even yesterday.You don't,won'tknow about it.Until the end.Two.I've done itThe first day, but just once.You don't,won'tknow about it.Until the end.Three.I've done itBefore, am doing it now.You don't,won'tknow about it.Until the end.not one of my personal favorites, but has gotten a big response at one of the other sites it's posted on -- any comments would be more than welcomed!
