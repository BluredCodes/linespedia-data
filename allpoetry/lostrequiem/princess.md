I used to think I knew you - 
The princess in her kingdom
in the clouds, her prince charming and
white horse, her riches and jewels.
You and your lady-in-waiting would
sit there in your own private world.
That everyone wanted to be part of.

But once I was part of it.

This princess has succumbed to the lure
of the witches cauldron. She gets shaken
and stirred at times, her prince
charming isn't. For all the riches she has,
she can never be too happy.
Her lady-in-waiting stole the show
and left her in her dark world alone.1998,
