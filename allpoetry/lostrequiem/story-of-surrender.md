---
title: "Story of Surrender"
writer: "lostrequiem"

You came into my life
like a breath of fresh air
rescuing me from the staleness
of a love long abandoned.
Sweeping me off my feet
without knowing your own strength.
This power over me, exerted through
your casual glances, your knowing smiles.
Vulnerable and open, I was merely your toy
like a lump of new clay I presented myself
to be shaped and moulded into something you'd want.
Begging you to change me into something better,
seeking your ever elusive approval.
Pining away quietly to myself
while those around me welcomed you to our world
And snatched you from me with their covert embraces.

I would have surrendered long ago
if there hadn't been some distant glimmer of hope
A window of opportunity 
that I could see but couldn't quite open.
I wonder if its key is long lost,
if this window is my oasis, but only a mirage.
A wilting flower trudging toward one last hope
Only to find it was an illusion all along.
