title: "All I want (for Nick)"

I'd do anything to have you near me
To hold you in my arms
To have you say you want me like I want you.
I'd do anything to see you every day
To marvel in your beauty
To watch you from a distance.
I'd do anything to have you speak to me
To hear your voice directed at me
To know your words are for my ears.
I'd do anything to have another chance
To try again at making it work
To be less of myself and more of something good.
All I want is you.May 98,
