I saw you again last week
Everything was cool,
Birdies whispered in my ears
Told me about you.
Things have changed, 
They told me
I should change too.

I came to you.

Birds had whispered in your ears
Whispering lies and untruths.
I spoke for myself
But you didn't speak.

I walked away.

You came back, all night.
Last time you returned 
I was too close to my sources.
You got the wrong idea - 
The right idea as it is - 

Now nothing's cool.

We agreed to be friends.
But even friendships don't last forever.
