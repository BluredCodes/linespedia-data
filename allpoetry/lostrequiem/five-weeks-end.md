title: "Five Weeks - end"
writer: "lostrequiem"

Time has passed
Quicker than I'd imagined.
Your return has crept up on me
And my heart is unsure if
It's a good thing, or not.

My mind won't stop
Sifting through the seemingly endless
combinations of possible situations
that may present themselves
in days to come.

Unconsciously you haven't even left.
Stayed alive in my heart,
beside the hope I still have in "us".
New thoughts click over and over
But in the last five weeks, I've heard it all before.

Half of my wants to hold you again
The other half wants to snap out of it,
To close the door on this life
and never look back
in a million and one years.

Stop.
