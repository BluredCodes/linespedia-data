There were days I looked into your eyes,
Days I saw you from a distance,
Days I thought about you,
Days I cried,
Days like today.

I told myself a million times
It wasn't meant to be, but
My heart rules my head in times like these.

I raise my eyes to the sky
And wait for a drop of mercy,
Freedom from this seemingly endless
Tunnel of loneliness.

I miss you.
I miss loving someone, and knowing
