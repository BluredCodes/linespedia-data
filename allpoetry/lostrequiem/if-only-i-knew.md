title: "If only I knew"
writer: "lostrequiem"

if I knew you weren't coming back
that when you walked out that morning
that it would be the last,
i would have kissed you
with more passion than you'd ever known
with more desire than you knew i was capable of
imploring you to stay and love me.
i would have held you this much closer
wrapped my arms around you and never let go
looked deep into your eyes and soul
and told you how i really feel.

if i knew this might never happen again
i would have made you stay with me
instead of smiling as you faded away.
i would have touched you more tenderly
held you more dearly
made it harder for you to say goodbye.

if i knew that you didn't really mean it
i would have cried when you called me beautiful
turned my head from your kisses
instead of relishing them like a flower needing rain
kept you at arms length and told you to go home
telling myself it isn't real
to make your departure easier.

if i knew it was just a game to you
i would never have played for keeps
your sincerity just another ploy
to keep me interested and my hopes alive.
i would never have let you into my home
my life, my heart, so you could destroy me.
i would have spat at your smiles
and sneered at your false honesty.

but i didn't have any idea
that you wouldn't be back.Feb 2001,
