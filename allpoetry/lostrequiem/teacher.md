You.
You who taught me 
how to speak.
Who helped me
to walk alone.
Who guided me
when my eyes were shut.
Who showed me
how to act.

You.
You who were my teacher.
Stop teaching.
Not everything is
a lesson.
Maybe now you
can learn something
from me.1998,
