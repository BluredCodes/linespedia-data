title: "The Secret in Knowing"

I know they found out 
Their heavily guarded eyes overflow with power 
The power that comes with knowing 
'Check her!', they order 
'We know what she has been doing.' 
Crying is the only thing I can do 
But no tears will come 
They rip at me, tear at me 
SHE stands over me 
SHE holds a bat 
'What has my little girl been getting into today?' 
That horrible sticky sweet smile that resides on her face, it makes me sick 
SHE is the enemy for which there is no artilliary in the world 
SHE is the one who knows 
And I, I can only run away 
for so long
