---
title: "For Scott"
writer: "beautifulworld"
slug: "for-scott"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am never sure whether you are laughing or crying
smiling or frowning
flying or sinking
bitter or loving
Grinning so hard your face hurts or fending off tears
I am never sure whether I hate you or love you
Whether you hurt me or help me
I never know what to say to you
when things go badly
or I let you down
I never know when something I say makes you smile
or makes you cry
I wish I did
I wish I did so very much
I wish I could make it all go away
I wish you would tell me
