I hate this feeling in my stomach
My enemy, my pain
Bread, oh bread, how i loathe thee
And all that you bring
Please everyone, just stop
Leave me be, let me alone
What need is there here?
Be gone, be gone
There is no need
Only blind eyes and a queasinessa rambling......heh.........
