stop that clicking, twirling, stop that tapping, flinching
why are you doing that? why again?
will you ever learn? will you ever get off that fat lazy butt?
stop that worrieing, slicing, stop that fluff and that finger
why do you shiver like that? why here and now?
will you ever stop? will you ever get up and open that shade?
stop that silver, that cold, stop that obsessing, pinching
why are you like this? why don't you fix it?
will you ever be beautiful? will you ever be selfless?
stop that whining, complaining, stop that lieing, honesty
why can't you just be truthful? why can't you lie?
will you ever find happiness? will you ever find peace?
stop being too lazy to search, stop those empty eyes and pitiful frown
why can't you be like them? why can't you be you?
will you ever stop wishing? will you ever start doing?
stop that flame, that ice, stop the gashes and the fake-outs
why can't you just keep a promise? why don't you smile?
will you ever be human? will you ever be real?
stop the plastic, the glass, stop the manufactured words
why do you hurry past me so quickly? why not stay?
will you ever stop and see? will you ever admit that you are not you?
stop that fluff, silver, stop that cold, lieing
why are you doing that? why again?
