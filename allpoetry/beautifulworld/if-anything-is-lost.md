---
title: "If Anything is Lost"
writer: "beautifulworld"
slug: "if-anything-is-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18193-If-Anything-is-Lost-by-beautifulworld"

the door creaks open
memories and smells and hugs from years gone by
hit me at full force
and you smile at me
from your chair
and everything is alright

after the kisses and the stories and the dinner we sit
in that room
your room
and we talk
we talk about family
and how things will be someday

you ask for a glass of water
so i get up
anything for you
and i fill the glass to the very top
and put ice in just how you like it

when i re-enter the room you don't look up
you don't smile
and faintly i hear you crying
buried inside your arm
crying inside your arm

everyone else smiles and continues to talk
just some crazy old lady
she needs a nap
but she looks up at me
and her eyes are so wise

i need to be held in those eyes
those deep blue eyes i have always admired
set in wrinkles and in pain
but always so strong
so strong

i need for everything to be as it was
i need you to be strong
for me
for me
i need you to be strong
