Love
Small word
Harmless, gentle
Leaving lips of many
Floating through air
As thick as blood
So easily flung around
So easily kept

Love
Harmless word
Gentle, small
Flung carelessly about
Breaking into the heart
As sharp as blade
So thoughtlessly  given
So thoughtlessly received

Love
Gentle word
Small, harmless
Whispered in the ears of the disheartened
Exchanged through warm adjoined hands
As soothing as sunset
So consciously forced
So consciously lied

I honestly love you
The phrase resounds
Throughout my mind and soul
Never before has my heart been ready
Never before have the angels sang
My eyes are assaulted with blinding light
I will step from the darkness
I will stand by your sideThis is a sappy love poem...not for one, but for many...for all those who I see every day...for all those I hug and smile at and whisper to in the dark...I love you...for the first time I can truely mean it and I will say it every day in every way I can
