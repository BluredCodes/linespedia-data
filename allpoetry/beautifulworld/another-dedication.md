The skies are parting, do you see?
Do you see the sun peaking through?
Why do you look away as though in pain?
Is there something there you do not wish to be?
The sun speaks loud to hearts that are true
As long as you keep alive the faith, you have much to gain

Your eyes, why so lost?
Do you see the clouds dissolving?
Why do you look away, as if quietly remembering?
You see the truth, but at what cost?
In the wheels of your mind, problems always solving
Look within yourself, begin rendering

Your smile, why so lifeless?
Do you see the rain clearing away?
Why do you move your lips, as if scilently speeking?
You keep that smile, though what hides behind you will never confess
You turn on your smile, wonder why won't it stay
Look to the sky, there lies what you are seeking

The skies are parting, will you let me lead you?
Do you see the sun appearing?
Why do you pause, stop singing the old harmonies?
Let me guide you through
You watch the clouds from afar, watch them clearing
I'll sing the song back, when you have forgotten the wordsfor you guys...(i cant rhyme harmonies...lol)
