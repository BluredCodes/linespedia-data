---
title: "Lovely"
writer: "beautifulworld"

Lovely how things turn out
The falls and the trembling shoulders at night
The emptyness, confusion
The numb and pain
Stairs and screams
so dark
so dark

Lovely the peeling wall paper
The cold and the gently fading light
The relief, the fear
The numb and the pain
Covers and blankets
so dark
so dark

Lovely the empty corner
The window and the decision to stay in the fight
The euphoria, downfall
The floor's sudden dropping
Rain and silence
so dark
so dark

Lovely the tiresome peace
The assumtion that all hearts should sail like a kite
The untapped trust
The unwrapping of the hidden
Smiles and Secrets
so dark
so dark

How lovely
How lovely
so dark
so dark
