Duck
Duck
The marshmellows are coming
THE BIG
the small
The Medium
hard ones
soft ones
icky ones
sticky ones
Duck
Duck
The marshmellows are coming
Better dance out of the way
Dance like that purple monkey taught you to
(whom you've never returned)
If the monkey is not here, then who's sitting behind me?
*turns around nervously*
Oh, it's just Fred
Duck
Duck
the marshmellows are coming
==================(_()
