Intense need I cannot 
quite voice 
An echo, a sound 
seeks reception 
Anonymously humming it's 
off-key tune 
Constantly hiding behind 
a smile 
'Why do you frown?' 
it asks 
'Let me make it better' 
it pleads 
Then it hides, deep 
and away 
It seeks refuge 
in tomorrow 
It seeks comfort 
in today 
In the moment, it 
now dwells 
Living for an addiction that will 
not disappear 
It convinces itself that it will be different 
TOMORROW
