The tree outside is tapping

tapping on the dark dark window

The window pane no doubt cracking

cracking ever so slowly



Perhaps in a year this crevace will be visible



What is a crack?

A seperation of something whole?

Whole in one, whole in the head

Spelling changes, spelling changes



Hole in one, hole in the head



Head of staff, head of the board

Stiff as a board, light as a feather

Feathers fly in the wind

They're so light and free



Feathers are the most beautiful



Beauty is all around

Can you catch it?

Can you feel it?

It's everywhere



Among the faces of many and the voices of cherubs



Angels voices

Calling, calling out in the wind

Wind, moving air

Air  air air
