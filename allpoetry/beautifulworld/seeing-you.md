title: "Seeing You"
writer: "beautifulworld"

I care about you more than words
can ever express
If ever there was to be
parting between us
I would forever blame, I
would forever cry
You're beautiful, just the
way you are
No strings, no lies, no
sugar-coated smile
Please, be careful, be healthy, cause
I love you
I care about you
more than words
Whatever it is that holds you hostage, let
it release you
Let yourself trust, let yourself love, let
your beauty shine
You are a true rarity
you are you
You are unique
you are lovedYou know who this is for, because it is for so many...and yet only so very few...i love you...i truely do
