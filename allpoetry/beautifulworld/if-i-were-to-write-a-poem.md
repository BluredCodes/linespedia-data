If I were to write a poem
A poem about hardship and long-awaited freedom
The words would fall short
Of the pure path of the dove
If I were to write a poem
A poem about the the glory of peace and the serenity of faith
The words would fall short
Of the innocent clouds of heaven
If I were to write a poem
A poem about falling hopelessly, completely, and deeply in love
The words would fall short
Of cupids sailing arrow
If I were to write a poem
A poem about writing a poem
The words would fall short
Of the beauty and relief that writing brings
Because even when the words are present
The feeling is not
Poetry is dry
And I, I am just a leaf blowing across this barren desert
