~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The beauty of poetry is freedom
A blank page can be a bird
Or a palace
Or even hope
It can rhyme
Or it can be unformed

Poetry can be written for a friend
A blank page can be a sincere apology
Or a declaration of love
Or even the most pure and unrefined form of trust
It can be anything
Or it can be nothing

The beauty or poetry is that it is for me
A blank page records my tears
And my faith
And my hope
It can be for me
And only me
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
