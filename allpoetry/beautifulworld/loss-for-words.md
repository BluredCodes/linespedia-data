title: "Loss for Words"
writer: "beautifulworld"

Screaming
Muffle
Sewn tight - shut
Dull needle
Endless silence
Long thread
Twisted in pain
Expressionless
Blank
Rabbit caught in a headlight
How will it end?
Will thy tongue be thy sword
That cut off thine limbs?
Dull needle
Endless silence
Long thread
Writhing in agony
Expressionless
Blank
Been sought out by lightening
How will it end, my dear?
The answer is up to you
