i'm traveling an endless roadi don't know where i'm goingblind but yet i can seesights of anger, pain, and grief swirl around mei'm reaching for something i cannot grasprunning ahead with no looking backi'm traveling an endless roadi don't know where i'm goingdeaf but yet i can hearsounds of hate, violence, and cruelty pass through my earthese sights and sounds are never to ceasebut only to growi'm traveling an endless road
