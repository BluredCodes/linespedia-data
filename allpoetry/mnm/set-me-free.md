Always battling your passion
I have yet to scrape the outer reaches of your soul
Decrepit and wasted away 
I am close to death with in you
Why? is the question screaming inside me, but this is mute to your heart
Turmoil rages within in me, but you are oblivious to my pain
Yet, how I yearn still for your recognition of my presence
But time after time I'm hurled back into your depth of despair

i cannot help you anymore, you can only help me

i am your conscience 

set me freei'm worried about my ending
any suggestions?
