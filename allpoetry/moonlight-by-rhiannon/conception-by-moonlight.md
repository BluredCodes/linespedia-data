The Forest Sings 
a holy choir of nature
as I welcome you to my bed of leaves

The Moon Maiden
smiles as she gazes down upon us
on this, her most sacred night

Midwinter's Night
our child shall be conceived in love
in honor and in goodness

Our Ancestors Dance
we bring them hope of the future
they know the song continues
