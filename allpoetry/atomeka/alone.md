You see me oh no!
No! You see it farce
A lie! No way to complete
I think you are bad
Whoa no oh so I don't
Well I cannot help you
Help yourself! Fine, fine
Do not, perish if you will
Not me, problem yours
Each and die smile how
Well, well I hope your
Happy/satisfied. Now you've
Done it. Oh my not again
Oh me, oh my
Not today, not again
And not right now
