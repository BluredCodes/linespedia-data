Cider in the face of GodFace! Thou face! Each biteThe rube and eat it onThe rock! Hark rock!I do not dare in everlastingEarth! In the water Brown and dead, tomorrow!Ha! I think not! Gone today!Far far away - winterSpace and each momentCollide! Boosh! And eachSpectral moon and crescentFace dive back intoSea. Help me save meFor I am not hereHark!
