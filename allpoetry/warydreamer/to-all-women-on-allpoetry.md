To all of the women on this poetry site who read DREAMWEAVER's (aka Sammy) poems....

Anyone who is not in love with Sammy...
   and secretely doesn't wish 
      that each poem was directed at you 
          and you alone...
              please line up -- stage left.  

All three of you can leave 
   and the rest of us will wait patiently
      for his next submission
         and dream it was meant for us.

***smile***
