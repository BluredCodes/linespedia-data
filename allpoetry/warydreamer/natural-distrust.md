title: "Natural Distrust"

I trusted you with my thoughts, my feelings,
the REAL ME
that I've hidden from every other living soul.
It's been a trust
without any reservations whatsoever...
...and yet, I've doubted at the same time.
~ ~ ~
Trust or faith and doubt are at total extremes.
There can be no middle ground.
I don't understand
how trust can be riddled with doubt.
That's like a truth full of lies.
~ ~ ~
I'm not sure what it is that I doubt.
Perhaps it's myself.

mJanuary 1987
