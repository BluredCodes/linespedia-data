How long will it take you to realize that, to me, you're...

  ...tall enough...
    The physical and inner strength you possess
      makes you at least eight feet tall.

  ...good-looking enough...
    The character I see in your face and hear in your voice
      makes you more handsome than anyone I know.
      It's accented by your smile lines and laughter.

  ...smart enough...
    The wisdom in your advice contains a depth of thinking I envy.
      Few people can ever acquire it and yet,
      it comes so naturally with you.

You say I'm biased.
  I don't think so...
    I prefer to think I'm perceptive.
                  mOctober 1986
