---
title: "Ourselves And Each Other"
writer: "WaryDreamer"
slug: "ourselves-and-each-other"
source: "AllPoetry"

What you are to yourself and me
    is far more important
      than what others see.

And...
  What I am to myself and you
    may be hidden
      from what others view.

But...
  As long as we both agree
    On what we need from each other,
  I would rather stay with you
    Instead of looking any further.


                       mJune 1987
