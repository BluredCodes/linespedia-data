There's such a differenceIn the RainWhen you haveSomeoneWho shares itWith you.  If you're alone,  The raindrops  Fall  On your face  And intermingle  With the tears  And even if it were possible,  No one  Is there who might try to  Distinguish the difference.  Their sound is  Intensified  Because there is  Nothing  Which interrupts the  Silence.    When someone's beside you,    The raindrops    Sparkle and glisten    As they wash away    Yesterday's dust,    And the     Future    Promises to be    Clean and fresh.    The patterned rhythmn    Becomes soothing    Background sound for    Quiet conversation.        There's such a difference      In the Rain      When you have      Someone      Who shares it      With you...      But      It must be      Someone...      Not just      Anyone.            mAugust 22, 1987
