title: "Today's Me"

I find myself...
  in places where I never thought I'd be
  meeting people I never thought I'd meet;
  seeing things I thought I'd never see
  thinking things I thought I'd never think;
  saying things I never thought I'd say
  doing things I never thought I'd do;
  living life how I thought I'd never live.

I ask myself...
  WHICH ME IS THE BEST?
    Is it the way that I was then...
    Sheltered, naive, afraid, boxed in?
    Or is it the way that I now see...
    Exposed, blasé, un-awed, set free?
  WOULD I RETURN TO YESTERDAY?
    There was a sense of peace I knew
    Which attended me in my limited view;
    But living and loving and being kissed
    Were a part of happiness that I had missed.
  WHO WILL I BE TOMORROW?
    If I can somehow mix the two --
    A little old with a little new,
    Maybe the person that I become
    Will be sure of herself with anyone.

                      mMarch 1988
