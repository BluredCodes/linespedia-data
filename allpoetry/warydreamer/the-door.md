When you left and
    the door closed behind you,
        it didn't lock me in.

I've returned there
    several times ~
        just to check on it.

It's still closed
    ...and unlocked.

Sometimes I opened it
    ever so slightly ~
        to peek on the other side ~
    but you were never there.

An air of sadness
    would drift through the opening
        and settle around my spirit;
    But the panic
        didn't filter through with it
    And so far,
        it hasn't found me again.
