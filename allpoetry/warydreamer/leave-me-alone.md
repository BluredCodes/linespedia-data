---
title: "Leave Me Alone!"
writer: "WaryDreamer"
slug: "leave-me-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't want to be a part of your life!
  I don't want to be in your thoughts!
I finally discovered freedom
  and I'm terrified of being caught!

Maybe I can be your friend,
  but that's all I'm prepared to risk.
I've got too many memories
  stored upon my "disk."

I hope you find your happiness.
  I hope your plans succeed.
But I don't want to be a part
  of anything you need!

I hope I never grow so calloused
  that I'm unaffected by your sorrow,
But I'm only someone from your past ~
  I've no place in your tomorrow.
