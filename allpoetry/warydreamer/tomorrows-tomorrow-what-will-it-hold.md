title: "Tomorrow's Tomorrow ~~ What will it hold?"
writer: "WaryDreamer"

Will you back off farther than ever?
   Will you grow more distant than before?
Will you cease to love me...
   Or will you love me more?

Will you remember me sometime?
   Will you be able to recall my face?
Will you hold fast my image...
   Or will I lose my place?

Will you still call me sweetheart?
   Will you think of me some each day?
Will you always deem me special...
   Or will I fade away?

Will I suddenly find a new love?
   Will I consider him more fair?
Will I someway hurt your feelings...
   Or will you even care?

                  mMay 1987
