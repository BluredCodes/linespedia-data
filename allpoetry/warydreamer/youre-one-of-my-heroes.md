title: "You're One of My Heroes"
writer: "WaryDreamer"

Heroes aren't always popular
   nor well-received by the powers that be.
Heroes aren't always recognized
   nor honored by their community.

Our heroes stand for something
   the rest of us may never attain,
And our heroes hold the secret
   for those things we want to gain.

Being a hero isn't easy
   because it takes such a sacrifice.
Heroes give of themselves and give of themselves
   so we can live in a world that's nice.

Heroes don't always receive the honors
   they're entitled to for their deeds.
The rest of us owe them our comforts,
   because they take care of our needs.

No...real heroes aren't always recognized
   nor do they receive the acclaim
For everyday tasks they perform at length
   in anonymity -- not expecting any fame.

Yes, heroes may sometimes be unsung
   and have flags that are never unfurled.
True heroes are the ones among us
   who make a difference and change our world.

Real heroes are people who do their job
   and hold for whatever's right.
Real heroes are people like you,
   who stand and won't back down from a fight.

Thanks for being who you are...
   ...and setting examples for the rest of us.

                      mNovember '99
