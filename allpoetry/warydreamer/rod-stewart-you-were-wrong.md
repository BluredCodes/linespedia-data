title: "Rod Stewart - You Were Wrong"

I hate to tell you this, my friend
  but I've heard you sing that song
Where "The first cut is the deepest"
  and you've got the words all wrong.

My first love? It WAS special;
  and it's true, we joined our hands,
But this one just absorbed my heart
  as we played out in the sands.

Although the loss of any love
  cuts the marrow of our soul,
When mine walked away from me,
  it left a great big hole.

No, it's not a tiny cut, my friend,
  but an enormous void I feel,
Regardless of the time that's passed
  it just hasn't seemed to heal.

Why, on the surface, I look just fine
  and I'm sure I'd be "approved,"
But it's not obviously apparent
  that my heart has been removed.

Perhaps one day I'll find someone
  who'll bandage up my wound,
But today, next week, or 100 years
  will probably be too soon.

Yes, Someday in the worlds to come
  Someone will surely come along,
But just to set the record straight,
  Mr. Stewart...you were wrong.
