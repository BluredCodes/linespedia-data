title: "He Took, You Gave, I Lost"

He took my love from me;
  You gave it back.
He took my confidence;
  You gave it back.
He took my courage;
  You gave it back
He took my pride;
  You gave it back.
He took my strength;
  You gave it back.

In one stupid act of idiocy,
  I relinquished all you gave me.

Now...
  I have to find love;
  I have to find confidence;
  I have to find courage;
  I have to find pride;
  I have to find strength.
But this time,
      I have to earn it by myself.
   Then,
      It will be mine
   And
      I won't lose it again.

               mMarch 1988
