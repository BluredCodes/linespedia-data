I think I heard a door close...
    but it was a very
        gentle sound.

It didn't slam
    nor did I hear
        the lock turn.

I don't have the feeling
    you shut me out,
        and so far...
            there's no panic.
 
                 mAugust 22, 1987 --The Closing
