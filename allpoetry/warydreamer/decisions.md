Sometimes,

it's really difficult to tell the difference

between temptation and opportunity.



With temptation,

you're supposed to walk away from it.



With opportunity,

you're supposed to walk toward it.



So, here I am...

standing in the middle of the road,

(with the yellow stripes and dead armadillos,)

because I can't decide

whether to run toward you

or to run away from you.
