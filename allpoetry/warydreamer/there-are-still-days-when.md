title: "There are still days when..."

I feel like a very small child
  holding a prized possession
    tightly against me screaming, "Mine!"

I don't want to let go...
  but at the same time,

I realize...
  You have to make
    your own decisions, ...and

I would not want
  to hold you where
    you do not want to be.

                mJan. 1987
