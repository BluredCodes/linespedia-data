There's been a door between us
    for a long time, now.

Occasionally,
    I've pushed it open just enough
        to peek through.
            Sometimes, you were waiting
                on the other side.
            Sometimes, you were not.
    You've tested it too.
            Sometimes, I've been here
                to listen and talk.
            Sometimes, I've gently
                closed it instead.

Last night,
    You opened it wide enough
        for me to pass through.
            The same door that let me in
                also let me out.
    You closed it whenever I left.

This morning,
    I distinctly remember turning the lock.
        Again, there's no panic.
            But this time, there's a smile.
    It was a sound
        we've both been waiting for.
