title: "A Smile Behind the Tears"

A smile
...because it was wonderful ~
         and tears
...because it's only a memory.


There is a void in my life now.
  It's the empty spaces left by the absence
    of your hugs, your encouragement, and your presence.

I'm saddened at their loss,
  but at the same time,
    I'm reassured of their existence.

Before meeting you,
  my experience with these and many other feelings
    was only within the words on a page
      coupled with the cynical conclusion that,
        "Real life's not that way!"

I know now,
  that real life
    CAN be that way.

One thing I'll not allow myself to doubt...
  regardless of how brief,
    it was REAL!

There were no promises, no demands, no commitments,
  and no heavy expectations ~
    but the company, the caring and the concern
      were genuine ~

        I BELIEVE THAT!

                        mDecember 1986
