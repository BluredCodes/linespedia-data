---
title: "Moving Forward"
writer: "WaryDreamer"

In order to face new horizons...
  ...it's necessary to turn my back
    on the old, familiar ones.

When the old horizons have been filled
  with valleys of trials,
    the new ones hold the promise
      of the adjoining mountains.

...But, if the old horizons are filled
  with mountains of pleasure,
    there's a fear of moving
      because valleys are in all directions.

...I must remember though,
  the only way to discover
    the mountain peak in the distance
      is to explore the valley
        that separates me from it.

I know it's time to move
  on to the next mountain,...but still~
    I want to hold your hand
      while I cross the valley.

                     mDecember 1986
