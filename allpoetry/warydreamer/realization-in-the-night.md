title: "Realization in the Night"

I lay down beside you last night,  but I didn't fall asleep.Usually, a contentment settles over me  and my cares and worries fade into obscurity    as I revel in the closeness of my body next to yours.Last night though,  your conflicts became a part of my thoughts    filling them with a series of WHAT IF'S...      It was not a burden and I readily accepted them from you.I rested, but I didn't sleep.  Instead, I lay listening to your night noises ~    the rhythm of your breathing as it slows;    the almost-not-there rumble that comes from deep within your throat      giving the impression of a cat purring in front of a warm fire;    the squeaks your teeth make as you grind them together...Outside noises interfered  and as my attention was drawn away,    I realized I was cold.Ever so carefully, determined not to disturb you,  I slowly sat up to reach and rearrange the covers over us.    Almost immediately, I felt your fingers groping for my waist,      pulling my back against your body,      holding me close to you again.  A tidal wave of pleasure settled over me    and I finally understood about caring and how different it is      when someone cares in return.Then...  I went to sleep.                                   mSeptember '86
