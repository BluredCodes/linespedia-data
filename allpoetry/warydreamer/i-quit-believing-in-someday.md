I quit believing in SOMEDAY.
  I still have hopes for TOMORROW,
    but plans only for TODAY.

  If those plans include you,
    then I will enjoy your company.
  If they do not,
    then I will enjoy my solitude...

This time...
  I will not lose sight of me.
