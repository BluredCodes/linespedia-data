title: "Night Thoughts"

I woke up tonight
and did not have you beside me.
I don't always awaken like this...
Why is tonight different?

All the conflicts in my mind
that I'm able to cast aside when you're here
keep creeping into my thoughts.

Alone...
I hate being along now,
whereas at one time,
I viewed it as a pleasure ~ even an accomplishment.
There's a haunting memory
of being so alone.
Being WITH someone and alone ~ that was the worst or hardest!

And yet,
a small fragment of me does not feel alone.
Somehow, there is a sense of togetherness I share with you ~
even when you're not here.

mOctober '86
