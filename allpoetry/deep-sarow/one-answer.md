When you look in my eyes I see so much.
I see hope, honisty, and some times hurt.
Yet I dont see the love I am looking for.
Are you hidding it or am I just blind and 
can not find it?


 Your touch is so conferting and gentle.
Is it me that you feel this way for?
I get butterflies in my stomach, and joy fills
my heart. But is that the love I feel? Or lust?

 The words you say are so gentle but honist and 
straight forward! But yet I still feel the securety
from everything you say. 

When you kiss me I cant stop from smileing.
when Im happy, sad, confused, and even mad I
still smile and feel the warmth of your kiss.
But yet is it still just lust?

 No it cant be! 
Lust is only temperarly.
Love is forever more.
And forever more is the world
around you!
And my world is you!

 So now I know I have finally found my one and only
                   true love!!!!!!!!!!
