---
title: "sarow"
writer: "deep sarow"
slug: "sarow"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It lingers through your mind,
It brings the never ending pain,
It will drive you completely insane.
         
When you sleep it will be waiting 
for you tomorrow.
And the rest will follow.
The painful thoughts,
The terrifying dreams,
It will never go away for what it
          seems.
Just relax and think of what else there could be
on the other side of that door.

Then you will be free from the sarow.



 by
deep sarow
