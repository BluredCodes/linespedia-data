title: "Hurt"
writer: "deep sarow"

Here we are apart.
For how long I wonder!
Our love is still there,
So I hope.

 But yet Im hurting inside.
The dought of us being together again.
Oh how it hurts,
My heart is torn apart and may not heal
with out you.
The thought of sleeping alone is 
unbearable.
Who will be there when I wake up from a bad dream?
Who will hold me untill I fall to sleep?
Who will kiss me in the morning?
Who will tell me they love me for no reason at all?
Who will do the little things I love the most?

  I cant stand the hurt anylonger!
I need  you because I truly love you?
Please tell me you feel the same!

Or is our love realy at the end?
