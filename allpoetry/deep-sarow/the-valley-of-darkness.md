I walk through the valley of darkness.
Not knowing where I'm going or why.
Is there something waiting for me?

 Or have I already found it?
The pain, the suffering of every day life,

 
 Will I find happiness in death?
If so please take me now.
End the misery,and the lies.

For a single heart can only be broken so many times!

 Deeper,and deeper I walk.
Yet I still find nothing has changed.


 As I grow weaker and weaker
I realize that I shall never escape. 

For the world is the valley and I AM
           the darkness!


         By
deep sarow
