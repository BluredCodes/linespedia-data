Here we are together still. 
Why I can not figure out. 

We cried.
We swore at each other.
We even hurt one another.
Was it because the fight was to tough?

No it couldnt of been.
Cause here we are together still.

We laughed and joked about us departing.
We talked seriouse about me leaving.
But here I am still fighting.

Was it meant to be this way?
Or are these 6 months of fighting a waist of time?

We confess our undieing love every day.
We hold each other all night.
We wake up and look into each others eyes.
We smile at each other.

But is the fight over?
No it will never be over.

To keep our love we must fight.
Or we will loose this feeling.

So to prove my love to you I will fight!
I shall fight till the end.


    By
Deep sarow
