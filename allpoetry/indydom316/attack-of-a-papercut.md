Oh, how it stings
it is so small
it burns so deeply
like the flames of hell
I am kissed on the finger
by a succulant pain
it bites me and runs
only a second it took
I want to scream
you can't even see it
maybe I am wrong
SQUEEZE
needles in my spine
it pours crimson 
my teeth clenched in agony
this small kiss
insignifigant kiss
will hurt me a lifetime
the fire burns on...figger it out...
