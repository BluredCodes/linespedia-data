A world of dim flames
their wicks spent and wasted
an enlightened soul enters
and rekindles their individuality
without eclipsing them 
embraces them and makes them stronger
together a glorious fire
ablaze with hope and courage
ambition and determination
but he is masquerading
wearing a mask of happiness 
to hide inner despair
held hostage by the ghosts
in the corners of his room
when his flame is spent
and smothered by the cold of another
and his mask is shattered
will he let those he saved
return the favor?
We hope so
In a world of enlightened souls so few
we must do all we can
to keep his soul aflame"Based on a true story...."
