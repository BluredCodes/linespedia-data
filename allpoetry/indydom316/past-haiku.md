Overcome the past
I faced it all before and
now I've kicked its ass

My friends bring it up
but I don't care anymore
cuz' I've kicked their ass

memories trigger
the feelings that were once there
but I've kicked their ass

read my sad poems
but don't worry about me
cuz I've kicked my ass

I have hurt myself
others too, but through it all
I have still stood up

so now you read this
and maybe understand why
