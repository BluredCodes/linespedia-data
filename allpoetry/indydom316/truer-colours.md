Turquoise isn't a colour
Neither is Chartreuse
Burghundy's a figment
no more real than puce
Red, Yellow, Green and Black
Blue purple and white
Those are the colors of the world
as plain as day and night
No pansy teals, no snippy pastels
invade my plain of life, 
when you add these different hues 
they add nothing but strife
much simpler life would be if it were black and white
with no grays or greys or graeys to complicate my plight
So what without these hues it's hard for ideas to be?
They do nothing but distract and make it harder to be me.
So you can think in verdent or indigo if you wish
But you will never find 
any burnt sienna upon my dish
