Their Looking at us
no IM's in the comp. Lab
time for us to go

sorry all my friends
time for me to leave this place
I'll see you all soon

I pay big money
to get kicked out of the lab
that I'm financing

don't you arrest me!
I CAN take this computer!
I bought it damnit!

You know what? you can
take this thing and shove it up...
