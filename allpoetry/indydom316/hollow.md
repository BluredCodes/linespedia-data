I'll destroy you
I'll tear up all that you are
and feed upon the remains
but first....
FIRST
I'll leave you in pieces
crying
bleeding
suffering
f-o-r-h-o-u-r-s-a-n-d-d-a-y-s
you'll be there
what you are inside
will finally be reflected
OUTSIDE
you're tough
and ingenius
and the best at everything
no no no no NO
you're scum
and vomit
and pestilence
and I will not abide it
I will bring light to you
and chase away the shadows you hide in
to show your true self...
