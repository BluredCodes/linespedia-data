an inner scream - a silenced dream - a dulled sheen

I've never seen 
  a vaccine
    for a heartbreak

I've never heard
  a stong enough word
    to heal

but with this breath
  I will progress
    to nurture

and then one day
  I'll come to say
    "I'll save you"

you see my dear
  I know you fear
    the outside

and I know you
  are tortured too
    by the past

but all you need
  is a tiny seed
    of hope

and it will grow
  to finally show
    you trust

you may doubt
  but I will shout
    and plead

and talk scream
  until your dreams
    are real

you see my dear
  I know you fear
    I love you

but that's ok
  for I will never
