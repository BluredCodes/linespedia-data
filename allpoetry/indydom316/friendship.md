Looking out for me?
LOOKING OUT FOR ME!?

you tell me to look left
so I cant see you plotting on my right

You tell me to be alone
then pursue the one I love

you tell me to be stronger
only to stab me from behind

you tell me you love me
and tell others I am a monster

you tell me to do things
you would never do yourself

you tell me not to do things
but then you do instead

you tell me I am good
but make me feel like I am bad

you tell me you've learned your lesson
but a week later it is all the same

looking out for ME?


  
                ......true friendship is rare
