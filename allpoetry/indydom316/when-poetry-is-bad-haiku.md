Sometimes you don't know
that what you write sometimes can
hurt someones feelings

It's just a poem
but the things you say are real
to those who apply

I hope you all know
I mean no harm when I write
I just feel sometimes

but if I catch you
off guard and make you cry, well
I'm truly sorry

I'm just trying to
write something that means something
please, please forgive me.Wrote a poem and hurt someones feelings....I'm sorry!
