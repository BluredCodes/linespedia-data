Stairs
    Straight and Narrow
         Climbing higher, towards another
             and another and another forever
                Neverending they are...there is always
                      more when you reach the top
                 Winding and turning gradually
             you never notice the twists 
          until they are upon you
              and by then, it is too late
                    you can keep going up or
                        you can climb back down
                          theres nothing the matter with going back


                          


                                       Just don't fall
