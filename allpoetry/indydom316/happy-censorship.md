Covering whats real for the sake of the fantasy
Distort the truth to enhance your reality
Planes fly and buildings fall
and...where were you through it all?
My best friend lost someone yesterday
Oh, you knew, well then that's ok
I'll just watch the news and see others strife
and you can tell me how to live my life.
