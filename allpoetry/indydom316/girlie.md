The stars light up my heavens skies
but I come ro realize they are your eyes
their glow spreads out and encompasses me
and I feel the joyous ecstasy
of your gaze

The water soothes my aching pains
just like a calming summer rain
but I'm very slow to realize much
it is not the rain, but 'tis your touch
that heals me

I look up now and see the sun
and know a new day has begun
today though I am feeling smart
'tis not the sun, but 'tis your heart
that warms me

I hear the soft song of the birds
that soothe and caress much like your words
sometimes they might peck and claw
it is ok, for through it all
I'll love you.I know I know.... "Indy's writing a love poem!? And theres no machanized dinosaurs running around chasing midgets??? What's the deal!?!?!!" Well, when someone enters your life in such a profound way, they deserve things like this ....LOVE YOU BABY!!!!
