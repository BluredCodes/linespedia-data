title: "Uncold Mist"
writer: "IndyDom316"

I walk through the morning mist
looking for that I have lost
I know not what it is
Only that it is missing

I search and strain my eyes
but the mist, it overcomes
it permeates my soul
and penetrates my very being

I strain to see beyond
and see only faint shadows of familiarity
But cannot grasp a thought
as to what they might be

Is the mist from morning?
Or is night soon to come?
I see no sense of time
nothing to get my bearings

I stretch a hand out into the mist
but cannot see it before me
cannot grasp the other side
cannot feel the boundary

The mist, it is cold
but I know this not from feeling
I know this because I know
that death is very cold.
