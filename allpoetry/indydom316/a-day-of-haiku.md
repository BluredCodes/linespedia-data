~
alarm clock annoys
awakens me from slumber
thrown across the room
~
pulling pants up leg
looking for the damn zipper
they are on backwards
~
milk in cereal
try some but it tastes funny
it was seven-up
~
look at my watch and
it's deffierent form alarm
daylight savings time
~
late for work again
run to the car with no shoes
gas pedal breaks off
~
get on bus shoeless
people give me funny looks
forgot to comb hair
~
get to work with 'fro
boss sees I have no shoes on
fires me - what a bitch
~
arrested today
for calling my boss a bitch
her boyfriend's a cop
