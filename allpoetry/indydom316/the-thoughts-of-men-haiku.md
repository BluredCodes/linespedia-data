Three Hundred Sixty
Horsepower in a Mustang!?
I just wet myself....

^^^^^

Huh? What do you mean?
I left the seat up again?
Standing up's not hard!

^^^^^

But it's leather and
it folds back, holds my beer, and
was only two grand!

^^^^^

Poetry? you want
me to miss the superbowl...
for dumb poetry?

^^^^^

Honey, I need bail
I was out with the guys and
this hooker...*CLICK* huh?

^^^^^The thoughts and Ideas here DO NOT reflect the personal views of the author...he was just feeling incredibly silly.
The author learned the hard way about the "seat up issue"
