They're there...
through the haze...
but my mind is like a maze...
and I
can't seem to grasp what I seem to be missing

they were here...
a second ago...
I look high and down below...
and I
dont know where they've gone off to.

It seems sometimes my mind is so small
but I cant seem to sift through it all
I climb and creep and then I crawl 
through the depths of my sleeping self

And then when I start to get close
to that dream 
that I loved oh the most
it escapes me and hide up on another shelf

and I know they were there just a moment ago,
Now I awake and find that they just decided to go
they were there, in the haze
but my damn mind is like a maze
and I...

I see through..
I see through the haze...

and see they've all...
