It's cold outside and the winds whip my face like a torrent
I'm trying to walk down the streets of life against the current
I pass a store with a television in the window
"War breaks out..." somewhere, I don't care to know
I pass by the "Bookstore of Morgan LaFey"
and step up to my old haunt, "The AllPoetry Cafe"
I close the door and turn to put my coat on the rack
and suddenly the warmth hits me like an attack
but this warmth is soothing, like a grandmothers hug
and I know that this place is filled with nothing but love
I look around and look at all my friends I adore
I've only know them but a week but it seems like much more
Tina's sittig with Deathy by the window I see
Tinas talking and Deathy's drawing her realm out beautifully
I give them both a wave and walk further inside
towards the table in the corner where I'm likely to hide
haikumonk is at his coffee table in front of me
he doesn't look up his pen is moving feverently
I leave him be cuz it seems he's found some inspiration
It makes me wonder if he'll ever take a vacation
I hear a bit of a scuffle in the corner again
I turn and see the same old group of my rowdy friends
Mooseman and zeusdawg are playin a game of chess
but the pieces are all adverbs and they seem to be stressed
but before I can get up and try to offer some help
mystysaint walks by and starts to quiet their yelps
I laugh to myself as this happens every other day
I hear Manicmuze laughing too, I turn to him, "hello" I say
I turn back as I feel a tap on my shoulder
It's Leylia saying hi, just stopping over
I say hello and see out of the corner of my eye
a pair of dark figures in the corner so I start to play spy
killed by Jack the Ripper and Lonkert over there
I turn away and leave them cuz it's rude to stare
they do their own thing and people may not like it
but the Cafe's a place that doesn't discriminate where you sit
I look to the door and I'm happy to see
a bunch of friendly faces walk in and I'm glad they know me
Desdemona, Sidewinder, gecko, gozling, Rodney, 
Bigmamajen, PHATwriter, Dillion and treasie
Suddenly I hear some dishes drop in the back
Just Kevin, tryin to keep the place from going out of whack
I order me a pencil, good ol' number two
and a piece of blank paper...no...make it college ruled
and I set them down in front and just take one look around
the materials all here, in these new friends I've found;)
