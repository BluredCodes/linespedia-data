Today I read a poem about a woman mistreated
by a lowly piece of slime that called himself a man
And all I thought about after I read the piece
was "How,in such a "civilized" world? 
How, in a world of such smart people...

"How is it, that no matter how good you may be
How perfect you are
How moral and beautiful inside
How innocent and kind
How perfect and imperfect you are
THERE ARE FILTHY DOGS EVERYWHERE
AND NO ONE CAN TELL THE DIFFERENCE
UNTIL IT'S TOO LATE...

and no matter what the outcome....
these slime never change
never break
never bend
never mend
never ascend
never amend
but always pretend
that they....THEY are right....
and THE WORLD is wrong....
AND THAT NO MATTER HOW HORRIBLE THEY MAY BE
NO MATTER HOW SCARED THE DEVIL HIMSELF IS OF THEM...

They tell you that it's all your fault...

All my fault?
ALL MY FAULT???

ONE DAY YOU WILL BE FACED WITH THE ULTIMATE TRUTH
AND I HOPE WITH ALL MY HEART
THAT DEATH HIMSELF IS IN A BAD MOOD
WHEN IT'S YOUR TURN TO BE JUDGED!"I read a poem by nightfighter called "Grrrrrrrrrr"
It had me very upset.
I hate horrible people...and if nightfighter reads this...I hope everything comes out ok.
Sorry everyone if this sounds like ranting and raving....
but when you get mad.....  your poetic vocabulary and rulebook kinda get misplaced.
