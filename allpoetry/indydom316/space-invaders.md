back and forth
here they come
slow slow slow
they wont stop
down down down
coming at me
forth and back
gotta stop 'em
down down down
gotta get 'em
slow slow slow
save the world
blast blast blast
this is easy
shoot shoot shoot
piece of cake
slow slow blast
one more down
fast fast fast
huh? oh no
fast fast fast
gotta keep up
back back forth
thats not fair
forth down fast
here they come
fast back fast
cant keep up
fast down forth
they're too close
fast fast fast
how'd this happen
fast shoot fast
shoot fast shoot
fast fast no
fast can't fast
stop fast fast
fast fast fast
thud
Continue?you all KNOW you've been there!
