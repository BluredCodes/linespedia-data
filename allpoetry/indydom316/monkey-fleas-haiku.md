Attacked by the fleas
through my computer screen from
Monks little doggie

Itchy itchy nose
my elephant's got tourettes
hope Monk gets it now!

revenge is too sweet
Monks got tics so now he can
scratch his puppy dogdamn cyber-fleas...this will show you!!!!!
