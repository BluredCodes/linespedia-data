(see all I can see)

it radiates off of you
its the cold sweat you wake up with
it drips down your back
its cold numbs your spine

(hear all I can hear)

you cannot disguise it
you stand and face me strong
but it lurks in your shadow 
awaiting you to turn around

(taste all I can smell)

it follows you in the room
I can smell it's stench before you enter
the ghosts that are haunting you
are driving you to madness

(I can taste your fear)

what are you so afraid of
you have nothing to fear
it lingers and walks behind you
turn around and it dissapears
only to emerge again
when you feel strong and safe
but it surfaces and surrounds you
and you are nothing more than a waife
you have come to me for help
to make it all go away
but this shadow will never leave you
for you were shot dead, yesterday
so cry and weep and moan if you will
if that is what you feel
but realize you cant feel anymore 
your corpse is as cold as steel
so what is that ghost that lingers behind
and causes you so much strife?
it is the shadow of all the things
