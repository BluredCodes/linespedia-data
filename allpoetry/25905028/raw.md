So you have stabbed me again.No actual physical pain.No blood.It is the anger with in me.Only to extinguish when I please.Free of any rules,Absolutely no boarders,I will strike revenge,To those who seek terror,But for now you may rest.For it is your final chuckle.Just a reminder that this piece is just another free verse. Comment if you will, but this is what it is.
