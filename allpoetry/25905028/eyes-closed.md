I feel your presence,
I smell your perfume,
I can taste the air become sweeter with each step.

I imagine you day and night.
I live with you day and night.
I need you day and night.

I can't imagine a life without you,
A life with no light.
I once felt your lips with mine,
It just felt so right.

So friends we are,
Friends we will be,
Love is what I share with you,
Because friends are forever.Please don't grade this, Its just something I had to write.
