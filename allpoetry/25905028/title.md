You were here yesterday.Not here today.You promised to be here.Not even breathing.You were so happy.Not any more.I missed you.Nothing matters.I thought you were scared of heights.Now I lay here with no motion.I trusted you, you promised you wouldn't.Just wanted to fly. Leave me alone.I'll miss you.Its something that had to be done.
