This road I've been down before,There are no fame nor glory,Still this risk I take,In these smiles I fake.In a daze,this faze,I realize,Everyone dies.Born free,Caring for me,This shell I aim,This life I claim,True to what you see.Accept me for who you see,Conclusion will come in time,I am the motionless mime,Oppose to what you agree.I am not depressed. I write for every emotion I go through. I'm sure you all have had that 'emotion' before.
