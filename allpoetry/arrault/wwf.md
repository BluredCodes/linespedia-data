The bell gave its sound
    while I stood in fear,
    the first moves came quickly 
    and sent me to the mat;

    up in confusion
    I acted slowly, 
    with a patent move
    I stunned your heart;

    turning my back
    when the crowd gave a cheer, 
    on top of my head
    came a folding chair;

    I sent you back
    with a poke in the eye,
    you recovered and sent 
    me to the ropes;

    Then you ended it,
    with two words
    and a clothesline.
