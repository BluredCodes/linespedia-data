A Midnight Stroll

A midnight stroll as I softly tread
Looking at the earth it seeming dead
Finding a flower standing all alone
I pick it up calling it one of my own
Holding it in my tightly clenched hand
Befalling the misery in all the land
Seeing the shatter of all that came down
For this I see blood and alagore all around
I look at the bodies the one's dying slowly
I shake my head and look down, feeling lowly
The flower it seemed weighed more with my tears
This is the day I ever felt fear in long years
Dropping the flower, it having a large thud sound
Darkness crept around, in the new corpses found 
Into a hallow of nothingness souls were callin'
Seeing the spirits of the one's who have fallen
But I see a light above my head, None the less 
Its now time to go to my Quest to happiness~*~ Just a little something I Thought up, I think it best describes a soul looking at all the madness in the world after a war ~*~
