How could you do this to me
I didn't want any part 
Then I seen the best of you 
That overturned my heart

I try to keep struggling ideas 
from entering my hurting mind
I looked around for someone new
Never have I met one ever so kind

Not anywhere nor anyone fit you
I didn't seem to surely find
Matching the essence of love 
you had, That showed so devine
 
Now I know in my heart there's a God above 
As well do you to, the way love stays strong
You took my beating heart to love
I'm not as much before as I thought ...Alone

It left me thinking how inside you are
How can I possibly ever get you out
Holding you tightly inside as if in a jar  
Being filled with love without a doubt

To take you as the precious treasure you are
you ripped my huge heart from out of my chest 
Never wanting you away loving you best by far 
And I offer it to you for one special request

Its up to you to find out, What to do with the rest
Thats why when I fell in love, I knew you were the best
You bring the ever warming movements from my chest
Me and you, Forever at home in our loving nest

To speak through these gentle of lips
With your sudden smooching ways and then 
Your gentle caressing of fingertips
Its time to fall in love all over againI know its not good,but I made it in dedication to Jonathan.~*~ Mystery Passion ~*~
