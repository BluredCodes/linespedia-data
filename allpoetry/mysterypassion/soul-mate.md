what have we done ...we created the ultimate bond
how did this happen? ...I can see you every day
I feel you all the time ...and you also do too
I see what you see ...each of us together forever
I hear also what you do ...whisperings of sweet nothings
my soul was brought up ....out of my own body and mind
I touched yours ...though you feel so very far away from me
I felt your hands and your heart beat ...right next to mine
you say it was just fun ...but this is getting way too serious
happiness you brought ...for a cost which I have paid for this
I gave my soul to you ...you held it in your hands gently caressed
I felt sad and alone for a while now ...but you have been so happy
You spoke you never felt this way ...in your entire life.
I have also felt your presence in my soul ...as well your happiness.
are you to be called my soul mate? ...after the bond was formed.
I don't know but for now on ...thats what I will call you my ~*~ Soul Mate ~*~


~*~ Mystery Passion ~*~
