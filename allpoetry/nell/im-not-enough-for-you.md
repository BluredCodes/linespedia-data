I'm not enough for you,
I never have been,
And i never will be,
At one stage i thought i was,
But i was only fooling myself.

Fooling myself into something,
I never was,
And never will be,
At one stage it was a hope,
But i was only dreaming.

Dreaming something that won't come true,
It never did,
And it never will,
Just a silly little girl,
Who lived in her own little world.

A little world that didn't exsist,
It never did,
And it never will,
At one stage i thought it did,
But realization finally set in.

Realization that i'm not enough for you,
I never have been,
And i never will be.Ok Nell is very bored,can't you notice?
