title: "In His Place"

I've got the infection 
The one where I know you're there 
And I get a headache 
I hear your voice 
And my ear bleeds 
I look at you 
And my eye blisters 
I've got that infection 
There's only on antibiotic 

Poor child 
Sixteen and fatherless 
Two younger sisters 
And a sickly mom 

Father died 
Too many pills 
Angry teenager 
Hates his father 
For what he's done 

To leave him 
Leave him with his shattered family 
Crying to his father's grave 
"oh papa why'd you leave us like this?  Why did you leave?" 
"Where you forced to take those pills?" 
"Whas it me?" 
"Oh papa, I miss you." 
"Papa...How do I fill your shoes?" 

In his place 
He quits school to work 
He still has hope for his sisters 
He's fearful though 
For his mother's begun 
To stare amlesly 
At the knives in the kitchen 
Poor boy 
So fearful now
