title: "Blistering Eye"

I've got the infection 
The one where I know you're there 
And I get a headache 
I hear your voice 
And my ear bleeds 
I look at you 
And my eye blisters 
I've got that infection 
There's only on antibiotic 
This time 
The only way for me to heal 
Is for you to leave 
I'm so sorry it has to end this way
Good bye
