Little child 
How adorable 
As she plays with her blocks 
Burning Pyramids 
Building castles 
Only for someone to break them 

Little child 
So adorable 
As she plays with her blocks 
Day in and day out 
She could care less for another toy 
She could care less if the blocks were made for a boy 

Little child 
Oh adorable 
As she plays with her blocks 
Concentrating on building 
This spectacular thing 
Building and building 
Until no more blocks remain 

Magnificant! 
Extrodinary! 
Intelect and genious! 
An artist beyond artists 
This would take money from a rich purse 
This she calls "blue" 
For the color of her blocks 
This masterpiece she's built 
It's fit for a mouse 
It's a tower at it's finest 
And the finest it shall be
