I'm addicted to this feeling 
I can get past all that is here 
I'm addicted to this feeling 
I hope this feeling is real 
I'm addicted to what I know 
And what I know isnce 
All I know is independance 

I'm addicted to this feeling 
My insides are turning 
My mind is burning
I'm addicted 
I'm addicted to this feeling 
I'm addicted to freedom 
I'm so hammered 
Hammered on life
