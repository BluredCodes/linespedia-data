---
title: "Together, we fall..."
writer: "Scarred Angel"

Read me, 
Carve your words into my flesh.
Listen to its crumpled skin
Hear it pulse and know my touch

Show me,
Spin your faith into my eyes
Breathe in its sour judgment
Taste my skin and know its poison

Touch me,
Imprint your thoughts within my heart
Feel it quiver in its fleshy coil
Sense a part of you imbedded inside me

Hold me,
Share your warmth with my body
Take away the pain of knowing
Alone in this darkness, we fall together…
