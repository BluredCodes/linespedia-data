title: "A cure for droopy eyelids"
writer: "Scarred Angel"
slug: "a-cure-for-droopy-eyelids"

A cascading sensation swirls before you,
Just a stretched arm away
A mingling of delights and torridly changing thought patterns
The Pinnacle of high, a vast horizon of unearthly beauty
Reach, it is so close
It is always there and attainable anytime you wish
However,
It may become cloudy and visions may blur
It could take an eternity to crawl above this wall
But even though distorted, it is still there
Just out of reach
Maybe the next step will lead to bliss
The unknown remains hidden until we brave it
Meaningless actions, generated by the imagination
A crumbling path that remains intact
Moments away
Forever in sight
Omnipresent
An unpredictable force unwithered through time
Just a little further, almost there
Around the next corner, over the next rise
Unreservered compassion in its essence
The perfect drug, purified within its peak
Stumble pathetically towards your end
Grasp it, you may take it
Hold heaven in the palm of your hand
If it slips through your fingers and speed ahead
It hasn’t left
It’s just being an evasive emotion
For even though it may be available
A fleeting glimpse is all it allows
Just a stretched arm away
