---
title: "Moonchild"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

Lulled by it's light,lured by it's majesty
Drawn forth from darkness, to shine upon myself
A pathway through a gate unknown
To a waiting bed
My one true home

Radiance from a smiling face
Forever frowning in sadness, alone
Visited by a lucky few, left unchosen
To walk upon
My silent mother

Her eyes gaze down towards damned creation
Tears of rain fell for the forgetful sinners
Blessing her children till eternity falls
Awaiting her time
For darkness to rule all.
