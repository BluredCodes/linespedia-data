An unspoken word, a thought of love
Stirs sacred emotions deep within
Through times of sadness and spaces of joy
They display an end to a lifetime of waiting

Starlight’s brilliance contained in a glistening drop
Rolling down a parchment of skin
Their salty tastes are bitterness and loss
A cascade of love, from pools of sorrow

For those who contain these inner memories 
Who’s guarded feelings never surface to air
A time shall come from pasts long shadow
To inflict your worst fear, an outpour of grief

When alone and lost they shall call
Unbidden they flow, age after age
Until a cleaner love, for the hurt to heal
Out last refuge, a shoulder to weep
