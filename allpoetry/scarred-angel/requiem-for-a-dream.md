title: "Requiem for a dream"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

Eyes shut...
Rising above this plane
Release my fleshy coil
I see you
Wearing my face as a mask
Beckoning me forwards
holding me down

Sinking...
You cut off my wings 
Tear a hole in my heart
Escaping in my darkness
Rip up the wound
let the fetid air breathe

No more...
Spiralling through the big come down
Spinning ever faster
To what end?
Nothing but his laughter
and Bright shining maniacal eyes

Lost...
On my Path with no end
A stinking abyss
Crumbling down around my feet
I rejoin this filth
it welcomes my rotten core

Sleep...
With no more dreams
Just unending silence
A taste of the sublime dark
So sweet now, on my tongue, flickering
Never remaining whole
