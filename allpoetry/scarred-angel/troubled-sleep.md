---
title: "Troubled Sleep"
writer: "Scarred Angel"
slug: "troubled-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"

Unknown to previous thoughts,
Hidden through fog
Darkness swirls, with vengeance in chaos
Answers are lost
A meaning is misplaced
Our time is forgotten and filed away
With nothing left
Silence encroaches, smiling
Masking what is
Leaving minds to wonder
And history fade
Minutes spent alone
Years wasted hoping
A crushing memory to hold
Muted
