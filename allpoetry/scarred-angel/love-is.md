---
title: "Love   is?"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

Love, for some, is the bringer of happiness
Bittersweet tales for lucky individuals
Romantic evenings, bathed in mystic moonlight
Making love near a roaring fireplace

For the others left behind?, it carries out misery
Thunderous black clouds, our own personal doom
Wrenching our hearts, destroying our hopes
Displaying deepest fears, our own immortality

Many a fool has been quoted as saying,
"love is the greatest feeling in life"
They mustn't have felt the scorn of a broken heart
That withers the life-force that binds us all

So what does that leave us...One golden commandment?
Love is not defined, love is our last choice
A cross-road of life, joy or despair,
We all must choose and live out our sentence
