---
title: "Looking back"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

A year has past on into histories gaze
Smeared by the truth, tainted by reality
Yet blessed with beauty and compassion
Not ending like it did in confusion
Words compare nothing to feelings, but can hurt as easily
I’m tired
And missing an angel beside me
For whom I am an annoyance it seems
I still fear that I am nothing
Merely a mouse in a cats world
To be tormented until my masters are bored
Then devoured, and left
For my mouth betrays my feelings
My hands disobey my meanings
Leaving painful mistakes in my path
Casting pure shadow
Over a once bright pyre, reduced to smouldering embers
Still flaring at times, fighting against night
But lost to her
Crumbled by illusion
And destroyed by stupidity and thoughtless words
