Depraved

Suited apes that steal my air
Shaved monkeys that pollute my earth
A loss of time that rings clear in my ears
Echoing sinister laughter

Soundlessly

Through a loop dream i've watched, revolted
Decays taken hold, in the guise of corporate tweed
Profit driven smiles that crush with a breath
Shaking skin as pockets grow fat

Greed
