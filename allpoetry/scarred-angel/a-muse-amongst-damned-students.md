---
title: "A Muse amongst damned students"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

Outside, faint noise erupts from within
Gleeful sounds of mirth and joyful actions
From here, it’s but a distraction from the diesel
An image we’re not part of,
Or ever can be.
Stuck from ever being able to leave or break free.
We are Prisoners by our own choice
An outsider through our own means
Nothing without any others perception
Silently scribbling alone, far from the house of decadence
Self-imposed solitude, free for mocking
“Come see the silent naked freaks in all there glory!”
Only a selective few looks on with any reflective understanding
Roses amongst daisies, unique and beautiful as winters mists
Someone who at least cares about the fools reality
But in the green we still sit,
Amongst the leaves we belligerently ponder
And recall the sounds of moments past
Of a thousand nameless insults now directed at us
So we shrink further away, tightening the knot
Left cold and forgotten, tired and bruised
An anomaly floating away,
While inside, the laughing continues unabated
