title: "A world of smoke"
writer: "Scarred Angel"

A wall of mess, my room of smoke
A world of junk, a message of hope
By the book, follow the numbers
Cross no line, it’s boundary broken

A Pack of lies, a Pack of smokes
A bleeding heart, My lasting hope
One step beyond our saving grace
To see the light, glimpse the end

The one lost love, The last dying breath
The finale of time, the lost lives left
Dying great sun of heaven and earth
The cold grey moons frozen silent scream

The peace dove killed, the contaminated mans breath
The valley of ashes, The child now left
Humanity in decay, anarchy thrives
Live in the shroud of smokes victory
