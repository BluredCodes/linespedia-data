---
title: "gLiMeR"
writer: "Scarred Angel"
slug: "glimer"

A Light in the darkness
Flickering and wavering inside insecurities
But some shade of hope after all
Maybe all is not destroyed
The last shard not shattered beyond all recognition
We shall see if this lingers
For I have grown tired of spiralling
With not a care for this whole world
With the warmth dissipated
But what is frozen, may yet be thawed
A single spark may give birth to a flame
And continue its dance with abandon
These freaky loops are learning curves
Of which, many answers may be written
However,
In the end it is still but a light in the darkness
A singular entity to flood a cavern
Which may still be snuffed out
Leaving a misty trail of spoken words
An echo that soundlessly rings for eternity
