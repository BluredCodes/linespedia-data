Broken stands the wheels of time
Fixed in place
Molded within flesh
Locked to its vision of darkness in glass

Smudged fingerprints smear the future
Glistening deceit
Filtered hopes
Our Hands fixed, pointing bone white fingers

Careless eyes gaze upon rigid silence
The soundless monotony
The endless monogamy
