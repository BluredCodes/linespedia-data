Fallen From a place unknown
It stretches tired thoughts of restrained apathy
Recalling serenity in a place far away
It begins grimacing & baring teeth Filed by words
Unrelenting a spectre of past approaches
Slithering through dogma’s, casting aside faith
Drawn by a new born babe, ripped from the womb
Whose tear carved face now stares back empty
Its dry parched lips mouth a silent plea,
“Why?”
The Phantom merely shakes his clothed head
“For your penance, my child”
With a sweeping gesture he reveals the truth
Another lifetime of debauchery and sin
Showing a white face the lies it’s believed
While the creature simply beckons him to follow
“Do you understand now my child?”
Confusion swirls and begins anew
For this was lost, this was forgotten
Again the word rings hollow in his ear
“Why?”
