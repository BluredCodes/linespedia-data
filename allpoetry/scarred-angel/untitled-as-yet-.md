Eye’s open, mind shut
Forever barred from becoming night
A doorway closed, locked within
Rusted solid on hinges of ice
Loose dreams, on wings of thought
Dancing inside a glass mirror
Reflecting dirt, lost lies
Hidden inside a path of light

Eye’s hazy, Mind blank
Casting pure shadow on torn stone
A Childs tear, glistens in the dark
Trapped inside a prism of creation
Cold hearts, reflect your fear
Announce your belligerence to humanity
Lost world, always in sight
Fading through my life stained fingers

Eye’s closed, Mind dead
Circular thoughts obscure true vision
Training wheels, Broken and fractured
Weaving a path to tomorrows end
Mobile home, Sleep in a jigsaw
Arrange the pieces to suit normality
Community space, Service in bondage
