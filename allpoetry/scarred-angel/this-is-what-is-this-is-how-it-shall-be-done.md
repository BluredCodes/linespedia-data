In times of silence we speak, without thought
A Few words, Meaning nothing, explaining everything.
Tumble
Fall
Spill from open mouths to awaiting ears
We listen.

An abrupt word, flows into a cascading sentence
Virtuous ambitions, Driven by madness, entropy beckons
Craving
Lusting
Wanting speech to end, and silence to return.
We disobey.

A Vicious circle, spun full Cycle, 
Words within words, Meanings blurred through emotion
Gurgle
Splutter
Speak the language, break first bonds
We begin.
