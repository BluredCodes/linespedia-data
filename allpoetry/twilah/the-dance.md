The room has been reserved for you and me,The orchestra is playing now…our song;The open terrace.. moon..a star-lit sea,We’ve waited for this moment for so long.Softly now the music starts to play, You place your bearded cheek so close to mine;Held in your arms I now begin to sway,Your closeness to me now is so devine.We hold each other..time goes swiftly by,Not caring that the music’s long been done;I feel you kiss my cheek. I softly sigh,I suddenly awaken…you are gone.The dance is over..memories linger on,My thoughts remain long after dreams are gone.Twilah 2000
