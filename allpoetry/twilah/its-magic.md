---
title: "It's Magic"
writer: "Twilah"

When someone can make your day so grandJust by a spoken word, a gesture..or a smile,When he says the right words at just the right timeTo make what you’re going thru all worth while...It’s MagicWhen he sends you a gift, unattached by stringsJust to let you know he understands..and cares,And that gift appears unique and cleverly designedTo remind you that all is not as it sometimes appears...It’s MagicWhen someone sends a card bearing a rose of silk,Telling the story of lovers, sadly parted for a time;And you feel the mystique that is enclosed withinAs you hold this precious gift in your trembling hands...It’s MagicThe magic is true, the magician all the more real,At times when I’m least expecting it, he appears...The spell that he casts is drawing us closer indeed,His potion has the ability to dry up all my tears.His spell?  Showing me that he actually cares.His potion?  The words that he wisely utters to me...Spoken in love, they encourage and strengthen,Now, if that isn’t special magic...then what can it be?Twilah 2000
