She always wanted to sing, oh
How she wanted to sing,
For this precious gift she
Would have given anything.

But God said "No"...

She wanted to paint...to transform
Canvas into imagery and beauty,
Being an artist was her desire... she
Wondered if it was ever to be.

But God said "No"...

Then thru the following years
She became a dreamer. Somehow
Music and poetry took first place...
Just how much she would soon know.

Because God said "Yes"...

He said.. "Give of the talent and gifts
That upon you I did lovingly bestow;
These gifts have been present all along,
You had to be still and listen...to know."


And I said..."Yes"...


Twilah 2000
