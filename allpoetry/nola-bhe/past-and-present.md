I look each day to try and seethe face I know is thereIt must be hidden, deep insideBeyond my reach somewhereThose care-worn lines, when did they comeThe eyes so full of painI try to grasp with knowledge sureYouth won't be back againSo, where are you, the one that's goneHow did you fade so fastI couldn't know what went beforeIs really ME - at lastNola BheAll rights reserved
