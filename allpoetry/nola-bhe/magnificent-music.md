Hear thunder roll and lightning flashThe wind begins to moanThe rain begins to build it's forceBecomes a steady droneLike some majestic orchestraThunder, Light and WindA hidden hand directs each partAnd brings them forth againAn organ bass, A lightning fluteA harp strummed out the windThey come together, blending wellIt starts to swell withinSuch music strength is seldom heardIt's joy and pure delightWe have to listen deep insideBefore it fades in flightNola BheAll rights reserved
