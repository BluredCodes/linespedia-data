---
title: "I Wonder"
writer: "Nola Bhe"
slug: "i-wonder"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4298-I-Wonder-by-Nola-Bhe"
---

Have you ever wondered
If anyone's like you
Is there more than one out there
Who seems to wonder, too
Have you ever thought and said
Am I the only one
Who figures out the ending scene
Before the story's done
And, have you ever turned and said
Does anybody else
Hear music playing in their head
And know it's just yourself
I've looked out there and everywhere
Just once I'd like to find
A kindred thought, a knowing glance
That knows it's just like mine


Nola Bhe
