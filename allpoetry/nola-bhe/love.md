---
title: "Love?"
writer: "Nola Bhe"
slug: "love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4276-Love--by-Nola-Bhe"
---

Have you ever soared so high
and looked down from above
Your essence filled with such delight
I wonder - is this LOVE?

Has your body thrilled to hear
this music all around
It measures way past anything
like nothing else I've found

My minds confused, shifts to and fro
My thoughts refuse to see
my being reaching blindly out
to where I wish to be

I feel in-half, not really whole
unless we're joined as one
Then, peace at last, my soul at rest
Just let the rightness come

Nola Bhe
