Those Lips
Those Sweet, Sensual Lips
Beckoning Me
Smiling Seductively

That Voice
That Deep, Sexy Voice
Calling Me
Saying My Name

I Come To You
Watching Your Eyes Sparkle
Brighter And Brighter
The Closer I Get

My Heart Is Pounding
My Blood Is Boiling
My Excitement Is Unbearable
As Your Smile Broadens
And Sends Shivers Down My Spine

You Speak
Voice Deep With Urgency




"Baby..........Get me another beer, would ya?"
