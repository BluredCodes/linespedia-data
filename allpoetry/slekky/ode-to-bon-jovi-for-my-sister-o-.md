His eyes are blue,
his hair is brownish blonde
He is my idol, 
his name is Jon
His smile is so perfect 
with his dimpled chin
He wears faded jeans
and leopard skin
He has four friends
and together they play
number one songs
that will blow you away
Terrific musicians 
they truly are
Jon sings lead vocals, 
Ritchie plays guitar
There's David on Keyboards
and Alec on Bass
Finally there's Tico
He's the Drum Ace
They're on the top and it's not over yet
They'll surely make history with Slippery When Wet!Author Unknown
