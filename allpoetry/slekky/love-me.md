---
title: "Love Me"
writer: "Slekky"

Let me lift you up above the clouds
as we listen to the winds 
whispering songs so beautiful
only your heart can hear the melody

Let us watch the stars
as they glide to the music
performing a dance so alluring 
even the strongest cannot resist them

Let me hold your hand
as we soar through the sky
and the silk of the night surrounds us
leaving nothing untouched
by its liquid caresses

Trust in me and lend me your heart
as I offer my own to you
Hold me, so I may hold you
Love me, so I may love youMarch 2001,
