---
title: "Give me Strength"
writer: "Slekky"

Frustration crawls up through 
my spine
As I would rather throw away this life
of mine
Though things do sometimes go 
my way
It's hard to take things day
by day
When life's ambitions are
trampled down
And I no longer hold
the crown
I shall stay alive and keep
my pride
While I open so many eyes
so wide
I hope I gain the strength to 
go on
I need the courage, for I am 
only oneThis is another Old one
