Dripping golden kisses
upon your broken soul
I weave my strengths
into your shattered heart
with each warm breath that embraces you

I speak of indigo promises
unbroken
untainted
as I whisper my devotion
in agonizing submission
and surrender my love to you
shedding my protective blanket
and offer it's shelter to you
as I allow you to turn these sapphire eyes
into pools of frozen tears

warm upon my cheeks 
as it chills my heart
and turns these crystal emotions
into a cloudy, grey hatred
as each icy word you speak
sends daggars racing toward my heart
and you steal another piece of it
holding it ransom until another day

When you see you cannot tear my hardened heart
and you cannot feel the heat of my desire
you will hand that piece back to me
nicely wrapped with a silver bow
as you melt the ice
and invite golden kisses upon your soul
with a piece of my own heartThe assignment was to write what my strengths and weaknesses are in a relationship.  Can you decipher my strengths/weaknesses from this?
