Silken raindrops caress my skin
Tracing my body
Drenching me in stormy lust
As I close my eyes and imagine you are here

Vos levres sure le mien
Notre entwined de corps

Your lips on mine
Our bodies entwined

Deux ames qui dansent
A une melodie de l’amant divin

Two souls dancing
To a melody of lovers divineI've seen some people do this type of English/Another language poem and I thought I might try one for myself.
