Happy Happy Birthday
to a sis that's just so cool
take it from deathgoddess
she says "Saggitarians Rule!"

The Energizer Bunny
must be Kym's own mascot
'cause she's often on the go
and she jumps around alot

She's always truely happy
to lend a helping hand
Maybe I should ask her
to help me find a man

So Happy Happy Birthday
to a sis that I adore
she's such a beautiful person
sweet down to the core12/06/2001   Happy 30th Kym, I love you  :o)
