title: "Unspoken Desire (A Challenge Submission)"

The scent of you makes me weak with desire
  the desire to claim you, to consume you
I want to be one with you
  to know you as no other has
I feel connected to you
as if I was made for you
  this hair you love to touch
  these eyes you say are so mysterious
    they're for you
You're everything I want in a man
  Beautiful inside and out
when I'm near you, I come alive, body and soul
  as if without you I am not whole
Your words intrigue me
Your eyes seduce me
Your voice melts me
You amaze me
In your presence, I am in awe
I cannot contain my myself
My heart wants to leap from my chest into your lap
and my body trembles for release
  For release of these words I cannot say
  For release of these feelings I cannot express
  For release of the desire I have locked within me
Please release me
  Free me from these shackles I have inflicted upon myself
Take my heart from your lap and hold it in your hands
  massage it with your love and adoring words
Slay this dragon of uncertainty that breaths its fire on me
  threatening to kill all hope for love
Rescue me from my own fear
Allow me to be completely yours
and become forever
  My Man
  My Lover
  My HeroThis was written about a crush I have but I've never expressed it to him, hence the title "Unspoken Desire"
