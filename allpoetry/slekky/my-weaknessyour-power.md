You eat away my heart
Piece by piece
savoring each taste of victory
with each tear that falls from my face

The salty smell of my burning soul
reaches your nostrils
and you breathe in my pain
filling your lungs with my sorrow
until my weakness becomes your power

You stomp on my dignity 
and bruise my pride
until I begin to believe your words
and I wish for just one glimpse
of something good I have to offer

I look in the miror
with tear swollen eyes 
and I wonder
"Why am I still alive?"This isn't great, but I just can't write well when I'm sad or angry......feel free to tear this apart, for I'm just a pawn.
