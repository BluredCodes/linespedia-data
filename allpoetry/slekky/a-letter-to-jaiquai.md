Dearest Jaiquai,

You are everything I live for.
You make me smile when I feel like crying.
The joy you give me is  so full of bursting love
that it explodes from my heart 
and I can barely contain it!

You bring a sense of pride to my life
and before you, there was none.
You, my son, are the center of every thought
which enters my head.
You, my son, are the final factor
before my decision is made.
You, my son, are the glue that keeps me from falling apart.

You are my EVERYTHING!


Love,
MommyFor my precious little Quai Quai.

I wrote this when he was about one year old (he is two now), when the other two were missing for a time, so he was my salvation that saved me from total despair! NO, he did not take their place, he merely helped me to feel a little happiness when their absence was becoming too much for me to handle, he helped me to be strong. Now the other two are back and they too dwell in the center of my heart.....so this kind of fits for all three of them!

Thanks
