I'm soaring through the sky 
carried on the wings of the wind 
plummeting to the earth 
to my destination 

I come to rest at this tree 
leaving behind my moist kisses 
as I allow myself to fall 
to the next woody arm that catches me 

Down Down Down I fall 
enjoying the peaceful ride 
giving up a piece of myself 
to each thirsty branch 

Finally my ride is over 
as I join the others in the earth 
where we all will be as one 
until we begin our journey again
