I wish I could sing to you and make everything betterI wish I could dress your wounds and say things will be ok tomorrowBut I can'tSo I will tell you this...You are a wonderful friendAn exceptional poetYou feel and write so deeplyYou inspire me to reach further into myselfYou bring a smile to many faces and many heartsYou are a true friend indeedAnd let me say this...I am here for you
