Sleep conquers me 
and my dreams hold me prisoner 
as they immerse me 
into a dismal world 
where my deepest fears are conceived 
and laid before me 
in waves of torment 

In my subconsious mind 
I live a life of anguish 
that seems as real 
as my waking hours 

I watch you in the field 
playing and giggling 
your energy and strength 
astounding me 
but your laughter turns to screams 
and you cry tears of blood 
until the crimson liquid 
drips from your skin 
and stains my fingers 

but nothing I do can stop your demise 
as my helpless hands tremble 
and my cries for help go unheard 

I watch your once hopeful eyes 
glaze over with your impending death 
and as they look into mine 
pleading for solace 
I try to sing you into your eternal slumber 

but my voice abandons me 
and I realize I cannot offer comfort 
in your last moment of life 

As your body stops fighting 
and your heart stops beating 
flaming tears of fire 
fill my stinging eyes 
and my spirit is soiled with guilt 
like the scarlet stains upon my hands 

................................ 

If ever you were torn from my arms 
my soul would bleed endlessly 
and crystal tears would freeze upon my cheeks 
to forever remain embedded on my mournful faceI didn't win this one  :o(
