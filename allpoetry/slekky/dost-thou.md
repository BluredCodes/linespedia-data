Dost thou leaveth me alone and naked
in the midst of this bitter storm?
Or dost thou wish to cover me
and slay this lover's scorn?

Your seed that moves within my womb
has grown with my devotion, 
so why dost thou not feel the love
or succumb to thy emotion?

A noble man, a knight of honor,
thy secrets are hidden well.
Must I carry the burden, a bastard child,
as my belly begins to swell?

Is thine ownership and privilege
more significant than I?
Even as I contemplate,
thy departure time is nigh.

I live by night on these stolen moments,
as I yearn for something more
and even as thy lips touch mine,
I know I am merely your whore.1.  Is "dost" a word and if so, is that the correct spelling?
