Sadly I crave your return 
As I reminisce about days past 
heart wrenching memories 
consume my thoughts 
and infiltrate my dreams 

I spend infinite hours 
playing all the memories in my mind 
I picture your smile 
as perfect as a sunset 
lighting up your face 
brightening up my life 

I hear your laughter 
ringing in my ears 
like the sweet songs 
of the mocking bird 

My hands are empty 
without your tiny fingers entwined in mine 
My shoulder aches 
for your little head to lie upon it 
My arms burn 
to embrace your small frame 
My soul is incomplete 
without you, my sweet child, to bring me joy 

My sun has faded 
leaving endless black clouds in my wake 
and I'm left to face this world alone 
and walk this earth without purpose 

I must let you go 
and release your soul to Heaven 
as my heart breaks into a million pieces 
I become......
