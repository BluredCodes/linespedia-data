When I saw him with her
my heart burst in flames
They were standing so close
as if they were more than friends

Then he saw me standing there
his eyes grew open wide
and he told me there was nothing between them
and that his heart was mine

But all I really had to do
was look into his eyes 
to know instead of adoring me
he was turning his love into lies

He was loving her and loving me
but his love just wasn't true
and I wouldn't put it past him
