Look through her crystal blue eyes
and find beauty in all you see
Feast upon the liquid colors
dripping from human souls
like the scene of a sunset
behind droplets of rain

She will peer beyond your mask
to find the hidden truths
and buried lies
She will reach into your heart
to melt the ice
and heal the festered wounds

Romantic at heart
she hunts with fierce determination
until she uncovers a true spirit
and forgotten innocence is unveiled

She has unclothed herself
laid emotions bare for all to see
offering pieces of her essence
to those valiant enough to explore

Within her being
she allows heartache to enter
For she knows without sorrow
EXHILERATION in PASSION
Is simply
