Challenge Description: I would like to see you write a poem creating your own language. Something original.. write it in the language you create and ..then interpret so we can understand :) Thanks and good luck. (100 points)



Coi za lonas ina pl’ai ji met 
Sien mel bonto un fr’ai ji corosso 
Ina gila 

Imag’ie mel poito spie 
Skel’a ta couti ji za f’ima 
Alli ta chera c’ori mono za spina 
Ina taumo 

Ombri ta yie mel l’ique sienis 
Savoi’a zoi 
Sapol’a ta plivo ji za catie 
Hiya pi mel oganis 
San zoi imag’ie za’shi 
Depan’a mel f’ima 
Ina chiva 

Alli ta elosa c’ori illomo zoi 
Ava isi isoe lon’ada minno pi peservi 
Apina za lonas ina f’anta met 
Lo donosae eni ta stassi 
Ai remere t’abi eni mel donie 
Ina s’ai ji zoi 

_____________________________________________________________________ 

Close your eyes and think of me 
Feel my breath on the back of your neck 
And shiver 

Imagine my finger tips 
Tracing the contours of your body 
Let the chills run down your spine 
And sigh 

Remember the way my tongue feels 
Tasting you 
Savoring the flavor of your skin 
Listen to my moans 
As you imagine yourself 
Ravishing my body 
And tremble 

Let the shocks run through you 
Until it is almost too much to endure 
Open your eyes and picture me 
For somewhere in the night 
I sleep alone in my bed 
And dream of youI didn't win, but I tried really hard and I'm proud of the hard work I put into this and I think my language is purty  :o)
