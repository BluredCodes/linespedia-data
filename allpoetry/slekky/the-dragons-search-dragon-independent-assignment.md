Deep in Dragon Kingdom
peace was known throughout
until one day their leader
the Myst Dragon was not about

The Dragons searched everywhere
but to their great dismay
they found that he was missing
he had gone away

They shouted to their ruler
to come out from the myst
but when he didn't show his face
the Dragons became pissed

Now chaos was iminent
because boredom did prevail
and all the Dragons searched and searched
but all to no avail

Now when the Myst Dragon does return
He'll be greeted with open arms
for all the Dragons love him
we just can't resist his charmsOk, Nauti, where are you?
