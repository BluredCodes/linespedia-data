I look deep into your eyes 
as your hands caress my flesh
I feel the warmth of your breath
as your tongue teases my thigh

As you move your body closer
I am overcome with passion
while my hunger for you grows
into a wild, heated desire

You kiss the nape of my neck
and caress the fullness of my breasts
while I cling to your every whisper
and explore every inch of your body

A low moan escapes from your lips
and your body trembles with mine
as your tongue teases my senses
and you turn my whines into cries

I strive to taste you
so sweet with growing lust
while you run your fingers through my hair
and your moans get more urgent with desire

Finally our bodies are one
entertwined in a lovers' dance
moving slowly but steadily
to the beating of our heartsI was fifteen when I wrote this and not very experienced (at all) so please be gentle.
