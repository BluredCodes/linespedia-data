Once I had a pet turtle 
who thought he was a dog 
each day he chewed a milkbone 
before his morning jog 

I loved that turtle dearly 
he was my favorite pet 
but one day things went wrong 
his death, I'll never forget 

The day began as usual 
that blistry winter morn 
I couldn't help but notice 
he was looking quite forlorne 

Maybe if I hadn't fed him 
those fish scales instead of a bone 
he would be a happy turtle-dog 
If only I had known! 

He walked through the house aimlessly 
ignoring my every plea 
I begged for his forgiveness 
but he wouldn't listen to me 

Then that's when it happened 
the suicide from HELL 
he walked right in the mousetrap 
as it clamped down, I heard him yell 

He started to eat the turnips 
which the mousetrap gingerly held 
I knew he was allergic 
but my attempts to save him failed 

He began to choke and sputter 
until his final breath he did take 
with tears streaming freely 
A grave, I did carefully make 

Now as I sit by his tomb 
I cry out my little heart 
and I place a flower atop his grave 
in memory of my turtle, BartYay!!!!!  I got runner-up!
