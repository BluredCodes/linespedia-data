You came into this world 
Emerging from my womb 
Leaving empty my body 
But filling my heart 

A strong little boy 
Ten fingers, ten toes 
With that first wail 
My heart expanded with love 
And my arms ached to hold you 

I was wallowing in happiness 
Drowning in pride 
A sense of purpose and meaning 
Penetrating my very being 

You changed me 
Awakened a strength from deep within 
That I didn't realize I possessed 
And you brought forth such extraordinary love 
I never dreamed I could feel 

A change in me was apparent 
To all those who knew me 

Pats on the back became full embraces 
Smiles are now constant 
When before they were forced upon my face 
And emotions are now overwhelming 
When in the past, they were kept locked within 

The magic of your being 
Has brought endless joy to my life 
My little man, my baby boy 
You are my Saving GraceYay!!!!!  I got runner-up!
