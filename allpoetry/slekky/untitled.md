The Blackness has overcome me
taken over my senses
I see nothing
I hear nothing
I smell nothing
Therefore, I feel nothing
This is where I want to be
surrounded by this sweet nothingness
this precious escape from reality
I learned about this place long ago
it hasn't failed me yet
My place
Darkness and Peace
I succumb to it
completely submerging myself in its depths
I surrender to it wholly
letting it surround me and embrace me
letting it carry me beyond emotions
where your words can no longer devastate meIt seems like the ending needs some help.
