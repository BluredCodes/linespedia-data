title: "Thall Shalt Not Judge"
writer: "Slekky"

Who Are you to condemn me?
These choices that I make are mine
How is it your business what mistakes I make?
They make me stronger and wiser
You are weak
unlearned of the ways of this world
too blind to see the good in me
Your perfection is flawed
For indeed you pray daily
you attend your church services three times a week
you pay your tithes faithfully
A perfect Christian, right?
Wrong!
Are you a model after Jesus Christ?
No!
For He would never look at me the way you do
He would not be found huddled in corners
gossiping about this person or that
He would not act as if he was too good to be seen with me
So who are you to judge me?This is for all those self righteous people out there that think they can do no wrong.  I realize there are some really great God fearing poeple who are not this way at all, so please don't interpret this as an attack on Christianity or any other religion, it's about those certain people who act this way, not the religion itself.
