Two pink lines... 
a positive indication 
a baby was on the way 

As the smile spread on my face 
and my heartbeat quickened 
tears of affection moistened my eyes 

I thought I would cry 
But out of fear, not happiness 
and here I was 
Beaming 
Glowing 
Elation emmenating from the top of my head 
to the tips of my toes 

God had sent me my very own Angel 
A baby for me to love and cherish 

Emotions burst forth as never before 
I could feel you loving me from within 
completely depending on me, Your Mommy 

Mommy... 
The most beloved title a woman can behold 
one I would wear more proudly than any other 
a label to give my name genuine beauty 

Your Daddy was full of pride when he heard 
With smile wide and eyes gleaming 
he hugged me 
and laughed for the first time in days 
with compassion in his voice 
he spoke to you through Mommy's belly 
and I felt the love 
burning inside me 
spreading through my body like an infection 

And I knew this was true love 
the love between Mother and Child 
a bond as solid as steel 
refusing to bend 

My Angel had finally arrived 
and through him 
I am Endlessly EnchantedChallenge was to write about my feelings when I found out I was pregnant and to incorporate others' reactions.
