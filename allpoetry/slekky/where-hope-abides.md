Where Hope Abides........


Deep within the enamored forest 
The last unicorn resides
She walks beneath the evergreens
Reflecting the sun’s rays
And the moon’s beams
In her fragile glass form

Adorned with sparkling glitter
Silver and gold shimmer over her translucent figure
As light echoes through her body
And shines vividly upon her

Her thick mane lies easily in soft tresses
Lush shades of purple and gold
with silver streaks throughout her mane and tail
add brilliance to her already magnificent physique
as she walk with proud assurance
among the verdant trees

The alicorn is her crown
Sparkling silver and speckled glass
Twisted gracefully on her forehead

The emblem of hope
For all those who touch it
Are blessed throughout eternity
With infinite happiness and unbounded health

And all those who feast their eyes upon her
Can grasp a part of her soul
As her supreme magnificence is burnt behind the eyes
And her genuine purity unlocks closed minds
 
Initiating passionate emotions to rupture from the heart
And explode in delightful bliss
Until the eyes see only beauty 
And the heart feels only love!Inspired by mystysaint's challenge about unicorns, although I did not finish in time to submit  hehe.
