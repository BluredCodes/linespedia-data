J is for the joy you bring to my life

A is for always, I'll always be there

I is for inquisitive, 'cause you have to know

Q is for quick, 'cause you're on the go

U is for unique, 'cause there's only one

A is for amazing, my amazing son                 

I is for I love you!This is a little lullabye I sing to my son, Jaiquai (Ju-quay).  I know it's not a great poetical piece, but I really felt compelled to add this piece today,   I am thinking alot about him while I'm here at work and wishing I was at home with him.

I plan to write a better poem for him, but I can never do the love I feel for him justice, I can never express in words the pure and unconditional love I feel for him.

Look for my tribute to Jaiquai to come later.

Thanks for reading!
