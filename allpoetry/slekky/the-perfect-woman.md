You left me stranded
drowning in my sorrow
the pain cutting into my heart
the lonliness unbearable
I close my eyes
and I feel the hot tears
running down my cheeks
I wish I was the woman you wanted
I tried so hard to be that
but no matter how hard I tried
you found some new imperfection
a new fault in me
and now you're with her
coming to me late nights
telling me all of HER imperfections
and wanting me back
Do you think I've forgotten?
Did you think I would welcome you back into my life?
You're still searching for that perfect woman,
Well,I'm not her and I never will be.
Your search will be long and impossible
but don't come back to me 
when you realize how good I am for you
because by then,
I will have found a man who sees the good in me,
and who loves me for who I am!This sucks, but people have been asking me to put on more, so here you go.  Sorry it's not better  LOL
