It's Saturday morning 
I jump out of bed 
blanket in tow 
To the kitchen I head 
Fruity Pebbles 
in a bowl piled high 
I head to the living room 
And by the TV I sigh 

Ahhhhhh 

It's time for my show 
with the little blue men 
of magic and witchcraft 
and Gargamel's grin 
And Azrael 
that evil cat 
will do Gargy's deed 
and surely go splat! 

And then there's Smurfette 
with clothes galore 
Too bad only one style 
is sold at the Smurf Store 
With her pick of the men 
She shall be the Smurf Queen 
It's enough envy to make 
the other smurfs green 

Don't forget Papa Smurf 
Red pants and white beard 
He's the Village Leader 
and by all he's revered 
A chemist at heart 
he whips up the right potion 
to save the young smurfs 
from harm and commotion 

There's Jokey and Greedy and Hefty Smurf too 
and I won't leave out Poet Smurf 
with his lyrical line or two 
There's Lazy and Brainy and Vanity Smurf 
Did I mention my favorite 
His name is Cook Smurf 
He'll bake you a cake and ask you to try it 
Once you've taken a taste 
you'll be ready to buy it 

Now my show is over 
and I'm bored once again 
but tune in next Saturday 
for more magic and sin!Yay!  I got runner up!
