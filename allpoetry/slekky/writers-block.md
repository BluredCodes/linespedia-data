title: "Writer's Block"
writer: "Slekky"

When you sit down to write
what could be such a fright
than to draw a blank in your mind

You try as hard as you can
but all your thoughts seem so bland
and the ideas never come to mind

So you sit down to write
to face such a fright
of nothing coming to mind



I can't think of much else to say
it seems I've drawn a blank
if you want to try me, you just may
but believe me, I can't think (thank)
