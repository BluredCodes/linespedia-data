---
title: "Sweet Revenge"
writer: "Slekky"
slug: "sweet-revenge"
source: "AllPoetry"

Her eyes gave way to pain unknown.
Her lips, though smiling, curved downward
as though the weight of her woes would not allow her to laugh.

She danced her provocative dance 
and swayed to the music with eyes descended,
never making eye contact, 
seeing only black and flashes of red

Her body moved luciously and instinctively,
though her mind was in another place, another time
Holding her baby to her bosom....
before they enslaved her and killed him

They murdered her one love, her only joy!
She no longer saw black, just bright red
while she plotted her sweet revenge 
against those who took her precious boy.

They would feel her sorrow as she avenged her son!
She would watch their pain and smile
as she made justice a reality.May 2001,
