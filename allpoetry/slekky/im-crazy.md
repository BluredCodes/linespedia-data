The Spirits fly around my bed
calling out my name
images going through my head
telling me I'm insane

The cries are silent 
to my ears
and much too violent 
to remember the fears

When the spirits arrive
they'll take away 
my last moment alive
'cause they won't let me stayI wasn't sure whether I should put this in "humor" because I find it quite amusing, but others may not, so I chose "other".

I wrote this when I was a Freshman when my Mom thought I needed a counselor (because of my dark poetry at that time)and I gave it to her as a joke, she laughed and I think it made her feel better.

Enjoy and I won't get mad if this only gets one or two stars, just sharing my older stuff  :o)
