I have ended the morose agony
that my mortal body endured
only to awaken new torture
deep within my soul
a pain I cannot reverse

I have cut deep into the hearts
of those I love the most
bestowing upon them eternal scars
from now to infinity......

I wallow in remorseThe challenge was to write about your suicide, explaining how you felt when watching your loved ones grieve for you as you float above your lifeless body -- in 50 words or less.  I had a hard time keeping this so short, but I am proud of myself for accomplishing this....I've never been very good at short poems.
