music vibrates...
              pulsates...
            	   trembles...
sounds become a blur


lights sparkle...
              flicker...
                   glimmer...
shadows become reflections of color


completely encircled...
		intertwined...
			tangled...
in a cloud of twirling bodies

standing in the middle
watching the movement
one individual
part of the whole
surrounded

Alone.
