I am a new shoe
That new shoe on the shelf
Please pick me up

Try me on for size
Walk around for a bit
Feel how comfortable I am

Think about me
Cost does not matter
I am reasonable

Do not be afraid
Take the plunge
Look to the future

Take me places
Show me off
Love me

Wear me out
Till my colors fade
And my laces gray

As others come along
I will always be the one
Because nobody can ever 
Fill this shoe
