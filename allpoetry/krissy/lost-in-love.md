---
title: "lost in love"
writer: "krissy"

I satnd before you, silenced you seem.
tears running but visibly seen.
my heart aches as confusion sets in.
I love you, but is it love i'm in?

Doing anything in my power to let you i know i care,
But to this day i did not share.
The pain it causes as i cry myself to slumber,
If it was felt in return, i would always wonder!

Unable to share my hearts tears,
as i went to speak, stopped on;y by fear.
The fear of losing the one i adore,
so silenced i will be once more.
