---
title: "friends are angels"
writer: "krissy"

source: "AllPoetry"

An angel will guide you like a shining star,
An angel will care and watch you go far.
An angel won't criticize or tell you whats right,
An angel won't change a day into night.

We all have an angel who has so much to give,
They shower us with love and teach us to live.

Friends are like angels,
Who brighten our days.
They are a gift from above,
In so many ways.

Friends are angels without wings.
