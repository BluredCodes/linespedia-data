The waves deep secrets rumble against the shore,
blurred visions of light where sky and water meet.
The rain drops fall upon your tender lips,
you whisper words softly, words so sweet.

i feel your touch upon me as night sky's fall dark,
your arms wrapped around me, a tear falls from my eye.
your gaze in my direction, a sight so cherished and true,
i look upon with amazement and escapes a sigh for you.

as known as dawn to day,
 you mean the world to me.
needed so much to say,
 i hope you to see.
