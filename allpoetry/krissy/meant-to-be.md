title: "meant to be"

Till dawn brake, within your arms i lay,
While night falls, in my thoughts you stay.
dew drops fall, as look at thee,
Till midnight ticks, dreams i see.

There in the distance, close you stand,
Searching daze, i hold out my hand.
Feeling the vibes that magically felt,
words spoken, my heart it melt.

soft glances, rough as secret sea,
Star bright, is it meant to be?
Your hand in mine, i do agree,
forever we shall be.
