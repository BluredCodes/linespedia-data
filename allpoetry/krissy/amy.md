It has been hard
but you are still here
living you life
making it the best it can be
Amy i am proud of you
you have had enough probelems
this last year to last a life time
but you have put them behind you
and moved on
you have made something of you life
and for that i am pleased,
proud and feel special to know some-one like you

we have been through alot
but i think we will put that behind us
cause our freindship is mendable
and we have been throguh alot
just to forget it all

Amy, you are special to me
and to others
i am glad you were put on this earth
and i am glad i met you
Amy i am so proud of you 
and i will always love you!
