In Our Thoughts
Copyright 2001 Doug Pretorius

A myriad thoughts twist through the crystal prism of a dream
Distorted and refined by the light of a creative mind
Whole worlds, even universes are created, destroyed and reborn
Just to search through the intricacies of a single whim
Traveling to and fro, tracing the patterns of the heavens
Delving down into the earth's core
and awaking into the souls of its children
Life, death, even true understanding are imagined
Though all is hardly more than a reflection of reality
By the mirror effect nearly to a head no one will recognize it
Until the whisper rises to a trumiph song
and the beauty and majesty is made apparent to all
