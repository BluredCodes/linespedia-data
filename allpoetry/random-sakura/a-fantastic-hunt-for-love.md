---
title: "A Fantastic Hunt For Love"
writer: "Random Sakura"

A little shake of lemon zest
Throw a bit of my Sunday best
When I feel like a sudden chase
I dry my eyes and get the paste
Throwing it across the room
Surely to make my lover swoon
I am on a fantastic hunt for love

Catch him, hug him, never let him go
As soon as he appears a rock I throw
knock him upside his cute little head
I lay him ever so gently onto my bed
If he tries to escape my loving grasp
I'll make sure my dog bites his...
I am on a fantastic hunt for love

The day arrives my plan is set
A net and some luck is all I get
Which place he hides I care not 
I'll catch him blind, on the spot
Never will a bright sunny day go by
When I'm not looking for that special guy
I am on a fantastic hunt for love
