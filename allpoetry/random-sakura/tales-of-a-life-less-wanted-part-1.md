My eyes look upon a book
Tales of a life less wanted
It speaks to me from above
It shouts to me from below
I pick it up, emotion powerful

Standing in the rain it calls
Write in me, be with me, love me
I sit in the falling water crying
its lust for my thoughts grow

I wrote of many things that night
of wild romance and my hearts delight
but most of all a life less wanted
a life of hurt, of pain, and fear

Taking up MY book I run to the corner
To write about my latest fear in detail
Of my hopes and dreams along with that
To write in this book is to truely live
