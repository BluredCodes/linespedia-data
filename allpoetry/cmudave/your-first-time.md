Hey Baby Come Next To Me
I'll Coax You Good
And Act Like I Should
My Love Will Set You Free

Will You, Lay Down?
Can I, Kiss You?
Shall We, Do This?
Could It, Break Us?

My Lips Don't Know Your Morals
Trust Me, This Is Fine
Bring Your Mouth, Near Mine
Lets End These Silly Quarals
