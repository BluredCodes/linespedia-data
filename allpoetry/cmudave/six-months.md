Six Months! Seven Too Short
To Know Her
Who thought we'd last?

Falling, A Bit Fast
But It's Worked
Who'd Bet Those Odds?

We Touched...Not Enough.
But It's moot.
Not That You Know That

Two Weeks! Too Long
To Be Sore
Who'd Have Thought We'd Fail?
