title: "In The Dark, My Parents Are Gone"

I thought about how the glow of the TV caught your face.
your eyes, your nose, your lips, and how they all went together

I thought about us, and how the beats of our hearts kept pace
your mind, your soul, your care,  I long for them, on forever

I think about you, your independence, womanly grace
empowered, intelligent, and congeal, sweetly clever.
