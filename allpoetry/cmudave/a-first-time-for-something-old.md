My Love, Will It Happen Tonight? 
Sweet One, Will This Turn Into A Fight? 
If You Give In, Will It Set Me Free? 

Your Hair, Brushed Nicely 
My Ears, They're Bloody 
Your Laugh, Enchants Me, 
My Lips, They Quiver 
Your Eyes, Glassed Over 
My Heart, It's Pounding 

Dear God, Thanks For Sending Her Here 
Maybe You Can Take Away The Fear 
Or Maybe It Is Up To Me..
