Hey babe, you might think my school is lame
I can’t say I don’t think the same

I’m not so Johnny Harvard
I’m not so Georgey-Yale

But you know, I’m no retard
And those guys are mighty stale.

Yeah, I’m outa place here
And so are you

But kick back, and shed your fear
And enjoy the farm-town view.

I’m not so Johnny Harvard
I’m not so Georgey-Yale

But you know, I’m no retard
And those guys are mighty stale.

I’ll make it, just wait and see
I might take that sacred oath

Who knows what time will make me?
Where will it take you and I both?

I’m not so Johnny Harvard
I’m not so Georgey-Yale

But you know, I’m no retard
And those guys are mighty stale.

Yeah, Those guys are mighty stale. 
Those guys are mighty stale.
