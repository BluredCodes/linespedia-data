Listen Girl, I need my rest
You know me, need too much sleep
I'll tell you, you are the best
I can't come, no need to weep

Saturday and each night since
I wake up next to you, tird
Sore and cramped, then brush and rince
If I worked, I'd be fired

Hurring, need to get there
Throw on some clothes, check my smell
I like you, dont worry `bout that
It's just that, you know, classes

It is good, just not with you
Not just yet, maybe sometime
You're still great, I'm a pink hue
Yes, it's a lie, but not a crime
