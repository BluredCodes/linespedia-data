Tell me, how did you get so sweet?
You're so good for me
Fallen? Guilty is my plea.
For you! My souls indulgent treat.

Why did it go this way for us?
Should have done without.
Trust? No, there is doubt..
I'll do you good, and catch my bus

I beg you, don't let me down now
Like I did you then
The fall, way back when
Won't you give more than I'd allow?

Shall I hurt you or maybe haunt?
To stay, to do twice
Wonder if I'll suffice...
You and I, I'll always flaunt

No, I will stay, and do my part
For both me and you
and God, over the pew.
So again, you will call me dear-heartShe Rocks
