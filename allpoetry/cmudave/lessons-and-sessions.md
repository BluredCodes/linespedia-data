Sweets, Do You Wonder Why
I Give, So Much?
Here's A, Lesson
About, Your Touch
Here's A, Session
On Your, Sweet Breath
Can I?...Before Death?
Your Rules, Made To Defy
Based On, Strange Things
You Don't, Want More?
The Stuff, Love Brings?
All Those, New Realms?
I'm At, The Helms
Can't We, Explore?
Just Once, Before I Die?
