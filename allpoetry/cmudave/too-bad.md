writer: "CMUDave"

Oh, The Jealousy
They’d Have
If They Knew

My, The Anger
That’d  Resound
Had They Heard

Lord, The Joy
She brings
This, You Know

Ha, It’s Sad
To Think..
Deprived Of Me

Well, Sorry
I’m Gone
And She’s Here
