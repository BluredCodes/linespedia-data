I Showed My Love, You Subpenioad My Soul
I Disclosed All
Perhaps It's To Your Dismay

I Have Found Life's Only Certain Goal
Now, Will I Fall?
Chasing, As You Run Away?

Do You Still Want Me? You're Treasured Troll?
Will You Still Call?
Knowing What I Said That Day?
