Was I Wrong Dear, To Tell You So Much
Love Is Here, But Is It There?
I Gave Lots, But Will You Share
Should I Not Have Told You Such

Maybe I Should Have Kept My Mouth Shut
Should I Dumb It Down Next Time?
Do You Fear We've Reached Our Prime
My Dear, Did I Put Us In A Rut

I Was Scared You Didn't Feel The Same
Unrequiteds' All I Know
The Only Care That I Show
Turns Out, I Was Right, It Seems I'm Lame
