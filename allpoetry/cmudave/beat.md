Hey sweet one, I Know You're Young Yet,
You Haven't Been Hazed, You're Feet Aren't Wet.

If My Heart Beats To You
Will It Stop?
When You Go Away Too?

Will I Keel Over When The Door Slams?
Will I Want Less When My God, Damns?
