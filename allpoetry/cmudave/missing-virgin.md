She is a virgin, 

Chased, untouched and pure,
When it's over there is no cure, 

A gift to any man, 

A present any man would redeem, 
He loves her, so it would seem, 

Nice guys finish last,  
He finished first, 

Now she knows better, 

Too bad she can't change the past, 
Of all of those I have known, he is the worst, 

A frightened feeling she can not shake,
He turned out to be such a fake,

No one could be more undeserving, 
Not everyone knows, just those observing, 

Now, both on the spotlight, in the eyes of their peers, 

Now he's a stud, 
Now she's a slut, 

I bet she wishes she had kept her legs shut,I know this lacks form, it was one of the first creative writings I've done, way back in 1999. I put it up because people read my other poems and get the idea I am cold and insensitive. Well, here you go.
