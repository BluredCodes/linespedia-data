Would You Notice Me?
If I Walked By,
Before We Met?

Would I Get Your Number?
Or Give You Mine,
Before We Met?

Where Would Your Eyes Go Then?
Might they? My way,
Before We Met?

Would My Charm Work The Same?
Anywhere Else,
Before We Met?

Would I Be Just As Smart,
Despite My Smile,
Before We Met?

Would I Have Grown Tird,
Of Resistance,
Before We Met?

Would You Be So Impressed,
By My Big Heart,
Before We Met?

Would I Even Be Here,
Six Months To The Day,
Before We Met?
