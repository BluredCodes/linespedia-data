At first you were elated
Now  it seems I am hated

It was my ego that was fed
The cerebral connection is dead

He was a fool, your guys' a miraculous goon
I'm so cool, I'm a shoe in  for the passionate poon

You wouldn't do to him as he did to you
Cheating yourself to serve that fuck makes me wana spew

Your eyes suggested loveless trust
Your belly inspired guiltless lust

Your passion and desire for me were strong
Yet  you kissing and cuddling me is wrong

Things between us would have been euphoric
To speculate now is simply rhetoric

Chaos in your heart, you barely kept it inside
Instead you ran and hide

When they made me, they broke the mold
You wont know it till your bitter and oldSummer 2000, This was before I had form
