title: "Sore To The Core"
writer: "CMUDave"

You Know, For You, I Am Strong
But You Don't Know All
True, I Catch Your Fall
If You Think That's It, You're Wrong

Indeed, For You Sweets, I'm Sore
Yes, I Take The Pain
No, I'm Not So Sane
I'm Fine Aching To The Core

So Long As You Stay, My Love
I Will Jump, To Break
Your Fall, It's Not Fake
I'm Here, To Take Any Shove
