I’m feeling crappy
	-and I don’t know why
	-don’t know why
	-don’t know why
Cause I feel sooo bad

I wanna curl up and die
	-curl up and die
	-curl up and die
Cause I feel sooo bad

I don’t want pity no more
	-pity no more
	-pity no more
Cause I feel sooo badThink PMS.
