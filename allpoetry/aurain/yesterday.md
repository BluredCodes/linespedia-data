---
title: "Yesterday"
writer: "Aurain"
slug: "yesterday"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The sun has lost its luster
And I am sitting here in much fluster

My writer’s block is gone
And suddenly I’m writing a song

I am frozen cold
I sold my soul

My hands are shaking
And I am leaking

I took a leap of faith
I ended up a month late

I trusted you
I lose

My legs are cramping
And my chair is damp

This stain you left behind
On the floor, on my mind

My toes are dirty
And you said I was pretty

My words are forced
Yes coerced

Fool I’ve been made
In blood I’ve paid

The sky has darkened
The car is parked

I walked alone
Never returned home

The stories told
Spent in its weight of gold

Tears shed
After the pie was digested

Nervous wreck
Threw it all in heck

I began to hate
You never knew cake

Sweet and simple
You're a squeezed pimple
