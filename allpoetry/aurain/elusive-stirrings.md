---
title: "Elusive Stirrings"
writer: "Aurain"
slug: "elusive-stirrings"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30085-Elusive-Stirrings-by-Aurain"

Simple pleasures extended
Contusions contained in my mind
In my pain and hunger
And unsearchable desire
Yearning for a passing indication
Futile glimpse of a flighty soul
Stretching out on a whisper
Reaching out only to touch absence
Moving within my every whim
Momentary reminders surrounding
Tranquil objects sing its songs
Senses brought forth culminate
Barriers unseen lean forward
Effected choices made clear
Spoiled fermentations of liquid
Poured out upon the laughing ground
Extracting truth, puns surmound.
