I am sitting here, watching the world go.
Under a shady tree, a sweet breeze blows.
Passing people are moving to and fro.
Meanwhile, the crackle of a chicken crows.


A peaceful sight is presented to me.
Sweet smells of morning, a feast for my nose!
Can the day naught end? Do I need a key?
Is there a lock which seals a door? Oh woe!


Perchance, this could not happen or can it?
My'n eyes hide themselves, from the thoughts enclosed.
Withholding memoir's are degenerate!
People change scenes, which are twice imposed.


I cannot instill changes within thee.
Nor, the sun, moon, stars, earth and sky 
-only me.
