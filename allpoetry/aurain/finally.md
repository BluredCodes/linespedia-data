Finally, to rest one night free and in peace
Finally, to wake up refreshed
Finally, Finally, Finally are words on everyone's lips.

To run in the wind that smells so sweet
To leap with joy in a world of happiness
To sing with others hand in hand.

Holding on to others to keep yourself from falling
Holding on each thread of sanity
Holding on, enduring, is what all of us had done.

We all deserve this release
We all rest finally in someone else's hand
We all relax with relief of joy, each tired but happy.

Finally, we have seen the promise fulfilled
Finally, we can enjoy true perfection
Finally, Finally, Finally, are words on everyone's lips.2 years ago,
