title: "Droplets of Water"

Tears that come down, shaped in a sprical form
Empty posessions in a mound
Erupting with a distructive force.


Crystals that sparkle in the murky shadows
Mixture of salt and honey
Liquid gradually forming a pool.


Oh, to drown in a sea of diamonds
Thrust out you hand, observe the tears that run down in a silky line!
Mimic's graceful lines and contours.


A trail of dust it leaves behind   
 -fades
Lo and Behold! Another forms tears
In truth, tears shed are same as others dipearsed
Vapors once again will form droplets of water.
