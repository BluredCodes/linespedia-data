title: "Nothing I Can Do"

Nothing I can do
Nothing I can change to make it all seem right
I haven’t done a thing
Yet I feel like I’m the one to blame

I don’t have a clue at what I’m supposed to do
In this world I haven’t got you
Nothing I can do to change this between us
How do I make it all right?


Nothing for me to do
I can’t tell the truth from the lie 
Haven’t done a thing but I hurt you
Twist this pain around 
Turn it upside down
It’s real and up to you


I’ve changed from what I used to be
I’ve realized my soul has died
And I am gone – 
Nothing I can do


I can’t turn back and make the difference
Would I ever care if I weren’t caught?
I think not.
