Turn the page 

He'll make me promises he won't keep
He'll give me love taps with his hard fists
He'll drowned me in my own blood someday
But the man is mine

Turn the page

He calls me to find out where I am
He drinks to make me more beautiful
He flirts with other women, while I sit and watch
A Lesson in life he says
But be sure the man is mine 

Turn the page

He demands me to wash him every night
I clean his house but it's never right
He goes out all night with his friends
While I enjoy his pain that never ends
But it's okay the man is mine

Turn the page 

He kisses my wounds he made again
and tells me it will be all right
and once again he tells me
HE LOVES ME
The man will always be mine!!

Turn the page
