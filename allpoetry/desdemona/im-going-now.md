---
title: "I'm Going Now"
writer: "Desdemona"

Sayonara, my soul I must leave.
Adios, to all my angers and grief.
Good-bye, to my misery and my pains.
Farewell, to all my crying games.

Tootles, to my troubled past.
Wish you well, my time did last.
See ya later, my love and hate.
Better go, 'fore its to late.

Later, all my memories hidden.
arriverderci, emotions forbidden.
So long, my compassion and my tears.
gotta go, my miserable fears.

I must depart, my heart so broken.
Begone I must, my gun of token.
Au revoir, I love you guys
Departing now, you'll miss my lies
all aboard, I will miss you
Killing myself now, I must adieu.
