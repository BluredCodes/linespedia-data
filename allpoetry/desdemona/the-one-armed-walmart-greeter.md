Today I met a Walmart greeter named Gus 
He had only one arm and cussed 
The button on his vest said "ask me where it's at?" 
So, I pointed at his missing arm and did just that. 
He said "Keep YOUR DAMN ARMS inside the window of the bus!"this is a limerick challenge.. Had to be funny.. Go figure. hahahha
