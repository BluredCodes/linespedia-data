Mama, I heard you weep today
So dry your weary eyes
I am here with my friend Steven
To tell you our last good-byes

We are both real angels now
Flying in this sky
We will always watch over you Mama
I know your asking why?

On our way home today Mama
We had that awful crash
But, me nor Steven felt a thing
It all happened within a flash

Mama, we are both in heaven now
So don't be afraid and sad
If that car didn't hit our car head on
It would have damaged that school bus bad

That bus was full of little kids
And the car was approaching near
Steven swerved to help them out
But it was the death to us I fear

Mama, Don't look at this tragedy so sad
We are hero's proud and true
God gave us special hero wings
They're a beautiful baby blue!
