---
title: "My Piece Of Ass"
writer: "Desdemona"
slug: "my-piece-of-ass"
source: "AllPoetry"

My friend so beautiful and so smart
Staring out into the crowd
through the thick fog and smoke
looking for the man of her dreams
Her future, her life time of fantasies

2nd set and still no one
no one to sing to
no sad songs to fullfill their meanings
just a gal on stage singing away
looking for her prince in the crowd


3rd set she is now drunk
singing loud and proud
looking for her dream man
There he sits smiling back at her
So gorgeous so cute
goatee, well dressed and he smells of success

4th set is almost over
She dreams of being with him later tonight
But, before you know it something isn't right
He gets up like he can't stand no more
Then I hear my drunk friend scream in the microphone
HEY, MY PIECE OF ASS JUST WALKED OUT THE DOOR!!!!
