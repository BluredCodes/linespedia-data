He watches my every move
He knows my every word
He sees through me
Yet I know not of this man

What does he want with me?
Does he enjoy my company?
Does he live my words?
Does he believe his lies?

Someone please help me
This has gone to far
Tattoos and promises
A best selling book tomorrow

This is one sick bastard
For four years played with my mind
Lead me on
Abused me
Used me
Mislead me
Trusted me
Ran far away from me

To those who think it can't happen to you?
Turn on the internet 
and see for yourself!!!!!!
