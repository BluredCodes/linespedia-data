It must have been cool to be the creator
Creator of life, the world, Humans
You would have to have a sick sense of humor for that job
Men born with a third leg
While women bitch and moan
Men getting cold have shrinkage
Women getting cold harden up
Men have physical strength and power
Women have physical beauty and pussy control
Men cling to a breast
Women cling to a strong arm
To be a man you must never cry
To be a women crying gets you everything
Wonders of the world, Miracles of life
Animals of day and night,
Bugs and parasites, Land and nature
God was one hell of a person...
To be a humorous world creator!!!
