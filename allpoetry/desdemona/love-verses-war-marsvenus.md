~Mars~

Broken picture of my love
Broken glass like my heart
Free - fall in crimson
Through my mind
Tearing me    a   p    a    r       t
I call on mars
Through my tears,, Through the stars
To avenge my shattered dreams
My dear blood - god
Tear him apart
Tear him apart at the seams.....


~ Venus ~

My anger passes
I dry my tears
I remember our love throughout the years
I pray to Venus
I beg her for forgiveness
Her love like the sea will keep me strong forever
My sweet Sea-foam Goddess
Watch over my heart
Put the pieces of my life back together
Love back in my heart
Love back in my heart like before....When I was 19 I took a creative writing class. Our teacher made us listen to mood music. We had to write a poem while listening in 4 minutes about Mars and Venus. The music was titled Mars and Venus. This is what I had Created. Thought I would share this since it's probably one of my first writings ever.
