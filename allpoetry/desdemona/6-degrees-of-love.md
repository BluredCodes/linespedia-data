My heart captivated by silhouetted words 
Ears ring of magical melodies 
Skin clammy of moist perspiration 
Throat dampens my tongue with lustful tendencies 
Your beauty consumes me 
Swallows me ETERNALLY 
Better than any Iv drip 

I breath in your performance 
My life begins 
An obsession I memorize 
I cry endless tears of blissful pleasure 
Stuck in a moment I can't overcome 
pause 

You are my BURNING FLAME 
I caress your image in my dreams 
My empty space that needed filled 
An actor on a movie screen 
I realize now I am in love 
Love is powerful.. 
Breathtaking..
