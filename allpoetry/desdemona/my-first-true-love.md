title: "My First True Love"

I found her sitting still one day
By herself over in a corner
Away from all the others
So Vibrant, 
So peaceful, 
So lively, 
So beautiful
I knew then and there she would be mine forever

Friday
I decide to buy her a drink
She will be all mine now 

As I take her for a drive 
I decide to pull off the side of the road
I reach out to touch her form
She is so cold
I run my fingers up and down her beautiful body
But, she don't move
I could really turn "HER" on but why?
I am a man you know
But why waste all my energy on her?

I chisel the ice off her
Letting her know it will be okay
I'll let her be free and show her true colors
My eyes keep gazing over her hot body 
perfect as always
Her insides are so soft to the touch
I love the smell of her
I push one button to make my hot baby vibrate
She purrs to let me know she is turned on

Her headlights are so magnificent and big
Hers are so much bigger then most 
That is what makes her special in my eyes
One of a kind
ALL MINE DAMN IT!!
Look everyone Look
She makes me want to shout to the world

I will admit one thing....
I will find another like her as long as I live
She is my first true love...
My first woman
My new car
a 65 Mustang!!!
