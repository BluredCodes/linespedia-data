title: "Show Me The Beads!!!!"
writer: "Desdemona"

Hey young lady show me your Tits
If you'll do this deed 
This is what you'll get....

Beads so long
And beads so true
Beads with money signs
And beads so blue

Beads you can always wear at night
Beads so thick
And beads so white

Beads of red
And Beads of green
Beads of yellow
Worn, proud to be seen

Beads At Marti Gras
Beads of pleasure
Beads that will get lost
And Beads of treasure

Ladies wear your beads so high and thin
Smile and give off a little grin  :)
If you wish to get more again
Show your tits to these silly men!!!
