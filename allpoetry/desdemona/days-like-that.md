Take a right
past the
private water running
Streaming from the
screams of skulls

Get your money back at the door
after your empty mind
Cries itself from
hatred and dances of
Worthless wiggles

The atmosphere surrounds you
of bright lights
and smoke like fog

Million dollar dreams
and frustrated memory's
of pussy control

Yellow darts pierce
my still framed ashtrays
of emptiness

Take some back to your pad
Tell your bald headed brother

Hey long time no see
Hell that is a wild dream
money like millions
We can jump out of something
palm tree okay?

You'll have days like that...
