If you stare at something long enough
it starts to come alive
and to your mind it's so beautiful
beauty like no other
beautiful images will sensor to your eyes
but mainly visions will rest in your soul
You cry over and over and over

WHY?

You can't have what you want? 
but the beauty if so overwhelming
tears of pain are joyous now
In the back of your head you see others laughing at you
crying for no reason
they don't hold imagery like your creativity
to see the picture come alive
scream out at you
tell you things
touch my face and you will feel the real pain
the real tears of beauty
and when the storm is over
the tears will dry up
and your face will leave a beautiful rainbow smile
shining like the day you were first born
and still images of beauty will arise
People will laugh once again
and yet you will be the one holding on....

The photo that once made you cry
Over and over and over again

the image that makes you so happy you weep
and yet people will laugh once again

they don't know what beauty really is....

or do they?
