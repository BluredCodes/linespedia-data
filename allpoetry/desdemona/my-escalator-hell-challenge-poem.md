Today, I took a big step 
One small step on the escalator 
One giant leap in my mind 
This is my biggest fear 
Why you ask? 
It moves so quickly 
What if my shoe string is untied? 
It could get sucked up, in the end of these fast moving stairs 
I once seen an episode of Emergency 911, it was very unpleasant 
A little boy got his arm caught in these horrifying escalators 
His arm turned blue and he about died 
I know I am an adult 
I probably wouldn't die 
I know I shouldn't be this afraid 
But my brain overcomes my heart overcomes my fears 
I can't breath 
To many people surround me 
Get away, damn you! 
I sweat profusely 
The stairs they move so fast, but so slow 
I need help 
I need air 
Get out of my way 

Next time.... 
I'm just taking the stairs
