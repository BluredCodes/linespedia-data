Today will be the day of the rest of my life! 
After nine long months of kissing and sucking my wittle toes 
Smiling for the ultrasound cameras 
Wiggling and bouncing inside my mommy's tummy 
Kicking to make more leg room 
My final destination is near 

OH Wow, 
      Here 
          We 
             Go........ 


~~~ The Ride Of My Lifetime ~~~~ 


Burr It's getting cold 


"What 
  Is 
 This 
Bright 
Light?" 


"Who 
     Is 
        This 
             Strange 
                      Man?" (I don't recognize his voice?) 


~~~

Ohhhhhhhh 
           Weeeeeeeeeeeee 
                         GUSHHHHHHHHHHHHHH 

~~~


"FREE AT LAST!!!!! FREE AT LAST!!!! I AM FREE AT LAST!!!!!" 


~~~


"Hey Mr., what are you doing?" 
"That hurts" ~~~ WAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!!!! 

"Oh where is my mommy?" 
Awwww there she is... 
My mommy is so beautiful 
Her cheeks let off a rosy glow that brings a small tear to my eye 
I have made my mommy beautiful! 
I think I have her eyes and nose 

Mommy please hold me and cuddle me 
Love me unconditionally 
Read to me like you have every single night 
And nurse me mommy with that tit that is shaped so right!!!!!
