As I am driving down the interstate
Route 7
 
Alone thinking to myself
Listening to the sad songs on the radio
 
I see a glare
a light so bright
a streak in the air
I decide to make a wish upon it
 
What to wish for I ask myself?
 
happiness?
wealth?
love for you?
marriage?
children?
 
as I stop to see this light hit the ground
I realized I wasn't wishing upon a star after all
I was wishing on a stupid cigerette butt
that a trucker just threw out of his damn window!!
 
Such my luck damn it!
