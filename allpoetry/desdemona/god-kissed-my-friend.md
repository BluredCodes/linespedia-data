God Blessed my friend today
I have him to thank she is still here
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

My friend Sarah so young so full of life
We used to do so many things together
So popular she was
She got married 
Had two wonderful children
Moved away and made a life for herself
 
My friend Sarah so pretty
Came back here to live in Ohio
Got a divorce
Met another man so sweet
A guy that waited forever for her
Her little brothers best friend
 
My friend Sarah so strong
Decided to rent a house
7 days they lived
House burnt to the ground
The fire took away her Boyfriend and Best Friends daughter
Sarah and her children got out
Horrible day it was
 
My friend Sarah so weak
Lost loved ones
Her best friend angry
No place to live 
Feeling helpless
Suicide is the place Sarah wants to be
Takes out a life insurance policy 
She is going to end her life but how?
Sarah cries every day
 
My friend Sarah miracles do happen
Found a new friend today
Love they made 
Virgin he was
Pregnant she became
She doesn't want a baby now
Her mind boggles some
Abortion 
Yes or no?
 
My friend Sarah spiritually known
Now has cervical cancer :(
Goes to church to preach the word
Preacher reads the story of Job
Sarah realizes this is how she feels
I am Job
Take everything away from me
A slap in my face
Just don't touch me Devil
God please give me back everything
I have been faithful to your word
She prays and prays
 
My friend Sarah a year later
Drives down the road
Sun setting and she is feeling worthless
Looks to God in the sky
God I am getting rid of this baby
Unless by chance you make it twins
That is the only way to make it right again
So God kissed Sarah's belly that day
A strange Miracle had happened
Next week she found out it was Definitely twins
 
My friend Sarah 24 yrs old alive and well
Two babies born 
Exactly the same day as the fire
One Year later
Two boys so precious as gold
My friend happy with her life
4 wonderful children and a man so humble
It's God we must thank
God kissed my friend
And blessed her with a new life again
 
Sarah smiles towards the sky now
Thanks God for everything
She sheds a small tear
I shed a small tear
We hug and embrace each other
I walk away slowly wiping away my tears
I thank god for giving my friend Sarah back
