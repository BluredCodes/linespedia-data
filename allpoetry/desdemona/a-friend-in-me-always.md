Somedays..
When you feel life isn't worth living anymore
When your coffee has all ran out
There is no more gas in your car
Remember you do have friends

Friends will always pick you up
Friends will always give you a wake up call
A hug 
A shoulder
Advice that seems always true

Somedays...
When you feel all run down
Remember one small thing

~~~~~~~~~~~

You will "Always" have a friend near by
A person that will dry your tears when you cry
A person that will always get you back up on your feet again
A name I am proud to honor ~ "FRIEND!"For my Friend Elizabeth
