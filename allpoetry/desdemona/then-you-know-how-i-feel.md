---
title: "Then You Know How I Feel"
writer: "Desdemona"
slug: "then-you-know-how-i-feel"
source: "AllPoetry"

Have you ever felt scared of the dark, but your a light switch away?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever felt a presence, when your kneeling down to pray?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever had one of those days, when you really need a friend?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever had a relationship, you swore would never end?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever been so excited, you felt like you could fly?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever been in so much pain, you felt like you could die?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever taken a shower, and felt dirty by the end of the day?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever been so speechless, not a word that you could say?
Then you know how I feel.

Have you ever done something in life, you later would regret?
Then you know how I feel.

If you answered "YES" to all these questions, then...
I know how "You" feel, I bet.
