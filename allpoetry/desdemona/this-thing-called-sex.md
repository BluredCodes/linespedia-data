What is this thing called sex?

I want to feel this need to yearn
I want my insides like yours to burn

I need to feel it deep inside
I need to take this pleasure ride

I want to feel this burning desire
I want to feel my thighs on fire

I want to be the one on top
I want to taste your ever drop

I deserve to taste this sweet caress
So someone take my innocense
Then finally my thoughts will be set to rest
Because I will have known this thing called sex!!!!
