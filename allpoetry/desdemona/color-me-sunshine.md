title: "Color Me Sunshine"

Once upon a time you gave me a name
Yet can I call you an enemy?
Can I call you a friend?
Can I call you by your real name?
Wait, I'm not even allowed to call you!

My wings once were fluffy and full
Now you pulled them out one by one
I haven't any left to fly away with
Stranded and wingless
alone 

I used to wear my clown suit well
White face and painted on smile
My red nose turned brown
My makeup smeared from the blue tear in the corner of my eye
My happy smile now turned upside down

Days gone by with worries attached
Pathetic thinking and anxiety attacks
Apologies accepted
But your laughter overcame all

Once upon a time someone gave me a name
They colored me Sunshine
I recolored me Rain
