I fall to the earth with the greatest of ease 
Kissing the branches of these swaying trees 
God made me special can't you see 
I am moist and sweet like nectar from a bumble bee 
The air current it carries me further down this shaky limb 
I glide with the wind on a curious whim 
I speed past the leaves and nestle with a bug 
For a while my best friend becomes a salty slug 
I then say goodbye and move on past 
This feeling so warm... feels like I'm free at last 
I fall with a thump and snuggle the ground 
I look like the other drops.. hard to be found 
But god gave me a gift can't you see 
I am moisture for the earth to make it preTTY 
So when you look up and see me falling down 
Remember I am a single raindrop kissing the ground!Challenge Description: You're a raindrop, dripping from branch to branch to branch on a tree...down down down
