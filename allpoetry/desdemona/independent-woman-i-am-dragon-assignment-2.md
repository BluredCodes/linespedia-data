Independent woman I am free to roam
Money all mine, no children of my own
Just small talk will seem to get me by
I seem to always catch the gay women's eyes
Relationships haven't been my specialty
Commitment is the thing that mostly scares me
I'm great at sports, dancing, poetry and music too
I'm like an ocean front property with a birds eye point of view
My weakness is if you think bad of me
I tend to get my feelings hurt way to easily
I do sometimes hold a grudge and become very mean
The dark side of me many rarely have seen
Trusting to much is a weakness I implore
Loving me to much might find me running for the door
I have a heart that is really big you see
Most men think I am the type of girl they want to settle down and marry
I can be your shoulder to cry on when you need me around
I can make everyone laugh like a silly old clown
Men often tell me, I am way to nice
And age makes no difference to me, is my words of advice
I am not a trashy girl and my heart holds no hate
Relationships are something that I really have no fate
I guess I am happy with my life and the way it tends to be
I am a just an 28 year old independent woman
Single, straight, girl named LeeThis is a dragon assignment where you must write your weaknesses and strong points in a relationship. I haven't had many relationships so I wrote it like this.
