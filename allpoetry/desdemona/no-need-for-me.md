title: "No Need For Me"

Growing up young is a difficult task
Shout up and listen becomes your everyday language
Tears roll down my dirty face
Memorable moments of neglect and abuse
Honorable kiddy medals for special attention

Teenage years are harder yet
Puberty, Acne, and maturity are top form
Hormones rush through your veins not in your head
Must study to get through this hard life
Who the fuck they think they are kidding?

Twenty-two years old and a bum
Fucked everyone in this town
College degree. You don't need one of those.
Just need a dime and a bag of weed
Life sucks when you're on the street

Twenty five and barely alive
Local job in a burger joint down the street
Welfare don't except bums, nor anyone with a part time job
Thinking of burning their building down tonight
Heat for my hands
Life is hard when no one cares about you anymore

Tonight is the night for my dreams to fly
No need for education, family or life
I don't look like they do
Some things will never change
I dont' wear my smile on my sleeve
But I will wear my blood so perfect
Tonight of all nights
It will stain me like society
And then it will keep me warm forever
And I will die in my own arms
The same way I came into this fucked up world!!!!!!!
