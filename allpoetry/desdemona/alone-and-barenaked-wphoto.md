ALONE
Here I am once again 
Sitting quietly alone in this dark theatre
Movie starts
I drift off to dream
Waking up 
Look around
ALONE

ALONE
Walking around
No hand to hold
Getting to feel bitter old
ALONE

ALONE
Where is my prince?
My dream come true?
ALONE I am and feeling blue

ALONE
The princess dies
Told through lies
People morn her
and passer by's
If I die will someone care?
ALONE again 
In a coffin 
Like on this earth right up here
ALONE

ALONE
Single
No family to share
No throne to the heir
No breath of fresh air
ALONE

ALONE
I am
Lonely I choose to be
Till someone overcomes the freak I see
ALONE

ALONE
No tune
No song they sing
To make my heart go ring a ding ding
ALONE

ALONE
I cry
no tears to really shed
Just a wishful thought in my weary little head
ALONE

ALONE
I wish I wasn't
I hope lonliness comforts me soon
Then once again like a dream
Someone will bust it like a balloon
ALONE ONCE AGAIN
