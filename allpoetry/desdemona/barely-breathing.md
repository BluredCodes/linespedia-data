---
title: "Barely Breathing"
writer: "Desdemona"
slug: "barely-breathing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15475-Barely-Breathing-by-Desdemona"
---

Tip back my neck
It will come easier
blockages in my airways
Lumps in my throat
Tears rolling down my face
I can't find the air

Hyperventilating
Panic attacks
My eyes becoming big as saucers
Fear overcomes my breath
Heartbeats increase
Muscles tense

Where is the air?
Where is the freshness?
Where is the life in me?
You gave me hope
You gave me reason
Now I am dying inside and out

I can't find the breath
I can't find the inhaler
I can't find the air
I can't be with you again

You took my breath when we first met
You took my breath when you walked away
I will always be barely breathing
