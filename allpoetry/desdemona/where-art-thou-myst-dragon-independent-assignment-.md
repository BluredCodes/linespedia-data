Oh hail all ye dragons
Come forth I do call
We lost our fierce leader
He's not in the hall

I looked in the observatory
I glanced in the den
I looked in the cellar
He is no where to have been

I searched in thy kitchen
I rendered the room of the ball
I ransacked the bed rooms
I even questioned Murphy's law

Where is our leader
Where had he gone
He isn't under the watery moat
Nor is he playing croquet in the lawn

Please dear Myst we need you home
Listen to our dragon hearts plea
Hurry quickly and hurry back
We need a new assignment you see

Why would you up and leave on us
Why ever could this possibly be
Those aren't tears in our dragon eyes
It's Myst within our hearts we needHope ye come back Myst.. We miss ya
