Seven days will be my life's ending 
Ending of a new era of time 
Time to say goodbye to friends 
Friends whom I loved in this life eternally 
Eternally burned into my hearts soul forever 
Forever I will feel grateful 
Grateful for their laughs, cheers, and tears 
Tears of joy knowing they have been there 
There in spirit and soon I too will be their angel 
Angel of their day, yesterday and tomorrow night 
Night might follow me soon but their smiles now will overwhelm all 
All that have known me will come to see 
See I was happy for those that loved me 
Me!Slekkys Challenge Description: I want you to write a poem about your last week alive on earth. How would you feel, what would you do, who would you forgive, and who would you beg forgiveness from, etc. I want you also to use the last word from each line and make it the first word in your next line. Good luck! (40 points)
