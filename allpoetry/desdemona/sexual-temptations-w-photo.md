Do you want to visually seduce me?
Arousing me begins with your visual appreciation.
Outline my body with your eyes, 
Stare at my sexually sensitive flesh.

Satisfy for a moment my sexual needs.
So acute are my sensations.
The small of my back so silky smooth like a new baby.
Provide me with virtual kisses of high degrees of flexibility.

Acrobatic positions are an interesting diversion.
What a highly erotic creature you are. 
So sensitive to erotic stimulation of my beauty.
There are several advantages to my positions.

Your lust craves my touches of erotica.
I too long for a bite of ecstasy and pleasure.
Masturbate to my photograph ~ I won't tell a soul
Besides I am nude for the world to see

Available in movement and without tension.
Screams of your endless pleasure haunt the silence of my nights.
After climax a gentle caress is what you need ~ I need
