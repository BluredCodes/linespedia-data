Who goes this creature of the night?
The one that comes out just to fright
Bright Red eyes, Six feet tall
The creature that made the Silver Bridge fall
Wings they stretch ten feet wide
This creature haunts the Point Pleasant town with ghoulish pride
A legend? A myth? What could he be?
From a chief ~ put a curse ~ back in history
100 mile an hour this creature can fly
Flapping his wings through out the dark sky
Tragedy strikes when he seems to be near
No one is ever hurt just always feared
If you go out to the T-n-T area you better take yourself a beer
Then make sure you check out the Mothman Prophecies movie starring Richard Gere!This creature is known to lurk the town that is about 20 minutes from where I live.. Its a cool creature that has been known to haunt the nights for many years now..
