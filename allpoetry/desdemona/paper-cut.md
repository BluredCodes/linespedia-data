---
title: "Paper Cut"
writer: "Desdemona"
slug: "paper-cut"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If I had a choice
I would be a piece of paper
I could always lie around anywhere I pleased
I would never hear anyone call me horrible names
I would feel someone else's emotion as they would write on me
I would be able to feel human tears as they ran down my face
I would be tickled with the scratches of a pen or pencil
I would enjoy the ride when the wind would carry me off the table
I could hurt you with just a small cut but I wouldn't mean to
I'd be around forever if you'd only let me

Then years would pass

I would turn a yellowish color and the words on me would fade
Exactly like the ink that once was so harsh
Only to be used and neglected like the real world
I would be tossed a side
Put away
Hidden from others eyes 
I would be later be known as "trash"
and
I then as I am now..
I would disappear forever!
