Drown in a raging flood 
Swim in your own semen 
Die in your own blood 
You piece of shit demon 

Taste your own medicine 
I hope it burns your taste buds off 
Cut your own throat 
Die of a gut wrenching cough 

Caught in your own lies 
Headaches that paralyze 
Suffocate and die 
Poke out your eyes 

Break your own heart 
Choke on your bone 
electrocute your brain 
Hello anyone home? 

You have blue balls, ohhhh soooo sad 
Stick your dick in a blender hope it hurts bad 

Poison your veins 
with silly drug games 

Stinking breath and nose hair pulls 
Pluck out your eyebrows 
Boy am I cruel 

Torture you forever 
Never let it end 
FUCK YOU, FUCK YOU, FUCK YOU 
You'll never be my friend 
So taste your salty tears 
YOU FUCKIN JERK!!!
