This goes out to a woman 
Across the other side of the world
A day away
But miles of laughs we share

This goes out to a woman
a mother of a beautiful daughter
a grandmother to a little boy
A wife that tried to please all

This goes out to a woman
who I wished I had met
Who called me by many names
Who was a victim at a young age

This goes out to a woman
Proud of her accomplishments
ashamed of nothing
Heart as big as all compilations of books

This goes out to a woman
Whom has recently passed away
A friend to the end
Now another angel in heaven
The strongest woman I never knew...

A Woman I called 'MUM'I met this woman online a few years ago. She was from Australia and I live in the United States. Even though we never really met, she would call me on the phone sometimes and talk. I know it was major long distance bills but she offered me to come stay with her and her daughter for awhile, If i wanted to. Well needless to say she died in her sleep one night. This woman had to have been one of the strongest women I have ever met. She was raped as a young child. Her parents left her cuz she was pregnant. So she raised herself and her daughter on her own. I have to say She will be truley Missed.
