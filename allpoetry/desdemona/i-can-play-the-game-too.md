title: "I Can Play The Game Too"

Everytime I turn around
You turn away from me
Knowing deep in your heart
Our love was meant to be

Sometimes your smile is so warm
Sometimes it's just like ice
You lie in bed and tell me tales
When I ask you for advice

You pull me in, you push me back
Til I can't take anymore
You took my hand, we danced our dance
Then you lead me out the back door

Touch my face, tell me lies
Hold me close as you can
Kiss my neck walk on past
Ask me to stay with you again

I draw a heart, you fold on me
Until you deal another hand
But baby, I am not the one to lay the blame
So now it's my turn to play your game!This is a very old poem a friend and I wrote years ago about this guy who would always want me to marry him then ignore me in public infront of his friends. I finally played his game back on him :)
