title: "Touching God With A Prayer"
writer: "Desdemona"

When you sit down tonight
Before you go to bed
While amongst your praying
Will you comfort your God?
Will you ask to touch his face?
Dry his tears?
Pat his back?
Tell him has done a wonderful job

Will you be among the trillions
Giving yourself to him
Thanking him for everything in your life?
Your friends life?
Will you ask him to take your hand?
Keep you in his heart forever?

When you sit down tonight
Before you go to bed
While amongst your praying
Will you ask God if he is okay?
Will you ask him to pray for himself?
Keep himself well and safe
Will you Pray FOR God instead of to him?
