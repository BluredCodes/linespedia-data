Members of the jury 
let me plead my case 
I have nothing to lie about 
Yes, I did break in their place 

I swear they have my alien hid 
A creature I really lust 
So I tried to sneak into their house 
but it's badly covered in dust 

I just wanted to see for myself 
I wanted to take a peek 
But this alien with only one eyeball 
Is the creature I must seek 

He is an alien with a dark eye 
And he has a funny looking nose 
He stands about 3 inches tall 
And he has about thirty toes 

I think my neighbors are holding him captive 
So I am trying to set him free 
Listen to me judge 
This alien belongs to me 

So members of the jury 
this is my final plea 
I just had to see for myself 
If my neighbors were tormenting me 

I know I may sound crazy 
But I know I'm fine and dandy 
I broke into my neighbors house
