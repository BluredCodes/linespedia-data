title: "You Washed Your Hands In Us"

It's been years now
Since you passed through our minds
Our once obsession
Our bottomless pit
Your string puppets
Pull us this way 
No wait... pull us that way
Taunting us with death threats
Taunting us with promises and love
Showing us we can do what we wish
You stole my heart
My tears of joy, my fears and hatred
You took away my inner being
You took away our fun and trust
Our day and night conversations gone
Like a flash of breath on your fingertips
You truly washed your hands in us
For that you will always be a bastard!
