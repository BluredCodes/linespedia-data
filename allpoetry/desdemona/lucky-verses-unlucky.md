"Lucky" they call me
Girl so free
Everything going for
Eyes full of miracles
Angel of hope
Smiles of sunshine

"Lucky" they call me
Single and free
Talk of the town
Amazing talents
Jack Of all trades
The innocent one

"Lucky" they call me
Nice lady you are
Dancer in a bar
Internet Night Queen
Kevin Spacey Feign

"Unlucky" I call me
Unlucky in love
Unlucky happiness
Unlucky and unwanted
Unlucky and wasted
Unlucky like this shitty poem
