Today, I'm off on a huge road trip 
I packed up some Slim Jims and some tator' chips 
A road map... 
And a old nap sack 
And a cooler full of Busch light beer 

I'm on my way to Buffalo 
Where the biggest ball of bubble gum grows 
I hope I don't step on a piece, with my toes 
I can't wait to stick a piece, on the end of my nose 
Just, like the teacher made me do in grade school 

Just when nothing could seem to spoil my trip 
I stopped to pee and there was "a backed up toilet" 
Poo and pee was everywhere 
Could it get any worse... I wonder I swear? 
So, I hopped back in my car and drove down the road 

I finally made it clear across the country 
Where I just about ran out of my money 
I didn't have enough to get me inside 
To see this biggest ball of gum this state did reside 
So, I hopped over the fence and ran like hell 

Boy, you must have seen this sucker 
It was one big huge mother f***er 
It was bigger than the biggest tree 
Taller than the Empire in New York City 
A lot of gum I thought in my mind 

I can't believe all this gum, my hearts a racin 
Pink and red and purple gum a blazin 
I wonder if they will let me take a bite? 
I don't know, but hey they just might 
So I stoled a piece and stuck it in my mouth 

Hey lookie what I have, a piece of the gum! 
Oh my gosh, oh know, Everybody run!!! 
The piece I had taken was making it stay 
Now, the ball has lost control and it's heading this way 
Run for the hills everyone 

Now, if you think all this "delightful chaos" is bad 
I went to jail and they had to call my dad 
He knows I am" Pragmatically oversensitive" and shouldn't yell at me 
Hey calm down dad,  It's just a road trip, don't treat me like I am three 
Good excuse for now *Wink* 

So, now I am doing time in this place of hell 
The inmates are weird and they have a horrible smell 
I have to sit in here and a 1,000 pieces of gum I must chew 
To fix all the chaos that I had to brew 
This sucks majorly!!!! 

So, if you want to take a road trip, the big ball of gum you must see 
Remember I was the one that moved that big ball of castrophe 
It rolled down the hill and smashed into that little town 
Everything is fixed now and the gum is still all round 
Thanks to my chewing 1,000 extra pieces 

But in the end this was much fun 
I got to see sights and I chewed a lot of gum 
But take my advice in all of this 
The big ball of gum in Buffalo, is something you don't want to miss 
I think I am going back next year!This is a deathgoddess Challenge Poem. Write about a funny road trip. Where you went, What you are doing. Destruction you are doing. ANd you must use her images. :)
