No matter what time it is
Where you go
When you get there
If your early
If your late
In a public place
Life beyond earth
Underwater
On a mountain
Way up high
In you backyard
In the city
On the highway
In the country
At a reunion
In a school
Subway
Train
Taxi
Near the sun
Above the stars
No matter if you are on the internet
There are Always ASSHOLES Among us!!!
