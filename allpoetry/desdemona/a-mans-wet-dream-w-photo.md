She is every mans wet dream
A woman with a big mouth
A woman so naked to the world
"I can take it them all" she moans
So wide they glide
One
Two...

Watch as I place them in my mouth
THE BALLS 
THE NICE HEAVY BALLS
I may have room for more
Can you help me?
I open wide
I place them with pride
These Three Pool Balls in my mouth!
