When your day is diabolical 
Look forth to the sky
You will find thee lurking in the mist
Singing a tune of Willow 
Sharing a wing of comfort

Flying with a musical melody
Soaring with the greatest of elegance
Batting the inevitable eyelashes of innocence
Legend of one-hundred twenty-seven years to this date
A Dragon of a mystical sunshine delight

Escapades of mischief occur occasionally
Practical jokes without malevolence
Deceptions with exemption of resentment
Smiling consistently hiding my vulgarity
Good Natured?  Up to no good?   ~ Can you tell which is which? 

Lady dragon thy heart so foresightful
Awakens the sad and provides stability and reassurance in others
A joke thrown in for pure amusement and fun
My elegance and deviousness will comfort those in need
The Dragon you must turn to for a cheering up indeedI am a royal blue/purple dragonette of 127 yrs old. I am a practical joker with a kind heart. I am always fashionably early and I change colors with my mood. I have very long eyelashes and a permy grin that covers up my mischief. When I fly about the sky, my wings play beautiful music that soothes even the common beast. I can smell rain for miles, and I am always there to comfort friends in need with a laugh or a hug. My pranks never do harm others, but if I get away with something, I do grow black dots on my tail. I am a dragon of music, love, and deception.
