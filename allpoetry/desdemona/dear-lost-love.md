It takes a special personto be a hero of the heartthrough all the good timesand the bad timeswhen we are far apartremember I will love you Sarahfrom now through eternityand i'll always remember how you werealways here for meNow you got to be strong my dearand do the best, that you can doyou got to move on my loveand cherish life so newYou know I left you from this earthWith a smile on my faceknowing that you made me happyIs the memories I embraceSo wipe away those foolish tearsBecause I'm wrapping my wings around youto let you know I am always hereand I will always love you trueSo I hear you call me a herohowever could this beFor your the one whom is the heroJust look in your heart and see!!!This is a poem I wrote for one of my best friends. She was 23 yrs old and had 2 small kids under the age of 5 that was in a house fire. Her boyfriend whom she loved dearly and her best friends child was inside this house when they lost their lives. My friend and her children got out but the other two did not. Everyone Called her boyfriend a hero cuz he was the one that woke them all up, to get them all out of the house. If it wasn't for him all of them would be gone today. So in our eyes he was a hero. So, I wrote this to my friend whom was going through a really tough time. It's sort of in the eyes of Donald to Sarah
