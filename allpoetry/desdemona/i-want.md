I want to run totally naked in your thoughts

I want to drink your contagious love nectar

I want to taste your hot beads of sweat

I want to hear your moans of sexual pleasure

I want to give you multiple orgasms over and over 

I want to free the beast inside you

I want to be your one and only true love

I want to outline your entire body with my tongue

I want to make love to you like there is no tomorrow morning

I want to take you all in and draw a photo later

I want to suck your passionate lips like a sweet peach

I want to fall asleep in your safe strong arms

I want to sing you a love song while undressing you

I want to gaze into your eyes and see forever

I want to smell the aroma of our sex in the air

I want you damn it so come here!!!!
