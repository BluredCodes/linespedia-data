title: "Dancing Promiseland"

Angels fly around your head
In silhouettes of love

While your heart beats like wings
Of beautiful white doves

Hugs come from within
When you're feeling run down

While kisses and smiles
Make your world go around

Because when heaven arrives
And you're standing tall

No more Multiple Sclerosis Disease
Will ever make you fall

Because you're proud to be one
Of God's little creatures

And no one will make fun of you
With unworkable features

So if God takes you away
From this beautiful land

Remember you're a Diease free angel
Dancing in the Promiseland
