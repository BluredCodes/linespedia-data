My friend she is so funny
Funny in every way
Her and I both decided... 
To take in a movie today

We both paid for our tickets
After walking through the door
We went to buy some popcorn
Some soda and some smores

A cop then decided to stop us
And my friend got really mad
He told us we could take in our items
But we couldn't take in our bags

My friend got quiet embarrassed
She didn't want to get caught
Because we had been in Spencers Gift Store
And sex toys she had bought

They were for some wacky presents
Not for my friend at all
But can you believe this damn security
They now have in these malls?

My friend glared right into the cops eyes
She was pissed off really bad
She bellowed out in a deep voice
