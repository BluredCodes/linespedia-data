So Wednesday is the day 
They come fix your heart
I hope your ass won't be showing
in the gown that is cut apart   (_X_)

So they are finally going to do it
finally fix your ticker
I hope they just don't patch it
With one of them scratch n sniff stickers   (scratch n sniff here)

So when you get out of the hospital
I'm sure you will be going out to swing dance
Unless you get caught
Stealing the KY jelly down your pants

I heard in today's society
the doctors aren't very smart
If the doctor asks you to pull his finger
Run because he really needs to fart  (pewww)

So Thursday you will happy
You will be finally on the mend
So you get your ass back soon to all poetry
Cause you have a million missing you Friends     (AMEN SISTA)
