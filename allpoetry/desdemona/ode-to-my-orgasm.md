I am secluded 
Alone 
By myself 
Laying flat on my back 
my own bed 
my eyes are closed tight

a strong force overcomes my fingers and moves them south 
Downwards 
sliding over my curvy abs 
longing for a sensitive touch 
You glide my fingers down to your need 
You overcome my mind 
You make both of my hands spread my lips for you 
Revealing and exposing myself to the silent air 
My right index finger glides itself to my clit 
not hard 
gentle but a little the to left 
pressure so soft but intense 
I concentrate 
Holding my breath 
Arching my back 
My toes point in a downward manner 
my finger has a mind of its own 
back and forth 
faster and faster 
sigh 
I take another deep breath 
This time holding it longer then before 
My legs start to move 
my toes curl 
my nipples become erect 
and then you take me 
I am yours for the next minute or so 
You urge me to take another ride 
Promising me this time it will be more intense 
How can I refuse? 
My breathing becomes more rapid 
The little man in the boat is holding on for dear life 
I deeply hold my breath 
As my body shakes for you 
I scream 
and then silence 
I lay still 
I take in the moment I shared with you 
I am now refreshed 
Thank you Mr. Orgasm!
