title: "Defined Idiot"
writer: "Desdemona"

Rapture -
great pleasure
enthusiasm or
the expression of it
torture chamber for us

Green -
gullible
jealous
envious actors
room of hell

Lies -
your words
intentionally false statements
your only truth
beneath reality

Trust -
Big word for small man
fact, reality, loyal
something you need to learn
never will I stay

Forever -
dust in the wind
always haunted
persistently reminded
Gone!!!!!!
