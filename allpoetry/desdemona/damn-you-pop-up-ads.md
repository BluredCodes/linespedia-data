I go to a fancy web page 
All I do is click
All these damn pop up adds
Are sure making me sick

Click to see Brittney Spears
Click to see some porn
Click for free DVDs
How about a free CD of Korn?

I am sick of seeing these damn ads
Geo cities is driving me nuts
Why would I pay 9.99 a month
For a server that always sucks

No I don't want to lose weight
No I don't want to buy a new phone
No I don't want a new web cam 
Just please leave me alone!

There is just no way around them
They are not a very fun game
Pop up adds are taken over
