---
title: "My Damn Luck"
writer: "Desdemona"
slug: "my-damn-luck"

I threw salt over my right shoulder
Gave away a tail's down penny to a friend
Hung a horse shoe over my bedroom door
To you get you to notice me

I picked a four-leaf clover
Spent a whole day throwing pennies in a wishing well
Rubbed on my lucky rabbit's foot all day
To get you to acknowledge I exist

I made a wish upon the first star
Crossed my fingers twice
Walked backwards in the rain for hours
To dance just one slow dance with you

I sent a wish upon a prayer
I got on stage and broke a leg
I sent a letter in a bottle out to sea
So, you would never have a chance to forget me

I tried all the luck
I could possibly could
But nothing seemed to work
Either all my luck has ran out of time
or you're just one hell of a jerk!!!
