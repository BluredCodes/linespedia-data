I pluck these petals 
one by, one by, one
I lay them on my bed 
so soft, so creative
I make sure to soften the pillows
and pull the silk sheets tight
I invite you in with whispers so sweet
As we undress and lie together
Our nakedness surrounds us
We roll, we fondle
We fuck all night long

I am one in you
your one inside me
Our passion is wet
our sweat is sticky
yet we are covered in a big rosary

After our night of togetherness
we shower ourselves with glistened smiles
and thorns of lust
I shed a tear of babies breath
because your beauty overcomes my eyes
Your soft smooth skin against my skin
Sigh

As the night ends I forget about the once was
The roses I had plucked away 
Was never mine to begin with
They were left on a table to die
from a bitter love gone wrong
So I took the flowers home with me
To make them alive again
I gave them a new name
Unforgotten Love Flowers
Became Unforgettable Night Petals
