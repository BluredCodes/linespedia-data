I asked my grandma today 
"Grandma, where does the unicorn's live?" 
She said, "under your pillow my dear." 
I said, "but why grandma?" 
She said, "because they come out when you have been bad." 
I said, "but grandma my unicorn is cute." 
"He is pink and purple with a smile so big" 
"He has a gold horn on his head and his eyes a baby blue" 
"Why would such a graceful creature want to hurt me grandma?" 
She said, "he doesn't my dear." 
"He is there to make sure you stay out of trouble." 
"Just a little reminder you see." 

Today my grandmother got really sick 
She went to the hospital to stay for awhile 
I had to stay with my aunt who is a meanie 
I have been really bad for her 
But yet I haven't seen my unicorn lately? 
I guess my grandma might know where he is... 
I will have to ask her when she gets home.
