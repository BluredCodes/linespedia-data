Damn him!!!*SMASH*'OHHH God''DRIP DRIP'What have I done to myself?Am I that umbrage over you?I am such a stigma.I let my trust overcome nonenon-entity>'OHHH GOD'My KnucklesBloody Pains of splintered glass particles'DRIP DRIP'Why did you shackle my emotions?Why did you terrorize my soul?Was you just a ruse in my life?I was temerity for loving you.Someone, get me a towel, a bandage. Something!!'Drip Drip'Damn MIRROR!!!Damn YOU!!!!Will this pain unravel you from my thoughts?Will it paint over your harsh quips?Unleash the unsavory lust from my memories.Your not worthy of my pain, or blood you bastard!!!See what you did to me????Look away, damn you!!!!!'OHHH God what a mess.''Drip Drip'Someone call a doctor please!!I think I need stitches.'Drip Drip' ©  on Nov 03 2001 03:08 AM PST, Lee Gillilan   18  • 0  • 1
