---
title: "Anyone Out There?"
writer: "Desdemona"

Day by Day
My past Floats away
Awakening old memories

Time by Time
I wonder If anyone is like me
Happy yet Fucked up

Night by Night
I cry myself to sleep
Can anyone help my demons

Fault by fault
Words but only words
Yet blame your parents for it

Hate on Hate
Dark clouds arise
Crossing the lines of trust

Anyone out there
Anyone in there
Anyone feel like me?
