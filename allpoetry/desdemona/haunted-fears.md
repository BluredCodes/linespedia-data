I captured a moment
A glimpse through the window
My heart skipped short in it's beats
Streaking
Blinking
Complicated images
What could it be?
Do my peepers deceive me?

Imagination runs wild
Like a star crossed child
Get out I shout
LOUDLY

I turn on the lights to see
A old naked man
Curled up in my Dog box
Smiling and glaring at meIn the newspaper there was a story about how this naked man was in this womans doghouse and wouldn't get out so they came and took him to the nut house. hahahah
