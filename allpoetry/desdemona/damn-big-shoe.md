Damn Big Shoe!!!
I heard him exclaim
Not Blaming god 
Not Blaming his disease
No tears in his eyes
Just a little boy
age 10
growing up without what he had
A hip that matched the other side
But he don't cry
He don't stop life
He lives with guilt
afraid of being made fun of
afraid people won't see him as real
real hearted 
a real human being
a man who's heart is very big
full of pride 
forgiveness for others whom are weak
Nothing will stop him now
When he gets mad at himself 
mad at the world
He don't curse life or others
He don't scream at the world
He just holds his breath and bellows out
DAMN BIG SHOE!!
