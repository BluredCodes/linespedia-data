The one thousand day President.
The `death of a thousand cuts'.
Thousand year periods.
Lousy life? Untread.

Things that look for love?
       Earth
       People
       Sky
       moisture in the air.
       Forest? Yes!

What looks for meaning:
       Family
       Employer
       Employees
       hand

Out here past Oort Cloud,
dinning on Ammonia, dirt and ice,
Comet-like.
Swinging wide.
Argus-eyed.
Escapement. Allowing this falling weight!
"Explorer caused a general protection fault."
Sighing.
Trick bait.
