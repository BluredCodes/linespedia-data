Where are these faults
I have distorted?
Growing as proportions
Of no return.
Sewed into my weaknesses,
Weaving it with bitterness,
Shaving off its glory
From where I burn.
Wanting to see myself,
I want to from your view.
I'm sure it's not as critical
And maybe I'm not as pitiful 
As I think I am.
