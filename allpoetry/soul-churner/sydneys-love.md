Her head leans up into the sky,
With arms unfolding out wide
And back down to her side
She tells me,
"Mommy, I love you this much".
I smile to think,
I too, 
Am also five.
My life began the day I had you.
Where the past held nothing but foolish pride,
Sustains now a dignity by having you.
You are my all.
You are Sydney's love.This is for my little girl Sydney. Mommy loves you soooo much.
