title: "What A Prince Is Made Of"

Your compliments are the warmth inside me.

I have my issues

I can not explain. 

Words are like waste

Used up from the beginning.

Sometimes just thrown away

And not retained.

But there are times

I hear you........

In the distance.

Just when I'm captured,

Longing for your comfort,

I see you.............

Holding no resistance,

Fighting my battles 

Like such a warrior.
