---
title: "Vast Beginnings"
writer: "Soul Churner"

Shelter all these past mistakes.
I know my own.
I've grown my own.
Sweltered in a fast beginning,
To makes its clone,
I stay alone;
Become the unknowing.
Caught up in a world still spinning,
I recognize
I'm not reckoned with
Being 2nd best to your demise.
Welcome to what's best forgiven,
Has been forgotten,
Is best unshown.
A view of myself
From lives once lived in,
Was never believed in,
Has never belonged.
