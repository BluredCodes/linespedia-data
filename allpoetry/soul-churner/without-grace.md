---
title: "Without Grace"
writer: "Soul Churner"

I am this journey

That needs to be completed.

I am one that bears too many prejudices.

Against the world,

You and I are the same.

We are good and evil marks along the way.

We would like to be of one sound mind.

I would like to be not wanting.

You would like to be not fearful.

I would like to be one of a kind.

Without grace,

We are nothing but a loud noise in the rain,

Or something more like a thunderstorm.

No, wait,

More like a tornado,

Or maybe even a hurricane.

It's nice having you listen to me as I ramble on.

It's nice to finally be myself around someone.
