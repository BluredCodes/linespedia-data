title: "Not Torn Down"

I'm not torn down.
This ache,
Will not be in my side.
No more like thorns surrounding me,
Cutting through,
Into the ribbons I've once tied.
Spreading out, 
Like the lace that covers me,
Washing clear,
In the face of another.
It is a  keep sake,
Not swelled with pride;
Looking away from the scenery.An old love and lingerie. What a mess of things to write about.
