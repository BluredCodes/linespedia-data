---
title: "I Shall Never Be"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "i-shall-never-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9091-I-Shall-Never-Be-by-Soul-Churner"
---

Screw all of you,
Who always knew
I never gave a damn
About any of your thoughts.
Some of you who never 
Alleviated my mind one bit.
Always just full of shit 
Towards being something you're not.
So opinionated,
Always acting out from your gut instinct.
What the hell is that all about?
Not quite up to your standards,
Am I?
I shall never be.
