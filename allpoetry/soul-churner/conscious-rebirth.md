title: "Conscious Rebirth"

Guided by an imagery

To tear myself away,

From perils once darkened over me,

I portion its voyage astray.

Removing what was karma's cycle,

I rebirth but not repeat.

Cleansing in planes with chains now broken,

I make this conscious complete.
