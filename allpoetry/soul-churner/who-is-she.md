---
title: "Who Is She?"
writer: "Soul Churner"

This lesser beauty has a right to be

Unchallenged for what she is worth. 

Sometimes a fury that hangs around,

Uncompromised unto the earth.

Ungrounded steps make her path.

She waits for no reply.

A drama queen

That makes you laugh,

With perfect harmony magnified.I would like for you to tell me who 'She' is. There are no wrong answers. There are simply your opinions. I want your own interpretation. This is all about what you think.
