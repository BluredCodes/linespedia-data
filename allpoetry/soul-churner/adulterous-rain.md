---
title: "Adulterous Rain"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "adulterous-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let me not listen through these quiet times,
Ones of slanderous echoes that mock my name.
Let my thoughts be ears that block out the sound
Of broken promises whispered through unsacred ties,
Ones made selfish through what was unrestrained,
Ones that made solitude of an adulterous rain.
