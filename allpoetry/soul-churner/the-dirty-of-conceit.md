---
title: "The Dirty of Conceit"
writer: "Soul Churner"

Glamour as an illusion,
Having its toll
Not taken form unto me.
I'm better without intrusion
Of its final course 
Controlling what we see.
Being left unknown,
Not having the graces
To where we could fall.
Inviting to some,
To others are not,
Unpaid by fools who have alot.
Some are sadly brought up this way
Left in its departure of spiritual decay.
