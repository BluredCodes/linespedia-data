---
title: "My New Muse"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "my-new-muse"
source: "AllPoetry"

Fate may lead you through hardships and downfalls, 

But God has given you endurance to cope. 

He framed you sturdy to be in it for a long haul.

He gave you merit as the merchant of hope.

When you are down,

I would like to be uplifting.

With your notions around

Swaying like the air of spring,

Connections are made through the tides of happy endings,

Prompting me a dance by the life you sing.This is for you Sweetbrother.
