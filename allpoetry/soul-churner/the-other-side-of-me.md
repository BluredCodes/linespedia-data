---
title: "The other side of me"
writer: "Soul Churner"

I don't want to be this now,
The fear of who I am.
I want to suppress it down,
Just let it drift away.
Slipping into this other part of me,
Until I'm crashing down
On the other side.
Goodbye uncertainty.
You can not conquer me;
Until I'm crashing down 
On the other side.
