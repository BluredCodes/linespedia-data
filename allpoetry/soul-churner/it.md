---
title: "It"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16550-It-by-Soul-Churner"
---

A train of thought never leaving

Searches for a refuge of no end.

Happy-go-lucky without hesitation

It breathes in and out again.

Like a restless soul grown weary,

I travel farther than beyond.

A place that holds it's future

Gathers me in where I belong.

I become this one being,

The being of me as one

Not stressing of future existance,

Of something that has not begun.

Presently it is I who is speaking.

Who gives a damn of what you say?

It is I,

Who will be open.

It is I,

Who chooses today.

It's all about each encounter;

Each life that touches me.

Giving hope to a new way of thinking,

It's all inspiration to me.
