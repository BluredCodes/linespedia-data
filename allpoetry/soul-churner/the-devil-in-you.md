---
title: "The Devil In You"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "the-devil-in-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19466-The-Devil-In-You-by-Soul-Churner"

While halos hold your horns into place,
I'm the imp not admired by you.
A doll of glass who throws her stones,
Unlike you,
I am looked through.
I'm alright.
I'm okay.
I'm not anyone's first choice, anyway.
I'll be fine,
Not today.
It takes a  while
When I come unglued.
Fate could never mend me,
Not after you.
