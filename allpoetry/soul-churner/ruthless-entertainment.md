title: "Ruthless Entertainment"
writer: "Soul Churner"
slug: "ruthless-entertainment"

Waiting for moments I've hidden from you,
As a chance that never crossed your mind.
Naked are the thoughts still unaware,
Taking numbers as I stand in line.
I keep myself busy while masquerading,
Not as your lover but as a friend.
Guiltless entertainments are just like these
Torrid vibes touching in the wind;
Inappropriate from where we are;
Mixing signals from where we begin.
