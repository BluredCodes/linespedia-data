Seven years were spent waiting for me to be
Expanding perspectives through this one night;
Venturing out from above my anxieties,
Emancipating through encounters of your delight.
Naturally I have not often dared
Yeilded to someone so carelessly.
Educing my nobility, I become impared,
An acceptance of some kind to be unseen
Rearing through circles of fate unprepared,
Seeking counsel over the tranquility.If you look real close you can find a hidden message written out in the first letters of each line. On the last line, the first letter of each word spells out his name. It was quite a night. Go and read "The Undressing". It's a follow up of this one.
