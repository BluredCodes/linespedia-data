You make me willful,
Dressing out into your world.
Come modify me 
Into a motif of lewd expressions.
I'll be your canvas.
Come paint me in your words.
We can be outlandish,
Drinking into a lust unsobered.
Seducing me to induce me 
Into your wave of mobility,
Transforming into hands that are coherent,
Acquiring this knowledge 
By knowing my soul.Yeah, talking about an orgasm here. Well, kind of. You have to know me to know my body. This is what it's really saying.
