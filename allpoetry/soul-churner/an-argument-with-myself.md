Please don't shut me out.I'm not the one Who's looked down on you. I have my patience,But it's thinning out. I have not done you wrong.I compromise sometimes,When you're not telling me What I should do.I share emotions more than body.Why can't you play along?I overburden all my strength.I've settled way too long.And I,Am You,And I,Notice everything.Trade what?My good aspirations,For nothing elseThat matters anyway.Nothing else matters anyway.
