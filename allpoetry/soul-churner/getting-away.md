Thoughts now reach me 
On a back road to home.
Sentenced by an era,
I lose my way again.
You can find me here
Wiping a few tears.
I pretend I don't care
Although it has consumed me.
I'm steadily driving on.
This moment passes soon,
Trading out for its wisdom,
Completing my afternoon.
