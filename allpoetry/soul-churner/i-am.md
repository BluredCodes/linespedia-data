ALONE
I AM ME 
I COPE WITH WHO I AM
I AM THIS CAPACITY 
THAT FACES EACH FEAT MYSELF
IT WEARS ME DOWN TO ACCEPT MYSELF
CLEARING OUT EVERY ADVERSARY
TO THE MANY MENTAL MASSES CARRIED
LIKE OBJECTS CONTAINED 
THEY BECAME CONTRARY 
TO WHO I WAS AS ME
I'M NOT WHO I USED TO BE
I USED TO BE MY BIGGEST NEMESIS
AN EMBODIMENT OF ANIMOSITY
FUELED BY SELF ILL WILL
ILL WILL OF MY OWN ENMITY
TRANSFERRING MIRRORS ALWAYS APPEARED
APPEARING I'VE SQUANDERED TOO MANY YEARS
GAVE ITS WAY UNTO THE MANY FEARS  
THROUGH THE CRACKS OF ADOLESCENCE
UNTO THE HANDS OF IDLENESS
BUT NOW
IF THROUGH THE CHANGE OF TIME I ERR
MAY IT DETATCH ITS WAY FROM ALL THAT'S AWARE
IN TIMES WHETHER I SUCCEED OR SUFFER THROUGH
IN TIME DEPICTING MOMENTS OF THINGS I CAN'T UNDO
