His form burned out the murkiness of twilight
To etch memories of a monument of perfection.
He lit quite a vision for my fatigued eyesight,
Stretching out into this chiseled mass. 
Smooth and hard and yet unscarred,
Firmed by nature that has been kind.
Giving me his complete and undivided attention
