PUSHING FROM THE INSIDE OUT,
HAVE YOU COME TO FIX ME?
MINUS ALL THE MINOR DETAILS,
ARE YOU TO SAY I'M BROKEN?
SHARPENING YOUR RAZOR EDGE,
YOU'RE WANTING TO REPLACE ME.
WANTING TO FILL IN THE CRACKS,
YOUR MOLD CAN NOT ERASE ME.
WAITING FOR YOU TO COMPREHEND,
NOT WAITING FOR YOUR APPROVAL.
I WILL NEVER LET YOU IN.
I AM ALWAYS OPEN.
