Drip, Drop, gone my painNumbly unaware I've gone insane...The Dragons back and he's here to stay...for its fire with whom I choose to playHe was not invited he barged right in~His evil games no man can win...He demands me to forfeit...I give in. I give up...To see that now overfloweth the cupI drink, and I drink,But I'm never full...I try to leave but he strengthens his pullTime passes and we begin to bindwhen he finally let's go, I cant pull away, we've become entwined
