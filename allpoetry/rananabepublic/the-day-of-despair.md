Weeping, mourning, praying 
These are just a few emotions that are pouring. 
Planes flying aimlessly to destroy 
Humanity and American Freedom 

A faceless coward 
Walking bodies covered in dust 
Clouds of ashes filling the sky 
Are just a few of the terrible scenes 
Stuck in our minds 

Life is fragile and indifferent to death 
Why must society try maliciousness? 
What does it accomplish? 
Broken hearts, shattered families, and cracked memories 
For all have been affected. 

Declaration’s of Liberty and Allies 
Have been established 
Making the United States not crushed, 
But stronger, more spirited, and together 

This is our Day of Triumph 
As we will prove victorious 
And show the terrorists that the United States 
Will not give up, but persevere through the toughest of times.
