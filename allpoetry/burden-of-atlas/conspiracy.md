Trapped, no way out
I hope they turn to dust
Now I just want to shout
Who can I still trust

I can't trust anyone after this mess
My best friend disagreed with me, what a burn
In front of other friends no less!
Said I was being childish and stubborn..

Well, I still think I'm cool, that's what I see
A real friend would always agree with me
So what if I said the sky was green, it could be!
I can't believe everyone laughed, it's a damned conspiracy!

Well, they won't have the last laugh, nope
I'll just sit here and hang with me
And they'll feel bad and apologize, I hope
And if they are worthy, I'll accept and agreePeople really need to grow up and put their egos aside sometimes.
