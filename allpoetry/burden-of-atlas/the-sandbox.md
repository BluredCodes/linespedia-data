title: "The Sandbox"

The sun shines down
On a boy in his sandbox
as he builds a sandcastle
He loves the sun
He loves his sandbox

With childish innocence he works, caring not for his future
He could become a doctor and save life
Or he could become a soldier and take life away
He could become a rich, successful businessman
Or he could become a poor homeless man on the street
But for right now, all he cares about is his sandcastle

Wow!  This is the best sandcastle I've ever built!
I'm gonna go get mom and show it to her!
And as he runs to the house, all he thinks about is
I love the sun
I love my sandbox
