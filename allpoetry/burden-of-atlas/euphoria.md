---
title: "Euphoria"
writer: "Burden of Atlas"

The burning sensation of the needle in the vein
The following absence of everything, including pain

A world of chaos and disorder, simplified with a fix
Gone is the urge to beat your tormentors with bricks

As intangible lights and shapes explode in front of your eyes
A certain symmetry and reasoning seems to form while you fantasize

Lying on the couch, unmoving, completely dead to the world
Secretly smiling as the solutions to your problems are unfurled

This is the salvation left to those without love or hope
But they'll gladly cling to it, as opposed to hanging from a rope...
