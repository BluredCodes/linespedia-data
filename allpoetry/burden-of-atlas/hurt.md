title: "Hurt"

My friend hurt me.
Why did they do it?
Did they mean to?   No.
Does it still hurt?   Yes.
Will it happen again?  Probably.

Why do people have to hurt others?
Is it in our nature?
Do we not understand what we're doing?
Is it inevitable that whenever we love, we will get hurt?
Why must causing pain come as easy to us as eating, sleeping, breathing?

My friend says they're sorry.  I believe them.
My friend says they'll change.  I don't believe them.
Can they change?  Is change asking too much?
Should I change?  Can I?
Why do people hurt?
I don't know.Dedicated to my friends Tim and Carolyn.
