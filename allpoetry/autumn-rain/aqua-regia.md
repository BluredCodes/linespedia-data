It falls not as a joyous rain
Though not that of evil and dark
It simply falls to wash away
The blackness from the world
It falls to purge the land of evil
So it must melt you into nothing
As though aqua regia falling on your headAqua regia is latin for water of the king's. It was the only acid to be able to disolve pure gold. It is very powerful.
