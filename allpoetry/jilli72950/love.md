So many signs point to Jeremy,
Trust me they do.
But how can I love you
when love isn't true?

Madonna states it plainly,
It's just something that we do.
But you won't do it,
and don't ask me to.

The flowers are wilting,
and all of the clocks ticking.
I don't understand you,
none of this is clicking.

You're master of the ATA,
Yea, I got it the first time.
But I'm not all that interested,
Should that be such a crime?

We most definatly are drifting,
and I know you feel it, too.
You're just such a guy, 
and it's all about you.

You need someone hyper,
who rarely is calm.
I need someone else,
who's not all about their lip balm.

I don't know how to tell you,
that it's not gonna happen.
'Cause you're talkin' of love,
and your jaws are flappen'.I understand it's not the best thing in the world, but I mainly wrote this because I'm having problems with my b/f. I'd rather not have to worry about relationships. I thought this would help me but it hasn't. If you can,  then please tell me what to do!
