MUST BREAK THIS TENSION!
You're too serious!!!
Your silence equals seriousness
your seriousness equals sadness
your sadness equals tears
my tears equals my death
Why do you speak of eager whales wallowing
in an ocean of tears?
Do your sad memories bring you to sullen reality?
Does it make you feel good?
Perhaps if you would except it..
I know too...
but no, nay, uh uh
Give it up
Not your past or you life or sweet ripeness
But give up your innosence
or what you think is so
I have
and you should too
