Sitting together watching, waiting, listening.
A cough,a sneeze,a yawn. Crunches of popcorn,
sips of Mr. Pibb. A smile on my face as I smell
this wonderfull cologne. Although just a tee-shirt and shorts...
He changed. The black and kahkis were wonderfully adorable.
No matter. Space between our bodies this time.
But my left hand and his right hand held tightly tgether.
Stroking his strong arm, feeling his breath and smelling the cologne.
Having no scent myself but of shampoo. Vanilla.
Artificial Intellegence on the screen. The room almost filled.
Abut fifty or sixty people. But to me it's just us two.
Still, I haven't tasted his lips yet. So perfect though.
Not ment for me though, but for whom I wonder. Sweety knows....That's
my Jean P. I've had a crush on her for about two months now.
So sweet, and uninnocent, smart and all herself.
Oh well
