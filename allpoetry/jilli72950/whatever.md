Enjoying the flow of things
Black roses and crying children
chipped and fading nailpolish
shatterd glass on the kitchen floor

Inkless pens and penless secretaries
cheating on your wife with the cat
The cat,ha, what a cute little name for your blond
No invitation for me though.

Broken hearts or heartless brokes?
Idoly thinking or thinking idoly
'But those flowers stay purple' too bad
Dirty sheets on the bed, or under it

I need to was my face before my hands
Or my hands before my face,  whichever
Pentograms and Holographs, what's a holograph?
Words mean nothing, nothing and everything.

Is anything what it seems? Or did the seams rip?
Leaving a black whole waiting to be filled.
The scars on my back are getting bigger
Stingin, hating, screaming, weeping....play.
