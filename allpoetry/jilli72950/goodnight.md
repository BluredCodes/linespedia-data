My poor hands shake with fear as the silence envelopes me.
You'd see nude newspaper, she saw wrapping paper. How creative
to look beyond her eyes.

I gaze now, lying in my bed, up at my ceiling.
Though it looks very much like a padded wall.
Perhaps to keep me sand, for insanity of my
pent up feelings for you are slowing taking way.

My chipped fingernails stare at me as I like thinking,
yes you were at the movies. Last, and this. The showing with the
pretty elven folk, right? You and Bridget, and during Harry P.

'Twas a magical time, that night, when I first felt for you.
Sitting upon thine legs in a completly crowded room, yes, I felt
for you then. Genuine perplexity with such interesting features.

You hair-each strand its own, your eyes
so concentrated, so happy yet with such painful
sadness.  Your lips with quivering madness and 
your hands. So skilled at what they love.

For you each day 
to my dearest love
goodnight
