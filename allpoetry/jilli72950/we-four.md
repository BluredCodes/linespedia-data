Jean
  brown eyed
     black shirted
         smirk of the moon.
               Does thou knowest thy destination???


             2 o'clock AM

Clickety clack with my keyboard
    to Britt Cagnina.
          Blue eyed
             lovely shaped
                  Italian mock of my eye!
        SMILE PRETTY FOR ME

 ME??? Ugh, 
   sound of distaste which rolls off my tongue and is handed 
to pants....
 PANTS???
Ah yes, Jason, Are you really Catholic?
      or are you tasting a savory morsel in mid-morning?
              No clickety clack from you though,
                   'least one not noticable....
