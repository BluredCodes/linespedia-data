She smiled still so sweetly
with a knife in one dead hand
a slash a gash beneath her chin
yet still her smile so innocent

She didn't know her white shirt-red
or that her vision-lost
but all she seemed to understand
was it was all my fault

Her crazy eyes did shift to mine
and her teeth white stained red
her weet smile dimmed to grin
as she called for me to come

I felt a pain as I did stir
to leave this fearful thought
waking in the mid of night
to find my sanity gone
