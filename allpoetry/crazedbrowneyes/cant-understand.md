---
title: "Can't Understand"
writer: "CrazedBrownEyes"
slug: "cant-understand"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51693-Cant-Understand-by-CrazedBrownEyes"
---

Can't understand what happened today,
the reasons for your going away.
It makes no sense, to me, or you,
of your leaving us, gee, who knew.

Can't understand just what I feel,
why I feel sick, and why I reel.
It makes no sense to me, or them,
why we won't ever see you again.

Can't understand the reasons why,
you had to go ahead and die,
it's not your fault, I know that well,
but here without you, still I dwell.

Can't understand if you're OK, 
why someone made you leave that day,
how someone could take your life away,
when we all wanted you to stay.

Can't understand a lot of things,
all these questions life still brings.
I know not where your spirit roams,
but this I understand the most,
that somewhere out there you still dwell,
keeping happy, keeping well.
