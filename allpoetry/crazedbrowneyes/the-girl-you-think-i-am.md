title: "The Girl You Think I Am"
writer: "CrazedBrownEyes"

I'm not the girl you think I am,
I'm not smart, I just ask why.

I'm not the girl you think you know,
I don't laugh I only cry.

I'm not the girl you love so much,
I almost always lie.

I'm not the girl you think I am,
I can't imagine why...
