---
title: "Let Them Come"
writer: "CrazedBrownEyes"
slug: "let-them-come"
source: "AllPoetry"

So, they think we're really crazy,
let them come.
So, they think we're wrong,
let them come.
So, they think our love won't last,
let them come.

So, they don't believe in us,
we believe.
So, they never understand us,
but we do.
So, they're ignorant about us,
but we know.

So, they want to try to fight us,
let them come.
So, they think we're weak,
let them come

So, they won't come to us....
We'll go to them.
