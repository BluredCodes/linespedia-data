Who'm I doing this for?
the grades, the papers all add up.
I don't do this for my health,
but I don't do it for you.

Who'm I doing this for? 
My parents? 
They don't know half the time,
my friends?
there's very few that understand,
teachers?
Sure,it could be, but probably not.

Who'm I doing this for?
I give what the world expects of me,
good work, 
responsibility,
the mind that can question many an answerthat Alex Trebeck can give,
but who do I do it all for?
Not for me,
not for you,
then who?

I do it because it's my role,
It's the part I was given in this play called life,
I guess that's the nearest answer.
But if that's not the reeason,
then, what is?
