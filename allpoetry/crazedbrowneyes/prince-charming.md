title: "Prince Charming"

Nobody ever realizes prince charming isn't there,
Nobody ever listnes and nobody seems to care.
Everybody searches to find him at any cost,
but when you think you've found him, that's when you've probly lost.
We spread these happy rumors all acroos our little land,
of Prince Charming the magnificent, the grandest of the Grand.
We seem to think that our prince charming can find us here or there,
but won't you be surprised to find he isn't anywhere.
But if you happen to find him all by your lonsome self,
tell me where I can find one, don't just hog all of the wealth.
