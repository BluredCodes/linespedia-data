I don't wanna change the world,
that's never been my wish.
I don't wanna turn any heads,
it sound's stupid now, I know.
I don't care about recognition,
I don't need it.
I'm probably one of those people you walk past everyday,
but you don't notice me,
because I don't want to be noticed.
I place my thoughts on paper,
I shout my understandings out,
for anyone to hear who wants to.
But if no one hears,
who am I to care.
You see you don't make me, 
I don't make you. 
We make ourselves,
choose to notice our surroundings,
we don't make ourselves, 
but we choose what does make us up.
That's what makes us all unique.
I don't wanna change the world,
that's your job.Well, not the best, but it'll have to do
