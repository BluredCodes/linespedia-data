live life to the fullest,
be true to yourself, 
never be foolish,
share all the wealth,

be cool and be kind,
never tell a lie,
be smart, never shy,
and if people ask why,
calmly walk by and,
say Live and Let DieHa, I was listening to that song by Paul, from the beatles duh, and had to add it in....
