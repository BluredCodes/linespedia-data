My guy is special,
not too tall,
he's there tho catch me,
if I fall.

My guy is different,
has a brain,
always interesting,
never lame,

My guy is brilliant,
very smart,
he has good manners,
never tart.

My guy is sweet,
likes to hug,
he's never mean,
never smug.

My guy's the best,
through and through,
before I met him,
never knew.

My guy is perfect,
what I mean,
is well..... um... yeah, he's
in my dreams.ok, forget the "tart" part, I just wanted it to rhyme..... te he
