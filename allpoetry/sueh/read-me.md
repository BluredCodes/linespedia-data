title: "Read Me"
writer: "sueh"

I am a book
Waiting to be read
Waiting for you to take me off the shelf
Caress my covers
With interest
Peek into the spaces
Inside where the important things live
Opening me gently
With curiosity and need to know
More about me
Unfold my pages, laying them before you
For your inspection
For your eyes to devour my every part
Swallowing the essence of me
As you move thru my words
Until you are engorged
With all I have to give you
Pushing now into crevices of my mind
As yet unexplored
Pages turning more quickly
As your passion for me rises and I open
Myself more widely to allow 
You to delve
More deeply 
To read more quickly
To rush ardently toward that ending that we
Both desire so much
We both know
It is
Not The End
But 
To be continued
