title: "The Verdict"

Ugly pain 
Dripping from my heart 
Like semen 
Running down the leg of some 
Sore-ridden crack whore 
Standing on this 
Corner 
Of indecision and confusion 
Hawking my wares 
To the un-wanting 
Selling my soul 
To the un-buying 
Crying out for the 
Next fix 
While eyes search for the 
Next trick 
I exhaust the last traces 
Of my idealism 
I give away the last 
specks of my 
Innocence 
And still 
Here 
I 
Stand
awaiting the verdict
