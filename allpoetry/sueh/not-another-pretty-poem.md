Reaching….
Trying For sexy
Only 
Achieving naughty
Nasty
Down and dirty
Verbal porn
Fit for late 
Nights in dingy
Motel rooms
For cheap sex
In dirty alleyways
Standing and grunting
Against a 
Cold brick wall
Undulating
Pelvis
Against 
Grinding hips
Semen
Running down
Churning thighs
As a cop
Patrolling the street
Yells
And you turn
Exposed
While I laugh
And wiggle 
Out of sight
Around 
Another corner
Pausing
Straightening my skirt
Smoothing
My top
Trying for sexy…..
