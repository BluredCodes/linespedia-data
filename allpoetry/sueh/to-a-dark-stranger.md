title: "To A Dark Stranger"
writer: "sueh"

Your man voice calls
To the woman
Inside me
Sending thrill messages
By smoke signal
To the core
Of 
My being
Your heart voice
Beats a tattoo
Of passion
Thrumming my woman skin
Sending damp
Words
To dry places
Causing floods of thought
To run 
Thru me
Lighting blazing want fires
To be 
Fanned higher
Or 
Doused with neglect
At your
whim
