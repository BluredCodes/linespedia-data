---
title: "Thank You Baby"
writer: "sueh"

Mama always told me to say thank you
When someone gives you something nice
And you
Have given me nights filled with passion
Sweatdrenchedwetsheeted
Mind numbing 
Passion
And I thank you
You have given me dreams that leave me shaking
Panting and starving for your touch
When you aren’t even near
And I thank you
You’ve made my life fuller just by being in it
And if I forget sometimes
How much I owe you
For services already rendered
And if
I forget sometimes to tell you
How much I miss you when you go 
And how 
Happy I am when you return
Just come to me
In the deep of the night
And I promise to thank you
