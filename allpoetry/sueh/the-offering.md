title: "The Offering"
writer: "sueh"

Body trembling
She kneels
Breasts heavy with desire
Panting in barely
Concealed anticipation she
Raises her self to him
Open
For his inspection
His reflection
Wet 
She feels his eyes enter her
Reaching deep
Caressing
Places
Untouched by other eyes
She quivers
Lips parted slightly
She lowers
Her upper body while
Raising herself
Higher
An offering to his man eyes
Eyes that penetrate
Eyes that 
Lap
At her moisture
That tease at her lips
That gently pry 
Them 
Apart so that the moisture
Reaches him
And he lovingly
Touches the first drop
With his tongue
And she sighs

Knowing what is to come
