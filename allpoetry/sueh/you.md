I saw your name on an email 
And I felt my body 
Respond 
Felt myself 
Opening a bit, dampening a bit 
Squirming in my chair 
Trying to get 
A little more comfortable 
A little more 
Accessible? 
Wishing you were here 
With your words 
But knowing too that they would 
Be enough 
This time 
Knowing that before I finish reading 
What you have to say 
I will be 
Ready, hot and ready 
Wet and ready 
Nipples hard and standing up 
Waiting 
For the touch 
For the loving touch 
That you imply 
That you describe 
That you 
Make me feel with your words 
You are sex 
Incarnate 
You are a cyber lover 
Beyond compare 
Your words 
Pierce me 
Impale me 
Fill me with longing 
And then 
Satisfy that longing 
Word 
By 
Beautiful word 
Thank you
