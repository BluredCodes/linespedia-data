title: "Disclosures"

I wander
Far from the normal conclusions
Searching
Under rocks of preconception
Behind walls of misunderstandings
Peeking up the skirts
Of censorship
Seeking a glimpse of knowledge
Hidden
Under petticoats voluminous
With deception
hoping to find
Enlightenment
I’m disillusioned
With life
And its hidden secrets
I’m  frightened
Of death
And its awful
disclosures
