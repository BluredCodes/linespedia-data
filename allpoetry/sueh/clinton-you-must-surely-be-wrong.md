Clinton said
That getting head was not having sex
Mmmm
If you are in front of me
As I kneel
Between your legs
Gently clasping 
You in my hand
If I run
My tongue
Up
And around your manhood
Swallowing you
Deeply
As I caress you with my throat
Taking you
Into me
As far as you want to go
Are we not
Then
Having sex?
I think that if you closed
Your eyes
After reading my words
And saw me in your minds eye
Felt me in your
Lower regions
Imagined me in just that position
Then yes
We have just had sex
I hope it
Was good for you

It was for me
