Your words come nudging
My knees apart
As I sit reading in this lonely chair

Whispering

“Let me come in lady fair
Let me dribble wet words onto your 
Damp thighs
Let my sighs travel up your
Legs
Gently parting them as I go
Let me mumble
Sweet nothings against your lips
That part for me
So that your pinkness comes out
To listen to what I say
To answer 
With erect honesty
As my words brush so softly
And my breath
Kisses 
So lovingly”

And I sigh, eager to hear it all

Lovely words
From your lips
To mine
