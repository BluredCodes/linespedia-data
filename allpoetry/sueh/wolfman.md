Wolfman

I am your aggressive male
Your stalker
Your nighttime lover
I catch the woman scent of you in the air
And the hair on my neck
Stands erect
I sniff
Growl deep in my throat
And follow
Your musk, your womanness
To where you 
Wait
Haunches raised
Open and wet
Ready to be mounted
Ready 
To be taken
To the wild places
Where the animal in us howls
And we mate
Hungry and frantic
My thrusts
Coming faster and deeper
As your voice
Raises up to meet mine
A crescendo
Of sex and passion
Loud 
In the silence of the nightI'm a woman, but i like to write from the male point of view at times, just to be sure i never forget that they have one
