title: "Dangling"

8 years old
At the school playground
I would climb to the highest rungs
Of the jungle gym
Dangle upside down 
Precariously
Looking at the other kids
Hoping they were 
Looking at me
Seriously
It was important
To be recognized, to be accepted
Just to be noticed
Was worth the scrapes and bruises
At 8 years old
30 years later, I’m still dangling here
Maybe not upside down but
Precariously
Hoping to be taken seriously
Looking for acceptance
From my peers
Curiously, it still matters to me
It’s kind of sad
How little has changed
In 30 years
I’m still that lonely little girl
Looking at faces
Hopefully
Wanting to see what?
I’m not sure
Exactly
Just hoping to see
Someone else
A little bit like me
