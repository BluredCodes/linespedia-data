Sometimes you have to decideWhere to draw the lineThe one between love and lustSet it straight in your mindYou know what you want But you can't always have bothSo right now I've decidedYour skin I must explore with my mouthDon't play any gamesDon't pull any stringJust give me total passion And straight to ecstasy I will bringLet it be wild Let it be realOh Baby, make me screamOh Baby , make me feelWe all have our needsSo let our bodies slam like a truckDon't try to fight itCome on Baby, lets just FUCKMJMP7:11am
