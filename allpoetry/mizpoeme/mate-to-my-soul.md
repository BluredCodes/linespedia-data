I can't help but stare at youYou are reading the words of my heartCan this be real, not a dreamIn my life I imagine you a permanent partMaybe I'm falling to fastBut they always say you will knowI think you are the oneThe mate to my soulYou actually listen when I speakAnd I hang on your every wordThis is all so newIt's exciting, so absurdIt came right out , it felt so naturalAre you shocked I said I love youI can't believe what I hearYou say you love me tooThis is the first time I walked in without fearBut you have made me feel safe and lovedCherished and dearMJMP12:04am
