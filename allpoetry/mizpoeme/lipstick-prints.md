Unexpected
I didn't think I'd be the one to fall
Trying to remain casual
I really had no say at all
Life is funny that way
Always full of surprise
Wanting to be near you
Feelings in disguise
Everyday a litter closer
But still the invisible wall
You had won me over
As I tried not to fall
Reality sets in
As we are so far apart
Distance comes between us
As my head fights with my heart
Stolen kisses
Lipstick prints
Wanting just a moment longer
Romance caught a glimpse
Was it all real
Or simply just a dream
Sometimes you must look a little deeper
As things are not always as they seem
MJMMP
