---
title: "Your Sanctuary"
writer: "Mizpoeme"

Do I frighten you as your thoughts I stalk

As you sleep I am not

You belong to the day I the night

I thrive on darkness you on light

Somewhere we have a haunting connection

Our journeys are different but in the same direction

You and I are as black is to white

You lay there dreaming as I write

But late at night your mind I trace

My mind you care not to face

I know we are creatures of the same

We created the rules we play the game

The words I write are of your dreams

Your sanctuary is not what it seems

I hold the power to possess your thought

Unaware your sub conscience leaves the door unlocked

I sneak I slither deep inside

There is no escape no where to hide

I cannot be locked out I am with you as you sleep

Your thoughts are mine there are no secrets you can keep

MJMP
