title: "~*~Thoughts Of You, My Love~*~"

How I dream of drowning in your passion and inhaling your love, ingraining it forever in my body's memory. Your hands like the warmth of the flames, your breath a sweet summer breeze. Your eyes a tranquil sea of blue, your words, a perfect verse. My heart pounds and you run like the blood through my veins. To be forever the keeper of my heart. You come to me in dreams and occupy my soul, this heart waits for you longing your arrival and just as quickly it breaks with the rise of the morning sun.

MJMP
