What is she looking for She's so messed upYou can't understand her way of thinkingShe doesn't give a fuckShe always gets what she wantsShe's such a manipulatorShe has no fearShe's a determined crusaderYou ask her why She'll only answer with liesThe truth is not realIt is faded by nervous alibisYou want to read her mindShe'll even look you right in the eyeShe only answers when she wants toBut still you ask her whyMJMP
