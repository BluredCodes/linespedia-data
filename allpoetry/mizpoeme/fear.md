To open up and bare your naked soul
To express ones thoughts and feeling
Makes us vounerable
The biggest fear of all
For someone to see what we really are
To let someone else inside your mind
Makes us vounerable
The biggest fear of all
To be honest and not afraid to show the real you
To be open up and risk being judged
Makes us strong
Why are we afraid
To see someone's fears thoughts and pains
To be given the privilage to see the inner mystery
Makes us strong
I'm not afraid anymore
MJMP
