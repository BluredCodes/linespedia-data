title: "I am"

I am a woman

I radiate from with in

Touch me I am soft

Silk describes my skin

My spirit is strong

My eyes are sharp

My voice is sweet

Like music from a harp

I am a woman

Strong and proud

I have something to say 

My voice is loud

My mind is power 

It can set you at ease

My heart is pure

It may bring you to your knees

I am a woman

I can make you dream

Even when things are not as they seem

Give me your hand

Your heart I may steal

If you dare to come closer

My words you may feel

I am a woman
