---
title: "The Test Of Time"
writer: "Mizpoeme"
slug: "the-test-of-time"

I would give you anything
If only there was a way
I would say the words to mend your heart
If only I knew what words to say

I will forever love you
And You will remain in my heart
A love not to be forgotten
Even when time has kept us apart

Maybe with a little time
Our love will continue to grow
But without this time
We may never know

Please don't close your heart
Nor forget your love for me
Time will give the answers
If we are truly meant to be

So remember My Love
As I set you free
If my heart calls for you
I pray you'll come back to me
