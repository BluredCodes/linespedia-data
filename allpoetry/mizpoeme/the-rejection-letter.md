Remember me of course you don'tYou wouldn't give me the time of dayYou couldn't spare five minutes To listen to what I had to sayBut don't you worryI haven't lost any sleepBut here's a little advice The best I ever keepCompassion and courtesy Will not cost you a dimeBut people will remember youWhen all they asked for was a minute of your timeThat is why I'm bigger than youRemember that when you take a second lookI'm the one across the tableAutographing this bookMJMP 13/965:04pm
