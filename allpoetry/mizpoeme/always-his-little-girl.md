---
title: "Always His Little Girl"
writer: "Mizpoeme"
slug: "always-his-little-girl"

My father through my eyes
He is compasionate and strong
A hero in my eyes
The man who taught me right from wrong

Be careful Sweetheart, don't let him break your heart
Is what he always says
I know caring for me has become a habit
And this is one of his ways

Today his hair is silver
And his smile is disguised
Even if he doesn't smile much
There's always a twinkle in his eyes

I remember how he'd hold my tiny hand
Or carry me up to bed at night
How he'd kiss my scrapes and bruises
Making everything alright

Now I'm all grown up
His shirts aren't so over sized
I'm too big to carry up the stairs
But I'll always be his little girl through my fathers eyes

MJMP
