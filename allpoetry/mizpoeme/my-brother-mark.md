#22 MacPhail is his name
His one true love is the hockey game
Devoted to the game,team and to score the goal
Giving it all with heart and soul
One day I will always remember
My 11th birthday the 16th of November
My birthday wish a goal for each year
He took over the ice in full gear
He scored the goals again and again
Making me the proudest sister and #1 fan.
