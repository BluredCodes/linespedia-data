I have a desire that burns deep insideA feeling of lust I just cannot hideAt night in my bed I toss and turn in my sleepI'm a slave to this dream I'm in so deepI dream of your warm hands on my skinThe feeling of your hot tongue licking me deep withinMy sweet juice covers your faceAs you rise from that special placeYour mouth wonders from my wetness to my breastThen you kiss your way back down to give me your bestMaking me drip under your spellSucking licking teasing you do it so wellYou brace your hands around my hipsI grab your head to pull you deeper into my lipsBuild and tease build and teaseMaking me tremble making me weak at the kneesAs I begin to explode I through my head back letting out a screamI wake myself up only to find things are not always what they seem
