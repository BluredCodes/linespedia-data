I dreamed of you lastnight

Were you aware as you crept into my slumber?

Into the privacy of my dreams

Can you sense me?

Dreaming of you?

 

I shaved  my legs today. 

I was going to have them waxed but shaved them anyway.

The hot water streamed down my naked body

I lathered up with orange smoothie shower gel.

Mmmm smells good enough to eat.

Skin soft and smooth as I wonder how your touch will feel on my body.

Can you sense me?

Dreaming of you?
