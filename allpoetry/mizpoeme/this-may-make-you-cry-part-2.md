I say I never really loved you
I'm also glad you are gone
Our love was just an a lie
Part of my life that went wrong

I hear your name in the crowd
I won't even blink an eye
I'm such a great pretender
I've even convinced you to believe this lie

If I ever see you 
Walking down the street
I'll treat you like a stranger
One I'll never meet

Late at night when I am alone
The walls close in on me
I can't hide from the truth
But my tears you'll never see

I still love you ONLY
I deny it but can't explain why
I'm such a great pretender
I've even convinced you to believe this lie
