---
title: "Screaming In Silence"
writer: "Mizpoeme"
slug: "screaming-in-silence"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

There is a feeling of anxiety that has swept over me
A feeling I must overcome, I need to be free
I want to scream I'm out of conrtrol
I can't seem to break from this desperate hold
I want to run, I want to be wild
But I'm being held back, I feel helpless and mild
Someone please tell me how to get away
Please speak louder I cannot hear what you say
The voices are screaming in my ear
But I can't make them out, they are unclear
Be silent, for I have found a voice, it guides me to the light
This is my voice, telling me it's my life, my fight
Suddenly I realize nothing is holding me here
Except for my own denial, it is my only fear
I was holding myself back, now I see
The answers I was searching for are found inside of me
