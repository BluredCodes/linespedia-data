Words they have many meaningsMy pen has taken controlMy mind is working overtimeThe words just seem to flowAny thought or experience Can be turned into rhymeThey have a way of escapingThey will take their meaning with timeSometimes I just cannot stopI have a compulsion to write what I feelThese thoughts from my mind My pen will stealThese words hold a certain mysteryThink of that as you read what I writeThe have escaped from my mindUsually deep with in the nightMJMP4:36am
