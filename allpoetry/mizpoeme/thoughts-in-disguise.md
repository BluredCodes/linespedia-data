The lights are dim
The music is loud
But I am detached
I am floating on a cloud
Everyone is speaking
But I haven't heard a word they have said
I pretend to listen
Though the words are not entering my head
I'm wondering where you are now
Will you show up tonight
Without you here there is something missing
It just doesn't feel right
The same old conversations
As I wonder what you are doing now
Are you thinking of me
Really theses thoughts I shouldn't allow
I look up from my paper
And you appear before my very eyes
I guess someone was listening 
To my thoughts in disguise
