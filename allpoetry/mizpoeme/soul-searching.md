Some travel the worldIn search of their soulOthers lay sedentaryAfraid to let goFears never to be revealedAfraid to take a chanceWhile others are fearlessNot missing a danceInventors of their destinyChasing a dreamGoing in circlesSwimming up streamNever giving upOn reaching that goalBut when the search is overThey never had far to goThere is no need for distanceOr sailing a seaWhen searching for ones soulJust search inside of theeMJMP4:22am
