Today is the last day I am a victim
No longer will I wear societies chains
I will not be their slave 
Their pretty little tortured slut
I have given into my desires
I am the creator of my thoughts
You do not have to understand
That is not my concern
I won't feel guilt or shame
For speaking my mind
I am accountable fo my actions
Say what you like about me
Think your small thoughts
You never crossed my mind
Do I offend you 
Maybe because I am free
Or because of the way I choose my life?
But probally is it because I am not like you?
Accept me for who I am ..or not
I accept you
Stop judging because I am not what you want me to be
I have no apoligies
I have my own set of rules, values
I created myself, I know who I am
Can you honestly say the same?
Dig Deep
