Do you feel me hard pressed from behind?Let looses the fantasies in your mindYou are so gorgeous hardly a childYou are definitely all man Strong and wildGive it up do as I sayGive in to me let me have my wayMy hands are hot like a burning flame I promise after this you will never be the sameDo you know how bad I want you?I know you want me tooI slide between your thighsLet your mind go crazy as you close your eyesAfter I slide up and we are face to faceAs close as possible our bodies embraceI want to unlock your desires deep with inTo drive you wild as my mouth explores your skinLay back let it all goLet me loose I'll teach you all I knowAs I show you exactly what I meanI whisper 'Open your eyes this isn't a dream.'MJMP3:04am
