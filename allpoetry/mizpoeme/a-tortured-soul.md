With the locket you gave meI scrape the etched frost that covers the windowThrough blurry eyes I grieve for my wounded heartThat you have trampled and thrown awayI fight to erase the memory of you and IThat I once held so dearI dam my tortured soul that clutchesAnd remembers this twisted painAs I fight to forget your existenceMJMP2/5/971:14am
