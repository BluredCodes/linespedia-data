---
title: "Animal Attraction"
writer: "Mizpoeme"
slug: "animal-attraction"
source: "AllPoetry"

Once in a while you will meet a certain man
And you sense a reaction
You automatically want him
You can't explain it's animal attraction
All you can think about is the way he makes you feel
You have a desire to taste his flesh
If only to convince yourself he's real
Every time your eyes meet
Between your legs you get wet
Your heart begins to race 
And your skin gets soaked with sweat
You know only he holds the power
To satisfy you to make you scream
It's animal attraction I think you know what I mean
You can't think straight
Your imagination is on fire
You know the flames won't go out
Until he quenches this desire
