As the ink begins to flow
I hear my pen whisper ' Where did you go?'
Your words have not floated across the page 
For what seems to be a million days

I've missed your words and thoughts so true
Life is precious and memories few
Share with me your thoughts and views
Even if you have no news

A writers spirit never dies
Life is captured through their eyes
To be transformed across the page
This blanket of white is the writers stage

So welcome back my trusted friend
It is so good to read you once again
You may not write for fame or glory
Just fill the page and tell your story
MJMP
6:11am
