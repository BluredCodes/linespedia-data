---
title: "You Can't Play a Player"
writer: "Mizpoeme"

You think I'm here waiting for you

Well I'm here but not alone

He was right here beside me

When I picked up the phone



You said you couldn't make it

That something's going down

I don't know her name

But I know she lives downtown



Do you really think I'm a fool

I know what's going on

Don't be surprised

When you are alone because I'm already gone



Tell me do you think about me 

When you make her scream

You can't play a player

And things aren't always what they seem
