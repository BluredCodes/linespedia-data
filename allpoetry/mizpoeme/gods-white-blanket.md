title: "God's White Blanket"

Here I lean on my window sill
The beauty of this sight warms me even with the chill
The snow is frozen with a diamond crust
To share this sight it is a must
The air is cold causing the rain to freeze
Making it glisten and sparkle on the trees
The dead of night has fallen
The winds voice whistling and calling
Darkness comes to life with the full moon's glow
Peaceful and mystical is the snow
The stars are on fire in the sky
Some are oblivious to this beauty, I can't understand why
I am encased in such beauty all around
When god's white blanket covers the ground

MJMP
