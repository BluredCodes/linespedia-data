Your strong warm hand encases mine tenderly. Your fingers interlock with mine, making a connection. Brushing the hair from my eyes. You place your thumb on my lips sliding over them then placing your hands on my face. I close my eyes as my body prepares for the first touch of your soft pink lips. Our mouths come together, the feeling of you sends sensations throughout my entire body. You prolong the actual kiss by just rubbing your lips against mine. I'm swept away on a tingling rush. Holding my face gently between the palms of your hands as gentle as a dove nestling on your fingertips. Then you open your mouth, your tongue feels cool in my hot mouth but tastes sweet as our juices mingle. You explore the inside of my mouth. a Simple kiss, but done so perfectly. Making it an unforgettable experience. Our fist kiss. MJMP 2:07am
