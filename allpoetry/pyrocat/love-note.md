I FUCKING HATE YOU 
YOU ASSHOLE!
WHY THE FUCK YOU GOTTA BREAK ME AGAIN!
IMMATURE DICKHEAD
I HOPE TO SEE YOU CRAWLING ALONG IN THE ECHO SIDE* FUCKER!!!!!
DID YOU THINK IT WAS FUNNY TO HIT ME LIKE THAT
DID YOU AND YOUR NEW CHIC HAVE A GOOD STONER LAUGH?
DON’T EVER HIT ME LIKE THAT AGAIN COCKSUCKER!!
AS FOR THE REST OF YOU WITH YOUR VOICES THAT STARE
AND YOUR EYES THAT LAUGH 
I BET IT WAS FUNNY FOR YOU TO!
EVERYTHINGS FUNNY HUH?!!!
FUCK EVERY LAST PIG FUCKER AMUNG YOU!
I’LL LAGHF WITH YOU
THEN I’LL LAGHF AT YOU AS I PULL OUT YOUR FUCKING INTESTINES RIGHT IN FRONT OF YOUR EYESAND FEED IT UP YOUR FUCKING ASSHOLE!!
HAHAHAHAHAHAAHAHA HOW FUNNY WAS THAT
WHAT IF I RIPPED OUT YOUR TOES AND REPLACED THEM WITH YOUR RIBS?
THAT’S FUNNY TOO!!!!
I THINK ALL YOU BIGOTS AND DICKHEADS AND JERKS AND PREPS AND WANNA-BE’S AND YOU EX-BOYFRIENDS THAT JUST WHANTED MY PUSSY
YOU MEN ARE ALL THE SAME
AS SOON AS I STOP PUTTING OUT YOU STOP GIVING IN AND START GIVING UP
WELL FUCK YOU
YOU CAN JUST KEEP YOUR LITTLE NEW WHORE
I HOPE SHE KEEPS YOUR DICK HAPPY
GOOD-NIGHT LAND OF DARKNESS
I AM DONE WITH YOU
CANT YOU DO THE SAME FOR ME?
~Non sono importante, e ne` lo sono
Ho usato sempre all`amore lei
A bom-noite, lo scatto.
Sei haben mich verraten~
