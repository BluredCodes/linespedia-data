Strong Little Mouse
Had it hard
Mama left after the humans came
They slashed and burned and dug up his home
Strong Little Mouse lived through it all
We know not when the first one died
Strong Little Mouse mourned his sibling
Now their were two
Little Mouse and Strong Little Mouse
All day the fires burned as they waited for Mama
NEVER she showed and Strong Little Mouse lived through it all
Little Mouse was squirting blood from its nose and growing thin
It was getting harder for Little Mouse to breath
Strong Little Mouse lay next to his kin, one dead and one barely holding on
In the fluffy nest Mama had left by the fire
Girl came, and took away Little Mouse and the Dead
Strong Little Mouse was left alone
Strong Little Mouse lived through it all
Girl cried for Little Mouse and thought Little Mouse was dead
Low and Behold, Little Mouse had survived
With nothing to give but tears,
Girl cried and cried and pleaded Little Mouse to die
Girl went back to get Strong Little Mouse
She picked up the nest of fluff n’ stuff
And carried it to the apple tree
Strong Little Mouse was out of the smoke at last
Strong Little Mouse had lived through it all
Strong Little Mouse heard Girl plead and beg Little Mouse to die
Girl knew it was the only escape to all the suffering
Finally, with no tears left, she set Little Mouse and Strong Little Mouse back together
In the fluffy nest Mama had left
Girl left and returned with cold strange liquid
Strong Little Mouse ached for Mama’s warm milk
Girl tried to give Strong Little Mouse this cold wetness
Strong Little Mouse drank it not and Girl cried and spoke and tried to comfort                    Strong Little Mouse
“I cannot help you” Girl sobbed to Strong Little Mouse
Girl tried to laugh and keep Strong Little Mouse cheery
But Strong Little Mouse knew now what she knew 
Mama was not coming back, and Strong Little Mouse was not going to survive after all
Girl walked off to sob in peace
Girl came back and told Strong Little Mouse she had to go and was not coming back 
Girl set Strong Little Mouse down on the fluffy nest Mama had built
Which now sat behind the apple tree
Girl buried Little Mouse and the other
And left Strong Little Mouse to the fluffy nest
Strong Little Mouse survived it all
Through fire and blood and family loss
Through noise and starvation
And ONE soul that cared; and loneliness at last
Strong Little Mouse had survived it all
Still Strong Little Mouse lays as I write this now
Struggling to survive more and more
Strong Little Mouse I love you
Strong Little Mouse shall suffer no more in the end
