the look in his eyes
as you stare him down
[beat him to the ground]
whispers in your head
hunt him down like a tiger on a hunt
you sniff out your prey
follow them
watch them
intimidate them
heh-heh
your a big man now
preying on the weak
devouring our young
big stong men
tough girls; ready to strike
-you lunge!
you've got him on the ground now
HE SCREAMS
you punch his mouth
--its so full of blood,
he cant scream
[he cant breath]
 you whisper
adrenaline pumping
his eyes are screaming in fear
they charge you up more
you get up, and kick his weak little side
--proveing your authority--
[yeah, it feels good to be in control]
you trive on it
your a big strong girl now
defending your right to be the boss
