title: "nothing"
writer: "pyrocat"

Everyone that I ever loved
Everyone that I ever lost
Everyone that I ever met
Everyone I ever hated
Everyone that I ever missed
That ever touched me
That ever hit me
 That ever looked in my direction
Or talked to my face
 All my friends
All my acquaintances
All the jerks
All the men women children animals and plants
Every speck of pollen and every scent that went by
Every emotion or lack of
Every shot from a gun
Ever motion you make
Every yell you scream at me
Every time I defy the norm
Every fairy and dragon and chlorine mermaid
Every pencil stroke
Every picture you sent me
Every time you lifted me in the air
 Every e-mail and letter
Every classroom
Every house
Every dead pet
Every stroke and cancer and tumor
Every mean word from a friend
Every lost friendship
Every pain
Any lost words unspoken or written
Anything you left undone and uncorrected to me
Every job I had to do
Every kind word from you
Every check-up 
Every break up
All the scars from you
All the scars from me
Each of the times you looked at me funny
All the times we laughed that you forgot
Everything and a thousand things more
Make up my soul my personality
If you don’t like what I am
Shut up and sit down because it was you that made me this way
I am what I am because of Everything
