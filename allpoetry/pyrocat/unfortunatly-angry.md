title: "unfortunatly angry"

Cant you just let me be
STOP BETRAYING
I feel this hate 
THIS PAIN
This self loathing
Splatter my blood again
This love
Splatter my thoughts against the wall
Drip my snot
POUND MY HEAD
Cant you be me
Cant you feel this too
YOU CANT SEE
this hurts to do
Split open my veins
Do it before I cry out for help
Hang up the phone
To PUSH YOU AWAY
Your confused
And you cant understand
Why am I upset like that?
What does it DO FOR YOU
As you wonder
So do I
I cant know
Spread myself open for you 
And I cant feel it
Don’t need you
Silence on the line
DEAD QUIET
I NEED YOU to help me out of this bloody hole
I cant understand
Overreacting
Ambushing myself
In and out of this state
This hurts so bad
IT DOESN’T STOP
It never stops
I try and it stays longer
I NEED ME
But I’m not there
