Lying 
naked
	in a sea of starfish
Swim to the shore
		But it’s so far away…
To drown would be a welcome
				Escape
Swim    on & on
	Fall     
to the beach & the little bonfire
To headbang to Rammstein
	Amid the fire and sand
SET THE SEA 
ON FIRE
Watch it
Burn
  Take a swim in the tub; The World
	goes up in FLAMES
Walk through a clearing
Sit and talk awhile
Sit and watch me Burn
	The Moon
Herself
	falls and burns inside the shadow of the
Sun

Watch it all blow up
BURNING in your face
So
…cold…
are the flames,
reach out and touch them,
reach out
	and touch me
