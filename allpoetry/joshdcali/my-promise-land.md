For people to hate you for your opinions
Diversity is interesting but not to some
But if so then are you really entitle for opinion?
For the opinion is what really matters
Ideas of leaving my homeland for a new life
But hatred comes up when its spoken

But how can you talk positive about it?
If a person feels opposite of what you feel
Though people will hate me cause of it
I have to do what is best for my future
To go back to the land where I feel at peace
For the culture that pumps through my vains
For a fresh beginning and a way of pride
So when it comes down to it I want to forget
My past pains of my family
Canada is my resting place for my peace of mind.
