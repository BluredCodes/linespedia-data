Someone please hand me a knife,
For the pain I feel will end from existing
For I don't know what to live for
A life without a goal is not worth living 
The sorrow is now gone from me 
For the blade has set me free
Everything seems to becoming slow
my breath that has slowed from flowing
Have I made a bad mistake?
But that's all it takes
I'm leaving this world now
For I am finally at peace with the world
My blooming flower of life has weithered away.
