title: "Gave up"

You gave up on me 
Even before you knew thee
The birth of me was a gift
But you didn't want to receive
You ran away from me
Hiding me from my culture
Now sad that bugs me knowing
I'm this way because of you
A spot to be filled
That terrors me everyday
Wondering what a person
You are but I see you in me 
You gave up on your child.
