Life is such a fragile gift given to us,
It can all end with one slit of the wrist
Then the problems of the world is dead,
No more pain or agony
But before you do such an act think of your life,
Remember the good things that God has given you
Think of the friends that love and cares for you,
That would do anything for you
Think of your church family,
Who are there to pray for you and to accept you
With one choice you can change other people and you,
With that one move you will never know your future
How will your father in heaven look upon you now?
When the rain falls he is crying over you
But the choice is up to you and what you do
But remember this
Life is too important to give up on now
The the wrist is slit and your dead,
Out of life.

                  Josh G.
