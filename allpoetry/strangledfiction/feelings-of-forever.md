title: "Feelings Of Forever"

Through the mist of a tear I can see that strong vision of forever with only you..
Through the silence of such emptiness I can hear the beauty of your voice calling me..
Through the scent of this feeling I can smell your body growing closer to mine..
Through the texture of this window I feel the glass will never be broken..

From what I can see, I know that as long as the tear is transparent, the vision will be the same, allowing forever to stay within reach...
From what I have heard, beauty such as yours can fill the space of an eternal empty heart, helping it understand the words in which you speak...
From the scent that is so very strong, there is no doubt that you are growing closer to me, as I entwine your soul with mine.
From the feeling of these hands, this window is forever sealed with trust and faith that will never be broken...
