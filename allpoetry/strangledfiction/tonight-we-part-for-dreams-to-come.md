Follow your dreams- You are so beautiful. You are so
special.
The best has yet to come- And so have I. Now feeling
left out.
Understand my feelings- I share yours. Will you share
mine?
Wait for me- My inspiration is you; Replacing
grieving with meaning.


...With meaning, I live for you...


Tonight we part for dreams to come,
before we know it, this battle will be won.

The tears will fall and so will I,
in a silent place for me to cry.

As spoken by many, 'Love is forever.',
that tear in the ocean is now a lost treasure.

The fog of loneliness will very soon rise,
giving a path to your beautiful eyes.

Gaze at the stars each night before sleep,
those stars are forever defining our dreams.


...With dreams, you live for me...
