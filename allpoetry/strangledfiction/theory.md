Her tears are as simple as man.
...Forever falling.
Though we will all run down the face of the earth, we will all dry up.
...Sooner or later.

She will cry forever.
...We will run for just as long.
The rivers will form, and we will swim.
...Those who tread that water will drown before it dries.

She will leave us here.
...We will wander...Ignorantly.
Our seasons will come, a life will be taken.
Our seasons will end...And we will begin.


Then, and only then, will she be understood.


Her tears are our future...
...Our adamant warning.
We swim these present waters...
...So helplessly drowning.
Her seasons will be our past...
...Our realm of understanding.
In this cycle of existence,
so quickly, we are vanishing...
