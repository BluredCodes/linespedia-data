Certainly, the skin must be shed.
Painfully, your sin has now bled.

...This will scar me, eternally.
...I am waiting, patiently.

Hands will begin to open.
Shame...Your fingers are broken.
Lands will differ night from day.
Blame has taken your time away.

...This will scar me, eternally.
...I am waiting, patiently.

Seperate the truth from skin...
End this battle from within...
