Please...
Follow me to this promised land.
Crawl through the image with me again.
Stumble over the whispers of the wanted words.
Shed the burden of your own beliefs for me.

Please...
Realize that my path is the only path to eternity.
Understand that second thoughts should be overlooked.
Pretend to throw away your promises...to yourself.
Regretfully stand with others that follow me forever.

Please...
Hate me with all of your being.
Displace me within your mind and heart.
Save yourself from this never ending story.
Follow not me, rather the one who suppresses me.

I am your promised land.
I am your eternal path.
I am your hate.

I am nothing... I am fiction...months ago,
