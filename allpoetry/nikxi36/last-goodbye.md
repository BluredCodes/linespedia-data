Will we find ourselves
Beyond the great beyond
Or must we keep searching
	For our true love
I choose not to search
	It is a vital journey
I cannot take another breath
	Goodbye my fellow friends
	I will find myself oh too soonThe word vital. I'm not really sure what this is about either. It depends on the reader.
