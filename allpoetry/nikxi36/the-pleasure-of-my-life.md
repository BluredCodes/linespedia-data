The pleasure of my life
Comes out from deep inside
It really doesn’t make much sense
I really do, try to hide
It doesn’t include mercy
Or helping others grow
But what makes me happy is when others
Are mad at each other and show

This does not involve my loved ones
But rather those sickled few
I really love it when those loved ones
Take those hated and chew

It is a real strange interest
But it makes me happy nonetheless
Does that mean that I’m a mess?This poem is based on my stepmom and dad fighting over the smallest dumbest things! Can you tell i have deep-seated distaste for her?
