we met by a mere laugh
sharing the night in talk and slumber
you thought i had rules
but instead you had yours
much pain was brought 
it was redressed by your proposal
we began the journey, 
falling down 
and much pain was brought
you sugar coated me
i felt like a princess
now i'm a mere wisp
we ended the journey with nothing more than a goodbye
i still hold you in my heart
and will forever
and much pain will be broughtThis is actually about me and my ex-bf. Interesting story actually.
