Is it you out there in the clouds?The one i've been wishing for?You know it's been an awefully long time,I almost couldn't wait anymore.I looked beyond the sunsetted hills,And in the murky lake.But nothing comes as close to you,No nothing! it's almost fake.I'll continue to wait for you love,Until the sea's go dry.Until the mountains collapse oh love,I'll love you til i die.I'm worried people aren't gonna know what this is about?
