When we kiss, 
when we touch, 
when we cry... 
We're together 

When we talk, 
when we yell, 
when we fight... 
We're together 

Every time I see your name, 
I think of us

The way u talk,
the way you flow,
the way you think...
Makes me love you

The way we speak,
the way we sing,
the way we yearn one another...
Makes me love you

Every time I see your name,
I think of usAny and all, please help me to better my skill
