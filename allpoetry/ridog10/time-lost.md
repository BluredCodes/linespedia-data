I sat at home again the other day,
Thinking of you.
And I realized, I love you.

Everything about you makes me wonder…
“Are you and angel from heaven?”
We’ve only met once and I know you are the one.

Love To be near- to trust- to love- to die for..
The feelings I feel for you.
The smell, taste, and the sound of your voice,
Does me in.
 
I’ve fallen for you Love and I don’t know how to tell you.
Maybe Writing this line, hopefully will make you realize
The agony I stress myself with,
Each day as I sit near the phone, questioning your call. 

But it never comes, day in, day out.
I sit and wait.
Till the day comes when I get an invitation.
To..No! GOD No!
Your wedding.

Death is prominent, because my Love, you've killed my heart.
I have no choice.
I have lost all that is important in my life, life seems like a blur as I lay here- bloody and smiling- wrists slit I am happy, because I know you have found the one for you, as I have my own.

An eternity without you.Hey, this poped out of my head, let me know what you think..be honest
