Through the years I've yet to see,
   The amount of beauty you've given me.
   I held your hand wishing desperately to be the one to hold and keep you
   near. 
We soon grew older- high school now over, with you up north and I back at
   home, we found others to fill the void.
The days soon past, I saw my love- love once there- fade to black.
You brought him home, I welcomed you both with open arms, even invited you
   both to join me in feast- a feast for two. We laughed; we talked, and
   cried at times. Memories of the past made me think of all we once had. As I walked you both out, I asked you, why. "Why did you say yes?"- Yes to
   his proposal. You did not respond only left with closed arms.
The love once there has not deceased and I'm wishing and waiting to be with
