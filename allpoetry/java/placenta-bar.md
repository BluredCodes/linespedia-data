Would you care for whipped cream?Aber, bitte mit Sahne?I'm sure you could douse yourself in it.Immerse yourself in it.Close your lips, and swallow it.Force it down, like rough medicinal sludge.Even though it sweetens your breath,You can't help but gag.Hold him down, boys,Stuff him good.Puff up his teeny weeny,Itty bitty,Stirrer.Fill that reservoir with misspelled words.And sweet, sweet cream.Ick.I think I'll wash my hands now.
