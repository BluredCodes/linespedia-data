First installment.101.  Squirrels make great bolo whips.100.  Good for cleaning shotgun barrels.99.   Great house slippers, just stick your foot right in their throat!98.   Filler for air mattresses used in Hollywood productions      involving a 10 story dive from atop a building.97.   Retrieving ingrown toenails.96.   Opening beer bottles.95.   Jamming up mufflers.94.   Denture adhesive.93.   Squirrels make great grappling hooks.92.   Fish scaler.91.   Stupid poem ideas.
