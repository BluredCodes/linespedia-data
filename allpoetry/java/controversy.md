Oh, look.
There's a tiny little spot on my shoe.
So small.
Hello down there!
I bet there's an entire civilization in that spot.
Lots of little things going to the spot grocery store.
To buy spot food.
To get spot diapers for poopy spot babies.
Just a little universe 
And on my shoe!  
Hey, Nauticalmyst7 will get a kick outta-
Whoops!
Shouldn't say kick, 
Or I might just kick something on accident.
I can't think about kicking anything.
Not now.
Clear my mind.  
MMMMMMMM
OHM
Not thinking about anything.
Not thinking about kicking that wall over there.
Hm hm hmmm.

...

OH, I CAN'T HELP IT!  MUST KICK WALL!
MUST KICK WALL TO DEATH!
DIE, LITTLE SPOT PEOPLE!
TAKE THAT!
AND THAT!
AND THAT1
DIE!
DIE!!
DIE!!!

What have I done?
*sniff*
They never had a chance.
And I was their god.


I was a god.
