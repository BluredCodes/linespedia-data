She swims across the cosmos
Into a sea of evergreens
Fluid lights surround her crown
The gateway to a dream
Wild butterflies and faerie dust
Suspended in the midst
Honey drips from open mouth
To candy coat her lips
She reaches out into the night
Like a sword across black sheets
To cross between a thousand miles
As a ballerina leaps
Her hopes, a banner stretched
From brow to eyes open wide
Offering her intentions
As the most willing of brides
In a cycle of forever
In a world with closing gates
Trapped inside this crystal world
Where forever no one waitsasdfjkl;, 1234567890.  so there
