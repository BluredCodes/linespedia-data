In his arms I long to bemystery man won't you come find me?I will love you foreverlove tried, and truewhen you are with me, you'll never be blueI love you so much,tho i know not who you arebut one day ill find youi hope your not farI'll cross oceans & mountains if thats what it takes..because the men in my life, so far have all been fakesMy love returned is all i aski do not think this is such a hard task ill be with u for the rest of timeill love you so much, u might think it's a crimebecause you'll make me happy and im wiling to betyou will deserve my all, and my best you shall get
