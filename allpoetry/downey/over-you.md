title: "Over You"

I walked right out & left you..left you in the coldI just put my hand on the doorand felt  my heart hit the floornever said i'm sorry, never said goodbyejust that I  was angry,  and that I wished YOU would diewell tonight die I did indeedmy heart with all my selfish needgot to my head, got into my brainlike a clear day, suddenly filled with rainI'm sorry for whatever it was that made you madbut are you sorry for making me sad?I'm not even sure why i care anymore all you did was treat me like your whorewell now your gone..and im realizing im fineand  now i know i can recover this time
