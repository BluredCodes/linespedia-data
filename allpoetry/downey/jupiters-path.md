~~Jupiters path~~Heaven is coming down upon us tonight and we pray, whilst looking for the lightSearching for his guidance, searching for a signBut all I can think of if the little hand entwined in mineWhy do we follow? Why don't we lead? how is it we came to surrender to another mans creed?We can never leave here, that would be a sinto go against the order, our only next of kinfollowing is the only way, we have no other choice,we have all been taught to listen, not to raise our voiceThe leader's standing up now,the sign to state our case,to follow our beloved leader, to find our rightful place.
