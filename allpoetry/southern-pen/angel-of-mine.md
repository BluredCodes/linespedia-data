I know this lady,
she's here on earth.
but she's been an Angel,
since the day of birth.

The love she brings,
from a smile so bright.
makes me think that I,
am staring into a heavenly light.

Her soft, tender touch,
can calm the aching soul.
Can pull ones spirits high,
when they're falling into a hole.

An Angel she is,
and forever will be.
I am just lucky,
to have her with me.
