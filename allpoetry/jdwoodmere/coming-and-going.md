---
title: "Coming and Going"

Wedding,
Rent an apartment.

Bedding,
The baby department.

More room,
Buy a home.
Boredom,
Start to roam

Pretense,
New cars.
Defense,
Hide scars.

Tension.
Feeling sad.
Dissension,
Things look bad.

Deprivation,
Pressures vent.

Separation,
Rent an apartment.
