He the husband, provider, the pappy,
Spends all his time making everyone happy.

This effort on his part is quite regressed,
Since all those he helps are totally depressed.

He never learns, just keeps on trying,
And his happy charges just keep on crying.

This happiness syndrome has some silly quirks,
For he keeps on doing it, and it never works.
