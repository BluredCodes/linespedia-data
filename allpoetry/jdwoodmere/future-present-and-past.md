Your future's coming, wait and seeFor you can't remember what will beAnd then in timeIf you think fastYou'll see your futureSlipping pastYour past that isOr is it wasIs what you did You were the causeYour present time Will never lastFor with the futureComes the pastYour future's nearThe present's hereThe past's now clearYou need not fearSo future's coming, wait and seeYou'll then remember what will be
