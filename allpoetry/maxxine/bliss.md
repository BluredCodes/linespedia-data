title: "Bliss"

Disregard me- and my kind.
   Let us lead our empty dreams
         to our empty promise lands.
 Take our magic, leave us raped of our   
        creativity and resorcefulness.
  Steal our innocence.
        We are naive- WE ENJOY IT.
Let us have this!  Our hallow bliss, 
        Our ignorance, our fruitless lives.
             
Good for you, you've exposed my lie to me-
                    NOW GIVE IT BACK.
