---
title: "I'm the whore you think I am."
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "im-the-whore-you-think-i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6640-Im-the--you-think-I-am.-by-Maxxine"
---

You changed
.        You changed your hair, your attitude,
You altitude, your latitude
.    You're no where near me now
.  You're far away      You're gone astray
. You changed your mind about me-
and...       I don't blame you?
