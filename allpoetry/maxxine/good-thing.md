.      Too much of a good thing-
.  Yes- I can see it coming (again).
.       You're too much of a good thing all the time.
.  And yes, I wrote him, I write them all.
.       (But you'd never know that.)
.   Besides, you're too much of a good thing.
.              You don't let me stray and I want to.
