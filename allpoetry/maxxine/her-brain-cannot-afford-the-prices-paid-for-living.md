Anger, Anger
    is in me.
A foolish, stupid girl.

A foolish, stupid girl, 
  I know.
Who follows me around.

She must take after
Her mistress, 
A foolish, foolish girl.

A girl so light and lazy, 
Her brain Cannot afford
The prices paid for living.

Fair shadow
  leave me, 
    Thy Mistress
For you are far more fair 
      Then she.
   Your darkness is so pure
      And you are far more
          fair then me.

Anger, Anger  
 is in thee
A foolish, stupid girl.my absolute favourite, I don't really know why...
