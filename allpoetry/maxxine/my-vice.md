---
title: "My Vice"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "my-vice"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Sit next to me?
        Make me feel loved?
Hold my hand tenderly?
   Let me know the answers to my 
        unasked
             questions?
  Tell me you still need me?
   Would you like to reassure me?
Apply some glue, be the vice, hold me together?
     

                  No?
