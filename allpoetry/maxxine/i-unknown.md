I am air conditioned reeds.
I create a black turbulence from amoung a tornado of airplanes
(somtimes like the speed of sound creeping invisably along behind the light).
     I am silenced bees kept in an angry sleep, cold in refrideration.
  I am the softest durge coming from within a peasent's ottoman.

I used to be the forest dweller's flute, making sounds like a burnt cookie.
      I remember the darkest cave where the tallest sicamore grew.

        I fear only myself and see the world though eyes unknown.
