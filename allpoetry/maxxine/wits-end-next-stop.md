title: "'Wit's End' Next Stop."

I see that none of you know me well, 
so here's the story then:
I'm slowly breaking into bits, 
I think I'm reaching my "Wit's End."

I see that none of you relate,
so let me try this plain:
I cannot take more cruel words
or I will end this pain.

I see that none of you do care, 
I think I'm glad now that I see...
I'll not ask you to help me mend
or ask for any sympathy...does it flow?  no? i thought so....
