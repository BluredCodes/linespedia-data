The 
wisest words
I ever 
got told
were from my 
eighth grade teacher:
When you point 
your 
finger, 
just
remember...
three
fingers
point 
back 
at 
YOU.This is for those people I have seen leaving absurd remarks about other people's opinions in their poems instead of ABOUT the writing style of their poems. Here's one for you.
