---
title: "Learning to Hate"
writer: "Maxxine"

Left in the middle
      This side line life.
No one to take me-
      A nameless wife.

No one to fake this.
      I'm just that type.
Left on the sidelines
      Watching the hype.

Left on the road-
      No life to live.
Waiting, I'm anxious-
      Wanting to give.

No one to wait for
      I guess that's fate.
Left in this black hole - 
      Learning to hate.
