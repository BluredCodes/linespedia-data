---
title: "Je N'amie Que Toi?"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "je-namie-que-toi"
source: "AllPoetry"

If happiness were a million tears 
      I would cry them all for you.
    (Your gorgeous smile melts me like soft butter on
fresh pancakes.)
 You hug me just right – 
       like the sun lingering on the horizon 
A little too long in a moment of perfect, timeless
romance.
           If love were a carnival 
                  I would try to win every prize 
In exchange for your heart.
     (Your words are better then music to my ears.)

   You brush my cheek softly with your lips and 
I wonder about how right kissing you could feel...
   If this love is suicide-
 I think I'd want to try dying with you.
