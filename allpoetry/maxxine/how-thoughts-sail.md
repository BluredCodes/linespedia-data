---
title: "How thoughts Sail"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "how-thoughts-sail"

I think as one sails many different ships.
         I 'O Captain, my Captain.'
I salute the breaking dawns.
I catch my balance and 
dance like I have never danced before,
 Thanks to the waves and the 
ale moving my body.
    I cry at sunset,
I sleep with one eye open.
I beware of pirates and never shed a tear.
I 'O Captain, my Captain.'
I've sailed many different ships,
As I've sailed many thoughts down different streams, oceans, bays... pennisulas.
