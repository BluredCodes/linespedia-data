title: "Cow Zombies"

A polka dot dress wearing a fat lady
waits, swaying in the breeze for it’s 
next bus.
A leather coat wearing a dark masked man
pulls a knife from its pocket and slashes, forgivingly at the screaming polka dot dress.
My car drives past, me in tow,
and the gun living in my hand
shoots that ugly leather jacket it hates so much.
Goddamn Cow Zombies taking over
human bodies.
