---
title: "Pathetic Tears"
writer: "Maxxine"

I can see you smiling.
I can see you wishing.
Wishing that your father 
Would care to take you fishing.

You are getting angry,
You don't have a clue
How your tears affect me - 
They stick to me like glue.

Now here comes the spring,
Some new hope for us all
Some pathetic reason to
Go take another fall.

You've just gone poem crazy.
You don't need me anymore.
Your feelings have gone bitter 
And I'm happily ignored.

And now I've disappered from your tender gaze
And let you disapper into this endless maze.
But I will never forget all you've done for me.
I'll always remember how you set my anxious heart ablaze.
