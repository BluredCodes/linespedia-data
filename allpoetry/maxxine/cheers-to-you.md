---
title: "Cheers to You"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "cheers-to-you"

I'm dark         You're light.
I'm wrong        You're right.
You hate         me now
Like you         hated me then
And there's nothing I can do about 
it 
BECAUSE I DON'T BEND.
I can only change so many times.
Learn so many songs and so many rhymes.
    So if you don't like this
Just leave now
Because I'm telling myself
'I DON'T CARE ANYHOW.'
I wait, You leave.
I lie and deceive
I'd flail and cry
But you would still say goodbye.

I've lost myself so many times
I can't see through this fog of tears
I'd look to you because you'd help me 
through but you raise your glass and make 'Cheers.'
