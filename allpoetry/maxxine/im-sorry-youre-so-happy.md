---
title: "(I'm sorry you're so happy)"
writer: "Maxxine"

Jealousy inspired by
  Nightmares of happiness.
Happiness that's true.
     Fun I've never had...
Grave-cold ropes, twist,
               For Christ's sake,
  Love, please.
                Just do this.  Inspire my 
   Happiness.  Loose me in the 
                              ragged marsh.
