I needed someone to talk to but you weren't there- 
So, I talked to a piece of paper instead-
           Crumpled it up and threw it away.

     Something I couldn't do with you.This is an old one too... my bestfriend and I had broken up for the first time probably 2 weeks before... I'm used to it now when he gets angry and won't talk to me, and that's probably not a good thing either...
