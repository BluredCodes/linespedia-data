Let me go
  To do no harm, 
 To leave No Damage
       After.
Leave me be to
     Misery- 
   To crumble every
  wall.
 Leave me be to sulk 
down here, bellow six feet
       of dirt.
Let me be a 
    dying body, 
        cursed with a mortal soul.wow, some old school poetry from a journal a long long time ago....
