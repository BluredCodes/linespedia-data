---
title: "Your Divorce"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "your-divorce"
source: "AllPoetry"

I guess I really don't know how to react-
Because it's tragic and stunning...
But I think it's hilarious.  I think you're being,
Both, So stupid- It's funny.  
I know it's not.
But.... it is.  Watching you degenerating.
