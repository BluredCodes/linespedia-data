"Bad things can only happen." -Nicole Meades
(if you start to date someone, especially someone you're friends with.) 

"Don't date your friends."  - Devin Whiteley
(Max, what have you learned?  Don't date your friends, right?)

   Unknowingly stummbling 
         into a Tupperware Convention 
  at the Sheridan Hotel.
     It doesn't help
 to see all the young couples here - 
     In fact, it makes me feel like hell.

   It doesn't help about the awkward situation you've put me in.
  That wasn't fair at all.
           (Setting up this box and noose so I could kick you down.)ok, so this started as one thing: Me and the best friend breaking up into one of my guy friends asking me out (the last three lines.) tell me what you think.
