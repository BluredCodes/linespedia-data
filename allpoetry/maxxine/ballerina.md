Pale, Pale Porcelain Princess:
Wish for washy willows,
Dance for dawning daffodils,
Grace, glory, gratified goose down!
Alabaster Ambassador of Austria.
Empress of Elegance, ingenuity-
Fly, flutter, fleeting fortune…
Dance on your pale alabaster toes till you fall!
