I'm so twisted, used and torn.
I'm ragged, old and utterly worn.
I'm just a scrap now, just felt like cloth-
To be placed on a jacket where 'tis been eaten by moth.

I'm so angry, livid, mad.
I'm dreaded and I know I've been had.
I'm just an end now, one unwanted,
