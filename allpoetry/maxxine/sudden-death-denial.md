---
title: "Sudden Death Denial"
writer: "Maxxine"
slug: "sudden-death-denial"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm afraid of falling much too quickly.
I've tripped and now sunk low. 
I've jested once too often 
And now become your foe.

It's sudden death denial,
It's a blow I cannot take.
So, with grace and ease I stand up.
And I begin to fake.

You think that I'm not for you.
You thought that I was mad.
But, no friend, I am neither,
It's just that I am sad.

For I know what we are.
And I know we cannot be.
So, I choose the path of silence.
I lock love up eternally.
