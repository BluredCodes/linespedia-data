---
title: "Dreams Of You"
writer: "Khayman"
slug: "dreams-of-you"

Last night I lay in bed dreaming of you 
Of the great times that we had 
Of the beautiful music in your laughter 
Of the gentle caress of your hands 
As we expressed our deep devotion and love 

I dreamt of the healing light in your eyes 
Of the beautiful body which you yeilded only to me 
I dreamt of the ever growning bond of trust between us 
And of those late nights where you let me cry on your shoulder 
I dreamt of nothing, nothing but you, and the beauty of your heart 

As I awoke this morning from my dreams of you 
I felt no need or longing for the sleep in which you comforted me 
Because as I look over my shoulder I can see you sleeping softly 
Every morning I can see all of those things I dreamt of 
I can caress your beautiful, soft skin and feel that you're still real 
Every morning awakening to you reminds me 
That I'm in a never ending dream of my love for youSensualism is an area of poetry I'm still exploring, so I hope you enjoy it.
