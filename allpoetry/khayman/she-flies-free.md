You fly free with white wings of the dove
Gracefully soaring through this heavy air
Over my life you spread your joy
Like a child I wanted you for a toy

Just as I reached to break your wings
You rode the wind and left me alone

Each morning I wake and reach for your love
Each night I sleep with the cage door open
But just as the dove who flies so free
When I loosened my grip you took a chance and left meSometimes we want to hold people close, but the danger is keeping them too close.
