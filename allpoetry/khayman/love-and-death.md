title: "Love And Death"
writer: "Khayman"

Since my younger years I knew I needed you
Your face shone pure in my fading light
Every engagement you resisted
After avoidance I persisted

The sexual tension between you and I
I dreamt of your passion and the taste of your skin
I felt you giving in
You’d be mine in the end

As last I had your body trembling beneath mine
Finally I’d broken your resistance and won
Yet the promise you heard 
Was only a misunderstood lie

We’d been bound by fate and you fell in love
As I moved to the next challenge 
In darkness you shed tears of blood
Knowing you couldn’t live without me
You made the only choice you felt was left

As I opened the door you slipped off your dress
You sought proof that I still loved your caress
If only I’d known it was you
If only she hadn’t asked who it was

Standing naked and wounded our love took a new meaning
You were determined to have me even in death
Only a flash filled my eyes
Deafening screams as we fell to the floor

By fate we’d been bound together, to romance in death
In life our fate was only a prelude to irony
You forced me into this post-mortem monogamous hell
