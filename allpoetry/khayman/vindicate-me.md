Just a common girl among the crowd
Yet we picked me out for the rites of love
And why was I so young inside?
You needed a vice to push it aside

Vindicate my love for you
And show me the ways in which we fell
Tell me why you've punished me
And show me why I fell in love with you

I turned to you believing the saint was me
And the tears you cried, I cried inside
And why was I blind to the pain you gave?
I needed a vice to push my life aside

Justify my love
Justify my fall
Justify my hate
Justify my life

Vindicate my love for you
And show me the ways in which we fell
Tell me why you've punished me
And show me why I fell in love with youThis poem is about falling in love with someone. However, that someone needed justification and they found such through using you. And in the end, you're left alone, with no justification for your pain.
