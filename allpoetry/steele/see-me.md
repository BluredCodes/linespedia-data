---
title: "See Me"
writer: "steele"
slug: "see-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

See my eyes, they are searching 
Longing to catch sight…of you

See my mouth, it is yearning
Longing to be kissed…by you.

See my hands, they are shaking
Longing to be held…by you

See my arms, they are aching 
Longing to be wrapped…’round you

See my legs, they are trembling
Longing to bring me…to you.

See my body, it is shiv’ring
Longing to be warmed…by you.

See my heart, feel it beating
Racing with my love…for you.

See my soul, sense it reaching
With wonder and joy…to you.

See my life, promise brimming
All I am given…to you.

See me, all my life loving
Living to be loved…by you.
