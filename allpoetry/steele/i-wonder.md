---
title: "I Wonder"
writer: "steele"
slug: "i-wonder"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8045-I-Wonder-by-steele"
---

As you look at me, I wonder: Do you know? 
That inside my heart a thing of beauty grows? 
I am the one who longs for your tender embrace,
Or merely the sight of your beautiful face.

As you look at me, I wonder: Can you not see? 
The man I am becoming, who longs for us to be? 
I have passed the tests that life has put me through,
No flying colors, but the man who's been forged is true.

As you look at me, I wonder: What else can I do?
I am devoted to one, no other choice for me, only you.
Freely I have made this choice, aware of most, still 
There's more to life than laughter, but I did, I do, I will.

As you look at me, I wonder: Can I love you more?
The answer is plainer each day, as has never been before.
Each day amazed, for as much as I loved you ere I slept,
Upon waking, my love for you is new and even greater yet.

As you look at me, I wonder: Will you ever ask me?
I have made my case, I have wooed you and I have given me.
The promises I have made, to love, to honor, to cherish;
Are not made lightly, but in hope you will grant my wish. 

As you look at me, I wonder: How do I prove my love?
That this feeling we share is a gift from God above?
All that I am is yours to have and hold for now and always.
I will prove my love by living it until the end of my days.
