I wait quietly for her touchUnable to make the least moveThe need I feel makes me suchShe teases, coming closer, now aboveI am bound…My body tenses, straining upwardsSilken hair, velvet skin inflamesHands over lips, she allows no wordsPushing down, she controls, her gameI am bound…My surrender, hard won, yet completeMy body, all of me, for her pleasureI beg for a touch, a kiss, so meetThat she demands, accepts only full measureI am bound…I am caught, a slave to every whimHeld fast, helpless, before her, for herNone other is able, nor do I want themOnly one hand to hold me, now, foreverI am bound…Not rope or chain, nor even soft silk bindingsSurrounds me, such bonds are easily offOther bonds I cannot fight, yet I am findingWhen she calls, I return, never having enoughI am bound…Softly, slowly, were these gentle bonds Put upon me, life and self, now givenStronger than steel, heart and soul the onesHolding me close, my lady, for her living I am bound…by love.New to site, but I wrote my poems some time ago.
