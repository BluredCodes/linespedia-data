You asked, I answered.
Will you kiss me for no reason?
I will kiss you, my beloved,
And there is always reason.

You asked, I answered.
What can you possibly see in me?
I see a future promised,
And a life of possibility.

You asked, I answered.
How do you see me, so clear?
Because I found I remembered
To trust, and forget my fear.

You asked, I answered.
How can you be so certain?
Because I dared and learned
To love with no expectation.

You asked, I answered.
Are you something I’m only dreaming of?
I am real, warm; your beloved
And more than any dream of love.

You asked,
Can you truly love me so much?
I answered,
Yes.
