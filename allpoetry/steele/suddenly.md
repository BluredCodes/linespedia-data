Into my world, you arrived so suddenly,Lighting the cold gray day with your smile.Only the breath of a whisper came to me,Very much alone, frightened for a while.Each day that passes brings new wondersYou are the source of the light that warmsOur love grows and my thought wandersUpon you, your beauty, and all your charms.Let us never lose or take for granted,Us, and this wonderful gift we’ve found.Could I ever feel or be more wantedI cannot see, my feet never touch the ground.Eternally.
