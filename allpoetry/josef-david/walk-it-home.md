summer,
saturday night...
Shy boy creeps out of his shell...

mom... I'm going to see the old gang...
haven't seen 'em since I left for college.

hours later...
lying on my back...
lying to myself...
coolness of the grass...
stars swing and sway
the murmur of a party mixes with the radio...
So social... yet so isolated...

How am I going to get home?
why did I drive a motorcycle?
It's not far, but I can't...
Lying here, even in this euphoric state,
I know my limitations...

It's not far... and it a beautiful night.

I'll walk it home.
