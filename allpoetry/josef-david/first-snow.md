not yet tainted by exhaust.
     Pure white, Virgin in the morning light.
     not yet raped by the boots of men,
     not yet scarred by dragging steps.

     Watch it fly at night, see it, enjoy.
     Until the world wakes up, to destroy.
     Beautiful and clean, only a short time.
     Falling so innocent, victim of crime.
