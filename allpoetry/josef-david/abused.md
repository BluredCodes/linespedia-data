poor little dog...
You've wandered to my place...
Maybe cause you think I'm different.
Unfamiliar farm, but the same fear.

I can tell you've been abused... 
by the way you look, the way you move.
Fearing my hand as I try to pet you...
Not trusting my tone as I call you...

you're life has been rough...
and it shows in your step...
you're beaten... broken...
Cruelty is all you know.
