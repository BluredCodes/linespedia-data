The world belongs to it's inhabitants.
The crystallized mountains in friged air,
Warm desert caressing winds, blue, storming
mysterious seas, Islands born beyond imagination,
Forests magically eternal, plains and prairies
display endless sky, all of it with-in human's grasp.

The tragedy of man, is dispite his ambition to travel
the world, he fades slowly into the mold of a work-filled,
monotonous life that leaves him craving.  

have no regrets on your death bed, experience your world.I kind of forced this one.... though I feel strongly that people should Travel their world rather than working day after
day for a lifetime.
