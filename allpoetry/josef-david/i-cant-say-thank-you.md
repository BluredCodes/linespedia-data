---
title: "I can't say Thank You."
writer: "josef david"

I appreciate it,
I really do...
It's a very generous gift...
Why, if I'm pleased, can't I look at your face?...
...Because the ground believes me.  
It doesn't look at me wanting more... 
If I say thank you, you'll think I'm lying...
I am paranoid of praise...
Pity and Shame... resulting from a compliment...
I don't believe yours... and I fear you don't believe mine.
Don't stare like that... I'm sincere...
