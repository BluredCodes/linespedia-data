"In solitude we are least alone"

it's truth is paramount...
In a world that focuses on what we see,
it's easy to forget about a friend,...
Imagination...

Imaginary friends may be for children...
But Imagination is for every one...
Being alone... no sound but our own...
Caressed, sometimes tortured, by our thoughts... 

Our thoughts are what we make them....
Our thoughts make us who we are...
Thinking, Knowing, Believing,...
minds tossing, turning, twisting...

in solitude,... we are least alone.
