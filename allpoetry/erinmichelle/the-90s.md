Body piercing, Internet
World Trade Center took a hit
OJ Simpson, Tanya Harding
Jerry Garcia, too much partying
Good ol' Clinton took a stand
"Daddy's Roomate" was quickly banned
Oklahoma bombing, Waco
In with Sony, out with Kraco
On death row sits McVeigh
Who killed JonBenet?
Hale-Bopp shined in the sky
Kurt Cobain, too young to die.I wrote this in my senior english class in high
school. The assignment was to write about something that affected you in some way.....
I guess all these things have or did....
