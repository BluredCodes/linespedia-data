I see the clouds of smoke
In someone elses eyes
I see the immaculate perception
That no one else can comprehend
I feel the strenth that only God can feel
What can this world mean
If only I could see.....
If only I could feel....
God can only perceive
As being somewhere
And something
Can this world know 
What life means
Taken what is ours
Forgiven what I've seen
Forget the silence
Remember what is true
Don't forget what is believed
What we remember in the end
I should feel this high 
As endless as the sky
Forever far away
Remembering what is mine
Have I, for this, lost all my dreams
My hopes, and my realities
Brung unto my sacred self
Made unto those, 
Who call themselves
The Infinite Aldremas
