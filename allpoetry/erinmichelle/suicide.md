title: "SUICIDE"

The river seems so far away
As she's standing in the rain
On the edge of a railing
Trying to escape her pain

Her heart is tortured daily 
From a past she cannot lose
She's trying to hide away
From a life she did not choose

Darkness has engulfed her
And kept her from the light
She strives to meet this beast
That no longer she can fight

She's no longer afraid 
Of what is on the other side
She wants to let go,
To again have her pride

So she releases her grip 
And she exhales when she falls
Because broken are her chains
And crumbled are her walls

She's there now, alone
In this new and different place
And she rediscovers something lost
A smile upon her face
