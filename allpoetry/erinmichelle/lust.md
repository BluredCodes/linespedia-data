title: "LUST"

This feeling is dark
It penetrates my soul
Regret and guilt rip at a heart
Which is now an empty hole
I lose myself in lust
Time and time again
As my state of mind is altered
This battle I cannot win
Impossible to give myself 
To someone I've just met
So their faces simply turn to names
But they are names I can't forget
I'm missing something in my life
Or why would this be so?
Afraid to hurt, to be alone
It's love I'll never know.
