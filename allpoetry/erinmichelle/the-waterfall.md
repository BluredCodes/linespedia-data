---
title: "The Waterfall"
writer: "erinmichelle"
slug: "the-waterfall"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/544-The-Waterfall-by-erinmichelle"
---

A cold mist sprays us;
And we laugh together.
Our bodies are numb,
But not from the water.
We lie together on the ground
But the ground is not hard 
Beneath us,
For everything in this moment
Is soft, and warm
And perfect.
We feel eachother 
And we feel our surroundings.
We hear the rushing water
Around us, and the birds
Up above.
Sunlight illuminates us
As our bodies entangle.
Soon, nature's music is gone,
And we can hear our own.
It is the sound of our 
Hearts beating loudly,
And the deep breaths that
We take.
It is so amazing here,
In this moment in time.
We are alone,
Yet we are 
Together.
You and I, it is the time
We spoke of,
The time that we promised 
To eachother.
Beginning in bliss, 
Ending in bliss,
It is all we could have asked for,
And more.
We look at eachother, 
And we smile like always,
Beneath this waterfall
That is so sacredly ours.
