---
title: "FOR YOU"
writer: "erinmichelle"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/350-FOR-YOU-by-erinmichelle"
---

If God put a wall between us,
A wall of strength and steel
That reached from heaven down to hell
To try to change the way we feel

I would find away around that wall
I'd drop down in hell's fire
And I'd walk on burning coals
Charged on my desire

And if hell proved to have no hope
I'd climb to heaven's height
And I'd find a door in that wall
And push with all my might

And if that door was sealed shut
I know just what I'd do,
Steel or not, it's just a door 
And for you I'd walk right through

Maybe I'd be broken
Or maybe I would bleed
But no pain could hurt me worse
Than not having who I need

These these I would do for you
Because my feelings are for real
Yes, I'd go to heaven, hell and back
To show you how I feel

So you see what could happen
We may stumble, we may fall
We may encounter demons along the way
But true love conquers all.
