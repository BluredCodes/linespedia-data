title: "Bass and Treble"

Hold your breath 
deep in a bottomless
of mirrored prawns

Circle kaleidoscope 
colors compound 
to once invincible 

Cavern lit glow 
worm yarn gazes
at starlight yawns

Survive restraints 
spin like tops 
on coral collectables 

Clouds will watch 
lichen glare 
become dark receding 

Prism pyramids 
peak the skies from
touches misleading

Predator blackness 
comfort secrets from
colors that bleed

Dream of days 
precious nights wearing
oysters in beads
