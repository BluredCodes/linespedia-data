a million cents buys your fragrance
rub it into the body
on all fours gagged in reverence 
upon me wrapped like a noose
sex blood cosmetics
of bonded slave in passion trapped
i give it to you like that bestiality 
carnal chaos of Sodom gore
i swing stiffly like a  hanging corpse
broken in thirst branded with scorn 
i love it when
thrown down cursed 
i build a ladder
of your scars
and climb on every rung
oil slick marked 
back tires foul and loose 
12 years spread like mange
with streaks of sin and cruelty 
blasphemy with penetration
our positions exchange
this meat your only reality
scabs itch lips drip
make it all fit 
with all your might 
up squealing to shove 
filled with a morbid mans cream 
lubed to fit like a glove 
cut more than the seam 
burning ache of bleeding love
spread out before me
to stain red this white feather dove
