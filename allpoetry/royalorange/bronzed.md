---
title: "Bronzed"
writer: "royalorange"
slug: "bronzed"
source: "AllPoetry"

... there are lots of different types of strength

know that

know myself.....yourself

even the things no one likes to talk about in themselves


and yes, you are very strong..thats what i love about you the most


including everything

that's what you love the most


your strength, yes..many forms, of course
grow into myself is all you could ask for
all you'll get
if you didn't have it, you never would have caught these eyes

I know

never would have caught these eyes ... 
if it wasn't for persistence.... 
And gentleness...... 
strength in pursuing what you wanted

understand
I'm not done yet.. I have my whole life

not finished being too strong it kills me.. 
in the same way.... only to evolve into a deeper being.. 
holding more complexity and knowledge

pursuing you always

so.. we have our whole lives


ahead of us

we have forever in front of us

we have the next life for us

we are preparing for that

now

yes

the next life is ours


yes

Bronzed

always


friendship
and love

passion and loyalty
always

truth and trust

infinity and eternity

friendship in one life.. 
love in the next.. and so on like 
forever and always
a chain
that binds my heart to yours
unbreakable

meant to be
our destinies are entwined
embedded into each other...
