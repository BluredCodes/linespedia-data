incandescent skull
fallen ladder rings of parallel, neon halo flash eyes 
curl lips to fit around words of your own murmurs
heart like heat, dark bulb, flickering moths circle through the night 
moons hide from the planets axis, run from exploding Saturn
Uranus crystals harden deep in between puddles
electric lips run this hot spinning fan 
light the chilling sun from exhaustion
didn't cool temperatures, engines redden with fusion
welding grind, spits branding flames
oil delirious engravings, whine of parts melting in steam
gauge on hit, full speed 
drag the flesh to remove on the outside, leave the inside for take over
brakes fail with every bend 
brake in a thousand pieces to pleasure, non stop
tires smoke like a gun, a little faster away from everyone
ignite reload compress
