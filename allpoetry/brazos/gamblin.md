Gamblin'

Throw your money on the table
and roll the dice
Holler until your face is blue
Lady Luck might be nice

Wait for cards to fill a straight
look at poker faces
Don't count your money, no not now
look instead for aces

Into a machine, feed your coins
and wait for bars
If they line up, you'll get a win
dream now of new cars

Watch while the big wheel spins and spins
let the ball drop
Red, black, odd, even,what will it be
win, you're heart might stop

That nag looks good, bet a hundred
smack on it's nose
If he wins, its off to the ship
an island to repose

And if you lose? Ah, well, that's fate
get back to work
Make one more check, then what the heck
your gamblin' you can't shirk

Head for bright lights, your loot lay down
pass not a dance
Gamblin's in your blood, you hear me stud
go now, take a chance!
