LOG IN

Log in, now......back into your heart
Log in, now.......back into humanity
Log in, now.........try to do your part
Log in, now...........face up to reality

'Whilst be the dreams that built your youth?
What happened to the undaunted bravado?
 Where is your unwavering faith in the truth?
Why does your soul now travel incognito?

Process data, now.......seek your heart
Process data, now.......find your humanity
Process data, now.......do your part
Process data, now.......this is the reality

Winds of time blow against you now!
Flames of faith have turned to ashes!
Fields of love you no longer plow!
Waves of anger have led to crashes!

but now...

You have a new OS.......your heart returns
You have a new OS......humanity blooms
You have a new OS.......your psyche learns
You have a new OS......reality looms

Now when you shut down, you can smile.
Or hit restart, and start a new life.
Maybe not shut down, just linger awhile.
For you can do it all now, without any strife.This is much more than a computer analogy.
