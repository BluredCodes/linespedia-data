Darkness

It's really dark in here, I can't see;
Any person or place, not even me!!!
I wonder where I am, or how I got here;
This place of darkness, now grows my fear.
I thought I had been in darkness before;
But now I know, this is something more.
No light can I discern, not glint nor ray;
Whatever happened, to the light of day?
I sense things around, but cannot see all;
At this place, this great black wall.
In a place this dark, no eyes really matter;
For I can't see, what things do scatter.
I grow fearful, of things that be in here;
Deprived of sight, I know not what's there.
I can't reach out, I might touch something!
And I may not like to touch that being.
And, what is that, a light over there?
Though it be not much bigger than a hair.
The only light in the darkness, what could it be?
Mommy? Mommy? Are you coming for me?
