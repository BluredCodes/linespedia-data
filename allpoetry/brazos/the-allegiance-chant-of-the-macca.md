O', king of kings, these are worthy men;
                                                 Men of such worth are seldom found;
                                               O', king of kings, these are worthy men;
                                                 Men of such worth are seldom found;
                                                 They prove their worth with their loyalty,
                                                 For they are loyal to only you;
                                                 They prove their worth with their bravery,
                                                 For they are brave for only you;
                                                 They prove their worth with their tenaciousness,
                                                 For they are tenacious for only you;
                                                 They prove their worth with their ability,
                                                 For they  hone their skills for only you;
                                                 Think not! that they would betray you,
                                                 They would rather die than to do so;
                                                 Think not! that they will not sing your praises,
                                                 They will do so every day and night;
                                                 Think not! that they will forget about you,
                                                 They will have you dwell in their heads forever;
                                                 Think not! that they would not die for you,
                                                 They willingly offer their lives in your defense;
                                                 O', king of kings, you are of good fortune,
                                                 Men like these are hard to find;
                                                 O', king of kings, your bounty is increased,
                                                 Men like these are hard to find;
                                                 O', king of kings, your future is brighter,
                                                 Men like these are hard to find;
                                                 O', king of kings, you are even more noble,
                                                 Men like these are hard to find.
                                                 O', Eperon, think now of your luck,
                                                 For these men, Zorrkahn and Scopon,
                                                 Have pledged their lives to you forever.
                                                 Of such fine matter is Allegiance made,
                                                 And Allegiance is now what you have. 

                                                 Take it not lightly!
                                                 SO SAYS THE MACCA!
