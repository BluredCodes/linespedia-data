Winter

I live in Houston, you hear me,
Houston
We're not supposed to have winter, not
Winter
So why is it so cold, as the north wind
Turns
It's the first week of March, it can't be
Cold
The wind's in my face, it's cold, cold,
Colder
As far as Houston goes, this has got to be the
Coldest
And I stand in my yard, with my beer in my hand,
Frozen!
