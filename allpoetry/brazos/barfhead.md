BARFHEAD

B is for the Breakfasts that you up-chucked,

A is for All the remnants on your shirt;

R is for the birthday you spent Ralphing,

F is for how far the chunks have Flown;

H is for How often that you vomit,

E is for Every meal you’ve lost;

A is for All the stains on your carpet,

D is for your porcelain Domain;

Put them all together, they spell

BARFHEAD!

And that’s anyone who is a-reading’ this here poem!
