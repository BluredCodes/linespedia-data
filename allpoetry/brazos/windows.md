Windows

Look out your window, at nature fair,
Or go to your den, your PC’s there.                                                                   Gaze out at the world we all do share,
Natural, video, as you dare!

Or turn on the tube, look at the news,
That is mostly bad, and gives me blues,
Or go on the net, and shop for shoes,
Maybe hardware.com, buy me some screws!

Inner, outer windows are the same,
There is much to like on both of them,
Are you In looking Out once again,
Or Out looking In, writing a poem?

Out or In windows are much the same,
You'll find something you like, or not,
You just have to learn to play the game,
Let windows joy you, give them a shot!There are a lot of windows in our lives besides the ones on our PC, LOL!! This is but a couple or so........
