Love, 
                       is some-thing
                        we all need...
                       It blooms like 
                          a flower,           
                         grows like
                           a seed
                        With the right 
                          nutrient's
                            Light,
                             air,      
                        soil,and water
                        Love can remain 
                         in our heart
                           for-ever!I sure hope, what happened last, time!!! Doesn't happen again!
