Empty 
                       is my 
                       heart
                      
                      My mind 
                      stricken
                        with 
                        pain  
                  
                   Burred is my soul
                         for
                   I don't wish to
                        remain    
                 
                       Tragic
                    are memories
                        kept
                         
                      Treasures 
                   a distanst time
          
                      Travling 
                       through
                      the years
        
                      Endearing
                 thought's of wisdom
                     embleshished     
                      with tears
        
                     Coping with 
                       sadness 
                  all the constent
                        fears
        
                      Happiness
                   yet to search for 
                        pity
                    is all I find  
      
                     Joyous love is
                     what I am after
                    one of a special
                          kind    
          
                     Darkened is my 
                          soul
                     You must truly
                          guess
   
                      My only goal
                     I will confess
                     letting of a sigh
                   
                      I want to live!
                         not, die!
