########### 
                           ########                     

                     A knight stand near
                        a castle wall
                       made of stone brick
                             
                       His thoughts bitter
                         A mind truly ill
                            and sick
                                           
                       He search his maiden
                             in waiting
                         That he had loved
                          from the start
                                                
                        But, he was twisted
                           corupt and mean   
                           For his maden had
                               seen... 
                            so she part
                           
                            Even though 
                          She loved him so
                         for he didn't know 
                         how much she cared  
                          right down to his
                              very soul
                          
                          She left she knew           
                           it could not be...
                                            
                           In the dugeon he
                              did  lay
                          for he could not
                                 see... 
                                                
                                 Yes, 
                             he was alone
                            deep within the
                                 dark
                           
                          No candle nor torch
                                to spark! 

                          Burred in that lair
                            he was with a
                            wicked dragon 
                               heart!
