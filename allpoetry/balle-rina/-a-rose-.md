---
title: "~~~ A  Rose ~~~"
writer: "Balle rina"
slug: "-a-rose-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2970-----A--Rose-----by-Balle-rina"
---

Beautiful scent
    my favorite flower
    It's as though there
           magic
    some translunent power
       A sign of love
      making our heart
          rejoice               
         mind sing 
                              
       a rose is special
      in it's own certain      
             way              
        When given to 
         some-one we care 
             a-bout  
        it may make them 
             smile
        and brighten there
              day!
