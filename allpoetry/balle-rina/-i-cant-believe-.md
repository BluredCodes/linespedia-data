title: "~~~ I Can't Believe ~~~"
writer: "Balle rina"
slug: "-i-cant-believe-"

I can't believe
                               you are the way
                                   you are!
                             
                              You don't want me
                                  to 'think'
                                 what, I want!
                           
                              You, don't want me 
                              to do what I want

                               I can not even say
                                   what I want!  

                                You only want me to
                                        be
                                 what, you want! 
                             
                              This, is what scares
                                       me!
                              This, is what is true!
                                      
                                Some times my heart
                                  aches,so badly... 
                              
                              I wish, I didn't even 
                                    love  you! 
                       
                               So, I don't know what 
                                       to do?   
                                
                               I don't know what to 
                                       say?  

                                Only maybe I would be
                                      better off...
                                
                                 If, you were to
                                      go-away! 

                                 This is what hurts...
                                 This, is what I fear!
                                  This, is why I tear!

                                  Because, you don't
                                       love me
                                    for who,what, 
                                        I am                             
                                                    
                                   You just love me!
                                        for...
                                 
                                  What, you want me
                                        to be!
                               
                                 This, I can no-longer 
                                          see...
                                    
                                     I am empty...
                                  I have nonthing left!

                                    So, Why, don't you 
                                       just, leave!
                                     and, let me be!!!These, are some of  my real feeling's... Pretty deep, Huh? I also have other versions to this poem as my others.
