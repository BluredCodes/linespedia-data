title: "Jasmine"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "jasmine"
source: "AllPoetry"

That heavy musk permeates the night
Sickly sweet and darkly bright.
Of woman it speaks and gently takes
A hold, as the beast awakes
Wild passion of raging form
Smooth pleasure from scent is born.
