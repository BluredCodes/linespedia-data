title: "Disappointedly happy"

Life is pain and grueling grind
Full of sadness and grief
An ever constant proof
Of Rape and pain, the murderer and the thief.

Then you question “why do I stay?”
Why live in society’s sty
When the grass is so much greener
In the field on the other side.

Well in all the pain I seem to grin
Adversity is the other man’s sin
And I am just disappointedly happy 
Laying here in the mud of my kin

I taste that rush of caring beyond my needs
Stupidly I drink from the tainted fountain 
Of another’s troubles
None of which I can claim

You took me in your arms, that day
And cradled my head against the storm,
The longing and the loss left me then
So whole I stay

Disappointedly happy
