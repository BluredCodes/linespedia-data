title: "Sleeping (A Bad Memory)"
writer: "Paul B"

I lost her as she lay in my bed,
Gently sleeping
Her body and mine
Close as we were
Lay apart and even our minds couldn’t touch.

I missed her as I lay in my bed
Gently crying
The wind blew outside
Inside too.
And still she slept, gently breathing.

Two bodies, two people, one bed.
Two souls and two fates.
I prayed for sleep
I prayed for dreams
And I fled to memories of one past.Just a bad memory of a bad time
