---
title: "Another kiss"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "another-kiss"
source: "AllPoetry"

And you kissed me
A taste so sweet
I laughed inside
And you held me 
An embrace so complete
We were closed and deified

Yes you kissed me
From your soul so strong
Such power, such hunger
You drained me
Sweet sleep never wrong
In your arms 
Deep in wonder.
