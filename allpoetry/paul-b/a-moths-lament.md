---
title: "A moth's Lament"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "a-moths-lament"
source: "AllPoetry"

Plagued by my power 
To dazzle and delight
Hooked by my desire 
For the noise and the light
A moth to the flame 
I dance around my fate
Rushing headlong to doom 
I could never wait.

But I will burn 
And I will fry
Incandescent in my glory
Yet fated to die
A sad fool I will make
No truths or lies
Ultimately useless
As one who dies
