title: "Boredom"

Boredom the deadly sin
Grabs me, 
holds me,
I’m caught within.
Satisfaction the deadly curse
Of bourgeois existence,
Suburbia nights 
And things far worse.

The mundane, the ordinary
Just enough but never  full
A wicked net 
For placid fish
Paradise for a fool
Save me from a lethargic dream
A trance, a coma.
Nightmare of peaceful wish
