---
title: "Silence and Doom"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "silence-and-doom"
source: "AllPoetry"

Mood swings through my body
Gently sways the founding of my life.
I never felt this way before
Never did joy cut me like a knife.
Never knew it was in me.

A poem without rhyme
A life without rhythm
Just patience and lust
The fear and the trust
Never could deal straight

A saying without meaning
I'm not sure this is really me
But I tap out the keys
And read what I wrote
I know it's my soul.

It's late now, the stars are out
Darkness and Bright
I wonder what you think
I'm scared of your choice
I'll cower at your voice.

Is it wrong?  The secrets and lies
I don't consider it too deep
Maybe you see it through my eyes
I'm cold now
Chilled on this hot summer night
