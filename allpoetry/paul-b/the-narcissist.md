---
title: "The Narcissist"
writer: "Paul B"

Hug me and Hold me
Comfort me and care for me
Embrace me and enslave me
Hurt me and help me

Touch me and taste me
Caress me and Curse me
Feel me and free me
Squeeze me and strangle me

Beat me and breathe me
Like me and love me
Hit me and hate me
Do me and damn me

Feed me and fondle me
Lick me and leave me
Kick me and kiss me
Make me and murder me
