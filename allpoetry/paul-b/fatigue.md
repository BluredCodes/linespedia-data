title: "Fatigue"

It seems like an ageSince I penned a thoughtI had so much to sayWords lost in a mazeFeelings frustratedMeanings just meaningsNo, it’s not distressNot emotionI don’t feel it lessI just seemed to die,Just deny.Too much passion.Sapped I lay,Maybe drained I’ll stayI don’t knowJust hope for a signThat the life of the livingWill again be mineI’m tired, feel my ageNo wisdom No sageManNaked But still just man.
