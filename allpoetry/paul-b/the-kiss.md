"Kiss me" he begged,
"Take my hands and cradle my head
And feed your hunger.
Don't refuse me now."
She kissed him
And starving as she was
Devoured him whole

"Kiss me again", his passion spoke
She answered his plea
And laughing they broke
The hunger still free.
He said, "I want  your soul
Breathed into my head.
Your power to be mine,
Take my soul instead.

So they kissed again,
Biting and nipping,
Passion and power,
Hunger and need,
A kiss of lovers.
