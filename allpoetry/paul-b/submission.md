Restored we met one twilight day
Many words left unsaid 
But pain was there, still deep, still tied
By lives we touched
And the days that passed by

We touched again in bright midnight
Searching that moment once again
A whisper we had felt one summer’s night
And missed our winter long
A breath as soft as a lover’s sigh

In the soft glare of a hard Sun
I spoke my mind
Laid bare my soul to your hand
To be touched and caressed
Like soft silk and gentle lie

Submission
