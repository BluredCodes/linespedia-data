title: "Will She Leave Me?"
writer: "Paul B"

Will she leave me?  
I don't know.
Would she stay?  
Well wouldn't she say so?

Can I be so bad, such a difficult man?
Maybe
But will she leave me?
I don't know

I don't want out
I've said it time and again
I keep trying 
Repeating the same refrain

But she could leave me
Before we make it whole
Hope is tenuous
For a despairing soulAnyone been there?
