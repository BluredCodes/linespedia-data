---
title: "I Lust"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "i-lust"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28049-I-Lust-by-Paul-B"
---

I lust for the material world
Its pleasures and desires
The bodily thrill of an unholy will
Its consumption and its fires

I lay content in this physical state
My every whim granted
My taste for life a sharpened breath
Sensation and feeling slanted

But lost I am
Without that essential mark
That being of intimate meaning
Reason and glorious spark

If I hide my sensibility
Stay only in presence felt
Why, I should be free
Drifting in never ending melt

Join with me 
In this pleasure filled palace
Of lucid mind and heightened sense
Gazing on the world without malice

But keep one foot
In the ground firmly planted
For corruption is only one side
The other is already granted
