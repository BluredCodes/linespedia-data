title: "Friendship or Lust"

Since when was my friendship not enough?
Replace it by Passion!
Since when did we loose this trust?
Take me as emotion!
What happened to my companion?
Give me your lust!

Yes my friend, turn to that animal
Of dark beating flame
Curl your tongue around me
Like  flaming pain
Be in me!
Take this now!

Dark and swirling mist
Latin beat and sweltering heat
Beat and stab in this deadly twist
Crush and meld until we meet
And float or drift
In that musk scented bliss

Yes my friendThis is a duet of alternate lines. Either two lovers or your own internal conflict.  I'm not sure which one myself.
