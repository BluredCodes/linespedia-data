title: "I Hate You!"

"I hate you!", she screamed in my face
As she stood in her flaming lace.
"You're running when I need you most"
"Like a phantom you've become your ghost!"

In the hallway crying I stood
I begged her and then I understood
That my life would be false
If  I left this course

She closed the door
I looked  at the floor
No comfort there
It was stripped and bare

No man am I
