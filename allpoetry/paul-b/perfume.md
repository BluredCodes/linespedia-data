title: "Perfume"
writer: "Paul B"

Join me for a day in bliss.
Drift with me on the crest of our kiss.
Let our hands feel our curves,
And bodies mingle and twist.
Sensations like waves rise and crest,
And melt, 
And blend.
Till little death
Holds your sweet breath.
Perfume


And sex!
