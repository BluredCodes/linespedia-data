---
title: "Kiss and Tell"
writer: "Paul B"
slug: "kiss-and-tell"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's always a story, a kiss and tell,
Life and love and all their scenes
Their failure and death as well.

I dream and feel like a bird 
Flying and free from the world below
But love and life hold my wings
They break the feeling of gentle flow.

I wonder where my life will take me
Bouncing and screaming as I go
Don't ever get scared, don't you ever flee.

Did you ever take that chance
And change your way
Did you ever run for your sun
And maybe burn 

Or are you like the rest 
Content or dead
Accepting what you got.

Don't look to me for inspiration
Study my face if you will
It's a mask of pure desperation.

It isn't that I lack the courage
Or the cruelty of spirit
It is only that I live in the real
The pain I cause is the pain I feel.

Hang in there
