title: "Sexual Darkness"
writer: "Paul B"

Too many dark thoughts plague my mind
A swirling mist of sexual delight
Musk and dust of female flesh
Heady nature and painful fire
I taste your skin of scented night
Your sweat upon my tongue
A taste of pleasure sweet
A plane of ecstatic light
Grips my vision as our bodies entwine
And cresting death of passioned wave
Takes my being once again
As you break beneath my hand
