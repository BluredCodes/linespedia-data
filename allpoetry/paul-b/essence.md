When I write I touch deep the essence
That makes me man
Quintessential it remains locked
Even as I feel what should be

It is a mirror that I can never face
For I am my last admirer
And my disillusionment is not 
The bane I want to taste

So short words and a mere hint
Of that soul inside
Is all that I let be free
No more than a shadow, less than a sigh 

Sometimes I ask what would be 
If once
Just once
I shined a light in there

But those shades and shadows
Flake the spirit out
And I’m not Ulysses
To enter my personal Hades

When I write I touch deep that essence….
