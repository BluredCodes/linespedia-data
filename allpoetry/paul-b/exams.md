title: "Exams!"
writer: "Paul B"

Numbers staring out of my head
Stuffed so tight, as heavy as lead
I'm in overload, unable to see the day
When I can release my head of clay
There has to be a better method
To stuff and cram without this effort
God!  I hate the numbers and the equations too
That numb me and leave without a clue
Of the solution to this insane cram
Sod them all, I hate this exam!!For all thowe facing exams, suffering from them and hating every moment!  Thank God I've finished with that!
