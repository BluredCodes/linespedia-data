Love Dream

If I close my eyes really tight I can hear your breath in my ear.
Feel the heat from your body.
Trace your sweet lips with my finger tips.
Looking deep in your eyes.
Wanting to enter your soul, will you open the door.
I look deeper into your brown eyes. I can see passion.
It's like fireworks on a warm summer night.
The passion goes up to my heart and explodes.
Tingles fill my whole body.
I breath faster with ever tingle.
You have taken my heart.
But I gave it so easy.
The fire works turns many colors of love all blending together.
MY heart scream's I LOVE YOU, I MISS YOU,
I really need you here with me.
You control my thoughts.
Like a want to be a star way up in the sky.
You are the light, you are the sunbeam that hits my face right now, 
You warm my heart with the love you share for me.
You are my beacon, I have found your love.And doing this I have found YOU!

My only hope is that my words 
carry meaning to your heart, 
That your heart door opens to me,
And nestles deep inside of your love.
There to stay forever!
