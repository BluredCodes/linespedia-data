Drift with me.

Close your eyes.

Let the soft fluttering of the brain take you away.

Deep into the other land of no return.

The land of passion that never escapes.

And lust turns to flames.

I have a dream.

The mind wonders across your skin.

Touches your lips with the wetness of my flavor.

Looking deep in your eyes.

I can see the happiness that rushes through your veins.

My lips touch yours once again.

The quivering of your soul dwells within.

There is happiness deep in my heart.

I drift deeper into the dream. 

I can almost smell your skin.

My heart burst with joy.

I am in your arms again.

Welcome to my dreamland.

I find happiness in this special place.

Wrapped in your loving arms.

Then I awake.
 
Then my body hungers to dream with you once again.
