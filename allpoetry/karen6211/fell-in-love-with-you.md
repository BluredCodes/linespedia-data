Why is it always my fault?
Why am I always wrong?
God made me with eyes.
And yes, I can see.
Why does it always have to be me?
He also gave me a heart.
You think I have no feeling.
But  I do!
Every time we talk  I’m the one that ends up blue.
With all of this why can’t my heart give up on you?
It’s simple you see.
Cause I fell in love with you.
