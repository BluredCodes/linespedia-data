Existence Of You In Me

My Heart is pounding
It feels your embrace.
The wanting of your touch,
Bring a smile to my face.
The hunger is real,
The want is strong.
What will I do, If I find out this is wrong?
My thoughts rush through my head.
Like a mighty wind that blows.
It let me have the answer to my Question.
That is nobody really has the distance of 
how far the heart will go.
With you my heart smiles and this I know.
I will hurt in my heart, because you will go!
I have taken you as you are!
I will see you’re ever existence.
I will remember you after there is no more.
I will not forget.
And I would not change a thing.
Even if my heart would of let me.
Here are a few things I hope you never forget!
