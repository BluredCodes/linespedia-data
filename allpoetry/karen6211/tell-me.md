Tell Me

Purple Passion.
Heart's sea.
All I need to know!
Is do you want me?
River is blazing.
You I'm not chasing.

Give to me your love.
On wings of a dove.
Set me free!
Or let me be!

Nectar from the vine. 
For you I won't make a shrine.
Would you like to dine?
Come to me and make chills run up my spine.

All I desire is for you to be mine.
Our heart and bodies will then entwine.
Just tell me do you want me to eat from your succulent vine?
