Within 

I see sun set. 

Early morning haze stretched across the sky. 

Leaves of green hanging from a tree. 

Feeling your soft lips, warm skin. 

Sun dancing on your skin.

Want from within. 

Oh how my body longs for you.

When I smell, and touch your skin. 

Passion starts to burn from within.

‘Touching my skin.’ Want from within. 

Oh how my body longs for you, heart throbbing, passion burning within. 

You’re soul is there! 

Fluttering of my heart. 

Wanting special thoughts of you. 

The lingering thought of your skin. 

Imagination overtakes me. 

Body is wanting. 

Heart is throbbing. 

Longing on your next word. 

Closing my eyes. 

Brain is searching. 

Skin tingling, you’re here with me. 

I see you hear with me in my thoughts. 

You become a need. 

My thoughts explore you. 

Longing to look deep in your eyes. 

The Sweet smell of your skin touches my senses. 

Wanting your body, I screaming with every caress. 

I become breathless. 

You have entered me. Became one, feeling, touching. 

Breathing with words.

Moonbeams stretched across the sky. 

The day has come to and end!
