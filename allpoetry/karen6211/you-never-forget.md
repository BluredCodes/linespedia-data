You never forget!

Once you fall you can never go back.

Life takes a new turn,

Your heart lets love in, waiting for the passion needed.

Then things change!

Tears start to flow from the heart.

You can never go back.

A cover of tears appears. 

You cry, You loose. You try to live.

But you can never go back.

The wanting of your words before the haunting of my heart.

You try to move on.

That is the sad thing about Internet Love.

You win you lose.

Maybe one day your dreams will come true.

But there is no turning the page back.

So walk softly on the heart

Let it rest in peace and soft kindness! 

There too linger forever.

Cause you will never forget!Dedicated to all who have fell for words on the Internet.
