Gone !

Love is like a helpless butterfly that had its wings yanked off.
No beautiful wings that move in the breeze.they wore tore to threads.
The butterflies tired body goes on.
It ends up struggling without any hope.
And in the end it slowly lays and withers away.
The life goes out of the beautiful thing god created. 
Then Death happens, No more love, Beauty gone.
Even with all this life must go on!
