Heart Took


It dwell's in a cage. 

Where it's dark, and alone.

Was put there by a man.

Who was afraid to be left alone.

Another love who left him alone put these fears there !

My heart is a lonely place.

He took my smiles turned them into his fears.

And now all there is left is tears.
 
My heart cry’s for love.

It wants to be let out so it can enjoy life again.

Oh my why can't he understand. 

There’s more to life than just his wants!

I need my life, Smiles, Tears, and my own fears.

I don't want to live in his shadows.

I want to make my own.

Beside him!
