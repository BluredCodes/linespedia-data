The Pillow 

Can you still see the green pillow? 
Where you once wanted to lay down your sweet head. 
So I could run my fingers through your hair; 
And trace you face as well. 

Looking at your sweet face. 
As sweet love songs danced through your head! 
Do you want to hear my heart beat? 
As it longs to reaches out trying to cuddle your head. 

YES my heart still thinks about this. 
How the thought of you lingers in my heart and head. 
My heart screams out to you. 
Come lay your head on my pillow. 
And enjoy the love it has to offer. 
As two bodies lay cuddled in bed. 

Do you ever think about this? 
How two hearts could have a chance to love so deep? 
If only with a pillow dream, this love could be? 
How the mind can wonder so deep? 
I do!
