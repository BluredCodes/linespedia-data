Letter To My Friend! 

I saw your name cross the screen today. 

I smiled, and then you went away. 

Spirit grew in my heart. 

I lowered my head. 

Then said a silent prayer. 

My dear friend, I wonder what’s going on. 

Has your heart been broken? 

Maybe others have been broke too! 

Wondering still, have I done something wrong? 

Tell me, I do need to know! 

Maybe I did not listen. 

If so, I'm SORRY! 

Should I say a prayer for you? 

I will pray every hour for you, to God above! 

Will your name ever stop long enough, to say hello? 

I sure do hope so, I MISS YOU! 

Could it be that your heart cry's? 

My words can become a bandage for your heart! 

Maybe it cries for something you want so bad and can't have? 

You might find out others have also! 

Have you lost the spirit to trust? 

I still trust you! 

I promised you a long time ago, 

I would be a friend to the end! 

Are you still mine? 

I have big ears! 

And I try to listen well! 

Come to me then, tell me your tale! 

God gave us friends that listen for a reason. 

I'm here! Remember friends to the END! 

Unless you don't want a friend! 

Just maybe tell me, I need to know !Freinds are so important to the soul.
