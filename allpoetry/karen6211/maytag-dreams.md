Maytag Dreams. 


Little red hat!
 
Maytag dreams.
 
Two bodies entwined together.
 
Spin cycle dreams.
 
Turn on the switch. 

The motion begins. 

I want to ride your Maytag. 

And hope, there is no end. 

I don't want a repairman. 

No, I don't want this to be. 

I only want you and me 

In this Maytag dream.
