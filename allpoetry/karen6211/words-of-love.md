Words Of Love 

                  She says to him:
                     I need you!
                     I want you!
                   I think of you.
           Your presence is always with me.
                  You make me whole!
           I want to give you the total me!

                  He says to her:
                I'm in need of you! 
            The want for you is strong!
        You travel my thoughts all the time!
 I can always feel your soft fingers caress my skin.
                  You walk my heart.
               My heart beats for you!

                  She says to him:
                  In a soft voice!
              I will love you forever.
                  And then a day!
