Passion Burns


Lay me down in a lush green forest.

Cover my body with your warm soft skin.

Make me dream of moonbeams with every touch of your skin.

Whisper softly in my ears.

Tell me everything I want to hear.

My body begins to burn.

The flames build up inside.

I slowly feel the passion rage in me.

Make my body want you with every inch of its desire.

Oh Baby please  will you take me there!
