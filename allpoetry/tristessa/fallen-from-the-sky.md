---
title: "fallen from the sky"
writer: "tristessa"

its another blue moon eclipsing
i see spirits in the shadows
silently beckoning for me
but i cant seem to find my soul

i've fallen from the sky
like a broken angel
but i never got to fly with these chains anyway
i've fallen from the world
and i just cant find my way home

its another filthy, glorious sunrise
and the spirit of the sky is awakening
but she's not by my side
and i cant seem to bring her back


because i've fallen from the sky
and now it's beyond my grasp
but the glass wall between us is stronger than fear
i've fallen from grace
and i'm stumbling blind along a rocky path
