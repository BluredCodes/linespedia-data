nothing but mist surrounds, 
the dark cloud over my head
isnt it sad when your darkness fades
and you have to see the sun again

and i looked at your smile again
it brought it all back to me
wish everything were black and white
hey faded grey its all i see

hey faded grey i need a shadow
i need something to fight
hey faded grey i covet blackness
wish i were black or white

but i am just faded greythis isnt very good... but its been kicking at my temples to get out, so i had to get it down :o)
