title: "clean"

an angry priest in scalding white
and a bald spot for a halo
standing like a stage prop
stared relentless at my red eyes
and called for my dirty confessions
and stripped me down like madness
and tore away my band-aids
and rubbed my face in lunacy
and said i'd never be clean...psychiatrists suck
