---
title: "wingless in blue eyes"
writer: "tristessa"
slug: "wingless-in-blue-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

the water crashes in,
to corrode into the sand
smoothes out the cracks
pools between my toes

like the pearly tears of a million pixies
crying for their saddened queen
she stands wingless in blue eyes
watching the tears roll down

a thousand tresses falling
in gravity's piecemeal waves
my lovely indigo fairie
i will wipe your tears away

the water crashes out,
to disperse as a million jewels
grains of salt crystals appear
from our tearstained cheeks
