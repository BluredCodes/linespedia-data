title: "woman in the mirror"

woman in the mirror
i tire of your red eyes
and your crooked half smile
that peeks from painted lips
no rouge will hide the battle scars
those faded prophecies
woman in the mirror
you'd bend to a strange enbrace
as your waves of dark tresses crash
to sugggest an unearthed beauty
all divinity a lie unspoken
of a voluptuous untruth
woman in the mirror
i don't believe in you
