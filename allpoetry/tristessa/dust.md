she stands weary and silent
in the coldest moonlit eve
the lunar glow sadly bathing 
on the palest of dusky petals, 
skin, draped over jutting bone
crying tears of glimmered pearls
in a world made out of floating dust
and seas of angel's sweetest grief
with pair of broken wings
her father made for her
dreaming of her once upon a timeme... romanticized
