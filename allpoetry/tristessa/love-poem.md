saw a picture of you today
a million diadems for your hair
silken and matted
i would give to you

for the heaviest mask
couldn't cover the place
where my kisses ventured
unhindered to your lipsi believe i shall add more later. just in a fit of madness i wrote this... as a photo overwhelms with memories best forgotten
