found my life at dusk; rising free
the dark moon, in his melancholy
watches the sun set, flames glorious
and willows casting the silent specters of dusk
and fingers of fire fading from sight
and the aurora dawning in fiery light
and the dark hills rising jagged shadows
and funeral pyres of a thousand tomorrows
are flickering before my lowered eyes
for a silent prayer; that the sun will rise
for dusk's light lingers, a pale prophesy
over a moonlight-dappled endless seaHello.
