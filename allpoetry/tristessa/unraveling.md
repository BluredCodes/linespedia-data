---
title: "unraveling"
writer: "tristessa"

i fell from someone else's sky
fizzled out in someone else's tears

shattered to a million peices
and cut my feet on the shards

unravel me 
i need to deconstruct
unravel me
before i self destruct

wrapped myself in insanity
danced in the darkest parts of the sky

i fell back into no one's arms
and i think it broke my head
