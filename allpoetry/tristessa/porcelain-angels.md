title: "porcelain angels"

porcelain angels smile impudent
from counter tops of marvelled grace
fingers upon their soundless trumpets
in flowing garments yet unstained
they sing of this blessed silence
dancing in a frozen time
they smile kindly upon my madness
as false as god or i
