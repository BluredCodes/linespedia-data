psyche adorned her sister's hands
let them lead her to the cliff
her endless love she threw to fall
into an endless sea

psyche cried with empty arms
acid tears to burn my thoughts
my new philosophy's in flames
and i'm standing watching it burn

psyche kissed the monster's lips
and found the words between the lines
psyche gave her lover's trust
but without darkness she was blind...i have to do a book report on greek mythology... this is what came of THAT...
