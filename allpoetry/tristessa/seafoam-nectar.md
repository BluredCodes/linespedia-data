---
title: "seafoam nectar"
writer: "tristessa"
slug: "seafoam-nectar"

the sea echoes 
with infinite crests
spraying softly miles 
as salty mists

and through a thousand wars 
and a billion tears
and sacraments lost 
over wretched years

nature's rebellious child remains
eternally deep under questing rains

cherishing ev'ry horizon, 
drowning ev'ry star
swallowing the sun 
as we watch from afar

and mighty rivers crash 
to a halt in her name
she consumes my fire, 
she drinks my shame

her endless waves frothing 
in maelstrom's blue rejoices
to see me once again
i dance inside the voices

of the sea-winds singing
and the sirens dancing 'round
bathing inside her seafoam nectar
there's not a tear to be foundoooh i love the beach!
