Victoria smiles with uneven eyebrows
from a photo album long undusted.
Outside, rains, heavy with ashes and angel's 
tears, fell sparkling and dead to the caking mud,
and Mommie and Daddy battled, reckless
weaving their spells of abandonment.
She sits idle in youth's oblivion;
a lazy smile graces large features blurred,
and i cant fathom this blank little girl
the way she's smiling in the wreckage.not done yet...
