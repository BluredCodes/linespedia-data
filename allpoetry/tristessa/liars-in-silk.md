---
title: "liars in silk"
writer: "tristessa"
slug: "liars-in-silk"
source: "AllPoetry"

i think i'll stalk the moon
with my silken lies to garb me
against the chilly astral wind

that swirls as the turmoil
i follow in the darkness
as you sway to your death dances

a million ballerinas 
coneal their jeweled daggers
will stab me with their hearts open

as they die of my poisons
i unknowingly excrete
i'll outdance your astral wander

and steal your pretty clothes
i stalked the moon to meet you
so we'd die numb in her cold glow
