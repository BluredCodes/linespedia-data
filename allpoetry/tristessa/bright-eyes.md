Mother Mary you forgot my name
I walked in upon your dreams
My trail of breadcrumbs was scratched out
My chariot's got broken wings

If I wander the blue moon forever
Will you look up should you need to cry?
Handed me a shattered glass rose
Swallowed in a Technicolor sky

Champagne lover awoke alone
Hedonistic prophecies in shards
Buys serenity from convenience stores
But she's shattered her diamond ring

You jumped upon my rainbow phantom
Snowflakes shimmer as you melt
Golden as your pretty diadems
Bright eyes burn my frozen faith

Yet I can't bring myself to wake
Because she's my smiling specter
And the real world is looking bleak these days
No one to wipe the tears away
