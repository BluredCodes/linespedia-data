---
title: "everyone hates a scorpio"
writer: "tristessa"
slug: "everyone-hates-a-scorpio"
source: "AllPoetry"

and she says "i'm all alone
on an island
in a daydream"
and she looks at me with
her marble eyes
the kind that i could drown in

and she says, "i love you
and i'm gunna 
make it better"
and she smiles at me
with her curious fingers
the kind that could combust me

and she says, "you're just like 
me and everyone 
hates a scorpio"
and she looks at me
with her specter leaking
and we drown gracefully
