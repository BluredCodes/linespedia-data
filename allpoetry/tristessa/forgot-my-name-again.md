title: "forgot my name again"
writer: "tristessa"

came in today with a whole new mask
and i'm naked but no one can tell
waiting in that plaza to be stoned
but the men never did come

tore off my veil and bared my soul
now he throws it back into my face
and he wont look me in the eyes
but i'm not sure i blame him

and i forgot my name again
no one left to remind me
and i forgot my mask again
and now they know i'm ugly
