title: "sorrows eternal"

she whispers quiet monodies
this sad poetic soul
her voice a great eutony
bursting from her soul
to a sorrow eternal
a journey she has to take
a song of unvanquished demons
she's left crying in their wake
within a paeon rythm
she rocks in silent sobs
swaying to a fleeting wind
she mourns innocence lost
