still waters running deep
silent pools of angel's tears
in a shallow valley green
i'm faded in blue air
the dawn chases my northern star
across the moonlit sky
ripples in my ancient gloom
the dawn breaks for my lovethis makes no real sense. i will prolli add more to it... later.
