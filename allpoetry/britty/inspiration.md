Inspiration.
A feeling
A fire that burns inside
A gift that stirs your mind
Your heart
Your soul.
It comes upon me
Like a thief in the night
One that can desire your wildest dreams
Or dream your wildest desires
Inspiration.
A thought
A hope
A feeling.
