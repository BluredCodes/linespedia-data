A simple seed
Made of many things
Not yet to know
What life will bring

It begins to feel
The trials of life
Shriveling up
And hiding from strife

Others will come 
And crowd your ground
They think they know 
Where real beauty is found

Your roots make you firm
There's nothing to fear
After going through it all
You realize you're still here

Tears fall from the sky
A sprout every hour
You finally have blossomed
