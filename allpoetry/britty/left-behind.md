---
title: "Left Behind"
writer: "Britty"
slug: "left-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Since the beginning of time, there's a place I want to go
I've asked for permission, and yet I still get no.

I did all I could so there could be a chance
But yet I ask again, and I just get a glance.

I do have much support, and everyone's been kind
As soon as the time comes, they'll leave me all behind.

A chance to see new things, the things i'll never know
Experiences i'd never forget, a place where I could grow.

But there's no reason now, how much meaner can they be
I hope one day they realize, it meant so much to me.
