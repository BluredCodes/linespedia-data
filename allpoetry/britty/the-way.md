High above me is an item unknownWhat is it?I don't knowBut I strive to get itSo, I leapBut I can't reachSee, a leap is a complete urge but only half the effortSo, I get a ladderI go up but it stops halfway thereBecause a ladder can only go as far as you take itSo, I climbI slowly but steadily approach the itemFinally, i'm there The struggle is overI reach out but suddenly I slip...Again.Failure.I look down as the ground comes closer and closer...I've fallen Just to find my way all over again
