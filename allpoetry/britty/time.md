title: "Time"
writer: "Britty"
slug: "time"

My past
Is just a memory
Steadily being forgotten
Over time it will be lost
No urge to be found

My present
Stands still
It hurts my heart to feel
Not knowing which way to turn
I'm lost with the memory of the past

My future
Is alone
Waiting for me to come 
I can't go forward with this pain I want to kill
This hole inside only one can fill
