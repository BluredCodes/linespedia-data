title: "Final Decision"
writer: "Britty"

I stumble around
My mind to stalk 
My words are jumbled
I can't even talk

Communication
Has words we should steal
It hurts to say 
These things I feel

A problem comes up
My heart skips a beat
These worried thoughts 
I dare not repeat

It was your word and mine
As one, back then
We battled together
We always would win

But that was then
Our time has come
After all the silence
The deal is done
