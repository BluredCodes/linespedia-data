---
title: "Words not seen"
writer: "Britty"
slug: "words-not-seen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51627-Words-not-seen-by-Britty"
---

Wanting to be apart of the crowd, and show how much you care
But getting a respone that you don't want, as if you're just not there.

Everytime wondering why your answers are unheard
You put in all can, but not even a mind is stirred.

So now why even try anymore, it's not like things will change
Say one thing, then do another, then they call you strange.

Another day, too much dismay, things are still the same
After times were forgotten, now things are rotten, as though you never came

Call it crazy, call it coincidence, call it very dismal
Whatever you say, it seems to me, that i'm just invisible.
