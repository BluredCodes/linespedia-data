title: "Perfection"

You are a rare and precious stone
whose flawless beauty transcends
the temporal, 
and trespasses on the celestial.
You walk with the grace of one
whose body blends in harmony
to the contours of the very earth.
Your voice is as a music softly played
by masterful directorship
that makes me want to dance
with joy unknown to man.
Your smile is as a beam of brightest sun
that reaches through the darkest day,
to bring the light of hope for which I yearn.
I hold within my heart
a deep abiding love for you.
A love that burns within
and melts my mind and soul.
This love I feel is but the love
that's felt by every man that ever looked
upon your grace and loveliness.
Now let these words that bring to life
my inner thoughts
be as a gentle force to lift your soul.
Then let me thus be granted leave to worship
at your temple.
A temple of an ultimate perfection.

                   Written by Gregory Fitt
