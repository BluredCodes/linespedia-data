There plays a line 
inside my head
inside my very soul.
It's coming from a Carol sweet
that's sung with mournful melody
that makes me warm
but leaves me cold.
A melody that's made 
of hope and dreams
and even love
 - or so it seems.
And then this line that plays
and plays inside my head
just serves to make me sad
although it makes me glad.
So answer me.
How can this be?
That just one line
can bring such joy,
yet cause such misery.

                  written by gregory fitt
