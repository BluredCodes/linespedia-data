title: "Wasted Youth"

Today I saw a young leaf die
cut short in prime of life.
I saw it lean and twist and sway
until from Tree of Life
it broke away.
It could not halt its wild descent
as slowly to its fate it went.
It spun and twisted as it fell,
forsaking life, embracing hell;
my tears I could not quell.
Today I saw a young leaf die
still green it fell to earth to lie
amongst those others 
on the ground.
And as the winds blew them around
my heart grew heavy with the
truth
of having witnessed wasted youth.
Today I saw a young leaf die.

           Written By Gregory Fitt
