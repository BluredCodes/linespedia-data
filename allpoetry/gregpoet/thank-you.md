Even now
after all this time
I am still awed by your beauty.

Every time I look at you
I still recall
that special day I fell for you;
so deeply in love with you.
In a moment of halted time.

Every time I see your lovely face
it feels as though 
I've never seen your loveliness 
before this time.

Every day
I fall in love with you anew.
Thank you for being mine.

              Written by Gregory Fitt
