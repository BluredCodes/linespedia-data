---
title: "To Live the Lie"
writer: "gregpoet"
slug: "to-live-the-lie"
source: "AllPoetry"

I lay beside you
full of warmth,
and feel a deep and dreadful cold
that flows like frosty stream
within your veins,
within your very heart.
A heart that offers not the love I seek,
and now I know the truth,
I'm moved to weep.
But still I want none other by my side
and so I cling to this my life, a lie.
The lie that keeps me from a truth 
my aching heart cannot abide.
I know that you are gone from me,
and that our love can never be.
Yet still I beg of you
on bended knee,
that this, my life,
my lie,
you never take from me.

            Written by Gregory Fitt
