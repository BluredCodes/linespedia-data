Beauty is not the sunset
with its wonderfully soft glow
casting a golden hue that captivates
And enthralls.
Beauty is not the gentle swell of the
waves as they make their majestic
Journey towards the shore,
Wearing their shawls of white purity.
Beauty is not the morning mist
Tinged with the birth of a welcome dawn
Bringing to musical life the harmony
Of Nature's symphonic choir.
Beauty is the touch of your hand in mine;
The light in your eyes when our gazes meet;
The joy of your companionship, and
the magic of your smile.
Should there be for me a thousand
Loves from which to make my choice,
I still would choose you everytime
To be my very own.
My secret dreams are all realized,
And every wish that I have made
has now come true because of you;
For yours is Love Divine.
Thank you again for being mine.

              Written by Gregory Fitt
