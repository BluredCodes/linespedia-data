title: "The Sad Storm"

I heard it speak with rumbling tones,
an imperious voice that thundered
through the valley.
A voice that shook the very earth
with awesome authority.
None that heard could help but cower
at the power of the storm.
I saw it flash a look of anger,
a brief and frightening show
of much intense displeasure.
A look that leapt from peak to peak,
that brought the tallest tree
to bended knee.
It heaved aloud a long and heavy sigh
that ripped and tore apart with deep regret,
all that was laid before it.
Then humbled by omnipotence,
its shoulders sagged,
it bowed its head
and bitterly it wept

              Written by Gregory Fitt
