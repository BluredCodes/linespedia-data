---
title: "Visions of  you"
writer: "Bigheadbob"
slug: "visions-of-you"

Deep in the distance,
I cannot see.
Visions of you,
Always plague me.

How can you ever know,
Just how it is I feel.
Sitting here with you,
As my sences reel.

My infactuations,
Holding me fast.
How can I escape,
Your irresistible grasp.
