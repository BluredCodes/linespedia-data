As time slowly fades
into nothing
what we know
and what we think we know
becomes clear.

The pointlessness of life
comes in to full view
you realize how pathetic
you really are
the life you've led
has been an insignificant one.

As time slowly fades
into nothing
what we know
and what we think we know
becomes clear.

You can not change
this life you have
go on an accept it
or don't go on at all
come to realize that
you yourself your whole
life is pointless and
useless to everyone.

As time slowly fades
into nothing
what we know
and what we think we know
becomes clear.This is the very first blank verse poem i ever wrote about a half a year ago.
