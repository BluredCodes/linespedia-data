Respect for you,
I have lost,
On the other day.

I wrote to you,
You did'nt respond,
Like I said to do.

All I wanted,
Was to know,
Exactly where I stood.

If I had,
A chance with you,
To ever be more than friends.

Apparently not,
Cause your a bitch,
I'll hate you forever now.

I was just fine,
With just being friends,
But you took it a step further.

This whole time,
That I have known you,
I've offered my heart to you.

You slapped it away,
I picked it up,
And offered it to you again.

You put me down,
Again and again,
But now i'll never come back.

For you see,
You've lost a friend,
That would have treated you like a queen.Girls can go to hell.(no offence to any girls who read this i sintended).
