title: "In the blink of an eye"

In my minds eye,
Are visions of you.
Always there
Ever present,
Visions of you.

I said to you
A while back,
That I liked you alot.
You said to me
In return,
You just want to be friends.

I said it was ok at the time,
But it really wasn't.
Id love to be your friend right now,
But I want, so much more.

You've read all of the poems.
That i've wrote to you.
After all that you have read,
How can you maintain;
This act like I can be a friend,
And only a friend to you.

You must realize
That I can't,
Because of my feelings for you.
I don't want to say this to you,
But I feel I must;
We can not be friends.

It breaks my heart to say it,
But I know its true.
That if we cant be more than friends,
I will always hate you.

Never showing, but always there,
Hidden from your sight.
Would lie the hate
I have for you,
Buried deep inside.

Then one day
You would say
A very wrong thing to me,
Any my hate would be told to you
For all the world to see.This is my first blank verse poem.  so please give me comments. im very unsure about it at this point.
