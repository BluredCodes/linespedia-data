title: "To the 'T'"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

I might have moved to fast,
And mabey that just scared you.
Please tell me what, is wrong with me,
So I can change for you.

I know that if you wont accept me,
For who I truely am.
Then you are not the girl for me,
There's some else out there.

I still dont understand you,
And I still think your full of shit.
For not telling me your feelings,
After I gave to you my heart.

I told you exactly how I felt,
All the way to the "T".
You gave me nothing on return,
For all my stress, and pain.

Never an "if", or a "mabey in",
You gave to me nothing.
Not a word, not a glance,
You were'nt even willing to take a chance.
On me.
