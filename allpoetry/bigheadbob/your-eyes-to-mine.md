The beauty in your eyes,
Flows deep in to my soul.
Always burning, always bright,
Just like a red hot coal.

Most guy's I know,
Wont look in to someones eyes.
The are false,
And filled with lies.

I will look,
Deep in you eyes,
For you see,
I have no lies.

Keep from you,
I will not.
Any truth,
That you do want.

I dont need beauty,
To fall for girls.
I just need eyes,
Like beautiful pearls.

Any color eyes,
Will always do for see.
The only requirement,
Is to have beauty.

Dont get me wrong,
Your sexy and hot.
But the eyes in your head,
Are what I want.

You have the prettiest,
I've ever seen.
Looking in mine,
And oh so keen.

The way they make me feel,
Oh very deep inside.
Has me wishing you,
Would stand next to my side.

To be my girl,
Forever and ever.
Exhausting my resources,
On one last endeavor.

To wim from you your heart,
Is my final goal.
Tunneling through your body,
Just like a mountain mole.

To find the sight,
I thought I saw.
That has left me,
Struck in awe.

The sight I seek,
Is to see your heart.
Always sweet,
And never tart.

Take from me this poem,
A glimpse into my heart.
Mabey you'll decide,
Me and you should start.

I know you said you want to be single,
But my heart wants more.
Because it's obvious, to all else,
It's you that I adore.
