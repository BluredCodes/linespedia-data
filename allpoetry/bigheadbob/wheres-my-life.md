---
title: "Where's my life...?"
writer: "Bigheadbob"
slug: "wheres-my-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

Where's the love...?
No where!
Where's the truth...?
No where!
Where's the Apology...?
No where!
Where's the compassion...?
No where!
Where's the drugs...?
Right here!
Where's the pain...?
Right here
Where's the sadness...?
Right here!
Where's the suffering...?
Right here!
Where are you...?
Who cares!
