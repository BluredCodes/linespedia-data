title: "Reincarnation of....WHO"

Difficult to decipher,
These thoughts within my head.
How many years must i suffer,
Until I wake up dead.

The road I've been down,
Has been a really bumpy one.
Soon I'll hit pavement,
And live my life on the run.

Accelerate through life,
Until I meet my end.
Mabey make it all the way,
Or to fast around a bend.

To live life forever,
In a fatal crash you burn.
Never knowing nothing,
Or a great lesson learned.

Sometiimes a lesson,
comes way to late.
Like trying to open,
an electric gate.

Once you've touched it,
Your fried to the bone.
Again your dead,
To return; not home.

Away to heaven,
You will go.
After you've recieved,
This fatal blow.

After you've seen,
The master of space.
He will tell you,
To get a new face.

Change your hair,
And get a new mind.
Brand new eyes,
And a new behind.

All that you were,
Has to be changed.
Like fall to winter,
Out on the range.

This feeling I have,
Of deja vu.
Always reminds me,
Of a person named...WHO?
