The eyes of deception,
Clouds the truth.
Insecure resurection,
In the voting booth.

Your eyes, to mine.
No lies, to find.

The eyes to your soul,
Or the door to the truth.
Do not fail your goal,
For the gossip is loose.

Your eyes, to mine.
No lies, to find.

The eyes of honesty,
Will always hold true.
This is not a dream,
I will not lie to you.
