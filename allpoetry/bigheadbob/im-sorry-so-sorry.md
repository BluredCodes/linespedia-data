Im sorry,
For ever bring up us.
Im sorry,
For wrriting you poems.
Im sorry,
For cooking you food.
Im sorry,
For talking to you.
Im sorry,
For listning to you.
Im sorry,
For hanging out with you.
Im sorry,
For rubbing your back.
Im sorry,
For driving all the way to your house.

But most of all,
Through all this shit.
I'm sorry,
I ever met you.The singer from Green Day said it best

"The world owe's me so fuck you"
