title: "The past year (a retrospective)"

I reminded myseclf,
To forget about she.
She that had feelings,
Feelings for me.

I went through a phase,
where I really didn't care.
I just wondered around,
In my heart was a tare.

I had trouble talking,
To girls in my life.
I thought I was better off,
In my room with a knife.

Before long I realized,
The error of my ways.
I opened my heart,
For just a couple of days.

Once it was open,
I was surprised to see.
The number of people,
who care about me.

This may surprise you,
Or you might think I'm sick.
The truth of it is,
My head, it is thick.

My life it has changed,
In the last passing year.
So much is over,
Yet so much is near.

I have no idea,
What I will do.
All i can say is,
I hope it invloves you.
