title: "Heavens Frozen Tears"

Heavens frozen tears,
Landing on the ground.
Praying for your uncle,
To live and stay around.

To live to see,
Another day.
And smell the flowers,
Scent in may.

To go to work,
And enjoy his job.
Your family need not,
Ever sob.

Even if he passes,
Do not shed a tear.
For he is in a better place,
Without lies, deceit, or fear.

I know it hurts to think,
About his fading thought.
From the hardest stuff on earth,
Your uncle he was wrought.

Death can never separate you,
No matter how hard he shoves.
Cause you have something he does not,
That thing in deed is Love.This is dedicated to Jackie's uncle who has just passed away. And to my uncle who has cancer.
