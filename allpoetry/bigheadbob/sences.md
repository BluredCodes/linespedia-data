---
title: "Sences"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

Thinking of you,
All alone in my place.
All my eyes see,
Are visions of your face.
Everywhere I look,
Up or down.
Everyone I see,
A cop, or a clown.

All my eyes see.
Are visions of your face?

Thinking of you,
Trying to make a choice.
All my ears hear,
Are reamnents of your voice.
Listning to a rap,
Or writing you a song.
Knowing the difference,
Between right, and wrong.

All my ears hear.
Are reamnents of your voice?

Whenever I see or hear you,
I always feel good.
If only you knew I felt this way,
Say it to you I would.
