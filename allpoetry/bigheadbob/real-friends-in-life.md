title: "Real friends in life"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

Real friends in life,
Are really rare.
At least the ones,
That really care.

Most of them,
They won't look back.
After they stabbed,
You in the back.

I myself,
Am not like that.
I can not lie,
And that is that.

You and me,
We can be friends.
At least until,
This world it ends.
