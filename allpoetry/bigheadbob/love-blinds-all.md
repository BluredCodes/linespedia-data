---
title: "Love blinds all"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

As depression sets in,
On a cold thursday night.
I realize my life sucks,
And everything wont be allright.

Your standing right behind me,
And you havent said a word.
This silence we are in,
Yes, its quite absurd.

You havent really broken my heart,
So why do I fell this way.
Is it because in tired of holding,
These feelings of mine at bay.

Hate you,
Yes I dont.
Love you,
No I do.

Why do you play,
These games with me.
You know im blind,
And can not see.

I'm starting to see you,
In a whole new light.
And I dont like it,
God Damn this sight.

If these sights are true,
God knows how much I hate you.
If im right, about these sights,
I know how much I LOVE YOU.
