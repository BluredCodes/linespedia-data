A rose is worth,
A thousand words,
So here is one for you.

I know there are,
Alot to list,
So here are but a few.

The beauty in your eyes,
The love from girls to guys.
The softness of your hair,
A cuddly teddy bear.

All of these,
Leave memories,
Imprinted on your mind.

This is a gift,
From me to you,
To make your V-day kind.
