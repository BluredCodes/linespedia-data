writer: "Bigheadbob"

Looking with your eyes
Listning with your ears
Not being able to see
Your deepest darkest fears.

Loving with your heart
Thinking with your brain
After all of this is over
I still cant feel your pain.

Talking with your mouth
Smelling with your nose
When will you realize
That that's just how life goes.

Wanting to know whats wrong
While your hiding in your booth
How many years must I suffer
until I know the truth.
