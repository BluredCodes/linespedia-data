Whole half truths,
And half whole lies.
I am stareing, 
Deep in your eyes.
To see the sight,
I thought I saw.
That has left me,
Struck in awe.
To see the person,
Within your eyes.
Deep inside,
This person lies.
Not afraid,
To be herself.
To put her heart,
Upon the shelf.

To let all those,
Around her know.
That she shows,
The truth.
Kind of like,
Those of old,
The Sayers,
Of the Sooth.
