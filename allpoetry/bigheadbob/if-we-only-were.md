---
title: "If we only were"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

On the other side of my station,
Standing in the light.
Is the most beautiful girl I know,
Half hidden from my sight.

We write notes back and forth,
About how we really feel.
Sometimes joking, sometimes not,
I've never felt so real.

She says to me on this day,
That she wants to be single.
Never knowing but having faith,
That me and her will mingle.

I keep writing to her with my pen,
About how it is I feel.
Hoping that one rainy day,
Her heart from her I'll steal.

I will not keep it for my self,
I will give it back.
Little by little piece by piece,
So our love wont lack.

She says that she just wants to be friends,
And thats alright with me.
Mabey one day she'll take a peak,
In my heart and see.

Just how strong my feelings are,
For her and only her.
Always thinking about how close we could be,
If we only were.
