title: "Easy does it"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

Depressed am I,
Sitting all alone.
I need to tell this world goodbye,
I will not moan and groan.

When I go deaths door,
And bequeth to him my life.
I will lie down in my moor,
And get rid of all my strife.Yes I know its short but its an old one and a frind told me to put it up here so I did.
