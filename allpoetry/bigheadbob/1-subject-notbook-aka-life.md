If your reading this right now,
I must really be dead.
Shot I was outside my school,
In my f**king head.

I know your feeling down,
I know your felling depressed.
But think about it this way,
I'm getting some needed rest.

From all the stress in day to day life,
I am now away.
Never again will I see you face,
Until your dieing day.

Don't cry for me cause I am gone,
Forever, and ever, and ever.
Your tears can never bring me back,
Your on a hopeless endeavor.

Around and around this life cycle go's,
I guess it was just my time.
For all of us one faithful day,
The church bell's will surely chime.

This is a gift from me to you,
From beyond the grave.
I wrote it for you with high hopes,
My memory; you will save.
