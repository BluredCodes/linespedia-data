---
title: "HEY"
writer: "Bigheadbob"

With all that we've done,
And all that has been.
How can you say,
That I'm still just a friend.

We have gone out,
And we have stayed in.
Along the long path,
Just around the bend.

The smiles we've shared,
Are sacred to me.
Is that so hard,
For you to see.

I don't know,
If you just like being chased.
But as for me,
I'm tired of this race.

If I never have a chance,
Of winning from you your heart.
Tell me to leave your sight right now,
And from you I will depart.

Never to return,
To the shores of Jacki.
Or smoke with you,
Any wacky tobaccie.

The fun we could have,
The games we could play.
It's all up to you,
So I'll just say "HEY".
