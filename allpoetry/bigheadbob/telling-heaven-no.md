---
title: "Telling heaven 'NO'"
writer: "Bigheadbob"
slug: "telling-heaven-no"

Lighting strikes,
A shattered tree,
Splinters in my brain.

Visions of,
All of us,
And our F**king pain.

Latley I,
Have go no sleep,
Because of what I see.

Terrible things,
Are always there,
Always plaguing me.

Telling me,
It would be just fine,
To take my life tonight.

Knowing that,
It really would'nt,
Adn things wont be alright.

Confusion of,
What to do,
And where it is I should go.

Straight to hell,
Sounds good to me,
I'll say to heaven "NO".
