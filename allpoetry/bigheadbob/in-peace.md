Thump Thump... Inhale

Thump Thump... Exhale

Thump Thump... Inhale
Thump Thump... Exhale
Thump Thu...
Interupted.
Sitting alone.
go away.
leave me.
everyone else does.

No sorrow.
No pity.
Alone with myself,
Thinking...
about nothing,
Imparticularly important.
Its only life,
no one cares,
get away from me.

Let my heart,
Beat again,
Get away from me.

Let my lungs,
Breath again,
Get away from me.

Leave me alone,
With my thoughts.
BLANK.
Never knowing,
wanting to.
NO!!!!
Leave me.
when you here,
I think.
Leave.
I dont want to,
Just let me be,
you dont.
Always there,
Interupting me.
You cant see,
At least I think.
Get away,
your to deep.
Let me be.
Let me die,
In peace.Kinda confusing I guess. but it means alot to me.
