title: "Dismissed"

Dismiss from me
These shaking hands
And wiery eyes.

Dismiss from me
These tired feet
And worn out mind.

Dismiss from me
This old grey hair
And deaf ears.

Dismiss from me
My battered soul
And beaten body.

And let me be free.Once again its and old one.  dont exactly know what i was thinking.
