---
title: "The day before tomorrow"
writer: "Bigheadbob"
slug: "the-day-before-tomorrow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50479-The-day-before-tomorrow-by-Bigheadbob"

The path of destiny,
Obscured from my view.
All I can think of,
Is me and you.

The first thing I see,
When I get out of bed.
Is a vision of you,
That lingers in my head.

The path of destiny,
Obscured from my view.
All I can think of,
Is me and you.

This vision of you,
Lasts all day long.
Whether I'm sitting at school,
Or jamming to a song.

The path of destiny,
Obscured from my view.
All I can think of,
Is me and you.

I love being around you,
I love when we talk.
I wish you would date me,
We could go for a walk.
