Well, im here..
I don’t care if  your brain is sick
I don’t care if your mind is dead
You know… I don’t care anything about you
I just want you to take my life
I just want to feel pain inside
I need to see someone inside my soul
So… take what I want and get the fuck out of here!
Ok, sorry.. but I don’t see anything in your eyes..
I don’t see your soul alive…well… I guess you mean
Nothing to me…sorry again…but I forgot, you are in heaven now!
