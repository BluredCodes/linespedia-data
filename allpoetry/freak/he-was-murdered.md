He was murdered… I saw his brain in the street
He took my hand and I felt sick, he was playing with
His eyes and I saw a doll killing his soul…
Where are his eyes now? Did you eat his eyes?
Well… are you trying to kill me little doll? 
Ok lets see…
Why you don’t  cut my hands and hit your head with it?
No, don’t laugh… I know you are scared now and my body
Is raw…. So… wanna laugh?
What if I cut your arms and you see the pink blood in the floor?
Like it? Its so funny! You should laugh!
Why did you leave this scars on my face?
I hate you… and I wish you could be dead
So.. he was murdered he just wanted a fuckin whore and he saw a doll
It was you… and he ate your skin without breath!
