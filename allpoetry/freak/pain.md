I feel the pain inside...
    I see your head in my eyes
    I know your blood it's blue
    and I feel like a whore, it is true
    I hate the deep inside and the way you look at my eyes
    so.. I need to feel pain every day and tell every body im not like  the others girls.... so.. i feel the pain inside and I want you to rape my eyes.....
