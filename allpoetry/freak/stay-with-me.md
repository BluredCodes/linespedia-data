title: "Stay with me!"
writer: "freak"

My mind is black
My head live in the space
I have something to say
So please love me anyway
I know you don’t know me
But I know something…
You are my soul and I’m drunk 
I need to find yourself inside my head
I saw your face and I’m afraid…
I felt alive without breath and you
Leave my body without skin
So.. my mind is black
And its hard to think, stay with me
And you will see….
