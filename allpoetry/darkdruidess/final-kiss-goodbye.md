As I watch you sleeping in our bed so beautifully
I know today is the day that my heart will break
Your suitcase is packed and setting by the door
I showed you all that was in my heart and gave all I could
Spent our last night together loving you with all my soul
Reaching those impossible heights that only you can take me to
Filling me with the ultimate bliss and contentment
But now I must tell you goodbye 
My body shakes with denial and the tears fall
Not knowing how I will fill the emptiness
That your departure will leave behind
Our bed will no longer hold the safety and comfort
That once brought me such serenity
Without your wonderful smile this house will turn cold
A place I am not sure that will hold any consolation
Without your tender kiss my mornings will be dreary
Where everyday will just be another day to get through
Without your gentle caresses my nights will be lonely
Filled with a darkness that will consume me
How long will it be before I go insane 
Without you here to hold me and tell me it is all right
Please tell me one more time that you will return
Promise me once again that I will see your handsome face soon
Touch me with compassion as you say farewell
Kiss the tears from my cheeks as yours start to form
Hold me tightly enough to last till you reenter my life
Carry this final kiss with you on your journey 
And remember that there is a woman waiting here for you
That loves you with all her heart and soul




Brad, may this time that we must spend apart go quickly for us.
And know that I will miss you. I love you baby. You will always
be my hero.
