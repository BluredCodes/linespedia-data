Dear Friends: 



Recently I have recieved some nasty (abusive and full of foul language, which I'm not used to)comments from some of the poets (sic) on this site, on my posting, Wars are never won...peace is". This has caused me much pain and hence I am removing ALL my postings from here....I'll be coming every now and then to read your postings though... 



Greg Ashcroft
