---
title: "Friends Change"
writer: "Madriar"
slug: "friends-change"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45109-Friends-Change-by-Madriar"
---

Nothing is the way 
it seems to be.
The once tight bond
suddenly shatters and
the walls that kept you
together tumble and fall.
Feelings are hurt and 
rumors are spread.
Your friendship is no
more and you feel like
the world’s going to come
to an end. 
Your security blanket is 
gone and your standing 
in the middle of no where
wondering what went wrong.
You try and figure it out but
nothing makes sense anymore. 
You try to go back to the way
things use to be but nothing’s
right and you feel like you
started world war 3.
