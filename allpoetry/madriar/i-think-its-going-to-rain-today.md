---
title: "I Think Its Going To Rain Today"
writer: "Madriar"
slug: "i-think-its-going-to-rain-today"

Though you may not see
my tears on the outside,
they are there.
For I promised myself long
ago that I would never
shed a tear in front of
friends.
The darkness has clouded
my thoughts and I can no
longer see the light of day.
My soul cries, for it can
not bear the pain it now
has to endure.
I gaze outside to find that
the sky is gray and the
clouds are dark.
My heart has scar where I
have been hurt and I
frequently feel the pain.
I can’t take this much longer,
my insides are ripping apart.
And I think its going to rain
today.
