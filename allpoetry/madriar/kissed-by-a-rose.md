title: "Kissed By A Rose"

When I say I have been kissed by a rose, it is true. 
He was gorgeous and absolutely perfect in my eyes. 
I would wrap myself around him and stay in 
his sweet serenity.
Then I was caught by his thorns. 
They stabbed and tore at my heart, causing 
utter pain and I bled. I bled until I could 
bleed no more. 
Then the scar appeared, and it burned every 
time I though of you.
