---
title: "Forever Isn't That Long"
writer: "Madriar"
slug: "forever-isnt-that-long"
source: "AllPoetry"

Finding you was the best thing that ever 
happened to me. 
When we were together, the whole world 
was perfect, and all my problems melted
away.
Then the summer came and we went our 
seperate ways. 
I wrote letters, but you never answered
them. 
When I saw you again, you said you were 
sorry and I forgave you. 
Then you said I don't want to do this 
anymore, and we broke up.
You have no idea how much that hurt me.
You left a deep scar that bled and bled
until I thought I was going to die. 
A scar eventually covered the wound, but
that still didnt stop the pain.
It burned every time I thought of you. 
Together forever, well forever isn't very
long.
