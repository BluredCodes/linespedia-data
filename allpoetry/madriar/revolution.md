Arguments stir in court houses,
riots bombard the streets. 
Neighbors turn on neighbors, 
and the true chaos begins.
No where to run, no where to 
hide. I can only pray my upstairs 
room isn’t discovered. 
Morning comes and half the city
is gone to ashes. 
When will the madness end?
