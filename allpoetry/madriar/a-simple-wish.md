Aphrodite in the world above,
Grant thou the love of one so 
fair. 
For my heart cries of loneliness
and my soul longs for happiness.
For to long I have waited, when 
will my turn arise?
For in your eyes, I am just a 
mere mortal, who is unhappy
with the fate she has received, 
but grant thou, please.
Change my simple life into 
something worth the suffering, I 
have bore through the years.
Oh goddess, grant a mortal’s wish
and bring her the happiness and joy
that was meant to be.
If thou shall grant my eternal wish, 
my loyalty to you shall not fail.
I shall always look to you for 
guidance and help as I always 
have. 
I will put forth all effort to do as 
thou pleases of me. 
Your every wish is my command
if thou shall grant my simple wish.
Make me your loyal servant, mold
me in any way thou sees fit.
If my wish is granted, I will have an
eternal price to pay and my loyalty
shall stay eternally thou’s.This is a poem I wrote after Valientine's Day last year...
