Countdown 3,2,1
Bing Bang Boom!
Off goes the space shuttle Endeavor, 
NASA’s latest case and the newest 
American pride. 
Up! Up! Up! Go! Go! Let’s
beat the Russians to the moon.
Away it flies into the atmosphere,
now all we can do is hope and wish 
it luck, that the good- old USA 
will walk the moon.This poem was writen in Chemistry class.. just sort of a random thing.
