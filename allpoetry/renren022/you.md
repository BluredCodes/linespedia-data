You put me up on a shelf,
Like I was dumb and forgotten,
But look at you,
Your soul is rotten.
How many times have I helped you out?
How many times were you stuck in doubt?
How can you just turn away, blow me off, and forget this day?
I can’t believe I’m doing this,
I can’t believe I’m here.
Why do I out up with this s***?
I know you’re never going to change.
You’ll never re-arrange.
You’re stuck this way forever,
And you’ll leave me here to endeavor.
