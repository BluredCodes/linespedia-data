I am always there,
There for you!
Are you ever there,
There for me?
I know you say,
Say you’ll be.
But There’s no way I,
I can believe.
You’ve yet to show, 
Show any real,
Real sense of,
Of emotional feel.
I don’t,
Don’t understand,
Why you have to be,
Be in demand.
It ALWAYS you.
NEVER me.
But I guess that’s the way,
Way it’s going to be.
