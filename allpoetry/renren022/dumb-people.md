title: "Dumb People"
writer: "renren022"

Why do people always have to argue?
Is it something they feel like they have to do?
The world wouldn’t have so may problems,
if people would just learn how to work things out.
That, I have no doubt about.
People will argue until they hit the floor,
even if death was knocking at their door.
That’s so dumb. 
Are people really that stupid!!
I guess so considering that’s what we do!
