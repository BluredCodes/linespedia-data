The pain is hurtful and sorrowing. 
A lot of people go through it all the time, everyday. 
Not knowing the right way to turn,
what will they do?
They’re such a mess with all the pain.
There’s all kinds of pain.
Some worse than others.
How will they ever heal?
The pain just dig into their hearts and souls.
Much, much more just harsh to say.
What will they do the rest of their days?
People need help!
Is anyone there for them?
I just wonder.
