---
title: "My Mind"
writer: "renren022"
slug: "my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Too much,
to fast,
to soon,
to last.
My mind, 
its gone,
too far,
to care.
Things,
messed up.
Never,
go back.
we're stuck,
like a tack.
Put somewhere,
somewhere to stay.
But there will,
be a day,
when we all,
wont stay.
We’ll be gone,
won’t be the first.
Out the door,
flustered,
burst.
Life, right now,
isn’t good.
I want to throw in my towel,
and put on my hood.
Leave this world.
Never come back.
And see how they handle,
life without their "tack".
