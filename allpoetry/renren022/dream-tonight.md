I had a dream tonight, 
A dream of you and me,
I had a dream tonight,
Just the way I wanted life to be,
A dream or love,
Hope,
Happiness,
All things good in life,
Just the way it should be,
I wanted to hold on to that dream tonight,
For just you and me,
I wanted that dream true,
The way life should be.
