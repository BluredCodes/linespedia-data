I see people grow up,
how toddlers sip from sipping cups,
elders need walkers,
teen turn into talkers,
Grown-ups go to work,
babies learn to use a fork,
how the sick go away,
and kids go out and play, 
and you too, shall see,
that very child still in me.
It’ll never leave anyone,
not even when they’re done.
