People tell I’m stupid,
People tell me I’m dumb,
People tell me that I don’t understand,
and that I am numb.
People sit there and diss me in some many ways,
they just started,
and have being doing it for days.
They don’t see what they’re doing,
they don’t see it at all,
they think it’s funny and start laughing.
Every time they push me into a wall,
the wall of humiliation.
