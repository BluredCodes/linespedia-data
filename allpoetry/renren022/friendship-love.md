title: "Friendship Love"

People can be mean,
people can be cruel,
but they’ll be to you,
how they please,
even thought you’re down on bendid knees.

And if you ever need a friend,
I’ll always be here for you,
in which I know you’ll do the same,
I wont put you to blame.

And I’ll miss when you’re gone,
for sorrows travel too,
but ill keep my spirits up,
and not be blue.

I know you’ll be home soon,
then my spirits will bloom,
my sorrows will go away,
and you’ll be here to stay.
