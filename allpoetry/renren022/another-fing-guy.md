You’re a f***ing panzy,
And you f***ing lie!
I can’t believe I trusted,
Another F***ing guy!
What the f*** is wrong with me,
I’m so d*** dumb!
I thought I was cold.
I thought I was numb.
You took my trust,
And used it against me.
What the f*** is wrong with this?
Why couldn’t I just let it be?
What do I mean, "what the f*** is wrong with this?"
What the f*** is wrong with me!?
