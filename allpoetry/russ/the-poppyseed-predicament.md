---
title: "The Poppyseed Predicament"
writer: "Russ"
slug: "the-poppyseed-predicament"

There's a poppy seed, it's stuck in my tooth
What is this all about?
I've gotta go right home, take a piece of string, 
And floss the damn thing out.

I've never endured such pain as this
Could this be from a poppy seed?
I take a glance in my rearview mirror
It's a poppy seed indeed!


Didjya ever choose a New York Style deli 
To fill your somewhat large and very hairy belly.
A tough decision 'tween the tater Knish and Bagel 
I'll take a poppyseed, I said to waitress Mabel.


So, I'm drivin' home to floss my tooth
To extract that poppyseed.
As a gosh darn lug nut flies off my wheel
Toward a minivan from Tennessee.

The minivan driver waves hello
With her big fat middle finger.	
As my right front wheel shimmys and shakes
Until it can no longer linger.	


Didjya ever have yourself a poppyseed
That caused you so much pain and then some misery.
All I did was have a bagel and cream cheese.
Why'd I have to have the one with poppyseeds?



So my Ford Explorer slows down fast
And comes to a 3 wheeled stop.
My fourth wheel continued to bounce and roll
On the feet of an 8 foot cop.

I said officer I'm so damn sorry
My wheel met up with your feet.
But I'm in a strange predicament
And I do not need your heat?


Yes that was a rather bad move on my part
To disrespect a cop in front of Donut Mart
He asked me for my registration and my VIN
I said you have a little frosting on your chin.
  

Before I knew it, he read my rights
And threw me down to the ground
As my face met up with a YooHoo bottle
And I heard a horrible sound. 
 

You see the bottle busted my top front tooth
As I proceeded to shout, 
Yee haw and a Yippie Ki Yay
You knocked the poppyseed out!


There's a poppy seed, it was stuck in my tooth,
It's clear what it's all about
Life, it has its God-awful days,
But ya gotta take the poppy seed out!
