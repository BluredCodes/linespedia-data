The eyes never lie
and broken spirits do fall down
to watch the souls of us that do nothing

to want, is to acquire a vision
yet the discouraged mind
remains idle

No tear from the brave 
could destroy a city
but idle minds 
make for decay

I shall stand up they said
who one might ask
The thousands that did not have the courage to stand
in the face of adversity
but in the midst of adversity
they did stand

I shall stand up they said
who one might ask
they that did not have the courage to stand
in the face of change 
but in the midst of change
they did stand

I do stand now
and present this truth
and my ancestors push me
forward into the horizon
with the integrity that i deserve

A horizon that is brighter than ever before
and in it we will all shine.
