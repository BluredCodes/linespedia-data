Now upon night shall I falterand in blackness are shadows my goallet fleecing and cloaking my standardand the blackness of this be my foldlet me stand in the midst of dark rainbowsthat bend to the curve of each cloud and walk in the shadow of angelsand wherever my soul is allowedSo intent is the soul of departureand the darkness yet that which's unknownso In darkness yet still is my comfortand my curse is to set me alone.
