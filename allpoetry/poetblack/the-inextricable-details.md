days of madness
complicate things
change things into faces

placing spaces in our
lives that we once lived
in our past

but not past lives
but present ones
that rekindle dead flames

and ashes that come
out of graves
that come from condemned buildings

cries that set the sun
and darken new moons
and make life void

come from the graves
graves of madness
made permissible

because madness
has been made
acceptable in a mad society

which makes me mad.
