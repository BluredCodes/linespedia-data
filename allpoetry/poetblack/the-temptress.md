Tell me of your kiss
fair maiden

of which men swoon
and toss away swords
to place them selves at
the feet of such as you

whose eyes breathe fire
into the heart of men of 
youth

whose mouth makes passion
and lips make love

Tell me of your way
whose laughter suggestively
stares men down

and power of desire
that make strong men crumble.

I liken you to my flower
for a rose has thorns

but how sweet is your pain
and exotic is you pleasure

For without you 
I would not know the many
faces passion

and without you 
I would not know the many
faces of love.
