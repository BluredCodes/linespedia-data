As I sit and think
and drink my tears of grief and sorrow
sorrow for the days of my good fortune are ended
and so my thoughts lead me to wonder
what will the new days bring

Will times tell of better day
of cool autumn breezes and higher heights to climb
or warmer summer with balmy days by the oceans
will the well of wealth return
return new unto me again

or have I begun my decent into the belly
that dark place with its smell of resentment
the stinch of poverty and corrupt
is this my new abode

For I have known the way of the righteous
and I have seen the way of the sinner
and I am neither but I stand in the midst
of both 

So my ways will lead me to follow either path...
