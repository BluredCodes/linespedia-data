Looking for something deep inside
Can't seem to find my place in this world
Where can I be myself
Away from all the gliter and glamour
I need some room
to break out my wings and fly away

**

Somewhere in time
I'll find what I need
Just hybernate my time away
Inside my little seed

It's not my time
to break out of here
Clammered up
Too much to fear

Somewhere in time
You'll see me there
Wasting away
and you might just care**Revised
