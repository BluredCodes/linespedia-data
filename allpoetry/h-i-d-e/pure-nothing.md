title: "Pure Nothing"

When I look at you
I see something amiss
Your tortured expression
Hides true bliss
Deep in your soul
I see what you are
Something of heaven
It's not too far
I feel your glow
It waffs in the air
Glistening in the sun
Just like your hair
But you don't want
What's inside
You'd rather suffer
Than confide
In another that cares
As much as you don't
Maybe someday you'll see it
Or maybe you won'tNot much to say here...
