The trees tremble, in the dim light of the moon
As I gaze into the shadows, I know they'll catch up soon

Tearing at the bark, of the dead grey trees
The creatures screech, making me freeze

At the edge of a chasm, I stare into the deep
Seeing my escape, to my death I may leep

The gentle roar of their rage, lets me know they are near
Filling all of my pores, to the brim with great fear

I consider my state, do I jump or do I wait
Searching for an answer, I realize my fate

To the bottom of the abyss, I shall go
This is the end, that I know

Just as they come, to my point on the ledge
I realize that, I'm standing at the edge

Facing the fears, that chased me here
They meant to kill me, their claws quite sheer

Slowly now, they crept to my side
For my body, they intended to divide

Just as the first, took a swing for my neck
I took my dive, on my short way to Heck

As I fell towards the shadows, to which my fate was fused
I looked up at them, and they seemed quite amused

For little had I known, there was no end
