title: "Superinterdimensional Wurmhole"
writer: "H i d e"

Breaking space time
This invention of mine
Squeezed the universe
Into a dime

Systems gone down
Out of control
This damn crazy thing
Created a hole

Sucking up everything in sight
Superinterdimensional Wurmhole
Can't even put up a fight

Jump in your rocket, space junkie
Blast off into space, gotta win the race
This crazy universe is going funky

Push the throttle
Utill it breaks
Going full blast
We got no brakes

Out of space
Out of time
There goes the universe
Into a dime
