Dark clouds blow my way
Shading out the light of day

Shadows consume all in their path
Covering all with It's black bath

I run and run, but It bears near
It thrives for a taste of my fear

It consumes me, through every pore
It reaches directly into my core

Showing me what none should know
Untill my mind is about to blow

The true evil that lays in "I"
To hurt, destroy, kill; Why?

So long I've forgoten who I really am
Just an awuful demon, hidden in a man

As I stare in the mirror at the figure I see
I can not believe It is actually me

Why can't someone just hit the alarm
Send me somewhere where I can do no harm

For the more you stare the more you see
Looking deep inside of me

You might discover something too
That the real demons lay in you
