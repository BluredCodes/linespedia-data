Dripping, bleeding
My wounds do not heal
Away from the light
The world, I can't feel

In the room I sit quietly
Bleeding to death
You dont seem to care
That you're all I have left


I think of the things
That we had long ago
The times we spent
Playing in the snow

If all I could do
Was take one more ride
I'd give up this life
With one graceful stride

So I ask of you not
To say to me please
"My love you are eternaly"
For my pain, you might ease

I found a portal
The gates to your heart
If I may enter
We'd never be apart

The key to get in ,however,
Is expensive at that
"You're life must be taken
In one selfless act"

So I waited and waited
For the day to come
Where I might have the chance
To jump at a gun

All this time
I never knew
This selfless act
Was to be with you

Not to die prematurely
On the emergency room floor
Shot by a maniac
Robbing the corner store

So as I bleed
Quietly, calmly
Realizing that
It's gotten quite balmy


I realize this was wrong
It was you all along
To be with, share with
Our entire lives as one


As the monitor beeped
And neared to a stop
You stood by my side
Plus a janitor with a mop

You cried out your eyes
Why, why, did I have to die
Let the man go
Why werent I shy

At that moment I remembered
What was so important that I had to say
I whispered it out
As the monitors beeps went away

My one true love
I lived only for you
Everything I did
Was to let me through

Your pearly gates
I strived to enter
So that I might live my days
Out with you forever



As the beeps came to a halt
The line waited
...
Then began to jolt


I felt my heart pounding
Furiously fast
It was like lightning 
Had come in a blast

As I opened my eyes
I knew I was dead
For there was an angel
