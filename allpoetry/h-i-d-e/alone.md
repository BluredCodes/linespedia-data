Something holds me back
Wondering what went wrong
All the things we had
How can they be all gone

Had the most fun
I'd do anything for you
Pulled all the strings
Just to be there too

*All I wanted to do, was be with you
 The day you left, my face went blue
 I'll hold my breath, untill I find you

Drained me dry
The blood from my heart
I'm a wandering desert
Now that we're apart

Like an impossible game
I had to choose
There's one way to win
Infinite to loose

*It killed me inside, seeing you and that guy
 Deep in my mind, I just can't figure out why
 All these things, when did they go awry

Now I'm alone, alone
Wandering like a drone

Caring not where I drift ashore
Because I dont have you anymore...
