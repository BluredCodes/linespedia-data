Forbidden from me
I watch from my cage
Can not touch you
So built up with rage

Out of my reach
They keep you from me
One of these days
Maybe "I" will be "we"

One look in your eyes
Took me out of this place
I saw an angel
Staring back at my face

I love your touch
Though you don't know
The gentle caress
Of freshly fallen snow
