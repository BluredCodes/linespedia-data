Indigenous to my brain
Come the bringers of pain
The evil little creatures
With nasty little features

Poking at my mind
As if trying to bind
That sense of disparity
Past all of the clarity

Arid is the space in my head
Where they lay, waiting in bed
Until I break the silence
And bring on their violence

They make me do bad things
And create the irittatings
But they are the core
Of what I deplore

I can't fight them
Or terminate them
They will always be there
Ready to tear

The evil little creatures
That ravage my mindAnother just for fun one ~_^
