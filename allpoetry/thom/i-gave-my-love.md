May 28, 2001
~
I Gave my Love
~
I held out my hands
And asked for love
Your eyes so everlasting
Green spark like watercress
Washed on early summer pond
~
I found you on that chilly night
Winter was upon us
Yet I felt the snow melting
Before it had fallen
In shadows black as night
~
Put out your hand
Feel me . . .I am as real as real can be
And now though summer is here
I am still cold
I think at times I am the earths element
~
There was something nesting 
In the hearts on that day
The snow was falling
Still the sun had shined
Those rented minutes we shared
~
Weary distances keep us apart
But they cannot erase the days
It’s sometimes difficult
To see so distinctly
In spite of the nose on our face
~
My friend and my treasure
Your hearts blood is still flowing
And the keys have been misplaced
But nothing stays lost forever
My dear friend . . .you are my forever
~
Thom
