---
title: "I Was Wrong"
writer: "EmpathyBlue"

I always thought we'd be forever
I wished for white weddings,
Picket fences,
Children, pets, love

I never imagined it would end like this
I thought we'd get through it,
Through anything,
Death, sadness, trouble

I think now, and see the clues
Times it was obvious we were through,
And I suppose, I guess,
I was wrong
