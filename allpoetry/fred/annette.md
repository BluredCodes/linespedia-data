This heifer named Annette,she did me really badShe played with my feelingsAnd the emotions that I had.I told her I was in loveA sweet love it was indeed.She smiled, and held my handThen she said she was gonna leave.Where are you going? I askedI begged to be able to come tooShe wouldn't let me get near herShe laughed and said we were through!Your kisses gross me outYelled the little whore!They make me scream and shoutBeing with you is a bore!I'm sorry, please don't hate meShe wanted to beg months laterI told her it was too lateAnd that I already hate her.I hate her stinking gutsFor the nights she made me cryI can't stand the sight of herI just wanna ask her why?Why did you hurt me so?When I loved you like I did?Why did you make me cry?Did you see the tears I shed?So now your sorry? Is that right!Well I think that's bull sh**Now you wanna patch things up?Well I don't wanna hear it!
