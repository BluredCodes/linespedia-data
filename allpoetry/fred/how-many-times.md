---
title: "How Many Times?"

How many times must I play the fool?
To an ignoramous who think they cool?
While they just be using me all the while for gain
And I fall victim to persecution and pain.

Yes the infamous duo, strong they be
Bound up together against little old me.
When will I learn whose friendship is true?
How many times must I play the fool?

They tricked me once, shame on them
Shame on me for letting them trick me again.
Now I must use knowledge not learned in school
No longer will I continue to play the fool.
