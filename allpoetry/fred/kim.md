Despite the wholehearted love I gave thee
You still turned away and scathed me
You told me you loved me so dear 
And that to your heart, I was oh so near.

I gave you the world, and you told me lies
My brain has healed, but my soul still cries.
It cries not because I want you, for I don't
It's not because I'll crawl back, I really won't!

It cries not because I love you, that's done!
It cries only because I thought you were the one.
I tried and tried my best to make it work
But you were still a scandalous little jerk!

So yes this poem is written just for you Kim
To tell you you're a fool with no brains within
You messed up a very very good thing my dear.
And now, your every plea, I refuse to hear.

You hurt me once, and it's over now.
But I'm still a pressin' on anyhow.
I'm doing great without you! Better in fact
No more do I want you, my life's back in tack!
