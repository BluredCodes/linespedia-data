---
title: "It's Not My Fault!"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "its-not-my-fault"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16168-Its-Not-My-Fault--by-Fred"
---

You put me down, made me feel like dirt
You called me names and it really hurt.
You told me I would never succeed
That I didn't have the sense I need

You hurt me, and abused me indeed
You told me I was a wasted seed.
So for years my life was at a hault
But now I know that it's not my fault.

You're a wicked, stupid jerk and creep
You made me to always feel defeat!
But it was not my fault, no indeed
Not my fault, and I'm a splendid seed.

I'm splendid and I'm better than you
Try all u want ain't jack u can do!
You can kick me, perhaps I may fall
But once more I'll rise bold proud and tall.

You may cut me, make me bleed in pain
But my cut shall heal, you'll fail again.
You may soil my name with dirty words
But again it shall soar just like the birds.

It's not my fault, no indeed it's not
You need to put your mouth to a stop
You're a smart-mouth, dumb son-of-a-gun
But me, I'm super and number one.
