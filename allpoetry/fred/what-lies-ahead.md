---
title: "What Lies Ahead?"
writer: "Fred"

Upon life's road, we sometimes trod in fear 
Wondering is our destination far or near 
Is the road one way or is there a U turn? 
At the thought of the future our stomachs churn. 

We're afraid wondering what lies ahead 
Is it an outlet, or an end that's dead? 
What exactly is it that our futures contain? 
Why is it that in suspense we must remain? 

Well, as for me, I answer just like this 
Taking this path we definitely can't miss. 
What lies ahead,  I really do not know 
God leads the path and I simply follow. 

God knows what lies ahead, He's all wise 
I can't see what's coming, but in Him I confide 
Every step I take on this road is in His hands 
So I will not question His perfect plans. 

There's no need for me to take a detour 
And what lies ahead I fear no more. 
Although I cannot see it, I know who does 
He sits high, looks low, and is full of love.
