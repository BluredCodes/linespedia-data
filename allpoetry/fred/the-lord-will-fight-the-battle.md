title: "The Lord Will Fight the Battle"

Whose battle is this to fight?
I would really like to know
Who lights our darkest night?
And clears up pain and sorrow?

If you answered the Lord,
Then how correct you are
And greater is your reward
For knowing He's not far.

The Lord will fight the battle
Yes the Lord will win it for all
No need to mount your saddle
All you could do is once more fall.

But the Lord can handle the war
And our battles He will fight
It's us whom He even died for
So our lives would be all right.

So why would you try to fight
This heavy battle all by yourself ?
When Jesus is the truth and light
Why would you call on anyone else

Give it to God, for He holds all power
Let Him step in and help you now
For even in your darkest, weakest hour
He's still fighting for you anyhow!
