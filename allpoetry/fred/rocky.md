title: "Rocky"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "rocky"

They labeled you a monster,And they locked you in a cageThey say you're this evil personWho evokes a sense of rage.But to me, you're still my heroYou were there for me through it allYou were the best older cousin you could beMy high perception of you will never fall. I remember you not as a convict,But as a role model I followed with prideAs one whom I could look to for guidanceThat's an image they can never deny.So they may label you a monster,But the true Rocky is who I knowIntelligent, with a zest for lifeRocky, to me you're still a hero.
