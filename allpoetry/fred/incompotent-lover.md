---
title: "Incompotent Lover"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "incompotent-lover"

I showed you true love, I really did care

For you I gave my all, I always was there.

But it seems as though it was never enough

You called me names and you did wrong stuff.

Stupid, retarded one who would never amount

Incompotent lover, what's that all about?


To me you are the incompotent lover

Always speaking evil of another.

You say you've got you a better man now

Tell me what's better, I really don't see how.

No one will love you like I did you fool

Watch, he will cheat and break every rule.


And when he does, I'll be there to laugh

Who's the incompotent lover now?

He'll give your heart a cold blooded bath

He'll milk your emotions like a cow.

Incomotent lover, that you were to me

Incompotent lover, that's all you can ever be. 



Do I want you now? Hell no I don't

Will I run back to you? Hell no I won't

You're incompotent, that's all you'll ever be

Incompotent lover, describes you, surely not me.

In case you didn't know, I'm doing just fine

Now I got me a new woman, to wine and dine.
