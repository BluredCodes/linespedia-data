---
title: "Life's Wavy Sea"
writer: "Fred"

Every wave that rolls in life
Will no doubt knock us down
And each and every howling wind
Will surely throw us around.

So Lord, I beseech Thee today
Please, here my cry tenfold!
I'm convinced that if I pray
Life's waves will cease to roll.

You calmed the sea with words
You simply said 'Peace be still.'
Immediately the storm did cease
According to Thy blessed will!

So as I'm tossed upon the waves
Lord, please speak peace to me
Your words make the storm behave
And I fear not life's wavy sea.I'd like for you all to tell me if you can relate to this piece.  Can you truly feel it?
