title: "The Storms of Life"

The lightening is surely flashing
And indeed the thunder rolls.
The rain is pouring down
In this storm, my world is cold.

The waves in my life are high
They now saturate this feeble boat
I look to all ends with no help in sight
Like a desert, I feel remote.

But who is it I see? Is it God?
Coming to rescue me from this wilderness I trod?
Yes, He has come to save me again.
And I am yet amazed but in Him I always win.
