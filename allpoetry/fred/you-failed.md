---
title: "You Failed"
writer: "Fred"

Did you think I'd let you steal my joyWith your evil, wicked ways.Did you think I'd never see sunshine?Did you want to dim my rays?Well guess what?  You failedYou definitely did not winYou never once prevailedAnd my sun's a shining again.Did you want to see me cry all dayWhile there you sat and laughed?Did you think I'd drown in sorrow's riverWeakened by the pain of the past?Well think again, I'm still not downYou've got no power  in this place?Try all you want, I won't frownI've got a perpetually smiling face.Are you mad that I'm so charming?Alluring ladies with my loving appealCan you not stand that I'm so handsomeTell me, how exactly do you feel?Wait, I have no desire to knowFor I know already what you'll sayYou'll say I've got too much hopeAnd that you must steal it away.Well guess again, you failed once moreFor I'm not going to yeild to your evil whimYou failed, I say you failed, no success for youYou failed, and failed, and failed again.
