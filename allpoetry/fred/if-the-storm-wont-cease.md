title: "If The Storm Won't Cease"

What if the storm won't cease?
And the currents rock my little boat?
Lord, wilt thou my soul release?
To the overcoming winds of sorrow?

Or if the storm keeps howling fierce?
And my whole world were just to darken?
Wilt thou allow Satan my ship to pierce?
Or to my fearful, cries wilt thou harken?

My child, I'll never leave thee alone
Is my grace not greater than the storm?
When upon life's waves thou art thrown
Did you think I'd just leave thee torn?

Or maybe you thought I'd let you drown
When still you are my precious child
Smile now, don't you wear no frown.
The storm you're facing is very mild

I call it mild because I walk with you
So you never go through by yourself
When it rains I carry my child through
Now don't you worry about nothing else.
