title: "All Night I Cried"

Mom,

I overheard you and Dad one night
'Don't tell the kids, I'll be all right.'
I listen closer saddened by my ears
You said you had Lupus, I shed tears.

I love you Mom, please don't go
Would you be all right? I didn't know.
All night I cried, as I tossed and I turned
Nervousness overcame me, my stomach churned

All night I shed tear after painful tear
All night I sobbed filled with pain and fear.
I couldn't be loud, bro. and sis. didn't need to know
So that night I buried my head under my pillow.

I've heard about Lupus, it attacks your cells
And makes you weak, breaking you down
When I think about it my red eyes will swell
And I'll be made to wear a perpetual frown.

All night I cried, Mom I cried for you
All night I cried, what more could I do
Drip, drip, my tears did ceaselessly flow
All night I cried, I drowned in sorrow!

Mom, I love you say you'll be okay
And take all of my sorrows away
All night I cried, grief's flames consuming me
All night I cried, I don't wanna lose my Mommy.
