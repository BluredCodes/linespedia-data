title: "Cease Storm Cease!"
writer: "Fred"

Cease storm cease! Go far away!
Return to me oh bright unclouded day!
Since she done gone, my heart's a breakin'
Breakin' with nay no hope for relief
Since she done gone, my weary body's achin'
Consumed by flames with the fuel of grief
Cease storm cease! Restore my calm station
Unite me again with my precious salvation!
