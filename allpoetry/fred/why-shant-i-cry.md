---
title: "Why Shan't I Cry"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "why-shant-i-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

She left my world and I thought I would die
You say I'm a man, but why shan't I cry?

So many nights I laid awake wondering why
This was the worst heartache! Now why shan't I cry?

So because I'm a man I can't feel? That's a lie!
So I ask you again, why shant I cry?

Drip drip . . I can't control the pain pouring out my eyes
As tears stream faster, I still wanna know why shant I cry?

What measures a man's strength? How he guards his pride?
Well, I've got plenty guarded and still I cried.

Why shant I cry, why shant I? Cause I'm a man?
Well in God's will tears are part of His healing plan!
