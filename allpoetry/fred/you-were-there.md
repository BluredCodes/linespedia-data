---
title: "You Were There"
writer: "Fred"

When I cried, you were thereIn times of trouble, you me showed you careWhen the rain pored down,You were there with a raincoatYou made sure I didn't drownIn the depths of despair and sorrow.When the times were going greatYou were there to share my joy When the sun was brightly shiningYou were there to applaud this little boy.So, Mom and Dad this is for  youTo tell you how much I love youNot once did you turn awayEven when the going was toughYou remained even on the cloudiest dayDespite the weather being severely rough.You were there, you were there, I love you tooYes you were there! Doing the best you could do.
