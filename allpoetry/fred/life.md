title: "Life"

Life is sometimes filled with painReplete with stormy clouds and rain.Life can be like a long dark nightWithout a trace of help in sight.Life can make you feel distressAnd put you through the hardest test.That's when you look toward the skyAnd say 'Lord I know Thou art my guide.'See better things are on the wayGod'll turn your darkness into day.Life then won't be filled with painReplete with sunshine no more rainLife won't be like a long dark nightFor Jesus is your guiding light.Life won't make you feel distressFor God will help you pass the test.
