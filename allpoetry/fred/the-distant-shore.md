While sailing upon life's waves
Not knowing what tide be in store
We must still give God praise
En route to the distant shore.

Though it seems so far away
And at times in sorrow we drown
Keep looking to the brighter day
Waiting on that shore is a crown.

Life's breakers fiercely dash
While sailing toward the shore
And with icebergs we may crash
Not wanting to paddle anymore.

But when we reach the shore
Celebration will never cease
The tiring battle will be o'er
And we'll finally be at peace.
