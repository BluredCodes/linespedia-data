---
title: "God's Cast"
writer: "Fred"

Is the role I play a leading one?
Or a few lines, then I'm done?
Am I the star or the backup man?
Someone tell me, what's the plan?

See, I really do not know my role
I don't even feel completely whole
I see the lights, camera, action too
But this still bites, what will I do?

Where do I fit into all of this?
Someone please tell me, I insist.

Well for starters, I know your role.
See, it is I God who makes you whole.
You are my child, companion, and friend.
Therefore, your role will never end.

It is leading so that others can see
It is a few lines to proclaim the truth about me.
You are the star so shine so bright.
Also the backup man who makes things right.

So where do you fit into all of this?
You are my creation, joy, and bliss.
