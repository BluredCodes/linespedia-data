Dedicated to the memory of a dear friend John Brewster, 
who tragically lost his life in an automobile collision

Even though I've flown away
Beyond what your eyes can see
Just know we'll meet again one day
So please don't cry for me.

Your joy will come once more
Just like the morning sun
Like an eagle with wings you'll soar
Never getting weary when you run

I'm sorry this time is for you so rough
Because through God my  victory I've won
After I fought on and toiled long enough
He said 'Well done, John, your race has been run!'
