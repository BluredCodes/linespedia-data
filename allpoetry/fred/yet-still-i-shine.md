---
title: "Yet Still I Shine"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "yet-still-i-shine"
source: "AllPoetry"

Like a gardener with shears you tried to cut me down
Anytime I tried to smile, you had to make me frown.
You did what you could to put clouds in the blue skies of mine
You tried to kick me down and defeat me bad, yet still I shine!

Your intentions were to thwart my eminent success
And make me feel weak, down and depressed.
So to keep me down, you kept on repeatedly lying
But now I've found a greater truth and I'm still a shining.

Did you think that you would get the last laugh
By sending black cats down my weary path.
Did you think you'd permanently darken my way
Well, guess what? I've found even more light and I shine today!
