---
title: "To Mama"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "to-mama"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Mama, as I almost fall apart
I'll speak straight from my heart.
I hope you're doing okay
With me being so far away.

I love you I just want to say
You're on my mind all day
You were always my first love
To me an angel sent from above.

As a child you taught me to do right
And  corrected me when I was wrong
You stayed up when I was sick at night
Even if I needed you there all night long.

I love you I just want to say
You're on my mind all day
You were always my first love
To me an angel sent from above.

When I was a teen, you let me grow
I was the plant you were the sun you know
You molded me in preparation for adulthood
By teaching me to make choices that were good.

I love you I just want to say
You're on my mind all day 
You always were my first love
To me an angel sent from above.

As I close, I'm an adult now
And I still love you anyhow
I'm the best adult I can be
Because you were there for me

I love you, I just want to say
You're on my mind all day.
You always were my first love
To me an angel sent from above.
