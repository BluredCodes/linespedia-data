title: "Rain"
writer: "Fred"

In every life, 
some rain must fall
But hold your head up 
through it all.
They eye of the Lord 
will see you through.
His eye is on the sparrow 
and He watches you too.

So when you are tossed 
upon the stormy sea
Hold onto God's hand, 
be as strong as you can be
For He'll never put more on you 
than you can bear
And when life is overwhelming, 
you can go to God in prayer.
