---
title: "I Cry Out Now!"
writer: "Fred"
slug: "i-cry-out-now"
source: "AllPoetry"

One hurt too many, now that's where I stand,

My feet firmly anchored in life's quick sand.

She hurt my heart and she broke me in two

I cry out now, with nothing left to do.


I was hurting badly when she came along

But with her love, she helped make me strong.

She said she would be there for me always

And for once, I saw the sun's golden rays.


But abruptly my sun set that dreadful night

She stole my sun's rays so shiny and bright.

She said that our relationship was through

I cry out now, with nothing left to do! 


Now my heart is broken permanently

No other lady can ever fix me!

For with the last one, my whole heart flew

I cry out now, with nothing left to do!
