---
title: "A Spirit Scarred"
writer: "Fred"

Upon his birth, was joy and mirthLove everywhere, in the world so fairA spirit so light, with memories brightIn a wonderful place, with a smiling face.Did he understand, life dealt bad hands?He didn't know, in the world was sorrow.He wanted perfection, but got rejection.Where's his light spirit? You wanna hear it?It's no longer light. This place ain't bright.Now he's a spirit scarred. His life is so hard.He was rejected and now he's severely affected.He's filled with woe, in the depths of sorrow.A spirit scarred bad. Oh the poor lad!He's scarred for life. That ain't right.Why did they do it? Now he gotta work thru it.A spirit scarred? Now, life is hard.It's okay, let God show you the wayTake heart today, God'll relieve dismay.So scarred spirit, it's okay I sayYour scars are gonna heal one day.
