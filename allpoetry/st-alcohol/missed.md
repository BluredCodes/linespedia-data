title: "missed"
writer: "St Alcohol"

open door
old fiend time
he hates me in three ways
and might be a friend

she's gone
she's here,
she's there

old man time
cackles as i walk past to work
button-down my empty bed
i can smell her hair
(so i tell myself)
on her pillow.

and it's just a month, 
till old time is a friend
ticking off seconds;
heartbeats that linger
between squeezed hands
as if to ask him to slow a bit
and let us enjoy it

the world is grey and cardboard, sans warmth or smile, like the low-light of evensong bells when i'd keep her awake because i couldn't stop touching her.
