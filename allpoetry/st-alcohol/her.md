i understand 
mr denver
leaving on a jet-plane

and i'm driving home
turn to take your hand
and only find my own

and like a muslim
i turn to my mecca
my thoughts look towards you

she's flying like an angel
among clouds, to a heaven
it's raining in purgatory

its an empty room, i never noticed
its empty, now
when you aren't here

but febuary is only a month away
mythical febuary
such a future dream, only a month
