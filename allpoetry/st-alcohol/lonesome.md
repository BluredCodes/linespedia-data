---
title: "lonesome"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "lonesome"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47410-lonesome-by-St-Alcohol"
---

if i told you i love you
ten times a day
this life would still be too short

if i cried a tear
for every moment of lonelyness
it would only fill oceans
not enough for old man time

oh heart!
love as a wildflower
to claim as mine
to set free on my mantle

where are you?
in the dark, i'm
where?
i need you, love
silly trust-of-mine
i love you, my mind repeats
my mantra
i'm empty

i'm lonely
but you are there
waiting for my hand in yours
your palm, to cup my heartbeat
there,
smiling in dianic moonlight
