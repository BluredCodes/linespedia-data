title: "open ended as an orgasm"
writer: "St Alcohol"

source: "AllPoetry"

to the future
a man
waits, smiles

to the past
a woman
worries, smiles

to the time when passion
is a flower
flames like popping balloons

when our future
explodes, not if

open ended
as love
