---
title: "house arrest"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "house-arrest"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27691-house-arrest-by-St-Alcohol"
---

grinding glass
an anger
at an anger

one deaf ear
some mistakes
anal-retentive asshole
decideds i'm unforgiven

passive makes a hole
in open mind
won't talk to...
old friend time
ticks off and continues

i'm pasted on the wall and confined
hung art piece to be mad at
"house arrested
mouse, in a cage
for him to rage at"

making me feel superior
in my ability to deal with anger
his anger is bulletproof
thank god i'm not vindictive
he should too.
