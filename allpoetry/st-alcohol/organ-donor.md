title: "organ donor"
writer: "St Alcohol"

so many parts
to keep straight
dont bend! dont break!

and they are filed
each place a home for 
wind-bones

and the lifting
scraping of our daily day
wends, winds (keep it all straight)

one out of tune,
and go digging, for an elusive vapor
press the key press the key press the key

one day two 
days a week, an organ
and its done gone donated
