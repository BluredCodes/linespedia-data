I

dead man
to an eagle's touch

i'm feeling
and i'm flying
and the ground
old mr. ground, has gone away
has dissapeared

and fear shakes me,
while anxiety slaps me around
and i go six more rounds
with a-fear-of-death


---------------
II

oh angel
oh angel
warmth
love, no tears
no "see you in a month"
wanton and need
i plead with time for 
good behavior
good behavior... vs time


------------------
III

paitently 
i await an execution
crooked back old man,
dead these long years
stood up, too sick to stand
but shot anyways
they give me a bull
and a red flag,
smirking, i tie it across my chest
come the morning, come the dawn

-------------------
IV

hello dear odin
old one-eye
bastard medic mystic
and wise wizard
old fate, old fate
chain that dog down


-------------------
V

one more to the ending
one more end
one less begining
one end, one end
one of others, pure chance putrid

-------------------
VI

all else is fate
all we wanton desire
and i say, "fuckitall"
inside, one-man funeral
a stormy day on my paradise island
i keep scribe'ing
the sand, the waves,
he keep washing my words away
