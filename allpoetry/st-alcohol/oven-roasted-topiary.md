like the open window
of frustration
i am simple
simple open window

simple
gone off like golf balls
swing and strike
and flying
in some direction

born in an egg salad
smelling of rice
wet, small stringy
like the hairs on a
wet dog

gullable puppets
open minds
simple songs
texas, jack, cheeseburgers

and i'm shaking my head
mournfully, my muse
oh clicking flicking
channels...
