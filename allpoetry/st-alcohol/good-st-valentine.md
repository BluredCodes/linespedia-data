---
title: "good st valentine"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "good-st-valentine"
source: "AllPoetry"

as if i needed a day to remind me
to love you 
a special moment
to be romantic

as if flowers, or chocolate
don't you just love chocolate my dear...
makes my love for you any more:
than what i can say with my hands
or my words
the glint i get in my eye, 
or the way my head swivels 
when you are in the room.

or is this an old man's day
a modern day in a modern world
3:00-3:15 PM, pick up flowers,
remind her, to look the hero
"Happy Valentine's Day, sweetheart"
as if i needed a day to remind me
I love you
