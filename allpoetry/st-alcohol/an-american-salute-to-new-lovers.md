title: "an american salute to new lovers"

she smiles, saluting
at half-mast
a dead man mourned
with half high flag
he laughs, at the poor sod
who thinks himself a conquerer,
a flagpole;
with no banners
the dysfunctions
of attentions
the attentions of dysfunctions
