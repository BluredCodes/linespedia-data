title: "i am green i am god"
writer: "St Alcohol"

in the green
i am god
oh golden god of green
to trees
and rubish
as a green god goes

and brown the streaks
upon my green
the leaves that left
and break blue skies
into VanGogh

old madman
green god
and passionate
to an ear.
