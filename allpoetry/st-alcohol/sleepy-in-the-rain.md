---
title: "sleepy in the rain"
writer: "St Alcohol"

hmm pitter
patter
and a rainstorm
to make love to

here's to rain in california
or maybe a warm night
for beach-blankets

wild man thoughts
we call them male
but the women are thinking them too
poor victoria is a'crumblin

whats a wall, when i'm climbing
where is the slope to drop off of
when i know the future, no details
but your warmth is there,
or else i'm in winter

pitter
patter pitter
pitter

a nice peal of 
nine-pins
the gods are playing in the clouds
i might have been a god once,
because i miss them

mortality, i realized it the other day
i knew i was dying, 40 60 80 years from now
"'course in my day, we only lived t' bout 80
anyhoo'"
lennon said he wanted to go first,
all you need is love
and a revolver
he wanted to go first to save her the pain

and i'm arguing with a teacher i recognize but never had
in a dream, over blowing his cover while we bust gangsters
i must be getting sleepy in this rain
