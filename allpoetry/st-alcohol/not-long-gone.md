---
title: "not long gone"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "not-long-gone"
source: "AllPoetry"

i debated staring at the screen
and letting my love leave it's own mark
but it doesn't type
it just loves

and in much the same way
we wake alone
we come screaming kicking
into this world
the house was alone

it was empty, it knew the future
all it wanted was her proximity
no need for a fire
save romantic judgment, 
no need for ether
but it was thick

i keep opening doors in the house
but i know she's only inside, for me,
now, so wrapped up
holding my heart, bathing it with:
tears
and
smiles
and
kisses

voices, annoying repetitive voices
how was the trip, welcome home
home
i'm not home
because she isn't here.

i hope she didn't cry on the dark road "home"
in the spirit of love, tears need companions
in the spirit, i'd kiss those tears away
