title: "sex and suicide"

old friends
by the coffee table
looking over magazines of suicide
fasionable...

but sex is so much more fun
when it's hidden, in the trees 
of the path, off the path
you grin methodically, a sincere secret
we join, one big pushme-pullme

and days after,
weeks, static rings
and we sit wasting air time
just listining to our breath
typing, vegging, just being together
in a very sensual analog



oh stupid rain,
that makes my socks wet and uncomfortable
and her bad day, paint, marines
e-5's and 3's
