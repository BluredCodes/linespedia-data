i don't know what
what to say
what to say to bring that smile
that i see when we are lying close
when i ponder in my head how 
how i can ask for this forever

you have created no anger, no angst
no hate, if i havent touched your heart
in minutes then i am failing you

even these words, i feel...
don't scare me, how do i say
not because i'm not screaming
i still want and feel those intimates
i want you, when i'm inside of YOU
how do i say i'm wholy satisfied
even to be at the base of olympus
