---
title: "zen and the art of gunracks"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "zen-and-the-art-of-gunracks"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26911-zen-and-the-art-of-gunracks-by-St-Alcohol"
---

i don't own a gun
the gun is nothingness
i need the rack
for somethingness
i have no gun
i am of a nothingness
but the rack is there 
to show them off
i have a gun,
of a somethingness
to rack,
the rack is nothingness



"here a nietzsche, there a nietzsche
everywhere a nietzsche-nietzsche"
