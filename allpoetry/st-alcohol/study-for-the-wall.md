---
title: "(Study for) The Wall"
writer: "St Alcohol"
slug: "study-for-the-wall"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44906--Study-for--The-Wall-by-St-Alcohol"
---

the boys had seen the wall
we all knew it was there
we'd been around it, seen it's back
but the boy still wanted over

he was a few years older than me
but we were friends on a lonely block
he wanted over the wall, past it 
beyond it, as if the wall itself held him in
rather than being a relic

we marched down to the wall
one sunday, skipping church,
knowing we were missed
we snuck in, took his pole from the school
and headed out to the wall

the grass was soft, the earth
but he wasn't worried about this side
he took his pole and running,
missed the crest and toppled down
again, he took his pole
and up, and that was the last I saw of him

church was out, my lunch was cold from corner-waiting
but when he did not come home
i was recruited to the search.
we searched the wall,
both sides out,
but there wasn't any trace

i still stand there, under the picnic-tree
i still see that crest, his determination,
as he leaped it
he made it over the wall
and that was the last i saw of him
