title: "Sad Eyes"

I’m a kind and generous person
I’d do anything for you
So tell me, If I’m so nice
Why do I feel so blue
My life is wrapped in clouds
Where sometimes the light shines through
But my mind takes its own pathway
A path I do not choose
I need to make a stand
Only I can take this step
On the road to better life
A life of no regrets
No recalling sadness
No But’s or If’s or Why’s 
Only focused on the good times
Upon the clear blue skies
The courage that I need
Seems like a million miles away
But It’s courage that I need to find
To bring back better days
I need help through this madness
Let me see clearer skies
To be a Cheerier, better person
To look through happy eyes
