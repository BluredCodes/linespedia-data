My innocence taken
My trust in tatters
His life went on
Mine? It didn't matter!
A family member
Oh how so often is the case
Sick perverted people
Putting my family in disgrace
Even when confronted
The lies they never cease
She’s making it all up
She must have been a tease
Oh please… I was a child
How could I know so much?
If I had not been soiled
By your filthy dirty touch
For years I lived and suffered
My life became a shell
Outside façade so friendly
Inside a living hell
Your death brought me some closure
To move on and make a start
To finally open up my soul
And give away my heart
It’s almost all behind me
I’ve moved on with my life
I’m a proud mum of 3 children
And an extra lucky wife

I’m glad you didn’t win!
