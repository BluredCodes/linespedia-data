---
title: "My Place"
writer: "kaz4sim"

A  black star filled sky
Stretching past my naked eye
Somewhere deep within that space
Is a secret spot, my special ‘place’
A place of peace and harmony
A place for no-one except for me
A tranquil spot where I can think
And sit and sip my nectar drink
A place where I can be at ease
A place where I don’t have to please
Moonbeams lighting up my way
Stars stopping to say good day
Comets rushing quietly by
In my peaceful star filled sky
I sit and look and strain my sight
Into my black star studded night
