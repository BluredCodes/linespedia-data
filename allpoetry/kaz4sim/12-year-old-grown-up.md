Yeah! I’m a rotten old mother, here to spoil all your fun
While you scream and strop like you’re the only one
Well listen to me girl this world ain’t all for you
And all of the things that YOU want to do
So you want to go out to party til late
And I’m supposed to say yes dear and not get into a state
Sure I’m gonna let you go clubbing ! just going to let you go off in the cold
Forget about the fact that your only 12 years old
But my friends all go there” is the plea that I hear
Well let me tell you something, your precious to me  do ya hear?
I know what its like out there in the dark
Men lurking in bushes, hiding in parks
Gangs out on the street waiting for prey
Drugs going round in the cold light of day
I wasn’t born a mum, Whatever you may think
I’ve been out there in clubs, having too much to drink
You might look all grown up when dressed up to go out
But you have no idea what’s out there and who will hear you shout?
Men coming on to my 12yr old babe  think not, no way no how
You look gorgeous and mature I admit, but sorry my darling you aint going out now
So pout and stamp, slam doors and cry
And when you’ve calmed down, just come ask me why
It’s a sad cruel world that we live in today
And I want to keep you safe in every possible way
Yes maybe I’m paranoid and no fun to you
But when you’re in my shoes you’ll do the same things tooI'm glad to say that my 'babe' calmed down and realised i only had her best interests in mind. Peace was restored to the house once more! It's so hard being mum to a 12 year old 'adult'!!
