It’s hard to put in words
The love I have inside
For my 3 beautiful children
It’s a love I cannot hide

They only have to look
In that special loving way
And I’m lost, completely smitten
I’m a mum! What can I say?

Fireworks burst inside my heart
With just one simple smile
I love them more than life itself
I’d walk a million miles

They are no longer babies
Don’t they grow so fast?
Where is all the time gone?
Why cant we make it last

Soon we’ll have the boyfriends
The jobs and social lives
They’ll soon have new partners
Become husbands and wives

Will I become redundant?
My job will cease to be
Oh never fear, my babies
I’ll never leave you 3
