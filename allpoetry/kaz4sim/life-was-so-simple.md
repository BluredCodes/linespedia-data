---
title: "Life was so simple"
writer: "kaz4sim"

I thought when they grew, my worries would wane
My life would be easier, I’d not feel the pain
But watching them now new fears in me have grown
Boyfriends and dating, going out on their own
Peer pressure to do the things that are bad
Not listening to me……. Getting so mad
Opinions once hidden are now put on show
Arguing their point, not letting it go
Mums not always right…… is something they learn
Realising the power as male heads they turn
Educating them all in the wiles of the world
Sorting out differences between boys and girls
Letting them go, to make their own choices
Hoping above all they hear the right voices
Waiting for the day when their heart you will mend
Listening and advising til you go round the bend
Life was so simple when they stayed by your side
They listened, and learnt with wide open eyes
It all seemed so simple when I was that girl
Going off with my mates, enjoying heads twirl
Meeting some lads going out on a date
Not thinking of mum, who was in a right state
I thought “oh mum, don’t panic” I’ll be back in by 10
But be careful she whispered, cos it’ll be right dark by then
Come straight home, no detours If your late then please phone
Not once did I think about my mum worrying at home
My mates and me giggled….MUMS! How not fun are they
Never thinking at all that I’d be that mum some day
