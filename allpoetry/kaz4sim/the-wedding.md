I wrote this for my sister sara who got married on the 9th of September 2000.The wedding is booked.The 9th of SeptemberA finer eventI cannot rememberIts all going smoothlyFull steam aheadThe babe in our familyIs now getting wedWe’ve been through the stagesBought all the dressesThe flowers are pickedTo fill the head-dressesSuits are on orderShoes being dyedOutfits are boughtFor the mum of the brideCars are on standbyCoaches at readyNo hiccups or hitchesIts all going steadyThe disco is orderedDJ at his bestThe Groom is all readyWith proud puffing chestHairdresser at 8’Teasing and curlsFlower studded braidsTo do on the girlsBest man is readyWith ring at his chestThe ushers are waitingBoth looking their bestEverything’s readyDetails re-checkedAs to ensureThe day is not wreckedEveryone’s eagerNow all is doneNo more to do…. The waitings begunSo ‘Babe; of us allMy dear little sisI wish you profoundly‘Sweet Wedded Bliss’
