Oh my god its startedThe teenage years so nearMy little baby girlHas daddy close to tearsWhere has all the time gone?I’m not ready for all this!Dating, boys and fashionThe dreaded first sloppy kiss!There should be a handbookTo tell us what to doCan I get away with locking you up…?Would that be fair to do?I know that’s it will happenYou’ll bring your boyfriend’s homeYou’ll be all excited…..I’ll have to listen to dad moanWe’ll give you all the lecturesThat my mum gave to meAnd you’ll just roll your eyesAnd say Mum! Let me be!Your dad is having nightmaresWatching you matureBut he’ll just have to handle itBecause there is no cureYou’re super, stunning, marvellousAnd I’m so very proud of youYou’re such a wonderful daughterAnd I think the world of youHappy 12th birthday princessHave a brilliant fantastic dayYou make my life worth livingI’m here for you alwaysWith love to you darlingNow and foreverLove you millionsMum
