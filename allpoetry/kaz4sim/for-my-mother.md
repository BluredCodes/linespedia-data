Sitting here thinking…Of how I should startTo tell you the thoughtsThat come from my heartI’ve told you so oftenYour more precious than goldA mother so specialOf which there’s no mould**********************You to me are everythingYou’re the best there is no doubtYou light up my lifeYou’re what life is all aboutWhat a difference you madeYou’ve given your allNow and foreverI’ll be there if you callYou never complainedYou gave Endless loveI’m proud to be your daughterI thank god up aboveNo childhood of richesNo wealth to seeBut I’m all that I amBecause you loved meLove is so preciousA rare gift to giveI will always love youFor as long as I liveTo love somebodyLike you have loved meGives me Feelings so strongThat no-one can seeThe power of loveCan build strong foundationsA love like we haveWould unite all the nationsMemories we haveBoth the bad and the goodAnd they all make us strongerLike we knew they wouldHave I told you lately that I love youAnd what you mean to meI love you more than everYour part of my familySo if you ever need meI’m always here for youEverything I do.I do it for youThis verse may seem a bit funnyAnd maybe a wee bit long!But if you look much closerI’ve used titles of some songs….Mum, I don’t need to tell you how I feel. I know that.I am always, always here for you. Nothing is too much trouble, believe me. Just knowing you’re alright is all I need.Love to you on Mother’s DayAnd for eternityKaren
