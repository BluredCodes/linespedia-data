Sure we can do it
How hard can it be?
Only a small job!
Turn 2 bedrooms into 3

Rip up the flooring
Empty the rooms
Scraping off paper
Now where is that broom?

Put up partitioning
To split into 2
Buckets of plaster
To make the cracks new

Climb into the attic
To alter the lighting
This job seemed so easy
Now it’s quite frightening

“We want our own door
We don’t want to share
Can we have a hallway?
And just put it there?”

A hole in the wall
To create a new door
Now guess what they want?
Yep! Laminate floor

Moving the radiators
Altering the switches
How can one job
Create so many hitches

Painting and papering
Replacing the wood
Finding new problems
And making them good

We had a fine budget
Which has seen better days
But try telling the kids
With their high spending ways

New guest beds on glides
When friends want to stay
New curtains and blinds
And a rug by the way

New wardrobes and chests
New desk, chair and lamp
New duvets and pillows
To complete the re-vamp

But kids let me warn you
When you next trash your room
I know where I’ve put
The tattered old broom!
