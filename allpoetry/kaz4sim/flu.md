Viral infections, cold heady flu
Sore streaming eyes.. ah. Ah.. AAachoo
Head feels like lead, with drummers inside
Need a warm dark room, somewhere I can hide
Potions and tablets, decongestant on tap
I’m awash with inertia, like a tree with no sap
The littlest noise, rings loud in my head
I need to be cosseted and placed in my bed
I need to have care, be spoilt just a bit
Be brought steaming hot drinks, until I feel fit
But alas, not to be, just get on with my day
I’m a mum with 3 kids, what more can I say
The chores just wont wait, the kids need me more
So I’ll just persevere til I feel better once more
