I don’t have untold fortuneTo buy you fancy thingsNo millions in the bank To buy you diamond ringsI wish that I could buy youA moment in the pastA moment spent with loved onesWhere heartache doesn’t lastBut all these things are dreamsOf what I’d like to doTo put the smile back in your eyesAnd make your grey skies blueBut all I can offer you is meAnd my family who love you soWe’re here for all eternityWherever we may goHave a very happy birthday mumI give you my love, which will never endYou’re my mother first and foremostBut also my best friendWith love to you now and foreverKarenXX
