They don’t come with handbooksNo instructions to findNo exchange or refundsYou enter it blindYou don’t need a licenseNo test you must takeSo it’s obvious reallyYou’ll make some mistakesWe all get it wrongIt’s called trial and errorBut one day you knowIt’ll all come togetherYou’ll know what to doIt just happens that wayYour instincts you’ll followAnd you’ll get through the dayBut don’t be disheartenedWhen things don’t go to planJust remember your partOf the new parent gang
