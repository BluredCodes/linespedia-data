title: "I need To Get Out"

I’m stuck in a place
That I’d rather not be
There’s no easy exit
For my eyes to see

This hole that I’m in
Is as deep as the sea
The exit I see 
Is as high as can be

The only way up
Is through blood sweat and tears
The only way out
By dispelling all fears

I know I must climb
On this tattered frayed rope
If I’m ever to find
That place where there’s hope

The first steps the hardest
Have to get a firm grip
For once I’m up on there
I don’t want to slip

At the top of this hole
People wait there for me
With words of encouragement
It’s my family
