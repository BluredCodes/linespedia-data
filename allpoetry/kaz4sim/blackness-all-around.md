---
title: "Blackness all around"
writer: "kaz4sim"
slug: "blackness-all-around"

With a heavy heart
I start my day
Paint on my smile
Go on my way
Life must go on
Or so it’s said
My body goes on
But not my head
My thoughts are still
Locked up in time
Of a place long ago
Where everything’s fine
My brothers here
My dad is too
The grass is greener
The sky’s so blue
So much has happened
In such short years
So much heartache
Rivers of tears
Life for me
Has become a pill
Where things are black
I’m always ill
My kids are great
But I’ve let them down
A painted smile
That hides a frown
Everything’s black
Scary and grim
But for the sake of my kids
I must not give in
I’ll fight this cloud
That darkens my days
With the help of my family
And their special ways
And who knows when
Maybe soon
I’ll return to myself
And escape all the doom
