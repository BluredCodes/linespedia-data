Mums are a breedUnique to this worldA flesh covered robotFor each boy and girlSuper hero mum!She can do any taskNothings impossibleYou just have to askCooking and cleaningFixing the houseWhen cartoons are onShe's as quiet as a mouseKids want attentionThe washing must waitGo see what they needBefore it's too lateSorting out scrapsWiping up tearsSorting out bedtimeAllaying all fearsGetting caught in the middleYou never can winYou're not being fair mum!You always side with HIM!!Trips to the beachThe cinema tooBowling and swimmingAre all down to youMoney for clothesNo problem at allI'll just get it outFrom that hole in the wallWant to play gamesAt a quarter to nine?Is mummy tired?No honey I'm fine!I'll go on foreverI'll do what you askI'm superhero mumI'll do any taskBut remember one thingAll this is for youUs FLESH COVERED ROBOTSNeed a little help too!!
