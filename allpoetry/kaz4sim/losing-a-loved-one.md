---
title: "Losing a loved one"
writer: "kaz4sim"
slug: "losing-a-loved-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

Desolate grief
No end in sight
Black tear filled clouds
Outing the light
Guilt ridden dreams
Of how if and why
Swollen sore eyes
That always cry
Heart wrenching sadness
Your soul ripped in two
Blocking out memories
That make your world blue
A sadness so deep
It messes your brain
Losing a loved one
Life’s never the same!
