Hear the little bearded manJihad has been declaredThe Islamic fundamentalistHas his nation all preparedHe is calling up all MuslimsTo join him in his fightWhile they give lives so freelyHe hides in the dead of nightSo he is “the” extremistCalling others to do his biddingHe is the great Islamic leaderOh yeah, are they kiddingIf he’s so extremeAnd he believes all he’s doneWhy wasn’t he on the planeThe day we lost the sunOh no, no fear, not Bin LadenHe’ll not give his life so freeHe prefers to hide in the shadowsAnd in the caves of Afghan safetyThis war was not IslamicIt’s not all about beliefIt’s a war waged by one man“The great Islamic Chief”So call up all your warriorsLet them die at your nameOne day they will recogniseThat their leader is insaneThe saddest part in all this messIs that innocents will dieOn all sides of this “Jihad”Can anyone tell me “Why?”This war has nothing to do with Islam.
