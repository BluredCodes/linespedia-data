Love’s much more than roses
Much more than diamonds too
It’s the special little treats
That your loved one does for you

The cup of tea in bed
Complete with a slice of toast
Means more than solid gold
The little things matter most

The gentle little touch
As you pass each other by
The strong and caring shoulder
When you need a cry

A tender kiss so loving
When you are feeling low
Sharing all the laughter
Is what makes love grow

Two strong and tender arms
To protect and keep you warm
Your soul-mate and protector
To keep away the harm

A deep and loving look
A sensual little pout
Is what makes it all so special
It’s what love’s all about
