---
title: "Speak Out"
writer: "kaz4sim"

Black lifeless eyes
Surveying her world
Where is that child?
The innocent girl
Shut is her mind
To the cold light of day
Praying these nightmares
Would just go away
Her innocence taken
Without thought or a care
Can he sleep at night?
Without chills or nightmares
She knows she should speak
Her tale is to tell
But her family know nothing
Of this foreboding hell
For years she has blamed
Herself for this fate
She must have done something
When she turned 8
She cried every night
Prayed hard to be good
So the hurting would stop
Oh how she wished she understood
Oh please God! No more
She silently cried
As he entered her room
With arms open wide
She remembered it all
The hurting and lies
This time it must end
They would hear her cries
