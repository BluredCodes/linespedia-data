If Heaven’s really up thereHows it look do you supposeIs it paradise untainted?Where thornless roses growDo people have to work?To keep God’s home aliveI wonder if its flexi timeOr maybe 9 to 5It must be full of goodnessThe bad guys take a hikeOn a winding perilous journeyTo that fella with the spikeDoes time stand still above usSo we can fit in all our workIs God a patient teacherOr does he sometimes go Beserk?All I know is that it’s out thereBeyond the Milky WayA place where loved ones gatherTil’ we all meet up one day
