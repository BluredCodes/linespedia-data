Contractions have startedI really can’t cope!Will it be a quick one?Oh dear God I hope!Everyone’s fussingGetting everything readyBags are all packedContractions are steadyTime to make tracksI’ve got a date with some painThis is somethingI’m not doing againMonitors beepingTo check baby’s ok“give me the drugs”until this pain goes awayMidwives are busyProdding and poking“wont be long now dear”Jeez! Are they jokingThe pain is so badFeels like I’m burning insideAnd dear hubby dearestIs right by my side“its your fault!” I screamas a contraction abatessee what you’ve doneI’m in a right stateIf they tell me one moreThat its not long to goI’ll scream and I’ll shoutJeez! What do they know?I’ve been here for agesThe pains getting worseThank god for the GasI say to the nurseThen everything happensThe baby’s so nearHubbys excited!I’m close to tearsThen all of a sudden I hear a faint cryMy baby’s been bornI let out a sigh!What have we got?A girl or a boy?I’m anxiously waitingWith heart full of joyI hold my new babyAnd look into its eyesNo wonder it hurt….Look at your size!The pains all forgottenI’m elated with gleeA beautiful additionTo my family
