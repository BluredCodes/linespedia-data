Oh my sweet babe When the time is right
You’ll know it It will happen and feel so right
Don’t let your first time Be Stolen Fumbled Moments In The Night
Don’t Listen To The Boys “If You Loved Me, You Would”
Just Remember That If They Loved You
They Would Wait Until Your Ready
Making Love Is Not About Pressure
Not About Uncomfortable Feelings And Regrets
Listen To Your Heart, Then Check It out With Your Soul
When Everything Seems Right, Then It Will Be
Your Girlfriends Giggle About ‘Who They Had’
And Others Laugh And Pretend They Are So Cool
But Who Are The Real Ones Laughing
When She Turns Up At School
Slapper, Slag, Trollop, Can Be Heard Echoing
Shes Easy, Is The Remark That Goes Around
Sure! Boys Love A Slapper
But Do They Really Want To Date One?
Don’t Be Duped Into Thinking
You’re The Odd One Out
Don’t Rush To Fill The Same Mould As Your ‘Mates’
Once You Have There Is No Going Back.. Only Regrets
When True Love Really Hits And The Time Is Right
You Wont Hear Questions Or Feel Pressure
It Will Happen, And Then You Will Think
Wow! My Mom Was Right…….. So Very Right
