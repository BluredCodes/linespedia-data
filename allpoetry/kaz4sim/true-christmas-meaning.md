title: "True Christmas Meaning"

Christmas bells hanging on a bright festive tree
Exciting wrapped parcels for you and for me
Turkey and trimmings, cupboards full to the seams
Dolls, trains and surprises fill the kid dreams
Holly and mistletoe, garlands more
Brightly decked rooms, from ceiling to floor
But stop for a while, and think of the old
Who maybe right now are sat in the cold
No turkey or trimmings no presents to see
Just a tin of cold soup and a tattered old tree
Will that table your setting, have room another
A forgotten old grandpa, a wife or a mother
Just spare a thought for the old and the frail
Open your hearts, let your goodness prevail
That one special gesture can mean oh so much
At this seasonal time of year don’t let us lose touch
It’s all about giving and celebrating a new birth
So open your home and let new friends feel their worth
Buy a gift for a child, who has no kin of its own
It doesn’t have to be much, just know they’re not alone
Make peace with your foes, start a fresh bright New Year
Filled with goodness and lots of good cheer
Put bygones to rest, let sleeping dogs lie
Be at one with yourself, no more eye for an eye
Thank god for your life, count all of your treasures
Your family and friends, their love you can’t measure
Hold on to the meaning of a true Christmas day
Not a commercial commodity, Happy Christmas I say!
