As I lay beside youAnd feel your warm embraceI feel so safe and loved by youThere is no finer placeThe kids are sleeping soundlyAll tucked up safe and soundThis time is ours my darlingOur love can now aboundGentle caressing fingersStroking each and every partTies of love entwiningOur bodies and our heartsI’ll love you till foreverI’m yours as you are mineMy love for you won’t falterIt’s here for all of timeSo lay with me my darlingLet me caress and care for youSink deep into my lovingThat is only here for you
