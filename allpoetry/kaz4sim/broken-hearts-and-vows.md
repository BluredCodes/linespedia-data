Vows taken, and broken, A heart ripped in twoWhat has really happened? To the man I once knewLies fall from your lips Deceit lies in your heartHow did I not notice?I thought I was smartI said I would knowIt would never be me!Things like this only happenedTo others you seeBut the day that I found ‘it’Was the day that I diedThe love in my heartJust withered, I criedMy mind started racingCould this be a mistake?Could the proof that I have Be somehow a fakeBut before I confronted youI knew in my mindThat our love was forsakenAnd the truth I would findThe lies and the storyPoured clean off your tongue“I tried hard to stop itBefore it had begun”“I didn’t want to hurt you”Ha! A line from a bookDon’t patronise me pleaseAnd take off that lookIf your leaving just goMake it quick, take your leaveI’ll not beg you to stayI will silently grieve
