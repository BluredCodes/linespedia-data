title: "Broken Love Affair"

Tis beauty etched with sadness
That I see upon your face
A broken shattered heart
Leaving an empty shallow space
You’re tear stained cheeks once rosy
Held many a wondrous smile
Your once blue eyes reminded me
Of an excited wondrous child
You’re arms now crave for loving
But they are empty and alone
Your ears once listened attentively
Now listen for the ringing phone
The grey skies that surround you
Will give way to sunnier skies
The pain you feel, so desperately
Will fade within your eyes
Take comfort in those around you
For we are the ones who care
We’ll pick up the shattered pieces
Of the broken love affair
