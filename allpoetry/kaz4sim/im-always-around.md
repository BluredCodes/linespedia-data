title: "I'm Always around"

One moment so fleetingWas it all in my mind?I could have sworn he was hereStanding right by my sideThe smell of his clothesHang sweet in the airThe touch of his handThe smell of his hairThis all cant be falseIt has to be trueBesides fore he wentHe said ”I’ll never leave you”I’ll watch over you dearNever let you fallI’ll always be closeAnd there if you callYou wont see my faceBut you’ll know I’m aroundYou’ll just feel my presenceAs my arms wrap aroundI’ll stay with you alwaysI’ll comfort your tearsFill your memory with good timesTo dispel all the fearsSleep safely my darlingAnd dream of our loveUntil we meet once againIn heaven above
