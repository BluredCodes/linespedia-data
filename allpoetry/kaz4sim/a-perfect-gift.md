---
title: "A Perfect Gift"
writer: "kaz4sim"
slug: "a-perfect-gift"

From a minuscule seed you started to grow
And from the moment we knew, we loved you so
A heart beating fast, striving for life
A beautiful gift between husband and wife
We’ve given you life, and you’re well on your way
To filling our house up with love some day
We can’t wait to meet you and hold you with care
And tell you we love you, and always be there
Watch you as you sleep, with our anxious eyes
Listen for your murmer or hungry cries
Stroke your silky soft hair and round button nose
Stare in awe at your beauty, count all your toes
Kiss your soft cheeks and thank God up above
That he sent us a perfect angel to love
We’ll love you forever of that there’s no doubt
Because this is what loving is all about
