---
title: "To Elise"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

I’ve touched the mirror’s silent sleep
beheld the raindrops puddles reap
torn the sky open just to reach you
beseech you 
hear the voices you hold deep

I’ve scraped the paint from memories
shed the scars that no one sees
faced tethered dreams just to receive you
believe you
blown away now in your breeze

I’ve missed the kisses that you kept
choked on goodbyes I can’t accept
bereaved  to nothing just to contain you
sustain you
drown in seas of tears you wept

I’ve touched the mirror’s silent sleep
beheld the raindrops puddles reap
torn the sky open just to reach you
beseech you
grasped for love I cannot keep
