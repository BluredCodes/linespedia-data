---
title: "People"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

there are people who break
people who love people who
break. there are people with wings
and people without. there are some
who search and some who find
people with wings and people without
there are people awake
there are people sleeping and people
awake but with their eyes closed
there are people firing guns
there are people ringing bells and
talking so loudly but still
there are people sleeping
there are people who care and people
who don’t. people who cry and
people who won’t. there are people
so pretty with watches and ties
there are people so pretty with watches
and lies. there are people who break
people who love people who break
there are people who give and people
who take people with wings and without
there are people who listen to people
who say that people are God in ruins
