title: "If Only You'll Wait"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

I can put tea on for two
I can clean this place and make it like new
I can iron my suit
and flatten out the wrinkles
if only you'll wait

I can think things over
I can brush my teeth and hair
I can open up the windows
invite the breeze and sunshine in
if only you'll wait

I can straighten this out
I can grow muscles and a big smile
I can patch over holes and fill ditches in
I can chase away your nightmares
and forget about mine
if only you'll wait

I can build us a white house
with balconies and gardens
I can get back into shape
I can paint you a pretty picture
cool blue seas and vermillian trees
I can disinfect my hands
and sterilize my past
time is on your side
because I am as well
if only you'll wait
if only you'll wait
