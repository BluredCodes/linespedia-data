everybody’s bled so much
everybody’s shoved the clutch
everybody’s everybodies
lost their touch

gotta save the mouse walking toward the trap
gotta spread the love clear across the map
we’ve gotta find the man responsibe
for this grudge

someone has to pay for the years of shame
someone has to lose til’ we’re all the same
big happy cellophane wrapped and ready
family

gotta hand in the pocket and its full of gold
gotta get my share for the days of old
pass it down take it and run and run
don’t look back

rip him off, he’s had his fun
slit his throat, he’s number one
slash his tires and lock him away
somebody’s somebodies
missing out

GBEthis is about affirmative action and how its been blown completely out of proportion. is it ok for educated, middle-class, white males to hang out together. the answer is yes
