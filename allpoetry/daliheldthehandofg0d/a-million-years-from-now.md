a million years from now
in the impending future
some scientists dig all day
into the past
I can't help but hope
that when they pop open my coffin
and stare into my vacant sockets
I send a shiver down their spines

as flying cars and nanotechs
hum around them
they'll speculate
and meditate
on my snoopy tie

a million years from now
in a neglected museum
some students laugh and laugh
amongst themselves
I can't help but hope
that when all of them have left this place
they somberly recall my remains
with a discomfort they can't shake

as flying cars and nanotechs
hum around them
they'll speculate
and meditate
on my snoopy tie

"a million years ago
in a primitive era
this person traded 'used cars'
for currency
we can't help but hope
that as we uncover more remnants
of this ancient world, perhaps we'll find
meaning in this god called 'Snoopy' "


GBEi often sit and imagine future archaeologists pondering over the substance of our modern society. I find it quite comical to consider what these poor fellows will make of my life and its remains. enjoy
