title: "Futz"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

we gallivant,
enamored with
such sweetly
swollen hearts

the gale sweeps through
the waltzing trees
with weeping
winter grace

your presence does
forever cull
my midst of
misery

I'm content in
believing these
surrogate
streets stretch on
