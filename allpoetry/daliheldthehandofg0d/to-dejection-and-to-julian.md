sparking neon traffic signs
blink warnings to the travelers
falling forth mascara bust
when it hailed and when it rained
Mesozoic era dust
blink warnings to the travelers
nothing lost and nothing gained
floodlights out full up then drained
garage sale magnet unexplained
blink warnings to the travelers
Mesozoic era dust
complain, obtain constructive trust
when it hailed and when it rained
so beautiful, yet still you must
blink warnings to the travelers


GBE
