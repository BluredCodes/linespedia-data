From such a distance
you have missed so very much
I’m a speck, static and obscure
on an electric horizon
me here, you there
a falling sky above us

From such a distance
my cries must sound like laughter
to hell with the space between us
and to hell with that behind us too
what’s the matter?
nothing, nothing, I’m OK

From such a distance
I’m just busy killing time
moving to the slow but steady 
ticking of opportunity 
I’ll shut up now
and try my best not to blink
