---
title: "science and scientists"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"
slug: "science-and-scientists"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

everflowing crux
evershifting gears
this forced milieu 
despite the years
continues the course
still chasing leads	
the public wants
the public needs
the public hears
the public reads
soar through space
and flip the switch
make the weaker strong
the poorer rich
hold off the superstate
at gunpoint perhaps
turn the tables
fill the chalkboards
with chickenscratched 
circular thought
fill every lab stool
with muddled men
their nodding heads
and monotone chattering
continues the course
saves the day
the public tugs
the public pays
the public shoves
the public stays
to the very bloody end
