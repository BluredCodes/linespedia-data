---
title: "Tangent"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

source: "AllPoetry"

the tide that slips into the sea
draws remnants of your mind to me
those sacred parts
those ancient skies
stretch nothing into something

your ways remain a mystery
that starves my curiosity
those cement stars
those urges to
leave my tongue in your mailbox
