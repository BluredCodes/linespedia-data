---
title: "Something For Shelly"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"
slug: "something-for-shelly"
source: "AllPoetry"

you’re reluctant to slip
into an alternate nuance
and abscond this casual town
it's really no wonder
it's really no wonder anymore
that you’re so down

I didn’t hesitate for you
I didn’t turn my head for you
these things I did, I did
on my own behalf
so take your compliments back

you crack open the valves
on tankers and watch this ocean
overflow with your fruitless lies
it's really no wonder
it's really no wonder anymore
these cloudless skies

I didn’t make amends for you
I didn’t wait around for you
these things I did, I did
on my own behalf
so take your compliments back
