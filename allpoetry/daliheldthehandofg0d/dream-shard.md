title: "Dream Shard"

awaken once more to lament
mistaken, mistrusted, misspent
freely falling rain dripping drops
from sky to the shadowed cement

satellites sailing through space
disputed, discerning, displace
silently streams the arched asteroid
through vastness bestowing no trace

sparrows leap forward in flight
contrasted, constricted, contrite
with wistful youth they come and then go
forgiving the wind of its blight
