I found a rose
beneath my window
on Valentine’s Day morning
two years in a row

you’re so silly
or maybe you’re brilliant
to visit me for the third time
with me there, waiting

its pouring rain
but still you arrived
never in a million years
would I have guessed, you

I startled you
and you screamed aloud
when I stepped from the darkness
a vase in my hands

its only me
so don’t be frightened
didn’t you know I’d be here
awaiting my rose?

I pulled you close
kissed your neck and lips
you dropped the rose and withdrew
I’ll see you next year
