beautiful you : faithless I


With your face in my hands
and all but my thumbs
immersed among your hair
a tear slipped from the slate
and trickled beneath my palm

beautiful you
and faithless I
watching the sun
plummet down and then die

Our pulsing bodies tumble
to a music heard
nowhere else by no one else
while wind rolls from your lips 
sent crashing to the floor

resolute you
and shattered I
share a mattress
and the will to comply

We topple into the streets
wide eyed head spinning
sunbathed simple careless love
with shame left to weeping
in the basement underwater

resilient you
and bashful I
live in denial
of each other’s goodbye
