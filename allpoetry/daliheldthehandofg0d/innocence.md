I can hear the age old wordscrack beneath her smilecatching breath and breaking past through her heart's bastionand her glance alights to minefaithlessly wandering little girllost in my groundless gazeand innocence fallsfearless and unkemptinnocence drownsdeep beneath her tearsinnocence criesquestioning its birthI can see her sunset eyesand the way she used to walkpast this senseless world and allinconsistence drawsthe simplicity of youthbetween the dreams she lingered inonce upon a timeand innocence goneshe clenched at nothing's faceinnocence sheathedwithin my cluttered headinnocence cries questioning its birthGBE
