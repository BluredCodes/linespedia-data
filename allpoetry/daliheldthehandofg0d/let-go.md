---
title: "Let Go"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"
slug: "let-go"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12093-Let-Go-by-daliheldthehandofg0d"
---

sinking your words are deeper than your essence
and the thoughts that i've been fed
thinking your wonder is deeper than your presence
and the ideas they've fit in my head
forgive my behavior
its human its selfish
its me
forgive my trespasses
its thoughtless its careless
its free
i'm what you made me to be
for what you gave me to see
you're what i refuse to need
i'll let go

trying your wisdom is feeling i'm losing your trace
a temple built upon conjecture
laughing, they curse you and spear you and spit in your face
its simple to worship a lecture
forgive my question
its stupid its pointless
its me
forgive my transgression
its lustful its greedy
its free
i'm what you made me to be
for what you gave me to see
you're what i refuse to need
i'll let go
