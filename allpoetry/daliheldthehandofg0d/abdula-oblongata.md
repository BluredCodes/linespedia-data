title: "Abdula Oblongata"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

he sits on the back porch
indian style
attempting ignorance
while wringing his tiny hands

he picks at the aged paint
peeled from the wall
through which shouting passes
his jaws clench in agony

he lowers his blonde head
surrendering
papa is breaking plates
mama screams apologies

he watches the sunset
all by himself
mama is cleaning up
papa lights a cigarette

he sits on the back porch
in denial
holding a grasshopper
pulling legs from its body
