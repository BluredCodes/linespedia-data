sitting in the cemetery
leaned against the grave
of a Mrs. Mordremarrie
I watch mourning doves
lunge from the trees
away into the swelling clouds
and I imagine that below me
within the ground I rest on
are thousands upon thousands
of lifeless bodies returning
to their earthly elements 
I raise my eyes to the distant traffic
full up with economy cars
carrying bodies just as lifeless
to and away from their business
it no longer feels strange
or unnecessary to be
sitting in the cemetery
