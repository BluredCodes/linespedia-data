title: "Innernothingleft"

the business of this place
the razed surrounding
skin soaked in my sight
skin soaked in my sight
your fragile, lonely face

innersearching, innerbreakdown
innerloss of hope
innerspotlight, innermousetrap
innernothingleft

I grasped your goodbye and so
you say I’m making
mountains of molehills
mountains of molehills
you’ll never really know

innergasping, innerticking
innerwait for dawn
innerashes, innergraveyard
innernothingleft

dismal ringing telephone
whose ringing repeats 
pull wires from the ground
pull wires from the ground
the boorish sound you’ve known

innergunfire, innerkeyhole
innerabandonment  
innerliving, innerdying
innernothingleft


GBE
