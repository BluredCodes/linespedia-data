faith, you kill spiders and cryfaith, you won’t ask questionshow can you not care why?you listen everyday to everythingas if it suits you at allfaithsight, you see light where none shinessight, your vision reflectsthe earth’s glow through my blindsbut do you present my chagrin acrossand how do you really love?sightsoul, my tear-soaked little childsoul, you take in this worldwith time you’ve grown mildas you throw away scars and words and daysthough I know, you’ll never changesoulheart, I betray you, I knowheart, you’ve stood by my lifeand it’s chains bind you soI’ve scrawled missives on your walls, where they’ll staya thousand days passed known byheartGBE
