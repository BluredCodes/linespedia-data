where are you running
with sagging luggage
and eyes to match?
each door approached
is one you’ve found locked
with chain and latch
and where is your place
in this genocide
called “modern art”

do you belong here
in this traffic jam
of black and white?
every handshake
every goodbye
every kiss goodnight
there is no answer-
just yesterday and
tomorrow alone
