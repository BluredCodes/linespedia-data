fitful recollections come screaming
from where i tried to leave them
behind the silence of broken nightmares
behind whispered words you ever heard
keep the things that hold your scent
but nothing, nothing holds your warmth
and the future holds a tale of solitude
inside myself i tried to leave them
inside the dust storms where misery died
my liable spirit bleeds colours
embellished the purity of radiance
scourged the levity in her eyes
most of me departed along with you
all of me that ever in my mind counted
on walking life from henceforth alone
refraining the tears that incessantly fall
to stream the skin you used to touch
to dream that you would find beauty 
with blushing brilliance and
you, whose arms brought me safety-
steal, steal, steal my heart
pour until its not
merrily, merrily, merrily, merrily
love was but a thought.
