title: "While the Sun’s Still Sleeping"

you sleep so soundly
unstirred by my
watchfulness 
your eyelids flutter
you’re dreaming

your chest rises and
falls underneath
the covers
you smile sweetly, glazed
by moonlight

I love seeing you
toss and turn, then
awaken
rubbing the sleep from
your young eyes

I have your shoes here
and your jacket
come on, babe
lets go while the sun’s
still sleeping
