sir and ma’am lend me your ear
the enemy’s grasp is closing near
chains and scraps and books and maps
we need to kill the monster
our boys need more, anything more
your nation warmly thanks you

sir and ma’am the news is here
the war has gone our way this year
still sheets and suits and lace up boots
can help us win our freedom
our boys must march, they simply must
your nation warmly thanks you

sir and ma’am you’re not to fear
our way to victory is clear
monkey wrenches and shovels for trenches
you see our men are dug in
our boys are fighting awfully hard
your nation warmly thanks you

sir and ma’am you have to hear
this day is one of grateful cheer
the rainproof tent and tires you sent
they paid off in the end
our boys fought very bravely
your things are on their way home
