I lie down this sore shellafter an arduous day,my faltering eyelids  leave meinside darkness;within a dream of my ownAs my sleeping bodysurely breathes in and breathes out,I am busy with creationof this substance;within a dream of my ownHands and feet followin accordance with the real.Rows of houses, columns of timecoordinate;within a dream of my ownshe appears to me thererefined, defined, and alive.Even in my realm she retires,shutting her eyes;within a dream of my ownwatching her beside memy sweet, sleeping entity.I wonder- have we united?awake, asleep;within a dream of my ownGBE
