---
title: "You Rose"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"
slug: "you-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

you rose, as indifferent halcyon 
you rose, an unfamiliar repugnance 
like a coiled sunset of thunder claps
like an obdurate clock in lapse
you rose

you walk, past this dignified tableau
you walk, a cascaded iridescence
as a quiescently flux ravens scatter
as through abashed worldly flatter
you walk

you smile, an idle comely beam
you smile, as these atmospheres rearrange
such as the daunted secrets left untold
such as the dregs amassed unfold
you smile

you rose, thicket of ground aggrandizing 
you rose, nor bleed enough to misconstrue 
like the furor besotted gathers rain
like your sweet pedals’ juice should stain
you rose
