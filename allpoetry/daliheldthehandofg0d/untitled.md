I watch you peripherally 
peripherally watching me,
and I can see clearly
the cranks churning
behind those intake eyes.
I know that you’re wondering,
“Is there really something
beyond his appearance- beyond
the bound between our strides?”
I’d have myself dart to you
and sweep you off of your feet
and kiss you gently, at first
and then without reserve,
and whisper in riposte 
“Yes, a thousand times yes!
my sweet, true love, its affinity.”
However, I remain.
For if it is there at all,
this bridge amid our hearts,
you too must know as well
that you and I are one.
