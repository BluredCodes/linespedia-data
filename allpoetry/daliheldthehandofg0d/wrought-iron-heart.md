wrought iron heartwhen will you learnyour shattered crescentturn sideways leapedinto spring warmthwrought iron heartwhich do you seekjunctured smile sortsyou into one windyavenue of solacewrought iron heartnever weaken nevershine enough to seeyour own reflectionof rusted, agingwrought iron heartteeth tear you in twoworld scares you intoyour silence unawakenshimmer forever and glowwrought iron heartforced the stammered speakadjacent to the wallsand ceiling that keep your destiny, my lovewrought iron heartwalk to nowhere at leastnowhere you havetwisted straight and bentto fit a shameful outcastwrought iron heartcaught melting downbetween such pillarsof piety and remorseerr whichever you makewrought iron heartunleash the scornliberate and redeemimmersed in love andtaken forgrantedwrought iron heart
