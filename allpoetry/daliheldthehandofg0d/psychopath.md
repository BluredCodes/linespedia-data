distracted byimpertinence I lose grasp-I would break your bonesto meet the idealI would gouge your eyesand rip your skinjust to feelfor one moment, realI want them to knowit could have beenanyoneinside my headvases shatterout pours this-I would spill your bloodto meet the idealI would take your tongueand crush your teethjust to feelfor one moment, realI want to scream outto the childrenbelongingsearching withinfor a shred ofsoul, I find-I would pierce your heartto meet the idealI would crack your skulland slit your throatjust to feelfor one moment, realI pray to the godsto take me awayto wipe me clearinto oblivioninto deep sleepor to let me feelfor one momentanythingGBEjust as to not worry anyone, these aren't my personal feelings.  I wrote this out of my fascination with abnormal psychology.  The content is largely based on books I've read about psychopathic behaviour.  In these books diagnosed psychopaths explain that they are not insane or illusional, but were born with the incapacity to feel.
