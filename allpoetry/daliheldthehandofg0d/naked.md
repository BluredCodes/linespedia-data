Naked


something tells me
there is more to you
than your gaudy jewelry
and sycophantic smile
your double-knotted laces
and your overcooked style
your cautious way of driving
and how you’re always striving
to live up to that something
that your mother says she is
and I don’t mind that you avoid
making eye contact with me
cause when you slip and sometimes do
in my eyes you see what's clearly true
I’d rather see you naked

something tells me
there is more to you
than your tidy cursive
and respect for the law
your unconfident aura 
and your trips to the mall
your healthy way of eating
and how you’re always cheating
yourself out of what is yours 
but your mother says is hers 
I’ll never forget the night
or the beauty that I saw
as I watched your soul shining through
your old abandoned reluctance to
stand before me naked
