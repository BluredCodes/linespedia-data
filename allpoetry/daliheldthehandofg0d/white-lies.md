No, Grace, of course not
I like you too, honestly
if you were only older
I’d date you for sure
you’re pretty, you know that

what’s that, Ed? oh, oh yeah!
absolutely- the tie rocks-
where did you find that anyway?
would you mind very much
if I bought that same one?

do you want the truth, Mrs. McKay?
This is the most delicious casserole 
that I think I’ve ever eaten.
I’d have a second helping, certainly
but a little goes so far. mmmmm!

hello Cindy, no, you didn’t wake me.
Friday night? let me take a look.
Gracious, wouldn’t you know,
I’ve a dentist appointment then
but do have a happy birthday, ok?

And while Grace, Ed, Mrs. McKay, and Cindy are tucked comfortably in bed
lulled to sleep by the demonstrative illusions of me
being favorable of their affection, fashion, cooking, and company-
I lie awake, being torn limb from limb by little, white lies.
