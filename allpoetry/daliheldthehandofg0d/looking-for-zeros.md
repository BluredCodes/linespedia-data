---
title: "Looking for Zeros"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

If I were to tell you
that everything and anything
around you
is but the figment of a lonely child’s 
vivid imagination,
would you surrender?
would you submit?
would you finally give up
on looking for zeros?

If I were to tell you
to look beyond this vapid plane’s
perspective
and into the eyes you choose to deny
for the sake of your comfort,
would you awaken?
would you reflect?
would you finally give up
on looking for zeros?

If I were to tell you
to take a step over the edge
and plummet
into depths of yourself that you had not
acknowledged were beautiful,
would you trust in me?
would you believe?
would you finally give up
on looking for zeros?
