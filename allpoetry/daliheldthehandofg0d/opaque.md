for just a taste of what will showa spark to veil the nightfor just a waste of what we'll knowa dark and helpless plightwithout a care the code restrainsfrom youthful actsand gloomy factsthe gorgeous life remains	for but a chance to find a wayin passive days of newto paint a stance of words to saypermissive shades of blueadbicated lives free from sinregrettful yearsdisdainful tearsfor what you loved back thenauthentic desires left to dryamong the silence missesabusive tuesdays never diereturns the stolen kissesabove the clouds still something burnscomplacent daysand still she stayslove she fails- love she learnsGBE
