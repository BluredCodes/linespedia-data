---
title: "Insomnia's Song"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"
slug: "insomnias-song"
source: "AllPoetry"

this air tastes the same
everywhere
it cracks when it dries
in the sun

and it’s easier to sleep
pretending there’s more
it’s much easier to sleep
curled up on the floor

a head full of your
promises
a heart full of faith
in your love

and it’s easier to sleep
when one of you stays
it’s much easier to sleep
I’ve not slept for days

think I’ll take a drive
come along
I’d like to spend a while
someplace else

and it’s easier to sleep
you’re right, my mistake
it’s much easier to sleep
not caring to wake

a head full of your
promises
a heart full of faith
in your love


GBEI can't sleep
