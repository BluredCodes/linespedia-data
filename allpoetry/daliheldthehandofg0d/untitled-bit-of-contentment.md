I lie back
in the middle of the lawn
surveying the sky above
a trillion shimmering flames
strung together by the richest
black fabric of space
racing since time began
simply to catch my
wonder

in this place
I forget to remember
and even time fades away
all I feel is completion
as everything and nothing
fall into balance
I stretch my toboggan
down over  my eyes
and smile
