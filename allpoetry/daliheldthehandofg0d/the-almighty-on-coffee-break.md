running fingersthrough his beardthick skin and swollen knucklesgrope the white locksstreaming from his chinlost in a daydreamchildish imaginationchildish omnipotencewith his other handhe automatically stirshis rich, black java on the surface isthe swirling creamof a universechurning in ignorancechurning in blissGBE
