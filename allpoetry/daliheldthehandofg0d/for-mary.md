---
title: "For Mary"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

you’re a daydream’s motive
you’re a fading care
you’re a whispered word of
someone, somewhere’s prayer

you’re an art unnoticed
you’re my heart in flight
you’re a love undying
through the storming night

you’re a soulsworn promise
you’re an autumn breeze
you’re my childhood’s echo
sounding from the seas

you’re the countless reasons
in my stammered speech
you’re the brightness among
stars I’ll never reach
