title: "Said Vulcan to Man"
writer: "daliheldthehandofg0d"

sailing into open space
remembrance of the last embrace
the fickly, falling words you wrote
stabbed deeply within my veins

peace is something we must find
universe whispered in its wind
this battered, bombshelled masquerade
left no one here unconquered

you’re right, its just like you said
the planets hum inside my head
our steaming, storming  stalemate spoke
son, peace is something we find
