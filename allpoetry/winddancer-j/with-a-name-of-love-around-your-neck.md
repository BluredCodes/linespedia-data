“A Name of Love 
Around Your Neck”

You wear her name proudly around your neck.
A proof that she is the one you love above the rest.
The dark side you left when first she did appear.
Bringing you a light of caring, chasing away your fears.

A union you both did form, with a promise to love forever.
To honor and keep her, you did vow to forsake her never.
The love you now share can last a lifetime and a day.
Filled with peace and joy you both shall forever stay.

Remember always that first moment when your eyes met.
And you knew in your heart, she was your soul mate at best.
You set your sites on her, hoping she would feel the same.
And returns your feelings of love without playing games.

From this point on when life seems so complicated and hard.
Look to your loving wife for comfort, her arms won’t be far.
Your soul mate she’ll be forever, your soul mate she’ll be for life.
With the understanding of a saint, she is now your loving wife. 


         ***Judith***
