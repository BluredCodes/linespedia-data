“The Rebirth”

Into the pit of despair I await,
For a light to shine a clear way.
Into the abyss of doubt I cast, 
All my confusion and hate

Into a cavern of futile attempts I hurl 
All my surrenders and make my own fate.
Into the desert of woe I put down, 
All the broken pieces for loves sake.

And all that remains is me,
An empty shell to be filled with wonders
Fill me with love, desires, and pleasures.
Watch me transform into a thing of beauty.

‘Tis now I can begin to live a life, 
A life of peaceful heavenly bliss.
Leaving behind, all my cut bonds,
The despair, the doubts, 
The confusion and hate,
My futile attempts, and surrendered life,
And the desert of woes, and of broken heart.

Here I am, anew.
Greet me now with a loving embrace,
For I am reborn, a new person today.
Never will I look back, 
For I have learned from my mistakes.
