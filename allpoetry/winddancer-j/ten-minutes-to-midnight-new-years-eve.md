“Ten Minutes to MidnightNew Years Eve”Alone I contemplate my life.Was I truly meant to be a wife?I wonder the question, and ask why?Why does my heart hurt so deep inside?Alone I review my past,I start with lovers that never will last.The errors I’ve made, the lies I’ve told.And why do I have such an empty soul?Alone I decide I need to change.I rid myself of all that causes me pain.The negative feeling, I carry inside.To it all, I bide them, a fond goodbye.    The birth of a New Year and Me!PLEASE!!!!
