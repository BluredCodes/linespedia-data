“A Place of Rainbow 
           Colored Flowers”

The forest is filled with magical wonders,
That draws me in, ever so deep.
The air all around, brings such tranquility 
That causes me, a very long sleep.

The fairies cast their spells,
And I’m whisked away.
To the world of enchantment,
Where my dream lover and I play.

Running through the tall fields,
Of bright colorful flowers.
Talks of love, under the blue skies
Talks that last for long hours.

These are our special moments,
The outside world, does not exist.
Here is where, I wish to remain,
Where my life feels so complete.

I awaken from this dream, much too soon, 
Leaving me yearning to return to my lover.
How do I return? How can I stay forever?
In this place, of rainbow colored flowers.
