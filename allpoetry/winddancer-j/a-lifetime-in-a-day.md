“A Life Time in a Day”

You came to me from the angels, 
filling my life with such wonders.
I never could have imagine,
A mother’s love could be so strong.

I cradled you in my arms speaking softly ,
Making plans for your entire life.
Planning your first words, first step, 
first day of school, even your first date.

My dreams for your wonderful life
Was planned on that one single day. 
I planned the day when you would
Walk down the aisle in a perfect union.

But Then….

As I spoke, the angels came back.
And took you away from my life.
Although you only lived one day,
In my heart you lived a life time.

Now you’re gone from my arms, 
and I feel empty and my heart aches,
My most precious little girl, 
you will remain in my memories always.
