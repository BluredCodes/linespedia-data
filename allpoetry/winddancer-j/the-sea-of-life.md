“The Sea Of Life”

The sea of life can be as blue as your eyes, 
Green as the grass or as black as my heart. 
Black and cold it exists without truly living. 
Green with envy and blue with sadness. 
How do I escape these bonds that constrain me? 

From the moment of my birth, my story was set. 
I was never to know love or any form of happiness. 
To live a miserable life; with constant struggles. 
Unending fights, failures, so I may record my experience.
 
To share with others, this knowledge I’ve gain 
The feeling of emptiness and being unnecessary.
A world filled with creatures whose lives are unbearable. 
Yet they manage to soar to new levels, in the sea of life.
To meet new challenges, never looking back with regrets. 

My mission in life is almost at its conclusion.
My life will be appraised, by the manner I lived.
My story will be reveal, and all will comprehend.
No matter how unfathomable the sea of life gets, 
Never give up, Never give into it.
