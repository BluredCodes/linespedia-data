“Song with no Words”

My songs have no word; that will make you come to me.
My eyes are filled with loneliness; how I wish you could see.
My songs go unnoticed as I go through life day to day.
My heart beats a love song for you, hoping you will stay.

Do you ever have a need for that perfect partner in life?
To comfort you, stand by you and just be a loving wife.
Have you ever thought of a time when you wanted more?
Someone who’ll listen, not judge you, or try to keep score.

When life is a time of misery, rearing its ugly head.
And you wish all your enemies would all just drop dead.
 I’d like to be there, being a friend you can count on.
Asking nothing in return, just a friendship to build on .

It’s ok to be weak; it doesn’t make you less of a man.
You can’t be strong all the time. This I understand.
A life long dream of mine is that you’ll hear my songs.
And from this new friendship of ours, a love will be born.
