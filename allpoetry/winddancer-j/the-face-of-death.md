“The Face of Death”

Speak, and I will heed your words,
Approach, and I will embrace you.
I surrender to you my life essence
For you are the Face of Death.
Bringing comfort when my time comes.

I will not attempt to cheat death, 
It’s no use to even make the effort.
If I decide to run away and hide,
My pain becomes increasingly arduous,
And you will locate and capture me.

So I remain and await my fate with anticipation,
As only I must do without fear.
For you are the Face of Death approaching, 
To deliver me to my new place.
A place with a warm loving light and no pain.

A breeze whisper, tells me you’re near.
I hold my breathe and count down to my end.
The Face of Death reveals itself to me,
But with a kiss, he passes me by and bids me farewell.
On this night I am spared my life’s termination.
