“Bitter Sweet Memories”

Bitter sweet memories fall like autumn leaves.
Leaves cascading down, on the abandon heart.
This is the place where a love once lived,
Now a place no one dares visits anymore.

Flowers will no longer bloom here, 
Or bring us the colors of the rainbow
As it was the heart, that nurtured the flowers.
And a heart, without love can not live.

A lover’s moon fills the night.
But this moon tonight goes unnoticed.
For this heart no longer dares to hope
This is the heart that lost all its dreams
