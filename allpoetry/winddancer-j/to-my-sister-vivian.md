"To My Sister Vivian"

Dear sister, dear sister, where have you gone now.
You said you’d never leave us, this you once vow.
Dear sister, dear sister, I know you’ve suffered with pain.
Cause I saw your tears coming down, like the heavy rain.

You left this life too early, leaving behind a silence so very loud.
But I know you’re with our father, floating high in those clouds.
Dear sister, dear sister, you’re absences is being felt by all.
By all who knew and loved you and those who’ve come to call.

Dear sister, dear sister, you left me with a hole in my heart.
A hole that can never be filled, but I know you have to depart
Dear sister, I will never forget the joy you brought to my days.
I will greatly miss you, and in my heart I know, I’ll see you one day.
