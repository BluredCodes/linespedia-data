"A Sweet Goodbye"

Fair thee well, for I must depart for sure.
To go under ground with words to conjure.
Conjure up magic words from my heart.
To convey a secret, of beauty and art.

So, Merry Part I say to thee.
My words are filled with love and glee
I thank thee all, with much appreciation.
I’ll be back at sunset with more creation.
