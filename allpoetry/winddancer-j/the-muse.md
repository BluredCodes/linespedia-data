“The Muse”

Be not afraid of your Muse...
It knows your mind and knows your heart. 
It only reveals what you want deep inside.
In careful riddles and obscure rhymes.

Embrace your Muse, they mean no harm.
They’re here to help you examine your mind.
As a tool they aid you, they brake down walls.
These walls so thick, that sustains us all. 

Count on your Muse.
They’ll never let you down.
When you need them most, they’re not far.
Right at your side, whispering word from your soul.


***Judith***For all who fear their Muses!
Or those tiny voices in your head!
