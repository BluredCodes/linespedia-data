title: "'Please Don't Tell'"

The hall so narrow, the room so cold
It will hurt a little, she was told

The bed so plain, she hated the smell
She confided in the nurse, 'Please don't tell'

Into the gown she slowly dressed
As the doctor poked and pressed

'Young lady are you sure you've made your choice?'
'Yes, sir,' trembled in her voice

It was over before she knew it; the procedure had been done
Her heart is heavy now; she feels it weighs a ton

She told the nurse, 'Don't get me wrong, this I don't condone'
'But it was the only way, I could not bear alone'

In life there are certain choices we just have to make
And just the same come the consequences for the risks we take
