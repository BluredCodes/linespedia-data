---
title: "Trapped in a Memory"
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "trapped-in-a-memory"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32199-Trapped-in-a-Memory-by-Blythe"
---

Your memory rings in my head
But I shall answer no more
You voice bounces in my heart
But I will listen no longer
Your scent lingers on my skin
But I shall hold my breath
Your flavor present in my mouth
But I will not swallow
Your harsh words branded in my mind
But I refuse to remember
Your body imprinted in mine
But I will resculpt it
Your dependance grips my heart
But I promise I'll break free
You trapped me in your memory
But I shall escape
