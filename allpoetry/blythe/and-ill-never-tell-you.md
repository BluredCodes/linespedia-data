---
title: "And I'll Never Tell You..."
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "and-ill-never-tell-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32213-And-Ill-Never-Tell-You...-by-Blythe"
---

And I'll never tell you...
And you'll never know...
All the things that I do...
But can't bare to show...

Wandering fingers grip the phone...
Dial all but the last...
God, how it hurts to be alone...
My strength is fading fast...

And I'll never tell you...
And you'll never know...
All the things that I do...
But can't bare to show...

I cry myself to sleep sometimes...
For hours at a time...
Full of pain I choose to keep...
Not yours...but pain of mine...

And I'll never tell you...
And you'll never know...
All the things that I do...
But can't bare to show...

My pride, it grips me...
It won't let me go...
My mind it slips me...
So I dare not show...

And I'll never tell you...
And you'll never know...
All the things that I do...
But can't bare to show...

It drives me crazy...
I share your kiss...
The past is hazy...
The past is this...

And I'll never tell you...
And you'll never know...
All the things that I do...
But can't bare to show...
