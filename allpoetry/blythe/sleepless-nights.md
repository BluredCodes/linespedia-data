---
title: "Sleepless Nights"
writer: "Blythe"

I count the teardrops...
One...Two...Three
I sometimes wonder....
Is all enough of me...

I count the hours...
Eleven...Twelve...One
My pillow has moistened
The night has just begun...

Tried counting sheep...
Six hundred...Seven...Eight
It isn't working...
My, It's getting late...

The ZZZs escape me...
Tears keep me awake...
Rings of black embrace me...
Sandman, salt you take...

I lie awake, alone...
Searching for a cure...
Broken Heart/Insomnia...
Incurable, I'm sure...

Jealousy overwhelms me...
Envy fills my pores...
This woman seems to plague me...
This past love of yours...

I thought the wounds had healed
But I was very wrong...
I thought you had forgotten...
You remembered all along...

You'll never see me cry at night...
But know I surely do...
On my cloud of confusion...
Raining tears for you...
