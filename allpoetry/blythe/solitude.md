---
title: "Solitude"
writer: "Blythe"

---

If you're full of emptiness
Are you still hallow?
If loneliness chokes you
Will you still swallow?

If love has left you
Will you leave it be?
If time forgets you
Will your eyes not see?

If you fear the pendulum
Will it slow its swing?
If your wound has closed some
Will the pain not sting?

If your bed is empty
Will you love the space?
If none at all is plenty
Will you hide your face?

When 'one' is your only
Is that all you've got?
Will you always be lonely
Or wish you were not?
