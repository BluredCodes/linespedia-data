---
title: "My Own Hell"
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "my-own-hell"
source: "AllPoetry"

A place I have created
Built with my two hands
Why I can't escape it
No one understands

I constructed these walls
Surely built to last
Bricks soaked in blood
Memories of the past

These halls I wander
I have set the pace
These wrinkles on my forehead
I have harmed this face

Swallowed the key
Sealed the lock
Sprung from dilusion
Gone into shock

I am the ruler
And this is my hell
I made the blueprints
And I know them well
