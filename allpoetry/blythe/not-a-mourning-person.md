---
title: "Not a Mourning Person"
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "not-a-mourning-person"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30413-Not-a-Mourning-Person-by-Blythe"
---

From the depths of a memory
In the pit of a dream
Locked away for what seemed like forever
Until today...it seems

Too many nightmares
3 a.m. fills the room
Left with only recollections
The echo of a hollow womb

Faint remeberance of a day
Months became a blur
4 weeks were all that stood
Between him, between her

Not a mourning person
Just a bit displaced
Haunting of a memory
That cannot be erased

Half a year and counting
Solace show your face
Not a mourning person
Fill this empty space

How's the saying go
Ignorance is bliss
Maybe once upon a time
But can she live with this

Not a mourning person
Blood dripped from her thighs
A memory of the past
But the pain holds in her eyes

No where to turn
No where to go
A part of him, a part of her
She'll never know

Nobody's fault
Nobody's choice
Echoes from within
The child's unheard voice
