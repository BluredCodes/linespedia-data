title: "When it Won't Hurt Anymore"
writer: "Blythe"

When the tears stop flowing
When the pain stops growing

When the scars start healing
When the wounds lose feeling

When the pillow finally dries
When the red leaves from these eyes

When the songs stop making sense
When I get back my innocence

When my chest feels full instead
When your name finally leaves my head

When the nights don't feel so cold
When I forget all the lies I've been told

When my memory becomes somewhat rusty
When my heart grows almost dusty

When the roses finally stop blooming
When love isn't so 'heart-consuming'

Maybe that's when it won't...

Hurt Anymore...
