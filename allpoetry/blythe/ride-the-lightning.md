title: "Ride the Lightning"

I want to ride the lightning
Embrace the silver moon
Breathe life into the tempest
Take hold the great monsoon

Gulp the starlight's twinkle
Dive into the milky way
Swim along Poseidon
Oh, take my breath away

Dancing in the moonlight
Tempt the wanting Sun
Swept up in a tornado
Ride the lightning with someone

Upon the clouds of satin
Beneath a bed of stars
Make love tome on Earth
Make love to me on Mars

Kiss me like a summer breeze
Run your fingers through my hair
If only for a moment
Wish you were there

Let me ride the lighting
Feel the thunder in my soul
Like drops of rain into the ocean
Be my universe, make me whole
