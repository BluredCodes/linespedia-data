---
title: "Angel in Disguise"
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "angel-in-disguise"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32195-Angel-in-Disguise-by-Blythe"
---

You spread your wings to shield me...
And I trampled them
You placed your halo on my finger...
And I pawned it
You leaned forward to kiss my cheek...
And I bit you
You offered to embrace me...
And I knocked you down
You whispered in my ear...
And I yelled at you
You offered me your breath...
And I took it
You opened up your heart...
And I broke it
You were my angel in disguise...
And I put you through hell
...And I'm sorry...
