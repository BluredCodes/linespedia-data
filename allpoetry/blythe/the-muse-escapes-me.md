---
title: "The Muse Escapes Me"
writer: "Blythe"
slug: "the-muse-escapes-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

I dreamt a dream tonight, he begged me to let go
He said he could no longer teach me what I already know

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

He whispered in my ear, 'There's no more left to tell'
Kissed my forehead and said, 'My dear I've taught you well'

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

I'm through digging this hole in your chest
Now my dear, it's time you rest

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

So he whispered on and on
Soon my dear, it won't be long

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

I've corrupted your mind and swallowed your heart
You've told my story, you've played my part

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

So you've served your time in hell
Goodnight my dear, I bid farewell

I've run out of rhymes, the muse escapes me

I awoke with such a burden lifted
The muse escapes me, my world has shifted
