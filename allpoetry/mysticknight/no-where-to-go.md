---
title: "No Where to go..."
writer: "mysticknight"

As the wind blows,
The sky goes gray.
The rains starts to flow
And I know not to Stay,
So I part away now.
With no kiss on the brow.
The gods are crying,
And I want to start dieing.
I am confused and lost
And knowing everything has a cost.
The winds and the rain become harder,
And I go farther...
I walk away from it all
And I to make a call???
I have nowhere to go, 
Not even to the local whore,
I have nowhere to hide,
I have nobody to get on my side,
Enemies & Friends,
Friends & Enemies...
All the same around here,
Just like every beer...if you want to critical, I'll accept it with open arms...
