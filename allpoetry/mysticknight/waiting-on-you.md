lurking within shadows of the house,
forever lost into this place.
Like a caged animal,
I wait for my death.
Maybe time will end,
or will it begin...
forever and ever I'll wait...
But never it will come.
Seeming to pass by this place.
Yet always looking in.
Maybe someday,
I won't have to lurk...
Within shadows of the house,
That will be the day,
when I won't be blue,
because I'll be with you!do you the reader have problems with understanding the point behind this?
should I change something if anything??? 
do I need help anywhere???
