title: "trapped behind paine"

Starring out from behind the glass,
Looking for something that he knows,
yet seeing nothing...
Memories haunt and plague his mind,
For he's condemned to suffer forever,
Stuck behind the paine of glass,
Trapped in the room...
Forever tormented with things that past,
And the pain for which was brought.
So lonely staying behind the paine of glass,
Is trapped forever, and without you!!!be honest and tell me what ya think...
