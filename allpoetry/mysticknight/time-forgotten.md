---
title: "Time Forgotten..."
writer: "mysticknight"
slug: "time-forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"

Seems so long ago since it happened,
The broken heart of a young child...
It was only you who done it, 
No sword nor weapon could do what you done to it...
Years past on as your child grew up,
Now 15 years later, the phone rings,
There you were on the phone wishing merry Christmas...
Tears filled the eyes, both out of anger and love,
Confusing it was to hear you...
Its been so long ago, but you try to fix what you broke...
But time forgotten and you were too...
The young child with the heart so broken,
Has become a young man, 
Whose heart is completely healed without you apart of it...
Effort you show, and it is returned...
Yet, still you know not what you done,
But trying to remember time forgotten...
Maybe one day before time is completely forgotten...
I’ll show something in my heart that’s better than what you broke...
But don’t forget a young child has become a young man...
but time forgotten when you been there, 
Yet its been forgotten by you...
So I must do the same... 
All in time...




In honor of my father, for carelessly not loving his child...
