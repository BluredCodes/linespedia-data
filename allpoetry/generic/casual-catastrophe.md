empty lyrics and brainwashed kids
that's all everything around me is
it makes me gag
another moment lags
kill me before i do

look at you-
a perfect enigma,
lacking stigma

look at me-
i'm a casual catastrophe
tired of the routine
tired of the screams
and another moment lags
the whole world steps back
kill me before they dothis is from me in high school...my junior year or something (i think it is crappy)
yep, but i like the phrase casual catastrophe, perhaps the best two-word description of myself that i will ever be able to come up with....and i think that makes it worthy of posting.
