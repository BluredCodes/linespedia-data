WHY DO YOU MISTRUST ME?
I WANT TO KNOW.
I SWEAR I WOULD NEVER HURT YOU SO.

ALL I'M ASKING FOR IS JUST A FRIEND;
OF SOMEONE OF WHOM I CAN DEPEND.

I WOULD NEVER HURT YOU, 
I WANT YOU TO KNOW.
IT'S YOUR FRIENDSHIP I CHERISH SO,

WHEN I HEAR YOUR VOICE I CAN SAY,
"HERE IS A FRIEND THAT WON'T GO AWAY.

QUESTIONS I SEEM TO ASK IN PAIRS;
IT'S ONLY BECAUSE I REALLY CARE.
AND IF ANYONE SHOULD EVER KNOW
WE ARE MORE THAN FRIENDS, "you"
LET IT SHOW.

BUT IT WOULD MAKE ME PROUD FOR THEM TO SEE;
A GREAT GUY LIKE YOU IS A FRIEND TO ME.Silly poems of yesterday
