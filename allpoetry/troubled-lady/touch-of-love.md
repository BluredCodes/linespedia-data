title: "Touch of Love"

I SEE THE SEA MIST ON YOUR BODY, 
GLISTENING AND GOLD. 
YOU WALK SLOWLY TOWARD ME, 
YOUR TONGUE, WET AND WARM, LICKING YOUR LIPS.

YOU RUB YOUR LEGS NEXT TO MINE, 
YOUR GOLDEN HAIR, DAMP WITH THE WATER.
I HAVE ONLY TO CALL AND YOU ARE THERE,
READY TO DO ANYTHING I ASK.
SOMETIMES WHEN YOU GET EXCITED, 
I PONDER WHETHER YOU ARE GOING TO JOIN ME ON THE BED.

YOU KNOW WHEN WE ARE PLAYING AROUND, 
THERE WILL BE A TREAT FOR YOU.

ONE TOUCH, ONE DAMP APPLICATION OF YOUR TONGUE,
TELLS ME YOU ARE READY, AND I GET YOUR LEASH,
AND TAKE YOU FOR A WALK ON THE BEACH.

MY BEAUTIFUL COSMO.   FOR MY PEKINGNESE.Can you think of a more devoted love?  lol
