"Three Simple Words"

Last night you said three simple words.
Three simple words I haved longed to hear,
Three simple words I will always hold dear.

Three simple words so erocitally said;
made me come alive.
Made me me want to please you with a vengence
from deep inside.

These three simple words;
whether said as lover or as friend, 
I really don't care;
Just knowing that you said them and were willing
to share.

I love you, these three simple words so easily said;
but love you I do, as your lover or as your friend.
I want to love you, for as long as I can.

Thank you for saying those; "Three Simple Words".                                                  '
