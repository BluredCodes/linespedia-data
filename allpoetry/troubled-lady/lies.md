title: "LIES"
writer: "troubled lady"

YOU SAID YOU DIG YOU SWING WORLD;
THAT YOU COULD MAKE LOVE TO MOST ANY GIRL.
THAT YOU CCAN LIVE IN YOUR WORLD OF FLIRT;
NOT KNOWING OR CARING HOW MANY YOU HURT.
YOU LAUGH AND TRY TO PRETEND TO BE CARE FREE;
BUT DOWN DEEP INSIDE THERE IS A NEED TO BE,
CLOSE TO SOMEONE WHO REALLY CARES,
CLOSE TO SOMEONE SHARES,
NOT WANTING ANYONE TO KNOW AND ALWAYS PRETENDING
TO BE SO COLD..
