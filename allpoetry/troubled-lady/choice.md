CHOICES

MAYBE YOU THINK I'M EASY;
AND MAYBE THIS IS TRUE,
IT WAS EASY FOR ME TO FALL;
FOR A COUPLE OF GUYS LIKE YOU.

BOTH OF YOU ARE DIFFERENT;
YET, BOTH OF YOU THE SAME.
YOU BOTH MADE ME FEEL, WHEN MY HEART
WAS FULL OF PAIN.

IF I WERE ASKED TO MAKE A CHOICE;
THIS IS SOMETHING I COULD NOT DO;
I WOULD RATHER LOVE YOU BOTH AS FRIENDS
THAN TO LOSE EITHER ONE OF YOU.1-17 - 88, ALL OF MY POEMS SEEM TO BE A LITTLE SING SONGIE(:
HELP! ALL OF YOUR POEMS SEEM TO BE SO DEEP.  IS THIS AS DEEP AS I GO?
