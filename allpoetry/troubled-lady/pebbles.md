PEBBLES


SHE CAME INTO MY LIFE ALL GOLDEN AND SWEET;
CHEEKS SO ROSY, EYES BRIGHT AND CLEAR.
SHE DID NOT CARE WHERE I HAD BEEN; 
ONLY THAT I WAS THERE.

TRUST AND LOVE SHOWN DEEP IN HER EYES AND 
LAUGHTER FOR ALL TO SHARE.
BUBBLING WITH JOY; DANCING MERRILY,
SHE ONLY WANTED SOMEONE TO CARE.
SHE DID NOT ASK WHERE I HAD BEEN,
ONLY THAT I WAS THERE.

SHE IS MY DAUGHTER, MY SPECIAL CHILD, 
HER LAUGHTER HER AND LOVE WITH ME SHE SHARED.
SHE DID NOT ASK WHERE I HAD BEEN;
ONLY THAT I WAS THERE.

NOW THAT SHE IS GROWN THE HAIR TURNED GOLD;
HER SECRETS, HER LOVE, SADNESS, AND FEARS SHE SHARES.
SHE HAS NEVER ASKED WHERE I HAD BEEN
ONLY THAT I WAS THERE.



FOR MY DAUGHTER, MY LOVE, MY BEST FRIEMD.
THANK YOU FOR LOVING ME WHEN I WAS NOT ALWAYS THERETime lost
