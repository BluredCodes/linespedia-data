GOD'S HIGHWAY
 DEAR MOMMY AND DADDY,

 I JUST WANTED YOU TO KNOW,
 DON'T BE SAD, BECAUSE I HAD TO GO.
 GOD SAID HE NEEDED ME BAD,
 BUT HE DIDN'T MEAN TO MAKE YOU SAD.
 FOR GOD  NEEDED ME RIGHT AWAY.

 HE NEEDED ME TO HELP PAVE GOD'S HIGHWAY.
 I GET TO TO SPRINKLE THE STARS AND PLAY IN THE CLOUDS.  
 IF YOU LISTEN REAL CLOSE, YOU CAN HEAR ME LAUGH ALOUD.

 PLEASE TELL PAYTON AND JAKOBY I'LL SEE YOU ALL SOMEDAY.
 I WILL BE PLAYING ON GOD'S HIGHWAY.

 SO MOMMY AND DADDY, DON'T BE SAD.
 HE NEEDED ME HERE REALLY , REALLY BAD'
 HE NEEDED ME HERE TO SPRINKLE THE STARS.
 SO IF YOU GET SAD, JUST LOOK UPWARD AND YOU WILL SEE ME,
 TIMMY SPRINKLING THE STARS.
 IT'S MY JOB TO TURN ON THE LIGHTS,
 BUT I AM TIRED NOW,
 I LOVE YOU...GOODNIGHT!1999, timmothy, was a child of love, he was 26 week old set of twins. we think of timmy as a hero. maybe he went to god to give his brother a chance to live. timothy and jakoby weighed a mear 1 pound 13 ounces. yes without timmy there would be no jakoby.
