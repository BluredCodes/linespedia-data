title: "Love lost"
writer: "troubled lady"

I'VE LOVED YOU SINCE YOU ENTERED MY LIFE;
AND WE SHARED ONE ANOTHER TILL THAT TERRIBLE NIGHT.

I TRIED NOT TO CRY OR TO SHOW THAT I CARED, BUT
I FALL APART EACH TIME I SEE YOU THERE.

HOLDING HER CLOSE AND GIVING LOOKS OF LOVE,
AND YOU LOOK AT ME AND THEN GIVE A SHRUG;

SHE ASK YOU WHO THAT LADY MIGHT BE;
YOU NEVER TOLD HER YOU ONCE LOVED ME.

THAT ONCE YOU WANTED ME TO BE YOUR WIFE;
TO SHARE ALL THE THINGS THAT MAKE A GOOD LIFE.

BUT I TRY NOT TO CRY OR TO SHOW THAT I CARE;
BUT I FALL APART EACH TIME I SEE YOU THERE.1974, we all get over things eventually!!!:)
