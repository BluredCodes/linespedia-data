FRIENDS


I ASK ONLY TO BE YOUR FRIEND;
IT'S REALLY THE GOING THING.

SOMETIMES YOU ACT AS IF I ASK
FOR A GOLDEN WEDDING RING.

I WOULDN'T WANT A WEDDING RING; 
IT'S SOMETHING I'VE HAD BEFORE.

IT TENDS TO TURN A FRIENDSHIP;
SOMEWHAT INTO A BOAR.

I LIKE YOUR EASY BANTER;
IT'S EASY ON THE EARS.

YOU MAKE ME LAUGH SO HARD AT TIMES;
IT TURNS MY EYES TO TEARS.

SO JUST IN CASE WONDER;
I HAVE ONLY THIS TO SAY.

A FRIEND IS WHAT I'M GIVING;
A FRIEND IS WHAT I'LL STAY.AT THE TIME TRUST WAS THIN. FRIENDSHIP WAS ALL I COULD HANDLE.
FYI WE'VE BEEN MARRIED NOW FOR ELEVEN YEARS(").
