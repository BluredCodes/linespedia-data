---
title: "Feel The Breeze"
writer: "Mike Lane"
slug: "feel-the-breeze"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9124-Feel-The-Breeze-by-Mike-Lane"

An angels wings-
I've felt the breeze-

She lived right here, oh so close to me-

I'm proud to have loved her-
For I've heard her sing-

Her song of love-
Listen, it flows through the trees-

Just how could it be-
Someone so pure-
Was standing next to me?

She'll always be there-
Very close you see-

How do I know?
I can feel the breeze-
