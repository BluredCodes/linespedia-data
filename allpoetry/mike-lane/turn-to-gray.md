---
title: "Turn To Gray"
writer: "Mike Lane"
slug: "turn-to-gray"
source: "AllPoetry"

He's traveling the learning alley-
where the children ride their bikes but never fly their kites-
too much crap over head, too much gettin' in the way-

He has made this alley his home-
never left the day-

It's hard for him to feel the guilt when he's never felt the pain-
And he has come to see that his every Sunday is someone else's holiday-

So he just keeps traveling-
kites held tightly at his side-

Although he never really moves-
he is really very smooth-
Keeps his eyes glued too long on the horizon-
never ever thinks to look upward-
catch a good whiff of the moon-

So what has he learned today?
he learned to stand in line-

Thoughts that turn to gray-
