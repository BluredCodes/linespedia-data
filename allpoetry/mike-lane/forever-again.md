---
title: "Forever Again"
writer: "Mike Lane"
slug: "forever-again"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7729-Forever-Again-by-Mike-Lane"
---

She has come to visit-
yet she is the only one in the room-

Candles flicker-
the light is low-
memories illuminate with a soft warm glow-
sitting there on the mantel-
is really what she has come to see-
to hear, to touch, to feel-
she longs to hold it-
bring it closer to her breast-
two hearts beating-
two souls pleading- 
let them say what they will-

and the sights and sounds of the old fragile clock-
bring grandma back home for a couple of minutes again-
lost in thought, going back in time-
the light grows dim, it is finally time to go-
she'll be back to visit that room-
stand there all alone-
embrace the familial rhythms forever again-
once more-
