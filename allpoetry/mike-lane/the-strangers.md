---
title: "The Strangers"
writer: "Mike Lane"
slug: "the-strangers"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Talk to the strangers but don't get too close-
Keep it light, cute, on the surface-
And when they ask you who you really are-
Tell them all a lie-
Don't say too much-
Don't give it all away-
You know each other just enough-
You are sharing something new-
