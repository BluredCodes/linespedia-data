---
title: "A Day At The Ball"
writer: "Mike Lane"

God damn her raccoon hands and my drunken dances- 
It's a never ending battle with mother nature- 
It's the element of danger- 
A minimalist post card point-of-view- 
So we dance another day away- 
like the four legged fur bearing fools that we are-
