Front Street

By: Namdam

I went down to Front Street to get a dime
I found a man to give me his time
hit this weed so I could remember no more
down at Front Street is where I score
when my boss doesn't want to pay me anymore
I said "hey man Quick"
he said "I only have a nick"
the very next day
he did say "hey"
and that's when on Front Street
I finally had to pay
Have a nice day
