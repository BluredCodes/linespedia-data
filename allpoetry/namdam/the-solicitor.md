the solicitor

By: NamdaM

walking to the corner store wall
"would you like this spy pup or radio"
anything at all?
they wave there hands in anger
and close the door
"oh I see you wanted four"
"I will give you a deal if you buy more"
that really pisses them off
the sign says no soliciting
and I just say, 
"hey, I'm not Sicilian"
and sometimes they smile and 
sometimes they spit
but I dont want to be left hanging 
with all of this shit
so I keep trying to sell my goods
I know where are all the cool hoods
and I wont stop until I ring my bell
and get rid of all these items that I need to sell
