pass me that, Gee I'm fat


Thanksgiving (ate my way through that, its time for X-mas) is here!!!
What's with all the cheer?
Beer Beer Beer
Smear the mashed potatoes across my mouth
Packing everything deep down south
Gravy makes me lazy
but I can't say no because I'm going crazy
with food everywhere
My woman is starting to stare
I have turkey hanging off my chin
and I'm dying to dig in 
the pies are sitting along the side
and my stomach is getting wide 
but fuck it I don't care
Roll me around while the little kids stare
Thanksgiving Rules! (X-mas!)
Have a nice day
