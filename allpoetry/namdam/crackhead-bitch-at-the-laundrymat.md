crackhead bitch at the laundrymat

By: NamdaM

walking down the street one day
he saw her there "ugh" 
but the solicitor still did say "hey"
"would you like to buy a phone today"
bitch looked scared and wanted to run away
so he pulled out his radio 
and turned it on
but it sure wasnt long
before this crackwhore did a flip
she straight had the nerve to give my leg a kick
"this crackbitch must really be sick"
so there we were in the laundrymat
"fuck me good and call me Pat"
he couldnt believe she was on that much crack
so before he flipped 
he left and never turned back
