this is a story of what happened to DJ Raving Shoes


DJ Raving Shoes must of always had the blues 
from too much Ecstasy
always running around all gay and pretty
one day I had to take a crap
the security guard said don't look back
"fuck that"
behind Raving Shoes' turntables there I sat
I was having a hard time so I arched my back
but Raving Shoes always played it slow
that gay ass bitch didn't even know
so in the trance room Raving Shoes put on a show
for all the glowstick kids and that ugly ho
he took his last 12 pills of roll and he spun a set
and this set sucked so bad I bet he didn't regret
that he died right there but didn't mess up his hair
not only did he fall in my crap but no one did care
Have a nice day
