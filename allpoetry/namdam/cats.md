Cats

By: Namdam

Cats Scratch and are sometimes sweet
I like when my Cat begs for a treat
He catches mice and sleeps all day
But when I want to sleep 
Thats when He wants to play
My Cats Bitchen when he sits in the kitchen
Getting high off Weed and Then looking at me pissed
Until him I feed
I hate when he licks his ass then passes gas
Fucking Cat stinks again
He still thinks I wanna be his friend
