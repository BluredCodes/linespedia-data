Why do you want to stare at me 

like you have something to say? 

Do you want to die tonight or 

are you just that fucking gay? 

but I really can't tell if your drooling 

at my lady applying lipstick 

or the bulge that is my Dick 

so just turn your head away 

or you will end up my prey 

Have a nice day
