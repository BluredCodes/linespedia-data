''did you shit your pants?''



I stepped in shit the other night
let me tell you that shit wasn't right
it smelled like death was all around
and it was hard to breathe not making a sound
my woman asked if I shit my pants
and I just yelled "not a chance"
but still something smelled like crap
and this man ain't playing that
I looked at my shoe and there it sat
smushy and gushy probably from a cat
my cats ass smells like that 
so I wiped it off on my neighbors mat
Have a nice day
