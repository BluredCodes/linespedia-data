security guard your territory 
when you follow around
just like a lost hound
your tight pants are squeaking
your breath like shit your reeking
just need to sell my stuff and
you want to play rough
get out of my face with
your big can of mace
I'll fuck your life then
bitch slap your wife
Have a nice day
