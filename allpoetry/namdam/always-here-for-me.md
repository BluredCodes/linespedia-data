always here for me



Did you go?
Where did I go?
You look ready for the show
I have nothing more to show
You are strong from the streets
I am freezing walking these streets
Please lets not fight tonight
I will come home early tonight
You look so sad in the morning
I have to leave you in the morning
You will always be here waiting
I hope I won't keep you waiting
too long
Have a nice day
