title: "got any Red Bull"

Morning meeting drags me down 
listening to that old greedy clown 
preach about the same old shit 
I would do anything to get out of it 
the grins around me are starting to amuse 
I have a receipt in my pocket for a sketch pad I use 
I draw them with ink and they start to fit 
each one accurate especially emphasizing that zit 
but I'm worn out already and they day just begun 
to the corner gas liquor store I need to run 
Red Bull Red Bull gimme need some now 
what a kick that drink has like fucking Pow!! 


Have a nice day
