fucking totaled

By: NamdaM

another fucking accident today
my license almost got taken away
rear ended by your Toyota truck
Seems like I have ran out of luck
This Insurance just ran out
I should of taken a different route
Don't make me blow up your car
Don't push me way too far
just pay my bill and I will let you live
about another week to you I'll give
have a nice day
