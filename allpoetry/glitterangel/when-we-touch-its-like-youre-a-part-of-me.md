When we touch it's like you're a part of me 
The emotions I wish I could verbalize 
The fears you've gently taken from my swollen heart 
The wants and needs 
You all too willingly fuffill 

And every morning I remind myself 
How wonderful it feels 
To hold you in my arms and never let you go 
Because every morning you give me a new day 
A new chance to make things right 
Even if they never seemed wrong 

I do believe 
That missing thing I saw in your eyes last February 
Was a refelection of my own longing 
To be with someone more like myself 
Someone 
More like you.
