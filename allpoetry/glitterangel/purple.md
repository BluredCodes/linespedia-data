Her grape flavored nailpolish 
Mocks my ability to see the drama 
She creates 
Not only in the classroom 
But in my mind 
Her obsessive ways never impressed anyone worth while 
And the way her purple eyeliner looks 
In the light 
Always has annoyed me 
But then again, 
My opinion never mattered much 
To someone of her staus.
