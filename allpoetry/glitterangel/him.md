Loving kisses embeded in letters 
All scattered in your car 
Every word makes it harder to go home tonight 
When I could be staring at your beautiful eyes 
Closed in peace laying in my arms 
I don't need words 
Your touch says more than words ever could 
I want to be the precious dream in your cruel reality 
Gently wiping your mind of all destructive emotions 
You take nothing less than all of me.
