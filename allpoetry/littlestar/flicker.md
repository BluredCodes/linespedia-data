There is a bright star
in a darkened sky.
It is you.

There is a white light
in my black room.
You are here.

And I only hope you stay.

Every door in my mind
opens to you.
Every wall in my heart 
has fallen for you.

You are my cathedral.
I no longer feel cold.
Only content,
only tranquil.

There is no fear with you.
I love you because I want to.
And all I can hope for
is that you’ll never
disappear.1991,
