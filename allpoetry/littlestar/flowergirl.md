title: "Flowergirl"
writer: "littlestar"

LOOK DEEP INSIDE
AND SEE THE FACES.
THE FEAR AND FROZEN 
HORROR OF MOUTHS
IN A WAR SNAPSHOT.
THE BLACK INKY
SHADOWED FACE 
OF SOMEONE TRAPPED
CRYING FOR HELP.

THIS DESTINY IS CHOSEN FOR YOU.
YOU CANNOT CLAIM A PRIZE.
YOU CANNOT EVEN CHOOSE
WHEN TO DIE.
BUT WHEN YOU DO
THE SOIL IS LIFTING
YOUR LIFE UP,
USING YOU,
TO CREATE THINGS YOU
MAY NEVER HAVE LOVED.

LOOK INSIDE THE PETALS
OF THAT ONE.
THE ONE LOWEST TO THE DIRT,
CLOSE TO THE ROAD.
THAT'S WHERE MY FACE IS.
SEE HOW SERENE AND BLANK
MY FEATURES ARE.
SEE HOW WELL I CAN ACCEPT THINGS.

THIS FUTURE I'VE NOT CHOSEN.
PERPETUATED BY UNKNOWN 
BOUNDLESS RULES.
THIS THING I'VE JUST DISCOVERED.
BY LOOKING INSIDE THIS ORCHID
AND SEEING
PUNCH-STAINED FACES
THAT I KNOW RESEMBLE 
YOU, AND YOU,
AND YES,
EVEN ME.1993,
