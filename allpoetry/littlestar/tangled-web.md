title: "Tangled Web"

Trap me in your web.

Wrap thin gauze
until I suffocate.

I start to hate.

Catch me in your lies

and pluck the legs
off of me

so I've nowhere to run.

Take all my friends.

So I'm alone.
Flesh and bone.
Hardened stone.

Trap me in your web.

Suck the blood
from my lifeless limbs.

And leave me rotting
in this dark encasement.

Leave me rotting
like a dead small
spider.

Wrapped inside her

mother's love.

Push and shove.

But never quite escape.-1991,
