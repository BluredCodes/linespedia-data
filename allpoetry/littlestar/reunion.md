---
title: "Reunion"
writer: "littlestar"
slug: "reunion"
source: "AllPoetry"

It won't be long
before I'm gone
away from you.
A tiny dot
on the timeline
of your life.
Until I will fade
like a bright happy day
in your mind-
and so many details are lost...

Until our reunion
I will lie
inside you dormant.
Until the time
when you bestow
warmth upon me
once again.

And then as if the sun had come-
I will rush out.
-No more cold-
I will run to you
with all my strength
to reach you again.
To live beyond this cave.
To love you-
with no more reunions.
But until then,
I will lie
quiet and hope
that time will fly.1991,
