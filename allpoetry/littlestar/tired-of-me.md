---
title: "Tired of Me"
writer: "littlestar"
slug: "tired-of-me"

listless
bored
spot me
watch me
roll away
YAWN
step on me
hear my bones
CRUNCH
like a new fall leaf
beneath your boots
leave me
to age and rust
see me
but leave me
a worthless penny
walk by
i don't catch your eye
losing interest
going...
gone
GOODBYE1992,
