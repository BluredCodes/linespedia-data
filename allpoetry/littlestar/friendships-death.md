I NEVER THOUGHT
I'D SEE THE DAY
OUR FRIENDSHIP
WOULD DIE.

BUT THE SCENT OF PERFUME
ON A DELICATE WRIST
FADES AWAY
AND SO DID YOU.

THE FLOWER DIED.
THE PETALS FELL
LIKE ASHES
OF YOUR STRENGTH AND WILL.

THE SUN GOES DOWN
IN DEFEAT.
AND YOU,
YOU HOLD ITS HAND.1987,
