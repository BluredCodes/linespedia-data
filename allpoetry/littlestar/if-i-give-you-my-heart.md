title: "If I give you my heart..."

If I give you my heart
will you know 
what to do with it?

A priceless jewel
is wasted when 
buried deep- undiscovered.

First, you must find it.

If you are worthy,
it will uncover itself to you.
Dive below this sparkling surface,
deep into the ocean
that is me. 

If you persist;- 
(are you patient?)
if you dare-
(are you courageous?)
You will find
beneath the surface,
beyond the dangers that lurk-
a priceless infinity,
a forever dream. 

If  only you knew
the worth-(do you?)
You would abhor the vendors
with their  plastic trinkets
which never last,
but come so cheap and easily.

Will you capture it,
only to squander it,
neglect it
use it for your pleasure
and then
cast it aside?

Or will you know the worth-
of the greatest gift.

If only you could look
with new eyes.

If only you knew 
what is waiting for you.
You would never turn aside,
You would never avert your gaze.

Take off the blindfold
of fear and restless wandering.
Look with the eyes that  never close-
The eyes of your heart.

If only you were wise,
(am I fool to believe or to doubt?)
then you would never 
give up seeking,
and never stop discovering
the endless rewards.
