---
title: "PURE"
writer: "littlestar"

White.
Undefiled.
Fresh milk 
from a mother's 
soft petal breast.
Warm and succulent.
Blessed security
The feathers of
a perfect swan-
swimming in a glistening pond,
and every sparkle
in the pool
reflects your eyes.
And I believe 
you've cleansed
all the impurities
to unadulterated bliss.

A shining round pearl.
Strong.
Perfect balance.
Created from degradation.
Special to me.
Held in highest esteem.
Like a gem.
Like a crown on my head.
Worn for remembrance.
I believe you are an angel-
white-winged and brilliant.
Rearranging all the clouds
that block my joy.
And the pure warm
sun
streams through.1991,
