It's strange
to peer
into a pool of water
and see no reflection 
of yourself.
There's an absurd unrealness
when you don't exist,
when you come from
someone who is not yours.

And I never belong and
don't care about fitting.
If I pulled out all the pain
and misery you've given me
I'd have a heart full of nothing.

You can never ask back
what you never wanted.
Change. Change. Change-
so I can sleep. Oh
I don't want this bed
in your house anymore.
I want to be real...
So give ME back.1990,
