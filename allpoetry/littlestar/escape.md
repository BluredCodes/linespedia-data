Little mouse
running through a maze
and everytime
it hopes for freedom
its zapped
for going the wrong way
and soon all that’s felt
is fear.

 Don’t move.
     Don’t sense.   
Don’t hope.

For diappointment follows.
Sit in a corner and wallow
and become hard and cold
and hollow
like the prison bars
that hold you in.1992,
