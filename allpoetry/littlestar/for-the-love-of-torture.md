---
title: "For the Love of Torture"
writer: "littlestar"

Blind me with hot pokers.
Leave scarred tissue
where my eyes were.
Bind me up and hang me
like a vampire from a tree.
Don't leave me hoping in you.
Don't feed the gardens green.
Just kill me off painfully.
Don't dull my senses more.
Your morbid love of loneliness
fills me with ill desire.
I do not long for your embrace.
Burn me with blueflamed fire.
And then kiss me when I'm gone.
Feel what I can't feel.
Torture me. Oh torture me.
You always did so well.1992,
