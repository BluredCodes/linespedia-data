Label me
like shelf
like a box
full of miscellaneous
objects
& papers with statistics.
Make a notation
to teach me 
how to be whole.
And don't forget,
yes, write a note,
to love me.
Tape it on the door.
And please
engrave my name
on the tomb
so I won't be
confused 
with another 
disappointment.-1989,
