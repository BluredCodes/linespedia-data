---
title: "*As One"
writer: "unclassified"
slug: "as-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

Black and white transparencies
distributing colour
suffocating sound
two piles of chaos
stand staring back at me
drastic yellow shines a saddened smile

Take the photos while their young
fresh and undisturbed
their smiles wont be forgotten 
as you clutch the frame
they reach for the bottle
years later

You laid batteries
on children's toys
plugged into them your weakness
and their source of life
polluted harmony
Destroyed creativity

Now they return to me
recharging, begging for it
creator of spirit
sustainer of life
provider of eternal love
need not search high and low
above nor beyond
I am here in your white space
holding your heart with your own blood
trapped we are here together

Look at the sailing moon
the diving mountains
the succulent fruits that dance on a tree
we are one and the same
lips touch your soul
and dance through eternity
as One.
