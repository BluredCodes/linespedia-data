Give to me 

again those glasses

so i the darkened lens 

keep apart my the reality

my own laughter just 

own my jokes inside 

not by own eyes, the sorrow

not the touch of pain, my own hands

lies more lies and deflated

it goes make away

bird let feed on the wire

a hunter, the hunted

decay soon found

dripping ground

fostered and feared

against the stem of flesh

i am

just

a power pee

on a cold burner

turn on it

let me away
