In Crimson I Stand
Devoted to love
Dreaming up storms
I've never before heard of
Laughing at solemness
Pretending it's ok

I've buried in mole holes
Dug deep in the earth
Stretched hours into seconds
Shattered love into birth
Staring at solemness
Pretending it's ok

Light before me is dark
Paths show me astray
I must feel it
I must taste it
Licking lips along the way
Sharing in solemness
Pretending it's ok

Dark only comes to me
You people are affecting me
Stand naked and shiver in hilarity
Don't laugh I want you to stand with me
Buried in solemness
Pretending it's ok
