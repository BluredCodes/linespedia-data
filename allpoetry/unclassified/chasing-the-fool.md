---
title: "*Chasing the Fool"
writer: "unclassified"

Well I have these eyes 
that choose not to believe
only touch in every way 
you may never see

So open your wounds, let them bleed
As long we are here there is nothing I can't understand
Perfection of a chase 
never quite the fallen dream

Change where I stand
There hasn't been one
So why do you insist to believe

That my eyes are for opening
My love will soon be fading
Feet turn against being here
Where else could I ever be

So cold inside it feels like rain
Pouring down upon a fire
Stirring what will grow higher
Believe as you may about the truth
Perhaps only I know how it lay
No choice inside for me to go another way

Who is to say what I am
When all that you know
Is the shallow of what is within
Nothing real beyond few controls
Each day to poison the hands to hold
What once all you could worship
That is the source of your fears
About laying down silent with me
Join me
