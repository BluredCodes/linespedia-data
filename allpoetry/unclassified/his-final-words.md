Poor old boy trapped indoors
why must we torture him so?
We could let him run free
but he'd chase all the fair ladies down the street

He drips when he drinks
and licks his lips dry
he craves the girl next door
but cant reach the window to sneak a peak
not even at midnight
but he knows she waits for him and only him

The front door flings open
he runs out for a breath of fresh air
he didnt see it coming but
SMACK! 
thrown to the ground he stays limp
the driveway cold
the tires hot and numbing

she saw it all through the window 
the Bitch comes running
"Poor Cabana boy", she says
"twas only yesterday I fucked him"

Sampson wags his tail in the distance
"Dumb fool, I could have warned you"This is for you Autotomy.  Enjoy it, but dont rate it cuz I know how horrible it really is!
