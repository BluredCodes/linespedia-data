title: "*Where Does Time Fly"

Passing flame to a torch we inhibit this nature
Obsessed with motion and fusion and delusion of sorts
A time of need and lust doesn't Dehumanize us
but illuminates us
We confuse it and it loses us, 
belittled not
We delude it and improve it

Alas the time is gone 
Where did it go?
Are they watching us?

Inspecting my secrets
Depriving laughter when called 
Wash ye hands of my soil
Tool in the yarn of my love
But never revealed they are

In the seas of the Blue Mother
Encoded in each twist of harmonious fate
Dancing on the pedals of wax
Singing to the skies 
In the eyes that peer over us
Indulging us
Forgiving us when forgiveness is lost

Watch my leaves change
From blue/green to red
In the Isles of Capricorn
Creation does await us
Standing still in perfect timeIn sand dunes hidden on the beach..
