Since Been Long

the times that we wept

the times we shared

to the parallels of the sacred.



So now lay before you

feel the sins within you

shared is the melody of our soul



Forget all that lingers

brave new world unfolded 

escape the sound, misery

	vast creations

	cruel celebrations
