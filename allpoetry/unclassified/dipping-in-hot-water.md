title: "*Dipping in Hot Water"

Something of nothing I wanted to become
Placed behind a glass door to watch
Seeds of my dreams surround me

An echo of silence stirs in darkness
Where evil laughter awaits a mirror 
Telling truths of a thousand lies

Caress these dark cheeks?
I dare you, I dare myself
Crowd my vision of love with your hate

Subdued the fly grazes the sidewalk
Pinch the blood of blindness
To find your weak devourment of life

I savour the pungent smell of your wrath
Bind me to the deepest sheet of your cover
Ill find you finding me there, unwelcomed

Throw a knife against the glass
Watch it shatter a million times slower
Maybe then I'll understand continuity
Maybe then I won't
