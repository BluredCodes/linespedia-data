title: "*Let it go"

Why the lamp shade shines so dim
and who dare speak of the anchored gin
of which hate spoke out of

let it go

capital ego speaks too loudly
deafening silence
do you even know who you are?
hiding behind the shade of ignorance

let it escape you
let it go

rustling beats of heartache attached
pressure from a source unknown
pen shakes
skin rattles
ears, eyes, mouth uncontrolled

let it never be known
let it go

candles burning one by one
deep shades of red, blue
even orange
white is pure, let it find you
and surround you

behold you are brave
not a new soul
but an old one re hatched
as an egg but not quite
more a goddess unburied
or god unleashed

let it possess you
let it go
