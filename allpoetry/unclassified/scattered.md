Thoughts inside, unaware.

Emotions hiding, cannot bare.



Crying out in sudden despair,

us lying alone, engulfed with a stare.



Animated love is all around.

One's true soul is rarely found.



Happy, sad…nobody knows.

The outer body is all that shows.



What do you see when you close your eyes?

The sound of fire, the heat of despise?



No one can read what's inside.

It's tucked away, not afraid to hide.



Make yourself think what you want to hear.

All is good with no trace of fear.



The truth you are scared to see.

You don't like your reality.



To follow your path, and let go of my own

Will you let me get lost, will I ever find home?



Speak to my spirit, dance with my soul

transforming my identity into your mold.



Slowly breathing, awaiting the fall

desperately seeking the truth in it all.
