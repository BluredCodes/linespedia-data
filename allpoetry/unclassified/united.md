Vhim:
I've been told I will live
And with along so many more
Rise as I always will do
Oh run but don't hide
For I will come unto you
How dare you make me wait
Yet another day
Swear it is as always to be true
There will be a way

How my life can return
Without pride but only hate
Who could have begun with a dream
End at this touch to make me feel all
That for so long I have only denied
What haunts me the most
As my flesh and soul start to become
Feeling almost at once completely alive
Is that you are dreaming of but only this
One more time I've got to take you higher

Vher:
My body shakes
So when you kiss me
Cannot remember feeling this way
Not on my own all as I gave my life to alone
But deny it to none that now know won’t hide
My body quakes in the confines of your kiss
Leaving me to have this alone to have said

(sing/dance)         Limits raised
                     Like candles on ice
                     Flowering soldiers
                     On green meadow grasses.
                     Come forth into me
                     My heart is forever open
                     Awaken us both in confines of
                     Barbaric animalism 


Vhim:
Each of our own
Places needing a home
Forgive the trespass
Without choice to be made
Must carry on forth
It's only a fire
So perfect as whole
But complete as our soul

Vher:
Lye on the bed
Make art out of me
Fantastic colours of your fire
Burning with my earthly flesh
My womb awaits you 
Eyes that cannot bare to look without you
Hands that swell just to touch you
My soul encased in a tomb.

How then I'd want to have your hands draw over me 
Barely touching me 
Swiftly attacking every part of this body 
To which is now yours to feed
Taste my moan
Move to the rhythm of our blood
