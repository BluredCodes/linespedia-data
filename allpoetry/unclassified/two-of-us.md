I was feeling just great

life a clear picture
coming into focus

life forms created
flying on a natural high

a phone call then shattered bliss
sadness in her voice 
deeply pulled was I to where she lay

sad with tears
she is hurting
two people, now three
suffering

she is above
she is beyond
soaring above the clouds
watching us, the lonely
drifting
unknowing any destination
battling false fear
playing innocent as children do

your daughter needs healing, too
now is not the time
you need the comfort today

only because you think you are there 
are you there

go beyond
reach higher
know the soul deep within you 
and fly

the most magic of woman
the most tender of girls

mysteries of the unknown, but known
we can watch each other fly
