Alone at last.  
Just memories and I
we're sure to have a blast.

I went by the place where you and I wrote our names in wet cement
and for a moment remembered how it felt
to have no one understand that there's this dream
how soon do those memories fade

childhood is so fucked up.
I never had any close friends
than the ones I had when I was young.
alone again just you and I 
While I and Memories wave goodbye.
