Why did I lose my old self?
Become the person I am now?
Lose all my old friends the way I did
I still don't understand
Was it supposed to happen this way?

Now That we've grown apart
I look back and cry
What ever happened to it?
We used to be so happy
Was that the way it was supposed to be?
Looking back, I know we might have seemed cliquey
But did it matter?
No, not at all
We were friends
Us against the world

Now we won't as much look at each other
Where did it go?
Now all I can do is look back and say:
Why do things always happen this way?I miss my old friends =(. Oh well..
