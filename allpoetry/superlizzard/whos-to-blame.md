When I look in the mirror 
I can't believe what I see 
Tell me, Tell me who's that freaky chick starin' back at me??!

I know I shouldn't worry
Shouldn't fret
I've got lots of friends
Or at least that's what I suspect

Broken hearted, Worn down
Alone in the cold

Excuse my bitching
-- I shouldn't complain
I shouldn't feel any more, cause' feeling is pain
Everyone I love is denied me
Who's to blame?
Me

--fuck this crap
I've had it!
I don't give a damn if you hate me
I never liked you either
You hear me??!Rivers Cuomo (from Weezer) is a genius when ever I listen to their stuff I feel like writing something..
