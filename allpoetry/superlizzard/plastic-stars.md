Plastic Stars upon my wall
One by one they fall, and fall.
They sit there, seeing everything.

If anyone knew why they are there I would surely die.

Plastic Stars upon my wall
One by one they fall, and fall.
They sit there, their world a blur, then a slowing pace.

Some in patterns, some in lines. Oh why, oh why do I take the time?

Plastic Stars upon my wall
One by one they fall and fall.
They sit there, unthinking, unknowing, unfeeling.

But then: they glow, they shine. Now I know why I take the time.

Plastic Stars upon my wall
One by one they fall and fall.
