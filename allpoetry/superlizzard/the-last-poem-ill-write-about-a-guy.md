Why do I never seem to learn?
That it just isn't worth it anymore..
Never seem to realize
that guys are all the same

Never seem to realize that love 
is pointless and shallow?
I guess I'll never fully realize 
what it's all about

I realized too late
that you might have been "the one"
now I sit here:
Alone
Broken hearted
Haunted by your voice

I'm still scared of talking to you
I've loved you for so many years
but now I'm wondering:
-- Have you become one of my greatest fears?Cute, ne? Hehehee.. I highly doubt this will be the last poem I write about a guy.. uggh, I need something else to write about *ponders*
