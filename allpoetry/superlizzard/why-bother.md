I know I should get next to you 
you got a look that makes me think you're cool 

I've know a bunch of guys before
-- Hey, what's the problem of knowing one more?
Mabey we could get together,
Then you could break my heart over the summer?

Why Bother?
It's gonna hurt me
and it's gonna kill me when you desert me

But hey, 
that's always fun, 
I could get depressed again!
How much fun would that be?
Aww, come on, it'll be fun!
Here's a gun,
and there's the trigger,
you know what to do with it..*sigh* it's dark but hey.. remember kiddies, don't listen to Pinkerton (Weezer) when you're sad..
