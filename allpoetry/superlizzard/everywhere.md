I can't avoid you, you're in the air, you're everywhere.
I see you every day. 
You always seem so sad, so lost, so alone. 
If only you knew. 
You look at me as if I've done something wrong, as if you are going to cry. 
You just seem to sit there, alone, as the world spins by.
You seem sad all the time, I wish I could make you smile, I wish I could be your happiness. 
But, alas- I am torn, confused, afraid.

Seeing you makes me happy, unexplainably so. 
It's just the question of "Should I hold on, or let go?"This is my first poem (here, not very first), so I'm open to any comments or criticism. I hope you enjoyed it..
