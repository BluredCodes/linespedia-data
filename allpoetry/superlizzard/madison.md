Did I ever tell you that I really loved you
and I think about you all day?
I really miss you and wish I could kiss you
But why did you go away?

Since you've been gone 
I've thought over and over about you
and I can't get you out of my head.

I guess I could call you and ask you, "How are you?"
But I really don't have much to say
So I sit all alone and I stare at the phone
hoping that you'll call me some day

Since you've been gone 
I've thought over and over about you
and I can't get you out of my head.

I wish I had never met you
never know you
never loved you
