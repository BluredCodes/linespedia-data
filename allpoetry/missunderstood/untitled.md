i wish you could see the way people look at you
the way they talk about u when you're not there
it makes me sick
but i'm affraid
i try to be a very moral person
and step in and stop them all
but i can't
its too hard
you don't understand
your eyes are still closed
open them
look out at the world
see the people
see the hate
see what i see when you're not around
see the hurt i feel for u 
they won't stop
they haven't stopped
they will never stop
just stop
