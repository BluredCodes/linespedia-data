the pink frog
and the purple duck
liked to fuck
they were quite different from myself and gappy
you see, we don't fuck
nor will we ever fuck
it just won't happen
now, the pink frog liked the purple duck
and they had a 'special' tree you see
and they'd go there and they'd fuck
there also was a blade of grass
the pink frog liked it up his ass
he likes it so much,
he called it ASS FUCKIN!
and thats how ass fuckin came to be.you see, you won't understand this "story" because neither me, nor gappy really understand it. it's our story. we made it up one day when kayla didn't feel like goin to class and she somehow came across me. so i hope you enjoy our pile of jibberish.
