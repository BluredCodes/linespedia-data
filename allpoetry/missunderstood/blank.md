upset. but feeling fine
flowers all around
the air is cool
but the breeze is warm
feelings flow through me
like a wild storm
i can't see straight
i'm like a cloud
everything around me
looks like a blob
a blob of useless beautiful colour
different shades of beautiful colour
like music playing in my head
sounds like
notes of beauty
everything is wonderful
but i'm still upset
