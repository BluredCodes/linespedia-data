you opened my eyes
you made me see
now i know i love u
its so clear to me
you hold me when i'm happy
you hold me when i cry
you hold me when i'm mad
you don't even have to try 
you made me see a light
i wouldn't have otherwise seen
i can't believe you're like this now
you used to be so mean
there is never a second in my day
when you're not on my mind
i can't believe someone like you
was so easy to find
