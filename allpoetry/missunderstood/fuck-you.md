---
title: "Fuck you"
writer: "MiSSundErStOOd"
slug: "fuck-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41151--you-by-MiSSundErStOOd"
---

all i can do is drown you out
i hate the way you treat me
i hate when u look at me as if i'm nothing
is that what i am to u? NOTHING?
take your idea of a perfect fucking life  
and a perfect daughter 
and throw it away
THOSE ARE ONLY DREAMS THAT 
WILL NEVER BE FULFILLED!!
