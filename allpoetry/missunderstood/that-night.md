when i told u i needed u
you didn't know what to say
when i told u i loved you 
you looked the other way

when i asked u how u felt
u said u didn't know what the future had in store
u told me you didn't love me 
i felt like a cheap whore
 
all those intimate moments
all those memories that will last forever
when u said those words to me 
i felt my heart being shattered

and then u saw the tears
rolling down my cheeks
you told me not to cry
i felt as if i could cry for weeks

you told me to be happy
i felt my knee's growing weak
i didn't know what to do
i didn't even speak

but then u said you'd love me one day
and i wouldn't have to worry
but then again, maybe it was a dream
it's all so distant and blurry
