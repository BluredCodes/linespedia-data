tell me what you're thinking
tell me what you feel
tell me what you want
tell me what you need
tell me if its ever gonna work
tell me if i'm putting all this effort in for nothing
tell me if i'm ever going to get anything out of this
tell me whats real
tell me the future in your eyes
do you want a future?
so many questions
so little answers
tell me the truth
