sorry
sorry doesn't cut it
sorry doesn't take back the things you said
"YOU'RE WORTHLESS", "YOU'RE A FUCK UP"
no thanks
sorry won't work here
sorry can't take back words
words are inbedded in my head forever
sorry isn't
sorry's just a pitiful word people use to make themselves feel better
fuck the sorries
fuck you
you're the one that screws me up
you're the reason i'm like this
and u think sorry's gonna heal me?
no 
nothing can heal the scars you've placed on my soul
nothing can take back the words you've said
nothing can take back the tears i've cried night after night
so what do u have to say for yourself?
sorry?
go tell somebody who gives a fuck cuz sorry means nothing to me
