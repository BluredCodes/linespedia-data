everythings fucked up
nothing is right
will it ever get better
will i make it through the night?
these are the questions that race through my mind
these are the questions with the answers so hard to find
if i ever get through this, do you promise that you'll be there?
ready to love me and ready to care?
because if your not ready then tell me now
i'll find my way
maybe...somehow
