---
title: "Two against one"
writer: "MiSSundErStOOd"
slug: "two-against-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

do you see the hurt you make me feel
do you see that pain you inflict inside of me
all of this could have been avoided
but you couldn't be yourself
instead it was two against one
all this inside of me
never will it be shown
i show no pain, no suffering, no emotion
i only show coldness towards the people that have done this
and thats all they will ever see
ever
