one look and my breath is gone
you're eyes seem so stare into my soul 
without any warning
you give me a feeling of safety
like you'll be here forever....
will u be here forever?
all i want is you
in my eyes your perfect
you were made for me
i can't express the love i feel for you with words nor actions
i want you to understand everything that i feel
i want you to feel more
more then what u say
i want it to be unconditional
forever
