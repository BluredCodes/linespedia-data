give me the courage to do it
i know the pain heals
i know the pain is pleasure 
i know it makes me feel in controll
i just want this all to go away
i want to feel free
    help me be free
take away all my  memories,
both good and bad
i won't be needing them n e more
i'll be gone
forever
maybe then you'll realize all the hurt i feel
all the pain thats in me
waiting to be released
all i need is one good cut
i'll be free
free
i need to get away
not just for a little while
forever
forever
i need to feel the wind
i need to be able to fly
forever
be free
let my soul free
let it run wild in the grass
let my eyes weep no more tears
forever
i've always pictured what it would be like
to be free
not worrying
no pain
no sorrow
no tears
forever
just take the blade
just one good cut
thats all i'll need
just one
just let me be free
free
forever
good bye
