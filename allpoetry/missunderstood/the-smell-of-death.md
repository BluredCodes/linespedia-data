confusion wraps its arms around me
its cold hands make it feel like a knife peircing my skin
taking me deeper inside itself
there's no way out
only darkness shows
i feel as if time is distant
and in distance there is no time
no way to tell
which way i'm going
its taking me far
there is no life
everything around me is
gone, dissapeared
will it return 
will there be light
it will not reveal itself
remains there
go away
