When given a hill…I shall “CLIMB IT !”bullshit I’d much rather stay here and sit!If at first you don’t succeed…”TRY TRY AGAIN “!Yeah right!… I could save myself a lot of painMy parents say…”Make us proud !”My fucking parents make me want toScream so damn loud!I know they mean well…. But they are putting me thru’ a living hell.			All I want is to be left alone…To dwell in my own freaking homeI think in life there are two kinds of peopleThe f!@#ers’ and the F@!kedI ‘d be a F!@#er with any luck.
