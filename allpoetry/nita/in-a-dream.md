In A Dream

Sometimes in a dream
I’m trapped- I run
I scream- all this
Sometimes in my dreams
There  is a cry for help
My cries break thru’ the darkness
As I awake and try to recall how I felt
My dream is done now
But it still feels so real
I am awake in a bright reality
My dreams are rested
Until tonight when again
With my soul they will fight
Sometimes in my dreams
My soul tells me things
Which it really means
All , this sometimes in my dreams……….
