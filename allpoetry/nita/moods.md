“MOODS”Today I’ve decided to be gladHowever someone messed aroundAnd got me extremely mad!Then someone else came andTold a story which made me very sadTomorrow I’ve decided not to be anythingBecause life is unpredictableIt has its UP’s and downsWe know that what goes around comes aroundAnd that which goes up must come downSo I say to you don’t waste your time being unhappy and blueWondering what in the world will become of youDon’t do anything!Just be yourselfLove who you are…not anyone else.
