Obey!!!

Sitting up in a tree…
Looking around to see
If there was anything to see ?

A woman passed saying…
“Child get down”
Look at her laughing like
A circus Clown

The mailman was leaving…
The woman’s’ perfume
Had him sneezing

Climbing yet higher…
For some reason wanting
To touch the electric wire

Again the woman said… 
“ Child get down!”
Again laughing at her like a Circus Clown
The limb broke
DAMN! This was no joke
Falling to the ground
Hearing not a sound
Except…
“Child get down”…
This time there was no laughing
Like a Circus Clown…
