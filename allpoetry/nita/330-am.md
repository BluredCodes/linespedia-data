title: "3:30 am"
writer: "Nita"

There was the body---breathing ---
yet still some what life less
Eyes open- but blank
There are no thoughts
A heart beats strong and steady
For another long day in the world
The Body is set and ready

At this time there is only emotion FEAR!
Uncertainty…DREAD
Everything spins
In the head

But nothing is registered
Nothing is said

Not wanting to move
The body stays silent
Quietly wishing it could always be night
But  it creeps in…
Slowly… The light

The Body senses this
It had not gotten its wish
It closes its eyes...not wanting to awake
To have to relive all the lies
All the hate…

Now it’s just a body
The spirit, the soul
It all dies…no one ever answered the body’s cries

So here’s the body
To the world it says…
I’m sorry ,sorry, sorry…then fades
Leaving this twisted maze.
