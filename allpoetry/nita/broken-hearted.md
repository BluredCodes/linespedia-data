‘Broken Hearted’Broken hearted she weeps alone,This is much more than her heart can condone.Once again she has loved and loss,She is beginning to think that love is a lost cause.She dries her tears and looks awayKnowing she’all survive another sad and lonely day.But…deep in her soul there are emotions she can’t control.There is a pain in her heart,A pain that’s ripping her apart. .innocent as the fresh fallen snowShe pushes on, knowing she has to let go.Love has come and gone…now with her life she must carry on.She holds her head high,And on her face wears a smileOnly she know it’s a charade…a lie!And every time she passes him byHis cheating heart skips a beat as it cry’sWhy ? O’ Why ?
