BlackThere are two of meLook deep in side and you’all seeThere’s black…it’s the evil in me-It fights to get outIt screams and shoutsI close my eyes and look withinThere is a battle insideThe black is evil it makes me sin-The black is unconquerableIT ALWAYS WINS!!!The white is weak-it shan’t speakIt shall not fight ,it goes to sleepwillingly accepting defeatSo the black I see, isn’t the real meFor I’m locked in a shellCondemned to a black hellI no longer want to fightI no longer want to be whiteThe black has conquered meFor me there is no turningBack….Hello all,i thank you for comments...i am to a professional writer or anything, just a girl expressing my thoughts.
