FriendsIn our unending questTo earn friendsWe’ve all takenSome unfortunate bendsBut how do you knowWho to trust?That is the big questionFor most of us!You see peopleThe thing is thatYour best friend-has a best friend and nothing you tell will ever stay under the hatYour Best friend should be deadThen you’ll never hear anythingBeing re-said.But if you insistIn searching for friendship that’s realBe prepared to get a raw dealFor a true friend, will never take youAround those unfortunate bends
