
I want to die…..want to be dead
Life is pointless
Wish I were a corpse instead
I want to lay in a black box
Covered by heavy rocks
So my soul can’t escape
To come back here…taking a new shape
Placed at an end of the earth
Covered with rocks and plenty of dirt
The grave …unmarked
Well except….maybe...for a black heart.

To show my grated
Upon this earth again…my body will never intrude.
