Take me to
Something REAL!

Make me HURT
Make me FEEL

Take me to the DARKNESS
Lets lay in a bed of roses
and do nothing but KISS

Take me to the other side
Away from this place
Lets RUN...LETS' hide

Take me any where
I don't belong HERE ...Well!!!
