Tonight … 						      Tonight , I close my eyes and think of you,With out you my life is sad and blue!Every time you hurt meI say never again!But then you come to me …And  with a smile heal all my pain.However tonight, I tell myselfThis hurt has to be because he loves someone else!Broken promises lie every whereIn my heart , I can’t believe That you are not there.I know that , I must go on with my lifeEven if you are goneAnd as sure as the sun will rise in the east,I’ll always wish you nothing but peace.I know, I’ll be better tomorrowI always bury my pain, hurt and sorrow.
