
Touch me, don’t touch me!
Watch me!
What is that I see?
Could it be me?

What am I?
Am I a bird flying high?
Can I swore?
Could I touch the sky?

NO! I’m not a bird 
I cannot fly!
Yet, I often times dream of touching the sky

What am I?
Am I a leaf in the wind?
Or am I a beautiful ballerina? 
Watch me spin!

No! I’m none of these things
I am ,ME !
I close my eyes…shut the world out
And just let 
Me be!
