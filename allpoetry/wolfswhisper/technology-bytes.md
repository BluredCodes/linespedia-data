Got a new computer,
Brought it home today.
Set it on my desk,
Decided to play.
Downloaded some of this,
A little of that.
It froze a few times so I gave it a tap.
Little did I know,
My computer would fight back.
Little machine with a mind all its own.
Took advantage of me and I started to growl.
Thought about mallets,
Pairs of steel toed boots.
Maybe an elephant gun would do…
We went at it for hours on end,
I thought my computer would be my new friend.
Well damn was I wrong.
I downloaded music,
It happily hummed along.
Did all I could do to soothe the savage beast.
It munched on its memory a tasty feast.
I watched in terror as the bytes slowly decreased.
What will happen when it is all gone?
Will it still even run?
Here’s a simple lesson for all who have home PC’s,
Be careful with them,
They’re impossible to please.A BIG BIG twist from my usual style. Hope ya'll enjoy it :D
