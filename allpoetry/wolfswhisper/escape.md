---
title: "Escape"
writer: "WolfsWhisper"
slug: "escape"
source: "AllPoetry"

Crying silent tears on my pillow,
Longing for a part to fill the hole.
Craving for the missing piece,
The one that makes my soul.
Drowning in my sorrows,
Doubting that they're real.
Trying to escape the pain,
And everything else I feel.
Wanting to shut down,
Hide deep within myself.
But that's not possible,
I can't dig deep enough.
