---
title: "Life To Death"
writer: "WolfsWhisper"

A tinge of pain an ounce of fear,That clears away every tear.Life to death then life again,Traveling in circles that never end.Trying not to feel the woe,Although it wasn’t a friend nor foe.Death can be good, or death can be bad,Death leaves everyone feeling sad.But in the end all is well,Yet only time can really tell,When it’s yours or when it’s mine,We’ll all surely cross the line.For in the end there’s happiness,Because it’s when spirits have their rest.So whenever someone must move on,Remember they’re not really gone.They’re resting in a happy place,And once again you’ll see their face.In memory of Eric Meador.
