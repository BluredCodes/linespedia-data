Oh Great Spirit,
Oh Goddess divine,
Up high in the sky the is the place where you shine.
Shine down your rays,
Shine down your light,
Shine down your blessings throughout the night.
You bless me with love,
You bless me with luck.
You bless me with power as you rise higher up.
The way you protect me,
The way that you care,
You have place in my heart and always are near.
I thank thee O Mother,
But I must be on my way.
The day is now coming and this prayer I do say.
May you always be with me,
May you always shine bright,
May you always help me when I am in fright.
The sun is now shining The God has taken his place.
I thank thee O Mother,
For your blessings and grace.
As you glide down you shall be with me,
We both have to go  now so Merry Part and Blessed Be.This one is from a while back
