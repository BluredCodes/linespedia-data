Slowly turning inside,
Gazing deep within my soul.
Searching endlessly inside myself,
For the secrets that I hold.
Swiftly moving through the brain,
Stopping immediately as I reach my heart.
Stone cold door a lock no key.
"Secrets please come out to me".
I beg a silent plea.
Something glitters in my fingers,
It is there that I head next.
Close my eyes what a surprise,
In my fingers is where it rests.
Gliding over reach down deeper,
Barely out of grasp.
Brush it lightly just by a hair,
It plumets down a crack.
Slump down on my knees,
Wonder if I'll ever get it back.
Think silently to myself,
"Is there another way to unlock heart of stone?",
A way there must be,
I sigh rather tensely.
Key or none getting through that door,
Is my only ticket home.
