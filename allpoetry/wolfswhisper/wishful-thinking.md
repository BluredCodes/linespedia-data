Wishing I could touch you,
I can only hold you in my heart.
Wishing I could take away the distance between us,
For we’re more than a world apart.
Wishing I could tell you exactly how I feel,
Keep denying my emotions,
They aren’t really real.
Wishing I could fill the emptiness inside,
Keep pushing it out,
Keep setting it aside.
Wishing I could see your face,
A picture’s all I got,
An image in time,
A snapshot in space.
Wishing somehow you were here,
‘Cause knowing you’re not,
Is almost too much to bear.
A half and a half can make a whole,
What do you do when you're but half a soul?I guess this is for those I love but can't be with. Those who are present in this life right now, and those who have gone beyond the veil.
