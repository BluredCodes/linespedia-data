Blue the sky the sea,
The air that living things all breathe,
The coolest of water lurking at the bottom of a stream.
Waterfalls looking like brand new,
Trickle softly over rocky ridges,
Near heavens skies so blue.
Silver hawks are flying past the sun,
For the new day’s just begun.this is one of my earlier poems. I'm posting some of them now while my brain takes a rest.
