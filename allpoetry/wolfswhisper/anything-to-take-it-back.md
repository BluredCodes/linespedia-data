I'm sorry I had to open my mouth,
I'm sorry that I had to scream and shout.
I never meant to pick a fight,
I'm sorry for all that I said last night.
I just had this feeling bursting inside,
You were there and I was blind.
How I wish I could take those words back,
Keep my soul from turning black.
By darkening myself I'm hurting you,
Which is the one thing I never wanted to do.
Please hear me out and know this to be true,
You are forever in my heart,
You are my other part.
We are connected by all that we do,
I know that we can make it through.
