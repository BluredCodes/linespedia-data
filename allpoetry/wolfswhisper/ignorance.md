title: "Ignorance"

Fear,
All are afraid.
Hatred,
All turn away.
Truth,
All tell lies.
Justice,
All want their way.
Change,
All never want to.
Ignorance,
Fear of the unknown.
Fear and hatred,
Justice and truth,
Change and ignorance.
All are mere clues.
Clues to the humans,
Clues to the inside,
Clues to the things that never seem to die.
Wars have been fought,
Battles begun,
But no one has ever really won.
True peace has never been attained,
Treaties are a way of fooling the brain.
What’s never forgotten is the endless pain,
The suffering,
It will always remain.
Never forgotten,
Never forgiven,
The people will always continue on,
Lashing out because of their feelings.
The cycle will continue spiraling down,
Because of peoples unforgiving nature.
 There will always be one thing,
One thing that never dies.
It makes many people cry,
It’s called,
Ignorance.
