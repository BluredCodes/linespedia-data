Deep down inside of me,
I hold a voice I can’t set free.
On the outside perfect silence,
On the inside crying pain.
I want to speak and pour my soul,
My mouth stays shut,
I smile and nod.
Put on a happy act,
All can believe.
Only I know what's really in me.
