---
title: "Hiding Place"
writer: "Ciona"
slug: "hiding-place"

.
.
.

building a fortress
building a hiding place
for my protection
protection against the pain

a thousand arrows cutting into me
weakening body and soul
creating a shame
i did not know 
i 
could 
feel


-i am so cold-


drop by drop
cut by cut
it’s building up
it hurts so much
--
slap by slap
word by word
it’s adding up
touch by touch
--
hit by hit
kick by kick
it’s building up
brick 
by 
brick 
.
.
.
