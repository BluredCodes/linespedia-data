---
title: "Extraordinary"
writer: "Ciona"
slug: "extraordinary"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22151-Extraordinary-by-Ciona"
---

.
.
.
.
You’re extraordinary
a work of art
your gifts amazing
you stand apart

don’t reach down
to that average life
you’re not ordinary
so don’t compromise

take the path
not trampled down
follow your own drum
not somebody else

don’t turn a blind eye 
to the wonders ahead
don’t seek the safety
of an ordinary man

You’re extraordinary
a work of art
you’re gift's amazing
but why do you stop and start?

if you could see yourself
see through my eyes
there’d be no more doubts
you could realize

How extraordinary
so beautiful
it’s fascinating
You’re so incredible
~to me~
