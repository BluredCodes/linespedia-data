Men don't LIKE aggressive women!
What the FUCK!
I can't tell someone how I feel
without worrying they will think I want to marry them?!
I just wanted to tell him
How I feel.
Make him SEE me. 
Is there ANYTHING wrong with that?
I didn't ASK for a fucking commitment
so get OFF my fucking back.
I had GOOD intentions.
Make him smile
Make him blush
Make him know someone cares.
Not a fucking thing wrong with that.
So get OFF my back.
I didn't ask him to MARRY me.
