When you get the ball you ask yourself
What have I done to deserve this honor
Oh how any of the other millions of men
Would glady give anything for this sacred chance
You've choosen me.... me of all men

I wont let you down
I will take this ball to the goal
No matter what it takes
I will... not... fail...

Problems arise as they always do
You've expect just as much 
and you've prepared for them
yet each one could mean the very end

You break one tackle, two, now three
Dance around yet a fourth, barley making it by
Things are going well
All thats bad is now behind.

You have beaten all the odds
and you still have the ball
You see no one standing in the way
Your steps from the goal
Out of the corner of your eye 
You see yet one more

The ball drops from your hands
You fall to the ground in tears 
Endless moments of silence as all are watching
You failed, miserably, when all looked good

As you lay on the ground weaping in pain
A teammate comes by to pick you up
Even though there may be "next year"
To hold the ball again, you dread with fear

For the rest of your days
One thought remains,

Was that my only chance .............
