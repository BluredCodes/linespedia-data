Why did you never tell me
Why did we grow so close
How long would you have waited
I thought you were MY rose

Who is this guy that you met
How is he really THAT great?
I still cant see the reason why
You want him as a marriage mate

I know that if I met him
I would probably like him too
Although I would ask myself
Does he really love you as I do?

Will he truely care for you
Can he fullfil you every desire
Will he put you on high
Is he someone you can admire

What if theres a mistletoe above
Or when the lights turn dim
Does he really deserve your love
Why'd you give your heart to him

Sometimes I just get these feelings
I am sure that he is a great guy
But at times I start to ask myself
wouldnt it be nice if he would just dieMan, what really bites, is that I found out about this the day before I was going to ask her. Wish something good would happen to me sometime this year, so I could try writing about something happy.
