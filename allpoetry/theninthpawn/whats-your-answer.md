Why do you do what you do
What makes you so special
Everytime I see you
My life has meaning again

The day I decided to ask you
Was a very special day to me
I always kept thinking about the day
You first said hello to me

Oh how great the plan was
To make it memorable was my aim
Then something stood in the way
And that day never came

Why didnt I see this
Why was I so blind
Now I cant get these thoughts
Out of my mind

Looking back in time, when we were young
oh how great were the times we'd share
I remembered just how awesome it was
Just knowing you were there

When times were good, when times were bad
When I felt good, when I felt sad
Whether you were far, or you were near
How much I treasured your listening ear

Oh how I wish I had these times back
I would not let you leave once again
Not without telling you just how I feel
And you making me happiest of all men

I still desire to ask you though
More than you could know...
What would your answer be
What if it was no?
