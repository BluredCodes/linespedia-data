---
title: "A Deadly Kiss"

Lips, burning fire

Desire, consuming blaze

Heat, rising higher

Eyes, erotic gaze


Tongue, igniting passion

Taste, bitter sweet

Touch, of infatuation

Breath, smoldering heat


Inside, jumping flame

Moment, pure bliss

Soul, calling your name

Memory, a deadly kiss
