You never listen to what I say.

It's only words you hear.

If I made 1 dollar for everyday,

You ignored me and neglected to be near.

My words have no meaning to you.

Just muttering in your ear.

If I didn't care I'd do the same to you.

I'd pretend not to hear.

Anyone to talk to but me.

I mean nothing to you.

How it hurts, if you could just see.

Your coldness makes me blue.

Anxiety grows within me fast.

Pushed up by Anger

Followed by pride held back.

Preventing me from becoming a stranger.



Can you hear my silent scream?
