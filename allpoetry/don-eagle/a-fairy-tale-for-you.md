A Fairy Tale For You!I’m sending you a make believe Paradise that you and I can share.We’ll walk along the shore hand and hand, and never have a care.We can live a fairy tale life and make believe it’s true.And the only ones that will be allowed is just me and you!So anytime you’re lonely and anytime blue.Take out this tiny fairy tale, and I’ll be there with you!                      Don Eagle
