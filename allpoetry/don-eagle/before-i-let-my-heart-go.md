Before I Let My Heart Go.
 There's something I’d like to know, before I let my heart go!
Will you be there when the winter winds blow?
Will you see how much I love you, when I worshipfully listen to every word you say?
Will you love me as much tomorrow as you do today, when my hair gets thin and turns to gray?
Would you be by my side if I should ever take ill, would you love me still?
When I say 'I love you!' Will you sincerely you reply?
Or will I read it in your eyes, that you’re telling me a lie?
I’ve been around and seen a lot in my days.
I’ve been through some things that would make you amazed.
I know if you asked me those questions, I know what I’d say.
'I’d love you and stay with you till the very last day!'
So before I let my heart go, I’d just like to know!

By DONEAGLE
