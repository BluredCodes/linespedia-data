*
*
*
Kinsaku --
standing beneath the Basho
a mantis
*
*
*Kinsaku was Basho's original name. Someone had nicknamed him Basho one day and he eventually changed his name. Basho is a banana style tree that shaded his front porch. So I wrote this as a humble tribute to him.     Monk
