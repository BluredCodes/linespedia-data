---
title: "Until Us My Love"
writer: "Don Baird"

I have attempted to write you
so many times

but

nothing reads and feels the way I want it to

I suppose it might be
that all the words have been used endlessly
by so many lovers
one to another

that

nothing written or said seems fresh enough

to me

for us

for you


so


forgive me for lacking creativity


but


though it has been said by many
it has never had so much meaning

until us



I love youTo my wife....... with love, Don
