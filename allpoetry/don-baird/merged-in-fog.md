---
title: "Merged in Fog"
writer: "Don Baird"

I cannot see
though
I hear the
          crying
          sobbing
          pain
          hunger and hate

I hear it

          I smell the burning of flesh

I cannot see
though 
I hear the
          laughter
          giggling
          ahs of comfort
          great feast and love

I hear it

          I smell the fresh powder new born

I cannot see
though
I hear it All
          sobs     giggles
          pain     comfort
          hate     love

I hear it

          one sound

          one moment
