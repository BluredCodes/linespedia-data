title: "A Voice from Within"
writer: "Don Baird"

Since I was a child,
you’ve watched.

I grew up as any 
but was inspired with a message.

    As I looked out over the everything,
	   you could sense essence.

	   When I prayed to my inner light,
	   it was loud and clear to all.

	   I spoke no words aloud
	   yet all could hear the message.

When I talked of peace,
you went to the mountains.

In hearing me discuss flow,
you sought the river.

You heard the words
but only the words.

	   There is essence,
	   a truth missed,

When you surrender yourself,
you will be victorious.

In giving up the urge to control,
you can receive the essence of all 
in the ALL.

Do not look with your eyes alone.
It is better to close them and see 
with your heart.

	   There you will sense love
	   and good feeling will be abundant.
	   An inner light glowing like a beacon
	   will create a beam of wonderfulness.

As I looked out over the Everything,
I sensed the essence of me within.

I no longer cared for was or to be
as I let go and gained.

		   My inner light grew strong
			  it became my life compass.
		   My inner light grew strong
			  and answered my every need.
		   My inner light grew strong
			  fulfilling me completely.

				     I gave up myself 

				        trusted the light

				           and now I am whole.
