twisting 
turning
churning
crying
hoping for an end
loving
playing
giggling
winking

hoping it lasts forever
but wanting it to end right now
one side to the other

continuous vacillation a human condition
hoping for one but getting another
wanting the other but getting one

aimlessly weaving down empty dreams
hoping for a compass but hope alone won't help

simplicity is lost
complexity is gained
peace of mind gone

but then who's calling the shots really
is there a mirror of where to point
why not use denial temper and blame

or

stand up and fight

and

conquer yourself the only enemy you really haveMy teacher used to always say "we have only one opponent and that is ourselves"..... and "to conquer that opponent is to conquer ourselves"...... and therefore "victory is at hand".
It's a turbulent sea but we have God's ability to swim.  Monk
