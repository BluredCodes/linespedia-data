title: "Never-Stopping World"

Round, round the worlds goes,But it never stops for me.Sick of going round and round,Just trying to hold onto steady ground.Breaking the rules and becoming anewSo, that the world stops for me.But, many attempts have I tried,Many lies I have lied.Just to make the world stopAnd notice me......Stop for me......Why don't you notice me?A hypocritic life doesn't work, Neither does the 'self' approach.Appreciation for 'me' Is what I search.But, now the rules are all broken,And I go away in shame.The world still goes round and round.And, still no one knows my name.
