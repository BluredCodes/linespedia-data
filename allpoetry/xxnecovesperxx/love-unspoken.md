---
title: "Love Unspoken"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

You know who you are, don't try and deny
Your feelings for me are still there.
But, will they ever reach the surface?
Or must you remain blinded by true love's stare?
True love must be blind indeed, 
For 'tis our friendship which shuts your eyes.
O! If only you had reached the epiphany earlier,
We wouldn't need to masquerade in disguise.
You toy with my head, 
As you do with the other girls.
You mistake me for true love,
And you speak to me in false words.
And yet, there's something about you,
It's unspeakably indescribable what you do to me.
You play with my head and lead me to believe
That in me you find release.
Even though you are such a liar
I find love in you yet.
And our past will you remember
Despite your arduous attempts to forget.
You'll remember me for the rest of your life,
I will make it so that it is so.
You will never forget my love, my friendship,
You will never be able to let it go.
I will let you know though that i love you still
Despite the fact that my heart is broken,
And until you learn to feel the same,
Consider our love unspoken...This is dedicated to my best friend, he knows who he is..i hope he still knows i love him..
