---
title: "Mistaken Wall"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "mistaken-wall"

I built a wall
So you weren't able to see,
How much your heart,
Meant to me.
Please, retrieve your heart from her,
So that I may receive,
The love I never 
Could perceive.
I can't fully love you,
If your heart is not on my side,
For within her,
Your heart abides.
