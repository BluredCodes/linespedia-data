title: "My Departure"

I'll be gone for a while, but don't you fret.
Our love, the "us", I won't forget.
But, I do believe, I had something I left,
Silly me! It was my heart, in which you had kept!
Remember you have it, and treasure it well,
It's never good-bye, merely a temporary farewell.
