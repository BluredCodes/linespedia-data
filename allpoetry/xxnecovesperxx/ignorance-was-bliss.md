While days have passed,An exposition so true,T'was better left unspoken,T'was better we not knew.Ah, too late, A lucid fact we miss.O! Oblivious self - Ignorance was bliss!Empty promises neverSee fulfillment.Hearts filled with regret,Realizing in vain, our time was spent.Now, too late, I have begun to fall in love,Reaffirming it only makes me cry.So, don't say 'I love you'.Just, plainly say, 'good-bye'......
