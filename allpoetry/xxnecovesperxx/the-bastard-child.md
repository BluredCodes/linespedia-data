I look outside my window,
Each passing day,
I see them laughing and smiling
Until it hurts to see them play.

They don't know how deep the wounds run,
Nor how many tears I have bled,
They don't know that it was they 
Upon which my anger fed.

They run around in their bonny circles
Outside my beaten door.
They hunted me down with a passion
But,chose to nettle and ignore.

I kept my blinds shut tight,
And stayed home in bed,
But the dreadful noise followed me,
Their taunting words deranged my head.

I refused to look outside my window,
The childrens' laughter drove me wild.
With choruses of unforgettable verses:
"COME OUT, BASTARD CHILD!"i hope no one should ever have to feel this way..
