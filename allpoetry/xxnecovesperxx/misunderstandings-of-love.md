---
title: "Misunderstandings of Love"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

Love is an echo, strong at first but later grows weak.
Love is a fire, but what are left are ashes.
Love is a rose, but through time, will whither.
Love doesn't conquer, you just choose to not fight.
Love can be explained, you just need to choose the right words.
The course of true love is smooth, but you must pave the way.
Love isn't blind, you just choose to not see.
Love doesn't hurt, you just choose to not heal.
But, if the rule is: an eye for an eye.
Where is your heart that I exchanged for mine?
