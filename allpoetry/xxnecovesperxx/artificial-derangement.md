title: "Artificial Derangement"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

All my life livedIn insolency vain.Hoping for the sun to rise,But, it only sets again.I hear voices,Simultaneously silence I hear.Was all this in misery?I cannot see through my tears.I cannot see, or is itBecause I lack the ability?Or is it I deny?Futile, this wretched eye!This world sees but the countenanceOf what they wish to see.Leave me these pleasant Words of insecurity.The one thing I treasure,Whose value is now bereft.Pilfered from it, it's one sole worth - Words which embodied the truth.Life can be demeaning.Life can be sincere.But, it can't be either,If there are no shedded tears.Love lost and disreputable shame - Epitomizes my name.T'was innocence misspent.T'was artificial derangement.
