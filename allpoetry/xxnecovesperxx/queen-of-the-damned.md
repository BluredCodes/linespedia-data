---
title: "Queen of the Damned"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "queen-of-the-damned"
source: "AllPoetry"

I will not fall victim to your
Masquerading conformity.
Follow you to that gallowed cross
And worship your superiority.
What dwells behind that alluring smile
That I should be aware?
Are you but a wolf in sheep's clothing
Luring me to your lair?
I followed not to where your path led
Instead I left a trail.
So, when you travel along MY course
It is I you will hail.
You think I am a heretic
For seeing with my own eyes.
You fall far short of speaking truth - 
Mere hypocrite, master of disguise.
All the world over, a warning take,
And cover that feiry hole.
T'will lead you to hell's dark gate
And bring ruin to your soul.
It is in insolent vain
To battle with your foolish reason.
Should I be Queen of the Damned, 
And you, my Apostle of Treason.
