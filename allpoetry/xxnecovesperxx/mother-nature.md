---
title: "Mother Nature"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

Desist Mother Nature,
The creation of tears.
The liquid that has brought 
Comfort through the years.
Sojourn, Mother Nature,
In the creation of a flame.
But, pursue in the
Creation of my name.
