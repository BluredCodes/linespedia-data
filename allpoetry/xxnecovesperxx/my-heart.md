---
title: "My Heart"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

My heart is my love,Destined to never see again.My heart is my ultimate Source of Pain.My heart - overused,By so many men.My heart, with the help of love,Has always been slain.My heart, always fooled By Cupid's love tricks.My heart always has tearsFor me to shed.My heart slowly aches, As the hour slowly ticks.My heart is my Guaranteed deathbed.
