You always said you loved me,And you left me the next day.You always promised forevers,But were never planning to stay.You always gave me your hand,But never offered your heart.You said we would never end,But you never chose to start.You offered me your kisses,But our souls never did fuse.My love was always there,But, you always refused.Your arms were always there,But never to hold me close.Your words, left unspoken,Even though it was you that I loved most.And, if you spoke, the words you speakedWere often times not true.Thank you for abandoning me,I had no one else but you.We occasionally bump into people like the one in this poem. and i would like to thank them for their hardshipsbecause it only made me stronger......
