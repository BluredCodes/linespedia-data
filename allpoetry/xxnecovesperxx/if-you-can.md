I can't find myself.Do you see her?I can't figure out who I am,Do you believe her?Why can't I love,CURSED be this intimidation, and due to what, I ask myself - 'tis mere vulnerability.Exposure to rays of pessimism and of this second-hand pollution.'tis a cruel unforgiving fact for this unthinkable solution.But, can you look past expression?And, listen to what I'm not?And, if you really mean it, you would never have forgot.Help me find out who I am because I cannot.Unstrip the paints off my face,Be careful because it hurts a lot.And, only do it if you think you can.
