Our myriad union
Our days of forever
This promise I can keep - 
I could leave thee never.

I love the way thou loves -
Perpetual and true.
I love thy dialogue
Though thy words are so few.

Men just like thee are none,
If not, just all too rare.
I fear thy pilferage -
If love should disappear.

Thou assumes I would leave
But may I prove thee wrong?
When the time grows weary
Love stays steadfast and strong.

Thou offers me such love
Incomparable is.
I find myself waiting
For morrow brings a kiss.

I tore thy heart from thee
This heart from me, thou tore
As days pass in seconds - 
I grow to love thee more.this is in dedication to my one and only babes. =)
