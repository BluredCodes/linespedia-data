---
title: "When you realize"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "when-you-realize"
source: "AllPoetry"

When will you realize that I am a youth?
And, maybe what I'm saying is the absolute truth?

You're the older one, so when will you realize,
That maybe you're the one speaking all the lies?
