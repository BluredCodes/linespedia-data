---
title: "Wink and Smile"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

I'm forced to hideIn this dire seclusion.A smile is but a guise,An ecstatically positive illusion.Anger is what I feel inside.Words justCannot describe.The odium theologicumAugments year by year.The only way to show itWas tear by tear.Falling on the cold, stone floor.My unnerved heart cannot take it anymore.Broken in pieces and blown away.Loneliness and abhorration are forced to stay.Tears of a furious juvenile.But, I just look at you to wink and smile.
