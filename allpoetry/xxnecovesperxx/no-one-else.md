---
title: "No one else"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "no-one-else"

If I had a choice to erase just one regret.
I'd pick you, so that I may forget.
Every kiss, every embrace, every word spoken.
And, all the times you've left my heart broken.
You're the kind of person I hate to love and love to hate.
I offered you my heart,which you never did appreciate.
With all the pain, I've kept inside, whatever you seem to do,
I never, never want to be in love with anyone else but you.
