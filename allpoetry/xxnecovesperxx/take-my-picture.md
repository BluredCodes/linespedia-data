---
title: "Take My Picture"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "take-my-picture"

Picture me in your mindThat's how you'll see me for all time.Take a picture of underneath my skin.But, you can't because you never bothered to look within.Try to dig beneath the core,At the bottom, they'll be something more.Won't be hard to dig in me,All you need is the shovel of patience and the light of fidelity.Dig beneath the core so loose.And, you shall unveil the truth.Search with your heart, your soul, your mind,Then the truth you seek, you shall find.
