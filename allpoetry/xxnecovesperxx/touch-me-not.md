You entered the room quite noiselessly so as not to disturb,So as not to draw attention to what you were plottingIn that deviant debauching mind of yours.You entered in silence.And your hand besomed my hair aside.You whispered ignominious sexual zeal into my earBut your words provided no sense of impregnated security.I just wanted your favorite past time to finish as apace as possible.As you motioned your hand in other which waysGently you caress my face,and you explore further down.So just in case you forgot,I am not your child to play with,So touch me not......i encourage all those who have or is experiencing this unfortunate predicament to actually speak out, this fear has gone far too often, and should not be left unspoken.
