title: "You Will Never Know"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

I have kissed your cheek,but only as a friend,and you will never know,that it's in this kiss i found relief, but I'll never let you know.I have held your hand,to cross the roads we walkand you will never knowthat our union suits me with bliss,but I'll never let you know.I now, kiss your foreheadas you keep quiet in the arms of Morpheus.i never let you knew:how much you meant to me,how fond I was of your company,how soothing it was to hear your voice,how nice it was to see your hand adjoined in mine - And now you will never know......o pity unrequited love has an expected sad ending......
