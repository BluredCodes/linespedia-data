title: "Because You Believed"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

I stood up on the stage.
I could not breathe at all.
The lights they glared
And the people stared
At me from across the hall.

My eyes transfixed
On your Australian eyes.
My knees - they shook
The microphone I took
As I let out a broken sigh.

I knew I couldn't make it.
But you said that I should.
You closed the lights,
Smiled, and said "it would be all right",
And I did what you said I could.

The light shone brightly,
So bright - I could barely see.
I sang with all my heart and soul,
The stage was under my control - 
All because you believed in me.
