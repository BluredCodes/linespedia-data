My heart tremblesEverytime I hear thy name.My hand quiversAnd my face turns away in shame.Thou art with a new love,As I am with mine,Yet if we made a more determined attempt,My heart would be forever thine.But, if thou offers me friendship,I must refuse in a matter deemed humble.For if I accept thy friendship,I will try to walk away, but stumble -Upon the remains of the ashesOf a love so true, my dear muse.But, if thou offers me friendship,I must silently refuse.sometimes, friendship is not enough......
