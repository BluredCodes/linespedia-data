title: "Don't Ask Me Anymore"

To every answer,
Another question you provide.
But, shouldn't you feel what I feel
Since my heart, in you, it bides?
You ask me to what extent
To you, my heart is for.
But, if you love me as I you - please
Don't ask me anymore......
