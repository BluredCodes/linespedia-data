title: "First Sight"

Love at first sight,Relies mostly upon vision.And you, my love.Depict the perfect description.I love you for your wit,I love you for your strength,And for you my loveWill go far beyond love's length.I love you for yourMagnetically seducing sweet charm.I love you for yourPerpetually supporting open arms.I love you for understanding That my heart must be set free.I love you most of all though,For loving what you do not see.
