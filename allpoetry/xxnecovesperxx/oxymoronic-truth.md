---
title: "Oxymoronic Truth"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "oxymoronic-truth"
source: "AllPoetry"

While being TORN WHOLE, with such LETHARGIC FORCE, and EFFORTLESS LABOR, this AUDACIOUS COWARD began DISMANTLING CREATION with WEAK VIGOR and with such ENTHUSIASTIC LAMENT, only at the end realizing that the WORTHY MISCREANT standing before herwas the mirror! Ah! BLIND VISION! Resulting in DRY TEARS!
