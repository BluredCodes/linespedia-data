title: "[upon that hallow brow]"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"

upon that hallow brow
i must restore,
that which made you
the man i adored.
retract your pride
stomach your free will
that which made me
love you still.
i forged my heart
in your promiscuous heat
from whence the day
our pilgrim eyes first did meet.
it was then i felt
infatuation's kiss,
there and then - 
my heart you dismissed.
i've fetished o'er you - 
this. . . . . . empty boy,
around his fingers - 
these emotions he toyed.
you coyly romanced me
and caught me in your net
entangled, threatened
and in constant regret.
that my steely heart 
could ever be sweetened
yet damaged by your passion
and mercilessly beaten.
your innocent charms transfigured
marked by eager desire
had reduced me to ashes
set by your careless fire.
has hurt consumed me all?
how was it that i was killed?
if whence i first touched
you seemed emptier still?
