title: "Sweet Memories"

I turn on my radio,
And, what do I hear?
A song that triggers
My memory's ear.
The song reminds me,
Of what used to be,
Talks, hugs, kisses,
Ah, such a sweet memory.
A sweet memory
That would stay in my mind,
It's like a lovely dream 
That I will never again find.
A tragic ending 
To this lovely tale,
At the beginning starts love,
At the ends love fails.
And, I keep on thinking
Of what we had, of what we made,
But soon the song ends,
And the memories fade.
