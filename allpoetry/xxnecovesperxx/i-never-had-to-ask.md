You raised my heart up on the highest shelf.You knew i couldn't reach.You put it there to taunt my heart In the hopes of setting it free.My hands are smallYou knew that, tooAnd, still you managed, to break my heart in two.With your hands you managed tobreak it and crush it and destroy it all at once.You saw me ache, you saw me cringe - You've seen me painfully wince.I never once had to askIf you loved me with your will.Though you continued to crush my heart,I loved you still.You broke one heartand divided it into many a piece.You threw the remains away,Thinking that the you would find release.You knew you had my heart in your hands,And you deliberately made it so,That you had made my heart stop it's beatBefore deciding to let it all go.this is to H.M.M.C. you know who you are, a HeArTbReAKeR
