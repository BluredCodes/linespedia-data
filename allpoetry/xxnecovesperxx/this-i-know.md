title: "This I Know"

I express't how I felt for thee.And thou pay'st me no heed.I told thee thou wert my shining knightAtop thy galliant steed.Ae, yet thy lips art more concerned and occupied -With other pilgrim kisses.Ye cannot fathom thine torture unto me.With each beat my heart misses.Thou intones thy love for her.Thou confides in me to not say a word.I discern all too well, but know deep down inside -Thou willst forever remain my world.Other pilgrim lips may dwell on thine lips.And reside in thy abode.But, they shall never love thee as much as I do,This I unmistakably know.dedicated to 'you' again......
