All I can do is let you go
And watch you turn away.
Look into the eyes of HER
And notice this look that seems to stay.

I see how deep you gaze
Into the eyes of HER
I motion towards you
But your heart could not be lured.

I observe your gestures
As you hold HER hand in yours.
I yearned for your attention - 
But you chose to lovingly ignore.

You tell me how much you need HER
How you'd best be left without
But I am just like you
My love for you was never in doubt.

All I can do is let you go
As you stare lovingly into HER eyes,
But my love has never faltered,
In YOU, my heart still lies.you know who you are
