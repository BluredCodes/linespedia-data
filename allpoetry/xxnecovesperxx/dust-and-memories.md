---
title: "Dust and Memories"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "dust-and-memories"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2624-Dust-and-Memories-by-XxnecovesperxX"

You, now are gone.
Ashes - taken by the purified seas.
Many have made attempts to blow away the dust - 
But fail to annihilate the memories.
