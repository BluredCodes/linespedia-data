Tired of showing love
You don't even notice.
Tired of feeling love
That isn't even there.
Tired of staring at you,
When I cant even look into your eyes.
Tired of pretending,
When I know my feelings are so true.
Tired of supplying you with my heart
When all you supply me are tears.
Tired of feeling so LIGHT,
When I'm really FALLING in love.
