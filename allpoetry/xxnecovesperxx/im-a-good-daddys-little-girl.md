I stretched my hand, so you would help me out when I fallBut, you kissed it instead.I didn't think anything of it, until you kept on kissing up my armAnd, you led me to mommy's bed.You started touching me in degrading ways, and I didn't say a wordBecause I knew this is how you show me you care.I lied in bed, and you began to go inI still didn't think wrong as you brushed away my hair.It started to hurt and then there was blood, and as you came out,You kissed me once more.After all that, you got up and before you closed the door,You said that, 'i'm a good daddy's little girl.'
