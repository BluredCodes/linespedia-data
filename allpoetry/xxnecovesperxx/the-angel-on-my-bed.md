I lay on the bed, and when I turned
My arms longed for you, for you it yearned.
You came jumping on my bed, you giddy little soul.
You kissed me on my neck, and started to take control.
You kissed my upper lips, then undressed me from my nightgown, 
You intended to kiss my lower lips and you went further down.
You kissed me like no man ever did, you brought pleasure all over.
I just thought this could not be a dream, and I made sure I was sober.
I couldn't think, my head was spinning,
Heavy, sweaty breaths were we breathing.
Your tongue went inside me and in all directions did it go.
I felt all wet with effervescent delectation.
I was overcome with bliss and sexual gratification.
You dragged me by my shoulder and pushed me unto the wall.
And grabbed me by my breasts, which I did not find awkward at all.
Our bodies naked, pressed up on each other it felt like sweet love was made.
You kissed me all over once more until tender it became.
You looked me all over and once more and again.
Then, I opened my eyes and you were once more just a friend.
