---
title: "I Chose"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "i-chose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3069-I-Chose-by-XxnecovesperxX"
---

Your heart had lips so you could relay
All the feelings you felt for me.
My heart didn't have the ears,
And in so, I denied your love for me.
I chose not to listen.
I chose not to see past friendship.
I chose to humiliate your feelings 
By pretending I did not know what you meant.
But, I did, and stayed with hypocrisy.
What I would do to take it all back.
But, I chose, and I regret.
Now, my pillows stay wet - 
With tears that will never dry.
I cannot say I don't need you, because I know I do.
I cannot say I don't love you, because that is not true.
I cannot forget you, because you're always a step ahead.
And no matter what I do, I cannot get you out of my head.
These wounds in my heart are self-brought and I will never find relief.
My heart lied within you, but I never chose to seek it.
I chose to not love you then, but I choose to love you now,
And if you choose to love me again, I'll never let you go......you know who you are......i still love you, i hope you still love me, too......
