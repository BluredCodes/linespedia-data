title: "Meaningless Kiss"

Awaiting for you will be a morning kiss,And, in this kiss, we shall seal our lips, As if thee and I were one in unity.Souls united,Hearts adjoined.In one pulsing heartbeat, we find bliss.And, all this ecstasy due to one meaningless kiss.
