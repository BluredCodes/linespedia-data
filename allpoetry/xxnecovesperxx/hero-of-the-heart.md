title: "Hero of the Heart"

Everyone perceives a Hero idealistic - Audacious, devoted, unbiased, intellectual,Are some characteristics.Heroes like these may we find.But, a negative side they may uphold,That's why my exemplary hero,Is one whom I may mold.I look inside my heart,To a mirror, then, I turn.To see if I may become,A person willing to learn - About the characteristics of a hero true.So someday children will say,'I want to be like you'.I fight to keep believing,That my goal, one day, I will fulfill.But, until that day comes - Learning I am still.
