---
title: "If I Could"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "if-i-could"
source: "AllPoetry"

If I could, I would like to share my world with you,Which already is you.If I could, I would like to spend my life with you,Even if I just met you.If I could, I'd love to offer you my hand,Even if you didn't ask for it.If I could, I would hold you close to my heart,Even if you decide to let me go first.If I could, I would ask to see forever in your eyes,Even if it lasts for seconds.
