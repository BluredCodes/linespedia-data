---
title: "Black Milk"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "black-milk"
source: "AllPoetry"

White, warm milk nourishes one's soul.
Black, cold milk hurts one's soul.
If white milk is able to make you feel nice.
Then, black milk is capable to freeze you like ice.
If white milk can touch one's heart.
Then, black milk can break your heart.
If white milk can shed your tears because of joy.
Then, black tears can shed your tears because of sorrow.
Black milk can bring pain into your home.
Black milk can make you feel all alone.
Black milk can cause you to sigh.
End everyday of yours with a cry.
Black milk can provide you with a frown.
Turn your ups into downs.
Black milk is the weed of your soul.
The more you drink it, the bigger this weed grows.
Black milk can cause unhappiness to people,
Black milk turns the strong feeble.
Black milk can do all of the above.
But, most of all, it can diminish love.
If white milk can cause you to be loved by all,
Black milk can cause you to be hated and fall.
If white milk is the definition of love and affection,
Then, black milk's definition would be execration.
