I taint my world so that I may see clearer......But after tainting my world,Too late I realize,That what I see Before me are lies.Tainting my world caused my sight to blur,Anyway, what areWe searching for?What are we after?To find the saga To this perpetual chapter?Nay, I say,We search not for perfection.We bother to look onlyFor our own reflection.
