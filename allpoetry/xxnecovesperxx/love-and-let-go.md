---
title: "Love and Let Go"
writer: "XxnecovesperxX"
slug: "love-and-let-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the one to whom
You have first expressed.
I am the one to whom
You have honestly confessed.
I am your first kiss,
Your first "more than a friend".
With me, you have experienced,
Something you thought would never end.
Now, don't look at me,
With a tear in your eye,
Only now must you learn,
How to say "good-bye".
Always remember that it was you
Whom I once shared my romance.
But, don't let this intervene
With giving love a second chance.
All these emotions,
Now already you know.
Only now must you learn,
How to love and let go.
