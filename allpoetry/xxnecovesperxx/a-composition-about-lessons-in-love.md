The pain I feel when you open closed wounds, makes this feeling real.The love we share is said to be blind,But, tis friendship which closes your eyes.No one needs another, as much as I need you.If there's no proof on what you feel, It must be a love that's true.Women fall in love through words they hear,Men, through what they see.I can't let you go, you know, You mean too much to me.Innate within your heart lies mine,Safely hidden for quite sometime.Love doesnt conquer at all,It ends and begins again.I will never say that it's over because,It's never good-bye my friend.How many times must I ask you, to ask me to stay?Love is the glue that binds us. For you: J'ayme a jamais.
