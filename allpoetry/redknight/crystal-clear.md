title: "crystal clear"
writer: "redknight"

greatful for what we share another buetiful night
and when it was all done we held eachother tight
listening to eachothers heart beats we felt eachother grow
feeling eachother inside we both just know
i want to be a good boyfriend and i hope that i am
so that when you mention me your proud to say thats your man
as for me im proud to have you by my side
and if anyone was to ask
i would tell them its all about you and i
crystal clear i know where i want to be
in your arms is where i feel free
in your arms is the place for me
                                   2/21/02
