title: "to the man i never knew"
writer: "redknight"

to the man i never knew
im all grown up with hate for you
tears come and tears go
but love for you i will never show
only a coward would walk away from what he created
how not to be a man best demonstrated
to the man i never knew
thanks for making me stronger i owe that to you
