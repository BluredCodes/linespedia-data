my intuition to be with you was not based on sex
for us to be togeather some would say it is complex
im attracted by your intellecual conversation
your words make me feel free
but when i talk about love your elusive about the hole thing
thats when i feel like you neglect me 
time away from you is something i loathe
but my love for you is internal
in love i am like a ronan
help me be free
