your eyes permeate through my body and into my soul
you alleviate all the aggravation i have with in
i knew you had to be more than just a friend
i congregate my thuoghts and try to set them free
meditating helps to satiate my needs
illegible feelings i couldnt even explain
eternal love is something we shall sustain
impecuniuos i may be
but if you will help me to grow
then the man every one can see will begin to show

                 2/21/02
