---
title: "I will never stop loving you"
writer: "TaraKM"
slug: "i-will-never-stop-loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51299-I-will-never-stop-loving-you-by-TaraKM"

I think about you night and day,
In your kind and gentle way,
And I often wonder if,
We'll meet again some day,
How can I love another,
When I am still in love with you,
And times I often wonder if....
You think about me too,
I will always love you deep inside,
My heart will remain true,
My feelings will not ever fade,
When I'm still in love with you.
