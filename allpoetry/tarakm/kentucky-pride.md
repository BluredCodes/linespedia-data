The streets were lined,
With red, white, and blue,
Symbols of liberty,
In significant hues.

It's Independence Day,
In the U. S. of A.,
Where beauty and balance,
Is ensured to each day.

We are all so thankful,
For the land of the free,
It's our country, our home,
Sweat land of liberty.

And so I stand with pride,
Here in the Bluegrass State,
Which is the fifteenth piece,
That makes up the U.S.A.
