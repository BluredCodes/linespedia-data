Humans play this daily game,
They rise and shine each morning,
Only to complete each day,
Then must snooze the night away.

Wondering why they feel incomplete,
And feel that life has no meaning,
Wishing to know their destiny,
To fulfill the empty wholes.

But if only they could understand, 
And not waste a single minute,
Maybe they would find true happiness,
To restore their God given talents.

If only Humans could live out each day,
Each soul might rise, in a satified way,
Loving life, God and the thought....
"Live without regrete or don't live at all."
