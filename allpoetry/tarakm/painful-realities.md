Instead of joining the fight against polution,
We simply accept it as fact, as reality,
That we are to be condemned to a future,
Where one cannot see their own hand before them,
Where children cannot play for fear of suffocation,
Where a tree cannot bloom or a flower see the morning sun,
Where things as acid rain and toxic fumes inhabit the world,
And the country is no different from the smog of the city,
Where trash is tossed and arrogance is common,
Do you fear this happening to our world?
I don't, because it already has!
