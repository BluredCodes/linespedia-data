Winter breaks 
a corset of spring
over my shoulders,
together 
with force
the lament of silence
is broken
and 
my heart 
opens up
to possibilities of tomorrows;
in desperate hopes
of all things 
to become new.
Enduring a season
of cold 
and stillness
my bones ache
and my veins remain 
very thin.
As I look 
and wonder
through this cloudy glass
starring intensively,
reaching blindly
to touch 
the smooth surface,
to understand,
and to show 
something
for all my heartbeats;
as an enduring study
tires 
my mind
and I pain 
myself
to always think,
I extend 
desperately 
to clear away the clouds
and realize
that
I am only allowed 
to see so far
and 
must
