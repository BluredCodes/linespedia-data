Waking up this morning 
to your melodic name;
ahhhh, yessss
dancing in my thoughts
as it so does 
always.
As sweet and gentle 
as a bouncing butterfly 
dancing circles in summer's heat,
it stills my shaking mind
and replenishes me 
to live 
some more.
As bold and fierce 
as a twisting tornado 
destroying
the inhabited land
it invades my very being
and rips apart my composure, 
tearing down every fiber 
that holds me
as one.
No other name makes me feel 
as does your's;
could it be that
my mind incessantly,
laboriously 
and freely
sings 
of an unforgetable you
and therefore 
you forever exist?
As the familiar reverberations 
continue 
and beyond
to labor your name 
is none to labor, 
but 
always
