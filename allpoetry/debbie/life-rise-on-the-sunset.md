The comfort of you
laying against my chest
soothes me.
The rhythm
of your steady breaths
parallel mine
and together
we are one.
Surrendered
to the limitless sky
above us
every thought I once had,
and 
I now feel
weightless
and free.
Peace hovering
my mind
commands me 
to feel nothing
and therefore 
I do not hear also.
It 
has surpassed my understanding,
has enveloped me;
as does the waves of an ocean
swallowing 
the edge of the timid shore.
As I stroke your fine hair
to the harmonious movement 
of our breathing bodies,
you rest still 
upon me.
I lay here
immersed in you
and with my eyes closed
I watch 
life
rise
