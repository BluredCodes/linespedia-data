Take my hand
and my heart
and with me 
sing melodies 
as our sojourn
is through this place 
of life.
Together 
we will stroke the moon
and risk the sun, 
smile at the deep, stark oceans 
and laugh in the sincere faces of mountains;
call each star by name 
as we inhabit
this life together; 
embrace the universe 
as it welcomes us
and accept 
the invitation to play 
in the great open field 
of life;
jump 
into the center of my heart, 
as it is your place in destiny.
Leave behind 
the importance of nothing
and consume yourself 
with the abundance of all.
....and with me
sing melodies 
as our sojourn
is through this place
