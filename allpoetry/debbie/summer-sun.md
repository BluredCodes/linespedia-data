Basking boldly 
the sun in the vast sky
incandescently rediscovering
hot beams
aiming with confidence
at targets in distant sight;
bouncing fiercly off mica rocks
and blinding.
Dry, brown dirt 
heated 
and decorated scantily
with dandelions half clothed
and wilted daisies
from heat's brutal beating.
Hours of pounding quiet fire 
on bare, open pavement
forbidding any movement across;
sweltering, rising with shimmering blur
playing eye tricks on my mind.
The sun drawn to the rhythm
of a vulnerable earth
steadily circling
waiting for relief
lest
