Silently
with my eyes
I beg you
to succumb.
In harmony 
with my heart
you run to me.
Quivering with excitement
the sudden chill
has stolen 
my thoughts.
Anticipating
your warm,
gentle
breath
lingering 
on the nape of my neck
I still weaken
as always
it pleasantly 
surprises me.
As you wear my desires
so lovely
upon your countenance
the deep,
crimsom 
rose pedals
that sleep 
around my heart
when you are gone
now bloom with love eternal
as you are here.
My eyes beg you;
you succumb to me,
your breath touches me;
I bloom for you.
My wish upon time
would be this 
moment everlasting.
For solely
does my delicate,
beating heart
bloom for you,
and only 
a sense of you
need 
make me
