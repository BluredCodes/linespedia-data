title: "Seasons Of Life"

Winter days of cold, 
so dry, so bold 
will pass to old, 
now spring is told.
Spring days awake 
for blossoms sake
now summer's place 
does to take.
Summer days of haze, 
hot past plays
no more ways,
fall comes into phase.
Fall days to send, 
many a change to lend,
around the bend 
we go once again.
Seasons come and go 
as in our lives we know,
must live to grow 
move forward on so.
