title: "gettin' strong today"
writer: "twotonevocalexpression"

no decency - no sympathy
no shame
with each passing moment
your callous heart scorns me
shows me the real you
the new you
"people change" - they say
but i didn,t think this much
my warm - caring - loving grrl
whatever did they do to you
whatever did you do to yourself
blatant disregard for my wellbeing
and for what?
is there a point to this
a lesson to be learned
cos if not ....
lets just put it behind us 
start a new 
i guess that what i get
for loving you to death
i get
a
slow
painful
death
by
words
