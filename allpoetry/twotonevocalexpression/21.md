i,m thru feeling sorry for myself
that whole episode is over
everything will be perfect from now on
but who am i kidding?
i wanna talk
you still hear?
i,m in love w/her
YOU
my life is falling apart and yet
i,m smiling
i wanted to tell you
but i didn,t
now i am
does it still have the same impact
the intense feel?
the point is
----i love you----
take it as is
tho i may change
you will too
it doesn,t matter what happens now
what does happen now?
