if you choose me - my consciousness
authentically
i abandon myself in you
and live a life of illusury thru you
this meeting is harmonious
it,s natural
i,ve fallen for you - in the spirit
a unique and irreduceable phenomenon
can you worship me
w/o thinking of yourself
i can feel you in me
as i in you
we are one in the same
yet completely independent
baby this ain,t infatuation
this is real
true love
