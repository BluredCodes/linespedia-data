deception and perception
bourne thru god
the eminant cause
more reality/more perfection
than effect
there is no guarantee
that the sun will rise
we,ll wait til tomorrow to see
but i,m always in the dark
you can,t change what you don,t acknowledge
i am finite
therefore i cannot comprehend the infinte
but hey
it,s all relative baby
