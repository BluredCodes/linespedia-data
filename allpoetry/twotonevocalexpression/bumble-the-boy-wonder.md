title: "Bumble: the boy wonder!"

the weight
its overbearing intensity
cripples my motivation
to move or to feel
to know my surroundings
waiting for this nitemare to pass
i cry in a corner of solitude
too affraid to speak
to fragile to move
the waiting  -  crushes
its splinters cut deep
so deep
i clean my wounds 
w/ my salty tears
the stinging is all i feel
all i know
and for that one brief moment
all is mine
time and space become one
i am the resurrection
.........the mystery white boy
.........the living end
