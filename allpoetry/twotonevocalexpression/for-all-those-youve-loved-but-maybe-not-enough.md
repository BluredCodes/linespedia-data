title: "For all those you,ve loved  -  but maybe not enough"

does he know you,re not faithful
cos everynight you sleep w/ me
in my dreams
and maybe its enough
to keep me from falling apart
and maybe its not
cos i miss that touch
maybe it took this to let me know
and maybe it took this to let you know
that i wasn,t ever out of step
merely listening to a different beat
too bad you never let me know
the trip to his heart is much easier for you
much faster than my journey to show my love was true
"when we were strangers i watched you from afar"
knowing that the effort was well worth your prize
"when we were lovers, i loved you with all of my heart"
don,t doubt that i loved you
but maybe it just wasn,t enough
