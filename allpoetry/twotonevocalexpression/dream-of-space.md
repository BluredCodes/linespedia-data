a passive action					[dec13-3:44am]
try me later
I,m not feeling at home
A callous gesture 
I call out in agony
As if you cared
Tell me lies
Stupid lullabies
To pass the tyme
We have an uncommon rapport
A lack of interest
In things 
Draws me closer
To the abstract
Blue red green
Blend into a velvet cream
A satin sheet - asleep
Lose myself in a dark alley
