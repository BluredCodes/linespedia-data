silver star
thumb tack blues

so this Is what it,s come to be				
not how we imagined it eh?
I did it on purpose
So I can sleep at nite
Can,t live w/ myself
Knowing that I am a king 
In you royal jester,s court
Take that back
Think of me as callous
Unforgiving
Break it off and turn away
The pain subsides
And you,ll learn to love again
Some day
It,s better that way
I,d hate to think you still cared

silver star
thumb tack blues

crisp blues press on						
trees and their leaves
stand at attention
the water flows
in raging torrents
of serenity
I lay looking onward and out
Thoughts of you stumble by
I concentrate on your face
My brown eyed grl
In my moment of solitude
I still am not free
Bound to the scars of my memory
Forever chained to thoughts of you
I rise
Soaked in my own self
The world is dark
But somewhere off I can see a light
As I clamber towards it
I know this is the end
