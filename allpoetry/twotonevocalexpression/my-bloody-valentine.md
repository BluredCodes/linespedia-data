title: "My bloody valentine"

distance is a lover,s worst enemy
i correct myself - a lack of communication
all i know is your touch
what happened to that emotional connection?
where,d you go?
i once loved a grrl
but now i fear she,s dead and gone
maybe i,m still in love w/her
but not this ...... not this new you
she knew me inside and out
and i i feel i knew the same
a dramatic crescendo
it leaves me cold and alone
(here on valentines day)
nothing compares to you my dear
except perhaps for the lack thereof (you)
come take my hand 
show me where you,ve been
and where you plan to go
baby - won,t you call my name
all my love
     your bloody valentine
