If you drew a painting				
Would you name it after me
Do you envision
A more subtle truth 
Lacking in any real substance
What is this story
What triggers such a response
What ugliness in man elicits such
DISGUST
If we promise to forever be alone
Will we one day die free
Of chains to our earthly desires
Might we lose the preconceived notions
That man must find faults w/in himself
I am perfect
In that I am perfect for you
As you for me
What else could possibly mean more to me
Take this vow
Promise me this
You will never
Let me steal your tyme
