this meeting not inconsequential
not incidental
i followed you here
i wanna shelter you 
be your stronghold
against all that which may trouble you
can you lose yourself in me?
devote oneself to a new meaning
there,s so much to be said
but not now - not here
let me hold you - feel your essence
such a beautiful grrl
such a magical touch
if only you would let this be true
i,d stay here forever
if only for a day it were true
i,d cherish you for a lifetime
