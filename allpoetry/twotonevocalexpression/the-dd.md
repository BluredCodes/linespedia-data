truth be told/spoken famously
an innocence perpetrated by two
lines drawn - sides taken
neutrality is underrated
innocent innocence
guilt of one deceives the other
a false perception
truth be told
assumptions deceive us all
but follow the emotions
do not blame the catalyst
be thankful
regret nothing
a deep and profound moment of intimacy
spoken famously
acts are interpreted
intermission
lights dim
the script - unwritten
curtains open
just the two stand
