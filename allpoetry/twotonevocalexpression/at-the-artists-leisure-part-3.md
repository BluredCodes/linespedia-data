pen down - pen on paper
tear smudged ink
permeates my fingertips
"i love a man in uniform"
can,t get you outta my mind
think too much - i suppose
i thought i knew what it took
guess my plans were flawed
i watched as you erased your name from the blueprints
can,t just replace you
gonna try and figuer it out
i,m so "f,n full of it"
solace is welcomed but not easy to come by
friends and liquor 
alone won,t exorcise you - demon
so i press on
try to find this new i dentity
a new me i suppose
"let,s just pretend that we knew this all along/
and when you were gone that you,d come find me"
