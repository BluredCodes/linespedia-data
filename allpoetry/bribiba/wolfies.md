When you go out with a wolfhound
the people all stand in your way
asking the daftest of questions
here are some things they will say

Will you just take a look at that donkey
who's taking who for a run
well I don't care you can mock me
cause I know that wolfies are fun

Does he stay outside or inside
does that get to sleep on your bed
It must cost a fortune to feed him
my dog is the size of his head


What do you say for an answer
I tell them as sweet as I can
he's better behaved than yor children
and cheaper to keep than your man
