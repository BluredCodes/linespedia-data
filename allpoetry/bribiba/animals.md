---
title: "Animals"
writer: "bribiba"

If I could chose an animal that I would like to be
perhaps I'd chose an elephant, but wait I'd better see
Don't the hunters kill them for their tusks and nothing more
I don't want to die like that upon the forest floor

Well how about a sea creature a lovely baby seal.
I would get to swim all day or dive to catch a meal
but what if hunters saw me and hit me on the head
stole my lovely white coat and left me lying dead

Then how about a parrot flying through the air
living in the jungle I wouldn't have a care
but no there's hunters there as well ready with their nets
to send us all around the world and sell what's left as pets

perhaps then I should be a man he made the jungle laws
and only now he's finding out it's full of awful flaws
for some creatures it is to late but maybe if you're willing
we can save what's left of them from all our needless killing
