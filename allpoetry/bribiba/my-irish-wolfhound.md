title: "My Irish Wolfhound"

YOUR HEAD FITS SO SNUGGLY UNDER MY CHIN YOUR PAW IN MY HAND LIKE A GLOVE 

I CUDDLE YOU NEAR ME MY WONDERFUL FRIEND THERE’S NOTHING AS WARM AS YOUR LOVE 

THE DAY YOU FELL ILL I JUST BLAMED MYSELF WAS THERE SOMETHING THAT I DIDN’T DO 

BUT I KNOW NOW THAT NOTHING I DID WOULD BE ABLE TO HELP AND SAVE YOU   

 

 AS YOU GREW UP I KNEW THAT YOU'D BE  THE BEST FRIEND THAT I'D EVER FOUND 

NOTHING CAN HELP MEND THE HOLE IN MY HEART WHEN I LOST YOU MY IRISH WOLFHOUND 

THE COUCH LOOKS SO EMPTY THE GARDEN IS BARE I BEND DOWN AND PICK UP YOUR TOY 

THE TEARS START AGAIN AND I WHISPER YOUR NAME I MISS YOU OH PADDY MY BOY 

 

I WILL BE WITH YOU EACH STEP OF THE WAY I PROMISE THAT WE’LL NEVER PART 

I CANT STAND TO LOSE YOU I LOVE YOU SO MUCH BELIEVE ME YOUR TAKING MY HEART 

IT’S BEEN A FEW YEARS SINCE YOU WENT ON YOUR WAY AND THOUGH MY WHOLE LIFE HAS CHANGED TOO 

MANY OTHERS SURROUND ME AND GIVE ME SUCH LOVE BUT MY HEART STILL LONGS SADLY FOR YOU
