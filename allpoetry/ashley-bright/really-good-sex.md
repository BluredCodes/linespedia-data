Carry me darling into the ocean spread 
with virgins and warmth and candy canes,
Open my desires with penalty cents 
As I call you "mine",
Like a sick sweet lily

Your lips bloom darker reasons 
Your fingers direct further down 
A hell, a taste to acquire, 
A son with strange winds
blowing in with sensation, 
graving deeper thoughts taunting inside,
I move you like the sky azure
 
Carry me darling into the evening wake 
Like cynics and gods and starving hands 
And after you touch me 
After you come, 
I'll think of you "gone" 
Lying awake… 
Dreaming of your face teehee
