title: "~Ashes To Ashes~"

Far away, exploiting my name
While These  bending arrows
Peel away the scabs and devotion
From silence and milky pours
To seraphic understudies 
==with sleeves too short==
She stutters when she’s calm
The insides mingling with your father’s hands
And the world seems a bit clearer

Can you take me elsewhere
The noise was angry and demanding
The king was bottomless 
and forgiving
But he wanted it all

My cold hands are eager midgets
Scolded for the window 
^being opened too wide^
The mouth just shutters
When fed... the air regorges
And when I’m back in place
...I’m willing and slavish 

My knees are filthy with prayers
||and alcohol//
An elbow in my thigh...
Reminding me of thirst and exposure
I crumble, I forsake
Among the ashes and imbecility
You’ll find my broken promises
         of
