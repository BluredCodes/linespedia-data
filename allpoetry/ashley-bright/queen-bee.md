title: "Queen Bee"
writer: "Ashley Bright"

Where they come
With gruelling spears
And tiny eyes
Their children asleep 
With their antique toasters near by
And below I could hear voices
Shouting, crying, merrily in tongue
One of the bastards lifted his eyes to me
"Mother!!!, I'm so happy you've come!!"
