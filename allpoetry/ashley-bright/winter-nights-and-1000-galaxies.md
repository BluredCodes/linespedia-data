---
title: "Winter Nights And 1000 Galaxies"
writer: "Ashley Bright"
slug: "winter-nights-and-1000-galaxies"

A thousand chimes sounding vocal cries 
On grim winter nights, 
And that whistled smile, reflecting beneath the stars, 
In all the galaxies where funerals drift far away, 
There collides immovable metaphors, 
Violently dancing with immortal legs, 
Knock-knee children are rewarded fancy feasts
For their virtues engraved in the skies, 
And measly varmits roam free
Without fear covering their strides, 
And these winter scenes do curl my wits, 
As hell appears to be so false,
And heaven, dear warped heaven
(Fragile dreams to break in the end)
I’ve learned to sleep with my eyes so aware
As passion and feeble hands do compose such music,
And winter nights!
You widow, you widower!
I’ve known you so long
As I look into your eyes, I see...I feel, 
A thousand galaxies at which do cry, 
Much like you
Quite like I
(winter nights... I am your child)Don't forget to paint my smile on your hands
