title: "Perfect Fit"

I can fit you like dry flesh
Worn in the evening
As children with the drunk
You speak with white tongues
Your lips a fading black
I fit you like plastic knives
With your touch for every lie
You sound me off in a sleep of tired eyes
With music as precious for any keeper
To score with your wits
Lying with ash and oil
And a mother desperatly calling out to you
Her feathers once tickling your dream
Your sandy angel, without cause
To suffer in reason
I can fit you like a lost lover
With your regret leaving me bare
And once you're gone
I can fit you to forgive you
