Suddenly aware, 
frost and conniving showers
Rising from her bitter steel gun, 
arms and bows flaunting
belonging, 
(From the sun) 
Beneath the graves
Into my hair...
Caught and misled 
Voices lie
Dead behind
--The bushes and sewers swear--

She was ninety of angels and deceased
all waxed, relaxed, 
perfect to the sea

From the gray heart
Shielding cannons and pianos
Body
Stripped
from a
Yellow wasp

Smoldering comely
Fits with the dark
Penetration
was prized, revived
through
her animated
hands

She loves her children
She loves her breed
I find 
her armored
beauty monger
~Perfect
