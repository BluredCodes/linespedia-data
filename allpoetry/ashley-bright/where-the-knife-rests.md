You marry my skin eagerly with lies
Like some lavish warrant
Held for the childrens taste
My ring seeks approval as a languid daughter
And behind your hiding
Those stale half-bitten lips for Elysium
And I'm heedless how you flush
From the worshipers donations
Scared, you wear that name like your own
While I've reasoned to your distant plot
The knife is resting in my heart dear
Misplaced by your careless words
Your arms are choking another dear
Weeping in their deficient reach
My reflection stapled upon your eyes
As you make your way without me
Each hour passes, Each night grows frail and empty
To "wait" would be nothing more than diseased sluts
Behind your hiding, Behind I'm fading
Fear...you fuck that name like she was your own
While I've long seen you in  your eyes
A charmer, A snake charmer
Howling me off to cold sleep
And even there you're a stalking regret
Lonely, Forsaken, and bitter to your word
I have but one request
