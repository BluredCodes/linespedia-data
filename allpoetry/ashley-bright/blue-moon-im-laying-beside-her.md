Sweet sadness
with the lips of smutty brides
swindling her bibles
and her chords 
to a diddled dominion-------
-------repressing the warmth
of tangled mothers
***********Their lively etches
squirming onto her lover’s shadows*********
AS Marriage would be her motive
While ruler to all the chokecherries and wombs
#~Stalking midnight~#
in her yielding garbs
I swallow her juice with swollen buds
Each root potent
and forever
//////The sun her carriage\\\\\\
Enemy of the moon
Both remembered
delicious, delirious
with a
beautiful wound
The sword>>>>the thorn
willing in my left eye
Once Saving me from the desert bed
>>>>Sweet tongues<<<<
A new taste
The stars calmly insist
Gliding in the skies
***she opens my wrists***

^^^Blue Moon^^^
__I’m laying beside her__for you sweetsadness
