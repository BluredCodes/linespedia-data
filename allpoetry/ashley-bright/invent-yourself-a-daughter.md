Devise yourself a shimmering daughter Sally
Spreading herself over branches and graves
Peeking, seeking another fortune
So amiss in her nighttime daze
And when she hangs limp from bricks and cotton
Her blonde teeth will give reason for peril
Down by her ankles, those freshly cut martyrs 
Begging, urging to be seen in her glow
With her nipples feeding mouths to sustain  
In every drowning crowd, Sinner Sally hangs
Behind the walls, craving other words
When a tongue as hers cut to starving cattle
Slices in undertone bellies, fed to a seer princess 
And she’ll die, she’ll give birth once more
Her Sunday mansion, swept behind ears to grieve 
Each one’s lungs and hearts kept in glass
Served to beggars, to tramps, to gods at mass
You’ve invented yourself a daughter for the world to roam
Save me Sally, you slutty Sally, Praise me within your home
And upon her whistle reigned lips, there she began to grin
Daughter Sally paints, cries, and dies for all your sinsJesus Was A Blonde
