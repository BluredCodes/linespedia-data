"Glorious abductions"
An abstract prisoner once knew his way
As his "her" delays an immortal journey
Of knowledge wriggling in wine
With hands of blue children
And mother's of bastard clonings
Before you, lying broken and forgotten
A star in heaven
A truth hovering above museums
Lonely being, hemorrhaging with the flowers
Our pretty little fiend
Sending me into space...sending me
Drowsy, drunk in Eden
"Not an alien"...
You'll never be known
Thawing out your cynical babe
Despondent upon disorder
As your pearly sky melts crimson fear
I'll call out to you...
"I thought You Knew me well"?!
Our shy impulsive emperor
