---
title: "Butterfly"
writer: "Isaac"
slug: "butterfly"

I'm falling into place
among the rest 
lights and cameras
and sountrack start
clothes hang on the
shower rod
she never left

I'm falling into space
with you
I'm falling int the race
beside the rest
sink or swim
foolish boy

I'm falling into place 
among the best
current events
yet to happen
and time's passing by
and everything's
the way it should be
for once in a lifetime

I'm falling out of grace
and letting the water run
over my face
beautiful

I'm letting the days go by
i'm letting the water run hot
letting the girl be shy
letting the time go by

I think this might be love
but this might be hate
might be groove
groovy 
on the radio

Let me prove the way
I feel
let me fly
let me cry
watch my cocoon
protect me
until I hatch
into this world
kiss me
