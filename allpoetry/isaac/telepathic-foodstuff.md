everyone is beautiful 
in this place i've come
through a door 
the size of a little scrap of paper
the sky spins around me
the land still below
flying 
stopping falling 
i haven't come here to find
the meaning of life
i haven't come to find god
nor my little corner of life
all of the time i spent here
has gone to women 
and sex
and drugs
i'm falling
into vices other than you

-isaac-
