title: "Better things in life"

a society of outcasts
tailor made for destruction
loving the high
they get from 
drugs injected
sharing needles
risk of AIDS
gathering together
to leave the world
of physical things.
passed out on railroad tracks
in abandoned warehouses
or under bridges
shoplifting from Wal-Mart
to sell the goods
for money
for the next high
caught by the man
locked in a cage
drug treatment center
clean for awhile
relapse.
egressing from reality
embracing the high
moving on to better things.
O.D.
oh God
he’s dead
