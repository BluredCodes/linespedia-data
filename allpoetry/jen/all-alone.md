suddenly i turn around and there you arewandering behind me like a fly around a horse's asswhy must you follow with so much drivewhen will you leave me alonei want to be alone, all alonewhy can't you, won't you ever leave my side?am i your obsession?your only desire?will you ever leave me to sit alone and retire?when the wind stops blowing,the moon stops glowingthen you will leave meto sit in my room, all alonesit in my room all alone.
