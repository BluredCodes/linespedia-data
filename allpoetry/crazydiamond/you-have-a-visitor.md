If only those I love would come visit me here
In my peaceful sanitarium, treatment for my soul
Come say hello - just stop in for a bit!
Don't be scared.
Not exactly cozy, but this is my home
This is where my mind resides
No limits, everything and nothing
A paradox within myself
I find beauty in this perfect balance
Simple and complex-
Freedom at its best!
I can't help but feel lonesome at times
Sitting in my bright world 
With white and padded walls
A small window to the outside
Seeing people pass
"You Have A Visitor"
Words I long to hear.
