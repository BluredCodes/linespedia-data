---
title: "Turn Your Back"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "turn-your-back"

Look down on me like the whore that i am
Pretend to be my friend, but don't give me your hand
Run away from me, you have the right to be scared
Don't dare to admit the moments we've shared
Smile at me, but don't look into my eyes
Laugh with your friends as you all pass by
Be sure to close your eyes as i hold this gun to my head
Pretend you didn't know, and fake a tear as i lay dead.
