How many have you killed?
I've lost count, my dear
As I sit amongst these decaying bodies,
I wonder what their souls have to say
Their cold eyes stare at me day after day
As the number of victims constantly grows
The more I feed my desire to kill,
The more necessary it becomes
It's not so bad now-
The blood and the screams
Except for with the babies
No one wants to hear a child cry 
Silence them quickly
