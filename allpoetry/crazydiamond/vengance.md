Show me the axe with which you slaughtered my mother.
Show me the gun you shot through the heart of my lover.
How did it feel when you were raping my friend?
Where did you go when you left that bed, soaked in sin?
What did my father look like, as he lied in his blood, gasping for breath?
What did my sister say before you tied that rope around her neck?
What will run through your mind as i'm tearing you apart?
Any last words before i drive this knife through your heart?i'd like to find a different way to say this line:
Did you feel powerful when their fate was up to you?
