title: "To My Guardian Angel"

I just want to thank you, for everything you've done
When i needed help the most, you were the only one
The only one who had enough patience not to give up on me
The only one who could help me reach the goal 
That before i could only see
At the time i seemed ungrateful, but i promise this is true
I realize now, in the end, that i owe it all to you
Without you my dream was just a quiet bird with broken wings
But your love gave that bird the strength to fly and the confidence to sing
I couldn't begin to tell you all the sorrow that i feel, or the guilt that tears me up inside
Please forgive me for my regret is real, it something i cannot hid
Even when things were rough, you didn't give up on me
You're just the kind of person that i hope one day i will be
You will always have a special place in my heart
You are my guardian angel, and i've loved you from the start.

With love to my grandmother - Christmas 2000
