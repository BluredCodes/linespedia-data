Slit your wrists
But make sure you're alive to tell me about it tomorrow
Overdose to the brink of death,
But don't go over the edge, or you'll have no stories to tell
Share with me your many woes
But by all means, refuse to try and heal
Beautiful plan
Make me cry for you 
As you cry for yourself
Drag me into your derangement
But leave me there alone
Because, of course, 
You have to run away.
