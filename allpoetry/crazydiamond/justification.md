I killed a man today
The devil made me do it
I tried to resist evil,
But the power was far too strong
As the frightened man pleaded for his life
My heart told me to stop
But Satan had taken control of me now
"This is not me, this is the devil and his temptation,"
I kept telling myself before i pulled the trigger 
The sound of the firing gun deafened me 
But i still heard voices
Voices of the greatest evil
I was too weak
