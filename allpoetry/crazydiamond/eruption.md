title: "eruption"

my body craves your loving touch
my worries diminish
as you explore all that i am
the inevitable chills begin to envelope me
the soft touch of your lips
and the perfect movement of your tongue,
fantasy unfolding
with every thrust
you send me closer to the edge
with every kiss you eliminate my woes
i brace myself for ultimate ecstacy
my body turns to a catalyst for pleasure 
my nails dig into your strong and beautiful skin
and for a moment, the world is right
it is the epitome of physical exhilaration
perfect release
