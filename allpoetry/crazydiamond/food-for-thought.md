title: "Food For Thought"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "food-for-thought"

The natives, dark and grinning
content with life and thriving on expansion
no fear of society 
simple complexity,
beautiful nature, beautiful presence of THE SPIRIT
perfect connection
dancing around a fire- a ritual!
naked and free, their bodies and their lives
pure and safe from syndicated life
cactus and mushrooms,
food for thought!
