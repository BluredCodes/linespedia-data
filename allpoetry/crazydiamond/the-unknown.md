---
title: "The Unknown"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "the-unknown"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm walking through a tunnel of pure white light,
With the piercing sounds of my mind's eternal fight
Living in a dream world, not knowing where to go
Giving into my fears, I have forgotten all I know
Give me something to hold onto,
Show me something that is true
I look into the mirror, but don't recognize my face
The person in the glass is familiar with this place
I don't know where I am
I just want to go home
I try to escape but i can't 
Don't leave me all alone
My soul is being tortured
My heart is being slain
How can I conquer this land?
How can i end this pain?
