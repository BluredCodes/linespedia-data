Closed eyes, pretty painted smiles
Simple and immoble. . .ROBOTS!
Programmed for superior achievement!
A tight rope walker 
You must not falter- just make it to the other end
What is the reward?
A good name, power among your peers
STOP! Breathe! Take everything in
Look at what you've been missing!
Revel in it- the marvelous beauty
Take a break- be warmed by the sunlight, amazed by the moon
Let the brilliance of the stars fill your tired eyes
Feel every emotion and know your spirit
Embrace change, know love
Experience the gift.
