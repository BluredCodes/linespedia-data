title: "Run Away"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"

Into my darkest depths
Take a look. . .
What a surprise- you're running away
Goodbye, beautiful
Join the other perfect products of the machine
Don't feel too much
Stay safe, stay SANE
Who would want to live this way- on the verge of lunacy?
Strive not for truth,
But for the coveted portrait of perfection.
