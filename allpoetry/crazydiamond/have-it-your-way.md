---
title: "Have It Your Way"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "have-it-your-way"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25817-Have-It-Your-Way-by-CRaZyDiAmOnD"
---

You want to have control of me
Tie me to the bed
You like to cause me pain
Pull my hair
You think I'm too stubborn
Bend me over - backwards
You think I'm selfish
Watch me as I satisfy myself
You like to abuse me
Slap me across the face
You say I scream too much
Make me scream your name
You say I do not trust you
Let me take it all in
You say I have wandering eyes
Blindfold me and do with me what you will
You think I like to cause trouble
Slap the handcuffs on my wrists
You say that I'm ungrateful
Just to say thanks, I give myself to you
