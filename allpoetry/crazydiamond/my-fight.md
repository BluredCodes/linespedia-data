---
title: "My Fight"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"

My emotions cover me in a blanket of steel,
Unfamiliar and cold
Trying to break free, before i grow old
Before all i have learned is forever lost
I need to find a way out, but at what cost?
I know it must require a sacrifice, 
A penance toward the passage
Perhaps it is my constant searching
That brings me to such odds
I'm fighting a war, but what is the cause?
My mind, in constant conflict with my spirit
Im torn between two worlds
But through it all, my mission the same
To treat life as a journey, not as a game.
