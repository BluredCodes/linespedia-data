I'm hiding behind the bathroom door,
Hearing them drag my brother's body across the floor
I must hold back my mournfoul tears
I must be quiet, they don't know i'm here

One of them now stands in front of me as i quiver with fright
My heart fills with anger at the very sight
I'm frozen with fear and i can't make a sound
He calls to the other "Look who I found"

The next walks in and takes me by the hand
My body is now trembling, i can hardly stand
He takes me over to the bed, and has his way with me 
Then he stabs the knife into my neck so I can hardly breathe

Now they lay me down to sleep 
From my throat the blood will creep 
My dying body writhes in pain
Nothing left but my soul; i have been slain.
