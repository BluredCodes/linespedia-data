---
title: "Don't Sit Too Close"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "dont-sit-too-close"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25863-Dont-Sit-Too-Close-by-CRaZyDiAmOnD"

Beautiful, complex creatures
Capable of so much
The windows of their souls, 
Fixed on this magnificent box 
Bursting with synthetic light and sound
Run by thin, lifeless wires, 
Disconnecting them from their spirits
The Appeal of the marvelous machine.
