---
title: "You Will Not Hurt Me"
writer: "CRaZyDiAmOnD"
slug: "you-will-not-hurt-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25869-You-Will-Not-Hurt-Me-by-CRaZyDiAmOnD"
---

Beat me to the brink of death,
I will not feel a thing
Rape me of my innocence,
You will not hear me cry
Insult my very existence
With your cruel and ruthless words,
They will fall on deaf ears
But simply love me,
And forever you will be part of my soul
