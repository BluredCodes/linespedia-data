it breaks my heart in two when i look at you and see all the hate that died in mehas conjured up in youlike a blazing inferno of flamesit burns inside--the pain of being alivethe sorrow of love that falls like a dove from the sky deep down insidethere's a torment that wont subside withinkeeping you from ever loving again
