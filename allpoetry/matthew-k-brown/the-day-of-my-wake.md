title: "\"The Day Of My Wake\""

The prophets foreseen
this day would come
When the skies turn black
And all are one
As the world spins backwards
On an axis of blood
And all the living freeze
From the lack of sun
When the seas boil
And the Earth begins to quake
They warned you of today
The day of my wake
The divinity of my power
The things you never believed
And now you'll pay the price
And burn in hell with me2000,
