---
title: "\"Me X 3\""
writer: "Matthew K. Brown"
slug: "me-x-3"
source: "AllPoetry"

So you think I'm a gentle lamb,
Innocence is all you can see,
I'll let you believe that I'm that man,
Until you try to fuck with me.

I'm aware you're playing mind games,
I can smell them from a mile away,
And I'm wise to your deceitful eyes,
When they stare me in the face.

But you can't play a player,
'Cause I'll always win,
And your intentions to hurt me,
Return three folds in the mirror of sin.

So I force feed you the idea,
Of what it's like to be me,
Inject the venom,
All my pain times three.
I'll leave your emotions in stitches,
Allowing them to scar like mine,
And now you're just a number,
Forever until the end of time.
And this is what it feels like,
To be doped up on love,
Only to have your dreams shot down,
And still craving the drug.

Death, betrayal, and loss of love,
Have perfected the craft of my scars,
And I'll laugh in psychotic splendor,
When I toy with you and break your heart.

It's time you learn your lesson,
You'll get all that you deserve,
I've put you in just the right spot,
Face down in the dirt.

And I'll smile when you walk by,
Knowing that I've won,
I've raped you of your precious pride,
How does it feel to be outdone.

Hush little bitch now don't you cry,
This twisted genius will drain you dry.

And if you dare to doubt my words,
The one who played with fire is the one who got burned!2000,
