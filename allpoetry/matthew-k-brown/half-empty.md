---
title: "\"Half Empty...\""
writer: "Matthew K. Brown"
slug: "half-empty"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2830-Half-Empty...-by-Matthew-K.-Brown"
---

The glass was half full,
But then knocked to the floor,
Just another day that I wake up,
Wondering what I'm still breathing for.

All of my hopes,
Spilled across the ground,
Everything lost,
Not a damn thing found.

Your voice I once longed to hear,
Your face could not wait to be seen,
But now alone I stand in the mirror,
Looking back at the depressing me.

I loved you!
I trusted in you!
And like a fool I played the pawn.
Gave my heart away!
For your gift of pain!
Night promises broken by the dawn.

So now like a child I run,
But with no place to hide,
For the beauty that I gave in to,
Has left me half empty inside.

So tonight again I'll try to sleep,
Just to face another tomorrow,
And I'll remember yesterday,
As I spit at the with arms of open sorrow.

Your tears I now long to see,
My pain I want you to know,
Half empty my soul is bleeding,
But why for you I do not know.
