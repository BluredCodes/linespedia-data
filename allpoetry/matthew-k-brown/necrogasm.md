I still remember your face    When I slit your throat   Loving every screamWatching as you choked       With a sparkle in my eyeI watched as you died        Still hungry for your touch   Still wanting insideAs I strip your bloody corpse   I spread your legs so wide     Caressing every feature  Again I'm deep inside           No need to tie you down'Cause you can never leave     You're always there to love meAnytime I feel the need!!!NECROGASM!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
