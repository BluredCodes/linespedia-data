---
title: "\"Words After Death\""
writer: "Matthew K. Brown"
slug: "words-after-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

Taking time out from the heavens above
To say goodbye to the ones I love
I didn't want to leave, I didn't want to go
Why I was taken, I guess we'll never know

But Father don't mourn
And please don't cry
Look into the mirror
My image is in your eyes

As you look around this world, notice and see
That now I'm apart of every little thing
From the house in which I lived, to the sun's golden light
I'm apart of everything, even the moon and stars at night

But Mother don't mourn
And please don't cry
Look into the mirror
My image is in your eyes

Taking time out from the heavens above
To give this message to the ones I love
I didn't mean to leave, but this is not the end
