So I’ve come to find myself here again
Cast in stone and pearls aside in silence
All these simple wishes left in hopelessness
Emptied from the lives I wish I’d led

Nothing left but love and death on my mind
So I must write the saddest poem I can tonight
Loss of all this reason forged in twilight
To wish for paths I might have gone in my lifeA poem with a meaning I'd rather not talk about.  I think I'd prefer your interpretations.  In fact, do that for all of my poems.  I command you!
