held this life crooked
against trees tall, grasses slight
beings unbent to mineIt's all haiku-y.  SweetSadness posted it under her name, but she can delete it now.  If she wants to.  I guess.  This one's shorter than my others, but it's supposed to be that way.  I think.  Waking up is fun.
