cigarette ashes and stillborn eyes
shadows beneath my poetic heart 
my soul to hers and hers to mine; all these
stars between you and ISimple, short... just something I wrote on a whim and kept.  By the way, Sweet, this is how you spell 'cigarette' *points up*

The only reason I get to annoy her is because I actually know her.
