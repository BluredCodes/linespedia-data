I left my shoes in the sand
And the prints that they leave
Are my soul’s left-handed artistry

I left my shoes in the sand
And the life I left with them
Is but a flawed portrait of meThe last poem I'm posting.... TONIGHT.  SweetSadness helped me out on that final line.  It used to be pretty crappy, now it's good.  It's short again!  I'm still tired.
