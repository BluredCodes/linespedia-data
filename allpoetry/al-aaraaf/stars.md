you drink a little wine, hello my sweet angel
drunk as i may be, i remember your face
your smell, your following eyes
o and maybe tonight mine could meet yours?
i dream your life and you dream mine
we dance on stars and fall downward
o where does all this lead?
‘we’re in love… it’s all you need to know’
but what do i know?
you, in a dress of angels
scream mourning and open before me
dead on the shore... it's another one of my weird ones.  I have no freaking idea what it's about, and I think it ends too quickly.  So I hate it.

Geez, I must listen to My Bloody Valentine nonstop when I write things like this.
