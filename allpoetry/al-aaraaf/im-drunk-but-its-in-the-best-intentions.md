O there’s always a light at the end
But that’s good fortune for everyone lost
For I’m as dead as Thomas or Plath
And I can barely find the way back

I hold up these thoughts in my mind
But it’s my mind that’s held up my thoughts
For I’m as drunk as those stealing a laugh
