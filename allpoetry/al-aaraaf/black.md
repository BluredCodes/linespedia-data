Sailing on my early seas of perfect undone whispered leaves
I somedays hope to find you here; waiting hopefully for me
And other days I’d bleed my heart out ever so to see
Past all these dead impatient waiting burning anarchies
Black; but I now hope to look past what I’d rather see
