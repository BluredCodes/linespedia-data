Love
Try my best
Say something worthwhile
Be someone worthwhile
Understand people
Calm down
Silence
Buy a beautiful house and die in it
Be happy
Something
Ask questions to my answerphone
Nothing
Find purpose
Drink (today)
Go away
Work for something
Remember good things
Stab an angel
Forget bad things
Cry with emotion
Define an end
Find definition within ending
Walk a highway differently
(at night)
Live my life as it is
CHANGE
Hope for a better day
Decide
Love more and more and more everyday
And begin to understand life
Something I shall find from eachJust a nice little list.
