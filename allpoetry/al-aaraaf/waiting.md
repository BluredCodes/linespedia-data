waiting.  a petty proposition fulfilled
as a resistance to think
or a halting of emotion
and there is NOTHINGYou get a cookie if you guess the meaning of this one.  Actually, I'm not giving out cookies.  They're imaginary, but you could always imagine it's good.  I wrote this during a very bad day.... I'm very tired.
