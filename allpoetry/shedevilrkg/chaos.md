Chaos
what if the world
was run by utter chaos
what if everything we
touched
saw
thought
wasn't really there
just a figment
of our imagination
What if nothing
could be trusted
fear would rule your
thoughts
dreams
life
like nothing else could
every move you'd make
would be based on fear
What if you could no longer
just be yourself
everything you
think
say
feel
would be run by society
and the quest for true freedom
would be forgotten
Looks like our world
is already being run
by complete and utter
Chaos
