From forth the crowded gates of hell
The darkness bleeds from souls of lost
The bodies of innocents that once fell
Not knowing what their lives would cost
An end to pain sounds so sweet
As fears will slowly fade
Give me one fateful chance to meet
The freeing silver of the blade
I'll draw the knife across my wrists
And watch the blood flow down
Then death will touch me with its kiss
And bring me crashing to the ground
Beneath the bloodied earth I'll lie
As demons haunt my hopeless soul
All this because I chose to die
To play my final role
