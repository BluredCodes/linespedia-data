Black rose
with wilting petals
stained by the burden of life
filled with hatred
burning with anger
kept hidden until now
breaking through
showing the world
deep, dark fears.
Black rose
petals slowly falling
as burdens follow
watch them burn
pain melts them away
overflowing with hatred
slipping away with
nothing more to fear
as the end comes near.
