title: "Dread"

The sea is filled
with peoples' tears
churning and burning
with peoples' fears
They twist and fall
down to the ground
soaking the earth
making no sound
Silently falling
from the sky
puddles are forming;
pools of lies
Quietly watching
the sky turns red
showing the future
when souls are dead
Not knowing where
our graves will lie
we dread the day
that we will die
Forever fearing
the sun's warm rays
we hide again
and there we stay
