---
title: "Just A Dream"
writer: "shedevilrkg"

Did you ever think
that maybe life is 
just 
    a 
     dream?
That you are dreaming 
about your past,
Or someone else is
dreaming
    about
       you...
Maybe we are all
just a figment of
someone
    else's
       imagination,
And everything that's
happening to us now has
already
    happened
       before.
So who's to say
we made a wrong choice?
Maybe, if life is
just a dream,
we made the right choice
in
  real
      life.
