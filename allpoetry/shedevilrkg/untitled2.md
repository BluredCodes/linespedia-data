A fallen angel
The silent sufferer
Holding the burden
As she travels alone
A hidden fear
The hungry serpent
Overriding everything
In its cold, black path
A dark demon
The pain causer
Lasting the eternity
With death the only escape
A silver knife
The flashing steel
Biting into the flesh
Of the unknowing
A lone tear
The one indication
Showing only a portion
Of the never-ending pain
A blood drop
The life warmth
Flowing out of the innocent
To lay upon the ground
A peaceful death
The dreamless sleep
Protecting the fallen
From the demons of fear
A black flame
The soul burner
Giving all who come
