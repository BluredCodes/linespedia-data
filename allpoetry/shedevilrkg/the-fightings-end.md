---
title: "The Fighting's End"
writer: "shedevilrkg"
slug: "the-fightings-end"
source: "AllPoetry"

The darkness burns, with endless light
Hiding the truth; veiling sight
However strong, none can breach
The door to souls, none can reach
The sea's in turmoil; peoples' tears
Wishing an end, to all the years
The fighting's end; soon to come
All will smile, when it's done

And if the fight, none will cease, my hopes and dreams shall then decease
And if none know 'bout the fight 'cept me, I'll let them know' so they can see
Won't hide the truth, from veiled eyes, for it may cause the fated demise
Forever I will continue to stand, still fighting, in this dreary land

And if the earth, begins to shake
Buildings falling, from the quake
Then I shall wait, until the end
If I know that you are there, my friend
And even if, the darkest storm
Will not cease, forever mourns
There I will stand, by your side
Willing to give up the fight, my life

And if the fight, none will cease, my hopes and dreams shall then decease
And if none know 'bout the fight 'cept me, I'll let them know' so they can see
Won't hide the truth, from veiled eyes, for it may cause the fated demise
Forever I will continue to stand, still fighting, in this dreary land

And if you try, to stop my fall
To stop the fighting's end, I shall
Hold you up, and help you stand
To the end, in this pitch black land
My hopeless soul, shall not be taken
From this land, that's now forsaken
Waiting, for my soul to mend
Forever fighting, till the end

And if the fight, none will cease, my hopes and dreams shall then decease
And if none know 'bout the fight 'cept me, I'll let them know' so they can see
Won't hide the truth, from veiled eyes, for it may cause the fated demise
Forever I will continue to stand, still fighting, in this dreary land
