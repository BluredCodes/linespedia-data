title: "Blood"

The blood is falling
on the floor
ever faster
ever more.
See it fall
ever faster
witnessing
this true disaster.
It slowly slides
onto the ground
a sea of red
is all around.
Ever falling
twisting, turning
ever slowly
watch it burning.
What once was there
now is gone
smiling, now
the job is done.
Surrounded by
a sea of red
the life is gone
her soul is dead.more dark stuff..
