---
title: "Like A Tiger"
writer: "shedevilrkg"

The anger inside me
is bounded
caged like a tiger
with its claws
unable to use them
against the steel
of the bars.
I stay locked
in chains
waiting
to be freed
but I know
I will have to
free myself
and my claws
are useless
against the steel
of the bars.
