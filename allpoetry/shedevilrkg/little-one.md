Hello, Little One.
You, who feels my pain.
I see you crying
showing yourself
to the world.
You question
whether or not
things will change.
I will tell you this:
If you want them to,
they will.
You can make them.
I see you crying, Little One.
How lucky you are.
Able to free yourself
from this world
that is determined
to hold you down.
Don't give up, Little One.
And do me a favor,
cry for me.
