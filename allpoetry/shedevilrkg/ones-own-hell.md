title: "One's Own Hell"
writer: "shedevilrkg"

In black hell they burnUnder dark light so pureKnowing not their cold fateAs they hope to be curedBut one small respiteShall never be theirsFor this is their hellWhere not one soul now caresTheir fate has been sealedBy a choice they once madeFor they once fell victimTo the sweet song of the bladeThe fury of fireBurns sweet in their mindsFor they know naughtOf the evil they shall findThey will never be forgivenFor their black mistakeThe dark demons now riseThere are new souls to takeSouls that were once ownedHave found a new placeThey circle aroundMore danger to faceThe earth gives a shudderOf both pain and fearThe black clouds aboveShed one lonely tearNow destined to wander;To travel the earthThey try not to succumbTo the dark demons' mirth
