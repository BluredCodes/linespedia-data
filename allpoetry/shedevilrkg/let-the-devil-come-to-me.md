Let the devil come to me
Let him bring his black reward
And embrace me in the never-ending sleep that I adore

Let him bring me that cold blade
Let him give me now to Death
And bring me down to hell's dark gates with that sweet and promised breath

Let him take me to his lair
Let him lie me in the depths
Where I'll wait in hopeless dreams for that final soft caress

Only he provides the end
Only he brings death so near
Only he can douse, with flames so hot, my pain throughout the years
