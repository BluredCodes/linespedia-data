title: "Room to Fly"

One wing left
No room to fly
Locked in a cage
Waiting to die
One last breath
Escapes her soul
The play has ended;
The final role
No more acting
No false words
No more lies
Which once were heard
Closing her eyes
She dreams once again
Waiting to come
The one, final end
After that is quiet
As she dreams of the sky
Once again she is free
There's now room to fly
