You hear everything
and nothing
on the outside.
The silent songs of hope
are lost to you
leaving you alone
with the pangs of failure.
The blissful innocence of dreams
is shot down by the reality
of the pictures you see.
Unguarded, unnoticed, uncared for.
Surrounded by the voices of the masses
you struggle to breathe
trying to get just one word
to come out of your tired throat
if only to announce to the world
that you exist.
