Life isn't made up
of what we want it to be.
The dream world
we construct for ourselves
only shelters us from the truth.
We see only what we want to see;
hear only what we want to hear.
We hide ourselves from the nightmare
of the real world.
But what if our eyes
were suddenly opened?
Could we accept this new reality
after years of telling ourselves
it couldn't be true?
If our little dream world
was suddenly shattered
what would we do?
