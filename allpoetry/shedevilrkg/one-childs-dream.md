---
title: "One Child's Dream"
writer: "shedevilrkg"
slug: "one-childs-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

If she goes for her friend, the dark cold knife
And leaves in the dust this world of pain
Would she regret this thing now lost; her life
That could not be taken back or re-gained?
So is this a good goal for which to strife?
To go against her mind where it's ingrained?

It's true that all her life was based on lies
And she can't leave her dark pain in the dust
And tears keep flowing as the young girl cries
The swirling tempest makes her soul now rust
If she could live on she would choose to fly
But then again, she would die if she must

So hoping that she could dream once again
She wonders how long she'd possibly last
Without the knife, her one and only friend
Perhaps this life of hers would move by fast
So she thinks once again of the cold end
Looks to the bright moon and dreams of her past

And thinks back to times where there were no fears
When she could dream like a child at night
And her lone pillow had tasted no tears
To a time when her parents did not fight
And puts back her friend from over the years
Hoping that her dreams will again take flight
