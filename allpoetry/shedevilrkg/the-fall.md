---
title: "The Fall"
writer: "shedevilrkg"

Toward the pitch black earth she falls
Hitting the lifeless ground that's
Eternally stained with others' blood.

Death is the only
Escape possible for her who's
Already dead inside, only
The outside is left of
Her soul, once full of life.

Once she believed she could go on, but now there is only the
Fall, and the end to everything.

Another shall follow her, keeping the
Never-ending pattern in check.

Under the black sun of her hell where nothing is
Forgiven, she waits in silence
Overflowing with the pain of years
Ready to give everything up, and
Go to her place of dreams
In which demons cannot haunt her. It's
Very dark where she is, hidden in
Eternal misty shadows that twist and turn
Never keeping the same form.

She knows there is
Only one way to go
Under a sky where no black sun
Lights up the horizon with its eerie light.

Always more demons around every corner
Never setting her free, with
Death's sweet song her only escape.

The silver knife flashes as the blood
Hot to the touch, slides down her pale wrists
Ever so slowly.

Fall, little one, fall
And dream of a place where you can
Live without the constant fear, without
Lies from those you trusted

Over her arms, it drops to the
Floor, falling like she soon shall fall.

Only the pain remains, a cold memory that she'll
Never have to face again
Everything here shall end.

Unknowingly she seals her fate
Nothing can stop her now as the warm blood
Drips down to the cold ground
Eventually covering the earth, and
Staining it with the pain of an
Eternity.  Soon there will be no more
Reminders of her pain, which before shook the boundaries of her
Very existence, leaving her
In the eternal torment of the fires of her hell
No one can save her, for she knows only of the
God of pain, who will forever continue to destroy.

At last it's over
Nothing more can she feel, as she
Goes up to her place of light
Ending what those before her had started
Leaving the world behind as she soars up above.
