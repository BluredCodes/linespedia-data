Away from dreams and other far-off lands,
Left in darkness without that shining light,
My life seems like it rests in others' hands
While I desperately try to mend my plight.
It seems as though there's no forgiveness now;
I'm left alone to travel well-worn paths.
With no friends to help, I start to wonder how
I'll make it through this hateful world's wrath.
Many tears were shed before I could see
Past cruel memories and false desires,
Soon I'll understand the meaning of "free"
And rip away from overwhelming fires.
When that day comes I'll realize true joy
'Cause I'll no longer be some person's toy.
