Endless possibility
Turning toward calamity
Losing the tenacity
To keep our promised liberty
That is actually a rarity
Stemming from our vanity
And the fear to break conformity.
What happened to stability?
Replaced by animosity
Toward all in the community
As we try to gain seniority.
Gone also is integrity;
How'd we lose our credibility?
Is it really such a scarcity
Replaced also by hostility?
But there's no plausibility,
And certainly no validity,
In explaining vulnerability
By weeping for fragility
And forgotten liability;
Slowly growing in intensity
By quickly passing geniality.
