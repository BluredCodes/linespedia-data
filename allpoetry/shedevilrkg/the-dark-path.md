I walk about in darkness
while things fall all around me
but I am lost in dreams
where I am no longer haunted
I see none of what is happening
outside my path
I see only the road ahead
long and hard
threatening to break me
at any moment
and all I can think of
is moving ahead
for I know that if I stop
I'll never be able to start again
there I will stay
fallen and still
forever waiting a chance to rise.
