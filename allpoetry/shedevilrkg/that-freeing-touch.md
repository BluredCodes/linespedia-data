title: "That Freeing Touch"

Don't ask me why
It relieves the pain
In my tortured mind
With its twisted dreams.
I couldn't tell you why
It eases my broken heart
And allows me to forget
Those memories that haunt me.
But the feel of that cold blade
Brings me up from the depths
And allows me to breathe 
That refreshing air once again.
Don't ask me why
It feels so good
And lessens the ache 
In my wretched soul.
I couldn't tell you why
It pushes back all other thoughts
Which lost their strength
At the razor's cool touch.
It scares me as it frees me
For who knows when that time will come
When I'll make a mistake
And cut just a little too deep...
