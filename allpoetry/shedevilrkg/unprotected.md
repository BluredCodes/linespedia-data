Dear Protector,
why dost thou flee
and hide away from me?
Top mountains high
and canyons deep;
what does my guardian see?
I'm not the best,
I tried to be;
I'm seeing clearer now.
But sweet protector,
please don't leave me;
I'll change myself somehow.
Blood still flows
from my scarred wrists;
I love the razor's touch.
Try as you may
you can't shelter me;
it doesn't hurt too much.
My mind still wanders
'twixt nightmares dark;
I'm lost within the maze.
Please, on please
try to forgive me;
relieve me from this haze.
