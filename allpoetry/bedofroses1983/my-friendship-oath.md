My heart is openso please come on inIf you choose to do soI promise to try and be a good friendyou can tell your secrets to meand secrets they will stayI will always be there for you every step of the wayWhenever you have a problemwhatever the problem may bewe can try and work it out together,you don't even have to ask meI will always be there, thru thick and thru thinI will be there when you are going thru Hellas we all do now and thenI will be a loyal friendas hopefully you can tellAnd I hope your trust in meshould never have cause to fail.
