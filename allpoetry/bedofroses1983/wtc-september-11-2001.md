So many smiles never again to be seen.So much laughter never again to be heard.So many lives that can no longer be lived.So quickly they were taken without a hint or word.Innocence lost buried under the stone.To all the families grieving, you are not feeling the pain alone.Tears, prayers, hope and love are shared by one and all.Try and remember that they are now angels.Those who took the fall.As our souls are full of pain, and our hearts are heavy with sorrow, Remember that the lives that have passed today will be that of new lives tomorrow.Shannon Smith September 12, 2001I wrote this on September 12, 2001
