A princess in law, by being wed to a prince.She has become so much more to everyone since.She was always ordinary, except for a few things.For one she had a heart that would make angels sing.She often thought of others and what she could do, she was and is the 'People's Princess' Through and Through.For anyone who doesn't know who this poem is about...It is about Princess Diana, without a doubt.
