I've been loved all my life,I am gratful for this.But yet I grow impatient for love's first kiss.My family has stood by me, all hand in hand.Yet still,I wait for the day to wear a golden wedding band.My family does love me, and I too, love them.But, still here I stand,waiting for HIMI dream, I hope, I prayto meet my true love right away.Whether we should meet, in a country store or a crowded street,It would be oh, so sweet.To be able to hug him and hear his heart beatwith excitement and joy,I would be his girland he would be my boy.
