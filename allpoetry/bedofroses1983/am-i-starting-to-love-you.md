title: "Am I Starting To Love You?"
writer: "BedOfRoses1983"

When we first met we never even spoke.
We both avoided paths until you were telling someone else a joke.
I overheard, burst out in laughter,
we slowly started talking from that moment there-after.
we found out we were opposites, like night is to day.
Yet it worked out for the good, in it's own unique way.
I thought that pals are what we are and what we would stay...
yet I cannot wait to see you every single day.
Everyone says we would make a good pair....
But I don't want to lose what we have, I don't even dare.
Maybe someday...we will build up the nerve to try...
Besides I don't love you...( God, I don't even know how to lie...!!)
