Do you love her?
Or is she just a fling?
Will her cherish her?
Or to you is she just a thing?
You tell her what to wear,
what to say and where to go...
While she continues to love you 
with her heart and her soul.
You get jealous at the tiniest thing....
Don't you see she loves you? 
She wants to wear your ring.
She is so scared to lose you...
she has given you her heart...
Don't take that honor lightly...
don't tear it all apart.
Give her some freedom...
and some room to grow...
You will see her love grow stronger...
If you'll only let her glow.I wrote this about my best friend. Her boyfriend is starting to smother her. He works with her, drives her everywhere and disapproves if she hangs out with anyone else but him...
