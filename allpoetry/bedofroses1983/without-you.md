---
title: "Without You"
writer: "BedOfRoses1983"

At loves first glance,
for you I have fell.

To be in Heaven without you,
For me it would be pure Hell.

Without the moisture of your tender lips,
Or the soft caress of your hands.

For me it would be like walking,
in the midsts of sinking sands.

A book with no words,
A bird with no song.

I can't live without you,
as I've known all along.

I've put on to paper, 
what I feel in my heart.

But to say this to your face,
That's the hardest part.

So read what I have written,
and after you are through,

Please come and tell me,
if you feel this way too.
