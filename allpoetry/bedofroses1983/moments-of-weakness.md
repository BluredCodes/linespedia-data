There is a guy that I know.
I see him almost every day.

My heart churns to think of him.
I love him in every way.

I know it will never be,
for he is already taken,
Plus he doesn't love me.

So I keep it all inside
and I let no one know.

I love him with all my heart.
I just hope that it doesn't show.2000,
