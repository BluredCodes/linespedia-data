Will I ever find love?
Will I ever fly high as the dove?
These are the things I wonder.
In My Sleep, In my slumber.
Will I ever be pretty? Will I ever be thin?
These are the things I ask myself,
again and again and again.
Will I ever be graceful, like a cat?
Or am I destined to always be fat?
I guess only time will tell,
If I pass, or if I fail. 
but for now I am forced to wait,
To learn of my decided fate.
