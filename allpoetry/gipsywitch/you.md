Like the wind your words are softly whispered to my ears,and your hands walk slowly and tender like the petals of a rose through the valley of my sex and the mystery of the night said that your eyes are going deeper into the secrets of my skin, without saying it to you I could clearly see that your love is like a hurricane that really makes me spin.
