My mind makes me more crazy since the day we sadi good bye, it was the best night of my life and i didn't want it to die,I don't have a clue if you felt the same way,I'm sad you're far away but I am sure I will see you again cause there are more things I wanna say.

A call confirmed the frienship,I knew this was going to grow,now call after call I like you and I'm not thinking of letting you go,Know that in our heart an island of rythim is more than hot, the vains are full with pleasure,passion and amor.

Romantic Romeo don't wake me up if I'm sleeping, let me dream with this white rose in my hands,it was given to me by somebody I hardly know and understand but I'm begining to want him,cause he's a real man,the thing would be if he like me so we can entone a jam.

God,thank's for letting me, meet this wonderfullguy,I've meet so many wrong ones before but this one is one of a kind, please don't let it end make the time slow,so this handsome prince I've found,finds a reason to stay.

Oh!,enchanted spells of all king and queens of dusks and dawns, make then long, make them fun, juice of lemon salts of sea make him just the one for me or let it be as good as can be for this is a one and only thing I don't want to swim in no more seas, listen baby this one is for you,get rid of bad times and let the good ones throu.
