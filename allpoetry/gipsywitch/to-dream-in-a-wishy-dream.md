This life is just one, every moment counts, first I was so lonely  but now the sadness is gone cause I got you in my days, to remember how nice is to have a friend and sometimes feel loved, I'm gratefull to GOD that you came and took my sorrow away.

How deep is the ocean, we'll never know but your words were deep enough to dive in my sea and who knows how far will they go, a friend goes deep in the skin giving aleve to all the akes, something clean, a feeling happiness, a reason to smile, somehting so pure as the wind that runs free into your hair while you're in the open nature,thats my frienship , are you here to stay and take?...

Mysteries suround our minds, our thinking minds, our dreamy souls, to be free, to fly away, we all learn from our mistakes, weak we are to the powerfull arms of destiny somehow, take charge, to be strong, how fragile are our hearts to pain, to endless love, to pray in our nights, to hope everything will be alright, are we or are we not, greatfull for having health, having a home, gratefull for having or finding someone that near or far, feels for you or with ya.

Gratefull I am to the all greatcious GOD for giving me a reason to live,a body to lend in which i try to rule and battle all evil in this earth, to wake up and smile to the shinny sun, to stay up late and wish upon a beutifull flying wishing star but a space besides all this is there, here in the very centered corner of my beating heart , a piece is missing in this puzzle, in this warior princess soul, a soft touch , a whispering  voice told me, a soft tenderness in my ears, Lucky petals of a rose!, Is it going from the sky to fall?, " No, you have to search for the truth" , ok , I got it !, I did it !, " Baby it's... you ! 


                              Vimari Santiago
