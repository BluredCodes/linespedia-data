The days will end as the hours pass by but you won't forget the day we saw eachother cause it was a shinny july with a beautifull blue sky.

It was love at first sight or maybe a strange romantic chemistry hat got us together, the thing is now if we'll last forever,even if you go away,when ever the sun goes down into the sea, you will remember this and one of your precious memories will be the first time you kissed me under that crazy old tree.
