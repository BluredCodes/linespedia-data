My heart would be so lucky to have a love like yours,no matter if I am sleeping I always think of you,are you really my romeo? or the joke it's on me,my heart is beating,is the answer coming yet?,where are you thy romeo,please find the way again,so we'll be happy for ever and never ever again feel this pain.

Impresive how life would show me,there's someone waiting for you!,even thou it's on the distance and we don't know if it's true,there's always a little angel waiting out for you his name is michael if you saw him,what would you do?,I'll thank the heavens above for giving me a sweet love,to share sometimes by a letter or just a simple note,until one day we meet eachother and love will conquer all.

Control on the computers!hey they are making our lives,they are mechanical toys,I hope they don't tell lies cause if they do,It'll be devastating,I won't be able then to find you and no that I have found you thy romeo I don't want to loose your touch, is this faith or is this a light of love,my heart will try to find the answer praying to heaven,oh God!

Hero you are of me,you have rescued my soul,you have make me smile again with that tender sweet love of yours,I have never seen you,so yes blind love that's right,sometimes it's worth a risk,you'll never know whats it,till one day out of no where touches your soul making it weak and weak,with a loving pure emotion,that makes you loose all time motion,to loose all control.

Always chose a path in your life,that's what I say but sometimes one who gives advice is the one who needs it the most,it's funny how life is,we never know what to do until it happens to us,until we go throu things,until by flesh and blood we see the real meaning of what this crazy thing called love is capable of.

Every once in a while we all dream how life would be with the ideal mate,how wonderful it would be to have it all,yes true we all cry at times but it's all worth a try,to find that soul we look for to live and to die for,destinyis unpredictable you'll be surprised what's on your way and I am grateful that I found you,I just hope you feel the same...

Love what a small word,for so many reasons to be,so many amazing feelings at one blink,love oh! that crazy love,if love was to be death i want to die in your arms,if love is a heaven to be then i want to fly away with thee,if thy love is to be a rose I would like to be the garden you plant it in,if thy love was to be the air that will let me live,I want to inhale you every second of my life with you to be.
