The taste of you makes me wonder through my skin,secret desires,that your words awake in me,to satisfy every inch of your body,make a river of internal hot juicesfloating on our sweaty naked boddies.To give you all you have ever dreamed of,latin hot fantasiesare moving the ground,you have awakened the she-devil in me,eat me,let your passionate tongue burn on the darkest corners of my soul.Take a bow,on your knees,I wanna hear you beg.I am that silence panther on your way,I watch every move but I will make sure I put you down,lay you down make you all mine feel you moaning my name out loud,begging me to do not stop.Make me yours in a waterfall even on the kitchen floor,put me all against the corners,make this nest our loving death,get this passion out make our boddies take the test,lets do all imposible possitions,come on baby don't delay!,lets devore our lips with a breath taking kisses seduce eachother and never end this amazing love making.i don't know what came over me , :)
