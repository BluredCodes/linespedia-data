The poetry from your lips showed me the way it is but I never thought that you would've spell me like this .Sweet talk, smooth and silky as the honey, that runs down fastly and pure like a bunny.But  whats the mystery in your eyes that i cannot discover, tell me I wanna know, constantly silence and poor sad looks too.My heart is falling in love and I don't dare say it to yousome poems of my have unique style in writing it's part of the nature in its  text..
