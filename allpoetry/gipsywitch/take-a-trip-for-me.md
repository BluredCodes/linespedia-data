He didn't understand,not even my tears , he was so dummy , i think more than a deer.
I told him, what was wrong but he kept on asking, what a damn moron.Knowing what his attitude was, I fallowed like an idiot his flote.
Now the jerk is with another , but i don't care someone will come more over.Thanks for all the memories, I'll always reasure them no matter where I go.
But next time you come to ruin my life ,and destroy my happines please baby let me know ,because i wont take this bullshit anymore..its old what can i say <<<
