While the time pass ,here in my room,I'm just thingking about your love,how beautifull it used to be walking around the halls of the school,laughing,crying,fighting and then making up with just a simple kiss or a tender hug but now those days are gone, couse I've graduated my boy,you my reason for living ,you were my dreams and my fantasies too.Why you left me,why changed me,it was a sincere frienship ,you had no meaning for it.Meanwhile after some time, my telephone rang,it was strange,it was amazing,some crazy,wild too,I couldn't believe that voice my highschool love it was you,asking for another chance,I said I'm sorry I've founfa very gentleman,my lve for you has fall somewhere beetween dusk and done and if you try to find it,I think you'll never win,it was pats of a farytale and it's got to an end whispering my words to the stars, while the moon was watching me, singing sad songs with her face all white and pale.
