Love,love is like the sea it's always there and it's always strange different,it's like a big huricane,blows my life away with him,that tides me like a rock to the beach sand cause the winds are so strong that no one can fallow the sun after the rain,cause when the love is over,it always leaves you a big pain.

It's bad and black like the night,a huge white bright wishing star,that flies lonely throu the sky, love is a stallion,a wolf that tears apart my pour heart,a vampire that sucks the blood that gives me life,out of my vains,love is the great black hole in the middle of no where,that someday we're all going to cross because love is the energy that no fearful mind can resist to escape,so when it gets to you recieve the call.
