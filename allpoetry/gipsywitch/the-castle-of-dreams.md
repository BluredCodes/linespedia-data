When is he coming to me,when would I feel his arms around me, when could I hear those words,I wan't that person to say when would I stop dreaming and make of those dreams a day , a reality.

Life is short, even if there are sometimes that it's endless, there are so many things that I would like to be and I don't find with which one to begin , with who should I talk to, who can I trust but the best person to be with it's with yourslef and not alone.

I've always been in my little castle, in my world of fantasies and dreams ,I could never live the reality, that's based on greedy,wars ,unhappines, I want to be in my cloud of dreams where I wait for my chnaces of finding my real prince and live for ever in harmony as king and queen.how does the rank thing work here?
