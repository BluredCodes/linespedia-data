Every time I'm without you,it feels like the day will never end.The birds don't sing like they used to when you were here.So you can imaginehow sad I am.Thats why when ever you feel lonely don't ever bring out your tears, just remember that I love you and that you'll always be my dear,I will wait with no fear,cause you are going to come back and if you don't keep your promise I'll make sure,to find you and with no doubts I'll atack you with my heart and with no fears.short but its my mind.
