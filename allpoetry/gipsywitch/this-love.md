Im all alone, no where to run, i try to get out
             but i cant, I see the corners of the house and

             and you're not here.I wish I knew the answer why 
             your love is just not near.I wonder why i have to 
             hear you and don't feel your body close to mine, 
             I wish that you were near .

             My love, im holding forever,the hope of having you,
             to have you near,I will be strong,I'll wait i have 
             no fear,but my love i miss you,I want you near.
             Constantly we have shared our smiles,our thoughts 
             but it aint enough, I want you near, is this vain 
             trying to reach a love that seems so far, but i 
             wont stop I want you near.

             When i sleep at night,I wish for a star to take me 
             there,where the skies are blue and the mountains 
             green, to go in my dreams,even over the clear sea, 
             many miles away from here, even in my dreams just 
             to have you near.

             I try to read,I sing a song, joke with my friends
