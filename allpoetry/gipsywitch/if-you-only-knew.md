---
title: "If you only knew"
writer: "Gipsywitch"
slug: "if-you-only-knew"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15292-If-you-only-knew-by-Gipsywitch"
---

I see throu the eyes of you,oh those eyes as bright as the sun,full of life but all alone,wish I was the one who made them alive ,to give them just a smile,to make them realize that love isn't lost that it has just begon.

You are the child in my eyes,the moon that makes the night,the breez the combs my hair,the truth that never swears,that siluet I adore when ever we are alone,anytime I want you near I close my eyes and you are here.

Enchanted man from far away won't you say that you are my dear,that wild stallion who rides me until I am full of joy tears,who makes me breath so deep,I sink in an ocean full of your passion,can someone save me if it's not you,give me your thoughts,give your dreams and I will love you endlesly.

Oh blue sky now that you reach him when the morning comes along,tell him,yes,how much I love him before this day is gone and when this dusk becomes as clear as life,bless him always,Oh where is he?,Oh he's here in my arms.Not all that you see , is what you you see.
