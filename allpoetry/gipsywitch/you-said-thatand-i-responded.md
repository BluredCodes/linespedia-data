In what time could I say I love you?, In what day could I say I dare?,In what hour can we be together?, Im what mind, could we ever share?.One thing is to say I love you, to mean it, well is different there, to a dark night can we dare to share, our passion, our boddies hitting of fire cause you held me and we went there, to that hidden place that we're all dreaming, to that loving wonder of living hell, where the evil doesn't know wrong, where throu the angels peace doesn't flow, have you heard where I'm landing, did you know where I've gone, a mortal of dreams and hopes, what's for sure, outstanding feeling the one of all, do not share it with everybody it is so precious and it's so rare that if you miss it, you'll never find it anywhere.
