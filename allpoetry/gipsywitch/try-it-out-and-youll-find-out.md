Near you the fire inside of me burn, a red hot passion wan't toget out,I try to control my desires but your temptations are too strong.I wanna have you with me here and not so far away, it has only been hours and baby I can't wait,I wish you were my man and not a friend,I wanna be with you for ever and never get to an end,good and bad days oh! baby we all have them,I'll be with you throu all the worsttimes,you will forget all those bad girls,I will treat you good and always with respect just give me the oportunity cause i know you won't regret it and I asure it will be worht it,pure and strictly perfect.
