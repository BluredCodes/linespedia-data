title: "Each Day Apart"
writer: "Gipsywitch"

Knowing that you're not here with me,makes me realize hot it feels to be deadand every minute that passes by I think of sixty different way of beign sad.

Knowing that you're not here,makes the rain come down very slow as my tears like a whisper fall,the sunses are eternal and the night does not have stars, it's hot,not for beign in heaven and it's gray like to be in mars.

Knowing that you're not here with me,I know that each afternoon you sit in your porch looking at other pretty girls and wondering if they look like me but I will tell you something I'll never do the same,cause a woman in love only needs one man,just for love and honor and for a litle respect, when yuo have a problem put them on the table and talk them out or even on a desk.
