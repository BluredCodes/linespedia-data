title: "To all the victims on these days..."

God gave us life,he made us be,it is sad to see that life has ended in such a cruel way,without asking permission from all those who today cry in sorrow.So many thigns that out society has created, for someone to destroy it,how mant sad looks,how many tears of endles suffering by a long lost love.Unexplainable for a kid,those small hands,those tender eyes,those angelical voices,who were and are our future,how could someone destroy it,a mother,a son,a daughter,a husband,a wife,OH!LORD what a pain,such a suffering in my heart,life is cruel,we'll never know,all this pain could have been one of us.

So many humanitarian souls,have given a hand,without having regrets or doubts,looking out for one or another,a unity of brotherly love,love is so magnificent,it breaks all barriers,this nation is one faith,I am gateful to GOD,for giving them a chance to live,we pray ,do we pray?,this makes us valorate life,to see our families or loved ones,have you ever wonder if you couldn't see them again,for a time in life,to do not hear their laughter,feel their hugs,a kiss of good night,a blessing of love,it would be sad,so many of us who don't realize the meaning of life,who just don't care for tohers,now it's the time to make it last for ever,to unite as one heart,one love for our country,for our losses,to feel for all.

We need to realize,how much have we done,how can we make it better,now that we strength,now that we have a shelter,life is cruel in many ways,one person could destroy all,hate will not overcome us, we are here one love,one heart beating as one,my heart goes out to all the victims,may the mighty Lord,bless their souls,guide them into heaven,make room for each and all,it's not easy to forgive or forget but we must still live,be as bright as the day before,be as bright as the sun today,give a hand when someone needs it,be a blessing,be a friend.
