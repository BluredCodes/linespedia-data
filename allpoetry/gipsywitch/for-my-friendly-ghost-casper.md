It's easy to say I LOVE YOU,It's easy to say good bye but what are you doing if some how ou make someone cry.Words are so easy to whisper, they are so softly to hear,sometimes they can be nice and warm like the summer or cruel and cold as the winter.What use It's to fight,if we are always holding eachother tight,oh! please angel don't ever let me out of your sight,cause you don't had or you'll never have the right.Near or far it doesn't matter, for love is big and it will be our master.Bad intentions can not separate us, for our frienship is to strong and even if the years pass by,I will never let you go,cause when you'll yell I LOVE YOU, I knew yopu meant it cause it's been a few days and you haven't do me no wrong.
