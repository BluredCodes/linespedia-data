title: "Thinking of you in a dream"

I wish I could have you near
                        and have you all in my dreams.

                        To touch your skin,to feel your
                        lips,to see your eyes staring at 
                        mine,I wish I had you in my dreams.

                        Told you every deepest thought I
                        have,every time I feel you near me
                        just to hear your sweet voice,I wish
                        I could have you in my dreams.I am a dreamer of love and life ,,
