Why to see thee or hear thou voice has become so important with no meaning to be.Why is that am always thinking of you all hours of a day? Since I met you something changed on my life and I can't possibly find the reason ,that has turned me like this, but I know it is you.

My days go by so fast.The dark nights are full of stars and the moon is whiter than ever. I'm living in a world filled with illusions. Wishing for things that maybe won't come true, but if it is so, what's wrong with dreaming of an eternal love. One that will rescue thou from the sorrow I've been encountering from sunrise to sunset, ever since a child .

Some people say that loving someone just ain't enough. Many believe that one has to give up thy body, mind and soul all that you are and all it will be.    Thus how could I possibly share these treasures of mine if bad lovers have turned them into stone.

I wish there was a person not hot or cold but warm. One that could love me with a tender love and make me the happiest girl of all...stop! Why? Why am I sharing my thoughts with thee? Thou rarely words flow out of thy lips at all, maybe I'm too open hearted, maybe I shouldn't be like this. This is a poem! Not the real thing!, or maybe it is ... Are you in here? Well yes... Then what are you waiting for? Look at me and tell my love what's going to be.This poem was remastered, hope it is better for thy readers . MaryAngel
