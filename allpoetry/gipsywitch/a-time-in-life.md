If life was so cruel and i had to died tonight,I would know I was blessed, cause I die in your arms.I would be happy if your eyes were the last thing 
I saw cause in them I'll know how much you loved me.

If life,gives the time for me to see another day, to live again, make it throu this lonely night, I'll have to hug you and say that you take my breath away, cause you're one of a kind, wonderfull in many ways.

Memories of what we have run, all over my mind, every second, every minute, every hour, every day is even better than the day before and I think to my self, If today is amazing, how would it be an eternity by yourside .

These moments we share, you and me ,we should treasure forever, If I have you now I wont make the mistake of letting you go, every single moment I hear or see you I will let you know ,that I dont ever think of letting you flow out of my heart, out of my soul.

So boy, I will say a prayer for our love, that when i wake up the first thing I see is your face, your smile, that this aint just a dream, a time in life, a moment in time .i love the poems of Nauticalmyst7 but they are so sad,,,even thou we all go throu sadness in our lives ...
