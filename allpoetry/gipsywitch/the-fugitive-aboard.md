Why you came to my life, you're a total stranger of love, one that desn't know the meaning of loyalty, one that no matter what gives it all.I've been betrayed by destiny, he put you in my way, now you have stoled my heart, blocked my senses you have ruined my days.Man of more than one woman, man of fortune, man of none, you dont get what you're made of, you don't take chances,what it's done is done.Lonely girl on a dark long winter,falling into your arms, you a full hot summer, with a blossom shining on your face.There you go take the precious girft without love and leave her for after sometime, cime back to reclaim the prizes, sh'e been gone to some place unknown, now you won't find her or your son.Don't play with peoples lkifes they aint just a simple toy and if you don't believe me search for your lover, search for your boy.
