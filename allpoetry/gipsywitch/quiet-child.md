Life, but what is life with out love, how to find it?, how to look for it?, would he come to me ?, Is he already running so free.

A man, a perfect man, I can't make it,I wish I could, with many qualyties but it would never be fun, we will be the same, there will be nothing i could claim, to turn on the flame.

Handsome, strong, blue eyes,golden hair, I don't think so,it wouldn't be fair, the physic doesnt matter but what's inside that's the rare, there are so many and so little have the special touch, that's why they don't come out , that's why they don't dare.

Would I find him?, Yes I found it, he's near mebut i don't know if of my love he will take care, different from others he's a bit of shy but if heavens are with me afterthe rain, there will be a rainbow of love with a shiny brightfull sky, cause he's my man and that love will never die.crazy poet, you have such an original touch ,very sweet are your words..love them poems
