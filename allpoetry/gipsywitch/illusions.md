A man walks into my life and he tries to destroy it, he just walked up to my desk and remind me of his neck cause it hurt.

He seduces me with his looks,he just asked me what was wrong and i answered that nothing was going on.

Plate of desires burning for my body,I forgot he was trying to talk with bobby,so they could go and see his honey.

Oh my God! he's holding my hand and serving some wine, he was just simply asking me for the time.

A bunch of illusions and romantic dinner,forgive me, we were jsut in the corner eating a weaner.
