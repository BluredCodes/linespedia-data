title: "The key to my heart"

A strange man comes into my life, a unique love, its making me loose my senses, it's the touch of his hand, the way that he smells, the caress of his lips next to mine, the look in his eyes when he looks at me, the burning ,oh! loving sensation when hes near me. The deepest tone of passion when he calls out my name.

Every detail of him makes it feel right, the smile of him, the words that he saids, I had the feeling from the start, ha was all I needed to fall in love again, every time we share is perfect, everything we have in common is even better.

He is the key to my heart this time I cant be wrong, is the way he makes me feel, every time I take the phone, even if its far or near, It's like a miracle, I have no fear of beign hurt or burst into tears, he became the light inside of me, a romance from decades ago, maybe in a past life , he has to be the one I know.

Every time I hear his voice the feeling is too much and I dont wanna let him go, he's all I've ever needed to make my life complete.Every whisper that he makes,every dream we share, cause he's all I ever need to make my life complete.
