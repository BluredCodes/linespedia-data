For only it's true that love is blind and seaks no exterior sight.For thy words, for thee fair lady in front of you, drove her to fall deep into the seas of you.For this lady have not yet seen thy face upon my sealed lips requesting an entrance to the kingdom of me heart.I shal not fear thy loneluness,I shall conquer it with my warm love and make you mine.This fair lady will make thy lord forget all that has caused trouble and sorrow thy mind.

Loving wonders of thy powerfull soul,oh! thau I wish upon a clear night ,my heart shall select a star and make thee dream of thee come reality, to have you all ,to share you all, breath throu you, become one on a whisper, feel thay bodies sofocate on passion and love, breathless I shall love to feel thee, to give it all until I loose all mentallity.

Thou feeling of love is real I can't seem to reach the love of thee but I have faith, thy darkness of night fades away, with a smile a full bright sun shine upon my life,Oh! my fair lord,gentlemen of the lady here before  your self,I wish to feel your love,to feel you all, I know Oh!, constant dream , Oh! me lord.If I find thee, can I keep you safe in thee arms of me, sleep you with the song of love of me,kiss thy lips every minute, every day and night, for you shall be mu life, my deam ,my all.This is a great web with many talented people !!
