I could write the saddest verse this night but they will never compare or replace the hole you left in my heart.I lost you,you did loose me, to another illusion,to another soul.Oh misery ,my reason to smile has just gone,into the arms of someone else.I go outside and a tear runs down my face.Why destiny keeps on trying to break my heart,what good is love if it can't give you a life to hold in your body to make it yours.

I saw a light one day in my front porch, a vision was given to me by GOD,to wait for a lover to save this breath of all,it's so sad to see you go ,I guess that some men they really are like dogs.I cry and I cry,madly,I put my trust in you and look what you did,what was the meaning of all the sweet, if of that candy all the ants eat and left nothing for me,my heart is broken,just look at me,don't tur away ,understand me,can't you feel the pain in me,endless hours I gave to you,I supposed it was a lie,It was nice knowing you and now I tell you bye. 

I wont waste my time on someone who don't deserve my lines and if I am wrong for some reason I dare you to tell me why,I feel betrayed as always agian in my life, my hopes no longer exist, don't know what to believe ,what to say,I wish you find what you want,I have seen it wasn't me..love is hard on us and to live a fantasie its worse ,you never know the cuelty of one ,until you feel it on your own ...
