The wind brings back to me your lips, your fresh skin, the way you used to love me when we were alone, I could feel my heart shaking, from the botton of my soul, when i felt your hands rushing my body.It's raining that's why you cant see my tears, my mind is dieying because It's full with fears, since you left me, I can't think, that's why my heart is empty you don't know how it feels but you knew the meaning of the word good bye in this beach, I remember the night that lonely night , where the stars above the sky were our only witnesses of that horrible emptyness I face today.
