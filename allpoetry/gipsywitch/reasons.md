Sometimes I wonder near the skies if your love will ever be mine, if I'll someday have your shadow or if I ever see you, if I will say the word or I wouldn't, knowing that I'll get stock in my throat with nothing to swallow.

I need you, I really need you to hold me tight in your arms, to tell me how much  you love me, that my body is all you need, to feel loved by a woman that wants you,to fill her desires to burn with her fires...
