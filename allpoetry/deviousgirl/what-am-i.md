---
title: "What Am I?"
writer: "deviousgirl"
slug: "what-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am what makes you cringe as I walk by.
I am what sits next to you and never gets up.
I am what clings to you, and refuses to leave.
You plead for me to go and make it stop!
But there I am - relentless.
I am what steals the warmth, in your life, heart and soul.
I wait for you, ready to fall.
I am what makes you cry into the middle of the night.
I am what makes you bleed, I see it trickling down.
I am what makes you wish for the opposite.
I am the dream crusher, I am present when things go bad.
I am what drives you insane and then turns you numb.
I am what makes you have goose bumps on your arms.
I am what makes you want to forget.
I am a bad dream in the middle of the night.
I am your screams of heartache, I am what makes you dread defeat.
I am what eats away at your heart.
I am what makes you feel empty and alone.
I am the thing that watches you day and night.
I am different to everyone, they all experience me in different ways.
Some get more or less.
But the experience is never positive.
I drive you do to things that otherwise you'd never think of.
I cause you destruction in more ways then one - 
What am I ??????????


I am Pain!!!
