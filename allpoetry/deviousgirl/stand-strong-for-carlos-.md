---
title: "Stand Strong ( For Carlos )"
writer: "deviousgirl"
slug: "stand-strong-for-carlos-"
source: "AllPoetry"

Wondering who you are,
not sure if things will get better.
Let me tell you, I will always be there!
You have these attacks, and I know them all too well.
You say that we make up who you are, and now you are lost.
I care for you as much as you can a friend.
I will lead the way. 
You can find who you are in me.
I want to see that glimmer of hope in your eyes.
The one that used to make me believe.
I want to hear you laugh, and know that it's sincere.
I can tell you who you are.
You are Carlos, my best friend.
You are kind loving, and noble.
You treasure every moment that you have here.
You are an amzing guy, and have so much to live for.
People love you, and I do too.
You needn't cry because you have nothing that's lost.
Times are hard, but we will stick together.
We will make it through, I promise.
Please don't cry, please live like you used to.
It gets hard before it gets better.
Remember that my dear!!
Don't let them call you those hate filled names.
Don't let it hurt you, you know who you are.
We're on this path together, 
there's nothing that you can't do! 
I love you Carlos, you're one of my greatest friends.
Please know that you don't need us.
You have so much in yourself.
Your strong, and have fought many battles.
It's time to rest my dear, and let us steer.
Don't cry, don't hate, don't be anything but happy.
Times are hard, that I know.
stay alive, be strong, and stand tall.
I am proud of you, I love you my friend.
I'd do anything for you.
Let me see that glimmer of hope, let me feel that
vibrance for life.
Display that passion that I know you have.
Feel all the love that we have for you.
And that's all you need, don't listen to the ignorant.
They know nothing, listen to the ones that love you.
We love you babe, stand tall!This poem is for my friend, he's seperated from the ones that mean the most to him. He feels lost with out them, because they used to make up who he was. He can't talk or see them everyday. This is for my friend, I luv ya dear!!
