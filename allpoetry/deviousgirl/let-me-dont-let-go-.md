title: "Let me  . . . ( Don't Let Go )"
writer: "deviousgirl"

I won't ever let you go-
I need you here with me!
You have to stay, we love you, 
more then you'll ever know.
I walked through the firey depths of hell for you,
not once but twice.
You can't go, I won't let you!
You was there to help pick up my pieces,
who had the smile that melted my hurt?
Who had that simple solution to everything?
By the way it was a hug.
We've gone through too much, and I refuse to loose that!
I refuse to give up on you.
I won't watch you "die."
I won't let you do this by yourself.
Let me help, let me mend the hole,
let me do what you did for me.
Let me be your reason to keep going.
I want to be your crutch.
We can dry tears together, and I can mend your heart.
Let me do for you what you did for me, 
let me allow you to live
