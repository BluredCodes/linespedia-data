---
title: "Never Dreamt Of It Being You"
writer: "deviousgirl"
slug: "never-dreamt-of-it-being-you"

I fall to my knees, and ask how this could be you?Why do act this way, why do you suddenly not care?Damnit, you were my best friend!What are you now?I can't believe that it was you.How could you hurt me like that?I didn't deserve it, and I wonder . . . Are you alright?How could you say such things, and expect my heart not to bleed.How could you write things like that, and not give it any thought?Why would you do that!Of all the people in the world, why'd it have to be you?How could you do this?I'll never understand!My hand is still outstretched, ready for you to take.I don't know YOU any more, it's not me who's changed.You ripped my heart out of my chest.I never thought that it would be you!Are you happy now, just like the rest of the vultures!For some reason, here I still am, please take my hand.Give me peace and end it all.Don't stay like this for it's not right.Shocked, that YOU could do such a thing.It's now, that you broke my faith.Faith that YOU were good, and that I could trust.It's broken, why please tell me why!!!?I never imagined an act so despicable from YOU.Hope you're happy, because I'm not.Taking out your problems on me, Ha, I don't know it any other way!I lost my faith in you, never thought that could be. . . I'm sorry that it came to this, how could you?
