Sometimes in life, there is one thing you must have
But you can't always get what you want
You can want it so much, but it doesn't matter
You think you can get it, but you can't
It may be the most important thing to you
It doesn't care, you're not going to get it
You can ponder ways to make it yours
But you're just wasting your time
It will never be yours, you won't get it
Try all you want, it's your time that is lost
No matter what, you must realize...
You can't always get what you wantI wrote this about a girl who I really loved, but couldn't 'get with,' for lack of a better term. This is some recent stuff for me, as I've moved into a more personal writing style.
