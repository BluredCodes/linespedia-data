---
title: "Finally"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "finally"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48676-Finally-by-VDub26"
---

I have searched far and wide
For another heart that matches mine
And finally, I have found that heart
The one person who makes me feel complete
When she is near, I am warm inside
And even when she is miles away
I can still feel her in my heart
I have waited for this heart for too long
And finally I have found this heart...
Finally
