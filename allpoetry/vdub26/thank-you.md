I can't ignore the pain you've made
It overcomes me, and I can't think straight
Your life has ruined mine and I am dead
You blinded me with beauty
While you showered me with lies
Too long, it took me to see the light
But now that I have, there is no turning back
You are gone, and out of my life
Things are bright once more
So, Thank You for lying
Thank you for killing me
Thank you for the torture you put me through
Thank you for breaking my heart
For through all this, I have seen the light
And life is fulfilling again
