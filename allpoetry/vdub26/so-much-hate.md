---
title: "So Much Hate"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "so-much-hate"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48670-So-Much-Hate-by-VDub26"
---

Hate in the world, and it keeps getting worse
Wish there was more I could do to stop it all
I can only do so much by myself
I need help, everyone needs to join
Help stop all the hate taking place
Racists, bigots, and such, it's so sad
All created equal, but still all this hate
It takes place everyday, just open your eyes
You'll see it, wherever you look
There is so much hate across the globe
Even in lands that are supposedly holy
Hate rules all, but it must stop
Before the hate kills everyone
