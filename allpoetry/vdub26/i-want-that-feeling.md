---
title: "I Want That Feeling"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "i-want-that-feeling"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is one feeling, I want so bad
I want to love, and to be loved
That is what I want. Is it too much?
I hope it isn't. I need that feeling
More then anything, I need love
I want to feel a love so perfect,
Nothing can be wrong about it
But I must wait for it to come
Wait for the right person to share
this perfect love with, so I wait
Until she comes into my life
I just want that feeling
