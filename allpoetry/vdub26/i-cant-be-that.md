---
title: "I Can't Be That"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "i-cant-be-that"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52947-I-Cant-Be-That-by-VDub26"

You tell me to be perfect
But you know that I can't
No one can, don't you realize that?
I'm never good enough for you
Isn't that right? Don't deny it!
You know you want me to be perfect
But you must realize that I can't be that
I am my own person, and yes,
I am far from perfect
But I try to be good enough for you
Will it ever be good enough?
No, because I can never be perfect
