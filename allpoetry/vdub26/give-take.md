---
title: "Give & Take"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "give-take"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52949-Give---Take-by-VDub26"
---

So many people in this world just take
The never give, they don't feel the need
They feel that they should have it all
While other suffer with the minimal
They take, and take, it's what they do
Never thinking about other, just them
And their needs, it makes me so sad
People need to pay attention to others
Not just worry about their needs
You've got to start giving, and quit taking
It's just that simple, don't you see?
Think about others, not just yourself
