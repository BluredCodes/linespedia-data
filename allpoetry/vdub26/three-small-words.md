title: "Three Small Words"
writer: "VDub26"

Your beautiful face fills all of my thoughts
I can think of nothing but you
Three months have gone by, and I still care so much
I haven't felt this way for a long time
There's something I'm trying to say, but i don't know how
And I wonder if things will change
I've tried to say it, but the words don't come out
I'm afraid that you aren't ready
These words are so precious and you probably know
The words that I'm talking about
When you truly mean it, it's hard to say
But I want to, more then ever
I've waited to long, it seems like years
To say these three little wirds that are so powerful
And now, with all my heart, I can say,
I Love You
