When I first saw you, I knew I must meet you
My feelings can't be described with words
The look in your eyes, reminds me of love
Your smile shines in my heart
We started talking, I took your hand
And then the magical moment
The sweetest kiss I have ever experienced
Oh, how I wish I could go back to that night
When I met the girl of my dreams
And touched her lips to mine
I feel like I am floating in Heaven
But my feet are still on the ground
I hope I never lose her, keep her in my heart
As long as I live, I will care for this girl
Who I met on the night of all nights
