title: "Can't Stop Thinking About Her"

A beautiful girl like she doesn't soon leave your mind
Wherever I go, I think about her
Her eyes are enchanting and they draw me near
Her hair flows like a field of grain in a cool summer breeze
Her smile shines like a ray of sun on a cloudy day
No matter what I do, she doesn't leave my mind
When I drive, she is in every car around me
When I sleep, I dream of her infinite beauty
When I talk, she is on my lips and I speak only of her
She is in my heart and stays there always
She is like an angel, sent here, from God
With perfect white wings and a halo of gold
No matter what, I can't stop thinking about her
