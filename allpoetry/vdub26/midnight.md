Once again, I'm awake,deep in the night
My mind is overcome by thoughts of you
And thoughts of what could be
Maybe soon, we will be together
That would be the greatest day
The day my life would change forever
If we joined together, become as one
But I know I must wait, not rush it
I wait for you, and I will be patient
Because my love for you is strong
It has no boundaries at all
So I write this, in the middle of the night
For you, the girl that keeps me awake
Just by thinking of you
