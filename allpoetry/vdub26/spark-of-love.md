title: "Spark Of Love"

A flame has been lit in my heart
And you're the match that holds the flame
Your gentle face has brought me to life
I've not been this happy for a long time
Your beauty is the very equal
To a sun setting on a cloudless day
Day and night I dream about you
Though I barely know you
But, oh, how I want to learn more
About you, this warmth that has strengthened my soul
And sparked my love once more
