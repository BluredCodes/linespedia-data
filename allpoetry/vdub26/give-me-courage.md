title: "Give Me Courage"

I want to talk to her, but I can't
I'm afraid of the rejection that is possible
I'm afraid I'll say the wrong thing
How can I do it? Talk to her,
This girl I feel for, so much
I long for her, but silently,
Because I do not have the courage
Needed to express what I feel
But how I want to tell her
Everything that I am feeling inside
So I hope for the courage I need
