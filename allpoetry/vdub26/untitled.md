As she sits, all I can do is stare
Her beauty is amazing, attention grabbing
All I can do is think about her
Holding her in my arms would be heaven
But will that day ever come?
Who knows? Only God above can know
But, how I long to touch her
To kiss her beautiful lips
She is so beautiful, I can't tear my gaze
I'm stuck on her, the girl, as she sits
Since I saw her, I have longed for her
But can I have her? Maybe I will never know
I long for her silently, not voicing my thoughts
But maybe, someday, I will have her
