I dress this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I walk this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I look this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I act this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I think this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I talk this way
'What's wrong with you?'
I feel this way
'What's wrong with you?'
It's not what's wrong with me
It's what's wrong with you!I wrote this awhile ago to express how I feel that people think that because someone is different, there is something 'wrong' with them. Who are they to judge? Who are any of us to judge someone else?
