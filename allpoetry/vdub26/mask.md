Look. Can you see my face?
You think you can, but it's actually a mask
I wear this mask to make you like me
So I can be 'cool' like everyone else
I know that if I took my mask off,
You wouldn't treat me the same
I know you couldn't accept the real me
Couldn't accept what I am really like
Do you want to know the real me?
Let me take off my mask. Here I am
I am faithful to God, and spend time with others
Who feel the same as I do
I show my emotions freely, when I mask is gone
I know you can't accept my mask-less face
So I put my mask back on
So I can be your so-called friend
But if you can't accept the real me
Then you must not be a true friend
If you don't like my mask-less face,
Then you aren't someone who cares
When you can accept the real, mask-less me
Then I will call you friend
