As I look in the sky, there are so many stars
Somewhere, looking at the same star as I,
Is the girl of my dreams
She has long hair, flowing like the ocean
Her lips sparkle like rubys
Her eyes reflect love, for the same things I love
She looks at the star, unknowing
Of this man, me, looking at it with her
Her smile sparkles like our star
Her voice is a choir of angels
And her hands give a loving touch
As I sit here and look at the star,
I wonder where the girl of my dreams is...
Somewhere
