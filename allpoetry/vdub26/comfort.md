When I met you, I was stunned
By your beauty, and your warm smile
We had just met, and I was already yours
But did you want me? Do you now?
I must wait to know for sure
Get to know you more, learn everything
Let you be comfortable with me
As I want to be with you
Someday it might happen that 'we' become 'us'
But I must wait, until that day comes
And let time pass, for you
So that you can be ready
And not feel rushed, Only,
When you are comfortable
