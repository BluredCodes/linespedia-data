Sunrise
Sunset
Cloudy Day
Starry Night
Rainy Afternoon
Sun Beams
Oceans
Lakes
Life
Death
Me
You
Him
Her
Them
Us
God's priceless works of artI wrote this poem one day when I was feeling pretty down about life in general. I just thought of all the gifts we're given, and wrote them down. Hope you enjoy it!
