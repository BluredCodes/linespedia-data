---
title: "Always On My Mind"
writer: "VDub26"
slug: "always-on-my-mind"

When I think, it's always of you
You are in all of my thoughts
My dreams are always of you
And sometimes, I can't sleep at night
Because all I can think about is you
You are always on my mind
Your smile, your eyes, your laughter
They are always with me
Because I think of only you
This is not a bad thing, oh no
Thinking about you is wonderful
That is why I am so glad
That you are always on my mind
