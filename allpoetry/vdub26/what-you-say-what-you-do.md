title: "What You Say, What You Do"

What you say and what you do are two different things
You can talk all you want, but can you be true to your words?
You have trouble with this, and you have two faces
You speak of your greatness, but I think it is false
I can't speak for others, but this is what I know
The words that exit your mouth are nothing but lies
Promises are broken, and your fakeness shows through
Why did I put up with it? Your Deception and untruths
Because I listened to the words you spoke for too long
Instead of opening my eyes to your spiteful actions
But I am through, now I only watch
As what you say and do kills another heart
