When love becomes stale
What does one do?
Throw it in the garbage or.
Feed it to the dogs?
Whose fault it is?
The one who didn’t take care? 
Or one who cared too much?
Forget the love one shared?
The toothpaste and the pillow 
The shirt, the comb. 
You shared a part of u 
And now you want to part 
What happens when one loves so much
That it breaks the heart?'
