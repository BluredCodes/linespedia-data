When I look in the mirror 
I see someone else
Looking back at me!!
Who are you I ask,
Oh! Now, you do not know me?
Says She.
And how will you?
As you have changed so…
You have forgotten somewhere
Your very own soul

The passion that burnt
The love that shined 
And now I see only dry eyes
Eyes no more smiles

Oh, please don’t stare 
As I can’t bear
The passion 
The Love and the smiles
Somewhere I left behind
Let them be there
As I have stopped to care
Cause caring didn’t help
It tore my soul and now the mirror
Recognizes me no more
