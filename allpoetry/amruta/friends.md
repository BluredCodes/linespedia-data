Friends
A friendship which is eternal,
never has a beginning or end
that is what you are to me
My eternal friend from the past and the the future
from the time without  
Begining 
or 
End.
we are always together my eternal friend.
whether I see you now or never or in the end
doesn't matter cause there is never an end
we were together always and be together always in heart and mind... 
giving a new meaning to the word 'FRIEND'
