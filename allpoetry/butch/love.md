---
title: "Love"
writer: "butch"
slug: "love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Lying here in the darkness
your breathing settles me
A moments fright chased away
by the nearness of you
The days close confrontations
come back to haunt me
I then realize, I need you
I've always needed you.....

Love is a flower; that when it blooms
the world around brightens
It's blossom is the beginning of
the magic on which we survive
Once we taste the fruit, we will
never be the same
We spend our lives in search of
it's fragrant beauty.....

If I awaken and you are not there
I cannot return to slumber
The comfort you give to me
is all I need in the night
To be near you gives the strength
I need to face the world
The surrender I feel with you
is my release from reality.....

To be in love is a change
that begins in the heart
Your breathe taken, by 
the memory of single moments
Your thoughts held up by
someone you barely know
Your life forever directed to
new and strange feelings.....
