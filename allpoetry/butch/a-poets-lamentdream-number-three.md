Dancing in the breeze;
Flight without wings;
I see you, but,
cannot touch you,

Helpless;
Suspended, floating;
I want you, but,
can only want,

Dreams, well worn;
Visions in desperateness;
I call your name, but,
you do not hear,

In a prison;
My own creation;
I want to escape, but,
have no route,

In your eyes;
Indifference;
I look for warmth, but,
find only chill,

Crying now;
Hating myself;
I want to run, but,
cannot forget,

Tears create prisms;
I see you in colors;
I ache for you, but,
you do not feel,

Caught in the light;
A fool exposed;
I should save myself, but,
cannot move,

Pale heart;
Dark soul;
I would get away, but,
the fire is hot.....

Like a moth attracted to candle flame,
I cannot resist, for I am weak..........
