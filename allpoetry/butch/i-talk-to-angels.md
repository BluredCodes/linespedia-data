---
title: "I Talk To Angels"
writer: "butch"
slug: "i-talk-to-angels"
source: "AllPoetry"

I talk to angels;
in the silence
with heart in clarity
I call to them;

They talk to me;
in insinuative moments
with soundless voices
they speak to me;

I know them well;
they belong to me
kinsman long gone
they meter me;

I question them;
musings in love
bereft of comfort
I relay my sorrow;

They console me;
counseling, tempering
resolving my fortitude
they soothe me;

I will rejoin;
brought to them
time as a vehicle
I will visit again;

I talk to angels;
faces of the past
gloried recollections
they talk to me.........
