---
title: "Whispered Rhapsody"
writer: "butch"
slug: "whispered-rhapsody"
source: "AllPoetry"

Her head 
lies on my shoulder
gently
her breath 
soft against my cheek
tingly
her hair
spread across my chest
warmly
her voice
plays in my ear
mildly

My hands 
rest on her skin
loverly  
my eyes
grasp her wondrous beauty
intently
my mind
stays in constant haze
mistily
my heart
agrees to be captured 
gladly.......
