title: "View From Inside Of A Raindrop"

Falling, the world looms near, I can see, 
Gray clouds behind, green earth pulls at me, 
The air rushes around me, I know the time is near, 
The sky is full, and, moist, I have no fear, 
I am not alone, others also fall, 
We rejoice, as we fulfill the distant call, 
Faster, the ground swells up quickly, inviting me, 
Ahead, the parched soil, begging, crying, wantonly, 
And, as the earth accepts me, I awake, 
I realize, this is the only course I could take, 
I see the reason I am here, as I witness birth, 
My life complete, as I enter the earth, 
And, it is a new world.............
