are they looking
at me, and only
me, as I lie in
quiet repose
naked in truth
and flesh
aware of only
the air on my skin
gentle breeze
goosebumps 
splendid and beautiful
I am the focus
I am the attraction
woman and vixen
do you love me.......I wrote this in response to a contest. A picture of a reposed naked woman was the focus of the contest
