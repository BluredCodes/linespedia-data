The meager house
atop the hill
chimney smoke
rising in wisps
haze aloft
placid image
not lonely
but, pacifying
the valley lookout
always alert
stern but generous
allowing the flow
of natures gifts
to continue unabated
vigilant, alive
giving credence
to a slowing
of time
mending spirit
rejuenating, restoring
brief moments
away from life
and, reality........
