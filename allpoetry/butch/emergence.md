---
title: "Emergence"
writer: "butch"
slug: "emergence"
source: "AllPoetry"

Underneath the footbridge
away from trample
in solitary plot
isolated, but vibrate
tiny bud emerges
given taste of light
through crack in wood
delicate petals open
bright colors shown
yellow and blue
unseen, unknown, untouched
each day blossoming
to greet the sun
in furious splendor
until tiny hands
reach to pluck
remove from ground
taken to somewhere
forgotten for time
until big hands
found and saved
pressed between pages
to be seen
again and again
remembrance for eternity.....
