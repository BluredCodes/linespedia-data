I see you there,
Looking at me with distaste,
Smiling, as if it hurts you face,

I see the disdain in your eyes,
Putting yourself on that pedestal,
In love with only you,

I see your money flashing,
All your toys in front of you,
Wrapping yourself in gold,

I see your unsecret ways,
Your high flying ways,
Your sickness consuming you,

I see you smile,
The smile of false pride,
Your greed infecting you soul,

I see your power addiction,
The need to control,
Your sins hiding behind you,

I see your uncaring excuses,
The things you tell yourself,
Those lies you pass off as truth,

I see your lies,
I know your lies,
I live your lies,

I see your fear,
The things you hide,
The pain you conceal,

I know who you are,
I know what you are,
I know why you are,

I see you,
I know you,
I am you,

I'm trying to get it together,
I've tried all these years,
I'll keep trying,

You're born,
You die,
And, in between you become human.......
