---
title: "The Lane"
writer: "butch"

I drove a country road
a little creek 
running beside it.
Trees hanging 
over the lane
birds dancing.
Leaves, so many
the view was 
blocked.
I didn't care
it was peaceful
and serene.
The world 
a distant
memory.
Just a road
nothing 
special.
The window
open with
wind blowing.
The odors
of fresh
earth.
Reminding me
of things
I forgot.
This journey
never
to end.
Playing 
like echoes
in recurring dreams...
