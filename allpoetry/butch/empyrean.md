title: "Empyrean"

I saw the gates of Heaven,
perched upon golden sky,
Framed by the solar rising,
the empyrean in Gods eye,
I watched in fixated awe,
this glimpse of paradise,
I felt an ecstasy of heart,
and sensed my passion rise,
I was drawn to this vision,
by a desire to be free,
Relieved of mans burden,
to gain the apogee,
Gilded stairway arise,
take flight to final fate,
Climbing to coveted glory,
awaiting inside the gate,
I saw the Heavens gates,
in silver, and blue,
Guide me to the promised land,
to live my life anew,
Daydream in the early light,
dawns simple longing.........
