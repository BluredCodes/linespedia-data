Along the wet hills
sculpted in white
alive in the fury
of winter dreams
captured alone
the soul of me
realization begin
conscious travails
in one breath
gelid air
into my lungs
awakens remembrance
of immature ardor
long snowy days
then nights
seeking comfort
and protection
from icy nip
guiding me toward
the glory of
riding tiny sled
to the bottom
of chaste slopes
laughing in the wind.......
