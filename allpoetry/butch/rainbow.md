title: "Rainbow"
writer: "butch"

Rainbow between the clouds
guiding my eye
towards the infinite
consoling, counciling
colors flowing
life affecting
creating me
forming me
lessons in consequence
I bow to it
I give myself
to that single innocence
a moment in time......
