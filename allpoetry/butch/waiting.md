---
title: "Waiting"
writer: "butch"
slug: "waiting"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Waiting,Always waiting,Always been waiting,Awakened anew,Arriving fresh,Just to wait,Awaiting love,Awaiting knowledge,Awaiting life,Waiting for patience,Waiting for incite,Waiting for wisdom,Growing,Each day changing,Each promise new,Tomorrow visible,Always hopeful,Always there,Grabbing moments,Making memories,Learning truth,Gaining passion,Making sense,Finding meaning,Time passes,Years fall away,A body changes,Waiting, always waiting,For everything,For nothing,Waiting for the ebb,As night approaches,The shadows come,The darkness,Too soon,Waiting for the end,Waiting;
