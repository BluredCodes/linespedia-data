The battle done;
fires glow again
reflecting weary faces
dirty and splattered
blood and pain;

fewer this time;
what were their names
those brave heroes
seeking glory and
finding none

silence now;
no boisterous talk
only quiet cogitation
too soon, recall
fatigue and relief

weapons at feet:
stacked away
no longer glorious
ignoble, plain
tainted by death

proof of mettle;
marred by doubt
weak and burnt
vacant eyes and
hollow heart

vestige of war;
lost souls
forever defiled
gone the innocence
gone the child

visions no more........
