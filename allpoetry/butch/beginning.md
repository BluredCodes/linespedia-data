title: "Beginning"

I cannot see, but, I can hear, 
The sounds in my heart, the touch of a tear, 
I cannot fear, but, I can learn, 
Those things in life, that I have to earn, 
I can feel the wind, and taste the rain, 
I can only hope for release from pain, 
I am what I have become, and, not what I chose, 
I am a casual novice, lost in the prose, 
I am just me.......
