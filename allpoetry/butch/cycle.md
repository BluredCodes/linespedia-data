title: "Cycle"

See the buds on the limbs
proof that life refreshes
the face of the flowers
the sun centering itself
in a sky much clearer
warmer air surrounds
mockingbird sings 
the everybird song
the grand monarch
majestic in presentation
though short lived
the tiny bee
with small stature
keeps nature replenished
colors flow into us
changing our essence
delight and jubilation
a mood swing
brings back hope.......
