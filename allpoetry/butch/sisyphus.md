On waking and seeing light
the day begins
a series
day in day out
over and over
the rising of the dead
flailing about
a dance with no end
human experience
the slog to nowhere
affliction with no cure
dolor and anguish
no acknowledgement
just acceptance
with buried guilt
divagation and 
woeful rest
desire for answers
persistence
continuity
resolve........
