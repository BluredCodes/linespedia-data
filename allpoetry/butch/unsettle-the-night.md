---
title: "Unsettle The Night"
writer: "butch"
slug: "unsettle-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38545-Unsettle-The-Night-by-butch"
---

There came a knock
upon the window
in winters 
frigid shroud

A startle sure
a surprise event
jolt with
fear endowed

A timid approach
while seeking voice
finding night
too dark

Bolt is locked
a safe effect
of risk
I hark

A window survey
hesitation my game
a wish
for security

Caution is called
advance is slow
calculated care
measured intuity

With shade pulled
a hurried scan
a wonder
whence came

A fervid exhale
sigh of relief
calming begin
heart tame

Twas nothing
but wind 
and errant
tree limb......
