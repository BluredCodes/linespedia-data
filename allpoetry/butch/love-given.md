title: "Love Given"
writer: "butch"

Impaled am I, by this love that was given, 
Received by one whose heart has been driven, 
Was lost in a wilderness of persistent lonely dreams, 
And, taken to the depths of times murky streams, 

A withered heart breeds a withered soul, 
A life in seclusion has it's own sad toll, 
A song of regret, passes from day to day, 
A constant remembrance of the hymns we play, 

But, love found the way to me, I rejoice, 
It fills the void and gives a new voice, 
My heart, though damaged, begins anew, 
A once pale life, regains forgotten hue, 

This love is mine, and, I cherish this burning, 
At last, the way to subdue my yearning, 
I give to you my love, my life, and, my time, 
To teach you my meaning in this spare rhyme, 

Love is the only way..........
