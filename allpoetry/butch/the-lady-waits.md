title: "The Lady Waits"

Within the Kings walls
inside the lonely room
a room with no key
...the Lady waits;

Within the royal chambers
upon the velvet chair
a chair with no comfort
...the King awaits;

Within the lonely heart
inside the passion pent
a passion with no light
...the Lady yearns;

Within the reclusive soul
upon the unsatisfied ardor
an ardor wih no scheme
...the King dreams;

Two parallel paths
two like hearts
two lone people
...two quiet desperations;

A Lady and a KIng
a love waiting to grow
a love with no risk
...the love untaken;

Two separate lives
two alike wishes
two foolish aims
...two sad stories;

A Lady and a King
a lonliness shared
a lonliness accepted
...the love unknown.....
