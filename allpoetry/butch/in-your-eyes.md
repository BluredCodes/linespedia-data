---
title: "In Your Eyes"
writer: "butch"
slug: "in-your-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9503-In-Your-Eyes-by-butch"
---

In your eyes, I could live forever,
 The way they look at me is, divine,
Losing myself in the softness of your gaze,
 I want to be here, I need to be here,

In your eyes, my life becomes simple,
 There is only you, and the love I feel,
The world goes by, not noticed or acknowledged,
 I can only see you, and these eyes that hold me,

In your eyes, I am helpless, breathless,
 Love takes me, I cannot resist,
I can only hope that this is real,
 I am caught in the moment of your love,

In your eyes, I could be a king,
 I could be the hero, the champion,
The savior of the world,
 I could be anything, and everything,

In your eyes, I am given to abandon,
 My soul becomes your slave,
My life no longer mine,
 I am yours tonite, and 'til time ends,

In your eyes, I give myself,
 I am driven to be near you,
I am in awe of your presence,
 I can only worship this emotion,

In your eyes, the things I do for you,
 I am not in control,
A prisoner of a feeling,
 I am lost forever..........
