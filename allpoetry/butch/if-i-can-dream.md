---
title: "If I Can dream"
writer: "butch"
slug: "if-i-can-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

If a heart can be broken
a mind can be bent
a soul can be tortured
a body can be rent......

My spirit runs free;

I may be tied to one time
or going in one direction
but, my spirit is free;

I can fly to the heavens
touch the wings of a hawk
wrap around the mist;

Travel to new lands
be the ruler of a kingdom
dance with the people;

Sail the mighty sea
pilot a skyrocket
or, walk on distant world;

I can be anything
and, everything
and, I can be free;

I just close my eyes
and, gently meditate
and, dream;

If I can dream
I'll have wings
and release........
