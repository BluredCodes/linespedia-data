title: "Little Ditty"
writer: "butch"

Planes fly,
bombs fall,
men die,

People bleed,
war begins,
God speed,

Hate grows,
buildings crash,
blood flows,

Questioned why,
parents falter,
children cry,

World afire,
innocent faces,
condition dire,

Love leaves,
people hide,
no reprieve.........
