title: "Ike And Sarah"
writer: "butch"

Ike was a guy, a very lonely guy
he had a twisty, turny
messed-up way of looking at things
he saw evil in everyones face
only his dog could make him smile

Sarah is a girl, a lovely girl
she loves everybody,
and everyone loves her
she was bright and sweet
just a joy to know

Ike spotted Sarah one day as
she walked below his attic window
in his parents house
where he hid from life
and fantasized

Sarah didn't notice
she never thought about
things like that 
they didn't exist 
in her pristene world

Ike built dreams about her
under the covers
in the dark night
all by himself
with no one to argue with

Sarah, alone in her bed
dreamed about a golden man
a knight in pre-washed armor
her hero, come to save her
take her home to paradise

Ike plotted and planned
he made maps and diagrams
time schedules and contingencies
he watched out his window
waiting for opportunity

Sarah went to work
the bus stop 
in front of Ikes
she did her job
and then went home

Ike waited in the bushes
knowing the time was near
palms wet from excitement
the bus came he stepped out 
and all went black

It was the downtown bus
and, Sarah never knew.........
