---
title: "A False Spring"
writer: "butch"
slug: "a-false-spring"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45629-A-False-Spring-by-butch"
---

The days, though not longer,
are brighter now
The sun returns from a
cloudy hiding place
The sky, so blue it takes
the breath from me
A warmness touches my skin
awakening new desire.....

Her eyes are green energy
I cannot turn away
Her lips, inviting me
find me transfixed
Her voice, seductive, reciting
the words of love
Captivated, out of control
a moment of release.....

The buds appear, and a 
new world awakens
The brown landscape refreshes
the colors recur
Life begins anew, birds
and crawlly things
Nature at it's best
the earth in ecstasy.....

She loves me, she says 
to me alone
She gave me her heart
and promised dreams
I fell to the depths
of enraptured ardor
No more need I seek
a coveted fulfillment.....

But, the season came too 
early, winter not done,
An icy blast came down 
from northern hills
Frost replacing solar glow
and wilting blooms
Winters last ride on 
tired frozen souls.....

I reach out for her
but, find only space
Only the scent of her
endures and remembrance
Those words she spoke
settle as frigid stone
The intimacy pledged,
nothing but false spring.........
