title: "Roses  wilt"
writer: "wanda forkum"

YES, HE SENDS ME ROSES
HE REMEMBERS SPECIAL DAYS
BUT THAT DON'T MEAN FOREVER
BECAUSE ROSES WILT AWAY

ROSES WILT AND THE PEDALS FALL
JUST LEAVES AN EMPTY STEM
STRIPPED OF PRIDE AND ALONE
LIKE MY LOVE IS FOR HIM

FRIENDS SAY I'M A LUCKY ONE
TO HAVE A MAN LIKE YOU
THEY DON'T KNOW THE TRUTH IS
YOUR LOVE FOR ME IS NOT TRUE

I'D RATHER HAVE A TRUE LOVE
WHO'D GIVE ONLY LOVE TO ME
THAN ALL THE ROSES IN THE WORLD
OR PEARLS IN THE DEEP BLUE SEA

I WISH YOU'D KEEP THE ROSES
FORGET THE SPECIAL DAYS
CAUSE THEY DON'T LAST FOREVER
AND ROSES WILT AWAY
