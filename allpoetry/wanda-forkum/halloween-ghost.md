HALLOWEEN IS COMING SOONSO LISTEN VERY CLOSEIT'S THE NIGHT OF THE WITCHESAND SNEAKY LITTLE GHOSTTHEY'LL RUN AROUND THE NEIGHBORHOODRINGING DOORBELLS EVERY WHEREAND IF YOU DARE PEEK OUTSIDEI PROMISE THEY'LL BE THERETHEIR REALLY QUITE HARMLESSTHESE WITCHES AND LITTLE GHOSTTHEY JUST WANT TO SCARE YOUCANDY'S WHAT THEY WANT MOSTTRICK OR TREAT, TRICK OR TREATLITTLE GHOSTLY VOICES SINGTHE LITTLE MONSTERS CAN'T BE STOPPEDBY SLEET, SNOW OR RAINWHEN TIRED OF SCARING EVERYONETHEY'LL SIT DOWN AND REST AWHILELITTLE HANDS ALL STICKYTHEY WEAR A CHOCOLATE SMILE
