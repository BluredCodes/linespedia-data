SKIN A BEAUTIFUL SHADE OF REDA BAND OF FEATHERS ADORN THEIR HEADLONG BLACK HAIR FLOWS IN THE WINDDEEP DARK EYES SHOW PRIDE WITHINFREE AS LONG AS THE BUFFALO ROAMEDWHERE THEY CAMPED, THEY CALLED HOMEAROUND THE FIRE THEY'D CHANT AND SINGBUT WHAT WOULD THE MORNING STAR BRINGTEEPEES DOTTED THE COUNTRY SIDESPOTTED PONIES WAS THEIRS TO RIDEBUCKSKIN CLOTHES MADE BY HANDWHAT THEY OWNED CAME FROM THE LANDWISH I COULD GO BACK IN TIMEGET SOME PEOPLE TO CHANGE THEIR MINDSTHE CHEROKEE, SIOUX AND CHEYENNE WOULD BESTILL HUNTING BUFFALO AND LIVING FREEIF IN HEAVEN I SHOULD MEETONE OF THE GREAT INDIAN CHEIFSHE'LL GREET ME WITH A RAISED HANDAND SAY, THIS WHITE ONE IS A FRIEND
