this is a true story about a girl i really liked.....and could have loved, if given the time to do so.

please, take the time to read it.

the clear gummi bear......

I once knew this girl,we worked together, and i liked her a whole lot but I didnt have the guts to tell her. but i told close friends of my feelings. and you know friends, it mysteriously got back to her. we talked and she threw it out that she also liked me. we had a couple of dates but there is one that i remember to the T. we went to see a movie and she got sour patch kids and i got gummi bears. before the movie started we talked and mengled. we traded candies. but she only would eat the clear gummi bears out of the whole bag. she said she liked only that flavor but could not figure out the name of the flavor. we went on and watched the movie and in the middle she wispered," pinia colada, its pinia colada, thats the flavor of the clear gummi bear."  it was just a little special that on that night with me she figured out her mind boggling mystery, with me. but as it goes that was one of our last dates she had to leave work for a while because her mom got sick and we grew appart. but its on me i should have tried to rekinddle it. but i didnt and now i pay for it on the nights i need someone to talk to and laugh with.......
i know its been said and overly said but, if you like someone tell them they just might feel the same way you do.............she did.
