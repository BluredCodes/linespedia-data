Little Man

a little man is just that a little man
men are not usually this little
but this one is
he is a cripple

its not his fault
or...is it so
he cant tell
he'll never know

he is little
but only in highth
HE IS STRONG NOW
HE WILL NOT DIE TONIGHT

HE WILL LIVE
HE WILL CONCORE ALL
HE IS NOT A LITTE MAN ANYMORE
HE NOW STANDS TALL

HE NOW IS A BIG MAN 
NOW WITH BIG PLANS
but for some reason
he still has little handsits my first be gentle
