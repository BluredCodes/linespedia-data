I wake up in the morning just like everyone else
I eat my breakfast just like everyone else
I dress myself just like everyone else
I go to school just like everyone else
I eat my lunch just like everyone else
I go home just like everyone else
I eat my dinner just like everyone else
I go to sleep just like everyone else
Then I wake up....
If I am just like everyone else, why I am I so different?This is the first poem I have written, Its about me and how everyone thinks I'm different, they say it through their words, their bodylanguage and their actions, I'm not that different, amI?
