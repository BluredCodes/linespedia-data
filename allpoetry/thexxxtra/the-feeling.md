---
title: "The Feeling"
writer: "thexxxtra"

Come into my circle,
And dance with me,
Sense me,
feel me,
Want and desire me.
Destory yourself while chasing after me.
I'm a vision of your love to be.
I create a world where power doesn't matter,
Where true love is the ultimate goal.
I touch you in places where you have never been touched,
I make you quiver and shake from fear. 
Come with me,
And gain a sense of what being human is like.
Feel the passion and lust you have never felt before.
Feel me.
