I felt so lonley
I felt so excluded.
then I found somebody who saw something in me
I wasn't eating by myself anymore
I had met so many people
they all liked me
I felt as if I had just opened the curtains, and it was the most beautiful day I have ever seen.
The sun was shining in my face, drying up my tears of hurt, but only to make room for the tears of happiness 
My freinds, this ones for you
I love you all and I will never forget you.
We have our differences
we say stupid things
We start others on bad habits
and we may not be exactly sure on our sexuality
but most of all we love each other and thats all that matters.This poem is for my friends
I Love You all!
