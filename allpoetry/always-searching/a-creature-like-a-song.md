---
title: "A Creature, Like a Song"
writer: "Always Searching"

Something that is nothing
true to form it glides to Sun and Set
East to West
Least to Best

A Creature, like a Song
cacaphony in melody
a crescendo, decrescendo
strident, stringent chords, hyperbole
it cannot see
it travels aimlessly
and yet with purpose
toward inevitability
it cannot see
it cannot see
and yet it knows
and yet it knows
of one sure fact
one sure fact
