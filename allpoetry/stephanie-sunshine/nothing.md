the plot i claim is not yet mine...
that Divine Supremacy which will not have me:
too young, too rash
to understand the plight.
Desire, unrelenting, cradles my thoughts...
Filters them until nothing remains but YOU,
Perfect perfection...
Beyond ideal.
Although what IS cannot be, it struggles on
demanding only a leap of faith.
A gesture of some kind
to prove its deniable existence.
The Enemy: dubbed TIME.
The Adversary: christened LOVE.
Upon the bloodied vanguard
of a war named "you",
they prepare for battle
each surveying the other.
With outcome unknown and purpose unfaltering,
LOVE advances against the foe:
moment of truth
hour of agony....
