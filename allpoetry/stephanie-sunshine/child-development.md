Belt of drunk whirls and cracks my face
while i cry for mother who
never hears but ALWAYS listens
to the insatiable god of sex and rocking chairs
that
    never
         stop
             creaking.
             Perpetual zombie state:
             snowball down the highs 
and hate is all i know or care to know
or even care about. 

Stability and sanity elude me.1997...sometime,
