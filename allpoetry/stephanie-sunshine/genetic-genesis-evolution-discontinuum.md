computized creation
DNA extrapolation;
unfaltering combination
freak show fusion:
human(in)human

designated intellect
wreaking havoc
on the human genome
unknown domino effect
affecting generations

genetic genesis
synthetic empathy
creationism at its best;
“test tube” darlin’
deviates naught
(evolution elect)
	
fabricated greatness
ordained success
survival instinct extinct
expect the exception:
pre-destined perfection

(creation designation devoid
of artful expression)

“A Brave New World” we live inphoto=new revision

(so i'm a glutton for exposure)
