an overabundance
of hypocrisy...
(gee oh dee) GOD
as an entity?
an experience?
a machination of the mind?

hardwired to send
coded(occult)obscurity
rebuking individuality
an enigmatic form encouraging
personalities monochromatic
via compliance

complex equations
lucid "revelations"
understanding contingent
upon perception (your own?)
religious bias/NONE concludes:
G.O.D. is influential

god is in my hair
god is in your breath
god is in the feces of a dog
suffering from consumption
god is these, and god is ME
(i believe in god, gee oh dee)mmm...please comment
