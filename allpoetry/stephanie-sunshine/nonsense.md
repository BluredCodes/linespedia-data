(non)sense


An isolated case of Intensified
Fury
and i spork your evenly licked
face
in a frenzy familiar.... 

damn, Gram.
mustard sauce on your
love organ
isn't that hot-dog GOOD? 

Especially those parachuting Elvi
and penis
impersonators with long sideburns! 

Food is good and god is great
so where the FUCK
is my barf-bag--- 
this is one trip i just can't handle. 

Snap Crackle 
POP
goes my sanity:
introspective ME and MINE
but momma's got the cheeze-whiz.picture uploaded!!!!!
