Cinco de mayo and ‘shrooms


A soccer speckled 
road ran
across my bedroom floor
and some chick i don't know
threw
grasshoppers.

"Does That Make You Horny?"

Somebody's pickle 
did a dance
i'd rather not do
for you
because my pants are
enTIREly too tight!

BREAK

A guitar shaped 
like a banana 
played my favorite song
and then He whispered
in my ear...
"did you forget there's a tortilla
in your mouth--maybe you should chew".1997,
