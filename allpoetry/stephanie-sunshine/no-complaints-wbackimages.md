LOVE GRAVY
is the middle name of a girl from
around the way
who can eat a hot-dog 
in 
one
BITE (swallow).
no mustard
no ketchup
no relish or cheese, 
just
G-R-A-V-Y.
Gravy's all she needs
to satisfy
her HUNGER (growl).
She has no teeth, but i've never heard
a dog...
HOT dog, that is...
