tensely taught
abdomen, women
conceive:
plagiarism
to a new degree 

(yes, i was with child) 

sleepy defenses
infection reaction
reflexive:
seeping sustenance,
life on linoleum 

(two months too early) 

siren ride
a bumpy night 

(i haven't picked a name) 

d
r
i
p
drip
impending presentation
stalled:
brief relief is sweet
like sour content 

("it's too early for the lungs") 

medically enhanced
maturation
ensures
"life"
at the end of the tunnel 

(steroids, you say?!) 

suppositively controlled
continuation
complete:
a teeny head
between my feet 

(i have a son!) 

whisk, whisk
removal is quick
rushed to the glass
housing
wires and monitors;
intubation (Ish!) 

(trent)

languo hair
and yellow skin
(i swear i'll never break him)isn't he adorable?!
