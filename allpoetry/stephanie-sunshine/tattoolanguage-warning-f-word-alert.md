Deaf Right (no) maybe Left

squeak crackle

let me think this over

[E S R E V E R]

perhaps tomorrow would be better but

then again maybe not and god, the music

is fast and i just can't stop writing so

i guess maybe this is totally 

FUCKED (up and down)

(and back again)

[E S R E V E R]

this is NOT what i had in mind at all

but wait... (question)

this is empty

and they say that what you write is what 

you are and you are what you eat...

[CONFUSION FUCKING CONFUSION]

i don't eat NOTHING but all i can

fucking write is Nothing at all.

Alcohol talks in strange ways

sometimes.

My room is cold

and...

i got my first TATTOO yesterday.i'll give you a little background information regarding this piece.  i joined the military right after highschool.  i went to advanced individual training in Georgia directly after Basic.  the weekend before heading off to Germany, i got a tattoo.  i was staying in a hotel room (all alone) because most of my classmates had already left for permanent duty station assignments.  i went to a bar for the first time (underage) and had my first REAL encounter with alcohol.  tonight, i found this poem i wrote in the middle of a dance club (bar) and considered it to be interesting.  hmm... so that's all i really have to say about that.
