REAL GENIUS

half-caste
headspace
(modern prophet dream)
----------------
the blur of insanity
full metal jacket
hostage 
(bound)
----------------
walking shadows
bringing out the DEAD
dead ALIVE
(sleepers)
----------------
blasphemy
beyond
delinquent
(on edge)
----------------
neverending story
nil by mouth
(hush)
(HusH)...
stir of echoes

REAL GENIUSfebruary 2002, this was written in response to an "exercise" found in another forum (poetrykingdom.com)  the challenge was to construct a poem consisting of ONLY movie titles.  one per line.
