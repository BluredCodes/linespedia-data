a frenetically kinetic orgasmiquick reaction 
regurgitated reality
spawn of circum(stance)/(stantial) evidence 
eradicates conviction
evicting convention: risking audience appeal
feel free to peel 
the layers of ME
and see
perfection through perceptionthis was written in response to a challenge:  write a poem about the way you write. Any comments would be appreciated.
