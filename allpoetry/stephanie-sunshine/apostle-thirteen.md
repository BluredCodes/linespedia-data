a teeny
tiny 
dot
of man-made-thought
plops
upon a page...
creating godandchristianityandconscience.

Confusion comes in
sizes-sexes-shapes,
preying on the impressionable.

..and I am a disciple
(APOSTLE THIRTEEN)
of the 
Impossible.1997...sometime (recently revised),
