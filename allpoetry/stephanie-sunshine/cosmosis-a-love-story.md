Classic Elusive:
(unnecessary) explanation
"concrete non-existence"
(un)formed expression
relative to a moment....

(a glance, by chance
was all it took
your smile - the bait
your eyes - the hook)

Cosmic Convergence:
a twilight infiltration
traces of tips of fingers
exclusively engaged in
silent conversation

(alive with expectations 
of satisfaction past enough;
engulfing in a moment
all freedom and control)

Unconventional Players:
(battle broken blues)
avid emotionalists
de-constructed (abused) 
beyond recognition.

(infatuation imposing
not-so-temporary
attention deficit disorder;
free from focus
bondage of thought)

Distance Unresolved:
occupational separation
re-directed affectations
"spawn" of settlement
My dear, it's elemental...

(imagined proximity
even better
-not quite-
than the real thing)
