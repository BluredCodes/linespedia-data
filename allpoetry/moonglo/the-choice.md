The Choice
Everything changes
I always told you so
people turn away
relationships become bitter
my heart grows weak
always moving on
the voices are much louder
time keeps rolling by
we're no longer together
the footsteps come nearer
I can hear them now
nothing lasts to eternity
the image of us disapears
the tears come gently
over promises broken
I know it's time to let go
I wish I knew how
the words come so hard
good-bye has come
this time forever
