title: "Heart Turned To Stone"

Lying on the blank white floor
my heart once so alive
now turned to cold grey stone
you rest your beautiful cheek upon me
my unceasing tears roll
falling upon your tanned skin
our love
once as real as anything
it's been lost 
I miss it so much
the pain is on my face
I know you see it
so clear
not fading
now staring into your eyes
I don't see a thing
