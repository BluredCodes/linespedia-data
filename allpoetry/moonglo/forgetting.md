writer: "moonglo"

Neglecting the right
mindless without care
thoughts vanish
you just sit there and stare
missing the days
I can't describe how so
continuing moving on
the wish remains
one last day
the smile melting ice
putting the past behind
forgetting the feelings
the words disapear
during long cold days
I forgot to say
the moment comes
I miss you so much
