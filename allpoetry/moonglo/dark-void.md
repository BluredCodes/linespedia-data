Filling in this dark void
deep within my heart
trying to keep my feelings from you
I don't know where to start
wishing to feel love
in it's purest form
longing to rid this blackness
with these thoughts I mourn
foolish hopes don't disapear
reality is just too cold
I need to kill this lasting fear
the answer you've fortold
