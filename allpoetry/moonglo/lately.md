---
title: "Lately"
writer: "moonglo"

Words wasted
jealousy rages
sadness overcoming
starting with a sigh
ending with tears
these weak words
I wish would disapear
naive feelings grow
as life has never been fair
promises fail
I should never believe
turning back to pathetic ways
I still can't forget
long cold days
nothing is different
just the same
freezing tears
no longer explained
