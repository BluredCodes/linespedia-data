---
title: "My Deepest Thoughts"

~Love
Beautiful red roses blossoming
the deepest feeling felt
devoted attachment of the heart
never to be forgotten
~Lust
Strong desire
decietful thoughts 
causing unessesary pain
mistaken emotions 
~Sadness
Depressed ways
tears stream
falling down to the dirt
sorrowfully consumed soul
unwanted remorse
~Hate
Jagged thorns from the wilted rose
cutting through soft skin
letting the blood run free
bringing the worst pain
tearing up the soul
