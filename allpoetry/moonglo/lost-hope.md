---
title: "Lost Hope"
writer: "moonglo"

Passing the days
losing what is loved
finally when all is failed
and beauty is washed away
a glimmer of hope appears
I saw it in your eyes
you carried me though the clouds
only to disapear
I no longer feel anything
when my tears finally subside
and I'm alone again
my thoughts turn cold
