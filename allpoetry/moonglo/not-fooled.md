---
title: "Not Fooled"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "not-fooled"
source: "AllPoetry"

Searching for the perfect love
true and at first sight
blackened heart awakens
no longer putting up a fight
pushing it all away
just living useless life
day to day passing
careless but not fooled
cautious to trust
feelings are overuled
