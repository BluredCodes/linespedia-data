---
title: "Forever"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "forever"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Words engraved
deep in my soul
the love I felt 
was never whole
regret confines me
between these white walls
false promises left me
and I began to fall
tired and bruised
I watched the bodies
they continued to pass by
in and out of my life
the pain they felt 
I will never forget
to eternity I will remember
I can promise you this
sins unforgiven 
were never missed
