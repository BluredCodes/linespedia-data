---
title: "Wake Up"
writer: "moonglo"

Sinking below
falling behind
waiting too long
so many answers to find
losing the chance
neglecting you so much
why can't you see
I long for your touch
naive in ways 
I can't explain
suspicious of you
I'm so very vain
I wish I could wake up
forget all this pain
instead I just lay here
listening to the rain
I don't know how to act
to make this right
everything is so different
when you are in sight
