title: "Vanished Sanctity"

Bringing down the angel wings
from passion which only hate brings
tainted by the cruel love
fallen from the sky above
the tarnished soul bleeds bitter blood
inside the mind where these thoughts flood
fire burns inside the corrupted soul
this disheartened life was never whole
