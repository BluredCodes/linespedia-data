title: "That Used To Be Me"

So pretty and young
holding his hand
smiling the whole time 
not much she demands
ignoring the warnings
they can't be true
adoring his sweet smile
if only she knew
finally the time is near
her world comes crashing to the ground
this was her biggest fear
no answers she ever found
I look at the pain on her face
and see what I used to be
memories flooding back I can't erase
now when I see her, I see me
