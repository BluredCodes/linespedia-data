---
title: "Need You Here"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "need-you-here"
source: "AllPoetry"

Crossing paths
causing pain
I am so sorry
I miss you again
wish you saw
need you here
tired eyes
so far but near
jealous thoughts
crossing minds
falling asleep
I am so blind
looking fake
long blonde hair
giving it all
why don't you care
