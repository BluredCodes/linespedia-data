---
title: "Queen Of Hearts"

White soft feathers fall
gleaming in the night
taking footsteps slowly
all hope was out of sight
pain sharp as glass shards
cutting through her soul
picking up the deck of cards
scattered down below
looking for the answers
inside her mind they're bound
searching for the queen of hearts
it's no where to be found
