---
title: "One Moment"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "one-moment"
source: "AllPoetry"

Frosty air
cold skin
touch unfelt
it's over
breaths cease
shaking uncontrollably
huddled in the corner
pitch black darkness
shadows approach
the times unexpected
the body thats fading
is only mine
