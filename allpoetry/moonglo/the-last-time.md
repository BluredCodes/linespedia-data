Looking one last time
through the ever fading body
out to the world known so well
tears blurring her vision
the bittersweet time arrived
too excruciatingly slow
now frighteningly fast
the shadow cast over
down to the ground she fell
dirty hands by her side
returning to the muting quietness
just the blank void
lifelessness recreated
still rest like before birth
no emotions, no pain
just nothing
