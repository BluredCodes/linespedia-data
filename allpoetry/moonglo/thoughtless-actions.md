---
title: "Thoughtless Actions"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "thoughtless-actions"
source: "AllPoetry"

Freezing ice cold hands
keep touching her pale skin
not knowing whats going on
no feelings from within
without stopping on the face
lucid eyes search the room
events erased from memory
in the shadows they loom
