---
title: "Giving Up"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "giving-up"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23705-Giving-Up-by-moonglo"
---

Curling smoke
fingers entwine
choking with fear
in these feelings of mine
burning my eyes
the flames grow larger
searching in vain
life becomes harsher
the smoke thickens
I can no longer breathe
giving it all up
on these thoughts I leave
