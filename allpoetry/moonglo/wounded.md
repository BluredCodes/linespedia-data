---
title: "Wounded"
writer: "moonglo"

The serenity of your life
has turned it's back on you
wicked tables have turned
and voices have shifted
faithfulness has faltered
you've stumbled and tripped
now you know what it's like
on the other side
jealousy has betrayed you
by becoming your best friend
this tiring life of hoplessness
must be the bitter end
cruel images
mock your very exsistence
mistake upon mistake
the deadly truth has been revealed
