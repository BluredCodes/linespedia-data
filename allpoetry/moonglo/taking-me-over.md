---
title: "Taking Me Over"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "taking-me-over"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46681-Taking-Me-Over-by-moonglo"

This feeling grows in me
like cancer deep inside
multiplying rapidly
I watch helpless
this infestation takes over
a disease that's controlling
growing fast and so painful
running my pitiful life
killing me slowly
when it's done the damage
and leaves finally
I die with it
