---
title: "Weakness"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "weakness"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Can't describe 
the feeling inside
keeping it trapped
something I need to hide
hating the ways
I have come to know so well
passing the days
in this living hell
everything builds up
trying to stay strong
maybe this whole time
I have always been wrong
