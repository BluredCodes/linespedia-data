---
title: "Broken Skies"
writer: "moonglo"

Angel wings
this beauty is bliss
ripped to shreds
love that is truly missed
rain from the storm
falls hard on the ground
envy is this form
is not often found
blood from the cuts
stream down quivering hands
stuck in a rut
deep in this deadly land
time changes the feeling
truth turned to lies
angels are weeping
under broken skies
