---
title: "Haunting My Thoughts"

Possessed by the harsh words
those restless memories stirred
sick laughter keeps haunting my thoughts
with those reminders I constantly fought
blameless explanations come to mind
purity in truth I wished to find
you threw away the honest ways
which I missed so many days
