Days pass endlessly
our times remain unforgotten
the words still rip through my soul
as fresh as that day
the wind whips my hair
the ice freezes on my skin
my soul, it aches
my heart, it kills
I feel your fingertips
as clear as before
what secrets do you hold
what feelings remain within
the blanket of emotions
it covers me thick
each answer uncovered
breaks my heart again
and when I think its over
another complication begins
