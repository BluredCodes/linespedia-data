---
title: "Paper Doll"
writer: "moonglo"
slug: "paper-doll"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23703-Paper-Doll-by-moonglo"
---

Cut me out
make me perfect
dress me up
do what you like
I am powerless
drained of life
abuse your right
crumple me up
throw in the garbage
as time passes
I no longer make you happy
bored with the ways
the time has come to be disposed of
light the match
and watch as I burn
desinigrate to mere ashes
wipe away the memories
time is standing still
