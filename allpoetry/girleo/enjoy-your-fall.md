---
title: "Enjoy Your Fall"
writer: "Girleo"
slug: "enjoy-your-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"

When you cannot break in to the sound.
When your ear drums close to the pound.

Whisper

When you die with your dolls
Pick up the dead bodies and crawl.
When you stabbed your self landing
try to fly away. Break free of the pains in your eyes.

When you kiss his lips
and you do not feel the tingling from his finger tips
Push him away, break his heart on a cold rainy day.

When you are desperate for the attention
you crave
Starve, Strive and be brave,
Jump in to the spot light for everyone to see
Break off the black jacket of misery
Let all know, The wings beneath the coat.
Let them guide your air, with a razor blade
cuting your throat.

When you lie
Keep it a secret
Let no one over power the fall.
Laugh at those who are troubled
and always stand tall.

Be cruel,
Be sexy
be calm and stay on ecstasy.

When you go out on a Friday night
enjoy the pitch black, and deaths moonlight.

After all
you did take the first step to falling
enjoy it all while crying.
