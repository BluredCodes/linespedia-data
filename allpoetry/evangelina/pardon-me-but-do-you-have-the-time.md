Excuse me, sirs, but in case you can't see,
there is definitely something wrong here 
between us and me.

You see, there is not one cup of coffee
in this boring little shop.
And quite honestly, I'm not sure if you
should be eating that porkchop.

The food has been on the floor,
but, hey!, if ya'll want more...
be my guest.

Mind if I stretch my legs 
right next to your precious 
plate of bacon and eggs?

Early morning blues
don't give too much of a boost
for my attitude.
Hahahahaha, oh you've noticed!
Well, don't mind me, dear stranger,
I'm only this way on a full moon.

Could I possibly charm you 
out of a cigarette?
And if I playfully winked at you
this mornin' and give you my best
southern drawl,
do you think I could borrow that
sweet little 'Vette?

No, I don't think I'll get my way.
But, yea, I thought it was worth a shot.
If at first you don't succeed...,
Yes, sir, that's what I was taught.

Keys? To the 'Vette?
What do you mean...to shut me up?
Well, better than nothin', I guess.
Here, let me buy you another cup...
of...what is this, anyway?
It reeks of fish.
Hell, I must be on my way.
Thank you for the keys,
and have a nice day!I'm bored.  It's almost four in the morning, and I'm bored. ;)
