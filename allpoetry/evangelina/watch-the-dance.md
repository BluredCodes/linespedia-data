---
title: "watch the dance"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "watch-the-dance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49668-watch-the-dance-by-evangelina"
---

Whisper the words with me;
  Come join the dance of pain and joy
 with the beating drums of the distant night
   til the morning dew reflects the light
  from the glorious sun.

Dance in the moments of forgetting
  or watch to release the pain
    of deadly poisons that reach your soul
   as you escape from the reign
   of a kingdom you can't control
  If you should dance, keep in mind
    the laughter that fills your heart
   can kill or heal your mind...

      so let's just forget in just a moment
     of time and let me hear the words
       as I tell you mine;
 clean your darkness as you expel the light 
     in the monotonous days of flight
   through the crystal sparkle in our eyes

  and hear the whispers
     feel the dance
        enjoy to forget
