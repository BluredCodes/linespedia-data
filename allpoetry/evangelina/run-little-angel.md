shhhhh
  
            don't cry out loud, little angel
                you know he's somewhere near
                waiting for the right moment
                to prey on your fear

        be ever so quiet, little darlin'
                i can hear the screams in your eyes
                patience, my dear
                  you're going to ruin his surprise

 shhhhhh
 
                 careful now, girl
                    or i'll lead him here
                 and then you'll be sorry, baby girl
                   when it's you lose that life you hold so dear.

       RUN!
         RUN AWAY, SWEET GIRL!
           darlin' angel, don't let me see.
           if that beast catches your heart,
            it would destroy me completely.
