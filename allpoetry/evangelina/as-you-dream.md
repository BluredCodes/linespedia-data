---
title: "As You Dream"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "as-you-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

Breaking the feverish scent of the image you place upon me
        wears my heart so thin.
   Dreaming consistently of another place of paradise
        only serves to rise with a passion that doesn't end.

   I hear you breathing next to me
     and the caress of your mouth
     nuzzles my ear...
         Just one touch as light as the wind
          sends me into a whirl of desire.
      You, my love, have always been able to take
            me higher
         than any other has done.

    Whisper my name as you dream of loving me.
    Taunt my body as you lay next to me.
    Keep me awake so I can feel the teasing pleasure
      of knowing your arms surround me.
    A soft sigh escapes from my lips
     as I long to wake you from your dream.
     Do you have any idea of what you mean to me?
     I give you my heart, my love,
      and I give you my breath.
     Sleep in everlasting dreams
      as it seems
     we are loving each other for the ultimate death.
