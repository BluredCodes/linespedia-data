title: "rain on the blues"

i shake with the petals,
and the moistened leaves,
as they beat to the music...

under the drops of warm rain.

the tones of the piano 
keys, singing the lonesome 
blues,are filling my soul...

with a beautiful tune.

this garden of serenity
releases a soft aroma
in a dream...

i never thought to come true.

and in this moment, 
a magical illusion of 
what i wish to see...
