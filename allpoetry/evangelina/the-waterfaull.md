maddening circles that
     
    seek to destroy the sanity of  the 
       kindling of flames 

        walking back and forth like

          a wild animal that does not
           want to be tamed

      caged in a dream that 
   
        won't ever come true is 
     what they feel when they are around
             
              you.

Care to take a fall in the 
     waters behind the rocks?

             Or maybe a cliff that sets
     so high above it all?the mispelling of the title is deliberate:)
