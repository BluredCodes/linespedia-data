---
title: "and is it good for you?"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "and-is-it-good-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51149-and-is-it-good-for-you--by-evangelina"
---

would you taste the incredible softness
 of our tongues laced with acid?
    would you shiver in the coldness
 of my touch?
  darlin', would you make me...
 could you take me 
  into the blush
 of exquisite rush?
   slide the excitement
 into my realm of darkest emotion.
  fade into the blackest of erosion.
  then you can get lost in the trail
  of unending passion,
as it beats to the call
    of Nature's breath.
   dandelions and roses
 fill the bed of satin.
  i feel you craving so much more.
 but i will let you know that there is
    no time for anything but a settled 
   score.
  slam the images from your mind.
  let me rape your dreams away from you
    in the same kind
  of fashion that you stole from me.
  can i unleash my anger on you
  with the scent of blood in my hands?
  can i hurt you too?

oohhh, sweet darlin', don't you see
    the hatred you created in me?
 there will never be another to betray me
   as you have done.
  i will never let another in.
  this is the vow that will remain within
     my soul
as long as i know
  that there are people like you.
 i have become what others have been to me.
  all these years, and now i finally see...
  my gentleness will only be stomped on 
    when others see it as weak.
   my strength has to rise to the top
   so that i will never have to seek
   any other who has taken my heart.
  dreams don't come true.
  there will never be 
  another you.
  there will never be
   another me.
  i won't let her in
  i won't let her show...
   that girl who knew how to love 
   will no longer be known.

     so close your eyes, my dear, and dream
       so sweetly of me.
      it will be the last time
      you will ever breathe into me.
