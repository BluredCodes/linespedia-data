---
title: "gazing"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "gazing"

---

there was a time that i would have fallen
on my knees without any pride,
in return for the truth
from any of you.

you missed the chance
to see me break down with
tears in my eyes,
for the shame i tried to hide.

you changed my eyes.
did you know you changed my eyes?
i no longer see the way i had before.

i don't want to see you anymore.
at one time, i had thought there was
connection between all of us.
now, i watch.
i watch with an empty gaze
and listen to the phrases
that are released from your mouth.
i can still laugh.
i can still find the humor
and heart.
  i just can't find a longing
  to be a part
  of you anymore.

do you understand the empty space?
does it show when you see my face?
   i just can't find the care
   to understand any
   of you anymore.
