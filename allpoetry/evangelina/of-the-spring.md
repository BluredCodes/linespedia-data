---
title: "Of  The Spring"
writer: "evangelina"

Wandering along a trail
             of dead grass and weeds,
             bare trees of broken branches,
             and blossoming flowers for the early spring...

                   I take in a deep breath
              of fresh air that lingers in 
              this slightly warm day.
              Anticipation creeps in for this 
              promise of a new way.

                   Nature is sending a preview
              of the beauty that is yet to show.
              Paintings of life after the 
              crystal diamonds of winter snow.

                   The pastel skies leave a path
              of blankets to cover the sun.
              A glorious moment to realize
              the seasons of life are never done.

                    Never questioning the riddle
              of who to thank for a day such as this.
              Those who help me see the beauty in
              a spring kiss.

                    Dancing for the joy
              of finding the hope again, I find
              my heart leaping for the fresh season
              to begin.

                    A spiritual journey, some may say,
              of knowing the darkness that exists.
              Whether it's truth or lies, I will say
              only this...
 
              To feel the splendor of knowing the darkest depth,
                 is even more fruitful when you take the first
                 step
                 to walk back again.
