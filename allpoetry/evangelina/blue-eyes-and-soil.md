---
title: "blue eyes and soil"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "blue-eyes-and-soil"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48787-blue-eyes-and-soil-by-evangelina"
---

he sings a croon 
that makes a heart fall in love
    with the lyrics of the moon
that stand above
the times of the flesh.

   blue eyes that can 
see into the depths of an ocean
     and never leave you alone

he makes me fly into the deepest crater
        of the silver lining.  I hate to fly.

      Keep me on the ground
   as I hear a sound
     of spring in July.
The heat of a grin
   that makes a bloodline boil
  as I play in the soil
     of the champagne.

          dine me with 
wine 
on the tables of the greatest romance.
     take me away 
with the witchcraft
   of the incurable dance
that you fill in my veins.

                  leave me
breathless
in the banks of the sand
        that brings the tree bark
     into the whispering of the wind.

sing me the song...
       the song that never ends
     in the hopeless fever running in my head.
