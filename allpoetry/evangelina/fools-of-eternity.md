---
title: "Fools of Eternity"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "fools-of-eternity"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52349-Fools-of-Eternity-by-evangelina"

Somewhere in the night skies
  of the mountain tops,
       which glow
         with
 a dim light of the moon
   and the stars above
       the clouds
          of
  your majestic rule,
     you have made
       me a fool
         of
       eternity.

Somewhere in the distant call
    of the orchestra's 
        symphony,
     which plays such 
        a tragedy,
     we will always be
        the fools
           of
        eternity.
