the sliding thrill,
cascading the lines,
enriches the hex
of what you do to me.

try to take me over
with a potent mix
of thick and heavy spells
that numb me.

bewitch my bones,
with a wave of song,
to tangle my mind
as you expose me.

leave me, with a hungry 
plea, to be satisfied
of your melody...

take me over...
try to take me over
as your voice entraps me.i once heard that music was the thread of life.
