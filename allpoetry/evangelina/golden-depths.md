title: "Golden Depths"
writer: "evangelina"

i walked along a golden pond to watch 
   the leaves fall from the trees
  and i was surprised to find you waiting there for me.
i noticed you had left a pile of rocks beside your feet
   to skip along the water around the sheet
   of bronze sunshine.

                            i walked along a dreaded path
                               to drink from a cup of death
                               and i was not surprised to find you had left me.
                            i noticed you had thrown the memory 
                               into the white lake of thirst
                               so that it could no longer be mine.

          i swam in the depths of the oceans
              looking for an island to be my home
             and i was not surprised to see that weird little gnome.
         i noticed you told him all kinds of lies
              and persuaded him with bribes
              to make me think you were doing just fine.

     so imagine the shock that went through every bone
          when i dialed your old number 
          and i heard that you left my life as best as you could.
     i noticed that your family blamed me for your 
         trouble, and even though it may be true,
         it was enough for me to die for you too.
