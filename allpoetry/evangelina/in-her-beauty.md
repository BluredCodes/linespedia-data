---
title: "In her beauty"
writer: "evangelina"
slug: "in-her-beauty"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49672-In-her-beauty-by-evangelina"
---

in the gracious beauty of those
  eyes that have seen so much

     i see a woman who has been able to touch
        the hearts of many 
      even though she may not know
 
in the heart of her beauty
    there has been so much
 
       and i see a woman who has been able to touch
           the hearts of many
        even though she may not know

in the enchanting laughter
    you can learn so much

        and i hear a woman who has been able to touch
           the hearts of many
         even though she may not know

in the bond of her love
      you can find so much

        and i feel the spirit of a woman who has touched
             the hearts of many
          even though she may not know
