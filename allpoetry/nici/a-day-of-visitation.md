title: "A Day of Visitation"

Wizards and witches came together
On that night we could hear
Their singing throughout the forest

One wizard brought rain
The other, a healed child
All through the village and nearby

Witches of all ages 
Chanting their sonnets of love
Introducing wisdom never before heard of

One was a child, with long red hair
She had a sister with a crystal smile
Thunder in her soul and aching cry

Wizards and witches, young and old
Warm the sky with a fallen moon
There is no wisdom if fate heeds doom.
