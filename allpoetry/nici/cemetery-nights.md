Cemetery nights have found usThe hour of beauty awakensThe sound of crying soulsAwait their final destination.Walking with you through this landHas opened my mind and eyesTo the possibility of loving An unnatural and tormented beast.The spirits protect us tonightThe moon has covered the skyBlackness hides behind the statuesYou are silent, yet I hear you sigh.We laugh together with passionThis moment of sin burns within usSpirits join us with our pleasureSoon, together, we will leave this place.
