title: "Feverish Nightmare"
writer: "Nici"

Vampire child came to me
Placed his arms above my bed
I took a knife behind my back
Stuck it through his bleeding head

Drug his body across the room
Closed the door outside the gate
Watched the sunrise crawling through
Waited for my prison mate

Indian came to cast a spell
Spoke three words I could not hear
Left me alone in my room
Nothing but a voice drawing near

"There is the killer," I heard them say
I hid behind the closet door
They had come for my disposal 
I would be breathing no more 

Softly I heard a whisper
Coming from the other side
"We will find you eventually,"
"There is no place for you to hide!"

I awoke to hear Jim Morrison
Sing seductive praises and rhyme
I wiped the sweat from my brow
And wished for better a time
