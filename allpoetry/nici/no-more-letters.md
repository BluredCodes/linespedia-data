title: "No More Letters"
writer: "Nici"

I'll send you no more letters
While you wait in your cell.
You'll have nothing to comfort you
While you rot in times Hell.

I'll send you no more pictures
No more poetry will I give.
The more I try to please you
The more you refuse to live.

I don't have to be pleasant 
I don't have to be gentle and kind.
It's not my fault I love you
And that you know nothing but crime.

I cannot change your nature
I have tried, yet only failed.
I realize now you are happy
Living life in an empty cell.

I'll send you no more cards
You don't care about the ones I have sent.
These are things I will send you no more
Until you are ready to call me again. 

They'll be no more emotions
No feelings of love or hurt.
You have lost my devotion
To the one thing that makes you a jerk.

All the poetry and letters
I sent to you during our time.
I tell you now, to detsroy them
And leave what is left behind.

Who will come to you when you are strung out
Or when you have puked green bile?
I can't imagine the poor soul
Who will have to step up to you now.
