title: "A Night Alone with Chopin"

It is I, the one who strokes the keys
The one who struggles to play along
To sit alone and feel your pain
Such misery embraces me through your song.

The room is empty and cold inside
Spirits surround me as I play
I wonder what you were thinking
While you printed your notes upon this page.

Oh lost love, immortal soul
How lovely is the hypnotic state
That fulfills my everlasting thirst
For I am cursed, being born too late.
