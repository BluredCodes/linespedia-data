She waits for him on the corner
of Maple St. and Broad.
Her long legs waning 
In the sweet southern sun.

He picks her up
Same time as usual
He's had a bad day at the office
The bills are past due.

His pregnant wife
Keeps calling his cell phone
'You want me to answer it?' she asks
He laughs and grabs her thighs.

'There's no time for the kinky stuff,'
He pulls off to the side of the road
The phone lands beneath the seat
The passenger seat can only go down so far.

Scarlet moans as he pushes inside
The mind of a whore is a cluttered dump
Pushing and shoving until she screams
The phone continues to ring.

He fucks her once more then lets her go
Leaves her four blocks away from home
Wiping the scent off of his dick
He sighs and answers the phone.

'Ive been in a meeting with my boss.'
Satisfied with his excuse, they hang up
Lighting a cigarette, he turns the corner
On Maple and Broad, she stands and waves.
