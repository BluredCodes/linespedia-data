title: "Death is Walking"
writer: "Nici"

I am walking towards death
The slick blackness of time
There are fools who wait
To see there is nothing to find

I alone, cannot change
How a human lives to die
The infectuous virus has no end
It hides behind the lie

The soul cannot escape misery
The delicate dream still exists
Memory is nothing more than light
Crawling through eternal bliss
