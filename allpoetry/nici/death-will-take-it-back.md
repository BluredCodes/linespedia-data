Bodies Bodies,everywhere

How does the stench of 

rotten flesh

not keep one from sleeping?

The cold night air

whispers hatred 

The dead walk the grounds

They search for their remains

The spirit of the dead

Will rise to their vengeance

The bodies will be claimed

The lake will be drained

Only the man who tried to hide

Will be dragged into the swamp.
