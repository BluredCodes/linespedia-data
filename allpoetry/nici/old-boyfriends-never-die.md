---
title: "Old Boyfriends Never Die"
writer: "Nici"
slug: "old-boyfriends-never-die"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6790-Old-Boyfriends-Never-Die-by-Nici"

One night he came to me
While my restless mind did creep.
His darkened eyes gazed upon my body
Slowly my soul began to weep.

Why I had not told him 'good bye' 
Or simply spoke from the heart
Was beyond my recognition
Though I knew we could not part.

His shameful eyes burned me
His pale flesh against my side
I looked upon my gold ring
Though I knew I could not hide.

The man I left five years ago
Had come for revenge.
The promise I had made to him
Had bitten me in the end.

Though the dream was over
The morning sun creeping in
In the back of my mind I heard a whisper
'Dont even think this is the end.'
