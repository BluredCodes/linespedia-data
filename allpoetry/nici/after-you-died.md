The evening was going well
We were on our third glass of cabernet,
I feared I would soon run out of things to say.

I began to notice his crooked mustache
And the way he chewed his food,
It was going to take an act of congress to get me in the mood.

I thought about him naked
and thought of his embrace,
I tried to see us together but I could not see his face.

The conversation was boring
He spoke of computers, stocks, and taxes,
If I had a knife I would've given myself several lashes.

Something made me think of you 
as he shut his mouth long enough to graze,
I knew if you were watching, you'd be rolling in your grave.

If you could have seen me with this asshole
and listened to his conservative views,
you would have wanted to spit at him and yank him by his shoes.

A terrible hacking cough
brought on by tobacco and undigested food,
interrupted my personal, internal conversation with you.

Soon the waiter brought us our bill,
I promised myself no more blind dates 
or my friend I'd have to kill.
