title: "Don't Tell Me, I'm The Devil"
writer: "Nici"

Don't Tell me
You are Old
You are Dead
You have visions
Inside your head

Don't Tell me
There is life
After Death
No more pain
With your last breath

Don't Tell Me
You have hope
You have will
The more you seek
The more it kills

And Don't Tell Me
You still love
You still pray
You have many 
Things to say

Don't Tell Me
I am evil
I am sad
I have no spirit
I am glad

Don't Tell Me
I have fallen
I am loved
I do not work 
For the spirit above

Don't Tell Me
I Am The Devil
It does no good
To tell me things 
You've never understood
