title: "A Glimpse Of Death"
writer: "Nici"

It happened 
Your addiction raped you
left you to drown.

I picked you up
Blood oozed from your mouth
Pale skin and slow heart beat
So still
So quiet.

I took you by my side
walked you down the stairs
your mother was crying
your father, still drunk on the couch.

I slid you in the seat of my car
Your head against the window
My hand inside yours
I cursed you
"I hate you! I hate you!"

Five years of love
Ten seconds of death
You tried to speak 
Your words gurgled from the blood.

Emergency Room Hell
I sat and waited
The white walls echoed
Angels played cards in the waiting room.

You had not died
No, not yet
I gave you back the ring
Once they removed
The tube from your stomach.
