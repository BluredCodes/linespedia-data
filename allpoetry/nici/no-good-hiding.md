---
title: "No Good Hiding"
writer: "Nici"
slug: "no-good-hiding"

It's not a good idea
To spill your heart
Write about your secrets
Describe your adventures 
Of sex
Drugs
Wild crazy nights
Of speaking incantations
While fucking gods

It's not a good idea
To leave your books open
Leave the house 
Without locking up your secrets
Find out later
When you return
Someone has read everything
Your soul has been violated
You are questioned
About truth
Honor
Love
Lust

It's not a good idea
To lie and say
"It isn't real"
"I often write in first person."
Because in the end
You only lie to yourself
No one should care if you had a past
No one should care if you gave yourself
To another man before marriage
Hid an escaped convict in your woods
Loved a devil
Sold your soul

It's not a good idea
To go through life
To hide your truth
Rape your innocence
Make excuses for your curse
Act as though you did not enjoy
The art of creating spirits
Afterall they were your spirits
You cared for them
And they knew you for who you were
They'd always know who you were
