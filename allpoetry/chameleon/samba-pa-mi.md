title: "Samba Pa Mi"
writer: "Chameleon"

For you,    I care, I worry.    What does she do?    What does she think?    For you,    I search my soul,    To please, to appease    You.                                                              Tristeza...    But, now, I am here.    And oh, how I hear.    I hear the beat swaying,    I hear the band playing,    I hear the samba.    I feel the earth groaning,    I feel her sweet moaning,    I feel the samba.    Don't kill the groove    With your incessant bitchin'    And if you don't move,    We will leave you.                                                             La La Ra La...    Now I know where I stand,    Away from your darkened tones.    I fly the colors of the samba    And for you, I care...                                   not!        Goodbye Sadness,    Quero de novo cantar......The phrases, 'Tristeza...La La Ra La...Quero de novo cantar....' are excerpted from a traditional Brazilian song, 'Tristeza' also known as 'Goodbye Sadness.'
