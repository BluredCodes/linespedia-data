Angel wings are everywhere
You may not see them but they are there
A glint of dust and a brush of air
To let us know how much they care

Sent by God to help us through
The bad times and the good ones too
Watching out day to day
And they never ever go away

I catch a glimpse now and then
Of what I think is my lifelong friend
Hidden in shadows and the top of the stairs
Watching silently, seeing the burdens I bear

When things get too hard and very rough
My angel steps in and says "Enough"
Powered by God to do this deed
Knowing when others are in need

Angel wings touch my heart
They have been with me from the start
They have been there my whole life through
And in the end they will be there too.
