---
title: "Control"
writer: "Just1meme"
slug: "control"
source: "AllPoetry"

Control...
Having your soul held down
Crushing your imagination
Stomping on your heart

Control...
Having enough strength to go on
Feeling your life pass you by
Living only in your past

Control...
Letting yourslef to continue breathing
Knowing your life will never be the same
Seeing a difficult future
