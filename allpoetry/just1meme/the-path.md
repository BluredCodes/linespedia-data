Traveling the path to freedom
Starting out in the right direction
Walking aimlessly to a bright future
Watching not what lied ahead
Seeing not the darkness unfold
Engulfing life in a blackened forest
Being stuck by thorns and burrs
Burying them deeper into the soul
Thrashing through the brush to find the lost path
Turning one way then the other
Hoping to catch a glimpse of where to go
Searching for that inner peace
Breathing is roughened and ragged
Running only to be pulled down into the thickness
Hitting the bottom hard
Hearing the past not far behind
Moving quickly, ready to strike
Catching of breath
Gulping of air
Pushing up to move forward
Looking down, the path is again found
Traveling will be easier now
Keeping the goal in mind.
