title: "The Cry"
writer: "Just1meme"

To sit down and cryTo pour out the emotional soulIn tiny drops for everyone to seeSpilling the pain on the groundPeople walking on itKicking it as they goScattering the inner selfThe embarrassment of having everyoneJudge the weakened stateNo pity is neededJust an ear to listenNo one is there to understandGoing on aloneWith tears rolling down the face
