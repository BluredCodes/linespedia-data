The crimson tide of love washed over them
as they shared their first embrace
trembling, crumbling in the oneness of their universe
lost in the lustrous waters of life
love unfurling in the breath of stars
drowning them,holding them prisoner to their passion
driving them to explore their desire
hearts running rampid, the tasting of flesh
swimming in their emotional ocean
the wind whispering the languages of the soul
beconing them forward
Surrendering, quinching their thirst at last.
