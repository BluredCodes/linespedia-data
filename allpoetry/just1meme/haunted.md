Death, I know you wellFeeling life slipping awayHoping this time eternal salvation awaitsJust to be born againMany lives I have knownHaunting my memoriesSplinters of an unknown pastBetrayal, greed and lost lovesLooking in the mirrorNot really knowing who I amOr how I should feelScenes flooding backSeeing through another's eyesGlimpses of what was left behindI go an striving blindlyFor and unknown futureWondering why I am still hereWhat bad things did I do?And how can I fix them now?
