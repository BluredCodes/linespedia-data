title: "Chase a Cloud"

Chase a cloud into the sky
Watch your life go floating by
Remember a time not so far away
When you were a child out at play
Remember how it was to have some fun
Running around in the sun
Not a care you had that day
To hear yourself laugh that way

Chase a cloud into the sky
Watch the rainbows floating by
All the colors are parts of your life
Even the ones that cut like knives
Life can sometimes cause you pain
Watching it all go down the drain
Pick yourself up and go on
Think of what would happen if you were gone

Chase a cloud into the sky
They don't just have to be memories floating by
Find out what's important to you 
Go out and make your dreams come true
Act like today is your last
Stop dwelling in the past
Go out and have some fun
It's your time to be in the sun.
