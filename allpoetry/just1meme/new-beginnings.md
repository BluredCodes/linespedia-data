The first rays of dawn have shown
The brightness of first light
Reaching out its fingers
Beckoning the soul
To grasp the purity within
Fighting its foe with might
Opening vast territories of promise
Darkness has flown on an unknown path
Stretching far beyond sight
A new day has begun
Also a new life.April 2001, Does my poem flow or does it seem to choppy?
Is my poem to short?
