Thank you God for our many choices
To behold the day and the night
To shine in the glory and wonder of this bright world
Not to be drawn in by shadow and mistrust and pain
To behold the enchantment of a child's smile
To see the love in that upturned face
Not to turn it away
To love a man completely, without secrets
Not setting limits and conditions for my love
To know what true friendship is
And be able to give it back
Not only one of convenience
To walk in the light and see promise in the world
And all who live in it
Negativity blackens the soul
Choose to look for the greatness in all things
The choice is yours to make
