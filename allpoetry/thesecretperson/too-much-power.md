---
title: "Too Much Power"
writer: "thesecretperson"

A smile of death
radiates from you all
You have grown far too strong
You are only human,
and yet have been given
the power of the Gods.
The power to chose, 
whether a man lives or dies
rests in your hands.
So instead of becoming 
as wise as you can
you fill your minds with hate.
A persons right to live 
and be loved does not rest
in his religion or race.
So come back to earth,
bring your head down from the clouds
And just be human again.
