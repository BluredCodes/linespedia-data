---
title: "I wonder"
writer: "thesecretperson"

I wonder how life would be,If Bush was not in the White HouseThe man who murdered over 120 men last year alone.Two wrongs don't make a right dude.I wonder how life would be,If America had not risen to power.Half the wars would not have happenedOur lives in peace.Sharing the world, not hogging the power.I wonder how life would be,If our TV's were not dominatedwith the ignorant 'American' Blissfully unawareOf all the worldNot really knowing the power they hold.I wonder how life would be,If we all had respect for each otherAll got along and there was calm in the worldno fighting, peaceHow very very unlikely now. This is an Australian chewing stick, see I know all the countries in Europe!
