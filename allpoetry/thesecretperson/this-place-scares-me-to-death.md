I'm going to die soon...this place, it will kill me, it will frighten meand I will drop and die because my heart will have stopped for too long.I'm going to crack soon...this place willtear me in halfit will be delightedto see the blood spiltthey will obviouslybe cheering a death on once more.I'm going to cry soon...the place won't stop until I do.they will do whatever it takes.they'll torment meand call me bad names. they will stab me, rape mesteal from me... anything to upset me.I'm going KILL SOON,because I'm going INSANE.this place has take SO MUCH from meand they expect me stay calm?Well I'm sorrybut that can't BE DONEI'm going to have to take revengejust as this place does.If someone ruins something of theirsthem immediately 'This IS WAR'and we all are lostwe are washed up in the systemforever.yes this is about America, i hope you don't say it's crap this time, it is a view, it shows emotion, thats all that matters really...
