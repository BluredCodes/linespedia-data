So you all thought this would work did you?
Bomb the hell out of the Afghani people,
kill a few thousand, and maybe they'd give up?
Well here's a joke, you've just proved their point!
That you Americans are nothing but hypocrytes.
You blather on about free speech and all that crap,
and then assult me for my views, the Afghanis for their life,
even to the point of murdering innocents.
You are no better than Oslama, Hitler, or Stalin. 
Satan has that special place in his heart for you.
