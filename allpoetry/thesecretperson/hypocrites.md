Oh Puleeeease!!! I never once asked you to 'be like me' Just to wake up, and stop following your leaders so blindly, with your rose coloured glasses oh so obvious. U Think that just becasue your leader is the president of the United States, that he must be right, not matter what. Well here's a wake up call: He's not, and you're all dicks for following him. And why would I be arrested??? Do you honestly have that much faith in your system? And I thought you liked free speech, though your bitching at me for excercising my rights...Hypocrytes...
