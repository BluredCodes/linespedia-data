Under the velvet starry skyI saw my life, it made me cryI saw him with eyes so brightin which angels once took flightI saw love lost, saw it sadly dieI dug his grave - I let it lieUnder empty sky: a watery graveFor love given I couldn't saveIn solitude now a stone does standIn swirling fog where lake meets landinto this secret waters he was bornewith Logres' threat he will be rebornIn eternal damnation there is no hopethrough darkest of nights I still gropefor whence he rises from Avalon's gatesmight I not be with as he Logres saves?
