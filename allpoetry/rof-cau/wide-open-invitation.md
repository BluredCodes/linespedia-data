In a kingdom far awayseagulls cross the airfishers' children playoh! colorful the fairat the castle's gatesI built it myselfwith silver and goldand inside on a shelfnestled in satin foldsis my image of youCountless the journeysof Isis across the skythe years hold the keysof chances gone byThis castle I built with gates wide openaccept my invitation
