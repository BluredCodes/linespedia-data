Camelot

Camelot, thy beauty is to me
like the forests of giant trees
recalled from the days of yore
like the morning mist at the shore
painted by an untainted sunrise

Camelot, thy well traveled road
will ever the weary travelers bore
to find home; lay down burdens
contend to memory the far shores

Camelot! blinding yon brilliance
How majestic thy are seen to stand,
spires reaching towards the clouds
thy king's glory exceeding mere lands
