Admiration

As the Italian statuette gushes forth a fountain 
I wouldst cascade all of my love unto thy desert
As wont to be for those who art so blessèd
I wouldst witness the blooming of thy garden

When starred sky lovingly embraces gliding moon
I wouldst free thee from thy fortresses high
I wouldst fly thee higher than the highest sky
for thy brightness to shine unto all thy gifted boon

As thy humbly receiveth thy world's adulation
Couldst I but kneel to receive thy salvation"me"
