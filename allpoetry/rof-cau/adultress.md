As the sun flashes from Excaliburmine eyes reflect love for theeAs the trees bloom in thy gardenmine love groweth with thy smileAnd wherever mine quests taketh methe starréd sky embraces thy dreamsthe moon guides these into mine heartby these alone I am truly blessédAlas! As I roamed far from thy homethy lovely dreams were not of we:but of my beloved and most trustedwho pledged to safeguard thee
