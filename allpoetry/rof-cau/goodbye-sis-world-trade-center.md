title: "GOODBYE SIS (WORLD TRADE CENTER)"
writer: "Rof Cau"

No poems today
the heart's gone astray
No poems tomorrow
the future is sorrow

if only my yesterday
could be born again
with teasing Sis at play
I used to shout at her then

Now with vision clouded
I yearn for the time
in which I never doubted
a future for me and mine

but the sky became black
dust turned all to gray
fortune changed tack
stole my Sis away

Swirling and falling
which is up - which is down?
In sleep I hear you calling 
and I cry your name

Yesterday I had my Sis
funning, clowning around
now she's forever stilled
in this quiet I'm mowed down
