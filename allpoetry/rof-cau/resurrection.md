I can hear the crickets'night-symphonyI can feel the grasssoftly under meI can taste the dewso wet upon meI can smell the rosessurrounding meI can see the moonshining down on meAll my senses must be lyingfor am I not dead?Have I not been dying since you left?The time my heart got broken ... or was it the pieces of my super inflated ego? With this I figured it out...
