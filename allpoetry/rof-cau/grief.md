title: "Grief"

The wind, it flapped about
that I can recall
and how I wanted to shout
my grief to all

My tongue remained still
my mind dumb
my mouth robbed of will
my body numb

The dark, it wrapped around
that which was my soul
In this grief truth abound:
        you -
 were my soul
