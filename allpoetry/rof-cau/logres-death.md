Logres oh Logres home of my heartwhich now must be kept as hardthough thy hills' silent screams echoand reverbate painfully in my soulHow I prayed to thee oh Mighty Onefor Guenevere make her sins undoneto turn and find her again by my sidenot led to the stake with hands tiedThough her body and beauty she gaveher love and soul for Lancelot she savedAs the pyre smoke fled toward the skymy grief pierces like a falcon's cryAs the flames grew bolder I sawknights' treason altering doomin this pureness of joy I sawLogres' death in gathering gloom
