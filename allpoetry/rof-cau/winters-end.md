---
title: "Winter's End"
writer: "Rof Cau"
slug: "winters-end"
source: "AllPoetry"

Somewhere in an ancient field
birds tentatively begin to sing
and flowers unfurl their bloom
all herald the birth of spring
and the end of winter's cold

The cathedral of stone is old
like the woman at its door
she shivers for in her soul
is a cold and murdered core
where blow the coldest winds

Long since her winter begun
where she stood on her own
dressed in white for the one
but now she is old and alone
praying for her winter's end
