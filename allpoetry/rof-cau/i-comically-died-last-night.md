---
title: "I Comically Died Last Night"
writer: "Rof Cau"
slug: "i-comically-died-last-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4063-I-Comically-Died-Last-Night-by-Rof-Cau"
---

I comically died last night
slip-falling while I tried
to lay my groping hands
on your Levi-perfect ass

I comically died last night
slip-falling while I eyed
the thought-provoking sight
of your wonder bra-filled plight

I comically died last night
slip-falling while I cried
lying artistically on the ground
losing you to the crowd

I comically died last night
my tombstone thus inscribed:
'He didn't eat much in his life
but for breast and thigh he died'
