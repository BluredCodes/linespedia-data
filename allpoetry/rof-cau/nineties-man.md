---
title: "Nineties Man"
writer: "Rof Cau"
slug: "nineties-man"
source: "AllPoetry"

a bleached hairdo comes walking past
ears pierced with diamonds bright
a thousand-buck smile, cut to last
a complete collection of teeth, polished white
shines from sun bed skin, darkly tanned
his uncalloused hands, tastefully manicured,
lightly displays a car key from a luxury brand
but in his heart he's not assured
of ever being a man
