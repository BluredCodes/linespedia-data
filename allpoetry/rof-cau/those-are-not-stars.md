---
title: "Those Are Not Stars"
writer: "Rof Cau"
slug: "those-are-not-stars"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3878-Those-Are-Not-Stars-by-Rof-Cau"
---

those are not stars
that so brilliantly shine
from places afar,
my friend,
but droplets of wine
spilled by wanton gods

Those are not stars 
that so brightly flicker
from heavens afar,
my friend,
but bitter tears
torn from mortal souls

Those are not stars
that so radiantly light
the skies overhead,
my friend,
but the souls of the Lost
denied their rest

those are not stars
that do illuminate
the universe so wide,
my friend,
but the blazing pyres
of our destiny
