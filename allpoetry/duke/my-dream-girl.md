You and i walk down the beach,
The minuture waves crashing at our feet,
The air getting cooler,
The breeze is so comfortable,
We just want to shut our eyes,
The sun looks like the light went underneath the sparkaly ocean,
As it gets darker you start to shiver,
So i give you my shirt to put over ya,
As we cuddle the night away,
Our lips found there way together,
We touch each  other all over softly,
The sky turing to a bunch of pretty colors over the water,
I wake up im back in the city,
Love ya Shawn Michelle,
