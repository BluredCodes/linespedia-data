We are about to go in a situation,
A situation is going to be different,
Unexplained thoughts runs threw everyones mind,
Hopefully everything is going to be fine,
Just waiting...Just waiting,
Thats the hardest part,
She screams out loud,
The pains are feeling greater,
squeezing my hand,
Doing all i can do,
Sometimes hyper-ventalating,
Hurry its almost time,
All of a sudden blood shot out,
Its starting,
A little while later,
My daughter is born,
Love you Cheyanne Renee Duke
Love you Shawn Michelle Osborne
