---
title: "My Revolution!"
writer: "Duke"
slug: "my-revolution"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49979-My-Revolution--by-Duke"
---

Black eyes look into the nothing,
Walls bleeding from the unknown,
World turning in all directions,
Fire rises from beneath,
Bells blasting in the distance,
Sky raining fear to us all,
What the hell is going on here,
People trying to move but is hopeless,
Confusion of the world burns into a panic,
Are we going to die asks everyone,
Nobody dies everyone has a revolution,
