title: "Stress From Struggle"

Fire fights with fire,
Know one knows who's hotter,
Water fights with water,
Know one knows who's  deeper,
Lightening fights lightening,
Know one knows who's brighter,
Bears fight with bears,
Know one knows who wins the fights,
Just like the human race,
And i ask,
Why do we hurt each other,
Why cant people live in peace,
With peace we can live in glory,
We just put more stress on our self,
Everyone forgot the power of real freedom,
