You wake up in the middle of the day,
Just another bloody day,
Looking over to the other side of the street,
Lays a rotting corpse,
What happened?You asked,
I feel fine,
Did i do that?Couldnt have,
You look down at your feet,
What the hell,
Seeing your own flesh tore up,
Two bullets hit your chest,
Holy shit im dead,
Then you look back over to the other body,
Walking over to the other corpse,
Two bullets in his chest,
You killed each other,
Why could we be so stupid,
Just to see who was the hard ass,
Shadows come from the walls,
What is that you ask?
Its demands coming to take you away,
You are in another demention,
Shadows screaming at you,
Youll never here the end of it,
Im sorry begging for forgiveness,
But where your at there is no such thing,
Where is the guy i shot?
In another lifetime,
Why cant i see him?
To kill your guilt,
Why does people have to kill for fun?
To prove who is more supid,
Blackness from the damned,
Its your turn,
