Dad was there to make me smile

Dad was there to make me laugh

He was there to comfort me

All those days in the past

He was there to protect me

To shelter me from harm

But those days have come and gone

No more holding me in his arms

He said he’d never leave me

That he would always be there

But his promises were lies

Why is life so unfair?

He disappeared without a trace

And left damage behind

All that lingers is hurt and pain

They tread throughout my mind

His voice becoming harder to hear

His face becoming harder to see

Makes me wonder if a day will come

When I’ve forgotten him totally

My love for him has never shaken

And my heart stills holds him true

Though I was once angered with him

Forgive him was all I could do

Dad’s not here to talk to me

Dad’s not here to walk by me

But still I know he loves me

And together again we will be.
