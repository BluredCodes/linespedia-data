I can see through your devilish lies
 
I see the evil that lurks behind your eyes
 
I know the betrayal that awaits for me
 
I recognize the false promises that come so easily
 
You cannot contain me with your words
 
Trust I once had in you is now all a blur
 
You and I will never be
 
For you are nothing, not good enough for me
 
You had your chance once long ago
 
But you destroyed it, you acted low
 
You live your life in constant denial
 
But will you break come the final trial?
 
I can see through your devilish lies
 
Can you see the pain that lurks behind MY eyes?I have no title for this one (obviously) But I'd love to hear any suggestions...
