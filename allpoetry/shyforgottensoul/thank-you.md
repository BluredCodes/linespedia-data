I see the star that shines bright in the distance
Makes me ponder about my own existence
When did I get here?
And when do I leave?
Not that I’m complaining
But the thoughts just come naturally
Where do I belong?
And how do I get there?
It’s so confusing, it just isn’t fair
A wave washes over me and I stop and think
There must be a purpose, and my doubts begin to shrink
To live on this earth as one of my own
Is something far more grand, then I ever thought I could bestow
Everyday brings new pleasures, some great and some small
But finding true love is the best one of all
Love is the grandest thing known to man
And I thank god, for being loved like I am
Though times may come when I feel down and blue
I just say your name, and look up to you
You shield me from danger, and lead me from harm
With you in my life, I can never go wrong
You are the star I see shining bright in the sky
Reminding me to never give up, and stay strong with pride.
