Here I stand
You are far away
I miss your smell
I miss your touch
I miss you
I long for the sound of your voice
For the warmth of your touch
For the comfort of your embrace
I miss your tender kiss
The one that makes me shiver
When will you come home to me?
My love at a distance.An old poem I found that I wrote when I was 14 in highschool.
