Light turns into dark 

And the feeling grows nearer

The feeling of needing you

Of holding you dear

Time has become my enemy

As it stands still

Frozen, unmoving

So hard it could kill

Quietly unnerving me

Down to the core

Visions of you inside my head

Begin to trick me once more

Sanity quivering

Straining to escape

Looking for an exit

One of any size, or shape

My thoughts begin to torment me

Like some ghost in the night

And I long for you more

With all of my might

As hard as it seems

For me to get by

I hold my head high

And continue to try

I await the moment

When the illusion fades

No more hoping and praying

No more fears to shade

Reality will be my saviour 

My one knight in arms 

The feeling of you holding me

Protecting me from harm

Hurry love

Please be quick

Come to me my darling

Waiting..... I sit.
