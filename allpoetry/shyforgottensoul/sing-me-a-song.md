title: "Sing Me A Song"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"

Sing me a song
A song that I'll love
One that will hold you dear to me

Sing me a song
A song from your heart
One that expresses your love

Sing me a song
A song that is pure
One with honesty and tenderness

Sing me a song
A song from your lips
One that flows with your heavenly voice

Sing me a song
A song to put me to sleep
One that will give me pleasant dreams

Sing me a song
A song for all time
One that I'll remember forever more.
