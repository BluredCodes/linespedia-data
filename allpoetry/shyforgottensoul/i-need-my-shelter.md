---
title: "I Need My Shelter"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"

Sounds of the raindrops
Falling softly
The feeling of them
Touching my face
Mixing with my tears
Sadness has overcome me
Alone, so alone
Unable to reach
The shelter I dream
Seems like it’s gone
Perhaps it’s crumbled
The rain, the cool rain
Is my only comfort
But it’s love is cold
So cold
Silence, it’s here
Along with the rain
But the silence is thick
And unsettling.
