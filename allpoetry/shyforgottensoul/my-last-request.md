If I had one last request
Come my dying day
My mind wonders what it'd be..

To be held by you one more time,
And feel your warmth embracing me

To kiss your soft lips again,
Feeling the butterflies in my stomach

To look deeply into your loving eyes,
Getting lost in the depths of them

To dance with you one last time,
To our song as we sway together on clouds

To hear you say you love me again,
My heart skipping at the sound of your sweet voice

To make love to you once more,
Melting into you and feeling your passion

To do them all would be grand
But come my dying day my love,
My last request is this...
Love me...
And remember me not with tears,
For that is not how I'll remember you.
Remember me with smiles...
