So I'm not your image of the 'perfect woman'
Just because I'm not some beauty queen
Doesn't mean you can ignore me
Or that I'm not good enough to be seen

I have style
I have grace
So what's with the nasty look
The one you carry upon your hateful face

I am beautiful
So much more then you realize
If you'd actually open your eyes
I think you'd be in for one hell of a surprise

But you constantly judge
Who do you think you are?
Now it's time for me to bitch
About how you've gone too far

You throw false words out
In my direction
About how I'm lacking
About how I'm not your idea of perfection

You don't even know me
And yet you cast this view
You don't even realize
I'm too good for you

That's fine, whatever
Go on with your shit
But when you wake up alone
