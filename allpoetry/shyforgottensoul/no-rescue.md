---
title: "No Rescue"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"

I'm surrounded
And yet, I'm all alone
Screaming
Yet, I can't be heard
Drowning
In my own self pity
No rescue
As usual
Hurting
Down to the core
My soul,
Bleeds
My heart,
Cries
Why do I exist?
Is there really a point for me?
Makes me wonder
I've stumbled
Into a deep hole
I try to claw my way out
But I only fall in further
Still,
No rescue
This will be the death of me
I'm certain of it
Oh well
Who cares?
Oh,
That's right
No one.
