Like a distant light in the dark

I see you

Your hands reaching out to me

I try to grasp them

But all I catch is the cold air

And before I know I'm standing alone

Surrounded by a fear that whispers my name

A fear like no other

My heart crumbles and breaks

Crying it's bittersweet symphony of longing

Of being alone

Alone and cold

Wanting to feel the passion of my destined love

I call to you but no words are heard

And before I know it

Like a flickering flame against the wind

You disappear

And my world turns black

No beautiful light cascading a path for me

And my fear takes hold of me.
