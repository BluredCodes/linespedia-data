Darkness has surrounded meIt’s forcing me to the groundI’m struggling to be freeTo make it safe and soundThe cold has overwhelmed meAnd I shutter in the nightTerrified of what lies aheadWill everything be alright?Hard times have fallenFallen down upon meThe journey is long and windingThough the future I cannot seeI pray for a light of hopeOne of which will be my guideI open my eyes and look about meThere’s my light, shining bright with prideYou’ve given me the light I neededThe faith that I had lostI now know I can make itEven through this bitter frostI know things will be hardThere is no doubting thisBut now I’ve found determinationSomething I once did missBut you have done itBrightened my pathNow I hold my head higherI can overcome this deadly wrath!
