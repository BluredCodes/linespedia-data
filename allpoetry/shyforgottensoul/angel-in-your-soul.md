There's an angel hidden in your soul
Whether you choose to let people see it, nobody knows
But I've seen a glmipse of it once or twice
I've seen the heavenly light dance within your eyes
You sheild it away deep inside
Maybe to save your pride
But now that I've seen it
I can't be turned away
Your angel, your soul, has enchanted me
And together with you, I'll always stay.
