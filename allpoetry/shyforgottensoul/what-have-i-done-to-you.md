title: "What Have I Done To You?"

You do not like me 
I thought you were my friend, you acted like you cared 
You confused me and bewildered me
Even made me scared
I told the truth
Now you’ve up and changed
You won’t even speak to me
You act like you’ve forgotten my name
You cast an unnecessary impression of me
What did I do that was so untrue?
Please tell me 
Was it because I didn’t want to get next to you?
It makes no sense to me
How could you be so blind
I never did anything to you
Are you out of your mind?
The thoughts that run through your head
How I wonder what you are thinking
Do you truly hate me?
Your once high opinion of me shrinking
You cannot comprehend how upset I am
Because of all the things you do
The cruel things you continue to say
Tell me this please, what have I done to you?
I have done nothing
Nothing that was wrong
I looked out for a friend
Now you’re singing this pitiful song.
