Forever love
My forever friend
My heart is yours
To hold in your hand
But you don't see
My eyes for you
You don't know
How I feel
My heart crumbles
Everytime you talk 
About her
Friend is all you are
But how I long for more
Though a part of me knows
You could never return
The love I have for you
So I stay mute
And smile as your friend
Standing by you
Though I'm dying inside
I love you so much
You couldn't possibly comprehend
But I don't want to lose you
Completely in the end
So for now, forever more
I hide my heart
