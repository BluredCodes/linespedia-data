Eyes like yours
Look deep into me
Seeing what others
Cannot see

Your soul
Speaks to mine
Singing as one
Voices intertwined

You know what lies
Within the depths of me
My passion and desire
Burning to be set free

Your lips touch my skin
Like no other has done
And in this world we're alone
Feeling as warm as the sunJust something else that came to mind...
