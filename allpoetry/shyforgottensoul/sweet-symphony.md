A sweet melody flows throughout my mind
My soul sings the lyrics
And my heart beats the rhythm like a drum
The enchanting sound can be heard for miles
The sound of your voice being sung through the wind
So hypnotic... Like a spell has been cast upon me
I dance
Aimlessly around with no worries
Carefree
I get dizzy and I stumble
But you're there, and you catch me
You always catch me
We dance together
A symphony of light
Pouring from our hearts
Such bliss has come over me
