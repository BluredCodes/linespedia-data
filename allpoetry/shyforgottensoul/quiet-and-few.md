---
title: "Quiet And Few"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"

Quiet and few
Words in a whisper
Softly unmasking
Emotions unheard
Thoughts and wishes
Secretly hiding
Behind glittering eyes
Precious lips
Smiling ever so sweetly
Quiet and few
Moments such as these
Rarely seen
Barely heard
Unique and exquisite
Cherish
Quiet and few
You and I
Shadows dance
And lovers cry
A sweet melody
Flowing
Quiet and few
Shall it be
Slowly unfolding
A love so wonderfully
Every second
Greater than the last
Feelings so grand
Yet quiet and few.
