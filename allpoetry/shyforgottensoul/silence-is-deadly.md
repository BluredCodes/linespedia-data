Silence is deadly

And deadlier still

When the one you love

Is silent to you

Your heart begs for them

To speak some word of promise

But all you get is a quiet,

Cold,

And hardening stare

How could someone you love,

Be so vain?

 

Silence is deadly

And deadlier still

When a mother gives birth

But doesn’t hear her baby cry

A life awaited so long

Could be ended so short

A new mothers worry

If her child is still there

She looks about for answers

But is given sceptical glances

Not one with a certain meaning

Her questions, remain

 

Silence is deadly

And deadlier still

When you call out for help

But no one appears

Some cannot hear you

Some do not care

And so you’re left alone to suffer

And wallow in fear

 

Silence is deadly

And deadlier still

When you turn to hug someone

And the air is thin

There’s no one there to hold you

No one to comfort at all

And you’re left shivering in the cold,

Lonely, darkness

Your eyes well up with tears

And you open your mouth to speak

But there’s no one there

All that comes out is silence

And your heart dies within

 

Silence is deadly

And deadlier still

When you realize you’re alone
