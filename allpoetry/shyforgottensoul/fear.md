Fear
 
It haunts me
 
It threatens me
 
It betrays me
 
It hurts me
 
It rejects me
 
It destroys me
 
I'm alone
 
My fear is arriving
 
No sound
 
Deathly fear is coming
 
No place to hide
 
Fear.
