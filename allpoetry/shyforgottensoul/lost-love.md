---
title: "Lost Love"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"

source: "AllPoetry"

He'll never hold her in his arms
 
He'll never caress her soft skin
 
He'll never stroke her hair
 
He'll never feel her passionate kiss
 
She is gone
 
Nothing can change that
 
He never had a chance to tell her how he felt
 
He was never ready
 
But now that he is
 
She is gone
 
Gone forever
 
Lost forever
 
His lost love
 
He should've told her
 
But he couldn't
 
He has no one to turn to
 
No one to tell
 
Tears stream down his face
 
Everytime he hears her name
 
He'll never know what might have been
 
He is now filled with pain and regret
 
He should've told her how he felt
 
But he couldn't of even if he tried
 
Now, ten years later
 
His heart still aches for her
 
His soul, still whipsers out to her
 
And everytime he hears her name
 
The tears begin to fall once again.
