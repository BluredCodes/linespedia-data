Softly touching
Smooth skin
Warmed by passion
As each other grins

Hearts beating
Like an army of drums
Longing to be with one another
Until daylight comes

Lips parted slightly
Awaiting the next kiss
Breathing gets heavy
There isn't one second to miss

Eyes twinkling brightly
In the candlelight
Taking in every image
Muscles clenching tight

Overwhelming desire
Peeking to the top
Intensity rising
No wish to stop

Hunger and thirst
Craving it all
Every need fulfilled
In each others arms they fall.
