For unto the night I find my comfort
The black velvet sky blanketed with
The bright shining stars
The lights twinkle their silent hymns
And even through the cold
I am warmed by this heavenly sight
My eyes, cannot be turned away
From this glittering frenzy
I smile
Almost as bright as the radiant stars above.This just popped into my head *Shrugs*
