---
title: "Far Away Love"
writer: "ShyForgottenSoul"
slug: "far-away-love"

A love so far away,
Unable to grasp,
Please come to me,
And hold my hand,
I need you with me,
To feel you close,
To hear that you love me,
Forever more.
Sitting here missing you,
Is all I can do,
Wishing for the day,
when I’m next to you.
Days turn to nights, 
But my love still shines bright
your name dances upon my lips
I close my eyes and imagine you’re here
Dreams so vivid, dreams so real
I open my eyes and the illusion fades
Though the smile, never flounders from my face.
The joy of knowing I’ll have you one day.
Keeps me going, keeps me strong everyday.
So until the time comes when we are together
Never forget me, And keeps dear my letters.
I’ll always hold a place for you in my heart,
Now that you’re mine, I never want to part.
