title: "You"

You captivate meAnd hypnotize meI can’t get enough of youYour voice lingers in my headYour words still echoing around meYour face, your sweet face I still see your smileYour nameHow I love your nameI miss youWith every breath I take,I need you moreMy heart singsA feeling so new and amazingHow did you become so wonderful?Were you sent here just for me?Does your heart beat like mine?I can’t stop thinking of youWhat is it about you that draws me to you?Do you feel it?The pull, the forceIs it just me?Does my name leave footprints in your mind?Maybe I’m just crazyBut if I am, I love itAnd I don’t want to be saneI crave youLike an overwhelming addictionDo you crave me?				You mesmerise meLike waves upon the sandI am in awe of youI wish you felt the same as I do
