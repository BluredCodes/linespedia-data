extra time;
on the floor
[imposed fractions of anything real]
expected to s[t]ay
[nothing's wrong]
in this place,
where grotesque obscurity grows
into risque art.
and post-modern smiles.
[big deal]

Murdered cliches as they turn to dust
de-mol[d]-ish
featuresmemoriesregrets
FastForward into a better state or
worth.
