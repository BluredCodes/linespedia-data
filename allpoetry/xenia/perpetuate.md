title: "perpetuate"
writer: "Xenia"

Another one to fall off my list 
one more to join
[and I'm almost done]
Screeching doors left open
fall
on 
the 
dark 
ground

for sound
doesn't exist

keep
those
eyes
open

for I can't see
[not for long]

invade my sorrow
before it disappears




Listen
