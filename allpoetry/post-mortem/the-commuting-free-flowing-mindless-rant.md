(Or things I rarely say out loud.)

Dreamt about a drunk lady with a snake
And making it with Agent Scully
Wonder if the stock market knows
Or just hurts in random
11:11 a.m.
11:11 p.m.
All ones
Subliminal message
Or the Ying and Yang of time
I watched water boil
So that’s a lie.
Must have passed a serial killer
On the way to work
Boss man
Boss lady
Boss me 
Stayed in the elevator
To hear the whole song
Bag of grease for lunch
A day closer to coronary care
Seat belt, cell phone, ticket
Made a funny face in the mirror
Didn't stay like that
That's a lie too
Guess I should take all the teeth
Out from under my pillow
Wait in the toll line
A million butts under the guardrail
At least 10 cotton bales of filters
Same re-tread
In the same spot
Three weeks
EZ-pass
Speed trap
Gauntlet done
Driveway parked
Mail
No news is good news
At least that is not a lie
Dead squirrel in the pool
No beer
Time to visit the snake charmer
And the FBI lady
Sleep is the only escape
Monday to Friday
Lonely commuting blues rant
But I suppose that is a lie too
It's only Monday.
